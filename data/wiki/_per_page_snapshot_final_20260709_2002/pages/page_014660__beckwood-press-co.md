# Beckwood Press Co.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-06-04

**Source:** [Beckwood Press to build 3,500-ton bulge forming press for Wabash National](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/bending/beckwood-press-to-build-3500-ton-bulge-forming-press-for-wabash-national)  ·  `page_014660`

## Facts

- Beckwood Press Co. is a St. Louis-based manufacturer of hydraulic and servo-electric presses and automation systems.
- Beckwood Press has been awarded a contract by Wabash National Corp. to engineer and build a custom 3,500-ton hydraulic bulge forming press.
- The press is scheduled for delivery in October 2019.
- The machine will be used to form stainless steel and aluminum tank heads with diameters up to 156 in.
- The equipment supports Wabash National’s tank and silo business located in Lafayette, Ind.
- The press measures 285- by 216-in., weighs about 650,000 lbs. when fully assembled, and will be installed in a 6-ft.-deep pit.
- The press features 160- by 160-in. bolsters containing machined t-slots for use with a quick die change system.
- A fully adjustable 8-point square gib-guidance system will help ensure bed-to-ram parallelism throughout the stroke.
- To accommodate Wabash National’s existing tooling, the press will be able to bulge form using either air or water.
- Beckwood will design a closed-loop pneumatic control system for the bulge forming operation.
- The control system will allow Wabash National to form parts to specific dome heights while using up to 750 PSI of air pressure.
- An automated quick die change system with chain-driven die clamps and a bed trolley will be included.
- The trolley will transport dies up to 30,000 lbs. across a 30-ft. track via a push-button controls station.
