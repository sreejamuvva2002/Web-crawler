# Inalfa Roof Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2013-07-08

**Source:** [Inalfa Roof Systems to create 300 jobs in Georgia](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/shopmanagement/inalfa-roof-systems-to-create-300-jobs-in-georgia)  ·  `page_045132`

## Facts

- Inalfa Roof Systems is a provider of vehicle roofing systems with North American headquarters in Auburn Hills, Mich.
- Inalfa Roof Systems has announced plans to open a manufacturing plant in Cherokee County, Ga.
- The investment for the new facility is $17.1 million.
- The new plant is expected to create 300 jobs.
- Inalfa Roof Systems supplies sunroofs and open roofing systems for automotive manufacturers including Hyundai Kia, Ford, General Motors, Volvo, Chrysler Group, and BMW Group.
- The new Georgia facility will be the company's fourth manufacturing location in the U.S.
- The plant is projected to begin operating in January 2014.
