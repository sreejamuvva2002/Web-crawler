## Facts

- RealTruck is a global aftermarket product and accessory brand and digital destination for truck, Jeep, Bronco, and off-road enthusiasts.
- RealTruck officially sold its one millionth Retrax truck bed cover on February 10, 2026.
- The brand is launching a second generation update to the best-selling product as part of the RealTruck RetraxPRO Series.
- Retrax was founded in 1997.
- Brad Derosier serves as RealTruck Manufacturing Site Leader in North Dakota.
- The new RealTruck RetraxPRO features improvements to strength, security, ease of use, and protection against the elements.
- RealTruck RetraxPRO XR offers an integrated T-Slot rails option that connects with racks, cargo holders, tents, and cross bars.
- RetraxPRO XR includes TraxLatch™, providing unlimited lockable stopping positions to cinch gear between the cover and tailgate.
- Maura Rioux is RealTruck’s Sr. Director of Brand Marketing.
- Retrax helped pioneer the retractable truck bed cover category.
- The RealTruck lineup of premium retractable Retrax truck bed covers is designed and manufactured in the USA.
- Retrax covers utilize low-profile designs with compact storage canisters to maximize cargo capacity.
- The covers use durable materials for security and feature optional T-slot rails compatible with a broad range of cargo management accessories.
- The one millionth tonneau cover was produced at the RealTruck facility in North Dakota.
- The facility has been producing covers in Grand Forks, ND since 1997.
- The new RealTruck RetraxPRO MX and RetraxPRO XR are available now.
- Pricing for the new models starts at $1,999.
