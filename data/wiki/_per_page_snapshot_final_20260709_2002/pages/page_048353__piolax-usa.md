# Piolax USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-09-08

**Source:** [Piolax USA Announces Expansion of Canton Facility | Canton, GA Patch](https://patch.com/georgia/canton-ga/piolax-usa-announces-expansion-canton-facility)  ·  `page_048353`

## Facts

- Canton-based automotive manufacturer Piolax USA has announced plans to expand its facility located in the Canton-Cherokee Business & Industrial Park.
- The company specializes in plastic and metal automotive parts.
- The company has been a part of the community for nearly 30 years.
- The groundbreaking for the 82,000 square foot expansion was held on Thursday, Sept. 3.
- The expansion is slated to bring 30 new jobs.
- The expansion will add 30 jobs to their already substantial workforce of 255 employees.
- Piolax first arrived in the community in 1988.
- Steve L. Holcomb, Cherokee Office of Economic Development (COED) vice chairman, stated that Piolax has made a significant impact on the Cherokee workforce and attracted skilled labor since first arriving in 1988.
- Steve L. Holcomb added that they are extremely proud to have this well establish company in their county.
- The new automotive warehouse facility is anticipated to open in 2016.
- Canton Mayor Gene Hobgood was excited to hear of the plans to expand, adding that Piolax USA has been a good corporate partner to the city.
- Mayor Gene Hobgood added that they are pleased that they continue to grow.
