# Murata Electronics North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Murata Electronics North America, Inc. and its parent company Murata Manufacturing Co., Ltd. (TSE/OSE: 6981) are the world's number one supplier of passive electronic components.
- The companies signed a definitive agreement to acquire RF Monolithics, Inc. (NASDAQ: RFMI) in an all cash transaction.
- The acquisition is targeted to close in the third calendar quarter of 2012.
- The deal will pay the holders of RFM common shares $1.78 per share.
- RF Monolithics, Inc. is headquartered in Dallas, Texas.
- RF Monolithics provides solutions-driven, technology-enabled wireless connectivity for a broad range of wireless applications, including standardized and custom components to modules for comprehensive industrial wireless sensor networks and machine-to-machine (M2M) technology.
- David M. Kirk serves as President and CEO of Murata Electronics North America, Inc., which operates as the Regional Headquarters of Murata Americas.
- The acquisition announcement was issued from Smyrna, Ga.
