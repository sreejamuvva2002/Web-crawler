# Vanguard National Trailer Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-09-01

**Source:** [Vanguard National to Build Plant in Dade County - WDEF](https://www.wdef.com/vanguard-national-to-build-plant-in-dade-county/)  ·  `page_013068`

## Facts

- Vanguard National Trailer Corporation will soon bring 400 new jobs to the area.
- County leaders say the new plant is a boon for the entire county.
- Dade County, Georgia has been preparing for the last few years to attract a major employer.
- The Industrial Development Authority purchased 260 to 280 acres of property adjoining the old industrial park in Dade County, Georgia.
- Vanguard National Trailer Corporation made several trips to Trenton to look over the property.
- The site benefits from proximity to Interstate 59 and a major railroad line for transportation options.
- Ted Rumley serves as Chairman of the Dade County Commission.
- Peter Cervelli serves as Executive Director of the Industrial Development Authority.
- The Industrial Development Authority provided 10 years of tax-free status for the company.
- Significant financial incentives were provided for infrastructure by the Industrial Development Authority and the state of Georgia.
- The State of Georgia is providing over a million dollars for infrastructure work for the plant.
- The new facility will be 350-thousand square feet.
- Vanguard National Trailer Corporation aims to have the plant up and running by the end of April next year.
- Job applications will be taken next year.
- Vanguard National Trailer Corporation manufactures dry freight and refrigerated semi-trailer vans.
- Vanguard National Trailer Corporation has 47-thousand employees world-wide.
- The Shaw plant in the area closed back in 2008, leaving 420 people without jobs.
