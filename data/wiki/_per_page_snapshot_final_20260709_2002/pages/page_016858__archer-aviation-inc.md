# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Source:** [Archer Announces Fourth Quarter & Full Year 2024 Results, Sets P](https://www.gurufocus.com/news/2720074/archer-announces-fourth-quarter-full-year-2024-results-sets-plans-for-midnight-commercial-deployments-ahead-of-faa-certification)  ·  `page_016858`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. announced operating and financial results for the fourth quarter and fiscal year ended December 31, 2024.
- Archer issued a shareholder letter discussing those results as well as its first quarter 2025 estimates.
- Archer announced a comprehensive “Launch Edition” commercialization program for its Midnight aircraft to deploy commercially in dozens of early adopter markets in advance of FAA type certification.
- Abu Dhabi Aviation (ADA) will be Archer’s first Launch Edition customer, with plans to deploy an initial fleet of Midnight aircraft beginning later this year.
- Archer plans to provide ADA with a team of pilots, technicians and engineers to support the initial operational ramp.
- In December, Archer announced an exclusive partnership with Anduril to jointly develop a hybrid vertical take off and landing (VTOL) aircraft for critical defense applications.
- Archer believes the potential demand for the Anduril joint development aircraft is stronger than expected.
- Archer is working closely with Anduril on future vertical lift use cases for defense, expecting sizable programs of record without FAA certification.
- Archer is now starting production of its first Midnight aircraft at its ARC facility in Covington, GA.
- Archer is planning to build up to 10 Midnight aircraft this year to support ongoing certification testing programs and deployments with key partners.
- Archer’s total liquidity is now over $1B.
- Archer has maintained one of the strongest balance sheets in its industry and strengthened its leadership position with additional capital raised since the end of Q3’24.
- Archer’s spending in Q4’24 remained within its guided range and stayed nearly flat for the second straight quarter.
- The company's liquidity includes a Q4’24 ending cash balance of $835M and $302M raised through an equity offering in February.
- Archer’s founder and CEO Adam Goldstein stated that with aircraft production starting at ARC, they are on track to deliver their first revenue-generating Midnight aircraft later this year.
- Archer is designing and developing key enabling technologies and aircraft necessary to power the future of aviation.
