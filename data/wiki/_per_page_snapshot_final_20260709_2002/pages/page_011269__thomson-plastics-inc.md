# Thomson Plastics, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Thomson Plastics Opens Howell Township Manufacturing Facility, Plans to Create 46 New Jobs and Invest Nearly $4 million - Ann Arbor SPARK](https://annarborusa.org/news/thomson-plastics-opens-howell-township-manufacturing-facility-plans-to-create-46-new-jobs-and-invests-nearly-4-million/)  ·  `page_011269`

## Facts

- Thomson Plastics, Inc. is expanding to Michigan with a new manufacturing operation in Howell Township, supported by Ann Arbor SPARK.
- The Howell Township facility will be Thomson Plastics' first location in Michigan.
- Thomson Plastics plans to create 46 new jobs at the new facility.
- Thomson Plastics plans to invest nearly $4 million over two years for the new facility.
- Rick Kibbey, president and chief operating officer of Thomson Plastics, stated that the decision to operate in Howell was due to easy access to local expressways and its central location for major regions of automotive manufacturing in Michigan.
- Rick Kibbey noted that the selected factory building is in very good condition and helped minimize launch costs.
- Ann Arbor SPARK assisted Thomson Plastics in applying for local incentives.
- Thomson Plastics is an injection-molding company headquartered in Thomson, Georgia.
- Howell Township awarded Thomson Plastics a 50 percent property tax abatement on its new investment.
- Thomson Plastics serves the industrial and automotive markets.
- Ann Arbor SPARK helped Thomson Plastics coordinate with Michigan Works! and Mott Community College.
- Thomson Plastics plans to participate in the Michigan New Jobs Training program, which provides on-the-job training to new hires.
- Paul Krutko, president and CEO of Ann Arbor SPARK, stated that Thomson Plastics chose Howell Township for its available manufacturing talent to ensure a quick operational ramp-up.
- Paul Krutko noted that Michigan's manufacturing expertise is a key asset for attracting and growing companies like Thomson Plastics.
