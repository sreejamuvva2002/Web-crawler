# Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Panasonic Automotive Powers First UL-Certified eBike with Totem USA, Blending User-Friendly Design and Safety | Panasonic North America - United States](https://na.panasonic.com/news/panasonic-automotive-powers-first-ul-certified-ebike-with-totem-usa-blending-user-friendly-design-and-safety)  ·  `page_047888`

## Facts

- At CES 2022, Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America announced a collaboration with Totem USA to bring Totem’s first UL-certified eBike to the U.S. market, “Zen Rider.”
- In 2019, Panasonic received the industry’s first e-Bike safety standard certification from UL (UL2849).
- The Totem USA eBike “Zen Rider” is powered by a Panasonic motor and battery system designed as an affordable, family-friendly option to meet growing eBike market demands.
- Research from NPD indicated a 240% growth rate for electric bicycles in the 12 months leading up to July 2021, with sustained interest expected after pandemic-era consumer spending on cycling and sporting equipment.
- Derek Arciniaga, senior manager of business development at Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America, stated that the UL-certified Panasonic system is intended to expand mobility transformation in the United States.
- The Zen Rider features a low step-through mounting design, making its motor/battery system especially suited for urban areas.
- Panasonic’s second UL 2849 certified motor & battery eBike system includes a 2-Axle motor + battery configuration.
- The system provides 75 Nm of max torque and a 404 Wh battery.
- The eBike offers pedal assistance up to 15mph, aligning with city/local ordinances that have 15mph speed limitations.
- Totem USA, in collaboration with Panasonic, is prepared to design, develop, and produce custom eBike offerings for companies interested in UL certified eBikes.
- The Panasonic drive system is suitable for two-wheel and three-wheel city, touring, or cargo eBike variants.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America is a division company of Panasonic Corporation of North America and a leading global supplier of automotive infotainment and connectivity system solutions.
- Panasonic Automotive is headquartered in Peachtree City, Georgia, with sales, marketing, and engineering operations in Farmington Hills, Michigan.
- Totem USA is located in McKinney, Texas, and is a division of the Golden Wheel Group, which is the 4th largest manufacturer in the world of bicycles and eBikes.
- Panasonic Corporation of North America is headquartered in Newark, NJ, and is the principal North American subsidiary of Osaka, Japan-based Panasonic Corporation.
- Panasonic was named one of Interbrand’s Top 100 Best Global Brands of 2021.
