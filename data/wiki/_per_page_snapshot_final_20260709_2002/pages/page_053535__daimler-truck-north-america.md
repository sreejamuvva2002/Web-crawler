# Daimler Truck North America

**Type:** supplier_relationship  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Daimler Truck North America delivers 20 electric Freightliner eCascadias to Reyes Beverage Group | MT Article | Motor Transport](https://motortransport.co.uk/operations/daimler-truck-north-america-delivers-20-electric-freightliner-ecascadias-to-reyes-beverage-group/22847.article)  ·  `page_053535`

## Facts

- Daimler Truck North America (DTNA) delivered 20 battery-electric Freightliner eCascadia Class 8 tractors to Reyes Beverage Group (RBG), a beverage distributor in California.
- The delivery marks the first phase of RBG’s plan to transition to a zero-emission fleet in California by 2039.
- David Carson serves as Senior Vice President of Sales and Marketing for DTNA.
- David Carson stated that the Freightliner eCascadia is built with maximum productivity in mind for fleets switching to efficient, zero emission vehicles.
- David Carson noted that DTNA is the leading manufacturer of medium- and heavy-duty trucks in the U.S. and is committed to reducing emissions with partners like RBG.
- RBG’s broader sustainability efforts include a recent transition to renewable diesel and an ongoing idle-time reduction programme.
- RBG expects these EVs to contribute significantly to their goal of reducing carbon emissions by 60% by 2030.
- Tom Reyes serves as President of RBG West.
- Tom Reyes commented that RBG is among the first in its industry to adopt these electric vehicles and sees it as a significant step toward sustainability goals and state regulation compliance.
- A typical driving range of the eCascadia is 220 miles.
- The eCascadia can recharge to 80% capacity in as little as 90 minutes.
- The eCascadia offers multiple battery and drive axle configurations with ranges between 155 and 230 miles.
- The eCascadia features a Detroit proprietary ePowertrain for power, efficiency, and reliability.
- The eCascadia includes standard Detroit Assurance with Active Brake Assist 5 Suite of Safety Systems.
- DTNA offers comprehensive fleet electrification solutions, including Detroit eFill commercial chargers and Detroit eConsulting services to assist customers transitioning to electric vehicles.
- The delivery represents a significant step in the commercial transportation industry’s move towards more sustainable operations.
