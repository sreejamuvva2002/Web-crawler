# Archer Midnight eVTOL Air Taxi

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-09

**Source:** [Archer Midnight eVTOL Air Taxi Could Replace Helicopters for 20–50 Mile City Flights -](https://mikeshouts.com/archer-midnight-evtol-air-taxi/)  ·  `page_016952`

## Facts

- The multi-rotor electric vertical take-off and landing vehicle was first announced in 2021.
- Commercial launch is on track for this year, contingent on FAA approval.
- Archer will serve as the official air taxi provider of the Olympic Games and Team USA at LA 2028.
- The long fuselage has room for up to four passengers, their carry-on luggage, and a pilot.
- It is powered by six independent proprietary battery packs, each supporting a pair of Archer custom electric engines.
- The battery packs feature proven safe and reliable lithium-ion cells.
- The battery system can be charged in around 10–12 minutes between flights.
- The aircraft is designed to cruise at 240 km/h (150 mph).
- It has a range of 160 km (100 miles).
- It operates at approximately 100 times quieter than conventional helicopters.
- Operations require dedicated airports for eVTOL aircraft called vertiports.
- These vertiports would also serve as charging stations.
- The aircraft is aimed at fleet operators such as air taxi services, corporate fleets, and government programs rather than individual buyers.
- The latest catalog price is US$5 million.
