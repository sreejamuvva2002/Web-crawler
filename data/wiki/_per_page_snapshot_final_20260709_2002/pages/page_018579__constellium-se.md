# Constellium SE

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-15

**Source:** [Constellium Drives Digital and Modular Innovation for Smart Aluminum Automotive Structures with ARENA2036](https://finviz.com/news/103585/constellium-drives-digital-and-modular-innovation-for-smart-aluminum-automotive-structures-with-arena2036)  ·  `page_018579`

## Facts

- Constellium SE (NYSE: CSTM) announced the successful completion and publication of the FlexCAR project.
- The FlexCAR project was an innovation effort with ARENA2036, Germany’s leading research campus for future mobility and production technologies.
- The FlexCAR platform envisions configuring or updating major vehicle systems such as the drivetrain, energy storage, and interior.
- Constellium worked alongside project partners Mercedes-Benz, Siemens, Bosch, and the German Aerospace Center (DLR) to complete the publicly funded, five-year project.
- Constellium developed a modular sill concept using aluminum extrusions made from HSA6™ high-strength aluminum alloys featuring significant recycled content.
- The sill structure is designed to support various powertrains including battery electric and hydrogen fuel cell.
- The sill structure offers enhanced crash performance, design flexibility, and a lower carbon footprint over the vehicle’s lifecycle.
- Patrick Böhler, Director of Sales & Product Development for Constellium Automotive Structures, stated that FlexCAR shows how modular thinking combined with aluminum's performance and sustainability benefits can revolutionize vehicle design.
- Böhler noted that such collaborations turn ideas into real-world innovations enabling longer lifespans, greater adaptability, and lower environmental impact.
- Constellium previously collaborated with ARENA2036 on the Digital Fingerprint innovation project, which was completed in 2024.
- The Digital Fingerprint project involved developing a digital twin of an aluminum component that tracks its performance across its full lifecycle from design and production to on-road use and end-of-life.
- As part of this project, Constellium engineered a smart aluminum housing for a power control unit, embedding sensors to collect crash and field data in a Mercedes-Benz test vehicle.
- This approach supports predictive maintenance, accelerates development cycles, and unlocks the full potential of connected, data-driven manufacturing.
- Constellium is a full-service supplier of rolled and extrusion-based aluminum solutions for the global automotive market.
- Constellium helps automakers produce lighter, safer, and more fuel-efficient vehicles, as well as electric vehicles with greater range.
- ARENA2036 (Active Research Environment for the Next Generation of Automobiles) is a research campus in Stuttgart-Vaihingen, Germany designed as a highly flexible and future-oriented research platform for mobility and production.
- Constellium has been a contributing member of ARENA2036 since 2018.
- Constellium (NYSE: CSTM) is a global sector leader that develops innovative, value-added aluminum products for aerospace, packaging, and automotive markets.
- Constellium generated $7.3 billion of revenue in 2024.
