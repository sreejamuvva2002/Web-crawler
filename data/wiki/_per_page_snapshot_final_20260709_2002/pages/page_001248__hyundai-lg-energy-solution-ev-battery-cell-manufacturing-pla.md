# Hyundai-LG Energy Solution EV Battery Cell Manufacturing Plant

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-26

**Source:** [Hyundai and LG Energy Solution build EV battery plant in Bryan County](https://www.savannahnow.com/story/business/2023/05/26/hyundai-partners-with-lg-energy-solutions-for-ev-battery-cell-plant/70260108007/)  ·  `page_001248`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group revealed plans on Friday for an EV battery cell manufacturing factory that is to be part of the Hyundai Metaplant in Bryan County.
- Hyundai and LG Energy Solution will partner on a $4.3 billion plant.
- The facility is expected to produce 300,000 batteries a year.
- The facility is expected to open in late 2025.
- The battery plant opening is scheduled just months after Hyundai's EV assembly factory is scheduled to come online.
- A supplier building a facility in Richmond Hill, Hyundai Mobis, will also play a role in battery production.
- The EV battery factory will be built next to the Hyundai assembly plant in Bryan County.
