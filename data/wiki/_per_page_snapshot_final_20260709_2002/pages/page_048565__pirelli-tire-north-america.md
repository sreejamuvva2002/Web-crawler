# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2007-10

**Source:** [Mannino Leaves PTNA - Tyrepress](https://www.tyrepress.com/2007/10/mannino-leaves-ptna/)  ·  `page_048565`

## Facts

- Guy Mannino, President of Pirelli Tire North America (PTNA), is leaving the company after 25 years of service to pursue other professional interests.
- Mannino began his career in 1982 and embarked on a series of international management assignments with the Pirelli Power Transmission Division.
- In 1993, Mannino joined the Tire Division and held management positions in Italy, Venezuela, and the United States.
- Hugh Pace, Chairman & CEO of PTNA, will assume leadership for all business and commercial operations in addition to his current executive role.
- Under Mannino’s leadership, PTNA relocated its corporate headquarters to Rome, Georgia.
- Under Mannino’s leadership, PTNA built a state-of-the-art MIRS manufacturing facility.
- Under Mannino’s leadership, PTNA expanded its national distribution network.
- Pirelli Tire North America extended its gratitude to Mannino for his many contributions to the company and wished him and his family personal and professional success.
