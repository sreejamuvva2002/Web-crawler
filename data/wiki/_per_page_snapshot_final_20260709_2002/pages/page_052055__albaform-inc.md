## Facts

- ALBAform Inc. is owned by its Czech parent company ALBA-METAL.
- The company designs, develops, and manufactures precision metal components with a focus on the automotive industry.
- ALBAform leverages over 20 years of ALBA-METAL’s metal production experience alongside its own industry expertise.
- The company is located in Flowery Branch, Georgia.
- ALBAform is growing quickly to service its partners and customers.
- In addition to manufacturing capabilities, the company offers consignment stock systems using standardized packaging and transport units to minimize inventory and transportation costs.
- The facility features the latest fully automated CNC bending technology.
- The facility operates 16 welding robots.
