# Pirelli S.p.A.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-05

**Source:** [PIRELLI WILL START CYBER™ TYRE PRODUCTION IN GEORGIA UNDERLINING THE STRATEGIC IMPORTANCE OF THE U.S. MARKET – News Incredible](https://newsincredible.com/pirelli-will-start-cyber-tyre-production-in-georgia-underlining-the-strategic-importance-of-the-u-s-market/)  ·  `page_048486`

## Facts

- Pirelli will start Cyber™ Tyre production at its Rome, Georgia plant.
- Investment and output expansion details for the Georgia facility will be communicated in the coming months following the finalization of development plans.
- The announcement comes as Pirelli participates in the SelectUSA Investment Summit, the flagship event promoted by the U.S. Department of Commerce.
- Cyber™ Tyre is the world’s first hardware-and-software system capable of collecting data and information from sensors embedded in tires.
- The system processes collected sensor data through Pirelli’s proprietary software and algorithms.
- Cyber™ Tyre communicates in real time with vehicle electronics to enable new functionalities integrated with driving and control systems.
- The technology aims to enhance the driving experience, increase safety levels, and support connected infrastructure.
- Cyber™ Tyre was showcased at the Georgia State booth during the SelectUSA summit.
- Pirelli has been established in Georgia for more than 20 years.
- Claudio Zanardo, CEO of Pirelli North America, stated that the start of Cyber™ Tyre production in Rome, Georgia is a significant milestone reflecting commitment to bringing advanced technologies closer to the market.
- Pirelli is finalizing the introduction of the latest version of the MIRS (Modular Integrated Robotized System) production process at the Rome plant.
- The MIRS process will be the most advanced manufacturing process for high-end, premium production within the entire Pirelli Group and is exclusive to the Georgia factory.
- The new MIRS process enhances robotized production capabilities, increasing productivity, flexibility, and quality.
- MIRS is a highly digitalized system that enables a direct link between product design and its industrial application.
- This development lays the groundwork for growth in Pirelli’s production capacity in Rome, Georgia.
- The Rome, Georgia plant specializes in high-value tire production for the North American market.
- The facility hosts a dedicated R&D center to strengthen its role in technological development and product innovation.
- The facility is recognized for responsible sourcing practices, including the use of FSC® (Forest Stewardship Council®)-certified natural rubber.
