# Rivian Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-03

## Facts

- Rivian ended 2025 with a 14% decline in vehicle production.
- Rivian's sales increased just 8% in 2025.
- Rivian had a GAAP loss of $3.79 per share in 2025.
- Rivian cut its workforce by less than 2% in June, following two rounds of layoffs last year.
- Rivian achieved two quarters of gross profitability in 2025, driven primarily by sales from its software and services segment.
- Rivian is rolling out its new R2 vehicle platform, which is a smaller SUV than the R1.
- The Rivian R2 starts at around $58,000.
- Rivian plans to continually roll out cheaper versions of the R2, eventually selling a $45,000 base version sometime next year.
- Rivian is led by founder RJ Scaringe, who founded the company in 2009.
