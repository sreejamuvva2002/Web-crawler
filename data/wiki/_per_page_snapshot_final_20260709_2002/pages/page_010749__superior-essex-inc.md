# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-08-19

**Source:** [Superior Essex Announces Essex Solutions, Relaunching Magnet Wire Brand](https://markets.financialcontent.com/wral/article/gnwcq-2024-8-19-superior-essex-announces-essex-solutions-relaunching-magnet-wire-brand)  ·  `page_010749`

## Facts

- Superior Essex, a global leader in the magnet wire and communication cables industry, announced the next phase in the rebranding of its magnet wire business.
- The company is changing its brand name to Essex Solutions as part of its recent buyout of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire.
- Essex Solutions will encompass all current Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire locations worldwide.
- Essex Energy in Italy will now be included, further reinforcing the company’s global reach and capabilities.
- Related businesses Lacroix + Kress, IVA Insulations, and Hi-Wire will join the Essex Solutions organization while continuing to operate under their existing brand identity.
- The consolidation enables better coordination and collaboration among the brands, resulting in improved efficiency and effectiveness.
- Daniel Choi, President and CEO of Superior Essex, stated that aligning brands under one grouping allows the company to offer a complete suite of solutions and take further advantage of vertical integration and global reach.
- Choi noted the company's ability to proactively shape the xEV automotive industry, as well as power, electrification, and renewable energy, paving the way for future technologies.
- During the transition period, customers and stakeholders may see materials bearing either the existing Essex Furukawa or the new Essex Solutions name.
- All changes are expected to be completed by April 30, 2025.
- Essex Solutions will unveil a new logo in the near future to reflect its evolution and commitment to innovation and excellence.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Solutions, Superior Essex Communications, IVA, Lacroix + Kress, Hi-Wire, and Essex Brownell.
- The company has over 3,000 employees in 11 countries across three continents.
- Superior Essex is on the forefront of communications fiber application as well as Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company is instrumental in creating disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex is committed to pioneering smart building technology while also setting the pace on sustainability.
