# Kia Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [KIA Georgia Plant Tour - West Point  (Morning) 2024 -  Georgia Manufacturing Alliance](https://www.georgiamanufacturingalliance.com/events/kia-georgia-plant-tour-morning-2024)  ·  `page_010240`

## Facts

- Kia Georgia, Inc. is located at 7777 Kia Parkway, West Point, GA 31833, United States.
- The facility has an annual production capacity of more than 350,000 vehicles.
- Kia Georgia produces the Telluride, Sorento, Sportage, and K5 midsize sedan models.
- These models are among the brand’s top-selling in the United States.
- The facility represents a total investment of $2.8 billion on a 2,200 acre site.
- The plant employs 3,200 team members.
- Kia Georgia is the only Kia manufacturing plant in the United States.
- It is the only automaker in Georgia.
- Mass production at the facility began in November 2009.
- Since starting mass production, the plant has produced more than 4 million vehicles.
- The facility produces one vehicle every 52 seconds.
- Production operates on three full shifts, running 24 hours a day, five days per week.
- Tour attendees observe the entire manufacturing process from rolled metal to finished product using high-tech stamping, weld shop advanced robotics, and highly automated assembly processes.
- The tour is available exclusively to Georgia Manufacturing Alliance (GMA) members.
- Initially, only one attendee per company is allowed to register for the tour.
- Members who have never taken the tour are prioritized for registration.
- A complimentary ticket is available for first-time GMA event attendees, with limited quantities available.
- Tour attendees must be at least 13 years old.
- A maximum of two people per company are allowed to attend the tour.
- Tobacco products, firearms, and photography are strictly prohibited on-site.
- Tours are subject to cancellation with little or no notice depending on the production schedule.
- Visitors must wear long pants and enclosed toe shoes to participate in the tour.
- The tour involves a tram ride with portions conducted outside.
- Visitor parking is located in numbered front rows, and attendees must enter through the Visitor's Center at Building 7797 for security confirmation.
