# Fox Factory Holding Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-07-27

## Facts

- Fox Factory released its 2022 global sustainability report detailing strategy and achievements.
- The company introduced 2030 aspirations for positive impacts across its operations and communities.
- CEO Mike Dennison stated the company formally embarked on its sustainability journey in 2021 and aims for win-win visions benefiting business, stakeholders, people, and the environment while increasing performance.
- Fox Factory awarded over $1.2 million USD in grants to 85 nonprofit organizations focused on sustainable adventure through its Trail Trust community outreach initiative.
- The company conducted a comprehensive climate risk and opportunity assessment aligned with the Task Force on Climate-Related Financial Disclosures framework.
- Fox Factory became the official suspension partner of Extreme E, described as the world’s most sustainable extreme racing series.
- The company achieved a 69% engagement score on its global employee survey, representing a 5% increase from 2021.
- Fox Factory launched the Women’s Interactive Network Employee Resource Group with over 75 members.
- The company provided over $180,000 USD in donations and corporate matches to 550 nonprofits through its employee giving program.
- Fox Factory rolled out a Supplier Code of Conduct to all Tier 1 suppliers outlining expectations for responsible and ethical practices.
- The company increased pre-production testing volume by 40% for the Specialty Sports Group and by 100% for the Powered Vehicles Group to ensure optimal product quality.
- Fox Factory achieved ISO 14001 certification at its largest manufacturing facility in Gainesville, Georgia, demonstrating a commitment to responsible environmental management in operations.
- The company began developing a new Enterprise Risk Management framework and Compliance Management System.
- Fox Factory achieved a 100% completion rate for Code of Ethics training by Director-level and above employees.
- The company developed a comprehensive Information Technology Policy to reinforce defenses against cyber threats and uphold data security standards.
- For 2030, Fox Factory aims to consistently achieve top quartile employee engagement scores and attain increased representation internally and externally.
- By 2030, the company plans to introduce cost-effective sustainable thinking into 100% of new development projects as well as packaging material usage and design.
- Fox Factory’s 2030 social impact goals include globally granting $10 million USD, expanding access to 100,000 underrepresented adventurers through Trail Trust, and achieving 35% employee giving participation in its global giving program.
- The company targets a 25% reduction in operational emissions (Scope 1 & 2) from its 2022 baseline by 2030.
- Fox Factory aims to ensure that at least 90% of its Tier 1 supply chain partners have signed its Supplier Code of Conduct by 2030.
