# TDK Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [EMC Components: TDK Offers Multilayer Chip Beads with the Industry's Highest Rated Current of 8 A for Power Supply Lines](https://www.powersystemsdesign.com/articles/emc-components-tdk-offers-multilayer-chip-beads-with-the-industrys-highest-rated-current-of-8-a-for-power-supply-lines/97/22756)  ·  `page_060213`

## Facts

- TDK Corporation has expanded its MPZ1608-PH series of large-current multilayer chip beads for automotive and commercial power supply lines, measuring 1.6 x 0.8 x 0.6 mm in length, width, and height.
- Mass production of the MPZ1608-PH series began in May 2025.
- The 1608-size chip beads achieve a rated current of 8 A, which is the industry’s highest value as of May 2025.
- These chip beads function as noise suppression components in power and signal circuits.
- Conventional circuits handling 8 A or more typically require two or more chip beads connected in parallel, which causes uneven current distribution between the ferrite beads.
- TDK’s new product simplifies circuit structure by requiring fewer components compared to conventional parallel methods.
- The new design improves the overall quality of power circuits.
- The MPZ1608-PH series halves the component footprint compared to circuits that use two conventional 1608-sized chip beads.
- The components are highly reliable with a specified operating temperature range up to +125 °C.
- The chips are designed for high-temperature environments, specifically targeting automotive and industrial equipment applications.
- TDK utilizes proprietary materials and structural designs adapted to market needs for this product line.
- The company is committed to expanding its lineup of high rated current products across the automotive, industrial equipment, and consumer equipment sectors.
- These multilayer chip beads serve as EMC components that enhance power circuit quality.
