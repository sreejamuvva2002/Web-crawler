# Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-12-15

**Source:** [Panasonic Automotive Systems Unveils New Brand Reflecting Vision to Drive Future of Mobility](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/panasonic-automotive-systems-unveils-brand-043000690.html)  ·  `page_047955`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd. announced it will change its company name and brand to Mobitera to better represent its commitment to creating a safe, comfortable, and sustainable mobility experience.
- The corporate name change is effective April 1, 2027.
- Masashi Nagayasu serves as the chief executive officer (CEO) of Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd.
- In December 2024, Panasonic Automotive Systems began a strategic partnership with the Apollo Group to strengthen software development capabilities for the in-vehicle cockpit space and optimize its overall business portfolio.
- The rebrand to Mobitera is positioned as the next step for accelerated growth following the Apollo Group partnership.
- The new corporate name will be Mobitera Inc.
- The brand name 'Mobitera' fuses 'mobility,' the Latin word 'iter' (path), and the Japanese word 'terasu' (to illuminate).
- The central 'i' in the brand name highlights a vow to keep individuals at the focus of all operations.
- The corporate color, Mobitera Blue-Green, symbolizes the harmonization of people, society, and the planet.
- The company's mission is to create a sustainable mobility society to promote a better life for everyone.
- The company's vision is defined as the 'Joy in Motion' design company.
- Domestic and overseas consolidated subsidiaries will change their corporate names to align with the new Mobitera brand, effective April 1, 2027.
- Panasonic ITS Co., Ltd. will become Mobitera ITS Inc.
- Panasonic Automotive Electronics Co., Ltd. will become Mobitera Solutions Inc.
- Panasonic System Networks Evaluation Technology Co., Ltd. will become Mobitera Evaluation Technologies Inc.
- Harima SANYO Industries Co., Ltd. will become Mobitera Wel Inc.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems America, LLC will become Mobitera America, LLC.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems de Mexico S.A. de C.V. will become Mobitera De Mexico, S.A de C.V.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Reynosa Mexico S.A. de C.V. will become Mobitera Reynosa Mexico, S.A. de C.V.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Monterrey Mexico S.A. de C.V. will become Mobitera Monterrey Mexico, S.A. de C.V.
- Panasonic Automotive Sales Company de Mexico, S.A. de C.V. will become Mobitera Sales Company de Mexico, S.A. de C.V.
- Panasonic Automotive Shared Services Company Mexico, S.A. de C.V. will become Mobitera Shared Service Company Mexico, S.A. de C.V.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Europe GmbH will become Mobitera Europe GmbH.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Czech, s.r.o. will become Mobitera Czech, s.r.o.
- OpenSynergy GmbH will undergo no name change.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Asia Pacific Co., Ltd. will become Mobitera Asia Pacific Co., Ltd.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Malaysia Sdn. Bhd. will become Mobitera Malaysia Sdn. Bhd.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems India Pvt. Ltd. will become Mobitera India Pvt. Ltd.
- Details regarding Panasonic Automotive Systems (China) Co., Ltd., Panasonic Automotive Systems (Dalian) Co., Ltd., and Panasonic Automotive Systems (Suzhou) Co., Ltd. will be announced after internal procedures are completed.
