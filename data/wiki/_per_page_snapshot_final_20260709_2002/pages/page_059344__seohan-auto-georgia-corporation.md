## Facts

- Seohan Auto Georgia Corporation is a Tier-1 automotive parts supplier for Hyundai Motor Group America.
- The facility utilizes state-of-the-art production facilities and company-wide quality management measures to produce 0% defective perfect products.
- Seohan Auto Georgia aims to raise the value of finished car brands through continuous technology development and serve as a trusted partner prioritizing American customer satisfaction.
