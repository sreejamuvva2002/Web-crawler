# Yazaki North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2013-06-05

**Source:** [pr-20-06-2013 | ams OSRAM](https://ams-osram.com/news/press-releases/pr-20-06-2013)  ·  `page_015964`

## Facts

- Yazaki North and Central America named Osram Opto Semiconductors Green Supplier of the Year at its annual awards ceremony.
- Yazaki recognized Osram Opto Semiconductors as the Best in Class Green supplier for 2012-2013, honoring them with both awards of excellence.
- Osram was selected from Yazaki’s top 103 suppliers in North and Central America.
- Olga Alavanou serves as executive vice president, Supply Chain Management for Yazaki.
- Yazaki’s Green Supplier of the Year program reinforces its commitment to helping create a society capable of sustainable development.
- Frank Tillner is Chief Operating Officer at Osram Opto Semiconductors.
- Osram follows a responsible, sustainable, and careful environmental policy.
- Osram's programs save energy, protect water, and avoid chemical waste in both production and products.
- Osram was chosen for significant contributions to environmental preservation through manufacturing, production, and delivery processes.
- The award ceremony was held on June 5, 2013, to honor World Environment Day.
- The Yazaki Green Supplier of the Year program was established in 2007 as a benchmark for all Yazaki companies and affiliates throughout North and Central America.
- OSRAM is headquartered in Munich and is one of two leading lighting manufacturers globally.
- Osram Opto Semiconductors GmbH is headquartered in Regensburg, Germany.
- Osram provides semiconductor technology solutions for lighting, sensor, and visualization applications.
- Osram operates production sites in Regensburg (Germany) and Penang (Malaysia).
- Osram's North America headquarters is located in Sunnyvale (USA).
- Osram's Asia region headquarters is located in Hong Kong.
- Yazaki Corporation is a global leader in the research, development, and delivery of vehicle power and data solutions for vehicle applications.
- Yazaki produces electrical distribution systems, vehicle information products, solid-state power centers, connection systems, and electronics.
- Yazaki employs more than 220,000 people worldwide across 41 countries.
- Yazaki has been committed to environmental preservation for more than 70 years.
- Yazaki develops advanced electric components for hybrid electric vehicles.
- Yazaki promotes recycling and the efficient use of resources.
- World Environment Day is an annual United Nations event that climaxes on June 5 each year, having begun in 1972.
