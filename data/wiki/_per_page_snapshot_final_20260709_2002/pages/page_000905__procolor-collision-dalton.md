# ProColor Collision Dalton

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2026-04-06

**Source:** [ProColor Collision Expands to Georgia](https://www.bodyshopbusiness.com/procolor-collision-strengthens-southeast-presence-with-new-dalton-georgia-location/)  ·  `page_000905`

## Facts

- ProColor Collision announced it has opened its first location in Georgia with ProColor Collision Dalton.
- The independently owned facility serves drivers across North Georgia and South Tennessee from 927 S. Hamilton St.
- The shop is owned and operated by Chris James, a lifelong Dalton resident with more than eight years of hands-on collision repair experience.
- James has a career spanning insurance, mechanical, parts, paint, and fixed operations leadership.
- James decided to join ProColor Collision after his two adult sons expressed interest in carrying on the shop's legacy.
- James stated that ProColor Collision offers resources, structure, and guidance while allowing full ownership and control of the business.
- The shop reports zero turnover, with the same employees who started with James years ago still serving customers today.
- The facility recently underwent a major expansion, adding 5,000 square feet of workspace to support growing demand.
- The facility is equipped with a Spanesi frame machine, OEM-certified GYS spot welders, and a full suite of Freon machines.
- ProColor Collision Dalton serves a predominantly truck-heavy market with many customers driving full-size pickup trucks common throughout North Georgia's "truck country."
- The team is equipped and experienced to repair vehicles of all makes, models, shapes, and sizes.
- Formerly operating as Dalton Collision Center, the shop received the 2023 Best of Georgia Award determined entirely by customer votes.
- ProColor Collision Dalton sponsors all four area high schools across all sports and activities, as well as Northstar School.
- James prioritizes daily production meetings to ensure parts, repair steps, and paint timelines stay on track.
- Nicky Woerner, vice president of sales and operations for ProColor Collision USA, stated that Chris embodies local roots, operational expertise, and a commitment to quality.
