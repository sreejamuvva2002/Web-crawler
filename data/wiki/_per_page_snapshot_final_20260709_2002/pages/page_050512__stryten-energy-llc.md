# Stryten Energy LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-09

**Source:** [View Press Releases | Battery Power Online](https://www.batterypoweronline.com/view-press-releases/entry/4288/)  ·  `page_050512`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC announced at AAPEX 2022 the launch of Stryten Energy Components on November 9, 2022 in Las Vegas, Nevada.
- The company is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- The new division provides engineered-to-order battery components for lead, lithium, flow and other emerging battery technologies for the automotive and industrial markets.
- Stryten Energy invested in automation in each of its four facilities to enhance quality, productivity and service to all customers.
- Jeremy Furr, Components Division Leader for Stryten Energy, stated that these investments helped scale up operations to provide U.S.-manufactured and sourced components required for the emerging domestic battery market.
- The division provides engineered plastic components from natural and recycled polypropylene, including battery containers, covers, vents, handles and terminal protectors.
- Stryten Energy Components offers cold-formed lead battery terminals designed to prevent corrosion and utilizes a patented SuperLock terminal to provide superior sealing.
- The company provides value-added technical services and expertise to help customers with product development, design, mold making and injection molding processing.
- Jeremy Furr noted an increase in market demand for critical battery components in the U.S. and internationally.
- Stryten Energy is continuing to invest in its component plants to expand its product offering and availability to serve international customers.
- The division combines the company's existing custom plastic injection molded battery component operations with the recent acquisition of Tulip Richardson Manufacturing’s plastic injection molding, lead terminal and reprocessed polypropylene operations.
- Stryten Energy Components operates manufacturing facilities in Lampeter, PA; Columbus, IN; Niagara Falls, NY; and Milwaukee, WI.
- The company provides energy storage solutions and components across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military and Government sectors.
- Stryten Energy powers applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains and trucks.
- The company's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers and energy performance management software.
