# Valeo Service

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Leading the Way in Electrification | Valeo Service](https://www.valeoservice.com/en-com/newsroom/leading-way-electrification)  ·  `page_012788`

## Facts

- As the OE leader in electrification, Valeo equips one in three hybrid and electric cars worldwide.
- Offers an extensive portfolio of over 6,000 essential references to support the maintenance and repair of electric and hybrid vehicles in the independent aftermarket.
- Product portfolio includes filtration systems, wipers, brake pads, discs, calipers, and thermal solutions such as e-compressors, radiators, and condensers.
- The large range of condensers currently covers more than 8 million electric and hybrid vehicles across Europe, with plans to increase this coverage to 10 million by the end of 2024.
- Announced that its cutting-edge chiller for battery thermal management in electric and hybrid vehicles is available for the VW ID.3.
- Plans to launch four new chillers by the second quarter of 2025 designed for Hyundai models like the Ioniq, Kona, and Tucson, as well as the Kia Niro.
- Expanding its offer with five new electric driven compressors designed specifically to fit the latest "eTech" platform from Renault, including the Kadjar, Scenic, Captur, and Arkana models.
- New electric driven compressors also fit the Tesla Model 3 and some applications from Kia and Hyundai.
- Launched electric oil pumps in July for popular models such as the BMW X3 and Audi Q5.
- The 48V DC-DC converter references 597202 and 597203, suitable for the Fiat 500, Peugeot 208, Citroen C5, and many other applications, will be launched during the first quarter of 2025.
- Offers a wide range of 48V iBSG systems, noting that the car park is primarily evolving through mild hybrid vehicles.
- New brake pads range includes 222 references with 92% electric and hybrid vehicle coverage, accounting for over 15 million European vehicles.
- The e-performance brake pads use soft friction materials composed of carbons and ceramics that absorb noise and vibrations made during braking.
- Offers a range of cables and mobile chargers for electric and hybrid vehicles, featuring 12 charging cable references and 15 mobile charger references in various lengths and charging speeds.
- All charging cables and mobile chargers are fully compatible with all rechargeable vehicles.
- Provides technical training through the Valeo Tech Academy program, which offers a comprehensive curriculum on Electric Vehicle and High Voltage systems.
- The Tech Academy curriculum includes in-depth courses on HV safety protocols, component diagnostics, and repair procedures to ensure technicians can handle EV maintenance demands.
