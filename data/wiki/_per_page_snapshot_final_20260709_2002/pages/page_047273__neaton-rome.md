## Facts

- The Neaton Rome plant is located at 1634 Technology Pkwy.
- Senior Production Manager Mike Hulsey explained plans for a $26 million expansion to the Rome Floyd County Development Authority.
- The planned expansion will add 113 jobs over three years.
- Mike Hulsey asked the Rome Floyd County Development Authority to support a five-year financing plan.
- Rome Floyd County Development Authority members present included Diane Lewis, Otis Raybon, and Angie Lewis.
- Attorneys Andy Davis and Alex Warren were also present at the meeting regarding the expansion plans.
