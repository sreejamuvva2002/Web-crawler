## Facts

- Launch 3 Telecom announced it has secured a new facility in Lakeland, Florida.
- The new Lakeland facility is located at 848 Kensinger Road, Lakeland, FL 33815.
- The facility spans approximately 25,000 square feet.
- Launch 3 expects to complete its move into the facility in the next two to three months from June 15, 2026.
- The expansion enables Launch 3 to store, manage, and distribute a wider range of telecom, electrical, and related products.
- CEO David Zoldan stated the company is investing heavily in logistics and new product categories as the traditional wireless market matures.
- Launch 3 is strategically pivoting into logistics and third-party logistics (3PL) services.
- Launch 3 is expanding into electrical products and complementary technologies.
- Launch 3 is forming new distribution partnerships with manufacturers outside its core wireless business.
- The Lakeland expansion supports Launch 3's goal to become a go-to logistics and distribution partner for manufacturers seeking reach, reliability, and scalable fulfillment in North America.
- Launch 3 is actively seeking new partnerships with manufacturers in electrical and related sectors looking for distribution and logistics support.
- To support its expansion, Launch 3 is actively hiring high-level, senior professionals across key functions.
- Hiring focuses on top-tier, leadership-level talent for roles supporting logistics, operations, business development, and strategic growth.
- Launch 3 Telecom is a nationwide distributor and logistics partner specializing in wireless infrastructure and electrical products and solutions.
- The company generates approximately $25 million in annual revenue.
- Launch 3 provides materials from leading manufacturers alongside value-added services including third-party logistics (3PL), site kitting, and operational support.
- The company is headquartered in Fairfield, New Jersey.
- Launch 3 serves the telecom, DAS, EV charging, data center, and electrical industries with scalable solutions designed to streamline project execution nationwide.
