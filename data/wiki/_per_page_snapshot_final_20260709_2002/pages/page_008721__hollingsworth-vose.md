# Hollingsworth & Vose

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hollingsworth & Vose wins 2026 BCI Innovation Award  - Automotive Industry Today - EIN Presswire](https://automotive.einnews.com/pr_news/907576437/hollingsworth-vose-wins-2026-bci-innovation-award)  ·  `page_008721`

## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose won the 2026 BCI Innovation Award.
- The award-winning innovation is known as Glass Electrode Material (GEM).
- GEM helps generate and support a unique microporous structure within the lead active mass of a battery to enhance chargeability performance and cycle life reliability without impacting water loss.
- Roger Miksad, President and Executive Director of BCI, stated that battery innovation is thriving and Hollingsworth & Vose is an example of technological leadership advancing the capabilities of the storied lead battery.
- GEM improves recharge efficiency through enhanced charge acceptance, reducing fuel-related emissions while improving battery performance, durability, and cycle life reliability.
- GEM has approximately three times higher Dynamic Charge Acceptance (DCA).
- GEM enables next-generation start-stop systems that support fuel economy gains of up to 5%.
- GEM's fast recharge response makes it well suited for advanced 12-V architectures in hybrid (HEV, PHEV) and electric vehicle auxiliary systems.
- The technology platform is applicable across multiple energy storage applications, including automotive batteries, motive power for forklifts, and stationary backup power solutions.
- Hollingsworth & Vose developed GEM in partnership with ArcActive.
- Nicolas Clement, R&D Director, and Dr. Amogh Karwa, Director of Product management, stated that GEM has true potential for creating a more sustainable future and will be a great tool for battery manufacturers to enhance performance and enable lead batteries to compete better with alternative chemistries.
- East Penn Manufacturing and Silicon Joule received Honorable Mention recognition for their joint work on the first commercial bi-pole battery based on lead chemistry and treated silicon wafers.
- Battery Council International (BCI) annually presents this award to celebrate innovation in equipment, processes, services, and products that advance the lead battery industry.
- The award was established to honor industry thought leader Sally Breidegam Miksiewicz, the former CEO of East Penn Manufacturing Co.
- BCI is the leading trade association representing the global battery industry and was founded in 1924.
- BCI advocates and educates on behalf of battery manufacturers and recyclers, marketers and retailers, suppliers of raw materials and equipment, and battery distributors.
- Hollingsworth & Vose is a global manufacturer of advanced materials used in filtration, battery, and industrial applications.
- Hollingsworth & Vose is family-owned for seven generations and is headquartered in East Walpole, Massachusetts, U.S.A.
