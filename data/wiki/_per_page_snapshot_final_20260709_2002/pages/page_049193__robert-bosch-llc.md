# Robert Bosch LLC

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-08

**Source:** [Robert Bosch LLC to invest $200 million in innovative green energy project | The Journal Online](https://thejournalonline.com/2022/12/08/robert-bosch-llc-to-invest-200-million-in-innovative-green-energy-project/)  ·  `page_049193`

## Facts

- Anderson County Council approved a fee in lieu of tax agreement with Robert Bosch LLC for "Project Triangle" resulting in a $200 million capital investment.
- The project will create 350 new jobs with an average hourly wage of $30 plus benefits.
- Annual payroll resulting from the project will be $21 million.
- Forty-year community impact is estimated at $1,532,385.
- According to Economic Development Director Burris Nelson, the project will bring innovative technology that will not only revolutionize green energy but will provide a new measuing toll for how the world will consider energy consumption.
- The technology is for hydrogen fuel cells for Class A trucks.
