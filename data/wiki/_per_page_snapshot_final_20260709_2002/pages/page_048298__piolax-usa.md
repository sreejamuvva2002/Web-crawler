# Piolax USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-09-11

**Source:** [Japanese Auto Supplier to Add 30 Jobs in Canton - Global Atlanta](https://www.globalatlanta.com/japanese-auto-supplier-to-add-30-jobs-in-canton/)  ·  `page_048298`

## Facts

- Piolax USA is a Japanese-owned supplier of plastic and metal parts with operations in Canton for nearly 30 years.
- Piolax USA is set to hire 30 workers as part of an 82,000-square-foot warehouse expansion planned for next year.
- The new hires will augment the existing 255-person workforce at the manufacturing facility.
- The manufacturing facility is located in the Canton-Cherokee Business & Industrial Park.
- Steve Holcomb, Cherokee Office of Economic Development vice chairman, stated they are extremely proud to have this well-established company in their county.
- Piolax operates facilities globally in Mexico, China, Korea, Thailand, and many other countries.
