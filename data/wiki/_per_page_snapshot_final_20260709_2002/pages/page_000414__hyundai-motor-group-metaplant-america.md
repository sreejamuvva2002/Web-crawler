## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America produced its first IONIQ 5 electric vehicle.
- The facility is an electric vehicle assembly and battery plant located in Ellabell, Georgia.
- Hyundai Motor Co. is investing $5.5 billion to build the facility near the port city of Savannah.
- Production at the Georgia plant started on Oct 4, 2024.
- The plant's electric vehicles will be available at dealerships by the end of the year (2024).
- A Hyundai supplier plans an $11 million expansion in Georgia.
- Hyundai is eyeing hybrid vehicle production in Georgia as EV demand wanes.
