# Blue Ridge Manufacturing

## Facts

- Blue Ridge Manufacturing’s success story began in 1979 when 19-year-old Charles Rayside opened a one-man business in a rented warehouse building.
- The initial operation was a small trailer business consisting of a few trailers, 4x4 truck accessories, and youthful optimism.
- In 1984, the business expanded to a West Palm Beach, FL location on West Palm Beach’s main commercial thoroughfare for growing trailer sales and commercial truck equipment.
- Five years later in 1989, the business moved into the truck body and equipment market.
- From a one-man business in 1979, Rayside Truck and Trailer grew to 70 experienced employees, 7 acres of fenced property, a 40,000 square foot showroom/plant, and two locations.
- In 2006, the company opened a new manufacturing plant in Blue Ridge, GA that is now known as Blue Ridge Manufacturing.
- Charles Rayside aimed to manufacture better truck bodies based on customer input, 20 years of field testing, modern manufacturing processes, and quality workmanship.
- Blue Ridge Manufacturing offers a full line of flatbed, stake, trash, dump, landscape & western truck bodies.
- Charles Rayside acknowledged that with a strong Southern market, the business has grown beyond expectations and will continue providing for this expanding market with the same quality products and customer service.
