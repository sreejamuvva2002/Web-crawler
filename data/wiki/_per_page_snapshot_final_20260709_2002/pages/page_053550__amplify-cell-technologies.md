# Amplify Cell Technologies

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-05-19

**Source:** [Tough year for Daimler Truck amid torrid market conditions](https://truckandbusbuilder.com/article/2025/05/19/tough-year-for-daimler-truck-amid-torrid-market-conditions)  ·  `page_053550`

## Facts

- Amplify Cell Technologies is a joint venture between Paccar, Accelera by Cummins, Daimler Truck, and EVE Energy.
- The joint venture has begun construction on a USD 2–3 billion battery cell manufacturing facility in Marshall County, Mississippi.
- The plant covers two million square feet of space.
- The facility is located near Byhalia and will span 500 acres.
- Construction is scheduled to open in 2027.
- The plant will produce up to 21 gigawatt hours (GWh) annually of lithium-iron-phosphate (LFP) battery cells.
- The battery cells are primarily intended for commercial vehicle applications.
- The facility will utilize technology from Chinese partner EVE Energy.
- The project aims to localize and strengthen the U.S. battery supply chain.
- The initiative supports the transition to zero-emission commercial transportation.
- The facility is expected to boost regional economic growth in Mississippi.
