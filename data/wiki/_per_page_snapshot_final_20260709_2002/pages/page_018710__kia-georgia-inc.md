## Facts

- Beginning in 2006, Kia made one of the biggest single foreign investments in the history of Georgia by establishing a state-of-the-art production facility in West Point.
- Kia and its suppliers have helped create more than 14,000 jobs since the facility's establishment.
- The facility has produced more than 4.6 million vehicles to date.
- Kia Georgia has an annual production capacity of more than 350,000 vehicles.
- The facility manufactures the Telluride SUV, Sorento SUV, Sportage SUV, and the all-electric EV9 and EV6 SUVs.
- The advanced manufacturing facility represents a $3.2 billion investment.
- The facility is located on a 2,200-acre site.
- The facility operates 24 hours a day.
- Kia Georgia’s goal is to be the best and most innovative automotive company in the world.
- Kia Georgia is driven by the belief that customer satisfaction is rooted in quality.
- Stuart Countess serves as the President and CEO of Kia Georgia, Inc.
- President and CEO Stuart Countess stated: "We are extremely proud to call Georgia home, a place where Southern hospitality is alive and well."
- Stuart Countess noted: "Our biggest responsibility and greatest honor is to continue to develop Kia Georgia’s production operations, maintain a focus on our people and to provide a clear vision for the future."
- Kia Georgia team members strive to achieve ambitious production goals.
