# Woory Industrial Co.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [New EV Supplier Coming To Georgia | AM 1180 Radio](https://chattooga1180.com/new-ev-supplier-coming-to-georgia/)  ·  `page_015386`

## Facts

- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp announced that a South Korea-based auto parts maker will build its first U.S. manufacturing plant in Georgia.
- Woory Industrial Co. will invest an estimated $18 million in a facility in Dublin.
- The new facility will create more than 130 jobs.
- The company specializes in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning components for both electric and internal combustion vehicles.
- Governor Brian Kemp said, “We’re proud to welcome Woory Industrial as the latest job creator to help us on the path to becoming the e-mobility capital of the nation.”
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that the emerging industry continues to bring historic levels of new jobs and investment to communities all over the state, thanks to a highly skilled workforce and reliable infrastructure network.
- The company is also working to develop other new products for EVs as well as hydrogen-fueled vehicles.
- Woory will be hiring for positions from all fields necessary for an automotive parts plant.
- The new plant is expected to begin operations in November.
