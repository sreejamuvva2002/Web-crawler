## Facts

- Savannah Riverkeeper formed a Working Group with representatives from local governments, nonprofits, businesses, and legal groups.
- The group meets monthly to assess how harbor development projects impact the river, nearby communities, and the environment.
- New harbor development plans include deepening and widening the river.
- New harbor development plans include building a large shipping terminal.
- New harbor development plans include either replacing the Talmadge Bridge or constructing a tunnel.
- Unfinished promises from past expansions add to the complexity of ensuring community voices are heard regarding these projects.
- Ongoing industrial growth in the area includes the Hyundai Metaplant and supporting infrastructure.
- Since 2001, Savannah Riverkeeper has worked to protect the lower Savannah River Basin.
