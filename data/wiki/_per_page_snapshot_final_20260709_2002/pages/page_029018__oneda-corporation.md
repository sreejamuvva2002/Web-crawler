# Oneda Corporation

## Facts

- Oneda Corporation is a third-generation family-owned contract precision metal stamping manufacturer based in Columbus, Georgia.
- The company was founded in 1989.
- Oneda Corporation is a subsidiary of Japan-based Oneda Electric Corp.
- The Columbus location has 67 employees.
- The company primarily serves the automotive and heating and air conditioning industries.
- Leaders at Oneda Corporation recognized the need to increase the company’s online presence and reputation to continue expanding the business.
- The company worked with the Georgia Manufacturing Extension Partnership (GaMEP) at Georgia Tech, part of the MEP National Network™, on other projects.
- GaMEP now offers marketing services to its manufacturing clients.
- Michael Brooks serves as Oneda’s manager of marketing, customer service, and logistics.
- Katie Takacs is the group manager for marketing at GaMEP.
- Takacs worked with Oneda to develop and implement a strategic marketing plan, including goals, tactics to meet the goals, and a system to track progress on the plan.
- GaMEP added services including implementation, strategizing about email and social media, writing content for those platforms, and redesigning the website.
- The project entailed writing a tip sheet to generate leads, writing customer success stories, and training Brooks on the development of content so that he could manage it.
- Michael Brooks stated, 'If you're not on social media, and you're not keeping up with that, you will lose your audience,' noting that younger audiences use websites and social postings to evaluate business relationships before contacting them.
- Following the launch of the website and revamped LinkedIn page, GaMEP identified 100 companies and purchasing managers in eight states as potential new customers for the company.
- GaMEP created a three-step lead generation campaign to reach those potential customers.
- Oneda engaged the GaMEP team on an energy assessment analysis that led to a decrease in energy consumption by the company.
- The website was so well received internally that parent company Oneda Electric Corp. is now working to upgrade its own site.
