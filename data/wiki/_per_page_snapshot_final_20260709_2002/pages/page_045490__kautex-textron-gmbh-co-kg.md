# Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Textron Inc.  - Kautex Secures New Business Award for Pentatonic Battery Enclosure Solution](https://investor.textron.com/news-releases/news-details/2026/Kautex-Secures-New-Business-Award-for-Pentatonic-Battery-Enclosure-Solution/default.aspx)  ·  `page_045490`

## Facts

- Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG has secured a new business award for its Pentatonic battery enclosure solution.
- The program is designated for a leading automotive OEM.
- The award covers the full battery enclosure system, including the top cover and bottom tray with an integrated thermal management solution.
- The system is engineered for a multi-vehicle platform.
- The design meets performance and integration requirements for next-generation hybrid-electric vehicles.
- Production will be localized in North America.
- Start of production is planned for 2028.
- The enclosure will be manufactured using an advanced and highly automated production concept.
- Joerg Rautenstrauch, President & CEO of Kautex, stated that the award highlights their ability to deliver fully integrated battery enclosure systems at scale.
- Rautenstrauch noted that through advanced automation and production, the company is driving efficiency, quality, and innovation to support customer electrification strategies.
- Kautex operates as a Tier One automotive supplier with 30 plants in 13 countries.
- Kautex designs and manufactures traditional and hybrid fuel systems, engine camshafts, and plastic industrial packaging solutions.
- Kautex is expanding its portfolio to offer thermoplastic composite and composite-metal hybrid battery systems.
- Kautex also develops assisted and autonomous drive vehicle cleaning systems.
- Textron Inc. owns Kautex as a subsidiary company.
- Textron Inc. leverages a global network of aircraft, defense, industrial, and finance businesses to provide innovative solutions and services.
