# LS Cable & System Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 2011-04-15

**Source:** [LS Cable to spend $96 mil. on power cable plants in US, India - The Korea Times](https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/business/companies/20110415/ls-cable-to-spend-96-mil-on-power-cable-plants-in-us-india)  ·  `page_010523`

## Facts

- LS Cable & System Ltd., South Korea's top wire and cable maker, will spend $96 million to build and expand power cable production facilities in the United States and India.
- The move comes as LS Cable tries to expand further in overseas markets by manufacturing products locally through its overseas subsidiaries.
- LS Cable's U.S. subsidiary Superior Essex Inc. will break ground for a $60 million plant in Tarboro, North Carolina, at the end of next month.
- The new plant will start production of low- and medium-voltage cable products starting in 2012.
- LS Cable will also spend $36 million to add a power cable production line to an existing communications cable plant located in Haryana, India.
- The Indian facility will be completed by the end of this year and start production in 2012.
- LS Cable aims to reap $136 million from power cable sales in 2015.
- Son Jong-ho, chief executive officer of LS Cable & System, stated that with power business localization, the company will lay the foundation for entry into both advanced and emerging power markets.
