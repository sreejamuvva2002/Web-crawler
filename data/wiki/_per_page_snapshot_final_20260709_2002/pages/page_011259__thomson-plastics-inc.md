## Facts

- Thomson Plastics was founded in 1975.
- Thomson Plastics provides large tonnage injection molding within the plastics manufacturing industry.
- The company delivers plastic injection molding services, assembly, and project management.
- Thomson Plastics serves markets including sports and recreation, lawn and garden, industrial, and automotive.
- Thomson Plastics is based in Thomson, Georgia.
- In December 2025, Thomson Plastics was acquired by Amara Industries.
- Thomson Plastics's headquarters is located at 130 Quality Drive North West, Thomson, Georgia, 30824, United States.
- The company's phone number is 706-595-0658.
- Thomson Plastics currently operates from two locations in Georgia and North Carolina.
- Amara Industries is the parent company to the Gracious Living group of companies.
- Toronto-based Amara Industries is buying Thomson Plastics Inc. (TPI), a large tonnage injection molder in the Southeastern U.S.
- The financial terms of the acquisition deal have not been disclosed.
- On Dec. 9, Amara Industries officials stated the company is combining TPI’s two plants with its three Gracious Living molding and extrusion plants in Canada and the U.S.
- Thomson Plastics has clamping force capacities up to 3,700 tons.
- Thirty percent of Thomson Plastics's fleet exceeds 1,000 tons in clamping force capacity.
- Thomson Plastics's technical portfolio includes injection molding, gas-assisted molding, over-molding, insert molding, in-mold labeling, robotic part picking, pad printing, and heat transfer decorating.
- Vince Orlando is the co-president at Amara Industries.
