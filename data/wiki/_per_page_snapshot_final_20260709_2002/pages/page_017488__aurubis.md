# Aurubis

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-21

**Source:** [Copper smelter Aurubis plans 530 million euros in new investments, ETAuto](https://auto.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/auto-components/copper-smelter-aurubis-plans-530-million-euros-in-new-investments/96393927)  ·  `page_017488`

## Facts

- Aurubis scrapped its existing dividend policy.
- The company announced 530 million euros ($562.44 million) in growth-oriented investments.
- The new investments will expand plants in the U.S. state of Georgia and in Hamburg, Germany.
- For fiscal year 2021/22, Aurubis reported operating earnings before tax (EBT) of 532 million euros.
- This EBT figure is up from an adjusted 381 million euros in the previous year.
- In November, CEO Roland Harings stated that strong growth in copper demand from the renewables and electric vehicle sectors compensated for a decline in demand from the construction sector.
- Aurubis trimmed its operating EBT forecast corridor for fiscal year 2022/23 to 400 to 500 million euros.
- The previous operating EBT forecast corridor was 500 to 600 million euros.
- The company will pay dividends for fiscal year 2021/22 at a rate of 1.80 euros per share.
