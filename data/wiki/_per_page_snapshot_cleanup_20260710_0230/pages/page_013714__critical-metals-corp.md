## Facts

- Critical Metals Corp. (Nasdaq: CRML) announced the commencement of its planned 10,000-meter diamond drilling campaign at its wholly owned Tanbreez Rare Earth Project in Southern Greenland.
- The drill program is designed to support resource expansion, geological modeling, mine planning, and future development activities across the project area.
- Over the past several weeks, the Company completed construction and commissioning of initial project infrastructure, including accommodation and operational support facilities.
- The completed infrastructure supports both the 2026 drilling program and the continued advancement of the Tanbreez Project toward development.
- Drill contractors and support teams successfully mobilized to site, with drill rigs arriving and positioned across priority target areas.
- Following equipment inspections, safety reviews, and operational readiness assessments, the Company’s 2026 diamond drilling program is now underway.
- Tony Sage serves as Chairman of CRML.
- Tony Sage stated that the start of the 10,000-meter drilling campaign marks another important step in advancing the world-class Tanbreez rare earth Project.
- Drilling is currently underway across priority target areas following a successful mobilization effort.
- Information generated from this program is expected to support resource expansion, geological modelling, mine planning and other activities aimed at advancing Tanbreez toward production.
- Over the coming two to three weeks, field teams will focus on final ground verification activities and completion of blast mark-outs associated with planned site access and development works.
- These upcoming activities are designed to validate drill collar locations, optimize access corridors and support the safe and efficient execution of the 2026 drilling program.
- The Company is advancing geological mapping and surface verification activities.
- The Company is finalizing drill pads, access routes and supporting site infrastructure.
- The Company is completing detailed collar surveys and positioning.
- The Company is conducting ongoing environmental, health and safety monitoring programs.
- The Company is expanding geological and technical data collection to support future resource updates, mine planning and project development activities.
- Initial drilling will focus on priority mineralized zones identified through historical exploration, recent technical reviews, and geological modeling.
- Results of the program are expected to support resource expansion, mine planning, infrastructure design and other workstreams associated with advancing Tanbreez toward production.
- Tanbreez is one of the world’s largest undeveloped rare earth projects.
- The project contains significant concentrations of heavy rare earth elements that are critical to permanent magnets, electric vehicles, defense systems and advanced manufacturing technologies.
- The 2026 drilling campaign is expected to generate important geological and engineering data that will support future resource updates, mine planning, feasibility studies and broader project development activities.
- The program will further enhance the Company’s understanding of the mineralization and geological characteristics across the project area.
- The Company expects to provide additional operational updates as drilling progresses and assay results become available.
- Critical Metals Corp is a leading mining development company focused on critical metals and minerals, producing strategic products essential to electrification and next generation technologies for Europe and its western world partners.
- Its flagship Project, Tanbreez, is one of the world’s largest rare earth deposits located in Southern Greenland.
- The Tanbreez deposit is expected to have access to key transportation outlets via year-round direct shipping access through deep water fjords leading directly to the North Atlantic Ocean.
- Another key asset for the Company is the Wolfsberg Lithium Project located in Carinthia, 270 km south of Vienna, Austria.
- The Wolfsberg Lithium Project is the first fully permitted mine in Europe.
- The Wolfsberg project is strategically located with access to established road and rail infrastructure.
- The Wolfsberg project is expected to be the next major producer of key lithium products to support the European market.
- Wolfsberg is well positioned with offtake and downstream partners to become a unique and valuable asset in an expanding geostrategic critical metals portfolio.
- With its strategic asset portfolio, Critical Metals Corp is positioned to become a reliable and sustainable supplier of critical minerals essential for defense applications, the clean energy transition, and next-generation technologies in the western world.
