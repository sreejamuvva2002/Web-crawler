# Aurubis AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2021-11-10

**Source:** [Aurubis to Open State-of-the-Art Recycling and Copper Smelting Facility in Augusta - Expansion Solutions](https://www.expansionsolutionsmagazine.com/aurubis-open-smelting-facility/)  ·  `page_017325`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced the opening of a new smelting and recycling facility in Augusta, Georgia.
- The facility will be the very first of its kind in the United States.
- The project will create 125 jobs in the Augusta area.
- Governor Kemp stated it marks the single-largest investment made by a German company in the State of Georgia.
- Aurubis plans to produce materials found in lithium-ion batteries and other electronics from the Augusta facility.
- CEO Roland Harings stated the investment sets an example of sustainable growth and positions Aurubis as a forerunner for multi-metal recycling in the U.S.
- The facility will be built on 150 acres at Augusta Corporate Park.
- Augusta Corporate Park is a “Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) Certified” site.
- Operations are expected to begin by the first half of 2024.
- The company plans to begin hiring for positions as soon as summer 2022.
- Steven Kendrick, Chairman of the Development Authority of Augusta, welcomed Aurubis as a leader in its industry building a world-class advanced manufacturing facility.
- Griff Lynch, Executive Director of the Georgia Ports Authority (GPA), noted that exports from the recycling operation will strengthen Georgia’s global position via Savannah port.
- Fernanda Kirchner, Senior Project Manager for GDEcD’s Global Commerce division, represented the department in partnership with the Augusta Economic Development Authority, Georgia Ports Authority, and Georgia Power.
- Sérgio Domingues, Managing Director for the State of Georgia’s Europe Office, played a supporting role in attracting Aurubis to Georgia.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson welcomed Aurubis as the first multi-metal recycling plant in the United States located in Richmond County.
- Augusta Corporate Park has completed due diligence and specific certification requirements prior to prospective business visits under the GRAD program.
- Thousands of Georgia jobs have been created on GRAD sites since the program’s inception in 2008.
- Aurubis AG is a leading global provider of non-ferrous metals and one of the largest copper recyclers worldwide.
- The company processes complex metal concentrates, scrap metals, organic and inorganic metal-bearing recycling materials, and industrial residues into high-quality metals.
- Aurubis produces more than 1 million tons of copper cathodes annually.
- From copper cathodes, Aurubis manufactures wire rod, continuous cast shapes, profiles, and flat rolled products made of copper and copper alloys.
- Aurubis also produces precious metals, selenium, lead, nickel, tin, zinc, sulfuric acid, and iron silicate.
