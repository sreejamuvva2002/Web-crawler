## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama (HMMA) celebrated the grand opening of its new Care Center in the winter quarter.
- The $14 million facility is designed to enhance the health, wealth and well-being of its 4,000 team members.
- The Care Center is located at a new central site on the southwest corner of the general assembly building on the HMMA campus.
- Chris Susock, HMMA’s President and CEO, stated the facility represents a commitment to its Alabama workforce and serves as a resource for wellness, fitness, physical therapy, fire prevention and emergency response.
