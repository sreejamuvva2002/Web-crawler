# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-09

**Source:** [Porsche reports Q3 2025 U.S. retail sales](https://www.sahmcapital.com/news/content/porsche-reports-q3-2025-us-retail-sales-2025-10-09)  ·  `page_048710`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) announced third-quarter retail deliveries totaled 18,403 cars in the U.S.
- PCNA's overall sales for the first nine months of 2025 reached 57,099 cars, up 5.6 percent year-to-date over 2024.
- The 718, Macan, and Panamera models posted significant gains year-to-date.
- Third-quarter sales of the Taycan and Macan Electric performed well.
- Porsche Approved Certified Pre-Owned (CPO) sales in the U.S. for Q3 reached a new PCNA quarterly record of 12,232 units.
- Year-to-date CPO sales stood at 35,439 units, representing a 13 percent increase compared to the same point in 2024.
- Timo Resch serves as President and CEO of PCNA.
- Timo Resch stated that Porsche remains strong in the United States due to customer support and loyalty during unprecedented change.
- Timo Resch emphasized that choice is vital for customers and highlighted the upcoming new Cayenne Electric joining the Cayenne gasoline and plug-in hybrid models by year's end.
- Compared to Q3 2024, PCNA sales decreased by 4.9 percent, driven in part by restricted supply.
- The Macan was the top-selling model in Q3 with 6,599 deliveries.
- The Cayenne was the second top seller in Q3 with 4,294 deliveries.
- The 911 was the third top seller in Q3 with 3,448 deliveries.
- PCNA follows the U.S. Auto Industry Sales Release Schedule issued by Motor Intelligence.
- Third quarter new car sales for PCNA began on July 1, 2025, and ended on September 30, 2025.
- ALL 911 model recorded 3,448 July-September 2025 sales and 8,872 year-to-date sales.
- ALL 718 model recorded 1,719 July-September 2025 sales and 5,398 year-to-date sales.
- ALL TAYCAN model recorded 1,553 July-September 2025 sales and 3,636 year-to-date sales.
- ALL PANAMERA model recorded 790 July-September 2025 sales and 3,410 year-to-date sales.
- ALL CAYENNE model recorded 4,294 July-September 2025 sales and 14,621 year-to-date sales.
- ALL MACAN model recorded 6,599 July-September 2025 sales and 21,162 year-to-date sales.
- Total PCNA sales were 18,403 for July-September 2025 and 57,099 year-to-date.
