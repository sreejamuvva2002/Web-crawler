# Vernay Laboratories

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-22

## Facts

- Vernay Laboratories announced the grand re-opening of its Milledgeville manufacturing plant on April 22, 2025.
- The re-opening event will feature a ribbon cutting ceremony, guided plant tours, and remarks from Vernay leadership, the Baldwin County Chamber of Commerce, and invited guests from Milledgeville College, Baldwin High School, and local government.
- Doug Cumpston serves as the Plant Manager for the Milledgeville facility.
- The updated facility represents Vernay’s commitment to operational excellence, local job growth, and long-term partnerships.
- The Milledgeville plant will play an increasingly important role in Vernay’s global supply network.
- The Milledgeville plant supports key customers in the Consumer Products and Mobility sectors.
- Vanna Villata serves as Vice President of Operations at Vernay.
- Vernay operates manufacturing facilities in Georgia across Milledgeville, Griffin, and Kennesaw.
- Georgia provides Vernay with access to top-tier talent, strong community partnerships, and a robust industrial ecosystem.
- Maurizio Trabucco serves as Director of Human Resources for North America at Vernay.
- Vernay is implementing a broader initiative to enhance employee retention and talent attraction across its North American operations.
- Vernay Laboratories was founded in 1946 and operates as a privately held firm.
- The company designs, develops, and manufactures customized flow-control components and formulates its own compounds for specific applications.
- Vernay’s products are utilized in millions of medical devices, automobiles, and consumer products.
- The company produces more than 1 billion components per year.
- Vernay maintains a library of more than 33,000 elastomeric materials and compound formulations.
- The firm operates six manufacturing plants globally located in the U.S., the Netherlands, Italy, and China.
