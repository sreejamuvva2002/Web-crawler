# Lear Corporation

**Source:** [Lear Corp. sets up new manufacturing plant in Chihuahua - MEXICONOW](https://mexico-now.com/lear-corp-sets-up-new-manufacturing-plant-in-chihuahua/)  ·  `page_057007`

## Facts

- Lear Corporation plans to set up a new manufacturing plant in the municipality of Meoqui, Chihuahua.
- The plan was reported by Autonews México citing a state Government official.
- Fabián Santana, head of Chihuahua state’s Investment Promotion Department, confirmed the details.
- The company plans to invest US$ 13 million in this operation.
- The operation will open 2,000 job positions.
- The facility will produce textile materials for automotive seating.
- Lear has just bought the building and is working at a fast pace to get the site operational by May.
- The region where the new plant will be installed stands out for its cotton production.
- The region is home to several companies that produce textile materials for apparel companies.
- It is unknown whether the local cotton production influenced Lear's decision to open a plant in that location.
- The new facility will be Lear’s 16th plant in Chihuahua state.
- Lear currently operates ten manufacturing sites in Ciudad Juárez, two in Chihuahua state capital, and one each in Nuevo Casas Grandes, Ascension, and Villa Ahumada.
- The new site will also be Lear’s 47th plant in Mexico.
- During the last four years, Lear has opened 12 plants in Mexico.
- The auto supplier estimates its Mexican workforce totaled 56,000 employees by the end of 2017.
