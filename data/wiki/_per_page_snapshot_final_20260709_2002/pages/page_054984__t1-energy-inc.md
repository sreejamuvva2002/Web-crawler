## Facts

- FREYR Battery, Inc. is a Luxembourg-headquartered energy solutions company focused on developing and manufacturing clean, next-generation battery cell production capacity.
- The company's mission is to accelerate the decarbonization of global energy and transportation systems.
- It historically served key markets including energy storage systems (ESS), commercial mobility, and the passenger electric vehicle (EV) segment.
- The company operated a Customer Qualification Plant (CQP) in Mo i Rana, Norway.
- FREYR advanced its Giga America battery manufacturing project in Coweta County, Georgia.
- In December 2024, FREYR completed an acquisition of Trina Solar's U.S. manufacturing assets.
- Following the acquisition, the company repositioned itself as a vertically integrated solar and battery storage supplier with an integrated U.S. supply chain.
- Effective February 19, 2025, the company rebranded as T1 Energy Inc.
- The company's common stock and warrants transitioned to ticker symbols "TE" and "TE WS" on the New York Stock Exchange as of March 3, 2025.
- The FREY-WT ticker is no longer active.
- At the time of reporting, the company had a market cap of $241.82M and reported 0 fulltime employees.
