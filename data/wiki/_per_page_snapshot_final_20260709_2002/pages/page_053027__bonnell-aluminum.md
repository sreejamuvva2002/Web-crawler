# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Bonnell Aluminum expands its TSLOTS operation in Newnan, Georgia; Aluminium Extrusion, Profiles, Price, Scrap, Recycling, Section](https://www.alcircle.com/news/bonnell-aluminum-expands-its-tslots-operation-in-newnan-georgia-92499)  ·  `page_053027`

## Facts

- On Tuesday, April 4, Bonnell Aluminum announced the operation expansion of its TSLOTS line of aluminium structural framing system into the Southeastern region of the United States.
- Bonnell is adding a new manufacturing and distribution site at Newnan, Georgia, to its existing facilities in Clearfield, Utah and Niles, Michigan.
- The expansion will make the TSLOTS product line more easily accessible to customers in the Southeastern United States.
- The TSLOTS aluminium framing system enables the creation of light, durable, versatile, and functional structures using T-slotted aluminium extrusions and connection hardware.
- Randy Johnson, Senior Brand Manager for TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum, stated that the company has been successful in growing its TSLOTS product line by positioning manufacturing and distribution sites closer to where customers operate.
- This expansion marks Bonnell's second major investment in TSLOTS in two years, following a successful expansion into the Midwest in 2021.
- The capacity addition will increase the company's footprint across the U.S. and provide customers with reduced shipping costs and shorter lead times.
- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation.
- The extrusions produced by Bonnell serve the building and construction industry, automotive and transportation, and machinery and equipment sectors.
- Besides TSLOTS, Futura Transitions is another framing system product line offered by Bonnell Aluminum.
