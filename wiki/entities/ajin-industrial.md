# Ajin Industrial

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** Eastern Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Ajin Industrial, a South Korean auto parts manufacturer, plans to develop a $317 million plant in eastern Georgia to supply the nearby Hyundai electric vehicle factory.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Supplier of automotive components for Hyundai's electric-vehicle manufacturing facility in Georgia.
