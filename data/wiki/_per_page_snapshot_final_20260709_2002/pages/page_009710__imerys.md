# Imerys

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-08-11

**Source:** [Imerys Invests in Marble Hill Georgia Plant Capacity Expansion - AllOnGeorgia](https://allongeorgia.com/georgia-business/imerys-invests-in-marble-hill-georgia-plant-capacity-expansion/)  ·  `page_009710`

## Facts

- Imerys announced a strategic multi-million investment in its Southeast Ground-Calcium-Carbonate business to expand capacity at its Marble Hill, Georgia plant.
- The expansion is part of a plan to support the growing building and construction markets such as coatings, roofing, joint compound and flooring.
- The investment is designed to increase manufacturing capacity, which includes the installation of new equipment and upgrades in plant automation systems, to support debottlenecking of critical areas.
- This investment complements the current expansion underway at Imerys’ Sylacauga, AL Calcium Carbonate facility.
- Jim Murberger, Senior Vice President, Imerys Performance Minerals Americas, stated that the significant investment in North Georgia strengthens commitment to growing customers and creates new jobs in the surrounding community.
- The infrastructure investment in Marble Hill will support customers’ ability to produce more sustainable products through lowering the carbon footprint of their products.
- Marble Hill produces specialty minerals that are used in a wide variety of markets including building and construction, coatings, and flooring.
- Calcium carbonate is one of the main products that goes into LVT flooring.
- Imerys reported €4.4 billion revenue and 17,000 employees in 2021 as the world leader in mineral-based specialty solutions for industry.
