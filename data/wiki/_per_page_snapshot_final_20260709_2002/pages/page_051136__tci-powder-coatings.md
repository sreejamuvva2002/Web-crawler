# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- TCI has been manufacturing powder coatings for more than 35 years with a strong commitment to people, products, and service.
- TCI was founded in Ellaville, GA, starting with a modest plant and a portable building that served as the lab and office space.
- TCI has expanded to become an international company with multiple warehouse locations.
- TCI operates a state-of-the-art product development center.
- TCI employs a staff of several hundred employees focused on servicing customers.
- TCI's highly trained technical service and sales teams extend across North America and provide expert advice and assistance to customers of all sizes, from large multinational corporations to independent custom coaters.
- TCI maintains an expansive network of distribution centers, consisting of either certified or approved external providers, allowing it to service customers throughout the United States, Canada, Mexico, Europe, and Asia.
- TCI ensures stock powders and products are readily available at a moment's notice.
- TCI focuses on being environmentally conscious and providing reliable service response at all levels under the motto 'Where Your World Matters'.
