# Decostar Industries Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-06-13

**Source:** [EPA: Georgia company to address concerns about hazardous air | AP News](https://apnews.com/article/7cec789ee92643b79af778dac9400c53)  ·  `page_053685`

## Facts

- Federal authorities say they’ve reached a settlement with a Georgia manufacturing plant which had been cited for failing to take steps to prevent hazardous air emissions.
- The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency on Wednesday announced that Decostar Industries Inc. of Carrollton will take steps to control hazardous air emissions to protect workers, residents and improve overall air quality.
- Decostar will implement a leak detection and repair program.
- Decostar will perform a supplemental environmental project valued at more than $2.7 million.
- Decostar will pay a $377,900 civil penalty.
- Decostar manufactures parts for automobile manufacturers including Nissan, BMW and Hyundai.
