## Facts

- F&P Georgia is a tier one automotive parts supplier located in Rome, Georgia, USA.
- Its parent company is Ftech Inc., with its head office located in Saitama prefecture in Japan.
- In North America, it operates as one of five subsidiaries.
- Two North American subsidiaries are located in Ontario, Canada, in Tottenham and Stratford.
- Two North American subsidiaries are located in the USA, in Troy, Ohio and Rome, Georgia.
- Two North American subsidiaries are located in Mexico, in Queretaro and Irapuato.
- Ftech currently has over 20 facilities throughout the globe.
- The company is a world class, tier one automotive supplier with a global presence in the suspension component market.
- Its goal is to produce high quality parts at competitive pricing and deliver them when and where customers require, without fail.
- Safety and Environment are its number one priorities.
- It relies on associate creativity, expertise, and teamwork to meet and exceed goals.
- Customer satisfaction is supported through continuous improvement, perpetual training of associates, a never ending pursuit of new technologies, and a daily challenge to existing processes.
