## Facts

- At a press conference at the Georgia State Capitol, Mercedes-Benz USA (MBUSA) announced the permanent and interim locations for its new corporate headquarters in Atlanta.
- Company President and CEO Stephen Cannon made the announcement alongside Georgia Governor Nathan Deal.
- MBUSA is currently headquartered in Montvale, New Jersey.
- The company will construct a new headquarters facility on a greenfield site at the corner of Abernathy and Barfield roads in Sandy Springs.
- The new headquarters facility is expected to be completed in early 2018.
- The approximately 12-acre parcel for the new site is located near the intersection of Georgia 400 and Interstate 285.
- The site rests in one of Atlanta's largest commercial districts with nearby hotel, retail, and restaurant options.
- The location offers easy access to public transit and Hartsfield–Jackson Atlanta International Airport.
- CEO Stephen Cannon stated that Atlanta will serve as the perfect foundation to foster the company's ambition to be among the best companies in the world.
- MBUSA will move into temporary office space beginning July 2015 at Sterling Pointe II.
- The interim facility is a 186,000-square-foot, Class A office building at the Perimeter Center complex in Dunwoody.
- Georgia Governor Nathan Deal praised the planned headquarters location in Sandy Springs as great for the city, metro Atlanta, and the entire state of Georgia.
- As part of the relocation, MBUSA plans to hire hundreds of new employees from the Atlanta area.
- MBUSA will continue to maintain several operational business units in Montvale and Robbinsville, New Jersey.
- Additional details on the new Sandy Springs facility and Atlanta recruiting efforts were scheduled to be released in the coming weeks following the February 3, 2015 announcement.
- MBUSA is responsible for the distribution, marketing, and customer service for all Mercedes-Benz products in the United States.
- MBUSA offers a lineup of 14 model lines ranging from the CLA-Class four-door coupe to the flagship S-Class.
- MBUSA is also responsible for the distribution, marketing, and customer service of Mercedes-Benz Sprinter Vans and smart products in the U.S.
