# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-02

**Source:** [Archer](https://news.archer.com/en-us/stellantis-invests-additional-55-million-into-archer-following-recent-flight-test-milestone)  ·  `page_016803`

## Facts

- Stellantis invested an additional $55 million into Archer following the achievement of its transition flight test milestone last month.
- This latest investment builds on Stellantis completing a series of open market purchases of 8.3 million shares of Archer’s stock in March of this year.
- During 2023, Stellantis invested $110 million into Archer through a combination of open market stock purchases and investments under the companies’ strategic funding agreement.
- Construction nears completion on Archer’s high-volume manufacturing facility in Covington, GA.
- The facility is on track to be completed later this year.
- The first phase of the build out is a ~350,000 square foot facility on an ~100 acre site.
- The facility is designed to support production of up to 650 aircraft annually.
- This capacity would make it one of the largest manufacturing facilities by volume in the aircraft industry.
- Archer’s goal with this facility is to establish a factory that can support its planned commercial ramp by leveraging Stellantis as its contract manufacturer.
- Stellantis CEO Carlos Tavares applauded the innovation, expertise and hard work of the engineering and manufacturing teams from Stellantis and Archer.
- Archer CEO Adam Goldstein stated that Stellantis’ commitment has been unrivaled, citing its foresight to provide manufacturing expertise and capital needed to accelerate Archer’s business objectives.
- Stellantis has been a strategic partner to Archer since 2020 through various collaboration initiatives.
- Stellantis has been an investor in Archer since 2021.
- Archer has leveraged Stellantis’ deep manufacturing, supply chain, and design expertise in connection with designing, developing, and commercializing its eVTOL aircraft.
- Archer’s goal is to transform urban travel, replacing 60–90-minute commutes by car with estimated 10–20-minute electric air taxi flights that are safe, sustainable, low noise, and cost-competitive with ground transportation.
- Archer’s Midnight is a piloted, four-passenger aircraft designed to perform rapid back-to-back flights with minimal charge time between flights.
- Stellantis is executing its Dare Forward 2030 strategic plan aiming to become a carbon net zero mobility tech company by 2038.
