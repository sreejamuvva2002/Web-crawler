# Hitachi Astemo Americas Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-02-07

**Source:** [Hitachi Astemo Americas Names Kading to Aftermarket Role](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/hitachi-astemo-americas-alan-kading-aftermarket-role/)  ·  `page_008467`

## Facts

- Alan Kading has been named industrial and powersports aftermarket sales manager for Hitachi Astemo Americas Inc.
- Kading has more than 25 years of automotive aftermarket experience and will be responsible for managing the expansion of Hitachi Astemo’s powersports product lines in the Americas.
- He previously held sales management positions at Horizon Global and Nextel Partners.
- Earlier in his career, he worked at General Motors as an OnStar product representative and as a GM accessories-channel sales representative.
- Kading stated he is excited about the opportunity to lead Hitachi Astemo’s powersports and industrial products team in the Americas.
- Hitachi Astemo will bring added value and innovation to current and future customers alike as it expands its catalog of racing, performance and maintenance parts.
- Kading holds a bachelor’s degree in business administration from the University of Wisconsin-Stout in Menomonie, Wisconsin.
- He resides in the Menomonie-Eau Claire area.
