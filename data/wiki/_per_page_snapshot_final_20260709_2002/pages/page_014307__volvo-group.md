# Volvo Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-07

**Source:** [Trump tariffs trigger more layoffs, Volvo Group to fire 800 workers over market uncertainty | Today News](https://www.livemint.com/news/us-news/trump-tariffs-trigger-more-layoffs-volvo-group-to-fire-800-workers-over-market-uncertainty-11745021577762.html)  ·  `page_014307`

## Facts

- Volvo Group plans to lay off as many as 800 workers at three US facilities over the next three months due to market uncertainty and demand concerns in the face of President Donald Trump's tariffs.
- Volvo Group North America told employees it plans to lay off 550-800 people across three US facilities: its Mack Trucks site in Macungie, Pennsylvania; two Volvo Group facilities in Dublin, Virginia; and a facility in Hagerstown, Maryland.
- The company, part of Sweden's AB Volvo, employs nearly 20,000 people in North America.
- President Donald Trump has upended the global trading system that has been in place for over 75 years with a plan for tariffs on products from across the world.
- Trump's vacillating trade policy has undermined consumer and business confidence and caused economists to raise their forecasts for a U.S. recession.
- Volvo Group's layoffs are the latest response from the car and truck industry that is reeling from the Republican president's tariffs on certain parts, which are expected to increase the cost of manufacturing vehicles.
- A spokesperson for Volvo Group North America stated that heavy-duty truck orders continue to be negatively affected by market uncertainty about freight rates and demand, possible regulatory changes, and the impact of tariffs.
- A spokesperson for Volvo Group North America stated, "We regret having to take this action, but we need to align production with reduced demand for our vehicles."
