## Facts

- Dongwon Autopart Technology plans to invest $30 million in a new production facility near Swainsboro, Emanuel County.
- The investment will bring 200 jobs to the area.
- Construction on the new manufacturing facility will take place at the Highway 297 Industrial Park near Swainsboro.
- The company will hire for management, system leads, and production personnel positions.
- Hiring is expected to begin later this year (2026).
- Governor Brian Kemp celebrated the announcement, saying Georgia’s thriving automotive industry is creating new opportunities for residents statewide, regardless of his zip code.
- Governor Kemp announced $4.3 million in grants from the third round of the Rural Site Development Initiative within the One Georgia Authority.
- Since the program launched in 2025, total investment in rural site development projects has reached $21 million.
