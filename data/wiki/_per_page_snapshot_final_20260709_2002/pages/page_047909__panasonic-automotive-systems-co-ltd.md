# Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2027-04-01

**Source:** [Introducing Mobitera: The Future of Mobility by Panasonic Automotive Systems (2026)](https://nephewapps.com/article/introducing-mobitera-the-future-of-mobility-by-panasonic-automotive-systems)  ·  `page_047909`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd. is a leading supplier of cutting-edge automotive and mobility technologies for global OEMs.
- The company announced a major rebrand effective April 1, 2027, officially becoming Mobitera Inc. with a new logo and corporate philosophy.
- CEO Masashi Nagayasu stated that 'Mobitera reflects our vision to power the future of mobility with technologies that elevate and enrich the human experience.'
- The company entered a partnership with the Apollo Group in 2024 aimed at accelerating growth through enhanced software development and optimizing its business portfolio.
- The name 'Mobitera' is a fusion of 'mobility,' the Latin word 'iter' (meaning path), and the Japanese word 'terasu' (to illuminate).
- The central 'i' in the Mobitera name and logo represents keeping individuals at the heart of everything the company does.
- The corporate color, Mobitera Blue-Green, was chosen to represent harmony between people, society, and the planet.
- The company's mission is to 'Create a sustainable mobility society to promote a better life for everyone.'
- The company's vision is to become 'The ‘Joy in Motion’ design company.'
- All domestic and international subsidiaries are rebranding to align with the Mobitera name effective April 1, 2027.
- Subsidiaries include entities such as Mobitera ITS Inc. and Mobitera Malaysia Sdn. Bhd.
- OpenSynergy GmbH is the only subsidiary that will retain its original name.
