# Michelin North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-10-18

**Source:** [Michelin North America Acquires Lehigh Technologies | Mergr](https://mergr.com/transaction/michelin-north-america-acquires-lehigh-technologies)  ·  `page_057044`

## Facts

- Michelin North America, Inc. acquired Lehigh Technologies in an add-on acquisition deal dated September 1, 2017.
- The seller for Lehigh Technologies was NGP Energy Technology Partners.
- Michelin North America is a designer, manufacturer and seller of tires for every type of vehicle, including airplanes, automobiles, bicycles, earthmovers, farm equipment, heavy-duty trucks and motorcycles.
- Michelin North America also publishes travel guides, hotel and restaurant guides, maps and road atlases.
- Lehigh Technologies was founded in 1950 and operates in the Automotive sector.
- NGP Energy Technology Partners is a private equity firm focused on the energy sector.
- NGP Energy Technology Partners targets buyout and growth capital transactions in sub-sectors spanning alternative energy, energy efficiency, oilfield technology and power.
- NGP Energy Technology Partners considers both minority and control investments and looks to invest $5 to $30 million per transaction.
- NGP Energy Technology Partners is a subsidiary of NGP Energy Capital Management.
- NGP Energy Technology Partners was formed in 2005 and is based in Washington DC with an additional office in New Orleans.
