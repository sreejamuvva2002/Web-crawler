# Aurubis AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2023-01-05

**Source:** [Aurubis Doubles U.S. Investment in Multimetal Recycling Facility - Expansion Solutions](https://www.expansionsolutionsmagazine.com/aurubis-doubles-u-s-investment-in-multimetal-recycling-facility/)  ·  `page_017284`

## Facts

- The Supervisory Board of Aurubis AG, a European-based global provider of non-ferrous metals and one of the largest copper recyclers worldwide, announced a significant and accelerated investment in its U.S. operations.
- The company plans to spend an additional €250 million to fast-track the expansion of its Richmond plant.
- The Richmond plant is currently under construction in Augusta, Georgia.
- This investment is projected to double throughput volume from the previously planned 90,000 tons of complex recycling materials to 180,000 tons per year.
- Once both phases are complete, Aurubis anticipates about 200 employees at the facility.
- Aurubis broke ground on its Richmond plant in June 2022.
- Taking into account a capital expenditure adjustment for infrastructure requirements and inflation of €90 million for the current construction project, Aurubis plans to invest a total of €640 million in the U.S. state of Georgia.
- Aurubis expects to generate operative earnings (EBITDA) of around €170 million starting in fiscal year 2026/27.
- CEO Roland Harings commented that the world needs metals such as copper, which can be recycled without loss of quality or performance, for cars, mobile phones, batteries, cables, and computers.
- CEO Roland Harings stated that today's high recycling demand cannot be met by existing facilities.
- CEO Roland Harings said the increased commitment to the U.S. will be a game-changer for the environment, for manufacturers, and for the larger transition to a renewable economy.
- Aurubis Richmond's first phase is expected to come online in mid-2024.
- The completed expansion of the Aurubis Richmond facility is scheduled for 2026.
- Aurubis received the necessary approvals for the operation of both facilities earlier in the year.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia welcomed Aurubis to the state last year and celebrated the start of its partnership with the Augusta community.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that the latest announcement is coming well ahead of schedule and thanked the company for bringing benefits to hardworking Georgians.
- Governor Brian Kemp said he looks forward to many successful years ahead as Aurubis plays a key role in meeting the needs of tomorrow's economy.
- Consistent U.S. market growth of complex recycling creates excellent conditions for Aurubis' rapid expansion by efficiently repurposing hundreds of thousands of tons of recyclable materials that otherwise would be shipped out of the U.S. or sent to local landfills.
- Following a meeting in Hamburg, Germany, CEO Harings said the company has doubled down on its commitments to the circular economy, the environment, and safeguarding the long-term health of investors by balancing profit with meaningful sustainability goals.
- Aurubis AG is a leading global provider of non-ferrous metals and one of the largest copper recyclers worldwide.
- The company processes complex metal concentrates, scrap metals, organic and inorganic metal-bearing recycling materials, and industrial residues into metals of the highest quality.
- Aurubis produces more than 1 million tons of copper cathodes annually.
- From its copper cathodes, Aurubis produces a variety of products such as wire rod, continuous cast shapes, profiles, and flat rolled products made of copper and copper alloys.
- Aurubis also produces other metals, including precious metals, selenium, lead, nickel, tin, and zinc.
- The company's product portfolio also includes additional products such as sulfuric acid and iron silicate.
- Sustainability is a fundamental component of the Aurubis strategy, following the maxim 'Aurubis responsibly transforms raw materials into value'.
- The company integrates sustainable conduct and business activities into its corporate culture, involving a careful approach to natural resources, responsible social and ecological conduct in everyday business, and sensible, healthy growth.
- Aurubis has around 6,900 employees globally.
- The company operates production sites in Europe and the USA, along with an extensive distribution network around the world.
- Aurubis shares are part of the Prime Standard Segment of the German Stock Exchange.
- Aurubis shares are listed in the MDAX, the Global Challenges Index (GCX), and the STOXX Europe 600.
