# Matthew A. Hinshaw

**Type:** person  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Experience | Matthew A. Hinshaw | People | Bradley](https://www.bradley.com/people/h/hinshaw-matthew-a?tab=experience)  ·  `page_000510`

## Facts

- Provided counsel to Panasonic Energy of North America in locating its first stand-alone lithium-ion battery cell manufacturing facility in De Soto, Kansas.
- The Panasonic facility is expected to cost over $4 billion to construct and equip.
- At full production, the Panasonic factory is expected to employ approximately 4,000 people.
- Provided counsel to Tesla Motors, Inc., an electric automobile manufacturer, in locating a lithium-ion battery manufacturing facility in Storey County, Nevada.
- The Tesla Gigafactory will cost approximately $10 billion to fully construct and equip.
- At full production, the Tesla Gigafactory will employ approximately 6,500 people.
- Provided incentives and real estate counsel to EnerSys for its industrial and defense lithium-ion battery cell manufacturing facility outside of Greenville, South Carolina.
- The EnerSys facility was projected to create 500 jobs and generate capital investment of $500 million.
- Provided counsel to several solar developers on utility-scale projects around the southeastern United States regarding tax incentives, site control, land use, and financing matters.
- Provided counsel to Canoo Technologies in the identification and selection of locations for its manufacturing, research and development, customer service, and headquarter facilities in Oklahoma and Arkansas.
- Canoo estimates investing approximately $1.5 billion and creating over 2,500 jobs for its facilities in Oklahoma and Arkansas.
- Provided counsel to VinFast in negotiating incentives for its electric vehicle assembly and battery manufacturing campus in Chatham County, North Carolina, outside of Raleigh.
- Provided incentives counsel to Volkswagen Group of America for its $600 million expansion of the Volkswagen Chattanooga manufacturing plant.
- The Volkswagen Chattanooga expansion project will add 2,000 jobs to the plant.
- Provided counsel to Spanish automotive supplier Truck & Wheel Group in locating a $30 million tire and wheel assembly facility in Tuscaloosa County, Alabama, which is their first facility in the U.S.
- The Truck & Wheel Group facility is one of the most technologically advanced assembly facilities of its kind in the world.
