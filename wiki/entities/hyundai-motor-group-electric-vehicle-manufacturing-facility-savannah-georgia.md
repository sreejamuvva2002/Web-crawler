# Hyundai Motor Group Electric Vehicle Manufacturing Facility (Savannah, Georgia)

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Near Savannah, Georgia, Chatham County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

A planned $5.5 billion electric-vehicle manufacturing plant by Hyundai Motor Group on a roughly 3,000-acre site near Savannah, Georgia, for which a joint development authority approved an economic-development agreement allowing the company to begin site improvements ahead of a projected 2025 opening.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The facility will produce electric vehicles in Georgia, expanding the state’s EV supply-chain and creating significant manufacturing capacity.
