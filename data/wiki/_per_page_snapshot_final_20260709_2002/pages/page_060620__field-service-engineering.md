# Field Service Engineering

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [GA: Field Service Engineering Relocates, Creates 80 jobs in Hart County | Trade and Industry Development](https://www.tradeandindustrydev.com/industry/back-officecall-centersdata-centers/news/ga-field-service-engineering-relocates-creates-80-8115)  ·  `page_060620`

## Facts

- Field Service Engineering is moving its corporate headquarters from Oakland County, Mich., to Hart County, Ga., creating 80 new full-time positions in an expanded facility.
- GDEcD Commissioner Chris Cummiskey stated that Georgia’s central location in the middle of the Southeast will make it very easy for FSE to travel from Hart County to anywhere in the southeastern U.S. to reach its customers.
- Field Service Engineering is a leader in global field mitigation and full service quality assurance.
- The company will invest $5 million in expanding its operations in Hart County.
- Plans include building a new 150,000-square-foot facility in Gateway Industrial Park at exit 177 on Interstate 85.
- FSE is adding 50 more positions to its current 30-person workforce in Hart County.
- The total number of employees will reach 80 when the company reaches full operation in 2015.
- FSE President and CEO Mark G. Belding stated that with support from Hart County and Georgia state officials, the company has established its presence in the Southeast and expects to continue growing in various sectors, creating larger demand for workforce expansion.
- FSE will expand its quality assurance services to the region’s robust automotive industry.
- The company also supports non-automotive manufacturers, as well as companies in the logistics and medical fields.
- Hart County Board of Commission Chairman Bill Myers expressed pleasure at seeing Field Service Engineering aggressively grow its business and workforce in the area.
- Hart County Industrial Building Authority Chairman Douglas Cleveland noted the expansion represents a significant commitment by FSE and expressed excitement about the career opportunities created for local citizens.
- Field Service Engineering was founded in 2005 and focuses on providing global field mitigation solutions.
- The company has evolved into a multifaceted organization focusing on quality improvement services with an overall objective of achieving total customer satisfaction.
- Services provided include product containment and inspection, CS2, End of Line (EOL) containment, line visualization, evaluation of plant quality and maintenance systems, transportation of hazardous or fuel-exposed products, and reduction of Cost of Poor Quality (COPQ).
