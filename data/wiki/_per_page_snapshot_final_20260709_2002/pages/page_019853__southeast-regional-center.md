## Facts

- The current offering involves the expansion of an existing advanced manufacturing facility operated by Ecoplastic America Corp in Bulloch County, Georgia.
- The Project will consist of a new Phase II production plant adjacent to the already operational facility.
- The Phase II plant is designed to meet growing demand for automotive components supplied to Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) in Bryan County, GA.
- The Project is structured with a senior, first-lien security interest in the land, building, and equipment.
- The Project is further enhanced by repayment guarantees from publicly listed companies.
- Following the Reform and Integrity Act (RIA) of 2022, Rural Area TEA projects now have a 20% visa set-aside.
- Investors of the Project qualify for the reduced EB-5 investment threshold of $800,000.
- Investors are provided with “Priority Processing”, reducing I-526E applications processing times to as few as 6 months.
- The Project benefits from being an expansion of an already operational facility, rather than a greenfield development.
- With existing infrastructure, workforce, and customer demand in place, the Phase II expansion is positioned to efficiently scale operations and support continued growth tied to Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA).
- The Project is expected to generate more than 2,200 total jobs upon completion.
- The job creation provides a substantial buffer above the EB-5 job creation requirement.
- SRC has completed multiple full cycles guiding investors through the initial capital investment, I-526 approvals, I-829 approvals and ultimately the full return of original capital over a long-standing relationship with Ajin USA.
- SRC Ajin Fund VI LLC involves the construction and operation of an 83-acre auto parts manufacturing plant by Joon Georgia, Inc.
- The Joon Georgia, Inc. plant supplies parts for HMGMA, Hyundai’s $7.59B mass production metaplant in Bryan County, GA.
- SRC Ajin Fund VI LLC supported 99 investors and fully funded $79.2M.
- There were 45+ approvals on adjudicated I-526 petitions filed between 2024 and 2025 for this fund.
- The fund achieved the fastest I-526E adjudication under 3.5 months with Rural Priority Processing.
- SRC Ajin Fund V LLC was a further expansion to support growing demand and was the beneficiary of a USCIS-approved project exemplar.
- SRC Ajin Fund V LLC had a 100% approval rate on adjudicated I-526 petitions, with I-829 in progress.
- SRC Ajin Fund V LLC returned 100% investment capital for investors that sustained the ‘at risk’ loan term.
- SRC Ajin Fund IV LLC was established to support rising demand by expanding Ajin USA's current manufacturing plant using EB-5 capital through SRC.
- SRC Ajin Fund IV LLC had a 100% approval rate on adjudicated I-526 petitions, with I-829 in progress.
- SRC Ajin Fund IV LLC returned 100% investment capital for investors that sustained the ‘at risk’ loan term.

- SRC Ajin Fund III LLC had a 100% approval rate on adjudicated I-526 and I-829 petitions.
