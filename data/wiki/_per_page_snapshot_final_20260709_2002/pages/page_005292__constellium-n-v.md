# Constellium N.V.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-05-11

**Source:** [Constellium celebrates the grand opening of its new plant for advanced aluminium automotive structures in White, Georgia](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/constellium-celebrates-the-grand-opening-of-its-new-plant-for-advanced-aluminium-automotive-structures-in-white-georgia-300455726.html)  ·  `page_005292`

## Facts

- Constellium N.V. announced the grand opening of its new plant in White, Georgia, on May 11, 2017.
- The facility is dedicated to the production of advanced aluminium automotive structural components and crash management systems.
- The plant occupies 135,000 sq. ft. of space.
- The facility is strategically located to supply automakers in the Southeast U.S.
- The plant may be expanded to 220,000 sq. ft. in the future to meet customers' supply needs.
- Constellium expects to have 150 employees at the White, Georgia location by 2019.
- Paul Warton, President of Constellium's Automotive Structures & Industry business unit, stated that being closer to customers' assembly plants will allow the company to better serve automakers in making vehicles lighter and safer.
- Paul Warton noted the plant responds to the industry's growing demand for aluminium structural parts.
- Eric Krepps, Vice President and General Manager of Constellium Automotive Structures North America, thanked the State of Georgia, Bartow County, local partners, and employees for their strong support in establishing the White, Georgia plant.
- Aluminium is a material of choice in the automotive industry to reduce vehicle weight, improve fuel economy, lower emissions, and increase safety.
- Constellium develops innovative, value-added aluminium products for aerospace, automotive, and packaging markets.
- Constellium generated €4.7 billion of revenue in 2016.
- The press release notes potential risks related to achieving expected synergies with Wise Metals and slower or lower than expected growth in the North American market for Body-in-White aluminium rolled products.
