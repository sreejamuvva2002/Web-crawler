## Facts

- Q4 GAAP net income was $18M.
- Q4 diluted EPS was $0.22.
- Q4 Adjusted diluted EPS was $0.52.
- Q4 Adjusted EBITDA was $226M.
- Q4 Adjusted EBITDA margin was 6.1%.
- For full FY25, Adient generated $204M of free cash flow and returned $125M to shareholders via share repurchases, representing ~7% of shares outstanding at the beginning of the fiscal year.
- At September 30, 2025, gross debt totaled ~$2.4B and net debt totaled ~$1.4B.
- At September 30, 2025, cash and cash equivalents totaled $958M.
- For FY26, Adient expects improved business performance to be offset by lower customer production volumes and increased growth investments.
- Adient's president and chief executive officer is Jerome Dorlack.
- Adient's executive vice president and chief financial officer is Mark Oswald.
- Adient has ~70,000 employees in 29 countries.
- Adient operates ~200 manufacturing/assembly plants worldwide.
- Adient produces and delivers automotive seating for all major OEMs.
- Adient's expertise spans every step of the automotive seat-making process from complete seating systems to individual components.
- Adient takes products from research and design to engineering and manufacturing into millions of vehicles every year.
- Adient identifies business risks including volatile currency exchange rates, U.S. trade policy and tariffs, automotive production levels and schedules, manufacturer concentration, joint venture risks, supply chain disruptions, wage inflation, restructuring execution, debt service requirements, global tax reform, climate change/sustainability goals, and leadership retention.
- Non-GAAP measures tracked include Adjusted EBIT, Adjusted EBITDA, Adjusted net income, Adjusted effective tax rate, Adjusted EPS, Adjusted equity income, Adjusted interest expense, Free cash flow, and Net debt.
- Adient's key performance indicator is defined as period-over-period Adjusted EBITDA excluding production volume/mix, equity income, foreign exchange, and net commodity pricing.
