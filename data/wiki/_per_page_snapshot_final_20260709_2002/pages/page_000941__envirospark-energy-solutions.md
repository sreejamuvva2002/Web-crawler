# EnviroSpark Energy Solutions

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- EnviroSpark Energy Solutions announced it has secured a $50 million investment from funds advised by Basalt Infrastructure Partners LLP.
- Basalt is a leading mid-market infrastructure equity investment firm focusing on investments in Europe and North America.
- The investment will fuel EnviroSpark’s mission to revolutionize the EV charging landscape, enabling rapid expansion of its owned and operated network across the US.
- The funding will support innovation of cutting-edge technologies and enhance accessibility and sustainability of EV infrastructure.
- This funding builds upon the momentum of a $15 million funding round led by Ultra Capital in 2022.
- EnviroSpark is a Georgia-based company that has established itself as a key player in the EV charging market.
- The company maintains a broad footprint of over 8,200 charging plugs across North America.
- Aaron Luque serves as co-founder and CEO of EnviroSpark.
- Aaron Luque stated that securing the funding marks a pivotal moment for the company and reinforces its commitment to making electric mobility accessible and convenient for all.
- Andrew Marsden serves as Basalt’s Head of Energy Transition.
- Andrew Marsden stated that Basalt is investing in compelling scale-up opportunities in low carbon infrastructure and is partnering with EnviroSpark to drive their next phase of growth.
- Andrew Marsden noted that EnviroSpark's industry-leading team is accelerating the build-out of the EV-charging network across North America at a pivotal time for the clean transportation sector.
- EnviroSpark has cultivated strategic partnerships with RaceTrac, Waffle House, IHG Hotels & Resorts, Ford Dealerships, HHHunt, and Asset Living.
- EnviroSpark maintains longstanding partnerships with Tesla, Volkswagen, Volta (recently acquired by Shell), and Starwood Capital Group.
- EnviroSpark has partnered with the U.S. Federal Government through the General Services Administration to drive widespread commercial and government EV adoption.
- EnviroSpark secured National Electric Vehicle Infrastructure (NEVI) awards in Georgia and Tennessee.
- The Georgia Department of Transportation (GDOT) selected EnviroSpark to construct significant DCFC charging projects in Tifton and Metter, Georgia.
- The Tennessee Department of Transportation (TDOT) selected EnviroSpark for installations at a Waffle House in Lakeland, Tennessee.
- The NEVI awards are funded by the Federal government through the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law.
- EnviroSpark was founded in Atlanta, Georgia, by Aaron and Stephanie Luque in 2014.
- EnviroSpark is an industry leader in the turnkey design, installation, and operation of EV charging solutions.
- The company was founded with two missions: to raise awareness around the benefits of electric vehicles and to build a more robust EV charging infrastructure.
- EnviroSpark has been responsible for more than 8,200 installations of charging plugs across North America to date.
- EnviroSpark addresses pain points of property owners and drivers by enhancing EV accessibility in customer-friendly ways.
