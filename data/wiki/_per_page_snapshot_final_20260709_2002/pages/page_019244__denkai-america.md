# Denkai America

## Facts

- Denkai America and Home Chef are both adding new locations soon in Right to Work Georgia.
- Altogether, these two companies will create 950 new jobs.
- Denkai America is investing $430 million in order to locate headquarters in Right to Work Richmond County, Georgia.
- Denkai America's investment will also create 250 new jobs.
- Georgia officials state that selecting Denkai America strengthens the state's status as a leader in emerging electric mobility and existing aerospace, defense, and technology industries.
- State officials emphasize they have been laser focused on bringing quality jobs to highly-trained, hardworking Georgians across the state.
- Denkai America is expected to thrive in Georgia's top-ranking pro-business environment.
- The State of Georgia is committed to harnessing widespread electric mobility adoption to ensure Georgians in today's automotive industry have access to future EV jobs at home, citing Denkai America's vital role in the EV ecosystem.
- The Development Authority of Augusta, Georgia, welcomes Denkai America and its North American Headquarters to the Augusta Corporate Park.
- Denkai America will bring high-skilled jobs to the growing list of companies that call Augusta and the Augusta Corporate Park home.
- The Augusta Commission and Augusta Utilities Department provided support and hard work to make Denkai America's development happen.
- Home Chef is creating 700 new jobs as a result of investing in a new location in Right to Work Douglasville, Georgia.
- Douglas County officials express an honor in welcoming Home Chef and are committed to recruiting great companies and partnering with them to thrive in the community.
- Home Chef's new production operations will be its first facility to incorporate a full service kitchen for cooking and batch production of ingredients for its meals.
- The Home Chef facility will feature a 6,000 square-foot space handling the cooking of vegetables, starches, and grains, as well as in-house sauce production.
