# SRG Global Inc.

## Facts

- SRG Global Inc. is a privately held manufacturer and supplier of chrome plated plastic parts for the automotive and commercial truck industries.
- The company has manufacturing operations across North America, Europe and Asia.
- SRG Global's Advanced Development Center in Taylor, Michigan, is a dedicated facility for developing new plastics coating technology.
- SRG Global was founded in 2009 and is headquartered in Troy, Michigan.
- The company reports revenue of $1 billion+.
- Revenues were $380 MM in 2024, $250 MM in 2023, $90 MM in 2022, $120 MM in 2021, and $65 MM in 2020.
- Gross profit was $190 MM in 2024, $125 MM in 2023, $45 MM in 2022, $60 MM in 2021, and $32.50 MM in 2020.
- Net income (loss) was $43 MM in 2024, $25 MM in 2023, $9 MM in 2022, $12 MM in 2021, and $6.50 MM in 2020.
- Total assets were $850 MM in 2024, $720 MM in 2023, $680 MM in 2022, $650 MM in 2021, and $600 MM in 2020.
- Total liabilities were $450 MM in 2024, $400 MM in 2023, $380 MM in 2022, $350 MM in 2021, and $320 MM in 2020.
- Total equity was $400 MM in 2024, $320 MM in 2023, $300 MM in 2022, $300 MM in 2021, and $280 MM in 2020.
- The company employs between 1,000 and 5,000 people.
- Key executives include Clyde Newcomer (Chief Chemist), Merritt Gaunt (President), Michael Lee (President), and Elk Krausz (Tools Engineer).
- On March 15, 2024, NorthBridge PE completed an acquisition of SRG Global for $120M, taking a 100% stake and taking the company private.
- The target company valuation was listed as $1.20 MM at the time of the March 2024 deal.
- A secondary transaction scheduled for September 1, 2023, was canceled.
- The canceled September 2023 secondary transaction involved a majority stake and techniques including a secondary sale and management buy-out (MBO), with 65% total acquired.
