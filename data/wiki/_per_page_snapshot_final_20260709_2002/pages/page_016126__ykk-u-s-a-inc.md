# YKK (U.S.A.) Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Macon-Bibb County Government](https://www.maconbibb.us/ykk-usa-receives-2021-gear-award/)  ·  `page_016126`

## Facts

- YKK (U.S.A.) Inc. was honored with a 2021 GEAR Award by the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD).
- The award ceremony took place on January 14 at YKK's Macon, Georgia plant.
- GDEcD named YKK the “Supplier of the Year, employing 200+ people” for its contributions to advancing Georgia’s automotive and mobility industries.
- YKK produces a wide range of zippers, hook and loop fasteners, plastic hardware, and snaps and buttons.
- These products are used for automobile seat assembly, truck tonneau cover hold-downs, and other applications.
- YKK supplies these components to more than 100 automotive customers.
- YKK operates a vertically integrated structure that allows the company to create its own materials and machinery.
- This vertical integration ensures quality, dependable supply, and minimization of manufacturing costs.
- GDEcD Chief Administrative Officer and General Counsel Andrew Capezzuto presented the award.
- The 2021 GEAR award for Supplier of the Year recognizes companies that are good corporate stewards.
- Macon-Bibb County Mayor Lester Miller stated that YKK is about investing in people and community.
- Robert Stamps, Vice President and Business Development Leader – Transportation Fastening Materials at YKK, demonstrated various automotive fastening products.
- Keith Kratz, Product Development Engineer at YKK, also demonstrated the products.
- The ERGO-J® is a sheet fastener for securing the edges of motor vehicle seat covering material.
- The ERGO-J® improves the ergonomic efficacy of seat assembly and reduces the rate of development of carpal tunnel syndrome for automotive workers.
- The QUICKFIT® is a concealed hook fastener for automotive seats that enables operators to achieve a craftsmanship-like appearance when molded into seat foam.
- The QUICKFIT® reduces seat weight compared to metal wire tie-down systems.
- The 5CH CONCEAL® zipper forms a perfect seam when closed.
- The 5CH CONCEAL® zipper allows seat manufacturers to significantly decrease the weight of seat back panels to improve fuel efficiency.
- YKK Corporation of America President Jim Reed expressed gratitude for the growth of the automotive group.
- YKK uses a cyclical process to contribute to the automotive industry, reinvest profits into employees, and remain a foundational pillar of the community.
- YKK operates under its "Cycle of Goodness" philosophy and aims to give back to local communities through environmental initiatives.
- YKK has pledged to reduce its emissions by 50% by 2030.
- YKK aims to achieve net-zero emissions by 2050.
