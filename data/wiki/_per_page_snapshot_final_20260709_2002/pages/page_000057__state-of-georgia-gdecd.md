# State of Georgia / GDEcD

## Facts

- Manufacturing in Georgia outpaced the Southeast in manufacturing GDP and job growth over the last decade, according to the 2025 JobsEQ report.
- Georgia leads in advanced manufacturing sectors including electric mobility, aerospace, advanced materials, and automation.
- Companies benefit from Georgia’s investments in workforce development, robust logistics infrastructure, and development-ready sites.
- Business Facilities continues to rank Georgia in the top five for aerospace and defense manufacturers.
- In fiscal year 2025, manufacturing accounted for 46% of new jobs announced by GDEcD.
- Georgia Tech ranks No. 1 among public universities for undergraduate engineering programs according to U.S. News & World Report.
- Georgia supports manufacturers at every stage of growth from concept to full-scale production and ongoing process improvement.
- Through services unique to the state, companies connect with leading university researchers, prototyping and fabrication shops, contract manufacturers, and more to identify opportunities to enhance efficiency and bring products to market.
- Qualified manufacturers are invited to join the Georgia Made® program, a promotional state branding and marketing opportunity that naturally enables stronger alliances with GDEcD’s business support teams.
