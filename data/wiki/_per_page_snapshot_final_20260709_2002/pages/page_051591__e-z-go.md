# E-Z-GO

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-06-02

**Source:** [REYNOLDS LAKE OCONEE TO USE E-Z-GO VEHICLES - The Golf Wire](https://thegolfwire.com/reynolds-lake-oconee-to-use-e-z-go-vehicles/)  ·  `page_051591`

## Facts

- Reynolds Lake Oconee announced it will now use vehicles from E-Z-GO, the worldwide leader in golf car design and manufacturing.
- The community’s six golf courses will use E-Z-GO RXV ELiTE golf cars and a fleet of Cushman utility vehicles.
- E-Z-GO and Cushman vehicles are designed and manufactured by Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a Textron Inc. (NYSE:TXT) company.
- The E-Z-GO RXV ELiTE golf cars feature the industry’s leading electric powertrain technology powered by Samsung SDI’s eco-friendly lithium batteries.
- The RXV ELiTE is equipped with an AC motor for best-in-class acceleration through hilly grades.
- The motor’s performance is enhanced with IntelliBrake technology that automatically applies the parking brake when the vehicle is stopped.
- The golf cars feature hi-definition Textron Fleet Management screens with HD-hole graphics, accurate yardage and pin locations, mobile food ordering, and a digital scorecard.
- Cushman vehicles offer innovative powertrains, including ELiTE models powered by Samsung SDI lithium batteries and 72-volt technology.
- Wes Forester, Director of Golf at Reynolds Lake Oconee, stated that E-Z-GO provides access to the largest fleet of factory-direct service technicians and a strong network of factory-owned branch locations.
- John Collins, Vice President of E-Z-GO for Textron Specialized Vehicles, commented on serving the prestigious property.
- E-Z-GO was founded in 1954 in Augusta, Ga.
- E-Z-GO became part of Textron Inc. in 1960 and operates as part of the company’s Textron Specialized Vehicles division.
- Products sold under the E-Z-GO brand include RXV and TXT fleet golf cars, Freedom RXV and TXT personal golf cars, Valor personal golf cars, and E-Z-GO Express personal utility vehicles.
- E-Z-GO’s newest offerings include the ELiTE series of lithium-ion powered golf cars and PTVs.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. markets products under brands including E-Z-GO, Arctic Cat, Cushman, Jacobsen, Ransomes, TUG, Douglas, Premier, and Safeaero.
