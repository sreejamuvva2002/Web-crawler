# Trenton Pressing

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-05

**Source:** [Rep. Mike Cameron Applauds Trenton Pressing’s $12 Million Expansion in Dade County – Discover Dade](https://www.discoverdade.com/local/rep-mike-cameron-applauds-trenton-pressings-12-million-expansion-in-dade-county/)  ·  `page_012419`

## Facts

- Trenton Pressing completed a $12 million expansion of its manufacturing operations in Dade County.
- The expansion adds 100,000 square feet to the company's manufacturing and assembly operations.
- State Representative Mike Cameron (R-Rossville) applauded the completion of the expansion.
- Rep. Cameron stated that the Southeast United States has become a magnet for automotive brands and suppliers over the last two decades.
- Rep. Cameron noted that Georgia leads by example in attracting automotive investment by investing in its workforce and making business conditions better.
- The 100,000-square-foot expansion represents a milestone for Trenton Pressing's growth.
- The expansion enhances Trenton Pressing's ability to supply high-quality components to its clients.
- Trenton Pressing supplies components to brands including BMW, Club Car, and ZF.
- Trenton Pressing is now one of the leading employers in Dade County.
- The company's workforce exceeds 200 employees.
- Trenton Pressing specializes in metal stamping, welding processes, and complex assemblies.
- The company provides comprehensive turnkey contract manufacturing solutions.
- Trenton Pressing offers warehousing and shipping services to its clients.
- The company caters to both automotive and non-automotive clients.
- Representative Mike Cameron represents District 1, which includes Dade County and portions of Walker County.
- Rep. Cameron was elected to the Georgia House of Representatives in 2020.
- He currently serves as Vice Chairman of the Human Relations & Aging committee.
- He serves as Secretary of the Interstate Cooperation committee.
- He serves on the Appropriations Subcommittee on Human Resources.
- He serves on the Agriculture & Consumer Affairs committee.
- He serves on the Budget and Fiscal Affairs Oversight committee.
- He serves on the Industry and Labor committees.
