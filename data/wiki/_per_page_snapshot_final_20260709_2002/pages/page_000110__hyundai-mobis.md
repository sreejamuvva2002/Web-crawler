## Facts

- Hyundai Mobis is the largest tier-one supplier for Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA).
- Hyundai Mobis manufactures front and rear end chassis modules.
- Hyundai Mobis manufactures cockpit modules.
- Hyundai Mobis manufactures key parts to build batteries for electric vehicles.
- Training for Mobis includes company-specific assembly courses, mechatronics, and electrical safety awareness.
- Trainees are provided with safety and organizational development courses, such as Lean.
- Trainees are provided with leadership training and conflict resolution courses.
- Quick Start is using its main Advanced Manufacturing Training Center in Pooler, Ga., to provide the training.
- Quick Start is using an interim facility for the training while a new center is under construction.
- The new Hyundai Mobility Training Center of Georgia is under construction adjacent to the Metaplant.
- The Metaplant, where Mobis’ operations in the Savannah area will be located, is slated to start production on vehicles by early 2025.
- Quick Start’s Eastern Operations around Savannah, Georgia, are busier than ever as opening day approaches for HMGMA.
