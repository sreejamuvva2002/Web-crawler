# Amara Industries

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Amara Industries, a parent company to the Gracious Living group of companies, announced the acquisition of Thomson Plastics.
- Thomson Plastics is a recognized leader in large tonnage injection molding in the Southeastern United States.
- Thomson Plastics currently operates from two locations in Georgia and North Carolina.
- The strategic acquisition enhances Amara Industries' manufacturing footprint, automation capabilities, and technical depth to a total of five manufacturing facilities.
- The acquisition strengthens Amara Industries' position as a key player in the North American injection molding industry.
- Thomson Plastics has clamping force capacities up to 3,700 tons.
- Thirty percent of Thomson Plastics' fleet exceeds 1,000 tons.
- Thomson Plastics is known for its scale, precision, and ability to meet the demands of major customers across multiple facilities.
- Thomson Plastics is supported by advanced robotics and automation to deliver consistent performance and efficiency across high-volume production environments.
- Thomson Plastics' technical portfolio includes injection molding, gas-assisted molding, over-molding, insert molding, in-mold labeling, robotic part picking, pad printing, and heat transfer decorating.
- Vince Orlando serves as Co-President at Amara Industries.
- Vince Orlando stated that the addition of Thomson Plastics represents a strategic milestone for the organization.
- Vince Orlando noted that advanced molding capabilities, technical expertise, and culture of innovation will enhance the ability to serve customers with scale, speed, and precision across North America.
- The acquisition is a significant development in the North American injection molding industry.
- With large-tonnage capacity and advanced automation, the unified company will meet rising demand for high-performance molded products in key sectors including automotive, consumer goods, industrial, and commercial markets.
- The expansion into the Southeastern United States strengthens Amara Industries' ability to provide customers with faster delivery, greater flexibility, and improved supply chain efficiency.
