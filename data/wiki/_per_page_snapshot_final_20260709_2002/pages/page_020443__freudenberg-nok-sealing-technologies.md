# Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies

**Type:** supplier_relationship  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-12-01

**Source:** [Freudenberg-NOK Named Meritor Rear Axle Supplier Of Year](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/freudenberg-nok-named-rear-axle-supplier-of-the-year-by-meritor/)  ·  `page_020443`

## Facts

- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies has been named the Rear Axle Supplier of the Year by Meritor Inc.
- The award ceremony took place on Nov. 12 at Meritor’s headquarters in Troy, Michigan.
- Executives cited Freudenberg-NOK as the “real seal deal” during the ceremony.
- Freudenberg-NOK’s pinion seals and other seals are used by Meritor in its commercial vehicle business.
- Freudenberg-NOK products can be found on the majority of commercial truck brands on the road today.
- This is the second consecutive year that Freudenberg-NOK has been recognized for innovation and technology contributing to Meritor’s success.
- Meritor is a leading global supplier of drivetrain, mobility, braking and aftermarket solutions for commercial vehicle and industrial markets.
- Freudenberg-NOK was the only seal company Meritor named as one of its top suppliers in 2015.
- Tim Guzman, senior sales director for Freudenberg-NOK’s Power Sector, said they are honored to receive Rear Axle Supplier of the Year recognition from Meritor, one of their largest general industry customers.
- Guzman stated the award represents their steadfast dedication to technology, innovation, quality and customer service to ensure they are meeting all of Meritor’s business needs, calling it a collaborative relationship.
- Meritor executives noted that Freudenberg-NOK consistently met demanding record volumes and competitiveness targets during 2015 while maintaining performance and quality levels of the seals supplied.
- Freudenberg-NOK’s Northfield, New Hampshire, competence center switched from compression molding to state-of-the-art injection molding in manufacturing pinion seals.
- The switch to injection molding improved quality, increased component volumes, and reduced engineered waste at the Northfield facility.
- Meritor recognized a total of 18 companies at its awards ceremony.
- Jon Rose, vice president, procurement for Meritor, said each of the 18 honored companies demonstrated a commitment to quality and excellence through the North America Supplier Partner Award.
- Rose noted their performance contributes to Meritor’s strategic goal of meeting and exceeding customer expectations and strengthens mutual businesses.
