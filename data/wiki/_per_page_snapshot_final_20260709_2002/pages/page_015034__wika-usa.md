## Facts

- WIKA USA’s corporate headquarters is located in Lawrenceville, Georgia (Metro Atlanta).
- The facility manufactures high-quality industrial pressure gauges.
- The facility manufactures precision thermometers.
- The facility produces other measurement solutions for companies in North America and around the globe.
- The Lawrenceville location includes a Class 100 cleanroom for producing ultra-high-purity gauges.
- The facility operates a state-of-the-art calibration and repair lab for instruments that handle sulfur hexafluoride and other insulating gases.
- WIKA supports U.S. manufacturing at its Lawrenceville location.
