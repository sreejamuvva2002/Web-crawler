# Visteon Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2014-03-19

**Source:** [Visteon signs agreement to sell shares in Duckyang JV for $ 24.1 mn - Auto Components India](https://autocomponentsindia.com/visteon-signs-agreement-to-sell-shares-in-duckyang-jv-for-24-1-million-as-step-toward-interiors-divestiture/)  ·  `page_007737`

## Facts

- Visteon Corporation announced an agreement to sell its 50 percent ownership stake in a Korean automotive interiors joint venture, Duckyang Industry Co., Ltd.
- The sale supports Visteon's previously announced intentions to divest its interiors business.
- Duckyang is based in Ulsan, South Korea.
- Duckyang manufactures and develops original equipment interior components for vehicles.
- The sale is to certain management shareholders of Duckyang and associated parties.
- The sale price is the equivalent of about $24.1 million (25.9 billion Korean Won).
- The transaction is expected to be completed in April after closing conditions are met.
- In addition to the share sale proceeds, Visteon expects to receive approximately $5.9 million (6.3 billion Korean Won) in dividends.
- Visteon president and CEO Timothy D. Leuliette stated they are pleased to have reached the agreement as another step in Visteon’s transformation and in support of their stated goal to exit their interiors business.
- Leuliette noted they are working toward the interiors divestiture as part of a commitment to deliver customer and shareholder value by focusing on core growth climate and electronics businesses.
