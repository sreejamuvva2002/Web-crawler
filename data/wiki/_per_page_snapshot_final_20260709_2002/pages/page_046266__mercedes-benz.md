# Mercedes-Benz

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Mercedes-Benz is strengthening its presence in metro Atlanta with a major headquarters expansion.
- The company will create up to 500 new jobs at its North American headquarters in Sandy Springs.
- Since relocating its North American headquarters from New Jersey in 2018, Mercedes-Benz has become an integral part of the local business community.
- The latest expansion will centralize key corporate functions by relocating roles from California and the suburbs of Detroit.
- Mercedes-Benz is investing millions to build a state-of-the-art research and development center near its 1MB campus.
- The new research and development center will accelerate innovation and create new, high-skilled jobs.
- Gregg Simon, senior vice president of project and partner development at the Metro Atlanta Chamber, stated: 'This project is a great example of why companies choose metro Atlanta. What began with Mercedes’ headquarters relocation in 2015 has evolved into a decade of growth — from the stadium partnership to expansions in Sandy Springs and the new R&D hub at Georgia Tech.'
- Georgia has been ranked the number 1 state for business for 12 consecutive years.
