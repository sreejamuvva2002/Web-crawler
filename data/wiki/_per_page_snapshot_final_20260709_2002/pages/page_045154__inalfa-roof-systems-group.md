## Facts

- Inalfa Roof Systems Group is a global provider of vehicle roof systems.

- Inalfa Roof Systems Group has a global footprint with manufacturing facilities in Europe, Asia, and America.
- The company's products are used by major car manufacturers such as BMW, Daimler, Ford, General Motors, and Volvo.
- Inalfa Roof Systems Group was founded in 1946.
- Inalfa Roof Systems Group is headquartered in Venray, the Netherlands.
