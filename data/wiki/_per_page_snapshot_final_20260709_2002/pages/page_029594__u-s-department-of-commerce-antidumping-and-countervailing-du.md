# U.S. Department of Commerce Antidumping and Countervailing Duty Investigations

**Type:** policy_or_incentive  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-25

**Source:** [Initiation of AD and CVD Investigations of Aluminum Extrusions from Multiple Countries](https://www.trade.gov/initiation-ad-and-cvd-investigations-aluminum-extrusions-multiple-countries)  ·  `page_029594`

## Facts

- On October 25, 2023, the U.S. Department of Commerce announced the initiation of antidumping duty (AD) and countervailing duty (CVD) investigations of aluminum extrusions from China, Indonesia, Mexico, Turkey, Colombia, Dominican Republic, Ecuador, India, Italy, Korea, Malaysia, Taiwan, Thailand, UAE, and Vietnam.
- The petitioners are the U.S. Aluminum Extrusions Coalition and the United Steel, Paper and Forestry, Rubber, Manufacturing, Energy, Allied Industrial and Service Workers International Union (Pittsburgh, PA).
- The Coalition consists of 14 U.S. aluminum extruders: Alexandria Extrusion Company (Alexandria, MN); APEL Extrusions Inc. (Coburg, OR); Bonnell Aluminum (Newnan, GA); Brazeway (Adrian, MI); Custom Aluminum Products (South Elgin, IL); Extrudex Aluminum (North Jackson, OH); International Extrusions (Garden City, MI); Jordan Aluminum Company (Memphis, TN); M-D Building Products (Oklahoma City, OK); Merit Aluminum (Corona, CA); MI Metals (Oldsmar, FL); Pennex Aluminum (Wellsville, PA); Tower Extrusions (Olney, TX); Western Extrusions (Carrollton, TX).
- Bonnell Aluminum is located in Newnan, GA.
- China has an alleged dumping margin of 376.85%.
- Colombia has an alleged dumping margin of 165.25%.
- Dominican Republic has an alleged dumping margin of 28.29%.
- Ecuador has an alleged dumping margin of 42.79-63.21%.
- India has an alleged dumping margin of 39.05%.
- Indonesia has an alleged dumping margin of 88.53%.
- Italy has an alleged dumping margin of 41.67%.
- Korea has an alleged dumping margin of 43.56%.
- Malaysia has an alleged dumping margin of 25.89-27.51%.
- Mexico has an alleged dumping margin of 76.68-82.03%.
- Taiwan has an alleged dumping margin of 60.25-67.86%.
- Thailand has an alleged dumping margin of 76.73%.
- Turkey has an alleged dumping margin of 48.43%.
- UAE has an alleged dumping margin of 42.29%.
- Vietnam has an alleged dumping margin of 41.84%.
- Alleged subsidy rates for China, Indonesia, Mexico, and Turkey are above de minimis (less than 1% for developed countries, less than 2% for developing countries).
- Petitions were filed on October 4, 2023, with Commerce initiation dates on October 24, 2023.
- ITC preliminary determinations are scheduled for November 20, 2023.
- Commerce preliminary AD determinations are due March 12, 2024, and CVD preliminary determinations December 28, 2023.
- Commerce final AD determinations are due May 28, 2024, and CVD final determinations March 12, 2024.
- ITC final determinations are scheduled for July 12, 2024 (AD) and April 26, 2024 (CVD).
- Issuance of AD orders is scheduled for July 19, 2024, and CVD orders for May 3, 2024.
