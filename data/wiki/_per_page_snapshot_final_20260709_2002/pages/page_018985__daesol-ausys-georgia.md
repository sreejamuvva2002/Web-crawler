# Daesol Ausys Georgia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-02

**Source:** [Daesol Ausys Opening New Manufacturing Facility In Georgia | Doc and the Block](https://docandtheblock.com/daesol-ausys-opening-new-manufacturing-facility-in-georgia/)  ·  `page_018985`

## Facts

- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp announced that Daesol Ausys Georgia would be creating a new manufacturing facility in West Point.
- The project will create more than 140 new jobs.
- The project will invest $72 million in Harris County.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that West Point has become an automotive capital in its own right.
- Daesol Ausys specializes in designing and manufacturing motor and electric vehicle interior components and accessories.
- Daesol Ausys was founded in 2017 in Korea.
- Min Ho Kwon serves as the CEO of Daesol Ausys.
- CEO Min Ho Kwon stated the company is committed to delivering world-class services that exceed client requirements and expectations.
- The new facility will be located in the Northwest Harris Business Park.
- Daesol Ausys plans to continue its partnership with the State of Georgia and Harris County for this project.
- The new facility will produce luggage boards and luggage covers.
- Operations at the new facility are expected to begin in December 2024.
- Robert Grant serves as the Chairman of the Harris County Commission.
- Chairman Robert Grant stated that locating in the Northwest Harris Business Park represents a commitment to Harris County.
- Daesol Ausys has sister companies named Daehan Solutions and Daesol Materials.
- West Point and Harris County have a strong relationship with Daesol Ausys’ sister companies and have worked closely with company officials to secure the new venture.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that Georgia’s automotive industry employs over 50,000 hardworking Georgians.
