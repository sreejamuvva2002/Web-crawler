# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-08-11

**Source:** [Archer](https://news.archer.com/archer-announces-second-quarter-results-highlighting-manufacturing-ramp-of-midnight-aircraft-ahead-of-early-commercial-deployments)  ·  `page_016971`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. announced operating and financial results for the second quarter ended June 30, 2025 on August 11, 2025.
- Six Midnight aircraft are in production, with three currently in final assembly at the company's facilities.
- The company is ramping aircraft manufacturing across its facilities in California and Georgia.
- Archer is building the Midnight aircraft across its golden manufacturing line in Silicon Valley and a high-volume facility in Georgia.
- The FAA continued conducting reviews and inspections of Archer’s manufacturing operations as part of production certificate efforts.
- The LA 2028 Olympic Games selected Archer as its Official Air Taxi Provider to help align federal and local stakeholders around achieving FAA type certification and scaled operations of the Midnight aircraft.
- Archer partnered with the U.S. Department of Transportation, the FAA, and the White House on an Executive Order in June 2025 to promote American dominance in eVTOL aircraft and create a Presidential mandate for early U.S. deployments as soon as next year.
- Archer signed definitive agreements with Abu Dhabi Aviation and the Abu Dhabi Investment Office to activate the first of several planned Launch Edition programs globally.
- The company delivered its first Midnight aircraft to the UAE, commenced its flight test program in Abu Dhabi, and expects initial commercial payments later this year.
- Archer Defense is seeing strong demand from four major allied defense programs worldwide.
- During the quarter, Archer made two strategic acquisitions: a patent portfolio and critical talent from Overair, and specialized defense composite manufacturing assets from Mission Critical Composites in Southern California.
- The company maintains a sector-leading balance sheet with over $1.7 billion of cash and cash equivalents.
- Archer's financial estimates for the third quarter of 2025 project an Adjusted EBITDA loss of $110 million to $130 million.
- Non-GAAP financial adjustments include non-cash warrant costs classified as research and development expenses for warrants issued to Stellantis N.V. in connection with services provided to the company.
- Adam Goldstein serves as Archer’s founder and CEO.
