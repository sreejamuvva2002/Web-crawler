# Amara Industries

## Facts

- Amara Industries has acquired Thomson Plastics.
- Thomson Plastics is a large tonnage injection moulding specialist operating from facilities in Georgia and North Carolina.
- The deal expands Amara Industries' manufacturing footprint to five facilities.
- The acquisition strengthens Amara Industries' position in the North American injection moulding industry.
- Thomson Plastics operates equipment with clamping force capacities up to 3,700 tonnes.
- Thirty percent of Thomson Plastics' fleet exceeds 1,000 tonnes.
- The company specialises in advanced techniques including gas-assisted moulding, over-moulding and in-mould labelling.
- Thomson Plastics' operations are supported by robotics and automation.
- The acquisition enhances Amara Industries' capabilities in serving automotive, consumer goods, industrial and commercial markets.
- The expansion into the Southeastern United States aims to provide customers with faster delivery and improved supply chain efficiency.
- The announcement was published in December 2025.
