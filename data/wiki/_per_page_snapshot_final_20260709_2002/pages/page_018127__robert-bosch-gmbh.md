# Robert Bosch GmbH

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [The 2024 business year: Bosch held back by market developments - Bosch Media Service](https://www.bosch-presse.de/pressportal/de/en/the-2024-business-year-273280.html)  ·  `page_018127`

## Facts

- Growth markets such as electromobility developed much more slowly than expected, adversely affecting Bosch Group’s business development.
- A lack of sales in electromobility areas and accompanying underutilized capacity negatively impacted results.
- Continued high upfront expenditure for future technologies and provisions for necessary strategic adjustments had a negative impact on result.
- By 2030, the company aims to be among the leading suppliers in its areas of business in key markets.
- The company is striving for average annual growth of between 6 and 8 percent, with a margin of at least 7 percent.
- Bosch systematically implemented its Strategy 2030 and reached important milestones in the past fiscal year.
- Bosch plans to acquire the HVAC business of Johnson Controls and Hitachi for around 8 billion U.S. dollars to expand presence in growth markets such as the U.S. and Asia.
- Electromobility, hydrogen, and sustainable technologies remain a growth business and the focus of Bosch's innovations.
- Bosch is currently testing a cryogenic pump in the U.S. that compresses up to 600 kilograms of liquid hydrogen per hour.
- The tested cryogenic pump will allow trucks to fill up with enough hydrogen for the next 1,000 kilometers in just 10 minutes.
- Bosch expects to generate sales of more than 6 billion euros with software and services by the beginning of the next decade, two-thirds of this in the Mobility business sector.
- AI has been playing an important role in assisted and automated driving at Bosch for years.
- Bosch developed a system solution called Vehicle Motion Management that enables brake-by-wire braking systems, in which an electronic brake pedal operates without a mechanical connection.
- Bosch aims to achieve its target margin of 7 percent by 2026.
- The Mobility business sector reported sales revenue of 55.9 billion euros, roughly reaching the previous year's level despite a declining market.
- North America sales revenue grew by 5 percent to 16 billion euros.
- As of December 31, 2024, the Bosch Group employed approximately 417,900 associates worldwide, nearly 3 percent lower than the previous year.
- In Germany, headcount fell by around 3 percent to just over 129,800.
