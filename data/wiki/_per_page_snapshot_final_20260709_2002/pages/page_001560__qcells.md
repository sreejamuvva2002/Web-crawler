# Qcells

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-02-06

**Source:** [Qcells, LG Energy Solution Vertech ink 5 GWh US battery storage deal - Energy Storage](https://www.ess-news.com/2026/02/06/qcells-lg-energy-solution-vertech-ink-5-gwh-us-battery-storage-deal/)  ·  `page_001560`

## Facts

- LG Energy Solution Vertech and Qcells announced a partnership to deploy 5 GWh of battery energy storage systems (BESS) across the United States.
- The agreement will see LG Energy Solution Vertech supply lithium iron phosphate (LFP) battery systems for Qcells’ utility-scale engineering, procurement and construction (EPC) arm.
- Projects under the agreement are scheduled between 2028 and 2030.
- The deal reflects a continued focus on domestic supply chains in the U.S. renewable energy market.
- Battery cells for the installations will be manufactured at LG Energy Solution facilities in Michigan and Arizona.
- The battery systems will be deployed alongside Qcells solar modules produced at the company’s Georgia manufacturing hub.
- Chris Hodrick, CEO of Qcells EPC, stated that Qcells is proud to partner with LG Energy Solution on a project that shows what’s possible when energy development supports U.S. manufacturing.
- Chris Hodrick noted the collaboration reflects a shared commitment to building clean energy by creating American jobs, strengthening supply chains, and delivering reliable, affordable power for communities across the country.
- This is the second significant agreement between Qcells and LG Energy Solution Vertech in recent years.
- In May 2024, Qcells and LG Energy Solution Vertech signed a 4.8 GWh supply deal.
- Reports from Wood Mackenzie and the American Clean Power Association (ACP) noted that the U.S. installed more than 35.4 GWh of energy storage in the first nine months of 2025 alone.
- The 35.4 GWh installed in the first nine months of 2025 surpassed the total installed in all of 2024.
- In late 2025, the U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA) reported that total capacity served by energy storage installations increased by nearly 14 GW in the preceding 12 months.
- The EIA forecast the installation of an additional 22 GW of capacity in the following 12-month period.
- Qcells is a subsidiary of Korean giant Hanwha Solutions.
- Qcells secured a massive 12 GW solar module supply agreement with Microsoft in 2024.
- Qcells offers manufactured products, project development and installation services for the residential, commercial, and utility-scale sectors.
- Qcells provides installation financing through its Enfin lending arm.
- Qcells says its annual solar panel production capacity in the U.S. will reach 8.4 gigawatts when the new Cartersville facility is complete.
- LG Energy Solution manufactures products across multiple U.S. sites for the electric vehicle, energy storage, mobility, and IT device sectors.
- LG Energy Solution Vertech was formed in 2022 following its acquisition of NEC Energy Solutions and operates as a system integrator.
- LG Energy Solution maintains a major manufacturing partnership with General Motors.
- A deal for LG Energy Solution to supply batteries to Ford Motor Company fell through in late 2025 when Ford discontinued its F-150 Lightning EV truck.
