# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Goodie Nation announced the Goodie Nation Product Strategy Sprint Series in collaboration with Porsche Cars North America, Inc. on Friday, November 19th, 2021.
- The initiative is a 90-day virtual program where management and related functional experts from Porsche will advise 20 founders around creating enterprise products.
- Joey Womack, CEO of Goodie Nation, stated the goal is to uplift the next generation of social entrepreneurs and diverse founders as they design product experiences.
- Womack expressed thrill at having Porsche's dynamic support in guiding founders through developing what's next.
- The Sprint Series targets startup companies at the beginning of their journey or seeking to reach the next level in their product roadmap.
- Eligible startup spaces for participation include Customer Experience, Future of Work, Logistics & Mobility, Supply Chain & Manufacturing, Big Data/Analytics & Security, and Climate Tech & Sustainability.
- Participants will experience virtual lunches with Porsche, weekly accountability group huddles, office hours, storytelling help from Goodie Nation, and invitations to appear on Goodie Nation podcast episodes.
- Kjell Gruner, President and CEO of Porsche Cars North America, Inc., stated that innovation is a fundamental part of the Porsche DNA.
- Gruner expressed excitement to work with Goodie Nation and the select group of founders as they take bold steps to grow their businesses.
- Together, Porsche and Goodie Nation hope to inspire participants to embody the same entrepreneurial spirit that Porsche was founded upon as they take their companies to the next level.
- Previous Goodie Nation mentorship programs have included collaborations with companies such as Verizon, AARP, and Deloitte.
- Womack added that Goodie Nation works to bring relationships of expertise to founders to accelerate business.
- Womack noted that these intensives provide tremendous value, insights, learnings, and connections for founders.
- Applications for the program are due by December 10.
