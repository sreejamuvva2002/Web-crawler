# Great Dane Trailers

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-01

## Facts

- Great Dane Trailers is one of the leading manufacturers of trailers in North America.
- The company has a long and rich history, dating back over 120 years.
- Great Dane Trailers was founded in 1900 in Savannah, Georgia, by J.P. Wheless and T.H. McMillan.
- The company started as a small repair shop for horse-drawn wagons before growing into a manufacturer of high-quality wagons and carriages.
- In the 1920s, Great Dane began to transition to the production of trailers for the transportation industry.
- The company quickly gained a reputation for building durable, reliable trailers that could withstand the rigors of long-haul transportation.
- During the 1930s and 1940s, Great Dane introduced refrigerated trailers for transporting perishable goods and livestock trailers for transporting animals.
- During World War II, Great Dane produced trailers for the military to transport troops, supplies, and equipment across the United States and around the world.
- During the 1950s and 1960s, Great Dane introduced flatbed trailers for transporting heavy equipment and bulk commodity trailers for transporting grain and other agricultural products.
- In the mid-1960s, Great Dane merged with Pines Trailer Corporation, another leading trailer manufacturer, to expand its manufacturing capabilities and offer a wider range of products.
- During the 1970s and 1980s, Great Dane introduced double-deck trailers for transporting automobiles and intermodal trailers for transporting shipping containers.
- In the late 1980s, Great Dane was acquired by the Chicago-based investment firm CC Industries to continue investing in new technology and expand its product line.
- During the 1990s and 2000s, Great Dane introduced lightweight trailers for increased fuel efficiency and trailers with advanced safety features.
- In 2007, Great Dane was acquired by the private equity firm Oak Hill Capital Partners to continue investing in new technology and expand its manufacturing capabilities.
- Today, Great Dane is one of the largest manufacturers of trailers in North America, focusing on quality and reliability for the transportation industry.
