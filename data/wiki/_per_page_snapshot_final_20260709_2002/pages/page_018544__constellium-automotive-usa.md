# Constellium Automotive USA

## Facts

- Constellium Automotive USA in White, Georgia will reduce its workforce.
- The workforce reduction affects dozens of local families.
- The layoffs are expected to impact many hardworking families throughout Bartow County and neighboring communities.
- Dr. Raj Miniyar serves as President and CEO of Dr. Miniyar’s Pediatrics, P.C.
- Dr. Miniyar announced that his practice will provide free pediatric healthcare services to children of employees who lose their jobs as a result of the layoffs.
- The community assistance initiative aims to ensure children continue to have access to quality healthcare during the financial uncertainty that often follows job loss.
- Eligible children of employees directly affected by the Constellium layoffs will be able to receive free pediatric office visits through Dr. Miniyar’s Pediatrics for a designated period.
- Dr. Miniyar’s Pediatrics has served Northwest Georgia families for more than two decades.
- The practice maintains offices in Rome, Cartersville, Cedartown, and Trion.
- Families do not need to provide paperwork from their employer or proof of layoff to access the free pediatric services.
