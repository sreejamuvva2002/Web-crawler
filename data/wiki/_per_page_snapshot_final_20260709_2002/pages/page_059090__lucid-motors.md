## Facts

- Lucid's year-over-year production rose 55% in 2025.
- Lucid's revenue rose 68% to nearly $1.4 billion in 2025.
- Lucid has had significant losses for years and a constant need for additional capital infusions.
- Lucid's GAAP net loss was $12.09 per share in 2025.
- Lucid received a combined $1 billion investment from a Saudi Arabian Public Investment Fund affiliate and Uber Technologies.
- The Saudi public fund owns an estimated 57% stake in Lucid.
- Lucid has made several additional public offerings of common stock, which have diluted existing shareholder value multiple times.
- Lucid is currently on its third CEO in just four years.
- Lucid's current CEO is Silvio Napoli, who took the helm in June and previously ran the Schindler Group.
- Silvio Napoli cut 18% of Lucid's workforce to reduce costs by $158 million.
- Lucid laid off 12% of its workforce in February prior to the June cuts.
- Lucid has production issues for its recently launched Gravity SUV.
- Lucid has announced lower-priced models, but production has not started yet.
