# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-25

**Source:** [Kia Launches Sportage Hybrid Production at Georgia Metaplant](https://autoworldjournal.com/kia-launches-sportage-hybrid-production/)  ·  `page_010110`

## Facts

- Kia America announced the commencement of production of its Sportage Hybrid SUV at Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) in Georgia.
- The Sportage Hybrid is the first hybrid vehicle to be manufactured at the new Georgia facility.
- The Georgia facility began producing electric vehicles in early 2025.
- The launch is regarded as a significant advancement for the Hyundai, Kia, and Genesis brands.
- The launch underscores the plant's role as a transformative hub in Hyundai Motor Group's electrification strategy.
- Kia stated that the Sportage Hybrid is the first hybrid-electric vehicle (HEV) to be manufactured at the facility.
- The facility was initially established for the production of battery electric vehicles (BEVs) under an EV-focused strategy.
- Kia operates a dedicated plant in West Point, Georgia.
- The West Point plant assembles the Telluride SUV, the EV9 and EV6 BEVs, and internal combustion engine (ICE) variants of the Sportage and Sorento SUVs.
- The Metaplant produces the Hyundai Ioniq 5 and Hyundai Ioniq 9 electric vehicles for Hyundai Motor Group.
- The facility was designed with the capacity to manufacture up to ten different models across Hyundai Motor Group's three brands.
- The facility's design potentially includes capacity for future body-on-frame vehicles, extended-range hybrid systems, and robotics applications.
- U.S.-built versions of the Sportage Hybrid are scheduled to be available from the 2027 model year.
- The U.S.-built Sportage Hybrid is expected to reach dealerships in the summer.
- The Metaplant will handle production of all Sportage Hybrid variants, including LX, S, EX, X-Line, and SX-Prestige trims.
- The 2027 Kia Sportage Hybrid is powered by a 1.6-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with a 1.49 kWh lithium-ion polymer battery.
- Together with its compact electric motor, the Sportage Hybrid's powertrain produces a combined output of 232 horsepower and 271 lb-ft (367 Nm) of torque.
- The 2027 Kia Sportage Hybrid is offered in both front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations.
- Kia emphasized that launching Sportage Hybrid production in Savannah expands its portfolio of SUVs manufactured for the U.S. market.
- The launch further supports Kia's broader strategy of accelerating electrification across its vehicle lineup.
