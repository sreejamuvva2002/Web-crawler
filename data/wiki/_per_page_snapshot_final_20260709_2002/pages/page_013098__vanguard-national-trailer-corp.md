## Facts

- Monon, Ind.-based Vanguard National Trailer Corp. will build a $30 million plant in Trenton, Ga., creating 400 jobs.
- Vanguard will develop nearly 100 acres of land and build a 350,000-square-foot facility.
- The facility will make dry freight and refrigerated semi-trailer vans for the North American transportation industry.
- Gov. Nathan Deal stated in a statement that they are excited to welcome Vanguard National Trailer Corp. to Dade County.
- Gov. Nathan Deal highlighted the company's decision to establish a manufacturing and assembly plant.
