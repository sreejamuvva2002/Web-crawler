# Thomas Built Buses, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-12-08

**Source:** [Thomas Built Buses expands Type A Minotour production capacity at plant in High Point | Daimler Truck North America](https://northamerica.daimlertruck.com/news-stories/2025/thomas-built-buses-expands-type-a-minotour-production-capacity-at-plant-in-high-point)  ·  `page_053616`

## Facts

- Thomas Built Buses (TBB), a division of Daimler Truck Specialty Vehicles (DTSV), gave an update on the expansion of its Type A Minotour production capacity at its legacy manufacturing plant located at 1408 Courtesy Road in High Point, N.C.
- The expansion builds on Thomas Built’s legacy in High Point, home to the company’s original facility dating back to 1916, where it first produced streetcars before evolving to build Type C, Type D and now Type A buses.
- The expansion reflects Thomas Built’s streamlined production strategy following the relocation of Type D production to the Saf-T-Liner plant earlier in 2025.
- Work on the Type A expansion began in late 2024 and is expected to be completed by 2026.
- T.J. Reed, president and CEO of DTSV, stated that investing in the legacy facility honors more than a century of craftsmanship in High Point while continuing to modernize for the future.
- The expansion includes a series of modernization efforts aimed at improving workflow, enhancing safety and supporting long-term sustainability.
- The project will increase production capacity by more than 50 percent, strengthening Thomas Built's operational efficiency and supporting continued growth in North Carolina.
- Dedicated space for Minotour production has nearly tripled, now encompassing roughly 72,000 square feet of the building.
- Jeff Allen, senior vice president of operations and specialty vehicles at Daimler Truck North America (DTNA), stated that the expansion preserves the company's people, community, and commitment to building safe, reliable buses for students everywhere.
- DTNA aims to strengthen its operations in North Carolina while ensuring it can serve more customers with greater efficiency, sustainability and innovation.
- Thomas Built Buses was founded in 1916 and is headquartered in High Point, North Carolina.
- Thomas Built Buses is a subsidiary of Daimler Truck North America LLC, which designs, engineers, manufactures and markets medium- and heavy-duty trucks, school buses, vehicle chassis and their associated technologies and components under the Freightliner, Western Star, Thomas Built Buses, Freightliner Custom Chassis Corp and Detroit brands.
- Thomas Built Buses and Freightliner Custom Chassis Corp together form Daimler Truck Specialty Vehicles.
- Daimler Truck North America is a subsidiary of Daimler Truck, one of the world’s leading commercial vehicle manufacturers.
