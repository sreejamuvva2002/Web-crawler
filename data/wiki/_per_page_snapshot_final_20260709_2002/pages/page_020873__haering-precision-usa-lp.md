## Facts

- Haering Precision USA LP is a leading manufacturer of high-quality automotive components.
- The company took part in National Manufacturing Week by hosting and participating in key events that emphasize the value of manufacturing and its connection to education, innovation, and community engagement.
- As part of its commitment to inspiring the next generation of manufacturing professionals, Haering Precision actively welcomes students for exclusive plant tours.
- During plant tours, students explore the company’s cutting-edge production processes, interact with skilled professionals, and see firsthand the technology behind the manufacturing of automotive components.
- The visits aim to expose students to the vast possibilities of a career in manufacturing and highlight the role of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) in shaping the industry’s future.
- Dustin Chambers, Commercial Plant Manager, stated that engaging with students at this stage is critical to building a strong pipeline of future talent.
- Haering Precision participated in Camp STEMPowerment, an empowering program hosted by North Georgia Technical College specifically designed for middle school girls to explore STEM fields through interactive workshops and real-world applications.
- The company’s involvement in Camp STEMPowerment underscores its dedication to fostering diversity and inclusion in STEM-related careers.
- Nathan Lindler, Technical Plant Manager at Haering Precision, stated that seeing students dive into STEM with enthusiasm is a reminder of why the company invests in education outreach.
- Haering Precision participated in Career Fairs at North Georgia Technical College’s Clarkesville and Currahee campuses to reinforce its leadership in industry collaboration and workforce development.
- The company is dedicated to continued involvement in educational and industry events that promote STEM education, workforce development, and community engagement.
- Haering Precision looks forward to further strengthening its ties with local schools, technical colleges, and industry leaders in the years to come.
