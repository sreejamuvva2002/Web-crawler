# Ascend Elements

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2023-04-01

**Source:** [Ascend Elements Opens North America's Largest Electric Vehicle Battery Recycling Facility in Georgia - AllOnGeorgia](https://allongeorgia.com/georgia-business/ascend-elements-opens-north-americas-largest-electric-vehicle-battery-recycling-facility-in-georgia/)  ·  `page_001456`

## Facts

- Ascend Elements celebrated the grand opening of its first commercial-scale lithium-ion battery recycling facility.
- The facility is located in Covington, Georgia.
- The $50 million Base 1 facility is North America’s largest electric vehicle battery recycling facility.
- Lithium-ion battery recycling is becoming an increasingly important part of the EV battery materials supply chain as demand for electric vehicles increases.
- The facility began partial operations in August 2022.
- The facility now has an annual capacity to process 30,000 metric tons of used lithium-ion batteries and manufacturing scrap.
- This processing capacity is equal to 70,000 electric vehicle batteries per year.
- Ascend Elements CEO Mike O’Kronley stated: “This facility is an important part of America’s EV battery infrastructure, and this is just the start.”
- Mike O’Kronley added: “As an industry, we need to continue building our closed-loop battery materials supply chain to make electric vehicle batteries cleaner and more sustainable.”
- Ascend Elements recovers up to 98% of the critical battery metals in used electric vehicle batteries and gigafactory manufacturing scrap.
- By recycling used lithium-ion batteries and scrap with its patented Hydro-to-Cathode™ process, Ascend Elements can reduce the carbon footprint of new electric vehicle battery cathode materials by up to 90%.
- The facility is powered by renewable energy.
- The facility features onsite wastewater recycling and treatment systems.
- The facility currently employs about 100 team members.
- Ascend Elements plans to increase headcount to 185 by 2024.
- Ascend Elements is based in Westborough, Massachusetts.
- Ascend Elements is the leading provider of sustainable, closed-loop battery material solutions.
- Ascend Elements produces lithium-ion battery precursor (pCAM) and cathode active materials (CAM) at a commercial scale.
- Ascend Elements' Hydro-to-Cathode™ direct precursor synthesis technology produces new CAM from spent lithium-ion cells more efficiently than traditional methods.
- This technology results in improved economics and lowered greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions.
- The company's operations aim to reduce batteries going to landfill and create a cleaner manufacturing process for the lithium-ion battery industry.
