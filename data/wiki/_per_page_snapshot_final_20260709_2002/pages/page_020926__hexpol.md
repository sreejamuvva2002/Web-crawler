# Hexpol

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-01

## Facts

- HEXPOL is involved in advanced polymer compounding, specializing in the development and manufacture of custom polymer compounds for various industries.
- The company provides a variety of compounding solutions including rubber, thermoplastic elastomer (TPE), and thermoplastic compounds, as well as engineered products like gaskets, seals, and wheels.
- HEXPOL serves sectors such as automotive, construction, healthcare, energy, industrial, and oil and gas.
- It was founded in 1893 and is based in Malmo, Sweden.
- On November 1, 2024, Hexpol acquired an 80 percent stake in nylon compounder Piedmont Resin Supply LLC in a deal valued at US$86 million.
- Piedmont Resin Supply LLC is headquartered in Cartersville, Georgia.
- Piedmont was founded in 2013 and has grown to become one of the largest independent nylon compounders in the U.S., with approximately 60 employees.
- Piedmont services a variety of customers within automotive, transportation, and the furniture industries.
- Jan Wikström, president of Hexpol Thermoplastic Compounding, stated that the acquisition adds new capabilities, application knowhow, and a new customer base to Hexpol Thermoplastic Compounding in the U.S.
- Hexpol is headquartered in Malmo, Sweden, and is organized into two business areas: Hexpol Compounding and Hexpol Engineered Products.
- Hexpol has acquired 19 companies total, with its latest acquisition being Piedmont Resin Supply on October 31, 2024.
- On March 3, 2023, Hexpol partnered with IFS for an upgrade implementation project overseen by IFS's partner Addovation.
