# Woory Industrial Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-07

**Source:** ["More Customers": Business Owners react to new manufacturing facility headed to Dublin](https://wgxa.tv/news/local/more-customers-business-owners-react-to-new-manufacturing-facility-headed-to-dublin)  ·  `page_015511`

## Facts

- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp announced plans for a new automotive parts manufacturing facility in Dublin.
- The investment from Woory Industrial Company will bring one hundred and thirty new jobs to Laurens County.
- Woory specializes in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning components for the automotive industry.
- Woory will be hiring for a variety of positions at the new facility.
- The company plans to place an existing manufacturing building back into service.
- Miller Soul Food co-owner Shenita Brown says the facility will bring more customers to her business, which has been open since 1955.
- Co-owner Rodney Brown believes the investment will increase the tax base and bring in new revenue.
- Rodney Brown believes the project opens the door for young adults to take technical classes that can land them a job.
- Local business owner Maggie Gilbert states that people need good-paying jobs to support their families.
- Dublin Mayor Joshua Kight issued a statement welcoming Woory and noting its commitment promotes infill development while creating new career paths for the community.
