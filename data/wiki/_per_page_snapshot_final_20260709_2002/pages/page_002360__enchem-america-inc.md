## Facts

- EnChem America, Inc. is a U.S. corporation engaged in the development, manufacture, and sale of battery electrolyte materials.
- EnChem America, Inc. is a U.S. subsidiary of EnChem Co., Ltd., a corporation organized under the laws of the Republic of Korea.
- EnChem America is proposing a reverse merger / reverse take over (RTO) transaction with The Growhub Limited (TGHL).
- The transaction will be structured as a reverse triangular merger where EnChem America becomes the surviving corporation and a wholly-owned subsidiary of TGHL.
- The Private Company shall be treated as the accounting acquiror for financial reporting purposes, and the Transaction shall be accounted for as a reverse merger under U.S. GAAP.
- The agreed equity value of EnChem America for purposes of the Merger is a minimum of USD 400,000,000.
- Pre-Closing equity holders of EnChem America will receive Merger Shares constituting 85% of the equity of TGHL on a fully diluted basis immediately following Closing.
- Pre-Closing equity holders of TGHL will retain 15% of the equity of TGHL on a fully diluted basis immediately following Closing.
- Post-Closing ownership allocation shows EnChem at 85.0% and TGHL at 15.0%.
- Total pro forma shares will be 168,147,986, comprising 142,848,176 TGHL Existing shares and 25,299,810 Merger Shares.
