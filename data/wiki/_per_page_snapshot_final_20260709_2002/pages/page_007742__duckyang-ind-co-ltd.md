# Duckyang Ind. Co. Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-05-24

**Source:** [S. Korean-based company to open first-of-its kind manufacturing plant - LocalNews8.com - KIFI](https://localnews8.com/news/2021/05/24/s-korean-based-company-to-open-first-of-its-kind-manufacturing-plant/)  ·  `page_007742`

## Facts

- Duckyang, a South Korean-based company, will open its first U.S. manufacturing facility in Georgia.
- The company is an electric parts supplier of automotive battery modules.
- Governor Brian Kemp announced that Duckyang will invest $10 million to open the manufacturing plant in Jackson County.
- The investment is expected to create an estimated 285 jobs.
- The new facility will be 230,000 square feet.
- The facility will be located in Braselton at 984 Broadway Avenue.
- Duckyang is looking to hire for production, maintenance, logistics, and warehouse management positions.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that the project creates high-paying, advanced automotive manufacturing jobs for Georgians in Jackson County.
- Governor Kemp noted that Georgia has the best workforce training programs in the nation and has remained the number one state for business for the past eight years.
- Duckyang supplies cockpit modules to customers worldwide, including Hyundai, Kia, Renault Samsung Motors, Genesis, and Mobis.
- Cockpit modules are auto parts made of more than 130 subcomponents found near the driver’s seat of a vehicle.
- The new Georgia facility will allow Duckyang to better serve automotive customers within the region.
- Dong-in Son, CEO of Duckyang Ind. Co. Ltd., stated that by entering the U.S. electric vehicle battery market with SKBA, the company will cooperate with other automotive companies like Hyundai and Kia Motors that already have a U.S. manufacturing base.
- Dong-in Son expected business expansion opportunities to supply electric vehicle parts and interior parts, including cockpit modules, door trim panel modules, and other flagship parts, to global automakers based in the United States.
