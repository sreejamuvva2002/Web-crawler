# OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [OTR Wheel Engineering Announces Port Wheel Inspection Program with Taylor Machine Works (TMW) - EIN Presswire](https://www.einnews.com/pr_news/596982644/otr-wheel-engineering-announces-port-wheel-inspection-program-with-taylor-machine-works-tmw)  ·  `page_058418`

## Facts

- OTR Wheel Engineering is collaborating with Taylor Machine Works (TMW) to launch a port wheel inspection program.
- The collaboration facilitates on-site wheel inspection at major U.S. ports and provides critical safety enhancement.
- OTR is expanding this program to its customer base and partners in Europe.
- Through the expansion, OTR will leverage its footprint and staff to replicate the same process being implemented in the U.S. globally.
- The program offers proper de-mounting and installation of OEM-approved wheels.
- Michael Stoeckel, Global Vice President, Sales & Marketing at OTR, describes the program as game-changing and notes it provides critical service to ports across the U.S. and soon globally.
- Charles Jackson, senior manager and sponsor of OTR’s program with TMW, explains that they inspect, diagnose, and confirm the condition of the wheel, its duty cycle, and the application.
- If a wheel has a stress crack, it can be visually seen or detected using a proprietary process.
- If a wheel is approaching end of life, OTR will inform the port staff accordingly.
- OTR recommends an OEM-approved replacement wheel allocated from its in-house manufactured stock to ensure safe and continuous vehicle operation at the ports.
- OTR will exhibit at Bauma from October 24-28, 2022, at Booth 430 in Hall A6, where a 10-bar OTR port wheel will be displayed.
- Staff will be on-site at Bauma to explain how the program can be implemented in additional locations worldwide.
- OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc. is a market-leading global enterprise specializing in innovative off-the-road tire and wheel solutions for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and aftermarket customers.
- OTR provides value-added services including tire mounting, sequencing, warehousing, and subassembly for strategic OEM and aftermarket partners.
- OTR serves the Construction, Lawn & Garden, Powersports, Agriculture, Forestry, Mining, Material Handling, and Specialty Vehicle markets.
- OTR’s infrastructure comprises 34 manufacturing facilities and warehouse locations throughout the U.S., Canada, Europe, and Asia to support localization and velocity in the supply chain.
