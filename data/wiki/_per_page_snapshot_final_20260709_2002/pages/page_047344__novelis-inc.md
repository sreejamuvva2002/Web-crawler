# Novelis Inc.

## Facts

- Novelis considers itself a global leader in the production of innovative, sustainable aluminum products and solutions and the world's largest recycler of aluminum.
- Novelis aims to be the leading provider of low-carbon, sustainable aluminum solutions and achieve a fully circular economy by partnering with suppliers and customers in beverage packaging, automotive, aerospace, and specialties markets throughout North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- Novelis has recycling operations in many of its plants to recycle both post-consumer and post-industrial aluminum.
- During fiscal 2025, Novelis had total shipment volumes of 3,757 kt and net sales of $17.1 billion.
- Novelis Inc. headquarters is located at 3550 Peachtree Road NE, Suite 1100, Atlanta, GA 30326.
- Megan Cochard serves as Head of Treasury and Investor Relations for Novelis.
- Julie Groover is Senior Director of Corporate Communications for Novelis.
- Karen Judd is Senior Manager of External Communications for Novelis North America.
