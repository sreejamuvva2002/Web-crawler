# Sewon America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-01

**Source:** [New $50M plant expansion brings 100 jobs, boosts EV industry in Effingham County](https://www.wtoc.com/2026/05/01/new-50m-plant-expansion-brings-100-jobs-boosts-ev-industry-effingham-county/)  ·  `page_001370`

## Facts

- ARCO Design/Build has completed a 100,000-square-foot expansion for Sewon America in Rincon, Effingham County.
- The project is valued at $50 million.
- The expansion is expected to bring approximately 100 new jobs to the area.
- The project supports the production of automotive components tied to electric vehicles.
- Sewon America is a subsidiary of South Korea-based Sewon Precision Industry.
- Sewon America manufactures automotive body components and supplies parts for major manufacturers, including Hyundai Motor Group’s nearby Metaplant.
- The facility was completed on an accelerated timeline, allowing operations and equipment to begin moving in ahead of full completion.
- Local leaders say the project highlights rapid growth along the Savannah industrial corridor.
- Brandt Herndon with the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority stated that the project strengthens the region's position in the electric vehicle supply chain while bringing quality jobs and long-term opportunity to the community.
- The expansion reflects increasing demand across Georgia for manufacturing facilities that can be built quickly and efficiently to support expanding electric vehicle production.
