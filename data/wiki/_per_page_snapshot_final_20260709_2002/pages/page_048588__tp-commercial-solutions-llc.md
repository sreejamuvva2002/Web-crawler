# TP Commercial Solutions LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-03-24

**Source:** [Pirelli Unveils Product Lines For North America At MATS 2017](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/pirelli-unveils-product-lines-north-america-mats-2017/)  ·  `page_048588`

## Facts

- Pirelli brand is previewing its new TP Commercial Solutions program at the 2017 Mid America Trucking Show (MATS), the largest annual heavy-duty trucking show in the world.
- TP Commercial Solutions is ready to launch the sales and marketing of the Pirelli Brand of radial truck tires, agricultural tires and off-the road tires along with the Formula brand in the U.S. and Canada.
- Clif Armstrong, president of TP Commercial Solutions, announced that the company has the start of a solid product line-up now available for sale that upholds Pirelli’s iconic brand values and quality after exhaustive testing.
- The products underwent a rigorous R&D and testing process with multiple fleets in multiple applications to develop tires for the specific needs of the U.S market.
- Pentathlon D is a premium long haul drive tire that is SmartWay verified, offering extremely high-mileage, and thanks to EcoImpact features, fuel savings and low emission benefits.
- G:85 Series for On/Off Road includes FG:85 and TG:85 tires specifically designed for the steer and drive on/off road application.
- FH:01 Motor Coach is designed for the demanding bus fleet market for long haul and regional all position use.
- Formula Driver II All Position rib is part of the Formula Brand, a Pirelli Tier II brand that will be launched in the U.S./Canada, starting with limited sizes and then expanding to a full product line.
- TP Commercial Solutions supplies commercial truck tires and fleet solutions with the Pirelli brand to the global market.
- TP Commercial Solutions LLC maintains the renowned quality and heritage of the Pirelli family dedicated to the North American market.
- TP Commercial Solutions LLC headquarters are in Rome, Georgia.
- Pirelli has been a world leader in tire technology for over 140 years.
