# Seojin Global

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-02-20

**Source:** [Lee & Associates Represents Seojin Global in 222,618 SF Lease in Braselton, Georgia - Atlanta](https://www.lee-associates.com/atlanta/2026/02/20/lee-associates-represents-seojin-global-in-222618-sf-lease-in-braselton-georgia/)  ·  `page_001711`

## Facts

- Seojin Global secured a 222,618-square-foot industrial lease at 984 Broadway Ave. in Braselton, Georgia.
- The company occupies the full Building 3 at 85 Braselton Logistics Center for a new distribution operation serving the greater Atlanta region.
- Seojin Global manufactures aluminum cases and cabinet systems used across telecom, server, automotive, and energy storage applications.
- Seojin Global operates as the U.S. subsidiary of South Korea-based Seojin System.
- Craig Viergever, SIOR, of Lee & Associates – Atlanta and Jason Um of Lee & Associates – Detroit represented the tenant in the transaction.
- Nathan Anderson and Darren Butler of NAI Brannen Goddard represented the landlord.
- The lease was recognized as one of CoStar’s Power Broker Awards – Q4 2025 Top Industrial Leases.
- The transaction reflects continued activity from international manufacturing groups expanding their logistics footprint within the Northeast Atlanta industrial corridor.
