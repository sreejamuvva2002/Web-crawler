# Job Tax Credits

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Job Tax Credits | Georgia Department of Community Affairs](https://dca.georgia.gov/financing-tools/incentives/job-tax-credits)  ·  `page_002489`

## Facts

- Provides for a statewide job tax credit for any business or headquarters of any such business engaged in manufacturing, warehousing and distribution, processing, telecommunications, tourism, research and development industries or services for the elderly and persons with disabilities.
- Explicitly excludes retail businesses from eligibility for the program.
