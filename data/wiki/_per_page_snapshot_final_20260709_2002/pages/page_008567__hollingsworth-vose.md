# Hollingsworth & Vose

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-01-03

**Source:** [Hollingsworth & Vose expanding Floyd County operation, creating 25 new jobs](https://augustafreepress.com/news/hollingsworth-vose-expanding-floyd-county-operation-creating-25-new-jobs/)  ·  `page_008567`

## Facts

- A Massachusetts-based manufacturer is committing $40.2 million to expand its Floyd County operation, creating 25 new jobs at the location.
- Hollingsworth & Vose is a global manufacturer of advanced materials used in filtration, battery, and industrial applications.
- The family-owned company traces its history back to a 1728 legislative act encouraging paper production and has evolved over nearly 300 years to focus on materials for filters (clean rooms, hospitals, computers, commercial buildings, homes, cars, trucks, heavy duty equipment) and batteries used mainly in telecom and hybrid vehicle applications.
- The expansion in Floyd County will add 28,000 square feet to the H&V facility at 365 Christiansburg Pike Northeast to accommodate new production equipment to meet increased demand.
- H&V currently employs more than 200 workers in Floyd County.
- Josh Ayer, CEO of H&V, stated the company has been part of the Floyd, Va., community since 1976 and considers the facility essential to serving global and domestic customers.
- The company chose Virginia for this expansion due to its positive business environment and strong support from the Commonwealth of Virginia and Floyd County.
- The Virginia Economic Development Partnership worked with Floyd County on the economic incentives package.
- Gov. Glenn Youngkin approved a $558,700 grant from the Commonwealth’s Opportunity Fund as part of that incentive package.
- Gov. Glenn Youngkin stated that Hollingsworth & Vose has generated positive economic impact and job opportunities in Floyd County for over four decades, and this investment further solidifies the company's commitment to Virginia.
- Gov. Glenn Youngkin noted that businesses with a long history of expansion in the Commonwealth offer powerful testimonials on why a Virginia location is a foundation for success, expressing a desire for continued partnership with H&V.
