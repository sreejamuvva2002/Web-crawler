# Tie Down Manufacturing Ingenuity

## Facts

- Tie Down Manufacturing Ingenuity was founded in 1969 and has been in business for over 50 years.
- The company currently holds over 80 patents.
- Tie Down Engineering employs a talented team of engineers that enable the business to succeed.
- The management team includes alumni from MIT and Georgia Tech.
- Tie Down was founded in Atlanta, Georgia, and operates under the mantra of "Made in the USA."
- The company's operations run over 500,000 square feet of ISO-9001 facilities in Atlanta.
- Tie Down's quality control equipment ranges from UV testing, accelerated weathering, laser QC, and tube inspection machines.
- For over 50 years, Tie Down continues to invest in high-quality machinery designed to allow total control over quality assurance audits.
- Products are developed via an ISO 9001:2015 certified agile process.
- The company offers integrated project services including feasibility studies, project design assistance, material specifications, and optimum production value engineering.
- Tie Down delivers high-quality manual and robotic welding from MIG welding stations to dual-arm and robotic welding.
- The team can handle any type of weld on parts up to 10,000 lbs.
- Tie Down provides tube forming, bending, and folding applications.
- The company's equipment can manipulate steel with almost 2 million pounds of force.
- Since the early 80's, Tie Down has expanded its capabilities where it can now form steel plates up to 1 thick and up to 24 ft in length.
