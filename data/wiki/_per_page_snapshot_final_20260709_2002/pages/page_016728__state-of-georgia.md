# State of Georgia

## Facts

- Kemp highlighted Georgia's rising prominence in key industries such as electric mobility during his State of the State address.
- Four of the largest economic development projects in state history were announced in less than 365 days.
- Just those four projects alone will bring over 20,000 new jobs and over $17 billion in investment to rural communities across Georgia.
- Those good-paying jobs are in fields that will define the next generation of manufacturing.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development announced that companies announced more than 17,000 new jobs totaling $13 billion in investment during the second half of 2022.
- 85% of those jobs and investments were located outside of metro Atlanta.
- Kemp reiterated his budget proposals to address workforce housing and job training and recruitment, including fully funding the state's education formula, the post-secondary HOPE Scholarship, and a "rural workforce housing fund."
