# Great Dane Statesboro Manufacturing Plant

## Facts

- Great Dane’s Statesboro, Georgia manufacturing plant recently produced its 25,000th trailer since opening in 2012.
- The facility uniquely builds 57-ft. trailers for freight and refrigerated transport applications.
- The Statesboro facility is the only Great Dane plant capable of producing trailers of this length.
- The milestone order was a 57-ft. Everest reefer built for customer Nolt Transportation.
- Nolt Transportation utilizes these trailers for refrigerated hauling operations, including produce shipments to central Pennsylvania and temperature-controlled freight to the Pacific Northwest.
- Ron Jones serves as the plant manager at the Great Dane Statesboro facility.
- Plant manager Ron Jones stated that building high-quality trailers and reaching this milestone takes hard work, skill, and collaboration from every person on the factory floor.
