# Woodbridge Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Coverstock Headliner Foam | Woodbridge](https://www.woodbridgegroup.com/Products/Coverstock-Headliner-Foam)  ·  `page_015327`

## Facts

- Woodbridge Group manufactures Strata-Plush™ coverstock headliner foams designed for wet and dry headliner forming and various soft-trim applications.
- Strata-Plush products are available in flat and round blocks and polyether and polyester foam grades.
- The foaming processes are finely tuned for density, firmness, cell size, cell structure, and airflow to provide outstanding performance.
- Applications for the headliner foams include headliners, sun visors, door trim, and interior systems.
- Products offer a 50” (1.27M) diameter round block option.
- Products provide 300+ linear yard rolls without foam join seams.
- Formulations deliver superior product consistency for cell structure and visual appearance.
- Foams are formulated for crisp definition, superior craftsmanship, and designed to recover post forming.
- High firmness and crushable options are available.
- Thin-gauge thickness capability is 1.65mm.
- The foams have excellent acoustical properties and a best-in-class footprint for customer value.
- Typical density ranges from 1.6-2.2 lb./ft3 or 25.5-35.25 kg/m3.
- Firmness range is 40-100 (25% IFD lb./50 in2).
- Thicknesses range from 065-0.275 inches or 1.65-7mm.
- Chemistry consists of polyether and polyester open-cell polyurethane.
- Pour types include round block and flat block.
- Roll fabrication methods include looping and peeling.
- Lamination options include PE film, PU film, adhesive films and webs, textile scrims, and non-woven scrims.
- Other key options include a variety of colors, FR/non-FR, acoustics, airflow, and low emission (VOCs and odor).
- Strata-Plush headliner foams are designed to meet most OEM specifications and requirements.
- Foams meet Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard FMVSS302.
- Foams meet General Motors specifications GMW15058, GM6291M, GM6083, and GM2101M.
- Foams meet Ford specifications WSS-99P48, WSS-M2D243, WSS-M2D491, and ESB-M4D113.
- Foams meet FCA specifications MSAY309 and CS-13398.
- Foams meet Toyota Motor Company specification TSM6712G.
- Foams meet Nissan specification NESM8047.
- Foams meet Tesla specification TM-4001.
- Foams meet Honda specification HES C209.
- Foams meet Hyundai Kia specification MS-300-34.
- Foams meet Daimler specifications DBL5450, DBL5454, and DBL5438.
- Foams meet Volkswagen specification VW50180.
- Product variants include RB, FB, HD, LV, LF, and SF, available in Ether or Ester chemistry with standard, low, or high definition variations.
