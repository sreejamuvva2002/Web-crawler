# Doowon Climate Control America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-02-06

**Source:** [Second Automotive Supplier Creating 200 New Jobs in Candler County | Georgia](https://georgia.org/press-release/second-automotive-supplier-creating-200-new-jobs-candler-county)  ·  `page_000051`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Doowon Climate Control America, Inc. will invest more than $30 million in a new manufacturing facility in Metter.
- The investment will create 200 new jobs in Candler County.
- This marks the second automotive supplier announced in Candler County in less than six months.
- In fiscal year 2023 alone, 82 percent of new jobs created and more than $20 billion of investments went to communities outside the metro Atlanta area.
- Doowon Group was established in Korea in 1974 and specializes in designing and manufacturing automotive climate control systems.
- The company will be a key supplier for Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia (Kia Georgia) and Hyundai Motor Group.
- Ki Chun Sung is the CEO of Doowon Climate Control America, Inc.
- Doowon Climate Control America, Inc. is establishing itself as Doowon Group’s inaugural automotive air conditioning system production facility in the United States.
- The new manufacturing facility will be located in the Metter-Candler County I-16 Industrial Park.
- The site is a “Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development” (GRAD) certified site.
- The facility will primarily produce climate control systems for use in traditional and electric vehicles.
- Operations are projected to begin in 2026.
- Doowon Climate Control America, Inc. plans to hire injection molding technicians, assembly operators, quality materials and production specialists, engineers, and management personnel.
- Metter Mayor Ed Boyd welcomed Doowon Climate Control America, Inc. as the newest corporate citizen in Metter and Candler County.
- Glyn Thrift is Chairman of the Candler County Board of Commissioners.
- Director of Project Implementation and Supplier Strategy Alyce Thornhill represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team on this competitive project.
- The project was completed in partnership with the Candler County Industrial Authority, Georgia Power, Georgia Ports Authority, and Georgia Quick Start.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated the City of Metter and Candler County’s intentional investment in site preparation positioned them for success.
- Since 2018, Georgia has pursued the entire EV supply chain, creating more than $25.7 billion in investments and 30,200 jobs.
- In fiscal year 2023, job creation in the automotive industry increased by 324 percent when compared to FY21.
- Suppliers attracted by Georgia’s mobility original equipment manufacturers resulted in over $2 billion in investment across the state in FY23.
- Doowon Group is the ultimate parent entity of Doowon Climate Control America, Inc. and is headquartered in South Korea.
- Doowon Group manages four domestic production plants, a technology research institute, and thirteen overseas production facilities, including operations in the United States.
- Doowon Group sustains a workforce exceeding 3,125 employees and achieved sales revenue surpassing $1.9 billion in fiscal year 2023.
- The company develops and produces air conditioning systems for all types of vehicles, including electric cars, hybrids, and internal combustion engine vehicles.
