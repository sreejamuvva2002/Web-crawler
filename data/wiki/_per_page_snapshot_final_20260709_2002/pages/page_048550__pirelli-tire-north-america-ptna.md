# Pirelli Tire North America (PTNA)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-08-10

## Facts

- On August 10, 2023, Pirelli North America announced a new partnership with The Ray, a non-profit organization based in Atlanta.
- The Ray has helped develop a net-zero highway testbed on an 18-mile stretch of Interstate 85 in West Georgia, located near Pirelli's factory in Rome.
- The partnership aims to advance research and solutions in road safety technology, the circular economy, EV charging infrastructure, renewable energy, and the environmental and social impacts of tires and roads.
- As part of the non-profit’s Advisory Board, Pirelli will contribute to strategic thinking around future roadways.
- Pirelli is investigating future projects with The Ray to research the impact of rubber-modified asphalt.
- Pirelli is also investigating the use of tire-derived aggregate for stormwater galleries.
- The brand is joining a multi-tiered partnership focused on tires and the circular economy to drive education, innovation, solutions development, and sharing of best practices.
- Maureen Kline, Vice President of Public Affairs and Sustainability at Pirelli North America, stated that being part of sustainability solutions extending beyond their product is important to their climate action commitment.
- Kline noted that tires are the only thing between the vehicle and the road, and working with The Ray allows them to identify challenges, develop solutions, and drive real change.
- Pirelli believes tire design will play a key role in shaping the future of mobility and demonstrated its commitment to pursuing increasingly challenging sustainability goals through this partnership.
- The company has set goals to increase the renewable and recycled content of tires and reduce environmental impacts.
- Pirelli is focusing on mitigation strategies for broader product impacts, including end-of-life tires and tire and road wear particles.
- Allie Kelly, Executive Director of The Ray, stated that Pirelli leads from Georgia with an advanced manufacturing facility that was the first FSC-certified tire plant in the world.
- Kelly added that the alignment between the organizations, focused on safety, circularity, and innovation, will power the partnership to produce groundbreaking projects and research in the coming years, benefiting communities across the country.
- Pirelli's Rome, Georgia headquarters includes a Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) facility and a research and development center.
- The Rome location is described as a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant.
- Pirelli Tire North America also operates a manufacturing plant in Silao, Mexico, and sales and marketing offices in New York City, Los Angeles, Detroit, Montreal, and Mexico City.
- The company manufactures, distributes, and markets original equipment and replacement tires for export and domestic car and motorcycle applications.
- Pirelli was founded in Milan in 1872 and produces innovative tires for cars, motorcycles, and bicycles tailored to specific consumer needs through partnerships with prestigious manufacturers.
- The Ray is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit charity and net-zero highway testbed located on 18 miles of Interstate 85 between Lagrange, Georgia, and the Georgia-Alabama state line.
- The highway stretch is named in memory of Ray C. Anderson (1934-2011), a Georgia native and green business leader who challenged Interface, Inc. to pursue a zero environmental footprint.
- The Ray's mission is to reimagine community connections in a way that is safer, more climate-responsive, regenerative to the environment, and capable of creating economic opportunity through innovative transportation infrastructure ideas and technologies.
- The Ray Highway aims to pave the way for a zero carbon, zero waste, zero deaths highway system.
- Current The Ray projects include using highway rights-of-way for solar arrays, cloud-based data management for connected vehicle safety and efficiency, drones used as first responders, pollinator gardens for endangered species, and further deployment of bioswales.
- The Ray defines net-zero transportation as “Zero Deaths, Zero Waste, Zero Carbon and Zero Impact.”
- The organization facilitates building and scaling clean transportation and energy projects with state Departments of Transportation, tollway and turnpike agencies, regional collaboratives, and local governments.
- Kelly stated that the partnership with Pirelli will bring The Ray one step closer to achieving its net-zero transportation goals.
