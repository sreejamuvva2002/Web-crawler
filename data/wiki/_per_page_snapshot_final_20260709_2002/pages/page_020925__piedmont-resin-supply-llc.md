## Facts

- Hexpol Group has acquired an 80 percent stake in nylon compounder Piedmont Resin Supply LLC.
- The acquisition deal is valued at US$86 million.
- Piedmont Resin Supply LLC is headquartered in Cartersville, Ga.
- Piedmont was founded in 2013.
- Piedmont has approximately 60 employees.
- Piedmont services a variety of customers within the automotive, transportation, and furniture industries.
- Piedmont is recognized as one of the largest independent nylon compounders in the U.S.
- Jan Wikström serves as the president of Hexpol Thermoplastic Compounding.
- The acquisition adds new capabilities, application knowhow and a new customer base to Hexpol Thermoplastic Compounding in the U.S., according to Jan Wikström.
- The announcement was made in an Oct. 31 news release.
- Hexpol Group is headquartered in Malmo, Sweden.
- Hexpol Group is recognized as the world’s largest rubber compounder.
- Hexpol operates through two business areas: Hexpol Compounding and Hexpol Engineered Products.
