# Ultium Cells

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-07-28

**Source:** [TN Doles Out $108.5M In Economic Development Grants First Half Of 2021 | Tennessee Conservative](https://tennesseeconservativenews.com/tn-doles-out-108-5m-in-economic-development-grants-first-half-of-2021/)  ·  `page_053849`

## Facts

- Tennessee Department of Economic and Community Development awarded more than $108.5 million in FastTrack economic development grants between 51 businesses in the first half of 2021.
- The largest grant was a $60 million award to Ultium Cells for its Spring Hill plant project.
- Ultium Cells is making a $2.3 billion investment to create 1,300 jobs at its 2.8-million-square-foot facility in Spring Hill.
- The Spring Hill plant will manufacture vehicle battery cells.
- The $60 million grant amounts to more than $46,000 per job created for the Ultium Cells project.
- This per-job incentive exceeds the previous high of $21,500 per job, which was offered to Denso Manufacturing for its 2017 expansion in Blount County.
- TNECD Commissioner Bob Rolfe stated that the investment supports advanced manufacturing efforts and strengthens Tennessee's position as the leader of the electric vehicle industry in the Southeast.
- At least 16 projects received at least $1 million in FastTrack grants during the first half of 2021.
- FastTrack grants are awarded for job training, infrastructure development, or from a special fund covering relocating/purchasing equipment, building repairs/improvements, and temporary office space related to relocation or expansion.
- These economic development funds are reserved for exceptional circumstances where they will make a proportionally significant economic impact on the affected community.
- TNECD typically announces a new project with its investment size and scope, then adds the FastTrack grant incentive amount to the department’s database within 15-30 days.
- Microvast Power Solutions received a $3 million FastTrack grant in Montgomery County for battery technology operations.
- PUREGraphite LLC received a $3 million FastTrack grant in Hamilton County for graphite and battery material operations.
