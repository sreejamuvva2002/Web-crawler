# Lund International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2005-01-26

**Source:** [Lund International Announces Warehouse Consolidation - AftermarketNews](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/lund-international-announces-warehouse-consolidation/)  ·  `page_045611`

## Facts

- Announced plans to consolidate the warehousing functions of its Lund Accessories and Ventshade Accessories Divisions.
- Forming a task force to direct the consolidation project.
- Entered into a long-term lease for a 200,000 square foot warehouse in Lawrenceville, Ga.
- The new warehouse is located next to the Ventshade Accessories Division manufacturing plant.
- Plans to modify the structures to create direct access between the two buildings.
- Expects the new facility to be operational by January 2006.
- Relocating certain administrative functions currently housed at the Lund Accessories Division and Ventshade Accessories Division to an enlarged corporate office in Duluth, GA.
