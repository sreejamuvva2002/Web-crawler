- Morgan Corp. maintains offices in Spartanburg, Charlotte, Savannah, and Raleigh to serve the entire Southeast region.
- The company focuses on safety, people, and relationships, maintaining a competitively low incident rate, low EMR, and extensive work hours without a lost-time injury.
- Morgan Corp. fosters a culture of integrity, respect, collaboration, teamwork, and purpose, applying innovation and discipline to maximize value.
- The company is recruiting for a Quality Control Inspector position responsible for monitoring and evaluating construction activities to ensure compliance with project specifications, industry standards, and quality requirements.
- The Quality Control Inspector role involves conducting inspections, documenting findings, working with field teams to address deficiencies, reviewing project plans and specifications, identifying construction codes and regulatory requirements, and developing Project Specific Inspection and Test Plans (ITPs).

- The role requires willingness to travel, work outside in various environmental conditions or operating process facilities, and strong computer skills with Word and Excel.
- Employees receive benefits including a 401K with company match, PTO, holidays, paid winter shutdown, professional development and career growth opportunities, and a cell phone allowance.
