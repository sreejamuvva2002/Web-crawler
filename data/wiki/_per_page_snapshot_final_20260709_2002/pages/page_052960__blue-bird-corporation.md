# Blue Bird Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2020-02-29

**Source:** [Sustainable Georgia Roundup - Georgia Trend Magazine](https://www.georgiatrend.com/2020/02/29/sustainable-georgia/)  ·  `page_052960`

## Facts

- Blue Bird Corp. is based in Fort Valley, Georgia.
- The company has more than 100 electric buses on order from school districts throughout the nation.
- Its electric buses have already hit the pavement in California, North Dakota, and Washington.
- On-order buses will transport students in Colorado, New Jersey, New York, and Quebec this year.
- 100% electric-powered school buses often cost two to three times the price of diesel counterparts.
- Interest in electric vehicles has been growing.
- Electric buses have zero emissions, thereby improving air quality.
- Electric buses require less maintenance, which saves school districts time and money.
- Blue Bird has been working on its electric bus technology for more than 25 years.
- The company's current buses have a 120-mile range.
- The buses can be recharged overnight.
