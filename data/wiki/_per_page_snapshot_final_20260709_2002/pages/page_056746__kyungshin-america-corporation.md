# Kyungshin America Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-12-06

**Source:** [Kyungshin America Selects Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub   - Broe Real Estate Group](https://broerealestate.com/kyungshin-america-selects-savannah-gateway-industrial-hub/)  ·  `page_056746`

## Facts

- Global automotive parts supplier Kyungshin America Corporation (KAC) has selected Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub (SGIH) for its new operation.
- SGIH is a 2,600-acre multimodal logistics park located near Georgia’s Port of Savannah.
- KAC is a division of the South Korean Kyungshin Corporation.
- KAC’s new operation will import and distribute automotive components to Hyundai Motor Group’s new $7.5B electric vehicle plant in Savannah.
- KAC’s new operation will also distribute automotive components to the growing automotive manufacturing network throughout the southeastern United States.
- Broe Real Estate Group Senior Vice President Sean Fitzsimmons stated that SGIH provides tremendous geographic and multi-modal benefits for park tenants.
- KAC’s new facility is strategically positioned with the Port of Savannah and the new Hyundai Meta Plan plant, both within 15 miles.
- This positioning creates exceptional supply chain efficiency and connectivity to global markets while leveraging Effingham County’s strong workforce and education system.
- KAC produces wire harnesses for automotive companies and is a certified Tier One supplier to Hyundai.
- KAC’s new facility will be 136,000 SF.
- The facility is expected to open in February.
- The move supports Kyungshin’s continued North American production expansion.
- Effingham County Industrial Development Authority CEO Brandt Herndon welcomed KAC to Georgia and highlighted the dynamic partnerships that will serve Hyundai’s new Georgia production plant.
- SGIH is a public-private partnership between the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority (ECIDA), Broe Real Estate Group, and OmniTRAX.
- SGIH is located 12 miles from the Port of Savannah.
- SGIH provides dual rail access to the Class I rail networks of CSX and Norfolk Southern.
- Broe Real Estate Group acquires, develops and manages commercial real estate assets, including office and industrial properties, medical office buildings and multi-family communities across the country.
- The Broe Group has a 50-year history of value-add real estate investing in Colorado and across the United States.
- OmniTRAX is one of North America’s largest and fastest growing private railroad and transportation management companies, providing transportation and supply chain management services to railroads and ports.
- The Broe Group was founded in 1972 as a private, multi-billion-dollar investment group with diversified holdings in real estate, rail, infrastructure, energy, agriculture, water, healthcare and technology throughout 37 North American states and provinces.
