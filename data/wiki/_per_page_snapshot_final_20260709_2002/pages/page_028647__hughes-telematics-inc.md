# Hughes Telematics Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2007-10-31

**Source:** [Telematics Services Agreement](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1410240/000119312509073864/dex1018.htm)  ·  `page_028647`

## Facts

- The Telematics Services Agreement is dated October 31, 2007, and executed between Hughes Telematics Inc. (HTI) and Mercedes-Benz USA, LLC (MBUSA).
- HTI maintains a principal address at 41 Perimeter Center East, Suite 400, Atlanta, Georgia 30346.
- HTI operates in the business of providing telematics equipment and services.
- MBUSA manufactures, distributes, markets, and sells automobiles throughout the United States and Puerto Rico through its vehicle manufacturing Affiliates.
- HTI will provide PASS Services (personal assistance safety) and MBTA Services to purchasers and lessees of MCG Vehicles and LCT Enabled Vehicles equipped with factory-installed telematics communicators.
- MBUSA will factory-install equipment capable of receiving the telematics services in its vehicles on the terms set forth in the agreement.
- HTI must make its HTI Operational System fully operational and available to all Subscribers not later than the Commencement Date.
- Services will be provided 24 hours a day, seven days a week, irrespective of where the Subscriber Vehicle is located within the Territory (50 states, D.C., and Puerto Rico).
- HTI must ensure that PASS Services and MBTA Services meet or exceed specific service levels in Exhibit B and maintain reasonable competitiveness.
- MBUSA will review HTI's written proposals for additional telematics services on a case-by-case basis in good faith.
- The agreement covers Embedded Cellular Technology Devices installed as original equipment by MBUSA or its vehicle manufacturing Affiliates.
- HTI Telematics Communicators may be provided through one or more Authorized Suppliers approved by MBUSA, with Hughes Network Systems, LLC specifically deemed an Authorized Supplier.
- MCG Vehicles are defined as vehicles manufactured by or for MBUSA during calendar year 2010 or thereafter for sale or lease within the Territory.
- The agreement outlines provisions for testing protocols and acceptance.
- The agreement includes data security and privacy contact point requirements.
- The agreement covers marketing plans, on-site engineering staffing, and access to Daimler premises.
- The agreement establishes milestone requirements, intellectual property ownership rules, indemnification terms, confidentiality obligations, and dispute resolution procedures.
- The agreement includes terms for post-termination transition assistance, subscriber agreements, billing, pricing for second and subsequent years, audit rights, and governing law compliance.
