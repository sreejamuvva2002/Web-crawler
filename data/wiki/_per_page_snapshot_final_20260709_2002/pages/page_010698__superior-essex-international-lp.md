## Facts

- Superior Essex Communications achieved a 98% landfill waste diversion rate at its Hoisington, Kansas production facility for copper data cables in 2013.
- The facility recycled or reused over 4 million pounds of waste instead of sending it to landfills in the past year.
- All communication cable products fully comply with RoHS 2 directive and REACH restricted substances list.
- While compliance with RoHS 2 and REACH is not mandated by US or Canadian law, these directives are used by North American authorities and companies to ensure environmental standards for purchased products.
- Lindsay Allen, Vice President Marketing at Superior Essex International LP, stated the achievement reflects high environmental goals and world-class performance levels.
- Matt Wheeler serves as Marketing Director.
- Superior Essex International LP is a leading manufacturer and provider of cable, wire, and accessory products for the communications industry, energy sector, and related distribution markets.
- Product lines include copper and fiber optic data cables for indoor and outdoor use, DAS cables and accessories for distributed antenna systems, FTTP closures, coaxial cables, fire alarm/security cables, and low/medium voltage power cable products and accessories.
- Superior Essex International LP is a subsidiary of Superior Essex Inc.
- Corporate address is located at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327 – USA.
