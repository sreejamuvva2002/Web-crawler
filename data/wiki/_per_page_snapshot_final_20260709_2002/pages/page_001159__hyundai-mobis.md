## Facts

- Hyundai Mobis has secured $940M for electrification investments in North America.
- The planned plants are located in Alabama and Georgia and will assemble battery systems and power electronics.
- This funding marks Hyundai Mobis' first overseas Green Loan.
- Hyundai Mobis issued green bonds domestically in 2021 to advance eco-friendly projects.
- This is the first occasion Hyundai Mobis has utilized the Green Loan method for environmentally-friendly business investments abroad.
- Green Loans refer to funding methods for eco-friendly businesses, such as electric vehicles and renewable energy.
- Hyundai Mobis currently operates electrification production hubs in nine locations globally (six domestic and three overseas).
- Hyundai Mobis is establishing six new electrification production hubs in South Korea, North America, and Indonesia.
- This $940M investment follows the company's successful funding for a battery cell joint venture factory in Indonesia last year.
