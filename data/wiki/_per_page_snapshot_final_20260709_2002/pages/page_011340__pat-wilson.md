## Facts

- Pat Wilson serves as the Commissioner of the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD).
- Pat Wilson was awarded the Georgia Creative Champion of 2023 at the Georgia Entertainment 100 event hosted by Skillshot Media.
- Randy Davidson, President of Georgia Entertainment, noted Pat’s efforts to recruit investment for new and expanding businesses in Georgia are widely recognized.
- Georgia continues to set records for job creation while investing in world-renowned workforce development programs.
- Georgia’s entertainment industry generates roughly $30 billion in yearly revenue.
- Georgia’s entertainment industry creates 150,000 jobs.
- Pat Wilson stated that Georgia’s position as a leader in creative industries and global film production is the result of years of hard work from dedicated teams and key partners.
- Pat Wilson emphasized that continued success in film, game development, and entertainment is vital to the overall health and diversity of the economy.
- The 2023 Georgia Entertainment 100 event connected north of 600 business leaders from across the globe to facilitate discussion on welcoming new businesses to Georgia.
