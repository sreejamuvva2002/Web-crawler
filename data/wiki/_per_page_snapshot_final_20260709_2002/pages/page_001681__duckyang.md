# Duckyang

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Duckyang is an electric automotive battery and parts manufacturing company based in Korea.
- Duckyang is locating its first U.S. manufacturing facility in Braselton, Georgia.
- The facility will be located in Jackson County, Georgia.
- Duckyang will invest $10 million toward the project to cover costs.
- The company plans to create 285 new jobs.
- The project is described as a great addition to Jackson County’s economy.
- Duckyang is entering the U.S. electric vehicle battery market through a partnership with SKBA.
- Through this partnership, Duckyang will have the opportunity to cooperate with other automotive companies that already have a U.S. manufacturing base, including Hyundai and Kia Motors.
- A Georgia official stated that the state is focused on growing the entire electric mobility supply chain in Georgia.
- The state leverages strong relationships with SK Battery America and other Korean companies, such as Duckyang, to help grow the supply chain.
- The state helps bring suppliers to Georgia to secure a local supply of needed parts for automotive manufacturers.
- Partners in Jackson County are providing support for these projects.
- The project is expected to create new opportunities locally and for the entire U.S. mobility sector.
