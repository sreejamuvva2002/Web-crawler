## Facts

- GEDIA Georgia is offering a Summer Manufacturing opportunity designed as an internship for students.
- The program duration lasts one to three months.
- Work takes place on-site in the company's laboratory from Monday through Friday.
- Interns are paid $16 per hour.
- Overtime pay is available on Saturdays if scheduled.
- Interns report to a Quality Supervisor and work under the direction of the Plant Manager.
- The program provides hands-on experience in the Quality Department conducting both destructive and non-destructive testing.
- Trainees will learn the Automotive Manufacturing business and develop production and laboratory skills.
- The opportunity targets students with minimal or no manufacturing experience to gain first-hand knowledge of a production operation.
- Task training covers Materials Testing Equipment, Robotics, Plant Maintenance Work, Production, and the Safety program.
- Participants have potential advancement to full-time career positions including a full benefit package.
- GEDIA Georgia is a partner for the automotive industry that develops and manufactures pressed vehicle body parts and welded assemblies worldwide.
- The company develops and manufactures innovative structural parts for lightweight body construction alongside almost all major automobile manufacturers.
- GEDIA employs 4,200 motivated and competent employees globally.
- GEDIA is an international, family-run company with eight manufacturing plants.
- The company culture is characterized by mutual respect and trust.
