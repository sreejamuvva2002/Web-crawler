## Facts

- Nidec Elesys Europe, a Japanese company, has recently signed a lease contract for 700 sqm of Class-A office space in Sofia Airport Center, which is part of Revetas Capital portfolio of assets in Bulgaria.
- The company was founded in 1973 and is now entering the Bulgarian market for the first time.
- Since July 1973, Nidec group with $12.1B turnover in 2021 is designing, manufacturing, and sales motors for the industrial, home appliance and automotive markets.
- The company operates in more than 40 countries with over 107,000 employees.
- Nidec strategy focuses on capturing four big industrial waves: electrification, robotics, energy saving appliances and drones.
- Nidec Elesys Europe will develop inverters for hybrid and full electric vehicles (e-Traction) in the premises rented at the Sofia Airport Center.
- The number of experts employed by the company in Bulgaria is expected to reach 100 in 2023.
- Nidec Bulgaria is hiring engineers in new major technologies to shape the new e-mobility world.
- Eric Assimakopoulos, Founding Partner at Revetas Capital, states that the contract signing indicates a shared vision towards high corporate responsibility and a greener future.
- Sofia Airport Center’s LEED Gold certification assures tenants that the complex follows successful eco-friendly practices.
- The Sofia Airport Center business complex comprises 47,000 sqm of offices and logistics buildings.
