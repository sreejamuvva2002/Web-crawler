## Facts

- Lund International Inc is an automotive accessories and parts manufacturer founded in 1997.
- The company operates a facility at 4325 Hamilton Mill Rd, Buford, GA 30518.
- The company is based in Suwanee, Georgia.
- Lund International maintains more than five locations throughout the world.
- The company employs a staff of over 1,000 employees.
- Lund International serves original equipment manufacturers (OEMs), warehouse distributors, auto accessory dealers, and vehicle owners.
- The company specializes in design and manufacturing services for automotive accessories.
- Manufacturing capabilities include assembly, custom packaging, laser engraving, hot stamping, and heat sealing solutions.
- Additional processing services include steel and aluminum welding, precision metal and drape forming, and die cutting.
- The company also provides binding and material molding capabilities.
