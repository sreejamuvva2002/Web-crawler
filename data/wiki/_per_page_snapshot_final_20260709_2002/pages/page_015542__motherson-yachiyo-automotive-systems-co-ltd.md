# Motherson Yachiyo Automotive Systems Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- In 1997, established Yachiyo of America Inc. in Columbus, Ohio.
- In 1997, established AY Manufacturing, Ltd. in Columbus, Ohio.
- In 1999, established US Yachiyo Inc. in Marion, Ohio.
- Began Plastic Fuel Tank production in 1999.
- In 2000, established Yachiyo Manufacturing of Alabama LLC.
- In 2014, established Yachiyo Manufacturing of America, LLC in Carrollton, Georgia.
- In 2017, supplied styling parts for Honda.
- In 2017, developed the S660.
- In 2018, completed the transfer of the finished vehicle business to Honda Motor Co., Ltd.
- In 2018, terminated consignment production of mini vehicles.
- In March 2024, Motherson Group became the parent company of the Company.
- In October 2024, changed its name to Motherson Yachiyo Automotive Systems Co., Ltd.
