# Mando America Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2013-06-05

**Source:** [Mando America announces new expansion in Opelika](https://www.wsfa.com/story/22502525/mando-america-announces-new-expansion-in-opelika/)  ·  `page_045885`

## Facts

- Mando America Corporation (MAC) announced a new expansion in Opelika, Alabama.
- MAC will add an additional $35 million for equipment and building improvements at its North American Headquarters over the next three years.
- The expansion promises 67 new jobs.
- With this expansion, MAC's total investment in Opelika will exceed $190 million.
- When the expansion is complete, MAC will employ nearly 700 people.
- Opelika Mayor Gary Fuller stated that Mando's continued confidence and additional investment are extremely honored by the city.
- Mayor Fuller noted that MAC is one of Opelika's largest manufacturing employers and appreciated the employment opportunities for citizens.
- The Mando America plant in Opelika produces brake corner modules, brake calipers, suspension modules, and steering systems.
- Mando America is headquartered in Korea.
- The company operates 17 production plants and 8 R&D centers across 10 countries.
- Mando employs over 7,500 associates worldwide.
