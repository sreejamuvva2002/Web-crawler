# Mount Holly Truck Manufacturing Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Joanna Cooper Named General Manager of Mount Holly Truck Plant | Daimler Truck North America](https://northamerica.daimlertruck.com/news-stories/2022/joanna-cooper-named-general-manager-of-mount-holly-truck-plant)  ·  `page_053604`

## Facts

- Joanna Cooper was appointed General Manager of the Mount Holly, N.C. manufacturing plant.
- The Mount Holly plant is home to Freightliner M2 and SD model production.
- Jeff Allen, senior vice president of operations and specialty vehicles at DTNA, stated: “Joanna is an incredible leader who has routinely demonstrated a passion for improving efficiencies in our manufacturing operations, and a commitment to meeting the needs of our ultimate customers.”
- Jeff Allen added that Cooper's dedication to those she leads and her ability to rally teams around a common vision make her a great fit to oversee the Mount Holly Truck Manufacturing Plant.
- Joanna Cooper is a 14-year veteran of the company.
- Cooper began her career as a production buyer at Detroit Diesel Corporation (DDC).
- Cooper previously served as general purchasing agent in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Cooper held successive roles including program manager and assembly business unit manager at DDC.
- Most recently, Cooper served as director of production at the Mount Holly Truck Manufacturing Plant.
- As director of production, Cooper led a 1,500-member team to achieve its production goals.
- Cooper earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Finance and a Master’s in Business Administration from the University of Detroit Mercy.
- Cooper worked as an adjunct professor at the University of Detroit Mercy and continues to serve on its alumni board.
- Daimler Truck North America (DTNA) is headquartered in Portland, Oregon.
- DTNA designs, engineers, manufactures, and markets medium- and heavy-duty trucks, school buses, vehicle chassis, and associated technologies and components.
- DTNA operates under the Freightliner, Western Star, Thomas Built Buses, Freightliner Custom Chassis Corp, and Detroit brands.
- DTNA is a subsidiary of Daimler Truck Holding AG (DTG), one of the world’s leading commercial vehicle manufacturers.
