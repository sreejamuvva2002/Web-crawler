## Facts

- Hyundai Mobis announced plans to build a manufacturing facility with 1,500 more automotive jobs near the Georgia coast.
- The factory will be located in Richmond Hill in Bryan County.
- It will manufacture powertrain systems for vehicles.
- Components will supply the future Hyundai EV factory near Ellabell.
- Components will also be provided to the Kia factory in West Point and Hyundai’s plant in Alabama.
- Gov. Brian Kemp stated that Hyundai’s new electric vehicle and battery manufacturing facility groundbreaking would unleash transformational job creation and investment in that entire region of the state.
- Kemp noted this is the second supplier announced in just two weeks to locate in that area, expressing excitement over their impact on surrounding communities and other upcoming job creators.
- The project supports Georgia's goal to become an e-mobility capital with significant incentives for automotive component manufacturing.
