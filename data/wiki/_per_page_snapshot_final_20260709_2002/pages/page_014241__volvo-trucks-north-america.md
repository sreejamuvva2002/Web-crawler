# Volvo Trucks North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Volvo Trucks Redefines the Trucking Industry in North America with Start of Series Production of the All-New Volvo VNL](https://www.volvotrucks.us/news-and-stories/press-releases/2024/october/volvo-trucks-redefines-the-trucking-industry-in-north-america-with-start-of-series-production-of-the-all-new-volvo-vnl/)  ·  `page_014241`

## Facts

- Volvo Trucks North America has officially started series production of the all-new Volvo VNL, a completely reimagined Class 8 truck.
- The all-new Volvo VNL features sleek aerodynamics and striking visual details.
- The New River Valley Assembly Plant in Dublin, Virginia is Volvo Group’s largest assembly facility in the world.
- The New River Valley Assembly Plant sits on 566 acres.
- The plant has over 2.3 million square feet of space.
- The commercial launch of the all-new Volvo VNL serves as a christening of the state-of-the-art cab plant.
- The cab plant launch is part of a $400 million dollar investment by the Volvo Group in North America.
- Peter Voorhoeve, president of Volvo Trucks North America, expressed excitement about rolling out the all-new VNL to dealer partners and customers.
- The truck combines state-of-the-art technology with unparalleled fuel efficiency, driver comfort, and safety.
- Throughout the summer, more than 1,800 customers, dealers, and sales staff participated in immersive events at the Volvo Trucks Customer Center.
- These events provided hands-on experience with the all-new Volvo VNL.
- The truck brings industry-first packaging to simplify the spec’ing process for customers and dealers.
- Dealers’ service representatives have access to extensive competency development through e-learning and in-person, instructor-led courses from the Volvo Trucks Academy.
- Over 400 all-new Volvo VNLs will start arriving at dealership locations across North America with the series production start.
- Customers will have opportunities to get behind the wheel for a test drive.
- The all-new Volvo VNL features four exterior and interior trim levels: Core, Edge, Edge Black, and Ultimate.
- The truck offers six cab configurations designed to fit customers’ preferences, brand identity, and operational needs.
- The initial 400 trucks will include both sleepers and day cab models.
- The initial delivery includes a variety of packages, trim levels, and powertrain offerings.
- The all-new Volvo VNL boasts advanced aerodynamics for improved fuel efficiency.
- It includes safety technologies like collision avoidance systems.
- It features best-in-class connectivity solutions.
- Designed with superior driver comfort in mind, the truck features ergonomic seating, advanced climate control, and noise reduction features.
- Volvo Trucks North America is headquartered in Greensboro, North Carolina.
- The company operates a network of nearly 400 authorized dealers across North America.
- The 24/7 Volvo Trucks Uptime Center delivers the Uptime Services commitment.
- Every Volvo truck is assembled in the Volvo Trucks New River Valley manufacturing facility in Dublin, Virginia.
- The Dublin, Virginia facility meets the ISO 9001 standard for quality.
- The facility meets the ISO 14001 standard for environmental care.
- The facility holds a dual ISO 50001/Superior Energy Performance certification at the platinum level for energy management.
- Volvo Trucks North America provides complete transport solutions, offering a full range of diesel, alternative-fuel, and all-electric vehicles.
- Volvo Trucks is part of the Volvo Group global organization.
- Volvo Trucks supplies medium- and heavy-duty trucks to professional customers via a global network of dealers with 2,200 service points in about 130 countries.
- Volvo trucks are assembled in 12 countries across the globe.
- In 2025 approximately 120,000 Volvo trucks were delivered worldwide.
- The Volvo Group also provides complete solutions for financing and service.
- Volvo Trucks’ work is based on the core values of quality, safety, and environmental care.
