# RealTruck

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-21

**Source:** [RealTruck Hires General Manager of Original Equipment (OE), John Thomas - Torqued Magazine](https://www.torquedmagazine.com/2025/04/24/realtruck-hires-general-manager-of-original-equipment-oe-john-thomas/)  ·  `page_049095`

## Facts

- RealTruck is a global aftermarket product and accessory brand and a digital destination for truck, Jeep®, Bronco® and off-road enthusiasts.
- RealTruck is continuing to build momentum in the Original Equipment (OE) space by hiring John Thomas as the new general manager of OE.
- John Thomas will guide the company's commercial and operational transformation into a world-class supplier to OEM partners.
- Carl-Martin Lindahl serves as the CEO of RealTruck.
- John Thomas brings more than 25 years of experience in the OEM and aftermarket automotive industry, where he has led, grown, and transformed organizations and built high-performing teams that delivered strong growth and profitability.
- Thomas will leverage his expertise to drive business growth, integrate RealTruck's recent acquisition with Vehicle Accessories Group (VAI), and ensure all aspects of operations meet the evolving needs of OEM customers.
- In recent years, Thomas served as global chief revenue and marketing officer of Ampure Charging Systems, chief executive officer of Advanced Automotive Advisors LLC, and chief operating officer of Autel Energy.
- RealTruck has 30 manufacturing and distribution facilities located in the United States.
- RealTruck employs more than 4,500 U.S.-based associates.
- RealTruck distributes its products through an omnichannel retail approach that includes its online channel, a 12,000+ dealer network, and automotive OEM partnerships.
