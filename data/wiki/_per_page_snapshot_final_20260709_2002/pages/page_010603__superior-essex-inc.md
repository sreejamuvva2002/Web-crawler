# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-05-26

**Source:** [Superior Essex in Talks with Furukawa Electric for Further Collaboration in High Performance Winding Wire Business - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.it/news/superior-essex-in-talks-with-furukawa-electric-for-further-collaboration-in-high-performance-winding-wire-business/)  ·  `page_010603`

## Facts

- Superior Essex Inc. and Furukawa Electric Co., Ltd. are currently seeking a global collaboration targeting the Chinese and North American markets.
- The companies have developed a strong commercial relationship through technical collaboration and cooperation in previous joint ventures.
- The companies currently operate a German joint venture named 'Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire Europe GmbH' with Superior Essex holding 51% and Furukawa holding 49%.
- The Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire Europe GmbH joint venture was formed in March 2017 to supply high voltage winding wire and other sophisticated products like HVWW® for the European market.
- A potential global venture would demonstrate innovation and distribution capabilities for both companies.
- Furukawa Electric pioneered the development of high-performance winding wire, HVWW®, for applications in electric motors, generators, and automotive reactors.
- Superior Essex manages 12 global production sites along with its worldwide sales and distribution network.
- The manufacturers will release further details regarding the potential collaboration once an agreement is reached.
- Superior Essex is the primary brand of Essex Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, and Essex Brownell.
- Superior Essex employs over 3,000 people across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is a leading global supplier of magnet wire and is at the forefront of applying communication fiber and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company's products are critical to creating disruptive technological advances in the electrification of automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex is headquartered at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
