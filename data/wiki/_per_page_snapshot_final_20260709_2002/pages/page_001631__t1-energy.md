## Facts

- T1 Energy is building domestic solar & battery supply chains to invigorate America with scalable, reliable, and low cost energy.
- America needs advanced manufacturing capacity to unlock its most scalable energy resources.
- T1 delivers durable solar components that efficiently convert sunlight into electricity.
- Solar modules are made of highly abundant materials such as silicon, glass and aluminum.
- T1 Energy is building a domestic solar supply chain.
- The company utilizes the latest generation of industrial scale PV tech designed to maximize efficiency and longevity.
- Silicon nitride coating protects the cell from moisture and increases light harvesting efficiency.
- Atomic-layer alumina front-passivation slashes surface recombination for ultra-high efficiency.
- A thin boron doped polysilicon region is used to increase the harvesting efficiency of higher energy photons.
- Ultra-pure single crystal silicon with excess negative charge from phosphorus delivers high lifetime and light induced degradation free power.
- A nanometer scale SiOX barrier blocks recombination while letting electrons quantum tunnel.
- A conductive layer is deposited on the tunnel oxide to selectively collect charge carriers.
- Screen-printed Silver/Aluminum busbars gather current with minimal shading.
- T1 Energy is an Austin-based manufacturer building the energy future.
