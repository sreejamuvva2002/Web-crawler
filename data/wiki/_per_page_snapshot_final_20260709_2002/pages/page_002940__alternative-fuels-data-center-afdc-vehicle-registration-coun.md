# Alternative Fuels Data Center (AFDC) Vehicle Registration Counts by State

## Facts

- The page provides approximate light-duty vehicle registration counts derived by the National Laboratory of the Rockies with data from Experian Information Solutions.
- Counts are rounded to the closest 100 vehicles and reflect the total number of light-duty registered vehicles through the selected year (2021).
- Fuel types are based on vehicle identification numbers (VINs), which do not reflect aftermarket conversions to use different fuels or power sources.
- Georgia had approximately 34,000 Electric (EV) vehicle registrations in 2021.
- Georgia had approximately 13,600 Plug-In Hybrid Electric (PHEV) vehicle registrations in 2021.
- Georgia had approximately 128,200 Hybrid Electric (HEV) vehicle registrations in 2021.
- Georgia had approximately 70,200 Biodiesel vehicle registrations in 2021.
- Georgia had approximately 745,200 Ethanol/Flex (E85) vehicle registrations in 2021.
- Georgia had approximately 700 Compressed Natural Gas (CNG) vehicle registrations in 2021.
- Georgia had approximately 300 Propane vehicle registrations in 2021.
- Georgia had 0 Hydrogen and 0 Methanol vehicle registrations in 2021.
- Georgia had approximately 8,123,100 Gasoline vehicle registrations in 2021.
- Georgia had approximately 203,200 Diesel vehicle registrations in 2021.
- Georgia had approximately 81,200 vehicles with unknown fuel types registered in 2021.
