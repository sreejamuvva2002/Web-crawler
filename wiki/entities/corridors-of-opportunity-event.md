# Corridors of Opportunity Event

**Type:** Event  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Pittsboro, Chatham County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

A smart-growth focused panel discussion held in Pittsboro, Georgia, where local leaders examined rapid population growth and the potential for major automotive and EV-related manufacturing facilities in Chatham County.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The event highlighted that Vietnamese carmaker VinFast and silicon-carbide producer Wolfspeed plan substantial manufacturing facilities in Chatham County, indicating future EV supply-chain activity.
