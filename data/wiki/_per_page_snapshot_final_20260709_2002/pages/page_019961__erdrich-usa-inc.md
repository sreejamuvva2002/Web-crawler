## Facts

- Erdrich USA, Inc. is an automotive parts supplier specialized in high technology metal forming.
- The company operates a plant in Dublin, GA.
- From its Dublin, GA plant, Erdrich USA ships parts to OEM and Tier 1 customers in the US, Canada, and México.
- Erdrich USA, Inc. is part of the Erdrich Group, a German-based enterprise.
- The Erdrich Group operates five plants in Germany and the Czech Republic.
