# Hitachi Astemo, Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-07-27

**Source:** [News Releases : July 27, 2022 : Hitachi Astemo, Ltd.](https://www.hitachi-automotive.us/about/press/07-27-2022.htm)  ·  `page_008499`

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo is a global automotive technology company formed in 2021 by the merger of Hitachi Automotive Systems with Honda’s Keihin, Showa and Nissin Kogyo groups.
- The company has approximately 90,000 employees.
- Hitachi Astemo holds leadership positions in AD/ADAS, software and powertrain, as well as chassis and motorcycle systems.
- Its Americas headquarters is located in Farmington Hills, Michigan.
- Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc. manufactures and markets engine management, electric powertrain and integrated vehicle controls for major automotive manufacturers worldwide.
- The company adheres to ISO/TS:16949:2009 industry standards.
- Hitachi Astemo is a technology partner with Chevrolet in the development of its highly successful INDYCAR engine program.
- Hitachi Astemo provides fuel-system components for all of Chevrolet's 2.2-liter, twin-turbocharged INDYCAR racing engines.
