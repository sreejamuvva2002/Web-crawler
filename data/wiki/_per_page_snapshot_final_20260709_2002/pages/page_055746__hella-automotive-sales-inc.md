## Facts

- HELLA Automotive Sales, Inc. is celebrating its 40th anniversary of operations in North America.
- The company was founded in Germany in 1899.
- HELLA has become a global leader in automotive lighting and electronics technology.
- The company has been a key player in the North American market since 1983.
- HELLA Automotive Sales, Inc. first established its North American presence by opening a headquarters in Cranford, New Jersey.
- In 1991, the company moved its North American headquarters to Peachtree City, Georgia.
- Over the past 40 years, HELLA has expanded its operations throughout the United States, Canada, and Central and South America.
- Alejandro Caballero serves as the President of HELLA Automotive Sales, Inc.
