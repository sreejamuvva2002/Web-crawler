- The Q2 delivery figure topped Rivian's own delivery outlook of 9,000 to 11,000 vehicles.

- Rivian raised its full-year 2026 delivery guidance to 65,000 to 70,000 vehicles.
- The new full-year 2026 delivery guidance is up from a previous range of 62,000 to 67,000 vehicles.
- Management cites the R2 ramp as the key growth and demand driver for the lower-priced SUV.
- Rivian delivered 10,365 units in Q1 2026.
- Rivian reported $1.38 billion in revenue for Q1 2026.
- Rivian's adjusted EPS was -$0.54 for Q1 2026, beating the consensus of -$0.7162.
- Software and Services revenue at Rivian jumped 49% year over year to $473 million.
- The Software and Services revenue growth was aided by a Volkswagen joint venture.
- Rivian received a $1 billion equity infusion from Volkswagen.
- Rivian has secured a $4.5 billion Department of Energy loan for its Georgia facility.
- Rivian expects a $300 million initial Uber equity investment in Q2 2026 tied to the R2 robotaxi program.
- Rivian will report its Q2 2026 financial results on July 30 after market close.
- The margin picture for Q2 deliveries is expected to be critical due to a mix shift toward R2 and continued cash burn.

- There were 28 recent RIVN insider transactions netting to buying.
