# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-01

**Source:** [Superior Essex Completes Essex Furukawa Buyout from Furukawa Electric](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/superior-essex-completes-essex-furukawa-130000593.html?fr=sycsrp_catchall)  ·  `page_010715`

## Facts

- Superior Essex successfully completed the acquisition of the remaining minority interest in Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire from Furukawa Electric Co., Ltd.
- The transaction became effective on April 30, 2024, establishing Superior Essex as the sole owner of the brand, assets, and operations previously conducted by the global joint venture with Furukawa Electric.
- Superior Essex plans to relaunch its magnet wire business under a new company name later in 2024.
- The company will continue operating all current facilities of the Essex Furukawa joint venture worldwide.
- Facilities include magnet wire plants in Japan and Malaysia (Kuala Lumpur) that were contributed by Furukawa Electric in October 2020.
- CEO Daniel Choi stated, "Over the last several years we have been able to accomplish many great things working with the team from Furukawa Electric. I am proud of what was created and thankful for their contributions."
- The acquisition solidifies Superior Essex's control over global operations and reinforces its commitment to delivering products and services to customers worldwide.
- CEO Daniel Choi stated, "Superior Essex plans to continuously respond to market demands in the xEV automotive industry, power, electrification, and renewable energy markets—paving the way for future technologies as a global leader," adding that the company will "strengthen its position by actively pursuing decarbonization and sustainability."
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, Essex Energy Italy, IVA, Lacroix + Kress, Hi-Wire, and Essex Brownell.
- Superior Essex has over 3,000 employees across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is the leading global provider of magnet wire and is at the forefront of communications fiber application as well as Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company is instrumental in creating disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Furukawa Electric Co., Ltd. was established in 1884 and comprises 127 group companies with approximately 50,000 employees worldwide.
- Furukawa Electric provides technologies, products, and services for infrastructure fields such as information and energy, automotive systems, and electronics using core technologies of metals, polymers, photonics, and high frequency.
