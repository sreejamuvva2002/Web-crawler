# Aurubis

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-08

## Facts

- Europe's biggest copper smelter Aurubis will invest around $700 million to build a smelter for complex recycling materials in North America.
- The commissioning of the secondary smelter in Augusta, Georgia, is planned for the second half of 2024.
- On Wednesday, U.S. first lady Jill Biden toured the facility.
- An expansion of the plant is already under way.
- The expansion will allow the site to process 180,000 tons of complex recycling materials, such as printed circuit boards and insulated copper cable, from 2026.
- Aurubis is creating long-term, secure jobs for more than 200 people here in an industry that is strategically important to U.S.
