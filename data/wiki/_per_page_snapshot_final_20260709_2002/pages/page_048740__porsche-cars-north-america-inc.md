# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-01-05

**Source:** [Latest 911 GT3 now available to drive at the Porsche Experience Centers in Atlanta and Los Angeles Intrado](https://smb.panews.com/article/Latest-911-GT3-now-available-to-drive-at-the-Porsche-Experience-Centers-in-Atlanta-and-Los-Angeles?storyId=61d5dd6e89d6c60952093a87)  ·  `page_048740`

## Facts

- A new 911 GT3 has joined the fleet available for customers at Porsche Experience Centers in Atlanta and Los Angeles for driver development courses.
- The road car is designed to fulfill every driving enthusiast’s wish, with a high-revving, naturally aspirated engine that is virtually identical to the track-only 911 GT3 Cup race car.
- Kjell Gruner serves as President and CEO of PCNA.
- Experience Center customers drive on a variety of track modules, some designed to simulate everyday driving conditions and others to provide more challenging environments.
- In Atlanta, the Porsche Experience Center offers a 90-minute 911 GT3 driving experience.
- In Atlanta, the Porsche Experience Center offers a 90-minute 911 GT3 vs. 911 Turbo driving experience.
- In Atlanta, the Porsche Experience Center offers a 90-minute 911 GT3 Generations experience comparing the 991 and 992 models, available during the 11:00 a.m. and 2:00 p.m. sessions until March 31.
- In Los Angeles, the Porsche Experience Center offers a 90-minute 911 GT3 driving experience.
- In Los Angeles, the Porsche Experience Center offers a 90-minute 911 GT3 vs. 911 Turbo driving experience.
- In Los Angeles, the Porsche Experience Center offers a 4-hour 911 GT3 Accelerated driving experience.
- In Los Angeles, the Porsche Experience Center offers a 90-minute 911 GT3 Generations experience comparing the 991 and 992 models, available until March 31.
- In Los Angeles, the Porsche Experience Center offers a 4-hour 911 GT3 Generations Accelerated driving experience, available until March 31.
- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, at One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA.
- PCNA was established in 1984 and has been headquartered in Atlanta since 1998.
- PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera, and Taycan.
- The Atlanta Porsche Experience Center features a module-based 1.6-mile driver development track, a business center, and a fine dining restaurant named 356.
- PCNA operates a second Porsche Experience Center near Los Angeles on a 53-acre complex.
- The Los Angeles Porsche Experience Center features a driver development track with eight educational modules totaling 4.1 miles, a business center, and Restaurant 917.
- PCNA supports 193 independently owned and operated Porsche dealerships in the U.S. by supplying parts, service, marketing, and training.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Porsche boasts a racing heritage with over 30,000 motorsport wins to date.
