## Facts

- SECO EcoPlastic is a Korea-based manufacturer and supplier of plastic components for the automotive industry.
- SECO EcoPlastic recently constructed its first U.S. facility in Statesboro, Georgia.
- The new 400,000-square-foot state-of-the-art plant is used for manufacturing and painting Hyundai electric vehicle (EV) components.
- The components include bumpers, interior consoles, trims, and molds.
- CHA provided fire protection engineering services for the facility.
- The project installed 4,376 sprinkler heads.
- The installation includes 9 wet pipe sprinkler systems.
- The installation includes 4 pre-action sprinkler systems.
- The installation includes a NOVEC clean agent system.
- The installation includes an electric fire pump.
- These systems were installed to meet comprehensive safety and code compliance requirements.
