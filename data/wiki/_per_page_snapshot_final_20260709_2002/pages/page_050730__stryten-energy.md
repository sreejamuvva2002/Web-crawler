# Stryten Energy

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-11-11

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Named a 2022 Military Friendly Employer and Brand](https://au.finance.yahoo.com/news/stryten-energy-named-2022-military-142500745.html)  ·  `page_050730`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy has been named a 2022 Silver Military Friendly® Employer and a Military Friendly® Brand.
- The recognition comes from an annual list by militaryfriendly.com that honors companies creating sustainable career paths, community outreach, brand enthusiasm, and enduring partnerships benefiting the military community.
- Stryten Energy hires military members across every level of the company, from executive management to the plant floor.
- The company maintains a dedicated section on its jobs website specifically for military and veteran job seekers.
- Mike Judd serves as President and Chief Operating Officer of Stryten Energy.
- Stryten Energy has been recognized for its veterans-friendly culture and commitment to hiring members of the military community.
- The Military Friendly Employers list is produced annually by Viqtory, Inc., a service-disabled, veteran-owned small business.
- List ratings are awarded based on public data sources, proprietary survey data, and personal surveys of veterans, processed through an algorithm overseen by The Military Friendly® Advisory Council and audited by Ernst & Young.
- Stryten Energy maintains high retention rates for its military personnel, with many having progressed through the company via multiple promotions.
- The company currently has job openings for military members across numerous U.S. locations.
- Stryten Energy is a sponsor of Rivers of Recovery, a national nonprofit that uses outdoor-based activities to treat symptoms of post-traumatic stress, depression, and anxiety in combat veterans and active-duty military.
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions across the transportation, motive power, essential power, and military sectors.
- The company is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Stryten Energy's technologies power applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- The company's stored energy technologies include advanced lead and lithium batteries, intelligent chargers, and cloud-based software.
- Stryten Energy is an industry leader backed by more than a century of expertise in delivering top-performing energy solutions.
