## Facts

- Novelis provides sustainable, low-carbon aluminum solutions to the best brands in beverage packaging, automotive, aerospace, building & construction, consumer electronics and other specialty markets.
- Novelis operating facilities span four continents: North America, Europe, Asia and South America.
- Novelis is investing more than $4 billion to build a state-of-the-art aluminum recycling and rolling plant in Bay Minette, Alabama.
- The Bay Minette facility is expected to create up to 1,000 new jobs.
- The Bay Minette facility will produce 600 kilometers of finished aluminum goods annually.
- Novelis's company vision is "Advance Aluminum as the Material of Choice with Circular Solutions," which sets three ambitious goals to achieve by the end of 2030.
- Novelis is actively seeking passionate professionals to join its Bay Minette team.
