# Milliken & Company

## Facts

- Milliken & Company is a global manufacturing leader focused on materials science that delivers products across textile, flooring, chemical, and healthcare businesses.
- Milliken & Company’s Chemical Division has been a leading global supplier of coatings, chemicals, and advanced additive and colorant technologies since its conception in 1957.
- The Chemical division was founded to supply proprietary processing chemicals within Milliken’s operations and has expanded into diverse industries ranging from plastics to children’s art products.
- Milliken’s Chemical division maintains application and development centers around the world and is committed to customer support.
- Milliken & Company is a globally recognized leader in polymer additives and colorants with expertise to help brand owners and converters achieve sustainability objectives.
- Milliken & Company is a diversified global manufacturer with more than 70 locations worldwide.
- In fall 2022, Milliken introduced its DeltaFlow Viscosity Modifier for recycled polypropylene (rPP) at the K 2022 trade fair, based on latest viscosity-modification chemistry.
- On October 19, 2022, Milliken & Company and PureCycle Technologies, Inc. (NASDAQ: PCT) jointly announced they created the first fully sustainable concentrate for PP on the market.
- The concentrate based on PureCycle materials allows for greater use of 100% recycled content.
- When used in combination with PureCycle’s recycled PP, the formulation produces a carbon footprint (GHG) approximately 35% lower than that of virgin PP.
- On October 19, 2022, Milliken & Company announced its science-based net-zero targets have been approved by the U.N.-backed Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi).
- SBTi is a collective of international organizations dedicated to helping companies set emission reduction targets consistent with climate science and the Paris Agreement.
- On October 12, 2022, Milliken & Company partnered with Finnish flexible packaging giant Huhtamaki Group to develop a more sustainable, mono-material laminate for use in tubes targeting cosmetics, toothpaste, and other personal-care applications.
- Milliken introduced its RESIST™ XTR colorants designed for extreme conditions and demanding applications at the K 2022 trade fair.
- The addition of RESIST™ XTR 9798 builds on Milliken’s existing portfolio while contributing to a more sustainable future.
- At the upcoming K 2022 show, Milliken partnered with European recycling leaders EREMA and PreZero to highlight the use of Hyperform® HPN® performance additives in recycled polypropylene (rPP) compounds.
- The common purpose of the partnership with EREMA and PreZero is to close the loop and advance circularity.
- On September 15, 2022, Milliken & Company achieved a gold rating on its 2022 EcoVadis assessment.
- Milliken’s EcoVadis score places it in the top 5% of more than 90,000 organizations rated by EcoVadis across environmental, social, and ethical considerations.
- At Pack Expo International 2022, Milliken showcased novel plastic additives aimed at moving the circular economy forward.
- Milliken offers customers opportunities to achieve sustainability goals through lightweighting, upgrading recycled content, conserving energy, simplifying recycling with mono-materials, and reducing carbon footprint via inter-material replacement of less-sustainable resins.
