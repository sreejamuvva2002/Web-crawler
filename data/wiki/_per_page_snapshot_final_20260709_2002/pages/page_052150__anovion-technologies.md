# Anovion Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-05

**Source:** [Anovion invests in GA facility creating over 400 jobs](https://valdostatoday.com/news-2/region/2023/05/anovion-invests-in-ga-facility-creating-over-400-jobs/)  ·  `page_052150`

## Facts

- Anovion Technologies will invest over $800 million in a new manufacturing facility in Bainbridge.
- The project will create over 400 jobs.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Anovion Technologies will build a new manufacturing facility in Bainbridge.
- Anovion is a supplier of premium synthetic graphite anode materials for lithium-ion batteries.
- The project will create more than 400 jobs and over $800 million in investment in Decatur County.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia is fast becoming the e-mobility capital of the nation.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that job creators are recognizing every corner of the state has the resources and workforce needed for success.
- Anovion is a climate tech-driven advanced materials company.
- Anovion is one of the first qualified U.S. suppliers of synthetic graphite anode materials for use in e-mobility applications.
- Graphite is the largest battery material used in electric vehicles by mass, more than copper, nickel, manganese, cobalt, and lithium.
- Eric Stopka, Chief Executive Officer of Anovion, stated the company is opening its first large-scale expansion manufacturing facility in Georgia.
- Eric Stopka noted that Georgia’s strong technical workforce and top-tier schools will help advance research partnerships and contribute to a highly skilled talent pipeline.
- Eric Stopka highlighted the region’s growing availability of clean energy, which will result in increased sustainability for Anovion’s manufacturing process.
- Anovion’s new facility will be 1,500,000 square feet and feature proprietary furnace technologies.
- The facility is located at Downrange Industrial Park in Bainbridge.
- The facility will be the first of its size and scale in North America.
- The facility will produce synthetic graphite for use in batteries that power EVs, electric storage systems, electronics, military applications, and other products.
- Operations at the new facility are expected to begin in late 2025.
- The company will be hiring for a variety of technical, production, and engineering roles.
- The company will also be hiring for administrative and management positions.
- Bainbridge Mayor Edward Reynolds welcomed Anovion to the city.
- Mayor Edward Reynolds described the $800 million investment and creation of over 400 high-paying, quality jobs as a huge win for the community.
- Pete Stephens, Chairman of the Decatur County Board of Commissioners, stated that Decatur County leadership has focused on attracting quality companies and jobs.
- Pete Stephens noted that diversifying the industry mix is necessary to remain competitive and grow.
- Pete Stephens described the announcement as a step toward diversification, with new green industry jobs representing a growing segment of the economy.
- Project Manager Taylor Walden represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team on the project.
- The project was developed in partnership with the City of Bainbridge, Development Authority of Bainbridge and Decatur County, University System of Georgia, Georgia EMC, and Georgia Quick Start.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that much of the critical supply chain for e-mobility and manufacturing is currently sourced outside of the U.S.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson described Anovion as an innovative industry leader filling that need and delivering a critical component to Georgia.
- Georgia is positioned as a hub for the electric mobility industry.
- Since 2020, 40 EV-related projects have announced more than $21.9 billion in investment and 28,000 jobs in Georgia.
- More than 10 of these projects are battery manufacturers, recyclers, and suppliers.
- The battery-related projects represent approximately $8.5 billion in investment and 6,000 jobs.
- Anovion Technologies is headquartered in Chicago, Illinois.
- Anovion Technologies is a U.S.-owned and U.S.-based advanced materials business.
- Anovion is currently America’s first commercial-scale manufacturer of premium, cleaner anode-grade synthetic graphite.
- Anovion's existing manufacturing footprint is located near Niagara Falls, New York, and Clarksburg, West Virginia.
- Anovion began commercial production in early 2021.
- Anovion is among a limited number of graphite anode producers in America to have successfully gained qualification for EV applications.
