# Kia America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-16

## Facts

- The all-electric EV9 is assembled in Kia’s West Point, Georgia plant.
- The EV9 qualifies for a $7,500 federal EV tax credit.
- Sales of the EV9 are anticipated to begin in the second quarter of 2025.
- The 2026 Kia EV9 Nightfall Edition debuted on April 16, 2025, at the 2025 New York International Auto Show.
- The EV9 is offered on a dual-motor Land AWD trim with standard six-passenger seating and an available seven-passenger configuration.
- The EV9 Nightfall Edition features exclusive 20-inch Gloss Black wheels wrapped in 275/50R-20 all-season tires.
- A standard Boost function increases torque from 443 lb.-ft to 516 lb.-ft, allowing acceleration from zero to 60 mph in 4.5 seconds.
- The EV9 generates 379 horsepower from its dual motors.
- The EV9's adoption of the North American Charging Standard allows owners to access the Tesla Supercharger network and, with an optional adapter, existing Combined Charging System networks.
- Kia America is headquartered in Irvine, California.
- Kia serves as the "Official Automotive Partner" of the NBA and WNBA.
- Kia sells its vehicles through a network of over 775 dealers in the U.S.
- The EV9 all-electric three-row SUV is assembled in the United States from U.S. and globally sourced parts.
- Steven Center serves as COO & EVP at Kia America.
- “This award-winning SUV continues to be a landmark win for the Kia brand,” said Steven Center, COO & EVP of Kia America.
