## Facts

- TI Fluid Systems reiterated full-year targets on Thursday after third-quarter revenues sparked.
- Group revenue in the nine months to 30 September jumped 10.4% to €2.41bn, or by 4.5% on a constant currency basis.
- Sales rose in Europe and Africa, its biggest region, by 2.1% to €880.9m.
- Sales rose in Asia Pacific by nearly 9% to €821.8m.
- The company is making "excellent progress" on its organic electrification growth strategy.
- €0.9bn of lifetime revenue for battery electric vehicle awards was booked in the year to date.
- €300m of the BEV award revenue was booked in the third quarter.
- Inflationary cost increases are expected to remain, and the firm is pursuing cost recoveries and price adjustments in response.
- The company expects full-year results in line with current consensus market expectations.
- Full-year revenue growth is expected to be consistent with, or slightly below, global light vehicle production.
- Full-year adjusted earnings before interest and tax margin is likely to be around 6%.
