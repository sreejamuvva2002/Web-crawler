# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing is located in Newnan, Georgia.
- The facility employs over 2000 workers across roles including engineers, accountants, assembly line workers, welders, safety technicians, team leaders, managers, and techies.
- Yamaha manufactures WaveRunners, Side-by-Sides, ATVs, and Golf Cars at its six facilities in Newnan.
- Over 4 million vehicles have been produced at the Newnan location.
- Yamaha does not sell or service its products directly; other Yamaha-affiliated companies support customers and dealers for sales, service, and marketing purposes.
- The Newnan facility has been manufacturing recreational vehicles for over 30 years.
