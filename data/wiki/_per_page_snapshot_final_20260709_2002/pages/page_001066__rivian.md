# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Rivian Breaks Ground on New Plant in Georgia – SOM](https://www.som.com/news/rivian-groundbreaking/)  ·  `page_001066`

## Facts

- Rivian held a formal groundbreaking ceremony for its new manufacturing plant in Stanton Springs, Georgia.
- The facility is designed for cutting-edge advanced manufacturing, efficiency, and sustainability.
- The nearly 2,000-acre site will employ modern construction techniques.
- Advanced environmental management will be used at the site.
- The site is intended to be elegantly integrated with the surrounding environment.
- The facility is intended to feature recreational trails, including a Rivian experience trail.
- The first phase of construction is expected to begin in 2026.
- Customer vehicle production in Georgia is anticipated to start in 2028.
- Upon completion of the second phase, the facility will have an annual production capacity of up to 400,000 vehicles.
- The plant is expected to create 7,500 jobs by 2030.
- The plant will build Rivian’s next generation vehicles.
- Rivian Founder and CEO RJ Scaringe stated that the project cements Rivian's future at the Georgia plant and helps ensure America maintains its technology leadership and excellence in automobile manufacturing.
- SOM is helping enable Rivian’s next generation of vehicles alongside Clayco and Affiliated Engineers.
