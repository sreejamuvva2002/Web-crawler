## Facts

- Originally known for plastic fastener innovation, Nifco’s range of products has grown significantly.
- In addition to superior fastening technology, Nifco has developed solutions for customers in areas such as interior mechanisms, fuel systems, and appearance parts.
- The company makes components for nearly all automotive needs.
- Nifco utilizes cutting-edge expertise with the best materials, techniques, and consumer buying trends to ensure cost-effective components.
- Nifco manufactures and assembles exterior parts found around the vehicle exterior, from doors and windows to under the hood and bumpers.
- The company's selection of plastic fasteners aims to lower costs, improve efficiency, and provide a better product for end customers.
- Nifco can help with applications requiring concealed storage, variable-size cupholders, positionable sun visors, or other movable features.
- Nifco is incorporating its injection-molding experience into hybrid, electric, and alternative powertrains of the future.
- Critical parts related to occupant safety are manufactured in a clean environment and subjected to the highest quality standards.
- Nifco partners with customers in mounting and setting complex and ever-changing safety systems in vehicles.
