## Facts

- Yazaki North America is the U.S. arm of a global auto parts manufacturer.
- The company lobbies Congress on trade and automotive supply issues.
- Its lobbying activities cover U.S.-Mexico-Canada trade rules (USMCA).
- It lobbies on tariff measures such as Section 232 and Section 301.
- It also lobbies on other global tariffs that affect parts sourcing and sales.
- Disclosed filings show multiple entries and about $370,000 reported in lobbying since 2026-01-16.
- Total lobbying disclosures amount to $660K through 2026.
- Stuart Feldman is listed as a top lobbyist handling this client.
