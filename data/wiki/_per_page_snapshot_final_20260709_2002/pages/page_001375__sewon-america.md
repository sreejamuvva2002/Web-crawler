# SEWON America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-10-24

**Source:** [SEWON America to create 100 jobs in LaGrange, GA - Location Georgia](https://www.locationgeorgia.com/2018/10/24/sewon-america-to-create-100-jobs-in-lagrange-ga/)  ·  `page_001375`

## Facts

- Gov. Nathan Deal announced that SEWON America will create 100 jobs in LaGrange, GA.
- SEWON America will invest $160 million in expanding its North American headquarters and manufacturing plant in LaGrange.
- SEWON America is a leading supplier to the automotive industry.
- Gov. Nathan Deal stated that Georgia is the No. 1 state for business.
- Gov. Nathan Deal noted that Georgia maintains a competitive economic environment encouraging growth and innovation among companies already operating there.
- SEWON America has a significant economic footprint in the Troup County area.
- The company will continue to benefit from proximity to a growing family of automotive manufacturers and a strong workforce.
- The expansion involves adding additional manufacturing lines to meet production requirements for new Kia and Hyundai models.
- The new manufacturing lines will improve efficiency and flexibility compared to current technology, allowing for greater productivity.
- Ken Horton, SEWON general manager of human resources, stated that with support from Gov. Nathan Deal, Troup County, and the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD), SEWON will remain operational and continue delivering parts to customers.
- Ken Horton thanked the State of Georgia and the City of LaGrange for investing in SEWON America and its team members, affirming the company will continue creating jobs in LaGrange and Troup County.
