# Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corporation

## Facts

- Since 2001, Suzuki has been building the award-winning Suzuki KingQuad ATVs in Rome, Georgia.
- The facility is a 35-acre plant valued at over $30 million.
- The plant is formally known as Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corporation (SMAC).
- SMAC's goal is to make the Rome facility the world’s number one off-road vehicle manufacturer.
- Approximately 200 employees work at the Rome, Georgia plant.
- The facility produces over 120 King Quad ATVs each day.
- The factory is located in the middle of the biggest ATV market on the planet, allowing Suzuki to harness real-world rider needs for future product development.
- To support just-in-time delivery, SMAC secures regional parts suppliers wherever possible.
- Sourcing regional suppliers improves competitive advantage and benefits the local Georgia economy.
- The facility ships finished vehicles to Suzuki dealers in the U.S. and Canada, as well as to almost 60 countries worldwide.
