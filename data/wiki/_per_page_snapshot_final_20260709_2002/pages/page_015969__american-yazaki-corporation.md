## Facts

- Founded in 2009 in Gainesville, Georgia.
- Family-owned manufacturing company with global ties to its parent organization in Japan.
- Designs and manufactures multi-layered recyclable HDPE jugs focused on sustainability and durability.
- Began operations producing multi-layered fuel tanks for off-road vehicles and power equipment.
- Fuel tanks meet and surpass EPA and CARB permeation standards.
- Expanded product line to include mono-layer ink cartridge bottles that exceed performance expectations.
- Launched golf bag assembly services in 2023, with the customer reporting zero returns since takeover.
- Multi-Layered Fuel Tanks product line established in 2012.
- Mono-Layer Ink Cartridge Bottles product line established in 2014.
- Re-Packing Services scheduled to launch in 2026.
- Recognized as a leader in the manufacturing and assembly industry based on operational efficiency, uncompromising quality, reliable delivery, and customer-centric partnerships.
- Fosters a culture of continuous learning where associates cross-train across departments to enhance flexibility and promote career growth.
- Rewards employee initiative with higher wages.
