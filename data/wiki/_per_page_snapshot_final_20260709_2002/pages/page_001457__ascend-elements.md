# Ascend Elements

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-15

**Source:** [Ascend Elements | Ascend Elements EV Battery Recycling Facility Certified to ISO 9001: 2015 Quality Management Standard by DQS Inc.](https://ascendelements.com/ascend-elements-ev-battery-recycling-facility-certified-to-iso-90012015-quality-management-standard-by-dqs-inc/)  ·  `page_001457`

## Facts

- DQS Inc., an independent certification body, certified Ascend Elements’ electric vehicle (EV) battery recycling facility in Covington, Ga. to the ISO 9001:2015 standard for Quality Management Systems on July 15, 2024.
- The ISO 9001:2015 standard is globally recognized for helping organizations improve performance, meet customer expectations, and demonstrate a commitment to quality.
- The certification process includes onsite audits and extensive policy and documentation reviews to establish, implement, maintain, and continually improve a quality management system (QMS).
- Andrew Aberdale, CFO/COO at Ascend Elements, stated that achieving ISO certification is a major achievement for any manufacturing company, especially for a startup organization in an emerging field like battery recycling.
- Aberdale noted that the ISO certification signals the company's commitment to quality, customer service, and rigorous safety procedures.
- The Ascend Elements facility in Covington is one of the largest EV battery recycling facilities in North America with capacity to process 30,000 metric tons of used lithium-ion batteries and scrap per year.
- The 154,000 square foot facility in Newton County contains battery discharge and disassembly processes, battery shredding, lithium extraction and analytical testing capabilities.
- Ascend Elements is a North America- and Europe-based battery materials leader converting spent lithium-ion batteries and manufacturing scrap into premium precursor cathode material (pCAM) and battery-grade lithium carbonate through its patented Hydro-to-Cathode® process.
- The company operates plants at scale with a proven technology platform to deliver circular supply for EV and energy storage markets.
