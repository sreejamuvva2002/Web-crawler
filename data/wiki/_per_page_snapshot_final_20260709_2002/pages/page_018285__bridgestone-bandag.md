## Facts

- Bridgestone and Bandag invested $400,000 in their retreading plant in Campana.
- The investment aims to optimize the country's tire retreading service.
- Funds were allocated to purchase state-of-the-art machinery.
- This announcement is part of a $5 million investment plan announced by Bridgestone and Bandag in 2021.
- The project will be implemented over the next five years.
- Both companies aim for sustained growth in the tire retreading business with innovation as the core focus.
- They offer a complete tire lifecycle and guarantee customer safety.
- Bandag is one of Bridgestone's sustainable mobility solutions in the country.
- The service maximizes tire lifespan responsibly, using 66% less petroleum, reducing CO2 emissions by 24%, and using 19% less water than manufacturing a new tire.
- Bandag retread treads feature innovative designs adaptable to various terrains, offering the same performance as new tires while decreasing rolling resistance.
- A main benefit is greater fuel efficiency and reduced total cost per kilometer for transport fleets.
