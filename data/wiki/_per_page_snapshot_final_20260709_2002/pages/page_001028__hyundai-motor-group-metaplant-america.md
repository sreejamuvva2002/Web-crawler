# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- The Hyundai Metaplant is home to the award-winning, all electric IONIQ 5 crossover SUV.
- HMGMA produces all IONIQ 5 models, with the exception of the N model, for the U.S. and Canadian markets.
- The Metaplant began production of the IONIQ 5 in October 2024 when a white, 2025 SEL model rolled off the assembly line.
- A private celebration was held for employees who signed the car.
- It is housed at the Hyundai Mobility Training Center of Georgia located on the megasite.
- In March 2025, the all-electric 2026 IONIQ 9 became Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America’s second production vehicle.
- The three-row SUV is often praised for its technology, design, comfort, performance and safety.
- In October 2025, the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety (IIHS) awarded the 2026 IONIQ 9 with its highly coveted TOP SAFETY PICK+ prize.
- In June 2026, Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) reached three significant milestones with the launch of its first Kia model, its first hybrid electric vehicle (HEV), and the addition of a third vehicle to its production lineup.
- The Kia Sportage Hybrid now joins the award-winning Hyundai IONIQ 5 and IONIQ 9, showcasing Hyundai Motor Group’s ability to build multiple brands and powertrain technologies on a single production line.
- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America produces the IONIQ 5, IONIQ 9 and Kia Sportage Hybrid models for the United States and Canadian markets.
- HMGMA has the ability to produce up to 10 different vehicles.
- As we ramp up to full production, we will announce new additions to our lineup.
