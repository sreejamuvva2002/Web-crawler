## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation (NYSE: TG).
- The U.S. Department of Commerce ruled to institute preliminary dumping duties on unfairly traded imports of aluminum extrusions.
- Bonnell Aluminum manufactures custom-fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions serving the automotive and transportation market.
- Branded product lines include TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum and Futura Transitions by Bonnell Aluminum.
- Bonnell Aluminum has manufacturing facilities located in Newnan, GA; Carthage, TN; Niles, MI; Elkhart, IN; and Clearfield, UT.
- Guy Charpentier serves as the Division Marketing Director for Bonnell Aluminum.
