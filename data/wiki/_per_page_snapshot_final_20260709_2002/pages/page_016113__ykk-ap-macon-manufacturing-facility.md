# YKK AP Macon Manufacturing Facility

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-07-07

**Source:** [New YKK AP manufacturing facility promises job growth in Middle Georgia - 41NBC News | WMGT-DT](https://www.41nbc.com/new-ykk-ap-manufacturing-facility-promises-job-growth-in-middle-georgia/)  ·  `page_016113`

## Facts

- YKK AP and JE Dunn have partnered to build a new manufacturing facility in Macon, Georgia.
- The facility will span 400,000 square feet.
- The facility is designated for the manufacturing of residential windows and doors.
- The project is expected to create 100 new jobs for residents in Macon and Middle Georgia.
- The expansion will increase YKK's total workforce in Macon to 350 employees.
- Construction on the facility is currently underway.
- Officials project the facility will be complete by October.
- JE Dunn Construction Superintendent Josh Oesterreicher stated that working conditions will be humidity and heat controlled, making them highly favorable for workers.
- Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority Board Chairman Robby Fountain noted the development is significant for retaining existing jobs and creating a few hundred more in Macon-Bibb.
