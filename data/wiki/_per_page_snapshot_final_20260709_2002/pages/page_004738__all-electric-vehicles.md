# All-Electric Vehicles

**Type:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-10

**Source:** [FOTW #1375, December 30, 2024: Median EV Range in Model Year 2024 Reached a Record High of 283 Miles per Charge | Department of Energy](https://www.energy.gov/cmei/vehicles/articles/fotw-1375-december-30-2024-median-ev-range-model-year-2024-reached-record)  ·  `page_004738`

## Facts

- The median range of all-electric vehicles (EVs) for model year (MY) 2024 reached a record high of 283 miles per charge.
- The median EV range gained 13 miles from the previous model year.
- The median EV range is more than four times higher than the median range for MY 2011.
- The highest maximum range for an EV certified by the EPA was the Lucid Air sedan in MY 2022, rated at 520 miles per charge.
- Data was sourced from the U.S. Department of Energy and U.S. Environmental Protection Agency Fuel Economy Website Datafiles, accessed October 10, 2024.
