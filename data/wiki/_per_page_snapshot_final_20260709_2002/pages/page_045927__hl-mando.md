# HL Mando

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-06

**Source:** [HL Mando deploys AI fire-prevention system at Hyundai’s US Metaplant - CHOSUNBIZ](https://biz.chosun.com/en/en-industry/2026/05/06/ALSDOXJR3FCCRNUSY6RGX2R26U/)  ·  `page_045927`

## Facts

- HL Mando said on the 6th it will push in earnest to commercialize its artificial intelligence (AI)-based electric fire prevention solution "e-HAECHIE."
- HAECHIE secured stability and reliability through proof of concept (PoC) at 17 factories in Korea.
- Mass production of the HAECHIE system will begin in June.
- Hyundai Motor Group is the first customer for the HAECHIE system.
- The HAECHIE system will be applied at Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) in Georgia.
- An HL Mando official stated that remote handling is essential because the plant is an unmanned automated facility.
- HL Mando's HAECHIE was selected for its integrated disaster-prevention process covering arc detection, monitoring, judgment, and control.
- The mass-production version of HAECHIE has evolved into a "smart disaster-prevention consultant."
- While the early model was a simple arc detection system, the mass-production version introduces AI to analyze arc characteristics and patterns and even assess risk levels.
- Depending on the assessed risk level, the system can perform essential preemptive measures such as inspection, equipment replacement, and changes to operating conditions.
- The system incorporates infrared sensors and thermal imaging cameras to track and detect even minute temperature changes.
- HL Mando will unveil the mass-production product at the 2026 International Electric Power Exhibition held at COEX in Seoul from the 6th to the 8th.
