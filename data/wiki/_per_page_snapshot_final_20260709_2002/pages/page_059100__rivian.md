# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-06

**Source:** [Rivian to Establish East Coast Headquarters in Atlanta as Part of Georgia Expansion](https://evmagz.com/rivian-to-establish-east-coast-headquarters-in-atlanta-as-part-of-georgia-expansion/)  ·  `page_059100`

## Facts

- Rivian, the electric vehicle manufacturer based in Illinois, announced plans to establish its East Coast headquarters in Atlanta, Georgia.
- The new East Coast headquarters office will be located in the Junction Krog District on Auburn Avenue, adjacent to the Atlanta Beltline’s Eastside Trail.
- The Atlanta headquarters facility is expected to open in late 2025.
- When fully operational, the Atlanta headquarters will employ approximately 500 people.
- In a statement announcing the new site, Rivian said: “Atlanta is a natural fit for Rivian, home to a talented and diverse workforce, world-class schools and universities, innovative spirit, and importantly, a community we want to become a part of.”
- The headquarters decision follows Rivian's previously announced plans to build a $5 billion manufacturing plant in Stanton Springs.
- The Stanton Springs manufacturing plant is located roughly 40 miles east of Atlanta near the town of Social Circle.
- If completed as planned, the Stanton Springs facility would be the largest industrial complex in Georgia.
- The Stanton Springs plant is designed to produce up to 400,000 electric vehicles per year.
- In 2022, Rivian received a state and local incentive package valued at approximately $1.5 billion to support the manufacturing project.
- The timeline for fulfilling the investment and hiring targets for the Georgia plant was extended to 2030 amid local legal challenges and temporary delays.
- Construction on the plant resumed after Rivian secured a $6.6 billion loan from the U.S. Department of Energy in 2024.
- The DOE loan is intended to accelerate domestic EV manufacturing capacity.
- Rivian went public in 2021.
- Rivian currently manufactures its R1 electric SUVs, pickup trucks, and delivery vans at its facility in Normal, Illinois.
- Rivian stated it will release further details regarding employment opportunities and partnerships in Georgia closer to the opening of the Atlanta office.
