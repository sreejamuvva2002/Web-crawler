# Textron Specialized Vehicles

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-01-13

**Source:** [Textron Specialized Vehicles Acquires Douglas Equipment | Aviation Pros](https://www.aviationpros.com/ground-support-worldwide/gse/press-release/12034303/textron-specialized-vehicles-acquires-douglas-equipment)  ·  `page_051694`

## Facts

- Textron Specialized Vehicles group of Textron Inc. acquired the Douglas Equipment business from Curtiss-Wright Flow Control (UK) Limited, a subsidiary of Curtiss-Wright Corporation.
- The acquisition was executed through Textron Ground Support Equipment UK Limited.
- Douglas Equipment is a manufacturer of ground support equipment (GSE) in the aviation industry based in Cheltenham, England, UK.
- Going forward, Douglas Equipment will operate as part of Textron Specialized Vehicles’ GSE business, which also includes TUG Technologies Corp.
- TUG Technologies Corp. is a leading manufacturer of ground support equipment based in Kennesaw, Ga.
- Douglas specializes in towbarless and conventional aircraft tractors and runway friction measurement systems.
- TUG Technologies leads in baggage and tow tractors, pushbacks, belt loaders, ground power units, air-starts, and mobile HVAC units.
- Douglas will continue to operate from its UK facilities in Cheltenham and Poyle, United Kingdom with its workforce of more than 90 employees intact.
- The Douglas brand joins the Textron Specialized Vehicles companies’ stable of brands alongside TUG™, E-Z-GO®, Cushman®, and Bad Boy® Buggies.
- Kevin Holleran serves as president of the Textron Specialized Vehicles group of Textron Inc.
- The acquisition was announced on January 13, 2015.
- The acquisition occurred only a few months after Textron's acquisition of TUG last spring.
- Stefaan Ver Eecke is vice president and general manager, GSE for Textron Specialized Vehicles.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles designs and manufactures products under the E-Z-GO®, Cushman®, Bad Boy® Buggies and TUG™ brands for environments ranging from golf courses to factories, airports to planned communities, and theme parks to hunting preserves.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company that leverages a global network of aircraft, defense, industrial, and finance businesses.
