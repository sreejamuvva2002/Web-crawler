# K&D Electric

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Coweta County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

K&D Electric provides professional electric vehicle (EV) charging station installation services for commercial properties across Coweta County, offering Level 2 and DC fast-charging solutions for retail, office, fleet, and multi-family applications, as well as high-power 480 V chargers for fleet operations and public hubs.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Provides EV charging infrastructure installation and related services to support electric mobility in Georgia.

## Timeline

- **1994** — Company began providing commercial electrical, HVAC, and petroleum system services (deep expertise since 1994).

## Related organizations & people

- City of Atlanta Small Business Enterprise — Certification for public-sector projects

## Key facts

- Offers Level 2 (208/240V) charging stations for retail, office, and multi-family properties.
- Provides high-power 480V charging for fleet operations, public charging hubs, and highway corridor locations.
- Delivers scalable charging solutions for commercial fleets, including depot charging and route optimization.
- Conducts comprehensive site evaluations to determine optimal charger placement and required electrical upgrades.
- Combines expertise in commercial electrical, HVAC, and petroleum systems under one roof for seamless coordination.
- Manages projects from concept to completion with a single contractor for total accountability.
- Fully licensed, bonded, and insured in Georgia and Florida.
- Certified as City of Atlanta Small Business Enterprise for public-sector projects.
