# Car Tech & HL Mando Opelika Facilities

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-28

**Source:** [Opelika Preparing For Multi-Million-Dollar Industry Expansions - The Observer](https://opelikaobserver.com/2022/12/28/opelika-preparing-for-multi-million-dollar-industry-expansions/)  ·  `page_045911`

## Facts

- Opelika Mayor Gary Fuller recently announced that two existing Opelika manufacturing companies will be expanding and creating more jobs.
- Car Tech will invest more capital to build and expand its current facility.
- Car Tech is a Tier 1 supplier for BMW Group.
- Car Tech is located in Opelika’s Fox Run Business Park along the I-85 corridor.
- Car Tech's current facility has over 388,000 square feet for stamping and assembly operations.
- Car Tech plans to invest $18.3 million for an additional 85,800 square feet of warehouse and production space.
- Car Tech plans to purchase additional manufacturing equipment as part of the expansion.
- Car Tech Plant Manager Shea Phillips stated that continued growth has come from both new customers and existing customers.
- Car Tech currently employs 115 employees.
- The Car Tech expansion will create 15 new team member positions filling warehousing, production, and technical roles.
- Mayor Gary Fuller stated that Opelika is clearly focused on providing opportunities for current businesses to grow and bring additional good-paying jobs to the community.
- City of Opelika Economic Development Director Lori Huguley called Car Tech a great community partner and a testament to the local workforce and business climate.
- HL Mando America Corporation will invest $11.1 million to upgrade its current facility.
- The HL Mando expansion will create 26 new jobs.
- HL Mando’s Opelika operations produce brake calipers and suspension systems for customers throughout the U.S.
- HL Mando's parent company, Mando Corporation, is a leading global automotive parts manufacturer specializing in chassis components and systems.
- Mando Corporation was the first manufacturer in Korea to develop advanced products including ABS, ESP, CDC, ESC, and EPS.
- Mando Corporation is headquartered in South Korea and operates 20 manufacturing plants and 18 R&D centers in 10 countries.
- Mando Corporation employs over 11,700 associates worldwide.
- Mayor Fuller noted HL Mando is one of Opelika's largest manufacturing employers and expressed appreciation for the employment opportunities provided to citizens.
- Economic Development Director Lori Huguley added that the city appreciates HL Mando's investment and supports their continued growth and success.
- A publisher's note corrected a previous article's error, clarifying Car Tech's investment is $18.3 million and HL Mando's investment is $11.1 million.
