## Facts

- Goodie Nation announced the Goodie Nation Product Strategy Sprint Series in collaboration with Porsche Cars North America, Inc.
- The initiative is a 90-day virtual program where management and related functional experts from Porsche will advise 20 founders around creating enterprise products.
- Eligible startup spaces for participation include Customer Experience, Future of Work, Logistics & Mobility, Supply Chain & Manufacturing, Big Data, Analytics & Security, and Climate Tech & Sustainability.
- Participants will experience virtual lunches with Porsche, weekly accountability group huddles, office hours, storytelling help from Goodie Nation, and invitations to appear on Goodie Nation podcast episodes.
- Applications for the program are due by December 10.
- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, at One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA.
- PCNA has been headquartered in Atlanta since 1998.
- PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera and Taycan.
- PCNA supports 193 independently owned and operated Porsche dealerships in the U.S., including supplying parts, service, marketing, and training.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Porsche's racing heritage boasts some 30,000-plus motorsport wins to date.
- Kjell Gruner serves as President and CEO of Porsche Cars North America, Inc.
- Joey Womack serves as CEO of Goodie Nation.
- Previous Goodie Nation mentorship programs have included collaborations with companies such as Verizon, AARP and Deloitte.
- More than 300 founders currently have the support of Goodie Nation to drive their business.
