## Facts

- The second generation Sorento was Kia's first model manufactured in the United States.
- It was built at Kia's new US$1 billion West Point, Georgia factory.
- The West Point, Georgia factory also builds the sister vehicle to the Sorento, the Hyundai Santa Fe.
- In the US, the Sorento continues to be produced at the West Point, Georgia assembly plant.
- The fourth generation Sorento is assembled at the United States: West Point, Georgia (KMMG) facility.
- Kia continued to build the North American-market Sorento at its assembly plant in West Point, Georgia, United States.
