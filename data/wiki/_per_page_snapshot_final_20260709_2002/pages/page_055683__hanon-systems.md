# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-28

**Source:** [Hanon Systems To Invest Over $155M In Ont. Facility - Clean Technology Canada](https://www.cleantechnology.ca/hanon-systems-to-invest-over-155m-in-ont-facility/)  ·  `page_055683`

## Facts

- Hanon Systems is an automotive parts supplier.
- Hanon Systems is investing $155 million to build a new facility in Woodbridge, Ontario.
- The new facility will be 284,200 square feet.
- The new facility will manufacture e-compressors, which are essential components for electric vehicles.
- The project will create 300 jobs.
- The investment aims to strengthen Ontario’s growing EV supply chain.
- Vic Fedeli is the minister of economic development in Ontario.
- Vic Fedeli stated that Ontario has all elements necessary to build future electric vehicles, including leading global parts manufacturers like Hanon Systems who are continuing to choose Ontario to expand and grow their operations.
- Vic Fedeli noted that Hanon Systems' investment in Woodbridge will create hundreds of local jobs for auto workers and strengthen Ontario's reputation as a North American leader in automotive manufacturing.
- Hanon Systems currently employs over 970 employees across its Belleville and Concord facilities.
- Hanon Systems supplies automakers throughout North America.
- The new Woodbridge facility will be North America’s first e-compressor plant.
- The facility will have the capability to produce up to 900,000 e-compressors annually.
- Ontario is providing Hanon Systems with $10 million through its Invest Ontario Fund in support of the investment.
- Nurdal Kücükkaya is president and co-CEO of Hanon Systems.
- Nurdal Kücükkaya stated that the company is pleased to expand its manufacturing presence in Ontario to increase proximity to vehicle manufacturers and better meet customer demand.
- Nurdal Kücükkaya noted that the expansion demonstrates a commitment to growth and proves customer confidence in the ability to deliver electric compressors to the market.
- Subu Nagasubramony is president and co-CEO of Hanon Systems.
- Subu Nagasubramony stated that the company investigated several locations for the new plant.
- Subu Nagasubramony noted that the partnership with the Government of Ontario and Invest Ontario was a significant element in the decision to locate the plant there.
