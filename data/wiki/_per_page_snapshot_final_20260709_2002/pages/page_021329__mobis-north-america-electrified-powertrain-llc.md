# MOBIS North America Electrified Powertrain, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-13

**Source:** [MOBIS North America Electrified Powertrain, LLC: Named Georgia Developers Association’s 2023 Deal of the Year for Mid-Size Communities - Heartwood Life](https://heartwoodlife.com/mobis-north-america-electrified-powertrain-llc-named-georgia-developers-associations-2023-deal-of-the-year-for-mid-size-communities/)  ·  `page_021329`

## Facts

- On Monday, November 13, 2023, MOBIS North America Electrified Powertrain, LLC received the Georgia Economic Developers Association (GEDA) Deal of the Year Award in the Mid-Size Community category.
- The GEDA award recognizes outstanding achievement in the location or expansion of new businesses in Georgia that meet community economic development goals and involve significant community collaboration.
- MOBIS was recognized alongside community leaders from the Development Authority of Bryan County (DABC), City of Richmond Hill, Bryan County, Raydient Places, and the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD).
- GEDA is a non-profit association of professionals and volunteers involved in economic development that aims to increase effectiveness, encourage cooperation, exchange information, upgrade professional skills, and foster closer working relationships among Georgia economic development practitioners.
- Jon Seagraves, DABC Chairman, stated that recruiting MOBIS to Belfast Commerce Park at Heartwood was a team effort and thanked MOBIS representatives Mr. Park and Mr. Lee for being instrumental in the company's decision to choose Bryan County.
- Hyundai MOBIS is a globally recognized South Korean automotive parts maker founded as Hyundai Precision & Industries Corporation in 1977.
- The company forms the "parts and services" arm for South Korean automakers Hyundai Motor Company, Genesis Motors, and Kia Motors.
- MOBIS's business scope encompasses key automotive parts, specifically e-powertrain systems (Battery Systems and Power Electric Systems), three major modules (Chassis Modules, Cockpit Modules, and Front-end Modules), and aftermarket sales and service.
- Since 2019, the Development Authority of Bryan County has assisted with projects representing over 5,000 new jobs and over $1.7 billion in capital investment.
- The DABC's tracked investments exclude the Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America project, which involves 8,500 jobs and $7.59 billion in capital investment, making it the largest economic development project in Georgia state history.
- The Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America project was recruited to Bryan County by the Savannah Harbor I-16 Corridor Joint Development Authority, of which Bryan County is a member.
- Heartwood is a new community being developed in Richmond Hill, Georgia, by Raydient Places + Properties, a taxable subsidiary of real estate investment trust Rayonier Inc.
