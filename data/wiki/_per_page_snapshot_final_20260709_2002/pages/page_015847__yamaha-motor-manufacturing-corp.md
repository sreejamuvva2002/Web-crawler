# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2013-06-12

**Source:** [Yamaha Expanding Vehicle Production In Georgia | Manufacturing.net](https://www.manufacturing.net/automotive/news/13091595/yamaha-expanding-vehicle-production-in-georgia)  ·  `page_015847`

## Facts

- Officials at Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corp. say they've opened a second assembly plant in Newnan.
- The company will hire 100 employees to work on a new utility vehicle.
- Yamaha has completed transferring all of its ATV production to the Coweta County plant south of Atlanta.
- The plant now employs 1,250 workers.
- In addition to the 100 to be hired, an additional 300 workers could be added in years to come.
- The facility already produces Yamaha's Grizzly 700 FI all-terrain vehicle and the YFZ450R sport ATV.
- The new plant will produce the Viking side-by-side vehicle and other models.
