## Facts

- Dorsett Industries is located at 1304 May Street, Dalton, GA 30721, United States.
- The company designs and manufactures quality automotive and marine carpet for the replacement market, as well as original equipment manufacturers.
- Products are sold only in full rolls to distributors, mat manufacturers, molders and Tier 1 suppliers to the truck and automotive industry.
- Dorsett Industries is an ISO 9001:2000 certified company.
- The company supplies high quality carpet to every major automotive manufacturer.
- Dorsett Industries has a total of 5 facilities in Dalton with 400,000 square feet of manufacturing and warehouse capacity.
- Plant tour attendees are strictly prohibited from being competitors, and the attendee list will be reviewed to uninvite any competitors.
- Closed-toed/flat bottom shoes are mandatory for tour attendees, with high heels explicitly banned.
