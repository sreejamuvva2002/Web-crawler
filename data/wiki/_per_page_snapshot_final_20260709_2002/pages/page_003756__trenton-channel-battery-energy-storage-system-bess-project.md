# Trenton Channel Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) Project

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-30

**Source:** [CX-270751: DTE Electric Company Trenton Channel Battery Energy Storage System Project | Department of Energy](https://www.energy.gov/nepa/articles/cx-270751-dte-electric-company-trenton-channel-battery-energy-storage-system-project)  ·  `page_003756`

## Facts

- The Trenton Channel Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) Project is located in Trenton, Michigan.
- The project entails constructing a 220-Megawatt (MW)/880 MWh lithium iron-phosphate (LFP) BESS.
- The project scope includes building a new substation.
- The project scope includes building new transmission lines connecting the BESS and the substation.
- The DOE Loan Programs Office (LPO) is considering whether to issue a loan guarantee of a funding facility to DTE Electric Company (DTE).
- The potential loan guarantee would be issued pursuant to the LPO's authority under the EIR 1706 Program.
- The Trenton Channel BESS Project is included in DTE’s recently released Integrated Resource Plan.
- The document was published by the Office of NEPA Policy and Compliance on November 21, 2024.
