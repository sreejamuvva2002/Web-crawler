## Facts

- Hyundai will open an auto plant and EV battery factory in Bryan County in 2025.
- The South Korean automaker will invest $5.5 billion and employ 8,100 workers in facilities to be built on a megasite property located adjacent to Interstate 16 near Ellabell.
- Trip Tollison serves as president and CEO of the Savannah Economic Development Authority (SEDA).
- SEDA signed an agreement with Hyundai on April 25, 2022, at 10:32 a.m.
- Tollison described signing the agreement with Hyundai as a big moment that changed Savannah's economy forever.
- Hyundai Motors announced plans on Friday to build a $5.5 billion manufacturing facility near Savannah.
- The site will employ 8,100 workers between an electric vehicle assembly plant and an adjacent EV battery factory.
- The project is the largest single economic development project in Georgia history.
