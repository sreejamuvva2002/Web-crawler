# Club Car

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-20

**Source:** [GOVERNOR BRIAN KEMP, FIRST LADY MARTY KEMP AND OTHER OFFICIALS JOIN CLUB CAR TO CELEBRATE OPENING FOR NEW APPLING MANUFACTURING FACILITY - The Golf Wire](https://thegolfwire.com/governor-kemp-club-car-manufacturing-facility/)  ·  `page_018489`

## Facts

- Club Car celebrated the opening of its new Appling Manufacturing Facility in Augusta, Ga., with a ribbon-cutting ceremony attended by Governor Brian Kemp and First Lady Marty Kemp.
- The Club Car Appling Manufacturing Facility provides 105,000 sq. ft. of flexible production and warehouse storage space.
- Club Car has committed nearly $8 million in capital investments into the facility over two years.
- The new facility currently operates a state-of-the-art production line that can produce up to 20 vehicles per day.
- The facility was opened to offer customers a better experience as demand in the United States for the Club Car Urban vehicle increases.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Club Car is bringing jobs into Georgia in the growing e-mobility sector and leading the way in compact utility EV manufacturing.
- Governor Kemp added that Club Car's growth and investment are a testament to work being done to continue to make Georgia the number one state to do business.
- Mark Wagner, Club Car President and CEO, stated that their innovative vehicles are impacting commercial fleet electrification, mini-mobility, and last mile delivery.
- Club Car has been a Georgia-based company for more than sixty years.
- The Club Car Urban is a street-legal, zero-emission vehicle designed as an alternative for college campuses, businesses, farms, and industrial sites around the world.
- The Club Car Urban can be equipped in different electric lithium options to provide extended range and improved performance in every climate.
- The Club Car Urban handles tasks such as facilities maintenance, moving equipment, last-mile delivery of packaged goods or food services.
- Club Car has over 65 years of experience producing small-wheel vehicles, including gas and electric golf, utility, and personal transportation vehicles.
- The Club Car Vehicle Group includes the luxury electric vehicle brand Garia and the lightweight electric utility vehicle brand Melex.
- Club Car is a global leader in sustainability and Zero Emission Vehicle (ZEV) technologies.
