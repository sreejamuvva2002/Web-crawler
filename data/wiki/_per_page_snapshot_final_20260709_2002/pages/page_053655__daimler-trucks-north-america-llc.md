# Daimler Trucks North America LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2017-05-09

**Source:** [Daimler Trucks North America Announces New Executive Appointments to its Operating Committee - Thomas Built Buses](https://thomasbuiltbuses.com/resources/news/daimler-trucks-north-america-announces-new-2017-05-09/)  ·  `page_053655`

## Facts

- Daimler Trucks North America (DTNA) announced executive appointments to its Operating Committee on May 9, 2017.
- Jeff Allen assumed the position of DTNA’s senior vice president of Production, Quality and Specialty Vehicles effective August 1, 2017.
- Stefan Kurschner will lead DTNA’s Aftermarket business units as senior vice president effective July 1, 2017.
- Roger Nielsen serves as president and CEO of Daimler Trucks North America.
- Jeff Allen currently serves as vice president of operations at Detroit Diesel Corporation (DDC) in Redford, Michigan.
- Jeff Allen has over twenty years of leadership and operational responsibility at Daimler Trucks.
- Jeff Allen previously led Detroit’s Powertrain Headquarters in Michigan and Detroit’s Axle Plant in Saltillo, Mexico.
- Roger Nielsen stated that Jeff Allen is the perfect candidate to lead DTNA’s manufacturing network, Freightliner Custom Chassis Corporation and Thomas Built Buses.
- Jeff Allen previously served as vice president of Operations for Thomas Built Buses in Highpoint, NC from 2004 through 2011.
- Jeff Allen started his career at DDC in 1992 as a Business Unit Manager.
- Jeff Allen accepted an assignment in Brazil with Detroit Diesel Motores do Brazil in 1997.
- Jeff Allen returned to Detroit as Director of Assembly from 2000 through 2004.
- Jeff Allen will be located in Fort Mill, South Carolina.
- Stefan Kurschner currently serves as president and CEO of Daimler Vehículos Comerciales Mexico.
- Stefan Kurschner brings over twenty-five years of global Daimler experience, including management positions in Germany, Argentina, Brazil, Spain, USA and Mexico.
- Stefan Kurschner has led Daimler Trucks Mexico since September 1, 2013.
- DTNA achieved 36.2% of the Mexican Class 8 market in 2016 under Stefan Kurschner’s leadership.
- Roger Nielsen stated that Stefan transformed the Mexican market for DTNA over the last four years and revitalized the Daimler Trucks Mexico workforce and Dealer Network.
- Stefan Kurschner previously served as president of Detroit Diesel Remanufacturing LLC until August 2013.
- In his new role, Stefan Kurschner will lead DTNA’s aftermarket functions including Central Service, Parts Sales & Marketing, Parts Distribution, Dealer Operations, and Remanufacturing.
- Stefan Kurschner will be located at the company’s headquarters in Portland, Oregon.
- Daimler Trucks North America LLC is headquartered in Portland, Ore.
- Daimler Trucks North America is the leading heavy-duty truck manufacturer in North America.
- Daimler Trucks North America produces and markets commercial vehicles under the Freightliner, Western Star and Thomas Built Buses nameplates.
- Daimler Trucks North America is a Daimler company, the world’s leading commercial vehicle manufacturer.
