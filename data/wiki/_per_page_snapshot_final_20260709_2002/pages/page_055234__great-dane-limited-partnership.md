# Great Dane Limited Partnership

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Great Dane’s Phill Pines to retire | FleetOwner](https://www.fleetowner.com/operations/article/21677792/great-danes-phill-pines-to-retire)  ·  `page_055234`

## Facts

- Great Dane Trailers president & COO Phill Pines announced his intention to retire from the company at the end of 2010 after more than 50 years in the trailer industry.
- Great Dane Trailers CEO William H. Crown will take on the additional roles of president & COO at the end of the year.
- Phill Pines led his family-owned business, Pines Trailer Corp., for 25 years before forming a partnership with the Crown Family in 1988.
- In January 1997, the partnership acquired Great Dane Trailers, and Pines assumed his current role as president & COO of Great Dane Limited Partnership.
- Bill Crown, president & CEO of CC Industries and CEO of Great Dane Limited Partnership, stated that Pines' focus, drive, and wisdom have been instrumental in creating the company's success.
- Phill Pines began his career in the trailer industry in 1958 working alongside his father, Leo Pines, at their Monon Trailer dealership, where they refurbished used trailers before starting Pines Trailer Corp. in 1965.
- Pines stated he will remain involved as a member of the Board of Advisors and has worked closely with Bill Crown for the last 13 years on strategic and operational issues.
- Bill Crown is the son of John Crown and grandson of Henry Crown, founder of Henry Crown and Company; he joined the family business in September 1993 in an executive marketing capacity before being promoted to president & CEO of CC Industries in October 1994.
- Bill Crown played a key role in the acquisition of Great Dane on January 1, 1997.
- Great Dane Trailers manufactures dry van, refrigerated and platform trailers.
- The company maintains division offices in Savannah, GA, and Chicago.
- Great Dane has manufacturing plants strategically located throughout the U.S., with distribution points across North and South America.
- Great Dane utilizes a network of company-owned branches and full-line independent dealers as well as parts-only independent dealers.
