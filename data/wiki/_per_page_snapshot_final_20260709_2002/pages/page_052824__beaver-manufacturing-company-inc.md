# Beaver Manufacturing Company, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Beaver Manufacturing expands with acquisition of Intercord Thüringen](https://www.innovationintextiles.com/industry-talk/beaver-manufacturing-expands-with-acquisition-of-intercord-thringen/)  ·  `page_052824`

## Facts

- Beaver Manufacturing Company, Inc. (BMC) is a North American supplier of technical fibre reinforcements for hoses and other industrial applications.
- BMC announced plans to expand its global presence by acquiring Intercord Thüringen GmbH of Germany.
- Intercord Thüringen GmbH is a producer of high-quality technical fibres.
- The acquisition brings together two technologies to increase the company’s customer base worldwide.
- The partnership enables BMC to add RFL treating technology to its product line.
- The acquisition adds BMC's third production plant across the globe.
- The manufacturing facility is located in Mühlhausen Thüringen, Germany.
- This new German facility rounds out BMC’s worldwide footprint alongside existing plants in the US and Mexico.
- Michael Dubin, Sr. Vice President & Chief Operating Officer of Beaver Manufacturing Company, Inc., stated that the acquisition makes a significant expansion into the European market and enters the tyre and belt industry.
- The German plant will continue to produce RFL treated yarns and cords, as well as hose products incorporating Beaverloc treatments.
- For more than 20 years, Intercord’s technology has produced cords for tyres, hoses and transmission belts in the automotive and industrial industries.
- Ramazan Yasbay, Managing Director of Intercord (who will continue as Managing Director of the German facility for BMC), stated that Beaver Manufacturing advanced the U.S. hose yarn industry with environmentally friendly treated yarn solutions and can now do the same in Germany.
- Adding their technology and product line to Intercord's will increase the level of innovation in this industry throughout Europe and the Americas.
- Despite a sluggish economy across the globe during the pandemic, BMC executives say this partnership will continue its growth and increase production.
- Only two years after opening its manufacturing site in Mexico, BMC promises job creation into 2021.
