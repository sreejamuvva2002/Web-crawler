## Facts

- Volvo Cars has made it its mission to provide freedom to move in a personal, sustainable and safe way.
- In 2024, Volvo Cars reported an operating income of 22.3bn SEK.
- In 2024, Volvo Cars reported an EBIT margin of 5.6%.
- In 2024, Volvo Cars recorded retail sales of 763.4k units.
- In 2024, Volvo Cars reported revenue of 400.2bn SEK.
- Since 2021, Volvo Cars has been publicly listed on the Nasdaq Stockholm stock exchange.
- The Volvo Car Group structure includes Volvo Cars, software company Zenseact, and HaleyTek, a developer for Android-powered infotainment systems.
- Volvo Car Group holds an 18% stake in its strategic affiliate, the electric performance brand Polestar.
- Since 1927, Volvo Cars' global headquarters has remained in Gothenburg, Sweden.
- Volvo Cars' Americas head office is located in Mahwah, New Jersey.
- Volvo Cars' APAC head office is located in Shanghai.
- Volvo Cars produces vehicles in Gothenburg (Sweden), Ghent (Belgium), Charleston (US), Chengdu (China), Daqing (China), and Taizhou (China).
- Volvo Cars sells its vehicles in more than 100 countries.
- Volvo Cars is committed to rethinking sustainability in its operations, cars, and society to help protect the shared world.
