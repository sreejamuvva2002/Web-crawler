# Shinsung Petrochemical

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** Lyons, Toombs County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Shinsung Petrochemical, an automotive supplier specializing in sealants, announced a $11.2 million investment to build a new manufacturing facility in Lyons, Toombs County, creating more than 30 jobs and serving as a key supplier for Hyundai Motor Group.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Automotive sealant supplier for Hyundai Motor Group, supporting EV component supply chain in Georgia.

## Key facts

- Shinsung specializes in automotive sealant products.
- The $11.2 million investment will create more than 30 new jobs in Toombs County.
- Construction of the facility at 1407 Highway 1 North, Lyons is expected to finish in 2025.
- Shinsung will be a key supplier for Hyundai Motor Group and the Metaplant in Bryan County.
- Governor Kemp noted that 83% of recent expansions are in rural communities, delivering over 18,300 new jobs and $12.5 billion in investment.
- Georgia has attracted more than 36,000 new jobs and over $28 billion in e-mobility, clean energy, and battery-related supply chain investments since 2018.
