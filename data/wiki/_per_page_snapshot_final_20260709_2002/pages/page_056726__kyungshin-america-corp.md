# Kyungshin America Corp.

**Source:** [Automotive Parts Supplier Signs 136,000 SF Lease at 2,600-Acre Logistics Park in Savannah - REBusinessOnline](https://rebusinessonline.com/automotive-parts-supplier-signs-136000-sf-lease-at-2600-acre-logistics-park-in-savannah/)  ·  `page_056726`

## Facts

- Automotive parts supplier Kyungshin America Corp. (KAC) signed a 136,000-square-foot lease at Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub (SGIH) in Georgia.
- The KAC facility is scheduled to open in February 2024.
- The facility will import and distribute automotive components to Hyundai Motor Group’s new electric vehicle plant.
- Hyundai Motor Group’s new electric vehicle plant represents a $7.5 billion investment.
- Hyundai Motor Group’s new electric vehicle plant is located near the Port of Savannah and serves the Southeast region.
- Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub (SGIH) comprises a total of 2,600 acres.
- SGIH is a public-private partnership between the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority (ECIDA) and Broe Real Estate Group.
- Broe Real Estate Group operates as the real estate affiliate of The Broe Group.
