## Facts

- Ozburn Electrical is a family-owned electrical company founded by Harold T. Ozburn in 1984.
- The company has operated as a licensed electrician and electrical contractor in Madison, Georgia for over 30 years.
- Ozburn Electrical provides commercial and industrial electrical services across Madison, Greensboro, Morgan County, and metro Atlanta, Georgia.
- The firm offers EV Charging Stations including Level 2 EV Chargers and Fast DC EV Charging.
- Ozburn Electrical provides Electric Vehicle (EV) Charger Installation including Level 2 and Level 3 Fast DC Charging for industrial, commercial, manufacturing, and retail facilities.
- All electrical installations and work comply with the NEC National Electrical Code, State, County, Municipality, and Electrical Power Provider Guidelines.
- The company's electricians complete a 2-year or 4-year electrical apprenticeship program established by the Independent Electrical Contractors Association (IEC), of which Ozburn is an active member.
