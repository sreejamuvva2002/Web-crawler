## Facts

- Georgia's strategic location helps move products and people seamlessly.
- Import and export with ease in Georgia by tapping into an extensive transportation network anchored by partners like the Georgia Ports Authority and Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport (ATL).
- Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport (ATL) is the most traveled and most efficient airport in the world.
- Georgia products can reach 80% of Americans within a 2-day drive.
- There are 4,600+ miles of rail and two Class 1 railroads, CSX and Norfolk Southern, creating the Southeast's most extensive rail system.
- 560,000+ tons of cargo moved annually via ATL.
- The Port of Savannah has approximately 10,000 feet of contiguous berth space.
- The Port of Savannah is America's single largest container terminal.
- Georgia's diverse talent and regions provide what businesses need to succeed across various industries.
- Select Georgia publishes annual industry publications covering top industries from aerospace and automotive manufacturing to life sciences and information technology.
