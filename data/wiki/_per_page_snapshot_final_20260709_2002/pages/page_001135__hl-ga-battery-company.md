# HL-GA Battery Company

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Georgia: Hyundai facility construction paused until 2026 - WJCL](https://www.wjcl.com/article/hyundai-ice-georgia-construction-paused/66064634)  ·  `page_001135`

## Facts

- Construction at the HL-GA Battery Company facility on the Hyundai megasite is paused until 2026 following an immigration raid.
- Homeland Security officials said 475 workers were detained at the construction site during the execution of a search warrant on September 4.
- More than 300 of those detained workers were South Korean nationals.
- Officials said the raid led to construction at the site being halted.
- In information released Friday, construction is not planned to resume for the next several months.
- According to a spokesperson, construction at the plant will remain on hold until the first half of 2026.
- LG Energy Solution stated that the U.S. is an extremely important market for them.
- LG Energy Solution has invested more than $25 billion in North American manufacturing.
- LG Energy Solution expects to create about 14,000 jobs in North America by 2027.
- In the mid-term, LG Energy Solution plans to locate half of its total global battery cell-making capacity in the U.S.
- LG Energy Solution remains committed to its projects in the U.S. and will continue to navigate the circumstances with the aim to continue necessary investments and business.
- Moving forward, LG Energy Solution will continue to cooperate with the Korean and U.S. governments on the visa process.
