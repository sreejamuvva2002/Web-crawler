# Hanon Systems

## Facts

- Hanon Systems offers a family of eCompressors ranging from compact 33cc units to high-output 45cc models.
- The eCompressor family delivers industry-leading acoustic performance and efficiency across a wide voltage range.
- The compressors are designed to handle advanced refrigerants including R744 and R290.
- These compressors provide the durability, control, and performance needed for today’s electrified and heat pump systems.
- The modular design for 33cc and 45cc eCompressors enables full-range manufacturing on a single line to cut costs and accelerate production.
- The modular design supports voltage applications up to 800V.
- The high-performance, high-durability 45cc eCompressor offers high speed operation to support system approaches like vapor injection or hot gas bypass systems.
- The R1234yf/R290 eCompressor is designed for EV and Hybrid vehicle platforms.
- The R744 eCompressor features a durable scroll design controlled by an integrated electric motor and sophisticated electronic inverter.
- The R744 eCompressor provides efficient cooling for conventional, heat pump and multi-loop cooling systems.
- To accommodate the R744 system’s unique requirements, the R744 eCompressor features minimized component clearances, an improved casting process producing low porosity and high strength, a backpressure control valve for scroll stability, and a modular inverter with a single ePin interface.
- The Rotary Suction Compressor features a fixed displacement swashplate design that draws refrigerant through the center shaft.
- The Rotary Suction Compressor delivers better fuel economy by creating less suction loss than a traditional suction valve.
- The Rotary Suction Compressor is offered in a wide range of displacements, with features designed to improve coefficient of performance (COP) and reliability.
- The Variable Swashplate Compressor varies its duty cycle and the amount of refrigerant flow to offer improved power consumption and fuel economy.
- The Variable Swashplate Compressor is available in a range of displacements and clutched or clutchless designs, with enhanced design features to improve efficiency, durability and noise, vibration and harshness (NVH).
- The Vapor Injection eCompressor allows two-stage compression within a single compressor.
- Optional liquid gas separator (LGS) or economizer and electronic expansion valves (EXVs) are modularized for efficient options in the Vapor Injection eCompressor.
- The Vapor Injection eCompressor improves driving range and battery cooling capability by providing a higher coefficient of performance (COP).
- The R1234yf/R290 eCompressor is designed for EV and Hybrid vehicle platforms.
- The R744 eCompressor is designed for EV and Hybrid vehicle platforms.
- The Rotary Suction Compressor is designed for ICE vehicle platforms.
- The Variable Swashplate Compressor is designed for ICE vehicle platforms.
- The Vapor Injection eCompressor is designed for EV and Hybrid vehicle platforms.
