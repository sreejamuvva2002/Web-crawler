# Perkins

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-27

**Source:** [Perkins Achieves a Milestone: 100,000 Engines Released in October - News Caterpillar](https://www.news-caterpillar.com/perkins-achieves-a-milestone-100000-engines-released-in-october/)  ·  `page_048089`

## Facts

- October 2023 marked a significant achievement for Perkins as their renowned 2.8 and 3.6-liter engines surpassed the production of 100,000 units.
- Since their inception in May 2019, these engines have powered an array of machinery, ranging from excavators to tractors and from dumpers to pumping equipment.
- The journey of these engines, in sync with both original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and end-users, reflects Perkins’ unwavering commitment to innovation and excellence.
- The 2.8 and 3.6-liter engines are integrated into over 80 different machine models.
- These engines offer high power density and torque despite their compact size.
- These models have shown up to an 8 percent improvement in fuel economy during operation.
- The 3.6-liter model delivers power up to 100 kW (134 hp) with up to 550 Nm of torque.
- Features ensuring ease of maintenance include single side servicing, hydraulic lash adjusters, and long-life belts.
- The 2.8 and 3.6-liter engines employ a DPF regeneration process that remains undetected by the operator to ensure uninterrupted operation.
- Matt Coleman, product manager of the range, stated that they have met high expectations for the 2.8 and 3.6-liter engines.
- Jaz Gill, vice president of global sales, marketing, service, and parts, stated that the addition of the 2.8 and 3.6-liter engines to their range of sustainable power solutions embodied that sentiment.
