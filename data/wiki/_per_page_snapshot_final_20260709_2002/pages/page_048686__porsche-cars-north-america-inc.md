# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-04-26

**Source:** [ANA5 - Driver And Passenger Seat Wire Harness Damage - 2020-2021 Porsche Taycan](https://porsche.oemdtc.com/473/ana5-driver-and-passenger-seat-wire-harness-damage-2020-2021-porsche-taycan/)  ·  `page_048686`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. is conducting a safety recall for certain 2020-2022 Taycan vehicles under campaign ANA5 / NHTSA ID 22V-278.
- The issue involves the driver and passenger seat wire harness potentially becoming damaged during horizontal or longitudinal seat adjustment.
- Damaged wiring harnesses may deactivate air bags and seat belt pre-tensioners, causing the red air bag warning light to illuminate.
- Approximately 9,570 units (or 9,500 VINs) are affected by this recall.
- Dealers will inspect the seat wire harness for damage, add fabric tape to secure the harness, and repair damaged wires as necessary, free of charge.
- Owner notification letters were mailed on July 22, 2022.
- The remedy was not immediately available at the time of the initial notice; customers were instructed to wait for a follow-up letter once remedy parts were obtained.
- Porsche decided to conduct the safety recall as a precautionary measure on April 20, 2022.
- Between January 2022 and April 2022, field analysis identified 3 instances outside the United States of wire harness damage resulting in the activation of the red air bag warning lamp.
- No incidents or injuries associated with this issue were reported by Porsche as of the recall announcement.
- The recall remedy takes up to 1 business day to perform at an authorized dealer.
- Vehicles affected by a stop delivery were to be remedied prior to vehicle delivery.
- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. customer support is located at One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354.
- The technical repair procedure involves removing front seats, checking the drive spindle for trapped fibers, and reworking or repairing the wiring harness using textile fabric tape (approx. 40-50 mm width) and commercially available connecting sleeves/repair cables.
- Warranty processing labor times vary by scope: Scope 1 (undelivered vehicles) is 110 TU; Scope 2 (delivered vehicles) is 126 TU; Scope 3 (1-9 lines repaired) is 197 TU; Scope 4 (10-18 lines repaired) is 269 TU.
- Required parts for warranty include external hexagon round-head bolt M10 x 28 (8 pieces) and textile fabric tape (ANA50000001, costing $1.88).
- For Scope 3 and 4 repairs, additional materials include connecting sleeve, repair cable, and heat shrink hose (ANA50000002), costing $1.88 for Scope 3 and $3.75 for Scope 4.
- Porsche requires the use of specific tools including a battery charger with at least 90 A current rating, PIWIS Tester 3, and torque wrenches (40-200 Nm).
- The recall applies to Taycan models Y1A, Y1B, and Y1C from model years 2020 through 2022.
