# Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. (TSV) has its principal location in Augusta, Georgia.
- TSV produces off-highway motor vehicle model years 2019-2022.
- In August 2023, the California Air Resources Board (CARB) reached a settlement with TSV related to the company’s production of vehicles that were not compliant with exhaust emissions certification requirements.
- The violation was in violation of Health and Safety Code sections 43151-43153, and the California Code of Regulations, title 13, sections 2400(a)(2) and 2410(a)(2).
- TSV fully cooperated with CARB to resolve this matter.
- Terms of the settlement include a requirement for TSV to comply with all other CARB regulations going forward.
- The total settlement amount is $72,500.
- Of the total settlement amount, $36,250 in civil penalties will be deposited into CARB’s Air Pollution Control Fund, which provides funding for projects and research to improve California’s air quality.
- The remaining amount of $36,250 will fund a Supplemental Environmental Project (SEP) entitled Fresno TREES Phase 4 – Malaga Recreation Park.
- This SEP provides tree planting, air quality tracking, and tree care and maintenance for Malaga Recreation Park.
