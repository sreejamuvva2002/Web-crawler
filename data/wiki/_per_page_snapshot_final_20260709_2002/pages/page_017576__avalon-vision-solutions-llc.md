# Avalon Vision Solutions, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Industrial QC Completes 4th Acquisition - Benford Capital Partners](https://benfordcapital.com/industrial-qc-completes-4th-acquisition/)  ·  `page_017576`

## Facts

- Benford Capital Partners and Industrial QC Holdings, LLC announced the acquisition of Avalon Vision Solutions, LLC (AVS).
- AVS represents Industrial QC’s fourth acquisition, following LiteSentry, LLC in November 2019, Softsolution GmbH in July 2021, and Strainoptics, LLC in August 2022.
- AVS was founded in 1991 and is headquartered in Lithia Springs, Georgia.
- AVS is a global leader in machine vision solutions for the injection molding and die casting industries.
- The company’s flagship product, MoldWatcher, is a proprietary vision system used by leading manufacturers worldwide to ensure consistent quality, enhance efficiency, and eliminate costly downtime in molding and casting operations.
- The addition of AVS expands Industrial QC into the plastics market, supporting its strategy of building a leading quality-control instrumentation platform across diverse applications and end markets.
- Industrial QC is actively seeking additional acquisitions of instrumentation and services businesses selling into the glass, plastics, and other industrial markets.
- Ed Kachnic is the Founder of AVS.
- Brian Staff is an Operating Partner at Benford Capital Partners.
- Founded in 2004, Benford Capital is a Chicago-based private equity firm focused on buying and building leading lower middle market companies in partnership with founders and management.
- Since inception, Benford Capital has acquired over 60 companies and currently owns 16 platform companies.
- BCP’s industry areas of focus include Food, Ingredients & Consumer Products, Industrial Technology & Manufacturing, Specialty Services, and Agricultural Products & Services.
- Ed Kachnic stated: “We’re excited to partner with Benford Capital and Industrial QC, whose partnership-oriented approach aligns well with our culture.”
- Ed Kachnic stated: “With Industrial QC’s extensive resources and experience in machine vision instrumentation, AVS is well positioned to grow in the years ahead.”
- Brian Staff stated: “We are thrilled to add another leading machine vision business to the Industrial QC platform.”
- Brian Staff stated: “AVS’s quality control solutions for high-cost-of-failure injection molding and die casting applications are an excellent complement to the systems of Industrial QC.”
