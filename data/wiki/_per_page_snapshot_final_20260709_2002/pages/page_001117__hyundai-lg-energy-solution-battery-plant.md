# Hyundai-LG Energy Solution Battery Plant

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-04

**Source:** [Hyundai battery plant in Georgia reportedly set to open months after ICE raid](https://www.ajc.com/business/2026/04/hyundai-battery-plant-in-georgia-set-to-open-months-after-ice-raid/)  ·  `page_001117`

## Facts

- The factory slated to provide batteries to Hyundai’s electric vehicle plant near Savannah will open later this month following a prolonged delay caused by an immigration raid last year that rippled across Georgia and the globe.
- Hyundai CEO José Muñoz announced the battery plant’s imminent opening Tuesday at the Semafor World Economy conference in Washington.
- Semafor is a digital news outlet and the annual event features Fortune 500 CEOs, government officials and business leaders.
- The battery factory is a joint operation between the Korean carmaker and Korean battery giant LG Energy Solution.
- The facility is located on the Hyundai Metaplant campus along I-16, about 30 minutes west of downtown Savannah.
- The plant will produce batteries for Hyundai's electric vehicle plant near Savannah.
