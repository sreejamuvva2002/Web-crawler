# Superior Essex

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Superior Essex Agrees to Acquire Remaining Ownership Stake in Essex Furukawa Global Joint Venture from Furukawa Electric | Essex Solutions](https://essexsolutions.com/news/superior-essex-agrees-to-acquire-remaining-ownership-stake-in-essex-furukawa-global-joint-venture-from-furukawa-electric/)  ·  `page_010707`

## Facts

- Superior Essex, a global leader in magnet wire and communication cables, is a majority owner of the Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire joint venture with Furukawa Electric.
- Superior Essex has agreed to take over the remaining minority interest held by Furukawa Electric in Essex Furukawa.
- When the transaction is completed, Superior Essex will become the sole owner of the brand, assets and operations currently conducted by the global Essex Furukawa joint venture.
- Superior Essex plans to relaunch its magnet wire business under a new company name in 2024.
- The new entity will continue to operate all current facilities of the Essex Furukawa joint venture around the world.
- These facilities include Japanese and Malaysian (Kuala Lumpur) magnet wire facilities contributed by Furukawa Electric in October 2020.
- Superior Essex CEO Daniel Choi thanked the entire Furukawa Electric team for their collaboration over the last several years.
- The collaboration between Superior Essex and Furukawa Electric in Essex Furukawa began in October 2020, with the aim of leveraging combined experience to drive innovation, manufacturing excellence, technological advancement, and customer service.
- Over the course of the joint venture, both companies established market leadership positions in North America, Europe, and Asia.
- Essex Furukawa forged strategic customer partnerships and expedited research and development advancements.
- The collaboration resulted in significant synergies, including the expansion of custom product solutions, sharing of best practices, and enhanced customer service capabilities.
- The joint venture facilitated the establishment of additional points of shipping, expanded global sales and technical teams, increased global rod & foundry resources, and created a diversified global innovation team.
- The global innovation team focused particularly on electric and hybrid vehicle development.
- The acquisition marks an important milestone for Superior Essex as it takes full control of its global operations and strengthens its commitment to delivering exceptional products and services worldwide.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, Essex Energy Italy, IVA, Lacroix + Kress, Hi-Wire, and Essex Brownell.
- Superior Essex has over 3,000 employees in 11 countries across three continents.
- Superior Essex is the leading global provider of magnet wire and is on the forefront of communications fiber application as well as Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- Superior Essex is instrumental in creating disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex is committed to pioneering smart building technology while setting the pace on sustainability.
- Furukawa Electric Group was established in 1884 and has 127 group companies including Furukawa Electric Co., Ltd.
- The Furukawa Electric Group employs approximately 50,000 people around the world.
- Furukawa Electric provides technologies, products, and services for infrastructure fields (information and energy), automotive systems fields, and electronics fields.
- Furukawa Electric's core technologies include metals, polymers, photonics, and high frequency.
