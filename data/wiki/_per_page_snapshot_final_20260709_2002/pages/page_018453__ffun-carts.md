# FFUN Carts

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [FFUN Carts](https://ffuncarts.com/)  ·  `page_018453`

## Facts

- FFUN Carts is an authorized distributor for Club Car and operates under the FFUN Group.
- The company carries new and used electric and gas golf carts and utility vehicles for applications including golf, work, transportation, hospitality, and recreation.
- FFUN Carts operates a 6,000-square-foot service facility equipped to service products using only genuine Club Car parts.
- Club Car has been building industry-leading vehicles since 1958 across golf, utility, transportation, food and beverage, hospitality, and rough-terrain applications.
- FFUN Carts maintains a rental fleet of over 100 vehicles for events and tournaments.
- The team is factory-trained and certified by Club Car to provide expert service and maintenance for Club Car vehicles and other major brands.
- FFUN Carts carries new Club Car Precedent, Tempo, and CarryAll products.
- FFUN Carts maintains constant communication with Club Car in Augusta, GA for custom or unique vehicle needs.
- The company installed a 57.6 kWp Roof Top 3 Phase Solar System in October 2020.
- The solar system produced 53.83 MWh in its first year and reached 223 MWh produced by August 20, 2024.
- FFUN Carts offers free Level 2 Tesla charging to the public on the west side of its building.
