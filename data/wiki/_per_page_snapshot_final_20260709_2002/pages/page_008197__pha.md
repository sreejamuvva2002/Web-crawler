# PHA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-13

**Source:** [Hyundai’s Georgia EV campus attracts another supplier | Manufacturing Dive](https://www.manufacturingdive.com/news/Hyundai-supplier-PHA-Georgia-Metaplant/644718/)  ·  `page_008197`

## Facts

- Auto parts manufacturer PHA will spend $67 million on a new facility in Georgia.
- The facility is intended to supply Hyundai Motor Group’s upcoming EV campus, known as the Metaplant.
- The governor’s office announced the investment last week.
- The Savannah-area facility will produce door modules.
- The facility will also produce tailgate and hood latches.
- These components are designed for direct use in automakers’ assembly lines.
- PHA selected the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center to house its new facility.
- Operations at the new facility are expected to begin in 2024.
- The facility is expected to create 402 jobs.
- South Korea-based PHA joins a growing number of parts manufacturers streaming to Georgia to support Hyundai’s future EV campus.
- Hyundai’s future EV campus broke ground in October.
- Georgia officials originally predicted offsite suppliers would invest nearly $1 billion in locations around the state.
- Investments from offsite suppliers to date have exceeded that prediction by $800 million.
- PHA will be the second supplier to settle at the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- Seoyon E-Hwa announced plans in early February to invest almost $76 million in a facility for interior auto parts at the same location.
- The industrial site is part of the Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) program.
- The GRAD program has fast-tracked up to 60 locations for construction through advance due diligence by the state.
- Georgia Department of Economic Development Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that projects like Hyundai and its suppliers underscore the importance of site preparation to attract jobs and opportunities to a community.
- Pat Wilson noted that companies are prioritizing locations that have already completed GRAD certification to meet demand and come online quickly.
