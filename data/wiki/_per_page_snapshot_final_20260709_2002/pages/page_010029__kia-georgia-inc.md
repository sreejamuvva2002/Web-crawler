# Kia Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2009-11-16

## Facts

- Kia Georgia, Inc. is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Motors Corporation based in Seoul, Korea.
- Kia Georgia has an annual production capacity of 350,000 units.
- Kia Georgia is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Georgia.
- Kia Georgia began mass production on Nov. 16, 2009.
- Kia Georgia manufactures the Telluride SUV.
- Kia Georgia manufactures the Sorento SUV.
- Kia Georgia manufactures the Sportage SUV.
- Kia Georgia manufactures the all-electric EV9.
- Kia Georgia manufactures the all-electric EV6.
- Kia's guiding principles include providing high-quality, high-value vehicles at prices well below the competition.
- Kia backs its vehicles with a 10-year/100,000-mile Warranty Program and a customer-first policy.
- Safety is always a priority for Kia from concept to crash test.
- Kia ensures vehicle safety through a rigorous system of collision tests and simulations.
- Kia maintains a thorough customer-oriented quality control system that assesses customer needs and demands to enhance the production process.
