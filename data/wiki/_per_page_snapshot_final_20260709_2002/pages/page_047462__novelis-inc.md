# Novelis, Inc.

## Facts

- Atlanta-headquartered aluminum rolling and recycling major Novelis, Inc. decided to collaborate with the Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech) to establish a new innovation hub at Georgia Tech.
- The hub will focus on innovative research initiatives by connecting Novelis’ technical and business innovators with the Institute’s students and faculty.
- Novelis committed $2.5 million to promote transformational research and related educational endeavors at Georgia Tech.
- Georgia Tech will recruit a senior faculty member to serve as the Director of the Novelis Innovation Hub.
- The director will take charge by 2020 academic year.
- As per agreement, Novelis will either identify sponsored research or provide a list of innovation needs.
- The Georgia Tech faculty will be invited to offer proposals, which will be evaluated by an advisory board comprising of members of both the entities.
- The approved proposals will receive funding from Novelis as well as other external agencies.
- The new partnership will provide the opportunity to train a new generation of the best and brightest students on Novelis’ scientific, engineering and business innovations, in order to develop new industry-leading solutions.
- Todd Summe, Chief Research and Development Officer, Novelis, Inc. stated that establishing closer ties with Georgia Tech will provide tremendous benefits for both the students and the company.
