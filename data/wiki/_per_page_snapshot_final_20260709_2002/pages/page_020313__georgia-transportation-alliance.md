# Georgia Transportation Alliance

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2024-11

**Source:** [Economic Development Expert Tapped to Lead Georgia Transportation Alliance - LaGrange CEO](https://lagrangeceo.com/features/2024/11/economic-development-expert-tapped-lead-georgia-transportation-alliance/)  ·  `page_020313`

## Facts

- The Georgia Transportation Alliance, an affiliate of the Georgia Chamber of Commerce, selected Joseph Santoro to serve as its new Executive Director.
- Joseph Santoro starts his new position on December 6 after a four-year tenure with the Council for Quality Growth.
- Prior to this appointment, Santoro served as the Director of Policy & Government Affairs for the Council for Quality Growth.
- Over the last four years at the Council for Quality Growth, Santoro worked on local, regional, and state policies related to infrastructure planning, legislative initiatives, regulatory issues, land use, transportation, and environmental planning impacting the development and growth industry.
- Santoro has over 10 years of government affairs experience working for membership-based organizations in metro Atlanta evaluating local, state, and federal policy priorities.
- Santoro earned a B.S. in Public Policy from the Georgia Institute of Technology.
- Santoro has been a Georgia Chamber Government Affairs Committee member since 2014 through various roles.
- Santoro is a 2023 graduate of the Atlanta Regional Commission Regional Leadership Institute.
- Santoro and his wife Kayla live in the City of Atlanta where they recently purchased their first home.
- Nick Juliano, Partner at Impact Public Affairs and Chair of the Georgia Transportation Alliance, stated that Georgia is at a critical moment for its transportation industry, particularly regarding freight and logistics.
- Chris Clark, President and CEO of the Georgia Chamber of Commerce, noted that Santoro’s broad experience with statewide transportation stakeholders and trusted economic development insight make him an ideal fit for the role.
- Chris Clark highlighted that Georgia has extensive surface transportation networks, two world-class ports, and one of the largest railroad systems in the country, which are essential drivers of the state's record-breaking economic success.
- The Georgia Transportation Alliance is the state’s only organization focused solely on identifying and promoting long-term transportation solutions specifically connected to economic development and job creation.
- The Georgia Transportation Alliance works closely with the Georgia Chamber of Commerce, the Georgia Department of Transportation, and other key stakeholders in Georgia and Washington, D.C., with the goal of ensuring an efficient and safe statewide multi-modal transportation system.
