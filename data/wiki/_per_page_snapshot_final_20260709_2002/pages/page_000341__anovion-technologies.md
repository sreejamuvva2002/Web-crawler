## Facts

- Anovion Technologies is a leading U.S.-based manufacturer of synthetic graphite anode materials for lithium-ion batteries.
- The company was founded by industry experts with over 140 years of combined experience in materials science.
- Anovion Technologies specializes in producing high-quality battery components essential for electric vehicles, energy storage systems, and various electronic applications.
- It is North America’s first commercial producer of synthetic graphite anode materials.
- The company contributes significantly to establishing a secure domestic battery supply chain.
- Its operations help reduce dependence on international suppliers.
- Anovion Technologies is headquartered in Chicago, United States.
