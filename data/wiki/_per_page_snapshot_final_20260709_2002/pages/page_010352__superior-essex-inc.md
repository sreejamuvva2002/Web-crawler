# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Superior Essex Inc. Names Daniel Choi As Its Chief Executive Officer - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.it/news/superior-essex-inc-names-daniel-choi-as-its-ceo/)  ·  `page_010352`

## Facts

- Superior Essex Inc. announced the appointment of Daniel Choi as CEO effective March 1, 2022.
- Choi will assume the role for Superior Essex Inc. and all its subsidiaries, including Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, Essex Brownell, Essex Energy Italy, IVA, and Hi-Wire.
- Choi brings decades of experience in strategy, operations, and sales development to the company.
- Choi previously worked at LS Cable and System, the parent company of Superior Essex Inc., most recently serving as Regional President, North and South America for LS Cable and System.
- Prior to that role, Choi served as President, LS Cable and System USA for MV and LV power cable systems.
- Choi was closely associated with Superior Essex as President of Cyprus Investments from 2008 to 2014 and Senior Vice President of PMO Europe and China in 2015.
- Choi holds a PhD in Materials Science and Engineering from Seoul National University and an MBA from Helsinki Business School.
- Choi lives and works in Atlanta, where he also chairs the Korean Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Georgia.
- Daniel Choi stated his goal is to develop the business based on past results for future success while maintaining a high level of quality and customer service.
- Brian Kim resigned as CEO of Superior Essex Inc. and its subsidiaries after nearly seven years leading the team.
- During his tenure, Kim increased hybrid and PoE cable infrastructure capacity and prioritized innovation and R&D with the launch of the MagForceX Innovation Center.
- Kim also launched the strategic joint venture Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire and committed the business to a sustainable future.
- Superior Essex is headquartered at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
- Superior Essex has over 3,000 employees across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is a global leading supplier of magnet wire and is at the forefront of communication fiber and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology application.
- The company's technology is crucial for creating disruptive technological advances in the electrification of automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex is pioneering smart building technology and setting the pace for sustainability.
