# HDA Truck Pride

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-03-27

**Source:** [Dinex Group joins HDA Truck Pride as a supplier partner | Trucks, Parts, Service](https://www.truckpartsandservice.com/business/growth-strategies/article/14984923/dinex-group-joins-hda-truck-pride-as-a-supplier-partner)  ·  `page_019385`

## Facts

- HDA Truck Pride recently announced it has enhanced its Aftertreatment Category with the addition of Dinex Emission Inc. as a supplier partner.
- Denmark-based Dinex Group operates 20 companies in 16 countries.
- The Dinex Group is a global manufacturer of engineered exhaust and emission control products and solutions for the heavy-duty diesel and gas engine industry.
- Aftertreatment is the fastest growing category in the commercial vehicle industry.
- The number of vehicles in operation continues to grow at a rapid pace, as does the number of maintenance concerns, according to an announcement.
- HDA Truck Pride has expanded its aftertreatment supplier partnerships to include Dinex Emission because of its distribution philosophy, along with its complete turbo-to-tailpipe strategy.
- Tina Hubbard serves as vice president of marketing and product management at HDA Truck Pride.
- Tina Hubbard stated that Dinex enhances members' capability of servicing the aftertreatment DPF and DOC needs of their customers.
- Tina Hubbard noted that the partnership gives members future opportunities within Pipe Treatment Solutions as they round out the entire system and category expansion.
- Tina Hubbard expressed eagerness about the growth opportunities the new partnership with Dinex brings to HDA Truck Pride.
