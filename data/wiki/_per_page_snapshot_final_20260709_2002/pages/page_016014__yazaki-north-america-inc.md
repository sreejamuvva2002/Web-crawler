# Yazaki North America, Inc.

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-06-10

**Source:** [Yazaki North America and MOTER Technologies Form  Financial Partnership for Future Strategic Collaboration](https://www.yazaki-na.com/News/1977/yazaki-north-america-and-moter-technologies-form-financial-partnership-for-futur)  ·  `page_016014`

## Facts

- Yazaki North America, Inc. (YNA) and MOTER Technologies, Inc. signed a strategic financial partnership agreement on May 14, 2025.
- YNA made a strategic equity investment in MOTER, with the transaction closing on June 10, 2025.
- The partnership will develop and promote AI-based insurance services to help mobility business players analyze and manage complex risks in an accelerating digital transformation environment.
- Together, the companies will fuse MOTER’s insurance-software risk-analysis methodologies with Yazaki’s advanced AI to reduce accidents.
- They will explore new business opportunities in the U.S. mobility market by working with automakers and mobility service providers.
- MOTER deploys sophisticated AI to power real-time insurance software that performs risk analysis and premium calculations, which is installable on in-vehicle devices.
- MOTER is developing next-generation insurance products and services specifically tailored for electric and autonomous vehicles.
- Yazaki holds a strong track record in components for EVs and hybrid vehicles.
- The Yazaki Group operates in three core business areas: Automotive Equipment (wire harnesses), Instrumentation (onboard devices for commercial vehicles), and Environmental Systems.
- Yazaki holds a world-leading share in the wire harness market and a top-tier share in Japan for commercial vehicle onboard devices such as digital tachographs and dashcams.
- Yazaki North America is headquartered in Canton, Michigan, USA, and engages in technology development, R&D activities, and new business development across North and Central America.
- Ko Yazaki, Representative Director & Executive Vice President of Yazaki Corporation, stated the mobility industry is undergoing a once-in-a-century transformation driven by trends such as CASE and MaaS.
- Kenichi Fujisawa, President & CEO of Yazaki North America, noted strong synergy between MOTER’s products and Yazaki’s AI-powered traffic accident reduction solutions currently under development within its AI & Digital Division.
- Mitsuru Yamaguchi, Representative Director & Executive Vice President of Aioi Nissay Dowa Insurance Co., Ltd., explained that MOTER was established in April 2021 to develop insurance software embeddable in in-vehicle devices and next-gen products for EVs and autonomous vehicles.
- Kenji Fujii, Founder & CEO of MOTER Technologies, Inc., highlighted the partnership's goal to develop innovative insurance products by leveraging cutting-edge technologies and data science alongside Yazaki’s automotive expertise.
- The strategic financial partnership signing ceremony took place at MOTER’s headquarters in Torrance, California on May 14, 2025.
- Attendees at the signing included Chopper Takeda (VP), Tets Hosoda (CFO), Kenji Fujii (CEO of MOTER), Itaru Motobayashi (EVP YNA), Tony Ishibashi (Head of Business Development of YNA), and Shaun Sato (EVP of MOTER).
- Yazaki Corporation’s AI & Digital Division leverages cutting-edge technologies such as AI to analyze vast mobility, factory, and energy data.
