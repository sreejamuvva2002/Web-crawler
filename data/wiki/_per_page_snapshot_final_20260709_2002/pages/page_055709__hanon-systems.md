# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-02

**Source:** [Hanon Systems Opening New Manufacturing Facility In Georgia, Creating 160 New Jobs | Doc and the Block](https://docandtheblock.com/hanon-systems-opening-new-manufacturing-facility-in-georgia-creating-160-new-jobs/)  ·  `page_055709`

## Facts

- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp (R) announced that Hanon Systems would open a new manufacturing facility in Bulloch County.
- The new facility is expected to create 160 new jobs.
- The project involves an investment of more than $40 million.
- Hanon Systems is a global provider of automotive thermal and energy management solutions.
- Hanon Systems was formed in 1986.
- Hanon Systems has grown into a leading supplier of automotive thermal and energy management solutions for electrified and conventional vehicles.
- Hanon Systems' offerings include solutions in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning; powertrain cooling; compressors; fluid transport; and electronics and fluid pressure.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia’s automotive and e-mobility industries continue to grow as the state welcomes Hanon Systems.
- Governor Brian Kemp described Georgia as furthering its position as the EV capital of America.
- The new facility will be built at Gateway Regional Industrial Park in Bulloch County.
- Operations at the new facility are expected to begin in May 2024.
- Min Sung is the President and Chief Executive Officer of Hanon Systems.
- Min Sung thanked the State of Georgia and the cooperation between its agencies for guiding the project.
- The facility opening aligns with Hanon Systems' strategy to support the industry shift toward electrification.
- The facility opening aligns with Hanon Systems' strategy to grow its North America footprint.
