## Facts

- Novelis is considered the global leader in aluminum rolling & recycling.
- Novelis is part of Hindalco Industries.
- Novelis operates under 4 segments in the world.
- Novelis produces aluminum tubing, aluminum sheet, aluminum plate, aluminum coil & coiled wire.
- Producing sheet & foil out of flat-rolled aluminum is the focus at Novelis.
- Novelis holds 19% of the world’s market for producing sheet & foil out of flat-rolled aluminum.
- High quality aluminum produced by Novelis is used by name brand customers for industries such as automotive, packaging, construction & printing.
- Novelis is the world leader in recycling of used aluminum beverage cans.
