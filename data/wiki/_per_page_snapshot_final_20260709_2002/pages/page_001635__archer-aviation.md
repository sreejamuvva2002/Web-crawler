# Archer Aviation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-06

**Source:** [Archer Aviation ($ACHR) Nears Completion of High-Volume eVTOL Manufacturing Facility In Georgia - Green Stock News](https://www.greenstocknews.com/newswire/archer-aviation-achr-nears-completion-of-high-volume-evtol-manufacturing-facility-in-georgia)  ·  `page_001635`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation has announced progress on its high-volume manufacturing facility in Covington, GA.
- The company reaffirmed an expected 2024 completion date for the facility.
- The 400,000-square-foot facility will enable the production of up to 650 Midnight aircraft annually.
- This makes it one of the aviation industry's largest facilities by volume.
- Earlier this year, Archer completed the first build phase, which included grading and foundation work.
- Archer is now nearing completion of phase two, which entails erecting the exterior walls and roof.
- Phase three, consisting of interior fit-out, is scheduled to be completed in the coming months.
- Phase four, consisting of equipment load-in and power-on, is scheduled to be completed in the coming months.
- Adam Goldstein, CEO and founder of Archer stated: “Once complete, we’ll focus on ramping production up to 650 aircraft a year, our goal is to be the first in the eVTOL industry to achieve scaled manufacturing.”
- Archer is leading the aviation electrification charge, designing and developing the key enabling technologies and aircraft that are necessary to power the next great transportation revolution.
