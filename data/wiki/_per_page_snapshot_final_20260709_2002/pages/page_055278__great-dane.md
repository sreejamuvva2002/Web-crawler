## Facts

- Great Dane company leaders will receive a special proclamation from the Georgia General Assembly this week in honor of the company's 125th anniversary.
- Great Dane was founded in 1900 in Savannah, Ga.
- The company is recognized as an industry leader in the transportation business.
- The recognition ceremony will take place at 10:40 a.m. Thursday in Georgia's Capitol House Chamber.
- The proclamation highlights the company's ongoing resilience, pioneering leadership in the transportation industry, and long-standing commitment to quality and innovation.
- Great Dane is one of only 0.5% of U.S. companies that have remained in business for more than 100 years.
- Great Dane joins a prestigious group of iconic American companies that have also reached this milestone, including Leopold's, Coca-Cola, and Harley-Davidson.
