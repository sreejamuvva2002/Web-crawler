## Facts

- Auto parts maker NVH Korea is opening a facility in Locust Grove.
- The $72 million investment will create more than 160 jobs in Henry County.
- The facility will produce battery system parts for electric vehicles made in the U.S., including Kia and Hyundai.
- Operations will begin in mid-2024.
