# Ecoplastic America Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-11

**Source:** [Ecoplastic America is proud to build new home in Bulloch County  - Grice Connect](https://www.griceconnect.com/business/ecoplastic-america-is-proud-to-build-new-home-in-bulloch-county-7644133)  ·  `page_001450`

## Facts

- Ecoplastic America Corporation specializes in the manufacture of plastic automobile parts for equipment manufacturers, most notably Hyundai Motors and KIA.
- Products include bumpers, consoles, trims, and plastic molds.
- Products are used in Hyundai/Kia vehicles including the Genesis G90, Ionic, Palisade, GV80, Santa Fe, Tucson, Sonata, and Venue.
- The company is following Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) to the Georgia region as a tier-1 supplier.
- Ecoplastic America Corporation is building a new facility in Bulloch County, Georgia.
- The new Bulloch County facility is currently under construction at 4800 Highway 301 South.
- The project will create more than 500 jobs.
- The company has invested over $205 million in the new Bulloch County facility.
- Corporate headquarters currently employs approximately 520 employees, and the scale of the Georgia operations will rival that size.
- CEO Chulhwan (Charlie) Kim stated the company intends to work closely with local businesses and residents to integrate into the community.
- The company is considering partnerships with local educational institutions to provide career opportunities for students.
