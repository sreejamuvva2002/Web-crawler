# Xtract One Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-09

**Source:** [Xtract One Technologies Selected by Hyundai Transys to Secure Facilities and Keep Weapons Out | Financial Post](https://financialpost.com/globe-newswire/xtract-one-technologies-selected-by-hyundai-transys-to-secure-facilities-and-keep-weapons-out)  ·  `page_009590`

## Facts

- Xtract One Technologies announced its SmartGateway security screening solution was selected to protect entrances at Hyundai Transys in West Point, Ga.
- The SmartGateway will be deployed at the manufacturing plant to protect workers and staff from weapons entering the buildings and to prevent mass casualty events.
- Jinho Kim, manager of GA & EHS at Hyundai Transys Georgia Powertrain Inc., stated they viewed and tested Xtract One’s Gateway at Kia Georgia Inc., which had great success with keeping weapons out.
- Jinho Kim noted they were impressed by the Gateway solution's quick and accurate weapon detection and felt its customization would fit seamlessly into their manufacturing facility.
- SmartGateway is the latest iteration of Xtract One's Gateway screening solution, designed as a fast, frictionless entry screener for manufacturing plants, casinos, and large arenas.
- The system unobtrusively detects guns, knives, and other potential weapons on people entering a facility without requiring them to empty their pockets of personal items.
- Peter Evans, CEO of Xtract One, stated auto manufacturers, their supply chains, and other large workplaces are well suited for the Gateway solution due to the need to process hundreds of workers through doors quickly during shift changes.
- This selection marks Xtract One's third customer in the auto manufacturing industry.
- Peter Evans criticized traditional walk-through metal detector systems as inefficient by modern standards, less secure, and more intrusive than their Gateway system.
- SmartGateway uses artificial intelligence-powered scanners to detect concealed guns and knives and alerts security personnel to conduct secondary screening.
- If people are not carrying weapons, they simply walk through the system without needing to empty pockets or other personal articles.
- Upgrades to the system make it easy to learn and use, requiring fewer personnel to operate relative to other scanning systems.
- The SmartGateway is customizable to fit the security needs and requirements of individual facilities, including its look and feel.
- Ryan Smith, Program Manager for Security at Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc., recommended Xtract One to Hyundai Transys after being pleased with how the Gateway solutions looked and worked at their facilities.
- Since implementing the solution at Kia Georgia, they have prevented a significant number of weapons from entering their facility.
- Personnel screening time was drastically reduced from 21 seconds to 2.5 seconds following implementation at Kia Georgia.
- Ryan Smith noted that in an industry based on “just in time” processes, the additional time savings provides production flexibility to maintain work areas during crucial times.
- Xtract One Technologies is a technology-driven threat detection and security solution company leveraging AI to provide seamless and secure patron access control experiences.
- The company manufactures unobtrusive threat detection systems that enable venue building operators to prioritize improved patron experiences while providing unprecedented safety.
- Xtract One’s multi-sensor Gateway product enables companies to covertly screen for weapons at points of entry without disrupting the flow of traffic.
- AI-based Xtract Vision allows venue and building operators to identify weapons and other threats inside and outside of facilities.
- Xtract One Insights provides valuable intelligence for optimizing operations.
