## Facts

- Electric vehicle startup Rivian Automotive Inc. plans to hold two ceremonial events to christen the forthcoming construction of its long-delayed $5 billion factory an hour east of Atlanta.
- The company will play host to a community event Sept. 14 and a formal groundbreaking ceremony Sept. 16 with stakeholders, media and government officials, including Gov. Brian Kemp.
- The events are at the roughly 2,000-acre project site along I-20.
- The events bolster promises by Rivian leaders to begin vertical construction of the factory next year.
- The factory project has followed multiple delays and setbacks.
