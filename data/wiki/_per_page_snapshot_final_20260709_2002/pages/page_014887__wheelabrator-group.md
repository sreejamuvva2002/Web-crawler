## Facts

- Wheelabrator manufactures a full range of wheel-type shot blast machines.
- Wheelabrator manufactures mass finishing equipment dedicated to the demands of the aerospace industry.
- Wheelabrator manufactures automated airblast solutions.
- Wheelabrator is NADCAP approved.
- Wheelabrator provides process safe solutions for engine and structural aircraft components.
- Wheelabrator machines are technically advanced and designed for ease of maintenance.
- Wheelabrator machines range from standard versions to fully customized and integrated systems.
- Wheelabrator is part of Norican Group, a world leading provider of formation and enhancement of metallic parts.
- Norican Group is home to four leading, globally operating brands: DISA, Italpresse Gauss, StrikoWestofen, and Wheelabrator.
- Wheelabrator Group is located in LaGrange, GA 30240, USA.
