## Facts

- SKF is the world leader in bearing manufacturing and technology.
- Bearings range in size from 2.4" outside diameter weighing 0.3lbs up to 55" in outside diameter and weighing 5000lbs.
- The Flowery Branch facility was constructed in 1975.
- The facility was originally operated by the TRW Company as an MRC (Marlin Rockwell Corporation) Ball Bearing Factory.
- SKF Flowery Branch is in the process of major upgrades at the facility.
- The Flowery Branch facility produces 9 million bearings per year.
- The plant tour will showcase an automated manufacturing process.
- The plant tour will showcase a diverse business model.
- Attendance for the tour is limited to 1 ticket for non-manufacturing companies and 2 tickets for manufacturing companies.
- Closed-toed/flat bottom shoes are mandatory for attendees, with no high heels allowed.
- Photos are not allowed during the tour.
