## Facts

- The manufacturing plant spans 2,000 acres.
- The facility is expected to eventually employ 7,500 workers.
- The plant will be home to production of the upcoming R2 crossover vehicle.
- Rivian received $1.5 billion in incentives from the state of Georgia.
- Rivian received $6.6 billion in federal grants from then-President Joe Biden before he left office.
- Construction of the massive EV plant is finally getting underway after a long development timeline.
- The project marks a major win for the state’s economic development team.
