## Facts

- Georgia Ports Authority (GPA) Executive Director Griff Lynch frequently describes his attitude about the ports’ progress as “excited” despite global economic and trade uncertainties.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) announced that Zummit Plastics, a manufacturer of plastic stretch film, will expand its Phoenix, Arizona, facility to Columbus with the opening of a new manufacturing facility in Muscogee County.
- Zummit Plastics will create 50 new jobs and invest $25 million in its new Columbus, Georgia, manufacturing facility project.
- Atlanta-based aluminum company Novelis Inc. has received approval from Chinese regulators for a $2.6 billion acquisition of Aleris.
- A major benefit of the Novelis-Aleris acquisition will be synergies between the two companies’ factories in China.
- Novelis Inc. now expects its $2.6 billion acquisition of Aleris to be completed by Jan. 21.
