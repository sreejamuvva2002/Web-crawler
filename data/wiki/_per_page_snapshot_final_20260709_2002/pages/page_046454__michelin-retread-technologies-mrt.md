# Michelin Retread Technologies (MRT)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Michelin Expands Georgia Retread Facility | Heavy Duty Trucking](https://www.truckinginfo.com/news/michelin-expands-georgia-retread-facility)  ·  `page_046454`

## Facts

- Michelin Retread Technologies (MRT) is expanding its Covington, Ga., manufacturing facility.
- The expansion aims to meet the continued increase in customer interest in Michelin retreads.
- John Yarbro serves as the facility manager.
- The expansion is necessary to meet demand for additional tread volume and keep pace with new tire technology features such as co-extruded rubber and extra wide treads for the new X One wide single tires.
- This marks the first major expansion of the facility since it began operation in November 1999.
- The company is doubling the production volume, emphasizing high technology volume.
- The facility is located southeast of Atlanta.
- The facility produces a variety of Pre-Mold treads for MRT franchises and retread plants throughout North America.
- MRT offers Pre-Mold treads for long haul, regional, on/off road, urban, and sanitation specific applications.
- Tread rubber products include full-width tread for the new generation X One wide single truck tires, which replace two dual tires on truck drive and trailer axles.
- Products also include co-extruded extra deep drive treads designed for extra miles and cooler running operating temperatures.
- Michelin Retreads apply new-tire technology to existing retreaded tires, offering proven tread designs and higher quality standards tailored to customer needs.
- MRT treads are manufactured using Michelin proprietary tread rubber compounds and patterns to mirror new Michelin tire aspects and performance.
- Michelin North America Inc., the parent company, is headquartered in Greenville, S.C.
- Michelin North America Inc. employs 23,920 people.
- Michelin North America Inc. operates 21 plants across 17 locations.
