# Hyundai MOBIS Savannah (MSV)

## Facts

- Hyundai MOBIS Savannah (MSV), also known as MOBIS Alabama, LLC, is the largest tier one supplier to the Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA).
- The facility supplies three major modules: front and rear chassis modules, cockpit module, and front-end module.
- These components are provided on time and in sequence to streamline the assembly process at HMGMA while reducing reliance on warehouse and storage space.
- Hyundai MOBIS serves as the parts and service arm for South Korean automakers Hyundai Motor Company, Genesis Motors, and Kia Motors.
- The company's product portfolio includes chassis, cockpit, and front-end modules; safety products such as airbags, headlights, anti-lock brake systems, and electronic stability control products; steering parts; multimedia systems; UVO systems; injection-molded plastic parts like instrument panels, carriers, and bumpers; and steel wheel rims and decks.
- Hyundai MOBIS also supplies after-sales service parts for vehicles.
- The Module Division supplies modules to automakers using a just-in-sequence (JIS) system.
- Hyundai MOBIS participates in the initial stages of new vehicle development processes, performing research and development of auto-parts by designing and testing modules for production applications based on specific specifications.
- System integration of modularization contributes to increased competitiveness, in-sequence processing, reduced total weight, and a reduced number of parts needed, which allows for easier assembly, effective inventory control, and cost reduction.
- The Savannah, Georgia facility offers competitive wages and benefits to its team members.
- Benefits include paid vacation and personal time off, 15 paid holidays, a 401(k) with company match, health/dental/vision insurance, life insurance, and disability insurance.
- Hyundai MOBIS maintains a Code of Conduct that guides directors, officers, and employees to perform and manage work fairly and responsibly under all circumstances while adhering to applicable rules and regulations.
- The company provides a confidential and anonymous Red Flag Reporting avenue for team members, business associates, and community partners to report problems.
- MOBIS Alabama embraces five core values of the Hyundai Motor Group to ensure organizational success.
- Hyundai MOBIS aims to affect positive changes in society and fulfill dreams through mobility innovation while maintaining transparent and ethical management practices.
