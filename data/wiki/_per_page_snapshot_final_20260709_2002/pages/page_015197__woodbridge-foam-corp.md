# Woodbridge Foam Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-01

**Source:** [Woodbridge Foam ratifies new deal, eliminating two-tier system | Unifor](https://www.unifor.org/news/all-news/woodbridge-foam-ratifies-new-deal-eliminating-two-tier-system)  ·  `page_015197`

## Facts

- Woodbridge Foam Corp. is an automotive seating and interior design company located in Woodbridge, Ont.
- Members of Unifor Local 112 ratified a new three-year contract with Woodbridge Foam Corp.
- The contract eliminates a two-tier wage system that had been in place for 13 years.
- There are 255 members covered by the contract, consisting of production workers and Skilled Trades personnel.
- The agreement provides significant wage increases for the membership.
- The agreement includes increased vacation allocation for members.
- The agreement establishes a new Defined Benefit Pension Plan.
- The contract is retroactive from June 1, 2023.
- The contract expires on May 31, 2026.
- Local 112 President John Turner stated that negotiating the agreement was a long and challenging process but felt supported by member solidarity and union backing.
- Turner noted the contract ends a 13-year struggle against the two-tier system and eliminates huge discrepancies in wages and benefits for members doing the same work.
- Turner emphasized that the new contract provides wage equality and a clear path to meaningful retirement for all members.
