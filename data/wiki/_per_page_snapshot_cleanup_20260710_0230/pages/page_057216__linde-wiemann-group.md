## Facts

- LINDE + WIEMANN US Inc. operates a manufacturing facility in Lavonia, Georgia.
- The Georgia plant is located at 1720 Knox Bridge Crossing Rd, 30553, Lavonia / GA – US.
- The Lavonia facility covers an area of 4.100m².
- LINDE + WIEMANN employs around 2,300 dedicated employees across 18 locations in 8 countries on 4 continents.
- The company reported an annual turnover of over 625 mio. EUR in 2024.
- LINDE + WIEMANN supplies 30 international automotive manufacturers and system suppliers globally.
- The company produces structural components for vehicle construction, including Sills, A- and B-Pillars as well as structural components in the Front End, Cockpit- and Chassis field.
- Manufacturing capabilities are based on a high level of understanding of processes used in stamping, profile, hot forming and joining technology.
- LINDE + WIEMANN focuses on developing new products that improve the safety of vehicles while reducing their weight at the same time.
- The company has supplied complex and structurally critical components and assemblies to vehicle manufacturers and suppliers around the world for more than 80 years.
- LINDE + WIEMANN acts as a preferred strategic supplier and development partner to premium OEM customers through product development and excellent manufacturing capabilities.
- Products contribute towards improving the safety of vehicles while reducing cost and weight for innovative Body-in-White, crash and safety relevant structural components and technology solutions for fast growing new BEV platforms.
- The company enables eMobility and sustainability with CO2 friendly roll-forming technology and best practice CSR / ESG system.
