# Kautex of Georgia Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2014-01-13

## Facts

- A German auto supplier in northeast Georgia is investing $3.3 millon to add a 95,000-square-foot warehouse to its Lavonia operation in Franklin County.
- Kautex of Georgia Inc. makes fuel tanks, systems that improve windshield visibility and various other automotive parts.
- The company has expanded multiple times since first landing in the state in 1993.
- The Bonn, Germany-based parent company is a subsidiary of Textron Inc.
