# Ecoplastic America Manufacturing Facility

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-03

**Source:** [Five South Architecture, LLC to Lead Design of New 285,000sf Ecoplastic America Manufacturing Facility in Statesboro, GA](https://www.einpresswire.com/article/799838294/five-south-architecture-llc-to-lead-design-of-new-285-000sf-ecoplastic-america-manufacturing-facility-in-statesboro-ga)  ·  `page_001454`

## Facts

- Five South Architecture, LLC is leading the architectural design for a new manufacturing facility for Ecoplastic America in Statesboro, Georgia.
- The new Ecoplastic America manufacturing facility will be 285,000 square feet.
- Ecoplastic America is a major supplier to Hyundai Motor Company.
- The new facility will enhance Ecoplastic America's current capacity to deliver high-quality automotive components.
- The project strengthens the supply chain for Hyundai’s expanding U.S. operations.
- This announcement follows Hyundai Motor Group’s recently announced $20 billion investment plan in the United States.
- Hyundai’s $20 billion investment plan includes the development of a new steel plant in Louisiana.
- Hyundai’s investment plan also covers ongoing electric vehicle (EV) and battery production initiatives.
- Hyundai’s investment aims to create thousands of jobs and foster economic growth across Louisiana, Georgia, and Alabama.
- The Ecoplastic America facility will supply critical components for Hyundai’s growing U.S. footprint.
- Justin Rogers is the Owner of Five South Architecture, LLC.
- Justin Rogers stated that designing a facility of this scale allows his firm to showcase expertise in creating innovative, efficient spaces and contribute to the momentum Ecoplastic America and Hyundai are driving in the U.S. automotive industry.
- Five South Architecture is also providing architectural design services for a new 465,000-square-foot Hyundai Mobis spare parts logistics facility in Montgomery, Alabama.
- The Hyundai Mobis logistics facility is valued at $52 million.
- Groundbreaking for the Hyundai Mobis logistics facility occurred in October 2024.
- The Hyundai Mobis facility will serve as a critical hub for aftermarket parts supporting Hyundai and Kia dealerships nationwide.
- The Hyundai Mobis facility integrates seamlessly with the existing Hyundai Mobis Campus.
- Five South Architecture is based in Birmingham, Alabama.
