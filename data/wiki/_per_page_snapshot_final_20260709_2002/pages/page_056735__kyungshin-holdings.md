# Kyungshin Holdings

## Facts

- Kyungshin Holdings is a South Korea-based manufacturer of electronic components for the automotive industry.
- The company announced an investment of $45m (58.59bn won) to establish three plants in Gomez Palacio, a city in northern Mexico.
- The project will create 2,200 jobs.
- The new plants will manufacture battery modules for electric cars.
- The new plants will also manufacture electronic cards and harnesses.
- Kyungshin Holdings is based in Incheon.
- The company already has existing facilities in Gomez Palacio, located in the state of Durango, Mexico.
- Kyungshin Holdings maintains international operations in Cambodia, China, Honduras, India, Indonesia, Mexico, Poland, Serbia, Turkey, the US and Vietnam.
- The company was founded in essence in 1974 as KyungShin Industrial.
- The company has operated under the name Kyungshin Holdings since 2016 after splitting from its parent company.
- Gomez Palacio is a city of around 350,000 inhabitants located on the eastern Durango border with the state of Coahuila.
- This announcement marks the second major investment the company has announced in the space of a few weeks.
- In July 2023, Linamar Corp, a Canada-based advanced manufacturing company, stated it would invest $60m (C$79.14m) to expand its operations in Gomez Palacio.
- Linamar Corp's expansion involves adding specialised equipment for the production of electric vehicles.
- Linamar Corp's expansion will create 200 jobs.
