# Stryten Energy LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-03-11

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Launches New Line of Lithium Batteries at MODEX 2024 - EIN Presswire](https://www.einnews.com/pr_news/694994046/stryten-energy-launches-new-line-of-lithium-batteries-at-modex-2024)  ·  `page_050628`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC will launch the M-Series Li600, a new line of Class I, II and III lithium batteries, at MODEX 2024 in Atlanta, Georgia.
- The Li600 batteries are available in Class I, II and III, in a full range of voltages with optional cold storage capability down to -40 C.
- The Li600 for Class III features an onboard charger option.
- The full line of Li600 batteries is compatible with the M-Series X-3 and X-7 chargers.
- The Li600 is based on lithium iron phosphate (LFP) chemistry, which has a longer calendar life and a longer cycle life than other lithium chemistries.
- The LFP chemistry used in the Li600 is comprised of readily available materials, making it a cost-competitive option and one of the safest lithium technologies.
- Key features of the M-Series Li600 include fully configurable CANbus protocol, remote monitoring capability, comprehensive diagnostics via a color touch screen and auto hibernation/sleep mode after inactivity to prevent unintended discharge.
- The M-Series Li600 products are designed to meet UL2580, with UL certification currently in process.
- Stryten Energy is showcasing the M-Series T310 Lead Solution, a low maintenance tubular battery that offers optimized power, performance, cost and reduced maintenance for medium to heavy duty lift truck applications.
- Stryten Energy’s round tube design for the T310 is more efficient and delivers longer run times than competitive square tube designs.
- The M-Series X-7 4-bay charger delivers 30kW of power while handling lead and lithium battery types, and can be configured for standard, opportunity or fast charging.
- The M-Series X-7 charger features a modular design that offers flexibility, reduced operating costs and accelerated charging times to meet the demands of the toughest fleets.
- Stryten Energy inCOMMAND™ is an energy performance management software tool designed to make it easy to design and deploy web-connected battery and charger solutions.
- The inCOMMAND software allows dealers and end users to monitor and manage their batteries and chargers right from their computers.
- Matt Gould, Vice President of Industrial Sales and Service at Stryten Energy, stated that with the addition of the M-Series Li600 line, the company is a “one-stop-shop" to serve customers’ needs with a complete portfolio of battery technologies.
- The Li600 line is field serviceable by Stryten Energy’s service network strategically located across the U.S., Canada and Mexico.
- Trained stored energy experts provide planned maintenance, repair services, predictive service, installations, de-installation, recycling, application audits, power studies, training, and inspections for the batteries.
- Stryten Energy is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- The company provides energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military and Government sectors.
- Stryten Energy’s stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers and energy performance management software.
