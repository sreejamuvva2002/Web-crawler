# Manufacturing Megaprojects Tracker

**Type:** event  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Manufacturing Megaprojects Tracker](https://map.engineered-vision.com/)  ·  `page_018357`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Co. with LG Energy Solution operates an active EV production and battery cell manufacturing facility in Bryan County GA.
- The Hyundai/LG Energy plant in Bryan County GA is producing vehicles including Ioniq 5, Ioniq 6, and GV70.
- An LG Energy battery module plant is co-located on the Hyundai campus in Bryan County GA.
- Hyundai has committed additional investment to its Georgia operations amid tariff concerns.
- Hyundai is constructing a 1,700-acre ultra-low carbon steel production plant in Ascension Parish LA.
- The Hyundai steel plant in Ascension Parish LA is under construction and will supply Hyundai's Georgia Metaplant with domestically produced steel.
- The Hyundai steel plant uses hydrogen-based steelmaking to reduce carbon emissions.
- Rivian Automotive is building a carbon-conscious manufacturing campus for R2/R3/R3X EVs in Stanton Springs GA.
- Rivian's Georgia facility spans 9 million sq ft and held its groundbreaking ceremony in September 2025.
- First-phase construction at Rivian's Georgia campus began in 2026, with production of R2/R3/R3X targeted for 2028.
- Hanwha Qcells operates a fully integrated solar panel supply chain facility in Dalton GA.
- The Hanwha Qcells facility in Dalton GA is operational and produces solar cells and panels.
- The Hanwha Qcells facility is noted as one of the most vertically integrated solar manufacturing operations in the U.S.
- Redwood Materials Inc. operates an active battery recycling and anode/cathode components campus in Ridgeville SC.
- The Redwood Materials campus recycles EV batteries and produces anode copper foil and cathode active materials.
- Redwood Materials supplies its recycled and produced components to Panasonic and others.
- Toyota operates eight new EV-battery lines at its Liberty NC campus for Toyota and Lexus EVs.
- Battery production at Toyota's Liberty NC campus began in 2025.
- Ford Motor Co. and SK On dissolved their BlueOval SK joint venture in December 2025, with Ford taking sole control of twin battery gigafactories with 86 GWh combined capacity in Glendale KY.
- The Ford/SK On plant in Glendale KY closed on Feb 14, 2026 pending restructuring, and a second planned plant was paused.
- Ford Motor Co. operates an integrated EV manufacturing campus for F-Series electric trucks in Stanton TN where construction was completed in September 2025.
- Battery production at Ford's Stanton TN campus is delayed to 2027, with full vehicle production delayed to 2028 due to slower EV demand.
- Ford ended its SK On JV but BlueOval City in Tennessee is separate from the Kentucky battery park.
- Envision AESC is constructing a 30 GWh EV battery cell plant in Bowling Green KY.
- The Envision AESC plant will supply Nissan and other automakers.
- Scout Motors (Volkswagen) is reviving its Scout brand with electric trucks and SUVs at a facility in Blythewood SC.
- Scout Motors' investment increased from $2B to $2.3B, including a $300M Supplier Park.
- Production timeline for Scout Motors shifted from 2026 to late 2027, with the Terra truck and Traveler SUV currently in production.
- First Solar operates an active solar panel factory in Lawrence County AL making 3.5 GW nameplate capacity.
- The First Solar plant was inaugurated in September 2024 and is fully operational producing Series 7 thin-film solar panels.
- Novelis Inc. is commissioning a low-carbon aluminum rolling mill and recycling center in Bay Minette AL.
- Novelis investment increased from $2.5B to $5B, with commissioning delayed from mid-2025 to the second half of 2026.
- The Novelis facility will produce automotive-grade aluminum sheet for EVs.
- ArcelorMittal has planned construction for an advanced manufacturing facility in Mobile County AL producing non-oriented electrical steel for EV motors.
- The ArcelorMittal facility will produce non-oriented electrical steel (NOES) critical for EV motors and transformers to support the domestic EV supply chain.
