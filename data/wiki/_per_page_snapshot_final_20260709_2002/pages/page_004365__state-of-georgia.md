## Facts

- Automotive leaders are choosing Georgia to build their future.
- The Southeast is an epicenter for automotive technology, with innovation occurring across the state.
- Hyundai Motor Group has a $5 billion smart facility in Georgia.
- Kia has a $1 billion manufacturing plant expansion in Georgia.
- Rivian has announced a facility in Georgia that will employ 7,500 workers.
- Mercedes-Benz, Porsche, and Groupe PSA have their headquarters in Georgia.
- Georgia auto companies gain a competitive edge through a prime location in the epicenter of the region’s auto manufacturing boom and with proximity to a canvas of manufacturers.
