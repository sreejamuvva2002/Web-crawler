## Facts

- Fouts Bros Fire Equipment is a family-owned and operated company that has been in business for more than 70 years and builds fire trucks to help first responders save lives in the USA and Canada.
- Fouts Bros designed a standardized 3000 Gallon Commercial Tanker in cooperation with partners W.S. Darley, UPF Poly-Tank, and Kenworth.
- Through its customization options and small-town Customer Direct Sales Approach (CDSA), the company has manufactured and delivered over 1000 tankers worldwide.
- Fouts Defense was awarded U.S. Army and U.S. Navy contracts for eight units designated for the Army and six units for the Navy, with deliveries across the U.S.
- The Shreveport Fire Department recently purchased a new Fouts Fire 3000 Gallon Tanker through dealer BFA of LA, Inc.
- Stock apparatus is available for quick delivery when grant funding comes through, including for FEMA AFG Grant Applications with a deadline of June 22nd.
- The Wrentham Fire Department utilizes a Fouts 3,000 Gallon Tanker powered by a Cummins L9 450 HP engine and outfitted with a Hale 1500 GPM Pump.
- The company participated in the SEAFC/GAFC Conference in Savannah, hosting a welcome dinner at Starland Yard and displaying a Medium Duty Rescue at booth B2 with Williams Fire Apparatus, Inc.
- The business is located at 138 Roberson Mill Road, Milledgeville, GA 31061, and operates Monday through Friday from 8am to 5pm.
