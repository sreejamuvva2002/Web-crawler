# Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-10-18

**Source:** [Peachtree City Panasonic receives state award for automotive innovation | The Citizen](https://thecitizen.com/2020/10/18/peachtree-city-panasonic-receives-state-award-for-automotive-innovation/)  ·  `page_048021`

## Facts

- Representatives from the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) arrived at Panasonic Automotive Systems offices in Peachtree City on Oct. 13 to present company officials with the 2020 Georgia Emerging Automotive Recognition (GEAR) Award for Automotive Innovation.
- The GEAR Award is conferred to a company demonstrating innovation through emerging technologies for the automotive industry, a new product design, or a new/updated manufacturing process.
- Panasonic Automotive President Scott Kirchner stated that developing innovative products for safer roadways and a transformed mobility experience is the company's core mission.
- Scott Kirchner noted that the company relies on remarkable talent and culture, viewing employees as its most sustainable competitive advantage.
- Scott Kirchner expressed honor at receiving the award recognizing Panasonic Automotive employees' innovation and dedication, and stated a desire to continue being part of Georgia's emerging automotive footprint.
- GDEcD noted that the automotive industry is rapidly evolving from ride-sharing apps to increasing electric vehicle purchases, with Panasonic innovating to meet customer needs.
- GDEcD described Panasonic Automotive as an integrated solutions leader in infotainment, connected mobility, and electrification, remaining at the intersection of current industry changes.
- The award noted that Panasonic's innovations in battery advancements, new display technologies, and 5G communication performance validated across the entire vehicle have helped numerous OEMs adapt to the modern consumer.
- Panasonic's Georgia facility is located in Peachtree City and employs roughly 1,500 people.
- Award criteria require that the recognized innovation must have been made at the organization's Georgia location.
- Georgia is home to more than 200 automotive-related facilities.
- In the last five years, Georgia located 102 automotive companies, creating 8,103 jobs.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America (PASA) is an industry-leading global supplier to automotive OEMs for infotainment systems and advanced connected car solutions.
- PASA's clients include Ford, GM, Chrysler, Daimler, Fiat, Tesla, Honda, Toyota, Nissan, and many others.
