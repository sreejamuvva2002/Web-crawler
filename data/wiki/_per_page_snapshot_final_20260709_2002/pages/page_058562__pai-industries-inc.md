## Facts

- Pai Industries, Inc. is a privately-held company that operates in the auto parts industry.
- Pai Industries, Inc. was founded in 1973.
- Pai Industries, Inc. headquarters are located in Suwanee, Georgia.
- The company reports revenue of $50-100 million and has 200-500 employees.
- Revenues were $380 MM in 2024, $250 MM in 2023, $90 MM in 2022, $120 MM in 2021, and $65 MM in 2020.
- Gross Profit was $190 MM in 2024, $125 MM in 2023, $45 MM in 2022, $60 MM in 2021, and $32.50 MM in 2020.
- Net Income was $43 MM in 2024, $25 MM in 2023, $9 MM in 2022, $12 MM in 2021, and $6.50 MM in 2020.
- Total Assets were $850 MM in 2024, $720 MM in 2023, $680 MM in 2022, $650 MM in 2021, and $600 MM in 2020.

- Total Equity was $400 MM in 2024, $320 MM in 2023, $300 MM in 2022, $300 MM in 2021, and $280 MM in 2020.
- Brandon Seadorf serves as Chief Executive Officer of Pai Industries, Inc.
- On March 15, 2024, NorthBridge PE completed an acquisition of Pai Industries, Inc. for a deal amount of $120M, taking a 100% stake and taking the company private via asset acquisition.
- The target company valuation was listed as $1.20 MM at the time of the March 2024 acquisition.
- On September 1, 2023, a secondary transaction involving a majority stake sale through a secondary sale and management buy-out (MBO) to acquire 65% was canceled, with an undisclosed deal amount and NaN valuation.
