# Phase IV Engineering, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2021-05-26

**Source:** [WIKA USA Partner Phase IV Engineering Launches New Wireless Sensor System - WIKA USA](https://www.wika.com/en-us/pr_2102_en_us.WIKA)  ·  `page_015096`

## Facts

- Phase IV Engineering, a WIKA USA partner located in Lawrenceville, GA, launched the next generation Leap Sensors® Wireless Sensor System for the Industrial Internet of Things on May 26, 2021.
- The newly redesigned industrial IoT sensor system combines wireless IIoT capabilities with robust features required by industrial and enterprise clients.
- Phase IV Engineering’s CEO Scott Dalgleish stated the company has been doing custom sensor design work for 25 years and integrated cutting-edge design features to meet Industry 4.0 needs.
- The Leap Sensors system is described as easy to configure, install, and update, making the transition to IIoT simple and cost-effective.
- The system features a breakthrough modular design with rugged polycarbonate enclosures rated to IP68 and UL 94 V-0 (non-Ex rated) flammability standards.
- Each sensor transceiver node includes an integrated internal antenna, though intrinsically safe-rated enclosures are not currently offered.
- The enclosures are designed to withstand industrial environment dust, vibration, and wash-downs.
- CEO Scott Dalgleish noted that customers typically see a return on investment within three months due to streamlined design and installation.
- A client example cited by Dalgleish involved detecting a failing motor within two weeks of installation, preventing a potential $200,000 loss from downtime, lost product, and replacement expenses, resulting in a manageable $3,500 scheduled repair instead.
- WIKA has continuously advanced instrumentation for pressure, temperature, level, and flow measurement for 75 years, leveraging decades of experience to develop stock and custom instrumentation and engineering services.
- WIKA is described as a global leader in lean manufacturing with manufacturing locations around the globe to meet high delivery demands.
- Phase IV Engineering has been a pioneer in wireless sensing for over 28 years, leveraging expertise in wireless technology and edge computing for its Leap Sensors product line.
- The Leap Sensors system is complete and adaptable, meeting demanding industrial client needs through both off-the-shelf and customized configurations for specific applications.
