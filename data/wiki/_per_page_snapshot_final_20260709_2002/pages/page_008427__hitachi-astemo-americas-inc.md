## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo Americas Inc. has announced plans to expand its operation in Berea, Kentucky.
- The automotive supplier will invest $153 million into the project, which will fund on-site improvements and renovations.
- The expansion includes additional production lines and equipment.
- 752,000 square feet will be added to the facility.
- 167 new jobs will be created.
- Tim Clark serves as president of Hitachi Astemo Americas.
- Tim Clark stated the company is approaching 40 years of manufacturing in Kentucky.
- Tim Clark described the announcement as a bold step in the direction of vehicle electrification.
