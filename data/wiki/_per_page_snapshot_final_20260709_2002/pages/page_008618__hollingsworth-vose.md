## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose is a global leader in filtration, battery separator and energy storage solutions.
- The company is family-owned for seven generations, with origins dating back to 1728 when the “Act for the Encouragement of Making Paper” was passed by the General Court of the Province of Massachusetts Bay.
- Hollingsworth & Vose’s advanced materials contribute to powering hybrid, start-stop and electric vehicles.
- The company’s battery separator and energy storage solutions are helping the world transition to a more sustainable future.
- Hollingsworth & Vose’s filtration solutions are used in vehicles and heavy-duty equipment, among other applications like clean rooms, hospitals, computers, commercial buildings, and homes.
- Hollingsworth & Vose operates 13 manufacturing and nine research sites strategically located in the Americas, Europe, and Asia.
- The company employs more than 2,200 people globally, including more than 40 PhDs on its research and development team.
- Hollingsworth & Vose holds more than 280 patents and applications related to filtration and separator materials.
- The company is headquartered at its plant site in East Walpole, Massachusetts, USA.
