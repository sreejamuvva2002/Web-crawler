# Daimler Truck North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Autonomous-Ready Truck Platform | The Road to Autonomy](https://www.roadtoautonomy.com/autonomous-ready-truck-platform/)  ·  `page_053585`

## Facts

- Joanna Buttler serves as Head of the Global Autonomous Technology Group at Daimler Truck North America.
- Joanna Buttler joined Grayson Brulte on The Road to Autonomy podcast to discuss the development of the autonomous-ready Freightliner Cascadia and Daimler Truck’s outlook for autonomous trucking.
- Joanna Buttler stated that the company sees autonomy as one of its biggest opportunities.
- Daimler Truck is focused on deploying long-haul autonomous trucks in the United States as part of a hub-to-hub strategy.
- Daimler is developing the autonomous-ready Freightliner Cascadia truck platform.
- Torc Robotics is developing the autonomous driving software for the platform.
- At the Daimler Truck Capital Market Day 2023 in July, the company announced that autonomous trucks represent a strong strategic pillar.
- The company projected that autonomous trucks have the potential to deliver €3 billion in revenue by 2030.
- The official launch for Daimler’s autonomous trucks is scheduled for 2027.
- Customers will purchase the autonomous-ready Freightliner Cascadia from Daimler Truck and an autonomous driving software subscription from Torc with flexible pricing models.
- Daimler will coordinate the entire transaction to simplify the sales process.
- Daimler will offer financing for the vehicles through Daimler Truck Financial.
- Customers will be able to achieve higher utilization with autonomous trucks as they will operate 24/7 with limited downtime.
- Higher utilization could lead to lower shipping costs and increased margins for fleets.
- Daimler is developing a redundant autonomous-ready chassis to ensure the highest levels of safety and functionality.
- The development process started with a list of 1,500 requirements jointly developed with Waymo and Torc to identify systems, features, and tasks required for autonomous driving.
- Joanna Buttler stated that autonomy can and will bring great benefits for society.
