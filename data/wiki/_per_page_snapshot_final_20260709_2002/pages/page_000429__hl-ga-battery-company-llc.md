## Facts

- HL-GA Battery Company LLC is a joint venture between Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy Solution.
- The company drives the EV revolution in North America with advanced lithium-ion battery manufacturing in Georgia.
- HL-GA Battery Company is ramping up operations, finalizing facility construction, and preparing for large-scale battery production to meet surging EV demand in North America.
- Major construction phases at the Georgia manufacturing facility are being completed.
- The company is making strategic efforts to expand its market presence and reach new customer segments.
- The company is implementing advanced technologies to improve service delivery and customer experience.
- HL-GA Battery Company LLC is dedicated to advancing electric vehicle technology by producing high-capacity lithium-ion battery cells.
- The company’s manufacturing plant is located in Ellabell, Georgia.
- The facility is one of the largest battery projects in the state’s history.
- The facility is expected to begin production in early 2026.
- The facility has an annual capacity of 30GWh.
- The facility will supply batteries for up to 300,000 EVs per year.
- The batteries will support Hyundai, Kia, and Genesis models.
- HL-GA Battery Company is committed to sustainability, innovation, and supporting the rapid growth of the EV market in North America.
- The company was founded in 2023.
- HL-GA Battery Company LLC is a private company.
