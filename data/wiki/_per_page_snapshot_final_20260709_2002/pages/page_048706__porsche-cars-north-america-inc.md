# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-01

**Source:** [Porsche reports Q3 2025 U.S. retail sales - Porsche Newsroom USA](https://newsroom.porsche.com/en_US/2025/company/porsche-reports-q3-2025-us-retail-sales.html)  ·  `page_048706`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) announced third-quarter retail deliveries of 18,403 cars.
- Overall sales for the first nine months of the year totaled 57,099 cars, up 5.6 percent year-to-date over 2024.
- The 718, Macan, and Panamera posted significant gains year-to-date.
- Third-quarter sales of the Taycan and Macan Electric performed well.
- Porsche Approved Certified Pre-Owned (CPO) sales in the U.S. for Q3 reached 12,232 units, a new PCNA quarterly record.
- Year-to-date CPO sales stand at 35,439 units, representing a 13 percent increase compared to the same point last year.
- Timo Resch, President and CEO of PCNA, stated that Porsche remains strong in the United States due to customer support and loyalty during unprecedented change.
- Timo Resch noted that choice is vital for customers and highlighted that the new Cayenne Electric will join the Cayenne gasoline and plug-in hybrid models by year's end.
- Compared to the same period in 2024, Q3 sales saw a decrease of 4.9 percent, driven in part by restricted supply.
- The Macan was the top seller in the third quarter with 6,599 deliveries.
- The Cayenne followed as the second top seller with 4,294 deliveries.
- The 911 was the third top seller with 3,448 deliveries.
- July-September 2025 sales for ALL 911 totaled 3,448 units.
- July-September 2025 sales for ALL 718 totaled 1,719 units.
- July-September 2025 sales for ALL TAYCAN totaled 1,553 units.
- July-September 2025 sales for ALL PANAMERA totaled 790 units.
- July-September 2025 sales for ALL CAYENNE totaled 4,294 units.
- July-September 2025 sales for ALL MACAN totaled 6,599 units.
- Year-to-date 2025 sales for ALL 911 totaled 8,872 units.
- Year-to-date 2025 sales for ALL 718 totaled 5,398 units.
- Year-to-date 2025 sales for ALL TAYCAN totaled 3,636 units.
- Year-to-date 2025 sales for ALL PANAMERA totaled 3,410 units.
- Year-to-date 2025 sales for ALL CAYENNE totaled 14,621 units.
- Year-to-date 2025 sales for ALL MACAN totaled 21,162 units.
- PCNA follows the U.S. Auto Industry Sales Release Schedule issued by Motor Intelligence.
- Third quarter new car sales began on July 1, 2025, and ended on September 30, 2025.
- The press release was issued from Atlanta, Georgia.
