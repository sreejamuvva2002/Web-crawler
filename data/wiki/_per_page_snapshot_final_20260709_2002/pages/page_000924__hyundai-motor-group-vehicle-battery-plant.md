# Hyundai Motor Group Vehicle Battery Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group Vehicle Battery Plant - UES](https://www.teamues.com/project/hyundai-motor-group-vehicle-battery-plant/)  ·  `page_000924`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group and SK On are beginning to build an electric vehicle battery manufacturing facility in Kingston, GA (northwest of Atlanta).
- The facility is set to open in 2025.
- SK On is identified as a lithium-ion battery producer.
- The project is described as one of the largest economic development projects in Georgia’s history.
- The site covers 600 acres.
- The project requires an approximate total of 10.2 million cubic yards of material (soil, rip rock, blast rock) to be moved.
- Earthwork is scheduled to take approximately 10 months.
- Contour, a UES company, is providing soil testing and earthwork observation for the project.
- Contour is providing rock verification and quantification of rock discovered during mass grading.
- Contour is handling settlement monitoring for the site.
- Contour is managing NPDES (National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System) monitoring.
- The project scope includes materials testing and environmental consulting services.
