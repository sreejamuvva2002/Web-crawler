## Facts

- Trenton Pressing is investing $10 million.
- The investment will fund a 105,000-square-foot facility on 12 acres next to the company's existing factory in Trenton, Georgia.
- The expansion will boost the plant's size by about a quarter.
- Trenton Pressing manufactures parts for automaker BMW and golf cart producer Club Car.
- Company officials stated that the business was struggling to keep its doors open just a few years ago before this turnaround.
- Mike Pfunder serves as the company's president and chief executive.
