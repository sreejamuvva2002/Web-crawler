# Hollingsworth & Vose Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hollingsworth & Vose Company Expands in Floyd County | Virginia Economic Development Partnership](https://www.vedp.org/press-release/1998-07/floyd-county-company-expands)  ·  `page_008626`

## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose Company will invest $4.8 million to expand its Floyd County plant.
- The expansion will create 25 new jobs over the next three years.
- Governor Jim Gilmore approved a $50,000 grant from the Governor's Opportunity Fund to assist the county with the expansion.
- Governor Jim Gilmore stated: "The Hollingsworth & Vose facility has been an important employer in Floyd for many years... I am delighted that they have chosen to expand here, and I am sure they will continue to prosper in the New River Valley."
- The company specializes in providing technically oriented, high quality, fibrous composite materials primarily for filtration and battery operations.
- The Floyd plant produces carded and meltblown nonwoven composites for filtration and other specialty industrial applications.
- The company has been in Floyd County since 1976.
- Virginia successfully competed with New York and Georgia for the 44,800-square-foot expansion.
- The expansion will add a third meltblown line to the existing plant.
- Hollingsworth & Vose Company is headquartered in East Walpole, Massachusetts.
- The company has six U.S. manufacturing locations, one in the United Kingdom, and a joint venture plant in Mexico.
- The company is one of the world's leading producers of meltblown composite materials.
- John Hunsinger serves as Plant Manager of the Hollingsworth & Vose Company - Floyd.
- John Hunsinger stated: "We are very pleased with the decision to expand our Floyd operations and are very appreciative of all the support that helped to make this possible."
- The Virginia Economic Development Partnership, Floyd County, and American Electric Power assisted the company in its decision to expand.
- The Virginia Department of Business Assistance will provide workforce training services for the expansion.
