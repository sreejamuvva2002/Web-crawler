# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-01-10

**Source:** [Porsche Cars North America starts new year with two new](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2022/01/10/2364400/0/en/Porsche-Cars-North-America-starts-new-year-with-two-new-executives.html)  ·  `page_029213`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- PCNA has been headquartered in Atlanta since 1998.
- Ayesha Coker was named Vice President, Marketing of PCNA, effective immediately.
- Margareta Mahlstedt joined PCNA as Vice President, Customer Experience in October 2021.
- The new appointments bring the PCNA executive leadership team to nine members.
- PCNA closed 2021 with new records for new vehicle and Certified Pre-Owned car deliveries.
- Coker previously served as Director of Experiential Marketing for PCNA and succeeds Pedro Mota.
- Coker has led the brand’s experiential brand marketing, strategic partnerships, sports marketing, motorsport, and the Porsche Club of America in the U.S.
- Coker oversaw operations of the Porsche Track Experience, Porsche Travel Experience, and the Porsche Experience Centers in Atlanta and Los Angeles.
- Mahlstedt is responsible for CRM practices, customer satisfaction, market research, Vehicle Initial Quality, and overseeing Porsche Contact Center operations.
- Kjell Gruner serves as President and CEO of PCNA.
- Coker previously held marketing roles at Merkley and Partners and GMR Marketing.
- Coker holds a Bachelor of Science in Fashion Merchandising from the University of Maryland Eastern Shore and an Associate Applied Science degree in Advertising and Integrated Marketing Communications from the Fashion Institute of Technology.
- Mahlstedt previously held leadership roles across marketing and public relations for Porsche Cars Canada, Volvo Cars of Canada, and BMW Group Canada.
- Mahlstedt received a B.A. from Western University and an M.B.A. from Copenhagen Business School.
- PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera, and Taycan.
- PCNA operates the first Porsche Experience Center in North America in Atlanta, featuring a module-based 1.6-mile driver development track, business center, and Restaurant 356.
- PCNA operates a second Porsche Experience Center near Los Angeles on a 53-acre complex with an eight-module, 4.1-mile driver development track, business center, and Restaurant 917.
- PCNA supports 193 independently owned and operated Porsche dealerships in the U.S.
- PCNA supplies parts, service, marketing, and training to its dealership network.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Porsche boasts over 30,000 motorsport wins to date.
