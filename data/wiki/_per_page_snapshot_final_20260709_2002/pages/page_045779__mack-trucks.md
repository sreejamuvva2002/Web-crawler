# Mack Trucks

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-27

**Source:** [Mack Trucks Makes First Public Debut of All-New Mack Anthem® at ATA MC&E 2025](https://www.volvogroup.com/en/news-and-media/news/2025/oct/mack-trucks-makes-first-public-debut-of-all-new-mack-anthem-at-ata-mc-and-e-2025.html)  ·  `page_045779`

## Facts

- Mack Trucks made the first public debut of the all-new Mack Anthem at the American Trucking Associations (ATA) Management Conference & Exhibition (MC&E), October 25-28, at the San Diego Convention Center.
- The completely redesigned truck was originally launched in June 2025.
- The new Anthem delivers up to 10% improvement in fuel efficiency compared with the previous model.
- The truck features enhanced maneuverability and industry-leading driver comfort.
- Jonathan Randall, president of Mack Trucks North America, stated that the all-new Anthem represents a complete reimagining of what a modern Class 8 truck should deliver.
- The truck was designed to address fleet manager challenges including rising fuel costs, driver recruitment and retention, and maximizing productivity.
- The new Anthem is available in day cab, 44-inch sleeper, and 64-inch sleeper configurations.
- The truck’s enhanced maneuverability comes from a shorter bumper-to-back-of-cab (BBC) length of 113.5 inches.
- The redesigned hood improves forward visibility, allowing drivers to see approximately 12% closer to the front bumper compared with previous designs.
- Fernando Couceiro, Mack Trucks vice president of highway trucks, noted that fleets need a truck that excels in maneuverability without sacrificing comfort or efficiency.
- The Anthem features a completely redesigned cab interior that is nine inches wider than the previous model.
- New wide-base seats feature optional ventilation, heating, and massage functions.
- Stand-up sleeper options provide enough headroom for drivers to stand upright.
- Premium quality mattresses and blackout curtains are included to enable improved rest.
- The truck features advanced safety technologies, including an optional digital mirror system that eliminates traditional blind spots.
- Standard front airbags and an optional Integrated Curtain Airbag (ICA) system are included.
- Additional integrated driver assistance systems have demonstrated a 43% reduction in near-miss incidents during controlled testing.
- The new Anthem features Mack Connect, the company’s most advanced telematics platform for fleet operations visibility.
- Over-the-air programming enables overnight software updates without service appointments.
- The predictive maintenance system has been shown to reduce unplanned stops by 20%.
- Integration with Mack’s GuardDog® Connect automatically transmits service data to dealers, resulting in a 37% reduction in overall downtime compared with previous-generation trucks.
- The Anthem is particularly well-suited for weight-sensitive applications including bulk haul, food and beverage distribution, and less-than-truckload (LTL) hub & spoke operators.
- The new Mack Anthem is available for order now, with production beginning in January 2026.
- Mack Trucks has provided purpose-built transportation solutions for 125 years and is one of North America’s largest producers of heavy-duty trucks.
- Mack trucks, diesel engines, and transmissions sold in North America are assembled in the United States.
- Mack manufacturing locations are certified to ISO 9001 (quality), ISO 14001 (environmental management), and ISO 45001 (health and safety) standards.
- Mack Trucks is part of the Volvo Group, which employs about 100,000 people and serves customers in almost 180 markets.
- In 2024, the Volvo Group reported net sales of about $47 billion.
