# Yamaha Motor Corp.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Plant Tour - November 9 - Newnan -  Georgia Manufacturing Alliance](https://www.georgiamanufacturingalliance.com/events/yamahamotormanufacturingtour)  ·  `page_015845`

## Facts

- The facility is located at 1000 Highway 34 E, Newnan, GA 30265.
- The manufacturing site spans over 500,000 square feet under one roof.
- The facility manufactures All Terrain Vehicles (ATVs), Personal Watercraft (Waverunners), and Golf Carts using a combination of automated and manual processes with high-speed fabrication and assembly operations.
- Yamaha has manufactured high-quality outdoor adventure vehicles since 1954, adhering to founder Genichi Kawakami's slogan to 'Make every challenge an opportunity.'
- The company operates an employee-driven Kaizen (continuous improvement) system that engages employees in improving productivity, eliminating waste, and standardizing activities and processes.
- A plant tour event was scheduled for November 9 in Newnan, Georgia.
- The tour agenda included registration from 11:30-12:00 pm, a corporate presentation and safety briefing from 12:00-12:30 pm, a plant tour from 12:30-1:45 pm, and a question and answer session from 1:45-2:00 pm.
- Event requirements mandated closed-toed shoes, photo ID for entry, and prohibited competitors, suppliers of competitors, high-heeled shoes, press, and photos.
- The event was restricted to Georgia Manufacturing Alliance (GMA) members only, limited to one person per company, and was sold out.
