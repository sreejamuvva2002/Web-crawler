# JTEKT North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-12-08

**Source:** [JTEKT North America Expands Manufacturing Operation in Greenville County - Expansion Solutions](https://www.expansionsolutionsmagazine.com/jtekt-north-america-manufacturing-operation-greenville-county/)  ·  `page_045405`

## Facts

- JTEKT North America announced it is expanding its manufacturing operation in Greenville County.
- The expansion requires a $48.4 million investment.
- The investment will create 87 new jobs.
- JTEKT North America operates as part of JTEKT Corporation.
- JTEKT North America manufactures products under the brands JTEKT Automotive Systems, Koyo Bearings, and Toyoda Machine Tools.
- JTEKT North America is a global leader in engineering and manufacturing automotive systems, bearing solutions, and high-performance machine tools.
- The company maintains its regional headquarters in Greenville, South Carolina.
- The company operates 12 manufacturing sites, three technical centers, and five distribution centers throughout North America.
- The expansion adds seven new machining lines to the existing facility.
- The expansion adds two new assembly lines to the existing facility.
- Additional processes will be added to machine components internally.
- JTEKT North America will produce front drive shafts at its existing facility.
- The existing facility is located at 1866 Old Grove Road in Piedmont, South Carolina.
- Operations for the expansion will be online in the third quarter of 2025.
- Gary Bourque serves as the President and CEO of JTEKT Americas.
- Gov. Henry McMaster praised South Carolina’s exceptional workforce and congratulated the company on its success and anticipated impact on Greenville County.
- Harry M. Lightsey III is the Secretary of Commerce for South Carolina.
- Dan Tripp is the Greenville County Council Chairman.
- The S.C. Department of Commerce serves as South Carolina’s leading economic development agency.
- S.C. Commerce works to recruit new businesses and help existing businesses grow.
- S.C. Commerce has recruited companies including BMW, Boeing, Continental, Giti Tire, LPL Financial Holdings, Mercedes-Benz Vans, Samsung, Toray, and Volvo Cars to South Carolina.
- S.C. Commerce supports startups, small and existing businesses, innovation, and rural development initiatives.
- S.C. Commerce partners with the S.C. Technical College System via readySC to support workforce training and recruiting.
- S.C. Commerce partners with the S.C. Department of Employment and Workforce to provide worker training and employment opportunities within the state.
- South Carolina has consistently been among the top states in the nation for attracting jobs through foreign direct investment on a per capita basis.
- Kelly Coakley serves as Director of Marketing and Communications at the South Carolina Department of Commerce.
