# Aspen Aerogels, Inc.

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels offers cutting-edge thermal management solutions for sectors including electric vehicles (EVs).
- The company's PyroThin® insulation tackles critical EV problems including thermal runaway in lithium-ion batteries.
- Aspen is positioned as a major player in an EV industry expected to reach over 100 million vehicles by 2030.
- Growing environmental and legislative concerns have helped Aspen since its products fulfill strict energy efficiency criteria and ESG targets.
- The company obtained a $150 million DOE loan in 2023 to grow its Georgia plant.
- The DOE loan raised PyroThin® manufacturing capacity at the Georgia facility.
- The expanded capacity helps satisfy growing domestic and international demand, especially as EV safety rules tighten worldwide.
- GM's move toward prismatic battery cells may lower Aspen's long-term income from that relationship.
- Aspen is diversifying among other OEMs and industrial uses to offset potential impacts from GM's technology shift.
- Aspen has larger prospects in cylindrical and pouch cell markets.
- Increasing demand in the EV industry and industrial uses has driven constant upward changes in Aspen's revenue projections.
- Aspen boosted its revenue guidance several times in 2024.
- The company's strategic efforts focus on growing production and enhancing operational efficiency to maintain a central role in energy-efficient markets.
