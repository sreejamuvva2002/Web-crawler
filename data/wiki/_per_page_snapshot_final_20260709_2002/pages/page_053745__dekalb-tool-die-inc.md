# DeKalb Tool & Die, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Incorporated in 1968.
- Specializes in the design, construction, and repair of a wide range of metalworking tools.
- Provides progressive dies, transfer dies, draw dies, or single hit tooling.
- Offers CAD design through its internal engineering staff.
- Delivers critical tooling requirements using company trucks.
- Operates mechanical presses, hydraulic presses, and double action presses from 60 to 1800 tons.
- Has coil feed capability up to 60 inches wide for producing stamped metal parts.
- Recently awarded Blue Bird Corporation’s Global Supplier of the Year award.
- Maintains a proven record of excellent customer service, on-time deliveries, and effective project management as a materials supplier.
- Considers the skills of its employees and tool makers its most valuable asset.
- Located in Tucker, Georgia, convenient to both the Atlanta International Airport and the Atlanta Perimeter Highway.
- Provides emergency metal stamping services to resolve urgent production needs.
- Pledges competitive and fair prices for all tooling and manufacturing services rendered.
