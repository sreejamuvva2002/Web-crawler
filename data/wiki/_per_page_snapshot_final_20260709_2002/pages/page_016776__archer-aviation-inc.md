# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Archer Aviation - Archer Closes Incentive Transaction; Begins Construction on First of its Kind High-Volume eVTOL Manufacturing Facility in Covington, GA](https://www.investors.archer.com/news/news-details/2023/Archer-Closes-Incentive-Transaction-Begins-Construction-on-First-of-its-Kind-High-Volume-eVTOL-Manufacturing-Facility-in-Covington-GA/default.aspx)  ·  `page_016776`

## Facts

- Industry-first high-volume facility for manufacturing eVTOL aircraft is strategically located adjacent to the Covington Municipal Airport in Covington, GA.
- Archer and Stellantis are set to join forces to begin manufacturing Archer’s Midnight eVTOL aircraft at the facility in 2024.
- On February 28th, Archer closed on the local incentive transaction for the project that, along with a Georgia REBA grant, totals ~33% of the planned capital investment.
- On March 2nd, Archer began construction on Phase 1 of the project -- build out of a ~350,000 square-foot facility capable of producing up to 650 aircraft per year.
- The Covington facility will be capable of being expanded by an additional 800,000 square feet, which is estimated to support long-term production targets of up to 2,300 aircraft per year.
- In January, Archer announced an expanded relationship with Stellantis under which the two companies will join forces to manufacture the company’s production aircraft, Midnight.
- The partnership leverages Archer’s world-class team of eVTOL, electric powertrain and certification experts alongside Stellantis’s advanced manufacturing technology and expertise, experienced personnel, and capital.
- The combination is intended to enable rapid scaling of aircraft production to meet Archer’s commercialization plans while allowing Archer to avoid hundreds of millions of dollars of spending during the manufacturing ramp up phase.
- The goal is for Stellantis to mass produce Archer’s eVTOL aircraft as its exclusive contract manufacturer.
- Adam Goldstein, Founder and CEO of Archer, stated that scale is key to unlocking the promise of the eVTOL industry and expressed excitement over support from the State of Georgia and Newton County.
- A ceremonial event marking the milestone will be held in April in Covington, GA.
- Archer Aviation Inc. designs and develops electric vertical takeoff and landing aircraft for use in urban air mobility networks, with its team based in Santa Clara, CA.
