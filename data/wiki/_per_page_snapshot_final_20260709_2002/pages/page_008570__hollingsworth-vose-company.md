## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose (H&V) is a global materials company focused on filtration and energy storage solutions.
- The company uses advanced materials across industries to support cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable environments.
- It operates from 13 facilities worldwide, with a footprint across the Americas, Europe, and Asia to meet global and regional customer needs efficiently and cost-effectively.
- The company emphasizes research and development and innovation to raise standards for filtration and separator materials.
- Applications for its advanced materials include cleanrooms, indoor air quality, drinking water protection, face masks and protective gear, battery separators, and start-stop engine systems.
- The company is headquartered at 112 Washington Street, East Walpole, Massachusetts.
- Hollingsworth & Vose employs between 501 and 1000 people.
- Recent activity includes expanding manufacturing capacity at its Mysuru, India plant.
- The company launched PlusZero PFAS-free products.
- H&V partnered with ArcActive on the GEM battery plate architecture.
- The GEM battery plate architecture earned the 2026 BCI Innovation Award recognition.
