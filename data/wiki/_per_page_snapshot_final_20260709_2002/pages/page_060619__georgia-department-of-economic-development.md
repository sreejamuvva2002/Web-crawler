# Georgia Department of Economic Development

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2024-06

**Source:** [Georgia Recognized for New Business Investment, Job Creation - Rome CEO](https://romeceo.com/features/2024/06/georgia-recognized-new-business-investment-job-creation/)  ·  `page_060619`

## Facts

- Georgia was awarded a Gold Shovel by economic development business publication Area Development for achieving significant success in attracting new business investment and job creation in 2023.
- The award announcement was made on Friday, June 28th, 2024, by the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD).
- Governor Brian P. Kemp stated that thousands of jobs were created across Georgia thanks to economic development teams in all four corners of the state.
- Kemp noted that these efforts helped Georgia land record-breaking investments and hundreds of thousands of jobs for its communities.
- Kemp attributed Georgia's continued No. 1 ranking for business to a pro-business approach, an unmatched workforce, world-renowned logistics, and letting the market work.
- Area Development’s annual Gold and Silver Shovel Awards recognize state economic development agencies that demonstrate excellence in creating a business-friendly environment, fostering innovation, and implementing strategies that successfully attracted high-quality investment projects.
- All states were invited to submit information to Area Development on major groundbreakings, investments, and business expansions occurring in 2023.
- Area Development Editor Andy Greiner said the awards highlight the exceptional efforts of state economic development teams and their unwavering commitment to building strong economies.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson highlighted advanced manufacturing, aerospace, e-mobility, life sciences, and fintech as industries instrumental in achieving a Shovel Award again this year.
- Wilson reflected on back-to-back record-breaking years of investment and job creation in Georgia.
- Wilson expressed anticipation for celebrating upcoming milestones with partners who make these opportunities possible.
- Wilson congratulated Governor Kemp, the state legislature, the state economic development team, and all statewide partners for their dedication to sowing opportunities that communities will reap for decades to come.
