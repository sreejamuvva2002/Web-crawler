# FREYR Battery

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Energy, Automobile, EV, Renewable News | FREYR Battery Announce the Appointments of  New Board of Directors](https://oilandgaspress.com/freyr-battery-announce-the-appointments-of-new-board-of-directors/)  ·  `page_055047`

## Facts

- FREYR Battery is a developer of clean, next-generation battery cell production capacity.
- The company appointed Todd Kantor, Tore Ivar Slettemoen, and David Manners to its Board of Directors effective immediately to fill vacancies and strengthen the board for growth and capital formation initiatives.
- Jason Forcier and Mimi Berdal notified the Board of their intentions not to stand for re-election as Board directors on April 14, 2024, and April 15, 2024, respectively, and subsequently resigned effective immediately.
- Todd Kantor noted that FREYR has a cash balance of $276 million at year end 2023 and ownership of a strategic site in Georgia for the Giga America project.
- The company is commencing development of the Giga America battery manufacturing project in Coweta County, Georgia, in the U.S.
- FREYR Battery's mission is to accelerate the decarbonization of global energy and transportation systems by producing clean, cost-competitive batteries.
- The company seeks to serve primary markets of energy storage systems (ESS) and commercial mobility, with an ambition to serve the passenger electric vehicles market (EV).
- FREYR is operating its Customer Qualification Plant (CQP) for technology development in Mo I, Rana, Norway.
- David Manners commented that development of localized supply chains and production capacity in the Western hemisphere are fundamental to energy security and geostrategic objectives, noting FREYR's vital role as an emerging battery producer.
- Tore Ivar Slettemoen developed the concept and founded FREYR Battery and serves as Chairman of Teknovekst AS – Vanir, an institutional investment fund focused on high-growth energy transition investments.
- Todd Kantor is the Founder and Managing Member of Encompass Capital Advisors LLC, a cornerstone investor in FREYR.
- Mr. Kantor has nearly 25 years of experience in the global energy markets as a portfolio manager, analyst, trader, and investment banker.
- Prior to founding Encompass Capital in 2012, Mr. Kantor was a portfolio manager at Citadel LLC’s PioneerPath Capital platform.
- David Manners served for 20 years at the U.S. Central Intelligence Agency, including multiple tours abroad and serving as the CIA’s Chief of Station on two occasions.
- Tom Einar Jensen is Co-Founder and Executive Chairperson of FREYR.
