# Aspen Aerogels, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2022-02-17

**Source:** [Energy & Sustainability Client Feature — Aspen Aerogels | Mintz](https://www.mintz.com/insights-center/viewpoints/2151/2022-03-08-energy-sustainability-client-feature-aspen-aerogels)  ·  `page_052675`

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels, Inc. (NYSE: ASPN) is a technology leader in aerogel-based sustainability and electrification solutions.
- On February 17, 2022, Koch Strategic Platforms (KSP), a subsidiary of Koch Investments Group, agreed to make a $150 million investment in Aspen through the purchase of convertible notes and common stock.
- As part of the investment, KSP agreed to purchase convertible notes in the aggregate principal amount of $100 million.
- KSP also agreed to purchase 1,791,986 shares of Aspen's common stock at a price of $27.902 per share, resulting in proceeds to Aspen of $50 million.
- Donald R. Young, Aspen's President and CEO, noted the company's strategy leverages its Aerogel Technology Platform into high-value, high-growth markets driven by PyroThin® thermal barriers which address thermal runaway in electric vehicles (EVs).
- KSP's investment will support growth and allow Aspen to address additional high-value applications in ESG-driven markets, including battery materials, hydrogen energy, carbon capture, and filtration.
- Aspen announced plans to construct an advanced manufacturing facility at Bulloch County, Georgia’s Southern Gateway Commerce Park.
- Combined with its original East Providence, Rhode Island plant, the planned Georgia facility is designed to triple Aspen's nameplate aerogel capacity in support of thermal barrier expansion plans in the EV market.
- Aspen aims to capitalize on increased demand for its Aerogel Technology Platform as EV OEMs continue to adopt its PyroThin thermal barrier solutions.
- Mintz has represented Aspen since 2010 in numerous matters including the company's 2014 IPO, additional financings, and strategic partnerships targeting new product and market opportunities.
