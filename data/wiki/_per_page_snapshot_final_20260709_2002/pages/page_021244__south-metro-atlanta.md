# South Metro Atlanta

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2024-02-29

**Source:** [South Metro Atlanta | Sophisticated Growth - Georgia Trend Magazine](https://www.georgiatrend.com/2024/02/29/south-metro-atlanta-sophisticated-growth/)  ·  `page_021244`

## Facts

- FREYR Battery is building a $2.6 billion clean-battery manufacturing plant in Coweta County.
- The FREYR Battery facility is expected to produce 724 jobs when fully operational in 2026.
- Hyundai Industrial Co., a global auto parts supplier, was selected to build a manufacturing facility in Coweta County.
- Hyundai Industrial Co. will invest more than $24 million to retrofit an existing building for its new Coweta County facility.
- The Hyundai Industrial Co. facility will produce arm and head rests for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).
- Hyundai Motor Group’s Metaplant in Bryan County and Kia’s West Point plant are identified as OEM customers for the Hyundai Industrial Co. project.
- The Hyundai Industrial Co. project will create 100 new jobs.
- Automotive parts manufacturer NVH Korea is joining a new industrial park project in Henry County alongside Renewal by Andersen.
- The NVH Korea project will create over 160 new jobs.
- The NVH Korea project will bring $72 million in investment to Henry County.
- Henry County's manufacturing job growth has ranked fourth in the state and fifth highest across all industries over the last four years.
- Reshoring efforts have attracted manufacturers to Henry County due to its location and existing supply-chain infrastructure.
- Coweta County's economic development strategy is focused almost exclusively on advanced manufacturing.
- Porsche’s North American headquarters is located in Hapeville near Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport.
