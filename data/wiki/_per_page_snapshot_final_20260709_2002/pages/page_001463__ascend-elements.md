# Ascend Elements

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2026-04

## Facts

- Battery recycler Ascend Elements has filed for bankruptcy.
- The company operates a major plant located 35 miles east of Atlanta.
- Ascend Elements is the latest firm in the electric vehicle and clean energy supply chain to show signs of struggle under President Donald Trump’s administration.
- The company filed for Chapter 11 protection on Thursday in the Southern District of Texas’ bankruptcy court.
- CEO Linh Austin revealed in a declaration that the company has $103.5 million in long-term debt.
- Austin cited a lack of early, sustained revenue generation as a primary issue.
- Construction delays, cost overruns, and production constraints exacerbated the financial situation.
- These factors created a near-term liquidity shortfall that limited available alternatives.
