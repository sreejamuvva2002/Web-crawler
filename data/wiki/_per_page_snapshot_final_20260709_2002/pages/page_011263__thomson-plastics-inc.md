# Thomson Plastics, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2014-02-17

**Source:** [Thomson Plastics, Inc. Announces the Opening of Howell Michigan Plastic Injection Molding Facility](https://www.prweb.com/releases/thomson_plastics_inc_announces_the_opening_of_howell_michigan_plastic_injection_molding_facility/prweb11585970.htm)  ·  `page_011263`

## Facts

- Thomson Plastics opened its newest facility in Howell, Michigan, in January 2014.
- The Howell, Michigan facility spans 50,000 square feet and is centrally located between Flint, Lansing, and the Greater Detroit area.
- The plant initially opened with seven presses ranging from 400 tons to 800 tons capacity, along with an experienced technical staff to support existing and new customers.
- Thomson Plastics has plans for additional investments in presses and related support equipment at the Howell facility.
- The company will produce assembled components at the new location.
- Thomson Plastics utilizes lean manufacturing principles and a customer-focused approach for molding and assembly product requirements.
- Additional services provided include Product Design, Tooling Design and Management, Just-In-Time Delivery, Value Analysis/Value Engineering (VA/VE), and Customer Site Representative Services.
- The company utilizes gas assist, in mold decoration, pad printing, heat staking, and hot stamping technologies.
- All molding and other operations are performed with the benefit of AIAG APQP guidelines.
- Thomson Plastics engaged Ann Arbor SPARK to assist in applying for local incentives.
- Thomson Plastics coordinated with Michigan Works! on workforce development programs.
- Paul Krutko, president and CEO of SPARK (Ann Arbor’s engine for economic development), stated that attracting an out-of-state company to Howell speaks volumes about the community's assets to a growing manufacturer like Thomson Plastics.
- Thomson Plastics was established in 1975 and develops processes that integrate design complexity with steady and reliable production.
- The company's facility in Thomson, Georgia, has over 200,000 square feet dedicated to manufacturing, assembly, and warehousing.
- With the addition of the Howell, Michigan location, Thomson Plastics' two plastic injection molding locations have a combined press capability ranging from 165 tons to 3,150 tons.
- Thomson Plastics, Inc. is a portfolio company of Altus Capital Partners I, L.P., an investment firm specializing in middle market manufacturing companies based in the U.S.
