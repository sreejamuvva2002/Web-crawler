# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2021-06-23

**Source:** [Carlyle, Bain submit preliminary bids for $5.8 billion stake in Hanon Systems -newspaper | Euronews](https://www.euronews.com/next/2021/06/23/uk-hanon-systems-m-a)  ·  `page_055703`

## Facts

- Carlyle Group, Bain Capital and auto supplier Mahle have each submitted preliminary bids to buy a 70% stake in South Korea's Hanon Systems worth an estimated 6.6 trillion won ($5.8 billion).
- The combined stake in Hanon has been put up for sale by local private equity firm Hahn & Company and Hankook Tire & Technology Co Ltd.
- Hanon Systems said in a regulatory filing that nothing has been decided regarding the potential sale.
- A spokesperson for Germany's Mahle declined to comment on the bidding process.
- Representatives for Carlyle and Bain did not immediately respond to requests for comment.
- France's Valeo had also submitted a preliminary bid but the auto supplier denied that it had.
- Hanon Systems is the world's second-largest supplier of auto thermal management systems.
- Hanon Systems reported revenue of 6.9 trillion won and operating profit of 315.8 billion won last year.
