# RACEMARK

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-02-20

**Source:** [Racing to sustainability: RACEMARK becomes first company from automotive industry to partner with Healthy Seas - EIN Presswire](https://www.einnews.com/pr_news/476974279/racing-to-sustainability-racemark-becomes-first-company-from-automotive-industry-to-partner-with-healthy-seas)  ·  `page_010313`

## Facts

- RACEMARK is a producer of the world’s finest automotive floor mats and interiors.
- RACEMARK has joined Healthy Seas, an environmental initiative that recovers discarded fishing nets from the seas to regenerate them into new products.
- RACEMARK is the first company from the automotive industry to partner with Healthy Seas.
- Healthy Seas was founded by the non-profit organisation Ghost Fishing and two sustainable enterprises, Aquafil and Star Sock.
- RACEMARK’s vision is to become the global leader in sustainable automotive floor protection by 2023.
- RACEMARK aims to develop and sell products that create no waste and require no new material resources because they can be recovered at the end of their useful life and endlessly upcycled, recycled or regenerated.
- Cannon Bailey serves as President of Racemark.
- Since its launch in 2013, Healthy Seas has collected 453 tons of waste nets from the seas and from land.
- The recovered fishing nets, sometimes called “ghost nets”, remain in the marine ecosystem for hundreds of years entangling and killing many marine animals including whales, turtles and birds.
- Thanks to the ECONYL Regeneration System, the nylon in the fishing nets is regenerated into high-quality ECONYL yarn which is then turned into brand new sustainable products.
- According to a joint report by the FAO and UNEP, an estimated 640,000 tons of fishing gear are left in oceans each year, accounting for one-tenth of all marine litter.
- Racemark’s vision includes reducing its environmental impact, innovating products & processes, and expanding into new markets by 2023.
- Racemark was the first company in the world to create a custom-fit carpet car mat.
- The company was founded in 1972 by Bob Bailey, a Porsche of America team world class race car driver, on the principal of engineering excellence.
- Today, Racemark is a 100% family owned and operated business that includes manufacturing locations in the United States, Germany, Hungary and Switzerland.
- Racemark delivers features and product enhancements to achieve “best in class” parts for the global automotive industry.
- Racemark’s customers include Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Tesla, Acura, Audi, Bentley, Chrysler, GM, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, MINI, Mitsubishi, Toyota and Volkswagen.
- Healthy Seas is an open initiative that welcomes new partners on board who are committed to circular economy, sustainability and marine protection.
- The mission of Healthy Seas is to remove waste from the seas, in particular fishing nets, for the purpose of creating healthier seas and recycling marine litter into textile products.
