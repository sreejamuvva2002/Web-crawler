# Foresight Autonomous Holdings Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Foresight Autonomous Holdings Ltd. announced signing an agreement with Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc. to test and evaluate autonomous-driving vision systems (ADAS).
- Hitachi Astemo is a global Tier One supplier of engine management, electric powertrain and integrated vehicle-control systems for the auto industry.
- The paid joint proof of concept project with Hitachi Astemo calls for evaluating and testing simulated and real-life autonomous driving scenarios.
- The program pairs Foresight QuadSight® and Mono2Stereo™ technologies with existing Hitachi Astemo camera systems to improve distance measurement and object detection.
- This project follows the successful evaluation of a QuadSight vision system prototype reported by Foresight in March 2021.
- Foresight’s proprietary Mono2Stereo software-based solution uses overlapping views of existing cameras with different fields of vision to create a three-dimensional stereo-vision system for improved object detection.
- Haim Siboni, CEO of Foresight, stated that being chosen by Hitachi Astemo underlines the importance of stereoscopic technology in enhancing ADAS systems, amplifying performance, distance accuracy, and active safety features.
- Foresight's solutions can be deployed in vehicles equipped with Level 2 and Level 2-plus autonomy systems without requiring additional hardware and design changes.
- Haim Siboni believes teaming up with Hitachi Astemo could potentially lead to further collaboration with other automotive OEMs.
- Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc. manufactures and markets engine management, electric powertrain, and integrated vehicle control for major automotive manufacturers worldwide while adhering to ISO/TS:16949:2009 industry standards.
- Foresight Autonomous Holdings Ltd. is a technology company developing smart multi-spectral vision software solutions and cellular-based applications.
- Through wholly owned subsidiaries Foresight Automotive Ltd., Foresight Changzhou Automotive Ltd. and Eye-Net Mobile Ltd., Foresight develops “in-line-of-sight” vision systems and “beyond-line-of-sight” accident-prevention solutions.
- Foresight’s vision solutions include modules of automatic calibration and dense three-dimensional (3D) point cloud applicable to automotive, defense, autonomous vehicles and heavy industrial equipment markets.
- Eye-Net Mobile’s cellular-based solution suite provides real-time pre-collision alerts to enhance road safety using AI technology and advanced analytics.
