# Georgia Department of Labor

## Facts

- Economic information is of interest to job seekers, students, workforce planners, employers, researchers, and educators.
- The provided data offers a snapshot of Georgia’s economy.
- Georgia economic indicators include employment by industry, weekly manufacturing earnings, unemployment insurance data, new business starts, motor vehicle sales, construction permits, and state revenues.
- Local Employment Dynamics (LED) and Quarterly Workforce Indicators (QWI) provide new, current, and local information on earnings and employment dynamics.
- Interactive mapping is available to show where people work and where workers live.
- Industry Focus indicators provide new, current, and local information on industries.
- Detailed industry information includes the top industries for a local area and its workers.
- Industry Focus data shows how a particular industry ranks among top industries.
- Industry Focus data provides characteristics of workers in a specific industry.
