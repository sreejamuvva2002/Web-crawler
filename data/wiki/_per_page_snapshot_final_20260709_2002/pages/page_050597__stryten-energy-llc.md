# Stryten Energy LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-04-13

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Launches New M‑Series Li610 Lithium‑Ion Battery at MODEX](https://www.bastillepost.com/global/article/5770814-stryten-energy-launches-new-m‑series-li610-lithium‑ion-battery-at-modex)  ·  `page_050597`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC, a U.S.-based energy storage solutions provider headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia, announced the launch of its new M-Series Li610 lithium-ion battery.
- The M-Series Li610 expands the company's motive power portfolio with a high-performance solution designed for demanding Class I forklift applications.
- At MODEX 2026 in Booth #B9325, Stryten Energy showcased its latest advancements in lead and lithium batteries, intelligent chargers, a new web-based battery finder and comparison tool, and energy performance management tools.
- Matt Gould, Vice President, Sales, Industrial Power at Stryten Energy, stated that the Li610 launch reflects the company's commitment to providing advanced, reliable solutions to help customers achieve higher uptime and greater productivity in warehouses and distribution operations.
- Stryten Energy featured its Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) offerings, which integrate both lead and lithium technologies to deliver dependable backup power and grid-support capabilities.
- The BESS solutions provide strong performance under high discharge rates, simplified maintenance, and high surge-current capabilities, making them well-suited for short- to medium-duration energy storage and high-power applications.
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors, partnering with major companies to meet demand for reliable and sustainable energy storage capacity.
- The company's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software, backed by more than a century of industry expertise.
