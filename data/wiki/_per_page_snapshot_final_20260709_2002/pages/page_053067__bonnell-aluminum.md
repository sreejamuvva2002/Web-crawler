# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-04

**Source:** [Bonnell Aluminum Commenting on Recent Trade Filing from Aluminum](https://www.gurufocus.com/news/2099474/bonnell-aluminum-commenting-on-recent-trade-filing-from-aluminum-extruders-coalition-and-united-steelworkers-union)  ·  `page_053067`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation (NYSE: TG).
- Bonnell Aluminum applauds the U.S. Aluminum Extruders Coalition and the United Steelworkers union following the October 4, 2023 filing of antidumping and countervailing duty petitions against unfairly traded imports of aluminum extrusions from China, Colombia, the Dominican Republic, Ecuador, India, Indonesia, Italy, Malaysia, Mexico, South Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, Turkey, the United Arab Emirates, and Vietnam.
- Bonnell Aluminum is part of the U.S. Aluminum Extruders Coalition.
- The petitions were filed concurrently with the U.S. Department of Commerce and the U.S. International Trade Commission (USITC) on October 4, 2023.
- The petitions address large and increasing volumes of unfairly priced imports that have materially injured the American aluminum extrusion industry since 2019.
- Producers in the subject countries used dumped and subsidized prices to gain significant market share in the United States at the direct expense of the U.S. industry and thousands of USW workers.
- Increasing volumes of dumped and subsidized imports prevented U.S. extruders from capitalizing on market growth, leading to declines in production, sales, profitability, and market share.
- Brook Hamilton, president of Bonnell Aluminum, stated the company is determined to end the surge of unfairly dumped and subsidized imports flooding the U.S. market.
- Hamilton emphasized that enforcing trade laws will ensure U.S. aluminum manufacturers compete on a level playing field and help end continuous layoffs devastating workers, the industry, and communities.
- Bonnell Aluminum manufactures custom-fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions serving the building and construction, automotive and transportation, consumer durables, machinery and equipment, and electrical and distribution markets.
- Branded product lines include TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum and Futura Transitions by Bonnell Aluminum.
- Bonnell Aluminum operates manufacturing facilities in Newnan GA, Carthage TN, Niles MI, Elkhart IN, and Clearfield UT.
