# Seoyon E-HWA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-01

**Source:** [Seoyon E-HWA to spend $76 Million to expand into new space in Savannah Georgia. - intelligence360](https://www.intelligence360.news/seoyon-e-hwa-to-spend-76-million-to-expand-into-new-space-in-savannah-georgia/)  ·  `page_059504`

## Facts

- Seoyon E-HWA plans to invest $76,000,000.00 to build out new space in Savannah, Georgia.
- The company plans to occupy the new space in Savannah on or about October 1, 2024.
- Seoyon E-HWA was established in 1972 and split its holding company in July 2014.
- The company started with the production of Pony interior products before expanding to produce Door Trim, Bumper, Seat, Head Lining, Covering Self, Package Tray, Screen Assy, and Pillar Trim domestically and overseas.
- Seoyon E-HWA is recognized as Korea’s leading automobile interior parts company.
- The company focuses on securing R&D, quality, and technological competitiveness by designing and proposing new materials, new product development, and new product models.
- Affiliates are specialized according to the quality and characteristics of products to increase responsiveness to global automakers.
- Audi and Nissan are listed as major global automaker customers for Seoyon E-HWA.
- Yongsuk Kang serves as the Chief Executive Officer of the company.
