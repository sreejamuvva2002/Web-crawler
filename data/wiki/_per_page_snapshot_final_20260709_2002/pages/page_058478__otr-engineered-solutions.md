# OTR Engineered Solutions

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2026-01-05

## Facts

- OTR announced the appointment of Oscar Torres as President and Chief Executive Officer.
- The leadership transition is effective January 5, 2026.
- The organizational announcement was issued from Rome, GA.
- Oscar Torres brings more than 25 years of executive leadership experience in private equity–backed organizations.
- He has deep expertise in the aerospace aftermarket.
- He spent the majority of his career at Kellstrom Aerospace, a global distributor and technical services provider.
- At Kellstrom, he advanced through senior leadership roles including Chief Financial Officer, Chief Operating Officer, and Chief Executive Officer.
- During his tenure at Kellstrom, he led initiatives that strengthened operational performance and drove sustainable growth for the company and its business partners.
- He played a key role in multiple strategic acquisitions and divestitures.
- He helped position Kellstrom as a leading global supply-chain solutions provider serving airlines, OEMs, MROs, and aircraft lessors worldwide.
- Hector Ramirez, Interim President of OTR, stated that Oscar’s proven leadership, collaborative approach, and deep industry knowledge make him exceptionally well suited to lead OTR into its next phase of growth.
- Hector Ramirez expressed excitement about welcoming Oscar and confidence in the value he will bring to customers, partners, and stakeholders.
- Oscar Torres is a Certified Public Accountant.
- He holds a Bachelor of Accounting from Florida International University.
- He holds an MBA from the University of Miami.
