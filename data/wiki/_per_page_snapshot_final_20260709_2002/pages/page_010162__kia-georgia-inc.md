# Kia Georgia, Inc.

**Source:** [Kia Georgia Demonstrates Commitment to Education with $100,000 Donation to the Technical College System of Georgia Foundation – TCSG | Technical College System of Georgia](https://www.tcsg.edu/kia-georgia-demonstrates-commitment-to-education-with-100000-donation-to-the-technical-college-system-of-georgia-foundation/)  ·  `page_010162`

## Facts

- Kia Georgia announced a $100,000 donation to the Technical College System of Georgia (TCSG) Foundation.
- Kia Georgia President and CEO Stuart Countess presented the donation check at TCSG’s annual GOAL and Rick Perkins Awards banquet celebrating top students and instructors.
- The donation will directly benefit students enrolled in technical colleges across Georgia by providing vital resources to enhance educational opportunities and career pathways.
- Since 2016, Kia Georgia has provided funding to award the state GOAL winner a brand-new Kia as the grand prize.
- This year’s GOAL winner will receive a brand-new Kia Sportage built at the Kia facility in West Point.
- Remaining funding from this year's contribution will support The Last Mile Fund to provide gap funding for more than 131,000 technical education students served within TCSG.
- TCSG Commissioner Greg Dozier stated the donation will help remove barriers to education and create pathways to success in high-demand fields.
- In 2006, Kia made one of the largest single foreign investments in Georgia's history by establishing a state-of-the-art production facility in West Point.
- Kia Georgia has an annual capacity of more than 350,000 vehicles.
- Kia Georgia is home to the Telluride SUV, Sorento SUV, Sportage SUV, and all-electric EV9.
- The advanced manufacturing facility represents a $2.8 billion investment on a 2,200-acre site.
- The facility operates 24 hours a day.
- Kia Georgia, Inc. is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Corporation based in Seoul, Korea.
- Kia Georgia began mass production on Nov. 16, 2009.
- TCSG consists of 22 colleges offering training in more than 600 associate degrees, diploma, and certificate programs.
- TCSG houses Georgia’s Office of Adult Education, which promotes adult literacy and education programs including the GED testing program throughout the state.
- TCSG partners with companies through Quick Start, described as the nation’s top customized workforce training program.
- The TCSG Foundation is a 501(c)(3) organization that supports TCSG through marketing, advocacy, and fundraising.
