# MöllerTech USA LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [MöllerTech Opens Injection Molding Facility | Gray](https://www.gray.com/insights/mollertech-holds-grand-opening-for-new-plastic-injection-molding-facility-in-alabama/)  ·  `page_058004`

## Facts

- German automotive supplier MöllerTech held a ribbon-cutting ceremony to officially open its new facility in Woodstock, Alabama.
- The 151,313 s.f. facility produces interior parts for automakers such as Mercedes-Benz.
- The plant's operational timeline was accelerated by eight months.
- Gunther Schmidt, CEO of MöllerGroup (MöllerTech’s parent company), stated the new state-of-the-art plant has been a tremendous effort for everyone involved.
- Steve Jordan, president of MöllerTech USA LLC, noted they just opened the building and plan to be there for many years to come.
- MöllerTech selected Gray Construction to design and build its advanced manufacturing facility, which will serve as a flagship U.S. operation.
- The facility is located just six miles from the Vance, Alabama campus for Mercedes-Benz.
- The plant will specifically manufacture parts for Mercedes-Benz’s next-generation SUVs.
- Stephen Gray, president and CEO of Gray Construction, commented on the partnership and the company's significant impact on Alabama and the auto industry.
- The plant currently employs approximately 50 people.
- MöllerTech expects to have 222 team members once the facility reaches full capacity in 2019.
- Founded nearly 300 years ago, parent company MöllerGroup is a family-owned business with 1,800 employees worldwide.
- MöllerTech is recognized worldwide for the development and production of plastic parts in the automotive industry, as well as surfacing, flocking, leather covering, gluing, and stitching services.
- Gray Construction is a nationally-ranked engineering, architecture and construction firm with nearly 60 years of experience in manufacturing, automotive, food and beverage, distribution and retail facility construction.
- Gray Construction currently has several large projects underway or recently completed, including facilities for Mercedes-Benz in Vance, Ala., MöllerTech USA in Woodstock, Ala., Fritz Winter North America LP in Franklin, Ky., Volvo Car US Operations Inc. in Ridgeville, S.C., and Sofidel in Circleville, Ohio.
