# The Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2021-09-28

**Source:** [Goodyear Invests In Autonomous Trucking Company Gatik And Collaborates To Enhance Safety And Efficiency For Autonomous Middle Mile Logistics | GT Stock News](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/GT/goodyear-invests-in-autonomous-trucking-company-gatik-and-nhyx66tc4ag4.html)  ·  `page_055163`

## Facts

- Goodyear and Gatik announced a multi-year collaboration to advance mobility solutions for autonomous B2B short-haul logistics.
- The partnership aims to improve safety and sustainability in goods movement while reducing costs for Gatik's customers nationwide.
- Goodyear Ventures participated in Gatik's Series B funding.
- Joint projects will utilize Goodyear SightLine technology to monitor tire performance in real-time.
- Gatik operates the first autonomous middle mile logistics network in North America.
- Gatik is expanding its fleet of Class 3-6 autonomous box trucks across Texas, Arkansas, Louisiana, and Ontario.
- Goodyear is one of the world's largest tire companies with over 120 years of experience developing technology and performance standards for the industry.
- The collaboration expands service levels and capacity for both organizations across the U.S.
- Gatik's medium-duty fleet will use tires equipped with tire intelligence technology powered by Goodyear SightLine.
- The tire intelligence technology aims to improve stopping distances in autonomous commercial vehicles and monitor tire pressure in real time.
- Use of tire intelligence solutions is expected to reduce fuel and maintenance costs while increasing operational efficiencies for retailers, e-commerce companies, and distributors employing Gatik's autonomous solution.
- Chris Helsel, Goodyear's senior vice president, global operations and chief technology officer, stated that leveraging advanced tire intelligence solutions can improve vehicle performance and safety in autonomous transportation.
- Arjun Narang, CTO and co-founder of Gatik, noted that the collaboration aligns with Gatik's mission to enhance safety, increase efficiency, and reduce costs.
- Abhijit Ganguly, managing director of Goodyear Ventures, said Gatik's mission aligns with their investment strategy and includes key autonomous delivery players in long-haul and last-mile markets.
- Goodyear employs about 72,000 people and manufactures products in 55 facilities across 23 countries.
- Goodyear operates two Innovation Centers in Akron, Ohio, and Colmar-Berg, Luxembourg.
- Gatik delivers goods using light and medium duty trucks for Fortune 500 retailers like Walmart and Loblaw.
- Gatik was founded in 2017 by veterans of the autonomous technology industry and has offices in Palo Alto and Toronto.
- Gatik was recognized on the 2021 Forbes AI 50 list and as a World Economic Forum Technology Pioneer.
