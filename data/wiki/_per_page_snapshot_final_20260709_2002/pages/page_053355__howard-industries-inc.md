## Facts

- Howard Industries, Inc. is headquartered in Laurel, MS.
- The company specializes in the design, manufacturing, and distribution of electric transformers.
- Howard Industries has decades of experience and is a leading provider of high-quality transformers and comprehensive service capabilities.
- The product portfolio includes dry-type transformers, oil-filled transformers, cast coil transformers, pad-mounted transformers, and custom-designed transformers.
- These transformers are engineered to meet diverse power distribution and control needs across industries.
- The company emphasizes customization, with skilled engineers and technicians collaborating with clients to develop tailored transformer solutions based on specific power requirements.
- Howard Industries operates state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities equipped with cutting-edge equipment and machinery.
- Rigorous quality control measures are applied throughout the manufacturing process, including premium material selection, stringent testing, and inspection procedures.
- The company provides comprehensive service capabilities including installation assistance, preventive maintenance services, repairs, retrofits, and technical support.
- Howard Industries serves a wide range of industries, including utilities, industrial, commercial, and renewable energy sectors.
