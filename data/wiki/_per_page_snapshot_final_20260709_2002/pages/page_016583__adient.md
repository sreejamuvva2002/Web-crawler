# Adient

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Adient celebrates expansion at Greenfield facility | The Highland County Press](https://highlandcountypress.com/news/adient-celebrates-expansion-greenfield-facility)  ·  `page_016583`

## Facts

- Adient celebrated the expansion of its Greenfield facility with a ribbon-cutting ceremony attended by company, local, and regional officials on Wednesday, Nov. 2.
- The company is opening a storage facility at the Greenfield plant as part of a $6 million investment.
- According to Adient’s Director of Operations NA Foam Business Unit Todd Bruno, the new storage facility will hold more than two million pounds of chemical.
- The chemical stored at the facility is used in manufacturing operations at all seven Adient locations in North America.
- The need for the facility stems from a “significant weather event” that affected a chemical supplier in southern Texas, causing no deliveries for two weeks.
- During the supply disruption, customer orders continued to arrive, forcing Adient to import the chemical by air charter from overseas at a cost of millions.
- Company executive leadership challenged staff to develop a plan to make the chemical more accessible and avoid future supply issues.
- Adient evaluated other North American locations for the facility but selected Greenfield due to its central location.
- The Greenfield plant will be responsible for ordering, managing, and shipping the chemical to other Adient locations when needed.
- Todd Bruno stated that the investment positions the Greenfield plant as a shipping site for all other plants and well for future opportunities.
- Bruno noted that the organization could have placed the investment anywhere but chose Greenfield, stating it should make the plant team and community proud.
