# Braselton 85 Phase II

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Rockefeller Group recently completed two distribution buildings totaling 427,655 square feet on 39 acres in Braselton, Ga.
- Braselton 85- Phase II is located on Jesse Cronic Road and Highway 124 along the I-85 North corridor in the North Atlanta submarket.
- The project broke ground in 2023.
- It includes two rear-load distribution buildings totaling 207,009 and 220,646 square feet respectively.
- Both buildings have 32’ clear ceiling heights.
- Both buildings have 52’ x 50’ column spacing.
- The site includes ample auto and trailer parking.
- Braselton’s location along I-85 provides potential tenants with a distribution channel from Atlanta to Charlotte, bypassing metro-Atlanta traffic.
- The company sold the smaller of the two buildings first.
- In December 2025, the company sold the 220,646 square foot distribution center.
- The business park is Rockefeller Group’s second development in Braselton.
- Rockefeller Group completed and sold Braselton 85 in 2021, a 222,618-square-foot distribution center located one mile from the new project.
- Braselton 85 was leased to Duckyang America Inc., a Korean automotive equipment supplier.
- Braselton 85 was sold to Cabot Properties.
