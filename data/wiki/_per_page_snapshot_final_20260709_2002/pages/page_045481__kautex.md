# Kautex

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-02-17

**Source:** [Kautex Expands Adoption of PowerFleet Telematics Solution :: Powerfleet, Inc. (AIOT)](https://ir.powerfleet.com/press-releases/detail/399/kautex-expands-adoption-of-powerfleet-telematics-solution)  ·  `page_045481`

## Facts

- PowerFleet announced it will further deploy its PowerFleet Enterprise industrial telematics solution to Kautex throughout 2022.
- Kautex has more than doubled the number of sites employing PowerFleet’s telematics solutions on its forklifts in the past 12 months.
- The deployed sites span six countries and three continents.
- An additional nine sites are planned for implementation in 2022.
- PowerFleet’s Enterprise solution combines the VAC4S telematics platform with Enterprise software to improve workplace safety and security.
- The solution restricts vehicle access to trained, authorized operators, enforces electronic safety inspection checklists, and senses vehicle impacts.
- The technology empowers Kautex to manage, track, monitor, and control its powered industrial vehicles and their operators.
- The technology improves material handling productivity, reduces costs, and increases visibility by establishing accountability for forklifts and other industrial trucks.
- It ensures equipment is in the proper place at the right time and streamlines workflow.
- Since partnering with PowerFleet, Kautex has been able to right-size its fleet in several locations across multiple time zones.
- In the first facility, Kautex reduced its fleet by over 10% after analyzing utilization data from the PowerFleet Enterprise system.
- ROI was achieved through cost reductions of more than $10,000 per year for each truck eliminated, including fork truck and maintenance costs.
- Martin Weiss, Global Director Material Excellence at Kautex, stated that deploying the solution reduced impacts, accidents, and damage to equipment and goods.
- Kautex expanded adoption from a single site to more than eight locations globally with plans for continued deployments.
- PowerFleet’s global presence and ability to work across multiple geographies played a major role in Kautex's decision to grow the partnership.
- PowerFleet’s easy-to-use enterprise software also played a major role in the decision.
- The Enterprise solution allows Kautex to aggregate data from multiple facilities within one application for faster, more accurate decision-making.
- This has helped Kautex deliver greater safety, modernization, and assess/implement best practices across facilities.
- Joel Norris, Vice President of Sales at PowerFleet, noted that customers quickly see ROI and that PowerFleet’s customer service and global operations support were key to the partnership.
- Kautex will continue using the PowerFleet Enterprise solution and analytics platform, PowerFleet IQ, for safety and equipment utilization decisions.
- PowerFleet is a global leader of subscription-based wireless solutions that secure, control, track, and manage high-value enterprise assets.
- PowerFleet’s patented technologies monitor and analyze assets to improve safety, increase efficiency, reduce costs, and drive profitability.
- PowerFleet’s offerings are sold under the global brands PowerFleet, Pointer, and Cellocator.
- PowerFleet’s global headquarters are in Woodcliff Lake, New Jersey, with additional offices around the globe.
- Kautex has more than 30 plants in 14 countries.
- Kautex designs, develops, and manufactures traditional and hybrid fuel systems, advanced cleaning solutions for assisted and autonomous driving, engine camshafts, and plastic industrial packaging solutions.
- Kautex is a pioneer in designing and manufacturing automotive plastic fuel systems.
- Kautex is expanding its portfolio to include smart fuel systems and thermoplastic composite and composite-metal hybrid battery systems.
- Kautex is developing solutions ranging from lightweight battery housing to hybrid fuel systems to autonomous drive vehicle cleaning systems for the era of new mobility.
