## Facts

- MBUSA is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- MBUSA is responsible for the distribution, marketing, customer service and financial services – including leasing, financing and insurance – for all Mercedes-Benz products in the United States.
- MBUSA offers drivers the most diverse lineup in the luxury segment with 18 model lines ranging from the sporty GLA SUV to the flagship S-Class and the dynamic all-electric vehicles from Mercedes-Benz.
- MBUSA is also responsible for Mercedes-Benz Vans in the United States.
- In 2025, retail sales totaled 343,200 Mercedes-Benz vehicles sold in total.
- In 2025, retail sales totaled 303,200 Mercedes-Benz cars sold.
- In 2025, retail sales totaled 40,000 Mercedes-Benz Vans sold.
