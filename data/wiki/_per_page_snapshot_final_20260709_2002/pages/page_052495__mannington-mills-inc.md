# Mannington Mills, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [GA: Mannington Mills to Invest $42M, Create 200+ Jobs in Gordon County | Trade and Industry Development](https://www.tradeandindustrydev.com/industry/manufacturing/news/ga-mannington-mills-invest-42m-create-200-jobs-14277)  ·  `page_052495`

## Facts

- Mannington Mills will invest $42 million in Calhoun by 2021.
- Mannington Mills will create more than 200 jobs at the new Calhoun site.
- Gov. Nathan Deal announced the investment and job creation for the expansion.
- Mannington Mills purchased a 78-acre site to expand upon its existing presence in Georgia.
- New jobs at the Calhoun site will include positions in production, management, engineering, marketing, supervisory, and administrative roles.
- The new site is located less than one mile from a current Mannington Commercial facility that employs more than 700 people.
- The existing Mannington Commercial facility occupies 800,000 square feet of manufacturing, warehouse, and office space.
- Gov. Nathan Deal stated that Georgia’s economic environment and skilled workforce provide businesses like Mannington Mills with opportunities for growth and innovation.
- Russell Grizzle, President and CEO of Mannington, stated the company is experiencing strong growth in Georgia and the site acquisition will help accommodate that growth.
- Russell Grizzle described the purchase as another installment in Mannington’s ongoing long-term growth investment plan for Calhoun.
- Kathy Johnson, president and CEO of the Gordon County Chamber of Commerce, stated the community was involved in the beginning of the modern flooring industry in the United States.
- Stephanie Scearce, GDEcD Regional Project Manager, represented the Global Commerce Division in collaboration with the Gordon County Chamber of Commerce and North Georgia EMC.
- North Georgia EMC partnered with the Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) for the project.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated the expansion speaks to the extensive resources northwest Georgia provides to the flooring industry.
- Mannington Mills, Inc. is based in Salem, New Jersey.
- The company manufactures residential and commercial sheet vinyl, luxury vinyl, laminate, resilient, and hardwood floors.
- The company also manufactures commercial carpet and rubber under the Mannington Residential, Mannington Commercial, Amtico, and Burke brands.
