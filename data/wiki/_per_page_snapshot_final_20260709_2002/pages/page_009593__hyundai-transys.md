## Facts

- Hyundai TRANSYS plans to invest $240,000,000.00 to build out 620,000 square feet of new space in West Point, Georgia.
- The company plans to occupy the new space at 6801 Kia Pkwy. in West Point on or about December 1, 2022.
- Hyundai TRANSYS is an automobile parts system company that was reborn as a new mission in 2019.
- The company was formed by the integration of Hyundai Dymos and Hyundai Powertech to lead the future mobility industry.
- Hyundai Dymos and Hyundai Powertech previously built a reputation as powertrain and seat specialists in the global auto parts industry.
- Hyundai TRANSYS provides innovative technology that changes the automobile manufacturing market.
- The company is ranked 38th among world auto parts companies by sales, according to American Automotive News magazine in 2018.
- Sangkil Jung serves as the President of Hyundai TRANSYS.
