# Mercedes-Benz AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-05-22

**Source:** [Gov. Kemp: Mercedes-Benz Establishing North American Headquarters, new Research & Development Hub in Metro Atlanta | Governor Brian P. Kemp Office of the Governor](https://gov.georgia.gov/press-releases/2025-05-22/gov-kemp-mercedes-benz-establishing-north-american-headquarters-new)  ·  `page_000092`

## Facts

- Mercedes-Benz will establish Atlanta as its headquarters in North America by centralizing and uniting key corporate functions.
- The company will move up to 500 jobs to the existing Mercedes-Benz facility, known as “1MB,” in Fulton County.
- The company will make a multi-million dollar investment in a future state-of-the-art Research & Development (R&D) facility to also be located nearby.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp stated that Georgia continues to lead the way in the future of mobility and technical innovation, attracting world-class companies like Mercedes-Benz that are driving the automotive industry forward.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp noted that Mercedes-Benz is a job creator with close ties to Georgia that is doubling down on its choice and growing its presence in the state.
- Mercedes-Benz opened its “1MB” facility in 2018 in Sandy Springs.
- The “1MB” facility currently supports approximately 800 jobs in Georgia.
- Jason Hoff, CEO of Mercedes-Benz North America, thanked the State of Georgia for its support in deepening Mercedes-Benz’s roots in the Atlanta area.
- Jason Hoff stated that bringing teams closer together will enable the company to be more agile, increase speed to market, and ensure the best customer experience.
- The “1MB” facility located in Sandy Springs will house existing sales teams, financial services teams, and corporate functions.
- The new state-of-the-art Research & Development hub will be located near Sandy Springs.
- The company anticipates that the move to metro Atlanta will be completed by August 2026.
- Mayor Rusty Paul of the City of Sandy Springs stated that Mercedes-Benz has been an outstanding corporate partner since establishing its headquarters there in 2018.
- Mayor Rusty Paul noted that the expansion highlights the success of the city’s recent infrastructure and capital investments.
- Chairman Robb Pitts of the Fulton County Board of Commissioners stated that Mercedes-Benz reaffirming its commitment proves Fulton County continues to be a destination for corporate solutions.
- Chairman Robb Pitts noted that the county provides major companies an accessible, vibrant, and growing community for their business to thrive in.
- Katie Kirkpatrick, President & CEO of the Metro Atlanta Chamber, stated that when the 1MB facility opened in 2018, it quickly became an integral part of the business landscape, driving economic growth and elevating the region’s global presence.
- Assistant Director of Statewide Projects Elizabeth McLean represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team on this competitive project.
- The GDEcD partnered with the City of Sandy Springs, Select Fulton, Metro Atlanta Chamber, and Georgia Power for this project.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that Mercedes-Benz has become an integral part of the business community since relocating to Sandy Springs in 2018.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson noted that the expansion and commitment to R&D highlights the talent and collaborative partnerships fostered by the University System of Georgia.
- Mercedes-Benz AG is part of the Mercedes-Benz Group AG with a total of around 175,000 employees worldwide.
- Ola Källenius is Chairman of the Board of Management of Mercedes-Benz AG.
- The company focuses on the development, production, and sales of passenger cars, vans, and vehicle-related services.
- The company aspires to be the leader in the fields of electric mobility and vehicle software.
- The product portfolio comprises the Mercedes-Benz brand with Mercedes AMG, Mercedes Maybach, and G Class with their all-electric models as well as products of the smart brand.
- Mercedes-Benz AG is one of the world’s largest manufacturers of high-end passenger cars.
