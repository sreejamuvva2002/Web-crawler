# Erdrich Umformtechnik GmbH & Co. KG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2012-01-31

**Source:** [Erdrich Umformtechnik to open plant in Georgia](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/shopmanagement/erdrich-umformtechnik-to-open-plant-in-georgia)  ·  `page_019971`

## Facts

- Erdrich Umformtechnik GmbH & Co. KG, a German automotive supplier, will construct a $39 million metal stamping facility in Dublin, Ga.
- The new facility will create 178 jobs.
- Erdrich is a midsized, family-owned company that produces complex metal parts and subassemblies for the automotive industry.
- The company operates two plants in Germany, one in the Czech Republic, and another in China.
- Erdrich supplies parts to other automotive supplier companies, as well as to BMW, Mercedes, and Volkswagen.
- The new plant will span 130,000 square feet.
- This facility will be Erdrich's first U.S. location.
- The company expects the Georgia facility to mirror the size and importance of its German operations in supplying its North American customers.
- Georgia's international office in Munich developed the relationship with Erdrich to initiate this business opportunity for the state.
- The plant opening was announced on January 31, 2012.
