# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Superior Essex Inc. Names Daniel Choi As Its Chief Executive Officer - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.de/news/superior-essex-inc-names-daniel-choi-as-its-ceo/)  ·  `page_010552`

## Facts

- Superior Essex Inc. announced the appointment of Daniel Choi as CEO effective March 1, 2022.
- Choi will lead Superior Essex Inc. and all its subsidiaries, including Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, Essex Brownell, Essex Energy Italy, IVA, and Hi-Wire.
- Choi previously worked at LS Cable and System, the parent company of Superior Essex Inc., since 1999.
- Prior to his appointment, Choi served as Regional President for North and South America at LS Cable & System.
- Choi's previous roles include President of LS Cable & System USA for MV and LV Power Cable Systems, President of Cyprus Investments from 2008 to 2014, and SVP of PMO for Europe and China in 2015.
- Choi holds a PhD in Materials Science and Engineering from Seoul National University and an MBA from Helsinki Business School.
- Choi resides in Atlanta, Georgia, and serves as Chairman of the Korean Chamber of Commerce and Industry in Georgia.
- Choi stated he is very grateful to take on the CEO position of this traditional and respected company and aims to build on past achievements while ensuring high quality and customer service.
- The leadership change follows the resignation of Brian Kim, who served as CEO for nearly seven years.
- During his tenure, Kim established core values, broke down business silos, expanded infrastructure for hybrid and PoE cables, increased production capacity, founded MagForceX Innovation Centers to focus on innovation and R&D, launched the strategic joint venture Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, and oriented the company toward a sustainable future.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, and Essex Brownell.
- The company employs over 3,000 people across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is the world's leading provider of magnet wires and a leader in communication fiber applications and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company contributes significantly to technological advances in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex aims to pioneer smart building technology while advancing sustainability initiatives.
- The company's headquarters is located at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327, USA.
