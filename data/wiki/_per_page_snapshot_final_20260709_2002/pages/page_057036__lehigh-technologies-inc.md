# Lehigh Technologies, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-28

## Facts

- Lehigh Technologies is headquartered at 120 Royal Woods Court SW, Tucker, Georgia, 30084, United States.
- The company was founded in 2003 and has reached the acquired stage.
- Lehigh Technologies raised a total of $99.5M in funding.
- Investors include Michelin Group, Horizon Technology Finance, Kleiner Perkins, Index Ventures, Florida Gulfshore Capital, and five additional investors.
- The company manufactures green materials by converting end-of-life tire materials and post-industrial rubber into sustainable powders for industrial and consumer applications.
- Lehigh Technologies has filed 26 patents, including a patent granted on March 15, 2022, related to polymers, thermoplastics, elastomers, rubber, and copolymers.
- The company is listed as a key player in the tire recycling market alongside Liberty Tire Recycling, GENAN HOLDING A/S, ResourceCo, GRP LTD, Entech Inc., Emanuel Tire LLC, BDS Tire Recycling, Contec, and CRM.
- Lehigh Technologies' recycled materials support circular economy practices by enabling the reuse of end-of-life tires instead of disposal.
