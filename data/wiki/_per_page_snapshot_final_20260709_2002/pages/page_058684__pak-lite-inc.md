## Facts

- Pak-Lite, Inc. was founded in Atlanta, Georgia by Frank Pritchard in 1972.
- The company is a leading North American converter and manufacturer of US-sourced foams, rubber, nonwovens, foils, films, and specialty materials.
- Pak-Lite's customer base includes many of the top OEM and industrial companies in the world.
- The company operates manufacturing facilities in Georgia, North Carolina, and Taiwan.
- Pak-Lite employs over 300 dedicated professionals committed to project success during design and manufacturing.
- The company assists customers in selecting sustainable materials for specific applications.
- Manufacturing capabilities include extrusion lamination to foams and rubber substrates.
- Pak-Lite combines materials into composite forms and supplies die-cut parts, components, roll stock, or sheeting.
- Finished parts often include pressure-sensitive adhesives and release liners.
- Additional capabilities include material selection, prototyping, skiving/slitting, rotary/flatbed die cutting, kiss cutting, spooling, precision parts manufacturing, and assembly.
- Pak-Lite holds ISO 9001:2015 and IATF 16949 certifications.
- The company converts foams, rubbers, and nonwoven fabrics for OEMs and Tier 1 automotive industry customers.
- Pak-Lite assists in designing exact components to meet demanding specifications.
- The company has extensive experience with 3M Adhesives and Avery Adhesives.
- Pak-Lite sources top foam, rubber, polymer, packaging, nonwoven, fiber, and film suppliers worldwide.
