## Facts

- Novelis Inc is a global manufacturer of semi-finished aluminium products.
- Novelis Inc is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, USA.
- Novelis operates 24 manufacturing facilities across North America, South America, Europe and Asia.
- Novelis is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited, an Indian-based supplier of aluminium and copper.
- Aleris Corporation is a global manufacturer of semi-finished aluminium products.
- Aleris Corporation is headquartered in Cleveland, Ohio, USA.
- Aleris operates 13 production facilities.
- The proposed acquisition of Aleris Corporation by Novelis Inc involved overlaps in markets for the supply of various semi-finished aluminium products.
- On 1 October 2019, the European Commission cleared the transaction subject to commitments.
- To address the Commission’s concerns, the parties offered to divest Aleris’ aluminium automotive body sheets business in Europe.
