## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America plans to invest $1,723,032.00 to build out 42,734 square feet of new space in Atlanta, Georgia.
- The company plans to occupy the new space at 1 Porsche Dr in Atlanta on or about October 1, 2023.
- According to state and local development sources, the investment covers the build-out of the new corporate facility.
- Porsche Cars North America states it thrives on a foundation of precision engineering fueled by the American entrepreneurial spirit.
- The company carries on the vision of founder Ferry Porsche by delivering impassioned experiences tied to the cars it builds.
- Porsche aims to fuel 'Porsche Passion' as a brand built on being better together as a diverse and gifted workforce united in the desire to be the world's most aspirational brand and most empowering employer.
- Each team member at Porsche Cars North America brings a unique perspective and master craft to the workforce.
- The company boasts engineers, brand builders, marketing minds, business development leaders, analytical aficionados, and corporate professionals in the automotive business.
- Porsche's goal is to lead its industry in performance and customer satisfaction.
- Kjell Gruner serves as the Chief Executive Officer of Porsche Cars North America.
