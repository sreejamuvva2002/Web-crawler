# Daechang Seat Corp. USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-24

**Source:** [Daechang Seat Corp. USA to create 500 jobs in Georgia](https://www.thefabricator.com/thewelder/news/arcwelding/daechang-seat-corp-usa-to-create-500-jobs-in-georgia)  ·  `page_018886`

## Facts

- Daechang Seat Corp. USA is a supplier of automotive seat frames.
- The company will invest $72.5 million in Georgia’s Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- The facility is located near Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA).
- The project will create more than 500 jobs.
- Daechang Seat Corp. USA is a Korea-based company that employs more than 5,000 people in seven countries.
- Jinsuk Lee, CEO of Daechang Seat Savannah Corp., stated the primary objective is to drive the growth of DSC through facility expansions, ultimately transforming DSC into a global entity.
- DSC currently holds a prominent partnership with Hyundai Transys and Hyundai Mobis at HMGMA.
- DSC expressed hope to collaborate with even more projects in the future.
