# Vista Metals Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-06-22

**Source:** [Vista Metals Georgia Breaks Ground on $30 Million Casthouse Expansion Project - Light Metal Age Magazine](https://www.lightmetalage.com/news/industry-news/casthouse/vista-metals-georgia-breaks-ground-on-30-million-casthouse-expansion-project/)  ·  `page_013574`

## Facts

- Vista Metals Georgia broke ground on a new $30 million casthouse expansion project at its Adairsville manufacturing facility in Georgia.
- The expansion project is designed to support the company's aerospace extrusion, forging, and rolling markets.
- Andrew Primack, president of Vista Metals Georgia, stated that the investment demonstrates the company's commitment to its customers and market space.
- The current expansion is combined with a long-range plan that calls for the eventual installation of casthouses 8 and 9 to ensure sufficient casting and finishing capacity for a recovering market.
- This project represents the second phase of Vista Metals Georgia’s nearly $100 million long-range capital plan.
- The long-range capital plan previously included the completion and launch of the company's 6th casthouse in the spring of 2021.
- The current project involves the installation of the company's 7th aluminum casthouse, melting furnaces, homogenizing ovens, and support equipment.
- Alongside the new casthouse, the project includes the construction of a new world-class finishing facility to support downstream processing and value-added products for the aerospace industry.
- Vista Metals Georgia operates under a licensing agreement with Vista Metals Corp.
- Along with Vista Progressive Metals, which produces aluminum lithium products, the three companies are market leaders in manufacturing specialty aluminum products.
- The specialty aluminum products support applications in aerospace, commercial aircraft, defense, automotive, and general industrial sectors.
- The expansion project is expected to be fully operational sometime in 2024.
