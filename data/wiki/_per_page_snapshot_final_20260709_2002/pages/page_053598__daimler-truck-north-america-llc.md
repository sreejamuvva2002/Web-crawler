# Daimler Truck North America LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-15

**Source:** [Women In Trucking Association Announces Continued Gold Partnership with Daimler Truck North America](https://www.womenintrucking.org/press-releases/women-in-trucking-association-announces-continued-gold-partnership-with-daimler-truck-north-america)  ·  `page_053598`

## Facts

- Daimler Truck North America renewed its Gold Level Partnership with the Women In Trucking Association on April 15, 2024.
- Daimler Truck North America has been a partner of the Women In Trucking Association since 2012.
- The partnership supports the mission to encourage the employment of women in the trucking industry, promote their accomplishments, and minimize obstacles they face.
- Daimler Truck North America sponsors the annual Influential Woman in Trucking Award recognition program that honors talented, resourceful, and high-performing female leaders in the industry.
- Kary Schaefer, Chief Engineer of Product Engineering at Daimler Truck North America, serves as chair on the Women In Trucking Association board of directors.
- Kary Schaefer stated that Daimler Truck North America recognizes the value of innovation and creativity that comes from a strong culture of diverse backgrounds and perspectives due to rapid transformation in the truck industry.
- Kary Schaefer noted the partnership allows Daimler Truck North America and its employees an opportunity to shape the future of the industry and workforce through the mission of the Women In Trucking Association.
- Jennifer Hedrick, president and CEO of the Women In Trucking Association, stated appreciation for Daimler Truck North America's ongoing support and commitment to increasing gender diversity in transportation.
- Jennifer Hedrick highlighted Kary Schaefer's leadership as chair of the Women In Trucking Association for over the past year.
- The Women In Trucking Association was founded in 2007 and serves as a resource for more than 8,000 corporate and individual members located in the United States, Canada, Mexico, Japan, Australia, Sweden, South Africa, and New Zealand.
- The Women In Trucking Association released the 2023 WIT Index, an official barometer to benchmark and measure the percentage of women who make up critical roles in transportation each year, with more than 300 company participants.
- The Women In Trucking Association awarded a deserving member a 2018 Volvo VNL670 in collaboration with Arrow Truck Sales, Inc.
- The Women In Trucking Association honored over 300 leaders in transportation through several programs and hosted more than 1,900 registered attendees at the 2023 Accelerate! Conference and Exhibition.
- Gold Level Partners supporting the Women In Trucking Association include Arrow Truck Sales, Bridgestone Americas, Daimler Truck North America, FedEx Freight, Great Dane, J.B. Hunt Transport, Michelin North America, Navistar International, Inc., PACCAR, Penske Transportation Solutions, Ryder System, Walmart, and WM.
- Daimler Truck North America LLC is headquartered in Portland, Oregon.
- Daimler Truck North America designs, engineers, manufactures, and markets medium- and heavy-duty trucks, school buses, vehicle chassis, and their associated technologies and components under the Freightliner, Western Star, Thomas Built Buses, Freightliner Custom Chassis Corp, and Detroit brands.
- Daimler Truck North America is a subsidiary of Daimler Truck Holding AG (DTG), one of the world's leading commercial vehicle manufacturers.
