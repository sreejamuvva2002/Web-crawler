## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Company plans to manufacture their line of electric vehicles on nearly 3,000 acres at the Bryan County megasite.
- The Bryan County megasite is located off Highway 80 in Bryan County, Georgia.
- Local resident Jennifer Fichthorn's home is just minutes away from the site.
- Fichthorn moved to the area more than two decades ago when there was little-to-no traffic in her neighborhood.
- Fichthorn worries the plant will add more speedsters and traffic to an area that was previously relatively quiet.
