## Facts

- Saft America, Inc. operates a manufacturing production facility located at 711 Gil Harbin Industrial Blvd., Valdosta, Georgia 31601, United States.
- The company produces nickel-cadmium (Ni-Cd) and lithium-ion (Li-ion) aircraft battery solutions.
- Saft's batteries provide high-peak-power for engine or auxiliary power unit (APU) starting and emergency backup power.
- The company's batteries outperform lead-acid batteries in both power and reliability.
- Saft's batteries offer a long and predictable service life with no risk of sudden death failure.
- The company maintains in-house development and production capabilities.
- Saft operates manufacturing production facilities in France and the United States.
- The company provides tailor-made solutions for its battery products.
- Even with maintenance costs included, Saft provides a lower total cost of ownership (TCO) compared to other aviation battery providers.
- At Saft, over 400 different battery designs are produced to suit virtually every requirement.
- Each battery design is optimized for energy and power.
- The batteries guarantee integration with the electrical architecture of all fixed-wing and helicopter aircraft.
