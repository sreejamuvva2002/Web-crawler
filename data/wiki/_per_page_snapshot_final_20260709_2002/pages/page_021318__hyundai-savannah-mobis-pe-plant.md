# Hyundai Savannah Mobis PE Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hyundai Savannah Mobis PE Plant - New South Construction](https://newsouthconstruction.com/project/hyundai-mobis-plant/)  ·  `page_021318`

## Facts

- Located in Savannah, Georgia.
- Client is Hyundai Engineering North America.
- Total facility size is 500,000 square feet.
- Construction involves two manufacturing facilities on an enabling site for the $6 billion Hyundai mega site in Savannah, Georgia.
- The facilities manufacture critical components to be incorporated in the new generation of EV cars being manufactured at the mega site.
- The facilities consist of a 400,000-square-foot Motor Plant and a 100,000-square-foot Control Unit Plant.
- The project is fast-tracked with nearly 90% of the work completed in 2023.
- Won First Place at the 2025 AGC Build Georgia Awards.
- Received an Award of Merit from 2025 ENR Southeast.
