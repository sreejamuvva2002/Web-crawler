# Club Car

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-06-05

**Source:** [Club Car joins GAM - Georgia Association of Manufacturers](https://www.gamfg.org/2024/06/05/club-car-joins-gam/)  ·  `page_018439`

## Facts

- Club Car has joined the Georgia Association of Manufacturers (GAM) as its newest community member.
- Club Car has a corporate history spanning over 60 years.
- Originally renowned for golf cars, Club Car has expanded its offerings to include commercial utility vehicles and personal-use transportation solutions.
- Every aspect of design, fabrication, and assembly at Club Car is driven by an unwavering commitment to superior performance and manufacturing excellence.
- Club Car has been recognized as one of EHS Today's America's Safest Companies.
- Club Car is headquartered in Augusta, Georgia.
- James Stevenson serves as Vice President of Operations at Club Car.
