## Facts

- On October 24, 2023, Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Daechang Seat Corp. USA will invest $72.5 million in the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- Daechang Seat Corp. USA specializes in manufacturing automotive seat frames.
- The facility is located in close proximity to Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA).
- The project will create over 500 jobs.
- This investment adds to seven already announced tier-one suppliers locating around the new Hyundai plant in Bryan County off Rt. 16.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that the announcement highlights the broader economic impact of projects like the Hyundai metaplant beyond their initial investment.
- Suppliers for the Hyundai metaplant project currently reach at least nine counties.
- The company will join a growing manufacturing base centered around Chatham County and the Savannah region.
- Daechang Seat Corp. (DSC) was established in 1979 in Kyungju, South Korea.
- DSC has become one of the largest seat frame producers globally.
- Globally, DSC employs more than 5,000 people across seven countries.
- The company's production process utilizes fully automatic lines equipped with arc welding and spot-welding machines.
- DSC expressed appreciation to the State of Georgia, local communities, and the City of Savannah for their support.
- DSC takes pride in creating improved employment opportunities within Chatham County and the Savannah area.
- DSC maintains a steadfast partnership with the City of Savannah.
