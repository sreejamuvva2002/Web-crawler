# PPG Industries, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [PPG Unveils MIX N SHAKE to Streamline Automotive Refinish](https://finviz.com/news/197483/ppg-unveils-mix-n-shake-to-streamline-automotive-refinish)  ·  `page_058896`

## Facts

- PPG recently unveiled its latest innovation for automotive refinish operations, the PPG MIX‘N’SHAKE automated stirring technology.
- The MIX‘N’SHAKE system automates one of the final manual steps in the paint preparation process, stirring and delivering consistent, ready-to-spray paint mix in under 90 seconds.
- PPG is integrating this technology with PPG’s LINQ digital refinish ecosystem to digitalize and automate every stage of the refinishing process.
- The system forms part of a broader push to help repair centers operate faster, in a cleaner way and more profitably.
- The technology utilizes a patent-pending notched mixing cup that connects directly to the spray gun, streamlining workflow while minimizing waste and contamination.
- MIX‘N’SHAKE replaces the repetitive, manual stirring process with an automated closed system that ensures color consistency, accuracy and superior finish quality.
- Its pre-programmed settings are optimized for primers, basecoats and clearcoats, making it compatible with both waterborne and solvent-borne paints.
- This flexibility allows body shops to use the system across a wide range of applications without needing multiple setups.
- The closed design prevents splashes, spills and cross-contamination, maintaining a safer and more organized workspace.
- Users simply insert the cup, select the paint type, and the machine delivers a homogenous mix that’s immediately ready for application.
- According to PPG’s internal data, shops can increase productive labor hours by up to 6% by automating paint stirring.
- The system can reduce annual manual labor time by as much as 16 working days per year, based on a facility handling around six repair jobs daily.
- The technology is expected to reduce consumable waste by up to 10%, cutting costs on items like stirring sticks, strainers and disposable cups.
- Improved color uniformity and consistent paint quality reduce rework, saving further time and material.
- PPG maintained its full-year 2025 adjusted earnings per share guidance of $7.75 to $8.05.
- This outlook factors in continued share gains and internal improvement initiatives, while accounting for current global economic conditions, currency exchange rates and varied demand trends across its regions and business segments.
