# Stryten Energy LLC

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC has been honored with the 2025 Women’s Choice Award® for Best Companies to Work – Inclusion.
- The recognition highlights organizations committed to fostering inclusive and supportive workplaces for women.
- Wendy Henderson, Senior Vice President and Chief Human Resource Officer, stated that receiving the recognition reflects the programs and benefits offered to all employees, with the objective of providing development opportunities, wellness, and a means to financially prepare for the future.
- Stryten Energy provides job satisfaction, stability, high-earning potential, flexibility, and advancement opportunities for women in a robust and growing industry.
- The company provides training and skills development, along with a team of experienced coworkers, to help employees be successful.
- Stryten Energy is very active in the Women in Auto Care Association, an organization dedicated to connecting, empowering, and mentoring women within the auto care industry.
- Women at Stryten Energy participate in the Women in Auto Care Association by attending conferences, leading discussion groups, and taking on other supporting roles.
- Women at Stryten Energy are active in the Women in the Global Battery Industry (WGBI), an organization formed by Battery Council International (BCI) to promote and develop the growth of women in the battery industry.
- WGBI programs and activities are intended to enhance members’ skills and experience, address challenges confronting the industry, and promote members’ career growth.
- Stryten Energy provides a broad range of energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- The company is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Stryten Energy partners with some of the world’s most recognized companies to meet the growing demand for reliable and sustainable energy storage capacity.
- Stryten powers applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- The company's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- Stryten Energy is an industry leader backed by more than a century of expertise in delivering top-performing energy solutions for today and tomorrow.
- The Women’s Choice Award identifies brands, products, and services that are most recommended and trusted by women.
- The award recognizes the best companies to work for based on a survey of women indicating what is most important when searching for a job.
- WomenCertified Inc., home of the Women’s Choice Award, created the first national award based on the ratings and preferences of women as the leading advocate for female consumers.
