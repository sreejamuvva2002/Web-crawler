# Inalfa Gabriel Sunroof Systems

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Gabriel India: Inalfa Roof Systems, Gabriel India sign a technical collaboration - The Economic Times](https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/industry/auto/auto-components/inalfa-roof-systems-gabriel-india-sign-a-technical-collaboration/articleshow/100102809.cms)  ·  `page_045215`

## Facts

- Gabriel and Inalfa plan to invest Rs 170 crore to set up a new manufacturing facility.
- The new facility will be named Inalfa Gabriel Sunroof Systems (IGSS).
- The facility is located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu.
- The facility is expected to become operational in the first quarter of the next calendar year.
- The facility will have a production capacity of 200,000 sunroofs per annum.
- Gabriel India is the listed arm of the $2 billion ANAND Group.
- IGSS will be converted into a joint-venture between Inalfa Roof Systems Group and Gabriel India.
- Inalfa will hold a 51% stake in the joint venture after receiving regulatory approvals later this year.
- ANAND Group Executive Chairperson Anjali Singh stated that demand for sunroofs in the Indian market is growing at a fast clip.
- The new venture will initially supply sunroofs to Hyundai-Kia.
- The venture is expected to generate revenues of Rs 1000 crore by 2030.
- The company plans to subsequently supply sunroofs to other OEMs including Maruti Suzuki, Tata Motors, and Mahindra & Mahindra (M&M).
- Industry estimates indicate automotive sunroof sales in India could cross 700,000 units this calendar year.
- Automotive sunroof sales in India were 500,000 units in 2022.
- Globally, the automotive sunroof market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.3% to reach $13.6 billion by 2030.
- IGSS will generate employment for 250 people at the Chennai facility.
- Inalfa Roof Systems Group has an annual turnover of about $1.5 billion.
- Inalfa is a global market leader in automotive sunroof systems and is present in 15 locations across the world.
