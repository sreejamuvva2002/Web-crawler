## Facts

- ACM is housed in a 65,000 square foot facility located near Augusta, Georgia.
- The facility address is 1048 Superior Way, Thomson, Georgia 30824-8162.
- It is centrally located in the Southeast near Interstate 20 and designed to accommodate easy access for hassle free shipping and receiving.
- ACM's well-trained staff has been providing quality coating services and kit assembly packaging for over 30 years.
- The company specializes in contract coating runs with the ability to accommodate a wide variety of sizes from small pieces to large parts.
- ACM is an ISO certified full service contract coater of steel, stainless steel and aluminum parts.
- It specializes in high corrosion resistant, quality finishes for the transportation, automotive, agricultural, and recreational and maintenance vehicle markets.
- The plant operates a nine stage zinc phosphate pretreatment system.
- The plant operates a 14,000 gallon cathodic epoxy E-coat tank.
- The plant operates a three booth roll-on roll-off state of the art powder coating system which is housed in an environmentally controlled room.
- ACM custom coating services offers corrosion resistant finishing systems utilizing zinc phosphate, cathodic epoxy electrodeposition(e-coat) primer and powder coating.
- ACM also provides custom welding, fabrication, packaging and kitting services within the 65,000 square foot facility located in Thomson, Georgia.
- US Supply started operations in Raleigh, NC in 1970 under the Blue Dot trade name primarily providing parts and services to the aftermarket Golf and Utility Vehicle industry.
- In 1997 the company saw a need to provide the same type of focus to Original Equipment Manufactures, which included a large investment in a modern e-coat/powder coat system.
