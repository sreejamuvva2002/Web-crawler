## Facts

- Seohan Group was founded in 1974 with the goal of creating technology domestically in Korea.
- Seohan Auto Georgia Corporation is a Tier-1 automotive parts supplier for Hyundai Motor Group America.
- The company produces and supplies high-quality auto parts, forged parts, flanges, and industrial machines based on an automated production system and strict quality assurance system.
- The Georgia plant is located in Midway, near Savannah and Hinesville GA.
- Seohan Auto Georgia Corporation strives to create and maintain a safe and pleasant workplace for its employees.
