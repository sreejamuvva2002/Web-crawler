# Trenton Pressing

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-04-06

**Source:** [Trenton Pressing breaks ground on $10 million facility - WDEF](https://www.wdef.com/trenton-pressing-breaks-ground-on-10-million-facility/)  ·  `page_012412`

## Facts

- Trenton Pressing broke ground Thursday on a $10 million facility to expand its manufacturing.
- The expansion will add 20 new jobs upon completion.
- The facility will be 105,000 square-feet.
- Construction should be completed by December of this year (2023).
- The expansion will help the company expand its assembly services and increase manufacturing capacity.
- The expansion will allow the company to better service its growth and meet manufacturing needs.
- Clients include BMW, Club Car and ZF, among others.
- Trenton Pressing bought out a struggling manufacturing company a little over two years ago and turned it around.
- The turnaround was possible through reorganization of management, technology and equipment upgrades, and personnel training.
- Trenton Pressing provides metal stamping, welding and assembly processes.
- Trenton Pressing also provides warehousing and shipping.
- The facility is located on North Industrial Boulevard in Dade County.
- The company currently has 220 employees and will be adding 20 more with this facility.
- Dade County Executive Ted Rumley stated that Trenton Pressing has been a great community partner since it began operations in the county in 2021.
- Rumley noted that the company has provided great jobs and supported local schools and the environment.
