# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-12

**Source:** [Archer Aviation Completes Georgia Air Taxi Factory - Augusta CEO](https://augustaceo.com/news/2024/12/archer-aviation-completes-georgia-air-taxi-factory/)  ·  `page_017086`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. announced that earlier this week it received the certificate of occupancy for its high-volume manufacturing facility, ARC.
- ARC is connected to the Covington Municipal Airport in Georgia.
- Archer is now working to complete the tooling load-in for the initial manufacturing line at this facility.
- Production is planned to begin in early 2025.
- The goal is to ramp to a rate of two aircraft per month by the end of the year.
- Archer built ARC in close partnership with Stellantis.
- Stellantis is contributing capital, advanced manufacturing technology and expertise and experienced personnel with the goal of scaling this facility to 650 aircraft annually by 2030.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp commented that Archer’s choice to build the new manufacturing facility in Newton County marks the start of an incredible relationship and reaffirms Georgia’s commitment to creating quality jobs and opportunities for years to come.
- Congressman Mike Collins of Georgia, who sits on the House Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure and Committee on Science, Space, and Technology, stated that the completion of the facility highlights Georgia’s leadership in innovation, advanced manufacturing and job creation, and will drive economic benefits for the state.
- Adam Goldstein, Archer’s founder and CEO, noted that construction on ARC is complete, production is planned for early next year, and the team got it done fast and on budget.
- Stellantis Chief Manufacturing and Supply Chain Officer Arnaud Deboeuf commented that supporting Archer’s electric aircraft growth with deep manufacturing expertise is a great opportunity for both companies.
- ARC is where Archer plans to produce its all-electric vertical take-off and landing aircraft, Midnight.
- The Midnight aircraft is designed to offer passengers a sustainable, low-noise and safe alternative to ground transportation in congested cities around the world.
