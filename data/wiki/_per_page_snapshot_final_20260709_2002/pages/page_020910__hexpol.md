## Facts

- HEXPOL develops, manufactures, and sells polymer compounds and engineered products.
- HEXPOL operates in two business areas: HEXPOL Compounding and HEXPOL Engineered Products.
- The HEXPOL Compounding business area offers rubber, thermoplastic elastomer, and thermoplastic compounds for automotive and engineering, construction, consumer and medical technology, cable and water treatment, energy, oil and gas, and general industries.
- The HEXPOL Engineered Products business area provides gaskets for plate heat exchangers, seals, as well as polyurethane, plastic, and rubber wheels for forklifts and electric trucks.
