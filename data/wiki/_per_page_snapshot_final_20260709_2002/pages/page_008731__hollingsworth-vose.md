## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose is the oldest company in Tugboat Institute membership.
- The company was founded nearly 300 years ago.
- In its early days, they made paper for customers including Ben Franklin and Paul Revere.
- Today, they are a global leader in filtration and energy storage systems.
- Their customers include Tesla and Elon Musk.
- Val Hollingsworth is the 7th generation leader and current Board Chair of Hollingsworth & Vose.
- Val Hollingsworth shares highlights from his family’s company’s incredible journey and gives insight into the spirit of innovation that has driven the company since its founding in a Tugboat Institute talk.
