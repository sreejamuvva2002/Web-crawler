# GSC Steel Stamping, LLC

## Facts

- GSC Steel Stamping, LLC is a minority and family-owned and operated automotive parts manufacturer.
- The company provides Body in White frame assembly brackets to some of the world’s leading OEMs and tier one suppliers.
- GSC Steel Stamping, LLC offers competitively priced metal stamping, engineering, welded assemblies, and tooling services.
- In June 2023, GSC acquired Dixien, LLC’s assets.
- The acquisition continues a 43-year history of automotive parts stamping at the company's facility in Lake City, GA.
- The facility spans 200,000 sq. ft.
- CEO Vincent Henderson previously purchased and led the turnaround of two award-winning automotive stamping operations in Michigan.
- Those Michigan operations were regularly recognized by customers for excellence in overall performance, quality, and on-time delivery.
- The company continuously re-evaluates ways to improve its processes, invests in advanced technology, and hires top-notch staff.
- GSC has a very experienced and dedicated staff.
- The plant operations management team alone possesses over 200 years of combined stamping experience.
- The management team takes pride in consistently delivering high-quality, precision parts on time.
- GSC Steel Stamping, LLC is classified as a private company.
- The company operates across multiple industries including Aerospace & Defense, Automotive & Transport, Automotive Manufacturing, Automotive Metal Stamping, Fabricated Metal Product Manufacturing, Forging & Stamping, and Transportation Equipment Manufacturing.
