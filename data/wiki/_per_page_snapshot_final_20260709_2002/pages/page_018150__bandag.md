## Facts

- The new facility is supported by an initial investment of USD 2 million.
- The plant aims to produce up to 5,000 retreaded tyres annually.
- Capacity is expected to double within five years.
- The facility was developed in partnership with Tegeta Motors and supported by Bridgestone MEA.
- It will be the first Bandag retreading factory in the Caucasus.
- Construction is scheduled to begin this year, with completion targeted for late 2026.
- The project is poised to become a key driver of environmentally responsible mobility practices.
