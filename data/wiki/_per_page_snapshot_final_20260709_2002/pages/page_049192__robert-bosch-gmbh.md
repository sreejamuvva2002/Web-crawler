# Robert Bosch GmbH

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-12-31

**Source:** [Bosch aims to accelerate regional and sectoral growth - Bosch Media Service US](https://us.bosch-press.com/pressportal/us/en/press-release-20416.html)  ·  `page_049192`

## Facts

- In 2022, Bosch exceeded its business targets in a challenging year, increasing total sales to $93 billion from $83 billion the previous year (a 12.0 percent increase, or 9.4 percent exchange-rate-adjusted).
- EBIT from operations reached $4 billion in 2022, up from $3.4 billion previously, with the EBIT margin rising from 4.0 to 4.3 percent.
- Dr. Stefan Hartung, chairman of the board of management of Robert Bosch GmbH, stated that both sales and margin were higher than expected despite a demanding economic and social environment.
- The company aims for annual sales growth of 6 to 8 percent on average in coming years, with margins reaching at least 7 percent.
- Hartung stated the strategic aim is to grow in every global region and be among the leading three suppliers in relevant markets.
- Bosch identifies electrification, automation, and digitalization as major trends driving technological transformation and opening growth opportunities in existing and new business areas.
- The company's 'Invented for life' ethos emphasizes leveraging software and artificial intelligence to seize these opportunities.
- Bosch has maintained a regional presence in North America since 1906.
- In 2021, the company generated consolidated sales of $13.5 billion across the U.S., Canada, and Mexico.
- As of December 31, 2021, Bosch employed nearly 35,500 associates in North America across more than 100 locations.
- The company's operations are divided into four business sectors: Mobility Solutions, Industrial Technology, Consumer Goods, and Energy and Building Technology.
- Bosch is pursuing a vision of sustainable, safe, and exciting mobility by utilizing expertise in sensor technology, software, services, and its IoT cloud to provide connected, cross-domain solutions.
- The strategic objective is to facilitate connected living with products and solutions that either contain artificial intelligence or were developed/manufactured with its help.
- The company has been carbon neutral since the first quarter of 2020 across its more than 400 locations worldwide.
- Bosch employs approximately 85,500 associates in research and development across 136 global locations, including nearly 44,000 software engineers.
