# Maxxis International USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Georgia-based Maxxis International USA is planning a 350,000 square foot distribution facility in Whitestown, Indiana.
- The company has not announced financial details for the new facility.
- The facility is expected to house 55 employees.
- The new facility is planned for Park 130 and is scheduled to open in August.
- Maxxis International – USA President James Tzen stated that Indiana was an appealing choice due to its excellent transportation infrastructure, friendly business environment, and proximity to major automakers.
- Company leadership noted that Whitestown is a rapidly growing area with easy access to both major interstates and rail.
- The company has operated in the US since 1985.
- Maxxis International USA offers automotive, light truck, bicycle and other types of tires as original equipment and aftermarket products.
- The company employs over 30,000 people worldwide.
- Maxxis International USA maintains other distribution centers in Rancho Cucamonga, California, and Grapevine, Texas.
- The company's corporate headquarters is located in Suwanee, Georgia.
