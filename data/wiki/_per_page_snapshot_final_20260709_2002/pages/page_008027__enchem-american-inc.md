# Enchem American, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-27

**Source:** [Enchem to open second U.S. facility in Brownsville | Tennessee Town & City - Tennessee Municipal League](https://ttc.tml1.org/2023/06/27/enchem-open-second-us-facility-brownsville)  ·  `page_008027`

## Facts

- Enchem American, Inc. will invest $152.5 million to establish electrolyte manufacturing operations in Brownsville.
- The investment will create 190 new jobs.
- The facility is located at the I-40 Advantage Industrial Park.
- The site is a Select Tennessee Certified Site.
- This plant will be Enchem’s second U.S. facility.
- Enchem is a South Korean-based company.
- The Brownsville facility will operate similarly to Enchem’s U.S. headquarters in Commerce, Georgia.
- The facility will manufacture electrolytes for electric vehicle (EV) batteries.
- The facility will provide other EV battery-related services.
- Enchem America Inc. is a subsidiary of Enchem Co., Ltd.
- Enchem specializes in the development and manufacturing of electrolytes and other additives for rechargeable batteries.
- The new Tennessee operations will allow Enchem to further support its EV partners across the Southeast.
