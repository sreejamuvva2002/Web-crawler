# Enchem America Inc.

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-01

**Source:** [$150M factory project canceled in Brownsville](https://www.actionnews5.com/2025/10/01/brownsville-plant-not-happening/)  ·  `page_007952`

## Facts

- Nearly 200 jobs will not be coming to Brownsville.
- Enchem America Inc. notified city officials that it was pulling out of a $150 million project to build a manufacturing facility near Ford’s Blue Oval City.
- The factory was planned for Interstate 40 Advantage Industrial Park in Brownsville, Tennessee.
- The canceled facility would have manufactured electrolytes, a key part in electric vehicle batteries.
- City officials are already working to find any company interested in building on the land.
