# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2022-06-10

**Source:** [Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation Breaks Ground on Innovative SMART Warehouse in Newnan Georgia | Yamaha](https://yamaha-motor-georgia.com/node/21)  ·  `page_015667`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation (YMMC), based in Newnan Georgia, broke ground on a new SMART Warehouse in Newnan, Georgia.
- The SMART Warehouse is the first of its kind for Yamaha Motor Company worldwide.
- The facility utilizes state-of-the-art automated storage and retrieval systems.
- The new warehouse will be 200,000 square feet.
- The facility will reduce response time for fulfilling dealer orders for Yamaha WaveRunners, Golf Cars, ATVs, and Side-by-Sides by more than 50 percent.
- Mike Chrzanowski, President and CEO of Yamaha Motor Corporation, stated the warehouse will improve operational efficiency and material flow velocity to factory assembly lines.
- Chrzanowski noted that demand for all outdoor recreational products produced at this factory continues to exceed supply.
- SMART is an acronym for Sequenced Material and Reduced Transportation.
- The medium parts automated storage and retrieval system will retrieve and put away 240 containers per hour, compared to the current rate of 170 containers per hour.
- The small part automated system will handle 2400 containers per hour, compared to the current capability of 550 containers per day.
- The new systems will enable Yamaha to reduce forklifts by 50 percent using automatic conveyors and tuggers.
- The significant investment underscores the company's long-term commitment to YMCC employees as well as the City of Newnan and Coweta County.
- The facility will bring higher paid and more skilled jobs into Coweta County, including robotics experts, software developers and systems engineers.
- Designed by SSI SCHAEFER, the new warehouse will include an observation platform for groups such as students from local schools to learn about manufacturing and automated processes.
- Yamaha expects to complete the SMART Warehouse in 2024.
- Yamaha is currently hiring for various roles, with most careers starting on a contract-to-perm basis.
- YMUS has a corporate office in California, two corporate offices in Georgia, facilities in Wisconsin and Alabama, and factory operations in Tennessee and Georgia.
- YMUS products include Motorcycles and Scooters, ATV and Side-by-Side vehicles, Snowmobiles, WaveRunner Personal Watercraft, Boats, Outboard Motors, Outdoor Power Equipment, Power Assist Bicycles, Golf Cars, Power Assist Wheelchair Systems, Surface Mount Technology (SMT) and Robotic Machines, Unmanned Helicopters, Accessories, Apparel, Yamalube products, and much more.
