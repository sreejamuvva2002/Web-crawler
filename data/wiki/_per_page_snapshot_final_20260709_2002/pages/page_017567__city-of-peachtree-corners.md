# City of Peachtree Corners

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2024-09-27

**Source:** [Autonomous Vehicles Launch in Georgia's Smart City - Self Drive News](https://selfdrivenews.com/autonomous-vehicles-launch-in-georgias-smart-city/)  ·  `page_017567`

## Facts

- Peachtree Corners partnered with May Mobility and T-Mobile to introduce autonomous vehicle (AV) technology, marking May Mobility’s first deployment in Georgia.
- The initiative aims to enhance transportation options using AVs powered by T-Mobile’s 5G network for residents and visitors.
- May Mobility’s vehicles will operate along Technology Parkway, offering on-demand services through an app.
- The partnership aligns with Peachtree Corners’ ongoing efforts to integrate innovative transportation solutions and establish itself as a hub for cutting-edge technologies.
- The autonomous service operates Monday through Friday from 8 AM to 4 PM.
- The service covers eight stops along Technology Parkway.
- Riders can book trips via the May Mobility app, with plans for a fully autonomous service in the near future.
- May Mobility’s vehicles utilize its patented Multi-Policy Decision Making (MPDM) system to enhance vehicle safety and decision-making in unpredictable scenarios.
- The service aims to gradually expand to connect more areas, including Peachtree Corners’ Town Center.
- Visitors can experience the autonomous Toyota Sienna Autono-MaaS on a designated route featuring the Innovation Center, restaurants, hotels, and Peachtree Corners’ City Hall.
- The deployment was initially available to the public on the week of October 7.
- Brian Johnson serves as the City Manager of Peachtree Corners.
- Brian Johnson stated: “We are excited to continue these efforts by welcoming May Mobility to Peachtree Corners and the state of Georgia.”
- The MPDM system manages real-time decision-making in complex environments by analyzing thousands of scenarios every second to select the safest course of action.
- T-Mobile’s 5G connectivity provides fast, low-latency data transmission for optimized vehicle performance and enhanced passenger experience.
- Manik Dhar serves as the Chief Commercial Officer of May Mobility.
- Manik Dhar stated: “This deployment allows us to showcase how MPDM operates both inside and outside of Curiosity Lab’s autonomous vehicle test lanes, while also benefiting from T-Mobile’s 5G.”
- The partnership highlights the growing role of autonomous vehicles and 5G technology in shaping the future of urban transportation.
- Peachtree Corners continues to lead as a model for integrating smart technologies to improve the quality of life for residents and the broader community.
