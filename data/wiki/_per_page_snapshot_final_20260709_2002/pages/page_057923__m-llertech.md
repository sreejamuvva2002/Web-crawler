# MöllerTech

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-06

**Source:** [German based automobile company opens manufacturing plant in Birmingham area | Bham Now](https://bhamnow.com/2018/03/16/german-based-automobile-company-opens-manufacturing-plant-in-birmingham-area/)  ·  `page_057923`

## Facts

- The area landed a new $46.3 million state-of-the-art automobile manufacturing facility.
- The facility is located in rural Bibb County, near the Woodstock community.
- The 150,000 sq ft facility is located inside the Scott G. Davis Industrial Park near Interstate 20/59.
- Steve Jordan, MöllerTech’s North American president and CEO, stated that “Bibb County is the perfect location for our new state-of-the-art facility.”
- The facility will be the flagship manufacturing plant for the company.
- The plant joins two other U.S. Mollertech facilities – one in Elberton, Georgia, and one in Shelby Township, Michigan.
- The plant will produce interior parts for the next generation of Mercedes-Bens SUV’s.
- Mollertech expects to employ 222 workers at the new plant.
- The company is currently hiring for Quality, Maintenance, Production Planning, and Production Operatives roles.
- Mercedes is currently building a $248.2 million Global Logistics Center and after-sales North American hub in Bibb County.
- The new Mercedes facility will create jobs for nearly 700 people.
- The existing Mercedes-Benz plant is located just 11 miles away in Vance, Alabama.
- Once the new Mercedes facility opens in Bibb County, the Birmingham 7 county region will be home to 16 German based companies that employ almost 6,700 people.
- Lee Smith, 2018 Chairman for the Birmingham Business Alliance and East Region Executive for BBVA Compass, stated that “Jobs can transform communities and we’ve already begun to see that happen in Bibb County with the addition of MöllerTech, followed by Mercedes-Benz.”
- Mollertech is a branch of German-based MollerGroup, one of the oldest manufacturing companies in Germany.
- The family-owned company employs over 1,800 people worldwide.
- Mollertech currently develops and produces interior parts for the automotive industry for customers including Audi, BMW, Daimler, GM, Honda, Porsche, Rolls-Royce, and Toyota.
