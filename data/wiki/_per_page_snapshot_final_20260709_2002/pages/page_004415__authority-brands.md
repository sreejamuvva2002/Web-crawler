# Authority Brands

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-02

**Source:** [Authority Brands Relocates HQ, Creates 390 Jobs in Cobb Co. | Georgia](https://georgia.org/press-releases/2026/authority-brands-relocates-hq-creates-390-jobs-cobb-co)  ·  `page_004415`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced on June 2, 2026, that Authority Brands will establish its new headquarters, known as the Franchisee Success Center, in Cobb County.
- The relocation project will create 390 new jobs.
- Authority Brands will invest $13 million over the next several years.
- Authority Brands is a leading home services franchisor with 15 brands operating in more than 2,700 territories and served by over 1,000 franchise owners.
- The company’s portfolio spans essential residential services including electrical, plumbing, HVAC, pool, house cleaning, lawn care, pest control, in-home caregivers, and junk removal.
- Authority Brands and its family of franchise brands earned recognition on the 2025 and 2026 lists of Entrepreneur’s Franchise 500, Franchise Business Review’s 2026 Top Franchises awards, Franchise Times, and Inc. Magazine.
- Jay Caiafa serves as the CEO of Authority Brands.
- The new 48,000-square-foot Franchisee Success Center will be located at 200 Galleria Parkway in Cobb County.
- The majority of the 390 new roles will be concentrated in operations, IT, finance, and marketing.
- Sharon Mason is the president and CEO of the Cobb Chamber.
- Katie Kirkpatrick is the president and CEO of the Metro Atlanta Chamber.
- Assistant Director of Statewide Projects John Soper represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development on this competitive project.
- The initiative was completed in partnership with Select Cobb, the Metro Atlanta Chamber, the University System of Georgia, and Georgia Power.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that Authority Brands grew from just six employees to more than 300 in Georgia prior to this expansion.
- Authority Brands' portfolio includes 15 home service franchise brands: America’s Swimming Pool Company, Benjamin Franklin Plumbing, The Cleaning Authority, DoodyCalls, DRYmedic Restoration Services, Homewatch CareGivers, The Junkluggers, Lawn Squad, Mister Sparky, Monster Tree Service, Mosquito Squad, One Hour Heating & Air Conditioning, Screenmobile, Service Team of Professionals Restoration, and Woofie’s.
- These brands provide home services through more than 2,700 territories operated by more than 1,000 franchise owners.
- Interested individuals can learn more about Authority Brands via its official website.
