# Columbus Technical College

## Facts

- Columbus Technical College’s Drafting and Tool & Die apprenticeship programs promote local trade education opportunities.
- The apprenticeship programs are part of a partnership with the U.S. Department of Labor.
- The program provides students with a learning and earning opportunity, combining full-time work in an industrial/trade occupation with part-time classes.
- Upon completion of the apprenticeship, students are awarded a certificate showcasing specialized training received in a particular occupation.
- Over the past 10 years, Oneda has hosted six students through this program.
- Oneda hired 100% of the students who completed the program for full-time jobs after graduation.
