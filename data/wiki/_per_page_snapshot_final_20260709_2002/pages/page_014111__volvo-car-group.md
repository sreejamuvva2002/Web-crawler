# Volvo Car Group

**Source:** [2023 (Full Year) USA: Volvo US and Canada Car Sales by Model - Car Sales Statistics](https://www.best-selling-cars.com/usa/2023-full-year-usa-volvo-us-and-canada-car-sales-by-model/)  ·  `page_014111`

## Facts

- In full-year 2023, Volvo increased car sales in the USA and Canada with the XC90 and XC60 the top-selling models.
- From January to December 2023, Volvo Car USA and Canada sold 141,566 cars for the full year 2023 – an increase of 26 percent compared to sales in 2022.
- Volvo’s recharge models — cars with a fully electric or plug-in hybrid powertrain — accounted for 28.3 percent of all Volvo cars sold in North America in 2023, an increase of 30.6 percent compared to 2022.
- Volvo sales in Canada were at the highest level in fifty years.
- For the full year 2023, Volvo sales in the US reached 128,701 cars, up 26.1 percent compared with the previous year.
- The 2023 US sales period included a record year for sales through the retail channel in the US.
- In the US, the XC90 midsize luxury SUV was the top-selling model, followed by the XC60 compact luxury SUV, and XC40 small luxury SUV.
- Worldwide, the XC60 is Volvo’s most popular model.
- In 2023, Volvo Canada set a new annual sales record with sales of 12,865 cars during the year.
- The previous Canadian annual sales record was set in 1974 when 11,763 Volvo cars were sold in Canada.
- Headquartered in Mahwah, New Jersey, Volvo Car USA LLC (VCUSA) is a subsidiary of Volvo Car Group of Gothenburg, Sweden (Nasdaq Stockholm VOLCAR B).
- VCUSA provides sales, marketing, parts, service, technology, and training support to Volvo automobile retailers in the United States since the 1950s.
- Operations also include a manufacturing campus in Ridgeville, SC that underlines the company’s long-term commitment to the U.S. as a key market.
- VCUSA maintains offices in California and parts distribution and training centers across the country.
- Volvo Car Canada Ltd. is a subsidiary of Volvo Car Group of Gothenburg, Sweden.
- VCCL provides marketing, sales, parts, service, technology, and training support to the 37 Volvo automobile retailers across Canada.
- Volvo Cars was founded in 1927.
- Volvo Cars has sales to customers in more than 100 countries.
- Volvo Cars is listed on the Nasdaq Stockholm exchange, traded under the ticker “VOLCAR B”.
- Volvo Cars aims to become a fully electric car maker by 2030.
- Volvo Cars has a commitment to an ongoing reduction of its carbon footprint, with the ambition to be a climate-neutral company by 2040.
- As of December 2022, Volvo Cars employed approximately 43,200 full-time employees.
- Volvo Cars’ head office, product development, marketing and administration functions are mainly located in Gothenburg, Sweden.
