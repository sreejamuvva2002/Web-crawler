# Fox Factory Holding Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-10-19

**Source:** [Fox Factory Partners With Society of Women Engineers - Cycle News](https://www.cyclenews.com/2022/10/article/fox-factory-partners-with-society-of-women-engineers/)  ·  `page_020248`

## Facts

- Fox Factory is based in Duluth, GA.
- On October 19, 2022, Fox Factory announced a partnership with the Society of Women Engineers (SWE).
- The company will attend the SWE National Conference in Houston, Texas, from October 20-22.
- The partnership aims to recruit talented professionals for Fox’s summer internship program.
- Fox Factory plans to interview and hire experienced engineers to help develop next-level performance products.
- Jackie Martin, Fox Factory’s Chief Purpose and Inclusion Officer, stated that engineering remains a heavily male-dominated field and emphasized the importance of having role models, a good support system, and a diverse network to help women professionals develop and thrive in the STEM workforce.
- As part of the partnership, Fox Factory is investing in women in engineering and other technical roles by offering SWE annual memberships to enhance their individual development and professional networks.
- The goal of this investment is to provide more opportunities for mentoring, training, and professional growth to support career trajectories at Fox.
- Kayleigh Hreha serves as Fox Factory’s Design Engineer and is the company's first female Design Engineer.
- Cat Vachon holds the title of Inclusion, Diversity, and Engagement Manager at Fox Factory.
- Fox Factory Holding Corp. designs, engineers, manufactures and markets performance-defining products and systems for customers worldwide.
- The company's premium products and systems are used primarily on bicycles, side-by-side vehicles, on-road vehicles with and without off-road capabilities, off-road vehicles and trucks, all-terrain vehicles, snowmobiles, specialty vehicles and applications, motorcycles, and commercial trucks.
- Fox Factory Holding Corp. is the holding company of FOX Factory, Inc.
