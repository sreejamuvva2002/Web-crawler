## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group concluded CES 2026 with major announcements and industry recognition underscoring its leadership in human-centered, AI-driven robotics and Physical AI.
- The Group’s CES 2026 activities focused on strategy, real-world deployment, product validation, and global partnerships with AI leaders under the theme ‘Partnering Human Progress’.

- Hyundai Motor Group is establishing capabilities to scale human-centered AI robotics and Physical AI applications through its Group-wide value chain and diversified product portfolio.

- Hyundai Motor Group has about 250,000 employees worldwide.
- The Group’s mobility brands include Hyundai, Kia, and Genesis.
