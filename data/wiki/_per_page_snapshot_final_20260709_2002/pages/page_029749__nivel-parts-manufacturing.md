# Nivel Parts & Manufacturing

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Morgan Stanley Capital Partners Completes Investment in Nivel Parts & Manufacturing](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20210521005241/en/Morgan-Stanley-Capital-Partners-Completes-Investment-in-Nivel-Parts-Manufacturing)  ·  `page_029749`

## Facts

- Investment funds managed by Morgan Stanley Capital Partners (“MSCP”) have completed an investment in Nivel Parts & Manufacturing (“Nivel”).
- Nivel was sold by funds managed by Kelso & Company.
- MSCP is partnering with the current management team led by Brent Moore, who will remain as CEO.
- Headquartered in Jacksonville, FL, Nivel is the leading manufacturer and supplier of branded aftermarket parts for personal transportation vehicles, utility terrain vehicles and heavy-duty equipment.
- The Company offers over 100,000 SKUs in accessories and maintenance parts across the full vehicle lifecycle to a diversified customer base including dealers, distributors and consumers.
- Aaron Sack serves as Managing Director and Head of Morgan Stanley Capital Partners.
- Nivel is MSCP’s most recent investment following AWT Labels & Packaging in December 2020 and US HealthConnect in October 2020.
- Jones Day acted as legal advisor to MSCP for the transaction.
- Morgan Stanley Capital Partners focuses on privately negotiated equity and equity-related investments primarily in North America across business services, consumer, healthcare and industrials markets.
- Morgan Stanley Investment Management has more than 731 investment professionals around the world and $1.4 trillion in assets under management or supervision as of March 31, 2021.
- Founded in 1968, Nivel is a global manufacturer, distributor, and seller of aftermarket parts and accessories for niche vehicles and heavy-duty equipment.
- Nivel owns the most valuable brands in the aftermarket niche vehicle parts and accessories categories: MadJax, Red Dot, GTW, Jake's, Reliance, Seizmik and High Lifter.
- Nivel serves as a trusted business partner to thousands of dealers, distributors, and consumers in the US and international markets.
