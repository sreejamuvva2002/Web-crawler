# Michelin North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-16

**Source:** [MICHELIN ANNOUNCES FULL ROLLOUT OF TREADEYE TECHNOLOGY ACROSS U.S. RETREAD NETWORK – ContentEngine](https://contentenginellc.com/2025/10/16/michelin-announces-full-rollout-of-treadeye-technology-across-us-retread-network/)  ·  `page_046446`

## Facts

- Michelin announced the full rollout of TreadEye technology across its U.S. retread network after a period of phased implementation.
- TreadEye is a proprietary tread depth assessment tool designed to enhance the retread process and deliver measurable value to fleets.
- The system accurately and consistently collects 1,200 measurement points to provide precise insights into tread wear and casing condition.
- Key benefits include identification of pull points by fleet location and maximization of rubber on the road by avoiding early pulls.
- The technology helps prevent casing damage and reduces DOT violations and downtime from late pulls.
- TreadEye improves efficiency in the tire removal process, directly impacting a fleet’s bottom line.
- Nate Kirian stated that “TreadEye is a game-changer for our retread operations and for the fleets we serve” and highlighted it as an example of Michelin’s innovation benefiting customers.
- Michelin is a world-leading manufacturer of engineered materials and composites that has pioneered its craft for more than 130 years.
- The company provides data- and AI-based connected solutions for professional fleets alongside high-quality tires and components for mobility, construction, aeronautics, low-carbon energies, and healthcare.
- Michelin North America, Inc. is headquartered in Greenville, S.C.
- Michelin North America, Inc. has approximately 23,500 employees.
- Michelin North America, Inc. operates 36 production facilities in the United States and Canada.
