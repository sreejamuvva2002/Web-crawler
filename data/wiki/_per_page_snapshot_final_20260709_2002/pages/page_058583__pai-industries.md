# PAI Industries

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-08-26

**Source:** [PAI Industries Partners with ECM to Expand Capabilities and Increase Efficiency by Adding In-House Carburizing Vacuum Furnace System - Motion + Power Manufacturers Alliance™](https://motionpower.org/pai-industries-partners-with-ecm-to-expand-capabilities-and-increase-efficiency-by-adding-in-house-carburizing-vacuum-furnace-system/)  ·  `page_058583`

## Facts

- PAI Industries partnered with ECM to bring heat treatment in-house with a NANO vacuum furnace system.
- Adding the in-house system expands PAI’s capabilities to expedite production and increase capacity.
- PAI operates an already impressive 112,000ft2 manufacturing facility with state-of-the-art machinery in Suwanee, GA.
- Prior to project acceptance, PAI relied on exemplary outsourced heat treating.
- Transitioning from primarily out-sourced heat treatment to in-house vacuum technology allows for low pressure carburizing and through-hardening 24/7.
- The ECM NANO system features modularity, an external loader, a 3-position temper oven, and queuing positions.
- Additional benefits provided by the vacuum technology include zero CO2 furnace emissions, no fire hazards or open flames, increased repeatability, and improved metallurgical results.
- Testing was conducted in the ECM NANO located in the ECM USA Synergy Center in Pleasant Prairie, WI to validate improved metallurgical properties prior to project acceptance.
- PAI Industries manufactures and distributes quality service parts to the heavy-duty truck industry.
- PAI has been supplying distributors throughout the world for over 50 years.
- ECM Technologies is an innovative low pressure vacuum furnace manufacturer with headquarters in Grenoble, France.
- ECM USA is located in Pleasant Prairie, Wisconsin.
- ECM serves industries including automotive, aerospace, nuclear, energy, electronic, induction, and 3D additive manufacturing.
- ECM provides service capabilities including advanced automation, robotics, after sales support, spare parts, and on-site training.
