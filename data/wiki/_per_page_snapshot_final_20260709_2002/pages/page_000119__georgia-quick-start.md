## Facts

- Quick Start provides customized workforce training as an incentive for companies investing and creating jobs in Georgia.
- To begin a training partnership, Quick Start meets with company leadership, subject-matter experts, and operations teams to define the scope of training needs.
- Different job tasks require different modes of training.
- With an emphasis on visual learning and hands-on training, Quick Start maps out training plans unique for each company.
- “Flexible and responsive” have been Quick Start’s watchwords for decades.
- Quick Start delivers the training whenever and wherever it makes sense for each company project.
- Quick Start upholds the highest standards of confidentiality to protect a company’s intellectual property.
