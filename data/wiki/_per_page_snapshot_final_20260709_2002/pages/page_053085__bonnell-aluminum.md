# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Bonnell Aluminum apprenticeship proves crucial in carving potential workers' future](https://www.alcircle.com/news/bonnell-aluminum-apprenticeship-proves-crucial-in-carving-potential-workers-future-112186)  ·  `page_053085`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredger Corporation.
- Bonnell Aluminum collaborated with the United States Department of Labor and the Aluminum Extruders Council (AEC) to introduce an apprenticeship programme designed to upskill potential aluminium extrusion workers.
- The programme has national accreditation and offers potential funding from the DOL.
- The programme is available to existing employees who want to focus on a specific trade career path.
- The initial phase of the programme came in 2023 with an agenda to recruit and groom industrial maintenance technicians in a two-year apprenticeship regime.
- A second phase of the programme was announced specifically designed to train aluminium extrusion press operators and die shop technicians, and is set to roll out in 2025.
- Bonnell Aluminum plans to add skilled trades such as CNC operation, robotics, and welding to its upskilling initiative shortly.
- Carl Czarnik serves as the Vice President of Operations for Bonnell Aluminum.
- Carl Czarnik explained that the AEC Apprentice Programme is beneficial to both participants and the company, helping identify, develop, and retain talent while allowing apprentices to learn a skilled trade at no cost.
- Bonnell Aluminum is one of the top manufacturers of custom-fabricated and finished aluminium extrusions serving the building and construction, automotive and transportation, consumer durables, machinery and equipment, and electrical and distribution markets.
- The company's branded products include TSLOTS and Futura Transitions.
- The company has manufacturing facilities in Newnan (Georgia), Carthage (Tunisia), Niles (Michigan), Elkhart (Indiana), and Clearfield (Utah).
