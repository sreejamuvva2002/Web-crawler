## Facts

- DAS Corp., a Korean supplier for Hyundai and Kia, is investing $35 million along Georgia’s Interstate 16.
- DAS Corp. is pledging to hire 300 workers in Metter.
- DAS Corp. serves as a top seat supplier for Hyundai and Kia.
- The Hyundai electric vehicle plant served by DAS Corp. is located in Ellabell, about 45 miles away.
- DAS Corp.’s facility is the latest announcement in a parade of suppliers coming in to serve the Hyundai electric vehicle plant.
