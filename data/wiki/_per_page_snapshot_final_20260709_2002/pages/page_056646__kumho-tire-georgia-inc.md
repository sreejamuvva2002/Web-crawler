# Kumho Tire Georgia Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-06

**Source:** [Kumho Tire Georgia Inc - World's Largest Tire Manufacturers](https://discounttiresworld.com/kumho-tire-georgia-inc/)  ·  `page_056646`

## Facts

- Kumho Tire Georgia Inc. is a tire manufacturing company located at 3051 Kumho Parkway, Macon, GA 31216.
- It operates as a subsidiary of South Korean parent company Kumho Tire Co.
- The Macon plant spans over 1 million square feet and was the first plant location for Kumho Tire in the United States.
- The facility has been operating since at least 2015.
- Kumho Tire Georgia Inc. is a member of the Greater Macon Chamber of Commerce.
- Kumho Tire USA, headquartered in Atlanta, serves as the sales, marketing, product development, and distribution arm for Kumho Tire Co. Inc. in the United States.
- The company produces tires for passenger cars, SUVs, trucks, and commercial vehicles.
- Its tire lineup includes all-season tires, summer tires, winter tires, and performance tires.
- Kumho tires are used by various car manufacturers as original equipment or recommended replacements, including Hyundai, Kia, Ford, General Motors, and Volkswagen.
- Kumho Tire has manufacturing facilities in South Korea, China, Vietnam, and the United States.
- The company focuses on product development, market expansion, technical progression, and environmental sustainability.
