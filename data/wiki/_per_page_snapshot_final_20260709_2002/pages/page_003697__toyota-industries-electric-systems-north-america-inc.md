# Toyota Industries Electric Systems North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-14

**Source:** [Gov. Kemp: Toyota Industries Corporation Expands Georgia Presence, Creating 250 New Jobs | Governor Brian P. Kemp Office of the Governor](https://gov.georgia.gov/press-releases/2023-11-14/gov-kemp-toyota-industries-corporation-expands-georgia-presence-creating)  ·  `page_003697`

## Facts

- TIESNA will create more than 250 new jobs while expanding the Toyota Industries Corporation campus in Pendergrass.
- The expansion includes a new manufacturing facility valued at $69 million in investment.
- TIESNA broke ground on the new facility on November 14, 2023.
- Japanese Consul General Mio Maeda and local representatives joined the groundbreaking ceremony.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp stated that Georgia has distinguished itself as a leader in the automotive industry.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp pledged to continue working with state and local partners to bring opportunity to communities across Georgia.
- TIESNA is a subsidiary of Toyota Industries Corporation, which is headquartered in Japan.
- Toyota Industries develops and manufactures automobile-related products, including car air-conditioning compressors and car electronics components for worldwide carmakers.
- Toyota Industries has operated in Pendergrass since 2004.
- The group's presence in Pendergrass began with TD Automotive Compressor Georgia, LLC (TACG).
- Toyota Industries Compressor Parts America, Co. (TICA) was established in Pendergrass in 2012.
- TACG and TICA currently support more than 400 jobs in Jackson County.
- TIESNA's new facility will be located at the 1700 Valentine Industrial Parkway campus in Pendergrass.
- The new facility will produce DC-to-DC converters that convert high voltage to low voltage to supply power to auxiliary equipment.
- Operations at the new TIESNA facility are expected to begin in 2025.
- Jackson County Commission Chair Tom Crow welcomed TIESNA as the third Toyota Industries-related company in Jackson County.
- Tom Crow stated that TACG and TICA have been outstanding corporate citizens in the community.
- Tom Crow pledged Jackson County's support for Toyota Industries' success.
- Griff Lynch, President and CEO of the Georgia Ports Authority, welcomed TIESNA's expansion as part of Georgia's growing automaking sector.
- Griff Lynch noted that significant investments from Toyota Industries and other automotive suppliers are delivering career opportunities for Georgians.
- Senior Regional Project Manager Kristen Miller represented the GDEcD Global Commerce team on the project.
- The project was supported in partnership with the Jackson County Area Chamber of Commerce, Georgia Power, the Georgia Ports Authority, and Georgia Quick Start.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that Toyota Industries has become part of the fabric of the Jackson County community since 2004.
- Pat Wilson emphasized that building long-term relationships gives Georgia a competitive advantage for company expansions.
- GDEcD has maintained 50 years of representation in Japan to keep direct communication lines open with corporate headquarters like Toyota Industries.
- Pat Wilson expressed excitement about welcoming TIESNA to Jackson County and congratulated the involved partners.
- Georgia has positioned itself as a leader in developing and harnessing emerging technologies for the automotive and mobility industry for over a century.
- In fiscal year 2023, job creation in Georgia's automotive industry increased by 324 percent compared to fiscal year 2021.
- Suppliers attracted by Georgia's original equipment manufacturers resulted in over $2 billion in investment across the state in FY23.
- Toyota Industries Corporation was established in 1926 in Japan for the manufacturing and sales of automatic looms.
- Toyota Industries currently holds more than 280 consolidated subsidiaries worldwide.
- Toyota Industries holds the largest world market share in air-jet looms, car air conditioning compressors, and lift trucks.
- TIESNA was established in 2013 in Novi, Michigan.
- TIESNA operates as a sales company handling automotive electronics components such as AC inverters, voltage stabilizers, and motor controllers.
- TIESNA's customers include major original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) in North America.
