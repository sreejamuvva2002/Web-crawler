# YKK AP America Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-25

## Facts

- YKK AP America Inc. announced the creation of its Manufacturing Excellence organization, along with leadership appointments across its commercial and residential manufacturing operations.
- The changes are designed to strengthen manufacturing performance, accountability, and alignment as the business continues to grow.
- Daymon Loyd has been promoted to lead the Manufacturing Excellence organization, which brings together manufacturing quality, engineering and maintenance under one structure.
- The organization is designed to improve coordination, accelerate problem-solving and drive more consistent execution across commercial manufacturing operations.
- In this role, Loyd will oversee key YKK AP manufacturing priorities including customer satisfaction, on-time delivery, quality, equipment reliability, process optimization and strategic oversight of the Dublin plant’s investment and modernization programs.
- Mike Turner, president, commercial business at YKK AP America Inc., stated Daymon’s appointment reflects the company’s continued commitment to building a strong manufacturing organization well-positioned to support customers and long-term growth.
- Mike Turner noted Loyd’s experience across both residential and commercial manufacturing gives him a strong foundation to lead the effort.
- Mike Turner emphasized that as the company continues to invest in quality, reliability and service, aligning key functions under focused leadership will strengthen operational execution and enhance the ability to serve the evolving needs of the market.
- To support its residential business, YKK AP has promoted Derek Bessenbacher to residential operations manager.
- Bessenbacher will provide dedicated leadership and strengthen day-to-day execution in residential manufacturing.
- The move reflects the company’s focus on supporting continued growth in its residential operations through greater oversight and accountability.
- Josh Jensen, president, residential business at YKK AP America Inc., stated focused plant leadership is critical to delivering the quality, consistency and service customers expect.
- Derek Bessenbacher is a 10-year employee who has served in roles of increasing responsibility across manufacturing engineering, process improvement, and quality control.
- Josh Jensen expressed full confidence that Bessenbacher will bring the experience and expertise needed to support continued investment in people and structure required to strengthen execution and position the residential business for long-term growth.
