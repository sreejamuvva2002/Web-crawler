## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is located in Ellaville, Georgia.
- TCI Powder Coatings is a subsidiary of RPM Intl. Inc.
- TCI Powder Coatings has named Dave Severson as automotive market segment manager.
- Dave Severson has more than 15 years of technical and commercial experience in the coatings industry.
- Dave Severson most recently was involved in regional sales and market management with Valspar Corp.
- Dave Severson has a bachelor’s degree in chemistry from the University of Minnesota Institute of Technology.
