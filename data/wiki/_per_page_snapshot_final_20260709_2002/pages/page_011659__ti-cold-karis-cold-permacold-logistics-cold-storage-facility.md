# Ti Cold / Karis Cold / PermaCold Logistics Cold Storage Facility

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-09

**Source:** [Kemp says groundbreakings mark development win for rural Georgia](https://www.savannahnow.com/story/news/state/2025/07/09/kemp-says-groundbreakings-mark-development-win-for-rural-georgia/84518659007/)  ·  `page_011659`

## Facts

- A $60 million cold storage facility is being built in Darien, Georgia.
- The project is a partnership between Ti Cold, Karis Cold, and PermaCold Logistics.
- The facility will be located at Tidewaters Industrial Park, which is designated as a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) site.
- The state's GRAD program allows for expedited industrial construction projects.
- Governor Brian Kemp announced the groundbreaking on Wednesday.
- Ti Cold serves as the cold storage builder and developer for the project.
- Karis Cold is a private investment and development company with expertise in the cold storage industry.
- The first phase of the project is expected to create 50 jobs upon completion.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that the announcement marks the largest known economic development win for McIntosh County and another success for rural Georgia.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that companies like Ti Cold, Karis Cold, and PermaCold Logistics, along with state ports and highway systems, support logistics industry jobs across the state.
- Sam Tippmann is the founder and CEO of Ti Cold.
- Sam Tippmann stated that his company has built in Georgia many times over the years and is thrilled to be working in McIntosh County.
- Sam Tippmann highlighted that the project received incredible support from state officials, the McIntosh County team, and the city of Darien.
- The facility will feature a flexible design capable of maintaining temperatures ranging from 40 degrees Fahrenheit to minus 10 degrees.
- Structural steel pallet racks at the facility will accommodate more than 30,000 pallet positions.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development's Global Commerce team worked on the project in partnership with the McIntosh County Industrial Development Authority.
- The Georgia Ports Authority, Georgia EMC, and Georgia Power also partnered with the GDEcD Global Commerce team on the project.
