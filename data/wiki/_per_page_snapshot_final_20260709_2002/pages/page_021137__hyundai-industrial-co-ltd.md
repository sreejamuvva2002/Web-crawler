# Hyundai Industrial Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-01

**Source:** [Hyundai, Kia Supplier to Invest in Manufacturing Facility in Coweta - Expansion Solutions](https://www.expansionsolutionsmagazine.com/hyundai-kia-supplier-to-invest-in-manufacturing-facility-in-coweta/)  ·  `page_021137`

## Facts

- Hyundai Industrial Co., Ltd. will invest more than $24 million in a new manufacturing facility near Newnan in Coweta County.
- The investment will create 100 new jobs.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that partners like Kia and Hyundai are attracting a large network of suppliers, creating jobs and opportunity for hardworking Georgians across the entire state.
- According to Governor Kemp, these suppliers have committed to bringing over 5,300 jobs to Georgia, mostly to communities outside of large urban areas, since last year’s Hyundai Metaplant announcement.
- Hyundai Industrial was established in 1978 and is headquartered in Ulsan, Korea.
- The company specializes in manufacturing car seats, arm rests, and head rests for the Hyundai and Kia supply chain.
- Mr. Woosuck Kim, Team Leader at HDI America Inc., stated that Hyundai Industrial has more than four decades of experience providing comfortable and safe experiences for drivers and passengers with some of the world’s biggest automotive brands.
- Mr. Kim added that the company is excited to be part of the e-mobility transition happening in Georgia and looks forward to working with Coweta County to create mutually beneficial growth for the community and future employees.
- Hyundai Industrial has purchased an existing building at 83 Amlajack Way in Coweta County.
- The purchased building will be retrofitted to produce arm and head rests for OEMs, including Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.
- Hyundai Industrial will focus on filling manufacturing production roles.
- Coweta County Board of Commissioners Chairman John Reidelbach stated they are thrilled to welcome Hyundai Industrial Co., Ltd. to Coweta County.
- Chairman Reidelbach noted that Hyundai joins the county's thriving manufacturing industries and will have close proximity to I-85, making transport to nearby automobile plants effortless.
- Chairman Reidelbach expressed excitement for the opportunities Hyundai will bring as workforce opportunities continue to increase within the community.
- Director of Korean Investment Yoonie Kim represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team on this competitive project in partnership with the Coweta County Development Authority and Georgia EMC.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that with Kia in West Georgia and Hyundai on the eastern coast, suppliers like Hyundai Industrial can locate almost anywhere in the state and still be center of at least two major manufacturers.
- Commissioner Wilson noted that companies are able to select a community that’s the right fit for them, and vice versa.
- Commissioner Wilson described the relationship between Coweta County and Hyundai Industrial as another classic example of Georgia’s partnership approach to economic development, matching a community with an existing asset and plan to a company whose goals aligned.
- Georgia has fostered healthy industry practices, encouraged collaboration and innovation, and positioned the state as a leader in developing and harnessing emerging technologies for the evolving automotive and mobility industry for over a century.
- As the electric vehicle market continues to grow, Georgia has pursued the entire supply chain, creating more than $25.1 billion in investments and 29,000 jobs since 2020.
- In fiscal year 2023, job creation in the automotive industry increased by 324 percent when compared to fiscal year 2021.
- Suppliers attracted by Georgia’s mobility original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) resulted in over $2 billion in investment across the state in FY23.
- Hyundai Industry Co., Ltd. aims to manufacture automotive car seats that provide comfort and play a critical role in safety.
- Hyundai Industrial creates safer and more comfortable experiences through developing cutting-edge products based on talent and advanced technology while striving for mutually beneficial growth for its employees and the community.
