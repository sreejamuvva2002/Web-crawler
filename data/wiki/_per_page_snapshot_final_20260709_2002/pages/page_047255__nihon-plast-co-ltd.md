# Nihon Plast Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-31

## Facts

- The Company is a manufacturer of automotive safety parts and plastic parts.
- Main products include airbags, steering wheels, and plastic products such as instrument panel parts and air ducts.
- Plastic products accounted for 48.2% of sales in the fiscal year ended March 31, 2025.
- Airbags accounted for 25.6% of sales in the fiscal year ended March 31, 2025.
- Steering wheels accounted for 26.2% of sales in the fiscal year ended March 31, 2025.
- The company produces auxiliary parts for batteries.
- In April 2000, the company established Neaton Rome Incorporated in Georgia, U.S.A., with Neaton Auto Products holding 100% of the shares.
- As of March 31, 2025, the company is listed on the Standard Market of the Tokyo Stock Exchange.
- Tokyo Small and Medium Business Investment & Consultation holds a 7.36% investment ratio as of March 31, 2025.
- Individual shareholders hold a combined 6.69% investment ratio as of March 31, 2025.
- Daicel Corporation holds a 5.15% investment ratio as of March 31, 2025.
- Itochu Plastics Inc. holds a 1.63% investment ratio as of March 31, 2025.
- The company's headquarters is located in Fujinomiya City, Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan.
- In July 2005, Honda Motor Co., Ltd. made a 20.6% investment in the company, making it a Honda affiliate until the capital relationship ended in 2013.
