## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd. is headquartered in Yokohama, Japan.
- The company was formally reorganized under this name in 2022.
- It is a specialist in in‑vehicle electronics and cockpit systems for the mobility sector.
- It carries forward an automotive legacy dating to 1939.
- It develops advanced products such as cockpit domain controllers.
- It develops high‑performance compute (HPC) platforms including Neuron™.
- It develops premium audio, vision & sensing, and infotainment systems.
- It develops electronic mirrors and ADAS/mirror systems.
- Its products are applied in vehicles globally.
- The company’s solutions integrate AI, software‑defined vehicle (SDV) architectures, human insight technology, and cyber‑security into safety‑critical and comfort‑enhancing systems.
- Driven by a Joy in Motion philosophy, it emphasizes sustainable mobility, DEI, functional safety, and environmental stewardship.
- It achieved net‑zero CO₂ across global sites as of 2023.
- It serves major OEMs across Asia, North America and Europe.
- Its regional presence includes design, R&D and manufacturing facilities in Japan, US (Peachtree City, Georgia and more), China, Thailand, Germany and beyond.
- The company announced an innovation called VERZEUSE for Virtualization Extensions Type-3 to detect and defend against cyberattacks, integrating VicOne's vehicle security software xCarbon.
