## Facts

- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a Textron Inc. company, announces the launch of the Cushman Hauler XL utility vehicle.
- The Cushman Hauler XL features a 68.5-inch-long aluminum flat bed and a 1,600-lb payload capacity.
- The vehicle provides truck-inspired design, functional dash, purpose-built accessories, and an automatic parking brake.
- Adam Harris, Vice President and General Manager of Cushman and E-Z-GO at Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., states the Hauler XL is an industry leader in bed size, vehicle rating, and bed load capacity.
- The Hauler XL comes in two powertrain options: a fully electric ELiTE series model powered by Samsung SDI lithium-battery technology, or a gas-powered model with a 13.5-hp gas engine.
- The ELiTE series is the result of years of collaboration between Textron Specialized Vehicles and Samsung SDI to create a zero-maintenance battery for golf cars and utility vehicles.
- The Hauler XL ELiTE provides customers with reduced maintenance, a five-year battery warranty, an automatic parking brake, and street-legal capability in most jurisdictions.
- The street-legal Hauler XL ELiTE is designed to drive on roads with up to 35 mph speed limits at up to 25 mph by meeting the federally required FMVSS 500 and voluntary SAE J2358 standards.
- Cushman vehicles are designed and manufactured in Augusta, Ga., by the Textron Specialized Vehicles business of Textron Inc.
- Since 1901, the diverse Cushman product line-up has included utility, personnel transport, burden carriers, tow tractors, food and beverage, and turf maintenance vehicles for commercial, industrial, and golf applications.
