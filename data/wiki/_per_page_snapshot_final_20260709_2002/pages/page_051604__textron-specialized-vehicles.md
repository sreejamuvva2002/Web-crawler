# Textron Specialized Vehicles

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Jacobsen and Textron Specialized Vehicles Will Combine Their Operations - Superintendent](https://superintendentmagazine.com/the-latest/around-the-industry/textron-jacobsen-combines-operations/)  ·  `page_051604`

## Facts

- Jacobsen and Textron Specialized Vehicles will combine their operations by the close of 2017.
- The combination will allow them to better serve shared customers and operate more efficiently through a common supply chain and efficiencies of scale in manufacturing and back-office operations.
- Kevin Holleran, president and CEO of Textron Specialized Vehicles, stated the combination will be nimble, optimally sized, and well equipped to meet evolving customer needs.
- The companies are working carefully to ensure a seamless transition that is invisible to customers.
- They will maintain the level of support and service – before, during, and after the sale – that both Jacobsen and TSV customers expect and demand.
- Jacobsen’s operations in Charlotte, North Carolina, will relocate to Textron Specialized Vehicles facilities in Augusta, Georgia.
- The first phase of relocation is expected to be completed by the end of March.
- All operations are planned to be relocated by the close of 2017.
- Jacobsen’s field sales and service teams will remain in place.
- Jacobsen’s operations in Coatesville, Indiana, and Ipswich, U.K., will continue.
