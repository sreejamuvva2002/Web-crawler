# Kyungshin America Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-12-06

**Source:** [Kyungshin America to create 70 new jobs in Rincon – Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority](https://savannahjda.com/2023/12/kyungshin-america-to-create-70-new-jobs-in-rincon/)  ·  `page_029685`

## Facts

- Kyungshin America Corp., an automotive supplier, will establish a new distribution facility in Rincon.
- The facility will create an estimated 70 new jobs.
- The project involves more than $22 million in investment in Effingham County.
- Kyungshin America Corporation was established as the U.S. subsidiary of the Kyungshin Corporation.
- Kyungshin Corporation manufactures automotive wire harnesses for traditional and electric vehicles.
- A representative of Kyungshin America Corp. stated that Kyungshin is growing alongside its customers and in line with the growth of the electric vehicle market.
- The representative noted that Rincon’s location provides convenient access to key customers.
- Brandt Herndon, CEO of the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority, welcomed Kyungshin America to Effingham County.
- Broe Real Estate Group assisted in locating Kyungshin America at the Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub.
- Kyungshin’s new facility will be located at 1001 Gateway Parkway in Rincon.
- The facility will supply products to key customers.
- Kyungshin is hiring for various positions at the facility.
- Kyungshin Corporation has a 50-year dedication to the automotive industry in Korea.
- Kyungshin currently operates 22 facilities in ten countries, supporting more than 25,000 jobs worldwide.
- Kyungshin is establishing a logistics warehouse in Rincon, Georgia as a supplier for its electric vehicle business in the United States.
