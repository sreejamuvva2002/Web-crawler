# Club Car

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Club Car announced plans on Friday, Oct. 13 to expand into a new 105,000 square foot facility in Appling.
- The new facility is located in the White Oak Industrial Park near an existing Club Car facility that opened in 2019.
- Club Car plans to use the additional space to support warehousing and manufacturing functions.
- Construction of the new facility is expected to be complete by December 2023.
- Mark Wagner, Club Car president, stated that the team is dedicated to providing the best vehicles and service for customers and that the new facility gives them the opportunity to extend operations in the United States.
- Rick Evans, chair of the Board of Directors for the Development Authority of Columbia County, noted Club Car's long-standing relationship with Columbia County and valued their investments to expand and manufacture world-class golf cars and utility vehicles in the county.
- Doug Duncan, chairman of Columbia County Board of Commissioners, described Club Car as a great community partner and expressed excitement for their continued growth in Columbia County.
- Club Car has maintained multi-purpose operations in Augusta since its founding in 1958.
- Today, Club Car designs and manufactures golf cars, commercial utility vehicles, and personal –use transportation.
