# Lund International

## Facts

- Based in Buford, Georgia, Lund International is the U.S. based leading designer and manufacturer of automotive accessories for passenger cars, Jeeps, and light, medium and heavy-duty trucks.
- The company offers the industry’s broadest range of functional body and appearance products that provide customers with ways to protect, customize and personalize their vehicles.
- Lund International products are marketed under the brand names of AMP Research, AVS, Belmor, Bushwacker, LUND, Rampage Products, Roadworks Manufacturing, Roll-N-Lock, Stampede Automotive Accessories and Tonno Pro.
- The company is an employer of over 1,000 American workers.
- Lund International serves as a premier designer and U.S.-based manufacturer of innovative accessories for trucks and Jeeps of all classes.
- Chief Executive Officer Mitch Fogle stated the new television series will take viewers on a journey to experience truck and Jeep lifestyle, shows, events, and products in use addressing common everyday problems.
- The company's new television series will feature installations and applications of its products, serving as a resource with perspectives from people who designed and built the parts and accessories popular with enthusiasts.
