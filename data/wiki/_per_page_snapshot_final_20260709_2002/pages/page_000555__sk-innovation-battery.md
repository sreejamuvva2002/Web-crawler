# SK Innovation Battery

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [SK Innovation Battery - EV Battery Manufacturer | Steel Service](https://www.steelservice.com/project/sk-innovation-battery/)  ·  `page_000555`

## Facts

- Located 65 miles northeast of downtown Atlanta on 283 acres along I-85 inside the Commerce industrial complex.
- The manufacturing facility is 2.4 million square feet.
- It is SK Innovation’s first EV battery factory in the U.S.
- Work began in spring of 2019 with phase 1 of the project.
- Phase 1 facilities include multiple roof elevations and mezzanine decks.
- Phase 2 began construction in the last quarter of 2020.
- Phase 2 consisted of a 1.2 million-square-foot facility located along Phase 1 of the project.
- Phase 2 included eight additional buildings totaling 90,000 square feet.
- The additional buildings include kitchens, cell discharge areas, administrative office expansion, and a bridge linking the two main buildings.
