# MollerTech USA, LLC

## Facts

- MollerTech USA, LLC hosted the Bibb County Chamber of Commerce monthly coffee group on Thursday, January 3 to kick off the 2019 New Year.
- A group of about 30 or more interested business, political and civic leaders attended the event.
- Tiffany Overton serves as Human Resources Manager at MollerTech USA.
- Tammy Lauderdale serves as Human Resources Administrator at MollerTech USA.
- The company manufactures trim parts for automobiles.
- The primary customer for the Bibb County plant is the nearby Mercedes plant, which takes all of their three-shift production capacity.
- MollerTech is a German company that also operates plants in two other U.S. states and produces parts for other auto makers.
- The Bibb County plant started production in October 2018.
- The facility began with 20 employees and has already grown to a staff of 165.
- MollerTech currently has 90 open jobs to fill at the Bibb County location.
- The plant is presently making 72 different trim parts for Mercedes vehicles.
- MollerTech plans to double the size of its Bibb County plant and its production capacity.
- Uncrated machinery is already on the factory floor, ready to be installed and set up to add to their capacity.
- The facility features clean conditions with wide, well-marked walkways and emphasizes safety for workers and visitors.
- The production area and office area are separated by glass so workers are always in view of management and there is frequent contact between office personnel and production personnel.
- HR Administrator Tammy Lauderdale knows every plant worker on a first-name basis.
- Every plant management-level worker maintains contact with the production staff.
- MollerTech is described as a good place to work.
- Charles Allen and Valerie Cook of the Bibb County Chamber of Commerce visited the facility to learn about the manufacturing process.
