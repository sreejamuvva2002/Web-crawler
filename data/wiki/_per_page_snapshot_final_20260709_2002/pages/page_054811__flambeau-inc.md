## Facts

- Flambeau, Inc. is a plastics manufacturing company based in Baraboo, Wisconsin, United States.
- The company operates at 801 Lynn Avenue (53913) and employs between 501 and 1000 people.
- Flambeau designs and manufactures plastic products for a worldwide customer base, including injection and blow molded thermoplastic components, tools and fixtures, and contract manufacturing of complete assemblies.
- The company operates through multiple retail divisions: Automotive Group, Industrial Markets & Packaging Group, Retail Markets Group, Medical Markets Group, and Tooling Group.
- Flambeau owns proprietary brands including ArtBin, Duncan, Flambeau Outdoors, Flambeau Fluid Systems, Flambeau Hardware, OrnaMates, and Flambeau Packaging & Storage Solutions.
- Together with the Nordic Group of Companies, Flambeau provides a broad resource network for design and specifications, tooling and fixtures, and contract manufacturing.
- In 2024, Flambeau closed facilities in Madison, Wisconsin and Madison, Georgia, with production moved to other company sites.
- The company has been recognized as one of the top plastics manufacturers in North America.
- Flambeau received the 2020 PACCAR Quality Award.
