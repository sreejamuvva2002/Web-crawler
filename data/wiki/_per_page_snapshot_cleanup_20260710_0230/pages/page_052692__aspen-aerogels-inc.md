## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels, Inc. operates as a technology leader in sustainability and electrification solutions, leveraging aerogel technology for e-mobility and clean energy applications.
- The company's PyroThin® products are specifically engineered to address thermal runaway challenges within the electric vehicle ("EV") market.
- Aspen Aerogels is demobilizing its previously planned second manufacturing plant in Statesboro, Georgia.
- Planned asset sales in Georgia are expected to materially reduce the company's debt over the coming quarters.
- Capital expenditures related to the Statesboro plant project totaled $11.3 million in the first half of 2025.
- Second quarter 2025 revenue was $78.0 million, with the Thermal Barrier segment contributing $55.2 million, a 13% increase quarter-over-quarter.
- First half of 2025 actual revenue totaled $157 million, while the second half of 2025 outlook projects revenue between $140 million and $160 million.
- Full year 2025 revenue outlook is set at $297 million to $317 million.
- Adjusted EBITDA for the second quarter of 2025 was $9.7 million, with a second half of 2025 outlook of $20 million to $30 million and a full year outlook of $35 million to $45 million.
- The company ended the second quarter of 2025 with $167.6 million in cash and equivalents.
- CEO Don Young cited regulatory headwinds facing the EV market, particularly in the U.S., as a driver for restructuring the company to operate with minimal capital expenditure requirements.
- Aspen Aerogels announced that CFO Ricardo Rodriguez will step down at the end of the third quarter, with Grant Thoele appointed as his internal successor effective immediately upon departure.
- Second quarter 2025 net loss was $9.1 million, which included $5.9 million in restructuring and impairment charges.
- The company's strategy focuses on partnering with industry leaders to leverage its Aerogel Technology Platform into additional high-value markets.
- A conference call to discuss second quarter 2025 results and recent business developments will be held Thursday, August 7, 2025 at 8:30 a.m. EST.
