# ABC Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-19

**Source:** [Key Leadership Appointments Announced For Combined ABC Technologies & TI Fluid Systems Group - ABC Technologies](https://abcgrp.com/key-leadership-appointments-announced-for-combined-abc-technologies-ti-fluid-systems-group/)  ·  `page_011554`

## Facts

- Terry Campbell will become President & Chief Executive Officer of the combined ABC Technologies and TI Fluid Systems group.
- Alexander De Bock will become Chief Financial Officer of the combined group.
- Hans Dieltjens, current President & Chief Executive Officer of TI Fluid Systems, will step down following completion of the acquisition.
- ABC Technologies' acquisition of TI Fluid Systems remains on track to complete during the first half of 2025.
- Terry Campbell became President & CEO of ABC in 2022 and previously held senior leadership roles at Johnson Controls, Magna International, and The Woodbridge Group.
- Alexander De Bock was appointed CFO of TIFS in 2023 after spending over 15 years in the industry with finance leadership roles at WABCO and ZF Group.
- The acquisition will be implemented by way of a court-sanctioned scheme of arrangement under Part 26 of the Companies Act 2006.
- TIFS shareholders approved the recommended all-cash offer from ABC Technologies Acquisitions Limited on February 5, 2025.
- TI Fluid Systems will announce the anticipated completion timetable via a Regulatory Information Service and its corporate website.
- The combination of ABC Technologies and TI Fluid Systems aims to create a larger, more diversified group better positioned to navigate the transition to electrification.
- ABC Technologies is a leading global manufacturer and supplier of custom technical plastics and lightweight innovations to the North American light vehicle industry.
- ABC Technologies serves more than 25 major original equipment manufacturer customers across 8 countries.
- ABC Technologies operates with a skilled workforce of over 11,000 team members.
- ABC Technologies is majority owned by affiliated funds of Apollo Global Management, Inc., with Oaktree Capital Management, L.P. owning a minority equity interest.
- TI Fluid Systems is a global innovator of thermal and fluid systems solutions for current and developing vehicle architectures, operating across 26 countries with over 100 years of automotive supply experience.
