## Facts

- Indian River Transport Company announced plans to build a new trucking terminal in Cordele, Crisp County, Georgia.
- The new facility will have frontage along Interstate 75 at Exit 102.
- The Cordele terminal will include offices, truck bays, washout facilities, and an asphalt pad of about eight acres for trailer storage.
- Construction should begin in mid-2019.
- Once completed, Indian River anticipates hiring approximately 125 people.
- About 100 of the new hires will be commercial truck drivers.
- Indian River is a privately-owned food grade tank carrier providing transportation services throughout the United States.
- The company offers import/export services between the US, Canada and Mexico.
- Much of its liquid freight includes milk, various juices, and other beverages.
- Gov. Nathan Deal announced that Forquímica will create 80 jobs in Moultrie, Colquitt County, Georgia.
- Forquímica is a developer of sustainable agricultural products.
- Forquímica will invest $1 million in the project.
- New jobs will include positions in general services, management, marketing and sales.
- Gov. Nathan Deal announced that Check-Mate Industries will create 230 jobs in Thomasville, GA.
- Check-Mate Industries is a family-owned manufacturer of metal stampings, assemblies, tools and dies.
- The company will invest more than $16 million in a new manufacturing location.
- New jobs will include positions throughout all levels of the organization including management teams and administrative personnel.
- Skilled positions will include computer numeric controlled machinists and tool and die makers.
- Gov. Nathan Deal announced that SK innovation is estimated to create more than 2,000 jobs in Jackson County.
- SK innovation is a developer and manufacturer of lithium-ion batteries for hybrid electric vehicles.
- SK innovation will invest $1.67 billion in a new manufacturing plant in the long term.
- Gov. Nathan Deal announced that Toyo Tire North America will create more than 150 jobs in Cartersville, GA.
- Toyo Tire North America is a manufacturer and seller of radial tires for passenger vehicles and light trucks.
- The company will invest over $138 million in the project.
- New jobs will include positions in production, support and administration.
