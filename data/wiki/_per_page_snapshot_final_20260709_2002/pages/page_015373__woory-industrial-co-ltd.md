- Woory Industrial Co., Ltd. manufactures and sells automotive parts and components for IC and electric engines in South Korea and internationally.
- The company offers actuators, including HVAC, AAF, HLLD, swivel, and bi-function actuators.
- The company offers heaters, such as high and low voltage PTC, coolant, and COD heaters.
- The company offers control heads and sensors, including power module, blower resistor, PAS, and seat belt reminder sensors.
- The company provides valves, which includes coolant and expansion valves.
- The company provides motor on drum and cable puller electric parking brake components.
- The company provides clutch coils and intergrated control units.
- Woory Industrial Co., Ltd. was incorporated in 1989.
- Woory Industrial Co., Ltd. is headquartered in Yongin-si, South Korea.
