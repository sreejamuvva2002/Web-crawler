- The company offers specialized services including EV charger networks for apartments or businesses.
- Ozburn Electrical handles smart electric vehicle (EV) charging station installations, site design, and installation services for businesses, apartment complexes, or retail locations.

- Ozburn Electric offers upgrades to Level 2 EV Chargers or Level 3 (Fast DC) EV Chargers.
