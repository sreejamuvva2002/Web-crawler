## Facts

- Hyundai recorded 901,686 in total domestic sales in 2025.
- Ira Gabriel holds the title of senior group manager for Hyundai Motor Company.
- Ira Gabriel stated that Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America will help the company reach a target goal to produce more than 80% of its vehicles sold in the United States domestically by 2030.
- The current domestic production percentage stands at 40%.
- Ira Gabriel described the facility as the largest economic development project in Georgia’s history.
- The facility is engineered to scale to 500,000 units by 2028.
- The facility's capacity supports the production of Hyundai, Genesis and Kia EV and hybrid vehicles.
- Localized manufacturing enhances availability, affordability and supply-chain resilience, which are key enablers of Hyundai's 2026 and long-term sales ambitions.
