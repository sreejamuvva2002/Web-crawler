## Facts

- Founded in 1952, the Cumming-Forsyth County Chamber of Commerce serves as the community’s leading advocate and champion for business.
- The chamber's primary objective is to create a climate of growth and success by leveraging member support, talent, and resources to improve economic vitality and quality of life in Cumming and Forsyth County.
- Cumming-Forsyth County operates as a hub for global commerce and international trade, hosting more than seventy international companies.
- Premier corporate sponsors and prestige members include Georgia Power Company, Lou Sobh Honda, Forsyth County Government, City of Cumming, Kaiser Permanente, Digitel Corporation / JTECH Networks, Children's Healthcare of Atlanta, DeKalb Office, Business Accounting & Tax Services, LLC, Atlanta Landscape Group, Alpharetta Children's Dentistry & Orthodontics, and Taubman.
