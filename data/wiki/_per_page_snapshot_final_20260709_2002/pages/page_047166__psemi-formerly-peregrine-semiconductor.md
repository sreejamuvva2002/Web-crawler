# pSemi (formerly Peregrine Semiconductor)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-18

**Source:** [pSemi, a Murata Company, Takes Power Conversion to the Next Level at APEC 2025](https://psemi.com/2024/06/17/psemi-takes-power-conversion-to-the-next-level-at-apec-2025/)  ·  `page_047166`

## Facts

- pSemi showcases the world’s first 4-level buck converter for battery charging applications alongside new charge pump and two-stage step-down DC-DC converters.
- The 4-level buck converter enables higher input voltages in 4-level buck mode and mid-to-low input voltages in 3-level buck mode.
- The device can be operated in fixed ratio, capacitor divider mode with divider ratios 2 and 3 when the input voltage is a programmable power source (PPS).
- Current delivery for the 4-level buck converter is up to 6A per device, with the option to parallel devices to achieve faster charging times across all operation modes using a 1 mm height inductor.
- This design allows systems to achieve equivalent power delivery and faster battery charge times regardless of the type of USB_PD, PPS, or Qi wireless charging source.
- pSemi will showcase these breakthroughs during the 2025 Applied Power Electronics Conference (APEC) on March 16-20, 2025, at the Georgia World Congress Center in Atlanta at Murata Electronics’ Exhibit booth #915.
- Laurence McGarry, director product line manager at pSemi, will present details regarding the world’s first 4-level buck converter during a presentation at APEC on March 18, 2025, from 3:45 p.m. ET – 4:15 p.m. ET in Expo Theatre 4.
- pSemi announced the PE24111, a 3.3Vin two-stage regulator capable of supplying up to 20A, ahead of the APEC Conference.
- The PE24111 targets applications requiring high efficiency in a low-profile form-factor under 1.2mm and consists of a two-phase interleaved charge pump followed by an interleaved buck regulator stage.
- This power system greatly reduces dependency on inductance for high efficiency solutions in small-footprint and height-constrained form-factors.
- The PE24111 output voltage can be adjusted between 0.35 and 0.85V using external feedback resistors or an external DAC.
- The PE24111 is ideal for step-down DC-DC converter solutions for low-profile point-of-load regulators, high-density optical transceiver modules, core supplies, ASICs, and FPGAs.
- To achieve higher power delivery, the PE24111 can be connected in parallel up to four devices with synchronization capability.
- Tatsuya Kubo, senior product marketing manager at pSemi, will highlight the PE24111 during a presentation entitled “Advances in 2-stage Buck Regulator Bring Higher Power Delivery to Optical Transceivers” at APEC on March 19, 2025, from 12:45 p.m. ET – 1:15 p.m. ET in Expo Theatre 3.
- The PE25213 is an ultra-high efficiency charge pump configurable to divide down an input voltage by two or three and delivers up to 10A with peak efficiency up to 99%.
- The PE25213 uses pSemi’s patented adiabatic or lossless switching architecture to maximize efficiency and reduce solution size.
- The PE25213 supports an input voltage range of 5.7V to 15V in divide-by-2 mode and 8.4V to 15V in divide-by-3 mode.
- The PE25213 is primarily used as a front-end converter to convert a two- or three-cell battery input to a 1-1.5S output for downstream regulators to improve overall system efficiency and extend run time.
- The PE25213 offers a unique auto-switch mode to change the divide-down ratio during operation to avoid a downstream under-voltage lockout event at heavy system loading during low battery conditions.
- The PE25213 comes in a 4.545 mm × 2.715 mm 47-pin WLCSP package with a pinout designed to be fully compatible with Type III PCB design to reduce system cost.
- The PE25213 targets 2 and 3S battery input systems such as laptops and 12V input systems such as datacenter and networking applications.
- The PE24111 and PE25213 are available for sampling now and will be demonstrated alongside the latest innovations in multi-level technology at APEC in Atlanta, Georgia, on March 16 – 19, 2025.
- pSemi is a leader in semiconductor integration providing high-performance RF, analog and mixed signal solutions with a 30-year legacy of technology advancements.
- pSemi’s solution portfolio spans power management, connected sensors, antenna tuning, and RF frontends which enable advanced modules for smartphones, base stations, personal computers, electric vehicles, data centers, IoT devices, healthcare and more.
- pSemi is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Murata Electronics North America, Inc., headquartered in San Diego with additional offices spanning North America, Europe, and Asia.
