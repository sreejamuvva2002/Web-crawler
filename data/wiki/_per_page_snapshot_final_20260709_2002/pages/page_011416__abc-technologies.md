# ABC Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-19

**Source:** [Key Leadership Appointments Announced For Combined ABC Technologies & TI Fluid Systems Group - ABC Technologies](https://abctechnologies.ca/key-leadership-appointments-announced-for-combined-abc-technologies-ti-fluid-systems-group/)  ·  `page_011416`

## Facts

- Terry Campbell will become President & Chief Executive Officer of the combined ABC Technologies and TI Fluid Systems group following the acquisition.
- Alexander De Bock will become Chief Financial Officer of the combined group.
- Hans Dieltjens, current President & Chief Executive Officer of TI Fluid Systems, will step down following completion of the acquisition.
- ABC Technologies' acquisition of TI Fluid Systems remains on track to complete during the first half of 2025.
- Terry Campbell became President & CEO of ABC in 2022 after previously holding senior leadership roles at Johnson Controls, Magna International, and The Woodbridge Group.
- Campbell brings over thirty years of experience in the global automotive sector to his new role.
- Alexander De Bock was appointed CFO of TIFS in 2023 after spending over 15 years in the industry, including finance leadership roles at WABCO and ZF Group.
- The acquisition will be implemented by way of a court-sanctioned scheme of arrangement under Part 26 of the Companies Act 2006.
- TIFS shareholders approved the recommended all cash offer from ABC Technologies Acquisitions Limited on 5 February 2025.
- The acquisition is expected to complete in the first half of 2025 following receipt of remaining regulatory and judicial clearances.
- TI Fluid Systems will announce the anticipated completion timetable through a Regulatory Information Service and its website.
- ABC Technologies is a leading global manufacturer and supplier of custom, highly engineered technical plastics and lightweight innovations to the North American light vehicle industry.
- ABC serves more than 25 major original equipment manufacturer customers across 8 countries.
- ABC operates with a skilled workforce of over 11,000 team members.
- ABC Technologies is majority owned by affiliated funds of Apollo Global Management, Inc., with Oaktree Capital Management owning a minority equity interest.
- TI Fluid Systems is a global innovator of thermal and fluid systems solutions for the full range of current and developing vehicle architectures.
- TIFS has more than 100 years of automotive supply experience and operates across 26 countries.
- The combination of ABC and TIFS aims to create a larger, diversified group better positioned to navigate the transition to electrification and deliver sustainable growth.
