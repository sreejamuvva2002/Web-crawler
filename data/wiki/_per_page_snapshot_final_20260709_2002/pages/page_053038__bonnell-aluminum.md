# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-01

**Source:** [Bonnell Aluminum names Tracy Bridges VP of sales and marketing By Investing.com](https://in.investing.com/news/company-news/bonnell-aluminum-names-tracy-bridges-vp-of-sales-and-marketing-93CH-5256889)  ·  `page_053038`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum, a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation (NYSE:TG), announced the appointment of Tracy Bridges as Vice President of Sales and Marketing, effective March 1, 2026.
- The leadership transition replaces Eric Yost, who is retiring after 28 years with the company.
- Tracy Bridges has been with Bonnell Aluminum for more than 20 years, most recently serving as Director of Customer Experience.
- Bridges' previous positions include Regional Sales Manager, Segment Sales Manager, and Market Manager.
- Brook Hamilton, President of Bonnell Aluminum, stated the company sought a successor who would support the business and customers while building on the commercial foundation established over 70 years.
- Bonnell Aluminum manufactures custom-fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions for the automotive and transportation markets, as well as the building and construction, consumer durables, machinery and equipment, and electrical and distribution markets.
- The company operates manufacturing facilities in Newnan, Georgia; Carthage, Tennessee; Niles, Michigan; Elkhart, Indiana; and Clearfield, Utah.
- Parent company Tredegar Corporation is valued at $301 million.
- Tredegar Corporation generated $691 million in revenue over the last twelve months.
- Tredegar Corporation is set to report earnings on March 12.
