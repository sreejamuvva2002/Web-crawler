# Daesol Ausys Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-08-29

**Source:** [Daesol Ausys Georgia to open new facility in West Point, GA | Columbus Ledger-Enquirer](https://www.ledger-enquirer.com/news/business/article278734114.html)  ·  `page_019007`

## Facts

- A $72 million auto parts plant project will be built in Harris County.
- The project will create more than 140 new jobs in the Columbus area.
- The manufacturing facility is located in West Point, Georgia.
- Daesol Ausys Georgia will join the local automotive industry alongside Kia Motors, Adient, Daehan Solutions, Daesol Materials, Kopla, and Hyundai Glovis.
- Kia Motors opened its only U.S. manufacturing plant in the area in 2010.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that West Point has become an automotive capital in its own right.
- Georgia's automotive industry currently employs over 50,000 Georgians and will continue to grow as e-mobility suppliers choose locations across the state.
- Daesol Ausys is a South Korean company established in 2017.
- Daesol Ausys designs and manufactures motor and electric vehicle interior components and accessories for carmakers such as Kia, Hyundai, and General Motors.
- CEO Min Ho Kwon stated the company is committed to delivering world-class services that exceed client requirements.
- The company expressed excitement about the project in the Northwest Harris Business Park and looks forward to continuing its partnership with the State of Georgia and Harris County.
- The new facility is expected to open in December 2024.
- At the new facility, Daesol Ausys will produce luggage boards and luggage covers.
- The company plans to hire personnel for roles in production, purchasing, quality control, maintenance, human resources, and management.
- Robert Grant, chairman of the Harris County Commission, stated that Daesol Ausys' decision to locate in the Northwest Harris Business Park represents a commitment to Harris County.
- West Point and Harris County have a strong relationship with Daesol Ausys' sister companies Daehan Solutions and Daesol Materials.
- Local officials have been working closely with company officials to establish this new venture in West Point.
- Craig Greenhaw, chairman of the Harris County Development Authority, welcomed Daesol Ausys as a new corporate citizen and noted the venture will bring jobs and capital investment into the region.
