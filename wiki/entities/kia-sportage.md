# Kia Sportage

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Georgia, United States

## Overview

The Kia Sportage is a compact crossover SUV produced by Kia since 1993, spanning five generations and sold worldwide.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Locally assembled in West Point, Georgia, the fifth-generation Sportage adds hybrid powertrains that support electric-vehicle market growth in the state.

## Timeline

- **July 1993** — First-generation Kia Sportage production begins (Hwaseong Plant, South Korea).
- **1995-1998** — Sportage built by Karmann in Osnabrück, Germany for European markets.
- **1997** — First-generation Sportage becomes the world’s first production vehicle equipped with a knee airbag.
- **2004 - February 2010** — Second-generation Sportage production period.
- **May 2008** — Facelift introduced for second generation.
- **2006** — Manufacturing of second-generation Sportage moves to Žilina Plant, Slovakia.
- **March 2010 - August 2015** — Third-generation Sportage production period.
- **April 2010** — Third-generation Sportage released to Asian and European markets.
- **August 2010** — Third-generation Sportage launched in North and Central America.
- **October 2010** — Australian market launch of third generation.
- **2011** — Sportage wins Car of the Year (Slovakia).
- **September 2015 - 2021** — Fourth-generation Sportage production period.
- **September 2015** — Fourth generation unveiled at Frankfurt Motor Show.
- **June 8 2021** — Fifth-generation Sportage unveiled.
- **July 2021** — Specifications for fifth generation released.
- **October 2021** — Long-wheelbase, U.S. version of fifth-generation Sportage announced; production to be in West Point, Georgia.
- **December 4 2024** — Fifth-generation Sportage launched in Malaysia (Inokom plant).
- **January 29 2025** — Sportage launched in Pakistan (long-wheelbase version).
- **June 8 2022** — Fifth-generation Sportage launched in Taiwan.
- **June 10 2022** — Sportage launched in Vietnam.
- **July 5 2024** — Sportage facelift released (global).

## Key facts

- Best-selling Kia model worldwide since 2016, surpassing the Rio.
- Reached a 5 million production milestone in 2018.
- First production vehicle equipped with a knee airbag (1997 model year).
- Hybrid and plug-in hybrid powertrains are offered on recent generations, supporting EV adoption.
- The fifth-generation Sportage is assembled in West Point, Georgia, creating local manufacturing jobs and supply-chain links for the U.S. EV market.
- Multiple safety agencies (NHTSA, IIHS, Euro NCAP, ANCAP, Latin NCAP) have awarded five-star ratings to various generations.
- Received numerous awards including 2022 Scottish Car of the Year (Family SUV & Full Hybrid categories) and 2023 Car of the Year in Morocco.
