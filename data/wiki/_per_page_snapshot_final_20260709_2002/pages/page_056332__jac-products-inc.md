# JAC Products Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2008-04-16

**Source:** [Automotive Exterior Trim Manufacturer JAC Products Announces Plant
    Consolidation Plan](https://www.theautochannel.com/news/2008/04/16/084344.html)  ·  `page_056332`

## Facts

- Steve Morrey, President and CEO of JAC Products, a leading designer and manufacturer of functional automotive exterior trim, announced the consolidation of manufacturing operations to improve productivity for the competitive automotive industry.
- Manufacturing programs from the Monclova, Ohio plant will be transferred in an orderly fashion to other JAC Products facilities.
- The company will close the Monclova plant during the spring of 2009.
- It is estimated that 120 positions will move to the company's facilities in either Georgia or Michigan.
- JAC Products' manufacturing operations in Europe will not be affected by this consolidation.
- Steve Morrey stated that the consolidation is part of ongoing efforts to continually improve productivity, a prerequisite for success within the global automotive industry and a mandate at JAC Products.
- Product innovation is a priority at JAC Products, which recently launched production of new cargo management systems for pickup trucks.
- Founded in 1967, JAC Products Inc. is a leading designer and manufacturer of original equipment roof racks, functional exterior trim, and cargo management systems supplying vehicles worldwide.
- Owned by Citi Group, JAC Products operates as a full-service global supplier with manufacturing at ISO/TS Registered locations in the U.S., Germany, and Portugal.
