# Giga America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-06

**Source:** [FREYR Battery ($FREY) Releases Plans to Build Clean Battery Gigafactory in Georgia - Green Stock News](https://greenstocknews.com/newswire/freyr-battery-frey-releases-plans-to-build-clean-battery-gigafactory-in-georgia)  ·  `page_001618`

## Facts

- FREYR Battery (NYSE: FREY) is a developer of next-generation battery cell production capacity.
- FREYR Battery announced the purchase of a site in Coweta County, Georgia for its planned Giga America battery plant.
- The company selected the Bridgeport Industrial Park site based on key operational, logistical, and financial criteria.
- The site is suitable to co-locate modular upstream and downstream projects with the Giga America cell manufacturing facility.
- The initial phase of the Giga America factory will include a cell production module of approximately 34 GWh.
- The cell production module will be based on the next-generation of 24M Technologies U.S.-based SemiSolid™ production platform.
- FREYR is evaluating value accretive upstream and downstream modules for the project.
- FREYR is evaluating additional cell production lines for the project.
- Total capital investments are expected to bring more than $2.6 billion through 2029.
- FREYR’s Co-Founder and CEO Tom Einar Jensen stated that the announcement underscores FREYR’s ambition to develop a very strong and near-term operational footprint in the United States.
- Tom Einar Jensen stated that expanding into the U.S. has been a foundational aspect of FREYR’s long-term strategy from its inception.
- Tom Einar Jensen stated that with the recent passage of the Inflation Reduction Act, FREYR expects U.S. demand for ESS, passenger EV and other electric mobility applications to grow rapidly over the next decade.
- The U.S. energy storage market is already the largest in the world.
- The U.S. energy storage market is expected to experience exponential growth over the next several years.
- The U.S. energy storage market could reach as much as 1.4 TWh by 2030.
- FREYR is evaluating clean power supply solutions for the Giga America project with U.S. independent power producers.
- FREYR’s long-term ambition is to replicate the strategy employed at Giga Arctic.
- At Giga Arctic, FREYR plans to produce next-generation batteries that are among the cleanest anywhere in the world.
- Shares of FREYR Battery trade on the NYSE under the symbol FREY.
