## Facts

- Constellium is a world leader in transforming aluminum into innovative, lightweight products primarily for the aerospace, packaging, and automotive markets.
- The company operates across North America, Europe, and Asia.
- Constellium supplies aluminum plates, sheets, and extrusions to commercial, military, and space markets for global aerospace manufacturers.
- Constellium is a world-leading supplier for the aluminum packaging market, designing, producing, and recycling aluminum coils and sheets for beverage and food cans, wine and spirits closures, and personal care products.
- The company provides advanced aluminum rolled products and extrusion-based components to global automotive manufacturers to enable production of vehicles that are lighter, safer, more fuel-efficient, and have greater range.
- Constellium develops specialty aluminum alloys for extrusions, sheets, plates, and semi-finished components used by the transportation and industry markets.
- In the defense market, Constellium's advanced alloys offer outstanding impact resistance for armored vehicles, turrets, and military bridges.
- Innovation is a driving force for the company, exploring ways aluminum can offer improved performance, enhanced manufacturability, and efficiency while minimizing its overall footprint.
- The company promotes collaborative research among its teams, joint development with customers, and partnerships with universities, labs, and scientific organizations worldwide.
- Constellium supports the circular economy of tomorrow while prioritizing the safety and well-being of employees and communities today.
- Aluminum increases efficiency by reducing weight and can be recycled without losing its properties.
- The company tracks its sustainability performance each year and transparently discloses it with independent verification.
- Constellium (NYSE:CSTM) is a global sector leader that develops innovative, value-added aluminum products for a broad scope of markets including aerospace, automotive, and packaging.
- Constellium generated $7.3 billion of revenue in 2024.
