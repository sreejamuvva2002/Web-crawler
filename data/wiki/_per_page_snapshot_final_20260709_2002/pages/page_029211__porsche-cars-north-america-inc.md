## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, since 1998.
- PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche Taycan electric vehicle, along with the 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, and Panamera.
- PCNA operates the first Porsche Experience Center in North America located in Atlanta.
- The Atlanta Porsche Experience Center features a module-based 1.6-mile driver development track, a business center, and a fine dining restaurant named 356.
- PCNA supports 193 independently owned and operated Porsche dealerships across the United States.
- PCNA supplies parts, service, marketing, and training to its network of U.S. dealerships.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- PCNA's Atlanta headquarters address is One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA.
