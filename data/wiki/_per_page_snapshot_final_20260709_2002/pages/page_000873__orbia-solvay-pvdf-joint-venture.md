# Orbia-Solvay PVDF Joint Venture

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-02

**Source:** [Orbia and Solvay Finalize Joint Venture Agreement to Build PVDF Production Capacity in North America for Lithium-ion Batteries  | Orbia](https://www.orbia.com/this-is-orbia/news-and-stories/Orbia-and-Solvay-Finalize-Joint-Venture-Agreement-to-Build-PVDF-Production-Capacity-in-North-America-for-Lithium-ion-Batteries/)  ·  `page_000873`

## Facts

- Orbia’s Fluorinated Solutions business and Solvay signed a joint venture agreement on November 2, 2023.
- The joint venture aims to create the largest polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF) production facilities for battery materials in North America.
- The partnership provides security of supply for Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) compliant materials.
- The partnership combines IRA-compliant materials with leading-edge PVDF technology for solutions critical to the North American EV transition.
- The joint venture addresses considerable growth in demand for lithium-ion batteries driven by the U.S. EV and stationary energy storage market.
- The partnership aims to establish a robust, secure, and local supply chain for lithium-ion batteries.
- The partnership secures the supply of critical minerals and intermediate materials from Orbia.
- Solvay will manufacture suspension-grade PVDF using Orbia's supplied materials.
- Suspension-grade PVDF serves as a lithium-ion binder and separator coating in electric vehicle batteries.
- Solvay contributes process technology and global market know-how to the joint venture.
- The joint venture combines Solvay’s Solef® PVDF innovations with Orbia’s material assets and production expertise.
- The delivered PVDF will allow electric vehicles to travel further on each charge.
- The delivered PVDF will extend battery life.
- The delivered PVDF will improve battery safety.
- Ilham Kadri, CEO of Solvay, stated the project is a key milestone in Solvay's electrification strategy and emphasizes its global commitment to sustainable mobility.
- Ilham Kadri stated the partnership positions Solvay to shore up an independent, sustainable EV battery supply chain in North America.
- The project aims to create clean energy jobs.
- Sameer Bharadwaj, CEO of Orbia, stated the partnership underscores Orbia's commitment to enabling the clean energy transition through investments in energy materials.
- Sameer Bharadwaj stated Orbia has a unique 'mine-to-market' position with integration in key battery materials.
- These integrated materials are needed to bolster North America's EV supply chain and ensure IRA compliance.
- The joint venture will utilize two production sites.
- One site will be located in Augusta, Georgia, for finished products.
- The other site will be located in St. Gabriel, Louisiana, for raw material conversion to needed intermediates.
- Both production plants are expected to become operational in 2026.
- Orbia's participation in the joint venture reinforces its strategic position as a key supplier of battery materials.
- Orbia supplies battery materials including electrolyte salts such as LiPF6.
- Orbia supplies custom electrolyte formulations.
- Orbia supplies specialty battery additives.
- Orbia supplies recycled anodes.
