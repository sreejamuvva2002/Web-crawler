# Seoyon E-HWA

**Source:** [Auto parts manufacturer breaks ground on $76 million facility in Chatham County](https://fox28savannah.com/news/local/auto-parts-manufacturing-facility-chatham-county-savannah-investment-jobs-georgia-economy-production-governor-brian-kemp-cars)  ·  `page_059516`

## Facts

- Auto parts manufacturer and Hyundai supplier broke ground on a $76 million facility in Chatham County on Friday.
- Seoyon E-HWA broke ground at the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- The new facility will create 740 new direct and indirect jobs.
- Officials expect production at the facility to start in October 2024.
- Governor Brian Kemp said in a Tweet Monday morning: "We're excited for this new chapter for Seoyon E-HWA and the Savannah-Chatham community as Georgia continues to see more jobs and investment come to all parts of our state."
- More than two-thirds, or around 500, of the announced jobs will be permanent roles in human resources, development, quality and production personnel.
