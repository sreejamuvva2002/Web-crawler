## Facts

- Pirelli has secured US approval to manufacture its connected Cyber Tyre technology at its Georgia plant.
- Chinese firm Sinochem owns 34.1 percent of Pirelli.
- The US almost blocked the project over China concerns.
- Italy intervened using ‘golden powers’ restrictions on Sinochem’s influence to enable the manufacturing approval.
- The Italian intervention offsets US tariff impacts on the project.
