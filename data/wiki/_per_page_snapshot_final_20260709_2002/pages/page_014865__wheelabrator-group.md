## Facts

- Founded in Pittsburgh, Pa. in 1908 by Verne E. Minich.
- Moved to LaGrange, Georgia, USA, from Mishawaka, Ind., in 1987.
- Is a global provider of surface preparation and finishing solutions.
- Celebrated its 100th anniversary in 2008.
- Electronics Inc. was founded in 1974 in Mishawaka, Indiana, by Jack Champaigne with products for the air pollution control industry.
- Electronics Inc. has been associated with Wheelabrator for many years.
- In 1978, Electronics Inc. furnished shot flow measurement and MagnaValve controls to Wheelabrator Corporation on a project for Boeing Commercial Airplane Co. in Seattle, Washington.
- Jack Champaigne serves as President of Electronics Inc.
- Jack Champaigne is the editor of this historical perspective of Wheelabrator.
- The pages offer copies of the Wheelabrator Parade and Wheelagram Newsletters published for employees during the Mishawaka, Indiana, years.
