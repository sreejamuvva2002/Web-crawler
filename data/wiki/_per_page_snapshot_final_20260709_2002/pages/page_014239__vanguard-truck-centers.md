# Vanguard Truck Centers

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Vanguard Truck Centers Acquires Nacarato Truck Centers, Becoming One of Volvo Trucks’ Largest Dealer Groups | Volvo Trucks USA](https://www.volvotrucks.us/news-and-stories/press-releases/2024/january/vanguard-truck-centers-acquires-nacarato-truck-centers-becoming-one-of-volvo-trucks-largest-dealer-groups/)  ·  `page_014239`

## Facts

- Vanguard Truck Centers acquired Nacarato Truck Centers to become one of Volvo Trucks’ largest dealer groups in North America.
- The acquisition expands Vanguard’s presence to 28 Volvo full-service and parts-and-service-only locations across 11 states: Arizona, Florida, Georgia, Kentucky, Maryland, Missouri, North Carolina, South Carolina, Tennessee, Texas, and Virginia.
- Vanguard assumed full ownership of the Nacarato locations, including all existing stock and parts.
- Peter Voorhoeve serves as president of Volvo Trucks North America.
- Nacarato Truck Centers was founded in 1976 in Nashville, Tennessee.
- Prior to the acquisition, Nacarato operated a network of nine Volvo Trucks dealerships and service centers throughout Tennessee, Florida, Georgia, Kentucky, Maryland, and Virginia.
- Tom Ewing is president and chief executive officer of Vanguard Truck Centers.
- Mike Nacarato is chief executive officer of Nacarato Truck Centers and will continue to lead the center under its existing brand following the acquisition.
- Will Blue serves as chief financial officer of Vanguard Truck Centers.
- Lloyd Baldridge serves as chief financial officer of Nacarato Truck Centers.
- Pat Daily serves as chief operating officer of Nacarato Truck Centers.
- Both dealership groups invested in required training and certification programs to become Volvo Trucks Certified Electric Vehicle Dealers, recognizing battery-electric trucks as important to the future of trucking.
- Vanguard’s sales and service teams are fully trained to support customers’ Volvo VNR Electric deployments at five locations: Phoenix, Arizona; Atlanta, Georgia (Forest Park); Charlotte, North Carolina; and Austin and Houston, Texas.
- The acquisition adds three additional Volvo Trucks Certified EV Dealer locations in Roanoke, Virginia; La Vergne, Tennessee (serving the Nashville area); and Hagerstown, Maryland.
- The acquisition of Nacarato Truck Centers was completed on January 8, 2024.
- Vanguard now employs more than 1,200 people following the acquisition.
- All 28 Volvo Trucks dealership locations are Certified Uptime Dealers, providing customers with access to almost 500 service bays for preventative service and maintenance.
- Vanguard Truck Centers operates 39 full-service commercial truck dealerships and service centers in the United States across Arizona, Florida, Georgia, Illinois, Kentucky, Maryland, Missouri, North Carolina, South Carolina, Tennessee, Texas, and Virginia.
- Volvo Trucks North America is headquartered in Greensboro, North Carolina.
- Every Volvo truck is assembled at the Volvo Trucks New River Valley manufacturing facility in Dublin, Virginia.
- The Dublin, Virginia manufacturing facility meets ISO 9001 standards for quality, ISO 14001 standards for environmental care, and holds dual ISO 50001/Superior Energy Performance certification at the platinum level.
- Volvo Trucks North America provides a full range of diesel, alternative-fuel, and all-electric vehicles as part of its complete transport solutions.
- Approximately 120,000 Volvo trucks were delivered worldwide in 2025.
- Volvo Trucks is part of the Volvo Group, which manufactures trucks, buses, construction equipment, and marine and industrial engines globally.
