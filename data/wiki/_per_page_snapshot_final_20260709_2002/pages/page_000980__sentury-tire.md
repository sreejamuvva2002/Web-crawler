# Sentury Tire

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-09-09

**Source:** [Sentury Opening Plant in Georgia - Tire Review Magazine](https://www.tirereview.com/sentury-opening-plant-in-georgia/)  ·  `page_000980`

## Facts

- Sentury Tire is opening a $530 million manufacturing facility in LaGrange, Ga.
- Rami Helminen serves as Sentury Tire executive vice president.
- Rami Helminen stated the company selected LaGrange for its North American manufacturing location.
- Rami Helminen cited education systems and a high quality workforce complementing leading edge technology in production and research and development.
- Rami Helminen expressed confidence that Georgia has infrastructure to support a successful logistical operation.
- The facility will supply growing aftermarket dealer sales and OEM sales to auto manufacturers in the North American market.
- Sentury anticipates starting production at the LaGrange facility in 2018.
- The facility will eventually provide 12 million passenger and light truck tires annually.
- The new facility will create roughly 1,000 jobs.
- Georgia Gov. Nathan Deal commented on Sentury Tire’s commitment to locate in LaGrange.
- Gov. Nathan Deal stated the commitment speaks to Georgia’s business-friendly climate and the strong relationship maintained with economic partners in China.
- Gov. Nathan Deal valued Sentury Tire’s investment in Georgia and the vision of the company’s leadership for continued growth in Troup County.
- Gov. Nathan Deal expressed positivity that Georgia’s automotive manufacturing and technology capabilities will be assets to Sentury Tire as it serves a growing customer base.
- Sentury Tire is headquartered in Qingdao, Shandong, China.
- The tiremaker has facilities in both China and Thailand.
- This LaGrange location will be Sentury’s first U.S. plant.
