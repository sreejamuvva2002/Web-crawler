# Daimler Truck North America

## Facts

- Daimler Truck North America (DTNA) is working with its transport provider Salem Carriers to deliver inbound parts on electric trucks in the US Carolinas.
- Salem Carriers transports parts between DTNA’s manufacturing sites, supply chain hubs, and supplier locations across the two states.
- Salem Carriers is using DTNA’s Freightliner eCascadia trucks on daily inbound routes.
- Operations are supported by a partnership with fleet electrification supplier Electrada for the development, operation, and maintenance of necessary charging infrastructure.
- The partnership is backed by Daimler Truck Financial Services (DTFS) to provide charging-as-a-service (CaaS) with a fixed and predictable monthly rate.
- Dennis Giff, general manager at Salem Carriers, stated that introducing battery electric vehicles to their operations was a new initiative.
- Dennis Giff noted the DTFS/Electrada approach was unified, aligned with operating needs, and set the stage to scale future electric vehicle integration into their services.
