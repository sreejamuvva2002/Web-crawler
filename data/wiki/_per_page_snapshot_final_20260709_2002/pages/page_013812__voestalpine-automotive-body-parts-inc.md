# voestalpine Automotive Body Parts, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-03

**Source:** [Parker Poe Consulting Assists voestalpine Group with Alabama Expansion  |  Parker Poe Consulting - Location Solutions](https://ppclocationsolutions.com/news/2017/03/parker-poe-consulting-assists-voestalpine-group-with-alabama)  ·  `page_013812`

## Facts

- Site selection professionals with Parker Poe Consulting (PPC) have helped Austria’s voestalpine Automotive Body Parts, Inc. move forward on plans to expand its Georgia operations and lease a new facility in Alabama.
- A $50 million expansion is located at the company's current production facility in Cartersville, GA.
- The Georgia expansion will create 150 new jobs.
- The expansion extends the company's ability to provide critical components to premium car manufacturers in the Southeast.
- voestalpine expects to begin production in September at a new plant in Birmingham, Ala.
- The Alabama facility is a new 52,360-square-foot plant.
- The Birmingham facility involves an $11 million investment in facility upfit and associated machinery.
- The Birmingham facility will operate four assembly lines.
- The Birmingham facility will create 42 jobs within the next five years.
- voestalpine serves as a leading-edge supplier to top automakers, including BMW and Mercedes-Benz.
- BMW and Mercedes-Benz have made tremendous investments in assembly plants in the Southeastern United States.
