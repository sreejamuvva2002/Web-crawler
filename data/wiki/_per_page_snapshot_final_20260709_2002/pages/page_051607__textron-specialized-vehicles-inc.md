# Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Textron Specialized Vehicles executive receives Georgia Manufacturing Extension Partnership’s 2017 Faces of Manufacturing Award - Enterprise Innovation Institute](https://innovate.gatech.edu/textron-specialized-vehicles-executive-receives-georgia-manufacturing-extension-partnerships-2017-faces-manufacturing-award/)  ·  `page_051607`

## Facts

- Jason Alford, vice president of Integrated Supply Chain for Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. in Augusta, is one of four Georgians to receive the 2017 Faces of Manufacturing Award.
- The Georgia Manufacturing Extension Partnership (GaMEP) named Jason Alford a 2017 Faces of Manufacturing Award recipient.
- Alford has been with Textron for 14 years and accepted his award on Oct. 10 at a special ceremony at the company’s Augusta facility.
- Jason Alford stated he credited hundreds of talented, dedicated TSV employees who build vehicles and equipment to serve customers around the world.
- GaMEP is a federally funded economic development program of the Georgia Institute of Technology’s Enterprise Innovation Institute that works with manufacturers across the state to help them remain viable and economically competitive.
- More than 10,000 manufacturing companies operate in Georgia.
- These companies employ more than 365,000 people and produce a total manufacturing output of $53 billion per year.
- The Faces campaign showcases the sector’s importance to Georgia’s economy.
- Alford and the other three award recipients were chosen through a public voting contest consisting of 10 finalists selected from an initial pool of nominations, with more than 4,500 votes cast resulting in Alford emerging as one of the top four.
- Kevin Holleran, president and CEO of Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., stated that Jason’s keen intellect, analytical mindset and tireless work ethic are matched only by his devotion to the company and employees.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles is a Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT) company that designs and manufactures vehicles and equipment under several well-known brands.
- TSV products include E-Z-GO golf cars, Arctic Cat snowmobiles, Cushman commercial utility vehicles, Textron Off Road side-by-sides and ATVs, Jacobsen professional turf-care equipment, and Dixie Chopper zero-turn mowers.
- The company manufactures ground support equipment for the aviation industry under its Textron GSE umbrella.
- TSV employs about 1,400 people in Georgia and more than 4,200 worldwide.
- Alford began at Textron as director of financial planning and analysis before discovering a natural talent for manufacturing integration.
- Alford spearheaded Textron Specialized Vehicles’ partnership with the Richmond County School System to create the Reaching Potential Through Manufacturing (RPM) program.
- The RPM program, now in its second year, provides high school students at risk of dropping out with an alternative path to graduation while earning manufacturing work experience building components for TSV products.
- Alford drew on his own lessons learned from persevering during recovery from a motorcycle accident that left him wheelchair-bound as inspiration for the RPM program.
- GaMEP Director Karen J. Fite stated that Jason’s commitment and success at Textron, along with his dedication to inspiring the industry’s next generation workforce, reflect the caliber of people in Georgia manufacturing.
- The Faces of Manufacturing in Georgia campaign is an initiative of the Georgia Manufacturing Extension Partnership that honors people who work in or are affected by manufacturing in Georgia.
- GaMEP has been serving Georgia manufacturers since 1960 and offers a solution-based approach through coaching and education designed to increase top line growth and reduce bottom line cost.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles markets products under the E-Z-GO®, Cushman®, Arctic Cat®, Textron Off Road™, Jacobsen®, Dixie Chopper®, Ransomes®, TUG™, Douglas™, Premier™ and Safeaero™ brands.
- TSV vehicles are found in environments ranging from golf courses to factories, airports to planned communities, and theme parks to hunting preserves.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company that leverages its global network of aircraft, defense, industrial and finance businesses to provide customers with innovative solutions and services.
