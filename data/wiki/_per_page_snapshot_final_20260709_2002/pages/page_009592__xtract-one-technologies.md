# Xtract One Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-09

**Source:** [Xtract One Technologies Selected by Hyundai Transys to Secure Facilities and Keep Weapons Out - Xtract One](https://xtractone.com/corporate-news/xtract-one-technologies-selected-by-hyundai-transys-to-secure-facilities-and-keep-weapons-out/)  ·  `page_009592`

## Facts

- Xtract One Technologies announced its SmartGateway security screening solution was selected to protect entrances at Hyundai Transys in West Point, Ga.
- The SmartGateway will be deployed at the manufacturing plant to protect workers and staff from weapons entering the buildings and to prevent mass casualty events.
- Jinho Kim, manager of GA & EHS at Hyundai Transys Georgia Powertrain Inc., stated they viewed and tested Xtract One’s Gateway at Kia Georgia Inc., which had great success with keeping weapons out.
- Jinho Kim noted they were impressed by the quick and accurate detection of weapons and felt the customization aspect would fit seamlessly into their manufacturing facility.
- SmartGateway is the latest iteration of Xtract One's Gateway screening solution.
- It is a fast, frictionless entry screener used by manufacturing plants, casinos, and large arenas to unobtrusively detect guns, knives, and other potential weapons on people entering without requiring them to empty pockets of personal items.
- Peter Evans, CEO of Xtract One, stated auto manufacturers, their supply chains, and large workplaces are well suited for the Gateway solution due to high worker throughput during shift changes.
- This marks Xtract One's third customer in the auto manufacturing industry.
- Modern walk-through metal detector systems are inefficient by modern standards, less secure, and more intrusive than the Gateway system.
- SmartGateway uses AI-powered scanners to detect concealed guns and knives and alerts security personnel to conduct a secondary screening.
- If people are not carrying weapons, they simply walk through without needing to empty pockets or other personal articles.
- Upgrades to the system have made SmartGateway very easy to learn and use, meaning it requires fewer personnel to operate relative to other scanning systems.
- The system is customizable to fit the security needs and requirements of individual facilities, including look and feel.
- Ryan Smith, Program Manager – Security at Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc., recommended Xtract One to Hyundai Transys after being pleased with the Gateway solutions at their facilities.
- Since implementing the solution at Kia Georgia, they have prevented a significant number of weapons from entering the facility.
- Personnel screening time was drastically reduced from 21 seconds to 2.5 seconds.
- The time reduction provides production flexibility to maintain work areas during crucial times in an industry based on just-in-time processes.
- Xtract One Technologies is a leading technology-driven threat detection and security solution leveraging AI to provide seamless and secure patron access control experiences.
- The company makes unobtrusive threat detection systems that enable venue building operators to prioritize and deliver improved patron experiences while providing unprecedented safety.
- Xtract One’s multi-sensor Gateway product enables companies to covertly screen for weapons at points of entry without disrupting the flow of traffic.
- AI-based Xtract Vision allows venue and building operators to identify weapons and other threats inside and outside of facilities, and Xtract One Insights provides valuable intelligence for optimizing operations.
