# Georgia Green Energy Services

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Atlanta, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

A locally owned and operated electrical contractor in Atlanta, Georgia that provides residential and commercial electrical services, including EV charging station installations, emergency repairs, and financing options.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Installs electric vehicle (EV) charging stations for homes and businesses throughout Atlanta and surrounding counties.

## Timeline

- **2008** — Georgia Green Energy Services was founded and began serving residential and business customers in Atlanta, Georgia.

## Related organizations & people

- [[qmerit|Qmerit]] — Partner (QMERIT Certified Solutions Partner). Qmerit is a leading expert in electrification; Georgia Green Energy Services is a certified Qmerit partner offering advanced EV charging installations.

## Workforce & partners

- Electrical Savings and Safety Program — Program offered by the company. Provides customers with guidance on electrical safety, energy savings, and financing options.

## Key facts

- Provides 24/7 emergency electrical repair services with fully stocked service vehicles.
- Offers financing options for electrical projects, including EV charger installations.
- Licensed, insured, and vetted electricians ensure compliance with local codes and safety standards.
- Serves a wide geographic area including Atlanta, Athens, Augusta, Columbus, Macon, Savannah, and multiple counties in neighboring states (Alabama, South Carolina).
- Certified QMERIT Partner, meeting the highest standards for electrification installation quality and safety.
- Committed to rigorous professional standards, personal conduct, and ethics for all employees.
