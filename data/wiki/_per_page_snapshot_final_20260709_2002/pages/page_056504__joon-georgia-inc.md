# Joon Georgia Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Automotive Supplier to Bring 317 Manufacturing Jobs to Georgia | ASSEMBLY](https://www.assemblymag.com/articles/97445-automotive-supplier-to-bring-317-manufacturing-jobs-to-georgia)  ·  `page_056504`

## Facts

- Auto parts manufacturer Joon Georgia Inc. is investing $317 million to build a new assembly plant in Statesboro, GA.
- The project is expected to create 317 jobs.
- The factory will be the first confirmed supplier for the Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.
- The Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America is a massive $5-billion electric vehicle assembly plant under construction in nearby Bryan County.
- Operations at the new Joon Georgia plant are planned to begin in mid-2024.
- Jung Ho Sea, CEO of Ajin USA, says the company is excited to be one of the first Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America automotive suppliers to build an automotive parts manufacturing facility in Bulloch County and for its first plant in Georgia.
- Joon Georgia is part of Ajin USA, a supplier for multiple Hyundai Motor Group facilities.
- The company produces a variety of automotive body parts and electronics for automotive OEMs.
