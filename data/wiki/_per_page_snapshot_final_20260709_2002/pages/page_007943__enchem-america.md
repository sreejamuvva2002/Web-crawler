# Enchem America

## Facts

- Korean company Enchem partnered with Carroll Daniel to build its first U.S. location.
- Enchem is a Korean technology leader specializing in the development and manufacturing of electrolytes for rechargeable batteries and electrostatic double-layer capacitors (ELDCs).
- The facility occupies an existing 283-acre complex that includes six buildings.
- Construction on the campus encompassed renovations to the existing 87,500-square-foot Main Accessory Building.
- Construction included building a new three-story process tower.
- Construction included installing a NMP tower containment dike and a tank farm containment dike.
- U.S. codes and standards differed from Korean codes and standards, which was identified as a proposed threat to design, schedule, and overall project success.
- The Carroll Daniel team traveled to Poland and Korea in February 2020 to visit two of Enchem’s facilities to understand requirements and how U.S. codes would affect construction, operation, and production.
- The project was delivered as an award-winning design-build project with a smooth process for the client.
