# RealTruck

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-01

## Facts

- RealTruck is America’s leading truck accessory brand.
- RealTruck released the “Built By American Innovation (A.I.)” campaign to celebrate America's 250th anniversary.
- The campaign highlights people and hands-on work across the U.S., including mechanics, welders, farmers, builders, and makers.
- As part of the campaign, RealTruck profiles truck owners and builders through documentary-style films featuring real-world product testing.
- Tony Ambroza serves as Chief Growth Officer at RealTruck.
- Tony Ambroza stated: “This campaign highlights the role our products play behind the scenes, supporting the people doing the work. Our approach to innovation is grounded in solving real-world problems. We think ahead, so the products they rely on feel intuitive, dependable, and ready for whatever the job demands.”
- The campaign showcases products including the award-winning Ascend Cap, the recently released MX4 EZ Lift, the Guardian Impact Bed and Tailgate Mat, and the SwingCase.
- RealTruck operates 23 manufacturing and distribution facilities in the U.S.
- RealTruck is headquartered in Ann Arbor, Michigan.
- RealTruck employs 6,000 associates across 78 facilities on four continents.
- RealTruck supports a portfolio of trusted brands including Husky Liners®.
- RealTruck holds more than 1,000 patents and pending applications.
- RealTruck delivers products through an omnichannel approach via its website, a network of more than 12,000 dealers, and partnerships with leading OEMs.
