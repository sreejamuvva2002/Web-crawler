# Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2016-02-29

**Source:** [The Georgia Manufacturing Alliance Tours KIA Motors Manufacturing Georgia Plant and finds that KIA Car(es)](https://www.prweb.com/releases/the_georgia_manufacturing_alliance_tours_kia_motors_manufacturing_georgia_plant_and_finds_that_kia_car_es_/prweb13241131.htm)  ·  `page_010232`

## Facts

- The Georgia Manufacturing Alliance conducted three tour groups at Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia.
- Guests were hosted and fed at the on-site KIA Georgia Training Center.
- Randy Jackson serves as Chief Administrative Officer for Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc.
- The facility is located in West Point, Georgia, within Troup County.
- Management philosophy emphasizes clear directives, two-way communication, team engagement, and profitable rewards to foster trust and teamwork.
- The plant covers a total of 2.2 million square feet across four main production areas: stamping, welding, painting, and assembly.
- Amenities include air conditioning, 24/7 food service, medical care, a fitness center, and a basketball court.
- Site protection is provided by an in-house security force and fire department.
- Operations run on three shifts with inventory turning over every four hours.
- The facility has a maximum production capacity of 360,000 vehicles per year.
- Company leadership prioritizes safety and job security for its team members.
- The Georgia Manufacturing Alliance was founded in 2008 to support the state's manufacturing community through plant tours, educational sessions, and networking opportunities.
