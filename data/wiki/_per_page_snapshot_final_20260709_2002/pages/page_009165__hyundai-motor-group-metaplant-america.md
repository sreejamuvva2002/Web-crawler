# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-27

**Source:** [With Hyundai’s arrival, Georgia becomes hub for next-gen manufacturing - The Korea Herald](https://www.koreaherald.com/article/10451775)  ·  `page_009165`

## Facts

- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp touted that the establishment of Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America in Bryan County opens up new jobs and breathes life into the local economy.
- The opening ceremony for HMGMA took place on Wednesday in Ellabell, Georgia.
- HMGMA represents about 8,500 jobs and over $7.5 billion in investment.
- Both the job and investment figures for HMGMA are records for projects with the state economic development team.
- Direct or outside suppliers have already committed another 6,900 jobs and over $25 billion in investment.
- Supplier investments are happening in over a dozen different counties.
- Hyundai plans to increase the new plant's annual production capacity from the current 300,000 to 500,000 vehicles.
- The planned capacity increase is equivalent to building an additional manufacturing plant.
- Gov. Brian Kemp described the project as a historic investment and a testament to Hyundai’s belief in Georgia, its people, and the benefit of their partnership.
- Kia, Hyundai Motor’s sister affiliate, has been operating a manufacturing plant in West Point, Georgia since 2009.
- Kia's Georgia plant has an annual production capacity of about 350,000 vehicles.
- Kia has invested around $2 billion in the state.
- More than 14,000 plant and supplier jobs are linked to Kia's operations in the region.
- US Rep. Buddy Carter, a Republican representing the local district, welcomed the grand opening of the megasized vehicle production plant.
- Rep. Carter stated that Hyundai made a wise decision and praised the area's people as dedicated, hard-working, and prideful.
- Rep. Carter extended a welcome to Hyundai leadership on behalf of the state.
- Gov. Kemp noted that organic growth for local businesses will contribute to a new level of economic success and prosperity for decades to come.
