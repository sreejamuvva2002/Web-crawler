# Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2023-12-08

**Source:** [Broe Group JV Lands New Tenant at 20 MSF Savannah Industrial Park - Commercial Property Executive](https://www.commercialsearch.com/news/broe-group-jv-lands-new-tenant-at-20-msf-savannah-industrial-park/)  ·  `page_056725`

## Facts

- Kyungshin America Corp. has selected the 20 million-square-foot Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub for the location of its new distribution center in Rincon, Ga.
- The South Korea-based automotive part supplier will open a 136,000-square-foot facility in February as part of its production expansion.
- The Broe Group, together with its transportation affiliate OmniTRAX and Effingham County Industrial Development Authority, is the owner of the 2,600-acre multimodal logistics park.
- Kyungshin’s new facility will import and distribute automotive components to Hyundai Motor Group’s $7.5 billion electric vehicle plant in Savannah.
- The company is a certified Hyundai supplier that produces wire harnesses for automotive companies.
- The move will create approximately 70 new jobs.
- The move will generate more than $22 million investments in Effingham County, according to the Effingham Herald.
- Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub offers various site configurations for lease or purchase and build-to-suit options.
- The industrial campus is part of the Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development Program.
- The industrial campus provides easy access to the Port of Savannah, CSXT and Norfolk Southern Railroads systems, and major transportation corridors including interstates 95, 16, and U.S. Highway 21.
- The master-planned industrial park currently has 33 buildings in planning stages or under construction.
- Colliers Principals Cliff Dales and David Sink are in charge of leasing at the hub.
- Tenants at Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub include DHL, Quantix, Bissell Homecare, Inc., and Lion Brand Yarns.
- Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub has been in development since 2016, when Effingham County Industrial Development Authority selected OmniTrax as its development partner for the megaproject.
- In 2019, a commitment from A&R Logistics (now rebranded as Quantix) sparked the groundbreaking on the industrial park’s first building, totaling 1 million square feet.
- At the time of the 2019 groundbreaking, Quantix’s build-to-suit lease amounted to 610,000 square feet over 12 years.
- In 2021, the Broe Group sold the 998,472-square-foot building at 1004 Gateway Parkway to Walton Street Capital.
- In late 2022, TerraCap Management LLC acquired Buildings 1H and 1F in a $116 million deal.
