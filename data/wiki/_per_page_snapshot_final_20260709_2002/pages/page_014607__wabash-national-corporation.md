# Wabash National Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2014-09-05

**Source:** [Wabash National to Expand Composites Production - TT](https://www.ttnews.com/articles/wabash-national-expand-composites-production)  ·  `page_014607`

## Facts

- Wabash National Corp. plans to invest more than $2 million to expand production operations for its composites business.
- The expansion targets a facility in Frankfort, Indiana.
- The company will utilize a plant previously occupied by Sun Chemical located about 25 miles southeast of Wabash’s headquarters in Lafayette, Indiana.
- Wabash expects to create more than 100 jobs during the next five years.
- The company plans to begin the hiring process in October.
- Initial operations at the expanded facility are planned to begin in December.
- The Frankfort facility spans 100,000 square feet.
- Manufacturing operations will house a variety of Wabash Composites products, including semi-trailer aerodynamic products, mobile storage containers, mobile offices, and other cargo units.
- CEO Dick Giromini stated in the Sept. 5 announcement that the expansion supports the continued success of strategic growth and diversification efforts.
