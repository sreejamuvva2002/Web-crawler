# Freudenberg & Co. KG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-03-20

**Source:** [Technology company presents financials for 2023](https://www.freudenberg.com/en/company/press-media/news-detail/2024-03-20-technology-company-presents-financials-for-2023)  ·  `page_020500`

## Facts

- Freudenberg achieved record sales of €11,902.8 million in fiscal year 2023, up 1.3 percent over the previous year.
- Operating profit reached €1,081.6 million in fiscal year 2023, representing a growth rate of about 15 percent and surpassing the €1 billion mark for the first time.
- The equity capital ratio climbed to 56 percent in fiscal year 2023.
- Investments in research and development grew to €603.6 million in fiscal year 2023, corresponding to 5.1 percent of sales.
- Adjusted for acquisitions, investments in intangible and tangible assets as well as real estate held as financial investments totaled €452.7 million in fiscal year 2023.
- The Freudenberg e-Power Systems Business Group expanded its manufacturing and assembly capacity for the production of battery systems in the US.
- Freudenberg Sealing Technologies moved into a new plant in Parets del Vallès for the production of elastomer products for the auto industry.
- In Morinda, India, Freudenberg invested in a new production facility for its Vibracoustic Business Group and its Freudenberg-NOK India joint venture.
- Investments in Germany reached €174.1 million in 2023.
- Freudenberg Performance Materials launched a new factory to produce gas diffusion layers (key components in fuel cells) in Weinheim.
- In Wolfratshausen, EagleBurgmann began building a new research and development center and continued projects to modernize, automate and digitalize the production of mechanical face seals and other products in Eurasburg.
- Freudenberg Medical invested in the construction of a new production facility for Hemoteq in Alsdorf.
- As of December 31, 2023, the Group employed 52,241 employees in 60 countries.
- The Freudenberg Group aims to become CO2-neutral by 2045 and increased its share of electricity from renewable sources from 30 to 36 percent of its total electrical consumption in 2023.
- Freudenberg installed its first-ever microgrid in Berlin, which functions as a battery system that operates as a virtual power plant to cover peak demand and stabilize the public grid.
- The company's strategic outlook for future investments focuses on the transition in mobility, digitalization, and sustainable solutions.
