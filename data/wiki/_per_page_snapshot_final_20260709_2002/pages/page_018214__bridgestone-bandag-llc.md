# Bridgestone Bandag, LLC

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Bridgestone Invests $60 Million to Expand Abilene Bandag Retread Tire Plant](https://www.automotive-technology.com/pressreleases/bridgestone-invests-to-expand-abilene-bandag-retread-tire-plant)  ·  `page_018214`

## Facts

- On 23 September 2022, Bridgestone Americas announced an investment of $60 million to expand its Bridgestone Bandag, LLC manufacturing plant in Abilene, Texas.
- The expansion adds a 50,000-square-foot footprint to the existing Abilene facility.
- The expansion targets growing demand for tread rubber products driven by the rapid growth of the retread business.
- Operational changes include an immediate increase in activity by running on six and seven-day production schedules.
- New mixing operations are expected to be completed and operational by January 2025.
- The investment and additional operating days will increase the plant's output by 16 percent.
- Barry Owens, senior vice president, Bridgestone Americas Manufacturing Group, said the company celebrates growth and commitment with new investment and physical expansion driven by growing demand for products and the team's commitment to delivering innovation and superior quality for customers here and around the world.
- Owens noted that the company and Abilene team play a vital role in the transformation of mobility by building greener retreads while helping maximize fuel efficiency for fleets.
- Bandag is an industry-leading supplier of retread tires for fleets, providing economic benefits and extending tire life as an environmentally responsible practice.
- Since 1957, Bandag has kept approximately 300 million tires out of the waste system, reducing material emissions, water use, and energy use associated with new tire production.
- The Bandag retreading process has saved four billion gallons of oil over its 50-year history.
- The Abilene plant expansion supports Bridgestone's E8 Commitment, which consists of eight values starting with the letter "E" (Energy, Ecology, Efficiency, Extension, Economy, Emotion, Ease, and Empowerment) to realize a sustainable society.
- Expanding Bandag operations specifically aligns with the company's "Efficiency," "Ecology," and "Energy" commitments.
- The Abilene plant celebrated 50 years of operations in 2021 and continues producing high-quality pre-cured truck tread, rubber cement, and solvent packaging.
- Bridgestone's investment builds upon the plant's existing 200,000-square-foot blueprint to provide capacity for future production.
- Bridgestone Americas is the U.S.-based subsidiary of Bridgestone Corporation, headquartered in Nashville, Tennessee, and employs more than 50,000 people across its worldwide operations.
