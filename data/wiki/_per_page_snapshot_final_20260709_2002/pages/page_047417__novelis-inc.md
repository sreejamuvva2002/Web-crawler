# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2016-07-28

**Source:** [Atlanta Falcons and Novelis Launch 'Recycle for Good' Campaign :: Novelis Inc.](https://investors.novelis.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/182/atlanta-falcons-and-novelis-launch-recycle-for-good-campaign)  ·  `page_047417`

## Facts

- Novelis launched the "Recycle for Good" campaign challenging fans to recycle 3 million aluminum cans throughout the 2016 football season.
- Novelis will recycle the collected cans locally at its Greensboro, Georgia recycling facility and convert the aluminum's value into funding an Atlanta Habitat for Humanity home in Atlanta's historic Westside community.
- Novelis serves as the Official Recycling Partner of the Atlanta Falcons.
- Sil Colalancia is the director of Recycling for Novelis North America.
- Local campaign partners include Novelis, the Atlanta Falcons, Mercedes-Benz Stadium, Atlanta Habitat for Humanity, The Arthur M. Blank Family Foundation, and the Georgia Dome.
- A drop-off location for cans will be hosted on the West Plaza by the Georgia Dome starting at the first Falcons home game on September 11.
- Novelis will provide recycling bags in tailgate areas around the Georgia Dome and sponsor an internal sorting program to maximize can collection during all 2016 home games.
- Rich McKay is the president and CEO of the Atlanta Falcons.
- Can collection centers will also be established at Atlanta Habitat for Humanity headquarters and a soon-to-be-determined Westside location for contributions during away games and throughout the season.
- Scott Jenkins is the general manager of Mercedes-Benz Stadium.
- Mercedes-Benz Stadium aims to become the first professional sports stadium to achieve LEED Platinum certification.
- Sil Colalancia stated that aluminum cans are the most valuable material in the recycling bin and that discarding them means throwing away money.
- Aluminum beverage cans can be recycled from store-shelf to store-shelf in as few as 60 days.
- Recycling aluminum cans saves 95 percent of the energy and greenhouse gas emissions associated with primary aluminum production and reduces landfill waste.
- The value of 3 million recycled aluminum cans is sufficient to fund the entire construction of the Atlanta Habitat for Humanity home.
- More than $70 million worth of aluminum goes to landfill in the state of Georgia each year.
- Lisa Gordon is the president and CEO of Atlanta Habitat for Humanity.
- Novelis Inc. is the global leader in aluminum rolled products and the world's largest recycler of aluminum.
- The company operates in 11 countries, employs approximately 12,000 people, and reported $10 billion in revenue for its 2016 fiscal year.
- Novelis supplies premium aluminum sheet and foil products to transportation, packaging, construction, industrial, and consumer electronics markets across North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- Novelis is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited, the flagship metals company of the Aditya Birla Group, a multinational conglomerate based in Mumbai, India.
