# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-08-02

**Source:** [Yamaha Motor USA Headlines Season Premiere of “Modern Marvels: Machines” - Americana Outdoors](https://americanaoutdoors.com/yamaha-motor-usa-headlines-season-premiere-of-modern-marvels-machines/)  ·  `page_015656`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Corp., USA was featured on The HISTORY Channel’s season premiere of “Modern Marvels: Machines.”
- An exclusive behind-the-scenes tour was conducted at the Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America (YMMC) facility in Newnan, Georgia.
- Host Adam Richman toured Yamaha’s U.S. production and product testing facility to show how recreational vehicles are designed, developed, tested, and produced.
- The season premiere episode highlighted Yamaha’s workforce in Georgia and the company’s extensive U.S.-based manufacturing at YMMC.
- Bob Brown, Vice President of Finance and Operations Support, stated that the production aimed to highlight vehicles built on the line and the workers responsible for their quality and craftsmanship.
- Since its founding in 1986, Yamaha has produced nearly 4 million vehicles at YMMC.
- Yamaha creates thousands of jobs across facilities in 11 U.S. states.
- The YMMC facility alone employs nearly 2,000 workers.
- YMMC manufactures Side-by-Side vehicles, full-size ATVs, WaveRunners, and Golf Cars for global distribution.
- Production at YMMC operates at a rate where an ATV or Golf Car rolls off the line every sixty seconds.
- Steve Nessl, Yamaha’s Motorsports marketing manager, noted pride in U.S. production and recognized Georgia factory workers for their hard work, expertise, and enthusiasm.
- The season premiere aired on Sunday, July 25th, with encore viewings available on History.com.
- YMMC holds ISO9001 Quality Management System certification.
- YMMC holds ISO14001 Environmental Management System certification.
- Yamaha Motor Corporation, USA (YMUS) maintains corporate offices in California and two in Georgia.
- YMUS operates facilities in Wisconsin and Alabama, plus factory operations in Tennessee and Georgia.
- YMUS product offerings include ATVs, Side-by-Side vehicles, Snowmobiles, WaveRunners, Boats, Outboard Motors, Golf Cars, Power Assist Bicycles, and Surface Mount Technology equipment.
