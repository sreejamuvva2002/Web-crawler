# Superior Essex International LP

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Superior Essex Communications Joins GeSI - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.de/news/superior-essex-communications-joins-gesi/)  ·  `page_010623`

## Facts

- Superior Essex Communications announced its membership in GeSI, an organization focused on enabling digital sustainability.
- Membership aligns Superior Essex Communications with a growing number of large ICT companies worldwide pursuing social and ecological sustainability through technology.
- Annie Bevan, Global Head of Sustainability at Superior Essex Communications, stated the GeSI membership reflects long-standing support for the company's global sustainability efforts.
- Superior Essex Communications is the only cable manufacturer to have received Living Product Challenge certification.
- It is the only company offering externally verified EPDs, HPDs, and Multi-Attribute Labels for over 55 product families.
- Many of its products are manufactured in a certified Zero Waste to Landfill facility.
- The company's products support customer sustainability goals by contributing to green building certifications such as Living Building Challenge, WELL, and LEED v2009 and v4.
- Superior Essex is a leading cable provider supporting Power-over-Ethernet systems that lead to energy savings and higher occupant productivity and well-being.
- Will Bryan, VP of Broadband Sales at Superior Essex Communications, stated the GeSI membership allows the company to participate in and facilitate global sustainability discussions.
- GeSI is a globally recognized leader in ICT sustainability, focusing on climate change, energy/resource efficiency, e-waste disposal, responsible supply chain practices, labor rights, and public order.
- Luis Neves, CEO of GeSI, welcomed Superior Essex Communications as a member, noting their commitment strengthens GeSI's work across the industry value chain.
- Superior Essex International LP manufactures a broad portfolio of wire and cable products, including copper and fiber optic cables for outside plant applications, DAS cables, and accessories for commercial, industrial, and utility markets.
- The company maintains high ecological sustainability in its manufacturing processes through safe, environmentally conscious operations and environmental performance measurement.
- Superior Essex Communications is headquartered at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327 – USA.
