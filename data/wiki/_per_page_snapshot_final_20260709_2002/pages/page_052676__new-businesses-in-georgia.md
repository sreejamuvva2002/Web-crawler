# New Businesses in Georgia

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Right to Work Georgia Sees New Businesses - NRTWC](https://nrtwc.org/right-to-work-georgia-sees-new-businesses/)  ·  `page_052676`

## Facts

- Georgia’s economy is constantly on the rise as a result of its Right to Work laws.
- Right to Work states provide benefits for businesses as well as workers, causing companies to locate in Georgia.
- Three of the most recent new Georgia locations are coming from Serena & Lily, Moderna, and Aspen Aerogels.
- Altogether, these three investments will create 475 new jobs.
- Serena & Lily is investing $55 million to add a new distribution center in Effingham County, Georgia.
- Serena & Lily’s investment will create 75 new jobs.
- Serena & Lily believes Effingham County will be an ideal location for its first East Coast distribution center.
- Serena & Lily plans to partner with local, county, and state authorities to foster the growth of its brand.
- Georgia offers unmatched logistics infrastructure through air, land, and sea.
- Georgia is home to the nation's fastest-growing port and has established itself as the logistics hub of the Southeast.
- Serena & Lily chose Georgia to grow its business and reach markets all over the world.
- Development partners Chesterfield and Stonemont helped attract Serena & Lily to Effingham County.
- Moderna is adding a new location soon in Atlanta, Georgia.
- Moderna’s new Atlanta location will create over 150 new jobs.
- Moderna looks forward to engaging with the diverse pool of talent in the Atlanta community.
- Atlanta was on the front lines in the development and clinical trials for the COVID-19 vaccine.
- Georgia’s spirit of collaboration and innovation has fostered impressive growth across the entire health and life sciences ecosystem.
- Aspen Aerogels is investing $325 million toward a new location soon in Bulloch County, Georgia.
- Aspen Aerogels’ investment will create over 250 new jobs.
- Aspen Aerogels provides innovative, sustainable solutions for the electric vehicle and energy storage markets.
- Aspen Aerogels employees will collaborate with Aspen’s network of world-class scientists, engineers, and additional skilled workforce to solve challenges of the changing mobility landscape.
- The Development Authority of Bulloch County welcomed Aspen Aerogels to the Statesboro-Bulloch County community.
- Aspen Aerogels’ decision to locate in Bulloch County is a testament to the region’s commitment to innovation, particularly in the electric mobility ecosystem.
- The region offers great site and logistics advantages.
