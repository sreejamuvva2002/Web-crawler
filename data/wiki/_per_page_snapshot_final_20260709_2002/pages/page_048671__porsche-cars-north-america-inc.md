## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) and Nobu Hotel Atlanta teamed up to create an immersive, first-of-its-kind Porsche-themed experience for travelers from across the globe.
- The Nobu Hotel Atlanta is located just north of Porsche’s U.S. headquarters.
- The hotel unveiled the Porsche Icon Suite and Porsche Classic Room as part of the collaboration.
- This marks the first Nobu property worldwide to offer a Porsche-themed experience.
- Ayesha Coker, Vice President of Marketing of PCNA, stated that from sports cars to experiences, the goal is to provide an elevated experience for everyone who engages with their brand.
- The collaboration brings together two luxury brands to offer Atlantans and travelers a unique and immersive stay while visiting Atlanta.
- Nobu Hotel Atlanta designed rooms and suites that integrate sleek Porsche-inspired art and decor alongside exclusive memorabilia celebrating iconic Porsche moments.
- Guests have access to a Porsche-dedicated executive conference room onsite for hosting business meetings.
- The property provides all-electric Porsche Taycan house cars for off-site excursions within a three-mile radius, expertly navigated by dedicated personal drivers.
- Package offerings include a two-night stay in the Porsche Icon Suite with breakfast for two and Nobu’s Signature Omakase dining experience.
- Guests will receive a stylized Nobu Champagne bottle in-room and a personalized Porsche bathrobe to take home.
- The Porsche Icon Suite and Porsche Classic Room are available to book now at Nobu Hotel Atlanta.
