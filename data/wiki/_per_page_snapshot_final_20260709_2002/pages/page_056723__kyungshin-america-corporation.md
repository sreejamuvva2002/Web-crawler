# Kyungshin America Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-12-06

**Source:** [Kyungshin America Selects Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub  | Effingham County Development Authority](https://effinghamindustry.com/news/article/kyungshin-america-selects-savannah-gateway-industrial-hub)  ·  `page_056723`

## Facts

- Broe Group’s Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub (SGIH) added global automotive parts supplier Kyungshin America Corporation (KAC) to its 2,600-acre multimodal logistics park near Georgia’s Port of Savannah.
- KAC is a division of the South Korean Kyungshin Corporation.
- KAC’s new operation will import and distribute automotive components to Hyundai Motor Group’s new $7.5B electric vehicle plant in Savannah.
- KAC’s new operation will also distribute automotive components to the growing automotive manufacturing network throughout the southeastern United States.
- Broe Real Estate Group Senior Vice President Sean Fitzsimmons stated that the Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub provides tremendous geographic and multi-modal benefits for park tenants.
- Sean Fitzsimmons noted that KAC’s new facility is strategically positioned with the Port of Savannah and the new Hyundai Metaplant, both within 15 miles.
- Sean Fitzsimmons added that this positioning creates exceptional supply chain efficiency and connectivity to global markets while leveraging Effingham County’s strong work force and education system.
- The South Korean-based company produces wire harnesses for automotive companies.
- KAC is a certified Tier One supplier to Hyundai.
- KAC’s new facility will be 136,000 SF.
- KAC’s new facility is expected to open in February.
- The move supports Kyungshin’s continued North American production expansion.
- Effingham County Industrial Development Authority CEO Brandt Herndon stated they are excited to welcome KAC to Georgia.
- Brandt Herndon described the addition as a great example of the dynamic partnerships that will serve Hyundai’s new Georgia production plant and expressed pride in world-class companies bringing new jobs and new investment to Effingham County.
- SGIH is a public-private partnership between the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority (ECIDA), The Broe Group’s real estate affiliate Broe Real Estate Group, and its transportation affiliate OmniTRAX.
- SGIH is located 12 miles from the Port of Savannah.
- SGIH provides dual rail access to the Class I rail networks of CSX and Norfolk Southern.
