# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2023-08-01

**Source:** [Hanon Systems bringing 600 new jobs to Tennessee - WDEF](https://www.wdef.com/hanon-systems-bringing-600-new-jobs-to-tennessee/)  ·  `page_001402`

## Facts

- Hanon Systems will invest close to $170 million in a new facility in Loudon, Tennessee.
- The new facility will create 600 new jobs in the area.
- The facility will be located in Huntingdon Park, according to the Tennessee Department of Economic and Community Development (TNECD).
- Hanon Systems is a global operation that supplies thermal solutions to automotive manufacturers.
- The company's products include heating ventilation, air conditioning, powertrain cooling, compressors, fluid transport, electronics and fluid pressure.
- This Tennessee location will supply North American automotive markets.
- The company’s headquarters sits in South Korea.
- Hanon Systems employs over 22,000 people in 21 countries.
- Governor Bill Lee and Commissioner Stuart McWhorter announced the company's arrival on Tuesday.
- Loudon County Mayor Buddy Bradshaw stated that Loudon County is very excited about an international company of Hanon Systems’ caliber locating there.
- Mayor Bradshaw noted the number and quality of jobs will improve the job market landscape not just locally but regionally.
- Mayor Bradshaw highlighted the nearly $170 million capital investment and pay scale will establish the company quickly in the community.
- Mayor Bradshaw expressed excitement to see the partnership flourish and look forward to all Hanon Systems will bring to Loudon County.
