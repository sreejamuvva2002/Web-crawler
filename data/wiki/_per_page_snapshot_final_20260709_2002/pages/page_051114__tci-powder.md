## Facts

- Established in 1987.
- Headquartered in Ellaville, Georgia, USA.
- Manufacturer of thermoset powder coatings.
- Operates as a subsidiary of RPM Inc.
- Specializes in formulating and producing a wide range of powder coatings for various industries, including automotive, architectural, and industrial applications.
- Main products comprise epoxy, polyester, and hybrid powder coatings.
- Coatings find applications in metal finishing, appliance manufacturing, and architectural projects.
- Holds FDA/NSF-51, QUALICOAT, and ISO 9001:2015 certifications ensuring adherence to quality management systems in powder coating production.
