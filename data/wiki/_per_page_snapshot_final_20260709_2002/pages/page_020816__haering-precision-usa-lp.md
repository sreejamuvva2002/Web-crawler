## Facts

- The company currently employs approximately 4,400 people across 5 locations on 4 continents.
- The American location in Lavonia is considered a major milestone for the Häring Group.
- President Dr.-Ing. Jürgen Häring expressed excitement about the Grand Opening ceremony.
- Approximately 400 regional high school students will attend the Future Day event as part of a school trip.

- The company aims to position itself as an attractive employer for young recruits through these events.

- The facility features modern workplace design and state-of-the-art machinery to attract employees.
- Miriam Häring serves as President of the Häring Group.
- Events provide students and the community access to tour the manufacturing facility.
- Numerous activities and information points are distributed across the company premises to showcase operations.
