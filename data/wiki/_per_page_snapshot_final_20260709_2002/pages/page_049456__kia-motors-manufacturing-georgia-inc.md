# Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2014-07-07

**Source:** [Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia Team Members and Area Suppliers Clean West Point Lake - Kia Georgia](https://www.kiageorgia.com/kia-motors-manufacturing-georgia-team-members-and-area-suppliers-clean-west-point-lake/)  ·  `page_049456`

## Facts

- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia (KMMG) team members and suppliers cleaned West Point Lake at Pyne Road Park in LaGrange on June 28, 2014.
- Sixty-eight KMMG team members, including managers and vice presidents, joined friends, family, and supplier partners to fill 52 trash bags with trash during the event.
- Supplier partners participating in the cleanup included Powertech America, Sewon America, and Johnson Controls Inc.
- Stuart Countess, vice president of quality for KMMG, stated that cleaning the lake helps the environment and serves as a team-building activity demonstrating the impact of working together.
- Tracy Rome, benefits at KMMG, noted she enjoyed spending the day cleaning with supplier partners, followed by lunch and kayaking activities.
- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Motors Corporation, which is based in Seoul, Korea.
- KMMG has an annual production capacity of 360,000 units.
- The KMMG facility is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Georgia.
- KMMG began mass production on November 16, 2009.
- The facility manufactures the Sorento CUV and the Optima mid-size sedan, which are two of Kia's top-selling models in the U.S.
