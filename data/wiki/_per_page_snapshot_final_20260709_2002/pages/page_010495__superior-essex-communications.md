# Superior Essex Communications

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Superior Essex Communications Announces Expansion of Manufacturing Facility - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://superioressex.de/news/superior-essex-communications-announces-expansion-of-manufacturing-facility/)  ·  `page_010495`

## Facts

- Superior Essex Communications announced continued investment in production capacity and equipment at its Brownwood, Texas facility.
- Total investments for the facility expansion amount to $73 million.
- The expansion will create 170 new manufacturing jobs in the United States.
- The project aims to support nationwide broadband deployment to provide high-speed internet and 5G services.
- Production facilities will be improved with an additional 90,000 square feet built next to the current Brownwood plant.
- The facility's total production area will expand to over 500,000 square feet.
- New jobs will be created and some existing roles will transition from old OSP copper cable manufacturing to fiber optic cable manufacturing.
- Environmental practices for the expansion include PoE lighting, use of reusable equipment, and exploration of solar and battery energy sources for the facility.
- Brad Johnson, President of Superior Essex Communications, stated the improvements are an investment in US manufacturing and American communities, supporting 'Build America, Buy America' products.
- The company supports government initiatives to increase US high-speed internet access, including the White House's Internet for All program and the NTIA's Broadband Equity, Access and Deployment (BEAD) program.
- Superior Essex Communications is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
- The company holds a 'Zero Waste to Landfill' certification at its Hoisington, Kansas plant.
