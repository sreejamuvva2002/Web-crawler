# Porter Logistics Savannah Battery Warehouse

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[logistics|Logistics]]  ·  **Location:** Savannah, GA, Chatham County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Porter Logistics operates a HAZMAT-certified 3PL warehouse in Savannah that stores and manages new and used batteries for electric vehicles, data centers, and industrial applications.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Provides warehousing, handling, and logistics services for new and used EV batteries, supporting the electric-vehicle supply chain in Georgia.

## Key facts

- Stores raw materials such as lithium carbonate, black mass, and cobalt, whether recycled or new.
- Handles both new and used batteries for EVs, data centers, and various industrial applications.
- Facility includes racked and bulk storage spaces equipped with heavy-duty forklifts, clamp trucks, and narrow-aisle equipment.
- Utilizes high-performance networking technology and an advanced Warehouse Management System (WMS) for real-time inventory tracking and order processing.
- Undergoes bi-annual audits by the Alliance for Chemical Distribution to ensure compliance with chemical warehouse standards.
- Registered with US DOT & EPA as a chemical handler.
- Offers additional services such as transloading, cross-docking, B2B order fulfillment, B2C eCommerce fulfillment, import/export, climate-controlled warehousing, and specialized HAZMAT warehousing.
- Implements containment measures for spill mitigation and maintains strict PPE standards.
