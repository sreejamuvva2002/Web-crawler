# Kia Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2009-11-16

**Source:** [KIA Georgia Plant Tour - West Point  (Afternoon) 2025 -  Georgia Manufacturing Alliance](https://www.georgiamanufacturingalliance.com/events/kia-georgia-plant-tour-afternoon-2025)  ·  `page_010094`

## Facts

- Kia Georgia, Inc. is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Corporation based in Seoul, Korea.
- The facility has an annual production capacity of 350,000 units.
- Kia Georgia is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Georgia.
- The plant began mass production on November 16, 2009.
- The facility produces the Telluride, Sorento, and Sportage SUVs.
- The facility also produces the all-electric EV9 and EV6 SUVs.
- Kia Georgia represents a total investment of $3.2 billion.
- The plant employs 3,200 team members.
- Manufacturing processes include high-tech stamping, weld shop advanced robotics, and highly automated assembly processes.
- The facility produces one vehicle every 51 seconds.
- Operations run on three full shifts, operating 24 hours a day, five days per week.
- Tour registration is open until July 22, 2025, at 12:00 AM EDT.
- The plant tour is available exclusively to Georgia Manufacturing Alliance (GMA) members.
- Each company is limited to two tickets for the tour.
- First-time GMA event attendees who are Georgia manufacturers can call for a complimentary ticket.
- Visitors must be at least 13 years old to attend the tour.
- Tobacco products, weapons (including firearms and knives), photography, and video recording are strictly prohibited on site.
- Large purses, bags, and backpacks are not allowed inside the plant.
- All tours are subject to cancellation with little or no notice depending on Kia Georgia's production schedule.
- Visitors must wear long pants and enclosed-toe shoes to participate in the tour.
- A portion of the tour will be outdoors, requiring weather-appropriate attire.
- The facility address is 7777 Kia Parkway, West Point, Georgia 31833.
- Navigation apps may occasionally direct visitors to the back of the property instead of the main entry gate.
- Visitor parking is located on numbered front rows, and security passes are provided at Building 7797.
