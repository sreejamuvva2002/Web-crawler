## Facts

- Kumho Tire is a high performance tire company that brings quality and innovative technology to consumers worldwide.
- With over 50 years of experience, Kumho Tire has transformed its humble beginning in South Korea into becoming one of the largest tire manufacturers with 8 factories.
- Kumho Tire first originated over 60 years ago due to a shortage of bus tires.
- The Kumho Tire facility in Macon spans over 1 million sq. ft. and is the company's first plant location in the United States.
- The Macon facility has over 380 employees on staff.
- The Macon facility has the capacity to produce 4 million tires per year.
- In 2002, Kumho built the world's first 26" tire.
- Kumho Tire is the official product supplier for the NBA.
