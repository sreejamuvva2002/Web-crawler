# Hyundai Motor Group and SK On

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-09

**Source:** [EV Battery Plant To Open In Bartow County | Cartersville, GA Patch](https://patch.com/georgia/cartersville/ev-battery-plant-open-bartow-county)  ·  `page_000651`

## Facts

- A new electric vehicle battery plant is coming to Bartow County in 2025.
- The project will bring more than 3,500 jobs.
- Gov. Brian Kemp announced the project on Thursday.
- Hyundai Motor Group and SK On are looking to build their manufacturing facility at Bartow Centre on Highway 411.
- About $4 to $5 billion will be invested into Bartow County.
- Gov. Brian Kemp called the plant one of the largest economic development projects in Georgia's history.
- Gov. Brian Kemp stated in a news release: "Hyundai Motor Group and SK On are valued partners and key players in our state's ever-growing automotive industry. Since day one, my administration has been focused on bringing jobs and opportunity to communities across the state that may have been overlooked in the past. SK and HMG share this goal, and we're proud they are choosing to invest even further in this No. 1 state for business."
- In May, Hyundai Motor Group announced plans to invest $5.54 billion into Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America in Bryan County.
- Ground was broken on the Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America facility in Bryan County in October.
