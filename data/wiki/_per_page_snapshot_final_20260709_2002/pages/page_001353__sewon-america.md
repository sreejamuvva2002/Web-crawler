# Sewon America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-08-28

**Source:** [Aug. 28 - Sewon America Launches $50 Million Expansion in Rincon | Effingham/Statesboro News | savannahbusinessjournal.com](https://www.savannahbusinessjournal.com/community/effingham_statesboro_news/aug-28---sewon-america-launches-50-million-expansion-in-rincon/article_36080dab-76f6-40a5-8513-3750f4d494b1.html)  ·  `page_001353`

## Facts

- The Effingham County Industrial Development Authority (ECIDA) announced that Sewon America, a subsidiary of Korean-based Sewon Precision Industry Co., is expanding its manufacturing operations in Rincon.
- The expansion project costs $50 million.
- The project will add 98,250 square feet to the facility.
- The project will create 100 new jobs.
- The expansion introduces new capabilities for Sewon America.
- This marks the first expansion by a Hyundai supplier since the launch of Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America in late 2024.
- Misti Martin, Deputy Commissioner of Global Commerce at the Georgia Department of Economic Development, called the expansion a full-circle moment and incredible milestone for the Metaplant’s supplier network.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development values long-lasting partnerships and seeks partners who share that commitment.
- Matt Saxon, Chairman of the ECIDA, stated that Sewon America chose Effingham County for its second U.S. facility.
- The expansion underscores Sewon America's confidence in the community, workforce, and strong business climate of Effingham County.
- Rincon Mayor Kevin Exley expressed excitement about the opportunity for more quality jobs in the community.
- The City of Rincon looks forward to partnering with Sewon to ensure local citizens can take advantage of the new positions.
- Robert Vazquez, Director of Human Resources at Sewon America, stated the expansion will allow the company to better serve customers and add new capabilities.
- Sewon America plans to grow alongside the community, create new job opportunities, and continue investing in its talented workforce in Effingham County.
- Sewon America operates one of the largest automobile body parts manufacturing plants in North America.
- The company opened its first Georgia facility in LaGrange in 2008.
- The LaGrange facility has since doubled in size.
- Sewon America began operations in Rincon in 2025.
- With the expansion, Sewon will continue to supply electric vehicle (EV) body parts for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).
- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America is one of the OEMs that receives EV body parts from Sewon.
- The project was supported by the Georgia Department of Economic Development, the City of Rincon, and the Effingham County Board of Commissioners.
