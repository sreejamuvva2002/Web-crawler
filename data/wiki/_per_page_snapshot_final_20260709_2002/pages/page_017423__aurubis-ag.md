# Aurubis AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2024-02-15

**Source:** [Aurubis achieves good full-year result of € 349 million despite extraordinary effects caused by criminal activities | Valuespectrum.com](https://www.valuespectrum.com/news/1039377-aurubis-achieves-good-full-year-result-of-349-million-despite-extraordinary-effects-caused-by-criminal-activities)  ·  `page_017423`

## Facts

- Aurubis AG is a leading global provider of non-ferrous metals and one of the largest copper recyclers worldwide.
- The company processes complex metal concentrates, scrap metals, organic and inorganic metal-bearing recycling materials, and industrial residues into metals of the highest quality.
- Aurubis produces more than 1 million tons of copper cathodes annually.
- From its copper cathodes, Aurubis manufactures wire rod, continuous cast shapes, profiles, and flat rolled products made of copper and copper alloys.
- The company also produces precious metals, selenium, lead, nickel, tin, and zinc, along with by-products such as sulfuric acid and iron silicate.
- CEO Roland Harings stated that high market demand for the company's products demonstrates its potential amid global developments like electrification and the transition to sustainability.
- Roland Harings emphasized that Aurubis is making an important contribution to meeting the growing demand for metals essential to the energy and mobility transition.
- Aurubis has a large-scale growth project named Aurubis Richmond located in Georgia.
- Around €1.7 billion is currently approved for strategic projects, including Aurubis Richmond in Georgia, the Complex Recycling Hamburg project, and the Industrial Heat project in Hamburg.
- Over the coming three to five years, these strategic projects will contribute a total of around €260 million to EBITDA per year.
- For the 2023/24 fiscal year, Aurubis anticipates an operating earnings before taxes (EBT) between €380 million and €480 million.
- The company expects an operating return on capital employed (ROCE) of between 10% and 14% for the current fiscal year.
- Aurubis achieved operating EBT of €349 million for the 2022/23 fiscal year ending September 30, 2023.
- IFRS consolidated earnings before taxes for the same period were €165 million.
- The Supervisory and Executive Boards will propose a dividend per share of €1.40 at the Annual General Meeting on February 15, 2024.
- Aurubis has approximately 7,200 employees and operates production sites in Europe and the US.
- CEO Roland Harings stated that the company is committed to investing in the US and Europe to drive growth, security, safety, and sustainability.
- Criminal activities directed against Aurubis resulted in a €169 million metals shortfall as of September 30, 2023.
- Insurance reimbursements for the criminal activity impact totaled €30 million.
- A new Precious Metals Refinery (PMR) plant at the Hamburg site will require a €300 million investment and is scheduled to come online by the end of 2026.
- The PMR project will lower operating costs by approximately 15% and significantly reduce throughput times for materials containing precious metals.
- In response to security threats, Aurubis implemented additional approval levels for raw material purchasing and increased spot checks of personnel and vehicles.
- A virtual press conference detailing the results was scheduled for December 20, 2023, at 10 a.m. CET.
- An English-language webcast for analysts, investors, and journalists was offered at 2 p.m. CET on December 20, 2023.
