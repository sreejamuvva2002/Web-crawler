# OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-14

**Source:** [OTR unveils new branding at CONEXPO-CON/AGG 2023 - Material Handling Wholesaler](https://www.mhwmag.com/nuts-bolts/otr-unveils-new-branding-at-conexpo-con-agg-2023/)  ·  `page_058424`

## Facts

- OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc. (OTR) is officially unveiling new branding at CONEXPO-CON/AGG 2023.
- The new branding includes a new trade name or DBA of “OTR Engineered Solutions,” as well as a new logo, tagline, website, and other branding elements rolled out to better reflect expanded capabilities as a global solutions provider.
- Founded in 1987 to provide off-the-road wheels and tires to OEMs and the aftermarket, OTR’s vast tire portfolio includes bias, radial, semi-pneumatic, non-marking, and airless tires.
- As its product line grew, OTR began providing value-added services for dealers and OEMs, including product development, sourcing, warehousing, tire mounting, foam filling, flat proofing, wheel inspection, certification, subassembly, and other customized solutions.
- John Major, Director of Marketing, stated that thanks to a history of innovation, OTR today has much more to offer beyond wheels and tires.
- OTR operates as a partner in the off-the-road market with 34 facilities located across North America, Europe, and Asia to better serve customers.
- The company feels the “OTR Engineered Solutions” name and new branding elements better encompass its full capabilities given its evolution.
- To complement the branding update, OTR has introduced the tagline “Roll More™.”
- John Major credited the company’s success to both innovative products and an unmatched level of service and support.
- The company provides services ranging from design and engineering to manufacturing and logistics, driven to help customers “Roll More.”
