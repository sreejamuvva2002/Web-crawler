## Facts

- Oneda Corporation (USA) is a family-owned, Georgia-based contract manufacturer.
- The company specializes in precision metal stamping, assembly, and tool and die designs.
- It utilizes innovative robotic transfer and progressive metal stamping capabilities.
- Its press sizes range from 25 to 500 tons.
