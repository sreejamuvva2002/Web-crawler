# Seoyon E-Hwa Co., Ltd. (Formerly Hanil E-Hwa Co., Ltd.)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-12-31

**Source:** [Seoyon E-Hwa Co., Ltd. (Formerly Hanil E-Hwa Co., Ltd.) Business Report FY ended Dec. 2015 - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/cf/s500_227_hl2015)  ·  `page_059552`

## Facts

- Seoyon E-Hwa Georgia, a U.S. unit of the Company, has received a five-star quality rating from Hyundai Motor Group.
- No other interior parts supplier to Kia Motors' Georgia plant has ever earned a five-star rating from the group.
- The Company and Beijing Hainachuan Automotive Parts Co., Ltd. have established two production joint-ventures in China: one in Chonqing; and one in Cangzhou.
- These companies will supply their products to Hyundai Motor's new vehicle assembly plants in Chonqing and Cangzhou, which are expected to become operational in 2017.
- The new Chongqing JV will begin plant construction in March 2016.
- The new Cangzhou JV is scheduled to begin mass production in October 2016.
- The Company has been awarded a KRW 128 billion bumper contract from Ford India.
- The bumpers will be used on 840,000 units of the successor model to the EcoSport compact crossover SUV, which will be sold in India and North America.
- The contract covers from 2017 through 2021.
- The bumpers will be produced at the Company's Indian plant.
- The Company has received a USD 120 billion contract from Beijing Benz Automotive, under which it will supply headliners for approximately 1.27 vehicles from 2018 through 2027.
- Daimler is currently developing some new global models based on the MFA2 (Mercedes Benz Front-wheel-drive Architecture 2).
- Hanil E-Hwa's headliners will be used on three of these models, which will be produced in China.
- The headliners will be manufactured at the Company's Beijing Plant.
- Seoyon R&D in Anyang City, Gyeonggi-Do has nine stories above the ground and two stories below the ground, this center has a total floor area of approximately 20,000 square meters.
- The facility is expected to serve as an integrated R&D center for the Seoyon Group.
- Number of R&D staff: 169 (as of December 31, 2015).
- Central Research Center is located in Asan.
- Development Center is located in Ulsan.
- Technical Research Center was established in Beijing, China in July 2007.
- For Hyundai's successor model to Equus (HI), the Company supplied door trim from October 2013 to January 2015, with the project completed.
- For Kia U.S.'s successor model to Optima (JFA), the Company supplied door trim from January 2014 to September 2015, with the project completed.
- For Kia China's successor model to K5 (JFc), the Company supplied a bumper from April 2014 to September 2015, with the project completed.
- For Hyundai's environmentally friendly model (AE), the Company supplied door trim from May 2014 to January 2015, with the project completed.
- For Kia Europe's successor model to Sportage (QLe), the Company supplied door trim from January 2014 to November 2015, with the project completed.
- For Hyundai's successor model to i30 (PD), the Company is supplying door trim from January 2015 to August 2016, with the project under development.
- For Hyundai's successor model to i30, the Company is supplying door trim from January 2015 to December 2016, with the project under development.
- For Hyundai's HEV (DE), the Company is supplying door trim from July 2014 to March 2016, with the project under development.
- The Company has a technical partnership with Kasai Kogyo and Sumitomo Chemical (Japan) for KPM manufacturing guidance and operational procedures from October 1995 to October 2015.
- The Company has a technical partnership with Tenryu Industries Co., Ltd. for technology to manufacture lightweight, aluminum seat frames for buses from January 2010 to December 2012.
- Planned amount of capital investment for 2016 is KRW 47,729 million.
- Capital investment in 2015 was KRW 5,972 million.
