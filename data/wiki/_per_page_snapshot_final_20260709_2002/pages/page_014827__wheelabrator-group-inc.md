# Wheelabrator Group Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-15

**Source:** [Wheelabrator Group (USA)](https://www.thefabricator.com/directory/showroom/wheelabrator-group-usa)  ·  `page_014827`

## Facts

- Wheelabrator Group designs and manufactures standard and custom shot blast machines for surface preparation of steel plates, structural shapes, bars, pipe and fabrications.
- Preservation lines are available for blasting and priming plates and shapes.
- The company provides surface preparation solutions for the structural steel, fabricated steel, aerospace, ship, railway, construction and more industries.
- Wheelabrator Plus professionals conduct thorough assessments of installed blast equipment to determine if productivity, cost savings, safety or environmental improvements can be realized by upgrading to newer technology.
- The company's facility is located at 1606 Executive Drive, LaGrange, GA 30240.
- Brad Lorson serves as the Director of Wheelabrator OEM Sales- NA.
- On May 15, 2026, Wheelabrator appointed shot blasting industry veteran Mike Reaves to head up its North American EMP (equipment modernization program) team as a technical specialist.
- On July 10, 2025, LaGrange, Ga.-based shot blast equipment manufacturer Wheelabrator appointed Matt Schweitzer to head up its North American equipment sales business.
- On May 29, 2024, Wheelabrator named Magdalene Höltke as senior vice president of its global wheelblast business.
- On March 27, 2018, Wheelabrator Group Inc. consolidated its North American operations by creating a hub for centralized sales, service, engineering, and technology development at its LaGrange, Ga., facility.
- On April 22, 2016, Wheelabrator developed a new HD rotary screen designed for blast machines operating in harsh environments, featuring harder steels in strategic locations, abrasive-resistant flighting and end bands, and DOM tubing.
- On March 6, 2015, Wheelabrator introduced a concave blade for its 35 Ezefit blast wheel that achieves a narrow blast pattern and a tight “hot spot” at high abrasive speeds, suitable for processing narrow workpieces like wire, rod, tube, pipe, bar, and automotive parts.
- Wheelabrator's structural steel shotblast cleaning systems are designed for descaling and cleaning structural shapes before or after fabrication.
- A Wheelabrator rotary mesh table system is suitable for cleaning small parts offline using one operator and can process flame-cut shapes, fabrications, automotive parts, aluminum die castings, and forgings with an airless wheel blast process.
- The Wheelabrator model 1824 structural preblast roll conveyor machine provides a flexible surface preparation method for structural shapes prior to fabrication, helping reduce wear and tear on cutting tools like band saw blades and drill bits.
- On May 6, 2009, DISA Group merged with Wheelabrator Group, with both companies owned by Mid Europa Partners at the time.
- The Wheelabrator Continuous Rocker Barrel machine is designed for inline cleaning of castings and forged parts, with production requirements ranging from less than 5 tons/hr. to more than 25 tons/hr.
- Wheelabrator's WS 4/210 spinner hanger machine cleans paint hooks and prepares odd-shaped parts that do not adapt easily to batch machine processing.
