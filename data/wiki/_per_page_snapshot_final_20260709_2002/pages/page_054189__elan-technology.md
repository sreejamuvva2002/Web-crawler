## Facts

- Elan Technology is located in Georgia, United States.
- The company operates in the Appliances, Electrical, and Electronics Manufacturing sector.
- Elan Technology has 11-50 employees.
- For over 75+ years, Elan Technology has been a leader in the glass, ceramic, toll spray drying, and rotary calcining material processing industries.
- Elan Technology is the largest independent U.S. company in its field.
- The company produces preforms for hermetic seals and spacers using technical glass, ceramics, and glass ceramic composites.
- Elan Technology offers advanced ceramic materials including alumina, steatite, mullite, cordierite, and zirconia.
- Over 600 standard and custom glass compositions are available from the company.
- Elan Technology's research and development department continues to search for new and innovative materials to meet demanding requirements.
- The company offers material processing services including toll spray drying and contract rotary calcining.
