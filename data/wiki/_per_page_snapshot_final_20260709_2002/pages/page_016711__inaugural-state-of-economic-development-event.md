# Inaugural State of Economic Development Event

**Type:** event  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-16

**Source:** [Georgia Chamber to host state economic event - Rough Draft Atlanta](https://roughdraftatlanta.com/2026/03/16/state-of-georgia-economy/)  ·  `page_016711`

## Facts

- State and local leaders will gather on Wednesday, March 18, at the Georgia Freight Depot in Downtown Atlanta to discuss factors shaping Georgia’s economic future during the inaugural “State of Economic Development” event.
- The event is hosted by the Georgia Chamber of Commerce.
- The gathering will bring together business leaders, economic developers and policymakers to explore strategies, partnerships and investments driving the state’s continued economic growth and competitiveness.
- The event is part of the Georgia Chamber’s ongoing “State of” series, which explores key issues shaping Georgia’s business climate.
- Confirmed speakers include Commissioner of the Georgia Department of Economic Development Pat Wilson.
- Georgia Chamber of Commerce President Chris Clark will speak at the event.
- Chief Executive Officer of the Bulloch County Development Authority Benjy Thompson is a confirmed speaker.
- Governor Brian Kemp will deliver video remarks at the event.
