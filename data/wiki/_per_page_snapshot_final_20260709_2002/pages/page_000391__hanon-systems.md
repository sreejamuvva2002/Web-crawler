# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-23

**Source:** [Bulloch County draws second EV supplier - The Augusta Press](https://theaugustapress.com/bulloch-county-draws-second-ev-supplier/)  ·  `page_000391`

## Facts

- South Korea-based Hanon Systems is a global provider of automotive thermal and energy management solutions for conventional and electric vehicles.
- Hanon Systems will invest more than $40 million in a manufacturing plant in Bulloch County.
- The Hanon Systems project is expected to create 160 jobs.
- Gov. Brian welcomed Hanon Systems to Georgia on Tuesday while in Israel leading a trade mission, stating that Georgia’s automotive and e-mobility industries continue to grow.
- Gov. Brian stated that Georgia is furthering its position as the EV capital of America with new jobs and opportunities coming.
- Billy Allen, chairman of the Development Authority of Bulloch County, said Hanon will work closely with Hyundai Motor Group.
- Hyundai Motor Group is building a huge EV manufacturing plant in Bryan County west of Savannah with a $5.5 billion investment.
- The Hanon Systems plant is expected to begin operations in May of next year.
- Min Sung, Hanon’s president and CEO, said the company is pleased to be contributing to the local economy of Bulloch County through job creation.
- Min Sung stated that the announcement aligns with Hanon's strategy to support the industry shift toward electrification while growing its North America footprint.
- The Hyundai plant broke ground last October and has been attracting EV suppliers to Bulloch County and other areas near Savannah.
- Ecoplastic Corp. announced plans in January to build an automotive parts plant in Bulloch that will create 456 jobs.
- Auto parts manufacturer Joon Georgia unveiled plans last November for a new manufacturing plant in Bulloch that will create 630 jobs.
- Pat Wilson, commissioner of the Georgia Department of Economic Development, noted that Bulloch County is part of the Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority and has been part of this growing success story from the beginning.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development's Global Commerce team worked on the Hanon Systems project with the Development Authority of Bulloch County, Georgia Power, the Georgia Ports Authority, and the Technical College System of Georgia’s Quick Start program.
