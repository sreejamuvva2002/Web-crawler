## Facts

- Aurubis AG achieved an operating EBT of ‑413 million for the 2023/24 fiscal year, which is 19% higher than the previous year.
- Aurubis AG reported an operating ROCE of 11.5% for the 2023/24 fiscal year.
- The company successfully completed the ribbon-cutting ceremony for Aurubis Richmond, its first secondary smelter for multimetal recycling in the US.
- Aurubis advanced its decarbonization roadmap by installing hydrogen-ready anode furnaces at the Hamburg site.
- Aurubis is expanding its Industrial Heat project to supply CO2-free heat to up to 28,000 households.
- The Group expanded its solar park in Bulgaria.
- The Group completed Copper Mark certification for all major smelter sites.
- Aurubis AG has a net zero target for 2050.
- Aurubis AG employs 5,000 people.
