## Facts

- ABC Technologies has completed its acquisition of TI Fluid Systems.
- The combined business will be rebranded as TI Automotive.
- The transaction brings together two highly complementary businesses to create a stronger, more diversified Tier 1 manufacturer supplying market-leading safety and performance critical products and highly engineered components.
- The business is positioned to support a diverse range of customers with its propulsion agnostic portfolio.
- Pro forma 2024 revenue was referenced in the announcement.
- TI Automotive will be led by ABC President & Chief Executive Officer.
- TI Automotive is a Tier 1 supplier of market-leading safety and performance critical products and highly engineered components to the global automotive industry.
- The company has more than 100 years' experience solving customers' safety, efficiency and environmental challenges.
- TI Automotive's capabilities include a powertrain agnostic product portfolio, vertical integration, a global footprint, and customer proximity.
- The company is a trusted partner to all the world's leading OEMs.
- TI Automotive was created through ABC Technologies' acquisition of TI Fluid Systems.
