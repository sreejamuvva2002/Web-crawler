## Facts

- DENSO Manufacturing Michigan, Inc. (DMMI) serves as the lead production facility for DENSO’s North American Thermal (TAC) business.
- The facility focuses on the manufacture of automotive air conditioning and engine cooling components and systems.
- The Battle Creek campus comprises more than 1,380,000 square feet of manufacturing, warehouse and administrative floor space in 7 buildings on over 120 acres of land.
- DMMI was established in 1984.
- The facility manufactures evaporators, condensers, cooling units, radiators, heaters, HVACs and manages sales of air-conditioner parts.
- DMMI is located at One Denso Road, Battle Creek, Michigan 49037-7356 U.S.A.
- Shu Kamakura serves as the President of DMMI.
- DENSO holds 100% ownership of DMMI.
- A satellite production location operates at DENSO Manufacturing Iowa Michigan, Inc., located at 11323 Meredith Drive, Urbandale, Iowa 50322 U.S.A.
- DMMI holds ISO 14001 certification (EMS 547385), IATF 16949:2016 DQS certification (Number 10000969), and ISO 9001 2015 DQS certification (Number 10000969).
