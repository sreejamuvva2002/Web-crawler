# HEXPOL Americas

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-01-02

**Source:** [Hexpol announces the closure of two facilities in the U.S. - Rubber World - The Technical Service Magazine for the Rubber Industry](https://rubberworld.com/hexpol-announces-the-closure-of-two-facilities-in-the-u-s/)  ·  `page_020980`

## Facts

- HEXPOL Americas announces the closure of its production site in Kennedale, Texas by the end of Q2, 2025.
- All business from the Kennedale production site will be relocated to other North American facilities.
- HEXPOL Americas will work closely with customers to ensure a seamless transition for the Kennedale closure.
- Costs associated with the closure of the Kennedale production site will be accounted for in Q4 2024.
- The Kennedale closure transaction will be reported separately in Q4 2024.
- HEXPOL Americas divested its production site in Muscle Shoals, Alabama effective immediately to private investment firm Karo Ventures LLC.
- HEXPOL Americas will work closely with Karo Ventures LLC to ensure a seamless transition for associates, customers, and suppliers at the Muscle Shoals site.
- The Muscle Shoals production site manufactured products outside of HEXPOL’s core business.
- The Muscle Shoals site had an annual turnover of some 7 MUSD.
- The Muscle Shoals site had a profitability level below that of the HEXPOL Group.
- The Muscle Shoals divestment transaction will be reported separately in Q4 2024, with minimal impact on the net result.
- Gary Moore, President HEXPOL Compounding Americas, stated both decisions are not a reflection of the skills or commitment of the Kennedale and Muscle Shoals workforces.
- Gary Moore stated the decisions are necessary to maintain competitiveness and focus on core business to improve supply chain support for customers.
- Klas Dahlberg, President and CEO of HEXPOL Group, stated the decisions align with their business model to focus on core business, optimize efficiency, and support customers effectively.
