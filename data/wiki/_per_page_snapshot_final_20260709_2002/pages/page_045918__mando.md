# Mando

## Facts

- Mando, a part of Halla Group, has opened a new auto parts facility in Meriwether, Georgia, US marking its second plant in the state.
- The new plant can accommodate 170,000 rack type-electronic power steering (R-EPS) systems and 600,000 electronic stability controls (ESC).
- It also comprises a steel-casting facility capable of producing 18,000 tons of goods per year.
- Mando plans to expand the plant's ESC capacity to one million units over the next four years.
- The facility will make electronic components that are used in half-a-million vehicles annually.
- A Mando source said: "The latest plant will permit the car parts manufacturer to better cope with moves by automakers to make more upscale, sophisticated cars."
- Apart from the US, Mando's operations are also spread across Poland, India, Brazil, Turkey and China.
