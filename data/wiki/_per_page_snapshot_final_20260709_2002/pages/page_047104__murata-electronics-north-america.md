# Murata Electronics North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Murata delivers 1-uF cap array in 0302 package - EE Times](https://www.eetimes.com/murata-delivers-1-uf-cap-array-in-0302-package/)  ·  `page_047104`

## Facts

- Murata Electronics North America is located in Smyrna, Ga.
- The company has introduced what it says is the industry's first 1.0 uF two-element capacitor array in a 0.9 x 0.6 mm package.
- The new GNM series 0302-sized capacitor array delivers 1.0 μF static capacitance.
- Murata states this achievement is unprecedented even for a single 0.6 x 0.3 mm (EIA size 0201) monolithic ceramic capacitor.
- The new device is well suited as a decoupling capacitor for mobile phones and PCs, as well as digital audio/visual equipment.
- The GNM series is priced at $0.07 for both sample and volume quantities.
- Samples are available now.
- Volume quantities are available with a 10-week lead time.
