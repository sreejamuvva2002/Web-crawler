# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Essex Brownell Renews Its Lease At Edison (NJ) Distribution Center - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.it/news/essex-brownell-renews-its-lease-at-edison-nj-distribution-center/)  ·  `page_010697`

## Facts

- Essex Brownell, a division of Superior Essex and one of the leading North American distributors/manufacturers of magnet wire, motors and drives, electrical insulation, tapes, repair parts, and shop and test equipment, renewed its lease for its Northeast distribution center in Edison, NJ.
- The lease renewal is a five-year extension on 60,000 square feet of space dedicated to supporting regional inventory.
- The distribution center in the New York metropolitan area will continue to host an expanding product line covering all Essex Brownell product families, including but not limited to flexible insulations, varnishes and resins, motors, parts, equipment, and lead and magnet wire.
- Michael McIlwain, President of Essex Brownell and Hi-Wire, stated that the lease renewal signals a long-term investment in this location and in Essex Brownell overall.
- McIlwain noted that during the global pandemic, stability and reliability are very important aspects of doing business, and the renewal sends a clear signal that Essex Brownell is committed to the Northeast and Mid-Atlantic regions while sourcing globally and delivering locally.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand for Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, and Essex Brownell.
- Superior Essex has over 3,000 employees across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is the leading global supplier of magnet wire and is at the forefront of applying communication fiber and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- Superior Essex's technologies are crucial for creating disruptive advances in the electrification of automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Essex Brownell operates as an international distributor of magnet wire and auxiliary products used to build and repair motors, generators, transformers, and electrical components.
- Essex Brownell provides discrete materials along with custom manufacturing and rewinding services through its Essex Active transformation facilities.
- Superior Essex corporate headquarters is located at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
