## Facts

- Adient plc is a leading global supplier of automotive seating and interior components.
- The company was established in 2016 through a spin-off from Johnson Controls.
- Adient designs, engineers and manufactures complete seat assemblies, seat structures, mechanisms, foams, textiles, trim and electronics.
- The product portfolio spans a wide range of seating solutions, from entry-level designs to luxury and high-performance seats.
- The portfolio extends to interior modules such as door panels and center consoles.
- Adient serves major original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) around the world.
- The company works closely with automakers to develop lightweight, comfortable and safety-oriented seating systems.
- Capabilities include concept design and prototyping, industrialization, volume production and aftermarket support.
- Adient has invested in advanced materials and manufacturing techniques such as 3D weaving and composite structures.
- Investments aim to meet evolving industry demands for electrification, connectivity and sustainable materials.
- The company operates in more than 30 countries across North and South America, Europe and Asia.
- Adient maintains over 200 manufacturing facilities globally.
- The company employs approximately 70,000 people.
- Adient is headquartered in Plymouth, Michigan.
- The company has regional engineering and sales centers in key automotive hubs.
- Since its public debut, Adient has been led by CEO R. Bruce McDonald.
- CEO R. Bruce McDonald focuses on operational efficiency, digital transformation and strengthening customer partnerships to drive long-term growth in the competitive global automotive supply chain.
