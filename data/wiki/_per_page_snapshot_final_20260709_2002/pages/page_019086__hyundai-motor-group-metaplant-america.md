# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- In May 2022, Hyundai Motor Group announced it would open Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA), a state-of-the-art U.S. smart manufacturing and battery facility in the Savannah region that would produce electric and hybrid vehicles.
- The HMGMA project involves a $7.6 billion investment.
- HMGMA will create 8,500 new jobs.
- HMGMA has a production capacity of 300,000 vehicles with the ability to produce up to 500,000 annually.
- Production at HMGMA began in October 2024.
- HMGMA is currently producing the IONIQ9 and IONIQ5 models.
- Hyundai Motor Group cited speed-to-market and workforce as key reasons for choosing the Savannah region for HMGMA.
- HMGMA suppliers are locating in the Savannah region, including Tier 1 suppliers Daechang Seat Savannah Corp., USA, Seoyon E-HWA and PHA in the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- The Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center is located 8.6 miles (13.8 km) from HMGMA.
- Supplier sites at the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center are still available.
