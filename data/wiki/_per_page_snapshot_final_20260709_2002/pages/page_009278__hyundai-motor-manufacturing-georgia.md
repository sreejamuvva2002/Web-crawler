# Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-05

**Source:** [First Kia hybrid from South Georgia Hyundai EV factory announced](https://www.ajc.com/news/2026/05/first-hybrid-soon-to-roll-out-of-south-georgia-hyundai-ev-factory/)  ·  `page_009278`

## Facts

- Hyundai is expanding its workforce and launching hybrid vehicle manufacturing at its electric vehicle factory near Savannah.
- The facility is located in Ellabelle, near Savannah, Georgia.
- The South Korean automaker will formally announce production of the Kia Sportage hybrid in the coming weeks.
- An evening work shift will start in September.
- Hiring for the second shift has reached 66%.
- Hyundai expects to meet its hiring needs and have all second shift workers in training by July.
- Brent Stubbs, the manufacturing facility’s chief administrative officer, stated the plant was designed for ultimate flexibility to build both electric vehicles and hybrids.
- The facility aims to reach a production capacity of half-a-million plus cars per year.
- Demand for hybrids is surging.
