# HSBMA (Hyundai SK Battery Manufacturing America)

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-27

**Source:** [The North American battery joint venture between Hyundai Motor Group a - Shanghai Metals Market (SMM)](https://news.metal.com/newscontent/103830125-hyundai-motor-group-and-sk-on-confirm-jv-name-hsbma)  ·  `page_009306`

## Facts

- The North American battery joint venture between Hyundai Motor Group and SK On has been officially named HSBMA (Hyundai SK Battery Manufacturing America).
- The joint venture was announced in April 2023.
- The project has reached over 90% completion.
- The plant is scheduled to begin commercial operations this year.
- The plant is located in Bartow County, Georgia.
- The plant will have an annual production capacity of 35GWh.
- The 35GWh capacity is equivalent to batteries for approximately 300,000 electric vehicles.
- Hyundai Motor Group and SK On have jointly invested a total of $5 billion in the venture.
- Each company holds a 50% stake in the venture.
