# Rivian

**Source:** [I drove the Rivian R1S for a week — here's my pros and cons | Tom's Guide](https://www.tomsguide.com/vehicle-tech/evs/2026-rivian-r1s-review)  ·  `page_059046`

## Facts

- The 2026 Rivian R1S Quad delivers an insane 1,025-hp performance from its four electric motors.
- The 2026 Rivian R1S introduces a more future-proof NACS charging port that opens up access to Tesla Supercharger locations across the country, while an included CCS1 DC adapter lets you charge at other stations.
- The 2026 Rivian R1S Quad has an MSRP of $121,990 and was tested at $124,890.
- The Dual Standard trim starts at $76,990.
- The 2026 Rivian R1S Dual Standard features a 92.5 kWh battery, dual-motor AWD, 0 to 60 mph in 4.5 seconds, and a range of 270 miles.
- The 2026 Rivian R1S Quad features a 140 kWh battery, quad-motor AWD, 0 to 60 mph in 2.6 seconds, and a range of 374 miles.
- During testing, the 2026 Rivian R1S Quad achieved an average efficiency of 1.85 mi/kWh over 165.4 miles driven.
- The new NACS port on the 2026 Rivian R1S managed an average Level 1 charging speed of 2.49 mi/hr at home.
- Rivian's Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS), officially known as the Rivian Autonomy Platform, includes adaptive cruise control that maintains speed and reacts promptly to traffic conditions.
- The 2026 Rivian R1S Quad features stadium headlights with Adaptive Drive Beam technology that dynamically adjusts lighting to avoid blinding other drivers.
- The 2026 model introduces RAD Tuner, allowing granular control over ride height and acceleration, and serves as a gateway to the Kick Turn feature for tighter pivots on loose surfaces.
- The 2026 Rivian R1S offers three-row seating accommodating up to seven passengers, with fold-flat third-row seats to expand trunk space.
- The 2026 Rivian R1S includes usable front trunk (frunk) space capable of holding several grocery bags.
- The tested price of the 2026 Rivian R1S Quad ($124,890) is compared to the $163,000+ Mercedes-Benz G 580 and the $99,915 Cadillac Escalade IQ.
