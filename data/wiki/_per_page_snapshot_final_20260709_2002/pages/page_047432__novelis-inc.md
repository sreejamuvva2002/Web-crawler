# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2012-10-11

**Source:** [Increased demand of flat-rolled aluminum incites Novelis Inc. to triple capacity | Waste Dive](https://www.wastedive.com/news/increased-demand-of-flat-rolled-aluminum-incites-novelis-inc-to-triple-cap/62852/)  ·  `page_047432`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is the largest supplier of flat-rolled aluminum products to the global auto industry.
- Novelis plans to more than triple its recycling capacity by 2020 to meet increased demand.
- The company currently recycles 1.2 million tons of aluminum.
- Novelis hopes to reach a recycling capacity of 2.1 million metric tons by 2015.
- Novelis aims to reach a recycling capacity of 4 million tons by 2020.
- Derek Prichett, vice president for global recycling at Novelis, provided the capacity figures in an interview.
- Novelis plans to make 50 percent of its products from recycled metal by 2015.
- Novelis aims to make as much as 80 percent of its products from recycled metal by 2020.
- At present, about 39 percent of Novelis's products are made from recycled metal.
- Prichett provided these details in Seoul on Oct. 9.
- Novelis is the U.S. unit of India’s Hindalco Industries Ltd. (HNDL).
- To reach its global capacity goals, Novelis plans to build two new plants, one in Germany and one in South Korea.
