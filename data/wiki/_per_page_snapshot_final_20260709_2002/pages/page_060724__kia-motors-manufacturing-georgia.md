# Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-21

**Source:** [Uncategorized Archives - Kia Georgia](https://www.kiageorgia.com/category/uncategorized/)  ·  `page_060724`

## Facts

- Following a $217 million expansion, EV6 joins the EV9 as the plant’s second electric vehicle.
- The plant is currently assembling five overall vehicles in West Point, Georgia.
- On Thursday (Nov. 21, 2024), it was announced at the 2024 Los Angeles Auto Show that the all-electric 2025 EV6 SUV will be assembled at the plant.
- Kia Georgia celebrated 15 years of manufacturing in Georgia on Saturday (Nov. 20, 2024).
- Production at Kia Georgia began on Nov. 16, 2009.
- Since beginning production, Kia Georgia has assembled more than 4.5 million vehicles.
- Kia Georgia has helped create more than 14,000 jobs in the West Point, Georgia region.
- Eligible Kia owners will receive $500 in assistance for the purchase or lease of select new Kia vehicles between October 4, 2024, and January 2, 2025.
- Kia America and Kia Georgia, Inc., donated $250,000 to the American Red Cross to aid in relief efforts.
- On November 28, 2017, Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia (KMMG) delivered a significant financial contribution to The Ray, Georgia’s living laboratory for transportation technology on Interstate 85.
- The $26,000 gift was the second installment of a three-year commitment for the WheelRight® tire safety station.
- The WheelRight® tire safety station was installed in December of 2016 at The Ray.
