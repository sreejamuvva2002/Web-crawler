## Facts

- Jefferson Southern Corporation operates as a Tier One automotive supplier in the motor vehicle manufacturing sector.
- The company is based in Rockmart, Georgia.
- JSC has between 201 and 500 employees.
- JSC concentrates on producing parts that form the vehicle interior shell, often referred to as the white body.
- Its principal customer is Honda Manufacturing Alabama.
- Honda Manufacturing Alabama assembles the Odyssey minivan, Pilot SUV, and Ridgeline pickup.
- The company focuses on interior components that contribute to the car's cabin and interior assembly.
- As a mid-sized Tier One supplier, JSC holds a direct role in the automotive supply chain, working with an original equipment manufacturer.
