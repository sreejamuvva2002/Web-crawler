# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-06-17

**Source:** [TCI Powder Coatings, BASF Announce Strategic Alliance - Coatings World](https://www.coatingsworld.com/issues/2016-10-01/view_breaking-news/tci-powder-coatings-basf-announce-strategic-alliance/7890)  ·  `page_051173`

## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is a subsidiary of RPM International.
- TCI Powder Coatings and BASF entered into an agreement that allows TCI to sell the BASF CathoGuard electrocoat technology to specified TCI market segments.
- Jack Bostock, TCI vice president of sales and marketing, stated that the alliance builds on TCI’s commitment to market differentiation.
- Bostock noted that pairing the CathoGuard technology series with premium powder coatings is a win-win for customers looking for performance.
- The partnership aims to deliver an innovative solution meeting global standards to make a significant difference for customers’ production lines.
- The alliance will help customers increase throughput while reducing costs.
- The BASF CathoGuard coating combines several unique performance qualities for parts that are more difficult to paint efficiently, including those with multiple angles and crevices.
- CathoGuard improves the application process as well as coverage and corrosion edge protection.
- CathoGuard lowers total material costs by nearly 20 percent.
- TCI market segments with access to this technology through the new alliance include off-road and heavy equipment, general finishing, office furniture, and appliance.
- CathoGuard will also be available to custom coaters that supply major subassemblies and modular components for the automotive market.
- Thaddeus Lepkowski, BASF tier metals market manager, stated they are very pleased that TCI can deliver their innovative product line more broadly to customers.
- Lepkowski noted that the chemistry in CathoGuard coating achieves a flawless coat on hard-to-paint parts found in many of TCI’s markets where finish quality and durability are critical.
