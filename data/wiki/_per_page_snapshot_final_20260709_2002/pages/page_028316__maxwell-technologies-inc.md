# Maxwell Technologies, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 2016-07-01

## Facts

- Maxwell Technologies, Inc. is a leading developer and manufacturer of ultracapacitor-based energy storage and power delivery solutions.
- Jörg Buchheim was appointed to Maxwell’s board of directors as a new independent director effective July 1, 2016.
- Buchheim served as Maxwell’s senior vice president and chief sales officer since March 2016.
- Buchheim resigned from Maxwell on June 30, 2016, to take a new position as the chief executive officer of INALFA Roof Systems B.V., a top 3 global supplier of vehicle roof systems located in Europe.
- Buchheim is a seasoned global sales leader and an automotive industry veteran.
- From 2002 through 2015 he worked at HELLA KGaA Hueck & Co. in a series of senior sales and management positions.
- His most recent role at HELLA was president and chief executive officer of HELLA China and a member of the HELLA Group Management Board based out of Shanghai.
- He previously served as HELLA’s Global Key Account Manager for Indian OEMs and Hyundai/Kia as well as Vice President Sales and Marketing China, Shanghai.
- Prior to joining HELLA, Buchheim worked in European Key Account Sales for Mitsubishi Electric and in the Project Management for Spoerle / Arrow.
- David Schlotterbeck, chairman of the board, stated that Jörg brings key automotive industry experience and relationships as well as an extensive background in operational and executive management to complement their already experienced Board of Directors.
- Schlotterbeck noted that Jörg’s deep knowledge of Chinese business practices and his strong relationships with key customers and industry executives will be an invaluable resource on the board.
- Maxwell's ultracapacitor products provide safe and reliable power solutions for applications in consumer and industrial electronics, transportation and telecommunications.
- Maxwell's high-voltage grading and coupling capacitors help to ensure the safety and reliability of electric utility infrastructure and other applications involving transport, distribution and measurement of high-voltage electrical energy.
