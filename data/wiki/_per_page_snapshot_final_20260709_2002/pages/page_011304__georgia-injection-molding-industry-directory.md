# Georgia Injection Molding Industry Directory

## Facts

- Atlanta Molding specializes in high-volume production runs ranging from millions down to a few thousand parts.
- Atlanta Molding offers shot capacities ranging from 3 oz to 39 oz.
- Atlanta Molding provides mold design and construction, injection molding, assembly and subassembly, and packaging services.
- Atlanta Molding works with a wide range of plastic resins to deliver quality parts on time at competitive prices.
- Advanced Plastiform has been providing custom plastic products and parts to various industries since 1988.
- Advanced Plastiform operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing hub in North Carolina while serving customers throughout the Southeast and Mid-Atlantic regions.
- Accurate Plastics & Mold is a family-owned injection molding company founded by Jeff Reuschle in 1991.
- Accurate Plastics & Mold's team has over 80 years of combined industry experience.
- EVCO Plastics CAL1 facility is located in Calhoun, Georgia.
- EVCO Plastics operates a certified ISO 9001 injection molding facility spanning 110,000 square feet.
- EVCO Plastics operates 20 plastic injection molding machines.
- EVCO Plastics specializes in in-mold decorating/labeling (IML), molded-in color, overmolding, insert molding, gas assist molding, secondary operations, tool and mold building, large part molding, metal to plastic conversions, contract manufacturing, plastic packaging, and prototyping.
- ADCO Plastics Corporation is located in Metro Atlanta, Georgia.
- ADCO Plastics has over 45 years of experience serving medical, commercial, industrial, transportation, and retail markets.
- ADCO Plastics specializes in manufacturing custom injection molded assemblies and parts using commodity grade and engineered resins and thermoplastic rubbers.
- Murray Plastics offers design and engineering services, molds and tooling expertise, and high-quality injection molding solutions.
- Murray Plastics portfolio includes projects in the film industry, welding industry, and military optics sector.
- Murray Plastics operates as a founder-led United States manufacturing company.
- Peliton Plastics is based in Valdosta, Georgia.
- Peliton Plastics was founded in 1996 by Robert McKinnon.
- Peliton Plastics operates from a state-of-the-art 35,000 sq. Ft facility.
- Peliton Plastics provides mold making, part assembly, packaging, machining, ultrasonic welding, and shipping services.
- Plastic Industries (Custom Molding) is located in East Tennessee but offers same-day shipping to Georgia.
- Plastic Industries utilizes injection molding machines ranging from 88 ton to 1500 ton capacity.
- Plastic Industries achieves daily capacities ranging from several hundred large parts to over 500,000 smaller parts.
- Plastic Industries offers design and engineering services, fully equipped tool rooms, warehousing, integrated ISO 9001 quality control, press side packaging, hot stamping, and sonic welding.
- TerBeek Molding & Tool is a privately held injection molding company based in Peachtree City, Georgia.
- TerBeek Molding & Tool was established in 2005 by industry veterans Brian and John TerBeek.
- TerBeek Molding & Tool specializes in high-precision plastic injection molds for medical device, automotive, food packaging, general industrial, and consumer products industries.
- TerBeek Molding & Tool operates from a new, modern, climate-controlled, 22,000 sq. ft. facility just south of Atlanta.
- 20/20 Custom Molded Plastics specializes in high and low pressure injection molding with a focus on cost reduction applications.
- 20/20 Custom Molded Plastics has multiple locations across Ohio, Indiana, and Georgia.
- Advanced Molding Inc is based in Georgia and has been providing custom plastic injection molding services since 2002.
- Advanced Molding Inc expertise covers Medical, Electrical, Construction, Telecommunications, and Recreational Industries.
- Advanced Molding Inc is equipped with 14 state-of-the-art machines ranging from 60 to 400 tons capacity.
- Premier Plastic Molding & Tooling, Inc is an Atlanta-based company specializing in plastic injection molding and tooling.
- SPF Plastic Group offers contract molding services with machine clamp sizes ranging from 300 to 1800 tons.
- SPF Plastic Group provides branding options, daily QC programs, in-house tool repair shop, material recycling/grinding, and preventative maintenance programs.
- Parkway Products, LLC has locations in Atlanta, Georgia and Machesney Park, Illinois.
- Parkway Products, LLC specializes in High Performance Polymer Molding using Ultra Polymers such as Torlon® (PAI), Ultem™ (PEI), Amodel® (PPA), Ryton® (PPS), and PEEK™.
- Parkway Products, LLC materials are chosen for lightweight properties and ability to withstand high stress, pressure, and temperature.
- Parkway Products, LLC serves Aerospace & Defense, Industrial, Energy & Infrastructure, and Automotive markets.
- Parkway Products, LLC provides secondary operations including machining and dual disc grinding.
- RPM Fast offers fast turnaround on injection molded parts using Aluminum, P20 Steel, and Steel Tools.
- RPM Fast provides T1 samples within 12 days and can complete full production in 25 days.
- RPM Fast expertise includes complex tool designs, two-shot molding, family tools, inserts, multi-cavity tools, insert molding, pad printing, ultrasonic welding, and assembly.
- RPM Fast offers domestic options including ITAR compliance.
- Plastics and Innovations is located in Mableton, GA.
- Plastics and Innovations pre-production and design services typically take 2 to 3 weeks to complete.
- Plastics and Innovations offers prototyping to test initial concepts before mass production.
- Recreational Group (CH3) was founded by industry experts focused on improved quality and cost control in a sustainable operation.
- Recreational Group is recognized as a Top 25 World Class Processor by Plastics Technology Magazine.
- Recreational Group offers injection molding, in-mold labeling, color molding, and contract manufacturing capabilities.
- Recreational Group is located in Dalton, GA and Orlando, FL.
- Underwood Mold Co is a well-established injection molding company based in metro Atlanta with over 50 years of experience.
- Underwood Mold Co provides custom injection molding, mold design and fabrication, and packaging products like aerosol caps, pails, and lids.
- Underwood Mold Co maintains a full in-house tool shop for PM & repair and offers a wide range of press sizes for assembly and secondary operations.
- The Rodon Group specializes in custom plastic injection molding offering various thermoplastics such as ABS, LDPE, HDPE, PC, nylon, HIPS, and PP.
- Jabil Atlanta offers thermoplastic polymer solutions for diverse packaging applications.
- Jabil Atlanta capabilities include accelerated product introduction, high cavitation and precision injection molding, design and in-mold labeling, and sustainable packaging options.
- Jabil leverages a global network of 250,000 professionals across more than 100 strategic sites worldwide for supply chain management and sourcing.
- Thomson Plastics features broad press capability ranging from 66 tons to 4400 tons of clamp force.
- Thomson Plastics specializes in producing medium and large-sized parts with Class-A, high gloss, and clear acrylic surface finishes.
- Thomson Plastics offers gas-assisted injection molding for large, dense parts with hollow interiors to achieve weight reduction and cycle time reduction.
- Thomson Plastics recent investments have been made to enhance high pressure gas control equipment.
