# Kia Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-16

**Source:** [Kia - Autos Drive America](https://autosdriveamerica.org/kia/)  ·  `page_010164`

## Facts

- Kia Georgia celebrated 15 years of production on November 16, 2024.
- Kia Georgia is Kia’s first North American manufacturing facility.
- To date, Kia Georgia has helped create more than 14,000 local jobs.
- To date, Kia Georgia has produced more than 4.5 million vehicles.
- The Kia Georgia project was announced on March 13, 2006.
- A groundbreaking ceremony for the facility took place on October 20, 2006.
- Kia Georgia began production of its first vehicle, a Kia Sorento, on November 16, 2009.
- The Kia Georgia facility sits on a 2,200-acre site.
- The facility has a production capacity of 350,000 vehicles per year.
- Kia Georgia is nearing completion of a $217 million expansion.
- The $217 million expansion will allow the plant to produce EVs alongside its traditional products.
- Kia Georgia currently produces the all-electric EV9 SUV.
- Kia Georgia currently produces the Telluride, Sorento, and Sportage SUVs.
