# Wabash

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-18

**Source:** [Wabash Expands Southeast Network with Atlanta Service Center | WNC Stock News](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/WNC/wabash-expands-southeast-footprint-to-strengthen-customer-access-and-b761yq8rwss2.html)  ·  `page_014779`

## Facts

- Wabash announced a major expansion of its network in the Southeast, focusing on three strategic initiatives in the Atlanta metro area.
- The company opened a new Parts & Service center in the Atlanta-area.
- The new facility joins existing Wabash service centers in California, Florida, Ohio, Pennsylvania, and Texas.
- The expansion will serve customers across Georgia, Alabama, South Carolina, and Tennessee.
- The Atlanta site offers Ready-to-Mount (RTM) truck bodies for dry freight and platform applications with rapid turnaround times for in-stock chassis.
- Doors to the new Atlanta-area Parts and Services center opened on September 18, 2025.
- Fleetco, a long-standing Wabash dealer, will now represent Wabash in the greater Atlanta market with trailer sales.
- Bill Tirrill serves as president at Fleetco Inc.
- Fleetco has more than 14 years of experience working closely with Wabash.
- Wabash added three CS Truck and Trailer locations to its Preferred Partner Network in Georgia.
- The Preferred Partner Network addition brings expert service, maintenance, and Wabash Genuine Parts to customers through authorized third-party dealerships.
- The Preferred Partner Network model is central to Wabash’s strategy of making high-quality parts more accessible through a seamless national network.
- Dave Hill is the vice president of Parts & Services at Wabash.
- Dave Hill stated that the expansions reflect how Wabash is building an extensive partner ecosystem that puts parts, service and support closer to customers.
- The Atlanta expansions mean faster turnaround, more coverage and better reliability for fleets operating across Georgia, Alabama, South Carolina and Tennessee.
- On the day the news was published, WNC stock gained 1.09%, reflecting a mild positive market reaction.
- Wabash designs, manufactures, and services an extensive range of products supporting first-to-final mile operations, including dry and refrigerated trailers and truck bodies, platform trailers, tank trailers, and structural composites.
- Wabash is headquartered in Lafayette, Indiana.
