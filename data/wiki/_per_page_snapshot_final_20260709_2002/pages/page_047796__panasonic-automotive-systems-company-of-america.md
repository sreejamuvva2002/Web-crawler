# Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-01-11

**Source:** [Panasonic Automotive Brings Expansive, Artificial Intelligence-Enhanced Situational Awareness to the Driver Experience with Augmented Reality Head-Up Display - Jan 11, 2021](https://ces.vporoom.com/2021-01-11-Panasonic-Automotive-Brings-Expansive-Artificial-Intelligence-Enhanced-Situational-Awareness-to-the-Driver-Experience-with-Augmented-Reality-Head-Up-Display)  ·  `page_047796`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems of America introduced its new Augmented Reality (AR) Head-Up Display at CES 2021 on January 11, 2021.
- The AR HUD combines advances in optics, volume optimization, and imaging technology with AI from the SPYDR cockpit domain controller to render near-field and far-field content for vehicle data, object/pedestrian detection, and route guidance.
- Scott Kirchner serves as president of Panasonic Automotive and executive director of Panasonic Smart Mobility.
- Traditional HUDs only cover a small section of the road, whereas Panasonic's AR HUD solutions cover more of the roadway with traditional cluster content in the near field and 3D overlays in the far field.
- The AR HUD projects 3D, AI-driven key information into the driver's line of sight to help reduce driver distraction and potentially increase safety on the road.
- Panasonic's AR HUD development utilizes a PRIZM process to address all aspects of users' needs today, tomorrow, and in the future.
- Panasonic collaborates with Envisics to provide striking, dual-plane, high-resolution laser holography for the AR HUD.
- Envisics develops a patent-protected, dynamic holographic platform that enables true holography across multiple mobility applications.
- Panasonic collaborates with Phiar to provide 3D localization technology and AI navigation and situation awareness analytics for the system.
- Phiar develops a patent-protected spatial-AI, AR navigation platform.
- The AR HUD could provide an important added level of comfort and assurance for autonomous vehicle passengers in the future.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America is headquartered in Peachtree City, Georgia.
- The company maintains sales, marketing, and engineering operations in Farmington Hills, Michigan.
- Panasonic Automotive operates as a division company of Panasonic Corporation of North America.
- Panasonic Automotive is a leading global supplier of automotive infotainment and connectivity system solutions.
- Panasonic Corporation of North America is headquartered in Newark, New Jersey.
- Panasonic Corporation of North America is the principal North American subsidiary of Osaka, Japan-based Panasonic Corporation.
- Panasonic was named one of Interbrand's Top 100 Best Global Brands of 2020.
