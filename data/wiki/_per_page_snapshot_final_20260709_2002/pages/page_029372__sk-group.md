## Facts

- Georgia is home to some of SK’s most significant investments, including manufacturing sites for advanced batteries that are part of U.S. “battery belt.”
- SK Battery America is the first U.S. battery manufacturing site of SK On.
- SK Battery America currently operates two EV battery plants in Commerce, Georgia.
- SK Battery America's facility address is 1760 SK Boulevard, Commerce, GA 30529.
- SK Inc. C&C is an advanced IT company providing local support in Georgia for the SK Battery America EV battery site.
- SK Inc. C&C's location is at 2915 Premiere Parkway, Duluth, GA 30097.
- Hyundai Motor Group and SK On formed a joint venture to establish an EV battery cell plant in Bartow County, Georgia.
- The Hyundai Motor Group and SK On joint venture EV battery cell plant is expected to be operational by the end of 2025.
- The joint venture facility location is Cartersville, GA.
- SK tes, a subsidiary of SK Ecoplant, is an electronic waste management company and global leader in e-waste recycling, IT asset disposition, and battery recycling.
- SK tes has a location at 5238 Royal Woods Pkwy, Suite 110, Tucker, GA 30084.
- SK On Tennessee is an electric vehicle battery manufacturing facility in Stanton, Tennessee.
- SK On Tennessee is expected to start production in 2028.
- GridFlex is a joint venture between SK Gas and SK Eternix that invests in energy storage and renewable energy projects across the US.
- Key Capture Energy identifies, develops, constructs and operates energy storage solutions (ESS) to foster a greater deployment of renewable energy.
- SK Enmove Americas Inc., a subsidiary of SK Enmove, is a supplier of auto transmission oil, engine oil, and other lubricants for the automotive industry.
- SK Gas is expanding beyond its legacy LPG business as it invests in new sustainable solutions like hydrogen charging stations and hydrogen fuel cells.
- SK Signet is a global leader in smart, convenient charging solutions for all types of electric vehicles - with a special emphasis on fast and ultra-fast EV chargers.
- Solid Power is an industry-leading developer of next-generation all-solid-state battery technology for electric vehicles.
- Group14 Technologies is a global provider of silicon-carbon composite materials for lithium-ion-powered devices and vehicles.
- SK Siltron CSS produces silicon carbide (SiC) wafers for the semiconductor industry that can be used for sustainable solutions in EVs, renewable energy, and telecommunications.
- PassKey, a subsidiary of SK innovation E&S, is a U.S.-based energy transition business with investments in technologies such as EV charging, energy storage and advanced software solutions support the move toward net-zero carbon emissions.
- SK innovation E&S, traditionally focused on natural gas, is pursuing renewable energy, clean hydrogen, and other alternative energy solutions in the transition to a carbon-neutral LNG value chain.
- SK Geo Centric is global chemical company accelerating its transition into eco-friendly materials and recycling solutions.
