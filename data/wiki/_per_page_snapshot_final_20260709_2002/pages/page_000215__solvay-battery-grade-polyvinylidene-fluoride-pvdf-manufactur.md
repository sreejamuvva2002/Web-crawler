# Solvay Battery-Grade Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF) Manufacturing Facility

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-30

**Source:** [DOE/EA-2237: Solvay Battery-Grade PVDF Manufacturing Facility; Richmond County, Georgia | Department of Energy](https://www.energy.gov/nepa/doeea-2237-solvay-battery-grade-pvdf-manufacturing-facility-richmond-county-georgia)  ·  `page_000215`

## Facts

- National Energy Technology Laboratory (NETL) prepared a Draft EA (DOE/EA-2237) for the Solvay Battery-Grade Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF) Manufacturing Facility project in Richmond County, Georgia.
- The Draft EA evaluates the potential environmental consequences of DOE providing financial assistance to Solvay Specialty Polymers USA, LLC.
- Solvay Specialty Polymers USA, LLC is a member of the Syensqo Group.
- The project involves the construction of a chemical manufacturing facility for the production of PVDF (a highly non-reactive thermoplastic) for use in electric vehicle batteries.
- At maximum capacity, the facility would support the production of more than 5 million EV batteries per year.
- There are no public comment opportunities at this time.
