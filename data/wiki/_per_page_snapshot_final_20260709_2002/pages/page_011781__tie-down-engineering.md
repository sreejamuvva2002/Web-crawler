## Facts

- Tie Down Engineering acquired the assets of Maxilator.
- Chuck MacKarvich serves as president and CEO of Tie Down Engineering.
- Maxilator’s products are sold through distributors and independent dealers in more than 40 states and Canada.
- Maxilator products gather, lift, load, split, transport and offload hay.
- Farm customers for Maxilator include hay growers, cattle, and horse farmers.
- Michael Goss represents Maxilator regarding the acquisition.
- Tie Down Engineering has nearly 50 years’ experience in contract manufacturing.
- Tie Down Engineering holds 90 patents.
- Tie Down Engineering produces more than 5,500 products.
- Tie Down Engineering is a preferred and trusted on-demand contract manufacturer for hundreds of companies across the nation.
- Tie Down Engineering's customers include leaders in manufactured housing, roofing, marine, OEM trailer and cable & hardware.
- Tie Down Engineering operates nine manufacturing facilities covering more than 650,000 square feet at its headquarters in Georgia.
- Tie Down Engineering provides services across the manufacturing cycle including innovation, design, quality/testing, delivery and support.
- Tie Down Engineering's corporate ethos is Ingenuity as a Service.
