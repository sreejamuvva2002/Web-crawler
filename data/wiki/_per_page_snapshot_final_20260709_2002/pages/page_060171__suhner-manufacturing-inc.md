# Suhner Manufacturing, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2017-06-14

**Source:** [Small Diameter Medical Grade Flexible Shafts for High-Performance Surgical Instruments - Medical Design and Outsourcing](https://www.medicaldesignandoutsourcing.com/small-diameter-medical-grade-flexible-shafts-for-high-performance-surgical-instruments/)  ·  `page_060171`

## Facts

- Suhner Manufacturing, Inc. is located in Rome, Georgia.
- The company introduced a line of standard and custom flexible shafts for rotary power transmission on medical, orthopedic, endoscopic, and laparoscopic instruments.
- Torsion stable rotary flexible shafts are designed to provide rotary motion on surgical tools at the focal point for minimally invasive procedures.
- The flexible shafts offer torsional strength and minimal deflection with uniformly smooth rotational behavior to ensure proper torsion and tactile feel on the distal end for surgeons.
- Shafts are available with options such as passivation, electropolishing, and Teflon coating.
- Products are provided as cleaned and assembled units to suit stringent medical procedure applications.
- The shafts provide precisely controlled rotary power transmission in limited space.
- A current application features a flexible shaft measuring only 0.024” in diameter for endoscopic surgeries.
- Other applications include endovascular aneurysm/aortic repair (EVAR) and thoracic endovascular (TEVAR) surgery, stapling devices, orthopedic drills, surgical screwdrivers, and laparoscopic devices.
- Shafts are available in clockwise, counterclockwise, and bi-directional motion designs.
- Materials include Type 302, 304, 316, and 17-7 stainless steel, as well as high-carbon steel.
- Suhner engineering works with OEMs to devise functional solutions for specific instrument, tool, and device applications.
- Medical grade rotary flexible shafts are available in diameters from 0.024” to 0.500”.
