# Northwest Georgia Joint Economic Development Authority

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Northwest Georgia Joint Economic Development Authority](https://northwestgeorgia.us/)  ·  `page_048253`

## Facts

- Georgia's upper left hand corner offers superior accessibility, stunning vistas and a work force primed for the manufacturing renaissance.
- Economic development in the region is led by the Northwest Georgia Joint Development Authority.
- The authority is located just minutes from Chattanooga, Tennessee.
- The Northwest Georgia JDA is comprised of Catoosa, Chattooga, Dade and Walker Counties.
- The region features high performing schools, top healthcare facilities and a low cost of living.
- The counties are located on or near three of the nation’s interstates: I-75, I-59, and I-24.
- The region is also located on or near U.S. Highway 27, known as "The Scenic Hometown Highway."
- Northwest Georgia has abundant access to water sources in the area.
- The region is less than two and a half hours from Atlanta, Nashville, and Knoxville.
- Northwest Georgia offers easy access to rail systems and commercial airports.
- The area provides a highly skilled workforce.
- Higher education opportunities are available at technical colleges as well as research universities.
- The counties are located in the right-to-work state of Georgia.
- The region is positioned as the best place in the Southeastern U.S. for manufacturing or distribution businesses.
