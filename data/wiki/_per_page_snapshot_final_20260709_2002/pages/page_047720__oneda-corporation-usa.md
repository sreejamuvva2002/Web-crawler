## Facts

- Oneda Corporation (USA) is a family-owned, Georgia-based contract manufacturer specializing in precision metal stamping, assembly, and tool and die designs.
- The company utilizes innovative robotic transfer and progressive metal stamping capabilities with press sizes ranging from 25 to 500 tons.
- Oneda Corporation is located in Columbus, Georgia.
- Oneda Corporation operates as a subsidiary of Oneda Electric Corporation.
- The company serves the automotive, medical, heating and air, transportation, refrigeration, and consumer electronics industries.
- Oneda Corporation has 11-50 employees.
- The company is classified under Motor Vehicle Manufacturing.
- Oneda Corporation believes in paying for craftsmanship and quality instead of overseas shipping and filler costs.
