# GreenPower Motor Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Alternative Fuels Data Center: Vehicle Search](https://afdc.energy.gov/vehicles/search/results?page=14)  ·  `page_002754`

## Facts

- Offers the Greenkraft Van, an electric-only medium- and heavy-duty vehicle with a 162-mile range and 101 kWh battery capacity.
- The Greenkraft Van uses a Bosch 170 kW power source and has a GVWR of 10,250 lbs according to the manufacturer.
- Offers the BEAST, an electric-only vehicle with a 150-mile range and 194 kWh battery capacity.
- The BEAST uses a Dana TM4 PMSM 350 kW power source, has a maximum seating of 90, and a GVWR of 42,990 lbs according to the manufacturer.
- Charging for the BEAST takes 8 hours at Level 2 (19.2 kW), 2 hours via DC Fast/CCS-1 (85 kW), or 3 hours wirelessly.
- Offers the EV250, an electric-only vehicle with a 163-mile range and 260 kWh battery capacity.
- The EV250 uses a Prestolite 160 kW electric motor, has a maximum seating of 26, and a 620 V battery according to the manufacturer.
- Charging for the EV250 takes 2.25 hours at 120 kW via DC Fast or wireless.
- Offers the EV350, an electric-only vehicle with a 212-mile range and 400 kWh battery capacity.
- The EV350 uses a Prestolite 350 kW electric motor and has a maximum seating of 40 according to the manufacturer.
- Charging for the EV350 takes 2.75 hours at 148 kW via DC Fast or wireless.
- Offers the EV500/EV550, an electric-only double-deck vehicle with a 175-mile range and up to 600 kWh battery capacity according to the manufacturer.
- The EV500/EV550 uses a Siemens electric motor and has a maximum seating of 100.
- Offers the EV Star and AV Star, electric-only vehicles with a 150-mile range and 118 kWh (576 V) battery capacity according to the manufacturer.
- The EV Star/AV Star uses a Dana TM4 150 kW power source, has a maximum seating of 19, and the AV Star includes an ADA wheelchair lift option.
- Charging for the EV Star/AV Star takes 8 hours at 19.2 kW at Level 2, 2 hours at 60 kW via DC Fast, or 2 hours at 60 kW wirelessly.
- Offers the EV Star Cab & Chassis, an electric-only vehicle with a 150-mile range and 118 kWh battery capacity according to the manufacturer.
- The EV Star Cab & Chassis uses Dana TM4 150 kW or 160 kW power sources, has a maximum seating of 20, and a carrying capacity of 7000 lbs according to the manufacturer.
- The EV Star Cab & Chassis is available in right-hand drive configuration with upfit options including stakebed, utility bed, and refrigerated/Reefer box.
- Charging for the EV Star Cab & Chassis takes 8 hours at 19.2 kW at Level 2, 2 hours at 60 kW via DC Fast, or 2 hours at 60 kW wirelessly.
- Offers the EV Star Cargo+, a box van based on chassis/cab with a 150-mile range and 118 kWh battery capacity according to the manufacturer.
- The EV Star Cargo+ uses Dana TM4 160 kW or 150 kW power sources, has a carrying capacity of 5000 lbs according to the manufacturer.
- Charging for the EV Star Cargo+ takes 8 hours at 19.2 kW at Level 2, 2 hours at 60 kW via DC Fast, or 2 hours at 60 kW wirelessly.
