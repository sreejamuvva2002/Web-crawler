# NeoVolta BESS Manufacturing Facility

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-22

**Source:** [NeoVolta BESS manufacturing plant should open in August in Georgia](https://www.solarpowerworldonline.com/2026/06/neovolta-bess-manufacturing-plant-should-open-in-august-in-georgia/)  ·  `page_060335`

## Facts

- NeoVolta provided an update on its battery manufacturing facility being built in Georgia.
- Site acceptance testing is targeted for completion by the end of August 2026.
- Production on BESS for residential, commercial and utility-scale applications will begin in Q3.
- The effort in Pendergrass, Georgia, is a joint-venture of NeoVolta, LONGi and PotisEdge.
- The companies announced the 2-GWh factory earlier this year.
- NeoVolta has received a foreign entity of concern (FEOC) compliance opinion from a national law firm.
- The FEOC compliance opinion confirms that the Georgia battery manufacturing facility is structured to meet applicable FEOC/PFE eligibility requirements.
- Ardes Johnson, CEO of NeoVolta, stated that receiving a FEOC compliance opinion from a top-tier law firm is a landmark milestone for NeoVolta and for NeoVolta Power.
- The opinion formally validates that the Pendergrass facility meets the compliance standard the utility-scale market demands.
- The opinion removes a key diligence hurdle for customers currently evaluating NeoVolta Power for initial procurement opportunities.
- The opinion ultimately positions NeoVolta to compete for the most high-value projects in the U.S. energy storage market.
