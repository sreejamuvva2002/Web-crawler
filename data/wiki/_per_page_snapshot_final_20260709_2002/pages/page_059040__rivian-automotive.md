# Rivian Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-05

**Source:** [Rivian applies for federal loan to restart Georgia EV factory construction, ETAuto](https://auto.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/passenger-vehicle/rivian-applies-for-federal-loan-to-restart-georgia-ev-factory-construction/113958569)  ·  `page_059040`

## Facts

- Rivian Automotive has applied for a federal loan to enable construction of its electric vehicle factory in Georgia, according to a U.S. Department of Energy filing.
- The U.S. Department of Energy has not made a final decision on Rivian's loan application.
- The loan filing did not disclose the requested amount or terms.
- The Georgia facility is scheduled to begin partial operations in the third quarter of 2027.
- The first production capacity block at the Georgia facility is scheduled to reach full operation in 2028.
- Rivian did not immediately respond to a request for comment.
- Rivian had paused construction of its planned USD 5 billion factory in Georgia.
- The construction pause was implemented to prioritize speeding up production of vehicles on the R2 midsize platform and conserving cash.
- Rivian aims to save USD 2.25 billion and speed up production by shifting manufacturing of its R2 midsize SUV to its Illinois factory.
- The R2 midsize SUV is set to compete with Tesla's Model Y.
- Production of the R2 at the Illinois factory is slated to begin in 2026.
- Rivian had received USD 827 million in an incentive package from the State of Illinois to expand operations at its Normal facility.
- The Illinois Normal facility also manufactures electric delivery vans for Amazon.com.
- In 2022, Rivian secured a USD 1.5 billion incentive package from the State of Georgia and local authorities for the Georgia facility.
- Rivian lowered its annual production forecast earlier in the day.
- Rivian missed third-quarter delivery expectations due to a parts shortage and a slowdown in EV demand.
- At the end of June, Rivian had cash and cash equivalents of USD 5.76 billion.
- At the end of June, Rivian had long-term debt of USD 5.53 billion.
- In June, Volkswagen said it would invest USD 5 billion in Rivian as part of a new joint venture to share EV architecture and software.
