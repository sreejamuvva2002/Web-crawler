## Facts

- Beginning in 2006, Kia established a state-of-the-art production facility in West Point.
- The establishment represented one of the biggest single foreign investments in the history of Georgia.
- Kia and its suppliers have helped create more than 14,000 jobs since the facility's establishment.
- The facility has produced more than 4.8 million vehicles to date.
- Kia Georgia has an annual production capacity of more than 350,000 vehicles.
- The facility produces the Telluride, Sorento, and Sportage SUVs.
- The facility produces the all-electric EV9 and EV6 SUVs.
- The advanced manufacturing facility represents a $3.2 billion investment.
- The facility is located on a 2,200-acre site.
- The facility operates 24 hours a day.
- Kia Georgia's goal is to be the best and most innovative automotive company in the world.
- The company is driven by the belief that customer satisfaction is rooted in quality.
