# Olympus Recycling

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Provides safe, responsible, and efficient battery recycling services in Georgia.
- Recovers valuable materials including lead, lithium, and nickel from scrap batteries.
- Offers competitive payouts for recycled batteries while preventing hazardous waste from entering landfills.
- Serves auto repair shops, industrial businesses, electricians, contractors, and homeowners.
- Accepts automotive batteries such as lead-acid car batteries, truck and heavy equipment batteries, and marine/RV batteries.
- Accepts industrial and commercial batteries including forklift and UPS backup batteries, and heavy-duty industrial batteries.
- Accepts rechargeable and specialty batteries including lithium-ion batteries used in electric vehicles, laptops, and power tools.
- Accepts nickel-cadmium (NiCd) and nickel-metal hydride (NiMH) batteries commonly found in consumer electronics.
- Provides safety guidelines for battery recycling, advising against disposal in trash, recommending storage in cool/dry places, taping terminals to prevent accidental discharge, and following state and federal guidelines for safe processing.
- Operates two drop-off facilities located in Atlanta and Gainesville, GA.
- Accepts both single batteries and bulk loads with no minimum amount requirement.
- Processes scrap batteries through a drop-off system that requires no appointment, followed by staff weighing and inspection, and immediate payment based on current scrap battery market rates.
