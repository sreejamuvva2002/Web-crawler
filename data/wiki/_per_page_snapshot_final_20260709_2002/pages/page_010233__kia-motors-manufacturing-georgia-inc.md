# Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2013-07-11

**Source:** [Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia reaches one million U.S.-built vehicles milestone - Kia Georgia](https://www.kiageorgia.com/kia-motors-manufacturing-georgia-reaches-one-million-u-s-built-vehicles-milestone/)  ·  `page_010233`

## Facts

- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc. (KMMG) celebrated its one millionth U.S.-built vehicle rolling off the assembly line on July 11, 2013.
- The one millionth vehicle produced was a snow white pearl 2014 Sorento SXL.
- KMMG represents an initial investment of $1 billion.
- Mass production of the Sorento CUV began at KMMG on November 16, 2009.
- Together with on-site and local suppliers, the plant has created more than 11,000 jobs in West Point and the surrounding region.
- A $100 million expansion completed in early 2012 increased KMMG's annual production capacity to 360,000 vehicles.
- The Optima mid-size sedan was added to the KMMG assembly line in 2011.
- The Optima has been Kia’s best-selling vehicle in the U.S. for 19 consecutive months.
- Byung Mo Ahn serves as Group President and CEO for Kia Motors America and Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia.
- KMMG employs more than 3,000 team members.
- Byung Mo Ahn stated that building one million vehicles in less than four years is a tremendous achievement for the company's team members.
- Byung Mo Ahn noted that team members' commitment to world-class quality has driven Kia's unprecedented sales and market share growth in the U.S. over the last few years.
- The one millionth Sorento will be allocated to one of Kia Motors America's more than 765 dealerships nationwide.
- KMMG is the first manufacturing site in North America for Seoul, Korea-based Kia Motors Corporation.
- KMMG is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Georgia.
- KMMG manufactures both the Sorento CUV and the Optima mid-size sedan.
- The Sorento CUV and Optima mid-size sedan are two of Kia's top-selling models in the U.S.
