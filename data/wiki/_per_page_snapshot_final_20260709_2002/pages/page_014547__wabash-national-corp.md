# Wabash National Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Wabash National reports hiring up | FleetOwner](https://www.fleetowner.com/operations/article/21662139/wabash-national-reports-hiring-up)  ·  `page_014547`

## Facts

- Trailer maker Wabash National Corp. announced that based on current demand levels, its need for additional hires continues to grow.
- The company met its plans announced earlier this year to have increased hourly staffing by 190 associates by the end of the first quarter.
- Wabash National far exceeded its quarterly hiring projection as order backlog has continued to grow.
- The company expects that by the end of May, it will have added upward of 500 associates since the beginning of the year.
- President & CEO Dick Giromini stated that the company is continuing to take a prudent route in filling near-term staffing needs with temporary-status associates because it is still in the early stages of an industry recovery.
- VP & GM of the Transportation Products Group Brent Yeagy noted that the company will evaluate converting temporary associates to full-time status once the recovery is deemed sustainable.
- Wabash National is based in Lafayette, IN.
- The company manufactures dry freight vans, refrigerated vans, flatbed trailers, drop deck trailers, dump trailers, truck bodies, and intermodal equipment.
