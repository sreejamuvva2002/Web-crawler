# JAC Products Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2006-02-28

**Source:** [Roof Rack for 2007 FJ Cruiser Designed Jointly by Toyota and JAC
    Products](https://www.theautochannel.com/news/2006/02/28/212048.html)  ·  `page_056352`

## Facts

- JAC Products worked jointly with the Toyota Motor Sales vehicle team to create a roof rack for the 2007 FJ Cruiser.
- The roof rack features an integrated, tubular design with a 165 lb. load capacity.
- The rack includes an integrated basket and a textured, black powder-coat finish that contrasts with the white roof.
- The FJ Cruiser is positioned to capture the youth market with straightforward styling, authentic off-road performance, and an affordable price.
- Optional bicycle and ski rack accessories from Mont Blanc, a subsidiary of JAC Products, are available for the vehicle.
- JAC Products managed the roof rack development through initial concept, design, prototype, and testing phases to the FJ production launch in 2006.
- JAC Products has been partnering with Toyota on roof racks and other accessory projects since 1978.
- JAC Products Inc. was founded in 1967 and is a leading designer and manufacturer of original equipment roof racks supplying vehicles worldwide.
- JAC Products is owned by Citicorp Venture Capital, Ltd.
- JAC Products manufactures products in the U.S., Germany, Portugal, Argentina, and the U.K.
- JAC owns and operates Mont Blanc (Sweden) and Paddy Hopkirk (England), which manufacture bicycle and ski racks.
- Franklin Aluminum, a component supplier owned by JAC Products, is located in Franklin, Georgia.
