# Novelis Inc.

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. offered Cary Chenanda the role of EVP and President of Novelis North America.
- The position is effective April 15, 2024, or later as mutually agreed, with a transition period not exceeding 90 days after the start date.
- Cary Chenanda will report to Steve Fisher, Novelis Inc.’s President and Chief Executive Officer.
- The role will be based in Atlanta, Georgia.
- The annual base salary for the position is USD 500,000.
- The next salary review is scheduled for July 1, 2025.
- Cary Chenanda is eligible for the Novelis Annual Incentive Plan (AIP) with a target opportunity of 70% of his annual base salary.
- For Fiscal Year 2025 (April 1, 2024 to March 31, 2025), the AIP target will be pro-rated based on the start date.
- Cary Chenanda is eligible for Novelis’ Long Term Incentive Plan (LTIP) with a target opportunity of USD 700,000 for Fiscal Year 2025.
- LTIP award details will be communicated no later than July 1, 2024.
- Employee benefits include a Defined Contribution Supplemental Executive Retirement Plan (DC SERP), life insurance, medical, prescription drug, and health savings plans, dental coverage, short-term disability, long-term disability, business travel and accident insurance, and executive physicals.
- The employee receives an annual Flex Perk stipend of USD 50,000 minus required deductions, paid over 12 months for personal use such as club memberships, tax preparation, car allowance, or financial services.
- Novelis does not pay for or provide club dues, vehicles, or other services beyond the Flex Perk stipend.
- Vacation entitlement is no less than 30 days annually, plus paid holidays per the Atlanta office schedule.
- Relocation assistance is coordinated through third-party provider Cartus.
- The employee must repay 100% of any relocation assistance if they separate from Novelis within the first 24 months of the position.
- A sign-on bonus totaling USD 1,250,000 will be paid in installments: USD 325,000 within 30 days of joining, USD 400,000 within 30 days of completing one year, and USD 500,000 within 30 days of completing two years.
- The sign-on bonus is conditioned on continued employment through applicable payment dates and subject to taxes and withholdings.
- If the employee voluntarily terminates or is terminated for cause within 12 months of receiving the sign-on bonus, they must repay any amount paid in the last 12 months (less applicable taxes).
- Severance pay upon termination other than for Cause or for Good Reason equals one times the annual base and short term incentive opportunity (USD 850,000), reduced by any other severance paid.
- The agreement is governed by the internal laws of the State of Georgia, with disputes handled exclusively in state or federal courts in Atlanta, Georgia.
- The offer was extended by HR Shashikant, EVP and Chief Human Resource Officer at Novelis Inc.
