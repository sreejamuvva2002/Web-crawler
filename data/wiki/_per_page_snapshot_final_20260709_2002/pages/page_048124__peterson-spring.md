# Peterson Spring

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-07-17

**Source:** [Peterson Spring Auction](https://www.hyperams.com/peterson-spring-011922)  ·  `page_048124`

## Facts

- Peterson Spring is liquidating assets from several of its plants through an auction.
- The auction includes lots 600-700 located at the Peterson Spring Georgia facility.
- Other auction locations include Peterson Spring Greenville in Greenville, IL; Peterson Spring Three Rivers North in Three Rivers, MI; and Peterson Spring Canada Ltd. in Kingsville, Ontario, Canada.
- Assets for sale include vehicles such as pickups, box trucks, over 35 trailers, snorkel lifts, electric- and gas-powered welders, forklifts, material handling equipment, transits, rigging equipment, and assorted tools and shop equipment.
- Industrial machinery available includes 4-Slide Machines, Spring Coilers, Presses, Cut-To-Length Lines, Lathes, Grinders, Furnaces, Milling Machines, Plant Support Equipment, and Office Furniture.
- The auction is being conducted as a webcast via the Bidspotter platform.
- The auction date and time is January 20, 2022 at 10:00 AM CDT.
- An earlier notice on the page states the auction ends July 17, 2018.
- Inspection for the assets is scheduled for Wednesday, January 19, 2022 from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM CDT by appointment only.
- A buyer's premium of 18% applies to online purchases and 15% applies to on-site purchases.
- Accepted payment methods include cash, cashier's check, company check with a bank letter of guarantee, or wire transfer; credit cards are not accepted.
- All assets are sold "AS IS, WHERE IS" without warranty or guarantee from the auctioneer.
- Buyers are solely responsible for removal, shipping, and providing necessary insurance certificates prior to picking up lots.
- Disputes arising from the agreement will be settled by arbitration in Illinois under American Arbitration Association rules.
