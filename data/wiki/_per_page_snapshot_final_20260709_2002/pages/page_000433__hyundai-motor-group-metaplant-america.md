# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-26

**Source:** [Hyundai plant in Ellabell to produce hybrids and electric vehicles](https://www.savannahnow.com/story/news/2025/09/26/hyundai-plant-in-ellabell-to-produce-hybrids-and-electric-vehicles/86343742007/)  ·  `page_000433`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Company recently unveiled Phase 2 production goals for Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.
- The plant is on pace to manufacture 500,000 hybrids and electric vehicles by 2028 at its site in Ellabell.
- HMC made the announcement last week, saying the additional $2.7 billion investment will create 3,000 direct and indirect jobs in Georgia.
- Hyundai's initial investment, which was announced in 2022, was $5.5 billion.
- A Hyundai official stated that a recent raid should not impact future production of vehicles at the site.
