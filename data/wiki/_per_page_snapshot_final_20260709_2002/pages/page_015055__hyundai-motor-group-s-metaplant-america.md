# Hyundai Motor Group's Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-06

**Source:** [Hyundai Metaplant America to boost its EV sales in the US](https://www.teslarati.com/hyndai-metaplant-america-plant-georgia/)  ·  `page_015055`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group is investing $7.59 billion in Metaplant America (HMGMA) in Georgia.
- The HMGMA plant is estimated to generate 8,500 jobs directly.
- Hyundai is building a $5 billion EV battery cell plant with SK ON in Georgia.
- The SK On battery cell plant is expected to employ 3,500 workers.
- A study by the Center for Automotive Research estimated that Hyundai’s investments in Georgia will create about 40,000 jobs and result in $4.6 billion in individual earnings per year.
- Hyundai Motor Group was second in EV sales last year in the United States, behind Tesla and ahead of Ford and General Motors.
- Hyundai's focus on affordable and compact electric vehicles helped boost its EV sales in the United States.
- HMGMA could help Hyundai retain its spot as the second top-selling EV automaker in the United States.
- The Georgia-based plant would make Hyundai’s EVs more affordable by allowing them to qualify for the Inflation Reduction Act’s full $7,500 tax credits.
- HMGMA-produced electric vehicles will gain IRA points for being assembled in the United States and using locally produced battery cells from SK On.
- Hyundai Motor Group announced that the official start of production at Metaplant America has been moved up to the fourth quarter of 2024.
- The automaker previously estimated that the start of production at HMGMA would occur in the first quarter of 2025.
- Hyundai plans to hold a grand opening celebration by the first quarter of next year.
- Oscar Kwon, HMGMA President and CEO, stated: “2024 is going to be a great year for HMGMA. This is the largest economic development project in Georgia’s history, and we have been moving at a rapid speed. Completing a project of this scale on this timeline has been a challenge, but our team has delivered amazing results.”
- Oscar Kwon also stated: “We owe a lot of this to the hard work of our Meta Pros and the continued support and cooperation of our state and local partners, including the Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Joint Development Authority.”
- Hyundai made the production timeline announcement during an event celebrating its positive impact on the State of Georgia.
- During the event, the Georgia General Assembly passed a Resolution making February 26, 2024 Hyundai Day.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated: “For decades, Hyundai Motor Group has been an incredible partner to our state, and we knew exactly what kind of historic benefits a project like this would bring to hardworking Georgians when we first began talks with HMG.”
