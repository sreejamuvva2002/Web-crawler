# L-H Battery Co. Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-02

**Source:** [Honda battery plant has started full-time production | Local | springfieldnewssun.com](https://www.springfieldnewssun.com/local/honda-battery-plant-says-it-has-launched-full-time-production/article_b2fb6e27-0605-58e2-bfa6-f8a3fbe2bb0d.html)  ·  `page_008940`

## Facts

- Honda’s Jeffersonville plant has begun production of lithium batteries for electric grid and storage purposes, not for EV batteries, as stated Wednesday, July 1.
- L-H Battery Co. Inc. launched lithium-ion battery cell mass production more than three years after construction began west of Ohio 729 and south of Interstate 71.
- The plant spans more than 2 million square feet.
- The plant has more than 1,000 employees.
- L-H Battery stated it will remain flexible with its products and production as the market for battery cells continues to evolve.
- The plant was originally envisioned to begin mass production to supply battery cells for electric vehicles.
- The plant pivoted to support energy storage systems and future production of hybrid-electric vehicle battery cells in North America.
- A spokeswoman for L-H Battery referred questions about when EV battery production will begin to Honda.
- LG Energy Solution will sell its stake in the joint venture EV battery production site in Fayette County to Honda Development and Manufacturing of America for $2.85 billion, according to a December 2025 Korean regulatory filing.
- The first battery cells produced at L-H Battery will be packaged into energy storage systems by LG Energy Solution Vertech.
- LG Energy Solution Vertech is the U.S. energy storage division of LG Energy Solution.
- The energy storage systems are delivered to customers for grid-scale, residential, commercial, industrial, and large-scale utility grid applications.
- The systems capture and store electricity created by traditional energy sources like oil and gas as well as renewables like wind and solar.
- L-H Battery Chief Executive Chahun Ku said these systems are an important part of L-H Battery’s future, along with production of battery cells for hybrid-electric vehicles.
- L-H Battery Chief Operating Officer Rick Riggle stated that nearly four years after establishment, progress includes completing construction, establishing a new workforce, and delivering meaningful economic benefits to the Fayette County community.
- Global lithium-ion battery deployment in 2025 was six times that of 2020, according to an International Energy Agency report cited by Reuters.
