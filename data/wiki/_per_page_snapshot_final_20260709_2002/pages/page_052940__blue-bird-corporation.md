# Blue Bird Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2013-02-04

**Source:** [Blue Bird Corporation Welcomes New Dealer - School Transportation News](https://stnonline.com/industry-releases/blue-bird-corporation-welcomes-new-alabama-dealer/)  ·  `page_052940`

## Facts

- Blue Bird Corporation has welcomed Busworx Blue Bird as the new dealer for the state of Alabama.
- Busworx Blue Bird operates as an extension of Truckworx Kenworth.
- Busworx Blue Bird maintains six factory-authorized locations with facilities in Birmingham, Dothan, Huntsville, Mobile, Montgomery and Thomasville.
- These locations offer school bus and parts sales while providing comprehensive service for bus customers throughout Alabama.
- Phil Horlock, president and CEO of Blue Bird Corporation, stated that the signing of Busworx Blue Bird provides customers in Alabama with an established operation known for outstanding sales, parts and service support.
- Busworx Blue Bird is a family-owned organization that has been a leading transportation provider in Alabama for over 35 years.
- Will Bruser, vice president of Truckworx Kenworth, stated that adding the Blue Bird franchise aligns with current product lines and gives school systems throughout Alabama easy access to certified Blue Bird service facilities.
- Busworx Blue Bird will be the first bus dealer with the ability to provide all Alabama school systems with daily parts delivery and mobile service.
- Mike Levering, corporate operations manager for Busworx Blue Bird, stated the company is committed to investing in state-of-the-art facilities and training while providing the latest equipment to better serve customers.
- Throughout the state of Alabama, the company operates 120 service bays.
- The company employs over 60 technicians with an average tenure of 14 years.
- Its parts department stocks $8 million in inventory with annual sales of approximately $40 million.
- Yancey Bus Sales transferred its Alabama territory to Busworx Blue Bird to fully focus on growing its market share in the state of Georgia.
- Seasoned employees from Yancey Bus Sales transitioned to Busworx Blue Bird to ensure smooth, uninterrupted service for customers throughout Alabama.
- Blue Bird offers a complete line of Type A, C and D school buses in a variety of options and configurations.
- For more than 86 years, Blue Bird Corporation has continued to set industry standards with innovative design and manufacturing capabilities.
- Blue Bird provides comprehensive financial solutions through Blue Bird Capital Services.
- Blue Bird currently employs more than 1,500 people.
- Blue Bird operates Georgia-based manufacturing facilities.
- Blue Bird maintains an extensive network of Dealers and parts & service facilities throughout North America.
- Blue Bird has a global presence in more than 60 countries through sales into Africa, Asia, the Caribbean, Latin America, Europe and the Middle East.
- Busworx Blue Bird is a subsidiary of Truckworx.
- The parent company Truckworx was founded in Birmingham, Alabama in 1978 by B.B. and Bob Mitchell.
- Truckworx has grown to 260 employees across 9 locations located throughout Alabama and Mississippi.
- Other product lines represented by Truckworx include Kenworth Trucks, Hino Trucks, Isuzu Commercial Trucks, Napa Auto & Truck Parts, and 8 heavy-duty trailer manufacturers.
