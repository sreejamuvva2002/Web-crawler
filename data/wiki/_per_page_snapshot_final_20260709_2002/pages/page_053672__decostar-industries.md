# Decostar Industries

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2013-12-17

**Source:** [Decostar to expand in Temple: Move brings 35 jobs, $2 million investment | Villa Rican | times-georgian.com](https://www.times-georgian.com/villa_rican/decostar-to-expand-in-temple-move-brings-35-jobs-2-million-investment/article_a963e6f1-79b6-5f70-a6b9-02a61471dbb2.html)  ·  `page_053672`

## Facts

- Decostar Industries, a manufacturing division of automotive parts supplier Magna Exteriors and Interiors, will expand its presence in Carroll County.
- The expansion will create up to 35 new jobs.
- The project includes a $2 million capital investment.
- Decostar has leased the 100,000-square-foot Janus International facility in Temple.
- The 60 jobs of employees at the former Chiquita facility in Carrollton will be retained.
- Decostar currently operates four satellite facilities supporting Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Nissan, and Volkswagen with molded and painted exterior parts.
- The new Temple facility will supply assembled components to Nissan, Kia, and other customer assembly lines.
- The facility will also supply past-model service parts to distribution centers throughout the world.
- New contracts secured by Decostar made the capital investment and headcount increase possible.
- Joe Pittel, President of Magna Exteriors and Interiors, stated the company is pleased to bring additional investments and jobs to Carroll County.
- Temple Mayor Rick Ford welcomed Decostar to the community and noted the project will have a tremendous economic impact through financial investment and job creation.
- Daniel Jackson, President and CEO of Carroll Tomorrow, described the expansion as an example of the community's proactive approach toward existing industries for job creation.
- EJane Caraway, senior project manager on the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s Existing Industry Team, managed the project for the state.
