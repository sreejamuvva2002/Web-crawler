## Facts

- Murata Electronics North America Inc. manufactures the PLT5BPH Series common mode choke coils, which are groomed for harsh operating environments.
- The PLT5BPH Series handles automotive noise-suppression applications.
- The coils are capable of handling currents up to 5.6A.
- The components operate over a temperature range from -55°C to +150°C.
- The case size measures 5 mm x 5 mm.
- The common mode impedance is up to 500Ω at 10 MHz.
- The product has AEC-Q200 compliance.
- Murata Electronics North America Inc. is located in Smyrna, GA.
