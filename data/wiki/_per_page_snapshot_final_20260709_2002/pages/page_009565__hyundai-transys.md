# Hyundai Transys

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-04-17

**Source:** [News | HYUNDAI TRANSYS](https://www.hyundai-transys.com/en/media/news-view.do?nSeq=1709&Seq=1709)  ·  `page_009565`

## Facts

- HYUNDAI TRANSYS ranked first in the Small/Compact Car Seats Category for Mass Market Car Brands in the “2024 J.D. Power Seat Quality and Satisfaction Study”.
- HYUNDAI TRANSYS has been consistently ranked among the top 3 in Passenger Car and SUV Seat Satisfaction in the United States for five consecutive years.
- HYUNDAI TRANSYS is the sole Korean company to achieve five consecutive years of top-3 rankings in US seat satisfaction surveys.
- The company clinched the top spot in the Small/Compact Car Seat category for Mass-Market Brands in August 2024 specifically for seats in the Kia Forte.
- This marks the first time HYUNDAI TRANSYS has claimed the top spot in the seat quality and satisfaction survey.
- The 2024 US Seat Quality and Satisfaction Survey measures complaints and satisfaction per 100 vehicles, targeting US purchasers and lessees who have owned a 2024 model for at least 90 days.
- The comprehensive survey was conducted from July 2023 to May 2024, encompassing 99,144 purchasers and lessees.
- A lower score in the survey indicates fewer complaints and reflects high satisfaction with quality.
- Since its integrated launch in 2019, HYUNDAI TRANSYS has consistently ranked among the top three in seat quality satisfaction surveys from 2020 to 2024.
- In 2020, Hyundai ACCENT and Kia Rio secured second and third positions, respectively, in the Small/Compact Car category for Mass-Market Brands.
- In 2021, Genesis G90 seats clinched third place in the Premium Car category.
- In 2022, HYUNDAI TRANSYS achieved second place for the Small/Compact Car category (Kia Forte), second place in the Small/Compact SUV category (Kia Soul), and third place for the Midsize/Large SUV category (Kia Sorento).
- In 2023, HYUNDAI TRANSYS secured second place with the Kia Rio in the Small/Compact Car category and the Kia Sorento in the Midsize/Large SUV category under Mass-Market segments.
- Seo Seung-woo, Executive Director of the Seat Division at HYUNDAI TRANSYS, stated that for the past 20 years, the company has prioritized passenger safety and comfort while incorporating advanced technology and differentiated design.
- Seo Seung-woo added that the company is committed to establishing itself as a top-tier seat manufacturer delivering high-quality products to domestic and global customers, including those in North America.
- HYUNDAI TRANSYS operates 33 global facilities across 11 countries to meet demands of global automakers and ensure continuous revenue growth.
- The company conducts research, development, and production of power trains and seats in various locations across Georgia, Illinois, and Alabama in the United States, as well as in Monterrey, Mexico.
- HYUNDAI TRANSYS’s Savannah facility is located at the Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) in Savannah, Georgia.
- HMGMA is an electric vehicle–dedicated factory of Hyundai Motor Group.
- The Savannah facility is set to manufacture seats for electric vehicles, supporting Hyundai Motor Group’s global electrification efforts.
