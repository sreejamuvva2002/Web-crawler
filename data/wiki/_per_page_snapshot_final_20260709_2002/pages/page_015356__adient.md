# Adient

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-04-27

**Source:** [Adient acquires automotive seating foam plant in Romulus, MI](https://www.adient.com/media/news/2026/04/27/adient-acquires-automotive-seating-foam-plant-in-romulus-mi/)  ·  `page_015356`

## Facts

- Adient announced the acquisition of a foam production plant in Romulus, MI.
- The acquired facility produces foam for seats for multiple automaker customers.
- The plant joins Adient’s network of 10 foam plants in the Americas region.
- Adient operates a total of 30 foam manufacturing plants globally.
- Adient purchased the plant from automotive supplier Woodbridge.
- The purchase agreement included the existing building and land, production equipment, inventory, and associated assets.
- Employees currently working at the plant will transition to become Adient employees.
- The collective bargaining agreement with the UAW will remain in place following the acquisition.
- Jim Conklin, executive vice president Americas for Adient, stated that welcoming the Romulus employees adds an important facility to Adient's footprint and positions the acquisition as a positive move for customers due to global scale and expertise.
- The acquisition supports Adient’s strategic growth plan and positions the company as a supplier and employer of choice.
- Adient is a global leader in automotive seating with more than 65,000 employees across 29 countries.
- Adient operates approximately 200 manufacturing and assembly plants worldwide.
- Adient produces and delivers automotive seating for all major OEMs.
- Adient's expertise spans every step of the automotive seat-making process, from research and design to engineering and manufacturing.
- Adient supplies its products into millions of vehicles every year.
