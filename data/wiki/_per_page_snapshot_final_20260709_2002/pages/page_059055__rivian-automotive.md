# Rivian Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-14

**Source:** [Rivian to raise nearly $15 billion in debt for EV plant in Georgia](https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/world/rivian-to-raise-nearly-15-billion-in-debt-for-ev-plant-in-georgia-11737411.html)  ·  `page_059055`

## Facts

- Rivian Automotive plans to raise nearly $15 billion in debt to help build an electric vehicle manufacturing plant in Georgia.
- The taxable bonds will be issued by the Georgia Department of Economic Development and the Joint Development Authority of Jasper, Morgan, Newton and Walton Counties per an agreement on Nov. 9.
- Rivian has agreed to purchase the bonds as they are issued.
- The company has agreed to pay a minimum of nearly $300 million in property tax payments through 2047.
- Property tax payments will increase if the carmaker exceeds its $5 billion investment.
- Rivian announced in 2021 that it plans to set up the Georgia plant.
- At the time of the 2021 announcement, Rivian stated the plant would be commissioned by 2024.
- The new plant will employ more than 7,500 people.
- The new plant will eventually build 400,000 vehicles a year.
