# OTR Wheel Engineering

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- OTR Wheel Engineering is headquartered at 6 Riverside Industrial Park, Rome, Georgia, 30161.
- The company designs and manufactures its own wheels and tires, mounts them, and provides just-in-time delivery to OEMs, resellers, wholesale distributors, and aftermarket customers worldwide.
- OTR supplies tire and wheel combinations for applications ranging from lawn and garden equipment to giant mining trucks.
- The product line includes specialized off-the-road tires, wheels, mounted tire and wheel assemblies, and rubber tracks.
- The HP-009 tire is a 6-ply option featuring an open tread pattern suitable for loose terrain and mud cleaning, with tread shoulders that protect rims and sidewalls from trail objects.
- The WIZZARD tires are 6-ply options with an aggressive tread pattern designed for loose terrain, though they produce extra noise and wear on hardpack surfaces.
- The Tomahawk tire features a very aggressive tread pattern intended for deep mud conditions, also resulting in increased noise on hard-packed surfaces.
- All OTR tires are available with the exclusive TyrLyner system, a permanent, self-healing polyurethane rubber adhered to the inside of the tire casing.
- The TyrLyner system seals cuts and punctures from objects up to 3/8-inch in diameter, maintains pressure, and provides sidewall and tread area protection by immediately fusing penetration holes shut.
- OTR is a sponsor of the AMSOIL Grand National Cross Country Series (an AMA National Championship) and the Mid-East Racing Series.
- OTR sponsors multiple PRO Class UTV racing teams and drivers, including Collin Truett, Mitch Truett, Alex Truett (Truett Racing), Jonathon Powers, Brent Vanderford, Donnie Harrison, Robert Boynton, and Jamey McCoy.
- Racers using OTR tires have achieved 19 wins and 38 podium finishes, often switching between HP-009 and WIZZARD tires based on track conditions or running a mix for maximum traction.
