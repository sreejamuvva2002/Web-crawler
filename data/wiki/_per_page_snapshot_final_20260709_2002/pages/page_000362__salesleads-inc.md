# SalesLeads, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** research_and_development  ·  **Published:** 2022-08-09

**Source:** [156 New Plans for Industrial Manufacturing Investment - Top Locations for July 2022 - Send2Press Newswire](https://www.send2press.com/wire/156-new-plans-for-industrial-manufacturing-investment-top-locations-for-july-2022/)  ·  `page_000362`

## Facts

- Industrial SalesLeads announced the July 2022 results for new planned capital project spending report for the Industrial Manufacturing industry.
- The firm tracks North American planned industrial capital project activity, including facility expansions, new plant construction and significant equipment modernization projects.
- Research confirms 156 new projects with Texas leading the top locations for July.
- Manufacturing/Production Facilities accounted for 139 New Projects.
- Distribution and Industrial Warehouse accounted for 71 New Projects.
- New Construction accounted for 51 New Projects.
- Expansion accounted for 58 New Projects.
- Renovations/Equipment Upgrades accounted for 59 New Projects.
- Plant Closings accounted for 11 New Projects.
- Texas led the top 10 states by project location with 13 projects.
- North Carolina ranked second with 11 projects.
- Florida, Georgia, and Indiana each had 9 projects.
- New York and Ohio each had 8 projects.
- California had 6 projects.
- Illinois and Michigan each had 5 projects.
- During the month of July, the research team identified 18 new Industrial Manufacturing facility construction projects with an estimated value of $100 million or more.
- The largest project is owned by Samsung Electronics America, Inc., who is planning to invest $192 billion for the construction of 11 semiconductor manufacturing facilities in the Austin, TX and Taylor, TX area.
- The Samsung project is currently in the early design phase.
- A semiconductor company is planning to invest $5 billion for the construction of a manufacturing facility in Chatham County, NC, and is currently seeking approval for the project.
- An electronics mfr. is planning to invest $4 billion for the construction of an EV battery manufacturing facility in De Soto, KS, and is currently seeking approval for the project.
- A battery mfr. is planning to invest $1.5 billion for the construction of a manufacturing facility in Kingston, ON.
- Construction for the Kingston, ON facility is expected to start in 2023, with completion slated for late 2025.
- A pharmaceutical company is planning to invest $470 million for a 260,000 sf expansion, renovation, and equipment upgrades at their processing and laboratory facility in Pearl River, NY.
- The Pearl River, NY project is currently seeking approval, with completion slated for early 2026.
- A specialty copper foil mfr. is planning to invest $430 million for the construction of a manufacturing and office facility in Augusta, GA.
- Construction for the Augusta, GA facility will occur in three phases starting in early Fall 2022.
- Completion for the Augusta, GA facility is slated for late 2024.
- A steel company is planning to invest $265 million for the construction of a manufacturing facility in Sulphur Springs, TX, and is currently seeking approval for the project.
- A steel company is planning to invest $150 million for the expansion of their manufacturing facilities in Keetac, MN or Minntac, MN, and is currently seeking approval for the project.
- A steel tube mfr. is planning to invest $110 million for the construction of a manufacturing, warehouse, and office facility at 1923 Airport Road in Columbus, MS.
- Construction for the Columbus, MS facility is expected to start in Fall 2022, with completion for Summer 2023.
- A specialty roofing mfr. is planning to invest $100 million for the construction of a 450,000 sf manufacturing, warehouse, research, and office facility in Georgetown, TX, and is currently seeking approval for the project.
- A medical equipment mfr. is planning to invest $90 million for the construction of a 270,000 sf manufacturing facility in North Charleston, SC, and is currently seeking approval for the project.
- Completion for the North Charleston, SC facility is slated for 2024.
- SalesLeads, Inc. has been based in Jacksonville, FL since 1959.
- The company delivers industrial capital project intelligence and prospecting services for sales and marketing teams to ensure a predictable and scalable pipeline.
- The company's Industrial Market Intelligence (IMI) identifies timely insights on companies planning significant capital investments such as new construction, expansion, relocation, equipment modernization and plant closings in industrial facilities.
