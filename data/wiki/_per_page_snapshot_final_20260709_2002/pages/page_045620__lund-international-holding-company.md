# Lund International Holding Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-05-10

**Source:** [Truck Hero, Inc. Announces Strategic Acquisition of Lund International - RPM Magazine - HARDCORE HORSEPOWER...EVERY DAY!](https://rpm-mag.com/2019/05/truck-hero-inc-announces-strategic-acquisition-of-lund-international/)  ·  `page_045620`

## Facts

- Truck Hero, Inc. announced the strategic acquisition of Lund International Holding Company on May 10, 2019.
- Lund International is a portfolio company of Highlander Partners, LP.
- Lund International is based in Buford, Georgia.
- Lund International is a leading designer, manufacturer, and marketer of branded automotive accessories for passenger cars, light trucks, SUVs, CUVs, Jeeps, and the heavy truck category.
- The transaction terms were not disclosed.
- Truck Hero provides functional pickup truck and Jeep accessories that match consumers’ lifestyles and enhance vehicle use.
- This transaction adds a highly complementary product portfolio to Truck Hero, diversifying its product category mix and increasing exposure to a larger addressable market of vehicles.
- Lund International was founded in 1965, with roots dating back to the 1920s, and started in Anoka, Minnesota.
- Lund International offers highly functional, protective, and stylish products in over 30 categories under brands including AMP Research, AVS, Belmor, Bushwacker, LUND, Rampage Products, RoadWorks Manufacturing, Roll-N-Lock, Stampede Automotive Accessories, and TonnoPro.
- Bill Reminder serves as President and CEO of Truck Hero.
- Mitch Fogle leads the Lund team.
- Joe Scharfenberger is a Managing Director at CCMP Capital.
- Jeff L. Hull is the President and CEO of Highlander Partners.
- Highlander Partners will be a significant shareholder in the combined company following the acquisition.
- This transaction marks Truck Hero’s fifteenth acquisition since its formation in 2007.
- The acquisition aligns with Truck Hero’s growth strategy of acquiring businesses within the vehicle accessory market to diversify its product portfolio into attractive, complementary, high-growth segments.
- Ropes & Gray LLP advised Truck Hero and CCMP on the transaction.
- Katten Muchin Rosenman LLP advised Lund and Highlander.
- Jefferies provided financing and served as advisors to Truck Hero.
