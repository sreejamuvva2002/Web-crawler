## Facts

- Blue Bird Corporation reported quarterly earnings of 66 cents per share, exceeding analyst expectations of 48 cents.
- Quarterly earnings represent a 200% increase over losses of 66 cents per share from the same period last year.
- Revenue for the quarter was $302.96 million, beating the analyst consensus estimate of $290.11 million.
- Quarterly revenue marks a 17.58% increase over sales of $257.67 million in the same period last year.
- Adjusted EBITDA for the quarter was $87.9 million, an increase of $102.7 million.
- The company sold 8,514 buses during the quarter, up 25%.
- Blue Bird increased its full-year 2024 revenue guidance to between $1.15 billion and $1.25 billion, versus the $1.12 billion estimate.
- The company reaffirmed its long-term outlook targeting approximately $2 billion in revenues and adjusted EBITDA margins of 12%+.
- Blue Bird raised its full-year 2024 adjusted EBITDA outlook to $115 million, or approximately a 10% margin.
- CEO Phil Horlock stated the company executed a rigorous transformational plan over the past two years to improve operations and throughput, control fixed costs, recover economics through pricing, and expand its leadership position in alternative-powered buses.
