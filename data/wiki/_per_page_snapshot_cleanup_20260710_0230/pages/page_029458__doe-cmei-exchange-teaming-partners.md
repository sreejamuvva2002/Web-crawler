## Facts

- The page lists teaming partners for the DOE CMEI eXCHANGE program.
- X UTILITY is a WBE/WOSB/LGBT certified consulting services firm based in Fresno, CA and Oahu, HI.
- X UTILITY is comprised of former PG&E utility renewable experts that have pioneered over 2.9 Gigawatts of safe and reliable retail and wholesale utility grid tie wind, solar, EVCS and energy storage battery resources.
- X UTILITY specializes in portfolio grid integration, renewable utility interconnection requirements, energy roadmap development, utility reconciliation, utility POI feasibility assessments, grid services aggregation, value stacking income streams, workforce development, vehicle electrification, and energy resilience for critical facilities.
- X UTILITY serves markets including Muni’s, Coops, Utilities, CCA’s, Developers, contractors, Tribal Governments, Cities/Counties, engineers, installers, non-profit 501-C3 entities and business owners.
- Ansys is a Large Business Technology Partner focused on Cross-Cutting areas.
- Ansys acquires a new technology company per quarter for the last 8-10 years.
- Ansys delivers a digital engineering environment to facilitate rapid transition through RDD&D process and increase likelihood that major multi-site, multi-lab, public-private partnership projects deliver on time, under budget, and at large scale.
- Ansys is the global leader in engineering simulation and helps 97% of industrial companies solve complex design challenges.
- Kohler Co. is a Large Business focused on Advanced Manufacturing (Industrial).
- Kohler Energy is a global leader in energy resilience solutions and an integral part of Kohler Co.
- Kohler Energy offers solutions across Home Energy, Industrial Energy Systems, and Powertrain Technologies.
- Kohler Energy's portfolio brands include Power Systems, Home Energy, Kohler Uninterruptible Power, Clarke Energy, Heila Technologies, Curtis Instruments, and Engines.
- Kohler Energy has more than a century of industry leadership.
- Syracuse University is an Academic partner focused on Cross-Cutting areas.
- Ian Shapiro serves as Associate Director for Building Science and Community Programs at the Syracuse Center of Excellence in Environmental and Energy Systems (SyracuseCOE).
- SyracuseCOE has a strong history in commercialization.
- Syracuse University faculty have decades of experience designing heat pumps for Carrier Corporation and consulting to world's largest heat pump manufacturers.
- Western Washington University is an Academic partner focused on Solar and Thermal Energy Storage.
- Western Washington University is home to the Institute for Energy Studies (IES).
- IES research focuses on large scale thermal energy storage using solid particles.
- Western Washington University is partnered with The University of Tulsa and Boise State University developing an improved particle flow control mechanism under DoE grant DE-EE0010251.
- Western Washington University previously worked on evaluation of abrasive and impact wear from solid particles at CSP relevant conditions.
- Western Washington University is developing CFD and DEM models to predict wear in material from particles and estimate thermal conductivity in solid particle media.
- Western Washington University capabilities include test equipment such as calorimeters, photo spectrometers, and custom built particle flow control system.
- SLB is a Large Business focused on Industrial Efficiency and Decarbonization Office (IEDO).
- SLB provides technology and subject matter expertise for both blue and green hydrogen.
- SLB has invested in ZEG, a Norwegian start-up that developed efficient hydrogen production from natural gas coupled with integrated carbon capture process with more than 95% CO2 capture rate.
- SLB advanced development of a solid oxide electrolyzer using thermally charged electrolysis process through public/private partnership Genvia.
- SLB is working on pilot projects with multinational companies in petrochemicals, cement, and steel industries.
- SLB integrated long- and short-duration energy storage solution based on nickel-hydrogen electrochemistry for commercial, industrial, and grid-scale applications.
- SLB's battery technology was developed by NASA, performed reliably for more than 30 years in Mars Rover and Hubble Telescope missions, and refined by Stanford University.
- Energetics is a Large Business focused on Project Management Coordination Office.
- Energetics is a clean energy consulting firm with 43 years experience supporting DOE projects in technology assessment, roadmapping, workshop facilitation, proposal and grant management, LCA and TEA, 3rd party data validation and verification, metric reporting, data analysis, resiliency planning and Community Benefits Plans.
- eSai LLC is a Small Business focused on Advanced Manufacturing (Industrial).
- eSai LLC provides utility, Government, Industrial and Commercial customers with comprehensive solutions to clean energy management and carbon footprint reduction.
- eSai employs Six Sigma stage-gated processes to lean and green manufacturing steps where energy consumption is excessive.
- eSai was founded in 2011 in Maryland and contributed to over 60 MW of greener power through design and engineering of CHP, microgrid and other distributed energy services.
- eSai received the “Manufacturing Champion of Maryland” award in 2022, “Woman-owned Small Business of the Year” award in 2018, and “Innovative Small Business of the Year” award in 2016.
- eSai is an 8(a) and Woman-Owned Small Business (EDWOSB) certified by US-SBA and a Maryland Small Business participating in Small Business Reserve Program.
- eSai technical expertise includes Business development, Education, Feasibility Studies and recruitment of companies for Long-Duration Energy Storage demonstrations.
- BRITE Energy Innovators is a Non-Profit focused on SmartGrid.
- BRITE was launched in 2011 to empower energy tech startups and create opportunity, good jobs and resilient economy.
- BRITE hosts signature startup programs and supportive services to enhance energy technology viability and community understanding.
- BRITE is headquartered in Warren, Ohio.
- Stryten Energy is a Large Business focused on Advanced Manufacturing (Industrial).
- Stryten Energy is a US Based developer and manufacturer of long duration (10+ hours and expandable to over 100 hours) stationary storage products based on proprietary advanced Vanadium Redox Flow Battery system.
- Stryten Energy demonstrated its battery system at an early moderately mature stage in a grid tied environment.
- Astro Technologies is a Small Business focused on Advanced Manufacturing (Industrial).
- Astro Technologies has extensive proficiency in advanced manufacturing, robotics and battery R&D.
- Astro Technologies established a 5gwh LFP battery manufacturing plant internationally.
- bp is a Large Business focused on Hydrogen and Fuel Cells.
- bp is working with partners to assess feasibility of designing, building, operating and supplying network of hydrogen refueling stations for freight transport by 2030.
- EPRI (Eva Gardow) is a Non-Profit focused on Office of Clean Energy Demonstration (OCED).
- VentureWell is a Non-Profit focused on Cross-Cutting Innovation & Commercialization Support.
- VentureWell has supported researchers and early-stage innovators since 1995 across 90+ countries.
- VentureWell supported 15,000+ innovators resulting in 4,800 ventures raising $3.8 billion.
- Approximately half of VentureWell's accelerator startups work in energy, agriculture, and other climate-tech sectors.
- VentureWell expanded programming to support mobility focused innovators as part of DOE’s Energy Program for Innovation Clusters (EPIC) prize.
- Hydrogen Technologies, LLC is a Small Business focused on Hydrogen and Fuel Cells.
- Hydrogen Technologies offers a 100% hydrogen fueled commercial and industrial steam boiler that emits zero greenhouse gas emissions.
- Hydrogen Technologies' boiler provides steam from 1,500 kg per hour up to 8,000 kg of steam per hour.
- Hydrogen Technologies is scaling up its system from 8,000 kg of steam per hour to 50,000 kg of steam per hour.
- Decent Energy, Inc. is a Small Business focused on Cross-Cutting Long Duration Energy Storage alignment.
- Decent Energy has worked in the central Midwest since 2008 with emphasis on Kansas.
- Decent Energy fosters pilot projects for advanced inverters, BIPV, thermal energy storage, and advanced HVAC controls.
- Benjamin L. Schenkman at EPRI is a Principal Technical Lead in the Energy Storage Group leading O&M, Cost estimation, and Reliability Metric tools programs.
- Newlab is a Small Business focused on Cross-Cutting areas.
- Newlab's flagship location opened in Brooklyn Navy Yard in 2016.
- Newlab's second location is a 250,000 square foot facility in Detroit launched recently in Michigan Central Innovation District.
- Newlab community includes more than 800 entrepreneurs across physical and digital platforms.
- Newlab deploys hundreds of pilots globally yearly through its Studios function to validate technologies and de-risk models.
- Newlab Studio partners include Ford Motor Co., Verizon, Ørsted, BHP, LG Electronics, Mercado Libre, NYCEDC and Uruguay Ministry of Industry, Energy and Mining.
