## Facts

- Hwashin is a South Korean EV supplier setting up shop in Georgia.
- Hwashin is investing over $176 million to build a plant.
- Hwashin plans to hire more than 460 employees.
- The company will build its plant in Dublin, located in the middle of the state.
- Production at the new Hwashin plant is planned to start in 2025.
- Hwashin will manufacture metal pieces for auto chassis.
- The manufactured parts will be supplied to Hyundai Motor Group's new plant being built in Ellabell.
- The manufactured parts will also be supplied to sister company Kia in West Point.
- The Hyundai Motor Group plant is a $7.6 billion facility for assembling electric vehicles and batteries.
- The Hyundai Motor Group plant was announced last year.
- The Hyundai Motor Group plant could grow to 8,500 employees.
- The Hyundai Motor Group plant could begin producing vehicles as early as 2024.
- Suppliers to the Hyundai Motor Group plant have pledged to invest more than $2.4 billion.
- Suppliers to the Hyundai Motor Group plant have pledged to hire nearly 6,300 people.
