# Kia Georgia Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-02-26

**Source:** [Georgia Kia Milestones & 2027 Telluride Launch | Jim Ellis Kia of Kennesaw](https://jimelliskiaofkennesaw.com/blog/kia-georgia-celebrates-major-milestones)  ·  `page_010025`

## Facts

- Kia Georgia recently celebrated three significant milestones, including the official start of production for the highly anticipated 2027 Kia Telluride.
- The next-generation 2027 Telluride has officially begun rolling off the assembly line in West Point, Georgia.
- Since its original debut, the Telluride has become one of Kia’s most awarded and sought-after SUVs, known for its bold styling, spacious three-row design, and family-focused versatility.
- With the 2027 model, Kia is expanding its powertrain offerings at the Georgia plant to include hybrid technology.
- The 2027 Telluride marks the first hybrid electric vehicle assembled at Kia’s Georgia plant.
- Expanding hybrid production locally represents a major step forward for Kia’s electrification strategy and automotive manufacturing in Georgia.
- By expanding hybrid production locally, Kia is reinforcing its long-term investment in advanced technology and sustainable mobility.
- The start of 2027 Telluride production coincided with the plant’s 5 millionth vehicle built since operations began in 2009.
- In just over a decade, Kia Georgia has grown into one of the region’s most important manufacturing facilities, supporting thousands of jobs and contributing significantly to the local economy.
- The milestone event was attended by company leaders and state officials, including Brian P. Kemp, who recognized the plant’s impact on economic growth and workforce development throughout Georgia.
- Having the Telluride built in Georgia highlights Kia’s commitment to quality, innovation, and American manufacturing for drivers in Kennesaw and the greater Atlanta area.
- As production ramps up, the 2027 Telluride will offer premium comfort, advanced safety features, confident capability, and expanded efficiency options.
