# Beaver Manufacturing Company, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-03-11

**Source:** [Healthcare Interactive Awarded Population Health and Wellness Contract from Beaver Manufacturing](https://www.hciactive.com/post/healthcare-interactive-awarded-population-health-and-wellness-contract-from-beaver-manufacturing)  ·  `page_052827`

## Facts

- Beaver Manufacturing Company, Inc. is based in Mansfield, Georgia.
- The company was founded in 1971.
- Beaver Manufacturing engages in the manufacture and supply of reinforcing yarns for industrial hoses used in automotive and various industrial applications.
- Healthcare Interactive (HCIactive) secured a contract to provide population health management, including corporate wellness services, to Beaver Manufacturing Company, Inc.
- Implementation of the wellness program is slated to begin as early as April 2016 for the company's 2016 enrollment period.
- The wellness program features include smoking cessation modules, onsite and telephonic RN health coaching, incentive management with premium deduction rewards for participation, a technology platform integrating all program features, and actionable analytics with real-time reporting.
- Steve Briley serves as Beaver’s Vice President of Finance.
- Steve Briley stated that offering these wellness services to employees is a worthwhile investment to create a workplace environment that supports their wellness goals and motivates them to be the healthiest they can be.
- A study published in the Annals of Internal Medicine and covered by CNN indicates that more than 80% of large employers are now using some form of financial incentive to increase physical activity among employees.
