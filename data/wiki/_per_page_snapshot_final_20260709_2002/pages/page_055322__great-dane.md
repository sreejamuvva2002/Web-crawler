# Great Dane

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-01-01

**Source:** [Dean Engelage Named Great Dane CEO - Trailer Technician](https://trailertechnician.com/2022/12/dean-engelage-named-great-dane-ceo/)  ·  `page_055322`

## Facts

- Great Dane announced that Chief Executive Officer Bill Crown intends to step away from his role as CEO.
- Effective January 1, 2023, Dean Engelage, Great Dane’s President, will succeed Crown as the company’s Chief Executive Officer.
- Mr. Engelage’s appointment is part of a managed executive transition coordinated by Bill Crown, the Great Dane executive leadership team and Great Dane’s ownership.
- Bill Crown served as Great Dane's CEO over the past 25 years.
- Dean Engelage has served as Great Dane’s President for the past nine years.
- Under Engelage and his team, Great Dane became the safest company in the trailer industry for its associates.
- Under Engelage and his team, Great Dane elevated quality to the highest levels in the company’s history in areas including products, services, and people.
- Bill Crown and Dean Engelage named Rick Mullininx to succeed Engelage as President and Chief Operating Officer of Great Dane, also effective January 1, 2023.
- Rick Mullininx has been with Great Dane for 40 years, serving in various engineering and manufacturing roles within the company.
- Most recently, Rick Mullininx served as Executive Vice President of Engineering before his new role.
- In his previous role, Mullininx was responsible for product design, production engineering, manufacturing engineering, quality assurance, and customer service.
- Rick Mullininx first joined Great Dane in 1980 as a summer engineering intern in the Research and Development department in Savannah.
- Rick Mullininx graduated from the Georgia Institute of Technology with a bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering.
- Rick Mullininx earned a Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering from the University of South Carolina.
- Dean Engelage joined Great Dane in 2011 as Executive Vice President of Sales and Strategic Planning.
- Dean Engelage was appointed President of Great Dane in 2013.
- Prior to Great Dane, Engelage worked for American Airlines and General Dynamics in marketing, purchasing and finance.
- Engelage spent more than 16 years with CC Industries in various management positions.
- Engelage began his career with CC Industries in 1996 in the marketing department of Woodard, becoming President of Woodard in 2004.
- After presiding over the sale of Woodard in 2008, Engelage worked on the Johnson Truck Bodies and Trail King acquisitions and managed CC Industries’ Asia operations.
- Great Dane is a leading manufacturer of high-performance commercial transportation equipment, including dry and refrigerated trailers and truck bodies, as well as steel, aluminum, and combo platform trailers.
- Great Dane has 11 manufacturing plants strategically located throughout the United States.
- Great Dane is headquartered in Chicago, Illinois, with additional corporate offices in Savannah, Georgia.
- Great Dane operates a network of corporate-owned service centers and full-line independent dealers offering distribution points for new and pre-owned trailers as well as aftermarket services across North and South America.
