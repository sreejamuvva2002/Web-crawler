# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Archer reaffirms high-volume facility completion for 2024 | CompositesWorld](https://www.compositesworld.com/news/archer-reaffirms-high-volume-facility-completion-for-2024)  ·  `page_016928`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. is based in Covington, Ga., U.S.
- Archer has shared an update on the construction progress of its high-volume manufacturing facility in Covington, GA.
- The company reaffirmed an expected 2024 completion date for the facility.
- The facility is located adjacent to the Covington Municipal Airport.
- The facility is nearly 400,000-square-foot.
- The facility is in the advanced stages of construction.
- Archer recently completed the first build phase, which included grading and foundation work.
- Archer is nearing the completion of phase two, which involves the erection of exterior walls and roofing.
- Upcoming construction phases will focus on interior fit-out and the installation of equipment and power systems.
- In preparation for the facility’s completion, Archer showcased its Midnight aircraft at Atlantic Aviation’s new terminal at DeKalb–Peachtree Municipal Airport.
- The showcase event was attended by government officials from Georgia’s Office of the Governor, Congressional Offices, City of Atlanta, State Legislature, Federal Aviation Administration, and other local governments.
- Once finished, the facility will support Archer's goal of producing up to 650 Midnight aircraft annually.
- The facility is positioned as one of the largest aviation manufacturing facilities by volume.
- Archer is collaborating closely with Stellantis to mass-produce the Midnight aircraft.
- Adam Goldstein serves as CEO and founder of Archer.
- Adam Goldstein stated that thanks to the hard work of the Archer and Stellantis teams, they are tracking towards completing the manufacturing facility by the end of the year.
- Once complete, Archer will focus on ramping production up to 650 aircraft a year.
- Archer's goal is to be the first in the eVTOL industry to achieve scaled manufacturing.
- The Midnight aircraft offers a sustainable, low-noise alternative to ground transportation.
- The Midnight aircraft is capable of traveling at speeds up to 150 mph.
- The Midnight aircraft is designed for short flights with minimal charge time.
