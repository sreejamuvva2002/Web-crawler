# JTEKT North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-02-28

**Source:** [JTEKT adds 87 jobs to Greenville Co., Harris Teeter opening](https://www.postandcourier.com/greenville/business/jtekt-investing-48-4m-in-piedmont-factory-greer-harris-teeter-opening-soon/article_73440d68-9534-11ee-953e-3753051370be.html)  ·  `page_045368`

## Facts

- Automotive parts manufacturer JTEKT announced plans to invest $48.4 million in its Piedmont factory, creating 87 new jobs in Greenville County.
- Part of JTEKT Corporation, JTEKT North America manufactures a broad range of products under the brands JTEKT Automotive Systems, Koyo Bearings and Toyoda Machine Tools.
- In addition to the company's Piedmont operation, it has 12 manufacturing sites, three technical centers and five distribution centers across North America.
- JTEKT Americas CEO Gary Bourque stated in a press release that the investment reflects absolute confidence in current and future associates, state leadership, and delivering next-generation solutions to customers and trusted brands.
- The $48.4 million investment will fund seven new machine lines, two new assembly lines, and additional processes for machine components.
- With these improvements, JTEKT North America will gain the capability to produce front drive shafts.
