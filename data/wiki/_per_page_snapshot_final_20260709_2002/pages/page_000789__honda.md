# Honda

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-22

**Source:** [Honda Honors 52 Suppliers in North America for Outstanding Performance](https://hondanews.com/en-US/honda-corporate/releases/release-f09141ba9e0061c24e5e5b0bb316ba72-honda-honors-52-suppliers-in-north-america-for-outstanding-performance-2)  ·  `page_000789`

## Facts

- Honda honored 52 North American suppliers during its annual Honda Supplier Conference in Columbus, Ohio, with awards for outstanding performance in supplying parts and materials in 2024.
- During the two-day conference, Honda recognized 33 suppliers for supporting mass production and 19 suppliers for service parts serving Honda and Acura customers.
- Honda purchased approximately $32.5 billion in parts for mass production operations from North American suppliers in 2024.
- The 2024 mass production purchases supported the sales of more than 1.6 million Honda and Acura vehicles in North America.
- Honda also purchased more than $1.96 billion in parts from its Service Parts suppliers in North America.
- Mike Lapham, vice president of Procurement in the Purchasing & Supply Chain Center at Honda Development & Manufacturing of America, LLC, stated that the automotive industry is facing a challenging time and a once in a hundred-year period of transformation.
- Lapham noted that Honda is pursuing its Second Founding to increase flexibility and agility to strike the right balance of internal combustion engine, hybrid and EV models to meet customer needs.
- Honda does business with approximately 700 OEM suppliers across North America which provide parts to the company’s 13 plants in the region that produce Honda and Acura cars and light trucks and the engines and transmissions that power them.
- The Piolax Corporation, located in Canton, Georgia, was recognized by Honda as a 2024 Mass Production Supplier Award Winner in the Excellence in Value category.
- Honda honored 19 service parts suppliers with awards reflecting combined performance in quality, cost, and on-time delivery.
- Jessica Hammontree, assistant vice president of the Parts Division at American Honda Motor Co., Inc., stated that despite market uncertainty and supply challenges, service parts suppliers enabled improved service levels.
- Hammontree noted that Honda ranked number one in the industry for service parts availability for eight consecutive months.
- Honda works with nearly 1,600 service parts suppliers in North America to provide service and accessory parts for more than 26 million Honda and Acura automobiles in operation in the United States.
- Honda manages a national network of parts and distribution facilities that support customers by delivering high-quality service parts to Honda and Acura dealers nationwide.
- Honda established operations in North America in 1959 and currently employs about 42,000 associates in the region engaged in development, manufacturing, sales and service support.
- Honda operates 18 major manufacturing plants in North America and works with over 750 suppliers in the region to produce a diverse range of quality products.
- In 2024, 99.6% of all Honda and Acura automobiles sold in the U.S. were produced in North America.
- Honda conducts research and development activities at 23 facilities in North America where it fully designs, develops and engineers many of its North American-made products.
