# TOTO USA

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2025-08-22

**Source:** [TOTO Opens $224M Georgia Manufacturing Facility, Increasing U.S. Luxury Toilet Production Capacity by 150%](https://www.totousa.com/press/toto-opens-224m-georgia-manufacturing-facility)  ·  `page_008189`

## Facts

- TOTO opened a $224 million state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Morrow, Georgia on August 22, 2025.
- TOTO is the world’s leading plumbing manufacturer with over $5.34 billion in annual sales.
- The new facility marks a major investment in the company’s Americas growth strategy.
- It enables faster delivery of strategic high-end one-piece toilets to meet surging market demand.
- TOTO is shifting more production from Asia-based TOTO Group plants to North America.
- This shift strengthens its regional supply chain, enhances manufacturing resilience, and advances long-term sustainability goals.
- Taro Muroi serves as the CEO of TOTO USA.
- TOTO began production in Morrow in 1991.
- With the new facility online, TOTO’s production capacity in the Americas rises to approximately one million units annually across its Morrow, Lakewood, and Mexico plants.
- The new building in Morrow alone adds production capacity of approximately 300,000 units per year.
- Luxury one-piece toilets are a strategic product for the Americas market.
- Previously sourced primarily from TOTO Global Group plants in Asia, they often faced extended lead times.
- Localized production now ensures timely delivery, strengthens TOTO’s North American supply chain, and supports growth strategy across the Americas.
- In June 2023, TOTO broke ground on the new facility on the site of its original Morrow plant.
- Construction concluded in November 2024.
- Production will begin in the fall of 2025.
- The expansion adds about 161,500 square feet (15,000 m²) of advanced manufacturing space.
- It brings the new three-story facility to over 363,393 square feet (33,800 m²).
- The facility sustains 420 local jobs.
- The new Morrow facility features a Tank Bonding Robot, a first for any TOTO Group plant, jointly developed with an equipment manufacturer to precisely position and bond tanks to bowls for one-piece toilets.
- An automation fleet of thirty-four advanced robots streamlines processes from molding to finishing while maintaining local artisanal craftsmanship.
- Six self-driving Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs) carry three toilets each between molding, drying, glazing, and firing over approximately 44 yards (40 meters).
- High-Pressure Casting Equipment overcomes previous production limitations by enabling the manufacture of larger, more complex designs, expanding the range of premium products made in the Americas.
- Heat-Resistant QR Codes are applied to each product for real-time tracking, quality control, and big-data analysis to optimize manufacturing processes.
- Two convection-heated kilns with advanced insulation and digital temperature control are planned for installation in 2027 to improve energy efficiency and cut CO₂ emissions by approximately 23% compared to current kilns.
- The planned new kilns will accommodate taller products, improve firing consistency, and reduce energy loss associated with older kiln technology.
- The new facility was built with sustainability at its core, aligning with TOTO WILL2030 — TOTO’s global strategy centered on Cleanliness, Comfort, and Health, the Environment, and Connections with People.
- Sustainability initiatives include energy-efficient manufacturing systems embedded throughout production.
- Localized production reduces shipping distances and lowers transportation-related carbon emissions.
- Water-conserving technologies are integrated into operations.
- Planned new kilns will cut CO₂ emissions by approximately 23% compared to current models through advanced insulation, convection heating, and precise electronic temperature control.
- These measures support TOTO’s mission to contribute to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and maintain its leadership in sustainable innovation.
- TOTO began operations in the Americas in 1989 in California.
- In 1991, the company launched U.S.-based manufacturing in Lakewood, Georgia.
- In 1996, TOTO opened its first major manufacturing facility in Morrow.
- The expanded Morrow facility increases capacity, enhances TOTO’s U.S. manufacturing footprint, and reaffirms its deep commitment to the Americas market.
- For fiscal year 2024, TOTO USA recorded sales of $464 million.
- TOTO is a key growth driver for the TOTO Global Group.
- Like its award-winning WASHLET® bidet seats, TOTO’s premium one-piece toilets combine luxury design, high performance, superior water conservation, and exceptional cleanliness and comfort.
- The expansion cements leadership in luxury bathroom innovation while creating a more agile, resilient manufacturing network.
- TOTO USA is headquarters for the Americas Division of the TOTO Global Group.
- The TOTO Global Group was established in 1917 with the founding of TOTO Ltd. in Kitakyushu, Japan.
- TOTO is the world’s largest bathroom fixtures and fittings manufacturer.
- Annual sales were $5.34 billion from April 2023 to March 2024.
- The company maintains employees in 17 countries.
- TOTO owns manufacturing facilities around the world in Japan, Mexico, China, India, Thailand, Vietnam, and the USA.
- TOTO is dedicated to engineering products that respect the environment while meeting people’s comfort, beauty, and performance needs.
- TOTO is the only plumbing manufacturer honored as Water Efficiency Leader by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency.
