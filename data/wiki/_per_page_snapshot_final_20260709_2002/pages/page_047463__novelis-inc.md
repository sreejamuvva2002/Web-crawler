# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-10

**Source:** [Novelis Reports Fourth Quarter and Full Fiscal Year 2023 Results :: Novelis Inc.](https://investors.novelis.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/28/novelis-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-fiscal-year-2023-results)  ·  `page_047463`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is a leading sustainable aluminum solutions provider and the world leader in aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Q4 Fiscal Year 2023 net income attributable to common shareholders was $156 million, down 27% year-over-year.
- Q4 Fiscal Year 2023 net income from continuing operations excluding special items was $175 million, down 7% year-over-year.
- Q4 Fiscal Year 2023 Adjusted EBITDA was $403 million, down 6% year-over-year.
- Q4 Fiscal Year 2023 shipments were 936 kilotonnes, down 5% year-over-year.
- Q4 Fiscal Year 2023 Adjusted EBITDA per ton shipped was $431, compared to $437 in the prior year.
- Full Fiscal Year 2023 net income attributable to common shareholders was $658 million, down 31% year-over-year.
- Full Fiscal Year 2023 net income from continuing operations excluding special items was $781 million, down 16% year-over-year.
- Full Fiscal Year 2023 Adjusted EBITDA was $1.8 billion, down 11% year-over-year.
- Full Fiscal Year 2023 shipments were 3,790 kilotonnes, down 2% year-over-year.
- Steve Fisher serves as President and CEO of Novelis Inc.
- Novelis remains committed to a transformational organic growth plan to further its position as a leading global provider of low-carbon, sustainable aluminum solutions.
- Q4 Fiscal Year 2023 net sales decreased 9% to $4.4 billion, primarily driven by lower average aluminum prices and a 5% decrease in total flat rolled product shipments.
- Record automotive shipments in Q4 were driven by higher OEM build rates due to pent-up demand.
- Full Fiscal Year 2023 net sales increased 8% to $18.5 billion, primarily driven by higher average aluminum prices and favorable product mix.
- Record automotive shipments in FY2023 resulted from easing supply chain constraints, including higher semiconductor availability.
- Adjusted free cash flow from continuing operations was $443 million for FY2023 compared to $649 million in the prior year.
- Total capital expenditures increased 76% over the prior year to $786 million in FY2023, as the company begins to ramp up spending associated with announced strategic, sustainability-focused, capital investment projects that support increased long-term customer demand.
- Dev Ahuja serves as Executive Vice President and CFO of Novelis Inc.
- The balance sheet net leverage ratio was 2.3x at the end of FY2023, compared to 2.2x in the prior year period.
- Total liquidity remained strong at $2.6 billion as of March 31, 2023.
- Novelis is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited, an industry leader in aluminum and copper.
- Novelis is the metals flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group, a multinational conglomerate based in Mumbai.
- Novelis aims to achieve a fully circular economy by partnering with customers in the aerospace, automotive, beverage can, and specialties industries throughout North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
