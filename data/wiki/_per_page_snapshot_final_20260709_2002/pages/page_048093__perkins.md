# Perkins

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-21

**Source:** [Perkins Showcases Next-Gen Power Systems - Plant & Equipment News](https://www.plantandequipment.news/news/product-updates/perkins-showcases-next-gen-power-systems/)  ·  `page_048093`

## Facts

- Perkins unveiled an expanded lineup of power systems, services, and innovative technologies at bauma 2025.
- The unveiling reinforced Perkins' commitment to helping OEMs build machines engineered for greater efficiency, productivity, and fuel flexibility.
- The upcoming Perkins 2600 Series is a new 13-litre diesel engine platform set for availability in 2026.
- The 2600 Series is designed to meet the rigorous demands of construction and other off-road, heavy-duty applications.
- The 2600 Series delivers power density, torque, fuel efficiency, and productivity.
- The engine will be available in eight power ratings ranging from 340 to 515 kW (456 to 690 hp).
- The 2600 Series offers up to 3,200 Nm of peak torque.
- The 2600 Series extends Perkins’ leadership in the high-performance, mid-sized engine category.
- A new 106 kW variant of the 904 Series platform was displayed at the event.
- The new 904 Series variant features a 6% power increase over the existing 100 kW model.
- The new 904 Series variant features a 3% boost in torque.
- The 904 Series is a compact yet powerful engine ideal for OEMs seeking enhanced performance without increasing machine size.
- Perkins introduced a new battery electric power unit technical demonstrator at bauma 2025.
- The battery electric power unit was installed in a pipe fusion machine as a plug-and-play solution.
- The plug-and-play solution is engineered to help off-highway OEMs transition smoothly from diesel to battery electric powertrains.
- This introduction expands Perkins’ growing portfolio of advanced power solutions.
