## Facts

- The Woodbridge Group operates multiple facilities as the leader in the manufacture of polyurethane and expanded polypropylene and polystyrene foam products.
- These products are designed to provide cushioning, structural support, energy management, acoustical and insulating properties in a range of applications.
- Woodbridge offers full assembly and sequencing services as well as certified laboratory testing.
- In-house design and engineering and tool manufacturing capabilities support the company's manufacturing base.
- The company is best known for automotive components including seat cushioning, acoustical absorbers/barriers and headliner substrates.
- Woodbridge serves diverse markets including commercial and recreational vehicles, the military, construction and building products sectors, industrial markets, medical applications, and other high demanding applications.
