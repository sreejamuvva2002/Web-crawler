## Facts

- Volvo Group North America Inc is based in Greensboro, North Carolina.
- Volvo NA began production of a new generation of trucks in the 'VNR' range at its New River Valley Plant in Dublin, Virginia.
- The VNR truck range was originally launched approximately one year prior to February 2026.
- Production of the new VNR range commenced in February 2026.
- The first units of the new VNR range have rolled off the production line.
- The newly produced VNR trucks will be delivered to Clayton Homes.
- Clayton Homes is a manufacturer of prefab houses.
