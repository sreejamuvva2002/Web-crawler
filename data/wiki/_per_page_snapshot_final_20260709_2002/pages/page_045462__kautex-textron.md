# Kautex Textron

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-10-26

**Source:** [Banner Engineering Partners with Kautex Textron for COVID-19 Safe Work Solutions | Industrial Equipment News](https://www.ien.com/safety/article/21200055/digikey-electronics-banner-engineering-partners-with-kautex-textron-for-covid19-safe-work-solutions)  ·  `page_045462`

## Facts

- Manufacturers and retailers had to quickly find scalable solutions to create safer environments for employees and customers during the 2020 COVID-19 pandemic.
- Employers required solutions to facilitate social distancing and automate occupancy monitoring and reporting due to varying and shifting guidance.
- Employers reviewed common spaces like shared workstations, locker rooms, bathrooms, and breakrooms through a new lens for safety.
- Kautex Textron partnered with Banner Engineering to implement an occupancy monitoring solution that alerts employees when capacity is reached and common areas are safe.
- Banner’s Occupancy Monitoring System automates counting and notifications using Q45 paired sensors.
- The sensors detect individuals entering and exiting at single-door and multiple-entry locations.
- The system was installed quickly without alterations to existing infrastructure.
- It provides notifications when traffic meets or exceeds a programmed maximum capacity.
- A Banner DXM wireless controller collects data from each sensor, logs it, and communicates status updates to a Banner Direct Select operator interface.
- Employee and customer lights trigger based on set occupancy limits.
- The system can be configured for real-time text and email notifications to management with an added cell plan or network connection.
- Aggregated data automatically uploads to Banner’s Cloud Data Services platform, which provides visualization and analysis tools.
- Customers can use the platform to track behavior at locations, monitor and compare behaviors across different plant areas and multiple locations, compare customer traffic to sales, and make data-driven decisions about employee scheduling.
- Highly visible LED lights alert employees and customers on safe access to common areas.
- Green and red indicators can include icons, with custom light selections offering various color options and animations.
- The solution is scalable and flexible, allowing devices to be easily added, moved, or removed to meet changing needs.
- The solution is available as a kit containing a DXM Series wireless controller, wireless Q45 directional detection sensor pair, Direct Select wireless operator interface, high-visibility indicator light, power supplies, and mounting brackets.
- Kautex Textron successfully provided its employees with a safer working environment and increased peace of mind through the new system.
- Vince Vlaminck serves as Director EHS Global at Kautex Textron.
- Vince Vlaminck stated: 'As we, along with other industries, battle against COVID-19 and work to provide our employees a safe return to work, we’ve partnered with Banner Engineering to bring Industry 4.0 solutions to social distancing and regular disinfection processes. The results bring a level of professionalism and increased visual management—and support a message to our employees that we are committed to your safety and willing to invest accordingly.'
- Banner Engineering partnered with Digi-Key Electronics to stock several versions of their new Occupancy Solutions Kits for purchase.
