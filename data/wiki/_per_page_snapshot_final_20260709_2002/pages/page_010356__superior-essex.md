# Superior Essex

## Facts

- Superior Essex is a fiber optic cable manufacturer based out of Atlanta, Georgia.
- The company manufactures products in Brownwood, Texas and maintains locations in Hoisington, Kansas.
- Kate Fegley-Lummus serves as the marketing project and partner manager at Superior Essex.
- Fegley-Lummus transitioned from a 13-year career in NASCAR to telecommunications approximately two years ago.
- The company aligns its strategy with BEAD and prior federal funding programs including Capital Projects Fund, RDOF, and Middle Mile.
- BEAD funding is projected to total over $100 billion when pre-match money is included.
- Superior Essex partners with ISPs and state broadband offices to bridge nationwide connectivity gaps.
- To address a projected shortage of 200,000 technicians for broadband deployment, the company supplies fiber optic cable to trade and technical schools for splicing training.
- Superior Essex has completed the self-certification process for BABA compliance in coordination with the NTIA.
- The company updated its part numbers to explicitly identify BABA-compliant cables for BEAD and other grant-funded projects.
- A dedicated webpage listing BABA-compliant products is maintained on the company's website.
- Superior Essex emphasizes keeping manufacturing jobs in the United States as a core operational goal.
