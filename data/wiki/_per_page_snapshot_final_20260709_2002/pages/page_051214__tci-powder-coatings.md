# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-09-29

**Source:** [TCI Powder Coatings Provides Innovation for Jeep’s 75th Anniversary - Coatings World](https://www.coatingsworld.com/contents/view_online-exclusives/2016-09-29/tci-powder-coatings-provides-innovation-for-jeeps-75th-anniversary/?widget=listSection)  ·  `page_051214`

## Facts

- The Jeep brand achieved its 75th anniversary milestone in 2016.
- To commemorate the milestone, the Jeep marketing team rolled out Anniversary Special Edition models of several vehicles.
- Exclusive details for the 2016 Jeep Wrangler Unlimited fleet included distinct vehicle colors and trim packages.
- A specialized bronze-colored trim package along with off-road bumpers are painted and protected with TCI Powder Coatings.
- TCI Powder Coatings collaborated with Chrysler to develop a coating that met manufacturing process demands of Flex-N-Gate and Powder Cote II as well as styling expectations.
- The successful metallic coating received positive feedback, prompting Chrysler’s styling team to expand its use to the tow hitch in the trim package.
- The metallic bumper's success led the broader Chrysler styling team to extend it as an option for higher-end Jeep Sahara and Wrangler anniversary edition models.
