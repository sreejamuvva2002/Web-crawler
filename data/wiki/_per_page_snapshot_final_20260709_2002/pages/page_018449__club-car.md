## Facts

- For more than 60 years, Club Car has set the standard in golf carts, utility vehicles, and personal transportation.
- Club Car was founded in Augusta, Georgia.
- Every detail from design and fabrication to assembly reflects a focus on durability, comfort, and performance.
- Club Car earned recognition as one of EHS Today’s America’s Safest Companies.
- Club Car operates a global network of over 400 authorized dealers and distributors.
- Club Car is the Official Golf Car of the PGA of America, PGA TOUR’s TPC Network, and the European Tour.
- Club Car vehicles are showcased at major events including the Ryder Cup, President's Cup, U.S. Kids Golf, and First Tee.
- Club Car was originally founded in 1958 in Houston, Texas.
- Club Car relocated to Augusta, Georgia in 1962 under Bill Stevens.
- In 1979, a team of former EZGO executives known as “The Original 8” acquired Club Car and reshaped the industry with groundbreaking products.
- In 1995, Club Car joined Ingersoll Rand.
- In 2021, Club Car entered a new era under Platinum Equity.
