## Facts

- Hyundai TRANSYS will build a $240 million plant near West Point.
- The new plant will hire 678 full-time workers to make transmissions.
- The facility will be 620,00 square-foot.
- The plant is located close to the Kia Motors plant.
- Hyundai’s Georgia employment will climb to 1,741 with this expansion.
- Sangkil Jung, president of Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Powertrain, stated in a written statement that trust built through years of capital investment, employment, and training support — along with new opportunities unmatched by any other states — made Georgia a natural choice.
- The announcement was made on a Tuesday.
- The new plant will be located near the company’s current location on the Kia campus.
- The new jobs will include hundreds of advanced manufacturing jobs along with management and office positions.
- The company plans to create 165 supplementary construction handling and inspection jobs.
