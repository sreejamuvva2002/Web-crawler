## Facts

- The Quick Start Advanced Manufacturing Training Center (QSAMTC) opened in 2018 on a 119-acre site in Pooler, Ga.
- The QSAMTC facility spans 50,000 square feet.
- Georgia has become a national leader in clean technology following years of market shifts and investment announcements.
- Hyundai Motor Group announced plans to build a Metaplant in Bryan County.
- Multiple suppliers for the Hyundai Metaplant are also establishing operations in the region.
- In 2022, Quick Start was tasked with building a training center specifically for the Hyundai Metaplant and its affiliates.
- In 2022, Quick Start was also tasked with expanding capacity at the QSAMTC.
- Quick Start provides comprehensive, customized, company-specific workforce training for qualified companies investing and creating jobs in Georgia.
