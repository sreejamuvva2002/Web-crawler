## Facts

- PHA Body Systems plans to invest $67,000,000.00 to build out new space in Savannah, Georgia.
- The company plans to occupy the new space at the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center in Savannah on or about May 1, 2024.
- PHA Body Systems is engaged in the assembly, production, and sales of complete door systems modules for manufacturing plants in the Alabama and Georgia areas.
- PHA Body Systems operates as part of Pyeong Hwa Automotive, a global network with 14 plants and 3 sales offices around the world, headquartered in Daegu, Korea.
- Pyeong Hwa Automotive was established in 1985 and has grown into a global company in the automotive door moving system field through research and development, technological innovation, and investment.
- The company aims to lead the Korean auto parts industry with top-notch technical personnel and state-of-the-art production facilities.
- Sangtae Kim serves as Chairman of PHA Body Systems.
