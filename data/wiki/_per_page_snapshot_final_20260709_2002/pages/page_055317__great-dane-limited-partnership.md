# Great Dane Limited Partnership

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Great Dane To Build New Reefer Plant in Statesboro, Georgia | Trailer Body Builders](https://www.trailer-bodybuilders.com/trailers/article/21729429/great-dane-to-build-new-reefer-plant-in-statesboro-georgia)  ·  `page_055317`

## Facts

- Great Dane Limited Partnership has announced plans to build a new refrigerated trailer plant on a 118-acre site in Statesboro, Ga., northwest of Savannah.
- Construction is expected to get under way later this year to bring the plant into production in 2012.
- The plant meets the company’s and the state's joint goal of keeping this facility close to Great Dane's heritage.
- The trailer manufacturer was founded more than 100 years ago in Savannah.
- The plant, in its first phase, will encompass 450,000 square feet, including office and show room space.
- The facility will have the capacity to produce 5,000-plus trailers annually.
- At that level of production, the facility will employ more than 400 people.
- After more than a year-long search for the ideal location, Statesboro, Ga., was chosen as the future home of the new refrigerated trailer plant.
- Many different sites throughout the Southeast region were considered before the decision was made.
- The company believes the Statesboro location best meets its strategic and corporate needs.
- Phill Pines serves as President and Chief Operating Officer for Great Dane Limited Partnership.
- The new reefer plant will serve the traditional Great Dane customer base.
- Manufacturing processes will feature the latest technology in foaming methods and automation of key production operations.
- Phill Pines stated that the company was impressed with the State of Georgia and Bulloch County's ability and willingness to effectively address their needs, noting they were professional and made the company feel welcome and wanted.
- The new plant will be located less than an hour's drive from Great Dane's Division Offices in Savannah, Ga.
- Great Dane's Division Offices in Savannah, Ga., will remain home to its sales, marketing, engineering, and state-of-the-art research and development center.
