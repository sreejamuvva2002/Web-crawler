# Kia Manufacturing Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Kia Georgia Reaches 5 Million Vehicles Produced, Begins Mass Production of Kia Telluride Hybrid - MK](https://www.mk.co.kr/en/business/11971273)  ·  `page_010249`

## Facts

- Kia Manufacturing Georgia plant in West Point, Georgia reached a cumulative production milestone of 5 million vehicles on Feb 24 (local time).
- On the same day, the Kia Manufacturing Georgia plant began production of the 2027 All-new Telluride.
- The facility marked the full-scale start of mass production for the Kia Telluride Hybrid.
- The vehicle commemorating the 5 million-unit milestone is a hybrid model of the All-new Telluride.
- It is the first hybrid vehicle ever produced in Georgia.
- An on-site ceremony was attended by Governor Brian Kemp, local government officials, community leaders, and Kia employees.
- Kia Georgia began production at the facility in 2009.
- Kia Georgia has grown into a leading local employer since its inception.
- Kia Georgia is established as a key manufacturing hub for the automotive industry in the southeastern United States.
- The Kia Telluride produced at the West Point plant is a strategic model developed around the needs of North American consumers and U.S. road conditions.
- It is widely viewed as a symbol of Kia’s growth in the U.S. market.
- The first-generation Telluride received major accolades in 2020, including the North American Car, Truck and Utility Vehicle of the Year Awards (NACTOY), World Car of the Year (WCOTY), and MotorTrend SUV of the Year.
- Seungkyu Yoon, Head of Kia North America and Head of Kia America, stated that Kia Georgia is the only plant in the world that produces the Telluride.
- Seungkyu Yoon added that the start of this production run carries significance that goes beyond simply launching a new model.
- Seungkyu Yoon noted that reaching 5 million units produced and building the first hybrid vehicle in Georgia demonstrates Kia’s technological progress and future strategy direction.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp stated that since its launch in 2019, the Telluride has been produced exclusively in Georgia and has become one of the most successful models in Kia’s lineup.
- Governor Brian Kemp assessed that this achievement is the result of a close partnership between the local community and the state government.
