## Key facts

- Mister Sparky® of Augusta specializes in EV charger installation for residential customers in Augusta, GA.
- The company offers Level 2 charger installation, service panel upgrades, and troubleshooting of faulty EV chargers.
- All work is performed by licensed electricians to ensure code-compliant, safe installations.
- Transparent pricing, on-time service, a 100% satisfaction guarantee, and the UWIN Guarantee are provided to customers.
- Emergency electrical services are available 24/7 for EV charger issues.
- Technicians evaluate existing panel capacity; Level 2 chargers typically require a 150-200 amp service, with upgrades recommended if the home has only a 100-amp panel.
- Mister Sparky also provides repair services for existing EV charging stations.
- Customer reviews highlight respectful, knowledgeable technicians (Rod, Dylan, Stuart) and positive experiences with scheduling, communication, and safety.
- The company emphasizes safety and proper installation to protect both the home’s electrical system and the electric vehicle.
