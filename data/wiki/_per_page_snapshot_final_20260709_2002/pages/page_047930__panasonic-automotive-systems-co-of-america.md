# Panasonic Automotive Systems Co. of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2013-09-26

**Source:** [Panasonic, Blue Note To Collaborate On Vehicle Audio Systems - CBS Detroit](https://www.cbsnews.com/detroit/news/panasonic-blue-note-to-collaborate-on-vehicle-audio-systems/)  ·  `page_047930`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Co. of America is a supplier of mobile audio and in-vehicle infotainment systems.
- The company announced a working agreement with Blue Note Records to develop custom branded audio systems and content for the global automotive market.
- The agreement provides the framework for creating customized audio systems and providing exclusive Blue Note content for Panasonic's automotive customers.
- Tom Gebhardt serves as President of Panasonic Automotive.
- According to a report by information and analytics provider IHS, in 2012 Panasonic remained the top supplier of automotive infotainment systems worldwide.
- The IHS report found that Panasonic along with the rest of the Top 10 suppliers accounted for nearly two-thirds of overall motor vehicle infotainment revenue in 2012.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America is based in Peachtree City, Ga., with an office in Farmington Hills.
