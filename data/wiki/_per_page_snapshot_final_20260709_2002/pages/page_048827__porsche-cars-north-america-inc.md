# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-09

**Source:** [Porsche reports Q3 2025 U.S. retail sales | Georgia Industry Online](https://www.georgiaindustryonline.com/article/856728877-porsche-reports-q3-2025-u-s-retail-sales)  ·  `page_048827`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) announced that third-quarter retail deliveries totaled 18,403 cars.
- Overall sales for the first nine months of 2025 reached 57,099 cars across all model lines.
- Year-to-date sales increased by 5.6 percent compared to 2024.
- The 718, Macan, and Panamera posted significant year-to-date gains.
- Third-quarter sales of the Taycan and Macan Electric performed well.
- Porsche Approved Certified Pre-Owned (CPO) sales in the U.S. for Q3 reached 12,232 units, a new PCNA quarterly record.
- Year-to-date CPO sales stand at 35,439, representing a 13 percent increase compared to the same point last year.
- Timo Resch serves as President and CEO of PCNA.
- Timo Resch stated that Porsche remains strong in the United States due to customer support and loyalty during unprecedented change.
- The new Cayenne Electric will join the Cayenne gasoline and plug-in hybrid models by year's end.
- Compared to the same period in 2024, Q3 sales decreased by 4.9 percent, driven in part by restricted supply.
- The Macan was the top seller in the third quarter with 6,599 deliveries.
- The Cayenne recorded 4,294 deliveries in the third quarter.
- The 911 recorded 3,448 deliveries in the third quarter.
- ALL 911 sales totaled 4,124 for July-Sept 2024 and 3,448 for July-Sept 2025, with year-to-date totals of 10,841 (2024) and 8,872 (2025).
- ALL 718 sales totaled 1,709 for July-Sept 2024 and 1,719 for July-Sept 2025, with year-to-date totals of 3,821 (2024) and 5,398 (2025).
- ALL TAYCAN sales totaled 1,340 for July-Sept 2024 and 1,553 for July-Sept 2025, with year-to-date totals of 3,394 (2024) and 3,636 (2025).
- ALL PANAMERA sales totaled 852 for July-Sept 2024 and 790 for July-Sept 2025, with year-to-date totals of 2,527 (2024) and 3,410 (2025).
- ALL CAYENNE sales totaled 5,339 for July-Sept 2024 and 4,294 for July-Sept 2025, with year-to-date totals of 15,507 (2024) and 14,621 (2025).
- ALL MACAN sales totaled 5,989 for July-Sept 2024 and 6,599 for July-Sept 2025, with year-to-date totals of 17,993 (2024) and 21,162 (2025).
- PCNA follows the U.S. Auto Industry Sales Release Schedule issued by Motor Intelligence.
- Third quarter new car sales covered the period from July 1, 2025 to September 30, 2025.
- The announcement was issued from Atlanta, Georgia on October 09, 2025.
