# Continental AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-16

**Source:** [In 2024, Continental celebrates important milestones in its three U.S. tire plants - Continental AG](https://www.continental.com/en/press/press-releases/20240416-us-anniversaries/)  ·  `page_018693`

## Facts

- In 2024, Continental celebrates important milestones in its three U.S. tire plants.
- The Clinton, Mississippi plant is celebrating 5 years of operation.
- The Sumter, South Carolina plant is celebrating 10 years of operation.
- The Mount Vernon, Illinois plant is celebrating 50 years of operation.
- Continental has invested around $1.5 billion USD in its U.S. tire manufacturing operations over the last decade.
- Continental’s U.S. tire plants have an annual production capacity of more than 16 million tires per year.
- Continental’s U.S. tire plants employ more than 6,700 employees.
- Jochen Etzel, CEO of Continental Tire the Americas, stated that since 1988 the company has strategically and consistently enhanced its manufacturing footprint to support growth in the American market.
- The Mount Vernon plant produced its first tire in 1974 and employs a team of over 3,900 people.
- The Mount Vernon plant's product portfolio includes both passenger light truck tires (PLT) and truck tires (TT).
- The Sumter plant officially opened in 2014, employs approximately 1,300 employees, and focuses on producing PLT tires.
- The Clinton plant officially opened in 2019, employs around 700 people, and produces TT tires.
- Continental serves both original equipment and replacement customers from its U.S. tire plants.
- Continental has additional manufacturing locations in Aldora Mills and Barnesville, Georgia, which produce tire cord.
- Hoosier produces racing tires at a location in Plymouth, Indiana.
- The U.S. market is identified as a strategic growth market and an important component of Continental Tires' 'Vision 2030' strategy program.
- Continental was recently named 'America's Best Large Employers' by Forbes for the eighth time.
- Mary Ann Kotlarich serves as Head of Brand Communication and Public Relations for Replacement Tires The Americas.
- Katharina Bühmann serves as Spokeswoman for Technology, Innovation & Original Equipment at Continental Tires.
