## Facts

- Nivel Parts & Manufacturing Company provides parts and accessories for golf carts and other specialty vehicles within the automotive aftermarket industry.
- The company offers products including performance and maintenance components, as well as body kits and accessories.
- Nivel serves the specialty vehicle market, providing both original equipment manufacturer (OEM) and aftermarket parts.
- On November 12, 2025, Nivel announced it had acquired Bolt Energy USA.
- Bolt Energy USA makes lithium batteries for golf carts and similar vehicles.
- CEO Donnie Jouppi stated the acquisition reinforces his company's personal transportation (PTV) business.
- Bobby Diaz, founder of Bolt Energy USA, said the purchase would help the companies design 'the best golf car lithium battery on the planet.'
- Nivel was founded in 1968 and is based in Jacksonville, Florida.
- Nivel's headquarters is located at 3510 Port Jacksonville Parkway, Jacksonville, Florida, 32226, United States.
- Nivel's latest funding round is Private Equity.
- Investors of Nivel Parts & Manufacturing Company include North Haven Capital Partners, Kelso & Company, American Capital, Sentinel Capital Partners and Audax Group.
