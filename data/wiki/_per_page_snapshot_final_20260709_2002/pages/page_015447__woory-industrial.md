# Woory Industrial

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Woory secures Hyundai Wia as customer  < IT·Gaming < 기사본문 - The Elec Inc.](https://www.thelec.net/news/articleView.html?idxno=3592)  ·  `page_015447`

## Facts

- Woory Industrial has secured Hyundai Wia as its customer.
- The company signed a long-term contract to supply components for the heat management system in electric vehicles.
- The deal is worth 21.7 billion won and lasts from 2023 to 2030.
- Woory will be supplying its 3-way valves under the deal.
- The component is used to distribute cooling water within the vehicle.
- Hyundai Wia developed a cooling water distribution and supply module for electric vehicles in January of this year.
- Besides 3-way valves, Woory will also be supplying its expansion valve, positive temperature coefficient (PTC), and e-compressor.
- Earlier, the company had secured Hanon Systems as its customer as well.
- The pair’s heat management system is to be used in Ioniq 5 and EV6 vehicles.
- Woory’s PTC is also expected to be supplied to Rivian, Tesla, BMW, Stellantis, and Daimler.
- The company recorded 215.3 billion won in revenue up to the third quarter of this year.
- The company recorded 3.4 billion won in operating income up to the third quarter of this year.
- Revenue increased by 5.28% from the same time period in 2020.
- Operating income increased by 37.04% from the same time period in 2020.
