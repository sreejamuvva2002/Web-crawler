# EVCO Plastics

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [EVCO Plastics and Kistler: partnership for zero defect production in injection molding | Kistler JP](https://www.kistler.com/JP/en/evco-plastics-and-kistler-partnership-for-zero-defect-production-in-injection-molding/C00000564)  ·  `page_054422`

## Facts

- EVCO Plastics and Kistler have formed a partnership for zero-defect production in injection molding.
- Manufacturers can exploit opportunities for optimization and added-value manufacturing by instrumenting a mold with cavity pressure sensors and process monitoring equipment.
- EVCO Plastics is a globally recognized leader in injection molding and many additional technologies.
- The family-run business was founded in Wisconsin in 1964.
- EVCO Plastics has a workforce of over 1,800 employees spread across eleven facilities in the US, China, and Mexico.
- Anna Bartz serves as the Director of Communications at EVCO Plastics.
- Anna Bartz states that together with customers and partners, the company takes plastics processing to the next level.
- Following some recent growth, EVCO Plastics now operates more than 260 molding machines.
- The company offers state-of-the-art technology, close cooperation, tight tolerances, and high quality demand in injection molding.
- EVCO Plastics provides technologies and services across the entire value chain, including automation, packaging, labelling, and more.
- Precision Molding is one of EVCO's four main divisions.
- The division's products and solutions cover diverse industries ranging from agriculture and powersports to packaging and consumer goods.
- EVCO Plastics is involved in just about everything except automotive parts and implantable devices.
- Medical technology accounts for roughly 20 percent of EVCO's annual sales.
- Two EVCO sites are equipped with an ISO Class 8 Clean Room.
- The clean room locations are the headquarters in Deforest, Wisconsin, and the Chinese production facility in Dongguan.
