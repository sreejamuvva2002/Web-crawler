# HL Mando Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-30

**Source:** [HL Mando Recognized as 2024 Supplier of the Year by General Motors | Taiwan News | Apr. 30, 2025 20:00](https://www.taiwannews.com.tw/en/news/6098967)  ·  `page_045937`

## Facts

- General Motors presented HL Mando with a 2024 Supplier of the Year Award at GM's 33rd annual Supplier of the Year event in Phoenix, Arizona, on April 8, 2025.
- HL Mando has received the Supplier of the Year award for five consecutive years and 11 times in total.
- HL Mando was recognized in the Brake Apply & Controls category for its Motor on Caliper (MoC) product.
- The Motor on Caliper (MoC) product is manufactured at HL Mando's Alabama plant.
- General Motors praised HL Mando's localization efforts and technological innovation.
- HL Mando's North American localization journey began in 1996.
- HL Mando established its first R&D center, Mando Engineering & Sales America (MESA), in Michigan to lay the groundwork for North American expansion.
- HL Mando launched its first manufacturing facility, Mando Corporation America (MCA) in Alabama, and won its first Supplier of the Year award in 2003.
- Following the completion of the Alabama plant in 2004, a second production facility was added in 2012 to meet growing demand for casting components, which became the MCA Georgia Plant.
- In 2013, HL Mando expanded its R&D capabilities by establishing a second R&D center, HL Mechatronics (HM), in Michigan.
- MESA focuses on development activities up to mass production, while HM specializes in future-oriented automotive technologies.
- HL Mando has recently invested approximately USD 700 million in its North American operations.
- Jeff Morrison, GM's global chief procurement officer, stated that GM recognizes top global suppliers annually to spotlight innovation and resiliency, and together they are bringing advanced technology and a broad portfolio of vehicles to market for GM customers.
- GM's Supplier of the Year awards recognize global suppliers for execution across safety, innovation, and resilience, with selections based on 2024 performance and alignment to GM's core values and goals.
- Jason (Jae-hyuk) Kim, Head of HL Mando Americas region, stated that three decades of North American localization efforts have led to strong customer satisfaction and expressed a look forward to shaping the future with GM through collaboration in automotive software and autonomous driving.
- Min-Chul Lee serves as Head of Production Engineering team at HL Mando America.
- Jeff Pontius serves as Director of Brake R&D at HL Mando America.
- Alison Ellis serves as Director of Sales at HL Mando America.
- Jason (Jae-hyuk) Kim serves as Head of Americas Region at HL Mando.
- Jeff Morrison serves as Global Chief Procurement Officer at GM.
- The 33rd SOY recognition event in Arizona was attended by HL Mando's Head of the Americas region, R&D Director, and Sales Director.
- 92 companies from 12 countries were recognized as excellent partners to General Motors at the event.
- Less than 1% of GM's suppliers are awarded the Supplier of the Year Award.
