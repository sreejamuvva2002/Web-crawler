# Hyundai Mobis

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-03

**Source:** [Hyundai Mobis breaks ground on $52 million facility](https://businessalabama.com/hyundai-mobis-breaks-ground-on-52-million-facility/)  ·  `page_046738`

## Facts

- Hyundai Mobis has broken ground on a $52 million, 460,000-square-foot facility in Montgomery.
- The new facility will serve the aftermarket parts needs for Hyundai and Kia’s national dealer networks.
- The expansion is expected to add 27 jobs with an average wage of more than $28 an hour.
- Mobis already operates a plant in Montgomery and currently employs more than 1,500 people there.
- Anna Buckalew, president and CEO of the Montgomery Area Chamber of Commerce, stated that investments by existing industries are the highest compliment they can receive.
- Buckalew noted that Mobis has demonstrated its commitment to the area by reinvesting in this facility.
- Buckalew added that over the past 20 years, partnerships and relationships have been the foundation of success for connecting and supporting existing industries to ensure they continue to grow and thrive.
- Graham & Co. Development partnered with Cooper Construction, Five South Architecture, Graham Capital, and the Montgomery Chamber to hold the groundbreaking event.
- State and local leaders, company representatives, and employees attended the groundbreaking to begin the construction phase of the expansion.
- Mobis is the largest tier 1 supplier to both Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama and Kia Motor Manufacturing Georgia.
- Hyundai Mobis’s total investment in Montgomery since 2002 exceeds $475 million.
