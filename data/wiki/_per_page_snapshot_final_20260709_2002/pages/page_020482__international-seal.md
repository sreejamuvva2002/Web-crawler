# International Seal

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 1998-07-01

**Source:** [Freudenberg-NOK, International Seal Co. to Merge O-Ring Businesses](https://www.theautochannel.com/news/press/date/19980609/press013318.html)  ·  `page_020482`

## Facts

- Freudenberg-NOK and International Seal Co., Inc. announced an agreement on June 9, 1998, to merge their respective O-ring businesses.
- The companies signed a letter of intent and expect to complete the agreement by July 1, 1998.
- The new company will be conducted under the name of International Seal.
- The new company will be headquartered in International Seal offices in Henderson, Nevada.
- The new company will have manufacturing facilities in Santa Ana, California, Henderson, Nevada and LaGrange, Georgia.
- The new company will have affiliated plants in Cuautla, Mexico and Sao Paulo, Brazil.
- The new company will be an integral part of the global O-ring business of the $6 billion Freudenberg and NOK Group Companies.
- The Freudenberg and NOK Group Companies have annual O-ring revenues in excess of $300 million.
- The group has O-ring plants in the United States, Mexico, Europe, Japan, Southeast Asia and China.
- The group has distribution capabilities for O-rings in 24 countries around the world.
- The new company will represent over 20 percent of the group's global O-ring sales.
- Joseph C. Day serves as CEO of Freudenberg-NOK.
- Harry Railton serves as Chairman of International Seal Co.
- Harry Railton will continue to serve as CEO of the new company.
- Skip Marvick will continue to serve as president of the new and expanded International Seal.
- International Seal is a leading supplier of industrial O-rings for the aerospace, semiconductor, chemical process and other related industries.
- International Seal is the fastest growing O-ring company in America with special focus on unique materials and low-cost processes.
- International Seal also has operations in Virginia, England, Canada, Mexico and Australia.
- Freudenberg-NOK is headquartered in Plymouth, Michigan.
- The Freudenberg and NOK Group Companies have total annual sales of more than $6 billion.
- The supplier group operates through a global network of facilities spanning 24 countries with some 21,500 employees worldwide.
- The group offers an extensive portfolio of precision-molded products for the aerospace, appliance, business machine, fluid power, marine, medical, off-highway equipment and recreational vehicle markets.
- The Freudenberg and NOK Group also offers its automotive customers globally integrated products, including total sealing packages for engines, transmissions and brakes.
- The group provides NVH components and modules to automotive customers.
- The group supplies all rubber, plastic and PTFE components for suspension, steering, electrical and fuel systems to automotive customers.
