# Pirelli

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2014-03-11

**Source:** [Pirelli Bringing New 125cc GP/Moto3 Rear Tire To North America - Roadracing World Magazine | Motorcycle Riding, Racing & Tech News](https://www.roadracingworld.com/news/pirelli-bringing-new-125cc-gpmoto3-rear-tire-to-north-america/)  ·  `page_048622`

## Facts

- Pirelli expands its 2014 roadracing product range by introducing new 125 GP/Moto 3 rear tires to North America.
- The new 115/70R17 Slicks come in three compounds (SC0, SC1, & SC2) and match up with the current 100/70R17 slick front.
- These new tires were developed in Europe with top Moto3 and 125GP teams and provide improved handling, performance, and grip over the recently discontinued 125/70R17 rear slick size.
- Jeff Johnston serves as Pirelli Tire North America Roadracing Manager.
- Pirelli offers a complete range of track day, slicks, intermediates and rain tires for almost any racing or track day application for small bore GP, 600 SS or Superbike riders.
- These new rear slicks are readily available from Pirelli roadracing tire distributors and vendors.
- Founded in 1872, Pirelli is the fifth largest tire manufacturer in the world in terms of sales.
- Pirelli is present in over 160 countries, operates 22 manufacturing sites, and has over 36,000 employees worldwide.
- Pirelli is a leading producer of premium tires due to its commitment to R&D.
- The company has successfully competed in motorsport since 1907 and is the exclusive supplier of the Superbike world championship and many single marque championships around the world.
- Pirelli has been the sole supplier for the Formula 1 championship since 2011.
- Pirelli Tire North America (PTNA) services NAFTA member countries through its Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) facility and research and development center at its Rome, Georgia headquarters.
- PTNA also utilizes a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant in Silao, Mexico and sales and marketing offices in New York City, Montreal, and Mexico City.
- The company manufactures, distributes and markets original equipment and replacement tires for export and domestic car/motorcycle applications (US, CA, MX) and Agro/Heavy Truck platforms (MX).
