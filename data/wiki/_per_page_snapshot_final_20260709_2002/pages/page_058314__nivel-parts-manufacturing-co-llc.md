# Nivel Parts & Manufacturing Co., LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-05-19

## Facts

- Nivel Parts & Manufacturing Co., LLC (Nivel) is a portfolio company of Kelso & Co., LP (Kelso).
- Investment funds managed by Morgan Stanley Capital Partners acquired Nivel.
- The acquisition transaction closed on May 19, 2021.
- Houlihan Lokey served as the financial advisor to Nivel for the transaction.
- Nivel is headquartered in Jacksonville, Florida.
- Nivel operates as a global manufacturer, distributor, and seller of aftermarket parts and accessories for niche vehicles and heavy-duty equipment.
- Nivel is a trusted business partner to thousands of dealers, distributors, and consumers across U.S. and international markets.
- Nivel owns the brands Madjax, Red Dot, GTW, Jake’s, Reliance, Seizmik, and High Lifter in the aftermarket niche vehicle parts and accessories categories.
- Kelso is a private equity firm specializing in investing that has been established since 1980.
- Since 1980, Kelso has invested over $15 billion of equity capital across 132 transactions.
- Kelso was founded by the inventor of the employee stock ownership plan (ESOP).
- Kelso is based in New York.
- Morgan Stanley Capital Partners operates as part of Morgan Stanley Investment Management.
- Morgan Stanley Capital Partners is a middle-market private equity platform that has invested capital across industries for over three decades.
- Morgan Stanley Capital Partners focuses on privately negotiated equity and equity-related investments primarily in North America.
- Morgan Stanley Capital Partners targets value creation in portfolio companies within business services, consumer, healthcare, and industrials markets.
- Houlihan Lokey’s Specialty Distribution team advised Nivel and has a reputation for working with premier distribution clients.
