## Facts

- Rivian is a relatively new electric-vehicle manufacturer headquartered in California.
- Rivian's flagship product is the R1T pickup truck.
- The R1T is offered alongside a range of SUVs, including the mid-size R1S and the smaller, more affordable R2.
- Rivian's passenger vehicles are able to tackle rough terrain and offer a host of tech features.
- The company also produces an electric commercial van that you may recognize as the vehicle that delivers your Amazon purchases.
