# Sejong Georgia LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2007-11-20

**Source:** [Kia supplier to locate in LaGrange | Columbus Ledger-Enquirer](https://www.ledger-enquirer.com/news/local/article28976074.html)  ·  `page_059283`

## Facts

- Sejong Georgia LLC will invest $27.8 million in a LaGrange facility.
- The investment will bring 250 jobs to the LaGrange area.
- The manufacturer will supply muffler and exhaust systems to the Kia Motors plant under construction in West Point.
- Georgia Governor Sonny Perdue stated, "With a great supplier like Sejong on board, Georgia is continuing to build on the success of landing Kia."
- The Wulsan, Korea-based company secured a 23-acre site in the LaGrange Industrial Park on Lukken Industrial Drive West.
- The city purchased the former Federal Mogul facility, which closed in 2004, and will lease the space to Sejong.
- Renovations on the 108,000-square-foot building, funded in part by state grants, are scheduled to begin in July 2008.
- Production at the new facility is planned to begin in October 2009.
- The city is offering the company a 7-year tax phase-in based on the investment and job creation.
- The incentives package includes reduced rent on the building for the first 60 months and an option to buy the site after that time.
- City officials and Sejong Georgia President H.S. Kim signed a memorandum of understanding regarding the site.
- Diethard Lindner, chairman of the Development Authority of LaGrange, stated, "Having visited Sejong's facilities in Korea and Alabama, I am certain that they will be an excellent community partner."
- This facility will be Sejong's second U.S. plant.
- The company currently operates a plant in Fort Deposit, Ala., to supply the Hyundai Motors plant in Montgomery.
- Other recent Kia supplier announcements for the area include Dongwon Autopart Technology of Korea building a metal-stamping plant in Meriwether County that will add 300 jobs.
- Hyundai Mobis announced plans in August to build a plant in West Point.
- Pretty Products, DaeLim USA, and Sumika Polymer Compounds America Inc. also recently made announcements regarding supplier locations in the area.
