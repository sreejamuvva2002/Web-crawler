## Facts

- BRPH is an expert in complex manufacturing facilities for the world’s most innovative manufacturers and is known for its decades of specialized aviation expertise.
- BRPH is consistently ranked nationally among manufacturing design firms.
- BRPH has served a growing list of industries, emphasizing companies requiring clean and secure manufacturing environments, automation, Industry 4.0 technology integration, and specialized process and tooling equipment installation.
- BRPH's portfolio includes operations centers ranging from 10,000 square feet to 1.3 million-square-foot assembly buildings.
- BRPH designs a host of ancillary facilities for research and development, warehousing and supply chain integration, and customer delivery centers.
- BRPH supports the development of greenfield facilities from site selection and master planning through process layout, concept development, full design, engineering, and construction, including projects executed on a fast-track and design/build basis.
- BRPH's manufacturing experts and industrial engineers consult on process flow improvement and facility consolidations to improve efficiency and unlock capacity in ways that advance the bottom line.
- BRPH teams pay close attention to production efficiency, energy consumption, and facility configurations that allow for future expansion.
- BRPH designers strive to help clients create safe, attractive, and engaging workplaces to address growing issues in manufacturing sector workforce development and employee retention.
- Rich Winters, President of FOX Factory, Inc., stated that BRPH worked with their team members to design a 'Factory of the Future' to allow process flexibility as manufacturing evolved to meet future market demands, and that BRPH always provided services with the highest level of professionalism and responsiveness.
