## Facts

- Fourth quarter 2024 earnings per share were $2.45, a fourth quarter record and up 4% over the fourth quarter of 2023.
- Excluding charges of $0.22 per share related to intangible amortization, $0.14 per share related to a multi-year restructuring program, and $0.02 per share related to acquisitions and divestitures, adjusted earnings per share of $2.83 were a fourth quarter record and up 11% over the fourth quarter of 2023.
- Fourth quarter sales were a record $6.2 billion, up 5% from the fourth quarter of 2023.
- Organic sales in the fourth quarter were up 6%, partially offset by 1% from negative currency translation.
- Hurricane Helene and labor strikes in the aerospace industry negatively impacted fourth quarter sales by approximately $80 million, or 130 basis points.
- Fourth quarter segment margins were a quarterly record of 24.7%, above the high end of the latest guidance range and up 190-basis points over the fourth quarter of 2023.
- Fourth quarter operating cash flow was $1.6 billion and free cash flow was $1.3 billion, both quarterly records up 23% and 27%, respectively, over the same period in 2023.
- Craig Arnold serves as Eaton chairman and chief executive officer.
- Craig Arnold stated that Eaton delivered on commitments with record segment margins and strong orders/backlog, and expressed confidence in continued momentum into 2025 amid megatrends driving higher growth.
- Full year 2024 sales were a record $24.9 billion, up 7% from 2023.
- Full year 2024 organic sales were up 8%, partially offset by 1% from negative currency translation.
- Full year 2024 segment margins were a record 24.0%, above the high end of the latest guidance range, representing a 200-basis point improvement over the full year 2023.
- Full year 2024 earnings per share were a record $9.50.
- Excluding charges of $0.84 per share related to intangible amortization, $0.40 per share related to a multi-year restructuring program, and $0.06 per share related to acquisitions and divestitures, adjusted earnings per share for 2024 were a record $10.80, up 18% over 2023.
- Full year 2024 operating cash flow was $4.3 billion and free cash flow was $3.5 billion, both records up 19% and 23%, respectively, over the same period in 2023.
- The company issued guidance for the first quarter and full year 2025.
- Electrical Americas segment fourth quarter sales were a record $2.9 billion, up 9% from the fourth quarter of 2023, driven entirely by organic sales growth.
- Electrical Americas segment fourth quarter operating profits were a record $918 million, up 20% over the fourth quarter of 2023, with margins at a record 31.6%.
- The twelve-month rolling average of orders for the Electrical Americas segment in the fourth quarter was up 16% organically.
- Electrical Americas backlog at the end of December remained at record levels, up 29% organically over December 2023.
- Electrical Global segment fourth quarter sales were a record $1.6 billion, up 4% from the fourth quarter of 2023.
- Electrical Global segment organic sales were up 5.5%, partially offset by 1.5% from negative currency translation.
- Electrical Global segment fourth quarter operating profits were $277 million and margins were 17.7%.
- The twelve-month rolling average of orders for the Electrical Global segment in the fourth quarter was up 4% organically, with backlog up 16% organically over December 2023.
- On a rolling twelve-month basis, the book-to-bill ratio for the Electrical businesses remained strong at 1.1.
- Aerospace segment fourth quarter sales were a record $971 million, up 9% from the fourth quarter of 2023, driven entirely by organic sales growth.
- Aerospace segment fourth quarter operating profits were a record $222 million, up 11% over the fourth quarter of 2023, with margins at 22.9%.
- The twelve-month rolling average of orders for the Aerospace segment in the fourth quarter was up 10% organically, with backlog up 16% organically over December 2023.
- On a rolling twelve-month basis, the book-to-bill ratio for the Aerospace segment remained strong at 1.1.
- The Vehicle segment posted fourth quarter sales of $647 million, down 10% from the fourth quarter of 2023, driven by an organic sales decline of 7% and negative currency translation of 3%.
- The Vehicle segment fourth quarter operating profits were $122 million with margins at a record 18.8%, up 90 basis points over the fourth quarter of 2023.
- The eMobility segment posted fourth quarter sales of $147 million, down 11% over the fourth quarter of 2023, driven by an organic sales decline of 10% and negative currency translation of 1%.
- The eMobility segment recorded an operating profit of $3 million with margins at 1.8%.
- Eaton makes products for the data center, utility, industrial, commercial, machine building, residential, aerospace and mobility markets.
- Eaton is capitalizing on global growth trends of electrification and digitalization to accelerate the transition to renewable energy sources.
- Founded in 1911, Eaton serves customers in more than 160 countries with revenues of nearly $25 billion in 2024.
