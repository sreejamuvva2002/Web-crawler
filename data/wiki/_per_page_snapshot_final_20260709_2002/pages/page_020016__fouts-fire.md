## Facts

- Established in 1952 and proudly family-owned.
- Premier manufacturer of high-quality fire apparatus headquartered in Milledgeville, Georgia.
- Specializes in the design and production of reliable, cost-effective fire apparatus, including pumpers, tankers, and rescue vehicles.
- Designs nine standard models engineered to meet the evolving demands of fire departments nationwide.
- Standardized designs eliminate unnecessary complexity and focus on performance, durability, and safety.
- Streamlined assembly process reduces production bottlenecks and accelerates build times.
- Apparatus are assembled by skilled technicians who understand the mission behind the machine.
- Operates one of the largest dealer networks in the country for delivery and nationwide support.
- Nationwide partners provide local support, training, and service.
