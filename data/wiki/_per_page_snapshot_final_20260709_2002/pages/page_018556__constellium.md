## Facts

- Constellium stands out as a leading aluminum supplier with the unique ability to deliver both advanced rolled products and extrusion-based components for the automotive industry.
- As a trusted partner and Automotive News Top Supplier, Constellium helps empower the world’s leading automakers with advanced aluminum solutions to shape the next generation of vehicles: lighter, cleaner, more sustainable, and built for performance.
- 25% of Constellium's 2025 revenue came from the automotive market.
- Constellium has an annual capacity of 350K+ mt for automotive rolled products.
- Constellium has an annual capacity of 100K+ mt for automotive extrusions.
- Constellium supplies a complete range of aluminum body sheet materials tailored for inner panels, outer skins, and structural parts.
- The company's automotive body sheet products are engineered to support the automotive industry’s shift toward lighter, more efficient vehicles – without compromising strength, safety, or visual quality.
- Constellium is a trusted supplier of advanced aluminum extrusion-based automotive components, engineered for superior safety and structural integrity.
- The company provides customized high-strength, and lightweight solutions that meet the rigorous demands of automakers, while enhancing vehicle performance and sustainability.
- Constellium brings deep expertise and a forward-thinking approach to specialty automotive applications, including enabling efficient thermal management with high-performance heat exchanger alloys.
- The company also elevates vehicle interiors and exteriors through precision-finished decorative surfaces.
- Aluminum’s thermal conductivity is about five times that of steel, making it the superior choice for thermal applications in battery enclosures.
- For battery enclosures, Constellium uses aluminum sheets and extrusions to provide a strong, crash- and intrusion-resistant exterior to protect the batteries that power EVs.
- Constellium's technical teams are working closely with automakers to design modular battery enclosures that can be easily configured to fit various shapes and sizes, optimizing both design flexibility and manufacturing costs.
