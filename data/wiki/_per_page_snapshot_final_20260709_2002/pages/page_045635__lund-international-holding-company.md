# Lund International Holding Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-01-20

**Source:** [Lund International, a Portfolio Company of Highlander Partners, Acquires Bushwacker – Highlander Partners](https://highlander-partners.com/news-posts/lund-international-a-portfolio-company-of-highlander-partners-acquires-bushwacker/)  ·  `page_045635`

## Facts

- Lund International Holding Company, a portfolio company of Highlander Partners, L.P., announced on January 20, 2015, that it has acquired Bushwacker, Inc. and its subsidiaries.
- The acquisition announcement was issued from Buford, GA.
- Bushwacker, based in Portland, Oregon, is a major manufacturer and marketer of branded automotive aftermarket accessories including fender flares, bedrail and tailgate caps, bumper protection products, and trail armor components for Jeeps.
- Bushwacker also manufactures and markets hood protection products and window visors under the Stampede brand acquired in 2012, which is based in Camanche, Iowa.
- Operations will continue from existing facilities and be led by Bushwacker’s President and CEO Russ Morgan, who will join the Lund International team.
- Mitch Fogle serves as President and CEO of Lund.
- Jeff L. Hull serves as Chairman of Lund and Managing Partner of Highlander.
- This acquisition marks Lund’s fifth since Highlander acquired the company in 2011 and represents its largest addition to date.
- The transaction significantly strengthens Lund’s geographic presence and product depth in the automotive aftermarket accessories market.
- Bushwacker will significantly add to Lund's Jeep offering, which was recently enhanced with the December 2014 acquisition of Rampage Products, LLC.
- Regions Business Capital, as Administrative Agent, along with a syndicate of participating lenders, provided senior financing for the transaction.
- AEA Mezzanine Fund III LP provided mezzanine debt in support of the transaction.
- Deloitte Corporate Finance served as the exclusive financial advisor to Bushwacker in connection with this transaction.
- Highlander Partners, L.P. is a Dallas-based private investment firm with over $1 billion of assets under management that uses a “buy and build” investment approach.
- Lund International Holding Company is a leading designer, manufacturer, and marketer of branded automotive aftermarket accessories for passenger cars, light trucks, and heavy trucks.
- Lund's product lineup includes Ventvisors®, hood shields, floor mats, fender flares, tonneau covers, storage boxes, nerf bars, and running boards.
