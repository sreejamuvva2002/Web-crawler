## Facts

- Novelis Inc. administers its Fiscal 2025 Annual Incentive Plan (AIP) through its Human Resources department.
- The AIP performance period commences on April 1, 2024, and ends on March 31, 2025.
- Payouts under the plan are made as a lump sum during the first fiscal quarter following the end of the performance year.
- Eligibility for the AIP includes employees in job bands 7 and higher, all Corporate and Regional Headquarters employees, and all employees assigned to the Bay Minette Project Plan.
- Employees must be employed in an eligible job band or position, or transferred or hired into one during the performance year, to receive a payout.
- For JB7+ employees, performance measure weightages are Global Safety (10%), Adjusted Operating EBITDA (50%), and Adjusted Operating Cash Flow (40%).
- Employees involved in the Bay Minette project have role-based weightings for the Steering Committee, Project Team, and Support Functions across metrics including Global Safety, Adjusted Operating EBITDA, and Adjusted Operating Cash Flow.
- The overall AIP plan threshold for payout on any metric is 75% of the target Adjusted Operating EBITDA.
- No payout will be made unless Adjusted Operating EBITDA reaches at least 75% of the target level, with results between threshold and maximum determined by interpolation.
- Individual performance ratings (IP, PDP, DFP, OP, SP) act as modifiers on payouts, with JB6-11B employees receiving multipliers ranging from 0% to 130%.
- Total annual payouts are capped at 170%, 190%, and 200% for employees rated DFP, OP, and SP respectively.
- Participants who terminate employment or transfer to an ineligible position during the performance year will have prorated payouts based on days of employment, except for voluntary termination which results in forfeiture.
- Global Safety is measured using Days Away from Work (DAFW) based on a standard OSHA calculation approximating 100 person-years.
- A fatality at a Company location during the fiscal year ended March 31, 2025, will result in select participants receiving no payout for the Global Safety measure.
- The plan defines Change in Control events involving beneficial ownership thresholds of 35% or more, board replacements, mergers with non-Aditya Birla Group entities, or asset sales.
- Novelis reserves the right to interpret, amend, or modify the plan at any time and does not confer a right to continued service.
