# ADVICS Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-31

**Source:** [ADVICS Co., Ltd. - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/advics)  ·  `page_051781`

## Facts

- The Company is a subsidiary of Aisin, a company which is partly owned by the Toyota Group.
- It is a specialized brake system manufacturer in the Aisin Group, which is led by Aisin Seiki.
- The Company was established jointly by Aisin Seiki Co., Ltd., Denso Corporation, Sumitomo Electric Industries, Ltd., and Toyota Motor Corporation in 2001.
- As of Mar. 31, 2025, Aisin Corporation holds a 51.1% shareholding and Denso Corporation holds a 34.0% shareholding.
- The Company manufactures brake systems categorized under Safety, Comfort, Environment, Components, Actuations, Foundations, and Parking brakes.
- Safety products include ABS (Anti-lock Brake Systems), BA (Brake Assist), ESC (Electronic Stability Control), AEBS (Autonomous Emergency Brake System), lane keep assist system, and autonomous emergency brake.
- Comfort products include ACC (Adaptive Cruise Control), A-TRC (Active Traction Control), HAC (Hill-start Assist Control), DAC (Downhill Assist Control), electric parking brake, and Crawl control.
- Environment products include regenerative brake systems.
- Components include ABS & ESC modulators, high-performance ESC modulator, and hydraulic booster with built-in ESC.
- Actuations include brake boosters & Master cylinders with brake assist, hydraulic booster, and load sensing proportioning valves.
- Foundations include disc brakes and drum brakes.
- Parking brakes include electric parking brakes, caliper integrated parking brake, integrated drum parking brake, and foot release parking brake pedals.
- In Jul. 2001, the company was established with registered capital of 1 billion yen.
- Commercial operations started in Oct. 2001.
- ADVICS North America, Inc. was established in USA in Jan. 2002.
- The company won a contract from GM to supply full brake systems for the Hummer H3 in Feb. 2002.
- In Apr. 2003, the company acquired two U.S. facilities from Sumitomo Electric Industries, Ltd. and established both ADVICS Manufacturing Ohio, Inc and SAFA L.L.C.

- The company acquired ISO14001 certification in Jan. 2005.

- Mass production of the world's first electric parking brake (EPB) system with a shift linked function started in Sep. 2006, featured on the Lexus LS460.
