## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America (YMMC) facility is located in Newnan, Georgia.
- The YMMC facility was founded in 1986.
- YMMC produces golf cars, personal watercraft, Side-by-Side vehicles, and all-terrain vehicles (ATVs).
- YMMC holds ISO9001 Quality Management System certification.
- YMMC holds ISO14001 Environmental Management System certification.
- Yamaha Motor Corp., USA (YMUS) maintains two corporate offices in Georgia, including one in Marietta.
- YMUS has factory operations located in Georgia.
- YMUS product offerings include Power Assist Bicycles and Power Assist Wheelchair Systems.
- YMUS facilities are also located in Wisconsin and Alabama.
- YMUS has additional factory operations in Tennessee.
- YMUS product offerings include Surface Mount Technology (SMT) and Robotic Machines.
- Additional U.S.-based subsidiaries include Yamaha Marine Systems Company with divisions Bennett Marine (Florida) and Kracor Systems (Wisconsin).
- Additional U.S.-based subsidiaries include Skeeter Boats (Texas) with division G3 Boats (Missouri).
- Additional U.S.-based subsidiaries include Yamaha Precision Propeller (Indiana).
- YMUS products are sold through a nationwide network of distributors and dealers in the United States.
