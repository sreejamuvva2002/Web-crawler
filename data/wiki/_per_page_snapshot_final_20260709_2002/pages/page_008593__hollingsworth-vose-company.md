## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose Company holds patent application 20260081303 for battery separators comprising embossments that may take the form of indentations formed in a surface via an embossing process, filed July 22, 2025, and published March 19, 2026.
- Hollingsworth & Vose Company holds patent 12401090 for battery separators comprising embossments formed by an embossing process, filed February 10, 2020, and granted August 26, 2025.
- The company has patent applications describing thermal barrier materials with desirable thermal properties such as low thermal conductivity and high thermal stability.
- The company's thermal barrier materials can have structural properties including high porosity, high pore tortuosity, and low air permeability to enhance mechanical resiliency, compressibility, elasticity, and thermal insulation performance.
- Hollingsworth & Vose Company holds patent applications for filter media designed to filter gas streams like air, featuring stable filtration efficiency, increased normalized gamma, relatively low pressure drop, and low basis weight.
- The company's filter media can be a composite of two or more fiber layers designed to enhance function without substantially negatively impacting the performance of another layer.
- Hollingsworth & Vose Company holds patents for filter media with stable filtration efficiency, high oil repellency, low instantaneous resistance, and stable service life.
- The company's filter media can optionally include a support layer with relatively high air permeability, such as greater than or equal to 1100 CFM.
- Hollingsworth & Vose Company has patent applications for filter media comprising non-woven fiber webs with enhanced physical properties from combinations of fibrillated fibers, glass fibers, and/or binder fibers.
- The company holds patents for filter media including nonwoven fiber webs comprising nanofibers.
- Hollingsworth & Vose Company has patents for filter media bonded to filter housings that can withstand sterilization processes like autoclaving without compromising adhesion strength or structural integrity.
- The company holds applications for filter media comprising water-repellent additives, including fluorinated and non-fluorinated types, and/or fibrillated fibers such as lyocell.
- Hollingsworth & Vose Company has patents covering multilayered media with irregular structures that are reversibly stretchable and/or comprise a polymeric film.
- The company holds patents for fiber webs used in filter media that include fibrillated fibers and optionally non-fibrillated fibers, with limited or no glass fiber to impart mechanical properties and filtration efficiency like dust holding capacity.
- Hollingsworth & Vose Company has patents for filter media comprising a pre-filter layer designed to improve upstream removal of fine particulate matter, increase service life, and increase gamma without adversely affecting downstream efficiency layers.
- The company holds patents for filter media comprising nanofibers where first and second fiber webs have similar water contact angles or surface energy to enhance structural stability under high temperature, pressure, or steam sterilization.
- Hollingsworth & Vose Company has patents for filter media comprising a waved filtration layer with a gradient in fiber diameter across its thickness to produce low pressure drop and long lifetime.
