# Lund International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Lund International designs, manufactures and markets branded automotive accessories primarily for light and heavy trucks, sport utility vehicles (SUVs), crossover utility vehicles (CUVs), vans and passenger cars.
- Products are distributed by top warehouse distributors and found in automotive parts retailers, farm and agriculture retailers, home centers, online retailers and original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).
- The company was founded in 1965, with roots dating back to the 1920s.
- Lund International started operations in Anoka, Minnesota.
- It is now one of the world's leading automotive accessories providers.
- Recognized brand names include AMP Research, AVS, Belmor, Bushwacker, LUND, Rampage Products, RoadWorks Manufacturing, Roll-N-Lock, Stampede Automotive Accessories and Tonno Pro.
- The company has been located in Buford, GA since 2002.
- Lund International’s global headquarters and distribution warehouse operates out of a 541,000 sq. ft. facility.
- The Georgia facility has convenient access for shipping by both air and land.
- Lund International offers over 30 categories of products.
- Product offerings include side window deflectors, hood protectors, nerf bars and running boards, fender flares, tonneau covers, storage boxes and toolboxes, liquid transfer tanks, premium floor liners, light covers, chrome, truck bed and Jeep accessories, and cargo management solutions.
- Each product is backed by an industry best warranty and exceptional customer service.
