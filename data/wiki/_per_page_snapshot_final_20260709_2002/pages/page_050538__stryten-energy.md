# Stryten Energy

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-11

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Named a 2024 Military Friendly Employer Intrado](https://pr.hollistontownnews.com/article/Stryten-Energy-Named-a-2024-Military-Friendly-Employer?storyId=654f89ba2661f3221612b2c7)  ·  `page_050538`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- The company provides a broad range of energy storage solutions and components across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- Stryten Energy powers applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- The company's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- Stryten Energy is backed by more than a century of industry expertise.
- Veterans make up approximately six percent of Stryten Energy's workforce.
- The company hires military members across every level from executive management to the plant floor, with many having progressed through multiple promotions.
- Stryten Energy currently has job openings for military members across numerous U.S. locations.
- Mike Judd serves as President and Chief Executive Officer at Stryten Energy.
- The company is a proud sponsor of Rivers of Recovery, an innovative national nonprofit that uses outdoor-based activities to effectively treat symptoms of post-traumatic stress, depression, and anxiety in combat veterans and active-duty military.
- Over 1,000 companies participated in the 2024 Military Friendly® survey used to evaluate the designation.
- Final ratings for the designation were determined by combining an organization's survey score with an assessment of thresholds for Applicant, New Hire Retention, Employee Turnover, and Promotion & Advancement of veterans and military employees.
