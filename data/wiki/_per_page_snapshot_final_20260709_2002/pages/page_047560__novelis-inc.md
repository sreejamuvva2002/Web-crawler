# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Novelis Inc. names Roberta Soares and Cary Chenanda as Regional Presidents for South and North America](https://www.alcircle.com/news/novelis-inc-names-roberta-soares-and-cary-chenanda-as-regional-presidents-for-south-and-north-america-111335)  ·  `page_047560`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is the world's leading provider of sustainable aluminium solutions and a global leader in aluminium rolling and recycling.
- Roberta Soares has been named Regional President for South America, effective June 1.
- Cary Chenanda has been named Regional President for North America.
- Francisco Pires, the former President of South America, will now serve as Chief Transformation Officer.
- Tadeu Nardocci, previously interim president of North America, will transition to Executive Vice President of Manufacturing Operations.
- Steve Fisher serves as President and CEO of Novelis.
- As head of the South America region, Roberta Soares leads all aspects of business that had net sales of $2.46 billion in fiscal year 2024.
- The Novelis South America region operates two plants and employs approximately 1,700 people serving beverage packaging and speciality markets.
- Soares was most recently Vice President of Operations at Novelis South America before her appointment.
- Prior to that role, Soares spent four years as Vice President of Finance and IT at Novelis South America.
- Soares joined Novelis in 2000 as a marketing intelligence and financial planning analyst in Brazil.
- Cary Chenanda has been appointed Executive Vice President and President of Novelis North America.
- The Novelis North America region had net sales of $6.72 billion in fiscal year 2024.
- The North America region operates 15 plants and employs over 4,000 people serving the beverage packaging, automotive, and speciality markets.
- Chenanda joined Novelis in April 2024 as President-designate of the North American region.
- Prior to joining Novelis, Chenanda spent 25 years at Cummins, most recently as Vice President & General Manager of Cummins Emissions Solutions.
- Cummins Emissions Solutions is a $4 billion business with 4,500 employees, 12 manufacturing plants, seven technical centres, and two joint ventures worldwide.
- Francisco Pires will focus on driving action on Novelis' vision to advance aluminium as the material of choice for circular solutions.
- Pires previously served as chief operating officer before his most recent role as senior vice president and president of Novelis South America.
- Pires joined Novelis in 2012 as director of procurement in South America.
- Tadeu Nardocci will oversee several transformational investment projects already underway.
- Novelis is making an approximately $4.1 billion investment to build an integrated 600-kilotonne rolling and recycling plant in Bay Minette, Alabama.
- The Bay Minette, Alabama plant will serve the beverage packaging and automotive markets.
- Nardocci is a 45-year veteran of Novelis with deep expertise in capital expansion projects.
- Nardocci has previously held roles including interim President of Novelis North America, Chief Manufacturing Officer, President of Novelis South America, President of Novelis Europe, and Senior Vice President of innovation, technology, and strategy.
- Steve Fisher stated that each new executive brings a wealth of experience in manufacturing leadership and a proven track record of success.
- Novelis expects the collective leadership to bolster its commitment to operational excellence, customer service, innovation, and sustainability.
