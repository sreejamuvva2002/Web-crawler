# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-05-26

**Source:** [Superior Essex in Talks with Furukawa Electric for Further Collaboration in High Performance Winding Wire Business - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.de/news/superior-essex-in-talks-with-furukawa-electric-for-further-collaboration-in-high-performance-winding-wire-business/)  ·  `page_010556`

## Facts

- Superior Essex Inc. and Furukawa Electric Co., Ltd. are currently pursuing a worldwide collaboration targeting the Chinese and North American markets.
- The companies have built a strong business relationship through technical cooperation and past joint ventures.
- The companies currently operate an established German joint venture, Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire Europe GmbH (Superior Essex: 51%, Furukawa: 49%), founded in March 2017 to supply high-voltage winding wire and other demanding products like HVWW® to the European market.
- A global company would open up innovation and sales opportunities for both companies.
- Furukawa Electric is a pioneer in developing high-performance winding wire HVWW® for applications in electric motors, generators, and chokes for automobiles.
- Superior Essex operates 12 global production facilities as well as its worldwide sales and distribution network.
- The wire manufacturers will announce further details once an agreement is reached.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, and Essex Brownell.
- The company employs over 3,000 employees in 11 countries across three continents.
- Superior Essex is the world's leading provider of magnet cables and a leader in communication fiber applications as well as Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company significantly contributes to disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex is headquartered at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327 – USA.
