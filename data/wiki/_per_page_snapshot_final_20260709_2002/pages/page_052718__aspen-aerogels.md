# Aspen Aerogels

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-04-02

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels is planning a $325 million factory investment.
- The facility will be located in Bulloch County’s Southern Gateway Commerce Park.
- The factory has been designed to be expandable for future growth.
- Phase 1 of the project will create at least 250 advanced manufacturing jobs.
- Top company executives stated that the total number of jobs could nearly double over time.
- Aspen Aerogels anticipates rapid growth in the electric vehicle market.
- The company cites a growing need for batteries and insulation as key drivers for its expansion.
- Company executives announced these details on Thursday.
