# Yamaha Motor Corp., USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-26

**Source:** [Yamaha Continues as an OEM Partner of Progressive AFT and Title Sponsor of Senoia Short Track - American Flat Track](https://www.americanflattrack.com/news/view/yamaha-continues-official-oem-partner-progressive-aft-and-title-sponsor-senoia-short-track)  ·  `page_015728`

## Facts

- Progressive American Flat Track, sanctioned by AMA Pro Racing, named Yamaha Motor Corp., USA an OEM Partner for the 2025 season.
- Yamaha continues as title sponsor for the Yamaha Senoia Short Track event on Saturday, March 29 at Senoia Raceway in Senoia, Georgia.
- This marks Yamaha’s third consecutive season holding the title sponsor role in Senoia.
- It is the sixth time the manufacturer has sponsored Progressive AFT rounds in the greater Atlanta area since opening its Marietta, Georgia, offices in 2018.
- In the 2024 Progressive AFT campaign, riders Dallas Daniels and JD Beach finished on the podium in all 16 races aboard Estenson Racing Yamaha MT-07 motorcycles.
- The Daniels/Beach duo won five races with an average finishing position of better than second place (1.875).
- Tom Drane led the AFT Singles competition with seven victories and 13 podiums in 16 attempts aboard the Estenson Racing Yamaha YZ450F.
- Daniels and Drane will return on Yamahas in 2025 as leading contenders for their respective championships.
- Yamaha will bring approximately 250 employees to the Senoia event, featuring a branded presence in the Fan Zone and dedicated parking area.
- Bob Brown, President of Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America, stated that YMUS and YMMC are thrilled to sponsor the Senoia Short Track for the third consecutive year, emphasizing passion for team member activities and community involvement while showcasing innovation.
- Yamaha Motor Corp., USA (YMUS) maintains two corporate offices in Georgia.
- YMUS operates factory facilities in Tennessee and Georgia, along with additional facilities in Wisconsin and Alabama.
- YMUS product portfolio includes motorcycles, scooters, ATVs, side-by-side vehicles, snowmobiles, outboard motors, WaveRunners, boats, outdoor power equipment, power assist bicycles, golf cars, wheelchair systems, SMT machines, unmanned helicopters, accessories, and apparel.
- YMUS products are distributed nationwide through a network of distributors and dealers.
- YMUS corporate headquarters is located in Cypress, California.
- Other U.S.-based Yamaha companies include Skeeter Boats (Texas), G3 Boats (Missouri), Bennet Marine (Florida), Yamaha Precision Propeller (Indiana), and Kracor, Inc. (Wisconsin).
