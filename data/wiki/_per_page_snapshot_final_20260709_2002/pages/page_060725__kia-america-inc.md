# Kia America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-04

**Source:** [KIA AMERICA ANNOUNCES CUSTOMER ASSISTANCE PROGRAM AND DONATES $250,000 TO THE AMERICAN RED CROSS FOR THOSE IMPACTED BY HURRICANE HELENE - Kia Georgia](https://www.kiageorgia.com/kia-america-announces-customer-assistance-program-and-donates-250000-to-the-american-red-cross-for-those-impacted-by-hurricane-helene/)  ·  `page_060725`

## Facts

- Kia America announced immediate assistance programs to help in recovery efforts following Hurricane Helene in the Southeastern U.S.
- Eligible Kia owners in Florida, Georgia, South Carolina, and North Carolina will receive $500 in support for the purchase or lease of select new Kia vehicles between October 4, 2024, and January 2, 2025.
- Customers must have an insurance claim for loss of a vehicle due to Hurricane Helene to be eligible for the payment assistance program.
- The $500 payment assistance can be combined with cash incentives from Kia America, Inc., and finance or lease offers from Kia Finance America (KFA).
- The offer is valid on new 2024 or 2025 Kia vehicles, excluding the 2024 K5, 2024 Sorento Hybrid, and 2024 Sorento PHEV.
- Customers must take delivery from retailer stock by January 2, 2025.
- Current customers affected by the disaster can contact Kia Finance America to discuss financial hardship support options.
- Kia America partnered with Kia Georgia, Inc., to donate $250,000 to the American Red Cross for disaster relief efforts throughout the region.
- SeungKyu (Sean) Yoon serves as President and CEO of Kia North America and Kia America.
- Alicia Doherty serves as Regional Executive for the Red Cross of Georgia.
- Hurricane Helene made landfall as a Category 4 hurricane on Sept. 26, causing catastrophic damage throughout Georgia, Florida, South Carolina, North Carolina, and Tennessee.
- The donation extends Kia’s “Accelerate the Good” charitable program, which has provided more than $30 million to those in need since 2019.
- Kia America is headquartered in Irvine, California.
- Kia is recognized as one of the TIME World’s Most Sustainable Companies of 2024.
- Kia serves as the “Official Automotive Partner” of the NBA and WNBA.
- Kia offers gasoline, hybrid, plug-in hybrid, and electric vehicles sold through a network of over 775 dealers in the U.S.
- Certain 2025 EV9 all-electric three-row SUV, Sportage (excludes HEV/PHEV), Sorento (excludes HEV/PHEV), and Telluride are assembled in the United States from U.S. and globally sourced parts.
- Kia Georgia, Inc. is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Corporation based in Seoul, Korea.
- Kia Georgia has an annual production capacity of 350,000 units.
- Kia Georgia is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Ga.
- Kia Georgia began mass production on Nov. 16, 2009.
- Kia Georgia manufactures the Telluride SUV, Sorento SUV (excludes HEV and PHEV models), Sportage SUV (excludes HEV and PHEV models), and the all-electric EV9 SUV.
- “Kia is committed to supporting our customers as well as the local communities, our friends and neighbors throughout the Southeast affected by Hurricane Helene, and we are grateful to have tremendous partners like the American Red Cross who are ready to quickly assist communities and provide a comforting hand in times of need,” said SeungKyu (Sean) Yoon.
- “Our team is so appreciative for Kia’s generous contribution, which allows the Red Cross and its partners to provide support to those with the greatest need following Hurricane Helene,” said Alicia Doherty.
