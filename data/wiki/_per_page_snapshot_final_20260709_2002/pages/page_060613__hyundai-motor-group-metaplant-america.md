# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-03

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America opens | Autocar Professional](https://www.autocarpro.in/news-international/hyundai-opens-its-us-factory-of-the-future-125589)  ·  `page_060613`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America is Hyundai Motor Group's third manufacturing plant in the US.
- The facility serves as the key pillar of Hyundai Motor Group’s $12.6 billion investment in Georgia.
- HMGMA will produce up to 500,000 electric and hybrid vehicles annually for the Hyundai, Kia, and Genesis brands.
- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America hosted a grand opening celebration on October 3, 2024.
- The ceremony marks the completion of the largest economic development project in Georgia’s history.
- Groundbreaking for the facility occurred two and a half years prior to the October 3, 2024 opening.
- Euisun Chung, Executive Chair of Hyundai Motor Group, stated that HMGMA represents the Group’s advanced manufacturing capabilities, commitment to innovation, and investment in relationships with partners and communities in Georgia.
- Euisun Chung also noted that together with the talented workforce at HMGMA, they are building the future of mobility with America, in America.
- The first vehicle rolled off the assembly line on October 3, 2024, less than two years after breaking ground.
- The first vehicle produced was a Hyundai Ioniq 5.
- The Hyundai Ioniq 9 three-row electric SUV is also now in production at HMGMA.
- The first Kia model to be built at the facility will join production in 2026.
- In addition to EVs, the plant is equipped to produce hybrid vehicles.
- HMGMA initially has the capacity to produce 300,000 vehicles annually.
- Hyundai Motor Group's total investment in Georgia related to HMGMA and joint battery ventures with LG Energy Solution and SK On is $12.6 billion.
- This $12.6 billion investment represents the largest single investment in Georgia's history.
- The investment includes a total of 8,500 jobs at HMGMA by 2031.
- Hyundai Motor Group’s total investments in Georgia are expected to create nearly 40,000 direct and indirect jobs.
- These total investments are expected to generate $4.6 billion in individual earnings every year, according to the Center for Automotive Research.
- As of July 2024, more than $2.5 billion in capital investment has been announced by HMGMA suppliers across 12 counties in Georgia.
- Approximately 6,900 jobs have been announced by HMGMA suppliers as of July 2024.
- The Korean auto major has invested more than $20.5 billion in the U.S. to date.
- Earlier in the week of the article's publication, the Group announced a significant investment of $21 billion in the U.S. from 2025 to 2028.
- Of the $21 billion announcement, $9 billion will be invested to establish an annual production capacity in the U.S. of 1.2 million vehicles.
- The Group plans to expand HMGMA's annual production capacity to a total of 500,000 units, an increase of 200,000 units from the original plan.
- Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama will see improvements in its production facilities.
- Kia Autoland Georgia will also see improvements in its production facilities.
