# Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy Solution Battery Cell Manufacturing Joint Venture

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-09-06

**Source:** [Hyundai and LG Energy to invest additional $2B in Georgia](https://evertiq.com/news/54300)  ·  `page_056178`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy Solution (LGES) will invest an additional USD 2 billion in their battery cell manufacturing joint venture at the Metaplant in Bryan County, Georgia.
- This additional investment raises the JV’s total investment value to more than USD 4.3 billion.
- The expansion is set to create another 400 new jobs.
- Back in May 2023, Hyundai Motor Group and LGES signed a memorandum of understanding establishing LGES as the partner for Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America’s (HMGMA) onsite battery cell manufacturing JV.
- With today’s announcement, the EV manufacturing facility and the battery JV represent an estimated USD 7.59 billion in investment.
- The combined projects will create 8,500 new jobs for the region over the next eight years.
- The 30 GWh facility will be able to support the production of 300,000 units of EVs annually at full operations.
- Hyundai Mobis will assemble battery packs using cells from the plant.
- The assembled battery packs will be supplied to the Hyundai Motor Group’s U.S. manufacturing facilities for the production of Hyundai, Kia, and Genesis EV models.
