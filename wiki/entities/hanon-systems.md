# Hanon Systems

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[thermal-management|Thermal Management]]  ·  **Location:** Gateway Regional Industrial Park, Bulloch County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Global provider of automotive thermal and energy management solutions that announced a $40 million investment to build a new manufacturing facility in Bulloch County, creating 160 new jobs and supporting Georgia’s growing EV and e-mobility ecosystem.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Supplier of thermal and energy management systems for electrified vehicles, contributing to Georgia’s EV supply chain and supporting the state’s goal to become the EV capital of America.
