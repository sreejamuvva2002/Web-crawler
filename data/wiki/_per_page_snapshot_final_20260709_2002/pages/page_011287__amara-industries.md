# Amara Industries

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-12-09

**Source:** [Amara Industries Acquires Thomson Plastics | The AI Journal](https://aijourn.com/amara-industries-acquires-thomson-plastics/)  ·  `page_011287`

## Facts

- Amara Industries, a parent company to the Gracious Living group of companies, announced the acquisition of Thomson Plastics on Dec. 9, 2025.
- Thomson Plastics is a recognized leader in large tonnage injection molding in the Southeastern United States.
- Thomson Plastics currently operates from two locations in Georgia and North Carolina.
- The acquisition enhances Amara Industries’ manufacturing footprint, automation capabilities, and technical depth to a total of five manufacturing facilities.
- Thomson Plastics has clamping force capacities up to 3,700 tons, with 30% of its fleet exceeding 1,000 tons.
- Thomson Plastics is supported by advanced robotics and automation to deliver consistent performance and efficiency across high-volume production environments.
- Thomson Plastics' technical portfolio includes injection molding, gas-assisted molding, over-molding, insert molding, in-mold labeling, robotic part picking, pad printing, and heat transfer decorating.
- Vince Orlando, Co-President at Amara Industries, stated that the addition of Thomson Plastics represents a strategic milestone for the organization.
- Vince Orlando noted that the advanced molding capabilities, technical expertise, and culture of innovation will enhance their ability to serve customers with scale, speed, and precision across North America.
- The unified company will meet rising demand for high-performance molded products in key sectors including automotive, consumer goods, industrial, and commercial markets.
- The expansion into the Southeastern United States strengthens Amara Industries' ability to provide customers with faster delivery, greater flexibility, and improved supply chain efficiency.
- Amara Industries has a dedicated team of over 500 employees.
- Amara Industries offers end-to-end capabilities spanning design, product development, engineering, and materials science.
- Amara Industries specializes in injection molding, assembly, profile extrusion, decorating, and material compounding.
- Amara Industries holds key certifications including ISO, IATF, and CTPAT.
