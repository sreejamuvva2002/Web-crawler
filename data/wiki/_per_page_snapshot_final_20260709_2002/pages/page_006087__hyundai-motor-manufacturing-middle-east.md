# Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Middle East

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [PIF’s joint venture with Hyundai Motor Company, Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Middle East, celebrates groundbreaking milestone](https://www.pif.gov.sa/en/news-and-insights/newswire/2025/pifs-joint-venture-with-hyundai-motor-company-hyundai-motor-manufacturing-middle-east/)  ·  `page_006087`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Middle East (HMMME) is a joint venture between PIF, which owns a 70% stake, and Hyundai Motor Company, which holds the remaining 30%.
- The new facility celebrated its groundbreaking ceremony in the King Salman Automotive Cluster within King Abdullah Economic City (KAEC).
- The plant will manufacture internal combustion engine (ICE) and electric vehicles (EV).
- The manufacturing plant targets an annual production of 50,000 vehicles to supply Saudi Arabia.
- This facility represents Hyundai's first manufacturing plant in the Middle East.
- The plant is scheduled to roll out its first vehicle by the fourth quarter of 2026.
- Yazeed A. Alhumied, Deputy Governor and Head of MENA Investments at PIF, stated that the groundbreaking strengthens Saudi Arabia's automotive industry and underscores PIF's commitment to building local capabilities, attracting cutting-edge technology, and creating highly skilled jobs in the automotive and mobility sector.
- Jaehoon Chang, Hyundai Motor Group Vice Chair, said the groundbreaking marks a new chapter for future mobility and technological innovation, aiming to contribute to regional talent development under Saudi Vision 2030.
- Wongyun Park, Vice President and CEO of HMMME, stated that the facility will become a platform for growth and industrial excellence in the Kingdom.
- The manufacturing plant will create thousands of jobs and facilitate knowledge transfer and skills development by harnessing local workforce skills.
- The localization of Hyundai's vehicles will accelerate the growth of Saudi Arabia's automotive and mobility ecosystem.
- This partnership is part of a series of PIF initiatives establishing Saudi Arabia as a global automotive player, driving transformation in the sector and boosting domestic manufacturing capabilities, infrastructure, and supply chains.
