# Big Tex Trailers

## Facts

- Big Tex Trailers is a utility trailer manufacturing company with manufacturing facilities in Odessa and Mount Pleasant, Texas.
- The company operates a trailer manufacturing plant and truck bed plant in Madill, Oklahoma.
- Big Tex maintains a network of over 500 trailer dealers across the US, Canada, and Mexico.
- The company operates trailer parts distribution centers in Mount Pleasant, Odessa, Madill, and Ocala, Florida.
- Big Tex owns 13 sales and service locations in California, Arizona, Texas, Louisiana, and Arkansas.
- Big Tex is in the final stages of purchasing a 175,000-square-foot facility and 55 acres on Interstate 75 in Cordele, Georgia.
- The Cordele, Georgia facility will be used to manufacture trailers and distribute products to dealers in the eastern US.
- The never-occupied, like-new Cordele facility includes two buildings: one dedicated to manufacturing trailers and another that will become the fifth Tex Trail distribution facility.
- Big Tex acquired CM Trailer Mfg and its subsidiaries, CM Truck Beds and S&H Trailers, from Ronald and Kay Jackson in 2007.
- Between 1988 and 1990, Big Tex acquired a 100,000-square-foot building on Interstate 20 between Odessa and Midland for manufacturing.
- Big Tex purchased a second 40,000-square-foot manufacturing facility on Business I-20 to expand into the heavy-duty equipment trailer market.
- In 1993, Big Tex acquired an adjacent facility to create Big Tex Sales and Service.
- Big Tex became the first utility trailer manufacturer to join the National Association of Trailer Manufacturers (NATM) in 1993.
- Land was purchased in Mount Pleasant in 1994, with construction starting and the first trailer produced in March 1995.
- Between 1997 and 1999, Big Tex acquired Rheas Trailer Sales of Mount Pleasant and expanded Tex Trail Trailer Parts to Mount Pleasant.
- In 2001, a new 15,000-square-foot Tex Trail Trailer Parts building was constructed in Odessa.
- A few years after 2001, Big Tex purchased a local fabrication company and renamed it Big Tex Fabrication.
- Big Tex acquired 30 acres adjacent to its property, bringing the total land ownership to 93 acres.
- The new corporate headquarters in Mount Pleasant is 51,500 square feet.
- The new headquarters building features three phases: loading and shipping with drive-through bays that triple shipping capacity, operational offices for dealer sales and order entry, and front-section corporate and administrative offices.
- In October, Tex Trail opened its third location in Ocala, Florida.
- Tex Trail is scheduled to open its fourth distribution location in Madill, Oklahoma on January 1.
