## Facts

- Georgia has international trade, investment, and tourism representatives in more than 12 strategic markets located throughout Europe, the Middle East, Asia, and the Americas.
- The state’s international representatives assist Georgia companies with market intelligence, customized export assistance, and guidance on local business practices.
- The state’s international representatives assist Georgia companies with locating and pre-qualifying appropriate partners and customers.
- Georgia’s international representatives also assist foreign companies looking to establish business or manufacturing operations in Georgia.
- Global Country Connections highlighting Georgia's relationships with select countries and territories are updated on an annual basis.
