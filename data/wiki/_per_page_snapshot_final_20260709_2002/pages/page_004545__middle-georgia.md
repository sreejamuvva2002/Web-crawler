## Facts

- Middle Georgia sits at the center of the state, where a rich cultural legacy meets manufacturing, agriculture, and military service.
- The region’s diverse economy spans manufacturing, agriculture, and military operations anchored by Robins Air Force Base.
- Communities like Warner Robins, Milledgeville, and Fort Valley support aerospace, agriculture, and food processing across the region.
- Purdue Farms operates a food processing facility in Middle Georgia.
- Irving Tissue has invested in Macon, with President Robert Irving stating: 'We greatly appreciate the State of Georgia’s commitment to economic prosperity, jobs, and growth. The team’s drive to promote the state’s advantages, while fostering a positive business environment, helped make this investment possible. We are proud to continue to invest in the State of Georgia and truly appreciate the commitment to helping us expand and grow in Macon.'
- The Middle Georgia region comprises 11 counties: Baldwin, Bibb, Crawford, Houston, Jones, Monroe, Peach, Pulaski, Putnam, Twiggs, and Wilkinson.
- Ocmulgee Mounds National Historical Park in the region has been proposed as Georgia’s first national park.
- The region hosts cultural events including Macon’s International Cherry Blossom Festival and the Georgia National Fair in Perry.
