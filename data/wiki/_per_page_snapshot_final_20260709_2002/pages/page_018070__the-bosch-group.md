# The Bosch Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-12-31

**Source:** [Bosch announces electric motor production in Charleston and more than $260 million in new investment  - Bosch Media Service US](https://us.bosch-press.com/pressportal/us/en/press-release-19392.html)  ·  `page_018070`

## Facts

- As of December 31, 2021, the Bosch Group employs nearly 35,500 associates in more than 100 locations in North America.
- In 2021, Bosch generated consolidated sales of $13.5 billion in the U.S., Canada and Mexico.
- The company employs roughly 402,600 associates worldwide as of December 31, 2021.
- Bosch generated sales of $93.1 billion in 2021.
- Operations are divided into four business sectors: Mobility Solutions, Industrial Technology, Consumer Goods, and Energy and Building Technology.
- As a leading IoT provider, Bosch offers innovative solutions for smart homes, Industry 4.0, and connected mobility.
- Bosch uses its expertise in sensor technology, software, and services to offer customers connected, cross-domain solutions from a single source.
- The company's strategic objective is to facilitate connected living with products and solutions that either contain artificial intelligence (AI) or have been developed or manufactured with its help.
- The Bosch Group comprises Robert Bosch GmbH and its roughly 440 subsidiary and regional companies in some 60 countries.
- Including sales and service partners, the global manufacturing, engineering, and sales network covers nearly every country in the world.
- With more than 400 locations worldwide, the company has been carbon neutral since the first quarter of 2020.
- At 128 locations across the globe, Bosch employs some 76,100 associates in research and development, of which more than 38,000 are software engineers.
