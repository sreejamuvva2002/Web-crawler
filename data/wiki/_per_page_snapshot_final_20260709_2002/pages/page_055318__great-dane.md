# Great Dane

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2012-04-04

**Source:** [Great Dane Expanding Corporate Offices - TT](https://www.ttnews.com/articles/great-dane-expanding-corporate-offices)  ·  `page_055318`

## Facts

- Great Dane officially opened its ninth trailer manufacturing facility in nearby Statesboro, Ga.
- A ceremony marked the start of construction on a research and development center, a road simulator for testing trailers, and a customer-care facility.
- CEO Bill Crown stated that the new facility will “allow us to map the future of our entire company.”
- The project includes the renovation of Great Dane’s existing corporate office building adjacent to the Port of Savannah.
- The expansion will allow the company to consolidate a number of functions currently housed in separate buildings.
- Employment is expected to grow from the current 130 to about 180 when construction is completed next year.
