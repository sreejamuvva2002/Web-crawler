# Textron Specialized Vehicles

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-23

**Source:** [Textron Specialized Vehicles Recalls Arctic Cat Alterra 600 and Tracker 600 All-Terrain Vehicles (ATVs) Due to Crash Hazard - Motor Sports NewsWire](https://motorsportsnewswire.com/2023/03/23/textron-specialized-vehicles-recalls-arctic-cat-alterra-600-and-tracker-600-all-terrain-vehicles-atvs-due-to-crash-hazard/)  ·  `page_051736`

## Facts

- Textron Specialized Vehicles, the manufacturer of the recalled ATVs, is located in Augusta, Georgia.
- The recall involves model years 2022 and 2023 Arctic Cat Alterra 600 and Tracker 600 all-terrain vehicles (ATVs).
- Approximately 3,800 units were sold in the United States, with an additional 1,200 sold in Canada.
- The recalled ATVs were manufactured in the United States.
- Arctic Cat Alterra 600 ATVs were sold at dealers nationwide from May 2021 through January 2023 for between $9,200 and $11,600.
- Tracker 600 ATVs were sold at Bass Pro Shops, Cabela’s, and Tracker dealers nationwide from May 2021 through January 2023 for between $7,700 and $9,900.
- The vehicles pose a crash hazard because the A-arm can separate from the frame, causing loss of steering control.
- Consumers are instructed to immediately stop using the recalled ATVs and contact Textron Specialized Vehicles to arrange for a free inspection and repair.
- Dealers will inspect the main frame A-arm mount brackets for cracks before adding a support bracket, and replace the main frame weldment along with strengthening brackets if cracking is found.
- The recall was announced on March 23, 2023, under recall number +23-739.
- Arctic Cat and Tracker ATVs were previously recalled in July 2022.
- No incidents or injuries have been reported related to this recall.
- Recalled VIN ranges for 2022 and 2023 Arctic Cat Alterra 600 models include NT200101 to NT202064 and PT200101 to PT200679.
- Recalled VIN ranges for 2022 and 2023 Tracker 600 models include NT200101 to NT201929 and PT200101 to PT200697.
- The Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) is located on the front right-hand frame tube beside the right-hand shock.
