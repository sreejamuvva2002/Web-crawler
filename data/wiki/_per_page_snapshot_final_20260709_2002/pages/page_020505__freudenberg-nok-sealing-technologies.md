# Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-01

**Source:** [Freudenberg NOK Archives - Transmission Digest](https://www.transmissiondigest.com/tag/freudenberg-nok/)  ·  `page_020505`

## Facts

- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies announced that it has acquired Alto Products Corp.
- Atmore, Alabama-based Alto is a manufacturer of clutch plates and automatic transmission parts with a 270,000-square-foot manufacturing facility.
- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies announced an update to the TransTec 68RFE overhaul kit.
- Effective immediately, all kits for Chrysler 68RFE transmissions (2019 and newer) will now include valve body gaskets.
- TransTec says including valve body gaskets is “a critical component for a complete and efficient rebuild.”
- Transmission Digest met with managers and executives at the Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies packaging facility located in Milan, Ohio.
- The Milan, Ohio facility is the home of the well-known TransTec brand of kits and related products.
- The Corteco brand is woven into the product offerings from the company.
- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies officially announced that it has acquired Toledo Driveline, LLC as part of its recent agreement to purchase Seal Aftermarket Products (SAP).
- Toledo Driveline provides miscellaneous transmission hard parts and electronics, such as drums, planetaries, and solenoids.
- Toledo Driveline will be integrated into Freudenberg Sealing Technologies’ Corteco Division.
- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies announced that it has acquired Seal Aftermarket Products (SAP), a Florida-based aftermarket company that sells automotive transmission kits and components.
- SAP will be integrated into Freudenberg Sealing Technologies’ Corteco Division.
- SAP sells a comprehensive line of automatic transmission rebuild kits, hard parts, components, electronic products, and specialty tools.
- Dr. Christian Dickopf is the senior vice president of the Corteco Division at Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies.
- Dr. Christian Dickopf has more than 18 years of experience at Freudenberg Group, Corteco’s parent company.
