# Foresight Autonomous Holdings Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-06-27

**Source:** [page_002569](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1691221/000121390022035035/ea162091ex99-1_foresightauto.htm)  ·  `page_002569`

## Facts

- Foresight Autonomous Holdings Ltd. (Nasdaq and TASE: FRSX) announced the signing of an agreement with Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc. to test and evaluate autonomous-driving vision systems (ADAS).
- Hitachi Astemo is a global Tier One supplier of engine management, electric powertrain and integrated vehicle-control systems for the auto industry.
- The paid joint proof of concept project with Hitachi Astemo calls for the evaluation and testing of simulated and real-life autonomous driving scenarios.
- The program will pair Foresight QuadSight® and Mono2Stereo™ technologies with existing Hitachi Astemo camera systems to improve distance measurement and object detection.
- The project follows the successful evaluation of a QuadSight vision system prototype reported by the Company in March 2021.
- Foresight’s proprietary Mono2Stereo software-based solution uses overlapping views of existing cameras with different fields of vision to create a three-dimensional stereo-vision system to improve object detection.
- Haim Siboni, CEO of Foresight, stated that being chosen by Hitachi Astemo underlines the importance of stereoscopic technology in enhancing existing ADAS systems and its ability to amplify performance, provide better distance accuracy, and offer more robust active safety features.
- Foresight's solutions can be deployed in vehicles equipped with Level 2 and Level 2-plus autonomy systems without requiring additional hardware and design changes.
- Siboni believes teaming up with Hitachi Astemo could potentially lead to further collaboration with other automotive OEMs.
- Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc. manufactures and markets engine management, electric powertrain, and integrated vehicle control for major automotive manufacturers worldwide, adhering to ISO/TS:16949:2009 industry standards.
- Foresight Autonomous Holdings Ltd. is a technology company developing smart multi-spectral vision software solutions and cellular-based applications.
- Through wholly owned subsidiaries including Foresight Automotive Ltd., Foresight Changzhou Automotive Ltd., and Eye-Net Mobile Ltd., Foresight develops in-line-of-sight vision systems and beyond-line-of-sight accident-prevention solutions.
- Foresight’s vision solutions include modules of automatic calibration and dense three-dimensional point cloud applicable to automotive, defense, autonomous vehicles, and heavy industrial equipment markets.
- Eye-Net Mobile’s cellular-based solution provides real-time pre-collision alerts to enhance road safety and situational awareness for urban mobility by incorporating AI technology and advanced analytics.
- The press release contains forward-looking statements regarding the agreement, testing of simulated and real-life autonomous driving scenarios, and potential future collaborations with other automotive OEMs.
