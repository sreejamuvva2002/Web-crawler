# Midstate Georgia Largest Employers List

**Type:** workforce_training  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** 2014-10-03

## Facts

- Robins Air Force Base in Warner Robins employs more than 23,500 people and has an annual federal payroll of $1.39 billion, serving as the economic engine driving Middle Georgia.
- Medical Center (Navicent Health) is a top midstate employer with 6,000 employees.
- Geico employs 5,600 workers in the midstate region.
- Houston County Board of Education has 3,793 employees.
- Bibb County Board of Education has 3,700 employees.
- Houston Healthcare employs 2,449 people.
- Perdue Farms employs 2,100 workers in the midstate.
- Macon-Bibb County government employs 1,900 people.
- Blue Bird Corp. employs 1,500 workers in the region.
- Coliseum Health System has 1,400 employees.
- Frito-Lay employs 1,338 workers in the midstate.
- Mercer University has 900 employees.
- Triumph Group Inc. employs 850 people in the region.
- Georgia College has 850 employees.
- Baldwin County Board of Education employs 830 workers.
- Bibb County government has 780 employees.
- Wal-Mart stores in Bibb County employ 740 people.
- Academy Sports + Outdoors distribution center employs 700 workers.
- Fort Valley State University has 615 employees.
- Houston County government employs 626 people.
- Monroe County Board of Education has 612 employees.
- YKK (USA) employs 600 workers in the midstate.
- United States Postal Service employs 600 people in the region.
- Ricoh USA has 575 employees.
- Georgia Farm Bureau and Oconee Regional Medical Center each employ 525 people.
