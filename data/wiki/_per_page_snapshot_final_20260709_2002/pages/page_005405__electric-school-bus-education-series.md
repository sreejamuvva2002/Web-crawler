## Facts

- Educational webinars and handouts provide information about the benefits of electric school buses and examples of their use.
- The resource series is titled "Flipping the Switch on Electric School Buses".
- A step-by-step guide to the school bus electrification process is available in the Electric School Bus Planning Guide.
- A technical assistance video series targets K-12 school districts interested in implementing electric school buses.
- The series begins with an introduction to Clean Cities and Communities coalitions and discusses how they can provide education and technical assistance throughout implementation.
- The introductory module covers key decision factors, charging infrastructure, and vehicle availability for electric school buses.

- Another module covers electric bus basics, key benefits and challenges, charging infrastructure basics, and vehicle availability.
- Part 2 of the series focuses on working with electric utilities, providing guidance on starting conversations with utilities and assessing power needs for future charging infrastructure.
- Associated handouts for Part 2 cover understanding various utility rate structures.
- A webinar module discusses how to work with utility partners and incorporate power need considerations into electrification plans.
- Another webinar module covers utility rate structures and how EV charging impacts monthly bills.
- Part 3 focuses on vehicle requirements, covering currently available electric school bus models and cost factors.
- Guidance is provided on analyzing vehicle routes to determine if electrification is a good option for a fleet.
- The series covers vehicle range and efficiency considerations.

- The Clean School Bus Program under the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (IIJA) provides $5 billion over 5 years for electric and alternative fuel buses.
- Each year, $500 million will be provided exclusively for EV buses and $500 million for EV and other alternative fuel buses.
- The IIJA also provides up to $300 million for a new Joint Office of Energy and Transportation to provide and coordinate technical assistance to states and communities.
- In partnership with the National Laboratory of the Rockies and the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency, the Joint Office is offering technical assistance on electric school bus deployment topics.
- Technical assistance covers electric bus basics, charging equipment, utility connections, bus performance, and operational considerations like routing and maintenance.
