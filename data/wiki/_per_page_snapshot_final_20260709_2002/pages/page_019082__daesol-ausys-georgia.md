# Daesol Ausys Georgia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-08-29

**Source:** [Gov. Kemp announces new manufacturing facility to be opened in West Point](https://www.wtvm.com/2023/08/29/gov-kemp-announces-new-manufacturing-facility-be-opened-west-point/)  ·  `page_019082`

## Facts

- An automotive supplier, Daesol Ausys Georgia, is investing $72 million in Harris County.
- The supplier is establishing a new manufacturing facility in West Point, creating more than 140 job opportunities for residents.
- The Daesol Ausys company was founded in Korea in 2017.
- The company designs and manufactures motor and electric vehicle interior components.
- The facility coming to West Point, Georgia will produce luggage boards and covers.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated in a press release that West Point has become an automotive capital in its own right and expressed excitement that Daesol Ausys will join other innovators in Harris County.
- Operations for the facility are planned to start in December 2024.
- Positions will be open in production, purchasing, quality control, maintenance, human resources, and management.
- Craig Greeenhaw, Chairman of the Harris County Development Authority, stated in a press release that they extend a gracious welcome to Daesol Ausys and look forward to a long and mutually beneficial partnership with the company.
- Greeenhaw noted that West Point and Harris County continue to work well together and that the venture brings jobs and capital investment into the region.
