## Facts

- Pirelli will begin production of its Cyber Tyre at its Georgia facility, with further details expected in the coming months.
- The company’s management is set to provide an update on this development during its first quarter 2026 results on Thursday.
- Pirelli’s only production facility in Georgia, located in Rome, is relatively small and currently satisfies approximately 5% of the company’s US exposure.
- While Cyber Tyre represents a small volume product, the commencement of production indicates that the US Department of Commerce is now more comfortable with Pirelli undertaking capital expenditure in the US and producing this technology-rich product.
- Citi analysts view this development as long-term positive for Pirelli, suggesting it may indicate improved ability for the company to undertake US factory expansion following recent governance changes.
- The analysts believe any such capacity expansion can be funded with existing capital expenditure budgets, enabling a more local manufacturing footprint over time.
