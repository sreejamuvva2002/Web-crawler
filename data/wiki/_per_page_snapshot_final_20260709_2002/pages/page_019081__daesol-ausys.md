# Daesol Ausys

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-08-29

**Source:** [Automotive Supplier Announces $72 Million Investment in Georgia | The Star News Network](http://thestarnewsnetwork.com/2023/08/30/automotive-supplier-announces-72-million-investment-in-georgia/)  ·  `page_019081`

## Facts

- Daesol Ausys is a South Korean automotive supplier that specializes in designing and manufacturing motor and electric vehicle interior components and accessories.
- The company announced a $72 million investment to establish a new manufacturing facility in Harris County, Georgia.
- Daesol Ausys serves as a key supplier for Kia Georgia, Hyundai Motor Group, and General Motors.
- The new facility will be located in West Point, Georgia, specifically within the Northwest Harris Business Park.
- The facility will specifically produce luggage boards and covers.
- The project is expected to create more than 140 new jobs in Harris County.
- Min Ho Kwon serves as the CEO of Daesol Ausys.
- New operations at the West Point facility are expected to begin in December 2024.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated: “West Point has become an automotive capital in its own right, and we are excited that Daesol Ausys will join other innovators in Harris County.”
- Governor Kemp noted that Georgia’s automotive industry employs over 50,000 hardworking Georgians and will continue to grow as e-mobility suppliers choose all corners of the state.
- South Korea was one of the top three international sources of job creation in Georgia during Fiscal Year 2023.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated: “Economic development is a team sport that is driven by the local level. Congratulations to Harris County and our community partners for helping bring Daesol Ausys to West Georgia.”
- Commissioner Wilson added: “The automotive industry is providing generational change in the region and across the state. We’re excited to see the continuation of this growth and look forward to the opportunities that Daesol Ausys will provide to the people of Harris County.”
