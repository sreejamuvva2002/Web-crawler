# MOBIS North America Electrified Powertrain, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-13

**Source:** [MOBIS North America Electrified Powertrain, LLC: Named Georgia Developers Association’s 2023 Deal of the Year for Mid-Size Communities - Richmond Hill Lifestyle](https://livingrichmondhillga.com/mobis-north-america-electrified-powertrain-llc-named-georgia-developers-associations-2023-deal-of-the-year-for-mid-size-communities/)  ·  `page_046825`

## Facts

- On November 13, 2023, MOBIS North America Electrified Powertrain, LLC received the Georgia Economic Developers Association (GEDA) Deal of the Year Award in the Mid-Size Community category.
- The GEDA award recognizes outstanding achievement in locating or expanding new businesses in Georgia that meet community economic development goals and involve significant community collaboration.
- MOBIS was recognized alongside community leaders from the Development Authority of Bryan County (DABC), City of Richmond Hill, Bryan County, Raydient Places, and the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD).
- GEDA is a non-profit association of professionals and volunteers involved in economic development, organized to increase effectiveness, encourage cooperation and information exchange, upgrade professional skills, and foster closer working relationships among Georgia economic development practitioners.
- Jon Seagraves, DABC Chairman, stated that recruiting MOBIS to Belfast Commerce Park at Heartwood was a team effort and thanked Mr. Park and Mr. Lee with MOBIS for being instrumental in the company's decision to choose Bryan County.
- Hyundai MOBIS is a globally recognized South Korean automotive parts maker founded as Hyundai Precision & Industries Corporation in 1977.
- The company forms the "parts and services" arm for South Korean automakers Hyundai Motor Company, Genesis Motors, and Kia Motors.
- MOBIS's business scope encompasses key automotive parts, specifically e-powertrain systems including Battery Systems and Power Electric Systems.
- MOBIS produces three major modules: Chassis Modules, Cockpit Modules, and Front-end Modules, along with aftermarket sales and service.
- The Development Authority of Bryan County (DABC) has a mission to recruit and retain industry in Bryan County to create jobs and investments.
- Since 2019, DABC has assisted with the location of projects representing over 5,000 new jobs and over $1.7 billion in capital investment.
- The Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) project represents 8,500 jobs and $7.59 billion in capital investment, making it the largest economic development project in Georgia state history.
- The HMGMA project was recruited to Bryan County by the Savannah Harbor I-16 Corridor Joint Development Authority, of which Bryan County is a member.
