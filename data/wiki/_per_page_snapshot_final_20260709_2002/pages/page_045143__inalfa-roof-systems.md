# Inalfa Roof Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2014-02-27

**Source:** [Inalfa Roof Systems Expanding in Cherokee County | Georgia Public Broadcasting](https://www.gpb.org/blogs/georgia-works/2014/02/27/inalfa-roof-systems-expanding-in-cherokee-county)  ·  `page_045143`

## Facts

- Inalfa Roof Systems is expanding its recently completed 165,000-square-foot regional headquarters in southwest Cherokee County by an additional 45,000 square feet.
- Governor Nathan Deal announced the expansion of the Cherokee County facility.
- The company will invest an additional $6 million for the expansion project.
- The expansion will create 100 new jobs.
- The 100 new jobs are in addition to 300 jobs already announced in June 2013.
- The anticipated completion date for the expansion is scheduled for September.
- Governor Nathan Deal stated that Cherokee County is an ideal location for Inalfa Roof Systems due to its significant expansion.
- Governor Deal noted that Inalfa Roof Systems has proven to be a true team player for economic development in the region during its relatively short time in Georgia.
- Governor Deal said the expansion demonstrates the company's confidence in Georgia's No. 1 business climate.
- Inalfa Roof Systems develops and manufactures vehicle roof systems worldwide.
