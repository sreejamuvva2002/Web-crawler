## Facts

- WIKA Instrument Co. has a new 110,000 square foot manufacturing facility located just outside of Atlanta in Lawrenceville, Georgia.
- The Lawrenceville facility features state-of-the-art equipment and manufacturing techniques.
- The company uses the latest technology to test and monitor each production process every step of the way to ensure products meet the highest quality standards.
- WIKA's parent company is WIKA Alexander Wiegand GmbH & Co., located near Frankfurt, Germany.
- The name WIKA is taken from the first two letters of the founders of the company, Alexander Wiegand and Philipp Kachel.
- In 1996, WIKA will celebrate 50 years in Europe and 30 years in the United States.
- WIKA employs over 2500 people worldwide.
- WIKA has additional manufacturing plants in Germany, Brazil, South Africa, and Switzerland.
- WIKA is currently producing over 24 million instruments per year.
- WIKA manufactures pressure gauges, chemical seals, transmitters, and thermometers for customers in more than 100 countries worldwide.
