## Facts

- Sewon America Rincon Effingham (S.A.R.E.) is located in Rincon, GA.
- Sewon America operates the largest automotive body parts manufacturing plant in North America.
- The facility delivers high-quality products through cutting-edge automated manufacturing processes and efficient logistics.
- In 2023, Sewon America broke ground on a new state-of-the-art automotive manufacturing plant in Rincon, Georgia.
- The new Rincon facility is set to begin production in 2024.
- This facility will be Sewon America's second plant in the United States.
- Once fully operational, the Rincon plant will have created 740 new jobs.
- The company is actively recruiting applicants to join its team in Rincon, GA.
