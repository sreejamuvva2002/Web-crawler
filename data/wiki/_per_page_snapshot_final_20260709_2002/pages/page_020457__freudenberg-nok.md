## Facts

- Freudenberg-NOK is an American joint venture partnership between Germany's Freudenberg & Company and Japan's NOK Corporation.
- The company is one of the world's largest producers of elastomeric seals and custom molded products.
- Its product portfolio includes bonded piston seals, brakes, fluid power products, gaskets, O-rings, radial shaft seals, and vibration control devices.
- These products are sold to the aerospace, automotive sealing, health care, appliance, industrial equipment, and chemical processing markets.
- Products are distributed under the brand names Flexitech, TransTec, Omega, Simrit, and Vibracoustic.
- Freudenberg-NOK employs nearly 6,000 associates across 39 facilities in the United States, Canada, Mexico, and Brazil.
- The company operates one facility in LaGrange, Georgia.
- The LaGrange facility is located at 1618 Lukken Industrial Dr W, Lagrange, GA 30240.
- The facility is situated near the intersection of Lukken Industrial Dr W and Airport Pkwy.
- Freudenberg-NOK was founded in 1989 and is headquartered in Plymouth, Michigan.
