# Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2018-07-11

**Source:** [Textron Specialized Vehicles Introduces Cushman 72-Volt AC Electric Shuttles - Inside Golf](https://insidegolf.ca/read/3734-textron-specialized-vehicles-introduces-cushman-72-volt-ac-electric-shuttles)  ·  `page_051663`

## Facts

- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a Textron Inc. company, announced its new line of Cushman Shuttle personnel carriers powered by the company's exclusive 72-volt AC electric drivetrain.
- The new product line includes the Cushman Shuttle 4, Shuttle 6, and Shuttle 8 models.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles is the leader in AC electric drivetrains in the golf-car industry under its E-Z-GO brand.
- The 72-volt AC drivetrain technology delivers improved energy efficiency, increased range, longer battery life, and remarkable hill-climbing capabilities compared to DC electric power.
- Cushman vehicles are designed and manufactured in Augusta, Georgia.
- Kevin Holleran, president and CEO of Textron Specialized Vehicles, stated that the new Shuttles leverage proven energy savings, power, and range benefits to move customers' employees and guests more efficiently and cost-effectively.
- Cushman Shuttle personnel carriers are commonly used for transporting people and cargo in applications including golf courses, resorts, airports, educational facilities, and college and commercial campuses.
- Standard features on the Cushman Shuttles include headlights, a brush guard, an hour meter, and a USB port.
- Optional equipment for the Shuttles includes a limited-slip differential, windshields, and canopies.
- The Cushman Shuttle 4 seats up to four people and includes a stake-side cargo bed for equipment, luggage, or personal belongings.
- The Cushman Shuttle 6 seats up to six people.
- The Cushman Shuttle 8 seats up to eight people.
- Since 1901, Cushman has provided light-transportation solutions for turf, commercial, and industrial applications.
- Cushman's product line includes more than 15 different vehicle models, ranging from rugged Hauler utility vehicles for outdoor hauling and towing to the fully electric Titan material-handling vehicle for indoor environments with a 3,000-pound load capacity.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. is a leading global manufacturer of golf cars, utility and personal transportation vehicles, snowmobiles, side-by-sides, all-terrain vehicles, professional turf-care equipment, and ground support equipment.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles markets products under the E-Z-GO, Cushman, Arctic Cat, Textron Off Road, Jacobsen, Dixie Chopper, Ransomes, TUG, Douglas, Premier, and Safeaero brands.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company that leverages a global network of aircraft, defense, industrial, and finance businesses to provide innovative solutions and services.
