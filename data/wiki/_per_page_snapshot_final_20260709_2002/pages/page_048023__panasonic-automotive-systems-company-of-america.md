# Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-10-04

**Source:** [Capgemini Group fuels collaboration with Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America through transformative new data platform - Sogeti Luxemburg](https://www.sogeti.lu/newsroom/capgemini-group-fuels-collaboration-with-panasonic-automotive-systems-company-of-america-through-transformative-new-data-platform/)  ·  `page_048023`

## Facts

- Capgemini Group announced the successful creation of a data ecosystem for longstanding client Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America on October 4, 2022.
- The new platform will improve the organization’s capabilities in data-driven decision making and innovation to drive efficiencies and more reliable extracts.
- Panasonic Automotive is a division of Panasonic Corporation of North America specializing in innovations that are revolutionizing the driving experience.
- Panasonic Automotive streamlined its data processes with the Microsoft Azure platform with Power BI for data visualization.
- The company built a centralized, stable foundation for its data team to promote innovation and capture more reliable results across the business.
- Scott Kirchner serves as President of Panasonic Automotive.
- Scott Kirchner stated that data enables people to see how their piece of the business is working, fostering more innovation and accountability regarding operational impact.
- Capgemini established an aggressive six-month timeline to migrate Panasonic Automotive’s existing data reporting platform to Microsoft Azure.
- The migration was built on a single-instance software system.
- The new data lake architecture solution utilizes data from across Panasonic Automotive’s business units to centralize their wealth of information.
- The centralized data foundation results in more reliable data extracts in a much shorter timeframe.
- The platform sets the organization up for future artificial intelligence and machine learning use cases.
- Venkat Padmanabhan serves as Account Executive for Panasonic at Capgemini.
- Venkat Padmanabhan stated that the collaboration utilizes Capgemini’s skills in data migration and automotive connectivity to support Panasonic Automotive’s dedication to passion, quality, and innovation.
- Since migrating to the data lake, the Panasonic Automotive Business Intelligence team spends less than 20% of their time on support-related tasks.
- The previous platform required approximately 50% of the Business Intelligence team's time for support-related tasks.
- The transformed ecosystem presents well-informed data in significantly less time with increased accuracy and reliability.
- Panasonic Automotive and Capgemini have worked together for more than five years.
- The long-term collaboration includes streamlining Panasonic Automotive’s applications with automation and ongoing support from Capgemini’s ADMnext application data management capabilities.
