# Kia Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-11-18

**Source:** [GDOL and Kia Georgia Will Host Virtual Recruitment on November 18th - Columbus CEO](https://thecolumbusceo.com/news/2021/11/gdol-and-kia-georgia-will-host-virtual-recruitment-november-18th/)  ·  `page_010123`

## Facts

- The Georgia Department of Labor will assist Kia Georgia, Inc. to host a virtual recruitment on November 18, 2021.
- The event will include general information sessions or scheduled interviews to fill approximately 550 Production Team Member positions.
- The virtual recruitment is scheduled for November 18, 2021, from 10 am until 12 pm and again from 2 pm until 4 pm.
- Applicants must be 18 years of age.
- A criminal background check will be conducted on applicants.
- Entry salary starts at $16.27 and will incrementally increase to $25.15 over a three-year period.
- Applicants can apply in advance to be considered for a scheduled virtual interview.
- Positions are full-time, permanent shifts with overtime required.
- Benefits include vacation/PTO and medical leave.
- Retirement benefits include 401K and profit sharing.
- Insurance coverage includes dental, health, life, and vision.
- Miscellaneous benefits include tuition, clothing, and uniforms.
