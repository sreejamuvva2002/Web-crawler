## Facts

- Stryten Energy expands its involvement in the Rebelle Rally as a new Gold Partner for 2026.
- Stryten Energy has been involved with the Rebelle Rally for several years through team entries and stage sponsorship prior to becoming an official partner.
- Stryten Energy is an energy storage solutions company specializing in battery technologies that support critical industries, including transportation, military, and industrial applications.
- In 2024 and 2025, Stryten Energy supported employees Jully Hirt and Amanda Elve by equipping their 2022 Jeep Gladiator with Enhanced Flooded Battery (EFB) technology.
- The EFB battery is designed for durability and reliability to withstand high heat and sustained demands.
- Mike Judd serves as the CEO and President of Stryten Energy.
- In 2026, two employee teams will compete in the Rebelle Rally, with additional teams already training for the 2027 event.
- Stryten Energy operates under five core values: Care, Challenge, Commit, Create, and Change.
- Stryten Energy is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Stryten Energy partners with recognized companies to meet the growing demand for reliable and sustainable energy storage capacity.
- Stryten Energy powers applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- Stryten Energy's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- Stryten Energy is backed by more than a century of expertise in the industry.
