## Facts

- A German car parts company is creating 200 jobs in Buford.
- The company is developing and producing automotive components for both diesel and gasoline engines.
- The company is hiring for production, quality, logistics, and maintenance roles.
- The new facility will be located in Gwinnett County.
- Local officials stated that global leaders have chosen to locate in Gwinnett County, offering new opportunities for the automotive industry base.
- A county commissioner noted that this is a great win for the county and expressed excitement to welcome the company as a new business.
- The company is excited about the economic development conditions arriving in Gwinnett County.
- Officials highlighted that German automotive manufacturers are growing in number, with several finding a home in Buford.
- Local leaders look forward to supporting the company's growth and continued success in the community.
