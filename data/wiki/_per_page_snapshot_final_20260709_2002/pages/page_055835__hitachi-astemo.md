# Hitachi Astemo

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hitachi Astemo planted its first solar power plant in India](https://currentaffairs.adda247.com/hitachi-astemo-planted-its-first-solar-power-plant-in-india/)  ·  `page_055835`

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo installed India’s first ground-mounted solar power plant of 3 megawatts (MW) at its Jalgaon manufacturing plant.
- The 3 MW solar power plant will be built in an area of 43,301 sqm.
- The ground-mounted solar power plant will consist of 7,128 ground-mounted solar panels and 10 inverters.
- Hitachi Astemo is known for the development, manufacture, sale, and service of automotive and transportation components.
- The solar power plant will embark on a new journey in the field of sustainable energy in India.
- Hitachi is working to reduce its carbon emissions and become carbon neutral by 2050.
- With this plant, the company would be able to eliminate close to 4,000 tons of carbon dioxide emissions each year.
- Eliminating this amount of CO2 will be equal to planting around 150,000 trees.
- The installation of the 3-megawatt solar power plant is just the beginning of the company’s plan.
- The company plans to commission an additional 1.5-megawatt solar power plant by March 2023.
- At its Jalgaon plant, the company produces brake systems for 3-wheelers and 4-wheelers.
- The Jalgaon plant manufacturing includes disc brakes and drum brakes.
- The Jalgaon plant is also engaged in foundry operations.
- Hitachi Astemo is a subsidiary of the Japanese firm Hitachi, established in 2021.
- The company is constantly working to provide efficient modifications in the automobile industry.
- Recently, Hitachi Astemo along with Hitachi developed thin-type inverter technology for EVs.
- The developed thin-type inverter technology makes the machine more compact as well as energy efficient.
