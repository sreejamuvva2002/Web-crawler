## Facts

- Hyundai Transys operates a GEORGIA P/T PLANT in the USA.
- The Georgia P/T plant is a legal entity producing Automatic Transmissions (AT).
- Hyundai Transys also has a USA GEORGIA SEAT PLANT.
- Hyundai Transys maintains a USA OFFICE.
- Hyundai Transys has 21 global manufacturing plants, 2 R&D centers, and 5 sales branches.
- The company's business network links bases across Europe, Asia, and America continents.
- The Hyundai Transys Powertrain R&D Center is located in Hwaseong Gyeonggi-do, Korea.
- The R&D Center develops core components of automobiles including transmissions, axles, 4WD systems and other advanced products.
- The R&D Center focuses on developing environment-friendly powertrains.
- Hyundai Transys has succeeded in developing powertrains for hybrid cars and next-generation vehicles such as electric/fuel cell vehicles.
- The R&D Center actively participates in projects involving the production of automatic transmissions for military tanks, and decelerators for high-speed rails and subway trains.
