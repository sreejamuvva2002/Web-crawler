# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-08-14

**Source:** [Owler Reports - Novelis: Novelis and DRT Collaborate to Advance Sustainability of Aluminum Beverage Can Ends](https://www.owler.com/reports/novelis--inc./novelis-and-drt-collaborate-to-advance-sustainabil/1755187741218)  ·  `page_047474`

## Facts

- Novelis and DRT Holdings entered into a joint development agreement to accelerate the adoption of high-recycled-content alloys in aluminum beverage can ends.
- The collaboration aims to improve the manufacturing process for sustainable can ends, ensuring seamless adoption across the global metal packaging industry.
- Novelis is a world leader in high-recycled-content, low-carbon flat rolled aluminum sheet products.
- Novelis currently has an average of approximately 85% recycled content in its beverage packaging globally.
- DRT is a leading provider of high-speed manufacturing equipment for beverage can ends, capable of producing up to 3,000 ends per minute.
- Novelis and DRT will work together to optimize the design, conversion process and sustainability attributes of beverage can ends made from the same alloy as the can body.
- The "uni-alloy" design solution enables beverage can-ends to be made from up to 99% recycled content.
- This uni-alloy design reduces the carbon footprint of can ends by more than half.
- Greg Schlicht serves as Senior Vice President and Chief Commercial Officer at Novelis.
- Greg Schlicht stated that Novelis is committed to accelerating the transition to a more circular aluminum beverage packaging industry and this partnership with DRT is a key step in that journey.
- Greg Schlicht noted that working with an industry leader in can end manufacturing will ensure beverage brands and can makers have solutions to accelerate uni-alloy adoption without compromising quality.
- The use of high-recycled-content aluminum alloys in can ends represents an important shift in material innovation, reducing carbon emissions, improving recyclability and strengthening the closed-loop recycling system.
- Novelis aims to be the leading provider of low-carbon, sustainable aluminum solutions and achieve a fully circular economy by partnering with suppliers and customers in aerospace, automotive, beverage can and specialties industries throughout North America, Europe, Asia and South America.
- Novelis had net sales of $17.1 billion in fiscal year 2025.
- Novelis is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited, an industry leader in aluminum and copper.
- Hindalco Industries Limited is the metals flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group, a multinational conglomerate based in Mumbai.
- DRT Holdings was founded in 1949 as the Dayton Reliable Tool & Mfg. Co.
- DRT operates three business segments: Metal Packaging, Aerospace, and Precision.
- DRT's Metal Packaging segment was the original developer of easy-open ("pull-top") ends used in food and beverage cans.
- DRT provides engineering expertise, design-for-manufacturability capabilities, go-to-market support, and systems integration for its customers.
- DRT operates eleven locations in the United States and Europe.
- Novelis is a Georgia-based company that recycles aluminum and produces flat-rolled aluminum products for industries such as aerospace, automotive and transportation.
