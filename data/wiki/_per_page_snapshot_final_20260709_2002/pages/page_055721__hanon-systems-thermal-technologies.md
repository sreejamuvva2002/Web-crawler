# Hanon Systems Thermal Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2023-08-25

**Source:** [Panattoni developing new premises for Hanon Systems - EuropaProperty.com](https://europaproperty.com/panattoni-developing-new-premises-for-hanon-systems/)  ·  `page_055721`

## Facts

- Panattoni is developing a new premises in the Kladno-South industrial zone for Hanon Systems Thermal Technologies.
- Hanon Systems already operates one hall in this location and is expanding with a new production hall while modernizing the older building to meet stringent sustainability criteria.
- Hanon Systems Thermal Technologies issued a tender for the project in February 2022 due to growth in the EV industry and the expansion of existing production for new customers.
- Negotiations and project preparation took approximately 14 months before Panattoni started construction work in July this year.
- The project consists of two transactions: improving the original hall primarily to make it more energy efficient, and constructing a completely new hall.
- Hanon Systems will take over the new hall next summer, at which point production is expected to start.
- Production operations in the existing hall will remain uninterrupted during the refurbishment and construction period.
- The entire project is designed according to high standards of a modern and sustainable industrial building.
- The new hall will include heat pumps, a photovoltaic power plant, and a solution for stormwater management.
- The Kladno region is highly valued for its excellent infrastructure, including nearby motorways D7, D6, D5, and Václav Havel International Airport.
- A planned modernized railway line connecting Prague, the airport, and Kladno is noted as a great promise for the future.
- The Kladno region is highly valued in terms of the quantity and availability of skilled labour.
- Pavel Sovička, Panattoni’s Managing Director for the Czech Republic and Slovakia, stated that the construction will contribute to the overall economic recovery of the region and build on the industrial history of Kladno.
- Ondřej Rys, Kladno’s architect and councillor for city development, welcomed the effective use and development of existing industrial areas and new job opportunities that complement new housing construction in the city.
