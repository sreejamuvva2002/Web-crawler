# ABC Technologies

## Facts

- ABC Technologies has announced the acquisition of TI Fluid Systems for £1.83 billion.
- The transaction is expected to close in H1 2025.
- The deal includes an all-cash offer of 200 pence per share for TI Fluid Systems shareholders.
- TI Fluid Systems is a London-listed company renowned for thermal fluid solutions catering to a range of vehicle architectures.
- TI Fluid Systems operates in 27 countries and works with leading original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).
- ABC Technologies is a leader in light vehicle components focused on developing sustainable manufacturing solutions.
- ABC Technologies serves over 25 OEMs across eight countries.
- The acquisition unites two automotive manufacturing leaders under one umbrella to enhance the combined entity's global footprint.
- ABC Technologies President and CEO Terry Campbell called the deal a strategic industry partnership with the potential to revolutionize automotive sector innovation.
- Integration of the two companies aims to drive growth by leveraging shared expertise in sustainable manufacturing solutions and thermal fluid solutions.
- The combined entity will address evolving demands in the automotive sector, including the shift toward electrification and sustainability.
- The acquisition will be financed through equity and debt.
- Financial advisory services for the transaction were led by Lazard, with support from Citi, TD Securities, and Scotiabank.
- Legal advice was provided by Kirkland & Ellis International and Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison.
- The merger positions ABC Technologies and TI Fluid Systems as key players in the global automotive industry through their combined expertise in light vehicle components and thermal fluid solutions.
