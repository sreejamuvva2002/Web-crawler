## Facts

- HL Holdings operates as the business-oriented holding company of the HL Group, overseeing key affiliates such as HL Mando and HL Klemove.
- HL Holdings is growing into a leading company in the future mobility market by managing affiliates, distributing mobility parts, providing logistics services, and exploring new business opportunities in global markets.
- HL Mando is a global leader in Software-Defined Vehicles (SDV), specializing in automotive safety-critical components, redundancy design, and by-wire technology.
- HL Mando provides eco-friendly vehicle solutions such as e-Drive and hydrogen fuel cell converters to drive the future of mobility.
- HL URIMAN has served as the exclusive distributor for the U.S. automotive aftermarket parts industry since its establishment in 1983.
- HL URIMAN is expanding its aftermarket business in North America to support HL Holdings' growth as a leading global company.
- HL Logistics Suzhou develops supply channels in the Chinese aftermarket at competitive prices for export and provides Total Supplier Chain Service by supplying products manufactured at HL Mando's Chinese plant to domestic and international automotive companies.
- HL Trading Europe was established to actively promote HL Holdings' expansion into the European automotive aftermarket, securing a sales network covering Europe, Turkey, the CIS region, Africa, and the Middle East.
- HL ANAND Automotive Parts Pvt Ltd is a joint venture established in January 2024 by HL Holdings and India’s Anand Group to expand into the Indian domestic market and global aftermarket business.
- HL ANAND Automotive Parts Pvt Ltd sources, develops, and supplies a diverse range of products as a forward base for global sourcing.
- HL Trading & Logistics Mexico plays a pivotal role in establishing HL Holdings as a leading company in Latin America through integrated logistics services and aftermarket business.
- HL Mando Softtech India Private, Ltd operates manufacturing and R&D facilities in Gurugram and Bangalore, India.
