# Aurubis

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-08

**Source:** [Aurubis to invest about $700 million to build smelter in US](https://ca.finance.yahoo.com/news/aurubis-invest-700-million-build-210613596.html)  ·  `page_017358`

## Facts

- Europe's biggest copper smelter Aurubis will invest around $700 million to build a smelter for complex recycling materials in North America.
- The commissioning of the secondary smelter in Augusta, Georgia, is planned for the second half of 2024.
- U.S. first lady Jill Biden toured the facility on Wednesday.
- An expansion of the plant is already under way.
- The expansion will allow the site to process 180,000 tons of complex recycling materials from 2026.
- The complex recycling materials include printed circuit boards and insulated copper cable.
- Aurubis is creating long-term, secure jobs for more than 200 people in an industry that is strategically important to the U.S., according to COO of multimetal recycling Inge Hofkens.
