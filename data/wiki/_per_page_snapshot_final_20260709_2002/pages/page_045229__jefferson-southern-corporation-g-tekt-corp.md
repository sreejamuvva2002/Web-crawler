## Facts

- The location is Rockmart, Georgia.
- The facility type is an automobile parts welding machine facility.
- The construction involved a warehouse building expansion for automobile parts and a welding machine.
- Building specifications include a concrete floor with AF, two 3-ton RTU units, fire alarm, PA system, T5 lighting fixtures, bollards, a PEMB structure, two egress doors, and power for the building.
