## Facts

- Top of Georgia is an economic development initiative that serves as a nonprofit fundraising arm to enhance the marketing efforts of the publicly funded Northwest Georgia Joint Development Authority (NWGAJDA).
- It serves private individuals or companies contributing to the economic well-being of the greater Northwest Georgia area, including Catoosa, Chattooga, Dade and Walker counties.
- Top of Georgia applied for and received its 501(c)(3) status in 2012 and became a certified nonprofit corporation.
- Its nonprofit status allows private companies and individuals to make tax deductible charitable contributions to further the economic development efforts of their community.
- The organization leverages its nonprofit status to launch an aggressive fundraising campaign.
- Through the Partners in Progress Campaign, it engages the local business communities of Northwest Georgia and Chattanooga in the economic prosperity of the region.
- It aims to increase the amount of funds available for regional economic development efforts and start a broader conversation about what economic development looks like for the future.
- Its investor base ranges from small companies to large corporations and individuals, with contributions supporting the economic welfare of the community.
