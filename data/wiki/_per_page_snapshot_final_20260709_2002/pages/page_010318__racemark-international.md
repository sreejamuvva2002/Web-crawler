## Facts

- RACEMARK International is a 100% family-owned and operated business founded in 1972 by Bob Bailey and Mark Donohue.
- The company was the first in the world to create a custom-fit carpet car mat.
- The company operates manufacturing locations in the United States, Germany, Hungary, and Switzerland.
- Its North American headquarters is located in Calhoun, Georgia, USA.
- Its European headquarters is located in Empfingen, Germany.
- RACEMARK International has a company size of 201-500 employees.
- The company specializes in floor mats, trunk mats, automotive OEM supply, Tier 1 supply, manufacturing, and design.
- RACEMARK delivers just-in-time products to customer assembly plants to provide the shortest lead-times possible at a competitive price.
- The company's customers include Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Tesla, Acura, Audi, Bentley, Chrysler, GM, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, MINI, Mitsubishi, Toyota, and Volkswagen.
- RACEMARK aims to become the global leader in sustainable automotive floor protection and design by 2023.
- The company's mission is to create innovative and sustainable automotive floor products that protect and enhance car interiors.
- The company's core values are Respect, Integrity, Initiative, Creativity, Leadership, and Sustainability.
- With a strong presence in North America and Europe, the company follows international market trends of the highest quality brands in the automotive and textile industries.
- The company operates within the automotive, motorcycles, and bicycles industry.
