# LG Energy Solution Vertech

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** United States, Georgia

## Overview

LG Energy Solution Vertech, an energy storage systems integrator backed by LG Energy Solution, announced a multi-year partnership with Qcells to deliver 5 GWh of utility-scale lithium-ion battery energy storage projects across the United States, supporting domestic content requirements and U.S. manufacturing jobs.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Provides large-scale lithium-ion battery storage systems that support grid reliability for electric vehicles and clean-energy infrastructure, while meeting U.S. domestic content rules.

## Investment

- **5 GWh** — Energy storage projects for Qcells development
  - Multi-year commitment to install American-made storage products across utility-scale sites scheduled 2028-2030.
- **4.8 GWh** (May 2024) — Battery purchase agreements for BESS projects developed across the U.S.

## Timeline

- **2022** — LG Energy Solution purchased NEC Energy Solutions and established LG Energy Solution Vertech Inc.
- **May 2024** — Qcells entered into battery purchase agreements with LG Energy Solution Vertech totaling 4.8 GWh of BESS projects.
- **Feb 3, 2026** — LG Energy Solution Vertech and Qcells announced a multi-year partnership to deliver 5 GWh of U.S. energy storage projects scheduled for 2028-2030.
- **End of 2026** — Planned ramp-up of LG Energy Solution battery cell production capacity to around 50 GWh across North America.
- **2028-2030** — Utility-scale energy storage projects under the partnership are scheduled for installation.
- **End of 2026** — LG Energy Solution aims to ramp production to around 50 GWh at sites across North America.

## Key facts

- The agreement includes 5 GWh of lithium-ion energy storage projects for utility-scale sites and lifecycle services from LG Energy Solution Vertech.
- Projects are scheduled for 2028-2030 and will meet U.S. domestic content requirements.
- LG Energy Solution’s battery products are manufactured in Michigan, supporting American manufacturing jobs and strengthening clean-energy supply chains.
- The partnership leverages made-in-America products to enhance grid reliability and reduce exposure to trade risks and tariffs.
- LG Energy Solution is manufacturing up to 16.5 GWh of battery cells in Holland, MI with plans to increase capacity to ~50 GWh by the end of 2026.
- Qcells operates the largest solar-panel manufacturing facility in the United States, located in Dalton, Georgia, and is building a second factory in Cartersville, Georgia.
