# HL-GA Battery Company

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-07

**Source:** [LG Energy Solution HR Chief prioritizes swift release of detainees in Georgia - CHOSUNBIZ](https://biz.chosun.com/en/en-industry/2025/09/07/UNXURPXPMFEDHEQ4VET52Z2SNU/)  ·  `page_001151`

## Facts

- U.S. immigration enforcement authorities conducted a crackdown at the Hyundai Motor Group-LG Energy Solution joint battery plant (HL-GA Battery Company) in Georgia.
- More than 300 people, including those from partner companies, were detained as a result of the crackdown.
- Kim Gi-soo, executive director and chief human resources officer (CHO) of LG Energy Solution, departed for the United States on September 7.
- Kim Gi-soo stated at Incheon International Airport that the top priority is the swift early release of all LG Energy Solution employees and employees of partner companies.
- Kim noted that since the government is putting in all efforts to respond, LG Energy Solution will do its best for everyone's safe and quick return.
- Kim refrained from mentioning details about U.S. response plans or the status of detained employees.
- On September 4 (local time), U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) and the Department of Homeland Security Investigations (HSI) launched a massive crackdown on undocumented immigrants at the construction site of the joint battery plant in Savannah, Georgia.
- 47 individuals affiliated with LG Energy Solution were detained, consisting of 46 South Korean nationals and 1 Indonesian national.
- More than 250 personnel from partner companies related to the HL-GA Battery Company were detained.
- Diplomatic authorities confirmed that over 300 South Koreans were detained.
- It was determined that there were no detained employees from Hyundai.
- LG Energy Solution stated in a press release that it is identifying regular medications for the detainees through an emergency contact network.
- LG Energy Solution plans to request that necessary medications be delivered to the detainees.
- LG Energy Solution is coordinating closely with the Korean government and relevant authorities to facilitate visits with the detainees.
- LG Energy Solution is requesting that communication and contact be possible for the detainees.
