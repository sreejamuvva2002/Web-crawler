# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-02

**Source:** [Rivian Raises Its Full-Year Delivery Forecast. Here’s Why the Stock Is Soaring. | The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/investing/2026/07/02/rivian-raises-its-full-year-delivery-forecast-heres-why-the-stock-is-soaring/)  ·  `page_059071`

## Facts

- Rivian is an electric vehicle (EV) company.
- Rivian produced 12,613 vehicles in its just-concluded second quarter.
- Rivian delivered 12,194 vehicles in the second quarter, topping its own forecast of 9,000 to 11,000.
- Second quarter deliveries were well above the second quarter 2025 number of 10,661 and first quarter 2026’s 10,365.
- Management attributed the strong delivery results to uptake of the EDV line of delivery vehicles.
- Management attributed the strong delivery results to demand for R1 pickups and large SUVs.
- Rivian's deliveries also benefited from the rollout of the R2 crossover SUV, which started shipping in June.
- Rivian raised its full-year 2026 delivery guidance to 65,000 to 70,000 units.
- The new full-year guidance is notably up from the previous estimate of 62,000 to 67,000.
- Rivian currently produces its vehicles at a single Illinois facility.
- Rivian will expand its manufacturing footprint significantly in the coming years.
- A Rivian factory in Georgia is currently under construction.
- The Georgia factory is aided by a downsized but still substantial low-interest $4.5 billion loan from the Department of Energy (DOE).
- Rivian sells two EDV models into a niche market for quick deliveries from merchants.
- The R2 is an attractively-priced crossover SUV that complements the larger and more expensive R1 SUVs in the lineup.
