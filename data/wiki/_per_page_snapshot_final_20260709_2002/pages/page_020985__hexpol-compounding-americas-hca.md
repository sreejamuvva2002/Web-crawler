# HEXPOL Compounding Americas (HCA)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [HEXPOL Compounding Americas Announces Leadership Changes - Modern Plastics India](https://modernplasticsindia.com/hexpol-compounding-americas-announces-leadership-changes/)  ·  `page_020985`

## Facts

- HEXPOL Compounding Americas (HCA), under the leadership of President and CEO Gary Moore, announced key changes to its Executive Leadership Team.
- Bruce Wynd was promoted from Director of Automotive to Chief Commercial Officer.
- Wynd will be supported by four Commercial Directors responsible for the Automotive, Specialty, Industrial, and Silicone/Performance Additives markets.
- Saul Reyes will replace Wynd as the Director of Automotive.
- Chris Airhart will continue as the Director of Industrial.
- Chris Poirier will continue to direct the Silicone and Additives business.
- Newly hired Brad Donat will serve as the Director of Specialty.
- Gary Moore stated that Bruce Wynd is a terrific leader with depth of experience in technology, supply chain, and commercial management, and has unparalleled industry knowledge at HEXPOL Compounding Americas.
- Brad Donat has over 30 years of industry experience and has served in various leadership roles within the Chemical Marketplace.
- Brad Donat most recently served as Senior Account Manager, Carbon Black, for Cabot Corporation based in Boston, MA.
- Bruce Wynd stated that Brad Donat's background of commercial success, market knowledge, and experience will benefit HEXPOL’s customers and drive growth in the Specialty marketplace.
- Current Purchasing and Supply Chain Director Ellen Clunk was promoted to Chief Procurement Officer to manage all aspects of the HCA supply chain.
- Ellen Clunk has been with the organization for over 13 years, starting as a development chemist before shifting into various purchasing roles within the company.
- Andrew Chan was promoted from Director of Operations of the South and West regions to the newly formed position of Chief Operations Officer.
- Chan will be supported by two Operations Directors: Ernesto Gutierrez overseeing HCA’s three Mexico sites, and newly hired Kevin Brown overseeing HCA’s five Northern sites along with its two West Coast sites.
- Chan will also lead the Business Systems, Continuous Improvement, and Engineering/Maintenance teams.
- Kevin Brown joins HCA from Morgan Truck Body, where he served as Sr. Regional Director of Operations.
- Kevin Brown has over 25 years of industry experience including leadership positions in Plant Management, Quality Control, and Operations.
- Andrew Chan stated that Kevin Brown’s exceptional leadership and organizational skills will benefit the Operations Team and noted he is a hands-on manufacturing professional who understands continuous improvement, culture development, and problem-solving.
- Gary Moore noted that these organizational changes will allow HEXPOL Compounding Americas to have clear visibility on the current and future needs of its customers.
