# Woory Industrial Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-07

**Source:** [EV parts manufacturer to build first U.S. facility in Laurens County creating 130 jobs](https://wgxa.tv/news/local/ev-parts-manufacturer-to-build-first-us-facility-in-laurens-county-creating-130-jobs)  ·  `page_015379`

## Facts

- An $18 million dollar investment will bring 130 new jobs to Laurens County.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp announced Woory Industrial Company will build a new automotive parts manufacturing facility in Dublin.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated, “We're proud to welcome Woory Industrial as the latest job creator to help us on the path to becoming the e-mobility capital of the nation,”
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that the emerging industry continues to bring historic levels of new jobs and investment to communities all over the state, thanks to a highly-skilled workforce and reliable infrastructure network.
- Governor Brian Kemp expressed confidence that Woory will have a long and beneficial partnership with Dublin-Laurens County.
- Woory was established in 1989 and specializes in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning components and systems for the automotive industry.
- Woory maintains traditional product lines but is also concentrating on the development of new products for electric vehicles and hydrogen-fueled cars.
- Jungwoo Kim, CEO of Woory Industrial Company, stated that the company is facing a revolution in the automobile industry for the first time in 100 years.
- Jungwoo Kim noted that the transition to electric vehicles is the future and the U.S. EV market is the key market among all automotive markets.
- Jungwoo Kim stated that over the past 10 years, Woory has successfully grown into a specialized EV component company through continuous innovation.
- Jungwoo Kim said Georgia will be a good opportunity for Woory to take another step forward as part of the vanguard of EV development.
- Jungwoo Kim expressed confidence that Woory's experience and technology will contribute to the prosperity of the community and appreciated the partnership of the State of Georgia and the City of Dublin.
- The new facility will be located at 404 Kellam Road in Dublin and will be the company's first U.S. manufacturing facility.
- Dublin Mayor Joshua Kight stated that fostering quality and sustainable growth is a goal of the City of Dublin.
- Dublin Mayor Joshua Kight noted that Woory’s commitment to place an existing manufacturing building back into service helps to promote more infill development while creating new career paths for the community.
- Woory will be hiring for positions in personnel from all fields necessary for an automotive parts manufacturing plant.
- Operations are expected to begin in November 2023.
