# Maxxis International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-07-15

**Source:** [Maxxis Tires to open in Whitestown - Town of Whitestown](https://whitestown.in.gov/news/maxxis-tires-to-open-in-whitestown/)  ·  `page_029719`

## Facts

- Maxxis will open a new distribution facility just northwest of Indianapolis, Indiana later this year as part of a major expansion of its U.S. warehouse capacity.
- The facility will feature more than 350,000 square feet of warehouse space, including 10,000 square feet of office space and two 2,500-square-foot administrative warehouse offices.
- Maxxis is one of the world’s top ten tire manufacturers and a Taiwan-based company that has operated in the U.S. since 1985.
- The company offers automotive, light truck, bicycle, motorcycle, and ATV tires as both original equipment and aftermarket products.
- In addition to the Indiana facility scheduled to open in August, Maxxis operates U.S. distribution centers in Rancho Cucamonga, California; Grapevine, Texas; and its corporate headquarters in Suwanee, Georgia.
- The new facility is located in the Park130 business park in Whitestown, Indiana.
- Maxxis management cited Indiana’s excellent transportation infrastructure, friendly business environment, and proximity to major automakers as reasons for choosing the state for the new facility.
- Management noted that Whitestown is a rapidly growing area with easy access to both major interstates and rail.
- The new center is located near I-65, just minutes from I-465, and less than a five-hour drive from all of the Midwest’s auto manufacturing hubs.
- Molly Whitehead, executive director of the Boone County Economic Development Corporation, stated that Maxxis will be a valuable asset to Boone County and expressed excitement for a productive relationship in the years to come.
- Maxxis will recruit for warehouse manager and warehouse supervisor positions in the Indianapolis area.
- For over 50 years, Maxxis International has delivered high-quality tires to original equipment and aftermarket customers across Asia, Europe, and North America, with products distributed in approximately 180 countries.
- Maxxis International employs more than 30,000 people around the world.
