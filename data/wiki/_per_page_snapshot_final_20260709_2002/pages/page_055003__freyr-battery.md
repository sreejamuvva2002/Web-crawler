# FREYR Battery

## Facts

- FREYR Battery plans to open a new assembly plant in Coweta County, GA, about 40 miles south of Atlanta.
- The factory will make batteries for electric vehicles.
- The project represents a total capital investment of approximately $2.6 billion.
- The exact location of the manufacturing facility was not disclosed.
- The manufacturing facility will be known as Giga America.
- The project is expected to create more than 700 new jobs over the next seven years.
- FREYR considered more than 150 sites across 25 states before settling on this specific location.
- Georgia has recently established itself as a hub for electric vehicle production.
- Last December, California-based electric truck manufacturer Rivian Inc. announced plans to build a $5 billion facility in Rutledge, about 50 miles east of Atlanta.
- In May of this year, Hyundai Motor Group revealed plans to develop a $5.5 billion manufacturing hub for its electric vehicle division in Bryan County, located near Savannah in coastal Georgia.
