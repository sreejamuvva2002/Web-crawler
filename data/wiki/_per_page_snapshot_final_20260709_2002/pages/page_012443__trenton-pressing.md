## Facts

- The company required software to handle full operational complexity including APQP, PPAP, shop floor scheduling, and quality control across its operations.
- Trenton Pressing operates across 192,000+ square feet of production space.
- The facility employs over 200 people.
- Operations are distributed across 3 buildings.
- Production equipment includes automatic presses ranging from 200 to 660 tons.
- The site features 20 or more automated weld cells.
- Previously, disconnected systems created data silos and slowed down critical processes at Trenton Pressing.
- GatesFlow was built on the production floor at Trenton Pressing using the same address, team, and standards.
- The GatesFlow platform unified APQP program tracking, cross-system data mapping, and quality management into a single system purpose-built for Tier 1 operations.
- Trenton Pressing completed a $12M facility expansion in 2024.
