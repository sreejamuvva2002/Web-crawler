## Facts

- In 1987, Stephen Barr and family purchased the E.L. Hollingsworth Company.
- Through a series of strategic mergers beginning in 1991, the company broadened its service capabilities.
- The company is nationally recognized as a certified minority business under the Hollingsworth name.
- Hollingsworth has emerged as a leading supply chain provider to manufacturing and distribution communities.
- Mr. Stephen Barr serves as chairman and CEO.
- The company is headquartered in Dearborn, Michigan.
- Hollingsworth has grown to over 3,000 employees across 21 US-based locations.
- The company operates over 10 million square feet of warehouse and production space.
- Hollingsworth supports over 550 million pieces shipped annually.
- The company states: "We have the best people in the business."
