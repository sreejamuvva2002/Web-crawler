# Sierra International Machinery

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2018-11-08

**Source:** [Sierra breaks ground at Georgia manufacturing facility on 24,000-square-foot expansion - Expansion Solutions](https://www.expansionsolutionsmagazine.com/sierra-breaks-ground-at-georgia-manufacturing-facility-on-24000-square-foot-expansion/)  ·  `page_008183`

## Facts

- Sierra International Machinery is adding a 24,000-square-foot expansion at their manufacturing facility in Jesup, Georgia.
- The expansion is due to overwhelming demand for the Sierra REB line of two-ram balers and conveyors.
- This expansion will allow Sierra the ability to increase production and meet the demand for the full Sierra product line.
- This is the third expansion Sierra has added to its Georgia facility since it opened in 2008.
- John Sacco, President and Owner of Sierra International Machinery, announced the expansion of their facility in Jesup, Georgia.
- The success of Sierra's two-ram balers and conveyors in the waste sector has grown exponentially, far more than initially anticipated.
- Growth can be largely attributed to the new REB-4 two-ram baler that has surpassed all expectations.
- The expansion was necessary to meet the demand for the Sierra catalog.
- Every machine in the Sierra product line passes through the Georgia facility.
- Every Sierra two-ram baler and conveyor is fully engineered, manufactured, and assembled in Georgia.
- The Georgia facility is the birthplace of the Sierra two-ram balers.
- Sierra offers three different two-ram models, including the patent pending REB-4 with Dual-Compression Doors, and many conveyor models.
- Once completed, Sierra’s Georgia manufacturing facility will be 72,000 square feet.
- The expansion will provide increased job opportunities in the Jesup, Georgia area.
