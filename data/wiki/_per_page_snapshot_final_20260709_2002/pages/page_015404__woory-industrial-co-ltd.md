# Woory Industrial Co., Ltd.

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Woory Industrial Co., Ltd. will establish a new manufacturing facility in Dublin, Georgia.
- The facility will create over 130 new jobs.
- The project brings an estimated $18 million in investment to Dublin-Laurens County.
- Woory Industrial Company was founded in 1989.
- The company specializes in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning components for the global automotive industry.
- Woory is now focusing on developing products for electric and hydrogen-fueled vehicles.
- This facility marks Woory's first manufacturing site in the United States.
- The facility will produce electrical heaters, control units, and actuators.
- Products will be supplied to electric and internal combustion carmakers in North America.
- Operations at the new facility are set to begin in November 2023.
