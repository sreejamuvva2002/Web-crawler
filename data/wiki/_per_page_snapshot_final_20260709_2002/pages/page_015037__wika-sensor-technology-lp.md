# WIKA Sensor Technology, LP

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2018-01-01

**Source:** [WIKA Sensor Technology Looks to the Future - WIKA USA](https://www.wika.com/en-us/pr_0621_en_us.WIKA)  ·  `page_015037`

## Facts

- WIKA USA announced the creation of a new company, WIKA Sensor Technology, formerly tecsis, LP, located in Lawrenceville, GA.
- tecsis, LP was fully integrated into the global WIKA Group of companies as of January 1, 2018.
- tecsis developed into a reliable global manufacturer of measurement technology.
- Solutions offered by tecsis range from custom load cell systems for hospital beds to off-the-shelf force measurement portfolios.
- As of 2021, WIKA updated tecsis, LP’s name to WIKA Sensor Technology, LP to reflect a focus on integrated, forward-looking measurement solutions.
- WIKA Sensor Technology, LP serves as WIKA USA’s hub for integrated measurement solutions with a focus on force, pressure, and supplementing IIoT technologies.
- The new brand primarily serves industrial markets around machine building/automation, custom machine solutions, OEM off-highway (mobile working machines), and expands expertise in serving aerospace and space applications.
