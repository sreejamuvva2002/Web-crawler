# Rivian Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-26

**Source:** [Funding tentatively approved for new $6.6 billion EV plant in Georgia. Here's what we know](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/funding-tentatively-approved-6-6-183748740.html)  ·  `page_059060`

## Facts

- The U.S. Department of Energy has tentatively approved funding to a plan that would promote manufacturing and clean energy in Georgia.
- Rivian Automotive is looking to build a new electric vehicle facility with the help of a loan for up to $6.6 billion from the energy department's Advanced Technology Vehicle Manufacturing Loan Program.
- The facility will build Rivian's midsize vehicles including its R2 SUV and R3/R3X crossover.
- The plant will be built in two phases, with the first phase starting in 2028.
- The facility will be located in the Social Circle area just outside Covington and about 35 miles south-west of Athens.
- Rivian estimates that this plant will create about 7,500 operations jobs by 2030.
- The funding commitment is conditional until the company satisfies certain technical, legal, environmental, and financial conditions.
