## Facts

- Haering Precision USA LP received a High-Demand Apprenticeship Program Award.
- The award underscores the company's commitment to workforce development and highlights the success of its partnership with North Georgia Technical College.
- The High-Demand Apprenticeship Program Award supports businesses that are actively investing in upskilling workers.
- These programs address workforce shortages in high-demand industries such as manufacturing through hands-on training and technical education.
- Haering Precision USA LP's CNC Machinist Apprenticeship Program combines classroom instruction at North Georgia Technical College with practical, on-the-job training at its Lavonia facility.
- The integrated apprenticeship approach ensures trainees acquire both technical expertise and real-world skills needed to excel in precision manufacturing.
- Dustin Chambers, Commercial Plant Manager in Lavonia, stated the recognition reflects the company's dedication to developing the next generation of skilled professionals in precision manufacturing and showcases the vital role of partnerships with educational institutions like North Georgia Technical College.
- Tim Bennett, Academy Manager in Lavonia, highlighted that such programs equip apprentices with the skills and knowledge they need to thrive in high-demand careers while meeting the needs of local industries.
