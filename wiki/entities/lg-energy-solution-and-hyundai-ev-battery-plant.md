# LG Energy Solution & Hyundai EV Battery Plant

**Type:** Investment Announcement  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Bryan County, Georgia, Bryan County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

LG Energy Solution and Hyundai announced a joint $4.3 billion investment to build an electric-vehicle battery manufacturing plant in Bryan County, Georgia that will supply Hyundai’s EV production line.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Battery cell manufacturing for Hyundai electric vehicles

## Key facts

- Manufacturing incentives in the Inflation Reduction Act have attracted a $4.3 billion EV battery investment to Georgia.
- The joint LG Energy Solution-Hyundai plant will supply Hyundai’s electric-vehicle manufacturing site in Bryan County.
- The investment is expected to create 3,000 jobs.
- Full-scale production is projected to be online by the end of 2025.
- Sen. Ossoff led an April trade delegation to South Korea that helped secure the battery investment.
- Georgia ranks second nationwide in new investments from IRA manufacturing incentives, according to the Financial Times.
- Earlier this year Sen. Ossoff announced a $2.5 billion solar-manufacturing investment by Qcells in Dalton and Cartersville.
