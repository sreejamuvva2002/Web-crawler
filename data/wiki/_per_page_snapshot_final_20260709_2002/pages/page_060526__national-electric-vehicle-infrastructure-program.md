# National Electric Vehicle Infrastructure Program

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [NEVI EV Charger Tracker - Plug In America](https://pluginamerica.org/nevi-funding-tracking/)  ·  `page_060526`

## Facts

- The NEVI program provides $7.5 billion to build the backbone of America’s EV charging network along highways and in communities.
- Five billion dollars of funding is allocated across all 50 states and Puerto Rico to build charging along highways.
- Two and one-half billion dollars is available as competitive grants to build out charging in communities and to address gaps in highway charging.
- NEVI was created through the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law in 2021.
- The program operates on a five-year timeline, with the page noting it is currently in its third year.
- EV chargers funded by NEVI are already operational in eight states.
- Deploying EV charging infrastructure requires contracting, planning, design and engineering, permitting, procurement of equipment, and installation.
- A custom map tracks announced and energized NEVI stations funded through the $5 billion formula program, excluding sites supported by competitive funding.
