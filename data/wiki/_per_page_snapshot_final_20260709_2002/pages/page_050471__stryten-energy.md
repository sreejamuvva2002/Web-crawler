# Stryten Energy

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [North American Clean Energy - Stryten Energy Named a 2026 Military Friendly Employer](https://www.nacleanenergy.com/energy-storage/stryten-energy-named-a-2026-military-friendly-employer)  ·  `page_050471`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy has been named a 2026 Silver Military Friendly Employer in its annual list.
- The designation recognizes companies that create sustainable and meaningful career paths, community outreach, brand enthusiasm and enduring partnerships that benefit the military community.
- Stryten Energy hires military members across every level of the company, from the plant floor to the executive management team.
- Many military employees at Stryten Energy have progressed through the company via multiple promotions.
- Military veterans make up over seven percent of Stryten’s workforce.
- Mike Judd serves as President and Chief Executive Officer at Stryten Energy.
- Mike Judd stated that Stryten Energy values the service and sacrifice of military veterans and offers opportunities for them to use skills gained in the military to build fulfilling careers.
- Mike Judd expressed pride in the recognition, noting it reaffirms the company's commitment to creating a veteran-friendly culture and hiring veterans.
- Institutions earning the Military Friendly Employer designation are evaluated using public data sources and responses from a proprietary survey.
- Over 1,200 companies participated in the Military Friendly survey.
- Methodology, criteria, and weightings for the evaluation were determined by Military Friendly in consultation with the Military Friendly Advisory Council.
- Final ratings combine an organization’s survey score with assessments of its ability to meet thresholds for Recruitment, New Hire Retention, Employee Turnover, and Promotion & Advancement of veterans and military employees.
- Kayla Lopez serves as Vice President of Memberships at Military Friendly.
- Kayla Lopez stated that earning the designation reflects deep-rooted values and strategic foresight, building pathways for lasting impact rather than performative commitment.
- Stryten Energy will be showcased in the 2026 Military Friendly Employers Guide in the Winter issue of G.I. Jobs magazine.
- Stryten Energy will also be featured on the Military Friendly website.
- Stryten Energy currently has job openings for military members across numerous U.S. locations.
- Stryten Energy maintains a dedicated section on its corporate website for military and veteran job seekers.
- Stryten Energy participates in events supporting the military and veterans throughout the year.
- Stryten Energy is a proud sponsor of Rivers of Recovery, a national nonprofit that uses outdoor-based activities to treat symptoms of post-traumatic stress, depression and anxiety in combat veterans and active-duty military.
- Through Operation Gratitude, Stryten Energy employees support active military and their families by making Battalion Buddies and hygiene kits.
- Stryten Energy employees hand fill more than 200 Battalion Buddy bears each year for distribution to children of deployed service members.
- Stryten Energy employees also assemble hygiene care kits sent to troops deployed overseas.
- Stryten Energy is a sponsor for Wreaths Across America, an organization that honors veterans by placing wreaths on their graves during the holidays.
- This year, Stryten Energy sponsored 1,000 wreaths to be placed on veterans’ graves at Green Lawn Cemetery & Mausoleum in Roswell, Georgia.
- Stryten Energy employees assisted with the placement of the wreaths at the Roswell, Georgia location.
