# JTEKT North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-09-28

**Source:** [JTEKT North America expands in South Carolina](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/shopmanagement/jtekt-north-america-expands-in-south-carolina)  ·  `page_045317`

## Facts

- JTEKT North America, a subsidiary of global automotive and industrial supplier JTEKT Corp., has completed a $5.8 million expansion of its headquarters on the campus of the Clemson University International Center for Automotive Research (CU-ICAR) in Greenville, S.C.
- The $5.8 million headquarters expansion is expected to create 100 jobs by 2020.
- JTEKT North America manufactures steering systems, driveline components, bearings, and precision machine tools for the automotive industry.
- The company employs more than 6,000 associates at 24 sites across North America.
- JTEKT North America operates four manufacturing facilities located in South Carolina.
- The firm previously consolidated a portion of its headquarters to CU-ICAR in 2015.
