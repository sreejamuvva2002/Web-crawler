## Facts

- Vista Metals Corp. completed another significant expansion at its Georgia facility.
- Investments for the expansion included large sawing equipment.
- Investments included additional tooling for casting mold plate slabs.
- The expansion involved large increases in overall capacity to improve lead times to its diverse customer base.
- Duramold-2 and Duramold-5 product lines are under constant lead-time pressure from distributors and customers.
- Producing, processing, and shipping from the Georgia facility saves customers days of transit time when compared to shipping from its California facility.
- The company maintains a continued dedication to domestic production.
- The company holds AS 9100 quality system certification.
- Vista Metals Corp. positions itself as an independent producer of specialty aluminum products.
