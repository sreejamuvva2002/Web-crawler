# Essex Solutions

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Essex Solutions, the New Brand for Superior Essex Magnet Wire, Continues Sustainability Advancements  - Magnetics Magazine](https://magneticsmag.com/essex-solutions-the-new-brand-for-superior-essex-magnet-wire-continues-sustainability-advancements/)  ·  `page_010493`

## Facts

- The magnet wire plant in Torreón, Mexico of Essex Solutions has become the company’s fourth facility to achieve sustainability certification in the U.S. as part an ongoing dedication to achieve new levels of environmental stewardship.
- The company continues its rebranding to the Essex Solutions identity following the recent buyout by Atlanta-based Superior Essex of the global joint venture Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire.
- The Torreón plant joins other sites in Fort Wayne and Franklin, Indiana as well as the copper rod manufacturing plant in Columbia City, Indiana to have completed The Copper Mark Assurance Process.
- The Copper Mark Assurance Process is an internationally recognized framework that promotes responsible copper production and ethical practices.
- Jared Rowntree serves as President North America for Essex Solutions.
- Under the new name, Essex Solutions will encompass all previous Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire locations worldwide.
- This includes magnet wire facilities in Japan and Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia that were contributed by Furukawa Electric in October 2020.
- Essex Energy in Italy is now included in the Essex Solutions organization.
- Related businesses Lacroix + Kress, IVA Insulations, and Hi-Wire will join the Essex Solutions organization while continuing to operate under their existing brand identity.
- The acquisition of Furukawa Electric’s minority stake in Essex Furukawa took place in April 2024.
- Neither party disclosed the acquisition amount, but analysts estimated the transaction to be about $200 million.
- The transaction ended a joint venture between the companies that began in 2020.
- Essex Solutions now has more than 2,500 employees across 18 facilities globally with a production capacity of 402,000 metric tons.
- Superior Essex has over 3,000 employees in 11 countries and provides magnet wire, communications fiber cable through Essex Brownell, and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- Superior Essex is a subsidiary of LS Cable & System based in Seoul, Korea.
- LS Cable & System operates a global network of about 100 subsidiaries and branches in 25 countries active in electric power, automation, machinery, materials and energy.
- LS holdings include operations involved in magnetics technology such as superconducting wire, magnet wire and EMI technology.
- In 2021 with partners, LS successfully developed a 3-phase coaxial cable featuring improved structural design of the superconducting layer that reduced production costs by more than 10% and increased the power transfer rate by more than 20%.
- LS aims to commercialize the developed 3-phase coaxial cable for large-capacity energy transmission.
- LS has been working with Korea Electrotechnology Research Institute to jointly research advancements in superconducting transmission and distribution power devices.
- LS Cable & System is a major producer of magnet wire, particularly for the Korean automotive market.
- LS has supplied its magnet wire products to Hyundai, Kia and General Motors electric vehicles.
- In 2021, LS became the first company in Korea to mass product 800V magnet wires.
- LS became the sole supplier of magnet wire for the drive motors of the Hyundai IONIQ 5 and Kia EV6 electric vehicles.
- Subsidiary LS Mtron unveiled new products at DesignCon 2024 including advancements in EMI connectivity.
- The complete 3-layer shielding structure allows stable transmission of 5G mmWave antenna module data and resolves noise issues during high-definition data transmission from high-pixel cameras.
