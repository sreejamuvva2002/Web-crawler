# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-10

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation (NYSE: TG).
- Bonnell Aluminum commends the Biden administration’s proclamation to strengthen the rules to qualify for trade benefits between the U.S. and Mexico.
- Under the proclamation, aluminum must meet a 'smelted and cast' requirement to qualify for relief from the Section 232 Tariff.
- The proclamation prohibits preferential treatment for imports where the primary country of smelt, secondary country of smelt, or country of most recent cast was Belarus, China, Russia, or Iran.
- This negotiated requirement recognizes unfair trade practices where U.S. trading partners process subsidized metal from countries like China and Russia.
- Bonnell Aluminum is a leading manufacturer of custom-fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions serving the automotive and transportation markets.
- Branded product lines include TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum and Futura Transitions by Bonnell Aluminum.
- The company has manufacturing facilities located in Newnan, GA; Carthage, TN; Niles, MI; Elkhart, IN; and Clearfield, UT.
- Guy Charpentier serves as the Division Marketing Director for Bonnell Aluminum.
