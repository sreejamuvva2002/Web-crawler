# Trenton Pressing

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-01

**Source:** [Rep. Mike Cameron commends Trenton Pressing&#39;s $12 million expansion in Dade County - Peach Tree Times](https://peachtreetimes.com/stories/661433390-rep-mike-cameron-commends-trenton-pressing-s-12-million-expansion-in-dade-county)  ·  `page_012414`

## Facts

- State Representative Mike Cameron (R-Rossville) commends Trenton Pressing on the completion of its $12 million expansion.
- The expansion enhances manufacturing operations in Dade County by 100,000 square feet.
- Rep. Mike Cameron stated: “The Southeast United States has become a magnet for automotive brands and suppliers in the last two decades, and as is the case in so many ways, Georgia leads by example.”
- Rep. Mike Cameron stated: “By investing in our workforce and identifying ways to make doing business better here, we continue to see great companies like Trenton Pressing create high-quality jobs and invest in Georgia.”
- The 100,000-square-foot expansion covers manufacturing and assembly operations at Trenton Pressing.
- The growth enhances Trenton Pressing’s ability to supply high-quality components to its clients.
- Clients include brands such as BMW, Club Car, and ZF.
- Trenton Pressing is now one of the leading employers in Dade County.
- The company has a workforce exceeding 200 employees.
- Trenton Pressing specializes in metal stamping, welding processes, and complex assemblies.
- The company provides comprehensive turnkey contract manufacturing solutions along with warehousing and shipping services.
- Services cater to both automotive and non-automotive clients.
