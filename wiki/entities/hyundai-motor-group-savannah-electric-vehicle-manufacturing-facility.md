# Hyundai Motor Group Savannah Electric Vehicle Manufacturing Facility

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Savannah, Georgia, Bryan County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

A $5.54 billion electric-vehicle manufacturing plant being built by Hyundai Motor Group on nearly 3,000 acres in Bryan County, about 30 miles west of Savannah, Georgia. Construction is months ahead of schedule and the facility is expected to begin vehicle production in the first half of 2025.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The plant will produce battery-powered Genesis, Hyundai and Kia vehicles for the U.S. market, expanding domestic EV manufacturing capacity.
