# Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-05-02

**Source:** [Panasonic Automotive Streamlines Vehicle Bluetooth Pairing with Patent Pending Friction Free Connectivity Solution - Cerebral-Overload](https://cerebral-overload.com/2019/05/panasonic-automotive-streamlines-vehicle-bluetooth-pairing-with-patent-pending-friction-free-connectivity-solution/)  ·  `page_047953`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive presented its new Friction Free Connectivity solution to members of congress at the 2019 CES on the Hill event in Washington D.C. on May 2, 2019.
- The company is a leading global supplier of automotive infotainment and connectivity systems solutions.
- Panasonic Automotive's headquarters are located in Peachtree City, Georgia.
- The company maintains sales, marketing, and engineering operations in Farmington Hills, Michigan.
- Friction Free Connectivity is a Bluetooth pairing via Wi-Fi solution that requires a smartphone app for one-time secure setup.
- New vehicle owners register within the smartphone app via a streamlined log-in process and establish rights to their vehicle using a Vehicle Identification Number (VIN).
- The solution can be integrated into existing vehicle manufacturer applications and ride-sharing or rental car apps for automatic Bluetooth pairing.
- The pairing solution offers enhanced security by cryptographically trusting both the user and the vehicle to prevent drive-by-pairing or accidental connections.
- Jeffrey Zellen, Senior Engineering Manager at Panasonic Automotive, stated that Bluetooth is standard in 86% of new vehicles but pairing complexity perpetuates distracted driving, and their solution makes it easy for drivers to utilize.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America is a division company of Panasonic Corporation of North America.
- Panasonic Corporation of North America is headquartered in Newark, New Jersey, and serves as the principal North American subsidiary of Osaka, Japan-based Panasonic Corporation.
