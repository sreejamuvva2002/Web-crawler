# Oneda Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Oneda Corporation is a Columbus, Georgia-based, third-generation, family-owned, contract precision metal stamping manufacturer.
- The company was founded in 1989 and operates as a subsidiary of Japan-based Oneda Electric Corp.
- The Columbus location employs 67 people.
- The company primarily serves the automotive and heating and air conditioning industries.
- Oneda Corporation previously worked with the Georgia Manufacturing Extension Partnership (GaMEP) at Georgia Tech and the Southeastern Trade Adjustment Assistance Center (SETAAC).
- GaMEP provided marketing services to Oneda, including developing a strategic marketing plan with goals, tactics, and a progress tracking system.
- GaMEP assisted with implementation, email and social media strategizing, and content writing.
- GaMEP redesigned Oneda's website and revamped its LinkedIn page.
- The project included writing a tip sheet to generate leads and writing customer success stories.
- GaMEP trained Michael Brooks on content development so he could manage it internally.
- Michael Brooks serves as Oneda’s manager of marketing, customer service, and logistics and is also an engineer.
- Katie Takacs serves as the group manager for marketing at GaMEP.
- Following the successful launch of Oneda's website and revamped LinkedIn page, parent company Oneda Electric Corp. began working to upgrade its own site.
- GaMEP identified 100 companies and purchasing managers across eight states as potential new customers for Oneda.
- GaMEP created a three-step lead generation campaign targeting the identified potential customers.
- Oneda engaged the GaMEP team on an energy assessment analysis that resulted in decreased energy consumption at the company.
