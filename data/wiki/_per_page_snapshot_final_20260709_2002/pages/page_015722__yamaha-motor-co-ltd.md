# Yamaha Motor Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Gov. Kemp: Yamaha to Move HQ to Georgia](https://www.einpresswire.com/article/898687958/gov-kemp-yamaha-to-move-hq-to-georgia)  ·  `page_015722`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Yamaha Motor Co., Ltd. has officially decided to move its U.S. headquarters to Kennesaw, Georgia.
- The relocation involves moving out of California following roughly 50 years of operation there.
- The decision follows a meeting between Governor Kemp and Yamaha executives in October of last year at the Southeast U.S./Japan (SEUS-Japan) annual gathering in Tokyo.
- Yamaha Motor Co., Ltd. is a manufacturer of ATVs, boat engines, personal watercraft, and other motorized products.
- The company already has a strong presence in Georgia, employing over 2,300 Georgians.
- The relocation of the Yamaha Motor Corp. U.S. headquarters will begin this year and is expected to continue through 2028.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated, “After many years of great partnership, we are honored and proud to welcome Yamaha's American headquarters to the No. 1 state for business.”
- Governor Kemp added that the move is a testament to what Georgia offers job creators and invited other California-based companies to consider relocating to Georgia.
- The Yamaha Motor Company currently employs thousands of people across various facilities in Georgia.
- The 1.3-million-square-foot Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America factory in Newnan opened in 1986 and currently employs roughly 2,000 Georgians.
- Yamaha relocated its marine division to Kennesaw in 1999.
- The company expanded the Kennesaw facility with a 75,000-square-foot Marine Innovation Center in 2023.
- In 2019, the company moved its motorsports operations to Georgia to benefit from close proximity to the existing facilities.
- Yamaha Motor Co., Ltd. is a world-leading producer of motorcycles, marine products, power products, and robotics.
- The company was founded in 1955 and is headquartered in Iwata, Shizuoka, Japan.
- The company operates under the corporate mission of being a "Kando Creating Company," aiming to offer new excitement and a more fulfilling life for people all over the world.
