# Nifco

## Facts

- The Nifco group is invested in designing, developing and manufacturing quality components for the global Automotive Industry.
- Products range from the interior of a vehicle to the exterior, inclusive of the engine.
- Exterior components range from corner covers to wheel arch liners.
- The company incorporates material and fastener know-how into larger, multi functional components.
- Nifco supplies Powertrain components to the Global Automotive Industry with full capabilities in design, development, manufacturing and testing.
- Kinematics trim assemblies feature controlled movement incorporating specialist Motion Control Devices.
- Nifco has specialist business units dedicated to fuel and safety critical parts.
- The company designs, develops and manufactures quality interior trim solutions from single mouldings to complex assemblies.
- In 1979, Nifco invented a small gear damper for Sharp cassette players and now supplies dampers as part of complex functional assemblies.
- Nifco produces valves which control the fuel in vehicles.
- The company manufactures car seat sides and door handles.
- Nifco originated as 'Nippon Industrial Fastener Corp' and fasteners remain at the heart of its operations.
- The company is built on values called the Nifco Spirit, fostered at inception and passed down through generations.
