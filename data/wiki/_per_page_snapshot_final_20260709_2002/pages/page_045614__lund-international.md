## Facts

- Lund International is an acquired company based in Buford, United States.
- The company was founded in 1965 by Sanjeev Thukral.
- It operates as a truck and auto accessories manufacturer and distributor specializing in aftermarket parts for trucks and other vehicles.
- Its extensive product range includes truck bed covers, bull bars, steps, fender flares, nerf bars, running boards, towing equipment, racks, carriers, and cargo lights.
- The company offers a variety of finishes, mounting options, and materials to customize vehicles.
- Lund International provides resources such as installation guides and dealer support for its products.
- The company has 166 employees as on May 31, 2026.
- Lund International was acquired by Truck Hero on May 10, 2019.
- Sanjeev Thukral serves as the Founder and Managing Partner of Lund International.
- Lund International is ranked 12th among 707 active competitors by Tracxn.
- Lund International has made four acquisitions, including companies founded in 1994 (Lafayette), 2009 (Buford), and 1967 (Buford).
- In April 2018, Lund International settled a patent infringement lawsuit with Rocky Ridge.
- The company focuses on delivering quality products and enhancing vehicle functionality and appearance for truck and auto enthusiasts.
