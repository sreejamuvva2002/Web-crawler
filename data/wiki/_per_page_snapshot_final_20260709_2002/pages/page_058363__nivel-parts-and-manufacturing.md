## Facts

- Nivel Parts and Manufacturing is a motor vehicle manufacturing company founded in 1968.
- The company is headquartered at 3510 Port Jacksonville Parkway, Jacksonville, Florida 32226, United States.
- It operates as a global aftermarket manufacturer, distributor, and seller of specialty and heavy-duty vehicle parts and accessories.
- The company has access to more than 100,000 parts and accessories.
- Nivel is described as the largest supplier in North America for golf cart/personal transportation vehicle aftermarket parts and accessories, off-road vehicles, and heavy-duty industries.
- Nivel sells its products directly to OEMs, independent distributors, golf car dealers, and agricultural and construction equipment dealers.
- The organization employs between 201 and 500 people.
