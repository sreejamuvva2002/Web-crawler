# MAT Holdings Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- MAT Holdings Inc. is a diversified global manufacturing, marketing, and distribution company headquartered in Long Grove, Illinois.
- The company operates across three continents and employs over 13,000 people worldwide.
- MAT Auto Group manufactures suspension components including shocks and struts under the Gabriel® brand for the North American market.
- MAT Auto Group also manufactures suspension systems under the Reyco Granning brand for the North American market.
- The MAT Foundry Group engineers and manufactures cast iron products that serve passenger vehicle, commercial vehicle, and industrial applications.
- The MAT Friction Group serves passenger, commercial, and heavy-duty vehicle markets with braking components including brake pads, shoes, shims, and back plates.
- MAT Industries operates domestic manufacturing facilities to produce high quality air compressors, air tools, and accessories for retailers under several popular brands.
- MAT Engine Technologies engineers and manufactures gasoline powered outdoor power equipment for the North American retail market.
- Midwest Air Technologies provides fencing products and lawn and garden accessories to the hardware and home center market.
- MAT Logistics provides logistic solutions to companies selling through retail channels and maintains 7 distribution centers with over 2.1 million square feet of floor space across the U.S.
- MAT Holdings Inc. has averaged double digit revenue increases over the past 5 years through organic initiatives and acquisitions.
- The company plans to drive organic growth and profitability in the consumer group through shared activities across operating companies, new product development, and distribution services.
- Auto Group results will be driven by synergies across the Friction Group and aftermarket opportunities in Mexico, Europe, and the US.
- New auto platform sales are expected to drive OE/OEM/OES growth for the MAT Foundry and Friction Groups.
- Recent acquisitions contributing to synergies and sales growth include Mincer HD, Prazisionstechnik Resources Altenburg (PTR), MAT Friction Noyon, and MAT Commercial Vehicle Products.
- Steve Wang serves as the CEO of MAT Holdings Inc.
- On May 19, 2022, MAT Auto Group supported Valeo Service Africa & Overseas by delivering products from its Roulund Braking Division in India to reduce environmental impact.
- On April 26, 2022, brake manufacturing at a UK location began undergoing a digital transformation using Artificial Intelligence to improve production visibility and resolve defects.
