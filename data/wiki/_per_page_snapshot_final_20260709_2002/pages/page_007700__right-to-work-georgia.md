# Right to Work Georgia

## Facts

- Three companies that are investing in Right to Work Georgia are Intuitive, Pratt Industries, and PureCycle Technologies. These will create 1,310 jobs!
- Four companies are expanding in Right to Work Georgia. These include Purple Innovation and ASOS as well as InFlex, and Woodgrain.
- Three companies that are locating to Right to Work Georgia include Freshly, Semperit Group, and Plug Power. These will create 759 new jobs!
- Three businesses bringing new location to Right to Work Georgia include Brightmark, Duckyang, and IC Biomedical. These will create new jobs all over the state!
- FanDuel Group, Domtar Corporation, Heliox, and Wittek Industries are all investing in Right to Work Georgia, creating a total of 1,095 new jobs!
- Three companies that are investing in Right to Work Georgia include Interfor, WebstaurantStore, and Amazon. Areas include Perry and Savannah.
- Three businesses that are adding new locations in Right to Work Georgia include GreyOrange, Ammeraal Beltech, and Bimbo QSR.
- There are three businesses currently working on adding new locations in Georgia. These include PAC Worldwide, GCP Applied Technologies, and Feit Electric.
- Dorel Sports, Danimer Scientific, and Sailfish Boats are all investing in Right to Work Georgia.
