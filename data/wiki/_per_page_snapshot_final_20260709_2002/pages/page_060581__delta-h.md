## Facts

- A leading tier 1 automotive supplier recently placed a large order for eight (8) custom designed walk-in ovens for aluminum aging of automotive parts with furnace manufacturer DELTA H.
- The ovens will be qualified at class 2 (+/-10°F).
- The ovens feature all necessary performance requirements for compliance to CQI-9 and AMS2750E specifications.
- DELTA H collaborated with the customer for 6 months on the design and approval phase.
- All 8 ovens will be commissioned in late summer 2020.
