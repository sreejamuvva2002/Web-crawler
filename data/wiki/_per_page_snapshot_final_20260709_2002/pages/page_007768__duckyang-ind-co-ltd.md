# Duckyang Ind. Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-01-19

**Source:** [Winner of the $ 700 Million Export Tower Award – Korean-Machinery.com](https://korean-machinery.com/tag/winner-of-the-700-million-export-tower-award/)  ·  `page_007768`

## Facts

- Duckyang Ind. Co., Ltd. was established in 1977.
- Its headquarters and main plant are located in Ulsan, which is considered to be the main industrial city of Korea.
- Its second/third plants are located in Gyeongju and Yesan.
- Duckyang’s sales amount is approximately $11 billion per year.
- The company started its business by supplying crash pads, a plastic item covering the front automotive part on the dashboard for Hyundai Motor Company’s first-ever car, the Pony.
- The Pony was the original model leading up to Hyundai Motor’s latest electric car, IONIQ 5.
- Duckyang is manufacturing mainly cockpit modules, door trims, and crash pads, which are automotive interior components.
- Among these, the cockpit module is the company’s best sales item.
- The cockpit module is assembled to accommodate audio, air conditioner, airbag, etc., for a total of approximately 130 items in the front automotive parts around the driver’s seat.
- This assembly process is known as ‘Modulization.’
- Duckyang is supplying these items for most Hyundai Motor’s models manufactured in Ulsan Plant, Korea.
- Duckyang contributes to Korea automotive export industry by supplying interior parts to exported Hyundai Motor’s models, known as indirect export.
- Duckyang sought new business opportunities due to paradigm trend changes in the auto industrial from ICE cars to EVs.
- Duckyang succeeded in developing components for EVs, including BMA (Battery Module Assembly) and ESS (Energy Storage System).
- Regarding BMA, Duckyang was awarded as an R&D national organization hosted by KIAT (Korea Institute for Advancement of Technology).
- Duckyang’s 2020 year exports increased by 4% compared with the 2019 year export amount despite challenging conditions caused by COVID-19.
- With increasing demand for EVs, Duckyang expects export amounts to increase further.
- Duckyang has a separate R&D department, and its R&D facilities are located in Suwon and Gyeongju.
- The company successfully focused on decreasing the weight of the cockpit module through R&D.
- Duckyang constantly seeks to ensure top-class quality.
- Duckyang received the 2021 National Quality Competitiveness Excellent Corporation award from the Ministry of Trade, Industry and energy for eight consecutive years.
- Duckyang is establishing Duckyang America in the state of Georgia, USA, with SK Innovation for BMA and ESS business.
