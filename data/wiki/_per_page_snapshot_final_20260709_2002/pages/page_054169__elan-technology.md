# Elan Technology

## Facts

- Elan Technology’s manufacturing facility was constructed in 1997.
- The facility has a 90,000 sq.ft. footprint located on 22 acres.
- The facility is based in Midway, GA.
- Since 1998, the company has been registered under the QS-9000 quality standard.
- The company has transitioned its quality system to ISO 9001:2015.
- Elan Technology is one of the largest technical glass manufacturers in the U.S.
- The company produces glass preforms from hundreds of proprietary electronic glass compositions.
- The glass preforms are commonly matched for glass-to-metal hermetic seals.
- Glass materials are also offered as milled or spray dried powders.
- Products are supplied to industries including the medical, defense, aerospace, lighting, automotive, and micro-electronics fields.
- The company provides engineering support to assist customers with material selection and preform design.
- Elan Technology has the capability to develop custom glass material compositions for specific customer applications.
- As a leading ceramic manufacturer with nearly 80 years of experience, the company offers materials such as alumina ceramic, steatite, zirconia, and cordierite.
- Ceramic materials are used for wear-resistant parts, insulators, and ceramic inserts for industrial applications.
- The company provides full engineering support for material selection and component design for ceramics.
- Elan Technology developed a special glass ceramic composite material adopted by customers as a replacement for traditional ceramics in certain applications.
- The Elan 46 ceramic glass material eliminates the need for brazing and lowers manufacturing costs while providing proven performance.
- The Elan 46 glass has a coefficient of thermal expansion from 110 to 220 (0°-300° × 10^-7 cm/cm/°C).
- The Elan 46 glass can withstand metallizing or brazing up to 700°C.
- The company provides contract spray drying services for applications such as catalysts, pigments, detergents, and ceramics.
- The company processes millions of pounds of material annually through its spray drying services.
- Experienced engineers work with customers to optimize the spray drying process for highest product quality.
- Spray drying services support both small developmental projects and large volume production.
- Storage, mixing, packaging, and atomization options are offered to customize processes to meet requirements.
- The company offers full-service rotary calciner toll processing services utilizing highly-effective thermal processing treatments.
- Toll processing services include calcination, thermal desorption, and drying for commonly calcined materials like catalysts, clay, dolomite, silica, and more.
- Elan Technology maintains a complete precise machine shop to support tool and die fabrication for all of its presses.
- The company's machining experts provide expert machining services to other industries.
- Machining capabilities cover most materials, including all tool steels, aluminum, ceramic, carbide, and plastic.
- Origin Glass is a division of Elan Technology that produces high-quality borosilicate glass for artistic applications.
- Origin Glass offers a wide range of brilliant colors used by hobbyists and professional artists.
- Boro Stix products are available from distributors located throughout North America and around the globe.
