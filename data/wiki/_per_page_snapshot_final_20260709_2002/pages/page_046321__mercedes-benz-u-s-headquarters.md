# Mercedes-Benz U.S. Headquarters

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Mercedes-Benz USA Headquarters - MillerClapperton](https://millerclapperton.com/project/mercedes-benz-usa-headquarters/)  ·  `page_046321`

## Facts

- Known as MB1, Mercedes-Benz U.S. Headquarters is located on 12 acres in Sandy Springs, GA.
- The 200,000-square-foot building currently houses 800 employees.
- The headquarters will undergo an expansion to add an additional 500 people by August 2026.
- The building features a spacious atrium that divides it into North and South Work Bars.
- The building includes open-plan office spaces with floor-to-ceiling windows.
- The building amenities include a conference center, coffee bar, restaurant-quality cafe, and fitness center.
- The building was designed to LEED Silver standards.
- The building is designed to operate at least 13% more efficiently than a standard code-compliant building.
- The building uses 50% less irrigation water than standard turf landscaping.
- The building uses 35% less water than a standard office.
- MillerClapperton fabricated and installed Metal Composite Material (MCM) panels, Sunshades, Screenwalls, and Profiled Single-Skin panels for the project.
- Approximately 9,000 square feet of MCM panels are featured at the entrance as the backdrop to the Mercedes-Benz logo.
- The entire entry is shaded with more than 4,000 square feet of Sunshades.
- Screenwalls offer a unique element to tie in the parking garage.
