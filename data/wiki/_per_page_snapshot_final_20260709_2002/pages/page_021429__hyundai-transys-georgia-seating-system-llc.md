# Hyundai-Transys Georgia Seating System, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-04

**Source:** [Inspire Academy students tour Hyundai-Transys](https://www.valleytimes-news.com/2022/04/inspire-academy-students-tour-hyundai-transys/)  ·  `page_021429`

## Facts

- A group of students and staff from Inspire Academy visited the Hyundai-Transys Georgia Seating System, LLC on Wednesday.
- Human Resources Director Robert Carlson led the tour of the Hyundai-Transys plant in West Point.
- Hyundai-Transys Georgia Seating System, LLC is a specialized car seat manufacturer that uses eco-friendly materials to satisfy customer needs.
- The company produces seats for automobiles including the Genesis series, K9, Grandeur (Azera), and Sonata.
- Job titles introduced to students included multi-craft maintenance technicians, production team members, quality team members, logistics team members, and foam team members.
- The plant employs over 600 workers running three different assembly lines on multiple shifts.
- Students viewed assembly line production and automated seat retrieval systems operated by robots.
- Robots are used to manufacture foam production at the facility.
- Chambers County School District Superintendent Casey Chambley emphasized developing local business partnerships to support students in pursuing meaningful careers after graduation.
- Dr. Travis Smith and UNITE, Inc provided funding for out-of-state travel for the Inspire Academy field trip.
- Inspire Academy Principal Dr. Tyler Nelson noted that students learned about internship opportunities offering work-based learning potential with Hyundai-Transys while reinforcing career technical education.
