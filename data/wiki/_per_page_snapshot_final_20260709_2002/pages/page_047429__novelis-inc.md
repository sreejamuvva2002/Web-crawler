## Facts

- Novelis Inc. produces and sells rolled aluminum products.
- The company operates through four segments: North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- It produces aluminum sheets and thin products primarily for use in beverage and food cans, as well as sheet products for the automotive, transportation, electronics, architecture, and industrial markets.
- The company is also involved in primary aluminum smelting and hydropower generation activities in Brazil.
- Novelis Inc. was founded in 2004.
- Its corporate headquarters is located in Atlanta, Georgia.
- Novelis Inc. is a subsidiary of AV Metals Inc.
