# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 2012-10-09

**Source:** [Superior Essex Announces the Grand Opening of the New Energy Manufacturing Facility in Tarboro, NC](https://www.cnbc.com/2012/10/09/superior-essex-announces-the-grand-opening-of-the-new-energy-manufacturing-facility-in-tarboro-nc.html)  ·  `page_010353`

## Facts

- Superior Essex Inc. announced the grand opening of its first North American energy cable manufacturing location.
- The grand opening event took place on October 9, 2012, at 11:30 a.m. EST.
- The new facility is located at 2901 Anaconda Road, Tarboro, NC 27886.
- The project resulted from a two-year collaborative effort between LS Cable & System, Superior Essex, and the city of Tarboro.
- The opening enables a significant increase in energy cable production to meet demand from commercial, industrial, and utility customers.
- The facility spans 250,000 square feet.
- The site includes a 125-foot tower specifically for producing medium voltage cables.
- Additional equipment at the facility covers a wide range of energy applications.
- LS Cable & System supplied the building design and cable technology for the facility.
- LS Cable & System is the parent company of Superior Essex and ranks among the top four global manufacturers in the energy cable market.
- The initial launch of the Superior Essex Energy cable portfolio occurred in February 2012.
- The initial product lineup included low voltage 300V and 600V instrumentation cables for control systems, audio, intercoms, energy management, and alarm controls.
- The initial lineup also featured low voltage 600V control cables for industrial, utility power, or station control circuits.
- The grand opening marks the company's expansion into medium voltage energy cables and larger low voltage cables for multiple markets and applications.
- Lindsay Allen, Vice President of Marketing for Superior Essex, stated the company aims to provide the energy cable market with high-quality products, world-class service, and overall solutions.
- Event hosts included Christopher Koo (Chairman of LS Cable & System), John Son (President and CEO of LS Cable & System), Stephen Carter (President and CEO of Superior Essex), and Lindsay Allen.
- Superior Essex Inc. manufactures a broad portfolio of wire and cable products for communications, magnet wire/winding wire, energy, and related distribution markets.
- The company is a leading manufacturer and supplier of copper and fiber optic communication wire and cable products to telephone companies, distributors, and system integrators.
- Superior Essex is a premier manufacturer and distributor of magnet wire/winding wire and fabricated insulation products to major OEMs for use in motors, transformers, generators, and electrical controls.
- The company distributes insulation and related products to smaller OEMs and motor repair facilities.
- Superior Essex offers low voltage, energy efficient cable products to commercial, industrial, and utility markets for control systems, audio, intercoms, energy management, and alarm controls.
