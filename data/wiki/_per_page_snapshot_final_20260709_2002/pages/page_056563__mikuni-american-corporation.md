## Facts

- The Mikuni Group manufactures superior products for a wide range of industries, including automotive, commercial vehicles, powersports, general purpose, household and safety, as well as export and sales of aerospace parts.
- Mikuni provides OEM parts for motorcycle, UTV, ATV, snowmobile, PWC, and outboard engine applications under its Powersports division.
- Mikuni provides OEM parts for passenger & cargo vehicles under its Automotive division.
- Mikuni provides OEM parts for commercial vehicles under its Commercial division.
- Mikuni offers performance carburetors, parts & accessories in the Aftermarket segment.
- Mikuni manufactures safety devices for gas appliances and water control valves.
- Mikuni provides OEM parts for general purpose engines.
- The company follows an Environmental Principle devoted to efforts towards a sustainable society, peaceful coexistence of humanity with the environment, and prosperity of future generations.
