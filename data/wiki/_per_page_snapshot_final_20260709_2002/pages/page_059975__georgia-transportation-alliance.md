# Georgia Transportation Alliance

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2024-11

**Source:** [Economic Development Expert Tapped to Lead Georgia Transportation Alliance - Rome CEO](https://romeceo.com/features/2024/11/economic-development-expert-tapped-lead-georgia-transportation-alliance/)  ·  `page_059975`

## Facts

- The Georgia Transportation Alliance is an affiliate of the Georgia Chamber of Commerce.
- Joseph Santoro was selected as the new Executive Director of the Georgia Transportation Alliance.
- Joseph Santoro starts his new position on December 6.
- Joseph Santoro comes to the Georgia Transportation Alliance after a successful four-year tenure with the Council for Quality Growth.
- Prior to joining the Georgia Transportation Alliance, Joseph Santoro worked as the Director of Policy & Government Affairs for the Council for Quality Growth.
- Over the last four years, Joseph Santoro worked on local, regional, and state policies related to infrastructure planning, legislative initiatives, regulatory issues, land use, transportation, and environmental planning impacting the development and growth industry.
- Joseph Santoro has over 10 years of government affairs experience working for membership-based organizations in metro Atlanta evaluating local, state, and federal policy priorities.
- Joseph Santoro sets forth recommended pathways to successfully advocate on behalf of Board leadership and membership.
- Joseph Santoro earned a B.S. in Public Policy from the Georgia Institute of Technology.
- Joseph Santoro has been a Georgia Chamber Government Affairs Committee member since 2014 through various roles.
- Joseph Santoro is a 2023 graduate of the Atlanta Regional Commission Regional Leadership Institute.
- Joseph Santoro and his wife Kayla live in the City of Atlanta where they recently purchased their first home.
- Nick Juliano is Partner at Impact Public Affairs and Chair of the Georgia Transportation Alliance.
- Nick Juliano stated that Georgia is at a critical moment for its transportation industry, particularly as it relates to freight and logistics.
- Chris Clark is CCE, President and CEO of the Georgia Chamber of Commerce.
- Chris Clark noted that Joseph Santoro’s broad experience working with statewide transportation stakeholders and his trusted economic development insight make him the ideal fit to serve as Executive Director of the Georgia Transportation Alliance.
- Chris Clark highlighted that with extensive surface transportation networks, two world-class ports, and one of the largest railroad systems in the country, Georgia’s transportation and logistics industry is an essential driver of record-breaking economic success.
- The Georgia Transportation Alliance is the state’s only organization focused solely on identifying and promoting long-term transportation solutions specifically connected to economic development and job creation.
- The Georgia Transportation Alliance works closely with the Georgia Chamber of Commerce, the Georgia Department of Transportation, and other key stakeholders in Georgia and Washington, D.C.
- The goal of the Georgia Transportation Alliance is ensuring an efficient and safe statewide multi-modal transportation system.
