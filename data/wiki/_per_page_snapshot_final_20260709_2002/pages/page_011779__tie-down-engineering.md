## Facts

- Tie Down Engineering is located at 605 Wheaton Drive, Atlanta, GA 30336, United States.
- The company is undertaking a "new" 160k facility expansion.
- The expansion features the only 2MM pound plus automated sheet/plate steel handling system in Georgia.
- The facility includes the latest high powered fiber laser metal processing systems from both Japan and Europe.
- Tie Down Engineering implemented the very first welding robot in Georgia back in 1981, which is not operable but held in storage for sentimental purposes.
- The new facility is about 50% complete and will be 100% full by Q2 2020.
- The company's whole campus in Fulton industrial park approaches 700k sqft.
- Tie Down Engineering holds over 90 patents and 7,000 products.
- Chuck Mackarvich serves as Chairman and Founder and continues to lead one of America’s leading contract and custom manufacturers.
