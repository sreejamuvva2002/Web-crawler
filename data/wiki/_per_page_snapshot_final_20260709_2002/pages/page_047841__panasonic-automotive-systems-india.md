## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems India (PASI) offers the development, manufacture, and sale of automotive components and parts for four-wheelers and two-wheelers.
- PASI's product portfolio includes infotainment solutions (audio, video, and navigation), head-up displays (HUDs), cameras, sensors, relays, parking assistance systems, telematics control units, smart entry systems, LED solutions for headlamps, and batteries.
- PASI is a subsidiary of Panasonic Automotive Systems Co. Ltd, a global leader in advanced automotive solutions that support safe driving and provide optimal in-vehicle systems for environmentally friendly vehicles.
- PASI focuses on developing, manufacturing, and selling automotive components such as infotainment solutions, sensors, relays, cameras, parking assistance systems, telematics control units, smart entry systems, LED headlamp solutions, and batteries.
- PASI offers technologies including Head-up Displays (HUD), Telematic Control Units (TCU), Temperature Oil Sensor, and in-car infotainment across global markets to advance India’s automotive industry.
- Bhupesh Kanyal serves as the Director of Panasonic Automotive Systems India (PASI).
- According to Bhupesh Kanyal, the Indian motor vehicle industry is the fourth largest in the world and continues to grow rapidly.
- India is the largest manufacturer in the two-wheeler segment.
- Strong economic growth and rising household incomes in India present significant growth potential for the automotive industry.
- The advent of new-age technologies and newer mobility forms, such as Electric Vehicles, is evolving consumer aspirations.
- Indian consumers are increasingly seeking premium commuting experiences along with passenger and environmental safety.
- This shift has resulted in demand for an improved passenger vehicle experience.
- Panasonic is transforming the in-car experience by harnessing new technologies including Connected Devices, Head-Up Displays, Wireless Chargers, and 360° Camera Solutions.
- These new technologies are designed to be simple to use, safe, durable, and cost-effective.
- PASI is currently catering to the top OEMs of the automotive industry through its wide range of products and solutions.
- PASI aims to expand its product gamut to serve more customers in the automotive sector.
