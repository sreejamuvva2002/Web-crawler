## Facts

- Incorporated in 1968, DeKalb Tool & Die, Inc. specializes in the design, construction, and repair of a wide range of metalworking tools.
- The company produces progressive dies, transfer dies, draw dies or single hit tooling.
- Mechanical presses, hydraulic presses, and double action presses from 60 to 1800 tons are available at the facility.
- The facility has coil feed capability up to 60 inches wide for producing stamped metal parts.
- The company's engineering staff handles CAD design for tooling projects.
- DeKalb Tool & Die delivers critical tooling requirements on one of its company trucks.
- DeKalb Tool & Die was recently awarded Blue Bird Corporation’s Global Supplier of the Year award.
- The manufacturing facility is located at 2220 Stephens St., Tucker, Georgia, United States ZIP: 30084.
- The Tucker, Georgia location is convenient to both the Atlanta International Airport and the Atlanta Perimeter Highway.
- The company offers personal tours of its tooling and manufacturing facility for visitors in the Atlanta area.
- DeKalb Tool & Die provides services including designing and building new tooling, modification and repair of existing tooling, or emergency metal stamping.
