# Rivian (NASDAQ: RIVN)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-09

**Source:** [Rivian Begins R2 Public Customer Deliveries, Opens Orders for Reservation Holders](https://rivian.com/newsroom/article/rivian-begins-r2-public-customer-deliveries)  ·  `page_000157`

## Facts

- Rivian announced the first public customer deliveries of the all-new R2 mid-size SUV on June 9, 2026.
- R2 vehicles have been rolling off the production line at Rivian's Normal, Illinois manufacturing facility as employees started taking delivery in April 2026.
- Rivian is extending invitations to order for existing R2 reservation holders on a rolling basis starting June 9, 2026.
- The R2 mid-size electric SUV was first unveiled on March 7, 2024, in Laguna Beach, California.
- Deliveries begin with R2 Performance with Launch Package starting at $57,990.
- R2 Premium configurations will follow in late 2026.
- Multiple R2 Standard configurations will be available through 2027.
- RJ Scaringe serves as the Founder and CEO of Rivian.
- R2 entered an intensive multi-year development and validation program following its unveiling.
- Every R2 in Rivian's development fleet accumulated tens of thousands of miles from coast to coast during testing.
- Engineering teams validated R2 in temperatures ranging from -45°F (-43°C) to 122°F (50°C) across challenging terrains.
- R2 is built on an all-new mid-size platform engineered for a more accessible price point without compromising capability.
- R2 is nearly 2,000 lbs lighter than the R1 and sits on a shorter 115.6-inch wheelbase.
- R2 features 9.6 inches of class-leading ground clearance, a 25° approach angle, and a 26° departure angle.
- R2 interior features 40.9 inches of front headroom, 40.4 inches of rear legroom, and a 40/20/40 folding rear seat.
- R2 offers 90.1 cubic feet of total storage with seats folded down.
- R2 reservation holders will receive order invitations in rolling batches based on reservation date and delivery location.
- Current Rivian owners will receive accelerated delivery timing balanced with non-owners to keep the production line moving.
- Customers with expiring leases can enter lease information in their Rivian account for consideration.
- By the end of June 2026, all reservation holders will receive an estimate of when to expect their order invitation.
- The first vehicle available to order is R2 Performance with Launch Package starting at $57,990.
- R2 Performance with Launch Package includes Autonomy+ featuring Universal Hands-Free assisted driving across 3.5 million miles of roads in the U.S. and Canada.
- R2 Performance with Launch Package features dual-motor all-wheel drive producing 656 horsepower and 609 lb-ft of torque.
- R2 Performance with Launch Package accelerates from 0–60 mph in as quick as 3.6 seconds.
- R2 Performance with Launch Package has an EPA-estimated range of up to 330 miles.
- R2 Performance trim includes semi-active suspension unique to that trim.
- R2 offers an exclusive paid upgrade for a Launch Green exterior color and a special Rivian Green anodized key fob.
- R2 includes a Tow Package with an integrated 2-inch hitch receiver and 4,400 pounds of towing capacity.
- R2 Premium trim will arrive in late 2026 starting at $53,990.
- R2 Standard trim begins in early 2027 with RWD Long Range starting at $48,490.
- An additional R2 Standard version will arrive in Summer 2027 starting at $44,990.
- Once an order is confirmed, customers can take delivery within 2–6 weeks.
- R2 marks an evolution in software-defined vehicles, pairing an AI-ready architecture with 5G connectivity.
- R2 features the most powerful infotainment compute in a consumer vehicle in North America and tactile haptic controls.
- R2's multi-modal perception stack includes 11 HDR cameras totaling 65MP and a five-radar system.
- Every R2 Performance with Launch Package includes Autonomy+ ($2,500 value) with future features added at no extra cost.
- For all other trims, Autonomy+ hardware comes standard with an included 60-day trial, after which the service is optional at $49.99/month or a $2,500 one-time fee.
- Autonomy+ brings L2+ hands-free assisted driving to 3.5 million miles of roads across the U.S. and Canada via Universal Hands-Free.
- A limited rollout of Point-to-Point driving will come to R2 and R1 Gen 2 fleet later in 2026, followed by a widespread rollout in 2027.
- R2 is outfitted with 200 sparse TOPS of edge AI compute dedicated to the in-cabin experience.
- Rivian Assistant, an in-vehicle voice assistant, will launch on R2 later in summer 2026.
- R2 features a redesigned steering wheel with haptic 'halo' dials designed in-house by Rivian.
- R2 offers two digital displays: a driver display and a center display for navigation and infotainment.
- R2 is built on an all-new, dedicated line at Rivian's Normal, Illinois manufacturing facility, which also houses the R1S and R1T.
- The R2 program supports thousands of jobs in Central Illinois.
- Rivian is constructing a second manufacturing facility in Stanton Springs, Georgia.
- The Stanton Springs, Georgia facility is expected to begin assembling vehicles in late 2028.
- In its initial phase, the Stanton Springs facility will provide up to 300,000 units annually in new capacity for R2, an upcoming robotaxi platform, and future models/variants.
- R2 reservations remain open with a $100 refundable deposit.
