## Facts

- U.S. Senator Reverend Raphael Warnock released a statement regarding the announcement that SK Battery America is laying off over 900 workers at its Commerce, Georgia plant.
- Warnock expressed sympathy for affected workers and emphasized the importance of these jobs in making Georgia a leader in advanced manufacturing.
- In his statement, Warnock criticized the current administration for prioritizing politics over people and urged for the protection of clean energy investments that create jobs.
- He referred to these layoffs as resulting from increasing manufacturing costs due to harmful tariffs.
- Warnock highlighted his ongoing efforts to support clean energy policies and domestic manufacturing.
- He mentioned that historic policies he helped pass have led to job growth in Georgia's clean energy sector, including significant electrification initiatives and tax credits for electric vehicles.
