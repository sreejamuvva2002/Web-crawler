# WIKA USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2021-05-20

**Source:** [WIKA USA Announces Exciting New Product Feature - WIKA USA](https://www.wika.com/en-us/pr_2101_en_us.WIKA)  ·  `page_015033`

## Facts

- WIKA USA is located in Lawrenceville, GA.
- WIKA announced a new FlexWindow™ option for select pressure gauges.
- The FlexWindow™ offers a versatile alternative to the traditional pressure gauge vent plug.
- Liquid-filled gauges are excellent solutions for applications with vibration, pulsation, shock, exposure to the elements, and other adverse conditions.
- Traditional liquid-filled gauges are susceptible to internal pressure build-up, requiring pressure compensation via a manually operated vent valve.
- Traditional liquid-filled gauges also have a large air bubble that can impair operators’ ability to accurately read the gauge.
- Hardy Orzikowski, Senior Product Manager at WIKA, stated that these challenges led to the development of the new FlexWindow™ option for select WIKA pressure gauges.
- WIKA’s patented FlexWindow compensates for pressure build-up due to changes in ambient temperature.
- The innovative new gauge window is made with liquid silicone rubber (LSR).
- The FlexWindow™ offers several advantages over pressure gauges with the traditional polycarbonate window and vent plug.
- The new option is currently available on the 2-1/2” version of models 213.53, 233.53, 233.54, and 233.55.
- WIKA plans to expand availability of the FlexWindow™ option in the future.
- For 75 years, WIKA has continuously advanced instrumentation for pressure, temperature, level, and flow measurement.
- WIKA leverages decades of experience to develop a broad selection of stock and custom instrumentation as well as engineering services to support plant safety, productivity, and profitability.
- WIKA is a global leader in lean manufacturing with manufacturing locations around the globe, which enables flexibility and the ability to meet high delivery demands.
