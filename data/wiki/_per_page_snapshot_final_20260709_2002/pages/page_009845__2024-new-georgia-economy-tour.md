# 2024 New Georgia Economy Tour

**Type:** event  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2024-06-05

**Source:** [Tomorrow in Savannah: Georgia Chamber’s New Georgia Economy Tour Focuses on Future Growth - Savannah Chamber](https://www.savannahchamber.com/news-and-events/news/chamber-news/tomorrow-in-savannah-georgia-chambers-new-georgia-economy-tour-focuses-on-future-growth/)  ·  `page_009845`

## Facts

- The Georgia Chamber is hosting a series of events known collectively as the 2024 New Georgia Economy Tour.
- The tour will gather input and data from every state region to prepare a comprehensive planning strategy for 2025 and beyond.
- The Savannah stop of the tour will be Tomorrow, June 5, from 7:30 – 9:15 a.m. at the Georgia Southern University’s Armstrong Campus Ballroom, located at 11935 Abercorn Street, Savannah.
- Georgia Chamber President and CEO Chris Clark stated the goal with the Tour is to listen and collect information from hardworking Georgians that will help create a strategic plan for the future of the state’s economy.
- Since 2020, the Georgia Chamber's strategic initiatives have focused on determining issues that will most impact competitiveness and affect Georgia’s ability to attract and maintain continued economic development success.
- The Georgia Chamber is shifting its focus to 2025 and beyond to garner input from local chamber partners to develop solutions and recommendations securing the future prosperity of Georgia.
- Savannah Chamber President and CEO Bert Brantley added that the New Georgia Economy tour provides valuable information to assist the Savannah business community in planning for the future.
- The Savannah Chamber looks forward to working with the Georgia Chamber to host this event and offer feedback from the community on how Georgia can best move forward.
