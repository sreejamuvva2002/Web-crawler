# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2020-12-07

**Source:** [Porsche launches parking app to help make day-to-day life just a little bit easier | Taycan Forum](https://www.taycanevforum.com/threads/porsche-launches-parking-app-to-help-make-day-to-day-life-just-a-little-bit-easier.2296/)  ·  `page_048831`

## Facts

- PCNA is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, since 1998.
- PCNA serves as the exclusive U.S. importer for the Porsche Taycan electric vehicle, alongside the 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, and Panamera.
- PCNA supports 192 independently owned and operated Porsche dealerships across the United States by supplying parts, service, marketing, and training.
- PCNA operates the first Porsche Experience Center in North America located in Atlanta, which features a module-based 1.6-mile driver development track, a business center, and fine dining restaurant 356.
- PCNA also operates a second Porsche Experience Center near Los Angeles situated on a 53-acre complex containing an eight-module, 4.1-mile driver development track, a business center, and Restaurant 917.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Kjell Gruner holds the title of President & CEO of PCNA, where he oversees operations for both the United States and Canada.
- Kjell Gruner also heads the executive leadership team of Porsche Digital, Inc., the sports car manufacturer's digital subsidiary in the U.S.
- PCNA introduced the Porsche Parking Plus app on Dec. 7, 2020, making it the second market to launch the application after Porsche AG began offering it earlier that year.
- The Porsche Parking Plus app includes search functionality that allows users to filter parking facility features for electric vehicle charging.
- The technology platform powering the Porsche Parking Plus app is managed by the Chicago-based company Arrive.
