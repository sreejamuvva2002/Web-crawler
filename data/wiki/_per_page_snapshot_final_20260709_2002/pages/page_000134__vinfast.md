# VinFast

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-07-18

**Source:** [VINFAST TO HOLD NORTH CAROLINA EV FACTORY GROUNDBREAKING CEREMONY JULY 28](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/vinfast-to-hold-north-carolina-ev-factory-groundbreaking-ceremony-july-28-301880466.html)  ·  `page_000134`

## Facts

- VinFast announced it will hold the groundbreaking ceremony of its electric vehicle factory in North Carolina on July 28.
- This will be the first electric vehicle manufacturing facility in North Carolina.
- The facility will help contribute to the supply of electric vehicles in North America and accelerate the global green mobility revolution.
- VinFast's manufacturing facility will be built at the Triangle Innovation Point in Chatham County, North Carolina.
- The site covers an area of approximately 1,800 acres.
- The factory is designed to reach a capacity of 150,000 vehicles per year in phase 1.
- The factory will consist of two main areas: electric vehicle production and assembly.
- The complex will also house supplementary supplier businesses.
- The project has received basic permits to begin Phase 1 construction.
- When the manufacturing complex commences operations, it will create an ecosystem of suppliers and help generate thousands of new jobs.
- Madam Le Thi Thu Thuy, CEO of VinFast Auto, stated that the North Carolina manufacturing facility is one of VinFast's key projects.
- When it begins operations, the factory will be VinFast's primary supplier of electric vehicles to the North American market.
- The facility will allow optimization of production and business activities.
- VinFast hopes the construction in Chatham County will contribute to advancing the clean energy economy in the US and support North Carolina's green mobility strategy.
- The factory is expected to start production in 2025.
- Last year, VinFast was awarded a $1.2 billion incentive package from the State of North Carolina for this project.
- The project also received critical financial support from the City of Sanford, Chatham County, and the Golden Leaf Foundation.
- In addition to the factory, VinFast is accelerating its business and brand recognition in the U.S. by expanding its retail store and service center system.
- VinFast is organizing local test drives and displaying products events throughout California to aid customer experience of its EVs.
- VinFast is a member of Vingroup and was envisioned to drive the movement of global smart electric vehicle revolution.
- VinFast was established in 2017.
- VinFast owns a state-of-the-art automotive manufacturing complex with globally leading scalability that boasts up to 90% automation in Hai Phong, Vietnam.
