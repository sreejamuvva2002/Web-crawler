# Daechang Seat Corp. USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-23

**Source:** [Daechang Seat Corp. USA to Create 500 Jobs in Chatham County | Georgia](https://georgia.org/press-release/automotive-supplier-daechang-seat-corp-usa-create-500-jobs-chatham-county)  ·  `page_000048`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Daechang Seat Corp. USA, a supplier specializing in automotive seat frames, will invest $72.5 million in the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- The facility is located in close proximity to Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA).
- The project will create over 500 jobs.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that suppliers for the HMGMA project alone reach at least nine counties.
- Governor Brian Kemp highlighted that the news highlights the impact projects like the Hyundai Metaplant have beyond their initial investment.
- Daechang Seat Corp. (DSC) was established in 1979 in Kyungju, South Korea.
- DSC has become one of the largest seat frame producers in the world.
- Globally, DSC employs more than 5,000 people in seven countries.
- The company's production process includes fully automatic lines with arc welding and spot-welding machines.
- Jinsuk Lee serves as the CEO of Daechang Seat Savannah Corp.
- DSC takes pride in creating improved opportunities and employment prospects within Chatham County and the Savannah area.
- DSC maintains a steadfast partnership with the City of Savannah.
- DSC's primary objective is to drive growth through facility expansions, ultimately transforming into a global entity.
- DSC currently holds a prominent partnership with Hyundai Transys and Hyundai Mobis at HMGMA.
- DSC hopes to collaborate with even more projects in the future.
- DSC’s new facility will be located at the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- The company will hire for assembly operators, equipment technicians, mechanical engineers, and electrical engineers.
- Hiring will occur through local job fairs and website postings in the future.
- DSC's location is only minutes from the Port of Savannah.
- Griff Lynch, Georgia Ports Authority President and CEO, stated that DSC is ideally located to take advantage of Georgia’s world-class connectivity.
- The Georgia Ports Authority is focused on providing the best global gateway for U.S. supply chains.
- Trip Tollison is the Savannah Economic Development Authority President and CEO.
- Daechang Seat Corp. USA will be the third Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America supplier to locate in the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- The project will bring 500 new jobs and $72.5 million in capital investment to Chatham County.
- Trip Tollison expressed excitement about the announcement.
- Daechang Seat Corp. USA is recognized as a well-established global company.
- The Savannah Economic Development Authority looks forward to working with DSC as it grows and becomes part of the community.
- Alyce Thornhill is the Director of Project Implementation and Supplier Strategy at the GDEcD's Global Commerce team.
- The project was developed in partnership with the Savannah Economic Development Authority, Georgia Ports Authority, Georgia Power, and Georgia Quick Start.
- Pat Wilson is the GDEcD Commissioner.
- Countless partners are helping transform Georgia into a center of the automotive and mobility industry.
- Pat Wilson expressed excitement about welcoming DSC in Savannah.
- Savannah is an established global gateway.
- Chatham County has leveraged its gateway advantage to create a bustling community for living and working.
- As the market transitions to electric vehicles, Georgia has pursued its entire EV supply chain.
- Georgia has created more than $25 billion in investments and 30,000 jobs since 2018.
- In fiscal year 2023, job creation in Georgia's automotive industry increased by 324 percent compared to FY21.
- Suppliers attracted by Georgia’s mobility original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) resulted in over $2 billion in investment across the state in FY23.
- DSC is recognized for its quality and technological expertise in the automotive seat sector.
- DSC aims to be a top-tier company that respects humanity, leveraging its accumulated technology and customer trust.
- DSC has recently ventured into the electric vehicle sector.
- DSC continues to expand its scope.
