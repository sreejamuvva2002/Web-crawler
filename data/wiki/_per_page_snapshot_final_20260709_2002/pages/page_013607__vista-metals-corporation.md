# Vista Metals Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-03-20

**Source:** [Vista Metals Bringing Jobs To Bowling Green](https://www.wvih.com/wp1/index.php/2024/03/20/vista-metals-bringing-jobs-to-bowling-green/)  ·  `page_013607`

## Facts

- Governor Andy Beshear congratulated Vista Metals Tuesday as Kentucky’s metals industry continues to grow at a rapid pace.
- Vista Metals plans to locate a new specialty aluminum products operation in Bowling Green, creating over 50 full-time jobs behind an approximate $60 million investment.
- Initial site preparation is already underway, and Vista Metals will commence with a phased build-out to support the growth in its core markets.
- As part of this project, Vista Metals plans to acquire more than 100 acres and the construction of a 75,000-plus-square-foot facility.
- Jobs created at the facility will include operations, planning, engineering and site management.
- VMC Specialty Alloys LLC (dba Vista Metals Georgia) operates under a licensing agreement with Fontana, California-based Vista Metals Corporation.
- Along with Vista Progressive Metals LLC (a producer of aluminum lithium products), the group of companies are market leaders in the manufacturing of specialty aluminum products supporting aerospace, commercial aircraft, defense, automotive and general industrial applications.
