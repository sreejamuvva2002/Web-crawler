# Enchem America Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-27

**Source:** [Enchem America Inc. Announces $152.5 Million Investment Project in Tennessee - Tennessee Star](https://tennesseestar.com/economy/enchem-america-inc-announces-152-5-million-investment-project-in-tennessee/khousler/2023/06/28/)  ·  `page_007975`

## Facts

- Enchem America Inc. officials announced the company will invest $152.5 million to establish electrolyte manufacturing operations in Haywood County, Tennessee.
- The Tennessee Department of Economic and Community Development (TNECD) categorizes Haywood County as an “economically distressed” county.
- Brownsville, Tennessee will be the site of Enchem America Inc.'s second U.S. facility.
- The new facility will be located at the I-40 Advantage Industrial Park in Brownsville.
- The electrolyte manufacturer is expected to create 190 new jobs and invest $150M+ in Haywood County.
- Enchem America Inc. is a subsidiary of South Korea-based Enchem Co., Ltd.
- According to TNECD Commissioner Stuart McWhorter, South Korea is one of Tennessee’s top 10 foreign direct investment partners.
- The new facility will manufacture electrolytes for electric vehicle (EV) batteries as well as provide other EV battery-related services.
- Jung Kang Oh, CEO and founder of Enchem Co. Ltd., expressed gratitude to Haywood County, the City of Brownsville, and professionals who assisted with the expansion.
- Jung Kang Oh noted that the growth of the EV market has been remarkable and expects it to continue at an accelerated pace over the coming decade.
- Jung Kang Oh stated confidence that the company is well positioned to capitalize on EV market growth and looks forward to exploring innovation and partnership opportunities with Haywood County and the City of Brownsville.
- TNECD categorizes Haywood County as a Tier-4, At-Risk, economically distressed county, which is one ranking away from being classified as a distressed county (the worst rating).
- Data by TNECD shows Haywood County’s poverty rate is 20.7 percent.
- The three-year average unemployment rate in Haywood County stands at 6.4 percent.
- Haywood County’s per capita market income is $25,529.
