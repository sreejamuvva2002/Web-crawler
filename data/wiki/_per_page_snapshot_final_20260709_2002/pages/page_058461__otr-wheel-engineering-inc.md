# OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc.

## Facts

- OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc. operates a principal engineering center in Rome, GA, USA.
- The company maintains regional offices in Omaha, NE, USA; Sault Ste. Marie, Canada; Wuxi, China; and Colombo, Sri Lanka.
- OTR provides CREA parametric modeling software platforms that support legacy applications including SolidWorks, AutoCAD, and Inventor.
- The company conducts in-house product testing for tire deflection, static loaded radius, and other tire characteristics, along with test tracks for tread wear analysis on real-world surface conditions.
- Additional product testing is conducted by third-party partners.
- OTR offers core design expertise covering total systems solutions for specialty tires, wheels, hubs, assemblies, and associated products ranging from 8” (203.2 mm) to 63” (1,600.2 mm) in diameter for off-the-road applications.
- The company utilizes advanced tools including FEA simulations, laser scanning for benchmarking and design verification, and 3D printing for new concept evaluations.
- OTR develops global sourcing strategies to support OEM product launch and commercialization needs, supplying tires, wheels, assemblies, accessories, and mounting programs locally or globally.
- The company manufactures or sources wheels, designs tires, develops supply chains, and provides turnkey solutions or à la carte services for OEMs.
- OTR acts as a Tier 1 supplier for many OEM partners, handling everything from material purchasing to final production of finished products.
- The company provides subassembly services that allow OEM customers to outsource larger portions of their unique equipment needs to its expert team.
- OTR offers warehousing support for OEM safety stock, consignment, or distributors, with strategically located facilities in the U.S., Canada, Europe, and Asia.
- Tire mounting services are available for OEMs covering high-volume and low-volume/high-mix programs, including tire band cutting, tires pressed on wheels after heating, and air pressure checked/filled using smart systems.
- All tire mounting services are approved by well-known global OEMs, with operations in North America, Europe, and Asia.
- Sequencing services configure and stage components/assemblies according to customer build schedules for maximum efficiency and cost abatement.
- JIT delivery services cover tire and wheel assemblies for high-volume and low-volume/high-mix programs, with operations in North America, Europe, and Asia.
- OTR offers a Tire/Wheel Exchange and Foam Fill program for qualified dealers servicing rental companies and end users requiring immediate replacements for damaged tires.
- The foam fill program removes old foam from returned tires and refills them using premium, OEM-approved resins and recycled foam called “crumb.”
- This foam fill program reduces costly downtime and maximizes customers’ ROI.
- Flat Proofing utilizes TyrLyner®, a proprietary permanent urethane material applied inside a tire’s casing to provide self-healing technology that seals the tire when punctured or cut.
- Wheel inspection and certification services are provided notably for mining vehicles, material handling vehicles, and gantry cranes used at ports.
- The recertification process for mining vehicles involves retrieving the wheel from the mine site, pressure washing the assembly, dismounting, registering documentation, inspecting the wheel, and either repainting/returning it or sending a report for repair/replacement/scrap.
- Material handling vehicle wheel inspection identifies wheels with metal fatigue in high-wear areas, determines age and in-service time, identifies manufacturers, and assesses duty cycle and configuration to determine if a wheel is under-engineered or mismatched for the application.
