# YKK AP America Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2024-05

**Source:** [YKK AP Holds Ribbon Cutting Ceremony for New Residential Manufacturing Facility in Macon - Middle Georgia CEO](https://middlegeorgiaceo.com/features/2024/05/ykk-ap-holds-ribbon-cutting-ceremony-new-residential-manufacturing-facility-macon/)  ·  `page_016189`

## Facts

- YKK AP America Inc. (YKK AP) held a ribbon cutting ceremony on Wednesday, May 1st, 2024.
- The event marks the official opening of its first greenfield residential manufacturing facility in Macon-Bibb County, Ga.
- Commissioner Pat Wilson of the Georgia Department of Economic Development attended the ceremony.
- Mayor Lester Miller of Macon-Bibb County attended the ceremony.
- Chairman Robby Fountain of the Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority attended the ceremony.
- The facility completed construction in December 2023.
- The facility began operations in January 2024.
- The facility currently employs more than 250 employees in the Macon community.
- Mayor Lester Miller stated that YKK AP’s expansion into a new, leading-edge facility is a testament to its strong connection with our community and marks the beginning of a new chapter offering more opportunities to enrich our economy, improve the lives of our families, and strengthen our position as a hub of innovation and industry.
- Oliver Stepe, President & CEO of YKK AP America Inc., stated that the ribbon cutting symbolizes growth and confidence in business and employees moving forward.
- Oliver Stepe expressed gratitude to the State of Georgia, the Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority, and partners for helping celebrate the milestone.
- The facility's purpose is to build a better society through architectural products.
