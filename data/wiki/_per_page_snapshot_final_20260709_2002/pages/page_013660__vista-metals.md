## Facts

- Founded in 1969, Vista Metals has established itself as a global leader in manufacturing high-strength, long-lasting, tungsten carbide preforms.
- The company maintains a company-wide commitment to quality and service, advanced facilities, and a highly-skilled workforce.
- Vista Metals provides preforms unsurpassed in excellence.
- The company's extensive experience and full range of cobalt grades give it the capability to produce a wide variety of high-quality, tungsten carbide preforms in virtually any desired hardness or density.
- Vista Metals also offers nickel-binder carbides that have corrosion resistance, non-magnetic properties, and high levels of wear strength advantages.
- Hundreds of companies globally have trusted Vista Metals to produce the parts that keep them operating.
- The company is located in Pennsylvania, United States.
- Vista Metals has 11-50 employees.
