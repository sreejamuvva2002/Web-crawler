# Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-06

**Source:** [E-Z-GO® LAUNCHES NEW VEHICLES -- FOR THE GOLF COURSE AND THE STREET](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/e-z-go-launches-new-vehicles----for-the-golf-course-and-the-street-301843889.html)  ·  `page_029527`

## Facts

- E-Z-GO introduced the Liberty LSV and updated the Freedom RXV on June 6, 2023.
- The Liberty LSV is a street-legal vehicle with a top speed of 25 mph that meets all National Highway Traffic Safety Administration standards for an LSV.
- The Liberty LSV can be operated on public roads with posted speed limits of 35 mph or less in most states.
- The Liberty LSV features four forward-facing seats, a compact wheelbase, a backup camera, operator protective structure, pedestrian warning system, seatbelts, and an optional infotainment system.
- The Liberty LSV adheres to FMVSS 500 and SAE J2358 standards.
- The Freedom RXV received a fresh new design for the 2024 model year featuring integrated LED headlights and a modernized ergonomic dash.
- The Freedom RXV is available in two- and four-passenger models, with the four-passenger version including a modular canopy with a water management system.
- Adam Harris, vice president of E-Z-GO for Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., stated: "These new models answer our customers' call for vehicles that are equally at home on the golf course and in the neighborhood."
- E-Z-GO vehicles are designed and manufactured by Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a subsidiary of Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT).
- The Liberty LSV is exclusively powered by Samsung SDI lithium batteries, which are part of E-Z-GO's ELiTE series.
- Samsung SDI lithium batteries charge up to twice as fast as traditional lead-acid batteries and require no maintenance.
- The Samsung SDI batteries in the Liberty LSV come with an 8-year warranty, described as the industry's longest.
- The Freedom RXV is available with either Samsung SDI lithium battery technology or E-Z-GO's exclusive EX1 gas powertrain.
- The EX1 gas powertrain is a first-of-its-kind engine purpose-built for golf-car applications to optimize fuel efficiency, featuring smart engine management that turns off accessories when not in use.
- Both the Liberty LSV and Freedom RXV are manufactured in Augusta, Ga., where E-Z-GO has operated for 69 years.
- E-Z-GO was founded in Augusta, Ga. in 1954 and became part of Textron Inc. in 1960.
- E-Z-GO is known for innovative sustainable electric-vehicle and powertrain technology, with models including RXV, TXT, Freedom, Valor, and Express series.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles integrates environmental performance into its business processes to reduce emissions.
