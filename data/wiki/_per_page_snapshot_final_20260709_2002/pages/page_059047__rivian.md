# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-09

**Source:** [2027 Rivian R2 First Drive: Smaller, Cheaper, Still a Rivian](https://www.motortrend.com/reviews/first-drive-2027-rivian-r2-performance)  ·  `page_059047`

## Facts

- Five years after its first vehicle drive, Rivian has delivered a new model.
- Rivian’s first two vehicles are the R1T electric truck and R1S SUV.
- The 2027 Rivian R2 Performance is smaller and cheaper than its predecessors.
- The R2 benchmarks against bestsellers like the Tesla Model Y, Toyota RAV4, and Subaru Outback.
- The R2 benchmarks against sport luxury models like the BMW X3.
- The R2 benchmarks against hardcore off-roaders like the Jeep Wrangler and Ford Bronco.
- Rivian expects to conquest sales from these benchmarked vehicles.
- Three variations of the R2 are planned for the next year.
- Options across trims are generally limited to colors, wheels, and software.
- The base R2 Standard starts in the mid-$40K range.
- The R2 Standard offers between 275 and 345 miles of range.
- The R2 Standard comes standard with a single rear motor producing 350 hp.
- The midgrade R2 Premium features a 400-volt, 87.9-kWh battery.
- The R2 Premium offers 330 miles of range.
- The R2 Premium includes dual-motor all-wheel drive.
- The R2 Premium provides 100 more horsepower than the base model.
- The R2 Premium includes slightly ritzier interior materials.
- The R2 Premium features a Toyota 4Runner–style lowerable rear glass.
- The R2 Performance trim is currently on sale.
- The R2 Performance produces 656 hp and 609 lb-ft of torque.
- The R2 Performance includes a semi-active electronically adaptive suspension.
- The R2 Performance features unique off-road drive modes.
- All-terrain tires are available for the R2 Performance.
- Installing all-terrain tires drops the R2 Performance's range from 330 to 307 miles.
- RAD-tuned versions of the R2 will hit the trails later.
- All early 2027 R2 Performance models include the Launch package.
- The Launch package includes Rivian’s Autonomy+ driver assist system, a tow hitch, and a physical key fob.
- The R2 Performance with the Launch package is priced at $59,485.
- The R2 is considered important to Rivian's bottom line.
- The EV marketplace is described as starved of real choice.
