# TI Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-15

**Source:** [ABC Technologies Completes Acquisition of TI Fluid Systems; Combined Business Will Rebrand as TI Automotive - ABC Technologies](https://www.abctechnologies.com/abc-technologies-completes-acquisition-of-ti-fluid-systems-combined-business-will-rebrand-as-ti-automotive/)  ·  `page_011590`

## Facts

- ABC Technologies completed its acquisition of TI Fluid Systems.
- The combined business will rebrand as TI Automotive.
- The transaction creates a stronger, more diversified Tier 1 manufacturer supplying market-leading safety and performance critical products and highly engineered components.
- TI Automotive will feature a propulsion agnostic portfolio ideally positioned to support a diverse range of customers.
- Combined pro forma revenue for 2024 is $5.4 billion.
- The combined entity employs 34,600 people across 26 countries.
- TI Automotive will be led by Terry Campbell as President & Chief Executive Officer.
- An Executive Leadership Team combining the skills and expertise of both ABC and TIFS will support Campbell.
- TI Automotive will be headquartered in Auburn Hills, Michigan.
- The business will be managed through a Regional Operating Model to ensure customer proximity and agility to respond rapidly to local market dynamics.
- Terry Campbell stated that the combination creates opportunities to drive innovation and manufacturing excellence while establishing foundations for future growth.
- TI Automotive is a Tier 1 supplier of safety and performance critical products and highly engineered components to the global automotive industry.
- The company has more than 100 years of experience solving customer safety, efficiency, and environmental challenges.
- TI Automotive possesses a powertrain agnostic product portfolio, vertical integration, and a global footprint.
- TI Automotive was created through ABC Technologies’ acquisition of TI Fluid Systems in April 2025.
- The company is majority owned by certain affiliated funds of Apollo Global Management, Inc. and its subsidiaries.
- Funds managed by Oaktree Capital Management, L.P. own a minority equity interest in the company.
