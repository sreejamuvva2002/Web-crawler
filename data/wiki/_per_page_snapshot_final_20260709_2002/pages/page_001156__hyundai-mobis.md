# Hyundai Mobis

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-06

**Source:** [Hyundai Mobis to invest $926 million for electric vehicle components plant in USA](https://evmagz.com/hyundai-mobis-to-invest-926-million-for-electric-vehicle-components-plant-in-usa/)  ·  `page_001156`

## Facts

- Hyundai Mobis invested US$926 million for an electric vehicle components factory in Bryan County in the US state of Georgia.
- The plant will begin construction in January 2023, with production due to start in 2024.
- Hyundai Motor Group, the parent company of Hyundai Mobis, recently started construction of its electric vehicle factory in the US.
- Hyundai Mobis follows a concept tried and tested by other manufacturers in the group by building its own components factory around Hyundai Motor Group's electric vehicle factory.
- The components produced will include various sensors for assisted or autonomous driving, as well as chassis, steering and braking systems, and electrification components.
- The components will supply not only the Hyundai electric car plant in Bryan County, but also the Hyundai plant in Montgomery, Alabama, and Kia’s plant in Georgia.
- H.S. Oh, Vice President of Hyundai Mobis Electric Powertrain Business Unit, stated that the investment project reflects an acceleration in the development of the EV supply chain in Georgia’s auto industry.
- H.S. Oh stated that Hyundai Mobis will be a major production player in the EV market and that this will trigger more growth within the sector.
- H.S. Oh noted that Mobis is looking forward to providing high-quality work opportunities for the growing local workforce.
- The Hyundai Mobis production facility in Georgia is expected to employ at least 1,500 people.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that the groundbreaking of Hyundai’s new electric vehicle and battery manufacturing facility in Bryan County would unleash transformational job creation and investment in that region of the state.
- Governor Kemp noted that they announced the second supplier to locate in the Bryan County area in just two weeks.
- The electric vehicle components produced by Hyundai Mobis include ICCUs, electric drive units, and battery systems including BMS.
- Georgia is generally positioned as the center of the electric mobility industry.
- Since 2020, more than 30 EV-related projects have contributed investment of more than $13.3 billion and announced more than 18,100 jobs in Georgia.
- On October 25, Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America officially debuted in Bryan County at its first dedicated EV and battery manufacturing facility.
- Offsite suppliers connected to the Hyundai Motor Group project are expected to invest approximately $1 billion in other locations throughout the state.
