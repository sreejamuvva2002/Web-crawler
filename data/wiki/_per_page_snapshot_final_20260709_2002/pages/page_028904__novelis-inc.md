# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-24

**Source:** [Novelis Releases 2025 Sustainability Report](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/novelis-releases-2025-sustainability-report-302566031.html)  ·  `page_028904`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. released its sustainability report for the 2025 fiscal year (April 1, 2024, to March 31, 2025) on September 24, 2025.
- The report outlines progress toward the company's 'Novelis 3x30' vision to advance aluminum as the material of choice with circular solutions.
- Novelis achieved a 10% reduction in greenhouse gas intensity in the last fiscal year across Scope 1, location-based Scope 2, and Scope 3 (categories 1 and 4).
- The company implemented new technologies in remelt furnaces to improve efficiency, reduce fuel and energy use, and increase the use of low-carbon primary aluminum inputs.
- Novelis recycled more than 84 billion used beverage cans during the reporting period.
- The company achieved a 63% average recycled content across all its products.
- Novelis reduced waste to landfill intensity by 12%.
- The company reduced water intensity by 8%.
- Novelis donated $8.8 million to the communities in which its employees work and live.
- The company aims to accelerate circularity and achieve 75% average recycled content in its products.
- Novelis targets becoming the lowest emissions flat rolled products (FRP) aluminum provider with less than 3 tonnes of CO2e per tonne of FRP shipped.
- Francisco Pires serves as SVP and Chief Strategy and Sustainability Officer, stating that the company aligns its business strategy with circular economy principles to deliver long-term value for customers, communities, and the planet.
- Novelis is a global leader in the production of innovative aluminum products and solutions and the world's largest recycler of aluminum.
- The company aims to be the leading provider of low-carbon, sustainable aluminum solutions and achieve a fully circular economy by partnering with suppliers and customers in the aerospace, automotive, beverage can, and specialties industries across North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- Novelis reported net sales of $17.1 billion in fiscal year 2025.
- Novelis Inc. is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited, an industry leader in aluminum and copper, and the metals flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group, a multinational conglomerate based in Mumbai.
