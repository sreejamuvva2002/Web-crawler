# Inalfa Roof Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Inalfa Roof Systems is a market leader in the design, engineering and production of automotive roof solutions.
- The company slogan is “Powered by Spirit, driven by Innovation”.
- Inalfa operates as a roof system solution enabler based on its know-how, production capacity and stable supply chain.
- The company collaborates with OEMs and non-automotive new entrants to enhance car interior functionality.
- A weight reduction trend in the automotive industry is driving demand for thinner glazing.
- 2.1//2.1 semi-tempered laminated glass is the current industry standard for laminated construction.
- Thickness reduction of glass results in a significant step in weight reduction.
- 1.6//1.6 semi-tempered thin laminated full encapsulated glass can reduce weight on glass and system level.
- Weight saving on glass level is 2.5kg/m² compared to 2.1//2.1 glass.
- The new glazing offers acoustic performance better than current 3.85 tempered glass.
- The technology is TRL5 ready (sellable).
- Inalfa partners with Saint-Gobain Sekurit for this glazing technology.
- Light transmission ranges from TL(6-13)%.
- Solar direct transmittance ranges from TE(4-12)%.
- There is no visible color difference compared to standard glass.
- The roof system will serve as a technology and innovation platform for the future.
- Inalfa will partner with OEMs (engineering/purchase/design/ADAS departments) and potential innovation partners including start-ups, tier 1’s, and tier 2’s.
- Contact details include an address at 5807 GW Venray (P.O. Box 505. 5800 AM Venray), The Netherlands.
