# PAI Industries

## Facts

- PAI Industries is a manufacturer and distributor of high-quality parts to the heavy-duty truck industry.
- PAI Industries has partnered with ECM to bring heat treatment in-house using an ECM NANO vacuum furnace system.
- The company operates a 112,000-square-foot manufacturing facility in Suwanee, Georgia.
- The facility already contains state-of-the-art machinery and previously relied on outsourced heat treating.
- The new in-house system expands PAI’s capabilities to expedite production and increase capacity.
- Transitioning from primarily outsourced heat treatment to in-house vacuum technology enables low-pressure carburizing and through-hardening 24/7.
- The ECM NANO system features modularity, an external loader, a three-position temper oven, and queuing positions.
- Vacuum technology benefits include no IGO, zero CO2 furnace emissions, no fire hazards or open flames, increased repeatability, and improved metallurgical results.
- Prior to project acceptance, testing was conducted at the ECM NANO located in the ECM USA Synergy Center in Pleasant Prairie, Wisconsin.
