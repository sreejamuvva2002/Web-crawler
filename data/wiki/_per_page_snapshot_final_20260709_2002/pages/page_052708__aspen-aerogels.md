## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels' Aerogel Technology Platform optimizes the performance and safety of electric vehicles and energy infrastructure assets.
- Global organizations have utilized Aspen’s engineering expertise and advanced aerogel technology for more than 20 years to help solve challenges in resource efficiency, electrification, and sustainability.
- Aspen's advanced insulation products now help to promote the adoption of electric vehicles, enhance energy efficiency and support the availability of cleaner fuels in traditional energy markets.
- The Aerogel Technology Platform is built on two decades of R&D investment.
- Aspen maintains a million square feet install base for its aerogel solutions.
- Solutions from Aspen are used by 25 of the largest refineries.
- Aspen Aerogels is deeply committed to producing quality insulation materials and making continuous improvements for all its customers across a range of applications and industries.
- Automated analyses of critical process parameters and production characterization within the onsite quality laboratory ensure control of process and product.
- Aspen Aerogels is ISO 9001:2015 certified (certificate No. USA/QMS/000222) for its Northborough, MA and East Providence, RI facilities.
- The scope of the ISO certification includes all major business processes including design, manufacturing, fabrication, quality, supply chain, research and development, administration, sales, and marketing.
- All Aspen suppliers are required to be ISO 9001 certified.
- Aspen cascades additional requirements, as appropriate, to its suppliers to ensure quality systems are intact and customers are protected.
- One of Aspen Aerogels' manufacturing facilities has officially achieved IATF 16949 certification according to the automotive-specific quality management standard.
- With the new certification, Aspen is well positioned as a reliable, committed, and quality-oriented automotive supplier for its varied automotive customer base.
