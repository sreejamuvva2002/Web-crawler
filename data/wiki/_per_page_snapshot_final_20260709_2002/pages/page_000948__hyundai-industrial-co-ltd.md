# Hyundai Industrial Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-11

**Source:** [Hyundai, Kia Supplier to Invest in Manufacturing Facility in Coweta - LaGrange CEO](https://lagrangeceo.com/news/2023/11/hyundai-kia-supplier-invest-manufacturing-facility-coweta-news/)  ·  `page_000948`

## Facts

- The investment and job creation were announced on Thursday, November 2nd, 2023.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Hyundai Industrial Co., Ltd. will create 100 new jobs.
- Hyundai Industrial Co., Ltd. will invest more than $24 million in a new manufacturing facility.
- The new manufacturing facility is located near Newnan in Coweta County.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that partners like Kia and Hyundai are attracting a large network of suppliers to Georgia.
- Suppliers have committed to bringing over 5,300 jobs to Georgia since last year's Hyundai Metaplant announcement.
- The over 5,300 committed jobs are mostly for communities outside of large urban areas.
- Hyundai Industrial was established in 1978.
- Hyundai Industrial is headquartered in Ulsan, Korea.
- The company specializes in manufacturing car seats, arm rests, and head rests.
- Hyundai Industrial has more than four decades of experience providing components for some of the world's biggest automotive brands.
- Hyundai Industrial has purchased an existing building located at 83 Amlajack Way in Coweta County.
- The purchased building will be retrofitted to produce arm and head rests for OEMs.
- One target OEM customer is Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.
- Hyundai Industrial will focus on filling manufacturing production roles.
- Open jobs will be posted online when available.
- Coweta County Board of Commissioners Chairman John Reidelbach welcomed Hyundai Industrial to the county.
- The facility will have close proximity to I-85.
- Proximity to I-85 will make transport to nearby automobile plants effortless.
- Director of Korean Investment Yoonie Kim represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team on the project.
- The project was completed in partnership with the Coweta County Development Authority and Georgia EMC.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson noted that Kia is located in West Georgia and Hyundai is on the eastern coast.
- Suppliers like Hyundai Industrial can locate almost anywhere in Georgia and still be near at least two major manufacturers.
- Companies can select communities that are the right fit for them, and vice versa.
- Georgia has positioned itself as a leader in developing and harnessing emerging technologies for the automotive and mobility industry.
- Since 2020, Georgia has created more than $25.1 billion in investments for the EV supply chain.
- Since 2020, Georgia has created 29,000 jobs for the EV supply chain.
- In fiscal year 2023, job creation in Georgia's automotive industry increased by 324 percent compared to fiscal year 2021.
- Suppliers attracted by Georgia’s mobility OEMs resulted in over $2 billion in investment across the state in FY23.
