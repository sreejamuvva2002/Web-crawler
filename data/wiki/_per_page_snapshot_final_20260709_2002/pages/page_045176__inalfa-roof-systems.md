# Inalfa Roof Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-03-02

**Source:** [Cherokee manufacturer to add hundreds of jobs](https://www.ajc.com/news/local/cherokee-manufacturer-add-hundreds-jobs/8QZ86uDBPEyRzg4TVdXhzI/)  ·  `page_045176`

## Facts

- Cherokee County’s economic development office said Inalfa Roof Systems’ recently announced expansion in the Cherokee 75 Corporate Park will generate at least 300 new jobs by 2019.
- The agency projects 60 of those jobs will be added this year, in positions ranging from management to skilled workers.
- The company soon plans to break ground on a 52,500-square-foot second building in the business park.
- Inalfa is one of the world’s biggest manufacturers of sunroof and open roof systems for the automotive industry.
- The company established its Cherokee operation in January of 2014, and now makes its regional headquarters there.
- The park is in southwest Cherokee near I-75 and Ga. 92.
