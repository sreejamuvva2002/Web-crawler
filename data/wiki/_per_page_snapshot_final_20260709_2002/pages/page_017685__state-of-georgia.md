# State of Georgia

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-28

## Facts

- Through December 2022, economic development totals were tracked across major industries including automotive and renewable energy.
- International companies accounted for 23% of the projects in Fiscal Year 2023.
- Georgia shattered all previous global trade records with $196 billion in total trade, a 40% increase over five years.
- In FY2023, most growth occurred outside the Metro Atlanta region, accounting for 79% of announced projects, 85% of total investment, and 62% of job growth.
- Georgia has been named the No. 1 state for doing business for nine consecutive years.
