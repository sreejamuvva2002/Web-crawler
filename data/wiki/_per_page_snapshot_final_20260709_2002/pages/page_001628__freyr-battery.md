# FREYR Battery

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [FREYR Battery Announces Austin, Texas as Location for New Corporate Headquarters](https://www.advfn.com/stock-market/NYSE/FREY/stock-news/95399919/freyr-battery-announces-austin-texas-as-location)  ·  `page_001628`

## Facts

- FREYR Battery announced Austin, Texas as the location for its new global headquarters to geographically align its workforce with operations and strategy to provide solar modules made in the United States.
- The announcement coincides with the ongoing ramp of production at FREYR’s G1 solar module facility in Wilmer, Texas, which already employs more than 1,000 people across the Wilmer and Dallas communities.
- Austin is strategically located near FREYR’s operating U.S. solar module manufacturing facility in Wilmer, Texas, and provides access to an emerging talent pool within the booming, business-friendly Texas economy to support growth aspirations.
- FREYR’s global headquarters will be located at 1211 E 4th St., Austin, TX 78746.
- Daniel Barcelo, FREYR’s Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer, stated that establishing the headquarters marks an important step in executing a strategic plan to establish an integrated U.S. solar + battery storage manufacturing enterprise built on advanced technology.
- Daniel Barcelo noted that Austin is a vibrant and fast-growing hub of renewable energy and advanced technology development, strategically located close to operations.
- As part of the strategy to build an integrated American solar + storage manufacturing network, FREYR intends to bring more than 1,000 new American jobs to the Texas economy.
- FREYR plans to build teams in Austin and Wilmer, establish a Texas job creation engine, and work with partners across industry and government to invest in critical U.S. infrastructure.
- In conjunction with the relocation to Texas, FREYR has entered into a definitive agreement to sell its 368-acre site in Coweta County, Georgia, to an undisclosed party for gross sales proceeds of $50 million.
- The sale transaction is expected to close on February 15, 2024.
- Estimated net proceeds to FREYR from the Georgia site sale are expected to total $22.5 million following repayment of previously received state and local grants.
- FREYR is an energy solutions provider building an integrated U.S. supply chain for solar and batteries.
- In December 2024, FREYR completed a transformative transaction, positioning the Company as one of the leading solar manufacturing companies in the U.S., with a complementary solar and battery storage strategy.
- Based in the U.S. with plans to expand operations in America, the Company is also exploring value optimization opportunities across its portfolio of assets in Europe.
