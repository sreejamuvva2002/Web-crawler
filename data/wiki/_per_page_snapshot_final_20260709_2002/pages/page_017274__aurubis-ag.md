# Aurubis AG

## Facts

- One of the world’s largest copper recyclers will bring about 125 jobs to the Augusta area after a local production plant begins construction next summer.
- Hamburg, Germany-based Aurubis AG plans to build a $340 million recycling and secondary-smelting facility on 150 acres in Augusta Corporate Park off Mike Padgett Highway.
- Secondary smelting is the process by which copper scrap is reformed into material used in producing finished products.
