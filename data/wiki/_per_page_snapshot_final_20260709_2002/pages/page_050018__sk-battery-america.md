# SK Battery America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** research_and_development  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-02

**Source:** [SK Battery America Opening New Facility In Georgia, Investing $19 Million | Doc and the Block](https://docandtheblock.com/sk-battery-america-opening-new-facility-in-georgia-investing-19-million/)  ·  `page_050018`

## Facts

- SK Battery America (SKBA) is opening a new regional IT hub in Roswell, Georgia.
- The new facility will create 200 tech jobs.
- SKBA will invest about $19 million in the next few years for the new IT hub.
- The new IT center will serve as an integrated IT management center for the company’s battery manufacturing facilities across the United States, including its Georgia facilities.
- The facility is located at 1110 Sanctuary Parkway in Roswell.
- Operations at the new IT hub are expected to begin in January.
- SKBA will be hiring IT experts for battery manufacturing execution system development and operations as well as data analysts.
- SK Battery America is investing nearly $2.6 billion in two lithium-ion battery manufacturing facilities in Jackson County.
- The Jackson County facilities will supply electric vehicles such as the Ford F-150 Lightning and Volkswagen ID.4.
- This Roswell IT hub is one of SK Group’s three major investments in the State of Georgia.
- SK Group was founded in 1976 and is the second-largest conglomerate in South Korea.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp announced the opening of the facility on a Thursday.
- Governor Kemp stated that the company continues to bring jobs of the future that will benefit hardworking Georgians in manufacturing and tap into tech talent from top-ranking universities.
- Jason Choi, Head of Information Technology at SK Battery America, stated the decision to open the IT regional center in Roswell aims to strengthen SK’s position as a leader in the battery industry.
- The new center will help build and operate an advanced manufacturing system in accordance with further expansion of battery production bases in the United States.
- SKBA plans to expand its presence and engage with the community and surrounding region through this center.
