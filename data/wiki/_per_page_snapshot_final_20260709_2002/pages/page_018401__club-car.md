# Club Car

**Source:** [Club Car Unveils New Manufacturing Facility in Georgia](https://www.bradshawev.com/news/club-car-unveils-new-manufacturing-facility-driving-georgia-s-e-mobility-future)  ·  `page_018401`

## Facts

- Club Car is a global leader in small-wheel, zero-emissions vehicle production.
- Club Car announced the grand opening of its new Appling Manufacturing Facility in Georgia.
- The facility opening event featured a ribbon-cutting ceremony and tours attended by Georgia Governor Brian Kemp, First Lady Marty Kemp, and other state and local elected officials and business leaders.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Club Car is bringing jobs into Georgia within the growing e-mobility sector.
- Governor Kemp noted that the company is leading the way in compact utility EV manufacturing.
- Governor Kemp lauded the company's significant contributions to Georgia's economy and its commitment to maintaining the state as a prime business destination.
- The new Appling facility spans 105,000 square feet of flexible production and warehouse space.
- Club Car committed an $8 million capital investment into the facility over a two-year period.
- The facility's state-of-the-art production line is capable of producing up to 20 vehicles daily.
- The expansion aims to meet growing U.S. demand for Club Car's vehicles through a customer-focused approach.
- Club Car President and CEO Mark Wagner highlighted the company's impact on commercial fleet electrification, mini-mobility, and last-mile delivery.
- Club Car has been based in Georgia for more than sixty years.
- The company emphasizes quality, safety, and reliability as core components of its product manufacturing.
