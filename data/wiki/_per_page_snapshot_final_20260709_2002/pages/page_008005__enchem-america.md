## Facts

- Enchem America is the U.S. subsidiary of secondary battery electrolyte company Enchem.
- Enchem America has agreed to merge with The GrowHub, a Singapore-based blockchain technology company listed on Nasdaq.
- The GrowHub filed a binding agreement for a business combination with Enchem America with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) on June 1 local time.
- The transaction is structured as a reverse merger.
- In a reverse merger, when a foreign company acquires a company listed on the U.S. stock market, the acquired U.S.-listed entity remains as the surviving company after the merger.
