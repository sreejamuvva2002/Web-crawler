# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-02

**Source:** [HMGMA - Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America](https://www.hmgma.com/)  ·  `page_007757`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) is Hyundai Motor Group’s first dedicated mass-production Electrified Vehicle plant.
- The all-new manufacturing facility is located just outside of Savannah, Georgia.
- The facility features a highly-connected, automated, and flexible manufacturing system where order collection, procurement, logistics, and production processes are optimized utilizing AI and data.
- The innovative manufacturing system is designed to create a human-centered work environment with robots assisting human workers.
- HMGMA is designed to build more than 500,000 vehicles per year.
- Production at the facility includes Hyundai’s luxury brand Genesis.
- HMGMA and Kia America celebrated the production of the Kia Sportage Hybrid in Ellabell, Georgia on June 2, 2026.
- HMGMA is home to the award-winning IONIQ 5 and the IONIQ 9 three-row SUV.
- HMGMA offers training opportunities to workers.
- HMGMA is located approximately 25 miles from downtown Savannah in Bryan County, Georgia.
- HMGMA welcomed a new fire truck with a traditional wet-down ceremony to strengthen on-site emergency response.
- Savannah Technical College and Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America commemorated a landmark apprenticeship partnership with an official signing ceremony.
- The facility promotes a "Meta Pro" career path for interested workers.
