# G-TEKT Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-31

**Source:** [G-TEKT Corporation - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/g-tekt)  ·  `page_056459`

## Facts

- G-TEKT Corporation is a manufacturer of the Honda Group that produces automotive body stamped parts and thick precision stamped parts.
- The company also produces and sells dies, jigs, and tools.
- In April 2011, Kikuchi Co., Ltd. merged with Takao Kinzoku Kogyo Co., Ltd. to establish G-Tekt Corporation.
- As of March 31, 2025, Honda Motor Co., Ltd. holds a 30.04% investment ratio in the company.
- Individual shareholders hold investment ratios of 8.02%, 7.16%, and 2.59%.
- The Master Trust Bank of Japan, Ltd. (Trust Account) holds a 6.94% investment ratio.
- Custody Bank of Japan, Ltd. (Trust Account) holds a 4.19% investment ratio.
- RE FUND 107-CLIENT AC holds a 1.59% investment ratio with Citibank as standing proxy.
- STATE STREET BANK AND TRUST COMPANY holds a 1.32% investment ratio with Mizuho Bank as standing proxy.
- DFA INTL SMALL CAP VALUE PORTFOLIO holds a 1.09% investment ratio with Citibank as standing proxy.
- Hanwa Co., Ltd. holds a 1.03% investment ratio.
- The company manufactures electrification parts including battery housing and motor cores.
- The company produces body structural parts such as front pillar upper, front pillar lower, center pillars, rear pillars, damper housings, front wheel housings, bulkhead front, dashboard upper, dashboard lower, frame front side, side sills, front sheet, mid floor crossmembers, crossmember B, rail roof front, roof panels, steering beams, rear wheel housings, center inner, parcel rear, rear combination, rear panels, rear seat reinforcement, back panel, and floor.
- The company produces automatic transmission components including torque converters (inner/outer), drum inner, EH drum assemblies, carry assemblies, pistons, clutch drums, and clutch hubs.
- The company produces continuously variable transmission components including primary pistons/cylinders and secondly cylinders/clutches.
- Other products include timing pulleys, holders for hybrids, forged products, rotation sensors, and transmission parts.
- In June 2000, the company invested in Jefferson Southern Corporation in Georgia, USA, for the manufacturing and sales of automotive parts; this entity is a subsidiary of Jefferson Industries Corporation.
- The company began supplying to Honda Motor Co., Ltd. in May 1964.
- In September 1987, Honda Motor Co., Ltd. made a capital participation in the company with an ownership stake of 9.52%.
- In May 1984, the company established KTH Parts Industries Inc. in Ohio, US as a joint venture among five companies including American Honda Motor Company Inc. to manufacture and supply automotive parts.
- In May 1988, the company established Jefferson Industries Corporation as a joint venture with Takao Kinzoku Kogyo and American Honda Motor Company Inc. in Ohio, US.
- The company established G-TEKT North America Corporation in Ohio, USA in April 2013.
- In November 2022, the company turned G-TEKT America Corporation into a wholly owned subsidiary.
