# Kautex

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Kautex Pentatonic Green+ Launched at Battery Show Stuttgart](https://www.composights.com/composites-industry-news/Kautex-Pentatonic-Green-Launched-at-Battery-Show-Stuttgart)  ·  `page_045432`

## Facts

- Kautex introduced its Green+ product line for the first time at the Battery Show in Stuttgart.
- Green+ products are made with 20% biomass materials and/or 25% recycled materials.
- Kautex showcased its very first Pentatonic Green+ product, a composite electric vehicle battery enclosure made up of fishing nets discarded in the ocean.
- The EV battery enclosure material makeup is reinforced with glass fiber and produced via injection molding.
- The Pentatonic Green+ product is the first of its kind at Kautex.
- The product is part of Kautex's Scope 3 carbon emission reduction efforts within its Sustainability Roadmap.
- Kautex's Sustainability Roadmap focuses on reducing carbon emissions in the supply chain by working with strategic suppliers, sourcing low-carbon material, and focusing on a circular economy.
- Kautex's procurement team regularly seeks out existing and new suppliers to source sustainable materials recycled via mechanical, physical, or chemical technologies.
- Sophie Louis, Director of Energy & Sustainability at Kautex, stated: "Green+ is another big step we're taking to reduce our carbon footprint and help our customers to achieve the highest performance with the most sustainable options."
- Kautex was recently given a Platinum rating from EcoVadis for its performance in Environment, Labor & Human Rights, Ethics, and Sustainable Procurement.
- EcoVadis assessed a total of 130,000 companies, and achieving the Platinum rating places Kautex among the top 1% of large companies evaluated.
- The Platinum rating reflects exceptional performance and improvement across these areas since Kautex's Bronze rating was given in 2021.
- Kautex earned an A- rating from CDP (Carbon Disclosure Project) in 2023.
- Kautex joined the United Nations Global Compact in 2023.
- Kautex submitted its information to the Science Based Targets Initiative (SBTi), which encourages companies to take action to reduce the global temperature.
