## Facts

- Kia and its parts suppliers now employ a collective 14,000 people in the region.
- Auto parts manufacturer Mando America Corp. is looking for workers to staff its second plant in Meriwether County, north of Columbus.
- The South Korean company is planning a recruiting event 9 a.m. to noon Saturday at its casting facility in Meriwether Park.
- The address for the park that sits on the border of Meriwether and Troup counties is 955 Meriwether Park Drive, Hogansville.
- Georgia Labor Department spokesman Sam Hall said more than 70 jobs are being filled at the new facility.
- The newly created positions are for the first shift, 7 a.m.-3:30 p.m.
- At its initial plant in Meriwether Park, Mando makes steering, brake and suspension components for several auto companies.
- Mando supplies parts to Kia and Hyundai, both of which have large manufacturing plants in west Georgia (West Point) and east Alabama (Opelika).
- The new casting facility is just now getting up and running.
