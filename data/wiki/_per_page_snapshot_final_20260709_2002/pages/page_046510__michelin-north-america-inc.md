# Michelin North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-16

**Source:** [MICHELIN Launches TreadEye Tech Nationwide in Retread Network - India Bulletin](https://indiabulletinusa.com/wordpress/2025/10/16/michelin-launches-treadeye-tech-nationwide-in-retread-network/)  ·  `page_046510`

## Facts

- Michelin is announcing the complete rollout of TreadEye, a new technology designed to improve the retreading process for commercial fleets.
- After several stages of testing and implementation, TreadEye is now fully operational within the Michelin Retread Technologies network across the United States.
- TreadEye is an advanced tool that measures tread depth, helping fleets get the most out of their tire investments.
- The technology collects detailed information from 1,200 measurement points to ensure accurate readings of tread wear and casing conditions.
- Nate Kirian, Vice President of Sales and Distribution for Michelin North America, stated that TreadEye is 'a revolutionary addition to our retread services and a significant benefit for the fleets we support' and 'a great demonstration of Michelin’s ongoing commitment to innovation for our customers.'
- TreadEye identifies optimal removal points based on fleet location.
- The technology extends tire life by preventing unnecessary removals.
- TreadEye reduces the risk of casing damage and DOT violations.
- The tool improves the overall efficiency of tire removal, positively impacting a fleet’s finances.
- Michelin continues to pioneer tire technology, staying focused on delivering quality and reliability.
- Based in Greenville, S.C., Michelin is a world leader in manufacturing tires and other diverse materials for over 130 years.
- Michelin North America employs around 23,500 people.
- Michelin North America operates 36 manufacturing facilities in the U.S. and Canada.
