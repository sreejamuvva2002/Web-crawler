# Sewon America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-08-25

**Source:** [Sewon America $50 Million Expansion to Bring 100 New Jobs to Rincon - Effingham Herald](https://www.effinghamherald.net/local/sewon-america-50-million-expansion-to-bring-100-new-jobs-to-rincon/)  ·  `page_001352`

## Facts

- Sewon America will create 100 new jobs in Rincon as part of a $50 million expansion of its manufacturing plant.
- The project will add 98,250 square feet to the facility and introduce new production capabilities.
- This marks the first expansion by a Hyundai supplier since Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America launched in late 2024 in Ellabell, about 25 minutes south of Rincon.
- Misti Martin, deputy commissioner of global commerce at the Georgia Department of Economic Development, called the expansion a full-circle moment and an incredible milestone for the Metaplant’s supplier network.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development stated it values long-lasting partnerships and looks forward to additional opportunities this expansion will create for Effingham County.
- ECIDA Chairman Matt Saxon said the project reflects the company’s confidence in the county’s workforce and business climate.
- Matt Saxon noted that from the moment Sewon America chose Effingham County for its second U.S. facility, the partnership was expected to be long-lasting.
- Rincon Mayor Kevin Exley said the jobs will benefit the city and surrounding region and expressed excitement about more quality jobs in the community.
- The City of Rincon looks forward to partnering with Sewon to ensure local citizens can take advantage of these positions.
- Robert Vazquez, Sewon America’s director of human resources, said the expansion will allow the company to better serve customers while investing in the local workforce.
- Sewon America operates one of the largest automobile body-parts manufacturing plants in North America.
- The company opened its first Georgia facility in LaGrange in 2008, which has since doubled in size.
- Sewon America began operations in Rincon in 2025.
- The company supplies electric-vehicle body parts for original equipment manufacturers, including Hyundai’s Metaplant.
- The expansion is supported by the Georgia Department of Economic Development, the City of Rincon, and the Effingham County Board of Commissioners.
