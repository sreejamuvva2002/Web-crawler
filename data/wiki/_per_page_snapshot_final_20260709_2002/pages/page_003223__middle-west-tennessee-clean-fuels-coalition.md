# Middle West Tennessee Clean Fuels Coalition

**Type:** workforce_training  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-26

**Source:** [Alternative Fuels Data Center: Training Builds Safety and Security Into Alternative Transportation](https://afdc.energy.gov/case/3145)  ·  `page_003223`

## Facts

- The Middle West Tennessee Clean Fuels Coalition is promoting fuel safety trainings throughout the state to increase safety and security in alternative fuel vehicle adoption.
- The coalition is conducting these trainings in partnership with the National Fire Protection Association.
- Additional information about the project can be obtained by contacting Middle-West Tennessee Clean Fuels.
- More videos related to the initiative are provided by Clean Cities and Communities and FuelEconomy.gov.
