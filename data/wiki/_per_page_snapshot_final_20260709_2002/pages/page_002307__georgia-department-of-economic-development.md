## Facts

- GDEcD's mission includes attracting new business investment.
- GDEcD supports the expansion of existing industry and small businesses.
- GDEcD locates new markets for Georgia products.
- GDEcD promotes the state as a desirable location for entertainment businesses and projects.
- GDEcD pursues and expands the State of Georgia’s international relationships.
- GDEcD drives innovation of strategic ecosystems through partnerships and resource connections.
- GDEcD offers an internship program that provides hands-on experience and valuable insights into the workplace for students and emerging professionals.
- GDEcD's Trade team works to bolster Georgia exports and brand the state as a competitive source of quality products and services.
- GDEcD's Tourism Division, Georgia, Explore, operates nine Visitor Information Centers across the state.
- GDEcD's state-run Visitor Information Centers are the first points of contact with more than 13 million visitors each year.
- GDEcD is a registered participant in the federal work authorization program commonly known as E-Verify.
- GDEcD uses this program to verify employment eligibility of all individuals.
- GDEcD's Federally Issued User ID# for E-Verify is 46327.
- GDEcD's Date of Authorization for E-Verify is 6/29/2007.
