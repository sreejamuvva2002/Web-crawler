# Yazaki North America, Inc.

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-06-10

**Source:** [Yazaki North America and MOTER Technologies Form Financial Partnership for Future Strategic Collaboration PR Newswire](https://pr.connectiredell.net/article/Yazaki-North-America-and-MOTER-Technologies-Form-Financial-Partnership-for-Future-Strategic-Collaboration?storyId=68488189a89edb403baf9d8f)  ·  `page_016015`

## Facts

- Yazaki North America and MOTER Technologies formed a financial partnership for future strategic collaboration accelerating DX in the mobility industry with AI-Driven solutions.
- Yazaki North America is a U.S. subsidiary of Yazaki Corporation.
- MOTER Technologies is a U.S. subsidiary of Aioi Nissay Dowa Insurance Co., Ltd. and develops insurance software that performs real-time risk analysis and insurance premium calculations installable on in-vehicle devices.
- The companies signed a strategic financial partnership agreement on May 14, 2025, where Yazaki North America invests in MOTER.
- The strategic equity investment transaction closed on June 10, 2025.
- The mobility industry is undergoing a once-in-a-century transformation driven by new technologies and trends such as electrification, autonomous driving, connected cars, and sharing economy.
- Yazaki and MOTER agreed to partner to develop and promote AI-based insurance services to provide solutions for mobility business players in the accelerating DX environment.
- The partnership will integrate Yazaki's cutting-edge AI research with MOTER's data-science platform and deep U.S. insurance expertise to unlock next-generation risk analytics and accident reduction solutions.
- Together, the companies will create value for the mobility society by fusing MOTER's insurance-software risk-analysis methodologies with Yazaki's advanced AI to reduce accidents.
- The partners will explore new business opportunities in the U.S. mobility market by working with automakers and mobility service providers.
- Ko Yazaki, Representative Director & Executive Vice President of Yazaki Corporation, stated that Yazaki has been engaged in the automotive industry for many years and aims to develop AI-driven innovations through this partnership.
- Kenichi Fujisawa, President & CEO of Yazaki North America, noted that discussions began in 2021 and highlighted strong synergy between MOTER's products and Yazaki's AI-powered traffic accident reduction solutions under development within its AI & Digital Division.
- Mitsuru Yamaguchi, Representative Director & Executive Vice President of Aioi Nissay Dowa Insurance Co., Ltd., explained that MOTER was established in April 2021 to develop insurance software embeddable in in-vehicle devices and next-gen insurance products tailored to electric and autonomous vehicles.
- Kenji Fujii, Founder & CEO of MOTER Technologies, stated that Yazaki holds a world-leading share in wire harnesses and has a strong track record in components for EVs and hybrid vehicles.
- The Yazaki Group operates in three core business areas: Automotive Equipment Business providing wire harnesses, Instrumentation Business supplying onboard devices for commercial vehicles, and Environmental Systems Business offering electric wires, air-conditioning units, solar thermal equipment, and gas devices.
- Yazaki holds a world-leading share in the wire harness market and a top-tier share in Japan for commercial vehicle onboard devices such as digital tachographs and dashcams.
- Yazaki North America is headquartered in Michigan, USA.
- Yazaki North America engages in manufacturing, sales, technology development, R&D activities, and new business development within North and Central America.
- Yazaki Corporation's AI & Digital Division leverages cutting-edge technologies such as AI to analyze vast mobility data, factory data, and energy data to create new value solving societal challenges.
- MOTER Technologies was established in April 2021 in California, U.S.A., as a wholly owned subsidiary of Aioi Nissay Dowa Insurance Co., Ltd., with the goal of conducting R&D on insurance software rooted in a software-first philosophy.
- MOTER is developing next-gen insurance products and services compatible with EVs and autonomous vehicles, leveraging technologies such as edge computing and computer vision to enable insurance software embeddable into in-vehicle devices.
- Aioi Nissay Dowa Insurance is a member of the MS&AD Insurance Group and aims to deliver new value through CSV × Digital Transformation by leveraging cutting-edge technologies, digital tools, and data.
