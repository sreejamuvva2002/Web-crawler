## Facts

- The Effingham County Industrial Development Authority (ECIDA) announced that Sewon America, a subsidiary of Korean-based Sewon Precision Industry Co., is expanding its manufacturing operations in Rincon.
- The expansion project is valued at $50 million.
- The project will add 98,250 square feet to the facility.
- The expansion will create 100 new jobs.
- The project introduces advanced manufacturing capabilities.
- This marks the first expansion by a Hyundai supplier since the launch of Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America in late 2024.
