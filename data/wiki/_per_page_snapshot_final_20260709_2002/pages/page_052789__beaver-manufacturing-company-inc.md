## Facts

- Beaver Manufacturing Company Inc is an industrial manufacturer based in Mansfield, Georgia.
- The company was founded in 1971 by Ed Needham.
- The company specializes in engineering high performance technical yarns and fibers including reinforcement fibers, tubular and processed yarns, and tailored fiber solutions.
- These products are designed to enhance the strength, durability, and flexibility of materials used in various applications.
- Beaver serves multiple industries including automotive, mechanical engineering, telecommunications, and oil and gas.
- The company's products are essential for reinforced hoses, tire components, high stress industrial belts, and heavy duty hoses for offshore rigs.
- The company focuses on collaborative research and development working closely with clients to create cost effective application specific fiber solutions that improve material resilience.
- With a commitment to innovation and sustainability, Beaver Manufacturing aims to lead the market in fiber converting solutions.
- The company's diverse workforce fosters collaborative problem solving to advance material science and meet customer needs effectively.
- The company is located at 12 Ed Needham Drive, Mansfield, Georgia 30055, USA.
