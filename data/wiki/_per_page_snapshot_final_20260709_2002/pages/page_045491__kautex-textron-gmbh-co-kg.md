# Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Kautex Secures New Business Award for Pentatonic Battery Enclosure Solution, by @businesswire](https://pro.ceo.ca/@businesswire/kautex-secures-new-business-award-for-pentatonic-battery)  ·  `page_045491`

## Facts

- Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG (Kautex), a Textron Inc. company, has secured a new business award for its Pentatonic battery enclosure solution.
- The award further strengthens Kautex's position in the growing electrified passenger vehicle segment.
- The program is for a leading automotive OEM.
- The award includes the full battery enclosure system – top cover and bottom tray including a thermal management solution.
- The system is engineered for a multi-vehicle platform.
- It is designed to meet the performance and integration requirements of next-generation hybrid-electric vehicles.
- Production will be localized in North America.
- Start of production is planned for 2028.
- The enclosure will be manufactured using an advanced and highly automated production concept.
- The award underscores Kautex’s ability to deliver complex, scalable, tested, and validated battery enclosure systems with a strong focus on sustainability, quality, and industrial efficiency.
- It further strengthens the company’s Pentatonic portfolio and reinforces its role as a trusted partner supporting customers’ electrification strategies through innovative product design and manufacturing excellence.
- Joerg Rautenstrauch, President & CEO of Kautex, stated: “We are thrilled to announce this latest Pentatonic award, which highlights our ability to deliver fully integrated battery enclosure systems at scale.”
- Joerg Rautenstrauch added: “Through advanced automation and production, we are driving efficiency, quality, and innovation to support our customers’ electrification strategies.”
- Kautex is a Tier One automotive supplier with 30 plants in 13 countries.
- Kautex is a pioneer in the design and manufacture of traditional and hybrid fuel systems, engine camshafts and plastic industrial packaging solutions.
- For the era of new mobility, Kautex is expanding its portfolio to offer thermoplastic composite and composite-metal hybrid battery systems as well as assisted and autonomous drive vehicle cleaning systems.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company that leverages its global network of aircraft, defense, industrial and finance businesses to provide customers with innovative solutions and services.
- Textron owns brands including Bell, Cessna, Beechcraft, Pipistrel, Jacobsen, Kautex, Lycoming, E-Z-GO and Textron Systems.
