# Werner Enterprises, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2021-10-22

**Source:** [Werner Enterprises, Inc. - Werner Collaborates With Daimler Trucks North America to Maximize Driver Uptime](https://investor.werner.com/news-and-events/press-releases/news-details/2021/Werner-Collaborates-With-Daimler-Trucks-North-America-to-Maximize-Driver-Uptime/default.aspx)  ·  `page_053639`

## Facts

- Werner Enterprises announced the integration of Daimler Trucks North America’s (DTNA) application programming interface (API) into its fleet management platform.
- The integration is anticipated to significantly increase uptime for Werner’s professional drivers and improve efficiencies for its event resolution team.
- The move will improve repair velocity at DTNA dealerships.
- Director of Equipment Purchasing Tony Wahl stated Werner is working with DTNA to create a cloud-based maintenance system that will greatly reduce manual inputs and phone calls to dealers.
- With the new systems, Werner will have better visibility of tractors and their repairs, faster decision making, and cleaner data flow pertaining to tractor repairs.
- The goal of the integration is to provide better predictive and scheduled maintenance for Werner's fleet and maximize uptime.
- The integration with DTNA’s API launched in September 2021.
- The integration was built to follow the industry standard of the Technology Maintenance Council.
- Werner will continue to focus on direct API integrations through its innovation arm, Werner EDGE.
- DTNA's Director of Customer Experience Erick Nelson stated that preventing unplanned downtime is key to keeping customers’ trucks on the road and ensuring fast and efficient service processes when unexpected issues occur.
- The API integration enables Werner’s team to address repair events in an average of two calls compared to a previous average of 12 calls per event.
- The integration results in a ten-minute reduction in time per event call.
- The integration reduces associated data entry.
- Streamlined service operations offer improved repair velocity, allowing Werner’s professional drivers to get back on the road faster with a reduced dwell time of 30 percent on average.
- Chief Information Officer Daragh Mahon stated that continuously improving productivity and efficiency is imperative and collaborating with industry experts on effective APIs helps achieve those goals.
- These APIs help support faster cloud adoption across Werner's organization and allow investment in building proprietary technology that benefits drivers, shippers, carriers, and associates.
- Werner Enterprises delivers truckload transportation and logistics services to customers across the United States, Mexico, and Canada.
- Werner had 2020 revenues of $2.4 billion.
- Werner operates an industry-leading modern truck and trailer fleet with over 13,000 associates.
- Werner provides Dedicated and One-Way Truckload services as well as Logistics services including truckload brokerage, freight management, intermodal, and final mile.
- Daimler Trucks North America LLC is headquartered in Portland, Oregon, and is the leading heavy-duty truck manufacturer in North America.
- DTNA produces and markets commercial vehicles under the Freightliner, Western Star, and Thomas Built Buses nameplates.
- DTNA is a Daimler company, the world’s leading commercial vehicle manufacturer.
