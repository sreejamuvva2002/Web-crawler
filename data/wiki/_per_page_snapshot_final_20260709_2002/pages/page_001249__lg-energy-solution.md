## Facts

- South Korean EV battery maker LG Energy Solution faced the largest single-site immigration raid in United States history.
- Operation Low Voltage swept more than 300 Korean workers accused of being in the U.S. illegally.
- The raid undercut LG Energy Solution’s efforts to open a factory to supply Hyundai’s electric vehicle manufacturing facility near Savannah.
- Operation Low Voltage and its sweep won’t deter LG from doing business in the country in the future.
- An LG official said Thursday the company still plans to locate half its total global battery cell-making capacity in the U.S.
- LG’s Philip Lienert said in an email: “The U.S. is an extremely important market for us.”
