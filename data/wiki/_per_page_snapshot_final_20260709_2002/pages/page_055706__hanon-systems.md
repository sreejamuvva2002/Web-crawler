# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2021-11-24

**Source:** [Hanon Systems expands its manufacturing capability in Hungary | Cooling India Monthly Business Magazine on the HVACR Business | Green HVAC industry | Heating, Ventilation, Air conditioning and Refrigeration News Magazine Updates, Articles, Publications on HVACR Business Industry | HVACR Business Magazine](https://www.coolingindia.in/hanon-systems-expands-its-manufacturing-capability-in-hungary/)  ·  `page_055706`

## Facts

- Hanon Systems is a leading global provider of automotive thermal and energy management solutions.
- The company inaugurated two new establishments in Hungary: a greenfield production facility in Pécs and a building expansion in Réstätg.
- The new manufacturing facility in Pécs is located in Baranya county.
- The Pécs facility increases production capacity for fluid transport products.
- The Pécs facility strengthens the company’s manufacturing footprint in Europe.
- The Pécs facility provides 22,464 square meters of manufacturing space.
- The Pécs facility accommodates equipment including forming, brazing, welding and bending, assembly lines and testing for automotive air conditioning (A/C) lines.
- The Pécs site offers the opportunity for further expansion to address future business needs.
- The Pécs site currently employs more than 300 employees.
- The building expansion in Réstätg is located in Nógrád county.
- The Réstätg expansion adds an additional 4,500 square meters to the existing site.
- The Réstätg expansion supports additional capacity of aluminium die castings for compressor operations in Europe.
- The Réstätg building is equipped with modern machinery for horizontal die casting.
- There are currently more than 150 employees at the Réstätg site.
- Min Sung, Hanon Systems President and CEO, said the company is pleased to grow in Hungary.
- Min Sung stated that the locations for their investment program were carefully chosen as an important hub in efforts to support the industry’s migration toward e-mobility.
- Min Sung noted that the company continues to explore bringing new and innovative technologies to market and implementing best practices that bring a positive impact to communities, employees, customers and the planet.
