# Daechang Seat Corp. USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-03-16

**Source:** [Media Release: Daechang Seat Corp. USA Announces Expansion Plans in Phenix City | Phenix City, Alabama](https://phenixcityal.gov/media-release-daechang-seat-corp-usa-announces-expansion-plans-in-phenix-city/)  ·  `page_005391`

## Facts

- Phenix City Mayor and City Council voted to approve an incentive package for Daechang Seat Corp., USA (DSC) to expand their manufacturing operations in Phenix City, AL.
- DSC is currently located at 903 Fontaine Road in Phenix City.
- The expansion will include a new facility on 22 acres, purchased from the City, in the Phenix Industrial Park.
- The manufacturing operation projects to create an additional 300 jobs over the next 3 years.
- The project involves an initial capital investment of approximately $23,366,072.
- DSC originally located in Phenix City in 2019 through the effort and support of the State of Alabama Department of Commerce, Alabama Power Company, the Columbus Area Chamber of Commerce, and the Phenix City leadership.
- Mayor Eddie Lowe stated that the expansion continues to show Phenix City is a great place to do business.
- Daechang Seat Co., USA was established in 1979 in Kyungju, South Korea, and has become one of the largest seat frame producers in the world.
- Globally, DSC employs more than 3,000 people across 7 countries.
- DSC’s manufacturing process includes fully automatic lines with arc welding and spot welding machines.
- Once construction is complete at the new manufacturing facility, DSC will have a larger footprint and greater opportunity for continued growth.
- DSC General Manager Mr. Kim expressed pleasure in working with the Mayor of Phenix City and local citizens.
- DSC is a car seat manufacturing supplier that currently has 24 factories worldwide.
- DSC is the first cooperative company with Hyundai and Kia.
- Construction plans are to begin immediately, with operations projected for the 1st quarter of 2023.
- Construction is being completed by Marshall Design Build, LLC, an industry leading Design Build Contractor specializing in Industrial Automotive facilities.
- Marshall Design Build has completed over 90 Hyundai, KIA and other tier supplier projects.
- Marshall Design Build has its headquarters in Montgomery, Alabama, and has been working with tier suppliers for over 19 years.
- Travis Marshall, owner of Marshall Design Build, expressed that the company looks forward to continuing the growth of tier suppliers in Alabama and Georgia.
