## Facts

- Freudenberg has been active in the US market for more than 175 years.
- The company supplies batteries for heavy-duty applications, gaskets and seals, vibration control technologies, filters, medical components, confectionary applications, technical textiles, release agents, lubricants, expansion joints, and household products to US customers.
- Freudenberg’s AI Innovation Center is based in the United States, where a team of data scientists and engineers use data-driven methods to improve material and product development through enhanced integration of artificial intelligence.
- The company employs more than 8,500 people across the US, helping power wind turbines, improve mobility and transportation, and develop life-saving medical devices.
- Freudenberg Performance Materials LP operates a facility at 660 Allied Industrial Blvd, Macon, GA 31206 USA.
