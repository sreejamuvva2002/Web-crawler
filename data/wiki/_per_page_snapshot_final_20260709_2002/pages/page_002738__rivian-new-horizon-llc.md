# Rivian New Horizon, LLC

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-30

**Source:** [Project Horizon | Department of Energy](https://www.energy.gov/edf/project-horizon)  ·  `page_002738`

## Facts

- In January 2025, the U.S. Department of Energy’s Loan Programs Office announced the closing of a $6.57 billion loan to Rivian New Horizon, LLC.
- The loan consists of $5.975 billion of principal and $592 million of capitalized interest.
- The loan finances the development and construction of Project Horizon, an electric vehicle manufacturing facility located in Stanton Springs North, near the City of Social Circle, Georgia.
- The project expects to support up to 2,000 full-time jobs through construction.
- The project expects to support 7,500 operations jobs by 2030.
- The loan supports construction of a nine million square foot facility.
- The facility will manufacture up to 400,000 mass-market electric sport utility vehicles (SUVs) and crossover vehicles.
- At full capacity, the EVs manufactured at the facility are expected to yield an annual fuel consumption savings of approximately 146 million gallons of petroleum.
- As outlined in its Community Benefit Plan, Rivian intends to hire at least a quarter of its employees at the Stanton Springs facility from the local community.
- Rivian is working alongside the local technical college system to create training programs to attract, train, and retain the required workforce.
- This includes working with the State of Georgia’s QuickStart technical training incentive program, which provides customized workforce training to companies making job-creating investments in the state.
- Rivian currently has approximately twenty apprentices local to Georgia who have completed their initial round of education.
- These apprentices are currently receiving job training at Rivian's Illinois plant.
- Upon completion of the program, the apprentices will return to Georgia to work in the facility once it is operational.
- Rivian is exploring expanding participation in this program to other career paths.
- The project owners are Rivian New Horizon, LLC.
- The facility location is listed as Stanton Springs North, Georgia.
- The loan program is ATVM.
- The loan type is a Direct Loan.
- The guaranteed loan amount is $6.57 billion.
- The issuance date is January 2025.
- The permanent U.S. jobs supported are estimated at 7,500.
- The loan amount is approximate and includes principal and any capitalized interest as of January 2025.
- The job support estimate was made at the time of closing.
