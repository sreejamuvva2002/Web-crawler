# Stryten Energy LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-01-04

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Announces Executive Leadership Appointments for Next Phase of Growth](https://www.stryten.com/stryten-announces-executive-leadership-appointments/)  ·  `page_050731`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC announced executive leadership changes and appointments to support its long-term strategy for growth.
- Tim Vargo will transition from Chief Executive Officer to Chairman of the Board before the end of the company’s fiscal year on March 31, 2023.
- Mike Judd will be promoted from President and Chief Operating Officer to Chief Executive Officer and President, effective before the end of the company’s fiscal year.
- Petar Oklobdzija joins the company as Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, effective February 1, 2023, and will lead Finance, Accounting, Treasury, Information Technology, and Strategic Sourcing functions.
- Dan Autey joined the company as Executive Vice President, Commercial, on January 3, 2023, and is responsible for leading commercial strategies across the Stryten Energy business.
- Tim Vargo stated that Mike Judd’s appointment to CEO is the culmination of a succession plan developed over the last several years and expressed confidence in his ability to achieve increased profitability and growth.
- Mike Judd noted he is proud to take on the CEO role and excited to welcome two seasoned leaders to the senior management team to meet the increased demand for sustainable, clean energy storage solutions.
- Mike Judd highlighted that Dan Autey’s automotive and industrial battery expertise will strengthen the company’s ability to serve customers’ evolving energy needs.
- Mike Judd noted that Petar Oklobdzija’s tenure in operations and finance in the automotive and battery industries will help accelerate growth strategy into new and existing energy storage markets.
- The past year marked a significant period of successful transformation for Stryten Energy with the integration of three strategic acquisitions that added advanced lithium-ion and vanadium redox flow battery technologies to its energy storage chemistry portfolio.
- These acquisitions combined with ongoing capital investments in manufacturing plants position the company to meet increased demand for domestically produced, clean and sustainable energy storage solutions.
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- The company is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia, and partners with recognized companies to meet growing demand for reliable and sustainable energy storage capacity.
- Stryten Energy’s stored energy technologies power applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- The company’s technology portfolio includes advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- Stryten Energy is an industry leader backed by more than a century of expertise in delivering top-performing energy solutions.
