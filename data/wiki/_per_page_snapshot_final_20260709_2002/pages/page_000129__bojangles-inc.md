# Bojangles, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Bojangles Partners With Alyath to Bring Electric Vehicle Charging Stations to Most Locations](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20250714862128/en/Bojangles-Partners-With-Alyath-to-Bring-Electric-Vehicle-Charging-Stations-to-Most-Locations)  ·  `page_000129`

## Facts

- Bojangles has partnered with Alyath EV, Smart Big Box, and Energy and Environmental Design Services to install turnkey electric vehicle (EV) charging stations at most of its locations.
- The initiative represents a first-of-its-kind deployment in the quick-service restaurant (QSR) industry.
- The new charging stations will cater to the growing number of EV drivers seeking reliable charging options while on the go.
- Charging station speeds will vary between level two (medium) and level three (high) efficiency with over 97% uptime.
- Each restaurant location will feature a minimum of four charging spots.
- Richard Del Valle, Chief Information Officer at Bojangles, stated that working with Alyath and Smart Big Box introduces a new convenience aligning with evolving customer needs and that the top-of-the-line EV chargers will improve guest accessibility and help drive an eco-friendlier future.
- Cristiane Rosul, CEO of Alyath, stated that the partnership brings fast, reliable, and convenient EV charging to Bojangles locations nationwide and positions the chain as a leader in the future of quick-service dining.
- Smart Big Box has contracted Energy and Environmental Design Services as the exclusive installer and maintenance partner for all EV chargers to ensure optimal performance and industry-leading uptime.
- The rollout of EV charging stations at Bojangles will begin in Q4 of 2025.
- Fans can expect the EV charging stations to be largely accessible in 2026.
- Bojangles is a Carolina-born restaurant chain specializing in Southern food, founded in 1977 as a single location in Charlotte, N.C., and currently operates around 800 company-owned and franchised restaurants.
- Alyath is building one of the fastest-growing electric vehicle charging networks in the U.S., focusing on high-demand locations including city centers, highways, retail hubs, and commercial properties.
- Alyath has deployments underway at over 5,500 private sites and aims to build America's largest and most reliable EV charging network while adhering to sustainability and ESG principles.
- Smart Big Box delivers outcome-driven solutions that enhance efficiency and reduce energy consumption through a holistic, integrated approach connecting strategic partners and leveraging cutting-edge technologies.
