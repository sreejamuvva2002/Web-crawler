## Facts

- The Hyundai Mobis Georgia Plant is located in West Point, GA.
- The facility operates within the motor vehicle manufacturing sector and employs 51-200 people.
- The plant uses cutting-edge vehicle modularization technology to assemble and supply chassis modules, cockpit modules, and front-end modules to the KIA Georgia factory.
- In addition to the three major modules, team members assemble front and rear bumpers in a just-in-sequence manufacturing environment.
- The company name MOBIS is an abbreviation for MObility Beyond Integrated Solution, originally composed of Mobile + System.
- The organization's five core values are Customer First, Daring Execution, Communication & Cooperation, Respect for People, and Go Global.
- The company aims to leap forward as a mobility platform provider and achieve sustainable growth and development by fully internalizing its core values across the organization.
