# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2010-11

**Source:** [Pirelli Adds Mixing Room to Rome, Georgia Operation - Tyrepress](https://www.tyrepress.com/2010/11/pirelli-adds-mixing-room-to-rome-georgia-operation/)  ·  `page_048433`

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America has added a compound mixing facility to its MIRS automated tyre manufacturing facility.
- The Rome, Georgia location serves as the company’s North American headquarters.
- The new mixing room addition represents a $12 million US investment.
- This investment is designed to support Pirelli Group’s strategic growth plan for the North American market.
- The facility aims to help PTNA sustain its market gains and a 30 percent sales increase realized over the past three years.
- Since its inception in 2001, the Rome, Georgia facility has received more than $92 million in investments from Pirelli.
- Mauro Pessi serves as PTNA’s Chairman and CEO.
- Pessi stated that the addition of the new mixing room demonstrates Pirelli’s dedication to strategic growth in the North American market.
- The project supports Pirelli’s commitment to investing in the economic development of the City of Rome and the State of Georgia.
- PTNA received excellent support from local, state, and regional governments and organizations for the project.
- The company plans to continue its relationships with local colleges to support growth.
- The company will utilize the Georgia QuickStart program to help realize its growth objectives.
