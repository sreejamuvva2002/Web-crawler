# Ecoplastic Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-01-05

**Source:** [Auto parts manufacturer Ecoplastic building site in Bulloch Co., to serve Hyundai Metaplant](https://www.connectsavannah.com/news-2/auto-parts-manufacturer-ecoplastic-building-site-in-bulloch-co-to-serve-hyundai-metaplant-19708426/)  ·  `page_019829`

## Facts

- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp's office announced that Ecoplastic Corporation will create 456 new jobs.
- Ecoplastic Corporation will invest approximately $205 million in a new manufacturing facility.
- The new manufacturing facility is located in Bulloch County, Georgia.
- Governor Brian Kemp welcomed Ecoplastic to the state's growing ecosystem of manufacturers, logistics professionals, and suppliers.
- The facility will be located in Statesboro, providing close proximity to Georgia's ports system.
- Ecoplastic will serve the Hyundai Metaplant from its new Statesboro location.
- Ecoplastic Corporation was established in 1984.
- Ecoplastic Corporation is a Korea-based company.
- Ecoplastic Corporation manufactures plastic automobile parts for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and other suppliers.
- Ecoplastic is a Tier-1 supplier for Hyundai Motor Group (HMG) and Kia.
- Ecoplastic manufactures automotive interior and exterior plastic parts, including bumpers, consoles, trims, and plastic molds.
- Han Shang, President and CEO of Ecoplastic, stated the company is honored to establish a production facility for electric vehicle parts in Bulloch County, Georgia.
- Ecoplastic aims to contribute to the economic development of the State of Georgia and Bulloch County by increasing local jobs and providing a stable supply of high-quality products to its client, HMGMA.
- Ecoplastic appreciates the support of the State and County governments and aims to preserve a continuous and mutually beneficial relationship with them.
- The new facility will be located at 4822 Highway 301 South Register near Statesboro.
- The new plant will be capable of injection molding and painting.
- The plant will produce exterior parts, bumpers and garnishes, interior parts, consoles, and trims specifically for electric vehicles.
- The company expects to begin operations by October 2024.
- Ecoplastic will be hiring for HR, finance, parts development, quality control, facility maintenance, injection, and paint production positions.
