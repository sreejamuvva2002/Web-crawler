# YKK U.S.A. Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-01

**Source:** [YKK U.S.A. Inc. Receives 2021 GEAR Award - Middle Georgia CEO](https://middlegeorgiaceo.com/news/2022/01/ykk-us-inc-receives-2021-gear-award/)  ·  `page_016215`

## Facts

- YKK (U.S.A.) Inc. was honored with a 2021 GEAR Award by the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD).
- The award ceremony took place on January 14 at YKK's Macon, Georgia plant.
- GDEcD named YKK the “Supplier of the Year, employing 200+ people” for its contributions to the advancement of the state’s automotive and mobility industries.
- YKK produces a wide range of zippers, hook and loop fasteners, plastic hardware, and snaps and buttons for automobile seat assembly.
- YKK supplies truck tonneau cover hold-downs and other applications to more than 100 automotive customers.
- YKK operates with a vertically integrated structure that allows the company to create its own materials and machinery while ensuring quality and minimizing manufacturing costs.
- Andrew Capezzuto, Chief Administrative Officer and General Counsel of GDEcD, presented the award and stated that the GEAR award recognizes companies that are good corporate stewards.
- Lester Miller, Macon-Bibb County mayor, stated that YKK invests in people and community.
- Robert Stamps, Vice President and Business Development Leader for Transportation Fastening Materials at YKK, demonstrated various automotive fastening products following the award presentation.
- Keith Kratz, Product Development Engineer at YKK, also demonstrated automotive fastening products during the ceremony.
- The ERGO-J® is a sheet fastener for securing the edges of motor vehicle seat covering material that improves ergonomic efficacy and reduces carpal tunnel syndrome rates for automotive workers.
- The QUICKFIT® is a concealed hook fastener for automotive seats that enables a craftsmanship-like appearance when molded into seat foam and reduces seat weight compared to metal wire tie down systems.
- The 5CH CONCEAL® zipper forms a perfect seam when closed and allows seat manufacturers to significantly decrease the weight of seat back panels to improve fuel efficiency.
- Jim Reed, YKK Corporation of America President, stated that the company uses a cyclical process to contribute to the automotive industry while reinvesting profits into employees.
- YKK aims to reduce its emissions by 50% by 2030 and achieve net-zero emissions by 2050 as part of its environmental initiatives under the Cycle of Goodness philosophy.
