# Fictiv and EVCO Plastics

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Fictiv And EVCO Plastics Announce Strategic Partnership To Deliver End-To-End Injection Molding Supply Chain Solutions](https://www.plasticsnet.com/doc/fictiv-and-evco-plastics-announce-strategic-injection-molding-supply-chain-solutions-0001)  ·  `page_054454`

## Facts

- Fictiv created a strategic partnership with EVCO Plastics to revolutionize injection molding services for U.S. OEMs.
- The alliance combines Fictiv's digitally connected global supply chain with EVCO's extensive manufacturing capabilities to efficiently bring innovations from concept to scale production.
- EVCO provides over 1 million square feet of manufacturing space and operates hundreds of injection molding machines, including press sizes as big as 4,400 tons.
- The partnership leverages deep expertise in design for manufacturability (DFM), AI, human-powered materials data and insights, program management, and global logistics to mitigate tariff concerns.
- Fictiv addresses complexities in Mexico's emerging manufacturing ecosystem, which is currently fragmented for tooling and specialized secondary processes.
- Fictiv GlobalFlex integrates tooling inserts produced in China with next-generation manufacturing facilities in the U.S. and Mexico to enhance gross margins and accelerate production timelines.
- The collaboration supports the entire product lifecycle, encompassing 3D printing, injection molding, assembly, testing, and packaging.
- Advanced capabilities include overmolding, gas-assist molding, in-mold decorating, and liquid silicone rubber (LSR) production.
- The partnership holds certifications including ISO 9001:2015, ISO 13485, IATF 16949, and FDA compliance to ensure quality and reliability throughout the supply chain.
- Dave Evans, Co-Founder and CEO of Fictiv, stated the partnership simplifies global manufacturing supply chains by reducing the overall number of suppliers involved in managing multiple supplier challenges.
- Carlos Gonzales, head of EVCO Plastics de Mexico, noted that Fictiv's capabilities allow them to concentrate on manufacturing excellence while handling complex workflow and supply chain challenges that impact OEM margins.
- The collaboration aims to provide scalable, tailored solutions with a shared commitment to innovation, quality, and sustainability in the manufacturing sector.
- Fictiv operates four global manufacturing centers in India, Mexico, China, and the U.S., having delivered more than 35 million commercial and prototype parts to date for early-stage companies and large enterprises.
- EVCO Plastics has 13 manufacturing facilities strategically located throughout the world, was founded in 1964, employs over 2,100 people, and operates 270 injection molding machines ranging from 28 to 4,400 tons.
