# SHIROKI Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2014-03-31

**Source:** [SHIROKI Corporation  Business Report FY ended 2014 - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/cf/s500_134_hl2013)  ·  `page_049835`

## Facts

- Shiroki-GA, LLC located in the state of Georgia currently serves as the Company's major production base of door frames in the U.S.
- The Company will increase production of door frames in the U.S. from the fall of 2013.
- This move is in response to the first order it has won from General Motors, as well as growing demand from its major customers, including Toyota and Nissan.
- Shiroki North America, Inc. (Tennessee), which is producing seat parts, will start producing door frames to be supplied to General Motors from September of 2013.
- By adding a new supply base, the Company will expand its production capacity to cope with requests from its customers to supply more door frames.
- Shiroki-GA, LLC (Georgia, USA) has planned capital investment of 317 million JPY for manufacturing equipment from January 2014 to December 2014.
- Shiroki North America, Inc. (Tennessee, USA) has planned capital investment of 679 million JPY for manufacturing equipment from January 2014 to December 2014.
- Shiroki-GT, LLC (Tennessee, USA) has planned capital investment of 274 million JPY for manufacturing equipment from January 2014 to December 2014.
- Production was launched to fill orders for door sashes placed by Toyota Motor North America's Mississippi plant for the "Collora".
- Production was launched to fill orders for door sashes placed by Nissan North America for the "Rogue".
- Shiroki North America, Inc. started delivering aluminum door sashes to GM.
