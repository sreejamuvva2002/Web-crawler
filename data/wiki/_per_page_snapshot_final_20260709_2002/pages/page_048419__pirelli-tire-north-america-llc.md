# Pirelli Tire North America, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2011-12-23

**Source:** [Pirelli "Redefines" North American Road Race Tire Distribution System - Roadracing World Magazine | Motorcycle Riding, Racing & Tech News](https://www.roadracingworld.com/news/pirelli-redefines-north-american-road-race-tire-distribution-system/)  ·  `page_048419`

## Facts

- Pirelli Motorcycle Tire North America, LLC reorganized its roadrace distribution and service partnerships effective immediately as of December 23, 2011.
- Distributor CT Racing will sell and service Pirelli road racing DOT, slick and racing rain tires in the western U.S.
- Orion Motorsports will continue to sell product and service customers in the Canadian and eastern US markets.
- Beginning in 2012, Pirelli will offer 125 GP/Moto 3 Slick and racing rain tires along with DOT tires (Diablo Rosso II).
- New tire sizes will be available for the popular Kawasaki EX250 and Honda CBR250 model sports bikes.
- Pirelli Tire North America, LLC designs, develops, manufactures and markets tires for passenger vehicles in both the original equipment and replacement markets.
- The company also markets and distributes tires for motorcycles and motorsports.
- Pirelli’s manufacturing facility is located in Rome, Georgia.
- Pirelli’s Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) employs state-of-the-art technology to manufacture tires for both export and domestic markets.
