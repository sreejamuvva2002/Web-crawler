# Truck Underride Protection Patent Archive

**Type:** research_center  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-10-07

## Facts

- Engineers have long recognized the problem of truck underride, where passenger vehicles go under trucks.
- A patent for a side underride protective device for street cars was issued on July 14, 1896 and cited by numerous more recent underride patents.
- A patent was issued in 1913 for a “Safety Device for Motor Vehicles” to provide underride protection for the sides of large trucks.
- H.H. Robinson was granted a patent for a Guard System for Tractor Trailer Combinations.
- US4688824 was filed on Mar 4, 1986 and published on Aug 25, 1987 to applicant Herring John D for a Safety device for vehicles.
- CN100572144C was filed on May 27, 2005 and published on Dec 23, 2009 to Volvo Lastvagnar Ab for a Device for a vehicle side anticollision box, and a vehicle comprising such a side anticollision box.
- US7407204 was filed on Nov 27, 2006 and published on Aug 5, 2008 to Volvo Lastvagnar Ab for an Arrangement for a vehicle side fairing, and a vehicle comprising such a side fairing.
- US20070085355 was filed on Nov 27, 2006 and published on Apr 19, 2007 to Volvo Lastvagnar Ab for an Arrangement for a vehicle side fairing, and a vehicle comprising such a side fairing.

- US20080303311 was filed on Jun 9, 2008 and published on Dec 11, 2008 to Vanguard National Trailer Corporation for a Crash attenuating underride guard.

- In 1977, William Page was granted US4060268 for a guard rail for side protection on large wheel vehicles.
- In 1996, Volvo began manufacturing trucks with Front Underrun Protection in Europe.
- A patent was filed in 2007 for an “Underrun protector and method of providing underrun protection.”
- A patent was filed in 2009 for a “Side impact guard device for industrial vehicles, particularly trailers or semi-trailers” (US7967349), filed Apr 7, 2009 and published Jun 28, 2011 to C.R.F. Societa Consortile Per Azioni.
- In 2012, Sapa Extrusions inventor/engineer Malcolm Deighton filed for a patent for a “Semi trailer under-run protection device” which was later developed into a rear underride guard successfully crash tested on a trailer in April 2017.
- In 2015, Aaron Kiefer, crash reconstructionist and forensic engineer, was issued a patent (US9463759) for an innovative combination side & rear trailer underride protection system.
- A 2015 patent for a Trailer rear impact guard cites numerous other underride protection patents (USD790407).
- In 2016, Stephen Batzer and Patrick Rogers were granted a patent for an Adjustable side under-ride guard for sliding axle trailer.
- On October 11, 2016, Aaron Kiefer was granted a TrailerGuard system underride protection patent (pat9463759).
- In 2016, Stephen Batzer and Patrick Rogers were granted a patent for a Telescoping side under-ride guard for sliding axle trailer.
- In 2017, Seven Hills Engineering inventor Perry Ponder's AngelWing side guard Underride Protection was successfully crash tested at 35 MPH by IIHS on 3/30/2017 and 40 MPH on 8/29/2017, with a patent pending.
- On March 1, 2017, the IIHS TOUGHGuard Award was announced for improved rear underride guard design by trailer manufacturers.
- In 2017, SAPA (Hydro Extrusions) published results of a 40 mph crash test of a Rear Impact (Underride) Guard in the Sapa 40-30 RIG Test Engineering Report Version 1.3.
- On September 29, 2017, Wabash Trailers announced a prototype side guard.
- In 2018, Aaron Kiefer was granted a patent for an underride collision device for large trucks (pat9908493).
- On June 20, 2019, Vanguard Trailers was granted an application for a strap side guard patent.
- In 2019, Fortier was granted Patent No.: US 10,343 for a Side protection device for a vehicle, related kit and vehicle provided with the same.
- In 2020, Wabash received a Side Underride Guard Patent (Wabash 2020 SUG patent).
- In 2021, a patent was issued for an Underride Guard Assembly for Trailers.
- In 2022, a SIDE UNDERRIDE GUARD ASSEMBLY FOR A TRAILER patent was issued.
- In 2023, Utility Trailer Manufacturing received a side guard patent.
- In 2023, Great Dane Trailers received a side guard patent.
