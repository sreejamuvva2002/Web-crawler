## Facts

- Horizon Technology Finance led a $15 million venture loan facility for Lehigh Technologies, Inc.
- Horizon Technology Finance provides capital in the form of secured loans to venture capital backed companies in the technology, life science, healthcare information and services, and cleantech industries.
- Lehigh Technologies is a specialty materials company that produces micronized rubber powders (MRP).
- MRP is described as a lower cost, high-performance, sustainable material.
- MRP can be used in applications such as tires, asphalt, coatings, and polyurethanes.
- Lehigh Technologies is backed by venture capital firms including Kleiner, Perkins, Caufield & Byers, Index Ventures, and Leaf Clean Energy Company.
- Lehigh Technologies has recently expanded into Europe.
- Lehigh will use the loan proceeds for working capital and capital expenditures.
- Gerald A. Michaud is the President of Horizon Technology Finance.
- Dr. Alan Barton is the CEO of Lehigh Technologies.
- Lehigh's proprietary manufacturing technology converts waste rubber into micronized rubber powders.
- The loan facility will support Lehigh's global expansion initiatives and manufacturing process upgrades.
- Dr. Alan Barton stated that Lehigh aims to help customers reduce costs and achieve sustainability goals through its materials.
