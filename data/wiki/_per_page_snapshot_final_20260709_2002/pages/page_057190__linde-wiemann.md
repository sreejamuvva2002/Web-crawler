## Facts

- LINDE + WIEMANN drives innovations in structural assemblies for the automotive industry by combining profiling, stamping, joining and gluing technology.
- The German site in Hagenbach produces ready-to-install assemblies of high-strength steel for global vehicle manufacturers and automotive suppliers.
- Automated 3D measurement technology solutions from ZEISS support the tier-1 supplier throughout the entire manufacturing process.
- Three integrated ScanBox systems scan measuring data and submit it directly to upstream production using ZEISS PiWeb.
- Deviations are detected quickly and appropriate corrective measures are initiated.
- LINDE + WIEMANN regularly shares its measuring data with customers.
- Peter Kluge is the Head of Quality Management at LINDE + WIEMANN.
- Michael Kray is the Quality Manager at LINDE + WIEMANN.
- ATOS sensors digitize the entire surface of structural components via fringe projection within seconds.
- ZEISS INSPECT software displays deviations between actual 3D coordinates and CAD data through a color comparison.
- The low set-up time of the ScanBox system enables inspection of other structurally relevant components within just a few minutes.
