# Wabash Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-18

**Source:** [Wabash opens Atlanta service center, expands dealer network](https://www.streetinsider.com/Corporate+News/Wabash+opens+Atlanta+service+center,+expands+dealer+network/25378609.html)  ·  `page_014583`

## Facts

- Wabash (NYSE: WNC) announced the expansion of its Southeast operations with three initiatives in the Atlanta region.
- The company opened a new Parts & Service center in the Atlanta area on September 18, 2025.
- The Atlanta-area facility provides parts, service, and truck body upfitting.
- The facility offers Ready-to-Mount truck bodies for dry freight and platform applications.
- The Atlanta location joins existing Wabash service centers in California, Florida, Ohio, Pennsylvania, and Texas.
- Fleetco, an existing Wabash dealer, will now represent the company in trailer sales in the greater Atlanta market.
- The expansion broadens Wabash's dealer presence in the Southeast region.
- Bill Tirrill, president at Fleetco Inc., stated that expanding the partnership allows them to grow their sales force across the region.
- Wabash added three CS Truck and Trailer locations in Georgia to its Preferred Partner Network.
- The Preferred Partner Network provides service, maintenance, and parts through authorized third-party dealerships.
- Dave Hill, vice president of Parts & Services at Wabash, stated that the Atlanta expansions would serve fleets operating across Georgia, Alabama, South Carolina, and Tennessee.
