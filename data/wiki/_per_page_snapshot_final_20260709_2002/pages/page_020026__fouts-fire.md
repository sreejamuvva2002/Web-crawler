## Facts

- Fouts Fire is an American fire apparatus manufacturer located in Milledgeville, Georgia.
- The company was formerly known as Fouts Bros. Fire Equipment or Fouts Brothers.
- Fouts Fire has been family-owned and operated since 1952.
- Fouts Fire is a subsidiary of Fouts Bros Inc., a collection of commercial automotive equipment brands including Fouts Commercial Vehicles Inc., Smyrna Truck, Fouts Defense, Big Deal Parts, and Electric Commercial Vehicles Inc.
- In 2019, Fouts moved their headquarters and primary production facility from Smyrna, GA, to Milledgeville, GA.
- Fouts Bros was founded in 1952 by CJ and Jerry Fouts as a single service station in Smyrna, GA.
- In 1989, the company opened its fire division with the introduction of the 3000 Gallon Tanker.
- The company eventually expanded into a standardized line of fire tankers, pumpers, light rescues, and brush trucks.
- In 2024, the fire division was rebranded as Fouts Fire.
- Currently under third-generation family management, Fouts Fire is led by Scott Edens.
- The company operates out of a 700,000 square foot production facility and headquarters in Milledgeville, GA.
- Fouts Fire has a second factory located in La Grange, TX.
- Fouts Fire builds pumpers, tankers, rescues, and quick attacks on both custom and commercial chassis.
- The company builds a limited, standardized line of apparatus to streamline production processes and costs.
- This business model supports keeping stock units of all models readily available for fast delivery nationwide.
- Product models include the FB-94 pumper and the Fouts FOUR mini-pumper.
