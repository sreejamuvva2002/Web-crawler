# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-03

**Source:** [First Kia, first hybrid vehicle built at Georgia Metaplant unveiled in Bryan County](https://www.wtoc.com/2026/06/03/first-kia-first-hybrid-vehicle-built-georgia-metaplant-unveiled-bryan-county/)  ·  `page_009302`

## Facts

- The first Kia vehicle and the first hybrid vehicle ever built at Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America were officially unveiled on Tuesday.
- The facility is recognized as Georgia’s largest economic development project.
- The unveiled vehicle is a 2027 Kia Sportage Hybrid SUV.
- State and local leaders, including Governor Brian Kemp and First Lady Marty Kemp, attended the unveiling at the Ellabell facility in Bryan County.
- The Kia Sportage Hybrid joins two electric vehicle models already being assembled at the plant.
- Kia Georgia President and CEO Stuart Countess stated that the launch demonstrates the facility’s continued growth and the economic opportunities it is creating throughout the region.
- Stuart Countess noted that jobs at the facility are multiplied many times over by all the suppliers that come in to support it.
- Governor Brian Kemp praised the metaplant’s impact on job creation, innovation, and economic development across Georgia.
- Governor Kemp stated that the Kia Sportage Hybrid is the latest Georgia-made vehicle that state leaders proudly stand behind.
- Governor Kemp added that the campus has become an integral part of the region’s economy and continues to evolve to meet consumer demand.
- Plant leaders said the 2026 Kia Sportage Hybrid is already available to consumers.
- The 2027 Kia Sportage Hybrid model is expected to arrive on dealership lots in the near future.
- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America’s manufacturing operations have become a major driver of economic activity in coastal Georgia since construction began.
