# Aspen Aerogels, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-31

**Source:** [Aspen Aerogels Announces $100M Secured Loan Agreement with General Motors | citybiz](https://www.citybiz.co/article/352961/aspen-aerogels-announces-100m-secured-loan-agreement-with-general-motors/)  ·  `page_052680`

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels, Inc. (NYSE: ASPN) announced a definitive loan agreement with General Motors Holdings LLC for a secured lending commitment of $100 million.
- The $100 million loan is designated for the construction and equipping of Aspen’s second aerogel manufacturing facility in Statesboro, Georgia.
- Loan proceeds will be available to draw on a periodic basis from January 2023 through September 2023, subject to certain conditions precedent to funding and other financing requirements.
- The loan carries a maturity date of March 31, 2025 and has no prepayment penalty.
- Donald R. Young serves as Aspen’s President and CEO.
- GM's investment will support the construction of a new, state-of-the-art aerogel manufacturing facility in Georgia designed to triple Aspen’s nameplate aerogel capacity.
- Aspen has leveraged its Aerogel Technology Platform for over two decades to bring sustainability solutions into large markets, with a focus on enhancing electric vehicle battery performance and safety.
- Aspen is a key supplier to GM and was named an Overdrive Award winner as part of GM’s 30th Annual Supplier of the Year awards earlier this year.
- Aspen has signed GM’s ESG Supplier Pledge, establishing a shared vision for a collaborative supply chain that minimizes environmental impact and enhances long-term sustainable electrification.
- Aspen’s sustainability performance was assessed by GM’s independent third-party supplier verification platform, EcoVadis, earning a Silver EcoVadis Medal in November 2022 and placing the company in the 87th percentile of assessed companies.
- Aspen’s PyroThin® products provide solutions to thermal runaway challenges within the electric vehicle market.
- Aspen Battery Materials is the company’s carbon aerogel initiative designed to increase the performance of lithium-ion battery cells.
- The carbon aerogel initiative aims to enable EV manufacturers to extend driving range and reduce the cost of EVs.
- Aspen’s Spaceloft® products provide building owners with industry-leading energy efficiency and fire safety.
- Aspen’s Cryogel® and Pyrogel® products are valued by the world’s largest energy infrastructure companies.
- Aspen is headquartered in Northborough, Mass., and manufactures its products at its East Providence, R.I. facilities.
