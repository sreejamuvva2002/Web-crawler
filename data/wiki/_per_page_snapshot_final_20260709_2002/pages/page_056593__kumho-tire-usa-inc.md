# Kumho Tire USA, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-23

**Source:** [Kumho Tire USA, Georgia Department of Veterans Service, and Georgia Veterans Service Foundation Announce Formal Partnership to Support Georgia's Veterans](https://finance.yahoo.com/sectors/healthcare/articles/kumho-tire-usa-georgia-department-131600476.html)  ·  `page_056593`

## Facts

- Kumho Tire USA, Inc. is the U.S. subsidiary of Kumho Tire Co., Ltd., a leading global tire manufacturer headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
- Kumho Tire USA's U.S. operations are headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- Kumho Tire USA announced the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) formalizing a long-term partnership with the Georgia Department of Veterans Service and the Georgia Veterans Service Foundation to support Georgia's approximately 680,000 veterans and their families.
- The partnership first became publicly visible earlier this year when Georgia Veterans Service Foundation representatives were honored as Co-Captains at Kumho Tire Night at State Farm Arena during an Atlanta Hawks vs. Boston Celtics game.
- Edmund Cho serves as the CEO of Kumho Tire USA, Inc.
- Kumho Tire USA is the official tire sponsor of the Atlanta Hawks.
- Specific initiatives for the veteran partnership will be announced as the partnership develops.
