## Facts

- Pirelli & C SpA is based in Milan, Italy.
- Pirelli & C SpA is the world’s fifth largest tyre manufacturer.
- Pirelli operates 22 factories in 13 countries around the world.
- Pirelli is currently expanding its operations in a number of locations, including Asia.
- North America is one of Pirelli's fast-growing markets for both OEM and replacement tyre sales.
- Pirelli increased its production of tyres within North America to significantly improve customer service rates and reduce logistics costs.
- Pirelli opened a second plant in Silao, Mexico during 2012.
- The opening of the Silao, Mexico plant served as a watershed for Pirelli's logistics in North America.
- The Silao plant expansion led to more regional tyre production, growth in local souring, and speedier lead times.
