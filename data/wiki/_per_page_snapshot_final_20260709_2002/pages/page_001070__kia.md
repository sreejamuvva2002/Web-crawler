# Kia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-03

**Source:** [Kia EV9 production begins in Georgia](https://www.teslarati.com/kia-ev9-production-georgia/)  ·  `page_001070`

## Facts

- Kia EV9 production has started at the South Korean automaker’s Georgia assembly plant in West Point.
- The Kia EV9 is an all-electric three-row SUV and the first electric vehicle (EV) made in Georgia to be put up for sale in the United States auto market.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that the expansion adds a new product line to vehicles already being built in West Point.
- Governor Brian Kemp thanked Kia for choosing Georgia again and noted the state's goal to become the "e-mobility capital of the nation."
- Kia’s Georgia plant will assemble the 2025 model year of the EV9.
- Since its global debut, the Kia EV9 has won multiple awards.
- In April 2024, the Kia EV9 won the “Best of the Best” Red Dot award in the Cars and Motorcycle category.
- Earlier this month, the Texas Auto Writers Association named the Kia EV9 the “Best in Class” in several categories, including Performance SUV, mid-size CUV, and electric vehicle.
- The Kia EV9 has been recognized for its “Opposites United” design philosophy, specifically its contemporary exterior with strong classic lines.
- The Kia EV9 has also been acknowledged for its usability.
- According to Eric Watson, Vice President of Sales Operations at Kia America, the Kia EV9 contributed heavily to the automaker’s 151% increase in EV sales in the United States.
- SeungKyu (Sean) Yoon, President and CEO of Kia North America and Kia America, stated that today's launch is an example of Kia's commitment to the U.S. market and Georgia.
- Through Plan S, Kia aims to be a leader in the e-mobility sector for years to come.
- The Kia EV9 has previously won accolades including World Car of the Year, World EV of the Year, and North American Utility Vehicle of the Year.
- SeungKyu (Sean) Yoon commented that the EV9 will be assembled by team members at Kia Georgia with a track record for assembling award-winning, world-renowned products.
