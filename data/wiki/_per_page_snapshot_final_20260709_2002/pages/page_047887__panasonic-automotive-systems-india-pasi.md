# Panasonic Automotive Systems India (PASI)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-01

## Facts

- PASI is a subsidiary of Panasonic Automotive Systems Co. Ltd, a global leader in advanced automotive solutions that make driving more enjoyable and fulfilling, providing systems that support safe driving, and offer optimal in-vehicle systems and solutions for environmentally friendly vehicles.
- PASI focuses on the development, manufacturing, and sale of automotive components and parts like infotainment solutions (audio, video and navigation), sensors, relays, cameras, parking assistance systems, telematics control units, smart entry systems, LED solutions for headlamps and batteries.
- PASI offers the latest technologies such as Head-up Displays (HUD), Telematic Control Units (TCU), Temperature Oil Sensor, and in-car infotainment to advance India’s automotive industry across global markets.
- Bhupesh Kanyal serves as the Director of Panasonic Automotive Systems India (PASI).
- The Indian motor vehicle industry is the fourth largest in the world and continues to grow rapidly.
- India is the largest manufacturer in the two-wheeler segment.
- Strong economic growth and rising income of Indian households drive significant growth potential in the automotive industry.
- The advent of new-age technologies and newer forms of mobility such as Electric Vehicles are evolving consumer aspirations.
- Indian consumers today demand premium commuting experiences, passenger safety, and environmental safety, resulting in demand for an improved passenger vehicle experience.
- Panasonic is transforming the in-car experience by harnessing new technologies such as Connected Devices, Head-Up Displays, Wireless Chargers, and 360° Camera Solutions that are simple to use, safe, durable, and cost-effective.
- PASI currently caters to the top OEMs of the automotive industry in India and aims to expand its product gamut.
- PASI’s range of solutions is categorized under three themes: Beyond Comfort, Integrated Safety, and Electrification.
- The Beyond Comfort theme caters to automobile interiors featuring cockpits, navigation systems, and HUDs.
- The Integrated Safety theme aids in safe commuting and reducing traffic accidents, featuring products like back & corner sensors, interior rearview mirrors, vehicle camera modules, LED modules for headlamps, and steering angle sensors.
- In the area of electrification, Panasonic caters to Electric Vehicles (EVs) in the form of Li-ion batteries for EVs, Heater Devices, and on-board charging systems.
- PASI was established in April 2010.
- As of the article's date, PASI has a presence across all automotive clusters in India working with top OEMs.
- In India, PASI maintains a vast delivery and service network with highly trained employees and fully equipped facilities to support OEMs and end users.
- Head-Up Displays (HUD) present data to drivers while keeping their attention on the road, with Panasonic offering in-house-designed, cost-effective HUD technologies meeting specific OEM needs, including windshield HUDs and combiner HUD systems.
- In-Vehicle Infotainment (IVI) solutions provide audio/video entertainment and information content including radio, media player, TV and video, telephony, Navigation, speech control, Apps, and Connectivity features to help OEMs provide a holistic electronic cockpit concept.
- Telematics Control Units (TCU) assist in locating the vehicle's position, making emergency calls during accidents or breakdowns, tracking stolen vehicles, recording diagnostic codes for service stations, and providing holistic information about vehicle health, riding/driving mode, tyre pressure, and fuel level/battery charge status via a mobile application.
- Panasonic provides Back/Lane Watch cameras integrated in Outer Rear View mirrors (ORVM) and rear view cameras for parking assistance to cover blindspots and avoid driver distraction.
- Vehicle sensors are provided for 2W and 4W applications for Engine applications, Speed monitoring, Ambient Temperature, and Crank Angle Detection.
- Panasonic provides high quality and reliable LED modules for headlamps, winkers, and tail lights.
- Panasonic's wireless chargers use moving coil technology with a proprietary algorithm to automatically detect the device's coil and align it with the charger's coil, improving alignment accuracy and enabling highly efficient charging.
