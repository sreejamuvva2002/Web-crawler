# U.S. Department of Energy Loan Programs Office

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-30

**Source:** [Introducing the LPO Portfolio Projects Dynamic Map | Department of Energy](https://www.energy.gov/edf/articles/introducing-lpo-portfolio-projects-dynamic-map)  ·  `page_029247`

## Facts

- LPO has launched a new portfolio projects dynamic map that showcases its clean energy and advanced technology vehicle manufacturing projects, including active conditional commitments and loans, across the United States.
- The Office of Energy Dominance Financing announced the launch on September 21, 2023.
- Visitors can use search filters to track and dynamically search all of LPO’s publicly announced projects by state, loan type (e.g., direct loan, loan guarantee, partial loan guarantee), technology sector, and loan status (e.g., active, conditional, repaid).
- The map's project details feature connects search results to every LPO project while summarizing statistics by customized sub-portfolio searches.
- The map provides geographic visualization that gives a dynamic geographic representation of LPO’s projects, enabling visitors to see the distribution of initiatives across the nation and identify regional trends and opportunities.
- Visitors can consult the map for the latest information on LPO’s portfolio, including new conditional commitment and financial close announcements.
