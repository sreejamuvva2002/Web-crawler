# Lund International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-10-24

**Source:** [Automotive - LUND INTERNATIONAL RECEIVES “GEORGIA AUTOMOTIVE COMPANY OF THE YEAR” AWARD](https://kahnmedia.com/lund-international-receives-georgia-automotive-company-year-award/)  ·  `page_045621`

## Facts

- Lund International was selected as the recipient of the "Georgia Automotive Company of the Year" award for 2017 by the Georgia Department of Economic Development.
- The Georgia Automotive Awards are presented by Georgia Governor Nathan Deal to recognize individual companies for their contributions to the state and communities.
- The awards celebrate the automotive industry's collective accomplishments in Georgia, which is considered a premier destination for automotive manufacturers and technology companies developing the future transportation system.
- Lund International was recognized for its active participation in statewide, regional, national, and international activities demonstrating pride in its Georgia location and contributions to civic organizations and initiatives.
- Lund International CEO Mitch Fogle stated that the award represents the culmination of thousands of hours of work and tireless days spent perfecting every company product seen on vehicles worldwide.
- CEO Mitch Fogle added that the honor motivates the company to work harder at innovating, producing industry-leading products, and providing uncompromising customer service.
- Lund International designs, engineers, manufactures, and markets branded automotive aftermarket accessories for passenger cars, SUVs, CUVs, light trucks, Jeeps, and heavy trucks.
- Lund International employs 1,200 culturally diverse workers, with 500 based in Georgia, many of whom are multi-generational.
- Nearly all of Lund International's products are manufactured in the USA, which is culturally important to the company.
- Lund International is headquartered in Buford, Georgia, and is the U.S.-based leading designer and manufacturer of automotive accessories for passenger cars, Jeeps, and light, medium, and heavy-duty trucks.
- The company offers a broad range of functional body and appearance products for vehicle protection, customization, and personalization under the brand names AMP Research, AVS, Belmor, Bushwacker, LUND, Rampage Products, Roadworks Manufacturing, Roll-N-Lock, Stampede Automotive Accessories, and Tonno Pro.
