## Facts

- Over the last three years, electric transportation companies continue to choose Georgia for their new locations, while current companies expand to meet demand.
- Rivian is establishing its East Coast headquarters in Atlanta.
- RJ Scaringe, Founder and CEO of Rivian, stated that Atlanta embodies top talent, exceptional creativity, and a desire to always be moving forward.
- The Rivian East Coast head office will feature a 500-person office space.
- The Rivian office space is intended to complement a new, state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Georgia.
- Rivian's headquarters announcement was sourced from a Rivian Press Release dated 7.72025.
- South Korea-based Doowon Climate Control is creating 200 jobs in Metter, Georgia.
- Doowon Climate Control's new manufacturing facility is located in the Metter-Candler County I-16 Industrial Park.
- The Doowon facility will primarily produce climate control systems for use in traditional and electric vehicles.
- Ki Chun Sung, CEO of Doowon Climate Control America, Inc., stated that the company is making significant investments to establish its inaugural automotive air conditioning system production facility in the United States.
- Doowon Climate Control America expressed optimism that its technological expertise and industry experience will play a vital role in the growth of the local community.
- The Doowon Climate Control announcement was published by Area Development News Desk on 2.7.2024.
