# Solvay Battery-Grade Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF) Manufacturing Facility

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-30

**Source:** [DOE/EA-2237: Draft Environmental Assessment (November 2024) | Department of Energy](https://www.energy.gov/nepa/articles/doeea-2237-draft-environmental-assessment-november-2024)  ·  `page_029404`

## Facts

- NETL prepared a Draft EA (DOE/EA-2237) for the Solvay Battery-Grade Polyvinylidene Fluoride (PVDF) Manufacturing Facility project in Richmond County, Georgia.
- The Draft EA evaluates the potential environmental consequences of DOE providing financial assistance to Solvay Specialty Polymers USA, LLC (a member of the Syensqo Group).
- The project involves the construction of a chemical manufacturing facility for the production of PVDF (a highly non-reactive thermoplastic) for use in electric vehicle batteries.
- The Draft Environmental Assessment was published on November 14, 2024.
