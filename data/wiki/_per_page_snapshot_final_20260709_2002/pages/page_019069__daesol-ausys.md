# Daesol Ausys

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [South Korean company to build $72 million electric vehicle parts plant in Georgia — Motor World](https://motorworld.app/south-korean-company-to-build-72-million-electric-vehicle-parts-plant-in-georgia/)  ·  `page_019069`

## Facts

- A South Korean automaker plans to build a $72 million plant in Georgia to make parts for electric vehicles.
- This will be the third floor located in the same West Point commercial park for members of the company’s family group.
- Daehan Solution Co. opened a million-euro factory in 2008 to manufacture roof cladding and other sound insulation parts for the area.
- Daesol Material opened a $35 million factory there in 2017 to manufacture fiber upholstery for automobiles.
- Daehan Solution now employs 350 full-time workers, while Daesol Material employs 50 workers.
- Meghan Richardson serves as an economic developer for the city of West Point.
- Daehan Solution also has a plant near Montgomery, Alabama, which manufactures Hyundai’s assembly plant.
- The new facility is part of a wave of electric vehicle suppliers that announced factories in Georgia after Hyundai announced in 2022 that it would build a $5.5 billion plant to gather electric vehicles and batteries in Ellabell, Georgia, near Savannah.
- The Hyundai plant in Ellabell is expected to start generating cars in 2025.
- Suppliers have pledged to invest about $2.2 billion and hire 5,000 people.
- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp stated that the state’s auto industry employs more than 50,000 Georgian workers and will continue to grow as providers of electric mobility in each and every corner of the state for the jobs of the future.
- Daesol Ausys is expected to start production in December 2024.
- The company will pay between $15 and $20 an hour.
- The state will pay for the exercise of personnel.
- The state donated $500,000 last year to Harris County to clean up and level the site and build a road, a budget the county matched through sales tax collection.
- The city of West Point and the county are offering 8 years of asset tax exemptions, worth an estimated $5.7 million.
- Daesol Ausys could earn benefits of $875,000 in income tax credits, at a rate of $1,250 per job over five years, as long as employees earn at least $31,300 per year.
