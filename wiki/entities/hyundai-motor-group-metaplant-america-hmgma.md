# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA)

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Bryan County Mega site, Georgia, Bryan County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

A new electric vehicle and battery manufacturing plant announced by Hyundai Motor Group in the Bryan County Mega site, expected to create over 8,100 jobs and begin commercial production in the first half of 2025.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The plant will build EV batteries and assemble Hyundai, Genesis, and Kia electric vehicles, supporting the U.S. EV supply chain.

## Key facts

- HMGMA is expected to add more than 8,100 jobs as it works to build a reliable supply chain for EV batteries and other EV parts on the American market.
- "Hyundai and the people of Georgia share many qualities... we are determined to be the global leader in electrification, safety, quality, and sustainability," said Executive Chair Euisun Chung.
- "Our partnership with Hyundai Motor Group and the groundbreaking of this innovative facility exemplifies unprecedented success," Governor Brian Kemp stated.
- The plant will produce a range of cutting-edge Hyundai, Genesis, and Kia electric cars alongside battery production.
- A future technology expo at Savannah’s Enmarket Arena highlighted advanced technology and human achievement following the groundbreaking ceremony.
- Georgia’s award-winning workforce development programs are cited as key to supporting the new facility’s employment impact.
- HMGMA uses a hyper-flexible manufacturing architecture that can switch between hybrid combustion systems and full-electric battery architectures with minimal retooling.
- Boston Dynamics robot dogs patrol the floor for thermal anomalies, illustrating advanced robotics integration.
- Workers at HMGMA are referred to as "Meta Pros," emphasizing the shift toward cognitive oversight of automated systems.
- The plant’s flexibility is positioned as a competitive advantage against rigid Gigafactory models that struggle to pivot between powertrain types.
- Agentic AI is described as the future cognitive layer that will orchestrate real-time supply chain and factory floor operations.
- The author observed autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) delivering the Sportage Hybrid during the ceremony.
- State governments and corporate HR departments are urged to provide substantial subsidies for upskilling workers in robotics, AI diagnostics, and systems management.
