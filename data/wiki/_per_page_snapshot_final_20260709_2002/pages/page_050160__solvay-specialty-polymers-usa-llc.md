# Solvay Specialty Polymers USA LLC

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-04

**Source:** [View virtual hearing on emissions by Solvay’s Augusta plant](https://www.wrdw.com/2025/02/04/view-virtual-hearing-emissions-by-solvays-augusta-plant/)  ·  `page_050160`

## Facts

- The Georgia Environmental Protection Division will hold a virtual hearing on Tuesday evening about Solvay Specialty Polymers USA LLC.
- Solvay is a chemical plant seeking a permit to significantly expand its operations in Augusta, Ga.
- The virtual hearing was requested by Savannah Riverkeeper and 17 local groups.
- Southern Environmental Law Center and Savannah Riverkeeper are concerned about plans for more air emissions from the facility.
- The groups state that Solvay’s current permit classifies it as a minor source of pollution that relies on weak and unenforceable limits.
- In a news release, the groups stated that “Given the potential for significant pollution, Solvay should be classified as a major source and held to stricter emissions standards.”
- The hearing begins at 6 p.m.
