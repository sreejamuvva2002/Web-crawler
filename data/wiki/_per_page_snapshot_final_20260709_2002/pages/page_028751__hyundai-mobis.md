# Hyundai Mobis

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-09-14

**Source:** [Hyundai Mobis takes a new step with future mobility through further expansion in the North American market](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/hyundai-mobis-takes-a-new-step-with-future-mobility-through-further-expansion-in-the-north-american-market-301624362.html)  ·  `page_028751`

## Facts

- Hyundai Mobis presented its expansion strategy in the North American market with highly value-added technologies at the NAIAS 2022 Press Conference.
- The company is planning to expand production facilities worldwide, including a new EV components plant for prompt supply to North American automakers.
- Hyundai Mobis aims to reach about 30-40% CAGR in the North American market by 2030 with future mobility technologies.
- Hyundai Mobis (KRX 012330), the sixth largest global automotive supplier, introduced its expansion strategy in the North American market during a press conference at the North American International Auto Show (NAIAS) on Sept. 14, 2022.
- Axel Maschka, Executive Vice President and head of Hyundai Mobis Global OE Sales, stated that the two keywords of the company's growth strategy are Plant Expansion and Value Expansion.
- The company plans to expand its production facilities across the globe for prompt and timely supply to global automakers.
- Axel Maschka said that Hyundai Mobis will construct more EV dedicated sites in the region and invest in EV components and battery system assemblies to allow rapid and environmentally optimized local production.
- Hyundai Mobis currently operates 44 production bases, with North American facilities located in Alabama, Georgia, Ohio, and Michigan.
- North America leading the electric vehicle shift to achieve carbon neutrality and green mobility is integral to the company's electrification production expansion plan.
- The company believes having a strong production capacity will be key to winning sales orders as the EV market continues to grow.
- A new manufacturing site, combined with over ten years of expertise in producing high-quality electrification components, will allow Hyundai Mobis to see continued growth in the market.
- Hyundai Mobis revealed a value expansion plan to increase its sales orders in the North American market with value-added technologies optimized specifically for electric and autonomous vehicles.
- The company presented future mobility technologies including the electric Complete Chassis Platform Module (eCCPM) and next-generation AR HUD.
- Axel Maschka stated that Hyundai Mobis has a wide range of innovations in its portfolio, is ready for the future of mobility, and aims to bring the future to the present and make it more valuable.
- The company highlighted its successful mass-production of key electrification components, including the battery system for the e-GMP platform used in Hyundai and Kia electric vehicles, and the PE module.
- Value-added products including the eCCPM module, electrification platform, AR HUD, and lighting grilles are drawing attention from clients in North America for their versatility and innovation.
- During the presentation, Hyundai Mobis announced its goal of driving a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 36% in the North American market through the year 2030.
- The company reported steady growth in total order volumes over the last few years, with $660 million USD in 2020, $1.4 billion USD in 2021, and $1.7 billion USD in the first half of 2022.
- Hyundai Mobis is the global no. 6 automotive supplier, headquartered in Seoul, Korea.
- The company has outstanding expertise in sensors, sensor fusion in ECUs and software development for safety control.
- Hyundai Mobis's products include various components for electrification, brakes, chassis and suspension, steering, airbags, lighting and automotive electronics.
- The company operates an R&D headquarter in Korea, with four technology centers in the United States, Germany, China and India.
