# Yazaki North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Yazaki is an auto parts maker based in Japan, and one of the world’s largest privately-owned automotive suppliers.
- Yazaki produces wire harnesses, power distribution and control products, connectors, driver information systems, and sensors.
- Yazaki produces products for hybrid and electric vehicles.
- Yazaki is a supplier to virtually every major automotive manufacturer in the world.
- Yazaki has locations in 46 countries.
- Yazaki’s focus on international trade includes customs modernization, export controls, and ESG compliance.
- Ramiro Munoz serves as Yazaki’s Director of Customs and Global Trade Compliance.
- Ramiro Munoz has 18 years of experience in international trade.
- Ramiro Munoz is a licensed customs broker and a certified customs specialist.
- Ramiro Munoz has a special interest in the corporate compliance aspects of U.S. Customs and Border Protection’s modernized customs broker regulations, which went into effect in December.
- The modernized customs broker regulations are one of the panel topics at AAEI’s 102nd Annual Conference and Expo.
- AAEI’s 102nd Annual Conference and Expo will be held in Washington, DC, from June 19-21, 2023.
- Before taking his role at Yazaki, Ramiro Munoz served as Manager of Global Customs-Trade Compliance at Livingston International.
