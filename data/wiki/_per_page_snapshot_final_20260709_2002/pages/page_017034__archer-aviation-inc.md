## Facts

- Archer Aviation, Inc. is a California-based aerospace company developing electric vertical takeoff and landing (eVTOL) aircraft designed to serve as sustainable urban air mobility solutions.
- The company was founded in 2018 by Adam Goldstein and Brett Adcock.
- Archer focuses on the design, development and certification of zero-emissions air taxis aimed at reducing traffic congestion in densely populated metropolitan areas.
- The company's flagship prototypes are named “Maker” and “Midnight.”
- The prototypes have been engineered to deliver quiet, efficient short-haul flights with ranges of up to 100 miles per charge.
- Archer is headquartered in Palo Alto, California.
- The company operates a manufacturing facility in nearby Santa Cruz County.
- Archer maintains research partnerships with automotive and energy companies.
- Archer has a collaboration with Stellantis to integrate advanced battery systems.
