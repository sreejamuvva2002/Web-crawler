# Michelin Retread Technologies

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Michelin installs new retread press at plant | Bulk Transporter](https://www.bulktransporter.com/archive/article/21651389/michelin-installs-new-retread-press-at-plant)  ·  `page_046512`

## Facts

- Michelin Retread Technologies (MRT) is increasing its production capacity, adding a new press at its Covington GA operation that can produce one million new treads per year.
- The new MRT press has been designed to produce new high-technology treads and to take advantage of Michelin's advanced co-extrusion technique, a process where two different rubber compounds are combined to ensure longer life and cooler casing temperatures, maximizing the life of the casing.
- Michelin is currently the only manufacturer in the world offering co-extruded treads to its retread customers.
- The new press also will allow the company to expand its offering of wider treads for Michelin's X One wide-single tire.
- Expansion of the Covington tread pressing plant is the first major expansion since the facility opened in 1999.
- The new retreading press along with other future additions is expected to double the volume of the facility.
- The facility currently produces a variety of Pre-Mold treads for MRT franchises and retread plants throughout North America.
