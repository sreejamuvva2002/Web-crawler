# Hyundai Motor Group Vehicle Battery Plant

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Kingston, GA, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Hyundai Motor Group and SK On are building an electric vehicle battery manufacturing facility on a 600-acre site in Kingston, Georgia, slated to open in 2025. The project is described as one of the largest economic development initiatives in the state's history.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The plant will produce lithium-ion batteries for electric vehicles, adding a critical manufacturing step to Georgia's EV supply chain.

## Key facts

- One of the largest economic development projects in Georgia’s history.
- The 600-acre site requires approximately 10.2 million cubic yards of material (soil, rip rock, blast rock) to be moved during construction.
- Earthwork is scheduled for roughly ten months.
- Contour provides soil testing, earthwork observation, rock verification and quantification, settlement monitoring, and NPDES monitoring.
