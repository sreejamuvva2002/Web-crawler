# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2026-01-05

## Facts

- Hanon Systems is a global automotive thermal energy management solutions corporation under the Hankook & Company group.
- Vice chairman and CEO Lee Su-il distributed a 2026 new year address to global employees outlining management goals and mid-to-long-term growth strategy.
- The company achieved annual sales of 10 trillion won for the first time in its history in 2025.
- Hanon Systems targets 11 trillion won in sales for 2026.
- A core goal is restoring profitability and achieving an operating margin (EBIT) level of 5%.
- The company plans to improve profitability and cash flow simultaneously through cost structure improvement and enhanced operational efficiency.
- An execution-focused management policy will be established.
- Following the completion of an ongoing paid-in capital increase, the debt ratio is expected to improve from about 246% at the end of the third quarter to about 164%.
- This financial improvement is expected to create a more stable environment regarding financial soundness and cash flow.
- Hanon Systems created a dedicated software organization over the past year.
- The company reorganized its global R&D organization and strengthened the efficiency of technology development and collaboration systems.
- Hanon Systems is positioning its technical and software capabilities to respond to automakers' multi-powertrain strategies (including battery electric vehicles, hybrid, plug-in hybrid, and EREV) as a core axis of mid-to-long-term growth.
- The company is reviewing new business opportunities based on thermal management technologies and long experience, driven by surging power and thermal management demand from AI data centers.
- New business opportunities include liquid cooling systems for data centers and ESS thermal management solutions.
- These new businesses are expected to contribute to sales growth and improved profitability in the mid to long term.
- Hanon Systems participated in AAPEX, the largest aftermarket exhibition in North America, last November.
- The company gained experience at major aftermarket exhibitions in the Middle East.
- A global aftermarket dedicated organization was newly established in January 2026.
- Centered on this new organization, Hanon Systems plans to expand its aftermarket business to make a tangible contribution to sales growth and improved profitability.
- Vice chairman and CEO Lee Su-il stated that 2025 focused on improving management efficiency and strengthening fundamentals, while 2026 will focus on completing that work, executing the mid-to-long-term strategy, and turning it into results.
- Lee Su-il emphasized responsible management to strengthen fundamentals and continue sustainable growth through expanding new businesses.
