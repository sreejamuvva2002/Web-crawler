## Facts

- North Point Volvo Cars operates as a Volvo dealership near Atlanta serving motorists in Alpharetta, GA.
- The dealership is located at 1570 Mansell Rd, Alpharetta, GA 30009.
- The new vehicle inventory includes Volvo S60 and S90 sedans, as well as the XC40, XC60, and XC90 SUVs.
- The new Volvo XC90 features three rows of seating for six or seven passengers, up to 85.7 cubic feet of cargo space, a towing capacity of up to 5,000 pounds when properly equipped, and built-in Google Assistant.
- The dealership offers a used vehicle inventory that customers can shop by age, mileage, price, or features.
- Certified pre-owned Volvo models must have fewer than 80,000 miles and undergo a thorough 170-point vehicle inspection.
- Certified pre-owned vehicles include a CARFAX Vehicle History Report, a limited warranty, and roadside assistance.
- The dealership provides financing and leasing options to help customers understand their buying power.
- Customers can trade in or sell their current vehicles through the dealership's online service.
- The on-site service center offers maintenance for oil changes, brake service, tire rotation, and EV battery maintenance.
- The dealership sells replacement parts and accessories for Volvo models.
