# Toyota Industries Compressor Parts America Co

## Facts

- Toyota Industries Compressor Parts America Co (TICA) is located in Pendergrass, Georgia.
- The company was established in 2004.
- TICA specializes in manufacturing automobile air conditioning compressors and DC/AC inverters.
- TICA supplies vehicle air conditioning compressors to over half of the North American auto industry.
- The company assembles inverters that power plug-in devices in vehicles, supporting the electrification of automotive systems.
- TICA serves several well-known automotive brands including Ford, Honda, BMW, Lexus, Subaru, Fiat Chrysler, and Toyota.
- The company has a workforce of approximately 1,300 associates.
- TICA is a key member of the Toyota Industries Group.
