# TI Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2005-02-23

**Source:** [TI Automotive Names Manager for New Georgia Facility - AftermarketNews](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/ti-automotive-names-manager-for-new-georgia-facility/)  ·  `page_011515`

## Facts

- Joseph Roznowski has been named manager of the new production facility TI Automotive plans to open later this year in Hartwell, Ga.
- The new facility will be 145,000 square feet.
- The plant will supply blow-molded fuel tanks and filler pipes for automakers located in the southeastern United States.
- Roznowski serves as manufacturing planning director for North American Fuel Systems at TI Automotive.
- Roznowski guided the new plant’s design and construction, and coordinated facility requirements for Global Fuel Systems locations in North America.
- Roznowski joined TI Automotive in 2000 and previously served as director of its Global Test Group in Auburn Hills, Michigan.
- TI Automotive is a supplier of fluid storage, transfer and delivery systems including brake, fuel and air conditioning applications.
- TI Automotive is based in Warren, Michigan.
- TI Automotive employs more than 20,000 people at more than 130 facilities in 29 countries on six continents.
