# Meritor Inc.

**Type:** supplier_relationship  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-12-06

**Source:** [Meritor announces supply agreements with Wabash National, Manitowoc Co.](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/shopmanagement/meritor-announces-supply-agreements-with-wabash-national-manitowoc-co-)  ·  `page_014629`

## Facts

- Meritor Inc., Troy, Mich., announced that various products have been selected as standard position and as preferred options on all Wabash National Corp. dry and refrigerated van trailer products.
- The three-year agreement positions Meritor's loose axles as standard equipment.
- The Meritor Tire Inflation System has been selected as the preferred automatic tire inflation system.
- The company’s air disc brakes are optional in all Wabash configurations.
- Meritor Trailing-Arm (MTA) suspension is optional in all Wabash configurations.
- Hubs are optional in all Wabash configurations.
- Drums and slack adjusters are optional in all Wabash configurations.
- MTec6™ large-diameter axle is optional in all Wabash configurations.
- The company announced a strategic supply agreement with The Manitowoc Co. Inc., a provider of lifting equipment, to deliver multiple drivetrain products, including planetary axles, to the manufacturer.
- Meritor products will be featured on several crane types.
