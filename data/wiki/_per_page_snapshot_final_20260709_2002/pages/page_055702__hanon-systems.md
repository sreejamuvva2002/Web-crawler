# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2023-08-10

**Source:** [Hanon Systems Appoints Nurdal Kücükkaya and Ramachandran (Subu)](https://www.gurufocus.com/news/2061977/hanon-systems-appoints-nurdal-kckkaya-and-ramachandran-subu-nagasubramony-as-cochief-executive-officers)  ·  `page_055702`

## Facts

- Global automotive thermal and energy management solutions supplier Hanon Systems announced its board of directors has approved the appointment of Nurdal Kücükkaya and Ramachandran (Subu) Nagasubramony as co-chief executive officers with immediate effect.
- Nagasubramony was appointed co-CEO along with a promotion to President, leading alongside President and co-CEO Nurdal Kücükkaya.
- Kücükkaya has been serving as President and representative executive officer of Hanon Systems.
- Nagasubramony served as the company's chief financial officer (CFO) since Jan. 1, 2019, and will retain his role as CFO.
- Both executives have a long tenure serving in executive roles with the company and more than three decades of automotive industry experience.
- Hanon Systems is a full-line supplier of automotive thermal and energy management solutions for electrified and conventional vehicles.
- Its offering includes a wide range of solutions in heating ventilation and air conditioning, powertrain cooling, compressor, fluid transport, and electronics and fluid pressure.
- The company currently operates 50 manufacturing sites and three regional innovation centers.
- Hanon Systems employs more than 22,000 people across 21 countries.
