# Rivian Georgia Manufacturing Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Rivian EV Manufacturing Plant | Clayco](https://claycorp.com/project/rivian-manufacturing-plant)  ·  `page_000720`

## Facts

- Clayco and Rivian are partnering to create a manufacturing plant aimed at growing electric vehicle adoption with deep commitments to sustainability.
- Clayco will build Rivian’s 1800-acre, state-of-the-art manufacturing plant at Stanton Springs, Georgia.
- The eco-conscious facility is slated to be operational by 2028.
- The project is expected to create approximately 7,500 employment opportunities.
- AEI will serve as the engineer of record for the project.
- Skidmore, Owings & Merrill (SOM) will act as the design architect.
- The comprehensive facility encompasses specialized areas including a stamping shop, body shop, paint shop, WWTP (Wastewater Treatment Plant), general assembly building, battery and drive unit shop, central utility plant, office and main entry building, and miscellaneous support facilities.
- Vehicle production is scheduled to commence in 2026.
- Clayco and LJC are delivering the Paint Shop and Civil scopes of work under a Design-Build delivery model.
- Groundbreaking for the manufacturing project occurred outside Social Circle, Georgia.
- Rivian’s new Georgia facility will support its global expansion and provide the scale to supply millions of electric vehicles.
- Clayco, Rivian, and CCDI hosted booths at a local career fair to promote manufacturing and construction careers.
- Four high schools sent juniors and seniors to explore career opportunities at the event.
- The Rivian team brought newer vehicle models to the career fair.
- Clayco provided a VR demo at the career fair while CCDI occupied an adjacent booth.
