# Superior Essex Communications

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** 2019-06-13

**Source:** [Superior Essex Communications Joins materialsCAN Collective - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.ms/news/superior-essex-communications-joins-materialscan-collective/)  ·  `page_010540`

## Facts

- Superior Essex Communications announced its membership in materialsCAN (Materials Carbon Action Network).
- materialsCAN is a global building industry network focused on prioritizing carbon reduction actions in construction materials and the built environment.
- The collective was launched in 2018 by Interface, Inc., Skanska, Gensler, USG, Saint-Gobain, and Armstrong Ceilings.
- Superior Essex Communications and Kingspan Insulated Panel are the newest members of the collaboration.
- Annie Bevan, Global Head of Sustainability for Superior Essex Communications, stated that membership demonstrates support for collective global sustainability initiatives.
- Superior Essex is the only cable manufacturer offering EPD, HPD, and externally verified GreenCircle Multi-Attribute Label for over 55 product families.
- The company offers the world's only Red Free communication cable registry and has Living Product Challenge certifications for various product families.
- All products are manufactured in Zero Waste to Landfill certified facilities designed to help customers achieve green building certifications like Living Building Challenge, WELL, and LEED v4/v4.1.
- Superior Essex is optimizing its product lifecycle impact to prove low carbon footprints and help buildings reduce overall carbon emissions.
- Smart sustainable buildings using Power-over-Ethernet technology support resilient design by lowering energy consumption and operational carbon.
- materialsCAN outlined a five-point action plan for architects, designers, contractors, and manufacturers to reduce embodied carbon in the supply chain.
- Lisa Conway, VP of Sustainability Americas at Interface, expressed excitement about Superior Essex joining the growing collective to advance education and action across the value chain.
- Superior Essex is a leading manufacturer and supplier of communication cables and accessories serving the communication and distribution industries for over 80 years.
- The company's headquarters/contact address is located in Atlanta, GA.
