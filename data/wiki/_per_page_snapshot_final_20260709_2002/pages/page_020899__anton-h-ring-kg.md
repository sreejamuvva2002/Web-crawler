## Facts

- Anton Häring KG is a family-run technology company with more than 4,000 employees across five locations on four continents, including 300 apprentices and students.
- The company is a leading specialist in precision manufacturing and simultaneous engineering, developing and producing complex parts and assemblies in large and medium series.
- Customers come from around the world, specifically in the automotive industry.
- Häring produces around 3 million components per day, each meeting the highest standards.
- Components are critical elements of highly complex systems and are used in key technologies such as brake systems, hydrogen valves, and hydraulic cylinders.
- Precision parts are often only a few centimeters in size and are manufactured with tolerances in the micrometer range — one-thousandth of a millimeter, about one-hundredth the thickness of a human hair.
- Parts perform critical functions: enabling movement, maintaining stable pressure, opening and closing passages, sealing systems reliably, and guiding fluids or gases precisely under extreme conditions.
- The company operates 1,300 000 ft² of global production space and around 1,000 production machines to handle a wide range of customer orders.
- Core expertise lies in creating optimal production processes for customer-specific parts from concept to series production using a simultaneous engineering approach where production experts are involved early in product development.
- Manufacturing competencies include forming and machining of diverse raw materials, hardening, coating and other finishing processes, as well as assembly of complete units.
- The company combines processes flexibly rather than relying on a single technology to deliver efficient and precise solutions for customers across a wide range of sectors.
- Häring takes ecological, economic, and social responsibility by supporting environmental protection at all locations and assisting communities, public institutions, and schools.
- Through its own educational initiatives, the company fosters young talent and creates opportunities for future generations.
- Employee support includes housing assistance, modern company restaurants, and other offerings designed to improve everyday work life.
