# Hyundai Transys

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-04-17

**Source:** [News | HYUNDAI TRANSYS](https://www.hyundai-transys.com/en/media/news-view.do?keyword=&nSeq=1704&Seq=1704)  ·  `page_009572`

## Facts

- Hyundai Transys is implementing its self-developed AI system, “Transys Advanced Data Analytics (TADA),” across its production sites to boost quality competitiveness.
- TADA encompasses developing AI systems for smart manufacturing technology, a big data platform for data-driven decision-making, and training programs to enhance employees’ digital transformation capabilities.
- The “TADA Smart Solution” and “TADA Edge Solution” are self-developed programs utilizing AI deep learning technology to enhance production efficiency and defect inspection accuracy on the manufacturing floor.
- TADA Smart Solution is a vision inspection system where deep learning AI detects defects during scanning, identifying small bubbles, damage, etc., in parts during manufacturing and assembly.
- Trained with high-quality component X-ray images, the TADA Smart Solution improved inspection accuracy from 93% to 99.9%, detecting defects that human inspectors might miss.
- As of July 2024, Hyundai Transys has implemented the TADA Smart Solution in 7 factories in Seosan, including Jigok and Seongyeon.
- The TADA Smart Solution has been implemented in 54 processes at the Georgia Powertrain Plant in the United States.
- Hyundai Transys has seen cost savings and productivity improvements by introducing a commercial deep learning program developed in-house.
- TADA Edge Solution is a small AI inspection system designed to allow nonexperts to easily create and apply customized deep learning models on-site.
- The TADA Edge Solution checks the number of bolts assembled, mounting status, and joint lifting inspection at four Hyundai Transys Seosan Plant locations.
- Hyundai Transys supports partners with the TADA Edge Solution to help establish smart factories and enhance collaborative management.
- The TADA Edge Solution can be installed at one-tenth the cost of traditional AI deep learning inspection equipment (vision systems).
- The TADA Edge Solution does not require network construction work or specialized maintenance technicians, allowing small and medium-sized partners to enhance production efficiency and reduce defect rates effortlessly.
- The TADA Edge Solution is currently installed and operational at a partner company responsible for manufacturing sheet parts.
- Hyundai Transys has installed AI-based responsive CCTV in four areas of the Jigok Plant to monitor where logistics vehicles and forklifts frequently pass through.
- These cameras sound an alarm to prevent accidents by alerting workers when vehicles approach.
- Around 30 responsive CCTVs detect equipment damage and immediately send alerts and messages to facility managers in case of oil or wastewater leaks.
- The Electrification R&D Division at Hyundai Transys has incorporated AI technology into the durability testing process for powertrain gear development.
- Gears used in transmissions and reducers are crucial components that affect a vehicle’s safety, efficiency, and quietness.
- A deep learning AI measurement machine can now analyze the damage range and complete the report within an hour for powertrain gear durability tests.
- This task previously took researchers up to 24 hours to determine the damaged areas in gear durability tests.
- The collected test data can assist in root cause analysis and the design process.
- Kim Youngwook, director of Hyundai Transys ICT Group, stated that since 2022, they have been shifting employees’ work methods to be data-driven through TADA in-house training.
- Kim Youngwook added that Hyundai Transys will continue to strive for digital innovation and improved work efficiency across all business areas, including production, development, and support.
