# Hyundai Mobis

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Governor Ivey Announces Hyundai Mobis Plans EV Battery Module Plant in Montgomery, Creating 400 Jobs - EIN Presswire](https://www.einnews.com/pr_news/598142188/governor-ivey-announces-hyundai-mobis-plans-ev-battery-module-plant-in-montgomery-creating-400-jobs)  ·  `page_001188`

## Facts

- Governor Kay Ivey joined executives of Hyundai Mobis to announce the company’s plans to invest $205 million to open an EV battery module plant in Montgomery.
- The new plant will eventually employ at least 400 people.
- Once it reaches full production, the 450,000-square-foot facility will be able to supply over 200,000 EV batteries annually to Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama (HMMA) in Montgomery and the Kia Georgia plant.
- Governor Kay Ivey stated that Alabama’s auto manufacturing sector is evolving rapidly to capitalize on the EV revolution sweeping the industry, and this new battery plant represents another milestone in that transition.
- Governor Ivey expressed excitement about the company’s new investment and what it represents for the next chapter of automaking in Alabama.
- The formal announcement ceremony took place Thursday morning in the Old House Chamber at the Alabama State Capitol.
- H.S. Oh, vice president of the electric powertrain business for Hyundai Mobis, said the new Alabama EV battery assembly facility will play a critical role in expanding the company’s global production network.
- H.S. Oh noted that having strong production capacity will be key to allowing Hyundai Mobis to see continued growth as the EV market continues to grow.
- H.S. Oh stated that the new Montgomery facility will be an important step in that process and expressed excitement for the project to be under way.
- The EV battery module plant expands Hyundai Mobis’ already large presence in Montgomery.
- Hyundai Mobis has operated a manufacturing facility on 83 acres in Montgomery since 2002.
- This existing facility is the largest Tier 1 supplier to HMMA and Kia Georgia.
- The existing plant supplies auto plants with complete cockpit modules, front and rear chassis modules, bumper assemblies, and instrument panels.
- Alabama Department of Commerce Secretary Greg Canfield stated that Hyundai Mobis’ investment reflects an acceleration in the development of the EV supply chain in Alabama’s auto industry.
- Greg Canfield added that Alabama will be a major production player in the EV market, which will trigger more growth within the sector.
- Construction on the new facility is expected to begin as early as December.
- The new facility will be located on the HMMA campus just off Interstate 65 in Montgomery.
- Production of EV battery assemblies is projected to begin in 2024.
- Earlier this year, Hyundai announced plans to begin EV production at its Montgomery manufacturing plant through a $300 million investment that will create 200 jobs.
- Hyundai’s Alabama workforce will produce the all-electric Genesis GV70 luxury SUV and a hybrid electric version of the Santa Fe SUV.
- Montgomery Mayor Steven Reed stated that Montgomery and Hyundai Mobis have a powerful partnership and expressed hope to continue strengthening it while supporting the investment.
- Mayor Reed noted this is a continuation of the biggest year for economic development in the history of their city.
- Montgomery County Commission Chairman Doug Singleton stated that their commitment to the partnership with Hyundai Mobis continues to deepen and they are honored by the significant investment.
- Chairman Singleton added that together they are charting the course for success and opportunity in the River Region.
- Montgomery Chamber of Commerce Chairman Cedric Campbell stated that partnership and teamwork are key to this economic growth and expressed gratitude for the impact on the entire region.
- Seoul, South Korean-based Hyundai Mobis is a longtime supply partner for Hyundai Motor Co. and its Kia and Genesis affiliates.
- Besides providing major component systems for the automakers, Hyundai Mobis also offers after-service parts.
