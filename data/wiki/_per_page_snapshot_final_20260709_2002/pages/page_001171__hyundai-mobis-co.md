# Hyundai Mobis Co.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hyundai Mobis to build EV module plant in Ulsan | Yonhap News Agency](https://en.yna.co.kr/view/AEN20240509002900320)  ·  `page_001171`

## Facts

- Hyundai Mobis Co., South Korea's leading auto parts maker, plans to build a 90 billion-won (US$65.8 million) electric vehicle (EV) module plant in the country's southeastern industrial city of Ulsan.
- The company signed a memorandum of understanding with the city government of Ulsan for the factory.
- The factory is envisioned to be built near a dedicated EV plant by Hyundai Motor Co. currently under construction in the city.
- Hyundai Mobis plans to supply chassis modules, driver seat modules and other components for Genesis EV models produced by Hyundai through this factory.
- The company aims to complete construction of the plant by the second half of 2025.
- Domestically, Hyundai Mobis operates battery system factories in Ulsan, Daegu, Cheongju and Pyeongtaek.
- Domestically, Hyundai Mobis operates power electric system factories in Ulsan, Daegu, Cheongju and Pyeongtaek.
- Overseas, Hyundai Mobis runs battery system plants in China for supply to Hyundai Motor Group.
- Overseas, Hyundai Mobis runs battery system plants in the Czech Republic for supply to Hyundai Motor Group.
- Hyundai Mobis is currently constructing a new battery system factory in Indonesia.
- Hyundai Mobis is establishing production bases to supply parts to Hyundai Motor Group's new EV plant under construction in the U.S. state of Georgia.
