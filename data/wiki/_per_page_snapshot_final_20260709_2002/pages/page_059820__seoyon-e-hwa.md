## Facts

- Seoyon E-Hwa is Korea’s representative automotive interior parts specialized company.
- The company produces door panels, bumpers, seats, headliners, rear parcel shelves, package trays, screen assemblies, and pillar posts both domestically and abroad.
- Seoyon E-Hwa was established in 1972 and spun off from its holding company in July 2014.
- The company began production with interior products for the Pony vehicle.
- Key management policies focus on R&D and securing quality and technological competitiveness by designing new materials, developing new products, and proposing new models to strengthen marketability.
- The company expands in domestic and overseas markets by specializing affiliates according to product characteristics and automaker production bases.
- Major global automaker customers include Mercedes-Benz, Ford, Volkswagen, Audi, and Nissan, in addition to domestic automakers.
- Seoyon E-Hwa is pursuing digital transformation away from human-centered decision-making toward data and information-oriented processes.
- The company has adopted an ESG management philosophy to fulfill corporate social responsibilities.
- Seoyon E-Hwa maintains independent infrastructure for product design, material development, testing, and evaluation, supported by bold investments in R&D and quality management.
- The company's address is 41-22 Burim-ro 170beon-gil, Dongan-gu, Anyang, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea.
