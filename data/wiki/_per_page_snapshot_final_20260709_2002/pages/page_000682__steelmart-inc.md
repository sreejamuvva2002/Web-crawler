# Steelmart Inc.

## Facts

- Steelmart Inc. processes and supplies steel to customers in Georgia, Alabama, South Carolina, North Carolina, and Tennessee.
- The company operates two physical store locations in Tucker and Gainesville for in-person purchases Monday through Friday.
- Steelmart maintains an extensive inventory of steel construction products, hardware, paint, and ornamental steel, with most items kept in stock for immediate pickup or delivery.
- Core processing services include bending and forming flat steel sheets and plates into custom shapes, welding and assembly using MIG, TIG, and stick welding, and coating and finishing via galvanizing, powder coating, and painting.
- The standard tolerance for all orders is +- ⅛” and the company can process exact critical tolerances as needed.
- Steelmart utilizes a 24-hour processing operation to minimize timing from order to delivery.
- Customers can place orders online via a quote request form, by phone, in person at store locations, or through dedicated inside sales representatives for existing accounts.
