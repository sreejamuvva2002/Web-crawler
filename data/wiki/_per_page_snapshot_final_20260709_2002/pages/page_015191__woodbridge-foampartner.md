# Woodbridge FoamPartner

## Facts

- Woodbridge FoamPartner is a Chattanooga, Tennessee-based manufacturer of slab urethane foams for a broad range of industries.
- Its markets include automotive, filtration, medical and industrial products.
- It is a joint venture of the Woodbridge Foam Corporation and the Foam Partner Group.
- The joint venture was first established in 2006.
- It began formal operations in January 2007.
- The Woodbridge Foam Corporation is a privately held Canadian company that has been in business since 1978.
- The Foam Partner Group has been operating since 1937 and is held by Conzetta Holdings, a publicly traded company on the Swiss Exchange.
- As joint venture partners, they market and distribute their own technical foams, most of which are produced at their Chattanooga facility.
- They also offer an array of non-technical products made internationally and domestically by partner companies.
- The company is committed to continuous improvement and encourages its employees to share ideas for improving quality and value.
- It respects honesty, integrity, workplace safety, sustainable environmental stewardship, fiscal prudence and enduring customer satisfaction.
