## Facts

- Georgia spent years wooing Hyundai, a foreign carmaker.
- A Hyundai electric-car factory is located just outside Ellabell, Georgia.
- On September 4th, federal agents arrested 475 foreign workers at a Hyundai electric-car factory just outside Ellabell, Georgia.
- Over 400 agents fanned out across the plant, ordering workers to present their papers or be carted off.
- Most of the people detained were South Korean nationals with the wrong kind of visas.
- Homeland Security declared it the biggest worksite raid ever conducted in the department’s two-decade history.
