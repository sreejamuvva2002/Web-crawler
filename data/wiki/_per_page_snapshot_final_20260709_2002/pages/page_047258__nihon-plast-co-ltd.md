## Facts

- Nihon Plast works with subsidiaries, affiliates, and partners in Japan and abroad, using synergy to make value-added products.
- The company collaborates with businesses around the globe through technical joint ventures and technological licensing.
- Neaton Auto Products Manufacturing Inc. serves as Nihon Plast's main factory in the US.
- Neaton Auto Products Manufacturing Inc. manufactures steering wheels, airbags, and interior and exterior trim resin components primarily for Honda.
- The Eaton facility is home to the Neaton Auto Technical Center (NATC), which acts as Nihon Plast's North American development base and part of its global development system.
- Neaton Auto Products Manufacturing Inc. was founded in February 1984 at 975 South Franklin Street, Eaton, Ohio 45320 USA.
- Neaton Auto Products Manufacturing Inc. has a capital of US$35.6 Million with a 100% direct investment stake from Nihon Plast.
- A Marysville satellite office was founded in October 2015 at 1100 Innovation Way Suite D, Marysville, OH 43040.
- Neaton Rome Inc. is Nihon Plast's second factory in the US.
- Neaton Rome Inc. manufactures steering wheels and interior and exterior trim resin components for Nissan and Honda.
- Neaton Rome Inc. was founded in April 2000 at 1634 Technology Parkway Rome, GA 30165 USA.
- Neaton Rome Inc. has a capital of US$27 Million with a 100% direct investment stake from Nihon Plast.
- Nihon Plast Mexicana, S.A. de C.V. provides leather-wrapped steering wheels and airbags to the two US factories.
