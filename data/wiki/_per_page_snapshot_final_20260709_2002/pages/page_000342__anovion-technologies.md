# Anovion Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-15

**Source:** [North America’s largest graphite factory is launching in Georgia](https://electrek.co/2023/05/15/north-america-graphite-factory-georgia/)  ·  `page_000342`

## Facts

- Graphite is in virtually all EV batteries.
- Chicago-based Anovion Technologies is opening the largest graphite production facility in North America.
- Anovion is initially investing $800 million to open a 1.5 million-square-foot graphite production factory.
- The factory will be located in Bainbridge, Decatur County, Georgia.
- The new facility will create around 400 jobs.
- This new expansion facility is the first of this scale for Anovion.
- The factory will produce 40,000 metric tonnes per year of synthetic graphite anode material for lithium-ion batteries.
- Anovion chose Bainbridge because of its existing and planned low- and carbon-free energy sources for power.
- Anovion chose Bainbridge because of short supply chains.
- Anovion chose Bainbridge because of access to existing rail infrastructure.
- Eric Stopka is the chief executive officer of Anovion.
- Eric Stopka stated that existing infrastructure makes Southwest Georgia an attractive location for Anovion’s new facility.
- Eric Stopka noted that proximity to other battery and EV manufacturing plants will allow Anovion to further reduce its carbon footprint.
- Chip Dunn is the executive chairman of Anovion.
- Chip Dunn noted that Anovion is working to secure a domestic source for critical materials required for US lithium-ion battery production.
- In October 2022, Anovion was awarded a $117 million grant from the US Department of Energy.
- The grant was awarded under the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law to help develop the domestic battery manufacturing supply chain.
- Anovion plans to expand its hydro-powered manufacturing facility near Niagara Falls, New York.
- The Niagara Falls facility is one of the only qualified US sources of battery-grade synthetic graphite that’s commercially shipping product today.
