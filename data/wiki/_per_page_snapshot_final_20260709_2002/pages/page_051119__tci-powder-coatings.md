# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [TCI Powder Coatings Expands in Georgia | 2016-01-17  | PCI Magazine](https://www.pcimag.com/articles/101558-tci-powder-coatings-expands-in-georgia)  ·  `page_051119`

## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings announced the opening of a new office in Americus, GA.
- The new office will function as corporate headquarters for the business and house the administrative, customer service, finance, and information systems departments.
- Doug Greene is President of TCI Powder Coatings.
- In the near future, the company plans to add laboratory and training facilities, sample product preparation, and light warehousing in the new facility.
- This expansion is part of a multi-year strategy to improve capabilities and provide innovative, premium solutions and service to customers.
- The Americus-Sumter Payroll Development Authority assisted in making the new facility possible for TCI.
- TCI Powder Coatings has been part of the community for 28 years.
- The company anticipates opening the office in February 2016 with approximately 20 employees.
- Additional functions will be added as growth allows.
- The facility is expected to accommodate up to 75 full-time employees.
- Barbara Grogan is Executive Director of the Payroll Development Authority.
- Grogan stated that supporting local and regional industry expansion is critical to Sumter County’s economic development strategy.
- Growth of long-term companies such as TCI Powder Coatings benefits Sumter County, particularly in their ability to hire locally from the existing workforce.
- The Development Authority partnered with TCI and will continue to support their expansion needs.
- TCI has an outstanding reputation, providing a positive work environment for citizens and a product that is used by many industries worldwide.
