## Facts

- NVH KOREA is a manufacturer and supplier of major auto components founded in 1984 and based in Ulsan, South Korea.
- The company manufactures interior NVH systems including isolation dash pad, tunnel pad, and floor module.
- The company manufactures exterior NVH systems including undercover, insulator tunnel, and wheel guard.
- The company manufactures overhead systems including headlining and sun visor.
- The company manufactures engine NVH systems including partition engine room and insulator dash.
- The company manufactures door trim systems including door trim.
- The company manufactures thermal management systems.
- The company manufactures cylinder block covers.
- NVH KOREA reported annual revenue of ₹500 - ₹1,000 Cr as on Mar 31, 2025.
- Its associated legal entity, NVH INDIA AUTO PARTS PRIVATE LIMITED, reported revenue of $50M - $100M as on Mar 31, 2025.
- NVH KOREA had an employee count of 210 as on Jan 31, 2024.
- The total employee count at NVH KOREA was 24.0% lower in Jan 2024 compared to Jan 2023.
- NVH KOREA is part of Hyundai’s Georgia EV supplier network, which added three more manufacturers as of Sep 15, 2023.
- NVH KOREA plans to create over 160 jobs in Henry County, Georgia, with Scannell Properties and Cushman involved, as of Jun 23, 2023.
- NVH KOREA plans a $72M plant in Georgia to manufacture battery parts and will hire 140 people, as of Jun 23, 2023.
