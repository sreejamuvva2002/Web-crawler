# WiM Georgia North Georgia Plant Tours

**Type:** event  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [WiM Georgia | North Georgia Plant Tours - Women In Manufacturing](https://www.womeninmanufacturing.org/events/wim-georgia-north-georgia-plant-tours)  ·  `page_020811`

## Facts

- WiM Georgia is hosting the North Georgia Plant Tours.
- Registration for the event is open until 8/11/23 at 4:00 PM (EDT).
- The first tour will be hosted by Linde + Wiemann U.S., Inc. located at 1720 Knox Bridge Crossing Road, Lavonia, GA 30553-3569.
- Linde + Wiemann U.S., Inc. is an auto parts manufacturer producing structural components for vehicle construction.
- Produced components include Sills, A- and B-Pillars as well as structural components in the Front End, Cockpit- and Chassis field.
- The second tour will be at Haering Precision USA, L.P., located at a neighboring facility to Linde + Wiemann.
- Haering Precision USA develops and manufactures precision parts and assemblies on a large scale for combustion engines, hybrids, e-mobility, hydrogen-based fuel cells, and autonomous vehicles.
- The event agenda includes a plant tour at Linde + Wiemann U.S., Inc. from 10:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m.
- The event agenda includes a plant tour at Haering Precision USA, L.P. from 11:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.
- The event agenda includes a group networking lunch sponsored by Haering Precision USA from 12:00 p.m. to 1:00 p.m. at their plant cafeteria.
- Networking lunch items are specifically prepared by Haering's in-house chef.
