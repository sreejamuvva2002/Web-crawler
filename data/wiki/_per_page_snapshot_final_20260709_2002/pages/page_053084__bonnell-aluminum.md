# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-10

**Source:** [Bonnell Aluminum Commenting on Proclamation to Strengthen the Rules to Qualify for Trade Benefits Between the U.S. and Mexico | Nasdaq](https://www.nasdaq.com/press-release/bonnell-aluminum-commenting-proclamation-strengthen-rules-qualify-trade-benefits)  ·  `page_053084`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation (NYSE: TG).
- Bonnell Aluminum commends the Biden administration’s proclamation to strengthen the rules to qualify for trade benefits between the U.S. and Mexico.
- Under the proclamation, aluminum must meet a "smelted and cast" requirement to qualify for relief from the Section 232 Tariff.
- The proclamation prohibits preferential treatment for imports where the primary country of smelt, secondary country of smelt, or country of most recent cast was Belarus, China, Russia, or Iran.
- This negotiated requirement recognizes the inherent unfair trade practice that occurs when U.S. trading partners process subsidized metal from countries like China and Russia.
- Bonnell Aluminum is a leading manufacturer of custom-fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions.
- The company serves the building and construction, automotive and transportation, consumer durables, machinery and equipment, and electrical and distribution markets.
- Branded product lines include TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum and Futura Transitions by Bonnell Aluminum.
- Bonnell Aluminum has manufacturing facilities located in Newnan GA, Carthage TN, Niles MI, Elkhart IN, and Clearfield UT.
