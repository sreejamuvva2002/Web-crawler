## Facts

- E-Z-GO partners with Rollick to enhance online purchasing experiences for consumers interested in E-Z-GO vehicles.
- E-Z-GO will utilize Rollick's digital engagement module, RollickDR, to enable consumers to complete more of their transaction online.
- E-Z-GO vehicles are designed and manufactured by Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT) company.
- The EliTE series of electric vehicles is powered by Samsung SDI lithium-ion technology.
- Consumers can view available incentives and special offers, prequalify for financing, estimate trade-in values, reserve units with a down payment, and schedule appointments with local dealers via the new system.
- A study by The Center for Generational Kinetics for WPEngine found that 65 percent of all generations rely more on technology now than ever before, and over 60 percent of GenZ and Millennials plan to continue using digital channels adopted during the pandemic.
- Gunnar Kleveland, President and CEO of Textron Specialized Vehicles, stated that crafting a straightforward and simple online experience for customers is at the forefront of E-Z-GO's mission.
- Meagan Kusek, Managing Editor of Motorcycle Powersports News, noted that consumers expect the internet to remember their entered information and preferences as technology advances.
- A PwC study found that great customer experiences can yield up to a 16 percent price premium on products and services plus increased loyalty, with 63 percent of U.S. consumers willing to share more personal information for great experiences.
- Bernie Brenner, CEO of Rollick, said E-Z-GO is leading in helping dealers serve customers in a richer, more personalized way that aligns with buyer expectations.
- E-Z-GO rolled out RollickDR in early December and added Rollick's Inventory Tools to its Aimbase lead management solution powered by Rollick last year.
- A study found that two-thirds of consumers say a frustrating website experience hurts their opinion of the brand.
- E-Z-GO was founded in Augusta, Ga. in 1954 and is a globally renowned leader in the design and manufacture of golf cars and personal transport vehicles.
- E-Z-GO models include RXV and TXT fleet golf cars; Liberty, Freedom and Valor personal golf cars; and E-Z-GO Express personal utility vehicles.
- The employees of Textron Specialized Vehicles are committed to a culture of compliance and pollution prevention, aiming to sustain premier status through integrating environmental performance into business processes.
- E-Z-GO became part of Textron Inc. in 1960 and operates as part of Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.
