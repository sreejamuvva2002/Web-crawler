# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Superior Essex, Essex Furukawa Partner With Waybridge On Agile Digitization Technology - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.de/news/superior-essex-essex-furukawa-partner-with-waybridge-on-agile-digitization-technology/)  ·  `page_010380`

## Facts

- Superior Essex announced an expanded partnership with Waybridge, a supply-chain operating system for raw materials.
- The announcement continues the Vision 2030 measures for Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire through agile digitization of the company's manufacturing facilities in Europe, North America, and Malaysia.
- Superior Essex is among the first users of the Waybridge platform, utilizing the technology since August 2021 at two North American magnet wire manufacturing plants in the USA and Mexico.
- Waybridge will be deployed in nine new facilities in Canada, Germany, Italy, Serbia, and the USA.
- Scott Wilde, VP Strategic Sourcing, highlighted innovation and holistic sustainability as procurement priorities.
- Scott Wilde stated that optimizing processes was necessary to provide the best experience for customers and suppliers and to anticipate supply chain problems like stockouts before they impact them.
- Since partnering with Waybridge, Superior Essex's view of its entire inventory and workflow process has fundamentally changed, allowing access to predictive information to flexibly solve supply chain problems.
- Inefficient supply chains and stockouts can cost companies more than direct revenue loss by increasing logistics costs, damaging brand image, and ultimately affecting future demand.
- Causes for stockouts include unexpected demand surges, shipping and supplier delays, human error, and inaccurate inventory data.
- Waybridge's Stock & Flow function addresses current and future inventory challenges by aggregating data from shipping forecasts, real-time shipping locations, estimated arrival times, and production schedules to provide predictive capabilities.
- In 2021 alone, Waybridge enabled Superior Essex to avoid four material failures that would have directly impacted customers.
- Superior Essex reduced enterprise-wide Excel usage in procurement by 90% and email volume around procurement and tracking by 50% through Waybridge's connected platform.
- Scott Evans, CEO and founder of Waybridge, noted that Superior Essex was so impressed with the results that they expanded implementation to facilities worldwide, calling them a forward-thinker in raw materials who understands the importance of standardization on a shared platform.
- Kyle McCarter, Chief Product Officer of Waybridge, added that Superior Essex provided incredible product feedback since the start of the partnership, helping continuously develop the product for all customers.
- Waybridge values Superior Essex as a partner and major participant in global markets as one of the largest magnet wire manufacturers in the world.
- Superior Essex currently manages its entire North American material through Waybridge and plans to expand the technology to Asian facilities in the near future.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, and Essex Brownell.
- The company employs over 3,000 people across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is the world's leading provider of magnet cables and leads in communication fiber applications and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company contributes significantly to disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex aims to pioneer smart building technology while setting the pace for sustainability.
- Superior Essex is headquartered at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327 – USA.
