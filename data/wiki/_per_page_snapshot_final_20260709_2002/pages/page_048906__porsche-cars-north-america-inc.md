# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- PCNA is celebrating the 40th anniversary of becoming a sales organization in North America.
- In 1984, PCNA was headquartered in Reno, Nevada, responsible for three model lines: the 944, 928, and 911.
- In 1998, PCNA's headquarters relocated to Hammond Drive, Atlanta, Georgia.
- In 2015, One Porsche Drive opened in Atlanta as PCNA's current headquarters.
- The Atlanta campus includes the Porsche Experience Center Atlanta, featuring two driver development tracks and serving as the U.S. headquarters for Porsche Classic.
- A second Porsche Experience Center opened in Los Angeles in 2016, hosting close to a million visitors across both locations to date.
- PCNA's model lineup has expanded from three models in 1984 to six today: the 718, 911, Taycan, Panamera, Macan, and Cayenne.
- There are currently over 200 independently owned and operated Porsche Centers in the U.S., along with three studios and seven satellite stores.
- Porsche sales in the U.S. exceeded 75,000 cars in 2023.
- Timo Resch serves as President and CEO of Porsche Cars North America.
- Max Hoffman placed the first order for Porsches in the U.S. in 1950, initiating long-term plans that led to PCNA's creation.
