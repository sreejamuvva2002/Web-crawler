# Seoyon E-HWA

## Facts

- Auto parts supplier Seoyon E-HWA plans to invest $76 million into a new manufacturing plant in Chatham County, Georgia.
- The project is expected to be completed in 2024.
- The facility will generate 740 jobs.
- The facility will produce automotive interior parts such as door trims, headlining, and tailgate trims.
- Seoyon E-HWA Chairman Ryu Yang-seok stated the company is an automobile manufacturer in various countries including Korea, Europe, and India.
- Seoyon E-HWA aims to supply electric vehicle parts from its new location in Chatham County, Georgia.
- The new facility will be named Seoyon E-Hwa Savannah.
- Chairman Ryu Yang-seok stated the company hopes to provide new jobs in the region through the new facility.
- Chairman Ryu Yang-seok stated the company aims to contribute to making Savannah a central city for automobile development and production.
- Seoyon E-HWA plans to continuously cooperate with the local government to support these goals.
