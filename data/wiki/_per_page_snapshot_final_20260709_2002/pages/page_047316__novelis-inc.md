# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is a leading sustainable aluminium solutions provider and the world leader in aluminium rolling and recycling.
- It has an integrated network of rolling and recycling facilities across North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- Novelis supplies premium aluminium sheet and foil products to the beverage can, automotive, aerospace and specialty markets.
- Its customer base includes iconic brands like Coca-Cola, AB InBev, Ford Motor Company, Jaguar Land Rover, Airbus and Samsung.
- Novelis’ ambition is to be the world’s leading provider of low carbon, sustainable aluminium solutions that advance its business, industry, and society toward the benefits of a circular economy.
- Novelis recycles more than 84 billion used beverage cans each year.
- A can recycled today can be turned back into a new can and returned to store shelves in roughly 60 days.
- Aluminium is infinitely recyclable.
- Recycling aluminium requires 95% less energy than primary aluminium production, resulting in 95% less carbon emissions.
- The company has created the first and the largest closed-loop recycling systems in the world.
- This allows it to take back as much of its customers’ aluminium scrap as possible, turning it back into the same product again and again.
- Closing the loop preserves the value of the alloy, reduces recycling and transportation costs, minimises environmental impact and establishes a secure supply chain.
- Novelis provides aluminium rolled products and solutions for the beverage can market.
- Novelis provides automotive and high-end specialty market products along with recycling services.
- Novelis provides aluminium plate products and solutions for the aerospace market.
- Mr. Steve Fisher is the President and Chief Executive Officer of Novelis Inc.
