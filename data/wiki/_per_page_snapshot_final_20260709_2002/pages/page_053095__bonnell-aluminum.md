# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Bonnell Aluminum unifies cross-departmental data with Oracle Analytics](https://www.oracle.com/ma/customers/bonnell-aluminum/)  ·  `page_053095`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a manufacturer of custom fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions.
- The company has over 65 years in the industry.
- Its production facilities serve many of North America’s largest and most respected manufacturing companies through design solutions and unmatched extrusion capabilities and services.
- Bonnell Aluminum operates five manufacturing plants.
- Approximately 80% of its focus is in custom manufacturing.
- The company places high value on delivering products on time to customers.
- Poor shipment visibility and delays caused by global material shortages previously made it difficult for Bonnell to keep up with growing customer expectations.
- Plant managers previously had to combine onsite data using spreadsheets with a homegrown ERP reporting system, leading to inconsistent data and decisions across locations.
- Michael Brown serves as the Vice President of Information Technology at Bonnell Aluminum.
- Bonnell chose Oracle Fusion Cloud ERP and Fusion ERP Analytics as its preferred solution to achieve visibility across finance, sales, and supply chain data.
- The company deployed Fusion Analytics across finance and HR departments in all locations simultaneously to improve decision-making.
- Bonnell plans to roll out Oracle Fusion SCM Analytics next to complete the vision of full data integration across all plants.
- With a single view of data across all locations, Bonnell Aluminum optimizes purchasing and makes timely inventory decisions based on customer needs.
- Oracle Fusion Analytics provides visibility into inventory, production floor, and back office operations for stakeholders ranging from plant management to key executives.
- The company uses enhanced analysis of its Fusion Cloud data across ERP, HCM, and SCM to identify problem areas in production that contribute to process inefficiencies and delayed orders.
- Managers can better optimize labor across sites and direct spending based on real-time insights.
- Fusion Analytics helps Bonnell connect cross-departmental data, allowing finance and procurement teams to find savings with suppliers and negotiate better prices during sourcing.
- The implementation aims to drive cultural change across the organization with a more data-driven approach.
