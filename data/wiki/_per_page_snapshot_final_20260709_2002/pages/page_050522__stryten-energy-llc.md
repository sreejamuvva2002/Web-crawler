# Stryten Energy LLC

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC is a U.S.-based energy storage solutions provider headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- The company will highlight its lead battery energy storage systems (BESS) at Battcon 2024 in Miami.
- Its Lead BESS technology is designed as a safe, proven, and reliable solution for microgrids and EV-charging applications.
- The technology safeguards against power outages to ensure continuous operation of critical infrastructure when traditional power sources fail.
- It acts as a grid support tool that provides rapid response capabilities to mitigate fluctuations, stabilize voltage, and enhance grid resilience.
- The Lead BESS technology can be sized for commercial, industrial, and utility-scale applications, with easy support for future expansion.
- Vice President of Essential Power Scott Childers stated that lead batteries are dependable for keeping operations running when other power sources are offline.
- The company noted that its lead battery technology already has an established domestic supply chain and a complete circular economy.
- Visitors to Booth #133 will see the E-Series Absolyte® AGP, a 2-volt valve-regulated lead-acid (VRLA) battery cell that powers Stryten Energy’s Lead BESS systems.
- The Absolyte AGP cells are optimized for standby float or cycling applications and are available with an ampere-hour capacity of 104-4800Ah.
- The Absolyte AGP provides a superior float life of 20 years at 25ºC and cycling capabilities of 1200 cycles to 80% depth of discharge.
- The company provides energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- Stryten Energy’s technology powers applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- The company’s stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- Stryten Energy is an industry leader backed by more than a century of expertise in delivering energy solutions.
