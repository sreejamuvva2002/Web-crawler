# Suhner Manufacturing

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-02-17

**Source:** [Flexible Shafts for Continuous Work at High Speeds From: Suhner | Industrial Equipment News](https://www.ien.com/product-development/motion-control/product/20490002/suhner-industrial-products-llc-flexible-shafts-for-continuous-work-at-high-speeds)  ·  `page_060170`

## Facts

- Located in Rome, GA.
- Offers a variety of flexible shafts with standard female couplings and set screws.
- Shafts have breaking strength ranging from 50 in-lb. up to 1,350 in-lb.
- Shafts are available with sleeve or ball bearings in almost any length.
- Products transmit rotary motion from a power source, motor or hand wheel to where the motion is required.
- Flexible shafts are rugged, high-longevity components that permit continuous work operations even up to 50,000 rpm.
