# Piedmont Resin Supply LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-31

**Source:** [HEXPOL acquires 80 percent of the shares in Piedmont Resin Supply LLC, a leading US Nylon compounder - Inderes](https://www.inderes.fi/en/releases/hexpol-acquires-80-percent-of-the-shares-in-piedmont-resin-supply-llc-a-leading-us-nylon-compounder)  ·  `page_021018`

## Facts

- HEXPOL has signed an agreement to acquire 80 percent of the shares in Piedmont Resin Supply, LLC from the founder Mr. Matt Griffith and Mr. Paul Daniel.
- Piedmont was founded in 2013 and has grown to become one of the largest independent nylon compounders in the US.
- The company is an industry leader in technical nylon compounds on the US market.
- Piedmont services a variety of customers within automotive, transportation and the furniture industries.
- Piedmont operates a well invested manufacturing facility in Cartersville, GA.
- The Cartersville, GA facility has some 60 employees and further capacity to grow.
- The company has a turnover of some 60 MUSD and a profitability on a similar level to the HEXPOL Group.
- Founders Matt Griffith and Paul Daniel stated that the merger is a perfect fit between two successful companies with similar cultures and future ambitions, bringing a portfolio of superior nylon compounds and strong market presence.
- Jan Wikström, President HEXPOL Thermoplastic Compounding, stated that the acquisition adds new capabilities, application knowhow and a new customer base to HEXPOL Thermoplastic Compounding in the US.
- Klas Dahlberg, President and CEO, HEXPOL Group, stated that Piedmont has a diverse product portfolio and is a leading independent nylon compounder, and with this acquisition they broaden the capabilities of the HEXPOL Group on the US market.
- The acquisition price amounts to 86 MUSD on a cash and debt free basis.
- The acquisition is funded by a combination of bank facilities and cash.
- The closing took place on October 31, 2024.
- Pursuant to the agreement, HEXPOL has an option to acquire the remaining shares in Piedmont.
- The founders have an option to sell their remaining shares to HEXPOL.
