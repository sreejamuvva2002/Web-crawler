# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-03

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group Inaugurates New U.S. Plant | Machine Maker - Latest Manufacturing News | Indian Manufacturing News - Latest Manufacturing News | Indian Manufacturing News - Machine Maker](https://themachinemaker.com/news/hyundai-motor-group-inaugurates-new-u-s-plant/)  ·  `page_009479`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) celebrated its grand opening on October 3, 2024.
- The grand opening marks the completion of Georgia’s largest economic development project.
- Construction at HMGMA began two and a half years prior to the grand opening.
- HMGMA is Hyundai’s third manufacturing facility in the U.S.
- It joins existing Hyundai and Kia plants in Alabama and Georgia, respectively.
- Euisun Chung, Executive Chair of Hyundai Motor Group, stated that HMGMA showcases advanced manufacturing capabilities and strong investment in partnerships with local communities in Georgia.
- Chung noted that a rich legacy of craftsmanship and a skilled workforce at HMGMA are shaping the future of mobility in America.
- The first vehicle, a Hyundai Ioniq 5, rolled off the production line on October 3, 2024.
- Production of the Hyundai Ioniq 9, a three-row electric SUV, is also underway at HMGMA.
- The plant will begin producing its first Kia model in 2026.
- HMGMA is designed to manufacture electric and hybrid vehicles for Hyundai, Genesis, and Kia.
- The plant is initially capable of producing up to 300,000 vehicles annually.
- Hyundai Motor Group’s total investment in Georgia, including HMGMA and joint battery ventures with LG Energy Solution and SK On, amounts to $12.6 billion.
- This $12.6 billion investment is the largest in Georgia’s history.
- The investment is expected to create 8,500 jobs at HMGMA by 2031.
- Overall, the Group’s investments in Georgia are expected to generate nearly 40,000 direct and indirect jobs.
- These investments are expected to generate an annual income impact of $4.6 billion, according to the Center for Automotive Research.
- As of July 2024, more than $2.5 billion in capital investment has been committed by HMGMA suppliers across 12 counties in Georgia.
- This supplier investment is generating about 6,900 jobs.
- Hyundai has already invested over $20.5 billion in the U.S.
- Hyundai recently announced a further $21 billion investment between 2025 and 2028.
- This new investment will support an annual production capacity of 1.2 million vehicles in the U.S.
- The Group plans to increase HMGMA’s production capacity to 500,000 units annually.
- This expansion represents an increase of 200,000 units from the original plan.
- Hyundai’s U.S. production facilities in Alabama and Georgia will undergo upgrades to support this growth.
- HMGMA marks a major milestone as Hyundai’s first dedicated facility for mass-producing electrified vehicles.
- The plant is located in Bryan County, Georgia.
- It integrates a highly connected, automated, and adaptable manufacturing system designed to link every component of the EV and hybrid ecosystem.
- All production stages at the facility, from order collection and procurement to logistics and assembly, are optimized through AI and data analytics.
- The plant fosters a human-centered work environment where robots collaborate with human workers to enhance productivity and safety.
- Hyundai Motor Group has a diverse value chain spanning mobility, steel and construction, logistics, finance, IT, and services.
- The Group employs approximately 250,000 people worldwide.
