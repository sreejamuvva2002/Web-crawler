# Ascend Elements

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Ascend Elements utilizes a patented Hydro-to-Cathode® direct cathode precursor (pCAM) synthesis process.
- The process simplifies lithium-ion battery recycling and transforms used batteries into customized battery materials.
- The resulting battery materials can meet or exceed the performance of new materials.
- Other processes leach metals out of spent battery materials, but this process leaches out impurities instead.
- The process keeps valuable metals in solution and eliminates multiple steps in the recycling flow.
- Traditional lithium-ion recycling processes involve many steps that focus on reclaiming intermediate compounds to sell to commodities markets.
- The Hydro-to-Cathode direct cathode precursor (pCAM) synthesis process increases efficiency while returning higher-level materials to the supply chain.
- The process uses methodology from Argonne National Labs.
- The process has demonstrated significant advantages in multiple categories when compared with traditional methods.
- Delivering engineered materials at lower cost greatly improves the cost-effectiveness of battery recycling.
- Improved economics is the best way to boost battery recycling as the lithium-ion market grows.
- The approach will reduce the pressure to mine and process raw materials for critical mineral applications.
- The closed-loop approach supports independent and secure supply chains.
