# Vista Metals Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-03-19

**Source:** [Vista Metals to invest $60 Million in Aluminum Casting Facility in Bowling Green](https://www.wbko.com/2024/03/19/vista-metals-invest-60-million-aluminum-casting-facility-bowling-green/)  ·  `page_013575`

## Facts

- Vista Metals plans to invest approximately $60 million in a new specialty aluminum products operation in Bowling Green, Kentucky.
- The project will create over 50 full-time jobs across operations, planning, engineering, and site management roles.
- Initial site preparation for the facility is already underway, with a phased build-out planned to support core market growth.
- Vista Metals intends to acquire more than 100 acres for the new site.
- The company will construct a 75,000-plus-square-foot facility in Bowling Green.
- Kentucky Governor Andy Beshear congratulated Vista Metals and highlighted Warren County as a leading community for the state's rapid metals industry growth.
- CEO Andy Primack cited Bowling Green's strong industrial base, qualified manufacturing talent pool, business-education collaboration, and long-term community development plan as critical factors for the location choice.
- Warren County Judge-Executive Doug Gorman stated the expansion will create job opportunities and solidify Warren County's position as a hub for innovation and manufacturing excellence.
- President Kelly Thomas noted the investment underscores Vista Metals' commitment to the aerospace industry and robust supply chains, anticipating commercial aircraft build rates will more than double over the next two decades.
- The company's strategy aims to enable customers to test and qualify the facility ahead of projected market growth, alongside ongoing investments in people, processes, and equipment at existing sites.
- VMC Specialty Alloys LLC, operating as Vista Metals Georgia, functions under a licensing agreement with Fontana, California-based Vista Metals Corp.
- Alongside Vista Progressive Metals LLC, which produces aluminum lithium products, the corporate group is a market leader in specialty aluminum products for aerospace, commercial aircraft, defense, automotive, and general industrial applications.
- Bowling Green Mayor Todd Alcott emphasized the city's skilled workforce and robust infrastructure as key reasons Vista Metals chose the area.
- Officials noted the investment builds upon what is described as the best four-year period for economic growth in Kentucky's history.
