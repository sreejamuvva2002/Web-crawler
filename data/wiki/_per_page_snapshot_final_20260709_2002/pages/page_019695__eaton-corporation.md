# Eaton Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-30

**Source:** [Just Announced! New Corporate Engagement Partnership with Eaton Corporation - IEEE Power & Energy Society](https://ieee-pes.org/news/ieee-power-energy-society-and-eaton-corporate-engagement-program/)  ·  `page_019695`

## Facts

- Eaton Corporation is a global power management company.
- Eaton formally agreed to join the IEEE PES Corporate Engagement Program (CEP) by entering into a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU).
- The CEP MOU was officially signed by Jessica Bian, IEEE PES President, and Raja Macha, Eaton’s Chief Technology Officer and Executive Vice President.
- As the newest participant in the PES Corporate Engagement Program, Eaton will work with IEEE PES to improve, guide, innovate, and promote the development of technology and standards.
- The agreement aligns both organizations’ efforts in advancing the power and energy industry.
- Eaton employees will have access to the PES University curriculum.
- Eaton can offer experts to provide instruction for the IEEE PES global community.
- Both parties wish to foster a long-term, peer-to-peer relationship for their mutual benefit.
