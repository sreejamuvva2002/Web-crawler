# Panasonic Automotive Systems America, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-25

**Source:** [Kevin Ostertag Named President of Panasonic Automotive Systems America, LLC | http://lifestyle.lmcordoba.com.ar/](https://lifestyle.lmcordoba.com.ar/story/576143/kevin-ostertag-named-president-of-panasonic-automotive-systems-america-llc/)  ·  `page_047951`

## Facts

- Kevin Ostertag was appointed President of Panasonic Automotive Systems America, LLC (PASA), effective April 1, 2026.
- Kevin Ostertag succeeds Scott Kirchner, who has been serving in a dual role as both President of PASA and Global Chief Operating Officer (COO) of Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd (PAS).
- Scott Kirchner stated that Kevin brings a deep understanding of the business, a strong track record of success, and trusted relationships established across the organization regionally and globally.
- Since 2021, Kevin Ostertag has served as Chief Financial Officer of PASA, where he played a key role in strengthening performance, driving efficiencies, and strategically positioning the organization for diversified growth.
- Kevin Ostertag stated he is honored to step into the role at a pivotal moment in the company’s journey and looks forward to upholding Scott Kirchner's vision to further strengthen PASA's position as a leader in the mobility industry.
- Scott Kirchner served as President of PASA since 2017, during which time the company underwent a significant transformation to position it for accelerated growth while remaining rooted in its cultural values and commitment to delivering high-quality, safe technologies to the world’s leading OEMs.
- Scott Kirchner will continue to serve as Global COO of PAS after stepping down as President of PASA.
- In April 2027, Panasonic Automotive will change its company name and brand to Mobitera to further position the organization for accelerated growth and optimize its overall business portfolio.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems America, LLC is a premier supplier of automotive and mobility technologies for the world’s leading OEMs.
- PASA's North American operations are headquartered in Peachtree City, GA, with locations across the region strategically positioned to serve customers.
- PASA strives to create a safe, comfortable, eco-friendly in-vehicle experience through its wide-range of high-quality solutions.
