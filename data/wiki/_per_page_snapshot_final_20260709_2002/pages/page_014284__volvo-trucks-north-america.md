# Volvo Trucks North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Volvo Trucks Continues Expansion of Electromobility Ecosystem with 11 New Certified EV Dealers in North America](https://www.volvotrucks.us/news-and-stories/press-releases/2023/october/volvo-trucks-continues-expansion-of-electromobility-ecosystem-with-11-new-certified-ev-dealers-in-north-america/)  ·  `page_014284`

## Facts

- Volvo Trucks North America recently confirmed that 11 dealership locations in the U.S. and Canada have completed sales and service training and facility upgrades to become Certified Electric Vehicle Dealerships.
- The certification of Kriete Truck Centers in Wisconsin and Transport Equipment in Montana adds two new states to Volvo Trucks’ North American dealer support network for battery-electric trucks.
- Volvo Trucks now has 47 certified dealership locations across 22 states and three Canadian provinces, with some locations yet to be announced publicly.
- The announcement was made at the American Trucking Associations’ (ATA) 2023 Management Conference & Exhibition in Austin, Texas.
- Jared Ruiz, regional vice president – West for Volvo Trucks North America, stated that the growth of the Certified EV Dealership network underscores the company's commitment to providing customers with resources to adopt battery-electric vehicles beyond just selling the truck.
- Earlier this year, Volvo announced it had tripled the number of certified dealerships in just one year.
- The rapid growth of the dealership network is ongoing with these 11 new locations and more than 45 others in the process of completing certification.
- New certified dealership locations include Coffman Truck Sales in Aurora, Illinois; Gerry’s Truck Centre in London, Ontario; McMahon Truck Centers in Columbus, Ohio; M&K Truck Centers in Summit, Illinois; Mid-Ontario Truck Center in Maple, Ontario; Nextran Truck Center in Kansas City, Missouri; Northwest Equipment Sales in Boise, Idaho; Kriete Truck Centers in Madison, Wisconsin; S&S Truck Sales in Lima, Ohio; TransEdge Truck Centers in Allentown, Pennsylvania; and Transport Equipment in Missoula, Montana.
- The Certified EV Dealership program ensures a robust sales and service ecosystem required to support customers with the commercial deployment of Class 8 battery-electric trucks.
- Each dealership location boasts a team of highly trained sales professionals who assist customers with vehicle configuration selection, securing available incentive funding, and identifying ideal routes for electrification.
- Sales teams at Volvo Trucks Certified EV Dealers have access to route planning and connected technology tools designed to help customers maximize the performance capabilities and benefits of the zero-tailpipe emission Volvo VNR Electric model.
- Service technicians at certified locations are fully trained to perform battery-electric truck maintenance and repairs using diagnostics tools and personal protective equipment necessary for high-voltage systems.
- Certified EV Dealers support the Volvo Gold Contract, a premier turnkey service offering for the Volvo VNR Electric model designed to ensure peak vehicle uptime, performance, and productivity.
- Dealers maintain a stock of key parts and components for the Volvo VNR Electric model to minimize service times and quickly return customers to operation.
- The certified dealer network spans 22 U.S. states (Alabama, California, Delaware, Florida, Idaho, Illinois, Indiana, Massachusetts, Minnesota, Missouri, Montana, New Jersey, New York, North Carolina, Ohio, Oklahoma, Oregon, Pennsylvania, Tennessee, Texas, Virginia, and Wisconsin) and three Canadian provinces (British Columbia, Ontario, and Quebec).
- Volvo Trucks is on track to certify 46 additional dealerships in 2023 and 2024, which will bring the total network to 37 U.S. states and four Canadian provinces.
- Volvo Trucks North America is headquartered in Greensboro, North Carolina.
- Volvo Trucks North America is one of the leading heavy-duty truck manufacturers in North America.
- The company's Uptime Services commitment is delivered by a network of nearly 400 authorized dealers across North America and the 24/7 Volvo Trucks Uptime Center.
- Every Volvo truck is assembled at the Volvo Trucks New River Valley manufacturing facility in Dublin, Virginia.
- The Dublin, Virginia manufacturing facility meets the ISO 9001 standard for quality and the ISO 14001 standard for environmental care.
- The Dublin, Virginia facility holds a dual ISO 50001/Superior Energy Performance certification at the platinum level for sustained excellence in energy management.
- Volvo Trucks North America provides complete transport solutions offering a full range of diesel, alternative-fuel, and all-electric vehicles.
- Volvo Trucks supplies medium- and heavy-duty trucks via a global dealer network with 2,200 service points in about 130 countries.
- Volvo trucks are assembled in 12 countries across the globe.
- In 2025 approximately 120,000 Volvo trucks were delivered worldwide.
- Volvo Trucks is part of the Volvo Group, one of the world’s leading manufacturers of trucks, buses, construction equipment and marine and industrial engines.
