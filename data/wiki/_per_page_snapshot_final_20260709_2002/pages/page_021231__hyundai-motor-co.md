## Facts

- Hyundai has become the world's third-largest carmaker.
- Hyundai surpassed General Motors Co., Nissan Motor Co., and Stellantis NV in annual volumes along the way.
- Hyundai was founded 55 years ago as a manufacturer of affordable vehicles for its home market.
- Hyundai's international expansion only began in earnest in the 80s.
- Hyundai's Genesis is now going head-to-head with other luxury car brands.
- Hyundai is duking it out with Ford Motor Co. for second place in US electric vehicle sales this year, behind Tesla Inc.
- President and co-Chief Executive Officer Jaehoon Chang, 58, stated that the company was on the right track and very strong this year.
- Jaehoon Chang identified supply chain management as key to Hyundai's performance.
- Hyundai is trying to be flexible and optimize and protect production despite a chip shortage.
