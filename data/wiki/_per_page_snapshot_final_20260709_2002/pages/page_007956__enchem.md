## Facts

- Enchem operates as Korea's leading electrolyte solution supplier to global top-tier customers.
- The company provides high-functional electrolyte additives and optimal electrolyte development and manufacturing technology for each customer.
- Enchem is establishing a one-stop solution for electrolyte by completing vertical integration through the internalization of raw materials.
- The company has established local supply systems in Korea, China, Europe, and the United States.
- Enchem is securing four global production bases located in the USA, Europe, China, and Korea.
- Production capacity targets for 2025 include 5,000 tons, 30,000 tons, 150,000 tons, 70,000 tons, 155,000 tons, and 78,000 tons.
- Expected production capacity targets for 2026 include 7,000 tons, 30,000 tons, 200,000 tons, 100,000 tons, 300,000 tons, 120,000 tons, 40,000 tons, and 200,000 tons.
- Expected production capacity targets for 2027 include 250,000 tons.
- A US production facility is located in Commerce, Georgia.
