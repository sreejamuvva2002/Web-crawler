# EnviroSpark

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- The company's mission is to accelerate the world’s transition to electric transportation by making EV Charging more accessible in the U.S. and Canada.
- EnviroSpark was founded in Atlanta in 2014 by Aaron and Stephanie Luque.
- After its founding, the company started building electric vehicle charging solutions and expanded rapidly across the country to serve the demands of national rollouts.
- In 2017, EnviroSpark partnered with Tesla to help scale their Destination Charging Program.
- In 2020, Frank Blake, Tim Tassopouslos, Mark Bonfigli, Marissa Mora, and David Stockert joined the team as seed investors and advisors.
- Between 2022 and 2023, Ultra Capital invested $15M in EnviroSpark to support EnviroSpark Network expansion.
- In 2024, Basalt Infrastructure Partners invested $50M in Series B funding, which enabled EnviroSpark to scale its network and specialty services offering.
- In 2025, the company secured new enterprise partnerships for scalable electric vehicle charging solutions.
- As of 2025, EnviroSpark hit the milestone of installing over 10,000 EV chargers.
- Frank Woodling serves as CEO of EnviroSpark and brings 11 years of EV charger installation experience, combined with in-house site acquisition, design, and project management capabilities.
- The leadership team possesses decades of combined experience in electric vehicle charging solutions, utilities, construction management, energy systems, and nationwide project delivery.
- EnviroSpark provides smart solutions custom-engineered for each customer, backed by nimble problem-solving and open communication throughout the process.
- The company offers industry-leading uptime and a seamless driver experience for commercial electric car charging stations serving cities, developers, and fleets.
- EnviroSpark provides direct support from real people with fast responses and clear answers, avoiding ticket queues.
