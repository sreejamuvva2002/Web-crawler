# Pirelli

**Type:** supplier_relationship  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-08-30

**Source:** [Pirelli Partners With Fisker On New 'EMotion' Vehicle Launch](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/pirelli-partners-fisker-new-emotion-vehicle-launch/)  ·  `page_048569`

## Facts

- Pirelli announced that it is working with Fisker Inc. to create advanced low rolling resistance tires for the new Fisker EMotion luxury electric vehicle, set to launch in late 2019.
- Pirelli will develop summer P Zero tires in the following sizes for the Fisker EMotion: 285/35R24 (F) and 305/35R24 (R).
- The focus of this tire development will be on low rolling resistance, spirited handling and extra comfort due to tall sidewalls.
- Pierluigi Dinelli, CEO and chairman of Pirelli Tire North America, stated the partnership underscores Pirelli’s commitment to innovation from the track to the street and aligns with a common goal of pushing the boundaries of premium driving performance.
- The Fisker EMotion is an AWD sedan featuring a range of more than 400 miles.
- The EMotion includes hardware to support fully autonomous driving.
- The EMotion features fast-charging capability that adds 125 miles in 9 minutes.
- The EMotion has internet connectivity enabling automatic updates to be streamed directly to the vehicle.
- Henrik Fisker, chairman and CEO of Fisker Inc., is the prominent automotive designer behind the EMotion, renowned for past work on iconic creations like the BMW Z8, Aston Martin DB9, VLF Force 1, Karma and more.
- The EMotion features never-before-seen design elements that reflect the brand’s supercar DNA.
- Henrik Fisker stated that incorporating Pirelli’s low-rolling resistance technology into the EMotion really completes the equation where groundbreaking supercar design elements and technology meet the very best in performance.
- The tires will be produced in Rome, Georgia, strengthening Pirelli’s ‘local for local’ approach in North America.
- Fisker is also exploring utilizing the Pirelli Noise Cancelling System and connected tire technology features.
- The Fisker EMotion will start at $129,900.
- The Fisker EMotion is slated for production in the United States.
