# Enchem

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-02

**Source:** [Enchem cancels plans for new facility in Brownsville, TN. Here's why](https://www.commercialappeal.com/story/money/business/2025/10/01/enchem-cancels-brownsville-project-in-west-tennessee-ford-blueoval-city/86459701007/)  ·  `page_008091`

## Facts

- South Korea-based Enchem will no longer move forward with its planned $152.5 million facility in Brownsville.
- The canceled project was expected to create 190 jobs.
- Enchem was one of the expected battery supply manufacturers for Ford's BlueOval City.
- Brownsville-Haywood Economic Development Corp. Executive Director Aaron Stewart confirmed that Enchem had canceled plans for its project.
- No construction has started on the site, except for preliminary site grading.
