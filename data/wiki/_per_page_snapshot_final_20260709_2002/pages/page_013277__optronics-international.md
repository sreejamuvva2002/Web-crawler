# Optronics International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-10-24

**Source:** [Optronics Introduces Custom LED Lighting | Crane Hot Line](https://www.cranehotline.com/articles/optronics_introduces_custom_led_lighting)  ·  `page_013277`

## Facts

- Optronics International is a manufacturer and supplier of heavy-duty LED vehicle lighting.
- The company created stop, tail, and turn lamps with integrated graphics.
- The new lamps merge brand identities into the lighting design by juxtaposing a smooth GloLight lens appearance against a more traditional LED pattern.
- GloLight technology allows Optronics to consolidate complex graphics into the functional operation of the lamps.
- A traditional LED-style lens pattern surrounds the logo graphic in the middle of the lamp.
- The GloLight logo portion of the lamp illuminates when operating in its standard function as a tail lamp.
- When the lamp’s turn signal or brake functions are in operation, all portions of the LED lamp brighten.
- Brett Johnson serves as president and CEO of Optronics International.
- Brett Johnson stated that logos and branding have been widely incorporated into conspicuity tape for decades, so the migration to lighting is a logical one.
- Professionals recognize that 4” round and 6” oval lighting formats are among the most noticeable features on any vehicle.
- Optronics employed its state-of-the-art 3D printing capabilities in preparing prototypes for manufacturers.
- The company worked with several OEMs during lamp development, including Vanguard National Trailer Corporation and Miller Industries.
- Charlie Mudd serves as president of Vanguard National Trailer Corporation.
- Charlie Mudd stated that Vanguard was among the first trailer manufacturers to make Optronics’ GloLight part of their standard lighting package.
- The LED lamps offer a broad level of creative design flexibility for company branding, as GloLight technology can be used in both red and white or a combination of both.
- Lens striations can be used to achieve unique and complementary visual effects.
- Examples of the new 4” round and 6” oval stop, tail, and turn lamps will be displayed in booth 2547 at the North American Commercial Vehicle (NACV) Show 2019.
- Optronics products are available in the U.S. and Canada through a distribution network of more than 20,000 convenient distribution locations.
