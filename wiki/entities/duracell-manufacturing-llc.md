# Duracell Manufacturing, LLC

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Cleveland, Tennessee, Bradley County, Tennessee, United States

## Overview

Duracell Manufacturing announced a $25 million investment to expand its Cleveland, Tennessee battery component plant, creating 25 new jobs and supporting its LaGrange, Georgia site with strategic battery components for North American demand.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The expansion will support Duracell’s LaGrange, Georgia site by providing strategic battery components supply, strengthening the regional EV battery supply chain.
