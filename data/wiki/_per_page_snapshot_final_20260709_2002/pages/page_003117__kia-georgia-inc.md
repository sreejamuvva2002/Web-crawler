# Kia Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-02-24

**Source:** [Kia Georgia celebrates trio of major milestones, including Start of Production for 2027 Kia Telluride](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/kia-georgia-celebrates-trio-of-major-milestones-including-start-of-production-for-2027-kia-telluride-302696315.html)  ·  `page_003117`

## Facts

- Kia Georgia team members marked another historic day at its West Point facility on Tuesday, Feb. 24, 2026, with the start of production of the all-new 2027 Kia Telluride.
- The ceremonial vehicle represented the first hybrid electric vehicle (HEV) assembled in the state of Georgia.
- The same vehicle marked the 5 millionth vehicle produced at Kia Georgia.
- The milestone ceremony was attended by Georgia Governor Brian P. Kemp, team members, and state/community leaders.
- Sean Yoon, President and CEO of Kia North America and Kia America, stated that Kia Georgia is the only plant in the world that builds the Telluride.
- The facility has reached five million vehicles produced and introduced Kia's first US-assembled HEV.
- Since beginning production in 2009, Kia Georgia has grown into one of the region's largest employers and a cornerstone of automotive manufacturing in the Southeast.
- The Telluride is one of the industry's most awarded vehicles and serves as a flagship model for the brand.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp noted that the Telluride has been exclusively made in Georgia since its introduction in 2019.
- The governor attributed the vehicle's success to a partnership approach between Kia Georgia, the local community, and the state.
- Stuart Countess, Kia Georgia's President and CEO, emphasized that producing world-class gasoline-powered vehicles, EVs, and HEVs demonstrates the plant's versatility and talent.
- Kia Georgia, Inc. is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Corporation, which is based in Seoul, Korea.
- Kia Georgia has an annual production capacity of 350,000 units.
- The facility is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Ga.
- Kia Georgia began mass production on Nov. 16, 2009.
- Kia Georgia produces the Telluride, Sorento (excludes HEV and PHEV models), and Sportage (excludes HEV and PHEV models) SUVs.
- Kia Georgia also produces the all-electric EV9 and EV6 vehicles.
