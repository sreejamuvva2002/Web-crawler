# Hitachi Automotive Systems Americas, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-12-01

**Source:** [News Releases : December 7, 2015 : HIAMS in U.S.A](https://www.hitachi-automotive.us/about/press/12022016.htm)  ·  `page_058230`

## Facts

- Hitachi Automotive Systems Americas’ Monroe, GA plant was recognized by The Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) on December 1, 2016 as the inaugural winner of the Community Impact Award.
- The first-ever Georgia Automotive Awards were awarded to companies and individuals in five unique categories: Company of the Year, Leader of the Year, Innovation Award, Supplier of the Year and Community Impact Award.
- Paul Carroll, President and CEO of Hitachi Automotive Systems Americas, stated that Georgia is a key market for Hitachi Automotive Systems and its work there is paramount to the overall success of the organization.
- Carroll noted that the robust automotive industry and network in the state, combined with business-friendly tax incentives and an overall low cost of doing business, make Georgia an ideal location for Hitachi Automotive Systems to continue on its growth path.
- The Community Impact Award was conferred upon Hitachi for strongly demonstrating a high level of corporate citizenship in and around the local community and/or within the state of Georgia.
- The award cited Hitachi’s contributions and involvement with two community impact programs including F.I.S.H (Faith in Serving Humanity) and the Communities in Schools program, which are focused on combating child hunger and ensuring at-risk children remain in school.
- Hitachi Automotive Systems Americas, Inc. manufactures, remanufactures and markets a wide range of automotive systems including engine management systems, electric power train systems, drive control systems and car information systems for all major automotive original equipment manufacturers and aftermarket customers worldwide.
- The company provides regional leadership in the Americas within the global operations of Hitachi Automotive Systems, Ltd.
- Hitachi Automotive Systems maintains sites throughout North America with sales and engineering in Michigan serving as its headquarters and manufacturing facility in Kentucky, along with additional sales and manufacturing facilities in Kentucky, Georgia, California and Mexico.
- Hitachi Automotive Systems, Ltd. is a wholly owned subsidiary of Hitachi, Ltd., headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
- Hitachi Automotive Systems Americas, Inc. is a subsidiary of Hitachi America, Ltd.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) serves as the state’s sales and marketing arm and the lead agency for attracting new business investment, encouraging the expansion of existing industry and small businesses, aligning workforce education and training with in-demand jobs, locating new markets for Georgia products, attracting tourists to Georgia, and promoting the state as a destination for arts and location for film, music and digital entertainment projects.
