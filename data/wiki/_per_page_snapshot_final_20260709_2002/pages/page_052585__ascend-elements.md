# Ascend Elements

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-10-27

**Source:** [Ascend Elements Raises $300M in Funding](https://www.finsmes.com/2022/10/ascend-elements-raises-300m-in-funding.html)  ·  `page_052585`

## Facts

- Ascend Elements is a Westborough, MA-based engineered materials and lithium-ion battery recycling company.
- The company raised $300M in equity and debt financing.
- The financing includes $200m in Series C equity investments.
- The funding round was led by Fifth Wall Climate.
- SK ecoplant joined the funding round.
- Other participants in the funding round include Oman Investment Authority, Lithium Americas Corporation, GLy Capital Management New Mobility Fund, Mirae Asset Capital & LS, Shinhan GIB, Hitachi Ventures, InMotion Ventures, TDK Ventures, Orbia Ventures, At One Ventures, TRUMPF Venture, and Doral Energy-Tech Ventures.
- The funding is in addition to two recently awarded grants totaling $480 million from the Department of Energy.
- Ascend Elements intends to use the funds to accelerate commercialization of its proprietary Hydro-to-Cathode direct precursor synthesis process.
- The company is led by CEO Mike O’Kronley.
- Ascend Elements is a provider of sustainable, closed-loop battery materials solutions.
- The company operates from EV battery recycling to commercial-scale production of lithium-ion battery precursor (pCAM) and cathode active materials (CAM).
- Its proprietary Hydro-to-Cathode direct precursor synthesis technology produces new CAM from spent lithium-ion cells in a different way.
- This technology results in reduced cost, improved performance, and lowered GHG emissions.
- The company recently disclosed plans to invest up to $1 billion to build a sustainable lithium-ion battery materials facility in Hopkinsville, Ky.
- The manufacturing facility is known as “Apex 1”.
- Apex 1 will produce enough lithium-ion battery pCAM and sustainable CAM to equip up to 250,000 electric vehicles per year.
