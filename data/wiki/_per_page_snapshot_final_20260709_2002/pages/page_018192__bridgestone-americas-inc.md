## Facts

- Bridgestone Americas, Inc. (BSAM) operates the Bandag plant in Griffin, GA.
- The Bandag plant in Griffin has been in operation for over 45 years.
- The facility produces tread rubber and retreading materials for Bandag dealers.
- Bandag is dedicated to the research, development, and manufacturing of premium retread tires.
- Bridgestone is hiring Production Technicians at the Griffin, GA location.
- Starting pay for Production Technicians is $22.28/hour.
- Automatic pay increases occur every 3 to 6 months, progressing to $27.32/hour in 24 months.
- Tenure-based pay steps are available at 5 and 10 years.
- Specialized roles offer earning potential up to $30.74/hour.
- Shift premiums and perfect attendance incentives are available.
- Employees are paid weekly.
- Competitive medical, prescription, dental, and vision coverage begins on the first day of employment.
- A 401k plan with company contribution is offered.
- Paid time off (PTO) and 11 paid holidays are provided.
- A teammate referral bonus of up to $750 per referral is available after employment.
- Basic/supplemental life insurance and flexible health spending account options are included.
- Free counseling, consultation services, wellbeing resources, and pet insurance through MetLife are offered.
- Fertility and family building benefits are provided through a partnership with Kindbody.
- Production Technician responsibilities include setting up, operating, maintaining, and troubleshooting manufacturing equipment.
- Employees must move, pack, and sort raw materials or finished goods.
- Workers monitor output quality to identify, discard, or re-manufacture faulty products.
- Maintaining accurate daily production records is required.
- The role requires heavy lifting of up to 70 lbs occasionally from heights of 3 to 5 feet.
- The position involves medium physical effort, climbing stairs, and considerable standing.
- Preferred qualifications include 2 years of employment stability and 1 year of manufacturing experience.
- A High School diploma or GED is required prior to start.
- Bridgestone Americas, Inc. is headquartered in Nashville, Tennessee.
- BSAM operates alongside Bridgestone Europe, Middle East and Africa (BSEMEA) as the 'Bridgestone West' strategic region.
- Both BSAM and BSEMEA are subsidiaries of Japan-headquartered Bridgestone Corporation.
- The company develops, manufactures, and markets Bridgestone, Firestone, and associate brand products for broad customer needs.
- Bridgestone is an Equal Employment Opportunity employer considering all individuals regardless of protected characteristics.
- Form I-9 Employment Eligibility Verification must be completed at the start of employment.
