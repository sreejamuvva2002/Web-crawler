## Facts

- Nisshinbo Micro Devices Inc. has been manufacturing and providing Electronic devices / Microwave products over 60 years.
- The company aims to grow as an "Analog solution provider" focusing on Energy Management and Signal Processing as core analog technology.
- Core analog technologies include operational amplifiers and power management ICs for Automotive, Industrial, and Consumer equipment.
- For Microwave products, the company contributes to manufacturing in various fields through achieving high reliability and stable, long-term supply.
- The company combines microwave technology cultivated over many years with advanced semiconductor technologies to support customers' product development aimed at meeting emerging market requirements.
