# Hyundai Mobis

## Facts

- South Korean auto parts manufacturer Hyundai Mobis plans to invest 926 million US dollars to build an electric vehicle power system factory in Bryan County, Georgia.
- The new plant is Hyundai's second electric car plant in Georgia and covers an area of 1.2 million square feet.
- Construction for the new plant is expected to begin in January 2023.
- Production at the new plant is expected to begin in 2024.
- The new plant will eventually employ 1500 people.
- In October, Hyundai broke ground on a $5.5 billion electric car plant in Brian County.
- The $5.5 billion plant is Hyundai's first plant in the United States and is expected to open in 2025.
- The $5.5 billion plant will employ 8100 people.
- The $5.5 billion plant will produce as many as 300000 electric vehicles a year.
- In early October, foreign media reported that Hyundai Mobis would invest $1.3 billion to build an electric car parts factory in the United States.
- Hyundai Mobis is one of the three main companies of Hyundai Motor Group.
- Hyundai Mobis supplies auto parts to other manufacturers as well as Hyundai and Kia.
- In mid-October, foreign media reported that Hyundai Mobis had begun supplying chassis modules to luxury carmaker Mercedes-Benz for its four pure electric SUV models.
