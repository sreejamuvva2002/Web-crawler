# Georgia Made program

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Kemp Launches ‘Georgia Made’ Logo For In-State Manufacturers – WABE](https://www.wabe.org/kemp-launches-georgia-made-logo-for-in-state-manufacturers/)  ·  `page_049479`

## Facts

- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp announced a “Georgia Made” program to encourage manufacturers to promote the state on their products.
- Kemp announced the program Wednesday at the state Capitol.
- Representatives of 11 companies, whose factories Kemp visited during a statewide tour last fall, were present at the announcement.
- The program parallels the existing Georgia Grown certification that promotes agricultural products.
