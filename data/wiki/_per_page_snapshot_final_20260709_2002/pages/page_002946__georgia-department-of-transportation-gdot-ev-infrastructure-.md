# Georgia Department of Transportation (GDOT) EV Infrastructure Deployment Plan

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- The U.S. Department of Transportation’s (DOT) NEVI Formula Program required the Georgia Department of Transportation (GDOT) to submit an annually updated EV Infrastructure Deployment Plan (Plan) for fiscal years 2022 through 2026 to their FHWA Division Office.
- The plan describes how the state intended to distribute NEVI funds and their physical and cybersecurity strategies.
- The plan includes a Community Engagement Outcomes Report.
- Submitted plans must address updated NEVI Guidance.
- The jurisdiction for this planning is Georgia.
- The program type is classified as State Incentives.
- The technologies covered include EVs and PHEVs.
