# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-30

**Source:** [Economic Development Around the State: Hyundai Motor Group Grand Opening & CNN Center Downtown News - Georgia Trend Magazine](https://www.georgiatrend.com/2025/04/30/georgia-economics-hyundai-motor-group-new-cnn-center-downtwon-news/)  ·  `page_009374`

## Facts

- After years of planning and construction, Hyundai Motor Group celebrated the official grand opening of its $12.6 billion Metaplant in March.
- Hyundai announced it is increasing production from 300,000 to 500,000 units a year on the Bryan County site.
- The production increase will bring an additional 14,000 new jobs by 2028.
- The first vehicle to be produced at the plant is the 2025 Hyundai IONIQ 5.
- SOPO US Inc., a company from the Czech Republic that makes coils, stators and rotors for motors in the electric vehicle industry, is planning a $2.3 million facility in Flowery Branch that will create 25 jobs.
- Another Czech company, SILENT, which produces insulated parts and components for the electrical industry, has plans to invest $1 million in Hall County to create 10 new jobs.
