# Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc. (KMMG)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2008-06-10

**Source:** [Kia Director Addresses Rotary Club of Carrollton - Kia Georgia](https://www.kiageorgia.com/kia-director-addresses-rotary-club-of-carrollton/)  ·  `page_010135`

## Facts

- Randy Jackson, Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc.’s (KMMG) director of human resources and administration, addressed the Rotary Club of Carrollton at Sunset Hills Country Club in Carrollton, Ga. on June 10, 2008.
- Jackson discussed the automotive industry's fast pace during the meeting.
- Kia received a benchmark-setting 43,000+ applications during its online application phase earlier in 2008.
- Many of the job applications came from the Carroll County area.
- Jackson highlighted Kia’s relationship with Hyundai.
- The plant site covers 2,245 acres.
- Indirect jobs are linked to Kia through suppliers and other businesses locating in the area due to Kia’s decision to come to West Point, Ga.
- Jackson stated, “The excitement level is overwhelming throughout this state.”
- At full production, the KMMG plant will produce around 300,000 vehicles a year.
- Kia’s policy requires potential employees to work 40 hours of pre-hire time before being offered a job to ensure they are a good fit for the company.
- Jackson emphasized Kia’s goal to hire people who will stay long-term and provide extensive on-the-job training upfront to ensure mutual good decisions.
- Jackson noted that local support through the purchase of Kia vehicles will help the plant grow.
- Jackson, born in Macon, Ga., stated he never thought he would return to the state 25 years later to lead what is the largest project in the history of Georgia.
- KMMG’s culture aims to blend Korean and American practices on a foundation of trust and open two-way communications to deliver the “Best of the Best” in North America.
- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc. (KMMG) is the first manufacturing site in North America for Seoul-based Kia Motors Corporation.
- KMMG is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Georgia.
- The plant was scheduled to begin production in the last quarter of 2009.
- At full capacity, the plant will employ approximately 2,500 team members.
