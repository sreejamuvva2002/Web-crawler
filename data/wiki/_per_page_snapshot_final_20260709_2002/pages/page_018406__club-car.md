# Club Car

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-13

**Source:** [Club Car Preparing to Expand into New Appling Facility | Club Car News](https://www.clubcar.com/en-us/our-company/news/expansion-new-appling-georgia-facility)  ·  `page_018406`

## Facts

- Club Car announced plans to expand into a new 105,000 square foot facility in Appling, Georgia.
- Mark Wagner serves as the President of Club Car.
- Mark Wagner stated that the new facility gives the company the opportunity to extend operations in the United States.
- The new facility is located in the White Oak Industrial Park.
- The new facility is situated near an existing Club Car facility that opened in 2019.
- Club Car plans to use the additional space to support warehousing and manufacturing functions.
- Construction of the new facility is expected to be completed by December 2023.
- Rick Evans serves as the Chair of the Board of Directors for the Development Authority of Columbia County.
- Rick Evans stated that Club Car has a long-standing relationship with Columbia County and values the investments made to expand and manufacture world-class golf cars and utility vehicles in Columbia County.
- Doug Duncan serves as the Chairman of the Columbia County Board of Commissioners.
- Doug Duncan stated that Club Car is a great community partner and expressed excitement for their continued growth in Columbia County.
- Club Car has maintained multi-purpose operations in Augusta since it was founded in 1958.
- Club Car designs and manufactures golf cars, commercial utility vehicles, and personal-use transportation.
- Club Car has over 60 years of experience producing small-wheel vehicles.
- Club Car is a leading manufacturer of gas and electric golf, utility, and personal transportation vehicles.
- The Club Car product portfolio includes the luxury electric vehicle brand Garia.
- The Club Car product portfolio includes the lightweight electric utility vehicle brand Melex.
- Club Car Vehicle Group focuses on environmentally responsible Zero Emission Vehicle (ZEV) technologies.
