# Aspen Aerogels, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2026-02-25

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels ended 2025 with $158.6 million in cash, cash equivalents, and restricted cash.
- Aspen expects to receive approximately $37.6 million from General Motors in Q1 2026 related to a commercial settlement for prior EV capacity adjustments.
- Aspen recorded a $291.2 million impairment charge in 2025 related to its previously planned Statesboro, Georgia plant.
- Aspen executed a non-binding letter of intent to sell assets from its demobilized Statesboro, Georgia facility and expects to complete the transaction during 2026.
- Aspen confirmed its Q4 2025 EV customer award relates to Volvo Cars, expanding its European OEM relationships, with additional awards anticipated in 2026.
- Aspen was awarded a North Sea subsea pipeline project with expected delivery in the third quarter of 2026.
- Aspen initiated a strategic review to evaluate opportunities to strengthen its long-term competitive position and optimize its capital structure.
- Aspen engaged Piper Sandler & Co. as its exclusive financial advisor for the strategic review.
- President and CEO Don Young stated the review aims to align the company's strategy, capital allocation, and asset base with value creation.
- CFO Grant Thoele noted that North American EV production levels decreased in response to evolving regulatory frameworks and end-market demand during 2025.
- Aspen reduced its fixed cost structure by more than $75 million in response to market conditions.
- Aspen anticipates continued softness in thermal barrier volumes as EV demand finds a floor at lower production levels, while expecting topline growth from Energy Industrial project momentum.
- Aspen's PyroThin® products enable solutions to thermal runaway challenges within the electric vehicle market.
- Thermal barrier segment revenue for Q4 2025 was $16.1 million, reflecting a significant reduction in customer demand following changes in regulatory frameworks and incentive programs.
- Full year 2025 thermal barrier segment revenue was $168.9 million, compared to $306.8 million in the prior year.
- Aspen's Q1 2026 revenue is expected to range between $35 million and $40 million.
- Aspen's FY 2026 capital expenditures are expected to be less than $10 million.
