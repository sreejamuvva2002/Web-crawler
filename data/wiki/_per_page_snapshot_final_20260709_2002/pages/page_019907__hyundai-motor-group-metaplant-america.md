# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-24

**Source:** [Hyundai is bringing jobs to Savannah. So are Hyundai suppliers. Here is who is coming.](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/hyundai-bringing-jobs-savannah-hyundai-141309502.html)  ·  `page_019907`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America opened in October and production has begun.
- The facility was announced in May 2022.
- It is the largest economic development project in Georgia history.
- It represents a $5.5 billion investment.
- It promises 8,500 jobs.
- More than a dozen suppliers announced they would build a facility in Georgia to support the plant.
- On May 24, 2023, Hanon Systems announced a planned facility in Bulloch County.
- Hanon Systems is a South Korean company specializing in automotive climate control systems.
- Hanon makes parts for multiple automakers, including General Motors, which named Hanon as 2022 supplier of the year.
- Hanon already supplies Hyundai with parts at factories in West Point, Georgia and Montgomery, Alabama.
- Hanon Systems investment is $40 million.
- Hanon Systems will create 160 jobs.
- Hanon Systems location is Gateway Regional Industrial Park near Statesboro.
- On Nov. 7, 2022, automobile frame manufacturer Joon Georgia Inc. announced a planned manufacturing facility in Bulloch County.
- Joon Georgia Inc. is part of Ajin USA, which is already a supplier for several Hyundai Motor Group facilities.
- Joon Georgia investment is $317 million.
- Joon Georgia will create 600-plus jobs.
- Joon Georgia location is Bruce Yawn Commerce Park, seven miles outside Statesboro.
- Just three weeks after Joon Georgia's announcement, Hyundai Mobis committed to build a facility in Bryan County.
- Hyundai Mobis is one of the world's biggest auto suppliers.
- Hyundai Mobis makes EV power electric systems and integrated charging controls.
- Hyundai Mobis investment is $926 million.
- Hyundai Mobis will create 1,500-plus jobs.
- Hyundai Mobis location is Belfast Commerce Park off Belfast-Keller Road in southern Bryan County.
- On Jan. 5, Korea-based Ecoplastic announced it would build consoles, bumpers and other parts at a Bulloch County manufacturing facility.
- Ecoplastic investment is $205 million.
- Ecoplastic will create 450-plus jobs.
- Ecoplastic location is U.S. 301, 10 miles south of Statesboro.
- Chatham County got its first Hyundai supplier Feb. 1 in Seoyon E-HWA.
- Seoyon E-HWA specializes in interior and exterior components such as headlinings and bumpers.
- Seoyon E-HWA investment is $76 million.
- Seoyon E-HWA will create 700-plus jobs.
- Seoyon E-HWA location is Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center near Bloomingdale.
- On Feb. 21, Kemp announced Sewon America would build a new manufacturing facility in Rincon.
- Sewon America first came to the U.S. in 2008 to Lagrange and has since doubled in size.
- Sewon America is one of the biggest auto body parts plants in the U.S.
- Sewon America production is slated to begin in 2025.
- Sewon America investment is $300 million plus.
- Sewon America will create 740 jobs.
- Sewon America location is Grande View Industrial Park in Rincon.
- PHA is a tier-1 auto parts supplier for Hyundai and Kia among others and has multiple facilities in the U.S.
- PHA new manufacturing facility will produce door modules, tailgate latches and more.
- PHA operations are expected to begin in 2024.
- PHA investment is $67 million plus.
- PHA will create 402 jobs.
- PHA location is Savannah Manufacturing Center in Chatham County.
- Daechang Seat Corp. USA specializes in automotive seat frames.
- Daechang Seat Corp. USA is recognized as one of the largest seat frame producers in the world and has more than 5,000 employees in seven countries.
- Daechang Seat Corp. USA investment is $72.5 million.
- Daechang Seat Corp. USA will create more than 570 jobs.
- Daechang Seat Corp. USA location is Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- Daesol Ausys was established in 2017 and designs and manufactures motor and electric vehicle interior components and accessories.
- Daesol Ausys is a key supplier for Kia Georgia, Hyundai Motor Group and General Motors.
- Daesol Ausys investment is $72 million.
- Daesol Ausys will create more than 140 jobs.
- Daesol Ausys location is Northwest Harris Business Park in Harris County.
- Das Corp. is located in the Candler County Industrial Park and will manufacture seating systems, safety seating components and seating structures.
- Das Corp. investment is $35 million.
- Das Corp. will create 300 jobs.
- Das Corp. location is Metter.
- Doowon Climate Control America, Inc. will be located in the Candler County Industrial Park and will produce automotive climate control systems.
- Doowon Climate Control America, Inc. investment is $30 million.
- Doowon Climate Control America, Inc. will create 200 jobs.
- Doowon Climate Control America, Inc. location is Metter.
- Hyundai Industrial Co., Ltd. is a Hyundai and Kia supplier that specializes in car seats, arm rests and head rests.
- Hyundai Industrial Co., Ltd. investment is $24 million.
- Hyundai Industrial Co., Ltd. will create 100 jobs.
- Hyundai Industrial Co., Ltd. location is Coweta County.
- Kyungshin America Corp. is a Hyundai supplier that will manufacture automotive wire harnesses for traditional and electric vehicles.
- Kyungshin America Corp. investment is $22 million.
- Kyungshin America Corp. will create 70 jobs.
- Kyungshin America Corp. location is Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub.
- Hwashin is a Tier-1 supplier with multiple facilities around the globe that will plant roots in Dublin and build a facility to craft chassis and body parts.
- Hwashin investment is $176 million.
- Hwashin will create 460 jobs.
- Hwashin location is Laurens County.
- NVA Korea is a Hyundai supplier that will produce batteries for electric vehicles.
- NVA Korea investment is $72 million.
- NVA Korea will create 160 jobs.
- NVA Korea location is Laurens County.
- Seohan Auto Georgia is a supplier that will manufacture H/shafts, axles and brake systems for Hyundai.
- Seohan Auto Georgia investment is $72 million.
- Seohan Auto Georgia will create 180 jobs.
- Seohan Auto Georgia location is Liberty County.
