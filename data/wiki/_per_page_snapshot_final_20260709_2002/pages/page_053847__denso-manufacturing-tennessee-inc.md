## Facts

- The Building 701 Plant Expansion project in Athens, TN doubled the existing facility from 180,000 SF to over 400,000 SF.
- The expanded plant manufactures a patented direct fuel injector system for automobiles.
- The project includes a new 2400 ton chiller plant.
- The project includes steam boilers for humidification.
- The project includes hot water boilers for heating.
- The project includes process water cooling for four kw centrifugal air compressors.
- The project features a new web based DDC control system designed to integrate and upgrade existing controls serving the entire campus and process utilities.
- ICT provided mechanical, electrical, plumbing and fire protection design for the new facility.
- Denso Manufacturing Tennessee, Inc. serves as both the owner and client of the project.
- The project falls under the manufacturing category.
