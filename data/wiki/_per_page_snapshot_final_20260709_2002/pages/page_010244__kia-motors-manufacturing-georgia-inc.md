# KIA Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2019-09-13

**Source:** [KIA Motors produces its 3 millionth vehicle in the U.S.A. – iloveza.com](https://www.iloveza.com/blogs/news/kia-motors-produces-its-3-millionth-vehicle-in-the-u-s-a)  ·  `page_010244`

## Facts

- KIA Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc (KMMG) produced its three millionth vehicle in the U.S.A., which was a white KIA 2020 Telluride SX Prestige.
- KMMG is located in West Point, Georgia.
- KMMG represents an investment of more than US$1.1 billion.
- KMMG began mass production on 16 November 2009.
- KMMG has an annual capacity of more than 340,000 units.
- KMMG produces more than 40% of all KIA vehicles sold in the United States.
- KMMG produced three million vehicles in less than 10 years.
- The plant operates under a “One System, One Team” philosophy.
- Jangsoo (Jason) Shin serves as the President and CEO of KMMG.
- KMMG builds the Telluride, Sorento, and Optima models.
- KMMG will celebrate its 10th year of production later in 2019.
- KMMG's products have been recognized for outstanding quality, reliability, and performance by J.D. Power & Associates, Consumer Reports, the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA), the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety (IIHS), and a variety of media outlets.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp praised KMMG as an example of hardworking Georgians performing at world-class levels.
- Governor Brian Kemp attributed KMMG's continued investment to Georgia's pro-business atmosphere.
- KIA Motors Corporation operates four manufacturing facilities in South Korea (Sohari, Hwasung, Gwangju and Seosan) with a combined annual capacity of more than 1,8 million units.
- KIA Motors operates three manufacturing facilities at Yancheng in China with a combined annual capacity of nearly 900,000 units.
- KIA Motors operates the €1 billion Zilina plant in Slovakia, which has an annual production capacity of 330,000 units and caters predominantly for the European production of Ceed and Sportage models.
- Both the Zilina and Yancheng plants opened in 2007.
- In 2016, KIA Motors opened a US$1 billion, 400,000 unit production facility in Monterrey, Mexico.
- In January 2019, KIA Motors opened its latest production facility in Anantapur, located in India’s Andra Pradesh province.
- The new Anantapur plant represents an investment of US$1 billion and has an annual capacity of 300,000 units.
- Production at the Anantapur plant commenced in August 2019.
- The first model produced at the Anantapur plant was the new KIA Seltos compact SUV.
- Several additional models are destined to be produced in Anantapur.
