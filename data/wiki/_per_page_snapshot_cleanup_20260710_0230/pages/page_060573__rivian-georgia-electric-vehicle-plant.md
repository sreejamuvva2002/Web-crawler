# Rivian Georgia Electric Vehicle Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-01-18

**Source:** [Neighbors in Social Circle and Rutledge left out of the discussion on Rivian Georgia EV plant](https://www.ajc.com/news/rivians-georgia-electric-vehicle-plant-sparks-fears-among-neighbors/MHSOOW54DNE4ZEOZ4ZDN3DVCVY/)  ·  `page_060573`

## Facts

- Rivian plans to begin building a giant electric vehicle factory in Georgia soon.
- The proposed plant site is located about 300 yards from Clint Powell’s home in Social Circle.
- Powell owns a two-acre lot and has lived in the area for 17 years.
- Residents are exploring ways to stop or limit the project, including throwing up possible zoning roadblocks.
- Local and state authorities negotiated in secret with Rivian for months last year while keeping residents in the dark.
- The site is situated in a largely rural area approximately 45 miles east of Atlanta.
- The development includes a 2,500-acre combined-use industrial park.
- $75 million is designated for road improvements related to the project.
- A local opposition group called 'Rivian Plant Opposed Group Local In Facebook' has formed.
- Eric Allsman is listed as a contact for the opposition group.
- Rivian originally announced the project back in December 2021.
- Residents are demanding answers on how many jobs will be created and what the overall impact will be.
- The Local Economic Development Department in Atlanta is a contact point for the project.
- The project is in the early stages of development.
