# Boogook Georgia, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [MBCIA Celebrates Boogook Georgia, LLC as a 2024 Trade & Industry CiCi Community Impact Winner - MBCIA](https://mbcia.com/industry/mbcia-celebrates-boogook-georgia-llc-as-a-2024-trade-industry-cici-community-impact-winner/)  ·  `page_053194`

## Facts

- Boogook Georgia, LLC was honored as a recipient of the 2024 CiCi Community Impact award by the Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority (MBCIA).
- Boogook is a global leader in automotive tube manufacturing that specializes in crafting automotive parts and materials crucial for fluid systems within vehicles.
- Last year, Boogook Georgia unveiled plans to invest $6.8 million to bolster its operations in Macon-Bibb County.
- The company also planned an additional $300,000 in leasehold improvements.
- This significant foreign investment enhances the automobile supply market in the Southeast and solidifies the region’s reputation as a hub for automotive manufacturing.
- Boogook Georgia is revitalizing a sprawling two-million-square-foot industrial building located at 2600 Weaver Road.
- The facility had sat vacant for nearly 15 years prior to this project.
- Boogook Georgia plans to employ 80 full-time professionals.
- This project signifies the company’s first step into the U.S. market.
- Stephen Adams, Executive Director of the MBCIA, stated that the decision by Boogook to locate to Macon reinforces the importance and value of growing their automotive industry partners in the region.
- Stephen Adams noted that Middle Georgia is an extremely attractive and competitive area for these companies.
- Stephen Adams expressed excitement about seeing this industry become a key player in future economic and job growth.
