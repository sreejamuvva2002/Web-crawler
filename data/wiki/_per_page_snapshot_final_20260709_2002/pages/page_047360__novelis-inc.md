## Facts

- On September 16, 2025, Novelis Inc. entered into an amendment to its Credit Agreement dated March 11, 2025 with Novelis Holdings Inc., lenders, and Citibank, N.A. as administrative agent.
- The Amendment reduces the Applicable Margin for loans under the Term Loan Credit Agreement effective September 16, 2025.
- Prior to September 16, 2025, borrowings accrued interest at either a base rate/federal funds rate/Term SOFR plus 1.00% margin or Term SOFR plus 2.00% margin.
- On or after September 16, 2025, the Term Loan accrues interest at either a base rate/federal funds rate/Term SOFR plus 0.75% margin or Term SOFR plus 1.75% margin.
- The Amendment extends the period during which a prepayment premium may apply from September 11, 2025 to March 16, 2026.
- On or after September 16, 2025, the Term Loan may be prepaid without penalty unless connected to a repricing amendment or refinancing prior to March 16, 2026, which subjects it to a 1.00% prepayment premium.
- On September 18, 2025, Novelis Corporation completed a financing transaction via a Loan Agreement dated September 1, 2025 with The Industrial Development Authority of Baldwin County.
- The Industrial Development Authority of Baldwin County loaned $100 million in proceeds from Solid Waste Disposal Revenue Bonds (Novelis Corporation Project), Series 2025B, to Novelis Corporation.
- The bond proceeds will finance a portion of the costs of constructing Novelis Corporation's solid waste disposal facilities located in Baldwin County, Alabama.
- The Bonds were issued pursuant to an Indenture of Trust dated September 1, 2025 with Regions Bank as trustee.
- The Bonds were sold at 100% of their principal amount and bear interest at 4.625% per annum.
- A mandatory tender for purchase of the Bonds occurs on June 1, 2032.
- After the initial term interest rate period on June 1, 2032, the Bonds may convert to a variable interest rate period or remain in a term interest rate period, with rates resetting at the end of each period.
- The Bonds mature on June 1, 2055.
- Novelis Corporation will make semiannual interest payments on the outstanding principal on January 15 and July 15 of each year, with the first payment due January 15, 2026.
- On the Mandatory Tender Date and other specified dates, bondholders must tender bonds for purchase by BofA Securities, Inc., acting as remarketing agent.
- The Bonds are guaranteed jointly and severally by Novelis Inc. and certain of its subsidiaries via a Guaranty Agreement dated September 18, 2025.
- If the Bonds are declared taxable or the Loan Agreement is invalid, they are subject to mandatory redemption within 60 days at 100% of principal plus accrued interest.
- The current report was signed by Christopher Courts, Executive Vice President and Chief Legal Officer of Novelis Inc., on September 18, 2025.
