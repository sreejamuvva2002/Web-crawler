## Facts

- Johns Manville is a Berkshire Hathaway company.
- Propex Operating Company, LLC is a global leader in developing and manufacturing innovative geosynthetic and erosion control products and systems.
- The company's engineered solutions stabilize the earth, extending the life of levees, dams, roadways, and other key infrastructure.
- The company culture emphasizes innovation and a commitment to developing new markets and applications for geosynthetic materials.
- Propex operates a manufacturing and distribution center in Ringgold, GA.
- The Ringgold facility encompasses more than 807,000 sq. ft. of manufacturing and distribution space.
- The plant boasts the industry’s largest geosynthetic capacity under one roof.
- The Ringgold facility houses an onsite testing facility and lab.
- Dozens of tests are performed on each product daily for quality assurance.
- Testing allows traceability for each product shipped from the facility and ensures delivery of top-quality products to customers.
- The lab is certified by the Geosynthetic Accreditation Institute/Laboratory Accreditation Program and American Association for Laboratory Accreditation (Testing Certificate # 2973.01).
