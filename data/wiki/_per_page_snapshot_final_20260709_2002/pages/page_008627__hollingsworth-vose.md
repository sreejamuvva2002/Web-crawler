# Hollingsworth & Vose

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-01-03

**Source:** [Hollingsworth & Vose expanding operation in Floyd County](https://www.wdbj7.com/2023/01/03/hollingsworth-vose-expanding-operation-floyd-county/)  ·  `page_008627`

## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose is investing over $40 million to expand its operation in Floyd County, Virginia.
- The expansion will create 25 jobs.
- The funds will be used to add over 28,000 square feet to the company's facility.
- The facility is located at 365 Christiansburg Pike Northeast in Floyd County, Virginia.
- The added space will make room for new production equipment to meet increased demand.
- Governor Glenn Youngkin stated that Hollingsworth & Vose has generated positive economic impact and job opportunities in Floyd County for more than four decades.
- Governor Glenn Youngkin noted that the significant investment further solidifies the company’s commitment to Virginia.
- Governor Glenn Youngkin highlighted that businesses with a long history of expansion in the Commonwealth offer powerful testimonials on why a Virginia location is a foundation for success.
- Josh Ayer, CEO of Hollingsworth & Vose, stated the company has been part of the Floyd, Virginia community since 1976.
- Josh Ayer said the facility is essential to serving both global and domestic customers.
- Josh Ayer explained that Virginia was chosen for the expansion due to its positive business environment and strong support from the Commonwealth of Virginia and Floyd County.
- Hollingsworth & Vose is a global manufacturer of advanced materials used in filtration, battery, and industrial appliances.
