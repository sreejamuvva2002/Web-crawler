# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-24

## Facts

- Hanon Systems will invest more than $40 million for a manufacturing facility in Bulloch County.
- The move is expected to create 160 jobs.
- Hanon Systems specializes in automotive climate control systems.
- Another Hyundai supplier is coming soon to the Savannah area.
- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp’s office announced the investment on Tuesday.
- Gov. Brian Kemp stated that Georgia’s automotive and e-mobility industries continue to grow as they proudly welcome Hanon Systems to Georgia.
- Gov. Brian Kemp noted that as the state furthers its position as the EV capital of America, new jobs and opportunities are coming to hardworking Georgians all over the state.
