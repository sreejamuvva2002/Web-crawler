# Hyundai MOBIS Georgia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2007-05-17

**Source:** [Hyundai MOBIS Tour - West Point -  Georgia Manufacturing Alliance](https://www.georgiamanufacturingalliance.com/events/hyundai-mobis-tour---west-point)  ·  `page_001185`

## Facts

- Hyundai MOBIS Georgia is located at 7001 Kia Parkway, West Point, GA 31833, United States.
- Established on May 17, 2007, the facility operates as a three-shift manufacturing operation.
- The plant functions as a Tier 1 supplier to Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, LLC (KMMG).
- MOBIS Georgia assembles four major vehicle modules: Front Chassis, Rear Chassis, Cockpit, and Front End.
- The facility also produces front and rear bumpers for the three vehicles manufactured by KMMG.
- Using components from domestic and overseas suppliers, the plant utilizes a just-in-time, just-in-sequence production system.
- MOBIS Georgia supplies parts for over 1,400 vehicles per day.
