# Volvo Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-21

**Source:** [Volvo Group to cut up to 800 jobs at US facilities – report](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/volvo-group-cut-800-jobs-102957738.html?fr=sycsrp_catchall)  ·  `page_014400`

## Facts

- Volvo Group is planning to lay off as many as 800 workers across three US facilities within the next three months.
- Reuters reported the layoffs citing a spokesperson.
- The decision comes amidst market uncertainty and demand concerns, which have been exacerbated by the tariffs imposed by US President Donald Trump.
- Volvo Group North America confirmed the layoffs are expected to affect the Mack Trucks site in Macungie, Pennsylvania.
- The layoffs are also expected to affect two other facilities in Dublin, Virginia, and Hagerstown, Maryland.
- Volvo Group North America represented about 29% of total 2024 sales for Sweden-based Volvo Group.
- Volvo Group employs more than 19,600 people in North America.
- The company operates 16 manufacturing and remanufacturing facilities in the US, Canada and Mexico.
- Volvo Group's layoffs are part of a broader response from the car and truck industry struggling with the impact of tariffs on certain parts imposed by the US Government.
- Tariffs have raised vehicle manufacturing costs.
