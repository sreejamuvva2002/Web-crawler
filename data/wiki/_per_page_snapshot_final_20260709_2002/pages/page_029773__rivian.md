# Rivian

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Rivian is constructing a new manufacturing plant outside Social Circle, Georgia.
- The project represents a multi-billion dollar investment expected to create 7,500+ jobs in the state by 2030.
- Vertical construction at the facility is scheduled to begin next year.
- A groundbreaking ceremony for the new manufacturing facility was held on Tuesday, September 16.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp and Georgia Speaker of the House Jon Burns attended the groundbreaking ceremony alongside other state and local officials.
- Customer vehicle production at the facility is planned to begin in 2028, starting with the midsize, 5-seater R2 SUV and R3 crossover.
- Rivian CEO and Founder RJ Scaringe stated that the new facility will soon employ thousands of Georgians.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp praised Rivian's commitment and tenacity, stating the facility will bring generational benefits to the region.
- The project is expected to generate billions in economic benefits by supporting suppliers, vendors, and local businesses in Jasper, Morgan, Newton, and Walton Counties and the surrounding region.
- Rivian is developing partnerships with universities, technical colleges, and local, regional, and state institutions as it scales operations.
- Serra Hall, executive director of the Newton County Industrial Development Authority, stated that Rivian's manufacturing plans have been paired with local community investment from the start.
- Rivian hosted a community day on Sunday, September 14, bringing together nearly 1,000 neighbors and friends for a celebration featuring local businesses, entertainment, and family activities.
- Mike Hopkins, executive director of the Newton County Water and Sewerage Authority, praised the community day event for bringing people together with strong turnout and local activities.
- The celebration featured the Rivian Adventure Track and Rally Road, taking families and attendees on a ride along to experience the R1S or R1T.
