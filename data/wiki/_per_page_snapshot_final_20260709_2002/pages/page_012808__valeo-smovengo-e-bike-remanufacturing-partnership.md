# Valeo-Smovengo E-Bike Remanufacturing Partnership

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-24

**Source:** [Valeo and Smovengo join to remanufacture  Vélib' e-bike motors and batteries, ETManufacturing](https://manufacturing.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/automotive/valeo-and-smovengo-join-to-remanufacture-vlib-e-bike-motors-and-batteries/110385021)  ·  `page_012808`

## Facts

- Valeo and Smovengo joined forces to remanufacture the motors and batteries of the 8,000 electric Vélib' bicycles that tour the streets of Greater Paris.
- Smovengo operates Vélib' Métropole, a large-scale public bicycle sharing system in Paris and 64 of its communes.
- Valeo offers a range of solutions for small mobility devices such as bicycles, scooters and electric rickshaws.
- Valeo's commitment to the e-bike sector includes the supply of Valeo Cyclee electric motors and the circular maintenance of other essential components used in e-bikes.
- This service is fully part of Valeo's circular economy strategy (4R) to expand the lifespan of existing mobility systems.
- For over 40 years, Valeo has put its expertise in the design and manufacture of original parts at the service of remanufacturing.
- Two Valeo sites specialising in remanufacturing have been selected for the partnership with Smovengo.
- Valeo launched the Circular Electronics Lab in Nevers (France) in 2023 to set up a dedicated team combining flexibility, quality and competitiveness to incubate circular electronics projects.
- The Nevers unit successfully launched the first ever remanufactured front camera and expanded its circular services for Small Mobility, starting with the repair of batteries for Smovengo.
- The “Circular Innovation Factory” in Czechowice (Poland) serves as Valeo's Competence Centre for remanufactured products such as starters, alternators, dual mass flywheels, dual clutches and air conditioning compressors.
- The Czechowice site is expanding its remanufacturing activity into the field of micromobility with the motors equipped on Smovengo’s electric bikes.
- Since becoming the operator of Vélib' Métropole in 2018, Smovengo covers 65 municipalities and 450 km².
- Every day, Smovengo's teams in the field and in the workshops repair 1,000 bikes to ensure a constant availability of the fleet.
- Smovengo's R&D department steers efforts to design parts that are increasingly durable, reliable and refurbishable to reduce dependence on Asian suppliers.
- Since 2020, Smovengo has implemented a complete industrial process for recycling spare parts, preparing all maintenance parts for a second life or recycled.
- Currently, 30 employees are dedicated daily to testing and repairing these components at Smovengo.
- A wheel production line in Alfortville produces the 2,500 wheels needed for fleet maintenance each week using new or recycled parts.
- Smovengo was certified as a bicycle manufacturer in October 2021, allowing for the annual production of over 2,000 CE-certified electric and mechanical bicycles using new and recycled parts.
- Smovengo entered into a partnership with Valeo to gain specific expertise and industrialisation capacity for repairing complex parts such as electric motors and batteries.
- Eric Schuler, CEO of Valeo Service, stated the collaboration demonstrates Valeo's commitment to safer and more sustainable mobility and its capability to offer solutions for the electrification of all forms of mobility.
- Jacques Greiveldinger, Managing Director of Smovengo, explained the partnership provides ecological, economic and logistical benefits, noting that motor and battery revaluation is only the first step to be extended to many other bicycle parts.
