## Facts

- Morgan Corporation is the client for a truck body manufacturing facility located in Rydal, Georgia.
- NOVA performed a structural condition survey and capacity analysis of the Morgan truck body manufacturing facility in Rydal, Georgia.
- The project scope included analyzing overhead suspended trolley cranes used to fabricate truck bodies for delivery trucks.
- A list of immediate and maintenance repairs to the facility was provided by NOVA.
- NOVA performed materials testing on the structural steel frame of the facility.
- NOVA conducted a structural capacity analysis to check the roof framing for code compliance with additional dead and live loads from the overhead trolley cranes.
- NOVA has been a trusted partner for engineering, environmental services, and consulting since 1996.
- Services provided by NOVA included Forensic and Non-Destructive Testing, Construction Materials Testing, and Special Inspections.
