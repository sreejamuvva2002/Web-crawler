# Thomson Plastics, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [TD Ameritrade, Thomson Plastics to expand in the Ann Arbor region - Ann Arbor SPARK](https://annarborusa.org/news/td-ameritrade-thomson-plastics-to-expand-in-the-ann-arbor-region/)  ·  `page_011317`

## Facts

- Thomson Plastics is establishing its first manufacturing facility in Michigan.
- The new facility will be located in Howell Township.
- Thomson Plastics plans to create 46 new jobs at the new plant.
- The company plans to invest nearly $4 million over two years.
- Thomson Plastics is an injection-molding company headquartered in Thomson, Ga.
- Howell Township awarded Thomson Plastics a 50 percent property tax abatement on its new investment.
- Ann Arbor SPARK assisted Thomson Plastics in applying for local incentives.
- Thomson Plastics coordinated with Michigan Works! and Mott Community College for the new plant.
- The company plans to participate in the Michigan New Jobs Training program that provides on-the-job training to new hires.
- Rick Kibbey, president and chief operating officer of Thomson Plastics, stated the decision to operate in Howell was due to easy access to a local expressway and central location for major regions of automotive manufacturing in Michigan.
