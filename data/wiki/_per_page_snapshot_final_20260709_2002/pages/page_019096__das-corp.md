# DAS Corp

## Facts

- DAS Corp plans to build a USD 35 million factory in eastern Georgia.
- The new factory will supply a new Hyundai Motor Group plant near Savannah.
- DAS Corp makes metal frames and other parts for automotive seats.
- DAS Corp will hire 300 people to work at the new plant in Metter.
- The DAS factory will take up about 200,000 square feet (18,600 square meters) of space.
- Starting pay for workers at the new plant will be USD 20 an hour.
- The state of Georgia will pay to train workers for the new plant.
- The state provided USD 500,000 last year to help local officials complete a road and a sewer connection in the industrial park where DAS is located.
- The city of Metter and Candler County are granting 10 years of property tax breaks to DAS Corp.
- DAS Corp could qualify for USD 4.5 million in state income tax credits, calculated at USD 3,000 per job over five years.
- To qualify for the USD 4.5 million in state income tax credits, workers must make at least USD 31,300 a year.
- DAS Corp is one of the companies supplying Hyundai's USD 7.5 billion plant to assemble electric vehicles and batteries in Ellabell, near Savannah.
- Hyundai's plant was announced last year.
- Hyundai's plant could grow to 8,500 employees.
- Hyundai's plant is scheduled to begin producing vehicles in 2025.
- Suppliers to the Hyundai plant have pledged to invest USD 2.2 billion.
- Suppliers to the Hyundai plant have pledged to hire nearly 5,300 people.
- DAS Corp is already a supplier to Hyundai and its sister automaker Kia.
- DAS Corp opened a plant in Montgomery, Alabama, in 2012.
- The Montgomery, Alabama plant has grown to nearly 500 employees.
- DAS Corp Chief Operating Officer Sean Kim stated the company wanted its plant located close to the new Hyundai plant.
- DAS Corp aims to begin production in Metter in late 2024.
- Besides South Korea, DAS Corp has other plants in China, Turkey, the Czech Republic, India, and Brazil.
- DAS Corp is the second company in a week to announce an investment in Metter, which has a population of 4,000 people.
