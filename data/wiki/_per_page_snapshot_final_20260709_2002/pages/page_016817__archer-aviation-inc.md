# Archer Aviation Inc.

## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. is an aerospace company advancing sustainable urban air mobility.
- Archer Aviation will create 1,000 jobs.
- Archer Aviation will invest $118 million over 10 years in a new manufacturing facility.
- The facility will be a state-of-the-art electric vertical takeoff and landing aircraft (eVTOL) manufacturing facility.
- The facility is located alongside the Covington Municipal Airport in Newton County.
- Archer plans to initially build out a 350,000 square-foot facility.
- The facility will be situated on a 96-acre site.
- The initial facility will be capable of producing up to 650 aircraft per year.
- The eVTOL aircraft manufacturing facility is expected to create over 1,000 jobs over the long term.
- Construction is expected to be completed in the first half of 2024.
- Initial production is expected to begin in the second half of 2024.
- The facility will be capable of being expanded by an additional 550,000 square feet.
- The expansion is estimated to support production of up to 2,300 aircraft per year.
- Plateau will be providing site development for the project.
- Plateau's scope includes clearing, grading, erosion control, excavation, storm drainage, sanitary sewer, and water (fire, domestic, irrigation).
- The service type for the project is OEM, Regional, Specialty.
- The location is listed as Oxford, Georgia.
- Additional project details list the site size as 78 acres.
- The project involves 577,596 CY of earthmoving.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia has made fostering innovation a priority to bring jobs of the future to the No. 1 state for business.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that Georgia's established leadership in the aerospace industry was essential to landing Archer Aviation in Newton County.
- Governor Brian Kemp expressed pleasure at welcoming Archer Aviation to the Peach State.
