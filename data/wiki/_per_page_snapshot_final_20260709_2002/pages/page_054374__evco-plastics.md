## Facts

- A part’s design determines 80% of its cost and impacts quality, reliability, functionality, serviceability and manufacturability.
- Design affects how quickly a new product can be introduced and is a key driver of competitive advantage.
- EVCO Plastics invests in design for use, design for manufacturability and design for competitive advantage.
- EVCO Plastics believes its success is determined by maximizing client success through the critical design and engineering process.
- Partnering with clients early in the design phase is identified as one of two most critical factors for success.
- EVCO Plastics focuses on five keys to success for engineering out cost and engineering in quality and optimal performance.
- The first key is Part Design, which involves understanding product application, intended market and performance, end use objectives, and use environment.
- The second key is Mold Design & Build, focusing on mold efficiency, longevity and performance.
- The third key is Molding Process, covering injection molding technologies, cycle times, material flow and employing scientific injection molding.
- The fourth key is Quality Assurance, focusing on tolerances, part inspection, steel safe mold and more.
- The fifth key is Automation in the injection molding process and downstream to make it simple and lean.
