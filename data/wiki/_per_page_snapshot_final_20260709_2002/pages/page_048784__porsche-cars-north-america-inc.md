# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-05-05

**Source:** [Porsche Becomes the Official Car of WeatherTech Raceway Laguna Seca](https://weathertechraceway.com/blogs/news/porsche-becomes-the-official-car-of-weathertech-raceway-laguna-seca)  ·  `page_048784`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) announced it will become The Official Car of WeatherTech Raceway Laguna Seca in Monterey, California.
- Since 2011, Laguna Seca has been home to the Rennsport Reunion, the world’s largest ticketed gathering of Porsche enthusiasts.
- As part of the sponsorship, Porsche will support Friends of Laguna Seca in its commitment to revitalize the Raceway and Recreation Area.
- A Porsche Driving Center (PDC) will launch at the raceway in 2026, offering introductory, intermediate, and advanced vehicle control training courses.
- Timo Resch, President and CEO of PCNA, stated that many greatest moments in motorsport history have happened at Laguna Seca and expressed excitement about allowing the next generation to drive Porsches.
- Friends of Laguna Seca board member Bruce Canepa welcomed Porsche as the official car, noting the partnership brings together two iconic brands.
- Porsche has hosted four Rennsport Reunions in the last 15 years at Laguna Seca and earned its 600th and 601st IMSA victories there last year.
- In February 2024, the Taycan Turbo GT set a track record of 1:27.87min, earning the title of fastest electric series-production car at Laguna Seca.
- Rennsport Reunion 7 in 2023 hosted over 91,000 attendees over four days, marking the biggest attendance for any Rennsport Reunion to date.
- The sponsorship launches with Porsche serving as the official safety car for the TireRack.com Monterey Sportscar Championship IMSA race weekend from May 9-11.
- The race will be broadcast live on Sunday, May 11, at noon PDT/3 p.m. EDT on NBC.
- PCNA is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, at One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA, and has been based there since 1998.
- Established in 1984, PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera, and Taycan.
- The Atlanta campus houses the first Porsche Experience Center in North America, featuring two module-based 1.6-mile driver development tracks, a business center, and Restaurant 356.
- The Atlanta campus also serves as the U.S. headquarters for Porsche Classic.
- PCNA operates a second Porsche Experience Center near Los Angeles with an 8-module, 4.1-mile driver development track, a business center, Restaurant 917, and Porsche Motorsport North America headquarters.
- PCNA supports 203 independently owned Porsche Centers in the U.S., four Porsche studios, and five satellite stores, supplying parts, service, marketing, and training.
- The Porsche Car Configurator can be used by anyone to customize and pre-order any vehicle in that lineup at any time.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Porsche boasts a racing heritage with over 30,000 motorsport wins to date.
- Photos and video footage are available to accredited journalists on the Porsche Press Database and on the Porsche Cars North America Newsroom.
- WeatherTech Raceway Laguna Seca is operated by Friends of Laguna Seca, a 501(c)(3) non-profit, and features the world-famous Corkscrew turn.
