# Optronics International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-10-23

**Source:** [Optronics introduces lamps with GloLight technology | Trucks, Parts, Service](https://www.truckpartsandservice.com/business/growth-strategies/article/14989823/optronics-introduces-lamps-with-glolight-technology)  ·  `page_013080`

## Facts

- Optronics International announced the debut of what it says is the industry’s first stop, tail, turn lamps with integrated graphics.
- The new lamps merge brand identities into the lighting design, juxtaposing a smooth GloLight lens appearance against a more traditional LED pattern.
- GloLight technology allows Optronics to consolidate complex graphics into the functional operation of the lamps.
- The lamp’s traditional LED-style lens pattern surrounds the logo graphic in the middle of the lamp.
- The GloLight logo portion of the lamp illuminates when operating in its standard function as a tail lamp, and when the lamp’s turn signal or brake functions are in operation, all portions of the LED lamp brighten.
- Brett Johnson, Optronics International president and CEO, stated this is an industry first and a novel use for their GloLight technology.
- Johnson noted that logos and branding have been widely incorporated into conspicuity tape for decades, so the migration to lighting is a logical one.
- Marketing professionals recognize that the 4-in. round and 6-in. oval lighting formats are among the most noticeable features on any vehicle.
- Johnson said whether consciously aware or not, drivers constantly monitor commercial vehicle taillights, particularly at night.
- Optronics employed its 3-D printing capabilities in preparing prototypes for manufacturers.
- The company worked with a number of OEMs during lamp development, including Vanguard National Trailer Corporation and Miller Industries.
- Charlie Mudd, president of Vanguard National Trailer Corporation, stated they were among the first trailer manufacturers to make Optronics’ GloLight part of their standard lighting package.
- Will Miller, president and co-CEO of Miller Industries, stated that with the new custom logo stop, tail, turn lamps, Optronics presented an opportunity to further their brand’s recognition and strength.
- The LED lamps offer users a broad level of creative design flexibility for branding because the GloLight technology can be used in both red and white or a combination of both.
- Lens striations can be used to achieve unique and complementary visual effects.
