# Hyundai Mobis

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-09-15

**Source:** [Hyundai Mobis takes a new step with future mobility through further expansion in the North American market - PR Newswire APAC](https://en.prnasia.com/releases/global/hyundai-mobis-takes-a-new-step-with-future-mobility-through-further-expansion-in-the-north-american-market-375412.shtml)  ·  `page_001158`

## Facts

- Hyundai Mobis introduced its expansion strategy in the North American market during a press conference at the North American International Auto Show (NAIAS) on Sept. 15, 2022.
- Axel Maschka serves as Executive Vice President and head of Hyundai Mobis Global OE Sales.
- The company's growth strategy is built on two keywords: Plant Expansion and Value Expansion.
- Hyundai Mobis plans to expand its production facilities across the globe for prompt and timely supply to global automakers.
- Hyundai Mobis will construct more EV dedicated sites in North America and invest in EV components and battery system assemblies to allow rapid and environmentally optimized local production.
- Hyundai Mobis currently operates 44 production bases globally.
- Hyundai Mobis's North American facilities are located in Alabama, Georgia, Ohio, and Michigan.
- North America leading the electric vehicle shift to achieve carbon neutrality and green mobility is integral to the company's electrification production expansion plan.
- Having a strong production capacity will be key to winning sales orders as the EV market continues to grow.
- Hyundai Mobis has over ten years of expertise in producing high-quality electrification components.
- Hyundai Mobis revealed its value expansion plan to increase its sales orders in the North American market with value-added technologies optimized specifically for electric and autonomous vehicles.
- Hyundai Mobis presented future mobility technologies including the electric Complete Chassis Platform Module (eCCPM) and next-generation AR HUD.
- Axel Maschka stated, "Hyundai Mobis has a wide range of innovations in its portfolio, and is ready for the future of mobility. Our vision is to bring the future to the present and make it more valuable."
- Hyundai Mobis successfully mass-produced key electrification components including the battery system for the e-GMP platform used in Hyundai and Kia electric vehicles.
- Hyundai Mobis also mass-produced PE modules.
- Value-added products including the eCCPM module, electrification platform, AR HUD, and lighting grilles are drawing attention from clients in North America for their versatility and innovation.
- Hyundai Mobis announced a goal of driving a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 36% in the North American market through the year 2030.
- Hyundai Mobis reported total order volumes of $660 million USD in 2020.
- Hyundai Mobis reported total order volumes of $1.4 billion USD in 2021.
- Hyundai Mobis reported total order volumes of $1.7 billion USD in the first half of 2022.
- Hyundai Mobis is ranked as the global no. 6 automotive supplier.
- Hyundai Mobis is headquartered in Seoul, Korea.
- Hyundai Mobis has outstanding expertise in sensors, sensor fusion in ECUs and software development for safety control.
- Hyundai Mobis's product portfolio includes various components for electrification, brakes, chassis and suspension, steering, airbags, lighting and automotive electronics.
- Hyundai Mobis operates an R&D headquarters in Korea.
- Hyundai Mobis operates four technology centers in the United States, Germany, China and India.
