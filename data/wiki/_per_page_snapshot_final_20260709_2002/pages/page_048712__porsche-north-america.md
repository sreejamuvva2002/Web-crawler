# Porsche North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-01

## Facts

- Porsche delivered 76,219 new vehicles in the U.S. in 2025, setting a sales record.
- Porsche’s North American headquarters is located in Atlanta.
- Sales growth was driven by an 8% increase in Macan crossover SUV models.
- Macan models account for more than a third of Porsche’s annual U.S. sales.
- The Macan is available in both conventional gasoline and all-electric powertrains.
- The 2025 sales record exceeds 2024 sales by 52 vehicles.
- The 2025 record marks the best year for new U.S. auto sales since before the COVID-19 pandemic in 2019.
- Porsche’s Atlanta headquarters is celebrating its 10th anniversary.
