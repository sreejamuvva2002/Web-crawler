# RealTruck

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-04-19

**Source:** [Seven Pro Players Invest in RealTruck, a Division of Truck Hero in Ann Arbor - DBusiness Magazine](https://www.dbusiness.com/daily-news/seven-pro-players-invest-in-realtruck-a-division-of-truck-hero-in-ann-arbor/)  ·  `page_049023`

## Facts

- RealTruck is an online destination retailer of U.S.-made aftermarket truck parts and accessories.
- RealTruck operates as part of Truck Hero Inc., which is located in Ann Arbor.
- RealTruck announced its first-ever advisory team composed of seven elite professional athletes who are also investors in the company.
- The athlete investors include Dak Prescott (Dallas Cowboys), George Kittle (San Francisco 49ers), C.J. McCollum (New Orleans Pelicans), Jason Kelce (Philadelphia Eagles), Dustin Brown (Los Angeles Kings), Byron Jones (Miami Dolphins), and Beau Allen (Super Bowl LII Champion).
- The athletes joined RealTruck through their November 2021 investments made in partnership with Patricof Co, a specialized private investment and advisory platform for professional athletes.
- Dak Prescott is a two-time Pro Bowl Quarterback who serves on the advisory team.
- George Kittle is a three-time Pro Bowl tight end who serves on the advisory team.
- The athlete investors will engage in quarterly meetings with RealTruck’s top executives.
- The advisory team will have behind-the-scenes access to company operations to provide insights on positioning products to enthusiast consumers and expanding brand reach into mainstream sports and lifestyle markets.
- RealTruck sells more than 1 million curated truck parts and accessories.
- Tony Ambroza serves as the chief growth officer of RealTruck.
- RealTruck.com is based in Ocala, Fla.
- RealTruck was founded in 1997 and is celebrating 25 years of serving truck owners across the United States.
- Truck Hero designs, develops, manufactures, and sells a full range of branded automotive accessories for trucks, Jeep brand vehicles, and cars.
- Truck Hero emphasizes market-leading functionality, engineering, quality, and design in its products.
