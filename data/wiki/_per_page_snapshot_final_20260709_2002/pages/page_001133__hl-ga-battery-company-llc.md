# HL-GA Battery Company LLC

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-12-04

**Source:** [Industry Expo – College of Engineering & Computing - Georgia Southern University](https://ww2.georgiasouthern.edu/cec/industry-expo/)  ·  `page_001133`

## Facts

- The 2025 HL-GA Battery Company Industry Expo was held December 4, 2025 in the Engineering Research Building at Georgia Southern University.
- HL-GA Battery Company LLC represents a collaboration between Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy Solution.
- HL-GA Battery Company manufactures state-of-the-art lithium-ion battery cells for electric vehicles.
- The facility is strategically located at the HMGMA Metaplant site near Savannah, Georgia.
- Upon completion, the factory will have a production capacity of 30GWh.
- This capacity is enough to power 300,000 electric and hybrid vehicles annually.
- HL-GA Battery Company LLC served as the Title Sponsor for the 2025 Industry Expo Project Report Out (PRO) Day.
- The sponsorship underwrote the use of RocketJudge as the judging platform, lunch, and the Awards Reception.
- Project winners were chosen in three categories: Computing; Electrical and Computer Engineering; Manufacturing Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Civil Engineering and Construction.
