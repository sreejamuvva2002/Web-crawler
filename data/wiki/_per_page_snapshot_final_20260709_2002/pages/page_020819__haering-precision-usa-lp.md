## Facts

- Haering Precision USA LP is the US location of Häring Group, which is headquartered in the south of Germany.
- The company was chosen as a strategic base for operations to spread global reach and establish a local presence in the region.
- Haering Precision manufactures precision components and subassemblies for the automotive industry.
- High innovative strength, exemplary quality and absolute reliability make Häring a highly sought-after partner from the development through the project phase.
- Office hours are 7:00am to 4:00pm, Monday-Friday.
- The facility is located off of I-85 Exit 177, at the end of Hamburg Avenue.
- The site is across from the Georgia State Patrol and next to Ritz Transformers.
- Visitors must check in with security outside of the gate.
- The company's operating philosophy is "Think It. Believe It. Achieve It."
- A stated goal of the facility is to provide jobs and career development opportunities to the region.
