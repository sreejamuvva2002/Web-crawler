# Trenton Pressing

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-06-28

## Facts

- Trenton Pressing is a Northwest Georgia supplier of parts for cars and golf carts.
- The company opened a new $12 million facility in Trenton, Ga., on Friday.
- The expansion added more than 100,000 square feet to the company's operations.
- Operations include making parts for BMW and other automakers.
- Operations also include making parts for golf cart producer Club Car.
- Company Chief Executive Mike Pfunder stated, "We wanted to show we're here to stay," during the announcement.
