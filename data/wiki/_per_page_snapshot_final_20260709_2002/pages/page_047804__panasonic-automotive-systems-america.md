## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems America is a premier supplier of automotive and mobility technologies for the world’s leading OEMs.
- The company strives to create a safe, comfortable, eco-friendly in-vehicle experience through high-quality solutions.
- It provides In-Vehicle Computing Technology, including Cockpit Domain Controllers (CDCs) and High-Performance Computing (HPCs), for intuitive and immersive digital experiences.
- It offers Premium Audio products built on decades of award-winning in-car audio expertise to deliver studio sound quality into vehicles.
- Its Sound & Voice Innovations technologies reduce unwanted noise, improve communication within the cabin, and enable hands-free control for drivers.
- Its Vision & Sensing Technology solutions allow drivers to see today’s roads more clearly, increasing safety and improving situational awareness.
- The company partners with leading automakers including Ford, GM, Stellantis, Toyota, Honda, Nissan, and many others.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems America operates as a unified entity across North America, seamlessly connecting its operations from coast-to-coast.
- Its corporate headquarters is located in Peachtree City, GA.
- It maintains a sales office in Farmington Hills, MI.
- It operates manufacturing facilities in Reynosa and Monterrey, MX.
