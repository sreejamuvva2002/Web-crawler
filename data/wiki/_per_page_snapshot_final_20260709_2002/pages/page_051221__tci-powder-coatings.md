# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [TCI Powder Coatings Opens Product Development Laboratory | 2017-06-04  | PCI Magazine](https://www.pcimag.com/articles/103561-tci-powder-coatings-opens-product-development-laboratory)  ·  `page_051221`

## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings, a subsidiary of RPM International Inc., announced the opening of its new Product Development Laboratory in Americus, Georgia.
- Planning for the laboratory began in the spring of 2016, with an official opening in January 2017.
- The lab's primary functions include developing innovative products, improving current technologies, and producing rapid color matches.
- Additional lab functions encompass analytical work, corrosion testing, environmental research and testing, manufacturing, and customer support.
- The facility features state-of-the-art color matching equipment and software combined with an upgraded database of improved pigments to enable unparalleled color matching capabilities and quick turnaround times.
- A styling room is available for critical color matching and styling work.
- The lab design includes spaces specifically created for customer collaboration and employee training.
- The laboratory is equipped with FTIR and DSC capabilities.
- TCI uses this infrastructure to support customers in key market segments with technical support, collaboration, and expertise while delivering high-quality coating solutions.
- Staff working within the lab include product formulators, chemists, segment technology leaders, color-matching specialists, and analytical chemists.
