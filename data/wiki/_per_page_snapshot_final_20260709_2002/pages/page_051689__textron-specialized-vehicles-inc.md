# Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-06

**Source:** [E-Z-GO® LAUNCHES NEW VEHICLES - FOR THE GOLF COURSE AND THE STREET](https://dynamicdrivesdesign.com/e-z-go-launches-new-vehicles-for-the-golf-course-and-the-street/)  ·  `page_051689`

## Facts

- E-Z-GO® introduces the Liberty LSV and updates the Freedom RXV for use on golf courses and neighborhoods.
- The new street-legal Liberty LSV has a top speed of 25 mph.
- The Liberty LSV meets National Highway Traffic Safety Administration standards for an LSV.
- The Liberty LSV can be operated on public roads with posted speed limits of 35 mph or less in most states.
- The Liberty LSV features four forward-facing seats and a compact wheelbase.
- The Liberty LSV is outfitted with a backup camera, operator protective structure, pedestrian warning system, seatbelts, and an optional infotainment system.
- The Liberty LSV adheres to FMVSS 500 and SAE J2358 standards.
- The 2024 Freedom RXV model features a new front end with integrated LED headlights.
- The 2024 Freedom RXV includes a modernized ergonomic dash with space for personal items.
- The Freedom RXV is available in two- and four-passenger models.
- The four-passenger Freedom RXV features a new modular canopy equipped with a water management system.
- Adam Harris, vice president of E-Z-GO for Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., stated that the new models address customer demand for vehicles suited for both golf courses and neighborhoods.
- The Liberty LSV is exclusively powered by Samsung SDI lithium batteries.
- Samsung SDI battery technology has been proven over many years in E-Z-GO’s industry-leading ELiTE series of lithium-powered vehicles.
- Samsung SDI battery technology is more energy-efficient and better for the environment than traditional lead-acid batteries.
- Samsung SDI batteries charge up to twice as fast as traditional batteries and require no maintenance.
- Samsung SDI batteries are backed by an 8-year warranty, which is the industry’s longest.
- The Freedom RXV is available with either Samsung SDI lithium battery technology (ELiTE model) or E-Z-GO’s exclusive EX1 gas powertrain.
- The EX1 engine is purpose-built for golf-car applications to optimize fuel efficiency.
- The EX1 engine features smart engine management that monitors the electrical system and turns off accessories when not in use.
- Both the Liberty LSV and Freedom RXV are manufactured in Augusta, Ga., where E-Z-GO has operated throughout its 69-year history.
- E-Z-GO was founded in Augusta, Ga. in 1954.
- E-Z-GO became part of Textron Inc. in 1960 and operates as part of Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a Textron Inc. subsidiary.
- New models will be available for order starting June 6, 2023, through local dealers.
