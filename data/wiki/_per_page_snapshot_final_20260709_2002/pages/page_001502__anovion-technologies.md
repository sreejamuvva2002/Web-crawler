# Anovion Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Anovion selects Georgia for $800 million graphite anode material production facility - Best Magazine](https://www.bestmag.co.uk/anovion-selects-georgia-for-800-million-graphite-anode-material-production-facility/)  ·  `page_001502`

## Facts

- US battery materials company Anovion Technologies selected Decatur County in Georgia for its first and initial large-scale expansion of manufacturing capacity.
- The total investment for the expansion is $800 million.
- The facility will produce premium synthetic graphite anode materials.
- The initial facility will be 1,500,000 square feet.
- The site is located near a skilled workforce and other battery and electric vehicle (EV) manufacturing plants.
- In October 2022, Anovion was awarded a grant of $117 million from the US Department of Energy under the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law.
- The Bipartisan Infrastructure Law aims to help secure the domestic battery manufacturing supply chain.
- The facility is the first of this scale for Anovion.
- Anovion views the project as a “crucial first step” to reshoring synthetic graphite anode production.
- Once fully operational, the company expects to initially produce 40,000 tonnes per annum.
- Chip Dunn serves as Executive Chairman of Anovion.
- Chip Dunn stated that Anovion's long-term strategic vision is to focus on growth and innovation, positioning the company as a leader in the US energy transition while securing a domestic source for critical materials required for US lithium-ion battery production.
- Anovion claims to be one of only a few graphite anode producers in the US to have successfully gained qualification for EV applications.
