# Textron Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-04-30

**Source:** [Textron (TXT) Q1 2026 Earnings Call Transcript - AOL](https://www.aol.com/articles/textron-txt-q1-2026-earnings-163413289.html)  ·  `page_051677`

## Facts

- Textron announced its intent to separate the Industrial segment within 12 to 18 months through a potential sale or tax-free spin-off into a stand-alone company.
- The Industrial segment is composed of Kautex and Textron Specialized Vehicles.
- Kautex operates as a Tier 1 auto supplier with a primary product line of fuel systems for the automotive industry.
- Kautex has built a meaningful position in hybrid fuel tanks which is a growing part of the industry.
- Pentatonic is a battery and closure business within the Industrial segment that supports EV and hybrid platforms.
- Pentatonic's platform support includes the Rivian R1 and a major European OEM start of production plan for 2027.
- Allegro is an Industrial segment growth platform focused on solutions to clean autonomous vehicle cameras and sensors.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles manufactures golf cars, off-road and turf vehicles, personal transportation vehicles, ransoms Jacobs and turf equipment, Cushman vehicles and tug ground support equipment.
- Kautex secured its largest award to date for its hybrid plastic fuel tank offering.
- Industrial segment revenues were $786 million, down $6 million on a reported basis but up 4% organically after accounting for the powersports divestiture.
- Kautex revenues were $486 million, up $36 million or 8% from a year ago, primarily due to a favorable impact of $20 million from foreign exchange rate fluctuations and higher volume and mix.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles revenue was $300 million, down $42 million, largely reflecting a $55 million impact from the divestiture of the powersports business in 2025.
- Management plans to reallocate a portion of R&D investment into supply chains and factories to make them more effective and improve production output.
- Textron expects enhanced supplier performance as part of operational efficiency improvements.
- The Industrial segment had positive organic growth in the quarter.
- Management stated that both Kautex and TSV are very well run and their end markets are in a stronger place.
