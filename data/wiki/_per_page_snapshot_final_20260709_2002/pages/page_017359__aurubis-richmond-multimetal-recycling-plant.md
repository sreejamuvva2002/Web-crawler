# Aurubis Richmond Multimetal Recycling Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Aurubis and SMS group forge ahead with the first multimetal recycling plant in the US - SMS group GmbH](https://www.sms-group.com/en-fi/press-and-media/press-releases/press-release-detail/aurubis-and-sms-group-forge-ahead-with-the-first-multimetal-recycling-plant-in-the-us)  ·  `page_017359`

## Facts

- Supply contract for a second plant module at the new Aurubis Richmond recycling plant in Georgia was signed as part of a strategic partnership.
- The plant utilizes a modular system that allows for flexible operations and quick growth in the dynamic US recycling market.
- Aurubis's investment aims to close essential raw material cycles.
- Multimetal producer and copper recycler Aurubis AG and Düsseldorf-based plant and machine manufacturer SMS group GmbH are expediting the development of the US recycling market for non-ferrous metals.
- The two companies signed a supply contract for a second plant module for the new Aurubis Richmond recycling plant in Georgia.
- Aurubis is considerably accelerating its growth course in the future recycling and circular economy market.
- Additional equipment will increase the capacity of the metal recycling plant in Richmond, currently under construction, from an annual 90,000 tons to 180,000 tons of complex metal scrap materials.
- Aurubis CEO Roland Harings stated that the next expansion stage of their new site in Georgia delivers on their “Driving Sustainable Growth” strategy.
- The project aims to keep strategically important and highly demanded mineral raw materials in the resource cycle.
- SMS group recycling equipment is designated for the second module of the new Aurubis plant in the US.
- The core technology is a top-blown rotary converter (TBRC), which processes complex recycling materials to recover raw materials like copper, nickel, tin, precious metals, and platinum group metals.
- Recovered metals serve as crucial raw materials for future technologies, including energy transition and sustainable mobility.
- Aurubis CEO Roland Harings noted that bundling SMS group's plant know-how with Aurubis' expertise creates an environment for highly efficient metal recovery.
- The partnership leverages the enormous potential of the US recycling market, with SMS group's backing ensuring professional and timely project realization.
- SMS Executive Board member Michael Rzepczyk stated that the plant helps Aurubis assume a leading role in US multimetal recycling.
- Planned production volume doubling at the new US site takes recycling to the next level in the US, according to Michael Rzepczyk.
- The partners aim to build a more sustainable future by closing value chains and maximizing valuable metal recovery from recycling materials.
- The recycling technology features modularity, allowing additional components to be added to expand the plant to meet future requirements.
- Aurubis CEO Roland Harings explained that the modular system enables a prudent market strategy to flexibly respond to US needs and benefit from constant market growth.
- For approximately €640 million across both phases, Aurubis is building the first secondary smelter specializing in multimetal recycling in the US.
- Groundbreaking for the Aurubis Richmond plant occurred in mid-2022, with construction running at full steam.
- Stage one of the plant is scheduled to go online in the first half of 2024.
- The TBRC equipment is designated for the second stage, which is anticipated to start production in 2026.
- The Georgia investment supports Aurubis' sustainability strategy, complementing its existing annual processing of roughly 1 million tons of recycling material.
- The Aurubis Richmond recycling plant will add an additional 180,000 tons of processing capacity annually.
- The Georgia investment will help meet group growth targets, comply with the highest US environmental standards, and create around 200 new jobs.
- Aurubis CEO Roland Harings emphasized that economic growth and comprehensive corporate responsibility go hand in hand.
- Aurubis AG is a leading global provider of non-ferrous metals and one of the largest copper recyclers worldwide.
- The company processes complex metal concentrates, scrap metals, organic/inorganic metal-bearing recycling materials, and industrial residues into high-quality metals.
- Aurubis produces over 1 million tons of copper cathodes annually, yielding products like wire rod, continuous cast shapes, profiles, and flat rolled copper/copper alloy products.
- Aurubis also produces precious metals, selenium, lead, nickel, tin, and zinc.
- The product portfolio includes additional items such as sulfuric acid and iron silicate.
- Sustainability is fundamental to Aurubis' strategy, guided by the maxim "Aurubis responsibly transforms raw materials into value," integrating sustainable conduct into its corporate culture.
- The company's sustainability approach involves careful resource management, responsible social/ecological conduct, and sensible growth.
- Aurubis has around 6,900 employees, production sites in Europe and the USA, and a global distribution network.
- Aurubis shares are listed on the German Stock Exchange's Prime Standard Segment, MDAX, Global Challenges Index (GCX), and STOXX Europe 600.
- SMS group is renowned worldwide for future-oriented technologies and outstanding service for the metals industry.
- SMS group leverages 150 years of experience and digital know-how to provide innovative products/processes, generating worldwide sales of around €2.6 billion.
- SMS group supports customers throughout plant lifecycles to enable profitable and resource-efficient value creation chains.
- Paving the way for a carbon-neutral and sustainable metals industry is SMS group's stated goal.
- SMS group employs 14,500 people globally.
