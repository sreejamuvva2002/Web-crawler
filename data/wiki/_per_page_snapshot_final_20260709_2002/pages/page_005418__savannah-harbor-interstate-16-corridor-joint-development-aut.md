- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) officially began production the week of October 1, 2024, ahead of its projected schedule and less than two years after breaking ground.
- The first electric vehicle to roll off HMGMA's assembly line was an IONIQ 5.
- HMGMA will produce several models of Hyundai, Genesis, and Kia vehicles at its Bryan County facility.
- On August 13, 2024, contributions to the Well Mitigation Fund reached $1 million, with each of the Development Authority of Bulloch County, Development Authority of Bryan County, Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority, and Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America contributing $250,000.

- On November 14, 2023, the Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority released a Regional Workforce Study conducted by Wadley Donovan Gutshaw Consulting, LLC to identify labor supply challenges for Savannah region industrial employers and develop initiatives to minimize future supply shortfalls.
- The November 2023 workforce study scope included a market analysis, identification of needs and challenges, and a strategic workforce plan.
- On October 23, 2023, Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that automotive supplier Daechang Seat Savannah Corp. would invest $72.5 million in the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center to create over 500 jobs.
- Daechang Seat Savannah Corp.'s new facility will be located in close proximity to Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America and will specialize in automotive seat frames.
- On September 6, 2023, Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that DAS Corp. would establish a new manufacturing facility in Metter, creating 300 new jobs and generating more than $35 million in investment in Candler County, involving collaborative indirect investments.
