## Facts

- Lehigh Technologies is a leading specialty chemicals company that produces high-performance micronized rubber powders (MRP).
- MRP is a sustainable raw material that reduces costs by up to 50%.
- Lehigh's cryogenic turbo mill technology converts end-of-life rubber into MRP used in a wide range of markets.
- Lehigh operates a state-of-the-art applications and development center where technical experts collaborate with customers to optimize products for each application.
- Lehigh's Application & Development Center has comprehensive compounding, formulation and testing capabilities to help customers optimize products for individual needs.
- Lehigh was recognized as a World Economic Forum Technology Pioneer in 2010.
- Lehigh was recognized as a Bloomberg New Energy Pioneer in 2015.
- Lehigh received the Sustainable Technology of the Year award by Tire Tech Expo in 2014.
- Lehigh is headquartered in Tucker, Georgia.
- Lehigh has a capacity of over 140MM pounds/year.
- Lehigh's capacity is the largest known capacity in the industry.
- Lehigh Technologies is privately held.
