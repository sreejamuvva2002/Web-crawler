# Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-04-07

**Source:** [Hyundai to expand operations, add jobs near Columbus, GA | Columbus Ledger-Enquirer](https://www.ledger-enquirer.com/news/business/article250492989.html)  ·  `page_021402`

## Facts

- A Hyundai affiliate is investing $9 million and bringing over 100 jobs to the West Point area.
- Hyundai TRANSYS will create over 150 jobs in Troup County with the expansion of its seating manufacturing operations in West Point, Georgia.
- The job creation details were announced in a news release from Gov. Brian Kemp’s office.
- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System President and CEO Sangkil Jung stated the company is excited for the new opportunity and proud of its partnership with the State of Georgia.
- The expansion will increase Hyundai TRANSYS’ total employment in Georgia to over 1,890 jobs.
- In Dec. 2020, the company announced plans for a new factory in West Point.
- The new factory is located near an existing Hyundai TRANSYS factory and a Kia Motors Corp. factory.
- Both facilities are part of Hyundai Motor Group, the Korean auto conglomerate that sells cars under the Kia and Hyundai brands.
- That previously announced West Point facility created 678 full-time, long-term positions.
- Gov. Brian Kemp stated that Georgia’s highly skilled workforce continues to attract manufacturing jobs in every corner of the state and highlighted Hyundai TRANSYS’ continued investment in West Point as proof of west Georgia's capability.
- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System manufactures seats and seat foam pads for the Hyundai Santa Fe, KIA Sorento, and other models.
- The company provides seating structures for automobile manufacturers.
- The company also supplies seats to leading electric vehicle makers.
