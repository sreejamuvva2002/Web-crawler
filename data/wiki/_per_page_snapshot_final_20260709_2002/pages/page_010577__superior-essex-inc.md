# Superior Essex Inc.

## Facts

- Superior Essex Inc. is aligning its sustainability efforts with those of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDG).
- This alignment allows Superior Essex, parent company of the brands Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire and Superior Essex Communications, to identify common goals and action opportunities with other entities.
- Superior Essex has committed to aligning with 12 of the 17 specific UN Sustainable Development Goals.
- The alignment will support green production, sustainable product goals, and specific Vision 2030 objectives of Essex Furukawa.
- Annie Bevan serves as Global Head of Sustainability for Superior Essex Inc.
- Annie Bevan stated that Superior Essex and its divisions are committed to being proactive and responsible toward the planet.
- Annie Bevan noted that while the company is not near its goal yet, its commitment demonstrates a focus on reducing environmental impact.
- Annie Bevan highlighted that in a market not known for sustainability or eco-friendly production methods, Superior Essex is aligning with UN SDGs as well as the environmental and social consciousness of its customers and the planet.
- The UN SDG program was launched in 2015 as part of the Agenda 2030 for sustainable development.
- Superior Essex is headquartered at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
- The company has over 3,000 employees in 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is the leading global supplier of magnet wire.
- Superior Essex is at the forefront of applying communication fiber and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company's products are critical for creating disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- A facility for Essex Solutions in Suzhou, China, opened in early 2025.
