# Evco Plastics

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-04-08

**Source:** [Evco Plastics’ Footprint Surpasses One Million Square Foot Mark](https://www.plasticstoday.com/injection-molding/evco-plastics-footprint-surpasses-one-million-square-foot-mark)  ·  `page_054385`

## Facts

- Evco Plastics recently purchased a new 40,000-sq-ft facility in Calhoun, GA.
- The new facility is located near the company’s CAL 1 plant and is designated as CAL 2.
- CAL 2 adds 40,000 square feet to Evco’s footprint, bringing its total to more than one million square feet across 10 plants globally.
- The new facility includes a 22,000-sq-ft white room.
- The new facility includes a 2,100-sq-ft liquid silicone rubber (LSR) production area.
- The facility is designed to meet demand from medical and commercial lighting customers.
- Kate Bashir, Director of Strategy, stated the expansion was timed after assessing customer needs and industry developments.
- CAL 2 will help meet customers’ high expectations and long-term production goals.
- The new facility has room for 20 injection molding machines up to 500 tons.
- There will be a separate room for new LSR machines.
- The facility is equipped with two bridge cranes.
- The facility is equipped with four material silos.
- The facility includes a 725-sq-ft metrology lab.
- CAL 2 will be a food-grade compliant facility.
- The facility will feature highly automated lights-out production.
- The inclusion of a white room allows for additional medical molding opportunities, representing a new capability for Evco.
- The facility is scheduled to be fully operational in the fall of 2021.
- Evco also is currently adding 13,000 square feet to its AMP facility in DeForest, WI to make room for more presses and expand on-site storage of raw materials and finished goods.
- Evco was founded in 1964.
- Evco has more than 1,700 employees.
- Evco operates 235 injection molding machines ranging from 28 to 3,500 tons.
- Gary Fortier, Director of US Operations, stated that reaching over one million square feet is a huge milestone for the company.
- Gary Fortier stated the new space allows Evco to create more opportunities to meet ongoing customer and industry requirements.
