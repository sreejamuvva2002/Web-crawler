# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Bryan County Megasite, Georgia, Bryan County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

The first dedicated mass-production electric vehicle (EV) and hybrid plant in Georgia, capable of producing up to 500,000 Hyundai, Kia, and Genesis EV/hybrid vehicles annually.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Dedicated mass-production EV and hybrid vehicle manufacturing plant supporting the production of Hyundai, Kia, and Genesis electric and hybrid models.
