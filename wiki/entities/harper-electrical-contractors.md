# Harper Electrical Contractors

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Newnan, GA, Coweta County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Harper Electrical Contractors is a licensed contractor that installs residential and commercial electric vehicle charging stations for all major EV brands throughout Newnan and the surrounding South Atlanta area, including Coweta and Fayette Counties.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Provides licensed installation of electric vehicle charging stations across Georgia, supporting EV adoption and infrastructure growth.

## Key facts

- Installs EV charging stations for all major makes and models, including Tesla, Ford, Chevrolet, and Nissan.
- Offers both residential and commercial installations, handling everything from simple outlet upgrades to full charging station systems.
- Licensed and insured electricians ensure compliance with local electrical codes, permits, and inspections.
- Provides panel upgrades, permitting assistance, and 24/7 emergency service as part of the installation process.
- Emphasizes safety, convenience, cost savings, and future-proofing for property owners adopting electric vehicles.
- Serves Newnan and the Greater South Atlanta Area, specifically Coweta and Fayette Counties in Georgia.
