# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2015-06

**Source:** [Yamaha’s Georgia Manufacturing Facility Featured in “Small Town, Big Deal” Television Program Leading RFD-TV network program showcases Yamaha and its Newnan, GA, Facility | Dirt Toys Magazine](https://www.dirttoysmag.com/2015/06/yamahas-georgia-manufacturing-facility-featured)  ·  `page_015629`

## Facts

- The Yamaha manufacturing facility in Newnan, GA, is featured in a new episode of “Small Town, Big Deal,” one of the most popular and widely viewed shows airing on the RFD-TV network.
- The episode featuring Newnan and Yamaha first aired on Thursday, June 11, at 8:30 p.m. Central Standard Time.
- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America (YMMC) serves as Yamaha’s worldwide hub of off-highway vehicle (OHV) manufacturing.
- All of the company's SxS and the majority of their ATV vehicles are produced at the Newnan facility for national and international distribution.
- The facility in Newnan covers 280 acres with more than 1.3 million square feet of manufacturing facilities.
- The facility has approximately 1,473 employees.
- Yamaha has invested more than $250 million into U.S. manufacturing over the past 15 years, creating a world-class facility in Newnan.
- Mike Chrzanowski is the executive vice president of YMMC.
- The facility produces ATV or side-by-side vehicles, golf cars, and personal watercraft.
- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation strives to maintain the highest production quality and environmental standards, while giving back to the community through comprehensive corporate social responsibility programs.
- Yamaha is partnering with Small Town, Big Deal for an online sweepstakes in which eligible participants can enter for a chance to win a brand new 2015 Viking EPS Side-by-Side.
- The approximate retail value of the 2015 Viking EPS Side-by-Side is $12,999.
- Steve Nessl is the ATV/SxS marketing manager for Yamaha Motor Corporation.
- The sweepstakes is open through Aug. 27 and no purchase is necessary to enter, with the winner determined by random drawing.
