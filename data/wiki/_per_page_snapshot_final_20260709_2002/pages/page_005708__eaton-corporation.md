## Facts

- Eaton is a power management company with 2016 sales of $19.7 billion.
- The company provides energy-efficient solutions that help customers effectively manage electrical, hydraulic and mechanical power more efficiently, safely and sustainably.
- Eaton is dedicated to improving the quality of life and the environment through the use of power management technologies and services.
- Eaton has approximately 95,000 employees.
- The company sells products to customers in more than 175 countries.
- Eaton Corporation achieved their initial energy intensity reduction goal in 2017.
- Eaton's manufacturing classification is NAICS 335, Electrical Equipment, Appliance, and Component Manufacturing.
- Eaton made its pledge to the Better Plants Challenge in January 2010.
- Eaton presented a solution on the implementation of ISO 50001 to support GHG reduction.
