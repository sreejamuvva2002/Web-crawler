## Facts

- Pirelli is getting closer to making a massive multi-year investment of between $1 billion and $1.2 billion in the United States.
- The proposal was presented to the company’s board of directors during a recent meeting.
- The investment will be formally put on the agenda for approval at an upcoming board meeting.
- A major highlight of the upcoming investment is the U.S. introduction of Pirelli’s proprietary Cyber Tyre technology.
- The Rome facility, home of Pirelli Tire North America and its primary US manufacturing facility and R&D center, will be expanded to support the project.
- The company announced the facility expansion in May.
- The project will be actively developed over the coming years.
- The investment is set to boost production capacity and bring advanced tire technology to the American market.
