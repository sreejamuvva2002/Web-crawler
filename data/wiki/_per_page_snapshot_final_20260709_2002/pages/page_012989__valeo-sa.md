# Valeo SA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2012-08-01

**Source:** [Valeo makes your car engine hybrid ...](https://www.eenewseurope.com/en/valeo-makes-your-car-engine-hybrid/)  ·  `page_012989`

## Facts

- Henri Trintignac, Valeo’s director of Electric Vehicle Activity, claimed that Valeo SA has developed an electrification solution for the powertrain called Hybrid4All.
- Hybrid4All enables car manufacturers to turn a traditional diesel or gasoline engine into a hybrid engine at an affordable price by using simple and standardized components.
- The Hybrid4All architecture is based on a compact motor generator that uses a low voltage electrical system (48V).
- Using a low voltage system reduces costs, making the solution more acceptable for the mass market.
- In the Hybrid4All architecture, the electric motor assisting the ICE can be installed in different positions: in front of the ICE (on the accessory drive belt), after the gear-box, or between the two.
- The solution integrates Valeo’s enhanced Stop-Start, regenerative braking, and torque assist functions.
- The system enables fuel savings of more than 15 percent on an average gasoline engine vehicle.
- The original Hybrid4All electro-technical architecture provides a high level of power (up to 15kW) and takes up no more room than a conventional alternator.
- Hybrid4All recovers energy during deceleration (regenerative braking) and re-uses it during acceleration to assist the combustion engine.
- The system reduces CO2 emissions by 15 percent, according to Henri Trintignac.
- A car driver currently spending 100 euros ($120) per month on fuel will see their fuel budget drop to 85 euros ($102).
- Valeo claimed a 15.5-percent CO2 reduction with a prototype car, the Peugeot 207 1.6 L THP.
- The system is designed for small cars such as the Renault Twingo, Clio, and Opel Corsa.
- These car models could previously not be equipped with hybrid engines due to cost.
- Cars equipped with the electrical system are expected to be available in 2017.
