## Facts

- Vista Metals is increasing its capacity by 60 percent.
- The company described establishing a facility in Rome, Georgia.
- The project is valued at up to $15 million.
- Vista Metals products include automotive grade steel, galvanized materials, and hot and cold automotive grades.
- Its core customer in Rome operates in the auto industry.
- The company came to Bartow County with help from a downtown economic development group.
- New economic development events were added to the plant level in 2013.
- The automotive industry accounts for about 60 percent of Rome's business.
- Demand for automotive materials has increased due to growth in Paulding and Cobb counties' auto parts production.
- Vista Metals announced a $10 million expansion with room for future growth.
- City officials emphasized the positive economic impact of the expansion on Rome.
- Vista Metals is noted as a good corporate partner for the city and community.
