## Facts

- RealTruck is the premier manufacturer and digital destination of accessories for truck, Jeep®, Bronco® and off-road enthusiasts around the world.
- The company is globally headquartered in Ann Arbor, Michigan.
- RealTruck employs over 6,000 associates operating from more than 35 facilities across four continents.
- The company's product portfolio includes the Husky Liners total vehicle protection brand and boasts over 1,000 patents and pending applications.
- RealTruck utilizes an omni-channel retail approach delivering a seamless consumer experience online, as well as through its 12,000+ dealer network and automotive (OEM) partnerships.
- RealTruck operates manufacturing and distribution facilities in Lawrenceville, GA.
- RealTruck operates manufacturing and distribution facilities in Pendergrass, GA.
- Carl-Martin E. Lindahl serves as CEO and Board Director of RealTruck since November 2022, following leadership roles at SVP Worldwide, KitchenAid, and the Whirlpool Corporation.
- Carl-Martin E. Lindahl is a former McKinsey & Company partner, serves on the board of the Fiskars Group, is originally from Sweden, holds an M.Sc. from Chalmers University of Technology, and attended UC Berkeley’s Haas School of Business.
- Baljinder Singh serves as Chief Financial Officer overseeing finance, procurement, and strategic growth initiatives, having joined in August 2023 after serving as CFO of Wahl Clipper and holding senior roles at SVP Worldwide, Serta Simmons Bedding, Grainger, and Motorola.
- Baljinder Singh holds an MBA from Roosevelt University and a BS in Accountancy from Northern Illinois University.
- Tammy Bohen serves as Chief Human Resources Officer leading HR, Environmental, and Health & Safety functions since March 2023, bringing over two decades of HR leadership experience from companies like SVP Worldwide, Whirlpool, and Johnson & Johnson.
- Tammy Bohen holds a Master’s degree in Human Resources and a Bachelor's in Recreation Management.
- Eric Dale serves as Chief Legal Officer and EVP of Government Affairs and Corporate Transactions, joining in April 2023 after senior roles at Balance Point Capital and Nielsen, and serves as Vice Chairman of Bankwell Financial Group.

- Eric Firer holds a degree from Binghamton University School of Management.
- Mark Hickey serves as Chief Engineering Officer, joining in April 2014 after holding executive roles at Truck Hero, Webasto Thermo & Comfort North America, and Magna International, specializing in engineering and product development.
