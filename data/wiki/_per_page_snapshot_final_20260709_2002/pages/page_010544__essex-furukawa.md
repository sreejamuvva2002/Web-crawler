## Facts

- Essex Furukawa High Voltage Winding Wire™ (HVWW™) helps EV powertrain designers achieve higher output and efficiency while reducing assembly steps for high-performance electric vehicles.
- The Essex Furukawa joint venture was formed specifically to meet the growing need for innovative HVWW in Europe.
- New developments in HVWW will be presented at CWIEME in Berlin from June 20-22, 2017.
- Toschinobu Harada, CTO of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire Europe, will present "Innovation in Magnet Wire" for the High Voltage Powertrain Seminar at 10:40 AM at the CWIEME Central Seminar Theater on June 22, 2017.
- Essex will showcase its full portfolio of winding wire solutions for automotive, energy, and industrial applications at Hall 4.2 Booth A29 during CWIEME.
- In addition to HVWW, Essex is presenting new developments in round and small wire for the automotive industry.
- As the world's largest magnet wire manufacturer, Essex provides innovative wire solutions across Europe, North America, China, and Southeast Asia.
- Essex Magnet Wire is a leading global magnet wire provider used by most major OEMs and industry leaders in the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Essex Magnet Wire operates facilities in 11 countries across three continents.
- With 90 years of experience, Essex Magnet Wire vertically integrates customer-requested innovations into manufacturing processes to ensure quality and seamless delivery from global distribution centers.
- The Essex MagForceX® Innovation Center is advancing magnet wire technology through R&D to provide custom solutions for future customer challenges.
- Superior Essex corporate headquarters is located at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
