# Tegeta Bandag Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [The new Bandag plant in Georgia will produce 5000 eco-friendly tires per year](https://mosautoshina.com/news/novyj-zavod-bandag-v-gruzii-budet-proizvodit-5000-ekologichnyh-shin-v-god/)  ·  `page_018307`

## Facts

- Bandag is the global leader in tire retreading solutions.
- Bandag has partnered with Tegeta Motors and Bridgestone MEA to establish the first Bandag retreading plant in the Caucasus region.
- The facility is named the Tegeta Bandag plant.
- The Tegeta Bandag plant is located in Rustavi, Georgia.
- The project represents a significant step towards expanding sustainable mobility practices across the region.
- Initial investments for the facility are $2 million.
- The facility is expected to produce up to 5,000 retreaded tires annually.
- There are plans to double this capacity within five years.
- Construction is set to begin this year (December 2025).
- Completion is scheduled for the end of 2026.
