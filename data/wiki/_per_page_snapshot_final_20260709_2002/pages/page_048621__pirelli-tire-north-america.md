# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America has announced that Claudio Zanardo will replace outgoing CEO Pietro Berardi as CEO, effective November 1.
- Outgoing CEO Pietro Berardi is leaving the company to pursue a new professional challenge.
- New CEO Claudio Zanardo joins the North American team from Pirelli’s Milan headquarters, where he most recently served as senior vice president, Moto and Velo.
- Zanardo has extensive experience in the automotive industry, including working for Ducati Motor Holding.
- As CEO of the North American region, Zanardo will be responsible for the car, motorcycle, and cycling businesses.
- Zanardo will oversee manufacturing facilities in Rome, Georgia, and Silao, Guanajuato, Mexico.
- In 2015, Zanardo joined Pirelli overseeing marketing and controlled retail for Region Europe.
- He assumed responsibility for Driver Italia (Pirelli’s franchise network) in 2016.
- He was appointed country CEO of Italy before moving to the role of senior vice president, Moto, in January 2020.
- In February 2021, he was given responsibility for Business Velo.
