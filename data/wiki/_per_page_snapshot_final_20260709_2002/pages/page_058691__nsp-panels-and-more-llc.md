# NSP Panels and More LLC

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2024-03-21

**Source:** [Pak-Lite launches NSP Panels and More venture - Floor Covering News](https://www.fcnews.net/2024/03/pak-lite-launches-nsp-panels-and-more-venture/)  ·  `page_058691`

## Facts

- Pak-Lite, a supplier of underlayment, announced the creation of a new joint venture called NSP Panels and More.
- The initiative is a partnership between David Pritchard, owner and president of Pak-Lite Inc. based in Commerce, Ga., Marco Seitner, owner and director of the Board of Selit North America Inc., and Franz Neuhofer, owner and managing director of FN Neuhofer.
- NSP Panels and More stands for premium acoustic panels and more.
- The company name is a combination of the names Neuhofer, Selit North America Inc., and Pak-Lite Inc.
- The joint venture was founded with the aim of establishing functional design in the form of acoustic panels, wall elements, and accessories for floors, walls, and ceilings on the American market.
- David Pritchard expressed excitement over the formation of NSP Panels and More LLC, citing a strong partnership combining manufacturing, marketing, and long-standing industry relationships.
- Pritchard stated certainty that the new innovative family of products will be a tremendous success and become another American-made success story.
- NSP acoustic panels combine a sound absorbing base panel made of 60% recycled PET fibers with real oak veneered MDF slats.
- The base panel is said to effectively reduce sound by up to 45% and minimizes noise reverberation.
- With a substructure of 2 inches, the sound insulation can increase to 70%, meeting highest quality standards and ensuring durability.
- The panels are designed to complement various residential spaces including bedrooms, hallways, waiting rooms, kitchens, study rooms, offices, children’s rooms, living rooms, and playrooms.
