# Ascend Elements

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2022-01-20

**Source:** [Battery Resourcers rebranding to Ascend Elements](https://www.wbjournal.com/article/battery-resourcers-rebranding-to-ascend-elements)  ·  `page_001491`

## Facts

- Battery Resourcers is changing its name to Ascend Elements.
- The company is based in Westborough.
- The company operates as a lithium ion battery recycling and manufacturing company.
- The company's technology reclaims critical battery elements from scrap batteries and elevates them to customized, battery-active materials with higher value and performance.
- The name change is part of a long-term strategy that includes total rebranding and a website redesign.
- The company is seeing increased demand for its products and cathode active materials (CAM) manufacturing process as electric vehicles increase in popularity.
- The company previously announced plans to construct a $43-million plant in Georgia.
- The planned Georgia facility will be North America’s largest lithium-ion battery recycling base.
- Roger Lin serves as Ascend Elements’ vice president of global marketing and government relations.
- Roger Lin stated that the name Ascend Elements and newly launched branding capture the company's mission to elevate the value of elements in the lithium-ion battery supply chain.
