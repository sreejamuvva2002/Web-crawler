## Facts

- PAK-Lite, Inc. is based in Suwanee, Georgia.
- The company provides foam fabricating solutions for various industries.
- PAK-Lite specializes in foam tape fabrication, kiss cutting gaskets, fabricating foil materials, laminating foam materials, and reinforced mesh.
- Their foam materials possess properties such as lightweight construction, excellent shock absorption, thermal insulation, and moisture resistance.
- The company utilizes prototyping, rotary die cutting, spooling, skiving, and material selection techniques to transform foam materials into various shapes, sizes, and forms.
- PAK-Lite offers comprehensive services supporting clients from concept development and design through prototyping and production.
- The company's foam fabricating solutions cater to industries including electronics, automotive, medical, aerospace, and packaging.
- PAK-Lite focuses on delivering customized foam products tailored to client requirements with an emphasis on innovation, quality, and sustainability.
