# Inalfa Roof Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Gov. Nathan Deal: Inalfa Roof Systems Creates 100 More Jobs in Cherokee County | GaPundit](https://gapundit.com/gov-nathan-deal-inalfa-roof-systems-creates-100-jobs-cherokee-county/)  ·  `page_045129`

## Facts

- Gov. Nathan Deal announced that Inalfa Roof Systems will expand its recently completed 165,000-square-foot regional headquarters in southwest Cherokee County by an additional 45,000 square feet.
- The company will invest an additional $6 million for the expansion.
- The expansion will create 100 new jobs in addition to the 300 jobs already announced in June 2013.
- Governor Nathan Deal stated that Cherokee County is an ideal location for Inalfa Roof Systems and praised the company as a true team player for regional economic development.
- The southwest Cherokee facility is strategically located off I-75 at Cherokee 75 Corporate Park, near automotive manufacturers throughout the Southeast.
- A new business unit will be added to vertically integrate a bi-processed injection molding operation to place black lining around Inalfa sunroofs.
- Inalfa Roof Systems recently received the State of Georgia Deal of the Year award for its partnership with the Cherokee County community.
- Mary Ellen McClanahan, metro Atlanta project manager at the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD), assisted with the expansion alongside the Cherokee Office of Economic Development (COED).
- Cherokee County Commission Chairman Buzz Ahrens stated that the community provides a supportive business environment for Inalfa's success.
- COED Chairman Marshall Day noted Inalfa is a great example of a company making the most of Cherokee County's offerings and emphasized advocacy for existing industry.
- GDEcD Commissioner Chris Carr said the expansion demonstrates Georgia's high-quality workforce and pro-business resources, noting companies who choose Georgia will grow within the state.
- The project is expected to be complete by September 2014.
- Inalfa Roof Systems is one of the world’s biggest providers of vehicle roof systems, designing, developing, and manufacturing sunroofs and open-roof systems for the automotive industry.
- Inalfa's automotive customers include BMW Group, Daimler, Chrysler Group, Ford, General Motors, Volkswagen, Audi, Volvo, Citroen, Chery, Geely, Hyundai Kia, Honda, Land Rover, Renault, and Nissan.
- Inalfa Roof Systems has its global headquarters in Venray, the Netherlands, with facilities in Poland, Slovakia, USA, Brazil, Mexico, South Korea, China, and Japan.
