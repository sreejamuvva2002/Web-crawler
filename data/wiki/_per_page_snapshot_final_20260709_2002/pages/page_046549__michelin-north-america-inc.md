# Michelin North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-17

**Source:** [Michelin Announces Rollout Of TreadEye Technology Across US Retread Network | Retreading Business](https://www.retreadingbusiness.com/michelin-announces-rollout-of-treadeye-technology-across-us-retread-network/)  ·  `page_046549`

## Facts

- Michelin is rolling out its industry-leading TreadEye tread depth assessment technology across its entire US retread network.
- TreadEye accurately and consistently collects 1,200 measurement points to provide precise insights into tread wear and casing condition.
- The system helps fleets maximize their tire investment by ensuring every 32nd of an inch counts.
- Key benefits of TreadEye include identifying pull points by fleet location, maximizing rubber on the road by avoiding early pulls, preventing casing damage, reducing DOT violations and downtime from late pulls, and improving efficiency in the tire removal process.
- Nate Kirian, Vice President of Sales and Distribution for Commercial Business Lines at Michelin North America, Inc., stated that TreadEye is a game-changer for retread operations and fleets served, highlighting it as a powerful example of Michelin's customer-benefiting innovation.
