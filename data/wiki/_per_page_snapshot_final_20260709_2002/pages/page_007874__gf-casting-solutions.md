# GF Casting Solutions

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-08

**Source:** [Augusta Lands $184 Million Manufacturing Deal Bringing 350 New E-Mobility Jobs to Richmond County - Tennessee Star](https://tennesseestar.com/news/augusta-lands-184-million-manufacturing-deal-bringing-350-new-e-mobility-jobs-to-richmond-county/dmcclure/2024/05/08/)  ·  `page_007874`

## Facts

- GF Casting Solutions will invest over $184 million in a new manufacturing facility in Augusta Corporate Park.
- The new facility is slated to start operations in 2027.
- The project will bring 350 additional jobs to Richmond County.
- GF Casting Solutions is a division of Georg Fischer AG, headquartered in Schaffhausen, Switzerland.
- The Swiss company produces aluminum, magnesium, iron, and super alloy parts for light vehicles, trucks, aerospace, energy, off-highway vehicles, and industrial applications.
- The Augusta facility will be a LEED-certified, state-of-the-art High Pressure Die Casting Facility developing cast aluminum parts primarily for the automotive industry.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia is proud to carry the title of the No. 1 state for business to companies across the globe.
- Governor Brian Kemp welcomed GF Casting Solutions to the Peach State after meeting its team during a recent mission to Switzerland.
- Carlos Vasto, President of GF Casting Solutions, called the project a very long-term investment that will complete the company's footprint to become truly global in the e-mobility market.
- Carlos Vasto stated the company is looking forward to bringing leading technology to the US and creating advanced manufacturing jobs for several generations in Georgia.
- Augusta Mayor Garnett Johnson noted that the city and Development Authority invested in water, sewer, roads, and workforce growth to establish the site as a premier industrial park in the Southeast United States.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that GF Casting Solutions was facilitated through Georgia's Europe Office connections with European companies.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson explained that European companies bring centuries of business experience combined with an intentional focus on innovation driving the market forward.
- GF Casting Solutions will hire for manufacturing and automation positions at the Augusta facility over the next few years.
- Since 2018, the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) has championed more than $27.3 billion of investments in the e-mobility industry.
- The state aims to attract more investment to become the nation’s “capital of EV manufacturing.”
- Companies with an existing e-mobility presence in Georgia include Kia, Hyundai Motor Group, Rivian, Caterpillar, SK Battery America, Yamaha Motor Manufacturing, Dongwon Tech, Duckyang America, Aspen Aerogels, Club Car, GEDIA, JCB, Heliox, Blue Bird Corporation, TEKLAS, and Textron.
