# Ecoplastic America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-11

**Source:** [Humility, hard work, and quality drive Ecoplastic CEO Thomas Kim as he makes Statesboro his home and casts a vision for the future  - Grice Connect](https://www.griceconnect.com/spotlight/humility-hard-work-and-quality-drive-ecoplastic-ceo-thomas-kim-as-he-makes-statesboro-his-home-and-casts-a-vision-for-the-future-8470023)  ·  `page_019892`

## Facts

- Ecoplastic America CEO Youngdoo "Thomas" Kim and his team are making downtown Statesboro their work home while awaiting the construction of their plant on 301 South.
- Kim began his role in Statesboro in January 2024 after spending the last 20 years gaining expertise with global SECO affiliates, which is Ecoplastic America's parent corporation.
- The Ecoplastic plant in Statesboro is the company’s first American subsidiary and represents an enormous investment by SECO to supply Hyundai and Kia electric vehicles with components, including for Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA).
- Kim envisions the plant becoming a building block for Bulloch County and a source of community pride where future generations will eagerly participate.
- Ecoplastic designs and supplies the face of cars (bumpers), a process that takes between one year and six months to two years to design.
- The company aims to become a key supplier while fostering job creation for the community and building a positive corporate culture.
- Kim prioritizes treating employees as family, valuing a workplace culture of unity, resilience, and familial bonds where colleagues are respected.
- Factory construction is scheduled to be complete in July.
- Equipment installation will follow factory construction.
- Until September, the company will focus on fostering quality through testing to produce defect-free products.
- Starting October 1st, 2024, Ecoplastic aims to mass-produce bumpers for the IONIC 5 model.
- In 2025, the company plans to produce bumpers for the IONIC 7 model.
- By 2026, Ecoplastic aims to produce bumpers for TV and TE (electric pickup truck) models.
- By 2027, Kim envisions a stable Ecoplastic operation in the region.
- SECO is one of the top 5 suppliers for Hyundai/Kia in Korea.
- Kim looks forward to welcoming Ecoplastic career applicants in the near future as the plant prepares for operations.
