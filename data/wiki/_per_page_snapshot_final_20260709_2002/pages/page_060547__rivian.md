## Facts

- Rivian is slated to build a factory in Morgan and Walton counties in Georgia.
- The factory is expected to produce 400,000 vehicles per year.
- It will be located 45 miles east of Atlanta.
- There is no definite timeline for the plant’s production.
- Rivian hopes to employ about 2,000 people at its new factory.
- The new facility will be nearly 20 million square feet and cover nearly two thousand acres.
- The Georgia site will be five times the size of Rivian’s current facility in Normal, Illinois.
- The company’s new Georgia factory is set to open in 2024.
- It is anticipated that the Georgia factory will produce 400,000 cars a year.
- The new factory is expected to have a significant impact on the environment.
- The company will be required to acquire environmental permits for the new factory.
- The permitting process will involve numerous committees and public comment sessions.
- Georgia has a large, educated workforce that is interested in technology.
- Rivian’s executives cite Georgia’s friendly attitude toward technology companies as a key factor in selecting this location.
- The state is also ideally located for distribution and logistics.
- The new site will allow Rivian to scale quickly while maintaining the highest quality standards in its products and facilities.
- Rivian’s opponents cite various concerns about the plant, including the potential for groundwater contamination.
- Opponents question the state’s incentive program for the project.
- Opponents fear that the enormous factory will ruin the bucolic character of the area.
- Former U.S. senator David Perdue has criticized Governor Kemp for accepting the project in Georgia.
- Rivian is already in a partnership with Amazon, which aims to buy 100,000 Rivian delivery vans by 2030.
- The company has received more than seven hundred orders for pickups and SUVs in the U.S. and Canada as of Dec. 15, 2018.
- Amazon is a major shareholder in Rivian, having invested $700 million in the company this year.
- Amazon led a series of funding rounds totaling $1.8 billion.
- As of January 2019, Amazon holds 16.2% of Rivian’s shares.
- Ford is the largest investor in Rivian.
- Other financial institutions have also invested in Rivian.
- The Inflation Reduction Act allocates $3 billion to electrify the delivery fleet of the Postal Service.
- USPS has doubled its order of electric trucks for its new fleet, from 5,000 to 10,019 vehicles.
- Ford says it plans to invest $30 billion in the production of electric vehicles through 2025.
- By 2030, Ford expects to sell 1.5 million EVs a year.
- Ford has invested $700 million in the creation of an electric vehicle center near the Rouge Complex where the F-Series truck is built.
- The Lightning’s battery pack and frame are assembled at the Rouge Complex.
- The pickup bed and cab are built in another location.
- A conveyor belt transports the assembled Lightnings from one factory to the next.
