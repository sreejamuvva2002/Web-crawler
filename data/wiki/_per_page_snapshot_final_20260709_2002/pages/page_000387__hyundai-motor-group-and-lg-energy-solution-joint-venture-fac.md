# Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy Solution Joint Venture Facility

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-26

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy to Construct USD 4.3 Billion EV Battery Plant in USA - America | Energetica India Magazine](https://www.energetica-india.net/news/hyundai-motor-group-and-lg-energy-to-construct-usd-43-billion-ev-battery-plant-in-usa)  ·  `page_000387`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy Solution stated they will construct a USD 4.3 billion electric vehicle battery plant in the United States as part of a move to take benefit of tax credits.
- Building of the factory in Bryan County, Georgia will commence in the second half of 2023.
- Battery manufacturing at the facility will begin at the end of 2025 at the earliest.
- The plant will have a yearly production capacity of 30 gigawatt-hours.
- The 30 GWh capacity is enough for 300,000 EVs.
- Hyundai Motor Group's corporate target is to attain EV sales of 2 million a year by 2030.
- Hyundai Motor Group is the world’s third-greatest auto manufacturer by vehicle sales.
- The new facility will assist in making a stable supply of batteries and permit Hyundai to respond quickly to the rising EV demand in the U.S. market.
- LGES and Hyundai Motor Group will each own 50% of the joint venture.
- Hyundai Motor Group includes Hyundai Motor, Kia, and auto parts manufacturer Hyundai Mobis.
- Youngsoo Kwon is the CEO of LG Energy Solution.
- Youngsoo Kwon stated that two strong leaders in the auto and battery industries have joined hands to drive the EV transition in America.
- Hyundai Motor Group is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
- LG Energy Solution Ltd. is a battery company based in Seoul, South Korea.
