# Kumho Tire Co. Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-10-23

**Source:** [Kim Jong-Ho Appointed Chairman of Kumho Tire | Kumho Tire USA](https://kumhotireusa.com/press-room/835)  ·  `page_056694`

## Facts

- Korean Development Bank appointed Kim Jong-Ho as the new chairman of Kumho Tire, effective December 1, 2017.
- Kim Jong-Ho previously held the title of CEO for Kumho Tire from 2008 to 2012 and successfully led the company through significant challenges during the global recession and recovery period.
- Chairman Kim holds extensive tire industry experience from various international leadership positions across global divisions in Singapore, the Middle East, Australia, and the U.S.
- During the early stages of the company’s growth, Jong-Ho’s focus on innovation and technology across all businesses contributed to substantial global expansion.
- Chairman Kim stated: “Our primary focus is to truly listen to the needs of our customers and implement a solution-based strategy and react to market changes with optimal agility. It is time for us to make disciplined and effective changes to better address and manage market demands”.
- Han Yong-seong, a former executive from Woori Bank, was selected as vice president of Kumho Tire.
- Han Yong-seong previously served as chief financial officer of Taihan Electric Wire from 2010 to 2014, overseeing the restructuring of the company’s subsidiaries.
- The appointments of Kim and Han will be finalized during a shareholders’ meeting on December 1, 2017.
- Kumho Tire was founded in 1960 and has more than 50 years of history immersed in an innovative approach to product development, market expansion, technical progression, and environmental awareness.
- Kumho Tire is positioned as one of the world’s top tire manufacturers and the world’s 9th largest global tire company.
- Kumho Tire products are distributed in 160 countries.
- Kumho Tire USA is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- Kumho Tire USA serves as the U.S. sales, marketing, product development, and distribution arm of Kumho Tire Co. Inc.
