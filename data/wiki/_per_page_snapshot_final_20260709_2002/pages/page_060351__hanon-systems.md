# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-24

**Source:** [Hanon Systems creating 160 jobs for $40 million Hyundai meta plant in Bulloch Co.](https://www.wtoc.com/2023/05/24/hanon-systems-creating-160-jobs-40-million-hyundai-meta-plant-bulloch-co/)  ·  `page_060351`

## Facts

- Hanon Systems will create a new manufacturing facility at Gateway Regional Industrial Park in Bulloch County, Georgia.
- Prep work has already started for this plant.
- Hanon Systems will employ 160 workers for its $40 million plant.
- The company will manufacture heater and AC components for Hyundai’s electric vehicles that will be built at the plant along Interstate 16 in Bryan County.
- They’re locating in the Gateway Industry Park that already houses Briggs and Stratton, Walmart Distribution, and more.
- Benjy Thompson stated that locating Hanon inside Gateway Industrial Park closer to the city of Statesboro will make it easier for some people who live in the city to get to a job at Hanon.
- Thompson says Bulloch County has now announced more than 4,000 jobs associated with the Hyundai plant since its announcement last May.
