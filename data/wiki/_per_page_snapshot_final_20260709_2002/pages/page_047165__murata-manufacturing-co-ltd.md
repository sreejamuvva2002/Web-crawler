# Murata Manufacturing Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Murata is showcasing intelligent system technologies for mobility, wellness, and connectivity at CES 2026.
- Smart mobility demonstrations feature embedded RFID tire tag and sensing, gyro compass, headlight leveling using MEMS, and motion tracing demonstration using low frequency magnetic fields.
- Embedded connectivity solutions utilize advanced Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and Ultra-Wideband (UWB) modules to deliver precise location detection and coverage across vast areas.
- Connectivity demos highlight Wi-Fi Mesh for range extension, Wi-Fi HaLow TM (Type 2HK/Type 2HL Module), Type 2NR Module (Nordic nRF54L15 based), UWB Smart Lock, indoor location tracking with Bluetooth Channel Sounding, and satellite position tracking.
- Health-centric sensing solutions on display include a molecular analysis platform for 15-minute DNA/RNA analysis, Vios Monitoring System for continuous hospital-grade telemetry and remote ECG, and an application example of the 3D substrate Multilayer LCP Product with piezoelectric film sensor PicoleafTM (Smart Ring Demo).
- Ultrasonic technology showcase includes an ultrasonic cleaning device to clear optics for AD/ADAS in any weather, ultrasonic tracking powered by pMUT for low-power 3D/6DoF spatial tracking for XR, and airborne ultrasound transmission metamaterial for bi-directional ultrasound through plastic/metal.
- At the time of this announcement, some exhibit products are concept products or under development, with specifications and appearance subject to change without notice.
- Murata Manufacturing Co., Ltd. is a worldwide leader in the design, manufacture, and sale of ceramic-based passive electronic components and 2NRs, communication modules and power supply modules.
- The company is committed to the development of advanced electronic materials and leading edge, multi-functional, high-density modules.
- Murata has employees and manufacturing facilities throughout the world.
- Media contact for inquiries is Amy Talley, General Manager of Corporate Communications.
