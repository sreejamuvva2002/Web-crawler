## Facts

- Seohan Group traces its origins to Korea Flange, which was established in 1974.
- Korea Flange is the predecessor company and is currently known as Korea Movenex.
- Seohan Group has grown into a leading auto parts and free forging corporation in Korea.
- The company continuously invests in R&D to develop new technologies and products.
- Seohan Group aims to build an efficient production and quality management system through continuous improvement.
- The company specializes in core components that provide support for future eco-friendly automobiles and green low-carbon growth.
