# Wabash National Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Wabash inks long-term deal aluminum supplier Hydro | Trailer Body Builders](https://www.trailer-bodybuilders.com/trailers/article/21180758/wabash-inks-long-term-deal-aluminum-supplier-hydro)  ·  `page_014625`

## Facts

- Wabash National Corp. has announced a 10-year agreement with Hydro, a leader in the global aluminum and energy industry.
- Under the agreement, Hydro will be a key supplier for Wabash National’s demand for dry van trailers, refrigerated trailers with molded structural composite (MSC) technology, flatbed trailers, and truck bodies for the next 10 years through 2031.
- The agreement also covers requirements of new products Wabash National may release through 2031.
- The enhanced supply aligns with Wabash National’s recent announcement of additional dry van capacity set to come online at the beginning of 2023.
- Wabash National is planning future capacity for MSC Technology products.
- Wabash National and Hydro have entered a mutually beneficial business relationship aligned on a shared long-term vision of delivering sustainability to customers through continued collaboration and innovation.
- Richard Mansilla, vice president of global supply chain at Wabash National, stated the company selected Hydro as a strategic supplier partner because they share the same values and vision for where the industry is headed.
- The partnership will help address Wabash National’s near-term aluminum supply needs to aid in increasing production of dry van trailers, refrigerated trailers, and truck bodies with MSC Technology.
- In the longer term, Wabash National plans to work more closely with Hydro on innovation and footprint optimization for the benefit of customers, employees, and shareholders.
- Hydro has operations in 40 countries and has supplied Wabash National with aluminum extrusions for over 12 years.
- Hydro is a three-time Wabash National supplier award winner and was named Supplier of the Year in 2020.
- Jerrod Hoeft, Hydro’s vice president of national accounts for transportation, stated that Hydro supports industries through innovative products and solutions and views Wabash National as a visionary leadership partner.
