# SK Innovation Battery

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Commerce industrial complex, along I-85, approximately 65 miles northeast of downtown Atlanta, Georgia, United States

## Overview

SK Innovation’s first EV battery factory in the United States, located on 283 acres along I-85 near Atlanta, comprising Phase 1 and Phase 2 construction.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Manufactures electric vehicle batteries supporting the Georgia EV supply chain.

## Timeline

- **Spring 2019** — Work began on Phase 1 of the SK Innovation Battery project.
- **Last quarter 2020** — Construction began on Phase 2, adding a 1.2 million-square-foot facility and eight additional buildings.

## Related organizations & people

- [[sk-innovation-co-ltd|SK Innovation]] — Parent company

## Key facts

- The site occupies 283 acres along I-85 inside the Commerce industrial complex.
- Phase 1 consists of a 2.4 million-square-foot manufacturing building with multiple roof elevations and mezzanine decks.
- Phase 2 adds a 1.2 million-square-foot facility plus eight ancillary buildings totaling 90,000 sq ft, including kitchens, a cell discharge area, expanded administrative offices, and a connecting bridge.
