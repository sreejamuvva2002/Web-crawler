## Facts

- Murata, a global leader in advanced electronics, will showcase intelligent systems and precision sensing technologies at CES 2026.
- The showcase will take place January 6-9, 2026, at Booth 4399 in West Hall at the Vehicle Technology & Advanced Mobility Pavilion in Las Vegas.
- Murata's Smart Mobility segment features MEMS-based systems, GNSS-free positioning, and sensor-enabled platforms for safer, smarter movement.
- Featured mobility demos include an Embedded RFID Tire Tag and Sensing system.
- Featured mobility demos include a Gyro Compass.
- Featured mobility demos include Headlight Leveling Using MEMS.
- Featured mobility demos include a Motion Tracing Demonstration Using Low Frequency Magnetic Fields.
- Murata's Embedded Connectivity segment features advanced Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and Ultra-Wideband (UWB) modules for intelligent infrastructure.
- Connectivity demos highlight Wi-Fi Mesh for Range Extension.
- Connectivity demos highlight Wi-Fi HaLowTM (Type 2HK/Type 2HL Module).
- Connectivity demos highlight a Type 2NR Module based on the Nordic nRF54L15.
- Connectivity demos highlight a UWB Smart Lock.
- Connectivity demos highlight Indoor Location Tracking with Bluetooth Channel Sounding.
- Connectivity demos highlight Satellite Position Tracking.
- Murata's Health-Centric Sensing segment features molecular analysis and wearable biosensors for non-invasive, continuous health monitoring.
- Health solutions on display include a Molecular Analysis Platform 1, 2, 3 capable of 15-minute DNA/RNA analysis.
- Health solutions on display include the Vios Monitoring System for continuous hospital-grade telemetry and remote ECG.
- Health solutions include an application example of a 3D substrate Multilayer LCP Product and a piezoelectric film sensor PicoleafTM used in a Smart Ring Demo.
- Murata's Ultrasonic Tech segment showcases ultrasound for cleaning, tracking, and sensing.
- Ultrasonic demos include an Ultrasonic Cleaning Device designed to clear optics for AD/ADAS systems in any weather.
- Ultrasonic demos include Ultrasonic Tracking Powered by pMUT for low-power 3D/6DoF spatial tracking for XR.
- Ultrasonic demos include an Airborne Ultrasound Transmission Metamaterial enabling bi-directional ultrasound through plastic/metal.
- Some exhibit products are concept products or under development, with specifications and appearance subject to change without notice.
- Murata Manufacturing Co., Ltd. is a worldwide leader in the design, manufacture, and sale of ceramic-based passive electronic components, 2NRs, communication modules, and power supply modules.
- The company has employees and manufacturing facilities throughout the world.
