## Facts

- SK Battery America (SKBA) is a subsidiary of SK On (South Korea’s SK Group).
- SKBA was established to meet North America’s EV battery demand.
- Since its founding, it has invested $2.6 billion in Georgia facilities.
- It has created over 3000 jobs.
- It supplies high-energy density batteries to OEMs like Ford and Volkswagen.
- Its operations support sustainable transportation in the United States.
