# Partners in Progress

## Facts

- Partners in Progress is a bold, public/private partnership to retain, create, and attract good jobs to the Northwest Georgia region.
- The ongoing initiative is designed to accelerate economic growth and sustainability, increase the regional tax base, create wealth, and generate meaningful career opportunities for future generations.
- Economic development is a globally competitive endeavor, but its success is determined at the local and regional level.
- Many of Northwest Georgia's neighboring regions possess significant resources to market, attract, and recruit new industries that provide good jobs and invest in those communities.
- Partners in Progress levels the playing field to give Northwest Georgia the competitive edge needed to win new business and industry for the region.
