# OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc. is headquartered in Rome, Georgia, located 1-1/2 hours northwest of Atlanta.
- The company operates a global footprint comprising 32 facilities across the U.S., Canada, Mexico, England, Germany, the Czech Republic, China, Thailand, and Sri Lanka.
- OTR provides manufacturing, painting, assembly, tire mounting, sequencing, warehousing, subassembly, and foam- and tire-fill services to support OEM localization initiatives.
- The company maintains 16 U.S. locations totaling nearly 2 million square feet to support OEMs and distributors with coast-to-coast aftermarket coverage.
- OTR manufactures and paints wheels, rims, and components in four U.S. locations: Rome, Georgia; Omaha, Nebraska; Jacksonville, Florida; and Quincy, Illinois.
- Additional U.S. facility locations include Chambersburg, Pennsylvania; Timmonsville and Clinton, South Carolina; two locations in Jacksonville, Florida; Louisville, Mississippi; Mansfield, Texas; Hesston and McCook, Kansas; Phoenix, Arizona; Denver, Colorado; and Oconomowoc, Wisconsin.
- Several U.S. locations provide subassembly services as a leveraged resource for OEMs to achieve cost savings.
- OTR was recognized with Excel Industries Gold Supplier Awards in 2017, 2018, and 2019.
- The company received the Honda Challenging Spirit Award for Support and Performance in 2019.
- OTR won the Kubota Kaizen Community Award in 2013 and 2014.
- OTR Canada is headquartered in Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario, with additional locations in Guelph and Sudbury, Ontario; Saskatoon, Saskatchewan; and Edmonton, Alberta.
- All five Canadian facilities produce multi-piece rims and components for earthmoving, surface and underground mining, construction, and specialty vehicle applications.
- The Sudbury facility recently installed a state-of-the-art bake oven system with temperature and humidity control and a new priming process to optimize adhesion and durability.
- OTR Europe operates facilities in Derby and Suffolk, England; Sande, Germany; and the Czech Republic, providing tire mounting, sequencing, just-in-time supply, and warehousing.
- The Suffolk facility manufactures and repairs wheels primarily for agricultural vehicles and provides foam-filling for OEM customers.
- OTR opened its Czech Republic facility in 2013 to service Europe, Russia, and eastern European countries.
- OTR's China facility is located in Wuxi, two hours northwest of Shanghai, and manufactures multi-piece steel rims, hubs, and components for surface and underground mining, material handling, heavy-duty, and specialty vehicles.
- The China facility produces wheels ranging from 10 inches to 63 inches using HSLA grade steel, seamless forgings, sub-arc welding, and precision CNC machining.
- OTR's China location operates four paint lines with advanced pretreatment and primer systems, wet and powder coating, and conducts in-process ultrasonic and magnetic particle testing for quality assurance.
- The China facility is ISO 9001:2015 certified.
- OTR's Thailand facility in Chon Buri manufactures steel wheels primarily for agricultural OEMs using automated construction for high-volume production.
- The Thailand location provides value-added services including tire mounting and sequencing, with a video demonstration available online.
- OTR's Sri Lanka facility in Ekala manufactures steel rims, wheels, accessories for off-the-road applications ranging from 22 to 49 inches, as well as steel bands.
- Manufacturing processes at the Sri Lanka facility feature multiple paint lines using advanced 5-stage wash, wet, and powder coat systems compliant with OEM standards.
- Rigorous testing at all facilities measures tensile strength, dimensional accuracy, and compliance with ISO 9001:2015 standards and OEM specifications.
