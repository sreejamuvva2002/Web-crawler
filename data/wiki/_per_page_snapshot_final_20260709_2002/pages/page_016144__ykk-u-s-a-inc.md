# YKK (U.S.A.) Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [YKK USA Launches Solutions Group To Help Companies Improve Manufacturing Efficiency, Processes And Develop Of New Products | SGB Media Online](https://sgbonline.com/pressrelease/ykk-usa-launches-solutions-group-to-help-companies-improve-manufacturing-efficiency-processes-and-develop-of-new-products/)  ·  `page_016144`

## Facts

- YKK (U.S.A.) Inc. announced the launch of its new Solutions Group ahead of the Industrial Fabrics Association International (IFAI) Expo.
- The Solutions Group was created to help manufacturers struggling with issues such as time-consuming manual processes, high scrap rate and wasted material, inconsistent quality, high inventory, multiple suppliers, and rising production costs.
- The group will provide consultation on new product development and manufacturing process improvement, custom machinery and assembly service.
- YKK (U.S.A.) Inc. has been engaging in manufacturing process improvement for customers through its Field Technology Centers since 1978.
- John Smith serves as president of YKK U.S.A. Inc.
- President John Smith stated that the YKK brand is synonymous with quality and innovation, and that YKK has long been a trusted partner in providing high-quality products that solve customers’ problems.
- President John Smith noted that eighty-six years of experience manufacturing zippers and other fastening products means YKK has an enormous amount of know-how regarding sewing operations, waste reduction and process improvement.
- President John Smith asked those who have turned to YKK for quality products to now seek their expertise on improving efficiency and reducing scrap and inventory.
- One of YKK’s fundamental behaviors is “listen to understand,” and consultations by YKK begin with a plant visit and listening session by a team of YKK engineers to understand the issues before solutions are offered.
- YKK’s technical support network is located across the U.S., Mexico, the Caribbean, and Central America.
- YKK Solutions Group customers can expect improved ergonomics for their employees and a more sustainable operation as waste and energy are reduced.
- President John Smith stated that YKK believes in the “Cycle of Goodness,” that no one prospers without rendering benefit to others, and that YKK commits to using its ingenuity to provide creative solutions for customers to improve their bottom line.
