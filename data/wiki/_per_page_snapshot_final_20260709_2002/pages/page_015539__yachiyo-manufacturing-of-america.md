# Yachiyo Manufacturing of America

**Source:** [New jobs coming to our Cedar Village, Carrollton community: Yachiyo Hiring for New Plant - Pentagon Homes Mobile Home Communities](https://pentagonhomes.biz/new-jobs-coming-to-our-cedar-village-carrollton-community-yachiyo-hiring-for-new-plant/)  ·  `page_015539`

## Facts

- Yachiyo, a Japanese auto parts supplier, will be locating not far from Cedar Village in the Carrollton community.
- The company will hold a recruitment fair with the Georgia Department of Labor on April 5 in Waco to help fill jobs for their forthcoming plant.
- The new plant will manufacture plastic fuel tanks and sunroofs for automobiles at its 130,000-square-foot facility.
- The facility is located on the old Sony Driving Range property near the intersection of Columbia Drive and Beulah Church Road in Carrollton.
- Yachiyo is recruiting for environmental health and safety coordinator, technical services manager, accounting assistant manager, purchasing coordinator, technical associates, quality assurance manager, shipping and receiving team leader, assembly team leaders, materials coordinator, final inspectors, and production associates.
- Federal Occupational Safety and Health Administration regulations require that applicants be at least 18 years old and have at least a high school diploma or a General Education Diploma (GED).
- Applicants are encouraged to bring a resume and will be required to complete a company application without assistance.
- Yachiyo announced last September it would locate to the Carrollton community, using the 23.5-acre tract owned by the Carrollton Payroll Development Authority.
- The company indicated at the time that it plans to fill about 230 new jobs.
- Jim Camp, attorney for the Carrollton Payroll Development Authority, told the Carroll County Board of Commissioners on Sept. 26 that the company will bring some high-level management jobs with them, but the majority of the jobs will be hired locally and will range in scope from manual labor to very specialized jobs.
- Yachiyo Industry was established in 1953 in Japan and has built an outstanding reputation as an automotive parts supplier.
- The company now employs 6,800 workers worldwide, with five manufacturing operations in North America.
- Yachiyo of America Inc. is headquartered in Columbus, Ohio.
- The job fair is scheduled for 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. at the West Georgia Technical College Murphy Campus in Waco.
