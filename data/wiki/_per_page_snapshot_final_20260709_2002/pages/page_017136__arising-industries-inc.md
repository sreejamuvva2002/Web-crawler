## Facts

- Ben Hill County Board of Commissioners unanimously approved rezoning 189 Ed Ward Rd. from General Farming (GF) to Wholesale Light Industrial (WLI).
- The rezoning is intended to allow the expansion of Arising Industries, Inc.
- The approval followed a recommendation from the Planning and Zoning Commission.
- A supporting public comment was provided prior to the vote.
- Building and Zoning Administrator Jake Hughes stated that the Planning and Zoning Commission had made the recommendation.
- The Ben Hill County Board of Commissioners voted on Oct. 7, 2025, to rezone the property from GF (General Farming) to WLI (Wholesale Light Industrial).
