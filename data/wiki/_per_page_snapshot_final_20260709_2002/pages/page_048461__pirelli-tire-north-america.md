# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-08-30

**Source:** [MADD and Pirelli Partner to Drive Change and Keep the Nation’s Roads Safe - MADD](https://madd.org/press-release/madd-and-pirelli-partner-to-drive-change-and-keep-the-nations-roads-safe/)  ·  `page_048461`

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America includes a Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) facility and research and development center at its Rome, Georgia headquarters.
- The Rome, Georgia headquarters is described as a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant.
- Pirelli focuses its business on the high-end, premium product segment where it is a world leader.
- Pirelli has a commercial presence in over 160 countries and 16 manufacturing sites in 11 countries.
- Pirelli Tire North America includes sales and marketing offices in New York City, Los Angeles, Detroit, Montreal, and Mexico City.
- The company manufactures, distributes, and markets original equipment and replacement tires for export and domestic car/motorcycle applications.
- Pirelli is the exclusive supplier of the Formula 1 championship, the Superbike world championship, and many other championships around the world.
