# FREYR Battery

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Coweta County, Georgia, Coweta County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Norwegian battery upstart Freyr Battery is planning a $2.57 billion factory in Coweta County, Georgia to produce energy storage systems and lithium-ion batteries, backed by more than $358 million in state and local incentives.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The plant will manufacture battery cells and large-scale energy storage systems that support electric vehicles and the broader EV ecosystem in Georgia.
