# Isuzu Motors Limited

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Isuzu selected Greenville County, S.C., to establish its new U.S. production base.
- The company's $280 million investment will create more than 700 new jobs.
- Isuzu is a subsidiary of Isuzu Motors Limited, one of the world’s largest manufacturers of medium- and heavy-duty trucks.
- Isuzu supplies commercial vehicles for the United States and Canadian markets.
- Isuzu trucks entered the North American truck market in 1984.
- The company sold 44,000 units in the last fiscal year, its highest total ever.
- Isuzu purchased a 1 million-square-foot facility on over 200 acres of land on Augusta Road in Greenville County.
- The company is converting the purchased facility into a state-of-the-art assembly plant to expand vehicle supply capabilities in North America.
- The new plant will include a variable-model, variable-volume production line that produces both internal combustion engine vehicles and electric vehicles.
- When completed, the plant will produce the company’s N-Series Gas, N-Series Electric, and F-Series Diesel trucks.
- The completed plant will have a production capacity of 50,000 units annually.
