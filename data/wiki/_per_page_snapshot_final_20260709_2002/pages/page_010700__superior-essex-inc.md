# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-01

**Source:** [Superior Essex Completes Essex Furukawa Buyout from Furukawa Electric | Georgia Technical Review](https://www.georgiatechnicalreview.com/article/708000574-superior-essex-completes-essex-furukawa-buyout-from-furukawa-electric)  ·  `page_010700`

## Facts

- Superior Essex successfully completed the acquisition of the remaining minority interest in Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire from Furukawa Electric Co., Ltd.
- The acquisition transaction was completed effective April 30, 2024.
- The completion establishes Superior Essex as the sole owner of the brand, assets, and operations previously conducted by the global joint venture with Furukawa Electric.
- Superior Essex plans to relaunch its magnet wire business under a new company name, which will be announced later in 2024.
- The company will continue to operate all current facilities of the Essex Furukawa joint venture worldwide.
- Current facilities include magnet wire facilities in Japan and Malaysia (Kuala Lumpur) that were contributed by Furukawa Electric in October 2020.
- Daniel Choi serves as the CEO of Superior Essex.
- CEO Daniel Choi stated, “Over the last several years we have been able to accomplish many great things working with the team from Furukawa Electric. I am proud of what was created and thankful for their contributions.”
- The acquisition solidifies Superior Essex's control over global operations and reinforces its commitment to delivering products and services to customers worldwide.
- Superior Essex plans to continuously respond to market demands in the xEV automotive industry, power, electrification, and renewable energy markets.
- The company aims to strengthen its position by actively pursuing decarbonization and sustainability initiatives.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, Essex Energy Italy, IVA, Lacroix + Kress, Hi-Wire, and Essex Brownell.
- Superior Essex employs over 3,000 people across 11 countries on three continents.
- The company is the leading global provider of magnet wire.
- Superior Essex is at the forefront of communications fiber application as well as Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company is instrumental in creating disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex is committed to pioneering smart building technology while setting the pace on sustainability.
- Furukawa Electric Group was established in 1884 and includes 127 group companies with approximately 50,000 employees worldwide.
- Furukawa Electric provides technologies, products, and services for infrastructure fields (information and energy), automotive systems fields, and electronics fields using core technologies of metals, polymers, photonics, and high frequency.
