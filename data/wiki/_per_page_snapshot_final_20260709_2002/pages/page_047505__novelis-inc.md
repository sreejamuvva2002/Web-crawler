## Facts

- Hindalco-backed aluminum recycling company Novelis has postponed its U.S. listing of over $900 million, citing market conditions.
- Novelis is targeting a valuation of up to $12.6 billion for its U.S. stock market listing.
- A subsidiary of Hindalco Industries, Novelis is the world's largest rolled aluminum sheet producer.
- Novelis serves customers including Coca-Cola, Ford, and Jaguar Landrover.
- Novelis Inc. was established in 2004 and is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- Novelis operates across North America, South America, Asia, and Europe in the aluminum recycling and production industry.
- Novelis is a subsidiary of AV Minerals (Netherlands) N.V.
- Novelis contributes significantly to the market for aluminum sheets, plates, and light gauge products.
- Novelis processes post-consumer aluminum from sources like used-beverage cans and post-industrial scrap through its extensive recycling operations.
- Novelis asserts its global presence by directly selling to customers and leveraging distributors for a wider reach.
