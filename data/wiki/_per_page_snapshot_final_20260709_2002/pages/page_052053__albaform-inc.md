## Facts

- ALBAform Inc. is owned by its Czech parent company, ALBA-METAL.
- The company designs, develops, and manufactures precision metal components for the automotive industry.
- ALBAform leverages over 20 years of ALBA-METAL’s metal production experience combined with its own industry expertise.
- The company is located in Flowery Branch, Georgia.
- ALBAform is growing quickly to service its partners and customers.
- In addition to manufacturing, the company offers consignment stock systems using standardized packaging and transport units to minimize inventory and transportation costs.
- A plant tour will showcase the latest fully automated CNC bending technology.
- The facility features 16 welding robots.
- The event agenda includes registration, networking, and a GMA welcome from 8:00 to 8:30 am.
- A tour host presentation and safety briefing is scheduled from 8:30 to 8:45 am.
- The plant tour itself runs from 8:45 to 9:45 am.
- A wrap-up and summary session concludes the event from 9:45 to 10:00 am.
- Two plant tours are offered on the day of the event, with separate registrations for the 8 am and 10 am sessions.
- Attendance is limited to one ticket per non-manufacturing company and two tickets per manufacturing company.
- Closed-toed or flat bottom shoes are mandatory for attendance, with high heels prohibited.
- Photography is not allowed during the tour.
