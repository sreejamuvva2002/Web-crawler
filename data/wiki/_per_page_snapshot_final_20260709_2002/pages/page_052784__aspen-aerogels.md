# Aspen Aerogels

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-16

**Source:** [Aspen Aerogels receives federal loan to build new plant in Georgia](https://bostonglobe-prod.cdn.arcpublishing.com/2024/10/16/business/northborough-aspen-aerogels-federal-loan-plant-talking-points/)  ·  `page_052784`

## Facts

- US Department of Energy approved a loan of up to $671 million to finance the construction of a manufacturing plant in Georgia.
- Aspen Aerogels plans to manufacture PyroThin aerogel blankets at the new Georgia facility.
- The PyroThin aerogel blankets are used as thermal barriers to prevent out-of-control vehicle battery fires.
- Aspen Aerogels currently operates one manufacturing plant in East Providence, R.I.
- Aspen Aerogels opened an engineering and prototyping center in Marlborough last year.
- The company currently outsources some of its manufacturing work to suppliers in China.
- Aspen Aerogels has a current workforce of about 550 employees.
- Aspen Aerogels plans to employ 255 full-time workers at the Georgia plant.
- Aspen chief executive Donald Young stated that the Department of Energy loan will fully fund the company’s capital expenses for building the Georgia factory.
- Shares in Northborough-based Aspen Aerogels rose by more than 13 percent following the disclosure of the DOE loan approval.
