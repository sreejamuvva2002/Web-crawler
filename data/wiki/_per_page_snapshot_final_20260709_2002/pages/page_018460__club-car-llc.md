# Club Car, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-06-10

**Source:** [Craig Scanlon Appointed CEO of Club Car](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/craig-scanlon-appointed-ceo-club-120000240.html?fr=sycsrp_catchall)  ·  `page_018460`

## Facts

- Club Car is headquartered in Augusta, Georgia.
- Craig Scanlon was appointed President and Chief Executive Officer of Club Car, effective June 9, 2025.
- Scanlon succeeds Mark Wagner, who announced his retirement in March.
- Scanlon has over two decades of executive leadership experience in private equity-owned and Fortune 500 organizations.
- Most recently, Scanlon served as CEO of K&N Engineering.
- Scanlon's career spans the industrial, powersports and automotive aftermarket sectors, including 18 years at Polaris where he rose to Chief Marketing Officer.
- Scanlon began his career in 1998 in commercial sales of Club Car Carryall vehicles while at Ingersoll Rand.
- Jared Hutchins, Principal at Platinum Equity, which acquired Club Car from Ingersoll Rand in 2021, stated that Scanlon is a proven leader with operational expertise and deep industry experience.
- Club Car is a global leader in golf cars, low-speed utility vehicles and personal-use transportation.
- Club Car is poised to rapidly innovate with upcoming initiatives focused on new product and features, technology, and digital connectivity.
- The Club Car product portfolio includes the luxury electric vehicle brand Garia and lightweight electric utility vehicle brand Melex.
- Club Car has a history of superior design and performance that spans over six decades.
