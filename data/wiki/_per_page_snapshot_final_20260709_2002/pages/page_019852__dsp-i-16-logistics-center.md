# DSP I-16 Logistics Center

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Dayton Street Partners Fully Leases DSP I-16 Logistics Center with Major Expansion by Ecoplastic America | Dayton Street](https://dspre.com/dayton-street-partners-fully-leases-dsp-i-16-logistics-center-with-major-expansion-by-ecoplastic-america/)  ·  `page_019852`

## Facts

- DSP I-16 Logistics Center is a 548,818-square-foot Class A distribution facility.
- The facility is now 100% leased.
- Ecoplastic America expanded its footprint by 117,692 square feet.
- Ecoplastic America's total occupancy at the center is 253,807 square feet.
- The facility is located at 11900 US-280 in Ellabell, Georgia.
- It is situated in Savannah’s I-16 West submarket.
- The facility is strategically positioned to support regional logistics and manufacturing growth.
- Ecoplastic America is a premier manufacturer of plastic automotive parts since 1984.
- Ecoplastic America remains focused on innovation and R&D.
- Ecoplastic America's expanded presence will directly support operations at the adjacent Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant.
- Veyer, Inc. signed a long-term lease agreement at the facility.
- GFA signed a long-term lease agreement at the facility.
- The leases solidify DSP I-16 Logistics Center as a key industrial hub in the region.
