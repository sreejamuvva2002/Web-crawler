# RealTruck

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-07

**Source:** [RealTruck Releases Two Bear Grylls Adventure Vehicle Concepts](https://www.forbes.com/sites/kristinshaw/2024/11/07/realtruck-debuts-two-bear-grylls-adventure-vehicle-concepts/)  ·  `page_049013`

## Facts

- RealTruck is a global aftermarket product and accessory brand.
- RealTruck partnered with survivalist Bear Grylls to design and build two extra-tough concept vehicles.
- The concepts were built from a 2021 Ford F-150 and a 2024 Toyota Tacoma.
- Both concept trucks are emblazoned with the word “Bear” in honor of Grylls.
- Bear Grylls is known for his TV show “Adventure Garage with Bear Grylls,” which airs next year.
- The two concepts feature extreme vehicle transformations fit for rugged parts of the world.
- The vehicles were built by the Superlift Suspension team in West Monroe, Louisiana.
- The concepts are currently on display at the RealTruck booth at this year’s SEMA (Specialty Equipment Market Association) Show.
- Companies like RealTruck and eBay Motors have seen a huge surge in accessories sales due to growing interest in overlanding, camping, and off-roading.
- The Ford F-150 concept includes a Husky Liners Rocker Guard, Low Profile Vent Visors and LightShield Pro, Bushwacker Pocket Style Fender Flares, Go Rhino Element Front Bumper, Sport Bar 2.0, Dominator Xtreme DT Side Step, and an XVenture Gear Hard Case.
- The Toyota Tacoma concept features GoRhino XVenture Gear Tool Rolls and Xplor Blackout Series Maxline Hi/Low LED Round Lights, RealTruck Sport Bar, Husky Liners Weatherbeaters, Universal Mud Guards and Low Profile Vent Visors.
- A collaboration with NBA superstar Dwayne Wade features a custom 2017 Ford F-250 Super Duty on display at the SEMA show.
- The Dwayne Wade truck modifications include a RealTruck Superlift 6-inch lift kit with King coilovers and rear shocks, RealTruck RetraxPRO MX tonneau cover, RealTruck AMP Research BedStep and Powerstep XL, and Husky Liners Weatherbeater floor liners.
- The Dwayne Wade truck will be auctioned off at Barrett-Jackson’s spring event in April, with all proceeds benefiting Wade’s non-profit organization Social Change Fund United (SCFU).
- SCFU is co-founded by Dwayne Wade to harness influence and resources to help underrepresented communities of color.
- Legendary artist and designer Chip Foose joined forces with RealTruck for another vehicle auctioned for charity.
- The Chip Foose vehicle is a 2021 Ford F-150 featuring a traction recovery winch, a Rugged Ridge traction recovery kit, Go Rhino rear and front bumpers, and Husky Liners RVL fender flares, all from RealTruck.
- Proceeds from the Chip Foose vehicle will be donated to the Progeria Research Foundation, which helps with treatments and works toward finding a cure for Progeria, a rare genetic premature-aging disease.
