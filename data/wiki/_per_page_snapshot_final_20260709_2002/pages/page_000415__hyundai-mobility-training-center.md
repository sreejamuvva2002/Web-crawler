# Hyundai Mobility Training Center

**Type:** workforce_training  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- The new Hyundai Mobility Training Center is located in Ellabelle, GA.
- The facility will support the automaker’s assembly plant.
- The 89,000-square-foot facility will teach workers how to build next-generation electric vehicles.
- The state-of-the-art center is operated by the state’s workforce development program and is part of the Technical College System of Georgia.
- At full capacity, it will be able to train up to 824 individuals at a time.
