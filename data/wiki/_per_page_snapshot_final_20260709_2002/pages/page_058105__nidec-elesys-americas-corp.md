## Facts

- Nidec Group operates a global network of more than 300 companies in 43 countries.
- Nidec Group designs and manufactures a comprehensive range of motors, from small ones found in computers to large ones used in manufacturing equipment.
- Nidec Elesys develops, manufactures, and sells automotive safety systems, such as electronic control units (ECU) for electric power steering and vehicle stability assist systems.
- Nidec Elesys pioneers advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) for Honda and other OEMs.
- Nidec Elesys Americas Corp. is the American subsidiary of Nidec Elesys.
- Nidec Elesys Americas Corp. has made significant strides in producing for the North American automotive market.
- Nidec Elesys Americas Corp. has won several awards for excellence in quality and delivery.
- Nidec Elesys Americas Corp. President Toshihiko Kono stated that the company strives to exceed customer expectations by creating safer, more comfortable mobility.
- The company has increased investment in research and development to address advanced automobile industry technology and stricter market demands.
- The company believes innovative products for vehicle electrification and autonomous vehicles will result in loyal customers and business expansion.
- Nidec Group aims to be the world’s best in “everything that spins and moves” and designs next-generation drive technologies.
