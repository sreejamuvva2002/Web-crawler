# HELLA GmbH & Co. KGaA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-03-01

**Source:** [Fred Snow becomes President at HELLA Automotive Sales Inc. | Vehicle Service Pros](https://www.vehicleservicepros.com/industry-news/service-repair/article/21197972/fred-snow-becomes-president-at-hella-automotive-sales-inc)  ·  `page_055757`

## Facts

- HELLA GmbH & Co. KGaA appointed Fred Snow as President of HELLA Automotive Sales, Incorporated effective March 1, 2019.
- The leadership change follows a planned handover process from predecessor Henning Schroeder to assure a seamless transition.
- Fred Snow has served as Vice President US & Canadian Aftermarket since 2006 and is in charge of Aftermarket Sales.
- Fred Snow holds a Master of Business Administration, is married with one child, and has worked for HELLA for 25 years.
- The aftermarket sales and marketing team was reorganized with new Sales Channel Director roles created.
- Jamie Barnes was named Sales Channel Director for Spare Parts (covering Traditional WD, Import Specialist, Spare Parts, and Thermal Specialist Channels) and will also handle Canadian sales.
- Urs Georges was named Sales Channel Director for Universal Parts (covering Heavy Duty, Accessory Specialist, E-commerce, and Retail Channels).
- Pablo Sanchez assumed marketing responsibilities for North and South America.
- Alejandro Caballero continues as Sales Director for Central / South America.
- Florian Mertins assumed the position of Head Product Management and Business Development last Fall, with a regional product development role.
- A new Head Regional Order Fulfillment position was created and filled by Dennis Hamann.
- Steve Profeta remains Vice President for Finance and Quality.
- Jeff Haas remains Vice President for Special Applications business division for North America.
- HELLA GmbH & Co. KGaA is headquartered in Lippstadt.
- HELLA is a global, family-owned company listed on the stock exchange.
- The company employs more than 40,000 people across over 125 locations in approximately 35 countries.
- HELLA develops and manufactures products for lighting technology and electronics for the automotive industry.
- HELLA operates one of the largest retail organizations for automotive parts, accessories, diagnostics, and services within Europe.
- Nearly 7,000 people work in research and development at HELLA.
- The HELLA Group reported sales of 6.6 billion euros in the fiscal year of 2016/2017.
- HELLA is ranked as one of the top 40 automotive parts suppliers in the world.
- HELLA is listed among the 100 largest German industrial companies.
