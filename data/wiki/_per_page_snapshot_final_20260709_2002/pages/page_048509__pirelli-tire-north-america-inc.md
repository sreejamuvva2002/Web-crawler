## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America Inc. announced that it is partnering with the Berry College Longleaf Pine Project, a collaboration between the college and The Nature Conservancy in Georgia to restore rare and essential longleaf pine habitat.
- The restoration site is located just north of Rome, Ga., a few miles away from Pirelli’s U.S. factory.
- The mountain containing the habitat is part of the Berry College campus and is home to one of the few remaining relict stands of Mountain Longleaf Pine.
- Longleaf pine forests once occupied more than 92 million acres throughout the southeastern U.S. and now cover only about 2% of the original range.
- One of the key pillars of Pirelli’s global sustainability strategy is to create value for the local communities in which the company operates.
