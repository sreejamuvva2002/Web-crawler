# Essex Brownell

## Facts

- On September 19, 2024, Essex Brownell announced the expansion of its McDonough, GA distribution center.
- The expansion reinforces Essex Brownell's position as a southeastern regional hub.
- The company renewed its lease at the facility in May 2023 to execute its regional support strategy.
- The McDonough distribution center expanded from 25,000 square feet to 133,000 square feet.
- The increased space enables better service for customers in the metro Atlanta and southeastern region.
- The expansion provides additional space for increased inventory storage.
- The facility will increase its hazardous material (HazMat) storage capacity.
- A new 40-foot by 40-foot HazMat room is currently under construction and expected to be completed in fall 2024.
- Essex Brownell has doubled its racking capacity, allowing for a significant increase in inventory storage.
- The expansion includes an increased supply of PureNet, LS Electric, and LS Cable product lines.
- Rebecca Hunnicutt, Vice President of Operations & Customer Service at Essex Brownell, stated that their investment in the McDonough facility has yielded better-than-expected results and given them greater confidence for this second expansion.
- Hunnicutt added that adding additional space will further enhance the service provided to customers in the region.
- Hunnicutt also stated, "We are excited about the opportunities brought by the expanded McDonough distribution center and look forward to continuing to serve our customers with the highest levels of quality and efficiency."
- Essex Brownell remains dedicated to meeting growing customer needs and delivering exceptional products and services.
- The expansion aligns with Essex Brownell's commitment to providing exceptional service and support to southeastern customers.
- By increasing storage capacity and diversifying its product lines, the company aims to solidify its position as a primary supplier in the electrical and industrial sectors.
- The facility also increases the company's commitment to safety and compliance.
