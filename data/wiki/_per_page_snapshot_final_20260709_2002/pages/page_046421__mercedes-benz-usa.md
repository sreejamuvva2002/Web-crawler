# Mercedes-Benz USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-01-11

**Source:** [Atlanta International School goes green with eSprinter van - Rough Draft Atlanta](https://roughdraftatlanta.com/2026/01/11/ais-mercedes-sustainability-initiative/)  ·  `page_046421`

## Facts

- Mercedes-Benz USA is based in Sandy Springs.
- Mercedes-Benz USA donated an all-electric eSprinter van to Atlanta International School (AIS) in December 2025 as part of its sustainability push.
- The donation was part of MBUSA’s “12 Days of Christmas” initiative, which allows celebrity brand ambassadors to donate sustainable transportation to community organizations.
- AIS operations team will use the new eSprinter to support campus maintenance and safety.
- AIS serves around 1,350 students in grades 3K–12 across its Buckhead and Sandy Springs campuses.
- Avery Nesbitt is the director of operations at Atlanta International School.
- The “Holidays with Love” concept began in 2023 to emphasize connection during the holiday season.
- Jee-Seop Kim is the managing director of Mercedes-Benz Vans USA.
- Kim stated the versatile, all-electric eSprinter is a workhorse designed to sustainably mobilize non-profits across the country.
- The campaign also donated an eSprinter van to Northside Youth Organization at Chastain Park via former Atlanta United goalie Brad Guzan.
- Another eSprinter was donated to Atlanta Public Schools via Atlanta Falcons running back Bijan Robinson.
- MBUSA will continue its “Holidays with Love” campaign by supporting students at partner schools in Atlanta: Michael R. Hollis Innovation Academy and Leonora P. Miles Elementary.
- This school support is part of Mercedes-Benz’s national corporate social responsibility program, Driving Your Future.
