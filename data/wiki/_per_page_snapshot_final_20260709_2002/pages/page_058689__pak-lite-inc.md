# Pak-Lite, Inc.

## Facts

- Founded in Atlanta, Georgia by Frank Pritchard in 1972 as a manufacturer of protective packaging products.
- Has embarked on a progressive path of product diversification and enhanced capabilities to become a leading North American converter/manufacturer of US-sourced foams, rubber, nonwovens, foils, films, and specialty materials.
- Customer base includes many of the top OEM and industrial companies in the world.
- Has manufacturing facilities in GA, NC, and Taiwan.
- Employs over 300+ dedicated professionals.
- Manufacturing capabilities include extrusion lamination to foams and rubber substrates.
- Combines materials to composite form and then supplies die-cut parts, components, roll stock, or sheeting.
- Finished parts often include pressure-sensitive adhesives and release liners.
- Additional capabilities include material selection, prototyping, skiving/slitting, rotary/flatbed die cutting, kiss cutting, spooling, precision parts manufacturing, assembly, and much more.
- Holds ISO 9001:2015 and IATF 16949 certifications with capabilities in converting foams, rubbers, and nonwoven fabrics for the OEMs and Tier 1 automotive industry.
- Has extensive experience with 3M Adhesives and Avery Adhesives as well as years of sourcing top foam, rubber, polymer, packaging, nonwoven, fiber, and film suppliers worldwide.
