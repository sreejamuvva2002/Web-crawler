# Paccar Australia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-03

**Source:** [Paccar Australia announces new managing director | News](https://www.ownerdriver.com.au/paccar-australia-announces-new-managing-director)  ·  `page_058529`

## Facts

- Damian Smethurst to take over from outgoing MD Andrew Hadjikakou on May 8.
- Truck manufacturer Paccar Australia has announced the appointment of Damian Smethurst as its new managing director who will officially take charge on Monday May 8.
- Smethurst has been with Paccar Australia for 25 years, holding leadership positions including director of operations, chief engineer and most recently general manager Paccar Parts Australia.
- PACCAR Australia manufactures Australian made Kenworth trucks, but also imports and assembles the leading European DAF brand.
- Smethurst succeeds outgoing managing director Andrew Hadjikakou, who Paccar says is leaving to begin the next phase of his life outside of Paccar and the road transport industry.
- Andrew has worked tirelessly throughout his eight years leading Paccar Australia.
- Andrew has championed many critical initiatives including their recent $50M factory expansion and leaves the business in excellent shape.
- Smethurst's focus is firmly on the people – staff, dealers, and suppliers – who make this organisation what it is, and on customers, who are the reason for its existence.
- Smethurst is excited about supporting and empowering teams to foster a culture of operational excellence and innovation, and strengthening relationships with customers across Australia and New Zealand.
