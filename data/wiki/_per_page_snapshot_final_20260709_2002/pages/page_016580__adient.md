## Facts

- Johnson Controls spin-off Adient will move its operational global headquarters, along with hundreds of jobs, to downtown Detroit.
- The global automotive seat manufacturer officially announced plans to make the Marquette Building its new home for 500 employees.
- The Marquette Building is located at 243 W. Congress across from Cobo Center.
- The move includes about 100 new positions that were with its former parent company in Milwaukee.
