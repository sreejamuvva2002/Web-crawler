## Facts

- Generative AI is driving increased demand for advanced cloud infrastructure and compute power.
- AWS’s investments will support the future of AI from data centers in Georgia.
- AWS data centers are flexible enough to efficiently run GPUs for traditional workloads or AI and machine learning models.
- The deployment of cutting-edge AI infrastructure will strengthen Georgia’s position as an innovation hub.
- Amazon has been a driving force behind Georgia's economic growth for more than a decade.

- Amazon supports 34,000 full- and part-time jobs in Georgia.
- Amazon supports 44,700 indirect jobs in Georgia.
- AWS’s planned investment is expected to create hundreds of new, direct AWS jobs in technical roles.
