# Valeo

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Valeo Boosts EV Production in Pune for India’s Green Future](https://allindiaev.com/valeo-ramps-up-ev-manufacturing-in-pune-to-drive-indias-electric-future/)  ·  `page_012819`

## Facts

- Valeo inaugurated advanced manufacturing and assembly lines at its Pune facility.
- The enhanced facility specializes in producing Valeo’s 3-in-1 Combo Unit, an integrated power electronics solution combining On-Board Chargers (OBCs), DC-DC Converters, and Power Distribution Units (PDUs).
- These components are vital for EV efficiency, enabling smoother performance, space optimization, and reduced production costs for Indian automakers.
- Xavier Dupont serves as CEO of Valeo Power Division and Group Executive Vice President.
- Jayakumar G serves as Group President & Managing Director of Valeo India.
- Dupont and Jayakumar G officiated the inauguration of the new lines at the Pune facility.
- The 3-in-1 Combo Unit integrates smart automation, modular assembly processes, and sustainable manufacturing practices.
- The On-Board Charger (OBC) converts AC grid power to charge high-voltage EV batteries ranging from 7kW to 22kW.
- The DC-DC Converter steps down high-voltage power to 12V to support auxiliary systems and low-voltage batteries.
- The Power Distribution Unit (PDU) distributes power efficiently across critical EV components.
- Valeo aims to reduce India’s reliance on imported EV components and bolster local supply chains through this expansion.
- Valeo has been operating in India since 1997.
- Valeo maintains eight production sites and a global R&D center across Chennai, Bangalore, Pune, Sanand, and Gurgaon.
- Valeo employs over 7,000 professionals in India, with nearly 50% working in engineering roles.
