## Facts

- Enplas is a leader in the development and manufacturing of custom mechanical plastic parts for applications across the automotive, consumer electronics, medical, and other industries.
- Enplas engineers utilize scientific and performance-driven processes to design reliable plastic gears for applications.
- Thorough DFM, in-house fine tool making, scientific injection molding, and automated assembly are conducted for all mechanical parts and devices to ensure mechanical performance in manufactured components.
- Enplas custom-designs and manufactures scientifically validated gears and gear trains to meet performance and reliability standards.
- Engineers utilize simulation and modeling technology following an empirical gear performance database and rigorous testing procedures to ensure reliable design and manufacturing.
- Enplas supplies spur gears, helical gears, worm and worm wheel gears, planetary gear systems, and many more custom and standard formats.
- Customers develop and manufacture other plastic components that conduct critical mechanical functions in automotive, medical, and other industries.
- Enplas delivers a steady supply of high volume, high quality, and high-performance parts with extensive DFM, performance-focused engineering, problem-solving, fine tool making, and deep expertise in injection molding.
- Automotive parts require special features such as heat tolerance and longevity.
- Enplas conducts extensive tests during product development (e.g., stress tests) to ensure consistent features in mass-produced parts.
- Enplas produces reliable parts for fuel pumps, ABS/ESC, seat belts, motor insulators, electric throttle valves, and more.
- Cam injection molding is used to provide contour precision for accurate and efficient mechanical cam systems.
- Enplas’s expertise in precision cam tool building and injection molding allows provision of high-quality plastic cams in mass volumes.
