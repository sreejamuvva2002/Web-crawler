# ABM Equipment Manufacturing Jobs Study

**Type:** research_center  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-13

**Source:** [Top 10 Cities for Manufacturing Jobs | Manufacturing.net](https://www.manufacturing.net/operations/news/22928690/top-10-cities-for-manufacturing-jobs)  ·  `page_000719`

## Facts

- ABM Equipment released a study ranking U.S. industrial job centers using metrics including total manufacturing employment, annual wages, manufacturing jobs as a percentage of the workforce, and employment growth rates.
- The company took data from the U.S. Census Bureau and U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics to conduct the analysis.
- Metrics were weighted and combined to create a 100-point scoring system highlighting both current and future industrial growth of the cities.
- Atlanta, GA ranked 10th on the list with an 81 total score.
