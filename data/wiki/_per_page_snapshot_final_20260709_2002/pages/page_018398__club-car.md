# Club Car

## Facts

- Club Car is an Augusta-area golf cart maker completing a 105,000-square-foot facility in Appling.
- Club Car’s Columbia County footprint is about to get 20% bigger.
- The local golf cart manufacturer announced it would expand into a 105,000-square-foot facility at White Oak Business Park.
- The new facility is located near the company’s existing 550,000-square-foot distribution facility that broke ground in 2018.
- The additional space will be used for “warehousing and manufacturing functions.”
- The new facility is expected to be completed by the end of the year.
- Club Car President Mark Wagner stated the company is dedicated to providing the best vehicles and service for customers.
- Mark Wagner noted that the new facility gives the company the opportunity to extend operations in the United States.
- Club Car began making golf carts in 1958.
- The company’s product line has grown to include utility vehicles for commercial use and nongolf transportation for personal use.
- Rick Evans, chairman of the Board of Directors for the Development Authority of Columbia County, stated that Club Car has a longstanding relationship with Columbia County.
- Rick Evans expressed value for the investments Club Car continues to make to expand and manufacture world-class golf carts and utility vehicles in Columbia County.
