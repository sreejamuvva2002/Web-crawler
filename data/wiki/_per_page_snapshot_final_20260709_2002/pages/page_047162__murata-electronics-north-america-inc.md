# Murata Electronics North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Murata Electronics Acquires RF Monolithics, Inc. - EE Times](https://www.eetimes.com/murata-electronics-acquires-rf-monolithics-inc/)  ·  `page_047162`

## Facts

- Murata Electronics North America, Inc. and its parent company Murata Manufacturing Co., Ltd. announced that it has closed the deal to acquire RF Monolithics, Inc.
- The cash transaction paid the holders of RFM common shares $1.78 per share.
- RFM will continue to market its broad range of solutions-driven, technology-enabled wireless connectivity for wireless applications under the RFM brand as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Murata Electronics North America, Inc.
- RFM's product portfolio includes individual standardized and custom components to modules for comprehensive industrial wireless sensor networks and machine-to-machine (M2M) technology.
- Murata Manufacturing Co., Ltd. is the world's number one supplier of passive electronic components.
- The acquisition announcement was issued from Smyrna, Ga.
