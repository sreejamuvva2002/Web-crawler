## Facts

- The initiative aims to enhance transportation efficiency and safety in the region.
- The collaboration integrates May Mobility’s self-driving shuttles with T-Mobile’s 5G network to create a seamless and reliable transportation system.
- The service will run from 6 a.m. to 9 p.m. on weekdays and from 6 a.m. to 7 p.m. on weekends.
- May Mobility is set to launch its first rider-only experience.
- The service is scheduled to open to the public on the week of October 7.
- Peachtree Corners has partnered with May Mobility to revolutionize urban transportation and create a living lab for AV technology.
- The partnership utilizes Multi-Policy De-Policy Technology, a system that allows for seamless integration of multiple transportation policies.
- May Mobility partnered with T-Mobile to leverage its robust 5G network for the autonomous vehicle service.
- The shuttles integrate Multi-Policy Decision Making (MPDM) systems, which enable real-time learning and adaptation from their environment.
- The service is expected to start in the fall and will initially cover a 1.5-mile route between downtown Peachtree Corners and the Peachtree Corners Transit Center.
- T-Mobile’s 5G network provides low latency, high speed connectivity, and high bandwidth to enhance the in-cabin experience for riders.
