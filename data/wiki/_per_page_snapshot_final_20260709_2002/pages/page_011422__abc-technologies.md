# ABC Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Canada’s ABC Technologies to acquire British TI Fluid for $1.32 bn - National Technology](https://nationaltechnology.co.uk/Canada_abc_technologies_to_acquire_british_ti_fluid_for_132_bn.php)  ·  `page_011422`

## Facts

- Toronto-based manufacturer ABC Technologies is acquiring British TI Fluid for $1.32 billion.
- ABC Technologies has more than 11,000 employees.
- ABC Technologies manufactures and supplies custom, highly-engineered technical plastics to the North American light vehicle industry.
- TI Fluid Systems is a London-listed, market-leading global manufacturer of thermal and fluid system solutions for current and developing vehicle architectures.
- Shareholders of TI Fluid Systems will receive 200 pence per share under the transaction terms.
- The transaction values TI Fluid Systems at an enterprise value of approximately £1,831 million.
- TI Fluid Systems currently operates across 27 countries.
- ABC Technologies is acquiring TI Fluid to expand both firms' global footprint and bolster its product portfolio.
- The acquisition aims to attract a broader range of customers, including automotive OEMs and Tier One suppliers worldwide.
- Terry Campbell, president and chief executive at ABC Technologies, stated the transaction represents a transformative strategic opportunity that will unlock value for stakeholders and accelerate growth.
- A combined business will enable better customer service, according to Campbell.
- ABC Technologies plans to look for further acquisitions in the future.
- The company will persistently seek alignment with organizations that have proven capabilities to further its success.
- The initial deadline for the sale offering was postponed to 22 November at ABC's request.
- TI Fluid Systems shares rose 1.8 percent to 184.00 pence each on the last Friday afternoon in London.
- ABC Technologies requested a further extension on Thursday of the previous week to finalize financing and other arrangements.
- ABC Technologies stated it has substantially completed due diligence and is reconfirming the possible offer price.
- The acquisition is expected to complete in the first half of 2025, subject to shareholder and relevant legal and regulatory approvals.
- ABC Technologies is backed by Apollo Global Management, which acquired 51 percent of its shares in April 2021.
