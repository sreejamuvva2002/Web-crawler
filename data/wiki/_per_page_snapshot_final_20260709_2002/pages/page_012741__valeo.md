# Valeo

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-01-04

**Source:** [French firm Valeo bets big on T.N., plans to invest ₹1,000 crore over next 5 years   - The Hindu](https://www.thehindu.com/news/national/tamil-nadu/french-firm-valeo-bets-big-on-tn-plans-to-invest-1000-crore-over-next-5-years/article67707224.ece)  ·  `page_012741`

## Facts

- French global auto parts manufacturer Valeo plans to invest around ₹1,000 crore over the next five to six years in Tamil Nadu.
- The planned expansion encompasses manufacturing facilities, aftermarket operations, and a global technical center.
- Valeo plans to increase its talent pool by 3,000-odd employees in the Chennai region.
- Valeo will increase its workforce in its state-of-the-art production facilities in Tamil Nadu for the expansion of its current product portfolio as well as new technologies.
- Valeo’s aftermarket operations will witness substantial growth through expanded distribution networks and service centers in Tamil Nadu.
- The expansion aims to provide enhanced accessibility to Valeo’s top-tier automotive products and services, catering to a wider customer base across the region.
- Jayakumar G, group president and managing director of Valeo India, stated that the company has been in Tamil Nadu for over 25 years and expanded its diverse product portfolios in manufacturing.
- Over the last 15 years, Valeo established a Global Tech Center for R&D and software activities in Chennai, citing it as testament to the local talent.
- Jayakumar G plans to expand operations and R&D in the upcoming years and thanked the government for its continued support, praising the State’s conducive business ecosystem and proactive industrial policies.
- At the Global Investors Meet 2024 (GIM 2024), Valeo’s stall in the French pavilion, in collaboration with Team France, will exhibit Four pillars of Valeo’s MoveUp Strategy: electrification acceleration, ADAS acceleration, lighting everywhere, and interior experience reinvention.
- The aftermarket specialist Valeo Service will display a wide range of product portfolio for the aftermarket segment at GIM 2024.
- In India, Valeo started its footprint in 1997 in Chennai for manufacturing Clutch and Friction Facing materials.
- Valeo established its first R&D Centre in Chennai in 2005 for Powertrain Systems.
- Valeo subsequently expanded with the Lighting Division, Thermal Systems, Wiper Division, Vision Systems Software Division, and Valeo Service.
- Valeo is working with all mobility actors to deliver solutions for electric mobility in India.
