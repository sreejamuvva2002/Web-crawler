# Great Dane Trailers

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Great Dane | Evans General Contractors](https://evansgeneralcontractors.com/projects/great-dane-trailers/)  ·  `page_055263`

## Facts

- Great Dane Trailers is one of the most recognizable trailer manufacturers in the world.
- They are known for the innovative design and manufacturing of several types of trailers including platform, refrigerated and dry freight trailers.
- Great Dane contracted with Evans to design-build their eighth plant.
- The eighth plant is a turn-key trailer manufacturing and distribution facility located in Statesboro, Georgia.
- The building spans 453,000 sf.
- The structure is a hybrid design composed of both conventional steel and pre-engineered structures along with concrete tilt-wall panels.
- Manufacturing areas included 124 specialty concrete equipment foundations that were isolated to reduce the transmission of harmonic vibrations to the adjacent structure and floor slab.
- The facility is climate controlled using an evaporative cooling system utilizing push/pull exhaust fans to maintain four air changes per hour.
- The building houses administrative offices, a product show room, foam storage silos and a hazardous materials area.
- The facility incorporates compressed air, weld gas, paint booths and process conveying systems.
- Three monorails and 24 bridge cranes, ranging from 2.5 to 13 tons, support production throughout the state-of-the-art facility.
- Evans provided design-build services and complete shell building construction with all finishes.
