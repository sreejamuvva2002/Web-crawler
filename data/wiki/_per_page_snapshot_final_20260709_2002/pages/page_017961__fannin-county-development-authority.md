# Fannin County Development Authority

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Zoning, Permitting & Inspections - Fannin County Development Authority](https://old.fannindevelopment.com/zoning-permitting-and-inspections/)  ·  `page_017961`

## Facts

- The City of Blue Ridge issues all land use and building permits to businesses locating within its city limits in accordance with city land use and zoning ordinances.
- There is no zoning in the unincorporated areas of Fannin County, as well as the Cities of McCaysville and Morganton.
- Businesses locating in the unincorporated areas of Fannin County, McCaysville, or Morganton must comply with Fannin County land use and building ordinances.
- All Fannin County ordinances and inspection requirements are available for downloading.
- The Fannin County Health Department handles inquiries regarding environmental health inspections, food service establishments, sewage, and tourist accommodations.
