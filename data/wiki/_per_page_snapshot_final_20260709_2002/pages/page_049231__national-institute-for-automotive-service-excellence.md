# National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence

**Type:** workforce_training  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** 2021-04-21

**Source:** [ASE Chairman’s Message Celebrates Critical Role Of Techs](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/ase-chairmans-message-celebrates-critical-role-of-techs/)  ·  `page_049231`

## Facts

- The National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (ASE) has released a message from Mark Polke, chairman of the ASE board of directors.
- Mark Polke serves as director of Bosch Workshop Concepts, North America for Robert Bosch LLC.
- ASE has undertaken initiatives including the expansion of Spanish testing.
- ASE has undertaken initiatives including military testing.
- ASE launched a newly redesigned ASE.com website that has enhanced and simplified the registration and certification process.
- June was introduced as “Automotive Service Professionals Month.”
- During the past year, ASE Certified professionals demonstrated why they are deemed essential by providing indispensable services to ensure vital vehicles stayed on the road and performed their important roles.
- Polke stated that these skilled individuals are critical to keeping the motoring public going.
