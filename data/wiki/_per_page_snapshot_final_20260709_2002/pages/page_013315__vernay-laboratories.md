# Vernay Laboratories

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-02-27

**Source:** [Vernay Invests in New Injection Silicone Molding Facility - Vernay](https://vernay.com/news-and-events/vernay-invests-in-new-injection-silicone-molding-facility/)  ·  `page_013315`

## Facts

- Vernay invested in a new injection silicone molding facility in Kennesaw.
- The facility initially features 10 advanced production presses.
- These initial presses occupy only a quarter of the facility’s capacity.
- Vernay plans to expand the facility by adding up to 45 presses in the near future.
- The facility currently boasts presses equipped with custom molds designed to cater to a wide range of manufacturing requirements.
- Vernay specializes in liquid silicone (LSR) molding, targeting markets where demand for high-quality, reliable components is paramount.
- Each state-of-the-art production cell can produce 64 parts every 32 seconds.
- The capacity for these production cells delivers over 200 million micro-molded parts per year.
- Part of the Kennesaw facility investment ensures ISO-13485 and Clean Room Class 8 compliance for the Medical market.
- Vernay Laboratories has been a technology leader in the design, development, and manufacture of customized flow-control components since its founding in 1946.
- The company formulates its own compounds to address each specific application.
- Vernay’s products play a vital role in millions of medical devices, automobiles, and consumer products.
- The privately held firm produces more than 1 billion components per year globally.
- Vernay draws from a library of more than 33,000 elastomeric materials and compound formulations.
- The company operates six plants around the world, located in the U.S., the Netherlands, Italy, and China.
