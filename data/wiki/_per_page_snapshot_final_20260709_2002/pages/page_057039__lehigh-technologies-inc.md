# Lehigh Technologies Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-07-18

**Source:** [Lehigh Opens Spanish Facility | Tyre and Rubber Recycling](https://www.tyreandrubberrecycling.com/articles/news/lehigh-opens-spanish-facility-tyre-and-rubber-recycling/)  ·  `page_057039`

## Facts

- Lehigh Technologies Inc. is a Georgia, USA-based producer of Micronised Rubber Powders (MRP) from end-of-life tyres and post-industrial rubber.
- Lehigh has completed its new MRP production facility in Murillo el Fruto, Spain.
- The plant is located in a village in the province of Navarra.
- The plant has an annual production capacity of 10,000 metric tons of MRPs.
- The Murillo el Fruto plant is Lehigh's first production facility outside of the U.S.
- The plant was commissioned in late June.
- The plant incorporates Lehigh’s proprietary cryogenic turbo mill technology to manufacture MRPs.
