# YKK AP America Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-28

**Source:** [YKK AP America Celebrates Opening of Newly | YKK AP](https://www.ykkap.com/ykk-ap-america-celebrates-opening-of-newly-expanded-commercial-and-residential-design-center/)  ·  `page_016112`

## Facts

- YKK AP America Inc. is a leading manufacturer of award-winning commercial facade systems and residential doors and windows.
- The company celebrated the opening of its newly expanded commercial and residential design center, Studio 101, on Thursday, June 22, 2023.
- Studio 101 is located at 101 Marietta Street in downtown Atlanta, Georgia.
- The design center was first launched in 2021 with a core focus on commercial architectural products and has been expanded to include residential windows and doors.
- Studio 101 includes a designated training center designed to provide customers and partners with hands-on training experiences for the proper installation of YKK AP’s entrances, storefronts, curtain walls, window walls, and window and door products.
- The training center is also designed as a shared space for the architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC) community to host their own training.
- The design center is co-located with YKK AP’s headquarters, which officially relocated from Austell to Atlanta last year.
- Oliver Stepe serves as president and CEO of YKK AP America Inc.
- Studio 101 is available on weekdays from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. by appointment only.
- YKK AP America Inc. operates world-class production facilities in Dublin, Georgia, Macon, Georgia, and Lakeshore, Ontario.
- Together with its subsidiary brand Erie Architectural Products (Erie AP), the company serves architects, glazing contractors, homebuilders, and resellers across the country.
- YKK AP America Inc. is a subsidiary of YKK AP Inc., a global architectural products company based in Tokyo, Japan.
