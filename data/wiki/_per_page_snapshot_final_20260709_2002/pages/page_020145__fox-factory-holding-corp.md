# Fox Factory Holding Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-11-05

**Source:** [Fox Factory Holding To Bring 800!! Jobs To Georgia | Georgia Public Broadcasting](https://www.gpb.org/blogs/georgia-at-work/2018/11/05/fox-factory-holding-bring-800-jobs-georgia)  ·  `page_020145`

## Facts

- Fox Factory Holding Corp. designs and manufactures performance-defining ride dynamics products for bikes, including traditional and motor versions.
- Fox Factory Holding Corp. will move its headquarters to Georgia.
- The company expects to create 800 jobs in Georgia.
- The company expects to invest $50 million in new facilities in Georgia.
- Hall County will be the location for Fox's new manufacturing, warehousing, distribution, and office space.
- Hall County is home to Lanier Technical College’s newest campus, which is one of the most advanced technical college campuses in the southeast.
- The first phase of the Hall County project will be completed in early 2020.
- Fox Factory Holding Corp. is expected to relocate its corporate headquarters from Scotts Valley, California, to its existing offices in Georgia by the end of 2018.
- It is not yet known when the jobs will come open.
