# Sewon Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-24

**Source:** [What is Brief History of Sewon Company? – businessmodelcanvastemplate.com](https://businessmodelcanvastemplate.com/blogs/brief-history/se-won-brief-history)  ·  `page_001368`

## Facts

- Sewon Company was founded in 1991 in Pyeongtaek-si, South Korea.
- The company specializes in manufacturing critical automotive parts and supplies major automotive manufacturers globally.
- Corporate headquarters are located at 56, Sandan-ro 64beon-gil, Pyeongtaek-si, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea.
- Early product focus included capacitors, radiator caps, and head brackets for air conditioning systems.
- As of March 31, 2025, Sewon Co., Ltd. reported a trailing 12-month revenue of $158 million.
- For fiscal year 2024, Sewon reported revenue of $149.98 million, EBITDA of $18.019 million, and net income of $5.893 million.
- Sewon Corporation (KOSDAQ:024830) recorded Q1 2025 revenue of 45.46 billion KRW, representing 8.09% growth.
- Trailing twelve-month revenue for Sewon Corporation reached 234.31 billion KRW, up 26.55% year-over-year.
- In 2024, annual revenue for Sewon Corporation was 230.91 billion KRW, showing 19.61% growth.
- Sewon Precision Industry Co., Ltd. (KRX:021820) reported trailing 12-month revenue of $116 million as of March 31, 2025, and fiscal year 2024 revenue of $121.76 million.
- Early product launches included oil filter modules, spin-on and ECO oil filters, air and fuel filters, air intake systems, and transmission, suction, and cabin filters.
- Sewon Precision Industry specializes in automotive stamping parts such as dash, radiator, and side inner panel assemblies, along with cowl crossbar assemblies.
- In February 2023, Sewon America announced an investment exceeding $300 million for a new manufacturing plant in Rincon, Georgia.
- The Rincon facility is set to begin operations in 2025 and will produce EV body parts for OEMs including Hyundai Motor Group’s Metaplant America.
- The new Rincon plant is expected to create 740 new jobs.
- This facility marks Sewon America's second U.S. location, following its first facility in LaGrange, Georgia which opened in 2008 and has since doubled in size.
- Sewon Precision Industry Co., Ltd. was founded in Daegu, South Korea in 1985, specializing in automotive body parts and molds.
- Sewon Co., Ltd. (Pyeongtaek) was listed on the KOSDAQ stock exchange in 2015.
- In March 2024, Sewon Co.,Ltd released full year 2024 earnings with an EPS of ₩147.
- The first shipment from the Rincon, Georgia facility to the new Hyundai Metaplant is expected in October 2024.
- New equipment installed at Sewon America-Rincon enables the production of lighter aluminum components.
- The shift to aluminum components aims to reduce vehicle weight and enhance safety by minimizing spark output during welding.
- Sewon is strategically focusing on electric vehicle (EV) components to meet growing demand from major clients like Hyundai Motor Group.
- The company faces challenges including maintaining profitability, adapting to rapid technological advancements, and managing global supply chain complexities.
- The Rincon facility is expected to support a total of 1,600 jobs in Georgia.
