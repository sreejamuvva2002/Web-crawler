# Yanfeng Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [| PressReleasePoint](https://www.pressreleasepoint.com/yanfeng-enters-agreement-adient-acquire-adient-asias-4999-percent-share-yanfeng-adient-seating-co)  ·  `page_016621`

## Facts

- Yanfeng entered into an agreement with Adient to acquire Adient Asia’s 49.99 percent share in the Yanfeng Adient Seating Co, Ltd (YFAS) joint venture in China.
- As part of this agreement, Yanfeng takes full ownership of the joint venture and all 16,000 employees.
- The joint venture includes 55 seat manufacturing facilities.
- The joint venture includes a full-service technical center and JARC (headliner company).
- Current and sourced future programs will transition to Yanfeng.
- YFAS was established in 1997.
- YFAS has developed independent and complete R&D and manufacturing systems in automotive seating with a wide range of OEM customers.
- YFAS products cover complete seats, frames, foam, and headliners.
- Gerald Jia, CEO of Yanfeng, stated that making YFAS a wholly-owned entity enhances capabilities and product offering as a full-service supplier through cross-business collaboration.
- The three seating technical centers in Shanghai, Hefei, and Chongqing will join Yanfeng’s existing global innovation and engineering network.
- This integration will offer product and innovation synergies including Interiors, Seating, Safety, and Cockpit Electronics.
- YFAS’ manufacturing locations in China, along with Yanfeng's existing seat manufacturing bases in Thailand, Serbia, and Mexico, will be consolidated to form a complete seat supply capacity.
- The consolidation aims to make the business fully globalized.
- The transaction includes equity share and controlling interest changes to other joint ventures held by Yanfeng and Adient in China.
- Yanfeng will continue its current JV relationship (Keiper) with Adient in mechanisms.
- Keiper is a consolidated entity under Yanfeng, focusing on seat mechanism components.
- The transaction is subject to regulatory approval.
- The transaction is expected to be completed in six months.
- During the transition period, Yanfeng will maintain close cooperation with Adient to ensure smooth business transition and continuity of program execution and delivery.
- Yanfeng remains committed to offering one-stop Smart Cabin solutions to global customers.
- Yanfeng is a leading global automotive supplier, focusing on interior, exterior, seating, cockpit electronics, and passive safety.
- Yanfeng has more than 240 locations and approximately 55,000 employees worldwide.
- The technical team of 4,200 experts is in 8 R&D centers and other regional offices.
- Yanfeng has complete capabilities including engineering and software development, styling, and test validation.
- Yanfeng focuses on Smart Cabin and lightweight technology to support automakers with future mobility and cabin solutions.
