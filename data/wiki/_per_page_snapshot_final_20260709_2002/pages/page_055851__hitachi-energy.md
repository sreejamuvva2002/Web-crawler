# Hitachi Energy

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 2025-09

**Source:** [Hitachi announces historic $1 billion USD manufacturing investment to power America’s energy future through production of critical grid infrastructure](https://www.hitachienergy.com/news-and-events/press-releases/2025/09/hitachi-announces-historic-1-billion-usd-manufacturing-investment-to-power-america-s-energy-future-through-production-of-critical-grid-infrastructure)  ·  `page_055851`

## Facts

- Hitachi Energy announced a historic $1 billion USD manufacturing investment to power America’s energy future through production of critical grid infrastructure.
- Power transformers are described as a linchpin technology for a robust and reliable electric grid and winning the AI race.
- Bringing production of large power transformers to the U.S. is critical to building a strong domestic supply chain and reducing production bottlenecks amid surging demand.
- A state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in South Boston, Virginia, will be established to produce large power transformers.
- The new facility will support applications including high-voltage transmission, power generation, AI data centers, and large-scale industrial applications.
- The facility will be built alongside Hitachi Energy’s existing campus in South Boston.
- This facility will become the largest manufacturing site for large power transformers in the United States.
- The South Boston facility alone will create more than 825 new jobs in Southside Virginia.
- The new positions will offer long-term employment opportunities in engineering, advanced manufacturing, and operations roles.
- The investment is part of a more than $9 billion USD global investment program, which is the largest in the industry.
- Hitachi Energy is expanding its manufacturing capacity, R&D, engineering, and partnerships globally to power the world’s energy system to be more reliable, secure, and resilient.
- CEO Andreas Schierenbeck stated that Hitachi Energy is uniquely positioned as the global leader in electrification to deliver critical power solutions for the American market.
- The company expressed gratitude for the leadership and support of the Trump administration, Governor Youngkin, Virginia’s General Assembly, and the Commonwealth’s congressional delegation in making the production capacity possible.
