# MöllerTech USA, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- MöllerTech is the premium partner within the MöllerGroup, acknowledged worldwide for the development and production of plastic parts in the automotive industry.
- The German automotive supplier selected Gray to provide design-build services for a new state-of-the-art manufacturing facility.
- The facility is located in Woodstock, AL.
- The facility covers 151,313 square feet.
- The project was accelerated and became operational in 8 months.
- The new plastic injection molding facility produces interior parts for Mercedes-Benz’s next generation SUVs.
- The plant serves as a flagship U.S. operation for the company.
- The plant is located just six miles from the Mercedes-Benz manufacturing campus in Vance, Alabama.
- The plant employs more than 200 people.
