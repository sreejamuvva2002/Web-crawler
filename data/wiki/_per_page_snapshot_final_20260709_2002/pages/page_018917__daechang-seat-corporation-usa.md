## Facts

- The expansion of Daechang Seat Corporation USA will bring hundreds of new jobs to East Alabama.
- Phenix City Mayor Eddie Low, city council members, and Governor Kay Ivey attended the groundbreaking ceremony for the company's expansion.
- The Phenix City Mayor and city council approved an incentive package earlier this year to support the international automotive supply company's manufacturing expansion in Phenix City.

- The new facility will be situated on 22 acres of land in the Phenix City Industrial Park.
- Construction plans for the new facility are scheduled to begin in 2023.
