# Nidec Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-31

**Source:** [Nidec Corporation - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/nidec)  ·  `page_058108`

## Facts

- Has world’s top market share of both brushless DC motors and motors for automotive power steering, according to the Company’s data.
- Products are grouped into precision small motors; products for automobiles; products for home appliances, commercial use, and industrial use; and parts for machine devices, electronics, and optics.
- Automotive products include automotive motors (electric power-steering motors, brake motors), in-vehicle cameras, control valves, and electric oil pumps.
- In the fiscal year that ended in March 2025, automotive products accounted for 25.5% of sales.
- Listed on the Prime Market of the Tokyo Stock Exchange as of March 31, 2025.
- Offers E-Axle and in-wheel motor products.
- Manufactures control valves, hydraulic solenoid valves, electric water pumps, electric oil pumps, and electric actuators.
- Produces DCT motors, power steering motors, millimeter wave radar units, and heat-conduction/dissipation devices.
- Nidec Motors & Actuators (Germany) GmbH produces motors, brake systems, seat adjustors, steering-wheel adjustors, engine cooling systems, and sunroof components.
- Nidec GPM produces electrical and mechanical water pumps, electrical and mechanical oil pumps, transmission oil pumps, switchable water pumps, fully variable water pumps, controlled water pumps, and thermal management modules.
- NIDEC INSTRUMENTS produces stepping motors, ultra wide angle lens units, wide angle lenses, super-wide angle lenses, precision plastic parts, head-up display mirror drive units, throttle actuators, and suspension actuators.
- NIDEC COMPONENTS produces polygon laser scanners and trimmer potentiometers.
- Nidec Mobility products include body control modules, power seat switches, seat-position and steering position controllers, seat temperature controllers, auto-light units, AC invertors, electric power steering units, DC-DC converters for idling stop systems and xEVs, OBC, DLCM, multi channel gateway ECUs, power window switches with anti-pinch functions, door control units, power tailgate controllers, occupant detection systems, non-contact stop lamp switches, and control boards for electric servo brake systems.
