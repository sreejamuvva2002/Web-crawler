# Volvo Group North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-02-21

**Source:** [Volvo Group to expand in High Point | News | hpenews.com](https://www.hpenews.com/news/volvo-group-to-expand-in-high-point/article_66328e57-4f54-564d-ad08-42f57855c49a.html)  ·  `page_014280`

## Facts

- One of High Point’s largest employers announced a $41 million expansion in the city.
- Volvo Group North America will construct a 62,000-square-foot addition to its U.S. Uptime Center at 8003 Piedmont Triad Parkway.
- The addition will house the global headquarters for Volvo Financial Services.
- The facility has a Greensboro mailing address but is located in the city of High Point.
- The facility serves as a customer service center for the company’s commercial truck lines and employs about 600 people.
- Volvo bought the 122,223-square-foot building and the 10.58 acres it sits on in December for $27.8 million.
- The building opened in 2014 and was previously leased by the company.
- The facility is part of Volvo Group North America’s corporate campus, most of which is located in Greensboro, just south of Interstate 40.
- Once the addition is complete early next year, Volvo Financial Services will relocate its 360 employees from its current leased office in Greensboro about 2 miles away.
- The addition will be connected by a glass atrium and two walkways, bringing the total size of the three-story building to about 185,000 square feet.
- Volvo Financial Services is the financing arm for customers of Mack Trucks and Volvo Trucks, as well as the company’s buses and construction equipment.
- Volvo Financial Services operates in 50 countries and has 1,700 employees.
- Volvo Group North America has about 3,100 total employees in the Triad region.
- Marcio Pedroso, Volvo Financial Services president, stated that moving Volvo Financial Services allows all Volvo Group entities in the Piedmont to be located on one campus.
- Co-location will enable deeper relationships with colleagues and help retain highly skilled, world-class jobs that support the local economy.
