# Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-29

**Source:** [Kautex Textron Secures Major BEV Battery Housing Deal with Top Automaker - The Manufacture Data](https://themanufacturedata.com/kautex-textron-secures-major-bev-battery-housing-deal-with-top-automaker/)  ·  `page_045483`

## Facts

- Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG (Kautex) is a subsidiary of Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT).
- Kautex announced a significant contract win with a leading global automotive OEM for the supply of a full-BEV thermoplastic composite lower battery housing unit.
- The ordered unit is part of Kautex’s Pentatonic battery enclosure portfolio, specifically engineered for electric and hybrid electric vehicles.
- The new order underscores Kautex’s growing influence in the e-mobility space and reinforces its position as a Tier One automotive supplier.
- The lower battery housing will be used in a major BEV platform that spans several vehicle brands.
- Designed using thermoplastic composite materials, the housing seals and protects the battery system while meeting rigorous structural and thermal performance standards.
- Performance standards include resistance to crush and bottom impact forces, as well as external and internal fire safety tests.
- Integration into multiple vehicle models demonstrates versatility and Kautex’s commitment to scalable EV solutions.
- The Pentatonic product line includes lightweight, customizable EV solutions constructed from thermoplastic composites or composite-metal hybrids.
- Thermoplastic composite construction enables weight reduction, contributing to improved vehicle efficiency and extended driving range.
- Use of high-functionality thermoplastic materials allows significant part integration, resulting in a simplified bill of materials and lower overall cost.
- The streamlined engineering reflects an industry trend toward multi-material platforms that blend cost-effectiveness, performance, and environmental sustainability.
- Thermoplastic composite construction enhances corrosion resistance, ensuring long-term durability without compromising structural integrity.
- Lower carbon footprint during production and vehicle operation supports global efforts to decarbonize the transportation sector.
- This contract represents Kautex’s second major business win for the Pentatonic product line.
- Kautex previously secured its first Pentatonic order in January 2023 for a full-BEV underbody protection skid plate, which went into serial production in 2024.
- The Pentatonic platform has earned multiple industry accolades, including the Society of Plastics Engineers (SPE) Grand Innovation Award in Europe and China’s Lingxuan and Gasgoo Innovation Awards.
- Kautex deployed three test vehicles featuring full-BEV and hybrid Pentatonic enclosures across different continents.
- Test units are located in the United States, Japan, and Europe, collectively logging over 100,000 miles.
- Trials serve as proof of concept for the robustness of Pentatonic enclosures under varying terrain and environmental conditions.
- Joerg Rautenstrauch serves as President and CEO of Kautex.
- Rautenstrauch stated the contract award marks a significant milestone and reflects dedication to providing cutting-edge solutions meeting highest standards of safety, performance, and sustainability.
- Kautex is headquartered in Bonn, Germany, and operates as a Tier One automotive supplier with a global manufacturing footprint encompassing 30 plants in 13 countries.
- Historically known for contributions to traditional and hybrid fuel systems, engine camshafts, and plastic industrial packaging.
- Proactively expanded offerings toward electrification and automation as the automotive sector shifts.
- Investing in development of thermoplastic composite and composite-metal hybrid battery systems, as well as advanced cleaning systems for assisted and autonomous driving.
- Strategic pivot positions Kautex as a comprehensive partner for automotive manufacturers transitioning to next-generation vehicle platforms.
