# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2025-02

**Source:** [Novelis Reports Third Quarter Fiscal Year 2025 Results - Metro Atlanta CEO](https://metroatlantaceo.com/news/2025/02/novelis-reports-third-quarter-fiscal-year-2025-results/)  ·  `page_047346`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is a leading sustainable aluminum solutions provider and the world leader in aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Net income attributable to common shareholders was $110 million in Q3 fiscal year 2025, down 9% year-over-year.
- Net income excluding special items was $119 million in Q3 fiscal year 2025, down 32% year-over-year.
- Adjusted EBITDA was $367 million in Q3 fiscal year 2025, down 19% year-over-year.
- Rolled product shipments totaled 904 kilotonnes in Q3 fiscal year 2025, down 1% year-over-year.
- Net sales for Q3 fiscal year 2025 increased 4% versus the prior year period to $4.1 billion, mainly driven by higher average aluminum prices.
- Continued strong demand for beverage packaging sheet was offset by lower automotive and specialty shipments in Q3 fiscal year 2025.
- Customers are increasingly asking for lower-carbon, higher-recycled-content aluminum solutions to reduce their carbon footprint.
- Novelis had a leading industry average of 63% recycled content in its products during the last fiscal year.
- Competition for scrap aluminum has intensified as others follow suit, creating significant pressure on scrap pricing and impacting financial results.
- Novelis is developing new technologies to expand the types of scrap inputs it can purchase for use in its system.
- Total capital expenditures were $1.2 billion for the first nine months of fiscal year 2025, representing a 22% increase versus the prior year period.
- Capital expenditures are primarily attributed to strategic investments in new rolling and recycling capacity under construction, most notably in the U.S. for Bay Minette.
- The company intends to fund strategic investments largely through internally generated cash flow while maintaining balance sheet discipline to stay in a net leverage ratio of approximately 3.5x during this investment cycle.
- Novelis had a total liquidity position of $1.6 billion as of December 31, 2024, consisting of $791 million in cash and cash equivalents and $790 million in availability under committed credit facilities.
- In January 2025, the company issued $750 million in senior unsecured notes due January 2030, with proceeds primarily used to repay outstanding borrowings under its ABL revolver.
- Steve Fisher serves as president and CEO of Novelis Inc.
- Devinder Ahuja serves as executive vice president and CFO of Novelis Inc.
