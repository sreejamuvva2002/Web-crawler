## Facts

- Clayton County is adding hundreds of jobs after snagging new locations for and expansions of national and international manufacturing companies.
- The new or expanding businesses include a supplier of eyeglasses (Glasses USA) and a business that stamps names on steel in cars (GSC Steel Stamping).
- Erica Rocker-Wills is the director of Clayton’s economic development department.
- Glasses USA and GSC Steel Stamping have begun operations in Clayton County.
- The two companies are adding more than 200 jobs between them.
- Erica Rocker-Wills stated: 'This is a tremendous win for Clayton County.'
- Erica Rocker-Wills said the companies searched the entire country before landing in Clayton County because they were not in the country at all.
- GSC Steel Stamping last year acquired an existing steel-stamping business already located in the county.
- GSC will add jobs because of expansion plans.
- The Clayton Board of Commissioners approved a fully taxable incentive package of up to $1.5 million.
- A total of $2,832,300 was referenced in connection with the approval.
