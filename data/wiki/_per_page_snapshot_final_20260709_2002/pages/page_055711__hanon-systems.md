# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hanon Systems Expands Manufacturing Capability in Canada](https://www.everythingpe.com/news/details/5514-hanon-systems-expands-manufacturing-capability-in-canada)  ·  `page_055711`

## Facts

- Hanon Systems is a leading global automotive thermal management supplier for electrified mobility.
- Hanon Systems announced a new manufacturing plant in Woodbridge, Ontario, Canada.
- The new Woodbridge facility includes approximately 284,200 square feet (26,400 square meters) of manufacturing space.
- The facility has room for future expansion based on business needs.
- The Woodbridge plant will be Hanon Systems' first North American location to produce electric scroll compressors.
- Electric scroll compressors are essential components for thermal management in hybrid and battery electric vehicles.
- Depending on customer demand, the facility can produce up to 900,000 electric compressors annually.
- At full production, the plant expects to employ nearly 300 hourly and salaried employees.
- President and co-CEO Nurdal Kücükkaya stated the expansion increases proximity to vehicle manufacturers and meets customer demand.
- Kücükkaya noted the expansion demonstrates commitment to growth and proves customer confidence in delivering electric compressors.
- President and co-CEO Subu Nagasubramony stated the company investigated several locations for the new plant.
- Nagasubramony cited partnership with the government of Ontario and investment development agency as a significant factor in the location decision.
- The company expressed pride in fostering local community development and economic growth in Woodbridge.
- Ontario Minister of Economic Development, Job Creation and Trade Vic Fedeli stated the province has all necessary elements to build future electric vehicles.
- Fedeli highlighted that leading global parts manufacturers like Hanon Systems continue to choose Ontario for expansion.
- Minister Vic Fedeli stated the investment will create 300 new local jobs for auto workers.
- Fedeli noted the project strengthens Ontario's reputation as a North American leader in automotive manufacturing.
- The new Canadian facility joins Hanon Systems' four existing electric compressor manufacturing operations in Pyeongtaek (Korea), Dalian and Changchun (China), and Portugal (Europe).
- Expanding production at the new site reinforces the company's competitiveness by producing key components for hybrid and battery electric vehicles.
- Company executives and local dignitaries gathered at a media event to announce the new plant.
- The new Woodbridge plant is expected to start production in the first half of 2025.
- Hanon Systems has a 35-year history in Canada.
- Hanon Systems' Belleville plant was established in 1989.
- The Belleville plant produces fluid transport products, HVAC modules, and electronic coolant valves for the North American market.
- The new Woodbridge plant is located 200 kilometers from the Belleville plant.
- The Woodbridge facility will create synergies with Hanon Systems' Americas Innovation Center in Novi, Michigan.
- Proximity to major automakers will allow the company to respond quickly to customer demands.
