# Kyungshin Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-31

**Source:** [Kyungshin Corporation - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/kyungshin)  ·  `page_056721`

## Facts

- Kyungshin Corporation manufactures automotive wire harnesses.
- The company supplies its products mainly to Hyundai Motor Company.
- In December 2004, Sumitomo Wiring Systems Ltd. and Sumitomo Electric Industries, Ltd. became shareholders of the company.
- Major shareholders as of December 31, 2024 include Kyungshin Holdings Co., Ltd. (50.0%), Sumitomo Electric Industries, Ltd. (30.0%), and Sumitomo Wiring Systems Ltd. (20.0%).
- The company's product portfolio includes connected components such as wiring harnesses, FPC modules, junction blocks, wire to wire connectors, wire to unit bundling parts, and joints.
- Eco-friendly products include ICCB (In Cable Control Box), charging couplers, power relay assemblies, V2L (Vehicle To Load) systems, HV wiring harnesses, flexible busbars, CDM, BEQ, B12V-48V converters, and gateways.
- Intelligent components include CCU (Communication Control Unit), ICU, Ethernet Service Unit (ESU), SPDU, VPMS modules, and B-LBM.
- The company began supplying wire harnesses for the Hyundai Pony in October 1974.
- Production of passenger car wire harnesses started at the Gyeongju Plant in October 1984.
- The company acquired ISO9001 certification for all wire harness plants in July 1997.
- Kyungshin Industrial Motherson Ltd. was established in India in October 1997.
- QS9000 certification was acquired for the wire harness plant in June 1999 and for all offices in July 2001.
- Kyungshin Cable Co., Ltd. was spun off in January 2000.
- A new plant was set up in Hwaseong, Gyeonggi-do in March 2000.
- Qingdao Kyungshin Electronic Co., Ltd. was established in China in July 2002.
- In June 2003, the company and Lear Corporation of the U.S.A. jointly established Honduras Electrical Distribution Systems, S. de R.L. de C.V. in Honduras.
- A new plant was set up in Gunsan, Jeollabuk-do in August 2003.
- In February 2004, the company established Lear-Kyungshin Sales and Engineering LLC in the U.S.A. as a joint venture with Lear Corporation of the U.S.A.
- The company acquired ISO/TS16949 certification in September 2004 and ISO14001 certification in October 2004.
- Headquarters and research center were relocated to Incheon in May 2005.
- Jiangsu Kyungshin Electronic Co., Ltd. was established in China in December 2005.
- Qingdao Kyungshin Electronic Co., Ltd. was expanded in October 2007.
- In April 2008, the company established Kyungshin America Corporation in Georgia, U.S.A.
- The company entered the connector business in February 2010.
- The company adopted its current name, Kyungshin Corporation, in September 2010.
- Qingdao Jimo Kyungshin Electronic Co., Ltd. was established in China in December 2010.
- Kyungshin (Cambodia) Corporation was established in May 2012.
- Anhui Kyungshin Electronic Co., Ltd. was established in China in September 2015.
- The company acquired ISO22301 certification in December 2017.
- PT. Dharma Kyungshin Indonesia (DKI) was established in Indonesia in 2020.
