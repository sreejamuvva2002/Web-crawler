# PPG Industries, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [PPG Paints | Oakwood, Georgia](https://www.carrolldaniel.com/case-study/ppg-paints-warehouse-expansion/)  ·  `page_058946`

## Facts

- PPG Industries, Inc. is a Fortune 500 company and global supplier of paints, coatings, and specialty materials.
- Carroll Daniel was selected as the Design-Builder for an addition to an existing PPG Manufacturing and R&D facility.
- The project involved designing and building a 66,500-square-foot addition to an existing and occupied PPG Manufacturing and R&D facility in Oakwood, Georgia.
- Construction included an addition and expansion of precast/tilt buildings.
- Construction included the installation of a new sprinkler system.
- The addition houses special PPG machinery, from robots that secure lids on paint cans to ones that tightly wrap a bundle of cans in plastic.
- The plant makes around 100,000 gallons of paint per day.
- The plant tints paint for special contractor orders.
- The plant ships all produced paint out to stores.
