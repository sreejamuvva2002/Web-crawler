## Facts

- ABC Technologies completed its acquisition of TI Fluid Systems plc (TIFS).
- The transaction was completed for an enterprise value of over £1.8 billion.
- The combined business will be rebranded as TI Automotive.
- TI Automotive is a Tier 1 manufacturer supplying market-leading safety and performance critical products and highly engineered components to the global automotive industry.
- The company has a propulsion agnostic portfolio positioned to support a diverse range of customers.
- Combined revenue for the new entity will be $5.4 billion.
- The combined workforce will consist of 34,600 employees in 26 countries.
- TI Automotive will be headquartered in Auburn Hills, Michigan.
- The business will be managed through a Regional Operating Model to ensure customer proximity and agility to respond rapidly to local market dynamics.
- Terry Campbell, President & CEO of ABC Technologies, will lead TI Automotive as its President & CEO.
- An Executive Leadership Team combining the extensive skills and expertise of both ABC and TIFS will support Campbell.
- TI Automotive has more than 100 years’ experience of solving customers’ safety, efficiency and environmental challenges.
- The company possesses a powertrain agnostic product portfolio, vertical integration, global footprint and customer proximity.
- TI Automotive was created through ABC Technologies’ acquisition of TI Fluid Systems in April 2025.
- TI Automotive is majority owned by certain of the affiliated funds of Apollo Global Management, Inc. and its subsidiaries.
- Funds managed by Oaktree Capital Management, L.P. own a minority equity interest in TI Automotive.
