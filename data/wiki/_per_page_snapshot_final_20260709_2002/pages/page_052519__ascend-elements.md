# Ascend Elements

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-15

**Source:** [Ascend Elements' Georgia facility earns ISO 9001:2015 certification](https://www.recyclingproductnews.com/article/42043/ascend-elements-georgia-facility-earns-iso-90012015-certification)  ·  `page_052519`

## Facts

- DQS Inc., an independent certification body, certified Ascend Elements' electric vehicle (EV) battery recycling facility in Covington, Georgia, to the ISO 9001:2015 standard for quality management systems.
- The certification process for the facility included onsite audits and extensive policy and documentation reviews.
- Andrew Aberdale, CFO/COO at Ascend Elements, described the certification as a major achievement for a startup organization in an emerging field like battery recycling.
- Andrew Aberdale stated that the ISO certification signals the company's commitment to quality, customer service, and rigorous safety procedures.
- The Ascend Elements facility in Covington can process 30,000 metric tons of used lithium-ion batteries and scrap per year.
- The 154,000-square-foot facility in Newton County contains battery discharge and disassembly processes, battery shredding, lithium extraction, and analytical testing capabilities.
