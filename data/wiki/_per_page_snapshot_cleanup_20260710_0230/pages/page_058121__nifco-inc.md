## Facts

- Nifco established a subsidiary named Nifco KTW America Corporation in Georgia, USA, in April 2015.
- The company is a major manufacturer and supplier of plastic fasteners for the Japanese auto industry.
- Nifco manufactures xEV powertrain components including breather units, LID modules, cooling pipes, filters/strainers, baffle plates/oil catch tanks, quick connectors, hydrogen combined piping for FCVs, inlet/outlets for FCVs, filters for FCVs, pressure relief valves, and battery spacers.
- Nifco produces ADAS components such as camera covers, camera brackets, front millimeter-wave radar brackets, corner radar brackets, corner radar covers, corner radar protectors, sonar brackets, and clearance sonar retainers.
- Nifco manufactures engine, fuel, and transmission parts including turbo ducts, engine oil separators, thrust washers/receivers, breather boxes, baffle plates/oil paths, engine oil strainers, oil control valve filters, quick connectors, cooling pipes, actuator inlets, filters, fill limit vent valves, inlet check valves, capless fillers, combo valves, rollover valves, baffle plates, nipples, lock nuts, push lifters, and outer handles.
- Nifco supplies interior automotive components such as overhead consoles, cup holders, ECU contract items, seat back tables, seat related items, center console lids, center consoles, glove compartments, under-seat space items, luggage compartment products, assist grips, glove compartment dampers, inner handles, tonneau covers, luggage handles, luggage related items, air vents, sun-visor holders, clip alternatives to Hog rings, sunglass holders, tether clips, seat hooks, trim storage, and fasteners.
- Nifco manufactures exterior automotive parts including corner seals, aerodynamics components, resin brackets, bumper retainers, quiet products, dust proofs, energy absorbers, plug holes, push lifters, lid modules, clips, clamps, fender seals, screw grommets, glass holders, cushion rubbers, shock absorbers, hole plugs, ECU brackets, outer handles, back door handles, deflectors, and fasteners.
- Nifco produces automotive fasteners such as push turn rivets, push rivets, anchor clips, box anchor clips, brush clips, trim clips, screw grommets, hole plugs, bands, bands with clips, weather strip clips, linkage bushings, protectors, hood bumper rubbers, garnish clips, mat fasteners, connectors, bolt caps, scrivets, canoe clips, turn fasteners, push pull rivets, double clips, Niflocks, pipe clamps, cable clips, purse locks, cable straps, rod holders, latches & strikes, and pair clamps.
- Nifco manufactures dampers including gear dampers, axis dampers, air dampers, linear dampers, latches, middle-torque gear dampers, and barrel dampers.
- Nifco produces electrical components for automotive applications including ECU boxes and power supply ports.
