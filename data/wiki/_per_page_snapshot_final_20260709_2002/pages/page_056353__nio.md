# Nio

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-05-24

**Source:** [Nio Doubles Production Outsourcing From JAC: 240,000 Cars/Year](https://insideevs.com/news/509270/nio-doubles-jac-production-deal/)  ·  `page_056353`

## Facts

- Nio outsources its vehicle production to JAC Motors.
- Nio plans to double its annual production volume from JAC from 120,000 to 240,000 cars per year.
- The production outsourcing contract between Nio and JAC was renewed for a term running from May 2021 through May 2024.
- Under the agreement, Nio is responsible for developing and engineering the vehicles, quality management and assurance, and supply chain management.
- JAC Motors handles parts assembly and operations management under the deal.
- JAC receives payment from Nio for each car it manufactures.
- JAC is entitled to use the regulatory credits generated from the production.
- Nio covers the costs for the vehicles, manufacturing taxes, and the amortization and depreciation of the production machines.
- Nio and JAC operate a joint venture named Jianglai Advanced Manufacturing Technology (Anhui) Co.
- Nio holds a 49 percent ownership stake in the Jianglai Advanced Manufacturing Technology joint venture.
- NeoPark is planned as a massive smart electric vehicle industry park that will include manufacturing and research & development activities.
- When fully operational, NeoPark aims to deliver 1 million EVs and 100 GWh of capacity per year.
- The target deadline for NeoPark reaching full operation is likely by 2024.
- On May 6, Nio announced that Norway would be the first international market for its vehicles.
- Nio vehicles will retain their battery pack swapping capability in the Norwegian market.
- The continuation of the Nio-JAC partnership depends on vehicle demand in China and worldwide.
