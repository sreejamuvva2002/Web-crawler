# TI Fluid Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [TI Fluid Systems agrees £1bn takeover by Canadian rival ABC Technologies  - The Mirror](https://www.mirror.co.uk/money/ti-fluid-systems-agrees-1bn-34213415)  ·  `page_011430`

## Facts

- ABC Technologies agreed to buy British car parts firm TI Fluid Systems in a £1bn deal.
- ABC is owned by investment giant Apollo.
- ABC offered 200p for each TI Fluid Systems share – representing a 53% premium to its closing share price on August 21, before ABC’s initial bid.
- The deal places TI Fluid System's market capital at £1.04bn.
- TI Fluid Systems has its roots in Oxford.
- TI Fluid Systems is known for making thermal management gear and fuel systems for vehicles including battery-electric, hybrid and internal combustion engine cars.
- TI Fluid Systems supplies its products around the world including the US, Canada, Europe, to China and India.
- ABC sees the deal as a way helping it to grow internationally.
- Tim Cobbold is TI Fluid Systems' chair.
- Tim Cobbold said: "The combination will result in a larger, more diversified business with a broader range of products and customers, better positioned to navigate the current challenges facing the automotive industry and deliver sustainable long-term growth."
