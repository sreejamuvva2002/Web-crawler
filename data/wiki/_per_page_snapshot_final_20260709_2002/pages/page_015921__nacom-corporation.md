## Facts

- Nacom Corporation (Yazaki) operates in the automotive sector in the Southern United States.
- The facility is located in Griffin, Georgia.
- The facility was completed in 1994.
- The initial facility size was 253,000 square feet.
- Subsequent expansions increased the size of the facility to almost 500,000 square feet.
- The facility functions as an automotive electronics parts manufacturing plant.
- Shimizu North America provided a complete turnkey project management approach to build the facility.
- Shimizu North America provided architectural design and construction management services for the project.
