# Daimler Truck North America LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-01-01

**Source:** [Angela Lentz named Chief People Officer for Daimler Truck North America | Daimler Truck North America](https://northamerica.daimlertruck.com/news-stories/2022/angela-lentz-named-chief-people-officer-for-daimler-truck-north-america)  ·  `page_053651`

## Facts

- Daimler Truck North America (DTNA) announced Angela Lentz has been appointed chief people officer (CPO) for DTNA and its affiliated companies in the United States, Canada, Mexico and Australia.
- Lentz joins DTNA’s Operating Committee (OpCom), along with the Daimler Truck AG Human Resources OpCom.
- In her role as CPO, Lentz will be responsible for all aspects of Human Resources, including performance management, learning and development, labor relations, corporate security and corporate real estate.
- Lentz began her career at DTNA in 1997 as a buyer in the purchasing department.
- She became director of project controlling in 2015.
- She subsequently served as director of procurement for mechatronics and Mexico truck and bus.
- In 2021, she took on the role of general manager of aftermarket and strategic value chain and technology.
- John O’Leary, president and CEO of Daimler Truck North America, stated that Lentz's broad-based experience ranges from finance to production to aftermarket, with extensive leadership experience and a drive to get things across the finish line.
- O’Leary noted Lentz's dedication to assembling a team that celebrates diversity.
- DTNA has over 26,000 talented employees who design, build, sell and service commercial vehicles.
- Lentz succeeds Eileen Frack, who will retire after 18 years at DTNA.
- Both executive moves will take effect on Jan. 1, 2023.
- Lentz will be located full-time in Portland, Oregon.
- Daimler Truck North America LLC is headquartered in Portland, Oregon.
- DTNA designs, engineers, manufactures and markets medium- and heavy-duty trucks, school buses, vehicle chassis and their associated technologies and components under the Freightliner, Western Star, Thomas Built Buses, Freightliner Custom Chassis Corp and Detroit brands.
- DTNA is a subsidiary of Daimler Truck Holding AG (DTG), one of the world’s leading commercial vehicle manufacturers.
- Angela Lentz earned a bachelor’s degree in Business Management and Communications from Concordia College.
