# Essex Solutions

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Mirae Asset invests $200 mn in LS Group affiliate in US  - KED Global](https://www.kedglobal.com/us/corporate-investment/newsView/ked202501060006)  ·  `page_010422`

## Facts

- South Korea's Mirae Asset Global Investments invested $200 million in Essex Solutions, a US magnet wire manufacturing affiliate under cable and industrial electric machinery conglomerate LS Group.
- Essex Solutions succeeded pre-IPO under a deal with a consortium consisting of Mirae Asset Global Management and KCGI.
- The consortium acquired a 20% stake in the company through a paid-in capital increase.
- Essex Solutions is the worldwide No. 1 company in the magnet wind market.
- Its predecessor, Essex, was an American wire company founded in 1930 and renamed Superior Essex (SPSX) in 1954 after acquiring the telecommunications wire business.
- LS Group acquired SPSX in 2008 with an investment of about 1 trillion won.
- In 2020, LS Group established Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire (EFMW), a joint venture specializing in windings with Japan's Furukawa Electric.
- In April last year, the company acquired all of EFMW's shares from Furukawa Electric and rebranded to Essex Solutions.
- Essex Solutions' main products are magnet windings for electronic devices such as transformers and motors.
- A market expected to grow rapidly in the future is special windings for electric vehicles, which are wound in coils around motors to convert electrical energy into kinetic energy.
- LS will soon select a listing organizer for Essex Solutions and plans to go public this year.
- Initially, the company had planned to list on the Kosdaq and is also considering listing on the Nasdaq due to growing interest from US investors.
