# Piedmont Resin Supply LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-01

**Source:** [Hexpol Acquires Majority Stake In Nylon Compounder Piedmont - Canadian Plastics](https://www.canplastics.com/materials/hexpol-acquires-majority-stake-in-nylon-compounder-piedmont/1003465318/)  ·  `page_020924`

## Facts

- Hexpol Group has acquired an 80 per cent stake in nylon compounder Piedmont Resin Supply LLC.
- The acquisition deal is valued at US$86 million.
- Piedmont is headquartered in Cartersville, Ga.
- Piedmont was founded in 2013.
- Piedmont has grown to approximately 60 employees.
- Piedmont services a variety of customers within automotive, transportation, and the furniture industries.
- Piedmont is said to be one of the largest independent nylon compounders in the U.S.
- Jan Wikström, president of Hexpol Thermoplastic Compounding, stated in an Oct. 31 news release that “the acquisition of Piedmont adds new capabilities, application knowhow and a new customer base to Hexpol Thermoplastic Compounding in the U.S.”
- Hexpol Group is headquartered in Malmo, Sweden.
- Hexpol Group is said to be the world’s largest rubber compounder.
- Hexpol is organized into two business areas: Hexpol Compounding and Hexpol Engineered Products.
