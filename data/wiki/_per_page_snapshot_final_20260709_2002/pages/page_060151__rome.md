# Rome

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2003-10-14

## Facts

- Members of Atlanta’s Foreign Trade Representative Group recently visited Rome and Floyd County to observe their success in attracting foreign-based manufacturers.
- The region has successfully attracted foreign-based manufacturers producing a variety of products, including all-terrain vehicles and auto parts.
- The region's economic development success is attributed to a commitment by city, county, and corporate officials to bring technology-based manufacturers to the area.
