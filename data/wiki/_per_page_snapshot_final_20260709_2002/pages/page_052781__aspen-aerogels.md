# Aspen Aerogels

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-02

**Source:** [Taxpayer dollars impacted after Aspen Aerogels abandons Bulloch County](https://www.wtoc.com/2025/04/02/taxpayer-dollars-impacted-after-aspen-aerogels-abandons-bulloch-county/)  ·  `page_052781`

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels abandoned a project consisting of a combined 320,000 square feet across two buildings in the Bruce Yawn Commerce Park in Bulloch County in February.
- The planned Aspen Aerogels plant building was projected to be a $3.7 million investment.
- The project was expected to create 250 jobs for Bulloch County residents.
- Aspen Aerogels was intended to become the largest company ever located in Bulloch County.
- Since February, local officials have been negotiating clawbacks with Aspen Aerogels to recover invested taxpayer funds after the company decided not to move in.
- The city of Statesboro anticipates receiving a $741,000 refund this week.
- The refunded $741,000 originated from a Georgia Department of Community Affairs grant intended as an incentive for hiring local Bulloch County employees.
- Statesboro officials followed a Georgia Department of Community Affairs (DCA) agreement requiring Aspen Aerogels to repay a $750,000 grant after deciding not to move forward.
- Statesboro City Manager Charles Penny stated that $500,000 in taxpayer funds will not be reimbursed until another company utilizes the infrastructure.
- The unreimbursed $500,000 was originally allocated to fund water and sewer lines for the Aspen Aerogels plant.
- City Manager Charles Penny described the unrecovered funds not as a loss but as a "temporary pause" in potential revenue, contingent on future industry utilization of the built infrastructure.
- Benjy Thompson of the Bulloch Development Authority expressed confidence that another large company will lease the pre-built infrastructure once it becomes available.
- The presence and success of multiple Hyundai suppliers located next door in the same commerce park are expected to attract a new tenant.
