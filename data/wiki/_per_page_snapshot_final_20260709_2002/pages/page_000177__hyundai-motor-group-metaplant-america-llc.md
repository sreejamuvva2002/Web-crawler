## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) is currently accepting applications for Production Team Member positions.
- HMGMA currently has open positions for Maintenance Team Members.
- HMGMA currently has open positions for Salaried/Administrative Positions.
- The facility offers competitive wages and bonus potential to employees.
- 93% of Medical, Dental, and Vision insurance is company-paid for Meta Pros and their families, effective the first day.
- HMGMA provides company-paid Life Insurance, AD&D, Short-Term Disability, and Long-Term Disability coverage.
- Employees receive 15 paid company holidays, comprising 10 federal holidays and a 5-day winter shutdown.
- Staff members are eligible for up to 23 days of paid time off, including up to 20 days of vacation based on length of service plus up to 3 days of emergency PTO.
- HMGMA offers a 401(k) savings plan that matches 100% of the first 6% contributed by the employee.
- Employees receive discounts on new Hyundai and Genesis vehicles.
- The facility includes an on-site cafeteria and clinic, plus a free limited amount of team wear.
- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America, LLC (HMGMA) is Hyundai Motor Group’s first dedicated mass-production Electric Vehicle plant.
- The all-new manufacturing facility is located just outside of Savannah, Georgia.
- The plant will feature a highly-connected, automated, and flexible manufacturing system.
- All production processes—order collection, procurement, logistics, and production—will be optimized utilizing AI and data.
- The innovative manufacturing system is designed to create a human-centered work environment with robots assisting human workers.
- The plant will build more than 300,000 vehicles per year.
- Production will include Hyundai's luxury brand Genesis.
- HMGMA refers to its facility as the Metaplant and its employees as Meta Pros.
- HMGMA has a policy prohibiting discrimination, harassment, and retaliation against team members, job applicants, or other individuals based on race, color, religion, gender, sex, sexual orientation, pregnancy, marital status, age, national origin, disability, genetic/family medical history, service in the uniformed services, filing for bankruptcy, engaging in concerted protected activity, filing a workers’ compensation claim, exercising rights under the Family and Medical Leave Act of 1993 (“FMLA”) and/or the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 (“OSHA Act”), or other legally protected activities.
