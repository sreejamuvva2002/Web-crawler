## Facts

- Yachiyo Manufacturing of America is an automotive parts manufacturing facility located in Carrollton, Georgia.
- The facility covers 96,753 square feet.
- Shimizu North America managed the design and construction of this greenfield project in Carrollton, GA.
- The fast-track project included warehousing, manufacturing, and office space.
- Shimizu designed and engineered the process utilities systems for the client's process equipment.
- The facility was completed in 2014.
- The project is located in the Southern United States.
