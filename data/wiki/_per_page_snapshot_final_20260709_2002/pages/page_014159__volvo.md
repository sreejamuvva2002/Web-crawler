# Volvo

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Volvo Announces Tech Updates for Full 2026 Lineup | Cars.com](https://www.cars.com/articles/volvo-announces-tech-updates-for-full-2026-lineup-510575/)  ·  `page_014159`

## Facts

- Volvo has ambitious plans to become a fully electric automaker by 2030.
- For the 2026 model year, Volvo is making adaptive cruise control standard across its entire lineup.
- Volvo’s next-generation infotainment tech is undergirded by Qualcomm Technologies’ Snapdragon Cockpit Platform.
- The new computing platform provides processing power more than twice as fast as prior tech and generates graphics up to 10 times faster.
- Volvo notes that making its screens more responsive decreases distraction and creates safer drivers.
- The latest version of Volvo Car UX will be standard across the Volvo lineup for 2026.
- The new interface features reconfigured menus that keep more functions visible and accessible as the driver navigates the vehicle’s system.
- Volvo will distribute the next-generation UX software to vehicles equipped with Google Built-In via an over-the-air update later this year.
- Volvo will offer the 2026 XC40 with the Black Edition styling package.
- The Black Edition package blacks out the wheels, exterior trim, and badging.
- Volvo is expanding the Black Edition package’s availability to Crystal White, Vapour Grey, and Denim Blue for both the XC40 and XC60.
