# Murata Electronics North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Murata Unveils Latest Additions To Its Temperature Sensing Thermistor Line](https://www.autocentral.com/doc/murata-unveils-latest-additions-to-its-0001)  ·  `page_047085`

## Facts

- Murata Electronics North America is located in Smyrna, GA.
- The company recently announced the addition of the PRF and NCP series to its temperature sensing thermistor line.
- Both series were developed to meet design flexibility, reliability, standardization, and cost savings needs expected by engineers in the automotive market.
- The PRF series consists of PTC (positive temperature coefficient) thermistors ideal for high accuracy overheat sensing.
- The NCP series consists of NTC (negative temperature coefficient) thermistors created for temperature measurement with high accuracy over wide temperature ranges.
- The PRF series offers quick and easy design for repeatable overheat sensing and provides resettable protection in the circuit because there is no hysteresis.
- The NCP series offers the same resistance and B constant in several different package sizes that allows for convenient redesign and cost reduction.
- Both series are surface mount series with operating temperatures in the range of -40°C to +125°C for commercial grade and up to +150°C for automotive grade.
- Both series are suitable for lead-free soldering.
- Both series meet ELV/UL and AEC-Q200 requirements.
- Both series utilize a very sturdy construction that makes them resistant to mechanical vibrations and shock.
- Mitch Nozaki, Product Manager, stated that as the inventor of ceramic thermistors, Murata continuously develops fine ceramic materials and firing processes to produce products meeting designers' needs.
- Mitch Nozaki noted that Murata has provided the automotive design community with products needed to regulate detrimental temperature variances.
- Mitch Nozaki stated that Murata has secured the most market share in providing these products.
