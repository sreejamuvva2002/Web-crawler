## Facts

- ALBAform is a woman-owned manufacturing company in the automotive industry.
- ALBAform has operations in Flowery Branch, Georgia, Czechia, and Mexico.
- ALBAform's clients include BMW, Ford Motor Co., and Volvo.
- ALBAform is a client of the Enterprise Innovation Institute’s Georgia Manufacturing Extension Partnership.
- The Georgia Manufacturing Extension Partnership assisted ALBAform with leadership and business strategy consulting, and workforce development.
- Chasten McCrary, a strategy consultant with the Enterprise Innovation Institute’s chief of staff, serves as a student consultant for ALBAform through Georgia Tech’s Scheller College of Business International Practicum Course.
- McCrary is a second-year Evening MBA student selected to partake in the program.
- The Bergmark Family Study Abroad Scholarship awarded to McCrary is named for Dick Bergmark (IM ‘75, Hon. PhD ’22), one of the Scheller College of Business’ main philanthropists and immediate past chair of the school’s advisory board.
