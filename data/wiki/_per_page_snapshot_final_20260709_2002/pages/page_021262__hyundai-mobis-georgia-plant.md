## Facts

- The Hyundai MOBIS Georgia Plant is located in West Point, GA.
- The plant uses cutting-edge vehicle modularization technology to assemble and supply chassis modules, cockpit modules, and front-end modules.
- The assembled modules are supplied to the KIA Georgia factory.
- Team members also assemble front and rear bumpers in a just-in-sequence manufacturing environment.
- The company's core values are Customer First, Daring Execution, Communication & Cooperation, Respect for People, and Go Global.
- Employees receive competitive wages and benefits including paid vacation and personal time off.
- Employees receive 15 paid holidays per year.
- The company offers a 401(k) plan with a company match.
- Health, dental, and vision insurance are provided to employees.
- Life insurance and disability insurance are included in the benefits package.
- Tuition assistance is available for team members.
- Mobis Georgia provides a confidential and anonymous Red Flag Reporting avenue for team members, business associates, and community partners to report ethics, safety, and fraud issues.
- The company offers an employee referral form that sends an email to referred friends about potential career opportunities.
