## Facts

- Lyle Industries, Inc. is a manufacturer of broadloom tufted, coated and finished automotive carpets for molded flooring, interior trim and floor mats.
- The company is fast becoming a premier supplier of automotive textile carpet for molded flooring, interior trim, and floor mats.
- Lyle Industries was founded in 1981 by Lamar and Loutrelle Lyle.
- The company is located in Dalton, Georgia.
- Lyle Industries uses a variety of yarn systems, substrate fabrics, coating applications, and innovative manufacturing techniques to supply carpet used in many of today's automobiles.
