# YKK AP America Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-14

**Source:** [YKK AP Releases 2024 Global Integrated Report - YKK AP](https://www.ykkap.com/ykk-ap-releases-2024-global-integrated-report/)  ·  `page_016224`

## Facts

- Released its annual report, “YKK AP Integrated Report 2024”, on Nov. 14, 2024, to communicate progress to stakeholders.
- Progress supports the organization’s global sustainability goals, “Evolution 2030.”
- Committed $330 million globally through 2030 for renewable energy, efficient equipment, and automation.
- Investments include YKK AP America’s new Residential facility and planned facilities upgrades to achieve an 80% reduction in global Scope 1 and 2 emissions by 2030.
- 80% of YKK AP America’s aluminum is now sourced from low-carbon North American suppliers, lowering Scope 3 emissions in line with a goal to cut these by 30% by 2030.
- Recycles 100% of in-house aluminum scrap, with 30% recycled content in its aluminum billets to support a sustainable circular model.
- Develops high-insulation, energy-saving products designed to reduce energy use and carbon emissions in buildings.
- Prioritizes responsible procurement and environmentally conscious material sourcing throughout the supply chain.
- President and CEO Oliver Stepe and Chief HR Officer Patrys Wiid highlighted the CARE Conversations program as essential to fostering an engaged workplace.
- Headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- Manufactures building products at world-class production facilities located in Dublin, Ga., Macon, Ga., and Lakeshore, Ontario.
- Operates subsidiary brand Erie Architectural Products (Erie AP).
- Serves architects, glazing contractors, homebuilders, and resellers across the country.
- Is a subsidiary of YKK AP Inc., a global architectural products company based in Tokyo, Japan.
- Offers commercial facade systems and residential doors and windows.
