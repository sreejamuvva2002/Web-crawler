## Facts

- Trenton Pressing made a $5,000 donation to Trenton Fire Department and Trenton City Police on Dec. 20.
- Hans Pfunder is the owner of Trenton Pressing.
- Two years ago, Hans Pfunder, his father, and two other gentlemen took over the company formerly known as Gill Industries.
- The plant was about to shut down completely when taken over, having previously laid off 220 people.
- The new ownership turned the bankrupt company into a very profitable company.
- Many of Trenton Pressing's employees are from Trenton and Dade County.
- Mandy Whitehead serves as the HR manager at Trenton Pressing.
- Randall Hamilton serves as the plant manager at Trenton Pressing.
- Mayor Alex Case of Trenton attended the donation event.
