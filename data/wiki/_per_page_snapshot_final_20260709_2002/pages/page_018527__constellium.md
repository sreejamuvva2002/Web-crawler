# Constellium

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-11-24

**Source:** [Constellium To Create 150 Jobs in Bartow County | Cartersville, GA Patch](https://patch.com/georgia/cartersville/constellium-create-150-jobs-bartow-county-0)  ·  `page_018527`

## Facts

- Constellium, a global leader and manufacturer of aluminum products for the automotive industry, will set up shop in Bartow County.
- The company is set to create 150 new jobs in the local community.
- The project involves an overall investment of $32 million with the opening of its manufacturing plant.
- Governor Deal announced the job creation and investment on Tuesday, November 24, 2015.
- Constellium creates solutions aiming to reduce vehicle mass while advancing safety, comfort, and performance.
- The company's portfolio includes advanced crash management systems, side impact beams, front-end structures, lower path energy absorption systems, and additional automotive structures.
- Eric Krepps, vice president and general manager of Constellium Automotive Structures North America, stated that Georgia has a strong business climate and an advanced technical education network to provide a skilled workforce for their operations.
- Constellium operates more than 20 manufacturing sites in North America, Europe, and Asia.
- The company’s world-class technology center is located in Voreppe, France.
- The project represents a $20 million investment by Constellium.
- An additional $12 million investment is provided by developer Seefried Properties.
- Bartow County Commissioner Steve Taylor welcomed Constellium to Highland 75 Corporate/Industrial Park.
- The automotive sector is strategically important in Bartow County, situated among many southeast original equipment manufacturers.
