## Facts

- Apex Connect has partnered with Superior Essex for the Middle East and Turkey region.
- Superior Essex has been an industry leader for more than 90 years.
- Superior Essex creates and innovates wire and cable requirements for the industrial, automotive, commercial, energy, residential, and communications markets.
- Superior Essex continually expands its global product portfolio.
- Superior Essex produces new manufacturing processes and drives industry change throughout Europe, North America, and Asia.
- Superior Essex main aim is to continually set – and then raise – the industry benchmark for product sustainability in the telecommunications industry.
- Their cabling products are manufactured sustainably and have been specifically designed to minimize environmental impact.
- Their cabling products contribute to creating sustainable intelligent buildings.
- Their cabling products help projects earn the leading, green building certifications including LEED, WELL and Living Building Challenge.
