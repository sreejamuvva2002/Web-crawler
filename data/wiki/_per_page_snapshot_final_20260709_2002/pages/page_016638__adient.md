# Adient

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-01-23

**Source:** [Adient announces breakthrough ModuTec innovation](https://adient.prezly.com/adient-announces-breakthrough-modutec-innovation)  ·  `page_016638`

## Facts

- Adient announced its latest breakthrough innovation, ModuTec, on January 23, 2026.
- ModuTec is a modular seat design solution intended to greatly simplify the seat build process and enable higher levels of automation.
- ModuTec was developed after in-depth analysis of the needs of automakers, manufacturing locations and consumers.
- With the ModuTec assembly process, build of the seat module happens offline and is then seamlessly sequenced into the main JIT line, reducing assembly time from minutes to seconds.
- The ModuTec solution can help achieve higher efficiency, lower costs, and enable automation in ways the industry has not yet seen.
- Traditional manufacturing of vehicle seats relies on complex and integrated processes that are labor-intensive.
- ModuTec’s use of modularity revolutionizes this approach to enhance assembly efficiency.
- ModuTec simplifies production workflows for automotive seating.
- ModuTec enables automation and reduces floor space requirements.
- ModuTec enhances seat comfort.
- ModuTec enables modularity and component integration.
- ModuTec allows for craftsmanship and warranty improvements.
- ModuTec enables easier serviceability and dealer upgrades.
- Pierre Alain Caillaut, Adient’s Vice President of Engineering EMEA, stated that ModuTec introduces a modular seating approach that significantly improves assembly efficiency while driving overall cost optimization.
- Caillaut noted that by standardizing interfaces and clearly structuring individual assembly steps, ModuTec creates a scalable, automation-ready manufacturing concept that reduces process complexity and can integrate OEM-specific features in a controlled and cost-effective way.
- Adient is a global leader in automotive seating with more than 65,000 employees in 29 countries.
- Adient operates approximately 200 manufacturing/assembly plants worldwide.
- Adient produces and delivers automotive seating for all major OEMs.
- Adient's expertise spans every step of the automotive seat-making process, from research and design to engineering and manufacturing.
- Adient delivers its products into millions of vehicles every year.
