# Kyungshin Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-31

## Facts

- Kyungshin Corporation manufactures automotive wire harnesses.
- The company supplies its products mainly to Hyundai Motor Company.
- Sumitomo Wiring Systems Ltd. and Sumitomo Electric Industries, Ltd. became shareholders in December 2004.
- As of December 31, 2024, Kyungshin Holdings Co., Ltd. holds a 50.0% interest ratio.
- As of December 31, 2024, Sumitomo Electric Industries, Ltd. holds a 30.0% interest ratio.
- As of December 31, 2024, Sumitomo Wiring Systems Ltd. holds a 20.0% interest ratio.
- The company produces Connected products including Wiring Harness, FPC Module, Junction Block, Wire to Wire Connector, Wire to Unit Bundling Parts, and Joint.
- The company produces Eco-Friendly products including ICCB (In Cable Control Box), Charging Coupler, Power relay assemblies, V2L (Vehicle To Load), HV Wiring Harness, Flexible Busbar, CDM, BEQ, and B12V-48V Convertor.
- The company produces Intelligent products including CCU (Communication Control Unit), ICU, Ethernet Service Unit (ESU), SPDU, VPMS module, B-LBM, and Gateway.
- In April 2008, the company established Kyungshin America Corporation in Georgia, U.S.A.
- The company began supply of wire harnesses for the Hyundai Pony in October 1974.
- The company acquired ISO9001 certification for all wire harness plants in July 1997.
- The company established Kyungshin Industrial Motherson Ltd. in India in October 1997.
- The company acquired QS9000 certification for its wire harness plant in June 1999.
- The company established Kyungshin Cable Co., Ltd. as a spun-off company in January 2000.
- The company set up a new plant in Hwaseong, Gyeonggi-do in March 2000.
- The company acquired QS9000 certification for all offices in July 2001.
- The company established Qingdao Kyungshin Electronic Co., Ltd. in China in July 2002.
- In June 2003, the company and Lear Corporation of the U.S.A. jointly established Honduras Electrical Distribution Systems, S. de R.L. de C.V. in Honduras.
- The company set up a new plant in Gunsan, Jeollabuk-do in August 2003.
- In February 2004, the company established Lear-Kyungshin Sales and Engineering LLC in the U.S.A. as a joint venture with Lear Corporation of U.S.A.
- The company acquired ISO/TS16949 certification in September 2004.
- The company acquired ISO14001 certification in October 2004.
- In May 2005, the company relocated its headquarters and research center to Incheon.
- The company established Jiangsu Kyungshin Electronic Co., Ltd. in China in December 2005.
- The company expanded Qingdao Kyungshin Electronic Co., Ltd. in October 2007.
- In February 2010, the company entered into the connector business.
- In September 2010, the company adopted a new company name, Kyungshin Corporation.
- The company established Qingdao Jimo Kyungshin Electronic Co., Ltd. in China in December 2010.
- The company established Kyungshin (Cambodia) Corporation in Cambodia in May 2012.
- The company established Anhui Kyungshin Electronic Co., Ltd. in China in September 2015.
- The company acquired ISO22301 certification in December 2017.
- The company established PT. Dharma Kyungshin Indonesia (DKI) in Indonesia in 2020.
