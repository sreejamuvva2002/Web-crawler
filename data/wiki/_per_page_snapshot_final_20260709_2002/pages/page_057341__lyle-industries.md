## Facts

- Premier supplier of automotive textile carpet for molded flooring, interior trim, and floor mats across OEM, Aftermarket, and Accessory markets.
- Provides tufting, precision cutting, and engineered finishing solutions.
- Has 34.5 million square yards of annual tufting capacity for automotive and specialty markets.
- Utilizes multiple gauge configurations including 1/10 Gauge, 5/64 Gauge, loop machines, high-speed creel, and beam systems tuned to precise OEM specifications.
- Uses Lectra CNC and press-based cutting systems including Bruno FH350 Ton Press, Bruno FH350T Press, and Freeman Schwabe 300 Ton Press for repeatable dimensional accuracy.
- Employs advanced latex and polyethylene coating processes tailored to exact performance requirements.
- Offers thermal bonding, moisture barriers, and specialty finishes configured to application and market specification.
- Products are engineered to meet durability and compliance standards for passenger cars, light trucks, and heavy transport vehicles.
- Is certified to ISO 9001:2015 with manufacturing operations structured around documented quality systems and traceability.
- Actively evaluates material inputs and production methods to reduce waste, improve recyclability, and align with evolving OEM sustainability requirements.
- Sponsors charities supporting healthcare, youth vocation, and community improvement throughout its operating regions.
- Is a Georgia employer committed to safe working conditions, fair labor practices, and contributing to the Dalton region's manufacturing workforce.
- Was founded in 1981 by Lamar and Loutrelle Lyle.
- Is headquartered in Dalton, Georgia.
- Its leadership team brings together decades of automotive textile manufacturing expertise, operational discipline, and deep industry relationships.
