## Facts

- Volvo Group North America represents about 29% of total 2025 sales for the Volvo Group.
- The company employs more than 19,100 people in North America.
- The company operates 16 manufacturing and remanufacturing facilities in the U.S., Canada, and Mexico.
- The company is driving the transition to sustainable and safe transport, mobility, and infrastructure solutions in North America.
- The company is making the journey to become a net-zero society.
- Volvo Group has made significant strides in North America.
- The company operates the largest R&D site for Volvo Group Trucks.
