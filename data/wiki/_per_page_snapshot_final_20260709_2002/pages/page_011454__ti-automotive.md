# TI Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2025-05-14

**Source:** [TI Fluid Systems to merge with ABC Technologies](https://truckandbusbuilder.com/article/2025/05/14/ti-fluid-systems-to-merge-with-abc-technologies)  ·  `page_011454`

## Facts

- TI Fluid Systems plc, a supplier of thermal management and fluid systems based in Oxford, UK, has been acquired by Canadian automotive parts firm ABC Technologies.
- The two companies plan to merge to form a single company called ‘TI Automotive’.
- The acquisition was announced late last year and completed for a sum of over GBP1.8bn.
- Following the merger, TI Automotive will move its headquarters to Auburn Hills, Michigan.
- The Auburn Hills location was originally founded in 1922 as “Harry Bundy & Co”.
- The combined company will have an annual turnover of USD5.4bn.
- The combined company will have 34,600 employees across 26 countries.
