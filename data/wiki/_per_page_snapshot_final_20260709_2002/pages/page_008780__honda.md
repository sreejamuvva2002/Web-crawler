# Honda

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Honda began manufacturing in America in 1979, opening its first plant in Marysville, Ohio.
- Honda currently manufactures products at 12 plants across six states, including 8 auto manufacturing plants.
- In 2024, nearly two-thirds of the vehicles Honda sold in America were built in the U.S.
- Honda products are built using U.S. and global parts.
- Over 40+ years, Honda and Acura have built 32 million vehicles in America.
- The Honda Accord is manufactured in Marysville, OH.
- The Honda Accord Hybrid is manufactured in Marysville, OH.
- The Honda Civic Hatchback is manufactured in Greensburg, IN.
- The Honda Civic Hatchback Hybrid is manufactured in Greensburg, IN.
- The Honda CR-V is manufactured in East Liberty, OH and Greensburg, IN.
- The Honda CR-V Hybrid is manufactured in East Liberty, OH and Greensburg, IN.
- The Honda Passport is manufactured in Lincoln, AL.
- The Honda Pilot is manufactured in Lincoln, AL.
- The Honda Ridgeline is manufactured in Lincoln, AL.
- The Honda Odyssey is manufactured in Lincoln, AL.
- The Acura Integra is manufactured in Marysville, OH.
- The Acura RDX is manufactured in East Liberty, OH.
- The Acura MDX is manufactured in East Liberty, OH.
- The Honda Talon Sport Side-by-Side is manufactured in Timmonsville, SC.
- The Honda Pioneer Side-by-Side is manufactured in Timmonsville, SC.
- The Honda FourTrax ATV is manufactured in Swepsonville, NC.
- Honda Snow Blowers are manufactured in Swepsonville, NC.
- Honda Mini-Tillers are manufactured in Swepsonville, NC.
- Honda V-6 Auto Engines are manufactured in Lincoln, AL and Anna, OH.
- Honda L4 Auto Engines are manufactured in Anna, OH.
- The Honda 2-Motor Hybrid System is manufactured in Russells Point, OH.
- Honda Auto Transmissions are manufactured in Tallapoosa, GA.
- Honda Auto Gear Sets are manufactured in Russells Point, OH.
- The HondaJet Elite II Aircraft is manufactured in Greensboro, NC.
- Honda Aero Aircraft Engines are manufactured in Burlington, NC.
- Honda has been building powersports products in America for more than 45 years.
- North Carolina is the exclusive production source for Honda ATVs in North America and South Carolina is the exclusive production source for Honda side-by-side vehicles globally.
- In 2024, Honda produced 116,800 power equipment units in the U.S.
- In 2024, Honda produced 37,000 powersports products in the U.S.
- The global headquarters for the Honda Aircraft Company is located in Greensboro, North Carolina, where associates have produced light duty jets since 2006.
- Honda Aircraft Company’s headquarters house design, engineering, and production facilities in a single, integrated campus.
- Honda is reimagining its approach to advanced auto production with flexible manufacturing that leverages human-friendly and sustainable practices to build gas, hybrid, and electric vehicles on the same production lines.
- Last year, Honda invested over $1 billion to retool three major auto plants in Ohio to serve as the foundation for Honda’s flexible manufacturing approach globally.
