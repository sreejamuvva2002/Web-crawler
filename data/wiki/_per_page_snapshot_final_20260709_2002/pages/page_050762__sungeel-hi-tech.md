## Facts

- South Korean battery recycler Sungeel Hi-tech is planning its first U.S. recycling plant for lithium-ion batteries in Georgia.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp made an announcement regarding the project in August.
- Sungeel plans to invest more than $37m (ca. €36.5m) in a plant.
- The plant is planned to be built in the town of Toccoa, Georgia.
- Toccoa, Georgia is located around 150 km northeast of Atlanta.
