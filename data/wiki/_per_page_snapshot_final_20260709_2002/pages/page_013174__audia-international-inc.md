# Audia International, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-09-03

**Source:** [Top of Georgia | Success Stories](https://topofgeorgia.us/success-stories)  ·  `page_013174`

## Facts

- In May 2014, Audia Group announced plans for a new manufacturing plant in Northwest Georgia.
- Audia International, Inc. is a plastics compounding, color solutions, and distribution company with a 60-year history of customer focused growth.
- Plans for the Walker County facility grew from an initial 240,000-square foot manufacturing facility to more than 300,000 square feet of space.
- The facility will serve as the anchor tenant for the new 460-acre Walker County Business Park.
- Audia first contacted the development office in August 2012 and initially sought an existing building before shifting to a new build-out.
- Site preparation work had already begun, with operations expected to be up and running in the third quarter of 2015.
- The company will invest over $50MM and create 200 jobs in Northwest Georgia when the build-out is complete.
- Northwest Georgia Joint Development Authority project manager Keith Barclift noted that having an anchor tenant increases the marketability of the greenfield park by providing necessary infrastructure like roads cut and industrial-grade utilities in place.
- Barclift commended Walker County Commissioner Heiskell for her foresight in ensuring the site's viability.
