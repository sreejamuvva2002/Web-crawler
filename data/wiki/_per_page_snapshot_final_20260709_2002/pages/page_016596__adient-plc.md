## Facts

- Adient acquired a foam production plant in Romulus, Michigan.
- The acquisition increases Adient's total foam plants in the Americas to 10 and globally to 30.
- The deal includes the purchase of the existing building, land, production equipment, inventory, and associated assets.
- The transaction ensures the current workforce remains employed and the collective bargaining agreement with the UAW stays intact.
- Adient operates approximately 200 manufacturing/assembly plants worldwide with over 65,000 employees across 29 countries.
- Adient designs, manufactures, and markets complete seating systems, frames, mechanisms, foam, head restraints, armrests, and trim covers for passenger cars, commercial vehicles, and light trucks.
- The acquired plant supplies seat foam to multiple automakers and is now part of Adient's Americas network.
