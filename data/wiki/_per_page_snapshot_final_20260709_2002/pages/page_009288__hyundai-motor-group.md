# Hyundai Motor Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-02-28

**Source:** [Hyundai moves up the opening of its EV factory in Georgia to Q4 2024 - electrive.com](https://www.electrive.com/2024/02/28/hyundai-moves-up-the-opening-of-its-ev-factory-in-georgia-to-q4-2024/)  ·  `page_009288`

## Facts

- The Hyundai Motor factory will be located in Bryan County.
- The factory will produce up to 300,000 Hyundai, Kia and Genesis electric cars and batteries per year.
- The South Korean company had previously brought forward the start of construction from the beginning of 2023 to October 2022.
- The earlier start of construction was cited as being due to new rules for electric car subsidies in the USA at the time.
- The start of production at the Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) in Bryan County was initially announced for the first half of 2025.
- The Group maintained the first half of 2025 production start timeline despite the earlier construction start.
- The Group has officially announced that the start of production will be brought forward to the fourth quarter.
- The accelerated production timeline is presumably due to the time gained from the early start of construction.
- A year and a half ago, Hyundai estimated the investment volume for the new vehicle plant at “just over 5.5 billion US dollars”.
- Hyundai previously announced the creation of 8,100 jobs for the project.
- In the current announcement, the Group cites a 7.59 billion dollar investment.
- The current announcement specifies 8,500 direct jobs.
- As announced at CES 2024, plant logistics will be partly based on Hyundai’s H2 truck Xcient.
- The logistics plan is part of the “Clean Logistics Project”.
- The Group is building two battery cell factories in Georgia to localize the value chain and qualify for subsidies.
- One battery cell factory is being built together with LG Energy Solution near the EV plant in Bryan County.
- The LG Energy Solution joint factory is scheduled to go into operation at the end of 2025 at the earliest.
- The LG Energy Solution joint factory will have an annual capacity of 30 GWh.
- The South Korean company is planning another battery cell factory with SK On.
- The SK On factory will be built in Bartow County in the north-west of Georgia near Atlanta.
- The production capacity of the SK On factory is not yet known.
- The opening of the SK On factory is also scheduled for 2025.
