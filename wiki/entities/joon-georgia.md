# Joon Georgia

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** Bruce Yawn Commerce Park (formerly Southern Gateway Commerce Park), near Statesboro, Bulloch County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Joon Georgia, Inc., a subsidiary of Ajin USA, will build a new automotive parts manufacturing facility in the Bruce Yawn Commerce Park near Statesboro, creating 630 jobs and investing $317 million. The plant is the first confirmed Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America supplier in Bulloch County and will serve automotive and EV manufacturers.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Supplier of body parts and electronics for Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America, an EV and battery manufacturing hub.
