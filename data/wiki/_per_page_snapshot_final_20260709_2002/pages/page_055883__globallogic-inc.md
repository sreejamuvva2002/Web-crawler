## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo, Ltd. and GlobalLogic Inc. have been strengthening their collaboration to lead the trends of software-defined vehicles.
- Hitachi Astemo President & CEO Kohei Takeuchi and GlobalLogic CEO Nitesh Banga discussed the future of mobility through digital transformation.
- As a global mega-supplier, Hitachi Astemo provides internal vehicle technology such as software that enables advanced vehicle functions including autonomous driving.
- Hitachi Astemo focuses on the Out-Car domain, which is technology outside of vehicles that expands functionality and improves performance through cloud and communications technology.
- To further strengthen support for the Out-Car field, Hitachi Astemo established Cypremos, Ltd. as a new company on November 1.
- The Hitachi Astemo Group operates as a Tier 0.5 supplier to develop technologies and provide value for future mobility.
- Vehicle OEMs are rapidly transforming to become tech companies and developing SDV architecture to provide over-the-air updates and generate additional revenue streams through the lifecycle of vehicles.
- GlobalLogic enables the mobility industry transformation by creating technology both in-vehicle and around-the-vehicle platforms that create revenue streams for OEMs and the broader mobility ecosystem.
- GlobalLogic has formed various partnerships with major automobile manufacturers, parts suppliers, hyperscalers, semiconductor companies, fleets, and automotive retail.
- GlobalLogic is focusing on the development of electric vehicle propulsion systems to contribute to next-generation sustainable transportation and enhanced vehicle safety, comfort, and connectivity.
- Hitachi Astemo established Cypremos to provide software and cloud services aimed at expanding support for SDV and realizing new mobility services.
- The new company Cypremos will provide value by improving car intelligence and mobility evolution linked to the cloud.
- GlobalLogic's purpose is to create a positive impact for society and the planet by engineering digital products, platforms, and services designed for desirability, curated for intelligence, and built for excellence.
- GlobalLogic drives business transformation through intelligent, sustainable solutions in close collaboration with clients across healthcare, energy, finance, mobility, and more.
- GlobalLogic operates design studios and engineering centers around the world, extending expertise to customers in automotive, communications, financial services, healthcare and life sciences, manufacturing, media and entertainment, semiconductor, and technology industries.
- GlobalLogic is a Hitachi Group Company operating under Hitachi, Ltd. (TSE: 6501).
