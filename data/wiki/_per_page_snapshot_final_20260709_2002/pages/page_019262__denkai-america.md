# Denkai America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-07-20

**Source:** [Japanese manufacturer to build North American headquarters in Augusta](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/japanese-manufacturer-build-north-american-142230336.html)  ·  `page_019262`

## Facts

- The Japanese manufacturer of copper foil used in printed electronics will build its North American headquarters in Georgia, as announced by Gov. Brian Kemp.
- Denkai America is making an initial investment of $150 million on a 115-acre site in Richmond County.
- Ultimately, the company will spend $430 million on a headquarters and manufacturing plant.
- The project will create 250 jobs over the next five years.
- Denkai America's products have traditionally been used in printed circuit board applications primarily for the aerospace and defense industries.
- The company's copper foil is now also used in lithium-ion batteries powering electric vehicles.
- Gov. Brian Kemp stated, "Georgia is proud to be selected as the home of Denkai America, further strengthening our state's status as a leader in the emerging electric mobility and existing aerospace, defense, and technology industries."
- Gov. Brian Kemp added, "We have been laser focused on bringing quality jobs to highly trained, hardworking Georgians across the state, and we look forward to seeing one-of-a-kind companies like Denkai thrive in Georgia's top-ranking pro-business environment."
- Nobu Masuda, president and CEO of Denkai America, stated, "We are thrilled to expand our manufacturing presence into Augusta, which has the skilled and talented work force our company needs for growth."
- Construction is expected to begin in September as the first part of a three-phase investment.
- The facility will be fully operational by December 2024.
- The state Department of Economic Development worked with the Augusta Economic Development Authority, Georgia Power, and the Technical College System of Georgia's Quick Start program to land the project.
- Denkai America will be hiring skilled industrial technicians, production managers, and engineers.
