## Facts

- Ecoplastic Corp., a Korean auto parts manufacturer, is investing $205 million to build a new manufacturing facility in Statesboro, GA.
- The company is a tier-1 supplier for Hyundai.
- Ecoplastic produces parts such as consoles and bumpers.
- The project is expected to bring more than 450 jobs to the region.
- Production is slated to begin in October 2024.
- Han Shang, president and CEO of Ecoplastic, stated that the company is establishing a production facility for electric vehicle parts in Bulloch County.
- Han Shang expressed appreciation for the support of Georgia state and county governments regarding the project.
