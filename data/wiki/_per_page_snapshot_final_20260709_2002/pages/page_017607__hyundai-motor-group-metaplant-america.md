## Facts

- The Hyundai Metaplant has begun initial production of consumer vehicles.
- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) confirmed in a statement that it has started production.
- HMGMA validated its production processes to ensure its vehicles meet Hyundai Motor Group’s high quality standards before starting initial production.
- Initial production of customer vehicles began ahead of schedule.
- Construction took less than two years from breaking ground at the megasite to the start of initial production.
- An employee-focused event was held on Oct. 3, 2024, to celebrate the first IONIQ 5 rolling off the line and recognize the "Meta Pros" workforce's hard work over the past 24 months.
- American-assembled IONIQ 5 EVs will be on sale in U.S. dealerships by the end of the year.
- A Grand Opening celebration is planned for the first quarter of 2025.
- Full production began earlier this week, according to the Atlanta Journal Constitution (AJC).
- The plant held a private opening celebration with its 1,400-plus employees on Thursday.
- Hyundai officials had projected full production would begin in the fourth quarter of 2024.
- Hyundai announced plans for the Metaplant in April 2022.
- Groundbreaking for the Metaplant occurred in October following the April 2022 announcement.
- The project is known as one of the largest economic developments in Georgia.
