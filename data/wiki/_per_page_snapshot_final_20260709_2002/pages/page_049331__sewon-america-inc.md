# Sewon America Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2012-08-27

**Source:** [Sewon America to add 150 jobs in Georgia](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/shopmanagement/sewon-america-to-add-150-jobs-in-georgia)  ·  `page_049331`

## Facts

- Sewon America Inc. is an auto manufacturing supplier located in LaGrange, Ga.
- The company announced plans for a $10 million expansion.
- The expansion will add 150 new jobs.
- The project is scheduled for completion by the end of the year.
- The plant provides metal stamping for a variety of automotive parts.
- The facility currently employs about 700 people.
- The facility is the largest supplier for Georgia's Kia Motors plant.
- The facility also supplies a Hyundai Corp. manufacturing plant in Alabama.
