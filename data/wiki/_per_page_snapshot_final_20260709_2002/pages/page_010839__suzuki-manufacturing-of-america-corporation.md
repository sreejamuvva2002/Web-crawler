# Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-03-05

**Source:** [Suzuki Cycles](https://cycles.aws-prod.suzuki.com/news/2023/08/suzuki-manufacturing-of-america-corp-to-become-subsidiary-of-suzuki-motor-usa-llc)  ·  `page_010839`

## Facts

- SMAC has become a 100% subsidiary of Suzuki Motor USA LLC (SMO) effective March 1, 2024.
- SMAC was founded in 2001 and is based in Rome, Georgia.
- SMAC serves as Suzuki’s All-Terrain Vehicle (ATV) manufacturing facility.
- For over two decades, all Suzuki adult KingQuad ATVs have been manufactured at the Rome, Georgia location.
- Suzuki ATVs are sold in the United States, Canada, and nearly 25 countries worldwide.
- The organizational change consolidates ATV sales and production arms to strengthen Suzuki's overall ATV business in the United States and globally.
- Masami Haga, President of SMO, stated that a primary goal is to expand commitment to the growth of the entire powersports business.
- The move aims to build on relationships with Suzuki dealers and SMAC vendor partners.
- Strengthening the supply chain and purchasing policies for locally sourced parts are objectives of the organizational change.
- Integrating production and sales functions is expected to improve responsiveness to the ATV market by more accurately linking production to sales demand.
- SMO aims to improve inventory management for both the company and its dealer body to protect sales margins, flooring costs, and profitability.
- The restructuring will create better, more direct communication between SMO and SMAC, fostering a collaborative environment between production and sales groups.
- The change fosters better connection between the Rome- and Brea-based teams.
- Suzuki’s United States distribution headquarters are in Brea, California, where SMO relocated in 1981 after entering the U.S. market in 1963.
- SMAC becoming a subsidiary sets the stage for new Suzuki ATV offerings and enhancements to existing products in the future.
