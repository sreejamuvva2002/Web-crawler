# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-23

**Source:** [Electric Vertical Aircraft Manufacturing Company Archer to Create 1,000 Jobs in Covington - Thomas & Hutton](https://www.thomasandhutton.com/2022/11/23/electric-vertical-aircraft-manufacturing-company-archer-to-create-1000-jobs-in-covington/)  ·  `page_016815`

## Facts

- Thomas & Hutton worked alongside Newton County Industrial Development Authority on site selection for Archer Aviation Inc.
- Archer Aviation Inc. is an aerospace company planning to locate alongside the Covington Municipal Airport in Newton County.
- Thomas & Hutton provided civil engineering services related to land analysis for the over 96-acre site.
- Thomas & Hutton oversaw coordination with local utilities and the City of Covington on water and sewer delivery and access road improvements to serve the area.
- Thomas & Hutton consulted with Airport staff on access to taxiways and height restrictions related to preliminary engineering work.
- Archer Aviation will produce state-of-the-art electric vertical takeoff and landing aircraft (eVTOL) at the proposed facility.
- Archer Aviation expects to create 1,000 jobs in Newton County.
- Archer Aviation expects to invest $118 million over ten years in Newton County.
- The project’s location next to the Covington Municipal Airport is congruent with the long-term airport master plan.
- The project's location provides access to Georgia’s interstate highway system.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia has made fostering innovation a priority as it works with companies to bring the jobs of the future to the No. 1 state for business.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that Georgia's established leadership in the aerospace industry was essential to landing Archer Aviation in Newton County.
- Governor Brian Kemp expressed pleasure at welcoming Archer Aviation to the Peach State.
