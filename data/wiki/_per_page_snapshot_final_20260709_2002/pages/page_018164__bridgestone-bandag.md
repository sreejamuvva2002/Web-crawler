# Bridgestone Bandag

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-09-29

**Source:** [The Development Corporation of Abilene Announces Bridgestone Bandag’s $60M Investment - Expansion Solutions](https://www.expansionsolutionsmagazine.com/the-development-corporation-of-abilene-announces-bridgestone-bandags-60m-investment/)  ·  `page_018164`

## Facts

- Bridgestone Bandag will expand its operations in Abilene, Texas with a $60M investment.
- The expansion adds 50,000 square feet to the company's existing 200,000 square foot facility.
- The project focuses on developing an automated operation for rubber mixing and production.
- The Development Corporation of Abilene (DCOA) presented “Project Eagle II” to the City Council of Abilene.
- The City Council approved a $6 million incentive package from the DCOA to support the expansion agreement.
- Bridgestone Bandag is expected to retain 179 employees through the expansion project.
- The project will create an additional 25 jobs with average salaries above the City of Abilene's average salary.
- Over the next 10 years, the $60 million investment is estimated to add over $21 million in taxes for the City of Abilene, Taylor County, and the Abilene Independent School District.
- Over the next 10 years, the total estimated indirect and direct economic impact of the company's retention and expansion is $1.81 billion.
- Bridgestone Bandag has been a part of the Abilene community since 1971.
- The expansion supports Bridgestone’s E8 Commitment, which consists of eight values starting with “E” (Energy, Ecology, Efficiency, Extension, Economy, Emotion, Ease, and Empowerment) to realize a sustainable society.
- Barry Owens, senior vice president of the Bridgestone Americas Manufacturing Group, stated the expansion is driven by growing demand for products and the team's commitment to innovation and quality.
- The company aims to build greener retreads while helping maximize fuel efficiency for fleets.
- The Abilene community supports the facility with four higher education institutions, a technical college, and a community college.
- Abilene is located in Taylor County, Texas, directly on I-20, approximately two and a half hours from the Dallas/Fort Worth metro area.
- The city has a population of 126,518 people and a labor shed of over 140,000.
- Bridgestone Americas, Inc., the U.S.-based subsidiary of Bridgestone Corporation, is headquartered in Nashville, Tennessee, and employs more than 50,000 people across its worldwide operations.
