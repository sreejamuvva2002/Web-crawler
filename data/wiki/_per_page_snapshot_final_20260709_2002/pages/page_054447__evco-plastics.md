## Facts

- EVCO Plastics is a plastics manufacturing company based in Wisconsin, United States.
- The company maintains a global presence with 13 world-class facilities strategically located across the United States, Mexico, and China.
- All of its facilities adhere to stringent ISO-certified standards.
- EVCO Plastics operates over 157 injection molding machines, both horizontal and vertical, ranging from 28 to 4,400 tons.
- The company employs a team of over 2,000 employees.
- EVCO Plastics specializes in ISO Class 8 clean room molding, large part molding, and thin wall plastic packaging.
- The company provides services for complex plastic parts and assemblies, metal to plastic conversions, robotics and automation, and contract manufacturing.
- EVCO Plastics offers multi-shot molding, molded-in color, in-mold decorating/in-mold labeling, overmolding, gas assist molding, and stack molds.
- The company performs full secondary and integrated operations for its customers.
- Best-in-class manufacturers have relied on EVCO Plastics for decades for projects spanning precision molding and large parts.
