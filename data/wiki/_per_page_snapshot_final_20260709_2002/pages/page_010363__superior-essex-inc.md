# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Superior Essex Continues Commitment To Sustainability, Partners With Copper Mark - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.it/news/superior-essex-continues-commitment-to-sustainability-partners-with-copper-mark/)  ·  `page_010363`

## Facts

- On May 4, 2021, Superior Essex concluded an agreement to become the eighth corporate partner of The Copper Mark.
- The partnership aligns with Superior Essex's corporate sustainability goals and its recently announced Vision 2030 for the Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire brand.
- The initiative aims to responsibly source raw materials used in its magnet wire products.
- Superior Essex Communications, a leading global manufacturer of communication cables, has been a leader in sustainability since 2011.
- The company is working to significantly reduce the impact of its products by seeking responsibly sourced materials in collaboration with its copper suppliers.
- The Copper Mark is an assurance framework used to demonstrate responsible production practices in the copper industry and the industry's contribution to UN Sustainable Development Goals.
- The framework evaluates copper producers against criteria covering 32 different environmental, social, and governance problem areas associated with mineral and metal sourcing.
- The Copper Mark aims to establish good management practices derived from voluntary sustainability standards commonly used in mineral and metal supply chains.
- Scott Wilde, Global Vice President of Strategic Sourcing at Superior Essex, stated that aligning responsible material sourcing is key to achieving the company's goals and meeting commercial partner needs.
- CEO Brian Kim recently launched commitments for Vision 2030, framing the partnership as a challenge to be forward-looking with green production and improving products from the source.
- The commitment demonstrates how procurement and sustainability efforts can work together within and outside the organization.
- Many commercial partners have changed their sustainability values, ensuring Superior Essex can be part of their solutions.
- Being one of the first to adopt The Copper Mark highlights the company's belief in reducing the impact of magnet wire and communication wiring.
- The Copper Mark evaluates management practices of copper mines, smelters, and refineries across categories including human rights, working conditions, community, environment, and general governance (transparency, disclosure, corporate integrity).
- Company processes are measured against standards defined in a risk readiness assessment created by the Responsible Minerals Initiative, which comprises over 380 cross-industry companies.
- Michèle Brülhart, Executive Director of The Copper Mark, stated that Superior Essex's commitment to sustainability and responsible copper production is key to the partnership.
- Annie Bevan, Global Head of Sustainability for Superior Essex, noted that having all parties present a joint statement shows the power of Vision 2030, The Copper Mark, and Superior Essex's future.
- Superior Essex is the flagship brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, and Essex Brownell.
- The company has over 3,000 employees across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is the leading global supplier of magnet wire and is at the forefront of applying communication fiber and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company's products are critical for creating disruptive technological advances in the electrification of automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex is committed to pioneering smart building technology while setting the pace for sustainability.
- The Copper Mark offers a comprehensive system addressing 32 environmental, social, and governance issues related to responsible copper production, using the Responsible Minerals Initiative's risk readiness assessment.
- Initially developed and funded by the International Copper Association (ICA), the Copper Mark operates as an independent entity headquartered in the UK.
- Superior Essex's corporate address is listed as 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
