## Facts

- Volvo Group North America participates in the Better Plants Challenge under the Better Buildings & Better Plants Initiative.
- Committed to a 25% reduction in portfolio energy intensity from a 2014 baseline by 2024, based on a commitment of 14 plants.
- Currently has 13 plants committed to this goal.
- Has achieved 26% cumulative progress toward the 2014 baseline goal.
- Committed to an additional goal of a 25% reduction in portfolio energy intensity from a 2009 baseline by 2024, which was met in 2014.
- Has achieved 26.8% cumulative progress toward the 2009 baseline goal.
- Committed to an additional goal of a 15% reduction in energy intensity by 2033, covering 15 facilities.
- Participates in the DOE Waste Reduction Network program.
- Participates in the Superior Energy Performance 50001 program.
