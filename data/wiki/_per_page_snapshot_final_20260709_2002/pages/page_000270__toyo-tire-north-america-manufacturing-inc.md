# Toyo Tire North America Manufacturing Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-12-05

**Source:** [Toyo Honored with Georgia Automotive Supplier Award - Tire Review Magazine](https://www.tirereview.com/toyo-honored-with-georgia-automotive-supplier-award/)  ·  `page_000270`

## Facts

- Georgia Department of Economic Development named Toyo Tire North America Manufacturing Inc. the first-ever winner of its Automotive Supplier of the Year Award.
- The Georgia Automotive Awards recognize companies and individuals throughout the state for their contributions to build the state’s automotive ecosystem in five categories: Company of the Year, Leader of the Year, Innovation Award, Supplier of the Year and Community Impact Award.
- Jim Hawk, Toyo Tires’ chairman, stated that Georgia is a key market for Toyo Tires and its work there is paramount to the organization's overall success.
- Jim Hawk cited Georgia’s robust automotive industry and network, business-friendly tax incentives, and low cost of doing business as factors making it an ideal location for Toyo Tires' growth path.
- Toyo Tire North America Manufacturing Inc. was awarded the Automotive Supplier of the Year Award for its growth and contributions to the state’s economic development efforts.
- Toyo has more than doubled the size of its facility in White, Ga., since its groundbreaking in 2004.
- The facility increased its staff from 81 employees to 1,180.
- The facility’s fourth expansion is scheduled to be complete in 2017.
- The expansion will bring the manufacturing space to 3 million square feet.
- The expansion will increase annual production to 9 million tires.
- Bartow County Commissioner Steve Taylor stated that the expansion will boost Toyo’s local investment to more than $1 billion.
