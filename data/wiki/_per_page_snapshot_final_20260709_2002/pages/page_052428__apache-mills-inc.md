# Apache Mills, Inc.

## Facts

- In business since 1970, Apache Mills leads the way in designing and manufacturing quality floor mats, matting, and OEM materials for the home, commercial, and industrial markets.
- The company's headquarters is a 750,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility located in Calhoun, Georgia.
- All four matting divisions (Apache At Home, Apache Commercial, Apache Industrial, and AMI) are serviced from the Calhoun, Georgia headquarters.
- The company employs over 600 people across its operations.
- Apache Mills uses Wasatch SoftRIP software to streamline workflow and improve production efficiency.
- The facility utilizes MacDermid film and a Roland SOLJET PRO III XJ-640 printer to produce film positives for screen printing on various mat materials.
- Jeff Young, an art director at Apache Mills, has used Wasatch software for nearly 20 years.
