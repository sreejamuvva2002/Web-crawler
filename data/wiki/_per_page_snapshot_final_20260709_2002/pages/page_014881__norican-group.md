## Facts

- NORICAN GROUP announced the expansion of operations at its LaGrange, Georgia Technology Hub.
- The facility has been operated as the Wheelabrator Group Americas headquarters since 1987.
- The facility offers manufacturing, inventory, and logistics capabilities.
- The planned expansion will move all Norican North America business operations into the LaGrange technology hub.
- The consolidated businesses include Wheelabrator, DISA, StrikoWestofen, and Italpresse Gauss.
- The company will ramp up current resources to meet commercial operations and administration requirements for all lines of business.
- Inside sales and customer support activities will join inventory and logistics centralized in LaGrange.
- The expansion will transfer the current operations of DISA from Oswego, Illinois to LaGrange.
- The expansion will transfer the current operations of StrikoWestofen and Italpresse Gauss from Holland, Michigan to LaGrange.
- The announcement was published in 2020.
