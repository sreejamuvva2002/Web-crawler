# Michelin North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-16

**Source:** [FinancialContent - MICHELIN ANNOUNCES FULL ROLLOUT OF TREADEYE TECHNOLOGY ACROSS U.S. RETREAD NETWORK](https://markets.financialcontent.com/stocks/article/gnwcq-2025-10-16-michelin-announces-full-rollout-of-treadeye-technology-across-us-retread-network)  ·  `page_046504`

## Facts

- Michelin announced the full deployment of TreadEye technology across its U.S. Retread Technologies franchise network.
- After a period of phased implementation, TreadEye is now available across the Michelin Retread Technologies franchise network in the United States.
- TreadEye is an industry-leading tread depth assessment tool that helps fleets maximize their tire investment.
- The system accurately and consistently collects 1,200 measurement points to provide precise insights into tread wear and casing condition.
- Nate Kirian, Vice President of Sales and Distribution for Commercial Business Lines at Michelin North America, Inc., stated that TreadEye is a game-changer for retread operations and fleets.
- Key benefits of TreadEye include identifying pull points by fleet location, maximizing rubber on the road, preventing casing damage, reducing DOT violations and downtime from late pulls, and improving tire removal efficiency.
- Michelin North America, Inc. is headquartered in Greenville, S.C.
- Michelin North America has approximately 23,500 employees.
- Michelin North America operates 36 production facilities in the United States and Canada.
- Michelin is a world-leading manufacturer of composites and experiences with over 130 years of pioneering engineered materials.
- The company manufactures high-quality tires and components for critical applications in mobility, construction, aeronautics, low-carbon energies, and healthcare.
