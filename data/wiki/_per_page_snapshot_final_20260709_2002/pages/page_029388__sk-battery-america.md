## Facts

- SK Battery America is actively seeking dedicated individuals with strong work ethics and commitment to excellence for its manufacturing team.
- The company offers career opportunities ranging from various production levels to administrative positions.
- SK Battery America values a strategic mindset, strong leadership capabilities, and a passion for excellence in its employees.
- Employees at SK Battery America have the opportunity to take on challenging projects, make critical decisions, and shape the direction of the organization.
- The company's Electrode team creates anode and cathode components for batteries starting from raw materials.
- The Assembly team combines electrode components into pretested battery cells.
- The Formation team finalizes battery cell production by subjecting each cell to rigorous aging and inspection processes.
- The Module team assembles individual battery cells into larger battery modules and prepares those modules for shipment to clients.
