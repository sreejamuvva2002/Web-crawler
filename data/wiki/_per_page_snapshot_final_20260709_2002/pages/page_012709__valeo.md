# Valeo

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-01-10

**Source:** [Moodify and Valeo Collaborate for Car Malodor Solution | Perfumer & Flavorist](https://www.perfumerflavorist.com/home/news/21873376/moodify-and-valeo-collaborate-for-car-malodor-solution)  ·  `page_012709`

## Facts

- Valeo and Moodify, an Israeli company that develops scent solutions for improved personal performance and well-being, have announced their first collaboration to bring a solution for car malodor to the market.
- The groundbreaking solution is based on the smart diffusion of a special scent called Moodify White.
- The solution is based on 10 years of research at Israel’s top technology research institute.
- Moodify White effectively masks the perception of foul smells in cars, like cigarettes, mold and decay.
- The scent is made entirely of environmentally friendly, hypoallergenic and non-intrusive compounds.
- The first version of Moodify White will be released in early 2020.
- Samples of the solution will be available at the Consumer Electronics Show [CES] in Valeo and Moodify’s exhibit area from January 7 to January 10, 2020 in Las Vegas.
- Georges de Pelsemaeker, Ph.D., health and wellbeing director at Valeo, stated that combining Valeo’s deep knowledge of the automotive industry and expertise in cabin air quality solutions with Moodify’s technological capacity enables them to bring an ultimate solution to bad smell in cars, a problem that pains the entire automotive supply chain.
- Yigal Sharon, CEO of Moodify, described Moodify White as the harbinger of the digital scent revolution and noted that their technology uses neuroscience and chemo-signals to tailor-make scent solutions easily.
- Moodify's investors include Toyota AI Ventures and Next Gear Ventures.
