# Wabash

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-25

**Source:** [Wabash Expands Southeast Footprint to Strengthen Customer Access and Service](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/wabash-expands-southeast-footprint-strengthen-201500825.html?fr=sycsrp_catchall)  ·  `page_014595`

## Facts

- Wabash announced a significant expansion of its network in the Southeast.
- The expansion includes opening a new Parts & Service center, expanding its dealer relationship with Fleetco, and adding CS Truck and Trailer to its Preferred Partner Network.
- These moves strengthen customer access and service in the greater Atlanta region.
- Dave Hill, vice president of Parts & Services at Wabash, stated the expansion reflects building an extensive partner ecosystem that puts parts, service, and support closer to customers.
- The Atlanta expansions provide faster turnaround, more coverage, and better reliability for fleets operating across Georgia, Alabama, South Carolina, and Tennessee.
- The new Atlanta-area Parts and Services center is strategically located at the heart of the Southeast's trucking corridor.
- The center offers Wabash's Ready-to-Mount (RTM) truck bodies for dry freight and platform applications with rapid turnaround times for in-stock chassis.
- The Atlanta site joins existing Wabash service centers in California, Florida, Ohio, Pennsylvania, and Texas, with additional locations planned.
- Doors at the new Atlanta center opened on September 18, 2025.
- Fleetco, a long-standing Wabash dealer, will now represent Wabash in the greater Atlanta market with trailer sales.
- Bill Tirrill, president at Fleetco Inc., noted over 14 years of experience working closely with Wabash and that the expansion allows them to expand their sales force across the region.
- The Fleetco partnership will provide clients immediate access to new and used trailers.
- Wabash added three CS Truck and Trailer locations to its Preferred Partner Network in Georgia.
- The Preferred Partner Network brings expert service, maintenance, and Wabash Genuine Parts to more customers through authorized third-party dealerships.
- Wabash designs, manufactures, and services an extensive range of products supporting first-to-final mile operations, including dry and refrigerated trailers and truck bodies, platform trailers, tank trailers, and structural composites.
- Wabash is headquartered in Lafayette, Indiana.
