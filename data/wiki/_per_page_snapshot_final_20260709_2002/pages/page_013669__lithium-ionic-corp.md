# Lithium Ionic Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_material  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-02

**Source:** [Lithium Ionic engages RTEK to advance Bandeira Project, Brazil – Resource World Magazine](https://resourceworld.com/lithium-ionic-engages-rtek-to-advance-bandeira-project-brazil/)  ·  `page_013669`

## Facts

- Under a services agreement signed April 2, 2025, Lithium Ionic Corp. engaged RTEK International DMCC as a strategic advisor to fast-track the development of its flagship Bandeira Lithium Project in Brazil’s Lithium Valley.
- The long-term partnership integrates world-class lithium expertise into Lithium Ionic’s owner’s team as the project transitions from feasibility to construction and operations.
- RTEK is recognized globally for its technical and operational expertise in the lithium sector.
- Blake Hylands, P.Geo., CEO of Lithium Ionic, stated that RTEK’s deeply experienced team aligns perfectly with the company's vision for Bandeira and will strengthen execution capabilities as construction approaches.
- RTEK is led by a globally recognized team of lithium industry veterans including Brian Talbot, Nicholas Rowley, Noel O’Brien, and Rodrigo Roso, who have delivered results across the full mine lifecycle from feasibility through to production.
- As part of the owner’s team, RTEK will conduct a detailed review of Lithium Ionic’s NI 43-101 Feasibility Study to identify opportunities to optimize plant and mine design, reduce CAPEX and OPEX, and compress the development timeline.
- The engagement aims to position Bandeira as one of Brazil’s next high-impact lithium producers.
- Beyond engineering management and construction planning, RTEK will develop a comprehensive operational strategy that includes optimizing processes, managing commissioning, and overseeing ramp-up activities for best-in-class production performance.
- Brian Talbot, Founder and Director of RTEK, commented that the company sees excellent potential to accelerate the Bandeira Project to production by applying proven strategies in design optimization, cost efficiency, and execution discipline.
- Under the agreement, RTEK consultants will be integrated with Lithium Ionic’s owner’s team to progress detailed engineering, optimize operational design, and identify opportunities to reduce CAPEX and OPEX for construction readiness.
- The RTEK Agreement and all Share Compensation are subject to TSX Venture Exchange approval.
- Lithium Ionic announced a change in its senior management team, appointing Mike Westendorf as Chief Operating Officer (COO).
- Mike Westendorf joined the company in February 2024 as Vice President, Technical Services and is replacing Paulo Misk in the COO role.
- Lithium Ionic’s Itinga and Salinas group of properties cover approximately 17,000 hectares in the northeastern part of Minas Gerais state.
- The region is described as a mining-friendly jurisdiction emerging as a world-class hard-rock lithium district.
- The Feasibility-stage Bandeira Project is situated in the same region as CBL’s Cachoeira lithium mine, which has produced lithium for over 30 years.
- The project area is also near Sigma Lithium Corp.’s Grota do Cirilo project, which hosts the largest hard-rock lithium deposit in the Americas.
