# TI Fluid Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [TI Fluid Systems supplies Volkswagen ID.3 and ID.4 with industry-first thermal technology - TaaS Magazine News](https://taas.news/article/112336/TI_Fluid_Systems_Supplies_Volkswagen_ID3_And_ID4_With_Industry-first_Thermal_Technology)  ·  `page_011561`

## Facts

- TI Fluid Systems is a leading global supplier of automotive fluid systems technology.
- TI Fluid Systems has started high volume production of new thermal products, assemblies and technology for the Volkswagen ID.3 and ID.4 battery electric vehicles.
- The Volkswagen ID.3 is a compact hatchback.
- The ID.3 is the first production model based on the modular electric drive matrix (MEB) from Volkswagen.
- The release of the ID.4, an electric compact SUV, will follow the ID.3.
- Bill Kozyra serves as President and CEO of TI Fluid Systems.
- Bill Kozyra stated that TI Fluid Systems has taken another important step toward becoming an integrated thermal management supplier for the battery electrical vehicle market with the launch of new BEV components.
- The new thermal application utilizes heat pump technology using R744 (also called CO2) refrigerant.
- The heat pump system provides both heating and cooling for cabin comfort.
- The system operates at a higher efficiency in certain conditions to provide significant battery range extension.
- TI Fluid Systems assembles the heat pump module with electronic valves, an accumulator, a bracket, a chiller, and associated high pressure aluminum lines.
- The heat pump valve assembly is supplied from TI Fluid System’s Jablonec Plant in the Czech Republic.
- TI Fluid Systems will supply Chassis Thermal Coolant Loop Line Assemblies manufactured in various materials including lightweight engineered polymers.
- TIFS has commissioned a new plant in Tangier, Morocco for the battery electric vehicle market.
- The Tangier plant supplies finished hoses to Tauste, Spain for final assembly and verification test.
- TI Fluid Systems is the Tier 1 Global Supplier for these BEV products.
