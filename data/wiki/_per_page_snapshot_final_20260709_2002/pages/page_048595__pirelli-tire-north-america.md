# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2008-04-18

**Source:** [Pirelli Tire Posts Big Contingency For AMA Nationals - Motocross Action Magazine](https://motocrossactionmag.com/pirelli-tire-posts-big-contingency-for-ama-nationals/)  ·  `page_048595`

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America specializes in manufacturing and marketing high-performance tires for passenger vehicles, motorcycles, ATVs, and motorsports.
- Pirelli operates its highly advanced Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) facility in Rome, Georgia.
- The Rome, Georgia MIRS facility serves the American OEM and Replacement markets.
- Pirelli Tire posted a $336,200 contingency for the 2008 Toyota AMA Motocross Championships.
- Pirelli will provide professional-level support and quality assistance to amateur racers at each of the 12 stops of the championships via a new full-sized support trailer.
- Pirelli's dedicated staff will be on-hand at each national to meet the needs of racers using Scorpion MX Tires.
- Josh Whitmire serves as Pirelli's MX Manager.
- The 2008 Toyota AMA Motocross Championships opens at Glen Helen Raceway over the weekend of May 24-25 and continues through Round 12 at Steel City Raceway on August 30-31.
