# Right to Work Georgia

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [New Right to Work Georgia Companies Create Over 2k Jobs - NRTWC](https://nrtwc.org/new-right-to-work-georgia-companies-create-over-2k-jobs/)  ·  `page_001650`

## Facts

- Businesses are choosing to invest in Georgia over other states due to the great economic benefits that the Right to Work state provides.
- The most recent Right to Work Georgia investments will soon be coming from Archer Aviation, Komar, and FREYR Battery.
- Altogether, they will create a total of 2,017 new Georgia jobs.
- Archer Aviation is investing $118 million in order to add a new facility in Right to Work Newton County, Georgia.
- Archer Aviation's investment will mean 1,000 new jobs.
- Archer Aviation utilizes an innovative, state-of-the art eVTOL aircraft design to sustainably tackle mobility issues in urban centers.
- Komar Brands is investing $87 million in order to add a new facility in Right to Work Bryan County, Georgia.
- Komar Brands will create 294 new jobs.
- Komar Brands is partnering with Georgia to establish a new manufacturing and distribution center as part of its strategic vision.
- Komar Brands plans to break ground on its facility in the very near future.
- Georgia's consistent economic policy and dedication to speed-to-market solutions have made it a dynamic place to grow.
- Strong partnerships between local communities and the Georgia Ports Authority make doing business in Georgia easier for companies like Komar Brands.
- FREYR Battery is investing $2.57 billion in order to add a new location and create 723 new jobs in Right to Work Coweta County, Georgia.
- FREYR Battery is deeply committed to decarbonizing the transportation and energy sectors through its U.S. expansion plan.
- FREYR Battery expects to spur job creation and develop localized, decarbonized supply chains in the U.S. to enhance energy security and economic activity.
- Metro Atlanta has quickly become a hub for battery cell research and production.
- FREYR Battery will benefit from metro Atlanta's vibrant research ecosystem and diverse talent pool to support its next-generation efforts.
