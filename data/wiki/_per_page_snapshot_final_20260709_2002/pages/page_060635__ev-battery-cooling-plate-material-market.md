- The "Others" category includes ceramic-based plates and hybrid materials addressing extreme thermal loads, chemical compatibility with battery electrolytes, and integration with smart sensors.

- Air cooling is favored for simplicity, lower cost, and ease of maintenance in entry-level or compact EVs where thermal loads are manageable.

- Nickel-metal hydride (NiMH) batteries continue use in certain hybrid models, generally more tolerant of temperature variations but still benefiting from effective thermal management.
