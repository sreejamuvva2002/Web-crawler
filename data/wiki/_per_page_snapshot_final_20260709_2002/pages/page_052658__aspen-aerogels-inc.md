# Aspen Aerogels Inc

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-02

**Source:** [Aspen Aerogels Arranges Funds For Construction Of Statesboro Facility Via Equity Offering, Loan](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/aspen-aerogels-arranges-funds-construction-152836581.html)  ·  `page_052658`

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels Inc priced its upsized public offering of 25.3 million shares at $9.50 per share.
- The company granted underwriters a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 3.8 million shares.
- Gross proceeds from the offering are expected to be approximately $240 million, previously proposed as $200 million.
- Aspen Aerogels intends to use net proceeds to partially fund the construction of Phase I of its second aerogel manufacturing facility in Statesboro, Georgia.
- The equity offering is expected to close on December 2, 2022.
- Aspen Aerogels entered into a definitive loan agreement with General Motors Holdings LLC for a secured lending commitment of $100 million in connection with construction of the Statesboro facility.
- General Motors Holdings LLC is an affiliate of General Motors Company.
