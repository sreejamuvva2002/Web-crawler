# Pirelli

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2009-12-04

**Source:** [Continental In, Pirelli Out of Grand-Am Series - AftermarketNews](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/continental-in-pirelli-out-of-grand-am-series/)  ·  `page_048609`

## Facts

- Pirelli announced that it is withdrawing as race tire supplier for the Grand-Am Rolex Sports Car Series after the 2010 racing season.
- Pirelli plans to redeploy resources into other motorsport activities.
- Pirelli, a Rome, Ga.-based tiremaker, signed a three-year exclusive supply deal with the series, beginning with the 2008 season.
- Continental signed a five-year pact with Grand-Am Road Racing to become title sponsor and exclusive tire supplier for its Sports Car Challenge.
- Bill Caldwell, CTNA consumer tire vice president of sales and marketing, stated that the entitlement sponsorship adds an entirely new dimension to their marketing strategy to promote the Continental brand.
- Bill Caldwell noted that Grand-Am Road Racing is one of the highest-caliber racing series in the world and expressed pride and excitement about being a part of it.
- Bill Caldwell mentioned looking forward to the start of the 2010 season.
