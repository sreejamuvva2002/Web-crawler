# Kyungshin America Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Kyungshin America Picks Broe's Savannah Industrial Hub - Industry Today - Leader in Manufacturing & Industry News](https://industrytoday.com/kyungshin-america-picks-broes-savannah-industrial-hub/)  ·  `page_056730`

## Facts

- Kyungshin America Corporation (KAC) is a global automotive parts supplier and the newest tenant in The Broe Group’s Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub.
- The Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub (SGIH) is a 2,600-acre multimodal logistics park near Georgia’s Port of Savannah.
- KAC is a division of the South Korean Kyungshin Corporation.
- KAC’s new operation will import and distribute automotive components to Hyundai Motor Group’s new $7.5 billion electric vehicle plant in Savannah.
- KAC will also supply the growing automotive manufacturing network throughout the southeastern United States.
- Sean Fitzsimmons is Broe Real Estate Group Senior Vice President.
- KAC’s new facility is strategically positioned with the Port of Savannah and the new Hyundai Metaplant, both within 15 miles.
- The location leverages Effingham County’s strong workforce and education system.
- KAC produces wire harnesses for automotive companies.
- KAC is a certified Tier One supplier to Hyundai.
- KAC’s new facility is 136,000 square feet.
- KAC’s new facility is expected to open in February.
- The move supports Kyungshin’s continued North American production expansion.
- Brandt Herndon is Effingham County Industrial Development Authority CEO.
- SGIH is a public-private partnership between the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority (ECIDA), Broe Real Estate Group, and OmniTRAX.
- SGIH is located 12 miles from the Port of Savannah.
- SGIH provides dual rail access to the Class I rail networks of CSX and Norfolk Southern.
