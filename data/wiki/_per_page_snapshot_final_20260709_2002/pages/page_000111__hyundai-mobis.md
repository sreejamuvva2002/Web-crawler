# Hyundai Mobis

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Quick Start provides customized training for Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America’s (HMGMA) leading tier-one supplier, Hyundai Mobis.
- Quick Start’s Eastern Operations around Savannah, Georgia, are highly active as the opening day for Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) approaches.
- Suppliers are an essential part of HMGMA, with Hyundai Mobis serving as the largest tier-one supplier.
- Hyundai Mobis manufactures front and rear end chassis modules, cockpit modules, and key parts used to build batteries for electric vehicles.
- Training provided to Mobis includes company-specific assembly courses, mechatronics, and electrical safety awareness.
- Trainees also receive safety and organizational development courses, including Lean, leadership training, and conflict resolution.
- Quick Start is utilizing its main Advanced Manufacturing Training Center in Pooler, Georgia, to deliver the training.
- An interim facility is being used while the new Hyundai Mobility Training Center of Georgia is under construction adjacent to the Metaplant.
- The Metaplant, which will host Mobis’s operations in the Savannah area, is scheduled to begin vehicle production by early 2025.
- Hyundai Mobis is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
