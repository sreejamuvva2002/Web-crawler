# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2013-10-16

**Source:** [Pirelli Pairs with Solar Power Generator - Tire Review Magazine](https://www.tirereview.com/pirelli-pairs-with-solar-power-generator/)  ·  `page_048627`

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America and Hannah Solar will partner on the construction of a 500 KW photovoltaic energy generation facility.
- Paolo Ferrari, CEO of PTNA, stated that Pirelli has a strong commitment to sustainability and the partnership with Hannah furthers this commitment.
- The facility will produce solar energy under the Georgia Power Advance Solar Initiative.
- The facility will be located directly in front of Pirelli’s manufacturing plant in Rome, Ga.
- The project will be built on three acres of land.
- Construction was scheduled to begin in late October.
- The facility aimed to start producing energy in January.
- Pirelli will lease the property for the solar facility.
- Georgia Solar Power Co. and Energy Ecosystem will develop the project.
- Hannah Solar will manage the solar facility.
- The project is expected to generate more than 13,000 megawatt hours of electricity during the next 20 years.
