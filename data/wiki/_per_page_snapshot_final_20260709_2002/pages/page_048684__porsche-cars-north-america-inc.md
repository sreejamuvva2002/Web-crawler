## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. has officially opened a new fine dining restaurant in Atlanta, Georgia.
- The facility is located on the complex of the company's new $100 million Porsche Experience Center and headquarters.
- The complex features a 1.6-mile driver development track with panoramic views.
- Restaurant 356 overlooks Runway 826 Right at the Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport.
- The Porsche Experience Center includes a classic car gallery, restoration center, human performance center, and driving simulator lab.
- A state-of-the-art business center within the complex features 13,000 square feet of conference and event space.
- The complex is designed as a public destination with an estimated 30,000 guests expected to visit each year.
- Andre Oosthuizen serves as vice president of marketing for Porsche Cars North America, Inc.
- Executive Chef Kyle Forson leads the culinary operations at Restaurant 356.
- Reservations can be made through the restaurant's website or via OpenTable.
