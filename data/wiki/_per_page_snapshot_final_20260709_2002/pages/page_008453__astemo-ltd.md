# Astemo, Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-04-01

**Source:** [Honda: plan to make ASTEMO into a consolidated subsidiary before end of Q3 of FY26/27](https://www.ainvest.com/news/honda-plan-astemo-consolidated-subsidiary-q3-fy26-27-2606/)  ·  `page_008453`

## Facts

- Honda Motor Co., Ltd. has announced plans to consolidate Astemo, Ltd. as a fully owned subsidiary by the end of the third quarter of fiscal years 2026/2027.
- The move follows Honda’s agreement to acquire an additional 21% stake in Astemo from Hitachi, Ltd.
- This acquisition increases Honda's ownership in Astemo from 40% to 61%.
- The remaining shares will be held by JIC Capital, Ltd. at 20%, and Hitachi at 19%.
- The transaction is expected to be finalized during the first quarter of the fiscal year beginning April 1, 2026, pending regulatory approvals.
- Astemo was formed in 2021 through the integration of four companies.
- Astemo has been a key player in automotive technology, combining hardware and software capabilities.
- The company has been actively developing solutions for software-defined vehicles (SDVs), a trend expected to reshape the automotive industry.
- Honda emphasized that Astemo’s growth is critical to enhancing its SDV development capabilities and maintaining cost competitiveness in a rapidly evolving market.
- Under the new ownership structure, Astemo aims to accelerate its transformation into a global supplier of integrated mobility solutions.
- Astemo's focus areas include electrification, AI, and cloud-based technologies.
- The company remains open to an initial public offering (IPO) in the future.
