# TI Fluid Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- TI Fluid Systems is a global innovator of thermal and fluid system solutions for the full range of current and developing vehicle architectures.
- TI Fluid Systems serves all major automotive manufacturers and has more than 100 years of automotive supply experience.
- TI Fluid Systems operates across 28 countries with a commitment to improving efficiency, performance, and sustainability worldwide.
- At IAA Mobility 2023, TI Fluid Systems is presenting an expanded product portfolio to reinforce its commitment to greener, smarter, highly efficient, and connected mobility.
- The company's primary focus at IAA Mobility 2023 is a recently developed modular thermal management system engineered for future mobility and efficiency demands.
- TI Fluid Systems will exhibit a holistic product portfolio ranging from component to system level at the event.
- A demo vehicle will be exhibited to demonstrate how an efficient TI thermal management system can improve battery range.
- Johannes Helmich states that while cybersecurity, connectivity, and autonomous driving are primary industry focuses, current customer needs should be prioritized.
- End customers expect electromobility without restrictions regarding battery range, charging time, and available charging infrastructure.
- TI Fluid Systems promotes the concept that "Efficiency is the New Currency," highlighting efficiency as critical for temperature control in battery electric vehicles (BEVs).
- The company's modular thermal management system allows for tailor-made solutions with maximum flexibility.
- Development focuses on system efficiency to increase overall vehicle efficiency, minimize energy losses, and maximize battery range by up to 20%.
- Johannes Helmich identifies creating clean, sustainable, and innovative mobility as the greatest potential of the mobility transition.
- Electrification is considered the first milestone of the mobility transition, with significant potential remaining in associated influencing factors.
- Expanding fast-charging station infrastructure in rural areas and along highways is crucial for meeting customer flexibility demands and facilitating BEV adoption.
- OEMs and legislators must ensure the generation and use of sustainable, CO2-neutral, and reusable materials, including limited raw materials used in batteries.
- The entire BEV supply chain must make a significant contribution to product sustainability.
- Energy sources must be renewable to make the entire vehicle life cycle more environmentally friendly.
- Hydrogen and e-fuels are identified as potential energy sources with enormous potential but require significant effort to become competitive.
- TI Fluid Systems is committed to strengthening its position as a leading provider of thermal management solutions for the automotive industry over the next 10 to 25 years.
- The company plans to expand its product portfolio, develop new breakthrough technologies, and invest more in research, development, and its workforce.
