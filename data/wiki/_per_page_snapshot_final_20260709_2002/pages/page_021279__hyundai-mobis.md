## Facts

- Hyundai Mobis plans to invest $926,000,000.00 to build out 1,200,000 square feet of new space in Midway, Georgia.
- The company plans to occupy the new space at Belfast Commerce Park in Midway on or about June 1, 2024.
- Hyundai MOBIS Technical Center of North America serves as the Research & Development Center for Hyundai MOBIS.
- Hyundai MOBIS is a Tier 1 supplier currently ranked #7 globally and an affiliate under Hyundai Motor Group.
- Hyundai MOBIS produces and supplies a wide variety of automotive components to customers including component modules (chassis, cockpit and front-end module), core system, eco-friendly and DAS-mechatronics.
- The Technical Center is one of five globally located in Plymouth, MI and delivers the design and development of advanced engineering, autonomous driving, infotainment, component modules, safety, brake and steering systems, lamp, ICS/electronics and software development solutions.
- Hyundai MOBIS is growing quickly and looking for passionate and engaged Engineers and Technical Professionals to join its team.
- James Lee serves as President & Chief Sales Officer for the company.
