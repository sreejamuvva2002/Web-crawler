# OTR Wheel Engineering

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [OTR Engineered Solutions - ABOUT OTR](https://partners.otrwheel.com/about-otr)  ·  `page_058430`

## Facts

- OTR Wheel Engineering was founded in Rome, Georgia in 1987 by Fred B. Taylor.
- OTR's product portfolio includes tires, wheels, assemblies, tracks, tubes, ballast, and more.
- The tire lineup comprises over 1,700 models and 300+ distinctive treads.
- Tire types include bias and radial pneumatic (directional and non-directional), semi-pneumatic, solid (directional and non-directional), non-marking, and airless.
- The portfolio includes over 1,000 wheels with single- and multi-piece versions in steel and aluminum, as well as cast disc units.
- OTR offers over 100,000 standard items, many of which can be customized for unique applications.
- AGCO has been a long-standing preferred supplier to OTR since 1997.
- In 2020, AGCO introduced OTR as its wheel and tire supplier for its cornerstone small HP tractor range.
- Honda has partnered with OTR since 2016.
- In 2019, Honda expanded its scope with OTR to include mounting for Side by Side lines.
- Nevada Gold Mines uses OTR wheels on CAT 795 haul trucks at mine sites.
- Nevada Gold Mines runs 7 piece Speed Wheels and 5 piece standard wheels from OTR on its CAT haul trucks.
- Aros Chile has worked with OTR Wheel Engineering for more than 15 years as an essential supplier in the mining wheel category.
- Aros Chile uses OTR to manufacture wheels for CAT Haulers and Loaders, providing a complete line of Wheels, Rims and Components.
- DCML uses OTR products on Komatsu 830 E dump trucks at Vale Mining Carajas.
- DCML also uses OTR products on CAT 777 haul trucks at Vale Mining South.
- In 1995, OTR opened a facility in Chambersburg, Pennsylvania for its first major OEM customer, JLG.
- In 1996, OTR Canada was formed via the acquisition of the business Northshore.
- In 1997, Silverstone was acquired, based in Omaha, Nebraska.
- In 1997, OTR opened a facility in Ackerman, Mississippi for OEM customer Taylor Machine Works.
- In 1999, OTR acquired OTR Wheel in Quincy, Illinois and folded it into OTR Wheel Engineering.
- In 2005, OTR China was established in Wuxi.
- In 2005, OTR opened a facility in Shady Grove, Pennsylvania to support Grove International.
- In 2005, OTR moved to a new facility in Louisville, Mississippi.
- In 2007, OTR Europe was established in Derby.
- In 2009, OTR Thailand was established in Chon Buri.
- In 2011, OTR Sri Lanka was established in Colombo.
- In 2013, Long’s Wheel & Rim was acquired.
- In 2015, SolidBoss was acquired.
- In 2016, the Rome facility was expanded to include new corporate headquarters.
- In 2017, Southern Wheel & Rim was acquired.
- In 2017, OTR International was formed.
- In 2017, Silver Diamond Services (SDS) in Mansfield, TX and Trans American Rubber (TAR) in Denver, CO were acquired.
- In 2018, Fieldens OTR was acquired and established in Suffolk, U.K.
- In 2019, a partnership was established with Owners Resource Group, configuring OTR as a member company.
- In 2019, OTR Germany was established in Sande, located near Wilhelmshaven in northeast Germany.
- In 2020, OTR Europe expanded to include operations in the Czech Republic.
- In 2020, SolidBoss OTR U.S. East operations were established.
- In 2021, Silverstone and its businesses, SDS and Trans American Rubber, were fully integrated into OTR, forming the basis for OTR U.S. (with SolidBoss included).
- In 2021, Tom Rizzi became the new CEO and president.
- OTR's brand pillars are SOLUTIONS, VALUE, INNOVATION, and PARTNERSHIP.
