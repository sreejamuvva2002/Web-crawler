# Aspen Aerogels

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-02-04

**Source:** [Aspen Aerogels already making a difference in our community  - Grice Connect](https://www.griceconnect.com/community/aspen-aerogels-already-making-a-difference-in-our-community-6506738)  ·  `page_052710`

## Facts

- Don Young serves as President and CEO of Aspen Aerogels.
- Aspen Aerogels' corporate culture expects leadership and team members to be actively involved in the community.
- In February 2022, Aspen Aerogels announced intentions to invest a minimum of $325 million at a new site.
- The investment is expected to create more than 250 advanced manufacturing jobs.
- Production at the new facility is expected to begin in late 2023.
- The plant site is located in Bruce Yawn Commerce Park, Statesboro.
- Brian McCarter was hired as Sr. Director of Operations for the Statesboro location.
- Heather Merritt was hired as Manager of Human Resources for the Statesboro location.
- Aspen Aerogels opened an office on South Main Street in downtown Statesboro to provide visibility and accessibility to the community.
- Aspen's leadership team held a planning session at Fostering Bulloch's 7th Mile Farm.
- The Aspen team spent time helping Chris Yaughn with construction needs at the farm.
- Brian McCarter stated that writing checks to support local agencies is important, but hands-on involvement helps employees understand community impact.
- McCarter emphasized that Bulloch County is their home and they have a responsibility as corporate citizens to step up and improve the community.
- Both McCarter and Merritt were residents of Statesboro when hired by Aspen Aerogels.
- Most of the team members who joined them are also residents of Statesboro.
- In addition to painting and carpentry work, Aspen provided financial resources for concrete pouring and finishing for a warehouse expansion at the farm.
- Aspen gave its contractors and vendors opportunities to participate in community initiatives alongside the company.
- Turner Construction serves as the general contractor for the new plant and has supported Aspen's community initiatives.
- Sean Strickland with Even Flow concrete worked with Aspen to provide concrete for the warehouse expansion.
- Chris Yaughn of Fostering Bulloch expressed gratitude for Aspen's financial support and willingness to engage in both small and large community efforts.
- Yaughn expressed hope for a long-term partnership with Aspen, noting they are serious about supporting the community and are not a 'one trick pony.'
- Aspen Aerogels employees have contributed to local initiatives including Tormenta soccer, the Village Builders project, public safety, Downtown Development Authority, Toys for Tots, United Way, and athletic boosters for Bulloch County Schools.
- Brian McCarter stated Aspen's goal is to be an employer that employees are proud of and excited to work with.
- McCarter aims for Aspen to become the employer of choice for the region.
