# Mercedes-Benz USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-01-01

**Source:** [Mercedes-Benz USA Announces Dimitris Psillakis as Head of Marketing and Sales, Mercedes-Benz Cars North America and CEO of MBUSA](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20201001005959/en/Mercedes-Benz-USA-Announces-Dimitris-Psillakis-as-Head-of-Marketing-and-Sales-Mercedes-Benz-Cars-North-America-and-CEO-of-MBUSA)  ·  `page_028679`

## Facts

- Mercedes-Benz USA (MBUSA) is headquartered in Atlanta, GA.
- The Daimler Board of Management appointed Dimitris Psillakis as Head of Marketing and Sales, Mercedes-Benz Cars North America and CEO of MBUSA, effective January 1, 2021.
- Psillakis reports to Britta Seeger, Member of the Board of Management of Daimler AG and Mercedes-Benz AG, responsible for Marketing and Sales.
- Psillakis succeeds Nicholas Speeks, who will leave the company after more than 40 years.
- Psillakis will be based in Atlanta, GA.
- In his new role, Psillakis assumes overall responsibility for Mercedes-Benz Cars in the United States and Canada.
- He leads MBUSA's more than 1,600 employees and more than 380 dealer partners throughout the U.S.
- He also oversees an additional 1,200 employees and almost 60 authorized dealers throughout Canada.
- Psillakis will continue as President and CEO of Mercedes-Benz Canada for the remainder of the year.
- For the interim period, MBUSA’s VP of Marketing Drew J. Slaven will serve as acting Head of Marketing and Sales, Mercedes-Benz Cars North America and CEO of MBUSA.
- Psillakis joined Daimler AG in 1992 as a management trainee with Mercedes-Benz Hellas and was promoted to general manager of passenger cars in 2001.
- In 2009, Psillakis moved to Mercedes-Benz do Brasil Ltda to serve as managing director for passenger cars in South America and Vans Sales Brazil, where his team tripled sales volume and reached the #1 spot in Brazil over six years.
- In 2015, Psillakis joined Mercedes-Benz Korea (MBK) as President and CEO, expanding the South Korean market to the 5th largest globally for Mercedes-Benz Cars while achieving top customer satisfaction ratings.
- MBUSA is responsible for the distribution, marketing, and customer service for all Mercedes-Benz products in the United States.
- MBUSA offers drivers a lineup of 15 model lines ranging from the A-Class sedan to the flagship S-Class and the Mercedes-AMG GT R.
- MBUSA is also responsible for Mercedes-Benz Vans products in the U.S.
- Britta Seeger stated that Dimitris is a proven leader who successfully expanded the growth of Mercedes-Benz in key markets around the world and will continue to advance North American operations during a time of unprecedented transformation.
