# Murata Electronics North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 1999-12-13

**Source:** [Murata Electronics Offers High Frequency Active Filter - EE Times](https://www.eetimes.com/murata-electronics-offers-high-frequency-active-filter/)  ·  `page_047147`

## Facts

- Murata Electronics North America announced the High Frequency (HF) Active Filter on December 13, 1999.
- The HF Active Filter is a leaded surface mounted device for use in next-generation electronics products.
- The component employs high-speed op-amps and advanced thick-film technology.
- The HF Active Filter reduces the size of a passive filter solution by 50%.
- The HF Active Filter is laser-tuned in an automated process.
- Murata Electronics N.A. operates out of Smyrna, Ga.
