# Truck Hero Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Truck Hero Inc. has acquired Lund International, which is a portfolio company of Highlander Partners, LP.
- Lund International is based in Buford, Georgia.
- Lund International is a leading designer and manufacturer of branded automotive accessories for all types of vehicles.
- Lund International was founded in 1965, with roots dating back to the 1920s.
- Lund International has built itself into one of the world’s leading automotive accessory providers.
- Lund International's brand portfolio includes AMP Research, AVS, Belmor, Bushwacker, LUND, Rampage Products, Roll-N-Lock, Stampede Automotive Accessories, and TonnoPro.
- Bill Reminder, President and CEO of Truck Hero, stated that bringing Truck Hero and Lund together is an opportune time.
- Mitch Fogle and the Lund team have built an impressive collection of companies that will be a perfect match to Truck Hero.
- Mitch Fogle stated that the merger of the two companies is a winning combination.
- The combined entities will manufacture and commercialize market-leading brands encompassing the broadest product offering of vehicle accessories.
- This transaction marks Truck Hero’s fifteenth acquisition since its formation in 2007.
- This is Truck Hero’s second acquisition in the last few weeks.
- Truck Hero's previous acquisition was Backrack, which closed at the end of April.
