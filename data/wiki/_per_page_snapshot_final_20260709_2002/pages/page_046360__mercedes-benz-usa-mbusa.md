# Mercedes-Benz USA (MBUSA)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-01-12

**Source:** [Mercedes-Benz USA Reports 2025 Year-Over-Year Retail Sales Growth](https://media.mbusa.com/releases/mercedes-benz-usa-reports-2025-year-over-year-retail-sales-growth)  ·  `page_046360`

## Facts

- Mercedes-Benz USA retailed 303,200 passenger cars in 2025.
- Demand for the Alabama-built GLE SUV continued to surge in the U.S., with 12% growth in Q4 propelling year-over-year growth to 14%, representing the best retail sales year ever for the GLE.
- The GLC closed 2025 recording a 20% increase in retail sales year-over-year.
- Mercedes-AMG retail sales increased by 12% in 2025, reporting its best year on record.
- The G-Class achieved its best sales ever with a 26% increase compared to 2024.
- Mercedes-Benz USA reported annual retail sales of 303,200 passenger cars, representing a 1% increase compared to 2024.
- Mercedes-Benz Vans reported 40,000 units retailed in 2025, resulting in a combined total of 343,200 units sold.
- Significant demand across the entire Mercedes-Benz SUV model portfolio and continued robust interest in Mercedes-AMG performance luxury models contributed to this growth.
- Adam Chamberlain serves as President and CEO of Mercedes-Benz USA.
- The CLE experienced 53% growth in 2025.
- GLE SUV sales rose 14% for the year, marking the best retail sales year ever for this model, which is proudly manufactured in the U.S. in Tuscaloosa, Alabama.
- The GLC continued to be extremely popular with U.S. consumers with sales up 20% from last year.
- The GLA led the entry segment with a 21% increase in retail sales year-over-year.
- Retail sales for Vans totaled 12,400 units for the fourth quarter, representing a 1% increase compared to Q4 2024 and bringing 2025 total sales to 40,000 units.
- On a global level, Mercedes-Benz Group sold 2,160,000 cars and vans in 2025 with a 7% surge in Mercedes-AMG deliveries and best-ever sales for the G-Class.
- Q4 marked the strongest quarter of 2025 for Mercedes-Benz Group with 558,400 cars and vans sold, a +6% increase vs. Q3 2025.
- Ola Källenius serves as Chairman of the Board of Management of Mercedes-Benz Group AG.
- In 2025, Mercedes-Benz kicked off its biggest product and tech launch program featuring cutting-edge electric-drive systems and intelligent software.
- The all-new CLA underscores the depth of quality and innovation in Mercedes-Benz products.
- In 2026, marking 140 years since inventing the automobile, Mercedes-Benz will continue its launch program to build desirable cars.
- Mercedes-Benz USA (MBUSA) is headquartered in Atlanta and is responsible for the distribution, marketing, and customer service for all Mercedes-Benz products in the United States.
- MBUSA offers drivers the most diverse lineup in the luxury segment with 18 model lines ranging from the sporty GLA SUV to the flagship S-Class and dynamic all-electric vehicles from Mercedes-Benz.
- MBUSA is also responsible for Mercedes-Benz Vans in the U.S.
- The Metris and Sprinter gas engine were discontinued for the U.S. market.
- Mercedes-Benz reports retail sales at the market level and group sales at a global level.
