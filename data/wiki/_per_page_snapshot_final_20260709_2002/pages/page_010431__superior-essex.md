- Strategic acquisitions focused on future-forward communications technology development and product sustainability have positioned Superior Essex Communications as an industry leader in communications cable product innovation, design, and manufacturing.
- Randy Butler serves as a Client Advisor on the Demand Creation Team at Superior Essex.

- Randy Butler supports the national sales team by writing project specifications and conducting technical presentations for Architects, Engineers, and Enterprise Corporations in the U.S., Canada, and the Caribbean.
