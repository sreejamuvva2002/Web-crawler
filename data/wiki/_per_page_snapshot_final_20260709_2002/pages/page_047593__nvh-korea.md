# NVH Korea

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [NVH Korea Leases Building 2 at Gardner Logistics Park - Connect CRE](https://www.connectcre.com/stories/nvh-korea-leases-building-2-at-gardner-logistics-park/)  ·  `page_047593`

## Facts

- Cushman & Wakefield has arranged a new long-term 234,200-square-foot lease for acoustic and thermal management company NVH Korea at Gardner Logistics Park in Locust Grove, GA.
- NVH Korea’s lease makes them the sixth major tenant for the park.
- The park is located at 381 Davis Lake Rd.
- Building 2 will be fully occupied by NVH Korea.
- Building 2 was built as part of Phase 2 of a six-building Class A development that will total roughly 4 million square feet when complete.
- The park will have two points of access to Interstate 75, both along US Route 23 and Market Place Boulevard and US Highway 42.
- Cushman & Wakefield’s James Phillpott, Ray Stache, Lisa Pittman, and Helen Cauthen represented the landlord, Scannell Properties, in the negotiations for NVH Korea’s new lease.
- The Cushman & Wakefield team has been overseeing leasing efforts for all three phases of the park’s development.
