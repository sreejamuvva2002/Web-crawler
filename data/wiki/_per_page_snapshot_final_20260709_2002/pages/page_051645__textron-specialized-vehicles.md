# Textron Specialized Vehicles

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2017-02-01

**Source:** [Ga. Dept. of Labor to hold recruitment for Textron Specialized Vehicles in Augusta](https://www.wrdw.com/content/news/Ga-Dept-of-Labor-to-hold-recruitment-for-Textron-Specialized-Vehicles-in-Augusta-412443773.html)  ·  `page_051645`

## Facts

- Textron Specialized Vehicles plans to recruit more than 75 people to work in Augusta, Georgia.
- The Georgia Department of Labor has said they will help with the recruitment process.
- The recruitment event will be held on Thursday, Feb. 9, from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.
- The recruitment location is the GDOL Augusta Career Center located at 601 Greene St. in Augusta.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles will recruit and conduct on-site interviews for assemblers, painter-production workers, powder painter technicians, press brake operators, sequence schedulers, team leaders, warehousing material handlers, welders, along with computer numerical control fabricators or laser operators.
- All applicants must be at least 18 years old to qualify for the positions.
- Experience requirements depend on the position.
