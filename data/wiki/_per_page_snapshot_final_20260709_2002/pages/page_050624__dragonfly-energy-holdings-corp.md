# Dragonfly Energy Holdings Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-30

**Source:** [Dragonfly Energy Enters into $30M Licensing Agreement for Battle Born Batteries Brand with Stryten Energy, a Leading North American Battery Manufacturer | Dragonfly Energy](https://dragonflyenergy.com/dragonfly-energy-enters-into-30m-licensing-agreement-for-battle-born-batteries-brand-with-stryten-energy-a-leading-north-american-battery-manufacturer/)  ·  `page_050624`

## Facts

- Dragonfly Energy Holdings Corp. signed a licensing agreement with a potential value of up to $30 million to license its Battle Born Batteries® brand to Stryten Energy for global distribution.
- The agreement grants Stryten Energy exclusive rights to market and distribute Battle Born Batteries for military, automotive, marine, power sports, lawn and garden, and golf cart applications.
- These licensed markets represent business-to-business (B2B) end markets where Dragonfly Energy currently has no presence.
- The partnership encompasses a mix of existing Battle Born Batteries products with a strong focus on developing new battery models tailored to Stryten Energy’s target markets.
- Dragonfly Energy will receive an initial upfront licensing fee of $5 million.
- Dragonfly Energy is entitled to mid-single digit royalty payments based on Stryten Energy’s product volume using the licensed branding.
- The royalty payments follow a tiered structure reaching up to an additional $25 million.
- Once the tiered royalties reach the maximum, Stryten Energy will be required to pay a nominal annual license fee.
- Additional fees will apply for battery design and contract manufacturing services provided outside of the licensing agreement.
- The agreement was announced on July 30, 2024, in Reno, Nev.
- Stryten Energy is a leading U.S.-based manufacturer of energy storage solutions headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Stryten Energy provides advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium batteries to customers across the transportation, industrial, military, and government sectors.
- Stryten Energy has a workforce of over 2,500 employees and operates ten manufacturing plants.
- Dragonfly Energy will initially contract manufacture all batteries distributed by Stryten Energy at its facility in Northern Nevada until Stryten Energy establishes its own domestic manufacturing footprint.
- Dragonfly Energy will continue independent operations in existing markets including recreational vehicles (RV), off-grid properties, heavy-duty trucking, stationary storage, and oil and gas.
- Dragonfly Energy specializes in cell manufacturing, battery pack assembly, and full system integration using a patented dry electrode manufacturing process for chemistry-agnostic power solutions.
- Dragonfly Energy's overarching mission includes the future deployment of proprietary, nonflammable, all-solid-state battery cells.
- Stryten Energy plans to leverage its vast distributor and customer network, including automotive aftermarket retailers and battery distributors, to introduce Battle Born Batteries branded products to new B2B markets.
- Dragonfly Energy remains committed to providing exceptional technical support for all Battle Born Batteries products and will continue to grow its support team along with increasing demand.
- Dr. Denis Phares serves as chief executive officer of Dragonfly Energy.
- Mike Judd serves as chief executive officer of Stryten Energy.
- Tyler Bourns serves as chief marketing officer at Dragonfly Energy.
- “At Dragonfly Energy, we’re incredibly proud to see the Battle Born Batteries brand take this leap forward into new end markets,” said Dr. Denis Phares, chief executive officer of Dragonfly Energy.
- “At Stryten Energy, we’re focused on providing premium energy storage solutions to our customers,” said Mike Judd, chief executive officer of Stryten Energy.
- “The impact of this partnership with Stryten Energy for the wider proliferation of the Battle Born Batteries brand cannot be understated,” said Tyler Bourns, chief marketing officer at Dragonfly Energy.
