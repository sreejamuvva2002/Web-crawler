# Pirelli Tire North America Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America Inc. has announced a multi-year partnership with the Miami Open.
- As an official partner, Pirelli will be integrated throughout the tournament experience, including on-court visibility, digital initiatives, and fan engagement activations, beginning with the 2026 edition of the event.
- The 2026 Miami Open is scheduled for March 15–29 at Hard Rock Stadium in Miami Gardens, Fla.
- Pirelli officials stated that North America represents a core pillar of Pirelli’s growth, innovation, and brand leadership as one of the most important stages for global sport.
- The Miami Open offers a platform to connect with fans, partners, and consumers across the region and around the world.
- Claudio Zanardo, CEO of Pirelli North America, stated that North America is a priority market for Pirelli from commercial, brand relevance, and cultural influence standpoints.
- Zanardo noted that partnering with the Miami Open strengthens Pirelli’s presence on a prestigious stage in a city reflecting the energy, diversity, and international reach of the North American market.
