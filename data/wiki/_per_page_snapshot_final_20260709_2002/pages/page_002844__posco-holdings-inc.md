# POSCO HOLDINGS INC.

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2027-12-31

**Source:** [Form 6-K](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/889132/000119312525320144/d16443d6k.htm)  ·  `page_002844`

## Facts

- POSCO HOLDINGS INC. is making an equity investment alongside Hyundai Motor Group to jointly construct an electric arc furnace steel mill in Louisiana, USA.
- The total contribution by POSCO HOLDINGS will be approximately USD 582 million (approximately KRW 858.6 billion).
- POSCO HOLDINGS will establish a wholly-owned special purpose company named POS-Louisiana (tentative), which will subsequently invest in 20% of Hyundai Steel Louisiana LLC shares.
- The acquisition cost is listed as KRW 858,561,094,870.
- The investment represents a 2.6% ratio to equity capital and is classified as a large-scale corporation.
- POSLouisiana (tentative) will be incorporated in Delaware, USA.
- The scheduled acquisition date is December 31, 2027, which marks the final year of capital payment for the steel mill construction.
- Payments will be executed in stages during the construction period, and schedules are subject to change based on project progress.
- The exchange rate used for conversion was KRW 1,474.10 per dollar as of December 16, 2025.
- The investment aims to respond to changes in the North American steel market and secure a foundation for sustainable automotive steel production.
- This disclosure confirms details previously reported by The Financial News on April 22, 2025, regarding POSCO investing in Hyundai Motor’s US steel mills to boost synergy in the EV supply chain.
- The ratio of total assets for this investment is 59.1% based on POSCO HOLDINGS INC's statement of financial position as of December 31, 2024.
- The board resolution date for this decision was December 16, 2025, with two outside directors present.
- Han, Young-Ah, Senior Vice President, signed the report on behalf of POSCO HOLDINGS INC.
