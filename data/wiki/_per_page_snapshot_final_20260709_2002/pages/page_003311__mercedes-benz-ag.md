# Mercedes-Benz AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-05-22

**Source:** [Mercedes-Benz Establishing North American Headquarters, new Research & | Georgia](https://georgia.org/press-releases/2025/mercedes-benz-establishing-north-american-headquarters-new-research-development-hub)  ·  `page_003311`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced on May 22, 2025, that Mercedes-Benz will establish Atlanta as its North American headquarters by centralizing and uniting key corporate functions.
- The company will move up to 500 jobs to the existing Mercedes-Benz facility known as “1MB” in Fulton County.
- The company will make a multi-million dollar investment in a future state-of-the-art Research & Development (R&D) facility located near the “1MB” facility.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp stated that Georgia continues to lead in the future of mobility and technical innovation, attracting world-class companies like Mercedes-Benz that are driving the automotive industry forward.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp noted that Mercedes-Benz is a job creator with close ties to Georgia that is doubling down on its choice and growing its presence in what he called the best state for business and opportunity.
- Mercedes-Benz opened its “1MB” facility in 2018 in Sandy Springs.
- The “1MB” facility currently supports approximately 800 jobs in Georgia.
- Jason Hoff, CEO of Mercedes-Benz North America, thanked the State of Georgia for its support in deepening Mercedes-Benz’s roots in the Atlanta area as the company brings more talented team members to the city.
- Jason Hoff stated that bringing teams closer together will enable Mercedes-Benz to be more agile, increase speed to market, and ensure the best customer experience.
- The “1MB” facility located in Sandy Springs will house existing sales teams, financial services teams, and corporate functions.
- The new state-of-the-art Research & Development hub will be located near Sandy Springs.
- The company anticipates that the move to metro Atlanta will be completed by August 2026.
- Sandy Springs Mayor Rusty Paul expressed excitement over Mercedes-Benz expanding in Sandy Springs, noting they have been outstanding corporate partners since establishing their headquarters there in 2018.
- Mayor Rusty Paul stated that the expansion highlights the success of the city’s recent infrastructure and capital investments which are now clearly paying dividends.
- Fulton County Board of Commissioners Chairman Robb Pitts stated that Mercedes-Benz reaffirming its commitment by investing and growing in Fulton County is a testament to the strength and vitality of their community.
- Chairman Robb Pitts noted that this proves Fulton County continues to be a destination for corporate solutions, providing major companies an accessible, vibrant, and growing community for their business to thrive.
- Metro Atlanta Chamber President & CEO Katie Kirkpatrick stated the expansion is a testament to both Mercedes-Benz’s commitment to excellence and metro Atlanta's strength as a hub for innovation and talent.
- Katie Kirkpatrick noted that when the 1MB facility opened in 2018, it quickly became an integral part of the business landscape, driving economic growth and elevating the region’s global presence.
- Assistant Director of Statewide Projects Elizabeth McLean represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team on this competitive project.
- The project was developed in partnership with the City of Sandy Springs, Select Fulton, Metro Atlanta Chamber, and Georgia Power.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that since the strategic decision to relocate Mercedes-Benz USA to Sandy Springs in 2018, they have watched Mercedes-Benz become an integral part of their business community.
- Commissioner Pat Wilson noted that this expansion and commitment to R&D in the metro Atlanta area will further strengthen the company’s long-term success and highlights talent and collaborative partnerships fostered by the University System of Georgia.
- Mercedes-Benz AG is part of the Mercedes-Benz Group AG, which has a total of around 175,000 employees worldwide.
- Mercedes-Benz AG is responsible for the global business of Mercedes-Benz Cars and Mercedes-Benz Vans.
- Ola Källenius serves as Chairman of the Board of Management of Mercedes-Benz AG.
- The company focuses on the development, production, and sales of passenger cars, vans, and vehicle-related services.
- The company aspires to be the leader in the fields of electric mobility and vehicle software.
- The product portfolio comprises the Mercedes-Benz brand with Mercedes AMG, Mercedes Maybach, and G-Class with their all-electric models as well as products of the smart brand.
- Mercedes-Benz AG is one of the world’s largest manufacturers of high-end passenger cars.
