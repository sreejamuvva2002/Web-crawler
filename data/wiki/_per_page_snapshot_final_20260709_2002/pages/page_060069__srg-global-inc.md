## Facts

- SRG Global, Inc. was established in 2009.
- The company is headquartered in Troy, Michigan, US.
- SRG Global specializes in designing and manufacturing high-quality automotive components.
- The company's product portfolio includes grilles, emblems, nameplates, trim pieces, bezels, and moldings.
- Products are designed to enhance vehicle aesthetics and functionality through innovative solutions tailored to client needs.
- Product development is driven by customer feedback and market dynamics.
- Manufacturing processes include injection molding, painting, and plating to ensure meticulous finishes.
- SRG Global's core client base includes major automotive manufacturers and suppliers across North America, Europe, and Asia.
- The company operates manufacturing facilities in Mexico, Poland, Spain, Germany, Thailand, and China.
- Revenue generation is primarily driven through B2B transactions with automotive manufacturers and suppliers, focusing on long-term partnerships.
- The company engages in direct sales agreements that provide tailored solutions based on specific client needs.
- Transaction structures involve contractual agreements facilitating bulk orders of automotive components to ensure efficient delivery and service.
- SRG Global leverages its global manufacturing presence to manage inventory and logistics, aligning with client demands to optimize performance and profitability.
- An emphasis on customized solutions allows for flexible pricing strategies catering to the unique requirements of each customer segment.
