# Mobis U.S. Alabama LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Mobis US Alabama](https://www.mua.us.com/)  ·  `page_046748`

## Facts

- The facility is located in McCalla, Alabama.
- It was established in 2020 as a Tier 1 supplier to Mercedes Benz U.S. International (MBUSI).
- It assembles and supplies front and rear chassis modules used on certain vehicles produced by MBUSI.
- The facility uses components from domestic and overseas suppliers to produce finished products for MBUSI using state-of-the-art technology in a just-in-time, just-in-sequence system.
- In 2020, Hyundai MOBIS officially announced plans to expand its supply of electric car parts to OEMs outside the Hyundai family.
- Mobis U.S. Alabama serves as a key part of Hyundai MOBIS's expansion into supplying electric car parts to non-Hyundai OEMs.
- The company maintains a job referral program where individuals can fill out a form to refer friends for potential career opportunities.
