# Michelin North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-16

**Source:** [MICHELIN ANNOUNCES FULL ROLLOUT OF TREADEYE TECHNOLOGY ACROSS U.S. RETREAD NETWORK](https://icrowdnewswire.com/2025/10/16/michelin-announces-full-rollout-of-treadeye-technology-across-u-s-retread-network/)  ·  `page_046509`

## Facts

- Michelin announced the full deployment of TreadEye technology across its U.S. retread network following a phased implementation period.
- TreadEye is a proprietary tread depth assessment tool designed to help fleets maximize their tire investment, with every 32nd of an inch counting toward value.
- The system accurately and consistently collects 1,200 measurement points to provide precise insights into tread wear and casing condition.
- Nate Kirian, Vice President of Sales and Distribution for Commercial Business Lines at Michelin North America, Inc., stated that TreadEye is a game-changer for retread operations and the fleets they serve.
- The technology identifies pull points by fleet location.
- TreadEye maximizes rubber on the road by avoiding early pulls.
- The system helps prevent casing damage.
- TreadEye helps reduce DOT violations and downtime from late pulls.
- The technology improves efficiency in the tire removal process, directly impacting a fleet’s bottom line.
- Michelin is a world-leading manufacturer of engineered materials and composites with over 130 years of experience.
- Drawing on deep knowledge in polymer composites, Michelin manufactures high-quality tires and components for mobility, construction, aeronautics, low-carbon energies, and healthcare.
- Michelin offers data- and AI-based connected solutions for professional fleets.
- Michelin North America, Inc. is headquartered in Greenville, S.C.
- Michelin North America has approximately 23,500 employees.
- Michelin North America operates 36 production facilities in the United States and Canada.
