## Facts

- Bridgestone Americas Tire Operations (BATO) is a business unit of Bridgestone Americas, Inc., whose parent company, Bridgestone Corporation, is the world's largest tire and rubber company.
- Reporting into BATO are Latin American tire operations; U.S. and Canadian consumer tire businesses; U.S. and Canadian commercial tire businesses; and Bridgestone Retail Operations LLC, which operates the largest network of company-owned automotive service providers in the world.
- BATO develops, manufactures, and markets Bridgestone, Firestone, and associate brand tires for retail, wholesale, and original equipment markets.
- The business unit supplies passenger, light truck, commercial vehicle, off-road, motorcycle, agricultural, and other tires, as well as retreads, to customers in the Americas.
- The Bridgestone M870 All-Position Tire is designed for urban waste fleets in high-scrub and short-haul applications, featuring longer wear performance.
- The Bridgestone Speedco Mobile App Update allows drivers to locate Speedco locations along planned routes and includes current weather forecasts.
- The Bridgestone V-Steel Snow Wedge All-Season (VSWAS) radial tire is designed for grader machines used for snow removal and surface leveling.
- Bridgestone Commercial offers eight Dayton commercial truck tire patterns through its dedicated website.
- The Bridgestone Bandag B799 tread product meets the needs of fleets operating in mixed service environments requiring on-road and off-road performance.
- Bridgestone Commercial Solutions' Ecopia tire line, No. R268, is designed for regional and pickup and delivery fleets to withstand high scrub environments.
- The Bridgestone Ecopia premium drive radial tire, No. M760, has both EPA SmartWay and CARB compliance and delivers high traction and long mileage in a fuel-efficient design.
- Bridgestone Commercial Solutions' FT491 All-Position Radial tire features ULTI-Rib and Defense Groove designs to combat irregular wear and includes a sidewall protector.
- Bridgestone voluntarily recalled approximately 2,700 all position, wide base radial commercial truck tires in size 425/65R22.5.
- May 20-27 is designated as National Tire Safety Week, an industry initiative educating the public on tire safety.
- Leaders from O’Reilly, Advance Auto Parts, and Bridgestone Retail Operations will speak on preparing for tomorrow’s changing automotive technologies.
- A new partnership with Wrench is expected to increase both companies' ability to meet car owners' needs in mobile vehicle inspection, repair, and maintenance.
- Bridgestone Americas announced two new Bandag retreads designed to balance dependable performance and affordability with tough compounds.
- A service program at more than 50 Speedco locations nationwide includes free battery testing and adds Interstate Batteries to the product offering.
- The Bandag BRM3 retread features a proprietary compound and advanced tread design engineered for long wear, durability, and traction.
- A program makes Shell Lubricants products, including Pennzoil, Quaker State motor oils, and Shell Rotella engine oils, available to Bridgestone Affiliated Retailers nationwide.
- Bridgestone Commercial will officially launch its newly refreshed Dayton commercial truck tires brand at the Mid-America Trucking Show.
- Bridgestone Americas announced that its Warren County, Tenn. tire manufacturing plant has earned LEED V3 recertification.
- BCS created a mobile app in response to customer demands for robust tools providing detailed product specifications.
- The Bridgestone M853 new on/off highway all-position radial tire for regional hauls combines wear resistance with extra load capacity.
- Bridgestone's Greatec M825 wide base radial drive tire has a wider tread and very deep 29/32-inch tread depth to distribute weight and torque over a wider area.
- Bridgestone Firestone North American Tire, LLC is offering fleets in the U.S. the new Bridgestone M775 on/off-highway drive radial, popular with logging and oil industries.
- The new Firestone FD835 On/Off-Highway drive delivers superior traction and exceptional removal mileage in severe-service applications with an aggressive design.
- New Bridgestone R250 ED extra-duty all-position radials use extra-durable tread compounds for long tread life in demanding P'D and high-scrub regional applications.
- New 32/32 in. mega-deep Bridgestone M726 EL drive radials use high-grip shoulder notches and aggressive blocks for excellent traction on soft surfaces.
- Ultra-deep 29/32 in. Firestone FD690 PLUS drive radials are specially designed to perform on tractors with single or tandem drive axles, featuring an extra-deep solid shoulder design.
- Bridgestone Firestone's National Preferred program offers guaranteed, published prices on tires and emergency road service through America's largest network of truckstops.
- Bridgestone's "Longer Mileage Program" Videos DVD helps technicians and maintenance personnel inspect, diagnose, and maintain truck tires to increase tread life.
