# DENSO Manufacturing Tennessee

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2012-05-10

**Source:** [DENSO Manufacturing Tennessee Ramps Up Production for Stop/Start Starters - AftermarketNews](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/denso-manufacturing-tennessee-ramps-up-production-for-stop-start-starters/)  ·  `page_053814`

## Facts

- DENSO is ramping up production of stop/start starters at its DENSO Manufacturing Tennessee (DMTN) plant in Maryville, Tenn.
- The new stop/start starters are due to hit U.S. roads later this year.
- The stop/start technology improves fuel efficiency by cutting the engine when the vehicle is at a stop or in other idle traffic situations.
- The new production line will result in approximately 75 positions over the next two years, according to DENSO.
- U.S. automakers are adopting stop/start technology to meet upcoming fuel requirements.
- Stop/start systems reduce fuel consumption and CO2 emissions by stopping the engine when the vehicle is idling at a stop light or in other traffic situations.
- Depending on automaker specifications, the technology can help improve fuel efficiency by approximately 3 to 5 percent.
- DENSO has future stop/start system technology that will improve fuel efficiency by more than 7 percent depending on the automaker’s overall systems approach.
