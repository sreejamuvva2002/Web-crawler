# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation (YMMC) SMART Warehouse

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation Breaks Ground on Innovative SMART Warehouse in Newnan, Georgia](https://www.01net.it/yamaha-motor-manufacturing-corporation-breaks-ground-on-innovative-smart-warehouse-in-newnan-georgia/)  ·  `page_015634`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation (YMMC), based in Newnan, Georgia, broke ground on a new “SMART” Warehouse.
- The SMART Warehouse is the first-of-its-kind facility of its type for Yamaha Motor Company worldwide.
- The 200,000 square foot facility will utilize state-of-the-art automated storage and retrieval systems.
- The warehouse will reduce response time for fulfilling dealer orders for Yamaha WaveRunners, Golf Cars, ATVs, and Side-by-Sides by more than 50 percent.
- Mike Chrzanowski, President and CEO of Yamaha Motor Corporation, stated the facility will improve operational efficiency and material flow velocity to factory assembly lines.
- The improved logistics will allow Yamaha to get product to dealers and customers faster.
- Demand for outdoor recreational products produced at this Georgia factory continues to exceed supply.
- SMART is an acronym for Sequenced Material and Reduced Transportation.
- The medium parts automated storage and retrieval system will retrieve and put away 240 containers per hour, compared to the current rate of 170 containers per hour.
- The small part automated system will handle 2400 containers per hour, compared to the current capability of 550 containers per day.
- Automatic conveyors and tuggers will move parts through the SMART center to factories, enabling a 50 percent reduction in forklifts.
- The investment underscores YMMC’s long-term commitment to employees, the City of Newnan, and Coweta County.
- The facility will bring higher paid and more skilled jobs to Coweta County, including roles for robotics experts, software developers, and systems engineers.
- SSI SCHAEFER designed the new SMART Warehouse.
- The warehouse will include an observation platform for local school students to learn about manufacturing and automated processes.
- Yamaha expects to complete the SMART Warehouse in 2024.
- Yamaha Motor Corporation, USA (YMUS) operates corporate offices in California and two in Georgia, with facilities in Wisconsin and Alabama, and factory operations in Tennessee and Georgia.
- YMUS sells products through a nationwide network of distributors and dealers in the United States.
- Additional U.S.-based subsidiaries include Yamaha Marine Systems Company (with divisions Bennett Marine in Florida, Kracor Systems in Wisconsin, and Siren Marine, Inc. in Rhode Island), Skeeter Boats in Texas with division G3 Boats in Missouri, and Yamaha Precision Propeller in Indiana.
