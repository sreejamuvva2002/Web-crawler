# Peterson Spring

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-04-22

**Source:** [MiddleGround Capital Acquires Peterson Spring - MiddleGround Capital](https://middleground.com/news/middleground-capital-acquires-peterson-spring/)  ·  `page_048139`

## Facts

- MiddleGround Capital acquired Peterson Spring, a leading specialty spring manufacturer with thirteen facilities throughout North America and the United Kingdom.
- The acquisition marks the closure of the second transaction in MiddleGround Capital's inaugural private equity fund.
- Peterson Spring is based in Southfield, Michigan, and designs and manufactures highly engineered springs, wire forms, related components, and sub-assemblies.
- Peterson Spring provides mission-critical components to a variety of industries.
- MiddleGround Capital co-founder John Stewart stated the investment seeks to build upon the company's strong foundation, position it for growth, and ensure manufacturing employment opportunities for generations to come.
- MiddleGround Capital Partner Scot Duncan stated the investment will enable the company to meet growing customer demands by increasing operational efficiency and providing necessary resources.
- Co-founders John Stewart and Scot Duncan have over 30 years of combined experience in operational roles at Toyota Motor Company, including leadership positions in engineering, maintenance, production, and manufacturing.
- Mr. Stewart, Ms. Mulholland, and Mr. Duncan have been working together in private equity for the past eight years as of April 2019.
