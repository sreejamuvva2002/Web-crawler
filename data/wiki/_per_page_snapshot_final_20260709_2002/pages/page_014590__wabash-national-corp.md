# Wabash National Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-09-08

**Source:** [Wabash National expanding to Ohio - Indianapolis News | Indiana Weather | Indiana Traffic | WISH-TV |](https://www.wishtv.com/news/inside-indiana-business/wabash-national-expanding-to-ohio/)  ·  `page_014590`

## Facts

- Wabash National Corp. (NYSE: WNC) announced a plan to expand its national footprint in the cargo van and truck body upfitting space with a new facility in Groveport, Ohio.
- The new location will allow for greater speed and innovation to meet needs in the areas of cold chain, home delivery and parts and service.
- The facility will service all brands of cargo and truck bodies.
- The facility will install several products, including hitches, steel flatbed bodies, thermal solutions, liftgates and more.
- Vice President of Parts and Services Nick Adler stated the company is building a culture around speed and safety to extend to customers seeking productivity solutions.
- With support from supplier partners, Wabash National is expanding capabilities to provide first to final mile upfitting solutions faster and configured to specific customer needs.
- The company plans to hold an open house at the new facility in early 2022.
