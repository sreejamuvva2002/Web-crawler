## Facts

- Jefferson Southern Corporation is a Tier 1 supplier of structural steel parts to Honda.
- The company is in the middle of an expansion of its production facility.
- The facility expansion will add 81,000 sq. ft. to the plant.
- The facility expansion includes new presses.
- The facility expansion includes an expanded recycling system.
- The expansion is creating 40 new jobs over the next year.
- Honda and other customers are world leaders in quality that are demanding of their suppliers.
- Jefferson Southern Corporation states it must produce the highest quality parts to keep customers like Honda and continue its business.
- Jefferson Southern Corporation maintains a reputation as a high quality supplier of stamped and welded parts at the most competitive prices.
