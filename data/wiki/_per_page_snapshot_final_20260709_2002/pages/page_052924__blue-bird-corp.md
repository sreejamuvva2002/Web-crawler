# Blue Bird Corp

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-06-10

**Source:** [Blue Bird Corp moves into truck segment with unveiling of Class 5-6 chassis at ACT Expo](https://truckandbusbuilder.com/article/2022/06/10/blue-bird-corp-moves-into-truck-segment-with-unveiling-of-class-5-6-chassis-at-act-expo)  ·  `page_052924`

## Facts

- Macon, Georgia-based school bus builder Blue Bird Corp has entered the truck segment.
- Blue Bird Corp announced its Class 5-6 EV stripped chassis at ACT Expo in Long Beach, California.
- The chassis powertrain is provided by Colorado-based Lightning eMotors Inc.
- The chassis is suitable for last-mile delivery step vans, motorhomes, and other specialty vehicles.
- Production of the chassis will begin in late 2023 at Blue Bird’s manufacturing plant in Fort Valley, Georgia.
- The chassis features a modular design that allows for multiple battery configurations ranging from 70kWh up to 225kWh.
- At its high-end configuration, the chassis supports a maximum vehicle range of 175 miles (280km) on a single charge.
- The chassis is offered in three wheelbase options of 178”, 190”, and 208”.
- The chassis supports a maximum GVWR of 26,000 lbs (11.8 tonnes).
