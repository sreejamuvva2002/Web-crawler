# ABC Technologies

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-29

**Source:** [ABC Technologies Announces Recommended Offer To Acquire TI Fluid Systems - ABC Technologies](https://abcmoldingthefuture.com/abc-technologies-announces-recommended-offerto-acquire-ti-fluid-systems/)  ·  `page_011555`

## Facts

- ABC Technologies announced a recommended all-cash offer to acquire the entire issued and to be issued ordinary share capital of TI Fluid Systems plc.
- The acquisition will be executed by ABC Technologies Acquisitions Limited.
- TI Fluid Systems is a London-listed, market-leading global manufacturer of thermal and fluid system solutions for the full range of current and developing vehicle architectures.
- TI Fluid Systems operates across 27 countries and serves all major automotive manufacturers.
- The combined entity would create an industry leader with an extensive global product portfolio and financial strength to support long-term growth objectives.
- The transaction provides access to a broader and more diversified range of customers, including some of the largest and most recognizable automotive OEMs and Tier One suppliers worldwide.
- Terry Campbell, President and CEO of ABC Technologies, stated the transaction is a transformative strategic opportunity that unlocks value for all stakeholders and provides a platform for further growth.
- ABC Technologies is majority-owned by funds managed by affiliates of Apollo Global Management, Inc.
- Shareholders of TI Fluid Systems will be entitled to receive 200 pence per share under the terms of the transaction.
- The deal values TI Fluid Systems at an enterprise value of approximately £1,831 million.
- The acquisition is expected to complete in H1 2025, subject to shareholder and other relevant legal and regulatory approvals.
- Lazard acted as lead financial advisor to ABC Technologies.
- Citi, TD Securities, and Scotiabank also acted as financial advisers for the transaction.
- Kirkland & Ellis International acted as legal advisor to ABC Technologies.
- Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison acted as legal advisor for regulatory and financing matters.
