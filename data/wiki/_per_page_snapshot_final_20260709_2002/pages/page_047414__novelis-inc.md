## Facts

- Novelis Inc., a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries, announced plans to raise $750 million through a bond issue.
- The bonds will be issued by its wholly-owned subsidiary, Novelis Corporation.
- The company intends to use the proceeds primarily to pay down $738 million of existing debt.
- Any remaining funds from the offering will be directed toward increasing its cash reserves.
- The company stated that it has priced the bond offering at $750 million in senior notes with a 6.875% interest rate.
- The senior notes mature in January 2030.
- This amount represents an increase of $250 million over the previously anticipated offering size.
- Novelis is looking to strengthen its balance sheet with this initiative.
- In its most recent financial report, Novelis revealed an 18% drop in net income.
- The company reported $128 million in net income for the September quarter of fiscal 2024-25.
- This compares to $157 million in net income during the same period the previous year.
- Despite the decrease in profits, Novelis saw a 4.5% increase in net sales, reaching $4.3 billion.
- The increase in net sales was driven by higher aluminum prices and a modest rise in shipments of flat-rolled products.
- The company’s second-quarter success was also fueled by a record number of beverage packaging shipments.
- Novelis's global footprint helped minimize the impact of production disruptions due to flooding at the Sierre facility.
- Steve Fisher serves as President and CEO of Novelis.
- Devinder Ahuja serves as Executive Vice President and CFO of Novelis.
- Fisher and Ahuja emphasized the importance of disciplined balance sheet management as the company navigates current market challenges and advances its ongoing investments.
- Novelis is driven by a commitment to shaping a more sustainable world through collaboration.
- The company is the world’s largest recycler of aluminum.
- Novelis aims to lead in providing low-carbon, sustainable aluminum solutions and establish a fully circular economy.
- The company works closely with suppliers and customers across industries such as aerospace, automotive, beverage packaging, and specialties.
- Novelis operates across North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- In fiscal year 2024, Novelis reported net sales of $16.2 billion.
- Novelis Inc. is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited.
- Novelis is part of the Aditya Birla Group, a multinational conglomerate based in Mumbai.
- The Aditya Birla Group is a recognized leader in aluminum and copper production.
