# JAC Products Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2011-11-01

**Source:** [Saline auto supplier JAC Products leases 46,500-square-foot warehouse](https://www.annarbor.com/business-review/saline-auto-supplier-jac-products-leases-46500-square-foot-warehouse/)  ·  `page_056402`

## Facts

- Auto supplier JAC Products Inc. struck a deal to lease a 46,500-square-foot warehouse on Woodland Drive in Saline.
- The deal expands JAC Products' presence in Saline.
- The company was acquired in late 2010 by Chicago-based private equity firm Wynnchurch Capital Ltd.
- JAC Products employs 412 workers at its 180,000-square-foot warehouse on Industrial Drive.
- The leased warehouse space will accommodate off-site storage of older model year parts and tools needed to service aftermarket needs.
- JAC Products moved its warehouse from Manchester to Saline to save cost and convenience.
- Signature Associates' Jay Chavey, Brad Viergever, Joe Hamway, and Steve Gordon represented the parties in the transaction.
- The warehouse property owner is auto supplier DURA Automotive Systems Inc., represented by Signature's Jay Chavey and Brad Viergever.
- JAC Products recently received a $572,970 tax credit from the Michigan Economic Development Corp. and a tax abatement from the city of Saline to support a 30,000-square-foot addition to its manufacturing facility.
- The company plans to add 87 jobs as part of the expansion.
- JAC Products specializes in roof rack manufacturing for major automakers including Toyota, Ford, Chrysler, Nissan, and Hyundai.
- The 180,000-square-foot facility on Industrial Drive is a plastic injection molding facility that houses JAC's corporate headquarters.
- JAC Products leases a 60,000-square-foot building to the north of its main facility where it assembles its product.
