# Ecoplastic America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-03

**Source:** [Five South Architecture, LLC to Lead Design of New 285,000sf Ecoplastic America Manufacturing Facility in Statesboro, GA - EIN Presswire](https://www.einnews.com/pr_news/799838294/five-south-architecture-llc-to-lead-design-of-new-285-000sf-ecoplastic-america-manufacturing-facility-in-statesboro-ga)  ·  `page_019857`

## Facts

- Five South Architecture, LLC is leading the architectural design of a new manufacturing facility for Ecoplastic America.
- The new manufacturing facility will be 285,000 square feet in size.
- The facility is located in Statesboro, Georgia.
- Ecoplastic America is a major supplier to Hyundai Motor Company.
- The new facility will enhance Ecoplastic America's current capacity to deliver high-quality automotive components.
- The facility will strengthen the supply chain for Hyundai’s expanding U.S. operations.
- Hyundai Motor Group announced a $20 billion investment plan in the United States.
- Hyundai's investment plan includes the development of a new steel plant in Louisiana.
- Hyundai's investment plan includes ongoing electric vehicle (EV) and battery production initiatives.
- Hyundai's investment aims to create thousands of jobs across multiple states, including Louisiana, Georgia, and Alabama.
- Justin Rogers serves as the Owner of Five South Architecture, LLC.
- Five South Architecture is currently providing architectural design services for a new 465,000-square-foot Hyundai Mobis spare parts logistics facility in Montgomery, Alabama.
- The Hyundai Mobis spare parts logistics facility is valued at $52 million.
- The Hyundai Mobis facility broke ground in October 2024.
- The Hyundai Mobis facility will serve as a critical hub for aftermarket parts supporting Hyundai and Kia dealerships nationwide.
- Five South Architecture, LLC is based in Birmingham, Alabama.
- Five South Architecture specializes in architectural solutions for industrial and commercial clients.
