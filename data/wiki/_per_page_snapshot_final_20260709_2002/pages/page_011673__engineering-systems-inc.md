# Engineering Systems Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-18

**Source:** [ESi Named 2023 GLOBE Award Recipient | Engineering Systems Inc.](https://www.engsys.com/news/esi-recognized-for-global-trade-by-georgia-department-of-economic-development/)  ·  `page_011673`

## Facts

- ESi was officially announced a 2023 GLOBE Award recipient by the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD).
- The award program is managed by the GDEcD International Trade team and highlights Georgia companies that contribute to the state’s economic growth through global trade and export development.
- This is ESi’s first time receiving the GLOBE Award.
- The recognition honors international outreach efforts driven by ESi’s Georgia office.
- ESi President and Chief Operating Officer Will Pinkston stated that many employees from across the company have been instrumental in introducing services into new international markets, creating a wealth of opportunities for ESi, its partners, and its clients.
- Jay Sohn served as Consultant and lead for Asia Pacific engagement.
- Matt Kenner served as Principal and Sr. Director of Mechanics for work in the Republic of Korea.
- Hernán Mercado-Corujo served as Managing Consultant and Director of Automotive Technology.
- Diana Estrada served as Sr. Consultant for efforts in Mexico and Central America.
- Bob Winn served as Principal Emeritus.
- Alfred Pettinger served as Principal and Vice President.
- Bill Broz served as Principal.
- Chuck Fox served as Sr. Director of Technical Services.
- Tim Jung served as Sr. Managing Consultant and Director of Aviation.
- Kara Greene served as Sr. Staff Consultant.
- Heather Slatton served as Business Development Director.
- Kate Champion served as Claims Relationship Manager.
- Tony Dill (COL Ret) leads ESi’s Security and Defense Services Group, expanding services to allies and defense companies abroad.
- ESi provides on-demand engineering consulting and technical support services spanning nearly every engineering specialty.
- ESi serves clients across diverse industries including construction, legal, industrial and manufacturing, insurance, oil and gas, pharmaceutical and medical devices, power and energy, security and defense, transportation, and more.
- ESi utilizes a go-to-market approach that taps into industry, cross-industry, and technical expertise from across its 20 offices to tailor teams to client needs.
- In 2022, ESi began exporting services from its Georgia office to new markets in the Republic of Korea and expanded business development efforts in Mexico and the United Kingdom.
- The GLOBE Awards program began in 2014 to recognize local businesses contributing to Georgia’s economic growth through global trade.
- Since 2014, GDEcD has presented 323 GLOBE Awards to companies across 46 Georgia counties.
