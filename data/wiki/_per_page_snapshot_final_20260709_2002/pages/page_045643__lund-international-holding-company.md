# Lund International Holding Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2013-10-04

**Source:** [Lund International, a Portfolio Company of Highlander Partners, Acquires the Assets of AMP Research – Highlander Partners](https://highlander-partners.com/news-posts/lund-international-a-portfolio-company-of-highlander-partners-acquires-the-assets-of-amp-research/)  ·  `page_045643`

## Facts

- Lund International Holding Company, a portfolio company of Highlander Partners, L.P., announced it has acquired all of the operating assets of AMP Research on October 4, 2013.
- AMP Research is based in Tustin, California and is the leading provider of automatic power articulating running boards, bed steps, bed extenders and related products.
- Lund will continue to use the trademarked POWERSTEP™, BEDSTEP®, BEDSTEP2™ and BEDXTENDER HD™ product names included in AMP’s Innovation in Motion product lines.
- The current Tustin location will continue to be the primary manufacturing facility for the acquired assets.
- AMP Research’s current workforce will join the Lund International team.
- Mitch Fogle serves as President of Lund.
- Jeff L. Hull serves as Chairman of Lund and Managing Partner of Highlander Partners.
- The acquisition reinforces Lund’s emerging position as the leader in the running board and nerf bar category.
- Lund will sell the patent protected and unique POWERSTEP™ automatic power articulating running board under the Lund brand in its new Innovation in Motion series.
- The BEDSTEP®, BEDSTEP2™, BEDXTENDER™ HD, and fuel door products are excellent complements to Lund’s existing business.
- This acquisition marks Lund’s third acquisition in 2013.
- Regions Business Capital, with Regions Bank as Administrative Agent, along with a syndicate of participating lenders, provided senior financing in support of the transaction.
- Lund International Holding Company is a leading designer, manufacturer, and marketer of branded automotive aftermarket accessories for passenger cars, light trucks, and heavy trucks.
- Lund’s product portfolio includes Ventvisors®, hood shields, floor mats, fender flares, tonneau covers, storage boxes, and running boards.
- Highlander Partners, L.P. is a Dallas-based private investment firm with partners that have committed over $500 million of principal funds for direct private equity investments in North America and Central Europe.
- Highlander Partners focuses on making investments in middle market businesses in targeted industries including basic manufacturing, food, building materials and others.
- Highlander Partners uses a “buy and build” investment approach to create value by helping companies grow organically and through acquisitions.
