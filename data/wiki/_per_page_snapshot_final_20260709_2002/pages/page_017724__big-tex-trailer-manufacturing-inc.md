# Big Tex Trailer Manufacturing, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Big Tex Acquires Circle J Trailers | Trailer Body Builders](https://www.trailer-bodybuilders.com/trailers/article/21736378/big-tex-acquires-circle-j-trailers)  ·  `page_017724`

## Facts

- Big Tex Trailer Manufacturing, Inc. has acquired Circle J Trailers of Caldwell, Idaho.
- Circle J Trailers is a 51-year-old manufacturer of steel, aluminum exterior, and all-aluminum horse trailers.
- Circle J Trailers is one of the oldest trailer companies in the United States and a respected name in the horse trailer industry.
- Operations will continue in Caldwell in the current facility.
- Big Tex recognizes the value and reputation of Circle J Trailers and its premium quality trailers continuing to play a leading role in the Western U.S. and Western Canadian markets through its network of loyal and capable dealers.
- In addition to the continued manufacture of Circle J trailers, Big Tex trailers will also be produced in Caldwell.
- The large facility will serve as a distribution center for CM Trailers and CM Truck Bed products.
- Ricky Baker serves as President/CEO of Big Tex.
- Ricky Baker stated: "We are really excited about this new opportunity with Circle J Trailers. The name is legendary and it is truly an honor to be associated with the history and reputation of Circle J."
- Baker noted that the acquisition will enable serving dealers in the region with other products, offering faster deliveries, lower prices, and the opportunity to purchase less than truckload quantities.
- In February, Big Tex announced the purchase of a manufacturing facility in Cordele, Georgia.
- The Cordele, Georgia facility has begun distribution of Big Tex, CM, S&H, and CM truck beds.
- Manufacturing operations at the Cordele, Georgia facility are scheduled to begin in the fourth quarter.
