# EVCO Plastics

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [EVCO Plastics to Add White-Room and LSR Molding | Plastics Technology](https://www.ptonline.com/news/evco-plastics-to-add-white-room-and-lsr-molding)  ·  `page_054397`

## Facts

- EVCO Plastics is a plastics injection molder with nine plants in the U.S., Mexico and China.
- Based in DeForest, Wis., EVCO recently purchased a facility near its CAL 1 plant located outside Calhoun, Ga.
- The newly purchased facility is dubbed CAL 2.
- CAL 2 adds 40,000 ft2 of space.
- CAL 2 includes room for 20 injection machines of up to 500 tons.
- CAL 2 includes a 725-ft2 metrology room.
- CAL 2 will introduce EVCO’s first white room, measuring 22,000 ft2.
- CAL 2 will also introduce EVCO’s first LSR molding capability, measuring 2100 ft2.
- Both the new white room and LSR molding capabilities are aimed to support medical and commercial lighting customers.
- CAL 2 is expected to be fully operational this fall.
- When fully operational, CAL 2 will be a food-grade compliant facility with highly automated “lights-out” production.
- The addition of CAL 2 brings EVCO’s total footprint to 1 million ft2.
- The company employs over 900 people to operate more than 140 injection presses from 28 to 3500 tons.
- EVCO is currently adding 13,000 ft2 to its AMP facility in DeForest.
- The expansion at the DeForest AMP facility provides room for more machines as well as storage of raw materials and finished goods.
