# Duracell

## Facts

- Duracell batteries is the #1 trusted battery brand that offers Quantum and High Density Core, Duralock, Powercheck and Ion Core technologies.
- The company's 309,000 sq. ft. facility in LaGrange uses high-speed, state of the art, advanced manufacturing processes that include combining electro-chemistry and mechanical functions.
- The LaGrange facility produces all of the AA and AAA batteries for Duracell in North America.
- The facility produces 1000s of batteries per hour.
- The facility employs over 300 associates.
- A recent construction development includes an 8 story expansion that is now the tallest building in LaGrange.
