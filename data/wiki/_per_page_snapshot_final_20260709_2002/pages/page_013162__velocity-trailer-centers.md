# Velocity Trailer Centers

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2026-04-21

**Source:** [Velocity Trailer Centers Expands Vanguard Trailer Availability | Supply Post - Canada's #1 Heavy Construction Equipment & Commercial Truck Newspaper](https://www.supplypost.com/news/2026/4/velocity-trailer-centers-expands-vanguard-trailer-availability)  ·  `page_013162`

## Facts

- Velocity Trailer Centers, a division of Velocity Vehicle Group, has expanded its Vanguard trailer distribution network across Tennessee, Alabama, and Georgia, effective February 16, 2026.
- The expansion strengthens the company’s ability to support fleets and owner-operators throughout the Southeastern United States, with greater access to Vanguard dry van and refrigerated trailers, along with customizable factory-built options.
- Velocity Trailer Centers is the dedicated trailer sales and support division of Velocity Vehicle Group, providing customers with access to new and used trailer inventory, equipment financing, and aftermarket support through the broader Velocity commercial vehicle network.
- The Southeast is one of the fastest-growing freight markets in the U.S., driven by continued expansion in warehouse infrastructure, port activity, and distribution center development.
- These factors have strengthened the region’s role as a key logistics gateway within the U.S. freight network while increasing demand for reliable trailer equipment across major transportation corridors.
- Robert Ramey, General Manager of Velocity Trailer Centers, stated that expanding Vanguard trailer availability into Tennessee, Alabama, and Georgia allows the company to better support fleets and owner-operators across one of the most dynamic freight regions in the U.S.
- By combining Vanguard’s proven trailer engineering with Velocity’s growing service network, the company is helping customers access reliable equipment faster while supporting the continued growth of transportation and logistics across the Southeast.
- Customers in the region will now have access to a range of Vanguard dry van and refrigerated trailer configurations, including the latest 2026 and 2027 VXP dry van trailers and R8000B refrigerated trailers.
- These models are engineered for durability and operational efficiency in demanding freight environments and can be configured with a range of specifications to meet fleet requirements.
- Available configurations include trailers equipped with Thermo King S-600 refrigeration units, stainless steel swing doors, Hendrickson suspension systems, and multiple logistics post configurations, allowing operators to align trailer specifications with their freight applications.
- Velocity Trailer Centers now offers Vanguard trailers across an expanded North American footprint, including Canada: British Columbia; United States: California, Nevada, Oregon, Utah, Washington, Georgia, Alabama, and Tennessee; Mexico: Nationwide coverage.
- This growing network enables Velocity Trailer Centers to support fleets operating across key freight corridors throughout North America, providing consistent trailer availability and knowledgeable sales support across multiple regions.
- The Southeast expansion will be supported by dedicated regional representatives who will assist customers with trailer specifications, inventory availability, and factory production orders.
