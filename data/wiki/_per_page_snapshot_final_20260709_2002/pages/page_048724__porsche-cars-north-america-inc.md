# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) was established in 1984.
- PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera and Taycan.
- PCNA has been headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, since 1998.
- PCNA's headquarters address is One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA.
- PCNA operates the first Porsche Experience Center in North America, featuring a module-based 1.6 mile driver development track, a business center, and fine dining restaurant 356.
- PCNA also operates a second Porsche Experience Center near Los Angeles.
- The second location is a 53-acre complex featuring a driver development track with eight educational modules totaling 4.1 miles, a business center, and Restaurant 917.
- PCNA supports 203 independently owned and operated Porsche Center locations in the U.S.
- PCNA supplies parts, service, marketing, and training to these independent Porsche Centers.
- Independent Porsche Centers work to provide customers with a best-in-class experience aligned with the Porsche brand's 75-year history of leadership in vehicle performance, safety, and efficiency.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Porsche boasts a racing heritage with some 30,000-plus motorsport wins to date.
