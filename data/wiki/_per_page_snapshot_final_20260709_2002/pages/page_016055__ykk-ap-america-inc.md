## Facts

- YKK AP America Inc. is a leading Georgia-based manufacturer of commercial façade systems and residential windows and doors.
- YKK AP America Inc. partnered with general contractor JE Dunn to host a topping-out ceremony for their new state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Macon, Georgia.
- The topping-out ceremony took place on Friday at the 400,000-square-foot YKK AP Manufacturing Plant.
- The ceremony kicked off at 11:00 A.M. and was followed by remarks from key stakeholders and refreshments for attendees.
- The event celebrated a construction milestone on the $125 million project.
- The manufacturing plant is on track to be finished by October.
- The facility will combine two company-owned and operated facilities under one roof.
- The new facility will create 100 new jobs.
- The expansion will increase YKK AP's Macon workforce to 350 employees.
- The facility will be located on Tractor Drive in Macon.
