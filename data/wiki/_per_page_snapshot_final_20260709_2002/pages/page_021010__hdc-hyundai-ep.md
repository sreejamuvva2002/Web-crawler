## Facts

- Polypropylene (PP) compounds are the products produced by blending Polypropylene with different components such as fillers and various additives.
- This process grants PP compounds diverse characteristics and they are widely used in producing automotive, interior / exterior materials and electrical & electronics components etc.
- With long-term experiences and technical know-how, HDC HYUNDAI EP is producing high-quality PP compounds with outstanding processibility, widening the range of its usage.
- SUPOL is a highly functional composite PP resin that has superior processibility and dimensional stability.
- HDC HYUNDAI EP's exclusive technology also granted SUPOL various colors, shock resistance and environmental resistance.
