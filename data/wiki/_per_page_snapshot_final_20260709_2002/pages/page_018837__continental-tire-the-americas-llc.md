# Continental Tire the Americas, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-05-01

**Source:** [Continental Announces Leadership Transition in its Replacement Tires Business in the Americas - Continental AG](https://www.continental.com/en/press/press-releases/20250318-tires-americas-transition/)  ·  `page_018837`

## Facts

- Dr. Jochen Etzel will retire as CEO of Continental Tire the Americas, LLC after a more than 30-year career with Continental.
- Tansu Işık will assume the CEO role on May 1, 2025.
- The press release was issued from Hanover, Germany, and Fort Mill, S.C., USA on March 18, 2025.
- Tansu Işık will be the new Head of Continental’s Business Area Replacement Tires the Americas.
- Tansu Işık will report directly to Christian Koetz, Executive Board Member of Continental and Head of Continental Tires.
- Jochen Etzel has served as CEO since 2011 and played an important role in uniting various Continental tire activities in the region.
- Jochen Etzel started his career as a lawyer in Continental’s corporate law department.
- Jochen Etzel then took over a financial manager position responsible for developing the company’s tire business in regions outside of Europe.
- Jochen Etzel later led Continental’s corporate Mergers and Acquisitions team.
- Christian Koetz stated that Jochen’s impact on Continental’s tire business has been immeasurable and thanked him for his leadership, dedication, and outstanding contributions throughout his tenure.
- Christian Koetz expressed excitement to welcome Tansu Işık into his new role as Head of the Business Area Replacement Tires the Americas.
- Tansu Işık brings more than 20 years of international experience in marketing, supply chain, strategy, and key account management within Continental’s tires business.
- Tansu Işık began his career as a Business Development Specialist in Istanbul, Türkiye.
- Tansu Işık moved to Continental Tires’ Headquarters in Hanover, Germany, to become Head of Pricing for the Europe, Middle East and Africa (EMEA) region.
- Tansu Işık then moved to Shanghai, China, as Director and Vice President for Key Account Management (OE) China and the Asia-Pacific region (APAC).
- From 2017, Işık worked as Head of Marketing for Continental’s replacement tire business in the EMEA region.
- Işık led the Strategic Business Development and Global Marketing organization of Continental Tires.
- Işık currently serves as the Head of Region South & Channels Fleet and OE Trailer for Continental replacement tire business in the EMEA region, overseeing operations in multiple countries, leading the truck tire business in EMEA, and responsible for retread and digital solutions.
- Işık holds an Executive MBA from Koç University and has completed trainings at INSEAD, Duke University, and Ashridge University.
- Allison Zora is the Head of PR & Brand Communication for Business Area Replacement Tires the Americas at Continental Tires.
