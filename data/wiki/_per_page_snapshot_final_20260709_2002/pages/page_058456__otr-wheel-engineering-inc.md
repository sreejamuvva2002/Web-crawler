## Facts

- OTR Wheel Engineering Inc is located at 195 Chatillon Road in Rome, Georgia.
- On November 19, 2025, the company welcomed CPR Training School to its facility for on-site emergency response training.
- Fifteen dedicated employees earned CPR, First Aid, AED, and Bloodborne Pathogen certifications during the session.
- The company specializes in off-the-road (OTR) wheel engineering and services.
- OTR Wheel Engineering supports industries including mining, construction, agriculture, and heavy equipment operations.
- The training curriculum covered recognizing cardiac emergencies, operating automated external defibrillators, managing bleeding and shock, protecting against bloodborne pathogen exposure, and performing adult, child, and infant CPR.
- Leadership's decision to certify 15 employees reflects a commitment to building a workplace safety culture and emergency preparedness.
- The on-site training model allowed teams to train together in their actual work environment while accommodating operational scheduling needs.
- OSHA guidelines recommend that every workplace include employees trained in first aid and CPR with emergency response capabilities available within 3-4 minutes.
