# Superior Essex Inc.

## Facts

- Superior Essex Inc. is headquartered at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
- On May 14, 2021, Superior Essex Inc. aligned its sustainability efforts with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
- The company has committed to aligning with 12 of the 17 specific UN SDGs.
- This alignment supports green manufacturing, sustainable products, and Essex Furukawa's 2030 Vision goals.
- Superior Essex is the parent company of the brands Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, and Essex Brownell.
- Annie Bevan serves as Chief Sustainability Officer for Superior Essex Inc.
- Annie Bevan stated that as a global manufacturer, the company is very excited to align with the UN SDG program.
- She noted that while they have not reached the finish line, this commitment and subsequent actions show the company sees a greater good and the ability to reduce its impact to support the collective.
- In markets not yet focused on sustainability or green manufacturing methods, Superior Essex and its divisions align with UN SDGs as well as customer and planetary environmental and social awareness.
- Superior Essex employs over 3,000 workers across 11 countries on three continents.
- The company is a leading global provider of magnet wire.
- Superior Essex operates at the forefront of fiber communication applications and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- Its products play a role in creating disruptive technological advancements in automotive electrification, energy, and industrial sectors.
- The company is committed to pioneering smart building technology while advancing sustainability.
