# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-07-12

**Source:** [Rivian to launch sales in Europe in 2022 - electrive.com](https://www.electrive.com/2021/07/12/rivian-to-launch-sales-in-europe-in-2022/)  ·  `page_059158`

## Facts

- The US start-up Rivian is backed by Amazon and Ford among others.
- Rivian wants to sell its electric vehicles in Europe from the beginning of 2022.
- The company confirmed the European sales launch in an email to a potential European customer.
- No further details on the European launch have been given so far.
- The confirmation email was reported in the Rivian Owners Forum, among other places.
- Rumors suggest the company was looking for a factory location allegedly in the United Kingdom, Hungary, or Germany.
- Job advertisements, such as a senior analyst for delivery planning in the EMEA region, pointed to European ambitions.
- The exact timing, distribution network, and models for the European launch remain unclear from the customer email, with early 2022 being the only rough timeline.
- According to earlier rumors, Rivian could initially only bring the R1S electric SUV to Europe.
- The somewhat larger R1T pickup truck is to be offered only in North America for the time being.
- Allegedly, the company is planning to develop slightly smaller series with a focus on Europe and China.
- Not much is known about the planned smaller series yet.
- Rivian is planning to start deliveries of the Launch Edition of its R1T electric pickup in the US this month.
- In March, Rivian presented ambitious plans for its own charging networks in the USA and Canada.
- It is not yet known whether such charging networks are also planned for Europe.
