# Continental Tire the Americas LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-11-13

**Source:** [OSHA fines Continental Tire for multiple safety violations at Illinois tire plant - Rubber World - The Technical Service Magazine for the Rubber Industry](https://rubberworld.com/osha-fines-continental-tire-for-multiple-safety-violations-at-illinois-tire-plant/)  ·  `page_018859`

## Facts

- Continental Tire the Americas LLC manufactures passenger, light truck and commercial tires at facilities in Barnesville, Georgia.
- Continental Tire the Americas LLC also operates manufacturing facilities in Mt. Vernon and Sumter, South Carolina.
- Continental Tire the Americas LLC is based in Fort Mill, South Carolina.
- Continental Tire the Americas LLC is the North American subsidiary of the German-based tire and automotive component manufacturer Continental AG.
- The company faces $341,866 in proposed penalties from OSHA for safety violations at its Illinois tire plant.
- OSHA cited the company for deficient safety procedures regarding energy control, combustible dust preventative engineering, housekeeping, personal protective equipment, emergency egress routes, and hazard communication.
- In November 2021, two consecutive incidents at the Illinois plant resulted in one employee suffering an amputation of three fingers, another requiring hospitalization for severe burns, and a third sustaining a concussion from a mixer explosion.
- OSHA issued citations for two repeated violations, 16 serious violations, and five other than serious violations following its investigation.
- OSHA Area Director Aaron Priddy stated that Continental Tire the Americas must review company safety procedures and employee training to ensure worker safety.
