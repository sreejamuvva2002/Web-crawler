# Piedmont Resin Supply LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-31

**Source:** [Hexpol acquires 80 percent of the shares in Piedmont Resin Supply LLC - Rubber World - The Technical Service Magazine for the Rubber Industry](https://rubberworld.com/hexpol-acquires-80-percent-of-the-shares-in-piedmont-resin-supply-llc/)  ·  `page_021001`

## Facts

- HEXPOL signed an agreement to acquire 80 percent of the shares in Piedmont Resin Supply, LLC from founders Mr. Matt Griffith and Mr. Paul Daniel.
- Piedmont was founded in 2013.
- Piedmont has grown to become one of the largest independent nylon compounders in the US.
- The company is an industry leader in technical nylon compounds on the US market.
- Piedmont services customers within the automotive, transportation, and furniture industries.
- Piedmont operates a manufacturing facility in Cartersville, GA.
- The Georgia facility has approximately 60 employees.
- The Georgia facility has further capacity to grow.
- The company has a turnover of some 60 MUSD.
- The company's profitability is on a similar level to the HEXPOL Group.
- Founders Matt Griffith and Paul Daniel stated that they view the merger with HEXPOL as a perfect fit between two successful companies with similar cultures and future ambitions.
- Piedmont brings a portfolio of superior nylon compounds and strong market presence, which together with HEXPOL’s complementary product portfolio and financial strength will enable further growth prospects.
- Jan Wikström, President of HEXPOL Thermoplastic Compounding, stated that the acquisition adds new capabilities, application know-how, and a new customer base to HEXPOL Thermoplastic Compounding in the US.
- Klas Dahlberg, President and CEO of HEXPOL Group, noted that Piedmont has a diverse product portfolio and is a leading independent nylon compounder.
- The acquisition broadens the capabilities of the HEXPOL Group on the US market.
- The acquisition price amounts to 86 MUSD on a cash and debt-free basis.
- The acquisition is funded by a combination of bank facilities and cash.
- The closing took place on October 31, 2024.
- Pursuant to the agreement, HEXPOL has an option to acquire the remaining shares in Piedmont.
- The founders have an option to sell their remaining shares to HEXPOL.
