# Stryten Energy LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-03-09

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Commits to the Clean Energy Transition with New Divisions Focused on Providing Sustainable Energy Storage Solutions Utilizing Multiple Battery Technologies](https://www.stryten.com/stryten-energy-commits-to-clean-energy/)  ·  `page_050508`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC announced the formation of its Motive Power and Essential Power divisions to provide advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium energy storage solutions to help customers transition to clean energy sources.
- The company's GNB Industrial Power division was rebranded to Stryten Energy to unify go-to-market efforts under the Stryten Energy brand both directly and through its dealer network and service partners.
- Stryten Energy is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- The Motive Power division provides a comprehensive portfolio of advanced lead and lithium batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software for material handling, mining, and railway applications.
- The Motive Power service team offers planned and predictive maintenance, repair services, installation and de-installation, recycling, application audits, power studies, training, and inspections to ensure customer fleet efficiency and sustainability.
- The Essential Power division provides a suite of advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium battery technologies and services, including chargers and racking systems for backup power in military, government, telecommunications, UPS, railway, utility, microgrids, and long-duration renewable energy storage applications such as solar and wind.
- The Essential Power research and development team will identify, develop, and commercialize innovative lithium and flow battery technologies to support emerging sustainable power and distribution markets.
- CEO Tim Vargo stated that the creation of the Motive Power and Essential Power divisions enables the company to focus on developing and manufacturing high-performing, top-quality energy storage solutions across multiple chemistries to meet growing U.S. clean power demand and decarbonization goals.
- The company supports customer sustainability goals by enabling transitions from internal combustion to battery-powered electric forklifts in distribution centers and providing environmentally friendly energy storage for on-demand power needs.
- President and COO Mike Judd noted the announcement follows significant investments in battery technology innovations, vertical integration, and continuous improvement at the company's plants.
- The rebrand of GNB Industrial Power to Stryten Energy was expected to be complete by the end of 2022, with significant support provided to dealer networks and service partners for a successful transition.
- Batteries and chargers previously carrying the GNB brand label are now offered as part of the Stryten Energy product portfolio.
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- The company's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- Stryten Energy is backed by more than a century of industry expertise in delivering energy solutions for applications ranging from submarines and subcompacts to microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
