# Ascend Elements

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-30

**Source:** [Nation’s Largest EV Battery Recycling Plant Opens in Georgia](https://www.waste360.com/waste-recycling/nation-s-largest-ev-battery-recycling-plant-opens-in-georgia)  ·  `page_000326`

## Facts

- Ascend Elements is a Massachusetts-based company.
- Ascend Elements has opened a new electric vehicle battery recycling facility in East Georgia.
- The facility measures 150,000 square feet.
- The facility can process 30,000 tons of used lithium-ion batteries and manufacturing scrap per year.
- The processing capacity equates to roughly 70,000 electric vehicle batteries per year.
- The facility is the largest of its type in the nation.
- Ascend Elements CEO Mike O’Kronley stated the facility is critical infrastructure needed in the United States to help support the energy transition.
- Mike O’Kronley noted that millions of EVs will be on the road soon and more facilities like this will be needed.
- The new facility will open 185 new jobs for the area.
