# YKK (U.S.A.) Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-08-18

**Source:** [YKK USA opens onsite medical clinic at Macon, GA Plant - YKK Americas](https://ykkamericas.com/ykk-usa-opens-onsite-medical-clinic-at-macon-ga-plant/)  ·  `page_016077`

## Facts

- YKK (U.S.A.) Inc. opened an onsite health clinic for full-time Macon manufacturing plant employees in August 2022.
- The clinic was established through a joint venture with Macon Occupational Medicine (MOM).
- The HIPAA-compliant employee health clinic operates on Tuesdays and Thursdays during normal working hours.
- The clinic provides treatments for conditions ranging from simple aches and pains to complex issues like diabetes and asthma.
- Medical staff at the clinic include a physician assistant and a medical technologist.
- The facility includes an on-site pharmacy.
- Employees can undergo blood work at the clinic.
- The clinic offers rapid testing for flu, COVID-19, strep, and pregnancy.
- John Smith, president of YKK USA, stated that the company is excited to provide onsite access for employee health and wellness needs.
- John Smith credited the partnership with MOM for enabling a high level of professional care for employees.
- Macon Occupational Medicine (MOM) provides occupational medicine services to employees across middle and southern Georgia.
- YKK stated that the workplace health clinic aligns with its Cycle of Goodness® philosophy, which emphasizes rendering benefit to others.
