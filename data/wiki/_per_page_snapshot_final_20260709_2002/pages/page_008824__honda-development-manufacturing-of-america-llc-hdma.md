# Honda Development & Manufacturing of America, LLC (HDMA)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Honda has a clear vision for the future in 2030 focused on innovation and reaching future-focused goals.
- Honda strives to realize “the joy and freedom of mobility” by developing new technologies and an innovative approach to achieve a “zero environmental footprint.”
- The position participates in Honda Development & Manufacturing of America, LLC’s (HDMA) Engineering Development Program (EDP).
- The EDP has been a critical program for developing entry-level engineers over the last 25 years.
- The program is a one to two-year duration where participants rotate through various departments and roles throughout HDMA.
- Successful graduates of the EDP will return to a “Home” department with experience across manufacturing and business operations facets.
- Openings are available in multiple locations, including Georgia: Tallapoosa.
- Other program locations include Ohio (Anna, East Liberty, Marysville, Raymond, Russells Point), Alabama (Lincoln), and Indiana (Greensburg).
- Manufacturing Engineering roles lead, create, and implement technical activities in manufacturing operations, monitoring and managing equipment and processes to ensure optimal performance while minimizing operating expenses.
- Equipment/Tooling Design Engineering roles design manufacturing equipment including stamping, hardware & software, and die & mold to ensure efficiency and stability of new models and technology introduction across HDMA North American facilities.
- Quality Engineering roles interact with Manufacturing Departments, Support Departments, Suppliers, and R&D for root cause analysis and countermeasures, and perform car testing during new model trial stages to verify design intent matches vehicle performance.
- Quality Engineering roles are available in Ohio & Georgia.
- Materials Development Engineer roles focus on the research and development of resins, fabrics, and other non-metal materials within the Organics Group, seeking materials or chemical engineering majors.
- Safety Engineer roles apply scientific and practical knowledge of Safety compliance to innovate, design, and improve systems, processes, equipment, and tools to align with daily operations, new models, business plans, and innovation targets.
- Data Analytics Engineer roles review multiple data sources to evaluate, summarize, and draw conclusions identifying trends for countermeasure activity, analyzing data covering quality, delivery, safety, and cost within manufacturing, design, and test environments.
- Applicants must have a bachelor’s degree from an ABET or ATMAE accredited program in Mechanical, Electrical, Industrial, or other equivalent engineering fields.
- Candidates require less than 2 years of engineering work experience, with 3 months co-op/internship experience in a relevant environment preferred.
- Sponsorship for employment visa status is unavailable for these positions, including F-1 CPT/OPT, H-1B, and TN visas.
- Compensation includes a competitive base salary based on variables like geographic location and work experience, paid overtime, and regional bonuses when applicable.
- Benefits include industry-leading plans (Medical, Dental, Vision, Rx), paid time off for vacation/holidays/shutdowns, company-paid short-term and long-term disability, and a 401K plan with company match plus additional contribution.
- Relocation assistance is available if eligible.
- Career growth offerings include advancement opportunities, career mobility, education reimbursement for continued learning, and training and development programs.
- Additional offerings include tuition assistance & student loan repayment, lifestyle accounts, childcare reimbursement accounts, elder care support, wellbeing programs, community service and engagement programs, product programs, and free drinks onsite.
