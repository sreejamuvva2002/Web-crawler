# Honda Motor Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-31

**Source:** [ANNUAL REPORT](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/715153/000119312523173074/d459191d20f.htm)  ·  `page_002719`

## Facts

- Plans to introduce two models jointly developed with General Motors Company (GM) in North America in 2024 (Honda brand: Prologue ZDX).
- Plans to launch a mid-to large-size EV based on Honda’s proprietary EV platform in North America in 2025.
- Will procure Ultium batteries from GM for the North American market.
- Will procure batteries from a joint venture company for EV battery production with LG Energy Solution Ltd. in North America.
- Is developing a platform for large FUN EV models and plans to release three models in Japan, the United States, and Europe between 2024 and 2025.
- Is pursuing an initiative for Commuter EVs involving two personal-use smart EVs adopting connectivity and battery-as-a-service.
- American Honda Motor Co., Inc. is a 100% owned U.S.A. subsidiary responsible for coordination of subsidiaries operation, research & development, manufacturing, and sales.
- Honda Development and Manufacturing of America, LLC is a 100% owned U.S.A. subsidiary handling research & development and manufacturing.
- Operates a facility in Lincoln, Alabama, U.S.A., with 4,830 employees producing automobiles and engines.
- Operates a facility in Greensboro, North Carolina, U.S.A., with 729 employees producing aircraft.
- Operates a facility in Burlington, North Carolina, U.S.A., with 98 employees producing aircraft engines.
- Operates a facility in Marysville, Ohio, U.S.A., with 6,161 employees producing automobiles.
- Operates a facility in Anna, Ohio, U.S.A., with 2,618 employees producing engines.
- Operates a facility in East Liberty, Ohio, U.S.A., with 2,891 employees producing automobiles.
- Operates a facility in Greensburg, Indiana, U.S.A., with 2,438 employees producing automobiles.
- Reported Automobile Business revenue of ¥ 5,990.5 billion in North America for the fiscal year ended March 31, 2023.
- Reported Financial Services Business revenue of ¥ 2,466.5 billion in North America for the fiscal year ended March 31, 2023.
- Recorded Honda Group Unit Sales for automobiles in North America of 1,195 thousand units for the fiscal year ended March 31, 2023.
- Recorded Consolidated Unit Sales for automobiles in North America of 1,195 thousand units for the fiscal year ended March 31, 2023.
- Is subject to regulatory amendments including those for electric vehicle traction battery safety requirements, EV safety requirements, and connection sets for conductive charging of electric vehicles.
- Seiji Kuraishi serves as Chairman and Director of Honda Motor Co., Ltd.
- Toshihiro Mibe serves as Director and Representative Executive Officer (CEO) of Honda Motor Co., Ltd.
- Shinji Aoyama serves as Vice Chief Officer for Regional Operations (North America) at Honda Motor Co., Ltd.
