## Facts

- Novelis Inc is a subsidiary of Aditya Birla-led Hindalco Industries.
- Novelis Inc completed an offering of 500 million euro aggregate principal amount of 3.375 per cent euro-denominated senior green notes due on April 15, 2029 by Novelis Sheet Ingot GmbH.
- Novelis Inc reported a 12 per cent year-on-year decline in net sales to 2.7 billion dollars for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2020 due to lower average LME aluminum prices and local market premiums.
- Aditya Birla Group metals flagship company Hindalco Industries Ltd announced the completion of acquiring Aleris by its wholly-owned subsidiary Novelis Inc.
- Novelis Inc received the final clearance needed to complete its acquisition of Aleris Corporation, a global supplier of rolled aluminum products.
- Novelis Inc reported net income attributable to its common shareholder of 107 million dollars for the third quarter (October to December) of the current fiscal year compared to 78 million dollars in the prior period.
- The European Commission approved Novelis Inc's proposed acquisition of Aleris Corporation.
- Novelis Inc reaffirmed its commitment to close the proposed 2.6 billion dollar acquisition of Aleris Corporation notwithstanding a US Department of Justice (DOJ) lawsuit to block the transaction.
- Crisil Ratings upgraded the rating on non-convertible debentures of Hindalco Industries Ltd to AA-plus with a stable outlook from AA with a positive outlook.
- Novelis Inc is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
