## Facts

- Blue Bird Corporation is headquartered in Macon, Ga.
- Georgia has been Blue Bird's home for nearly a century, according to president and CEO John Wyskiel.
- The Blue Bird School Bus Foundation hosted a community event at its corporate headquarters in Macon, Ga., and awarded a total of $53,300 to ten local area nonprofit organizations.
- Donations from the foundation ranged from $2,500 up to $12,500 per organization.
- Safe Kids Central Georgia received the top-level donation of $12,500 to promote child car seat safety and provide free car seats and educational materials to low-income families.
- Since its inception in 2015, the Blue Bird School Bus Foundation has donated more than $600,000 in charitable contributions to various community organizations, including more than 85 Georgia-based nonprofits.
- The foundation awarded grants to ten specific Georgia nonprofits: Atrium Health Navicent Medical Center, Brave Meadows Therapeutic Riding Center Inc., Fort Valley Lions Club, Girl Scouts of Historic Georgia Inc., Macon Reviving Baseball in the Inner Cities Program by the Boys and Girls Club, Peach Association for Intellectual Disabled Inc. aka Kay Center, The Salvation Army Warner Robins Corps, Safe Kids Central Georgia, Sleep in Heavenly Peace Inc., and United in Pink Inc.
- Since December 2023, Blue Bird has donated three all-electric, zero-emission school buses to community organizations.
- Peach County Schools in Fort Valley, Ga., received a donated zero-emission bus to add to its fleet as a clean transportation option for day and field trips.
- The Jerome Bettis Bus Stops Here Foundation in Pittsburgh, Pa., received a donated electric bus to convert into a mobile computer lab for digital literacy programs.
- Hoop Bus in Los Angeles, Ca., received a donated zero-emission bus equipped with basketball hoops to serve as an interactive, mobile court for at-risk youth.
- Blue Bird Corporation was founded in 1927 and is recognized as a technology leader and innovator of school buses.
- The company designs, engineers, and manufactures school buses with a singular focus on safety, reliability, and durability.
- Blue Bird school buses transport approximately 25 million children twice a day.
- Blue Bird has sold more than 25,000 propane, natural gas, and electric powered buses.
- The company is the proven leader in low- and zero-emission school buses and is transforming the student transportation industry through cleaner energy solutions.
