# Superior Essex

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-30

**Source:** [Superior Essex Completes Buyout in Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire](https://essexsolutions.com/news/superior-essex-completes-essex-furukawa-buyout-from-furukawa-electric/)  ·  `page_010714`

## Facts

- Superior Essex successfully completed the acquisition of the remaining minority interest in Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire from Furukawa Electric Co., Ltd.
- The transaction became effective on April 30, 2024.
- The completion establishes Superior Essex as the sole owner of the brand, assets, and operations previously conducted by the global joint venture with Furukawa Electric.
- Superior Essex plans to relaunch its magnet wire business under a new company name later in 2024.
- The company will continue to operate all current facilities of the Essex Furukawa joint venture worldwide.
- These facilities include magnet wire operations in Japan and Malaysia (Kuala Lumpur) that were contributed by Furukawa Electric in October 2020.
- Daniel Choi serves as the CEO of Superior Essex.
- Choi stated, “Over the last several years we have been able to accomplish many great things working with the team from Furukawa Electric. I am proud of what was created and thankful for their contributions.”
- The acquisition solidifies Superior Essex's control over global operations and reinforces its commitment to delivering products and services to customers worldwide.
- Superior Essex plans to continuously respond to market demands in the xEV automotive industry, power, electrification, and renewable energy markets.
- The company will strengthen its position by actively pursuing decarbonization and sustainability initiatives.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, Essex Energy Italy, IVA, Lacroix + Kress, Hi-Wire, and Essex Brownell.
- The company employs over 3,000 people across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is the leading global provider of magnet wire and is at the forefront of communications fiber application and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company is instrumental in creating disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Furukawa Electric was established in 1884 and comprises 127 group companies with approximately 50,000 employees worldwide.
- Furukawa Electric provides technologies, products, and services for infrastructure fields such as information and energy, automotive systems, and electronics using core technologies of metals, polymers, photonics, and high frequency.
