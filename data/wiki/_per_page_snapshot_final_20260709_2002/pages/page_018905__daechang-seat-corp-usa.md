## Facts

- More than 500 new jobs are coming soon to Chatham County.
- Gov. Brian Kemp announced that Daechang Seat Corp. USA, a supplier specializing in automotive seat frames, will invest $72.5 million in the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- Globally, DSC employs more than 5,000 people in seven countries.
- DSC’s facility will be located at the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- The company will be hiring for assembly operators, equipment technicians, mechanical engineers, and electrical engineers through local job fairs and website postings.
- Gov. Kemp stated that suppliers for the Hyundai Metaplant project reach at least nine counties.
- Gov. Kemp noted that this announcement is a reminder of the impact projects like the Hyundai Metaplant have beyond their initial investment.
- The company will be adding to Georgia's growing manufacturing base centered around Chatham County and the Savannah region.
