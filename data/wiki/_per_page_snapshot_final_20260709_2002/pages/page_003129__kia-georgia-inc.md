# Kia Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-07-12

**Source:** [Kia Georgia Plans Expansion to Accommodate EV9 Production](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/kia-georgia-plans-expansion-to-accommodate-ev9-production-301875140.html)  ·  `page_003129`

## Facts

- Kia Georgia's West Point assembly plant is undergoing a transformation that will allow for the assembly of the all-electric 2024 EV9 three-row SUV in the second quarter of 2024.
- The expansion costs over $200 million.
- The project will create nearly 200 additional jobs in Troup County.
- The EV9 will join the Telluride, Sorento and Sportage SUVs and the K5 mid-size sedan as the fifth model assembled at Kia Georgia.
- Sean Yoon, president & CEO of Kia North America and Kia America, stated that the EV9 has the potential to be another 'game changer' for Kia.
- The EV9 will be the most sophisticated vehicle Kia has ever built and will be a standout in the EV market.
- The EV9 will be assembled in West Point, Georgia.
- The EV9 will be the first EV for Kia to be assembled in North America.
- The EV9 combines all the best SUV elements of the award-winning Telluride with the best EV elements of the award-winning EV6.
- Kia America is headquartered in Irvine, California.
- Kia serves as the 'Official Automotive Partner' of the NBA.
- Kia sells a range of gasoline, hybrid, plug-in hybrid and electrified vehicles through a network of over 750 dealers in the U.S.
- The K5, Sportage, Sorento, and Telluride (excludes HEV and PHEV models) are assembled in the United States from U.S. and globally sourced parts.
- The EV9 is expected to be assembled in the United States from U.S. and globally sourced parts.
- Kia Georgia, Inc. is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Corporation based in Seoul, Korea.
- Kia Georgia has an annual production capacity of 340,000 units.
- Kia Georgia is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Ga.
- Kia Georgia began mass production on Nov. 16, 2009.
