# Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corp.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2011-03-01

**Source:** [Suzuki ATV plant reaches milestone | Powersports Business](https://powersportsbusiness.com/features/2011/03/01/suzuki-atv-plant-reaches-milestone/)  ·  `page_010819`

## Facts

- Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corp. recently celebrated its 10-year anniversary.
- The Rome, Ga., plant was built in the United States in 2001 to demonstrate Suzuki’s commitment to its ATV business.
- Initially the plant built the all-new 400cc Eiger and 500cc Vinson ATVs.
- Currently, it assembles the award-winning Kingquad line of ATVs for the North American and global markets.
- Nobuyuki Yamane, president of Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corp., stated that the last couple of years have been very difficult due to the overall economic situation and its effect on the entire ATV industry.
- The company is looking forward to a rebound in the ATV industry as it focuses its Management Plan for FY2011 under the theme '10 Years and Beyond'.
- The plant has recently increased production capacity.
- The plant recalled some team members affected by previous workforce reductions.
