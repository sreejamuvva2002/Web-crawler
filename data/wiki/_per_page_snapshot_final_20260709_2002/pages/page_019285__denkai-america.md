## Facts

- Denkai America is a leader in the manufacture of high-quality electrodeposited copper foils for printed circuit board (PCB), industrial, and energy storage applications.
- The company delivers both conventional cladding and application specific copper foils required by technology leading companies.
- Denkai America has over 40 years of experience manufacturing copper foil with a focus on quality and service for domestic and international customers.
- Copper foils are manufactured using an electroplating process on revolving titanium drums to achieve a high-quality surface.
- Plated foil rolls undergo various surface treating operations tailored to specific end-use requirements.
- The manufacturing process deposits copper dendrites onto the foil surface to enhance bonding to resin systems during lamination.
- A barrier-layer is applied to promote reliability in thermal applications.
- Proprietary organic layers are used to promote chemical adhesion, and foils are stain-proofed to prevent oxidation during shipping, storage, lamination, and customer processing.
