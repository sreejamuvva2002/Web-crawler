# Hyundai Motor Group Savannah Metaplant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-09

**Source:** [Hyundai Group Metaplant Under Construction Near Savannah, Georgia](https://carvisionnews.com/autos/hyundai-group-metaplant-under-construction-near-savannah-georgia/)  ·  `page_009320`

## Facts

- Plans were unveiled for a sprawling Hyundai Motor Group battery and EV components manufacturing facility near Savannah, GA.
- The facility is estimated to occupy over 5,000 acres.
- The site is located 30 minutes west of Savannah.
- The metaplant will employ 4,000 workers.
- The facility will boost company annual EV capacity to 300,000 a year.
- The project supports Hyundai, Kia and Genesis electric vehicles.
- The facilities will also house 6 or more major suppliers.
- The estimated investment is $4.3 billion.
- The investment is described as a boon to Georgia and the rest of the mid-south economies.
- LG Solutions has recently taken a substantial partnership in this deal.
- The partnership focuses on EV and onboard tech.
