## Facts

- Häring has been one of the leading manufacturers of precision components and subassemblies for the automotive industry for many years.
- Haering Precision is the US location of Häring Group, which is headquartered in the south of Germany.
- Haering Precision USA LP was chosen as a strategic base for operations to spread global reach and establish a local presence in the region.
- Office hours are 7:00am - 4:00pm, Monday-Friday.
- The facility is located off of I-85 Exit 177, at the end of Hamburg Avenue, across from the Georgia State Patrol and next to Ritz Transformers.
- Visitors and employees must check in with security outside of the gate.
- The company operates under the philosophy 'Think It. Believe It. Achieve It.'
- The goal is to provide jobs and career development opportunities to the region.
