## Facts

- Bosch Automotive Workshop Services LLC (Bosch Auto Service) has announced its expansion into the Southeastern states of Georgia, Florida, North Carolina, South Carolina and Tennessee.
- The company plans to expand into additional states in the future.
- Bosch Auto Service Franchisees have access to scalable growth opportunities through the Bosch service model that allows business owners to expand their portfolio in the automotive sector.
- Franchise workshops will receive custom business solutions including dedicated consultants who specialize in fixed operations and workshop financial planning.
- Workshops will get technical training through Bosch-certified experts focused on the latest in vehicle service.
- Franchise resources include customer acquisition and retention programs, analytics, state-of-the-art diagnostics, OEM-approved parts and more.
- The program provides strategic partnerships to increase visibility, value, efficiency and business growth.
- Franchisees gain brand recognition with over 100 years of Bosch experience and innovation in the automotive sector.
- Uli Jaschek serves as the CEO of the Bosch Auto Service franchise.
- Uli Jaschek stated that the program is supported by extensive Bosch expertise and innovation, elevating automotive service standards through top-tier maintenance and repair.
- Uli Jaschek added that they are pleased to bring the program to the Southeast to meet the growing demand for quality automotive service with care centered around precision and reliability.
