## Facts

- Volvo Cars has a focused portfolio of premium cars offering a chargeable alternative for each model.
- Since 2020, all new models from Volvo Cars have been fully electric.
- The latest addition to the lineup is the Volvo EX90, a fully electric large SUV.
- Volvo Cars operates a car production facility in Charleston, SC as part of its Americas region footprint.
- Volvo Cars employs approximately 43,000 people globally.
- Volvo Cars achieved approximately 615,000 retail deliveries.
- Volvo Cars serves customers in over 100 countries.
- The company was founded in 1927 in Gothenburg, Sweden.
- Global headquarters is located in Gothenburg, with R&D and design centres in Gothenburg, Shanghai, and Camarillo.
- Volvo Cars operates an owned assembly factory in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia and contract assembly in Bangalore, India.
