# Down 2 Earth Trailers

## Facts

- Down 2 Earth Trailers was founded in 1999 by Paul Dubberly and his son Andrew.
- The company started in the small town of Baxley, GA.
- In its early days, the company focused on single axle utility trailers.
- Over time, the company added Equipment, Gooseneck, and Dump trailer lines as commercial use became more common.
- Down 2 Earth has been building trailers for more than 23 years.
- Each month, more than 1,000 trailers roll out of the Baxley, GA plant.
- The company produces over 70 different models at various stages of the build process on any given day.
- Down 2 Earth employs around 100 staff members from Appling and surrounding counties.
- Employee roles include Welders, Electricians, Carpenters, Powder coat specialists, Salesmen, Logistics, Drivers, Parts specialists, and Office personnel.
- In July 2020, the company was acquired by Triple Crown Trailers of Ocala, FL.
- Despite the acquisition, the company continues to build the Down 2 Earth brand.
- The broader trailer manufacturing industry is a multi-billion dollar industry producing a wide range of trailers for various purposes.
- The combined Triple Crown/Down 2 Earth company is now top 5 in the country in trailer manufacturing.
- Down 2 Earth trailer dealers are located all over the Eastern side of the United States.
