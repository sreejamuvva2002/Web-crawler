# IMMI

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-10

**Source:** [IMMI & Blue Bird Celebrate Opening of New Manufacturing Plant in Macon - Middle Georgia CEO](https://middlegeorgiaceo.com/features/2025/10/immi-and-blue-bird-celebrate-opening-new-manufacturing-plant-macon-georgia/)  ·  `page_009933`

## Facts

- IMMI joined Blue Bird Corp. to celebrate the grand opening of its manufacturing facility in Macon, Georgia on Wednesday, October 8th, 2025.
- IMMI’s new Macon plant spans 75,000 square feet and will support school bus manufacturer Blue Bird with industry-first safety upgrades for select model buses.
- The facility will manufacture seats equipped with three-point seat belts as standard protection for all student passengers.
- The facility will also produce steering wheel deployed airbags to safeguard school bus drivers.
- IMMI announced the creation of 80 good-paying U.S. manufacturing jobs in middle Georgia.
- Larry Gray, CEO of IMMI, announced the job creation and celebrated safety leadership alongside partner Blue Bird.
- Norm Gould III, School Bus President & IMMI CFO, recognized Blue Bird’s commitment to advancing school bus safety by being the first manufacturer to make lap-shoulder belts standard and provide frontal airbags for drivers nationwide.
- IMMI’s 4Front airbag safety system will be standard safety equipment on Blue Bird’s Vision-model school buses beginning in October 2025.
- John Wyskiel, President and CEO of Blue Bird Corporation, stated that millions of children travel on school buses daily and called them the most precious cargo in the world.
- Blue Bird has led in school bus safety for nearly 100 years and partnered with IMMI to bring industry-first standard student and driver safety systems to North American school districts.
- Under the brand SafeGuard, IMMI’s Macon facility manufactures state-of-the-art school bus seats designed to protect millions of children daily.
- Blue Bird was the first school bus manufacturer to announce in 2024 that lap-shoulder belts would be standard safety equipment on their school bus seats.
- The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration and the National Transportation Safety Board have called for school bus manufacturers to provide lap-shoulder belts for over 25 million children who ride buses annually, which Blue Bird has answered.
- IMMI expects to add more jobs to its Macon workforce as demand increases for SafeGuard’s lap-shoulder belt seats.
- Illinois recently passed legislation mandating lap-shoulder belts on new school buses, joining eight other U.S. states in the requirement.
- IMMI CEO Larry Gray expects other states to follow suit as they recognize that seat belts save lives and reduce injuries even on the safest vehicles on the road.
