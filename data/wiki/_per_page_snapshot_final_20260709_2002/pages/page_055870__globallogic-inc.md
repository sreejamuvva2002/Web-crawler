# GlobalLogic Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hitachi Astemo CEO Takeuchi and GlobalLogic CEO Nitesh discuss the future of mobility](https://www.globallogic.com/uki/about/press-room/press-release/hitachi-astemo-takeuchi-ceo-and-globallogic-nitesh-ceo-discuss-the-future-of-mobility/)  ·  `page_055870`

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo, Ltd. (Astemo) and GlobalLogic Inc. (GlobalLogic) have been strengthening their collaboration to lead the trends of software-defined vehicles.
- Hitachi Astemo President & CEO Kohei Takeuchi and GlobalLogic CEO Nitesh Banga discussed the future of mobility through digital transformation.
- Astemo acts as a global mega-supplier providing tangible value in the In-Car domain, including internal vehicle technology such as software that enables advanced vehicle functions like autonomous driving.
- Astemo focuses on the Out-Car domain, utilizing cloud and communications technology to expand functionality and improve performance outside of vehicles.
- Astemo established Cypremos, Ltd. (Cypremos) on November 1 to further strengthen support for the Out-Car field.
- The Astemo Group operates as a Tier 0.5 supplier developing technologies and providing value for future mobility.
- Vehicle OEMs are rapidly transforming into tech companies and developing SDV architecture to provide over-the-air updates and generate additional revenue streams throughout the vehicle lifecycle.
- GlobalLogic enables this industry transformation by creating in-vehicle and around-the-vehicle platform technologies that generate revenue streams for OEMs and the broader mobility ecosystem.
- GlobalLogic has formed partnerships with major automobile manufacturers, parts suppliers, hyperscalers, semiconductor companies, fleets, and automotive retail.
- GlobalLogic is focusing on the development of electric vehicle propulsion systems.
- GlobalLogic's work contributes to next-generation sustainable transportation and enhanced vehicle safety, comfort, and connectivity.
- Kohei Takeuchi stated that Astemo's vision involves providing more than just safety and peace of mind by removing barriers like time and space to empower people worldwide with greater freedom and quality of life.
- Takeuchi identified software and digital transformation as the keys to achieving Astemo's vision.
- The new company Cypremos will provide software and cloud services aimed at expanding support for SDVs and realizing new mobility services.
- Cypremos will improve car intelligence and mobility evolution linked to the cloud.
- Astemo continues to accelerate future mobility technology development in partnership with companies like GlobalLogic.
- Nitesh Banga stated GlobalLogic's purpose is to create a positive impact for society and the planet by engineering digital products, platforms, and services designed for desirability, curated for intelligence, and built for excellence.
- GlobalLogic drives business transformation through intelligent, sustainable solutions in collaboration with clients across healthcare, energy, finance, mobility, and other sectors.
- Banga noted that GlobalLogic shares Astemo's vision and will build a future where technology elevates daily life and promotes meaningful, sustainable change for the mobility industry and communities worldwide.
- Astemo will accelerate efforts together with GlobalLogic and various partners to develop the mobility of the future for sustainable growth.
- GlobalLogic is a leader in digital engineering that helps brands design and build innovative products, platforms, and digital experiences for the modern world.
- GlobalLogic integrates experience design, complex engineering, and data expertise to help clients transition into digital businesses.
- GlobalLogic is headquartered in Silicon Valley and operates design studios and engineering centers globally.
- GlobalLogic serves customers in the automotive, communications, financial services, healthcare and life sciences, manufacturing, media and entertainment, semiconductor, and technology industries.
- GlobalLogic is a Hitachi Group Company operating under Hitachi, Ltd. (TSE: 6501).
- Hitachi, Ltd. contributes to a sustainable society with a higher quality of life by driving innovation through data and technology as the Social Innovation Business.
