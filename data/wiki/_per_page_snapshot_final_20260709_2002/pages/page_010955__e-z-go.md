## Facts

- E-Z-GO is headquartered in Augusta, Georgia.
- The company is celebrating its 71st anniversary of American manufacturing.
- From June 13-15, buyers will receive a $500 instant rebate off the purchase of any new E-Z-GO model in stock at local dealers.
- The rebate applies to models including the Freedom® RXV personal golf car and the E-Z-GO Express™ L6 six-passenger vehicle.
- The June 13-15 discount stacks with the ongoing "Red, White, & Built Right" savings event.
- The "Red, White, & Built Right" event offers instant rebates ranging from $1,000-$4,000 on specific E-Z-GO models.
- Customers could save up to $4,500 on a brand-new American-made golf cart when combining promotions.
- June 13 marks the exact day in 1954 when brothers Billy and Bev Dolan started E-Z-GO in a garage in Augusta, Georgia.
- E-Z-GO operates from a 54-acre campus in Augusta, Georgia.
- The Augusta campus employs over 1,000 people who design, manufacture, and distribute golf cars and utility vehicles worldwide.
- The region also has manufacturing jobs with the golf cart/utility vehicle company E-Z-Go Texxon.
- E-Z-Go Texxon earned the Augusta metro area the nickname "Golf Cart Capital of the World."
- Ninety percent of the world’s golf carts are made in Augusta, Georgia.
- Nine out of every ten golf carts rolling around courses from California to Florida were built by American hands in Georgia.
- E-Z-GO builds its vehicles with American engineering and craftsmanship rather than relying on cheap Chinese parts or foreign subsidies used by competitors.
- Local competitors Club Car and E-Z-GO combine to employ thousands of local Augusta residents and make a lasting economic impact each year.
- Close to 20 percent of Georgia’s workforce is dedicated to traditional and advanced manufacturing, including sterile environment technology.
- Georgia has built one of the strongest manufacturing economies in the country through decades of smart policies that support manufacturing and American workers.
- Adam Harris serves as Vice President and General Manager at E-Z-GO.
- Adam Harris stated: "We are proud to celebrate National Golf Cart Day and our anniversary with our employees, our community, and our customers around the world."
- Adam Harris added: "E-Z-GO is an iconic American brand, and it’s an honor to have this occasion to commemorate its unique place in the sport of golf and the world of transportation."
- The company began operations in a cramped one-room machine shop in 1954 before expanding to its current campus.
- The National Golf Cart Day promotion runs through June 15.
