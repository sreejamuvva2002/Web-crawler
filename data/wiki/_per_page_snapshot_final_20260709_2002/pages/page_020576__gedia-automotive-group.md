# GEDIA Automotive Group

## Facts

- GEDIA has been in existence for over a hundred years.
- The company has been producing for the automotive industry since 1955.
- GEDIA employs more than 4,700 people at nine production sites worldwide and is involved in joint ventures and research companies.
- Since selling its handle division in 2003, the company has focused on developing lightweight automotive technologies and chassis components.
- In 2016, GEDIA expanded its U.S. footprint by adding a new location in Michigan.
- In June 2018, the go-ahead was given for the construction of the “Askay II” production plant, which went into operation in 2019.
- In 2022, GEDIA integrated a new production plant in Georgia (USA) into its global production network.
- The Georgia facility supplies body components to both German and American OEMs.
- GEDIA develops new vehicle concepts for bodies and chassis in close cooperation with car manufacturers worldwide.
- The company focuses on weight-reduced and crash-resistant parts and components to contribute to greater sustainability and safety.
