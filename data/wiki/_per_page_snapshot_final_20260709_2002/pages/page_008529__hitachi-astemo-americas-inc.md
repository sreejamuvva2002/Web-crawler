# Hitachi Astemo Americas Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-05-16

**Source:** [Hitachi Astemo Americas’ Gary Plover Appointed to CAWA’s MAC](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/hitachi-astemo-americas-gary-plover-appointed-to-cawas-mac/)  ·  `page_008529`

## Facts

- Gary Plover of Hitachi Astemo Americas Inc. has been appointed to CAWA’s Manufacturers’ Advisory Council.
- Michael Rukov, chair of the CAWA board of directors, commented on the appointment process.
- Plover is currently vice president of the Hitachi Astemo Americas Aftermarket Division.
- He has been a part of the automotive industry for more than 25 years.
- He served his first six years in OE sales for Nichirin, on an international team that was responsible for mergers and acquisitions worldwide.
- He then moved to the aftermarket, working for Aisin World Corp of America, Goodridge, Superwinch.
- Plover is instrumental in creating strategies that increase global sales significantly.
- He has a proven record of accomplishment in driving record-setting revenues.
