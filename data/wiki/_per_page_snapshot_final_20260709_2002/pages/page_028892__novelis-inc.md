# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-08-11

**Source:** [Novelis Reports First Quarter Fiscal Year 2026 Results](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/novelis-reports-first-quarter-fiscal-year-2026-results-302526344.html)  ·  `page_028892`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is a leading sustainable aluminum solutions provider and the world leader in aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Novelis is constructing a new greenfield rolling and recycling plant in Bay Minette, Alabama.
- Total capital expenditures for the first three months of fiscal year 2026 were $386 million, primarily attributed to strategic investments in new rolling and recycling capacity under construction at the Bay Minette facility.
- In June 2025, Novelis issued $400 million of tax-exempt bonds with a mandatory tender for purchase in 2032 and maturation in 2055 to finance a portion of the Bay Minette construction costs.
- During the quarter ended June 30, 2025, Novelis incurred $5 million of start-up costs related to the Bay Minette plant construction, recorded in Selling, general and administrative expenses.
- Novelis is executing a comprehensive cost reduction program expected to deliver run-rate cost savings exceeding $100 million by the end of fiscal year 2026.
- The company's aluminum products serve customers in the aerospace, automotive, beverage packaging, and specialties industries across North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- Novelis aims to be the leading provider of low-carbon, sustainable aluminum solutions and achieve a fully circular economy through supplier and customer partnerships.
- Steve Fisher serves as president and CEO of Novelis Inc.
- Dev Ahuja serves as executive vice president and CFO of Novelis Inc.
- Net sales for Q1 FY2026 increased 13% year-over-year to $4.7 billion, driven by higher average aluminum prices and a 1% increase in total rolled product shipments.
- Rolled product shipments reached 963 kilotonnes in Q1 FY2026, up 1% year-over-year.
- Adjusted EBITDA for Q1 FY2026 was $416 million, down 17% year-over-year due to higher aluminum scrap prices, unfavorable product mix, and a net negative tariff impact.
- Net income attributable to common shareholders decreased 36% year-over-year to $96 million in Q1 FY2026, primarily driven by restructuring charges and lower operating performance.
- Restructuring and impairment expenses for Q1 FY2026 totaled $85 million, including $83 million related to the 2025 Efficiency Plan.
- Novelis reported a net leverage ratio of 3.2x at the end of Q1 FY2026.
- Total liquidity stood at $3.0 billion as of June 30, 2025, consisting of $1.1 billion in cash and cash equivalents and $2.0 billion in availability under committed credit facilities.
- Novelis had net sales of $17.1 billion in fiscal year 2025.
- Novelis is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited, the metals flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group.
