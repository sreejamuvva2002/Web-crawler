# Aurubis

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2023-02-02

## Facts

- Aurubis is adding one of the world’s largest state-of-the-art multi-metal recycling facilities to Augusta.
- Cal Wray serves as President of the Augusta Economic Development Authority.
- The Augusta Economic Development Authority hosted a panel discussion addressing Aurubis's investment.
- Aurubis's investment is the largest single investment by a German company in Georgia.
- The project will create up to 1,000 jobs.
- The project has a 1 Billion dollar impact.
- Aurubis aims to expand Georgia’s electromobility value chain through sustainable development and raw material security.
- The new Augusta facility will produce materials required in lithium-ion batteries and other electronics.
- The facility is located at the Augusta Corporate Park.
- The facility spans 150 acres.
- Further expansions are expected at the Augusta facility.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development is tracking the project's progress through a blog on its website.
