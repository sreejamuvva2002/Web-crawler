# Pirelli Rome, Georgia Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [PIRELLI WILL START CYBER™ TYRE PRODUCTION IN GEORGIA UNDERLINING THE STRATEGIC IMPORTANCE OF THE U.S. MARKET](https://press.pirelli.com/pirelli-will-start-cyber-tyre-production-in-georgia-underlining-the-strategic-importance-of-the-us-market/)  ·  `page_048446`

## Facts

- Pirelli is reinforcing its long-term commitment to the United States with a key step in its product and industrial strategy.
- Investment and output expansion details for the Georgia plant will be communicated in the coming months following the finalization of development plans.
- The Georgia plant, already dedicated to technologically advanced products for the U.S. market in the High-Value segment and Motorsport, will be enhanced by the production of connected tires featuring Cyber™ Tyre technology.
- This development highlights the strategic importance of the U.S. for Pirelli’s global growth roadmap and strengthens the company’s integrated industrial and technological presence in the country.
- The announcement was made as Pirelli participated in the SelectUSA Investment Summit, a flagship event promoted by the U.S. Department of Commerce.
- Cyber™ Tyre is being showcased at the summit as a core innovation shaping the future of smart mobility.
- Cyber™ Tyre is the world’s first hardware-and-software system capable of collecting data and information from sensors embedded in tires, processing them through Pirelli’s proprietary software and algorithms, and communicating in real time with the vehicle’s electronics.
- The technology enables new functionalities integrated with driving and control systems to enhance the driving experience and increase safety levels, as well as supporting connected infrastructure.
- In the highly advanced U.S. market where digital and smart mobility solutions are rapidly expanding, Cyber™ Tyre represents a distinctive competitive advantage for Pirelli.
- At SelectUSA, Cyber™ Tyre was showcased at the Georgia State booth, highlighting Pirelli’s industrial footprint in the state where it has been established for more than 20 years.
- Claudio Zanardo, CEO of Pirelli North America, stated that the start of Cyber™ Tyre production in the Rome, Georgia plant is a significant milestone for Pirelli in the country.
- The production start reflects Pirelli’s commitment to bringing advanced technologies like Cyber™ Tyre closer to the market and further strengthening its industrial footprint and innovation capabilities in the United States.
- To enhance Rome as a high-tech production site, Pirelli is finalizing the introduction of the latest version of the MIRS (Modular Integrated Robotized System) production process.
- The MIRS process will be the most advanced manufacturing process for high-end, premium production within the entire Pirelli Group and is exclusive to the Georgia factory.
- The new process further enhances robotized production capabilities, increasing productivity, flexibility, and quality.
- It is a highly digitalized system that enables a direct link between product design and its industrial application.
- This development lays the groundwork for growth in Pirelli’s production capacity in Rome, which is an integral part of the Group’s industrial development plans.
- The Rome, Georgia plant specializes in high-value tire production for the North American market.
- The facility also hosts a dedicated R&D center, further strengthening its role in technological development and product innovation.
- The facility is recognized for responsible sourcing practices, including the use of FSC-certified natural rubber, underscoring the company’s broader environmental commitment across its supply chain.
