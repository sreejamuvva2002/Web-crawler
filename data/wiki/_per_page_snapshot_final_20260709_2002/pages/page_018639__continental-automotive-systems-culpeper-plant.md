# Continental Automotive Systems Culpeper Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-09-01

**Source:** [Continental to close Culpeper plant in 2024, eliminating 225 jobs](https://starexponent.com/news/article_7c433c02-584a-5c42-a6d4-8a2c17134323.html)  ·  `page_018639`

## Facts

- Continental has operated a brake parts manufacturing facility on Lovers Lane in Culpeper since 1977.
- The Germany-based parent company announced it would close the local facility in 2024.
- The closure is part of a major global restructuring in response to dramatic decreases in vehicle production made worse by the pandemic.
- The plant closing will eliminate 225 jobs.
- The Culpeper County plant manufactures a valve block and its housing unit.
- The valve block and housing unit are used by carmakers in a range of vehicles.
