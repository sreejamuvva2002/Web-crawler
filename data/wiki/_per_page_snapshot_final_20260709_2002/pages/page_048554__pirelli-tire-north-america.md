## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America designs, develops, manufactures and markets tires for passenger vehicles, including cars, vans and motorcycles.
- The company sells and distributes products to clients in the original equipment, replacement, export and domestic markets.
- It also offers a range of tires for motorsport competitions.
- Pirelli Tire North America is a part of Pirelli & C. S.p.A., which is one of the largest tire manufacturers in the world.
- Pirelli & C. S.p.A. is an operational holding company for a group involved in the design, development, production and marketing of tires for cars, light vehicles and motorcycles, buses, trucks, and agricultural and earth moving machinery.
