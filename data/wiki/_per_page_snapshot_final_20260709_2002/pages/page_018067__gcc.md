## Facts

- GCC is preparing now to be the exclusive Bosch Rexroth Mobile Partner for six additional states: North Carolina, South Carolina, Virginia, West Virginia, Tennessee and all of Georgia.
- Effective April 1, 2025, GCC will provide Bosch Rexroth products to NC, SC, VA, WV, GA, TN, FL, WA, OR, AK, PR and Northern ID.
- GCC has long been a trusted partner in the hydraulics sector, specializing in providing technical expertise and tailored solutions to OEMs.
- Businesses in the expanded states can now access GCC’s complete range of services, including system design, circuit optimization, and technical support, backed by Bosch Rexroth’s superior products.
- GCC collaborates with OEMs to develop custom subassemblies that can meet unique project requirements.
- Efficient inventory management minimizes downtime and maximizes productivity for GCC customers.
- GCC’s aftermarket services ensure that equipment can stay operational for the long haul, covering installation to maintenance throughout its lifecycle.
- The GCC sales team includes Certified Fluid Power Specialists, ensuring customers receive expert guidance.
- GCC integrates the latest technologies to deliver smarter, more effective mobile solutions focused on safety and efficiency.
- GCC promoted Jordan Pritt to Executive Vice President in April 2025, overseeing company expansion in the Southeast.
- The partnership expansion enables customers across these territories to access world-class hydraulic and electrohydraulic expertise closer to home.
