## Facts

- LINDE + WIEMANN operates approximately 2,300 dedicated employees across 18 production facilities in 8 countries on 4 continents.
- The company produces structural components for the vehicle construction industry, including Sills, A- and B-Pillars, and structural components in the Front End, Cockpit, and Chassis fields.
- LINDE + WIEMANN utilizes stamping, profile, hot forming, and joining technology to deliver crash-relevant body-in-white components to Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs).
- LINDE + WIEMANN US Inc. operates a facility located at 1720 Knox Bridge Crossing Rd, 30553 Lavonia / GA – US.
- The Georgia facility covers an area of 4,100 m².
- The company's products are designed to enhance vehicle safety while simultaneously reducing their overall weight.
- LINDE + WIEMANN has been a trusted partner with OEMs in the international automobile industry for over 85 years.
- The company's long-term sustainability goal is to achieve CO2-neutrality by 2039.
- LINDE + WIEMANN aligns its climate targets with the Paris Convention's goal of limiting temperature increase to 1.5 degrees Celsius.
- Environmental and energy management systems are certified under ISO 14001 and 50001, with annual external audits.
- A Sustainability Committee comprising ESG managers drives corporate ESG initiatives and ensures compliance with ethical and legal requirements.
- The company aims to reach 100% supplier compliance with its Supplier Code of Conduct and Ethics by 2028, aligning with the German Act on Corporate Due Diligence Obligations in Supply Chains (LkSG).
- LINDE + WIEMANN operates a whistleblower platform operated by EQS Group AG for anonymous reporting of misconduct and risks related to human rights and the environment.
- The company's governance model is guided by the Declaration of Human Rights and United Nations Global Compact.
