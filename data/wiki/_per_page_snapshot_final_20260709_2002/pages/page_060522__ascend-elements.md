# Ascend Elements

## Facts

- Battery recycling startup Ascend Elements filed for bankruptcy on Thursday, according to the company.
- Westborough, Massachusetts-based Ascend specializes in reprocessing old batteries into cathode material at a factory in Georgia.
- The company has raised more than $1.1 billion in equity and grants since its founding in 2015.
- Chief Executive Officer Linh Austin stated that financial difficulties were insurmountable and the company voluntarily initiated a Chapter 11 process.
- CEO Linh Austin said creating a new industry to recycle old batteries is capital-intensive and complex, while the US seeks to break its dependency on battery materials from China.
- Ascend saw a $316 million grant canceled by the administration last October.
- Both grants were made under former President Joe Biden.
- The company counts top investment houses, including Temasek, Qatar Investment Authority, and BHP Ventures, among its backers.
