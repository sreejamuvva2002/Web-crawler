# voestalpine Automotive Components Cartersville Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Voestalpine in Cartersville, Georgia, USA secures production with AP&T service commitment](https://www.ffjournal.net/industry-news/5-news/17501-voestalpine-in-cartersville-georgia-usa-secures-production-with-apt-service-commitment?highlight=WyJwcmV0dHkiLCJtdWNoIl0=)  ·  `page_013902`

## Facts

- voestalpine in Cartersville, Georgia, USA is a sub-supplier of parts and components to the automotive industry and has many leading OEMs as customers.
- The facility produces structural parts for some of the world's chief OEMs whose production relies on having a constant flow of parts and components.
- Production equipment includes two press-hardening lines from AP&T used to produce structural parts.
- The two press-hardening lines have been in operation since 2017.
- About a year ago, production was running around the clock without pause while manufacturing enormous volumes.
- To secure production capacity and minimize unplanned downtime risk, voestalpine signed an extensive service agreement with AP&T in the spring of 2022.
- The service agreement covers two press-hardening lines critical to the facility's production capacity.
- The agreement includes regular preventative maintenance, 24/7 online support, and on-site assistance with priority response time at a fixed monthly cost.
- voestalpine now has access to quick, qualified support in the evenings and on weekends, which they have benefited from on several occasions.
- Most operational problems can be resolved remotely, but AP&T technicians can visit on-site at short notice if needed.
- voestalpine is currently in discussions with AP&T about designing an agreement to guarantee uptime.
- Potential future agreement terms could include handing over stocking spare machine parts to AP&T and having them guarantee parts delivery within a certain timeframe to free up warehouse space and reduce tied-up capital.
- Fredrik Stålberg serves as Managing Director and CTO of voestalpine in Cartersville.
- Magnus Svenningsson is the Director of Aftermarket Sales at AP&T.
- Tobias Kronstedt is the Aftermarket Service Manager for AP&T in the US.
- Andrea Clark is the President of AP&T North America.
