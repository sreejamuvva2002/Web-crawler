## Facts

- Electrification will have a cascading impact on energy infrastructure, extending from the electric grid to homes and buildings.
- Eaton is developing comprehensive electric vehicle charging infrastructure (EVCI) solutions and services.
- Eaton's EVCI solutions unite the power needs of buildings and electric vehicles with on-site renewable energy generation.
- Eaton provides planning, deployment, and management services for sustainable systems supporting safe and reliable electric vehicle charging to building owners/managers, builders, consultants, designers, homeowners, and fleet managers.
- Eaton's approach utilizes scalable, secure, resilient energy hubs to power buildings, homes, facilities, and charge EVs with reliable, sustainable, cost-effective energy.
- Eaton offers a customizable decentralized energy approach to help customers prepare for upcoming demand.
- Eaton is developing a comprehensive portfolio of EVCI software, services, and hardware.
- Eaton designs its comprehensive solution to integrate EV charging infrastructure and distributed energy resources (DERs).
- Eaton's solutions can optimize sustainability, resiliency, and cost-effectiveness.
- Planning and careful design are required to add electric vehicle charging to new or existing buildings for efficient, tailored infrastructure.
- Eaton leverages more than 100 years of experience, technology leadership, and extensive research to provide end-to-end project management capabilities.
- Eaton supports its project management with field engineers and teams across the region.
- eMobility is a new business within Eaton that combines elements of its Electrical and Vehicle businesses.
- The eMobility business delivers electric vehicle solutions to passenger car, commercial vehicle, and off-highway OEMs.
- Eaton is helping companies and communities around the world simplify their energy systems and safely transition to a low-carbon future as the energy transition advances.
