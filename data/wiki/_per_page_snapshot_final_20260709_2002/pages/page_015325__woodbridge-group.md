# Woodbridge Group

## Facts

- Woodbridge’s Technical Foam offers specialty-engineered polyurethane foam solutions for a wide range of product requirements.
- The foams produced by Woodbridge are used in a wide variety of applications throughout the vehicle.
- Applications include reticulated polyether or polyester for air filters.
- Applications include low-permeability ethers for air/dust sealing.
- Applications include acoustic foams for sound absorption/NVH.
- Applications include low-VOC/fog polyether/polyester for interior applications.
- Applications include high-density/firmness polyether for comfort or safety pads.
- Product range includes polyester filter foams with 7-100 ppi.
- Product range includes polyether filter foams with 8-80 ppi.
- Product densities range from 1-6 lb/ft3.
- Ester firmness ranges from 30-100 lb.
- Ether firmness ranges from 20-250 lb.
- Product range includes high-durability esters.
- Product range includes FR modified esters and ethers meeting FMVSS-302, CAL117, and UL standards.
- Product range includes air-and-water sealable ester and ether foams.
- Product range includes low-VOC/Low-fog esters and ethers.
- Product range includes hydrophilic esters and ethers.
- Product range includes explosion suppression foam.
- Woodbridge's technical foams meet Ford customer specification WSS-M99P48.
- Woodbridge's technical foams meet GM customer specification GMW16750.
- Woodbridge's technical foams meet FCA customer specification MS-AY 309.
- Woodbridge's technical foams meet Toyota customer specification TSK6712G.
- Woodbridge's technical foams meet Hyundai/Kia customer specifications MS257-06 and MS300-55.
- Woodbridge's technical foams meet Daimler customer specification DBL 5450.
