# YKK AP America Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [YKK AP Holds Ribbon-Cutting Ceremony for New Macon Facility | Window + Door](https://www.windowanddoor.com/news/ykk-ap-holds-ribbon-cutting-ceremony-new-macon-facility)  ·  `page_016190`

## Facts

- YKK AP America Inc. held a ribbon-cutting ceremony to mark the official opening of its first greenfield residential manufacturing facility in Macon-Bibb County, Georgia.
- The event was held in Macon with Commissioner Pat Wilson of the Georgia Department of Economic Development, Mayor Lester Miller of Macon-Bibb County, Chairman Robby Fountain of the Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority, and other local leaders in attendance.
- The facility completed construction in December 2023 and began operations in January 2024.
- It currently employs more than 250 employees in the Macon community.
- Mayor Lester Miller stated that YKK AP’s expansion into a new, leading-edge facility is a testament to its strong connection with the community and marks the beginning of a new chapter offering opportunities to enrich the local economy, improve families' lives, and strengthen Macon's position as a hub of innovation and industry.
- Oliver Stepe, president and CEO of YKK AP America Inc., stated that the facility symbolizes the company's growth and confidence in its business and employees moving forward, and expressed gratitude to the state of Georgia, the Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority, and partners for helping celebrate the milestone.
