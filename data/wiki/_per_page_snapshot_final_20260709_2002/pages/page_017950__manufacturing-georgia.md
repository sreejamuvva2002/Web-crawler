# Manufacturing Georgia

**Type:** workforce_training  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Georgia Association of Manufacturers Launches "Manufacturing Georgia" to Tackle Workforce Crisis and Redefine Industry Image](https://unmudl.com/news/georgia-association-of-manufacturers-launches-manufacturing-georgia-to-tackle-workforce-crisis-and-redefine-industry-image)  ·  `page_017950`

## Facts

- The Georgia Association of Manufacturers (GAM) has launched Manufacturing Georgia, a digital marketplace designed to address Georgia's manufacturing labor crisis, redefine the industry's public perception, and build a stronger talent pipeline across the state.
- Manufacturing Georgia breaks the no-win cycle of manufacturers being forced to rob Peter to pay Paul by hiring away from their peers just to fill immediate roles.
- It is a dynamic, two-way marketplace where companies connect with talent in real time and define the skills they need so new talent and current employees can upskill directly to job requirements.
- The platform is powered by social engagement, real relationships, and transparent skill paths to deliver a stronger labor pool and a modern image of manufacturing.
- Developed by GAM alongside manufacturers, it is an inclusive, statewide solution open to all Georgia-based manufacturers.
- The platform lets users explore local jobs, message employers directly, and discover real career benefits like tuition assistance, childcare, and second-chance hiring.
- Employers must manufacture in Georgia to participate, while job seekers can come from anywhere.
- So far, over 1,400 users have joined the platform, with strong interest from prospective employees in neighboring states.
- Early participating manufacturers include Toyo Tires, Engineered Floors, Kia Georgia, Shaw Industries, and Grenzebach.
- Stephanie Scearce, Vice President of Workforce Innovation at GAM, said the platform breaks the unsustainable hiring cycle.
- Patrick Lenz, President and COO of Toyo Tires NA Manufacturing, stated the project is a unique and timely way to engage the younger generation and their parents, local high schools, and grow the future workforce.
- Manufacturing Georgia helps companies hire for today while building for the future through upskilling and reskilling.
- The platform's tagline is 'Make Your Way,' highlighting modern, high-tech careers ranging from robotics to software rather than just shop floor roles.
- It aims to break old stereotypes and connect people to real family-sustaining opportunities in one of Georgia's most important industries.
- The Manufacturing Georgia Marketplace was built in partnership with Unmudl Skills-to-Jobs® Marketplace, which provided the marketplace technology and Skills-to-Jobs® expertise.
- GAM represents an industry of approximately 10,000 manufacturing entities and almost 424,000 workers.
- For 125 years, GAM has advocated on issues that matter most to manufacturers and championed manufacturing growth and excellence in Georgia.
