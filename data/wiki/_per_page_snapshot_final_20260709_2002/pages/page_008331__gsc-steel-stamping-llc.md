## Facts

- GSC Steel Stamping, LLC is a minority and family-owned and operated automotive parts manufacturer.
- The company provides Body in White frame assembly brackets to some of the world’s leading OEMs and tier one suppliers.
- GSC Steel Stamping offers competitively priced metal stamping, engineering, welded assemblies, and tooling services.
- In June 2023, GSC acquired Dixien, LLC’s assets.
- The acquisition continues a 43-year history of automotive parts stamping at the company's Lake City, GA location.
- The facility is located at 5286 Circle Drive, Lake City, Georgia, 30260, United States.
- The Lake City, GA manufacturing site spans 200,000 sq. ft.
- CEO Vincent Henderson purchased and led the turnaround of two award-winning automotive stamping operations in Michigan prior to GSC Steel Stamping.
- Those Michigan operations were regularly recognized by customers for excellence in overall performance, quality, and on-time delivery.
- The plant operations management team possesses over 200 years of combined stamping experience.
- GSC continuously re-evaluates processes, invests in advanced technology, and hires staff to maintain excellence in overall performance, quality, and on-time delivery.
- The company has between 100 and 249 employees.
