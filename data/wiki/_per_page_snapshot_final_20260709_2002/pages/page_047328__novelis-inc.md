# Novelis, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-05-11

**Source:** [Novelis, Inc. announces $2.5 billion investment in South Alabama Mega Site](https://www.fox10tv.com/2022/05/11/novelis-inc-announces-25-billion-dollar-investment-south-alabama-mega-site/)  ·  `page_047328`

## Facts

- Novelis will make a $2.5 billion investment as the anchor tenant to the South Alabama Mega Site.
- The company will need 1,000 employees to operate its state-of-the-art plant.
- President of North American Operations for Novelis Tom Boney said the search for key roles has already begun.
- Key roles and responsibilities are expected to be announced hopefully before the end of May 2022.
- Groundbreaking is scheduled to occur in approximately two and a half months from the announcement, with employee hiring starting very early in the project.
- Groundbreaking is expected in early summer and the plant should be in operation in 2025.
- The company’s initial footprint on the Mega Site property will be 1,000 acres.
- It will be the first fully integrated aluminum mill built in the United States in 40 years.
- The facility will use recycled water and operate as a zero-waste facility.
- Novelis produces products from recycled aluminum, including soda and beer cans, pickup truck beds, and aircraft components.
- President of the Baldwin County Economic Development Alliance Lee Lawson played a critical role in landing Novelis at the Mega Site.
- Lee Lawson stated that spending in the billions will ripple through the community and benefit local and regional suppliers.
- Local resident Larry Vaughn noted the project will help school systems and create future job opportunities for children to raise families locally.
- Novelis North American headquarters is located in Atlanta, and the company operates on four continents.
- The company plans to reduce its carbon footprint by 30 percent by 2026 and achieve carbon neutrality by 2050.
