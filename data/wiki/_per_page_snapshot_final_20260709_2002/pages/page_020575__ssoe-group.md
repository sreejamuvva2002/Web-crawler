# SSOE Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-08-25

**Source:** [SSOE Group Selected to Provide Planning, Design, and Owner Representative Services for GEDIA’s Greenfield Manufacturing Facility in the Southeast - SSOE Group](https://www.ssoe.com/press-releases/ssoe-group-selected-to-provide-planning-design-and-owner-representative-services-for-gedias-greenfield-manufacturing-facility-in-the-southeast/)  ·  `page_020575`

## Facts

- SSOE Group was selected to provide planning, design, and owner representative services to GEDIA Automotive Group for a new advanced manufacturing facility in Dalton, Georgia.
- The announcement of the selection was made on August 25, 2020.
- GEDIA Georgia LLC will produce lightweight body-in-white automotive parts and assemblies at the Dalton plant.
- GEDIA Georgia LLC will also produce chassis components at the Dalton plant.
- The new Dalton facility will be GEDIA’s second U.S. plant.
- GEDIA’s first U.S. plant is located in Michigan.
- Brandon Darroch, PMP, serves as the Southeast Division Manager for SSOE.
- SSOE supports international clients looking to expand or set up manufacturing in the U.S., particularly foreign direct investment (FDI) clients.
- SSOE has a long history of supporting European and Asian-based clients.
- SSOE holds memberships with TBIC and other organizations that encourage FDI.
- SSOE is nationally ranked as a Top 5 automotive assembly plant design firm by ENR in 2020.
- SSOE’s roster of clients manufactures more than 50% of the cars sold worldwide.
- SSOE recently added two new electric vehicle manufacturers to its client list.
- Lucid Motors is located in Casa Grande, Arizona.
- Lucid is building an 820,000 SF facility.
- The Lucid facility will produce the Lucid Air, a luxury electric car with a 400-mile range battery.
- SSOE is serving as the architect and engineer of record for the Lucid Motors project.
