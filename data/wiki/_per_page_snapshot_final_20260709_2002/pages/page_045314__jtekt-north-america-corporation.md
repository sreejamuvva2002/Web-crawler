## Facts

- JTEKT North America Corporation is a motor vehicle parts manufacturer based in Greenville, South Carolina.
- The company employs between 1,001 and 5,000 people.
- It operates as a subsidiary of JTEKT Corporation.
- JTEKT North America develops and manufactures steering systems, driveline components, and bearings for the automotive, industrial, and energy sectors.
- The company's bearings were previously marketed under the Koyo brand.
- It maintains quality management standards indicated by certifications such as ISO 14001 and IATF 16949.
- Recent activity includes expansions at facilities in Greenville and Piedmont, South Carolina.
- These expansions involve adding multiple production lines and equipment to increase manufacturing capacity.
- In 2024, Yoshihito Kondo was appointed president of the company.
- The organization is part of the JTEKT global group, focusing on steering, driveline, and bearing technologies for a range of industries.
