# Adient

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Adient is a leader in automotive seating.
- Adient presented its latest seating innovations at IAA 2023.
- The current automotive business landscape is marked by shifting industry dynamics, showcasing a strong desire for mobility, with an emphasis on digitalization, cost, and sustainable products.
- Adient’s overall approach focuses on responding to the need for more sustainable material use while taking advantage of the potential that sustainable practices hold for streamlining processes.
- The Pure Essential seat is designed for overall cost and complexity reduction in manufacturing and is especially lightweight.
- Environmentally-conscious practices such as material separation and recycling, and design for disassembly are embedded into the manufacturing process from the development stage for the Pure Essential seat.
- The Pure Essential seat consists of two materials only: green steel and recyclable polyester (PET).
- The Autonomous Elegance seat is specifically developed to meet new customer needs in terms of premium comfort and to fit Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS).
- State-of-the-art findings on ergonomics and human body kinematics have been incorporated into the Autonomous Elegance seat following extensive occupant research.
- The Autonomous Elegance seat is complemented by advanced comfort assets such as noise cancellation and advanced climate functions.
- David Herberg serves as Vice President Engineering Adient EMEA.
- David Herberg highlighted: “Our seat demonstrators provide solutions to our customers’ main concerns, and we are looking forward to continuing the strategic product dialogue with them, based on our new demonstrators.”
- Most features of the Autonomous Elegance seat can already be offered for sourcing, such as the metal structure and seat kinematics (adjustment functions and mechanisms).
- The Smart Efficiency seat has been updated considering optimized use of space as well as sustainability aspects.
- The Smart Efficiency seat features a slimmer appearance than its predecessor without compromising on comfort.
- The Smart Efficiency seat design helps save space and paves the way for new mobility concepts based on battery packaging in electric vehicles.
- The showcased products will be available for demonstration in customer roadshows as of December 2023.
