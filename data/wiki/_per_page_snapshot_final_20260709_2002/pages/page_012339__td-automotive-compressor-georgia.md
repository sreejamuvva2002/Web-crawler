# TD Automotive Compressor Georgia

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Consulate-General of Japan in Atlanta](https://www.atlanta.us.emb-japan.go.jp/TCGA10anniversary_E.html)  ·  `page_012339`

## Facts

- Held a ceremony on October 9 commemorating the 10-year anniversary of its establishment in Pendergrass, GA.
- Jackson County Chairman of Commissioners Tom Crowe attended as a VIP guest.
- State Department of Commerce International Investment Director Scott McMurray attended as a VIP guest.
- President Akira Onishi from Toyota Industries Corporation Japan, TCGA's parent company, attended as a VIP guest.
- Denso Managing Officer Katsuhisa Shimokawa attended as a VIP guest.
- Consul General Sunaga attended as a VIP guest.
- State Senator Frank Ginn, State Representative Tommy Benton, Jefferson Mayor Roy Plot, and economic development officials attended from the local community.
- Approximately 500 company employees attended the celebratory event.
- Founded in 2004 as a joint venture by Denso International America, Inc. (the US subsidiary of the Denso Corporation) along with Toyota Industries North America, Inc. (TINA, Indianapolis, Columbus).
- Manufactures automobile air-conditioning compressors.
- Employs approximately 580 employees.
- In 2012, TCGA air-compressor production volume topped 1 million units.
- In 2013, TCGA and TICA began a $190 million joint production expansion plan.
- The 2013 joint production expansion plan would create 120 new jobs.
