# Hyundai-SK On Georgia Battery Cell Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-04-25

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group and SK On to build battery cell plant in Georgia](https://businessalabama.com/hyundai-motor-group-and-sk-on-to-build-battery-cell-plant-in-georgia/)  ·  `page_000633`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group and SK On plan to build an EV battery cell production plant in Bartow County, Georgia.
- Hyundai Motor Group and SK On each will have a 50% stake in the new battery cell plant.
- The new battery cell plant is estimated to cost $5 billion to build.
- The battery cell plant is expected to become operational in the second half of 2025.
- Once operational, the battery cell plant is expected to have an annual production capacity of 35 GWh.
- The 35 GWh annual capacity is sufficient to support the production of 300,000 EVs.
- Battery cells built at the new plant will be shipped to Hyundai Mobis in Montgomery for assembly into battery modules.
- Assembled battery modules will be shipped to Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama (HMMA), Kia Georgia, and Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant Atlanta.
- Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama started producing the EV Genesis earlier this year.
- The Electrified GV70 is Hyundai's third all-electric model.
- The start of production at HMMA was made possible by a $300 million infrastructure expansion and upgrade at Hyundai’s Montgomery plant in 2022.
- Hyundai Mobis announced in 2022 that it would invest $205 million to develop an EV battery module plant.
- The Hyundai Mobis EV battery module plant is intended to support both Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama and Kia Georgia.
- Hyundai Mobis currently employs about 1,100 workers in Montgomery making front and rear chassis and cockpit modules for HMMA.
- Hyundai Mobis will add about 400 employees to make the new battery modules.
- H.S. Oh, vice president of electric powertrain business of Korean-based parent company Hyundai Mobis, stated that having a strong production capacity will be key in allowing Hyundai Mobis to see continued growth in the market as the EV market continues to grow.
