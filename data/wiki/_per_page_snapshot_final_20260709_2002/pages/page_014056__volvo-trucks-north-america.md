## Facts

- Volvo Trucks North America is headquartered in Greensboro, North Carolina.
- The company delivers its Uptime Services commitment through a network of nearly 400 authorized dealers across North America.
- A 24/7 Volvo Trucks Uptime Center supports the dealer network.
- Every Volvo truck is assembled at the Volvo Trucks New River Valley manufacturing facility in Dublin, Virginia.
- The Dublin, Virginia manufacturing facility meets the ISO 9001 standard for quality.
- The Dublin, Virginia manufacturing facility meets the ISO 14001 standard for environmental care.
- The Dublin, Virginia manufacturing facility holds a dual ISO 50001/Superior Energy Performance certification at the platinum level for sustained excellence in energy management.
- Volvo Trucks North America provides complete transport solutions offering a full range of diesel, alternative-fuel, and all-electric vehicles.
- Volvo Trucks North America is part of the Volvo Trucks global organization.
- Volvo Trucks supplies medium- and heavy-duty trucks to professional customers via a global dealer network with 2,200 service points in about 130 countries.
- Volvo trucks are assembled in 12 countries across the globe.
- Approximately 120,000 Volvo trucks were delivered worldwide in 2025.
- Volvo Trucks is part of the Volvo Group, one of the world’s leading manufacturers of trucks, buses, construction equipment, and marine and industrial engines.
- The Volvo Group also provides complete solutions for financing and service.
- Volvo Trucks’ work is based on the core values of quality, safety, and environmental care.
