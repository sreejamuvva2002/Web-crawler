# Continental AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Continental AG’s Tires Group posted $3.6 billion in sales during the third quarter of 2024.
- Tire group sales represented a 1.9% increase from the same period in 2023.
- Adjusted EBIT margin for the tire business was up 13.3% versus the third quarter of 2023.
- Continental CEO Olaf Schick stated that tires are performing well in terms of profitability, with the winter tire business getting off to a good start.
- The company posted consolidated sales of $10.3 billion for the third quarter.
- Global adjusted EBIT for the company was $918.5 million.
- Continental’s ContiTech division faced challenges during Q3 2024 due to continued weak industrial development in Europe and North America.
- Continental does not expect the industrial business to recover during the fourth quarter of 2024.
- The company adjusted its sales and earnings outlook for ContiTech, lowering overall sales expectations for the Continental Group.
- Continental Tire the Americas LLC continued to invest in its U.S. operations during Q3 2024.
- The company launched two consumer tires: the Continental TrueContact Tour54 all-season touring tire and the Grabber H/T all-season touring tire.
- Continental launched its first electric vehicle-compatible TBR tire, the Conti HAU 5.
- The company opened its 24th BestDrive commercial tire location in Charlotte, N.C.
- Continental announced its first fully owned distribution center, a 753,000-square-foot facility in the Dallas, Texas, metro area.
- Two new iterations of the ContiConnect medium truck tire management tool were rolled out in the U.S.
