# Thomson Plastics, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-07-10

**Source:** [Altus Capital Partners | News](https://www.altuscapitalpartners.com/article/10-jul-2023/thomson-plastics-inc-installs-spotting-press)  ·  `page_011343`

## Facts

- Thomson Plastics Inc. (TPI) has announced the installation of a spotting press in Thomson, GA to strengthen the company’s preventive maintenance program and provide increased support to its injection molding customers.
- The investment allows TPI to internally increase performance capability for tool maintenance and repair, enhancing overall product quality and consistency while minimizing production downtime and customer costs.
- Though located at TPI’s Georgia headquarters, the company’s facilities in Lexington, NC, Duncan, SC, and Howell, MI, will be able to utilize the spotting press capabilities to serve their customers.
- As the premier large-tonnage injection molding firm in the Southeast since 1995, TPI continues to invest in the equipment and people needed to exceed customer expectations for quality, delivery, and price.
- The company is currently in the second year of fulfilling its five-year strategic plan to invest in and to refresh its fleet of injection molding equipment across all four facilities by the close of 2026.
- Thomson Plastics is one of only a handful of companies that can utilize as much as 4,400 tons to turn plastic pellets into parts.
- TPI has capabilities up to 4400 tons of clamping force and has numerous machines at multiple locations to complete this.
- The company utilizes robotics to give time off and keep production efficient.
