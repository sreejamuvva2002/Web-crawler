# Truck Hero Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-05-20

**Source:** [Truck Hero Set to Make Strategic Acquisition of Lund International | Modern Jeeping News & Education](https://modernjeeper.com/truck-hero-set-to-make-strategic-acquisition-of-lund-international/)  ·  `page_045607`

## Facts

- Truck Hero Inc. is set to acquire Lund International Holding Co., a portfolio company of Highlander Partners.
- The terms of the deal have not been disclosed.
- Headquartered in Ann Arbor, Michigan, Truck Hero, Inc. is a fast-growing designer and manufacturer of accessories for pickup trucks with some movement into the Jeep market as well.
- Truck Hero provides pickup truck owners a wide variety of truck bed covers and accessory options including truck caps and bed liners.
- Existing brands include: Advantage, A.R.E., BACKRACK, BAK, BedRug, Extang, Husky Liners, N-FAB, Omix-ADA, Retrax, Rugged Liner, Rugged Ridge, Superlift Suspension, TruXedo, Undercover and online retailer RealTruck.
- This new pending transaction marks Truck Hero’s 15th acquisition since forming in 2007.
- The company stated the acquisition is well-aligned with its growth strategy of acquiring businesses within the vehicle accessory market that further diversify its product portfolio into attractive, complementary, high growth segments.
- Buford, Georgia-based Lund was founded in 1965 in Anoka, Minnesota with the mission of leading the industry in meeting customer needs for innovative automotive accessories.
- Lund’s accessories span 30 product categories from brands that include AMP Research, AVS, Belmor, Bushwacker, LUND, Rampage Products, RoadWorks Manufacturing, Roll-N-Lock, Stampede Automotive Accessories and TonnoPro.
- Lund is led by president and CEO Mitch Fogle.
- Bill Reminder, president and CEO of Truck Hero, stated: This is an opportune time to bring Truck Hero and Lund together and that the Lund team has built an impressive collection of great companies which will be a perfect match to Truck Hero.
- The acquisition provides a complementary product portfolio that further diversifies Truck Hero’s product category mix and helps increase its exposure to a larger market of vehicles.
- Mitch Fogle stated: We believe the merger of these two great companies is a winning combination and together we manufacture and commercialize market leading brands that encompass the broadest product offering of vehicle accessories.
- Joe Scharfenberger, managing director at CCMP Capital, stated: The highly talented Lund and Highlander teams have built a very attractive, profitable and scalable portfolio of functional automotive accessories brands.
- Jeff L. Hull, President and CEO of Highlander Partners added that combining Lund into Truck Hero was an outstanding opportunity to further enhance an already successful investment and that Highlander will be a significant shareholder in the combined company.
