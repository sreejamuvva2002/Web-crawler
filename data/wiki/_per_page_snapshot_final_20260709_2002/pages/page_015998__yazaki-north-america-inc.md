# Yazaki North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2002-06-17

**Source:** [Yazaki North America, Inc. Launches New Online Connector Search
    Tool](https://www.theautochannel.com/news/2002/06/17/142138.html)  ·  `page_015998`

## Facts

- Yazaki North America, Inc. launched Component Central, a new online connector search engine that replaces the Connector Connection search capability on its website.
- David Conrad, vice president of Information Technology & Global e-Business at Yazaki North America, stated the tool addresses customer needs and lays the foundation for increased e-commerce opportunities in the future.
- Rich Tajer, director of Component Business Unit at Yazaki North America, highlighted the tool's additional functionality and ease of use for connector and terminal customers.
- Component Central allows customers to pull component specifications, dimensions, and application data by inputting whole or partial part numbers and product type.
- Future versions of Component Central will include sample and quote requests, customer design options, and online ordering.
- Yazaki Corporation is a global leader in the research and development of vehicle power and data solutions, as well as advanced electronic technologies for vehicle applications.
- Yazaki produces electrical distribution systems, fiber optics, advanced networking products, instrumentation, junction blocks, connector systems, and advanced electronics.
- Worldwide, Yazaki employs over 115,000 people in 31 countries.
- The company's North American headquarters and research center campus is located in Canton, Michigan.
