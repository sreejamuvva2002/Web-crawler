# Asama Coldwater Manufacturing

## Facts

- Asama Coldwater Manufacturing (ACM) was founded in Coldwater, MI in January 1996 as a joint venture between Asama Giken and Honda Motor Company.
- ACM was established to manufacturer automotive components for Honda passenger vehicles.
- ACM's manufacturing capabilities include a foundry, high capacity machining facility, Geomet® paint lines, and extensive research and development technologies.
- In March 2013, ACM acquired a second foundry located in Warrenton, GA.
- In 2015, a state of the art machining and paint facility was constructed at this site.
- The secondary location allows ACM to better meet the delivery needs of customers in the southern United States.
- Asama Giken is the parent company, established in Japan in 1973.
- Asama Indonesian Manufacturing (AIM) is a sister company located in Indonesia.
