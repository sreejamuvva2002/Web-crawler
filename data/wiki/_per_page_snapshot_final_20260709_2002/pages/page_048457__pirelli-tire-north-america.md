# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-05-05

**Source:** [Pirelli Reopens Georgia Plant - Tire Review Magazine](https://www.tirereview.com/pirelli-reopens-georgia-plant/)  ·  `page_048457`

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America reopened its plant in Rome, Georgia this week.
- The Rome, Georgia plant is currently running at 60% capacity.
- The plant will gradually ramp up production to meet both OE and replacement demand.
- The Rome, Georgia plant manufactures specialty tires.
- Pirelli has sanitized the Rome, Georgia plant.
- Pirelli is following local and federal safety protocols at the Rome, Georgia facility.
- Pirelli is implementing procedures learned from the opening of its plants in China and in Europe at the Rome, Georgia location.
- Pirelli continues to monitor the situation and will implement additional measures as it evolves.
