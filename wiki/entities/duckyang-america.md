# Duckyang America

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** LaGrange, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Duckyang America, an affiliate of a Korean automotive parts manufacturer and importer that supplies battery modules and related components, leased a newly built 270,144-square-foot distribution center at 89 Pegasus Parkway in LaGrange, Georgia. The facility is part of the 134-acre Lafayette Logistics Park industrial campus and is expected to boost the electric vehicle supply chain in western Georgia.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The lease provides a large distribution hub for battery modules and automotive components, adding critical momentum to the EV supply-chain ecosystem in West Georgia.
