# Textron Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2017-03-06

**Source:** [Textron Inc. Announces Acquisition of Arctic Cat Inc., a Leading Company in the Recreational Vehicle Industry - ATVILLUSTRATED](https://atvillustrated.com/textron-inc-announces-acquisition-of-arctic-cat-inc-a-leading-company-in-the-recreational-vehicle-industry/)  ·  `page_051628`

## Facts

- Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT) announced today that it has completed the acquisition of Arctic Cat Inc. by means of a short-form merger under Minnesota law.
- As a result, Arctic Cat has become an indirect wholly owned subsidiary of Textron.
- Going forward, Arctic Cat will operate as a subsidiary of Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. is based in Augusta, Ga.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles designs and manufactures vehicles and equipment for a range of uses and industries.
- The company's product portfolio includes E-Z-GO golf cars and personal transportation vehicles.
- The company's product portfolio includes Cushman commercial utility vehicles.
- The company's product portfolio includes Textron Off Road side-by-sides.
- The company's product portfolio includes Dixie Chopper zero-turn mowers.
- The company's product portfolio includes Jacobsen professional turf-care equipment.
- The company's product portfolio includes TUG, Douglas, Premier and Safeaero ground support equipment.
- The acquisition brings the Arctic Cat brand lineup of snowmobiles, side-by-sides and ATVs into the Textron Specialized Vehicles product family.
- Textron president and CEO Scott Donnelly stated that Arctic Cat is an ideal fit with their growing range of off-road recreational vehicles.
- The addition of Arctic Cat instantly gives Textron a deeper product line for customers, greater potential for innovation, and introduces new sales opportunities for the combined worldwide dealer network.
- Arctic Cat’s operations will remain in Minnesota following the acquisition.
- The business will gain valuable new capabilities through its integration with Textron Specialized Vehicles and the global resources of Textron Inc.
- Textron Inc. is a $13.8 billion multi-industry business with operations in more than 25 countries.
- The Arctic Cat brand designs, engineers, manufactures and markets all-terrain vehicles (ATVs), side-by-sides and snowmobiles.
- Arctic Cat also markets related parts, garments and accessories under the Arctic Cat and Motorfist brand names.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles markets products under the E-Z-GO, Cushman, Arctic Cat, Textron Off Road, Jacobsen, Dixie Chopper, Ransomes, TUG, Douglas, Premier and Safeaero brands.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles vehicles are found in environments ranging from golf courses to factories, airports to planned communities, and theme parks to hunting preserves.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company that leverages its global network of aircraft, defense, industrial and finance businesses to provide customers with innovative solutions and services.
- Textron is known around the world for brands such as Bell Helicopter, Cessna, Beechcraft, Hawker, Jacobsen, Kautex, Lycoming, E-Z-GO, Greenlee, Textron Off Road, Textron Systems, and TRU Simulation + Training.
