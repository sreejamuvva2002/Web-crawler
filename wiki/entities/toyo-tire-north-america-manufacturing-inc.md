# Toyo Tire North America Manufacturing Inc.

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** White, Georgia, Bartow County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Toyo Tire North America Manufacturing Inc., a tire manufacturer operating a large facility in White, Georgia, was honored as the first-ever winner of the Georgia Automotive Supplier of the Year Award for its growth and contributions to the state's automotive ecosystem.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Supplier of tires used on electric vehicles, supporting Georgia's EV market.

## Key facts

- Toyo Tire North America Manufacturing Inc. was the first-ever winner of Georgia's Automotive Supplier of the Year Award.
- The White, GA facility more than doubled in size since its groundbreaking in 2004.
- Employee count grew from 81 to 1,180 as the facility expanded.
- Fourth expansion will bring manufacturing space to 3 million square feet and increase annual production to 9 million tires.
- Expansion boosts local investment to over $1 billion.
- "Georgia is a key market for Toyo Tires and our work here is paramount to the overall success of our organization," said Jim Hawk, emphasizing the importance of Georgia's automotive ecosystem.
