# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-04-12

**Source:** [Bonnell Expanding Its TSLOTS Aluminum Framing System Operations - Light Metal Age Magazine](https://www.lightmetalage.com/news/industry-news/joining-fabrication/bonnell-expanding-its-tslots-aluminum-framing-system-operations/)  ·  `page_053077`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation.
- Bonnell Aluminum is expanding its TSLOTS line of aluminum structural framing systems into the Midwest region of the U.S. with a new location and increased storage capacity.
- Steve Silvester serves as the sales manager for TSLOTS.
- Customers in the Midwest and East requested a location closer to them due to TSLOTS's growth.
- Shipping materials from the Midwest will provide customers with competitive costs and lead-time benefits.
- TSLOTS provides project design assistance, fabrication, custom packaging, and full assembly of aluminum framing systems.
- The operation designs systems using custom T-slotted aluminum extrusions and connecting hardware to create light, durable, attractive, strong, versatile, and functional structures.
- TSLOTS currently operates manufacturing and distribution from its site in Clearfield, Utah.
- Bonnell Aluminum leased a new facility to operate in conjunction with its existing Niles, Michigan, aluminum extrusion manufacturing location.
- The new facility will provide 12,550 sq ft of warehouse space for TSLOTS inventory.
- The additional storage capacity aims to improve customer service levels for the Eastern and Midwestern United States, including reduced shipping costs and shorter lead-times.
- TSLOTS is owned and operated by Bonnell Aluminum, a leading aluminum extruder.
- Bonnell Aluminum supplies finished aluminum extrusions to the building and construction market.
- Bonnell Aluminum supplies finished aluminum extrusions to the automotive and transportation market.
- Bonnell Aluminum supplies finished aluminum extrusions to the consumer durables market.
- Bonnell Aluminum supplies finished aluminum extrusions to the machinery and equipment market.
- Bonnell Aluminum supplies finished aluminum extrusions to the electrical and distribution market.
- Bonnell Aluminum operates manufacturing facilities in Niles, Michigan; Newnan, Georgia; Carthage, Tennessee; Elkhart, Indiana; and Clearfield, Utah.
- Silvester noted pride in the company's growth over the past two decades and stated that nationwide locations allow TSLOTS to service customers effectively.
