## Facts

- The facility is located in Cordele, GA, and operates in the Transportation Equipment sector under NAICS code 336212.
- The facility is operated under parent company Bcd Parent Inc.
- Across 5 years of records spanning 2019–2023, the site has disclosed 253 lbs in cumulative toxic releases across 4 distinct chemicals.
- Two of the reported chemicals (50% of the chemical profile) carry a carcinogenic classification.
- Air releases are the dominant pathway at 194 lbs (76.7% of total volume), followed by off-site transfers at 23.3%.
- Reported toxic release totals decreased by 100% from 61 lbs in 2019 to 0 lbs in 2023.
- Yearly reported toxic releases were 61 lbs in 2019, 60 lbs in 2020, 90 lbs in 2021, 42 lbs in 2022, and 0 lbs in 2023.
- The facility files annual Form R disclosures to the EPA Toxics Release Inventory under EPCRA Section 313.
- TRI release totals are self-reported estimates, not measured stack emissions, and treat every pound of reported chemical equally regardless of toxicity.
- Inclusion in the TRI database does not establish a health risk to nearby residents; it documents the annual reporting record filed with the EPA under federal community right-to-know requirements.
