# US AV Jobs Coalition

## Facts

- Experts have been researching autonomous vehicles (AVs) and the future of the workforce for over a decade.
- Research has found that AVs will create new jobs spanning many skill levels and areas of expertise, countering narratives that they will eliminate jobs.
- The leading automation companies were founded in the U.S., and maintaining global leadership will strengthen American workers’ ability to compete globally.
- The average trucker is nearly 50 years of age, and the industry is struggling to attract new workers.
- Automated trucking alone could increase GDP by more than $68 billion over 30 years.
- The AV industry has already introduced thousands of jobs into the American economy.
- The AV industry could create up to 450,000 more jobs over the next 15 years.
- Many new AV-related jobs will not require a 4-year college degree and will span multiple areas of expertise and interest.
- AVs will remove transportation barriers to employment for individuals with disabilities.
- Workforce changes from AV deployment will occur gradually over several decades rather than overnight, minimizing impacts on individual careers.
- "The true value of automation lies in its ability to enhance human capabilities, not replace them."
