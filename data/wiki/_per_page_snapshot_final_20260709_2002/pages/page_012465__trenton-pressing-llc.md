# Trenton Pressing LLC

## Facts

- Trenton Pressing produces a wide range of products through high-quality presses, world-class assembly, and other manufacturing technologies.
- The company's team of engineers can help designs come to life or assist in making products more efficient, stronger, and sustainable.
- They stamp out metal components using 7 automatic presses ranging from 200 to 660T.
- Their team operates 4 hand presses ranging from 110 to 250T to produce high-quality products.
- They have more than 20 fully- and semi-automated weld cells for metal fastening.
- They employ a skilled team for manual welding processes.
- Precision coating is often the next step in the process following pressing, depending on specifications.
- They partner with multiple nearby finishing providers to guarantee the best application and cost for products.
- After coating, their expert team provides assembly services for many of their products.
- Engineering services include manufacturing engineering, design for manufacturability, product design assistance, FEA analysis, die design, robotic weld and assembly cell design, check fixture design, process poke yoke and camera inspection, and a full suite of CAD packages.
- They assemble a wide range of different components.
- Their assembly team conducts automated inspections to ensure parts are fully functional.
- They are experts in metal fastening including GMAW welding (robotic and manual), resistance welding (spot and projection), assembling multi-component mechanisms, fastener torquing, DC tools, latch assemblies, riveting, tox joints, clinch fasteners, tapes, felts and grease, and automated inspection.
- Quality services include Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM) Layout, Quality Inspection Lab, Destruct Lab with Cut and Etch, Safe Launch, and End-of-Line (EOL) Checking.
- Their quality management systems are certified to meet internationally recognized standards.
- Trenton Pressing LLC has undergone a TISAX assessment conducted by an accredited audit provider, with results available on the ENX portal for authorized partners.
- Their Quality Management System is certified as being in conformity with IATF 16949 by Intertek.
- The IATF 16949 certification demonstrates a commitment to continual improvement, defect prevention, and reduction of variation and waste in the automotive supply chain.
- This certification ensures consistent product quality, reliable delivery performance, commitment to customer satisfaction, and alignment with global industry standards.
