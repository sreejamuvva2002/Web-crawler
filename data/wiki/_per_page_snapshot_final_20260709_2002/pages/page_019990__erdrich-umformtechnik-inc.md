# Erdrich Umformtechnik, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Erdrich Umformtechnik is one of the world’s leading manufacturers of complex metal parts and subassemblies for the automotive industry.
- Erdrich contracted with Evans to design-build their first U.S. based production facility in Dublin, Georgia.
- The 123,000 sf building is composed of a structural steel frame and concrete tilt-up walls wrapped in insulated metal panels with TPO roofing and ESFR sprinkler systems.
- The structure includes 95’ and 120’ spans with four 15’ deep press pits and 500 linear feet of conveyor supply trenches.
- Inside the fully air-conditioned facility is approximately 100,000 sf production and assembly space featuring two mezzanines.
- The remaining 23,000 sf is used for office and administrative purposes.
- Nine overhead cranes were installed throughout the manufacturing areas to aid in metal forming and assembly operations.
- Scope of work included design-build services, shell building construction with finishes, and a comprehensive site work package.
