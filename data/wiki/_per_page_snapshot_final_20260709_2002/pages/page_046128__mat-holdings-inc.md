## Facts

- MAT Foundry Group designs and manufactures OE/OEM/OES commercial cast iron braking products.
- MAT Foundry Group offers a comprehensive line of high-quality aftermarket brake discs, drums, and hubs in the US, Mexico, Central and South America, and Europe.
- MAT Foundry Group develops rotor metallurgy and design based on extensive product testing and knowledge of OE performance standards.
- The casting process produces brake discs and drums with equal wall thickness to provide equal brake stress and thermal distribution, reducing noise, vibration, and harshness.
- MAT Friction Group offers aftermarket customers a complete range of air disc pads and linings with comprehensive coverage of commercial and heavy-duty vehicles in Europe and North America.
- MAT Friction Group provides OE quality copper-free products designed to meet European standards including KBA for CV linings and ECE 90 for pads.
- MAT Friction Group maintains strategically located distribution, applications engineering, and sales support.
- MAT Friction Group operates North American relining centers that provide a coast-to-coast presence.
- Customers benefit from MAT's expertise in OE friction development, broad experience in brake design, and extensive dynamometer and vehicle testing performed each year.
- MAT Holdings' Ride Control LLC offers shock absorbers to aftermarket retailers under the Gabriel brand in the US and Canada, and the GRC brand to Mexico.
- Gabriel is a leading shock absorber brand differentiated by product quality, with all piston rods chrome plated for durability, OEM ride and handling, and comprehensive coverage.
- Gabriel developed its Fleetline and GasSLX lines specifically for commercial and HD applications, combining reliability, durability, and adjustable comfort for fleet owners.
