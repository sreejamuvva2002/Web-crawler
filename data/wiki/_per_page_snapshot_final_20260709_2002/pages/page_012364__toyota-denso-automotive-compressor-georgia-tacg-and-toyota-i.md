# Toyota-Denso Automotive Compressor Georgia (TACG) and Toyota Industries Compressor Parts America (TICA)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Toyota Industries TICA-TACG Pendergrass, Ga Auto Manufacturing](https://toyotacompressor.com/home-dev/)  ·  `page_012364`

## Facts

- TACG and TICA are members of the Toyota Industries Group.
- TACG and TICA manufacture and supply vehicle air conditioning compressors for more than half of the North American auto industry.
- TACG and TICA assemble inverters that supply electronics to power plug-in devices used in vehicles.
- The company distributes its energy-efficient products throughout North America.
- The manufacturing campus is situated on 150 acres off Valentine Industrial Parkway in Pendergrass, Ga.
- TACG and TICA are pioneers in automotive air compressor technology, with their compressors having become the industry standard.
- Their compressors are used in over half of all cars, trucks, and vans manufactured in North America.
- Well-known automotive brands relying on TACG and TICA compressors include Ford, Honda, BMW, Lexus, Subaru, Fiat-Chrysler, and Toyota Motor vehicles.
- The facilities are located in Northeast Georgia, 60 miles north of Atlanta.
- The Toyota Industries Group operates state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities with cutting-edge technology that adheres to Toyota manufacturing’s high standards of quality and efficiency.
- TACG was established in 2004 and specializes in the manufacturing of automobile air conditioning compressors.
- TICA was built adjacent to TACG in 2012 to meet increasing demand, expanding production capacity by manufacturing component parts used in TACG’s AC compressors.
- In November 2020, TACG and TICA expanded their production capabilities to include DC-AC Inverters.
- The DC-AC inverters enable the use of electronic devices such as cellphones, laptops, job-site tools, and small appliances in vehicles.
- Environmental responsibility is at the core of TACG and TICA’s values, with a dedication to finding innovative solutions to environmental challenges.
- Through research and development, TACG and TICA are providing eco-friendly automotive solutions for the automotive manufacturing industry.
- Hiroto Ikeno serves as the President of TACG & TICA in Georgia.
- TACG and TICA are among the leading makers of vehicle air compressors and DC-AC Inverters for the North American market.
- The company rewards its team with the area’s top wages and company benefits, plus special incentives for creativity, ingenuity, and efficiency in its plants.
- Associates participate in special celebrations, get-togethers at work, volunteering outside the workplace, and giving back to the local community.
