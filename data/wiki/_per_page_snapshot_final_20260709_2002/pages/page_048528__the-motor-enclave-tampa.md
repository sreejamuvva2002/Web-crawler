# The Motor Enclave Tampa

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-01-08

**Source:** [The Motor Enclave Tampa Announces Partnership With Pirelli Tire North ... 2024-01-08](https://www.pr-inside.com/the-motor-enclave-tampa-announces-partnership-with-pirelli-tire-north-r4974015.htm)  ·  `page_048528`

## Facts

- The Motor Enclave announced a sponsorship agreement with Pirelli Tire North America to become the official tire partner of the 200-acre experiential motorsports venue.
- The venue is located at the corner of I-4 and I-75, less than 15 minutes from downtown Tampa, Florida.
- The pit lane will be branded "The Pirelli Pit Lane" with trademark yellow and red branding throughout key development locations.
- All events on the 1.6-mile Hermann Tilke-designed performance circuit will begin and end in the newly named Pirelli Pit Lane.
- A "Pirelli Podium" architectural element featuring authentic Pirelli Formula 1 tires will be installed for guest photography.
- Brad Oleshansky, Founder and CEO of The Motor Enclave, stated that Pirelli's racing heritage and Formula 1 partnership align with the venue's mission to deliver unique experiences to automotive enthusiasts.
- Andre Oosthuizen, Chief Marketing Officer of Pirelli Tire North America, noted that Pirelli has tracked the development of The Motor Enclave Tampa since its initial announcement in 2019.
- Oosthuizen cited the success of Oleshansky's previous Michigan project, M1 Concourse, as a key factor in selecting this venue to demonstrate Pirelli's full product line across various driving surfaces.
- The Motor Enclave development features a 1.6-mile performance circuit, a 2-acre vehicle dynamics pad, and a 100-acre off-road experience with purpose-built trails.
- The site includes a 37,000-square-foot corporate event center and over 300 privately owned luxury garages.
- Pirelli Tire North America operates its Rome, Georgia headquarters, which houses a Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) facility and a research and development center.
- PTNA also operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant in Silao, Mexico, along with sales and marketing offices in New York City, Los Angeles, Detroit, Montreal, and Mexico City.
- Pirelli manufactures, distributes, and markets original equipment and replacement tires for domestic and export car and motorcycle applications.
- The company's product range includes innovative tires for cars, motorcycles, and bicycles developed through partnerships with prestigious manufacturers to meet specific consumer needs.
- Founded in Milan in 1872, Pirelli is a global brand recognized for cutting-edge technology, high-end production excellence, and Italian-rooted innovation.
