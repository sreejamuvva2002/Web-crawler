# Solvay-Orbia Joint Venture

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-02

## Facts

- Solvay and Orbia are forming a joint venture in Georgia.
- The joint venture will supply PVDF resin for electric vehicles.
- The new PVDF resin plant is located in Augusta, Ga.
- Electric vehicle market growth is driving Solvay to build the major new plant.
- The joint venture will provide materials for more than 5 million electric vehicle batteries.
- The announcement was published on November 02, 2023.
