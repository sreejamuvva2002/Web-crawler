## Facts

- GSC Steel Stamping, LLC is a minority- and family-owned automotive parts manufacturer.
- GSC Steel Stamping has acquired Dixien, LLC's assets.
- The company continues its 43-year history of automotive parts stamping.
- The company is led by CEO Vincent Henderson.
- Vincent Henderson is known for turning around award-winning operations.
- GSC Steel Stamping is committed to excellence and investing in technology.
- GSC Steel Stamping maintains a highly experienced staff.
- The company operates a 200,000 sq. ft. facility in Lake City, GA.
- This facility is the largest minority-owned automotive stamping plant globally.
- The press release announcing these developments was published on January 30, 2024.
