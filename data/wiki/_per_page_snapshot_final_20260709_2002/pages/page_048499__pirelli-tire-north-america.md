# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2008-10-24

**Source:** [Pirelli Congratulates Dunlop On Winning AMA Superbike Spec Tire Bid - Roadracing World Magazine | Motorcycle Riding, Racing & Tech News](https://www.roadracingworld.com/news/pirelli-congratulates-dunlop-on-winning-ama-superbike-spec-tire-bid/)  ·  `page_048499`

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America specializes in the manufacture and marketing of high-performance tires for passenger vehicles, motorcycles and motorsports.
- Pirelli operates a highly advanced Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) in Rome, Georgia.
- The Rome, Georgia facility is used to better serve the North American OEM and Replacement markets.
- Scott Griffin serves as VP of Pirelli Motorcycle Tire North America.
- Scott Griffin stated that the AMA invited bids from all tire companies for its forthcoming road-racing series and Dunlop was chosen, adding that Pirelli respects the AMA’s transparent bidding process and congratulates Dunlop on the technical and logistical challenge ahead.
- Dunlop was selected by the AMA to supply spec tires for its upcoming road-racing series.
- Pirelli will apply assets from its ongoing development of high-performance tires in World Superbike and World Supersport to other avenues in the North American market.
