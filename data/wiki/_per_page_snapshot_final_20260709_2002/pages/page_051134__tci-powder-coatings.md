## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is located in Americus, GA.
- Due to significant increases in raw material, labor and freight costs, TCI Powder Coatings has implemented a price increase across all business segments.
- The price increase takes effect as of Jan. 1, 2018, or when contracts allow.
- TCI continues to look for ways to respond to raw material cost inflation through more efficient operations.
- Cost-control measures have not sufficiently offset rapidly rising costs in the company’s total supply chain and operation.
