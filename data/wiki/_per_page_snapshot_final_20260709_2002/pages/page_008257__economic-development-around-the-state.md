# Economic Development Around the State

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-30

**Source:** [Economic Development Around the State - Georgia Trend Magazine](https://www.georgiatrend.com/2026/06/30/georgia-economics-cobb-county-macon-manufacturing/)  ·  `page_008257`

## Facts

- Authority Brands is establishing its new headquarters, dubbed Franchisee Success Center, in Cobb County.
- The $13 million investment by Authority Brands will create 390 new jobs over the next several years.
- Authority Brands is a home services franchisor with 15 brands spanning essential residential services from electrical to lawn care, served by more than 1,000 franchise owners.
- Precision metal fabrication and manufacturing company Unified Legacy is expanding in Bibb County.
- The $125 million investment by Unified Legacy will create 500 new jobs.
- Unified Legacy makes components for the defense and aerospace industries, data centers and industrial markets.
- HK USA is investing $13 million to expand its Columbus-Muscogee County facility, adding 35 jobs.
- HK USA makes firearms for the military, law enforcement and civilian markets.
- Parent company Heckler & Koch first established operations in Georgia in 2005 and currently supports more than 100 jobs in Columbus.
- French company Socomec recently celebrated the grand opening of its Gwinnett County manufacturing facility, which makes electrical components.
- The $10 million project by Socomec will eventually employ 300 people.
- ArcelorMittal Building Solutions is establishing its North American headquarters and a new manufacturing facility in Macon-Bibb County.
- The $57 million investment by ArcelorMittal Building Solutions will create up to 70 new jobs initially.
- ArcelorMittal Building Solutions plans for an additional $50 million investment and 70 more jobs over the next several years.
- ArcelorMittal Building Solutions designs and manufactures building envelope systems used to insulate and protect warehouses, data centers and more.
- The ArcelorMittal Building Solutions facility plans to open in the second half of 2027.
- Japanese water-heater manufacturer Rinnai is expanding in Spalding County, opening its third facility in Georgia.
- The 306,000-square-foot Rinnai plant will make and distribute commercial products serving large-scale buildings like hotels and apartments.
- The Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America site in Ellabell is celebrating the addition of its first hybrid vehicle to its manufacturing lineup.
- Hyundai Motor Group began making the Kia Sportage Turbo-Hybrid in June.
