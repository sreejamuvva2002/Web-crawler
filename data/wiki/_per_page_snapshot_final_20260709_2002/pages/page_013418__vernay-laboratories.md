# Vernay Laboratories

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-01-23

## Facts

- Vernay Laboratories celebrated the successful grand opening of its new Kennesaw facility on January 16, 2025.
- The grand opening ceremony included a ribbon-cutting ceremony, facility tours, and insightful presentations by Vernay’s leadership team.
- More than 50 local officials, industry partners, and employees gathered to celebrate the event.
- Andy Woodward, Vice President of Sales & Marketing at Vernay, stated that hosting valued partners highlighted strong relationships and the importance of the facility to Vernay’s future and the industries it serves.
- The Kennesaw facility represents a major investment in Vernay’s future, strengthening its ability to serve global customers while fostering collaboration, innovation, and excellence.
- Andy Woodward invited clients to challenge the company with complex projects, noting the new Kennesaw facility is equipped to take ideas from concept to launch with unmatched precision and efficiency.
- Vernay Laboratories has been a technology leader in the design, development, and manufacture of customized flow-control components since its founding in 1946.
- The company formulates its own compounds to address each specific application.
- Vernay’s products play a vital role in millions of medical devices, automobiles, and consumer products.
- The privately held firm produces more than 1 billion components per year.
- Vernay draws from a library of more than 33,000 elastomeric materials and compound formulations.
- The company operates six plants around the world, located in the U.S., the Netherlands, Italy, and China.
