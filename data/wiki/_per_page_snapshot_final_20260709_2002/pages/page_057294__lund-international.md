# Lund International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2007-01-05

**Source:** [Lund International Announces Restructuring](https://www.theautochannel.com/news/2007/01/05/033153.html)  ·  `page_057294`

## Facts

- Lund International is announcing further reorganization and restructuring initiatives.
- The Company consolidated its Duluth, GA Corporate offices, its Lund Accessories Division in Lawrenceville, GA, and its warehousing function at the Ventshade Accessories Division into a new Corporate Center in Suwanee, Georgia.
- The consolidation of the corporate offices and divisions into the Suwanee, Georgia Corporate Center was completed in August 2006.
- The restructuring initiatives include the closure of two facilities and a new business structure to reduce fixed costs, increase management effectiveness, and improve customer service.
- The Company's Autotron Accessories Division in Longmont, CO is expected to close by May 31, 2007.
- Approximately 75 employees work at the Longmont facility producing plastic accessories for light truck and automotive vehicles.
- Production from the closing Longmont facility is being transferred to the Company's Ventshade Accessories Division in Lawrenceville, GA.
- Customers will benefit from additional manufacturing processes, well established and proven product quality systems, and timely response to customer needs at the Lawrenceville location.
- The Company's offices in Anoka, MN will also close by the third quarter of 2007.
- Approximately 35 employees are engaged in administrative support functions at the Anoka facility.
- The majority of the Anoka positions will be relocated to the Company's Corporate Center in Suwanee, GA.
- Lund International has reorganized into three strategic business units for improved management effectiveness.
- The largest business unit markets Light Truck and Automotive Accessories under brands Lund, AVS, and Nifty through warehouse distributors, specialty retail chains, and mail order distributors.
- A second strategic business unit was established to market Light Truck and Automotive Accessories sold through Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) to improve the Company's position in this channel.
- The third strategic business unit markets Heavy Truck Accessories sold through OEMs and their aftermarket distribution centers.
- Management is being realigned into these three business units for improved focus on satisfying customer needs.
- Terry Cole, Vice President of Sales and Marketing for Light Truck and Automotive Accessories, has left the Company as part of the restructuring.
- Steve Treichel, Corporate Vice President responsible for Information Systems and the Corporate Distribution Center, has left the Company.
- Jerry Schomaker, previously Vice President and General Manager of the Ventshade Accessories Division, becomes Vice President of Product Supply and Engineering.
- Jerry Schomaker joined Lund International in 2000 as Vice President and General Manager of the Deflecta-Shield Accessories Division in Howe, IN.
- These changes are intended to contribute to the continuing viability of Lund International and ensure its capability of providing superior products and services to customers.
- Lund International designs, manufactures, and markets a wide variety of appearance products for the automotive aftermarket, including window vents, visors, hood shields, running boards, bed covers, spoilers, toolboxes, cargo management, and other accessories.
- Products are sold under the brand names AVS, Belmor, Deflecta-Shield, Lund, and Nifty.
- The company is headquartered in Suwanee, Georgia.
- The company has seven continuing locations with approximately 900 employees.
