# The Volvo Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Volvo to Open $38M Distribution Hub in Washington - Connect CRE](https://www.connectcre.com/stories/volvo-to-open-38m-distribution-hub-in-washington/)  ·  `page_014327`

## Facts

- The Volvo Group will invest $37.7 million to open a new 115,000-square-foot, state-of-the-art Regional Distribution Center (RDC) in Tacoma, Washington.
- The facility is currently undergoing renovations and is expected to open in the first quarter of 2027.
- The new facility will serve as the primary RDC for approximately 240 dealers.
- Centralizing order distribution at the new facility is expected to help the carmaker significantly reduce its CO2 emissions.
- Peter Andell, vice president of service market logistics for Volvo Group North America, stated that the facility represents a significant expansion to the Volvo Group’s parts distribution capabilities, allowing them to reduce transportation lead times and improve reliability, especially for dealers in more remote areas of Western Canada.
- The new facility is located at 7323 Valley Ave. E. and will support several brands.
- The property is situated near the Port of Tacoma and is approximately 30 miles from the Port of Seattle.
