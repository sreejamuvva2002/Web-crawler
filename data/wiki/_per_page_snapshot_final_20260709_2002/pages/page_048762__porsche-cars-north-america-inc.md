## Facts

- Ayesha Coker serves as Vice President of Marketing for Porsche Cars North America, Inc.
- Coker has led marketing for the sports car maker's largest market since 2022.
- Timo Resch, President and CEO of Porsche Cars North America, stated that Coker's work on campaigns for new models such as the Macan and Taycan, along with large-scale events like Rennsport Reunion, has elevated marketing at Porsche.
- Coker is a graduate of the University of Maryland Eastern Shore and the Fashion Institute of Technology.
- Coker began her career with PCNA in 2010 as an experiential brand event manager responsible for planning, developing, and managing major event marketing programs.
- During her tenure at PCNA, she held various marketing roles including manager and senior manager of events and sponsorships, and director of experiential marketing.
- Coker joined the Executive Committee at PCNA in January 2022 as Vice-President of Marketing, making her the first African American and first woman of color to join the leadership team.
- The 2025 class of Automotive News' 100 Leading Women in the North American Auto Industry was selected through a months-long nomination and judging process that attracted hundreds of entries from the United States, Canada, and Mexico.
- Mary Beth Vander Schaaf, Automotive News senior director of editorial operations, noted that the selection committee made many difficult decisions to identify talented executives at the forefront of thousands of successful women in the auto industry.
- Automotive News has published the list of the industry's top female executives every five years since 2000.
