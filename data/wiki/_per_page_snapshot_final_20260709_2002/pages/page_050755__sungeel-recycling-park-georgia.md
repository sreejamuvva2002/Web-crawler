# SungEel Recycling Park Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [SungEel to open lithium-ion battery recycling facility in the US - Best Magazine](https://www.bestmag.co.uk/sungeel-to-open-lithium-ion-battery-recycling-facility-in-the-us/)  ·  `page_050755`

## Facts

- SungEel Recycling Park Georgia is a subsidiary of SungEel Hi Tech.
- SungEel Recycling Park Georgia will locate its first US lithium-ion battery recycling facility in Georgia.
- The subsidiary is part of the Korea-based global industry leader SungEel HiTech.
- The new facility will receive an investment of more than $37 million.
- The facility will recover metals including nickel, cobalt, and lithium.
- SungEel specialises in complete recycling technology using a 100% full circulatory system.
- The technology recycles end-of-life batteries and battery manufacturing scrap.
- The new facility will be located at the Hayestone Brady Business Park.
- Hayestone Brady Business Park is a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) Certified site.
- The facility is located in Toccoa.
- The GRAD Program is a proactive effort by Georgia’s economic development community to develop a portfolio of available sites ready for fast-track development.
- Operations are expected to begin in early 2024.
- Georgia’s location, extensive infrastructure, skilled workforce, and business-friendly climate have made it an attractive location for developing industries focused on creating a sustainable future.
- Georgia is cultivating a vertically integrated supply chain that will help companies increase efficiencies by reducing the reliance on imported materials.
- SungEel HiTech operates recycling facilities in South Korea, China, Malaysia, India, and Hungary.
- SungEel HiTech has new facilities underway in Poland and Germany.
