# Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America, a division of Panasonic Corporation of North America, collaborates with Totem USA to bring Totem’s first UL-certified eBike “Zen Rider” to the U.S. market.
- In 2019, Panasonic established the industry’s first e-Bike safety standard development organization and has led safety for science companies.
- The Totem USA eBike “Zen Rider” is powered by Panasonic systems, providing consumers an affordable, family-friendly option to meet rising eBike market demands.
- NPD research indicates a 240% growth rate for electric bicycles within the 12 months leading up to July 12, 2021.
- Derek Arciniaga, senior manager of business development at Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America, announced Totem USA’s first eBike with a UL certified Panasonic system to expand mobility transformation in the US.
- The Zen Rider features a low step-through mounting, making its design and motor/battery system especially suited for urban areas.
- Panasonic’s second UL 2849 motor & battery system includes a 2-Axle motor + battery eBike system.
- The system provides 75 Nm of max torque and a 404 Wh battery.
- The system offers pedal assistance up to 15mph.
- The collaboration between Totem USA and Panasonic is ready to design, develop, and produce custom eBikes for companies interested in UL certified eBikes.
- The latest Panasonic drive system is suitable for two-wheel and three-wheel city tour or cargo eBike variants.
