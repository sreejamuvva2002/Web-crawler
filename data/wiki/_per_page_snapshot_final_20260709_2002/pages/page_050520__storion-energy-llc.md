# Storion Energy, LLC

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-07

**Source:** [Largo and Stryten Energy to Form Storion Energy for the Manufacture of Vanadium Electrolyte and Battery Components for Long Duration Energy Storage - Green Stock News](https://greenstocknews.com/news/nasdaq/lgo/largo-and-stryten-energy-to-form-storion-energy-for-the-manufacture-of-vanadium-electrolyte-and-battery-components-for-long-duration-energy-storage)  ·  `page_050520`

## Facts

- Largo Inc. signed definitive agreements with Stryten Critical E-Storage, LLC (an affiliate of Stryten Energy LLC) to establish Storion Energy, LLC as a joint venture.
- The transaction establishing Storion Energy is subject to customary closing conditions.
- Francesco D’Alessio serves as Chief Commercial Officer of Largo and President of LCE.
- The partnership aims to maximize the value of Largo’s vanadium products and utilize Storion’s patented purification process to accelerate vanadium electrolyte manufacture.
- Accelerated vanadium electrolyte manufacturing is expected to increase demand for vanadium from Largo Physical Vanadium Corp. (LPV) and the broader vanadium sector.
- Storion Energy intends to provide domestically sourced, price-competitive electrolyte for vanadium redox flow batteries (VRFB) used in long-duration energy storage (LDES).
- Storion will operate locations in Wilmington, Massachusetts and Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Storion Energy is a joint venture between a Stryten Energy affiliate and Largo Clean Energy Corp., a subsidiary of Largo Inc.
- The JV aims to support scalable domestic electrolyte production to establish a fully integrated vertical supply chain for utility-scale VRFB LDES solutions.
- VRFB technology provides long-duration energy storage greater than four hours to ensure grid stability and facilitate increased utilization of renewables.
- Gallatin Capital LLC acted as financial advisor to Largo, and Linklaters LLP acted as legal counsel for the transaction.
- Stryten Energy is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia, and provides energy storage solutions across Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- Stryten Energy's technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- Largo Physical Vanadium Corp. (LPV) aims to provide direct exposure to physical vanadium, a metal essential for steel, aerospace, and energy storage industries.
- LPV's strategy includes owning and actively supplying vanadium to end users of vanadium flow batteries to advance renewable energy integration in long duration storage.
- Largo produces critical materials from its Maracás Menchen Mine in Brazil and is one of the world’s largest primary vanadium producers.
- Largo holds a 50% ownership stake in Storion Energy.
- Largo Inc. common shares trade on the Nasdaq Stock Market and Toronto Stock Exchange under the symbol LGO.
- The press release containing these details was published on December 19, 2024, via Business Wire and on July 7, 2026, via Green Stock News.
