## Facts

- Superior Essex announced a partnership with Delta Air Lines to purchase Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF).
- The partnership aims to reduce emissions from Superior Essex and its subsidiary brands' air travel over the next year.
- SAF reduces lifecycle carbon emissions of aviation fuel by up to 80% compared to conventional jet fuel.
- Austin Robinson, Sustainability Manager, stated they are proud to create this long-term commitment with Delta to reduce travel's environmental impact.
- The initiative aligns with Superior Essex's core belief that everyone plays a role in reducing their carbon footprint.
- Superior Essex operates out of Atlanta, GA (5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327).
