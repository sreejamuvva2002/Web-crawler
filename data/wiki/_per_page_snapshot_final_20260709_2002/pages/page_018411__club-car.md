# Club Car

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-23

**Source:** [Club Car Opens New Manufacturing Facility - Asian Golf Industry Federation](https://agif.asia/2024/04/club-car-opens-new-manufacturing-facility/)  ·  `page_018411`

## Facts

- Club Car celebrated the opening of its new Appling Manufacturing Facility in Augusta, Georgia, with tours and a ribbon cutting ceremony.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp and First Lady Marty Kemp toured the facility alongside state and local elected officials and area business leaders.
- Attendees learned about the Club Car Urban vehicle during the tour.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Club Car is bringing jobs into Georgia in the growing e-mobility sector and leading the way in compact utility EV manufacturing.
- Governor Kemp added that Club Car's growth and investment demonstrate efforts to make Georgia the number one state to do business.
- The Club Car Appling Manufacturing Facility comprises 105,000 square feet of flexible production and warehouse storage space.
- Club Car opened the facility to improve customer experience as demand for the Club Car Urban vehicle increases in the United States.
- Club Car has committed nearly US$8 million in capital investments into the facility over two years.
- The new facility operates a state-of-the-art production line capable of producing up to 20 vehicles per day.
- Club Car President and CEO Mark Wagner stated that their innovative vehicles impact commercial fleet electrification, mini-mobility, and last-mile delivery.
- Mark Wagner serves as an Executive Member of the Asian Golf Industry Federation.
- Club Car has been a Georgia-based company for more than 60 years.
- The company highlights quality, safety, and reliability in its products as a key differentiator.
