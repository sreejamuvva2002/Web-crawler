# Constellium N.V.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-11-24

**Source:** [Constellium to build greenfield manufacturing facility for automotive structures in Bartow County, Georgia, USA](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/constellium-to-build-greenfield-manufacturing-facility-for-automotive-structures-in-bartow-county-georgia-usa-300183663.html)  ·  `page_005294`

## Facts

- Constellium N.V. announced it will construct a new greenfield manufacturing facility in Bartow County, Georgia.
- The project responds to growing demand for automotive structures in North America.
- Constellium will invest $20 million in the facility.
- Developer Seefried Properties will invest an additional $12 million.
- The total project value is $32 million.
- Construction of the plant will begin in early 2016.
- Start of production is anticipated in 2017.
- Constellium expects to create approximately 150 high-tech manufacturing jobs at the location by 2019.
- Paul Warton, President of Constellium's Automotive Structures and Industry business unit, stated the new Georgia plant demonstrates a commitment to partnering with North American automakers and locating near their assembly plants.
- The expansion will allow Constellium to better serve automotive customers and respond to industry demand for aluminum structural parts to lighten vehicles, improve fuel economy, and reduce environmental impact.
- The planned facility will be 84,000 square feet.
- The facility may be expanded up to 220,000 square feet in the future to adapt to customer supply needs.
- Eric Krepps, Vice President and General Manager of Constellium Automotive Structures North America, highlighted Georgia's strong business climate and advanced technical education network for providing a skilled workforce.
- Constellium expressed gratitude for the welcome received from the State of Georgia, Bartow County, and various partners.
- Constellium is a global sector leader developing innovative aluminum products for aerospace, automotive, and packaging markets.
- Constellium generated €3.7 billion in revenue in 2014.
