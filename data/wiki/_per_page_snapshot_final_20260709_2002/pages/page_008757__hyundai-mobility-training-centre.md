# Hyundai Mobility Training Centre

**Type:** workforce_training  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hyundai's new EV training facility in Georgia: Boosting workforce for electric and hybrid vehicle production](https://www.automotivemanufacturingsolutions.com/editors-pick/hyundai-opens-georgias-first-ev-training-facility/2054662)  ·  `page_008757`

## Facts

- The facility spans 89,000 square feet.
- The facility is located in Bryan County, Georgia.
- The facility can train 824 workers simultaneously for electric and hybrid vehicle production.
- The facility uses simulation lines and high-voltage labs to prepare employees for Hyundai's advanced manufacturing processes.
- EV assembly demands navigating high-voltage systems, automated processes, and digital quality controls that differ from traditional combustion engine factories.
- The Hyundai Mobility Training Centre was unveiled on November 5th in Ellabell.
- It is Georgia's first purpose-built EV training facility.
- The centre is positioned adjacent to Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America in Bryan County.
- Trainees are prepared for roles at the Metaplant and its network of affiliate companies.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia's focus remains on bringing quality jobs to hardworking Georgians and connecting them to necessary skills amid record-breaking economic development statewide.
