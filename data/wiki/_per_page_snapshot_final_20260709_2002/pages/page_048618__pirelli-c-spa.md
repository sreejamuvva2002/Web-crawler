# Pirelli & C. SpA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [HOMBRE1 | HOMBRE Exclusive: PIRELLI Conquers the U.S. Market Led by CEO Pierluigi Dinelli](https://hombre1.com/hombre-exclusive-pirelli-prepares-to-conquer-the-us-market-led-by-ceo-pierluigi-dinelli/)  ·  `page_048618`

## Facts

- Pirelli & C. SpA is a multinational company and the world’s fifth-largest tire manufacturer.
- The company operates 19 manufacturing sites around the world.
- Pirelli maintains a network of around 10,000 distributors and retailers globally.
- Pierluigi Dinelli joined Pirelli Tire North America in January 2015 as head of the company’s businesses in the United States, Mexico, and Canada.
- The North American business includes three sales units for car tires, truck tires, and motorcycle tires.
- Pirelli operates two manufacturing facilities in North America: one located in Rome, Georgia, USA, and another in Silao, Guanajuato, Mexico.
- Dinelli previously served as Chief Marketing Officer of Gewiss SpA for nearly four years before joining Pirelli.
- Prior to that role, he was Chief Marketing Officer of high-end brakes manufacturer Brembo SpA and its subsidiaries.
- He also held the position of Vice President of Sales, Europe, for the housewares business unit of Bormioli Rocco.
- Dinelli began his career at Pirelli in 1999 in the sales and marketing department in Italy.
- He quickly advanced to key account manager for Europe, responsible for fleets, leasing companies, and rental companies.
- From 2004 to 2006, he served as Marketing Director for Pirelli Tire North America before moving to Romania as Vice President of Sales & Marketing for Southeast Europe.
- Pirelli is leveraging its manufacturing facility close to Atlanta to grow consistently in the U.S. market.
- The company aims to be closer to consumers by providing region-specific products rather than selling identical products globally.
- Many U.S. consumers are unaware that Pirelli tires can fit their vehicles, so local manufacturing helps increase brand appreciation.
- Pirelli recently opened a PZero store in Los Angeles as a showroom and shop concept targeting the prestige segment for luxury and sports cars.
- In the market where the PZero store operates, Pirelli holds close to 50% market share.
- The store provides customized tire fitting services with exact tires developed specifically for vehicles such as Lamborghini, Bentley, and Ferrari.
- Pirelli has been the exclusive tire supplier for the Formula 1 Championship for nine consecutive years.
- Pirelli is the unique tire supplier for Lamborghini for many years.
- The company’s future U.S. strategy involves penetrating both the original equipment business and the replacement business through separate distribution channels.
- All-Season tires account for 91% of the U.S. tire market because they can be used year-round, unlike the summer and winter tire split in other regions.
- Pirelli currently has 80% of its product range covered for the U.S. market and is developing additional products to complete the range.
- The company considers its customers its most important asset and maintains very important partnerships with them.
