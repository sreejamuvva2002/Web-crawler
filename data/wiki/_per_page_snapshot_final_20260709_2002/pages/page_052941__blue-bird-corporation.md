# Blue Bird Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Blue Bird Expands Its Electrification Ecosystem](https://www.centralcharts.com/en/14375-blue-bird-corp/news/3804665-blue-bird-expands-its-electrification-ecosystem)  ·  `page_052941`

## Facts

- Blue Bird Corporation (Nasdaq: BLBD) is the leader in electric and low-emission school buses.
- The company launched its Blue Bird Energy Services business to accelerate the electrification of the student transportation industry by providing turnkey charging infrastructure solutions for electric school bus fleets.
- Blue Bird Energy Services helps electric school bus fleet customers meet their charging infrastructure needs.
- The company offers various electric school bus charging options ranging from AC Level 2 to DC fast-chargers rated at 30, 60, and 120kW.
- Blue Bird has nearly 650 electric-powered, zero-emission school buses in operation today.
- The company builds a full range of electric school buses which can carry a maximum of 84 passengers for up to 120 miles on a single charge.
- Depending on the charging infrastructure, the buses take between three and eight hours to recharge fully.
- Blue Bird develops a complete ecosystem of electric products and services for school districts, bus fleet operators, and its dealer network across the United States.
- The company helps customers assess electric vehicle and charging infrastructure needs.
- The company helps customers identify funding sources including grants.
- The company helps customers understand vehicle and fleet performance through a world-class telematics solution.
- The company helps customers safely operate and maintain vehicles through driver and technician training.
- The company ensures bus performance through high-quality service over the life of the vehicle.
- Through Blue Bird Energy Services, the company provides site engineering, permitting, construction, customizable hardware and software, warranties, and maintenance for charging infrastructure.
- Chargers feature single or dual ports to connect and charge simultaneously up to two electric-powered vehicles.
- Britton Smith serves as senior vice president of Electrification and Chief Strategy Officer at Blue Bird Corporation.
- Blue Bird was founded in 1927.
- Blue Bird buses carry the majority of 25 million children twice a day.
- The company has more than 20,000 propane, natural gas, and electric powered buses in operation today.
