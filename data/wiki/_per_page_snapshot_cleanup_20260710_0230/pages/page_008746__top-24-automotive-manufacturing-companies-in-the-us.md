# Top 24 Automotive Manufacturing Companies in the US

## Facts

- The US automotive manufacturing industry is pivoting toward electric and hybrid vehicles driven by consumer demand and regulatory pressures, while increasing adoption of automation and robotics in manufacturing processes.
- Chevrolet is actively involved in the transition to electric vehicles, showcasing models like the Equinox EV and Silverado EV.
- Mazda Toyota Manufacturing is a joint venture between Mazda and Toyota located in Huntsville, Alabama, established in 2018.
- Mazda Toyota Manufacturing specializes in assembling the Mazda CX-50 and Toyota Corolla Cross with an annual production capacity of 300,000 vehicles.
- Mazda Toyota Manufacturing has made significant investments totaling $2.311 billion and employs around 1,466 people.
- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc. (KMMG) is located in West Point, Georgia, serving as the first manufacturing site for Kia Corporation in North America.
- KMMG began mass production on November 16, 2009, and has produced over 4.2 million vehicles.
- The KMMG facility operates on a 2,200-acre site with an annual production capacity of 350,000 units.
- KMMG assembles the Telluride SUV, Sorento SUV, Sportage SUV, and K5 mid-size sedan.
- KMMG recently expanded its production capabilities to include the all-electric EV9.
- KMMG has created more than 14,000 jobs and emphasizes community engagement and workforce development.
- Mercedes-Benz U.S. International, Inc. (MBUSI) is located in Vance, Alabama, established in 1993, and assembles the GLE and GLS SUVs.
- MBUSI has seen significant investments exceeding $7 billion, including a recent $1 billion allocation for electric vehicle production and the establishment of a battery factory.
- MBUSI has introduced training programs for aspiring automotive technicians to contribute to local workforce development.
- Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama, LLC (HMMA) is located in Montgomery, Alabama, founded in 2002 as Hyundai's first US assembly plant.
- HMMA spans 3.4 million square feet and can produce up to 399,500 vehicles annually.
- HMMA assembles the Tucson, Santa Fe, and Genesis GV70, and employs nearly 4,000 individuals.
- HMMA operates three engine shops that produce engines for Hyundai and Kia vehicles.
- BMW Manufacturing Co., LLC is located in Greer, South Carolina, with its Spartanburg facility being one of the largest BMW plants globally.
- BMW Manufacturing employs over 10,000 individuals and produces hybrid models as part of a commitment to sustainability and electrified vehicles.
- Dura Automotive Systems LLC specializes in electrification solutions and battery enclosures for battery electric vehicles.
- Mitsubishi Motors is making strides in the electric vehicle market with models like the Outlander PHEV.
- BMW of North America is actively involved in the transition towards electric mobility with a range of electrified vehicles.
