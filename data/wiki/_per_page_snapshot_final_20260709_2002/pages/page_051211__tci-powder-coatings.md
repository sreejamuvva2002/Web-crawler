## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is located in Ellaville, GA.
- TCI Powder Coatings is a subsidiary of RPM International Inc.
- Thomas G. Whalen was named Director of Marketing and Segment Strategy at TCI Powder Coatings.
- Whalen brings over 25 years of experience in the industrial coatings market to this position.
- For the past two years prior to this appointment, Whalen served as Global Business Director, Consumer Electronics, and Performance Glass Coatings at PPG Industries.
- Prior to PPG, Whalen was Vice President of Liquid and Mirror Coatings Units at Spraylat, which was acquired by PPG.
- Before joining Spraylat, Whalen worked at Valspar with increasing management experience, including Global Segment Director.
- Whalen started his coatings career in 1986 at Moline Paint in sales and held senior positions including International Business Director.
