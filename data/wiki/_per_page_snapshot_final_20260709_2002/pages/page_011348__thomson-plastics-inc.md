# Thomson Plastics, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-10

**Source:** [Altus Capital Partners | News](https://www.altuscapitalpartners.com/article/10-oct-2023/investment-and-installation---2-3700t-machines)  ·  `page_011348`

## Facts

- Thomson Plastics, Inc. (“TPI”) formally announced the investment and installation of two new 3,700-ton injection molding machines for its southern locations.
- The purchase fulfills TPI’s strategic plan to refresh the fleet of injection molding equipment across all facilities by the end of 2026.
- TPI President and CEO Steve Dyer stated that long-term investments are critical to TPI’s ongoing commitment to exceeding customer expectations in quality, cost, and customer service.
- The new presses are faster and significantly more efficient, using 40% less energy than older vintage machines.
- According to Dyer, the upgrades produce a more consistent and higher-quality product that will continue to serve marquee customers.
- TPI is working to step up its game as marquee customers grow and expand, with growing expectations from them.
- TPI will also be looking for many new customers.
- This effort follows a Year One investment in five new production machines during 2022.
- Three of the 2022 machines ranged in capacities between 1,000 and 1,980 tons and were installed at Thomson headquarters.
- Two of the 2022 machines were 400-ton units brought online at TPI’s Lexington, NC, facility.
- TPI is one of only a handful of companies that can employ as much as 4,400 tons of clamping force.
- TPI’s mission is to be the leader in large tonnage injection molding in the southeast.
