# Aspen Aerogels

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels is adding a new location soon in Right to Work Bulloch County, Georgia.
- The company is investing a total of $325 million toward the project.
- The new location will create over 250 new jobs.
- The facility will be Aspen's second aerogel manufacturing plant.
- The City of Statesboro and Bulloch County region offer access to efficient ports, a strong available workforce, and secure low-cost utilities and critical raw materials.
- Aspen Aerogels provides innovative, sustainable solutions for the electric vehicle and energy storage markets.
- Georgia employees will collaborate with Aspen’s network of world-class scientists, engineers, and additional skilled workforce to solve challenges in the changing mobility landscape.
- The Development Authority of Bulloch County welcomed Aspen Aerogels to the Statesboro-Bulloch County community.
- Aspen's decision to locate in the region is seen as a testament to the region’s commitment to innovation, particularly in the electric mobility ecosystem.
- Georgia Southern University and Ogeechee Technical College will provide critical education resources and support to help Aspen flourish.
