# Fat Boys Golf Carts

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-materials|Battery Materials]]  ·  **Location:** Georgia, United States

## Overview

Dealer offering a wide range of new and custom golf carts, parts, accessories, financing, and battery supply in Covington and Athens, Georgia.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Authorized supplier of Crown and Trojan batteries for electric golf carts, supporting the EV segment of low-speed vehicles.

## Key facts

- Offers a "Wide Range" of golf carts, from stock models to fully custom builds with options such as rear seat kits, custom dashboards, steering wheels, stereo systems, wheel & tire kits, lift kits, street legal kits, interior and paint customization.
- Provides hunting & fishing accessories and can outfit carts for serious off-road ATV use.
- Maintains a large in-house inventory of parts and accessories; if an item is not stocked, it can usually be sourced within 24 hours or shipped directly to the customer.
- Authorized Crown and Trojan Battery Supplier, ensuring popular batteries are available when needed.
- Factory-trained service department capable of installing parts & accessories as well as servicing and repairing golf carts.
- Financing options are available subject to credit approval; customers should see a store associate for details.
