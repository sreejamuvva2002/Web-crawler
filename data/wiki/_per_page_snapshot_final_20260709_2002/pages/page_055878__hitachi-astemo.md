# Hitachi Astemo

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hitachi Astemo shines at Automechanika shanghai 2024｜News & Events | Astemo | Global Aftermarket](https://www.aftermarket.astemo.com/en/news/2025/0107_003942.html)  ·  `page_055878`

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo Aftermarket BU China region attended Automechanika shanghai organized from December 2 to 5.
- The total exhibition area spans 350,000 square meters with a record high of 6,763 exhibitors, marking a 20% increase from the previous session.
- Hitachi Astemo celebrated the 20th anniversary of Automechanika shanghai alongside its brands HITACHI, TOKICO, NISSIN, KEIHIN, and SHOWA at Booth E50 in Hall 2.1.
- The exhibition theme was "Green, Innovation, Diversity," highlighting Hitachi Astemo's commitment to green and new energy mobility solutions.
- Hitachi Astemo displayed aftermarket product solutions in powertrain systems, intelligent chassis systems, steering systems, and braking systems, highlighting Honda's original auto parts.
- For the first time, Hitachi Astemo introduced motorcycle modified shock absorbers and braking systems at the event.
- Several new product lines were showcased to reinforce localized product development in China.
- Traditional star products displayed include high-pressure fuel pumps, ignition coils, airflow sensors, and direct injectors.
- Focus areas included engine and chassis systems such as fuel pumps, oxygen sensors, ABS wheel speed sensors, VTC, OCV, EGR valves, brake discs, brake calipers, brake master cylinders, brake fluid, and shock absorbers.
- Nearly 20 product lines were exhibited, covering powertrain, braking, and suspension systems.
- Hitachi Astemo acts as a Tier 1 supplier of fuel injectors, with all products being original equipment (OE) that provide rapid and precise fuel injection using high-speed electromagnets and pivots.
- All brake disc products undergo rigorous bench testing to ensure stable performance under extreme conditions, meeting OE dimensional and tolerance standards with quality equal to or exceeding original factory requirements.
- Hitachi Astemo has been supplying shock absorbers to major OEMs since 1934 and equips one in every five vehicles worldwide.
- The company operates 13 shock absorber production facilities across seven countries, making it one of the world's largest shock absorber suppliers.
- All shock absorber products undergo stringent durability tests and 100% quality inspections to ensure stable and reliable performance.
- The motorcycle product portfolio highlighted included brake pads, calipers, front master cylinders, and high-performance front and rear shock absorbers.
- The exhibition concluded with the signing of the Hitachi Astemo engine oil contract project.
- A FY25 Honda distributor agreement was continued with Guangzhou Yishan Enterprise.
- An outstanding Honda after-sales product distributor was recognized for FY24.
- Honda key product training was completed during the event.
- Ms. Teresa Li delivered a presentation titled "Adhere to the path of sustainable development in the involution" at the Sustainable Development Forum on December 5.
- The four-day exhibition concluded as a resounding success.
