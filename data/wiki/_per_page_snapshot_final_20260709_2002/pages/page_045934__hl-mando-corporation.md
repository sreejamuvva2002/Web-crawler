# HL Mando Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-31

**Source:** [HL Mando Awarded "2022 Supplier of the Year" by General Motors Company (GM)](https://www.newswire.ca/news-releases/hl-mando-awarded-2022-supplier-of-the-year-by-general-motors-company-gm--898720630.html)  ·  `page_045934`

## Facts

- HL Mando was awarded the "2022 Supplier of the Year" by General Motors Company (GM).
- This marks the third consecutive year HL Mando has received a Supplier of the Year award from GM, beginning with the "Launch Excellence Award" in 2020.
- The company has won the SOY Award nine times in total since 2003.
- HL Mando overcame the COVID-19 pandemic and a semiconductor supply shortage in 2020 to secure three straight years of awards.
- In 2022, HL Mando received top marks for Business Performance, including defect-free product launching, product supply, and quality maintenance.
- HL Mando also earned top marks for Cultural Priorities, specifically safety, communication, and transparency in 2022.
- The 31st ceremony of the 2022 SOY was held on March 23, 2023, in San Antonio, Texas.
- The award is given to outstanding partner companies within the top 1% of GM's suppliers, selected annually from over 20,000 suppliers worldwide.
- One hundred seven suppliers from ten countries attended the ceremony, including HL Mando America.
- Senior President and CEO Steve (Seong-hyeon) Cho stated it is a great honor to receive the award for three consecutive years.
- Vice President Yun Lee, Executive Director Jason (Jae-hyuk) Kim, and Director Jeff Pontius also attended the ceremony representing HL Mando Americas.
- GM vice president of Global Purchasing and Supply Chain Jeff Morrison recognized the suppliers for overcoming obstacles and demonstrating resilience, resourcefulness, determination, and commitment to sustainable innovation.
- CEO Steve Cho expressed gratitude to labor union members and branches/subsidiaries across the world during the ceremony.
- HL Mando Corporation is a leading global automotive parts supplier specializing in xEV and autonomous driving solutions.
- The company was founded in 1962 and is headquartered in the Republic of Korea, trading under stock code KS 204320.
- HL Mando holds an A grade in the MSCI (2021) ESG ratings.
- The company offers state-of-the-art driving systems including Brake, Steering, Suspension, Propulsion, and Power solutions, along with software solutions for vehicle manufacturers and mobility service providers.
- HL Mando's technology focuses on three domains: vehicle integrated safety, autonomous driving, and electric mobility.
- The company serves over sixty (60) customers from over 50 locations worldwide, encompassing local and global automakers in every major region.
