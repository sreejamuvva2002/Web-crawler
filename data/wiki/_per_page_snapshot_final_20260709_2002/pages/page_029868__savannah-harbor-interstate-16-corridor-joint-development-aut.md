# Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2023-08-31

**Source:** [News – Page 2 – Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority](https://savannahjda.com/news/page/2/)  ·  `page_029868`

## Facts

- On August 31, 2023, Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy Solution (LGES) will invest an additional $2 billion in their battery cell manufacturing joint venture at the Metaplant in Bryan County.
- This expansion raises the joint venture's total investment value to more than $4.3 billion.
- The expansion will create another 400 new jobs.
- On June 28, 2023, representatives from Sewon Group visited Effingham County to discuss plans for construction of a new plant.
- The new plant will manufacture electric vehicle parts for the Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant in Bryan County.
- Sewon Precision Industry has offices in South Korea and Sewon America is located in LaGrange, Ga.
- On June 22, 2023, Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that automotive parts manufacturer NVH Korea will establish a new facility in Locust Grove, Henry County.
- The NVH Korea project will create over 160 new jobs.
- The NVH Korea project will bring $72 million in investment to Henry County.
- On June 7, 2023, Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Woory Industrial Co., Ltd. will establish a new manufacturing facility in Dublin, Georgia.
- The Woory facility will create more than 130 new jobs.
- The Woory facility will bring an estimated $18 million in investment to Dublin-Laurens County.
- On June 5, 2023, Hyundai Motor America announced a new partnership with Savannah State University.
- Hyundai continues its partnership with Memorial Health Dwaine & Cynthia Willett Children’s Hospital of Savannah and Safe Kids Savannah.
- Hyundai donated $50,000 to Savannah organizations on June 5, 2023.
- On June 5, 2023, Genesis Gives, the corporate social responsibility initiative of Genesis Motor America, donated a total of $30,000 to two youth sports nonprofits in Savannah, Georgia.
- On May 26, 2023, Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy Solution announced an EV battery cell manufacturing joint venture in the U.S.
- On May 23, 2023, Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Hanon Systems will create 160 new jobs.
- Hanon Systems will invest more than $40 million in a new manufacturing facility in Bulloch County.
- Hanon Systems is a global provider of automotive thermal and energy management solutions.
- On April 11, 2023, Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Seohan Auto Georgia will create at least 180 new jobs.
- Seohan Auto Georgia will invest more than $72 million in a new manufacturing facility in Liberty County.
- Seohan Auto Georgia is a developer and manufacturer of automobile parts.
- On March 6, 2023, Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that PHA will create 402 new jobs.
- PHA will invest more than $67 million in a new facility in Chatham County.
- PHA is a global auto parts manufacturer that will supply the Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America in Bryan County.
