# Liberty County Development Authority

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Foreign Direct Investment – LCDA](https://comegrow.global/foreign-direct-investment/)  ·  `page_059462`

## Facts

- Foreign direct investment has resulted in 70% of the region's industrial employment.
- The area maintains an export and import roster spanning 70 countries.
- SNF Holding, a French company and world leader in manufacturing water-soluble polymers for water treatment, wastewater treatment, and cosmetics, established its North American headquarters in Riceboro.
- SNF Floquip was started on SNF Holding’s Riceboro campus to manufacture equipment for the parent company’s chemicals before moving to Midway Industrial Park in 2011.
- SNF Floquip has doubled in size since moving to Midway Industrial Park, now occupying 160,000 square feet.
- German-based apparel manufacturer Hugo Boss established a distribution center at Midway Industrial Park in 2001 that has expanded to 330,000 square feet with three-tier decking.
- Belgian-based Balta Group division Balta Home USA operates a warehouse and distribution facility in Liberty County that leverages local workforce and market access.
- The Balta Home USA facility is located two miles from Interstate 95 and 30 minutes from the Port of Savannah.
- Korean companies Pactra International and Hankook Tire formed a partnership to distribute tires across 10 Southeastern states.
- The Pactra/Hankook Tire partnership operates in a 500,000-square-foot Silver LEED-certified facility located in the Tradeport East Business Center a mile off Interstate 95.
- The Pactra/Hankook Tire facility provides ideal access to the Port of Savannah.
- Seohan Auto Georgia is a Tier 1 provider to the Hyundai Meta Plant of Florapharm and is part of South Korea’s diversified Seohan Group.
- The Seohan Group focuses on innovation in auto parts, free forging, wind power, and future mobility sectors.
