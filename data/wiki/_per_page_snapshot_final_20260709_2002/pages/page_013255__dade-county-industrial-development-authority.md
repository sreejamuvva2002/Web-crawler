# Dade County Industrial Development Authority

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2023-02-16

**Source:** [Industrial Park Tour – Dade County Sentinel](https://www.dadecountysentinel.com/2023/02/16/industrial-park-tour/)  ·  `page_013255`

## Facts

- On Feb. 7, the Industrial Development Authority hosted a tour of three manufacturers in the Trenton industrial park.
- The IDA invited city and county government, school officials, and members of the Alliance for Dade to join the tour.
- IDA Executive Director Evan Stone stated that attendees would be amazed at what’s being produced in the city of Trenton and shipped ’round the world.
- Evan Stone noted that despite his tenure on the IDA, he was unaware of all the products manufactured locally and their global shipping destinations prior to the tour.
- The industrial park tour commenced at Vanguard National Trailer Corp.
