# Bridgestone Bandag, LLC

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-09-26

**Source:** [Bridgestone to Expand Abilene Bandag Retread Plant | Retreading Business](https://www.retreadingbusiness.com/bridgestone-to-expand-abilene-bandag-retread-plant/)  ·  `page_018219`

## Facts

- Bridgestone Americas announced a USD 60 million investment to expand its Bridgestone Bandag, LLC manufacturing plant in Abilene, Texas.
- The expansion adds 50,000 square feet of space to the Abilene plant.
- The expansion addresses growing demand for the company’s tread rubber products driven by rapid growth in its retread business.
- Operational changes include an immediate increase to six and seven-day production schedules.
- New mixing operations will be constructed at the facility.
- The new mixing operations are expected to be completed and operational by January 2025.
- The investment and additional operating days will increase the plant’s output by 16 percent.
- Barry Owens, senior vice president of Bridgestone Americas Manufacturing Group, stated that the company celebrates the growth and commitment of the plant and community.
- Owens noted the physical expansion is driven by growing demand for products and the team’s commitment to delivering innovation and superior quality for customers globally.
- Owens highlighted the company's vital role in the transformation of mobility through building greener retreads to maximize fuel efficiency for fleets.
