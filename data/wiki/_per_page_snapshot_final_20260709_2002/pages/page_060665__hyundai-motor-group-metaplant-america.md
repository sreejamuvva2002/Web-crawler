# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-21

**Source:** [HMGMA Reveals 2026 IONIQ 9 as Newest Production Vehicle - HMGMA](https://www.hmgma.com/2024/11/21/hmgma-reveals-2026-ioniq-9-as-newest-production-vehicle/)  ·  `page_060665`

## Facts

- HMGMA announced that its second production vehicle will be the all-electric 2026 IONIQ 9 three-row SUV.
- The IONIQ 9 will be the first all-new Hyundai model assembled at the Metaplant.
- In June, Metaplant authorities announced that the first production vehicle would be the 2025 IONIQ 5, an updated version of the award-winning EV.
- HMGMA is starting to build the all-new IONIQ 9 at the Metaplant, expanding the locally manufactured IONIQ lineup.
- HMGMA is the only facility building IONIQ 9s in North America.
- Oscar Kwon serves as the CEO of HMGMA.
- The IONIQ 9 was revealed today at AutoMobility LA 2024 in Los Angeles and simultaneously unveiled by HMGMA at AutoNation Hyundai Savannah.
- Meta Pros who assembled the preproduction vehicle on display were present for the unveiling.
- The IONIQ 9 features a 12.3-inch panoramic curved touch display and available first- and second-row Relaxation Seats.
- The IONIQ 9 includes an impressive array of standard Advanced Driver Assistant Systems.
- The 2026 IONIQ 9 targets 300+ miles of range for all trims and features a standard NACS port capable of ultra-fast 800V charging, charging from 10 to 80% in just 24 minutes with a 350-kW DC charger.
- Production of the 2026 IONIQ 9 starts in Q1 2025, and the SUV will go on sale in Q2.
- The IONIQ 9 is expected to qualify for the full $7,500 federal tax credit thanks to U.S. assembly and battery sourcing.
- The Metaplant will produce Hyundai, Genesis, and Kia electrified vehicles.
- Additional vehicle models will continue to be announced as the Metaplant ramps up to full production status.
