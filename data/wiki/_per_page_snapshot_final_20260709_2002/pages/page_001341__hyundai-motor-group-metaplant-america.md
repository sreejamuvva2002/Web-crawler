# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-03-28

**Source:** [Hyundai Metaplant, area suppliers to hold job fair - Coastal Courier](https://coastalcourier.com/news/hyundai-metaplant-area-suppliers-hold-job-fair/)  ·  `page_001341`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America will hold a job fair in Savannah with recruiters for three plants currently under construction in Bulloch County that will supply parts to the Hyundai plant.
- The job fair is set for 10 a.m.-4 p.m. on Thursday, April 4 at the Eckburg Auditorium of Savannah Technical College on White Bluff Road.
- According to a WorkSource Georgia release partnering with the Metaplant as a sponsor, approximately 3,500 jobs will be available between the Hyundai EV plant and its local suppliers through 2024.
- Career opportunities at the event will include manufacturing, logistics, technology, and administration roles.
- Ecoplastic America Corporation, a supplier of injection-molded plastic automotive body parts to Hyundai Motor Group, is building a $205-million plant off Highway 301 South that will create 456 jobs phased in over eight years.
- Ajin USA, a supplier of metal auto body parts to Hyundai, is investing $317 million and will eventually employ 630 people at its Joon Georgia plant on 83 acres in the Bruce Yawn Commerce Park off Interstate 16.
- Hanon Systems, a manufacturer of vehicle air conditioning systems, will build a plant in Statesboro’s Gateway Regional Industrial Park off Highway 301 South, with a $40 million investment projected to create 160 jobs.
- Recruiters from additional suppliers scheduled to attend include DSC Savannah, Glovis EV Logistics America, Hyundai Materials, Hyundai Steel Georgia, Hyundai Transys GA, Kyungshin America, Mobis Alabama Savannah Plant, Mobis North America Electrified Powertrain, PHA Georgia, Seohan Auto Georgia, Seoyon E-Hwa, and Sewon America.
- The event is free, and candidates of all ages and experience levels are encouraged to attend dressed for success with résumés in hand.
- Job seekers must create an account and résumé on the WorkSource Georgia portal to expedite admission to the event.
