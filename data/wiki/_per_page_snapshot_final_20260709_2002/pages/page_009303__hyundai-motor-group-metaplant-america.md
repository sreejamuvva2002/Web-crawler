# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Bryan County Hyundai Metaplant America Project | Thomas & Hutton](https://www.thomasandhutton.com/portfolio_page/hyundai-motor-group-metaplant-america/?trk=article-ssr-frontend-pulse_little-text-block)  ·  `page_009303`

## Facts

- Thomas & Hutton provided engineering design services for the Hyundai Metaplant America electric automotive manufacturing facility at the Bryan County Megasite.
- Thomas & Hutton's services included site selection, general consulting, due diligence assistance, site master planning, project management, design, permitting, and construction phase services for the Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Joint Development Authority for the Hyundai campus, including mass grading.
- The planned Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant will include five buildings totaling approximately 4 million square feet.
- Thomas & Hutton’s design services for Haeahn/Hyundai Motor Group include onsite road, site, water, sewer, and storm drainage infrastructure; mass grading plans; and wetland permitting to support the approximately 3,000-acre site located in the southeast quadrant of U.S. Highway 280 and Interstate 16 in Bryan County, Georgia.
- The Hyundai Metaplant is Hyundai Motor Group’s first dedicated fully EV and battery manufacturing facility in the United States.
- The Hyundai Metaplant is the largest economic development deal in the state of Georgia’s history.
- Thomas & Hutton began working with the Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Joint Development Authority (JDA) in 2019 on planning, survey, design, and permitting.
- Thomas & Hutton assisted with grant writing and funding support for regional water and wastewater improvements to service the Megasite and the region at large.
