# OTR Engineered Solutions

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [OTR Engineered Solutions - Locations](https://partners.otrwheel.com/locations)  ·  `page_058459`

## Facts

- OTR is headquartered in Rome, Georgia, located 1-½ hours northwest of Atlanta.
- OTR operates a global footprint comprising 32 facilities across the U.S., Canada, Mexico, England, Germany, Czech Republic, China, Thailand, and Sri Lanka.
- OTR provides manufacturing, painting, and assembly; tire mounting, sequencing, and warehousing; subassembly; and foam- and tire-fill services.
- OTR supports localization initiatives for OEMs by maintaining stocks for distribution partners to ensure coast-to-coast aftermarket coverage.
- OTR operates 16 U.S. locations totaling nearly 2 million square feet that support well-known OEMs and distributors.
- OTR's business model enables it to quickly open new facilities with dedicated teams and capacity.
- In the U.S., OTR manufactures and paints wheels, rims, and components in four locations: Rome, Georgia; Omaha, Nebraska; Jacksonville, Florida; and Quincy, Illinois.
- OTR provides foam- and tire-fill services in multiple U.S. facilities to service OEMs, distributors, and dealers nationwide.
- Several U.S. locations provide subassembly services as a leveraged resource for OEMs, resulting in cost savings.
- Other U.S. facility locations include Chambersburg, Pennsylvania; Timmonsville and Clinton, South Carolina; Jacksonville, Florida (2); Louisville, Mississippi; Mansfield, Texas; Hesston and McCook, Kansas; Phoenix, Arizona; Denver, Colorado; and Oconomowoc, Wisconsin.
- OTR received Excel Industries Gold Supplier Awards in 2017, 2018, and 2019.
- OTR was the Honda Challenging Spirit Award Winner for Support and Performance in 2019.
- OTR won the Kubota Kaizen Community Award in 2013 and 2014.
- OTR Canada is headquartered in Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario, with additional locations in Guelph and Sudbury, Ontario; Saskatoon, Saskatchewan; and Edmonton, Alberta.
- All five Canadian facilities produce multi-piece rims and components for earthmoving, surface and underground mining, construction, and specialty vehicle applications.
- The Sudbury facility recently installed a state-of-the-art bake oven system with temperature and humidity control and a new priming process to optimize adhesion and durability.
- OTR supports a large aftermarket in Canada through partnerships with major distributors and dealers, including a wheel inspection and recertification program.
- OTR Europe operates facilities in Derby and Suffolk, England; Sande, Germany; and the Czech Republic, providing tire mounting, sequencing, just-in-time supply, and warehousing.
- The Suffolk facility manufactures and repairs wheels primarily for agricultural vehicles and provides foam-filling for OEM customers.
- OTR's Czech Republic facility opened in 2013 to service Europe, Russia, and eastern European countries.
- OTR's team speaks multiple languages and has experience setting up programs across Europe for OEM and aftermarket customers.
- OTR's China facility is located in Wuxi, two hours northwest of Shanghai, and manufactures multi-piece steel rims, hubs, and components for surface and underground mining, material handling, heavy-duty, and specialty vehicles.
- Wheels produced at the China facility range from 10″ to 63″, using HSLA grade steel with seamless forgings for critical sections.
- The China facility utilizes sub-arc welding (SAW) and precision CNC machining to ensure tight tolerances, operates four paint lines with advanced pretreatment and primer systems, and conducts in-process ultrasonic and magnetic particle testing for weld integrity.
- OTR's Thailand facility in Chon Buri manufactures steel wheels primarily for agricultural OEMs using automated construction for high-volume production with stringent quality and cycle time targets.
- The Thailand facility provides value-added services including tire mounting and sequencing.
- OTR's Sri Lanka facility in Ekala manufactures steel rims, wheels, accessories for off-the-road applications (22"-49"), and steel bands using cutting-edge technology and multiple paint lines with advanced 5-stage wash, wet, and powder coat systems compliant with OEM standards.
- The Sri Lanka facility provides tire mounting and just-in-time supply services.
- Rigorous testing at all production stages measures attributes such as tensile strength, dimensional accuracy, and compliance with ISO 9001:2015 standards and OEM specifications.
