# Toyota Industries Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- TACG and TICA are two entities of the Toyota Industries Group, an international corporation originating in Kariya, Japan in 1926.
- Fortune magazine has recognized Toyota Industries Corporation as one of the World’s Most Admired Motor Vehicle Parts Suppliers.
- In 2017, Georgia honored TACG and TICA as one of the State’s Top Automotive Suppliers.
- More than 1,300 associates make up the heart of the two local companies and contribute a positive economic impact in Jackson County and the surrounding region.
- The company's customers span across North America, representing many of the world’s most respected brands.
- As the largest manufacturer of vehicle compressors for air conditioning systems, its products represent over 40 percent of the worldwide compressor market.
- Toyota Industries Group companies are among the world’s leading makers of automobile air-conditioning compressors and AC/DC inverters.
- TD Automotive Compressor Georgia (TACG) was established in 2004 as a joint venture between Toyota Industries North America Inc. and DENSO International America Inc.
- TACG is a 943,000 square foot facility built on 150 acres of land in Jackson County, Georgia.
- Toyota Industries Compressor Parts America, Co. (TICA) was established in 2012 to expand Toyota Industries Corporation’s production capacity for variable displacement type compressors in North America.
- TICA is a 600,000 square foot facility which is physically connected to TACG, its primary customer.
- In November 2020, the company expanded production to produce DC-AC inverters that allow electronics such as cellphones, laptops, job-site tools and small appliances to plug in and run off power from the vehicle.
- The manufacturing process involves roles including assembly, logistics, electronics, CNC machine operation, sand & die cast operations, maintenance & engineering, and administration.
- Toyota Industries Corporation was founded by Sakichi Toyoda, a visionary Japanese entrepreneur and inventor who invented the Toyoda Wooden Hand Loom in 1890.
- The company expanded from textile machinery and materials-handling equipment into electronics, automobiles, and components, including automobile air-conditioning compressors and DC-AC inverters for the North American market.
- In 2017, TACG and TICA were named Georgia’s Automotive Supplier of the Year.
- Today, TACG and TICA have grown to approximately 1,300 associates working together to supply products to many of the world’s top vehicle manufacturers.
- The Georgia-based company is located in Pendergrass and Jackson County.
- The community is within an hour’s drive to Atlanta and minutes of Lake Lanier.
- Local education opportunities include Jefferson and Jackson County school systems, the University of Georgia in Athens, the University of North Georgia and Brenau University in Gainesville, UNG’s Dahlonega Campus, and Georgia Tech in Atlanta.
