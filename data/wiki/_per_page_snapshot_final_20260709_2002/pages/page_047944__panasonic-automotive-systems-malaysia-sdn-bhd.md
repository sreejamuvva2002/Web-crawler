# Panasonic Automotive Systems Malaysia Sdn Bhd

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Panasonic becomes first in Penang to operate net-zero carbon emission plant | NEWS | Reccessary](https://www.reccessary.com/en/news/my-announcement/panasonic-becomes-first-penang-operate-net-zero-carbon-emission-plant)  ·  `page_047944`

## Facts

- Panasonic has announced that its automotive systems factory in Penang, Malaysia is now operating as a net-zero carbon emission plant.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Malaysia Sdn Bhd manufactures audio and navigation systems for vehicles.
- The company achieved net-zero CO2 status earlier this year.
- It is the first facility in Penang to declare achievement of net-zero CO2 status.
- The ISO 14001-certified plant covers a land area of 100,700 sqm with a build-up area of 28,430 sqm.
- The facility has been actively working to reduce emissions across various aspects of its operations.
- Key emission reduction measures include the replacement of water pumps, lighting, air-conditioning, and cooling systems' equipment with energy-efficient alternatives.
- Additional emission reduction measures include the installation of motion sensors, air leak surveys, and implementing corresponding countermeasures.
