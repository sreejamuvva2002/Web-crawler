## Facts

- Mativ Holdings, Inc. (formerly Schweitzer-Mauduit International, Inc.) is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia, United States of America.
- The company operates 47 production locations worldwide across the United States, United Kingdom, China, Germany, France, Belgium, Poland, India, Brazil, Canada, Spain, Italy, Mexico, Netherlands, Malaysia, and Luxembourg.
- Mativ holds a 50% equity interest in two joint ventures located in China.
- The company's technology portfolio combines polymers, fibers, and resins to optimize customer product performance across multiple stages of the value chain.
- Mativ targets premium applications across diversified and growing end-markets.
- On July 6, 2022, Schweitzer-Mauduit International, Inc. consummated a merger with Neenah, Inc., after which SWM changed its name to Mativ Holdings, Inc.
- The company reports under two segments: Advanced Technical Materials (ATM) and Fiber-Based Solutions (FBS).
