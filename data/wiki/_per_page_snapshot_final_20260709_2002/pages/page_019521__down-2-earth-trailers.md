## Facts

- Down 2 Earth Trailers has a long and rich history that began in 1999 by Paul Dubberly and his son Andrew.
- The company started in the small town of Baxley, GA.
- In the early days its focus was on single axle utility trailers.
- Over time, trailers became more common for commercial use and they added Equipment, Gooseneck, and Dump lines.
- Down 2 Earth has been building trailer for more than 23 years.
- Each month more than 1,000 trailers roll out of the Baxley, Ga plant.
