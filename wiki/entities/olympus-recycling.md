# Olympus Recycling

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-materials|Battery Materials]]  ·  **Location:** Atlanta and Gainesville, GA, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Olympus Recycling offers safe, responsible, and efficient battery recycling services in Georgia, accepting a wide range of automotive, industrial, and consumer batteries and providing competitive payouts while complying with state and federal safety guidelines.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Recycles lithium-ion batteries from electric vehicles, recovering valuable metals for the EV supply chain.

## Key facts

- Accepts automotive lead-acid batteries, forklift and UPS backup batteries, lithium-ion batteries, NiCd and NiMH batteries.
- Operates two convenient drop-off locations in Atlanta and Gainesville, GA with no appointment required.
- Provides immediate payment based on current scrap battery market rates after weighing and inspecting the batteries.
- Follows all state and federal guidelines to ensure safe processing of hazardous battery materials.
- Offers competitive payouts for both lead-acid and rechargeable batteries.
- Encourages safe handling practices such as storing batteries in a cool, dry place and taping terminals on lithium-ion batteries.
