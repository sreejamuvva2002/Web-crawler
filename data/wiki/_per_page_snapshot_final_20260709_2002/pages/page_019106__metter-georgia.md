## Facts

- DAS North America announced an advanced manufacturing facility in 2023.
- The DAS North America facility held its ribbon-cutting ceremony in August 2024.
- The DAS North America facility is expected to create hundreds of jobs and attract workers to the Metter area.
- Candler County voters approved a Floating Local Option Sales Tax (FLOST) by 1,027 votes to 368 to fund infrastructure improvements such as roads and utilities.
- The FLOST measure supports economic incentives through enhanced public works and aligns with local leadership priorities for fostering growth and diversity.
- Metter's population reached an estimated 4,021 as of July 2024, showing modest recovery partly attributed to recent industrial developments.
- Metter's location along Interstate 16 supports commuting to nearby employment hubs, helping to retain and draw residents.
- The city's annual operating budget totals approximately $5-6 million based on recent fiscal reports.
