# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [TSLOTS Aluminum Framing System to Expand Operations into Southeastern U.S.](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20230404005535/en/TSLOTS-Aluminum-Framing-System-to-Expand-Operations-into-Southeastern-U.S.)  ·  `page_029588`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum, a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation (NYSE: TG), is expanding its TSLOTS line of aluminum structural framing systems into the Southeastern region of the United States.
- The expansion project will add the Newnan, Georgia facility as Bonnell Aluminum's newest manufacturing and distribution site.
- TSLOTS aluminum framing systems enable manufacturers to create light, durable, attractive, strong, versatile, and functional structures using custom T-slotted aluminum extrusions and connecting hardware.
- Randy Johnson, Senior Brand Manager for TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum, stated: 'We have been successful in growing our TSLOTS product line by positioning our manufacturing and distribution sites closer to where our customers operate.'
- Johnson added: 'This is our second major investment in TSLOTS in two years, following a very successful expansion into the Midwest in 2021. This capacity addition will increase our footprint across the U.S. and provide our customers with reduced shipping costs and shorter lead times.'
- TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum operates as a full-service product line providing project design assistance, fabrication, custom packaging, and full assembly.
- Bonnell Aluminum serves the building and construction, automotive and transportation, consumer durables, machinery and equipment, electrical, and distribution markets with custom-fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions.
- The company's branded product lines include TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum and Futura Transitions by Bonnell Aluminum.
- Bonnell Aluminum currently operates manufacturing facilities in Newnan, Georgia; Carthage, Tennessee; Niles, Michigan; Elkhart, Indiana; and Clearfield, Utah.
