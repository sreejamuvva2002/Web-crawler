# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America (PTNA) is adding a compound mixing facility to its Rome, Ga., facility.
- The investment amount for the new facility is $12 million.
- The $12 million investment will support the Pirelli Group's strategic growth plan for the North American market.
- The investment will help PTNA sustain its market gains and the 30% sales increase realized over the past three years.
- Mauro Pessi serves as PTNA's chairman and CEO.
- Mauro Pessi stated he is very proud of Pirelli's commitment to growing the Rome-based MIRS (Modular Integrated Robotized System) manufacturing facility.
- The new mixing room facility demonstrates dedication to Pirelli's strategic growth in the North American market.
- The project supports Pirelli's commitment to investing in the economic development of the city of Rome and the state of Georgia.
- PTNA has received excellent support from local, state and regional governments and organizations to make this project happen.
- PTNA looks forward to continuing relationships with local colleges and the Georgia QuickStart program that will help realize growth.
