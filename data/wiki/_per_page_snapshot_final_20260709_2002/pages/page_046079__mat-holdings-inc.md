# MAT Holdings, Inc.

## Facts

- MAT Holdings, Inc. is a privately held, globally diversified manufacturing, marketing, and distribution company providing products to Automotive and Consumer business categories.
- The Automotive segment includes the MAT Foundry Group, Friction Group, and Suspension Components Group.
- The MAT Foundry Group manufactures cast iron parts for automotive and industrial applications.
- The Friction Group supplies friction braking components.
- The Suspension Components Group supplies suspension components and systems.
- For over 30 years, the company has operated multi-faceted manufacturing, distribution, and sourcing facilities in Asia, Europe, and the United States.
- MAT Auto Group manufactures suspension components including shocks and struts under the Gabriel® brand for the North American market.
- Its suspension systems meet specifications for tractor, trailer, emergency response, bus, military, and motor home manufacturers.
- The MAT Foundry Group consists of 7 companies on 3 continents engineering and manufacturing cast iron products for passenger, commercial, heavy duty vehicle markets, industrial applications, and racing.
- The foundry group produces over 350,000 tons of iron per year across 7 foundries with 1,750 employees.
- The Foundry Group maintains 8 machining facilities across Europe, Asia, and Central America.
- The Foundry Group supports OEM and aftermarket customers with R&D including 80 process and development engineers.
- The MAT Friction Group consists of 8 companies on 3 continents serving passenger, commercial, and heavy duty vehicle markets with braking components including pads, shims, back plates, brake shoes, and linings.
- The friction group produces over 24 million sets of passenger brake pads per year, 2.5M sets of PV shoes per year, 200,000 sets of CV brake pads, and 4M pieces of CV linings per year across twelve factories.
- The Friction Group maintains three R&D centers and 42 dynamometers for product development and testing.
- MAT Industries operates domestic manufacturing facilities offering air compressors, air tools, and accessories under brands such as Powermate®, Industrial Air®, Industrial Air Contractor®, Sanborn®, and private label.
- Its air compressors are sold through major home centers, hardware co-ops, independents, and farm and fleet stores around the US.
- MAT Engine Technologies engineers and manufactures gasoline powered outdoor power equipment for the North American retail market under the Powermate® and Southland® brands.
- The company offers two and four cycle engines that are EPA and CARB compliant.
- Product offerings include walk behind edgers, field trimmers, garden tillers, blowers, hand-held trimmers, blowers, and walk behind cultivators.
- Midwest Air Technologies is a leading producer of fencing products, lawn and garden accessories, and pet accessories.
- It manufactures residential, chain link, and agricultural fencing under the YardGard® and FarmGard® brands.
- It produces trellis's, shepherds hooks, and other lawn and garden accessories under the Gilbert & Bennett® brand.
- MAT Logistics provides strategic logistic solutions for moving goods and maintains 7 U.S. facilities strategically positioned from coast-to-coast.
- MAT Logistics operates over 2.1 million square feet of floor space to service North America including Canada, the contiguous 48 United States, Alaska, Hawaii, and Puerto Rico.
- The company's philosophy focuses on creating value through process improvement, global supply & distribution, scale and scope economies, and engineering and marketing expertise.
- MAT Holdings, Inc. has averaged double-digit revenue increases over the past 5 years through acquisitions and organic growth.
- A key success factor is the ability to find synergies in acquisitions and assimilate them quickly to enable higher performance and ROI.
