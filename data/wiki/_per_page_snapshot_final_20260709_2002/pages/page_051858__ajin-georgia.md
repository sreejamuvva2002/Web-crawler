## Facts

- AJIN Georgia is one of Statesboro’s standout industries located in Bulloch County, Georgia.
- The company held its annual charitable donation luncheon on December 5, 2024, at Uncle Shug’s Bar-B-Q Place.
- Jamie Callaway serves as the General Manager of Human Resources at AJIN Georgia and hosted the event.
- AJIN Georgia's workforce grew from five employees at the Georgia plant last year to nearly 300 employees currently.
- The company plans to add even more staff as production ramps up.
- AJIN Georgia is fully operational and producing parts for the Hyundai IONIQ 5.
- Finished parts are shipped out on trucks that travel along Interstate 16 (I-16).
- AJIN sponsored 15 local educators for a 12-day immersion trip to South Korea to support workforce preparation in relevant industries.
- Ogeechee Technical College is developing an Industrial Systems and Robotics Training Facility set to open in 2026 to support the workforce needs of local industries like AJIN.
- Evans County Schools partners with Ogeechee Technical College on vocational training programs to help students identify and achieve career ambitions.
- Bulloch County Schools runs a workforce development program aimed at preparing students for careers, military service, or higher education.
