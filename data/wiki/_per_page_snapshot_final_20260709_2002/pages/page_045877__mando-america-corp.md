## Facts

- Mando America Corp. announced plans for a $19 million expansion to its North American headquarters in Opelika.
- The expansion will create an additional 32 jobs.
- Mando moved the location of its North American headquarters from Detroit to Opelika in 2003.
- Since moving to Opelika, the South Korean company has been regularly producing automotive parts for companies like Hyundai since the mid-2000’s.
- Mando produces brake, suspension and steering components at the Opelika location.
- The Opelika City Council granted the company a 15 year exemption from city ad valorem taxes.
- The new expansion will bring Mando’s total investment in Opelika to $220 million.
