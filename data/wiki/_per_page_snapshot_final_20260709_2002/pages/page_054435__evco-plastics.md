# EVCO Plastics

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [EVCO Plastics and Kistler: partnership for zero defect production in injection molding | Kistler TR](https://www.kistler.com/TR/en/evco-plastics-and-kistler-partnership-for-zero-defect-production-in-injection-molding/C00000564)  ·  `page_054435`

## Facts

- EVCO Plastics is a globally recognized leader in injection molding and many additional technologies.
- The family-run business was founded in Wisconsin in 1964.
- EVCO Plastics has a workforce of over 1,800 employees spread across eleven facilities in the US, China, and Mexico.
- Anna Bartz serves as the Director of Communications at EVCO Plastics.
- Following recent growth, EVCO Plastics operates more than 260 molding machines.
- EVCO Plastics offers technologies and services across the entire value chain, including automation, packaging, labelling, and more.
- Precision Molding is one of EVCO's four main divisions.
- EVCO's products and solutions cover diverse industries ranging from agriculture and powersports to packaging and consumer goods.
- EVCO Plastics explicitly states it is involved in just about everything except automotive parts and implantable devices.
- Medical technology accounts for roughly 20 percent of EVCO's annual sales.
- Two EVCO sites are equipped with an ISO Class 8 Clean Room: the headquarters in Deforest, Wisconsin, and the Chinese production facility in Dongguan.
- EVCO Plastics has partnered with Kistler to achieve zero-defect production in injection molding by instrumenting molds with cavity pressure sensors and process monitoring equipment.
