## Facts

- Seoyon E-Hwa Georgia, a U.S. unit of the company, received a five-star quality rating from the Hyundai Motor Group.
- No other interior parts supplier to Kia Motors' Georgia plant has ever earned a five-star rating from the Hyundai Motor Group.
- The five-star quality rating announcement was made via press release on May 31, 2016.
- The company held a ceremony to celebrate the completion of Seoyon R&D in Anyang City, Gyeonggi-Do.
- The new Anyang R&D center has nine stories above ground and two below, with a total floor area of approximately 20,000 square meters.
- The Anyang facility is expected to serve as an integrated R&D center for the Seoyon Group.
- The company had 153 R&D staff as of December 31, 2016.
- The Central Research Center is located in Asan.
- The Development Center is located in Ulsan.
- A Technical Research Center was established in Beijing, China in July 2007.
- Consolidated sales for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2016 were 2,402,877 million won.
- Operating income for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2016 was 107,825 million won.
- Net income for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2016 was 93,906 million won.
- R&D expenditure for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2016 was 35,217 million won, accounting for 3.5% of sales.
- Capital investment in the manufacture of automotive components was 5,385 million won in 2016.
- The planned capital investment for the manufacture of automotive components for 2017 is 4,287 million won.
