## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group will build its first dedicated electric vehicle manufacturing plant in Georgia.
- The facility site is located on a dusty expanse of vacant land outside Savannah in Bryan County.
- Governor Brian Kemp, a Republican, officially announced the project to political and business figures gathered under a large tent at the site.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated: “It is my great honor to officially announce that Hyundai Motor Group will build their first dedicated electric vehicle manufacturing plant right here in this good soil in Bryan County.”
- The announcement took place on a sweltering Friday during the summer.
- Georgia prioritizes creating green jobs over adopting specific climate targets.
