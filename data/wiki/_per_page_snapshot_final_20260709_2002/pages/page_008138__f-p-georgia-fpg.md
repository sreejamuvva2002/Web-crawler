# F&P Georgia (FPG)

## Facts

- F&P Georgia is a tier one automotive parts supplier located in Rome, Georgia, USA.
- Its parent company is Ftech Inc., with its head office located in Saitama prefecture, Japan.
- In North America, F&P Georgia operates as one of five subsidiaries.
- Two North American subsidiaries are located in Ontario, Canada, specifically in Tottenham and Stratford.
- Two North American subsidiaries are located in the USA, including Troy, Ohio, and F&P Georgia itself.
- Two North American subsidiaries are located in Mexico, in Queretaro and Irapuato.
- Ftech currently has over 20 facilities throughout the globe.
- F&P Georgia is a world-class, tier one automotive supplier with a global presence in the suspension component market.
- The company's goal is to produce high-quality parts at competitive pricing and deliver them exactly when and where customers require.
- Safety and Environment are designated as the number one priorities for F&P Georgia's associates.
- The company relies on associate creativity, expertise, and teamwork to meet and exceed operational goals.
- Customer satisfaction is supported through continuous improvement, perpetual training of associates, pursuit of new technologies, and daily challenges to existing processes.
- F&P Georgia aims to excel in the world's automotive suspension component market and be recognized as the #1 supplier in the industry.
- The company provides unrivaled customer service for high-quality automotive components manufactured cost-effectively.
- Operational principles include conducting affairs on mutual respect, refusing status quo, thinking/acting/evolving, facing adversity with challenge, maintaining flexibility for quick decisions, and acting as good corporate citizens adding value to communities and the environment.
- F&P Georgia holds ISO 14001 accreditation for its Environmental Management System (EMS), which validates environmental policy, impact planning, audit/corrective actions, and management review.
- The company maintains ISO/IATF16949 accreditation for its Quality Management System (QMS), focusing on continuous improvement, defect prevention, variation reduction (SPC), and waste reduction (Lean Manufacturing) specific to automotive components based on ISO9000.
- F&P Georgia received Honda's Green Factory Achievement in 2009 for excellence in environmental activities, including pollution prevention, energy savings, and conservation of natural resources.
- The company is committed to manufacturing high-quality suspension systems through technology, innovation, and efficient work.
- F&P Georgia strives to create a work culture that enhances teamwork, drives manufacturing excellence, and promotes product leadership.
- The work culture supports individual growth through skill enhancement, implementation of suggestions, and individual responsibility for quality.
