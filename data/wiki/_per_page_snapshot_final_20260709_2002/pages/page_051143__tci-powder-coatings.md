# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-01-14

**Source:** [TCI Powder Coatings Expands to Americus, GA - Coatings World](https://www.coatingsworld.com/issues/2016-04-01/view_breaking-news/tci-powder-coatings-expands-to-americus/)  ·  `page_051143`

## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings has announced the opening of a new office in Americus, GA.
- Doug Greene serves as President of TCI Powder Coatings.
- The new office will function as corporate headquarters for the business and house administrative, customer service, finance, and information systems departments.
- The addition allows the company to accommodate steady growth.
- Laboratory and training facilities are planned for the near future in the new facility.
- Sample product preparation and light warehousing will also be added to the new facility.
- This expansion is part of a multi-year strategy to improve capabilities and provide innovative, premium solutions and service to customers.
- The Americus-Sumter Payroll Development Authority assisted in making the new facility possible for TCI.
- TCI has been part of the community for 28 years.
- The company anticipates opening the office in February 2016 with approximately 20 employees.
- Additional functions will be added as growth allows.
- The facility is expected to accommodate up to 75 full-time employees.
- Barbara Grogan serves as Executive Director of the Payroll Development Authority.
- Grogan stated that supporting local and regional industry expansion is critical to Sumter County’s economic development strategy.
- Growth of long-term companies such as TCI Powder Coatings benefits Sumter County by hiring locally from the existing workforce.
- The Development Authority has partnered with TCI and will continue to support their expansion needs.
- TCI has been in the region for many years and maintains an outstanding reputation, providing a positive work environment for citizens and products used by many industries worldwide.
- The community is proud to be the new home of TCI's corporate headquarters.
