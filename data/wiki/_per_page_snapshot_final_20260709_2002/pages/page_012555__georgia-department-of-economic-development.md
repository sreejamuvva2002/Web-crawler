# Georgia Department of Economic Development

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2023-01

**Source:** [GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson Named ‘Georgian of the Year’ by Georgia Trend - Tifton CEO](https://tiftonceo.com/news/2023/01/gdecd-commissioner-pat-wilson-named-georgian-year-georgia-trend/)  ·  `page_012555`

## Facts

- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson was named the 2023 “Georgian of the Year” by Georgia Trend.
- The award recognizes Commissioner Wilson as the top salesperson for the state and commends his work promoting the advantages of Georgia to attract investments and create jobs.
- Former Governor Nathan Deal appointed Wilson as economic development Commissioner six years prior to January 2023.
- Georgia maintained its place as the No. 1 state in the nation to do business, earning the recognition for the ninth consecutive year in 2022.
- Former Georgia Governor Sonny Perdue credited Wilson with helping make Georgia one of the best places to grow a business by communicating the actions of the governor and legislature.
- In FY22, investments in expansions and new locations totaled more than $21.2 billion.
- The FY22 investments would create 51,132 direct new jobs in the state, setting new records for Georgia.
- GDEcD’s work providing solutions for companies and keeping communication open helped Georgia land two of its largest-ever investments in FY22.
- Lee Thomas, Director of the Georgia Film Office, was recognized by Georgia Trend for his contributions to the state.
- David Nuckolls, Executive Director of the Center of Innovation, was also recognized by Georgia Trend for his contributions to the state.
- GDEcD is described as the “quarterback” that brings partners across the state together to make business happen.
