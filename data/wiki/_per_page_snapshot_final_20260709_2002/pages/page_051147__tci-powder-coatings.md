# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-01

## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is located in Americus, Georgia.
- TCI Powder Coatings has maintained its Committed Badge from EcoVadis for a third consecutive assessment cycle.
- The EcoVadis framework evaluates 21 sustainability criteria across four core themes: environment, labor and human rights, ethics and sustainable procurement.
- Tom Whalen serves as vice president of commercial operations at TCI Powder Coatings.
- Tom Whalen stated the company is excited and proud to receive an EcoVadis sustainability badge for a third submission in a row, emphasizing that environmental impact is central to their innovation, service, and performance.
- The company lives out its "Your World Matters" commitment by demonstrating care for the planet.
- All of TCI's powder coating products are inherently VOC-free.
- TCI's research and development teams continue to work on formulations free of PFAs and TGIC.
- Technical service staff provide training and troubleshooting support aimed at reducing product use and waste in the field.
- All shipping boxes used by the company remain 100% recyclable.
- The company is continuing to implement digital initiatives across its business operations.
