## Facts

- PIOLAX, INC. is a manufacturer that specializes in developing, designing, and producing components utilizing its core elasticity technology.
- The company primarily serves the automotive industry, supplying original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) with a range of products including industrial fasteners, springs, and precision components.
- These parts are integral to vehicle fuel systems, drive systems, and opening/closing mechanisms.
- PIOLAX is a market leader, holding a top global share for air-dampers and a top domestic share for latches used in glove compartments.
- The company has diversified its expertise into other sectors, including the development of minimally invasive medical devices and products for the consumer and security markets.
