# Economic Development Around the State

**Type:** event  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2023-04-30

**Source:** [Economic Development Around the State - Georgia Trend Magazine](https://www.georgiatrend.com/2023/04/30/economic-development-around-the-state-73/)  ·  `page_048252`

## Facts

- Global auto parts manufacturer PHA is building a $67 million facility in Chatham County.
- The Tier 1 supplier will make door modules, tailgate latches and hood latches for the nearby Hyundai Metaplant in Bryan County.
- Operations are scheduled to start in 2024.
- The project will create 402 new jobs.
- Massachusetts-based Ascend Elements recently celebrated its grand opening in Covington.
- The 150,000-square-foot lithium-ion battery recycling facility can shred 30,000 metric tons of used batteries and scrap per year.
- The facility employs 100 people.
- It is the largest lithium-ion battery recycling plant in North America.
- Sustainable technology material manufacturer Hanwha Advanced Materials Georgia (HAGA) is investing $147 million to build a manufacturing facility in Cartersville.
- The project is the only facility in the U.S. to make encapsulant film used in solar cells for long-term durability.
- The facility will support the nearby Qcells solar panel facility.
- The project will create more than 160 new jobs.
- The facility is expected to be operational next summer.
