## Facts

- Mobis Alabama, LLC is known as Hyundai Mobis.
- It has become one of the largest Korean auto parts companies.
- Its operating areas include A/S parts sales, auto parts export, and module parts manufacture.
- Over the course of five years, Gray was chosen to design and build five separate projects for Mobis.
- The project location is Montgomery, AL.
- The total square footage of the completed projects is 966,985 s.f.
- Gray completed a 234,000 s.f. manufacturing plant for cockpit assemblies and automotive front and rear wheel chassis.
- Gray completed a 430,000 s.f. bumper and sub-assembly manufacturing plant for Hyundai automobiles.
- Gray completed a 94,878 s.f. distribution warehouse.
- Gray completed a 129,276 s.f. addition to the existing distribution warehouse.
- Gray completed a 78,831 s.f. E-coat painting facility.
