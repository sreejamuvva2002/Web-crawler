## Facts

- Fouts Fire is an American fire apparatus manufacturer located in Milledgeville, Georgia.
- The company was formerly known as Fouts Bros. Fire Equipment or Fouts Brothers.
- It has been family-owned and operated since 1952.
- Fouts Fire is a subsidiary of Fouts Bros Inc., a collection of commercial automotive equipment brands including Fouts Commercial Vehicles Inc., Smyrna Truck, Fouts Defense, Big Deal Parts, and Electric Commercial Vehicles Inc.
- In 2019, the company moved its headquarters and primary production facility from Smyrna, Georgia, to Milledgeville, Georgia.
- The company was founded in 1952 by CJ and Jerry Fouts as a single service station in Smyrna, Georgia.
- In 1989, the company opened its fire division with the introduction of the 3000 Gallon Tanker.
- The company expanded into a standardized line of fire tankers, pumpers, light rescues, and brush trucks.
- In 2024, the fire division was rebranded as Fouts Fire.
- The company is currently under third-generation family management.
- Fouts Fire is led by Scott Edens.
- The company operates out of a 700,000 square foot production facility and headquarters in Milledgeville, Georgia.
- The company maintains a second factory in La Grange, Texas.
- Fouts Fire builds pumpers, tankers, rescues, and quick attacks on both custom and commercial chassis.
- The company produces a limited, standardized line of apparatus to streamline production processes and costs.
- The business model relies on keeping stock units of all models readily available for fast delivery nationwide.
- Product models include the FB-94 pumper and the Fouts FOUR mini-pumper.
