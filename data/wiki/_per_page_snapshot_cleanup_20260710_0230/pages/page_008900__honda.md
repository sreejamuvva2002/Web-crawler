# Honda

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Honda operates a global manufacturing network spanning Japan, USA, Canada, Mexico, Brazil, Argentina, Peru, France, Italy, Spain, Africa & Middle East, Asia Oceania, Australia, and Taiwan.
- The Saitama Factory Automobile Plant is located in Yorii-machi, Osato-gun, Saitama, Japan, started production in July 2013, and has a production capacity of 250,000 units/year.
- The Saitama Factory Engine Plant is located in Ogawa-machi, Hiki-gun, Saitama, Japan, started production in April 2009, and has a production capacity of 250,000 units/year.
- Suzuka Factory in Suzuka-shi, Mie manufactures automobiles; No.1 Line started in April 1960, and the combined capacity for No.1+No.2 lines is 530,000 units/year.
- Honda Auto Body Co., Ltd. in Yokkaichi-shi, Mie was established in April 2018, started production in April 2018, and has a production capacity of 48,000 units/year.
- Kumamoto Factory in Ozu-machi, Kikuchi-gun, Kumamoto manufactures motorcycles and power products; the Motorcycle line started in January 1976 with a capacity of 320,000 units/year, and the Power Products line started in January 2002 with a capacity of 90,000 units/year for products and 30,000 units/year for ENG.
- The Outboard Engine Plant in Hamamatsu-shi, Shizuoka started production in August 2001 with a capacity of 80,000 units/year.
- Hamamatsu Factory in Hamamatsu-shi, Shizuoka manufactures automobile transmissions and started production in April 1954.
- Honda Development and Manufacturing of America, LLC was established in February 1978 and is located in Marysville, Ohio (Performance Manufacturing Center) and East Liberty.
- The Marysville Auto Plant started production in November 1982 with a capacity of 230,000 units/year, producing Accord, Acura TLX, Acura ILX, and CR-V.
- The East Liberty Auto Plant started production in December 1989 with a capacity of 230,000 units/year, producing CR-V, Acura MDX, and Acura RDX.
- The Performance Manufacturing Center started production in Spring 2016, producing Honda & Acura NSX, Acura TLX PMC Edition, and Acura MDX PMC Edition.
- The Anna Engine Plant started production in July 1985, producing L4/V6 Engines and CVT Pulleys.
- The Indiana Auto Plant produces Civic Sedan, CR-V, and Insight.
- Alabama Auto Plant No.1 started production in November 2001 with a capacity of 350,000 units/year, producing Odyssey and Ridgeline.
- Alabama Auto Plant No.2 started production in April 2004, producing Pilot and Passport.
- The Alabama Engine Plant started production in November 2001, producing V6 Engines.
- Honda operates a Transmission Plant-Georgia that manufactures automobile automatic transmissions.
- Honda operates a Transmission Plant-Ohio that manufactures automobile automatic transmissions and engine components.
- American Honda Motor Co., Inc. was established in April 1997, located in Timmonsville, South Carolina, started production in July 1998 with a capacity of 153,000 units/year, and manufactures ATVs and engines.
- Honda North Carolina Manufacturing was established in August 1983, located in Swepsonville, North Carolina; started production in August 1984 with a capacity of 790,000 units/year for products and 2 million units/year for ENG.
- Honda Aero, LLC is a subsidiary located in Burlington, USA, established in October 2004 for the production, research & development of manufacturing technology, and MRO of aero engines.
- Honda Aircraft Company, LLC is located in Greensboro, USA, established in October 2006 for the development, sales promotion, and production of aircraft.
- Honda Canada Inc. was established in September 1969, headquartered in Markham, Ontario with a factory in Alliston, Ontario; Plant 1 started in November 1986 (200,000 units/year), Plant 2 started in September 1998 (200,000 units/year), and the Engine Plant started in 2008.
- Honda de Mexico S.A. de C.V. was established in September 1985, located in El Salto, Jalisco; the El Salto plant started in November 1995 (178,000 units/year), the Power Products plant started in December 2015 (50,000 units/year), the Celaya Auto Plant started in February 2014 (200,000 units/year), and the Celaya Transmission Plant started in the second half of 2015.
- Honda Automoveis do Brasil Ltda. was established in May 1996, located in Itirapina, Sao Paulo, started production in 2019 with a capacity of 120,000 units/year.
- Moto Honda da Amazonia Ltda. in Manaus manufactures motorcycles and power products; the Motorcycle No.1 plant started in 1976, No.2 plant started in September 2009 (1.40 million units/year), and the Power Products line started in June 2001 (ENG 50,000 units/year, CBU 50,000 units/year).
- Honda Componentes Da Amazonia Ltda. in Manaus manufactures motorcycle parts and was established in January 1985.
- Honda Motor de Argentina S.A. was established in August 1978, located in Buenos Aires; the Motorcycle line started in June 2006 with a capacity of 155,000 units/year.
- Honda Selva del Peru S.A. was established in September 2006, located in Iquitos, Loreto, started production in October 2007 with a capacity of 35,000 units/year.
- HONDA FRANCE MANUFACTURING S.A.S. in Ormes manufactures power products; established in April 2008 (originally Honda Europe Power Equipment S.A.S in January 1986), started production in January 1986 with a capacity of 320,000 units/year for products.
- Honda Italia Industriale, S.P.A. in Atessa, Abruzzo manufactures motorcycles; established in September 1971, started production in 1976 with a capacity of 150,000 units/year.
- C.I.A.P. S.P.A. in Bologna manufactures motorcycle parts and was established in July 1988.
- Montesa Honda, S.A.U. in Barcelona manufactures motorcycles and after market parts for automobile and motorcycle and was established in July 1986.
- Honda Motorcycle Kenya Limited was established in March 2013, located in Nairobi; started production in October 2013 with a capacity of 35,000 units/year. Contact details include Tel: +254 (0) 701 87 6860 and Fax: +254 (0) 20 248 1973.
- Honda Automobile Western Africa Ltd. was established in April 2013, manufactures automobiles (Manufacture: Ogun, Sales: Lagos); started production in July 2015 with a capacity of 500 units/year.
- Honda Manufacturing (Nigeria) Ltd. manufactures motorcycles; No.1 Plant (Ota, Ogun State) started in November 1980 (1,590,000 units/year), No.2 Plant (Abuja) started in April 2023 (3,000 units/year), and No.3 Plant (Tokarawa, Kano State) started in April 2024 (5,000 units/year).
- Honda Manufacturing Ghana Ltd. was established in September 2022, located in Tema; started production in December 2023 with a capacity of 500 units/year.
- Dongfeng Honda Automobile Co., Ltd. in Wuhan, Hubei manufactures automobiles; established in July 2003; No.1 Plant started in April 2004 (240,000 units/year), No.3 Plant started in 2019 (240,000 units/year), and the New Energy Vehicle (NEV) Production Plant started in September 2024 (120,000 units/year).
- GAC Honda Automobile Co., Ltd. in Guangzhou, Guangdong manufactures automobiles; established in July 1998; HuangPu Factory started in March 1999 (240,000 units/year), ZengCheng Factory started in September 2006 (240,000 units/year), and the New Energy Vehicle (NEV) Production Plant started in December 2024 (120,000 units/year).
- Honda Power Products (China) Co., Ltd. in Chongqing manufactures power products; established in January 1993; Chongqing plant started in August 2002 with a capacity of 40,000 units/year for products and 1.84 million units/year for ENG.
- Sundiro Honda Motorcycle Co., Ltd. in Shanghai manufactures motorcycles; established in November 2001; Taicang plant started in August 2018 with a capacity of 795,000 units/year.
- Wuyang-Honda Motors (Guangzhou) Co., Ltd. manufactures motorcycles; established in August 1992; No.1 Plant started in August 1992 (1.0 million units/year), and No.2 Plant started in December 2014 (250,000 units/year).
- Dongfeng Honda Auto Parts Co., Ltd. in Guangdong, Huizhou manufactures engines parts and suspensions and was established in December 1994.
- GAC Honda Engine Co., Ltd. in Guangzhou, Guangdong manufactures automobile engines and was established in July 1998.
- Honda Auto Parts Manufacturing Co., Ltd. in Foshan, Guangdong manufactures automobile automatic transmissions and was established in September 2005.
- Honda Automobile (Thailand) Co.,Ltd. in Bangkok/Prachinburi/Ayutthaya manufactures automobiles and automotive parts; established in December 2000; Prachinburi Plant started in 2016 with a capacity of 120,000 units/year.
- Thai Honda Co., Ltd. in Bangkok manufactures motorcycles and power products; established in March 2021 (initial production Motorcycle May 1967, Power Products February 1987); Motorcycles started in May 1967 with a capacity of 1.67 million units/year, and Power Products started in February 1987 with a capacity of 230,000 units/year for products and 2 million units/year for ENG.
- Asian Parts Manufacturing Co., Ltd. in Ayutthaya manufactures after market parts for automobile and was established in April 2006.
- Bangladesh Honda Private Limited in Munshiganj manufactures motorcycles; established in December 2012; started production in October 2013 with a capacity of 120,000 units/year.
- Honda Cars India Ltd. in Tapukara, Rajasthan manufactures automobiles; established in December 1995; Tapukara Plant has a capacity of 180,000 units/year.

- Honda Malaysia Sdn Bhd. in Malacca manufactures automobiles; established in November 2000; No.1 started in October 2002 (46,000 units/year), and No.2 started in October 2013 (49,000 units/year).
- Honda Assembly (Malaysia) Sdn. Bhd. in Malacca manufactures automobile engines and was established in April 2013.
- Atlas Honda Limited in Karachi manufactures motorcycles; established in October 1962; Karachi Plant started in 1964 (210,000 units/year), and Sheikhupura Plant started in 1982 (1.33 million units/year).
- Honda Atlas Cars (Pakistan) Limited in Lahore manufactures automobiles; established in November 1992; started production in 1994 with a capacity of 55,000 units/year.
- Honda Philippines Inc. in Batangas manufactures motorcycles; established in June 1973 (current company December 1983); started production in 1973 with a capacity of 890,000 units/year.

- Honda Vietnam Co., Ltd. manufactures automobiles and motorcycles; established in March 1996; Motorcycle-No.1 Plant started in December 1997 (500,000 units/year), Motorcycle-No.2 Plant started in August 2008 (1.25 million units/year), Motorcycle-No.3 Plant started in March 2014 for parts and November 2014 for CBU (1 million units/year), and the Automobile plant started in July 2006 (35,000 units/year).
- Vietnam Auto Parts Co.,Ltd. in Minh Khai Village, Nhu Quynh Town, Van Lam District, Hung Yen Province manufactures motorcycle parts; established in October 2003; Motorcycle parts production started on January 1, 2004, and Cast wheel factory production started on December 1, 2009.
