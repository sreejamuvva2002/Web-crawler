## Facts

- PPG Industries Inc. is hiring for a Plant Operations Manager position at its Aerospace Transparency manufacturing site in Huntsville, Alabama.
- The role provides direction, leadership, and guidance to three operation managers, supervisors, and leads.
- The manager will manage manufacturing operations through the first line of supervisors/leads.
- The position involves leading plant safety initiatives with support from EHS, industrial, and Leadership Teams.
- The manager must promote a culture of safety and ensure stringent housekeeping, 5S standards, and safety procedures are followed.
- The role requires promoting quality procedures and driving adherence to the quality system and manufacturing process control.
- Operations will be monitored using a KPI-driven method to drive actions towards operational excellence.
- The manager will provide production updates on WIP level, lead time, and action items to support critical programs and AOG.
- Strategic objectives must be formulated and executed to achieve operations goals.
- Daily operations will be managed in compliance with monthly/weekly/daily goals to meet production schedules, quality standards, and SQCDT targets.
- The manager will lead critical daily/weekly rhythms including Tier3, Gemba Walks, safety reviews, 5S, Production meetings, Strategic planning meetings, and participate in daily Tier4 meetings (SQCDT).
- Responsibilities include the cost-effective use of resources in the production process, including overtime, to improve productivity.
- The role requires leadership and coordination of continuous improvement with support departments including Eng, Mfg, Qual, SC, EHS, OPEX, and indirect support departments.
- The manager will provide back-up support to the plant manager from a global operation perspective as needed by the business.
- The position involves creating a flexible workforce to manage forecast and budgets effectively.
- The manager must motivate, inspire, and maintain high employee morale and engagement to support continuous improvement and culture.
- Staff recruitment, coaching, and mentoring will be conducted to develop high-performance teams.
- Qualifications require a Master's degree or equivalent combination of education and experience.
- Candidates need proven successful work experience as a Plant Operation Manager or similar role.
- Proficiency in Excel for maintaining complex spreadsheets and mathematical skills sufficient to analyze production needs for resources, budget, and planning is required.
- Familiarity with business and financial principles and 2+ years of experience with budgeting and forecasting are necessary.
- Experience building strong working relationships at all levels inside and outside the organization is required.
- Lean / Six Sigma experience is needed.
- Candidates must be a US Citizen.
- The position requires onsite work in Huntsville, Alabama, reporting to the Plant Manager.
- The job type is listed as Full-Time.
- Eligible benefits include health benefits, 401K, paid vacation, and holidays.
- Benefits will be discussed with candidates by their recruiter during the hiring process.
- The listed salary range for the position is $17.00 - $25.00 per hour.
