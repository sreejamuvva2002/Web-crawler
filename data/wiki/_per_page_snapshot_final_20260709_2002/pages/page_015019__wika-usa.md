# WIKA USA

## Facts

- WIKA USA is a worldwide leader in industrial measurement technology.
- The company was established when WIKA was founded in 1946, using core values as standards to measure itself and its employees.
- WIKA USA operates its main campus in Lawrenceville, Georgia.
- The company has run a monthly peer-nominated Core Value Award program for two decades to acknowledge employee dedication.
- Award recipients receive a plaque, a monetary award, and a designated parking spot at the main WIKA USA campus.
- The seven core values recognized by the awards are Integrity, Customer Satisfaction, Partnership, Safety, Continuous Improvement, Social Responsibility, and Excellence.
- In March 2020, maintenance employees Peter Braun and Emmet Moore from the Pressure Gauge Division were recognized for Social Responsibility amid COVID-19 social distancing measures.
- Inside sales employee Eden Hailemicael from the Pressure Gauge Division was recognized for Excellence after preventing a fraudulent purchase order that would have cost the company $35,000 in product.
