## Facts

- Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG received an A- Climate Change Score from CDP.
- The rating underscores the company’s commitment to sustainability and environmental performance.
- In 2022, Kautex challenged its 30-plus plants worldwide to reduce their Scope 1 and 2 emissions through practical steps like energy audits and strategic investments in renewable energy.
- The company took significant steps to improve its own Scope 1 and 2 emissions and drive its suppliers to do the same.
- Kautex developed an application to assist customers in estimating the carbon impact of Kautex-designed products for product life cycle assessment.
- CDP enables companies to drive transparency in environmental reporting, track and benchmark progress, and uncover potential risks in sustainability strategies.
- Kautex operates as a Tier One automotive supplier with more than 30 plants in 14 countries.
- Kautex designs, develops, and manufactures traditional and hybrid fuel systems, advanced cleaning solutions for assisted and autonomous driving, engine camshafts, and plastic industrial packaging solutions.
- Kautex is a pioneer in the design and manufacture of automotive plastic fuel systems.
- Kautex is expanding its portfolio to offer smart products and data-driven services, including smart fuel systems, thermoplastic composite and composite-metal hybrid battery systems.
- Kautex offers lightweight battery systems, hybrid fuel systems, and autonomous drive vehicle cleaning systems as solutions for the era of new mobility.
