# Foresight Autonomous Holdings Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-06-29

**Source:** [Foresight Signs Agreement with Hitachi Astemo Americas](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/foresight-signs-agreement-with-hitachi-astemo-americas/)  ·  `page_008447`

## Facts

- Foresight Autonomous Holdings Ltd. is an innovator in automotive vision systems.
- Foresight signed an agreement with Hitachi Astemo Americas to test and evaluate autonomous-driving vision systems (ADAS).
- Hitachi Astemo is a global Tier One supplier of engine management, electric powertrain and integrated vehicle-control systems for the auto industry.
- The project with Hitachi Astemo is a paid joint proof of concept (POC) that calls for the evaluation and testing of simulated and real-life autonomous driving scenarios.
- The program will pair Foresight QuadSight and Mono2Stereo technologies with existing Hitachi Astemo camera systems to improve distance measurement and object detection.
- The project with Hitachi Astemo follows the successful evaluation of a QuadSight vision system prototype reported by the company in March 2021.
- Foresight’s proprietary Mono2Stereo software-based solution uses overlapping views of existing cameras with different fields of vision to create a three-dimensional stereo-vision system to improve object detection.
- Haim Siboni, CEO of Foresight, stated the company is excited to be involved in another auto industry POC project.
- Siboni noted that being chosen by Hitachi Astemo, a major supplier of automotive stereo vision systems, underlines the importance of stereoscopic technology in enhancing existing ADAS systems.
- The technology can amplify performance, provide better distance accuracy, and offer more robust active safety features.
- Foresight's solutions can be readily deployed in vehicles equipped with Level 2 and Level 2-plus autonomy systems without requiring additional hardware and design changes.
- Siboni believes teaming up with Hitachi Astemo could potentially lead to further collaboration with other automotive OEMs.
