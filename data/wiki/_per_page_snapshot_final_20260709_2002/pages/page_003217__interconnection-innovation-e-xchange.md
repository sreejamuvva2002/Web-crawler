# Interconnection Innovation e-Xchange

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-30

**Source:** [i2X Leadership Team | Department of Energy](https://www.energy.gov/cmei/i2x/i2x-leadership-team)  ·  `page_003217`

## Facts

- The Interconnection Innovation e-Xchange (i2X) is led by U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) staff from the Solar Energy Technologies Office (SETO), Wind Energy Technologies Office (WETO), and multiple national laboratories.
- The i2X team’s experience spans nearly all aspects of interconnection from residential to bulk power, utility to developer, and policy to engineering.
- SETO leadership for i2X includes Michele Boyd, Dexter Hendricks, and Ammar Qusaibaty.
- WETO leadership for i2X includes Jian Fu, Bret Barker, and Cynthia Bothwell.
- Grid Deployment Office leadership for i2X includes Avi Gopstein and Ariel Horowitz.
- Office of Electricity leadership for i2X includes Chris Irwin.
- Vehicle Technologies Office leadership for i2X includes Fernando Salcedo.
- Joint Office of Energy and Transportation leadership for i2X includes Sejal Shah.
- Pacific Northwest National Laboratory (PNNL) co-leadership for i2X includes Lara Aston and Diane Baldwin.
- Sandia National Laboratories (SNL) co-leadership for i2X includes Matt Reno and Mike Ropp.
- National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) co-leadership for i2X includes Jeff Cook and Robert Margolis.
