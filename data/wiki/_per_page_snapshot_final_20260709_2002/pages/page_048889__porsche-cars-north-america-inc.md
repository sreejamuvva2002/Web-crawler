# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-01-12

**Source:** [75k cars in our 75th year: Porsche reports record U.S. retail sales for 2023](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/75k-cars-75th-porsche-reports-142100435.html?fr=sycsrp_catchall)  ·  `page_048889`

## Facts

- PCNA retailed 75,415 new vehicles in the U.S. in 2023, setting a new record and representing a 7.6 percent increase versus the previous record set in 2022.
- Timo Resch serves as President and CEO of PCNA.
- Porsche ranked number one among luxury brands in the 2023 J.D. Power U.S. Sales Satisfaction Index Study.
- The 911 model sold 11,692 units in 2023, an increase of 14.6 percent versus 2022, marking the first time since 2007 that PCNA sold more than 11,000 911 models in a single year.
- The Macan set a new annual record with 26,947 deliveries in 2023, up 13.8 percent versus last year against a previous high of 24,716 set in 2021.
- Taycan sales grew by 4.1 percent to 7,570 cars in 2023, representing 10 percent of PCNA sales that year.
- The 718 model line was the fastest-growing in 2023 with an increase of nearly 30 percent to total 4,526 cars.
- Sales of the third-generation Cayenne drove past 20,000 vehicles for only the second time in PCNA history during 2023.
- Porsche Approved Certified Pre-Owned (CPO) sales in the U.S. totaled a record 38,788 cars, a 17.7 percent year-over-year increase.
- PCNA is headquartered at One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA, having been based in Atlanta since 1998.
- PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera and Taycan.
- The Atlanta campus is home to the first Porsche Experience Center in North America, featuring two module-based 1.6 mile driver development tracks, a business center, and Restaurant 356.
- The same Atlanta campus also houses the U.S. headquarters of Porsche Classic.
- PCNA operates a second Porsche Experience Center near Los Angeles with an eight-module educational track totaling 4.1 miles, a business center, Restaurant 917, and the headquarters of Porsche Motorsport North America.
- PCNA supports 198 independently owned and operated Porsche centers, two Porsche studios, and five satellite stores in the U.S., supplying parts, service, marketing, and training.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Porsche boasts a racing heritage with some 30,000-plus motorsport wins to date.
