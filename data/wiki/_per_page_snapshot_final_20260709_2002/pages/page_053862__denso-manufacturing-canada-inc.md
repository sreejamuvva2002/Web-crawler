# DENSO Manufacturing Canada, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-25

**Source:** [DENSO Manufacturing Canada Expands Support for 2026 Tour de Guelph as Co-Presenting Sponsor](https://ca.finance.yahoo.com/news/denso-manufacturing-canada-expands-support-171700315.html)  ·  `page_053862`

## Facts

- DENSO Manufacturing Canada, Inc. (DMCN) will co-sponsor the 2026 Tour de Guelph as a co-presenting sponsor.
- DMCN's partnership with the event traces back to a 2021 collaboration with the Guelph General Hospital that expanded into five years of participation.
- In 2024 and 2025, DMCN served as the event's Snack and Hydration sponsor.
- In 2025, DMCN increased its contributions by donating bicycles through its Build-a-Bike program.
- John Klassen serves as president of DMCN.
- DMCN was recently inducted into the Tour de Guelph Hall of Fame for its ongoing contributions.
- Julie Byczynski is CEO of The Foundation of Guelph General Hospital.
- The 2026 Tour de Guelph will take place on June 28 to raise funds for patient care and critical equipment at Guelph General Hospital.
- Funds from the event will support Phase Two expansion of Guelph General Hospital's Speedvale Campus, which will house endoscopy and fracture clinics.
- DENSO is globally headquartered in Kariya, Japan.
- DENSO is a leading mobility supplier with 7,540.0 billion yen (US$47.2 billion) in revenue that develops advanced technology and components for nearly every vehicle make and model on the road today.
- DENSO invests in around 170 facilities worldwide to produce cutting-edge electrification, powertrain, thermal and mobility electronics products.
- DENSO employs 155,000 global employees.
- DENSO spent around 9.2 percent of its global consolidated sales on research and development in the fiscal year ending March 31, 2026.
- In North America, DENSO is headquartered in Southfield, Michigan.
- DENSO employs 23,000+ team members across nearly 50 sites in the U.S., Canada and Mexico.
- In the fiscal year ending March 31, 2026, DENSO in North America generated $12.7 billion in consolidated sales.
