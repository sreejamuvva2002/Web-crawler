# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Superior Essex Continues Commitment To Sustainability, Partners With Copper Mark - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.ms/news/superior-essex-continues-commitment-to-sustainability-partners-with-copper-mark/)  ·  `page_010629`

## Facts

- On May 4, 2021, Superior Essex completed an agreement to become the eighth partner company of The Copper Mark.
- The Copper Mark is described as the only comprehensive social and environmental assurance program for the copper industry.
- The partnership aligns with Superior Essex's corporate sustainability goals and the newly announced Vision 2030 for the Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire brand to source raw materials used in its magnet wire products responsibly.
- Superior Essex Communications, a leading global manufacturer of communication cabling, has been a sustainability leader since 2011 and aims to reduce its product footprint by finding responsibly sourced materials through cooperation with copper suppliers.
- The Copper Mark is an assurance framework demonstrating responsible copper production practices and the industry's contribution to UN Sustainable Development Goals.
- The framework evaluates copper producers against a set of criteria for responsible production covering 32 distinct environmental, social, and governance issues related to mineral and metal sourcing.
- The Copper Mark aims to establish good management practices originating from voluntary sustainability standard requirements typically used in mineral and metal supply chains.
- Scott Wilde, Vice President of Global Strategic Sourcing at Superior Essex, stated that aligning responsible material sourcing is crucial for achieving company goals and meeting partner needs.
- CEO Brian Kim recently launched the Vision 2030 commitment, framing this partnership as a challenge to continue advancing green production and improving products from responsibly sourced materials.
- The commitment highlights how sourcing and sustainability efforts can run in parallel within the organization and externally.
- Many partners have shifted their sustainability values, ensuring Superior Essex can be part of their solutions.
- Being an early adopter of the Copper Mark highlights confidence in producing magnet wire and communication cables with a lower footprint.
- The Copper Mark evaluates copper mining, smelting, and refining management practices across categories including human rights, working conditions, community, environment, and overall governance, including transparency, disclosure, and business integrity.
- Company processes are measured against standards defined in the Risk Readiness Assessment made by the Responsible Minerals Initiative, which consists of over 380 cross-industry companies.
- Michèle Brülhart, Executive Director of The Copper Mark, stated that Superior Essex's commitment to sustainability and responsible copper production is key to the partnership.
- Annie Bevan, Global Sustainability Chief for Superior Essex, said having all parties issue a joint statement demonstrates the excellence of Vision 2030, The Copper Mark, and Superior Essex's future.
- Bevan noted that while far from the finish line, this is an extraordinary step toward Vision 2030 goals and hopes to continue reducing impact in products and the company for years to come.
- Superior Essex is a parent brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, and Essex Brownell.
- Superior Essex has over 3,000 employees across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is a leading global provider of magnet wire and at the forefront of fiber communication applications and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company plays a role in creating disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex is committed to pioneering smart building technology while also paving the way for continuity.
- Superior Essex headquarters address is 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
- Copper Mark was initially developed and funded by the International Copper Association (ICA) before being incorporated as an independent entity headquartered in the UK.
