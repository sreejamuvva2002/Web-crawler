## Facts

- Daesol Ausys Georgia has announced plans to open a new manufacturing plant in West Point, Georgia.
- The company specializes in motor and electric vehicle interior components and accessories.
- The company will invest $72 million into the project.
- The project will generate 140 new jobs.
- Production at the new facility is expected to begin in late 2024.
- The plant will produce luggage boards and luggage covers.
- The new manufacturing plant will be located in the Northwest Harris Business Park.
- Min Ho Kwon, CEO of Daesol Ausys, stated, “We’re committed to delivering world-class services that exceed our clients’ requirements and expectations. We are very excited about this project in the Northwest Harris Business Park and look forward to continuing the partnership with the State of Georgia and Harris County.”
