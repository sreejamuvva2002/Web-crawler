# Club Car

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-17

**Source:** [Columbia County industry announces major expansion - Augusta Business Daily](https://augustabusinessdaily.com/columbia-county-industry-announces-major-expansion/)  ·  `page_018429`

## Facts

- One of the largest employers in Columbia County has announced a major expansion.
- Club Car will be expanding into a 105,000-square-foot facility in the White Oak Industrial Park in Appling, Ga.
- The new facility will join Club Car’s existing facility that opened in the industrial park in 2019.
- Mark Wagner is president of Club Car.
- President Mark Wagner stated that the team is dedicated to providing the best vehicles and service for customers, and the new facility gives them the opportunity to extend operations in the United States.
- The new building will provide additional space for warehousing and manufacturing functions.
- Rick Evans is chair of the Board of Directors for the Development Authority of Columbia County.
- Chairman Rick Evans stated that Club Car has a long-standing relationship with Columbia County and continues to make investments to expand and manufacture world-class golf carts and utility vehicles in the county.
- Club Car was founded in Columbia County in 1958.
- Club Car is recognized as one of the global leaders in producing gas and electric-powered golf vehicles along with consumer and utility vehicles.
- Doug Duncan is chairman of the Columbia County Board of Commissioners.
- Chairman Doug Duncan stated that Club Car is a great community partner and expressed excitement and thankfulness for its continued growth in Columbia County.
- In addition to manufacturing and warehouse facilities in the White Oak Industrial Park, Club Car’s global headquarters are located on Washington Road.
- The Development Authority of Columbia County lists Club Car as the county’s second-largest employer.
- John Deere Commercial Products is the largest employer in Columbia County.
- Club Car expects the new facility in Appling will be complete by the end of 2023.
