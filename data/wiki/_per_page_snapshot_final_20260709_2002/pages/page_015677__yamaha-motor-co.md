# Yamaha Motor Co.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-03

**Source:** [Yamaha Motor Co. to move U.S. HQ to Georgia](https://www.ajc.com/business/2026/03/motorsports-and-boating-giant-to-move-us-hq-to-kennesaw/)  ·  `page_015677`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Co. announced it will relocate its U.S. headquarters to Kennesaw, Georgia.
- The Japanese company already operates a marine sports campus in Georgia.
- The relocation will occur in stages through 2028.
- The move will vacate Yamaha’s longtime corporate home near Los Angeles.
- After a half-century in California, the international motorsports company is moving its American home base to Georgia.
- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp used the occasion to tout the Peach State’s business-friendly leadership and policies.
- Kemp invited other companies to follow suit.
- Kemp stated: “This is another loud and clear testament to what we offer job creators from around the world.”
- Kemp added: “To any other California-based companies looking for a better home, we’ll give you plenty of reasons to keep Georgia on your mind.”
