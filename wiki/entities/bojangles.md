# Bojangles

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Charlotte, North Carolina, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Bojangles, a Southern quick-service restaurant chain, is partnering with Alyath and Smart Big Box to install turnkey electric vehicle (EV) charging stations at its restaurants nationwide.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Installation of level-2 and level-3 EV chargers at Bojangles locations expands public charging infrastructure for drivers in Georgia and across the U.S.

- Each Bojangles restaurant will have a minimum of four charging spots.
