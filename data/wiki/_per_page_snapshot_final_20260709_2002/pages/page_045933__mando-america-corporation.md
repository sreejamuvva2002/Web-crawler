# Mando America Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2018-10-02

**Source:** [Mando America Corporation to host job fair Oct. 17](https://www.lagrangenews.com/2018/10/02/mando-america-corporation-to-host-job-fair-oct-17/)  ·  `page_045933`

## Facts

- Georgia Department of Labor is partnering with Mando America Corporation to host a job fair.
- The job fair event will be held on Oct. 17 from 10 a.m. until 1 p.m.
- The event location is the Thomas Crossroads Goodwill Career Center at 3121 Hwy 34 East in Newnan.
- Mando America Corporation will be hiring for maintenance technicians, engineer technicians, machine operators, material handlers, casting and assembly production, quality auditors, environmental technicians, quality inspectors, melt operators, crane operators, core settlers, and refractory workers.
- Job seekers interested in attending may create an account on Employ Georgia to prepare and submit a résumé.
- Having an Employ Georgia account expedites the interview process for applicants.
- Applicants are encouraged to bring their résumés and driver's licenses to the event.
- Applicants are advised to dress business casual to improve their chances of being hired.
