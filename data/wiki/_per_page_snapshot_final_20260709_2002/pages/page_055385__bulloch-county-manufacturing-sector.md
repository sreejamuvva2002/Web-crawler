# Bulloch County Manufacturing Sector

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2016-04-19

**Source:** [Inside Bulloch Business with DeWayne Grice - Manufacturing key part of Bulloch economy - Statesboro Herald](https://www.statesboroherald.com/local/inside-bulloch-business-with-dewayne-grice-manufacturing-key-part-of-bulloch-economy/)  ·  `page_055385`

## Facts

- Local and state leaders set aside one week annually to recognize the impact manufacturing has on the Georgia economy.
- Bulloch County contains six manufacturing facilities: Braswell's, Briggs & Stratton, Brodie Meter, Great Dane Trailers, Loxscreen, and Viracon.
- These manufacturers directly employ 1,729 people in Bulloch County.
- The direct employment figure of 1,729 people is based on a study conducted by the Georgia Southern University Bureau of Business Research and Economic Development.
- Manufacturing is identified as a key part of the Bulloch County economy.
