# TI Fluid Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-15

**Source:** [TI Fluid Systems’ Regional Business Structure Completed as Julien Plenchette Joins the Company as President for the Americas - TIFS](https://tifluidsystems.com/tifs-plenchette-24/)  ·  `page_011630`

## Facts

- Julien Plenchette joined TI Fluid Systems as President – Americas, effective 08 April 2024.
- TI Fluid Systems is a leading global supplier of automotive fluid systems technology.
- Plenchette has over 20 years’ experience in the industry, including senior positions with other leading tier-one automotive suppliers such as ZF/Wabco, LS Automotive and Valeo.
- He was latterly Managing Director and Vice President of Aptiv’s Active Safety and User Experience Business Unit in North America.
- The appointment completes TI Fluid Systems’ regional leadership team, joining President for Asia Pacific, Henri Tsang, and President for Europe, Middle East, and Africa, Steve Vercauteren.
- The Group’s regional structure has been in place since 1 January 2024, improving alignment with customers, increasing flexibility to shift resources between product lines and creating synergy opportunities.
- The structure enhances regional leaders’ agility to respond to changes in their markets, particularly the transition to electrification which is occurring at different speeds in regions.
- Stefan Rau will focus on his role as Chief Operating Officer, leading the Group’s operational efficiency and productivity programmes to drive progress towards TI Fluid Systems’ mid-term targets and ensure long-term cost competitiveness.
- CEO and President Hans Dieltjens stated the company has built a strong, well-balanced leadership team with a broad range of skills and experience.
- Dieltjens thanked Stefan Rau for taking on the role of President for the Americas over the last few months in addition to his COO role.
- Dieltjens noted productivity and efficiency have always been in TI Fluid Systems’ DNA and they are well on track with implementing planned initiatives.
- Dieltjens highlighted their 'Taking-the-Turn strategy' is delivering and their propulsion agnostic portfolio means they are ideally positioned for the industry transition.
- TI Fluid Systems is a global innovator of thermal and fluid systems solutions for the full range of current and developing vehicle architectures.
- The company serves all major automotive manufacturers, with more than 100 years of automotive supply experience.
- TI Fluid Systems operates across 26 countries with a commitment to improving efficiency, performance and sustainability worldwide.
