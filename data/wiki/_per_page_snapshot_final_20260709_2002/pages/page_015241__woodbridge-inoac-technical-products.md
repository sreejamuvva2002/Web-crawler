## Facts

- WITP is a joint venture partnership between award-winning Woodbridge and INOAC.
- The company operates over 50 production and manufacturing facilities located in over 10 countries.
- WITP specializes in custom-engineered polyurethane (PUR) foam products and solutions for various industries.
- Their trademarked filter foams are designed for air and ceramic filter applications in industries including automotive, transportation, manufacturing, foundry, industrial water treatment, HVAC, medical, military, and more.
- WITP provides finished technical foam products used for padding, packaging, sealing, filtration, and custom design.
- The company works with customers to deliver exceptional foam products fit for use in consumer goods.
- Woodbridge INOAC Technical Products provides custom PU foam to meet specific team requirements.
