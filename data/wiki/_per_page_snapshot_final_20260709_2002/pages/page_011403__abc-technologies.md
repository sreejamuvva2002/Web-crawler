# ABC Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-29

**Source:** [ABC TECHNOLOGIES ANNOUNCES RECOMMENDED OFFER TO ACQUIRE TI FLUID SYSTEMS](https://www.prnewswire.co.uk/news-releases/abc-technologies-announces-recommended-offer-to-acquire-ti-fluid-systems-302318634.html)  ·  `page_011403`

## Facts

- ABC Technologies announced a recommended all-cash offer for the acquisition of the entire issued and to be issued ordinary share capital of TI Fluid Systems plc by ABC Technologies Acquisitions Limited.
- TI Fluid Systems is a London-listed, market-leading global manufacturer of thermal and fluid system solutions for the full range of current and developing vehicle architectures.
- TI Fluid Systems operates across 27 countries and serves all major automotive manufacturers.
- The combined group would create an industry leader with an extensive global product portfolio and financial strength to support long-term growth objectives.
- The transaction will provide access to a broader and more diversified range of customers, including some of the largest and most recognizable automotive OEMs and Tier One suppliers worldwide.
- Terry Campbell, President and CEO of ABC Technologies, stated that the transaction is a transformative strategic opportunity which unlocks value for all stakeholders and provides a platform for further growth.
- Under the terms of the transaction, shareholders of TI Fluid Systems will be entitled to receive 200 pence per share.
- The offer values TI Fluid Systems at an enterprise value of approximately £1,831 million.
- The acquisition is currently expected to complete in H1 2025, subject to shareholder and other relevant legal and regulatory approvals.
- Lazard acted as lead financial advisor to ABC Technologies for the transaction.
- Citi, TD Securities, and Scotiabank also acted as financial advisers on the deal.
- Kirkland & Ellis International acted as legal advisor to ABC Technologies.
- Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison acted as legal advisor in respect of regulatory and financing matters.
- ABC Technologies is a leading global manufacturer and supplier of custom, highly engineered, technical plastics, and light-weight innovations to the North American light vehicle industry.
- ABC Technologies serves more than 25 major original equipment manufacturer customers in 8 countries.
- ABC Technologies operates through a skilled workforce of over 11,000 team members.
- ABC Technologies is majority-owned by certain affiliated funds of Apollo Global Management, Inc. and its subsidiaries.
- Funds managed by Oaktree Capital Management, L.P. own a minority equity interest in ABC Technologies.
