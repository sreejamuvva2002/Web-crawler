# MOBIS

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-02

**Source:** [MOBIS breaks ground on $52 million automotive facility in Montgomery - WAKA 8](https://www.waka.com/2024/10/02/mobis-breaks-ground-on-52-million-automotive-facility-in-montgomery/)  ·  `page_046744`

## Facts

- MOBIS is a Tier 1 supplier to Hyundai and Kia.
- MOBIS has broken ground on a new project in Montgomery.
- The new facility will cost $52 million.
- The new facility will be 460,000 square feet.
- The facility will serve the aftermarket parts needs for both Hyundai and Kia’s national dealer networks.
- Graham & Co. Development, Cooper Construction, FIVESOUTH Architecture, Graham Capital and the Montgomery Area Chamber of Commerce hosted a groundbreaking to celebrate MOBIS’s expansion.
- The new facility is expected to create 27 jobs that will pay an average of $28 per hour.
- MOBIS already has more than 1,500 employees in the region.
- MOBIS has made a capital investment in Montgomery of $475 million since 2002.
- Hyundai operates an assembly plant in Montgomery that opened in 2005.
- Kia has an assembly plant just across the state line in West Point, Georgia, just off Interstate 85.
