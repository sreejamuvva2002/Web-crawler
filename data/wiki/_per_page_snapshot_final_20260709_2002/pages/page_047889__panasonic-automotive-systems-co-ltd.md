# Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-12-15

**Source:** [Panasonic Automotive Systems Unveils New Brand Reflecting Vision to Drive Future of Mobility](https://stockstreetnews.com/zz-quotemedia-topics-and-sectors/technology/panasonic-automotive-systems-unveils-new-brand-reflecting-vision-to-drive-future-of-mobility/)  ·  `page_047889`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd. is a premier supplier of automotive and mobility technologies for the world’s leading OEMs.
- The company announced it will change its name and brand to Mobitera to better represent its commitment to creating a safe, comfortable and sustainable mobility experience.
- The name and brand change will be effective on April 1, 2027.
- Masashi Nagayasu serves as the chief executive officer (CEO) of Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd.
- Nagayasu stated that Mobitera reflects their vision to power the future of mobility with high-quality technologies that elevate and enrich the human experience.
- He noted the organization is evolving to meet the needs of today's rapidly changing market while staying true to its core values.
- In December 2024, Panasonic Automotive Systems began a strategic partnership with the Apollo Group.
- The partnership aims to position the organization for accelerated growth by strengthening software development capabilities.
- The collaboration focuses on driving the evolution of the in-vehicle cockpit space and optimizing their overall business portfolio.
- The rebrand to Mobitera is positioned as the next step in positioning the organization for success in this pursuit.
- The new corporate name will be Mobitera Inc.
- The name Mobitera fuses mobility from their business domain, the Latin word iter (path), and the Japanese word terasu (to illuminate).
- These words express a wish to shine light upon the future.
- The central i in Mobitera highlights a vow to keep individuals the focus of all they do.
- The initial M symbolizes the aspiration to illuminate the future.
- The corporate color is Mobitera Blue-Green, developed to symbolize the harmonization of people, society, and the planet.
- The company's mission is to create a sustainable mobility society to promote a better life for everyone.
- The company's vision is to be the Joy in Motion design company.
- Domestic and overseas consolidated subsidiaries will also change their corporate names to align with the new Mobitera brand name, effective April 1, 2027.
- Panasonic ITS Co., Ltd. will become Mobitera ITS Inc.
- Panasonic Automotive Electronics Co., Ltd. will become Mobitera Solutions Inc.
- Panasonic System Networks Evaluation Technology Co., Ltd. will become Mobitera Evaluation Technologies Inc.
- Harima SANYO Industries Co., Ltd. will become Mobitera Wel Inc.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems America, LLC will become Mobitera America, LLC.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems de Mexico S.A. de C.V. will become Mobitera De Mexico, S.A de C.V.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Reynosa Mexico S.A. de C.V. will become Mobitera Reynosa Mexico, S.A de C.V.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Monterrey Mexico S.A. de C.V. will become Mobitera Monterrey Mexico, S.A. de C.V.
- Panasonic Automotive Sales Company de Mexico, S.A. de C.V. will become Mobitera Sales Company de Mexico, S.A. de C.V.
- Panasonic Automotive Shared Services Company Mexico, S.A. de C.V. will become Mobitera Shared Service Company Mexico, S.A. de C.V.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Europe GmbH will become Mobitera Europe GmbH.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Czech, s.r.o. will become Mobitera Czech, s.r.o.
- OpenSynergy GmbH will undergo no name change.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Asia Pacific Co., Ltd. will become Mobitera Asia Pacific Co., Ltd.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Malaysia Sdn. Bhd. will become Mobitera Malaysia Sdn. Bhd.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems India Pvt. Ltd. will become Mobitera India Pvt. Ltd.
- Details regarding Panasonic Automotive Systems (China) Co.,Ltd., Panasonic Automotive Systems (Dalian) Co., Ltd., and Panasonic Automotive Systems (Suzhou) Co., Ltd. will be announced on their website after completing internal procedures.
