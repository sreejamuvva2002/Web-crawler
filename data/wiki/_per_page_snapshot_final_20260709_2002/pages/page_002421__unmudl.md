## Facts

- Manufacturers know the cost of downtime but few calculate the cost of workforce decay.
- A weakening technician pipeline causes manufacturers to lose capacity, predictability, and profit.
- Signs of a weakening technician pipeline include more overtime, slower ramp-up, and rising rework rates.
- Over time, hidden inefficiencies from workforce decay can drain millions from annual output.
- Unmudl’s Technician Pipeline Health Check™ helps manufacturers quantify invisible losses before they hit the balance sheet.
- The Health Check connects people metrics (hiring speed, onboarding flow, retention) with operational impact (productivity, safety, uptime).
- Every unfilled technician role is a capacity constraint.
- Every delayed hire is lost throughput.
- Every turnover event is a restart cost.
- The Health Check converts these realities into an early-warning system to uncover where the technician ecosystem is leaking value and identify targeted interventions for double-digit ROI.
- Workforce risk has become a board-level concern, making technician health a financial leading indicator.
- Manufacturers who track technician health like a P&L line item will outlast those who treat it like a staffing statistic.
