# Continental Tire the Americas, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Continental annonce un changement de direction dans son activité de pneus de remplacement dans les Amériques](https://www.continental-tires.com/ca/fr/about-us/Salledepresse/Continental-annonce-un-changement-de-direction-dans-son-activit--de-pneus-de-remplacement-dans-les-Amriques/)  ·  `page_018869`

## Facts

- Continental announced a leadership change in its replacement tire business in the Americas on March 18, 2025.
- Dr. Jochen Etzel, President and CEO of Continental Tire the Americas, LLC, is retiring effective May 1, 2025.
- Tansu Işık will succeed Dr. Jochen Etzel as the new head of Continental's Replacement Tire sector for the Americas.
- Tansu Işık will report directly to Christian Koetz, a member of Continental's board of directors and head of Continental Tires.
- Jochen Etzel has been the cornerstone of Continental's tire business in the Americas since 2011.
- Etzel played a key role in unifying Continental's various tire activities in the region and strengthening their competitive position, contributing significantly to the company's overall growth and success.
- Etzel began his career as a lawyer in Continental's corporate law department, later served as a financial director overseeing tire business development outside Europe, and led Continental's M&A team.
- Christian Koetz stated that Etzel's impact on Continental's tire business was immeasurable and thanked him for his leadership and contributions.
- Tansu Işık brings over 20 years of international experience in Continental's tire business across various markets and functions, including marketing, supply chain, strategy, and key account management.
- Işık started his career as a business development specialist in Istanbul, Turkey.
- After holding various roles within the company, Işık joined Continental Tires headquarters in Hanover, Germany, as pricing manager for the EMEA region.
- Işık later moved to Shanghai, China, as director and then vice president of key account management (OE) for China and APAC.
- From 2017, Işık worked as marketing manager for Continental's replacement tire business in EMEA.
- He subsequently led the organization for strategic business development and global marketing at Continental Tires.
- Currently, Işık is responsible for fleet and trailer offtake in the South region and channels for Continental's replacement tire business in EMEA.
- In this role, he oversees commercial operations in several countries, leads Continental's truck tire activity in EMEA, and is responsible for retreading and digital solutions in the region.
- Işık holds an Executive MBA from Koç University and training from INSEAD, Duke University, and Ashridge University.
- Continental was founded in 1871 and generated sales of €39.7 billion in 2024, employing around 190,000 people in 55 countries.
- The Tires group sector generated preliminary sales of €13.9 billion in fiscal 2024.
- Continental's tire division employs more than 57,000 people worldwide and operates 20 production and 16 development sites globally.
- Continental Tire the Americas is located in Fort Mill, South Carolina, United States.
- Press contact for Continental Tires – The Americas is Allison Zora, Head of Brand and Public Relations, located at 1794 MacMillan Park Drive, Fort Mill, SC 29707.
