# Cartersville Solar Panel Facility

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[unknown|Uncategorized]]  ·  **Location:** Cartersville, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

A new factory in Cartersville, Georgia, will support Qcells' planned 3.3-gigawatt solar panel plant, the first U.S. facility to build silicon solar panels from raw materials.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The facility will produce silicon solar panels that can supply renewable electricity for electric vehicles.

## Investment

- **$147 million** (2023-03-23) — Invest in Cartersville facility to support Qcells' plant
