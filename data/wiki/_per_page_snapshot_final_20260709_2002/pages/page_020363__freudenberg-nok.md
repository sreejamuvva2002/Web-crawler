# Freudenberg-NOK

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Freudenberg-NOK Wins Hino Award for Product Quality | Quality Magazine](https://www.qualitymag.com/articles/86505-freudenberg-nok-wins-hino-award-for-product-quality)  ·  `page_020363`

## Facts

- Freudenberg-NOK's LaGrange and Cleveland, GA facilities have received a quality award from Hino Motor Manufacturing USA Inc, a part of the Toyota group of companies.
- The award was presented at Hino's annual Quality Excellent Recognition event on April 23.
- Freudenberg-NOK is a maker of elastomeric seals and custom molded products.
- Gary VanWambeke and Alban Silarbi serve as general managers for Freudenberg-NOK's Georgia facilities.
- The quality award is based on the number of parts per million defect as well as on-time delivery.
- Parts per million (ppm) serves as a measurement of good to bad product in the industry.
- Alban Silarbi oversees the LaGrange operation.
- Freudenberg-NOK's Georgia facilities are ISO 9000, QS 9000/2000, TS 16949 and AS 9100 certified.
- The Georgia facilities manufacture elastomer products for several industries, ranging from automotive to aerospace.
- Freudenberg-NOK has been supplying seals to Hino for three years.
- The oil seal and o-rings used by Hino ultimately are placed in the Toyota Tundra.
