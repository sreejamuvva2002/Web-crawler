# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-02-13

**Source:** [Rivian introduces new entry-level battery options - electrive.com](https://www.electrive.com/2024/02/13/rivian-offers-new-entry-level-battery-options/)  ·  `page_059102`

## Facts

- Previously, Rivian offered two battery options: Large with 135 kWh and Max with 149 kWh.
- Rivian has introduced two new entry-level battery options called Standard and Standard+.
- The Standard battery pack offers 106 kWh of capacity with a range of up to 270 miles (435 kilometres).
- The Standard+ battery pack offers 121 kWh of capacity with a range of up to 315 miles (507 kilometres).
- The published range figures are Rivian's values and not official EPA figures.
- The entry-level price for both EVs has been reduced by about $3,100 due to the new battery options.
- The Rivian R1T pickup truck starts at $69,900 with a dual-motor AWD and the Standard battery pack.
- The Rivian R1T pickup truck costs $73,000 with the Standard+ battery pack.
- The Rivian R1S electric SUV now starts at $84,000.
- Customers can choose the “Performance Dual-Motor AWD” option for an extra $5,000 (plus $3,100) when selecting the Standard+ battery pack, though this option is not available for the Standard variant.
- Rivian recently started offering the R1S for leasing in the US, allowing customers to benefit from the full $7,500 tax credit which is unavailable when purchasing the vehicle.
- The US carmaker sold 57,232 electric vehicles in 2023, exceeding its revised annual target of 54,000 units.
- Rivian currently builds the R1T Pickup, the R1S SUV, and the Electric Delivery Van (EDV) for Amazon on a separate production line.
- Rivian received final approval from relevant authorities in November 2023 to build its second plant.
- The new factory in Georgia will be able to produce 200,000 electric vehicles per year in its first expansion stage and is scheduled to go into operation in 2024.
- An expansion of the Georgia plant to 400,000 electric vehicles per year will be completed by 2030.
