## Facts

- SungEel Hitech has a recycling capacity of 50,000 tonnes.
- SungEel Hitech utilizes hydrometallurgical technology with electrolyte solution treatment to ensure an eco-friendly process.
- SungEel Hitech is increasing its presence in the US by building a mega-facility in the state of Georgia.
- The new facility in Georgia is named SungEel Recycling Park Georgia.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Korean Lithium-Ion Battery Recycler SungEel HiTech will build the first U.S. recycling facility, SungEel Recycling Park Georgia.
- SK Innovation and SungEel HiTech formed a battery-recycling joint venture.
