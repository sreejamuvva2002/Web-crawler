# The Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-05

**Source:** [GOODYEAR EDS TIRE WINS 2025 TIRE TECHNOLOGY INTERNATIONAL AWARDS-Grand View Hotel Tianjin-Tourism News](https://grandviewtianjin.cn/newsdetail-817626.html)  ·  `page_055143`

## Facts

- Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company's ElectricDrive Sustainable-Material (EDS) tire won the Environmental Achievement of the Year – Tire Design award at the 2025 Tire Technology International (TTI) Awards for Innovation and Excellence.
- The award was presented at the Tire Technology Expo held at Deutsche Messe in Hannover, Germany.
- Matt Ross, editor-in-chief of TTI and chairman of the Tire Technology Awards for Innovation and Excellence, stated that judges were particularly impressed by Goodyear's EDS tire for its unique combination of sustainability and tire design.
- The EDS tire was unveiled at the 7th China International Import Expo (CIIE).
- The EDS tire represents a major step in Goodyear's pursuit of developing the industry's first 100% sustainable-material tire by 2030.
- It is China's first ISCC mark retail tire with over 70% sustainable materials and is engineered for electric vehicles.
- Nathaniel Madarang, Goodyear Asia Pacific President, stated that the EDS tire provides EV drivers an excellent option of high-performing tires with ISCC-certified sustainable materials exceeding 70%.
- Goodyear is committed to introducing the industry's first 100% sustainable-material tire by 2030.
- Goodyear's Better Future framework guides its strategic vision to be #1 in tires and service in Luxury, SUV and EV across Asia Pacific.
- The EDS tire is designed to meet the growing demand for sustainable and high-performing tires in the electric vehicle sector.
- Engineered for the unique demands of EVs, the EDS tire delivers superior wet braking, enhanced handling, lower rolling resistance, and reduced noise.
- Goodyear has integrated corporate responsibility into its company culture through its Corporate Responsibility framework, known as Goodyear Better Future.
- The framework's pillars include Sustainable Sourcing, Responsible Operations, Advanced Mobility, and Inspiring Culture.
- Goodyear employs about 68,000 people and manufactures its products in 53 facilities in 20 countries around the world.
- Goodyear operates two Innovation Centers in Akron, Ohio, and Colmar-Berg, Luxembourg.
