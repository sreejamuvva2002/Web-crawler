# Nifco KTW

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Nifco KTW is a global supplier of high-quality interior and exterior injection molded components for the automotive industry.
- Nifco KTW contracted with Evans for the design-build construction of their U.S. Headquarters in Toccoa, Georgia.
- The facility is a 154,000 sf pre-engineered metal building.
- The building contains an injection molding bay.
- The building includes assembly and warehouse areas.
- The building features a high-gloss paint booth.
- The building includes 10,000 sf of administrative space.
- The state-of-the-art production area contains two 35-ton bridge cranes.
- Manufacturing processes at the facility include 2-component injection molding.
- Manufacturing processes at the facility include TSG-physical foaming.
- Manufacturing processes at the facility include variotherm.
- Manufacturing processes at the facility include automatic assembly.
- The project scope included design-build services, shell building construction, equipment installation, tenant build-out, and a comprehensive site work package.
