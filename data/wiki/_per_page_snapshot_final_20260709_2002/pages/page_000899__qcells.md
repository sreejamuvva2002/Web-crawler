# Qcells

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [LG Energy Solution Vertech and Qcells Partner to Deliver 5GWh of U.S. Energy Storage Projects – LG Energy Solution](https://news.lgensol.com/company-news/press-releases/4563/)  ·  `page_000899`

## Facts

- LG Energy Solution Vertech and Qcells announced a multi-year commitment to install reliable, affordable, American-made storage products for Qcells’ development projects across the country.
- The agreement provides American-made energy storage projects across a portfolio of utility-scale sites.
- Projects are scheduled for 2028-2030.
- All projects will meet U.S. domestic content requirements.
- The agreement includes 5GWh of lithium-ion energy storage projects for utility-scale projects.
- The agreement includes lifecycle services from LG Energy Solution Vertech.
- This agreement supports Qcells EPC projects scheduled from 2028 – 2030.
- In May 2024, Qcells entered into battery purchase agreements with LG Energy Solution Vertech totaling 4.8 GWh of BESS projects developed across the U.S.
- The partnership secures energy storage systems for Qcells that meet domestic content requirements.
- LG Energy Solution’s battery energy storage products are made in Michigan.
- Qcells’ solar panels are made in Georgia.
- Leveraging made-in-America products supports U.S. manufacturing jobs, strengthens the nation’s clean-energy supply chains, enhances grid reliability, and reduces exposure to trade risks and tariffs.
- Chris Hodrick is CEO of Qcells EPC.
- Qcells operates the largest solar panel manufacturing facility of its kind in the United States, based in Dalton, Georgia.
- The company is building a second factory in Cartersville, Georgia.
- The Cartersville factory will manufacture ingots, wafers, cells and finished panels.
- This second factory represents the largest investment in U.S. solar history to create an integrated, sustainable domestic supply chain.
- Once fully operational, Qcells’ annual solar panel production capacity in the US will reach 8.4 gigawatts.
- This capacity equals nearly 46,000 panels every day.
- The production is enough to power more than 1.3 million American homes each year.
- Jaehong Park is CEO and President at LG Energy Solution Vertech.
- The energy storage systems included in this commitment are built using the latest LG Energy Solution containerized lithium-ion battery solutions with LFP cells manufactured at U.S. facilities.
- LG Energy Solution is manufacturing up to 16.5 GWh of battery energy storage cells in Holland, MI.
- LG Energy Solution's production is set to ramp up to around 50GWh at sites across North America by the end of 2026.
