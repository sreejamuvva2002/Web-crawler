## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America (PASA) is an industry-leading global supplier to Automotive Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM’s) around the world for automotive infotainment systems and advanced connectivity.
- PASA's client list includes Ford, GM, Chrysler, Daimler, Fiat, Tesla, Honda, Toyota, Nissan and many others.
- PASA has been a trusted partner to the Toyota Motor Corporation for over 70 years.
- PASA collaborated on landmark programs including the recently integrated next-generation 2026 RAV4 infotainment system.
- PASA's global engineering teams worked to perfect the response times and interface for the 2026 RAV4 infotainment system.
- PASA contributed to bringing the 2027 INFINITI QX65 to life.
- PASA engineered every speaker placement and acoustic detail for the Klipsch Premium Audio system in the vehicle cabin.
- Toyota USA recognized PASA with four supplier awards at their Annual Business Meeting: Excellent Technology & Development Award, TEAM (Total Exceptional Achievement for Manufacturing) Award, Excellent Value Improvement Award, and Excellent VA (Value Analysis) Achievement Award.
- Kevin Ostertag serves as the President of Panasonic Automotive Systems America.
- Kevin Ostertag was named President of Panasonic Automotive Systems America, LLC, effective April 1, 2026.
- Scott Kirchner previously served as both President of PASA and Global Chief Operating Officer (COO) of Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd (PAS).
- PASA maintains operational teams in Farmington Hills and Peachtree City.
- The Peachtree City facility is located at postal code 30269, GA.
