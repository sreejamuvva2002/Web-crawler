## Facts

- Pak-Lite, Inc. is based in Commerce, Ga.
- Pak-Lite announced the addition of DuBoise White as president and CEO.
- DuBoise will oversee go-to-market strategies, business development and ongoing process improvements to grow Pak-Lite's presence in the market.
- David Pritchard serves as chairman of the board for Pak-Lite.
- Pritchard remarked that DuBoise is a great fit whose business development skills will help Pak-Lite enhance partnerships, create strategic alliances and grow its presence.
- DuBoise most recently led Glass Enterprises, a producer of materials for the luxury goods market.
- DuBoise previously founded TerraHaven Holdings, a consultancy that supported agriculture and energy clients.
- DuBoise's early career included multiple assignments within Golden Peanut and Texas Industries.
- DuBoise earned an MS in Agricultural Economics from Texas A&M University.
- DuBoise earned an MBA from Southern Methodist University.
- DuBoise earned a BS is Agricultural Economics from The University of Georgia.
