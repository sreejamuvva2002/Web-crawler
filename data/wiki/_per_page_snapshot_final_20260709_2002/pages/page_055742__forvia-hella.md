## Facts

- FORVIA HELLA is a listed, international automotive supplier.
- The company is part of the FORVIA Group, which is the world's seventh largest supplier of automotive technologies.
- In the USA, FORVIA HELLA has over 650 employees located across three sites.
- As of 31 December 2024, the company reported a total of 650 employees.
- The company reported €8.1 billion in sales for Fiscal Year 2024.
- FORVIA HELLA's US operations are home to more than 650 employees across three strategic locations driving innovation across core business groups: Electronics, Lighting, and Lifecycle Solutions.
- The company's high-resolution headlamp system is being integrated into the new Zeekr 8X.
- The company provides robust, efficient LED lighting technology with high-boost mode and welcome and goodbye functions.
- Currency-adjusted sales in the US remains at the prior-year level of €2.0 billion.
- Negative exchange rate effects caused sales to decline by 2.9 percent to €1.9 billion.
- Since its foundation as a small lamp factory in 1899, FORVIA HELLA has become a leading supplier of vehicle lighting, including headlamps and rear combination lamps as well as car body and interior lighting.
- Development activities currently focus on features and technologies expected to be especially important in the future, such as light-based driver assistance systems, LEDs and design elements.
- The number and complexity of electronic components used in vehicles has increased tremendously over the past few years.
- FORVIA HELLA is an expert supplier of all kinds of vehicle electronic solutions, ranging from complex body electronics such as central control units and access systems to various electronic components including sensors and vacuum pumps.
- The company is active in the rapidly growing energy management and driver assistance systems segments, offering a wide range of innovations.
- Within the Lifecycle Solutions Business Group, the company develops, produces and distributes products for the independent parts trade and for workshops.
- The company serves target groups including agricultural and construction machinery manufacturers, municipalities and small series manufacturers with innovative lighting and electronic products.
- The systematic transfer and appropriate application of technology, process and quality know-how from the automotive sector form the basis for high competitiveness in innovation and costs.
