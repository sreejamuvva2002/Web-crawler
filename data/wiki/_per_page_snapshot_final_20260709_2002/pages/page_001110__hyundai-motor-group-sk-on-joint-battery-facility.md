# Hyundai Motor Group & SK On Joint Battery Facility

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-09

**Source:** [Hyundai Announces Major EV Battery Facility To Be Built In America](https://carbuzz.com/news/hyundai-announces-major-battery-facility-to-be-built-in-america/)  ·  `page_001110`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group has officially announced it has inked a deal with the state of Georgia and battery manufacturer SK On to build a new electric vehicle battery production plant.
- The project is one of Georgia's most significant economic development projects in its history.
- It will create an estimated 3,500 new jobs.
- It involves approximately $4-$5 billion worth of investment in Bartow County.
- Bartow County is located about an hour northwest of Atlanta.
- The announcement comes only a couple of weeks after Hyundai and SK On signed a memorandum of understanding (MOU) outlining their strategic partnership.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated: "Hyundai Motor Group and SK On are valued partners and key players in our state's ever-growing automotive industry."
- Governor Kemp added: "Since day one, my administration has been focused on bringing jobs and opportunity to communities across the state that may have been overlooked in the past. SK and HMG share this goal, and we're proud they are choosing to invest even further in this No. 1 state for business."
- Specific details of the companies' partnership are still being ironed out, but the project is definitely happening.
- The new facility will be located at Bartow Centre, a manufacturing and industrial site on Highway 411.
- Construction should be getting underway relatively soon because operations are set to begin in 2025.
- SK On is a subsidiary of South Korea's SK Innovation.
- SK On was established in 2021.
- SK On already employs over 2,000 Georgia residents at an existing facility in Commerce.
- Last summer, SK Battery America announced a partnership with Work for Warriors Georgia, a program dedicated to hiring veterans, service members, and their families.
- Hyundai has been very aggressive with its EV lineup, most recently with unveiling the Ioniq 6 GT at last month's LA Auto Show.
- Hyundai Motor Group includes the Kia and Genesis brands.
- Hyundai has a massive vehicle production plant located in nearby Alabama.
- Along with this new battery plant, Hyundai announced last May plans to invest $5.54 billion in a separate and also new dedicated EV manufacturing facility in Bryan County, Georgia.
- The automaker broke ground on the Bryan County facility in October.
- All told, Hyundai's EV-related projects announced since 2020 amount to nearly $17 billion in investment and over 22,800 new jobs in the state.
