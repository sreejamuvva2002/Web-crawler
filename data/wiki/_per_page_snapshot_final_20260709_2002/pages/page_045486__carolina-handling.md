# Carolina Handling

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-25

**Source:** [Carolina Handling Wins Kautex Safety Award](https://www.carolinahandling.com/news/2024/wins-kautex-safety-award)  ·  `page_045486`

## Facts

- Carolina Handling received the 2023 Health & Safety Award for Excellence from Kautex, a Textron Company.
- The award was received for an Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) project at the company’s facility in Lavonia, Georgia.
- The award recognizes Carolina Handling as a top-performing supplier.
- The AGV courier implementation was one of the smoothest among any of Kautex Textron’s equipment distributors worldwide.
- Kautex Textron operates Automated Guided Vehicles at most of its facilities around the globe.
- Carolina Handling is one of the Southeast’s leading integrated material handling solutions providers.
- The company serves as the exclusive Raymond Solutions and Support Center for North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Alabama, and Florida’s Central time zone.
- Carolina Handling provides a full range of lift trucks and automation solutions for manufacturers, warehouses, and distribution centers.
- The company also supplies parts, warehouse equipment, and supplies.
- Kautex Textron is a Tier One global automotive supplier that designs, develops, and manufactures traditional and hybrid fuel systems, advanced cleaning solutions for assisted and autonomous driving, engine camshafts, and plastic industrial packaging solutions.
- Carolina Handling was founded in 1966.
- The company is headquartered in Charlotte, North Carolina.
- Carolina Handling operates branch offices in Atlanta, Georgia; Birmingham, Alabama; Greensboro, North Carolina; Raleigh, North Carolina; and Greenville, South Carolina.
- The company operates an Equipment Distribution Center in Piedmont, South Carolina.
- Carolina Handling has 800 associates.
