# Archer Aviation Inc.

## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. received the certificate of occupancy for its high-volume manufacturing facility, ARC.
- ARC is connected to the Covington Municipal Airport in Georgia.
- Archer is now working to complete the tooling load-in for the initial manufacturing line at this facility.
- Production of its all-electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft, Midnight, is planned to begin in early 2025.
- The goal is to ramp to a rate of two aircraft per month by the end of the year.
- Archer built ARC in close partnership with Stellantis.
- Archer and Stellantis are continuing to progress their plans to mass-produce Archer’s Midnight aircraft.
- Stellantis is contributing capital, advanced manufacturing technology, and expertise and experienced personnel.
- The goal is to scale the facility to 650 aircraft annually by 2030.
