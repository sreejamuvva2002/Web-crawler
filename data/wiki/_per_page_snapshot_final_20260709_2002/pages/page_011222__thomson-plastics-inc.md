## Facts

- Thomson Plastics, Inc. is a plastics manufacturing company located in Georgia, United States with 51-200 employees.
- Established in 1975, Thomson Plastics, Inc. (TPI) has developed processes that integrate the complexity of design with steady and reliable production.
- The company serves a broad range of markets by providing its expertise in logistics management, quality control and just-in-time delivery to every project.
- TPI has three facilities located in Thomson, GA., Howell, MI, and Lexington, NC.
- Plastic injection molding capacity ranges from 60 tons to 3,150 tons.
