# Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-10-14

**Source:** [Hitachi Astemo Expands Aftermarket Product Line, Just in Time for AAPEX | News & Events | Astemo Americas | Aftermarket](https://www.aftermarket.astemo.com/americas/en/news/2022/1014_002164.html)  ·  `page_008475`

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc. is one of the largest manufacturers of Original Equipment (OE) quality aftermarket automotive products.
- The company showcased 230 brand new stock keeping units (SKUs) at AAPEX.
- Product coverage additions include electronic throttle bodies, ignition coils, engine crankshaft and camshaft position sensors, timing control covers, variable timing solenoids, and Tokico shock absorbers lines.
- Hitachi Astemo has been adding products throughout 2022 with additional parts ready to ship in October 2022.
- The company spotlighted its growing powersport lines: KEIHIN, NISSIN, and SHOWA.
- Hannah Hua, North American marketing manager at Hitachi Astemo Americas, stated the team is ready to feature newest throttle bodies, ignition coils, camshaft and crankshaft sensors, and shock absorbers at AAPEX.
- The latest products carry on the company's tradition of delivering premium, OE-quality parts for dealers and their customers.
- All Hitachi Astemo products have applications for Asian, Domestic, and European vehicles and cover millions of US vehicles in operation.
- Sample new SKUs added include 115 Camshaft and Crankshaft Sensors, 27 Electronic Throttle Bodies, 23 Ignition Coils, and 51 Shock Absorbers.
- Hannah Hua noted that AAPEX is one of the company's favorite events and they are ready to discuss new products with clients and potential retail partners at booth 5045.
- Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc. manufactures and markets engine management, electric powertrain, and integrated vehicle controls for major automotive manufacturers worldwide.
- The company adheres to ISO/TS:16949:2009 industry standards.
