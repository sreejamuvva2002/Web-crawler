# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Bonnell Aluminum to Expand Anodizing Capacity | 2014-12-19  | PCI Magazine](https://www.pcimag.com/articles/99983-bonnell-aluminum-to-expand-anodizing-capacity)  ·  `page_053032`

## Facts

- Tredegar Corp.’s subsidiary Bonnell Aluminum plans to expand and upgrade its anodizing capacity to serve the commercial-architectural construction market.
- The company expects to invest approximately $5 million to expand and modernize the anodizing line at its Carthage, TN, facility.
- The upgraded line is planned to be fully operational in the second quarter of 2015.
- The expansion will increase the plant’s anodizing capacity by an additional 35%.
- Ira Endres, Vice President Sales and Marketing, commented that the upgrade will increase capacity to better service customers’ anodizing requirements while enhancing quality and consistency.
- Brook Hamilton, President of Bonnell Aluminum, added that the company’s 2012 acquisition of AACOA increased its expertise in anodizing processes and technology.
- The project will benefit from the added knowledge from the AACOA acquisition and raise the bar for anodizing quality and consistency in the commercial-architectural construction market.
