# Forward Forsyth (Forsyth County Economic Development)

## Facts

- Forward Forsyth works to support companies that choose Forsyth County as their home.
- Forward Forsyth offers a multi-pronged business development approach providing infrastructure resources, incentive programs, key data, and expertise.
- Almost 9,000 businesses already call Forsyth County (FOCO) home.
- Forward Forsyth provides specific industry support plans for tech, health care, and manufacturing businesses.
- Forsyth County utilizes local, state, international, and green incentives to intentionally create a business-friendly environment that attracts companies and helps them thrive.
- The Forsyth County Chamber of Commerce strives to provide small, existing, and emerging businesses with resources to succeed and serves as a forum for collaboration on economic growth issues.
- Forsyth County offers a wide range of incentives and programs to help businesses locate or expand in the county.
- Forward Forsyth provides a supportive team of professionals to help companies launch, move, or grow their business.
