## Facts

- The company holds a registered trademark (Registration Number 2773139) for the word mark "HOLLINGSWORTH & VOSE COMPANY".
- The company's goods and services include technical and industrial papers and nonwovens made of natural and/or synthetic fibers for use as air, fuel, water, oil, fluid, dust, airborne particle and exhaust filters.
- The company manufactures battery separators.
- The company produces gaskets for vehicular and industrial sealing.
- The company provides nonwoven materials for use as gaskets for vehicular and industrial sealing and as thermal and acoustical insulation.
- The company offers nonwoven filter fabric, chemically and thermally bonded fabric for industrial and labelling use, soft cutaway embroidery backing, and nonwoven wallcovering materials.
