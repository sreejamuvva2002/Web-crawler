# Lund International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2006-02-07

**Source:** [Lund International Realigns Sales Structure; Plans to Open Corporate Distribution Center - AftermarketNews](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/lund-international-realigns-sales-structure-plans-to-open-corporate-distribution-center/)  ·  `page_045648`

## Facts

- Lund International announced an internal restructuring within its aftermarket sales team to realign assets and increase customer satisfaction.
- Eric Medves was promoted to director of channel management, WD, responsible for developing and implementing growth sales strategy.
- Vern Kaufman serves all Lund International brands as a WD sales director covering the Rocky Mountains, Southwest, South, and Southeast territories.
- Joe Zeiser continues as a WD sales director focusing specifically on the Keystone/Reliable account.
- Rick Engleman continues as director of channel management with additional focus on mail order, Internet, power sports, and sporting goods channels.
- Mark Greffet continues as a sales director focusing on automotive retail and programmed distribution channels.
- Bob Schuetz continues as VP of aftermarket sales, directing growth strategies for all Lund International brands except OE.
- Brian Wiland, Mike Stevens, and Daryl Lundberg continue as sales directors covering the WD channel, automotive retailers, and mail order/sporting goods channels respectively.
- Bob Cobb continues as a director of channel management responsible for specialty channels including hardware, home center, tool warehouses, and farm/fleet accounts.
- Lund International announced plans to establish a corporate distribution center.
- The company plans to relocate its corporate headquarters to the new location in April.
- Lund International entered into a long-term lease for a 356,000-square-foot facility in Suwanee, GA.
- The new facility is located approximately seven miles from the Ventshade Accessories Division, the company's largest manufacturing operation.
- The company will relocate its Lund Accessories Division from Lawrenceville, GA to the new facility.
- Georgia-based corporate and sales and marketing staff will move to the new facility.
- The new facility is being designed to allow for future growth of the company.
