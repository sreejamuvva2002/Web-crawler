# Lund International Holding Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [​Truck Hero, Inc. Announces Strategic Acquisition of Lund International - Industry News - Magazine - Movin' Out](https://www.movinout.com/magazine/article/truck-hero-inc-announces-strategic-acquisition-of-lund-international-1727.php)  ·  `page_045595`

## Facts

- Truck Hero, Inc. announced the strategic acquisition of Lund International Holding Company.
- Lund is a portfolio company of Highlander Partners, LP.
- Lund is based in Buford, Georgia.
- Lund is a leading designer, manufacturer, and marketer of branded automotive accessories for passenger cars, light trucks, SUVs, CUVs, Jeeps, and heavy trucks.
- Transaction terms were not disclosed.
- The acquisition adds a complementary product portfolio to diversify Truck Hero's product category mix and increases exposure to a larger addressable market of vehicles.
- Lund International was founded in 1965, with roots dating back to the 1920s.
- Lund started in Anoka, Minnesota before expanding operations.
- Lund offers highly functional, protective, and stylish products in over 30 categories under brands including AMP Research, AVS, Belmor, Bushwacker, LUND, Rampage Products, RoadWorks Manufacturing, Roll-N-Lock, Stampede Automotive Accessories, and TonnoPro.
- Bill Reminder serves as President and CEO of Truck Hero.
- Mitch Fogle leads the Lund team that built the company's portfolio.
- Joe Scharfenberger is a Managing Director at CCMP Capital.
- Jeff L. Hull is President and CEO of Highlander Partners.
- This transaction marks Truck Hero’s fifteenth acquisition since its formation in 2007.
- The acquisition aligns with Truck Hero’s growth strategy of acquiring vehicle accessory businesses to diversify its product portfolio into high-growth segments.
- Ropes & Gray LLP advised Truck Hero and CCMP on the transaction.
- Katten Muchin Rosenman LLP advised Lund and Highlander on the transaction.
- Jefferies provided financing and served as advisors to Truck Hero.
- Truck Hero is headquartered in Ann Arbor, MI.
- Truck Hero provides a full range of truck bed covers, truck caps, bed liners, floor liners, steps, suspension kits, Jeep parts, off-road accessories, and operates the online retailer RealTruck.
- Truck Hero’s industry-leading brands include Advantage, A.R.E., BACKRACK, BAK, BedRug, Extang, Husky Liners, N-FAB, Omix-ADA, Retrax, Rugged Liner, Rugged Ridge, Superlift, TruXedo, and UnderCover.
- Lund International’s product lineup includes Ventvisors®, hood shields, Powersteps™, floor mats, fender flares, tonneau covers, storage boxes, nerf bars, and running boards.
- Lund offers products under the brand names Lund, AVS, Belmor, AMP Research, Rampage, Bushwacker, Roll-N-Lock, TonnoPro, and Stampede.
