# Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-07

**Source:** [Panasonic Automotive announces in vehicle wireless charging solutions - BusinessToday](https://www.businesstoday.in/technology/news/story/panasonic-automotive-announces-in-vehicle-wireless-charging-solutions-284347-2021-01-14)  ·  `page_047967`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive announced two variants of in-vehicle wireless charging technology (moving coil and static coil) at the first-ever virtual CES 2021.
- Both variants claim to provide efficient in-vehicle charging that meets or exceeds most current in-vehicle charging systems.
- Panasonic launched an automotive wireless charging system aimed at being a more accurate and efficient way to charge compared to the majority of vehicle charging systems that remain wired or tethered.
- The moving coil variant includes Exclusive Detection Technology, a patent-pending tracking technology where the wireless charging coil moves into the optimum position to align with the mobile device's charging coil and quickly begins to charge wirelessly.
- This moving coil technology has better coupling capability against other wireless charging systems and supports 15W of charging power.
- The wireless charger is Qi 1.3 certified for both static/moving coil variants.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America is a division company of Panasonic Corporation of North America.
- Panasonic Automotive operates as a global supplier of automotive infotainment and connectivity system solutions.
- Panasonic Automotive is headquartered in Peachtree City, Georgia.
- The company maintains sales, marketing, and engineering operations in Farmington Hills, Michigan.
