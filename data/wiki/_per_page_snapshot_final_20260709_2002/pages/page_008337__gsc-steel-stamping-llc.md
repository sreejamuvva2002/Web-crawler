## Facts

- GSC Steel Stamping, LLC is a minority- and family-owned automotive parts manufacturer.
- The company has acquired Dixien, LLC's assets.
- GSC Steel Stamping continues a 43-year history of automotive parts stamping.
- The company is led by CEO Vincent Henderson, who is known for turning around award-winning operations.
- GSC is committed to excellence, investing in technology, and maintaining a highly experienced staff.
- The company operates a 200,000 sq. ft. facility located in Lake City, GA.
- GSC's Lake City, GA facility is claimed to be the largest minority-owned automotive stamping plant globally.
- The press release was published on January 30, 2024.
