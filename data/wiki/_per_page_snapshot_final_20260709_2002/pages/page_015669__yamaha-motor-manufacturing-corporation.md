# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2022-06-10

**Source:** [Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation Breaks Ground on Innovative SMART Warehouse in Newnan, Georgia - Motor Sports NewsWire](https://motorsportsnewswire.com/2022/06/10/yamaha-motor-manufacturing-corporation-breaks-ground-on-innovative-smart-warehouse-in-newnan-georgia/)  ·  `page_015669`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation (YMMC) broke ground on a new “SMART” Warehouse in Newnan, Georgia.
- The groundbreaking occurred yesterday relative to June 10, 2022.
- This is the first-of-its-kind SMART Warehouse for Yamaha Motor Company worldwide.
- The facility spans 200,000 square feet and utilizes state-of-the-art automated storage and retrieval systems.
- The warehouse will reduce response time for fulfilling dealer orders for Yamaha WaveRunners, Golf Cars, ATVs, and Side-by-Sides by more than 50 percent.
- Mike Chrzanowski, President and CEO of Yamaha Motor Corporation, stated the facility will improve operational efficiency and material flow velocity to factory assembly lines.
- Demand for outdoor recreational products produced at this Georgia factory continues to exceed supply.
- SMART stands for Sequenced Material and Reduced Transportation.
- The medium parts automated storage and retrieval system will retrieve and put away 240 containers per hour, compared to the current rate of 170 containers per hour.
- The small part automated system will handle 2400 containers per hour, compared to the current capability of 550 containers per day.
- Automatic conveyors and tuggers will move parts through the SMART center to the factories, enabling a 50 percent reduction in forklifts.
- The significant investment underscores Yamaha’s long-term commitment to YMMC employees, the City of Newnan, and Coweta County.
- The facility will bring higher paid and more skilled jobs into Coweta County, including robotics experts, software developers, and systems engineers.
- The warehouse was designed by SSI SCHAEFER.
- An observation platform will be included for local school students to learn about manufacturing and automated processes.
- Yamaha expects to complete the SMART Warehouse in 2024.
- Yamaha Motor Corporation, USA (YMUS) maintains corporate offices in California and two in Georgia, facilities in Wisconsin and Alabama, and factory operations in Tennessee and Georgia.
- YMUS sells products including ATVs, Side-by-Side vehicles, WaveRunners, Golf Cars, motorcycles, snowmobiles, boats, outboard motors, and outdoor power equipment through a nationwide network of distributors and dealers.
