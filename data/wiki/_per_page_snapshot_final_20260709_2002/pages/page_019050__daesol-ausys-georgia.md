## Facts

- Automotive supplier Daesol Ausys Georgia will invest $72 million in Harris County.
- Daesol Ausys Georgia will build a new manufacturing facility in the City of West Point.
- The new facility will create 140 new jobs.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that West Point has become an automotive capital in its own right and expressed excitement that Daesol Ausys will join other innovators in Harris County.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that Georgia’s automotive industry employs over 50,000 hardworking Georgians and will continue to grow.
