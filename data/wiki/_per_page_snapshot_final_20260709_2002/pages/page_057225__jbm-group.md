# JBM Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-02-25

**Source:** [JBM Auto: JBM Group acquires majority stake in German auto firm Linde-Wiemann, ETAuto](https://auto.economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/auto-components/jbm-auto-acquires-majority-stake-in-german-auto-firm-linde-wiemann/68147633)  ·  `page_057225`

## Facts

- JBM Group acquired a majority stake in Germany-based Linde-Wiemann GmbH KG.
- The acquisition provides synergies including new products, global customers, and German technologies to expand JBM's global footprint and add strategic value to its Indian operations.
- Nishant Arya, Executive Director of JBM Group, stated the combination brings frugal engineering and world-class technology.
- JBM and Linde-Wiemann will focus on developing new products that improve vehicle safety and performance through lightweighting.
- These efforts aim to increase product quality and minimize time to market for OEMs, creating an integrated one-stop solution.
- Linde-Wiemann is headquartered in Dillenburg, Germany.
- The company specializes in proprietary technologies including hot forming, tubular assemblies, and the use of alternate materials like aluminum for lightweighting auto systems.
- Linde-Wiemann operates 17 manufacturing plants across eight countries.
- Linde-Wiemann has an employee strength of over 2300 people.
- JBM will gain access to Linde-Wiemann's strengths in electric vehicle components & systems, and battery structure & systems.
