# Racemark International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-10

**Source:** [Floor Mat Maker Racemark International Receives Award From | GGBAILEY](https://www.ggbailey.com/floor-mat-maker-racemark-international-receives-award-from-mitsubishi/)  ·  `page_010311`

## Facts

- Racemark International received the Mitsubishi Diamond Quality Supplier Award, which honors OEM suppliers that excel in product development, performance, support, marketing, and customer satisfaction.
- Racemark supplies original equipment floor mats to top automotive brands including Bentley, BMW, General Motors, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, Mercedes-Benz, Maserati, Mitsubishi, Toyota, and Volkswagen.
- Racemark created the first carpet floor mat in the industry in 1974.
- Racemark operates production plants in Georgia, Switzerland, and Hungary.
- Mitsubishi has selected Racemark to produce its floor mats for more than 16 years.
- CEO Ginger Cannon Bailey stated that receiving the award for the 14th time demonstrates Racemark's dedication to excellence in service and products.
- The company's online platform features a 'Design Your Car Mats™' section allowing customers to customize solid color floor mats with various carpets, edgings, and heel pad designs.
- Floor mats can be personalized with custom names or monograms.
- Racemark offers more than 500,000 possible design combinations for floor mats that fit any car make and model, with manufacturing completed in two business days.
- The company also offers 'Car Couture™' floor mats made from woven carpet in patterns including leopard, zebra, tiger, oriental, plaid, and camouflage.
- Car Couture™ designs are also available as home mats and pet placemats.
- The Mitsubishi award is the second recent honor for Racemark, following the Toyota Quality Alliance 2013 Gold Supplier Award from Toyota Motor Sales, U.S.A., Inc. received the previous month.
- The Toyota Gold Supplier Award recognizes top parts and accessories suppliers for consistent commitment to product development, product quality, operational quality, and product support.
- This marks the second time Racemark has received the Gold Award from Toyota.
