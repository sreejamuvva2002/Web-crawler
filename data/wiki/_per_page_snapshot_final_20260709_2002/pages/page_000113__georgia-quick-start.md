## Facts

- Georgia Quick Start is a discretionary incentive that provides customized, company-specific workforce training at no cost to companies investing in and creating jobs in Georgia.
- In 1967, Georgia’s leadership passed legislation to create Georgia Quick Start as a first-of-its-kind organization to provide free customized workforce training to attract new industry to the state.
- The program's success exceeded expectations and it remains the No. 1-ranked program of its type, serving as a model for other states.
- Georgia Quick Start is considered the state’s most effective discretionary incentive and plays a key role in decisions by companies large and small to create more and better jobs for Georgians.
- The organization's headquarters and Northern Operations Regional Office are located at 75 5th Street, NW, Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30308.
- An Eastern Operations Regional Office and Advanced Manufacturing Training Center are located at 1500 Pine Meadow Drive, Savannah, GA 31322.
- A Bioscience Training Center is located at 320 Shire Parkway, Social Circle, GA 30025.
- A Western Operations Regional Office and Kia Georgia Training Center are located at 900 Kia Boulevard, West Point, GA 31833.
- The Quick Start Cartersville Training Complex is located at 315 Etowah Drive, Cartersville, GA 30125.
- The Quick Start Hyundai Mobility Training Center of GA is located at 700 Genesis Drive, Ellabell, GA 31308.
