# Lund International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-08-20

**Source:** [Automotive - LUND INTERNATIONAL HIRES LEE McGUIRE AS SENIOR DIRECTOR OF MARKETING FOR JEEP & OFF-ROAD](https://kahnmedia.com/lund-international-hires-lee-mcguire-senior-director-marketing-jeep-off-road/)  ·  `page_045628`

## Facts

- Lund International is based in Buford, Georgia.
- Lund International is a leading designer, manufacturer, and marketer of branded automotive accessories for passenger cars, SUVs, vans, light trucks, and Jeeps.
- In August 2018, Lund International hired Lee McGuire as its new senior director of marketing for Jeep and off-road.
- Lee McGuire brings years of automotive aftermarket experience with a strong focus on the Jeep and off-road markets to the Lund International marketing department.
- Mitch Fogle serves as President and CEO of Lund International.
- Lee McGuire earned a BBA in Marketing from the University of Louisiana at Monroe.
- Lee McGuire earned an MBA from the University of Louisiana at Monroe in 2006.
- Lee McGuire previously worked at Skyjacker Suspensions after college.
- Lee McGuire has served on several SEMA Select Committees and task forces, including the Light Truck Accessory Alliance (LTAA), Young Executives Network (YEN), and the SEMA Business Women’s Network (SBN).
- Lee McGuire was recognized by SEMA as one of its “35 Under 35.”
- Lee McGuire was a Northeast Louisiana Young Professionals Top 20 under 40 Nominee.
- Lund International products are marketed under the brand names AMP Research, AVS, Belmor, Bushwacker, LUND, Rampage Products, Roadworks Manufacturing, Roll-N-Lock, Stampede, and Tonno Pro.
- Lund International offers functional body and appearance products that provide customers with ways to protect, customize, and personalize their vehicles.
- Lee McGuire began her education in marketing at the University of Hawaii at Manoa before transferring to the University of Louisiana at Monroe.
