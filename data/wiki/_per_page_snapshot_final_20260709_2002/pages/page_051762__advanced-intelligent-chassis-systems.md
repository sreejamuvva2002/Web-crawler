## Facts

- The ADVICS name stands for Advanced Intelligent Chassis Systems.
- ADVICS operates with the combined experience of AISIN, DENSO and SUMITOMO ELECTRIC, three of the world’s premier automotive suppliers.
- ADVICS has earned its reputation as a world-class manufacturer of the market’s most trusted brake systems.
- ADVICS is a supplier of ultra-premium OE brake system components that are engineered and manufactured with industry-leading technology and innovation for the aftermarket.
- The ADVICS journey represents the very best of Japanese quality, global expertise and environmental sustainability for the road ahead.
- ADVICS advanced technology yields brake pads, cylinders, boosters, rotors, calipers, and brake fluid that provide optimal performance and ultimate durability for the most comfortable and reliable brakes on the road.
- ADVICS dedication to the art of braking technology enables it to design peak performance systems while strengthening its foundation as a leader in the U.S. aftermarket.
- Today, ADVICS is a leader in ultra-premium aftermarket braking systems and components for Asian and domestic vehicles, in North America and around the globe.
