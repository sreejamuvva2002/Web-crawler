# Doowon Climate Control America, Inc.

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** Metter, Candler County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Doowon Climate Control America, Inc., a subsidiary of South Korea's Doowon Group, is an automotive climate control systems manufacturer investing more than $30 million to build a new manufacturing facility in Metter, Candler County, Georgia, creating 200 jobs and producing climate control systems for traditional and electric vehicles.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Produces climate control systems for electric vehicles.
