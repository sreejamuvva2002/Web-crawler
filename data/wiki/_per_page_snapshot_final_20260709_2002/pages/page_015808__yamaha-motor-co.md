## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Co. is officially bringing its U.S. headquarters operations to Kennesaw.
- The decision was confirmed by the Georgia Department of Economic Development.
- The move reflects a shift in how companies view Georgia, treating it as a place to think, lead, and grow rather than just build.
- Kennesaw offers proximity to metro Atlanta’s infrastructure, a deep talent pool, and access to a network of suppliers that already understand advanced manufacturing and mobility.
- State leaders say the move strengthens Georgia’s position in the advanced mobility sector.
- The advanced mobility category is expanding beyond traditional automotive into everything from powersports to next-generation transportation systems.
- Headquarters are following the same path once carved out by production facilities.
- The investment adds high-quality jobs, expanded supplier relationships, and a steady pull on local talent who might otherwise look out of state for opportunities.
- Georgia is proving it can compete for executive leadership and long-term strategic operations in addition to logistics and manufacturing.
- More decisions and innovation regarding the region's business landscape will be guided from Georgia.
