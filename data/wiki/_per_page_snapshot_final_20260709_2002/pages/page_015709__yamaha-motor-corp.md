# Yamaha Motor Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-10

**Source:** [Yamaha Relocating US Headquarters from California to Georgia - GV Wire](https://gvwire.com/2026/03/10/yamaha-relocating-us-headquarters-from-california-to-georgia/)  ·  `page_015709`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Corp. is moving its U.S. headquarters from Cypress, California, to Kennesaw, Georgia.
- The company has been based in Cypress, California, since 1979 after acquiring the land.
- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp announced on Tuesday that Yamaha will relocate to Kennesaw between late 2026 and 2028.
- Yamaha cited structural reform to improve profitability amid changing market conditions and U.S. tariffs as reasons for the move.
- Yamaha already employs over 2,300 people in Georgia.
- The company previously moved its marine and motorsports businesses to Kennesaw, Georgia.
- Gov. Kemp called the relocation a “loud and clear testament” to the state’s appeal to global job creators.
