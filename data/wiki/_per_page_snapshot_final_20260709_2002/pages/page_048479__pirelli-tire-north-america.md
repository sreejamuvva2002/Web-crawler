# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America maintains a headquarters location in Rome, GA.
- The company manufactures tire models including the Pirelli P Zero PZ4 Sport, Scorpion Strada, Trefero RS, Scorpion Zero, and P-Zero with seal inside technology.
- The Scorpion Strada tires were sold with a 65,000-mile warranty.
- Some Pirelli tires are marketed with a 75,000-mile warranty.
- Pirelli prohibits the repair of products with a speed rating of 'V' or higher.
- Pirelli recommends following USTMA industry guidelines for tire repairs.
- Tires equipped with Pirelli's seal inside technology contain an internal gel that prevents standard patching after punctures, necessitating full replacement.
- Pirelli does not offer a standard road hazard warranty, requiring customers to purchase separate warranties through authorized dealers for coverage of damage like nails or punctures.
- Warranty claims for premature wear are handled via prorated refunds based on remaining tread depth down to 2/32, with no mileage warranty provided for sport tires.
