# Vernay Laboratories

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-28

**Source:** [Vernay VoltaVent® Wins Silver Edison Award  - Vernay](https://vernay.com/news-and-events/vernay-wins-2024-edison-award/)  ·  `page_013319`

## Facts

- Vernay is based in Griffin, Georgia.
- Vernay received the Silver Award in the Smart Transportation Battery Solutions Category of the 2024 Edison Awards for developing the Vernay VoltaVent®.
- The Vernay VoltaVent® is a pioneering battery ventilation device designed to enhance safety and efficiency in battery systems.
- The VoltaVent® intelligently manages the internal pressure of the battery pack at critical moments, such as during thermal runaway.
- The device swiftly vents hot gases to prevent explosions and then reseals.
- This innovative flow control solution approach enhances the safety of battery-operated devices and extends their operational life and reliability.
- Bob Ferguson, VP of Global R&D, stated that the award acknowledges the remarkable engineering innovation of VoltaVent® and the teamwork in place at Vernay.
- Andy Woodward, VP of Sales & Marketing, described the VoltaVent® as a commitment to safety, performance, and sustainability.
- Vernay Laboratories has been a technology leader in the design, development, and manufacture of customized flow-control components since its founding in 1946.
- The company formulates its own compounds to address each specific application.
- Vernay’s products play a vital role in millions of medical devices, automobiles, and consumer products.
- The privately held firm produces more than 1 billion components per year.
- Vernay draws from a library of more than 33,000 elastomeric materials and compound formulations.
- The company operates six plants around the world, in the U.S., the Netherlands, Italy, and China.
