# Volvo Car USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-04-09

**Source:** [Volvo Car USA reports first quarter 2026 sales | Volvo Cars Media US](https://www.volvocars.com/us/media/press-releases/2437867B324A0402/)  ·  `page_014193`

## Facts

- Volvo Car USA reported sales of 22,651 vehicles for the first quarter of 2026, down 32 percent year over year.
- Electrified vehicles accounted for 25 percent of total U.S. sales in the first quarter.
- Electrified sales declined 39 percent compared to the same period last year.
- Q1 results reflect ongoing challenges impacting the automotive industry overall.
- Results reflect the impact of reduced subsidies for electrified vehicles.
- Results reflect a comparison to a strong first quarter in 2025 when consumers accelerated car purchases.
- Volvo Cars continues to focus on the higher profitability retail channel, with that channel outperforming total results.
- Sales increased month-over-month each month in the first quarter, reflecting the positive impact of actions taken by the business to drive increased volume.
- Sales of the XC90 increased 3 percent in March, reflecting momentum heading into the second quarter.
- Sales of certified pre-owned vehicles grew 14 percent compared to the first quarter last year, with 13,287 units sold.
- Volvo Cars revealed the all-new EX60 in January, which is expected to reach the U.S. market in late Summer and contribute to volumes.
- Luis Rezende, President of Volvo Car Americas, stated that focus remains on meeting customer needs with a diverse portfolio of powertrain options including MHEV, PHEV, and BEV.
- The EX60 is expected to deliver on range, charging speed, and price, and will bring new customers to the brand.
- Q1 2026 electrified sales totaled 5,762 units compared to 9,449 in Q1 2025.
- BEV sales were 2,326 units in Q1 2026 versus 2,706 in Q1 2025.
- PHEV sales were 3,436 units in Q1 2026 versus 6,743 in Q1 2025.
- Mild hybrid sales were 16,889 units in Q1 2026 versus 23,836 in Q1 2025.
- S60 sales dropped to 2 units in Q1 2026 from 685 in Q1 2025.
- S90 sales dropped to 17 units in Q1 2026 from 377 in Q1 2025.
- V60 Cross Country sales were 565 units in Q1 2026 versus 983 in Q1 2025.
- V90 Cross Country sales dropped to 15 units in Q1 2026 from 166 in Q1 2025.
- XC40 sales were 3,403 units in Q1 2026 versus 6,469 in Q1 2025.
- XC60 sales were 8,061 units in Q1 2026 versus 12,706 in Q1 2025.
- XC90 sales were 8,513 units in Q1 2026 versus 9,369 in Q1 2025.
- EX30 sales were 915 units in Q1 2026 versus 1,185 in Q1 2025.
- EX30 Cross Country sales were 458 units in Q1 2026 with no prior year comparison.
- EX90 sales were 702 units in Q1 2026 versus 1,000 in Q1 2025.
