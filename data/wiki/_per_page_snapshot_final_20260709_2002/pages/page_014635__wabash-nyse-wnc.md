# Wabash (NYSE: WNC)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-02-22

**Source:** [Wabash to Speak on Dynamic Changes in Logistics at ACT Research Seminar | WNC Stock News](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/WNC/wabash-to-speak-on-dynamic-changes-in-logistics-at-act-research-3fj85sneqpxh.html)  ·  `page_014635`

## Facts

- Wabash is a leader in transportation solutions and connected solutions for the transportation, logistics and distribution industries.
- President and Chief Executive Officer Brent Yeagy will speak at ACT Research’s OUTLOOK Seminar 68 on February 22, 2023, in Columbus, Indiana.
- Yeagy's presentation is titled “Understanding the Dynamic Changes within Logistics that Are Altering the Future of the Trailer and Truck Body Markets.”
- The presentation will discuss how changing supply chain dynamics, the rise of e-commerce, and a growing desire for sustainable business models are impacting the outlook for transportation equipment.
- Yeagy stated, “We can no longer look at the past to predict the future of the trailer and truck body markets,” emphasizing the need for new perspectives to address customer demands as traditional models become less relevant.
- Yeagy noted that Wabash is focused on pragmatic solutions with business partners to solve real problems, adding that “the engine is becoming considerably less relevant.”
- ACT Research’s seminars are held twice each year for clients and the larger transportation, logistics and distribution industry.
- Seminar attendees include industry executives and decision-makers representing C-suite and upper management of OEMs, Tier 1 & 2 suppliers, autonomous and electric new entrants, fleets, dealers and financial institutions.
- OUTLOOK Seminar 68 centers on the theme “Driving Decarbonization: The Future of Commercial Vehicles.”
- Wabash is headquartered in Lafayette, Indiana.
- Wabash designs, manufactures, and services a diverse range of products including dry freight and refrigerated trailers, flatbed trailers, tank trailers, dry and refrigerated truck bodies, structural composite panels and products, trailer aerodynamic solutions, and specialty food grade processing equipment.
