# Mercedes-Benz

## Facts

- Mercedes-Benz announced it finalized plans for a large research and development facility in West Midtown as part of its Atlanta expansion efforts.
- The automaker signed a lease for a future 60,000-square-foot technology center within Atlanta’s Northyards industrial project.
- The Mercedes-Benz Atlanta Technology Center is located near North Avenue and Northside Drive.
- The $34 million Mercedes-Benz Atlanta Technology Center is expected to open next summer.
- Mercedes-Benz plans to expand its U.S. headquarters in Sandy Springs into its North American headquarters.
- The headquarters expansion involves relocating up to 500 jobs to the Atlanta area.
- The dual initiatives bolster Mercedes-Benz’s investment and corporate presence in metro Atlanta.
- The dual initiatives accelerate Georgia’s fast-growing auto industry.
- Jason Hoff, CEO of Mercedes-Benz North America, stated that finding a home for the new tech center in Midtown Atlanta is a major milestone in establishing their unified North American headquarters.
- Jason Hoff noted the project represents a $34 million investment in the region.
