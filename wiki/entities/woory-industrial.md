# Woory Industrial

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** 404 Kellam Road, Dublin, GA, Laurens County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Woory Industrial Co., Ltd., a South Korea-based heating, ventilation and air conditioning components manufacturer, is investing $18 million to build its first U.S. manufacturing facility in Dublin, Georgia. The plant will produce electrical heaters, control units and actuators for electric vehicles (EVs), hydrogen-fueled cars and internal combustion carmakers, and will supply Hyundai’s Metaplant in Bryan County as well as Rivian’s planned EV facility near Athens.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Will supply EV components to Hyundai Metaplant in Bryan County and Rivian’s upcoming EV facility; produces parts for electric and hydrogen-fuel vehicles.
