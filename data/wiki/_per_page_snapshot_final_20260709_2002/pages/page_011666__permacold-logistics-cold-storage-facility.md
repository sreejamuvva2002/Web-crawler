# PermaCold Logistics Cold Storage Facility

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-10

**Source:** [Kemp: Rural Georgia gets major economic development win | James Magazine Online](https://jamesmagazinega.com/2025/07/10/kemp-rural-georgia-gets-major-economic-development-win/)  ·  `page_011666`

## Facts

- Gov. Brian Kemp announced a $60 million investment in a cold storage facility for PermaCold Logistics in Darien, McIntosh County.
- Ti Cold and Karis Cold have officially broken ground on the project.
- The facility will support 50 jobs upon completion of phase one.
- State and local leaders are calling the announcement a major victory for rural Georgia.
- Gov. Brian Kemp stated the announcement is the largest known economic development win for McIntosh County.
- Ti Cold provides a full suite of cold storage construction and development services, including complete design and engineering, master site planning, operations guidance, and consulting services.
- Commissioner Pat Wilson of the Georgia Department of Economic Development noted that cold storage plays a vital role in Georgia’s logistics and supply chain infrastructure for agricultural and food products.
- The new facility is located at Tidewaters Industrial Park, a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) site, in Darien.
- The advanced cold storage complex will have a clear height of 50 feet.
- The facility will feature a flexible, convertible design maintaining temperatures ranging from 40 degrees to minus 10 degrees Fahrenheit.
- The facility will incorporate an environmentally friendly refrigeration system.
- Chairwoman Kate Pontello Karwacki of the McIntosh County Board of Commissioners highlighted the project's strategic location along the I-95 corridor and proximity to ports.
- Ti Cold, Karis Cold, and PermaCold Logistics, along with Georgia’s ports and highway systems, support logistics industry jobs across the state.
