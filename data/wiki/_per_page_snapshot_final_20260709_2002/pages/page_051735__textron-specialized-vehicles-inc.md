## Facts

- Jinhua Suncart and three other defendants have settled a legal complaint filed by Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT) company, for infringement of patents related to TSV’s E-Z-GO® RXV® golf-car platform.
- Under the terms of the agreement, Jinhua Suncart and defendants Vitacci Motorcycles Inc., Cougar International Inc., and TPSP LLC (doing business as Turbo Powersports) will stop sales of all products within the scope of TSV's patent enforcement complaint.
- The defendants must also stop using E-Z-GO’s registered RXV® and TXT® trademarks and remove all online listings that promote the products named in the complaint.
- The settlement resolves a complaint filed by TSV in U.S. District Court for the Southern District of Georgia.
- TSV General Counsel Rocco Sica stated that TSV will vigorously defend its intellectual property, which is the direct result of investments to generate innovation for products in the golf-car industry and other served industries.
- The defendants were investigated for copying technologies developed through a substantial investment of time and money by TSV.
- TSV warned other parties copying innovations that it is watching them and will take any appropriate actions necessary to protect innovations created by its employees.
- E-Z-GO was founded in Augusta, Ga. in 1954 as a globally renowned leader in the design and manufacture of golf cars and personal transport vehicles.
- E-Z-GO is known for innovation in sustainable electric-vehicle and powertrain technology.
- E-Z-GO models include RXV® and TXT® fleet golf cars; Freedom® and Valor personal golf cars; and E-Z-GO Express™ personal utility vehicles.
- E-Z-GO's latest innovation is the Liberty™, the industry’s first vehicle to offer four forward-facing seats in a compact, golf-car-sized footprint.
- Employees of Textron Specialized Vehicles are committed to a culture of compliance and reduction of emissions.
- E-Z-GO became part of Textron Inc. in 1960 and currently operates as part of the company’s Textron Specialized Vehicles division.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company that leverages its global network of aircraft, defense, industrial, and finance businesses to provide customers with innovative solutions and services.
- Textron Inc. owns brands including Bell, Cessna, Beechcraft, Pipistrel, Jacobsen, Kautex, Lycoming, E-Z-GO, Arctic Cat, and Textron Systems.
