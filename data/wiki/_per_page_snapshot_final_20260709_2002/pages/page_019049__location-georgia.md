# Location Georgia

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-12

**Source:** [Press Releases Archives - Page 3 of 34 - Location Georgia](https://www.locationgeorgia.com/category/press-releases/page/3/)  ·  `page_019049`

## Facts

- Albany-Dougherty Economic Development Commission (ADEDC) celebrated AMADAS Industries as a participant in the Georgia Made program on October 16, 2024.
- The Georgia Made program recognizes high-quality products manufactured across the state of Georgia.
- Governor Kemp highlighted Georgia's business rankings at a Workforce Summit on September 13, 2024.
- Area Development magazine named Georgia the No. 1 state for business for the 11th year in a row as of September 2024.
- Workforce development was cited as a major contributor to Georgia's overall top state ranking.
- Georgia earned a No. 1 ranking for its workforce development program, Georgia Quick Start, for the 15th year in a row as of September 2024.
- Georgia FLEX held the second annual FLEX State Finals in Fitzgerald, Georgia, on April 12, 2024.
- Georgia FLEX (Foundational Leadership & Entrepreneur X-perience) is a Fitzgerald, GA-based non-profit organization established to promote high school student entrepreneurship within the state.
- Automotive parts company Hwashin will invest over $176 million to establish a new manufacturing facility in Dublin, Georgia, creating 460+ jobs and supporting the state’s e-mobility ambitions.
- Hwashin is a Tier-1 supplier aiming to shape a global city for electric vehicles (EVs) through a strategic alliance with Georgia and Laurens County.
- Hwashin leverages its 50-year expertise in crafting chassis and body parts.
- Area Development magazine ranked Georgia as the No. 1 state for business for 10 consecutive years as of October 3, 2023.
- This achievement marked an unprecedented milestone of a full decade of business excellence for the state.
- No other state in the nation had achieved this benchmark at that time.
