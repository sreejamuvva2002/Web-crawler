## Facts

- Enchem is a global leader in electrolyte production for lithium-ion batteries.
- The company established its electrolyte production facility in Georgia as early as 2019.
- The Georgia plant is the only facility in the U.S. capable of producing 105,000 tons of electrolyte annually.
- The Georgia plant supplies major global battery makers including Tesla, SK On, Ultium Cells (a joint venture between LGES and GM), Panasonic, and AESC.
- Enchem plans to expand the Georgia plant’s capacity to 200,000 tons annually by 2026.
- Additional facilities are planned in Tennessee and Ontario, Canada.
