# Lund International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2007-01-06

**Source:** [Lund International Announces Restructuring Plans - AftermarketNews](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/lund-international-announces-restructuring-plans/)  ·  `page_045713`

## Facts

- Lund International announced further reorganization and restructuring initiatives from Suwanee, GA.
- The company first announced changes to its corporate structure in 2006.
- The company consolidated its Duluth, GA corporate offices, its Lund Accessories Division in Lawrenceville, GA, and its Ventshade Accessories Division warehousing function into a new Corporate Center in Suwanee, GA.
- The consolidation moves were completed in August 2006.
- The restructuring includes the closure of two facilities and a new business structure designed to reduce fixed costs, increase management effectiveness, and improve customer service.
- The Autotron Accessories Division in Longmont, CO is expected to close by May 31.
- Approximately 75 employees work at the Longmont facility producing plastic accessories for light truck and automotive vehicles.
- Production from the Longmont facility is being transferred to the company’s Ventshade Accessories Division in Lawrenceville, GA.
- The company’s offices in Anoka, MN will close by the third quarter of 2007.
- Approximately 35 employees are engaged in administrative support functions at the Anoka facility.
- The majority of the Anoka positions will be relocated to the company’s Corporate Center in Suwanee, GA.
- Lund International reorganized into three strategic business units for improved management effectiveness.
- The Light Truck and Automotive Accessories business unit, marketed under the Lund, AVS, and Nifty brands and sold through warehouse distributors, specialty retail chains, and mail order distributors, is the company's largest business unit.
- A separate Light Truck and Automotive Accessories business unit focused on sales through original equipment manufacturers was established to improve the company's position in that channel.
- The third strategic business unit focuses on Heavy Truck Accessories sold through original equipment manufacturers and their aftermarket distribution centers.
- Management is being realigned into the three business units to improve focus on satisfying customer needs.
- Vice president of sales and marketing for Light Truck and Automotive Accessories Terry Cole has left the company.
- Corporate vice president responsible for Information Systems and the Corporate Distribution Center Steve Treichel has left the company.
- Jerry Schomaker, previously vice president and general manager of the Ventshade Accessories Division, has become vice president of Product Supply and Engineering.
- Schomaker joined Lund International in 2000 as vice president and general manager of the Deflecta-Shield Division in Howe, IN.
