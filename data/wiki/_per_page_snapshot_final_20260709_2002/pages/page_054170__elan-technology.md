## Facts

- Elan Technology has been a leading glass manufacturing company for nearly 80 years, playing an integral role in the advanced technical ceramics and contract spray drying industries.
- Elan Technology is the largest independent American company in its field producing preforms and spacers using technical glass, ceramics, and glass ceramic composites.
- The company's technical glass and ceramic parts are used in the defense industry, space exploration equipment, cell phones, industrial machinery, refrigerators, automobiles, flashlights, and numerous other applications.
- Elan Technology was asked by Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) for research on strong manufacturing companies in America.
- Elan Technology offers toll spray drying services to convert products from aqueous solutions into powder form.
- The company has provided these toll spray drying services to a variety of industrial, chemical, and agricultural companies requiring tailored specifications for particle characteristics.
- Elan Technology’s manufacturing facility was constructed in Midway, Georgia, in 1997.
- The manufacturing facility has a 90,000 sq. ft. footprint on 22 acres.
- Liberty County firm Elan Technology was included in an MIT manufacturing study according to the Savannah Morning News.
- Elan Technology moved its insulation operation from New Jersey to Midway, Georgia according to the Liberty County Development Authority.
- Tak Argentinis, Chairman of Elan Technology, Inc., delivered the commencement address for Savannah Technical College Spring Graduation.
- Georgia Tech helped the Midway manufacturer cut energy costs.
- Liberty teens were eyeing potential apprenticeships with the company according to Business in Savannah.
- The manufacturing group is offering paid apprenticeships according to the Coastal Courier.
