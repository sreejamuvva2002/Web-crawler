# Development Authority of Early County

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-31

**Source:** [Building a Strong Foundation](https://www.developearly.com/post/building-a-strong-foundation)  ·  `page_046207`

## Facts

- Susanne Reynolds has served as the director of the Development Authority of Early County for two years as of January 2, 2023.
- The Development Authority office had been vacant for approximately six years prior to her appointment.
- Since 2023, Susanne Reynolds and the Georgia Economic Development Region 10 representative have conducted site visits to Georgia-Pacific, Nucor, Brewers Manufacturing, Early Trucking Company, Southern AgComm, Nantze Springs, Blakely Barns, Olam Edible Nuts, and Golden Peanut Company.
- The authority plans site visits with existing industries every other month to share local and state incentive information.
- According to MEAG Power, the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) announced that for the third consecutive year, Georgia broke records for economic development during Fiscal Year 2023.
- In FY23, total investments in facility expansions and new locations across Georgia totaled more than $24 billion.
- FY23 investments resulted in 38,400 new jobs through 426 projects supported by GDEcD’s Global Commerce team.
- Prior to 2023, the only local incentive available was the Early County Revolving Loan Fund (RLF).
- The RLF allows the Development Authority to recommend businesses for gap financing to assist with economic development projects that create and retain jobs.
- The Early County Board of Commissioners makes the final decision on each RLF application.
- As of December 31, 2024, additional entities and incentives created to promote economic development in Blakely and Early County include the Downtown Development Authority, Local Historic Preservation Ordinances, Local Historic District & Commission, Rural Zone Designation, Enterprise Zone Ordinances & Designations, Alumni of Georgia Initiative for Community Housing, and a GA Main Street Start-Up Community.
- The Blakely DDA developed branding for Downtown Blakely and commissioned a professional website to showcase the downtown area.
- Early County and Blakely are entering their 200th year as Susanne Reynolds begins her third year in office.
