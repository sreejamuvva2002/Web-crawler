# Lehigh Technologies Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2012-08-27

**Source:** [Lehigh Technologies receives $16 million in new investments | Waste Dive](https://www.wastedive.com/news/lehigh-technologies-receives-16-million-in-new-investments/50049/)  ·  `page_057060`

## Facts

- Lehigh Technologies Inc. is an Atlanta-based tire recycling company.
- The company completed $16 million in financing.
- Investments were secured from venture capital firms led by Leaf Clean Energy.
- Additional investors include Kleiner Perkins Caufield Byers, Index Ventures, and NGP Energy Technology Partners.
- Lehigh stated the funding will expand its geographic reach.
- The funding will also support research and development initiatives.
- The investment aims to strengthen Lehigh's ability to meet commercial demand for its micronized rubber powder (MRP) technology.
- Lehigh uses MRP to recycle end-of-life tire and post-industrial rubber into new materials.
- These recycled materials are applied in high-performance tires, plastics goods, asphalt, coatings, and construction materials.
