# Dragonfly Energy Holdings Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-30

**Source:** [Dragonfly Energy Enters into $30M Licensing Agreement for Battle Born Batteries Brand with Stryten Energy, a Leading North American Battery Manufacturer - EIN Presswire](https://www.einnews.com/pr_news/731564086/dragonfly-energy-enters-into-30m-licensing-agreement-for-battle-born-batteries-brand-with-stryten-energy-a-leading-north-american-battery)  ·  `page_050562`

## Facts

- Dragonfly Energy Holdings Corp. signed a $30 million licensing agreement to license its Battle Born Batteries® lithium-ion battery brand to Stryten Energy for global distribution.
- The agreement grants Stryten Energy exclusive rights to market and distribute Battle Born Batteries in B2B end markets where Dragonfly Energy currently has no presence, including military, automotive, marine, power sports, lawn and garden, and golf cart applications.
- Dragonfly Energy will receive an initial upfront licensing fee of $5 million.
- Dragonfly Energy is entitled to mid-single digit royalty payments based on Stryten Energy’s product volume, with a tiered structure reaching up to an additional $25 million.
- Upon reaching the additional $25 million threshold, Stryten Energy will be required to pay a nominal annual license fee.
- Additional fees for battery design and contract manufacturing services outside the licensing agreement are expected to provide additional revenue to Dragonfly Energy.
- The partnership focuses on battery design, contract manufacturing, and licensing of Battle Born Batteries products for new B2B end markets.
- Dragonfly Energy will continue its independent operations in existing markets including recreational vehicles (RV), off-grid properties, heavy-duty trucking, stationary storage, and oil and gas.
- Stryten Energy is a leading U.S.-based manufacturer of energy storage solutions providing advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium batteries to customers across transportation, industrial, military, and government sectors.
- Stryten Energy has a workforce of over 2,500 employees.
- Stryten Energy operates ten manufacturing plants.
- Stryten Energy is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Dragonfly Energy will initially contract manufacture all batteries distributed by Stryten Energy at its facility in Northern Nevada until Stryten Energy establishes its own domestic manufacturing footprint.
- The partnership encompasses a mix of existing Battle Born Batteries products with a strong focus on developing new battery models specifically tailored to Stryten Energy's target markets.
- Dragonfly Energy will continue to grow its technical support team alongside increasing demand for Battle Born Batteries products.
- Dragonfly Energy specializes in cell manufacturing, battery pack assembly, and full system integration.
- Hundreds of thousands of reliable Battle Born Batteries packs have been deployed in the field through top-tier OEMs and a diverse retail customer base.
- Dragonfly Energy's patented dry electrode manufacturing process delivers chemistry-agnostic power solutions for applications including energy storage systems, electric vehicles, and consumer electronics.
- Dragonfly Energy's overarching mission includes the future deployment of its proprietary, nonflammable, all-solid-state battery cells.
- Stryten Energy's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- The licensing agreement was announced on July 30, 2024.
- Dr. Denis Phares, chief executive officer of Dragonfly Energy, stated that the partnership aims to make lithium batteries more accessible to consumers nationwide alongside other leading brands’ components at major retailers.
- Mike Judd, chief executive officer of Stryten Energy, stated that the partnership will allow Stryten Energy to expand its transportation product portfolio and provide customers access to the Battle Born Batteries brand of quality lithium batteries.
- Tyler Bourns, chief marketing officer at Dragonfly Energy, stated that leveraging Stryten Energy's vast retail and distribution customer network is a major step forward in making Battle Born Batteries a household name across North America.
