# Superior Essex International LP / Superior Essex Inc.

## Facts

- Superior Essex is a leading manufacturer and supplier of communication cabling products and accessories serving the communication industry and related distribution markets for over 80 years.
- Superior Essex operates as a trademark for Superior Essex International LP and other companies including Superior Essex Inc.
- The company's corporate address is located at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
- Superior Essex received the Exceptional Case Study Award from the Sustainable Purchasing Leadership Council (SPLC) on Wednesday, May 16.
- The award recognized the company's retrofit lighting project at the historic West Baden Springs Hotel in French Lick, Indiana, and its contribution to SPLC's online library.
- Will Bryan, Vice President of Marketing and Technology at Superior Essex, stated the company is proud to be recognized by the SPLC for documenting sustainable purchasing efforts.
- The hotel solution utilized Superior Essex's PowerWise® Power-over-Ethernet cable suite to update the historic building with modern technology, improving operational and energy efficiency.
- West Baden Springs Hotel features a domed atrium often called the "Eighth Wonder of the World" and is one of America's oldest and most valuable resort hotels.
- Superior Essex collaborated with Platformatics to create a customized lighting solution for the atrium using innovative physical infrastructure and leading lighting technology.
- The lighting solution maintains the iconic space's architectural integrity while achieving a 98.5% increase in energy efficiency.
- Additional benefits of the solution include data collection and analysis, ease of use, occupant health and wellness, safety, and productivity.
- Superior Essex developed the PowerWise Power-over-Ethernet cable network to meet demands for smarter, more adaptive, and integrated environments.
- The company's products contribute to all green building standards, including LEED, WELL Building, and Living Building Challenge.
- The SPLC was founded in 2013 to help large organizations develop and standardize sustainable purchasing programs.
- The SPLC has over 180 members with collective buying power exceeding $200 billion.
- In 2015, the SPLC published the Sustainable Purchasing Leadership Guide v1.0, described as the most comprehensive literature on sustainable purchasing to date.
