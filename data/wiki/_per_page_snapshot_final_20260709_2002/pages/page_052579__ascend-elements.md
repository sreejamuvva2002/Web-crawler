# Ascend Elements

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-08

**Source:** [Ascend Elements announces major battery material order - electrive.com](https://www.electrive.com/2023/06/08/ascend-elements-announces-major-battery-material-order/)  ·  `page_052579`

## Facts

- Ascend Elements is a US company that specializes in sustainable battery materials.
- Ascend Elements announced a major battery material order worth up to $5 billion.
- The initial contract value is around $1 billion, with deliveries expected to start in the fourth quarter of 2024.
- The multi-year contract will supply cathode precursor materials (high nickel NMC) for battery production to an unnamed major US company.
- The customer has the option to extend the contract to a larger quantity worth up to $5 billion.
- Ascend Elements is building a factory in Kentucky to produce sustainable battery materials.
- The planned completion date for the Kentucky factory is the end of 2023.
- The initial planned capacity of the Kentucky facility is enough cathode material and precursors for up to 250,000 electric vehicle batteries per year.
- Ascend Elements later cited an updated output capacity three times as high, sufficient for up to 750,000 electric vehicle batteries.
- In October 2022, the US Department of Energy awarded Ascend Elements two grants totaling $480 million to accelerate construction of the Kentucky plant.
- In March 2023, Ascend Elements opened its first commercial-scale lithium-ion battery recycling plant in Georgia.
- Mike O’Kronley serves as the CEO of Ascend Elements.
- CEO Mike O’Kronley stated that nearly 100% of the world’s pCAM is produced in Asia.
- CEO Mike O’Kronley emphasized the need to manufacture critical battery materials in the United States to secure the North American supply chain, reduce carbon emissions, and ensure energy independence.
