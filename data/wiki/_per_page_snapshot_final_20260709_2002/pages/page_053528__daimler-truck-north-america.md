# Daimler Truck North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-02

**Source:** [DTNA delivers Freightliner eM2 trucks to PITT OHIO](https://www.thebuzzevnews.com/dtna-em2-trucks-pitt-ohio/)  ·  `page_053528`

## Facts

- Daimler Truck North America (DTNA) delivered its first battery-electric Freightliner eM2 trucks.
- The Freightliner eM2 is manufactured at DTNA’s plant in Portland, Oregon.
- PITT OHIO, a transportation, warehouse, and logistics services company, is the first to deploy the eM2.
- Two Freightliner eM2 Class 7 trucks were delivered to PITT OHIO for integration into its fleet.
- The delivered trucks have 26-foot-long box bodies.
- The trucks will operate out of PITT OHIO’s Cleveland terminal.
- The deployment supports PITT OHIO’s Less-Than-Truckload (LTL) freight shipping business.
- David Carson serves as senior vice president of sales and marketing at Daimler Truck North America.
- DTNA is dedicated to providing innovative, safe and reliable solutions that adapt to the changing needs of its customers.
- The delivery of the eM2 demonstrates DTNA’s ongoing commitment to its growing portfolio of zero-emissions vehicles.
- The eM2 builds on the success of the conventional M2 and extends efficiency and versatility into the zero-emission vehicle space.
- DTNA’s electric lineup includes the Jouley, an all-electric bus from Thomas Built Bus available since 2020.
- DTNA offers the FCCC MT50e walk-in van chassis for final mile delivery, available since 2021.
- The Class 8 Freightliner eCascadia has been available since 2022.
- DTNA added the eM2 to its lineup in 2023.
- The eM2 has a typical range of 180 miles for Class 6 and 250 miles for Class 7.
- DTNA states the eM2 is ideal for pick-up and delivery customers who require an adaptable electric solution.
- Vocational innovation vehicles were introduced last year and are in use with customers.
- Fyda Freightliner, a DTNA Elite Support certified dealership with seven locations serving the Midwest, supported the eM2 sales process and delivery.
- Fyda Freightliner has supported PITT OHIO for the past 20 years.
