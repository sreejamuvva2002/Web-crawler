# Seoyon E-Hwa Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-04-08

**Source:** [Seoyon E-Hwa - Korea Auto Parts Info](https://koreapartsinfo.com/2019/04/08/seoyon-e-hwa/)  ·  `page_059483`

## Facts

- Seoyon E-Hwa Co., Ltd. is a middle-sized company belonging to the Seoyon group.
- The CEO of the company is Kim Geunshik.
- The company manufactures new automotive steering and suspension systems.
- Business contents include manufacturing and wholesale of auto parts, as well as technology services.
- The company was established on July 1, 2014.
- In July 2014, the company spun off into Seoyon (Holding Company) and Hanil E-Hwa.
- As of 2018, the company had 952 employees.
- In 2018, the company reported a sales turnover of KRW 93,667,000,000.
- In 2018, the company reported capital of KRW 13,510,000,000.
- In 2018, the company reported a business profit of KRW 3,840,000,000.
- The company's headquarters address is 3F, 330, Gangnam-daero, Gangnam-gu, Seoul, Republic of Korea.
- In April 2010, the company established Hanil E-Hwa Interior Systems Georgia Inc. in the United States.
- In May 2005, the company established Hanil E-Hwa Interior Systems Alabama, L.L.C. in the United States.
- The company has expanded its global manufacturing and R&D footprint with subsidiaries and centers in India, China, Slovakia, Mexico, Brazil, Germany, Turkey, and Iran.
- In March 2018, a related entity Assan Hanil won the Best Supplier Silver Award in 2017 from Ford Turkey Factory.
- The company obtained ISO/TS 16949 Certification in March 2004, which is an automotive industry quality management standard.
- In October 1996, the company established a joint venture company named ASSAN-Hanil in Turkey.
- In December 2000, the company signed a technical assistance contract with IRAN KHODRO Co. for interior factory construction.
- In January 1999, the company signed a technical assistance contract for trim and seat development and production with Assan-Hanil in Turkey.
