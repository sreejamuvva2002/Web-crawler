## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc. (HIAMS) maintains a comprehensive Supplier Handbook outlining vendor guidelines and requirements.
- The handbook covers supplier development, quality assurance, production control, purchasing, and supplier rating processes.
- HIAMS provides a Logistics Guide dated June 1, 2020 for its suppliers.
- Suppliers must follow CMRT (Conflict Minerals Reporting Template) instructions and a CSR Procurement Guideline from July 2021.
- The company operates a Minority Development Program for supply chain partners.
- HIAMS mandates compliance with ISO 9001:2015 and IATF 16949 standards (Issue 10 policy).
- Safety Data Sheets (SDS) issuance guidelines are provided in Section 07.
- Transportation supplier guidelines were updated on September 17, 2018.
- Content reporting requirements for suppliers are outlined in Revision 2 of Section 09.
- Accounts payable procedures and a 2020 Packaging Manual with specific forms (PM-Form 40, RFQ Document) are detailed.
- EDI specifications require X12 certification and compliance with SupplyWEB_X12 standards for documents 830, 862, 997, and 856.
- Organizational charts and phone lists for key contacts are maintained in Issue 20 of Section 13.
- Berea-specific supplier requirements include a dedicated packaging manual and PPAP submission agreements.
- Suppliers must submit specific delivery notices for ECR parts, new products, permanent countermeasures, PPAP samples, and trial samples.
- Section 17 of the handbook explicitly outlines Georgia (GA) specific requirements for suppliers.
- A document revision tracker is used to manage updates to the Supplier Handbook.
- Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc. sponsors the No. 2 Team Penske Chevrolet racing team.
- The company maintains official social media channels on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Instagram under the handle @hitachiastemo.
