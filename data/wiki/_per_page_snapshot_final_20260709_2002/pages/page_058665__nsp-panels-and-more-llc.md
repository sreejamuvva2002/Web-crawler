# NSP Panels and more LLC

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Joint venture NSP Panels and more LLC was founded in the USA.
- The joint venture is headquartered in Commerce, Georgia, USA.
- Founding partners include Franz Neuhofer (owner and managing director of Austrian family company FN Neuhofer), Marco Seitner (owner and board member of Selit North America Inc.), and David Pritchard (owner and president of Pak-Lite Inc.).
- The name NSP Panels and more is derived from the names Neuhofer, Selit North America Inc., and Pak-Lite Inc.
- The joint venture was established to introduce functional design in the form of acoustic panels, wall elements, and accessories for floors, walls, and ceilings to the American market.
- Franz Neuhofer stated that he is pleased to announce the founding, highlighting a strong partnership among the founders, combined expertise in product and production know-how, and knowledge of the American market.
- NSP Panels and more successfully appeared as a company at the Surfaces (TISE) and IBS 2024 trade shows in Las Vegas, USA.
- Interest in the acoustic panels at the trade shows was outstanding, with promising conversations held.
