# Kia Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-02-25

**Source:** [Kia Georgia hits five million units as 2027 Telluride rolls out | Automotive World](https://www.automotiveworld.com/news/kia-georgia-hits-five-million-units-as-2027-telluride-rolls-out/)  ·  `page_000299`

## Facts

- Kia Georgia has started production of the 2027 Telluride HEV at its West Point facility.
- The ceremonial first vehicle represents the plant’s 5 millionth unit.
- The 2027 Telluride HEV is the first hybrid electric vehicle assembled in the state of Georgia.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp attended the production start event.
- The West Point plant is the sole global production site for the Telluride model.
- The Telluride has been manufactured at the West Point facility since its launch in 2019.
- The West Point facility has operated since 2009.
- The West Point facility has grown into one of the largest employers in the region.
- Sean Yoon, president and CEO of Kia North America and Kia America, stated that reaching five million vehicles and introducing Kia’s first US-assembled HEV is a testament to both Kia’s storied history and its direction going forward.
