## Facts

- Oneda Corporation is a contract manufacturer located in Columbus, Georgia (Southeast).
- The company utilizes the latest technologies in precision metal stamping, assembly, and tool and die designs.
- Oneda specializes in innovative robotic transfer and progressive tooling capabilities with press sizes ranging from 25 to 500 tons.
- Oneda Corporation is a subsidiary of Oneda Electric Corporation in Japan.
- The company serves the Automotive, Medical, and Consumer Electronics industries with competitive performance and cost-effective metal stampings.
- Services range from prototyping of initial design to full mass production.
- Oneda is a customer driven and process centered organization striving to ensure the highest standards of quality, customer satisfaction, and timelines of all projects.
- The company works in partnership with customers using combined extensive experience in flexible manufacturing processes to significantly reduce tooling expenses, improve overall unit costs, and increase production capacity.
- Oneda uses advanced comprehensive engineering and design experience to achieve unique manufacturing techniques in factory automation.
- Oneda has developed tooling technology and manufacturing capabilities to produce several different part numbers all at once within the same process.
- This methodology provides significant cost saving advantages.
- Oneda Corporation is TS16949, ISO9001, and ISO14001 certified.
