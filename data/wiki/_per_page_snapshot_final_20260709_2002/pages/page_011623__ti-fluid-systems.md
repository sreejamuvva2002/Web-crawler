# TI Fluid Systems

## Facts

- TI Fluid Systems is an automotive fluid systems manufacturer.
- Full-year revenues and operating profits improved year-on-year in 2021.
- Full-year revenues were up 5.6% at constant currency rates to €2.95bn.
- Margin improved by more than a percentage point, rising from -6.3% to 4.3%.
- Adjusted underlying earnings rose from a reported loss of €176.3m to a profit of €126.8m.
- Earnings per share were EUR 2.76 cents, a marked improvement on the prior year's loss of EUR 48.88 cents.
- Declared a dividend of EUR 3.39 cents, down from 2020's EUR 6.74 cent payment.
- Adjusted free cash flow generation was €117.3m.
- Net cash generated from operating activities was €215.1m.
- The group ended the year with a strong balance sheet and a cash position of €499.1m.
- Chief executive Hans Dieltjens reported a strong performance in 2021 despite ongoing pandemic impacts, lower production volumes, global supply disruptions, labour shortages, rising costs, and volatile customer orders.
- TI Fluid anticipates that industry production volumes will remain uncertain and volatile in the near term given geopolitical conflicts and the macro-economic environment.
- Annual global light vehicle production volumes were forecast to return to pre-Covid levels by the end of 2023.
