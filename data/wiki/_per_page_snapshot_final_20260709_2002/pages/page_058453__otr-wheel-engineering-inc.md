## Facts

- OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc. is officially unveiling new branding at CONEXPO-CON/AGG 2023.
- The company's new trade name or DBA is “OTR Engineered Solutions.”
- New branding elements include a new logo, tagline, website, and other updates to reflect expanded capabilities as a global solutions provider.
- OTR was founded in 1987 to provide off-the-road wheels and tires to OEMs and the aftermarket.
- The company's tire portfolio includes bias, radial, semi-pneumatic, non-marking, and airless tires.
- OTR provides value-added services for dealers and OEMs including product development, sourcing, warehousing, tire mounting, foam filling, flat proofing, wheel inspection, certification, and subassembly.
- John Major serves as the Global Director of Marketing at OTR.
- OTR operates 34 facilities located across North America, Europe, and Asia.
- The company introduced the tagline “Roll More™” to complement its branding update.
- OTR is a market-leading global enterprise specializing in off-the-road ground-engaging solutions for OEMs and aftermarkets.
- The firm has long-established strategic partnerships with OEMs covering Construction, Lawn & Garden, Powersports, Agriculture, Forestry, Mining, Material Handling, and Specialty Vehicle markets.
- OTR's infrastructure comprises 34 manufacturing facilities and warehouse locations throughout the U.S., Canada, Europe, and Asia.
