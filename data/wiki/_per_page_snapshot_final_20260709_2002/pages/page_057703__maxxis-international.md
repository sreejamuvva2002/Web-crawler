# Maxxis International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-07-16

**Source:** [Maxxis to boost U.S. distribution capacity with new Indiana warehouse](https://www.tractionnews.com/maxxis-boost-u-s-distribution-capacity-new-indiana-warehouse/)  ·  `page_057703`

## Facts

- Maxxis International, a Taiwan-based tire manufacturer, announced it will boost its U.S. distribution capacity by opening a new warehouse near Indianapolis, Indiana later this year.
- The facility will feature more than 350,000 square feet of warehouse space.
- The facility includes 10,000 square feet of office space.
- The facility includes two 2,500-square-foot administrative warehouse offices.
- The new facility is located in the Park130 at Whitestown corporate industrial park in Lebanon, Indiana.
- Maxxis International – USA President James Tzen stated that Indiana was chosen for its excellent transportation infrastructure, friendly business environment, and proximity to major automakers.
- The new center is located near I-65, minutes from I-465, and less than a five-hour drive from all of the Midwest’s auto manufacturing hubs.
- The Indiana facility is scheduled to open in August.
- Molly Whitehead, executive director of the Boone County Economic Development Corporation, welcomed Maxxis Tires to Whitestown and expressed excitement for a productive relationship.
- In addition to the Indiana facility, Maxxis has U.S. distribution centers in Rancho Cucamonga, California; Grapevine, Texas; and at its corporate headquarters in Suwanee, Georgia.
