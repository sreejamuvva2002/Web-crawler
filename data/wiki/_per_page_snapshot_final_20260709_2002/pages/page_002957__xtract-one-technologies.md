# Xtract One Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-09

**Source:** [Xtract One Technologies Selected by Hyundai Transys to](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2022/12/09/2570999/0/en/Xtract-One-Technologies-Selected-by-Hyundai-Transys-to-Secure-Facilities-and-Keep-Weapons-Out.html)  ·  `page_002957`

## Facts

- Xtract One Technologies announced its SmartGateway security screening solution was selected to protect entrances at Hyundai Transys in West Point, Ga.
- The SmartGateway will be deployed at the manufacturing plant to protect workers and staff from weapons entering the buildings and to prevent mass casualty events.
- Jinho Kim, manager of GA & EHS at Hyundai Transys Georgia Powertrain Inc., viewed and tested Xtract One’s Gateway at Kia Georgia Inc.
- Kia Georgia Inc. had great success with keeping weapons out using the Gateway solution.
- Hyundai Transys was impressed by the way the Gateway solution was quickly and accurately detecting weapons.
- The customization aspect of the Gateway solution would fit seamlessly into Hyundai Transys's manufacturing facility.
- SmartGateway is the latest iteration of Xtract One’s Gateway screening solution, designed as a fast, frictionless entry screener for manufacturing plants, casinos, and large arenas.
- The system unobtrusively detects guns, knives, and other potential weapons on people entering facilities without requiring them to empty their pockets of personal items.
- Peter Evans, CEO of Xtract One, stated that auto manufacturers, their supply chains, and other large workplaces are well suited for the Gateway solution due to needing to process hundreds of workers quickly during shift changes.
- This selection marks Xtract One's third customer in the auto manufacturing industry.
- SmartGateway uses artificial intelligence-powered scanners to detect concealed guns and knives and alerts security personnel to conduct secondary screening.
- If people are not carrying weapons, they simply walk through without needing to empty their pockets or other personal articles.
- Upgrades to the system make SmartGateway very easy to learn and use, requiring fewer personnel to operate relative to other scanning systems.
- The system is customizable to fit the security needs and requirements of individual facilities, including look and feel.
- Ryan Smith, Program Manager – Security at Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc., recommended Xtract One to Hyundai Transys after being pleased with how the Gateway solutions looked and worked at their facilities.
- Since implementing the solution at Kia Georgia, a significant number of weapons have been prevented from entering the facility.
- Personnel screening time at Kia Georgia was drastically reduced from 21 seconds to 2.5 seconds.
- The additional time savings provides production flexibility to maintain work areas during crucial times in an industry based on "just in time" processes.
- Xtract One Technologies is a leading technology-driven threat detection and security solution company leveraging AI to provide seamless and secure patron access control experiences.
- The company makes unobtrusive threat detection systems that enable venue building operators to prioritize improved patron experiences while providing unprecedented safety.
- Its innovative multi-sensor Gateway product enables companies to covertly screen for weapons at points of entry without disrupting the flow of traffic.
- AI-based Xtract Vision allows venue and building operators to identify weapons and other threats inside and outside of facilities.
- Xtract One Insights provides valuable intelligence for optimizing operations.
