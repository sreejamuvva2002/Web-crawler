# Pirelli

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-05

**Source:** [PIRELLI WILL START CYBER™ TYRE PRODUCTION IN GEORGIA UNDERLINING THE STRATEGIC IMPORTANCE OF THE U.S. MARKET | Press Releases | lincolnjournal.com](https://www.lincolnjournal.com/online_features/press_releases/pirelli-will-start-cyber-tyre-production-in-georgia-underlining-the-strategic-importance-of-the-u/article_a1565c2e-aeaf-5712-b2c3-ee89b6a755b0.html)  ·  `page_048564`

## Facts

- Pirelli is reinforcing its long-term commitment to the United States with a key step in its product and industrial strategy.
- The Georgia plant, already dedicated to technologically advanced products for the U.S. market in the High-Value segment and Motorsport, will be further enhanced by production of connected tires featuring Cyber™ Tyre technology.
- The announcement was made as Pirelli participated in the SelectUSA Investment Summit, a flagship event promoted by the U.S. Department of Commerce, where Cyber™ Tyre was showcased as a core innovation shaping smart mobility.
- Cyber™ Tyre is the world's first hardware-and-software system capable of collecting data and information from sensors embedded in tires, processing them through Pirelli's proprietary software and algorithms, and communicating in real time with vehicle electronics to enable new functionalities integrated with driving and control systems to enhance driving experience and increase safety levels while supporting connected infrastructure.
- In the highly advanced U.S. market where digital and smart mobility solutions are rapidly expanding, Cyber™ Tyre represents a distinctive competitive advantage for Pirelli.
- At SelectUSA, Cyber™ Tyre was showcased at the Georgia State booth, highlighting Pirelli's industrial footprint in the state where it has been established for more than 20 years.
- Claudio Zanardo, CEO of Pirelli North America, stated that the start of Cyber™ Tyre production in the Rome, Georgia plant is a significant milestone for Pirelli in the country, reflecting commitment to bringing advanced technologies closer to the market and strengthening industrial footprint and innovation capabilities in the United States.
- To enhance Rome's role as a high-tech production site, Pirelli is finalizing the introduction of the latest version of the MIRS (Modular Integrated Robotized System) production process.
- The MIRS process will be the most advanced manufacturing process for high-end, premium production within the entire Pirelli Group and is exclusive to the Georgia factory.
- The new process further enhances robotized production capabilities, increasing productivity, flexibility, and quality.
- It is a highly digitalized system that enables a direct link between product design and its industrial application.
- This development lays groundwork for growth in Pirelli's production capacity in Rome, an integral part of the Group's industrial development plans, and will further strengthen Pirelli's presence in the United States, one of its most important and strategic markets.
- Investment and output expansion details will be communicated in the coming months following finalization of development plans.
- The Rome, Georgia plant specializes in high-value tire production for the North American market.
- The facility hosts a dedicated R&D center, further strengthening its role in technological development and product innovation.
- The facility is recognized for responsible sourcing practices, including use of FSC®-certified natural rubber, underscoring broader environmental commitment across the supply chain.
