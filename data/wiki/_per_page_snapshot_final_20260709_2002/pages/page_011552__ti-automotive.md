# TI Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-15

**Source:** [ABC Technologies Completes Acquisition of TI Fluid Systems; Combined Business Will Rebrand as TI Automotive - ABC Technologies](https://abcgroupinc.com/abc-technologies-completes-acquisition-of-ti-fluid-systems-combined-business-will-rebrand-as-ti-automotive/)  ·  `page_011552`

## Facts

- ABC Technologies completed the acquisition of TI Fluid Systems.
- The combined business will be rebranded as TI Automotive.
- The transaction creates a stronger, more diversified Tier 1 manufacturer supplying market-leading safety and performance critical products and highly engineered components.
- The new entity has a propulsion agnostic portfolio positioned to support a diverse range of customers.
- Combined revenue is $5.4 billion based on pro forma 2024 figures.
- The combined organization will have 34,600 employees across 26 countries.
- TI Automotive will be led by ABC President & Chief Executive Officer Terry Campbell as its President & CEO.
- An Executive Leadership Team combining the extensive skills and expertise of both ABC and TIFS will support Campbell.
- TI Automotive will be headquartered in Auburn Hills, Michigan.
- The business will be managed through a Regional Operating Model to ensure customer proximity and agility to respond rapidly to local market dynamics.
- Terry Campbell stated that the company has exceptional people, differentiated products, a deep commitment to safety, and a relentless focus on delivering for customers.
- Campbell noted that the combination creates exciting opportunities to drive innovation and manufacturing excellence while establishing foundations for future growth.
- TI Automotive is a Tier 1 supplier of market-leading safety and performance critical products and highly engineered components to the global automotive industry.
- The company has more than 100 years’ experience of solving customers’ safety, efficiency and environmental challenges.
- TI Automotive's capabilities include an exceptional team, powertrain agnostic product portfolio, vertical integration, global footprint, and customer proximity.
- The entity is a trusted partner to all the world’s leading OEMs.
- TI Automotive was created through ABC Technologies’ acquisition of TI Fluid Systems in April 2025.
- The company is majority owned by certain of the affiliated funds of Apollo Global Management, Inc. and its subsidiaries.
- Funds managed by Oaktree Capital Management, L.P. own a minority equity interest in the company.
