# Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd. (PAS) and Arm announced a strategic partnership to standardize automotive architecture for Software-Defined Vehicles (SDVs).
- The two organizations share a common vision for creating a software stack with the necessary flexibility to meet current and future needs of the automotive industry.
- They aligned on this vision through their active participation in SOAFEE, an industry-wide initiative promoting collaboration for the development of standardized software within the automotive market.
- Under this new partnership, PAS and Arm will apply and expand the VirtIO device virtualization framework to decouple automotive software development from hardware.
- This initiative aims to accelerate development cycles in the automotive industry.
- The announcement was issued from Yokohama, Japan & Cambridge, England.
