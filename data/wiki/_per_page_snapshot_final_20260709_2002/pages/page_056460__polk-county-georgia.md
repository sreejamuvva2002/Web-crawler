## Facts

- Polk County Georgia maintains a business-friendly attitude, dedicated workforce, and talent pipeline that contribute to the success of its present manufacturing base.
- Jefferson Southern Corp. is located in Polk County and employs 400 people.
- Jefferson Southern Corp. manufactures motor vehicles and passenger car bodies.
- Jefferson Southern Corp. operates as a structural auto parts manufacturer.
- The Development Authority of Polk County (DAPC) runs an Existing Industry Program that annually contacts local businesses to determine their operational needs.
- DAPC provides opportunities and resources addressing international trade, tax assistance, hiring support, workforce training, problem solving, and resource matching.
- Polk County and its municipalities are committed to collaborating to foster a manufacturing-friendly environment for industry.
