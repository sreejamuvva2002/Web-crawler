# Kubota Tractor Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Georgia Governor Joins Kubota for Gainesville Facility Groundbreaking - Dixie Contractor](https://www.acppubs.com/DXC/article/1D2FA29C-georgia-governor-joins-kubota-for-gainesville-facility-groundbreaking)  ·  `page_060394`

## Facts

- Kubota’s newest facility will consist of engineering offices, workshops, and testing buildings, as well as large-scale grading and development of several outdoor test courses for turf, utility vehicles, tractors, and other construction and agricultural-related equipment.
- The facility is the fourth and final link in what will complete the global chain of Kubota R&D facilities in Japan, Thailand, Europe, and now North America.
- The new facility enables Kubota to realize its goal to have products under development 24 hours per day for markets around the world.
- Haruyuki (Harry) Yoshida is President and CEO of Kubota North America and Kubota Tractor Corporations.
- The investment in this new facility marks a giant leap into a new era of growth and success in North America for Kubota.
- The facility will make it possible to design new products in Georgia, manufacture them there, and sell these new products all across the country.
- The equipment manufacturing industry is a core pillar of the Peach State’s economy.
- The equipment manufacturing industry supports nearly 39,000 jobs for hardworking Georgians.
- The equipment manufacturing industry has a $4-billion economic impact each year in Georgia.
- Governor Kemp joined Kubota Tractor Corporation for the groundbreaking of their newest facility as they expand their footprint in Georgia.
- Governor Kemp thanked Kubota for their continued commitment to creating opportunity for the people of Georgia.
