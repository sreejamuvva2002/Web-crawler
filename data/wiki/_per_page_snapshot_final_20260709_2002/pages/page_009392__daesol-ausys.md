# Daesol Ausys

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-08-29

**Source:** [Automotive Supplier Announces $72 Million Investment in Georgia | The Star News Network](https://thestarnewsnetwork.com/2023/08/30/automotive-supplier-announces-72-million-investment-in-georgia/?bureau=the-georgia-star-news&redirected=1)  ·  `page_009392`

## Facts

- Daesol Ausys is a South Korean automotive supplier that specializes in designing and manufacturing motor and electric vehicle interior components and accessories.
- Daesol Ausys announced an investment of $72 million to establish a new manufacturing facility in Harris County, Georgia.
- The company is identified as a key supplier for Kia Georgia, Hyundai Motor Group, and General Motors by the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD).
- The new facility will be located in West Point, Georgia, and will specifically produce luggage boards and covers.
- The facility will create more than 140 new jobs in Harris County.
- Daesol Ausys CEO Min Ho Kwon stated the company is committed to delivering world-class services that exceed its clients' requirements and expectations.
- Min Ho Kwon expressed excitement about the project in the Northwest Harris Business Park and looking forward to continuing partnerships with the State of Georgia and Harris County.
- New operations at the West Point facility are expected to begin in December 2024.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that West Point has become an automotive capital in its own right and is excited that Daesol Ausys will join other innovators in Harris County.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that Georgia's automotive industry employs over 50,000 hardworking Georgians and will continue to grow as e-mobility suppliers choose all corners of the state for future jobs.
- South Korea was one of the top three international sources of job creation in Georgia during Fiscal Year 2023.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that economic development is a team sport driven by the local level and congratulated Harris County and community partners for helping bring Daesol Ausys to West Georgia.
- Pat Wilson noted that the automotive industry is providing generational change in the region and across the state and expressed excitement for the opportunities Daesol Ausys will provide to Harris County residents.
