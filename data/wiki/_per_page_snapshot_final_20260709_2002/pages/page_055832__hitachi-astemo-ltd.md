# Hitachi Astemo, Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-19

**Source:** [Hitachi Astemo Unveils Innovative Autonomous Driving Technology for Narrow Roads - Self Drive News](https://selfdrivenews.com/hitachi-astemo-unveils-innovative-autonomous-driving-technology-for-narrow-roads/)  ·  `page_055832`

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo, Ltd. is developing cutting-edge autonomous driving technology to enable vehicles to safely and smoothly navigate and overtake other vehicles on narrow roads.
- The technology uses 3D sensing results to perceive the vehicle’s surrounding environment.
- Hitachi Astemo is focusing on using new stereo cameras that offer improved cost benefits to gather 3D sensing data, aiming to boost the firm’s cost competitiveness.
- Autonomous driving technology aims to address social issues such as traffic congestion and provide transportation for individuals with special needs, including the elderly and the disabled.
- Successful deployment of autonomous vehicles on public roads requires anticipating pedestrian behavior risks, circumventing sudden dangers, and planning safe driving trajectories using 3D sensing information.
- Astemo’s previous technology primarily focused on preventing collisions through predicting the behavior changes of pedestrians and other road users to enable safe deceleration during autonomous driving on general roads.
- The company pioneered a hazard prediction and avoidance system that drives in a manner akin to a human driver by predicting high-risk areas and adjusting speed and trajectory accordingly.
- The latest development integrates 3D information from LiDAR sensors to recognize the three-dimensional driving environment around the vehicle.
- The technology understands intended movement from detected free spaces, predicts the behavior of oncoming vehicles, and performs route prediction.
- This capability enables smooth passing automated driving in narrow roads by coordinating with oncoming vehicles.
- The company is collaborating with a newly launched high-precision, versatile stereo camera that can detect pedestrians and bicycles with high accuracy.
- The stereo camera measures distances by combining long-range detection with a wide viewing angle.
- This technology will be introduced at the “Automotive Engineering Exposition 2023 Yokohama” (booth number: 148) taking place at Pacifico Yokohama starting May 23rd.
- Hitachi Astemo continues to strengthen its business portfolio, which includes Powertrain & Safety Systems, Chassis, Motorcycle, Software, and Aftermarket businesses.
- The company aims for sustainable growth through “green,” “digital,” and “innovation” pillars.
- Hitachi Astemo develops xEV systems and highly efficient internal combustion engine systems that reduce emissions.
- The company provides advanced mobility solutions including autonomous driving systems, advanced driver assistance systems, and advanced chassis systems to enhance corporate value for customers.
