# Hyatt Place Augusta

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** 160 Mason McKnight Jr Pkwy, Augusta, GA 30907, United States, Georgia, United States

## Overview

A hotel in Augusta, Georgia that provides EV charging stations for its guests, including two Tesla High Power Wall Connectors and one Clipper Creek J1772 charger.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Offers on-site electric vehicle charging (two Tesla High Power Wall Connectors up to 40 A and one Clipper Creek J1772 up to 32 A) for hotel patrons.

## Related organizations & people

## Key facts

- The hotel provides two Tesla High Power Wall Connectors up to 40 A for guests.
- One Clipper Creek J1772 charger up to 32 A is also available on site.
- All chargers are located in the back side parking lot and are reserved for hotel patrons.
- Hotel amenities include a 24-hour front desk, ADA accessible business center, free continental breakfast, free WiFi, lounge/bar, meeting facilities, non-smoking rooms, parking, pet-friendly policy, and an on-site restaurant.
