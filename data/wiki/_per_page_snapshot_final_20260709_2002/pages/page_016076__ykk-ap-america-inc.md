# YKK AP America Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2022-08-19

**Source:** [Japan’s YKK AP Investing Another $125M in Georgia  - Global Atlanta](https://www.globalatlanta.com/japans-ykk-ap-investing-another-125m-in-georgia/)  ·  `page_016076`

## Facts

- YKK AP America Inc. announced an expansion investment of $125 million.
- The expansion includes hiring 100 additional people.
- Equipment installation is planned at a newly constructed plant in Macon.
- YKK AP is a subsidiary of YKK AP Inc. in Tokyo.
- YKK AP is a sister company of YKK, the Japanese zipper giant that first entered Georgia in the early 1970s, starting in Macon.
- YKK AP America operates its largest plant in Dublin, Ga., which opened in 1991 and focuses on commercial fenestration systems for facades.
- The Macon location has housed the residential division since 2007, primarily making vinyl windows and doors for builders and homeowners.
- The new plant is scheduled to open by the first quarter of 2024.
- The goal is to make the new plant a showpiece for future facilities across the U.S. and Canada.
- The facility will feature vertical integration consolidating vinyl extrusion, glass, and window production in one location.
- The current Macon workforce of about 250 people will move to the new plant upon completion.
- YKK AP America Inc. President Oliver Stepe stated the investment builds toward a long-term vision to lead the residential window and door market and expand business in and beyond the Southeast region.
- Across North America, YKK AP America employs 900 people in Georgia.
- YKK AP America also employs another 300 people at a Canadian subsidiary in Ontario.
- The new facility will be located at Macon-Bibb I-75 Industrial Park.
- JE Dunn will manage construction of the new facility.
- SSOE Group will serve as architects and engineers for the project.
