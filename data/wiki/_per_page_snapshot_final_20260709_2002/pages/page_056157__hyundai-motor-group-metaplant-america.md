# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hyundai and LG expand Georgia battery plant - Investment Monitor](https://www.investmentmonitor.ai/news/hyundai-lg-invest-georgia-ev-battery-plant/)  ·  `page_056157`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy Solution will invest an additional $2bn (2.64trn won) in their joint venture electric vehicle battery plant in Bryan County, Georgia.
- The joint investment will create 400 jobs.
- The investment brings the total project value to more than $4.3bn.
- The facility has a capacity of 30 gigawatt-hours.
- The plant will contribute to the production of 300,000 units of EVs annually.
- Hyundai will use cells from this Georgia plant to assemble battery packs for EV models for Hyundai, Kia, and Genesis at US manufacturing facilities.
- LG became a partner in Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America’s onsite battery cell manufacturing joint venture in May.
- Governor Brian P Kemp welcomed the investment expansion.
- Governor Kemp stated that in a single year they broke ground on the largest project in state history, landed multiple suppliers across the state for Hyundai’s Metaplant, and welcomed LGES to Bryan County.
- Governor Kemp said Georgia is continuing to make itself the e-mobility capital of the nation.
- Governor Kemp noted that major investments ultimately go to hardworking Georgians in the form of pay cheques, improved schools and infrastructure, and more.
- Governor Kemp thanked Hyundai Motor Group and LGES for again recognising Georgia as the number one state for business.
- Bryan County is located south of the coastal city of Savannah.
- In 2021, manufacturing companies around Savannah accounted for 16.5% of the region’s economic output, or $4.2bn, according to figures released by the Savannah Area Chamber of Commerce.
- The new battery plant in Georgia is part of US plans to boost domestic investment in the EV batteries sector.
- In June 2022, US President Joe Biden allowed the Department of Energy to use the Defense Production Act to encourage domestic production of minerals required to make EV batteries.
- As of June 2023, the White House reported that private companies had pledged $491bn of investment through the ‘Manufacturing the Future’ programme.
- Of the total pledge, $149bn was allocated to EVs and batteries projects, according to Verdict’s report on the changing global map of battery cell manufacturing for the EV market.
- Hyundai is an automotive manufacturer based in South Korea.
- In June, Hyundai announced it was building a $60m factory in Bekasi, Indonesia to expand its global supply of EV batteries and provide systems for flagship vehicle models in the region.
- José Muñoz, president and CEO at Hyundai and Genesis Motor North America, stated that the incremental investment in Bryan County reflects continued commitment to create a more sustainable future powered by American workers.
- José Muñoz said Hyundai is proud to partner with LGES and is grateful for the support of Governor Kemp and communities throughout Georgia.
