# Mack Trucks

## Facts

- Jonathan Randall, president of Mack Trucks North America, announced that the order book for the all-new Anthem officially opened this week.
- Since unveiling the truck in June, customer response has been tremendous.
- Fleet managers and owner-operators can now order this game-changing truck that truly represents the new standard in the industry.
- The new Anthem features a 113.5-inch bumper-to-back-of-cab length, making it ideal for navigating tight spaces and urban environments.
- The redesigned hood dramatically improves forward visibility, allowing drivers to see approximately 12 % closer to the front bumper.
- Fernando Couceiro, vice president of highway trucks at Mack Trucks, stated that customers can finally experience these innovations firsthand.
- The Anthem excels in maneuverability.
- Fleet operators can save up to $5,000 annually in fuel costs per truck with the Anthem.
- The Anthem is available in day cab, 44-inch sleeper, and 64-inch sleeper configurations.
- The Anthem features premium interior cab space and a cab that's nine inches wider than the previous model.
- Advanced safety features include Mack Protect, Mack’s new standard collision mitigation system.
- Advanced safety features include a front airbag.
- An optional Side Integrated Curtain Airbag (ICA) system is available on the Anthem.
- An optional digital mirror system is available on the Anthem.
- The truck incorporates Mack Connect telematics platform and GuardDog Connect integration.
- GuardDog Connect has demonstrated a 37% reduction in overall downtime through predictive maintenance capabilities.
- GuardDog Connect has demonstrated a 20% reduction in unplanned stops through predictive maintenance capabilities.
- Target industries for the new Anthem include bulk haulers, dry bulk operations, food and beverage distribution, general freight operations and less-than-truckload (LTL) services.
- Serial production on the all-new Anthem is scheduled to begin in January 2026.
- Mack Trucks has provided purpose-built transportation solutions for 125 years.
- Mack is one of North America’s largest producers of heavy-duty trucks.
- Mack trucks are sold and serviced through an extensive distribution network in more than 45 countries.
- Mack trucks, diesel engines and transmissions sold in North America are assembled in the United States.
- Mack manufacturing locations are certified to the internationally recognized ISO 9001 standard for quality.
- Mack manufacturing locations are certified to the ISO 14001 standard for environmental management systems.
- Mack manufacturing locations are certified to the ISO 45001 standard for health and safety management systems.
- Mack is a proud sponsor of Share the Road, an American Trucking Associations public information campaign aimed at enhancing the safety of our nation's roadways.
- Mack Trucks is part of the Volvo Group.
- The Volvo Group offers trucks, buses, construction equipment, power solutions for marine and industrial applications, financing and services that increase customer uptime and productivity.
- The Volvo Group was founded in 1927 and is committed to shaping the future landscape of sustainable transport and infrastructure solutions.
- The Volvo Group is headquartered in Gothenburg, Sweden.
- The Volvo Group employs about 104,000 people.
- The Volvo Group serves customers in almost 190 markets.
- In 2024, net sales amounted to about $50 billion.
