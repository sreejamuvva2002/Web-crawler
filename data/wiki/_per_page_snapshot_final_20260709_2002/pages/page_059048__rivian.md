# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-12

**Source:** [2027 Rivian R2: Pricing, Trims, Range, and Specs Revealed](https://www.motortrend.com/reviews/2027-rivian-r2-trims-pricing-specs-range)  ·  `page_059048`

## Facts

- Rivian is building a new plant east of Atlanta, Georgia, which will come online to expand R2 sales to other regions after production at the Normal, Illinois plant ramps up to full speed.
- The midsize electric SUV R2 is expected to bring international scale to the EV brand and is designed to meet global regulations.
- Rivian CEO RJ Scaringe has promised a $45,000 base price for the R2, though destination charges will add $1,495 to the cost.
- The Standard entry model is scheduled for late 2027 as a 2028 model, with potential inflation possibly increasing the price by launch.
- The base RWD model features a single 350-hp, 355-lb-ft motor mounted in back, achieves 0-60 mph in 5.9 seconds, and includes a standard-range battery providing over 275 miles of range.
- The Standard Long Range RWD will arrive in the first half of 2027 with an 87.9-kWh battery pack for up to 345 miles of range at a cost of $49,985.
- Standard R2 trims include a 525-watt five-speaker audio system, power heated front seats and steering wheel, four drive modes (All-Purpose, Conserve, Snow, Sport), 19-inch wheels, and only a Black Crater interior.
- Optional equipment for Standard models includes 20-inch wheels, a spare tire, an Autonomy+ L2+ hands-off/eyes-on cruise control subscription, and an upgrade to the Premium model’s dual-motor AWD setup (450-hp, 537-lb-ft).
- The R2 Premium trim opens at $55,485, arrives in late 2026 as a 2027 model, and comes standard with a dual-motor AWD setup (450 hp, 537 lb-ft) achieving 0-60 mph in 4.6 seconds.
- The Premium trim uses a long-range 87.9-kWh battery pack for up to 330 miles of range and includes standard upgrades like 20-inch wheels, tow hooks, All-Terrain drive mode, a premium interior, 975-watt nine-speaker audio, matrix-LED adaptive headlights, a Rivian Torch flashlight, and rear glass that motors down into the hatchback.
- Premium options include an Autonomy+ subscription, spare tire, 21-inch wheels, a towing package, and a Performance upgrade.
- The R2 pricing straddles America’s average transaction price.
- Transitioning from the R1’s body-on-skateboard architecture to the R2’s unitized construction with the battery as the structural floor pan makes adding a pickup bed exponentially harder, resulting in no truck variant.
