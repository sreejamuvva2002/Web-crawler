# Great Dane LP

## Facts

- Great Dane LP, Savannah, Ga., has acquired a production facility in Scott County, Tenn.
- The anticipated start-up date of the new facility is the first half of 2005.
- Sam Gupta, recently promoted to senior vice president of manufacturing, will lead preparation of the plant for the production of Great Dane trailers.
- Gupta has more than 26 years of truck trailer manufacturing experience, including 14 years as Great Dane’s vice president of manufacturing for the Van Division.
- The Scott County facility, located in the northeastern part of Tennessee, was previously owned by Wabash National Corp.
