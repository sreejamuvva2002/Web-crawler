# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2018-06-11

**Source:** [Yamaha Celebrates 30 Years of U.S. Manufacturing](https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2018/06/11/1519719/0/en/Yamaha-Celebrates-30-Years-of-U-S-Manufacturing.html)  ·  `page_004506`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America (YMMC) and Yamaha Motor Corp., USA (YMUS) are celebrating 30 years of U.S. manufacturing at the Newnan, Georgia facility as of June 11, 2018.
- Over three decades, YMMC has produced more than 3.5 million vehicles including ATVs, SxS vehicles, golf cars, and watercraft for worldwide distribution.
- Yamaha’s "Assembled in USA" brand represents the highest standards in powersports manufacturing and supports thousands of local U.S. jobs.
- YMMC President Mike Chrzanowski stated that U.S. production ensures legendary quality and reliability while providing high responsiveness to U.S. market demands and streamlined distribution.
- Over the past 15 years leading up to 2018, Yamaha invested more than $354 million to create and maintain a state-of-the-art facility in Newnan.
- The Newnan manufacturing facility spans 1,355,000 square feet.
- Of Yamaha’s approximately 3,400 total U.S. employees, more than 1,500 people are directly employed at the YMMC factory.
- The YMMC factory is certified under the ISO9001 Quality Management System and ISO14001 Environmental Management System.
- Founded in 1986, the YMMC facility produces Golf Cars, Personal Watercraft, Side-by-Side Vehicles, and All-Terrain Vehicles (ATVs) to high standards for quality, safety, and environmentally-responsible manufacturing.
- Yamaha Motor Corporation, USA (YMUS), headquartered in California since 1960, also maintains facilities in Wisconsin and Georgia, along with factory operations in Tennessee and Georgia.
- YMUS products are sold through a nationwide network of dealers in the United States.
