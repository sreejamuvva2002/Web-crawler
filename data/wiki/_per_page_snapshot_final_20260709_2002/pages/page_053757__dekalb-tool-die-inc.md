# Dekalb Tool & Die, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Deep Drawn Metal Stamping Tool and Die Laser Cutting Manufacturing - Tucker Georgia](https://dekalbtool.com/deep-drawn-stamping-of-galvanized-steel-bus-roof-cap/)  ·  `page_053757`

## Facts

- Dekalb Tool & Die, Inc. operates as a metal stamping manufacturing source for the transportation industry.
- The company manufactures galvanized steel roof caps for buses with eight different hole configuration variations.
- The main manufacturing process is deep drawn metal stamping utilizing Dekalb Tool and Die’s 1,800 ton large double action press.
- The press stamps a 12″ deep draw into a 52″ x 112″ galvanized steel blank with a .047″ (18 ga.) thickness.
- DeKalb Tool and Die uses a precision Mitsubishi VZ-1 5-axis laser for the laser trimming and hole creation process.
- The use of the laser eliminates the need for an additional trim die, creating lower tooling costs for the customer and a more efficient production run with the requirement for multiple variations.
- Galvanized steel roof cap metal stampings are finished with a powder coat by one of Dekalb Tool and Die’s finishing production partners.
- Finished metal stampings are shipped and delivered to Dekalb Tool & Die’s customer located in the Southeastern U.S. on a Dekalb Tool & Die truck.
- The sheet metal stamped bus roof cap was custom manufactured in 8 different variations in a low volume part run.
- Trim line tolerance is ± .03.
- Hole position tolerance is ± .015.
- Material specifications include 18 Ga. Galvanized Steel with an E-coat base/ Powder Coat Finish (Out sourced).
- Each finished unit weighs 21 lbs.
- Applications for the stamped components include Truck, Bus and Heavy Equipment.
- The production run size is 5,000 units.
- Customers supply prints and 2D CAD drawings for manufacturing.
