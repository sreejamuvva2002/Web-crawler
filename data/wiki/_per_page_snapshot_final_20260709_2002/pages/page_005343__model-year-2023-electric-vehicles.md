# Model Year 2023 Electric Vehicles

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-07

**Source:** [FOTW #1323, January 1, 2024: Top Range for Model Year 2023 EVs was 516 Miles on a Single Charge | Department of Energy](https://www.energy.gov/cmei/vehicles/articles/fotw-1323-january-1-2024-top-range-model-year-2023-evs-was-516-miles-single)  ·  `page_005343`

## Facts

- In model year (MY) 2023, the highest top range for an EV was 516 miles on a single charge (Lucid Air).
- The median range for all EV models rose to a new high of 270 miles.
- The number of models and their ranges have greatly increased since modern EVs were introduced in 2011, when four models were available with ranges spanning 63 to 94 miles per charge.
- Range is based on Environmental Protection Agency estimates.
- Data does not include plug-in hybrid vehicles.
- Only vehicles that were certified by the EPA are included in the data.
- Source is the U.S. Department of Energy and U.S. Environmental Protection Agency, Fueleconomy.gov website, accessed November 7, 2023.
