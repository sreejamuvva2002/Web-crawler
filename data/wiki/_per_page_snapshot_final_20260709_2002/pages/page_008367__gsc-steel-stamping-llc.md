## Facts

- GSC Steel Stamping, LLC is a minority- and family-owned automotive parts manufacturer.
- GSC Steel Stamping has acquired Dixien, LLC's assets.
- The company continues its 43-year history of automotive parts stamping.
- GSC Steel Stamping is led by CEO Vincent Henderson.
- The company is committed to excellence, investing in technology, and maintaining a highly experienced staff.
- GSC Steel Stamping's facility is located in Lake City, GA.
- The facility spans 200,000 square feet.
- It operates as the largest minority-owned automotive stamping plant globally.
- Bulldog Steel Fabrication is a premier supplier of steel fabricated parts and components in Georgia.
- Bulldog Steel Fabrication's main facility is located at 1580 Greensboro Hwy., Madison GA.
- Bulldog Steel Fabrication hosted an in-person job fair on March 5th from 11am to 2pm for job seekers in Morgan County and surrounding areas.
- Carlin Thomas has served as President and General Manager of Bulldog Steel Fabrication since 2011.
- U.S. Congressman Jody Hice visited Bulldog Steel Fabrication to recognize their success and thank them for community support.
- Bulldog Steel Fabrication completed construction of a 15,000 square foot welding facility expansion and an eco-friendly Side Down Draft Paint Booth.
- Bulldog Steel Fabrication added three new machines to its production line during its expansion.
- Georgia Piedmont Technical College nominated Bulldog Steel Fabrication for the 2017 Manufacturer of the Year award in the Morgan County Georgia – Small Business Category.
- Bulldog Steel Fabrication introduced custom Tiny House Chassis capabilities at the 2nd annual Georgia Tiny House Festival.
- Loni Johnson was promoted to Operations and Finance Manager at Bulldog Steel Fabrication to oversee various departments including finance.
