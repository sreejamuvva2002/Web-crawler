# Augusta, Georgia EV Charging Infrastructure

**Type:** Charging Infrastructure  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Augusta, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

The city of Augusta in Georgia, United States, has 126 public charging station ports (Level 2 and Level 3) within a 15 km radius.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Public electric-vehicle charging infrastructure serving drivers in Augusta, Georgia.

## Related organizations & people

- Tesla — Charging network provider
- ChargePoint — Charging network provider
- Flash — Charging network provider
- City of Augusta — Municipality

## Key facts

- 126 public charging station ports (Level 2 and Level 3) are available within a 15 km radius of Augusta.
- 56% of the ports are Level 2 stations, totaling 71 Level 2 ports.
- 44% of the ports are Level 3 stations, totaling 55 Level 3 ports.
- 32% of the ports (40 stations) offer free charging.
- The main charging networks operating in Augusta are Tesla, ChargePoint, and Flash.
