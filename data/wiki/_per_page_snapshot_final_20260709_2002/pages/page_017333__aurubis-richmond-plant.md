# Aurubis Richmond Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Aurubis began commissioning its first US multimetal recycling plant in September 2025.
- The facility produces strategic metals including copper, nickel, tin, and precious metals for American energy infrastructure, data centers, AI applications, and the defense industry.
- The project created more than 240 jobs in Georgia.
- The investment amount is approximately €740 million.
- During the expansion stage starting in 2026, the site will process up to 180,000 t of complex recycling material annually.
- The complex recycling material includes circuit boards, copper cable, and other metal-bearing products.
- The Georgia site serves as a springboard for further US growth, including options for value chain expansion and new strategic partnerships.
- Aurubis is scaling its value creation close to crucial resource and customer streams of the future.
