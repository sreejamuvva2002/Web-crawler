## Facts

- Ascend Elements is a U.S.-based company revolutionizing the production of sustainable, engineered battery materials for electric vehicles and other energy storage applications.
- The company specializes in a patented Hydro-to-Cathode® direct precursor synthesis process that transforms recycled lithium-ion batteries and manufacturing scrap into high-performance, customized cathode active materials (CAM) and cathode precursor (pCAM).
- This approach significantly lowers costs, reduces carbon footprint, and supports the creation of a closed-loop, circular supply chain for critical battery components, primarily in North America.
