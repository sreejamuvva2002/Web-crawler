## Facts

- Enchem America, Inc. is a global leader specializing in the development and manufacturing of electrolytes and high functionality additives for rechargeable batteries.
- The company was founded in 2012 in South Korea.
- Enchem achieved the first commercialization of electrolytes for the world's first second-generation electric vehicles.
- Enchem operates factories in South Korea, China, Poland, Hungary, and the United States.
- Enchem America, Inc. is seeking a Quality Assurance Manager based in Commerce.
- The predicted salary for the Quality Assurance Manager position is between 72000 - 108000 $ per year.
- The role requires a Bachelor's degree or higher in Chemistry or a related field.
- Candidates must have 10 + years of relevant experience in a quality assurance role within the chemical or manufacturing industry.
- The position is at the Senior Manager level or above.
- Responsibilities include leading responses to customer Voice of Customer, Voice of Business, and Customer Satisfaction concerns, as well as audit requests.
- The manager will manage and maintain ISO/IATF certification systems, including preparation for external and internal audits.
- The role involves overseeing quality processes such as VDA, 4M change management, PPAP submissions, delivery specifications, and internal quality audits.
- The position requires revising and managing standard operating procedures related to quality and manufacturing, and leading the standardization of non-standard workflows.
- The manager will develop and oversee an Environmental, Social, and Governance compliance framework for quality management.
- The role includes coordinating the implementation and effectiveness evaluation of global quality control, assurance, and certification improvement initiatives.
- Preferred qualifications include hands-on experience with audits in the chemical industry and experience managing audits from automotive clients.
- Candidates are preferred to be certified internal auditors for ISO 9001 and IATF 16949.
- Possession of ISO, IATF, or VDA certifications is considered a strong plus.
- Experience implementing and managing ESG-related quality initiatives is preferred.
