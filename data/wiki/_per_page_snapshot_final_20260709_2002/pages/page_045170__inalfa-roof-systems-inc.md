## Facts

- In late 2020, Inalfa Roof Systems employed workers as assembly operators.
- The assembly operator position required the ability to stand and walk for up to 12 hours; to stoop, kneel, and crouch; and to repetitively lift, carry, push, and pull up to 50 pounds.
- Inalfa relied upon physical examinations and post-hire questionnaires during its hiring process to determine if candidates could meet the physical demands of the job.
- Employees at Inalfa Roof Systems work on an assembly line where they reach across to pick up brackets.
