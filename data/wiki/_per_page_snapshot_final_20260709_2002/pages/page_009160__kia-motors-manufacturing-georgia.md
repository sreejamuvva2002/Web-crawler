# Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2019-10-11

**Source:** [Manufacturing Leaders Share Success Stories at Georgia Manufacturing Summit -  Georgia Manufacturing Alliance](https://www.georgiamanufacturingalliance.com/news/manufacturing-leaders-share-success-stories-at-georgia-manufacturing-summit)  ·  `page_009160`

## Facts

- The 5th annual Georgia Manufacturing Summit was held on Oct. 9, 2019, at the Cobb Galleria in Atlanta.
- Almost 800 manufacturing insiders attended the summit.
- Stuart Countess, COO of Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, spoke at the summit.
- Werhner Washington, recently retired plant manager for the Procter and Gamble Albany plant, also spoke at the summit.
- Werhner Washington’s career at Procter and Gamble spanned more than 35 years.
- In 2019, Werhner Washington led the Albany plant to win the “Manufacturer of the Year” award for Georgia.
- Washington stated that delivering total shareholder returns was always their goal rather than winning awards.
- Washington added that having the right culture is the key to success.
