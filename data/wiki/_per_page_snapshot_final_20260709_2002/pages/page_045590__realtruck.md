# RealTruck

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-05-10

**Source:** [RealTruck Acquires Lund International | Mergr](https://mergr.com/transaction/realtruck-acquires-lund-international)  ·  `page_045590`

## Facts

- RealTruck is a designer, manufacturer and marketer of branded consumer accessories for pickup trucks.
- RealTruck produces truck bed liners under the BedRug brand.
- RealTruck was founded in 2007.
- RealTruck is based in Ann Arbor, Michigan.
- RealTruck acquired Lund International on May 10, 2019 as an add-on acquisition.
- Highlander Partners served as the seller for the Lund International acquisition.
- Katten Muchin Rosenman acted as legal advisor for the transaction.
- RealTruck acquired Backrack on April 23, 2019; Backrack manufactures heavy-duty truck racks for pickup trucks in North America and was founded in 1988 in Oakville, Ontario.
- RealTruck acquired Go Rhino on August 22, 2022; Go Rhino develops and manufactures accessories for truck, Jeep and SUV and is based in Guadalajara, Mexico.
- Highlander Partners is a private equity firm focused on direct private equity and mezzanine investments in companies located in the US and Europe.
- Highlander Partners targets investments in small and mid-size private companies with strong management teams, fundamentally strong operations and positive cash flows.
- Highlander Partners looks to invest $10 to $75 million as equity and $10 to $25 million in mezzanine financing in companies with revenues of $10 to $500 million and enterprise value up to $100 million.
- Specific areas of interest for Highlander Partners include manufacturing, food, healthcare products and services, healthcare IT, retail and building materials.
- Highlander Partners was formed in 2004 and is based in Dallas, Texas with an additional office in Warszawa, Poland.
