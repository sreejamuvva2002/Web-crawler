# Inalfa Roof Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-08-22

**Source:** [Inalfa Roof Systems Appoints Dave Winter as Regional President North America](https://www.prnewswire.co.uk/news-releases/inalfa-roof-systems-appoints-dave-winter-as-regional-president-north-america-302228457.html)  ·  `page_045195`

## Facts

- Inalfa Roof Systems is a global leader in automotive roof system design and manufacturing.
- Dave Winter was appointed as Regional President for North America, effective immediately on Aug. 22, 2024.
- Winter brings over 25 years of industry experience, having held key leadership roles in major automotive companies where he drove operational excellence, efficiency, and growth.
- At Inalfa, Winter will oversee operations at the company's North American facilities, collaborating closely with teams in the United States, Mexico, and across the global network.
- The appointment underscores Inalfa's commitment to strengthening its leadership and advancing its position in the global automotive market.
- Winter stated he is excited to join Inalfa Roof Systems and work with a talented team to build on the company's strong foundation and drive innovation and excellence.
- Together, Winter and the team will continue delivering high-quality products that meet and exceed customer expectations.
- Prior to joining Inalfa, Winter held various executive positions in operations at automotive companies such as Lear and Faurecia.
- Most recently, Winter served as Executive Vice President of Operations at Camaco-Amvian.
- Winter holds a Master degree in Business Administration obtained at the Keller Graduate School of Management in Illinois, United States.
- Inalfa Roof Systems is based in Venray, The Netherlands, with factories and development centers in Europe, Northern America and Asia.
- Inalfa holds over 800 life patents and has a global market share of approximately 25%.
- Inalfa delivers roof systems to almost every major car and truck manufacturer in the world.
- Since 2011, Inalfa Roof Systems has been part of Beijing Hainachuan Automotive Parts Co., Ltd. (BHAP).
- BHAP is one of the biggest suppliers of automotive parts in China.
- The strategic acquisition of Inalfa by BHAP further diversifies BHAP's product portfolio, strengthens its R&D and marketing capabilities, and enhances its competitiveness.
