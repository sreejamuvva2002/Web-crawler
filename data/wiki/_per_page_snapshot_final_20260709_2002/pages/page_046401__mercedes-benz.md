# Mercedes-Benz

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-05-22

**Source:** [Mercedes-Benz to Establish North American HQ in Georgia | Industrial Equipment News](https://www.ien.com/operations/news/22941838/mercedesbenz-to-establish-north-american-hq-in-georgia)  ·  `page_046401`

## Facts

- Mercedes-Benz announced plans to establish its North American headquarters in metro Atlanta.
- The automaker will expand its existing U.S. corporate hub by adding hundreds of jobs relocated from other cities.
- The change will add about 500 jobs to the Georgia facility that has served as Mercedes-Benz's U.S. headquarters since 2018.
- The Georgia facility currently employs about 800 people.
- Mercedes-Benz plans to add a new, multimillion-dollar research and development center nearby the headquarters.
- Most of the additional jobs coming to Sandy Springs north of Atlanta are being moved from the Detroit area.
- Mercedes-Benz is closing its financial services headquarters in the Detroit area.
- The headquarters expansion in Georgia should be completed by August 2026.
- Jason Hoff, CEO of Mercedes-Benz North America, stated that bringing teams closer together will enable greater agility, increased speed to market, and ensure the best customer experience.
- The headquarters expansion bolsters Georgia's reputation for luring automotive jobs.
- Porsche also has its North American headquarters outside Atlanta.
- Hyundai and Kia both have large manufacturing plants in Georgia.
- Electric SUV maker Rivian is also building a Georgia factory.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia continues to lead the way in the future of mobility and technical innovation, attracting world-class companies like Mercedes-Benz that are driving the automotive industry forward.
