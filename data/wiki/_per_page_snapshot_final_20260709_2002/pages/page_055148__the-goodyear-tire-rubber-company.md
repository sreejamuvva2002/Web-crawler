# The Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company

## Facts

- Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company has entered into a definitive agreement to sell its Off-the-Road (OTR) tire business to The Yokohama Rubber Company, Limited for $905 million in cash.
- The sale is part of Goodyear's broader Goodyear Forward transformation plan.
- Yokohama will acquire Goodyear’s OTR tire segment, which provides high-quality tires for mining, construction, and industrial markets globally.
- Mark Stewart, Goodyear Chief Executive Officer and President, stated that the sale marks an important milestone in executing the Goodyear Forward transformation plan.
- Goodyear will continue to supply OTR tires for U.S. military and defense applications following the transaction.
- Through a Product Supply Agreement, Goodyear will manufacture certain OTR tires for Yokohama at specific facilities for up to five years post-transaction.
- The transaction is pending regulatory and customary approvals.
- The deal is expected to close by early 2025.
- Proceeds from the sale will help Goodyear reduce debt and support initiatives under the Goodyear Forward transformation plan.
