# Daesol Ausys Georgia

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** West Point, Harris County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Daesol Ausys Georgia, an automotive supplier specializing in motor and electric vehicle interior components, announced a $72 million investment to build a new manufacturing facility in West Point’s Northwest Harris Business Park. The plant will produce luggage boards and covers, create more than 140 jobs, and begin operations in December 2024.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Supplier of electric-vehicle interior components (luggage boards and covers) supporting Georgia’s EV supply chain.
