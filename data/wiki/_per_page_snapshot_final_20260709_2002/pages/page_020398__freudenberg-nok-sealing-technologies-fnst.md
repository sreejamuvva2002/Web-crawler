# Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies (FNST)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-06-11

**Source:** [Freudenberg-NOK To Expand Morristown, Indiana, Facility - AftermarketNews](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/freudenberg-nok-to-expand-morristown-indiana-facility/)  ·  `page_020398`

## Facts

- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies (FNST) announced plans to expand its Morristown, Indiana manufacturing facility due to an uptick in automotive industry activity.
- The company will pursue a 20,000-square-foot expansion of its existing 65,000-square-foot facility.
- FNST plans to hire an estimated 75 new production, supervisory, and administrative employees over the next five years.
- The plant currently employs 266 workers.
- Freudenberg-NOK’s Morristown plant produces products for both general industry and automotive customers.
- The facility’s core products include off-set seals, transmission seals, fuel system seals, driveline seals, and diaphragms.
- The plant produces more than 100 million components annually.
- The company expects to invest $20 million in the five-year expansion project.
- The project investment will include new building construction, interior modifications, driveway modifications, and additional employee parking.
- The project investment also includes a significant investment in capital equipment.
- FNST executives worked with the Indiana Economic Development Corp., Shelby County Development Corp., and the city of Morristown to secure the expansion project within Indiana and Morristown.
