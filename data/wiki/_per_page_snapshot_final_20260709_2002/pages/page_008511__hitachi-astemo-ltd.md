# Hitachi Astemo, Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-17

**Source:** [Hitachi Astemo Continues INDYCAR Partnership with Team Penske and Chevrolet - Roastbrief US](https://roastbrief.us/hitachi-astemo-continues-indycar-partnership-with-team-penske-and-chevrolet/)  ·  `page_008511`

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo (Astemo), a leading automotive industry supplier, continues its NTT INDYCAR SERIES partnership with Team Penske for a 14th consecutive year in 2025 as a sponsor of the No. 2 Dallara/Chevrolet driven by Josef Newgarden.
- Astemo supplies fuel-system components for all of the 2.2-liter twin-turbocharged Chevrolet race engines in the series.
- Hitachi Astemo is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, and is a joint venture between Hitachi, Ltd., Honda Motor Co., and JIC Capital, Ltd.
- The company has 80,000 employees worldwide with operations in the Americas, Asia, China, Europe, and Japan.
- Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc. manufactures and markets engine management, electric powertrain, and integrated vehicle controls for major automotive manufacturers worldwide.
- Rob Sharpe serves as senior vice president of sales and marketing at Hitachi Astemo Americas.
