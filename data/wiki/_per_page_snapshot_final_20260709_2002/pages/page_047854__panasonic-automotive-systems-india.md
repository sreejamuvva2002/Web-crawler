# Panasonic Automotive Systems India

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-01-22

**Source:** [Panasonic Automotive Systems India to offer futuristic technological solutions | Online News 9](https://onlinenews9.in/business/panasonic-automotive-systems-india-to-offer-futuristic-technological-solutions/)  ·  `page_047854`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems India (PASI) is a subsidiary of Panasonic Automotive Systems Co. Ltd, a global leader in advanced automotive solutions that make driving more enjoyable and fulfilling, providing systems that support safe driving, and offer optimal in-vehicle systems and solutions for environmentally friendly vehicles.
- PASI focuses on the development, manufacturing, and sale of automotive components and parts like infotainment solutions (audio, video, and navigation), sensors, relays, cameras, parking assistance systems, telematics control units, smart entry systems, LED solutions for headlamps and batteries.
- PASI offers the latest technologies such as Head-up Displays (HUD), Telematic Control Units (TCU), Temperature Oil Sensor, and in-car infotainment to name a few that are used across global markets to advance India’s automotive industry.
- Bhupesh Kanyal serves as the Director of Panasonic Automotive Systems India (PASI).
- Bhupesh Kanyal stated that the Indian motor vehicle industry is the fourth largest in the world and continues to grow rapidly.
- In the two-wheeler segment, India is the largest manufacturer.
- With strong economic growth of India and rising income of Indian households, the potential for growth in the automotive industry is significant.
- The advent of new-age technologies and newer forms of mobility such as Electric Vehicles has caused consumer aspirations to evolve.
- Indian consumers are looking for a premium commuting experience as well as passenger and environmental safety, resulting in demand for an improved passenger vehicle experience.
- Panasonic is transforming the in-car experience by harnessing new technologies such as Connected Devices, Head-Up Displays, Wireless Chargers, and 360° Camera Solutions that are simple to use, safe and durable, and cost-effective.
- PASI is currently catering to the top OEMs of the automotive industry and aims to expand its customer base.
- PASI’s range of solutions can be categorized under three themes: Beyond Comfort, Integrated Safety, and Electrification.
- The Beyond Comfort theme caters to automobile interiors featuring cockpits, navigation systems, and HUDs.
- The Integrated Safety theme aids in safe commuting and reducing traffic accidents, featuring products like back & corner sensors, interior rearview mirrors, vehicle camera modules, LED modules for head lamps, and steering angle sensors.
- In the area of electrification, Panasonic caters to the Electric Vehicles (EV) category in the form of Li-ion batteries for EVs, Heater Devices, and on-board charging systems.
- PASI was established in April 2010.
- As of the publication date, PASI has a presence across all automotive clusters working with top OEMs in India.
- In India, PASI continues to have a vast delivery and service network with highly trained employees and fully equipped facilities to support OEMs and end users.
- PASI offers in-house-designed, cost-effective Head-Up Display (HUD) technologies to meet specific OEM needs, including windshield HUDs and combiner HUD systems that present data to drivers while keeping their attention on the road.
- In-Vehicle Infotainment (IVI) solutions provide audio/video entertainment and information content including radio, media player, TV and video, telephony, Navigation, speech control, Apps, and Connectivity features to help OEMs provide a holistic electronic cockpit concept.
- Telematics Control Units (TCU) assist in locating the position of the vehicle, making an emergency call during an accident or breakdown, tracking a stolen vehicle, and recording diagnostic codes for service stations.
- Panasonic’s advanced TCU solutions provide holistic information to the vehicle owner about the health of the vehicle, mode of riding/driving, tyre pressure, and fuel level/battery charge status accessible on a mobile application.
- PASI provides Back/Lane Watch cameras integrated in Outer Rear View mirrors (ORVM) and rear view cameras for parking assistance, enabling drivers to avoid distraction and enhance safety by covering the blindspot.
- Panasonic provides vehicle sensors for 2W and 4W applications for Engine applications, Speed monitoring, Ambient Temperature, and Crank Angle Detection.
- PASI provides high quality and reliable LED modules for head lamps, winkers, and tail lights.
- PASI’s wireless chargers use Panasonic moving coil technology with a proprietary algorithm to automatically detect the device’s coil and align it with the charger’s coil, improving alignment accuracy and enabling highly efficient charging between smartphones and wireless chargers.
