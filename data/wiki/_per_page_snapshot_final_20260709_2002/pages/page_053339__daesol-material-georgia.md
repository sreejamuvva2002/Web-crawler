# Daesol Material Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-01-05

**Source:** [Daesol Material Georgia builds USD 35 million manufacturing plant in Harris County - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/news/210047)  ·  `page_053339`

## Facts

- Daesol Material Georgia is building a manufacturing plant in Harris County.
- The investment for the manufacturing plant is USD 35 million.
- Daesol will produce automotive fiber padding at the facility.
- The produced automotive fiber padding is supplied to Daesol's nearby sister company, Daehon Solution.
- Daehon Solution supplies noise, vibration and harshness systems.
