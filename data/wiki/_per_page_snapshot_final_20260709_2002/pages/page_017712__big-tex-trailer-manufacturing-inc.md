# Big Tex Trailer Manufacturing, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-12-21

**Source:** [Brain Capital Private Equity Acquires Big Tex - CM Truck Beds](https://cmtruckbeds.com/bain-capital-acquires-big-tex-leading-manufacturer-of-professional-grade-trailers/)  ·  `page_017712`

## Facts

- Bain Capital Private Equity completed the acquisition of Big Tex from H.I.G. Capital on December 21, 2015.
- Financial terms of the private transaction were not disclosed.
- Big Tex is headquartered in Mt. Pleasant, Texas.
- Big Tex manufactures professional-grade open utility, equipment, horse, livestock, and cargo trailers and truck beds under brands including Big Tex, CM, S&H, and Circle J.
- The company was founded in 1982 and serves dealers and customers across North America.
- Curt Howell is the Chief Executive Officer of Big Tex and will continue to lead the business with the existing management team.
- Todd Cook is a Managing Director at Bain Capital.
- Kirkland & Ellis LLP served as legal counsel for Bain Capital Private Equity.
- PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP served as accounting advisor to Bain Capital Private Equity.
- Piper Jaffray acted as Big Tex’s exclusive financial advisor.
- McDermott Will & Emery LLP provided legal advice to Big Tex and H.I.G. Capital.
- Big Tex sells its family of trailer brands through a diversified network of over 600 dealers nationwide.
- The company maintains manufacturing and assembly facilities in Texas, Oklahoma, Georgia, and Idaho.
- The company’s Tex Trail division operates as a leading parts distributor with 12 locations.
- Bain Capital has approximately $75 billion under management across private equity, venture capital, public equity, and credit products.
- Bain Capital was founded in 1984 and employs more than 400 professionals.
- Bain Capital has deep experience in industrial manufacturing and distribution, with investments in companies including TI Automotive, Sensata Technologies, FTE Automotive, Trinseo SA, ASIMCO, and Hero MotoCorp Ltd.
