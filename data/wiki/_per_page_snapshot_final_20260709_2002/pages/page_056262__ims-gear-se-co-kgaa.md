## Facts

- IMS Gear develops and produces specific drive solutions for international customers with an industry focus on Automotive, industry, and e-mobility concepts.
- The company employs around 3,100 people worldwide.
- IMS Gear is organized into 29 entrepreneurial business units, each maintaining a closed value chain.
- The company maintains locations in Gainesville, Georgia, and Virginia Beach, Virginia within the southeastern United States.
- Employees at the U.S. locations benefit from proximity to major metropolitan areas, natural settings like the Appalachian Mountain chain and Chesapeake Bay, as well as good airport connections and shipping options.
- IMS Gear provides a comprehensive benefits package that includes medical, dental, and vision insurance, vacation and paid time off, holidays, performance bonus participation, a 401(k) plan, tuition reimbursement, disability and life insurance, a wellness program, and an employee referral program.
- The company offers competitive pay within its industry and geographical locations.
- IMS Gear provides two-year apprenticeship programs for a defined career path as an alternative to a four-year college degree program.
- Apprentices receive hands-on career training in an apprenticeship lab under the guidance of talented, industry professionals.
- Apprentices earn a competitive, guaranteed hourly rate while working and participate in a competitive benefits package.
- Apprentices receive job-specific classroom instruction with free tuition and books, resulting in zero student debt.
- Upon successful completion of the apprenticeship, participants receive a Journey-Worker Certificate presented by the Department of Labor and Industry.
- IMS Gear offers global opportunities for employees to work at production facilities in the USA, Germany, Mexico, and China to understand the global marketplace and immerse themselves in different cultures.
- The company's wellness program provides access to e-learning programs and events related to wellness.
- Employees can receive a discount on medical insurance premiums when they complete certain annual and preventative screenings.
- IMS Gear offers committed students the opportunity each semester to work on exciting projects and gain practical experience in gear and transmission technology.
- Direct entry newcomers and graduates participate in a trainee program where a mentor is responsible for looking after them from the start to help build a network among colleagues.
