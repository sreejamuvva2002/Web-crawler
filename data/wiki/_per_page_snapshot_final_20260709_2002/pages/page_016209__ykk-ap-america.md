# YKK AP America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2022-08-16

**Source:** [Incentives remain unknown as Georgia officials announce expansion at Bibb County's YKK AP facility | Georgia | thecentersquare.com](https://www.thecentersquare.com/georgia/article_629dcc34-1da7-11ed-84b0-232fafd5ff16.html)  ·  `page_016209`

## Facts

- Atlanta-based YKK AP America, a subsidiary of Tokyo-based YKK AP, plans to invest up to $125 million to expand its middle Georgia facility.
- The expansion will create 100 additional jobs at the Bibb County manufacturing facility.
- As is typical for jobs classified as in progress, state officials declined to divulge whether the state offered any incentives to entice the expansion or what it might cost Georgia taxpayers.
- Georgia Department of Economic Development communications specialist Jessica Atwell confirmed that the project is located in a Tier 1 tax credit zone.
- Under the Tier 1 credit, companies that create at least two jobs are eligible for a $4,000 tax credit per new job.
- Companies may use 100% of that credit toward their tax liability.
- Georgia Department of Economic Development Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that YKK AP has a strong history of growth and success in Georgia.
- YKK AP opened its first branch in Atlanta in 1986.
- YKK AP America chose middle Georgia to establish its manufacturing presence in the U.S. five years after opening the Atlanta branch.
- The company has since expanded its presence in Georgia and built valuable relationships with local communities.
- Companies in advanced manufacturing, like YKK AP, have played a key role in bringing jobs and opportunities to Georgia, creating the second most new jobs in the state last year.
- The company plans to add machinery and equipment as part of its expansion at the facility located at the Macon-Bibb I-75 Industrial Park.
- The company currently supports 250 employees in Macon.
- YKK AP America’s president Oliver Stepe said the new facility will help introduce further vertical integration of vinyl extrusion, glass, and window production.
- The expansion will allow the company to create enhanced efficiencies to manufacture the highest quality windows and doors for homebuilders and homeowners.
