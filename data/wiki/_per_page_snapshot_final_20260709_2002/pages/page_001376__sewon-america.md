# Sewon America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-02-21

**Source:** [Effingham County gets first Hyundai supplier for Bryan County megasite](https://www.savannahnow.com/story/business/automotive/2023/02/21/effingham-county-ga-hyundai-supplier-bryan-county-megasite/69927048007/)  ·  `page_001376`

## Facts

- Sewon America will invest more than $300 million into a new manufacturing facility in Rincon.
- The investment will bring more than 700 jobs to Effingham County.
- Bryan, Bulloch, and Chatham Counties each have a Hyundai supplier committed to building a facility in their county.
- Effingham County joins Bryan, Bulloch, and Chatham Counties as the fourth location for a Hyundai supplier in the JDA.
- Gov. Brian Kemp announced the Sewon America investment on Tuesday.
- Gov. Brian Kemp stated that the announcement celebrates delivering on the promise to create jobs in all four counties in the JDA and the growth of a valued partner in the business community.
- Gov. Brian Kemp had previously congratulated Chairman Kim on the company's then-recent expansion during his first international trip as governor to Korea.
- During that trip, Gov. Kemp discussed how they could continue to work together with Chairman Kim and Sewon America.
- Those discussions led to the announcement of Sewon America continuing to grow their Georgia operations.
