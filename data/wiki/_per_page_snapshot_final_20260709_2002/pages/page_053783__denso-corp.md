# DENSO Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [DENSO facilities across North America recognized by Toyota for Quality Performance | News | DENSO US/Canada Website](https://www.denso.com/us-ca/en/news/newsroom/2017/20170721-r023/)  ·  `page_053783`

## Facts

- DENSO is a leading global automotive supplier of advanced technology, systems and components in thermal, powertrain control, electronics, information, and safety.
- DENSO’s North American headquarters is located in Southfield, Michigan.
- DENSO employs more than 23,000 people at 28 consolidated subsidiaries and 4 affiliates across the North American region.
- Of these North American entities, 25 are manufacturing facilities located in the United States, Canada, and Mexico.
- In the United States alone, DENSO employs more than 17,000 people in Alabama, Arkansas, California, Iowa, Georgia, Kentucky, Michigan, North Carolina, Ohio, Pennsylvania, South Carolina, Tennessee, and Texas.
- DENSO’s North American consolidated sales totaled US$9.6 billion for the fiscal year ending March 31, 2017.
- Toyota presented three DENSO facilities with Excellent Quality Performance Awards at its 2017 Supplier Business Meeting.
- Several more DENSO facilities across North America received Certificates of Achievement for exceeding expectations across several key performance metrics.
- Toyota bestows quality performance awards upon top suppliers who achieve high levels of performance in Zero Mileage Quality, Mass Production Delivery Performance, Service Delivery Performance, and Warranty Performance.
- Eligible suppliers must meet all relevant targets in each area throughout the calendar year to be considered for Toyota's quality awards.
- DENSO facilities receiving 2017 Toyota Excellent Quality Performance Awards include DENSO Manufacturing Arkansas in Osceola, Arkansas; DENSO Manufacturing Tennessee in Maryville, Tennessee; and DENSO Air Systems Mexico in Ciudad Frontera, Coahuila, Mexico.
- Facilities that received Certificates of Achievement for quality performance include ASMO Manufacturing Michigan in Battle Creek, Michigan; ASMO Manufacturing North Carolina in Statesville, North Carolina; ASMO Manufacturing Texas in Ennis, Texas; DENSO Air Systems Michigan in Battle Creek, Michigan; DENSO Logistics Texas in Laredo, Texas; DENSO Logistics California in Vista, California; DENSO Manufacturing Tennessee in Athens, Tennessee; DENSO Manufacturing Tennessee in Maryville, Tennessee (S/A Division and E/P Division); DENSO Manufacturing Canada in Guelph, Ontario, Canada; and DENSO Mexico in Silao, Guanajuato, Guadalupe City, and Apodaca, Nuevo Léon, Mexico.
- Ben Luppino, vice president of Toyota Sales Group at DENSO International America, Inc., stated that DENSO strives to provide the highest quality products that deliver consistent and reliable performance, and the recognition reflects the tireless effort of employees across the continent to uphold the DENSO brand promise.
- DENSO Corp., headquartered in Kariya, Aichi prefecture, Japan, has more than 200 subsidiaries and affiliates in 38 countries and regions and employs more than 150,000 people worldwide.
- DENSO’s consolidated global sales for the fiscal year ending March 31, 2017, totaled US$40.4 billion.
- Last fiscal year, DENSO spent 8.8 percent of its global consolidated sales on research and development.
- DENSO common stock is traded on the Tokyo and Nagoya stock exchanges.
