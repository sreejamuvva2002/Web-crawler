# Daesol Ausys Georgia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-08-29

**Source:** [Automotive Supplier Daesol Ausys Georgia to Invest $72 Million in Harris County - Location Georgia](https://www.locationgeorgia.com/daesol-ausys-georgia-to-invest-72-million-in-harris-county-august-2023/)  ·  `page_019035`

## Facts

- Daesol Ausys Georgia will build a new manufacturing facility in the City of West Point.
- The new facility will create 140 new jobs.
- The project involves a $72 million investment in Harris County.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Daesol Ausys Georgia will establish the new manufacturing facility in West Point.
- Daesol Ausys was established in Korea in 2017.
- The company specializes in designing and manufacturing motor and electric vehicle interior components and accessories.
- Daesol Ausys is a key supplier for Kia Georgia, Hyundai Motor Group, and General Motors.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that West Point has become an automotive capital in its own right.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that Georgia’s automotive industry employs over 50,000 hardworking Georgians.
- CEO Min Ho Kwon expressed excitement about the project in the Northwest Harris Business Park and the partnership with the State of Georgia and Harris County.
- The new facility will produce luggage boards and luggage covers.
- Operations at the new facility are expected to begin in December 2024.
- The company will be hiring for positions in production, purchasing, quality control, maintenance, human resources, and management.
- Robert Grant, Chairman of the Harris County Commission, stated that Daesol Ausys’ decision to locate in the Northwest Harris Business Park represents a commitment to Harris County.
- Robert Grant noted that West Point and Harris County have a wonderful relationship with Daesol Ausys’ sister companies Daehan Solutions and Daesol Materials.
- Craig Greenhaw, Chairman of the Harris County Development Authority, welcomed Daesol Ausys as a new corporate citizen bringing jobs and capital investment to the region.
- Alyce Thornhill, Director of Project Implementation and Supplier Strategy, represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team on the project.
- The project was developed in partnership with the Harris County Commission, Harris County Development Authority, City of West Point, Georgia Power, and Georgia Quick Start.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that economic development is driven by the local level and congratulated community partners for bringing Daesol Ausys to West Georgia.
- Since 2018, Georgia has created more than $25 billion in investments and 30,000 jobs pursuing the entire electric vehicle supply chain.
- In fiscal year 2023, job creation in Georgia’s automotive industry increased by 324 percent compared to FY21.
- Suppliers attracted by Georgia’s mobility original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) resulted in over $2 billion in investment across the state in FY23.
- Daesol Ausys Georgia is a leading manufacturer of automotive components and has a long history as one of the top suppliers to Kia and Hyundai worldwide.
