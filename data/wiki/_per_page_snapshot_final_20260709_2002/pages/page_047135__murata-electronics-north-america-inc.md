## Facts

- LQP15M series inductors claim to have the narrowest tolerance figures of any inductors currently on the market.
- The components promise precise inductance and impedance matching.
- The inductors feature a ±0.05 nH tolerance compared to the current industry standard of ±0.3 nH.
- Available in a 0402 size measuring 1 mm x 0.5 mm x 0.35 mm.
- The inductors employ an ultra-high accuracy coil based on photo-lithographic technology.
- The photo-lithographic process is said to produce an accurate coil pattern and a more precise inductance level.
- Available in small inductance steps from 0.1 nH to 3.9 nH.
- Sample price for the LQP15M series is $0.08 cents each.
- Lead times are eight weeks.
- Manufacturer is MURATA ELECTRONICS NORTH AMERICA INC., located in Smyrna, GA.
