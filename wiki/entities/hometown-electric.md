# Hometown Electric

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Atlanta, Georgia, United States

## Overview

A locally owned company offering residential electric vehicle charging station installation services in the Atlanta metro area.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Provides home EV charging stations, enabling Georgia residents to charge electric vehicles safely and efficiently.

## Key facts

- A dedicated circuit with its own breaker is required for safe home charging station installation.
- Charging speed depends on amperage; a 20 amp outlet typically provides about 20 miles of range per hour.
- Most charging stations are capable of 30 amp connections, allowing higher charging rates.
- Hometown Electric offers consultation and inspection to determine optimal power flow for each vehicle.
- Best value in town: expert work delivered at a fair price with flat-rate pricing.
- Top-level engineers with background checks perform installations.
- Locally owned and operated in the Atlanta area.
- All employees adhere to a zero-tolerance drug policy.
