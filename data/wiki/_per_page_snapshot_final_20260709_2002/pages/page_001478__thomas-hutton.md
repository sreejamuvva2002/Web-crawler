# Thomas & Hutton

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-29

**Source:** [T&H Projects In The News: November 2022 - Thomas & Hutton](https://www.thomasandhutton.com/2022/11/29/th-projects-in-the-news-november-2022/)  ·  `page_001478`

## Facts

- Thomas & Hutton assisted the Development Authority of Bryan County with planning and site selection for a 1,200,000-square-foot Hyundai Mobis electric vehicle power electric system manufacturing facility.
- The Hyundai Mobis facility is located at the Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) Certified Belfast Commerce Park in Richmond Hill.
- The plant will supply essential production components, EV power electric systems, and integrated charging control units to nearby Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.
- Thomas & Hutton worked alongside the Newton County Industrial Development Authority on site selection for Archer Aviation Inc.
- Archer Aviation plans to locate its facility alongside the Covington Municipal Airport in Newton County.
- Thomas & Hutton provided civil engineering services related to land analysis for the over 96-acre Archer Aviation site.
- Thomas & Hutton oversaw coordination with local utilities and the City of Covington on water and sewer delivery and access road improvements.
- The team consulted with Airport staff on access to taxiways and height restrictions related to preliminary engineering work.
- Archer Aviation will produce state-of-the-art electric vertical takeoff and landing aircraft (eVTOL) at the proposed facility.
- Archer Aviation expects to create 1,000 jobs and invest $118 million over ten years in Newton County.
- The Archer Aviation project location next to the Covington Municipal Airport is congruent with the long-term airport master plan, providing access to Georgia’s interstate highway system.
- SJT Properties broke ground on the Georgia International Commerce Centre in Bryan County.
- The Georgia International Commerce Centre distribution facility will be 6.5 million square feet and consist of at least eight buildings.
- Proximity to I-16 and the Hyundai Metaplant America were cited as influential drivers for SJT Properties locating in the area.
- Norma Precision Inc. is expanding in Bryan County, Georgia, by building a new 300,000-square-foot manufacturing and distribution facility at Interstate Centre III adjacent to U.S. 280 and I-16.
- The Norma Precision site is Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) certified and was fast-tracked for development via advanced due diligence.
- Norma Precision is already hiring for various positions at its Bryan County facility.
- The Norma Precision project is expected to bring 600 jobs to Bryan County.
- Fairfield County Economic Development expects that speed to market via completed due diligence and a pre-graded site will draw manufacturers to the I-77 International Megasite in Fairfield County, South Carolina.
- Construction is underway on Marion Lake, a multifamily community in western Chatham County near Savannah.
- T&H is on the Mariner Group team for the creation of the Marion Lake property just west of I-95 in Savannah.
- The Marion Lake multifamily property will include 340 units on a 23-acre lakefront site.
- Thomas & Hutton expanded its service footprint into Kentucky with Ascend Elements’ Apex II groundbreaking at Hopkinsville’s Commerce Park II.
- The Ascend Elements Apex II facility will be 450,000 square feet and process used lithium-ion batteries into materials compatible with battery systems in electric vehicles.
- The Ascend Elements operation is described as the most significant investment in southwestern Kentucky’s history.
- Thomas & Hutton works with SSOE Group on the 140-acre site for the Ascend Elements facility.
