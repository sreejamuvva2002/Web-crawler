# Volvo Cars

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-06-06

**Source:** [Volvo EX90 production starts in South Carolina - electrive.com](https://www.electrive.com/2024/06/06/volvo-ex90-production-start-us/)  ·  `page_014031`

## Facts

- The Ridgeville plant had previously only housed production of the Volvo S60.
- Production of the Volvo EX90 was originally scheduled to commence in the fourth quarter of 2023 but was delayed due to software issues.
- During Volvo Cars’ Q4 2023 earnings conference call, CEO Jim Rowan stated that the EX90 is the company’s most technically advanced car ever.
- Volvo prioritized ensuring its software “was as good as it possibly could be” before shipping the vehicle to customers.
- Production of the Volvo EX90 has now commenced at the South Carolina facility.
- First deliveries of the Volvo EX90 are expected in North America and Europe to commence in the second half of the year.
- Volvo Cars’ US plant has an annual production capacity of 150,000 vehicles.
- The company has made significant investments in its South Carolina factory in recent years.
- Recent investments included significantly expanding the facility and refurbishing the body shop and paint shop.
- The North American strategic production site now houses a battery pack production line as well.
- Production of the Volvo EX90 will also take place in China, with deliveries scheduled to commence in November.
- The Volvo EX90 will be available in three variants: Single Motor (RWD), Twin Motor (AWD), and Twin Motor Performance (AWD).
- The Single Motor (RWD) variant features 205 kW power, 490 Nm of torque, accelerates from 0 to 100 kph in 8.4 seconds, has a top speed of 180 kph, uses a 104 kWh battery pack, and offers a WLTP range of approximately 580 km.
- The Twin Motor (AWD) variant features 300 kW power, 700 Nm of torque, accelerates from 0 to 100 kph in 5.9 seconds, has a top speed of 180 kph, uses a 111 kWh battery pack, and offers a WLTP range of approximately 600 km.
- Volvo Cars confirmed that the Twin Motor (AWD) variant's battery pack is supplied by CATL.
- The Twin Motor Performance (AWD) variant features 380 kW power, 910 Nm of torque, accelerates from 0 to 100 kph in 4.9 seconds, has a top speed of 180 kph, shares the 111 kWh battery pack with the Twin Motor (AWD) variant (with 107 kWh usable), and offers a WLTP range of approximately 590 km due to higher power and torque.
- Pricing for the Volvo EX90 starts at $76,695 in the US and €83,700 in Germany.
