# Kumho Tire USA Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-01-01

**Source:** [Kumho Tire Outlines 2015 Business Plan- Tire Review](https://www.tirereview.com/kumho-tire-works-house/)  ·  `page_056608`

## Facts

- Kumho Tire USA Inc. is constructing a new tire manufacturing facility in Macon-Bibb County, Georgia.
- The first phase of the new Macon-Bibb County plant requires a $410 million investment.
- The first phase will produce four million passenger and light truck tires annually.
- The Macon-Bibb County plant is expected to open in January 2016.
- Initially, the Georgia plant will produce original equipment (OE) tires, with replacement units to follow later.
- Production at the new facility will help resolve fill rate issues caused by increased OE demand and pending U.S. duties on China-produced tires.
- Kumho moved its tire molds from China to South Korea in preparation for operations at the Georgia plant.
- Depending on marketplace conditions, the Macon-Bibb County plant could undergo a second stage of expansion.
- Harry Choi serves as president of Kumho Tire USA Inc. and outlined the company's five-year business plan targeting 2018.
- John Hagan joined Kumho as national sales vice president in late 2013 to strengthen the sales organization.
- Kumho aims to sell 10 million tire units locally in the U.S. and gain a 3.5% market share by 2018.
- The company announced six new tires set for rollout by mid-2016, including the Ecsta V720, Ecsta 4X II, Solus TA71, Solus TA11, Road Venture AT51, and Crugen Premium KL33.
- Kumho established a partnership with the NBA as its "Official Tire Sponsor" to raise brand awareness and support sales.
- The company introduced an Apple iOS app for dealers and consumers to browse products, view details, and access training materials.
- Kumho is working to eliminate spot deals and establish a stable, consistent pricing policy for its dealer network.
- The tiremaker refreshed its consumer tire naming system to a four-character format identifying performance/season and grade.
