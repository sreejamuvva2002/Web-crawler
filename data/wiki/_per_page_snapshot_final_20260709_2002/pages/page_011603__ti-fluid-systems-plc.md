# TI Fluid Systems plc

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [TI Fluid Systems plc - AnnualReports.com](https://www.annualreports.com/Company/ti-fluid-systems-plc)  ·  `page_011603`

## Facts

- Manufactures and sells fluid storage, carrying, and delivery systems primarily for the light duty automotive market worldwide.
- Operates through two segments: Fluid Carrying Systems (FCS) and Fuel Tank and Delivery Systems (FTDS).
- The FCS segment offers brake and fuel lines and bundles, such as double-wall and single-wall steel fluid carrying lines, and bundles for brake and fuel systems.
- The FCS segment provides multi-layer plastic lines for vapor, thermal management, and exhaust treatment.
- The FCS segment supplies sensor-integrated connectors for installation and system assembly.
- The FCS segment includes heated plastic lines for selective catalyst reduction urea fluids.
- The FCS segment provides thermal products, including thermal management technology for hybrid-electric vehicle battery packs.
- The FCS segment offers climate control tube and hose assemblies, coaxial or tube-in-tube assemblies, accumulators and receiver dryers, and heater and radiator hoses.
- The FCS segment supplies powertrain products comprising gasoline direct injection lines and rails, high-pressure diesel lines, and turbocharger cooling lines.
- The FTDS segment offers fuel tank products, such as plastic fuel tanks for gasoline, diesel, and flex fuel vehicles.
- The FTDS segment provides partial zero emissions vehicle fuel tanks.
- The FTDS segment supplies pressurized fuel tank systems for hybrid vehicles.
- The FTDS segment offers low emission vehicle fuel tanks, delivery and return components, and plastic fuel filler tubes.
- The FTDS segment provides pump and module products, such as brushed and brushless pumps, modules, electronics controls, additive dosing systems, and sensors.
- The FTDS segment supplies aftermarket products.
- Serves automotive original equipment manufacturers.
- Was founded in 1922.
- Is headquartered in Oxford, the United Kingdom.
