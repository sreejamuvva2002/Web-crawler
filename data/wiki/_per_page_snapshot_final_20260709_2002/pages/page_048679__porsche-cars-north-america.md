# Porsche Cars North America

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America officially opened its Porsche Experience Center and headquarters in Atlanta today.
- The new facility is located off of One Porsche Drive in Atlanta, Georgia.
- Porsche invested $100 million into the development of the property, making it the single largest investment the automaker has made in North America.
- The 27-acre complex provides office space for PCNA, Porsche Financial Services, Porsche Business Services, Porsche Consulting, and Mieschke Hofmann & Partners.
- The site includes a 1.6-mile driver development track, a classic Porsche gallery, a restoration center, a human performance center, a driving simulator lab, and a fine-dining restaurant named 356.
- It is estimated that 30,000 guests will visit the Porsche Experience Center and headquarters.
- More than 70 locations were considered for PCNA’s new headquarters before Atlanta was selected as the ideal location.
- The facility will ultimately employ 450 people with more than 100 positions that are new to Georgia.
- Matthias Müller, Chairman of the Executive Board of Porsche, stated that the opening celebrates a significant milestone in expanding Porsche's footprint in the United States and reaffirms the company's commitment to Georgia and Atlanta.
- Detlev von Platen, President and CEO of Porsche Cars North America, described the new home as a bold, energizing environment designed to demonstrate the unique capabilities of Porsche sports cars, calling it the first of its kind in the automotive industry.
- Atlanta Mayor Kasim Reed cited low cost of doing business, high quality of life, access to the world's busiest and most efficient passenger airport, and the fastest-growing container port in the country as reasons for choosing Atlanta.
