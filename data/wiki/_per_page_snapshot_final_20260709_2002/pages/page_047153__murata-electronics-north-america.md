## Facts

- Murata Electronics North America (MENA) operates a facility in Rockmart, Georgia.
- The company provides solutions for automotive electronics, alongside mobile communications, computers, networking, medical devices, power solutions, and smart home technologies.
- A Supply Chain Analyst position is available at the Rockmart location with a hybrid workplace policy.
- The role constructs and analyzes data impacting cost, efficiency, and responsibilities within the Supply Chain organization.
- The position controls supply chain processes including production control points, product returns handling, scrap, supply chain security, and general supply chain support.
- Responsibilities include analyzing key logistics cost factors and preparing metrics for SCM and Logistics.
- The analyst maintains and supports dashboards and tools for SCM data management and reporting.
- The role supports Transportation Coordinators with inbound freight control, shipment notification, forwarder communication, freight tracking, and reporting.
- The position manages the damaged freight process, including tracking, claims processing, cause and impact investigation, and stakeholder communication.
- The analyst collaborates with Sales Operations to review slow-moving and excess inventory for utilization opportunities while minimizing scrap.
- The role supports Sales Operations with inventory analysis, substitutions, factory returns, quarantines, and other stock actions.
- The position analyzes quarterly reserves for inventory scrap/obsolescence and processes scrap at least twice annually.
- The analyst maintains lead time data from factories and the Product Division to ensure system accuracy.
- Required qualifications include a Bachelor’s degree and three or more years of supply chain or logistics experience.
- Candidates must demonstrate intermediate or advanced Microsoft Excel proficiency and strong analytical skills for Murata’s supply chain data.
- The role requires a strong understanding of capacity principles and manufacturing constraints relative to customer requirements.
- Applicants need knowledge of demand methodologies for production planning and meeting customer requirements.
- Murata offers competitive compensation and comprehensive benefits for this position.
