## Facts

- Mercedes-Benz is relocating 500 corporate and tech jobs to Greater Atlanta.
- The relocation consolidates key operations from Michigan.
- Mercedes-Benz is launching a new R&D hub in Sandy Springs, GA.
- The move underscores a drive for greater agility, faster innovation, and access to top talent from Georgia Tech and the region’s expanding tech scene.
- Georgia has long helped European companies invest and grow in the U.S. as an active CASE member.
- CASE's transatlantic mission guides firms through site selection, incentives, and partnerships.
- The relocation shows how Georgia and CASE are shaping the future of mobility and international business.
