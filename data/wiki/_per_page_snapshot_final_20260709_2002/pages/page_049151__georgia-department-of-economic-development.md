# Georgia Department of Economic Development

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2013-08-28

## Facts

- Cindy Eidson serves as Director of Tourism Product Development for the Georgia Department of Economic Development.
- The Tourism Product Development Resource Team has streamlined its initiative to assist Georgia communities with funding for tourism product development, aiming to create new jobs and sustainable economic development across the state.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development committed $225,000 in support of the NSSF Industry Summit and sponsorship messages on the NSSF website.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development agreed with NCR and Invest Atlanta on additional incentives for NCR to invest USD145m in a second tower and bring over 1,800 skilled professionals to Georgia over the next five years.
- Georgia's Manufacturing Appreciation Week was developed by the Technical College System of Georgia and the Georgia Department of Economic Development.
- Businesses must have been in operation for a minimum of three years in Georgia to be nominated for awards given during Georgia's Manufacturing Appreciation Week.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development founded the Health IT Leadership Summit in 2010 alongside the Metro Atlanta Chamber and the Technology Association of Georgia's Health Society.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development collaborated with the Georgia Economic Developers Association (GEDA) on Georgia's first “Small Business Rock Stars” competition, with Azalea Health announced as a winner in Valdosta, GA on August 28, 2013.
- According to the Georgia Department of Economic Development, Japan ranked as the fourth largest export market for Georgia in 2010.
