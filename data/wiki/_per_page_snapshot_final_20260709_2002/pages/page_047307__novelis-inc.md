## Facts

- Novelis Inc is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Ltd and a producer of aluminum products and solutions.
- The company focuses on recycling operations to produce low-carbon aluminum solutions.
- Product portfolio includes sheet ingot, foils, recycled aluminum, can stock, and electronic products.
- Offers various sheets including automotive sheets, industrial sheets, construction sheets, and painted sheets.
- Supplies products to diverse industries including building and construction, signage, foil and packaging, commercial transportation, architectural, industrial and consumer durables, and other markets.
- Operates across North America, Europe, Asia, and South America regions.
- Headquarters is located in Atlanta, Georgia, the US.
- In August 2025, the company received sustainability awards by the Business Intelligence Group for its company vision, Novelis 3x30.
- In August 2025, the company partnered with DRT Holdings, LLC to accelerate the adoption of high-recycled-content alloys in aluminum beverage can ends.
- In June 2025, the company partnered with The Velux Group for the supply of high recycled content aluminium.
- Has 13,250 employees.
- Kumar Mangalam Birla serves as Chairman on the Executive Board since 2007.
- Steven Fisher serves as President, Director, and Chief Executive Officer on the Executive Board since 2023.
- Devinder Ahuja serves as Chief Financial Officer and Executive Vice President in Senior Management since 2016.
- Emilio Braghi serves as Chief Operating Officer and President - Novelis Europe in Senior Management starting in 2026.
- Stephanie Rauls serves as Senior Vice President, Deputy Chief Financial Officer, and Chief Accounting Officer in Senior Management since 2016.
