## Facts

- Is a mid-sized auto parts manufacturer based in Pontiac, Michigan.
- Employs 501-1000 employees.
- For more than five decades, it designs, engineers, and manufactures exterior functional components and a broad range of standard and custom extrusions for the automotive sector.
- Its core focus is roof carrier systems for the international automotive industry.
- Traced its product history back to the introduction of its first load carriers in 1970.
- Has grown into a leading supplier in the roof carrier systems area.
- Provides value-added differentiation for vehicles through the integration of extruded, molded, and roll-formed components.
- Serves customers worldwide with an emphasis on innovative product development.
- Recent developments include collaborations with Ford on sliding load rack and folding roof rack concepts.
- Has expanded its offices in the United States as part of broadening its global footprint.
