# Nivel Parts & Manufacturing Co., LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-01-18

**Source:** [Nivel Parts & Manufacturing Acquires SnorkelyourATV](https://sportsdigest.in/nivel-parts-manufacturing-acquires-snorkelyouratv/26973/)  ·  `page_058344`

## Facts

- Nivel Parts & Manufacturing Co., LLC announced the acquisition of SnorkelyourATV LLC on Jan. 18, 2022.
- SnorkelyourATV LLC is a leading manufacturer in the ATV and UTV accessory space with a primary focus on the snorkel product line.
- The purchase brings together Nivel’s manufacturing, sourcing, and distribution capabilities with SnorkelyourATV’s highly regarded product line and new product development expertise.
- Kyle Tallman is the founder and former owner of SnorkelyourATV.
- Kyle Tallman stated he has grown to respect Nivel and looks forward to growing the business further with them.
- Nivel recognizes increasing growth in the UTV and ATV segments through the acquisition.
- The acquisition expands Nivel’s snorkel product line with a well-recognized brand and provides important product insight.
- Nivel CEO Brett Hankey commented on welcoming SnorkelyourATV into the Nivel family.
- Kyle Tallman built a business centered around new product development, quality production, and customer-focused sales and service.
- Nivel is a global manufacturer, distributor, and seller of aftermarket parts and accessories for vehicles in the Personal Transportation Vehicle (PTV), Utility Terrain Vehicle (UTV), Heavy Duty, and Automotive sector.
- Nivel was founded in 1968.
- Nivel is a trusted business partner to thousands of dealers, distributors, and consumers in the US and international markets.
- Nivel owns the most valuable brands in the aftermarket niche vehicle parts and accessories categories: MadJax, Red Dot, GTW, Jake’s, Reliance, Seizmik, Highlifter, and Falcon Ridge.
- SnorkelyourATV LLC was founded in 2008.
- SnorkelyourATV engineers, fabricates, and direct-sells 160+ proprietary accessories for most all-terrain-vehicles (ATVs) and utility-terrain-vehicles (UTVs).
- SnorkelyourATV’s primary products are snorkel kits allowing the ATV/UTV to operate with its engine underwater.
- SnorkelyourATV’s products allow ATV/UTV owners to customize, increase performance, and protect their vehicles.
