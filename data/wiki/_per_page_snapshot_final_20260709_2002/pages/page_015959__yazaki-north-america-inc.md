# Yazaki North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2001-01-21

**Source:** [Yazaki North America Returns for its Second Year as an Official Sponsor

## Facts

- Yazaki North America, Inc. (YNA) is a Tier One supplier and a leading provider of vehicle power and data solutions to the automotive industry.
- YNA announced it will return as an official sponsor for the North American International Auto Show (NAIAS) 2001.
- NAIAS 2001 will take place at Cobo Center in Detroit from January 13 to January 21, 2001.
- Nigel Thompson, executive vice president for planning for YNA, stated that Yazaki's participation reflects its integral involvement in the automotive industry and dedication to the evolution of power and data solutions as a critical component of the automobile.
- Yazaki produces electronics and electrical systems for virtually every leading OEM worldwide.
- YNA's exhibit will be located on the Cobo Center Main Concourse just outside the Macomb entrance to the show floor.
- The exhibit will display YNA's existing technology as well as new trends in technology that the company is developing for tomorrow's automobile.
- Mark Snethkamp, senior co-chair of NAIAS, stated that Yazaki is a leader in supplying the industry with power and data solutions and its presence at NAIAS is testament to that leadership.
- Yazaki Corporation is a global leader in the development and manufacturing of vehicle power and data solutions, continually researching and developing advanced electronic technologies for vehicles.
- Yazaki produces electrical distribution systems, fiber optics, advanced networking, instrumentation, switches, junction blocks, connector systems, electronics, and advanced technology.
- Worldwide, the company employs over 100,000 people in 31 countries.
- 1,400 employees are based at the company's new North American corporate headquarters and research center campus in Canton, Mich.
