# Yachiyo Manufacturing of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2014-03-20

**Source:** [Carrollton Manufacturing Plant Hosting Job Fair | Georgia Public Broadcasting](https://www.gpb.org/blogs/georgia-works/2014/03/20/carrollton-manufacturing-plant-hosting-job-fair)  ·  `page_015546`

## Facts

- Yachiyo Manufacturing of America in Carrollton is hosting a job fair on Saturday, April 5th, from 10 am to 2 pm at the West Georgia Technical College Murphy Campus in Waco.
- The hiring event is being held with assistance from the Georgia Department of Labor.
- Open positions include environmental health and safety coordinator, technical services manager, accounting assistant manager, purchasing coordinator, technical associates, quality assurance manager, shipping and receiving team leader, assembly team leaders, materials coordinator, final inspectors, and production associates.
- Applicants must be at least 18 years old to apply.
- Interested applicants are required to bring a resume and be prepared to complete a company application.
