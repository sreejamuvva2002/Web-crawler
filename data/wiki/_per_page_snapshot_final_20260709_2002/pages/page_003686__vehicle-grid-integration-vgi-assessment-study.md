# Vehicle Grid Integration (VGI) Assessment Study

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-30

**Source:** [Vehicle Grid Integration Assessment Report | Department of Energy](https://www.energy.gov/cmei/vehicles/articles/vehicle-grid-integration-assessment-report)  ·  `page_003686`

## Facts

- The Department of Energy (DOE) established a program for the integration of electric vehicles (EVs) onto the electric grid and to conduct and report on an Assessment Study of Vehicle Grid Integration (VGI).
- The Transportation Technologies Office published the report on January 13, 2025.
- Vehicle Grid Integration (VGI) is critical to the electrification of the transportation sector and the modernization of the electric grid.
- The Department of Energy (DOE) is prepared to conduct research, development, demonstration, and deployment needed to establish U.S. leadership in VGI, create good paying U.S. jobs, and contribute to combating climate change.
- Through the VGI Assessment Study DOE details a 10-year roadmap for DOE’s VGI work.
- Within this workplan, DOE will conduct research on specific aspects of VGI covering impacts on EVs and the grid, grid services, codes and standards, and cybersecurity.
- This roadmap was developed as the result of work performed across the DOE complex, consultations with National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), review of reports and studies by non-DOE researchers and VGI stakeholders, and input received directly from stakeholders.
