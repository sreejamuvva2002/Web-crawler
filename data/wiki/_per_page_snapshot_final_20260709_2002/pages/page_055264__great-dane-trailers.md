# Great Dane Trailers

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2010-11-01

**Source:** [Great Dane Trailers breaks ground for Statesboro plant](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/shopmanagement/great-dane-trailers-breaks-ground-for-statesboro-plant)  ·  `page_055264`

## Facts

- Great Dane Trailers has begun building a new manufacturing plant in Statesboro, Ga.
- The project represents an investment of $33.5 million by the company.
- The project is expected to create more than 400 jobs over the next four years.
- The new plant will produce single- and multitemp refrigerated trailers to support the food service industry.
- The facility will have the capacity to produce more than 5,000 trailers a year.
- The 450,000-sq.-ft. facility is located on a 118-acre site.
- The facility also will include office and show space.
- Construction of the new facility will be complete in 2012.
- Great Dane Trailers produces dry vans and refrigerated and platform trailers.
- The company maintains a sales, marketing, engineering, and R&D center in Savannah, Ga.
- The company has seven other manufacturing plants in the U.S.
