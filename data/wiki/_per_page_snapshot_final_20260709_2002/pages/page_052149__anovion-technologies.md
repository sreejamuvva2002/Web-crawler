# Anovion Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Anovion Technologies invests $800 million to build new synthetic graphite anode materials manufacturing plant in Georgia | Plant Services](https://www.plantservices.com/industry-news/news/33005177/anovion-technologies-invests-800-million-to-build-new-synthetic-graphite-anode-materials-manufacturing-plant-in-georgia)  ·  `page_052149`

## Facts

- Anovion Technologies will make Decatur County, Georgia, the location for its latest manufacturing facility.
- The 1,500,000-square-foot plant will produce 40,000 metric tonnes per annum of synthetic graphite anode material for lithium-ion batteries.
- According to the company, Decatur County was selected because of its abundance of low- and carbon-free energy sources, existing infrastructure, and pipeline of skilled workers.
- Anovion’s $800 million investment will create over 400 jobs.
- Eric Stopka, Chief Executive Officer of Anovion, said the company conducted a thorough search to identify a location that addressed energy, transportation, logistics, and human capital needs to ensure an ecosystem for producing the highest quality and sustainable synthetic graphite anode material on the market today.
- Stopka stated that existing infrastructure makes Southwest Georgia an attractive location for Anovion’s new facility and that proximity to other battery and EV manufacturing plants will allow them to further reduce their carbon footprint.
