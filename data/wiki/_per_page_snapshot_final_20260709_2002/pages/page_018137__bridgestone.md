# Bridgestone

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Bridgestone operates 30+ manufacturing facilities across the Americas in the United States.
- The company employs Operations Technicians, Engineers, Maintenance Technicians, Analysts, Researchers, and other roles across its U.S. facilities.
- The Bandag plant in Griffin, Georgia has been producing tread rubber and retreading materials since 1976.
- Tread rubber and retreading materials from the Griffin, Georgia plant are shipped to Bandag retread dealers for end-user tire production.
- The Firestone Airide plant and distribution center in Williamsburg, Kentucky produces and distributes air springs for automotive, heavy-duty truck and trailer, and seat/cab spring applications.
- The Williamsburg, Kentucky location recently underwent a major automotive expansion to meet growing demand for advanced air suspension technology in electric vehicles.
- A distribution center expansion at the Williamsburg, Kentucky location was implemented to enhance logistics operations and provide scalability for future growth.
- The major investment in production and logistics capacity at the Williamsburg, Kentucky facility increases the workforce by 250 employees.
- The Firestone Airide plant and distribution center in Dyersburg, Tennessee produces stamped metal plates for air suspension systems and heavy-duty products.
- The Dyersburg, Tennessee facility finishes and crimps air springs for automotive aftermarket (Ride-Rite), heavy duty, and off-highway applications.
- The Dyersburg, Tennessee location recently completed a major 80,000 square foot expansion to meet growing demand for advanced air suspension technology.
- A distribution center expansion at the Dyersburg, Tennessee facility was implemented to enhance logistics operations and provide scalability for future growth.
- The investment in production and logistics capacity at the Dyersburg, Tennessee facility increases the workforce by approximately 40 teammates.
- In 2023, the Dyersburg, Tennessee plant received recertification for the Volunteer STAR award, which is Tennessee’s highest honor for workplace safety and health.
- The Passenger/Light Truck Radial Tire plant in Graniteville, South Carolina (Aiken County) opened in 1998.
- The Graniteville, South Carolina facility spans 2.7 million square feet and employs more than 1,700 teammates as Maintenance Technicians, Mechanical Engineers, Business Analysts, and other roles.
- The Wilson, North Carolina manufacturing facility produces passenger radial, light truck radial tread, and run flat tires.
- The Wilson, North Carolina facility spans 2.67 million square feet and employs more than 2,000 teammates.
- The Bridgestone Americas Tire Operations plant in Warren County, Tennessee produces truck and bus radial tires, including premium steer and wide base models.
- The Warren County, Tennessee facility spans nearly 2 million square feet and employs more than 1,000 teammates as Operating Technicians, Safety and Health Managers, Electrical Engineers, and Production Analysts.
- Production at the Trenton, South Carolina Aiken County Off-Road Tire Plant began in 2013.
- The Trenton, South Carolina facility spans 1.5 million square feet and manufactures large off-the-road radial tires for mining and aggregate customers.
- Firestone Fibers & Textiles has owned and operated the Kings Mountain, North Carolina plant since 1992.
- The Kings Mountain, North Carolina plant produces tire cord and industrial fabric for Bridgestone Americas Tire Operations and Firestone Airide, as well as for external customers.
- The Bandag plant in Oxford, North Carolina has been in operation for 50 years producing tread rubber and retreading materials for Bandag dealers.
- The Oxford, North Carolina plant location has received numerous safety awards and certifications since its groundbreaking in 1967.
- The Gastonia, North Carolina plant has been owned and operated by Firestone Fibers & Textiles since 1935.
- The Gastonia, North Carolina plant produces reinforcement materials for Bridgestone Americas Tire Operations and Firestone Industrial Products, as well as for external customers.
