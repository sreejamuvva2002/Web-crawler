## Facts

- Yazaki North America, Inc. is part of the Yazaki Group.
- The company was founded in Japan in 1941 as an independent automotive component maker.
- Yazaki is a global leader in the research, development and delivery of vehicle power and data solutions for automotive applications.
- Yazaki produces wire harnesses, power distribution & control products, connectors, driver information systems, sensors and hybrid & electric vehicle products.
- The company has more than 300,000 employees in 46 countries.
- Yazaki is one of the world's largest privately-owned automotive suppliers.
- Yazaki has become a valued supplier to virtually every major automotive manufacturer in the world.
- A customer-centric focus guides Yazaki's global manufacturing footprint, allowing delivery of the right products in the right quantities at the right time anywhere in the world.
- Long-term collaboration with customers has established Yazaki as a leader in research, development, and delivery of vehicle power and data solutions.
