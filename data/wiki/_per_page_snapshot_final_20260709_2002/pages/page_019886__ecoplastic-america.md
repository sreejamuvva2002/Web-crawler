## Facts

- Ecoplastic America is conducting its biggest hiring push to date with an all-day hiring event on Wednesday, August 14, 2024.
- The hiring event will be held at Georgia Southern's Continuing and Professional Education (CPE) Building on 301 South.
- The event takes place from 10am to 4pm.
- The event location is 10449 US 301 South, Statesboro, Georgia, in room 1092.
- Event organizers are asking participants to apply for the "Production Team Member" job post on their website before attending if possible.
- Applicants must have at least one year of continuous employment history with one company to be eligible for the dynamic production role.
- New hires will work hand-in-hand with experienced Production Supervisors to ensure production lines run smoothly and efficiently.
- The position focuses on helping the operation achieve safety, quality, and delivery goals.
