# Seohan Auto Georgia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-04-11

**Source:** [Auto parts manufacturer bringing 180 new jobs to Liberty County: Gov. Kemp](https://fox28savannah.com/news/local/liberty-county-seohan-automobile-parts-georgia-hyundai-motor-group-manufacturing-facility-jobs-governor-brian-kemp-economy-sector-project)  ·  `page_059352`

## Facts

- Governor Brian Kemp announced that an auto parts manufacturer will bring 180 new jobs to Liberty County with a new facility.
- Seohan Auto Georgia is a developer and manufacturer of automobile parts.
- Seohan Auto Georgia will create at least 180 new jobs.
- Seohan Auto Georgia will invest more than $72 million in a new manufacturing facility in Liberty County.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that the Hyundai Metaplant is and will continue to be an economic driver for the entire region, creating more jobs for hardworking Georgians in communities like Liberty County.
- Governor Brian Kemp welcomed Seohan as the latest supplier in a growing list to invest in Georgia and expressed pride in the continued growth of this fast emerging sector.
- Seohan is a Tier-1 supplier for Hyundai Motor Group America.
- The new facility is scheduled to begin production in late 2024.
- The new facility will be located at the Dunwoody Court Site in the Tradeport East Business Center.
- Since 2020, more than 35 Electric Vehicle-related projects have announced more than $21 billion in investment and 27,800 jobs in Georgia.
