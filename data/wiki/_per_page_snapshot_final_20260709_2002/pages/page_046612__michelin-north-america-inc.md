# Michelin North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-16

**Source:** [MICHELIN ANNOUNCES FULL ROLLOUT OF TREADEYE TECHNOLOGY ACROSS U.S. RETREAD NETWORK](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/michelin-announces-full-rollout-treadeye-190000029.html?fr=sycsrp_catchall)  ·  `page_046612`

## Facts

- Michelin announced the full deployment of TreadEye, a proprietary technology developed to enhance the retread process and deliver measurable value to fleets.
- Following phased implementation, TreadEye is now available across the Michelin Retread Technologies franchise network in the United States.
- TreadEye is an industry-leading tread depth assessment tool that helps fleets maximize tire investment by accurately and consistently collecting 1,200 measurement points to provide precise insights into tread wear and casing condition.
- Nate Kirian, Vice President of Sales and Distribution for Commercial Business Lines at Michelin North America, Inc., stated that TreadEye is a game-changer for retread operations and fleets, highlighting how Michelin continues to innovate to directly benefit customers.
- Key benefits of TreadEye include identification of pull points by fleet location, maximization of rubber on the road by avoiding early pulls, prevention of casing damage, reduction of DOT violations and downtime from late pulls, and improved efficiency in the tire removal process that directly impacts a fleet's bottom line.
- The announcement regarding the full rollout was made on Oct. 16, 2025.
- Michelin North America, Inc. is headquartered in Greenville, S.C.
- Michelin North America, Inc. has approximately 23,500 employees.
- Michelin North America, Inc. operates 36 production facilities in the United States and Canada.
