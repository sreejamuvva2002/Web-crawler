# Adient plc

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-10-31

## Facts

- Adient began trading on Oct. 31, 2016, when Johnson Controls spun off its automotive experience segment.
- Adient is a leading seating supplier to the automotive industry with about a midteens share of the global market, including unconsolidated joint venture business.
- Adient's market share in China is around 20%, down from about 45% following the sale of its main joint venture there at the end of fiscal 2021.
- Unconsolidated revenue from Adient's joint ventures was about $3.5 billion in fiscal 2025.
- Consolidated China revenue for Adient was $1.3 billion.
- The company is headquartered in Ireland but has corporate offices in the Detroit area.
- Fiscal 2025 consolidated revenue, which excludes joint venture sales, was $14.5 billion.
