# Aspen Aerogels

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-01

**Source:** [Aspen Aerogels Engages MJW for Fire Protection and Suppression Systems at New Bulloch County, Georgia Plant](https://www.mjwood.com/projects/aspen-aerogels-engages-mjw-for-fire-protection-and-suppression-systems-at-new-bulloch-county-georgia-plant)  ·  `page_052678`

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels will invest in excess of $300 million to build a new factory in Bulloch County, Georgia.
- The new factory will produce aerogel thermal insulating material used in electric vehicle batteries.
- Aspen Aerogels' investment coincides with the construction of a Hyundai Motors electric vehicle and battery manufacturing plant in nearby Bryan County, Georgia.
- Once operational, the Aspen Aerogels plant is expected to create approximately 250 jobs for the region.
- MJW has been contracted by Turner Construction to design, fabricate, and install plant-wide fire protection and fire suppression systems for the facility.
- MJW's team initially served in a design-assist role alongside Turner Construction and Engineer of Record Thompson Consultants Inc. to develop a cost-effective design and construction approach for the facility's protection systems.
- The installation sequence began with approximately 1,200 linear feet of fused 10-inch HDPE high-pressure discharge piping running from a fire pump in the main utility building to several system risers inside the manufacturing facility.
- The facility will be protected by multiple wet and dry automatic sprinkler systems, several foam/water sprinkler systems, and equipment-specific high-pressure CO2 fire suppression systems.
- MJW's current work represents an early phase of the project, with potential to expand the partnership with Turner Construction and Aspen Aerogels on future manufacturing building additions and expansions at the site.
