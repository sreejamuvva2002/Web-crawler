# Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-04-06

**Source:** [Auto part company announces $9M expansion in Georgia | FOX 5 Atlanta](https://www.fox5atlanta.com/news/auto-part-company-announces-9m-expansion-in-georgia)  ·  `page_021389`

## Facts

- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System LLC will invest $9 million to expand its production operations in West Point, Georgia.
- The expansion will create more than 150 jobs for residents of Troup County.
- The new jobs will include positions in production seat assembly, foam, quality, and logistics.
- The expansion will increase the total number of people employed by Hyundai TRANSYS in Georgia to more than 1,890 jobs.
- Hyundai TRANSYS's Georgia manufacturing center produces seats and seat foam pads for the Hyundai Santa Fe, KIA Sorento, electric vehicles, and other car models.
- The company also produces automobile parts including transmissions and axles for Hyundai vehicles.
- Governor Brian Kemp announced the expansion and stated that Georgia's highly skilled workforce continues to attract manufacturing jobs across the state.
- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System President and CEO Sangkil Jung expressed excitement for expanding their presence in the West Point community and praised the partnership with the State of Georgia.
