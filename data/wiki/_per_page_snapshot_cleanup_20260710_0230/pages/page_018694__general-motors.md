# General Motors

## Facts

- The page provides a list of General Motors factories that are being or have been used to produce automobiles and automobile components, noting that factories are occasionally idled for re-tooling.
- Bowling Green Assembly Plant in Bowling Green, Kentucky produces Chevrolet Corvette (C8), LT4 V8 engine for Chevrolet Corvette Z06 (C7) (2015-2016 models with Z07 package or build your own engine option, 2017-2019 all Z06 models), Chevrolet Camaro ZL1 (Gen 6) (phased in during 2020), Cadillac CT5-V Blackwing, and Cadillac Escalade-V.
- The Bowling Green Assembly Plant opened in 1981 and is located at 600 Corvette Drive.
- The Performance Build Center relocated from Wixom, MI to Bowling Green Assembly in 2014.
- Spring Hill Manufacturing in Spring Hill, Tennessee produces Cadillac XT5, Cadillac XT6, Cadillac Lyriq, 1.5L Turbo I4 Ecotec Gen III, 2.0L Turbo I4, 2.7L L3B turbo I4, and 5.3 & 6.2 Gen V Small-Block V8 Engine Stamping Components.
- Spring Hill Manufacturing opened in 1990, was idled from 2009 to 2012, and is located at 100 Saturn Parkway.
- Lockport Operations in Lockport, NY produces thermal products (climate control systems, powertrain cooling systems) and stators for EV motors.
- Lockport Operations opened in 1914 and is located at 200 Upper Mountain Road.
- Lockport Operations was founded in 1910 as the Harrison Radiator Company, acquired by United Motors in 1916 which was then acquired by GM in 1918, transferred to Delphi Automotive Systems in 1999 (Harrison Thermal Systems), and taken back under Delphi Corporation bankruptcy and renamed General Motors Components Holdings in 2009.
- Brownstown Battery Assembly Plant in Brownstown Charter Township, Michigan produces battery packs for GMC Hummer EV & Cadillac Lyriq Roof module for Cruise AV Assembles prototype battery packs.
- The Brownstown Battery Assembly Plant opened in 2009 and is located at 20001 Brownstown Center Dr.
- The plant previously assembled battery packs for Chevrolet Volt, Holden Volt, Opel/Vauxhall Ampera, Cadillac ELR, and Chevrolet Spark EV (2015-2016 only - LG Chem cells).
- Detroit/Hamtramck Assembly in Hamtramck & Detroit, Michigan produces GMC Hummer EV, Chevrolet Silverado EV, GMC Sierra EV, and Cadillac Escalade IQ.
- The Detroit/Hamtramck Assembly also assembles Ultium battery cells into modules and packs for a variety of vehicles.
- This facility opened in 1985, is also called 'Poletown' and 'Factory ZERO', and is located at 2500 East Grand Blvd.
- Fairfax II in Kansas City, Kansas produces Chevrolet Bolt and Chevrolet Bolt EUV.
- Fairfax II opened in 1987 and is located at 3201 Fairfax Trafficway.
- The facility replaced the original Fairfax Assembly Plant (Fairfax I) for 1988 model year production.
- Orion Assembly in Orion Township, Michigan produces Chevrolet Silverado EV and GMC Sierra EV.
- Orion Assembly opened in 1983, was idled in 2009, reopened in 2011, and is located at 4555 Giddings Road.
- Ultium Cells LLC - Warren in Warren, Ohio produces Ultium lithium-ion battery cells for EV's.
- This facility opened in 2022 and is owned by Ultium Cells LLC, a 50/50 joint venture between General Motors and LG Energy Solution.
- It is Ultium Cells' first plant and is located at 7400 Tod Ave SW.
- KUKA in Livonia, Michigan produced BrightDrop Zevo 600 under contract for GM in a limited run of less than 500 units.
- The KUKA facility opened in 2021 and was idled in 2022 as a temporary measure until GM's CAMI plant is ready to start building BrightDrop electric vans.
- Navistar - Springfield Assembly Plant (Main Line) in Springfield, Ohio produces Chevrolet Silverado Medium Duty International CV.
- This facility opened in 2019 and is located at 6125 Urbana Road.
- It was jointly developed by GM & Navistar and built under contract by Navistar for GM.
- Navistar - Springfield Assembly Plant (Secondary Line) in Springfield, Ohio produces Chevrolet Express cutaway and GMC Savana cutaway.
- This facility opened in 2017 and is located at 6125 Urbana Road.
- It was built under contract by Navistar for GM.
