# Pirelli

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Pirelli Unveils P Zero Trofeo Track | Modern Tire Dealer](https://www.moderntiredealer.com/suppliers/news/55291305/pirelli-unveils-p-zero-trofeo-track)  ·  `page_048649`

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America has unveiled the P Zero Trofeo Track, a Department of Transportation (DOT)-marked, track-focused tire engineered for the United States market.
- The new tire is designed to excel in dry conditions and uses ultra high-performance road tire technology purpose-built for the circuit.
- The P Zero Trofeo Track is produced at Pirelli’s Rome, Ga. facility.
- The tire is developed for club racers, amateur motorsports enthusiasts, and serious track-day drivers.
- It offers sharp handling, grip, and consistency while maintaining DOT compliance for sanctioned racing events.
- Claudio Zanardo serves as CEO and president of Pirelli North America.
- The tire is designed, engineered, and manufactured at Pirelli's American facility in Rome, Ga.
- Pirelli views the tire as a symbol of its continued investment in U.S. motorsport and delivering locally developed, locally made products for this market.
- Other features include a slick-like tread pattern, bold groove designs for DOT compliance, racing-derived compound, and a fitment range of 17-inch to 21-inch for performance coverage.
- The P Zero Trofeo Track is DOT-marked for compliance with U.S. motorsport regulations and is specifically marked "For Competition Use Only."
- It is designed with P-metric and Light Load sizes in accordance with Tire and Rim Association standards.
- The tire is exclusively targeted for the U.S. market.
