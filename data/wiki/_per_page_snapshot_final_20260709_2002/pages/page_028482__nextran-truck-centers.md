# Nextran Truck Centers

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-22

**Source:** [NEXTRAN TRUCK CENTERS ACQUIRES QUINCY MACK, DECATUR MACK, AND H&L MACK](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/nextran-truck-centers-acquires-quincy-mack-decatur-mack-and-hl-mack-302314625.html)  ·  `page_028482`

## Facts

- Nextran Truck Centers announced today that it has acquired Quincy Mack, Decatur Mack, and H&L Mack.
- Quincy Mack, Decatur Mack, and H&L Mack is a well-respected Mack Trucks dealer with five locations across Illinois, Missouri, and Iowa.
- This strategic acquisition further expands Nextran's footprint from 26 to 31 locations across the United States.
- Founded over 40 years ago, Quincy Mack, Decatur Mack, and H&L Mack have built a strong reputation in the Midwest.
- The acquisition will enhance Nextran's industry-leading sales, parts, service and rental & leasing operations.
- Jon W. Pritchett is President and CEO of Nextran Truck Centers.
- The integration of these new locations into Nextran's operations will bolster its position as the world's top-selling Mack Truck dealer, driving the total annual units sold to over 5,500 new and used commercial trucks.
- The acquisition expands Nextran's rental and lease fleet to nearly 1,000 units in operation.
- This growth is supported by a robust network of over 530 service bays and nearly 1,400 employees nationwide.
- This acquisition marks a significant step in Nextran's strategic growth, bringing the total to 12 new locations added over the past three years.
- Jack Sharkey is Owner of Quincy Mack, Decatur Mack, and H&L Mack.
- Established in 1993, Nextran Truck Centers is a full-service commercial truck dealership network located throughout Florida, Georgia, Alabama, Missouri, Colorado, Kansas, Wyoming, and now Illinois and Iowa.
- Nextran's 31 locations are strategically positioned along key transportation routes throughout the United States.
- Nextran sells and services medium and heavy-duty trucks from top manufacturers such as Mack Trucks, Volvo Trucks, Isuzu, HINO, Kalmar Ottawa, GMC, and Ford.
