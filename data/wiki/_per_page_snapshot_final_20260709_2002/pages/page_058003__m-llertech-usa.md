## Facts

- Möllertech USA is an automotive parts supplier.
- The company manufactures interior plastic pillars for Mercedes-Benz vehicles.
- The company manufactures tailgate covers for Mercedes-Benz vehicles.
- These components are used in Mercedes-Benz vehicles produced in Vance, Alabama.
- These components are also used in other Mercedes-Benz factories worldwide.
- The plant is the German auto supplier’s third location in North America.
- The facility will serve as a flagship production facility for the company.
- The design team was challenged to produce budget-friendly and easily constructible details with typical materials but express the owner’s intent for a modern, premium-designed aesthetic.
- The building features floor-to-ceiling glazing, canted recesses, highlighted material usage, and specifically placed accent colors.
