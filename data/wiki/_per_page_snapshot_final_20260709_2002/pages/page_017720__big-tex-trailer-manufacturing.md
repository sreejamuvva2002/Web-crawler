# Big Tex Trailer Manufacturing

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-02-09

**Source:** [Big Tex Trailer World to close Georgia facility and cut 100 jobs | Trucks, Parts, Service](https://www.truckpartsandservice.com/workforce/article/15816663/big-tex-trailer-world-to-close-georgia-facility-and-cut-100-jobs)  ·  `page_017720`

## Facts

- Big Tex Trailer Manufacturing is closing its Trailer World location in Cordele, Ga., and laying off 100 workers.
- The closure and layoffs were disclosed in a Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification Act filing with the state of Georgia.
- The Cordele facility is located at 502 S. Midway Road.
- The location operates as a BigTex Trailer World dealership offering sales, parts, and service on Big Tex and CM trailers.
- The facility will be permanently closed.
- The first worker separations began in January.
- Big Tex manufactures utility, equipment, dump, and heavy-duty gooseneck trailers, along with various work, cargo, and tilt trailers.
- The company is headquartered in Mount Pleasant, Texas.
- Big Tex's trailers are manufactured across facilities in Texas, Oklahoma, Georgia, and Idaho.
