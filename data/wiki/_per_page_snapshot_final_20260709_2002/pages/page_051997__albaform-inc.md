# AlbaForm Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2013-09-26

**Source:** [Czech Republic Manufacturing Firm Opens Doors in Hall County | Georgia Public Broadcasting](https://www.gpb.org/blogs/georgia-works/2013/09/26/czech-republic-manufacturing-firm-opens-doors-in-hall-county)  ·  `page_051997`

## Facts

- AlbaForm Inc. officially opened its doors September 23rd at a ribbon cutting for a new Hall County manufacturing plant.
- The Czech Republic based manufacturer makes metal components for the automotive industry.
- The Flowery Branch facility is AlbaForm's first plant in the United States.
- Currently the Hall County location employs 16 people but is expecting to hire an additional 40 employees, primarily computer programmers, during the next year.
- According to the Hall County Chamber of Commerce, the county is now home to more than 40 international companies.
