# Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-05-23

**Source:** [Suzuki Celebrates 20 Years of ATV Manufacturing in the United States - Motor Sports NewsWire](https://motorsportsnewswire.com/2022/05/23/suzuki-celebrates-20-years-of-atv-manufacturing-in-the-united-states/)  ·  `page_010942`

## Facts

- Suzuki Motor USA, LLC celebrated 20 years of manufacturing KingQuad ATVs at the Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corporation (SMAC) facility in Rome, Georgia on May 23, 2022.
- KingQuad ATVs first rolled off the assembly line in Rome, Georgia in May 2002 and have been built exclusively in the United States ever since.
- The SMAC facility is a multi-million dollar, 35-acre manufacturing site located in Rome, Georgia.
- The facility's location places it in the center of the biggest ATV market in the world, enabling access to real-world rider feedback for product refinement and timely delivery to dealers worldwide.
- Suzuki invented the four-wheel ATV in 1983 and has been building award-winning KingQuad ATVs at the Rome, Georgia facility since 2002.
- Kerry Graeber, Vice President and Division Manager of MC/ATV Sales and Marketing at Suzuki, stated: 'This is an extremely important milestone for Suzuki,' and added that 'Suzuki is very proud to have built KingQuad ATVs in America for the past 20 years,' emphasizing that the vehicles' performance, toughness, and durability reflect the commitment and dedication of the Rome, Georgia workforce.
- The SMAC facility is a key component of Suzuki’s global ATV business success.
- KingQuad ATVs manufactured at the Rome, Georgia facility are shipped to U.S. Suzuki dealers and nearly 30 countries worldwide.
- All KingQuad ATVs built at the facility are constructed in the USA using both U.S. and imported parts.
- Suzuki Motor USA, LLC distributes motorcycles, ATVs, scooters, accessories, and ECSTAR oils & chemicals through a dealer network across 49 states.
- Suzuki Motor Corporation (SMC), the parent company based in Hamamatsu, Japan, was founded in 1909, incorporated in 1920, and maintains business relations with 201 countries/regions.
