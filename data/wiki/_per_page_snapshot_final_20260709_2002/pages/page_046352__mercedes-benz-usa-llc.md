# Mercedes-Benz USA LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Mercedes-Benz USA Intends to Transition the Distribution of smart from Penske Automotive Group](https://www.autoarabia.org/articles/808/Mercedes-Benz+USA+Intends+to+Transition+the+Distribution+of+smart+from+...)  ·  `page_046352`

## Facts

- Mercedes-Benz USA LLC and Penske Automotive Group (NYSE:PAG) announced discussions to transition the distribution rights, management, sales and marketing activities of smart USA from Penske Automotive Group to Mercedes-Benz USA.
- The transition is expected to occur by the end of the second quarter of 2011.
- The proposed change marks a further step in implementing the future growth strategy of the smart brand.
- The move reflects last year's reorganization at smart headquarters in Germany, where smart is now managed as an independent brand but fully integrated into Mercedes-Benz Cars business unit of Daimler AG.
- Mercedes-Benz President and CEO Ernst Lieb stated excitement about working toward integration of smart into the MBUSA organization and looking forward to working with dealer partners to exceed customer expectations for the vehicle.
- Ernst Lieb noted that adding smart, combined with the Autohaus dealer initiative, will help build upon the momentum the Mercedes-Benz brand achieved in the U.S.
- Penske Automotive Group Chairman Roger Penske stated that introducing the smart brand to the U.S. has been a unique opportunity and highlighted delivering over 45,000 vehicles over the last three years.
- Roger Penske explained that aligning smart distribution with Daimler worldwide is the logical next step for the smart brand in the U.S., enabling growth through the Mercedes-Benz dealership network and leading in conservation and meeting future fuel economy standards.
- Dr. Annette Winkler, Vice President smart, credited Roger Penske and his team for successfully paving the way for the smart brand in the US.
- Dr. Annette Winkler stated that integrating smart wholesale into their own national sales organization is an important part of the global growth strategy for smart and a step towards success in the US market.
- The addition of the fuel efficient smart fortwo and smart fortwo electric vehicles to Mercedes-Benz USA's product portfolio underscores the company's commitment to meet even more stringent fuel economy standards in the future.
- The smart fortwo achieves EPA estimated 41 mpg in highway driving.
- Manufactured by Daimler AG, smart is currently sold in more than 40 countries.
- More than 1.2 million smart fortwo have been sold worldwide since 1998.
- Smart has established itself as the think tank for urban and electric mobility for Daimler AG.
- Smart plays an important role in Daimler AG's plans to make innovative future mobility concepts such as car2go available in Europe and the United States.
