# Club Car, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-15

**Source:** [CLUB CAR LAUNCHES "CRU" NEIGHBORHOOD ELECTRIC VEHICLE](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/club-car-launches-cru-neighborhood-electric-vehicle-301773267.html)  ·  `page_005259`

## Facts

- Club Car is a world-leading manufacturer of small-wheel, zero-emissions electric vehicles.
- Club Car unveiled the CRU, a street-legal lifestyle vehicle designed to disrupt the low-speed vehicle (LSV) market for consumers.
- The CRU expands Club Car's leading electric vehicle portfolio into a new category of Neighborhood Electric Vehicles (NEVs).
- NEVs are designed to meet the needs of consumers seeking a more fun and sustainable option for short trips.
- The CRU was designed in partnership with BMW Designworks.
- The CRU features a sleek, ergonomic shape and superior handling.
- The CRU has a capacity for six occupants and a top speed of 25 miles per hour.
- The vehicle includes a table for riders to gather around and front bucket seats that can be flipped around to face rear passengers.
- Club Car is launching the CRU through a campaign encouraging consumers to "Be a Detourist."
- The campaign illustrates the spontaneous, social, and easygoing lifestyle the vehicle enables.
- Ross Atherton, Vice President at Club Car, stated that the CRU is a game-changer for the electric vehicle market and a missing link in the micro-mobility ecosystem.
- Atherton noted that with more than 60 years of innovation and a reputation for safety and high quality, Club Car is reimagining an entirely new category of Neighborhood Electric Vehicles.
- The CRU serves as a sustainable and fun alternative to using a full-sized car to run errands or take quick trips around town.
- The vehicle directly meets the needs of consumers who currently use golf cars or other vehicles to run errands or take quick trips down the road in their neighborhoods.
- Club Car CRU is launching to a limited dealer network across Atlanta, Raleigh, Dallas, Phoenix, Miami, Tampa, Orlando, and Charleston.
- Additional markets will open this year.
- Club Car was founded in 1958.
- Club Car's product portfolio includes gas and electric golf, utility, and personal transportation vehicles for commercial and consumer markets.
- As an industry leader in electrification and sustainability, Club Car is on the forefront of environmentally responsible Zero Emission Vehicle (ZEV) technologies.
