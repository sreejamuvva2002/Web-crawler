# JTEKT North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-12-07

**Source:** [JTEKT North America’s new expansion to create dozens of new jobs in Greenville Co.](https://www.foxcarolina.com/2023/12/07/jtekt-north-americas-new-expansion-create-dozens-new-jobs-greenville-co/)  ·  `page_045324`

## Facts

- JTEKT North America announced it is expanding its manufacturing operation in Greenville County and creating 87 new jobs.
- JTEKT North America manufactures a broad range of products under the brands JTEKT Automotive Systems, Koyo Bearings and Toyoda Machine Tools.
- The company maintains its regional headquarters in Greenville, South Carolina.
- JTEKT North America operates 12 manufacturing sites, three technical centers and five distribution centers throughout North America.
- The expansion adds seven new machine lines and two new assembly lines to the existing facility located at 1866 Old Grove Road in Piedmont.
- Additional processes will be added to machine components internally at the site.
- JTEKT North America will have the capability to produce front drive shafts at its expanded facility.
- Operations are expected to be online in the third quarter of 2025.
