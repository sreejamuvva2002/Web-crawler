# Propex Operating Company, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- The Georgia Department of Labor (GDOL) will host a recruitment for Propex Operating Company, LLC, a geosynthetic solutions company, to hire 13 employees to work in Ringgold.
- The recruitment will be held on Thursday, June 29, from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. at the GDOL’s Dalton Career Center located at 1406 Chattanooga Ave. in Dalton.
- The employer is recruiting beaming machine operators, extrusion line operators, industrial maintenance technicians, nonwoven line operators, tie in machine operators, weavers and weaving loom technicians.
- Due to U.S. Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) regulations, all applicants must be at least 18 years old.
- While it is not required, a high school diploma or a General Education Diploma (GED) is preferred.
- All applicants are required to have at least six months of related experience.
- At least two years of related experience is preferred for the industrial maintenance technicians.
- Salaries will depend on the position and experience.
- Benefits include health, dental, vision and a 401k plan.
- Applicants can apply online by creating an account and uploading or preparing a resume.
- Having an Employ Georgia account expedites the interview process.
- All applicants are encouraged to dress appropriately to improve their opportunities for jobs.
