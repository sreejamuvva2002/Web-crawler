# Storion Energy LLC

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2025-01-31

**Source:** [Largo and Stryten Energy Finalize Formation of Storion Energy to Strengthen U.S. Energy Resilience and Address Long Duration Energy Storage Needs](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20250204863107/en/Largo-and-Stryten-Energy-Finalize-Formation-of-Storion-Energy-to-Strengthen-U.S.-Energy-Resilience-and-Address-Long-Duration-Energy-Storage-Needs)  ·  `page_029427`

## Facts

- Largo Inc. subsidiary Largo Clean Energy Corp. and Stryten Critical E-Storage, LLC affiliate of Stryten Energy LLC finalized the formation of Storion Energy LLC effective Friday, January 31, 2025.
- Storion Energy is a joint venture focused on removing barriers to entry for battery manufacturers to domestically sourced, price-competitive components for the flow battery industry, including vanadium electrolyte.
- The partnership leverages access to Largo’s high-quality vanadium supply, Largo Physical Vanadium Corp.’s innovative electrolyte leasing model, and Stryten’s U.S.-based manufacturing expertise.
- Francesco D’Alessio, President of LCE, stated the closing represents a transformational step for Largo as it advances its investment strategy into the fast-growing vanadium flow battery market.
- Storion Energy provides a direct channel for Largo’s high-quality vanadium products into the long duration energy storage sector, reinforcing Largo's position as a leading and reliable supplier of vanadium.
- Through Storion, the partners are establishing a fully integrated vanadium electrolyte supply chain in North America to address a key challenge for vanadium flow battery adoption while unlocking new growth opportunities for Largo.
- The combination of Stryten’s battery manufacturing expertise, Largo’s vanadium expertise, and LPV’s innovative electrolyte leasing model positions Storion Energy to deliver innovative, cost-competitive solutions for long duration energy storage.
- Each of LCE and Stryten contributed certain vanadium flow battery-related assets and liabilities to Storion.
- Stryten paid US$1 million in cash directly to Largo and will contribute US$6 million over time to fund Storion’s operations.
- LCE and Stryten each hold a 50% equity interest in Storion, with customary pre-emption rights and anti-dilution protections.
- Storion’s board composition will be generally proportional to ownership, with Stryten holding one additional seat.
- Largo amended its Safekeeping and Supply Agreements with LPV and assigned its Safekeeping Agreement to Storion.
- Largo and Storion entered a separate supply agreement granting Storion a right of first offer for vanadium products.
- Required third party consents, which were closing conditions, were received.
- Storion Energy intends to bring energy resilience and security to the U.S. by removing barriers to entry for battery manufacturers to domestically sourced, price-competitive electrolyte used in vanadium redox flow batteries for long-duration energy storage.
- Storion will support scalable domestic electrolyte production to establish a fully integrated vertical supply chain for utility-scale VRFB LDES solutions.
- VRFB technology is a safe and reliable option for Battery Energy Storage Systems that need to provide energy storage of four or more hours.
- Storion has locations in Alpharetta, Georgia and Wilmington, Massachusetts.
- Stryten Energy is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia, and partners with recognized companies to meet growing demand for reliable and sustainable energy storage capacity.
- Stryten Energy's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers and energy performance management software.
- Largo Physical Vanadium Corp. aims to provide an exchange-traded investment alternative for investors interested in direct exposure to physical vanadium, a metal essential to achieving a greener world in key industries such as steel, aerospace and energy storage.
- LPV's strategy includes owning and actively supplying vanadium to end users of vanadium flow batteries to advance integration of renewable energy in long duration storage.
- Largo is a globally recognized supplier of high-quality vanadium and ilmenite products sourced from its Maracás Menchen Mine in Brazil.
- As one of the world's largest primary vanadium producers, Largo produces critical materials that empower global industries including steel, aerospace, defense, chemical, and energy storage sectors.
- Largo is strategically invested in the clean energy storage sector through its 50% ownership of Storion Energy.
