# Federal Tax Credits for EV Charging Infrastructure

**Type:** policy_or_incentive  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Tax Incentives](https://www.fueleconomy.gov/feg/taxcenter.shtml)  ·  `page_005348`

## Facts

- Infrastructure tax credits could save up to 30% off the cost of installing EV charging property.
- The tax credits also apply to associated energy storage property.
- Eligible locations for these infrastructure tax credits include homes, businesses, or facilities.
- The program website is administered by Oak Ridge National Laboratory for the U.S. Department of Energy and the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency.
