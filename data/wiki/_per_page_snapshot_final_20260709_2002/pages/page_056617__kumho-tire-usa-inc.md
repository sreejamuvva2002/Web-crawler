## Facts

- Kumho Tire USA established its U.S. headquarters in Atlanta, Georgia.
- Kumho Tire USA serves as the U.S. sales, marketing, product development, and distribution arm of Kumho Tire Co. Inc.
- Kumho Tire was founded in 1960.
- Kumho Tire has more than 50 years of history immersed in an innovative approach to product development, market expansion, technical progression, and environmental awareness.
- Kumho Tire is positioned as one of the world’s top tire manufacturers.
- Kumho Tire products are distributed in 160 countries.
