# Porsche Cars North America, Incorporated

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2017-12-21

**Source:** [Any Porsche You Want Any Time You Want for $100 Per Day — But... - The Gate](https://thegatewithbriancohen.com/any-porsche-you-want-any-time-you-want-for-100-per-day-but/)  ·  `page_048900`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Incorporated operates a subscription program called Porsche Passport that allows members to access the latest models of Porsche cars for a monthly fee.
- The program is currently only available in the greater Atlanta metropolitan area because Porsche Cars North America's headquarters is located there.
- Members use an official mobile application to select vehicles, which are cleaned by a personal concierge and delivered to their home or office with contents transferred from the previous vehicle.
- The program includes unlimited mileage, comprehensive liability insurance up to one million dollars for liability, maintenance, repairs, full detail washes, and roadside assistance, but excludes fuel.
- The Launch membership plan provides access to four different Porsche models for $2,000.00 per month, or as low as $64.52 per day.
- The Accelerate membership plan provides access to seven different Porsche models for $3,000.00 per month, or as low as $96.78 per day.
- Both membership plans require a one-time activation fee of $500.00 and a minimum commitment period of 31 days.
- The insurance coverage includes a $1,000.00 deductible that the member is responsible for paying on any claim.
- Not all applicants are approved for the Porsche Passport program.
- Service availability is restricted to a defined part of the greater Atlanta metropolitan area.
- The article author previously rode in a Porsche courtesy of Delta Air Lines around the international airport serving the greater Atlanta metropolitan area.
