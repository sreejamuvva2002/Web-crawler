# Wabash National Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-11-08

**Source:** [Wabash National Enhances New Capacity with Increased Supply Certainty](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/wabash-national-enhances-capacity-increased-115500848.html)  ·  `page_014623`

## Facts

- Wabash National Corporation announced a 10-year agreement with Hydro, a leader in the global aluminum and energy industry.
- Under the agreement, Hydro will be a key supplier for dry van trailers, refrigerated trailers with molded structural composite (MSC) technology, flatbed trailers, and truck bodies through 2031.
- The agreement also covers requirements for new products Wabash National may release through 2031.
- The enhanced supply aligns with Wabash National's recent announcement of additional dry van capacity set to come online at the beginning of 2023.
- Future capacity for MSC Technology products is also planned under the agreement.
- Richard Mansilla, Vice President, Global Supply Chain at Wabash National, stated the partnership will help address aluminum supply needs to increase production of dry van trailers, refrigerated trailers, and truck bodies with MSC Technology in the near term.
- In the longer term, Wabash National plans to work more closely with Hydro on innovation and footprint optimization.
- Prior to this agreement, Hydro had supplied Wabash National with aluminum extrusions for over 12 years.
- Hydro is a three-time Wabash National supplier award winner and was named Supplier of the Year in 2020.
- Jerrod Hoeft, Hydro's VP National Accounts, Transportation, stated that Hydro supports industries through innovative products and views Wabash National as a visionary leadership partner for creating breakthrough customer value.
- Wabash National designs and manufactures dry freight and refrigerated trailers, platform trailers, tank trailers, dry and refrigerated truck bodies, structural composite panels and products, trailer aerodynamic solutions, and specialty food grade processing equipment.
- Wabash National's products are sold under the brand names Wabash National®, Benson®, Brenner® Tank, Bulk Tank International, DuraPlate®, Supreme®, Transcraft®, Walker Engineered Products, and Walker Transport.
- Wabash National Corporation is headquartered in Lafayette, Indiana.
- Hydro has operations in 40 countries with over 140 locations and approximately 30,000 employees.
- Hydro owns and operates businesses and investments with a base in sustainable industries including aluminum, energy, metal recycling, renewables, and batteries.
