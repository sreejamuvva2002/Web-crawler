## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels, Inc. designs, develops, manufactures, and sells high-performance aerogel insulation materials primarily serving the energy industrial and electric vehicle (EV) thermal barrier markets.
- The company operates through two reportable segments: Energy Industrial and Thermal Barrier.
- Proprietary products include PyroThin thermal barriers specifically designed for EV battery packs.
- Other product lines include Pyrogel and Cryogel blankets for refineries, petrochemical plants, LNG facilities, and power generation assets, as well as Spaceloft products for building and subsea applications.
- Manufacturing operations are concentrated in the United States, including a facility in East Providence, Rhode Island.
- The East Providence facility experienced an explosion in April and began a staged restart of manufacturing operations following mechanical and safety reviews.
- Aspen Aerogels serves a global customer base spanning North America, Europe, Asia, Latin America, and beyond.
- Top-tier partners include General Motors, SK, Evonik, and ExxonMobil.
- General Motors named Aspen Aerogels its 2025 Supplier of the Year for Innovation in Electric Vehicle Solutions.
- The company employs approximately 554 full-time staff.
- Q1 2026 earnings call highlighted expectations of approximately 20% energy industrial growth for 2026, suggesting a meaningful demand recovery in the EV segment.
- Investors are watching the company's EV battery thermal barrier expansion as a longer-term growth catalyst.
- Aspen Aerogels reported a 66% Q4 revenue decline and launched a strategic review with Piper Sandler as financial advisor in February.
- The company reported a steep 51.88% year-over-year revenue decline and a 92.14% drop in net profit in its latest quarterly results.
