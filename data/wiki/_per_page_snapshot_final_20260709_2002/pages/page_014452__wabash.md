# Wabash

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-18

**Source:** [Wabash Expands Southeast Network to Enhance Service](https://www.fleetequipmentmag.com/wabash-southeast-network-expansion/)  ·  `page_014452`

## Facts

- Wabash is expanding its network in the Southeast to improve customer access and service in the Atlanta metro area.
- The company is opening a new Parts & Service center in the Atlanta area.
- Wabash is expanding its dealer relationship with Fleetco.
- Wabash is adding CS Truck and Trailer to its Preferred Partner Network.
- The new Atlanta-area Parts & Service Center is strategically located in the Southeast’s trucking corridor.
- The facility offers faster access to parts, service, and truck body upfitting.
- The center provides Wabash’s Ready-to-Mount (RTM) truck bodies for dry freight and platform applications.
- Wabash stated these RTM truck bodies provide rapid turnaround for in-stock chassis.
- The Atlanta site officially opened on Sept. 18, 2025.
- The Atlanta center joins existing Wabash service centers in California, Florida, Ohio, Pennsylvania, and Texas.
- Fleetco, a long-standing Wabash dealer, will now represent Wabash in the greater Atlanta market.
- This dealer expansion expands Fleetco's footprint across the Southeast.
- The expansion gives customers additional access to new and used equipment and enhances local sales and service coverage.
- Three CS Truck and Trailer locations in Georgia were added to the Preferred Partner Network.
- The network addition extends expert service, maintenance, and access to Wabash Genuine Parts through authorized third-party dealerships.
- The company said the Preferred Partner Network is designed to make high-quality parts and service more accessible through a unified national network.
