# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hyundai Megasite - Hussey Gay Bell](https://husseygaybell.com/portfolio/hyundai-megasite/)  ·  `page_021162`

## Facts

- In 2022, Hyundai Motor Group announced a $5.5 billion investment to establish Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA).
- HMGMA is Hyundai's first US-based electric vehicle (EV) mass production facility.
- The HMGMA facility is located on a 3,000-plus acre site in Bryan County, Georgia.
- HMGMA integrates cutting-edge technology and sustainability initiatives.
- Total investment in Georgia related to the project has surged to over $8 billion.
- HMGMA and its network of Tier 1 suppliers are creating 12,000 direct, long-term jobs.
- The project is driving significant economic growth across the coastal region.
- In March 2025, the HMGMA Metaplant officially opened, marking the start of commercial production.
- The project reinforces Georgia's position as a leader in EV innovation and manufacturing.
- Hussey Gay Bell provided architecture, engineering, and surveying services for multiple Tier 1 supplier projects related to the development.
- On-site facilities were built for Hyundai Glovis.
- On-site facilities were built for Hyundai Mobis.
- On-site facilities were built for Hyundai Materials.
- An on-site facility was built for the Hyundai Motor Group/LG Energy Solution (LGES) JV EV Battery Cell Manufacturing Plant.
- Off-site supplier projects were completed for Seoyon E-Hwa.
- Off-site supplier projects were completed for Daechang Seat Corp.
- Off-site supplier projects were completed for Seohan Auto Georgia.
- Off-site supplier projects were completed for Sewon America.
- Off-site supplier projects were completed for PHA.
- The supplier projects were designed and constructed in parallel with HMGMA’s state-of-the-art smart factory.
- The design and construction ensured seamless integration and operational efficiency to support Hyundai’s next-generation EV production.
