# JAC Products Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-11-01

**Source:** [Pontiac Automotive Supplier Acquired by Oklahoma Investment Firms - DBusiness Magazine](https://www.dbusiness.com/daily-news/pontiac-automotive-supplier-acquired-by-oklahoma-investment-firms/)  ·  `page_056334`

## Facts

- JAC Products Inc., a Tier 1 designer, manufacturer, and global supplier in Pontiac, has been acquired by Tulsa, Okla.-based Private Equity Fund Argonaut Private Equity and Oklahoma City-based Hall Capital.
- Terms of the acquisition deal were not disclosed.
- The existing management team at JAC will remain with the company to drive the implementation of strategic growth plans.
- The supplier employs more than 1,500 workers.
- JAC produces original equipment roof track systems, cargo management systems, running boards, and step rails.
- The company plans to diversify its product mix and expand globally into new markets.
- Mike Wood, CEO of JAC, stated that JAC is in a major growth phase and the new ownership is committed to implementing their strategic plan.
- Mike Wood will continue his leadership at JAC following the acquisition.
- JAC aims to strengthen relationships with customers by providing new innovative products in the markets they serve.
- Argonaut Private Equity was founded in 2002 and manages investments across multiple asset classes.
- Argonaut Private Equity has deployed $6 billion of capital.
- Argonaut Private Equity has over 200 investments.
- Hall Capital is a third-generation, family-owned private investment company based in Oklahoma City.
- Hall Capital’s family of private investment funds provides capital for growth, acquisitions, recapitalizations, management buyouts, real estate, and energy investments.
- JAC Products was founded in 1967.
- JAC supplies OEM manufacturers worldwide with engineering, sales, manufacturing, and partnership locations in North America, Europe, China, Japan, and South America.
- Wynnchurch Capital, an Illinois-based investment firm, previously owned JAC Products.
