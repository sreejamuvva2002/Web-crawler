# Anovion Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-22

**Source:** [Anovion Technologies to build $800M facility in Georgia | Manufacturing Dive](https://www.manufacturingdive.com/news/anovion-technologies-800m-facility-in-georgia/650808/)  ·  `page_000441`

## Facts

- Synthetic graphite maker Anovion Technologies plans to invest $800 million in a manufacturing facility in Bainbridge, Georgia.
- The company’s first large-scale facility is expected to produce 40,000 metric tons of synthetic graphite annually for lithium-ion batteries.
- CEO Eric Stopka stated that existing infrastructure makes Southwest Georgia an attractive location for Anovion’s new facility.
- CEO Eric Stopka noted that proximity to other battery and EV manufacturing plants will allow the company to further reduce its carbon footprint.
- The 1.5 million-square-foot facility will be located near low- and carbon-free energy sources.
- The facility site will offer short supply chains and access to rail infrastructure.
- CEO Eric Stopka said Anovion conducted a thorough search to identify a location addressing energy, transportation, logistics, and human capital needs to produce high-quality and sustainable synthetic graphite anode material.
- Operations at the new Georgia facility are expected to come online in late 2025.
- The company plans to hire for over 400 jobs in technical, production, and engineering roles, as well as administrative and management positions.
- Anovion Technologies received $117 million from the Department of Energy last year under the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law.
- The DOE funding will go toward building the new Georgia facility and expanding its hydro-powered manufacturing facility in Sanborn, New York.
- The expanded Sanborn, New York site will provide support and research in addition to production.
- Since receiving its federal grant in October, Anovion Technologies has begun signing supply deals with battery makers.
- In November, Anovion Technologies partnered with American Battery Factory to supply materials for the latter's lithium-iron phosphate battery production network.
- Hyundai Motor Group is building an EV and battery manufacturing hub near Savannah, Georgia.
- Battery maker SK On announced plans last month for a $5 billion EV battery manufacturing facility in Bartow County, Georgia.
