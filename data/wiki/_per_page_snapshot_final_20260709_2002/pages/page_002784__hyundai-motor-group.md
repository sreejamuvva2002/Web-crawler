# Hyundai Motor Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-29

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group Signs MOU with SK On to Secure EV Battery Supply for North America](https://www.hyundai.com/worldwide/en/newsroom/detail/hyundai-motor-group-signs-mou-with-sk-on-to-secure-ev-battery-supply-for-north-america-0000000155)  ·  `page_002784`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group signed a memorandum of understanding (MOU) with SK On Co., Ltd. to secure a supply of EV batteries in North America.
- The MOU signing took place at SK Group Headquarters in Korea.
- Heung-soo Kim, Executive Vice President and Head of Corporate Future Growth Planning Division & EV Division of Hyundai Motor Group, participated in the signing.
- Young-chan Choi, Chief Administrative Officer of SK On, participated in the signing.
- Under the MOU, both parties will cooperate to provide SK On batteries to Hyundai Motor Group’s plants in the U.S. after 2025 for EV production.
- Executive Vice President Kim stated that the EV battery MOU will accelerate efforts to secure EV leadership in the North American market.
- Hyundai expects the stable supply of EV batteries from SK On to contribute to emissions reduction and meet climate goals in the market.
- SK On Chief Administrative Officer Choi Young-chan stated that the cooperation between SK On and Hyundai Motor Group is expected to create significant synergy.
- Both companies expect to hold a solid position in the process of electrification in the North American auto market.
- Hyundai Motor Group has set a global target to sell 3.23 million full electric vehicles annually by 2030.
- To realize its electrification goals, Hyundai Motor Group plans to establish a global EV production network to ensure a stable supply of EVs around the globe.
