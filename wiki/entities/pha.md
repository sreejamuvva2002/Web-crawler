# PHA

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center, Chatham County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Global Tier-1 automotive parts supplier that will build a new production facility in Chatham County to supply Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America with door modules, tailgate latches and hood latches.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Supplier of components for Hyundai's Metaplant EV and battery manufacturing hub, contributing to Georgia’s electric-mobility supply chain.
