# Georgia System Operations Corporation

## Facts

- Manages and monitors electric generation and transmission for 38 of Georgia’s electric membership corporations.
- The managed corporations light up half the households in Georgia and cover two-thirds of the state.
- Provides safe, reliable, independent electric system operations to deliver affordable power for nearly 5 million Georgians every day and every hour.
- Operates as a not-for-profit corporation on a cooperative basis.
- Is member-owned and member-directed, with each EMC having a voice in how it serves them and operates.
