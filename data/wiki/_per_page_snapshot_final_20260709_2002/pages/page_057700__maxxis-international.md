# Maxxis International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2005-11-10

**Source:** [Maxxis Adds New R&D Centers in Asia and US - Racer X](https://racerxonline.com/2005/11/10/maxxis-adds-new-rd-centers-in-asia-and-us)  ·  `page_057700`

## Facts

- Construction is complete on the first new center, near Shanghai, China.
- Workers are currently putting the finishing touches on the six-story, 143,182-square-foot facility, which will employ 200 engineers.
- Plans are also underway for construction of a nearby world-class proving ground, expected to open in 2008.
- The three-story, 56,694-square-foot Taiwan facility is scheduled for completion in June 2006.
- Like the Shanghai center, the Taiwan operation will employ 200 engineers.
- A new 25,600-square-foot, one-story center in Suwanee, Georgia, near Maxxis’ North American headquarters, will also open in June 2006.
- The Suwanee facility will replace a smaller Maxxis tech center located in Norcross, Georgia.
- The new centers will allow Maxxis to continue to improve its product offerings, with cutting edge research and groundbreaking technology.
- Maxxis International is a producer of high quality automotive, light truck, motorcycle, ATV, bicycle, lawn & garden and industrial tires.
- Maxxis has delivered quality tires to original equipment and aftermarket customers for over 38 years.
