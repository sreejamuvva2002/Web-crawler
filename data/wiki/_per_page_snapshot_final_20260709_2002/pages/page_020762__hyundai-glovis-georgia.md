# Hyundai Glovis Georgia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2014-12-18

**Source:** [Hyundai Glovis Georgia to create 190 jobs in Harris County – Sowegalive](https://sowegalive.com/2014/12/18/hyundai-glovis-georgia-to-create-190-jobs-in-harris-county/)  ·  `page_020762`

## Facts

- Gov. Nathan Deal announced on December 18, 2014, that Hyundai Glovis Georgia will relocate its parts consolidation center to West Point.
- The relocation will create up to 190 jobs over four years.
- The company will develop a new, state-of-the-art 310,080-square-foot sub-assembly and sequencing facility in the Northwest Harris Business Park.
- The facility will be used to assemble and distribute components to meet the strict demands and timetables of its suppliers.
- Hyundai Glovis Georgia Chief Operating Officer Hong Kee Rhee expressed appreciation for support from West Point, Harris County, and the state of Georgia.
- Yoonie Kim, senior project manager on the international investment team at the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD), assisted Hyundai Glovis Georgia on behalf of the state.
- The City of West Point Development Authority and Georgia Electric Membership Corp also assisted with the project.
- Transwestern represented Hyundai Glovis Georgia during the site selection process.
- Harris County Commission Chairman Harry Lange welcomed Hyundai Glovis Georgia as a new corporate citizen and noted its commitment to Harris County.
- Grand opening for the facility is anticipated in the fall of 2015.
- GDEcD Commissioner Chris Carr highlighted Georgia's competitive business climate, solid network of suppliers, direct access to leading original equipment manufacturers, and interconnected transportation infrastructure as key factors for companies like Hyundai Glovis Georgia.
- Hyundai Glovis designs logistics strategies tailored to customers' environments and utilizes advanced information systems and logistics technologies.
- The company provides total logistics services in domestic and international markets, shipping business operations, and logistics consulting.
