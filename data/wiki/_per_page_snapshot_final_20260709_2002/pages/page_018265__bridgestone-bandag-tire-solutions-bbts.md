# Bridgestone Bandag Tire Solutions (BBTS)

**Source:** [Bridgestone Bandag offers new fuel economy guide | FleetOwner](https://www.fleetowner.com/emissions-efficiency/article/21661253/bridgestone-bandag-offers-new-fuel-economy-guide)  ·  `page_018265`

## Facts

- Bridgestone Bandag Tire Solutions (BBTS) has released its fourth fuel economy guide for large trucks titled “Tires & Truck Fuel Economy – a New Perspective.”
- The guide provides fleets with recommendations that take into consideration real-world factors affecting fuel economy.
- Singh Ahluwalia, president of sales & marketing at BBTS, stated that trucking is a business of pennies and emphasized there is no single action to maximize fuel economy or ensure financial success in the industry.
- The 37-page guide lists factors in tires, drivers, vehicles, documentation, environment, and operations that can affect fuel economy.
- The guide notes how differences between tests and real-world operations can skew expectations and stresses the importance of sound fuel efficiency techniques.
- The guide states that no fuel-efficient component is worth using unless it saves more money than it costs over its useful life, advising users to weigh total cost against total savings.
- Copies of the guide are available through Bridgestone Bandag sales representatives or by visiting their website.
