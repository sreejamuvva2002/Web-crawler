# Vanguard National Trailer Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2016-11-02

**Source:** [Trenton Vanguard Factory](https://semitrailers.net/semi-trailers/trenton-vanguard-factory/)  ·  `page_013166`

## Facts

- Vanguard National Trailer Corporation opened its new 356,000-square-foot dry van trailer manufacturing plant in Trenton on August 15, 2016.
- The Trenton Vanguard factory brought 400 new jobs to the area.
- This is Vanguard’s second dry van manufacturing plant, joining the facility in Monon as a leading producer of dry vans and reefers.
- The company broke ground at the site in the Dade County Industrial Park on September 3, 2015.
- Vanguard is a longtime and valued partner of ILoca Services.
- ILoca Services sells, rents, and leases Vanguard's dry vans and refrigerated trailers to customers.
