# HSBMA

## Facts

- HSBMA is a major development in Georgia's industrial sector.
- HSBMA is a joint venture company between Hyundai Motor Group and SK On.
- The joint venture has established an electric vehicle battery cell manufacturer covering 26,136,000 square feet at 5059 HWY 411, Kingston, Georgia 30145.
- Choosing this location shows a focus on efficiency and innovation, with easy access to HMG's vehicle production site.
- The factory will produce 50 million battery cells.
- Production capacity supports around 300,000 electric vehicles.
- HSBMA is preparing to fully launch in 2025.
- HSBMA is committed to promoting sustainable transportation and boosting the local economy.
- HSBMA was named "2023 Georgia’s Deal of the Year".
- The company's mission is to drive the future of sustainable transportation by producing high-performance EV batteries using SK On’s cutting-edge battery technology.
