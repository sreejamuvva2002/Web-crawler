## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is a manufacturer and distributor of premier thermoset powder coatings for the Architectural, Heavy Duty Equipment, Automotive, TruCoater: Custom Coater, Furniture and Fixtures, Appliance and General Industrial markets.
- TCI Powder Coatings was founded in Ellaville, GA.
- The company initially operated from a modest plant and a portable building that served as the lab and office space.
- TCI has since expanded to become an international company with multiple warehouse locations.
- TCI operates a state-of-the-art product development center.
- TCI employs a staff of several hundred employees focused on servicing customers.
