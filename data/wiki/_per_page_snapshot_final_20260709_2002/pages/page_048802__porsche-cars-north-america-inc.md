# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-24

**Source:** [Porsche Unveils Electric Macan At Fashion’s Biggest Night In New York - DriveSpark News](https://www.drivespark.com/four-wheelers/2024/porsche-celebrates-fashion-night-with-electric-macan-launch-011-46651.html)  ·  `page_048802`

## Facts

- Porsche is set to launch its second all-electric vehicle, the Macan, during a star-studded event at Soho House New York on Monday, May 6th.
- Actor Barry Keoghan will host the evening, which will also feature a live musical performance by British singer and songwriter RAYE.
- The launch unveils the all-electric Macan in the striking color of Provence.
- Ayesha Coker, Vice President of Marketing for Porsche Cars North America, Inc., emphasized the brand's enduring appeal in the fashion sector and its commitment to maintaining the Macan's status as a segment leader through its sleek and sporty design.
- The all-electric Macan embodies a progressive and timeless design while delivering characteristic performance expected from the brand.
- Porsche is setting new standards in e-performance with models like the Macan 4 and Macan Turbo.
- The Macan 4, equipped with Launch Control, offers up to 300 kW (402 hp) of power.
- The Macan Turbo boasts up to 470 kW (630 hp), enabling rapid acceleration and impressive top speeds.
- Porsche Cars North America, Inc., headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, serves as the exclusive U.S. importer of various Porsche models.
- The company supports a network of independently owned and operated Porsche centers across the U.S. to ensure customers receive an unparalleled experience reflecting the brand's history of automotive excellence and innovation.
