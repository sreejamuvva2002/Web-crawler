## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corp. (YMMC) employs more than 2,000 metro Atlanta residents to design and build recreational vehicles at its Newnan manufacturing facilities.
- All of the world’s Yamaha golf cars and Side-by-Sides are manufactured at the facility in Coweta County, Georgia.
- Most of the world’s Yamaha WaveRunners and ATVs are manufactured at the facility in Coweta County, Georgia.
- In 2025, YMMC transitioned from a traditional open-request format to a structured grant cycle for its Outdoor Adventure Grant Program, aligning resources with Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) goals.
- Six inaugural Outdoor Adventure Grants were awarded to the Central Lions Fishing Team, East Coweta High School Bass Fishing Team, Heard County High School Bass Fishing Team, SouthWest Atlanta Youth (SWAY) Mountain Bike Team, Swing Chic Foundation Girls Golf Team, and Whitewater High School Bass Fishing Team.
- Bob Brown, President at Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corp., stated that the shift to a grant-based cycle reflects the company’s long-term commitment to purposeful giving and that outdoor adventure builds confidence, resilience, and connection.
- Spring Outdoor Adventure Grant applications will open on January 15 for local high schools and qualified 501(c)(3) nonprofit organizations.
