# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-03

**Source:** [Inside Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant's advanced assembly process](https://www.savannahnow.com/picture-gallery/business/automotive/2026/06/03/inside-hyundai-motor-group-metaplants-advanced-assembly-process/90385288007/)  ·  `page_011065`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America is located in Ellabell, GA.
- A tour of Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America took place on Tuesday, June 2, 2026.
- Workers at the plant are referred to as 'Meta Pros.'
- Meta Pros inspect the bodies after welding is complete.
- Vehicles can be seen on the bridge moving between buildings on the assembly line.
- Stamps are used to shape body parts for a vehicle, including molds for the Kia Sportage Hybrid.
- Meta Pros work on the molds used to cast vehicle body parts and in the stamping area.
- Robotic arms load vehicle body parts to be moved from stamping to the next area of the building process.
- Vehicle body parts are stored on racks after the stamping process.
- Rows of stamped hoods and side panels are ready to be added during welding.
- Robotic arms move vehicles through the welding process.
- Vehicles move through general assembly after welding is completed.
- Through a partnership with St. Joseph's/Candler, Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America offers onsite medical care.
- A completed Ioniq 5 is moved during operations at the plant.
- An Ioniq 5 goes through the final cleaning process.
- A Kia Sportage Hybrid goes through the final cleaning process.
- The rollout of the first Kia Sportage Turbo-Hybrid assembled at Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America featured a displayed Boston Dynamics Atlas Robot.
- A Hyundai Motor Group sign is on the exterior of the Metaplant.
