# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Novelis Announces Aluminum Recycling and Rolling Plant](https://www.adityabirla.com/en/media/press-releases/novelis-to-build-low-carbon-aluminum-recycling-and-rolling-plant/)  ·  `page_047434`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is the leading sustainable aluminum solutions provider and world leader in aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Novelis will invest $2.5 billion to build a new low-carbon recycling and rolling plant.
- The facility will be located in Bay Minette, Alabama, USA.
- The facility will have an initial capacity of 600 kilotonnes of finished aluminum goods per year.
- Mr. Kumar Mangalam Birla serves as Chairman of the Aditya Birla Group and the Novelis Board of Directors.
- This investment marks the start of another transformational growth phase for Novelis.
- Novelis serves markets including beverage can, automotive, aerospace, and specialties across all geographies.
- Mr. Kumar Mangalam Birla stated this is the largest global greenfield expansion project of the Aditya Birla Group.
- The investment will take the Aditya Birla Group's total investment in US businesses to over $14 billion.
- More than half of the new facility's capacity will serve growing demand for aluminum beverage can sheet in North America.
- Demand is driven by consumer preference for more sustainable packaging.
- Steve Fisher serves as President and CEO of Novelis Inc.
- Novelis is well-positioned to efficiently expand capacity at this facility above the 600kt announced to capture ongoing strong demand.
- Novelis' decision to build a fully integrated, greenfield recycling and rolling plant is backed by strong North American demand for flat-rolled, low-carbon aluminum from can makers and beverage companies.
- Aluminum beverage cans, bottles, and cups are models of sustainable packaging and the circular economy.
- The average "can-to-can" lifecycle is just a couple of months, with recycled cans returning to store shelves in as little as 60 days.
- Ron Lewis serves as Ball Corporation's chief operating officer for global beverage packaging.
- Ball Corporation is committed to creating a circular economy within the aluminum industry and decarbonizing the value chain.
- Novelis' new plant will add domestic production of sustainable aluminum in North America while decreasing the carbon footprint of products.
- The facility will be the first fully integrated aluminum mill built in the U.S. in 40 years.
- The facility is expected to create up to 1,000 high-paying, advanced careers in modern manufacturing.
- The facility will aim to be net carbon neutral for Scope 1 and 2 emissions.
- The facility will be powered primarily by renewable energy.
- The facility will use recycled water and operate as a zero-waste facility.
- The plant will rely on railroad transportation, reducing logistics-related carbon emissions by up to 70 percent compared to road transport.
- The plant will make significant use of advanced automation and digital technologies, including artificial intelligence, augmented reality, and robotics.
- With the new recycling center for beverage cans, Novelis will soon be able to recycle 90 billion cans globally.
- This is an increase from the 74 billion used beverage cans the company currently recycles.
- Novelis has been working to develop circular economies for aluminum through state and federal public policies.
- Novelis has formed partnerships with customers and other stakeholders on new approaches that encourage and incentivize U.S. consumers to recycle more often.
- John Murphy serves as Chief Financial Officer of The Coca-Cola Company.
- The Coca-Cola Company views aluminum cans as an important form of packaging that reduces waste when recycled.
- Novelis' new facility will benefit the Coca-Cola system, customers, and consumers while reducing environmental impact.
- Site work at the Bay Minette location is under way now.
- The company expects to begin commissioning in mid-2025.
- In addition to the beverage can market, the facility will serve the automotive market.
- Aluminum is the fastest growing material in the automotive market as automakers pursue sustainability goals.
- Novelis Inc. is driven by its purpose of shaping a sustainable world together.
- Novelis Inc. is a global leader in the production of innovative aluminum products and solutions.
- Novelis is the world's largest recycler of aluminum.
- Novelis aims to be the leading provider of low-carbon, sustainable aluminum solutions and achieve a fully circular economy.
- Novelis partners with suppliers and customers in aerospace, automotive, beverage can, and specialties industries across North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- Novelis had net sales of $17.1 billion in fiscal year 2022.
- Novelis is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited.
- Hindalco Industries Limited is an industry leader in aluminum and copper.
- Hindalco Industries Limited is the metals flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group, a multinational conglomerate based in Mumbai.
- Novelis cautions that forward-looking statements involve risk and uncertainty.
- Important risk factors are included under the caption "Risk Factors" in the company's Form 10-K filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for the fiscal year ended March 31, 2022.
