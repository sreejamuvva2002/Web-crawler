# Immersion Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-06

**Source:** [Immersion Signs Korean-Based Woory Industrial to a New Multi-Year License for Haptic Technology Use in Automotive Touchscreens | IMMR Stock News](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/IMMR/immersion-signs-korean-based-woory-industrial-to-a-new-multi-year-786bnargynbr.html)  ·  `page_015528`

## Facts

- Immersion Corporation signed a multi-year license agreement with Korean-based Woory Industrial Co. Ltd.
- The agreement licenses Immersion's haptic technologies to Woory for automotive touchscreens manufactured by Woory and sold to automotive OEMs.
- Woory Industrial is one of the leading automotive component suppliers in Korea.
- Mr. Martin Heo, SVP at Woory, stated they are partnering with Immersion to add advanced haptic feedback components to their offering to provide customers with a larger breadth of products and capabilities.
- Haptic technologies improve ease-of-use and screen navigation in automotive touchscreens, reducing distraction and improving safety.
- Mr. Jared Smith, Interim CEO at Immersion, stated the company is pleased to grow its ecosystem with the addition of Woory Industrial haptic-enabled components for the touchscreen.
- The adoption of haptics in automotive HMIs will lead to more innovative use cases and advanced implementations that showcase the important role touch plays as an interface in the car.
- Immersion provides haptic technology solutions for automotive component manufacturers and tier 1 suppliers.
- Immersion’s automotive haptic solutions provide tier-one suppliers with innovative use cases, reference designs for haptic implementations, software, and technologies to facilitate haptics in advanced automotive HMIs.
- Haptic technologies increase the usability of touchscreens and provide a more sustainable and flexible design approach than mechanical buttons.
- Immersion Corporation is the leading innovator of touch feedback technologies, also known as haptics.
- The company invents, accelerates, and scales haptic experiences by providing technology solutions for mobile, automotive, gaming, and consumer electronics.
- Haptic technologies create immersive and realistic experiences that enhance digital interactions by engaging users' sense of touch.
- Immersion is headquartered in San Francisco, California, with offices worldwide.
