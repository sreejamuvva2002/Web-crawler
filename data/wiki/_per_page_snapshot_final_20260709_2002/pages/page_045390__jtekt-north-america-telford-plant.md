# JTEKT North America Telford Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-12-05

**Source:** [JTEKT announcing 2026 closure of Telford plant - SuperTalk 92.9](https://www.supertalk929.com/2025/12/05/jtekt-announcing-2026-closure-of-telford-plant/)  ·  `page_045390`

## Facts

- JTEKT North America has sent a WARN notice announcing 136 workers losing their jobs at its Telford plant in Washington County, Tennessee.
- The shutdown timeline begins at the end of April next year and concludes in August.
- A change in automotive design with conventional engines being used less is one factor in closing the plant, according to the company.
- A decline in the use of their parts is another factor cited by the company for the closure.
