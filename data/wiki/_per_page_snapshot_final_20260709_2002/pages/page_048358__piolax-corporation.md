## Facts

- Piolax Corporation held a grand opening ceremony for its automated warehouse in Canton, Georgia on September 30th.
- Approximately 100 people attended the event, including Consul General Shinozuka, President Shimazu from Piolax Inc. in Japan, Canton Mayor Hobgood, Cherokee County Economic Development Officer President Martin, and other officials and representatives.
- Piolax Corporation is a US subsidiary of Piolax Inc., Japan, with Genichi Ishikawa serving as its president.
- The company specializes in manufacturing plastic and metal automotive parts.
- In light of recent trade expansion in the United States, Piolax Corporation established its own automated warehouse to replace previously rented warehouse space.
- The new fully automated warehouse will increase storage capacity.
- The facility will reduce more than half the current forklifts used.
- The automation initiative will increase safety and efficiency of the warehouse operations.
