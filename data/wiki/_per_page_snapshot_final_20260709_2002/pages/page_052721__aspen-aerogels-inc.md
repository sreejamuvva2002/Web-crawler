## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels Inc released its 8-K filing on February 12, 2024, announcing financial results for the fourth quarter and full fiscal year 2023.
- The company is a leader in aerogel technology for sustainability and electrification solutions.
- Aspen Aerogels Inc offers high-performance aerogel insulation used across various industries, including energy infrastructure and electric vehicles.
- The company operates primarily in the U.S. and serves international markets.
- Fiscal year 2023 revenue was $238.7 million, compared to $180.4 million in fiscal year 2022.
- The thermal barriers segment saw a 110% year-over-year increase in revenue during 2023.
- Fiscal year 2023 net loss was $45.8 million, down from $82.7 million in fiscal year 2022.
- Fiscal year 2023 gross margin was 24%, up from 3% in fiscal year 2022.
- Fiscal year 2023 Adjusted EBITDA was ($22.9 million), compared to ($60.6 million) in fiscal year 2022.
- Net loss per share improved from $2.10 in 2022 to $0.66 in 2023.
- Capital expenditures were significantly reduced, and the company ended the year with a healthy cash balance.
- Fourth quarter 2023 revenue was $84.2 million, compared to $59.6 million in the same period of 2022.
- Fourth quarter 2023 net loss was $0.5 million, down from $9.6 million in Q4 2022.
- Fourth quarter 2023 gross margin was 35%, up from 23% in Q4 2022.
- Fourth quarter 2023 Adjusted EBITDA was $9.1 million, compared to ($4.4 million) in Q4 2022.
- The company's long-term strategy focuses on scaling to profitability and capitalizing on growing demand for its products in the electrification and sustainability sectors.
- Aspen Aerogels Inc anticipates fiscal year 2024 revenues to exceed $350 million.
- The company projects fiscal year 2024 Adjusted EBITDA to be at least $30 million.
- Fiscal year 2024 net loss is projected to be under $23 million, with net loss per share under $0.30.
- Capital expenditures for 2024 are expected to be around $50 million, excluding investments in Plant II.
- Don Young, Aspen’s President and CEO, stated that diversifying the EV Thermal Barrier customer base will drive growth beyond 2024 and validate recent investments in this segment.
- Ricardo C. Rodriguez, Chief Financial Officer and Treasurer, emphasized the company's focus on sustained profitability and efficiently managing fixed costs and capital investments.
