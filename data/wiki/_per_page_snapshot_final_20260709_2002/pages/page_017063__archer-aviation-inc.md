# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-14

**Source:** [Funding Secured for Archer’s Manufacturing Facility in Georgia · Helis](https://www.helis.com/database/news/factory-covington-georgia-archer/)  ·  `page_017063`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. closed agreements with Synovus Bank and Evans General Contractors, LLC for the financing and development of its high-volume manufacturing facility in Covington, Georgia.
- Construction is already underway on the first phase of the Covington facility.
- The first phase build-out covers approximately 350,000 square feet.
- The first phase will support production of up to 650 aircraft per year.
- This capacity would make it the world’s largest eVTOL aircraft manufacturing facility by volume.
- Archer expects to produce the highest volume of eVTOL aircraft while bearing a dramatically lower manufacturing capital cost than competitor projects.
- The announcement secures at least $65 million in financing.
- This financing represents a substantial majority of the cost of construction for the first phase of Archer’s eVTOL manufacturing facility.
- The project is in conjunction with previously announced incentives from the state of Georgia.
- The project leverages Archer’s partnership with Stellantis.
- These elements advance Archer’s strategy of creating a capital-efficient path to scaled manufacturing.
- Archer partners with top-tier aerospace suppliers like Honeywell and Safran for a majority of its Midnight aircraft's components.
- Partnering with these suppliers streamlines the manufacturing and assembly process.
- Phase one of the facility is on target to be completed in 2024.
- The Midnight aircraft is planned to enter service in 2025.
- Phase two will expand the facility to approximately 900,000 square feet.
- Phase two will support long-term production targets of over 2,000 aircraft per year.
- Adam Goldstein serves as Archer’s Founder and CEO.
- Goldstein stated that manufacturing eVTOL aircraft at scale is critical to building a successful business in the industry.
- Archer secured a design-build agreement and financing agreement for the facility.
- Archer achieved attractive terms on the project consistent with its capital-efficient commercialization strategy.
- The agreements and partnership with Stellantis provide flexibility to ramp up capacity and become the leading scale manufacturer of eVTOL aircraft globally.
- Archer considered a range of companies with expertise in high-volume manufacturing facilities in the southeast U.S. when selecting partners for the Covington project.
- Goldstein stated Archer is committed as a long-term partner to the Covington, Georgia community.
- Archer aims to replace 60-90 minute car commutes with estimated 10-20 minute electric aircraft flights.
- The planned flights will be safe, sustainable, low noise, and cost-competitive with ground transportation.
- Archer’s Midnight is a piloted, four-passenger aircraft.
- The Midnight is designed to perform rapid back-to-back flights with minimal charge time between flights.
- The Midnight aircraft will be manufactured at the Covington, GA facility.
- The aircraft will be deployed to various customers and markets in the U.S. and abroad.
- Archer (NYSE: ACHR) designs and develops eVTOL aircraft for urban air mobility networks.
- Archer's mission is to unlock the skies and free people to reimagine movement and time usage.
- Archer's team is based in Santa Clara, CA.
