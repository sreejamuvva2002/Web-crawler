## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose (H&V) is a global manufacturer of advanced materials used in filtration, battery and industrial applications.
- H&V will invest $40.2 million to expand its operation in Floyd County, Va.
- The company will add over 28,000 square feet to its facility at 365 Christiansburg Pike Northeast to accommodate new production equipment to meet increased demand.
- Virginia successfully competed with Georgia for the project, which will create 25 new jobs.
- Gov. Glenn Youngkin announced the investment and stated H&V has generated positive economic impact and job opportunities in Floyd County for more than four decades.
- Autoneum signed an agreement to acquire the automotive business of Borgers Group for an enterprise value of €117 million, expected to close in April 2023 following antitrust clearance.
- Borgers specializes in textile acoustics protection, insulation and trim for automobiles, including wheel arch liners and trunk liner product lines, and is the market leader in Europe in textile wheel arch liners.
- Borgers generated €610 million in revenue in fiscal year 2021 with around 4,700 employees.
- Autoneum will finance the transaction through a new credit facility and a planned CHF 100 million capital increase.
- Mann+Hummel Group announced a strategic investment in M-Filter Group, one of the largest filter manufacturing companies in Northern and Eastern Europe.
- M-Filter produces HVAC and air filtration including HEPA filters and oil/hydraulic filters for engines, with a strong footprint in OEM products for industrial and engine filtration.
- Sonobond Ultrasonics president Janet Devine noted the company's involvement in the growing market for EV vehicles through ultrasonic welding equipment for non-ferrous metals.
