# Hyundai Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-26

**Source:** [From announcement to development of the Hyundai Metaplant](https://www.savannahnow.com/picture-gallery/news/2025/03/26/from-announcement-to-development-of-the-hyundai-metaplant/82650238007/)  ·  `page_007760`

## Facts

- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp officially announced plans for Hyundai Motor Group to build a manufacturing plant in Ellabell, Georgia at the Bryan County mega-site on Friday, May 20, 2022.
- On May 20, 2022, Governor Brian Kemp and Jaehoon "Jay" Chang, Hyundai Motor Company President and CEO, signed an agreement and celebrated with a champagne toast to finalize the deal for the plant.
- The Bryan County mega-site is located just off Interstate 16 near Ellabell, Georgia.
- By October 2022, approximately 1,700 acres of the site were cleared for development of the Hyundai Metaplant.
- On Tuesday, October 25, 2022, Euisun Chung (executive chairman of Hyundai Motor Group), Georgia Governor Brian Kemp, and Jose Munoz (president and CEO of Hyundai North America) held an official groundbreaking ceremony at the Bryan County mega-site near Ellabell.
- During the October 25, 2022 groundbreaking, a community celebration was held at the enmarket Arena where Hyundai electric vehicles including the IONIQ 5 and the 2021 IONIQ Concept Seven were on display.
- A map displayed during the project timeline shows the planned location of Hyundai facilities as well as other support companies being planned for the mega-site.
- An 180-acre pond is being dug on the Bryan County mega-site to support development.
- Onsite cement plants are operating at the Bryan County mega-site to support construction.
- Construction has begun on the future paint shop at the Hyundai Metaplant.
- A bridge between the Paint Shop and Final Assembly will carry vehicles on a conveyor from building to building at the Hyundai Metaplant America in Ellabell, with the vehicles visible from Interstate 16 as they move across the bridge.
- An onsite water treatment facility is under construction at the Hyundai Metaplant America in Ellabell, Georgia.
- Governor Brian Kemp signed HB 408 at the future site of the Hyundai Meta Plant in Ellabell, Georgia on Friday, May 5, 2023.
- Construction progress was documented on Wednesday, May 3, 2023, showing steel rising and buildings taking shape for the future Hyundai Metaplant.
- Work is underway on the final assembly facility at the Hyundai Metaplant America in Ellabell, Georgia as of Wednesday, October 25, 2023.
- The Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America is currently under construction and nearing completion in Ellabell, Georgia.
- A future battery facility is planned at the Bryan County mega-site near the Hyundai Metaplant.
- On Thursday, September 19, 2024, a "topping off" ceremony was held for Georgia Quick Start’s Hyundai Mobility Training Center near the Hyundai Metaplant in Ellabell.
- Oscar Kwon, president and CEO of Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America, and Technical College System of Georgia Commissioner Greg Dozier attended the September 19, 2024 training center topping off ceremony.
- The Hyundai IONIQ 9 offers 3 rows of seating with the ability to fold down the back 2 rows.
- Governor Brian Kemp was greeted by Senator Lester Jackson upon arrival at the Bryan County mega-site.
- Senator Ben Watson reviewed a map of the mega-site area with Eric Johnson, Scott Allison, an assistant city manager from Richmond Hill, and Carter Infinger, chairman of the board of commissioners for Bryan County.
- Traffic signs on-site direct visitors to both the Hyundai Metaplant America and the future site of the battery plant at the Bryan County Mega Site.
- A new tower is visible over Highway 280 at the Hyundai site in Ellabell.
- A water tower is currently being painted on the site of the Hyundai Metaplant in Ellabell.
