# Mack Trucks

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-07

**Source:** [Mack Trucks Reaches Major Milestone with 200,000 Connected Vehicles](https://www.volvogroup.com/en/news-and-media/news/2024/nov/mack-trucks-reaches-major-milestone-with-200-000-connected-vehicles.html)  ·  `page_045803`

## Facts

- Mack Trucks announced it has achieved a milestone of more than 200,000 Class 8 trucks built with its proprietary telematics gateway since 2014.
- Connected Mack trucks are supported around the clock by Mack OneCall agents at the company's 24/7 Uptime Center.
- Jonathan Randall serves as president of Mack Trucks North America.
- Connected vehicles include diagnostics through Mack GuardDog Connect, remote software updates via Mack Over The Air, and fleet monitoring services through the Mack Connect portal.
- The proprietary telematics gateway has been standard on all Mack trucks with Mack engines since late 2014.
- Mack GuardDog Connect monitors critical fault codes, immediately notifies customers of issues requiring attention, categorizes alerts by severity, and provides detailed repair information for informed service decisions.
- Mack Over The Air (OTA) provides remote software update capability that has demonstrated a reduction of unplanned downtime by more than 24%.
- The Mack Connect portal provides real-time visibility into vehicle location, fuel economy, and safety metrics to enable fleet managers to optimize operations.
- The connected vehicle portal supports proactive maintenance services through Mack's service contracts using real-time vehicle data to anticipate maintenance needs based on actual operating conditions.
- Patrick Brown serves as director of strategy at Mack Trucks.
- Mack Trucks has provided purpose-built transportation solutions for more than a century and is one of North America’s largest producers of heavy-duty trucks.
- Mack trucks are sold and serviced through an extensive distribution network in more than 45 countries.
- Mack trucks, diesel engines, and transmissions sold in North America are assembled in the United States.
- Mack manufacturing locations are certified to ISO 9001 for quality, ISO 14001 for environmental management systems, and ISO 45001 for health and safety management systems.
- Mack is a sponsor of Share the Road, an American Trucking Associations public information campaign aimed at enhancing roadway safety.
- Mack Trucks is part of the Volvo Group, which employs about 104,000 people and serves customers in almost 190 markets.
- In 2023, the Volvo Group's net sales amounted to about $52 billion.
