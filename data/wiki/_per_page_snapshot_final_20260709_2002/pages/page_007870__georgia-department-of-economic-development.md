# Georgia Department of Economic Development

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2023-01

**Source:** [GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson Named ‘Georgian of the Year’ by Georgia Trend - Valdosta CEO](https://valdostaceo.com/news/2023/01/gdecd-commissioner-pat-wilson-named-georgian-year-georgia-trend/)  ·  `page_007870`

## Facts

- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson was named the 2023 “Georgian of the Year” by Georgia Trend.
- The award recognizes Commissioner Wilson as the top salesperson for the state and commends his work promoting the advantages of Georgia to attract investments and create jobs.
- Wilson was appointed as economic development Commissioner by former Governor Nathan Deal six years prior to the article's publication.
- Under Wilson's tenure, Georgia maintained its place as the No. 1 state in the nation to do business for the ninth consecutive year in 2022.
- Former Georgia Governor Sonny Perdue credited Wilson with much of the credit for Georgia being one of the best places to grow a business by communicating the achievements of the governor and legislature.
- In August 2022, GDEcD announced that investments in expansions and new locations during FY22 totaled more than $21.2 billion.
- The FY22 investments were projected to create 51,132 direct new jobs in the state, setting new records for Georgia.
- GDEcD's provision of solutions for companies and open communication helped secure two of its largest-ever investments in FY22.
- Wilson expressed that he gets emotional hearing how job creation has changed people's lives and is grateful to the governors who appointed him.
- GDEcD teammates Lee Thomas (Director of the Georgia Film Office) and David Nuckolls (Executive Director of the Center of Innovation) were also recognized by Georgia Trend for their state contributions.
- GDEcD describes itself as the “quarterback” that brings partners across the state together to facilitate business development.
