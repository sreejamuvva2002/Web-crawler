# Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2018-07-11

**Source:** [Introducing New Cushman 72-Volt AC Electric Shuttles - The Golf Wire](https://thegolfwire.com/introducing-new-cushman-72-volt-ac-electric-shuttles/)  ·  `page_051709`

## Facts

- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT) company, announces its new line of Cushman Shuttle personnel carriers powered by the company’s exclusive 72-volt AC electric drivetrain.
- The leader in AC electric drivetrains in the golf-car industry under its E-Z-GO brand, Textron Specialized Vehicles has incorporated this innovative technology, with its improved energy efficiency and increased range, into its Cushman Shuttle 4, Shuttle 6 and Shuttle 8 models.
- Kevin Holleran, president and CEO of Textron Specialized Vehicles, stated that the newest Cushman Shuttles are the latest example of the company's commitment to developing vehicles that support the evolving needs and demands of its customers.
- The company leverages the proven energy savings, power and range benefits of its 72-volt AC technology to move customers’ employees and guests more efficiently and cost-effectively.
- AC drivetrain technology is superior to DC electric power due to its alternating flow of charge, allowing it to deliver more power and performance while using less energy.
- The 72-volt AC drivetrain on the newest Shuttle models generates longer battery life, enabling vehicles to travel further between charges, and delivers remarkable hill-climbing capabilities.
- Shuttle vehicles are commonly found hard at work in a range of applications, including golf courses, resorts, airports, educational facilities, and college and commercial campuses.
- Cushman Shuttles feature standard headlights, brush guard, hour meter and USB port, and can be equipped with a range of options and accessories, including a limited-slip differential, windshields, canopies, and more.
- The Cushman Shuttle 4 features seating for up to four people, with a stake-side cargo bed to carry equipment, luggage or personal belongings.
- The Shuttle 6 features seating for up to six people.
- The Shuttle 8 can carry up to eight.
- Cushman vehicles are designed and manufactured in Augusta, Ga. by Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.
- Since 1901, Cushman vehicles have been recognized light-transportation solutions for turf, commercial and industrial applications.
- Today, Cushman products include more than 15 different vehicle models.
- The Hauler utility vehicles are ideal for tough hauling and towing tasks in outdoor environments.
- The Titan is a fully electric material-handling vehicle for indoor environments with a load capacity of 3,000 pounds.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. is a leading global manufacturer of golf cars, utility and personal transportation vehicles, snowmobiles, side-by-sides, all-terrain vehicles, professional turf-care equipment, and ground support equipment.
- The company markets products under the E-Z-GO, Cushman, Arctic Cat, Textron Off Road, Jacobsen, Dixie Chopper, Ransomes, TUG, Douglas, Premier and Safeaero brands.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company that leverages its global network of aircraft, defense, industrial and finance businesses to provide customers with innovative solutions and services.
