# TI Fluid Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-15

**Source:** [Press Releases Archives - TIFS](https://tifluidsystems.com/category/press-releases/)  ·  `page_011522`

## Facts

- Terry Campbell will become President & Chief Executive Officer of the combined group following ABC’s acquisition of TI Fluid Systems.
- Alexander De Bock will become Chief Financial Officer of the combined group.
- Current TIFS President & CEO Hans Dieltjens will step down from his role following completion of the acquisition.
- ABC Technologies’ acquisition of TI Fluid Systems remains on track to complete during the first half of 2025.
- The acquisition was approved by TIFS shareholders on February 5, 2025.
- Completion is expected in the first half of the year following receipt of remaining regulatory and judicial clearances.
- Terry Campbell brings over thirty years of experience in the global automotive sector, with prior senior leadership roles at Johnson Controls, Magna International, and The Woodbridge Group.
- Alexander De Bock has spent over 15 years in the industry, including finance leadership roles at WABCO and ZF Group.
- Terry Campbell stated that as a larger, diversified business with an expanded geographic footprint, the combined group will be extremely well positioned to navigate the transition to electrification.
- The acquisition will be implemented by way of a court-sanctioned scheme of arrangement under Part 26 of the Companies Act 2006.
- TI Fluid Systems is a global innovator of thermal and fluid systems solutions for current and developing vehicle architectures, operating across 26 countries with over 100 years of automotive supply experience.
- ABC Technologies manufactures custom technical plastics and lightweight innovations for the North American light vehicle industry, serving more than 25 major original equipment manufacturer customers in 8 countries with a workforce of over 11,000 team members.
- ABC Technologies is majority owned by affiliated funds of Apollo Global Management, Inc., with Oaktree Capital Management, L.P. owning a minority equity interest.
- TI Fluid Systems will announce the anticipated completion timetable through a Regulatory Information Service and on its corporate website.
