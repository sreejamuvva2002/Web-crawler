# Vernay Laboratories

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-01-15

**Source:** [Vernay to Host Grand Opening of Kennesaw Facility - Vernay](https://vernay.com/news-and-events/vernay-to-host-grand-opening-of-kennesaw-facility/)  ·  `page_013338`

## Facts

- Vernay Laboratories will host a grand opening ceremony for its new Kennesaw facility on January 16, 2025.
- The grand opening event will include a ribbon-cutting ceremony, facility tours, and presentations by Vernay’s leadership team.
- Local officials, industry partners, and Vernay employees have been invited to attend the grand opening celebration.
- The Kennesaw facility specializes in liquid silicone rubber (LSR) molding and micro-molding for medical and industrial markets.
- The facility is currently equipped with 10 advanced production presses.
- The facility currently utilizes only a quarter of its total capacity.
- Vernay plans to expand the Kennesaw facility's press count to 45.
- Each state-of-the-art production cell at the facility can produce 64 parts every 32 seconds.
- The facility enables an annual production capacity of over 200 million micro-molded parts.
- Andy Woodward, Vice President of Sales & Marketing, stated that the opening demonstrates Vernay’s dedication to advancing manufacturing technologies and meeting customer needs.
- Woodward noted that the investment reinforces a focus on precision, efficiency, and delivering high-quality, custom-engineered solutions for highly regulated industries.
- The facility allows Vernay to deliver fully functional prototypes quickly, moving ideas from concept to production faster than ever before.
- Vernay Laboratories was founded in 1946 and is a technology leader in the design, development, and manufacture of customized flow-control components.
- The company formulates its own compounds to address each specific application.
- Vernay’s products play a vital role in millions of medical devices, automobiles, and consumer products.
- Vernay is a privately held firm that produces more than 1 billion components per year globally.
- The company draws from a library of more than 33,000 elastomeric materials and compound formulations.
- Vernay operates six plants worldwide located in the U.S., the Netherlands, Italy, and China.
