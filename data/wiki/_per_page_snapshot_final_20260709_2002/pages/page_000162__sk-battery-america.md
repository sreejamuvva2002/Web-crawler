- The NCM9 battery is developed by SK On and produced by SK Battery America.
- The NCM9 battery derives its name from key components nickel, manganese, and cobalt (NCM), with the '9' signifying a nickel composition nearly reaching 90%.
- The elevated nickel content of the NCM9 battery translates to superior energy density and an extended driving range.
- The NCM9 battery is a preferred choice for enhanced performance.
