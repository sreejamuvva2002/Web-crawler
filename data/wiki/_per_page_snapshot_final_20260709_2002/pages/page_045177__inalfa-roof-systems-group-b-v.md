# Inalfa Roof Systems Group B.V.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Inalfa Roof Systems Group B.V. - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/inalfa-roof-systems-group)  ·  `page_045177`

## Facts

- The Company develops and produces vehicle roof solutions for the automotive and commercial vehicle industry.
- The Company has a global market share in automotive roof systems of approximately 25%.
- The Company manufactures approximately six million automotive roof systems per year.
- The Company is a wholly owned subsidiary of Beijing Hainachuan Automotive Parts Co., Ltd.
- The Company produces inslider roof systems where a front panel slides under the rear glass panel or roof skin.
- The Company produces single-panel insliders.
- The Company produces two-panel insliders.
- The Company produces topslider two-panel roof systems where a front panel slides over the rear panel.
- The Company produces bottom-loaded topsliders.
- The Company produces top-loaded topsliders.
- The Company produces exterior slider roof systems where a front panel slides over rear panel or roof skin without the use of a rear support lever.
- The Company produces fixed panel roof systems with stationary panels.
- The Company produces sunblinds including rollo fabric that can be rolled up, solid panel sunshades that can slide in front of roof systems, and stacking sunshades for easier storage.
- The Company produces truck hatches with only ventilation functionality, hatches with ventilation and slide functionality, and hatches with ventilation and escape functionality.
- The Company has in-house glass encapsulation capabilities for polymer trim finish to the edge of a panel.
- The Company offers roof functions including glass technologies for reduced weight.
- The Company offers improved acoustics and NVH capabilities.
- The Company integrates V2X communication systems.
- The Company integrates in-textile lighting.
- The Company integrates solar cells.
- The Company offers switchable glass.
- The Company offers hybrid glass solutions.
- The Company offers ambient lighting systems.
- The Company offers integrated gesture control systems.
- The Company offers intuitive machine interfaces.
- The Company offers integrated data management systems.
- The Company offers integrated augmented or virtual reality systems.
- The Company offers integrated car-sharing services.
- The Company offers panoramic displays.
- The Company produces tailgate actuators.
- The Company produces active rear spoilers.
- The Company produces electrically controlled steps.
- The Company was founded in 1946 by J.A.M Burgman and started in the Netherlands.
- The Company started production of sunroofs in 1974.
- The Company made a decision to form separate business units for Roof systems and Metal in 1995.
- The Company established roof systems and metal business as two separate companies in 1997.
- The Company opened its factory in Yantai, China in 2006.
- The Company established a plant in Slovakia in 2010 as well as in Mexico in 2011.
- The Company was sold to the Chinese company Beijing Hainachuan Automotive Parts Co., Ltd. (BHAP).
- The Company established a plant in Chongqing and Lanfang in China in April 2013.
- The Company opened its new plant in Wrzesnia, Poland in July 2014.
- The Company opened its new assembly facility in Cherokee County, Georgia, USA in November 2014.
- The Company opened its new plant in Jiading, Shanghai, China in July 2015.
- The Company officially opened its new headquarters in Venray, the Netherlands in April 2016.
