# Racemark International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-10

**Source:** [Floor Mat Maker Racemark International Receives Toyota | GGBAILEY](https://www.ggbailey.com/floor-mat-maker-racemark-international-receives-toyota-gold-award/)  ·  `page_010335`

## Facts

- Racemark International recently received the Toyota Quality Alliance 2013 Gold Supplier Award from Toyota Motor Sales, U.S.A., Inc.
- The Gold Supplier Award is presented to top parts and accessories suppliers who have consistently demonstrated their commitment to product development, product quality, operational quality and product support.
- This is the second time Racemark has received the Gold Award from Toyota.
- Racemark supplies automotive floor mats to many top automotive brands including Bentley, BMW, General Motors, Honda, Hyundai, Kia, Mercedes-Benz, Maserati, Mitsubishi, Toyota, and Volkswagen.
- Racemark created the first carpet floor mat in the industry in 1974 and is the leader in floor mat innovation.
- Racemark has production plants in New York, Georgia, Switzerland and Hungary.
- Ginger Cannon Bailey, CEO of Racemark International and GG Bailey.com, stated it is a great honor to be recognized by Toyota, noting Toyota sets the highest standards in the industry and focuses on the customer.
- Bailey recalled a marketing session where Toyota's team suggested adding pink to their product lineup to reach female customers, which aligned with Racemark's existing pink products.
- Bailey praised Toyota for thinking about all customers, including women, and having an amazing women on its management team in a very male-dominated industry.
- Through the website’s Design Your Car Mats section, customers can design solid color floor mats with various carpets, edgings, and heel pad designs, personalize them with names or monograms, and choose from more than 500,000 possible design combinations.
- Floor mats are manufactured in two business days and can be made to fit any car make and model.
- GG Bailey recognizes Breast Cancer Awareness Month every October by donating 10% of every order that includes pink carpet, pink serging, a pink heel pad, pink embroidery, or a Pink Ribbon logo to the Breast Cancer Research Foundation.
- The company offers Car Couture floor mats in woven carpet leopard, zebra, tiger, oriental, plaid, and camouflage designs.
