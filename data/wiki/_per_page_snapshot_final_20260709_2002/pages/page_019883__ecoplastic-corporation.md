## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Ecoplastic Corporation will create 456 new jobs.
- Ecoplastic Corporation will invest approximately $205 million in a new manufacturing facility in Bulloch County.
- Ecoplastic Corporation is an automotive parts manufacturer based in Korea.
- The company was established in 1984 and manufactures plastic automobile parts for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and other suppliers.
- Ecoplastic is a Tier-1 supplier for Hyundai Motor Group (HMG) and Kia.
- It manufactures automotive interior and exterior plastic parts, including bumpers, consoles, trims, and plastic molds.
- Han Shang serves as the President and CEO of Ecoplastic Corporation.
- The new facility will be located at 4822 Highway 301 South Register near Statesboro.
- The plant will be capable of injection molding and painting.
- It will produce exterior parts, bumpers and garnishes, interior parts, consoles, and trims for electric vehicles.
- The company expects to begin operations by October 2024.
- Hiring positions include HR, finance, parts development, quality control, facility maintenance, injection, and paint production.
- Billy Allen is the Development Authority of Bulloch County Chair.
- Alyce Thornhill is the Director of Project Implementation and Supplier Strategy at the Georgia Department of Economic Development's (GDEcD) Global Commerce team.
- The project was supported in partnership with the Development Authority of Bulloch County, Georgia Ports Authority, Georgia Quick Start, and Georgia EMC.
- Pat Wilson is the GDEcD Commissioner.
- The facility will serve the Hyundai Metaplant from Statesboro, leveraging close proximity to Georgia's ports system.
- Since 2020, more than 30 EV-related projects have announced more than $21 billion in investment and over 26,300 jobs in Georgia.
- On October 25, Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America officially broke ground in neighboring Bryan County on its first fully dedicated EV and battery manufacturing facility.
- Offsite suppliers connected to the Hyundai project were predicted to invest approximately $1 billion in other locations around the state.
- Ecoplastic Corporation was originally established in June 1984 as Apollo Ind Co., Ltd.
- The company established its first Technical Research Center in 1987.
- Ecoplastic was publicly listed on the Korean Stock Exchange in March 2000.
- It currently employs more than 770 employees.
- The company recorded sales revenue of more than $700 million in 2021.
