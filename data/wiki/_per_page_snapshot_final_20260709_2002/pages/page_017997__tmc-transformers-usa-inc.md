# TMC Transformers USA Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-28

**Source:** [TMC Transformers Invests in New Georgia Manufacturing Facility | Manufacturing.net](https://www.manufacturing.net/operations/news/22924711/tmc-transformers-invests-in-new-georgia-manufacturing-facility)  ·  `page_017997`

## Facts

- TMC Transformers USA Inc. announced it would invest more than $15.3 million over the next five years in a new manufacturing facility in Waynesboro, Georgia.
- The investment will create at least 110 jobs.
- Operations have already started at the company’s first facility in Burke County.
- The new plant is expected to be operational at the beginning of 2026.
- TMC is now hiring for roles in management, administrative staff, production technicians, operators, testers, sales and quality control.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that after meeting with TMC's leadership in Italy, they were truly impressed by the company's warmth, hospitality and enthusiasm for their decision to invest in Georgia.
- Pat Wilson noted that the transformers manufactured in Burke County are critical in addressing energy infrastructure needs for the state and the nation.
- This expansion follows recent manufacturing expansions in Georgia, including Shinsung Petrochemical's investment of an additional $11.2 million in a new facility in Bryan County, creating 30 new jobs.
- GF Casting Solutions AG announced a more than $184 million investment in Augusta to produce lightweight components for the mobility and energy industries, creating 350 new jobs in Richmond County.
- StandardAero began work on a $33 million expansion of its business aircraft maintenance operations in Augusta, which will create 90 new jobs in Richmond County.
