**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-10-23

## Facts

- SEOHAN INDUSTRIES CO.,LTD. is part of the Korea Flange(KOFCO) group.
- The company's CEO is Lee Hyeon Duk.
- SEOHAN INDUSTRIES CO.,LTD. operates as a middle-sized export/import company engaged in the manufacturing of new automotive parts, specifically fittings and flanges.
- The company was established on 3rd April 1996.
- As of 2019, the company employed 212 people.
- In 2018, the company reported a sales turnover of KRW 429,815,930,000.
- In 2018, the company reported a business profit of KRW 4,252,750,000.
- The company's capital stands at KRW 10,600,000,000 (as of 2018).
- The company is headquartered at Insan-li, Ducksan-myun, Jincheon goon, Choongchongbook-do, Korea.
- On 2016. 03. 30, the company completed its Mexico factory (SeohanAuto Mexico).
- On 2013. 06. 11, the construction of the company's third building was completed.
- On 2012. 11. 30, the company received the National Trade Day “Two Hundred Million Dollars Exporter” Award.
