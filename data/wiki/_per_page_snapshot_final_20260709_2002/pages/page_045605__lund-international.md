# Lund International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2007-12-05

**Source:** [Lund International Acquired by Two Cleveland, Ohio, Private Equity Firms - AftermarketNews](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/lund-international-acquired-by-two-cleveland-ohio-private-equity-firms/)  ·  `page_045605`

## Facts

- Lund International is a Georgia-based manufacturer of automotive accessories.
- Lund International has entered into an agreement to be purchased by Linsalata Capital Partners and Resilience Capital Partners, both based in Cleveland, Ohio.
- Terms of the acquisition agreement were not provided.
- Lund manufactures automotive accessories under the AVS, Lund, Nifty, Deflecta-Shield Aluminum, and Belmor brands.
- The company has annual sales in excess of $130 million.
- Lund International is headquartered in Suwanne, GA.
- Lund operates plants in Lawrenceville, GA; Hamilton, Ohio; Howe, IN; and Chicago.
- Linsalata Capital Partners and Resilience Capital Partners will bring additional management resources to Lund International.
- The acquiring firms will provide industry veterans to serve on Lund's board of directors.
- George Scherff, CEO of Lund, stated, “We are pleased with the transition. Both firms bring a strong history of supporting growth of quality companies.”
