# Superior Essex Inc

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-08-19

**Source:** [Superior Essex Announces Essex Solutions, Relaunching Magnet Wire Brand - EIN Presswire](https://www.einnews.com/pr_news/736539024/superior-essex-announces-essex-solutions-relaunching-magnet-wire-brand)  ·  `page_010567`

## Facts

- Superior Essex is rebranding its magnet wire business to Essex Solutions as part of the next phase in its rebranding efforts.
- The brand name change follows the company's recent buyout of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire.
- Under the new name, Essex Solutions will encompass all current Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire locations worldwide.
- Essex Energy in Italy will now be included in the Essex Solutions organization, further reinforcing the company’s global reach and capabilities.
- Related businesses Lacroix + Kress, IVA Insulations, and Hi-Wire will join the Essex Solutions organization while continuing to operate under their existing brand identities.
- Daniel Choi serves as President and CEO of Superior Essex.
- During the transition period, customers and stakeholders may see materials bearing either the existing Essex Furukawa or the new Essex Solutions name.
- All name changes are expected to be completed by April 30, 2025.
- Essex Solutions will unveil a new logo in the near future to reflect its evolution and commitment to innovation and excellence.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Solutions, Superior Essex Communications, IVA, Lacroix + Kress, Hi-Wire, and Essex Brownell.
- Superior Essex has over 3,000 employees across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is a leading global provider of magnet wire and is at the forefront of communications fiber application as well as Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company is instrumental in creating disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex proactively shapes the xEV automotive industry, as well as power, electrification, and renewable energy markets to pave the way for future technologies.
