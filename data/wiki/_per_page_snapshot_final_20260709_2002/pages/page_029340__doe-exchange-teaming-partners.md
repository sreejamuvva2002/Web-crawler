# DOE eXCHANGE Teaming Partners

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [DOE: CMEI eXCHANGE: Teaming Partners](https://eere-exchange.energy.gov/TeamingPartners.aspx?foaid=defdc203-6b11-45db-a6cc-39a5bb592ad9)  ·  `page_029340`

## Facts

- The page lists teaming partners available for the DOE Onsite Energy Technical Assistance Partnerships (TAPs) initiative.
- University of Houston researchers conduct R&D on optimal siting and selection of EV charging stations, battery modeling and degradation quantification, and the impact of EVs on the power grid.
- NU Engineering LLC collaborates with manufacturers of residential, commercial, and industrial battery storage systems and EV charging stations.
- NU Engineering LLC is developing a commercial EV charging station supported by solar, wind, battery storage, and hydrogen generation to function as a Virtual Power Plant (VPP).
- Robert Bosch LLC provides technology solutions for proton exchange membrane (PEM) fuel cell mobility applications.
- SRI International offers capabilities for granular analysis of workforce skills and discovery of skills gaps and workforce pipeline opportunities in the energy sector.
- The Association of Energy Engineers provides Certified Energy Manager (CEM) and Certified Industrial Efficiency Professional (CIEP) certification programs to support industrial energy professionals and On-Site TAPs.
- GreenTech Energy LLC specializes in delivering energy projects across industries including renewable diesel, CHP, gas, coal, carbon capture, nuclear, and biomass power plants to support organizations working towards carbon neutrality.
- Vertiv Corporation seeks academic and industry partners to build an ecosystem supplying campus-centric data center communities with renewable energy infrastructure for backup storage, transportation, and industrial processes.
- Launch Alaska acts as a deployment accelerator connecting companies with technologies that decarbonize and electrify critical systems across energy, transportation, and industry.
- Kohler Co. provides turnkey hydrogen fuel-cell-based power systems capable of emergency and prime power for various applications.
- Advanced Environmental Engineering (AE^2) provides services in renewable energy source evaluation, circular economies, environmentally sustainable operations, water resources, corporate development, technology investigations, air permitting, construction management, and environmental assessments.
- Kanin Energy, Inc. develops industrial waste heat to power projects, offering expertise in technical design, costing, interconnection, sale of power, financing, thermal waste resource identification, and economic modeling for decarbonizing heavy industry.
- DE Solutions, Inc. offers consulting and engineering services for distributed energy markets, with over 25 years of experience in energy/environmental technology development, feasibility studies, and market development.
- Green System Consulting LLC provides commercial and industrial energy audits, electrification and decarbonization studies, process improvement studies, metering installation/studies, smart metering, energy efficient controls, commissioning, peer review, energy modeling, inspection services, utility program support, and project management.
