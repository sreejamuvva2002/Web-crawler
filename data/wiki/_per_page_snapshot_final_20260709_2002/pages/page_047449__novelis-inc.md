# Novelis Inc

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Novelis North America is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- Novelis North America operates eight aluminum rolled products facilities, including two fully dedicated recycling facilities and one facility with recycling operations.
- Novelis manufactures a broad range of aluminum sheet and light gauge products for end-use markets that include beverage and food cans, containers and packaging, automotive and other transportation applications, architectural and other industrial applications.
- The majority of Novelis North America’s volumes are currently directed toward the beverage can sheet market.
- Three facilities in North America re-melt post-consumer aluminum and recycled process material as part of the manufacturing process.
- Most recycled material comes from used beverage cans (UBCs) and automotive scrap, and is cast into sheet ingot at plants in Greensboro, Georgia; Berea, Kentucky; and Oswego, New York.
- The Logan Aluminum joint venture facility in Russellville, Kentucky manufactures aluminum sheet products for the can stock market and operates modern and high-speed equipment for ingot casting, hot-rolling, cold-rolling, and finishing.
- Novelis expanded its Oswego, New York facility by constructing three automotive finishing lines and supporting automotive scrap recycling capabilities in response to the lightweighting trend in the automotive industry.
- Novelis Europe operates ten aluminum rolled product facilities, including two fully dedicated recycling facilities and two facilities with recycling operations.
- Novelis Europe manufactures sheet and foil products for end-use markets including beverage and food can, automotive, architectural and industrial products, foil and technical products, and lithographic sheet.
- Beverage and food can represent the largest end-use market in terms of shipment volume for Novelis Europe.
- Novelis holds a 50% joint venture interest in Aluminium Norf GmbH (Alunorf), which is the world’s largest aluminum rolling and remelt facility.
- Alunorf supplies high quality can stock, foilstock and feeder stock for finishing at Novelis's other European operations.
- Novelis built a fully integrated recycling facility at its Nachterstedt, Germany plant that commissioned in fiscal 2015 and is the largest aluminum recycling facility in the world.
- A second automotive finishing line at the Nachterstedt, Germany facility was commissioned in fiscal 2016 to expand production of aluminum automotive sheet products in Europe.
- Novelis Asia operates five aluminum rolled product facilities, including three facilities with recycling operations.
- End-use markets for Novelis Asia include beverage and food cans, electronics, architectural, automotive, foil, industrial and other products.
- Beverage can represents the largest end-use market in terms of volume for Novelis Asia.
- Novelis has recycling facilities at its Ulsan and Yeongju, South Korea plants.
- Novelis operates a facility in Binh Duong, Vietnam that handles the collection and processing of used beverage cans (UBCs).
- Novelis expanded its aluminum rolling operations beginning in fiscal 2014 and its recycling operations in fiscal 2013 in South Korea to bring high-quality aluminum rolling capacity to market.
- The South Korea expansion includes a state-of-the-art recycling center primarily for UBCs and a casting operation.
- Novelis South America operates two aluminum rolled product facilities, including one facility with recycling operations.
- Main markets for Novelis South America include beverage and food can, specialty, industrial, foil and other packaging and transportation end-use applications.
