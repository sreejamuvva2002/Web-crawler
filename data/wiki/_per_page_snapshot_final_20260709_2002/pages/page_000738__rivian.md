## Facts

- Rivian held a kickoff ceremony for its upcoming Georgia manufacturing plant near Social Circle, Georgia.
- The facility is projected to generate approximately 7,500 direct jobs by 2030.
- Thousands more indirect roles across the supply chain will be created in surrounding counties including Newton County.
- Construction at the site is expected to begin in 2026.
- Production at the plant is slated for 2028.
- The event underscores regional momentum in advanced manufacturing, workforce development, and long-term economic vitality for communities in east-central Georgia.
- The plant offers access to a growing pipeline of skilled manufacturing and engineering roles for Newton County residents and regional employers.
