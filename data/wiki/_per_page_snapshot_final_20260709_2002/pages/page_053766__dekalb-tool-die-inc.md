## Facts

- Dekalb Tool & Die is a leading provider of metal stamping services with over 50 years of experience delivering a wide range of solutions to more than 10 different industries.
- The company is an ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturer that provides knowledgeable engineering support and in-house die maintenance.
- The facility is equipped with 20 presses ranging from 55 to 1,800 tons.
- The largest progressive stamping line is 800 tons with a 144” press bed and coil capacity of 30,000 pounds.
- There are five smaller progressive lines ranging from 60 to 400 tons.
- The 1,100-ton hydraulic press has a bed size of 84” by 120” with about 12” of cushion travel for large draws and SSDC capability to 1,000 tons.
- Dekalb Tool & Die can accommodate high-volume and low-volume production programs.
- The company has experience in multiple material types and grades, including mild steel, galvanized, pre-paint, high strength low alloy, stainless steel, aluminum, brass, and plastic.
- The metal stamping process produces high volumes of identical metal components to exacting customer requirements across a wide range of industries.
- Dekalb Tool & Die, Inc. is a reliable and trusted provider of metal stamping services for various industrial production projects.
- The company offers long-run production, short-run orders, or assistance during unexpected equipment failures.
