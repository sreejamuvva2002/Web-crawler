# Stryten Energy

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-11

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Named a 2024 Military Friendly Employer](https://stryten.flywheelstaging.com/stryten-energy-named-a-2024-military-friendly-employer/)  ·  `page_050537`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy has been named a 2024 Gold Military Friendly® Employer for the second year.
- The recognition from militaryfriendly.com acknowledges companies that create sustainable career paths, community outreach, brand enthusiasm, and enduring partnerships benefiting the military community.
- Stryten Energy hires military members across every level of the company, from executive management to the plant floor, with many having progressed through internal promotions.
- Military veterans make up approximately six percent of Stryten Energy's workforce.
- Mike Judd, President and Chief Executive Officer at Stryten Energy, stated that the company values the service of veterans and provides opportunities for them to translate military skills into careers in the battery business.
- The designation announcement was made on Veterans Day.
- Institutions earning the Military Friendly® Employer designation are evaluated using public data sources and responses from a proprietary survey.
- Over one thousand companies participated in the 2024 Military Friendly® survey.
- Methodology, criteria, and weightings were determined by VIQTORY with input from the Military Friendly® Advisory Council of independent military recruitment leaders.
- Final ratings are determined by combining an organization's survey score with assessments of thresholds for Applicant, New Hire Retention, Employee Turnover, and Promotion & Advancement of veterans and military employees.
- Kayla Lopez, Senior Director of Military Partnerships at Military Friendly®, stated that designated organizations have invested in initiatives benefiting service members, military spouses, and veterans.
- Stryten Energy currently has job openings for military members across numerous U.S. locations.
- Stryten Energy sponsors Rivers of Recovery, a national nonprofit that uses outdoor-based activities to treat symptoms of post-traumatic stress, depression, and anxiety in combat veterans and active-duty military.
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions and components across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- The company is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Stryten Energy's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- The company powers applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- Stryten Energy is backed by more than a century of industry expertise.
