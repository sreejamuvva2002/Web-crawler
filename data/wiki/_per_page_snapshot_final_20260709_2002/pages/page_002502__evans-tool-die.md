# Evans Tool & Die

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-05-05

**Source:** [Evans Tool & Die | 2025 Small Business Rock Star | Georgia](https://georgia.org/blogs/evans-tool-die-precision-built-scale)  ·  `page_002502`

## Facts

- Evans Tool & Die has been a trusted provider of custom tooling and precision manufacturing services for more than 75 years.
- The company specializes in the design, development, and production of high-quality metal stampings.
- Evans Tool & Die is located in Conyers, near Atlanta.
- The company serves a variety of industries, offering expert solutions for complex tooling and part-manufacturing needs.
- CEO and President Dee Barnes serves as a board member of the Georgia Association of Manufacturers (GAM).
- Through GAM, the company helps foster innovation and collaboration to support more than 150 companies, suppliers, and professional services firms.
- Evans Tool & Die joins other Georgia manufacturers including GE Appliances, Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation, Irving Consumer Products, and Kia Georgia in shaping the future of manufacturing in Georgia.
- The company is a multi-generational family business that has grown from its beginnings over 75 years ago.
- Evans Tool & Die has expanded its capabilities to include stamping, laser cutting, and metal fabrication alongside its original tool and die business.
- Dee Barnes leads the company started by her grandfather, supported by her father and uncles, after returning from time at Georgia College and State University.
- The company was recognized as a 2025 Small Business ROCK STAR award recipient.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD), in partnership with the Georgia Economic Developers Association, established the Small Business ROCK STAR awards to recognize innovations, community engagement, and positive impact of state small businesses.
