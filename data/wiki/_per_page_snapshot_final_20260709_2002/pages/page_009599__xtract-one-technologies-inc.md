## Facts

- Xtract One Technologies' SmartGateway security screening solution has been selected by Hyundai Transys Georgia Powertrain Inc. to secure facilities in West Point, Ga.
- SmartGateway will be deployed at the manufacturing plant to protect workers and staff from weapons entering the buildings and to prevent mass-casualty events.
- Jinho Kim, manager of GA and EHS at Hyundai Transys Georgia Powertrain, stated they were able to view and test Xtract One's Gateway at Kia Georgia Inc., which had great success with keeping weapons out.
- Jinho Kim noted the Gateway solution was quickly and accurately detecting weapons, and its customization aspect would fit seamlessly into their manufacturing facility.
- SmartGateway is the latest iteration of Xtract One's Gateway screening solution.
- It is a fast, frictionless entry screener used by manufacturing plants, casinos, and large arenas to unobtrusively detect guns, knives, and other potential weapons on people entering a facility without requiring them to empty pockets of personal items.
- Peter Evans, CEO of Xtract One, stated auto manufacturers, their supply chains, and other large workplaces are well suited for the Gateway solution due to needing to process hundreds of workers through doors quickly during shift changes.
- This is Xtract One's third customer in the auto manufacturing industry.
- Peter Evans claimed modern walk-through metal detector systems are inefficient by modern standards, less secure, and more intrusive than SmartGateway.
- SmartGateway uses artificial-intelligence-powered scanners to detect concealed guns and knives, alerting security personnel for secondary screening if weapons are found.
- If people are not carrying weapons, they simply walk through without emptying pockets or other personal articles.
- Upgrades to the system have made SmartGateway very easy to learn and use, meaning it requires fewer personnel to operate relative to other scanning systems.
- The system is customizable to fit the security needs and requirements of individual facilities, including look and feel.
- Ryan Smith, program manager of security at Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia Inc., stated they were pleased with how Gateway solutions looked and worked at their facilities and recommended Xtract One to Hyundai Transys.
- Since implementing the solution at Kia's facility, they have prevented a significant number of weapons from entering.
- Personnel screening time at Kia's facility has been drastically reduced from 21 seconds to 2.5 seconds.
- Ryan Smith noted that in an industry based on just-in-time processes, this additional time provides production flexibility to maintain work areas during crucial times.
- Xtract One Technologies is a leading, technology-driven threat detection and security solution company leveraging artificial intelligence for seamless and secure patron access control experiences.
- The company makes unobtrusive threat detection systems that enable venue building operators to prioritize improved patron experiences while providing unprecedented safety.
- Xtract One's multisensor Gateway product enables companies to covertly screen for weapons at points of entry without disrupting traffic flow.
- Its AI-based Xtract Vision allows venue and building operators to identify weapons and other threats inside and outside facilities.
- Xtract One Insights provides valuable intelligence for optimizing operations.
