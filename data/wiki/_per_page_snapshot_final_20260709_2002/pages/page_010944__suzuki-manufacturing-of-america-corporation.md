# Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-05-25

**Source:** [Suzuki celebrates 20 years of ATV manufacturing in the U.S. | Powersports Business](https://powersportsbusiness.com/top-stories/2022/05/25/suzuki-celebrates-20-years-of-atv-manufacturing-in-the-u-s/)  ·  `page_010944`

## Facts

- Rome, Georgia-based Suzuki Motor USA, LLC is celebrating 20 years of manufacturing KingQuad ATVs at the SMAC facility.
- KingQuad ATVs first rolled off the assembly line in Rome in May 2002 and have been built exclusively in the U.S. ever since.
- Suzuki has been building the award-winning KingQuad ATVs at a multi-million dollar, 35-acre manufacturing facility since 2002.
- The SMAC facility is located in the center of the biggest ATV market in the world, allowing access to real-world rider feedback for product refinement and timely delivery of KingQuad ATVs to Suzuki dealers worldwide.
- Suzuki has been an innovator in engineering since the company’s founding in 1909.
- In the early 1980s, Suzuki led the industry with the creation of the first ever four-wheel ATV in 1983.
- Kerry Graeber, vice president and division manager of MC/ATV Sales and Marketing, stated that building KingQuad ATVs in America for the past 20 years represents the commitment, hard work, and dedication of the people who build them at the Rome, Georgia facility.
- The SMAC facility is one key to the continued success of Suzuki’s global ATV business.
- KingQuad ATVs built at the facility are shipped to U.S. Suzuki dealers and nearly 30 countries worldwide.
