# HYUNDAI TRANSYS

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-04-17

## Facts

- HYUNDAI TRANSYS provides customized seat solutions optimized for various vehicle models and classes.
- The company offers seat technologies specialized for sedans, SUVs, and electric vehicles to provide a differentiated mobility experience.
- HYUNDAI TRANSYS seats are utilized in Genesis G80 EV, GV70 EV, Hyundai IONIQ 9, IONIQ 6, IONIQ 5, KONA EV, Kia EV9, EV6, Rivian R1S, R1T, Lucid Air, and Lucid Gravity.
- The Kia EV9 is Korea’s first flagship large SUV electric vehicle, equipped with HYUNDAI TRANSYS' specialized EV seat technologies.
- Specialized EV seat features include dynamic body care, low-power carbon heated seats, tilting walk-in technology, and an innovative ventilated docking system to maximize space utilization and convenience unique to EVs.
- HYUNDAI TRANSYS designs, develops, and mass-produces seats for over 200 vehicle models annually.
- The company integrates R&D capabilities to develop everything from individual seat components to complete seat assemblies in-house.
- The HYUNDAI TRANSYS Seat Research Center conducts product verification using approximately 160 test equipment and around 460 types of testing and evaluation for safety, durability, performance, and materials.
- Global R&D centers are located in Korea, the U.S., and India.
- These global R&D centers develop products that meet the latest automotive regulations and required specifications for each country.
- HYUNDAI TRANSYS actively integrates innovative technologies such as big data and AI into its production processes to enhance efficiency and quality.
- HYUNDAI TRANSYS seats have consistently received high ratings in the U.S. J.D. Power quality satisfaction survey.
- In 2024, Kia Forte small/compact car seats ranked 1st in the J.D. Power Seat Quality and Satisfaction Study.
- In 2023, Kia Rio small/compact car seats ranked 2nd in the J.D. Power Seat Quality and Satisfaction Study.
- In 2023, Kia Sorento midsize/large SUV seats ranked 2nd in the J.D. Power Seat Quality and Satisfaction Study.
- In 2022, Kia Forte small/compact car seats ranked 2nd in the J.D. Power Seat Quality and Satisfaction Study.
- In 2022, Kia Soul small/compact SUV seats ranked 2nd in the J.D. Power Seat Quality and Satisfaction Study.
- In 2022, Kia Sorento midsize/large SUV seats ranked 3rd in the J.D. Power Seat Quality and Satisfaction Study.
- In 2021, Genesis G90 premium car seats ranked 3rd in the J.D. Power Seat Quality and Satisfaction Study.
- In 2020, Hyundai Accent small/compact car seats ranked 2nd in the J.D. Power Seat Quality and Satisfaction Study.
- In 2020, Kia Forte small/compact car seats ranked 3rd in the J.D. Power Seat Quality and Satisfaction Study.
- Production areas in the USA manufacture seats for Genesis GV70, Hyundai Santa Fe, Kia Sorento, Sportage, Genesis GV70 EV, Hyundai IONIQ 9, IONIQ 5, Kia EV9, EV6, Lucid Motors Lucid Air, Lucid Gravity, and Rivian R1S, R1T.
