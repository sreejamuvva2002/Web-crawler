# ZF Industries

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2010-04-23

## Facts

- A new ZF Industries plant in Gainesville is expected to create 215 new jobs.
- The $90 million, 250,000-square-foot facility will build transmissions for wind turbines.
- Groundbreaking ceremony was held today (April 23, 2010).
- Gov. Sonny Perdue and Lt. Gov. Casey Cagle attended the groundbreaking as guests.
- Guests at the event spoke to the importance of renewable energy for the future of Georgia’s economy.
- Gov. Sonny Perdue said the state has positioned itself to become an inviting location for businesses.
- Gov. Sonny Perdue stated, "Those things don’t just happen," and praised Hall County for making businesses feel welcome.
- ZF Industries currently has a plant in Hall County that manufactures automotive transmissions and axles.
