# Murata Manufacturing Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-05

**Source:** [Unlocking the Future of Connected Mobility: Murata Heads to CES 2025](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/unlocking-the-future-of-connected-mobility-murata-heads-to-ces-2025-302321051.html)  ·  `page_028849`

## Facts

- Murata will be attending CES 2025 January 7-10 in Las Vegas.
- The booth in the Vehicle Technology Pavilion (West Hall Booth #6500) will serve as a showcase for products enabling innovation throughout the electronics industry.
- With a focus on mobility and connectivity, attendees are invited to meet with solution experts to see how Murata is unlocking the future of connected mobility.
- Potential and existing customers, partners, and fellow innovators will see an extensive line-up of product demos and technologies, as well as those still under development.
- The SCH16T is a high-performance MEMS-based 6DoF sensor in a single package, offering low drift over temperature and a wide gyro measurement range for centimeter-level accuracy in flight.
- Murata's integrated lens cleaning device ensures clear optical camera vision to meet the need for safety in adverse weather for AD/ADAS-equipped vehicles.
- In-Cabin/Out-of-Cabin Radar Sensing Solutions can minimize the need for traditional sensors in vehicles while enhancing safety for in-cabin use and providing added convenience for out-of-cabin applications.
- Matter™ is a new smart home connectivity standard for IoT devices, enabling seamless, secure connections to home networks, supported by Murata's miniaturized, high-performance modules on display.
- The Type 1SC-NTN is the first ultra-compact resin mold module recognized by an NTN service provider, fully certified from Skylo for its dual-mode cellular and non-terrestrial network capabilities, integrating the latest Rel-17 NTN standard to enable satellite data communication without hardware changes.
- LF antennas transmit and receive magnetic field energy to enable high accuracy positioning, low power, and stable communication without blind spots, with Murata applying the position detection function to an auto landing system for drones in a precision landing demo.
- Murata Manufacturing Co., Ltd. is a worldwide leader in the design, manufacture and sale of ceramic-based passive electronic components and solutions, communication modules and power supply modules.
- Murata is committed to the development of advanced electronic materials and leading edge, multi-functional, high-density modules.
- The company has employees and manufacturing facilities throughout the world.
