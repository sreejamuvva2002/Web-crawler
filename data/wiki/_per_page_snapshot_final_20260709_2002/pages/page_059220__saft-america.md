## Facts

- For 100+ years SAFT has been specializing in advanced-technology battery solutions for industry, in space, at sea, in the air and on land in remote and harsh environments from the Arctic Circle to the Sahara Desert.
- SAFT stationary batteries are designed to work when needed for up to 20+ years in challenging conditions with low maintenance.
- The Arthur N. Ulrich Company serves as SAFT’s industrial standby sales representative in Ohio, Kentucky, West Virginia, Indiana, Michigan and Western Pennsylvania.
- The Arthur N. Ulrich Company is SAFT’s exclusive representative for railroad and transit markets in the United States and Canada.
- SAFT is a leading manufacturer of nickel cadmium (Ni-Cd) and lithium ion (Li-Ion) batteries for industrial standby applications.
- SAFT provides a complete range of low, medium and high rate characteristic batteries suited for virtually any standby application.
- Markets served by SAFT include generator starting, switchgear, UPS, rail signaling & communication, energy storage, solar (PV) & wind, emergency & security systems, and power backup.
- The Arthur N. Ulrich Company has worked alongside SAFT as a manufacturer’s representative agency and has been instrumental in the introduction of many new types of batteries for stationary applications.
