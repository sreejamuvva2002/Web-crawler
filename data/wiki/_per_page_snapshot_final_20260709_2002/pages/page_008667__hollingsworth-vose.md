# Hollingsworth & Vose

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2024-08-17

**Source:** [Hollingsworth & Vose introduce PlusZero solution to create a sustainable world | Thermal Control Business Update | HVAC-R Industry](https://www.thermalcontrolmagazine.com/news/hollingsworth-vose-introduce-pluszero-solution-to-create-a-sustainable-world/)  ·  `page_008667`

## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose is a global leader in filtration and energy storage solutions.
- The company is introducing PlusZero, a line of high-performance solutions made without PFAS.
- PlusZero aims to create a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable world.
- The environmentally friendly solutions are designed to meet performance, reliability, and affordability goals.
- Mike Clark serves as H&V’s Division President.
- Clark stated that PlusZero is the company's latest breakthrough in innovation and sustainability after years of research.
- The PlusZero line utilizes advanced technology to eliminate forever chemicals (PFAS) in manufacturing.
- The solutions deliver high performance in most applications and are priced similarly to legacy counterparts.
- H&V is committed to providing sustainable business solutions, including PlusZero, despite not yet being required to eliminate PFAS use in product manufacturing.
