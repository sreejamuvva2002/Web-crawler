# Enchem America Inc.

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-materials|Battery Materials]]  ·  **Location:** Commerce, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Enchem America INC. is a subsidiary of South Korean-based Enchem Co., Ltd. that manufactures electrolytes and high-functionality additives for rechargeable batteries used in electric vehicles and energy storage systems.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Produces essential battery electrolytes and additives for electric vehicle batteries and energy-storage systems.
