# Eastman Chemical Company

## Facts

- Eastman Chemical Company is a global specialty materials company headquartered in Kingsport, Tennessee, with 41 manufacturing facilities and equity interests in three manufacturing joint ventures across 12 countries.
- The company supplies Saflex™ PVB and HUD acoustic interlayers to the automotive sector, which are essential for passenger safety and advance improvements in solar, heat, and ultraviolet management.
- Solar-absorbing Saflex™ PVB interlayer solutions, including the Saflex™ S series, Saflex™ Solar Connect, and XIR™ Solar Control Technology, are ideal for electric vehicle ("EV") glass in cabin-forward designs and larger sunroofs.
- These EV glass interlayers reduce loads on air-conditioning systems and help maximize EV driving range.
- The Saflex™ Q series advanced acoustic interlayers enable weight reduction of vehicles.
- Tetrashield™ performance polyester resins provide improved performance and sustainability features for automotive coatings end-users.
- Eastman's CORE analytics-based software platform provides automotive groups and professional installers access to shop management and automotive film patterns to improve customer experience and accelerate category development.
- Eastman began construction of a methanolysis manufacturing plant in Kingsport, Tennessee, expected to be mechanically complete by the end of 2022, as part of its molecular recycling and circular economy initiatives.
- Eastman's molecular recycling processes are expected to reduce greenhouse gas ("GHG") emissions by 20 percent to 50 percent when compared to processes using fossil fuels in various Renew products.
- The company has committed to reducing its absolute scope 1 (direct GHG emissions occurring from sources that are owned by Eastman) and scope 2 (indirect GHG associated with the purchase of electricity, steam, heat, or cooling and are a result of Eastman's energy use) emissions by one-third by 2030 to achieve carbon neutrality by 2050.
