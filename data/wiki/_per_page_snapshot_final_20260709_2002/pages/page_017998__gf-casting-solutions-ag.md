# GF Casting Solutions AG

## Facts

- Gov. Brian Kemp announced that hundreds of new manufacturing jobs are heading to east central Georgia.
- GF Casting Solutions AG will invest over $184 million for a new facility in Augusta.
- The project is expected to create 350 new jobs in Richmond County.
- GF Casting Solutions is part of Georg Fischer AG, based in Schaffhausen, Switzerland.
- The company develops and creates manufacturing parts from aluminum, magnesium, iron, and super alloys for light vehicles, trucks, aerospace, energy, off-highway vehicles, and industrial uses.
- Carlos Vasto, President of GF Casting Solutions, stated that building the high-pressure die casting facility is a very long-term investment to complete their global footprint in the e-mobility market.
- The company aims to bring leading technology to the US and create advanced manufacturing jobs for several generations in Georgia.
- Carlos Vasto noted that the Augusta location is ideal for addressing the thriving regional automotive industry.
- The new facility will be located at a new LEED-certified site at Augusta Corporate Park in Richmond County.
- The manufacturing site is expected to produce aluminum parts, with a focus on large structural components for the automotive industry.
- Operations are expected to begin in 2027.
- GF Casting Solutions will hire for roles in both manufacturing and automation over the coming years.
