# PHA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-06

**Source:** [Hyundai Supplier PHA to Create Over 400 Jobs in Chatham County | Georgia](https://georgia.org/press-releases/2023/hyundai-supplier-pha-create-over-400-jobs-chatham-county)  ·  `page_000043`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced on March 6, 2023 that PHA will create 402 new jobs and invest more than $67 million in a new facility in Chatham County.
- PHA is a global auto parts manufacturer that will supply the Hyundai Metaplant in Bryan County.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that in a single month, Georgia’s economic development community announced more than 1,900 new jobs for hardworking Georgians, with the bulk related to the Hyundai Metaplant.
- PHA is a Tier-1 automotive parts supplier for Hyundai Motor Group, Kia, and many other original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).
- PHA is a Korea-based company with locations throughout the world, including multiple facilities within the United States.
- Since its establishment in 1985, PHA has grown into a global company in automotive door moving systems through research and development, innovative technology, and aggressive investment.
- Sangtae Kim is Chairman of PHA.
- PHA’s new facility will be located at the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center, a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) Certified site.
- The new manufacturing facility will produce door modules, tailgate latches, and hood latches for OEMs, including Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.
- Operations are expected to begin in 2024.
- Trip Tollison is the President and CEO of the Savannah Economic Development Authority.
- PHA is the second Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America supplier to locate in the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- Alyce Thornhill, Director of Project Implementation and Supplier Strategy, represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s Global Commerce team on this competitive project.
- The project was completed in partnership with the Savannah Economic Development Authority, Georgia Ports Authority, Georgia Quick Start, and Georgia Power.
- Pat Wilson is GDEcD Commissioner.
- On October 25, Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America officially broke ground in neighboring Bryan County on its first fully dedicated EV and battery manufacturing facility.
- Offsite suppliers connected to the project were predicted to invest approximately $1 billion in other locations around the state.
- Projects announced to date have exceeded this projection by more than $800 million.
- Georgia is positioned as a hub for the electric mobility industry, building on assets that make the automotive industry successful.
- Since 2020, more than 35 EV-related projects have announced more than $21 billion in investment and 27,400 jobs in Georgia.
- PHA operates in nine countries with more than 3,300 employees worldwide and $750 million in revenue.
