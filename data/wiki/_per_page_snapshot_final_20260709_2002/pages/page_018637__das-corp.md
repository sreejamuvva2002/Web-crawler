# DAS Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-09-06

**Source:** [Automotive parts company to lead $35M Candler County 'investment' | Georgia | thecentersquare.com](https://www.thecentersquare.com/georgia/article_8ff66078-4cdc-11ee-baff-b7963c27bfbd.html)  ·  `page_018637`

## Facts

- DAS Corp. plans to establish a new manufacturing facility in Candler County.
- DAS Corp.'s investment, coupled with collaborative indirect investments, will total more than $35 million.
- The new Metter facility will create 300 jobs.
- A Georgia Department of Economic Development representative confirmed the project is still active and that Georgia Quick Start is involved.
- The Georgia Quick Start designation allows state officials to decline to release how much the state offered in incentives.
- DAS Corp. designs and manufactures seating systems, structures, and safety seating components.
- DAS Corp. is a supplier to Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia and Hyundai Motor Group.
- Gov. Brian Kemp stated that logistics assets, including a reliable network of rail lines and highways, connect companies like DAS to key business partners in all corners of the state and to markets across the world.
- The company will primarily produce automotive seat structures at its planned Candler County Industrial Park facility.
- Operations at the new facility should start toward the end of 2024.
- Metter Mayor Ed Boyd stated that the project's impact on the community will be transformative, stimulating investment in housing, creating other business opportunities, and improving the social economic wellbeing of its citizenry.
