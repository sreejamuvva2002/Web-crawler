## Facts

- SHIROKI North America, Inc. is a mid-sized manufacturer of motor vehicle parts based in Smithville, Tennessee.
- The company serves the North American automotive supply chain.
- It operates multiple facilities located in Smithville, Tennessee; Dalton, Georgia; and Gordonsville, Tennessee.
- SHIROKI North America is part of AISIN SHIROKI Corporation within the AISIN Group that encompasses its parent organization and related operations.
- The company traces its origins to 1988 when it began as SW Manufacturing, Inc., the first international manufacturing facility for Shiroki Corporation.
- The operation expanded from a single Tennessee site to a second plant in Dalton, Georgia in 1995.
- A third facility in Gordonsville, Tennessee was established by late 2006.
- In 2007, the firm was renamed SHIROKI North America, Inc., with the Smithville site designated as the North American headquarters.
- Following AISIN SEIKI's subsumption of Shiroki Corporation, the North American unit became part of AISIN SHIROKI Corporation in 2016 within the AISIN Group.
- The company has between 201 and 500 employees.
