# Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Suzuki Achieves Accumulated ATV Production of 500,000 Units in the US | GLOBAL NEWS | Global Suzuki](https://www.globalsuzuki.com/globalnews/2024/0628.html)  ·  `page_010835`

## Facts

- Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corporation (SMAC) achieved accumulated ATV production of 500,000 units on 13 June 2024.
- SMAC started ATV production in May 2002.
- SMAC currently produces four ATV models: KingQuad 750AXi 4x4, KingQuad 500AXi 4x4, KingQuad 400ASi 4x4, and KingQuad 400FSi 4x4.
- The accumulated production milestone of 500,000 units was reached in 22 years and 2 months, driven by the flagship model KingQuad 750AXi 4x4 Power Steering Special Edition.
- Suzuki’s ATVs are recognized worldwide for high durability and ease of handling, with the United States serving as the main market.
- SMAC is located in Rome, Georgia.
- SMAC employed 145 people as of March 2024.
- SMAC produced 15,651 units in FY2023.
- Suzuki plans to continue producing ATVs with high quality and reliability.
