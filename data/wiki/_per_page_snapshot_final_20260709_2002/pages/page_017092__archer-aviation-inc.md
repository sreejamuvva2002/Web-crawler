# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-20

**Source:** [HeliHub.com : Archer Aviation completes Manufacturing Facility build phase - HeliHub.com](https://www.helihub.com/2024/12/20/archer-aviation-completes-manufacturing-facility-build-phase/)  ·  `page_017092`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. (NYSE : ACHR) announced that earlier this week it received the certificate of occupancy for its high-volume manufacturing facility, ARC.
- ARC is connected to the Covington Municipal Airport in Georgia and located in Newton County.
- Archer is now working to complete the tooling load-in for the initial manufacturing line at this facility.
- Production is planned to begin in early 2025, with a goal of ramping to a rate of two aircraft per month by the end of the year.
- Archer built ARC in close partnership with Stellantis, and the two companies are continuing to progress their plans to mass-produce Archer’s Midnight aircraft.
- Stellantis is contributing capital, advanced manufacturing technology and expertise and experienced personnel with the goal of scaling this facility to 650 aircraft annually by 2030.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp commented that today's announcement marks the next step in a journey reaffirming Georgia’s commitment to working with innovative companies like Archer to create quality jobs and opportunities for years to come.
- Congressman Mike Collins of Georgia, who sits on the House Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure Committee and Committee on Science, Space, and Technology, added that the completion highlights Georgia’s leadership in innovation, advanced manufacturing and job creation.
- Archer founder and CEO Adam Goldstein stated that construction on ARC is now complete, marking a shift from R&D into commercialization, and praised the team for getting it done fast and on budget.
- Stellantis Chief Manufacturing and Supply Chain Officer Arnaud Deboeuf commented that supporting Archer’s electric aircraft growth with deep manufacturing expertise is a great opportunity and a significant step in taking clean mobility to the sky.
- ARC is where Archer plans to produce its all-electric vertical take-off and landing aircraft, Midnight, which is designed to offer passengers a sustainable, low-noise and safe alternative to ground transportation in congested cities around the world.
