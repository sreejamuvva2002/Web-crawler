## Facts

- Constellium is a global leader in the development, manufacturing, and recycling of aluminum products and solutions.
- The company operates a recycling facility in Muscle Shoals that processes used aluminum cans from shredded scrap into new aluminum coils ready to become cans again.
- Constellium Airware® offers low density, high stiffness & strength, plus improved fatigue and corrosion resistance.
- Aluminum can sheet is recyclable, preserves freshness, enables branding, and includes body, end, and tab stock supplied to canmakers globally.
- Constellium provides aluminum solutions for automotive applications, enabling sustainable mobility through innovation in materials, testing, and production from design to recycling.
- Constellium will host a conference call and webcast on Wednesday, April 29, 2026, at 10:00 AM (Eastern Time) to announce its first quarter 2026 results.
