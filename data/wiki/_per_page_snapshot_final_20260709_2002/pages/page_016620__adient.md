## Facts

- Adient is a global leader in automotive seating solutions.
- Adient has completed a significant expansion of its China Technical Center in Chongqing.
- The expansion milestone was marked by a ceremony led by Adient President and CEO Jerome Dorlack, APAC Executive Vice President James Huang, and key leadership from the APAC and China teams along with staff at the China Technical Center.
- The expansion is a major step in Adient’s long-term growth strategy in China.
- The upgrade reinforces the company’s dedication to product innovation.
- The upgraded center now boasts advanced facilities, such as a sled test lab and MAST test lab.
- These facilities will enhance the company’s capabilities in engineering design, testing, validation, and product development.
- The newly expanded center offers a collaborative, flexible, and digitally advanced environment for the Adient China engineering team.
- This environment enables the team to create innovative automotive seating solutions that meet both local preferences and global automotive trends.
- Jerome Dorlack stated that China is the largest automotive market in the world and a key growth driver for Adient.
- The expansion highlights Adient’s commitment to blending global leading technologies with local market needs.
- This strategy ensures the company continues to deliver outstanding products and services to both local and international customers while advancing innovations in electrification and smart features.
- Dorlack expressed confidence in the future of China’s automotive sector, calling the expansion a clear demonstration of ongoing investment in the market.
- Joe Wang, Vice President of China Seating, stated that the expansion is a significant milestone for their business as they continually strive to provide superior products and services to customers.
- Mark Sutton, Vice President of APAC Engineering, commented that the upgraded facility will enhance their ability to develop cutting-edge products and strengthen their competitive position, supporting Adient’s sustained growth in China.
- The enhanced technical center will collaborate closely with Adient’s global tech centers to lead innovation in the automotive seating industry.
- Adient’s strategy focuses on driving business growth through technological advancements by working with OEM customers and partners.
- Key exploration areas for these partnerships include comfort, connectivity, safety, reconfiguration, and sustainability.
- These efforts aim to shape the future of mobility and propel the global automotive seating industry forward.
- Adient has a workforce of around 70,000 employees across 29 countries.
- The company operates more than 200 manufacturing and assembly plants worldwide.
- Adient delivers automotive seating solutions for major OEMs and millions of vehicles annually.
- The company’s offerings cover everything from complete seating systems to individual components.
- Adient manages every stage of the automotive seat-making process.
