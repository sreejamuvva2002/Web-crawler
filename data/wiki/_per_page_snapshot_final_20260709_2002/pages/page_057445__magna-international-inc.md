# Magna International Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Magna International - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magna_International)  ·  `page_057445`

## Facts

- Magna International Inc. is a Canadian parts manufacturer for automakers and the largest automobile parts manufacturer in North America by sales of original equipment parts.
- The company produces automotive systems, assemblies, modules, and components supplied to General Motors, Ford, Stellantis, BMW, Mercedes, Volkswagen, Toyota, Tesla, and Tata Motors.
- Magna is headquartered in Aurora, Ontario, with its chief executive officer being Swamy Kotagiri.
- As of 2023, the company has 179,000 employees across 342 manufacturing operations and 91 product development, engineering and sales centres in 27 countries.
- The company's corporate constitution requires consensus from management, shareholders, and employees to make changes, and it shares 10 percent of pretax profits with its employees.
- In February 2021, the office of the governor of Michigan announced that Magna plans to open a facility in St. Clair, Michigan to build battery enclosures for the 2022 GMC Hummer electric pickup.
- The new St. Clair facility is expected to cost $70 million and create over 300 jobs.
- Battery enclosures for the GMC Hummer EV will be produced at General Motors' facilities in Detroit and Hamtramck.
- An electric version of the Ineos Grenadier off-road utility vehicle is to be developed by Ineos and Magna, scheduled to enter production in Graz, Austria in 2026.
- In March 2018, Magna announced a partnership with Lyft to supply high-tech kits that turn vehicles into self-driving cars, investing $200 million into the project while jointly owning the developed intellectual property as Lyft's exclusive supplier of self-driving kits.
- In June 2018, Magna announced a partnership with BAIC Group to develop next-generation smart electric vehicles for Chinese consumers.
- In February 2015, Samsung SDI agreed to purchase the battery pack business from Magna Steyr for $120 million.
- The company partnered with BMW on electrification to produce electric cars on a contract basis.
- Magna has produced more than 46 million automotive components and opened a $66.5 million plant to produce cameras and driver-assistance components.
- In 2018, Magna introduced an Icon radar system to help automakers reach Level 5 autonomy as well as automatic emergency braking systems.
- The company uses mobility technology to develop customized computing for fully automated driving systems and is known for using new materials for lightweighting vehicles.
- Magna acquired Boston startup Optimus Ride and its more than 120 employees in January 2022 to advance autonomous vehicle technology.
- In December 2022, Magna announced the acquisition of the Stockholm-headquartered Veoneer Active Safety business from SSW Partners for US$1.52 billion.
- As of 2024, Magna International consists of seven groups: Cosma, Magna Electronics, Magna Powertrain, Magna Exteriors, Magna Seating, Magna Steyr, and Mechatronics, Mirrors & Lighting.
- The company's United States operations are headed out of Troy, Michigan with its European head office in Vienna, Austria.
