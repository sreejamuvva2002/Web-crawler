# Sewon America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [GA: Sewon America Launches $50 Million Expansion in Rincon, With 100 New Jobs | Trade and Industry Development](https://www.tradeandindustrydev.com/region/georgia/news/ga-sewon-america-launches-50-million-expansion-35136)  ·  `page_001354`

## Facts

- Sewon America is a subsidiary of Korean-based Sewon Precision Industry Co.
- Sewon America is expanding its manufacturing operations in Rincon, Georgia.
- The expansion project is valued at $50 million.
- The project will add 98,250 square feet to the facility.
- The project will create 100 new jobs.
- The expansion introduces new capabilities to Sewon America's operations.
- This marks the first expansion by a Hyundai supplier since the launch of Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America in late 2024.
- Misti Martin, Deputy Commissioner of Global Commerce at the Georgia Department of Economic Development, called the expansion a full-circle moment and milestone for the Metaplant’s supplier network.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development supports the expansion and anticipates additional opportunities it will create for Effingham County.
- Matt Saxon, Chairman of the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority (ECIDA), stated that Sewon America chose Effingham County for its second U.S. facility.
- ECIDA views the expansion as underscoring Sewon America's confidence in the community, workforce, and business climate.
- Rincon Mayor Kevin Exley expressed excitement about the opportunity for more quality jobs in the community.
- The City of Rincon plans to partner with Sewon America to help local citizens access the new positions.
- Robert Vazquez, Director of Human Resources at Sewon America, stated the expansion will allow the company to better serve customers and add new capabilities.
- Sewon America plans to grow alongside the community, create new job opportunities, and continue investing in its workforce in Effingham County.
- Sewon America operates one of the largest automobile body parts manufacturing plants in North America.
- Sewon America opened its first Georgia facility in LaGrange in 2008.
- The LaGrange facility has since doubled in size.
- Sewon America began operations at its Rincon facility in 2025.
- With the expansion, Sewon America will continue to supply electric vehicle (EV) body parts for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).
- One of Sewon America's key customers is Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.
- The expansion project received support from the Georgia Department of Economic Development, the City of Rincon, and the Effingham County Board of Commissioners.
