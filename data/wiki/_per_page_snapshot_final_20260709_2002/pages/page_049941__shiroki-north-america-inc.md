## Facts

- SHIROKI North America has an estimated annual revenue of $209.6M.
- SHIROKI North America has an estimated revenue per employee of $412,500.
- SHIROKI North America currently employs 508 people.
- SHIROKI North America increased its employee count by 11% in the last year.
- The company originated in 1988 as SW Manufacturing, Inc., serving as the first international manufacturing facility for Shiroki Corporation.
- In 1995, the company expanded from one plant to two plants by adding a facility in Dalton, GA.
- In late 2006, the company expanded to three plants by adding a facility in Gordonsville, TN.
- In 2007, SW Manufacturing was renamed SHIROKI North America, Inc., and the Smithville, TN site was designated as the Headquarters for all North American business.
- At the end of 2014, it was announced that AISIN SEIKI (Japan) would subsume Shiroki Corporation.
- The acquisition by AISIN SEIKI was concluded in April 2016, making Shiroki Corporation a subsidiary of AISIN SEIKI.
- SHIROKI North America operates manufacturing facilities in Smithville, TN; Dalton, GA; and Gordonsville, TN.
- The company's executive structure includes positions such as EVP, General Manager, Human Resources Manager, Senior General Manager, PC Support Manager, Corporate Project Manager, Assistant General Manager Quality, Plant Manager, Manager of Production Control and Shipping & Receiving, and Corporate General Manager Human Resources.
- SHIROKI North America conducts business across North America (United States, Canada, and Mexico), Europe, India, China, and Japan.
