# Hussey Gay Bell

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Georgia, United States

## Overview

Hussey Gay Bell is an architecture, engineering and surveying firm that served as the prime consultant and sub-consultant for the Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) project in Bryan County, providing design and development services for the EV plant and associated Tier 1 supplier facilities.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Provides engineering and consulting services that enable the construction and operation of Georgia’s largest electric-vehicle manufacturing facility, supporting the state’s EV supply-chain ecosystem.

## Investment

- **$5.5 billion** (2022) — Establish Hyundai Motor Group’s first U.S. EV mass-production facility (Metaplant America)
  - Initial investment announced in 2022; later grew to over $8 billion encompassing the plant and its Tier 1 supplier network.

## Jobs

- **12,000+ direct, long-term jobs**
  - Projected employment impact of the Hyundai Metaplant America project across coastal Georgia.

## Timeline

- **2022** — Hyundai Motor Group announced a $5.5 billion investment to build its first U.S. EV mass-production facility on a 3,000+ acre site in Bryan County, Georgia.
- **March 2025** — Commercial production officially began at Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA).

## Key facts

- Georgia achieved a historic milestone with the grand opening of the Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America in Bryan County, the largest economic development project in the state’s history.
- The investment in HMGMA grew from $5.5 billion to over $8 billion, encompassing both the plant and its network of Tier 1 suppliers.
- The project is projected to create more than 12,000 direct, long-term jobs, driving transformative economic growth across coastal Georgia.
- Hussey Gay Bell served as prime consultant and subconsultant for multiple Tier 1 supplier projects, delivering design and development services that align with Hyundai’s next-generation EV production standards.
- The success of HMGMA reflects an extraordinary public-private partnership among Hyundai Motor Group, the State of Georgia, the Georgia Department of Economic Development, and regional development authorities.
- Georgia has been ranked #1 State for Business in the United States for over a decade, underscoring its favorable environment for EV manufacturing investments.
