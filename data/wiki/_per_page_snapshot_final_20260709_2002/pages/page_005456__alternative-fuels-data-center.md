## Facts

- Provides maps and charts showing transportation data and trends related to alternative fuels and vehicles.
- Features Electric Vehicle Registrations by State, displaying the U.S. light-duty electric vehicle population as of December 2023 (last updated September 2024).
- Includes Change in U.S. Light-Duty Vehicle Registration Counts, tracking trends of light-duty vehicle registrations by fuel type measured in percent change from 2016 through 2025 (last updated June 2026).
- Offers TransAtlas, an interactive map that displays light-duty vehicle registration counts over time by state and fuel type.
