# Enchem America, Inc.

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-02

**Source:** [EV battery manufacturer Enchem America cancels plans for $152.2 million facility in Brownsville - Talk-N West TN](https://talknwesttn.com/ev-battery-manufacturer-enchem-america-cancels-plans-for-150-million-facility-in-brownsville/)  ·  `page_007965`

## Facts

- Enchem America, Inc. has canceled plans for a $152.2 million EV battery facility in Brownsville.
- The cancellation occurs two years after the company initially announced construction plans for the new EV battery supplier.
- In June 2023, South Korean officials announced intentions to construct the facility.
- The planned facility was intended to offer 190 jobs.
- The site is located in the I-40 Industrial Park.
- The I-40 Industrial Park is located near Ford Motor Company’s BlueOval City in Haywood County.
- In August 2025, Enchem delayed construction on the facility prior to its cancellation.
- State Rep. Chris Todd attributed the project cancellation to market changes in the EV and battery markets caused by shifting consumer demand.
- State Rep. Chris Todd noted that consumers' purchasing preferences change regularly, requiring manufacturers to build what people want to buy.
- State Rep. Chris Todd stated that the property is a certified site through the state's site development program and should revert back to the community for marketing to other companies.
- State Rep. Chris Todd explained that parameters around certified sites help prospects know what they are getting and how to plan for their facilities.
