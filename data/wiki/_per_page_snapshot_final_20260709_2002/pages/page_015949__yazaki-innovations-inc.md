# Yazaki Innovations, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-01-07

**Source:** [Yazaki Innovations Showcases Leading-Edge Busbar Technology at CES 2025 - Yazaki](https://www.yii.tech/newsroom/yazaki-innovations-showcases-leading-edge-busbar-technology-at-ces-2025/)  ·  `page_015949`

## Facts

- Yazaki Innovations, Inc. (YII), the tech-disrupting subsidiary of Yazaki North America, unveiled a new line of busbars for electric vehicles, mobility, and industrial applications at CES 2025 in Las Vegas, NV on Jan. 7, 2025.
- The newly unveiled busbars are engineered with cutting-edge technology to offer exceptional lightweight construction and efficiency.
- Swarnjeet Dhaliwal, director of innovation at Yazaki Innovations, stated that the revolutionary line of busbars can be a game changer for EV manufacturers and industrial applications moving away from traditional approaches to moving electrical power.
- The busbars are crafted from advanced materials, making them substantially lighter than traditional models.
- The lightweight design results in reduced transportation costs, simplified installation with easier handling and faster deployment, and improved maneuverability for complex installations and tight spaces.
- The innovative design maximizes current flow while minimizing energy loss, leading to reduced energy consumption, improved system reliability by minimizing heat generation and potential electrical faults, and enhanced system performance enabling higher power delivery with minimal losses.
- The busbars are built to withstand demanding applications, offering exceptional corrosion resistance, high mechanical strength to withstand vibrations and mechanical stresses, and superior thermal stability across a wide range of operating temperatures.
- YII’s parent company, Yazaki, is a global leader in the research, development, and delivery of vehicle power and data solutions for automotive applications.
- Yazaki produces wire harnesses, power distribution & control products, connectors, driver information systems, sensors, and hybrid & electric vehicle products.
- Yazaki's products can be found on nearly every vehicle on the road today.
