# Club Car

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-08-01

## Facts

- Club Car is located in Augusta, Georgia, United States.
- President and Chief Executive Officer Mark Wagner announced his retirement effective August 1, 2025.
- Wagner served as President and CEO for eight years during a 29-year career with the company.
- In an employee letter, Wagner stated: “We have achieved remarkable milestones, navigated historic challenges, and built a culture – a strong Black & Gold family.”
- Wagner noted that current new product launches and tariff success require commitment to achieve growth plans and share gains this year.
- Club Car is owned by global investment firm Platinum Equity.
- Platinum Equity will conduct a comprehensive search for a successor with plans for a seamless transition.
- Club Car is an Executive Member of the Asian Golf Industry Federation.
