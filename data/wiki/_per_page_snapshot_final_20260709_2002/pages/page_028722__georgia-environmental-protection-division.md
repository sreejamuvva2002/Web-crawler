# Georgia Environmental Protection Division

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-21

**Source:** [EPD Public Advisory and Public Notice for the Week of July 21, 2025 (PA0725-4)](https://content.govdelivery.com/accounts/GADNR/bulletins/3eacdd3)  ·  `page_028722`

## Facts

- The Georgia Environmental Protection Division Air Protection Branch issued a public advisory for air quality permit applications for the week of July 21, 2025.
- Johns Manville Corp. Winder Facility in Barrow County applied for Air Quality Permit No. 923756 to construct a new non-bonded insulation manufacturing line at 713 Bankhead Hwy, Winder, 30680.
- Sewon America, Inc. in Effingham County applied for Air Quality Permit No. 29785 to construct and operate an E-coating process line at 1000 Sewon EV Rd, Rincon, 31326.
- LX Hausys America, Inc. in Gordon County applied for Air Quality Permit No. 932921 to change emissions control paths for Process Line L001 - HiMACS at 310 LX Dr SE, Adairsville, 30103.
- Lunawood, LLC in White County applied for Air Quality Permit No. 29794 to construct and operate a wood treatment facility with two thermo vacuum kiln systems at 176 Appalachian Trail Drive, Cleveland, 30528.
- Hanwha Q Cells USA, Inc. in Whitfield County applied for Air Quality Permit No. 29791 to reclassify its status from synthetic minor to Title V major at 300 Nexus Dr, Dalton, 30721.
- Chemical Products Corporation in Bartow County was proposed a renewal of its Title V Operating Permit (No. 616365) for its inorganic chemical manufacturing plant that processes barite ore and celestite ore at 102 Old Mill Rd, Cartersville, 301201692.
- Milliken & Co Valway Plant in Troup County was proposed a renewal of its Title V Operating Permit (No. 690941) for its fabric finishing and coating facility at 1300 Fourth Ave, LaGrange, 30240.
- Milliken & Co Valway Plant in Troup County was also proposed a Title V Significant Modification (No. 877082) to construct and operate two boilers rated at 21 MMBtu/hr each.
- The Milliken & Co Valway Plant modification includes emission increases of +17.9 tpy NOx, +15.1 tpy CO, +1.0 tpy VOC, +1.36 tpy PM/PM10/PM2.5, +0.1 tpy SO2, +0.3 tpy single HAP (Hexane), and +0.3 tpy combined HAP.
- The comment deadline for SIP public advisories is August 22, 2025.
- The comment period/deadline for Milliken & Co Valway Plant permit renewal expires on August 25, 2025.
- The comment period/deadline for Milliken & Co Valway Plant Title V significant modification expires on August 25, 2025.
- Written comments can be submitted via email or by mail to the Air Permit Manager at 4244 International Parkway, Suite 120, Atlanta, Georgia 30354.
- Draft permits and supporting materials are available for review at the EPD Air Protection Branch office in Atlanta by appointment only.
- Final issued permits are available for public viewing online.
- Further information can be obtained via email or phone contact with the agency.
- Copies of draft permits and applications may also be requested by contacting Steve Allison.
