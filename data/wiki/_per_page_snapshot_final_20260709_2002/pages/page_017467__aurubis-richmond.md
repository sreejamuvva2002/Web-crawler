# Aurubis Richmond

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2025-09

**Source:** [German metal recycler Aurubis starts smelting at $800M Augusta plant](https://www.ajc.com/business/2025/09/georgia-factory-melts-metals-to-return-them-to-the-high-tech-supply-chain/)  ·  `page_017467`

## Facts

- Aurubis Richmond is a nearly $800 million recycling facility located in Augusta, Georgia.
- The Augusta plant began smelting last week according to a news release.
- The facility aims to play a critical role in recycling metals for fast-growing electronic and battery uses, including EVs, data centers, and energy infrastructure.
- The Augusta plant was first announced four years ago.
- It is the first facility of its kind in the U.S., smelting both common and rare metals for high-tech sectors.
- Toralf Haag, Aurubis CEO, stated that 'With Aurubis Richmond, we are the front-runner in multimetal recycling in the U.S.'
- Aurubis is a German company.
- The new Augusta facility is turning up the heat on Georgia’s fast-growing energy sector and electric vehicle supply chain.
