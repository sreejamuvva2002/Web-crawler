# Nivel Manufacturing

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Nivel Manufacturing is a 110,000 sq ft. state-of-the-art facility based in Cairo, GA which produces Nivel’s Made in the USA products.
- The facility utilizes CNC Band Saws, CNC Horizontal Mills, CNC Lathes, CNC Plasma Table, CNC Press Brakes, CNC Routers, CNC Vertical Mills, NC Tube Benders, NC Tube Notches, a Small Batch Powder Coater, a Thermo former, Welding Cells, and Wire benders.
- Nivel Manufacturing produces brake kits, brush guards, bumpers, club protectors, enclosures, lift kits, shifters, tops/assemblies, valances, and windshields for PTVs and UTVs.
- Through the Arcos division, Nivel provides customers the ability to create custom solutions for their PTVs, combining tradition and innovation.
- Nivel offers tailored solutions on a commercial scale leveraging its unique, U.S. based manufacturing capabilities.
