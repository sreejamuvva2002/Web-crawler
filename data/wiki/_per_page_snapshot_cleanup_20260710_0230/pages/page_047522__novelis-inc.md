# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-03-31

**Source:** [Novelis Inc. - FORM 10-K - May 8, 2018](https://getfilings.com/sec-filings/180508/Novelis-Inc_10-K/)  ·  `page_047522`

## Facts

- Novelis is the leading producer of flat-rolled aluminum products and the world's largest recycler of aluminum.
- The company provides innovative solutions to the beverage can, automotive, and high-end specialty markets.
- As of March 31, 2018, Novelis had manufacturing operations in ten countries on four continents through 24 operating facilities, including recycling operations in eleven of these plants.
- Fiscal year 2018 shipment volumes reached 3,333 kt with net sales of $11,462 million for the year ended March 31, 2018.
- All common shares are owned indirectly by Hindalco Industries Limited, which acquired Novelis in May 2007.
- The company's principal executive offices are located at 3560 Lenox Road, Suite 2000, Atlanta, GA 30326.
- Aluminum rolled products serve as raw material for vehicle structures (body-in-white) and automotive body panel applications including hoods, deck lids, fenders, and lift gates.
- Growth in passenger and commercial vehicle applications is driven by lighter weight, better fuel economy, and improved emissions performance.
- Novelis anticipates increased growth in the transportation end-use market due to government regulations requiring improved emissions and fuel economy.
- Heat exchangers like radiators and air conditioners are key transportation applications for aluminum rolled products.
- Original equipment manufacturers utilize specially treated aluminum sheet for interior and exterior automotive applications.
- Novelis operates automotive finishing lines in North America, Europe, and Asia that are running near full production levels.
- In February 2018, Novelis announced a greenfield expansion in Guthrie, Kentucky to add approximately 200 kt of additional automotive finishing capacity in North America.
- Construction at the Guthrie, Kentucky facility is underway with an expected commissioning date in 2020.
- The company defines recycled content as total scrap metal used in production less melt loss, increasing from 33% to 57% between fiscal 2011 and fiscal 2018.
- Novelis collaborates with automotive customers to redesign alloys for higher recycled inputs and purchases aluminum scrap from closed-loop recycling systems.
- Aluminum competes with steel and composites in transportation, with substitution trends accelerated by fuel efficiency regulations and carbon emission reduction goals.
- The company's strategy focuses on defending its leadership in beverage can and automotive markets through quality, service, and innovative supply solutions.
- Novelis aims to maximize shareholder value via free cash flow generation and increased return on capital employed.
- The firm maintains a competitive cost structure by managing metal input costs and improving operational efficiencies across its global network.
- Novelis is the only company of its size offering high-recycled content aluminum sheet for beverage and specialty product customers.
- Primary competitors in the aluminum rolled products market include Alcoa, Arconic, Aleris, Constellium, Gränges, Maaden, UACJ, Amag Austria Metall, Companhia Brasileira de Alumínio, Hulamin, Norsk Hydro, and others across multiple regions.
- Novelis reports financials in U.S. dollars using daily exchange rates published by Citibank.
- The company faces competition from substitute materials such as glass, PET plastic, steel, wood, cement, magnesium, copper, and carbon fiber depending on the application.
