## Facts

- Eaton Corporation plc is an American-Irish multinational power management company with a primary administrative center in Beachwood, Ohio.
- The eMobility sector combines elements of Eaton's electrical and vehicle businesses to deliver electric vehicles to passenger car, commercial vehicle and off-highway OEMs.
- The Mobility Group comprises the Vehicle and eMobility segments, including the Roadranger division.
- Eaton produces clutches, automated and mechanical transmissions, and hybrid power systems.
- Eaton's hybrid power systems are mounted between the UltraShift automated manual transmission and clutch, featuring an electric motor/generator connected to a power inverter using lithium-ion batteries, controlled with an electronic control module.
- The system has a fail-safe that reverts to conventional engine-powered operation should some fault occur.
- Eaton's truck segment is involved in the design, manufacture and marketing of powertrain systems and other components for commercial vehicle markets.
- Key products for the truck segment include manual and automated transmissions, clutches, drive-line components, and hybrid power.
- Eaton's automotive segment produces superchargers, engine valves, valve train components, cylinder heads, locking and limited-slip differentials, heavy-duty drive-line components, fuel, emissions, and safety controls, transmission and engine controls, spoilers, exterior moldings, plastic components, and fluid connectors.
- Eaton's revenue was US$24.88 billion in 2024.
- Eaton had 94,000 employees as of 2024.
