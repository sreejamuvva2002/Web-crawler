# Aspen Aerogels (NYSE: ASPN)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Aspen Aerogels to Open Advanced Manufacturing Facility in Bulloch County | Development Authority of Bulloch County](https://advantagebulloch.com/news/article/aspen-aerogels-announcement)  ·  `page_052740`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced on 02.17.2022 that Aspen Aerogels plans to construct an advanced manufacturing facility at Bulloch County’s Southern Gateway Commerce Park.
- Aspen intends to invest a minimum of $325 million in the new project.
- The company plans to create more than 250 advanced manufacturing jobs at the site near Statesboro in southeast Georgia.
- Production at the new facility is expected to begin in late 2023.
- Hiring for personnel will commence in the second half of 2022 across skilled manufacturing operators, advanced process engineers, quality and maintenance technicians, and professional support roles.
- The planned Georgia facility will triple Aspen’s nameplate aerogel capacity when combined with its original East Providence, Rhode Island plant.
- The project supports Aspen’s thermal barrier expansion plans in the fast-growing electric vehicle market.
- Aspen aims to capitalize on increased demand for its Aerogel Technology Platform™ as EV OEMs adopt PyroThin® aerogel thermal barrier solutions.
- Don Young, President and CEO of Aspen, cited the region's access to efficient ports, strong available workforce, and secure low-cost utilities and critical raw materials as key siting factors.
- PyroThin materials provide thermal management, mechanical stability, and fire protection properties to EV battery systems.
- Aspen plans to build a 500,000-square-foot advanced manufacturing facility situated on a 90-acre parcel in southeast Georgia.
- Billy Allen, Vice Chair of the Development Authority of Bulloch County, highlighted the region's site and logistics advantages and its commitment to innovation in the electric mobility ecosystem.
- Georgia Southern University and Ogeechee Technical College are expected to provide critical education resources to support the workforce.
- Assistant Director Alyce Thornhill represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s Global Commerce division on the project.
- The project was developed in partnership with the Development Authority of Bulloch County, Georgia Quick Start, and Georgia EMC.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson noted that Georgia employees will collaborate with Aspen’s network of scientists, engineers, and skilled workforce to address mobility challenges.
- Southern Gateway Commerce Park is a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) Certified park requiring due diligence and specific certification requirements prior to business visits.
- Aspen Aerogels is headquartered in Northborough, MA, and currently manufactures products at its East Providence, R.I. facilities.
- The company’s carbon aerogel program seeks to increase lithium-ion battery cell performance to extend EV driving range and reduce vehicle costs.
- Aspen’s Spaceloft® sustainable building materials provide industry-leading energy efficiency and fire safety to building owners.
- Cryogel® and Pyrogel® products are valued by the world's largest energy infrastructure companies.
- Aspen’s strategy is to partner with world-class industry leaders to leverage its Aerogel Technology Platform™ into additional high-value markets.
