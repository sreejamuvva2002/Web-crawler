# Enchem America Inc.

## Facts

- Enchem America Inc. has announced plans to build a new electrolyte manufacturing facility in Brownsville, Tennessee.
- The $152.5 million facility will be the company’s second manufacturing plant in the U.S.
- The facility is expected to create 190 new jobs for the area.
- The facility will be used to manufacture electrolytes for electric vehicle (EV) batteries.
- Jung Kang Oh, CEO and founder of Enchem Co. Ltd., thanked Haywood County, the City of Brownsville, and all professionals that assisted Enchem in its journey as it expands its presence in North America.
- Jung Kang Oh stated that the growth of the EV market in recent years has been truly remarkable and indicated that indications suggest this trend will continue at an accelerated pace over the coming decade.
- Jung Kang Oh expressed confidence that Enchem is well positioned to further capitalize on the growth of the EV market.
- Jung Kang Oh stated that Enchem looks forward to exploring new avenues of innovation and partnership opportunities with Haywood County and the City of Brownsville.
