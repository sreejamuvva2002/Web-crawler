# EVCO Plastics

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- EVCO Plastics is a global plastic injection molder with world-class facilities in the United States, Mexico and China.
- The company applies consistent and stringent ISO-certified standards in every location.
- Each facility is staffed locally, offering expertise in plastic injection molding, tooling, production, quality and logistics.
- Each facility is fully supported and connected with a centralized design and engineering team.
- All facilities provide the same high level of quality and service regardless of where products are produced.
- The company uses a global project management system that allows OEMs to develop projects locally in the United States and then source and manufacture them in any one or more globally located facilities.
- OEMs save shipping costs by molding large parts close to where they are needed.
- Smaller parts requiring more assembly or a greater amount of labor may be more cost-effective produced in Mexico or China.
- In some instances, there are advantages to running production in more than one location.
- Each facility is equipped for contract manufacturing, enabling local sourcing and cost savings.
- All molds are designed and built such that any molds built in the United States or China can run at any of the company's facilities.
- The company provides a globally focused plastic injection molding system equipped to provide best cost savings and competitive advantage.
