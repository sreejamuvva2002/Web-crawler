# Bridgestone Bandag Manufacturing Plant, Abilene

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Bridgestone to expand Texas retread tire plant | Tire Technology International](https://www.tiretechnologyinternational.com/news/manufacturing-facilities/bridgestone-to-expand-texas-retread-tire-plant.html)  ·  `page_018215`

## Facts

- An official ground-breaking ceremony was held at Bridgestone Bandag’s manufacturing plant in Abilene, Texas.
- The facility is undergoing a 50,000ft2 expansion costing U$60m.
- The investment supports growth in demand for the company’s tread rubber products.
- The site will grow from 200,000ft2 to 250,000ft2 through the expansion.
- Operations at the site will run on six and seven-day production schedules.
- New mixing operations will be built as part of the expansion.
- The new mixing operations are estimated to be in full operation by January 2025.
- The expansion will increase the plant’s output by 16%.
- The expansion provides capacity required for future expansion projects.
- Billy Fralick serves as the plant manager at the Abilene Plant.
- Chris Daniel serves as the executive director of Bandag Manufacturing, Bridgestone Americas.
- Bridgestone and its Abilene team are focused on building greener retreads to help maximize fuel efficiency for fleets.
