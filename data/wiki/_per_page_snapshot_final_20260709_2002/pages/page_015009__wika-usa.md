# WIKA USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Partnership Gwinnett and Gwinnett Technical College selected WIKA USA as Large Manufacturer of the Year for 2021.
- WIKA USA was a finalist in the Large Manufacturers (200+ employees) category for Georgia Power’s 2019 Movers & Makers Manufacturing and Supply Chain Awards, competing against 14 other companies.
- WIKA USA employs 800-plus workers at its Lawrenceville headquarters in Gwinnett County, Georgia.
- A sizable portion of WIKA USA’s workforce consists of first-generation immigrants.
- In 2020, WIKA USA manufactured pressure transducers for anesthesia machines, pressure gauges for oxygen manifolds, and pressure sensors for ventilators.
- WIKA USA maintains close educational partnerships with Gwinnett Technical College, located three minutes from its Lawrenceville headquarters.
- Through its partnership with Gwinnett Technical College, WIKA USA collaborates on training programs in welding and management.
- Business students at Gwinnett Technical College study WIKA USA’s lean manufacturing and Kaizen approach, which the company refers to as 'WIKA-tizing'.
- Gwinnett Technical College assists WIKA USA with customer-service training for its employees.
- WIKA USA has close ties with Georgia Gwinnett College, which is the alma mater of two of the company's graduate trainees.
- Zippia.com named WIKA USA the #1 Top Overall company to work for in Lawrenceville.
- WIKA USA is a family-owned manufacturer that contributes to Gwinnett County’s economic wealth.
- The company participates in civic initiatives including Toys for Tots, canned food drives, the Bed Race to end homelessness, and encouraging employee involvement in From Hunger to Hope during work hours.
