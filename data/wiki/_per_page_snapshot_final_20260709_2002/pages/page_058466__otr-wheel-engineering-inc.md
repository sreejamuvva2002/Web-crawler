# OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc. - About OTR](https://www.otrb2b.com/about-otr)  ·  `page_058466`

## Facts

- OTR’s product portfolio includes tires, wheels, assemblies, tracks, tubes, ballast and more.
- The tire lineup comprises over 1,700 models and 300+ distinctive treads, with bias and radial pneumatic (directional and non-directional); semi-pneumatic; solid (directional and non-directional); non-marking; and airless.
- The portfolio includes over 1,000 wheels, with single- and multi-piece versions in steel and aluminum, as well as cast disc units.
- There are over 100,000 standard items available, many of which can be customized for unique applications.
- In 1987, OTR Wheel Engineering was founded in Rome, Georgia by Fred B. Taylor.
- OTR has been a long-standing preferred supplier to AGCO since 1997 and an instrumental part of their supply chain to grow their AG equipment business in North America.
- In 2020, AGCO introduced OTR as its wheel and tire supplier for its cornerstone small HP tractor range.
- OTR has supported Honda since 2016 and expanded its scope in 2019 to include mounting for Honda's Side by Side lines.
- OTR wheels are used on CAT 795 haul trucks at Nevada Gold Mines mine sites, specifically running 7 piece Speed Wheels and 5 piece standard wheels.
- Aros Chile has worked with OTR Wheel Engineering for more than 15 years as an essential supplier in the mining wheel category, manufacturing wheels for CAT Haulers and Loaders.
- DCML uses OTR products on Komatsu 830 E dump trucks at Vale Mining Carajas and CAT 777 haul trucks at Vale Mining South, noting that product quality ranks among the very best in the industry.
- In 1995, a facility was opened in Chambersburg, Pennsylvania for first major OEM customer JLG, launching a complete line of aerial work platform tires.
- In 1996, OTR Canada was formed via acquisition of the business Northshore.
- In 1997, Silverstone was acquired and is based in Omaha, Nebraska.
- In 1997, a facility was opened in Ackerman, Mississippi for OEM customer Taylor Machine Works.
- In 1999, OTR Wheel in Quincy, Illinois was acquired and folded into OTR Wheel Engineering, providing additional manufacturing capability.
- In 2005, OTR China was established in Wuxi.
- In 2005, a facility was opened in Shady Grove, Pennsylvania to support Grove International.
- In 2005, the company moved to a new facility in Louisville, Mississippi.
- In 2007, OTR Europe was established in Derby.
- In 2009, OTR Thailand was established in Chon Buri.
- In 2011, OTR Sri Lanka was established in Colombo.
- In 2013, Long’s Wheel & Rim was acquired.
- In 2015, SolidBoss was acquired.
- In 2016, the Rome facility was expanded to include new corporate headquarters.
- In 2017, Southern Wheel & Rim was acquired and OTR International was formed; Silver Diamond Services (SDS) in Mansfield, TX and Trans American Rubber (TAR) in Denver, CO were also acquired.
- In 2018, Fieldens OTR was acquired and established in Suffolk, U.K.
- In 2019, a partnership was established with Owners Resource Group with OTR configured as a member company.
- In 2019, OTR Germany was established in Sande, located near Wilhelmshaven in northeast Germany.
- In 2020, OTR Europe expanded to include operations in the Czech Republic.
- In 2020, SolidBoss OTR U.S. East operations were established.
- In 2021, Silverstone and its businesses, SDS and Trans American Rubber, were fully integrated into OTR, forming the basis for OTR U.S. (with SolidBoss included).
- In 2021, Tom Rizzi became the new CEO and president of OTR.
