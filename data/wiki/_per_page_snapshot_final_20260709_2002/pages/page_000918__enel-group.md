# Enel Group

## Facts

- Offers public and private charging solutions capable of charging electric vehicles anywhere, anytime.
- Provides an interconnected network that is easily accessible and bookable via smartphone.
- Maintains a widespread network of more than 70,000 charging points around the world.
- Ensures reliable and accessible service for worry-free charging wherever users are.
- Public EV charging services are offered by various operators or Mobility Service Providers (MSPs).
- Each provider allows customers to view a map of their EV charging stations on their website or dedicated app.
- The Enel map shows the charging infrastructure of MSPs with whom Enel has interoperability agreements, along with their status.
- Interoperability agreements allow Enel's customers to access a network of hundreds of thousands of charging points through an Enel service subscription.
- The Enel On Your Way app lets users manage and monitor charging activity while locating stations via an interactive map.
- Users can find nearby charging stations and check availability in real time via smartphone or car infotainment screen.
- Charging station maps provide visual clues to show station status, such as in use, reserved, under maintenance, or available.
- Operational charging points appear green on the Enel On Your Way app map.
- Clicking an operational point allows users to check availability of various charging points and supported socket or connector types.
- The Enel On Your Way app is at the heart of an ecosystem of products and services for monitoring and managing charging sessions with a simple tap.
- Offers dedicated, flexible, and customizable solutions for corporate clients that enable monitoring of electric vehicles and charging stations and simplify corporate fleet management.
