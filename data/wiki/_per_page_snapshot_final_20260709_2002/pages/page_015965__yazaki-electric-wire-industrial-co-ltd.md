# Yazaki Electric Wire Industrial Co. Ltd.

## Facts

- Sadami Yazaki began selling wiring harnesses for automobiles in 1929.
- Governmental regulations changed in 1935, allowing Japanese companies to start domestic automotive production, which had positive effects for Yazaki.
- The business expanded in 1939.
- Yazaki Electric Wire Industrial Co. Ltd. was established in 1941 with about 70 employees.
- In 1949, Sadami Yazaki made a strategic decision to focus on the production of automotive wiring harnesses.
- This decision resulted in Yazaki's reputation as a global leader today.
- Yazaki developed equipment for the city gas industry and the world's first solar-powered absorption cooling system in 1974.
- The company has developed and provided products that support electricity transmission cables, gas security systems, air-conditioning equipment, and absorption chillers.
- These products are integrated under the environment & energy equipment operations.
- The environment & energy equipment operations is the second largest Yazaki Group organization.
