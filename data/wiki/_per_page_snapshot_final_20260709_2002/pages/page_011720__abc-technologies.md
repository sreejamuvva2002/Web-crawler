# ABC Technologies

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-01

**Source:** [ABC Technologies acquires TI Fluid Systems | Automotive Powertrain Technology International](https://www.automotivepowertraintechnologyinternational.com/news/partnerships-investments-acquisitions/abc-technologies-acquires-ti-fluid-systems.html)  ·  `page_011720`

## Facts

- ABC Technologies (ABC) has announced the completion of its acquisition of TI Fluid Systems (TIFS).
- The acquisition was completed for an enterprise value of over £1.8bn (US$2.38bn).
- The combined business will be rebranded as TI Automotive.
- The transaction brings together two complementary businesses to create a stronger, more diversified Tier 1 manufacturer supplying safety and performance critical products and highly engineered components.
- TI Automotive will be led by ABC president and CEO Terry Campbell.
- TI Automotive will be headquartered in Auburn Hills, Michigan.
- TI Automotive will be managed through a regional operating model to ensure customer proximity and the agility to respond rapidly to local market dynamics.
- ABC president and CEO Terry Campbell stated that the company has exceptional people, differentiated products, a deep commitment to safety, and a relentless focus on delivering for customers.
- Terry Campbell added that the combination creates opportunities to drive innovation and manufacturing excellence while establishing foundations for future growth.
