# Vista Metals

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-03

**Source:** [Vista Metals Introduces the Largest Forging Ingot in the Western World - Light Metal Age Magazine](https://www.lightmetalage.com/news/industry-news/casthouse/vista-metals-introduces-the-largest-forging-ingot-in-the-western-world/)  ·  `page_013674`

## Facts

- Vista Metals is located in Adairsville, Georgia.
- Vista Metals introduced the largest forging ingot in the Western World.
- The new forging ingot was cast at a 52-inch (1,321 mm) diameter with a weight of approximately 50,000 lbs (22,680 kg).
- This represents the largest raw material input weight for forging applications currently offered in the market.
- After being cast the ingot was finished at 48 inches (1,219 mm) diameter and weighed up to 30,000 lbs (13,608 kg).
- The billet was developed to supply aluminum forging applications serving the needs of the aerospace, industrial, and power generation sectors.
- Bob Praefke is the executive vice president of Vista Metals.
- Vista Metals anticipates market needs and is aggressively investing in the future.
- The company's Georgia location works closely with customers to bring this product to various applications today.
- Vista Metals has the industry’s widest range of sizes and configurations with over 270 alloy and size combinations across its product offerings.
- Product offerings include cast and scalped forging ingot, hard and soft alloy extrusion billet, rolling slab ingot, tooling and mold plate, redraw rod, and extruded forge stock.
- VMC Specialty Alloys LLC (dba Vista Metals Georgia) operates under a licensing agreement with Fontana, California-based Vista Metals Corp.
- Along with Vista Progressive Metals LLC, the group of companies are market leaders in the manufacturing of specialty aluminum products supporting aerospace, commercial aircraft, defense, automotive, and general industrial applications.
- Vista Progressive Metals LLC is a producer of aluminum lithium products.
