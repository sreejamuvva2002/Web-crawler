# Daimler Truck North America LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-10-24

**Source:** [Women In Trucking Association Announces Continued Partnership with Daimler Truck North America](https://www.womenintrucking.org/press-releases/women-in-trucking-association-announces-continued-partnership-with-daimler-truck-north-america)  ·  `page_053634`

## Facts

- Daimler Truck North America renewed its Gold Level Partnership with the Women In Trucking Association to support gender diversity in transportation and logistics.
- Daimler Truck North America has been an essential partner of the Women In Trucking Association since 2012.
- The partnership includes sponsorship of the annual Influential Woman in Trucking Award for female industry leaders.
- Kary Schaefer, general manager of Product Strategy and Market Development for Daimler Truck North America, serves on the Women In Trucking Association board of directors.
- Ellen Voie serves as president and CEO of the Women In Trucking Association.
- The Women In Trucking Association was founded in 2007 and serves over 7,000 corporate and individual members across the United States, Canada, Mexico, Japan, Australia, Sweden, South Africa, and New Zealand.
- The association released the 2022 WIT Index to benchmark the percentage of women in critical transportation roles.
- The percentage of professional female drivers increased to 13.7% according to recent data.
- The Women In Trucking Association participated in White House and FMCSA roundtables and events.
- The association launched the Professional Driver Hub, an online resource for driver success.
- The 2021 Accelerate! Conference and Exhibition had over 800 registered attendees.
- The Women In Trucking Association is a nonprofit encouraging women's employment in trucking, with 17 percent of its members being men.
- Gold Level Partners supporting the association include Amazon, Arrow Truck Sales, Daimler Truck North America, DAT Solutions, FedEx Freight, Great Dane, J.B. Hunt Transport, Michelin North America, PACCAR, Penske Transportation Solutions, Ryder System, Walmart, and WM.
- Daimler Truck North America LLC is headquartered in Portland, Oregon.
- Daimler Truck North America designs, engineers, manufactures, and markets medium- and heavy-duty trucks, school buses, vehicle chassis, and associated technologies and components under the Freightliner, Western Star, Thomas Built Buses, Freightliner Custom Chassis Corp, and Detroit brands.
- Daimler Truck North America is a subsidiary of Daimler Truck Holding AG (DTG), one of the world’s leading commercial vehicle manufacturers.
