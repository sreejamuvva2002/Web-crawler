# TE Connectivity plc

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Sam Eldessouky Appointed To TE Connectivity Board Of Directors](https://menafn.com/1108748034/Sam-Eldessouky-Appointed-To-TE-Connectivity-Board-Of-Directors)  ·  `page_060291`

## Facts

- TE Connectivity plc is a global industrial technology leader creating connectivity and sensor solutions.
- TE's connectivity and sensor solutions enable the distribution of power, signal, and data to advance next-generation transportation, renewable energy, automated factories, data centers, and medical technology.
- TE has more than 85,000 employees, including 8,000 engineers, working alongside customers in approximately 140 countries.
- Sam Eldessouky has been appointed to TE Connectivity's board of directors.
- TE Connectivity Chairman John Davidson stated that Eldessouky brings valuable expertise to the board, including accounting and financial leadership at public companies.
- Eldessouky has served in his current role at Bausch + Lomb Corporation, a global company developing, manufacturing, and marketing products primarily in eye health, since January 2022.
- Eldessouky previously served as executive vice president and chief financial officer of Bausch Health Companies from 2021 to 2022.
- From 2016 to 2021, Eldessouky was controller and chief accounting officer at Bausch Health Companies, responsible for overseeing global controllership functions including financial reporting, regional finance, and global policies.
- Eldessouky spent more than 12 years at Tyco International plc, most recently serving as senior vice president, controller, and chief accounting officer from 2012 to 2016.
- Prior to Tyco, Eldessouky spent 10 years at PwC holding roles of increasing responsibility and serving in the National Office providing technical accounting guidance on complex accounting matters.
- Eldessouky holds a Bachelor of Science in accountancy from Ain Shams University and a master's degree in accounting and finance from the University of Liverpool.
- Eldessouky is a certified public accountant (inactive) and chartered global management accountant.
