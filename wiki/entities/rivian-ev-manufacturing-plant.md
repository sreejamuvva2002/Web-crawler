# Rivian EV Manufacturing Plant

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Stanton Springs, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Clayco will build Rivian’s 1800-acre state-of-the-art manufacturing plant at Stanton Springs, Georgia. The facility is slated to be operational by 2028 and to begin vehicle production in 2026, creating approximately 7,500 employment opportunities. It will house stamping, body shop, paint shop, wastewater treatment plant, general assembly building, battery and drive unit shop, central utility plant, office and main entry building, and other support facilities.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The plant will produce Rivian electric vehicles and includes a battery and drive-unit shop, directly supporting EV manufacturing in Georgia.
