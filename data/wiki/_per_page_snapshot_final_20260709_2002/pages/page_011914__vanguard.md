# Vanguard

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-02

## Facts

- Vanguard has added i3 Product Development to its Battery Technology Partner program, expanding engineering and integration support for OEMs developing battery-powered commercial equipment.
- The partnership combines Vanguard’s commercial-grade lithium-ion battery systems with i3 Product Development’s engineering and systems integration capabilities.
- The companies said the collaboration is intended to help OEMs move electrified equipment programs from concept toward production.
- Vanguard’s Battery Technology Partner program supports small- and mid-sized OEMs working with scalable battery systems.
- The program gives manufacturers access to Vanguard battery technology alongside engineering support for integrating battery power into equipment platforms.
- i3 Product Development, a Helios Technologies company based in Sun Prairie, Wisconsin, provides mechanical and electrical engineering, embedded systems, software development, industrial design, and prototyping services.
- The partnership is focused on off-highway and commercial equipment applications, including turf care, construction, material handling, and utility vehicles.
