## Facts

- Offers new Volvo sedans like the Volvo S60 Country, which features an innovative mild hybrid powertrain.
- Maintains a portfolio of pre-owned luxury vehicles, including Certified by Volvo cars and used luxury vehicles acquired as trade-ins.
