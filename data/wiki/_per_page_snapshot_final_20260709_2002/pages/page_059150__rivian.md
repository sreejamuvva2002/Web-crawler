## Facts

- Electric vehicle startup Rivian officially closed on a complicated land agreement Thursday to gain access to a 1,800-acre megasite an hour east of Atlanta.
- The automaker will soon begin vertical construction on its planned $5 billion EV factory.
- The Joint Development Authority (JDA) of Jasper, Morgan, Newton and Walton counties approved multiple resolutions earlier this week to finalize the lease agreement.
- JDA will issue bonds related to the project’s property tax financing.
- JDA owns the site within the Stanton Springs industrial park.
- JDA will rent the site to Rivian for 50 years.
- The planned EV plant will be 16 million square feet.
- Rivian is a California-based startup.
