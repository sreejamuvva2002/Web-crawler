## Facts

- HL-GA Battery Company LLC represents a groundbreaking collaboration between Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy Solution.
- Both parent companies are global leaders committed to driving innovation in the electric vehicle (EV) industry.
- The company is manufacturing state-of-the-art lithium-ion battery cells for electric vehicles to advance sustainable mobility.
- The facility is strategically located at the HMGMA Metaplant site near the city of Savannah, Georgia.
- Upon completion, the factory will boast a production capacity of 30GWh.
- The 30GWh capacity is enough to power 300,000 electric vehicles annually.
- HL-GA Battery Co. is listed as a Trustee Member and categorized under Aerospace, Auto Industry Suppliers by the Savannah Chamber.
- The facility signifies a transformative investment in EV battery technology, setting new standards in efficiency, sustainability, and innovation.
