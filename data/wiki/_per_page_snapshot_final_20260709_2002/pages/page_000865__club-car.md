# Club Car

## Facts

- Club Car is headquartered in Augusta, Georgia.
- Club Car is a global leader in golf, consumer, and utility vehicles with a history spanning over six decades.
- Club Car serves as the Worldwide Supplier of the Ryder Cup, marking 26 years of partnership that began at Valderrama, Spain in 1997.
- For the 2023 Ryder Cup held September 25 – October 1 at Marco Simone Golf & Country Club in Rome, Italy, Club Car assembled and delivered a fleet of 550 vehicles.
- The 2023 Ryder Cup fleet included six red and six blue team cars for captains Luke Donald and Zach Johnson and their vice-captains.
- The fleet also included hundreds of golf, turf, and utility cars supporting media, broadcasting, security, and medical transportation at the event.
- Club Car prepared and worked towards the 2023 Ryder Cup event over a four-year period.
- Kevin Hart is the Golf Sales Director for Club Car in Europe, Middle East, and Africa.
- Paul Dunstan is the Ryder Cup Operations Director.
- For the first time at the Ryder Cup, the majority of Club Car vehicles were charged using solar-powered stations on-site.
- The Ryder Cup has a sustainability policy and commitment to be carbon neutral by 2040.
- Club Car supplies many of the world’s top golf destinations with sustainable electric vehicles.
- Club Car is an official supplier to the DP World Tour, PGA, and St Andrews Links Trust.
- The Club Car product portfolio includes vehicles for commercial, personal, and street-legal low speed use.
- The Club Car Vehicle Group includes the luxury electric vehicle brand Garia and the lightweight electric utility vehicle brand Melex.
