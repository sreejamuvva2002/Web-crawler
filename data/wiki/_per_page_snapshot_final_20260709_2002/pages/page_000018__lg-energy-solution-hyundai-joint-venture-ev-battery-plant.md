# LG Energy Solution-Hyundai Joint Venture EV Battery Plant

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-25

**Source:** [Sen. Ossoff: LG Energy Solution & Hyundai to Build $4.3 Billion EV Battery Plant in Georgia, Create 3,000 Jobs – U.S. Senator for Georgia Jon Ossoff](https://www.ossoff.senate.gov/press-releases/sen-ossoff-lg-energy-solutions-hyundai-to-build-4-3-billion-ev-battery-plant-in-georgia-create-3000-jobs/)  ·  `page_000018`

## Facts

- U.S. Senator Jon Ossoff announced that LG Energy Solution and Hyundai will jointly build a $4.3 billion EV battery plant in Bryan County, Georgia.
- The two companies will jointly invest $4.3 billion to open the new EV battery plant, which will supply Hyundai’s EV manufacturing site in Bryan County.
- The investment is expected to create 3,000 jobs.
- According to the companies, full-scale production at the plant is expected to be online by the end of 2025.
- Sen. Ossoff announced the news on a Thursday, weeks after leading an April trade mission to South Korea where he met with senior executives from both firms.
- Sen. Ossoff met again in late April with LG Energy Solution executives in Washington to discuss the deal.
- Sen. Ossoff stated that securing the LG-Hyundai battery investment was a key goal of his trade delegation to South Korea and noted that the IRA’s manufacturing incentives continue to bring jobs and investment to Georgia.
- Sen. Ossoff expressed his goal to make Georgia the world leader in advanced energy production and thanked LG Chairman Koo Kwang-mo, LG Energy Solutions President David Kim, Hyundai Chairman Chung Eui-sun, Senator Reverend Warnock, and local/state officials for making the announcement possible.
- According to the Financial Times, Georgia ranks 2nd nationwide in new investments from IRA manufacturing incentives.
- Earlier this year, Sen. Ossoff announced another $2.5 billion investment in solar manufacturing by Qcells in Dalton and Cartersville, incentivized by his solar manufacturing legislation within the IRA.
