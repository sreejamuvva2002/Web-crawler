## Facts

- Mack partners with TRC to help customers access grants, vouchers, and incentives to reduce EV investment costs.
- The Incentive Finder is a smart tool that helps Mack customers unlock the full value of transitioning to electric vehicles.
- The Incentive Finder provides constantly updated information on federal, state, and local programs.
- The Incentive Finder identifies available grants, tax credits, rebates, and infrastructure funding tailored to fleet and location.
- Eligibility Guidance ensures Mack customers have clear, expert support when navigating the complex requirements tied to EV and infrastructure incentives.
- Mack provides step-by-step assistance to determine which opportunities apply to a fleet, whether applying for vehicle rebates, charger installation grants, or utility-funded programs.
- Mack’s team works directly with customers to guide the entire application process, including preparing documentation, meeting deadlines, submitting forms, and responding to agency requests.
- Mack leverages experience with federal, state, and utility programs to help customers avoid common pitfalls and improve approval chances.
- Mack customers can take advantage of point-of-sale incentives that significantly reduce the upfront cost of electric trucks and charging equipment.
- Mack works closely with authorized dealers and program administrators to streamline participation in state-run voucher programs.
- Examples of these state-run voucher programs include California’s HVIP and New York’s NYTVIP.
- Participation in these voucher programs ensures eligible fleets receive their discounts quickly and efficiently.
- Purchasing a Mack Electric Vehicle provides access to a coast-to-coast network of service, support, and over 120 years of trucking experience.
