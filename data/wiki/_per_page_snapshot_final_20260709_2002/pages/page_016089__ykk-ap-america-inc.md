## Facts

- YKK AP America Inc. announced the creation of its Manufacturing Excellence organization along with leadership appointments across commercial and residential manufacturing operations.
- Daymon Loyd will oversee key YKK AP manufacturing priorities including customer satisfaction, on-time delivery, quality, equipment reliability, process optimization, and strategic oversight of the Dublin plant’s investment and modernization programs.
- Mike Turner, president of Commercial Business at YKK AP America Inc., stated that Daymon’s appointment reflects a continued commitment to building a strong manufacturing organization positioned to support customers and long-term growth.
- Josh Jensen, president of Residential Business at YKK AP America Inc., emphasized that focused plant leadership is critical to delivering quality, consistency, and service in the residential business.
- Derek will step into the residential plant leadership position as a 10-year employee with experience in manufacturing engineering, process improvement, and quality control.
- YKK AP America Inc. is a leading manufacturer of award-winning commercial facade systems and residential windows and doors.
- The company is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- All building products are manufactured in production facilities located in Dublin, Ga., Macon, Ga., and Lakeshore, Ontario.
- Together with its subsidiary brand Erie Architectural Products (Erie AP), the company serves architects, glazing contractors, homebuilders, and resellers across the country.
- YKK AP America Inc. is a subsidiary of YKK AP Inc., a global architectural products company based in Tokyo, Japan.
