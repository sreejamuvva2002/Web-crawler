# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-06-18

**Source:** [Hyundai announces first vehicle produced at Metaplant - Bryan County News](https://www.bryancountynews.com/news/hyundai-announces-first-vehicle-produced-metaplant/)  ·  `page_060354`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) officially named the 2025 Hyundai IONIQ 5 as the first production vehicle manufactured at its new Metaplant in north Bryan County during a presentation at Savannah’s AutoNation Hyundai dealership.
- The 2025 all-electric SUV has yet to be officially released for North American markets.
- HMGMA is readying to begin production of the IONIQ 5 in the fourth quarter of this year (2024).
- The new Ellabell plant will produce all IONIQ 5 trim lines except the IONIQ 5 N.
- The Ellabell plant will eventually become the sole facility building IONIQ 5 models for the U.S. market.
- Joe LaMuralglia, head of communications at HMGMA, stated that the Hyundai IONIQ 5 is one of the most award-winning and popular EVs on sale today.
- The Hyundai IONIQ 5 has received numerous accolades from MotorTrend, Car and Driver, and U.S. News and World Report.
- Joe LaMuralglia described the production milestone as a monumental occasion for HMGMA and expressed pride in partnering with the local community for the future of mobility.
- 81% of employees hired so far at the Metaplant are native to southeast Georgia, living within 60 miles of the north Bryan facility.
- Hyundai Motor Group is investing $7.59 billion to develop HMGMA.
- The development of HMGMA will create 8,500 direct jobs.
- Hyundai and battery supplier SK On are currently building a $5 billion EV battery cell plant in Bartow County, Georgia.
- The SK On battery cell plant will employ 3,500 workers.
- Additional vehicles to be produced at the Metaplant will be announced in the future according to official sources.
- The Metaplant will produce Hyundai, Genesis, and Kia vehicles.
