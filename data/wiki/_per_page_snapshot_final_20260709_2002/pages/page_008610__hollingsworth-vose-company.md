## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose Company (H&V) is a global manufacturer of advanced materials used in filtration, battery, and industrial applications.
- The company's products include air and liquid filtration media, battery separators, and industrial nonwovens.
- H&V serves customers in the transportation, indoor air quality, and energy storage markets.
- The company was founded in 1843.
- The company is headquartered in East Walpole, Massachusetts.
