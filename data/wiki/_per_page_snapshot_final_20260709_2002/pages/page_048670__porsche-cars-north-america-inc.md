# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-08

**Source:** [Porsche and Nobu Hotel Atlanta debut immersive hospitality experience](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/porsche-nobu-hotel-atlanta-debut-140000808.html)  ·  `page_048670`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) and Nobu Hotel Atlanta teamed up to create a first-of-its-kind immersive hospitality experience for travelers.
- The collaboration features the new Porsche Icon Suite and Porsche Classic Room at the newly opened Nobu Hotel Atlanta in Buckhead, located just north of PCNA's U.S. headquarters.
- Ayesha Coker, Vice President of Marketing of PCNA, stated the goal is to provide an elevated experience for everyone who engages with the brand.
- Nobu Hotel Atlanta offers all-electric Porsche Taycan house cars expertly navigated by dedicated personal drivers for guest excursions within a three-mile radius.
- Rachael Palumbo, VP Global Hotel Branding at Nobu Hospitality, noted that Porsche's reputation as an automotive legend complements their global vision of delivering luxury hospitality.
- The Porsche Icon Suite and Porsche Classic Room are available to book now at Nobu Hotel Atlanta.
- Package excursions are subject to availability.
- PCNA has been headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia since 1998.
- The official address for PCNA is One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA.
- PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera and Taycan.
- The PCNA campus in Atlanta hosts the first Porsche Experience Center in North America, which features two module-based 1.6 mile driver development tracks, a business center, and Restaurant 356.
- The same Atlanta campus houses the U.S. headquarters of Porsche Classic.
- PCNA supports 197 independently owned and operated Porsche Centers in the U.S., including supplying parts, service, marketing, and training to them.
- These supported centers work to provide Porsche customers with a best-in-class experience aligned with the brand's 75-year history of leadership in vehicle performance, safety, and efficiency.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Porsche boasts a racing heritage that includes some 30,000-plus motorsport wins to date.
