# Boogook Georgia, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-29

**Source:** [From tobacco titan to auto innovator: Korean company breathes new life into historic Macon building](https://wgxa.tv/news/local/from-tobacco-titan-to-auto-innovator-korean-company-breathes-new-life-into-historic-macon-building)  ·  `page_053114`

## Facts

- Boogook Georgia, LLC is a Korean automotive supplier known for its expertise in fluid-carrying systems for vehicles.
- The company announced its entry into the United States market with the establishment of its first facility in Macon, Georgia.
- The manufacturer will be located on Weaver Road in Macon in the building that was once home to Brown and Williamson Tobacco.
- At the time, over 3,000 people worked in the B&W building when it was Macon's largest employer.
- Upon its opening in 2024, Boogook Georgia is projected to generate 80 full-time jobs, with further job growth anticipated in the future.
- These employment opportunities will provide the local community with professional roles in the automotive sector, spanning management, manufacturing, assembly, quality assurance, and delivery functions.
