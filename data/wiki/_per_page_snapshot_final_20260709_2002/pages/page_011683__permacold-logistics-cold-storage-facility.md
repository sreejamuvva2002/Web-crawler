# PermaCold Logistics Cold Storage Facility

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Gov. Kemp: Ti Cold and Karis Cold Building $60 Million Facility in McIntosh County](https://www.einpresswire.com/article/829878203/gov-kemp-ti-cold-and-karis-cold-building-60-million-facility-in-mcintosh-county)  ·  `page_011683`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Ti Cold and Karis Cold officially broke ground on a new $60 million cold storage facility for PermaCold Logistics in Darien, McIntosh County.
- The industrial cold storage construction project will support 50 jobs upon completion of phase one.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated the announcement is the largest known economic development win for McIntosh County and marks another success for rural Georgia.
- Ti Cold provides a full suite of cold storage construction and development services, including complete design and engineering, master site planning, operations guidance, and consulting services.
- Sam Tippmann serves as the Founder and CEO of Ti Cold.
- The new facility is located at Tidewater Industrial Park, a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) site, in Darien.
- The advanced cold storage complex will have a clear height of 50 feet and feature a flexible, convertible design that maintains temperatures ranging from 40 degrees to minus 10 degrees Fahrenheit.
- The facility will incorporate an environmentally friendly refrigeration system.
- Structural steel pallet racks will accommodate over 30,000 pallet positions.
- The facility will post more information regarding open positions throughout the construction process.
- Kate Pontello Karwacki is the Chairwoman of the McIntosh County Board of Commissioners.
- The project highlights the site's strategic location along the I-95 corridor and proximity to ports, alongside a welcoming business climate.
- Dorie Bacon, Senior Regional Project Manager, represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team on this project.
- GDEcD partnered with the McIntosh County Industrial Development Authority, Georgia Ports Authority, Georgia EMC, and Georgia Power for this development.
- Pat Wilson is the Commissioner of the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD).
- Ti Cold is a specialty, award-winning industrial cold storage builder and developer with decades of experience in designing and building state-of-the-art facilities.
- Karis Cold is a private investment and development company with expertise in the cold storage industry, investing in and developing properties across the U.S. with build-to-suit solutions, sale-leaseback financing, and expansions/redevelopment.
- Jessica Atwell serves as the Communication Manager for the Georgia Department of Economic Development.
