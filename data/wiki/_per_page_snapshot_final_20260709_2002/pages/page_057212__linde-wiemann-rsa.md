# Linde + Wiemann RSA

**Source:** [Linde + Wiemann RSA, East London – Graduate Development Programme Opportunity - Latest Learnerships, Internships & Jobs In South Africa 2026.](https://career27.co.za/2025/02/13/linde-wiemann-rsa-east-london-graduate-development-programme-opportunity/)  ·  `page_057212`

## Facts

- Linde + Wiemann RSA is inviting applications for a one-year Graduate Development Programme.
- The application closing date is 16th February 2025.
- Five (5) graduate positions are available across the Plant, Quality, Maintenance, and Manufacturing Engineering departments.
- The programme is designed to transform graduates into high-performing professionals through work exposure and on-the-job training at the East London Plant.
- Graduate trainees will be monitored throughout the 12-month period and receive mentorship and coaching from relevant Department Managers.
- Eligibility requires a completed degree or diploma-level qualification, one to two years of relevant work experience (advantageous), computer literacy, strong problem-solving abilities, and effective communication skills.
- Only applications including relevant qualifications, experience, and supporting documentation will be considered.
- Linde + Wiemann supports Employment Equity principles and advertises the position both internally and externally.
- Applicants who do not hear back within 14 days of the closing date should consider their application unsuccessful.
- The Plant Department is seeking an Industrial Engineer Graduate in Training to assist with day-to-day activities, requiring a National Diploma in Industrial Engineering, one to two years of experience (preferably in an Automotive Environment), Office proficiency, and advantageous AutoCAD & SolidWorks knowledge.
- The Quality Department is seeking a Quality Graduate in Training for receiving inspections, quality alerts, G8Ds, scrap analysis, supplier claims, performance reviews, and audits, requiring a National Diploma in Mechanical Engineering and Office proficiency.
- Two Manufacturing Engineering Graduates in Training are needed to provide technical and administrative support, requiring a National Diploma or Bachelor’s Degree in Industrial or Mechanical Engineering and Microsoft Office proficiency.
- The Maintenance Department (Assembly Plant) is seeking a Maintenance Graduate in Training for planned and corrective machinery maintenance, requiring a National Diploma in Electrical or Mechanical Engineering, ability to use workshop tools and electrical testers, engineering drawing interpretation skills, and Office proficiency.
- Working conditions require frequent walking or standing, hand use, visual inspections, occasionally lifting up to 15kgs, working at heights or in confined spaces, driving forklifts or boom lifts, and occasional extended hours.
- The work environment may involve exposure to chemicals, noise, dust, temperature changes, and cramped areas.
- Linde + Wiemann RSA is located at Ikhala Road, BW3 Building, Zone 1A, Sunnyridge, East London 5201.
