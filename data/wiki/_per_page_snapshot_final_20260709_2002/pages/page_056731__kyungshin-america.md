## Facts

- Kyungshin America connects technology and people, driving innovation through seamless connections between technology and mobility.
- Kyungshin Cable was founded in 1974 and has emerged as a leading player in South Korea’s automotive cable market.
- The company has expanded globally by introducing industrial cables and eco-friendly vehicle components to bring future value to customers through core and cutting-edge technologies.
- Kyungshin operates ten business sites across three continents, offering localized R&D and production capabilities worldwide.
- The company specializes in advanced automotive components, including wiring harnesses, high-voltage systems, connectors, and electronic junction blocks.
- Kyungshin's manufacturing facilities span from Incheon to Gyeongju, Gunsan, and Hwaseong.
- The company maintains an expanding international footprint with branches in the USA, Mexico, China, and India.
