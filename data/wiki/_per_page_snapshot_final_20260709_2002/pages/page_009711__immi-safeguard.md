# IMMI SafeGuard

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-11-22

**Source:** [IMMI and Blue Bird Celebrate Opening of New Manufacturing Plant Safeguard Immi | Remote Control Boat Races](https://remotecontrolboatraces2.pages.dev/posts/safeguard-immi/)  ·  `page_009711`

## Facts

- IMMI and Blue Bird Bus announced that beginning in October 2024, lap shoulder belts will be standard on their school buses.
- As demand increases for IMMI SafeGuard's lap-shoulder belt seats, IMMI expects to add more jobs to its workforce in Macon.
- IMMI SafeGuard manufactures school bus seats at its facility in High Point, North Carolina, supplying Thomas Built Buses.
- The company offers the SafeGuard Sabre lap-shoulder belt upgrade and Gen4 student lap belts for IC Bus, available in middle seating (approx 60" OAL) and aisle/wall configurations.
- IMMI's Center for Advanced Product Evaluation testing facility is located in High Point, North Carolina, where SafeGuard school bus seats undergo rigorous crash testing.
- SafeGuard STAR and SuperSTAR products feature a 5-point restraint system designed for preschool and Head Start children weighing 26.5 to 90 lbs.
- IMMI developed the SmartFrame Plus technology, which protects both belted and unbelted passengers, and easily adjusts for children 26.5 to 65 lbs. up to 57 inches tall.
- The company's SafeGuard ICS provides a built-in child restraint system with a five-point harness for children 26.5 to 85 lbs.
- IMMI introduced the 4Front frontal airbag for school buses, designed to ensure driver safety and minimize the impact of frontal crashes.
- SafeGuard hosted crash test demonstrations at STN EXPO Indy at the CAPE Testing facility, featuring side-impact and bus-to-bus tests with multiple dummies.
- Company leadership includes President Tom Anthony, who provided a history of IMMI and SafeGuard, and CEO Larry Gray, who discussed the company's safety expertise across industries.
- Congresswoman Susan Brooks and seat belt safety expert Melissa Dobbs have publicly endorsed IMMI & SafeGuard's school bus safety initiatives and crash test data.
- IMMI operates a Summer Internship Program and participates in community outreach through its IMMI Serves initiative, including Habitat for Humanity panel builds.
