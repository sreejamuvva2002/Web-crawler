# PIOLAX CORPORATION

## Facts

- PIOLAX CORPORATION operates a Sales & Design Office in Plymouth, Michigan, established in November 1986.
- The Michigan office handles production, marketing, sales, and designing of industrial fasteners and precision springs mainly for automobiles in North America.
- PIOLAX CORPORATION operates its Head Office & Plant in Canton, Georgia, established in August 1988.
- The Georgia facility serves as the headquarters and plant for the production and sales of industrial fasteners and precision springs mainly for automobiles in North America.
- PIOLAX MEXICANA S.A. de C.V. is located in Apodaca, N.L., Mexico, established in January 2001.
- The Mexico base produces plastic products and open/close mechanical products such as air dampers.
- PIOLAX LTD. is located in Accrington, Lancashire, U.K., established in October 1994.
- The U.K. base handles production and sales of industrial fasteners and precision springs mainly for automobiles in the U.K. and other European countries.
- PIOLAX (THAILAND) LTD. is located in Rayong, Thailand, established in August 2000.
- The Thailand facility serves as a head office and plant for the production and sales of industrial fasteners for automobiles in Thailand and other Asian countries.
- PIOLAX CO., LTD. is located in Incheon, South Korea, established in June 1996.
- The South Korea base handles production and sales of industrial fasteners for automobiles in South Korea.
- PIOLAX (CHINA) CO., LTD is located in Shanghai, China, established in January 2019.
- The Shanghai office handles development and sales of industrial fasteners, compact units, and precision springs for automobiles in China.
- DONGGUAN PIOLAX CO., LTD. is located in Dongguan City, Guangdong, China, established in August 2003.
- The Dongguan facility handles production of industrial fasteners, compact units, and precision springs for automobiles in China.
- WUHAN PIOLAX CO., LTD. is located in Wuhan City, Hubei Province, China, established in August 2012.
- The Wuhan facility handles production of industrial fasteners, compact units, and precision springs for automobiles in China.
- PIOLAX INDIA PRIVATE LTD. is located in Tirupati District, Andhra Pradesh, India, established in December 2009.
- The India base handles production and sales of automotive parts such as industrial fasteners and compact units.
- PT. PIOLAX INDONESIA is located in Karawang, Indonesia, established in July 2012.
- The Indonesia base handles production and sales of parts for automobiles and motorcycles.
