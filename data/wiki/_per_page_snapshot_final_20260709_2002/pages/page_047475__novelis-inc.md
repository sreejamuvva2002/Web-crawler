# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-07-29

**Source:** [Novelis drives responsible aluminum supply in the North American market with ASI Performance Standard Certification – Agrigate Global](https://agrigateglobal.com/press-release/novelis-drives-responsible-aluminum-supply-in-the-north-american-market-with-asi-performance-standard-certification/)  ·  `page_047475`

## Facts

- Novelis is a leading sustainable aluminum solutions provider and the world leader in aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Three of Novelis' North American plants that produce material for the beverage can market have been awarded ASI Performance Standard Certifications.
- The certified North American plants are located in Greensboro, GA; Berea, KY; and Warren, OH.
- Novelis has been delivering ASI-certified material since August 2021 in Europe, South America, and Asia.
- Tom Boney serves as Executive Vice President and President of Novelis North America.
- The ASI Performance Standard addresses environmental, social, and governance principles for the production of aluminum.
- Novelis aims to achieve ASI Certification in all its plants worldwide.
- Fiona Solomon is the Chief Executive Officer at the Aluminium Stewardship Initiative (ASI).
- One of Novelis' certified North American plants recycles approximately 20% of the region's used beverage cans (UBCs).
- Novelis' plant in Pindamonhangaba, Brazil, has also earned the ASI Performance Standard Certification.
- Novelis has achieved both Performance Standard and Chain of Custody Certifications by ASI at all of its 11 European manufacturing sites.
- All rolled aluminum products of Novelis Korea are now sourced and produced in accordance with the ASI Performance Standard and Chain of Custody Standard.
- Novelis aims to be a carbon-neutral company by 2050 or sooner.
- Novelis aims to reduce its carbon footprint by 30% by 2026.
- Novelis has targets to reduce waste to landfills by 20%, energy intensity by 10%, and water intensity by 10% by 2026.
- Increasing the amount of recycled aluminum in its products is at the core of Novelis' carbon reduction strategy.
- Novelis serves customers in the aerospace, automotive, beverage can, and specialties industries throughout North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- Novelis had net sales of $17.1 billion in fiscal year 2022.
- Novelis is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited.
- Hindalco Industries Limited is the metals flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group, a multinational conglomerate based in Mumbai.
- Tom Boney stated that the certification is the latest step in their sustainability journey and demonstrates an unwavering commitment to providing sustainable aluminum solutions to valued customers.
- Suzanne Lindsay-Walker stated that Novelis aims to achieve ASI Certification in all its plants worldwide as part of an ongoing commitment to being a responsible partner to customers and working with them to achieve sustainability goals.
- Fiona Solomon stated that the certified plants are significant nodes in the North American beverage can market and expressed delight at Novelis' ongoing ASI Certification journey extending through global operations.
