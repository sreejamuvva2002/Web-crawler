## Facts

- The award was presented by Georgia Governor Nathan Deal on Nov. 2, 2017.
- The award recognizes leaders throughout the state for their contributions to build a robust automotive industry.
- Lund acquired the assets of Tradesman Truck Accessories on April 22, 2013.
- Tradesman will continue to operate in Winters, Texas with its current management team remaining in place.
