# Pirelli

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Pirelli to Enhance U.S. Operations with New Cyber™ Tyre Production Facility - Third News](https://third-news.com/article/29b2743a-48d5-11f1-88da-9ca3ba0a67df)  ·  `page_048527`

## Facts

- Pirelli announced the commencement of production for its innovative Cyber™ Tyre at its Georgia plant.
- The Georgia plant was initially dedicated to manufacturing high-value products targeting both the consumer market and motorsport enthusiasts.
- The facility will now incorporate state-of-the-art Cyber™ Tyre technology into its existing production line.
- Cyber™ Tyre is the first system of its kind that merges hardware with software to gather and process data directly from sensors embedded in the tires.
- The tire relays information in real-time to the vehicle’s electronic systems through Pirelli’s proprietary software and algorithms.
- The technology aims to deliver an enhanced driving experience, improved safety features, and seamless integration with smart road infrastructure.
- Pirelli unveiled the Cyber™ Tyre technology at the SelectUSA Investment Summit.
- Claudio Zanardo serves as the CEO of Pirelli North America.
- Georgia has been a key site for Pirelli for over twenty years.
- Pirelli is finalizing the introduction of the latest MIRS (Modular Integrated Robotized System) production process at the Georgia facility.
- The MIRS system is designed to elevate productivity and flexibility while maintaining quality commitments.
- The digital nature of the MIRS process facilitates a direct connection between product design and manufacturing.
- The Rome plant in Georgia hosts a dedicated research and development center that actively contributes to Pirelli’s push for innovation in product technology.
- Pirelli utilizes FSC® (Forest Stewardship Council®)-certified natural rubber in its products to support responsible sourcing and environmental sustainability across its supply chain.
