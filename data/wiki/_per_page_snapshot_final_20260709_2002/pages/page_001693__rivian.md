# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-12-16

**Source:** [Georgia Power welcomes Rivian to Georgia - Coastal Courier](https://coastalcourier.com/news/georgia-power-welcomes-rivian-georgia/)  ·  `page_001693`

## Facts

- Georgia Power joined with Governor Brian Kemp and the Georgia Department of Economic Development to welcome Rivian to Georgia on December 16, 2021.
- Rivian is a world-class electric sport utility vehicle and pickup company that places a focus on sustainability and positive environmental impact.
- Rivian was named the 2022 MotorTrend Truck of the Year.
- Rivian selected Georgia for a nearly 13 million square foot plant for vehicle and battery operations.
- The plant is located along Interstate 20 near Social Circle.
