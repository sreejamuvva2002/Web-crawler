# Kumho Tires

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2008-07-07

**Source:** [Kumho Tires to Supply OE Tires for Ford Focus - Kumho Tire USA, Inc.](https://web.archive.org/web/20140725154905/http://www.kumhotireusa.com/news/index/44)  ·  `page_056667`

## Facts

- On July 7, 2008, Kumho Tires announced it has been selected by Ford Motor Company to supply original equipment tires for the majority of the new 2009 Ford Focus models.
- Kumho Tires will supply approximately 200,000 of its 215/45R17 Solus KH16 Ultra High Performance (UHP) tires for the Focus.
- These UHP tires provide a more stable ride while minimizing road noise.
- The Solus KH16 has been in the U.S. tire replacement market since 2005.
- The Solus KH16 has demonstrated all seasonal performance along with the best practical value as proven by sales volumes of over one million units per year.
- Sae-chul Oh, President and CEO of Kumho Tires, stated that the contract is an important step because they now have supply contracts with all of the big three automakers in North America.
- The automakers have recognized that Kumho Tires shares their commitment to quality, technology and performance.
- Kumho Tires will continue to expand its North American presence to become one of the top five global tire manufacturers by 2015.
- Kumho Tires started business in the U.S. market in 1975.
- Kumho Tires recently established a firm base in North America, with a groundbreaking ceremony for an automated production facility in Macon, Georgia.
- The Macon, Georgia facility is capable of supplying 2.1 million tires annually.
- The company is currently an original equipment supplier for General Motors as well as Chrysler.
- In addition, Kumho Tires also supplies original equipment tires to Volkswagen, Daimler, Mitsubishi, Hyundai and Kia.
- Sae-chul Oh stated that as environmental issues become more important and oil prices rise, the company is bolstering its commitment to design and make durable, efficient products that help to increase fuel efficiency and minimize environmental impact.
