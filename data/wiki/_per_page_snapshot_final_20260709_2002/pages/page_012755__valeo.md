# Valeo

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-25

**Source:** [Valeo breaks ground on $225m manufacturing plant in US](https://finance.yahoo.com/sectors/technology/articles/valeo-breaks-ground-225m-manufacturing-192321004.html)  ·  `page_012755`

## Facts

- Valeo has started construction of a new manufacturing facility in McAllen, Texas.
- The French automotive supplier will invest $225m over the next five years in the site.
- The facility will span 337,000ft2.
- The project will create up to 500 jobs.
- Production is due to begin in late 2027.
- The McAllen facility supports one of Valeo's largest orders in its history.
- The project is part of Valeo's "Elevate 2028" strategic plan focused on software-defined vehicle (SDV) technology.
- The McAllen plant will produce General Motors' central compute unit, a liquid-cooled system that processes data from multiple sensors and systems to manage essential vehicle functions.
- Jeffrey Shay, Valeo North America president, said: "We're proud to contribute technologies that enable the next generation of software-defined vehicles with this significant investment in the region and the future of automotive technology."
- Jeffrey Shay added: "We're grateful for the opportunity to grow our business with GM in the state of Texas."
- Kristin Toth, purchasing executive director, electrical systems, software and connectivity at General Motors, said: "A software-defined vehicle architecture is a key enabler to dramatically improve the customer experience through faster connectivity, richer entertainment options and more frequent updates."
- Kristin Toth added: "Projects such as Valeo's plant in Texas will play a crucial role as we bring our next-generation electrical architecture to market."
- Last month, Valeo announced plans to invest more than €200m ($236.8m) in India to expand electrification, advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) and artificial intelligence (AI) engineering activities under its Elevate 2028 strategy.
- Valeo plans to triple its local revenue in India to about €700m by 2028.
- The funding for the India expansion is about Rs21.49bn at current exchange rates and will enlarge Valeo's manufacturing and engineering presence in India to support domestic and export vehicle programmes.
- In September, Valeo opened a new production site in Shanghai's Waigang Industrial Park, situated in Jiading District, to enhance its manufacturing capabilities for advanced driving assistance systems (ADAS) in China.
