# Nisshinbo Micro Devices Inc.

## Facts

- Nisshinbo Micro Devices Inc. is an analog solution provider formed by the integration of former New Japan Radio Co., Ltd. and former RICOH Electronic Devices Co., Ltd.
- Former New Japan Radio Co., Ltd. is known as an Op-amp supplier.
- Former RICOH Electronic Devices Co., Ltd. is known as a Power management IC supplier.
- The company offers the NB7401 Series, a 2 to 4 serial cell Li-ion or Li-polymer battery protection IC for secondary protection.
- The company offers the NC2650 Series, an ultra-low quiescent current (IQVIN = 85nA) 1A step-down switching regulator.
- Nisshinbo Micro Devices Inc. achieved an industry-leading ultra-low quiescent current (Iq) of 85 nA with the new release of the NC2650 Series.
- The company offers the NB7130 Series, a 1-cell Li-ion battery protection IC for low-side FETs.
- The company offers the NT9000/NT9001/NT9002/NT9003 diode for 1W/1mW class rectifier.
