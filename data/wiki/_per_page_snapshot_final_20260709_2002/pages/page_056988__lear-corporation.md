# Lear Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-03-25

**Source:** [Auto Supplier Lear Corp. Acquires M&N Plastics in Sterling Heights - DBusiness Magazine](https://www.dbusiness.com/daily-news/auto-supplier-lear-corp-acquires-mn-plastics-in-sterling-heights/)  ·  `page_056988`

## Facts

- Lear Corp., a global automotive technology company based in Southfield, announced that it has acquired Sterling Heights’ M&N Plastics.
- M&N Plastics is a privately owned injection molding specialist and manufacturer of engineered plastic components for automotive electrical distribution applications.
- Terms of the acquisition deal were not disclosed.
- The addition of M&N Plastics increases Lear’s capabilities to engineer and produce complex parts for electrical distribution, including high-voltage wire harnesses and power electronics.
- Carl Esposito serves as president of E-Systems at Lear and a senior vice president of the corporation.
- Carl Esposito stated that the acquisition bolsters Lear’s position as the leading Tier 1 supplier of full architecture solutions for both electrical distribution systems and power electronics.
- The move enhances Lear’s ability to vertically integrate engineered components, creating a platform for future revenue growth and margin expansion in its overall E-Systems business segment.
- M&N Plastics possesses industry experience and technical expertise across its management, engineering, administrative, and other functions.
- M&N Plastics operates manufacturing facilities in Sterling Heights and El Paso, Texas.
- M&N Plastics utilizes state-of-the-art injection molding capabilities, enabling it to serve as a niche market leader and critical partner to its customers.
- Terry Nagle serves as president of M&N Plastics.
- Terry Nagle stated that M&N Plastics has made significant investments in manufacturing and engineering, resulting in best-in-class production facilities and capabilities.
