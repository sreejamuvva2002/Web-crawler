# TI Fluid Systems PLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-10-01

**Source:** [TI Fluid Systems Announces Hans Dieltjens As CEO | The Michigan Chronicle](https://michiganchronicle.com/ti-fluid-systems-announces-hans-dieltjens-as-ceo/)  ·  `page_011383`

## Facts

- TI Fluid Systems PLC has close to 100 years of automotive fluid systems experience.
- The company is headquartered in the United Kingdom with corporate offices in Auburn Hills.
- Originally founded under the name Harry Bundy and Company in 1922 by Harry Warren Bundy in Detroit.
- The company provided unfabricated gas lines on the Model T during its early days.
- TI Fluid Systems joined Michigan in 2016, opening corporate offices there.
- The corporation has manufacturing facilities in 107 locations across 28 countries.
- William L. Kozyra is the company’s current President and Chief Executive Officer and will retire later this year, stepping down from the Board of Directors.
- Hans Dieltjens is taking over as CEO and President.
- Dieltjens has 25 years of automotive experience spanning Asia, Europe, and the Americas.
- Dieltjens previously served as leader of the Global Fuel Tank and Delivery Systems Division.
- Dieltjens led the development and manufacturing of products to improve vehicle performance and limit harmful emissions.
- The executive leadership exchange will be effective as of October 1, 2021.
- Stephanie Jett was appointed Chief Commercial Officer, effective October 1.
- Jett will be based at the corporation’s headquarters in Auburn Hills.
- Jett has over 20 years of automotive experience holding several senior commercial roles.
- Jett will be responsible for sales and commercial activities, including focusing on the company’s electric vehicle product portfolio.
- Mark Sullivan joined as Chief Operating Officer, officially starting earlier this year.
- Sullivan has more than 35 years in the automotive industry and has lived and worked in the United States, France, and Germany.
- Johannes Helmich took over as Chief Technology Officer in September.
- Manfred Wennemer serves as Chair of the Board of Directors.
- Wennemer stated that the executive additions will strengthen the company’s ability to execute its electric vehicle strategy and deliver near-term objectives.
- Kozyra served more than 13 years, led the company through the global financial crisis into a decade of record performance, and was instrumental in developing the electric vehicle strategy.
- Kozyra will remain connected to the company in an advisory capacity until the end of the year.
- TI Fluid Systems PLC has an expansive customer base that includes luxury brands such as Audi, Land Rover, Bentley, and Porsche.
- The company also services The Big Three automotive manufacturers.
