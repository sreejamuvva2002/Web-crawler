# Pirelli North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-05

**Source:** [PIRELLI WILL START CYBER™ TYRE PRODUCTION IN GEORGIA UNDERLINING THE STRATEGIC IMPORTANCE OF THE U.S. MARKET | Morningstar](https://www.morningstar.com/news/pr-newswire/20260505la51934/pirelli-will-start-cyber-tyre-production-in-georgia-underlining-the-strategic-importance-of-the-us-market)  ·  `page_048525`

## Facts

- Pirelli is reinforcing its long-term commitment to the United States with a key step in its product and industrial strategy.
- The Georgia plant will be enhanced by the production of connected tires featuring Cyber™ Tyre technology.
- Investment and output expansion details for the project will be communicated in the coming months following the finalization of development plans.
- Pirelli made the announcement while participating in the SelectUSA Investment Summit, a flagship event promoted by the U.S. Department of Commerce.
- Cyber™ Tyre is being showcased at the summit as a core innovation shaping the future of smart mobility.
- Cyber™ Tyre is the world's first hardware-and-software system capable of collecting data and information from sensors embedded in tires.
- The system processes sensor data through Pirelli's proprietary software and algorithms.
- It communicates in real time with the vehicle's electronics to enable new functionalities integrated with driving and control systems.
- These functionalities enhance the driving experience, increase safety levels, and support connected infrastructure.
- Cyber™ Tyre was showcased at the Georgia State booth during the SelectUSA summit.
- Pirelli has been established in Georgia for more than 20 years.
- Claudio Zanardo, CEO of Pirelli North America, stated that the start of Cyber™ Tyre production in the Rome, Georgia plant is a significant milestone for Pirelli in the country.
- The production start reflects Pirelli's commitment to bringing advanced technologies like Cyber™ Tyre closer to the market.
- It further strengthens Pirelli's industrial footprint and innovation capabilities in the United States.
- To enhance Rome as a high-tech production site, Pirelli is finalizing the introduction of the latest version of the MIRS (Modular Integrated Robotized System) production process.
- The MIRS process will be the most advanced manufacturing process for high-end, premium production within the entire Pirelli Group.
- The MIRS process is exclusive to the Georgia factory.
- The new process enhances robotized production capabilities, increasing productivity, flexibility, and quality.
- It is a highly digitalized system that enables a direct link between product design and its industrial application.
- This development lays the groundwork for growth in Pirelli's production capacity in Rome.
- The Rome, Georgia plant specializes in high-value tire production for the North American market.
- The facility also hosts a dedicated R&D center to strengthen technological development and product innovation.
- The facility is recognized for responsible sourcing practices, including the use of FSC® (Forest Stewardship Council®)-certified natural rubber.
