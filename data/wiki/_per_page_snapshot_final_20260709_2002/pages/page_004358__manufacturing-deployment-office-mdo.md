# Manufacturing Deployment Office (MDO)

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-30

**Source:** [Manufacturing Deployment Office (MDO) | Department of Energy](https://www.energy.gov/cmei/manufacturing/manufacturing-deployment-office-mdo)  ·  `page_004358`

## Facts

- The Manufacturing Deployment Office (MDO) serves as the frontline of America's energy manufacturing deployment and supply chain security.
- MDO ensures America’s resilient energy future through direct investments in manufacturing capacity and workforce development.
- MDO's mission is to enhance economic and national security by eliminating vulnerabilities in the United States' energy supply chains.
- MDO catalyzes processing and manufacturing for energy resilience and independence.
- MDO reinvigorates the manufacturing workforce through education and training.
- MDO guides investment decisions and policy formulation through data-backed supply chain insights.
- MDO is currently funding more than 375 projects focused on workforce training and manufacturing expansion.
- These funded efforts span batteries, grid components, critical minerals processing and recycling, energy generation, nuclear power, buildings and energy efficiency, transportation and industrial materials.
- MDO focuses on Manufacturing Capacity by investing in catalyzing processing and manufacturing capacity in critical junctures of the supply chain to enable resilience and energy independence.
- MDO focuses on Workforce Deployment by supporting workforce education and training through programs at universities, community colleges, and trade schools.
- MDO focuses on Analysis & Strategic Investment by providing data-driven insights on supply chain vulnerabilities and opportunities to inform investment decisions and the national security mission.
- The Office of Manufacturing and Energy Supply Chains contact address is 1000 Independence Ave SW, Washington, D.C. 20585.
