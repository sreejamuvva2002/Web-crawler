# Ecoplastic Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-01

**Source:** [Ecoplastic Corp. plans to invest in new GA facility](https://valdostatoday.com/news-2/region/2023/01/ecoplastic-corp-plans-to-invest-in-new-ga-facility/)  ·  `page_019821`

## Facts

- Ecoplastic Corporation will invest about $205 million in a new Georgia facility.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Ecoplastic Corporation will create 456 new jobs at the new manufacturing facility in Bulloch County.
- The company is an automotive parts manufacturer based in Korea, established in 1984.
- Ecoplastic manufactures plastic automobile parts for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and other suppliers.
- The company is a Tier-1 supplier for Hyundai Motor Group (HMG) and Kia.
- Ecoplastic engages in the manufacturing of automotive interior and exterior plastic parts, including bumpers, consoles, trims, and plastic molds.
- Han Shang, President and CEO of Ecoplastic, stated the company is honored to establish a production facility for electric vehicle parts in Bulloch County, Georgia.
- The new facility will be located at 4822 Highway 301 South Register near Statesboro.
- The plant will be capable of injection molding and painting.
- The facility will produce exterior parts, bumpers and garnishes, interior parts, consoles, and trims for electric vehicles.
- Ecoplastic expects to begin operations by October 2024.
- The company will be hiring for HR, finance, parts development, quality control, facility maintenance, injection, and paint production positions.
- Development Authority of Bulloch County Chair Billy Allen welcomed Ecoplastic to Bulloch County.
- Director of Project Implementation and Supplier Strategy Alyce Thornhill represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team on the project.
- The project is in partnership with the Development Authority of Bulloch County, Georgia Ports Authority, Georgia Quick Start, and Georgia EMC.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson noted that Ecoplastic has decades of experience as a supplier for Kia and Hyundai.
- Georgia is positioned as a hub for the electric mobility industry.
- Since 2020, more than 30 EV-related projects have announced more than $21 billion in investment and announced more than 26,300 jobs in Georgia.
- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America officially broke ground on October 25 on its first fully dedicated EV and battery manufacturing facility in neighboring Bryan County.
- Offsite suppliers connected to Hyundai's project were predicted to invest approximately $1 billion in other locations around the state.
- Ecoplastic Corporation was established in June 1984 as Apollo Ind Co., Ltd.
- The company established its first Technical Research Center in 1987.
- Ecoplastic was publicly listed on the Korean Stock Exchange in March 2000.
- The company currently employs more than 770 employees.
- Ecoplastic recorded sales revenue of more than $700 million in 2021.
- The new facility will serve the Hyundai Metaplant from Statesboro, taking advantage of close proximity to Georgia's ports system.
