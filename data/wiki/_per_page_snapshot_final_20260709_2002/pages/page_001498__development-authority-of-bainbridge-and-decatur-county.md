# Development Authority of Bainbridge and Decatur County

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-20

**Source:** [Development Authority gets Anovion, Chamber updates | The Post-Searchlight](https://thepostsearchlight.com/2026/03/20/development-authority-gets-anovion-chamber-updates/)  ·  `page_001498`

## Facts

- Executive Director Rick McCaskill reported that Anovion Technologies has a site in the Down Range Industrial Park and is currently in a "fundraising mode."
- President Donald Trump’s changes to electric vehicle, battery, and tariff policies have affected Anovion Technologies’ ability to find investors.
- Anovion Technologies is rebuilding its investor package and still expects to start construction this year.
- Southern Electric Motorworks is in the process of engineering its property in the Commodore Industrial Park.
- CSX and Georgia Southwestern Railroad are actively sending business prospects to the Bainbridge-Decatur County region.
- Decatur County participates in Locate South Georgia, a group of 23 counties that markets the region for business and industry.
- Executive Director Rick McCaskill found new prospects through trade shows and the February Georgia Quail Hunt.
- The Development Authority of Bainbridge and Decatur County board approved its fourth-quarter financials, with McCaskill noting the authority "paid a lot of debt off" in 2025.
- A bond resolution to provide millions of dollars for Spring Creek Charter Academy’s campus renovation is expected to be finalized within the next three or four weeks.
- Several prospects are seeking space for a spec building in the Commodore Industrial Park.
- The Development Authority of Bainbridge and Decatur County’s next meeting is scheduled for Thursday, May 21, at noon at Bainbridge City Council Chambers.
