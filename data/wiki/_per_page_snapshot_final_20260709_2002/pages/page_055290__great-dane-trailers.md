## Facts

- Great Dane is a premier trailer manufacturer with a history dating back to 1900.
- Great Dane's product offerings include refrigerated trailers (Everest, Alpine, and Johnson models), dry trailers (Champion Sheet & Post, Champion Composite, and Sahara models), and open deck solutions.
- Great Dane engineers durable and high-quality trailers and truck bodies.
- Great Dane operates nine manufacturing plants across the United States.
- Great Dane operates 20 sales branches across the United States.
- The Savannah facility is located at 400 N Lathrop Ave, Savannah, GA 31401.
- The Savannah location offers new equipment, used equipment, parts, service, accessories, and financing.
