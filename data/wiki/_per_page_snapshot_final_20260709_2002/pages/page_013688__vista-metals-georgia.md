## Facts

- Vista Metals Georgia is located at 800 Martin Luther King Dr, Adairsville, GA 30103.
- The company specializes in casting 2###, 5###, 6### and 7### series alloys.
- The facility utilizes two large casting stations ("pits") with fully automated data logging.
- The facility utilizes three ultra-low nox, reverb melting furnaces.
- Products include cast and scalped forging ingot, hard and soft alloy extrusion billet, and rectangular slab and sheet ingot.
- Vista Metals claims to be the world's largest independent producer of specialty aluminum products.
- The company's specialty aluminum is used on some of the world's most advanced machines.
