# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-08

**Source:** [Novelis Reports Second Quarter Fiscal Year 2023 Results](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/novelis-reports-second-quarter-fiscal-year-2023-results-301671434.html)  ·  `page_028905`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is a leading global provider of sustainable aluminum solutions and the world leader in aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Novelis broke ground on a new US recycling and rolling plant in Bay Minette, Alabama last month.
- Net income attributable to common shareholders decreased 23% year-over-year to $183 million for the second quarter of fiscal year 2023.
- Net income from continuing operations was $184 million, down 23% year-over-year; net income from continuing operations excluding special items was $203 million, down 17% year-over-year.
- Adjusted EBITDA was $506 million, down 8% year-over-year, with Adjusted EBITDA per tonne at $514.
- Total shipments reached 984 kilotonnes, representing a 2% year-over-year increase.
- The company maintained a net leverage ratio of 2.3x and total liquidity of $2.8 billion.
- Net sales increased 17% to $4.8 billion for the second quarter of fiscal year 2023, compared to $4.1 billion in the prior year period.
- Shipment growth was primarily driven by higher automotive shipments as semiconductor shortages impacting the automotive industry began to ease, alongside slightly higher beverage can and aerospace shipments.
- The 8% decrease in Adjusted EBITDA was primarily due to higher energy and operating costs from geopolitical instability, inflation, and global supply chain disruptions, partially offset by higher product pricing, volume, and favorable product mix.
- Adjusted free cash flow from continuing operations was an outflow of $90 million for the first six months of fiscal year 2023.
- Steve Fisher serves as President and CEO of Novelis Inc.
- Devinder Ahuja serves as Executive Vice President and CFO of Novelis Inc.
- Novelis provides innovative aluminum solutions to customers in the aerospace, automotive, beverage can, and specialties industries throughout North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- Novelis reported net sales of $17.1 billion for fiscal year 2022.
- Novelis Inc. is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited, the metals flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group.
- Novelis will discuss its second quarter fiscal year 2023 results via a live webcast and conference call at 7:00 a.m. EST on Tuesday, November 8, 2022.
- The company's forward-looking statements highlight risks related to the impact of the global semiconductor shortage on automotive production and demand for automotive aluminum sheet.
