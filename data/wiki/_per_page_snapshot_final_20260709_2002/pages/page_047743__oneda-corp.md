## Facts

- Oneda Corp., located in Columbus GA, has developed tooling technology and manufacturing capabilities that allow the company to produce multiple parts within the same process.

- The methodology provides improved overall unit costs.
- The methodology provides increased production capacity.
- Company officials cite advanced comprehensive engineering and design experience as keys to achieving this technique.
- The technique is used to produce precision metal stampings.
- Oneda Corp. is a subsidiary of Oneda Electric Corp. Japan.
- Oneda Corp. serves the automotive, medical and electronics industries.
- Technologies utilized include Stamping Presses and Tooling.
