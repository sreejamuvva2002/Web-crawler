# Club Car LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-19

**Source:** [Club Car Opens UTV Factory in US | For Construction Pros](https://www.forconstructionpros.com/equipment/trucks/utility-vehicles/article/22893611/club-car-llc-club-car-opens-utv-factory-in-us)  ·  `page_018435`

## Facts

- Club Car has opened a new factory in Georgia for producing electric utility task vehicles (UTVs).
- UTVs are gaining popularity across industries like construction and landscaping because they are easy to operate and do not require a commercial driver's license, helping address worker shortages.
- The Club Car Urban line UTVs can be equipped with different electric lithium options for extended range in multiple climates and feature truck-like payload capacity.
- Club Car celebrated the opening of the Club Car Appling Manufacturing Facility with tours and a ribbon cutting ceremony.
- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp and First Lady Marty Kemp joined state and local elected officials and area business leaders to tour the facility and learn about the Club Car Urban vehicle.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Club Car is bringing jobs into Georgia in the growing e-mobility sector and leading the way in compact utility EV manufacturing.
- Governor Kemp noted that Club Car's growth and investment are a testament to Georgia's work to remain the number one state to do business.
- The Club Car Appling Manufacturing Facility comprises 105,000 sq. ft. of flexible production and warehouse storage space.
- Club Car opened the facility to offer customers a better experience as demand in the United States for the Club Car Urban vehicle increases.
- Club Car has committed nearly $8 million in capital investments into the facility over two years.
- The new facility currently operates a state-of-the-art production line and can produce up to 20 vehicles per day.
- Club Car President and CEO Mark Wagner stated that their innovative vehicles are impacting commercial fleet electrification, mini-mobility and last mile delivery.
- Club Car has been a Georgia based company for more than sixty years.
