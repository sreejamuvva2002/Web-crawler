# Aspen Aerogels

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-02-19

**Source:** [Aspen Aerogels to Open Advanced Manufacturing Facility in Bulloch County, Will Create More Than 250 Jobs - AllOnGeorgia](https://allongeorgia.com/georgia-business/aspen-aerogels-to-open-advanced-manufacturing-facility-in-bulloch-county-will-create-more-than-250-jobs/)  ·  `page_052714`

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels is a technology leader in aerogel-based sustainability and electrification solutions.
- Aspen plans to construct an advanced manufacturing facility at Bulloch County’s Southern Gateway Commerce Park.
- Aspen intends to invest a minimum of $325 million in the project.
- The project will create more than 250 advanced manufacturing jobs.
- The new site is located near Statesboro in southeast Georgia.
- Production at the facility is expected to begin in late 2023.
- Combined with Aspen’s original East Providence, Rhode Island plant, the Georgia facility is designed to triple Aspen’s nameplate aerogel capacity.
- The expansion supports Aspen’s thermal barrier expansion plans in the fast-growing electric vehicle (EV) market.
- Aspen aims to capitalize on increased demand for its Aerogel Technology Platform™ as EV OEMs adopt its PyroThin® aerogel thermal barrier solutions.
- Don Young, President and CEO of Aspen, stated the company is pleased to be in Georgia for the siting, development, and construction of its PyroThin materials plant.
- The City of Statesboro and Bulloch County region offer Aspen access to efficient ports, a strong available workforce, and secure low-cost utilities and critical raw materials.
- Aspen is increasingly being asked to provide broader solutions for improved battery performance and safety centered on PyroThin materials that provide thermal management, mechanical stability, and fire protection properties to EV battery systems.
- Aspen plans to build a 500,000-square-foot advanced manufacturing facility situated on a 90-acre parcel in southeast Georgia.
- The company plans to begin hiring personnel in the second half of 2022 across skilled manufacturing operators, advanced process engineers, quality and maintenance technicians, and professional support roles.
- Governor Kemp said Aspen has put confidence in Georgia, its people, and the growing electric mobility sector, adding this American company to the state's rapidly expanding EV portfolio.
- Governor Kemp noted the state is utilizing many assets to bring jobs to hardworking Georgians in every corner of the Peach State, calling this the latest to come to Bulloch County and southeast Georgia.
- Billy Allen, Vice Chair of the Development Authority of Bulloch County, welcomed Aspen to the Statesboro-Bulloch County community and region.
- Billy Allen cited the region's great site and logistics advantages and stated Aspen’s decision is a testament to the region’s commitment to innovation in the electric mobility ecosystem.
- Billy Allen expressed confidence that tremendous education resources, including Georgia Southern University and Ogeechee Technical College, will provide critical support to help Aspen flourish.
- Assistant Director Alyce Thornhill represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce division on this project in partnership with the Development Authority of Bulloch County, Georgia Quick Start, and Georgia EMC.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson welcomed Aspen’s innovative, sustainable solutions for the electric vehicle and energy storage markets to Georgia.
- Pat Wilson looked forward to having Georgia employees collaborate with Aspen’s network of world-class scientists, engineers, and additional skilled workforce to solve challenges in the changing mobility landscape.
- Pat Wilson thanked partners in Bulloch County for their partnership on the project and sent congratulations on bringing the company to the community.
- Southern Gateway Commerce Park is a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) Certified park.
- To earn GRAD program certification, a property must undergo due diligence and complete specific certification requirements prior to a visit by a prospective business.
- Completing the GRAD certification program with the Georgia Department of Economic Development is a proactive way community planners and landowners can help catalyze economic growth and industrial development for their regions.
- Aspen’s aerogel technology enables customers and partners to achieve objectives around resource efficiency, electrification, and sustainability.
- Aspen’s PyroThin® thermal barrier products enable solutions to thermal runaway challenges within the electric vehicle and energy storage markets.
- The company’s carbon aerogel program seeks to increase the performance of lithium-ion battery cells to enable EV manufacturers to extend driving range and reduce the cost of electric vehicles.
- Aspen’s Spaceloft® sustainable building materials provide industry-leading energy efficiency and fire safety to building owners.
- The company’s Cryogel® and Pyrogel® products are valued by the world’s largest energy infrastructure companies.
- Aspen’s strategy is to partner with world-class industry leaders to leverage its Aerogel Technology Platform™ into additional high-value markets.
- Aspen is headquartered in Northborough, MA, and manufactures its products at its East Providence, R.I. facilities.
