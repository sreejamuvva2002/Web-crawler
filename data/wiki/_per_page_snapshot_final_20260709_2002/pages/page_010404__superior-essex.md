## Facts

- For over 90 years, Superior Essex has met and exceeded wire and cable requirements for the automotive, industrial, commercial, residential, energy and communications markets.
- The company traces its history back to 1930, when the Essex Wire Corporation was founded in Detroit, Michigan.
- Superior Cable Corporation was founded in 1954 in Hickory, North Carolina.
- In 1999, Superior TeleCom (formerly Superior Cable) acquired Essex International (formerly Essex Wire).
- The acquisition formed the company now known globally as Superior Essex.
- Superior Essex is currently based in Atlanta, Georgia.
- The company produces communication cable designed to be more adaptable, efficient, and sustainable.
- Annie Bevan serves as the Global Head of Sustainability at Superior Essex.
- Annie Bevan has over 10 years of experience developing processes, procedures, and standards for sustainability claims in products, supply chains, buildings, and manufacturing operations.
- She is widely regarded in the industry as an expert in sustainability program development, corporate social responsibility, and third-party verification.
- In her role, she oversees Superior Essex's market-leading sustainability program and works to accelerate it through engagement with customers and suppliers.
- Annie Bevan supports or has supported various leading entities as a technical advisor and collaborator, including the US Green Building Council’s Materials and Resources TAG for the LEED rating system.
- She also collaborates with the International Living Future Institute (ILFI), my green lab, the Department of Defense, the General Services Administration, Clean Production Action, the GreenScreen Program, the Sustainable Purchasing Leadership Council, and the Collaborative for High Performing Schools.
