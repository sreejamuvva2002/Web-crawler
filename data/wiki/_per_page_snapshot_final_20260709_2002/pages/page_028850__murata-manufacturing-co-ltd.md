# Murata Manufacturing Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-12-04

**Source:** [Powered By Innovation: Murata to Showcase Intelligent System Technologies for Mobility, Wellness, and Connectivity at CES 2026](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/powered-by-innovation-murata-to-showcase-intelligent-system-technologies-for-mobility-wellness-and-connectivity-at-ces-2026-302631457.html)  ·  `page_028850`

## Facts

- Murata, a global leader in advanced electronics, will join industry leaders at CES 2026 January 6-9 in Las Vegas.
- Murata's booth location is Booth 4399 in West Hall at the Vehicle Technology & Advanced Mobility Pavilion.
- Murata will spotlight how intelligent systems and precision sensing are reshaping mobility, wellness, and connectivity.
- Featured Smart Mobility demos include Embedded RFID Tire Tag and Sensing.
- Featured Smart Mobility demos include Gyro Compass.
- Featured Smart Mobility demos include Headlight Leveling Using MEMS.
- Featured Smart Mobility demos include Motion Tracing Demonstration Using Low Frequency Magnetic Fields.
- Murata will showcase advanced Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and Ultra-Wideband (UWB) modules for embedded connectivity.
- Connectivity demos highlight Wi-Fi Mesh for Range Extension.
- Connectivity demos highlight Wi-Fi HaLowTM (Type 2HK/Type 2HL Module).
- Connectivity demos highlight Type 2NR Module (Nordic nRF54L15 based).
- Connectivity demos highlight UWB Smart Lock.
- Connectivity demos highlight Indoor Location Tracking with Bluetooth Channel Sounding.
- Connectivity demos highlight Satellite Position Tracking.
- Health-Centric Sensing solutions on display include Molecular Analysis Platform 1, 2,3 (15-minute DNA/RNA analysis).
- Health-Centric Sensing solutions on display include Vios Monitoring System (continuous hospital-grade telemetry and remote ECG).
- Application example of the 3D substrate Multilayer LCP Product and the piezoelectric film sensor PicoleafTM (Smart Ring Demo) will be shown.
- Ultrasonic Tech showcase includes Ultrasonic Cleaning Device (clear optics for AD/ADAS in any weather).
- Ultrasonic Tech showcase includes Ultrasonic Tracking Powered by pMUT (low-power 3D/6DoF spatial tracking for XR).
- Ultrasonic Tech showcase includes Airborne Ultrasound Transmission Metamaterial (bi-directional ultrasound through plastic/metal).
- At the time of this announcement, some of the exhibit products are concept products or under development.
- Specifications and appearance of exhibit products are subject to change without notice.
- Murata Manufacturing Co., Ltd. is a worldwide leader in the design, manufacture, and sale of ceramic-based passive electronic components and 2NRs, communication modules and power supply modules.
- Murata is committed to the development of advanced electronic materials and leading edge, multi-functional, high-density modules.
- The company has employees and manufacturing facilities throughout the world.
