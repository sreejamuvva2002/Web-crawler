# Georgia Department of Economic Development

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2023-01-19

**Source:** [GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson Named ‘Georgian of the Year’ by Georgia Trend - Georgia Entertainment](https://www.georgiaentertainment.com/2023/01/gdecd-commissioner-pat-wilson-named-georgian-of-the-year-by-georgia-trend/)  ·  `page_012265`

## Facts

- Commissioner Pat Wilson was named the 2023 “Georgian of the Year” by Georgia Trend.
- The award recognizes Wilson as the top salesperson for the state and commends his work promoting Georgia to attract investments and create jobs.
- Wilson credits the state’s success to a team approach in conducting business and emphasizes keeping relationships strong with partners and businesses across the state and globe.
- Wilson was appointed economic development Commissioner by former Governor Nathan Deal six years prior to the article's publication.
- Georgia maintained its place as the No. 1 state in the nation to do business for the ninth consecutive year in 2022.
- Former Georgia Governor Sonny Perdue credited Wilson with helping make Georgia one of the best places to grow a business by communicating the actions taken by the governor and legislature.
- In August 2022, GDEcD announced that investments in expansions and new locations for fiscal year 2022 totaled more than $21.2 billion.
- The FY22 investments were projected to create 51,132 direct new jobs in the state, setting new records for Georgia.
- GDEcD’s work providing solutions for companies and maintaining open communication helped Georgia secure two of its largest-ever investments in FY22.
- Wilson expressed emotional gratitude for his position and the impact jobs have had on people's lives.
- GDEcD teammates Lee Thomas, Director of the Georgia Film Office, and David Nuckolls, Executive Director of the Center of Innovation, were also recognized in Georgia Trend’s lists.
- GDEcD describes itself as the “quarterback” that brings partners across the state together to facilitate business development.
