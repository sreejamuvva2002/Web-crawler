## Facts

- Family-owned and operated for over 70 years, Fouts Bros manufactures a standard line of tankers, pumpers, quick attacks and brush trucks for fire departments nationwide.
- The company utilizes standardized manufacturing processes to produce more fire trucks at lower costs with faster turnaround times than competitors.
- In 2023, Fouts Bros introduced new 4000 Gallon Tanker and 5000 Gallon Tanker models designed specifically to help fire departments meet challenges created by the growing number of electric vehicles on America’s roads.
