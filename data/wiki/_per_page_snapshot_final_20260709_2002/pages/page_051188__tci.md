## Facts

- For nearly 40 years, TCI has been manufacturing industrial coatings with a strong commitment to people, products, and service.
- The company aspires to be the premium brand supplier for OEM coatings through superior product innovation and performance.
- TCI was founded in Ellaville, Georgia, initially operating out of a modest plant and a portable building that served as lab and office space.
- TCI has expanded into an international company with multiple manufacturing locations.
- TCI operates a state-of-the-art product development center.
- TCI employs a staff of several hundred employees focused on servicing customers.
- TCI's highly trained technical service and sales teams extend across North America, providing expert advice to customers ranging from large multinational corporations to independent custom coaters.
- TCI maintains an expansive network of distribution centers composed of certified or approved external providers.
- The distribution network services customers throughout the United States, Canada, and Mexico, ensuring stock powders and products are readily available.
- TCI is owned by RPM International Inc., a multinational company with subsidiaries that are world leaders in specialty coatings, sealants, building materials, and related services.
