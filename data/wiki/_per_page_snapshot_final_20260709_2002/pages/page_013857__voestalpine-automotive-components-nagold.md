# voestalpine Automotive Components Nagold

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-01-01

**Source:** [voestalpine Automotive Components Nagold finds an ideal strategic partner in Tempel Steel - Livingstone Partners](https://livingstonepartners.com/en-us/transactions/voestalpine-automotive-components-nagold-finds-an-ideal-strategic-partner-in-tempel-steel/)  ·  `page_013857`

## Facts

- voestalpine Automotive Components Nagold is a company of the Metal Forming Division of the voestalpine Group.
- The company was acquired in 2005 and currently employs around 130 people.
- Based in Germany, the company develops and produces stamped and formed parts for the automotive industry, such as radiator components, side member reinforcements and other pressed parts and assemblies.
- The site also produces inline bonded stator and rotor stacks for electric motors.
- The voestalpine Group has decided to sell voestalpine Automotive Components Nagold after 18 years as part of the Group for purely strategic reasons.
- The sale takes into account customer expectations regarding coverage of the complete technology portfolio in electrical steel processing, which is guaranteed by the buyer.
- Negotiations have been held with potential buyers for the Automotive Components plant in Nagold.
- Tempel Steel (the electrical steel operations of Worthington Steel) was found as an ideal buyer for the Nagold plant to secure the site in the long term.
- For Tempel Steel, the acquisition represents a strategic expansion of the portfolio.
- All employees and existing technologies at the site were taken over by the buyer.
- The Livingstone Duesseldorf office advised voestalpine Group on this transaction.
- The terms of the transaction have not been disclosed.
- Jochen Hense, Partner at Livingstone commented that they are optimistic that the ideal partner has been found and that teaming up with Tempel Steel offers a great long-term perspective for the employees, the management and the customers of voestalpine Automotive Components Nagold.
