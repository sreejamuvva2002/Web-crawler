# Wheelabrator Group

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Wheelabrator Group will celebrate its twenty years in operation at its LaGrange, Ga. facility in September 2007.
- The LaGrange location opened on Sept. 21, 1987.
- The facility houses Wheelabrator Plus, the aftermarket-sales division that provides a full range of services, replacement parts and training.
- LaGrange is also home to the Wheelabrator Group North American Research and Development Center along with a one-of-a-kind Customer Demonstration and Testing Facility and the Equipment Modernization Program (EMP) Team.
- The EMP Team focuses on providing equipment upgrades that are effective solutions for customers’ requested improvements in productivity and to reduce the costs associated with the operation and maintenance of their existing blasting equipment.
- A core group of employees moved to LaGrange from Mishawaka, Ind., in 1987 to start up the facility.
- Pierre Tanguay, Vice President North American Supply Chain at Wheelabrator Group, stated that the Georgia town is located southwest of Atlanta on I-85 and was chosen for its proximity to a major interstate and Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport in Atlanta.
- The City of LaGrange worked closely with Wheelabrator to train the local workforce.
- The LaGrange facility now employs 130 people, with more than 20 additional employees working in field sales and service.
- The LaGrange facility will celebrate its 20th anniversary with a luncheon and employee recognition this fall.
- Pierre Tanguay stated that Wheelabrator is the global leader in surface preparation technology and has the talent to stay number one.
- Wheelabrator will honor another historical milestone in 2008 as the company celebrates its 100th anniversary.
- The company was founded in 1908 in Ohio as the Homogeneous Sand Mixing Machine Company.
- The company developed the Wheelabrator® airless blast cleaning wheel in 1932, a simple mechanical unit that used controlled centrifugal force instead of compressed air for abrasive blasting.
- The Wheelabrator trademark became part of the corporate name spanning from the 1940s to the present day.
- Wheelabrator Group is the global leader in providing surface preparation and finishing solutions.
- Committed to offering the broadest array of technologies, products, services and technical know-how, Wheelabrator Group works closely with customers to deliver tailored solutions for their specific requirements.
- The company operates strategically located and electronically linked offices worldwide to provide customer service and support.
