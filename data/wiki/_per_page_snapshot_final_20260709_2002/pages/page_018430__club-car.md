# Club Car

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-13

**Source:** [Club Car Expanding, Adding New Facility in Appling - Kicks 99](https://kicks99.com/2023/10/13/club-car-expanding-adding-new-facility-in-appling/)  ·  `page_018430`

## Facts

- Club Car announced it is expanding in Columbia County and will be building a 105,000 square foot facility in Appling.
- The new facility will be constructed in White Oak Industrial Park.
- The new facility is located near an existing Club Car facility that opened in 2019.
- The additional space at the new facility will be used to support warehousing and manufacturing functions.
- The new facility is expected to be finished and open in December this year.
- Rick Evans, Chair of the Board of Directors for the Development Authority of Columbia County, stated that Club Car has a long-standing relationship with Columbia County and values the investments made to expand and manufacture world-class golf cars and utility vehicles in the county.
- Club Car has an over 60-year history in the design and production of small-wheel vehicles.
- Club Car is the leading manufacturer of gas and electric golf, utility, and personal transportation vehicles.
- Club Car started out in Houston, Texas in 1958 before moving to Augusta in 1962, where it remains headquartered.
- Club Car is the official golf car of the PGA of America, PGA Tour Tournament Players Club (TPC) Network, and European Tour.
- The company actively supports US Kids Golf and the First Tee.
- Doug Duncan, Chairman of the Columbia County Board of Commissioners, stated that Club Car is a great community partner and expressed excitement for their continued growth in Columbia County.
