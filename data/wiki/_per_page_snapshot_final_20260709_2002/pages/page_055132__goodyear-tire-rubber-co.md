# Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Goodyear launches three new all-terrain Wrangler tires | Race Auto India](https://raceautoindia.com/post/goodyear-launches-three-new-all-terrain-wrangler-tires)  ·  `page_055132`

## Facts

- Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co. launched three new all-terrain Wrangler tires: Outbound AT, Workhorse AT 2, and ElectricDrive AT.
- The tire lineup targets a wide range of vehicles including full-size SUVs, light trucks, work vans, and electric pickups.
- Jonathan Thomas, Goodyear’s director of Category and Strategy, stated the lineup reflects customer feedback and aims to deliver solutions for job sites to backcountry trails across the U.S. and Canada.
- The Wrangler Outbound AT targets adventure-ready SUVs and light trucks such as the GMC Yukon, Ford F-150, Jeep Grand Cherokee, and Toyota 4Runner.
- The Wrangler Outbound AT features DuPont Kevlar for added durability and an all-new tread design and compound to boost grip on dirt and gravel while maintaining wet-weather performance.
- Goodyear backs the Wrangler Outbound AT with an up to 65,000-mile (105,000 km) tread life limited warranty.
- The Wrangler Workhorse AT 2 is built for light trucks, work vans, and SUVs including the Ford F-Series, Ram ProMaster, and Chevrolet Tahoe.
- The Wrangler Workhorse AT 2 delivers enhanced wet traction and carries an improved 55,000-mile (90,000 km) tread life limited warranty.
- Every size of the Wrangler Workhorse AT 2 carries the Three-Peak Mountain Snowflake (3PMSF) designation for tested winter capability.
- The Wrangler ElectricDrive AT is designed specifically for electric SUVs and pickups such as the Ford F-150 Lightning and Rivian R1T/R1S.
- The Wrangler ElectricDrive AT combines rugged all-terrain capability with 3PMSF winter confidence and adds SoundComfort® Technology to reduce in-cabin noise for quieter EV platforms.
- Goodyear backs the Wrangler ElectricDrive AT with a 50,000-mile (80,000 km) tread life limited warranty.
- The launch expands Goodyear’s consumer portfolio to meet growing demand for rugged, versatile performance on and off the road.
