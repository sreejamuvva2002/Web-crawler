# Freyr Battery

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-11

**Source:** [Norway-based battery manufacturer Freyr promises 720 new jobs in Coweta County](https://www.atlantanewsfirst.com/2022/11/11/norway-based-battery-manufacturer-freyr-promises-720-new-jobs-coweta-county/)  ·  `page_055034`

## Facts

- A Norway-based battery manufacturer announced plans to build its first American factory in Georgia.
- More than 720 new jobs are coming to Coweta County.
- The company is slated to build a plant on a more than 368-acre site in the Bridgeport Industrial Park off I-85 near Newnan, Ga.
- The facility will create more than 720 jobs over the next seven years.
- The plant will produce lithium-ion battery cells often used in cars and for energy storage.
- Tom Jensen, CEO of Freyr Battery, stated that batteries can be put in large containers to store sunlight and wind when the sun isn’t shining and the wind isn’t blowing.
- Freyr picked Georgia over more than 130 other sites in 25 states.
- Coweta County was chosen because it is perfectly positioned to easily move products in and out and has a workforce hungry for jobs.
- Freyr cited the availability of talent and access to competent people in the region as a core factor.
- Georgia is already proving to be a leader in the EV battery and car industry.
- The state is offering Freyr financial incentives to build its first US plant, but details were not disclosed by the Georgia Economic Development (GED).
- A GED representative stated that as an active economic development project, the state isn’t able to comment beyond the press release until agreements are finalized.
- Coweta County is offering incentives including a 20-year abatement on property taxes and grant money to help with a payroll of over $60,000 per year.
- Michael Fouts of Coweta County Administrator noted the proposed average salary is a little over $60,000 a year.
- This average salary represents 120 percent of the current average wage in the county.
- Freyr believes battery storage is the future and says it is very likely they will expand and hire even more people.
- The company plans to be up and running in about two years and fully operational by 2029.
