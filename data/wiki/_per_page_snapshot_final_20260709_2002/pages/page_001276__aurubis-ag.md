# Aurubis AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2022-06-22

**Source:** [Aurubis starts building its €300M multimetal recycling plant](https://evertiq.com/news/52046)  ·  `page_001276`

## Facts

- German-based Aurubis AG has broken on its latest investment – a new plant for multimetal recycling in Augusta, Richmond County in Georgia.
- The company is investing an estimated EUR 300 million in the project.
- The plant is expected to be fully operational by mid-2024.
- Roland Harings, CEO of Aurubis AG, stated that the company has doubled down on its commitments to use its technology and experience to become a forerunner for recycling valuable materials containing copper, nickel, tin, and other industrial and precious metals locally in the US.
- Once fully on-line, Aurubis Richmond, USA will have the capacity to process about 90,000 tons of complex recycling materials annually.
- The processed materials include printed circuit boards, copper cables, and other recycling materials containing metals.
- Currently, much of these materials in the US are either shipped to Asia and Europe or trucked to local landfills.
- Roland Harings noted that the US currently produces about 6 million tons of recycling materials that contain valuable metals.
- Aurubis aims to take advantage of this potential and help ensure that these materials get reused as critical input materials for many industries in the US.
- Copper is 100% recyclable without any loss in performance.
- Secondary smelter recycling at an industrial scale requires significant long-term investment.
