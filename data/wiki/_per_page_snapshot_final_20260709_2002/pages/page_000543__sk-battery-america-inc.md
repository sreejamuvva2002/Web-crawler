# SK Battery America, Inc.

## Facts

- SK Innovation is constructing a new EV battery manufacturing plant in Commerce, Georgia.
- The facility site covers 283 acres along I-85 about 65 miles northeast of downtown Atlanta inside the Commerce industrial complex.
- Phase 1 of the project comprises a 2.4 million-square-foot facility.
- This facility marks SK Innovation’s first EV battery factory in the U.S.
- Construction work on the site began in spring 2019.
- Phase 1 project completion was scheduled for spring 2020.
- Clayco led site mobilization and Phase I construction efforts for the plant.
- The initial Phase 1 scope included sitework, concrete, steel, IMP and roofing for electrode, assembly, formation, module and other ancillary support structures throughout the site.
- The facility design features multiple roof elevations and mezzanine decks.
- The greenfield project required 886,000 yards of rock hammering and excavation.
- Concrete Strategies completed all foundation and flatwork work on site.
- Later phases included finishes, MEP and clean/dry rooms.
- This plant represents one of the largest single investments in a job-creating initiative in Georgia’s history.
- It is also the largest such investment in Georgia in more than a decade.
- By 2025, the site will employ nearly 2,000 workers.
- The site will reach a production capacity of 9.8GWh by 2022.
- Phase 2 began construction in Q4 of 2020.
- Phase 2 consists of a 1.2 million square-foot facility located along Phase 1.
- Phase 2 work included site and utility work, construction of medium voltage banks and cross country piping racks.
- Phase 2 project challenges included the close proximity of the jobsite to the active facility and an accelerated timeline.
- Substantial completion for Phase 2 was scheduled for December 2021.
- Phase 2 also included eight additional buildings totaling 90,000 square feet.
- The additional buildings include kitchens, cell discharge areas, an administrative office expansion, and a bridge linking the two main buildings.
- SK Innovation manufactures lithium-ion batteries for hybrid and fully-electric automobiles.
- SK Innovation builds batteries for multiple vehicle manufacturers, including Volkswagen in Chattanooga.
- WEG Transformers USA (Washington, Missouri) is listed as a project participant or supplier.
