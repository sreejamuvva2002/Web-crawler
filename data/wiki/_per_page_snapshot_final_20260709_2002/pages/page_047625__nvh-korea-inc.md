## Facts

- NVH Korea Inc., together with its subsidiaries, engages in the manufacture and sale of automotive noise, vibration, and heat control parts in South Korea and internationally.
- The company offers interior systems, such as isolation dash pads, tunnel pads, floor modules, car mats, carpet skins, and sound absorption pads.
- The company offers exterior systems, including under covers, insulator tunnels, and wheel guards.
- The company offers overhead systems comprising headliners and sun visors.
- The company manufactures and sells non-woven fabrics and pallets.
- The company provides engine systems consisting of partition engine rooms, insulator dashes, and hood insulators.
- The company provides door trims.
- The company provides control assy-water temp solutions, cylinder block covers, and thermal management systems.
- The company offers management advisory and consulting services.
- The company was founded in 1984.
- The company is headquartered in Ulsan, South Korea.
