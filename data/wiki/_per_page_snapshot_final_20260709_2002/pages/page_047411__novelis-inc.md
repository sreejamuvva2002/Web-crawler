## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is the world leader in aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Novelis announced the expansion of its research partnership with the Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech) Novelis Innovation Hub.
- The expanded partnership focuses on materials and process research to help Novelis reach a net zero carbon footprint by 2050.
- The collaboration is already underway and utilizes high-throughput development techniques combined with artificial intelligence to accelerate innovation.
- The partnership aims to develop a new generation of electric vehicle (EV) batteries that use aluminum to improve performance.
- The expansion program specifically targets improving battery performance by increasing the amount of recycled aluminum used in the batteries.
