# Continental Tire the Americas, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-18

**Source:** [Continental Announces Leadership Transition in its Replacement Tires Business in the Americas](https://www.continental-tires.com/us/en/about-us/newsroom/truck-and-bus/continental-announces-leadership-transition-in-the-americas/)  ·  `page_018867`

## Facts

- Continental has announced the retirement of Dr. Jochen Etzel, Chief Executive Officer of Continental Tire the Americas, LLC, effective May 1, 2025.
- Tansu Işık will succeed Dr. Etzel as Head of Continental’s Business Area Replacement Tires the Americas and will report directly to Christian Koetz, Executive Board Member of Continental and Head of Continental Tires.
- Dr. Etzel has served in his capacity with Continental’s tire business in the Americas since 2011.
- Dr. Etzel played an important role in uniting various Continental tire activities in the region and strengthening their competitive position, contributing to the company’s overall growth and success.
- Dr. Etzel began his career as a lawyer in Continental’s corporate law department before taking over a financial manager position responsible for developing the company’s tire business outside of Europe and leading the corporate Mergers and Acquisitions team.
- Christian Koetz stated that Jochen’s impact on Continental’s tire business has been immeasurable and thanked him for his leadership, dedication, and outstanding contributions throughout his tenure.
- Tansu Işık brings more than 20 years of international experience in marketing, supply chain, strategy, and key account management within Continental’s tires business.
- Işık began his career as a Business Development Specialist in Istanbul, Türkiye, before moving to Continental Tires’ Headquarters in Hanover, Germany, to become Head of Pricing for the Europe, Middle East and Africa (EMEA) region.
- Işık subsequently moved to Shanghai, China, as Director and then Vice President for Key Account Management (OE) China and the Asia-Pacific region (APAC).
- From 2017, Işık worked as Head of Marketing for Continental’s replacement tire business in the EMEA region before leading the Strategic Business Development and Global Marketing organization.
- Işık currently serves as Head of Region South & Channels Fleet and OE Trailer for Continental replacement tire business in the EMEA region, overseeing business operations in multiple countries and leading the truck tire business there.
- Işık is responsible for the retread and digital solutions business in the EMEA region and holds an Executive MBA from Koç University along with trainings at INSEAD, Duke University, and Ashridge University.
- Continental was founded in 1871 and generates sales of €39.7 billion, currently employing around 190,000 people in 55 countries and markets.
- The Tires group sector offers a premium portfolio encompassing car, truck, bus, two-wheel, and specialty tires as well as smart solutions and services for fleets and tire retailers.
- In fiscal 2024, the Tires group sector generated preliminary sales of €13.9 billion.
- Continental's tire division employs more than 57,000 people worldwide and operates 20 production and 16 development sites.
- Press contact for Continental Tires – The Americas is Allison Zora, Head of Brand and Public Relations, located at 1794 MacMillan Park Drive, Fort Mill, SC 29707.
- ContiConnect Tire Monitoring Integration delivers actionable tire insights for every trailer in a fleet and is seamlessly integrated with leading telematics provider Samsara.
- ContiConnect digital tire management provides valuable insights into truck or bus tires to allow for safer, more cost-efficient fleet operations.
