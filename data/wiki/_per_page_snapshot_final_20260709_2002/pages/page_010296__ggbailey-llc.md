## Facts

- GGBAILEY is part of the RACEMARK International group of companies that include manufacturing plants located in the United States and Europe.
- Manufacturing plants are dedicated to the production of automobile car mats, supplying premier brands in the automotive industry.
- The company designs its own carpet based on the highest quality specifications.
- Custom-fit patterns for each vehicle are done to the highest engineering standards and fit the exact contours of the vehicle.
- Production processes do not cut corners to speed up manufacturing.
- GGBAILEY is proudly family owned and operated and built on four decades of devotion to automobiles and accessories.
- The direct to consumer division was founded on the principles of innovation, driving products to new levels of functionality and design.
- GGBAILEY’s products are made in the USA and Switzerland and are ready to ship in two business days.
- Ginger Cannon Bailey serves as CEO – RACEMARK International, Co-Founder | CEO GGBAILEY.
- The Head of Sales & New Business Development for RACEMARK International also serves as Co-Founder & President of GGBAILEY, LLC & Aftermarket.
