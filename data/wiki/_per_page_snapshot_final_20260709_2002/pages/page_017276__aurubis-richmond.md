# Aurubis Richmond

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-24

**Source:** [Aurubis Richmond](https://www.aurubis.com/richmond)  ·  `page_017276`

## Facts

- Aurubis is a global leader in non-ferrous metals and the world’s largest copper recycler.
- On September 24, 2025, Aurubis fired up the largest and most technologically advanced secondary smelter ever built in the US.
- The hot commissioning marks a milestone for Aurubis’ $800 million investment at the US site.
- The facility keeps valuable metals for the US market in the loop, including copper, nickel, tin, and zinc.
- AI and data centers, energy infrastructure, electrification, and defense fuel the growing demand for critical metals supplied by the facility.
- The project strengthens supply security by making US supply chains more resilient, enhancing domestic security, and reducing America’s dependence on foreign critical metals.
- Aurubis Richmond is the nation’s largest and most advanced secondary smelter, specialized in multimetal recycling.
- Approximately $800 million has been invested in the US site.
- The facility creates around 240 high-quality and well-paid industrial jobs.
- Aurubis buys a broad range of recyclable materials, including copper scrap, e-scrap, and complex industrial residues.
- Aurubis experts review customer material specs to provide recovery solutions for the recycled materials.
