## Facts

- Founded in 1952 and still family owned and operated.
- One of the largest and most diverse commercial truck, van, and automotive equipment manufacturers in the U.S.
- Grew from a single service station in Smyrna, GA, into a thriving collection of industrial brands over the last seven decades.
- Each brand focuses on manufacturing smart, efficient apparatus to help keep fire, fleet, service, and military customers across the country on the move and ready to serve.
- Family-owned company of brands dedicated to the manufacturing and upfitting of excellent American work trucks for a variety of private and public sectors.
- Based out of a 700,000 square foot headquarters in Milledgeville, GA.
- Operates a second facility in La Grange, TX.
