# Volvo Car USA LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-01-07

**Source:** [Volvo Car USA and Canada reports sales of 138,647 in 2024, with strong electrified results | Volvo Cars Media US](https://www.volvocars.com/us/media/press-releases/769E08C3F8C31F4E/)  ·  `page_014186`

## Facts

- Volvo Car USA and Canada reported sales of 138,647 cars for the full year 2024, down 2.1 percent compared with last year.
- Electrified models, defined as those with a fully electric or plug-in hybrid powertrain, accounted for 35.4 percent of sales during 2024, up 22.3 percent compared to last year.
- In December, Volvo Car USA and Canada sold 15,131 cars, up 2.1 percent over the same period last year.
- Electrified models represented 35.7 percent of December sales, an increase of 44.5 percent compared with the same period last year.
- For the full year 2024, sales in the US reached 125,243 cars, down 2.7 percent compared with the previous year.
- Electrified models accounted for 34.3 percent of US sales during the year, up 20.3 percent compared to last year.
- December sales in the US reached 14,077 cars, up 3.4 percent over the same period last year.
- Sales of electrified models represented 33.9 percent of US December sales, an increase of 47.6 percent compared to the same period last year.
- In 2024, Canada set a new annual sales record with 13,404 cars sold, up 4.2 percent compared with the previous record-setting year.
- Sales of electrified models accounted for 46.1 percent of Canadian sales during the year, up 38.2 percent compared to last year.
- December sales in Canada were down 12.7 percent over the same period last year, with a volume of 1,054 cars.
- Sales of electrified models accounted for 59.6 percent of Canadian December sales, an increase of 24.1 percent compared to the same period last year.
- In the US, the XC60 compact luxury SUV was the top-selling model with a record-setting 5,245 cars sold during December.
- The XC90 midsize luxury SUV followed as the second best-selling US model in December with 5,006 cars sold.
- The XC40 small luxury SUV was the third best-selling US model in December with 2,591 cars sold.
- Mike Cottone, President of Volvo Car USA and Canada, stated that demand continued through the end of the year, especially for electrified models.
- Volvo Car USA LLC is headquartered in Mahwah, New Jersey.
- Volvo Car USA LLC is a subsidiary of Volvo Car Group of Gothenburg, Sweden (Nasdaq Stockholm VOLCAR B).
- Volvo Car USA provides sales, marketing, parts, service, technology, and training support to Volvo automobile retailers in the United States since the 1950s.
- Operations include a manufacturing campus in Ridgeville, SC that underlines the company’s long-term commitment to the U.S. as a key market.
- VCUSA maintains offices in California and parts distribution and training centers across the country.
- Volvo Car Canada Ltd. is a subsidiary of Volvo Car Group of Gothenburg, Sweden.
- Volvo Car Canada provides marketing, sales, parts, service, technology, and training support to 37 Volvo automobile retailers across Canada.
- For the full year 2023, Volvo Car Group recorded a record-breaking core operating profit of SEK 25.6 billion.
- Revenue in 2023 amounted to an all-time high of SEK 399.3 billion.
- Global sales for Volvo Car Group reached a record 708,716 cars in 2023.
- Volvo Cars was founded in 1927 and sells to customers in more than 100 countries.
- Volvo Cars is listed on the Nasdaq Stockholm exchange under the ticker “VOLCAR B”.
- Volvo Cars' ambition is to become a fully electric car maker with a commitment to be a climate-neutral company by 2040.
- As of December 2023, Volvo Cars employed approximately 43,400 full-time employees.
- Volvo Cars' head office, product development, marketing, and administration functions are mainly located in Gothenburg, Sweden.
- Volvo Cars' production plants are located in Gothenburg, Ghent (Belgium), South Carolina (US), Chengdu, Daqing, and Taizhou (China).
- The company has R&D and design centres in Gothenburg and Shanghai (China).
