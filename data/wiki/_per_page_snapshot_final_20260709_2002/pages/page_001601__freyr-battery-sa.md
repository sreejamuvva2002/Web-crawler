# FREYR Battery SA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-11

**Source:** [FREYR buys Georgia site for battery plant, sees $1.7 bln initial capital cost](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/freyr-buys-georgia-battery-plant-135727966.html)  ·  `page_001601`

## Facts

- Luxembourg-based FREYR Battery SA purchased a site in Coweta County, Georgia for its Giga America battery plant.
- The company projected an initial capital spend of $1.7 billion for the project.
- FREYR plans to undertake detailed plant engineering in the coming months.
- The company expects to create more than 720 jobs in the region.
- Georgia is experiencing a string of electric vehicle (EV) and battery announcements.
- SK Innovation has a battery unit among the recent EV and battery developments in Georgia.
- Hyundai Motor Co is developing a $5.54 billion EV and battery plant in Georgia for which the company broke ground last month.
- Chief Executive Officer Tom Jensen said that with the recent passage of the Inflation Reduction Act, the company expects U.S. demand for ESS (energy storage system), passenger EV and other electric mobility applications to grow rapidly over the next decade.
- The company is evaluating value accretive upstream and downstream modules and additional cell production lines.
- These evaluations are expected to bring FREYR's total capital investments to more than $2.6 billion through 2029.
