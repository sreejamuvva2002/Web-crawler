# Aspen Aerogels

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2015-11-10

**Source:** [New jobs coming to Statesboro with Aspen Aerogels plant](https://www.wtoc.com/story/30475467/new-jobs-coming-to-statesboro-with-aspen-aerogels-plant/)  ·  `page_052711`

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels announced plans to build a new plant in Statesboro/Bulloch County, Georgia.
- The company will invest $70 million in the new facility.
- The plant is expected to create 100 new jobs.
- The facility will be located at the Gateway Industrial Park.
- The new plant will be situated near Great Dane.
- Aspen Aerogels manufactures specific insulation for refineries, petroleum plants, and other uses.
- The deal took 11 months to finalize after the company's first visit to Statesboro last December.
- The company visited the area about half a dozen times during the negotiation process.
- Economic Development Director Benjy Thompson stated that adding jobs from several small to medium plants helps broaden and stabilize the local economy.
- Thompson noted that the new industry brings diversity to the community and attracts people with different skills to fill those jobs.
- Aspen Aerogels has plans to expand the facility, though no timeline or details were provided.
- Hiring procedures and opening dates have not yet been announced.
