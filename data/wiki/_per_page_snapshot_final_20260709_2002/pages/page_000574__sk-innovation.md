# SK Innovation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [SK Innovation expands its investment in its Georgia, US battery plant](https://www.evspecifications.com/en/news/d598241)  ·  `page_000574`

## Facts

- SK Innovation is expanding its investment in its Georgia, US battery plant.
- The total investment of KRW 1.9 trillion, based on net foreign capital, is the largest in the history of Georgia.
- It is estimated that more than 2,000 jobs will be created.
- If further investment is realized, the impact on the state's economy will amount to several tens of trillion won in the coming decades, even considering the backward effect.
- SK Innovation will secure a stable supply of batteries to nearby global automakers by having the only large battery plant in Georgia.
- From 2022, the plant's production will be supplied to Volkswagen's plant in nearby Chattanooga, Tennessee.
- SK Innovation plans to open the era of a full-fledged "3rd generation electric vehicle" by incorporating cutting-edge battery technology into its Georgia battery plant.
- The third generation of electric vehicles has a range of more than 500 km on a single charge.
- SK Innovation has been producing prototypes for 3rd generation EV batteries and will begin mass production in 2022.
- It is the only Korean manufacturer to produce its own separator by securing Lithium-ion Battery Separator (LiBS) technology and production capacity.
- It has introduced differentiated methods in cell production by eliminating the gluing process, reducing production steps and giving a technical advantage over others in performance and margin.
- SK Innovation produces cells by cutting electrodes in a single sheet and alternately stacking them with separators (Zigzag Stacking).
