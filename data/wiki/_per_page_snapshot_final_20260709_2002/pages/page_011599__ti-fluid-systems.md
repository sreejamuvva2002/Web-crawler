## Facts

- TI Fluid Systems is an automotive components manufacturer.
- TI Fluid Systems posted interim profits that came in ahead of expectations on Thursday.
- TI Fluid Systems said it was confident that adjusted underlying earnings margins would continue to expand throughout FY24.
- TI Fluid's adjusted pre-tax earnings were up 2.7% year-on-year at €135.5m, beating market expectations for a print of €131.0m.
- TI Fluid's EBIT margins expanded from 7.5% to 7.9%, above the 7.4% targeted by the group.
- TI Fluid's revenues dropped 1.4% on a constant currency basis to €1.7bn.
- TI Fluid's bookings increased 11% to €1.5bn.
- TI Fluid's bookings included €600.0m worth of electric vehicle wins.
- TI Fluid stated that productivity and efficiency measures put in place at the start of FY24 reaffirmed its confidence in expanding adjusted EBIT margins moving ahead.
- TI Fluid increased its full-year adjusted EBIT margin expectation to above 7.6%.
- The increase in margin expectation was notwithstanding a slight decline in revenue at constant currency due to the recent softening of the 2024 industry outlook.
- As of 0925 BST, TI Fluid shares were up 11.46% at 124.84p.
