# Kia Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-04-20

**Source:** [Kia to invest $200 million in US plant, build EV9 SUV in 2024 By Reuters](https://www.investing.com/news/stock-market-news/kia-to-invest-200-million-in-georgia-plant-build-ev9-suv-in-2024-3124586)  ·  `page_010217`

## Facts

- Kia announced it will invest more than $200 million in its assembly plant in West Point, Georgia.
- The investment is to begin production of Kia's electric EV9 SUV next year (2024).
- Kia will add about 200 jobs to build the three-row SUV alongside four other models at the West Point, Georgia, plant.
- The EV9 will be the first Kia electric vehicle assembled in North America.
- Assembly in North America is a requirement for vehicles to be eligible for up to $7,500 in U.S. consumer electric vehicle tax credits under the $430 billion U.S. Inflation Reduction Act (IRA) signed in August 2022.
- Electric carmaker Rivian Automotive is building a $5 billion plant in Georgia.
- Hyundai Motor Group and SK On said in December they will build a new battery manufacturing plant in Georgia to supply Kia's U.S. assembly plants.
- Hyundai and SK aim to begin operations at their joint facility in 2025.
- Stakeholders estimate the Hyundai-SK project will create more than 3,500 new jobs through approximately $4-5 billion of investment in Georgia's Bartow County.
- Hyundai separately broke ground in October on a $5.54 billion EV and battery plant in Georgia's Bryan County.
- SK Innovation opened a $2.6 billion battery plant in Commerce, Georgia, in January 2022 that is producing batteries for the Ford F-150 EV.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp said more than $22.7 billion have been invested in Georgia with more than 28,400 jobs created since 2020 in the EV supply chain.
