# Adient

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-10-31

**Source:** [Adient Appoints Jerome Dorlack As EVP | citybiz](https://www.citybiz.co/article/341209/adient-appoints-jerome-dorlack-as-evp/)  ·  `page_016727`

## Facts

- Adient announced that Jerome Dorlack will be appointed executive vice president and chief financial officer, effective Dec. 1, 2022.
- Jeff Stafeil will resign from his role as Adient’s executive vice president and chief financial officer, effective Nov. 30, 2022.
- Jim Conklin will become the executive vice president, Americas, also effective Dec. 1, 2022.
- Doug Del Grosso, Adient’s president and chief executive officer, stated that Jerome has been a key member of the leadership team and they are fortunate to have someone of his caliber step into the CFO role.
- Doug Del Grosso noted that Jim’s long tenure and deep knowledge make him ideally positioned to lead the Americas region.
- Doug Del Grosso expressed confidence that Adient is strongly positioned to continue driving value for stakeholders due to its leading market position, leadership depth, and talented employees.
- Doug Del Grosso thanked Jeff Stafeil for his commitment and contributions since joining the company in 2016.
- Jeff Stafeil stated it has been an honor to work with a dedicated team and expressed confidence that Adient is well-positioned for the future.
- Jim Conklin has been with Adient in various roles of increasing responsibility since October 2000, most recently serving as vice president, North Americas operations.
- Jerome Dorlack has served as Adient’s executive vice president, Americas, since 2019.
- Jerome Dorlack previously served as vice president and chief purchasing officer of Adient from 2018 to 2019.
- Prior to joining Adient, Jerome Dorlack served as senior vice president and president, electrical distribution system and president, South America of Aptiv plc from 2017 to 2018.
- Prior to that, Jerome Dorlack served as vice president, powertrain systems and general manager, global powertrain products of Delphi Automotive plc from 2016 to 2017.
- Jerome Dorlack previously served as executive vice president global procurement of ZF Friedrichshafen from 2015 to 2016.
- Jerome Dorlack previously served as vice president, global purchasing, supplier development and logistics of ZF Friedrichshafen from 2013 to 2015.
- Adient operates approximately 75,000 employees in 31 countries.
- Adient operates more than 200 manufacturing/assembly plants worldwide.
- Adient produces and delivers automotive seating for all major OEMs.
- Adient's products are integrated into more than 20 million vehicles every year.
