## Facts

- GSC Steel Stamping, LLC is a minority and family-owned and operated automotive parts manufacturer.
- The company provides Body in White frame assembly brackets to some of the world’s leading OEMs and tier one suppliers.
- GSC Steel Stamping offers competitively priced metal stamping, engineering, welded assemblies, and tooling services.
- In June 2023, GSC acquired Dixien, LLC’s assets.
- The acquisition continues the 43-year history of automotive parts stamping at its Lake City, GA location.
- The company's facility in Lake City, GA spans 200,000 square feet.
- Prior to leading GSC Steel Stamping, CEO Vincent Henderson purchased and led the turn-around of two award-winning automotive stamping operations in Michigan.
- Those previous Michigan operations were regularly recognized by customers for excellence in overall performance, quality, and on-time delivery.
- The company continuously re-evaluates ways to improve all its processes.
- GSC invests in advanced technology and hires top-notch staff.
- The plant operations management team alone possesses over 200 years of combined stamping experience.
- The company takes pride in consistently delivering high-quality, precision parts on time.
- The company's mission is to provide quality parts, shipped on time, every time.
