# Ecoplastic Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Ecoplastic Corporation is investing $205 million in order to establish a new facility in Right to Work Bulloch County, Georgia.
- The investment will create 456 new jobs.
- Ecoplastic Corporation is establishing a production facility for electric vehicle parts in Bulloch County, Georgia.
- The facility will provide a stable supply of high quality products to client HMGMA.
- Ecoplastic Corporation looks forward to contributing to the economic development of the State of Georgia and Bulloch County through the project.
- Ecoplastic Corporation appreciates the support of the State and County governments for the initiative.
- Ecoplastic has decades of experience as a supplier for Kia and Hyundai.
- The company is building the supplier network for the Hyundai metaplant in Bryan County.
- Suppliers are spreading out across the region to provide well-paying, high-tech manufacturing jobs for Georgians.
- Bulloch County officials welcome Ecoplastic to their community and cite the area's location, workforce, and quality of life as factors that will help them succeed.
