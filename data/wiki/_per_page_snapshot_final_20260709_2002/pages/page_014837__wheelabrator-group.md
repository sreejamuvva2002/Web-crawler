## Facts

- Wheelabrator’s Contract Manufacturing Service (WCMS) operation is a full service machining and fabrication facility dedicated to producing products to the highest quality standard at competitive prices.
- The facility works with materials including carbon steel, alloy steel, stainless steel, and manganese steel.
- WCMS Machine Shop equipment includes CNC machining centers, lathes and mills, a horizontal boring mill, a vertical turning mill, manual lathes, mills, and drills.
- WCMS Fabrication Shop supports plasma or oxy-fuel burning, sawing, bending, punching, rolling, drilling, welding, blast cleaning, and painting.
- WCMS operates within a 135,000 sq ft facility with an overhead crane lifting capacity up to 30 tons and a 33ft ceiling under hook.
- The site features a wide range of Wheelabrator shot blast machines capable of cleaning products in either small or large batches.
- Wheelabrator has been certified to the ISO9001 quality standard since 1997.
- The company is classified as a Job Shop / Contract Manufacturer.
- The facility employs between 200 and 500 people.
- Wheelabrator Group is located in LaGrange, Georgia, 30240, United States.
