## Facts

- A textile plant in Barnesville, about 60 miles south of Atlanta, is laying off 235 people and closing by the end of the year.
- The closure marks the end of a nearly 40-year chapter at a historic mill.
- Continental Tire The Americas will lay off all its employees at Aldora Mills, its Lamar County facility.
- The layoffs were announced via a Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification (WARN) filed with the state at the end of January.
- Continental Tire The Americas is headquartered in South Carolina.
- Continental Tire The Americas is a subsidiary of Germany-based Continental AG.
- Layoffs will be spread across four rounds scheduled for March, April, June, and December.
