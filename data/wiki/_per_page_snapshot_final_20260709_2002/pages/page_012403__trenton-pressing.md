## Facts

- Trenton Pressing is a successful mid-sized Tier One manufacturing supplier located in Georgia.
- The company was established in the highly competitive automotive sector.
- Trenton Pressing provides engineered stamping and welding assembly solutions.
- The company produces a wide range of products through use of high-quality presses, world-class assembly and other manufacturing technologies.
- Trenton Pressing's engineering team assists customers with design for manufacturability, product design assistance, FEA analysis, robotic weld and assembly cell design and more.
- The company uses automatic and hand presses to stamp out metal components.
- Trenton Pressing conducts automated inspections during assembly to ensure parts are fully functional.
- The company operates as a direct Tier 1 supplier to customers such as BMW and Club Car.
- Trenton Pressing works collaboratively with customers to understand challenges and opportunities and to develop innovative and practical solutions.
- The company leverages its manufacturing experience, industry expertise and the passion of its people to help drive its success.
- Trenton Pressing offers worldwide shipping for its products.
