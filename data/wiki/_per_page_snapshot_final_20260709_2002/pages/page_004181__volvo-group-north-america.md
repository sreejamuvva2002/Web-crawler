# Volvo Group North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-13

## Facts

- U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) recognizes Volvo Group North America for certification to the Superior Energy Performance 50001 (SEP 50001) program.
- The certification applies to three U.S. facilities operated by the company.
- One facility is the New River Valley plant located in Dublin, Virginia.
- A second facility is Mack Trucks Inc. located in Macungie, Pennsylvania.
- A third facility is Volvo Powertrain North America located in Hagerstown, Maryland.
