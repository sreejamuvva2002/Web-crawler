# Shaw Industries

## Facts

- Shaw Industries is headquartered in Dalton, Georgia.
- The flooring company is a unit of billionaire investor Warren Buffett's Berkshire Hathaway Inc conglomerate.
- The company is growing so quickly that finding good talent 'can be challenging,' according to Brian Cooksey, director of workforce development.
- Brian Cooksey serves as the director of workforce development at Shaw Industries.
- Shaw Industries is funding programs in local high schools and even middle schools to get students interested early in the high-tech manufacturing in which it specializes.
