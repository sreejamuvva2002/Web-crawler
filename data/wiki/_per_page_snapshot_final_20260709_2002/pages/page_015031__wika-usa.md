## Facts

- WIKA USA is expanding its strategic partnership with AWC to bring WIKA precision products and services to 12 states in the southeastern US with local AWC support.
- WIKA's comprehensive evaluation affirmed that AWC’s expanded geographic footprint will deliver advantages to existing and prospective clientele.
- The collaboration will not lead to any alterations in the manufacturing or design of the WIKA product portfolio, assuring steadfast maintenance of product quality and uninterrupted service.
- WIKA and AWC have maintained an enduring partnership spanning nearly a decade.
- Todd Gardner, WIKA USA President, stated that the expanded geographic partnership marks a significant milestone reinforcing the strength of their partner relationship.
- Todd Gardner noted that the strategic expansion bolsters commitment to serving partners more effectively and exemplifies synergy fostering innovation and excellence.
- The expanded partnership guarantees a smooth and streamlined transition for customers.
- WIKA Group employs 11,200 people globally and is a market leader in pressure and temperature measurement.
- WIKA sets the standard in the measurement of level, force, flow, and calibration technology.
- WIKA's portfolio includes high-precision products, solutions, and services aimed at making sensing technology smarter for a sustainable future.
- AWC is an Automation and Controls Technology company with 600+ employee-owners.
- AWC focuses on two main areas: Process Technology & Instrumentation and Industrial Automation.
- AWC operates as an authorized local sales, support, and service partner to leading manufacturing partners.
- AWC offers a full suite of products, services, and integrated systems to optimize US Manufacturing and Utility operations.
- AWC was founded in 1965.
- AWC operates 32 full-service locations across the U.S., spanning from Denver, CO to Tampa, FL.
- AWC maintains a heavy concentration of locations along the Gulf Coast of TX and LA.
