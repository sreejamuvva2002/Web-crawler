## Facts

- Hindalco Industries announced a halt in production at its Novelis plant in Oswego, New York, following a fire on September 16, 2025.
- The company is currently investigating the cause and assessing the impact on operations at the Oswego plant.
- The plant is insured for property damage and business interruption.
- Novelis Inc., the US subsidiary of Hindalco Industries, reported a 36% drop in net income to $96 million for Q1FY26.
- Despite a 13% rise in net sales to $4.7 billion, adjusted EBITDA fell 17% to $416 million.
- The decline in profitability was attributed to restructuring charges and higher scrap prices.
- Beverage packaging shipments increased during the period.
- Novelis Inc. has renewed its multi-year partnership with thyssenkrupp Aerospace for supplying specialised aerospace-grade aluminium from facilities in Germany and China.
- The agreement with thyssenkrupp Aerospace builds on their successful collaboration since 2014.
- Shipments to the aerospace segment accounted for 3% of Novelis' total shipments in FY24.
- Novelis Inc. plans to raise USD 750 million through bonds.
- The bond proceeds will be used to repay USD 738 million of debt and to fund their balance sheet.
- Novelis reported a decline in net income for the September quarter, but net sales rose due to higher aluminum prices and increased shipments.
- Aditya Birla group company will prioritize projects worth $3.3 billion at its US subsidiary Novelis over 2024-28 (April-March).
- These prioritized projects are already underway, with spending on other growth projects being staggered.
- Projects include the greenfield rolling capacity in the US, and debottlenecking and recycling units across regions.
- Steve Fisher is the President and CEO of Novelis Inc.
