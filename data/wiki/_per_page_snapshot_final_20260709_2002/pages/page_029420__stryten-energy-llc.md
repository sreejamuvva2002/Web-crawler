# Stryten Energy LLC

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC is a U.S.-based energy storage solutions provider headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Stryten Energy announced an investment in REVKIT, a new digital platform that brings together a dynamic community of automotive enthusiasts.
- Stryten will become the preferred provider of advanced automotive batteries on REVKIT’s digital platform.
- Mike Judd serves as CEO and President of Stryten Energy.
- “Stryten Energy’s investment in REVKIT expands our existing footprint within the automotive aftermarket to reach consumers looking for premium lead and lithium batteries to power their custom vehicles,” said Mike Judd.
- “As the lead investor in this first round of funding, we will become the preferred provider of automotive batteries for the custom build kits featured on the REVKIT platform,” said Mike Judd.
- Over 75 million vehicles in the U.S. are customized annually.
- Over 70 percent of parts are installed by owners.
- Almost 80 percent use social media to validate their purchases.
- The capital investment from Stryten Energy will allow REVKIT to grow its platform, add features, and expand into other categories.
- Dan Hart is the Co-Founder and CEO of REVKIT.
- “We see how technology helps drive passion,” said Dan Hart.
- “REVKIT bridges the gap between offroad enthusiasts and the ever-changing world of vehicle modifications,” said Dan Hart.
- “Our user-generated content creates a powerful data warehouse and sales lead generator,” said Dan Hart.
- “The funding from Stryten Energy will help generate greater awareness among automotive aftermarket retailers and introduce more businesses to this passionate community,” said Dan Hart.
- Future enhancements to the REVKIT platform include expanded vehicle verticals, an enhanced peer-to-peer and B2C marketplace, streamlined B2C interactions via chatbots, and on-demand business intelligence for advertisers.
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- Stryten powers vehicles and equipment ranging from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- Stryten’s stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- REVKIT has been active since 2017 and has served over a million enthusiasts for vehicle modification inspiration.
- REVKIT allows users to create virtual garages, itemize upgrades into over a dozen categories, track, find, and buy parts, and share build stories with pictures and videos.
