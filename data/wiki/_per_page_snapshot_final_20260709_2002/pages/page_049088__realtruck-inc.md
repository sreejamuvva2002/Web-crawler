# RealTruck, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-17

**Source:** [REALTRUCK, INC. ACQUIRES MOUNTAIN TOP](https://realtruck.eu/blogs/news/realtruck-inc-acquires-mountain-top)  ·  `page_049088`

## Facts

- On Oct. 17, 2023, RealTruck, Inc. acquired Mountain Top, the leading designer and manufacturer of truck bed accessories outside North America.
- The acquisition accelerates RealTruck’s international expansion and complements its recent acquisition of Mexico-based brand Go Rhino.
- Mountain Top supplies major original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and sells truck accessories throughout Europe, Asia, Australia, and the U.S.
- Mountain Top has state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in Denmark, Thailand, and the U.S.
- Mountain Top is the only European truck bed cover supplier of scale certified by the International Automotive Task Force (IATF).
- Mountain Top’s flagship product line is the premium retractable truck bed cover that comes in both electronic and manual versions.
- In August 2023, RealTruck acquired IDDEA (Industria Diseñadora De Autopartes, S.A. De C.V.), which goes to market under the Go Rhino brand.
- Go Rhino holds 40 issued and pending patents for innovative premium products developed and manufactured for truck, Jeep, and SUV owners.
- RealTruck’s acquisition of Mountain Top further expands RealTruck’s global footprint and supply base in the OEM-focused European truck bed accessories market.
- RealTruck is headquartered in Ann Arbor, Mich., with over 5,000 associates across 47 locations throughout North America.
- RealTruck holds more than 630 patents and sells products through more than 12,000 dealer locations and automotive (OEM) partnerships.
