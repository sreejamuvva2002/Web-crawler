# Steves & Sons, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-21

**Source:** [Manufacturer Steves & Sons, Inc. to Invest Over $100 Million in Jackso | Georgia](https://georgia.org/press-releases/2022/manufacturer-steves-sons-inc-invest-over-100-million-jackson-county)  ·  `page_002426`

## Facts

- Gov. Brian P. Kemp announced that Steves & Sons, Inc. will invest over $100 million and create 170 jobs over the next three years as it builds a new facility in Jackson County.
- This is the company’s second expansion announcement in Georgia in the last year.
- Based in San Antonio, Texas for six generations, Steves & Sons offers a variety of interior and exterior door styles and types.
- Steves & Sons serves other leading millwork distributors across the country and has been recognized four times as vendor of the year by The Home Depot.
- Last December, Steves & Sons announced a new facility in Turner County that will ultimately create 200 jobs in Ashburn.
- Edward Steves, CEO of Steves & Sons, stated that Georgia has proven to be extremely helpful for the company's long-term manufacturing and distribution needs.
- Sam Steves, President of Steves & Sons, added that Georgia's abundant wood fiber sources and world-class port in Savannah are critical to their expansion into Georgia.
- The new 310,000-square-foot facility will be located near US-441 and Athens.
- The facility will produce millions of interior molded door skins annually for use in new housing construction, repair, and remodeling.
- The company will be hiring skilled operators, electricians, and engineers.
- Scott Martin, Chairman of the Jackson County Industrial Development Authority, stated that Steves & Sons' decision to locate in Jackson County is proof of the area's thriving business climate.
- Global Commerce Senior Project Manager Kristen Miller represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) on this competitive project.
- The project was completed in partnership with the Jackson County Area Chamber of Commerce and Georgia Quick Start.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that Steves & Sons will take advantage of Georgia Quick Start to fill newly created positions.
- For over 156 years, Steves & Sons, Inc. has provided quality products for homes with an emphasis on doors, built on a commitment to quality, superior craftsmanship, style, and environmental responsibility expressed in long-lasting products incorporating recycled elements.
