# YKK AP America Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-06

**Source:** [YKK AP holds ribbon cutting for new residential manufacturing facility in Macon | LBM Journal](https://www.lbmjournal.com/industry-news/news/15785600/ykk-ap-holds-ribbon-cutting-for-new-residential-manufacturing-facility-in-macon)  ·  `page_016188`

## Facts

- YKK AP America Inc. held a ribbon cutting ceremony to mark the official opening of its first greenfield residential manufacturing facility in Macon-Bibb County, GA.
- The ribbon cutting event was held in Macon with Commissioner Pat Wilson of the Georgia Department of Economic Development, Mayor Lester Miller of Macon-Bibb County, Chairman Robby Fountain of the Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority, and other local leaders in attendance.
- Mayor Lester Miller stated that YKK AP’s expansion into a new, leading-edge facility is a testament to its strong connection with the community and marked the beginning of a new chapter offering more opportunities to enrich the economy, improve the lives of families, and strengthen Macon's position as a hub of innovation and industry.
- The facility completed construction in December 2023.
- The facility began operations in January 2024.
- The facility currently employs more than 250 employees in the Macon community.
- Oliver Stepe, President & CEO of YKK AP America Inc., stated that the event marked more than a celebration of the new residential manufacturing facility, symbolized the company's growth and confidence in its business and employees moving forward, and expressed gratitude to the State of Georgia, the Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority, and all partners for helping celebrate this important milestone in the company’s journey.
