# Beaver Manufacturing Company, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Beaver Manufacturing expands with acquisition of Intercord Thüringen](https://www.innovationintextiles.com/fibres-yarns-fabrics/beaver-manufacturing-expands-with-acquisition-of-intercord-thringen/)  ·  `page_052847`

## Facts

- Beaver Manufacturing Company, Inc. (BMC) is a North American supplier of technical fibre reinforcements for hoses and other industrial applications based in Mansfield, GA.
- BMC announced plans to expand its global presence by acquiring Intercord Thüringen GmbH of Germany, a producer of high-quality technical fibres.
- The acquisition combines two technologies to increase the company’s customer base worldwide.
- The partnership enables BMC to add RFL treating technology to its product line.
- The acquisition adds a third production plant for BMC across the globe.
- The new manufacturing facility is located in Mühlhausen Thüringen, Germany.
- This German facility rounds out BMC’s worldwide footprint alongside existing plants in the US and Mexico.
- Michael Dubin, Sr. Vice President & Chief Operating Officer of Beaver Manufacturing Company, Inc., stated that the acquisition represents a significant expansion into the European market.
- The acquisition also marks BMC's entry into the tyre and belt industry.
- BMC’s plant in Germany will continue to produce RFL treated yarns and cords.
- The German facility will also produce hose products that incorporate Beaverloc treatments.
- For more than 20 years, Intercord’s technology has produced cords for tyres, hoses, and transmission belts in the automotive and industrial industries.
- Ramazan Yasbay, Managing Director of Intercord who will continue as Managing Director of the German facility for BMC, stated that Beaver Manufacturing advanced the U.S. hose yarn industry with environmentally friendly treated yarn solutions.
- Yasbay noted that adding Beaver’s technology and product line to theirs will increase innovation in this industry throughout Europe and the Americas.
- Despite a sluggish global economy during the pandemic, BMC executives say this partnership will continue its growth.
- The partnership is expected to increase production and create jobs into 2021.
- Only two years after opening its manufacturing site in Mexico, BMC announced this acquisition.
- Executives involved in the deal include Craig Chandler (Vice President of Finance), Michael Dubin (Sr. Vice President & Chief Operating Officer), Ramazan Yasbay (Managing Director for Germany), and Walter vom Stein (former owner of Intercord).
