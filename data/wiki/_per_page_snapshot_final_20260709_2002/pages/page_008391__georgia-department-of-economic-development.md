# Georgia Department of Economic Development

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2023-01

**Source:** [GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson Named ‘Georgian of the Year’ by Georgia Trend - Savannah CEO](https://savannahceo.com/news/2023/01/gdecd-commissioner-pat-wilson-named-georgian-year-georgia-trend/)  ·  `page_008391`

## Facts

- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson was named the 2023 “Georgian of the Year” by Georgia Trend.
- The award recognizes Commissioner Wilson as the top salesperson for the state and commends his work promoting the advantages of Georgia to attract investments and create jobs.
- Wilson credits the state’s success with the team approach Georgia takes when conducting business.
- Wilson maintains a personal focus on keeping relationships strong at every level, including with partners and businesses across the state and the globe.
- In the six years since Wilson was appointed as economic development Commissioner by former Governor Nathan Deal, Georgia maintained its place as the No. 1 state in the nation to do business, earning this recognition for the ninth consecutive year in 2022.
- Former Georgia Governor Sonny Perdue credited Wilson with much of the credit for Georgia being one of the best places to grow a business by communicating all the great things that the Georgia governor and legislature have done during those years.
- Wilson’s leadership and focus on relationship-building helped Georgia play the long game in attracting business and fostering its growth.
- In August 2022, GDEcD announced that in FY22, investments in expansions and new locations totaled more than $21.2 billion and would create 51,132 direct new jobs in the state, setting new records for Georgia.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development’s work providing solutions for companies and keeping communication open were two key factors that helped Georgia land two of its largest-ever investments in FY22.
- Wilson expressed passion for selling Georgia and gratitude for the opportunity to work with governors who gave him the chance to do this.
- GDEcD teammates Lee Thomas, Director of the Georgia Film Office, and David Nuckolls, Executive Director of the Center of Innovation, were also recognized in Georgia Trend’s lists for their contributions to the state.
- GDEcD acts as the “quarterback” that brings partners across the state together to make business happen.
