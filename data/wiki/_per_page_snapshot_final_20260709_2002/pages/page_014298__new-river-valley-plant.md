# New River Valley Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [New River Valley Plant | Volvo Trucks USA](https://www.volvotrucks.us/about-volvo/facilities/nrv-assembly-plant/)  ·  `page_014298`

## Facts

- The New River Valley assembly plant is located on 566 acres at 4881 Cougar Trail Road, P.O. Box 1126, Dublin, VA 24084 U.S.A.
- The facility covers 2.3 million square feet and serves as the largest Volvo truck manufacturing facility in the world.
- The plant is certified under ISO 9001 quality, ISO 14001 environmental, and ISO 50001 energy standards.
- The New River Valley plant produces all Volvo trucks sold in North America.
- Vehicle models manufactured at the site include the VNR, VNR Electric, VNL, VNX, VHD, and VAH.
- Production of the all-new Volvo VNL model at the New River Valley plant is scheduled to start in mid-2024.
