# Hollingsworth & Vose Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hollingsworth & Vose Member Spotlight](https://ny-best.org/page/HollingsworthVoseMemberSpotlight)  ·  `page_008672`

## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose Company is a globally recognized leader in technically advanced fibrous composite and non-woven materials for battery separators, filtration media, and industrial applications.
- The company sells products in more than 80 countries.
- H&V is privately held with headquarters in East Walpole, MA.
- The company operates 12 manufacturing facilities across North America, Europe, and China.
- H&V maintains sales offices in the Americas, Europe, and Asia.
- H&V produces battery separator products at facilities in Easton, NY (Washington County), West Groton, MA, the UK, and China.
- H&V has captured U.S. market share for absorbed glass mat (AGM) separators used in demanding lead-acid battery applications.
- The company has 30 years of experience manufacturing AGM battery separators, positioning it for separator development, testing, validation, and commercial qualification with battery manufacturers.
- Modern passenger cars have greatly increased electrical demand with additional systems such as GPS, displays, entertainment equipment, video cameras, collision avoidance systems, and other electronics.
- H&V delivers AGM battery separators that ensure batteries perform at optimum levels in vehicles with high electrical demand.
- The EnergyGuard® product provides 200% of the tensile strength and 320% greater puncture resistance versus standard glass mat separators.
- These features make the EnergyGuard® product ideal for high-speed automated manufacturing and resilient in challenging environments.
- H&V manufactures separators at regional mills around the world to enable fast delivery and local application.
- Start-Stop micro-hybrids save fuel and reduce emissions by turning off the engine in place of idling.
- This innovation places new demands on car batteries, particularly with respect to partial state of charge management and charge acceptance.
- The duty cycles of a stop-start lead acid battery are much more demanding and require advanced design, manufacture and processing from suppliers to the industry.
- H&V is positioned to supply separator technology to the automotive market based on already established industrial and telecommunications battery systems.
- Advanced designs for these systems can now be applied to the emerging stop-start micro-hybrid automotive market.
- H&V has been the recipient of a previous New York State Department of Energy & Development Authority (NYSERDA) grant for micro-hybrid vehicle battery separator research, which was conducted in their NY facilities.
- H&V currently continues to work with NYSERDA and NY-BEST support on micro-hybrid battery separator research.
- H&V serves as a supplier of key battery separators for this cutting-edge technology.
- Kevin Porter, Director of Grants and Funding at H&V, stated that the company appreciates NYSERDA's support and is pleased to be a member of NY-BEST, participating in a forum to bring industry, academia and government together to foster innovation and research for the stakeholders of the State of New York.
- Established in 1843, H&V is recognized as the technology leader in the manufacture of specialty fibrous composite and nonwoven materials.
- The company has a heritage of investment in research and development as well as manufacturing processes, continually striving to advance the state of the art in technology, production, and quality by employing systems such as Lean and Six Sigma and an internal customized Stage-Gate process (InnoHVision).
- For over 160 years, H&V has combined high-quality raw materials, leading technical expertise, and a broad array of wet and dry manufacturing processes to create materials and media with critical properties demanded by customers, from mechanical strength to precisely controlled pore structure.
