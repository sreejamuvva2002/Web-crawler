# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [TCI Powder Coatings Announces Personnel Changes | 2015-01-16  | PCI Magazine](https://www.pcimag.com/articles/100089-tci-powder-coatings-announces-personnel-changes)  ·  `page_051168`

## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is a powder coatings manufacturer located in Ellaville, GA.
- Steve Jones has been appointed to the new position of Director, National Markets at TCI Powder Coatings.
- Jeff Wilhelms has been appointed to the position of Director of Field Technical Support at TCI Powder Coatings.
- Mike Webb has joined TCI as a Senior Technical Representative.
- In his new role, Jones will continue the company’s pursuit of key market segments and geographies and direct programs supporting marketing and sales efforts throughout North America.
- These programs include marketing and branding, the TruCoater Network, website utilization, and architectural segment development.
- Jones comes to his position with 40 years in the coatings industry and extensive knowledge of powder coatings.
- His work in the past 14 years has been in powder coatings, both with DuPont and with TCI.
- As the new Director of Field Technical Support, Wilhelms is responsible for planning and directing TCI’s technical support to customers in the field.
- Wilhelms will provide the support needed to demonstrate the effectiveness of TCI products and solutions for new potential customers.
- Wilhelms brings 26 years of experience in the coatings industry.
- Webb brings 30 years of experience over a broad range of applications in the coatings industry to his new position.
