# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2010-11-15

**Source:** [Pirelli Pumps Up U.S. Factory in Georgia](https://www.torquenews.com/108/pirelli-pumps-us-factory-georgia)  ·  `page_048417`

## Facts

- Pirelli announced an investment in a U.S. factory in Georgia as part of its strategic growth plan for the North American market.
- The investment aims to help PTNA sustain its market gains and the 30 percent sales increase realized over the past three years.
- Pirelli Tire North America designs, develops, manufactures and markets tires for passenger vehicles in both the original equipment and replacement markets.
- Pirelli Tire North America also markets and distributes tires for motorcycles and motor sports.
- The company's Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) in Georgia employs state-of-the-art technology to manufacture tires for both export and domestic markets.
- Pirelli has just completed its three-year tenure as the official tire supplier to the FIA World Rally Championship.
- Pirelli was awarded the exclusive agreement to supply tires for the FIA World Rally Championship in 2008.
- Since being awarded the agreement in 2008, Pirelli has supplied a total of 27,995 tires for use on 40 WRC events on snow, ice, asphalt and gravel.
- Of those 27,995 tires, only 43 suffered failures on the punishing terrain encountered on world championship rallies during almost 9000 miles of special stages.
- In 2011, Pirelli begins its three-year agreement as the official tire supplier to the FIA Formula One World.
