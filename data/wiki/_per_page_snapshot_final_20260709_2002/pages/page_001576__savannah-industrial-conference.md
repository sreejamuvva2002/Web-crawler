## Facts

- Event ended on Thursday March 27 2025.
- Savannah Industrial market remains in growth mode despite economic and real estate financing headwinds.
- Port of Savannah has seen twenty-foot equivalent unit growth of over 20% year-over-year.
- Active tenant demand for industrial space is approximately 30M SF.
- Hyundai Metaplant grand opening is set for early 2025.
- The Hyundai Metaplant project spans nearly 3,000 acres.
- Hyundai Metaplant will have a profound additional impact on the real estate landscape in the Coastal Georgia region.
- Vacancy has crept up to nearly 10% across the Savannah industrial market due to a rush of deliveries.
- Questions have arisen regarding labor availability to service these properties and how vacancy will continue to be filled in the short term.
- Panel discussion covers Hyundai and its suppliers impact on labor, land availability and valuations, and the impact the Metaplant will have on area industrial assets.
- Panel discussion covers developing, managing and building to compete for today's tenants, including cost effective upgrades and how owners make decisions on where to invest.
- Event location is Hyatt Regency at 2 W Bay Street, Savannah, Georgia 31401 in the Harborside Ballroom.
- Pre-event networking, registration & breakfast runs from 8:00 AM to 9:30 AM.
- Brief networking break runs from 10:15 AM to 10:30 AM.
- Closing networking runs from 11:15 AM to 12:00 PM.
- Valet parking costs $20.00 for 0-4 hours and $35.00 for 4-8 hours.
