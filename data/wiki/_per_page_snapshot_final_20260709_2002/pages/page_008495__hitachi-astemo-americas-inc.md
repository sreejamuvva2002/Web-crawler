# Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc.

## Facts

- Hitachi Automotive Systems Americas, Inc. has had a long involvement in the development of electric motors with high output ratio for use as electric drive motors for vehicles.
- The company adopted the IPM (interior permanent magnet) synchronous motor for use as an electric drive motor for vehicles.
- IPM synchronous motors combine small size, light weight, high output and high efficiency.
- Hitachi Automotive Systems Americas, Inc. is in production on a "hybrid" vehicle.
- The company produces an electric traction motor housing that sits between the internal combustion engine and the gear.
- The traction motor can provide main or supplemental power to the vehicle.
- Hitachi produces an auxiliary electric motor which provides starting ability in internal combustion engines and electrical power generation to charge batteries.
- Hitachi produces an electric motor that drives the CVT transmission oil pump providing power to control the transmission ratio (drive).
- Hitachi Automotive Systems Americas, Inc. is in production of and provides complete system compatibility for EV and PHEV applications.
- The company's other motor and inverter is used in a small Nissan full electric vehicle (Hyper-Mini).
