## Facts

- KBD Group worked as the design-builder for Joon Georgia, a subsidiary of Ajin USA.
- Joon Georgia is constructing a brand-new manufacturing plant in Statesboro, GA.
- The project received first place honors for the AGC Georgia Build Award.
- The facility spans 850,000 square feet.
- Ajin Georgia is located 30 miles west of the upcoming Hyundai EV plant in Bryan County.
- According to a news release from Gov. Brian Kemp’s office, Joon plans to hire 630 workers for the future $317 million parts factory.
