# The Motor Enclave Tampa

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-01-08

**Source:** [The Motor Enclave Tampa Announces Partnership With Pirelli Tire North America](https://www.centralcharts.com/en/news/4512718-the-motor-enclave-tampa-announces-partnership-with-pirelli-tire-north-america)  ·  `page_048474`

## Facts

- The Motor Enclave announced a sponsorship agreement with Pirelli Tire North America to become "The Official Tire" of the venue.
- The partnership branding includes naming the pit lane "The Pirelli Pit Lane".
- The Motor Enclave is a 200-acre experiential motorsports venue located at the corner of I-4 and I-75, less than 15 minutes from downtown Tampa, FL.
- Pirelli will receive exposure in key locations throughout the development, including trademark yellow and red branding throughout the "Pirelli Pit Lane".
- The "Pirelli Pit Lane" is where all events on the 1.6-mile Hermann Tilke-designed performance circuit begin and end.
- The pit lane will incorporate a "Pirelli Podium" featuring authentic Pirelli Formula 1 tires for guest photos.
- Brad Oleshansky, Founder and CEO of The Motor Enclave, stated that Pirelli is synonymous with innovation and quality and aligns with their mission to build the premiere motorsports venue in the world.
- Andre Oosthuizen, Chief Marketing Officer of Pirelli Tire North America, noted they have tracked The Motor Enclave's development since its 2019 announcement.
- Oosthuizen cited the success of Oleshansky's initial project in Michigan, M1 Concourse, as a reason for choosing this platform to highlight Pirelli's full product line across driving surfaces including the performance circuit, vehicle dynamics pad, and off-road experience.
- Pirelli Tire North America includes a Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) facility and research and development center at its Rome, Georgia headquarters.
- Pirelli also operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant in Silao, Mexico, with sales and marketing offices in New York City, Los Angeles, Detroit, Montreal, and Mexico City.
- The company manufactures, distributes, and markets original equipment and replacement tires for export and domestic car/motorcycle applications.
- Pirelli was founded in Milan in 1872 and develops tailor-made tires through partnerships with prestigious manufacturers.
- The Motor Enclave development includes a 1.6-mile Hermann Tilke-designed driving circuit, a 2-acre vehicle dynamics pad, a 100-acre off-road experience with purpose-built trails, a 37,000-square-foot corporate event center, and over 300 privately owned luxury garages.
- Complete details about The Motor Enclave are available on its official website.
