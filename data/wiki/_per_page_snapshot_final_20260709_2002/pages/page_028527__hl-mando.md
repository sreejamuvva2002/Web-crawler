# HL Mando

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-30

**Source:** [HL Mando Recognized as 2024 Supplier of the Year by General Motors](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/hl-mando-recognized-as-2024-supplier-of-the-year-by-general-motors-302442430.html)  ·  `page_028527`

## Facts

- On April 8, 2025, General Motors presented HL Mando with a 2024 Supplier of the Year Award at GM's 33rd annual Supplier of the Year event in Phoenix, Arizona.
- HL Mando is HL Group's leading Tier 1 automotive Software Defined Vehicle (SDV) company.
- HL Mando was selected by General Motors for its 2024 Supplier of the Year award.
- HL Mando has been awarded the Supplier of the Year honor for five consecutive years and 11 times in total.
- The April 8 recognition event was attended by Head of HL Mando Americas region Jason (Jae-hyuk) Kim, R&D Director Jeff Pontius, and Sales Director Alison Ellis.
- A total of 92 companies from 12 countries were recognized as excellent partners to General Motors at the event.
- Less than 1% of General Motors' suppliers are awarded the Supplier of the Year Award.
- HL Mando was recognized in the Brake Apply & Controls category.
- HL Mando's Motor on Caliper (MoC) product received the spotlight and is manufactured at HL Mando's Alabama plant.
- General Motors praised HL Mando for its localization efforts and technological innovation.
- HL Mando's North American localization journey began in 1996.
- The corporation established its first R&D center, Mando Engineering & Sales America (MESA), in Michigan to lay the groundwork for North American expansion.
- HL Mando launched its first manufacturing facility, Mando Corporation America in Alabama (MCA).
- HL Mando won its first Supplier of the Year award in 2003.
- The Alabama plant was completed in 2004.
- A second production facility was added in 2012 to meet growing demand for casting components.
- This second facility became the MCA Georgia Plant.
- In 2013, HL Mando expanded its R&D capabilities by establishing a second R&D center, HL Mechatronics (HM), also in Michigan.
- MESA focuses on development activities up to mass production, while HM specializes in future-oriented automotive technologies.
- HL Mando has recently invested approximately USD 700 million in its North American operations.
- The USD 700 million investment demonstrates HL Mando's commitment to the North American sector and continued focus on cutting-edge technology.
- Jeff Morrison, GM's global chief procurement officer, stated that General Motors recognizes top global suppliers annually to spotlight innovation and resiliency, and together they are bringing advanced technology and a broad portfolio of vehicles to market for customers.
- Jason (Jae-hyuk) Kim, Head of HL Mando Americas region, stated that three decades of North American localization efforts have led to strong customer satisfaction and expressed a look forward to shaping the future with GM through collaboration in automotive software and autonomous driving.
- General Motors' Supplier of the Year awards recognize global suppliers for execution across safety, innovation, and resilience.
- Award selections are made by a global cross-functional team based on 2024 performance and alignment to GM's core values and ambitious goals.
