# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- TCI has been manufacturing powder coatings for more than 35 years.
- TCI aspires to be the premium brand supplier for OEM coatings.
- TCI was founded in Ellaville, GA with a modest plant and a portable building that served as the lab and office space.
- TCI has expanded to become an international company with multiple warehouse locations.
- TCI operates a state of the art product development center.
- TCI employs a staff of several hundred employees focused on servicing customers.
- TCI's highly trained technical service and sales teams extend across North America.
- TCI provides expert advice and assistance to customers ranging from large multinational corporations to independent custom coaters.
- TCI maintains an expansive network of distribution centers, either certified or approved external providers, that services customers throughout the United States, Canada, Mexico, Europe, and Asia.
- TCI focuses on environmental consciousness and reliable service response at all levels.
