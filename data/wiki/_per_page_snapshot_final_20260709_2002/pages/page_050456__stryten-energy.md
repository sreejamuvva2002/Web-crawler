# Stryten Energy

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-11-11

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Named a 2022 Military Friendly Employer](https://stryten.flywheelstaging.com/stryten-energy-named-2022-military-friendly/)  ·  `page_050456`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy has been named a 2022 Silver Military Friendly® Employer and a Military Friendly® Brand.
- The recognition is part of an annual list from militaryfriendly.com that identifies companies creating sustainable career paths, community outreach, brand enthusiasm, and enduring partnerships benefiting the military community.
- Stryten hires military members across every level of the company, from executive management to the plant floor.
- Stryten maintains a dedicated section on its Jobs website specifically for military and veteran job seekers.
- Mike Judd serves as President and Chief Operating Officer of Stryten Energy.
- Mike Judd stated that Stryten values veterans' service and proactively translates their military skills into jobs, announcing the recognition on Veterans Day.
- The Military Friendly Employers list is produced annually by Viqtory, Inc., a service-disabled, veteran-owned small business.
- Ratings for the list are awarded based on data from public sources, proprietary survey data, and personal surveys of veterans, processed through an algorithm overseen by The Military Friendly® Advisory Council and audited by Ernst & Young.
- Stryten maintains high retention rates for its military personnel, with many progressing through multiple promotions within the company.
- Stryten currently has job openings available for military members across numerous U.S. locations.
- Mike Judd reiterated the company's commitment to hiring military community members and integrating them into the Stryten community.
- Stryten sponsors Rivers of Recovery, a national nonprofit that uses outdoor-based activities to treat PTSD, depression, and anxiety in combat veterans and active-duty military.
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions across the transportation, motive power, essential power, and military sectors.
- Stryten Energy is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- The company partners with major recognized firms to meet demand for reliable and sustainable energy storage capacity.
- Stryten's products power applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- Stryten's stored energy technologies include advanced lead and lithium batteries, intelligent chargers, and cloud-based software.
- Stryten Energy is an industry leader backed by more than a century of expertise in delivering energy solutions.
