# Chatham-Siler City Advanced Manufacturing Site

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Chatham-Siler City Advanced Manufacturing Site - Chatham County Economic Development Corporation](https://www.chathamedc.org/real-estate/chatham-siler-city-advanced-manufacturing-site/)  ·  `page_000505`

## Facts

- The Chatham-Siler City Advanced Manufacturing Site is located in North Carolina’s Research Triangle region.
- It is a North Carolina certified, shovel-ready megasite.
- The site encompasses more than 1,300 acres.
- The CAM Site sits just 12 minutes from Toyota’s electric vehicle battery manufacturing facility in central North Carolina.
- The site is served by Duke Energy to support large-scale industrial operations.
- It is positioned at the geographic center of North Carolina.
- The site is directly adjacent to U.S. Highway 421.
- U.S. Highway 421 is currently in the process of becoming Interstate 685.
- The highway conversion provides enhanced regional and national connectivity for advanced manufacturing investment.
- The site is ideally situated to access skilled talent, transportation options, suppliers, and consumers.
