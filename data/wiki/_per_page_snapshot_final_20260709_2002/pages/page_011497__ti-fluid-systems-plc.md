# TI Fluid Systems plc

## Facts

- TI Fluid Systems plc announced the release of its Annual Report and Accounts 2023, Sustainability Report 2023, and Notice of the Annual General Meeting 2024 on 4 April 2024.
- The AGM is scheduled for 9 am on Tuesday 14 May 2024 at Latham & Watkins (London) LLP offices in London.
- Shareholder registration for the AGM will be available from 8.30 am.
- The Board intends to recommend a final dividend of 4.53 Euro cents per share, amounting to €23.2 million.
- Subject to shareholder approval at the AGM on 14 May 2024, the final dividend will be paid on 21 June 2024 to shareholders on the register at the close of business on 24 May 2024.
- The dividend will be converted to Sterling at a fixed rate on the payment day.
- TI Fluid Systems is a global innovator of thermal and fluid system solutions for current and developing vehicle architectures.
- The company serves all major automotive manufacturers with more than 100 years of automotive supply experience.
- TI Fluid Systems operates across 27 countries.
- Management is pivoting to electrification as part of the 'Taking-the-Turn' strategy.
- Technological agility to develop and adapt product offerings to meet EV requirements of customers is critical.
- Vehicle electrification impacts human resource management, requiring sufficient skilled personnel for product design and development.
- The company faces risks from geopolitical tensions, supply chain challenges, and evolving electrification trends.
- Inflationary pressures across operations impact cost increases, recovery through pricing, and efficiency.
- Climate change drives technology and product-development risk as vehicle electrification continues across the industry.
- The company is transitioning to lower-carbon, more efficient manufacturing operations to address physical risks to facilities.
- Increasing regulations on plastic use and sustainable supply chains are areas of focus for the sustainability strategy.
- Potential delays in regulatory changes mandating vehicle electrification may extend timelines for return on new technology investments but expand opportunities for conventional ICE vehicles.
- Management is monitoring the potential impact of generative artificial intelligence on the labour force.
- The Group holds 97% of the shares in Bundy India Ltd.
- During the year, the Group purchased €4.3 million worth of goods from Gentherm Incorporated (which acquired supplier Alfmeier Precision SE).
- Bain Capital, a significant shareholder, charged the company €0.02 million for passed-through costs from a third-party provider.
- Hans Dieltjens serves as Chief Executive Officer and President.
- Alexander De Bock serves as Chief Financial Officer.
- The responsibility statement was approved by the Board of Directors on 11 March 2024.
