# Lehigh Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Lehigh Technologies owns the world’s largest MRP manufacturing plant.
- The plant is located just outside of Atlanta in Tucker, Georgia.
- The Tucker, Georgia facility has an annual production capacity of 140 million pounds.
- Lehigh Technologies operates an Application and Development Center (ADC).
- The ADC routinely tests MRP from different end-of-life rubber materials in model formulations to ensure product performance meets market and customer expectations.
- Manufacturing lines at the facility are managed using a state-of-the-art process control system with a “human machine interface” (HMI).
- The HMI system allows production engineers to control dozens of important process parameters simultaneously.
- Lehigh Technologies is ISO9001 and ISO14001 certified.
- Continuous investment in equipment and systems, on-going safety training, and meeting environmental, health and safety (EHS) common standards are core elements of the company's production management system.
