# MP Materials

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [MP Materials | Rare Earth Materials & Magnetics Producer](https://mpmaterials.com/)  ·  `page_013651`

## Facts

- MP Materials is America’s only fully integrated rare earth producer with capabilities spanning the entire supply chain—from mining and processing to advanced metallization and magnet manufacturing.
- The company extracts and refines materials from one of the world’s richest rare earth deposits in California.
- MP Materials manufactures the world’s strongest and most efficient permanent magnets.
- Its products enable innovation across critical sectors including transportation, energy, robotics, defense, and aerospace.
- High grade ore is responsibly mined and processed in Mountain Pass, California.
- Through multiple stages of flotation, the company produces a high-purity rare earth concentrate.
- The concentrate is separated and refined into high-purity rare earth compounds at Mountain Pass and reduced to metal.
- Rare earths are combined with other metals to produce permanent magnets at Independence, a state-of-the-art facility in Texas.
- MP Materials is founder-led and owner-operated.
- The company is on a mission to restore the full rare earth supply chain to the United States.
- MP Materials collaborates with customers to build revolutionary products and the materials infrastructure that powers them.
- The company delivers unmatched scale, efficiency, and operational precision through end-to-end capabilities spanning mining, refining, and magnet manufacturing.
- MP Materials provides customers with visibility and accountability in an otherwise opaque supply chain by setting standards for ethical and responsible practices.
- The company relies on rigorous quality control to reliably deliver the materials and magnets that power customer success.
- It is committed to environmental excellence through closed-loop water recycling and industry-leading safety performance, prioritizing people and communities at every step of the journey.
