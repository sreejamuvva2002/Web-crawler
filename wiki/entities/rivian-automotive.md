# Rivian Automotive

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** East Atlanta Megasite, Georgia, Jasper, Morgan, Newton, Walton County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Rivian announced a $5 billion investment to build a vehicle manufacturing facility on the 2,000-acre East Atlanta Megasite in Georgia. The plant will create 7,500 jobs, produce up to 400,000 electric vehicles per year, and include technology centers for research and development.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Rivian is investing $5 billion to construct a large-scale EV manufacturing plant in Georgia, adding thousands of jobs and expanding vehicle production capacity.
