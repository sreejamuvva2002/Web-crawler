# Toyo Tire North America Manufacturing Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-11-20

**Source:** [Toyo Tire North America to create more than 150 jobs in Bartow County | WBHF](https://wbhfradio.org/toyo-tire-north-america-to-create-more-than-150-jobs-in-bartow-county/)  ·  `page_048633`

## Facts

- Gov. Nathan Deal announced on November 20, 2018 that Toyo Tire North America Manufacturing Inc. will create more than 150 jobs and invest over $138 million in Cartersville.
- New jobs created by the investment will include positions in production, support, and administration.
- Gov. Nathan Deal stated Georgia is the No. 1 state for business and offers strategic resources necessary to retain dynamic companies and support industry leaders in long-term growth.
- Gov. Nathan Deal noted Toyo Tires is a valued member of Georgia’s family of innovative automotive manufacturers and suppliers, and its leadership recognizes the benefits of operating in Georgia's competitive economic environment.
- Gov. Nathan Deal said by choosing Cartersville over international competition, Toyo Tires will continue to hire from Georgia’s talented workforce and utilize highly connected transportation infrastructure to reach its growing customer base.
- Gov. Nathan Deal expressed appreciation for Toyo Tires’ commitment to creating more quality jobs in Georgia and looked forward to further strengthening their partnership.
- Through a two-phase expansion, Toyo Tires will construct a new 254,000-square-foot facility.
- The new facility will have an annual production capacity of 2.4 million passenger vehicle tires.
- Upon completion of the expansion, Toyo Tires will employ approximately 2,100 Georgia residents.
- Don Bunn, president of Toyo Tire North America Manufacturing Inc., said the level of cooperation from the State of Georgia and Bartow County over the years continues to exceed expectations.
- Don Bunn stated Toyo Tires is proud to be a member of the Georgia business community and strives to show appreciation by being a great place to work and supporting community organizations throughout the state.
- In 2016, Toyo Tire North America Manufacturing Inc. became the Toyo Tires Group’s largest manufacturing operation with an annual production capacity of 11.5 million tires.
- Bartow County Sole Commissioner Steve Taylor said Bartow County was fortunate to be selected as Toyo Tires’ home years ago and is honored each time they choose to invest again.
- Steve Taylor stated the county strives for long-term partnerships with industries, noting this partnership has been incredibly successful.
- Steve Taylor said Toyo Tires has had a significant impact on Georgia’s automotive sector and Bartow’s economy, with many accolades to show for it.
- Steve Taylor appreciated Toyo Tires' corporate citizenship, citing donations and volunteer hours to schools, environmental causes, and citizens in need.
- Steve Taylor highlighted the company’s annual commitment to provide food for the community during the holiday season as a significant example of corporate citizenship.
- Steve Taylor called Toyo Tires great neighbors and expressed pleasure in supporting the company’s growth again.
- The state-of-the-art Toyo Tires manufacturing facility began operations in 2006.
- The facility features a proprietary Advanced Tire Operation Module production system.
- The facility location was chosen to enhance distribution and inventory availability to meet increasing demands for Toyo and Nitto tires sold through Toyo Tires’ expanding network of retailers.
- Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) Director of Existing Industry & Regional Recruitment Lindsay Martin represented the Global Commerce Division in partnership with the Cartersville-Bartow County Department of Economic Development and Georgia Power.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson said Toyo Tires is an outstanding example of an international manufacturer that has found rapid success in Georgia.
- Pat Wilson stated Toyo Tires and Bartow County have proven to be a winning team and looked forward to their continued success in Georgia.
- Toyo Tire North America Manufacturing Inc. is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Toyo Tire Holdings of Americas Inc.
- The company is responsible for the production of both the Toyo Tires and Nitto tire brands.
- Toyo Tires maintains a commitment to innovation, quality, performance, and excellent customer service, with significant and continuous investments made in its facility in White, Georgia, to produce high-quality tires.
- The company’s tire portfolio covers a broad range of applications, including high-performance racing-inspired tires, rugged off-road tires for light trucks and SUVs, luxury touring tires, and tires for truck and bus applications.
- Toyo Tires was named the 2016 Georgia Automotive Supplier of the Year by GDEcD.
