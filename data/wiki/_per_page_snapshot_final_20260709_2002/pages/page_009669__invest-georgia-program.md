# Invest Georgia Program

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Invest Georgia](https://investgeorgia.net/)  ·  `page_009669`

## Facts

- The Invest Georgia Program is a long-term investment program, backed by the State of Georgia.
- The program is designed to help grow and mentor current, new and future Georgia Venture Capital and Private Equity Investment Funds in the state of Georgia.
- The program helps create new Georgia companies and new Georgia entrepreneurs.
- The program helps create long-term, high-paying Georgia jobs.
- Invest Georgia manages 18 Georgia Venture Funds.
- Invest Georgia supports 119 Georgia companies.
- Invest Georgia has supported over 4,300 Georgia jobs.
