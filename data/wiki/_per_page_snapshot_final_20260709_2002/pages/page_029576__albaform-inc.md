# Albaform, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-09-19

**Source:** [Women in Business: Monika Vintrlíková | Embassy of the Czech Republic in Washington, D.C.](https://mzv.gov.cz/washington/en/economy_trade/news/women_in_business_monika_vintrlikova.html)  ·  `page_029576`

## Facts

- The Embassy of the Czech Republic hosted an online talk with Monika Vintrlíková, CEO of Albaform, Inc., on September 19, 2023, as part of its "Women in Business" series.
- Monika Vintrlíková heads ALBAform, a second-generation family business with 30+ years of experience in forming, welding, and assembling metal components for the automotive, agriculture, and e‑mobility sectors.
- Albaform operates manufacturing locations in both the Czech Republic and the United States.
- The company's U.S. facility employs over 150 employees.
- Vintrlíková discussed the company's founding in 1992, its expansion into the American market, and plans for a future facility in Mexico.
- She highlighted the strategic importance of locating manufacturing close to customers to minimize transportation costs.
- She advised that Czech companies expanding to the United States should focus on site selection rather than expecting government incentives.
- Vintrlíková noted that succeeding in the large American market requires heavy investment in the business.
- She expressed a strong preference for the Southeast, specifically praising Georgia’s booming economy and its reliable workforce.
- The company collaborates with local technical schools as well as the major universities around Atlanta.
- Vintrlíková emphasized the importance of having support from Albaform’s Czech employees when starting out in Atlanta.
- She noted the dual language capability of locally based employees to communicate with the factory in the Czech Republic.
- Vintrlíková shared that her upbringing in the family business gave her the know-how to be successful as a woman in the automotive industry without facing setbacks while balancing work and family life.
- Monika Vintrlíková currently serves as the Honorary Consul of the Czech Republic in Atlanta, Georgia.
- The Czech Embassy collaborates with Vintrlíková to bring Czech delegations and companies to her jurisdiction, leveraging her knowledge of the American market.
- The "Women in Business" series is part of the Czech Embassy’s Mutual Inspirations Festival 2023 – Eliška Junková.
