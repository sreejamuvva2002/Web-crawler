# Essex Magnet Wire

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-03-16

**Source:** [Groundbreaking Ceremony for New Essex Factory Held in Zrenjanin](https://essexsolutions.com/news/groundbreaking-ceremony-for-new-essex-factory-held-in-zrenjanin/)  ·  `page_010342`

## Facts

- A groundbreaking ceremony for a new Essex wire factory was held in Zrenjanin’s industrial zone Southeast on March 16, 2018.
- The new factory will focus on the production of enameled copper wires.
- The facility represents a EUR 30 million investment.
- The project is expected to provide 150 new employment opportunities by 2023.
- Production operations at the Zrenjanin facility are scheduled to begin in autumn of 2018.
- Future employees at the production facility will receive professional training at Essex plants in Germany.
- Essex expanded its business in Serbia to meet demands of Eastern European customers and strengthen capacities related to the development of electric vehicles.
- The expansion decision took into account Serbia's favorable geographic location and broad base of qualified personnel.
- Christopher Koo, Chairman of LS Group (the parent company within which Essex operates), stated that Essex employs over 3,100 people globally.
- Essex is the largest manufacturer of winding wire products in the world.
- The Zrenjanin factory will be one of the most energy-efficient plants in Serbia due to an optimized, cutting-edge production process and a commitment to sustainable development.
- The plant will be equipped with state-of-the-art technology to implement Industry 4.0 processes that employ digital technologies in the production process.
- Essex Magnet Wire is a leading global provider of magnet wire used by most major original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and industry leaders in the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Essex Magnet Wire operates facilities in 11 countries across three continents.
- The company has 90 years of experience and maintains vertical integration in its manufacturing process to ensure quality and seamless delivery.
- The Essex MagForceX Innovation Center is conducting research and development to provide custom solutions for customer challenges.
- Essex Solutions corporate headquarters is located at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
