# Daesol Ausys Georgia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-08-29

**Source:** [Automotive Supplier Daesol Ausys Georgia to Invest $72M in Harris County - Expansion Solutions](https://www.expansionsolutionsmagazine.com/automotive-supplier-daesol-ausys-georgia-to-invest-72m-in-harris-county/)  ·  `page_019008`

## Facts

- Daesol Ausys Georgia will establish a new manufacturing facility in West Point, Georgia.
- The facility will create more than 140 new jobs.
- The project involves $72 million in investment in Harris County.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that West Point has become an automotive capital in its own right and is excited that Daesol Ausys will join other innovators in Harris County.
- Georgia’s automotive industry employs over 50,000 hardworking Georgians.
- The state’s automotive industry will continue to grow as e-mobility suppliers choose all corners of the state for future jobs.
- Daesol Ausys was established in Korea in 2017.
- The company specializes in designing and manufacturing motor and electric vehicle interior components and accessories.
- It is a key supplier for Kia Georgia, Hyundai Motor Group, and General Motors.
- Min Ho Kwon, CEO of Daesol Ausys, stated the company is committed to delivering world-class services that exceed clients’ requirements and expectations.
- Min Ho Kwon expressed excitement about the project in the Northwest Harris Business Park and looking forward to continuing the partnership with the State of Georgia and Harris County.
- Daesol Ausys’ new facility will produce luggage boards and luggage covers.
- Operations at the new facility are expected to begin in December 2024.
- The company will be hiring for positions in production, purchasing, quality control, maintenance, human resources, and management.
- Robert Grant, Chairman of the Harris County Commission, stated that Daesol Ausys’ decision to locate in the Northwest Harris Business Park represents a commitment to Harris County.
- West Point and Harris County have a wonderful relationship with Daesol Ausys’ sister companies Daehan Solutions and Daesol Materials.
- Officials have been working closely with company officials to get this new venture in West Point.
- Craig Greenhaw, Chairman of the Harris County Development Authority, extended a welcome to Daesol Ausys and looked forward to a long and mutually beneficial partnership with the newest corporate citizen.
- Craig Greenhaw noted that West Point and Harris County continue to work well together, calling this another successful venture bringing jobs and capital investment into the region.
- Alyce Thornhill, Director of Project Implementation and Supplier Strategy, represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team on this competitive project.
- The project was executed in partnership with the Harris County Commission, Harris County Development Authority, City of West Point, Georgia Power, and Georgia Quick Start.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that economic development is a team sport driven by the local level and congratulated Harris County and community partners for helping bring Daesol Ausys to West Georgia.
- Pat Wilson noted that the automotive industry is providing generational change in the region and across the state, and expressed excitement for the opportunities Daesol Ausys will provide.
- For over a century, Georgia has fostered healthy industry practices, encouraged collaboration and innovation, and positioned the state as a leader in developing and harnessing emerging technologies for the evolving automotive and mobility industry.
- As the market transitions to electric vehicles, Georgia has pursued the entire supply chain.
- Georgia has created more than $25 billion in investments and 30,000 jobs since 2018.
- In fiscal year 2023, job creation in the automotive industry increased by 324 percent when compared to FY21.
- Suppliers attracted by Georgia’s mobility original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) resulted in over $2 billion in investment across the state in FY23.
- Daesol Ausys Georgia is a leading manufacturer of automotive components and a key supplier of luggage boards and luggage covers.
- The company is committed to exceeding customer expectations with award-winning products and services through innovation and efficiency.
- The company has a long history as one of the top suppliers to Kia and Hyundai worldwide.
