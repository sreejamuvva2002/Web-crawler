# Aurubis AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2024-02-15

**Source:** [EQS-News: Aurubis achieves good full-year result of € 349 million despite extraordinary effects caused by criminal activities](https://www.centralcharts.com/en/549-aurubis/news/4497183-eqs-news-aurubis-achieves-good-full-year-result-of-349-million-despite-extraordinary-effects-caused-by-criminal-activities)  ·  `page_017411`

## Facts

- Aurubis AG achieved operating earnings before taxes (EBT) of €349 million for the 2022/23 fiscal year as at the balance sheet date of September 30.
- Operating return on capital employed (ROCE) amounted to 11.3% in the reporting period, compared to 19% in the previous year.
- Deviations from the record result in fiscal year 2021/22 were mainly attributable to the financial impact of criminal activities directed against Aurubis.
- IFRS consolidated earnings before taxes (EBT) were €165 million for the period, compared to €935 million in the previous year.
- CEO Roland Harings stated the result is the third-best in company history and highlighted potential due to global developments like electrification, the circular economy, and sustainability transitions.
- The Supervisory and Executive Boards will propose a dividend per share of €1.40 at the Annual General Meeting on February 15, 2024, corresponding to a payout ratio of 23% of operating consolidated net income.
- Treatment charges for concentrates and recycling materials were key earnings drivers for the past fiscal year.
- Higher wire rod revenues with increased product surcharges and a significantly higher copper premium positively influenced earnings.
- A considerably lower metal result at decreased prices for industrial metals copper, tin, and zinc had an opposite effect on earnings.
- Lower sales prices for sulfuric acid cut revenues from the Aurubis by-product, and demand for flat rolled products also dropped.
- Inflation-related higher Group costs and launching expenses for strategic projects currently in implementation lowered earnings.
- The financial impact from criminal activities resulted in a €169 million metals shortfall as of September 30, 2023, which was €16 million less than the assessed inventory difference of €185 million disclosed on August 31, 2023.
- Insurance reimbursements provided a compensating effect of €30 million against the criminal activity impacts.
- CEO Roland Harings emphasized that security levels have been significantly increased and comprehensive investments are being made in new facilities with tighter, multistage security areas at the Hamburg site.
- Aurubis established immediate measures and long-term improvements to increase security, including more frequent spot checks of personnel and vehicles, intensified surveillance of risk areas, and additional approval levels in raw material purchasing.
- A new precious metals processing plant (PMR) will come online at the end of 2026 with a €300 million investment, mapping out the entire precious metal processing chain in a closed security area.
- The newly developed PMR process will raise efficiency, considerably reduce throughput times for materials containing precious metals, and lower operating costs by around 15%.
- Around €1.7 billion is currently approved for strategic projects to further develop the smelter network.
- Strategic projects include the Precious Metals Refinery, the large-scale growth project Aurubis Richmond in Georgia, the expansion of recycling capacity with the Complex Recycling Hamburg project, and the extension of the Industrial Heat project in Hamburg.
- Over the next three to five years, the strategic projects will contribute a total of around €260 million to EBITDA per year.
- CEO Roland Harings emphasized commitment to investing in the US and Europe for growth, strength, security, safety, and sustainability, contributing to meeting growing demand for metals essential to the energy and mobility transition.
- Aurubis anticipates an operating EBT of between €380 and €480 million for the current 2023/24 fiscal year.
- The company expects an operating ROCE of between 10% and 14% for the 2023/24 fiscal year.
- Aurubis AG produces more than 1 million tons of copper cathodes annually, along with wire rod, continuous cast shapes, profiles, flat rolled products made of copper and copper alloys, precious metals, selenium, lead, nickel, tin, zinc, sulfuric acid, and iron silicate.
- Aurubis has around 7,200 employees, production sites in Europe and the US, and an extensive distribution network around the world.
