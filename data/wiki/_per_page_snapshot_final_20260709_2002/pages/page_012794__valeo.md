## Facts

- Valeo offers an Organosheet Battery Casing for PHEVs using thermoplastic composite for lightweighting and reinforced mechanical and thermal battery protection.
- The casing is up to 20% lighter than a steel baseline, making it the lightest solution on the market.
- The material used is 100% recyclable.
- The product has a low carbon footprint of 1.4 to 3.1 CO2eq./kg.
- It provides optimum thermal insulation.
- Easy function integration is enabled by plastic overmolding.
- The casing is constructed from glass-fiber resin pre-impregnated thermoplastic composite.
- The design meets crash compliance standards and is corrosion resistant.
- Thermal insulation performance ranges from 0.4 to 0.6 W/m.K.
- It is fire resistant (UL94 V0), withstanding temperatures above 1000°C for more than 5 minutes.
- Manufacturing utilizes a One Shot/NetShape process.
- The product is validated for mass production at a capacity of 300,000 units per year.
- It provides lightweight and strong properties with a strength of 400-500M Pa.
- The casing can be up to 40% lighter than metal solutions.
- Compatible vehicle types include passenger cars, buses, trucks, robot taxis, motorcycles, bikes, droids, and off-road vehicles.
