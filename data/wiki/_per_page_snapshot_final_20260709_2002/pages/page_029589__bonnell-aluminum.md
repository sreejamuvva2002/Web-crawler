# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [TSLOTS Aluminum Framing System is Expanding Operations](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20210304005277/en/TSLOTS-Aluminum-Framing-System-is-Expanding-Operations)  ·  `page_029589`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation (NYSE: TG).
- The TSLOTS line of aluminum structural framing systems is expanding operations into the Midwest region of the United States.
- The TSLOTS aluminum framing system allows manufacturers to create light, durable, attractive, strong, versatile, and functional structures using custom T-slotted aluminum extrusions and connecting hardware.
- Manufacturing and distribution for TSLOTS occurs from the Clearfield, Utah site.
- A new leased facility is operated in conjunction with Bonnell Aluminum’s existing Niles, Michigan location.
- The new leased facility provides 12,550 square feet of warehouse space for TSLOTS inventory.
- This additional storage capacity will improve customer service levels for the Eastern and Midwestern United States.
- Expansion aims to reduce shipping costs and shorten lead-times for customers in the Eastern and Midwestern United States.
- Steve Silvester serves as the TSLOTS Sales Manager.
- Customers in the Midwest and East requested a location closer to them over the years.
- Shipping materials from the Midwest provides cost and lead-time benefits to customers.
- TSLOTS has experienced growth over the past two decades.
- Bonnell Aluminum operates locations across the country to service customers effectively.
- TSLOTS is a full-service operation providing project design assistance, fabrication, custom packaging, and full assembly.
- Bonnell Aluminum serves the building and construction, automotive and transportation, consumer durables, machinery and equipment, electrical and distribution markets.
- Branded product lines include TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum and Futura Transitions by Bonnell Aluminum.
- Bonnell Aluminum has manufacturing facilities located in Newnan GA, Carthage TN, Niles MI, Elkhart IN, and Clearfield, UT.
