# Mando Corporation

## Facts

- Mando Corporation was selected as General Motors' (GM) "Supplier of the Year 2021".
- The 30th edition of the Supplier of the Year Awards ceremony took place on March 17 (local time) in Phoenix, Arizona, United States.
- 134 suppliers from 16 countries, including Mando America Corporation, participated in the awards and received their prizes.
- The award recognizes exceptional suppliers in the top 1% tier, selected from approximately 20,000 suppliers worldwide.
- Mando has been recognized as GM's best supplier eight times since 2003, including this award.
- Supplier selection was based on company performance metrics such as launch, supply chain, and quality, along with qualitative evaluation scores for innovation and engineering, communication and transparency, and safety.
- Mando achieved the highest score in the cultural priorities category.
- Mando also won the "Launch Excellence Award" at the 29th special award ceremony for delivering electric parking brakes according to the supply schedule despite a 165 km/h hurricane in September 2020.
- Shilpan Amin, GM's Vice President of International Purchasing, stated that this year's event was special due to the program's 30th anniversary and provided an opportunity to recognize supplier perseverance during one of the industry's most difficult years.
- Amin noted that these leading suppliers demonstrated resilience and strengthened their commitment to sustainability and innovation, with GM and its suppliers ready to build a better future through strong relationships and collaboration.
- Mando CEO Cho Seong-hyeon stated he was very happy and encouraged to receive the 30th award, adding that they will overcome greater difficulties through joint efforts of leaders and employees worldwide, including union members, despite an unclear market situation.
- Mando Corporation (KS 204320) is a leading global supplier of auto parts specializing in hybrid electric vehicle solutions and autonomous driving.
- The company received an A rating in MSCI environmental, social, and governance assessments in 2021.
- Mando was founded in 1962 with its headquarters located in Seoul, South Korea.
- The company provides advanced driving systems including braking, steering, suspension, propulsion, and power solutions, as well as software solutions for automakers and new mobility service providers.
- Mando's technologies span three fields: integrated vehicle safety, autonomous driving, and electric mobility, which provide vehicles with intuitive detection, decision-making, and intelligent movement capabilities.
- Mando serves more than 60 clients from over 50 sites worldwide, encompassing local and global automakers in all major regions.
