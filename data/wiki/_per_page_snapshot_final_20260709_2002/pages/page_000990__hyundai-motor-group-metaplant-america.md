# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-06-18

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America Announces First Production Vehicle to Be 2025 Hyundai IONIQ 5 - HMGMA](https://www.hmgma.com/2024/06/18/hyundai-motor-group-metaplant-america-announces-first-production-vehicle-to-be-2025-hyundai-ioniq-5/)  ·  `page_000990`

## Facts

- The first production vehicle to be produced at Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America will be the 2025 Hyundai IONIQ 5.
- Production of the award-winning electric SUV is scheduled to start in the fourth quarter of this year.
- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America represents a $7.59 billion investment that will create 8,500 direct jobs in Georgia.
- The company is currently training and preparing to begin production in the fourth quarter of this year.
- The new plant will produce all trim lines of the IONIQ 5 except for the IONIQ 5 N.
- The facility will eventually become the sole plant building IONIQ 5 models for the U.S. market.
- Oscar Kwon, president and CEO of HMGMA, stated that the company is in the final phase of construction and getting ready for the 2025 Hyundai IONIQ 5 to roll off the line.
- Hyundai markets its electric vehicle lineup as America’s Most Awarded EV Lineup.
- The IONIQ 5 has been named Best Electric SUV, Best EV for the Money, and one of the Best Cars for Families by U.S. News & World Report this year.
- Cars.com named the IONIQ 5 the Best Electric Vehicle for the second consecutive year.
- In previous years, the IONIQ 5 was named the 2022 World Car of the Year, 2023 MotorTrend SUV of the Year, and 2022 Car and Driver EV of the Year.
- The IONIQ 5 received the highest TOP SAFETY PICK+ award from the Insurance Institute For Highway Safety (IIHS).
- The IONIQ 5 is Hyundai’s highest volume electric vehicle, with sales up 43% so far in 2024 and an increase of 82% in May alone.
- Buyers who purchase the 2024 IONIQ 5 are eligible for a $7,500 cash rebate for the month of June.
- Customers who lease the 2024 IONIQ 5 are currently eligible for a $7,500 federal tax credit.
- Hyundai and SK On are building a $5 billion EV battery cell plant in Bartow County, Georgia that will employ 3,500 workers.
- A Center for Automotive Research study projects that Hyundai’s investments in Georgia will create nearly 40,000 jobs and generate $4.6 billion in individual earnings annually.
- Additional vehicles to be produced at the Metaplant will be announced in the future.
- The Metaplant will produce vehicles for Hyundai, Genesis, and Kia brands.
