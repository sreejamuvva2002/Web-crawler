## Facts

- The Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) provides support for businesses across the state through its online resources, assistance with regulatory requirements, tax-incentives for business development, and connections to industry experts.
- The Center of Innovation is a division of GDEcD that serves as a strategic resource hub to help businesses across the state accelerate growth through innovation.
- The Center of Innovation provides connections and resources in aerospace, AgTech, IT, logistics, and manufacturing.
- GDEcD maintains an Innovation and Entrepreneurship Support Network that lists entrepreneurship hubs it works with across Georgia.
- A statewide hub is Georgia Power, with contacts Elyse Davis, Scott Purvis, and Vanessa Wagner.
