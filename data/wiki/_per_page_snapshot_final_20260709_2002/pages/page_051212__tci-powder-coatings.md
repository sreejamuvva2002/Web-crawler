# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-06-17

**Source:** [TCI Powder Coatings, BASF form strategic alliance](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/finishing/tci-powder-coatings-basf-form-strategic-alliance)  ·  `page_051212`

## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is located in Ellaville, Georgia.
- TCI Powder Coatings is a subsidiary of RPM Intl.
- The strategic alliance between TCI Powder Coatings and BASF was announced on June 17, 2016.
- The agreement allows TCI to sell the BASF CathoGuard® electrocoat technology to specified market segments.
- The electrocoat technology is suitable for use on parts that are difficult to paint efficiently, including those with multiple angles and crevices.
- The technology offers coverage and corrosion edge protection.
- The company states the technology lowers total material costs by nearly 20 percent.
- TCI market segments with access to this technology through the new alliance include off-road and heavy equipment, general finishing, office furniture, and appliance.
- The electrocoat technology will also be available to custom coaters that supply major subassemblies and modular components for the automotive market.
