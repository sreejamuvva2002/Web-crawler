# Pirelli

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-05

**Source:** [PIRELLI WILL START CYBER™ TYRE PRODUCTION IN GEORGIA UNDERLINING THE STRATEGIC IMPORTANCE OF THE U.S. MARKET](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/pirelli-will-start-cyber-tyre-production-in-georgia-underlining-the-strategic-importance-of-the-us-market-302763293.html)  ·  `page_029184`

## Facts

- Pirelli is reinforcing its long-term commitment to the United States with a key step in its product and industrial strategy.
- The Georgia plant, already dedicated to the most technologically advanced products for the U.S. market—both in the High-Value segment and in Motorsport—will be further enhanced by the production of connected tires featuring Cyber™ Tyre technology.
- Investment and output expansion details will be communicated in the coming months following the finalization of the development plans.
- The announcement was made as Pirelli participated in the SelectUSA Investment Summit, a flagship event promoted by the U.S. Department of Commerce.
- Cyber™ Tyre is being showcased at the summit as a core innovation shaping the future of smart mobility.
- Cyber™ Tyre is the world's first hardware-and-software system capable of collecting data and information from sensors embedded in tires.
- The system processes collected data through Pirelli's proprietary software and algorithms.
- It communicates in real time with the vehicle's electronics to enable new functionalities integrated with driving and control systems.
- These functionalities enhance the driving experience, increase safety levels, and support connected infrastructure.
- In a highly advanced market such as the United States, where digital and smart mobility solutions are rapidly expanding, Cyber™ Tyre represents a distinctive competitive advantage for Pirelli.
- At SelectUSA, Cyber™ Tyre was showcased at the Georgia State booth.
- Pirelli has been established in Georgia for more than 20 years.
- Claudio Zanardo, CEO of Pirelli North America, stated that the start of Cyber™ Tyre production in the Rome, Georgia plant is a significant milestone for Pirelli in the country.
- The milestone reflects a commitment to bringing advanced technologies like Cyber™ Tyre closer to the market and further strengthening industrial footprint and innovation capabilities in the United States.
- Pirelli is finalizing the introduction of the latest version of the MIRS (Modular Integrated Robotized System) production process at the Rome plant.
- The MIRS process will be the most advanced manufacturing process for high-end, premium production within the entire Pirelli Group.
- The MIRS process is exclusive to the Georgia factory.
- The new process enhances robotized production capabilities, increasing productivity, flexibility, and quality.
- It is a highly digitalized system that enables a direct link between product design and its industrial application.
- This development lays the groundwork for growth in Pirelli's production capacity in Rome.
- The Rome plant specializes in high-value tire production for the North American market.
- The facility hosts a dedicated R&D center, further strengthening its role in technological development and product innovation.
- The facility is recognized for responsible sourcing practices, including the use of FSC®-certified natural rubber.
