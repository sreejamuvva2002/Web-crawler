# Aurubis Richmond Recycling Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Aurubis and SMS group forge ahead with the first multimetal recycling plant in the US](https://www.foundry-planet.com/d/aurubis-and-sms-group-forge-ahead-with-the-first-multimetal-recycling-plant-in-the-us/)  ·  `page_017381`

## Facts

- Aurubis AG and SMS group GmbH signed a supply contract for a second plant module for the new Aurubis Richmond recycling plant in Georgia.
- The additional equipment will increase the capacity of the metal recycling plant in Richmond from an annual 90,000 tons to 180,000 tons of complex metal scrap materials.
- Aurubis CEO Roland Harings stated the expansion delivers on the company’s “Driving Sustainable Growth” strategy while keeping strategically important and high-demand mineral raw materials in the resource cycle.
- The SMS group recycling equipment is destined for the second module of the new US plant.
- The core technology is the top-blown rotary converter (TBRC), used for processing complex recycling materials to recover copper, nickel, tin, precious metals, and platinum group metals in downstream stages.
- Recovered metals are crucial raw materials for energy transition, sustainable mobility, and digitalization technologies.
- SMS Executive Board member Michael Rzepczyk stated the partnership helps Aurubis assume a leading role in multimetal recycling in the US.
- The planned doubling of production volume at the new US site takes recycling to the next level in the US.
- Together, the companies are building a more sustainable future by closing value chains and maximizing the recovery of valuable metals from recycling materials.
- The plant features modular technology that allows additional components to be added to expand capacity for future requirements.
- Aurubis is securing its role as a forerunner in the US recycling market while ensuring capacity can be further expanded at any time.
- The modular system enables a prudent market strategy to respond flexibly to US needs and benefit from constant US market growth.
- Aurubis is investing about €640 million across both phases of the project to build the first secondary smelter specializing in multimetal recycling in the US.
- Groundbreaking for the Aurubis Richmond plant took place in mid-2022.
- Construction at the site is currently running at full steam.
- Stage one of the plant is scheduled to go online in the first half of 2024.
- The TBRC from the signed contract is destined for the second stage, which is anticipated to start production in 2026.
- As a leader in multimetal recycling, Aurubis currently processes roughly 1 million tons of recycling material annually.
- The Aurubis Richmond recycling plant will add another 180,000 tons of annual processing capacity.
- The Georgia investment will contribute to meeting Aurubis Group growth targets.
- The new plant will comply with the highest environmental standards in the US.
- The new plant will create around 200 new jobs.
- Aurubis CEO Roland Harings stated that economic growth and comprehensive corporate responsibility go hand in hand for the company.
