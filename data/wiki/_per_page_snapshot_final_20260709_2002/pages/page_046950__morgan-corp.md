# Morgan Corp.

## Facts

- Morgan Corp. has decades of experience in new generation and maintenance projects.
- The company's portfolio dates back to the 1970s.
- Morgan Corp. has significant experience in new power plant construction and new generation in both fossil and nuclear plants.
- Morgan Corp. executed the Cliffside Project in Cliffside, NC, which encompassed the construction of a new coal-fired power unit at the Cliffside Steam Station.
- Morgan Corp. provided a total site preparation package for the Plant Vogtle Project in Waynesboro, GA, for the construction of two new AP1000 Model, 1100Mw Nuclear Power Generating Units.
- The company has worked on a number of gas turbine projects, including combined cycle or simple cycle.
- Morgan Corp. offers comprehensive maintenance services, including rehabilitating roads, laying pipe, re-routing fly ash lines, and routine ash handling.
- Morgan Corp. offers disposal of CCP landfill construction and coal composition products.
- New technology enables Morgan Corp. to capture, transport, and place ash into lined landfill cells, capping them once filled.
