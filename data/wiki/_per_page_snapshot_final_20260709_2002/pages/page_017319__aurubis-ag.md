# Aurubis AG

**Source:** [Aurubis Opens First US Multimetal Recycling Plant](https://chemxplore.com/news/aurubis-richmond-first-us-multimetal-recycling-plant-starts-production-of-strategic-metals)  ·  `page_017319`

## Facts

- Aurubis AG has launched its first US multimetal recycling plant in Richmond, Georgia.
- The plant marks a strategic milestone in producing essential metals like copper, nickel, tin, and precious metals.
- Demand for strategic metals, particularly copper, is increasing globally.
- The US currently imports about half of its copper needs.
- Copper demand is expected to rise by nearly 30% in the next five years.
- The Richmond plant will process up to 180,000 tons of complex recycling materials annually.
- The recycled materials include printed circuit boards and copper cables.
- The expansion phase for the facility is set to begin in 2026.
- Aurubis invested $800 million in the Georgia plant.
- The facility is creating over 240 jobs in Georgia.
- The facility aims to enhance US supply chain independence by processing complex recycling materials.
- The plant is equipped with cutting-edge technology and meets the latest environmental standards.
- Operations consider water, air, and soil quality.
- The facility serves as a flexible “one-stop shop” for metal-bearing recycling materials from US suppliers.
