# Carcoustics Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-06-13

**Source:** [| PressReleasePoint](https://www.pressreleasepoint.com/carcoustics-create-200-jobs-gwinnett-county?lang=en)  ·  `page_018337`

## Facts

- Gov. Nathan Deal announced on June 13, 2017, that Carcoustics will create 200 jobs and invest $6 million in a new Buford facility over the next five years.
- The newly created jobs will include positions in manufacturing.
- Gov. Nathan Deal stated that Georgia offers automotive industry suppliers like Carcoustics resources to compete worldwide, including a well-trained workforce, a thriving automotive industry, and the nation’s top business environment.
- Gov. Nathan Deal expressed pride in seeing another German company locate in Georgia as efforts to build international relationships continue to yield positive results.
- The international Carcoustics Group model combines a central research center with a manufacturing structure to provide proximity to major markets for quick global product availability.
- Stefan Vallentin, project manager at Carcoustics, stated the company chose Georgia for its great international transport connections and logistical location between BMW in South Carolina and Mercedes in Alabama.
- Potential customers for Carcoustics include Automotive Tier I/ Tier II suppliers, Volkswagen, Mercedes Vans, Volvo, and Asian OEMs like Nissan, Honda, Toyota, Kia, and Hyundai.
- The Georgia location offers new possibilities for Carcoustics' growth strategy, especially in the U.S., according to Stefan Vallentin.
- Gwinnett County Board of Commissioners Chair Charlotte Nash welcomed Carcoustics to Gwinnett County, citing its ideal mix of talent, resources, and location for international companies.
- Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) Director of International Investment Nico Wijnberg represented the Global Commerce division in partnership with Partnership Gwinnett, the Metro Atlanta Chamber, Georgia Power, and Georgia Quick Start.
- Hala Moddelmog, president and CEO of the Metro Atlanta Chamber, noted that Carcoustics joins international companies recognizing metro Atlanta’s prime location, rich resources, and skilled talent pool.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that Carcoustics is joining a growing number of German automotive manufacturers in Georgia and expressed excitement about continued economic development opportunities in Gwinnett County.
- Carcoustics is an innovative, midsize company headquartered in Leverkusen, Germany.
- The company develops and produces solution-oriented acoustic and thermally effective components for the automotive industry.
- Carcoustics offers its expertise in a wide range of technologies to other industrial sectors, with customized solutions at the heart of its product portfolio.
- Carcoustics’ customers include major manufacturers of cars, trucks, domestic appliances, agricultural machinery, and HVAC systems.
