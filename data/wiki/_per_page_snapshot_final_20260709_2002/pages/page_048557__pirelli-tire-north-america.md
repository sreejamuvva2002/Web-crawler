# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Pirelli Tire North America selects Kuehne + Nagel to optimize and manage North American logistics](https://newsroom.kuehne-nagel.com/pirelli-tire-north-america-selects-kuehne--nagel-to-optimize-and-manage-north-american-logistics/)  ·  `page_048557`

## Facts

- Kuehne + Nagel will manage inbound receipt, storage and distribution of Pirelli tires to customers across America.
- The logistics selection is part of Pirelli’s new ‘Logistics and Customer Care Action Plan’ targeted to enhance its total client service.
- Pirelli chose Kuehne + Nagel for its optimization capabilities, transition expertise, and proven industry experience.
- Guy Mannino, President and CEO of Pirelli Tire North America, stated that the company listened intently to trade customers to better understand their needs, particularly regarding delivery service.
- Guy Mannino commented that the new arrangement with Kuehne + Nagel will represent a major step forward in logistics service and a major contribution to expanding business in North America.
- Pirelli Tire North America specializes in the manufacture and marketing of high performance car, light truck and motorcycle tires.
- Pirelli currently has three highly advanced MIRS (Modular Integrated Robotized System) modules in operation in its new factory in Rome, Georgia.
- The Rome, Georgia factory operates to better serve the American OEM and Replacement markets.
- Kuehne + Nagel provides an in-place network of 40 multi-client distribution centers with 11 million square feet of space in North America for companies needing high-performance, flexible distribution infrastructure.
