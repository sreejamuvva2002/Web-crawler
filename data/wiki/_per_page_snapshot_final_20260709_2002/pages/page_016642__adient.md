## Facts

- Adient announced fourth quarter 2022 financial results on Nov. 4, 2022.
- The company expects increased earnings, margin and free cash flow in FY23.
- Adient's Board of Directors approved a $600M share repurchase program.
- Douglas Del Grosso serves as Adient's president and chief executive officer.
- Jeff Stafeil serves as Adient's executive vice president and chief financial officer.
- A conference call to discuss the results was hosted on Nov. 4, 2022 at 8:30 a.m. Eastern.
- Adient is a global leader in automotive seating with approximately 72,000 employees in 31 countries.
- The company operates more than 200 manufacturing/assembly plants worldwide.
- Adient produces and delivers automotive seating for all major OEMs, ranging from complete seating systems to individual components.
- The company's integrated in-house skills span every step of the automotive seat-making process, from research and design to engineering and manufacturing.
- Adient products are delivered into more than 20 million vehicles every year.
