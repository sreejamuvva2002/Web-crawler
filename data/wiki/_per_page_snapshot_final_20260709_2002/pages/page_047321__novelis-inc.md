# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Novelis Inc. | Members | About ASI | Aluminium Stewardship Initiative](https://aluminium-stewardship.org/about-asi/members/Novelis-Inc-)  ·  `page_047321`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. holds a Production and Transformation member class with the Aluminium Stewardship Initiative since January 2015.
- Novelis Inc. is headquartered in Georgia, United States.
- Novelis Inc. is the world's largest recycler of aluminum.
- Novelis partners with customers in the aerospace, automotive, beverage can, and specialties industries to deliver lightweight aluminum solutions across North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- Novelis Inc. is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited, the metals flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group based in Mumbai, India.
- Aluminium Norf GmbH (Alunorf) in Neuss, Germany is a joint venture owned by Speira and Novelis Inc to produce aluminum coils.
- Novelis operates a Greensboro, Georgia manufacturing site that produces aluminum sheet ingot through shredding, decoating, melting, and casting.
- Novelis regional management functions are located at its headquarters in Georgia, USA.
- Novelis produces aluminum automotive sheet for lightweight vehicle structures and body panels using Continuous Annealing Solution Heat (CASH) Lines, Slitting Line, Laser Cutting Line, and Annealing Furnace.
- Novelis supplies rolled aluminum sheet products to the beverage packaging industry from its Warren, Ohio manufacturing site.
- Novelis operates North American production facilities in Berea, Kentucky; Greensboro, Georgia; Guthrie, Kentucky; Kingston, Ontario; Oswego, New York; and Warren, Ohio.
- Novelis has a joint venture site at Logan Aluminum Inc. in Kentucky.
- Novelis produces pre-stretched plates and wing skin plates for the aerospace industry at its Zhenjiang, China facility.
- Novelis operates collection centers across multiple Brazilian cities (Ananindeua, Bauru, Brasília, Campinas, Curitiba, Gravatai, João Pessoa, Juiz de Fora, Parnamirim, Pindamonhangaba, Recife, Salvador, São Gonçalo, São Paulo, and Sertãozinho) for post-use recyclable material.
- Novelis operates a distribution center in Cariacica, Brazil.
- Novelis produces flat-rolled aluminum at its Pindamonhangaba facility in Brazil.
- Novelis operates facilities in Bresso (Italy), Goettingen (Germany), Koblenz (Germany), Latchford (UK), Nachterstedt Recycling (Germany), Nachterstedt Rolling (Germany), Ohle (Germany), Pieve (Italy), Sierre (Switzerland), Voerde (Germany), and Yeongju (Korea).
- Novelis operates a facility in Ulsan, South Korea manufacturing aluminum and aluminum coil/sheet specifically for automotive, can, and specialty products.
- Novelis has an outsourcing contractor relationship with Suzhou Lilai Steel Co., Ltd in China for blank aluminum sheet tolling service.
- Novelis has outsourcing contractors Changzhou Changling Heavy CNC Machine Tool Co., Ltd and Wuxi Heng Tai Aerospace Science and Technology Co., Ltd in China for aluminum plate tolling service.
- Novelis operates an R&D Center & Testing Laboratory at its Zhenjiang, China site.
- Novelis performs cold mill rolling, coil slitting, and coil finishing/annealing at its Kingston, Ontario facility.
- Novelis performs hot milling, pre-treatment, and finishing of automotive aluminum products at its Guthrie, Kentucky facility.
- Novelis performs remelting, recycling, casting, hot mill rolling, cold mill rolling, and coil finishing at its Oswego Works Mill in New York.
- Novelis produces aluminum sheet ingot at its Berea, Kentucky site through shredding, decoating, melting, and casting.
