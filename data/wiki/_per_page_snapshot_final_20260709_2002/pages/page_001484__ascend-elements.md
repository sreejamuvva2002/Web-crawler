## Facts

- Ascend Elements recycles electric vehicle battery components in metro Atlanta.
- Ascend Elements will mark a huge milestone next year.
- Ascend Elements said it will start to market lithium carbonate, a key component in EV batteries.
- The company has been able to recycle and refine lithium carbonate at its Covington facility.
- This marks the first time a U.S. company has been able to produce the compound on American soil without mining it from the earth.
- Producing the compound domestically is a key part of onshoring the EV battery supply chain.
- Eric Gratz, co-founder and chief technology officer of Ascend Elements, said in a news release that this new domestic supply of a critical battery material will help U.S. industries meet growing demand while avoiding the possibility of tariffs on imported materials.
