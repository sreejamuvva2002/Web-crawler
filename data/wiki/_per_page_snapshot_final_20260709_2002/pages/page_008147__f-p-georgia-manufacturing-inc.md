# F & P Georgia Manufacturing Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-02

**Source:** [F & P Georgia Manufacturing Inc.: F & P Georgia Data Breach Affects 12,132 Individuals](https://blog.rankiteo.com/f-p1777927093-f-p-georgia-manufacturing-inc-breach-june-2025/)  ·  `page_008147`

## Facts

- F & P Georgia Manufacturing Inc. is a Tier 1 automotive parts supplier based in Rome, Georgia.
- The company disclosed a data breach affecting 12,132 individuals across the U.S., including 22 residents of Indiana.
- The company detected suspicious activity on its network and confirmed that an unauthorized actor accessed and exfiltrated files between June 24 and July 2, 2025.
- Exposed data included Social Security numbers and driver’s license information, raising concerns about potential identity theft.
- F & P Georgia reported the breach to the Indiana Attorney General on April 20, 2026.
- F & P Georgia reported the breach to the Massachusetts Office of Consumer Affairs and Business Regulation on April 20, 2026.
- The company began notifying affected individuals on April 20, 2026.
- As part of its response, the company is offering 12 months of free credit monitoring and identity restoration services through Cyberscout.
- The offered services include single-bureau credit monitoring, credit reports, and real-time alerts for credit file changes.
- Affected individuals must enroll in the services within 90 days of receiving their notification letter using a unique activation code.
- For further assistance, the company established a dedicated helpline and a mailing address at 88 Enterprise Drive, Acworth, GA 30101.
- The breach underscores the ongoing risks of cyber threats in the manufacturing sector and the importance of timely breach notifications.
- The investigation into the breach has been completed.
