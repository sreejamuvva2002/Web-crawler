# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-05

**Source:** [DHL Supply Chain to Build New Distribution Facility on Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Campus  | The Citizen](https://thecitizen.com/2025/09/05/dhl-supply-chain-to-build-new-distribution-facility-on-yamaha-motor-manufacturing-campus/)  ·  `page_015777`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation (YMMC) and DHL Supply Chain are progressing with studies, permitting, and filings that could lead to the construction of a new, state-of-the-art distribution facility on YMMC’s Newnan, Georgia campus.
- The proposed distribution facility would be 784,700 square feet in size.
- The project represents an estimated investment of $60–90 million.
- The facility would be solely dedicated to YMMC operations.
- Currently, freight traffic flows between YMMC’s main plant and off-site distribution centers, contributing to congestion along Highway 34 and surrounding intersections.
- Consolidating logistics operations on the YMMC campus could reduce truck traffic on local roads.
- The project could ease congestion and improve safety with no added cost to taxpayers.
- Bob Brown, President of YMMC, stated that the opportunity is about strengthening their supply chain while being a good neighbor.
- Key potential benefits include a dedicated, world-class logistics hub to support Yamaha’s manufacturing operations.
- The project will invest in roadway improvements such as new turn lanes and upgraded intersections to improve traffic flow and safety.
- Sustainable features will include stormwater management systems, tree buffers, and pervious surfaces to reduce environmental impact.
- Pending successful completion of studies and required approvals, construction could begin as early as 2026.
- Operations at the new facility could potentially start in the first quarter of 2028.
- Dave Rumler, President of Automotive Engineering and Manufacturing at DHL Supply Chain North America, stated that DHL is committed to delivering integrated, end-to-end solutions and is proud to collaborate with Yamaha on a project that enhances supply chain performance and supports infrastructure improvements for the Newnan community.
