## Facts

- Novelis Inc is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries.
- Novelis Inc reported an 18% decline in net income to USD 128 million for the September quarter 2024-25.
- Net sales rose by 4.5% to USD 4,295 million.
- Sales gains were attributed to higher aluminium prices.
- Flat rolled product shipments slightly increased to 945 kilotonnes.
- The company achieved record shipments for beverage packaging.
- Novelis Inc is an aluminium producer based in the US.
- Steve Fisher serves as President and CEO of Novelis Inc.
- Devinder Ahuja serves as Executive Vice President and CFO of Novelis Inc.
- Devinder Ahuja emphasized the company's focus on managing the balance sheet while navigating market dynamics and progressing with growth investments.
