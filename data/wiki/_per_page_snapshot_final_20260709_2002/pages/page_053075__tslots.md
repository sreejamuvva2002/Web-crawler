# TSLOTS

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [TSLOTS is Expanding Operations | Bonnell Aluminum](https://bonnellaluminum.com/news-events/tslots-is-expanding-operations/)  ·  `page_053075`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum, a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation (NYSE: TG), announced that its TSLOTS line of aluminum structural framing systems is expanding operations into the Midwest region of the United States.
- The TSLOTS aluminum framing system allows manufacturers to create light, durable, attractive, strong, versatile, and functional structures using custom T-slotted aluminum extrusions and connecting hardware.
- A new leased facility operated in conjunction with Bonnell Aluminum’s existing Niles, Michigan location provides 12,550 square feet of warehouse space for TSLOTS inventory.
- The additional storage capacity will improve customer service levels for the Eastern and Midwestern United States, including reduced shipping costs and shorter lead-times.
- Steve Silvester, TSLOTS Sales Manager, stated that over the years as TSLOTS has grown, customers in the Midwest and East have been asking for a location closer to theirs.
- Mr. Silvester added that providing materials that ship from the Midwest will allow customers to gain competitive benefits in terms of costs and lead-time.
- TSLOTS is a full-service operation providing project design assistance, fabrication, custom packaging, and full assembly.
