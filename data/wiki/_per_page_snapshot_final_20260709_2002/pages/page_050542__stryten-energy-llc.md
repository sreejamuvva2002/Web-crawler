# Stryten Energy LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Energy, Automobile, EV, Renewable News | Stryten Energy Introduces Advanced Lithium Material Handling Batteries at ProMat 2025](https://oilandgaspress.com/stryten-energy-introduces-advanced-lithium-material-handling-batteries-at-promat-2025/)  ·  `page_050542`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC is a U.S.-based energy storage solutions provider headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- The company is introducing the M-Series Li710, a new line of Class I and II lithium batteries in a range of voltages, at ProMat 2025 in Chicago.
- The proprietary module design for the M-Series Li710 is assembled in the USA.
- The M-Series Li710 provides greater energy density in a maintenance-free package to power Class I and II lift trucks.
- Stryten’s proprietary lithium module design utilizes nickel manganese cobalt (NMC) chemistry.
- The NMC chemistry offers greater energy density for longer run times and a faster charge rate than lead and lithium iron phosphate (LFP) chemistries.
- The batteries are ideal for 24x7 operations with limited charging opportunities.
- Stryten will assemble the M-Series Li710 lithium batteries at its new lithium assembly plant in Cumming, Georgia.
- Matt Gould serves as Vice President of Industrial Sales and Service at Stryten Energy.
- The Li710 is field serviceable by a service network strategically located across North America.
- The M-Series Li710 line features fault tolerance to maintain system availability.
- The Li710 includes an automated hibernation/sleep mode after inactivity to prevent unintended discharge.
- The Li710 products are compatible with the M-Series X-3 and X-7 chargers.
- The Li710 products are designed to meet UL2580, and UL certification is in process.
- The M-Series Li600 is based on lithium iron phosphate (LFP) chemistry.
- The M-Series Li600 is available for Class I, Class II, and Class III motive power applications.
- The M-Series Li600 features a fully configurable CANbus protocol, remote monitoring capability, comprehensive diagnostics via a color touch screen, and auto hibernation/sleep mode after inactivity.
- The M-Series Li600 products are designed to meet UL2580, and UL certification is in process.
- The M-Series T310 is a low maintenance tubular lead battery for medium to heavy duty lift truck applications.
- The M-Series F110 features an extremely conductive and robust flat-plate grid design coupled with an industry-leading manufacturing process for superior performance in heavy-duty applications.
- The M-Series X-7 charger allows configuration to multiple power levels, including 208-240V ideal for areas with lower power infrastructure such as the Northeast U.S., Florida, Mexico, and Latin America.
- The new chargers offer IoT communication capabilities, including Wi-fi and Bluetooth.
- The chargers can be configured for standard, opportunity, or fast charging to meet fleet demands.
- The M-Series X-3 charger has an advanced 24/36/48V and 72/80V multi-voltage modular design available in 3 bay (3-9kW), 6 bay (3-18kW), and 10 bay (21-30kW) cabinet power levels.
- The M-Series X-3 charger can be configured for either opportunity or fast charging.
- Stryten Energy inCOMMAND™ is an energy performance management software tool that makes it easy to design and deploy web-connected battery and charger solutions.
- inCOMMAND allows dealers and end users to monitor and manage batteries and chargers from computers.
- Stryten Energy’s BESS leverages lead battery technology to provide safe, dependable energy storage solutions for backup power, emergency power supply, renewable energy integration, and grid stabilization.
- BESS solutions offer simple maintenance, high reliability, strong performance under high discharge rates, mature technology, ease of recycling, and high surge current capabilities.
- Stryten Energy provides comprehensive, nationwide motive power services to ensure fleets operate at full capacity.
- Trained stored energy experts provide planned maintenance, repair services, predictive service, installations, de-installation, recycling, application audits, power studies, training, and inspections.
- Sarem Yousuf, Director of Essential Power Sales and Business Development, and Heron Plaza, Senior Director of IoT Development, will present “Modern Energy Solutions for Warehouse Management” on Tuesday, March 18 at 2:15 p.m. in Theater H.
- Stryten Energy powers applications including submarines, subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- The company’s stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- Stryten Energy is an industry leader backed by more than a century of expertise.
