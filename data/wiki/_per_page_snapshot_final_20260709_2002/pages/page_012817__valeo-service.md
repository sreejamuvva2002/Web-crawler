# Valeo Service

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Road transportation accounts for 18% of CO2 emissions.
- Valeo is committed to playing a major role in the decarbonization of the automotive industry for new vehicles and current rolling vehicles.
- Valeo Service operates under an environmental initiative called “I Care For The Planet” to limit its activity's impact on the planet.
- Valeo aims to involve its entire ecosystem – plants, employees, suppliers, and partners – in the process of decarbonizing its activities.
- CanopyTM was launched in June 2023 in Europe as the first Valeo Aftermarket innovation designed for the environment.
- Canopy wipers produce -61% CO2 emissions compared to a wiper blade produced in Asia and packaged in plastic, verified by Bureau Veritas.
- Valeo remanufactures around 1 million parts each year, with more than 8,000 part numbers (P/N).
- Valeo addresses the rapid rise of light electric mobility (e-bikes, e-kick scooters, and e-mopeds) through industrial repair and remanufacturing for fleet operators, repairers, and manufacturers.
- Valeo is a leader in Electrification for Powertrain and Battery Thermal Management.
- More than 13 million electric or hybrid vehicles are estimated to be equipped with Valeo parts, which reduce CO2 emissions.
- Valeo provides workshop training to help distributors and repairers understand new EV technologies.
- Ineez cables and EV charging stations contribute to extending the charging station network, making EV charging more convenient and flexible for EV drivers.
- Electric retrofitting consists of replacing a vehicle's internal combustion engine and fuel tank with an electric motor and a battery, offering an alternative to purchasing new electric models.
- Valeo is expanding its product and technology portfolio into the EV retrofit market, adapting its solutions to the specific needs of industry professionals.
- Since 2022, plastic packagings in Europe are replaced by eco-conceived cardboard packagings adapted to each sales channel and customer that are 100% recyclable.
- More than 400 tons of plastic is saved each year due to the packaging change, which corresponds to the weight of more than 11 million plastic bottles based on 2020 sales volumes in Europe.
- Valeo Service promotes direct delivery from factory to customers to save delivery time and reduce costs.
- Since 2021, around 55,000 trees have been planted all over the world through a tree plantation program connected to direct delivery.
