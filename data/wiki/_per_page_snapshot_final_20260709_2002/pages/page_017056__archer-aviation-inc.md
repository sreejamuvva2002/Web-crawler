# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Energy, Automobile, EV, Renewable News | Stellantis Increases Strategic Shareholding In Archer Aviation](https://oilandgaspress.com/stellantis-increases-strategic-shareholding-in-archer-aviation/)  ·  `page_017056`

## Facts

- On 20 March 2024, Stellantis N.V. and Archer Aviation Inc. announced that Stellantis recently completed a series of open market purchases of ~8.3 million shares of Archer’s stock.
- The share purchase signals Stellantis’ continued confidence in Archer’s plans to bring electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft to market beginning in 2025.
- Stellantis’ increased investment follows CEO Carlos Tavares’ recent visit to Archer’s headquarters and manufacturing facilities in Santa Clara, California.
- In January 2023, Stellantis announced its goal to mass produce Archer’s Midnight aircraft as its exclusive contract manufacturer.
- The exclusive manufacturing partnership allows Archer to strengthen its path to commercialization by helping it avoid hundreds of millions of dollars of spending.
- Construction on the first phase of Archer’s high-volume manufacturing facility in Georgia remains on track to be completed later this year.
- The first phase of the Georgia facility is a build out of ~350,000 square feet on a ~100 acre site.
- The Georgia facility is designed to support production of up to 650 aircraft annually.
- At full capacity, the Georgia facility would make it one of the largest manufacturing facilities by volume in the aircraft industry.
- Archer’s Midnight aircraft is designed to be safe, sustainable, quiet and carry four passengers plus a pilot.
- The Midnight aircraft is optimized for back-to-back short distance trips of around 20-50 miles, with a charging time of approximately 10 minutes in-between.
- Stellantis CEO Carlos Tavares stated: “Archer has established itself as a market leader in the electrification of aviation and we believe that together we can usher in the next transportation revolution.”
- Archer founder and CEO Adam Goldstein stated: “We are on the cusp of changing the way the world moves in the sky.”
- Adam Goldstein added: “Archer and Stellantis are working side by side to realize a once-in-a-generation opportunity to redefine urban transportation and deliver tremendous value to the world’s cities and our shareholders.”
