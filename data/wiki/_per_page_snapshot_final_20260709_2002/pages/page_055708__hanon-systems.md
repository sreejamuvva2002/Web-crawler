# Hanon Systems

## Facts

- Hanon Systems will invest more than $40 million for a manufacturing facility in Bulloch County.
- The investment is expected to create 160 jobs.
- Hanon Systems specializes in automotive climate control systems.
- Hanon Systems is a supplier for Hyundai.
- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp’s office announced the investment on Tuesday.
- The new facility is located in the Savannah area of Georgia.
- Gov. Brian Kemp stated that Georgia’s automotive and e-mobility industries continue to grow as they welcome Hanon Systems to the state.
- Gov. Brian Kemp noted that as Georgia furthers its position as the EV capital of America, new jobs and opportunities are coming to hardworking Georgians.
