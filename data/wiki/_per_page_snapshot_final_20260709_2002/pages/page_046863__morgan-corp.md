# Morgan Corp.

## Facts

- Morgan Corp. provides all infrastructure construction necessary for commercial projects.
- The company has experience ranging from a 10-acre office site to a 300-acre regional shopping mall complex.
- Morgan Corp. handles the majority of work in-house to minimize costly delays and maintain control over the sequence and flow of work activities.
- The company utilizes innovative ideas, advanced technology, and vast equipment resources to complete commercial projects safely, timely, and efficiently.
- Commercial project types range from small, one building office and retail sites to large office parks, shopping centers, and regional malls.
- Morgan Corp. offers site preparation services for a variety of industrial projects.
- Industrial development includes site preparation for warehouses, manufacturing facilities, power generation stations, distribution centers, airports, and mining and quarrying operations.
- Morgan Corp. has completed various industrial projects throughout the Southeast for national and international companies including BMW, Duke Power Co., Microsoft, and Wal-Mart.
- Morgan Corp. had a 10-year involvement in the initial development, operations, and final closure of the Ridgeway Gold Mine in Ridgeway, SC.
- The Ridgeway Gold Mine was one of the largest precious metals mining operations in the eastern United States.
- Morgan Corp. provided a total site preparation package for Plant Vogtle Units 3 and 4 in Waynesboro, GA.
- Plant Vogtle Units 3 and 4 consist of two new AP1000 Model, 1100Mw Nuclear Power Generating Units.
