# Nifco KTW America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-10-16

**Source:** [Nifco KTW receives 2020 Georgia GEAR Award for Supplier of the Year - WNEG](https://wnegradio.com/nifco-ktw-receives-2020-georgia-gear-award-for-supplier-of-the-year/)  ·  `page_058163`

## Facts

- Nifco KTW America is the recipient of the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s 2020 Georgia GEAR Award for Supplier of the Year—Employing 200 or more people.
- “GEAR” stands for: Georgia Emerging Automotive Recognition Award.
- On Wednesday, leaders from the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s Global Commerce Division came to Toccoa to recognized Nifco KTW with the award.
- Nifco KTW President Joe Hibdon says he is thankful for this recognition from the State of Georgia.
- Georgia Department of Economic Development’s Deputy Commissioner of Global Commerce Scott McMurray presented the award to Hibdon.
- Stephens County Development Authority Executive Director Tim Martin says Stephens County is grateful for the impact Nifco KTW has had on the local economy.
- This year, automotive and mobility companies from across the State of Georgi were nominated for their leadership in several categories: Supplier of the Year—employing 200 or more people, Supplier of the Year—employing 199 or fewer people, Transportation Equipment Manufacture of the Year, Automotive Innovation Award, and Citizenship Award.
- Nifco KTW opened its first U.S. plant in Toccoa, Georgia in 2016.
