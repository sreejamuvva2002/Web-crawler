## Facts

- On Thursday, Governor Brian Kemp signed an executive order extending the temporary suspension of the state gas tax and the supply chain state of emergency.
- Solar energy giant QCells will create 470 new jobs through an expansion of its facilities in Whitfield County.
- The governor announced the purchase of a more than 1,100-acre economic development site in partnership between the state of Georgia and the Development Authority of Peach County, designated as the Middle Georgia Megasite to position the community for significant investment and job creation.
- On June 17, Governor Brian Kemp joined officials from Aurubis and state, local, and federal leaders to break ground on a recycling and copper smelting facility in Augusta.
- The Aurubis facility is the first-of-its-kind in the U.S. and represents the largest investment by a German company in Georgia.
- The Aurubis recycling and copper smelting facility will create 125 jobs for the Augusta community.
