# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-08-11

**Source:** [Novelis Announces Senior Notes Offering](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/novelis-announces-senior-notes-offering-302526454.html)  ·  `page_028897`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is a leading sustainable aluminum solutions provider and the world leader in aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Novelis announced it is proposing to offer $750 million aggregate principal amount of its senior unsecured notes due 2033 in a private offering exempt from registration under the Securities Act of 1933.
- The issuer intends to use net proceeds from the proposed offering, together with cash on hand, to purchase all outstanding $750 million aggregate principal amount of its 3.250% Senior Notes due November 2026 that are validly tendered and not withdrawn pursuant to a previously announced tender offer.
- To the extent there are remaining proceeds, the issuer intends but is not obligated to use them to redeem the remaining 2026 Notes not validly tendered at the applicable optional redemption price plus accrued interest.
- Novelis partners with customers in the aerospace, automotive, beverage packaging, and specialties industries throughout North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- Novelis had net sales of $17.1 billion in fiscal year 2025.
- Novelis is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited, an industry leader in aluminum and copper, and the metals flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group, a multinational conglomerate based in Mumbai.
