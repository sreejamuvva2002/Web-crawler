## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) is headquartered at One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA.
- PCNA has been headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia since 1998.
- PCNA supports 192 independently owned and operated Porsche dealerships in the U.S., supplying parts, service, marketing, and training.
- PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera and Taycan.
- The Taycan electric vehicle is among the models imported by PCNA.
- The newly launched Porsche Parking Plus app includes a filter for parking facility features that specifically lists "electric vehicle charging."
- PCNA is home to the first Porsche Experience Center in North America featuring a module-based 1.6 mile driver development track, business center, and fine dining restaurant, 356.
- Kjell Gruner serves as President & CEO of PCNA, where he is responsible for both the United States and Canada.
- Kjell Gruner also heads the executive leadership team of Porsche Digital, Inc., the sports car manufacturer’s digital subsidiary in the U.S.
- The technology platform for the Porsche Parking Plus app will be managed by the Chicago-based company Arrive.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
