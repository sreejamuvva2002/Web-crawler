# NIFCO KTW

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-21

**Source:** [NIFCO KTW – Stephens County Development Authority](https://scda.biz/nifco-ktw/)  ·  `page_058125`

## Facts

- NIFCO KTW is hiring for multiple Molding Process Technician positions in Stephens County.
- Compensation for the technician roles is based on experience.
- The application deadline for the positions was November 21, 2022.
- Technicians are responsible for basic mold setup and molding process operations, including independent setup, startup, and maintenance of tooling.
- Responsibilities include performing mold changes and checking auxiliary equipment such as dryers, thermolators, and chillers to ensure they are working and filters are clean.
- Technicians must submit work order requests for equipment maintenance and handle the startup and shutdown of presses.
- The role requires cleaning and maintaining molds in the area and submitting work orders when starting up a job.
- Employees must review job requirements with personnel responsible for running the job and respond to all corrective actions as quickly as possible.
- Technicians must keep all processes up to date, recording reasons for changes, hours, and codes on the molding log if changes are made.
- The position allows technicians to adjust production parameters within tolerance specifications as permitted and authorized.
- Employees are required to report any safety issues or concerns to the molding manager and the maintenance manager.
- Technicians must understand current company quality guidelines and report changes in production sequence, workplace design, or parameters.
- The role involves checking and verifying that correct mold changes are completed accurately by the mold setter.
- Employees must review production releases and ensure accurate completion by the due date.
- Technicians are responsible for supporting the implementation of the defined 5S standard and adhering to all safety guidelines outlined by OSHA and Nifco KTW.
- The position requires taking responsibility for equipment and operator safety, as well as assisting in the training of new employees.
- Technicians must assist in troubleshooting and identifying defects, implementing solutions, and accurately completing paperwork or entering data into a computer.
- Employees must continuously participate in improving processes and procedures and forward suggestions for improvement to the shift supervisor and manager.
- Technicians are required to immediately inform the shift supervisor about detected quality issues and malfunctions within their work area.
- Candidates must have completed Mold Set Up training, Mold Processing and Troubleshooting courses, or possess equivalent experience.
- Applicants need knowledge of injection molding and operations, along with the ability to work with all auxiliary equipment including dryers, thermolators, and chillers.
- The technician serves as a technical go-to person for associates in the production area.
- Employees participate in implementing new production plans and technologies, including validation of new molds.
- Technicians are responsible for maintaining cleanliness of their work area and ensuring compliance with order and cleanliness regulations throughout the company.
- The role requires good verbal communication between departments and shifts to support the shift supervisor in ensuring all regulations regarding order and cleanliness are complied with.
