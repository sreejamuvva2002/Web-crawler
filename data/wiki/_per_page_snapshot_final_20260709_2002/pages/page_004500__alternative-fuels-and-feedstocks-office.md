# Alternative Fuels and Feedstocks Office

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-30

**Source:** [Hydrogen and Fuel Cell Technologies Accomplishments and Progress | Department of Energy](https://www.energy.gov/cmei/fuels/hydrogen-and-fuel-cell-technologies-accomplishments-and-progress)  ·  `page_004500`

## Facts

- Leads federal efforts to enable the commercial viability of hydrogen and fuel cell technologies.
- Conducts a broad portfolio of research, development, and demonstration activities for hydrogen and fuel cell technologies.
- Conducts activities in manufacturing, safety, codes and standards, and workforce development to further support deployment and market adoption.
- Efforts help support domestic energy security and resilience.
- Efforts strengthen American competitiveness and innovation in the rapidly growing global hydrogen and fuel cell industry.
- Published a fact sheet titled 'Progress in Hydrogen and Fuel Cells' in March 2025 describing how DOE funding has spurred significant progress in several areas.
