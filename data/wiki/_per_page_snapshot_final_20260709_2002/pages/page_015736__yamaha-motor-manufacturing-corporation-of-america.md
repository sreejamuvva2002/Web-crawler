# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America

## Facts

- YMMC is a product manufacturing affiliate of Yamaha Motor Corporation (YMC) and a subsidiary of Yamaha Motor (YMUS).
- YMMC develops, tests, fabricates, and assembles ATVs, Golf Cars, Water Vehicles, and Recreational Off-Road Vehicles (Side by Sides).
- Products are produced for global distribution.
- The Newnan Yamaha Campus spans 1,355,000 square feet across six buildings on 280 acres.
- The facility employs over 2,000 team members.
- The Newnan facility was founded in 1986.
- Production at the facility started in 1988 with golf cars.
- Since 1988, the facility has produced over 4 million vehicles.
- Yamaha is investing in a new SMART (Sequenced Material and Reduced Transportation) distribution facility, which will be the seventh building on the campus.
- The facility is located in Coweta County, Georgia.
- Product Sales and Marketing functions are specific to YMC, YMUS, and YGC; YMMC does not directly sell products to the public or service them.
- Yamaha partners utilize a network of dealerships for product sales and service.
- Yamaha does not manage dealership relationships or certify new dealers.
