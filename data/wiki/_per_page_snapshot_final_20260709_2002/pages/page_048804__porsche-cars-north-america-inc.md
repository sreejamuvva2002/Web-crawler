# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-04-26

**Source:** [Porsche customer teams look beyond the surface at Monterey Peninsula | Conceptcarz.com](https://www.conceptcarz.com/a46160/porsche-customer-teams.aspx)  ·  `page_048804`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) is headquartered at One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA.
- PCNA was established in 1984.
- PCNA has been headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, since 1998.
- PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera and Taycan.
- PCNA is home to the first Porsche Experience Center in North America featuring a module-based 1.6 mile driver development track, business center, and fine dining restaurant, 356.
- The company operates a second Porsche Experience Center near Los Angeles.
- The Los Angeles complex is a 53-acre facility featuring a driver development track with eight educational modules totaling 4.1 miles, a business center, and Restaurant 917.
- PCNA supports 194 independently owned and operated Porsche dealerships in the U.S., including supplying parts, service, marketing, and training.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG.
- Porsche AG is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
