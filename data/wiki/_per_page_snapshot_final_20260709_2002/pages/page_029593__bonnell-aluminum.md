# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-01

**Source:** [Bonnell Aluminum Announces New Vice President of Sales and Marketing](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260225281892/en/Bonnell-Aluminum-Announces-New-Vice-President-of-Sales-and-Marketing)  ·  `page_029593`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum appointed Tracy Bridges as Vice President of Sales and Marketing, effective March 1, 2026.
- Tracy Bridges is replacing Eric Yost, who will retire from the company after 28 years of service.
- Tracy Bridges has been with Bonnell Aluminum for more than 20 years.
- Tracy Bridges previously served as Director of Customer Experience at Bonnell Aluminum.
- Tracy Bridges' prior roles at Bonnell Aluminum include Regional Sales Manager, Segment Sales Manager, and Market Manager.
- Brook Hamilton serves as President of Bonnell Aluminum.
- Brook Hamilton emphasized the importance of identifying a successor to advance the company's commercial legacy cultivated over the last 70 years.
- Brook Hamilton stated that Tracy Bridges' leadership and commercial expertise will support the company's continued growth and sales excellence culture.
- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation (NYSE:TG).
- Bonnell Aluminum manufactures custom-fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions for the building and construction, automotive and transportation, consumer durables, machinery and equipment, and electrical and distribution markets.
- Bonnell Aluminum's branded product lines include TSLOTS and Futura Transitions.
- Bonnell Aluminum operates manufacturing facilities in Newnan, Georgia; Carthage, Tennessee; Niles, Michigan; Elkhart, Indiana; and Clearfield, Utah.
