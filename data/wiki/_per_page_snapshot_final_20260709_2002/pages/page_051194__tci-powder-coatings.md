# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-05-18

**Source:** [TCI Powder Coatings, a Subsidiary of RPM International, Hires Rich Chappel as Business Development Manager for the Southwest Territory > Powder Coated Tough Magazine](https://www.powdercoatedtough.com/News/ID/641/TCI-Powder-Coatings-a-Subsidiary-of-RPM-International-Hires-Rich-Chappel-as-Business-Development-Manager-for-the-Southwest-Territory)  ·  `page_051194`

## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is located in Ellaville, Georgia.
- TCI Powder Coatings operates as a subsidiary of RPM International Inc.
- Rich Chappel was appointed as the business development manager for the Southwest Region, covering Texas, Louisiana, Colorado, New Mexico, and Oklahoma.
- Rich Chappel brings more than 25 years of experience in powder coatings and the Industrial market segment to his role at TCI.
- For the past 16 years, Chappel held various positions at Valspar in Sales & Market Segment Management.
- Prior to working for Valspar, Chappel was an integral part of the team that launched the Lilly Powder Coatings business.
- Jack Bostock serves as TCI vice president of sales and marketing.
- Jack Bostock stated that Rich Chappel brings a unique blend of skills in business development and account management that will benefit customers and advance TCI’s premium brand position in the powder coatings market.
- Rich Chappel holds a B.A. in Business Administration (BBA) from the University of North Texas.
- Rich Chappel will continue to reside in the Dallas, Texas area with his wife Martha.
