# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-08-21

**Source:** [TCI Powder Coatings, RPM International, Names Thomas G. Whalen Director of Marketing and Segment Strategy](http://www.tcipowdercoatings.com/2015/08/tci-powder-coatings-rpm-international.html)  ·  `page_051235`

## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is a subsidiary of RPM International Inc. (NYSE: RPM).
- Thomas G. Whalen was appointed as Director of Marketing and Segment Strategy at TCI Powder Coatings.
- The appointment was announced from Ellaville, GA on August 21, 2015.
- Whalen brings over 25 years of experience in the industrial coatings market to his new role.
- For the two years prior to this appointment, Whalen served as Global Business Director, Consumer Electronics, and Performance Glass Coatings at PPG Industries.
- Before joining PPG, Whalen was Vice President of Liquid and Mirror Coatings Units at Spraylat, which was acquired by PPG.
- Prior to Spraylat, Whalen worked at Valspar with increasing management experience, including serving as Global Segment Director.
- Whalen began his coatings career in 1986 at Moline Paint in sales and later held senior positions including International Business Director.
- Jack Bostock, TCI Vice President Sales and Marketing, stated the company is thrilled to have Whalen join their team.
- Whalen will help lead TCI in organizing around global segmentation and identifying strategic segments to differentiate TCI from other powder coating companies.
- Whalen holds a B.S. in International Business from Illinois State University and resides in Frankfort, IL with his wife and three children.
- TCI Powder Coatings has been manufacturing powder coatings for over 25 years, focusing on developing superior thermoset powder coatings.
- RPM International Inc. is a holding company that owns subsidiaries leading in specialty coatings, sealants, building materials, and related services for industrial and consumer markets.
- RPM's industrial brands include Stonhard, Tremco, Illbruck, Carboline, Flowcrete, Universal Sealants, and Euco.
- RPM's consumer brands include Zinsser, Rust-Oleum, DAP, Varathane, and Testors.
