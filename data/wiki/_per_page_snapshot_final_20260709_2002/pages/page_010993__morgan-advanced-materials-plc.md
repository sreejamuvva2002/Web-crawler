## Facts

- Morgan Advanced Materials plc operates an Americas manufacturing footprint with sites in Canada, the United States, Mexico, and Argentina.
- The company's Canada site manufactures inorganic Superwool® and Kaowool® Board and Shape products.
- The US site manufactures a complete portfolio of products and systems including Pyro-Bloc® Modules and Pyro-Log (Emporia), WDS® and Min-K® microporous (Elkhart), Monolithics and TR™ Structural Insulation (Girard).
- The Augusta facility produces all product forms from Superwool, Kaowool and Cera® fibres, as well as the refractories portfolio of Kaolite®, Kao-Tuff®, and Kaocrete® Monolithics and K™-Series IFB.
- Mexico sites manufacture fibre based products from Superwool and Cera Fibre chemistries and a complete line of Kaolite and Kaocrete refractories serving Mexico and Central America.
- The Argentina site manufactures Carbo Monolithics with a comprehensive fibre portfolio of Cera and Kaowool chemistries.
- Morgan Advanced Materials lists Electric Vehicles as one of its key application categories for its products.
- Additional product application categories include Aerospace, Iron & Steel, Transportation Industry, Petrochemical & Refinery Processes, Passive Fire Protection Applications, Cement, and Power Generation Applications.
