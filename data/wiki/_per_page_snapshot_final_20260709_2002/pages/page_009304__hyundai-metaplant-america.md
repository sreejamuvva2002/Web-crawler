# Hyundai Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group set out to build a nearly 3,000-acre Metaplant in Ellabell, Georgia.
- The facility is designed for producing electric and hybrid vehicles.
- Moses Grass Company served as the primary erosion control contractor for the project.
- Moses Grass Company has over 75 years of expertise supporting infrastructure growth throughout Georgia.
- Construction crews implemented comprehensive erosion control strategies throughout the site.
- Crews installed miles of silt fencing to protect nearby waterways and wetlands.
- Crews carried out wetland mitigation activities on the site.
- Hydroseeding was utilized to rapidly stabilize disturbed areas.
- Agricultural drones were introduced to plant temporary grass efficiently in challenging locations like materials stockpiles.
- The erosion control efforts ensured continuous environmental protection and regulatory compliance.
- The project is described as enhancing infrastructure, protecting the environment, and strengthening local economies across Georgia.
