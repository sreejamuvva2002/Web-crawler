# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-12-02

**Source:** [Yamaha donates $10K for kids to learn science on floating classroom | The Citizen](https://thecitizen.com/2021/12/02/yamaha-donates-10k-for-kids-to-learn-science-on-floating-classroom/)  ·  `page_015756`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America designs and assembles WaveRunners, ATVs, Side by Sides, and Golf Cars at its Newnan plant in Coweta County, Georgia.
- The Newnan facility is one of Coweta County’s largest private employers.
- Yamaha is currently expanding its team by hiring another 200 contract employees before the end of the year (2021).
- Most jobs with Yamaha start as contracts at $16.50 per hour and are eligible to be converted to full-time hires in 30 days.
- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing in Newnan donated $10,000 to fund scholarships for over 300 students from Title I schools in Coweta County to attend a floating classroom program on West Point Lake.
- The $10,000 donation specifically provides opportunities for 333 students to attend the three-hour field trip.
- The donation was paid for through the company's recycling program at the Newnan facility.
- Bob Brown, Vice President of Finance and Operations Support at Yamaha, stated the company aims to excite kids about science and invest in future generations of scientists and vehicle assemblers.
