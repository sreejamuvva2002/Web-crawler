# Norton Packaging, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2013-02-12

**Source:** [Norton Packaging to Open Georgia Manufacturing Operation in Monticello - Location Georgia](https://www.locationgeorgia.com/2013/02/12/norton-packaging-to-open-georgia-manufacturing-operation-in-monticello/)  ·  `page_008274`

## Facts

- Norton Packaging, Inc. is creating 90 new jobs and investing $12 million in a new manufacturing operation in Jasper County.
- Norton Packaging broke ground for its new facility on Feb. 12, 2013.
- The company specializes in containers that hold a wide variety of products.
- The Monticello facility will manufacture plastic injection molded rigid shipping containers.
- GDEcD Commissioner Chris Cummiskey stated that Georgia hosts a thriving environment for manufacturers and offers well-trained workers and an outstanding logistics system.
- Norton Packaging is owned by the same family who founded it in 1901.
- The company supplies customers in the United States, Southeast Asia, Europe, and Australia.
- Industries served include paint, janitorial, petrochemical, adhesive, agricultural chemicals, and food packaging.
- Norton Packaging is headquartered in Hayward, Calif., and operates three other facilities in California and Indiana.
- The 76,000-square-foot Georgia location will service the southeast region of the United States as well as Puerto Rico and Europe.
- Scott Norton, co-president of Norton Packaging, stated the location extends existing customer relationships and expands the customer base in fast-growing markets.
- The company was drawn to Georgia by its advantageous business climate.
- Norton plans to begin hiring by July 2013.
- Georgia Quick Start will assist the company with customized workforce training needs.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development partnered with the Development Authority of Jasper County to manage this project.
- GDEcD senior project manager Andrew Neumann assisted Norton on behalf of the state.
- David Dyer, Executive Director of the Development Authority of Jasper County, commented on the company finding a perfect fit for its needs.
- Monticello Mayor Bryan Standifer commented on the job opportunities created in the community.
- Since 1901, Norton Packaging has developed and grown under four generations of Norton family members.
- The company currently numbers over 600 domestic and foreign customers.
- Norton Packaging currently operates two production facilities nationally.
