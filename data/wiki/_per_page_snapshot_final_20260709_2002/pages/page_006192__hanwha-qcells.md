# Hanwha QCells

## Facts

- The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) Loan Programs Office (LPO) is preparing an Environmental Assessment (EA).
- The EA will consider environmental impacts associated with providing potential financial assistance.
- The potential financial assistance will take the form of a federal loan guarantee.
- The federal loan guarantee is intended to support the construction completion and operation of a new solar panel production facility.
- The new solar panel production facility is located in Bartow County, Georgia.
- The project is identified as the Hanwha QCells Project Redeemer.
