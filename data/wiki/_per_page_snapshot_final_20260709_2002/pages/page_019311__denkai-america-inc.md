## Facts

- Denkai America is an electronic parts manufacturer based in South Carolina.
- The company was founded in 1976.
- Denkai America specializes in the production of electrodeposited copper foils for printed circuit board (PCB), industrial, and energy storage applications.
- With domestic manufacturing and a global presence, Denkai America delivers both conventional cladding and application specific copper foils required by technology leading companies.
- Denkai America is headquartered in Camden, South Carolina.
- Denkai America employs over 50 individuals.
