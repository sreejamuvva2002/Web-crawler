## Facts

- ALBAform Inc. is owned by Czech parent company ALBA-METAL.
- The company designs, develops, and manufactures precision metal components with a focus on the automotive industry.
- The company has a seasoned international team of industry experts.
- ALBAform leverages over 20 years of ALBA-METAL’s metal production experience combined with its own to meet automotive industry demands and standards.
- The facility is located in Flowery Branch, Georgia.
- The company is growing quickly to service its partners and customers.
- In addition to manufacturing capabilities, the company offers consignment stock systems using standardized packaging and transport units to minimize inventory and transportation costs.
- The plant tour showcased the latest fully automated CNC bending technology.
- The facility utilizes 16 welding robots.
