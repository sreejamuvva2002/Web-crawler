## Facts

- Haering Precision, a German technology company, opened a new production plant in northeastern Georgia.
- The new facility is located in Lavonia, G.A.
- The plant currently occupies 160,000 square feet.
- The Lavonia site has the potential to be expanded to more than 500,000 square feet.
- Haering Group manufactures precision parts for a wide range of industries.
- President Dr.-Ing. Jürgen Häring stated that the company needs committed individuals from Lavonia or the broader region who are looking forward to a new professional challenge.
- The company is currently hiring for open positions at the new facility.
