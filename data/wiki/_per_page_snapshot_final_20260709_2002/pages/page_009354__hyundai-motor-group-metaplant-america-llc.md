# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America LLC

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Enforcement Order EPD-WP-9650 |  Georgia EPD Enforcement Orders](https://enfo.gaepd.org/Details/75211)  ·  `page_009354`

## Facts

- The facility is Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America LLC located in Bryan County.
- The facility discharged industrial wastewater to a publicly owned treatment works without an industrial pretreatment permit for such discharge.
- The facility utilized a sewage holding tank at the Facility for purposes other than construction trailers at a construction site.
- The order requires submission of all required documents to EPD Industrial Permitting Program for final action by the Director regarding an industrial pretreatment permit for discharge to the North Bryan WRF.
- The order requires compliance with requirements for pumping and hauling Non-Process Wastewater.
- The order requires submission of a revised Corrective Action Plan for review and approval.
- The settlement amount for the enforcement order is $30,000.00.
- The legal authority for the order is the Water Quality Control Act (including Surface Water Allocation).
- The enforcement order was executed on 25-Apr-2025.
- The publication date for the executed order is 5-May-2025.
