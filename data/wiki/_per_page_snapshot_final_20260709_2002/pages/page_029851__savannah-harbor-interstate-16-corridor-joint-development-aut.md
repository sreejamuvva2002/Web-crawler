## Facts

- The Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority includes the development authorities of Bryan, Bulloch, Chatham and Effingham counties in South Georgia.
- Founded in 2014, the Savannah JDA combines resources to attract and facilitate regionally significant projects.
- The Savannah JDA Board of Directors will meet Thursday, April 16, 10 a.m. at the Georgia Southern University’s Division of Continuing and Professional Education (CPE Center), 10449 US-301, Statesboro, Ga. 30458.
