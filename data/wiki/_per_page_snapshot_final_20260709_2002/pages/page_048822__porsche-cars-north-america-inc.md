# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-24

**Source:** [Porsche Experience Centers welcome the new Macan Electric to the track - EIN Presswire](https://www.einnews.com/pr_news/788706486/porsche-experience-centers-welcome-the-new-macan-electric-to-the-track)  ·  `page_048822`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) announced the addition of the Macan Electric to driving programs at the Porsche Experience Centers in Atlanta and Los Angeles.
- The Macan Electric joins existing PEC driving programs that feature models ranging from the 911 to the Cayenne.
- Michelle Rainey serves as Director of Porsche Experience Centers.
- Atlanta customers can select from three Drive experiences: Macan Electric, Macan Turbo Electric, and a comparison of the Macan Turbo Electric and Macan GTS.
- Los Angeles customers can select from three options: Macan Turbo Electric drive experience, Macan Turbo Electric vs Taycan Turbo, and Macan Turbo Electric vs internal combustion engine Macan GTS.
- Each 90-minute Drive program begins with a safety orientation conducted by a Porsche Drive Instructor.
- Track activities include driver development modules such as the handling circuit, kick plate, and low friction circle.
- PEC Atlanta and PEC Los Angeles are celebrating their 10th anniversary in the United States.
- The experience centers feature immersive brand destinations with track experiences, curated vehicle displays, simulators, dining, retail stores, meeting spaces, and architecture.
- Drive programs begin at $600.
