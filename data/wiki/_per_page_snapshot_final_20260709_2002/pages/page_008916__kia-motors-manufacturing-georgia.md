# KIA Motors Manufacturing Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2013-03-01

**Source:** [Georgia Built Vehicles Leading the Way for KIA | Georgia Public Broadcasting](https://www.gpb.org/blogs/georgia-works/2013/03/01/georgia-built-vehicles-leading-the-way-for-kia)  ·  `page_008916`

## Facts

- KIA Motors Manufacturing Georgia continues to lead the way for one of the world’s fastest growing automobile manufacturers.
- February 2013 vehicle sales figures show the KIA Optima and KIA Sorento as the leading units for KIA Motors with over 20,000 units sold during the month.
- KIA announced that 2012 was the 18th consecutive year in which the manufacturer has gained U.S. market share.
- KIA Motors recently became one of Interbrand’s “Top 100 Global Brands” thanks in large part to the quality products being produced at the West Point, GA manufacturing facility.
- Since its groundbreaking, KIA Motors Manufacturing Georgia has invested more than $1.4 billion in Georgia while creating over 11,000 manufacturing and supplier related jobs.
