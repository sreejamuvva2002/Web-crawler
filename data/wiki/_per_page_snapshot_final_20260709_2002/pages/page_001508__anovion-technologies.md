# Anovion Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Electric Battery Graphite to Be Produced in Georgia | National Oil and Lube News](https://www.noln.net/site-placement/latest-news/news/33005187/electric-battery-graphite-to-be-produced-in-georgia)  ·  `page_001508`

## Facts

- Anovion Technologies has announced that it will invest $800 million into a factory located in Bainbridge, Georgia.
- Anovion Technologies is based in Chicago and was created last year when Pyrotek and Amsted Graphite Materials merged together.
- The company operates existing factories in Sanborn, New York, and Clarksburg, West Virginia.
- Anovion recently announced plans to construct a facility near Muscle Shoals, Alabama.
- The Georgia factory will employ 400 workers.
- The Bainbridge facility is expected to begin production on synthetic graphite anode in 2025.
- According to Georgia officials, this project is one of more than 40 EV-related projects initiated in the state since 2020.
- Anovion Technologies is a supplier of graphite for electric batteries.
- CEO Eric Stopk stated that the company looks forward to providing solutions that will further secure the country's electrified future with the support of partners in Georgia and the federal government.
