# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2007-02-10

**Source:** [Hindalco Industries Completes Acquisition of Novelis Inc. :: Novelis Inc.](https://investors.novelis.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/415/hindalco-industries-completes-acquisition-of-novelis-inc)  ·  `page_047351`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. announced the completion of its acquisition by Hindalco Industries Limited.
- The all-cash transaction values Novelis at approximately US$6.0 billion, including debt.
- Novelis shareholders will receive US$44.93 in cash for each outstanding common share.
- Novelis shareholders approved the transaction at a special meeting on May 10.
- Hindalco is based in Mumbai, India, and serves as the flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group.
- The Aditya Birla Group reports annual revenue of US$14 billion and a market capitalization in excess of US$23 billion.
- Novelis will operate as a subsidiary of Hindalco following the acquisition.
- Martha Brooks, Novelis' chief operating officer, stated the arrangement created significant value for shareholders while providing new opportunities for the combined company's future.
- Kumar Mangalam Birla, chairman of Hindalco, described aluminum as a core business with enormous growth potential and viewed the acquisition as a step toward becoming a premium metals major.
- Debnarayan Bhattacharya, managing director of Hindalco, highlighted geographical market and product synergies, noting Novelis is the global leader in value-added, high-end aluminum rolled products and aluminum can recycling.
- The transaction was accomplished by way of a statutory plan of arrangement under Canadian law.
- Hindalco, through its wholly-owned subsidiary AV Metals Inc., acquired 75,415,536 common shares of Novelis, representing 100 percent of the issued and outstanding common shares.
- Immediately after closing, AV Metals Inc. transferred the common shares to its wholly-owned subsidiary AV Aluminum Inc.
- Novelis' stock has ceased trading on the New York Stock Exchange.
- De-listing on the New York Stock Exchange and the Toronto Stock Exchange is expected to occur shortly.
- Novelis is the global leader in aluminum rolled products and aluminum can recycling.
- The Company operates in 11 countries, has approximately 12,900 employees, and reported revenue of $9.8 billion in 2006.
- Novelis provides customers with a regional supply of technologically sophisticated rolled aluminum products throughout Asia, Europe, North America and South America.
- The Company supplies aluminum sheet and foil to the automotive and transportation, beverage and food packaging, construction and industrial, and printing markets.
