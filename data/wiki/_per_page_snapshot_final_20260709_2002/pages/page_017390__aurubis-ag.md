# Aurubis AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Tin-plated solutions For](https://www.yumpu.com/en/document/view/3828958/aurubis-rolled-products-2-mb/9)  ·  `page_017390`

## Facts

- Aurubis operates two hot-dip tinning and two electroplating lines in Europe and the US.
- Aurubis offers additional coating types including TN, TQ, silver, and gold, with selective procedures available via service partners.
- The three main coating processes used are hot-dip tinning, electroplating, and electroplating with a subsequent reflow treatment.
- Automotive, electrical, and electronics industries place high demands on connectors and stamped grids, often requiring surface-treated copper and copper alloys.
- Hot-dip tinning strip thickness ranges from 0.10 to 1.50 mm, with tin layer thicknesses from 0.8 to 12 µm providing properties like low insertion force, corrosion protection, good solderability, and coverage for special applications.
- Electroplated strip thickness ranges from 0.15 to 4.0 mm, offering processes such as Matte/Bright/Brushed Tin, Reflowed TN (available in the US), TQ (available in the US), Advanced Reflow Tin, Super Thin Advanced Reflow Tin (STAR), Nickel Diffusion barrier, and Silver.
- Coating properties include super low insertion forces, long-term solderability, whisker formation avoidance, high corrosion protection, and higher temperature resistance.
- Other alloys and selective plating are available upon request.
- Commercial services include copper market competence, long-term copper supply, market information, and hedging advice.
- Technical services feature support from experienced engineers, joint production chain optimization projects, and a laboratory with physical/chemical analysis facilities including scanning electron microscopy.
- Logistics and service offerings include customer recycling concepts, an integrated supply chain from concentrate to strip, and worldwide availability through a global sales network.
- Aurubis maintains a global operational presence described as operating globally while acting locally.
- Contact details for Rolled Products are provided across Europe (Finland, France, Germany, Italy, Netherlands, Russia, Slovakia, Spain, Sweden, UK), the USA (Buffalo and Chicago Engine Cooling Products), and Asia (China, Hong Kong, Japan, Korea, Singapore, Taiwan, Thailand, Vietnam).
