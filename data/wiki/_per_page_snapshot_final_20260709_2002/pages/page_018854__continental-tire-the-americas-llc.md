## Facts

- Continental Tire the Americas is a major subsidiary of Continental AG.
- The company manufactures and distributes a complete premium line of passenger, light truck, and commercial tires for original equipment and replacement markets.
- Founded in 1871, the technology company offers safe, efficient, intelligent, and affordable solutions for vehicles, machines, traffic, and transportation.
- Continental Tire the Americas is listed as a participating contractor under the NASPO ValuePoint portfolio program for the State of Georgia (along with other states).
- The statewide contract was awarded on 04/01/2019, with an expiration date of 06/30/2024 and a renewal limit of 06/30/2024.
