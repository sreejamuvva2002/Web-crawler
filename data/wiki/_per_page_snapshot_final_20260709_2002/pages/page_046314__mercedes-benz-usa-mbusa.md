# Mercedes-Benz USA (MBUSA)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-08-11

**Source:** [Mercedes-Benz announces new U.S. HQ in Atlanta | UWire](https://www.uwire.com/2025/08/11/mercedes-benz-announces-new-u-s-hq-in-atlanta/)  ·  `page_046314`

## Facts

- Mercedes-Benz is establishing Atlanta as its North American headquarters.
- Gov. Brian Kemp announced the news in May.
- The company will add 500 jobs to its existing facility.
- A new research and development wing will be built near the HQ office.
- Gov. Brian Kemp stated: “Georgia continues to lead the way in the future of mobility and technical innovation, attracting world-class companies like Mercedes-Benz that are driving the automotive industry forward.”
- Gov. Brian Kemp stated: “We’re excited that a job creator that already has close ties to Georgia is doubling down on that choice and growing their presence here in the best state for business and opportunity.”
- The facility known as “1MB” currently houses 829 employees.
- The employee count at 1MB will increase with new hires and the new R&D hub.
- 1MB will be known as Mercedes-Benz USA (MBUSA) and will be responsible for the distribution, development, and marketing of all Mercedes-Benz products in the United States.
- The new R&D hub will bring engineering and technical talent to Georgia.
- The R&D hub will create opportunities to collaborate with Atlanta’s tech ecosystem.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated: “Since the strategic decision to relocate Mercedes-Benz USA to Sandy Springs in 2018, we have watched Mercedes-Benz become an integral part of our business community.”
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated: “Their continued growth and community involvement are a prime example of why we recruit industry leaders such as Mercedes-Benz to Georgia.”
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated: “This expansion and commitment to R&D in the metro Atlanta area will further strengthen the company’s long-term success, and highlights the talent and collaborative partnerships fostered by the University System of Georgia.”
- The office located in Sandy Springs aims to complete the transition in August 2026.
- The transition involves consolidating all of Mercedes-Benz's North American operations.
- Mayor Rusty Paul of the City of Sandy Springs stated: “We’re excited to see Mercedes-Benz expanding in Sandy Springs.”
- Mayor Rusty Paul stated: “Since establishing their headquarters here in 2018, they have been outstanding corporate partners.”
- Mayor Rusty Paul stated: “Their decision to grow in Sandy Springs highlights the success of the city’s recent infrastructure and capital investments, which are now clearly paying dividends.”
- Mayor Rusty Paul stated: “This expansion represents a wonderful opportunity and a significant milestone for our continued development.”
- Mercedes-Benz USA is part of the Mercedes-Benz Group AG, which includes a total of 175,000 employees worldwide.
- The company focuses on the development and sales of passenger cars, vans, and other vehicle-related services.
- The corporation is one of the world’s largest manufacturers of high-end passenger cars.
- MBUSA will continue to offer drivers a diverse lineup.
- The U.S. is an important market for the company with sales of 374,100 vehicles last year.
- The company is responsible for 11,100 direct jobs in the U.S., including corporate roles and customer service roles in dealerships around the country.
- Jason Hoff, CEO of Mercedes-Benz North America, stated: “We thank the State of Georgia for its support in deepening Mercedes-Benz’s roots in the Atlanta area as we bring even more talented team members to this world-class city.”
- Jason Hoff stated: “This strengthens our position for continued growth and reinforces our established commitment to the U.S. market.”
- Jason Hoff stated: “Bringing our teams closer together will enable us to be more agile, increase speed to market, and ensure the best customer experience.”
