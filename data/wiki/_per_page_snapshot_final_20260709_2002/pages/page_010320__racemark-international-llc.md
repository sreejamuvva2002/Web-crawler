# RACEMARK INTERNATIONAL, LLC

## Facts

- Located at 1711 HWY 41 SOUTH, CALHOUN, GA 30703.
- Operates under NAICS code 336360 for Motor Vehicle Seating and Interior Trim Manufacturing.
- Had one OSHA inspection on record as of February 28, 2007, resulting in 6 violations and $5,000 in penalties.
- Serious violations accounted for 50% (3 out of 6) of the total citations at this location.
- No willful or repeat violations have been recorded at this establishment.
- All inspections at this establishment have been closed.
- The most recent OSHA inspection was opened on February 28, 2007, and has since been closed.
- Violations cited include Control of Hazardous Energy (Lockout/Tagout), Medical Services and First Aid, Powered Industrial Trucks, General Requirements for All Machines (Machine Guarding), Electrical - General Requirements, and Electrical - Wiring Methods, Components, and Equipment.
- Penalties for the six violations were $1,000, $750, $2,000, $950, $150, and $150 respectively.
- The NAICS 336360 industry group comprises establishments primarily engaged in manufacturing motor vehicle gasoline engines and engine parts, motor vehicle electrical and electronic equipment, steering and suspension components, brakes, and transmission parts.
- Common workplace hazards in this industry include machine guarding deficiencies on stamping and machining equipment, exposure to metalworking fluids and coolants, noise-induced hearing loss, caught-in hazards from automated production lines, amputation risks from presses, and chemical exposure.
- Employers in motor vehicle seating and interior trim manufacturing reported 3.3 work-related injuries and illnesses per 100 full-time workers in 2024, according to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics Survey of Occupational Injuries and Illnesses.
