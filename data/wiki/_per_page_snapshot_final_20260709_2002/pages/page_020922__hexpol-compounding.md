## Facts

- HEXPOL Compounding is one of the world’s leading developers and producers of advanced Rubber Compounds, Thermoplastic Elastomer (TPE) Compounds and Thermoplastic Compounds.
- The business area has units around the globe with operations in the Americas, Europe and Asia.
- Largest end-customer segments are the automotive and engineering industries.
- Other important customer segments include the building and construction sector, transportation sector, energy sector, consumer sector, cable and wire industry, and manufacturers of medical technology.
- Product brands include HEXFLAME, HEXLIGHT, GloMold, Dryflex, Mediprene, Epseal, and RheTech.
- Polymer compounding enhances base polymers by blending them with additives and stabilizers to create tailor-made material combinations for demanding applications.
