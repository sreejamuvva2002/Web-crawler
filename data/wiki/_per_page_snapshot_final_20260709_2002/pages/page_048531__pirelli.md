# Pirelli

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2013-06-18

**Source:** [Pirelli Announces "Fly Into Summer" Angel GT Promotion - Roadracing World Magazine | Motorcycle Riding, Racing & Tech News](https://www.roadracingworld.com/news/pirelli-announces-fly-into-summer-angel-gt-promotion/)  ·  `page_048531`

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America (PTNA) services NAFTA (North American Free Trade Agreement) member countries through its Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) facility and research and development center at its Rome, Georgia headquarters.
- PTNA operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant in Silao, Mexico.
- The company manufactures, distributes and markets original equipment and replacement tires for export and domestic car/motorcycle applications (US, CA, MX) and Agro/Heavy Truck platforms (MX).
- Pirelli is the fifth largest tire manufacturer in the world in terms of sales.
- Pirelli is present in over 160 countries.
- Pirelli has 22 manufacturing sites globally.
- Pirelli employs approximately 36,000 people around the world.
- Pirelli is a leading producer of premium tires due to its commitment to R&D.
- Pirelli has successfully competed in motorsport since 1907.
- Pirelli is the exclusive supplier of the Superbike world championship and many single marque championships around the world.
- Pirelli was the sole supplier for the Formula 1 championship for the 3-year period 2011-13.
- The Angel GT tire features a new tread pattern and bi-compound technology, offering a 30 percent increase in mileage over the ST model.
- The Angel GT reduces braking distance by approximately three feet at speeds of 47 mph and boasts improved grip and handling in wet conditions.
- Pirelli partners with AIMExpo for promotional events, including VIP tours during the inaugural show in Orlando from October 16–20.
