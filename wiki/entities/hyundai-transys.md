# Hyundai Transys

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** West Point, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Hyundai Transys manufactures seating systems and powertrains and is a major supplier of Kia Motors in West Point, GA. It will also serve as a core affiliate of Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America near Savannah, GA, receiving company-specific training from Georgia Quick Start for upcoming EV production.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Provides seating and powertrain components for Hyundai electric vehicle models and receives specialized EV-related manufacturing training.

## Key facts

- Hyundai Transys makes seating systems and powertrains and is a major supplier of Kia Motors in West Point, GA.
- Quick Start provides company-specific training covering seat assembly for various Hyundai EV models, electrical motor controls, and manufacturing process overview.
- Training includes safety and organizational development courses such as Lean, 5S and workplace ethics.
- Quick Start uses its main Advanced Manufacturing Training Center in Pooler, GA and two interim facilities while the new Hyundai Mobility Training Center of Georgia is under construction adjacent to the Metaplant.
- The Metaplant is slated to start production by early 2025.
