# Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Advanced BioFuels USA – Innovative Research, Applications and Approaches Keep Freudenberg-NOK Ahead of the Competition](https://advancedbiofuelsusa.info/innovative-research-applications-and-approaches-keep-freudenberg-nok-ahead-of-the-competition/)  ·  `page_020472`

## Facts

- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies is the Americas joint venture between Freudenberg and Co. in Germany and NOK Corp. in Japan.
- The company was founded in 1989 under the legal name Freudenberg-NOK General Partnership.
- Freudenberg-NOK is headquartered in Plymouth, Mich., and operates more than 20 facilities across the Americas.
- The company's seals and gaskets play a critical role in helping OEM customers achieve optimal engine and transmission performance and are found in virtually every vehicle on the road today.
- Freudenberg-NOK completed a series of studies on how ethanol, methanol, lubricants and biodiesel fuel-oil blends impact the molecular structure and performance of various elastomeric sealing materials.
- The company collaborated with a major customer on analyzing how exhaust fuel-oil condensate affects vehicle seals.
- Freudenberg-NOK provides high temperature alloy (HTA) metal materials, originally developed for the aerospace industry, for use in its automotive Stamped Gaskets product division.
- HTA is a family of alloys made from a high nickel content metal that possesses extreme temperature and fatigue cracking resistance suited for smaller powertrains.
- Freudenberg-NOK developed Low Emission Sealing Solutions (LESS) products for automotive applications that reduce friction, improve service life, cut emissions, and are smaller and lighter than conventional components.
- The Levitex® seal reduces friction up to 90 percent over conventional crankshaft seals and may have applications in construction, agricultural and recreational vehicles.
- Material innovations associated with the LESS program include unique elastomeric formulas, nanotechnology treatments and use of nonwovens and fiber in rubber formulas.
- Freudenberg-NOK offers custom validation testing services that include the development of performance rigs and product protocols.
- The company developed a comprehensive Innovation Management System (IMS) to align its product strategies with those of its customers.
- Freudenberg-NOK's industrial portfolio spans 32 market segments from automotive and aerospace to heavy industry, food and beverage and fluid technology.
- Torsten Maschke serves as president, Automotive Sales and Marketing, Freudenberg Sealing Technologies.
- Freudenberg-NOK received the 2014 TOP Innovation Award from the Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung (F.A.Z.) Institute in Berlin.
- The company earned finalist honors in the 2014 Society of Plastics Engineers (SPE®) Automotive Division Innovation Awards.
- Freudenberg-NOK earned finalist honors in the 2014 IndustryWeek Best Plants competition.
- The company's technologies help automotive customers navigate a changing landscape focused on lower fuel consumption, CO2 emissions, alternate fuels and propulsion concepts.
