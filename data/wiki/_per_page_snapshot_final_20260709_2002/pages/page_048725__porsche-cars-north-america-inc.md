# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2014-04-15

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. is completing it's new $100 million project that began in November 2012.
- The 28-acre headquarters will be located at the northeast corner of Hartsfield-Jackson Airport.
- It will feature a technical training center.
- It will feature a classic car restoration and display area.
- It will feature restaurants.
- It will feature a Porsche Experience Center with a 1.6 mile test track.
- The new Atlanta headquarters will employ 400.
- 100 of the employees at the new Atlanta headquarters will be new to Georgia.
