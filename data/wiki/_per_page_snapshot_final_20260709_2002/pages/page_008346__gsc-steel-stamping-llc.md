## Facts

- In January 2023, GSC Steel Stamping, LLC purchased the assets of Dixien, LLC.
- GSC Steel Stamping, LLC is a minority- and family-owned business that specializes in the production of automotive parts.
- The acquisition allowed GSC Steel Stamping to continue its 43-year history of stamping automobile parts.
- The company's factory is located in Lake City, Georgia.
- The factory spans 200,000 square feet.
- The facility is the largest automobile stamping plant owned by a minority group globally.
