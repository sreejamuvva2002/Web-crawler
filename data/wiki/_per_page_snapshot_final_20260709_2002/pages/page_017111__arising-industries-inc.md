## Facts

- Began production of enclosed cargo trailers in January 2008 in Hazlehurst, Georgia.
- Has manufactured and sold over 75,000 trailer units since its inception.
- Products feature a true all-tube steel perimeter frame made with the highest quality materials coupled with exceptional fit-and-finish and quality workmanship.
- Employs 150+ individuals that devote significant time and effort to making its products.
- Operates bifurcated production lines across two physical plants to accommodate high-volume production demands without compromising quality standards.
- Focuses on value rather than price, with the ultimate goal of providing customers with the best trailer at a cost that is most economically valuable.
- Corporate mission is summarized by the Four Ps: People, Process, Product, and Price.
- Company motto and corporate mission statement is 'Just Do The Right Thing.'
