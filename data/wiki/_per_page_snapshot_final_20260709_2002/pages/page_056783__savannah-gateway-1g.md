# Savannah Gateway 1G

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-31

**Source:** [Savannah Gateway 1G sells for $34.1M in booming industrial market](https://www.jll.com/en-us/newsroom/jll-capital-markets-arranged-sale-of-savannah-gateway-1g)  ·  `page_056783`

## Facts

- The sale of Savannah Gateway 1G was announced on October 31, 2024.
- JLL Capital Markets facilitated the $34.1 million sale of 1001 Gateway Parkway, a Class A distribution center in Rincon, Georgia, also known as Savannah Gateway 1G.
- JLL represented the seller, Broe Real Estate Group, the multi-billion-dollar real estate developer of the 2,600-acre Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub.
- An affiliate of LXP Industrial Trust acquired the property.
- The 204,800-square-foot facility is fully leased to two tenants: Kyungshin America Corporation and Weida Freight.
- Kyungshin America Corporation is a tier 1 Hyundai supplier and a subsidiary of Kyungshin Corporation.
- The center features tilt-wall construction, a 32-foot clear height, an 185-foot truck court depth, 38 dock-high doors, two drive-ins, 31 stalls, 25 levelers, and 133 auto spaces.
- The property is located in the Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub, 11.9 miles from the Garden City terminal at the Port of Savannah.
- The site offers easy access to Interstate 95 and Interstate 16.
- The industrial park provides dual-rail service from CSX and Norfolk Southern.
- The upcoming Effingham Parkway is slated for completion by September 2025, which will enhance the property's accessibility.
- The facility will be positioned as the closest dual-access facility to the port in the Highway 21 Corridor upon the parkway's completion.
- JLL Investment Sales and Advisory team led the transaction with Senior Managing Directors Britton Burdette, Matt Wirth, and Dennis Mitchell, along with Senior Director Jim Freeman.
- Senior Managing Director Britton Burdette stated that the transaction highlights the growing importance of the Savannah market in the national industrial landscape.
- Burdette noted that as port activity continues to increase, well-positioned facilities like 1001 Gateway Parkway are becoming increasingly valuable to investors and tenants seeking to optimize supply chain operations.
