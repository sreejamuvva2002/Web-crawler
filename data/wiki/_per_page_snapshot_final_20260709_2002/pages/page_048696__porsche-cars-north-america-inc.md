# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2020-05-19

**Source:** [Track My Porsche As Its Being Built - Images From Porsche Production Stirs Tingles Of Anticipation In Buyer](https://www.theautochannel.com/news/2020/05/19/815041-track-my-porsche.html)  ·  `page_048696`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) is headquartered at One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA.
- PCNA was established in 1984 and has been headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia since 1998.
- PCNA serves as the exclusive U.S. importer for the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera, and Taycan.
- PCNA supports 192 independently owned and operated Porsche dealerships across the U.S.
- PCNA supplies parts, service, marketing, and training to its supported dealerships.
- PCNA operates as an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Porsche Digital, Inc. maintains an Atlanta, Georgia office co-located with the PCNA headquarters.
- The Porsche Digital Atlanta office develops the "My Porsche" sports car owners site, a central e-commerce platform, and digital services for wholesale and retail operations.
- Klaus Zellmer holds the title of President and CEO at Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA).
- Christian Friedl is the Head of the Porsche factory in Zuffenhausen.
- Robert Ader serves as Vice President Customer Relations at Porsche.
- The "My Porsche" platform functions as a centralized customer portal bundling offerings and services for individual cars and is available as a mobile app.
- Porsche plans to enroll its all-electric Taycan model in the "Porsche Track Your Dream" tracking service in the future.
- The "Porsche Track Your Dream" digital service allows customers to track their vehicle through 14 milestone events, including order creation, freeze point for changes, production updates, vessel departure from Germany, port entry into the U.S., and dealership arrival.
- Initial rollout of the tracking service targets new car customers in the USA, Germany, Great Britain, Canada, Switzerland, and Spain, with additional markets to follow in coming months.
- PCNA operates the first Porsche Experience Center in North America in Atlanta, featuring a 1.6-mile driver development track, a business center, and Restaurant 356.
- Porsche Digital GmbH was founded in 2016 as a subsidiary of Porsche AG to focus on innovation, digital product development, and venture investments.
- Porsche Digital's global headquarters are located in Ludwigsburg, Germany, with additional offices in Berlin, Tel Aviv, and Shanghai.
