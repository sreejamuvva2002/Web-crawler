## Facts

- Candler County is getting a hefty financial boost thanks to the Hyundai Metaplant in Bryan County.
- Gov. Brian Kemp announced that DAS Corp will create 300 automotive jobs in a facility in the Candler County Industrial Park.
- DAS Corp will make an investment in the county’s economy of more than $35 million.
- DAS Corp specializes in designing and manufacturing seating systems, safety seating components, and seating structures.
- The producer is a major supplier to both the Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia and Hyundai Motor Group.
- Sean Kim, chief Operating Officer at Das, said the Candler County location was selected because of its proximity to Savannah and Macon.
- Sean Kim noted that Metter was an ideal location in anticipation of its role as a global electric vehicle production hub.
- Operations at the Candler County site are projected to begin in the latter half of 2024.
- DAS will be recruiting assembly technicians using online outlets as well as local employment services.
