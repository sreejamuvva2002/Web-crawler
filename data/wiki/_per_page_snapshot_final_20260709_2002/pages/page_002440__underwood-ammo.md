# Underwood Ammo

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-01

**Source:** [Underwood Ammo to Create 120 New Manufacturing Jobs | Georgia](https://georgia.org/press-releases/2025/underwood-ammo-create-120-new-manufacturing-jobs)  ·  `page_002440`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Underwood Ammo will invest $41 million in building a new manufacturing facility.
- The investment will create 120 new jobs.
- The facility is located in Effingham County.
- Georgia is described as the nation’s premier destination for manufacturers, including the defense and sporting sectors.
- Underwood Ammo produces handgun and rifle ammunition for hunting and self-defense.
- Underwood Ammo is based in the United States.
- Kevin Underwood serves as President and Founder of Underwood Ammo.
- The new manufacturing and assembly facility will be located at the Savannah Portside International Park.
- Construction on the facility is expected to begin in 2026.
- Hiring for positions in operations, maintenance, and assembly is expected to begin in 2027.
- Forrest Floyd serves as District One Commissioner on the Effingham County Board of Commissioners.
- Matt Saxon serves as Chairman of the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority.
- The project received support from the Georgia Department of Economic Development.
- The project received support from Electric Cities of Georgia.
- Connor Hall represents the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s Global Commerce team as Project Manager for this competitive project.
- The project was developed in partnership with Effingham County Industrial Development Authority, Georgia Quick Start, and Electric Cities of Georgia.
- Pat Wilson serves as Commissioner of the Georgia Department of Economic Development.
- Underwood Ammo is a U.S.-based manufacturer focused on the design, production, and distribution of precision-engineered products.
- The company continues to expand its U.S. operations footprint with the addition of its Georgia-based facility.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated, “Georgia is the nation’s premier destination for manufacturers, including the defense and sporting sectors.”
- Kevin Underwood stated that today marks an important milestone in Underwood Ammo's history and that the investment positions the company for long-term success.
- Forrest Floyd stated that Underwood Ammo's investment will create quality jobs and contribute to Effingham County's continued growth.
- Matt Saxon stated that Underwood’s decision adds to the strength of the industrial community and creates diversity in available jobs.
- Pat Wilson stated that the investment underscores confidence in Georgia’s skilled workforce, dependable infrastructure, and supportive business environment.
