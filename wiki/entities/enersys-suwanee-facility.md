# EnerSys Suwanee Facility

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[power-electronics|Power Electronics]]  ·  **Location:** Suwanee, GA, USA, Gwinnett County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

The EnerSys facility in Suwanee, Georgia manufactures industrial-grade battery systems, chargers and uninterruptible power supplies for power generation facilities, substations, utilities, transportation systems, communication networks and other enterprises with unique powering needs.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Produces large-scale battery systems used in transportation systems, supporting electric vehicle and related infrastructure needs.

## Timeline

- **2001** — Facility established

## Related organizations & people

- International Organization for Standardization (ISO) — Certification body. Facility holds ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 14001:2015 certifications.

## Key facts

- Facility size is 150,000 sq ft.
- Employs approximately 200 people.
- Manufactures large-scale battery systems, chargers and uninterruptible power supplies for a wide range of critical applications.
- Certified to ISO 9001:2015 (quality management) and ISO 14001:2015 (environmental management).
- Established in 2001.
