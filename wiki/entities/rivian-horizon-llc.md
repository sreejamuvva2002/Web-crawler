# Rivian Horizon, LLC

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Morgan County, Newton County, City of Social Circle, Georgia, Morgan, Newton, Walton County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Rivian Horizon, LLC entered into an Economic Development Agreement with the State of Georgia, the Georgia Department of Economic Development and a joint development authority to locate a $5 billion electric-vehicle manufacturing plant in Georgia, creating 7,500 full-time jobs by 2028.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Establishing a large-scale electric-vehicle manufacturing plant in Georgia.

## Key facts

- Rivian Horizon, LLC will locate a new electric-vehicle manufacturing plant on approximately 1,978 acres across 44 parcels near I-20 and US-278.
- The project is funded with a $5 billion capital investment commitment.
- The company has pledged to create 7,500 full-time jobs with an average annual wage of $56,000.
- Public parties will provide the Facility Site on a long-term basis via a Rental Agreement.
- Workforce training facilities and programs, including a Quick Start Training Program, are included in the agreement.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development has allocated $111,307,760 from the Department of Community Affairs for project costs.
- A commitment letter from the Georgia Department of Transportation supports public roadway improvements for the site.
- Project Bonds (Rivian Horizon, LLC Project) may be issued up to $15 billion in taxable revenue bonds.
- The Joint Development Authority represents Jasper, Morgan, Newton and Walton counties as a single public authority.
