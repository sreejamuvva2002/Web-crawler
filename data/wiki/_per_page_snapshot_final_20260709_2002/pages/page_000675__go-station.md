# Go-Station

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-01

**Source:** [Go-Station to Present at Georgia's Clean Energy Roadshow](https://www.einpresswire.com/article/723828424/go-station-to-present-at-georgia-s-clean-energy-roadshow)  ·  `page_000675`

## Facts

- Go-Station is presenting at the 2024 Clean Energy Roadshow on July 11th and 12th.
- The event will be held in Cartersville at the Savoy Automobile Museum and in Alpharetta on the North Fulton campus of Gwinnett Technical College, GA.
- Mike Anderson, Go-Station's Chief Operating Officer, will present information on Georgia's electrification transition efforts and how Go-Station is supporting a sustainable future for the state.
- The Clean Energy Roadshow was founded by Georgia Public Service Commissioner Tim Echols in 2010 as the Alternative Fuel Vehicle Roadshow™.
- The event was originally conceived as a round table of experts to discuss integrating new alternative fuel technologies for governments, workplaces, and enterprise fleets.
- The Roadshow has evolved into an annual, statewide series covering benefits, expanding options, and funding programs for zero and near-zero emission vehicles.
- Each event includes a showcase and demonstration of the latest technology vehicles.
- Georgia Public Service Commissioner Tim Echols stated that Go-Station continues to play a critical role in Georgia's transition to alternative energy and their sustainable future.
- Go-Station is an EV charging company dedicated to building confidence in owning and driving electric vehicles.
- The company serves drivers, multi-family developments, workplaces, parking facilities, healthcare, businesses, and fleet operators seeking to go electric.
- Go-Station offers a full range of charging solutions, including turn-key deployments.
