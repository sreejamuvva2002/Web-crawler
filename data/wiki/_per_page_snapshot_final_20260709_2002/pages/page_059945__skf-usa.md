# SKF USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-10-25

**Source:** [SKF USA to Close Factory, Eliminate 170 Jobs | Industrial Equipment News](https://www.ien.com/operations/news/20980545/skf-usa-to-close-factory-eliminate-170-jobs)  ·  `page_059945`

## Facts

- SKF USA plans to close its plant in Seneca during the next 18 months.
- The closure will eliminate 170 jobs in the small town north of Topeka.
- The plant is one of the main employers in Seneca, which has a population of about 2,000 people.
- SKF USA is owned by Sweden-based SKF Group.
- The company stated it is consolidating its industrial seals manufacturing work.
- The work being done in Seneca will be transferred to a plant in Salt Lake City.
- Topeka Mayor Joe Mitchell called the decision a "huge blow" to the city.
- Mayor Mitchell said he's hopeful the town will find another manufacturer to replace SKF.
