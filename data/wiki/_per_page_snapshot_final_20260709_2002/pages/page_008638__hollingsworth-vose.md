# Hollingsworth & Vose

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-01-20

**Source:** [More than 200 years in, Hollingsworth & Vose continues its pursuit of a ‘cleaner, healthier, more sustainable world’ - International Filtration News](https://www.filtnews.com/more-than-200-years-in-hollingsworth-vose-continues-its-pursuit-of-a-cleaner-healthier-more-sustainable-world/)  ·  `page_008638`

## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose is a global manufacturer of advanced materials used in filtration, battery and industrial applications.
- The company operates manufacturing and research & development facilities in the Americas, Europe, China and India.
- H&V's battery separators went to support medical equipment and contribute to backup power sources for emergency vehicles, hospitals and other facilities.
- H&V’s materials can be found in batteries used mainly in telecom and hybrid vehicle applications.
- H&V’s materials can be found in filters for cars, trucks and heavy-duty equipment.
- Hollingsworth & Vose became a leading supplier of automotive start-stop battery separator material just a few years ago.
- Hollingsworth & Vose recently announced several distribution partnerships, with I.W. Tremont and Superior Felt & Filtration, to expand the reach of the Trupor microporous filter media both in the U.S. and globally.
- Trupor enables enhanced flow compared to traditional membranes, which improves the ability to filter novel liquid filter streams.
- H&V is developing new filtration solutions that provide better air quality with lower energy consumption.
- Filtration will continue to play a huge role as we continue to move toward more efficient cars, hybrids and EVs.
- Josh Ayer serves as Hollingsworth & Vose’s president and chief operating officer.
- H&V operates 13 R&D and manufacturing facilities around the world.
- The company is family-owned for seven generations, with origins dating back to 1728.
- Hollingsworth & Vose is headquartered in East Walpole, Massachusetts USA.
