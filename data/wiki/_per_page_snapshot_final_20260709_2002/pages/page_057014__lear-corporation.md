# Lear Corporation

## Facts

- Lear Corporation held its Q1 earnings call to report on operational performance and strategic achievements.
- The E-Systems segment secured new business worth over $750 million in annual sales, marking the highest quarterly achievement in over a decade.
- The E-Systems segment's new business wins include key partnerships with automotive companies Ford and BMW.
- Lear delivered $5.6 billion in revenue and improved operating margins of 4.9% despite a tough production environment.
- Adjusted earnings per share stood at $3.12 during the quarter.
- Operational improvements saved $11 million in the first quarter.
- Restructuring efforts saved an additional $12 million in the first quarter.
- Lear reduced its global hourly headcount by 3,600 employees to contribute to margin improvements.
- The Seating segment secured two new ComfortFlex programs with major Chinese automakers.
- The Seating segment also secured a Global Seat program with major Chinese automakers.
- Lear received a PACE Award from Automotive News for its zone control module.
- The award recognizes the company's advancements in scalable and flexible wire harness designs.
- International trade negotiations are creating significant uncertainty that is affecting production volumes and product mix.
- Uncertainty from international trade negotiations led Lear to withdraw its full-year guidance.
- Tariffs, particularly from Honduras, are imposing gross tariff costs estimated at $200 million on the company.
- Sales in both the Seating and E-Systems segments declined by 7% compared to the previous year.
- The sales decline was primarily due to lower volumes on Lear platforms and the phase-out of discontinued product lines.
- Customer production plans remain uncertain due to tariffs and broader macroeconomic conditions.
- Core operating earnings were reported at $270 million during the earnings call.
- Lear achieved historic levels of positive net performance, contributing significantly to margins in both the Seating and E-Systems segments.
- The company emphasized its strategic business wins and proactive measures to address global trade dynamics during the call.
