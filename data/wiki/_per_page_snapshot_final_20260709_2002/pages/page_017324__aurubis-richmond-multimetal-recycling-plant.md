# Aurubis Richmond Multimetal Recycling Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Aurubis and SMS group forge ahead with the first multimetal recycling plant in the US - SMS group GmbH](https://www.sms-group.com/en-ae/press-and-media/press-releases/press-release-detail/aurubis-and-sms-group-forge-ahead-with-the-first-multimetal-recycling-plant-in-the-us)  ·  `page_017324`

## Facts

- Aurubis AG and SMS group GmbH signed a supply contract for a second plant module at the new Aurubis Richmond recycling plant in Georgia as part of their strategic partnership.
- The modular system design allows for flexible operations and quick growth in the dynamic US recycling market.
- Aurubis is considerably accelerating its growth course in the future recycling and circular economy market.
- The additional equipment will increase the capacity of the metal recycling plant in Richmond from an annual 90,000 tons to 180,000 tons of complex metal scrap materials per year.
- Aurubis CEO Roland Harings stated that the expansion stage delivers on the company’s “Driving Sustainable Growth” strategy while keeping strategically important and high-demand mineral raw materials in the resource cycle.
- SMS group recycling equipment will be installed in the second module of the new US plant.
- The core technology is the top-blown rotary converter (TBRC), a state-of-the-art system for processing complex recycling materials.
- The TBRC enables recovery of raw materials like copper, nickel, tin, precious metals, and platinum group metals in additional downstream stages.
- Recovered metals are crucial raw materials for future technologies including the energy transition, sustainable mobility, and digitalization.
- SMS Executive Board member Michael Rzepczyk stated that Aurubis is assuming a leading role in multimetal recycling in the US with the planned doubling of production volume at the new site.
- Michael Rzepczyk added that the partnership closes value chains and maximizes recovery of valuable metals from recycling materials to build a more sustainable future.
- Additional components can be added to the Aurubis Richmond plant to expand capacity precisely to meet future requirements.
- The modular system allows Aurubis to plan a prudent market strategy and respond flexibly to US market needs.
- Aurubis is investing about €640 million for both phases of the project to build the first secondary smelter specializing in multimetal recycling in the US.
- Groundbreaking for the Aurubis Richmond plant took place in mid-2022, with construction running at full steam.
- Stage one of the plant is scheduled to go online in the first half of 2024.
- The TBRC from the signed contract is destined for the second stage, which is anticipated to start production in 2026.
- Aurubis processes roughly 1 million tons of recycling material annually as a leader in multimetal recycling.
- The Aurubis Richmond plant will add another 180,000 tons of annual processing capacity.
- The Georgia investment will comply with the highest environmental standards in the US and create around 200 new jobs.
- Aurubis AG is a leading global provider of non-ferrous metals and one of the largest copper recyclers worldwide.
- The company processes complex metal concentrates, scrap metals, organic and inorganic metal-bearing recycling materials, and industrial residues into high-quality metals.
- Aurubis produces more than 1 million tons of copper cathodes annually, along with wire rod, continuous cast shapes, profiles, and flat rolled products made of copper and copper alloys.
- Aurubis also produces precious metals, selenium, lead, nickel, tin, and zinc.
- The product portfolio includes sulfuric acid and iron silicate.
- Aurubis has around 6,900 employees and production sites in Europe and the USA.
- SMS group generates worldwide sales of around €2.6 billion and employs 14,500 people.
- Aurubis shares are listed on the Prime Standard Segment of the German Stock Exchange, the MDAX, the Global Challenges Index (GCX), and the STOXX Europe 600.
