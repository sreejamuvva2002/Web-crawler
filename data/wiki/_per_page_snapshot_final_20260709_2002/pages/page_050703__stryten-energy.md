# Stryten Energy

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-03-24

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Introduces Expanded Line of GNB Industrial Power Branded Solutions at MODEX 2022](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/stryten-energy-introduces-expanded-line-125800626.html)  ·  `page_050703`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- The company showcased an extended line of GNB Industrial Power branded energy storage solutions and tools for the material handling industry at MODEX 2022.
- Stryten Energy's advanced lead and lithium batteries are combined with intelligent chargers and energy performance management software to provide end-to-end solutions for sustainable energy models.
- At MODEX, Stryten Energy unveiled the GNB Fury X-7 charger designed for 208-240 line voltages to support areas with lower power infrastructure, such as those with many historical buildings in the Northeast U.S., Florida, Mexico, and South America.
- The new GNB Fury X-7 chargers for 208VAC-240VAC 3-phase power are available now and feature the same capabilities as the 480-volt models.
- Stryten Energy's booth at MODEX featured the LiFTFORCE LPX 2.0 lithium solution, which delivers faster charging, greater efficiency, increased cycle life, and lower operational costs compared to traditional power sources.
- The booth also featured the Tubular LMX advanced lead solution, which allows up to 90 days between watering intervals to increase uptime and reduce maintenance costs.
- Stryten Energy showcased Fury X-7 chargers in 2-bay (7-15 kW) and 4-bay (7-30 kW) configurations that can be set up for standard, opportunity, or fast charging.
- The Fury X-7 chargers include built-in Bluetooth and Wi-Fi for integration with GNB Cloud software.
- GNB Cloud is an IoT-enabled decision support software suite designed to help users design and deploy web-connected battery and charger solutions.
- Bret Aker, Vice President of Software Solutions, and Andrea Vasquez, Fleet Energy Data Specialist, presented at a MODEX seminar on Monday, March 28 from 12 p.m. to 12:45 p.m. in Theater F titled "You Have 10,000 Batteries Talking to the Web… What Now? Discover How Advanced IoT Software Can Make Your Life Easier."
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- The company's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
