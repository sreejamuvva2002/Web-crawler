# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America (YMMC)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-07-27

**Source:** [Yamaha Motor USA Headlines Season Premiere of “Modern Marvels: Machines” on The HISTORY® Channel](https://au.finance.yahoo.com/news/yamaha-motor-usa-headlines-season-160000865.html)  ·  `page_015660`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America (YMMC) is a state-of-the-art U.S.-based manufacturing facility located in Newnan, Georgia.
- The YMMC campus designs and manufactures four recreational product lines for worldwide distribution: All-Terrain Vehicles (ATV), Side-by-Sides (SxS), Personal Watercraft, and Golf Cars.
- Since its founding in 1986, Yamaha has produced nearly 4 million vehicles at the YMMC facility.
- The facility creates thousands of jobs across facilities in 11 U.S. states, including nearly 2,000 employees at the YMMC facility alone.
- At YMMC, all Side-by-Side vehicles, full-size ATVs, and WaveRunners are manufactured for global distribution.
- Production capacity at the facility is highlighted by an employee stating that every sixty seconds an ATV or a Golf Car rolls off the line.
- The YMMC factory holds the highest certifications for quality, safety, and environmental manufacturing, specifically ISO9001 Quality Management System and ISO14001 Environmental Management System certifications.
- Bob Brown, Vice President of Finance and Operations Support at Yamaha, stated that the company wanted to highlight vehicles built with pride on the line and the dedication and craftsmanship of the workforce.
- Steve Nessl, Yamaha's Motorsports marketing manager, noted that everyone at Yamaha takes great pride in the U.S. production of their products and values the recognition of the Georgia factory workers' expertise and enthusiasm.
- The facility was featured on the HISTORY Channel's season premiere of 'Modern Marvels: Machines,' which aired on Sunday, July 25th, with encore viewings available on History.com.
- Yamaha Motor Corporation, USA (YMUS) maintains a corporate office in California, two corporate offices in Georgia, facilities in Wisconsin and Alabama, and factory operations in Tennessee and Georgia.
- YMUS additional U.S.-based subsidiaries include Yamaha Marine Systems Company (with Bennett Marine in Florida and Kracor Systems in Wisconsin), Skeeter Boats (with division G3 Boats in Missouri), and Yamaha Precision Propeller (in Indiana).
- ATV models over 90cc are recommended for use only by riders 16 years and older.
- SxS vehicles are recommended for use only by licensed drivers 16 years and older.
