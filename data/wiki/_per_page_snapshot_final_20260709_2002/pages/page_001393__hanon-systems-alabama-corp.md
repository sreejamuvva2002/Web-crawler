# Hanon Systems Alabama, Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2024-09-19

**Source:** [Sale and acquisition financing arranged for Hanon Systems Build-to-Suit industrial property](https://www.jll.com/en-us/newsroom/sale-and-financing-arranged-for-hanon-systems-build-to-suit)  ·  `page_001393`

## Facts

- JLL Capital Markets led the sale and financing efforts for a mission-critical operation with potential for expansion.
- Sale and acquisition financing was arranged for a Hanon Systems build-to-suit industrial property located at 8540 Zell Miller Road in Statesboro, Georgia.
- The facility is described as a fully occupied Class A light manufacturing and distribution building.
- Statesboro serves as an extension of the established Savannah, GA industrial market.
- JLL represented the seller, which is a partnership between Graham Capital and Chesterfield.
- Spaulding & Slye Investments acquired the property.
- JLL arranged the acquisition financing with Seacoast Bank on behalf of the new owner.
- The facility is strategically located 1.1 miles from Highway 301.
- It is situated 7.2 miles from I-16 and 34.9 miles from the Hyundai Metaplant.
- The location positions the facility to serve both the Hyundai Metaplant and the broader Savannah market.
- The property spans 112,011 square feet.
- The institutionally designed facility features a 32-foot clear height.
- The building includes multi-sided loading, 11 dock-high doors, and two drive-in doors.
- The facility contains 14,880 square feet of office space.
- The property is 100% leased to Hanon Systems Alabama, Corp., a subsidiary of Hanon Systems.
- Hanon Systems operates as a full-line thermal and energy management solutions provider.
- The build-to-suit project was completed in April 2024.
- The facility was designed for assembling HVAC products, radiators, condensers, heat exchangers, and battery heating and cooling products for automobiles.
- The facility primarily serves the nearby Hyundai Metaplant.
- The lease includes two expansion options for the tenant: a first option of 68,000 square feet and a second option of 145,600 square feet.
- JLL Investment Sales and Advisory team was led by Directors Jim Freeman and Anthony Walters.
- Senior Managing Directors on the JLL Investment Sales and Advisory team include Britton Burdette, Dennis Mitchell, and Matt Wirth.
- Associate Bo Osgood served on the JLL Investment Sales and Advisory team.
- JLL Debt Advisory team was spearheaded by Senior Director Bobby Norwood.
- Senior Managing Director Mark Sixour also led the JLL Debt Advisory team.
- Jim Freeman stated that Savannah's industrial market is experiencing exceptional growth driven by record-high cargo volumes at the Port and new operations entering to supply the Hyundai Metaplant.
- The Savannah market has seen strong fundamentals and rental rate growth over the past two years.
- The market boasts one of the highest levels of tracked tenant demand as a percentage of inventory in the country.
- Graham Capital is a real estate investment company based in Birmingham, Alabama, focused on distribution centers in high-growth Southeastern industrial markets.
- Chesterfield specializes in developing class A industrial, manufacturing, and office projects throughout the southeastern United States.
- Spaulding & Slye Investments was founded in Boston in 1966 and manages approximately 4.5 million SF of development and operating assets.
- Seacoast Banking Corporation of Florida is one of the largest community banks headquartered in Florida, with approximately $14.8 billion in assets and $12 billion in deposits as of March 31, 2024.
