# Hyundai Mobis

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-10-27

## Facts

- Hyundai Mobis announced plans to add an EV battery plant to its Montgomery campus.
- The announcement creates 400 new jobs for Montgomery’s workforce.
- The MOBIS module division was founded in 2002.
- The MOBIS module division sits on 83 acres.
- Hyundai Mobis is a Tier One supplier to Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama and KIA Motor Manufacturing Georgia.
- The company holds ISO14001 Certification for Environmental Management.
- The company holds IATF16949 Certification for Quality Management.
- The new EV battery plant is scheduled to open in 2024.
- Construction for the new plant is slated to start in December 2022.
- Alabama Governor Kay Ivey stated that Alabama’s auto manufacturing sector is evolving rapidly to capitalize on the EV revolution sweeping the industry.
- Governor Kay Ivey noted that the new Hyundai Mobis battery plant represents another milestone in that transition.
- The 400 jobs will not be available to fill until 2024.
- Other opportunities at Hyundai Mobis are currently listed on the company's job board.
