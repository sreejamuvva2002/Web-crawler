# Pirelli

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-05

**Source:** [PIRELLI WILL START CYBE TYRE PRODUCTION IN GEORGIA UNDERLINING THE STRATEGIC IMPORTANCE OF THE U.S. MARKET](https://www.theautochannel.com/news/2026/05/05/1665183-pirelli-will-start-cyber-tyre-production-georgia-underlining-strategic-importance.html)  ·  `page_048468`

## Facts

- Pirelli will start production of Cyber Tyre at its Rome, Georgia plant.
- Investment and output expansion details for the project will be communicated in the coming months following the finalization of development plans.
- The Georgia plant is already dedicated to producing technologically advanced products for the U.S. market, including the High-Value segment and Motorsport.
- The production start highlights the strategic importance of the U.S. market for Pirelli's global growth roadmap.
- The announcement coincides with Pirelli's participation in the SelectUSA Investment Summit, a flagship event promoted by the U.S. Department of Commerce.
- Cyber Tyre is being showcased at the summit as a core innovation shaping the future of smart mobility.
- Cyber Tyre is the world's first hardware-and-software system capable of collecting data and information from sensors embedded in tires.
- The system processes sensor data through Pirelli's proprietary software and algorithms.
- It communicates in real time with a vehicle's electronics to enable new functionalities integrated with driving and control systems.
- These functionalities enhance the driving experience, increase safety levels, and support connected infrastructure.
- At the SelectUSA summit, Cyber Tyre was showcased at the Georgia State booth.
- Pirelli has been established in Georgia for more than 20 years.
- Claudio Zanardo, CEO of Pirelli North America, stated that the start of Cyber Tyre production in Rome, Georgia is a significant milestone reflecting the company's commitment to bringing advanced technologies closer to the market and strengthening its industrial footprint and innovation capabilities in the United States.
- Pirelli is finalizing the introduction of the latest version of the MIRS (Modular Integrated Robotized System) production process at the Rome plant.
- The MIRS process will be the most advanced manufacturing process for high-end, premium production within the entire Pirelli Group and is exclusive to the Georgia factory.
- MIRS enhances robotized production capabilities, increasing productivity, flexibility, and quality.
- The system is highly digitalized and enables a direct link between product design and its industrial application.
- This development lays the groundwork for growth in Pirelli's production capacity in Rome as part of the Group's industrial development plans.
- The Rome, Georgia plant specializes in high-value tire production for the North American market.
- The facility hosts a dedicated R&D center to strengthen its role in technological development and product innovation.
- The facility is recognized for responsible sourcing practices, including the use of FSC®-certified natural rubber.
