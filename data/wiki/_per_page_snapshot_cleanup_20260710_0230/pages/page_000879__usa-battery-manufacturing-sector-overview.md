## Facts

- Battery manufacturers in the USA currently employ 34,891 workers across 292 companies, generating combined sales of $52 billion.
- The domestic battery manufacturing sector demonstrates concentrated regional production, with 32% of operations located in the South.
- Electric vehicle market projections indicate sales increases exceeding 35% in 2023, creating expanded demand for domestic battery production capabilities.
- The U.S. represents approximately 6% of global battery manufacturing capacity, indicating significant potential for domestic production expansion.

- The company has 7,995 employees based in Pennsylvania facilities.
