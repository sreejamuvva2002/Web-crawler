## Key facts

- Greentec Recycling offers hybrid and EV battery recycling services by appointment throughout Georgia.
- The company recovers valuable materials such as nickel-metal hydride, lithium, and other metals from spent batteries, reducing the need for virgin resources.
- Recycling processes are designed to lower greenhouse gas emissions and combat climate change.
- Greentec provides fair compensation to businesses and individuals for their spent battery packs.
- The Georgia facility handles all types of hybrid and EV batteries, including those from plug-in hybrids, fully electric vehicles, and large-format industrial storage systems.
- Service coverage includes major cities and towns across the state, ensuring local access to responsible battery disposal.
- Greentec’s nationwide network supports local communities while maintaining compliance with vehicle battery regulations.
