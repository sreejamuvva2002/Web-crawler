# Great Dane

## Facts

- Great Dane was recognized by the Georgia General Assembly in commemoration of the company’s 125th anniversary.
- Proclamations honoring Great Dane were read in both the House and Senate chambers to mark the milestone.
- Governor Brian Kemp issued a special commendation to the company.
- The recognition highlights Great Dane’s enduring history of innovation, quality, and leadership in the transportation industry.
- The House proclamation was sponsored by State Rep. Ron Stephens from Savannah, GA.
- Great Dane was founded in Savannah, GA in 1900.
- Rick Mullininx, Great Dane’s President and COO, accepted the House proclamation on behalf of the company during the House session.
- Rick Mullininx stated that the commendations are a testament to the hard work, dedication, and innovation of the team throughout each decade.
- Rick Mullininx expressed pride in the company's story intertwining with America’s story of ingenuity and looked forward to continuing to lead the industry for generations.
- The Senate proclamation was sponsored by State Sen. Ben Watson from Savannah.
- Chris Hammond, Great Dane’s Executive Vice President of Sales, accepted the Senate proclamation during the Senate session.
- Chris Hammond stated that Great Dane’s 125 years of success reflects the resilience of its teams and the trust built with customers and partners across generations and decades.
- Chris Hammond noted that the recognition underscores the company's focus on the future, enabled by technology and customer relationships.
