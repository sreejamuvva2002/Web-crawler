# TI Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-15

**Source:** [ABC Technologies Acquires TI Fluid Systems in Auburn Hills to Create TI Automotive - DBusiness Magazine](https://www.dbusiness.com/daily-news/abc-technologies-acquires-ti-fluid-systems-in-auburn-hills-to-create-ti-automotive/)  ·  `page_011381`

## Facts

- ABC Technologies in Toronto completed its acquisition of TI Fluid Systems (TIFS) in Auburn Hills.
- The transaction has an enterprise value of just over $2 billion.
- The combined business will be rebranded as TI Automotive.
- The rebranding reflects both companies’ shared focus on world-class engineering, people, and customer service.
- The acquisition brings together two complementary businesses to create a stronger, more diversified Tier 1 manufacturer.
- TI Automotive will supply market-leading safety and performance critical products and highly engineered components.
- The business is positioned to support a diverse range of customers with its propulsion agnostic portfolio.
- Combined revenue for the new entity will be $5.4 billion.
- TI Automotive will have 34,600 employees in 26 countries.
- TI Automotive will have the global scale and local reach needed to serve the automotive industry.
- TI Automotive will be led by ABC President and CEO Terry Campbell.
- An executive leadership team combining the skills and expertise of both ABC and TIFS will support Campbell.
- TI Automotive will be headquartered in Auburn Hills.
- The company will be managed through a regional operating model to ensure customer proximity and agility to respond rapidly to local market dynamics.
- ABC President and CEO Terry Campbell stated the business has exceptional people, differentiated products, a deep commitment to safety, and a relentless focus on delivering for customers.
- Campbell noted the combination creates opportunities to drive innovation and manufacturing excellence while establishing foundations for future growth.
