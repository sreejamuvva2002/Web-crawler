# Textron, Inc.

## Facts

- Textron, Inc. is a multi-industry company that leverages its global network of aircraft, defense, industrial, and finance businesses to provide customers with innovative solutions and services.
- The company operates under brands such as Bell, Cessna, Beechcraft, Pipistrel, Jacobsen, Kautex, Lycoming, E-Z-GO, Arctic Cat, and Textron Systems.
- Textron started as a small New England business in 1923 and has grown into a $12.9 billion company with 34,000 employees.
- The company serves customers in industries including aerospace and defense, specialized vehicles, turf care, and automotive systems.
- Product development pipelines include the evolution of aircraft, rotorcraft, armored vehicles, electrical vehicles, and automotive systems.
- As part of its Archive 2025 initiative, Textron established a new set of 5-year sustainability goals focused on greenhouse gas emissions, energy use, water use, and waste generation.
- Textron aims to reduce greenhouse gas emission intensity by 20% by the end of 2025 compared to its 2019 baseline year.
- Textron aims to reduce energy use intensity by 10% by the end of 2025 compared to its 2019 baseline year.
- Textron aims to reduce water use intensity by 10% by the end of 2025 compared to its 2019 baseline year.
- Textron aims to reduce waste generation intensity by 10% by the end of 2025 compared to its 2019 baseline year.
- Textron is classified under NAICS code 336, Transportation Equipment Manufacturing.
- Textron pledged to the Better Plants Challenge in December 2019.
