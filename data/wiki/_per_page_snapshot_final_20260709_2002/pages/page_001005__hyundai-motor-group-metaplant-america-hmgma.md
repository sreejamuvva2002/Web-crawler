# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-27

**Source:** [Hyundai Celebrates Grand Opening of Metaplant America - Autobody News](https://www.autobodynews.com/news/hyundai-celebrates-grand-opening-of-metaplant-america)  ·  `page_001005`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group celebrated the grand opening of its Metaplant America (HMGMA) in Ellabell, GA.
- The event marked the completion of the largest economic development project in Georgia’s history.
- The project's groundbreaking occurred in 2022.
- Hyundai’s supplier network has announced more than $2.5 billion in capital investment across 12 Georgia counties.
- This supplier investment is more than double the initially projected $1 billion.
- The supplier investments are bringing approximately 6,900 new jobs to the region.
- Euisun Chung, executive chair of Hyundai Motor Group, stated that the Metaplant America represents the group’s advanced manufacturing capabilities and commitment to innovation, as well as investment in relationships with partners and communities in Georgia.
- HMGMA is expected to play a central role in Hyundai’s long-term electrification strategy.
- The plant began production in October 2024.
- Initial production vehicles include the Hyundai IONIQ 5 and later the IONIQ 9.
- The plant is equipped to manufacture both electric and hybrid vehicles for Hyundai, Genesis, and Kia.
- Hyundai plans to add a Kia model to the production line by 2026.
- Hyundai plans to increase annual production capacity at HMGMA from 300,000 to 500,000 units.
- Hyundai and its joint ventures with LG Energy Solution and SK On have committed $12.6 billion to the HMGMA project.
- According to the Center for Automotive Research, these investments are projected to generate nearly 40,000 direct and indirect jobs across Georgia.
- These investments are projected to generate $4.6 billion in annual individual earnings across Georgia.
- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp stated that the Metaplant America is a once-in-a-generation opportunity.
- Gov. Kemp noted that collaborations between HMGMA and Georgia’s universities and technical colleges are preparing Georgians for high-quality jobs of today and tomorrow.
- Hyundai announced plans to invest $21 billion in the U.S. from 2025 to 2028.
- $9 billion of that investment is aimed at scaling domestic production capacity to 1.2 million vehicles annually.
- To date, Hyundai has invested over $20.5 billion in the U.S.
- This investment has contributed to the creation or support of more than 570,000 jobs nationwide.
