# Georgia Automotive Manufacturing Sector

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Georgia has emerged as a significant hub for automotive manufacturing, housing a mix of traditional and innovative companies.
- Firms in the state are involved in the production of vehicles, parts, and electric components.
- Advancements in technology drive efficiency, while automation reshapes workforce dynamics.
- Georgia's automotive sector is well-equipped to handle a shift towards sustainability.
- The state continues to invest heavily in fostering workforce skills pertinent to the evolving automotive landscape.
- Most companies highlighted were founded before the 2000s, indicating stable growth and commitment to innovation.
- Many operations focus on electric vehicle components, reflecting a transition toward greener alternatives.
- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc. (KMMG) is an automotive manufacturer located in West Point, Georgia.
- KMMG represents Kia's first manufacturing site in North America.
- The facility spans 2,200 acres and has an annual production capacity of 340,000 vehicles.
- KMMG began mass production on November 16, 2009.
- It has produced over 4.2 million vehicles since starting mass production.
- The plant assembles the Telluride SUV, Sorento SUV, Sportage SUV, and K5 mid-size sedan.
- KMMG recently started production of the all-electric EV9.
- The company has created thousands of jobs and contributes to community development through outreach initiatives.
- Kia Georgia, Inc. is an automotive manufacturer located in West Point, Georgia.
- Kia Georgia serves as Kia Corporation's first manufacturing site in North America.
- The facility began mass production in 2009 and has produced over 4.2 million vehicles.
- It specializes in assembling the Telluride SUV, Sorento SUV, Sportage SUV, and K5 mid-size sedan.
- Kia Georgia recently expanded production capabilities to include the all-electric EV9.
- The company operates on a 2,200-acre site with an annual production capacity of 350,000 units.
- It is committed to community engagement, contributing to local economic development and workforce training initiatives.
- Mercedes-Benz USA is based in Atlanta, Georgia.
- It is a prominent automobile manufacturer and luxury vehicle retailer founded in 1965.
- The company specializes in electric models, SUVs, and sedans.
- It has a workforce of over 11,000 employees.
- The company is actively involved in the electric vehicle market.
- Haering Precision USA LP is a family-owned company based in Lavonia, Georgia.
- Established in 1961, it specializes in precision turned parts for automotive applications.
- Product offerings include components for traditional combustion engines, hybrid and electric vehicles, and hydrogen fuel cells.
- The company operates internationally with significant presence in Poland, Germany, and Tunisia.
- Jefferson Southern Corporation is based in Rockmart, Georgia.
- Founded in 2001, it specializes in high-quality stamped and welded components for vehicles.
- It operates as a Tier One supplier providing parts ready to be installed in automobiles.
- Products primarily form part of the vehicle's interior shell.
- The company is part of the North American J-group, which includes operations in Georgia, Ohio, and Canada.
- It is affiliated with G-TEKT.
- Ajin Georgia is a private automotive parts manufacturer based in Register, Georgia.
- Founded in 2023, it is part of the South Korea-based Ajin Corporation.
- The company is constructing a factory in Bulloch County.
- The new factory is expected to create over 600 jobs.
- The investment for the new facility is approximately $278 million.
- Operations will support the Hyundai Motor Group's Metaplant.
- The company donated $40,000 to local organizations before commencing production.
- Sewon America Inc. is based in Rincon, Georgia.
- Established in 1985, it specializes in automotive stamping and interior parts.
- The company maintains a robust research and development framework to enhance manufacturing processes.
- Mack Trucks INC is based in Fayetteville, Georgia.
- It has manufactured heavy-duty trucks since 1900.
- Truck models include the Mack Anthem and Mack Granite.
- The company provides fleet management and maintenance services.
- It develops electric vehicle options such as the Mack LR Electric.
- It supports customers with 24/7 roadside assistance through the Mack OneCall program.
- Hyundai MOBIS Alabama, LLC - Georgia Plant was established in 2007 and operates out of West Point, Georgia.
- The plant specializes in assembling and supplying chassis modules, cockpit modules, and front-end modules.
- It plays a vital role in supporting the KIA Georgia factory.
- The company employs cutting-edge vehicle modularization technology and a just-in-sequence manufacturing environment.
- Toyota Industries Group TACG-TICA was established in 2004 and is based in Pendergrass, Georgia.
- The company specializes in producing vehicle air conditioning compressors and DC-AC inverters.
- It has a workforce of approximately 1,300 associates.
- It produces around 4.6 million air conditioning compressors annually.
- In 2020, it expanded operations to include the assembly of DC-AC inverters.
- Ecoplastic America Corporation was founded in 1966 and is based in Register, Georgia.
- It specializes in eco-friendly plastic components for vehicles.
- It serves as a tier-1 supplier for Hyundai Motor Group.
- Products include bumpers, consoles, trims, and plastic molds.
- The company is actively building a manufacturing facility in Bulloch County, Georgia.
- Trenton Pressing was founded in 1995 and located in Trenton, Georgia.
- It specializes in engineered stamping and welding assembly solutions for the automotive industry.
- The company operates from over 192,000 square feet across three facilities.
- It is a direct Tier One supplier to major manufacturers including BMW and Club Car.
- The company is IATF-certified.
- SK battery America is based in Commerce, Georgia.
- Founded in 2019, it specializes in electric vehicle batteries.
- It has a workforce of over 3,000 employees.
- The company focuses on high-nickel technology for superior energy density and extended driving range.
- It is dedicated to sustainability through the development of high-efficiency lithium-ion batteries.
- KTX America Corporation, also known as NIFCO KTW America, is based in Toccoa, Georgia.
- Founded in 2016, it specializes in high-quality plastic components for vehicle interiors and exteriors.
- It is a tier 1 supplier to major manufacturers such as BMW and Daimler.
- The company employs advanced manufacturing techniques including multi-component injection molding and high-gloss painting.
- Its production facility handles both small and large-scale series production.
- Porsche Cars North America is based in Atlanta, Georgia.
- Founded in 1948, it manufactures high-performance vehicles and luxury lifestyle products.
- Product lineup includes the Macan, Taycan, and 911 models.
- The company offers lifestyle products including eBikes and smartwatches.
- It is making strides in the electric vehicle market.
- Lyle Industries Inc was founded in 1981 and based in Dalton, Georgia.
- It manufactures textile products for the automotive industry, including automotive carpet, molded flooring, and interior trim.
- The company produces approximately 14 million square meters of carpet annually.
- Yazaki Morocco is a subsidiary of Yazaki Europe specializing in electrical distribution systems and components.
- Founded in 2000, it focuses on customized cabling systems, sub-harnesses, and electronic components for e-mobility applications.
- Fouts Bros Inc. is a family-owned commercial vehicle manufacturer based in Milledgeville, Georgia.
- Its history dates back to 1952.
- It focuses on production and upfitting of work trucks, vans, and automotive equipment for public safety and military sectors.
- Headquarters spans 700,000 square feet with a second facility in La Grange, Texas.
- Rocky Ridge Trucks was founded in 1986 and based in Franklin Springs, Georgia.
- It focuses on design and manufacture of custom and lifted 4x4 trucks.
- It offers modifications for Chevrolet, Ford, GMC, Nissan, and RAM brands.
- Modifications help maintain the factory warranty of original vehicles.
