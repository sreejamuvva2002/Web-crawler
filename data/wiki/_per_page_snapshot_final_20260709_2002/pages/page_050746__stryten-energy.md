# Stryten Energy

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Stryten Energy showcasing advanced battery energy storage solutions at CES 2026](https://www.renewableenergymagazine.com/storage/stryten-energy-showcasing-advanced-battery-energy-storage-20260107)  ·  `page_050746`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy is presenting a portfolio of solutions at CES 2026, located at booth 6929 in the West Hall of the Las Vegas Convention Centre.
- The company's portfolio includes advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium technologies designed to ensure customers have the right chemistry for their specific application.
- Stryten offers modular lead battery energy storage system (BESS) solutions that can be scaled from microgrids to large industrial sites.
- These lead BESS solutions can integrate with other energy sources to deliver safe and dependable power for peak shaving and as a safeguard against power outages.
- A significant domestic circular economy for lead batteries already exists, making lead one of the most sustainable storage options available.
- Stryten also provides lithium and vanadium flow battery solutions.
- The vanadium flow battery technology offers safe, scalable, long-duration energy storage with 5-24+ hours of resilience for data centers and microgrids.
- Vanadium redox flow battery components are available through Stryten Energy’s joint venture, Storion Energy.
- Storion Energy offers domestically sourced vanadium electrolyte and high-performance stack power assemblies.
- Mike Judd serves as the CEO of Stryten Energy.
- CEO Mike Judd stated that Stryten is driving energy transformation with advanced battery solutions built in the US to strengthen communities, secure supply chains, and power America’s energy future.
- According to Battery Council International (BCI), US electricity demand is projected to quadruple by 2030, primarily driven by AI adoption and business sector electrification.
- Batteries can support surging electricity demand by storing excess energy, balancing supply and demand, and providing reliable backup power.
- Domestic battery production strengthens energy security by reducing reliance on foreign supply chains to ensure a resilient grid.
