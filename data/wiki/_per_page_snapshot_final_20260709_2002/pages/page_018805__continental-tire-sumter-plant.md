# Continental Tire Sumter Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-08-31

**Source:** [Continental Tire Breaks Ground on New Sumter Building](https://www.thelinksc.com/post/continental-tire-breaks-ground-on-new-sumter-building)  ·  `page_018805`

## Facts

- Continental Tire broke ground on a new building in Sumter, South Carolina on August 29, 2022.
- Over 126,000 square feet will be added to the existing two million-square-foot tire manufacturing facility.
- The new expansion area will house additional production equipment for current production and future capacity needs.
- Benny Harmse, Continental plant manager and vice president of Manufacturing, stated that the new production building is an investment in the future of the Sumter facility and exemplifies the trust Continental has in dedicated employees who continue to deliver quality products to customers.
- Continental will begin the installation of a new extruder in May 2023.
- The new extruder will be operational by November 2023.
- The extruder installation is intended to support the current production capacity.
- Approximately 10 new jobs will be created by 2023 to support tire production at the facility.
- The Continental Tire Sumter plant produces high-quality, premium lines of passenger and light truck tires for original equipment and replacement markets.
- The Sumter Plant employs a growing team of approximately 1,300 employees.
- The facility holds IATF 16949:2016 certification.
- The facility holds ISO 14001 certification.
- The Sumter Plant operates as a zero-landfill facility.
- Continental Tire the Americas, LLC is the parent organization of the Sumter plant.
- Continental was founded in 1871 and develops technologies and services for sustainable and connected mobility.
- In 2021, Continental generated sales of €33.8 billion and employed more than 190,000 people across 58 countries and markets.
- On October 8, 2021, Continental celebrated its 150th anniversary.
- The Tires group sector operates 24 production and development locations worldwide.
- Continental is a leading tire manufacturer with more than 57,000 employees in the tire sector and posted €11.8 billion in sales for the sector in 2021.
- The company's tire business portfolio includes services for the tire trade and fleet applications, as well as digital management systems for tires.
