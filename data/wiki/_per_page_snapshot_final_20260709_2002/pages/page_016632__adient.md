## Facts

- The ADIENT plant in Lučenec was established in 2005.
- It currently employs more than 800 people.
- Due to its complexity, it is one of the largest plants of the Adient company and is continuously modernized according to Industry 4.0 standards.
- The facility focuses on the production of headrests and foam parts for car seats.
- Its products are supplied to 21 car manufacturers worldwide.
- The product portfolio ranges from standard items to luxury components.
- The company plays a significant role in implementing RFID technology into its production process.
- The contactless RFID identification technology allows customers to track individual operations throughout the entire production process up to the final product.
- RFID technology is also used for the inspection of final packaging and shipping.
