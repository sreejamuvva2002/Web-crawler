# Blue Bird Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-10-01

**Source:** [Blue Bird Appoints Razvan Radulescu Chief Financial Officer Effective October 1, 2021](https://www.centralcharts.com/en/14375-blue-bird-corp/news/3325603-blue-bird-appoints-razvan-radulescu-chief-financial-officer-effective-october-1-2021)  ·  `page_052939`

## Facts

- Blue Bird Corporation (Nasdaq: BLBD) is the leading independent designer and manufacturer of school buses.
- Blue Bird Corporation appointed Razvan Radulescu as Chief Financial Officer effective October 1, 2021.
- Razvan Radulescu has over 20 years of financial and operating experience at Daimler Trucks in Finance, Controlling, Procurement, IT, and Compliance.
- Most recently, Razvan Radulescu served as General Manager of Procurement for Daimler Trucks North America, responsible for all production and aftersales component purchases.
- Prior to that role, Razvan Radulescu was CFO of Daimler’s Global Powertrain Truck Business Unit in Stuttgart, Germany.
- From 2013 to 2017, Razvan Radulescu served as Controller for Freightliner Trucks inside Daimler Trucks North America.
- Phil Horlock, Chief Executive Officer of Blue Bird Corporation, stated that Razvan is an accomplished global executive with extensive financial experience in the truck industry and will join the senior leadership team.
- Razvan Radulescu will take over from interim CFO Phil Tighe on October 1, 2021, while Phil Tighe continues through the end of the year to facilitate a smooth transition.
- Razvan Radulescu holds a Bachelor’s of Computer Science from the Academy of Economics Studies in Bucharest, Romania, and an MBA from Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, OH.
- Blue Bird has sold more than 570,000 buses since its formation in 1927 and has approximately 180,000 buses in operation today.
- Blue Bird distinguishes itself by its singular focus on the design, engineering, manufacture, and sale of school buses and related parts.
- Blue Bird is the only manufacturer of chassis and body production specifically designed for school bus applications.
- Blue Bird is the market leader in alternative-fuel applications with a best-in-industry range of electric-, propane-, and compressed natural gas-powered school buses.
- Blue Bird manufactures school buses at two facilities in Fort Valley, Georgia.
- The Micro Bird joint venture operates a manufacturing facility in Drummondville, Quebec, Canada.
- Service and after-market parts are distributed from Blue Bird’s parts distribution center located in Delaware, Ohio.
