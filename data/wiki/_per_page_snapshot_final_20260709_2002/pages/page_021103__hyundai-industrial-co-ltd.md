# Hyundai Industrial Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-02

**Source:** [Hyundai Industrial purchases building to settle in Coweta | News | times-herald.com](https://www.times-herald.com/news/hyundai-industrial-purchases-building-to-settle-in-coweta/article_586b6352-7990-11ee-a963-e758305b2d40.html)  ·  `page_021103`

## Facts

- On Nov. 1, Hyundai Industrial Co. purchased a building at 83 Amlajack Way in Coweta County.
- Gov. Brian Kemp announced that morning that the auto parts supplier for Kia and Hyundai plants will create 100 new jobs in the county.
- Kemp stated in a written statement that partners like Kia and Hyundai are attracting a large network of suppliers, creating jobs and opportunity for Georgians across the state.
- The building on Amlajack Way will be retrofitted to produce arm and head rests for manufacturers including Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America in Bryan County, Georgia.
- Woosuck Kim serves as Team Leader at HDI America Inc.
- Kim stated Hyundai Industrial has more than four decades of experience providing comfortable and safe experiences for drivers and passengers with some of the world’s biggest automotive brands.
- Kim expressed excitement about being part of the e-mobility transition in Georgia and working with Coweta County to create mutually beneficial growth for the community and future employees.
- Sarah Jacobs, president of the Coweta County Development Authority, relayed the announcement to authority members at their meeting Thursday morning.
- The Coweta County Development Authority began working with the company in 2021.
- Jacobs noted that by 2021, the company had already picked out the Amlajack property.
- Jacobs stated the Georgia Department of Economic Development brought the project to them and they pitched for Coweta.
- Jacobs was unsure how many other communities were in the running for the factory.
- John Reidelbach, Coweta County Board of Commissioners Chairman, welcomed Hyundai Industrial Co., Ltd. to Coweta County in a written statement.
- Reidelbach noted that Hyundai joins thriving manufacturing industries and will have close proximity to I-85, making transport to nearby automobile plants effortless.
- Reidelbach expressed excitement for the workforce opportunities Hyundai will bring to citizens as they continue to increase within the community.
