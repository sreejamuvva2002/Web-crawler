# Charge Forward Georgia

## Facts

- Charge Forward Georgia connects job seekers to opportunities in Georgia’s electric vehicle (EV) industry.
- The initiative aims to build a future of growth, financial security, and purpose for workers in the Peach State.
- Georgia is positioned as leading the EV revolution by driving innovation, jobs, and investment toward a cleaner transportation future.
- EV career opportunities across Georgia range from entry-level to expert positions.
- The state boasts Fortune 500 companies, a booming EV network, and what is cited as the nation's top workforce training program.
- EV careers in Georgia span advanced manufacturing, software development, and engineering roles.
- These roles leverage cutting-edge tools, technology, and processes to ensure safety, efficiency, and sustainability.
- The work culture prioritizes productivity, employee health, and longevity.
- EV careers offer competitive salaries, excellent benefits, financial stability, and tremendous growth opportunities.
- Charge Forward Georgia provides clear pathways for students, job seekers, and career changers to enter high-growth, in-demand EV fields.
- The platform offers comprehensive job listings and training program exploration tools.
- Featured training programs are located at TCSG colleges around the state to prepare a robust workforce for future EV needs.
- The Electrical Vehicle Professional Technician (EVPI) program develops foundational skills in EV production.
- The Electrical Maintenance Technician (RT41) program is tailored to meet specific employer training needs.
- The Robotic Technician (RT41) program focuses on sharpening and upgrading automation skills for technician roles using robotics and innovative technologies.
- Georgia's workforce training program is claimed to be ranked No. 1 in the nation.
