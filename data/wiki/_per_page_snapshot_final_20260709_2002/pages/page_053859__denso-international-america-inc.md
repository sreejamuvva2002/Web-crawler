# DENSO International America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2013-01-15

**Source:** [DENSO to Invest Nearly $1 Billion in North America Over Next Four Years | Automotive World](https://www.automotiveworld.com/news-releases/denso-to-invest-nearly-1-billion-in-north-america-over-next-four-years/)  ·  `page_053859`

## Facts

- Global automotive supplier DENSO is investing nearly $1 billion in North America over the next four years.
- The investment will result in more than 2,000 jobs across North America.
- More than $750 million of the investment will be directed to the United States alone.
- The U.S. investment will create more than 1,200 jobs.
- DENSO aims to better support its North American customers and expand new business areas through this investment.
- The investment will focus on localizing products, many of which will help automakers meet upcoming fuel requirements.
- Terry Helgesen, senior vice president of Industry Relations at DENSO International America, Inc., stated that DENSO announced it would drastically localize products in the regions where it operates.
- Helgesen noted that DENSO is not only making products in North America but also localizing critical tooling.
- In the U.S., DENSO plans to invest in Michigan, Tennessee, Iowa, California, and North Carolina.
- The U.S. investment includes an increase in research and development, expansion of existing production lines, creation of new production lines, and opening a new assembly and warehouse facility for Heavy Duty customers.
- DENSO is investing in manufacturing more of its own equipment and dies in North America.
- A building at DENSO Manufacturing Tennessee in Maryville, Tenn., has been dedicated for making proprietary machinery, serving as the regional headquarters for manufacturing machinery and dies.
- DENSO plans to manufacture all of its critical manufacturing equipment in North America in the future.
- The company has sourced several millions of dollars of business to American machine shops, which will positively impact the community and create additional jobs.
- Currently, DENSO employs more than 14,000 people at 28 consolidated companies and affiliates in North America.
- Of these North American entities, 26 are manufacturing facilities located in the U.S., Canada, and Mexico.
- In the U.S. alone, DENSO currently employs more than 10,000 people across California, Michigan, North Carolina, South Carolina, Tennessee, Kentucky, Georgia, Iowa, Ohio, and Arkansas.
