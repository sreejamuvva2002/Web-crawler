# RealTruck, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-17

**Source:** [RealTruck, Inc. acquires Mountain Top | ศูนย์จำหน่ายอุปกรณ์แต่งรถกระบะ 11349](https://realtruck.co.th/en/realtruck-inc-acquires-mountain-top/)  ·  `page_049083`

## Facts

- RealTruck, Inc. acquired Mountain Top, the leading designer and manufacturer of truck bed accessories outside North America.
- The acquisition was announced on Oct. 17, 2023, in Ann Arbor, Mich.
- Mountain Top is based in Denmark and supplies major original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) while selling truck accessories throughout Europe, Asia, Australia, and the U.S.
- The acquisition accelerates RealTruck’s international expansion and complements its recent acquisition of Mexico-based brand Go Rhino.
- RealTruck CEO Carl-Martin Lindahl stated they are bringing Mountain Top into the RealTruck family to power international expansion and growth.
- Mountain Top has state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in Denmark, Thailand, and the U.S.
- Mountain Top is the only European truck bed cover supplier of scale certified by the International Automotive Task Force (IATF).
- Mountain Top’s advanced manufacturing capabilities provide RealTruck with more capacity to support global operations and accelerate sales into markets like Australia, Thailand, and across Europe.
- Mountain Top’s flagship product line is the premium retractable truck bed cover available in both electronic and manual versions.
- Mountain Top CEO Henrik Støwer Petersen stated the team is excited to join RealTruck to provide more value to business partners with access to a broader range of product offerings.
- In August 2023, RealTruck acquired IDDEA (Industria Diseñadora De Autopartes, S.A. De C.V.), which markets under the Go Rhino brand.
- Go Rhino holds 40 issued and pending patents for innovative premium products developed and manufactured for truck, Jeep, and SUV owners.
- The acquisition strengthens RealTruck’s existing portfolio of brands spanning truck bed covers, truck bed liners, steps, floor liners, fender flares, vent visors, and cargo management.
- RealTruck is headquartered in Ann Arbor, Mich., with over 5,000 associates across 47 locations throughout North America.
- RealTruck holds more than 630 patents and continuously designs, develops, manufactures, and sells industry-defining products.
- RealTruck’s products are sold through its website, more than 12,000 dealer locations, and automotive (OEM) partnerships.
