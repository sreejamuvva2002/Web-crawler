# Porsche Financial Services, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-27

**Source:** [Porsche Financial Services, Inc. Issues Prime Auto Transaction in the U.S. ABS Market](https://markets.financialcontent.com/worldnow.kwtv/article/gnwcq-2025-10-27-porsche-financial-services-inc-issues-prime-auto-transaction-in-the-us-abs-market)  ·  `page_048709`

## Facts

- Porsche Financial Services, Inc. is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- PFS issued an auto Asset Backed Securities (ABS) offering in the USA with a principal amount of $870.4 million dollars.
- This transaction marked the second bond issuance for PFS in 2025.
- PFS is an indirect, wholly owned subsidiary of German luxury car maker Dr. Ing. h.c. F. Porsche AG (“Porsche AG”).
- The securities maintained a ‘AAA’ rating from rating agencies and achieved competitive pricing.
- The transaction was divided into five tranches, including a floating rate tranche.
- The ABS offering was backed by a pool of auto contracts financing Porsche vehicles.
- RBC Capital Markets led the transaction, with BofA Securities, Scotiabank, Société Générale, and Truist Securities serving as book runners.
- The deal reached subscription levels in each tranche to achieve an upsize from a growing investor base comprised of investment funds, asset managers of financial institutions, trusts, banks, and corporates.
- “Despite launching during one of the busiest weeks in the 2025 ABS primary market, PFAST 2025-1 received solid investor support,” said Tobias Hausladen, Treasurer & Chief Financial Officer at Porsche Financial Services, Inc.
- “We greatly appreciate the confidence investors continue to place in Porsche Financial Services. The outcome reflects the strength of our credit platform and the consistency of our performance, even in challenging market environments.”
- PFS provides leasing and financing products for Porsche in the United States.
- PFS was founded in 1991.
- In 2012, PFS expanded its North America operations to become the captive finance provider for Bentley, Lamborghini, and Bugatti under the Volkswagen Group.
