## Facts

- Planning to add as many as 370 jobs at their plant in Wauwatosa.
- A portion of the 475 employee-workforce from the Georgia facility are moving to the Wauwatosa plant.
- Company officials claim they saw an improved business climate and that tax, business climate and employee base made sense to bring the work here from right-to-work Georgia.
- Company officials are in preliminary discussions with the Wisconsin Economic Development Corp. and the city of Wauwatosa to see what financial incentives Briggs might be able to receive in connection with the move.
- Todd Teske, Briggs and Stratton’s CEO, just ended a two-year tenure as the Chairman of the Board of Directors at Wisconsin Manufacturers and Commerce.
- Briggs and Stratton employees have overwhelmingly given to Republicans over the years, including over $30,000 to Scott Walker.
