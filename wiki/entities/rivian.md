# Rivian

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Stanton Springs North, East Atlanta Megasite, Jasper, Morgan, Newton, Walton County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Rivian announced a $5 billion investment to build a carbon-conscious electric vehicle manufacturing campus on the East Atlanta Megasite (Stanton Springs North) in Georgia, creating about 7,500 jobs and a plant capable of producing up to 400,000 vehicles per year.

## EV supply-chain relevance

High - Rivian is a major electric-vehicle manufacturer establishing its largest U.S. plant in Georgia.
