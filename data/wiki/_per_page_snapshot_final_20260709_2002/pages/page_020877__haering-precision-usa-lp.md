# Haering Precision USA LP

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-06

**Source:** [2025 Haering Precision Skills Invitational showcases local talent | Hartwell Sun, Hartwell, Georgia](https://www.thehartwellsun.com/local/2025-haering-precision-skills-invitational-showcases-local-talent)  ·  `page_020877`

## Facts

- Haering Precision USA LP partnered with North Georgia Technical College (NGTC) to host the second annual Haering Precision Skills Invitational in March 2025 at NGTC’s Clarkesville campus.
- The event brought together high school students from across Northeast Georgia for a dynamic, hands-on competition aimed at inspiring the next generation of manufacturing talent.
- Students from Hart, Franklin, White, and Union counties participated in the pro-am style invitational.
- Each participating high school student was paired with a precision machining student from NGTC to complete real-world challenges together.
- Teams worked on challenges designed to reflect modern manufacturing processes, including interpreting technical drawings, operating CNC equipment, and fabricating a custom wax stamp.
- The competition was judged by industry and educational professionals who evaluated teams on technical accuracy, creativity, teamwork, and overall execution.
- The top three students were recognized and received prizes including professional tools to begin their careers in manufacturing.
- Dustin Chambers, Commercial Plant Manager at Haering Precision, stated the goal is to create meaningful, hands-on experiences that connect students with career opportunities in high-tech manufacturing.
- All participating students have been invited to complete a paid internship or work-based learning program at Haering Precision’s Lavonia facility.
- These programs provide direct exposure to cutting-edge production environments and serve as a pathway into high-demand technical careers.
- The 2025 Haering Precision Skills Invitational is part of Haering’s ongoing commitment to workforce development, technical education, and community collaboration.
