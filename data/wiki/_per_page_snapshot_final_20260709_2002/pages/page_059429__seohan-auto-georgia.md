# Seohan Auto Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-09

**Source:** [Seohan Auto Georgia is Open for Business in Liberty County - LaGrange CEO](https://lagrangeceo.com/news/2024/09/seohan-auto-georgia-open-business-liberty-county-news/)  ·  `page_059429`

## Facts

- Seohan Auto Georgia held a Grand Opening Ceremony for its new manufacturing facility in the Tradeport East Business Center on Friday, September 6th, 2024.
- The facility will produce H/shafts, axles, and brake systems for Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs).
- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America is an OEM customer for the facility's products.
- Operations at the new facility will create at least 180 new jobs.
- The project represents an investment of more than $72 million in Liberty County.
- Georgia Department of Economic Development Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that community investment in the Tradeport East Business Center allowed Seohan Auto Georgia to meet a challenging timeline to open.
- State Representative Al Williams, who also serves as Liberty County Development Authority Board Chairman, noted that the process to locate the facility began in November of 2022 after other locations were considered.
- Yong Suk Kim serves as Seohan Group Chairman.
- Jung Kee Koo serves as Seohan Auto Georgia CEO.
- Myon Han serves as Seohan Auto USA President.
- Woon Dae Paek serves as Seohan Group Vice Chairman.
- Yong Jun Kim serves as Seohan President of Sales Headquarters.
- Sydne Smith serves as GDEcD COO.
- Kristi Brigman serves as GDEcD Deputy Commissioner.
- Mayor Karl Riles, Liberty County Commission Chairman Donald Lovett, Luke Moses, Melissa Carter Ray, Willa Lewis, and Marcus Sack serve on the LCDA Board.
