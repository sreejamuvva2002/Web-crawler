# Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Panasonic Automotive Joins SOAFEE Governing Body To Lead SDV Advancements - Mobility Outlook](https://www.mobilityoutlook.com/news/panasonic-automotive-joins-soafee-governing-body-to-lead-sdv-advancements/)  ·  `page_048024`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd. (PAS) has been appointed to the Governing Body of SOAFEE (Scalable Open Architecture for Embedded Edge).
- The appointment marks a major recognition of PAS's leadership in driving the evolution of Software-Defined Vehicle (SDV) technologies.
- SOAFEE is a global initiative uniting automotive and software industry leaders to accelerate the realization of AI-powered, cloud-native SDVs.
- PAS joined the SOAFEE Technical Steering Committee in 2022 before becoming the ninth company on the Governing Body.
- PAS has been instrumental in developing VirtIO-based, hardware-agnostic solutions such as Unified HMI and Virtual SkipGen.
- These innovations have been adopted as official SOAFEE blueprints.
- The solutions support the decoupled development of automotive software from hardware to enable faster and more flexible advancements in next-gen cockpit systems.
- PAS assumed leadership of the newly created SOAFEE Asia-Pacific (APAC) Regional Hub in November 2024, with full operations beginning May 2025.
- The APAC hub is designed to energize SDV collaboration in a region comprising a significant share of SOAFEE’s 150+ global member companies.
- Masashige Mizuyama, Executive Vice President and CTO of Panasonic Automotive Systems, stated that PAS fully supports SOAFEE’s vision of enabling seamless, cloud-native environments for fast and continuous delivery of automotive software.
- Mizuyama added that device virtualization will be a key enabler of this transition.
- As a Governing Body member, PAS will contribute to global standardization efforts, collaborate with key industry organizations, and lead initiatives to overcome challenges in cloud-native and SDV development.
- This role reinforces PAS's strategic position in shaping the next generation of connected, intelligent vehicles.
