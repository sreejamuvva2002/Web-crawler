# Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-01-06

**Source:** [Goodyear ready to roll out a more environmentally friendly tire | Ideastream Public Media](https://www.ideastream.org/environment-energy/2023-01-06/goodyear-ready-to-roll-out-a-more-environmentally-friendly-tire)  ·  `page_055138`

## Facts

- Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company has unveiled tires that have a substantially increased amount of sustainable materials used in their production.
- The new tires are made of 70% sustainable materials.
- Senior Vice President of Global Operations and Chief Technology Officer Chris Helsel stated this is a significant improvement from current tires.
- Normal tires sold today might have up to a 30% sustainable content, predominantly due to natural rubber.
- In 2020, Goodyear set the goal of creating a tire made 100% with sustainable materials.
- The company analyzed traditional tire materials like petroleum, silica, and carbon black to find eco-friendly alternatives or sustainable sourcing methods.
- Carbon black is essential for vulcanization, allowing rubber to stiffen and withstand friction.
- Goodyear sources four types of carbon black from methane, carbon dioxide, plant-based oils, and oils pulled from recycled tires via pyrolysis.
- These carbon black technologies target reduced carbon emissions, circularity, and the use of bio-based carbons while maintaining performance.
- The company harvests silica from rice husk waste residue due to its high silica content.
- Goodyear uses soybean oil in place of petroleum in its sustainable tires.
- Soybean oil provides better low-temperature performance and maintains rubber pliability across a broader temperature range.
- Polyester cords in the tire are made with recycled plastic bottles.
- Traction material is made with pine tree resin.
- The new sustainable tires last just as long as traditional ones and meet European standards on fuel economy, traction, noise, and tread.
- Goodyear is using a marketing strategy asking interested buyers to register for updates online, working similarly to a presale to gauge demand before market release.
- Goodyear debuted a demonstration tire made with 90% sustainable material.
- The 90% sustainable tire will not be on the market for a few years due to limited material supply.
- The company hopes the 70% sustainable tire will increase interest, motivate investors to fund sustainable tires, and increase material supply.
- Chris Helsel encouraged motorists to maintain their current tires to improve fuel economy and tire lifespan, noting that proper maintenance is also a form of sustainability.
