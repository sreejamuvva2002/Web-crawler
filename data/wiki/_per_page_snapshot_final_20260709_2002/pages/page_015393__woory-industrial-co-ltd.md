# Woory Industrial Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Woory to build $18 million automotive parts plant in Georgia | Plant Services](https://www.plantservices.com/industry-news/news/33006486/woory-to-build-18-million-automotive-parts-plant-in-georgia)  ·  `page_015393`

## Facts

- Woory Industrial Co., Ltd. has announced plans to build a new manufacturing facility in Dublin, Georgia.
- The automotive parts manufacturer will invest $18 million into the project.
- The project will create over 130 new jobs for the area.
- This facility will be Woory’s first plant in the U.S.
- The plant is expected to be operational in November 2023.
- According to the company, the plant will produce electrical heaters, control units, and actuators for electric vehicle and internal combustion carmakers.
- Jungwoo Kim, CEO of Woory Industrial Company, stated that the company is facing a revolution in the automobile industry for the first time in 100 years.
- Jungwoo Kim noted that the transition to electric vehicles is the future and the U.S. EV market is the key market among all automotive markets.
- Over the past 10 years, Woory has successfully grown into a specialized EV component company through continuous innovation.
- Jungwoo Kim said Georgia will be a good opportunity for Woory to take another step forward as part of the vanguard of EV development.
- Jungwoo Kim expressed confidence that Woory's experience and technology will contribute to the prosperity of the community.
- Jungwoo Kim sincerely appreciated the partnership of the State of Georgia and the City of Dublin.
