# Hwashin Georgia Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-02-13

## Facts

- Hwashin Georgia Corporation was awarded a $1.2 million Economic Development Growth and Expansion (EDGE) grant.
- The grant award was celebrated in partnership with the Georgia Department of Economic Development and the Department of Community Affairs.
- Hwashin Georgia is the first tenant at the new Industrial Park on Highway 257.
- Hwashin Georgia is investing $176 million into its facility.
- The project will bring 475 new jobs to Laurens County.
- The EDGE grant specifically funded the purchase of 56 advanced robots for Hwashin's manufacturing process.
- Hwashin Georgia operates as a tier-one supplier to the Hyundai Motor Group Meta Plant.
- Hwashin Georgia manufactures components for Kia, Ford, and Rivian.
- Hwashin Georgia's operations are located in Dublin, Georgia.
- The Heart of Georgia Altamaha Regional Commission (HOGARC) partnered in securing the grant and other critical grants for the industrial park.
