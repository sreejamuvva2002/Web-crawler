# Inalfa Roof Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-20

**Source:** [Global Auto Parts Manufacturer Announces 127 Layoffs at Two Georgia Facilities](https://whatnow.com/atlanta/local-news/global-auto-parts-manufacturer-announces-127-layoffs-at-two-georgia-facilities/)  ·  `page_045127`

## Facts

- Inalfa Roof Systems filed a Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification (WARN) notice on May 19 with the state of Georgia.
- The company is conducting mass layoffs affecting two manufacturing facilities in Cherokee County, Georgia.
- The layoffs are expected to be permanent and will occur gradually between July 17 and July 31, 2026.
- A total of 127 workers will be impacted by the workforce reduction.
- The facility at 1000 Cherokee Pkwy, Acworth, Georgia 30102 will have 83 jobs impacted.
- The facility at 1300 Cherokee Pkwy, Acworth, Georgia 30102 will have 44 jobs impacted.
- Inalfa Roof Systems was established in 1946 and specializes in the design, development, and production of vehicle roof systems.
- The company's product portfolio includes panoramic roofs, sunroof systems, convertible roof technologies, and integrated roof systems.
- Inalfa supplies over 40 vehicle brands globally.
- The company maintains engineering hubs in North America, Europe, and China.
- Extensive R&D teams at Inalfa work on advanced mobility and vehicle design technologies.
- Inalfa collaborates with suppliers, startups, and industry partners to develop next-generation automotive solutions.
- These collaborative efforts include the development of electric and smart vehicle platforms.
- The company operates 16 manufacturing sites globally, supplying products to major automotive manufacturers worldwide.
- The specific reason for the mass workforce reduction was not provided in the WARN filing.
