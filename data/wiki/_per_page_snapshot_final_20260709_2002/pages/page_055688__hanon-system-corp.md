# Hanon System Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-31

**Source:** [Hanon Systems (Formerly Halla Visteon Climate Control) - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/hanon-systems)  ·  `page_055688`

## Facts

- A leading automotive air conditioning system supplier in Korea.
- The company mainly supplies products to Hyundai, Kia, and Ford.
- The company manufactures air conditioning, powertrain cooling, compressors, fluid transport, and hydraulic control systems.
- Established in 1986 as a joint venture between Ford and Mando Machinery (now Mando) of Korea.
- In 1999, Ford transferred its interest to Visteon (USA) and the company became a subsidiary of Visteon.
- Acquired thermal exhaust business from Cooper-Standard in 2014.
- In 2015, Visteon sold its interest to affiliates of Hahn & Company and Hankook Tire, and the company changed its name to Hanon System Corp.
- Acquired Magna International's hydraulic controls business (Fluid Pressure and Controls) in 2019.
- Acquired condenser business of Keihin Corporation (now Astemo) in 2021.
- Established a new engineering center in Portugal in 2024.
- The company had the largest market share for automotive air conditioning systems in Korea in the fiscal year ended December 31, 2024.
- Korean automotive air conditioning market share was 48% in FY ended Dec 31, 2024, 46% in FY ended Dec 31, 2023, and 46% in FY ended Dec 31, 2022.
- Main customers for the Korean market share data are Hyundai Kia.
- As of Jan 3, 2025, major shareholders were Hankook Tire & Technology Co., Ltd. (54.77%) and Hahn & Co. Auto Holdings Co., Ltd. (21.63%).
- HVAC products include Ultra-Flat HVAC, Super slim HVAC system, Straight Airflow Path HVAC, Ultraviolet (UV) LED photocatalyst, CO2 sensor, fragrance system, and ionizers.
- Compressor products include Fixed Swash Plate Compressor, Rotary Suction Compressor, Variable Swash Plate Compressor, and Electric Compressor.
- Offers fluid transport controllers, cooling modules, condensers, radiators, charge air coolers (CAC), EGR systems, pumps & valves, electronic throttle bodies, and electronic wastegate actuators.
- EV/HEV thermal management products include battery thermal management systems, heat pump systems, and high voltage cooling fan motors.
- FCEV products include centrifugal air compressors, high voltage cooling fan motors, and e-compressors.
- In July 2009, the company acquired the remaining shares in Halla Climate Control Alabama Inc. (HCSA), making it a wholly owned subsidiary.
- Visteon sold its approximately 70 percent ownership interest to an affiliate of Hahn & Company and Hankook Tire Co., Ltd. for approximately USD 3.6 billion in Dec 2014, with completion expected in the first half of 2015.
- Established Beijing BHAP Hanon Systems Co., Ltd. in Dec 2017 as a joint venture with Shandon Binzhou Bohai Piston Co., Ltd. (subsidiary of BAIC) to manufacture HVAC control systems.
- Established Chongqing Hanon Jianshe Automotive Thermal Systems Co., Ltd. in Jan 2018.
- Established Hanon EFP Corporation, FAWER Hanon Automotive Components (Changchun) Company Ltd., and Hanon systems EFP Deutschland GmbH in Aug 2018.
- Established EFP Operations Mexicana, S.A.de C.V and Climate Systems Services Mexicana EFP, S.A. de C.V. in Nov 2018.
- Completed acquisition of Gibbs-Hungary Die Casting Ipari Termelo Kft in Nov 2018.
- Completed acquisition of Magna International’s Fluid Pressure and Controls Division in Mar 2019.
- Merged Hanon Systems Southern Germany GmbH into Hanon Systems Deutschland GmbH in Nov 2019.
- Merged Hanon Systems EFP Bad Homburg GmbH and Hanon Systems Bad Homburg GmbH into Hanon Systems EFP Deutschland GmbH in Nov 2019.
- Divested Jinan Han Cai Automotive Parts Company in Dec 2019.
- Sold Hanon Bangalore Operations Private Limited in Feb 2021.
- Acquired Hanon Thermal Technology Czech, s.r.o. in Mar 2021.
- Acquired Powertrain Cooling de Mexico, S.A. de C.V. in May 2021.
- Acquired Keihin Corporation’s North American and European condenser operations in May 2021.
- Established Hanon Systems Hubei Co., Ltd. in Dec 2021.
- Liquidated Hanon Systems Rus LLC in Oct 2024.
