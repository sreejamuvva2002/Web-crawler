# ABC Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-19

**Source:** [Key Leadership Appointments Announced For Combined ABC Technologies & TI Fluid Systems Group - ABC Technologies](https://abc-tech.net/key-leadership-appointments-announced-for-combined-abc-technologies-ti-fluid-systems-group/)  ·  `page_011560`

## Facts

- Terry Campbell will become President & Chief Executive Officer and Alexander De Bock will become Chief Financial Officer of the combined group following the acquisition.
- TI Fluid Systems’ current President & Chief Executive Officer Hans Dieltjens will step down following completion of the acquisition.
- ABC Technologies’ acquisition of TI Fluid Systems remains on track to complete during the first half of 2025.
- The announcement was made in Toronto, Canada and Auburn Hills, Michigan, USA on February 19, 2025.
- Terry Campbell has served as President & CEO of ABC Technologies since 2022 and previously held senior leadership roles at Johnson Controls, Magna International, and The Woodbridge Group.
- Campbell brings over thirty years’ experience in the global automotive sector with expertise in operational excellence and product innovation.
- Alexander De Bock was appointed CFO of TI Fluid Systems in 2023 after spending over 15 years in the industry, including finance leadership roles at WABCO and ZF Group.
- Terry Campbell stated that as a larger, diversified business with a broad range of products and an expanded geographic footprint, the combined group will be extremely well positioned to navigate the transition to electrification, deliver value for customers, and achieve sustainable growth.
- Hans Dieltjens was appointed President & CEO of TI Fluid Systems in 2021 following 25 years in leadership positions across the business.
- Hans Dieltjens stated that together they have positioned the TI Fluid Systems group for a successful future and he is confident the business will continue to thrive and grow together with ABC.
- The Board of TI Fluid Systems announced the terms and conditions of a recommended all cash offer from ABC Technologies Acquisitions Limited (“Bidco”) for the entire issued and to be issued ordinary share capital of TI Fluid Systems.
- The acquisition is intended to be implemented by way of a court-sanctioned scheme of arrangement under Part 26 of the Companies Act 2006.
- The acquisition was approved by TI Fluid Systems shareholders on February 5, 2025, and is expected to complete in the first half of the year following receipt of remaining regulatory and judicial clearances.
- TI Fluid Systems will give notice of the anticipated completion timetable by issuing an announcement through a Regulatory Information Service and making it available on its website.
- The combination with ABC Technologies will bring together two strategically complementary businesses to create a larger, more diversified group better positioned to navigate the transition to electrification and deliver sustainable, long-term growth.
- ABC Technologies is a leading global manufacturer and supplier of custom, highly engineered, technical plastics, and light-weight innovations to the North American light vehicle industry.
- ABC Technologies serves more than 25 major original equipment manufacturer customers in 8 countries.
- ABC Technologies offers vertically integrated product and process solutions through a skilled workforce of over 11,000 team members.
- ABC Technologies is majority owned by certain affiliated funds of Apollo Global Management, Inc., with Oaktree Capital Management, L.P. owning a minority equity interest.
- TI Fluid Systems is a global innovator of thermal and fluid systems solutions for the full range of current and developing vehicle architectures.
- TI Fluid Systems serves all major automotive manufacturers and has more than 100 years of automotive supply experience.
- TI Fluid Systems operates across 26 countries with a commitment to improving efficiency, performance, and sustainability worldwide.
