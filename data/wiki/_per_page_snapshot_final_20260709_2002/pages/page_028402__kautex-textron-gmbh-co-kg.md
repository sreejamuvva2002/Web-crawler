# Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG Receives A- Climate Change Score from CDP](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20221222005440/en/Kautex-Textron-GmbH-Co.-KG-Receives-A--Climate-Change-Score-from-CDP)  ·  `page_028402`

## Facts

- Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG received an “A-” Climate Change rating from CDP.
- The company is a subsidiary of Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT).
- Kautex announced its sustainability roadmap in November 2021.
- The roadmap commits to targeting an 80 percent reduction in Scope 1 and 2 emissions and a 30 percent reduction in Scope 3 emissions by 2030.
- The company aspires to be net-zero by 2050 in all three scopes.
- In 2022, Kautex challenged its 30-plus plants worldwide to reduce their Scope 1 and 2 emissions through practical steps like energy audits and strategic investments in renewable energy.
- In 2022, all of the company’s facilities on mainland Europe, China, Mexico, and in Lavonia, GA are sourcing renewable energy.
- The company joined CDP as a Supply Chain Corporate member.
- Kautex is challenging its suppliers to join CDP to disclose their own environmental impact and take concrete steps to reduce their impact over time.
- Sylvain Genesi serves as Kautex’s vice president, Global Supply Chain and Sustainability.
- Kautex developed an application to assist customers in estimating the carbon impact of Kautex-designed products for product life cycle assessment.
- Sophie Louis serves as global director, Energy and Sustainability at Kautex.
- Kautex is a Tier One automotive supplier with more than 30 plants in 14 countries.
- The company designs, develops, and manufactures traditional and hybrid fuel systems, advanced cleaning solutions for assisted and autonomous driving, engine camshafts, and plastic industrial packaging solutions.
- Kautex is expanding its portfolio to offer smart products and data-driven services, including smart fuel systems, thermoplastic composite, and composite-metal hybrid battery systems.
