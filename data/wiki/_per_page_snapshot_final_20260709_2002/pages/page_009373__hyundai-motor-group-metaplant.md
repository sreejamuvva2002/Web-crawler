# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hyundai Makes a Historic Investment in Georgia’s Clean Transportation Future - Southern Alliance for Clean Energy (SACE)](https://cleanenergy.org/news/hyundai-makes-a-historic-investment-in-georgias-clean-transportation-future/)  ·  `page_009373`

## Facts

- The Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant is located in Ellabell, Georgia.
- The plant represents the largest economic development project in Georgia’s history.
- Vehicles produced at the South Georgia plant will be shipped across the country and around the world.
- The facility provides local community opportunities including new jobs, workforce training opportunities, and exposure to the growing electric mobility sector.
- The project helps advance the clean energy transition in Georgia.
- SACE was invited to tour the facility on behalf of former Georgia Public Service Commissioner Tim Echols.
- Tim Echols created and previously managed the EV Braintrust.
- The EV Braintrust is a collaborative initiative that brings together EV stakeholders, partners, and industry leaders to discuss advancements in EV technology, charging infrastructure, and workforce development.
- The factory tour highlighted a highly coordinated balance between human workers ("meta pros") and advanced automation/robotics.
- Human employees lead operations and manage different stations across the floor.
- Robots handle heavy lifting, moving car parts, and repetitive labor-intensive tasks.
- A Boston Dynamics robotic dog was used to check engineers' work before components moved to the next assembly stage.
- Hyundai representatives emphasized that their investment in EV manufacturing is a long-term commitment.
- The decision to build the plant was made in May 2022.
- The Inflation Reduction Act was passed and signed into law in August 2022, after the decision to build the plant.
- Hyundai announced it remains committed to electrification and maintaining its presence in Georgia regardless of political changes.
- Prior to the plant's official opening, there was initial skepticism among local residents.
- Three tragic workplace deaths occurred prior to the plant's official opening, creating concern and mistrust within the community.
- The facility includes an on-site emergency medical center designed to respond quickly to workplace incidents.
- SACE utilizes a Hyundai IONIQ 5 for EV outreach efforts.
- The Hyundai IONIQ 5 is used at ride-and-drive events and community showcases where people can experience electric vehicles firsthand.
- The tour provided visibility into the assembly process of the Hyundai IONIQ 5.
