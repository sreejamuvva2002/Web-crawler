# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Yamaha hiring over 100 new employees in Newnan – Winters Media](https://www.wintersmedia.net/yamaha-hiring-over-100-new-employees-in-newnan/)  ·  `page_015654`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America (YMMC) announced plans to immediately hire over 100 new employees.
- The hiring will assist in many assembly line functions to keep pace with growing demand for recreational products assembled at its Newnan, Ga. facility.
- Based in Coweta County, YMMC designs and assembles four key product lines: Golf Cars, All Terrain Vehicles (ATV’s), Personal Watercraft, and Recreational Off-road Vehicles (ROVs) or Side-by-Side vehicles.
- Bob Brown, Vice President of Finance and Operations Support, stated that demand for outdoor products increased as people sought family and friends activities while maintaining social distance.
- YMMC is seeking eager new talent to immediately join the team and assemble products for customers.
- Brown noted that Yamaha's worldwide reputation for quality, reliability, and durability stems from employee dedication and skill, and the company is expanding production capacity through new team members.
- Trae Westmoreland, President of the Coweta County Development Authority, described YMMC as the largest private employer in Coweta County and an essential community partner for over 30 years.
- Westmoreland expressed excitement about increases in labor productivity and standard of living driven by the company's growth.
- Ronnie Clotfelter, Chairman of the Coweta County Development Authority, stated that YMMC has been an integral stakeholder providing a solid foundation for Coweta County for many years.
- YMMC is looking to fill open positions for assemblers, material handlers, welders, certified forklift operators, and more.
