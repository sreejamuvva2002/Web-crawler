# Manufacturing Georgia

## Facts

- Manufacturing Georgia is the result of a collaborative effort involving many stakeholders who are deeply invested in the success of Georgia’s manufacturing industry.
- The Georgia Association of Manufacturers (GAM) launched an initiative in 2023 to address outdated perceptions of manufacturing and increase the availability of skilled workers.
- The initiative aims to engage future workers, especially students and young professionals, by changing the narrative around the industry.
- GAM formed a strategic partnership with Unmudl, a Skills-to-Jobs Marketplace renowned for connecting learners with employers and focusing on mid-level technician skills.
- Together, GAM and Unmudl developed a platform that blends social media-like capabilities with cutting-edge technology to directly connect manufacturers with students, job seekers, and the broader public.
- The full rollout of Manufacturing Georgia is scheduled for 2025.
