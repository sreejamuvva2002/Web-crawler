## Facts

- OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc. is a privately held corporation located in Rome, Georgia.
- The company delivers specialized off-the-road (OTR) tires, wheels, mounted tire/wheel assemblies, and rubber tracks to Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM), resellers, wholesale distributors, and aftermarket customers.
- OTR Wheel Engineering specializes in manufacturing, distributing, and engineering off-the-road tire and wheel products for its customers.
- The company can uniquely manufacture orders in-house or outsource them to various domestic and global suppliers.
- OTR’s manufacturing, sourcing, and integration capabilities make it a competitive and cost-efficient single-source provider for a range of off-the-road tire and wheel products.
- OTR operates as a one-stop shop for all tire, wheel, and assembly components.
- The company offers Just in Time (JIT) delivery programs designed to reduce on-site material inventory.
- OTR is capable of global integration, including importing and exporting.
- The company maintains sales and service facilities throughout the U.S., Canada, Europe, South Africa, and China, with additional affiliates in Australia and South America.
- In 2013, OTR Wheel Engineering was ranked on Inc. 500's list of the 5000 fastest-growing companies.
- The company has been in business since 1987.
- Languages spoken within the company include English, French, Spanish, and Mandarin Chinese.
- Accepted credit cards for transactions include Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover.
