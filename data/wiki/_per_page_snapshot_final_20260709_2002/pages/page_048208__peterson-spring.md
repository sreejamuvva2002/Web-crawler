## Facts

- Peterson Spring is part of the Peterson American Corporation, an international group of spring makers.
- It is the largest privately owned spring manufacturer in North America.
- The company offers a broad array of springs and related components, including engine valve springs, compression and extension springs, torsion springs, coil springs, transmission rings, retaining rings, snap rings, multiform clips, wire forms, hose clamps, and stampings (four-slide and flat wire), along with a growing list of patented components.
- Its customer base spans automotive OEM and powertrain applications, chassis, and other sectors such as appliances, agriculture, lawn and garden machinery, medical equipment, furniture, heavy equipment, marine, and auto sport.
- The company is headquartered in Southfield, Michigan.
- Peterson Spring operates manufacturing plants and specialty operations across the United States, Canada, Mexico, and England.
- The company employs 201-500 people.
- It emphasizes an integrated design and manufacturing capability to support projects across multiple industries.
