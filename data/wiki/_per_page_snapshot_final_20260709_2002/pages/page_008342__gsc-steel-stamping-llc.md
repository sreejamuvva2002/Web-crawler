# GSC Steel Stamping, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-01-30

**Source:** [GSC Steel Stamping, LLC Propels Forward as One of the World's Largest Minority-Owned Automotive Stamping Plant - PR.com](https://www.pr.com/press-release/905100)  ·  `page_008342`

## Facts

- GSC Steel Stamping, LLC is a minority- and family-owned automotive parts manufacturer.
- The company announced the successful acquisition of Dixien, LLC's assets in June 2023.
- GSC has a 43-year history of automotive parts stamping.
- The company is led by CEO Vincent Henderson.
- Vincent Henderson previously orchestrated the turnaround of two award-winning automotive stamping operations in Michigan.
- Those Michigan operations were consistently recognized by customers for their excellence in overall performance, quality, and on-time delivery.
- GSC currently operates a 200,000 sq. ft. facility in Lake City, GA.
- GSC's current facility is recognized as the largest minority-owned automotive stamping plant globally.
- The company provides Body in White frame assembly brackets to leading Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) and tier one suppliers.
- GSC offers comprehensive services including metal stamping, engineering, welded assemblies, and tooling.
- The company plans to relocate from its current 200,000 square foot facility to a sprawling 500,000+ square foot state-of-the-art facility by the end of the year.
- GSC's plant operations management team possesses over 200 years of combined stamping experience.
- The company is poised for remarkable growth by the year 2026.
