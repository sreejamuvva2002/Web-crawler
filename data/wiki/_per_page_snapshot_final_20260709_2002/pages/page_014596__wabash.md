# Wabash

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-25

**Source:** [WNC Press Release: Wabash Expands Southeast Footprint to Strength...](https://marketchameleon.com/PressReleases/i/2174793/WNC/wabash-expands-southeast-footprint-to-strengthen-customer)  ·  `page_014596`

## Facts

- Wabash (NYSE: WNC) announced a significant expansion of its network in the Southeast to strengthen customer access and service in the greater Atlanta region.
- The expansion consists of three strategic moves: opening a new Parts & Service center, expanding its dealer relationship with Fleetco, and adding CS Truck and Trailer to its Preferred Partner Network.
- Dave Hill serves as vice president of Parts & Services at Wabash.
- Wabash is building an extensive partner ecosystem that puts parts, service, and support closer to customers.
- The Atlanta expansions are intended to mean faster turnaround, more coverage, and better reliability for fleets operating across Georgia, Alabama, South Carolina, and Tennessee.
- The new Atlanta-area Parts and Services center provides faster access to parts, service, and truck body upfitting.
- The Atlanta site will offer Wabash's Ready-to-Mount (RTM) truck bodies for dry freight and platform applications with rapid turnaround times for in-stock chassis.
- The Atlanta site joins existing Wabash service centers in California, Florida, Ohio, Pennsylvania, and Texas.
- Doors to the new Atlanta center opened September 18, 2025.
- Fleetco, a long-standing Wabash dealer, will now represent Wabash in the greater Atlanta market with trailer sales.
- Bill Tirrill is president at Fleetco Inc.
- Fleetco has more than 14 years of experience working closely with Wabash.
- The expansion allows Fleetco to expand its sales force across the region and provide clients immediate access to new and used trailers.
- Fleetco aims to improve the expertise and commitment clients expect from the Fleetco/Wabash team.
- Wabash added three CS Truck and Trailer locations to its Preferred Partner Network in Georgia.
- The Preferred Partner Network brings expert service, maintenance, and Wabash Genuine Parts to customers through authorized third-party dealerships.
- The Preferred Partner Network model is central to Wabash's strategy of making high-quality parts more accessible through a seamless national network.
- Expanding the dealer and partner network is critical to delivering the speed and service customers expect as Wabash's customer base grows.
- Atlanta serves as an example of Wabash's strategy combining service centers, dealer partners, and the Preferred Partner Network to increase coverage and elevate the customer experience.
- Wabash combines physical and digital technologies to deliver innovative, end-to-end solutions that optimize supply chains across transportation, logistics, and infrastructure markets.
- Wabash is headquartered in Lafayette, Indiana.
- Wabash designs, manufactures, and services an extensive range of products supporting first-to-final mile operations, including dry and refrigerated trailers and truck bodies, platform trailers, tank trailers, and structural composites.
- Through the Wabash Marketplace and Wabash Parts, customers gain access to a nationwide parts and service network, Trailers as a Service (TaaS), and advanced tools designed to streamline operations and drive growth.
