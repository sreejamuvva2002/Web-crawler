# Stryten Energy LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-09-12

**Source:** [View Press Releases | Battery Power Online](https://www.batterypoweronline.com/view-press-releases/entry/4732/?gvid=9973)  ·  `page_050561`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC is a U.S.-based energy storage solutions provider headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Stryten Energy was awarded part of a five-year contract by the Department of Defense for submarine valve regulated lead acid (SVRLA) batteries.
- Mike Judd serves as Chief Executive Officer and President of Stryten Energy.
- Stryten’s E-Series SVRLA AGM battery technology is cost-effective and virtually maintenance-free.
- The E-Series SVRLA AGM battery technology meets the U.S. Navy’s requirements.
- Stryten Energy will deliver the batteries and components to the U.S. Navy starting in the fall of 2023 through the summer of 2028.
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military and Government sectors.
- Stryten Energy's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers and energy performance management software.
- Stryten Energy powers applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains and trucks.
- Stryten Energy is backed by more than a century of industry expertise.
