# Anovion Technologies

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-materials|Battery Materials]]  ·  **Location:** Chicago, Illinois, Decatur County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Anovion Technologies is a U.S.-based advanced materials company that supplies premium synthetic graphite anode material for lithium-ion batteries used in electric vehicles and other applications. The company announced a $800 million investment to build a 1,500,000-sq-ft manufacturing facility in Bainbridge, Georgia, creating more than 400 jobs.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Supplier of synthetic graphite anode material - a critical component for EV lithium-ion batteries.
