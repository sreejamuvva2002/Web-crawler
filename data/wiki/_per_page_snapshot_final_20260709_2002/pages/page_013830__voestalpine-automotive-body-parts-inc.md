# Voestalpine Automotive Body Parts Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-06-24

**Source:** [Voestalpine to invest USD 11 million in Alabama facility - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/news/195051)  ·  `page_013830`

## Facts

- Austrian automotive supplier Voestalpine Automotive Body Parts Inc. announced plans on June 21 to open a facility.
- The investment amount for the new facility is USD 11.1 million.
- The facility will be located in Birmingham, Alabama.
- The project is expected to create 42 jobs.
- Voestalpine will produce a variety of automotive components at the site.
- Products will include stamped and hot formed parts.
- The facility size is 52,000 square feet.
