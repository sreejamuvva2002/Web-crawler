## Facts

- Great Dane Trailers is the No. 1 trailer manufacturer in the world.
- The company traces its origins to humble beginnings in 1900 as a small steel fabricating company.
- Great Dane manufactures dry vans, refrigerated trailers, and platform trailers.
- The company's corporate headquarters is located in Chicago, IL.
- Great Dane maintains an additional corporate office in Savannah, GA.
- The company operates a parts distribution center.
- Great Dane has nine strategically located manufacturing plants throughout the U.S.
- The company distributes trailers and aftermarket parts across North and South America through an extensive network of company-owned branches and full-line independent dealers.
- Parts-only independent dealers are also utilized for aftermarket parts distribution.
- Dean Engelage serves as President of Great Dane Trailers.
- Rick Mullininx holds the title of Executive Vice President of Engineering at Great Dane Trailers.
- Bob Brege holds the titles of Vice President and Vice President Procurement at Great Dane Trailers.
- Tony Wiese works as a Plant Manager for Great Dane Trailers.
- Brad Harvey also serves as a Plant Manager at Great Dane Trailers.
