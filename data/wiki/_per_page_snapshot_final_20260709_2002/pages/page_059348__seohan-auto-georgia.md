# Seohan Auto Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Seohan Auto Georgia Manufacturing Plant | Projects | Choate Construction](https://choateco.com/project/seohan-auto-georgia-manufacturing-plant/)  ·  `page_059348`

## Facts

- Seohan Auto Georgia was designed and built on a 64-acre property with the intent to be expanded.
- The construction team conducted mass grading onsite, added five retention ponds, concrete paving for the loading dock and truck court, asphalt parking lots, a fire road loop, and landscaping.
- The site can facilitate expansion of the manufacturing plant by 300,000 square feet.
- The facility includes two 3,000-amp main distribution panels and a robust fire loop system to support the current building and future expansion.
- Manufacturing facility staff and visitors are greeted by a two-story open-concept lobby with an eye-catching glass staircase.
- The spacious office area includes two multi-function conference rooms, open office spaces, a cafeteria, bathrooms, and locker rooms.
- The space also features a server room with a raised access floor and two independent, dedicated cooling systems, training rooms, and a QC lab.
- The manufacturing project includes six production lines, consisting of two for fabrication and four for assembly.
- There are seven loading docks designated for the company's fleet of vehicles.
- The team constructed racking systems equipped with full automated picking systems.
- Dedicated electrical circuits were installed specifically for charging EV forklifts and tuggers.
- An Early Suppression Fast Response (ESFR) fire sprinkler system was installed with a dedicated transformer for the fire pump.
- A gas generator was installed to serve as a backup for emergency systems.
- The team constructed a processed water system for cooling and cleaning all products and equipment.
- A compressed air system was built to service the production lines and paint shop.
