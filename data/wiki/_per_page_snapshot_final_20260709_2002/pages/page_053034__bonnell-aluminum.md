# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-01

**Source:** [Tredegar Announces Bonnell Aluminum Leadership Changes](https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/stocks/articles/tredegar-announces-bonnell-aluminum-leadership-200500111.html)  ·  `page_053034`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation.
- President Brook Hamilton will retire in July 2026 after more than two decades of leadership and service at Bonnell Aluminum.
- Carl Czarnik has been promoted to Senior Vice President and General Manager of Bonnell, effective June 15, 2026.
- Mr. Hamilton led Bonnell since 2013, following a career with the company that began in 2005.
- Mr. Czarnik joined Bonnell in 2019 as Vice President of Operations.
- Prior to joining Bonnell, Mr. Czarnik served as Vice President of Operations at Gerdau Long Steel and Vice President of Human Resources for Gerdau North America.
- Dr. Arijit (Bapi) DasGupta serves as president and CEO of Tredegar Corporation.
- Bonnell Aluminum manufactures custom-fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions serving the building and construction, automotive and transportation, consumer durables, machinery and equipment, and electrical and distribution markets.
- Branded product lines include TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum and Futura Transitions by Bonnell Aluminum.
- The company operates manufacturing facilities in Newnan GA, Carthage TN, Niles MI, Elkhart IN, and Clearfield UT.
