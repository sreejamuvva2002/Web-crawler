## Facts

- YTC America Inc. (YTCA) is the US-based advanced materials and energy research center of Yazaki Corporation headquartered in Susono, Japan.
- YTCA is engaged in fundamental and applied research to create innovative materials solutions that enable Yazaki to execute on its existing technology roadmap and to expand into new businesses.
- Research projects cover metal alloys, polymers, composites, energy storage devices, and printed electronics.
- The facility is located at 3401 Calle Tecate, Camarillo, CA 93012.
