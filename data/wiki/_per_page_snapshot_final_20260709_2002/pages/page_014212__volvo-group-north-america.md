# Volvo Group North America

## Facts

- Martin Weissburg serves as chairman of Volvo Group North America and president of Mack Trucks.
- The National Association of Manufacturers (NAM) announced Martin Weissburg's appointment to its Board of Directors.
- Volvo Group North America is headquartered in Greeensboro, NC.
- Volvo Group North America employs 11,500 people in the United States.
- Volvo Group North America operates 11 manufacturing facilities in seven states.
- Volvo Group North America offers trucks, buses, construction equipment, power solutions for marine and industrial applications, financing, and services that increase customer uptime and productivity.
- The National Association of Manufacturers runs the "Creators Wanted" campaign to inspire Americans to pursue careers in modern manufacturing.
- The "Creators Wanted" campaign aims to cut the skills gap by 600,000 workers by 2025.
- The "Creators Wanted" campaign supports Manufacturing Institute programs for students, women, veterans, and underrepresented communities.
- NAM President and CEO Jay Timmons stated that manufacturers will continue to play a pivotal role in economic recovery and long-term American renewal.
- The Volvo Group was founded in 1927 and is headquartered in Gothenburg, Sweden.
- The Volvo Group employs nearly 100,000 people globally and serves customers in more than 190 markets.
- In 2019, the Volvo Group’s global net sales amounted to about $45.7 billion.
- Manufacturing in the U.S. employs more than 12.23 million men and women and contributes $2.37 trillion to the U.S. economy annually.
