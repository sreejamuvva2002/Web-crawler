# Woodbridge Foam Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Strategic Innovation Fund (SIF): $20M for Woodbridge | Ryan](https://funding.ryan.com/blog/government-funding/strategic-innovation-fund-woodbridge-success/)  ·  `page_015254`

## Facts

- Woodbridge Foam Corporation is based out of Mississauga, Ontario.
- The company is a global systems solution provider specializing in fully-integrated foam solutions.
- Its foam solutions include polyurethane, polypropylene, polystyrene, and polyethylene.
- These solutions are destined for both automotive and non-automotive markets.
- Woodbridge has been made the recipient of $20,000,000 in automotive funding from the Strategic Innovation Fund.
- The Strategic Innovation Fund (SIF) is a Canadian government funding program that offers industrial and technology-based businesses financial support to fuel company expansion and R&D of processes, products, and services.
- Funding facilitates the growth of innovative firms.
- Funding is supporting a modernization project and the centralization of Woodbridge’s global R&D facility in Ontario.
- Woodbridge has been innovating foam and technology solutions for over 40 years.
- The company operates 67 facilities across the world.
- The company has worked with the majority of key automotive OEMs.
- Nearly 60% of all vehicles have their products and solutions integrated into them.
- Woodbridge applied for and was successfully awarded $20M in Canadian government funding from the Strategic Innovation Fund (SIF).
- Funding from SIF will help pay for upgrades at manufacturing facilities in Woodbridge and Blenheim, ON.
- The company will create a Centre of Excellence to centralize its global research and development initiatives.
- R&D activities include advanced materials and product design of molded foam products like armrests and car seats.
- By modernizing the manufacturing plants and building the R&D centre, Woodbridge will be better equipped to meet market demands for products.
- The company will expand its collaboration with high schools and post-secondary institutions.
- 110 new jobs will be created.
- 727 jobs will be maintained.
- The Strategic Innovation Fund is uniquely targeted at Canada’s industrial and technology sectors.
- It offsets the costs of commercialization activities and R&D.
- It helps companies implement advanced technologies to improve products and services.
- The Strategic Innovation Fund provides up to 50% of eligible project costs with a minimum funding request of $10 million in business expansion funding.
- There are five streams of the Strategic Innovation Fund: Research, Development, and Commercialization; Business Growth and Expansion; Investment Attraction and Retention; Collaborative Technology Development and Demonstration; National Innovation Ecosystems Collaboration.
