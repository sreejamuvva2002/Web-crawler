# Hyundai Motor Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-04

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group rolls out hydrogen trucks at Georgia plant | Trucks, Parts, Service](https://www.truckpartsandservice.com/alternative-power/hydrogen-fuel-cell/article/15709667/hyundai-motor-group-rolls-out-hydrogen-trucks-at-georgia-plant)  ·  `page_009182`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group's Metaplant America deployed 21 XCIENT hydrogen fuel cell electric trucks for logistics operations at its Bryan County, Ga., facility.
- The deployment was announced on Wednesday.
- Oscar Kwon serves as CEO of HMGMA.
- HMGMA is committed to creating sustainable transportation solutions.
- The Hyundai XCIENT hydrogen fuel cell trucks that support the Metaplant release zero emissions.
- The trucks offer a cleaner alternative to traditional freight logistics methods used at other manufacturing facilities.
- The trucks will transport vehicle parts from suppliers across the region to the plant.
- During the initial rollout, XCIENT trucks will transport parts between suppliers and the on-site Consolidation Center.
- The truck operations are planned to expand later to a broader network.
- Earlier this year, Hyundai Motor Group announced HTWO, a new hydrogen value chain business brand.
- HTWO encompasses Hyundai's businesses and affiliates, enabling each stage of the entire hydrogen value chain from production to storage to transportation to utilization.
- HTWO represents hydrogen and humanity, two main pillars of Hyundai's hydrogen business.
- Jim Park serves as senior vice president of commercial vehicle and hydrogen business development for Hyundai Motor North America.
- HMGMA's deployment of XCIENT trucks for logistics and operation is one of the main initiatives of HTWO.
- The deployment delivers on Hyundai's strategy and vision to showcase a closed-loop hydrogen logistics ecosystem.
- The initiative aims to improve the environmental sustainability of manufacturing operations.
