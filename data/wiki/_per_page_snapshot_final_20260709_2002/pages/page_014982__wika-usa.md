# WIKA USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [WIKA USA Selected as Large Manufacturer of the Year - WIKA blog](https://blog.wika.us/wika-insight/wika-usa-wins-gwinnett-countys-manufacturer-of-the-year-award/?doing_wp_cron=1672189749.1420190334320068359375)  ·  `page_014982`

## Facts

- Partnership Gwinnett and Gwinnett Technical College selected WIKA USA as Large Manufacturer of the Year for 2021.
- Georgia Power nominated WIKA USA for the 2021 Movers & Makers Manufacturing and Supply Chain Awards in the Large Manufacturers (200+ employees) category.
- WIKA USA was a finalist among 14 other excellent companies for the award.
- WIKA USA has over 800 employees.
- In 2020, WIKA USA manufactured pressure transducers for anesthesia machines, pressure gauges for oxygen manifolds, and pressure sensors for ventilators.
- WIKA USA's headquarters is located in Lawrenceville, Gwinnett County.
- WIKA USA collaborates with Gwinnett Technical College on programs in welding and management.
- Business students at Gwinnett Technical College study WIKA USA's lean manufacturing and Kaizen approach.
- Gwinnett Technical College assists WIKA USA with customer-service training.
- WIKA USA has close ties with Georgia Gwinnett College, which is the alma mater of two of its graduate trainees.
- WIKA USA participates in civic initiatives including Toys for Tots, canned food drives, Bed Race to end homelessness, and From Hunger to Hope during work hours.
- Companies nominated for the award were judged on nine criteria: economic impact, innovation, operational history, community involvement, educational partnership, environmental/safety efforts, work-life balance, employee education, and employee recognition programs.
- WIKA USA received high marks across all nine judging criteria.
- Zippia.com named WIKA USA the #1 Top Overall company to work for in Lawrenceville.
- WIKA USA is a family-owned company with a diverse workforce that includes a sizable portion of first-generation immigrants.
- Andrew Carnes serves as Vice President of Economic Development at Gwinnett Partnership.
- Brent Shadix serves as Vice President for Human Resources at WIKA USA.
- Drew Firestone serves as Vice President of Strategy and Marketing at WIKA USA.
- Todd Gardner serves as President and Head of Americas at WIKA Group.
