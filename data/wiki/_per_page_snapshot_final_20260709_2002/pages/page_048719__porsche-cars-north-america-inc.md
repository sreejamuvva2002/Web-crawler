# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2020-04-17

**Source:** [Porsche Taycan 4S Arrives At U.S. Dealers - Cerebral-Overload](https://cerebral-overload.com/2020/04/porsche-taycan-4s-arrives-at-u-s-dealers/)  ·  `page_048719`

## Facts

- The 2020 Porsche Taycan 4S joins the Taycan Turbo and Turbo S at dealerships in preparation for customer deliveries.
- Klaus Zellmer serves as President and CEO of PCNA.
- The new Taycan 4S sits right at the heart of Porsche's electrified model line-up, delivering effortless performance and engaging dynamics expected of a Porsche sports car with the space and connectivity that make it viable for everyday use.
- Dealers have developed innovative ways to support customers during challenging times.
- The first of the Taycan 4S’s to arrive in the US will be delivered by dealers to awaiting customers in the coming days.
- The jurors of the World Car of the Year Awards gave the Taycan two awards, a first for Porsche: ‘World Performance Car of the Year’ and ‘World Luxury Car of the Year.’
- Powering the Taycan 4S is the 93.4-kWh Performance Battery Plus that allows its dual motors to generate up to 562 hp (420kW) using its overboost function and when launch control is activated.
- When configured in this way, it can accelerate from a standstill to 60 mph in just 3.8 seconds.
- Its top track speed is 155 mph, made possible through its two-speed gearbox.
- The EPA estimated range for the Taycan 4S is 203 miles.
- Supplemental third party range testing conducted by AMCI will be available on Porsche's website.
- The Taycan was unveiled in September of 2019 and the global debut of the Taycan 4S, and subsequent media launch followed at the Los Angeles Auto Show in November.
- Customers can collect their cars in person where feasible, or receive them via contactless home delivery as part of the innovative ‘Porsche at Your Service’ program for online sales, vehicle collection, delivery, and servicing flexibility.
- The Taycan was designed and engineered from the ground up by a new team at Porsche, and built in a completely new factory located in Stuttgart-Zuffenhausen.
- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) is headquartered at One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA.
- PCNA was established in 1984 and is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster and 718 Cayman; Macan and Cayenne; Panamera; and Taycan.
- PCNA has been headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, since 1998.
- PCNA's Atlanta headquarters is home to the first Porsche Experience Center in North America featuring a module-based 1.6 mile driver development track, business center, and fine dining restaurant, 356.
- PCNA supports 192 independently owned and operated Porsche dealerships in the U.S., including supplying parts, service, marketing, and training.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Porsche boasts a racing heritage that includes some 30,000-plus motorsport wins to date.
