## Facts

- Jefferson Southern Corporation is a Tier 1 supplier of structural steel parts to Honda.
- The company is currently in the middle of an expansion of its production facility.
- The expansion will add 81,000 sq. ft. to the plant.
- The expansion includes new presses and an expanded recycling system.
- The expansion will create 40 new jobs over the next year.
- Honda and other customers are world leaders in quality and demand high-quality parts from suppliers.
- JSC maintains a reputation as a high-quality supplier of stamped and welded parts at competitive prices.
- JSC is seeking workforce participation to support efficient production for its customers.
