# DAS Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-15

**Source:** [DAS Corp. and 4 Fungi's Regenerative break ground in Metter, GA  - Grice Connect](https://www.griceconnect.com/business/das-corp-and-4-fungis-regenerative-break-ground-in-metter-ga-7835594)  ·  `page_019100`

## Facts

- Two pioneering companies, DAS Corp. and 4 Fungi's Regenerative, took the first steps toward a more sustainable tomorrow with a double groundbreaking ceremony set to create economic opportunities and foster growth in Candler County.
- The two companies officially broke ground on Wednesday, November 15, 2023, in the I-16 Industrial Park in Metter.
- Combined, the two companies will invest $62 million into Candler County.
- Combined, the two companies will create 340 local jobs.
- DAS Corp. (DAS) is a leading automotive parts company with a global presence that has chosen Metter as the location for its newest manufacturing facility.
- The new DAS facility is state-of-the-art, will bolster production capacity, and stimulate economic growth in the region.
- DAS specializes in designing and manufacturing seating systems, safety seating components, and seating structures.
- DAS is a crucial supplier for Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia (KMMG) and Hyundai Motor Group.
- The new DAS manufacturing facility is projected to create 300 new local jobs.
- The new DAS manufacturing facility will contribute over $35 million in investments to Candler County.
- Sean Kim, Chief Operating Officer at DAS, stated that the strategic location near prominent cities like Savannah and Macon, facilitating convenient access, played a pivotal role in their decision-making process.
- Operations at the new DAS facility are expected to commence in the latter half of 2024.
- 4 Fungi's Regenerative is a sustainably controlled environment agriculture (CEA) and food production company making waves in Metter with its new greenhouse project.
- The company's unique approach to growing mushrooms and various crops aligns with the region's sustainable agricultural practices.
- 4 Fungi's Regenerative utilizes agricultural waste, circular economy principles, and reconstituted byproducts to enhance soil quality.
- The new 4 Fungi's greenhouse is located at the Metter-Candler 1-16 Industrial Park.
- The 4 Fungi's greenhouse project represents a $27 million investment.
- The 4 Fungi's greenhouse project will create nearly 50 local jobs.
- Grant Anderson, CEO of 4 Fungi's Regenerative, stated that their focus is on providing regional food resilient to global market volatility, nurturing community and soil health, and growing the local economy.
