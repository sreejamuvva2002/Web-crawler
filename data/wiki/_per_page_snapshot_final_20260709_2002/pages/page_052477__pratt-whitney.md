# Pratt & Whitney

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [GA: Pratt & Whitney Georgia Expansion Takes Off at Paris Air Show, With 400 New Jobs in Muscogee Co. | Trade and Industry Development](https://www.tradeandindustrydev.com/region/georgia/news/ga-pratt-whitney-georgia-expansion-takes-paris-air-31914)  ·  `page_052477`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced Pratt & Whitney's expansion at the 2023 Paris Air Show in France.
- Pratt & Whitney is an RTX business specializing in aircraft and helicopter engines.
- The expansion project will create nearly 400 new jobs.
- The project brings $206 million in investment to Muscogee County.
- Georgia exported $9.2 billion worth of aerospace products in 2022, ranking as a top five exporter in the U.S.
- The Pratt & Whitney Columbus facility opened in 1984 and includes the Columbus Engine Center and Columbus Forge on the same campus.
- The Columbus Engine Center is located at 8987 Macon Road in Columbus.
- Columbus Forge is located at 8801 Midland Road in Columbus.
- The Columbus Engine Center maintains Pratt & Whitney GTF™ engines for the Embraer E-Jets E2, Airbus A220, and Airbus A320neo aircraft families, along with F117 and F100 military engines.
- Columbus Forge produces compressor airfoils and other critical components for Pratt & Whitney’s commercial and military engines.
- Pratt & Whitney has operated in Georgia for nearly 40 years, growing from a small manufacturing facility to a large overhaul center.
- The Columbus business currently employs approximately 2,000 people.
- Shane Eddy serves as President of Pratt & Whitney.
- The expansion aligns with Pratt & Whitney's Industry 4.0 strategy to expand workforce and company capabilities in Columbus.
- The expansion will add 81,000 square feet to the Columbus Engine Center.
- The expansion will increase overhaul capacity to 400 GTF engines per year.
- The expansion is expected to be completed by 2028.
- The company will hire for engineers, mechanics, and operators roles.
- Columbus Mayor Skip Henderson stated that economic vitality for residents and area businesses remains a top priority.
- Heath Schondelmayer serves as Chair of the Board for the Development Authority of Columbus.
- Christy Bozeman is the Regional Project Manager representing the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s Global Commerce team on this project.
- The project involved partnerships with the Greater Columbus Chamber of Commerce, Georgia Power, and Georgia Quick Start.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson noted Pratt & Whitney is a long-time partner for the state and other industry leaders, including Atlanta-based Delta Airlines.
- In February 2023, Delta TechOps announced plans to open a new engine repair shop at Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport to support Pratt & Whitney GTF engines.
- The Delta TechOps after-market facility will service PW1100G and PW1500G engines used in Delta’s Airbus A321neo and A220 airliners.
