## Facts

- Taiwanese electric vehicle company Ahamani EV Technology Co., Ltd. has announced plans to enter India’s electric vehicle market by establishing a large-scale battery manufacturing plant.
- The strategic investment aims to capitalize on India’s growing demand for electric mobility, which is bolstered by supportive government policies and an increasing consumer shift towards eco-friendly transportation options.
- Ahamani EV Technology is actively seeking technology transfer partnerships with leading Indian EV companies.
- The company is in advanced discussions with 3-4 major players in the Indian market.
- These collaborations are intended to foster knowledge sharing and resource pooling, accelerating the development of advanced EV technologies tailored to the unique needs of the Indian market.
- Ahamani has over two decades of experience in EV innovation.
- The company specializes in battery technology, motor systems, and smart fleet management.
- Ahamani’s renowned UrDa AIOT Mobility System effectively manages electric two-wheeler fleets.
- By building a local manufacturing facility, Ahamani aims to enhance India’s self-reliance in EV production, generate employment opportunities, and contribute to the local economy.
- Tzu Chi Kung is the Founder & CEO of Ahamani.
- Tzu Chi Kung stated that the company has been studying the Indian EV market for the past five years and sees great potential.
- Tzu Chi Kung noted that Ahamani’s strong R&D capabilities and successful track record make them ready to work with Indian partners and promote electric vehicles in the country.
- Ahamani’s initiatives in battery manufacturing and technology partnerships are poised to play a significant role in advancing India’s journey towards sustainable electric mobility.
