## Facts

- The PCNA campus underwent a $50 million expansion, completed in late 2023.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp visited the Porsche Cars North America headquarters at One Porsche Drive during an all-staff meeting.
- During the visit, Governor Kemp and PCNA President and CEO Timo Resch discussed Porsche’s new cars, electrification, Porsche’s investment in Georgia, and a new solar array installed at the Porsche Experience Center in Atlanta.
- First Lady Marty Kemp accompanied Governor Kemp on the visit to One Porsche Drive.
- Governor Kemp last visited a Porsche site in January 2020 when he toured the factory in Zuffenhausen.
- Porsche Cars North America headquarters has been located in Atlanta since 1998.
- PCNA will celebrate its 40th anniversary in the United States in September.
