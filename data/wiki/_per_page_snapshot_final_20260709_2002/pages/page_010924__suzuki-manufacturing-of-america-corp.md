## Facts

- The Suzuki factory on Technology Parkway manufactures in the neighborhood of 60-plus ATVs per day.
- Approximately 60% of the ATVs made in Rome remain in the U.S. while the remainder are sold internationally.
- Kelly Floyd, a public relations representative with Suzuki, talks to a group of Rome Floyd Chamber members during a tour of the Suzuki plant on Wednesday.
- A show model is one of several located in the Suzuki showroom at the plant on Technology Parkway.
- Suzuki’s website states: “As the inventor of the 4-wheel ATV, you might say we know a thing or four about what it takes to build them right — and it starts right here on American soil, at our Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corp. factory in Rome, GA.”
