# Georgia Transportation Alliance

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2024-11

**Source:** [Economic Development Expert Tapped to Lead Georgia Transportation Alliance - Augusta CEO](https://augustaceo.com/features/2024/11/economic-development-expert-tapped-lead-georgia-transportation-alliance/)  ·  `page_010651`

## Facts

- The Georgia Transportation Alliance, an affiliate of the Georgia Chamber of Commerce, selected Joseph Santoro to serve as its new Executive Director.
- Joseph Santoro starts his new position on December 6.
- Prior to joining the Georgia Transportation Alliance, Santoro worked for four years at the Council for Quality Growth as Director of Policy & Government Affairs.
- Over the last four years at the Council for Quality Growth, Santoro worked on local, regional, and state policies related to infrastructure planning, legislative initiatives, regulatory issues, land use, transportation, and environmental planning impacting the development and growth industry.
- Santoro has over 10 years of government affairs experience working for membership-based organizations in metro Atlanta evaluating local, state, and federal policy priorities.
- Santoro earned a B.S. in Public Policy from the Georgia Institute of Technology.
- Santoro has been a Georgia Chamber Government Affairs Committee member since 2014 through various roles.
- Santoro is a 2023 graduate of the Atlanta Regional Commission Regional Leadership Institute.
- Nick Juliano, Partner at Impact Public Affairs and Chair of the Georgia Transportation Alliance, commented on the announcement.
- The Georgia Transportation Alliance is the state’s only organization focused solely on identifying and promoting long-term transportation solutions specifically connected to economic development and job creation.
- The Georgia Transportation Alliance works closely with the Georgia Chamber of Commerce, the Georgia Department of Transportation, and other key stakeholders in Georgia and Washington, D.C.
- The goal of the Georgia Transportation Alliance is ensuring an efficient and safe statewide multi-modal transportation system.
- Chris Clark, CCE, President and CEO of the Georgia Chamber of Commerce, stated that Georgia has extensive surface transportation networks, two world-class ports, and one of the largest railroad systems in the country.
- Chris Clark noted that Georgia’s transportation and logistics industry is an essential driver of record-breaking economic success.
