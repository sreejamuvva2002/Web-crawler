## Facts

- Shelbyville Battery Manufacturing announced the largest project in Shelby County (Louisville MSA) history in the winter quarter.
- The project is a state-of-the-art, 6-gigawatt-hour battery cell and module plant in an existing 1 million-square-foot building in Shelbyville.
- The $712 million plant will house almost 1,600 new workers.
