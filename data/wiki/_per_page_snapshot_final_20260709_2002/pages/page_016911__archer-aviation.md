## Facts

- Archer Aviation is an electric vertical takeoff and landing (eVTOL) aircraft developer transitioning from research and development toward commercial flight.
- The company unveiled its “Launch Edition” program to highlight tangible steps towards bringing its Midnight aircraft to market.
- Archer Aviation formed a strategic partnership with Palantir Technologies Inc. to infuse artificial intelligence into next-generation aviation.
- The partnership establishes an exclusive collaboration focused on constructing an artificial intelligence foundation for the future of next-generation aviation technologies.
- The alliance will initially leverage Palantir’s Foundry and Artificial Intelligence Platform (AIP) to significantly accelerate the scaling of Archer’s aircraft manufacturing capabilities at its cutting-edge facilities located in Georgia and Silicon Valley.
- The application of Palantir’s platforms is intended to optimize and streamline Archer’s manufacturing processes, enhancing efficiency and output as the company progresses towards high-volume production of its Midnight eVTOL aircraft.
- The partnership extends to a broader scope involving the joint development of next-generation software solutions designed to revolutionize critical aviation systems.
- Archer and Palantir engineers are slated to work in parallel to create AI-powered software aimed at significant improvements across essential aviation functions.
- These advancements include improved air traffic control for safer, more efficient airspace management, optimized ground operations to reduce congestion at vertiports and airports, and enhanced route planning for better flight paths and energy efficiency.
- The formalization of the partnership took place at Palantir's AIPCon during a public signing ceremony.
- Palantir co-founder and Chief Executive Officer Alex Karp and Archer founder and Chief Executive Officer Adam Goldstein participated in the public signing ceremony.
