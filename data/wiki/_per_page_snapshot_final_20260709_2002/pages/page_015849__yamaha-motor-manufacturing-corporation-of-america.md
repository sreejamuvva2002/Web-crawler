# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2018-03-26

**Source:** [Video: Yamaha Watercraft Celebrates One Millionth WaveRunner - The Watercraft Journal | the best resource for JetSki, WaveRunner, and SeaDoo enthusiasts and most popular Personal WaterCraft site in the world!](https://watercraftjournal.com/video-yamaha-watercraft-celebrates-one-millionth-waverunner/)  ·  `page_015849`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America (YMMC) celebrated the completion of its one millionth personal watercraft after 29 years of manufacturing WaveRunners.
- The one millionth model was a 2018 Yamaha VX Deluxe WaveRunner in Black and Azure Blue Metallic.
- The special one millionth unit will have a permanent place in Yamaha’s Newnan, Georgia manufacturing facility.
- The milestone marks the culmination of more than 29 years of manufacturing personal watercraft at the Georgia-based facility.
- The first WaveRunner was produced on Monday, November 21, 1988, at 7:00 a.m.
- Mike Chrzanowski serves as president of Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America.
- Mike Chrzanowski stated that the event marks the culmination of hard work from Georgia-based employees and noted that several team members present were there when the first WaveRunner was produced.
- Dean Burnett serves as president of Yamaha WaterCraft Group, a division of Yamaha Motor Corporation USA.
- Dean Burnett stated that producing watercraft in Georgia impacts the lives of local community employees and called the milestone a testament to their daily hard work.
- The celebration involved pausing the assembly line for a group photo around the one millionth WaveRunner.
- Yamaha WaterCraft Group is a division of Yamaha Motor Corporation USA.
- WaveRunner manufacturing traces its production process back to single sheets of fiberglass.
