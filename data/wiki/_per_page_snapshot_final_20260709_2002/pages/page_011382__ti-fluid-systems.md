# TI Fluid Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2020-03-05

**Source:** [TI Fluid Systems Thermal Products On Mustang Mach-E](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/ti-fluid-systems-thermal-products-on-mustang-mach-e/)  ·  `page_011382`

## Facts

- TI Fluid Systems is a leading global supplier of automotive fluid systems technology.
- TI Fluid Systems will provide thermal management systems to Ford’s all-new all-electric SUV, the Mustang Mach-E.
- The Mustang Mach-E vehicle arrives late in 2020.
- The Mustang Mach-E was first unveiled to the public in November last year ahead of the 2019 LA Auto Show.
- TI Fluid Systems will supply air conditioning refrigerant lines for cabin comfort.
- TI Fluid Systems will supply battery chiller lines for battery thermal management using a combination of optimized materials.
- Stefan Rau is executive VP of Fluid Carrying Systems at TI Fluid Systems.
- Stefan Rau stated that TI Fluid Systems worked with Ford’s Engineering teams on technical support, best practice design, production integration, and onsite design support.
- Stefan Rau noted that TI Fluid Systems has more than 40 years of experience in the Automotive Thermal space.
- Stefan Rau expressed excitement about working with Ford as they enter the market with the all-electric Mustang Mach-E.
- TI Fluid Systems began supplying to Ford by providing fuel lines on the first Model T in 1922.
- TI Fluid Systems has partnered on hundreds of vehicles with Ford since 1922.
- Bill Kozyra is president and CEO of TI Fluid Systems.
- Bill Kozyra stated that TI can continue to build on its 98-year relationship with Ford in supplying fluid handling products and systems for tomorrow’s electrified vehicles.
