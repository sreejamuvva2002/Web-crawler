# Fox Factory

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Fox Factory - Gainesville, GA](https://www.carrolldaniel.com/case-study/fox-factory-offices/)  ·  `page_020210`

## Facts

- Fox Factory selected Carroll Daniel to develop a new facility in Gainesville, Georgia.
- The project consisted of a 350,000 SF manufacturing facility and a 50,000 SF office component located on a 30-acre site.
- The facility houses 300 employees.
- Fox Factory offices reside on the second level of the facility, providing views down into assembly areas to offer an integrated and collaborative experience.
- The building includes a large cafeteria, locker rooms and all-hands meeting areas in addition to office space.
- Construction occurred during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic.
- Carroll Daniel navigated schedule changes and material shortages to keep the project on track and on-time.
- After completion, the Fox Factory project received three awards for pushing the boundaries of possibility for design-build project design and construction.
