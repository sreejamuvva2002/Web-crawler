## Facts

- US-based Novelis is an aluminum rolling company with headquarters in Atlanta.
- The company has operations on four continents, including 25 facilities in 11 countries.
- Novelis was formed in 2005 as a spin-off from aluminum producer Alcan.
- Novelis is currently a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited, which in turn is the flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group.
- In Brazil, Novelis operates two aluminum rolled product facilities in Santo André and Pindamonhangaba, which is also the largest aluminum rolling and recycling facility in the region.
- In October 2014, Novelis announced the closure of its Ouro Preto primary aluminum smelter in Brazil to focus operations on its core business of aluminum rolling and recycling.
- As a consequence of this decision, the company sold its power generation assets in the country, which were only used to supply power to its smelter operations.
