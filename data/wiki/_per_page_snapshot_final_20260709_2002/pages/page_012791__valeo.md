## Facts

- Valeo has been selected by a leading Chinese New Energy Vehicle (NEV) manufacturer to supply its new 5-in-1 Deep Integration Power Electronics Module for future electrified powertrains.
- The compact and cost-optimised unit integrates five core power electronics functions into a single box.
- Production of the module is set for 2026.
- The module achieves around a 20% weight reduction.
- The module has been fast-tracked from concept to production-ready in under a year.
- The module combines an inverter, OBC, DC/DC converter, power distribution unit, and eFuse into a single compact module with a shared SoC architecture.
- The module supports trends in electric vehicle development such as EE architecture consolidation, system integration, and platform-based engineering.
- The module provides simplified wiring for engineers.
- The module reduces system complexity.
- The module improves thermal and energy efficiency.
- The module lowers validation effort, which is key for next-gen EV design and scalability.
