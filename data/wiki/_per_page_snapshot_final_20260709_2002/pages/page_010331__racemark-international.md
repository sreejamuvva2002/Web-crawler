# RACEMARK International

## Facts

- RACEMARK was founded in 1964 by Bob Bailey, a Porsche of America team world-class race car driver.
- Mark Donohue partnered with Bob Bailey and joined RACEMARK in 1972.
- The company's philosophy is to design and engineer innovative interior trim parts to meet and exceed the styling expectations of international OEM customers.
- In 1973, RACEMARK became the first company in its industry to create a custom-fit carpet car mat.
- RACEMARK is recognized as the reference point for innovative and stylish car mats in the automotive market.
- High material standards, competitive manufacturing processes, and dedication to quality result in optimized product life cycle cost.
- Capabilities include high volume production as well as premium and luxury designs for customized products supporting a wide range of vehicle segments.
- Manufacturing plants are located in the United States, Germany, Hungary, and Switzerland.
- The company follows international market trends of the highest quality brands in the automotive and textile industries.
- RACEMARK delivers features and product enhancements to achieve “best in class” parts for the global automotive industry.
- The company expanded globally to supply just-in-time delivery to customer assembly plants.
- This strategy enables short lead-times at competitive prices.
- Plants are dedicated to producing automobile floor mats for premier brands in the automotive industry.
