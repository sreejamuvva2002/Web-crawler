## Facts

- ADVICS Manufacturing Ohio, Inc. is an original equipment manufacturer (OEM) supplier of disc brake calipers and control systems to the automotive industry.
- In 1989 ADVICS Manufacturing Ohio, Inc. was established when 50 Team Members began producing brake components for one vehicle model.
- Today, over 900 experienced and dedicated people produce disc brake calipers, electronic stability control units, and electric parking brakes for over 20 different vehicles for many of the world’s leading auto manufacturers.
- The company’s vision is to improve the quality of life through the evolution of vehicle safety.
- Toyota, Chrysler, Ford, Mazda, and Nissan (and their customers) trust ADVICS to produce only the highest-quality, safety-sensitive products.
- The company is continuing to grow by adding more Team Members and producing new, technology-leading brake system products designed by its parent company.
- Management and Team Members are active in the local community, sponsoring events like the Lebanon Carriage Parade & Festival and contributing to United Way and other charities.
- Team Members volunteer time for 3rd Friday and Food Drives, and contribute time, donations, and expertise as coaches and volunteers.
- The greater Lebanon area has been a great home for ADVICS-Ohio and the company is committed to giving back.
- ADVICS Manufacturing Ohio, Inc. is located at 1650 Kingsview Drive, Lebanon, OH 45036.
- The company offers competitive compensation.
- The company provides flexible benefits with low premiums covering Medical, Dental & Vision.
- The company offers a 401(k) plan and gainsharing.
- The company provides paid vacation and extensive holidays.
- Prospective new Team Members are informed that the work environment is fast-paced and dynamic, where value is placed on their ideas and commitment to provide the highest-quality products.
