# FREYR Battery

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-06

**Source:** [FREYR buys Trina solar panel factory in Texas for $340 million](https://www.solarpowerworldonline.com/2024/11/freyr-buys-trina-solar-panel-factory-in-texas-for-340-million/)  ·  `page_054996`

## Facts

- FREYR Battery is a Norwegian developer of battery cells that is entering solar panel manufacturing by acquiring the U.S. manufacturing operations of Trina Solar.
- The company previously sought to serve the energy storage and EV markets and was pursuing a gigawatt-scale battery factory in Coweta County, Georgia.
- FREYR will take control of Trina’s 5-GW solar panel assembly facility in Wilmer, Texas, paying $340 million for its U.S. manufacturing assets.
- An investor presentation details that FREYR is establishing an operations and intellectual property agreement between Trina Solar and FREYR.
- The transaction closing is expected before the end of 2024, at which point FREYR will begin efforts on domestic cell manufacturing.
- Site selection for the new facility is underway, with construction targeted to begin in the second quarter of 2025.
- The new cell manufacturing outfit is expected to support 1,800 jobs and begin production in 2026.
- Newly appointed CEO Daniel Barcelo stated that the transaction will immediately position the company as one of the leading solar manufacturing companies in the U.S.
- Barcelo noted that domestic manufacturing capacity for solar and batteries is essential for energy transition and job creation, and added that the U.S. was once the global leader in solar and can be again.
- FREYR has secured a U.S. polysilicon supply with Hemlock for its eventual cell production, establishing a platform for its U.S. solar and battery storage strategy.
- The company announced it has terminated its battery technology license with 24M Technologies, but no further news was released regarding its potential Georgia battery factory.
- Upon closing of the Trina transaction, Trina employees Mingxing Lin will join FREYR as chief strategy officer and Dave Gustafson will join as chief operating officer.
- Solar panels produced at the Texas plant will continue to carry the Trina brand, and both companies will jointly market the panels to the U.S. market.
