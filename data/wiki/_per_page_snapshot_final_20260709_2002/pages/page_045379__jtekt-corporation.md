# JTEKT Corporation

## Facts

- JTEKT is a member of the Toyota Group.
- The company formulated Vision 2030 and a Medium- to Long-term Business Plan divided into three periods: FY'21–'23, FY'24–'26, and FY'27–'30.
- The Automotive Business Unit focuses on steering systems and driveline components to support comfortable and safe driving.
- JTEKT produces rack parallel type electric power steering (RP-EPS) and TORSEN components.
- Sales for the Automotive Business Unit increased by 14.1% year-on-year.
- The Industrial and Bearings Business Unit supplies advanced low-friction torque, high-quality bearings adapted from automobiles to general industry including industrial robots, semiconductors, LCD manufacturing machinery, and wind power generation.
- Sales for the Industrial and Bearings Business Unit increased by 15.3% year-on-year.
- The Machine Tools & Manufacturing Systems Business Unit provides products that create high-productivity lines, including G1 Series CNC cylindrical grinders, gear skiving technology, and FH5000 Series spindle machining centers.
- Sales for the Machine Tools & Manufacturing Systems Business Unit increased by 16.3% year-on-year.
- JTEKT's business component ratios are 68% Automotive, 22% Industrial and Bearings, and 11% Machine Tools & Manufacturing Systems.
- The company is developing new businesses including high heat-resistant Lithium-ion Capacitors and the J-PAS fairy Power Assist Suit for nursing care use.
- The report covers the target period of April 2021 to March 2022 and was published in September 2022.
- JTEKT aims to contribute to a recycling society, prevent global warming, and provide key components supporting industry while extending machine lifespan through preventive maintenance.
- The company emphasizes human resource development focusing on problem-solving methods and IT skills, alongside diversity and compliance governance.
