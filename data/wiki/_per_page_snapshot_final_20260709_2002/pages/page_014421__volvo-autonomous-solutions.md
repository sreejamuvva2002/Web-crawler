# Volvo Autonomous Solutions

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2021-09-28

**Source:** [Volvo Autonomous Solutions reveals prototype long-haul autonomous truck for North America application - AB Volvo](https://news.cision.com/ab-volvo/r/volvo-autonomous-solutions-reveals-prototype-long-haul-autonomous-truck-for-north-america-applicatio,c3422877)  ·  `page_014421`

## Facts

- Volvo Autonomous Solutions and Aurora have achieved a milestone in their partnership to jointly develop on-highway autonomous trucks in the U.S.
- A prototype of Volvo Trucks’ flagship long-haul VNL model, integrated with Aurora Driver technology, was revealed on September 28, 2021.
- The prototype represents an important step toward launching fully autonomous Class 8 trucks commercially in North America.
- Volvo Autonomous Solutions is developing comprehensive Transport as a Service (TaaS) solutions for autonomous commercial trucks tailored to specific customer needs.
- On-highway autonomous truck applications are being designed and engineered in the U.S.
- Future production is planned at Volvo Trucks’ New River Valley Assembly Operations in Dublin, Virginia.
- The autonomous truck’s core systems build on proven safety technologies already on the Volvo VNL, including Volvo Dynamic Steering (VDS) and automated transmission (I-Shift).
- These existing technologies create a redundant safety-based solution combined with Aurora Driver technology to ensure safe operations during development.
- Peter Voorhoeve, president of Volvo Trucks North America, stated the Volvo VNL model will serve as the foundation for developing North American autonomous trucks.
- Bringing autonomous on-highway trucks to market aims to complement the transport system, meet increasing goods-moving demand, and minimize environmental impact.
- Sasko Cuklev, head of on-road solutions for Volvo Autonomous Solutions, noted autonomous solutions will be implemented where there is a purpose and makes sense, emphasizing leadership in building the new ecosystem with customers and partners.
- Volvo Autonomous Solutions and Aurora are working toward implementing their hub-to-hub transport vision in North America.
- The partners are identifying specific regions and routes to serve as initial hubs for on-road highway testing.
- Volvo Autonomous Solutions is working closely with customers to understand current and future needs, priorities, and demands in preparation for piloting the autonomous Volvo VNL in on-road, hub-to-hub transport scenarios.
- The goal includes eventual commercial adoption of the autonomous technology.
- Volvo Autonomous Solutions aims to increase development speed to support changing customer needs across segments and markets, ultimately bringing safer, more efficient, and sustainable commercial transport of goods across the U.S.
