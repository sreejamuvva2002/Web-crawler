# Club Car

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-10

## Facts

- Club Car is headquartered in Augusta, Georgia.
- President and Chief Executive Officer Mark Wagner announced his retirement effective August 1, 2025.
- Wagner served as President and CEO for the last 8 years of his 29-year career with the company.
- Club Car is owned by global investment firm Platinum Equity.
- Platinum Equity will conduct a comprehensive search for a successor with plans for a seamless transition.
- Club Car's product portfolio includes vehicles for commercial, personal, and street-legal low-speed use.
- The Club Car Vehicle Group includes the luxury electric vehicle brand Garia.
- The Club Car Vehicle Group includes the lightweight electric utility vehicle brand Melex.
- Wagner stated that current new product launches and tariff success require commitment and focus to achieve growth plans and share gain this year.
- Club Car has a history spanning over six decades in superior design and performance for small-wheel vehicles.
