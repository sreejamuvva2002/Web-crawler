# Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-28

**Source:** [Kautex Textron Wins Major BEV Contract - The EV Report](https://theevreport.com/kautex-textron-wins-major-bev-contract)  ·  `page_045458`

## Facts

- Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG, a Textron Inc. company, has announced a significant business win with a leading automotive original equipment manufacturer (OEM).
- The order is for a full-BEV (Battery Electric Vehicle) thermoplastic composite lower battery housing unit.
- The component is part of the Kautex Pentatonic battery enclosure product line.
- The thermoplastic solution offers advantages over metal, including lower cost, higher functional integration, and a reduced carbon footprint.
- This marks the second major business win for the award-winning Pentatonic product line.
- The component will be used on a major BEV platform that spans multiple vehicle brands.
- The Kautex Pentatonic product line provides lightweight and customizable solutions for the growing EV market.
- Pentatonic products are made from thermoplastic composite or composite-metal hybrids.
- These products are designed to enhance the thermal management of the battery pack.
- Their lightweight properties contribute to improved vehicle performance and an extended driving range.
- The lower battery housing is a critical safety component, designed to seal and protect the vehicle’s battery.
- It meets extensive structural requirements and stringent thermal standards, including crush and bottom impact testing, as well as both external and internal fire tests.
- Compared to conventional metal alternatives, this thermoplastic composite solution achieves a lower cost by reducing the number of components through high functional integration, resulting in a leaner bill of material.
- The material helps lower the part’s carbon footprint, while the lightweight design contributes to reducing carbon emissions during vehicle operation.
- The multi-material approach ensures excellent corrosion resistance without compromising performance over the vehicle’s lifespan.
- Joerg Rautenstrauch serves as President & CEO of Kautex.
- He noted that this order from a leading automotive OEM marks a significant milestone for the company.
- This contract is Kautex’s second Pentatonic business win.
- Its first success came in January 2023 for a full-BEV underbody protection skid plate, which entered serial production in 2024.
- The Pentatonic portfolio has received numerous accolades, including the SPE Grand Innovation Award in Europe and the Lingxuan and Gasgoo Innovation Awards in China.
- Kautex has validated the performance of its Pentatonic enclosures by deploying three on-road test vehicles in the US, Japan, and Europe.
- These test vehicles have collectively covered over 100,000 miles across various terrains and conditions.
- Kautex is a Tier One automotive supplier with 30 plants in 13 countries.
- The company is a pioneer in designing and manufacturing fuel systems, engine camshafts, and industrial packaging.
- For the new era of mobility, Kautex is expanding its portfolio to include thermoplastic composite battery systems and cleaning systems for assisted and autonomous vehicles.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company known for its global network of aircraft, defense, industrial, and finance businesses.
- Textron’s brands include Bell, Cessna, Beechcraft, Pipistrel, Jacobsen, Kautex, Lycoming, E-Z-GO, and Textron Systems.
