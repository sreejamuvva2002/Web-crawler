# Hyundai Mobis

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-09-14

**Source:** [Hyundai Mobis Announces Expansion Plan in North America for Future Mobility](https://www.prnewswire.co.uk/news-releases/hyundai-mobis-announces-expansion-plan-in-north-america-for-future-mobility-816847707.html)  ·  `page_001189`

## Facts

- Presented an expansion strategy in the North American market with its highly value-added technologies at the NAIAS 2022 Press Conference.
- Plans to expand production facilities worldwide, including a new EV components plant for prompt supply to North American automakers.
- Aims to reach about 30-40% CAGR in the North American market by 2030 with future mobility technologies.
- Hyundai Mobis (KRX 012330), the sixth largest global automotive supplier, introduced its expansion strategy in the North American market during a press conference at the North American International Auto Show (NAIAS).
- According to Axel Maschka, Executive Vice President and head of Hyundai Mobis Global OE Sales, the two keywords of the company's growth strategy are Plant Expansion and Value Expansion.
- Plans to expand its production facilities across the globe for prompt and timely supply to global automakers.
- Plans to construct more EV dedicated sites in the region and invest in EV components and battery system assemblies to allow rapid and environmentally optimized local production.
- Currently operates 44 production bases, with North American facilities located in Alabama, Georgia, Ohio, and Michigan.
- Considers North America leading the electric vehicle shift to achieve carbon neutrality and green mobility as integral to its electrification production expansion plan.
- Believes having a strong production capacity will be key to winning sales orders as the EV market continues to grow.
- Plans to leverage a new manufacturing site, combined with over ten years of expertise in producing high-quality electrification components, for continued growth in the market.
- Revealed a value expansion plan to increase its sales orders in the North American market with its value-added technologies, optimized specifically for electric and autonomous vehicles.
- Presented future mobility technologies, including the electric Complete Chassis Platform Module (eCCPM) and next-generation AR HUD.
- States that Hyundai Mobis has a wide range of innovations in its portfolio, is ready for the future of mobility, and aims to bring the future to the present and make it more valuable.
- Points to its successful mass-production of key electrification components, including the battery system for the e-GMP platform used in Hyundai and Kia electric vehicles, and the PE module.
- Shows confidence in the paradigm shift for electric vehicle platforms.
- Notes that value-added products including the eCCPM module, electrification platform, AR HUD, and lighting grilles are drawing attention from clients in North America for their versatility and innovation.
- Announced a goal of driving a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 36% in the North American market through the year 2030.
- Reported steady growth in total order volumes over the last few years, with $660 million USD in 2020, $1.4 billion USD in 2021, and $1.7 billion USD in the first half of 2022.
- Is the global no. 6 automotive supplier, headquartered in Seoul, Korea.
- Has outstanding expertise in sensors, sensor fusion in ECUs and software development for safety control.
- Products include various components for electrification, brakes, chassis and suspension, steering, airbags, lighting and automotive electronics.
- Operates R&D headquarters in Korea, with four technology centers in the United States, Germany, China and India.
