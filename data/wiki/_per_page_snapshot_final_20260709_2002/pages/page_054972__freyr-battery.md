## Facts

- Freyr Battery has decided not to proceed with the development of a large-scale electric battery factory in Atlanta's suburbs.
- Freyr is shifting its focus instead to a solar panel facility in Texas.
- The decision was first reported by Yahoo Finance.
- Freyr had initially planned a $2.6 billion investment in the battery factory.
- The canceled Atlanta plant project was expected to create over 700 jobs.
- U.S. lithium-ion accumulator export value stood at $3.3 billion in 2023.
