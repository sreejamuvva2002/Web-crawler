## Facts

- Shiroki Corporation is an automotive parts manufacturer supplying door sashes, window regulators, seat components, and mechatronics components.
- Shiroki North America, Inc., located in the state of Tennessee, reported FY2006 sales of 27,020 million yen, a 5.9% year-on-year increase.
- Sales growth at the Tennessee facility was mainly due to increased production volume of door sashes for the Nissan Altima, which began mass production in North America in the fall of 2006.
- The Tennessee facility's operating profit reached 3 million yen in FY2006, compared to a 141 million yen operating loss the previous year.
- In December 2006, Shiroki-GA L.L.C. in Georgia expanded its plant.
- Shiroki-GT L.L.C. in Tennessee started operations in March 2007 as Shiroki's third North American plant.
- The company prepared for door sash production for the Nissan Altima, with production starting in September 2006.
- Shiroki announced plans to increase U.S. production capacity by enlarging its Georgia plant for doorframe production and purchasing a closed local plant in Tennessee for functional seat components production.
- The total investment for the U.S. expansion plans, excluding equipment and molds, will be limited to approximately 1.1 billion yen.
- Shiroki established SWM-GT, L.L.C. as a fully-owned subsidiary of SW Manufacturing Inc. to oversee its third U.S. plant.
- Construction of the new U.S. plant began in August 2006, with operations starting in January 2007.
- The total investment for the new plant was 440 million yen.
- The new plant will have a workforce of 80 people, comprised mainly of local nationals.
- The facility aims to generate sales of over 1.7 billion yen in its first fiscal year.
- Starting from January 2007, the new plant will produce mechanical parts for automotive seats, such as seat recliners and adjusters, and parts for doors, such as window regulators and door locks.
- For new equipment installation from January 2007 to December 2007, Shiroki North America, Inc. in Tennessee is allocated an estimated investment of 313 thousand USD.
- Shiroki-GA, LLC. in Georgia is allocated an estimated investment of 776 thousand USD for new manufacturing equipment from January 2007 to December 2007.
- The investment table lists Shiroki-GT, L.L.C. as located in Georgia, USA, with an estimated equipment investment of 160 thousand USD from January 2007 to December 2007.
- The company already operates overseas plants in Tennessee, Georgia, China, and Thailand, bringing its total overseas plant count to five after adding the new U.S. facility.
