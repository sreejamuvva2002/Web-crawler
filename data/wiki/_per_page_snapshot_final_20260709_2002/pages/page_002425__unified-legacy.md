# Unified Legacy

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-15

**Source:** [Georgia-Based Unified Legacy to Create 500 New Jobs in Macon-Bibb Coun | Georgia](https://georgia.org/press-releases/2026/georgia-based-unified-legacy-create-500-new-jobs-macon-bibb-county)  ·  `page_002425`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced on May 15, 2026 that Unified Legacy is expanding its existing footprint in Macon-Bibb County.
- The company will invest $125 million in a new manufacturing facility in Macon.
- The investment will create 500 new jobs over the next several years in Bibb County.
- Unified Legacy manufactures complex products and components for the defense, aerospace, data center, and industrial markets.
- Through advanced machining, welding, and fabrication capabilities, the company delivers custom solutions including ground support equipment, welded assemblies, generator enclosures, fuel storage tanks, and precision-machined components.
- Unified Defense, their existing manufacturing facility, has been operating in Byron, Georgia since 2022.
- Eric Williams serves as CEO of United Legacy.
- The new manufacturing facility will be located at Barnes Ferry Road in Macon.
- Construction is expected to begin in 2026.
- The company plans to hire for manufacturing, skilled trades, engineering, logistics, quality control, and administrative roles.
- Unified Legacy is headquartered in Macon, Georgia, and is the parent company of Unified Defense and Prince Service & Manufacturing.
- Macon-Bibb County Mayor Lester Miller stated that economic development and workforce development are two of the community’s top priorities moving forward.
- Candice Scott, Senior Regional Project Manager, represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development on this competitive project in partnership with the Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority and Georgia Power.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that strengthening domestic manufacturing is critical to national security and highlighted Georgia's competitive edge with a highly skilled workforce, world-class logistics, and strong local and state partnerships.
