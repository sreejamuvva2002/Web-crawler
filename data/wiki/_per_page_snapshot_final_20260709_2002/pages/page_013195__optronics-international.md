# Optronics International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Optronics introduces custom LED logo lamps with GloLight technology | Vehicle Service Pros](https://www.vehicleservicepros.com/home/press-release/21111376/optronics-international-optronics-introduces-custom-led-logo-lamps-with-glolight-technology)  ·  `page_013195`

## Facts

- Optronics International announced that it will be displaying stop, tail, turn lamps with integrated graphics.
- The new lamps merge brand identities into the lighting design by juxtaposing a smooth GloLight lens appearance against a more traditional LED pattern.
- Examples of the new 4” round and 6” oval stop, tail, turn lamps will be displayed publicly for the first time in booth 2547 at the North American Commercial Vehicle (NACV) Show 2019.
- GloLight technology allows Optronics to consolidate even complex graphics into the functional operation of the lamps.
- The new lamp’s traditional LED-style lens pattern surrounds the logo graphic in the middle of the lamp.
- The GloLight logo portion of the lamp illuminates when operating in its standard function as a tail lamp.
- When the lamp’s turn signal or brake functions are in operation, all portions of the LED lamp brighten.
- Brett Johnson, president and CEO of Optronics International, stated that this is an industry first and a novel use for their GloLight technology.
- Johnson noted that logos and branding have been widely incorporated into conspicuity tape for decades, so the migration to lighting is a logical one.
- Marketing professionals recognize that the 4” round and 6” oval lighting formats are among the most noticeable features on any vehicle.
- Johnson stated that whether consciously aware or not, drivers constantly monitor commercial vehicle tail lights, particularly at night.
- Optronics employed its 3-D printing capabilities in preparing prototypes for the manufacturers.
- The company worked with a number of OEMs during the development of the lamp, including Vanguard National Trailer Corporation and Miller Industries.
- Charlie Mudd, president of Vanguard National Trailer Corporation, stated they were among the first trailer manufacturers to make Optronics’ GloLight part of their standard lighting package.
- Mudd praised Optronics’ technical, creative, and pioneering spirit evident in the design of these new lamps.
- The LED lamps offer users a broad level of creative design flexibility for company branding because the GloLight technology can be used in both red and white or a combination of both.
- Lens striations can also be used to achieve unique and complementary visual effects.
- Will Miller, president and Co-CEO of Miller Industries, said Optronics’ new custom logo stop, tail, turn lamps present an opportunity to further their brand’s recognition and strength.
