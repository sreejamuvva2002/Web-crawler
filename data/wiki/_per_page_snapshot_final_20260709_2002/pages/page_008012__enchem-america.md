# Enchem America

## Facts

- Enchem America is a South Korean battery component maker.
- The company backed out of plans to build an electrolyte manufacturing facility.
- The planned facility had a projected investment value of $152.5 million.
- The project would have brought 190 jobs to Haywood County.
- Enchem America announced its plan to build the facility in 2023.
- The proposed site was located on the 'shovel ready' I-40 Advantage Industrial Park in Brownsville.
- The site is roughly 16 miles from BlueOval.
