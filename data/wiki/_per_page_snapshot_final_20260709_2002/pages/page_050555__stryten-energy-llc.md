## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC is a US-based energy storage solutions provider.
- Installed its advanced vanadium redox flow battery (VRFB) at Snapping Shoals EMC.
- Snapping Shoals EMC is a utility provider for some of the fastest-growing areas in the nation.
- Partnership with Snapping Shoals will focus on demonstrating the VRFB’s energy storage capabilities.
- Partnership will evaluate additional use cases for long-duration energy storage technology.
- Project represents the first VRFB energy storage system manufactured and installed in the state of Georgia.
- Shaun Mock serves as President and CEO of Snapping Shoals EMC.
- Snapping Shoals EMC serves over 100,000 residential and business customers.
- Mike Judd serves as CEO and President of Stryten Energy.
- VRFB offers industry-leading power density with a versatile, modular platform independently scalable in power and capacity.
- VRFB features an unlimited cycle life with proper maintenance.
- VRFB system can operate for more than 20 years without the electrolyte losing energy storage capacity.
- VRFB is suited for medium to long-duration energy storage applications requiring six or more hours.
- VRFB helps ensure grid stability and facilitates increased utilization of renewables for businesses and consumers across the US.
- Demonstration project aims to prove VRFB capabilities in utility use cases including energy cost control, peak shaving, and avoiding curtailment.
