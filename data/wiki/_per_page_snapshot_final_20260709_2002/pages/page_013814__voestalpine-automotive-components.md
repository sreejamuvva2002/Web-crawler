## Facts

- Automotive Components is a specialized business unit in the Metal Forming Division of Voestalpine.
- It is the Voestalpine Group’s 17th production site in the United States.
- As a global partner to the automotive industry, their goal is to provide optimum support for innovative light-weight solutions.
- The plant is primarily producing high quality automotive components (body-in-white parts) for premium car manufacturers.
- In Cartersville (Georgia, USA) site they have installed three units of Cooling Towers from EWK, which have been provided with access ladders and access door.
- The client has asked for extra sound attenuators that fit in the basin for waterfall sound reduction.
