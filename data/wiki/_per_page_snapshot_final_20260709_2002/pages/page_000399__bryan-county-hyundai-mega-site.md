# Bryan County Hyundai Mega-Site

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Making Way for Bryan County's Hyundai Megasite » C.W. Matthews](https://cwmatthews.com/projects/making-way-for-bryan-countys-hyundai-megasite/)  ·  `page_000399`

## Facts

- The I-16 Frontage Road Project, US 280 Widening Project, and Old Cuyler Road Interchange are three initiatives currently overseen by the Georgia Department of Transportation (GDOT) in Bryan County, Georgia.
- These infrastructure projects are situated just outside of Savannah and aim to reinforce and expand infrastructure around the Bryan County Mega-Site.
- The Bryan County Mega-Site is hosting both a Hyundai electric vehicle assembly plant and an adjacent EV battery factory.
- The mega-site development promises to generate 8,000 employment opportunities for the region.
- C.W. Matthews is contracted for all three infrastructure projects.
- The I-16 Frontage Road Project is priced at $29.7 million.
- The US 280 Widening Project is priced at $83 million.
- The Old Cuyler Interchange project is priced at $75 million.
- The I-16 Frontage Road Project provides a new access road alongside I-16 to enhance connectivity for the Mega-Site between US280 and the new Old Cuyler Road Interchange.
- The US 280 Widening Project focuses on expanding the roadway and incorporating five roundabouts along US 280 to improve traffic flow and safety in the area.
- The Old Culyer project adds additional direct interstate access and egress to and from I-16 and the mega-site via the I-16 Frontage Road project.
- A significant increase in regional traffic is anticipated due to the ongoing development of the mega-site, underscoring the critical role these projects will play in alleviating impending congestion.
- C.W. Matthews has formed a joint venture with McLendon Enterprises to ensure effective execution of these projects.
- McLendon Enterprises has been an established presence in Southeast Georgia since 1979 and brings invaluable local expertise to the partnership.
- C.W. Matthews contributes its Design Build knowledge to the joint venture.
- Both C.W. Matthews and McLendon will actively participate in the construction of these projects.
- The collaboration creates an opportunity for C.W. Matthews to engage in an area of Georgia that is newer to the company.
- The collaborative approach underscores a shared commitment to advancing infrastructure and fostering economic growth in Bryan County, Georgia.
- These infrastructure projects hold substantial promise for the local community and economy.
