# Wheelabrator Group

## Facts

- Wheelabrator designs and manufactures a full range of wheel-type shot blast machines, mass finishing equipment dedicated to the demands of the industry, and automated airblast solutions.
- Wheelabrator Plus professionals will inspect your current blasting equipment and conduct a thorough assessment.
- Surface preparation is a critical process for clean energy applications, whether the focus is on metal fabrication, assembling wind towers or on the metal components.
- Given the harsh field conditions, metal components used in manufacturing wind towers require a surface preparation process prior to assembly.
- Mechanical pre-treatment of steel can be carried out using centrifugal blast wheels or using blast nozzles.
- Wheelabrator Plate and Structural Steel systems meet the needs of Wind Tower manufacturers.
- Wheelabrator plate systems are fully-automated and designed to descale and remove rust from plate.
- The Wheelabrator horizontal shot blast plate machine models have been specially developed to meet the needs of various industries, including the Wind Power sector.
- The machine effectively pre-cleans large plates for wind tower sections and components, eliminating the high labor costs associated with manual air blasting.
- Wheelabrator plate machines are available for plate sizes up to 15-feet wide.
- Plate machines can easily be integrated into Wheelabrator Preservation Lines for automatic painting.
- Depending on customers’ needs, there are three models to choose from.
- Each model comes equipped with a pass height range of 20-inches to 48-inches with custom heights available on request.
- The plate machines come with EZEFIT® blast wheels that have a history of outstanding performance and reliability while increasing productivity and reducing maintenance.
- The plate system blast compartments are constructed of manganese steel and equipped with replaceable cast ship lapped Long-Lyfe® liners in line designed for a heavy-duty continuous operation using steel shot media.
- Roll conveyors are available to meet varying part load and length requirements for individual customers.
- A combination of brush and blow-off options also contributes to the ease of automatic operations.
- The low profile system provides a reduced overall height, allowing for installation in facilities with limited height requirements.
- This enables routine maintenance and inspection actions to be performed near floor level; a convenient and efficient solution for any plant site.
- Wheelabrator Group is a global provider of surface preparation and finishing solutions.
- The company is committed to offering the broadest array of technologies, products, replacement parts, services and technical know-how.
- Wheelabrator Group works closely with customers to deliver the best solutions for their specific requirements.
- The company’s strategically located and electronically linked offices allow Wheelabrator Group to provide unrivalled customer service and support worldwide.
