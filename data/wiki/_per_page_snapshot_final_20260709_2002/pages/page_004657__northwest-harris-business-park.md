# Northwest Harris Business Park

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-29

**Source:** [County Promotes Business Park - Harris County, Georgia](https://www.harriscountyga.gov/county-promotes-business-park/)  ·  `page_004657`

## Facts

- The county-owned Northwest Harris Business Park is located on State Route 103 near Interstate 85 in the City of West Point.
- The business park encompasses over 450 acres, with 375 acres remaining ready for development.
- Current tenants primarily include Kia Motors suppliers Adient, Daehan Solutions Georgia, Daesol Materials Georgia, Kopla America, Hyundai Glovis Georgia, and WOW!
- Harris County has parcels available and ready for development at the park.
- Utilities are provided by the City of West Point.
- Meghan Duke serves as the Economic Development Director for the City of West Point.
