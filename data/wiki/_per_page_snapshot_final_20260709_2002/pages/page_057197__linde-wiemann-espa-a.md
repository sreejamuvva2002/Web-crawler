# Linde + Wiemann España

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Linde + Wiemann Espanya receives 2.5 million euros from ICF for the manufacturing of the new Mercedes-Benz electric van](https://www.icf.cat/en/actualitat/noticies/2025/linde-wiemann-espanya-2-5-milions-icf-fabricacio-nova-furgoneta-electrica-mercedes-benz)  ·  `page_057197`

## Facts

- ICF has provided 2.5 million euros in financing to Linde + Wiemann Spain.
- The financing is for the construction of vehicle structural parts at the company’s plant in La Garriga.
- The work drives the global launch of the new 100% electric Mercedes-Benz van.
- Linde + Wiemann España is a German group based in La Garriga dedicated to the manufacturing of industrial vehicle components.
- The company specializes in producing parts that form the structural components of vehicles.
- The collaboration reflects the company’s commitment to financing alternatives beyond traditional banking, enabling a more agile financial structure adapted to the industrial timeline of the program.
- The process with ICF has been marked by its operational simplicity and a high degree of cooperation between teams.
- José Carlos López Pérez, manager of Linde + Wiemann España, stated: “We wish to express our sincere gratitude to the ICF for its support. Its proximity and flexibility have been key to securing the financing for these projects and accompanying a strategic challenge such as the launch of the new electric van.”
- Vanessa Servera, CEO of ICF, stated: “this is an industrial project that combines innovation, sustainability, and growth — the kind we are proud to be part of and help make possible through the Generalitat’s public promotional bank.”
- With this operation, Linde + Wiemann España strengthens its commitment to electrification, industrial competitiveness, and collaboration with public financial partners to spur innovation across the automotive value chain.
