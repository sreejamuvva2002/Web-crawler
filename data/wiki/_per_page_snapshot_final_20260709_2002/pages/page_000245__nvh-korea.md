# NVH Korea

## Facts

- Automotive parts manufacturer NVH Korea will invest $72 million to build a new assembly plant in Locust Grove, GA.
- The factory is expected to create more than 160 jobs.
- NVH Korea specializes in door trim, thermal management systems, and components for reducing noise and vibration.
- The company already has a factory in Columbus, GA that produces floor mats, cargo mats and cargo trays.
- The new facility will produce battery parts for electric vehicles for Hyundai Mobis, Kia Georgia, and Hyundai Motor Group.
- The factory is scheduled to begin production in the second quarter of 2024.
