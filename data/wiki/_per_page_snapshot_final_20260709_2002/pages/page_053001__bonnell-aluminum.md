## Facts

- For more than 65 years, Bonnell Aluminum has built a reputation as one of North America's leading manufacturers of custom aluminum extrusions.
- The company made recent sizable investments to broaden its market and product reach.
- Bonnell Aluminum provides customers with some of the most comprehensive range of capabilities in aluminum extrusions, fabrication and finishing services in the industry.
- The company operates four strategically located manufacturing facilities.
- Bonnell Aluminum and its subsidiary, AACOA (Division of Bonnell Aluminum), serve many of the nation's leading and most respected manufacturing companies in the Building and Construction, Automotive, and Specialty Markets.
- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation (NYSE:TG), a global company specializing in the manufacturing of plastic films and aluminum extrusions.
