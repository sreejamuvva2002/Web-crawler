# Pirelli Tire North America (PTNA)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [MTD Mid-Year Q&A: Pirelli CEO Details Growth Plans | Modern Tire Dealer](https://www.moderntiredealer.com/retail/article/55055600/mtd-mid-year-qa-pirelli-ceo-details-growth-plans)  ·  `page_048549`

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America (PTNA) CEO Claudio Zanardo states the company is in growth mode with a positive first half of 2024 driven by new products and partner cooperations.
- The U.S. consumer tire market is flat, but the high-value market for 18- and 19-inch tires and up is showing resilience and growing.
- The 19-inch tire market in the U.S. is growing by double digits.
- The EV tire market and all-weather segment are growing and supporting Pirelli's growth.
- Pirelli makes a concentrated investment in designing and manufacturing EV-specific tires to meet different requirements for weight, torque, and driving dynamics without compromising performance, quality, or handling.
- Pirelli's strategy is based on achieving 'perfect fitment' by developing dedicated products specifically for each car model.
- Research and development of new materials for EV tires benefits Pirelli's entire tire range.
- Pirelli's Elect EV tire line has attained more than 550 OE homologations worldwide for EV tires.
- The Elect technology is the driver of growth in the EV tire segment, with growth pursued through both the OE and replacement markets.
- In North America, Pirelli is working in partnership with pure electric vehicle players like Rivian and Tesla, as well as traditional automakers.
- Pirelli's plant in Mexico recently produced its 50th millionth tire and receives continuous investment in growth, new technologies, machinery, production capacity, and R&D for North American products.
- Pirelli's Rome, Georgia plant produces small lots of very high-technology tires and is dedicated to small product runs, contrasting with the high-volume production at its Mexico facility.
- Pirelli launched the Pirelli Performance associate dealer program at the end of 2023 and is currently expanding it nationwide with monthly feature additions.
- Pirelli's biggest priority for the second half of the year is efficiently serving huge demand, developing new products, and improving product quality and brand power in the North American market.
