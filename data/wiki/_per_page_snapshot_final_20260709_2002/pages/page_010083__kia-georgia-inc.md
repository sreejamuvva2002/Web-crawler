# Kia Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2009-11-16

**Source:** [KIA Georgia Plant Tour - West Point  (Morning) 2025 -  Georgia Manufacturing Alliance](https://www.georgiamanufacturingalliance.com/events/kia-georgia-plant-tour-morning-2025)  ·  `page_010083`

## Facts

- Kia Georgia, Inc. is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Corporation based in Seoul, Korea.
- The facility has an annual capacity of 350,000 units.
- Kia Georgia is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Ga.
- The facility address is 7777 Kia Parkway, West Point, GA 31833, United States.
- Mass production at the facility began on Nov. 16, 2009.
- Kia Georgia is home to the Telluride, Sorento, and Sportage SUVs.
- Kia Georgia is also home to the all-electric EV9 and EV6 SUVs.
- The facility represents a total investment of $3.2 billion.
- The plant employs 3,200 team members.
- Manufacturing processes utilize high-tech stamping, weld shop advanced robotics, and highly automated assembly processes.
- The plant produces a finished vehicle every 51 seconds.
- Operations run on three full shifts operating at the plant 24 hours a day/five days per week.
