## Facts

- Vanguard National Trailer Corp. is a subsidiary of the CIMC Vehicle Group.
- CIMC Vehicle Group is the world’s leading manufacturer of commercial transport dry and refrigerated container and trailer equipment with subsidiaries across the globe.
- Vanguard has grown to be a premier manufacturer of dry and refrigerated semi-trailers for the North American market.
- Vanguard offers superior corrosion protection, high strength and long-life specifications and advanced safety components as standard features on every trailer.
- The company serves the Automotive and Transportation industries.
- Available equipment includes container chassis, converter dollies, dry freight van trailers, flatbed trailers, and refrigerated trailers.
- Container chassis is listed under NAPCS Code 671150101.
- Converter dollies are listed under NAPCS Code 671150101.
- Dry freight van trailers are listed under NAPCS Code 31101010101.
- Flatbed trailers are listed under NAPCS Code 31101010101.
- Refrigerated trailers are listed under NAPCS Code 31101010101.
- Vanguard National Trailer Corp. is located at 289 Water Tower Dr, Monon, IN 47959 in White County.
- Jim Wilkerson holds the title of Director, Manufacturing.
- Craig Czupka holds the title of Director, Purchasing & General Service Parts Manager.
