# Blue Bird Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2013-02-27

**Source:** [Blue Bird Delivers 10 All American Transport Buses to the Georgia Department of Corrections - School Transportation News](https://stnonline.com/industry-releases/blue-bird-delivers-10-all-american-transport-buses-to-the-georgia-department-of-corrections/)  ·  `page_052957`

## Facts

- Blue Bird Corporation and Yancey Bus Sales and Service delivered 10 new 2014 All American transport buses to the Georgia Department of Corrections.
- The delivery ceremony took place on Feb. 27, 2013, at the Georgia Department of Corrections State Offices South at Tift College in Forsyth, Ga.
- Attendees included State of Georgia Chief Operating Officer Bart Gorbeil, Commissioner of the Georgia Department of Corrections Brian Owens, Commissioner of the Georgia Department of Economic Development Chris Cummiskey, Blue Bird President and CEO Phil Horlock, and Yancey Brothers Company Vice President Eric Arnold.
- The new buses replace existing fleet vehicles that have each accumulated over 300,000 miles.
- The Georgia Department of Corrections tasked Blue Bird to provide increased passenger and storage capacity with its newly redesigned All American bus.
- The delivered buses increase seating capacity by four, seating a total of 50 passengers.
- Storage is fully-maximized with pass-through luggage compartments and interior storage bins.
- The buses feature a custom on-board lavatory.
- These units are the first of Blue Bird’s newly redesigned 2014 All American buses to roll off the production line in Fort Valley.
- The buses utilize Blue Bird’s exclusive purpose-built chassis and boast an ergonomic driver’s cockpit for increased driver comfort.
- Design features include a larger windshield providing improved sightlines, the industry’s best turning radius, a larger entrance door, enhanced serviceability, and the industry’s best fuel economy.
- The buses offer greater parts commonality with other Blue Bird buses.
- Blue Bird’s All American bus line is the industry’s sales leader in the Type D bus segment.
- A 2009 economic multiplier study found that for every one dollar Georgia school districts and other customers spend on Blue Bird buses, that dollar multiplies four times within the state.
- Every Blue Bird bus sold in Georgia provides a positive impact to the state Gross Domestic Product (GDP) by approximately $86,000.
- Blue Bird has been a Georgia-based manufacturer for over 86 years and is the second largest manufacturer in the automotive category in Georgia.
- Blue Bird’s manufacturing facilities are all based in Fort Valley.
- Blue Bird employs 1,500 Georgians from over 20 Georgia counties.
- Over 20% of the materials used to build Blue Bird buses are sourced from Georgia-based companies.
- Phil Horlock stated, “As a Georgia-based manufacturer for over 86 years and as the second largest manufacturer in the automotive category in Georgia, Blue Bird is pleased to provide the Georgia Department of Corrections with buses that have been crafted by and for Georgians.”
- Brian Owens stated, “Our partnership with Blue Bird Corporation is a new beginning for the Department. We are pleased to own buses that were designed and built by a Georgia-based company.”
- Eric Arnold stated, “Yancey Bus Sales and Service is honored to have the opportunity to supply transport buses to the Georgia Department of Corrections. We look forward to future opportunities where we can provide products and services that allow for safe and dependable transportation throughout Georgia.”
- Yancey Brothers Company is the parent company of Yancey Bus Sales and Service.
- Yancey Brothers Company was founded in downtown Atlanta in 1914 as the Yancey Hardware Company, originally selling hardware, inmate wagons, and prison uniforms to government agencies and county prisons across the southeastern U.S.
- Yancey Brothers Company is headquartered in Austell and operates 30 facilities across the state of Georgia.
- Blue Bird offers a complete line of Type A, C, and D school buses in a variety of options and configurations.
- Blue Bird provides comprehensive financial solutions through Blue Bird Capital Services.
- Blue Bird has a global presence with sales into more than 60 countries across Africa, Asia, the Caribbean, Latin America, Europe, and the Middle East.
