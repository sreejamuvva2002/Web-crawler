# DE-FOA-0001384 Fiscal Year 2016 Vehicle Technologies Program Wide Funding Opportunity Announcement

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** research_and_development  ·  **Published:** 2016-01-28

**Source:** [DOE: CMEI eXCHANGE: Funding Opportunities](https://eere-exchange.energy.gov/Default.aspx?Search=1384&SearchType=)  ·  `page_003843`

## Facts

- The Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy is issuing a Funding Opportunity Announcement (FOA) on behalf of the Vehicle Technologies Office.
- The FOA is entitled 'Fiscal Year 2016 Vehicle Technologies Program Wide Funding Opportunity Announcement.'
- This FOA supports a broad portfolio of advanced highway transportation technologies that reduce petroleum consumption and greenhouse gas emissions, while meeting or exceeding vehicle performance and cost expectations.
- Projects will focus on reducing the cost and improving the performance of a mix of near-and-long-term vehicle technologies.
- Activities will contribute to achieving the goals of the EV Everywhere Grand Challenge.
- The FOA focuses on accelerating the development of advanced batteries, power electronics, and lightweight materials technologies.
- The FOA also supports technology development to reduce petroleum consumption through advancements in combustion engines, alternative fuels, and other enabling technologies.
- The FOA supports Clean Cities initiatives to overcome market barriers.
- An applicant webinar titled 'DE-FOA-0001384 Fiscal Year 2016 Vehicle Technologies Program Wide Funding Opportunity Announcement Applicant Webinar' was scheduled for January 28, 2016.
- The webinar ran from 2:00 PM to 3:00 PM EST.
- Attendees were instructed to register via a provided link to receive a confirmation email containing information about joining.
