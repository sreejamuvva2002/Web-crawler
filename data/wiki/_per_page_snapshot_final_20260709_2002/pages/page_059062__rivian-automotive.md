# Rivian Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-05-16

**Source:** [3 Top All-American Companies Thriving on Domestic Success | The Motley Fool](https://www.fool.com/investing/2025/05/16/3-top-all-american-companies-thriving-on-domestic/)  ·  `page_059062`

## Facts

- Rivian Automotive is looking to break ground on a new facility in Georgia.
- The company's main manufacturing plant is located in Illinois.
- The bulk of Rivian's sales are derived from the U.S., with California being one of its largest markets.
- Over the past year, the company worked diligently to lower the cost of its vehicles and improve its manufacturing process, which helped the company turn gross-margin-positive.
- The most important move the company made was transitioning to a zonal architecture system, which allowed it to reduce its number of electronic control units and wiring.
- German automaker Volkswagen invested in Rivian and formed a joint venture to gain access to this zonal architecture technology.
- The investment gives Rivian a runway to increase its manufacturing capacity.
- Rivian plans to introduce its more affordable R2 model.
- Rivian has been a leader in the luxury EV SUV market.
- The new R2 model will have a starting price of around $45,000.
- The R2 model is expected to open up a much larger potential customer base.
- Rivian is working to mitigate tariff impacts by looking to procure batteries built in the U.S. from LG by 2027.
- Rivian is currently an unprofitable company that is still burning cash.
- Rivian has significant potential as it begins R2 production next year.
