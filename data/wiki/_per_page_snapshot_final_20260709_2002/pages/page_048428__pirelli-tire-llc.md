## Facts

- Pirelli Tire LLC includes a Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) facility and research and development center at its Rome, Georgia headquarters.
- Pirelli Tire LLC lists Rome, Georgia as a U.S. manufacturing facility producing passenger tires.
- The company manufactures, distributes, and markets original equipment and replacement tires for export and domestic car applications.
- Pirelli maintains sales and marketing offices in Atlanta, New York City, Los Angeles, Detroit, and Montreal.
- Pirelli has a commercial presence in over 160 countries and operates 19 manufacturing sites across 12 countries.
- The company focuses its business on the high-end, premium product segment where it is a world leader.
- Pirelli has successfully competed in motorsports since 1907 and is currently the exclusive supplier for the Formula 1 championship, the Superbike world championship, and many other global championships.
