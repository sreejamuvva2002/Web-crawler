# Seohan Auto Georgia Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-09-12

**Source:** [Seohan Auto Georgia holds grand opening - Coastal Courier](https://coastalcourier.com/news/seohan-auto-georgia-holds-grand-opening/)  ·  `page_059354`

## Facts

- Seohan Auto Georgia held a grand opening ceremony for its new manufacturing facility in Midway, Liberty County, at the Tradeport East Business Center.
- The new manufacturing facility will produce H/shafts, axles, and brake systems for Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs).
- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America is an OEM customer for the facility's products.
- The project will create at least 180 new jobs in Liberty County.
- The project represents an investment of more than $72 million in Liberty County.
- Georgia Department of Economic Development Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that community investment in the Tradeport East Business Center allowed Seohan Auto Georgia to meet a challenging timeline to get up and running.
- State Rep. Al Williams, chairman of the Liberty County Development Authority board, said the process to locate the facility began in November 2022 after other locations were considered.
- Williams credited the Liberty County Development Authority staff, board members, community partners, and state partners for successfully winning the project for Liberty County.
- Seohan Auto Georgia Corporation is a new subsidiary of Korea Movenex Co., Ltd.
- Korea Movenex Co., Ltd was established in 1974 and employs more than 2,800 employees globally.
- Korea Movenex possesses advanced technology and experience in the automotive parts industry.
