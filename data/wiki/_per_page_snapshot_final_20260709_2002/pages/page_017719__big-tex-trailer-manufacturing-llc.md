## Facts

- Big Tex Trailer Manufacturing LLC is headquartered in Mt. Pleasant, Texas.
- The company designs and manufactures trailers for a range of commercial and personal hauling needs.
- Its products are used across industries such as construction, landscaping, agriculture, and transportation.
- On February 5, 2026, Big Tex Trailers notified the Georgia Office of Workforce Development of its decision to conduct a mass layoff at its facility in Cordele, Georgia.
- The company is being investigated for potentially laying off 100 employees without providing the required 60 days' prior written notice under the federal Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification (WARN) Act.
- The WARN Act requires employers with 100 or more employees to provide at least 60 days' notice of significant layoffs or plant closings.
- Employees laid off from Big Tex Trailers in Cordele, Georgia may be entitled to 60 days of severance pay and benefits if the company violated the WARN Act notice requirements.
- Employers who fail to follow WARN Act rules by giving notice too late or providing unclear notices may have to pay employees back pay and benefits for the violation period.
