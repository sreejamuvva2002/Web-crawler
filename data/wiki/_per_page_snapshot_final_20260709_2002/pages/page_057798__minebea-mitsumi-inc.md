# MINEBEA MITSUMI Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-08-04

**Source:** [Announcement of Completion of Acquisition of All Shares of Honda Lock Mfg. Co., Ltd. - MinebeaMitsumi](https://www.minebeamitsumi.com/english/news/press/2023/1204737_17686.html)  ·  `page_057798`

## Facts

- MINEBEA MITSUMI Inc. completed the acquisition of all shares of Honda Lock Mfg. Co., Ltd. on Jan. 27, 2023.
- Honda Lock Mfg. Co., Ltd. has been renamed to Minebea AccessSolutions Inc. following the completion of the share acquisition.
- The initial announcement regarding the acquisition was made in a press release dated August 4, 2022.
- MinebeaMitsumi's core business strategy focuses on products designated as the "Eight Spears," which include access products for automobiles, industrial applications, and housing.
- Automotive access products manufactured by the company include key sets, latches, door handles, keyless entry systems, and rear gate automatic open/close systems.
- The automotive sector is undergoing a major shift driven by CASE technologies, which stand for Connected, Autonomous/Automated, Shared, and Electric.
- MinebeaMitsumi plans to expand its business by developing next-generation door lock/unlock systems and integrated products for vehicle electrification.
- Minebea AccessSolutions previously operated as a core subsidiary of Honda Motor Co., Ltd., providing automobile security and safety products.
- The company's access product business has been integrated with U-Shin Ltd. since 2019 to advance automobile open/close mechanisms and smart locks.
- The acquisition is intended to strengthen collaboration within the corporate group as a supplier to Honda and generate synergies between Minebea AccessSolutions and U-Shin.
- Synergies will be achieved by integrating leading technologies, combining customer bases, expanding sales opportunities, and enhancing global customer service coverage.
- The company expects to generate additional synergies beyond access products through the mutual use of sales channels and integration of product portfolios, technologies, and customer networks.
- The acquisition supports MinebeaMitsumi's FY28 financial targets for its access product business, aiming for 300 billion yen in sales and a 10 percent operating margin.
- Minebea AccessSolutions Inc. is headquartered at 3700, Shimonaka, Sadowara-cho, Miyazaki-City, Miyazaki, Japan.
- Kazuhisa Kawashima serves as the President and CEO of Minebea AccessSolutions Inc.
- Minebea AccessSolutions has a registered capital of 2,150 million yen and was established in April 1962.
- MinebeaMitsumi Inc. holds a 100 percent ownership stake in Minebea AccessSolutions.
- Minebea AccessSolutions reported consolidated sales of 93,538 million yen for the fiscal year ended March 31, 2022.
- Principal subsidiaries include Minebea AccessSolutions USA Inc., Minebea AccessSolutions Mexico S.A. de C.V., Minebea AccessSolutions Brazil Ltd., and facilities in Thailand, Indonesia, Vietnam, and China.
- Yoshihisa Kainuma is the Representative Director, CEO & COO of MINEBEA MITSUMI Inc.
