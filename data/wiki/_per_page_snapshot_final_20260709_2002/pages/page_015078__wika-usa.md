# WIKA USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-30

**Source:** [WIKA USA Expands Strategic Partnership with AWC, Strengthening Service Reach by Wika](https://www.1888pressrelease.com/wika-usa-expands-strategic-partnership-with-awc-strengtheni-pr-727925.html)  ·  `page_015078`

## Facts

- WIKA USA announced an expanded strategic partnership with AWC, a Baton Rouge, LA-based automation and controls technology company.
- The partnership builds on a nearly decade-long collaboration between the two companies.
- The expansion aims to extend WIKA USA's service reach and enhance customer satisfaction through AWC's broader geographic footprint.
- WIKA USA confirmed the partnership will not alter the manufacturing or design of its product portfolio, ensuring consistent product quality and uninterrupted service.
- WIKA USA President Todd Gardner stated the expanded geographic partnership marks a significant milestone reinforcing their partner relationship and commitment to serving partners effectively.
- The WIKA Group employs 11,200 people globally and is a market leader in pressure and temperature measurement, as well as level, force, flow, and calibration technology.
- AWC has over 600 employee-owners and focuses on Process Technology & Instrumentation and Industrial Automation.
- AWC operates as an authorized local sales, support, and service partner to leading manufacturing partners, providing integrated systems to optimize US Manufacturing and Utility operations.
- Founded in 1965, AWC operates 32 full-service locations across the U.S., ranging from Denver, CO to Tampa, FL, with a heavy concentration along the Gulf Coast of Texas and Louisiana.
