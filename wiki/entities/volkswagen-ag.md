# Volkswagen AG

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Georgia, United States

## Overview

Volkswagen called itself an “unintended victim” in a trade-secrets dispute between battery suppliers LG Energy Solution and SK Innovation and urged the U.S. government to extend a reprieve for importing batteries needed for its planned U.S. electric-vehicle production.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Seeks an extended import reprieve for batteries critical to its U.S. EV production, including vehicles assembled in Tennessee and future models that will use batteries from the SK Innovation plant in Jackson County, Georgia.
