# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2012-10-02

**Source:** [Porsche Reports September Sales up 26 percent over 2011](https://www.cnbc.com/2012/10/02/porsche-reports-september-sales-up-26-percent-over-2011.html)  ·  `page_048905`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) is based in Atlanta, Georgia.
- PCNA serves as the exclusive U.S. importer and distributor of Porsche sports cars, Cayenne SUVs, and Panamera four-door sports sedans.
- The company was established in 1984.
- PCNA is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- PCNA employs approximately 220 people who provide parts, service, marketing, and training for 192 dealers.
- In September 2012, PCNA reported U.S. sales of 2,736 vehicles, a 26.08 percent increase over the 2,170 vehicles sold in September 2011.
- Year-to-date through September 2012, Porsche sold 25,015 cars in the U.S., a 10.37 percent increase over the prior year's 22,664.
- Cayenne sport utility sales increased 47.3 percent in September 2012 compared to the same month in the prior year.
- Panamera sport sedan sales increased 17.9 percent in September 2012 compared to the same month in the prior year.
- The Cayenne Diesel model provides 406 lb.-ft of torque and a range of up to 765 miles on a tank of fuel, accounting for nearly 20 percent of Cayenne sales in September 2012.
- Porsche Approved Certified Pre-Owned vehicle sales reached 871 in September 2012, a 22 percent increase over September 2011.
- Year-to-date CPO sales totaled 7,370 models, an 11.7 percent increase versus 2011.
- Boxster/Cayman sales were 251 in September 2012 and 222 year-to-date.
- 911 sales were 509 in September 2012 and 504 year-to-date.
- Cayenne sales were 1,370 in September 2012 and 930 year-to-date.
- Panamera sales were 606 in September 2012 and 514 year-to-date.
