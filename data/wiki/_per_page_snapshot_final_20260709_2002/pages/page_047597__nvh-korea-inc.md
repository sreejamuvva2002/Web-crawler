## Facts

- Engages in the manufacture and sale of automotive noise, vibration, and heat control parts in South Korea and internationally.
- Offers interior systems, such as isolation dash pads, tunnel pads, floor modules, car mats, carpet skins, and sound absorption pads.
- Offers exterior systems, including under covers, insulator tunnels, and wheel guards.
- Offers overhead systems comprising headliners and sun visors.
- Manufactures and sells non-woven fabrics and pallets.
- Provides engine systems consisting of partition engine rooms, insulator dashes, and hood insulators.
- Provides door trims.
- Provides control assy-water temp solutions, cylinder block covers, and thermal management systems.
- Offers management advisory and consulting services.
- Was founded in 1984.
- Is headquartered in Ulsan, South Korea.
- JaKyeom Goo serves as Chairman & CEO since 2024 and is 67 years old.
- GyuYang Lee serves as Vice Chairman & CEO since 2023 and is 67 years old.
