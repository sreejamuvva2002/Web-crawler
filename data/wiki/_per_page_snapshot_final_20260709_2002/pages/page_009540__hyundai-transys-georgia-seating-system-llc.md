# Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-04-05

**Source:** [Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System, LLC Expands Manufacturing Operations in Georgia - Location Georgia](https://www.locationgeorgia.com/hyundai-transys-manufacturing-operations-press-release/)  ·  `page_009540`

## Facts

- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System, LLC will invest $9 million in expanding their West Point, Georgia operations.
- The expansion brings an additional 150 jobs, which brings their total employment in Georgia to more than 1,890 jobs.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System, LLC will invest $9 million in expanding seating manufacturing operations in West Point, Georgia.
- The expansion will create more than 150 jobs in Troup County.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated: “Contributing more than $3 billion to Georgia’s economy, the state is committed to growing Georgia’s robust automotive ecosystem and creating solid manufacturing jobs of the future.”
- Governor Kemp stated: “Georgia’s highly skilled workforce continues to attract manufacturing jobs in every corner of the state, and Hyundai TRANSYS’ continued investment in West Point shows that the hardworking people of west Georgia have proven they’re up to the task.”
- Governor Kemp added: “I thank Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System for continuing to invest in Georgia, and I look forward to seeing the opportunities this expansion creates in Troup County.”
- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System manufactures seats and seat foam pads for the Hyundai Santa Fe, KIA Sorento, and other models.
- The company supplies seating structures to automobile manufacturers and leading electric vehicle makers.
- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System’s President and CEO Sangkil Jung stated: “We are excited for the new opportunity ahead as we expand our presence in the West Point community.”
- Sangkil Jung added: “We are proud of our partnership with the State of Georgia.”
- This expansion will take Hyundai TRANSYS’ employment total in Georgia to more than 1,890 jobs.
- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System is continuing to hire for careers in production seat assembly, foam, quality, and logistics.
- Chairman of the West Point Development Authority Kevin Patrick stated: “On behalf of the West Point Development Authority we want to thank Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System for its continued investment.”
- Kevin Patrick added: “Our automotive and existing industries are the backbone of our economic success, and we look forward to working with Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System as they undertake their latest expansion.”
- Senior Project Manager Amanda Fields represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce division on this competitive project in partnership with the West Point Development Authority, Georgia Power, and Georgia Quick Start.
- Pat Wilson extended thanks to Hyundai TRANSYS and Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System for their continued investment in Georgia.
- Pat Wilson thanked partners at the local, state, and federal levels who have worked to foster Georgia’s pro-business environment, which has helped give automotive partners confidence to continue investing in the state.
- Georgia is home to more than 200 automotive-related facilities and remains a national leader in advanced manufacturing, outpacing the United States in 10-year GDP growth in machinery, electrical equipment and components, and fabricated metals.
- Hyundai TRANSYS (parent company) is an automotive parts company that produces critical automobile parts, including transmissions, axles, and seats.
- Hyundai TRANSYS is the only company in the world specializing in transmissions.
- The parent company produces all types of automatic transmissions, manual transmissions, dual clutch transmissions (DCT), and continuously variable transmissions.
- Hyundai TRANSYS develops and produces top quality seats for Genesis, Santa Fe, Sorento, and other models.
