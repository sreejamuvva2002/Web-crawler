# Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [2018 Textron Off Road Wildcat XX - UTV Planet Magazine](https://www.utvplanet.ca/2018-textron-off-road-wildcat-xx)  ·  `page_051669`

## Facts

- Located in Augusta, GA, Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. and its Textron Off Road brand launched the Wildcat XX, a new high-performance side-by-side designed for off-roading.
- The Wildcat XX was designed in collaboration with off-road racing legend Robby Gordon.
- The vehicle features an off-road racing-inspired suspension system delivering best-in-class handling with more than 18 inches of travel in the front and rear.
- It is equipped with FOX 2.5 PODIUM QS3 Shocks with Bottom-Out Control that are precision-tuned at the factory.
- The Wildcat XX includes an all-new TEAM front gear case and rear transaxle providing 2WD/4WD and 4WD lock functionality.
- It is powered by a 125HP, 998cc three-cylinder EFI engine, which is the highest horsepower aspirated engine in its class.
- John Collins, Vice President, Consumer for Textron Specialized Vehicles, stated that the Wildcat XX was inspired by extreme off-road racing vehicles and built to outperform competitors.
- The front suspension features unequal-length A-arms that angle the tire into every turn to better negotiate hairpin turns.
- A rear trailing arm design results in 80 percent less track width change in the rear compared to competitive vehicles, providing a smoother ride.
- The vehicle is built with durability and serviceability in mind, featuring a race-inspired cage, double-shear joints, a forged aluminum front knuckle, and HSLA tubing throughout.
- The engine is top-mounted to avoid damage during bottom outs.
- The front steel clip and rear bottom skid are easily removable for service access to the powertrain, transaxle, engine, and front gear case.
- The cargo box is removable without requiring any tools.
- The Wildcat XX features the largest cab in the industry, ergonomic seating, and an automotive style interior.
- It includes a standard 65-amp alternator to power accessories.
- An integrated wiring system cuts light bar installation time in half.
- Electronic Power Steering (EPS) is standard.
- The cargo box has a class-leading 300-lb capacity and fits up to a 32-inch spare tire.
- It features a class-leading four-gallon glove box to protect gear in the cab.
- The vehicle is available in two packages with more than 30 purpose-built accessories from Textron Off Road.
- Ground clearance is 14 inches.
- It comes equipped with 15-inch custom KMC wheels and 30-inch CST Behemoth tires.
- The Wildcat XX is available in Lime Green, Satin Charcoal Metallic, or a limited edition Horizon Blue.
- The vehicle is covered by a six-month warranty.
- Textron Off Road's product line includes gas and electric off-road vehicles for work and play, including the Prowler, Stampede™, Havoc™, and Wildcat™ lines of side-by-sides and Alterra™ ATVs.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. manufactures golf cars, utility and personal transportation vehicles, snowmobiles, side-by-sides, all-terrain vehicles, professional turf-care equipment, and ground support equipment under brands including E-Z-GO®, Cushman®, Arctic Cat®, Textron Off Road, Jacobsen®, Dixie Chopper®, Ransomes®, TUG™, Douglas™, Premier™, and Safeaero™.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company leveraging a global network of aircraft, defense, industrial, and finance businesses, known for brands such as Bell Helicopter, Cessna, Beechcraft, Hawker, Jacobsen, Kautex, Lycoming, E-Z-GO, Greenlee, Textron Off Road, Arctic Cat, Textron Systems, and TRU Simulation + Training.
