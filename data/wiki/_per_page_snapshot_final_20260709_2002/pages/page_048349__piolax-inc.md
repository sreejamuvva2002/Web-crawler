# Piolax Inc.

## Facts

- Overseas, Piolax Corporation manufactures and sells automotive parts in Georgia, USA.
- The main products in the automotive-related, etc. business are industrial fasteners (parts used for fastening interior and exterior automobile trim) and precision springs.
- The automotive industry, which is the Piolax Group's main customer, continues to face a severe business environment, with both production and sales volumes declining year-on-year, along with revisions to supply chains and production systems, sluggish sales by Japanese automakers in the Chinese automotive market, a rapid shift toward EVs, and sluggish growth in the U.S. automotive market.
- Net sales for the automotive-related segment decreased by 1,407 million yen (△2.4%) year-on-year to 56,770 million yen due to production cuts at major Japanese automaker customers.
- Operating profit for the automotive-related segment decreased by 727 million yen (△23.5%) year-on-year to 2,368 million yen, primarily due to a decrease in marginal profit accompanying production cuts at Japanese automakers.
- Domestically, Piolax HFS Co., Ltd., PNS Co., Ltd., Piolax Kyushu Co., Ltd., and K&K Co., Ltd. handle manufacturing, selling primarily through Piolax to Nissan Motor Co., Ltd., other automobile manufacturers and parts manufacturers, and others.
- Piolax forecasts consolidated net sales of 63,000 million yen for the fiscal year ending March 2027.
- The assumed exchange rate for the fiscal year ending March 2027 forecast is 1 USD = 150 yen.
