# Nisshinbo Automotive Manufacturing Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-09-06

**Source:** [Automotive Supplier to Invest $72M to Expand Manufacturing Facility Near Atlanta - REBusinessOnline](https://rebusinessonline.com/automotive-supplier-to-invest-72m-to-expand-manufacturing-facility-near-atlanta/)  ·  `page_058257`

## Facts

- Nisshinbo Automotive Manufacturing Inc. (NAMI) will invest $72 million to expand its manufacturing facility in Covington, Georgia.
- Georgia Gov. Nathan Deal announced the investment on Tuesday, Sept. 4.
- The facility expansion will create 100 production and administration jobs.
- The Covington facility is located roughly 35 miles southeast of Atlanta.
- NAMI is a subsidiary of Japan-based Nisshinbo Group.
- NAMI has maintained a presence in Covington for 20 years.
- The Covington facility produces friction materials for automotive manufacturers.
- The Covington facility develops environmentally friendly, copper-free brake pads.
- According to the governor’s office, Japanese-affiliated companies currently operate more than 500 facilities in Georgia.
- Japanese-affiliated companies in Georgia employ approximately 30,000 people.
