## Facts

- Apache Mills was founded in 1970.
- Apache Mills is one of the largest mat manufacturers in the United States.
- The company operates four business units: Apache at Home, Apache Commercial, Apache Industry, and AMI.
- Apache Mills designs and manufactures high-quality floor mats, rugs, and OEM materials for residential, commercial, and industrial markets.
- The company's manufacturing facility is located in Calhoun, Georgia.
- The manufacturing facility covers 750,000 square feet.
- Apache Mills employs over 600 people.
- Art Director Jeff Young has used Wasatch software for nearly 20 years.
- Apache Mills uses Wasatch SoftRIP along with McDermid AutoType to produce film positives for screen printing on various mat materials.
- The company utilizes a Roland Soljet PRO III XJ-640 printer for its production process.
- Apache Mills provides solutions for all mat requirements across commercial, industrial, and residential product lines.
