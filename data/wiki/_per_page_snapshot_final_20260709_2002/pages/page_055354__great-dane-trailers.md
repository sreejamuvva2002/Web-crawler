# Great Dane Trailers

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Great Dane Atlanta branch moves to new facility | Vehicle Service Pros](https://www.vehicleservicepros.com/home/press-release/20865144/great-dane-trailers-great-dane-atlanta-branch-moves-to-new-facility)  ·  `page_055354`

## Facts

- Great Dane’s Atlanta, Georgia, branch has relocated to a new facility located at 1637 Forest Parkway.
- The new facility is located near several major highways.
- The new facility is significantly larger than its predecessor and features an expanded parts showroom and bigger stock yard.
- Al Cox, Great Dane regional sales manager, stated the new facility is designed to better serve customers with a safer, more convenient location.
- The larger facility allows the branch to stock more trailers, truck bodies, and parts to provide faster service.
- The Great Dane Atlanta branch occupies more than 25,000 sq. ft. of space.
- The new location features an expansive new and pre-owned trailer and truck body display lot.
- The facility includes a large retail parts center and an updated service center that is open seven days a week.
- The new location has a large drop yard and serves as a designated Great Dane truck body center.
- The branch operates as the company’s newest flatbed distribution center.
- Al Cox described the Atlanta branch as a one-stop-shop for trailers, truck bodies, parts, and service.
- The Atlanta branch is located near Great Dane’s manufacturing plants in Statesboro, Georgia, and Huntsville, Tennessee.
- The new branch location officially opened in May.
- A grand opening celebration will be held later this year.
