## Facts

- Hyatt Place Augusta provides EV charging infrastructure at its hotel location in Augusta, Georgia.
- The facility is located at 160 Mason McKnight Jr Pkwy, Augusta, Georgia, 30907, United States.
- Charging station types available include J-1772 connectors.
- The site is equipped with two Tesla High Power Wall Connectors capable of up to 40A.
- The site is equipped with one Clipper Creek J1772 connector capable of up to 32A.
- All charging stations are physically located in the back side parking lot.
- Charging access is restricted exclusively to hotel patrons, who must visit the front desk for use.
- The hotel offers amenities including a 24-hour front desk, ADA accessible business center, free continental breakfast, free WiFi, lounge/bar, meeting facilities, non-smoking rooms, parking, pet friendly accommodations, and an onsite restaurant.
