# Toyota Industries Group TACG-TICA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-16

**Source:** [Toyota Industries Group TACG-TICA to spend $44,415,543.00 to occupy 206,961 square feet of space in Pendergrass Georgia. - intelligence360](https://www.intelligence360.news/toyota-industries-group-tacg-tica-to-spend-44415543-00-to-occupy-206961-square-feet-of-space-in-pendergrass-georgia/)  ·  `page_012338`

## Facts

- Toyota Industries Group TACG-TICA plans to invest $44,415,543.00 to build out 206,961 square feet of new space in Pendergrass, Georgia.
- The company plans to occupy the new space at 1000 Valentine Industrial Pkwy in Pendergrass on or about 4/1/2025.
- TACG and TICA are members of the Toyota Industries Group and are among the world's leading manufacturers of automobile air conditioning compressors and component parts.
- The company manufactures DC-AC inverters for automobile plug-in devices.
- The manufacturing process covers operations from raw materials to final product.
- TACG and TICA have grown to approximately 1,300 associates working together.
- The company supplies its products to many of the world's top vehicle manufacturers.
- Haru Ito serves as President.
