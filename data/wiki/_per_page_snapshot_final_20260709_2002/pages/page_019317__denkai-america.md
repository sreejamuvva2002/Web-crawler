# Denkai America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-07-20

**Source:** [Copper Foil Manufacturer Building New Facility in Richmond County - WGAC](https://wgac.com/2022/07/20/copper-foil-manufacturer-building-new-facility-in-richmond-county/)  ·  `page_019317`

## Facts

- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp announced plans for copper foil maker Denkai America to build a new manufacturing facility in Richmond County.
- The $430 million facility will be built on a 115-acre site at the Augusta Corporate Park in south Augusta.
- The company's initial investment will be $150 million to establish an electrodeposited (ED) copper foil production facility.
- The new facility will focus on electric vehicle battery foil.
- Denkai America's materials have typically been used in printed circuit board applications, primarily for the aerospace and defense industries.
- Denkai America's materials are also now used in lithium-ion batteries powering electric vehicles.
- Construction on the new facility is expected to begin in September.
- The project will be a three-phase development.
- The facility is expected to be fully operational by December 2024.
- A total of 250 jobs are expected to be created over the next five years.
