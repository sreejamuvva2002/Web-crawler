# Mobis US Alabama LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-11-09

**Source:** [Mobis Alabama Expands Into Birmingham](https://businessalabama.com/mobis-alabama-expands-into-birmingham/)  ·  `page_046749`

## Facts

- Mobis US Alabama LLC is a subsidiary of Hyundai Mobis and a tier one supplier to Hyundai and Kia.
- The company will open a new facility at the Jefferson County Metropolitan Industrial Park in McCalla.
- The expansion represents a $15.8 million capital investment.
- The project will create 120 jobs within three years.
- Established in 2002, Mobis Alabama already operates a plant in Montgomery.
- The existing Montgomery facility manufactures bumper assemblies and instrument panels for Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama and Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia.
- Through its Redistribution Center, the company provides after-service parts to dealerships, auto parts stores, and other parts distribution centers.
- James Jeon, program manager for Mobis US Alabama, stated the company is excited about opening its second facility in Alabama and utilizing the Jefferson County workforce.
- The company expressed gratitude for support from the Jefferson County Commission, the city of Birmingham, and the Birmingham Business Alliance.
- The Birmingham Business Alliance has worked with Mobis on this expansion since 2018.
- Brian Jennings, vice president of economic development at the Birmingham Business Alliance, described landing the partnership as a tremendous win for Jefferson County jobs and investment.
- When operational, the project will help put the region closer to building a cluster of next-generation automobile technologies and manufacturers, according to Brian Jennings.
- The Jefferson County Metropolitan Industrial Park is currently home to 13 companies.
- Other auto suppliers located at the industrial park include Gestamp and Yanfeng.
