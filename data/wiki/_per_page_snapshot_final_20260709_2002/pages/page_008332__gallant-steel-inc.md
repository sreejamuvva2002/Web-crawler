## Facts

- Gallant Steel, Inc., operating as GSC Steel Stamping, LLC, is a minority and family-owned automotive parts manufacturer established in 2008.
- The company operates a 200,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility located at 5286 Circle Drive, Lake City, GA 30260.
- Gallant Steel delivers high-volume metal stamping, welded sub-assemblies, and engineering support for critical Body in White (BIW) frame assembly brackets and structural components.
- The facility is equipped with over 40 presses ranging from 20 to 1000 tons, utilizing line, progressive, transfer, and tandem operations at a speed of 80 SPM and a max bed length of 3.70m.
- The site includes 26 welding stations that support robotic MIG, projection, stud, and spot-welding to produce assembly-ready components.
- Gallant Steel strengthened its operations by acquiring Dixien, LLC's assets in June 2023.
- The company partners with leading automotive OEMs and Tier 1 suppliers, supporting customers from early engineering through production launch and ongoing supply.
- Engineering capabilities include Catia V5 R19 CAD and simulation tools for blank development, form prediction, and problem-solving to de-risk launches.
- An in-house tool shop builds, maintains, and adjusts most tooling systems to keep programs stable and responsive to change.
- Dedicated program leaders coordinate APQP, PPAP, and sample warrants to align internal teams with customer requirements and meet launch gates.
- The company is led by CEO Vincent Henderson and a seasoned team focused on operational excellence, quality, and on-time delivery.
