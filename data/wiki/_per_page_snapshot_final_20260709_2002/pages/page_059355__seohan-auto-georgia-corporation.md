# Seohan Auto Georgia Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Seohan Auto Georgia to invest $72 million into new manufacturing facility | Plant Services](https://www.plantservices.com/industry-news/news/33003475/seohan-auto-georgia-to-invest-72-million-into-new-manufacturing-facility)  ·  `page_059355`

## Facts

- Seohan Auto Georgia has announced that it will build a new production plant in Liberty County, Georgia.
- The company manufactures automotive parts for Hyundai Motor Group America.
- Seohan Auto Georgia will invest $72 million into the new facility.
- The project will generate 180 new jobs in the process.
- Production at the new plant will begin in 2024.
- The new plant will produce H/shafts, axles, and brake systems for OEMs.
- Jung Kee Koo serves as the CEO of Seohan Auto Georgia Corporation.
- CEO Jung Kee Koo stated that sustained growth of the EV market over the past few years suggests accelerated changes to the automotive market are inevitable.
- CEO Jung Kee Koo stated that he believes Georgia will be the center of the EV industry and a new frontier for Seohan's future with limitless opportunities and potential.
- CEO Jung Kee Koo stated that the company is very pleased to collaborate in partnership with Georgia and move towards a promising new future.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp stated that the Hyundai Metaplant is and will continue to be an economic driver for the entire region, creating more jobs for hardworking Georgians in communities like Liberty County.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp stated that he is proud to welcome Seohan as the latest supplier in a growing list to invest in Georgia and looks forward to the continued growth of this fast emerging sector.
