## Facts

- Eastman Chemical Company's headquarters and largest manufacturing facility are located in Kingsport, Tennessee.
- Eastman expects its methanolysis manufacturing plant in Kingsport, Tennessee to begin commissioning and startup activities by early summer 2023.
- Approximately 75 percent of the required feedstock for the Kingsport, Tennessee methanolysis manufacturing plant is being procured under contract or in active negotiation.
- Solar-absorbing Saflex™ PVB interlayer solutions, including the Saflex™ S series, Saflex™ Solar Connect, and XIR™ Solar Control Technology, are ideal for electric vehicle ("EV") glass in cabin-forward designs and larger sunroofs to reduce air-conditioning loads and maximize EV driving range.
- Eastman's Saflex™ Horizon is a next-generation polyvinyl butyral ("PVB") interlayer product that supports advanced Head-up Displays ("HUD") systems with longer virtual image distance, expanded field of view, and augmented reality features.
- Eastman produces performance films innovations for automotive window films and paint protection films.
- Saflex™ PVB and HUD acoustic interlayers in the automotive sector are essential to ensure passenger safety and enable the adoption of digital technologies within the cabin.
