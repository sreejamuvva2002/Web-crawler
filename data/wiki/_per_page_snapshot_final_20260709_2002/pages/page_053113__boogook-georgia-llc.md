## Facts

- The resolution to bring Boogook Georgia to Macon was motivated, in part, by the proximity of Hyundai Motor’s $5.5 billion Metaplant America in Savannah and Kia’s West Point production facility.
- Joong Suk Oh, CEO of Boogook Georgia, LLC, stated that their control identified an exclusive opportunity for Korean automotive production partners more locally.
- The expansion of operations in Macon will be a driving force in the company's ability to serve the southeastern United States.
- The company looks forward to a successful partnership with MBCIA.
- The location provides quick highway access via I-16, less than 3 miles from the company’s new location.
- This strategic positioning ensures seamless connectivity with major transportation routes, further enhancing the company's ability to serve its consumers well with greater access to critical parts.
- Boogook Georgia will create 80 full-time jobs when it opens in 2024 and more jobs are expected on the horizon.
- These employment opportunities will provide the local network with professional jobs in the automotive sector in management, manufacturing, assembly, quality and warranty and delivery functions.
