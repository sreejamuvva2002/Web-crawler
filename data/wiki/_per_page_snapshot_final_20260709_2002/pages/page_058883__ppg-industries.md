## Facts

- PPG Industries is a global supplier of paints, coatings and specialty materials that serves industrial, transportation, consumer and construction markets.
- Founded in 1883 as the Pittsburgh Plate Glass Company, PPG has evolved from its origins in glass manufacturing into a diversified coatings and materials company headquartered in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.
- The company develops and manufactures a broad array of products used to protect and enhance surfaces, from consumer paints to highly engineered coatings for demanding industrial applications.
- PPG’s product portfolio includes architectural and decorative paints, automotive original equipment and refinish coatings, industrial coatings for machinery and equipment, protective and marine coatings, aerospace and defense coatings, and packaging coatings and materials.
- The company provides color and design services, technical support, application systems and coatings-related services for manufacturers, distributors, contractors and retailers.
- Its operations encompass research and development centers, manufacturing facilities and distribution networks aimed at delivering tailored coating solutions and color consistency for customers.
- PPG operates on a global scale, with manufacturing, sales and technical support spanning the Americas, Europe, Asia, the Middle East and Africa.
- The company sells to a wide range of end markets including automotive, aerospace, marine, industrial manufacturers, commercial builders and retail consumers.
- As of mid-2024, PPG is led by Chief Executive Officer Michael H. McGarry.
- Under CEO Michael H. McGarry's leadership, the company has emphasized product innovation and operational performance.
- Strategically, PPG places emphasis on innovation, sustainability and application expertise, investing in new coating chemistries, color technologies and formulation improvements that address durability, appearance and environmental regulations.
- Its offerings target performance and lifecycle benefits such as corrosion resistance, chemical and UV protection, weight reduction opportunities in transportation, and efficient application systems for contractor and industrial customers.
- The company’s combination of global scale, R&D capability and diverse end-market exposure positions it as a major provider of coatings and specialty materials worldwide.
