# Seohan Auto USA Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-01

**Source:** [Seohan Auto USA to spend $72 Million to expand into new space in Midway Georgia. - intelligence360](https://www.intelligence360.news/seohan-auto-usa-to-spend-72-million-to-expand-into-new-space-in-midway-georgia/)  ·  `page_059368`

## Facts

- Seohan Auto USA plans to invest $72,000,000.00 to build out new space in Midway, Georgia.
- The company plans to occupy the new space at Tradeport East Business Center in Midway on or about May 1, 2024.
- Seohan Auto USA Corporation engages in the development, manufacture and sale of automobile parts.
- The company is a tier-1 supplier for Hyundai and KIA manufacturing front & rear axle assembly and drive shafts.
- Seohan Auto USA Corporation is a subsidiary of Korea Flange Co., Ltd.
- Jung-Kee Koo serves as the Chief Executive Officer of Seohan Auto USA.
- Seohan Group states that it 'does not transfer value through distribution or sales, but instead develops new technology and supplies parts through partners to create new value.'
