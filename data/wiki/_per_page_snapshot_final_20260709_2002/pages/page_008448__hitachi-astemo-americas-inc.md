# Hitachi Astemo Americas Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-07-11

**Source:** [Hitachi Astemo Americas to expand EV production in Berea, creating 167 jobs](https://www.lex18.com/news/covering-kentucky/hitachi-astemo-americas-to-expand-electric-vehicle-production-in-berea-creating-167-jobs)  ·  `page_008448`

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo Americas Inc. is one of Kentucky’s largest Tier 1 automotive suppliers.
- The company announced an expansion of its operation in Madison County.
- The expansion will create 167 full-time jobs for Kentuckians.
- Hitachi is making a $153 million investment.
- The investment will bring the company’s total Kentucky employment across the Harrodsburg and Berea facilities to over 2,100 workers.
- The facility expansion will include an additional 752,000 square feet on 62 acres.
- The expansion comes in response to increased market demand and growth within the EV industry.
- Company leaders will invest in the current manufacturing operation in Berea to support site improvements and renovations.
- The project includes increased production lines and additional equipment.
- The expansion adds to Kentucky’s manufacturing sector.
- Administration-to-date, Kentucky’s manufacturing sector has seen nearly 600 private-sector new-location or expansion projects commit to invest over $22.9 billion.
- These projects are expected to create more than 30,000 jobs.
