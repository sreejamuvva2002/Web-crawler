# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-21

**Source:** [Hyundai's Metaplant Reveals 2026 IONIQ 9 as Newest Production Vehicle](https://thekoreancarblog.com/hyundais-metaplant-reveals-2026-ioniq-9-as-newest-production-vehicle/)  ·  `page_013656`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) announced its second production vehicle: the 2026 IONIQ 9, an all-electric, three-row SUV.
- The 2026 IONIQ 9 will be the first all-new Hyundai vehicle assembled at the state-of-the-art Metaplant.
- Production of the IONIQ 9 is slated to begin in Q1 2025, with the SUV hitting the market in Q2.
- The announcement follows news from June about the 2025 IONIQ 5, an updated version of Hyundai’s award-winning electric vehicle, which served as the first production vehicle at the Metaplant.
- With the introduction of the IONIQ 9, HMGMA is doubling down on its electric lineup.
- Oscar Kwon, CEO of HMGMA, stated that starting to build the all-new IONIQ 9 at the Metaplant brings an enhanced level of excitement for their 'Meta Pros.'
- Kwon noted that seeing the expansion of the acclaimed IONIQ lineup being manufactured locally is particularly special.
- HMGMA is the only facility to build IONIQ 9s in North America.
- The IONIQ 9 was unveiled at AutoMobility LA 2024 in Los Angeles, alongside a simultaneous reveal by HMGMA at AutoNation Hyundai Savannah.
- Meta Pros who contributed to the assembly of the preproduction vehicle were on-site to celebrate the launch.
- Thanks to its U.S.-based assembly and battery sourcing, the 2026 IONIQ 9 is expected to qualify for the full $7,500 federal EV tax credit.
