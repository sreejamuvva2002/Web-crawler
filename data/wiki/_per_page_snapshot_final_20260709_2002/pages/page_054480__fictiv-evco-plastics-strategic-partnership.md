# Fictiv & EVCO Plastics Strategic Partnership

**Type:** supplier_relationship  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-08

**Source:** [Fictiv & EVCO Partner to Tariff-Proof Injection Molding for U.S. OEMs](https://www.fictiv.com/articles/fictiv-and-evco-plastics-announce-strategic-partnership-to-deliver-end-to-end-injection-molding-supply-chain-solutions)  ·  `page_054480`

## Facts

- Fictiv has created a strategic partnership with EVCO Plastics to deliver end-to-end injection molding supply chain solutions for U.S. OEMs.
- The alliance combines Fictiv’s digitally connected global supply chain with EVCO’s extensive manufacturing capabilities.
- EVCO Plastics operates over 1 million square feet of manufacturing space.
- EVCO Plastics utilizes hundreds of injection molding machines, including presses as large as 4,400 tons.
- The partnership leverages deep expertise in design for manufacturability (DFM), AI, human-powered materials data and insights, program management, and global logistics.
- The collaboration aims to mitigate tariff concerns and deliver a highly resilient, fully managed supply chain to OEMs across multiple industries.
- Mexico’s manufacturing ecosystem is described as emerging, formative, and fragmented, particularly for tooling and specialized secondary processes.
- Fictiv streamlines these complexities to allow OEMs to improve material sourcing and lower BOM costs.
- Fictiv GlobalFlex integrates tooling inserts produced in China with next-generation manufacturing facilities in the U.S. and Mexico.
- This synergy enhances gross margins, accelerates production timelines, and elevates customer experiences.
- The collaboration supports the entire product lifecycle, encompassing 3D printing, injection molding, assembly, testing, and packaging.
- Advanced manufacturing capabilities include overmolding, gas-assist molding, in-mold decorating, and liquid silicone rubber (LSR) production.
- The partnership holds ISO 9001:2015, ISO 13485, IATF 16949, and FDA compliance certifications to ensure quality and reliability.
- Dave Evans, Co-Founder and CEO of Fictiv, stated the partnership simplifies global manufacturing supply chains by reducing the overall number of suppliers and management complexity.
- Carlos Gonzales, head of EVCO Plastics de Mexico, noted that Fictiv handles complex workflow and supply chain challenges while EVCO focuses on manufacturing excellence.
- The collaboration aims to provide scalable, tailored solutions with a shared commitment to innovation, quality, and sustainability in the manufacturing sector.
- Fictiv operates four global manufacturing centers located in India, Mexico, China, and the U.S.
- To date, Fictiv has delivered more than 35 million commercial and prototype parts for early-stage companies and large enterprises.
- EVCO Plastics was founded in 1964.
- EVCO Plastics employs over 2,100 people globally.
- EVCO Plastics operates 270 injection molding machines ranging from 28 to 4,400 tons.
