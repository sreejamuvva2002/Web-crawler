# Thermal Ceramics Emporia Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2025-01-14

**Source:** [Morgan Advanced Materials to shutter Thermal Ceramics Emporia plant by July | Business | emporiagazette.com](https://www.emporiagazette.com/business/article_9cc13bda-d2cc-11ef-94d2-bf6a73dc0800.html)  ·  `page_010988`

## Facts

- Thermal Ceramics is a manufacturing plant owned and operated by Morgan Advanced Materials.
- The Emporia-based plant will close operations by either June or July 1.
- The plant currently employs 38 people.
- Employees were notified of the expected closure during a meeting today.
- Operations will be moved to Augusta, Ga., consolidating with another plant.
- Morgan Advanced Materials will work with Boilermaker Union Chapter 83 to negotiate severance packages and other forms of assistance for employees.
- The company cited a lack of business opportunity and a desire to consolidate resources as motivating factors for the closure.
- Plant management declined to comment and could neither confirm nor deny the closure.
- Christine Torrens, Emporia city communications manager, expressed sympathy for affected employees.
- The City of Emporia's Rapid Response Team will work with Thermal Ceramics employees to provide job placement services and career counseling.
- The Rapid Response Team was originally created to help displaced employees from Tyson and Holiday Resort.
- Torrens stated the goal is to ease the transition for those affected and continue encouraging economic development efforts that will benefit the local workforce.
- Torrens expressed gratitude for the cooperative spirit of the Emporia community and commitment to fostering a diversified, growing, and resilient local economy.
