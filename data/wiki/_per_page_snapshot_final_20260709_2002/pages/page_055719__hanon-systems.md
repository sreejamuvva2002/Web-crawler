# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-28

**Source:** [Hanon Systems Expands Manufacturing Capability in Canada](https://www.newswire.ca/news-releases/hanon-systems-expands-manufacturing-capability-in-canada-810901949.html)  ·  `page_055719`

## Facts

- Hanon Systems (KS:018880) is a leading global automotive thermal management supplier for electrified mobility.
- The company announced a new plant in Woodbridge, Ontario, Canada, to support its growing business with global vehicle manufacturers.
- The facility includes approximately 284,200 square feet (26,400 square meters) of manufacturing space.
- The facility has room for expansion based on business needs.
- It will be the company's first North American location to produce electric scroll compressors.
- Electric scroll compressors are essential components for thermal management in hybrid and battery electric vehicles.
- Depending on customer demand, the facility can produce up to 900,000 electric compressors annually.
- Production is expected to start in the first half of 2025.
- At full production, the plant expects to employ nearly 300 hourly and salaried employees.
- Nurdal Kücükkaya stated that expanding manufacturing presence increases proximity to vehicle manufacturers, better meets customer demand, demonstrates commitment to growth, and proves customer confidence in delivering electric compressors to the market.
- Subu Nagasubramony stated that the company investigated several locations for the new plant, their partnership with the government of Ontario and investment development agency was a significant element in the decision, and they are proud to foster local community development and economic growth.
- Vic Fedeli stated that Ontario has all elements necessary to build electric vehicles of the future, including leading global parts manufacturers like Hanon Systems continuing to choose Ontario to expand operations, and that the investment will create 300 new local jobs for world-class auto workers and strengthen North American leadership in automotive manufacturing.
- The new facility joins four existing electric compressor manufacturing operations located in Pyeongtaek (Korea), Dalian and Changchun (China), and Portugal (Europe).
- The expansion reinforces the company's competitiveness by expanding production of key components for hybrid and battery electric vehicles.
- Company executives and local dignitaries gathered at a media event to announce the new plant.
- Hanon Systems has a 35-year history in Canada.
- The Belleville plant was established in 1989 and produces fluid transport products, HVAC modules, and electronic coolant valves for the North American market.
- The new Woodbridge plant is located 200 kilometers from Belleville.
- The Woodbridge plant will create synergies with the company's Americas Innovation Center in Novi, Mich.
- Proximity to major automakers will allow the company to respond quickly to customer demands.
- Hanon Systems is a full-line supplier of automotive thermal and energy management solutions for electrified and conventional vehicles.
- Its product offerings include heating ventilation and air conditioning; powertrain cooling; compressor; fluid transport; and electronics and fluid pressure.
- The company currently operates 50 manufacturing sites and three regional innovation centers.
- The company employs more than 20,000 people across 21 countries.
