# GSC Steel Stamping, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-12-24

## Facts

- GSC Steel Stamping, LLC is a minority- and family-owned automotive parts manufacturer.
- The company has acquired Dixien, LLC's assets.
- It continues its 43-year history of automotive parts stamping.
- The company is led by CEO Vincent Henderson, known for turning around award-winning operations.
- GSC is committed to excellence, investing in technology, and maintaining a highly experienced staff.
- The company operates a 200,000 sq. ft. facility in Lake City, GA.
- The Lake City, GA facility is the largest minority-owned automotive stamping plant globally.
