# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2022-06-19

**Source:** [YAMAHA MOTOR MANUFACTURING CORPORATION BREAKS GROUND ON INNOVATIVE SMART WAREHOUSE IN NEWNAN, GEORGIA - The Golf Wire](https://thegolfwire.com/yamaha-manufacturing-newnan-ga/)  ·  `page_015665`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation (YMMC), based in Newnan, Georgia, broke ground on a new 'SMART' Warehouse.
- The SMART Warehouse is the first-of-its-kind automated facility for Yamaha Motor Company worldwide.
- The new warehouse will be 200,000 square feet and utilize state-of-the-art automated storage and retrieval systems.
- The facility will reduce response time for fulfilling dealer orders for Yamaha WaveRunners, Golf Cars, ATVs, and Side-by-Sides by more than 50 percent.
- Mike Chrzanowski serves as President and CEO of Yamaha Motor Corporation.
- The warehouse will improve operational efficiency and material flow velocity to factory assembly lines to meet demand that currently exceeds supply for outdoor recreational products produced at the factory.
- SMART is an acronym for Sequenced Material and Reduced Transportation.
- The medium parts automated storage and retrieval system will retrieve and put away 240 containers per hour, compared to the current rate of 170 containers per hour.
- The small part automated system will handle 2400 containers per hour, compared to the current capability of 550 containers per day.
- New systems will enable Yamaha to reduce forklift usage by 50 percent using automatic conveyors and tuggers.
- The investment underscores Yamaha's long-term commitment to YMMC employees, the City of Newnan, and Coweta County.
- The warehouse will bring higher paid and more skilled jobs into Coweta County.
- New job roles will include robotics experts, software developers, and systems engineers.
- The SMART Warehouse was designed by SSI SCHAEFER.
- The facility will include an observation platform for local school students to learn about manufacturing and automated processes.
- Yamaha expects to complete the SMART Warehouse in 2024.
- Yamaha Motor Corporation, USA (YMUS) operates corporate offices in California and two in Georgia, with facilities in Wisconsin and Alabama, and factory operations in Tennessee and Georgia.
- YMUS sells products including motorcycles, scooters, ATVs, side-by-sides, snowmobiles, WaveRunners, boats, outboard motors, outdoor power equipment, power assist bicycles, golf cars, and robotic machines through a nationwide network of distributors and dealers.
- Additional U.S.-based subsidiaries include Yamaha Marine Systems Company (YMSC) with divisions Bennett Marine, Kracor Systems, Siren Marine, Skeeter Boats, G3 Boats, and Yamaha Precision Propeller across multiple states.
