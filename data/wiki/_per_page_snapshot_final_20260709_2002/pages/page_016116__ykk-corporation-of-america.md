# YKK Corporation of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [YKK USA Expands Product Offerings to Support COVID-19 Relief - YKK Americas](https://ykkamericas.com/ykk-usa-expands-product-offerings-to-support-covid-19-relief/)  ·  `page_016116`

## Facts

- YKK USA expanded its product offerings to reinforce the domestic supply chain for manufacturers throughout the United States.
- YKK offers a wide variety of metal wire, hook & loop, injection molded plastic parts, webbing, snaps, and buttons.
- POWERHOOK extruded hooks are used in Foley Catheter Holders, DVT compression sleeves, endotracheal tube holders, arm boards, blood pressure cuffs, and radial compression devices.
- SOFIX incorporates plastic snaps injected onto tape for surgical gowns, alongside POWERHOOK and polyester tape.
- Medical curtain manufacturers use YKK plastic snaps, stainless SNAPET, and gypsy stud snaps.
- Back to back hook and loop fasteners, SOFIX, plastic detachable clips, and plastic cord stoppers are manufactured as ear savers for face masks.
- 3.0 mm and 4.0 mm aluminum wire is available for face mask manufacturers.
- 3.0 mm and 5.0 mm elastic tape is available for face mask manufacturers.
- Plastic and metal snaps, hook and loop fasteners, and plastic cord stoppers are produced for medical face shield manufacturers.
- PROSEAL airtight and watertight zippers are used in isolation chambers, tent shelters, and Level B Hazmat suits.
- AquaGuard water repellent zippers are used for medical mattresses.
- AquaSeal waterproof zippers are used for body bags.
- YKK has 86 years of manufacturing experience and operates four manufacturing plants in the United States.
- Core competencies include wire manufacturing and forming, metal stamping, metal die casting, metal finishing and plating, weaving and knitting, textile dyeing, plastic extrusion, plastic injection molding, fabrication and assembly, and custom machinery and support.
- YKK aims to partner with more organizations to find inventive uses for its fastening products and core manufacturing technologies.
- Jim Reed serves as President of YKK Corporation of America.
