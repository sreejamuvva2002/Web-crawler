# Magna International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-02-24

**Source:** [Magna Breaks Ground for $70M EV Battery Enclosure Plant in St. Clair - DBusiness Magazine](https://www.dbusiness.com/daily-news/magna-breaks-ground-for-70m-ev-battery-enclosure-plant-in-st-clair/)  ·  `page_057453`

## Facts

- Canadian mobility technology company Magna International has broken ground for a $70-million manufacturing facility in St. Clair (south of Port Huron).
- The new facility will build complex battery enclosures for the 2022 GMC Hummer EV.
- The 345,000-square-foot facility is named Magna Electric Vehicle Structures.
- Production at the facility is expected to begin in early 2022.
- The project will bring more than 300 new jobs to the area over the next five years.
- The battery enclosures contribute to the structural and safety aspects of a vehicle’s frame and protect high-voltage batteries from damage and water.
- Magna can develop these advanced assemblies in steel, aluminum, and multi-material configurations, including lightweight composites.
- John Farrell, president of Cosma International (Magna’s body and chassis group), stated that bringing this battery enclosure technology to market demonstrates Magna’s ability to deliver full-system solutions supporting a lower emissions future.
- Farrell noted that the move toward electrification allows the company to leverage its core strengths to bring customers innovative solutions.
- The new business with General Motors Co. marks a point in Magna’s evolution as it expands its capabilities in the vehicle electrification space.
- Magna has been an industry leader in vehicle structures, providing comprehensive engineering solutions globally.
- Magna currently operates three technical facilities in Troy.
- The company is hiring for managers, engineers, operators, and other roles at the new facility.
