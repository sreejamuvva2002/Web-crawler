# Hyundai Motor Group & SK On EV Battery Manufacturing Facility (Bartow Centre)

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Bartow County, Georgia, Bartow County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

A planned electric vehicle battery manufacturing facility selected by Hyundai Motor Group and SK On to be built at the Bartow Centre site in Bartow County, Georgia, intended to supply HMG plants in the United States.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The facility will produce lithium-ion EV batteries for Hyundai Motor Group’s U.S. vehicle plants, expanding Georgia’s EV supply chain.

## Investment

- **$4-5 billion** — Construction and operation of the Bartow Centre EV battery manufacturing facility
  - Stakeholders estimate the investment will create more than 3,500 new jobs.
- **$5.54 billion** (May (year not specified)) — Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America EV manufacturing facility in Bryan County
  - Separate HMG project mentioned for context; groundbreaking occurred in October.

## Jobs

- **More than 3,500 new jobs** — Operations expected to start in 2025
  - Jobs will be created through the $4-5 billion investment in Bartow County.
