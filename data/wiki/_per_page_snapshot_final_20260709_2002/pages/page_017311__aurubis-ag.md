# Aurubis AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-03

**Source:** [Aurubis To Invest Around $700 Mln To Build Smelter For Complex Recycling Materials In U.S.](https://www.rttnews.com/3404009/aurubis-to-invest-around-700-mln-to-build-smelter-for-complex-recycling-materials-in-u-s.aspx)  ·  `page_017311`

## Facts

- Aurubis AG will invest around US$700 million to build the first secondary smelter for complex recycling materials in North America.
- Commissioning of the secondary smelter in Augusta, Georgia is planned for the second half of the coming year.
- The company noted that first Lady of the United States visited Aurubis Richmond, the first secondary smelter for complex recycling materials in North America.
- An expansion of the Aurubis Richmond plant is already under construction.
- The plant expansion will allow Aurubis Richmond to process a total of 180,000 tons of complex recycling materials per year as of 2026.
- Complex recycling materials processed at the facility include printed circuit boards and insulated copper cable.
- Aurubis is creating long-term, secure jobs for more than 200 people by building this state-of-the-art recycling plant.
- Aurubis has pledged a community benefit agreement with the Technical Colleges in Augusta and Aiken (South Carolina) as well as with the Richmond County School System.
- As part of the agreement, Aurubis will invest more than US$200,000 in scholarships and job training programs for young workers over the next five years.
- The company actively promotes women in the metal industry through its international Women4Metals initiative.
