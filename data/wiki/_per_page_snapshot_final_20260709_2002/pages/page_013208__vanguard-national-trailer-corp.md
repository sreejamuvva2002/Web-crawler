## Facts

- Vanguard National Trailer Corp. started in Monon, Indiana in 2003 and has grown to be a premier manufacturer of dry and refrigerated semi-trailers for the North American market.
- The company produces dry van trailers and composite plate trailers that elevate industry standards.
- Home office is located at 289 East Water Tower Dr. Monon, IN 47959.
- Additional offices are located at 105 E 2nd St Reynolds, IN 47980.
- A Monon Warehouse is located at 838 W Broadway St, Monon, IN 47959.
- A Portland Warehouse is located at 14940 North Lombard Street, Portland, OR 97203.
- The company operates a facility at 8 Vanguard Drive, Trenton, GA 30752.
- Client Services team hours are Monday through Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 9 p.m., Eastern time.
- The company offers one of the industry’s widest selection of heavy duty trailer parts, as well as parts for intermodal vehicle chassis and shipping containers.
- Product offerings include Dry Cargo Containers in ISO 20GP/40GP/40HC/45HC sizes and Domestic 53-foot trailers.
- Vanguard trailers feature High Strength Lightweight Corrosion resistant 6061-T6 extruded Aluminum, a mechanically fastened continuous main beam, low drag design, no crossmembers, and improved aerodynamics.
- One source indicates the company has 100 employees.
- Another source states Vanguard National Trailer Corporation has 500 total employees across all locations and generates $218.97 million in sales (USD).
- Key personnel include Andy Mcdonald as Human Resources Manager, Kirk Forshee in Production, and Brian May as Purchasing Manager.
