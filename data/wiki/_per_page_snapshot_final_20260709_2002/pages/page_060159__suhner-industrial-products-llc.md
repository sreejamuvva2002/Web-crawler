## Facts

- Suhner Industrial Products LLC operates a facility located at 25 Anderson Rd - Plant 2 in Rome, GA 30161.
- The company offers a Battery series of cordless power packs and tools designed for processing metal workpieces to support unlimited mobility.
- The ROTOmax 4.2 Twin is a flexible shaft machine suitable for processing and finishing the surface of metal workpieces in multi-shift operation.
- The BRUSHmax 3.0 tool cleans, polishes, and passivates stainless steel weld seams in one operation without changing the metal's surface texture.
- The liquids used with the BRUSHmax only become reactive at high temperatures and are comparatively harmless to users and the environment under normal conditions.
- Suhner ABRASIVE provides free product and application counselling through technical sales engineers to find the best grinding machine and abrasive solutions for customers.
- Company vehicles are equipped with most available machines and a wide selection of high-quality abrasives to test different applications on-site.
- Technical sales engineers advise customers throughout the entire project life cycle, not just during the decision-making process.
