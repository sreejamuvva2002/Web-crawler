# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-27

**Source:** [Hyundai's New Metaplant Could Save Its EVs In America](https://www.topspeed.com/hyundais-new-metaplant-arrived-to-save-its-ev-efforts/?post=64bd-40ac-8104faa6257c)  ·  `page_001031`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group celebrated the grand opening of its new Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) production facility in Georgia.
- The Trump administration announced a massive 25-percent tariff on all vehicles assembled outside the United States.
- Initial plans called for 300,000 vehicles per year to be built at HMGMA.
- The announcement noted the intention to expand production to 500,000 cars per year eventually.
- HMGMA will produce hybrid and electric vehicles for Hyundai, Kia, and Genesis.
- Hyundai has already announced that its Ioniq 5 crossover will be produced at HMGMA.
- Hyundai has already announced that its upcoming Ioniq 9 three-row crossover will be produced at HMGMA for the 2026 model year.
- Kia has not confirmed a vehicle for the facility yet, but Kia will start building at the HMGMA facility in 2026.
- Hyundai Group announced the deal to build the plant in Georgia in May 2022.
- The initial announcement came months before the Biden administration's Inflation Reduction Act passed that incentivized domestic production of EVs.
- Kia's KMMG plant in Georgia already manufactures the brand's three best-selling SUVs (Telluride, Sorento, Sportage) as well as the K5 sedan and the EV9 electric three-row crossover.
- Hyundai builds its best-selling crossovers, the Tucson and the Santa Fe, along with the Santa Cruz and Genesis Electrified GV70 at its HMMA plant in Alabama.
- Hyundai Motor Company was founded in 1967 and is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
- Hyundai may build a new midsize truck on the Chevrolet Colorado and GMC Canyon platform to take on the Toyota Tacoma.
- The Metaplant may prove the difference between a vehicle like the World Car Award finalist Kia EV3 coming to America and not.
