# Kia

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** West Point, Georgia, Troup County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Kia announced a more than $200 million investment to expand production at its West Point, Georgia plant for the all-electric 2024 EV9 three-row SUV, creating nearly 200 new jobs and adding the EV9 as the fifth model assembled at Kia Georgia.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Investment expands assembly of an all-electric SUV (EV9) in Georgia, increasing the state's EV vehicle manufacturing capacity.

- **Kia Georgia Plant** — Located on 2,200 acres; produces Telluride, Sorento, Sportage SUVs and K5 sedan; will add EV9 production in Q2 2024.
- **Kia Georgia Training Center** — Operated by Georgia Quick Start to provide customized workforce training for the new EV9 assembly line.
- **Kia Georgia Plant** — Manufactures the Telluride, Sorento and Sportage SUVs, the K5 sedan, and will add the all-electric 2024 EV9. The plant sits on 2,200 acres and began mass production on November 16 2009.
- **Kia West Point Plant** — Produces Telluride, Sorrento, Sportage SUVs and K5 sedans; will add EV9 electric SUV production on the same assembly line.
