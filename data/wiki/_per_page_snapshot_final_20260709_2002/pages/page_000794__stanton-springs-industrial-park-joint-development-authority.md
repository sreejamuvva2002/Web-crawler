# Stanton Springs Industrial Park & Joint Development Authority

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-13

## Facts

- The Department of Energy provided a conditional loan that could restart construction of the planned Rivian electric vehicle and battery plant in the Stanton Springs industrial park near Social Circle.
- The Joint Development Authority of Jasper, Morgan, Newton and Walton Counties (JDA) greeted the DOE news enthusiastically, if somewhat reservedly.
- The chairman of the JDA had little to say about calls from a co-chair of a governmental cost-cutting commission established by President-elect Donald Trump.
- The JDA oversees the 1,600-acre Stanton Springs technology and biopharmaceutical industrial park off Interstate 20 near Social Circle.
- Electric vehicle manufacturer Rivian finalized a $6 billion loan agreement with the federal government.
- Shane Short has served as executive director of the Development Authority of Walton County since 2013.
- The Stanton Springs site is being developed as a mixed-use development where electric vehicle maker Rivian is planning to establish a manufacturing facility.
