# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-12-16

**Source:** [Rivian to site second manufacturing plant in Georgia](https://rivian.com/newsroom/article/rivian-to-site-second-manufacturing-plant-in-georgia?Cw6xX=xywyn9a8x)  ·  `page_028780`

## Facts

- Rivian will expand its manufacturing operations, locating its second U.S. plant in the state of Georgia.
- A carbon-conscious campus is planned east of Atlanta, in Morgan and Walton Counties.
- The project represents a $5-billion site development and manufacturing investment.
- The plant will eventually employ more than 7,500 workers.
- Once ramped, the Georgia facility will be capable of producing up to 400,000 vehicles per year.
- Construction on the facility is expected to begin in summer 2022.
- The start of production is slated for 2024.
- Site considerations included logistics, environmental impact, renewable energy production, availability and quality of talent, and fit with Rivian company culture.
- Rivian Chief People Officer Helen Russell stated the company is happy to partner with Georgia on the new manufacturing site to build next-generation products important for scaling the business.
- Helen Russell noted the partnership is driven by a compelling purpose and rooted in building a highly collaborative environment that creates a true sense of belonging.
- Helen Russell expressed confidence that alongside Illinois, Georgia is the place for Rivian to continue to scale and thrive.
- Rivian’s parcel for the site will be almost 2,000 acres and include abundant natural space.
- Rivian will develop community engagement and workforce training programs in the area, similar to its facility in Normal.
- Rivian is also scaling capacity at its existing plant in Normal, Illinois.
- The Normal plant was recently approved for a 623,000-square-foot expansion, bringing its total footprint to approximately 4 million square feet.
- There are further plans to extend warehouse, storage, and production capacity onsite at the Normal facility.
- Rivian plans to hire an additional 800-1,000 employees in Normal by the second quarter of 2022.
- Rivian and Georgia are planning 2022 town hall events near the manufacturing site so area residents can learn more about the company and the site plan.
- Rivian offers industry-leading compensation, generous benefits, and equity in the company for every team member to drive a real sense of ownership.
- New Normal and Georgia-based positions were posted on December 16, 2021.
