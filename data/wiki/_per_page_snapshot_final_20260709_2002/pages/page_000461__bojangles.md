# Bojangles

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Bojangles has installed its first electric vehicle charging station.
- The new charging hub is located in Savannah, Ga.
- The Savannah location was developed in partnership with XLR8 America and Energy and Environmental Design Services.
- This installation marks the first milestone in a larger initiative to bring EV charging infrastructure to Bojangles restaurants across the country.
- Richard Del Valle, chief information officer at Bojangles, stated that driving trends are changing along with customer expectations.
- Del Valle noted that with EV charging, time becomes an asset and the company is turning the stop into a meaningful experience for customers.
- The Savannah charging hub serves as the first step in a planned national rollout for the 870-unit Bojangles brand.
- Bojangles currently operates locations in 23 states.
- Future stations will vary between level two (medium) and level three (high) speed charging efficiency.
- The charging stations are designed to maintain over 97% uptime.
