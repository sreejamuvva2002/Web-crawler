# Inalfa Roof Systems Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-02

**Source:** [Inalfa Roof Systems Group Appoints Georges Andary as New CEO](https://www.prnewswire.co.uk/news-releases/inalfa-roof-systems-group-appoints-georges-andary-as-new-ceo-301943044.html)  ·  `page_045165`

## Facts

- Inalfa Roof Systems Group appointed Georges Andary as its new President and Chief Executive Officer (CEO), effective October 1, 2023.
- Georges Andary brings over 28 years of executive leadership experience in the automotive industry to his new role.
- Inalfa Roof Systems develops and produces integrated roof systems for nearly every major automotive brand.
- Shareholder BHAP and the Supervisory Board are confident that Mr. Andary and his leadership team will enhance Inalfa's position as the first-choice partner for the automotive industry in roof systems and integrated technologies.
- Georges Andary stated: "It is Inalfa's purpose to open up your world with every drive. I am honored to lead the company to deliver on this promise together with the talented teams and to pursue new opportunities and ensure that we continue to meet and exceed our customers' expectations around the globe."
- Prior to joining Inalfa, Mr. Andary held several executive management positions at Bosch.
- Most recently, Mr. Andary served as the General Manager of Bosch Automotive Products (Suzhou) and as President of the Automotive Electronics Division in China.
- Inalfa Roof Systems is a global automotive roof systems manufacturer based in Venray, The Netherlands, with factories and development centers in Europe, Northern America, and Asia.
- Inalfa holds over 800 life patents and maintains a global market share of approximately 25%.
- Inalfa delivers roof systems to almost every major car and truck manufacturer in the world.
- Since 2011, Inalfa Roof Systems has been part of Beijing Hainachuan Automotive Parts Co., Ltd. (BHAP), one of the biggest suppliers of automotive parts in China.
- The strategic acquisition of Inalfa by BHAP further diversifies BHAP's product portfolio, strengthens its R&D and marketing capabilities, and enhances its competitiveness.
