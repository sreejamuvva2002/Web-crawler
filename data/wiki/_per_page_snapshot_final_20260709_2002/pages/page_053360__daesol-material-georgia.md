# Daesol Material Georgia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-12-20

**Source:** [West Georgia Nets Another Korean Auto Investor - Global Atlanta](https://www.globalatlanta.com/west-georgia-nets-another-korean-auto-investor/)  ·  `page_053360`

## Facts

- West Georgia has attracted yet another Korean auto supplier with a $35 million investment that will create 110 jobs.
- Daesol Material Georgia is a sister company to existing Kia Motors supplier Daehon Solution.
- Daesol Material Georgia is putting a new 120,000-square-foot factory in Harris County, just north of Columbus.
- The Daesol plant will supply Daehon with automotive fiber padding for door panels.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development announced the deal on Dec. 20.
- West Georgia has seen a flood of Korean investment since Kia set up shop in West Point, Ga.
- Kia's West Point facility makes more than 300,000 cars per year.
