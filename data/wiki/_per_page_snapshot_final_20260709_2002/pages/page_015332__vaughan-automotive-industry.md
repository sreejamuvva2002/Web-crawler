## Facts

- Vaughan is home to many globally recognized tier 1 suppliers, such as Magna International, Martinrea, Multimatic, Hanon Systems, Litens Automotive, IAC, and Woodbridge Foam Corporation.
- The city hosts numerous tier 2 and tier 3 automotive suppliers.
- The City’s central location is in direct proximity to assembly plants for giants such as Stellantis, Ford, Honda, and Toyota.
- Businesses can plug into a strong and well-connected supply chain.
- JEMS Coating Ltd. President Robert German states that Vaughan offers highly conscientious support from the city’s economic development team.
- Robert German notes that Vaughan provides unparalleled proximity for most of who’s who in the automotive industry.
- Robert German highlights excellent access via the 407 to those that are further away.
- Robert German mentions challenges amongst peers with finding labour, but finds that Vaughan is well situated for having access to both skilled and unskilled talent pools.
- The Automotive Parts Manufacturers sector in Vaughan includes 45 companies and 7,200 employees.
- The Motor Vehicle and Parts Dealers sector in Vaughan includes 110 companies and 1,200 employees.
- The Vehicle Dealers sector in Vaughan includes 125 companies and 3,400 employees.
