# Valeo

**Source:** [Valeo: Valeo signs MoU with 2 others to provide electric power trains - Times of India](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/business/india-business/valeo-signs-mou-with-2-others-to-provide-electric-power-trains/articleshow/92370140.cms)  ·  `page_013049`

## Facts

- Valeo, Atul Greentech Private Limited, and Honda Powerpack Energy Private Limited have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU).
- Through the MoU, Valeo will provide electric power trains and a powertrain control unit for Atul's electric three-wheelers to work with Honda’s swappable battery solution.
- Valeo will provide its 48V integrated compact electric powertrain system along with the Powertrain Control Unit for Atul's upcoming cargo and passenger three-wheelers launching in India soon.
- Valeo’s engineers will provide technology integration support to help Atul go to market quickly with Honda’s swappable battery.
- Valeo launched a fully integrated compact 48V electric powertrain system in India in 2020 to aid affordable electrification for small mobility vehicles, specifically two- and three-wheelers used for first- and last-mile connectivity.
- Jayakumar G, Group President and Managing Director of Valeo India, stated that the tri-party engagement brings domain expertise together to accelerate three-wheeler electrification in both passenger and cargo segments.
- Jayakumar G noted that Valeo's CO2-reducing technologies, strong engineering, and local manufacturing expertise will strengthen their partnership in the fast-growing Indian market and other markets.
- Jayakumar G added that Valeo has the full spectrum of electric powertrain systems, from low to high voltage, powering vehicles ranging from two- and three-wheel urban vehicles to premium sedans and SUVs in India.
- Valeo produces its electric powertrain units at its manufacturing facility in Pune, Maharashtra.
