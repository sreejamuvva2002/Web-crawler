# pSemi, a Murata Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-20

**Source:** [pSemi, a Murata Company, Unveils Breakthrough Power Solutions for Fast Charging Mobile Devices and Next Generation Humanoid Robotics at APEC 2026](https://psemi.com/2026/03/20/psemi-unveils-breakthrough-power-solutions-for-fast-charging-mobile-devices-and-next-generation-humanoid-robotics-at-apec-2026/)  ·  `page_047169`

## Facts

- pSemi unveiled breakthrough power solutions for fast charging mobile devices and next generation humanoid robotics at APEC 2026.
- The company showcases the world’s first 4-level buck converter for battery charging applications alongside new charge pump and two-stage step-down DC-DC converters.
- New power products are designed to solve power challenges in low-profile, high-energy density applications.
- PE26100 architecture supports 4.5V to 18.5V input and enables 4-level buck mode for higher USB-PD voltage inputs.
- The PE26100 engages 3-level buck mode for mid-to-low input voltages.
- In USB PPS applications, the PE26100 operates as a fixed-ratio, capacitor-divider charge pump with divider ratios of 2:1 and 3:1 depending on programmed input voltage.
- pSemi announced the release of the PE25304, an advanced integrated charge pump switching-capacitor power module.
- The PE25304 is engineered to deliver unprecedented efficiency in space-constrained, high-power applications.
- Designed to divide input voltage by four, the PE25304 is purpose-built for 48V input architectures with a wide operating range from 20V to 60V.
- The PE25304 is suitable for emerging dexterous hand robotics and mechatronic systems.
- The PE25304 comes in an ultra-low-profile package (2mm) and can deliver up to 72W of output power.
- The PE25304 achieves 97% conversion efficiency, significantly reducing power loss and thermal buildup.
- Beyond robotics, the PE25304 is suitable for drones, medical devices, embedded AI modules, and industrial automation systems.
- pSemi will showcase two-stage buck regulators powering optical transceiver and sub 10nm low output voltage core applications at APEC 2026.
- The showcased two-stage buck regulators are ideal for low profile (~1.2mm) applications for under-side PCB placement to reduce board area and power losses.
- PE24111 is a latest product in this category, providing 3.3Vin capable of 20A output delivery to voltages in the range of 0.35-0.85V.
- pSemi will showcase ultra-high efficiency switching capacitor charge pump divider products for intermediate bus power conversion at APEC 2026.
- PE25213 supports divide by two and three ratios at 10A output current with efficiencies up to 99%.
- These products can significantly improve overall system efficiency and extend battery life.
- pSemi will showcase two-stage boost LED drivers, combining charge pump followed by a boost regulator and LED drivers in a single package.
- PE23108 is an 8-channel driver for high efficiency, low-profile panel displays included in this category.
- pSemi (formerly Peregrine Semiconductor) is a leader in semiconductor integration design and development.
- The company provides high-performance RF, analog, and mixed signal solutions with a 30-year legacy of technology advancements.
- pSemi's solution portfolio spans power management, connected sensors, antenna tuning, and RF frontends.
- These solutions enable advanced modules for smartphones, base stations, personal computers, electric vehicles, data centers, IoT devices, healthcare, and more.
- pSemi is a wholly owned subsidiary of Murata Electronics North America, Inc.
- pSemi is headquartered in San Diego with additional offices spanning North America, Europe, and Asia.
