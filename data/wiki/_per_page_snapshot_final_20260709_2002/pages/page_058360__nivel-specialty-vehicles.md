# Nivel Specialty Vehicles

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-01

**Source:** [Nivel Parts & Manufacturing Expands Premium Accessory Portfolio with Acquisition of Xtreme Golf Cart Mats - Golf Carting Magazine](https://golfcarting.com/nivel-parts-manufacturing-expands-premium-accessory-portfolio-with-acquisition-of-xtreme-golf-cart-mats/)  ·  `page_058360`

## Facts

- Nivel is the industry leader in aftermarket solutions for personal transportation vehicles (PTV).
- Nivel announced the acquisition of Xtreme Golf Cart Mats.
- Xtreme Golf Cart Mats is the premier provider of high-quality, custom-fit golf cart floor mats.
- Every mat is designed and engineered in America, ensuring a precision fit that protects vehicle flooring from debris, water, and wear.
- Donnie Jouppi serves as Chief Executive Officer of Nivel.
- Donnie Jouppi stated they were attracted to Xtreme Golf Cart Mats for their unwavering dedication to excellence, their “gold standard” approach to product design, and speed to market for new fitments.
- Adding Xtreme’s American-engineered golf floor mats to its catalog allows Nivel to offer customers a level of interior protection and aesthetic appeal that is unmatched in the industry.
- Nivel plans to leverage its extensive distribution network to make Xtreme products more accessible.
- Customers can expect continued innovation through collaborative engineering focused on expanding the fitment catalog for new vehicle models.
- Customers can expect premium quality with maintenance of the rigorous manufacturing standards customers have come to rely on.
- Customers will be able to order Xtreme products through Nivel at the same time as their other parts, accessories, and Bolt batteries.
- The acquisition marks a milestone in Nivel’s aggressive growth strategy focusing on brands that prioritize quality and American-led design.
- Brandon Burkey serves as CEO of Xtreme Golf Cart Mats.
- Brandon Burkey stated that every mat started with the question of whether he would put it in his own cart, which built Xtreme from the ground up into the best-fitting, longest-lasting mat in the industry.
- Brandon Burkey noted Nivel has the network and resources to put that product in front of every golf cart owner in the country.
- For over 59 years Nivel has been redefining the Personal Transportation Vehicle (PTV), Off Road Vehicle (ORV), Heavy Duty (HD), and Automotive experience with innovation, style, and functionality.
- Nivel manufactures today’s best brands in these industries, many right here in the U.S.
