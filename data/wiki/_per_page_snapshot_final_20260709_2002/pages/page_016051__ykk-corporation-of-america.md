# YKK Corporation of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [YKK USA Receives 2021 GEAR Award - YKK Americas](https://ykkamericas.com/ykk-usa-receives-2021-gear-award/)  ·  `page_016051`

## Facts

- YKK (U.S.A.) Inc. was honored with a 2021 GEAR Award by the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD).
- The award ceremony took place on January 14 at YKK's Macon, Georgia plant.
- GDEcD named YKK “Supplier of the Year, employing 200+ people” for its contributions to advancing Georgia’s automotive and mobility industries.
- YKK employs over 200 people at its Macon, Georgia facility.
- YKK produces a wide range of zippers, hook and loop fasteners, plastic hardware, snaps, and buttons for automobile seat assembly and truck tonneau cover hold-downs.
- YKK supplies its automotive fastening products to more than 100 automotive customers.
- The company utilizes a vertically integrated structure that allows it to create its own materials and machinery while ensuring quality, dependable supply, and minimized manufacturing costs.
- Andrew Capezzuto, Chief Administrative Officer and General Counsel of GDEcD, presented the award and stated that the 2021 GEAR award recognizes companies that are good corporate stewards.
- Capezzuto noted that GDEcD facilitates partnerships with local businesses to foster a team approach between communities and companies.
- Macon-Bibb County Mayor Lester Miller praised YKK, stating it is about investing in people and community rather than just making zippers.
- Robert Stamps, Vice President and Business Development Leader – Transportation Fastening Materials, and Keith Kratz, Product Development Engineer, demonstrated various YKK automotive fastening products following the award presentation.
- The ERGO-J® sheet fastener secures motor vehicle seat covering material edges, improves ergonomic efficacy during seat assembly, and reduces carpal tunnel syndrome rates for automotive workers.
- The QUICKFIT® concealed hook fastener allows operators to achieve a craftsmanship-like appearance when molded into seat foam and reduces seat weight compared to metal wire tie-down systems.
- The 5CH CONCEAL® zipper forms a perfect seam when closed, allowing seat manufacturers to significantly decrease seat back panel weight to improve fuel efficiency.
- Jim Reed, President of YKK Corporation of America, expressed gratitude for the automotive group's growth and noted the company reinvests profits into employees through its cyclical process.
- YKK aims to reduce its emissions by 50% by 2030 and achieve net-zero emissions by 2050 under its Cycle of Goodness philosophy.
