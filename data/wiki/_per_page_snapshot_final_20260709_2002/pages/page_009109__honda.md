# Honda

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-19

**Source:** [Honda has $1B plan to make gas, hybrid, electric cars on same Ohio assembly line | WYSO](https://www.wyso.org/news/2025-02-19/honda-has-1b-plan-to-make-gas-hybrid-electric-cars-on-same-ohio-assembly-line)  ·  `page_009109`

## Facts

- Honda is spending $1 billion to revamp its Ohio manufacturing plants.
- The plant revamp will allow Honda to build gasoline, hybrid, and electric vehicles on the same assembly line.
- This flexibility allows the company to quickly adapt to changing market demand.
- Mike Fischer serves as chief engineer of Honda development and manufacturing of America.
- Fischer describes the initiative as a "huge deal" for the company and calls it the "second founding of Honda."
- The initiative aims to reimagine manufacturing and set a new footprint for the manufacturing business model in Ohio.
- This sets the business model for how Honda will operate in North America and ultimately deploy it globally.
- Fischer states the initiative is gaining incredible traction internally and as products hit the market later this fall.
- The shift represents a paradigm change from solely internal combustion engines to a new way of making vehicles.
- Facilities are designed to be flexible to adapt to changing customer tastes and market demands.
- This approach allows Honda to maintain efficiency and keep operations full, which equals good value for customers in price positioning.
- Ohio was chosen because the majority of Honda's customer base is in North America.
- Ohio serves as an EV hub with heavily located R&D facilities, manufacturing, purchasing, supply base, and supply chain.
- The business model focuses on flexibility to utilize existing facilities: Marysville Auto Plant, East Liberty Auto Plant, and Engine Plant.
- Honda's business model sets a goal to build EV vehicles at 100% positioning towards 2040.
- The flexible line allows shifting production between internal combustion, hybrid, and EV vehicles based on customer demand and value proposition.
- The shift will require workforce changes, specifically for battery case manufacturing and battery assembly operations.
- Honda projects adding about 300 additional associates to its Ohio manufacturing operations.
- Honda expects to debut the new electric Acura RSX later this year (2025), which will be built in Ohio.
- Several new all-electric vehicle models with the Honda nameplate are planned thereafter, also to be built in Ohio.
- Honda has a goal of producing only zero-emission vehicles by 2040.
