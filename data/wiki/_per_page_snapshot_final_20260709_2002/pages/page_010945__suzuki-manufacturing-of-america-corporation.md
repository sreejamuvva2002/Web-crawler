# Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-05-23

**Source:** [Suzuki Celebrates 20-Years At U.S. Assembly Plant - Dirt Wheels Magazine](https://dirtwheelsmag.com/suzuki-celebrates-20-years-at-u-s-assembly-plant/)  ·  `page_010945`

## Facts

- Suzuki Motor USA, LLC is celebrating 20 years of manufacturing KingQuad ATVs at the SMAC facility.
- KingQuad ATVs first rolled off the assembly line in Rome, Georgia in May of 2002.
- KingQuad ATVs have continued to be built exclusively in the United States since 2002.
- The SMAC facility is a multi-million dollar, 35-acre manufacturing site.
- The facility is located in the center of the biggest ATV market in the world.
- The Rome, Georgia location allows access to real-world rider feedback for product refinement and timely delivery of KingQuad ATVs to Suzuki dealers worldwide.
- Suzuki was founded in 1909 and has been an innovator in engineering since its founding.
- In the early 1980s, Suzuki led the industry with the creation of the first ever four-wheel ATV in 1983.
- Kerry Graeber serves as Vice President, Division Manager MC/ATV Sales and Marketing at Suzuki.
- The SMAC facility is a key to the continued success of Suzuki’s global ATV business.
- Suzuki KingQuad ATVs built at the Rome, Georgia facility are shipped to U.S. Suzuki dealers and nearly 30 countries worldwide.
