# YKK AP America Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2026-04-13

**Source:** [YKK Corporation Completes Share Transfer Agreement for Panasonic Housing Solutions](https://www.prweb.com/releases/ykk-corporation-completes-share-transfer-agreement-for-panasonic-housing-solutions-302740089.html)  ·  `page_016149`

## Facts

- YKK Corporation and Panasonic Holdings Corporation of Japan announced the completion of the share transfer of Panasonic Housing Solutions Co., Ltd. (PHS).
- The share purchase agreement was executed on November 17, 2025.
- Through the transaction completed on March 31, 2026, the YKK Group acquired 80 percent of PHS shares previously held by Panasonic Holdings Corporation.
- Panasonic Holdings Corporation will continue to hold the remaining 20 percent of PHS shares.
- PHS will continue to operate under the Panasonic brand and PHS company name.
- The partnership combines YKK AP's advanced technological capabilities and extensive product lineup with PHS's strengths in manufacturing, sales, and engineering of interior and exterior housing equipment and construction materials.
- Oliver Stepe serves as president and CEO of YKK AP America Inc.
- Oliver Stepe stated the partnership marks an important step in advancing innovation and expanding the diversity of YKK AP's architectural products business on a global scale, including potential opportunities in North America.
- The YKK Group operates in approximately 70 countries and regions around the world.
- YKK Corporation was originally founded in 1934 as San-S Shokai.
- YKK Corporation changed its name from Yoshida Kogyo K.K. to YKK Corporation in 1994.
- The YKK Group employs 46,305 people, including 18,468 in Japan and 27,837 overseas.
- Consolidated net sales for the fiscal year ended March 2025 totaled 998.2 billion yen.
- YKK AP America Inc. is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- All building products are manufactured in world-class production facilities located in Dublin, Ga., Macon, Ga., and Lakeshore, Ontario.
- YKK AP America Inc. serves architects, glazing contractors, homebuilders, and resellers across the country together with its subsidiary brand Erie Architectural Products (Erie AP).
- YKK AP America Inc. is a subsidiary of YKK AP Inc., a global architectural products company based in Tokyo, Japan.
