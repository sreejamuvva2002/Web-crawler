## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is a leading sustainable aluminum solutions provider and the world leader in aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Novelis released its second Green Bond report on April 21, 2023.
- The report highlights sustainability-focused investments funded by a €500 million Euro Green Bond issued in March 2021.
- Investments aim to increase aluminum recycling capacity and reduce primary aluminum use across the U.S., Brazil, South Korea, the United Kingdom, and Germany.
- These investments are expected to reduce carbon emissions by more than three million tons annually once fully operational.
- Featured projects include the construction of a $365 million Automotive Recycling Center in Guthrie, Kentucky.
- Featured projects include a new recycling plant in Bay Minette, Alabama.
- Featured projects include a $1.2 million investment to install nearly 1,000 photovoltaic solar panels across 14 UBC collection centers in Brazil.
- Steve Fisher serves as President & Chief Executive Officer of Novelis Inc.
- Steve Fisher stated that the projects demonstrate Novelis's purpose of 'Shaping a Sustainable World Together' by funding investments that lower manufacturing carbon emissions and increase recycled content in products to industry-leading levels.
- Novelis cautions that forward-looking statements involve risk and uncertainty, with details provided in SEC Form 10-K for the fiscal year ended March 31, 2022, and Form 10-Q for the fiscal quarter ended September 30, 2022.
