## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corp. is a privately-held automobile and electronics manufacturing company.
- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corp. was founded in 1986.
- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corp. headquarters are located in Newnan, Georgia.
- Revenues were $380 MM in 2024, $250 MM in 2023, $90 MM in 2022, $120 MM in 2021, and $65 MM in 2020.
- Gross Profit was $190 MM in 2024, $125 MM in 2023, $45 MM in 2022, $60 MM in 2021, and $32.50 MM in 2020.
- Net Income (Loss) was $43 MM in 2024, $25 MM in 2023, $9 MM in 2022, $12 MM in 2021, and $6.50 MM in 2020.
- Total Assets were $850 MM in 2024, $720 MM in 2023, $680 MM in 2022, $650 MM in 2021, and $600 MM in 2020.
- Total Liabilities were $450 MM in 2024, $400 MM in 2023, $380 MM in 2022, $350 MM in 2021, and $320 MM in 2020.
- Total Equity was $400 MM in 2024, $320 MM in 2023, $300 MM in 2022, $300 MM in 2021, and $280 MM in 2020.
- Alan Burt holds the title of Business Owner.
- Bob Brown holds the title of President And Ceo.
- On March 15, 2024, NorthBridge PE completed an acquisition of a 100% stake for $120M, taking the company private via asset acquisition with a target company valuation of $1.20 MM.
- On September 1, 2023, a secondary transaction involving a majority stake and 65% total acquired via secondary sale/management buy-out was canceled with an undisclosed deal amount.
