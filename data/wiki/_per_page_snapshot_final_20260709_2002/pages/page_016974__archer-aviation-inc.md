# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-06-30

**Source:** [ACHR Press Release: Archer Announces Second Quarter Results, High...](https://marketchameleon.com/PressReleases/i/2148120/ACHR/archer-announces-second-quarter-results-highlighting-manufac)  ·  `page_016974`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. ramped aircraft manufacturing across its facilities in California and Georgia during Q2 2025.
- Six Midnight aircraft were in production during Q2 2025, including three in final assembly.
- Archer is building these aircraft across its "golden manufacturing line" in Silicon Valley and its high-volume facility in Georgia.
- The FAA continued conducting reviews and inspections of Archer's manufacturing operations as part of the production certificate efforts.
- LA 2028 Olympic Games selected Archer as its Official Air Taxi Provider to help align federal and local stakeholders around achieving FAA type certification and scaled operations.
- Archer partnered with the U.S. Department of Transportation, the FAA, and the White House on an Executive Order in June 2025 to promote American dominance in eVTOL aircraft.
- The June 2025 Executive Order creates a Presidential mandate for early U.S. deployments as soon as next year.
- Archer signed definitive agreements with Abu Dhabi Aviation and the Abu Dhabi Investment Office to activate the first of several planned Launch Edition programs globally.
- Archer delivered its first Midnight aircraft to the UAE and commenced its flight test program in Abu Dhabi.
- Archer expects initial commercial payments for the UAE Launch Edition program later this year.
- Archer Defense is seeing strong demand from four major allied defense programs worldwide.
- During Q2 2025, Archer acquired a patent portfolio and critical talent from Overair to accelerate its defense program.
- During Q2 2025, Archer acquired specialized defense composite manufacturing assets from Mission Critical Composites in Southern California.
- Archer reported total operating expenses of $176.1 million (GAAP) and $123.5 million (Non-GAAP) for Q2 2025.
- Archer reported a net loss of $206.0 million for Q2 2025.
- Archer reported adjusted EBITDA of $(118.7) million for Q2 2025.
- Archer held cash and cash equivalents of $1,724.0 million as of Q2 2025.
- Stock-based compensation expense for Q2 2025 was $51.8 million.
- Stellantis warrant expense for Q2 2025 was $0.8 million.
- Adam Goldstein, Archer's founder and CEO, commented on the company's second quarter 2025 results and noted execution from a position of strength with sector-leading liquidity.
