## Facts

- Metals distributor Ryerson Holding Corp. (NYSE: RYI), Chicago, IL, has acquired Fanello Industries.
- Fanello Industries is a metal service company based in Lavonia, GA.
- Fanello supplies blanking, stamping, laser cutting, bending, and machining metal solutions to industries in the Southeast.
- The company has 150 employees.
- The company has annual revenue of $20 million.
