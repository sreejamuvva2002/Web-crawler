# Rivian

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-16

**Source:** [Rivian Groundbreaking in Georgia - Southern Economic Development Council](https://www.sedc.org/news/rivian-groundbreaking-in-georgia)  ·  `page_000778`

## Facts

- Rivian held a groundbreaking ceremony for its $5 billion electric vehicle manufacturing plant.
- The plant is located at Stanton Springs North in Social Circle (Walton County), Georgia.
- The site falls within the Joint Development Authority (JDA) region of Jasper, Morgan, Newton & Walton Counties.
- When first announced in 2021, the project was the largest single economic development investment in Georgia’s history.
- The project is expected to create 7,500 jobs.
- Shane Short, President and CEO of the Development Authority of Walton County, led the recruitment efforts on behalf of the JDA.
- Serra Hall, Executive Director of the Newton County Industrial Development Authority, led the recruitment efforts on behalf of the JDA.
- Rivian’s decision highlights Georgia’s strength as a hub for advanced manufacturing and the importance of regional collaboration in securing transformational projects.
- The groundbreaking marks the start of construction and the beginning of new opportunities for workforce development, supplier growth, and long-term prosperity across the four-county region.
