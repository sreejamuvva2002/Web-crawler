## Facts

- Provides mobile EV charging units that come directly to customers anytime and anywhere in the Atlanta metro area.
- Operates 24/7 for emergency calls with fast response times across the Atlanta metro area.
- Covers a 45-mile radius from downtown Atlanta, serving the entire metro area and beyond.
- EEERescue Atlanta operators aim to arrive within 30-60 minutes for emergency calls.
- Charges virtually every EV on the road, with J1772 and Tesla adapters included.
- Supports Level 1, Level 2, and DC fast charging capabilities.
- Serves major EV brands including Tesla, Ford, GM, BMW, Audi, Rivian, Lucid, and all major EV brands.
- Offers on-site mobile charging solutions for fleets without requiring the installation of new charging infrastructure.
- Provides daily, weekly, or on-demand scheduling tailored to a fleet's operational hours.
- Offers custom pricing for recurring contracts and volume discounts for large fleets.
- Supports delivery vehicles, rideshare drivers, company EVs, and rental EVs by keeping them charged and ready.
- Atlanta Mobile EV Charging is a proud member of the EEERescue National Network — America's #1 Mobile EV Rescue Platform.
- EEERescue provides 24/7 mobile EV charging throughout Atlanta through the EEERescue National Network.
