# DENKAI America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2024-01-19

**Source:** [LCY Group invests $9.5 million in DENKAI America](https://evertiq.com/design/55083)  ·  `page_019283`

## Facts

- Taiwanese industrial giant LCY Group has made a USD 9.5 million loan to DENKAI America to expand its presence in the US.
- The deal was made by LCY's tech division LCY Technology.
- LCY Technology develops copper foil products, which it sells to domestic and foreign printed circuit board (PCB) and copper clad laminate (CCL) manufacturers.
- DENKAI Group specializes in the research, development and production of copper foil for high-end lithium-ion batteries, circuit boards and 5G substrates.
- DENKAI has two production bases located in Japan and the US.
- The two companies hope that the loan and licence agreement will help expand their respective reach and customer portfolios.
- The partnership aims to enhance research and development in rigid and flexible boards.
