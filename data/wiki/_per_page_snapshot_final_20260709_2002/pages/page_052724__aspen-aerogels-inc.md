# Aspen Aerogels, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-16

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels, Inc. received a conditional commitment from the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") for a proposed loan of up to $670.6 million under the Advanced Technology Vehicles Manufacturing loan program within DOE's Loan Programs Office ("LPO").
- The proposed loan is designated for financing the construction of Aspen's planned second aerogel manufacturing facility, referred to as the "Register Plant," located in Register, Georgia.
- Donald R. Young serves as Aspen's President and CEO.
- The conditional commitment indicates the DOE intends to fully fund the required CAPEX for the second aerogel manufacturing facility.
- The new facility is expected to scale PyroThin supply for Aspen's Thermal Barrier business.
- PyroThin is a product designed to enable increased battery safety and performance.
- Aspen's Thermal Barrier segment revenues reached $232 million for the trailing twelve months ended June 30, 2024.
- The Thermal Barrier segment revenue increase represents a 277% year-over-year growth compared to the same period in 2023.
- Aspen has secured additional EV production contracts, bringing its total number of major OEM customers in the United States and Europe to six.
- Aspen won a prestigious Automotive News Pace Award.
- The project supports the DOE's mission to onshore and re-shore domestic manufacturing technologies critical to meeting the federal goal of having half of all new vehicles sold in 2030 be zero-emissions vehicles.
- Ricardo C. Rodriguez serves as Aspen's Chief Financial Officer and Treasurer.
- The DOE and Aspen must satisfy certain technical, legal, environmental, and financial conditions before definitive financing documents are signed and the loan is funded.
- Aspen reported approximately $113 million in cash and cash equivalents as of September 30, 2024.
- Aspen expects to release preliminary Q3 2024 financial results on Wednesday, November 6, 2024, after the market close.
- A conference call to discuss Q3 2024 financial results is scheduled for Thursday, November 7, 2024, at 8:30 a.m. ET.
