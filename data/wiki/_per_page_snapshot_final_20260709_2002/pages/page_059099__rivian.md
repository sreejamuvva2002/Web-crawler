# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-17

## Facts

- Rivian is opening a new East Coast Headquarters in Atlanta by the end of 2025.
- The headquarters office will be located at the Junction Krog District building along the Atlanta Beltline.
- The office is expected to house 100 employees initially.
- The workforce is expected to grow to 500 employees as operations expand in 2026.
- The headquarters move ties into Rivian's broader $5 billion investment in Georgia.
- Rivian has an upcoming 7,500-person manufacturing plant in Stanton Springs North near Social Circle.
- Governor Brian Kemp and Mayor Andre Dickens praised the announcement, highlighting Atlanta's growing role in EV innovation and job creation.
- Rivian CEO RJ Scaringe called Atlanta "a city full of top talent and creativity."
- The company plans to partner with Georgia's universities and technical colleges as it scales.
