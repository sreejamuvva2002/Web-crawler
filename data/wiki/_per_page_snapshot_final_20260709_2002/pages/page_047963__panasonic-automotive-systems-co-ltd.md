# Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-11-20

**Source:** [Panasonic Automotive Systems joins the Open Invention Network | lifestyle.ribbon.co](https://lifestyle.ribbon.co/story/300201/panasonic-automotive-systems-joins-the-open-invention-network/)  ·  `page_047963`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd. joined the open-source patent mutual use institution Open Invention Network (OIN) on Nov. 20, 2025.
- The company is headquartered in Yokohama, Kanagawa, Japan, with Masashi Nagayasu serving as President.
- OIN protects open source software and has over 4,000 corporate members worldwide that engage in a system of mutual, royalty-free cross-licenses for patents related to the Linux System.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems contributes to multiple open-source software communities, including Automotive Grade Linux.
- The company leads the standardization of VirtIO, a key virtualization technology for Software Defined Vehicles (SDV).
- Keith Bergelt, CEO of OIN, stated that advanced automotive technology and connectivity are propelling innovation, making automobiles more integrated, safe, and secure.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems was launched on April 1, 2022, as an operating company responsible for the automotive systems business under the Panasonic Group’s operating company system.
- On December 2, 2024, the company shifted to a management structure where 80% of its shares are held by funds managed by an affiliate of Apollo Global Management, Inc., and 20% by Panasonic Holdings Corporation.
- As a global Tier 1 company with subsidiaries in eight other countries, it provides advanced proprietary technologies such as infotainment systems to automakers in Japan and overseas.
