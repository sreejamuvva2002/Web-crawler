# Blue Bird Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2017-10-30

**Source:** [ADOMANI and Blue Bird Enter into Exclusive Electric Drivetrain Supply Agreement for Type C and D School Bus Product Lines |

## Facts

- ADOMANI, Inc. signed an exclusive three-year supply agreement with Blue Bird Corporation for zero-emission electric drivetrains.
- The agreement covers Blue Bird's Type C and D school bus product lines.
- Blue Bird will purchase all of its production requirements for these drivetrains exclusively from ADOMANI, with no minimum purchase requirements.
- Blue Bird's new electric-powered school buses are expected to be in production in 2018.
- Jim Reynolds serves as President and CEO of ADOMANI.
- ADOMANI will provide drivetrains leveraging state-of-the-art components, advanced battery technology, and its engineering team to help Blue Bird provide a safe, reliable, cost-effective zero-emissions transportation solution.
- The timing for the agreement aligns with increasing availability of funding from government programs, incentives, and the unprecedented multibillion-dollar Volkswagen environmental mitigation trust.
- Phil Horlock serves as President and CEO of Blue Bird Corporation.
- Blue Bird unveiled its all-new electric bus chassis at the Student Transportation News conference in Reno in July.
- Blue Bird has been rigorously testing its electric-powered school bus and demonstrated its on-road capabilities to potential customers.
- Blue Bird is celebrating its 90th anniversary in 2017.
- ADOMANI designs advanced zero-emission electric and hybrid drivetrain systems for integration into new school buses and medium to heavy-duty commercial fleet vehicles.
- ADOMANI recently opened a new headquarters location in Corona, California, and maintains an administrative office in Silicon Valley.
- Blue Bird has sold more than 550,000 buses since its formation in 1927 and currently has approximately 180,000 buses in operation today.
- Blue Bird manufactures school buses at two facilities located in Fort Valley, Georgia.
- Blue Bird's Micro Bird joint venture operates a manufacturing facility in Drummondville, Quebec, Canada.
- Service and after-market parts for Blue Bird are distributed from its parts distribution center located in Delaware, Ohio.
- Blue Bird is the market leader in alternative fuel applications with its propane-powered and compressed natural gas-powered school buses.
