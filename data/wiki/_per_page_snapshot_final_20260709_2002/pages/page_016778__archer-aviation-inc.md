# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-13

**Source:** [Archer closes financing to complete eVTOL aircraft manufacturing facility - Aerospace Manufacturing and Design](https://www.aerospacemanufacturinganddesign.com/news/archer-closes-financing-complete-evtol-aircraft-manufacturing-facility/)  ·  `page_016778`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. is a developer of electric vertical takeoff and landing (eVTOL) aircraft.
- Archer closed agreements with Synovus Bank and Evans General Contractors LLC for the financing and development of its high-volume manufacturing facility in Covington, Georgia.
- Construction is already underway on the first phase of the Covington facility build-out.
- The first phase covers approximately 350,000 square feet to support production of up to 650 aircraft per year.
- This capacity would make it the world’s largest eVTOL aircraft manufacturing facility by volume.
- The agreement secures at least $65 million in financing, which represents a substantial majority of the cost of construction for the first phase.
- The announcement advances Archer’s strategy of creating the most capital efficient path to scaled manufacturing, in conjunction with previously announced incentives from the state of Georgia and Archer’s partnership with Stellantis.
- Archer partners with top tier aerospace suppliers, such as Honeywell and Safran, for a majority of Midnight’s components.
- Partnering with these suppliers streamlines the manufacturing and assembly process dramatically while allowing for high-volume output.
- Phase one of the high-volume manufacturing facility is on target to be completed in 2024.
- Midnight’s planned entry into service is in 2025.
- Phase two of the facility provides the capability to expand the operation to approximately 900,000 square feet.
- The phase two expansion supports long-term production targets of more than 2,000 aircraft per year.
- Adam Goldstein is Archer’s founder and CEO.
- Goldstein stated that the ability to manufacture eVTOL aircraft at scale is critical to building a successful business in the industry.
- He noted that securing the design-build and financing agreements for the world’s first and largest by volume eVTOL manufacturing facility marks a major step forward on their manufacturing buildout.
- Goldstein added that they achieved attractive terms consistent with a capital efficient strategy to get to commercialization.
- He emphasized that these agreements, along with the partnership with Stellantis, provide flexibility to ramp up capacity and become the leading scale manufacturer of eVTOL aircraft globally.
- Archer selected Synovus Bank as its financing partner and Evans General Contractors LLC as its development partner for the Covington project after considering companies with expertise in high-volume manufacturing facilities in the southeast United States.
- Goldstein stated that Archer is committed as a long-term partner to the Covington, Georgia community and is thrilled to work with companies sharing their vision for a state-of-the-art U.S. manufacturing facility.
- Archer aims to transform urban travel by replacing 60-to-90 minute commutes by car with estimated 10-to-20 minute electric aircraft flights that are safe, sustainable, low noise, and cost competitive with ground transportation.
- Archer’s Midnight is a piloted, four passenger aircraft designed to perform rapid back-to-back flights with minimal charge time between flights.
- The Midnight aircraft will be manufactured at the Covington, Georgia facility before being deployed to different customers and markets in the U.S. and abroad.
- Archer's team is based in Santa Clara, California.
