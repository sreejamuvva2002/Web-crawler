# Wabash National Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2013-12-10

**Source:** [Wabash National Builds 500,000th DuraPlate® Van](https://news.onewabash.com/wabash-national-builds-500000th-duraplate-van/)  ·  `page_014599`

## Facts

- On Dec. 10, 2013, Wabash National Corporation announced its Commercial Trailer Products group produced its 500,000th DuraPlate van trailer.
- The 500,000th DuraPlate trailer was built for Crete Carrier Corporation.
- Crete Carrier Corporation is one of the largest privately owned trucking companies in the United States.
- Tonn Ostergard, chief executive officer and president of Crete Carrier Corporation, stated that the DuraPlate van provides maximum cube space to help serve customers.
- Tonn Ostergard noted that the smooth interior walls reduce cargo damage.
- Tonn Ostergard added that the durability of the DuraPlate van provides a quality asset with low total cost of ownership and solid residual value at time of sale or trade.
- Wabash National offers a range of DuraPlate dry freight vans designed for superior performance.
- The DuraPlate HD van includes a high-strength aluminum base plate in the lower sidewall.
- DuraPlate XD-35 is the only dry van with a 35,000-pound floor rating.
- DuraPlate Pups are specifically designed to reduce total cost of ownership in less-than-truckload applications.
- Brent Yeagy, group president of Commercial Trailer Products at Wabash National, stated that reaching the milestone of building half a million DuraPlate dry vans is a significant accomplishment for associates and customers.
- Brent Yeagy noted that the achievement further evidences how customers value the durability, longevity and low total cost of ownership provided by the line of American-made DuraPlate vans.
- Nearly 20 of the top 50 truckload and less-than-truckload carriers in the United States use Wabash National DuraPlate vans.
- The DuraPlate van was introduced in 1996.
- The DuraPlate van was designed for a wide range of freight hauls, providing maximum cube capacity, exceptional durability, lightweight performance and resale values among the best in the industry.
- DuraPlate composite sidewall panels are produced by Wabash Composites.
- DuraPlate composite sidewall panels are constructed of a high-density polyethylene (HDPE) core bonded between two high-strength steel skins.
- The composite sidewall panels provide unmatched damage resistance and maximum interior width.
- They are the only composite plate sidewall panels manufactured in the United States.
- Wabash National is headquartered in Lafayette, Indiana.
- Wabash designs, manufactures, and services dry freight and refrigerated trailers, flatbed trailers, tank trailers, dry and refrigerated truck bodies, structural composite panels and products, trailer aerodynamic solutions, and specialty food grade processing equipment.
