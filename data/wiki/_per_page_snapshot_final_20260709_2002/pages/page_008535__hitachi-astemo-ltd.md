# Hitachi Astemo, Ltd.

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo is a global automotive technology company formed by the recent merger of Hitachi Automotive Systems with Honda's Keihin, Showa and Nissin Kogyo groups.
- The company has approximately 90,000 employees and holds leadership positions in AD/ADAS, software and powertrain, as well as chassis and motorcycle systems.
- Its headquarters for the Americas is located in Farmington Hills, Michigan.
- Hitachi Astemo provides fuel-system components for all of the 2.2-liter, twin-turbocharged Chevrolet racing engines in the INDYCAR Series.
- Hitachi Astemo is a technology partner with Chevrolet in the development of its highly successful INDYCAR engine program.
- Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc. manufactures and markets engine management, electric powertrain and integrated vehicle controls for major automotive manufacturers worldwide.
- Hitachi Astemo adheres to ISO/TS:16949:2009 industry standards.
- Rob Sharpe serves as senior vice president, sales & marketing, Hitachi Astemo Americas.
