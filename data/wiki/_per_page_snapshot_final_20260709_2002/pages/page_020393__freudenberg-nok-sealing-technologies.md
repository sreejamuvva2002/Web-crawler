# Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2012-01-09

**Source:** [Freudenberg-NOK Promoting New Materials, Green Technologies - AftermarketNews](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/freudenberg-nok-promoting-new-materials-green-technologies/)  ·  `page_020393`

## Facts

- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies is one of the world’s largest producers of elastomeric seals and custom molded products.
- The company helps automakers reduce emissions and improve fuel economy in three powertrain areas: gaskets, fluid power and special sealing solutions.
- Brad Norton, senior vice president of sales and marketing at Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies, noted that ‘green’ vehicles were expected to garner significant attention at the North American International Auto Show.
- Norton stated Freudenberg-NOK is a leading supplier of products helping automakers meet market demand for green vehicles.
- The company is committed to developing innovative products for cars on the road today and for future concepts.
- A common theme in many 2012 vehicles is fuel efficiency and lowered emissions.
- Freudenberg Sealing Technologies’ new Low-Emission Sealing Solutions (LESS) package addresses automaker demands with reduced-friction sealing solutions that cut wear and power loss.
- The LESS package contains innovative products that optimize the combustion process, support modern downsizing concepts and make start-stop systems possible.
- According to Freudenberg, LESS contributes to a sustainable reduction in consumption and emissions while paving the way for better performance and enhanced driving pleasure.
- LESS solutions now in production include friction-reduced dynamic seal ranges from the Elastomeric Energy-Saving Seal or the Power-Optimized PTFE Seal.
- Freudenberg-NOK has developed two new piston seals that reduce friction and lower energy consumption in applications such as mechanical transmissions for the automotive industry.
- The new piston seal products include the Torque Control Seal (TCS) and D-ring with PTFE enhancement.
- Freudenberg-NOK’s gasket division offers a global EPDM Compound and gasket design ideal for use in coolant circuits using automotive coolants in engines, battery packs, power inverters or other applications using liquid thermal management.
- This material is currently in use at four major automotive OEMs and one commercial vehicle OEM.
- The company’s gasket division has become a major partner in the electric vehicle and hybrid electric vehicle market by sealing oversized large battery housings.
- Freudenberg-NOK fluid power sealing applications innovate with new materials such as transmission seal rings, transmission thrust washers and plastic accumulator pistons to help customers save costs or improve fuel efficiency.
- Freudenberg-NOK’s transmission seal rings are the primary source for PTFE seal rings in North America.
- The company has developed substantially more durable materials and can engineer solutions for most seal, groove and bore requirements.
- Quantum, PTFE, PEEK, Torlon and other specialty resins continue to push the boundaries of high value, engineering resins for automotive sealing applications.
- Freudenberg-NOK has expanded its sealing expertise into metal replacement with thermoplastic designs.
- The industry-recognized thermoplastic accumulator piston is chemical-resistant, cost-reducing, has high temperature with high load capabilities and extremely tight dimensional stability, resulting in increased system efficiency and reduced cost.
- Norton stated that these solutions only scratch the surface of Freudenberg-NOK’s innovations and that the company will continue to solve customer needs in the automotive industry to meet current and future sealing requirements.
