# Stryten Energy LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-01-04

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Announces Executive Leadership Appointments for Next Phase of Growth » Atlas Holdings](https://www.atlasholdingsllc.com/news/stryten-energy-announces-executive-leadership-appointments-for-next-phase-of-growth/)  ·  `page_050461`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC is a U.S.-based energy storage solutions provider headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- On January 4, 2023, the company announced executive leadership changes to support its long-term growth strategy.
- Tim Vargo will transition from Chief Executive Officer to Chairman of the Board before the end of the company’s fiscal year on March 31, 2023.
- Mike Judd will be promoted from President and Chief Operating Officer to Chief Executive Officer and President, effective before the end of the company’s fiscal year.
- Petar Oklobdzija joined as Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer on February 1, 2023, leading Finance, Accounting, Treasury, Information Technology, and Strategic Sourcing functions.
- Dan Autey joined as Executive Vice President, Commercial, on January 3, 2023, responsible for leading commercial strategies across the business.
- Tim Vargo stated that Mike Judd's CEO appointment is the culmination of a succession plan developed over several years to achieve increased profitability and growth.
- Mike Judd noted that the new leadership team will help meet increased demand for sustainable, clean energy storage solutions.
- Dan Autey brings automotive and industrial battery expertise to strengthen customer service capabilities.
- Petar Oklobdzija brings tenure in operations and finance in the automotive and battery industries to accelerate growth into new and existing energy storage markets.
- Over the past year, Stryten Energy integrated three strategic acquisitions that added advanced lithium-ion and vanadium redox flow battery technologies to its energy storage chemistry portfolio.
- The company established a vertically integrated supply chain for engineered energy components to address growing market capacity needs.
- Stryten Energy is making ongoing capital investments in its manufacturing plants to meet increased demand for domestically produced, clean, and sustainable energy storage solutions.
- The company serves the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- Stryten Energy's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- The company's solutions power applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
