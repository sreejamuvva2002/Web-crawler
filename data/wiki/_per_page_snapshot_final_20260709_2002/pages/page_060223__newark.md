# Newark

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-21

**Source:** [Newark introduces TDK components for e-bike and pedelec into portfolio to enable next wave of e-mobility revolution – EEJournal](https://www.eejournal.com/industry_news/newark-introduces-tdk-components-for-e-bike-and-pedelec-into-portfolio-to-enable-next-wave-of-e-mobility-revolution/)  ·  `page_060223`

## Facts

- The announcement regarding Newark stocking TDK components for e-bike and pedelec electronically power assisted cycles was made on May 21, 2024.
- Newark now stocks a comprehensive range of electronic components for e-bike and pedelec electronically power assisted cycles from TDK, a world leader in technology innovation who is playing a vital role in advancing e-bike technology.
- As micromobility takes center stage and the demand for eco-friendly transportation surges, TDK’s contribution is powering the development and production of efficient and reliable e-bikes and pedelecs.
- Newark offers a wide range of TDK sensors essential for developing safe and reliable e-bikes, including Barometric Pressure Sensors, Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU), Ultrasonic Sensors, NTC Sensor Assembly and Systems.
- Newark offers TDK capacitors, including EMI Suppression Capacitors and Hybrid Polymer Capacitors.
- Newark offers TDK power line chokes, including Power Line Common Mode Chokes and Filters.
- Newark offers TDK circuit protection products, including Leaded Disk Varistors and Inductors.
- Dave Jones, Senior Manager for Passives at Newark, states that these key additions to the portfolio demonstrate a commitment to offering the very best in electric bicycle technology to customers, ensuring every ride is powered by components that set the industry standard for excellence and sustainability.
- TDK e-bike components are shaping the future of e-mobility, making electric bicycles a more sustainable and attractive option for transportation.
- The products are marketed under the product brands TDK, EPCOS, InvenSense, Micronas, Tronics and TDK-Lambda.
- Customers can now purchase TDK’s range of electronic components for e-bike and pedelec electronically power assisted cycles from Newark in North America, Farnell in EMEA and element14 in APAC.
