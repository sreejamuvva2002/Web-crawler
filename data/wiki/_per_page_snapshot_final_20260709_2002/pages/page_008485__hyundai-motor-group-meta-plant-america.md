# Hyundai Motor Group Meta Plant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group Completes US Metaplant America "Realizing the Future of Mobility" - The Asia Business Daily](https://www.asiae.co.kr/en/article/2025032706380802545)  ·  `page_008485`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group established a cutting-edge manufacturing innovation hub in the United States.
- The site area is four times the size of Yeouido.
- Once production reaches one million units in the U.S., Hyundai Motor Group will solidify its position as a top-tier automaker there.
- On the 26th (local time), Hyundai Motor Group held the completion ceremony for Hyundai Motor Group Meta Plant America (HMGMA) located in Ellabell, Georgia, USA.
- About 500 people attended the ceremony, including Hyundai Motor Group Chairman Chung Eui-sun, Vice Chairman Jang Jae-hoon, Hyundai Motor CEO Jose Munoz, Kia CEO Song Ho-sung, HMGMA executives and employees, Georgia Governor Brian Kemp, U.S. Congressman Buddy Carter, Georgia Tech President Ángel Cabrera, and Ambassador Cho Hyun-dong to the U.S.
- Chairman Chung emphasized that HMGMA represents value beyond innovative manufacturing capabilities and focuses on opening the future of mobility together.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp stated HMGMA is the result of long preparation and praised Hyundai Motor Group's swift decisions and execution capabilities.
- Governor Kemp added that Georgia will support Hyundai Motor Group's innovation based on excellent universities, workforce development programs, logistics, and infrastructure.
- Governor Kemp personally signed an Ioniq 5 produced at HMGMA during the celebration.
- At the event entrance, Hyundai Motor Alabama Plant (HMMA), Kia Georgia Plant (KaGA), and electrified models such as the GV70, EV9, and Ioniq 5 were displayed.
- Boston Dynamics' quadruped robot Spot assisted with guidance at the venue.
- Before the ceremony, Governor Brian Kemp toured the production line from the press shop to the assembly line with Chairman Chung.
- HMGMA is a software-defined factory (SDF) that realizes manufacturing innovation through automated technology, intelligence, and flexibility.
- The facility connects AI, robotics, and humans based on data within a human-centered manufacturing environment.
- The name 'Meta Plant' combines 'Meta,' meaning transcendence, and 'Plant,' referring to a production base, symbolizing the ambition to transcend current limits.
- Hyundai Motor Group designated HMGMA workers as 'Metapros'.
- Hyundai Motor Group decided in October 2022 to build HMGMA as its third U.S. production base.
- The plant is designed as a smart factory with an annual capacity of 300,000 units.
- Construction began after the October 2022 decision.
- Production of the Ioniq 5 started in October last year.
- Mass production of Hyundai's electrified flagship SUV model, the Ioniq 9, began this month.
- Kia models are scheduled to be added next year.
- The production lineup will expand to include Genesis vehicles in the future.
- By introducing a mixed production system, Hyundai plans to add hybrid models next year in addition to electric vehicles.
- With HMGMA's completion, Hyundai Motor Group established a production system capable of producing one million vehicles annually in the U.S.
- This achievement comes 20 years after launching its first plant in Alabama in 2005.
- Hyundai plans to invest a total of $8.6 billion by 2028 to increase capacity by 200,000 units.
- The expanded capacity will reach 1.2 million units.
- The total site area of HMGMA is 11.76 million square meters (approximately 3.55 million pyeong).
- The site includes a complete vehicle production plant with press, body, painting, and assembly lines.
- The site includes affiliated companies producing key vehicle components and a joint battery cell factory.
- Together with nearby domestic suppliers, an 'advanced future vehicle cluster' has been formed.
- Hyundai Mobis operates on the site, producing and supplying battery systems and parts modules for 300,000 vehicles annually.
- This facility is the largest overseas production base for Hyundai Mobis.
- Hyundai Glovis operates an integrated logistics center and a pre-delivery vehicle management center on the site.
- Autonomous flying drones monitor parts inventory in real-time and predict parts quantities based on demand data to supply parts promptly.
- Hyundai Steel processes and supplies automotive steel sheets made of ultra-high-strength steel at the Georgia Steel Service Center on site.
- Hyundai Steel can currently supply steel for 200,000 vehicles annually.
- Plans exist to expand Hyundai Steel's capacity to 400,000 vehicles.
- Hyundai Transys procures 420,000 seats and seat frames, key automotive components, annually.
- A joint battery cell factory between Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy Solution is under construction on the site.
- The joint battery cell factory aims for completion next year.
- The joint battery cell factory has an annual capacity of 30 GWh.
- It can supply batteries for approximately 360,000 Ioniq 5 vehicles.
- HMGMA is driving the entry and sales expansion of domestic parts suppliers in the U.S.
- The site is located about 420 km from Kia's Georgia plant and about 510 km from Hyundai Motor's Alabama plant.
- Since the establishment of HMGMA, 17 partner companies have newly entered or expanded their presence in Georgia.
