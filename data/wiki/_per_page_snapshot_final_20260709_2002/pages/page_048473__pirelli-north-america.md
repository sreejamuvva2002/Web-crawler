## Facts

- Pirelli North America was named a 2026 Ford Supplier of the Year in the “Collaboration: Seek to Understand” category at the Ford Supplier Awards & Summit.
- The award recognizes Pirelli's outstanding performance, dedication, and partnership with Ford.
- The Ford Supplier Awards & Summit took place in Dearborn on June 23rd, 2026.
- Pirelli North America announced the launch of the Scorpion™ AS 4, the next evolution in its successful Scorpion all-season family.
- The new tire delivers next-level performance with exceptional wet braking, precise handling, reduced noise, and outstanding mileage engineered specifically for North American roads.
- The Scorpion™ AS 4 is designed for crossovers, SUVs, and pick-up trucks.
- The launch continues Pirelli’s commitment to innovation and market-focused development, raising the bar in the touring all-season segment with a balanced combination of durability and comfort.
- The new tire is equipped with a 70,000-mile treadwear warranty to provide long-lasting performance in all conditions.
- The Scorpion™ AS 4 launch announcement was made from Rome, Georgia on June 4th, 2026.
- Pirelli North America kicked off its first closed-loop circular recycling initiative, turning scrap tires into new tires.
- The recycling initiative is part of a broader strategy to increase the use of recycled and bio-based materials in Pirelli's products.
- The closed-loop recycling project was recognized with the Tire Recycling Foundation’s Value Chain Collaboration Award.
- The recycling initiative launch was announced from Rome, Georgia on May 14th, 2026.
- Pirelli and Swedish company Univrses signed a partnership to enhance Cyber Tyre™ technology by integrating AI-based computer vision systems.
- Under the agreement, Pirelli acquired a 30% stake in Univrses, with an option to reach a majority stake.
- Univrses’ 3DAI™ technologies will be integrated into Pirelli’s Cyber Tyre system solutions.
- The combined technology aims to deliver vehicles that are safer and more performant.
- The technology has potential applications in ADAS and autonomous driving systems.
- The partnership will provide timely, relevant, and actionable data for a range of road management use cases.
- Insights from the partnership will enable road authorities to make better decisions and deploy resources more effectively.
- The initiative has the potential to reduce road-related accidents and save lives.
- The Pirelli-Univrses partnership was announced in Milan/Stockholm on April 29th, 2026.
