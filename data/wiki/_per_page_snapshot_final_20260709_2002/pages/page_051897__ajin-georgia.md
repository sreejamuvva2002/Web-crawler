## Facts

- Our Georgia team provided civil engineering services for this $100M project for Ajin Georgia.
- Ajin Georgia is a tier-one supplier to Hyundai Motor Group.
- The facility is situated on 83 acres.
- The facility represents a critical addition to the North American electric vehicle (EV) supply chain.
- The project supports Hyundai’s rapidly growing EV production.
- Challenges associated with the project included significant groundwater issues which required innovative drainage design solutions.
- The team leveraged local knowledge, prior efforts with an industrial development authority, and familiarity with local permitting requirements and processes.
- The team was able to fast-track the project to meet Hyundai’s demand for EV production.
- The project was delivered in just under 2 years.
- Project Type is Industrial.
