# Porsche North American Headquarters

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [How Porsche's Atlanta headquarters expansion is zooming along | Urbanize Atlanta](https://atlanta.urbanize.city/post/porsche-experience-center-expansion-images-headquarters-airport)  ·  `page_048783`

## Facts

- Porsche broke ground on the first phase of its North American Headquarters expansion project in September 2020.
- The expansion is located between Interstate 75 and downtown Hapeville, positioned at the northeast corner of the Atlanta airport.
- The multifaceted addition will be 33 acres and adjacent to the existing 27-acre headquarters that opened in 2015.
- Other attractions for the expansion include a museum and two dining concepts: Restaurant 356 and Carrera Café.
- Additions to the initial investment include a new standalone service center and a Porsche Classic Restoration facility.
- The project includes a technical new driver development course that can be linked with the existing one for almost 3 miles of track.
- Corners of the new course were specified by Porsche to be inspired by the Nürburgring carousel turn, the Laguna Seca corkscrew, and the Tail of the Dragon road.
- Other features of the new course include a dynamic zone for slaloms, a low friction circle, and a new ice hill.
- An additional parking structure is also in the works.
- Porsche Cars of North America stated the new track will open in mid-2023.
- The restoration building is scheduled to complete the expansion later in 2023.
- Porsche has not specified the exact investment amount for the expansion, only calling it a multi-million dollar development.
- Porsche representatives noted that the original facilities tallied more than a quarter-million visitors in their first five years alone.
- Hapeville City Council member at-large Brett Reichert commended the ongoing project in an email to Urbanize Atlanta.
- Reichert stated that Porsche continues to drive major investment and residential development in and around Hapeville.
