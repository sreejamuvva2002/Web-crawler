# Development Authority of Columbia County

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-13

**Source:** [News & Events - Columbia County](https://developcolumbiacounty.com/media-center/news-events/)  ·  `page_018483`

## Facts

- Club Car, a global leader in golf, consumer, and utility vehicles, announced plans to expand into a new 105,000 square foot facility in Appling, Georgia.
- Mark Wagner serves as Club Car President.
- DACC released an RFP for a custom career and company exploration tool on March 9, 2022 at noon ET, with pre-response questions due March 23, 2022 at 5:00 p.m. EST.
- DACC is the economic development organization for Columbia County, Georgia, supporting existing businesses and recruiting new companies.
- Amazon is moving forward with plans to open a second facility in Columbia County, creating hundreds of new jobs.
- White Oak Business Park grew to 612 acres as of December 29, 2020, located at I-20 Evans, GA.
- DACC received an award for Large Community Deal of the Year.
- Columbia County is part of the Augusta metro and one of the fastest-growing counties in the nation.
- Evans was recognized as the #1 Place to Live in the United States by Money Magazine.
- Columbia County is positioned along the Savannah River and within a two-hour flight from nearly three-fourths of the U.S.
- Hajek is one of the first recipients of the Destination Retail Incentive program, which offers businesses locating in The Plaza area low- to no-cost loans up to $100,000.
- Robbie Bennett, Executive Director of DACC, described Amazon's investment as a "game-changer" and stated there is "nothing like it anywhere in this market."
- DACC joined Governor Kemp's Georgia Safety Promise campaign on July 17, 2020 to help keep Georgia's economy open and safe.
- Columbia County officials are targeting Martinez for its first tax allocation district, an economic development designation that could be established by next year.
- Amazon announced a new fulfillment center in Appling, Georgia, featuring innovative robotics technology and creating 800 new full-time jobs.
- The Amazon fulfillment center announcement was made on April 27, 2020, by Governor Brian P. Kemp.
- Robbie Bennett, Executive Director of DACC, won a 40 Under 40 award in Economic Development.
- John Deere's top-producing facility is located in Columbia County, Georgia, with recent reports describing its production as having "gone full supernova."
- Columbia County was recognized as the #28 fastest-growing county in the nation, with growth across industries, population, residential markets, and businesses.
- DACC and the Columbia County Board of Commissioners unveiled White Oak Business Park at I-20 on March 19, 2018, following a December 28, 2017 groundbreaking for Club Car.
- DACC and the Columbia County Board of Commissioners announced the development of a new industrial park on January 11, 2018, after purchasing 271.9 acres on December 28, 2017.
- World Class Industries will locate in Horizon South Industrial Park in Grovetown, Georgia, and broke ground on its new Southeast facility.
