# AE Elemental

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-09

**Source:** [Elemental Strategic Metals & Ascend Elements e-battery JV kicks off in Zawiercie - PolandWeekly](https://polandweekly.com/2024/05/09/elemental-strategic-metals-ascend-elements-e-battery-jv-kicks-off-in-zawiercie/)  ·  `page_052608`

## Facts

- Elemental Strategic Metals is based in Zawiercie.
- Ascend Elements is based in Westborough, Massachusetts.
- In April, Elemental Strategic Metals and Ascend Elements announced the formation of AE Elemental.
- AE Elemental is a joint venture based in Zawiercie.
- Each company will own 50% of the JV.
- The companies will be the operators of a newly built electric vehicle (EV) battery recycling plant.
- The plant handles dismantling, discharging, and recycling EV batteries.
- The plant processes active cathode material (CAM) and cathode precursor (pCAM).
- The plant has a capacity to recycle up to 12,000 tons of batteries per year.
- The plant capacity is equivalent to approximately 28,000 EV batteries per year.
- The co-owners of AE Elemental agreed on further joint investments in the field.
- Construction of a lithium extraction facility will begin in the fall of 2024.
- The lithium extraction facility will have a production capacity of up to 20,000 metric tons of black mass per year.
- The lithium extraction facility is expected to become operational in 2026.
- The companies plan to start construction of a modern EV battery recycling plant in central Germany.
- The AE Elemental plant in Germany will have a recycling capacity of up to 25,000 tons of batteries per year.
- The German plant capacity is equivalent to approximately 58,000 EVs per year.
- Paweł Jarski serves as president of Elemental Holding.
- Mike O’Kronley serves as CEO of Ascend Elements.
- Paweł Jarski stated: “This is a step towards becoming a key global player in the industry of materials necessary for the production of new EV batteries.”
- Mike O’Kronley stated: “The AE Elemental joint venture is a milestone for Ascend Elements, representing our first commercial-scale battery recycling facility in Europe.”
