## Facts

- Volvo Group North America aims to achieve 35% electric vehicles by 2030.
- Initiatives for fuel cell vehicles are set for the second half of this decade.
- The company faces challenges in the heavy-duty zero-emission vehicle (ZEV) market, primarily related to charging infrastructure.
- The company emphasizes early engagement with utilities and stakeholders.
- The VNR Electric was launched commercially in December 2020.
- The VNR Electric exemplifies their commitment to electrifying trucking with significant fleet orders and diverse duty cycle applications.
