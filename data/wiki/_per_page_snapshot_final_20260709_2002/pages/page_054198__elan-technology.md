## Facts

- Elan Technology relocated to Liberty County from New Jersey in 1998.
- The company is America’s largest independent manufacturer of ceramic and electronic glass insulators used in the automotive, telecommunications, appliance, health, aerospace and defense industries.
- Elan has 50 employees.
- Elan operates from a 90,000 SF facility in the Midway Industrial Park.
- The company added an industrial calcinator to expand its operation in 2018.
