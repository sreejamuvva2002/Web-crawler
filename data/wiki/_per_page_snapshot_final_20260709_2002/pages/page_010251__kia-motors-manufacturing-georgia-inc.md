# Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2019-03-13

**Source:** [Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia Donates $50,000 to American Red Cross for Tornado Recovery Assistance - Kia Georgia](https://www.kiageorgia.com/kia-motors-manufacturing-georgia-donates-50000-to-american-red-cross-for-tornado-recovery-assistance/)  ·  `page_010251`

## Facts

- KMMG announced a donation of $50,000 to the American Red Cross to assist with tornado relief efforts across the region.
- KMMG team members are raising funds through the American Red Cross internally that will be added to this contribution.
- KMMG President and CEO Jason Shin stated KMMG is committed to supporting neighbors, friends, and families impacted by recent storms.
- Georgia’s Red Cross CEO Jennifer Pipa noted Kia Motors Manufacturing has proven to be a caring neighbor and friend of the Red Cross, with the gift for tornado and flood relief in the South greatly appreciated.
- KMMG is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Motors Corporation based in Seoul, Korea.
- KMMG has an annual capacity of 340,000 units.
- KMMG is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Georgia.
- KMMG began mass production on Nov. 16, 2009.
- KMMG is home to the Telluride SUV, Sorento SUV, and Optima mid-size sedan.
