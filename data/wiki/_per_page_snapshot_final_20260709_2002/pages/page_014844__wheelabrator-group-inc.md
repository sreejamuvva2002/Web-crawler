# Wheelabrator Group Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2018-03-27

**Source:** [Wheelabrator Group consolidates North American operations in Georgia](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/finishing/wheelabrator-group-consolidates-north-american-operations-in-georgia)  ·  `page_014844`

## Facts

- Wheelabrator Group Inc., a Norican Group company, announced it will consolidate its North American operations by creating a North American hub for centralized sales, service, engineering, and technology development at its LaGrange, Ga., facility.
- The company’s Burlington, Ont., facility will close in a phased shutdown.
- Services from the Burlington facility will transition to the LaGrange location by the end of 2018.
- Wheelabrator expects to expand its operations in Monterrey, Mexico this year (2018).
- Wheelabrator offers surface preparation technology, including airblast, wheelblast, and mass finishing systems.
