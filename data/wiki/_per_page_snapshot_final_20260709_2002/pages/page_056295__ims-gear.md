# IMS Gear

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-22

**Source:** [IMS Gear Building 1st Plant in Europe Outside Germany in Osijek - Total Croatia](https://total-croatia-news.com/news/ims-gear-osijek/)  ·  `page_056295`

## Facts

- German company IMS Gear is set to construct its first plant on European soil outside of Germany in Osijek.
- The future IMS Gear Croatia production facility will be constructed in the Nemetin Business Zone in Osijek.
- This marks the first large-scale production site for the company on European soil outside its home country of Germany.
- IMS Gear is a globally recognised industrial company that specialises in the development and production of extremely high-quality drive solutions for the automotive industry, as well as other branches of industry.
- Osijek Mayor Ivan Radić visited the headquarters and production facilities of IMS Gear to assess the city's economic growth and investment potential.
- The City of Osijek sold a 33,442 square metre plot of land to IMS Gear Croatia in 2023 for 1.1 million euros.
- During a tour of the company's headquarters, Mayor Radić noted that IMS Gear employs just over three thousand people in Germany, Asia, and America.
- According to Mayor Ivan Radić, every fourth car produced worldwide contains a part manufactured by IMS Gear.
- Mayor Radić stated that since taking office in 2021, Osijek has seen an increase of 4,500 registered employees and expects this trend to continue with the new facility's arrival.
- The project aims to create employment and encourage local inhabitants to remain living and working in Croatia, addressing the region's history of emigration and economic disparity.
