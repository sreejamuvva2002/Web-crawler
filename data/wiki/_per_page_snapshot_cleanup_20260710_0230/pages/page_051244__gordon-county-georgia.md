## Facts

- In addition to service industries, the economy of Gordon County is rooted in manufacturing and both heavy and light industry.
- Mohawk Industries, a leading manufacturer of flooring, is headquartered in Gordon County.
- Shaw Industries has a significant presence in Gordon County.
- Beaulieu International Group has a significant presence in Gordon County.
- LG Chem has a significant presence in Gordon County.
- Kobelco Construction Machinery America - a division of Kobe Steel - has a significant presence in Gordon County.
- Gordon County has the most interstate exits out of all of the counties in Georgia, because of its location in the middle of Interstate 75.
