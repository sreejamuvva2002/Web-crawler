## Facts

- Protestors gathered outside the Hyundai Metaplant in Ellabell, Georgia on Saturday, September 6, 2025.
- The protest was organized against ICE.
- The gathering occurred in response to an ICE raid at the HL-GA Battery Company on Thursday, September 4, 2025.
- The ICE raid resulted in the detention of 475 workers at the HL-GA Battery Company.
- Approximately 40 protestors from multiple organizations attended the gathering outside the Hyundai Metaplant.
- Nat Villasna led a chant during the protest.
- Cesar Mosquera spoke during the protest.
- Gerald Green, representing The Party for Socialism and Liberation, spoke during the protest.
- Kim spoke during the protest.
- Nilson Barahona, a member of ICE Breakers who was previously held in ICE detention for 13 months, spoke during the protest.
- Angel yelled into a bullhorn during the protest.
- A Bryan County Sheriff's Deputy drove past the protest site.
- Protestors held signs reading "Communities Over Cages" and "Ice out of SAV".
- The Hyundai Metaplant America is located in Ellabell, Georgia.
