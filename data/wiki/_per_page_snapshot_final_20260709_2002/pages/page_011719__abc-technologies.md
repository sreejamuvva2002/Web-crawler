## Facts

- On April 15, 2025, ABC Technologies announced the successful acquisition of TI Fluid Systems.
- Details of the acquisition remain undisclosed.
- The combined entity will be rebranded as TI Automotive.
- TI Fluid Systems was founded in 1929 and is a global leader in fluid storage and delivery technology for the automotive industry.
- TI Fluid Systems has over 25,600 employees across 98 manufacturing locations in 28 countries.
- TI Fluid Systems develops fluid carrying systems, powertrain products, thermal management solutions, and tank systems.
- ABC Technologies is a Canadian-based leader in highly engineered components.
- The acquisition integrates TI Fluid Systems' advanced fluid management technologies with ABC Technologies' engineering expertise.
- A representative from ABC Technologies stated the acquisition allows them to leverage combined capabilities to better serve customers in an industry demanding constant innovation.
- The merger is poised to reshape automotive supply chain dynamics as electric and hybrid vehicles gain traction.
- Advanced thermal management and fluid systems are becoming critical for electric and hybrid vehicles.
- The unified company aims to enhance its competitive edge and set new industry standards for efficiency and sustainability.
- The acquisition underscores a trend in the automotive industry where collaboration is key to navigating future complexities.
