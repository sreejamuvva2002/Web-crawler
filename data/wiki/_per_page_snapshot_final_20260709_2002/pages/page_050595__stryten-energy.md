## Facts

- During a May gathering of headquarters employees, Stryten Energy presented Rivers of Recovery with a check for $256,000.
- The donation amount was a combination of the company’s annual contribution and the results of winning a cornhole match.
- Mike Judd serves as President and CEO of Stryten Energy.
- Rodger Meyer holds the title of Executive Vice President of Operations at Stryten Energy.
- Petar Oklobdzija is Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer at Stryten Energy.
- In January 2024, the Stryten Energy leadership team attended the annual Atlas Holdings leadership meeting.
- The January 2024 Atlas leadership meeting included leaders from approximately 30 other Atlas companies.
- All participating Atlas companies engaged in January Madness, an annual cornhole tournament that raises money for charity.
- Stryten Energy split its tournament winnings with Georgia-based Iconex, which finished as the second-place team.
- Herff Jones, also an Atlas company, provided rings and banners for the winners' ring ceremony.
