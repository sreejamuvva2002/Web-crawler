# Novelis, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-05-11

**Source:** [Novelis, Inc. announces $2.5 billion investment in South Alabama Mega Site](https://www.waff.com/2022/05/11/novelis-inc-announces-25-billion-investment-south-alabama-mega-site/)  ·  `page_047563`

## Facts

- Novelis, Inc. announced a $2.5 billion investment as the anchor tenant to the South Alabama Mega Site in Baldwin County, Alabama.
- The company's initial footprint on the Mega Site property will be a thousand acres.
- The facility will be the first fully integrated aluminum mill built in the United States in 40 years.
- Novelis is an aluminum recycler and manufacturer that produces products including soda and beer cans, pickup truck beds, and aircraft components out of recycled aluminum.
- The plant will use recycled water and operate as a zero-waste facility.
- The company will need 1,000 employees to operate its state-of-the-art plant.
- President of North American Operations for Novelis, Tom Boney, stated that the search for employees has already begun.
- The company is actively looking for key roles and responsibilities, with hiring expected to start very early in the project.
- Groundbreaking for the facility is expected in early summer following the May 11, 2022 announcement.
- The plant is scheduled to be in operation in 2025.
- Novelis's North American headquarters is located in Atlanta, and the company operates on four continents.
- The company plans to reduce its carbon footprint by 30-percent by 2026 and achieve carbon neutrality by 2050.
- President of the Baldwin County Economic Development Alliance, Lee Lawson, stated that the investment will have a generational economic impact felt for decades and expects local and regional suppliers to benefit significantly.
- Local resident and plumber Larry Vaughn expressed pride in the project, noting it will help school systems and provide job opportunities for children and families to stay in the area.
