# DENSO Manufacturing Michigan Iowa Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2013-05-13

**Source:** [DENSO Manufacturing Michigan Iowa Plant Celebrates Opening Of Urbandale Facility](https://www.techshopmag.com/denso-manufacturing-michigan-iowa-plant-celebrates-opening-of-urbandale-facility/)  ·  `page_053786`

## Facts

- DENSO celebrated the opening of its new thermal assembly plant and warehouse facility in Urbandale, Iowa.
- The new facility is named DENSO Manufacturing Michigan Iowa Plant (DMIA).
- The facility represents a $500,000 investment and created seven new jobs.
- The company expects to expand the workforce to 20 employees and nearly double its production within two years.
- The Urbandale facility assembles cooling modules for the agricultural and construction industries.
- The plant handles pass-through products, including radiator assemblies and cooling modules for the construction, agriculture, and electric power industries.
- The plant localizes products that were previously assembled in the United Kingdom for U.S. customers.
- This opening is part of DENSO’s recent expansion initiative to invest nearly $2 billion in North America, including more than $750 million in the United States, to strengthen and expand product development and manufacturing capabilities across the region.
- DENSO executives and local city officials celebrated DMIA’s grand opening with a ribbon-cutting ceremony.
- Attendees included Urbandale Mayor Bob Andeweg, DENSO International America President and CEO Howard Sugi, DENSO Manufacturing Michigan President John Kumagai, DENSO Marston President David Hanson (U.K.), and DMIA Plant Manager Paolo Vercelli.
- Howard Sugi stated that while the facility extends existing thermal operations in Michigan and the U.K., it is very much an Iowa facility.
- DENSO has operated in Iowa since 1974, when it opened its first facility in Cedar Falls to support heavy-duty, off-highway engine management and others.
- Sugi expressed excitement about opportunities for the new site and DENSO’s heavy-duty business in Iowa in the years ahead.
- Paolo Vercelli credited the strong support of the Greater Des Moines Partnership, the city of Urbandale, the Urbandale Development Association, and the Urbandale Chamber of Commerce for making the opportunity possible.
- DENSO’s motto is “Protecting lives, preserving the planet and preparing a bright future for generations to come.”
