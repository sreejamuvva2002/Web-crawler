# Porsche Experience Centers

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-24

**Source:** [Porsche Experience Centers welcome the new Macan Electric to the track Intrado](https://smb.panews.com/article/Porsche-Experience-Centers-welcome-the-new-Macan-Electric-to-the-track/67bc7e91d49532008e5d8213)  ·  `page_048733`

## Facts

- On Feb. 24, 2025, Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) announced the addition of the Macan Electric to driving programs at the Porsche Experience Centers in Atlanta and Los Angeles.
- The Macan Electric joins existing PEC driving programs that feature vehicles such as the iconic 911 and versatile Cayenne.
- Michelle Rainey, Director of Porsche Experience Centers, stated that introducing new cars is special and highlighted the program's ability to demonstrate vehicle performance through high-speed laps, acceleration runs, and low grip training.
- In Atlanta, customers can choose from three Drive experiences: Macan Electric, Macan Turbo Electric, and a comparison of the Macan Turbo Electric and Macan GTS.
- In Los Angeles, PECLA offers three options including the Macan Turbo Electric drive experience and two comparison programs against the Taycan Turbo or internal combustion engine Macan GTS.
- Drive programs last 90 minutes and include a safety orientation with a Porsche Drive Instructor followed by track-based driver development modules such as handling circuit, kick plate, and low friction circle.
- PEC Atlanta and PEC Los Angeles are celebrating 10 years in the United States as fully immersive brand destinations.
- The facilities feature off-track elements including ever-changing displays of curated vehicles, simulators, unique dining experiences, retail stores for enthusiasts, meeting spaces, and iconic architecture.
- Drive programs begin at $600 per experience.
