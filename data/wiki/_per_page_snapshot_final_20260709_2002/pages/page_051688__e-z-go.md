# E-Z-GO

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-01-11

**Source:** [E-Z-GO Partners with Rollick to Build Deeper Connection with Consumers](https://sportsdigest.in/e-z-go-partners-with-rollick-to-build-deeper-connection-with-consumers/26716/)  ·  `page_051688`

## Facts

- E-Z-GO vehicles are designed and manufactured by Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a Textron Inc. company.
- E-Z-GO produces the EliTE series of electric vehicles powered by Samsung SDI lithium-ion technology.
- Gunnar Kleveland serves as President and CEO of Textron Specialized Vehicles.
- E-Z-GO was founded in Augusta, Ga. in 1954.
- E-Z-GO is globally renowned for its innovation in sustainable electric-vehicle and powertrain technology.
- E-Z-GO models include RXV and TXT fleet golf cars; Liberty, Freedom and Valor personal golf cars; and E-Z-GO Express personal utility vehicles.
- E-Z-GO became part of Textron Inc. in 1960 and currently operates as part of Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.
- The employees of Textron Specialized Vehicles are committed to a culture of compliance and pollution prevention, aiming to sustain premier status by integrating environmental performance into their business processes.
- E-Z-GO partnered with Rollick to utilize the RollickDR digital engagement module to enable consumers to complete more of their transaction online.
- E-Z-GO added Rollick’s Inventory Tools to its Aimbase lead management solution powered by Rollick prior to January 2022.
