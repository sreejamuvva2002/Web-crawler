## Facts

- Eaton Corporation plc is a Dublin, Ireland-headquartered intelligent power management company founded in 1911.
- The company provides energy-efficient solutions for electrical, hydraulic, and mechanical power across more than 180 countries.
- Eaton operates through five key segments: Electrical Americas, Electrical Global, Aerospace, Vehicle, and eMobility.
- Its electrical segments account for the vast majority of revenue through products such as circuit breakers, switchgear, power distribution equipment, and UPS systems serving data centers, utilities, industrial, and commercial markets.
- Eaton's Aerospace segment supplies fuel, hydraulic, and pneumatic systems for commercial and military applications.
- Its Vehicle and eMobility segments focus on drivetrain systems, emissions reduction technologies, and electrification solutions for both on-road and off-road vehicles.
- The company has approximately 97,000 full-time employees.
- Eaton reported revenues of $27.4 billion in 2025.
- The company announced a definitive agreement to spin off its Mobility Group via a merger with Dana Incorporated in a Reverse Morris Trust transaction valued at over $10 billion.
- The deal values the Mobility business at approximately $5.1 billion and includes a ~$1.1 billion cash distribution to Eaton.
- The spin-off transaction is expected to close in Q1 2027.
- Eaton completed the acquisition of Boyd Thermal in March, bolstering its 'grid to chip' data center cooling narrative.
- The company published its 2025 Sustainability Report highlighting $2.1 billion in R&D investment.
- Eaton's Electrical Americas segment reported 42% rolling average order growth in Q1.
- The company reported record Q1 2026 revenue of $7.5 billion with a 22.7% segment margin.
- Eaton raised its full-year organic growth guidance to 9%-11%.
- Eaton announced a strategic partnership and investment with VoltServer to develop Digital Electricity solutions for longer-distance power delivery.
