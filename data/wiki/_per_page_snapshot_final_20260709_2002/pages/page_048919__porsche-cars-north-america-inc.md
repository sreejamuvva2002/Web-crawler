# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-26

**Source:** [Porsche, Instagram and the City of Atlanta team up to celebrate five decades of hip hop | Conceptcar](https://www.conceptcarz.com/a51991/porsche-instagram-city-atlanta-hip-hop.aspx)  ·  `page_048919`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) has called Atlanta its home for 25 years.
- PCNA joined forces with Instagram and the City of Atlanta Mayor's Office of Film, Entertainment & Nightlife to celebrate five decades of hip hop.
- A private event celebrating Atlanta's hip-hop community will be held on October 27 at the Porsche Experience Center Atlanta.
- The Porsche Experience Center Atlanta is located next to Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport.
- Select musical artists will experience Porsche's first all-electric sports car, the Taycan, which was launched in 2019, at the center's new West Track.
- Ayesha Coker serves as Vice President of Marketing for Porsche Cars North America, Inc.
- Atlanta City Councilmember Marci Collier Overstreet stated that hip hop has emerged as a powerful form of self-expression and artistic innovation shaping the music industry and influencing fashion, language, and contemporary global culture.
- Malika Quemerais serves as Head of Artist Partnerships for Meta Platforms, Inc.
- The Atlanta City Council will issue city proclamations to several musical pioneers during the year-long celebration of hip hop's anniversary.
- Guests at the event will enjoy iconic Atlanta DJ sets from KP The Great, DJ OHSO, and Mix Master David.
