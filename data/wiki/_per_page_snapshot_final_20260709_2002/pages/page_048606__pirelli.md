## Facts

- Pirelli recorded an excellent financial performance in 2023 and successfully met its annual targets.
- Pirelli executed strategic initiatives contained in the Industrial Plan (2021/2022-2025) spanning business model optimization, innovation, competitiveness, operational efficiency, and digitization.
- Pirelli exceeded for the first time the one-billion-euro level in Adjusted Ebit in 2023.
- Pirelli achieved profitability at 15.1% in 2023.
- Pirelli recorded a net cash flow before dividends of 509 million euro in 2023.
- Pirelli reduced financial leverage to 1.56 times Adjusted Ebitda in 2023.
- Pirelli is pursuing carbon neutrality by 2030 and Net Zero by 2040.
- Pirelli reached its CO2 reduction targets two years ahead of schedule in 2023.
- Pirelli obtained the highest overall score by far in the Auto Components and Automotive sectors on the 2023 S&P Global Dow Jones Sustainability Index.
- Pirelli secured a place in the 2023 CDP Climate A list as a global leader in the fight against climate change.
- Pirelli's strategic vision focuses on opportunities linked to electrified cars, connectivity, and sensors.
- Pirelli's Elect technology is applied to its entire family of products destined for electrified cars.
- Pirelli is a partner with all Premium and Prestige carmakers for its EV tire products.
- Pirelli developed CyberTyre, a sensor-fitted tyre representing a competitive advantage in the connected automotive world.
