# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-02-29

**Source:** [Bonnell Investing in Niles to Expand its Automotive and Specialty Markets - Light Metal Age Magazine](https://www.lightmetalage.com/news/industry-news/extrusion/bonnell-investing-in-niles/)  ·  `page_053082`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation.
- Bonnell Aluminum announced it will be investing approximately $18 million over the next 12 months to fund an expansion project.
- The investment will fund the purchase of a new state-of-the-art aluminum extrusion line at its AACOA facility in Niles, Michigan.
- The new line will be comprised of a state-of-the-art 3,600-ton extrusion press, housing a 9-inch container, handling systems, and ancillary equipment.
- Capacity is estimated at approximately 16 million lbs.
- The project also includes additional floor space to accommodate future value-added fabrication capacity.
- Planned startup of the new extrusion line is the second quarter 2017.
- AACOA primarily serves the consumer durables and machinery and equipment markets.
- The Niles facility serves the automotive market due to its regional location.
- Brook Hamilton is president and general manager for Bonnell Aluminum.
- Ira Endres is vice president Sales and Marketing for Bonnell Aluminum.
- Bonnell Aluminum operates a facility in Newnan, Georgia.
