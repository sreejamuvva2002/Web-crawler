# Novelis

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Novelis is a global leader in aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Novelis has 13,250 team members working across 29 facilities in North & South America, Europe and Asia.
- The aluminum recycling process uses only 5% of the energy it takes to produce primary aluminum, lowering carbon emissions by as much as 95%.
- Novelis launched its company-wide vision, Novelis 3x30, in October 2024.
- Novelis aims to increase average recycled content from 63% in FY24 to 75% by FY30.
- Novelis aims to become the lowest emissions flat rolled products (FRP) aluminum provider with less than 3 tonnes of CO₂e per tonne of FRP shipped by the end of 2030.
- Novelis operates a 40.6-acre used beverage can recycling facility in Greensboro, Ga.
- The Greensboro, Ga. facility was Novelis's first stand-alone used beverage can recycling facility and is responsible for pioneering the majority of its recycling technologies.
- Novelis established the Novelis Innovation Hub at Georgia Tech in 2019 to collaborate with students on aluminum research and development.
- The collaboration with Georgia Tech focuses on creating advanced alloy technologies, lightweighting solutions, and closed-loop recycling systems that reduce waste and improve material efficiency.
- Novelis is the official recycling partner of Mercedes-Benz Stadium.
- As the official recycling partner of Mercedes-Benz Stadium, Novelis collects aluminum cans at Recycle for Good bins throughout the facility.
- Novelis has leveraged its stadium recycling efforts to fund and build six Habitat for Humanity homes on Atlanta's historic Westside.
- Novelis has an annual goal of collecting 120,000 pounds of aluminum, a value equal to the cost of one Habitat home.
