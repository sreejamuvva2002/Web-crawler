# Great Dane

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-08-05

**Source:** [Doggett Industries Acquires Great Dane Trailer Dealership](https://www.doggett.com/dev/newsroom-alias/press-releases/179-leslie-doggett-industries-acquires-great-dane-trailers)  ·  `page_055301`

## Facts

- Leslie Doggett Industries through its newly acquired Truck Enterprises Group has acquired the privately-held Great Dane Trailer distributor/dealer for El Paso, Laredo and McAllen/Pharr, Texas.
- The dealership territory stretches to Las Cruces, New Mexico.
- Doggett purchased this business from El Paso’s legendary trucking magnate and International Truck dealer, Royal Jones.
- The purchase price was undisclosed.
- This acquisition follows Doggett’s acquisition of El Paso’s Truck Enterprises two weeks ago.
- It also follows Doggett’s acquisition of the Freightliner dealerships throughout the State of Arkansas last year.
- The addition of Great Dane complements Doggett’s existing Freightliner and Western Star dealerships in El Paso, Laredo and McAllen/Pharr.
- Doggett will now sell and service flatbeds, dry freight and refrigerated vans with their over-the-road truck offerings.
- Paul Burk, SVP of the Doggett Truck Group, stated that adding Great Dane products to Doggett’s world class lineup allows further partnership with trucking companies and truck dealers on a measurable and meaningful basis.
- Burk expressed pride in partnering with Great Dane and expected extraordinary growth with their products alongside Freightliner, John Deere, Toyota, LinkBelt, and Ford businesses.
- Great Dane’s history dates back to 1900, having established itself as the transportation solutions leader of today delivering innovative products and exceptional services for trucking customers and employees.
- Great Dane engineers and builds the industry’s premier high-quality trailers and truck bodies.
- Great Dane manufactured 49,000 trailers in 2018.
- Great Dane’s headquarters are in Savannah, Georgia with manufacturing plants throughout North America.
- Doggett is a Houston-based diversified heavy equipment dealer for seven first-tier manufacturers that are either number 1 or 2 in their respective industries.
- Doggett's dealership portfolio includes John Deere construction and forestry equipment across 17 dealerships, Toyota Industrial Equipment forklifts & material handling across 7 dealerships, Freightliner and Western Star on-highway and vocational trucks across 9 dealerships, Link Belt Cranes across 3 dealerships, a Ford auto and truck dealership, and now Great Dane Trailers across 3 dealerships.
- Leslie Doggett Industries was founded by Leslie Doggett and Brady Carruth in 1993 with 17 employees.
- The Houston Chronicle ranked Doggett in 2019 as Houston’s 12th largest private company.
- Doggett reported annual sales exceeding US $1 billion.
- Doggett employs over 1400 full-time staff, including more than 500 factory trained and certified, highly professional technicians.
- Doggett operates as a family-owned heavy equipment, highway truck and automotive dealership group without outside investment of any kind.
