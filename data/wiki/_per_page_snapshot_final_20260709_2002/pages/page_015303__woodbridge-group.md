## Facts

- Woodbridge announced the release of its 2024 Sustainability Report on Feb. 24, 2025.
- The report highlights the company’s progress and approach to integrating sustainability throughout the organization.
- Juho Song serves as General Counsel and Compliance Officer at Woodbridge.
- Woodbridge integrates Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles into every facet of its organization.
- Woodbridge maintains corporate headquarters in Mississauga, Ontario, Canada, and automotive headquarters in Troy, Michigan, USA.
- The company employs more than 7,000 teammates across over 40 locations in nine countries.
- Woodbridge provides material technologies for automotive, commercial, recreational, packaging, healthcare, and building product applications.
- Woodbridge offers prototyping, consulting, technical support, chemical R&D, and accredited laboratory testing services.
- Woodbridge has established strategic partnerships to deliver fully-integrated systems and solutions.
