# Developing Markets for Zero Emission Vehicles in Short Haul Goods Movement

## Facts

- The report 'Developing Markets for Zero Emission Vehicles in Short Haul Goods Movement' was published on 12/10/2020.
- It examines the potential market share for electric heavy duty trucks (EHDTs) via simulation modeling, case studies, interviews, and a survey.
- It assesses the impacts of EHDTs on freight operations.
- It compares the costs and benefits of using diesel, natural gas hybrid, and all-electric vehicles for 2020, 2025, and 2030.
- It presents recommendations for promoting and increasing the market share of EHDTs and hybrids.
- The report was authored by Giuliano, G., Dessouky, M., Dexter, S., Fang, J., Hu, S., and Steimetz, S.
- The copyrighted publication can be accessed through the University of California, Davis National Center for Sustainable Transportation website.
