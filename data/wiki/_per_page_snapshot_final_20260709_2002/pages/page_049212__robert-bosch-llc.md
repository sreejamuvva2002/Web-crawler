# Robert Bosch LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2012-03-13

**Source:** [Robert Bosch LLC Wins Palmetto Workforce Partnership Award - Dorchester County Economic Development](https://www.dorchesterforbusiness.com/news/robert-bosch-llc-wins-palmetto-workforce-partnership-award/)  ·  `page_049212`

## Facts

- Representatives from the Robert Bosch Charleston plant received the 2011 Palmetto Workforce Partnership Award from the SC Department of Employment and Workforce on March 13, 2012.
- The award was presented during a luncheon on February 22 in Columbia, SC.
- Robert Bosch Charleston won this year’s Palmetto Workforce Partnership Award in the large business category.
- The winner was selected based on 5 categories: job creation, innovative partnerships, support and development of the workforce, quality of work environment, and environmental awareness.
- Through Bosch Charleston’s holistic business excellence approach using the EFQM Model, there was a great story to tell in each category that landed the company the award.
- Larry Lautenschlager, Senior Vice President Manufacturing of Robert Bosch, LLC, said the recognition shows they are on the right path of balancing efforts between a successful business and being a good corporate citizen and employer in South Carolina, and accepted it for associates' dedication and community involvement.
- Each year, the SC Department of Employment and Workforce joins forces with workforce partners to recognize the state’s top employers for jobs provided and community contributions.
- Michael L. "Mike" Thurmond, former Commissioner of Labor for the state of Georgia, served as keynote speaker and presented Bosch with the award after addressing community leaders and workforce professionals.
- Robert Bosch LLC in Charleston, SC is an automotive component manufacturing plant producing Clean Diesel products, ESP and ABS active safety systems and gasoline systems products.
- Bosch Charleston currently employs about 1700 associates.
- In the U.S., Canada and Mexico, the Bosch Group manufactures and markets automotive original equipment and aftermarket products, industrial drives and control technology, power tools, security and communication systems, packaging technology, thermotechnology, household appliances, solar energy, healthcare telemedicine and software innovations.
- Having established a regional presence in 1906, Bosch employs over 22,500 associates in more than 100 locations with sales of $9.8 billion in fiscal year 2011 per preliminary figures.
- The Bosch Group comprises Robert Bosch GmbH and its more than 350 subsidiaries and regional companies in some 60 countries, or roughly 150 countries if sales and service partners are included.
- More than 300,000 associates generated sales of 51.4 billion euros ($71.5 billion) in automotive and industrial technology, consumer goods, and building technology for the Bosch Group in fiscal year 2011.
- The Bosch Group spent more than 4 billion euros ($5.5 billion) for research and development in 2011 and applied for over 4,100 patents worldwide.
