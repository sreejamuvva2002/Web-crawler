# Archer Aviation

## Facts

- Archer Aviation's Midnight electric air taxi successfully completed its inaugural uncrewed, untethered hover flight.
- The test flight marks a critical step towards FAA type certification and commercial launch by 2025.
- The five-seat eVTOL is designed for rapid, short-hop flights.
- The Midnight aims to be cost-competitive with ground rideshare.
- Archer has partnerships with United Airlines.
- Stellantis is a manufacturing partner for the Midnight.
- Archer has a significant contract with the U.S. Air Force.
- Archer is rapidly advancing its flight test program.
- Archer is constructing a large production facility in Georgia.
- The company received a special airworthiness certificate from the FAA in August to begin testing the electric vertical takeoff and landing (eVTOL) design.
- A nonconforming Midnight prototype completed its inaugural test flight.
