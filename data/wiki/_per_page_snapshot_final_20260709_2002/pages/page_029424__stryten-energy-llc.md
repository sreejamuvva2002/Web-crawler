## Facts

- Stryten Energy is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- The company partners with some of the world’s most recognized companies to meet the growing demand for reliable and sustainable energy storage capacity.
- Stryten's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium and vanadium redox flow batteries.
- The company produces intelligent chargers and energy performance management software that keep people on the move and supply chains running.
- Stryten powers everything from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains and trucks.
