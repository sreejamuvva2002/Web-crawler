# State of Georgia Government

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2026-03

**Source:** [Gov. Brian Kemp approves taxpayer rebates of up to $500 this summer](https://www.ajc.com/politics/2026/03/georgia-taxpayers-will-get-rebates-of-up-to-500-this-summer/)  ·  `page_009912`

## Facts

- Gov. Brian Kemp signed a state budget that includes more than $2 billion in tax relief, bonuses for teachers and state employees, and plenty of other new spending.
- The amended $43.7 billion budget covers fiscal year 2026, which ends in June.
- The budget includes $1.2 billion in income tax rebates for Georgians.
- Single filers will receive up to $250 in income tax rebates.
- Heads of household will get up to $375 in income tax rebates.
- Married couples filing jointly will get up to $500 in income tax rebates.
- Tax rebate checks are scheduled to go out later this year if legislators approve a separate bill authorizing the refund.
