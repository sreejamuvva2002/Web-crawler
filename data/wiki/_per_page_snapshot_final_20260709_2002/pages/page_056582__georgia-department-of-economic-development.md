# Georgia Department of Economic Development

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2015-09-21

**Source:** [Macon Celebrates British, Irish and Georgia Ties at Its 'BIG Dinner' - Global Atlanta](https://www.globalatlanta.com/macon-celebrates-british-irish-and-georgia-ties-at-its-big-dinner/)  ·  `page_056582`

## Facts

- The 'Big Dinner' event took place on Sept. 15 at the Mandarin restaurant on Riverside Drive in Macon.
- The event was hosted by Chris Smith, honorary consul of Denmark, who lives in Macon.
- Deputy Commissioner for International Relations Ember Bishop briefed guests on the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s efforts to increase inbound investment to Georgia.
- Bishop also briefed guests on efforts to increase the state’s exports.
- Bishop stated she would work closely with the Atlanta-based consular corps and binational chambers in generating business for the state.
- Georgia will be represented at the Dubai Airshow to be held Nov. 8-12.
- Companies and organizations represented at the dinner included Kumho Tire USA, Dinex Emission Inc., YKK USA Inc., Macon Economic Development Commission, and Macon-Bibb Industrial Authority.
