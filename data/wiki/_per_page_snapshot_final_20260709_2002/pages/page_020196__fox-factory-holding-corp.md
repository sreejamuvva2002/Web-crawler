# Fox Factory Holding Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-02-10

**Source:** [Fox Factory Announces Strategic Board Refresh - Motor Sports NewsWire](https://motorsportsnewswire.com/2026/02/10/fox-factory-announces-strategic-board-refresh/)  ·  `page_020196`

## Facts

- Fox Factory Holding Corp. is headquartered in Duluth, GA.
- On February 10, 2026, the company announced a strategic board refresh and entered into a cooperation agreement with Engine Capital to enhance operational performance and profitability.
- The board will appoint two new independent directors, including Alan L. Bazaar, CEO and Co-Chief Investment Officer of Hollow Brook Wealth Management LLC.
- The second independent director will be identified by Engine Capital and approved by the Board, with expected expertise in manufacturing excellence and margin improvement initiatives.
- A Board-level Transformation Committee will be formed to drive operational excellence and improve profitability, chaired by Sidney Johnson and including both new directors plus an additional existing independent director.
- The Transformation Committee will oversee management’s execution of operational improvement initiatives targeting enhanced profitability, working capital efficiency, and disciplined capital allocation.
- Chairman Dudley Mendenhall and Ted Waitman will retire from the Board effective at the company’s 2026 annual meeting of stockholders.
- Jefferies LLC is serving as financial advisor to Fox Factory, while Wachtell, Lipton, Rosen & Katz is serving as legal counsel for the company.
- Olshan Frome Wolosky LLP is acting as legal counsel to Engine Capital.
- Fox Factory designs, engineers, and manufactures premium shocks, suspension systems, and components for leading powered vehicle and bicycle original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).
- The company’s brand portfolio includes FOX, Marucci, and Method Race Wheels, serving professional athletes and enthusiasts globally.
- Fox Factory supplies aftermarket products through a global network of retailers, distributors, and direct-to-consumer channels.
- The company acquires complementary businesses to integrate engineering and manufacturing expertise beyond its core shock and suspension segment.
- CEO Mike Dennison stated the board refresh reflects a shared commitment to unlocking value embedded in the business through stronger operational execution and enhanced financial performance.
- Arnaud Ajdler, Managing Partner of Engine Capital, stated the company is well positioned to drive meaningful and sustainable long-term shareholder value with a sharpened focus on execution and disciplined cost management.
