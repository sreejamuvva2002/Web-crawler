# Seoyon E-Hwa Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-31

**Source:** [Seoyon E-Hwa Co., Ltd. (Formerly Hanil E-Hwa Co., Ltd.) - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/seoyon-e-hwa)  ·  `page_001335`

## Facts

- Manufacturer of automotive interior products such as seats, door trim, consoles, luggage-space side trim, headlining, etc.; as well as exterior parts and equipment such as bumpers.
- The Company provides the majority of its products to Hyundai Motor and Kia Motors.
- Percentage of sales by destination as of December 31, 2024: Hyundai Motor accounted for 53%, while Kia Motors comprised 34%.
- Main shareholder Seoyon Co., Ltd. holds a 48.70% interest ratio as of Dec. 31, 2024.
- Main shareholder Yang-Seok Yu holds a 5.45% interest ratio as of Dec. 31, 2024.
- Product line includes door trim assemblies with arm rests, map pockets, inside handles, and speaker grilles.
- Product line includes bumpers with front/rear bumper covers and radiator grilles.
- Product line includes console assemblies with upper covers, floor consoles, arm rests, and rear covers.
- Product line includes package tray assemblies with speaker grilles, package trim boards, and child anchor covers.
- Product line includes pillar trim assemblies.
- Product line includes seat assemblies with seat belt buckles, cushions, headrests, and back seats.
- Product line includes screen assemblies with sunshades and housing.
- Established Hanil E-Hwa Interior Systems Alabama LLC in the USA in May 2005.
- Established Hanil E-Hwa Interior Systems Georgia LLC in the U.S.A. in April 2010.
- Established Seoyon E-Hwa Savannah in the U.S. in October 2022.
- Incorporated Seoyon E-Hwa Auburn in the U.S. in April 2023.
- Established Seoyon E-hwa Texas in the U.S. in March 2024.
- The company was established in April 1972 and changed its name to Seoyon E-Hwa Co., Ltd. in January 2016.
