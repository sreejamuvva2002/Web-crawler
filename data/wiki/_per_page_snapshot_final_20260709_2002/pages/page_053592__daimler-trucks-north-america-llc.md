# Daimler Trucks North America LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2017-06-22

**Source:** [Daimler Trucks North America Expands its Parts Distribution Network - Thomas Built Buses](https://thomasbuiltbuses.com/resources/news/daimler-trucks-north-america-expands-its-2017-06-22/)  ·  `page_053592`

## Facts

- Daimler Trucks North America (DTNA) announced the addition of two new parts distribution centers to its supply chain network.
- The new facilities are located in Grimes, Iowa and Whitestown, Indiana.
- The new facilities will hold more than $15 million of inventory.
- The new facilities will hold over 20,000 parts.
- The facility in Grimes is scheduled to be completed January 2018.
- The Whitestown location is scheduled to be fully operational at the same time as the Grimes facility completion.
- Jay A. Johnson serves as the general manager of Supply Chain Network for DTNA.
- DTNA’s supply chain system has been architected to be anticipatory in nature, with the goal of having parts at service locations when customers need them.
- DTNA utilizes Dedicated Delivery and Variable Cut-Off processes to enable same-day part delivery to service locations if a part is not in stock.
- Daimler Trucks North America LLC is headquartered in Portland, Oregon.
- DTNA manufactures, sells, and services commercial vehicles under the Freightliner, Western Star, Detroit, and Thomas Built Buses nameplates.
- DTNA is a Daimler company.
