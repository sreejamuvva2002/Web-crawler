# Woory Industrial Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-07

**Source:** [Hyundai supplier to build manufacturing facility in Dublin](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/hyundai-supplier-build-manufacturing-facility-193714475.html)  ·  `page_015460`

## Facts

- Woory Industrial Co., Ltd. is a South Korea-based company that makes heating, ventilation and air conditioning components and systems.
- The company will invest $18 million into a new manufacturing facility in Dublin, Georgia.
- The investment will create more than 130 jobs.
- Woory Industrial is developing a new line of products for EVs and hydrogen-fueled cars.
- The facility will supply the Hyundai Metaplant in Bryan County.
- The facility will also supply Rivian's EV facility planned for Social Circle near Athens.
- The facility is located at 404 Kellam Road in Dublin.
- This facility will be Woory Industrial's first in the United States.
- The facility will produce electrical heaters, control units and actuators for EV and internal combustion carmakers in the states.
- Operations at the new facility are slated to begin in November.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Woory Industrial is helping Georgia on its path to becoming the e-mobility capital of the nation.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that the emerging industry continues to bring historic levels of new jobs and investment to communities across the state thanks to a highly-skilled workforce and reliable infrastructure network.
- Governor Brian Kemp expressed confidence that Woory will have a long and beneficial partnership with Dublin-Laurens County.
- Dublin Mayor Joshua Kight stated that fostering quality and sustainable growth is a goal of the city of Dublin.
- Mayor Joshua Kight noted that Woory's commitment to place an existing manufacturing building back into service helps promote more infill development while creating new career paths for the community.
- Fred Williams, chair of the Board of Directors of the Dublin-Laurens County Development Authority, expressed excitement about welcoming Woory to the Dublin-Laurens County community.
- Fred Williams stated that Dublin and Laurens County has a rapidly growing automotive sector due to its geographic location on Interstate 16 and access to a skilled and trained workforce.
- Fred Williams noted that Woory's partnership with area education systems and training providers will ensure training programs are in place to fill Woory's talent pipeline for years to come.
