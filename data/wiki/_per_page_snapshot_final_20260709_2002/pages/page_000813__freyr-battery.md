# FREYR Battery

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-12

**Source:** [FREYR Battery Announces Austin, Texas as Location for New Corpor](https://www.gurufocus.com/news/2688681/freyr-battery-announces-austin-texas-as-location-for-new-corporate-headquarters)  ·  `page_000813`

## Facts

- FREYR Battery selected Austin, Texas as the location for its new global headquarters.
- The selection aims to geographically align the Company’s workforce with its operations and strategy to provide solar modules made in the United States.
- This announcement coincides with the ongoing ramp of production at FREYR’s G1 solar module facility in Wilmer, Texas.
- The Wilmer facility already employs more than 1,000 people across the Wilmer and Dallas communities.
- Austin is strategically located near FREYR’s operating U.S. solar module manufacturing facility in Wilmer, Texas.
- Austin provides access to an emerging talent pool within the booming, business-friendly Texas economy to support the Company’s growth aspirations.
- FREYR’s global headquarters will be located at 1211 E 4th St., Austin, TX 78746.
- Daniel Barcelo serves as FREYR’s Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer.
- Barcelo stated that establishing the headquarters marks an important step in executing a strategic plan to establish an integrated U.S. solar + battery storage manufacturing enterprise built on advanced technology.
- The company intends to bring more than 1,000 new American jobs to the Texas economy as it builds teams in Austin and Wilmer.
- FREYR plans to work with partners across industry and government to invest in critical U.S. infrastructure.
- In conjunction with the relocation to Texas, FREYR has entered into a definitive agreement to sell its 368-acre site in Coweta County, Georgia, to an undisclosed party.
- The sale of the Coweta County site is for gross sales proceeds of $50 million.
- The transaction is expected to close on February 15, 2024.
- Estimated net proceeds to FREYR are expected to total $22.5 million following repayment of previously received state and local grants.
- In December 2024, FREYR completed a transformative transaction positioning it as one of the leading solar manufacturing companies in the U.S., with a complementary solar and battery storage strategy.
- The Company is exploring value optimization opportunities across its portfolio of assets in Europe.
