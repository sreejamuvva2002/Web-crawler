## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System, LLC will invest $9 million to expand its seating manufacturing operations in West Point, Georgia.
- The expansion will create more than 150 jobs in Troup County.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp stated that Georgia’s highly skilled workforce continues to attract manufacturing jobs statewide and highlighted Hyundai TRANSYS’ continued investment in West Point as proof of west Georgia's capability.
- Governor Kemp thanked Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System for its ongoing investment in Georgia and expressed anticipation for the opportunities the expansion will create in Troup County.
