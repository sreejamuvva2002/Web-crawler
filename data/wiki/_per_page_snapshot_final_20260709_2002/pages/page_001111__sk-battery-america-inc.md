- The last working day for employees was March 6, 2026, and their employment will terminate on May 6, 2026.
- The company said affected employees will receive pay equivalent to the 60-day notice period.
- The facility is located at 1760 SK Blvd in Commerce, Georgia.

- SKBA belongs to the SK Group, which is among the largest industrial conglomerates in South Korea and has businesses in energy, telecommunications, finance, and construction.
- The company was established in 2019 and serves the expanding electric mobility market in North America.
- Its business focuses on EV battery technology, including high-energy-density lithium-ion cells, NCM9 battery technology, and power battery electric vehicles (BEVs).
- SK Battery America has invested approximately $2.6 billion in constructing its EV battery facilities in Georgia.
