## Facts

- Georgia is at the epicenter of the nation’s electric vehicle revolution, and its system of higher education is working to drive the industry forward.
- Georgia’s college and university systems are prioritizing research and development and gathering top researchers and players together as a prime incubator for collaboration and innovation to support the quickly evolving EV field.
- In April, the University of Georgia hosted more than 150 people for a one-day Electric Mobility Summit.
- The summit was held to learn more about the current state of electric mobility and how educational institutions can support the expansion of the EV industry.
- The summit connected researchers with key stakeholders including government officials, economic development professionals, and leaders from companies like Kia, Rivian, and SK Battery America.
