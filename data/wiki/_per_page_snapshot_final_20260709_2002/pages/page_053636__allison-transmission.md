## Facts

- Allison Transmission completed the acquisition of Dana Incorporated's Off-Highway Drive & Motion Systems Business, creating a premier, global industrial leader.
- The powerful combination of propulsion technology and global service network maximizes efficiency and productivity.
- Allison offers a broad portfolio that includes conventional, electric-hybrid and fully electric propulsion solutions.
- Allison propulsion solutions deliver unmatched quality, reliability and durability across a wide variety of applications.
- Allison has an Authorized Service Network of approximately 1,600 dealer and distributor locations worldwide that provide sales and service support for Allison products.
- The company employs approximately 6,200 certified technicians worldwide to provide sales and service support for its products.
- Unlike automated manual transmissions, Allison Automatics incorporate a hydraulic torque converter and enable drivers to navigate difficult terrain without the concern of the vehicle getting stuck.
