## Facts

- Over the last 5 years, Newton County has attracted $6.9 billion in investments.
- Over the last 5 years, Newton County has created 9,000 new jobs.
- All companies listed on the page employ over 150 employees.
- Newton County, particularly the city of Covington, is a cornerstone of Georgia's film and entertainment industry.
- Covington is known as the 'Hollywood of the South' and has hosted over 130 productions since the 1950s.
- Iconic titles filmed in Newton County include In the Heat of the Night, The Vampire Diaries, and Remember the Titans.
- In the past year, Newton County welcomed high-profile productions including Marvel films such as Ant-Man and Captain America: Civil War.
- The NCIDA opposes any measures that would negatively impact the business climate, are discriminatory in nature, or harm the ability to attract business investment, advancement, and promotion in Newton County.
