# Woory Industrial Holdings Co.,Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Engages in the manufacture and sale of automotive air conditioning systems and electronic components in South Korea and internationally.
- Offers HVAC, active air flap, HLLD, swivel, bi-function shield, and charging door actuators.
- Provides high and low-voltage positive temperature coefficient, coolant, and cathode oxygen depletion heaters.
- Supplies control heads, clutch coils, power modules, blower resistors, current sensors, parking assist systems, and seat belt reminder sensors.
- Provides coolant and expansion valves.
- Manufactures motor on drum electric parking brake (EPB) and cable puller EPB systems.
- Was formerly known as Woory Industrial Co. Ltd. and changed its name to Woory Industrial Holdings Co.,Ltd. in May 2015.
- Was incorporated in 1989.
- Is headquartered in Yongin-si, South Korea.
- Myung-Joon Kim serves as Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, age 63.
- Han-Kyu Hwang serves as Director, age 79.
- Ho-Kee Shin serves as Independent Outside Director, age 83.
