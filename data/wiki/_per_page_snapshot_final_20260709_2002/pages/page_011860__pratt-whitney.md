# Pratt & Whitney

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2017-02-27

**Source:** [Pratt & Whitney Announce Georgia Expansion, Add 500+ New Careers | Georgia Public Broadcasting](https://www.gpb.org/blogs/georgia-at-work/2017/02/27/pratt-whitney-announce-georgia-expansion-add-500-new-careers)  ·  `page_011860`

## Facts

- Pratt & Whitney has been a major economic driver in Georgia since 1984.
- Pratt & Whitney committed more than $380 million in new investment to its Columbus campus.
- The investment is expected to lead to more than 500 new careers.
- Governor Nathan Deal stated, “This is a great day for Georgia,” while announcing the major investment from the North Wing of the State Capitol alongside Pratt & Whitney’s leadership and other economic development members.
- Pratt & Whitney designs and builds jet engines for commercial and military aircraft.
- Pratt & Whitney is a unit of United Technologies.
- The newly added careers will span administration, supervisory and technical support, production, and maintenance roles.
- Tom Bode serves as the general manager of the Columbus Engine Center.
- Pratt & Whitney anticipates adding approximately 500 manufacturing and engineering jobs over the next five years at its Columbus location.
- Available positions include mechanics, maintenance professionals, inspectors, machinists, engineers, and various quality and operations roles.
- Specific skills, experience, and qualifications for each position are detailed in the posted job descriptions.
- Positions to be hired over the next few months are currently posted online.
- Interviews for the new positions have already begun.
- The interview process begins as soon as qualified candidates are identified for posted jobs.
- The interview process generally starts with phone screens followed by on-site interviews.
- In some cases, Pratt & Whitney holds interview “super days” to interview multiple candidates on the same day.
- The hiring process begins with resume screening to identify candidates with the required qualifications, experience, education, and skills.
- Applicants who pass the initial screen are contacted for a phone interview, with successful candidates invited to an in-person interview.
- Salaries for the new positions will vary based on position type/level, experience, education, and skills.
- Pratt & Whitney offers a competitive total compensation package, including benefits for regular positions.
