## Facts

- Wabash (NYSE: WNC) is expanding its Wabash Parts and Preferred Partner Network to make genuine trailer and truck body parts more accessible.
- The company's network of authorized dealers serves as the backbone of its aftermarket support, providing expert service, maintenance, and Wabash Genuine Parts to keep fleets operating efficiently.
- Wabash introduced 14 new locations to its Preferred Partner Network, expanding the availability of Wabash Genuine Parts in areas not currently served by Wabash van trailer dealers.

- U.S. Trailer Parts & Supply of Chicago, IL joined the network as a new member.
- Wabash Parts was created in 2022 to unify and expand Wabash’s tech-enabled parts distribution capabilities across all of its Transportation Solutions product lines.
- The distribution channel covers van trailers, platform trailers, tank trailers, and truck bodies while ensuring immediate scalability.
- The single distribution channel utilizes Wabash’s extensive network of equipment dealers’ service capabilities.
- The channel leverages the infrastructure of industry-leading partners in national wholesale distribution for aftermarket heavy-duty truck and trailer parts.
- The network operates multiple distribution centers across the United States and Canada.
- Dan Millar serves as the managing director of Wabash Parts.
- Customers can purchase Wabash Genuine Parts through authorized Wabash dealers or preferred partners.
- Wabash is headquartered in Lafayette, Indiana.
- Wabash designs, manufactures, and services dry freight and refrigerated trailers, flatbed trailers, tank trailers, dry and refrigerated truck bodies, structural composite panels and products, trailer aerodynamic solutions, and specialty food grade processing equipment.
