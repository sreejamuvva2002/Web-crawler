# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2007-12-31

**Source:** [page_003252](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1271193/000104746908002063/a2182916z10-k.htm)  ·  `page_003252`

## Facts

- Superior Essex Inc. is headquartered at 150 Interstate North Parkway, Atlanta, Georgia 30339.
- The company operates as one of the largest wire and cable manufacturers in the world, manufacturing and supplying a broad portfolio of wire and cable products for the communications, energy, automotive, industrial, and commercial/residential end-markets.
- Superior Essex is a leading manufacturer of magnet wire, fabricated insulation products, and copper and fiber optic communications wire and cable products.
- Magnet wire consists of insulated copper or aluminum wire wound into coils and is used in electrical motors, transformers, controls, switches, and other electronic devices.
- Automotive applications for the company's magnet wire include motors, electronics, and electrical devices used in cars and other transport vehicles.
- The North American magnet wire and distribution segment's largest OEM customers include A.O. Smith, Cooper Industries, Emerson Electric, Howard Industries, Regal-Beloit, Tecumseh, and Visteon.
- In 2007, the company completed acquisitions including Invex S.p.A. in Italy, Nexans' remaining North American magnet wire business in Simcoe, Canada, and an 80% ownership interest in Essex Magnet Wire (Tianjin) Ltd. in China.
- These 2007 acquisitions significantly increased the company's position in the energy end-markets in North America, Europe, and China.
- The company currently operates 25 manufacturing facilities in North America, Europe, and China, and employs approximately 4,500 people.
- Superior Essex converts copper cathode to copper rod for internal consumption and for sale to other wire and cable manufacturers and OEMs.
- In January 2008, the company announced a consolidation and restructuring of its North American magnet wire manufacturing facilities, involving a phased closure of its Vincennes, Indiana facility expected to be completed by the fourth quarter of 2008.
- The European magnet wire segment produces magnet wire in eight facilities in France, Germany, Italy, Portugal, and the U.K., and produces enamels in one production facility in France.
- The company is the second largest magnet wire enamel producer in Europe in terms of sales and volume.
- In North America, approximately 82% of magnet wire volume shipped in 2007 was sold direct to OEM customers pursuant to annual or multi-year supply arrangements.
- The company distributes magnet wire and fabricated insulation products manufactured by itself and related accessory products purchased from third parties to small OEMs and motor/transformer repair facilities.
- At the end of 2007, Superior Essex believed it had an approximate 68% share of the total North American copper OSP market.
- The company has sales arrangements with AT&T and Verizon, with sales to AT&T accounting for approximately 10% of consolidated total sales in 2007.
