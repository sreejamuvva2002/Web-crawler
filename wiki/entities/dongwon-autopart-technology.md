# Dongwon Autopart Technology

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-materials|Battery Materials]]  ·  **Location:** Emanuel County, Georgia, Emanuel County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Dongwon Autopart Technology is a Korean automotive supplier that manufactures parts such as doors, seats and battery frames. It announced a $30 million investment to build its second Georgia production facility in Emanuel County, creating 200 jobs.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Produces battery frames used in electric vehicle battery packs.

## Key facts

- Dongwon Autopart Technology manufactures battery frames, a component for electric vehicle batteries.
- The company is establishing its second Georgia facility, expanding its U.S. footprint.
- The announced investment totals $30 million.
- The project will create 200 new jobs in rural Emanuel County.
- "It is literally the biggest new industrial development project that we ever had in Emanuel County," said Ken Warnock, CEO of the local Chamber of Commerce.
- Emanuel County is known as a hub for metal working, which attracted Dongwon to the area.
