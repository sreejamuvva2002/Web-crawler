## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a premier manufacturer of custom fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions.

- Bonnell Aluminum is headquartered in Newnan, Georgia.
- In addition to its Newnan facility, Bonnell Aluminum has manufacturing operations in Carthage, TN; Niles, MI; Clearfield, UT; and Elkhart, IN.
