# Salesforce, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2025-11-18

**Source:** [Salesforce to Invest $15 Million in Georgia Expansion - Expansion Solutions](https://www.expansionsolutionsmagazine.com/salesforce-to-invest-15-million-in-georgia-expansion/)  ·  `page_017175`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Salesforce, Inc. will expand its Georgia footprint with a $15 million investment expected to create more than 250 new jobs in Fulton County by the end of 2028.
- The expansion further strengthens Georgia’s position as a national leader in technology, innovation, and business growth.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Salesforce’s decision to expand in the No. 1 state for business is the latest direct result of Georgia’s unwavering commitment to fostering innovation and economic opportunity.
- Governor Brian Kemp cited the state’s pro-business environment and workforce investments as continuing to pay off as companies choose to operate and grow in communities across the state.
- Salesforce is a global leader in AI-powered customer relationship management (CRM) and trusted cloud technology, helping organizations connect with customers in innovative, data-driven ways.
- The company currently supports more than 2,000 jobs in Georgia.
- Salesforce serves clients across financial services, healthcare, retail, manufacturing, logistics, and the public sector.
- Tanya Counter, Site Lead for Salesforce Atlanta, stated that Atlanta has always been a cornerstone of Salesforce’s growth story.
- Tanya Counter highlighted the company’s confidence in Atlanta’s extraordinary talent and commitment to building an inclusive, values-driven workplace.
- Salesforce’s Atlanta operations are headquartered at Salesforce Tower Atlanta, 950 East Paces Ferry Rd NE.
- Atlanta Mayor Andre Dickens emphasized that Atlanta offers diverse talent, global connectivity through Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport, and a welcoming community for companies to expand and thrive.
- Fulton County Board of Commissioners Chairman Robb Pitts stated that the investment demonstrates confidence from globally premier companies in the community and will create opportunities for residents and the local economy.
- Metro Atlanta Chamber President and CEO Katie Kirkpatrick noted that the expansion underscores the strength and vibrancy of the workforce, citing a deep pool of talented professionals and thriving communities.
- The expansion project was supported by the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team, Invest Atlanta, Fulton County, the Metro Atlanta Chamber, and Georgia Power.
- GDEcD Senior Regional Project Manager Caroline Kinchler represented the state agency in supporting the project.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that by supporting industries that drive technology and innovation, Georgia ensures businesses succeed while boosting local economies.
- Salesforce helps organizations of all sizes become agentic enterprises by integrating humans, agents, apps, and data on a trusted, unified platform.
- Salesforce trades on the NYSE under the ticker symbol CRM.
