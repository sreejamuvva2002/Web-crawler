# Ford Motor Company

## Facts

- The West Tennessee Ford plant being built near a site between Memphis and Jackson, Tenn., is still two years away from opening.
- Electric pickups will be assembled at the West Tennessee site along with battery manufacturing.
- Ford electric vehicle sales got off to a strong start in 2025, continuing to improve year over year after a record 2024.
- The Michigan-based company sold more than 5,600 EVs in January 2025, a 24 percent increase from the same month a year prior.
- Ford also sold more than 13,000 hybrid vehicles in January 2025, a 19 percent jump year over year.
- Ford classes EVs and hybrids as “electrified” vehicles.
- BlueOval SK’s Kentucky One plant should come online this year.
- BlueOval SK’s Tennessee facility is scheduled to come online near the end of this year.
- Those facilities are expected to significantly increase Ford’s EV production capacity.
