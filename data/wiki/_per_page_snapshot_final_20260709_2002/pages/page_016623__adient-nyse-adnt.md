## Facts

- Adient is a global leader in automotive seating.
- Adient will present at the Barclays Global Automotive and Mobility Tech Conference on November 17, 2021.

- Adient has a workforce of approximately 75,000 employees across 33 countries.
- Adient operates 208 manufacturing and assembly plants worldwide.
- Adient produces and delivers automotive seating for all major OEMs.
- From complete seating systems to individual components, Adient's expertise spans every step of the automotive seat-making process.
- Adient's integrated, in-house skills allow it to take products from research and design to engineering and manufacturing.
- Adient contributes its products into more than 20 million vehicles every year.
