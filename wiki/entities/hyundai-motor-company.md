# Hyundai Motor Company

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Savannah, Bryan County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Hyundai broke ground on its Georgia electric vehicle factory, referred to as the Metaplant, in fall 2022 and showcased an all-electric SUV concept called SEVEN at an open-house event.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Construction of a Georgia-based electric vehicle manufacturing plant to produce all-electric SUVs.

- **Hyundai Motor Company Manufacturing Facility** — Sprawling auto plant announced in 2022 with a $5.5 billion investment to produce electric vehicles.
- **HL-GA Battery Company Plant (under construction)** — Joint venture between Hyundai and LG Energy Solution announced in 2023 with a $2 billion investment to produce EV battery cells.

- **Daesol Ausys West Point Plant** — Will produce luggage boards and covers; also supplies General Motors and Kia.
- **Das Corp Metter Plant** — Will produce automotive seat structures.
- **Hyundai EV Metaplant Campus** — $5.5 billion manufacturing campus that will host vehicle assembly and a battery-cell joint venture with LG Energy Solution.
- **Hyundai Electric Vehicle Manufacturing Plant** — Planned to be built on nearly 3,000 acres.
- **Hyundai Electric Vehicle Assembly Plant** — First Hyundai EV plant in the United States; part of Georgia’s largest economic development project.
- **Hyundai EV Assembly and Battery Plant** — First IONIQ 5 electric vehicle produced; plant includes both vehicle assembly and battery production.
- **Hyundai Electric Vehicle Assembly Plant** — Part of the $5.5 billion investment; will be built on a megasite property adjacent to Interstate 16.
- **Hyundai EV Battery Factory** — Adjacent to the electric-vehicle assembly plant; included in the $5.5 billion investment.
- **Hanon Systems Manufacturing Facility** — Will assemble automotive thermal solutions.
- **Woory Industrial Manufacturing Facility** — Will produce electrical heaters, control units and actuators for EV and internal-combustion automakers.
- **Ellabell Plant** — Phase 2 production goals announced for Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.
- **EV Manufacturing Facility** — Located on a 2,923-acre site; construction began early 2023; production slated for the first half of 2025.
- **Battery Manufacturing Facility** — Will be built through a strategic collaboration; shares the same 2,923-acre site as the EV plant.
- **Hyundai Metaplant (Savannah EV Smart Factory)** — Electric vehicle and battery manufacturing plant slated to open early 2025; first smart factory in the region.
- **Hyundai Metaplant (Savannah)** — $5.54 billion plant with on-site battery facility partnered with LG Energy Solution; co-located supplier investments total nearly $2 billion on the 2,900-acre site.
- **Hyundai EV Battery Plant (Cartersville)** — Investment slated to top $5 billion; partnership with SK for battery production.

- HL-GA Battery Company is a joint partnership between Hyundai and LG Energy Solution aimed at producing electric vehicle battery cells.
- Hyundai stated that none of those detained were directly employed by Hyundai Motor Company.
- North America Chief Manufacturing Officer Chris Susock will now assume governance of the entire megasite in Georgia.
- The battery plant construction was paused after the raid, with an opening still scheduled for 2026.
