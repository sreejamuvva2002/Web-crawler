# Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2017-04-25

**Source:** [RELEASE: Textron Officials Outline Plans For Arctic Cat At Dealer Event | Snow Goer](https://snowgoer.com/press-releases/release-textron-officials-outline-plans-for-arctic-cat-at-dealer-event/24620/)  ·  `page_051695`

## Facts

- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. is a Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT) company based in Augusta, Ga.
- The company announced plans to its dealer network regarding the incorporation of Arctic Cat into the company, including future powersports brand portfolio and upcoming products across snowmobile, side-by-side, and ATV lines.
- Company President and CEO Kevin Holleran welcomed a select gathering of employees and dealers in Texas and outlined synergies between Arctic Cat and Textron.
- Arctic Cat joins a portfolio that includes E-Z-GO golf cars, Textron Off Road side-by-sides, Cessna and Beechcraft fixed-wing aircraft, Bell helicopters, Greenlee professional tools, and Kautex automotive fuel systems.
- TSV Vice President of Engineering Chris Spencer spent more than two decades at Bell Helicopter as a lead engineer on military programs such as the Bell-Boeing V-22 Osprey.
- All side-by-sides and ATVs will transition to become part of the Textron Off Road brand.
- The Arctic Cat brand will remain in use for high-performance snowmobile product lines.
- Arctic Cat's legacy in side-by-sides and ATVs will live on through Alterra™, Wildcat™, and Prowler™ products, which will join Textron Off Road's existing Stampede® side-by-side.
- The decision to rebrand side-by-sides and ATVs under the Textron Off Road name resulted from consultation and research with consumers and dealers showing it offers the best growth opportunity, particularly outside the snow belt.
- A new unnamed crossover side-by-side under Textron Off Road will be released in fall 2017, featuring 100-plus horsepower, King Shocks suspension, bucket seats, extended cab, and industry-leading usable storage.
- The next-generation Wildcat XX sport side-by-side will be released in spring 2018, powered by a 998cc triple engine generating 125hp, featuring King shocks suspension with 18-inches of travel, 30-inch tires on 15-inch KMC wheels, and full doors.
- E-Z-GO models offer dealers a new customer base seeking personal golf cars and transportation vehicles.
- TSV president and general manager for snow and international Erik Nelson stated Textron's engineering expertise will accelerate innovation in Arctic Cat snowmobiles.
- New snowmobile models include the M 8000 and ZR 8000 with a new 800cc C-TEC2 two-stroke twin engine, and the ZR200 youth model.
- The dealer event took place in an airplane hangar at Fort Worth’s Alliance Airport in Texas.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles markets products under brands including E-Z-GO®, Cushman®, Arctic Cat®, Textron Off Road™, Jacobsen®, Dixie Chopper®, Ransomes®, TUG™, Douglas™, Premier™ and Safeaero™.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company leveraging aircraft, defense, industrial, and finance businesses globally.
