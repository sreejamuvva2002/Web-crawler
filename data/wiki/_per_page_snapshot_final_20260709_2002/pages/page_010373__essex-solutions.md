# Essex Solutions

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-08-19

**Source:** [Superior Essex Announces Essex Solutions, Relaunching Magnet Wire Brand | Georgia Industry Online](https://www.georgiaindustryonline.com/article/736528660-superior-essex-announces-essex-solutions-relaunching-magnet-wire-brand)  ·  `page_010373`

## Facts

- Superior Essex is a global leader in the magnet wire and communication cables industry.
- The company is rebranding its magnet wire business to Essex Solutions as part of the next phase in its rebranding efforts.
- This rebranding follows the company's recent buyout of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire.
- Under the new name, Essex Solutions will encompass all current Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire locations worldwide.
- Essex Energy in Italy will now be included in Essex Solutions, further reinforcing the company’s global reach and capabilities.
- Related businesses Lacroix + Kress, IVA Insulations, and Hi-Wire will join the Essex Solutions organization while continuing to operate under their existing brand identities.
- This consolidation aims to enable better coordination and collaboration among the brands, resulting in improved efficiency and effectiveness.
- Daniel Choi serves as President and CEO of Superior Essex.
- Daniel Choi stated that aligning brands under one grouping allows the company to offer a complete suite of solutions for customers and take further advantage of vertical integration and global reach.
- The company aims to proactively shape the xEV automotive industry, as well as power, electrification, and renewable energy markets experiencing rapid transformations.
- During the transition period, materials may bear either the existing Essex Furukawa or the new Essex Solutions name.
- All changes are expected to be completed by April 30, 2025.
- Essex Solutions will unveil a new logo in the near future to reflect its evolution and commitment to innovation and excellence.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Solutions, Superior Essex Communications, IVA, Lacroix + Kress, Hi-Wire, and Essex Brownell.
- Superior Essex has over 3,000 employees across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is the leading global provider of magnet wire and is at the forefront of communications fiber application as well as Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company is instrumental in creating disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex is committed to pioneering smart building technology while setting the pace on sustainability.
