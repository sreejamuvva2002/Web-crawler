# Sewon America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-02-21

**Source:** [Sewon America Announces 740 New Jobs in Effingham County | Georgia](https://georgia.org/press-releases/2023/sewon-america-announces-740-new-jobs-effingham-county)  ·  `page_000205`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Sewon America will create 740 new jobs in Rincon.
- Sewon America will invest more than $300 million in a new Effingham County manufacturing facility.
- This project is the largest known private investment in the City of Rincon to-date.
- The announcement fulfills the commitment to create jobs and investment in all four Savannah Harbor Interstate-16 Joint Development Authority (JDA) counties.
- Hyundai's Metaplant location in Bryan County is the largest economic development project in state history.
- Suppliers for the Metaplant have now committed over $1.8 billion in investment.
- The suppliers' investment commitment surpasses initial estimates by at least $800 million.
- Governor Brian Kemp congratulated Chairman Kim on his company's recent expansion during his first international trip as governor to Korea and discussed continuing to work together.
- Speaker of the House Jon Burns represents part of Effingham County and played a role in securing the project and engaging Sewon America executives.
- Lt. Governor Burt Jones commended Governor Kemp, Sewon America, and stakeholders on the historic economic development announcement for Rincon.
- Sewon America operates one of the largest automobile body parts manufacturing plants in North America.
- The company opened a manufacturing facility in LaGrange in 2008, which has since doubled in size.
- Following the completion of the Rincon facility, Sewon is expected to support 1,600 jobs in Georgia.
- Vice Chairman Kim stated that Sewon America was founded 15 years ago in west Georgia and is excited to build another facility in east Georgia.
- The growth is possible thanks to full support from Governor Kemp, the Georgia Department of Economic Development, and Effingham County.
- Sewon's new facility will be located at the Grande View industrial park in Rincon.
- The new manufacturing location will produce electric vehicle (EV) body parts for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs), including Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.
- Operations at the new facility are expected to begin in 2025.
- Rincon Mayor Ken Lee welcomed Sewon America and commended the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority for bringing the largest employment and private investment project in the city's history.
- Chairman of the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority (IDA) Troy Smith stated that Grande View industrial park was selected as the best location for significant investment.
- The investment will allow current and future generations to have fulfilling, well-paying careers without leaving Effingham County.
- Executive Director of the Georgia Ports Authority Griff Lynch called the announcement terrific news for Effingham County and the State of Georgia.
- Director of Project Implementation and Supplier Strategy Alyce Thornhill represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team on this competitive project.
- The GDEcD worked in partnership with the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority, Georgia Ports Authority, Georgia Quick Start, and Georgia EMC.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that Sewon has integrated into and lent its industry experience to Georgia’s automotive community through working with local colleges and Georgia Quick Start.
- Sewon is reinvesting in Georgia as they support Hyundai Motor Group’s operations from Effingham County, which has the skilled workforce, infrastructure, and collaborative spirit to support growth.
- On October 25, 2022, Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America officially broke ground in neighboring Bryan County on its first fully dedicated EV and battery manufacturing facility.
- Since 2020, more than 35 EV-related projects have announced more than $21 billion in investment and 26,700 jobs in Georgia.
- Sewon America was established in 2008 in LaGrange as a Georgia company.
- Sewon primarily operates in the Motor Vehicles and Car Bodies industry within the Transportation Equipment sector, producing stamped body components.
- Sewon Group is celebrated for high-quality automotive parts and will continue its tradition of excellence in the new Rincon facility.
