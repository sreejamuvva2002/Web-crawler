## Facts

- FORVIA HELLA is a listed, internationally positioned automotive supplier.
- The company stands for high-performance lighting technology and vehicle electronics.
- Its Business Group Lifecycle Solutions covers a broad service and product portfolio for the spare parts and workshop business as well as for manufacturers of special vehicles.
- The company offers products that integrate lithium-ion cells and a low-voltage battery management system in one product.
- FORVIA is the first automotive company with a net-zero target approved by the Science Based Targets Initiative (SBTi).
