# Astemo Americas Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-30

**Source:** [Astemo Tallapoosa Plant Closing Displaces 60 Workers](https://hoodline.com/2026/03/tallapoosa-auto-parts-plant-axed-after-contract-collapse/)  ·  `page_008428`

## Facts

- Astemo Americas Inc. will close its auto-parts plant in Tallapoosa.
- The closure will leave about 60 people out of work.
- The facility was considered one of the town's manufacturing anchors and is being stripped from the local economy with barely a whisper of public notice.
- The shutdown follows the termination of a principal customer contract covering parts for four vehicle models.
- The plant is located at 550 Honda Parkway in Tallapoosa, placing it side by side with Honda’s transmission operations.
- The Tallapoosa site is listed as one of several U.S. plants operated by Astemo Americas.
- Tallapoosa has roughly 3,200 residents.
- Losing about 60 manufacturing jobs represents a meaningful shock to the local job base and dependent small businesses.
- Exact shutdown timelines, severance or transition support details for employees, and plans for moving equipment to other Astemo sites were not included in initial coverage.
- The closure highlights how tightly auto suppliers can be hitched to one automaker’s programs and how sourcing shifts can suddenly impact hometown factories.
