# Kyungshin America Corp.

## Facts

- Kyungshin America Corp. has selected the 20-million-square-foot Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub as the location of its new distribution center in Rincon, GA.
- The South Korea-based automotive part supplier will open a 136,000-square-foot facility in February as part of its production expansion.
- The Broe Group, along with its transportation affiliate OmniTRAX and the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority, owns the 2,600-acre multimodal logistics park.
- Kyungshin’s new facility will import and distribute automotive components to Hyundai Motor Group’s $7.5 billion electric vehicle plant in Savannah.
- Kyungshin America Corp. is a certified supplier for Hyundai Motor Group.
- The company produces wire harnesses for automotive companies.
- Press reports suggest the move will create approximately 70 new jobs.
- The project will generate more than $22 million in investments in Effingham County.
- The facility opening is expected to support the growing automotive manufacturing industry in the Southeastern U.S.
