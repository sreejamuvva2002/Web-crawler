## Facts

- Dana is a world leader in providing power-conveyance and energy-management solutions for vehicles and machinery.
- The company's portfolio improves the efficiency, performance, and sustainability of light vehicles, commercial vehicles, and off-highway equipment.
- Dana supplies axles, driveshafts, and transmissions to vehicle manufacturers.
- Dana provides electrodynamic, thermal, sealing, and digital solutions for automotive applications.
- The company enables the propulsion of conventional, hybrid, and electric-powered vehicles by supplying nearly every vehicle and engine manufacturer in the world.
- Dana was founded in 1904.
- Dana employs more than 36,000 people globally.
- Dana is based in Maumee, Ohio, USA, with locations in 33 countries across six continents.
- Dana reported sales of $8.1 billion in 2018.
- The company has been recognized globally as a top employer with significant honors in Asia, India, Italy, Mexico, and the United States.
