# Neaton Auto Products Manufacturing, Inc.

## Facts

- Neaton Auto Products Manufacturing, Inc. is a Tier 1 automotive supplier.
- Neaton is a wholly owned subsidiary of Nihon Plast Co., Ltd.
- Nihon Plast Co., Ltd. is described as a global leader in safety and trim systems.
- Neaton was founded in 1984.
- Neaton's U.S. headquarters is located in Eaton, Ohio.
- Neaton's primary manufacturing campus is in Eaton, Ohio, featuring over 565,000 sq. ft. of production space.
- The Eaton, Ohio facility specializes in airbag modules, steering wheels, and injection-molded components.
- Neaton operates a sales and technical support office in Marysville, Ohio.
- Neaton operates a secondary U.S. production site in Rome, Georgia (NRI).
- The Rome, Georgia facility focuses on painting, assembly, and cockpit trim.
- Neaton supports major OEMs across North America with a commitment to innovation, quality, and operational excellence.
- Takeshi (Casey) Toyota serves as the President of Neaton Auto Products Manufacturing, Inc.
- Neaton's parent company maintains an expansive international manufacturing and R&D network spanning Japan, China, Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, and Mexico.
- This global reach enables Neaton to align with customer needs across platforms while maintaining localized production strength.
