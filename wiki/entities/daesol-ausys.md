# Daesol Ausys

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** West Point, Georgia, Harris County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

South Korean auto supplier Daesol Ausys plans to build a $72 million factory in West Point, Georgia to produce luggage boards and covers for electric-vehicle compartments, hiring more than 140 workers with production slated for December 2024.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Manufactures interior components (luggage boards and covers) for electric vehicles.
