# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Company (YMMC)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-30

**Source:** [Bob Brown assumes role of President at Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation – Winters Media](https://www.wintersmedia.net/bob-brown-assumes-role-of-president-at-yamaha-motor-manufacturing-corporation/)  ·  `page_015643`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Company (YMMC) announced that Robert (Bob) Brown has been appointed President, effective December 30, 2024.
- Bob Brown succeeds Takahiro (Taka) Imanishi, who served as President of YMMC since 2020 and returned to Japan for a new position.
- Bob Brown began his career at YMMC in May 2019 as Vice President of Finance & Operations Support.
- He was later promoted to Vice President of Manufacturing, where he helped develop the strategic plan to optimize Yamaha’s manufacturing footprint in North America.
- His leadership has been instrumental in driving YMMC’s success in the ATV, Side-by-Side, Water Vehicle, and Golf Car businesses.
- For nearly five years, Bob Brown has been a key figure at YMMC’s largest U.S.-based manufacturing facility in Newnan, Georgia.
- He served as Chair of the Coweta Grand Prix, a soapbox derby fundraiser promoting STEM education through hands-on learning and collaboration.
- He is an active member of the Newnan-Coweta Chamber of Commerce Board of Directors.
- He is an active member of the Coweta Community Foundation Board of Directors.
- Bob Brown led YMMC’s Corporate Social Responsibility Committee, identifying engaging and impactful projects for employees to give back to surrounding communities.
- Bob Brown holds a degree in Accounting from Eastern Michigan University.
- He completed the Global Executive Program facilitated by Hitotsubashi University, School of International Corporate Strategy.
- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Company (YMMC) employs more than 2,000 metro Atlanta residents to design and build recreational vehicles at its Newnan manufacturing facilities in Coweta County, Georgia.
- All of the world’s Yamaha golf cars are manufactured at the facility in Coweta County, Georgia.
- All of the world’s Yamaha Side-by-Sides are manufactured at the facility in Coweta County, Georgia.
- Most of Yamaha's WaveRunners are manufactured at the facility in Coweta County, Georgia.
- Most of Yamaha's ATVs are manufactured at the facility in Coweta County, Georgia.
- Bob Brown stated he is honored to step into the role of President and looks forward to leading YMMC into a new era of growth and innovation.
- YMMC embraces the concept of ‘Kando’ – creating experiences that deeply resonate, inspire joy, and inspire the heart and spirit.
- The company aims to cultivate an enduring positive impact and build a stronger, more engaged community by bringing Kando to employees and community partners.
