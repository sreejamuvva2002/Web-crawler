# Kia Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-21

**Source:** [Kia Georgia Adding EV6 Production to its Lineup - Kia Georgia](https://www.kiageorgia.com/kia-georgia-adding-ev6-production-to-its-lineup/)  ·  `page_000294`

## Facts

- Following a $217 million expansion, EV6 joins the EV9 as the plant’s second electric vehicle and fifth overall vehicle currently being assembled in West Point, Georgia.
- On Nov. 21, 2024, it was announced at the 2024 Los Angeles Auto Show that the all-electric 2025 EV6 SUV will be assembled by the Kia Georgia team in West Point, Ga., beginning in early 2025.
- The EV6 joins the all-electric EV9 SUV and the Telluride, Sorento and Sportage SUVs on the plant’s assembly line.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp stated that Kia's announcement is a testament to the decades-long partnership between Kia and Georgia, helping make the state the No. 1 state for business and accelerating its development as the electric mobility capital of America.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp thanked President Sean Yoon and Stuart Countess, as well as their teams, for the decision and the opportunities it will bring to hardworking Georgians.
- During the past year-and-a-half, Kia Georgia has undergone a transformation that allows for the production of EVs and the plant’s traditional products all along the same line.
- The $217 million expansion created approximately 200 jobs in Troup County.
- The expansion brings the plant’s total employment to more than 3,200 team members.
- SeungKyu (Sean) Yoon, President and CEO of Kia North America and Kia America, stated that the award-winning EV6 has been an integral part of Kia’s ‘Plan S’ strategy since day one.
- The announcement that the EV6 will be built in Georgia is a testament to Kia’s ongoing commitment to the U.S. market and the state of Georgia.
- As the home to previous World Car of the Year winners Telluride and EV9, there is no doubt that the team at Kia Georgia will continue its legacy of successful launches with EV6, according to SeungKyu (Sean) Yoon.
- Kia Georgia, Inc. is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Corporation based in Seoul, Korea.
- With an annual capacity of 350,000 units, Kia Georgia is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Ga. and began mass production on Nov. 16, 2009.
- Kia Georgia is home to the Telluride SUV, Sorento SUV (excludes HEV and PHEV models), Sportage SUV (excludes HEV and PHEV models) and the all-electric EV9 SUV.
- The announcement excludes production of the EV6 GT model.
