# Kyungshin America Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-12-06

**Source:** [Effingham County Nets $22M Distribution Center from Korean Auto Supplier - Global Atlanta](https://www.globalatlanta.com/effingham-county-nets-22m-distribution-center-from-korean-auto-supplier/)  ·  `page_056724`

## Facts

- A Korean automotive supplier is setting up shop in Effingham County.
- The company is investing $22 million to establish a new distribution center.
- The investment will create 70 jobs.
- The distribution center will supply "key customers" from the coastal Georgia location.
- The Korean automotive supplier is identified as Kyungshin America Corp.
- Kyungshin America Corp.'s news release does not explicitly mention Hyundai.
- Kyungshin America Corp. is a subsidiary of Incheon, South Korea-based Kyungshin Co. Ltd.
- Parent company Kyungshin Co. Ltd. has won supplier awards from Hyundai Motor Group.
- Kyungshin Co. Ltd. is building a massive electric vehicle plant in Ellabell.
- The Ellabell EV plant will be the parent company's first dedicated EV facility in the U.S.
- Effingham County is one of four counties marketing a 2,900-acre site through a joint development authority.
- A major Hyundai project is precipitating spillover investment effects in each partner county.
- The city of Rincon secured a $300 million investment from longtime Hyundai and Kia supplier Sewon America.
- The Sewon America investment will create 740 jobs.
- The Sewon America investment was announced in February.
- Sewon America will locate at the Grande View Industrial Park.
- Kyungshin America is locating at 1001 Gateway Parkway.
- The Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub reportedly has industrial sites of up to 500 acres available.
- Brandt Herndon serves as the CEO of the joint development authority marketing the site.
- Broe Real Estate Group assisted in locating Kyungshin America at the Savannah Gateway Industrial Hub.
- Kyungshin manufactures wire harnesses, assemblies, and other electrical components.
- The company has been in operation for approximately half a century.
- Kyungshin is focusing its efforts on the growing U.S. electric vehicle market.
- Details regarding the investment were provided in a release from the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority.
