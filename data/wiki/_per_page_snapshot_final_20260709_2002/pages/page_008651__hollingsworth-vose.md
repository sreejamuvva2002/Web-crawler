## Facts

- H&V is a global leader in filtration, battery separator, and energy storage solutions.
- The company originated in 1728.
- H&V provides innovative material solutions that help create clean air, water, and energy across the globe.
- H&V's advanced materials contribute to a cleaner world by providing clean air and liquids.
- H&V's advanced materials power hybrid, start-stop, and electric vehicles.
- H&V's advanced materials enable energy storage.
- H&V's filtration solutions can be found in clean rooms, hospitals, computers, commercial buildings, homes, vehicles, and heavy-duty equipment.
- H&V's battery separator and energy storage solutions are helping the world transition to a more sustainable future.
- The company is headquartered at its plant site in East Walpole, Massachusetts, USA.
- The enterprise began on the same river where it is currently headquartered nearly 300 years ago.
