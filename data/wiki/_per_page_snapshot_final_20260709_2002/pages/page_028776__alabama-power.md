# Alabama Power

## Facts

- Alabama Power is an investor-owned utility (IOU) that operates in Alabama.
- The utility provides incentives related to alternative fuels and vehicles.
- A residential EV Time-of-Use (TOU) Rate incentive is available for fuel prices.
- No residential infrastructure incentives are currently offered.
- No residential vehicle incentives are currently offered.
- A non-residential EV Time-of-Use (TOU) Rate incentive is available for fuel prices.
- A non-residential EV Direct Current (DC) Fast Charging Station Purchase Rebate is offered as an infrastructure incentive.
- A non-residential EV Direct Current (DC) Fast Charging Station Installation Rebate is offered as an infrastructure incentive.
- No non-residential vehicle incentives are currently offered.
- Alabama Power operates in the Birmingham-Hoover Metropolitan Statistical Area.
