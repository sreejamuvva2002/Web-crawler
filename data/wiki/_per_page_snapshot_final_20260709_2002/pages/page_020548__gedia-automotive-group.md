# GEDIA Automotive Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-07-24

**Source:** [Automotive Group GEDIA: $85M Plant in Georgia, Create 200 Jobs | NILRR](https://nilrr.org/automotive-group-gedia-85m-plant-in-georgia-create-200-jobs/)  ·  `page_020548`

## Facts

- GEDIA Automotive Group is a family-owned international company that specializes in making state-of-the-art automotive parts.
- GEDIA currently supplies automotive parts to nearby companies such as Volkswagen, BMW, and Mercedes-Benz.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that GEDIA will build an advanced manufacturing facility in Georgia.
- The new facility will begin production with a focus on parts for electric vehicles.
- GEDIA will invest $85 million in the project.
- The project will deliver 200 jobs to the greater Dalton area.
- The facility will be located at a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) certified property in Whitfield County.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp stated that Georgia is a national leader in manufacturing, logistics, and workforce training.
- Governor Kemp expressed confidence that GEDIA will be pleased to join the growing electric vehicle ecosystem in Georgia.
- Governor Kemp noted that the state's consistent investment in its Germany-based European office is paying off.
- Governor Kemp stated he looks forward to seeing the opportunities GEDIA creates for hardworking Georgians in Whitfield County.
- The announcement was sourced from the State of Georgia on July 24, 2020.
- Auto manufacturing in Right to Work states is bringing more construction jobs to the region.
- The project plans to create 200 new permanent GEDIA jobs.
