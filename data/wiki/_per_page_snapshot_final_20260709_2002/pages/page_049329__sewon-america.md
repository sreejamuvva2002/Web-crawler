## Facts

- Sewon America is an automobile body parts manufacturer.
- Plans to open a new production facility in Rincon, Georgia.
- The investment amount for the new facility is $300 million.
- The investment will generate 740 new jobs for the area.
- The new facility will be used to produce electric vehicle body parts for OEMs.
- The company already operates a facility in LaGrange, Georgia.
- The LaGrange facility has doubled in size since it began production in 2018.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated: “Today we not only celebrate delivering on our promise to create jobs in all four counties in the JDA, but also the growth of a valued partner in our business community.”
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that on his very first international trip as governor to Korea, he congratulated Chairman Kim on the company’s then-recent expansion and discussed how they could continue to work together.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that those discussions led to this announcement as Sewon America continues to grow their Georgia operations.
