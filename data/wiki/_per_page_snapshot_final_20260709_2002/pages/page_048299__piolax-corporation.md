# Piolax Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Piolax Corporation | Case Studies | Solutions | DAIFUKU](https://www.daifuku.com/solution/casestudy/case019/)  ·  `page_048299`

## Facts

- Piolax Corporation is a U.S. subsidiary of Piolax Inc. that produces and sells automotive parts such as precision springs, plastic and metal fasteners, and fuel system components.
- Piolax established a new logistics base for automotive parts at its main plant in Georgia (GA).
- The company introduced an automated pallet warehouse and a high-speed rail-guided vehicle system called “STV” to handle increased goods volume due to growing demand.
- Prior to the new facility, Piolax conducted its logistics operations at an external warehouse adjacent to the main plant.
- Finished auto parts were previously stored on pallet racking with a capacity of about 3,100 pallets, and forklifts were used to move them in and out of the warehouse.
- A significant increase in the volume of goods over the past five years made the previous storage capacity and processing capacity for warehousing, shipping, and picking insufficient.
- Piolax built a 92,000m2 logistics center on the plant site in October 2016 to reduce warehouse rent, improve operation efficiency, and increase safety levels.
- The new automated warehouse features five stacker cranes with a storage capacity of 5,632 pallets.
- The storage capacity increased by approximately 80% compared to the previous system to accommodate future increases in goods volume.
- Seven STVs operate in a loop system, transporting goods at high speed between the automated warehouse and the receiving, shipping, and picking stations.
- Operating the STV system increased processing capacity and reduced worker waiting time.
- The new system drastically reduced the time required for logistics operations.
- Only two forklifts are needed instead of the six to eight that were previously required.
- Inventory management and First-In First-Out operations have been thoroughly carried out, enabling prompt and accurate delivery of goods according to the drawback.
- Piolax reported that the new system achieved cost reduction and safety assurance goals, improved the work environment, and reduced overtime hours.
- The company stated it will consider installing automated warehouses at its other locations in the future.
