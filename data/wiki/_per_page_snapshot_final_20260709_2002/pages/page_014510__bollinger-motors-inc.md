# Bollinger Motors Inc

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-10-22

**Source:** [Bollinger and Wabash to produce refrigerated delivery electric truck](https://truckandbusbuilder.com/article/2022/10/22/bollinger-and-wabash-to-produce-refrigerated-delivery-electric-truck)  ·  `page_014510`

## Facts

- Bollinger Motors Inc of Oak Park, Michigan has partnered with Lafayette, Indiana-based trailer builder, Wabash National Corp to build a last-mile refrigerated electric delivery truck.
- Wabash’s lightweight composite EcoNex Technology for truck and trailer bodies will be integrated with Bollinger's Class 4 electric chassis cab.
- The EcoNex Technology is made from a composite material that, Wabash claims, is more durable, lighter, and thermally efficient than other commercial products resulting in increased total payload capacity due to lower overall truck weight.
