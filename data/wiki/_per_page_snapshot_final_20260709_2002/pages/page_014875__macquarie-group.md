# Macquarie Group

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Macquarie Completes Wheelabrator Acquisition, Buys Tunnel Hill](https://www.waste360.com/mergers-acquisitions/macquarie-completes-wheelabrator-acquisition-buys-tunnel-hill-partners)  ·  `page_014875`

## Facts

- Macquarie Infrastructure Partners (MIP) completed the purchase of Wheelabrator from funds managed by Energy Capital Partners.
- Ironwood Capital announced an exit from its investment in Tunnel Hill Partners, which was sold to Macquarie Group.
- MIP operates within the Macquarie Infrastructure and Real Assets (MIRA) division of Macquarie Group.
- Karl Kuchel is CEO of MIP.
- Wheelabrator is a leading owner and operator of waste-to-energy (WTE) facilities that deliver baseload, renewable energy to residential and commercial customers.
- Wheelabrator currently has a platform of 25 strategically located assets in the U.S. and the U.K., including 19 WTE facilities (three under construction), two waste fuel facilities and four ash monofills.
- Robert Boucher is president and CEO of Wheelabrator.
- Wheelabrator remains focused on executing its strategy to develop, deliver and realize the potential of clean energy.
- The Wheelabrator sale was approved by the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, Committee on Foreign Investment in the United States, Department of Justice, Federal Trade Commission, Federal Communications Commission and the New Jersey Department of Environmental Protection.
- Credit Suisse and UBS Investment Bank served as financial advisors to Wheelabrator on the sale.
- Latham & Watkins LLP served as legal counsel for Wheelabrator.
- Greentech Capital Advisors and RBC Capital Markets served as financial advisors to MIP.
- Kirkland & Ellis served as legal counsel for MIP.
- Tunnel Hill is the largest integrated waste-by-rail operator in the United States.
- Tunnel Hill operates five waste-by-rail transfer stations in the Northeast, two rail-served landfills in Ohio, the largest beneficial use site in Pennsylvania (also rail-served), a hauling operation with transfer stations in Connecticut and a recycling operation in Massachusetts.
- Tunnel Hill provides hauling, recycling, beneficial reuse, transfer and disposal services to commercial and industrial customers throughout the Northeast and Ohio.
- Dickson Suit is Managing Director at Ironwood Capital.
