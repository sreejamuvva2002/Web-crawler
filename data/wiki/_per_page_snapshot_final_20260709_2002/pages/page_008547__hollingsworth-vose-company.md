## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose Company (H&V) is a global manufacturer of nonwoven materials and engineered papers used in filtration, energy, and industrial applications.
- The company is divided into three business divisions: air and liquid filtration media; battery separators and energy products; and industrial products.
- In 2014 the company operates 12 manufacturing sites, 15 sales offices, and four research centers globally.
- After 1950 the company later launched production of microfiberglass battery separators.
- The company has a manufacturing site located in Hawkinsville, GA.
