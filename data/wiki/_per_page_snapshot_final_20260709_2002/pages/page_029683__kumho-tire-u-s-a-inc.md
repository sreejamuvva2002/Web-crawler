## Facts

- Kumho Tire U.S.A. is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- Kumho Tire U.S.A. serves as the US sales, marketing, product development, and distribution arm of Kumho Tire Co. Inc.
- Kumho Tire USA is one of the world's leading tire manufacturers.
- The company is significantly expanding its engagement with SUV and truck consumers through full-scale participation in off-road events throughout 2026.
- Ed Cho serves as CEO of Kumho Tire U.S.A.
- Kumho Tire will participate in a comprehensive schedule of off-road events from March through October 2026 to directly engage SUV and truck enthusiasts.
- The 2026 event schedule includes Overland Expo® SoCal on March 14-15 in Costa Mesa, Calif.
- The 2026 event schedule includes Jeep Beach on April 17-26 in Daytona Beach, Fla.
- The 2026 event schedule includes Overland Expo® West on May 15-17 in Flagstaff, Ariz.
- The 2026 event schedule includes Overland Expo® PNW on June 26-28 in Redmond, Ore.
- The 2026 event schedule includes New Jersey Jeep Invasion on July 10-12 in Wildwood, N.J.
- The 2026 event schedule includes New Jersey Bronco Invasion on July 17-19 in Wildwood, N.J.
- The 2026 event schedule includes The Smoky Mountain Bronco® Stampede® on August 14-16 in Pigeon Forge, Tenn.
- The 2026 event schedule includes The Smoky Mountain Invasion™ on August 20-22 in Pigeon Forge, Tenn.
- The 2026 event schedule includes Off-Road Expo on October 3-4 in Costa Mesa, Calif.
- Kumho Tire will launch its 2026 event tour at Overland Expo SoCal on March 14 with an all-new 80-foot marketing truck and trailer.
- The marketing rig will feature interactive experiences, swag giveaways, and showcase key tires from the KUMHO Road Venture line.
- Key tires showcased include the Road Venture RT, Road Venture AT52, and Road Venture MT71.
- The Road Venture lineup is engineered to meet rigorous off-road conditions and offers superior durability, performance, and an industry-leading warranty.
- Kumho Tire ambassadors will be on-site at events to answer questions and help attendees identify optimal tires for their vehicles.
- Kumho Tire has more than 60 years of history of pioneering innovative approaches to developing tires.
