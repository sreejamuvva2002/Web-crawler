# YKK AP America Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** 2023-07-06

**Source:** [YKK AP America Unveils Newly Expanded Design Center](https://www.dwmmag.com/2023/07/06/ykk-ap-america-unveils-newly-expanded-design-center/)  ·  `page_016115`

## Facts

- YKK AP America Inc. opened a newly expanded commercial and residential design center in Atlanta.
- The center, named Studio 101, launched in 2021 with a focus on commercial architectural projects.
- The expansion allows YKK AP to feature a wider range of products, including residential doors and windows and a training center.
- Officials say the training center is designed to provide customers with hands-on experiences to ensure proper installation of entrances, storefront, curtainwall, window wall, and door and window products.
- The center is also designed to be a shared space for the architecture, engineering and construction community.
- President and CEO Oliver Stepe stated that the newly expanded commercial and residential design center and training space brings to life a more creative, collaborative way to work.
- Oliver Stepe highlighted the company's vision to frame the future by partnering more deeply with customers, partners and industry, and expressed honor at celebrating the space’s official opening with the local Atlanta community.
- Studio 101 is located at YKK AP’s headquarters in Atlanta.
