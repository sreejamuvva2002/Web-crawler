# Anovion Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-16

**Source:** [Maker of EV battery materials Anovion Technologies is coming to Bainbridge](https://thegeorgiasun.com/business-economy/maker-of-ev-battery-materials-anovion-technologies-is-coming-to-bainbridge/)  ·  `page_052263`

## Facts

- Anovion Technologies will invest $800 million in a plant in Southwest Georgia that will create more than 400 jobs, according to Gov. Brian Kemp.
- Chicago-based Anovion Technologies supplies premium synthetic graphite, the largest battery material used in EVs by mass, exceeding copper, nickel, manganese, cobalt and lithium.
- The new plant will be 1.5 million square feet and located in Bainbridge.
- The Bainbridge facility will be the first of its size and scale for synthetic graphite manufacturing in North America.
- Anovion CEO Eric Stopka stated that Georgia's strong technical workforce and top-tier schools will help advance research partnerships and contribute to a highly skilled talent pipeline for the company's innovation and climate technology jobs.
- Gov. Brian Kemp stated that Georgia is fast becoming the e-mobility capital of the nation, noting that job creators are recognizing every corner of the state has the resources and workforce needed for success.
- The two largest economic development projects in Georgia history are electric vehicle manufacturing plants.
- Hyundai is investing $5.5 billion in a plant near Savannah in Bryan County expected to generate 8,100 jobs when fully built out.
- Rivian's $5 billion plant east of Atlanta will create 7,500 jobs.
- Suppliers of electric vehicle batteries are springing up across Georgia to serve the two major EV manufacturing plants.
- Anovion will be hiring for technical, production, and engineering jobs, as well as administrative and management positions.
- The Bainbridge plant is expected to begin operations in late 2025.
