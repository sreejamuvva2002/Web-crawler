# Thermal Ceramics

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2025-01-15

**Source:** [Emporia set to lose another major factory](https://www.wibw.com/2025/01/15/emporia-set-lose-another-major-factory/)  ·  `page_011167`

## Facts

- Parent company Morgan Advanced Materials told Thermal Ceramics employees that the Emporia, Kansas plant is being moved to Atlanta, Georgia.
- The Emporia factory is expected to close this summer.
- Morgan Advanced Materials cited a lack of business opportunity in the Emporia area as the reason for the relocation.
- The company stated it would work with the local union to provide assistance moving forward for the affected staff.
- The closure will impact 38 employees at the facility.
- This factory closure comes just one month before the expected closure of a Tyson Foods plant in the same city.
