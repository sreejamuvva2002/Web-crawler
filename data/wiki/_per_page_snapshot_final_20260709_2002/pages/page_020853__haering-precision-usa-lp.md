# Haering Precision USA LP

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-06

**Source:** [Haering Precision, school system expand partnership | Hartwell Sun, Hartwell, Georgia](https://www.thehartwellsun.com/local/haering-precision-school-system-expand-partnership)  ·  `page_020853`

## Facts

- Haering Precision USA LP partnered with Hart County College and Career Academy to place precision machining equipment from Hart County High School directly within Haering’s Lavonia facility.
- The strategic collaboration enhances local workforce development by giving high school students access to real-world training in a high-tech manufacturing environment.
- The machines are now located in the Haering Academy for Apprenticeship and Further Training, a dedicated training space on site at Haering Precision.
- The academy serves as a hub for technical education, combining classroom instruction with practical, industry-relevant experience.
- The equipment installation is part of a broader initiative to enhance Work-Based Learning (WBL) opportunities for students in Hart County.
- Through the program, students can train on familiar school-owned machines in an industry setting at Haering while learning directly from experienced professionals.
- Nathan Lindler, Technical Plant Manager at Haering Precision, stated that this is often the students’ very first exposure to a real manufacturing facility.
- Lindler noted that giving students access to industry tools and professionals early helps them explore interests, build confidence, and understand career possibilities in manufacturing.
- The integration supports a growing demand for skilled machinists and industrial technicians by accelerating career readiness and reinforcing the connection between technical education and real-world applications.
- Students in the program gain hands-on experience, mentorship, and the opportunity to be part of a global company known for high-precision components in the automotive industry.
- Steve Burton, CEO of Hart County College and Career Academy, expressed pride in working with Haering Precision to provide students with access and opportunity, calling it a strong example of education and industry supporting student success and community growth.
- The program is currently active and welcoming Work-Based Learning (WBL) students.
