# Duracell

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-materials|Battery Materials]]  ·  **Location:** Atlanta, GA, Fulton County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Duracell, a leading battery manufacturer, announced the establishment of its new Global Headquarters for Research and Development at Science Square in Atlanta, creating 110 jobs and investing approximately $56 million. The company already operates a manufacturing facility in LaGrange (since 1980) and a logistics/distribution plant in Fairburn (since 2020).

## EV supply-chain relevance

Duracell’s expanded R&D presence strengthens Georgia’s battery ecosystem and supports innovation in battery technologies that are critical for electric-vehicle applications.
