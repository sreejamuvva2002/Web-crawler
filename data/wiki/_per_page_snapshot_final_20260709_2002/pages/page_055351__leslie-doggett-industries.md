# Leslie Doggett Industries

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2019-08-05

**Source:** [Doggett Industries Acquires Great Dane Trailer Dealership](https://www.doggett.com/newsroom/press-releases/179-leslie-doggett-industries-acquires-great-dane-trailers)  ·  `page_055351`

## Facts

- Leslie Doggett Industries acquired the privately-held Great Dane Trailer distributor/dealer for El Paso, Laredo and McAllen/Pharr, Texas with territory stretching to Las Cruces, New Mexico.
- The acquisition was purchased from El Paso’s trucking magnate and International Truck dealer, Royal Jones.
- The purchase price was undisclosed.
- This acquisition follows Doggett’s acquisition of El Paso’s Truck Enterprises two weeks prior.
- Doggett previously acquired Freightliner dealerships throughout the State of Arkansas last year.
- The addition complements Doggett’s existing Freightliner and Western Star dealerships in El Paso, Laredo and McAllen/Pharr to sell and service flatbeds, dry freight and refrigerated vans with over-the-road truck offerings.
- Paul Burk, SVP of the Doggett Truck Group, stated the addition allows partnering with trucking companies and dealers on a measurable basis and expects extraordinary growth with Great Dane products alongside Freightliner, John Deere, Toyota, LinkBelt, and Ford businesses.
- Great Dane’s history dates back to 1900, establishing itself as a transportation solutions leader delivering innovative products and services for trucking customers.
- Great Dane engineers and builds high-quality trailers and truck bodies.
- Great Dane manufactured 49,000 trailers in 2018.
- Great Dane’s headquarters are in Savannah, Georgia with manufacturing plants throughout North America.
- Doggett is a Houston-based diversified heavy equipment dealer for seven first-tier manufacturers: John Deere construction and forestry equipment (17 dealerships), Toyota Industrial Equipment forklifts & material handling (7 dealerships), Freightliner and Western Star on-highway and vocational trucks (9 dealerships), Link Belt Cranes (3 dealerships), a Ford auto and truck dealership, and Great Dane Trailers (3 dealerships).
- Doggett was founded by Leslie Doggett and Brady Carruth in 1993 with 17 employees.
- Ranked by the Houston Chronicle in 2019 as Houston’s 12th largest private company with annual sales exceeding US $1 billion and 1400+ full-time employees including 500+ factory trained and certified technicians.
- Doggett is a family-owned heavy equipment, highway truck and automotive dealership group without outside investment of any kind.
