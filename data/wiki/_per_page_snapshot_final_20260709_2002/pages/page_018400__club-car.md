# Club Car

## Facts

- The 105,391 square foot facility leased by Club Car is located at 780 Innovation Parkway in Appling.
- The facility went on the market for just over $16 million last week.
- The facility is one of several sites Club Car occupies in Columbia County.
- The facility is one of two Club Car sites located at the White Oak Business Park in Appling.
- White Oak Business Park is an industrial park developed and managed by the Columbia County Development Authority.
- Club Car is paying $816,780 in rent for the location.
- Club Car has committed to lease the newly built building for seven years, with two options to extend for additional five-year periods.
- Club Car also occupies a second building in Appling that is half a million square feet and opened in 2020.
- Both Club Car buildings are located next to Amazon’s distribution center.
- The building is currently owned by Frank Mullins, who was the developer of Mullins Crossing Shopping Center in Evans.
