## Facts

- Samual J. Brady served as the Project Architect.
- The project size is 13,000 square feet.
- The procurement method used was Design Build.
- The project duration ran from July 2023 to January 2024.
- Great Dane Trailers is the owner of the shop expansion project.
- The project involved adding 13,000 square feet of space to accommodate the growing needs of the service and repair department.
- A key challenge was stabilizing the soil in a high water table area, as the site was a former vehicle garbage dump.
- The expanded shop features an overhead bridge crane with concrete floor tie-down anchor points.
- Gramoll Construction managed the shop addition to ensure completion without interruptions to existing facilities’ continuous operations.
- The project demonstrates Gramoll Construction’s ability to manage complex expansion projects while facilities are actively in use.
