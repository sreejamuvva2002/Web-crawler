# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) held its Grand Opening on March 26.
- At the grand opening, Hyundai Motor Company President and CEO José Muñoz stated that Hyundai planned to increase direct jobs to 14,000.
- The job commitment update appeared to correlate with an announcement that the metaplant would increase production from 300,000 units to 500,000.
- Confusion exists regarding how many direct jobs Hyundai committed to bringing to the Savannah region versus how many are officially connected to on-site affiliate suppliers.
- Even officials making statements have had trouble keeping the job and production numbers straight.
