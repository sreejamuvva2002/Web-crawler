# Astemo, Ltd.

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-12-16

**Source:** [Hitachi to transfer a portion of its shares in Astemo : Hitachi Global](https://www.hitachi.com/en/press/articles/2025/12/1216/)  ·  `page_008544`

## Facts

- Hitachi entered into an agreement with Honda to transfer a portion of its common shares in Astemo to Honda.
- Hitachi will transfer 21% of its common shares in Astemo to Honda at a transaction price of approximately 152.3 billion yen.
- Following the share transfer, Hitachi’s ownership ratio of voting rights for Astemo will change from Hitachi: Honda: JICC-01 = 40.0%: 40.0%: 20.0% to 19.0%: 61.0%: 20.0%.
- The share transfer is expected to close in the first quarter of fiscal year ending March 31, 2027, subject to regulatory approvals and customary closing conditions.
- Astemo will no longer be an equity-method associate of Hitachi following the transfer.
- Hitachi will continue supporting Astemo’s sustainable growth and enhancement of corporate value as a shareholder, taking an IPO into consideration, through assistance focused on digital technologies.
- Hitachi will utilize funds obtained through the share transfer in accordance with its capital allocation policy to maximize corporate value and accelerate growth toward achieving its management plan, “Inspire 2027.”
- The impact of the share transfer on Hitachi’s consolidated financial statement is not material.
- Hitachi operates globally in Digital Systems & Services, Energy, Mobility, and Connective Industries sectors through its Social Innovation Business (SIB) and Strategic SIB Business Unit.
- Hitachi generates value from integrating data, technology, and domain knowledge via its Lumada platform to solve customer and social challenges.
- Hitachi’s revenues for FY2024 (ended March 31, 2025) totaled 9,783.3 billion yen.
- Hitachi operates 618 consolidated subsidiaries and employs approximately 280,000 people worldwide.
