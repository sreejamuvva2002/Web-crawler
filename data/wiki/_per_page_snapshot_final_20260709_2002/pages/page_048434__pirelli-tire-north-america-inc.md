# Pirelli Tire North America Inc.

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America Inc. is preparing to suspend operations in Rome, Georgia, to protect worker health amid the spread of the coronavirus.
- The company plans to scale back production starting March 22.
- Manufacturing will be suspended for one week starting March 29.
- The factory will be cleaned and sanitized while closed.
- Slowing the rate of production will reduce the number of employees present to ensure safety while not interrupting supply to clients.
- Pirelli noted that the reduced output corresponds to lower demand.
- The tire maker is constantly monitoring the situation and is ready to adopt additional measures if necessary.
- Pirelli employs 300 workers at the Rome car tire plant.
- The Rome plant is 18 years old and has a listed capacity of 400,000 units per year.
- The company did not comment on plans for its pair of plants in operation in Silao, Mexico.
