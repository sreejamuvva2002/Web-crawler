# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Yamaha hiring 300 more in Newnan – Winters Media](https://www.wintersmedia.net/yamaha-hiring-300-more-in-newnan/)  ·  `page_015623`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America (YMMC) is immediately hiring over 300 new contract employees to assist in many assembly line functions.
- The hiring aims to keep pace with growing demand for all Yamaha recreational products assembled at its Newnan facility.
- YMMC is based in Coweta County, Georgia.
- YMMC designs and assembles four key product lines including Golf Cars, All Terrain Vehicles (ATVs), Personal Watercraft, and Recreational Off-road Vehicles (ROVs/Side-by-Sides).
- Yamaha's last hiring push was just over a month ago, adding over 100 employees to the team.
- Bob Brown, Vice President of Finance and Operations Support, stated that demand for products has stayed incredibly strong because people are foregoing travel and choosing local recreation.
- The company is gearing up for an incredible 2021, requiring more staff for a motivated, diverse, and talented team.
- Richard Jones, Vice President of Operations, noted that Yamaha has produced almost 4 million power sports recreation vehicles at this location over 32 years.
- The greatest hiring need is vehicle assembly.
- Every Yamaha assembly worker starts through a contract partner at $13 an hour.
- At 30 days, team members are eligible for full Yamaha hire, with pay jumping immediately to $15 and soon thereafter to $18 an hour.
- Yamaha benefits include full healthcare, medical, dental, vision, profit sharing, 401k with company match, 10 paid vacation days, and 10 paid holidays per year.
- Yamaha is a diverse and dynamic corporation with over 1500 employees.
- Chris McGarrity, a Yamaha employee for eight years, stated he started as a material handler and moved to the front office as a new model planner, highlighting astronomical career opportunities.
- Yamaha is offering over $1300 in bonuses for contract employees in their first six months.
- Open positions include assemblers, material handlers, welders, certified forklift operators, and more.
- YMMC was formed in 1986 and started manufacturing units in 1988.
- YMMC manufactures Golf Cars, ATVs, Water Vehicles, and Recreational Off-Road Vehicles for global distribution.
- YMMC celebrated 30 years of manufacturing at its Newnan location in June of 2018.
- Yamaha products are marketed through Yamaha Motor Corporation, USA (YMUS), a recognized leader in the outdoor recreation industry.
- YMUS product offerings include motorcycles, scooters, ATVs, side-by-side vehicles, snowmobiles, WaveRunner personal watercraft, boats, outboard motors, outdoor power equipment, power assist bicycles, golf cars, power assist wheelchair systems, surface mount technology (SMT), robotic machines, unmanned helicopters, accessories, apparel, and Yamalube products.
- YMUS products are sold through a nationwide network of distributors and dealers in the United States.
- YMUS has a corporate office in California, two corporate offices in Georgia, facilities in Wisconsin and Alabama, and factory operations in Tennessee and Georgia.
- Additional U.S.-based subsidiaries include Yamaha Marine Systems Company (YMSC) with divisions Bennett Marine (Florida) and Kracor Systems (Wisconsin), Skeeter Boats (Texas) with division G3 Boats (Missouri), and Yamaha Precision Propeller (Indiana).
