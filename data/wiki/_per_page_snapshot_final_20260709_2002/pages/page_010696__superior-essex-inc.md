## Facts

- Superior Essex, a leading global manufacturer of wire and cable products, moved to The Pavilion, an A-class office building located at 5770 Powers Ferry Road, Sandy Springs, GA, to open in March.
- The new location covers two full floors and has 57,078 square feet of space, featuring an open floor plan designed to facilitate collaboration and spark creativity.
- After serving various industries for 85 years, this relocation allows the global company to continue optimizing productivity and efficiency while enhancing talent acquisition by embracing modern workplace trends.
- Anna Basista, Global VP of Human Resources for Essex Superior, stated that the transition will provide opportunities for the company over the years and align its collaborative culture, agility, engagement, and respect for the workplace.
- The move from a nearby office allows Superior Essex to maintain its strategic position in the Northwest Atlanta market, providing employees with seamless access to served customers and surrounding business centers.
- The executive team announced the opening of an employee library aligned with the new corporate office space.
- Two library spaces located in the central facility area offer various materials, including bilingual books and magazines for employees to read during leisure time or off-site.
- CEO Brian Kim stated that plans for the project were laid out simultaneously with the move to The Pavilion building and believe it will continue to enrich the company's important work-life balance culture.
- Kim added that the new library space is a great addition to the social hub and corporate vision, serving as a symbol of a company that prioritizes learning and positive development to enhance the quality work experience for everyone.
- The company's address is listed as 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
