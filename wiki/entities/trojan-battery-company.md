# Trojan Battery Company

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Santa Fe Springs, California, California, United States

## Overview

Trojan Battery Company is the leading brand of flooded lead-acid, AGM and lithium-ion deep-cycle batteries for motive applications such as golf carts, LSVs, boats, RVs, work platforms, industrial equipment, solar and renewable energy.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Provides deep-cycle batteries used in electric golf carts, low-speed vehicles and renewable-energy storage, supporting Georgia's EV supply-chain ecosystem.

## Key facts

- Trojan Battery Company has been manufacturing deep-cycle batteries since 1925.
- Pioneered the first golf-cart battery for the Autoette vehicle in 1952, establishing leadership in the golf-cart industry.
- Operates three ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing plants across California and Georgia.
- Member of Battery Council International (BCI) and the Advanced Lead Acid Battery Consortium, adhering to rigorous testing standards.
- Provides flooded lead-acid, AGM and lithium-ion batteries for a wide range of motive applications, including electric golf carts and low-speed vehicles.
- Acquired by C&D Technologies in 2018, creating one of the world’s largest energy-storage providers.
- Mission: "We will lead the innovation and delivery of energy storage solutions to power the world safely and sustainably."
