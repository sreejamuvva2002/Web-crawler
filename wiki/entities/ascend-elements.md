# Ascend Elements

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-recycling|Battery Recycling]]  ·  **Location:** Covington, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Ascend Elements celebrated the grand opening of its first commercial-scale lithium-ion battery recycling facility, named Base 1, in Covington, Georgia. The plant can process up to 30,000 metric tons of used batteries and manufacturing scrap per year (about 70,000 EV batteries) and is powered by renewable energy.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Battery recycling is a critical component of the electric-vehicle battery materials supply chain.
