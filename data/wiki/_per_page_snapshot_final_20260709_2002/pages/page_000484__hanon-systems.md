# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-23

**Source:** [Automotive Supplier Hanon Systems to Create 160 New Jobs in Bulloch County - AllOnGeorgia](https://allongeorgia.com/georgia-business/automotive-supplier-hanon-systems-to-create-160-new-jobs-in-bulloch-county/)  ·  `page_000484`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Hanon Systems will create 160 new jobs and invest more than $40 million in a new manufacturing facility in Bulloch County.
- Hanon Systems is a global provider of automotive thermal and energy management solutions.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia’s automotive and e-mobility industries continue to grow as the state welcomes Hanon Systems.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that Georgia furthers its position as the EV capital of America, with new jobs and opportunities coming to Georgians statewide.
- Min Sung is President and Chief Executive Officer of Hanon Systems.
- Min Sung stated that Hanon Systems is pleased to contribute to the local economy of Bulloch County through job creation.
- Min Sung thanked the State of Georgia and the cooperation between its agencies for guiding the project.
- Min Sung said the announcement aligns with Hanon Systems' strategy to support the industry shift toward electrification while growing its North America footprint.
- Hanon Systems will build a new facility at Gateway Regional Industrial Park in Bulloch County.
- The new facility will assemble automotive thermal solutions.
- Operations at the new facility are expected to begin in May 2024.
- Billy Allen is Chair of the Development Authority of Bulloch County.
- Billy Allen stated that Statesboro-Bulloch County is honored by Hanon Systems' decision to locate its new facility there.
- Billy Allen expressed excitement to welcome another automotive supplier to the community, particularly one working closely with the Hyundai Metaplant and the electric vehicle industry.
- Billy Allen cited the decision as an example of the resources offered by the community and region to help companies succeed.
- Griff Lynch is Executive Director of the Georgia Ports Authority (GPA).
- Griff Lynch stated that the announcement further solidifies Georgia’s position as a growing hub for auto manufacturing.
- Griff Lynch noted that GPA supports the operations of Hyundai Motor Group and its suppliers to deliver jobs and opportunity for Georgia residents.
- Alyce Thornhill is Director of Project Implementation and Supplier Strategy at the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD).
- Alyce Thornhill represented the GDEcD Global Commerce team on this competitive project.
- The project was executed in partnership with the Development Authority of Bulloch County, Georgia Ports Authority, Georgia Power, and Georgia Quick Start.
- Pat Wilson is GDEcD Commissioner.
- Pat Wilson stated that suppliers like Hanon Systems successfully support larger industry ecosystems through partnership-driven solutions ranging from workforce training to logistics systems efficiencies.
- Pat Wilson noted that Bulloch County is part of the Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority and has been part of its growing success story from the beginning.
- Georgia is positioned as a hub for the electric mobility industry.
- Since 2020, over 40 EV-related projects have announced more than $22.7 billion in investment and 28,400 jobs in Georgia.
- On October 25, Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America officially broke ground in neighboring Bryan County on its first fully dedicated EV and battery manufacturing facility.
- Hanon Systems was formed in 1986.
- Hanon Systems is a leading supplier of automotive thermal and energy management solutions for electrified and conventional vehicles.
- Hanon Systems' offerings include heating ventilation and air conditioning, powertrain cooling, compressor, fluid transport, and electronics and fluid pressure solutions.
