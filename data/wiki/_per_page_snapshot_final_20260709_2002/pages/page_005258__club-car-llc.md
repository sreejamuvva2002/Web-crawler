# Club Car, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-29

**Source:** [CLUB CAR LAUNCHES NEW U.S. STREET LEGAL UTILITY VEHICLE](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/club-car-launches-new-us-street-legal-utility-vehicle-301784545.html)  ·  `page_005258`

## Facts

- Club Car is a world-leading manufacturer of small-wheel, zero-emissions electric vehicles based in Augusta, Ga.
- The company is rolling out its latest street-legal electric vehicle, the Club Car Urban LSV and XR, now available through its commercial distributor network in North America.
- The new Urban platform expands Club Car's leading electric vehicle portfolio, which has been prominent in commercial markets for decades.
- The vehicle features automotive styling, ergonomic design, a top speed of 25 mph for road use, and customizable configurations for specific needs.
- The Club Car Urban addresses needs for large campuses, last mile delivery, municipalities, and hospitality spaces.
- Mark Wagner, President of Club Car, stated the company is excited to expand its electric vehicle portfolio and that industrial and commercial customers seeking safe, on-road, street-legal, 100% electric, and low-cost transportation will find the Urban an ideal fit.
- The Club Car Urban offers low operational costs compared to pickup trucks or vans.
- The vehicle is available in several different lithium-ion battery options that enable fast and efficient charging to reduce operating expenses.
- The Club Car Urban sets a safety standard in its class with automotive-grade features including roll over protection, 3-point safety belts, and extensive testing processes.
- Jeff Tyminski, Vice President of Product Management at Club Car, noted that most campus operations teams currently use pickup trucks for facility management, which is often the wrong tool for many jobs.
- A blended fleet of traditional pickup trucks and electric utility vehicles would improve operational productivity while lowering ownership costs and reducing greenhouse gas emissions, according to Tyminski.
- Following the acquisition of Garia and Melex in early 2022, Club Car has been steadily growing its line of compact vehicles for utility applications including public sector, facility management, leisure and outdoor, and last-mile delivery.
- The Club Car Urban LSV is a street-legal vehicle with a top speed of 25 mph, while the Urban XR is a non-street legal option with a top speed of 19 mph.
- The Club Car Urban is offered with a pickup bed or van box and allows customers to choose features such as different heater options, Bluetooth stereo, in-cab refrigerator, first aid kit, and fire extinguisher.
- The Club Car Urban is currently available to purchase through the commercial dealer network and can be demoed at key scheduled events or through dealers.
- Club Car has over 60 years of experience in innovation and design for small-wheel vehicles and was founded in 1958.
- The company is a leading manufacturer of gas and electric golf, utility, and personal transportation vehicles with a product portfolio encompassing commercial and consumer markets.
- Club Car is an industry leader in electrification and sustainability and is on the forefront of environmentally responsible Zero Emission Vehicle (ZEV) technologies.
