# Porsche Experience Center Atlanta

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Restaurant 356: Porsche opens fine dining restaurant in Atlanta](https://eturbonews.com/restaurant-356-porsche-opens-fine-dining-restaurant-atlanta/)  ·  `page_048662`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. has officially opened its new fine dining “Restaurant 356” in Atlanta, Georgia.
- The restaurant sits on the complex of the company’s new $100 million Porsche Experience Center and headquarters.
- Restaurant 356 offers panoramic views of a 1.6-mile driver development track.
- Andre Oosthuizen, vice president of marketing for Porsche Cars North America, Inc., stated that Atlanta is known for its diverse tapestry and international cuisine.
- At Restaurant 356, guests will enjoy elegant dishes that honor Atlanta’s global flavors while culminating a unique Porsche experience.
- The restaurant is located on the second level of the Porsche Experience Center.
- From the restaurant, guests can view aircraft taking off and landing at Runway 826 Right at the Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport.
- Innovative dishes are served inside a dining room featuring contemporary decor.
- The seasonal menu showcases refined world cuisine made with locally farmed and foraged ingredients.
- A vast wine selection pays tribute to the great winegrowing regions of the world.
- Classically-focused cocktails are available for those favoring tradition.
- Executive Chef Kyle Forson, former executive sous chef at The Spence, leads the restaurant's culinary approach.
- Chef Forson focuses on carefully hand-crafted dishes that let the quality of seasonal ingredients shine through.
- The menu reflects influences from Forson’s Polish heritage and experiences with French, Californian, and Southern fare.
- No single type of cuisine will dominate the menu.
- Chef Forson stated he wants guests to enjoy food that enhances the Porsche experience and fuses it with a sense of place.
- Restaurant 356 is open to the public Tuesday through Saturday for lunch and dinner, and on Sunday for brunch and dinner.
- The restaurant provides catering services and operates as an event venue.
- In addition to Restaurant 356 and the driver development track it overlooks, the Porsche Experience Center features a classic car gallery, restoration center, human performance center, and driving simulator lab.
- A state-of-the-art business center within the complex includes 13,000 square feet of conference and event space.
- The complex was specifically designed as a public destination with an estimated 30,000 guests expected to visit each year.
