# Honda

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-01

**Source:** [Honda honors 52 suppliers in North America for outstanding performance | Automotive World](https://www.automotiveworld.com/news-releases/honda-honors-52-suppliers-in-north-america-for-outstanding-performance/)  ·  `page_015582`

## Facts

- Honda recently honored 52 North American suppliers with awards for outstanding performance in supplying parts and materials in 2023.
- Honda recognized 31 mass production suppliers during its annual Honda Supplier Conference in Columbus, Ohio.
- Honda recognized 21 service parts suppliers for supporting customer needs after purchasing a Honda or Acura product.
- Honda purchased over $38 billion in parts for mass production operations from North American suppliers in 2023.
- The mass production parts supported the sales of more than 1.3 million Honda and Acura vehicles in North America.
- Honda purchased more than $1.2 billion in parts from its Service Parts suppliers in North America.
- Mike Lapham, vice president of Procurement in the Purchasing & Supply Chain Center at Honda Development & Manufacturing of America, LLC, stated that as Honda prepares for EV production at its North American plants, suppliers continue to support petrol and hybrid-electric vehicle customers.
- Mike Lapham emphasized maintaining a focus on stabilizing production with outstanding quality to establish the foundation for the EV future.
- 31 mass production suppliers were recognized in three categories: Excellence in Quality & Delivery, Value, and Sustainability.
- The Excellence in Sustainability Award recognizes a company exemplifying Honda’s commitment to social responsibility based on leadership in compliance, ethics, environmental governance, diversity, health and safety, and social/trade compliance.
- Michelin Group, located in Greenville and Lexington, South Carolina, received the Supplier Sustainability Award.
- Honda does business with 746 OEM suppliers across North America.
- These OEM suppliers provide parts to Honda’s 13 plants in the region that produce Honda and Acura cars, light trucks, engines, and transmissions.
- Jessica Hammontree, assistant vice president of the Parts Division at American Honda Motor Co., Inc., stated that future success depends on taking new business approaches focused on customer needs as Honda transitions to an EV future.
- Jessica Hammontree noted that after new vehicle sales become 100% electrified, customers will rely on Honda for service parts for petrol and hybrid-electric vehicles for the next twenty years.
- Honda works with nearly 1,400 service parts suppliers in North America to provide service and accessory parts for more than 20 million Honda and Acura automobiles in operation in the United States.
- Honda also provides service parts for powersports and power equipment products through its supplier network.
- Honda manages a national network of parts and distribution facilities to deliver high-quality service parts to dealers nationwide and support global parts operations.
