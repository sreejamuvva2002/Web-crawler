## Facts

- Great Dane Trailers is headquartered in Savannah, Ga.
- Great Dane Trailers announced the addition of All Points Great Dane in Dearborn, Mich., to its North American distribution network.
- The new full-service dealer location was established to better serve Detroit and Eastern Michigan customers.
- Chris Hammond serves as vice president of Dealer Sales at Great Dane Trailers.
- All Points Great Dane is located at 12851 Prospect St. just off I-94 in Dearborn.
- The facility is staffed with 32 employees, including four salespeople, 15 mechanics, and two parts and service people.
- The All Points Great Dane facility spans 22,000 square feet.
- The site offers eight drive-through service bays, one of which is a paint bay.
- Three trucks provide mobile service at the location, including a parts liner supported by a salesman.
