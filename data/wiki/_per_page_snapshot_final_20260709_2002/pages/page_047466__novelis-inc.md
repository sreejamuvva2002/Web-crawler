# Novelis Inc.

## Facts

- Novelis is the world's leading provider of low-carbon, recycled aluminum.
- Aluminum-intensive body-in-white structures weigh up to 45% less than steel-intensive vehicles, enabling automakers to improve performance and increase payload capacity.
- Reduced vehicle weight using aluminum extends battery range for electric vehicles (EVs), which is a critical factor for consumer adoption.
- Lighter vehicles require fewer batteries and smaller engines, suspension systems, and brakes, translating to additional weight and cost savings for automakers.
- For internal combustion engine vehicles, lighter vehicles produce fewer emissions and require less fuel during operation.
- For EVs, using fewer batteries or smaller battery packs reduces the vehicle's carbon footprint by consuming less energy while driving, producing less CO2 from electricity generation, and lessening the need for resource-intensive mining of critical minerals.
- Aluminum's infinite recyclability creates opportunities for a more circular economy through the recovery of aluminum in body and battery enclosure systems at the end of the life of the vehicle.
- Reusing recovered aluminum scrap significantly reduces the need for primary aluminum production, leading to substantial environmental benefits.
- Recycling aluminum consumes only about 5% of the energy required for primary production, resulting in up to a 95% reduction in energy usage.
- The aluminum recycling process also achieves a similar percentage reduction in greenhouse gas emissions.
- Aluminum's versatility supports modular design and manufacturing efficiencies, helping automakers streamline production across vehicle segments.
- Using aluminum results in fewer total parts, reduced robots, tools, and joints, improving assembly efficiency and lowering factory emissions.
- Using aluminum reduces the complexity of material grades compared to steel while achieving similar functionality.
- These efficiencies reduce downstream supply chain requirements, simplify manufacturing complexity, and create value for automakers.
- High-strength aluminum alloys deliver exceptional crash performance, protecting occupants while still allowing for vehicle weight reduction.
- Aluminum demonstrates better energy absorption, allowing vehicle architectures to be engineered for improved occupant protection and high marks in 5-star crash safety testing.
- Aluminum's lightweight properties enhance vehicle dynamics, improving handling, braking, and acceleration.
- Novelis positions itself as a partner to automakers with unmatched expertise, innovative solutions, and commitment to sustainability.
