# OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-12-13

**Source:** [Industry mourns loss of Fred Taylor, OTR Wheel Engineering Founder, Community Leader & Philanthropist - Material Handling Wholesaler](https://www.mhwmag.com/nuts-bolts/industry-mourns-loss-of-fred-taylor-otr-wheel-engineering-founder-community-leader-philanthropist/)  ·  `page_058409`

## Facts

- Founder Fredrick (Fred) Taylor passed away peacefully on December 13, 2021, at the age of 70.
- Taylor was born in Pontiac, Michigan, on March 31, 1951.
- Taylor grew up in Ellsworth, Michigan, and attended high school there.
- Taylor earned a mechanical engineering degree from Michigan Tech University in Houghton, Michigan.
- In 1982, Taylor moved his wife and children to Rome, Georgia, to start his own enterprise after working in his family’s business.
- Taylor founded OTR Wheel Engineering in 1987.
- Taylor served as CEO of the company until early 2021 when Tom Rizzi was hired.
- Following the CEO transition in early 2021, Taylor assumed the role of Chairman of the Board.
- Taylor secured numerous patents and trademarks for innovative tire and wheel assemblies, including the Outrigger tire.
- OTR Wheel Engineering conducts business with original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and aftermarket customers.
- Tom Rizzi serves as OTR’s CEO and president.
- Taylor was actively involved in philanthropy and community support within Rome, Georgia.
- Taylor supported numerous Rome-based organizations, including the First Presbyterian Church, YMCA, Boys & Girls Club, Unity Christian School, and Boy Scouts of America.
