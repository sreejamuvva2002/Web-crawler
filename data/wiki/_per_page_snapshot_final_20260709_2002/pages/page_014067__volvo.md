# Volvo

## Facts

- Volvo is launching a new fully electric car model called the ES90.
- The ES90 features the debut of Volvo's new 800V technology.
- An 800-volt electric system charges the battery faster, improves overall performance, and increases efficiency compared to a 400-volt system.
- The ES90 can add 300 kilometres of range in just 10 minutes at 350 kW fast charging stations.
- The ES90 offers a driving range of up to 700 kilometres under the WLTP testing cycle.
- Volvo chief engineering and technology officer Anders Bell states that the 800V technology marks a significant technological upgrade as the company moves towards full electrification.
- Every component in the ES90's electric system has been upgraded for 800-volt compatibility, including battery cells, e-motors, inverters, charging systems, and climate and thermal systems.
- The higher voltage system delivers more power and range with the same current as a 400-volt system while generating less heat to prevent overloading the electric system.
- The 800-volt system uses lighter e-motors and other components to reduce the vehicle's overall weight, improving efficiency, acceleration, and driving range.
- Volvo developed new in-house battery management software that cuts charging time from 10 to 80 percent by up to 30 percent, down to 20 minutes.
- The improved charging speed is partly due to the integration of adaptive charging software from Breathe Battery Technologies.
- Volvo invested in Breathe Battery Technologies last year via the Volvo Cars Tech Fund, its corporate venture capital arm.
- The ES90 uses recycled and natural materials to contribute to lower CO2 emissions in production.
- 29 percent of all aluminium used in the ES90 is recycled.
- 18 percent of all steel used in the ES90 is recycled.
- The ES90 contains 16 percent recycled polymers and bio-based materials.
- Wood panels inside the ES90 are made from FSC-certified wood.
- The ES90 includes a battery passport based on blockchain technology to track raw material origins for lithium, cobalt, nickel, and graphite.
- The battery passport highlights the CO2 footprint of the full battery pack and provides transparency on responsible raw material sourcing.
- The ES90 will be revealed to the world on March 5.
- Under the CLTC driving cycle in China, the WLTP range of up to 700km translates to 740 km for all-wheel drive variants with a twin motor and 106 kWh battery.
- Charging times vary based on outdoor temperature, battery condition, and car condition, with testing conducted at 350 kW charging facilities.
