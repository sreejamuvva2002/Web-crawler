## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo, a leader in the direct injection gasoline market, has launched its latest high-pressure pump (HPP Generation 5) on the aftermarket market.
- The HPP Generation 5 pump is one of Hitachi Astemo's latest developments.
- The pump is manufactured from stainless steel for improved durability and designed to reduce operating noise.
- This mechanically driven model mounts at the cylinder head and can achieve pressures of up to 500 bars.
- These performance improvements enhance combustion, reduce fuel consumption, and limit CO2 emissions while complying with current environmental standards.
- The Generation 5 high-pressure pump offering covers more than 2.5 million vehicles in the EMEA market with only two references.
- The pumps meet the needs of 1.0 TSI and 1.5 TFSI engines, equipping recent models such as the Audi Q3, VW Tiguan, T-Roc, and Seat Ibiza 5.
- Hitachi Astemo's fuel supply offering includes more than 1,150 references of fuel pumps, high-pressure pumps, and injectors.
- The Astemo high-pressure pump range consists of 60 references covering more than 1,000 vehicle applications produced since 2002.
