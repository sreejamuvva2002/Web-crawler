# Piedmont Auto Group LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2025-11-06

**Source:** [U-Haul Dealer Network Welcomes Piedmont Auto Group  | U-Haul](https://www.uhaul.com/Articles/About/U-Haul-Dealer-Network-Welcomes-Piedmont-Auto-Group-36335/)  ·  `page_058854`

## Facts

- On Nov. 6, 2025, U-Haul Co. of Georgia announced that Piedmont Auto Group LLC signed on as a U-Haul neighborhood dealer to serve the Stone Mountain community.
- Piedmont Auto Group is located at 1137 Goldsmith Road in Stone Mountain, Ga.
- The dealership will offer essential services including U-Haul trucks, trailers, towing equipment, and moving supplies.
- Normal business hours are 9 a.m.-5 p.m. Monday-Friday and 10 a.m.-4 p.m. Saturday.
- Piedmont Auto Group owner Levon Karpoev is partnering with the industry leader in do-it-yourself moving and self-storage to better meet the demands of Dekalb County.
- U-Haul has teamed with independent dealers to offer rental equipment to do-it-yourself movers since 1945.
- More than 21,000 dealers across the U.S. and Canada are generating supplemental income through their U-Haul affiliation during challenging times for small businesses.
- Local dealer affiliates are not U-Haul franchises but are simply small businesses that have committed a portion of their lot space for U-Haul equipment and time to help meet mobility needs.
- No financial investment is required to become a U-Haul dealer.
- Since 2002, Moving Help® has been the top choice for customers seeking labor for loading and unloading, packing and unpacking, home safe moving, piano moving, cleaning, and U-Box storage container services.
- Customers can choose the number of workers, hours, and service date needed from a network of 7,800 customer-rated local and independent Moving Help Providers across the U.S. and Canada.
- Transparent upfront pricing is provided for moving labor services, with payment release subject to customer approval using Moving Help's patented payment code.
- Celebrating its 80th anniversary in 2025, U-Haul operates more than 24,000 rental locations across all 50 states and 10 Canadian provinces.
- The U-Haul app allows customers to access U-Haul Truck Share 24/7 through self-dispatch and return options on smartphones using patented Live Verify technology.
- U-Haul's fleet has grown to 197,500 trucks, 137,200 trailers, and 41,300 towing devices.
- U-Haul is the third largest self-storage operator in North America, offering 1,093,000 rentable storage units and 94.9 million square feet of self-storage space at owned and managed facilities.
- U-Haul is the top retailer of propane in the U.S. and the largest installer of permanent trailer hitches in the automotive aftermarket industry.
