# JTEKT North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [JTEKT North America Expands Greenville County Headquarters | S.C. Governor Henry McMaster](https://governor.sc.gov/news/2018-09/jtekt-north-america-expands-greenville-county-headquarters)  ·  `page_028376`

## Facts

- JTEKT North America is a subsidiary of global automotive and industrial supplier JTEKT Corporation.
- The company completed a $5.8 million expansion of its headquarters on the campus of the Clemson University International Center for Automotive Research (CU-ICAR).
- The expansion project began in 2017 to accommodate new jobs in Greenville.
- The expansion will create a total of 100 positions, all of which are scheduled to be filled by 2020.
- JTEKT manufactures steering systems, driveline components, bearings technologies and precision machine tools, which are critical to the state’s automotive industry.
- The company employs more than 6,000 associates at 24 sites across North America.
- JTEKT operates four manufacturing facilities located in South Carolina.
- In 2015, JTEKT announced the consolidation of a portion of its headquarters to CU-ICAR in Greenville.
- The expansion added 19,000 square feet of office and lab space at CU-ICAR’s One Research Drive building.
- This complements the 135,000 square feet of space JTEKT already occupies between two buildings on the CU-ICAR campus.
- The new space will provide work areas for associates who have relocated to Greenville as well as for new positions currently being filled.
- Executive Vice President and Chief Operating Officer Mike Davidson stated that Greenville is an excellent location for the growing headquarters due to its central geography relative to the manufacturing footprint and the availability of talented professionals in business and technical fields.
- Mike Davidson noted the expansion facilitates co-location of vital functions, continuation of research and development, better customer service, and an innovative environment for associates.
- Governor Henry McMaster stated that creating 100 new jobs through the headquarters expansion is a big win for South Carolina’s automotive industry and demonstrates that the state remains an ideal destination for automobile manufacturers and suppliers.
- Secretary of Commerce Bobby Hitt stated that South Carolina’s robust automotive supply chain continues to grow and thrive, citing JTEKT’s expansion as a perfect example of that success.
- Greenville County Council chairman and director of Greenville Area Development Corporation H.G. “Butch” Kirven Jr. stated that JTEKT’s decision is a testament to the area's dedicated workforce and strong pro-business environment, highlighting it as an example of leading international companies locating in Greenville.
