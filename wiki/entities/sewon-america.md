# Sewon America

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** Rincon, Effingham County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Sewon America, a subsidiary of Korean-based Sewon Precision Industry Co., announced a $300 million+ investment to build a new manufacturing facility in the Grande View industrial park in Rincon, Effingham County. The project will create 740 new jobs and produce electric-vehicle (EV) body parts for original equipment manufacturers including Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America, bringing the company's Georgia workforce to an estimated 1,600 jobs.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Produces stamped body components for electric-vehicle manufacturers, supporting EV supply chain in Georgia.
