# Down to Earth Trailers

## Facts

- Down to Earth Trailers (D2E) is centered in Baxley, Georgia.
- Southern Trailer Depot in Douglas, Georgia, provides Down to Earth Trailers as part of its cargo trailer inventory.
- Since its inception in 1999, Down to Earth Trailers has built cargo haulers for customers across the nation.
- Down to Earth Trailers manufactures a wide range of car trailers, ATV trailers, landscape trailers, utility trailers, gooseneck trailers, and enclosed cargo trailers.
- Down to Earth trailers are designed as workhorses for landscaping businesses, car enthusiasts, and general hauling needs.
- The company operates with a strict code of ethics and practices honest business and integral operations.
- Down to Earth builds its trailers to withstand heavy use and ensure longevity through quality craftsmanship and top-tier components.
- Down to Earth utility trailers are ideal for plumbers, tradesmen, lawn care businesses, and general hauling needs.
- Utility trailer options include 6x12 single axles, 7x16 tandems, and larger sizes.
- Down to Earth utility trailers feature open-air designs with rear loading ramps and tie-down points for easy loading and securement.
- Customers can add compartments to utility trailers to store smaller items like cords, trimmers, and gas tanks.
- Down to Earth landscape/dump trailers are designed for hauling loose elements like dirt, gravel, mulch, and yard debris.
- The dump trailers feature a smooth tilt and containment design with solid side walls built to a standard 24-inch height.
- The axles on Down to Earth dump trailers come equipped with a built-in braking system to handle heavy loads.
- Dump trailer options include various bed sizes and power lift configurations.
- Down to Earth expanded its lineup to offer enclosed cargo trailers with a range of sizes and payload capacities.
- Enclosed cargo trailers are constructed to strict standards using top-of-the-line materials for excellence and longevity.
- Enclosed trailer customization options include additional tie-downs, lighting upgrades, extra payload capacity, and storage options.
