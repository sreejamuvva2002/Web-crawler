## Facts

- Commercial roofing in Greenville SC is shaped by one of the deepest industrial building inventories in the Southeast.

- BMW Manufacturing Spartanburg (Greer SC) is the largest BMW plant in the world by output, spanning more than 8 million sqft of plant building.
- The plant produces X3, X4, X5, X6, X7, and XM models.
- A Tier-1 supplier ring around BMW spans 50+ miles and includes facilities from Adient, BorgWarner, Bosch, Continental, Denso, Faurecia, Magna, Mahle, Plastic Omnium, Thyssenkrupp, and ZF.

- The Upstate region hosts Michelin North America HQ in Greenville with multiple tire plants across the region.
