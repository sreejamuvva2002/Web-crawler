# Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2013-07-11

**Source:** [KIA MOTORS MANUFACTURING GEORGIA REACHES ONE MILLION U.S.-BUILT VEHICLES MILESTONE](https://www.kiamedia.com/us/en/media/pressreleases/6414/kia-motors-manufacturing-georgia-reaches-one-million-us-built-vehicles-milestone)  ·  `page_010250`

## Facts

- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc. (KMMG) celebrated the one millionth vehicle rolling off its assembly line on July 11, 2013.
- The one millionth vehicle produced was a snow white pearl 2014 Sorento SXL.
- The facility is located in West Point, Georgia.
- KMMG represented an initial investment of $1 billion.
- Mass production of the Sorento CUV began at KMMG on November 16, 2009.
- Together with on-site and local suppliers, the plant is responsible for the creation of more than 11,000 jobs in West Point and the surrounding region.
- A $100 million expansion was completed in early-2012.
- The expansion increased KMMG's annual production capacity to 360,000 vehicles.
- The Optima mid-size sedan was added to the assembly line in 2011.
- As of July 2013, the Optima had been Kia’s best-selling vehicle in the U.S. for 19 consecutive months.
- KMMG employs more than 3,000 team members.
- Byung Mo Ahn serves as Group President and CEO for Kia Motors America and Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia.
- The one millionth Sorento will be allocated to one of Kia Motors America’s more than 765 dealerships nationwide.
- KMMG is the first manufacturing site in North America for Seoul, Korea-based Kia Motors Corporation.
- KMMG occupies a 2,200-acre site in West Point, Georgia.
