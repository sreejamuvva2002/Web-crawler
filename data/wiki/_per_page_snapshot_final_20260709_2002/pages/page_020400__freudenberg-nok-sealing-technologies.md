# Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-11-05

**Source:** [Freudenberg-NOK Acquires Alto Products Corp.](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/freudenberg-nok-expanding-aftermarket-portfolio/)  ·  `page_020400`

## Facts

- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies, in conjunction with its aftermarket division Corteco, has acquired Alto Products Corp.
- The acquisition bolsters Freudenberg’s position in the North American and global aftermarket, broadening product and service offerings.
- Alto Products Corp. operates a 270,000-square-foot manufacturing facility in Atmore, Alabama.
- Alto maintains sales and distribution locations in Mexico, the Netherlands and China.
- Alto employs approximately 240 people, with 230 based in the United States.
- Alto is known for producing friction plates, steel reaction plates and comprehensive kits for automatic transmissions.
- Alto serves the automotive, heavy-duty and marine segments.
- Dr. Christian Dickopf, senior vice president of Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies’ Corteco division, stated that the acquisition expands their product portfolio and reinforces their commitment to delivering comprehensive, high-quality solutions to customers.
- The acquisition enables FNST to offer complete transmission rebuild solutions by integrating Alto’s friction and steel plates into new product offerings.
- This development fosters growth in heavy-duty and recreational vehicle segments and enhances vertical integration.
- The move improves risk management and strengthens supply chain resilience through Alto’s U.S.-centric manufacturing.
- The acquisition expands FNST’s reach into international markets by leveraging Alto’s presence in Latin America and Asia.
- This creates opportunities to consolidate product lines and operations across FNST’s global footprint.
- Judy Love, North American vice president of the Corteco division, stated that the partnership strengthens their ability to deliver comprehensive solutions to customers across North America and globally.
- Judy Love expressed excitement about welcoming Alto’s skilled team into the Freudenberg organization.
- The acquisition marks a strategic move for Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies, reinforcing its commitment to long-term growth, operational excellence and customer-focused innovation in the transmission aftermarket sector.
