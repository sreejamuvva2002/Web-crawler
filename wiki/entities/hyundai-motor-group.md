# Hyundai Motor Group

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Savannah, Georgia, Bryan County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Hyundai Motor Group announced a $12.6 billion investment in Georgia that includes a new metaplant near Savannah and two battery joint ventures with LG Energy System and SK On, projected to produce 500,000 electric and hybrid vehicles annually and create up to 40,000 direct and indirect jobs.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The metaplant will manufacture electric and hybrid vehicles for the U.S. market, expanding Georgia’s EV vehicle manufacturing capacity.
