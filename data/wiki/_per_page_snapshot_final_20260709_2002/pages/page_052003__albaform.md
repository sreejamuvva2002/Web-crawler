# ALBAform

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-17

**Source:** [ALBAform Facility Tour – Next Generation Manufacturing](https://nextgenerationmfg.org/event/albaform-facility-tour/)  ·  `page_052003`

## Facts

- ALBAform is a second-generation family business with 30+ years of experience and manufacturing locations in the U.S. and the Czech Republic.
- ALBAform’s expertise is in forming, welding, and assembling metal components for the automotive, agriculture, and e-mobility sectors.
- A facility tour at ALBAform's Flowery Branch, GA location was scheduled for May 17, 2023 @ 10:00 am – 12:30 pm EDT.
- Monika Vintrlikova, Senior Executive and Honorary Consul for the Czech Republic, along with the ALBAform team, hosted a tour of their Flowery Branch, GA facility for Next Generation Manufacturing.
- The Flowery Branch facility encompasses 120,000 square feet of production, distribution, and storage space.
- The facility employs 145 employees.
- Operations include 20 high-tech CNC bending machines.
- Operations include 16 high-tech welding robots.
- The facility produces 1.2 million seat structures per year.
- The facility processes 110 truckloads of steel per year.
- ALBAform holds Georga MADE certification.
- ALBAform holds ISO and IATF certifications.
- The Flowery Branch location is home to Altanta Cylinders, subsidiary of Cylinders Holdings.
- ALBAform is Georgia’s newest Czech company to invest and establish operations in the state.
- Event registration cost $35 and included a facility tour, discussion with leadership, lunch, and networking.
- Registration is open to executives, directors, and senior managers of manufacturing companies.
- Refunds are provided for cancellations made up to 72 hours in advance.
