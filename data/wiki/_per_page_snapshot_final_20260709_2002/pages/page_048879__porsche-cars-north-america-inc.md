# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-08-20

**Source:** [Automotive - PETERSEN AUTOMOTIVE MUSEUM TO DISPLAY WORLD’S MOST ICONIC PORSCHE STREET AND RACE CARS IN NEW EXHIBIT](https://kahnmedia.com/petersen-automotive-museum-display-worlds-iconic-porsche-street-race-cars-new-exhibit/)  ·  `page_048879`

## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) was established in 1984.
- PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of Porsche 911, 718 Boxster and 718 Cayman sports cars, Macan and Cayenne SUVs, and Panamera sports sedans.
- PCNA has been headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia since 1998.
- PCNA is located at One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA.
- PCNA is home to the first Porsche Experience Center in North America.
- The first Porsche Experience Center features a module-based 1.6 mile driver development track, business center, and fine dining restaurant, 356.
- PCNA operates a second Porsche Experience Center near Los Angeles.
- The second Porsche Experience Center is a 53-acre complex featuring a driver development track with eight educational modules totaling 4.1 miles, a business center, and Restaurant 917.
- PCNA employs over 300 people who provide parts, service, marketing, and training for 188 dealers.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Klaus Zellmer serves as President and CEO of Porsche Cars North America.
