# Great Dane

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-02

**Source:** [Great Dane Brand and Logo Redesign - Trailer Technician](https://trailertechnician.com/2022/02/great-dane-brand-and-logo-redesign/)  ·  `page_055375`

## Facts

- Great Dane is located in Savannah, Georgia.
- Great Dane is unveiling a new brand evolution representing a focus on end-to-end solutions for the transportation industry.
- The company is launching a new visual identity to support future communications, including a redesigned iconic Great Dane logo.
- Dean Engelage, President of Great Dane, stated that the new strategic direction builds on strengths while maximizing growth potential for an increasingly digital future.
- Engelage added that the bold new brand design reflects a vision to seamlessly connect products, data, and services for a complete transportation solution.
- The company is putting people responsible for the transportation of goods at the heart of its brand.
- The strategy highlights connections within the Great Dane ecosystem with a future focus on trailer and truck body innovations, telematics, and enhanced aftermarket services.
- Brandie Fuller, Great Dane’s VP of Commercial Excellence, stated that dedicating resources to people, products, and data-driven services delivers on promises of a digital tomorrow.
- Great Dane will continue meeting industry demands through collective experience, high-performance products, connectivity, and custom solutions.
- Emblems on the back of Great Dane trailers and truck bodies will remain part of the visual identity to honor company heritage.
- The company has over 120 years of history in connections and is reloading innovation with new ways of thinking beyond the trailer through connectivity within an integrated ecosystem.
