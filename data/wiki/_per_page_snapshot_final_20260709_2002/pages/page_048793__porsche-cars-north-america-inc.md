## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) is headquartered at One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA.
- PCNA was established in 1984 and has been headquartered in Atlanta since 1998.
- PCNA serves as the exclusive U.S. importer for the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera, and Taycan.
- PCNA operates a Porsche Experience Center in Atlanta featuring a module-based 1.6-mile driver development track, a business center, and Restaurant 356.
- PCNA operates a second Porsche Experience Center near Los Angeles featuring a 53-acre complex with an eight-module, 4.1-mile driver development track, a business center, and Restaurant 917.
- PCNA supports 192 independently owned and operated Porsche dealerships across the U.S., supplying parts, service, marketing, and training to them.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- The IMSA WeatherTech SportsCar Championship features Prototype (P) cars showcasing the latest automotive technology and Grand Touring (GT) cars based on road-going production models.
- The IMSA Michelin Pilot Challenge features sports cars closely identifiable with street vehicles across GS and TCR classes.
