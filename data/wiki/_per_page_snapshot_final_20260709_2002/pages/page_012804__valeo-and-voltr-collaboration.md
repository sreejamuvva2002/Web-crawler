# Valeo and VoltR Collaboration

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-06

**Source:** [Valeo and VoltR collaborate on the circularity of micromobility batteries - electrive.com](https://www.electrive.com/2025/10/06/valeo-and-voltr-collaborate-on-the-circularity-of-micromobility-batteries/)  ·  `page_012804`

## Facts

- Valeo and VoltR are two French companies collaborating on the circularity of micromobility batteries.
- The collaboration utilizes Valeo’s experience in electronic battery repair and VoltR’s expertise in battery reconditioning and cell pack manufacturing.
- The companies state the initiative will enable material savings of “at least 95 per cent.”
- The offer targets fleet managers, vehicle manufacturers, repair networks, and battery producers amid a boom of micromobility electric vehicles in Europe.
- Micromobility electric vehicles include electric bicycles, scooters, and other light vehicles.
- The process first determines whether used batteries can be repaired.
- If batteries need replacement, VoltR provides “remanufactured energy packs with a compatible cell reference to ensure the maintenance of initial certifications and pack performances.”
- Valeo and VoltR provide a structured, local warranty and after-sales service for reconditioned energy packs.
- Isabelle d’Ambrosio-Pierre, Valeo Service OES & Smart Mobility Vice President, stated the solution addresses environmentally-friendly battery management, economical mobility, and reliable access to repair components.
- d’Ambrosio-Pierre noted the battery repair solution is innovative and essential for less carbon-intensive urban mobility.
- Valeo collaborates with PanGood to develop an Axial Flux Generator.
- Valeo uses Rohm SiC modules for electric drives.
- Valeo cooperates on electric motor development with Mahle.
- Maxime Bleskine, General Manager of VoltR, stated VoltR’s mission is to minimize the environmental impact of batteries by maximizing their lifespan through remanufacturing.
- Bleskine added that partnering with Valeo allows them to industrialize their approach and scale their offering across Europe.
