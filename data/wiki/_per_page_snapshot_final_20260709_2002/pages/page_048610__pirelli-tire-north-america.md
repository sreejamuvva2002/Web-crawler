# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2009-10-01

**Source:** [Mauro Pessi Named New CEO of Pirelli Tire North America - Auto Service World](https://www.autoserviceworld.com/mauro-pessi-named-new-ceo-of-pirelli-tire-north-america/)  ·  `page_048610`

## Facts

- Mauro Pessi will assume the position of CEO of Pirelli Tire North America, effective October 1, 2009.
- Pessi previously served as VP of sales and marketing and CEO of Latin American operations at Pirelli before departing in 2008 to become CEO at Brembo.
- Pessi will replace Hugh Pace, who is retiring after having been chairman and CEO of the company for nearly three years.
- Hugh Pace will continue his role as chairman of Pirelli Tire North America through year end 2009.
- Pirelli Tire North America designs, develops, manufactures, and markets tires for passenger vehicles in both the original equipment and replacement markets.
- The company also markets and distributes tires for motorcycles and motorsports.
- Pirelli's facility is located in Rome, Georgia.
- The Rome, Georgia facility employs a Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) with state-of-the-art technology to manufacture tires for both export and domestic markets.
