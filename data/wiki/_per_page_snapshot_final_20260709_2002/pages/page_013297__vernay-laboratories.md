# Vernay Laboratories

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2008-11-27

**Source:** [Vernay to demolish building • The Yellow Springs News](https://ysnews.com/news/2008/11/vernay-to-demolish-building)  ·  `page_013297`

## Facts

- In 2004, Vernay moved its North American production operations to Georgia and closed its manufacturing plant at the corner of Dayton Street and East Enon Road.
- Vernay relocated its production force, which once numbered about 200 employees, to two plants in Georgia and a small facility in South Carolina.
- The company cited shifting markets and the demands of contamination cleanup in Yellow Springs as reasons for moving its production operations to Georgia.
