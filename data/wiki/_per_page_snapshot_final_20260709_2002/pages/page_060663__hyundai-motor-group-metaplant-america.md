## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group deploys XCIENT hydrogen fuel cell trucks for HMGMA clean logistics.
- In cooperation with Glovis America, HMGMA announced the deployment of Hyundai XCIENT hydrogen fuel cell trucks.
- HMGMA announced that its second production vehicle will be the all-electric 2026 IONIQ 9 three-row SUV.
- Hyundai announced the release of the refreshed 2025 IONIQ 5, including a rugged new IONIQ 5 XRT variant.
- HMGMA officially announced that the first production vehicle to be produced at its new Metaplant will be the 2025 Hyundai IONIQ 5.
- Hyundai Motor Company shared its hydrogen vision and introduced its U.S. clean logistics business powered by the Class 8 XCIENT.
- Hyundai contributed to local Savannah nonprofit organizations to support safety, education, and reforestation in the community.
- Hyundai Motor Group signed a 15-year power purchase agreement (PPA) with Matrix Renewables.
- Hyundai Motor America and HMGMA joined the 200th Anniversary of the Saint Patrick’s Day Parade in Savannah, Georgia.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp, First Lady Marty A. Kemp, along with the president and global COO of Hyundai Motor Company, participated in HMGMA events.
- HMGMA announced the design of a Bryan County water tank created by Savannah College of Art and Design (SCAD) students during a SCADpro design challenge.
- Hyundai announced a new wage structure for its production team members at Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama.
- HMGMA celebrated the one-year anniversary of its groundbreaking in Bryan County by officially releasing the latest design renderings.
