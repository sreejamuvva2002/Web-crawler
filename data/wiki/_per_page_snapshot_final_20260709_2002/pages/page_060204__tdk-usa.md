# TDK USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Delivers precision passive components including ceramic capacitors, inductors, power supplies, and sensors to keep networks running and equipment performing.
- Offers multi-layer ceramic capacitors from 01005 to 2220 packages, rated 6.3V to 630V, with high-reliability automotive and telecom grades available.
- Provides wire-wound, thin-film, and multilayer inductors for power conversion, signal filtering, and EMI suppression in network equipment.
- Supplies Mn-Zn and Ni-Zn ferrite cores, snap-on filters, and common mode chokes for electromagnetic interference suppression in data lines.
- Offers AC-DC, DC-DC converters, and programmable power supplies (TDK-Lambda series) covering 5W to 10kW output, with medical and industrial safety certifications.
- Provides MEMS motion sensors (InvenSense), temperature sensors, pressure sensors, and piezoelectric actuators for IoT, automotive, and industrial monitoring.
- Supplies RF inductors, multilayer baluns, couplers, and filters designed for 5G NR, Wi-Fi 6E, and Bluetooth LE front-end modules and base stations.
- AEC-Q200 qualified capacitors, sensors, and power inductors are available for ADAS, EV powertrains, and in-vehicle infotainment systems.
- Supplies high-reliability passive components and power supplies for PLC controllers, motor drives, and factory SCADA communication systems.
- Provides UL 60601 rated power supplies, low-noise capacitors, and precision sensors for diagnostic imaging, patient monitors, and surgical equipment.
- Offers high-capacitance MLCCs, DC-DC converters, and EMI suppression components for server PSUs, storage arrays, and switching fabrics.
- Provides ultra-compact capacitors, thin-film inductors, and MEMS sensors for smartphones, wearables, and smart home devices.
- Maintains over 80,000 SKUs across capacitors, inductors, ferrites, power supplies, and sensors as a single supplier for passive BOMs.
- Operates 30+ manufacturing and distribution facilities worldwide with regional warehousing for reduced lead times and 98.5% on-time delivery.
- Supports order quantities from prototype (as low as 10 pcs) to volume production runs, with tape-and-reel, bulk, and custom packaging options.
- Holds ISO 9001, IATF 16949, and ISO 14001 certifications, with every component lot traceable from raw material to final shipment.
- A major base station OEM consolidated its passive BOM for a 64T64R Massive MIMO radio unit requiring 1,200+ MLCCs per board, selecting TDK's C2012X7R1C475K capacitor after SEAT simulation confirmed 4.2 uF retained capacitance at operating voltage.
- The base station project used 15 TDK inductor part numbers across 8 voltage rails, reducing the supplier count from four to one and cutting procurement lead time from 14 weeks to 6 weeks.
- A hyperscale data center operator transitioning from 12V to 48V bus architecture selected TDK's SPM12565 series inductor for requirements above 30A saturation current and below 1 m-ohm DCR.
- The Lambda HFE2500 rectifier shelf provided 96.2% efficiency at 50% load, reducing annual cooling costs by an estimated $18 per rack compared to the previous 94% efficient platform.
