# Foam Products Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Leading Underlay Factories For Laminate Flooring In The Usa [2025 Edition] - BAIER FLOOR](https://www.baierfloor.com/blog/leading-underlay-factories-for-laminate-flooring-in-the-usa-2025-edition/)  ·  `page_058626`

## Facts

- FPC stands out as one of the world’s largest producers of acoustical underlayment for laminate flooring in the USA.
- As of 2025, it supplies a wide range of high-performance solutions for both residential and commercial markets, with a strong focus on noise reduction and easy installation.
- In late 2024, FPC invested $15 million into a state-of-the-art IXPE (Irradiated Cross-linked Polyethylene) manufacturing plant in Trion, Georgia.
- The facility covers 100,000 sq. ft. and can expand up to 300,000 sq. ft., located on a 20-acre site.
- This strategic location near America’s flooring manufacturing corridor cuts logistics time and costs, providing faster delivery for “Made in USA” underlayment—reducing reliance on Asian imports.
- The new plant is projected to create about 100 new jobs by the end of 2025.
- FPC’s top products include Eco Ultimate Silencer™, Eco Silencer HD FOF, and Silencer™ LVT.
- These product lines support all installation types: floating, glue-down, and nail-down.
- The underlayments are compatible with laminate, hardwood, engineered, bamboo, WPC, SPC, EVP, and luxury vinyl flooring.
- All FPC underlayments are GREENGUARD Gold Certified for low emissions.
- Wide North American distribution includes OEMs, flooring distributors, and major multi-store retailers.
- FPC rejoined the North American Laminate Flooring Association (NALFA) in 2021 as an associate member.
- The Georgia facility was built with support from state incentives and new infrastructure.
- Current capacity allows for both present and future growth as market needs evolve.
- The new plant can also manufacture foams for automotive, medical, and industrial use.
- FPC produces items like shoe insoles, non-skid pads, seat cushions, mouse pads, and laundry press pads, serving a worldwide client base.
- FPC meets the rising demand for underlayments that are both U.S.-made and environmentally certified.
- Its premium products are known for versatility, great sound absorption, and broad flooring compatibility.
