# Perkins Small Engines LLC (Griffin Plant)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- The Perkins manufacturing facility in Griffin, Georgia supplies engines to the construction, agriculture, material handling, and power markets in North America.
- The facility was established in 2004.
- It produces the compact and powerful 400 series engines with an annual capacity of 50,000 units.
- The Griffin manufacturing facility utilizes a single-piece flow production system that electronically tracks every engine from assembly through heat testing to painting and final customer coating.
- Jeff Rohr serves as the Plant Manager for the Griffin facility.
- The 400 series engines produced in Griffin are available in 2, 3, and 4 cylinder configurations with a seamless power range of 10.2-49.2 kW (13.7-66 hp) for the North American market.
- Production plans include the new 404F-E22T/TA common rail direct injection engine, which generates 36-50 kW (48-67 hp) of power.
- Engine assembly occurs on crawler tracks and features electronic screens that provide operators with build data specific to each engine.
- The facility uses electronic torque control tools to ensure every fastener is tightened precisely according to technical specifications during assembly.
- After assembly, engines undergo comprehensive heat testing on one of ten test benches located within the same soundproof enclosure.
- Following testing, engines are painted, coated, subjected to 100% inspection, and then shipped directly to customers.
- The Griffin facility minimizes lead times and delivery times by supplying engines directly from the factory to North American customers.
- Engine quality follows the Caterpillar Production System (CPS), ensuring efficient processes, tested and validated parts, and strict quality control across all Perkins manufacturing facilities.
- The workforce consists of a trained US team focused on the North American market.
- The facility is located at 325 Green Valley Road, Griffin, Georgia, USA, 30224.
- The site covers 6 acres (5,100 m²).
- The facility employs 100 people.
- Engines manufactured in Griffin meet the strictest emission standards and can be customized to OEM requirements for years of reliable service.
