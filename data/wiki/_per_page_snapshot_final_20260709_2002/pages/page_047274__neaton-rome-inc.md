# Neaton Rome Inc.

## Facts

- A subsidiary of Neaton Auto Products Manufacturing Inc. will invest $8 million to expand a plant in Floyd County and create 50 jobs.
- Neaton Rome Inc. currently employs 450 people.
- Neaton Rome Inc. is a provider of airbags, steering wheels and interior/exterior plastic trim to automakers.
- As part of the expansion, the company is adding four injection molding presses at the Rome plant used to mold plastics.
- The Governor’s Office announced Neaton Rome’s planned expansion.
- According to the Governor’s Office, there are more than 250 automotive manufacturing plants in the state employing nearly 18,000 people.
- In June, Hitachi Automotive Systems Americas completed work on a third production plant in Walton County.
- Hitachi Automotive Systems Americas produces electric power steering systems and variable displacement pumps.
- The new Hitachi plant in Walton County will add up to 250 jobs to an existing workforce of about 350.
