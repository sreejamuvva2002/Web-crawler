# Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-06-25

**Source:** [Panasonic Automotive Systems Joins SOAFEE Governing Body, Strengthening Global SDV Standardization Leadership](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/panasonic-automotive-systems-joins-soafee-governing-body-strengthening-global-sdv-standardization-leadership-302491002.html)  ·  `page_029079`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd. (PAS) announced its appointment to the Governing Body of SOAFEE (Scalable Open Architecture for Embedded Edge), a global board dedicated to driving Software-Defined Vehicle (SDV) advancement.
- Since joining SOAFEE as a Technical Steering Committee (TSC) member in 2022, PAS has demonstrated strong leadership in Open-Source Software (OSS) standardization efforts, particularly in the Asia-Pacific region.
- PAS developed VirtIO-based solutions—Unified HMI and Virtual SkipGen—which align with SOAFEE's vision of hardware-agnostic software.
- These solutions significantly contribute to promoting OSS that enables decoupled development of software from hardware in automotive systems, including next-generation cockpits.
- PAS's solutions have already been adopted as SOAFEE blueprints to enable broader utilization and collaboration for future automotive software development evolution.
- In November 2024, PAS assumed leadership of the newly established SOAFEE APAC Regional Hub, with operations officially launching in May 2025.
- The SOAFEE APAC Regional Hub initiative aims to invigorate SOAFEE activities for members in the Asia-Pacific region, who represent a significant portion of SOAFEE's global membership.
- PAS became the ninth Governing Body member among SOAFEE's current membership of over 150 companies.
- Masashige Mizuyama, Executive Vice President and CTO of Panasonic Automotive Systems, stated that the company shares SOAFEE's vision to develop a seamless cloud-native environment for fast and continuous deliveries to automotive products and believes device virtualization will play a significant role in achieving it.
- PAS will contribute to industry evolution by promoting collaboration with other standardization organizations, supporting global SOAFEE activities growth, and addressing opportunities and challenges in SDV and cloud-native development.
- PAS was launched on April 1, 2022, as an operating company responsible for the automotive systems business under the Panasonic Group's operating company system.
- On December 2, 2024, PAS moved to a management structure where 80% of its shares are held by funds managed by an affiliate of Apollo Global Management, Inc., and 20% by Panasonic Holdings Corporation.
- As a Tier 1 company, PAS provides advanced proprietary technologies such as infotainment systems to automakers in Japan and overseas.
