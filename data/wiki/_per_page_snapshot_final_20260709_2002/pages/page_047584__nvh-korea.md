# NVH Korea

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-22

**Source:** [South Korean firm plans $72M Georgia plant to make battery parts, hiring 140 | AP News](https://apnews.com/article/georgia-nvh-korea-electric-vehicles-new-factory-e41946fbbaa9da8953c0909c73dc3704)  ·  `page_047584`

## Facts

- A South Korean company will open a factory in suburban Atlanta to make parts for electric batteries.
- NVH Korea plans to invest $72 million in a plant in Locust Grove, south of Atlanta.
- The plant will hire 140 workers.
- The plant will manufacture sensors, connections, and protectors for electric vehicle batteries.
- Operations are scheduled to begin in 2024.
- Chairman and CEO Ja Kyum Koo stated that this is NVH Korea’s first venture into making components for electric batteries.
- NVH Korea is based in Ulsan, South Korea.
- NVH Korea will be the 10th supplier to locate in the region.
- Hyundai announced in 2022 that it would build a $5.5 billion plant to assemble electric vehicles and batteries in Ellabell, Georgia, near Savannah.
- The Hyundai site could grow to 8,100 employees.
- The Hyundai site is slated to begin producing vehicles in 2025.
- Suppliers have pledged to invest nearly $2.1 billion.
- Suppliers have pledged to hire nearly 4,900 people.
- This will be NVH Korea’s second plant in Georgia.
- Subsidiary AFS America is located in Columbus.
- Subsidiary AFS America makes floor mats and cargo trays for the Hyundai plant in Montgomery, Alabama.
- Subsidiary AFS America makes floor mats and cargo trays for the sister Kia plant in West Point, Georgia.
- Subsidiary AFS America makes floor mats and cargo trays for General Motors.
- NVH Korea operates 19 plants worldwide across South Korea, North America, China, India, Europe, and Russia.
- NVH Korea manufactures sound-dampening parts and materials, floor mats, headliners, insulators, and parts to help manage engine and cooling system temperatures.
- The state of Georgia will pay to train NVH Korea’s workers.
- The company could qualify for $1.2 million in state income tax credits, at $1,750 per job over five years.
- To qualify for the tax credits, workers must earn at least $31,300 a year.
- The company could qualify for additional incentives, including property tax breaks from Locust Grove and Henry County.
