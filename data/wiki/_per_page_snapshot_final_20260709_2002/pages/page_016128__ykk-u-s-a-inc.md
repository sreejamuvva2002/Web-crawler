## Facts

- In 1960, YKK® entered the United States zipper market.
- YKK® (U.S.A.) Inc. originated in New York’s garment district with only four metal zipper styles in its line.
- YKK® (U.S.A.) Inc. is now the country’s number one supplier of zippers.
- The company operates a factory site in Macon, Ga.
- The Macon facility contains 14 modern plants and employs over 900 workers.
- The Macon site produces 7 million zippers a day across 1,500 styles in more than 500+ standard colors.
- It is the largest zipper factory in the world.
- The Macon site produces its own brass to make the teeth and slider parts of a zipper.
- The same factory also produces and dyes the yarn that is woven into the fabric portion of a zipper.
- YKK® produces the machines that make the zippers.
- YKK® controls every step of the manufacturing process to ensure the highest possible quality at the lowest possible cost.
- YKK® products use the best material, made on the most modern equipment and are mass produced under extremely severe quality control.
- This attention to detail is why YKK® is the most reliable and by far the most used zipper in the world.
- Counterfeit YKK® zippers are becoming a growing problem because no other manufacturer can match YKK®'s quality at its low price.
- While counterfeits imitate YKK® zippers in appearance, they do not compare to the quality of genuine YKK®.
- The company advises customers to insist on genuine YKK® zippers to protect their products and brand.
- Quality Zipper & Supply only stocks genuine, made-in-the USA YKK®.
- Many distributors selling discounted YKK® products are actually jobbers that combine components and make the zippers themselves.
- Some of the components these distributors buy, like sliders, are YKK®, but they assemble them on cheaper generic zipper chain from China.
- The slider says YKK® but the chain is not, which allows these distributors to offer lower prices.
- These mixed-component products are not made in a YKK® factory by YKK® workers, meaning no quality assurances can be made on their zippers.
- There is no quality control and no way for these distributors to back up the quality control paperwork and government safety controls that genuine YKK® provides directly.
- Every genuine YKK® zipper and part adheres to all quality control specs for use in clothing.
