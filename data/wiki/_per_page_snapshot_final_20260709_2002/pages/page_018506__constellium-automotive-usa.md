## Facts

- Constellium Automotive USA in Georgia is laying off about half of its workforce.
- The company said the elimination of 57 of its 99 positions is permanent, according to a Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification (WARN) filed with the state Department of Labor.
- Jobs include operator, material handler, maintenance technician, quality manager, EHS specialist, operations supervisor, and shift supervisor.
- The employees are not represented by a union.
- The facility in White, Georgia, opened in 2017 and produces advanced aluminum automotive structural components and crash management systems.
- The company did not immediately respond to a request for comment.
