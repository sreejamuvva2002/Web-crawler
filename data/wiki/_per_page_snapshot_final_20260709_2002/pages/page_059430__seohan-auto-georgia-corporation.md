# Seohan Auto Georgia Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-09-12

**Source:** [Seohan Auto Georgia is Open for Business in Liberty County - Expansion Solutions](https://www.expansionsolutionsmagazine.com/seohan-auto-georgia-is-open-for-business-in-liberty-county/)  ·  `page_059430`

## Facts

- Seohan Auto Georgia held a Grand Opening Ceremony for its new manufacturing facility located in the Tradeport East Business Center in Liberty County.
- The facility will produce H/shafts, axles, and brake systems for Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs).
- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America is identified as an OEM customer for the facility's products.
- The facility's operations are expected to create at least 180 new jobs.
- The project represents a total investment of more than $72 million in Liberty County.
- Georgia Department of Economic Development Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that local community investments in the Tradeport East Business Center enabled Seohan Auto Georgia to meet a challenging timeline to get up and running.
- State Representative Al Williams, who serves as Liberty County Development Authority Board Chairman, noted that the site selection process began in November 2022 and required competing against other locations before successfully securing the project for Liberty County.
- Seohan Auto Georgia Corporation operates as a new subsidiary of Korea Movenex Co., Ltd.
- Parent company Korea Movenex was established in 1974 and employs more than 2,800 people globally.
- Korea Movenex leverages advanced technology and automotive parts industry experience to develop innovative solutions and supply components to collaborative partners.
- The Liberty County Development Authority (LCDA) recruits industries for new locations, develops industrial parks, constructs shell buildings, and supports workforce development programs.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) serves as the state’s sales and marketing arm and lead agency for attracting new business investment and encouraging industry expansion.
