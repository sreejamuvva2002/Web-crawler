# Wabash National Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-18

**Source:** [Wabash Expands Southeast Footprint to Strengthen Customer Access and Service - Red Book Lumber Data](https://www.redbooklumberdata.com/2025/09/25/wabash-expands-southeast-footprint-to-strengthen-customer-access-and-service/)  ·  `page_014594`

## Facts

- Wabash, a leader in end-to-end supply chain solutions for the transportation, logistics and infrastructure markets, announced a significant expansion of its network in the Southeast.
- Wabash is strengthening customer access and service in the greater Atlanta region with three strategic growth moves: opening a new Parts & Service center, expanding its dealer relationship with Fleetco and adding CS Truck and Trailer to its Preferred Partner Network.
- Dave Hill, vice president of Parts & Services at Wabash, said these moves reflect how Wabash is building an extensive partner ecosystem that puts parts, service and support closer to customers.
- Hill stated that together, the Atlanta expansions mean faster turnaround, more coverage and better reliability for fleets operating across Georgia, Alabama, South Carolina and Tennessee.
- Wabash’s new Atlanta-area Parts and Services center is strategically located at the heart of the Southeast’s trucking corridor.
- The center provides faster access to parts, service and truck body upfitting.
- It will offer Wabash’s Ready-to-Mount (RTM) truck bodies for dry freight and platform applications with rapid turnaround times for in-stock chassis.
- The Atlanta site joins Wabash service centers in California, Florida, Ohio, Pennsylvania and Texas, with additional locations on the way.
- Doors to the new Atlanta Parts & Service center opened September 18, 2025.
- Fleetco, a long-standing Wabash dealer, will now represent Wabash in the greater Atlanta market with trailer sales.
- This expansion broadens Wabash’s dealer footprint in the Southeast and gives customers additional access to new and used equipment through a trusted partner.
- Bill Tirrill, president at Fleetco Inc., said they are proud to grow their existing partnership with Wabash in the greater Atlanta market.
- Tirrill noted that with more than 14 years of experience working closely with Wabash, the expansion allows Fleetco to expand its sales force across the region and provide clients immediate access to new and used trailers.
- Fleetco will improve the expertise and commitment clients expect from the Fleetco/Wabash team and create more opportunities to serve clients.
- Wabash added three CS Truck and Trailer locations to its Preferred Partner Network in Georgia.
- The addition brings expert service, maintenance and Wabash Genuine Parts to more customers through authorized third-party dealerships.
- The Preferred Partner Network model is central to Wabash’s strategy of making high-quality parts more accessible through a seamless national network.
- Hill added that expanding the dealer and partner network is critical to delivering the speed and service customers expect as their customer base grows.
- Atlanta serves as an example of how Wabash is executing its strategy by combining service centers, dealer partners, and the Preferred Partner Network to increase coverage and elevate the customer experience.
- Wabash (NYSE: WNC) is headquartered in Lafayette, Indiana, and designs, manufactures, and services dry freight and refrigerated trailers, flatbed trailers, tank trailers, dry and refrigerated truck bodies, structural composite panels and products, trailer aerodynamic solutions, and specialty food grade processing equipment.
- Wabash enables customers to thrive by providing insight into tomorrow and delivering pragmatic solutions today to move everything from first to final mile.
