## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- PCNA was established in 1984.
- PCNA has been headquartered in Atlanta since 1998.
- PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche 911, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera, and Taycan.
- The Porsche Taycan is a fully electric car.
- The Porsche Taycan Turbo S features four-wheel drive and four-wheel steering.
- The Porsche Taycan Turbo S is equipped with navigation systems.
- The Porsche Taycan Turbo S contains 396 battery cells.
- The Porsche Taycan Turbo S has two motors developing a peak of 750hp directed through all four wheels via a two-speed gearbox.
- The Porsche Car Configurator can be used by anyone to customize and pre-order any vehicle in the lineup at any time.
- Porsche boasts some 30,000-plus motorsport wins to date.
- Frank Wiesmann serves as Manager of Product Communications for the Public Relations Department at PCNA, located at One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA.
