# YKK AP America Inc.

## Facts

- YKK AP America Inc. has opened a newly expanded commercial and residential design center in Atlanta.
- The center, named Studio 101, originally launched in 2021 with a focus on commercial architectural projects.
- The expansion allows YKK AP to include a wider range of residential door and window products and a training center.
- The training center is designed to provide customers with hands-on experiences to ensure proper installation of entrances, storefront, curtainwall, window wall, window and door products.
- Studio 101 is located at YKK AP’s headquarters in Atlanta.
- President and CEO Oliver Stepe stated that the newly expanded commercial and residential design center and training space brings a more creative and collaborative way to work.
- Oliver Stepe noted the expansion illuminates the company's vision to frame the future by partnering more deeply with customers, partners, and the industry.
