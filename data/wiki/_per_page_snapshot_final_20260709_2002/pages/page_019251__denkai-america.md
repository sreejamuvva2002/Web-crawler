# Denkai America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-07-20

**Source:** [The new headquarters for copper foil manufacturer Denkai America will bring 250 jobs to Richmond County](https://thegeorgiasun.com/business-economy/the-new-headquarters-for-copper-foil-manufacturer-denkai-america-will-bring-250-jobs-to-richmond-county/)  ·  `page_019251`

## Facts

- Denkai America is the only North American manufacturer of high-quality electrodeposited copper foil used in printed electronics.
- Denkai America will locate its new manufacturing facility and North American Headquarters in Richmond County.
- The company’s initial investment will be $150 million to establish a copper foil production facility, with a focus on electric vehicle battery foil.
- The facility will be located on a 115-acre site.
- The company has a goal to invest a total of $430 million and create 250 new jobs over the next five years.
- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp stated that selecting Denkai America strengthens Georgia's status as a leader in the emerging electric mobility industry.
- Gov. Brian Kemp emphasized the state has been laser focused on bringing quality jobs to highly-trained Georgians and highlighted Georgia’s top-ranking pro-business environment.
- Denkai America’s materials have traditionally been used in printed circuit board applications, primarily for the aerospace and defense industries.
- The company's materials are also now used in the lithium-ion batteries powering electric vehicles.
- Denkai has operated for more than 60 years with team members worldwide and operations in Japan and North America.
- Denkai Group President and CEO Heday Nakajima, Ph.D., stated the investment highlights their commitment to leading ED battery foil manufacturing in North America.
- Heday Nakajima expressed gratitude to the teams in Richmond County and the State of Georgia for making this project possible.
- Construction is expected to begin in September 2022 as the first part of a three-phase investment.
- The facility will be fully operational by December 2024.
- The company will be hiring for skilled industrial technicians, production managers, and engineers.
- Denkai America President and CEO Nobu Masuda stated the company is thrilled to expand its manufacturing presence into Augusta, which has the skilled and talented work force needed for growth.
- EV-related projects have contributed more than $12.6 billion in investments to Georgia since 2020.
- EV-related projects have contributed more than 17,800 new jobs to Georgia since 2020.
- The Development Authority of Augusta, Georgia, welcomed Denkai America and its North American Headquarters to the Augusta Corporate Park.
- Augusta Economic Development Authority Chairman Steven Kendrick stated the company will bring high-skilled jobs to Augusta and the Augusta Corporate Park.
- Steven Kendrick thanked the Augusta Commission and the Augusta Utilities Department for their support and hard work to make this happen.
- Augusta Corporate Park is a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development Certified park.
- To earn a GRAD program certification, a property must undergo due diligence and complete specific certification requirements prior to a visit by a prospective business.
- Completing the certification program with the Georgia Department of Economic Development is a proactive way both community planners and landowners can help catalyze economic growth and industrial development.
