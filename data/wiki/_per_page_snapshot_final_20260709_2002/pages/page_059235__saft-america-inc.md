## Facts

- Saft America Inc., Valdosta, Georgia, has been awarded a maximum $7,542,506 firm-fixed-price, indefinite-quantity contract for storage batteries used in support of the H1, Seahawk and CH-53 helicopters.
- This was a competitive acquisition with two responses received.
- This is a three-year base contract with two one-year option periods.
- The performance completion date is April 24, 2028.
- Using customers are Air Force, Navy, Army, Marine Corps and Coast Guard.
- Type of appropriation is fiscal 2025 through 2028 defense working capital funds.
- The contracting activity is the Defense Logistics Agency Land and Maritime, Columbus, Ohio (SPE7LX-25-D-0045).
