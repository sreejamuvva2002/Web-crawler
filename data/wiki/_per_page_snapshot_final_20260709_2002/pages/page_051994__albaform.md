# ALBAform

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-09

**Source:** [GAM welcomes Albaform - Georgia Association of Manufacturers](https://www.gamfg.org/2024/10/09/albaform-joins-gam/)  ·  `page_051994`

## Facts

- ALBAform, a manufacturer of metal fabrication and engineering services, has joined the Georgia Association of Manufacturers (GAM).
- Headquartered in Flowery Branch, ALBAform excels in custom metal fabrication, CNC tube bending, and the assembly of metal components for the automotive, heavy equipment, and e-mobility sectors.
- The company boasts over 30 years of international experience.
- ALBAform is a second-generation family business with multiple locations across the US, Mexico, and Czech Republic.
- Monika Vintrlikova serves as Chief Executive Officer.
