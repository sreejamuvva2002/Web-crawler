# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-12-17

**Source:** [GA Gov. Kemp announces $5 billion EV maker Rivian plant east of Atlanta](https://www.savannahnow.com/story/news/2021/12/17/georgia-rivian-plant-jobs-atlanta-brian-kemp/8939605002/)  ·  `page_000754`

## Facts

- Electric vehicle maker Rivian confirmed it plans to build a $5 billion assembly plant and battery factory in Georgia.
- The confirmation was announced on Thursday, December 17, 2021.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp called the project “the largest single economic development project ever in this state’s history.”
- A Rivian executive outlined the employment plans at a press conference with Gov. Kemp at the State Capitol.
- The factory is planned to initially employ 7,500 workers.
- State officials have said the jobs tally could grow to 10,000.
- The plant will be built on a sprawling swath of farmland and pine forest about an hour’s drive east of Atlanta.
- Rivian had previously pitched the new Bryan County megasite earlier in the year.
