# Wabash National Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-10-26

**Source:** [Trailer Maker Wabash National Introduces First Truck Bodies - TT](https://www.ttnews.com/articles/trailer-maker-wabash-national-introduces-first-truck-bodies)  ·  `page_014470`

## Facts

- Semi-trailer manufacturer Wabash National Corp. introduced its first line of dry and refrigerated truck bodies to meet growing demand for urban delivery services.
- The new truck bodies are designed for Class 6, 7 and 8 chassis.
- Brent Yeagy, group president of commercial trailer products at Wabash National in Lafayette, Indiana, stated the company is very conscious of a shift happening in the trucking industry fueled by re-urbanization and e-commerce trends.
- Wabash stated its mission is to help customers be successful and support them as they work to meet final-mile delivery needs with the new truck body line.
- The new truck bodies will be displayed at the International Foodservice Distributors Association conference Oct. 27-28 in Phoenix.
- Production of truck bodies and other related equipment will take place at a dedicated facility in Lafayette and will ramp up over the next 18 months.
- Wabash is a leading manufacturer of dry and refrigerated trailers, platform and bulk tank trailers, intermodal equipment, aircraft refueling equipment, structural composite panels and trailer aerodynamic devices.
