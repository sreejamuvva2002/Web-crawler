# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-07-27

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America continuing to host job fairs to hire locally](https://fox28savannah.com/news/local/hyundai-metaplant-america-electric-vehicle-plant-manufacturing-bryan-county-georgia-hiring-fair-local-community-workforce-development-economy)  ·  `page_001213`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America is located in Chatham County, Ga.
- Many major companies around the city and world have partnered with Hyundai for the upcoming Metaplant.
- St. Joseph’s/Candler’s St. Mary’s Community Center is stepping up to help fill the need for local hires.
- Khailiah Robinson, Workforce Developer at St. Mary's Community Center, stated: “We’re strategically placed here for a reason... A lot of the members of our community don’t have a lot of transportation options. So, I thought it would be great to bring the employers to the community.”
- The company had two departments that were interviewing candidates at the event.
- Hyundai Glovis is a partner with Hyundai and is a logistics company looking to fill management positions.
- Kyle Horton, Hyundai Glovis Transportation Department Manager, stated: “We’re providing job opportunities, a large number of job opportunities in the area... So, the benefit of us being here in the aspect of potential employment opportunities for qualified candidates and what we’re going to put back into the community and cities and the state as a whole.”
- Robinson added that it’s important to bring large and small businesses, organizations, and companies to the area to hire locally.
- Khailiah Robinson noted: “Especially with an event like this, with Hyundai, a lot of our clients don’t know how to start to apply or feel like they’re qualified... So, this way it brings them into the community that way people can come in, ask questions, meet them face to face, and that way they can get a good view of what the local workforce really looks like.”
- With thousands of jobs created by the new Hyundai Meta Plant, the company’s staff say they plan to create numerous opportunities to develop strong employees.
- Tony Sutton with Hyundai Transys human resources stated: “One thing that Hyundai Transys is doing... is becoming the preferred employer by developing within the region... It’s very important for us to hire and we have to do it now, we can’t wait.”
- St. Joseph’s/Candler’s Hospital also announced a partnership with Hyundai to bring on-site medical centers to Hyundai’s Metaplant.
- Event organizers say the job fair was a success with over 50 residents in Savannah and surrounding areas showing up.
- Tony Sutton stated: "We want to give everyone in Savannah a seat at the table... We don’t want to leave anyone out when it comes down to these opportunities. So it’s important for us to come into these communities and engage with the local community."
