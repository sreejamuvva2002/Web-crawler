# Daechang Seat Corp. USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-03-18

**Source:** [Daechang Seat Corp. plans Phenix City expansion](https://businessalabama.com/daechang-seat-corp-plans-phenix-city-expansion/)  ·  `page_018897`

## Facts

- Daechang Seat Corp. USA will expand its manufacturing operations in Phenix City with a $23.4 million investment that will include a new facility.
- The new facility will be built on 22 acres purchased from the city in the Phenix Industrial Park.
- Daechang has been located in Phenix City since 2019.
- Daechang projects 300 new jobs over the next three years.
- Eddie Lowe, mayor of Phenix City, stated: “We’re thankful for the continued partnership that we have with DSC, and we’re excited to work alongside them in this expansion project.”
- Eddie Lowe added: “This continues to show that we are moving in the right direction and is evidence that Phenix City is a great place to do business.”
- Daechang is a South Korean company.
- Daechang has become one of the largest car seat frame producers in the world.
- Construction on the new facility will begin immediately.
- The new facility is projected to be in operation the first quarter of 2023.
- Montgomery’s Marshall Design Build LLC is the general contractor for the project.
