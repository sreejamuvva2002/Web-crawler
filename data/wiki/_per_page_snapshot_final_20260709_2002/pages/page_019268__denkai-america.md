# Denkai America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Denkai America Plans 250 Jobs in Right to Work Georgia | NILRR](https://nilrr.org/denkai-america-plans-250-jobs-in-right-to-work-georgia/)  ·  `page_019268`

## Facts

- Denkai America is adding its North American headquarters soon in Right to Work Richmond County, Georgia.
- The project will be made possible by an investment totaling $430 million.
- The company plans to create 250 new jobs.
- Georgia officials state the state is proud to be selected as the home of Denkai America, further strengthening its status as a leader in the emerging electric mobility and existing aerospace, defense, and technology industries.
- The state has been laser focused on bringing quality jobs to highly-trained, hardworking Georgians across the state.
- As the adoption of electric mobility becomes widespread, the State of Georgia is committed to harnessing this generational opportunity to ensure that Georgians employed in today’s automotive industry will have access to these jobs of the future here at home.
- Denkai America plays a vital role in the EV ecosystem, and officials are excited to welcome the company to Augusta and Richmond County.
- The investment is further evidence of how Georgia’s commitment to building lasting international relationships continues to drive economic partnerships across our state, including the recruitment of the wider electric mobility value chain.
- The Development Authority of Augusta, Georgia, welcomes Denkai America and its North American Headquarters to the Augusta Corporate Park.
- The company will bring high-skilled jobs to the growing list of companies that call Augusta and the Augusta Corporate Park home.
- The Augusta Commission and the Augusta Utilities Department provided support and hard work to make this happen.
