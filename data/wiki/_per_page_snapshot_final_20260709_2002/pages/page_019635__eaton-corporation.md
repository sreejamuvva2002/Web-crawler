# Eaton Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-11

## Facts

- Eaton Corporation announced plans to invest $340 million in a new manufacturing facility in Union County.
- The project will create 700 new jobs.
- The project represents the largest single economic development announcement in the county’s history.
- The investment reinforces Union County’s position within the advanced manufacturing and automotive supply chain.
- Eaton selected Union County following a competitive, multi-state site selection process.
- Key factors for the selection included the availability of a large, modern facility, access to a skilled workforce, and the county’s ability to coordinate state, local, and utility partners quickly and effectively.
- Union County worked alongside workforce and training partners, utility providers, and the South Carolina Department of Commerce to demonstrate readiness at scale.
- The county helped secure adjacent property to allow for future expansion, signaling a long-term commitment to the project.
- The investment brings significant economic impact to Union County by strengthening the local tax base and creating long-term employment opportunities for residents across the region.
