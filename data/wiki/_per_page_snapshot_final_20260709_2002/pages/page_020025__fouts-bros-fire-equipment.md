## Facts

- Fouts Bros Fire Equipment is a fire apparatus manufacturer located in Central Georgia.
- The company maintains locations in Smyrna, GA, USA; Smyrna, DE, USA; and Milledgeville, GA, USA.
- Fouts Bros operates within the Automotive, Industrial Engineering, Industrial Manufacturing, and Manufacturing industries.
- The company has an employee size of 11 to 50 people.
- Fouts Bros was founded in 2019.
- For over 30 years, the company has built custom fire trucks, fire rescue vehicles, pumper trucks, and tankers.
- The company applies rigorous production and engineering standards to develop its line of firefighting trucks and rescue vehicles.
- Product offerings include 2000-gallon and 3000-gallon tankers, brush trucks, light-duty rescue vehicles, pumpers, and the Fouts FOUR fire truck.
