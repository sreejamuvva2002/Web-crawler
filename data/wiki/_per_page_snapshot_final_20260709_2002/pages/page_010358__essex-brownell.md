# Essex Brownell

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Essex Brownell Announces Investment At McDonough, Georgia Distribution Center - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.de/news/essex-brownell-announces-investment-at-mcdonough-georgia-distribution-center/)  ·  `page_010358`

## Facts

- On May 15, 2023, Essex Brownell announced it will expand its operational presence in the southeastern U.S. by relocating and expanding its distribution center in McDonough, Georgia.
- The new facility will be located within the same industrial complex as the current site to ensure uninterrupted service for customers.
- Essex Brownell will retain its current staff and create opportunities to double employment at the new location.
- Rebecca Hunnicutt, Vice President of Operations and Customer Service, stated that expanding from over 7,000 square meters to over 40,000 square meters was an easy decision due to additional benefits.
- Hunnicutt noted that the company has been in McDonough since 1999 and prioritized retaining and growing its existing personnel while maintaining a strong relationship with the local property management team.
- Leadership cited business goals of "continuous investment in the region" and "better support for our customers" as key drivers for the expansion.
- This marks Essex Brownell's second operational expansion within one year, following the opening of a state-of-the-art facility in Reno, Nevada, in the second quarter of 2022.
- Hunnicutt expects current customers served outside the site to experience no service disruption and may perceive faster speeds due to the expansion.
- The company aims to reduce redundancies or overlaps with post-COVID supply chains by being larger, closer to customers, more flexible, and faster at delivering products.
- Essex Brownell is an international distributor of magnet wire and ancillary products for the construction and repair of motors, generators, transformers, and electrical components.
- Through its Essex Active processing facilities, Essex Brownell also provides custom manufacturing and rewinding services alongside discrete material delivery.
- The announcement was issued from Fort Wayne, Indiana.
