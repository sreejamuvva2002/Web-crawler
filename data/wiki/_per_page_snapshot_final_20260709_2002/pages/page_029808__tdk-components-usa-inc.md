# TDK Components USA, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 2008-08-18

**Source:** [08/18/2008: EPA to Honor TDK Components USA, Inc., in Peachtree City for Environmental Performance](https://www.epa.gov/archive/epapages/newsroom_archive/newsreleases/2cf56fc5b8c1adaf852574a90055bd83.html)  ·  `page_029808`

## Facts

- TDK Components USA, Inc. operates a manufacturing facility in Peachtree City, Ga.
- The facility produces multi-layer ceramic chip capacitors.
- TDK is one of two companies in the southeast and just five nationwide to receive the 2008 Performance Track Environmental Performance Award from the EPA.
- The award recognition event will include a lunch and a tour of TDK’s manufacturing facility.
- EPA Regional Administrator Jimmy Palmer will recognize TDK Components USA, Inc. as a recipient of the award.
- Marlin Gottschalk, Acting Director of the Pollution Prevention Assistance Division at Georgia Department of Natural Resources, will attend the event.
- Georgia State Representative Matt Ramsey will attend the event.
- Masatoshi Fujimoto, TDK President, will attend the event.
- Masaaki Taniguchi, TDK Plant Manager, will attend the event.
- Ken Takekawa, TDK Production Director, will attend the event.
- The recognition event is scheduled for Tuesday, August 19, 2008, from 10 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.
- The event will be held at TDK Components USA, Inc., located at 1 TDK Boulevard, Peachtree City, Ga.
- The Performance Track program recognizes and drives environmental excellence by encouraging facilities with strong environmental records to go above and beyond their legal requirements.
- Performance Track members typically set four public, measurable goals to improve the quality of air, water, and land.
- Program members include major corporations, small businesses, and public facilities that are steering a course toward environmental excellence.
