# Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Kautex Textron Wins Significant BEV Battery Housing Contract with Leading Automotive OEM](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20250725322671/en/Kautex-Textron-Wins-Significant-BEV-Battery-Housing-Contract-with-Leading-Automotive-OEM)  ·  `page_028404`

## Facts

- Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG (Kautex), a Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT) company, announced it has received an order from a leading automotive OEM for a full-BEV thermoplastic composite lower battery housing unit.
- The lower housing is part of the company’s Pentatonic battery enclosure portfolio, facilitating battery electric and hybrid electric vehicle production.
- Joerg Rautenstrauch serves as President & CEO of Kautex.
- The Kautex Pentatonic product line offers lightweight, customizable EV solutions made from thermoplastic composite or composite metal hybrids.
- These solutions enhance the thermal management of the battery pack and contribute to overall lightweight design, boosting vehicle performance and extending driving range.
- The lower housing seals and protects the vehicle battery while meeting extensive structural and stringent thermal requirements, including crush and bottom impact testing, and external and internal fire tests.
- The housing will be produced for a major BEV platform spanning across multiple vehicle brands.
- The Pentatonic solution offers lower cost compared to conventional metal counterparts due to reduced components from high functional integration and a leaner bill of material.
- The thermoplastic composite material helps lower the carbon footprint of the part, while the lightweight housing reduces carbon emissions during driving.
- The multi-material approach provides corrosion resistance without compromising performance throughout the vehicle's lifespan.
- This order marks Kautex’s second Pentatonic business win, following an initial success in January 2023 with a full-BEV underbody protection skid plate that entered serial production in 2024.
- The Pentatonic product line has won the SPE Grand Innovation Award in Europe, as well as the Lingxuan and Gasgoo Innovation Awards in China.
- Kautex has deployed three on-road test vehicles in the US, Japan, and Europe to validate full-BEV and hybrid Pentatonic enclosures across various terrains and weather conditions.
- These test vehicles have collectively been road-tested for over 100,000 miles.
- Kautex operates as a Tier One automotive supplier with 30 plants in 13 countries.
- Kautex is a pioneer in the design and manufacture of traditional and hybrid fuel systems, engine camshafts, and plastic industrial packaging solutions.
- Kautex is expanding its portfolio to offer thermoplastic composite and composite-metal hybrid battery systems, as well as assisted and autonomous drive vehicle cleaning systems.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company leveraging a global network of aircraft, defense, industrial, and finance businesses.
- Textron owns brands including Bell, Cessna, Beechcraft, Pipistrel, Jacobsen, Kautex, Lycoming, E-Z-GO, and Textron Systems.
