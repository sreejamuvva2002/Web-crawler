# Adient Automotive Seating Sdn Bhd

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-06-29

**Source:** [ADIENT MALAYSIA AIMS TO BE SOUTHEAST ASIA AUTOMOTIVE SEATING FOAM HUB - drivingMotion](https://drivingmotion.com/adient-malaysia-aims-to-be-southeast-asia-automotive-seating-foam-hub/)  ·  `page_016602`

## Facts

- Adient Automotive Seating Sdn Bhd is a subsidiary of Adient PLC (NYSE: ADNT), a global leader in automotive seating.
- Its latest foam plant is located in Alor Gajah, Melaka, Malaysia.
- The new RM10 million foam plant has started exporting headrests to Thailand and Indonesia.
- The plant is equipped with state-of-the-art foam manufacturing technology to serve both the local market and for export.
- Adient has been consistently investing in its Malaysian manufacturing operations since 1983 to improve and expand capability and capacity.
- The new Alor Gajah foam plant elevates Adient's operations to become an automotive export hub for the Southeast Asia region.
- Through this initiative, Adient aims to drive parts localisation, develop local suppliers, and create more job opportunities and export revenues for Malaysians and Malaysia.
- AJ Dabydin serves as the Managing Director of Adient.
- The plant's environmental strategy includes a rainwater harvesting system to reduce dependency on town water.
- Solar panels were installed at the plant to produce electricity that powers various pieces of equipment.
- The factory uses five-star power rated and low carbon emission equipment.
- LED lighting is installed across the entire plant.
- Environmental risk is managed through environmental management systems and certifications such as ISO14001.
- The plant implements Hydra Manufacturing System, which is a first in Malaysia.
- Hydra Manufacturing System collects vast amounts of production data from various equipment in real time onsite and remotely.
- Visual analytics are used to improve right first time, reduce product defects and rework, and quickly identify root causes of potential issues from heat maps.
- Adient focused on re-skilling and up-skilling operators for effective man-machine interaction due to the automation approach.
- The plant layout allows for one-meter physical distance between workers and features carefully planned logistic routes to keep them safe.
- The Adient Alor Gajah plant won the Automotive Services category at SBR’s Malaysia International Business Awards 2021.
- Adient thanked the Ministry of International Trade and Investment (MITI), Malaysian Industrial Development Authority (MIDA), local authorities, customers, business partners, and its team for their support.
- Adient plans to host an official opening ceremony of the new plant when circumstances permit.
- Adient operates approximately 77,000 employees in 32 countries.
- Adient operates 202 manufacturing/assembly plants worldwide.
- Adient produces and delivers automotive seating for all major OEMs.
- Adient's expertise spans from research and design to engineering and manufacturing of complete seating systems and individual components.
- Adient's products are integrated into more than 19 million vehicles every year.
- Adient Automotive Seating Sdn. Bhd. was incorporated on 7 September 1983.
- Adient Malaysia currently operates three manufacturing plants, one engineering/prototype/testing lab, and one sequencing centre in the country.
- Adient Malaysia has about 500 employees in the country.
- Adient Malaysia's facilities are IATF 16949:2016, ISO 14001:2015, and ISO 45001:2018 certified.
