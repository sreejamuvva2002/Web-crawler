# U.S. Battery Mfg. Augusta Inc.

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Augusta, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

A Georgia-based battery manufacturer that designs and produces deep-cycle batteries for scissor lifts, golf carts, boats and other equipment. The company was cited by OSHA in September 2023 for lead exposure violations at its Augusta facility.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Produces deep-cycle batteries that can be used in electric vehicles and related equipment, making it a component of Georgia's EV supply chain.
