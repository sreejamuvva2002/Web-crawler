# IMMI

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-02

**Source:** [IMMI and Blue Bird Celebrate Opening of New Manufacturing Plant in Macon, Georgia - Blue Bird Corporation](https://www.blue-bird.com/immi-and-blue-bird-celebrate-opening-of-new-manufacturing-plant-in-macon-georgia/)  ·  `page_009604`

## Facts

- IMMI celebrated the grand opening of its new manufacturing facility in Macon, Georgia on October 2, 2025.
- The new plant spans 75,000 square feet and will support school bus manufacturer Blue Bird Corp.
- IMMI will equip select Blue Bird model buses with industry-first safety upgrades, including standard three-point seat belts for all student passengers and a steering wheel-deployed airbag for drivers.
- The facility opening adds 80 good-paying U.S. manufacturing jobs to middle Georgia.
- Larry Gray, CEO of IMMI, announced the job creation and partnership with Blue Bird.
- Norm Gould III, School Bus President & IMMI CFO, recognized Blue Bird’s commitment to advancing school bus safety.
- IMMI’s 4Front airbag safety system will become standard equipment on Blue Bird’s Vision-model school buses beginning in October 2025.
- Under the SafeGuard brand, the Macon facility manufactures state-of-the-art school bus seats designed to protect millions of children daily.
- Blue Bird was the first school bus manufacturer to announce in 2024 that lap-shoulder belts would be standard safety equipment on its seats.
- John Wyskiel, President and CEO of Blue Bird Corporation, stated that the partnership brings industry-first standard student and driver safety systems to school districts in North America.
- Wyskiel noted that the joint commitment to safety drives investments, manufacturing expansion, and job growth in their home state and beyond.
- The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) and the National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) have called for lap-shoulder belts for over 25 million children who ride school buses annually.
- IMMI expects to add additional jobs to its Macon workforce as demand increases for SafeGuard lap-shoulder belt seats.
- Illinois recently passed legislation mandating lap-shoulder belts on new school buses, joining eight other U.S. states in the requirement.
- IMMI is an employee-owned global leader in safety solutions headquartered in Westfield, Indiana, with nine additional global facilities and nearly sixty years of experience developing innovative safety restraints and systems.
- IMMI’s safety products are utilized on car seats, heavy trucks, school buses, recreational/off-road vehicles, military applications, fire/EMS, motor coaches, and construction vehicles.
- Blue Bird Corporation (NASDAQ: BLBD), founded in 1927, is a technology leader in school bus design, engineering, and manufacturing with a focus on safety, reliability, and durability.
- Blue Bird has sold more than 25,000 propane, natural gas, and electric powered buses and positions itself as the proven leader in low- and zero-emission school buses.
