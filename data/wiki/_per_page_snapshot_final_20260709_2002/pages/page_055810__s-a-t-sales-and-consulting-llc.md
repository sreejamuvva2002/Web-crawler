# S.A.T. Sales and Consulting LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2019-08-05

**Source:** [Automotive Industry Veteran, Siegfried Tigges, Launches New Sales Agency with Speed Autoteile as Charter Client - Aftermarket Intel](https://aftermarketintel.com/siegfried-tigges-launches-new-sales-agency/)  ·  `page_055810`

## Facts

- Siegfried (Siggi) Tigges formed his own sales organization and consultancy called S.A.T. Sales and Consulting LLC.
- The company is based out of Peachtree City, GA.
- S.A.T. Sales will begin by representing Speed Autoteile of Berlin, Germany in the North American market.
- The agency will also serve clients in the Automotive, Heavy Duty, Mining and Industrial markets.
- Tigges spent over 40 years with the Hella organization, a world leader in automotive lighting and electronics.
- Tigges was an integral part of the management team at Hella Automotive Sales, Peachtree City, GA, for the past 28 years.
- While at Hella, Tigges served as Vice President - Canadian Aftermarket Sales, Mining, and Heavy Duty U.S., VP Canadian Sales – Aftermarket, Special OE, Mining, National Sales Manager Canada, and Director of Product Development and Marketing for North America.
- Speed Autoteile is a leading independent exporter of import parts and accessories and will be S.A.T. Sales’ first client.
- Speed Autoteile is a family-owned and operated parts supplier that has served WDs, retailers, jobbers, maintenance shops, and service specialists working on German and European cars since 1999.
- Speed Autoteile has over 1000 customers worldwide and provides an exceptional portfolio of over one million high quality genuine and OEM replacement parts for Audi, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Opel, Porsche, Seat, Smart, and VW vehicles, as well as U.S. Asian Imports and heavy duty vehicles.
- Uwe Kuether is the President of Speed Autoteile.
- Speed Autoteile will exhibit at AAIW in Las Vegas, NV, Nov. 5-7, 2019 at Booth # 4631.
