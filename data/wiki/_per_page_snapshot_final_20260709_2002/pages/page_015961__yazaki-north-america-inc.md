# Yazaki North America, Inc.

## Facts

- Yazaki North America, Inc. (YNA) has entered into a Service Agreement with NEXCO-West USA (NEXCO), a leading provider of advanced road maintenance and inspection technologies in the United States.
- The agreement supports NEXCO’s efforts to enhance road and bridge maintenance through innovative technologies, with a focus on non-destructive evaluation (NDE) solutions.
- YNA aims to lay the groundwork for the potential future deployment of digital road maintenance software developed by Yazaki’s AI & Digital Division in the U.S. market.
- As the first phase of this collaboration, YNA and NEXCO jointly conducted a bridge inspection project in the State of Minnesota in April 2025.
- The bridge inspection project utilized cutting-edge NDE technologies, including Infrared Thermography (IRT) and Ground-Penetrating Rader (GPR), to assess bridge deck conditions.
- This partnership provides YNA with an opportunity to support safer and more efficient infrastructure management in the United States, while laying the groundwork to apply its technologies in overseas markets in the future.
- Yazaki North America is a leading automotive solutions supplier that designs and manufactures electrical distribution systems, components, electronics, and instrumentation solutions that power and connect modern vehicles worldwide.
