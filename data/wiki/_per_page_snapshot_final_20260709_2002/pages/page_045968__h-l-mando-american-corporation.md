## Facts

- The Opelika City Council on Dec. 16 approved a resolution authorizing tax abatements and exemptions for H.L. Mando American Corporation.
- The council voted unanimously to approve the resolution.
- Staff stated the company plans approximately $5,000,000 in new investment.
- The investment package is tied to the creation of roughly 15 new jobs.
- H.L. Mando approached the city a couple weeks prior to the Dec. 16 meeting with a proposal for continued investment around $5,000,000.
- The tax abatements and exemptions are structured as part of an economic development package.
