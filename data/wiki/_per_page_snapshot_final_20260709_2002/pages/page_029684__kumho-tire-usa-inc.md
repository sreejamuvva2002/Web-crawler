# Kumho Tire USA, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-23

**Source:** [Kumho Tire USA, Georgia Department of Veterans Service, and Georgia Veterans Service Foundation Announce Formal Partnership to Support Georgia's Veterans](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/kumho-tire-usa-georgia-department-of-veterans-service-and-georgia-veterans-service-foundation-announce-formal-partnership-to-support-georgias-veterans-302780517.html)  ·  `page_029684`

## Facts

- Kumho Tire USA, Inc. announced the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with the Georgia Veterans Service Foundation and the Georgia Department of Veterans Service to formalize a long-term collaborative partnership supporting Georgia's approximately 680,000 veterans and their families.
- The partnership spans private industry, state government, and nonprofits to identify and deliver programs creating meaningful impact for Georgia's veteran community, with specific initiatives to be announced as the partnership develops.
- The partnership first came into public view earlier this year when GVSF representatives were honored as Co-Captains at Kumho Tire Night at State Farm Arena during an Atlanta Hawks vs. Boston Celtics game.
- Edmund Cho, CEO of Kumho Tire USA, Inc., stated that the company believes doing business in America means giving back to America and looks forward to building something lasting for Georgia's veterans alongside GVSF.
- Dr. Patricia Ross, Commissioner of the Georgia Department of Veterans Service, expressed pride in welcoming Kumho Tire USA as a committed partner to deliver on the promise of unwavering support for Georgia's veterans.
- Joe Higgins, Board Chair of the Georgia Veterans Service Foundation, noted that private sector partnerships strengthen the foundation's work connecting veterans with resources and support.
- Kumho Tire USA is the U.S. subsidiary of Kumho Tire Co., Ltd., a leading global tire manufacturer headquartered in Seoul, South Korea.
- Kumho Tire USA's U.S. operations are headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, where it serves as an active corporate citizen and the official tire sponsor of the Atlanta Hawks.
