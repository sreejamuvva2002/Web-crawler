# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2011-07-21

**Source:** [Personnel News From Pirelli Tire North America - Roadracing World Magazine | Motorcycle Riding, Racing & Tech News](https://www.roadracingworld.com/news/personnel-news-from-pirelli-tire-north-america/)  ·  `page_048438`

## Facts

- Mattia Bussacchini appointed General Manager of Canadian operations for the NAFTA Region.
- Bussacchini has been working closely with logistics and demand planning for Canada and the USA for the past few years.
- Bussacchini will continue to support Pirelli’s factory project in Mexico in his new role as a direct report of Matteo Battaini.
- Matteo Battaini named President and CEO of Pirelli’s NAFTA Region.
- Pirelli Tire North America designs, develops, manufactures and markets tires for passenger vehicles in both the original equipment and replacement markets.
- Pirelli Tire North America markets and distributes tires for motorcycles and motorsports.
- Pirelli operates a facility located in Rome, Georgia.
- Pirelli’s Rome, Georgia facility employs the Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS).
- The Rome, Georgia MIRS facility uses state-of-the-art technology to manufacture tires for both export and domestic markets.
- Rafael Navarro, III appointed to newly formed post of Vice President, Communications and Media Relations at Pirelli Tire North America.
- Navarro responsible for all internal and external communications for Pirelli in the NAFTA region (USA, Canada and Mexico).
- Navarro will integrate public relations and work closely with marketing and motorsport departments across all three country offices to oversee brand activity.
- Navarro continues to manage key influencer relationships in the region.
- Navarro joined Pirelli as Senior Business Development Manager in 2006, where he developed key tuning and influencer relationships and led the company’s celebrity partnership/spokespersons program.
- Navarro signed partnership agreements with automotive designer and TV personality Chip Foose, DUB Magazine publisher and TV personality Myles Kovacs, and American rally driver Ken Block.
- In 2007, Navarro promoted to Director of Media Relations and Motorsport, a role that utilized his 20 years of racing experience to direct the company’s North American Motorsports programs.
- Navarro involved in successful execution of key supply contracts for Grand-Am Rolex Sports Car Racing Series, Pirelli Driver’s Cup, VW TDI Cup, and SCCA Pirelli World Challenge racing series.
- Navarro directed support operations for Ferrari Challenge and Pike’s Peak hill climb racing series.
