## Facts

- MAT HD is a leader in commercial vehicle braking products.
- MAT HD works with heavy duty parts distributors, fleet owners, and OEM's to deliver quality brake part solutions.
- MAT HD was forged out of the MAT Auto Group to provide heavy braking components and solutions to the heavy after market.
- MAT Auto Group is a leading global manufacturer of cast iron braking and transmission, friction, and suspension products for OEM, OES, and aftermarket passenger and commercial vehicle markets.
- The company produces products in 23 locations across 3 continents.
- The company has a workforce of over 10,000.
