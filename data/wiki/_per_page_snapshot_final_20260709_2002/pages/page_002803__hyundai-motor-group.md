## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group signed a 147 MW photovoltaic power purchase agreement (PPA) at its new Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) in Georgia.
- Hyundai Motor Group is establishing an advanced system for recycling end-of-life batteries to support circular economy expansion.
- Hyundai Motor Group introduced a ‘Just Transition’ initiative providing training and development opportunities to help employees navigate the industry's evolution toward electrification and artificial intelligence.
- Hyundai Motor Group is expanding its integrated hydrogen value chain and next-generation electrification strategy.
- The company achieved RE100, whereby 100 percent of electricity comes from renewable sources at all operational sites in Europe, North America and India.
- José Muñoz, President and CEO of Hyundai Motor Company, stated that sustainability is a core part of the company's transformation into a high-technology mobility company.
