# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Novelis Inc. reports strong Q3 FY 2024 results, emphasizes sustainable](https://business-news-today.com/novelis-inc-reports-strong-q3-fy-2024-results-emphasizes-sustainable-growth/)  ·  `page_047381`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is a global leader in sustainable aluminum solutions and aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Steve Fisher serves as President and CEO of Novelis Inc.
- The company reported substantial year-over-year growth in Adjusted EBITDA and Adjusted EBITDA per tonne margin for Q3 FY 2024, attributing the success to its unparalleled global scale, diverse product portfolio, and leadership in recycling.
- Q3 FY 2024 net sales decreased by 6% to $3.9 billion due to lower average aluminum prices.
- Total flat rolled product shipments remained steady at 910 kilotonnes in Q3 FY 2024, matching the previous year.
- Automotive shipments grew during Q3 FY 2024.
- Demand for beverage packaging sheet resurged in Q3 FY 2024.
- Net income improved to $121 million in Q3 FY 2024.
- Adjusted EBITDA increased by 33% to $454 million in Q3 FY 2024.
- Financial improvements were driven by favorable metal benefits from recycling, enhanced pricing strategies, and reduced operating costs.
- Net cash flow from operating activities was $420 million in Q3 FY 2024.
- Adjusted free cash flow showed an outflow of $517 million due to strategic investments in new capacity expansions.
- Fiscal year-to-date capital expenditures amounted to $960 million.
- Novelis Inc. is constructing a state-of-the-art, greenfield rolling and recycling plant in Bay Minette, Alabama.
- The Bay Minette project represents a historic investment of $4.1 billion.
- The facility will be the first of its kind built in the U.S. in nearly four decades.
- The Bay Minette facility is expected to produce 600 kilotonnes of finished goods for the beverage packaging and automotive markets in North America.
