## Facts

- Grand opening of Haering Precision USA LP in Lavonia, Georgia.
- Inauguration events kick off Friday, November 04 with Future Day for local high school students and culminate Saturday, November 05 with official inauguration ceremony and community event from 11am to 6pm.
- Over 60 years ago, Anton Häring founded the precision engineering plant in Bubsheim, Germany, starting with no more than 2 automatic cut-off machines in his parents' barn.
- The company currently employs about 4,400 people at 5 locations on 4 continents.
- The American location is a major milestone for the Häring Group.
- President Dr.-Ing. Jürgen Häring explains the company's future plans and looks forward to the Grand Opening ceremony.
- Around 400 regional students will participate in the Future Day school trip.
- Students compete in a team challenge for a trip to Six Flags Theme Park, answer company questions, and collect points at stations.
- The company aims to be remembered by young recruits as an attractive employer and highlight new opportunities.
- The company cooperates with schools and universities, providing dual training and apprenticeships where students split time between classroom and company.
- A highlight is the Global Rotational Training Program with headquarters in Germany for international experience.
- Modern workplace design with state-of-the-art machinery is cited as an important criterion for a solid career start.
- Events allow students and community to tour the manufacturing facility premises with infopoints spread across the site.
- Community Event activities include a human foosball table, go-kart course, and international food market featuring products from all five continents where Häring has locations.
- Guests can win prizes by visiting infopoints, answering quiz questions, or participating in a social media photo challenge.
- President Dr.-Ing. Jürgen Häring states the ambition to put the production plant independently in the hands of American management personnel.
- The company needs committed local individuals with roots in the region or beyond for professional challenges.
- Miriam Häring, President of the Häring Group, explains the recruitment strategy and training programs.
