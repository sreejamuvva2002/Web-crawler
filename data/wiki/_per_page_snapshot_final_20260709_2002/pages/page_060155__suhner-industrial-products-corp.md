# Suhner Industrial Products Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Suhner Launches New Tool Repair Center](https://bernardandcompany.com/suhner-launches-new-tool-repair-center/)  ·  `page_060155`

## Facts

- Suhner has launched a new North American service center with larger capacity, new equipment, better organization, and more staff.
- The new facility is located immediately next to the existing repair center on the company campus in Rome, Georgia.
- While the actual size is about the same as the previous repair center, the new center offers 40% more workspace due to better storage and materials handling operations.
- The center’s repair area has increased by 50%.
- The facility includes new repair function areas and new repair equipment.
- The North American center features improved training facilities and a test area.
- The center has substantial room for expansion according to company sources.
- Organizationally, the center utilizes shelving for vertical storage with 18’ racking.
- The facility uses industrial cabinets for organized parts storage along with separate and dedicated stock areas.
- Suhner has hired additional staff to provide better customer service to clients.
- Customer service offerings include onsite training in the operations and maintenance of Suhner automated tooling equipment and other products in the line.
- The new factory service center repairs spindles, multiple spindle heads, slides, and live tooling.
- In the near future, Suhner will also service rotary index tables for CNC machines.
- Suhner repairs its own line of abrasive power tools as well as competitive brands of spindles, multi-heads, and live tooling.
