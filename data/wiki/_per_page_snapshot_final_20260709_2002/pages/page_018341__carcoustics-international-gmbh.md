# Carcoustics International GmbH

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Carcoustics - Expert in Acoustics, Thermodynamics and electromagnetic shielding - Carcoustics International GmbH](https://www.carcoustics.com/company/about-carcoustics/)  ·  `page_018341`

## Facts

- Carcoustics specializes in the development and production of acoustic, thermal and electromagnetic insulation solutions for passenger cars and commercial vehicles.
- The company focuses on four major areas of vehicle requirements: Interior Solutions, Powertrain Solutions, Exterior Solutions and New Enery Vehicles.
- Carcoustics is independent from predefined raw materials and works with seven different production technologies: Thermoforming, Aluminumforming, PU Foaming, Die Cutting, Vacuumforming, Injection Molding and Blow Molding.
- The company relies on a multicultural team of employees and managers and promotes individual employee development in all areas.
- Carcoustics maintains a global footprint with production plants, development centers, and an international sales structure in Europe, America, and Asia.
- Proximity to existing and potential new customers enables the company to make the benefits of new innovations available quickly and efficiently worldwide.
- The company's focus is on sustainable and profitable growth in the relevant automotive markets.
- In 1952, the Illbruck Company was founded.
- In 2001, following a change of ownership, Illbruck's automotive division became a separate company called Carcoustics as part of a management buyout.
- In 2005, the company opened its first Slovakian factory in Senec.
- In 2008, Carcoustics expanded its business activities by applying its core competencies to the industrial and truck sectors.
- In 2009, the first Carcoustics facility was opened in Querétaro, Mexico.
- In 2012, the Langfang plant was opened, giving Carcoustics its own manufacturing facility in China.
- In 2013, a second production facility was opened in Novaky, Slovakia.
- In 2014, identical molded polyurethane foam production lines were established in China, Mexico and Slovakia to manufacture products to the same high standard worldwide.
- In 2015, Thermoforming was introduced at the Querétaro plant, expanded through additional machinery capacity and the opening of a new location in Rankweil, Austria.
- In 2016, Dare Group became the new investor.
- In 2017, the Wroclaw plant in Poland was opened, along with a new plant in Buford, USA.
- In 2018, new plants were opened in San Miguel de Allende (Mexico) and Shenyang (China), as well as a Test Center in Shanghai (China).
- In 2021, Carcoustics started its 20th year with a new organizational structure and strategy focused on innovative solutions for customers and sustainable growth in all major automotive markets.
- In 2023, the Innovation & Technology Center in Shanghai was officially opened, involving the relocation and extension of the former Test Center.
