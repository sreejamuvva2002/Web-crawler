## Facts

- F&P Georgia considers itself an integral part of its community.
- The company's mission statement is: "We will be good corporate citizens and add value to the environment and the communities in which we live and work."
- F&P Georgia is a proud company of over 500 associates who are highly involved in many religious, philanthropic, social, and charitable organization.
- For the past five years we have hosted a charity 5K to raise money for many worthy causes.
- We are a proud member of the Rome Floyd Chamber of Commerce as well as being in the Partners in Prosperity program.
- Our team serves on the board of Greater Rome Existing Industrial Association.
- We are so happy to be a key business partner and to have a team member on the board of the Floyd County School's College and Career Academy.
- As we recognize the value of the youth of our community, our associates support all of the local schools from coaching to mentoring.
- As a company we participate in the Youth Apprenticeship Program.
- We have developed a junior tech program to teach the young people of Rome and Floyd county a valuable life trade.
