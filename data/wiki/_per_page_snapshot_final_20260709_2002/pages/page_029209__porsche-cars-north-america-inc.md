## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) has been headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia since 1998.
- PCNA was established in 1984.
- PCNA serves as the exclusive U.S. importer for the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera and Taycan.
- PCNA operates a Porsche Experience Center in Atlanta featuring a module-based 1.6 mile driver development track, business center, and fine dining restaurant, 356.
- PCNA supports 193 independently owned and operated Porsche dealerships in the U.S., including supplying parts, service, marketing, and training.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Porsche boasts some 30,000-plus motorsport wins to date.
