# Mine of the Future – Proving Ground Initiative

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2025-11-14

**Source:** [Funding Notice: Mine of the Future – Proving Ground Initiative | Department of Energy](https://www.energy.gov/cmei/mining/funding-notice-mine-future-proving-ground-initiative)  ·  `page_028880`

## Facts

- NOFO number is DE-FOA-0003390.
- Funding Amount is $80,000,000.
- On November 14, 2025, the U.S. Department of Energy’s Office of Fossil Energy announced up to $80 million in federal funding to establish Mine of the Future proving grounds for real-world testing of next-generation mining technologies.
- The Department announced in August its intent to invest $1 billion to advance and scale mining, processing, and manufacturing technologies.
- These actions will deliver on President Trump’s Executive Orders, Unleashing American Energy and Immediate Measures to Increase American Mineral Production.
- The initiative aims to secure America’s critical material supply chain, increase domestic mineral production, reduce reliance on foreign sources, and strengthen U.S. energy independence.
- Field sites are essential for minerals and mining stakeholders to test innovative technologies under real-world conditions.
- Awards made under this NOFO will develop mining proving grounds and support research and development projects that accelerate technologies past laboratory and/or bench scale to testing and demonstration in a field-scale setting.
- This field-scale testing is an essential step to de-risk commercial adoption of new mining technologies.
- Projects selected under this initiative will create opportunities for hands-on workforce training to prepare the next generation of American miners and engineers to operate advanced mining systems safely and efficiently.
- Eligible applicants include institutions of higher education, for- and non-profit entities, state and local governments, and Indian Tribes.
- The funding opportunity covers one topic area: Development of Mining Technology Proving Grounds & One or Two Accelerated Mine Technology R&D Projects.
- DOE expects to award up to $80 million in federal funding with a minimum of 20% cost-sharing from the awardees.
- NOI Issue Date is 09/26/2025.
- Funding Opportunity Issue Date is 11/14/2025.
- Submission Deadline for Full Applications is 1/30/2026 at 5:00 PM ET.
- Expected Date for Selection Notifications is February 2026.
- Programs will advance innovative mining processes and technologies to help revitalize the domestic mining landscape and enhance U.S. mineral security.
- The U.S. Department of Energy announced $355 million for two notices of funding opportunities to expand domestic production of critical materials essential for advancing U.S. energy production, manufacturing, transportation and national defense.
