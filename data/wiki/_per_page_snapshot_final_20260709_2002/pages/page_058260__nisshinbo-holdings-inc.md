# Nisshinbo Holdings Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-30

**Source:** [Nisshinbo Holdings Inc. - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/nisshinbo-holdings?&sitesearchKey=日産)  ·  `page_058260`

## Facts

- Nisshinbo Holdings Inc. is a global manufacturer with a focus on wireless, communications, microdevices, and other electronics businesses.
- The company's business is divided into seven segments: wireless communications, microdevices, brakes, precision instruments, chemicals, textiles, and real estate.
- Nisshinbo announced it will transfer all outstanding shares of TMD Friction Group S.A. (TMD) and loan receivables against TMD and its subsidiary TMD Friction Holdings GmbH to German company AEQUITA SE & Co. KGaA.
- The TMD transfer transaction includes four subsidiaries: TMD Friction Holdings (UK) Limited, TMD Friction UK Limited, TMD Friction do Brasil S.A., and TMD Friction Mexico S.A. de C.V.
- The date of the TMD transfer transaction is scheduled to be November 30, 2023.
- Nisshinbo is listed on the Prime Market of the Tokyo Stock Exchange as of Dec. 31, 2024.
- The Master Trust Bank of Japan holds a 14.91% investment ratio in Nisshinbo.
- Fukoku Mutual Life Insurance holds a 5.73% investment ratio in Nisshinbo.
- Custody Bank of Japan holds a 4.52% investment ratio in Nisshinbo.
- STATE STREET BANK AND TRUST COMPANY holds a 3.76% investment ratio in Nisshinbo.
- Teijin Ltd. holds a 2.01% investment ratio in Nisshinbo.
- Shikoku Kasei Holdings Corporation holds a 1.65% investment ratio in Nisshinbo.
- The Nisshinbo Group Employee Stock Ownership Association holds a 1.48% investment ratio in Nisshinbo.
- The Japan Wool Textile Co., Ltd. holds a 1.45% investment ratio in Nisshinbo.
- DFA INTL SMALL CAP VALUE PORTFOLIO holds a 1.33% investment ratio in Nisshinbo.
- The Nisshinbo Business Partners Stock Ownership Association holds a 1.13% investment ratio in Nisshinbo.
- The wireless and communications segment produces communication systems for ADAS, ETC, locators, and GNSS receivers.
- The microdevices segment produces electronic devices for car audio, power control units, EPS, BMS, and parking assist.
- The brakes segment produces friction materials for brakes.
- The precision equipment segment produces EBS valve blocks and molded products for automotive use.
- The chemicals segment produces fuel cell separators.
- The wireless and communications segment produces high power transformers, reactors, and choke coils as electronics parts for automotive.
- These automotive electronics parts are applied to DC-DC converters for 12V output, electric inverters for compressors, EV battery chargers, electric power steering, on-board chargers, and DC/AC inverters.
- The wireless and communications segment also produces GPS modules.
- The microdevice segment produces ICs and operational amps for automobiles applied to PCU, EPS, BCM, safety/clash avoidance (ADAS), parking assist, entertainment/car navigation/audio, BMS, LED Headlight ECU, V2X, TFT LCD, corner sensor ECU, and center console.
- The microdevice segment produces power management ICs for automotive including LDO regulators, watchdog timers, LED controllers, reset ICs, DC-DC converters, and USB switches.
- The automobile brakes segment produces brake linings, disc pads, drum brakes, and drum brakes for medium- and heavy-duty trucks.
- The automobile brakes segment produces brake components including brake master cylinders, proportioning valves, disc brake calipers, wheel cylinders, and joint brackets.
- The precision instruments segment produces molding products such as cross flow fans, Sirocco fans, propeller fans, interior and exterior decorative parts, seat plastic parts, luggage trim, back door trim, pillar garnish, front grill, and wire harness.
- The precision instruments segment produces lighting and functional parts including thick wall light guide side turn lenses, lamp outer/inner lenses, lamp LED light guide parts, lamp housings, door mirror power units, meter dial plates, meter panels, connector parts, and HV high-voltage cable protectors.
- The precision instruments segment produces precision parts for automobiles including EBS valve blocks, housing for diesel fuel-injection management pumps, cylinders for diesel fuel-injection management pumps, EGR valves, air control valves, supercharged air control valves, exhaust brake valves, intake-air shutter valves, exhaust shutter valves, actuators, check/delay valves, clutch master cylinders, clutch release cylinders, filler caps, fuel cut valves, hoses with check valves, and control valves for automotive steering systems.
- The chemicals segment produces bipolar plates for fuel cells.
- Nisshinbo Automotive Corporation was established in the U.S.A. in Feb. 1995.
- Nisshinbo Automotive Manufacturing Inc. was established in the U.S.A. in Mar. 1997.
- In Dec. 2023, Nisshinbo acquired all shares of HVJ Holdings, Inc., making Hitachi Kokusai Electric Inc. a subsidiary as well.
