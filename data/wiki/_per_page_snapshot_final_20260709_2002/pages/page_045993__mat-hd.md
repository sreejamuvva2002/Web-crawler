# MAT HD

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- MAT HD is a subsidiary of MAT Holdings, Inc.
- MAT HD announced that it has acquired certain assets of GBC Inc., a manufacturer of brake shoes and other automotive components.
- The acquisition brings the Gorilla brand and product line into the MAT family.
- The acquisition complements MAT HD's existing portfolio of product lines.
- The acquisition adds geographic presence that will enable serving customers as a one-source provider for all their automotive needs.
- MAT HD provides standard OE position or fleet specified air disc pads and lining for several recognized names in the US.
- All lining and air disc friction, including Severe and Heavy Duty applications, are rated N to meet copper-free legislation.
- MAT HD utilizes multiple distribution points to be proactive in today's dynamic supply chain environment.
- MAT HD brings a systems design approach to its complete range of braking components.
- MAT HD works closely with OE manufacturers to deliver solutions to each customer's unique platform.
- MAT HD's expertise in logistics ensures the right product is at the right place at the right time.
