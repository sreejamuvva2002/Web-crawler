# Mercedes-Benz North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-05

**Source:** [Mercedes-Benz Establishing North American Headquarters, New Research & Development Hub in Metro Atlanta - Middle Georgia CEO](https://middlegeorgiaceo.com/features/2025/05/mercedes-benz-establishing-north-american-headquarters-new-research-development-hub-metro-atlanta/)  ·  `page_046267`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Mercedes-Benz will establish Atlanta as its headquarters in North America by centralizing and uniting key corporate functions.
- The company will move up to 500 jobs to its existing facility known as “1MB” in Fulton County.
- Mercedes-Benz will make a multi-million dollar investment in a future state-of-the-art Research & Development (R&D) facility located near the 1MB site.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia continues to lead in the future of mobility and technical innovation by attracting world-class companies like Mercedes-Benz.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that Mercedes-Benz, which already has close ties to Georgia, is doubling down on its presence in the state.
- Mercedes-Benz opened its “1MB” facility in Sandy Springs in 2018.
- The existing “1MB” facility currently supports approximately 800 jobs in Georgia.
- Jason Hoff, CEO of Mercedes-Benz North America, thanked the State of Georgia for supporting the deepening of the company's roots in the Atlanta area.
- Jason Hoff stated that the expansion strengthens the company's position for continued growth and reinforces its commitment to the U.S. market.
- Jason Hoff noted that consolidating teams will enable greater agility, faster speed to market, and improved customer experience.
- The “1MB” facility in Sandy Springs will house existing sales teams, financial services teams, and corporate functions.
- The new state-of-the-art Research & Development hub will be located near Sandy Springs.
- Mercedes-Benz anticipates completing the move to metro Atlanta by August 2026.
- Mayor Rusty Paul of Sandy Springs expressed excitement over Mercedes-Benz's expansion in the city.
- Mayor Rusty Paul noted that since establishing their headquarters in Sandy Springs in 2018, Mercedes-Benz has been an outstanding corporate partner.
- Mayor Rusty Paul stated that the expansion highlights the success of Sandy Springs' recent infrastructure and capital investments.
- Mayor Rusty Paul described the expansion as a wonderful opportunity and significant milestone for the city's continued development.
- Chairman Robb Pitts of the Fulton County Board of Commissioners stated that Mercedes-Benz's investment and growth reaffirm the strength and vitality of the community.
- Chairman Robb Pitts noted that the expansion proves Fulton County remains a destination for corporate solutions with an accessible, vibrant, and growing community.
- Katie Kirkpatrick, President & CEO of the Metro Atlanta Chamber, stated that the expansion testifies to Mercedes-Benz's commitment to excellence and metro Atlanta's strength as an innovation and talent hub.
- Katie Kirkpatrick noted that when the 1MB facility opened in 2018, it quickly became an integral part of the local business landscape, driving economic growth and elevating the region's global presence.
- Katie Kirkpatrick expressed pride in seeing the partnership deepen as Mercedes-Benz continues to invest in the region's future success.
- Assistant Director of Statewide Projects Elizabeth McLean represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team on the project.
- The GDEcD partnered with the City of Sandy Springs, Select Fulton, Metro Atlanta Chamber, and Georgia Power for this competitive project.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson noted that since relocating Mercedes-Benz USA to Sandy Springs in 2018, the company has become an integral part of the local business community.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that Mercedes-Benz's continued growth and community involvement exemplify why the state recruits industry leaders like them.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that the expansion and R&D commitment will strengthen the company's long-term success and highlight talent and partnerships fostered by the University System of Georgia.
