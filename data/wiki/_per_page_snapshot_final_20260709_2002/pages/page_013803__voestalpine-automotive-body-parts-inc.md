# voestalpine Automotive Body Parts Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Voestalpine Automotive Body Parts Invests $50 Million For Expansion Of Manufacturing Complex In Bart](https://www.automotive-technology.com/projects/voestalpine-automotive-body-parts-invests-for-expansion)  ·  `page_013803`

## Facts

- Germany-based voestalpine Automotive Body Parts Inc. will expand its existing operation in Bartow County, Georgia.
- The expansion will create 150 jobs.
- The company is investing $50 million in the local community.
- Voestalpine Automotive Body Parts Inc. produces ultra-high strength body-in-white components for premium car manufacturers.
- Voestalpine Automotive Body Parts Inc. is a subsidiary company of the Metal Forming Division of the German-based voestalpine Group.
- The Metal Forming Division produces highly refined sections, tubes and precision strip steel products.
- The Metal Forming Division also produces ready-to-install components made of pressed, stamped and roll formed parts.
- The newly created jobs will range from advanced manufacturing to hot-forming and assembly positions.
- Gov. Nathan Deal stated that voestalpine’s decision to expand operations in Bartow County strengthens Georgia’s growing automotive industry and highlights the state’s extensive manufacturing capabilities.
