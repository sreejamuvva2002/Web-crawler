# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Bonnell Aluminum unifies cross-departmental data with Oracle Analytics](https://www.oracle.com/tr/customers/bonnell-aluminum/)  ·  `page_053056`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum aims for complete supply chain visibility to drive strategic decisions across all departments with Oracle Fusion Analytics.
- The company is a United States-based manufacturer of custom fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions in the Industrial Products sector.
- Bonnell sought to connect complex data across HR, finance, and operations.
- Previous disparate systems prevented management from having inventory visibility across its five manufacturing plants.
- Plant managers previously had to combine onsite data using spreadsheets with a homegrown ERP reporting system.
- This reliance on disparate systems led to inconsistent data and decisions across locations.
- 80% of Bonnell's focus is in custom manufacturing.
- The company places high value on delivering products on time to customers.
- Poor shipment visibility and delays caused by global material shortages made it difficult to keep up with growing customer expectations.
- Michael Brown, Vice President of Information Technology at Bonnell Aluminum, stated: 'It was difficult to get a unified view of operations connecting complex data across multiple spreadsheets.'
- Achieving visibility across finance, sales, and supply chain data was key to the company's mission of gaining full control over operations.
- Getting this cross-departmental view was central to Bonnell choosing Oracle Fusion Cloud ERP and Fusion ERP Analytics as its preferred solution.
- The company was impressed with Oracle’s continued commitment to innovation.
- Bonnell decided to deploy Fusion Analytics across finance and HR departments in all locations simultaneously to improve decision-making.
- Rolling out Oracle Fusion SCM Analytics is next on the list to complete the vision of full data integration across all plants.
- With a single view of data across all locations, Bonnell Aluminum optimizes purchasing and makes timely inventory decisions based on what customers need.
- Oracle Fusion Analytics provides all stakeholders, from plant management to key executives, with visibility into inventory, production floor, and back office operations with the click of a button.
- Empowered with enhanced analysis of its Fusion Cloud data across ERP, HCM, and SCM, the company can identify problem areas in production that contribute to process inefficiencies and delayed orders.
- Managers can better optimize labor across sites and direct spending based on real-time insights.
- Beyond shop-floor analysis, Fusion Analytics helps Bonnell connect cross-departmental data, allowing finance and procurement teams to find savings with suppliers and negotiate better prices during sourcing.
- Fusion Analytics will also help drive cultural change across the organization with a more data-driven approach.
- Empowering every user with key insights is critical to improving processes and increasing collaboration across functions.
- Bonnell Aluminum has over 65 years in the industry.
- Its production facilities serve many of North America’s largest and most respected manufacturing companies through design solutions and unmatched extrusion capabilities and services.
