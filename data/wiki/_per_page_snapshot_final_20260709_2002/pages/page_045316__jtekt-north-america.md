# JTEKT North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-10

**Source:** [Ransomware gang says it hacked BMW and Tesla parts maker JTEKT - Comparitech](https://www.comparitech.com/news/ransomware-gang-says-it-hacked-bmw-and-tesla-parts-maker-jtekt/)  ·  `page_045316`

## Facts

- Ransomware group BlackSuit claimed responsibility for an October 2024 data breach at JTEKT North America.
- JTEKT North America is an automotive manufacturing corporation whose customers include auto makers like BMW, Tesla, and Rivian.
- BlackSuit claims to have stolen 894 GB of data from JTEKT North America.
- JTEKT North America is the US arm of the Japanese company JTEKT.
- JTEKT has not verified BlackSuit’s claim regarding the data theft.
- JTEKT reported that a third party gained unauthorized access to its network on October 10, 2024.
- JTEKT disconnected the affected devices following the unauthorized access.
- A second report from JTEKT confirmed customer information stored on its servers was compromised.
- JTEKT stated that unauthorized access by a third party to the network of its US group company may have led to the leakage of customer information stored on the server.
- A third report from JTEKT confirmed unauthorized access to systems in North America and South America.
- JTEKT disconnected systems in the Czech Republic and Germany but did not confirm those locations were compromised.
- JTEKT did not provide details about the compromised customer data.
- It is unknown whether JTEKT paid a ransom, how much BlackSuit demanded, or how attackers breached the network.
- JTEKT is a Japanese automotive parts manufacturing corporation owned by Toyota Group.
- JTEKT makes machine tools and components like bearings and gears for auto makers like BMW, GM, Toyota, Volkswagen, Nissan, Tesla, Caterpillar, and Rivian.
- JTEKT North America is a separate legal entity that reports to the CEO in Japan.
