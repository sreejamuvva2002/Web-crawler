# Porsche Cars North America Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2014-09-10

**Source:** [Porsche Sees Strong Growth in U.S. and Abroad - Global Atlanta](https://www.globalatlanta.com/porsche-sees-strong-growth-in-u-s-and-abroad/)  ·  `page_048899`

## Facts

- Atlanta-based Porsche Cars North America Inc. has found a nearly 12 percent increase in U.S. sales during the January-August period compared to the same time last year.
- PCNA’s German parent company, Porsche AG, noted a jump in worldwide sales of about 30 percent for August alone.
- Bernhard Maier, member of the executive board of management for Porsche AG, said that despite the company facing “uncertainties” in several European markets, it has achieved “strong growth worldwide” over the past year.
- The sports car maker announced major sales growth for 2014 in China and Europe as well, which join the U.S. as the company’s top markets.
- Detlev von Platen, president and CEO of Porsche Cars North America, said in a news release: “It is gratifying to see our iconic sports car continue to be a strong performer.”
- Established in 1984, Porsche Cars North America imports and distributes cars for 189 dealers.
