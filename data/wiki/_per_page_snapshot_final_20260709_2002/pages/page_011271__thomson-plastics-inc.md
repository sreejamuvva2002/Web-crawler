# Thomson Plastics Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2015-03-19

**Source:** [Georgia company to open Davidson County plant](https://www.wxii12.com/article/georgia-company-to-open-davidson-county-plant/2058741)  ·  `page_011271`

## Facts

- Thomson Plastics Inc., a Georgia company based in Thomson, Ga., will locate in a Davidson County plant.
- The facility expansion will create about 50 local jobs.
- The project involves a $2.5 million investment in Davidson County.
- The new plant will occupy 106,200 square feet.
- Steve Googe, executive director of the Davidson County Economic Development Commission, welcomed the expansion and stated it serves as an endorsement and commitment that Davidson County and the Triad region's growing manufacturing sector is on the comeback.
