# Continental AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-22

**Source:** [Aruna Anand Appointed President & CEO of Continental Automotive North America](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/aruna-anand-appointed-president-ceo-of-continental-automotive-north-america/)  ·  `page_018736`

## Facts

- Aruna Anand has been named the new president and CEO of Continental’s Automotive group sector in North America.
- Anand will assume responsibility as president and CEO effective Dec. 1.
- She will retain her current role as head of the Architecture and Networking business area for the North American region of Continental Automotive.
- In this role, Anand is responsible for all global architecture and networking business activities that support customers based out of North America.
- Anand joined the company in 1997 and has held numerous leadership positions across different business areas of the Continental Automotive group sector.
- She previously served as head of Software for Gasoline Engine and Transmissions Systems for the Electronic Controls business unit within the company’s then Powertrain division.
- She also led the Wireless Product Group Engineering within the Connected Car Networking business unit of Continental’s Vehicle Networking and Information business area.
- Prior to her current appointment, Anand was responsible for leading Continental Engineering Services, an independently operating business unit that offers comprehensive engineering services.
- Nikolai Setzer, CEO of Continental AG, stated that Anand brings an outstanding combination of experience and talent to the President and CEO Automotive North America position.
- Setzer described Anand as a respected leader and technical expert in areas that are the focus of Continental's future growth.
- Setzer noted that Anand’s dedication to customers and employees will help guide the success of Continental in the important North American market.
- Anand earned her master’s degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Oakland University.
- She earned a bachelor’s degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Anna University in Chennai, India.
- In 2020, Anand was recognized by Automotive News as one of the 100 Leading Women in the North American Automotive industry.
- In 2018, Anand was honored with the prestigious Automotive News Rising Star award.
- She was recently recognized at the Women of Color Awards Gala and received the Professional Achievement in Industry award.
- Anand stated that it is an honor to be selected as Automotive President and CEO for North America and looks forward to leading her team toward a bright future and providing customers with leading technological solutions.
