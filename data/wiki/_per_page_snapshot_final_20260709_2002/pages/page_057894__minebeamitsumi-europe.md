# MinebeaMitsumi Europe

## Facts

- MinebeaMitsumi Sales Europe GmbH, Minebea Slovakia s.r.o., Minebea Intec GmbH, myonic GmbH, Paradox Engineering SA, CEROBEAR GmbH, S.A.S. Mach Aero, NMB-Minebea U.K. Ltd., U-Shin Ltd., RO-RA Aviation Systems GmbH, Mast Kunststoffe GmbH, and MinebeaMitsumi Technology Center Europe GmbH form a strong European Network.
- The network locations with various core competencies form a strong partner for the development of applications in the automotive sector, industry, health care, aerospace, or medical technology.
- Central sales office MinebeaMitsumi Sales Europe GmbH is located close to Frankfurt in Neu-Isenburg.
- The central sales office's available product portfolio covers all MinebeaMitsumi products.
- Minebea Slovakia s.r.o. in Košice in eastern Slovakia is the newest and largest European production facility in the MinebeaMitsumi Group.
- The Slovakian facility spans 20,500 square meters and will have up to 70 production lines in operation by 2022.
- The Slovakian facility primarily manufactures drive systems for the automotive sector and industry.
- The largest motor development center of the MinebeaMitsumi Group is located in Villingen-Schwenningen.
- More than 500 employees at the Villingen-Schwenningen center develop innovative drive technologies, including electronically commutated brushless DC motors and energy harvesting systems.
- Minebea Intec GmbH is a leading manufacturer of industrial weighing and inspection technologies.
- Minebea Intec GmbH has its headquarters in Hamburg, Germany, and has offered products and services for over 150 years.
- Minebea Intec's product portfolio includes platform scales, load cells, vessel and silo scales, X-Ray inspection systems, and user-friendly software solutions.
- myonic GmbH is an international industrial company with branch offices in Germany, the Czech Republic, and Austria.
- myonic has over 80 years of experience manufacturing precision miniature ball bearings for advanced technologies in the medical and automotive industries, precision engineering, mechanical engineering, and aerospace technology.
- Paradox Engineering was established in 2005, is headquartered in Switzerland, and designs pioneering technologies to implement unique, interoperable, and highly scalable network infrastructures for industrial and urban applications.
- CEROBEAR is the leading manufacturer of ceramic roller bearings and a pioneer in roller bearing technology.
- Ceramic roller bearings are a key product for industries and processes with extreme requirements, such as the semiconductor and aerospace industries, as well as motor sports.
- Mach Aero started its career as a grinding specialist mainly for the automotive industry.
- Mach Aero is now a leading company for the manufacture of spherical bearings and rod ends, and proposes high-precision machined parts and sub-assemblies mainly for the aerospace industry.
- NMB-Minebea U.K., located in Lincoln, is the world leader in the design and manufacture of bearing technologies in sectors where precision, speed, and reliability are essential.
- NMB-Minebea U.K.'s core competence includes landing gear bearings, aerospace bearings, and special vehicle suspension bearings for customers in Europe as well as overseas.
- Minebea AccessSolutions Inc. supplies the automotive industry worldwide with high-quality and innovative access and control systems that ensure increased safety while maintaining comfort.
- Minebea AccessSolutions was founded in 1926 and has a long-standing tradition among Tier-1 automotive suppliers.
- Minebea AccessSolutions Deutschland GmbH is located in Erdweg near Munich and serves as a center of competence in the fields of mechatronics and access systems.
