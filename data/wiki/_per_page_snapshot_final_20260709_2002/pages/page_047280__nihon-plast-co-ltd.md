# Nihon Plast Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-31

**Source:** [Nihon Plast Co., Ltd. - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/nihon-plast/)  ·  `page_047280`

## Facts

- The Company is a manufacturer of automotive safety parts and plastic parts.
- The Company’s main products are airbags, steering wheels, and plastic products such as instrument panel parts and air ducts.
- Plastic products accounted for 48.2% of sales in FY ended Mar. 31, 2025.
- Airbags accounted for 25.6% of sales in FY ended Mar. 31, 2025.
- Steering wheels accounted for 26.2% of sales in FY ended Mar. 31, 2025.
- The Company produces safety parts including steering wheels and airbag modules (passenger-side, side, side curtain, and knee).
- The Company produces instrument panel parts including ventilators, glove boxes, console boxes, cup holders, column covers, air conditioner ducts, and shift knobs.
- The Company produces interior trim parts including pillar garnishes.
- The Company produces exterior trim parts including cover cowl tops, fender protectors, and wheel resonators.
- The Company produces aero parts including air spoilers and front under covers.
- The Company manufactures auxiliary parts for batteries.
- In April 2000, the company established Neaton Rome Incorporated in Georgia, U.S.A., with Neaton Auto Products holding 100% of the shares.
- In July 2005, Honda Motor Co., Ltd. made a 20.6% investment in the Company, making it a Honda affiliate.
- In 2013, the company ended its capital relationship with Honda.
- The company is listed on the Standard Market of the Tokyo Stock Exchange as of Mar. 31, 2025.
