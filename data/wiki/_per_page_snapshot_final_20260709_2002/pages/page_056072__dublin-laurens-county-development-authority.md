# Dublin-Laurens County Development Authority

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2015-02-26

**Source:** [News Archive | Dublin-Laurens County Development Authority](https://dlcda.com/about-us/news/P30)  ·  `page_056072`

## Facts

- The Dublin-Laurens County Development Authority (DLCDA) acquired a 331-acre strategic rail-served industrial tract at the intersection of GA Hwy 257 and US Hwy 441 Bypass.
- The land acquisition was completed after due diligence and long-term planning to provide the community with a site for potential industrial clients.
- AWEBA Group, one of the largest and most advanced die-making enterprises in Europe, will establish its first North American headquarters in Dublin.
- AWEBA Group's new facility will create 52 jobs in Laurens County.
- AWEBA Group plans to invest $6 million into the die-making facility over the next five years.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) announced the AWEBA Group investment on December 31, 2014.
