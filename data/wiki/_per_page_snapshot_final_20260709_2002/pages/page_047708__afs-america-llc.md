# AFS America LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2012-07-24

**Source:** [Automotive supplier AFS America to bring new jobs to Columbus](https://www.wtvm.com/story/19092106/automotive-supplier-afs-america-announces-columbus-georgia-manufacturing-operation/)  ·  `page_047708`

## Facts

- Korean automotive parts supplier AFS America LLC plans to open a manufacturing facility and warehouse in Columbus, Georgia.
- AFS America LLC is a subsidiary of parent company NVH Korea Co., Ltd.
- The company will create 65 new jobs at the new facility.
- The company will invest more than $3 million in the facility.
- AFS America LLC will produce automobile floor mats and cargo trays at the Columbus, Georgia facility.
- The company is a key supplier for Hyundai Mobis, Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia (KMMG), Hyundai Motors Manufacturing Alabama (HMMA), and General Motors.
- The new facility is expected to begin operations by the fourth quarter of 2012.
- The facility is expected to be fully operational by 2013.
- Jakyum Koo, Chairman of NVH Korea, stated they are excited to start U.S. operations and take advantage of the highly skilled workforce in the Columbus, Georgia region.
- Jakyum Koo expressed gratitude for the support of the Greater Columbus Chamber of Commerce.
- Jakyum Koo stated their goal is to create meaningful opportunities in the community and better serve customers in the USA.
- Jay Alexander, Chairman of the Greater Columbus Georgia Chamber of Commerce, praised the total team effort behind the announcement.
- Jay Alexander noted that the Chamber's private sector focus combined with public sector support from the Development Authority of Columbus, Columbus Consolidated Government, and the state of Georgia made the new jobs and capital investment possible.
- Dick Ellis, chairman of the Development Authority of Columbus, Georgia, stated that recruiting automotive supplier companies remains a targeted industry for them.
- Dick Ellis noted they have been working with leadership at NVH Korea since November and are pleased the company decided to locate its first U.S. manufacturing facility in Columbus.
- Dick Ellis stated their economic development team is focused on assisting AFS America with setting up the facility, hiring, training, and getting them in business quickly.
- AFS America's Columbus facility will be located in the Corporate Ridge Business Park.
- The location provides strategic access to key customers in Georgia and the Southeast.
- The company will benefit from highly-skilled talent in the Columbus Region trained for manufacturing operations.
- The company will receive operations-specific workforce training from Georgia Quick Start, described as the nation's top-ranked workforce training program.
- The Greater Columbus Georgia Chamber of Commerce, Development Authority of Columbus, and The Valley Partnership partnered with the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) to secure this business opportunity.
- Brian Sillitto, Chamber Senior Vice President of Economic Development, is managing the project for Columbus.
- Yoonie Kim, GDEcD statewide Project Manager, facilitated the project for the state.
- Job applications will be accepted at the Georgia Department of Labor, Columbus Career Center located at 700 Veterans Parkway, Monday through Friday from 8:00 am until 4:30 pm.
- AFS America LLC is a manufacturing corporation for automobile floor mats and cargo trays.
- Parent company NVH Korea is the number one supplier of automotive interior parts in Korea.
- NVH Korea has 1,754 employees globally and facilities in India, China, and Russia.
- NVH Korea's revenue in 2011 was $590 million globally.
