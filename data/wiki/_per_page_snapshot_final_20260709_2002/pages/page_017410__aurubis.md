## Facts

- Aurubis approved investment projects include the second module of its Aurubis Richmond recycling plant in the US state of Georgia.
- The company is intensively exploring options for recycling end-of-life batteries to recover lithium on an industrial scale.
- One and a half years of testing in Aurubis's pilot plant confirmed that its patented process for recovering metals from black mass works.
- The process recovers around 95% of the metals contained in black mass.
- Extracted from used batteries are lithium, nickel, cobalt, manganese, and graphite, which are strategically key elements for electric vehicles.
- Aurubis will build on the pilot plant success with a demo plant set for commissioning in spring 2024.
- The demo plant represents a decisive step towards the industrial application of the battery recycling process.
- Roland Harings serves as CEO of Aurubis.
- Dr. Heiko Arnold serves as COO of Aurubis.
- Inge Hofkens serves as COO of Aurubis.
- Rainer Verhoeven serves as CFO of Aurubis.
- Aurubis aims to achieve carbon-neutral operations well before 2050.
- Life cycle assessment results show that Aurubis has cut its carbon footprint for copper cathodes by more than 50% below the global average.
