# YKK AP America Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2022-10-06

**Source:** [YKK AP America Breaks Ground on New Residential | YKK AP](https://www.ykkap.com/ykk-ap-america-breaks-ground-on-new-residential-manufacturing-facility-in-macon-ga/)  ·  `page_016080`

## Facts

- On Oct. 4, 2022, YKK AP America Inc. officially broke ground on a new residential window and door manufacturing facility in Macon-Bibb County, Ga.
- The company announced plans to invest $125 million into the middle Georgia region for the new residential manufacturing facility and additional machinery.
- Construction will begin immediately with plans to open the facility in January 2024.
- YKK AP America Inc. is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, and manufactures building products at production facilities in Dublin, Ga., Macon, Ga., and Lakeshore, Ontario.
- Through its main brand and subsidiary Erie Architectural Products (Erie AP), the company serves architects, glazing contractors, homebuilders, and resellers across the country.
- YKK AP America Inc. is a subsidiary of Tokyo-based global architectural products company YKK AP Inc.
- Stepe stated that the groundbreaking represents YKK AP America’s “Cycle of Goodness” philosophy and noted that an existing large workforce was a critical imperative for choosing Macon.
- Miller highlighted the company's 30-year partnership with the Macon-Bibb community, emphasizing investments in training, new skills for the workforce, and commitment to the state.
