# General Motors Company

## Facts

- General Motors expects massive growth from OnStar and Super Cruise subscriptions.
- Last year, GM logged $2.7 billion in realized revenue and $5.4 billion in deferred revenue from OnStar and Super Cruise subscriptions.
- This represents healthy growth from $1.7 billion realized and only $200 million deferred as recently as 2020.
- Management expects those software services to generate $3.1 billion in realized revenue and $7.5 billion in deferred revenue this year.
- GM's connected business could approach 70% gross margin.
- CFO Paul Jacobson stated that these software-like margins can drive, and potentially over time, dwarf even the wholesale business.
- Beginning with the 2025 model year, every new GM vehicle that rolls off the production line includes an eight-year basic OnStar subscription.
- Vehicles with Super Cruise will have a three-year subscription built into the price.
- At least 30% of the 35,000 GM drivers with an expiring three-year Super Cruise subscription renewed in 2025.
