# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-11-04

**Source:** [Novelis Reports Second Quarter Fiscal Year 2026 Results](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/novelis-reports-second-quarter-fiscal-year-2026-results-302603929.html)  ·  `page_028891`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is constructing a state-of-the-art greenfield rolling and recycling plant in Bay Minette, Alabama.
- The company describes the Bay Minette facility as a significant U.S. investment intended to bring needed capacity to an undersupplied domestic market.
- Total capital expenditures reached $913 million for the first six months of fiscal year 2026, primarily driven by strategic investments in new rolling and recycling capacity under construction, most notably the Bay Minette plant.
- Start-up costs related to the construction of the Bay Minette rolling and recycling plant are included in Selling, general and administrative expenses.
- Demand for infinitely recyclable, lightweight aluminum continues to grow as a fundamental material in modern transportation, according to CEO Steve Fisher.
- Automotive shipments saw slightly higher volumes in the second quarter of fiscal year 2026, contributing to total rolled product shipments of 941 kilotonnes.
- Novelis supplies aluminum products and solutions to customers in the automotive, aerospace, beverage packaging, and specialties industries across North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- Novelis Inc. is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited, the metals flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group.
