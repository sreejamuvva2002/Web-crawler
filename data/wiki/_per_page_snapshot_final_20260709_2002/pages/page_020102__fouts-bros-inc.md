# Fouts Bros Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Fouts Defense announced that it has been awarded contracts through the Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) to supply a total of 14 FB-94 fire apparatus to support U.S. military operations.
- The contracts include eight units designated for the U.S. Army and six units for the U.S. Navy, with deliveries scheduled to various locations across the United States.
- The FB-94 is a flagship model within Fouts Fire’s line of dependable, standardized apparatus designed for rapid deployment, ease of operation, and long-term reliability.
- These units are built to support critical response needs while maintaining the efficiency and value Fouts Fire is known for.
- Scott Edens, president and CEO, Fouts Bros Inc., says the company is honored to be entrusted with supporting the U.S. Army and U.S. Navy.
- Edens states the FB-94 was designed with mission readiness in mind, delivering reliability, standardized operation, and long-term maintainability.
- Edens notes the awards reflect confidence placed in their team, manufacturing discipline, and ability to deliver dependable fire apparatus on time and without compromise.
- Fouts Bros Inc. is a family-owned company since 1952.
- The company continues to focus on providing high-quality, affordable fire apparatus with industry-leading delivery times.
- The company’s standardized manufacturing approach allows for consistent quality while meeting the evolving needs of departments and organizations nationwide.
