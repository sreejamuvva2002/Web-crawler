# Lyle Industries' Molded Products Division

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-02-01

**Source:** [SALE OF LYLE INDUSTRIES' MOLDED PRODUCTS DIVISION](https://www.angleadvisors.com/post/sale-of-lyle-industries-molded-products-division)  ·  `page_057376`

## Facts

- Techniplas LLC has acquired the Molded Products Division of Lyle Industries Inc.
- The acquisition was announced on December 2, 2021, in Birmingham, Michigan.
- Angle Advisors acted as the exclusive investment banking advisor to Lyle Industries for the transaction.
- The Molded Products Division is based in Dalton, Georgia.
- The division operates as a full-service Tier I supplier of premium cargo systems, carpeted trim and molded floors for luxury sport utility vehicles and battery electric passenger cars.
- The division operates a state-of-the-art fabrication facility located in Dalton, Georgia.
- The division also operates a just-in-time assembly plant in Spartanburg, South Carolina.
- The division utilizes differentiated process technologies including low pressure molding, high pressure back molding, thermoforming, load floor cells, water jet trimming, and final assembly.
- Lamar Lyle, CEO and Founder of Lyle Industries, stated excitement over completing the sale to Techniplas.
- Lamar Lyle noted that Lyle Industries will continue to supply premium carpet to the Business following the sale.
- Ali El-Haj, CEO of Techniplas, commented that the acquisition provides new strategic locations in the Southeast US and complementary manufacturing process technologies.
- Techniplas has a consolidated annual turnover in excess of 700 million USD.
- The combined enterprise will operate 30 high-performance locations with more than 3,500 employees worldwide.
- Techniplas serves global blue-chip customers primarily in the automotive sector as well as industrial, consumer, medical, and other markets.
- Techniplas products align with industry trends including vehicle light weighting and electrification.
