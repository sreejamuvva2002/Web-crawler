# Novelis Inc.

## Facts

- Novelis produces aluminum sheet, plate, and light gauge products for use in the automotive, transportation, aerospace, electronics, architectural, and industrial product markets.
- Flat-rolled aluminum sheet is used in the production of battery enclosures for the growing electric vehicle market.
- Novelis expects increased growth in the transportation end-use market driven by OEM customers' desire to lightweight vehicles to improve vehicle performance, safety, and sustainability profile, as well as by increased adoption of electric vehicles, which use higher amounts of aluminum.
- During fiscal 2025, Novelis had total shipment volumes of 3,972 kt and net sales of $17.1 billion.
- As of March 31, 2025, Novelis had manufacturing operations in nine countries on four continents: North America, South America, Europe, and Asia, with 31 operating facilities.
- Novelis has recycling operations in 15 of its operating facilities to recycle both post-consumer and post-industrial aluminum.
- Novelis is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Ltd., the registrant's parent company, which acquired Novelis in May 2007.
- As of September 30, 2024, there was no established public trading market for the common stock of Novelis Inc., and all outstanding shares were held indirectly by Hindalco Industries Ltd.
- As of May 9, 2025, Novelis had 600,000,000 common shares outstanding.
- Novelis is building a greenfield, fully-integrated rolling and recycling mill currently being constructed in Bay Minette, Alabama.
