# HL-GA Battery Company

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- HL-GA Battery Company is excited to call Bryan County home and drive the EV transition in America!
- Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy Solution committed an investment of more than $4.3 billion to establish HL-GA Battery Company.
- HL-GA Battery Company operates as a battery cell manufacturing joint venture.
- The facility is strategically located outside Savannah, Georgia.
- The facility is scheduled to start production early 2026.
- HL-GA Battery Company is one of the largest projects in the state of Georgia history.
- HL-GA Battery Company will supply next-generation battery technology to manufacturing facilities for production of Hyundai, Kia and Genesis EV models.
- The new facility will help create a stable supply of batteries in the region and allow for a quick response to the global EV demand.
- LG Energy Solution and Hyundai Motor Group have long been partners in the field of electrification.
- LG Energy Solution worked with Hyundai Motor Group to supply EV batteries for vehicles, including Elantra Hybrid, Kona Electric, and IONIQ 6 dedicated EV.
- The Hyundai Elantra Hybrid, LPi vehicle introduced in 2009, was HMG’s first electrified model.
- In 2021, the two power houses started a battery cell joint venture in Indonesia which started production in early 2024.
- Hyundai Motor Group is a global enterprise that has created a value chain based on mobility, steel, and construction, as well as logistics, finance, IT, and service.
- Hyundai Motor Group has about 250,000 employees worldwide.
- The Group’s mobility brands include Hyundai, Kia, and Genesis.
- LG Energy Solution is a leading global manufacturer of lithium-ion batteries for electric vehicles, mobility, IT, and energy storage systems.
- LG Energy Solution has 30 years of experience in revolutionary battery technology and extensive research and development (R&D).
- LG Energy Solution is the top battery-related patent holder in the world with over 58,000 patents.
- LG Energy Solution's robust global network spans North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia.
- LG Energy Solution has established battery manufacturing facilities through joint ventures with major automakers such as General Motors, Stellantis N.V., Hyundai Motor Group, and Honda Motor Co., Ltd.
- HL-GA Battery is transforming the automotive space and leading the way for sustainable automotive energy with over 30 years of research behind its lithium ion battery technology.
