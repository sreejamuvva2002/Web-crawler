## Facts

- The company operates a manufacturing facility exceeding 75,000 square feet.
- Production capability reaches up to 30 trailers per day.
- Product lines include cargo trailers, concession trailers, gooseneck trailers, dump trailers, equipment trailers, utility trailers, and car haulers.
- The Victory Series is a well-known line of enclosed cargo trailers that offers multiple specifications and customization options.
- Common construction features across the trailer lineup include robust leaf spring axles, anodized front corners for enhanced corrosion resistance, and aluminum fenders.
- Lark United products are distributed exclusively through an official dealer network across the United States.
