# Lund International Holding Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2013-03-11

**Source:** [Lund International, a Portfolio Company of Highlander Partners, Acquires Prestige Fender Flares – Highlander Partners](https://highlander-partners.com/news-posts/lund-international-a-portfolio-company-of-highlander-partners-acquires-prestige-fender-flares/)  ·  `page_045597`

## Facts

- Lund International Holding Company is a portfolio company of Highlander Partners, L.P.
- On March 11, 2013, Lund announced the acquisition of the Prestige Fender Flare assets from Empower Group, Inc.
- Prestige Fender Flares is a leading designer and producer of premium quality fender flares sold throughout the United States.
- Co-founders Anthony Liu and Robert Chen successfully built Prestige into a leading provider of fender flares in the automotive aftermarket over six years.
- Anthony Liu and Robert Chen will join Lund in a management capacity focusing on new product development.
- Prestige has developed a thorough and complete offering of fender flares for light trucks and SUVs.
- Co-founders Anthony Liu and Robert Chen stated they are excited to complete the transaction with Lund and look forward to leveraging its extensive, multi-channel distribution capabilities to continue growing in the fender flare market and other new product categories.
- Mitch Fogle serves as President of Lund.
- Mitch Fogle stated that adding Prestige’s fender flares to the Lund family of products solidifies Lund's leadership in the automotive aftermarket accessories industry.
- Mitch Fogle expressed looking forward to working with Anthony Liu and Robert Chen to maximize the potential of their product offering and exploit combined new product ideas.
- Prestige is the first add-on acquisition completed by Lund under Highlander’s ownership.
- Jeff L. Hull serves as Chairman of Lund International and Managing Partner of Highlander.
- Jeff L. Hull stated the acquisition validates Lund's desire to be a leader across all product categories in the exterior aftermarket automotive accessories market.
- Jeff L. Hull noted the acquisition confirms Lund's ongoing interest in utilizing an acquisition-based strategy to complement organic growth efforts.
- Jeff L. Hull stated Lund continues to evaluate numerous other potential acquisitions and is always on the lookout for new opportunities.
- Lund International Holding Company is a leading designer, manufacturer, and marketer of branded automotive aftermarket accessories for passenger cars, light trucks, and heavy trucks.
- Lund's product portfolio includes ventvisors, hood shields, heavy-duty floor mats, tonneau covers, storage boxes, and running boards.
- Lund offers the industry’s broadest range of functional body and appearance products that allow customers to customize and personalize their vehicles.
- Highlander Partners, L.P. is a Dallas-based private investment firm.
- Highlander Partners' partners have committed over $500 million of principal funds for direct private equity investments in North America and Central Europe.
- Highlander Partners focuses on making investments in middle market businesses in targeted industries where principals have significant operating and investing experience, including basic manufacturing, food, building materials, and others.
- Highlander Partners uses a “buy and build” investment approach to create value by helping companies grow organically and through acquisitions.
