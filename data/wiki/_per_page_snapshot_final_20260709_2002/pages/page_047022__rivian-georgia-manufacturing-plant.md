# Rivian Georgia Manufacturing Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-16

**Source:** [State leaders break ground on massive Rivian manufacturing plant – WGAU](https://www.wgauradio.com/news/local/state-leaders-break-ground-massive-rivian-manufacturing-plant/FUT6RP6KFRA7JOSEJQFI4M7EHU/)  ·  `page_047022`

## Facts

- Governor Brian Kemp and other state leaders broke ground Tuesday morning on the massive multi-billion dollar Rivian manufacturing plant east of Atlanta in both Morgan County and Walton County.
- Rivian CEO RJ Scaringe stated that it has been a long road to reach this milestone.
- Rivian CEO RJ Scaringe said, "Thank you all for being on this journey. Thanks for being on this incredible adventure and helping us turn that beautiful site that we see out there in the distance into a thriving manufacturing facility."
- In 2021, state officials lauded the project as a generational partnership.
- Governor Brian Kemp touted that the $5B plan for the plant in east Georgia would create thousands of jobs.
- The project was troubled from the start with local opposition from residents and raised questions about the massive tax incentives given to the company.
- The electric vehicle plant will occupy a 2,000 acre site.
- Company officials hope to have cars rolling off the line by 2028.
