# MOBIS Alabama, LLC

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2007-05-17

**Source:** [Topic: [MOBIS AL LLC Georgia Plant] Sales Specialist | WorkingUS.com](https://www.workingus.com/forums/topic/mobis-al-llc-georgia-plant-sales-specialist-4/)  ·  `page_046788`

## Facts

- MOBIS Alabama, LLC’s Georgia Plant is located in West Point, Georgia.
- The Georgia plant operates as a Tier 1 supplier to Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, LLC (KMMG).
- The Georgia plant was established on May 17, 2007.
- MOBIS Georgia operates as a three-shift manufacturing operation.
- The facility assembles four major modules: Front Chassis, Rear Chassis, Cockpit, and Front End.
- The facility also assembles front and rear bumpers for the three vehicles produced by KMMG.
- A Sales Specialist role at the plant involves accomplishing day-to-day tactical objectives in support of company and department goals.
- Employees are expected to adhere to company policies and procedures supporting safety, quality, delivery, cost, and morale (SQDCM) initiatives.
- The work environment is described as union-free.
- Sales Specialist responsibilities include managing monthly costs and billing of parts, trading goods, and price negotiations.
- The role requires collaboration with plant teams and HQ in Korea from RFQ to product specification and pricing negotiation.
- Price decisions are prepared based on cost calculations with HQ in Korea.
- The specialist negotiates parts prices with the OEM for new projects and engineering changes.
- Tooling costs and other expenses are negotiated with the OEM.
- Communication with the customer is conducted in both English and Korean regarding sales-related issues.
- Daily billing for modules and training goods is monitored for monthly closing.
- Sales goals are maintained on a monthly, quarterly, and yearly basis, and sales results reports are created and analyzed.
- Accounts receivable (A/R) from the OEM is monitored.
- Daily requirements are received from the OEM through systems.
- Minimum education requirements for the role include a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration, Industrial Engineering, or a related field.
- Candidates need 0 to 3 years of experience in Sales, Manufacturing, or Automotive Manufacturing.
- Required skills include strong computer proficiency with Microsoft Office Products and Project Management Skills.
- Bilingual proficiency in English and Korean is required.
