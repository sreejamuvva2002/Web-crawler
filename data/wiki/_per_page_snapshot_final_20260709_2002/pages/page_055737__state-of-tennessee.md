# State of Tennessee

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2023-08-16

**Source:** [Tennessee To Give Ford Electric Truck Supplier $13M In Incentives | Tennessee Conservative](https://tennesseeconservativenews.com/tennessee-to-give-ford-electric-truck-supplier-13m-in-incentives/)  ·  `page_055737`

## Facts

- Tennessee has committed to give $13 million in incentives to suppliers for Ford’s new electric truck factory at BlueOval City.
- Magna Seating will receive $3 million for its factory at BlueOval City.
- Cosma International's factory at the BlueOval City site in Haywood County will receive $7.5 million in incentives.
- A third manufacturing facility in Lawrence County will receive $2.5 million in incentives.
- The incentive package is set to be approved at a meeting of the State Funding Board on Monday.
- Hanon Systems USA will receive $4.5 million in incentives for its operations in Loudon City.
- EnChem America will receive $2 million in incentives for its operations in Haywood County.
- McNeilus Truck and Manufacturing will receive $1.3 million in incentives for its Murfreesboro facility.
- Additional incentives of $1 million and $404,000 for unnamed companies will be announced at the State Funding Board meeting.
- Magna has planned to spend $790 million to build an 800,000-square-foot frame and battery enclosures facility and a 140,000-square-foot seating facility at BlueOval City.
- The Tennessee Legislature previously approved $884 million in incentives for Ford at the BlueOval City facility during a special session in October 2021.
- Magna is expected to employ 1,300 workers across its three facilities, with 750 at the battery enclosure plant and 300 at the seating plant.
- The Magna plants are expected to be ready for production in 2025.
- The components produced by these suppliers will be used on the Ford F-150 Lightning trucks manufactured at BlueOval City.
- Ford is expected to produce 500,000 electric trucks per year at full production capacity at the site.
- Hanon Systems is a South Korean company that plans to build a $170 million manufacturing facility for its thermal supplies for electric vehicles.
- These Hanon thermal supplies include components for heating ventilation and air conditioning, powertrain cooling, compressors, fluid transport, and electronics and fluid pressure.
