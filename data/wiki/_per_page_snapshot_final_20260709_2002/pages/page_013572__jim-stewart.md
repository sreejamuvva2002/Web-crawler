## Facts

- Mr. Stewart has been with the Vista Metals organization for over 35 years.
- He has a tremendous amount of experience in the aluminum and casting industry.
- Prior to his current position as Vice President of Vista Metals Georgia, Jim held roles including Production Leadman, General Foreman, and Casting Superintendent.
- Jim was instrumental in the establishment and subsequent growth of Vista Metals Georgia.
- He served as the Plant Manager for Vista Metals Georgia for over 8 years.
- As Vice President of Vista Metals Georgia, he oversees and manages the operations of Vista Metals Georgia and Vista Progressive Metals.
- Mr. Stewart served in the U.S. Army for over 4 years with domestic and overseas tours of duty.
