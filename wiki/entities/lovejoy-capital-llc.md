# Lovejoy Capital LLC

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Atlanta, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Atlanta-based investment firm specializing in luxury outdoors rentals that is proposing a public-private partnership with Macon-Bibb County to develop glamping cabins, British Landpods, EV charging stations, bike racks, fishing docks and other amenities at Lake Tobesofkee.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Proposed installation of EV charging stations as part of a glamping-focused redevelopment of Lake Tobesofkee.

## Key facts

- Lovejoy Capital specializes in luxury outdoors rentals and aims to bring "glamping," EV chargers and fishing docks to Lake Tobesofkee.
- The project is estimated to cost about $23 million, pending a feasibility study and revenue-share agreement with the county.
- Under the proposed public-private partnership, Lovejoy would fund the development while Macon-Bibb County provides existing facilities; revenue from rentals would be shared.
- Dozens of rental units, holiday cabins, British Landpods, a tourist maze, bike racks, EV charging stations, trail system and additional fishing docks are planned.
- Lovejoy Capital’s members have experience with outdoor hospitality ventures across 36 states.
- The firm also has an ongoing similar proposal in Troup County for improvements to Pyne Road Park.
- Residents expressed concerns about park fees and maintenance; Lovejoy assured that lake fees would not increase and jobs would be retained.
- "I want growth, we need growth, our county needs growth for sure," said resident Tony Thomaston, emphasizing community desire for development without sacrificing family access.
