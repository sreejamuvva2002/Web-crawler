# The Hollingsworth Companies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-13

**Source:** [The Hollingsworth Companies Continues Industrial Expansion](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/the-hollingsworth-companies-continues-industrial-expansion-301848395.html)  ·  `page_002622`

## Facts

- The Hollingsworth Companies completed a 108,960 SF facility at SouthPoint Business Park in Huntsville, AL.
- The company leased and expanded a 174,000 SF facility to 195,000 SF at SouthPoint Business Park in Huntsville, AL.
- SouthPoint Business Park in Huntsville has grown to 12 buildings with over 2,000,000 SF under roof and is fully leased.
- Tenants in the park include automotive suppliers, defense contractors, building materials companies, and marine and beverage distributors.
- Brooks Kracke, President and CEO of the North Alabama Industrial Development Association, stated that a balanced mix of industries in the park has provided stability to the regional labor market.
- Brooks Kracke noted that the park mirrors regional growth over the last ten years fueled by major projects from Defense Contractors, Automotive Suppliers, and support industries.
- Joe Hollingsworth, CEO of The Hollingsworth Companies, stated he is very bullish on the North Alabama market and believes American manufacturing will continue to prosper with the Southeastern United States leading growth.
- Joe Hollingsworth thanked the community for investing time, effort, and money as partners in making the park successful, predicting the next 10 years will be the best economic period of their lives.
- SouthPoint Industrial Park is located adjacent to both I-65 and I-565, providing access to markets from Nashville TN and Louisville KY to the north and Birmingham and Montgomery AL to the south.
- The corridor surrounding SouthPoint Industrial Park is highly favored by companies that serve the Southern Automotive market.
- North Alabama is home to the Army Contracting Command, NASA's Marshall Space Flight Center, and the Missile and Space Intelligence Center.
- Recently opened manufacturing sites in the North Alabama area include Mazda/Toyota, Polaris, GE Aviation, Dynetics, Blue Origin, and Space X.
- The surrounding area has a population of almost 700,000 that includes a well seasoned, high tech work force, posturing it for advanced manufacturing growth and distribution.
- The Hollingsworth Companies is the largest non-urban Industrial Real Estate developer and construction firm in the Southeastern United States.
- The Hollingsworth Companies manages 125 tenants across 18 million square feet of industrial space spread over 17 states.
- The Hollingsworth Companies has facilities located in Alabama, Georgia, Mississippi, North Carolina, South Carolina, Tennessee, and Virginia.
