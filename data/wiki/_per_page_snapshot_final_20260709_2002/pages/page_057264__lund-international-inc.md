## Facts

- Lund International is a premier manufacturer and marketer of automotive aftermarket accessories primarily for light and heavy trucks, sport utility vehicles (SUVs), and crossovers.
- The company's product portfolio includes running boards, hood shields, side window deflectors, visors, bug shields, tonneau covers, folding bed covers, grille guards & bull bars, guide lights, cargo bed extenders, fender flares, and ABS plastic parts.
- In 1998, Lund International acquired AVS, taking it to new levels of success and innovation; AVS became synonymous with Ventvisor® side window deflectors and hood protectors.
- Lund International is one of the world's leading automotive accessories providers offering highly functional, protective, and stylish products from the industry's most recognized brands.
- The company operates within the Transportation Equipment Manufacturing industry with a reported revenue range of $100 to $500 million (USD).
- Lund International is located in Buford, GA.
- Lund International launched a new look and new capabilities for its consumer and commercial website on May 17, 2005.
