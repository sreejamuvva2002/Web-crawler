# Volvo Car US Operations (VCCH)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- The Volvo Car Charleston Plant, also known as Volvo Car US Operations (VCCH), is located in Ridgeville, South Carolina, United States.
- Production at the facility commenced in 2018 and continues to the present.
- It serves as the first Volvo Car plant established in the United States.
- The operational scale of the plant is manufacturing.
- Current production includes the EX90 and Polestar 3 models.
- The facility previously produced SPA-based S60 models.
- Vehicles manufactured at this plant serve worldwide markets.
