## Facts

- Hyundai TRANSYS is a world-renowned producer of automotive parts.
- Hyundai TRANSYS will invest more than $240 million to expand its operations in Georgia.
- The expansion will create 678 full-time positions at a new manufacturing facility in the West Point area.
- President of Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Powertrain Sangkil Jung stated that Georgia was chosen due to trust built through years of capital investment, employment, and training support, alongside unmatched opportunities.
- Hyundai TRANSYS expressed excitement about expanding its presence in the West Point community.
- Hyundai TRANSYS is the only automobile manufacturing company in the world that specializes in transmissions.
- The new West Point plant will be 620,000 square feet.
- The new plant is located near the company’s current location on the Kia campus.
- The new plant will be responsible for producing eight-speed transmissions for a global automotive leader.
- Hyundai TRANSYS will bring hundreds of advanced manufacturing jobs, along with management and office staff positions, to the local community.
- The company plans to create 165 supplementary construction handling and inspection jobs.
- This expansion will bring Hyundai TRANSYS’s total employment in Georgia to 1,741 jobs.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp stated that Georgia's No. 1 business climate, robust logistics network, and top-ranked workforce training programs attract global companies, retain existing industries, and help them grow.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp thanked Hyundai TRANSYS for its long-term commitment to Georgia and expressed a desire to continue working together to ensure the investment benefits both the company and West Georgia residents.
- Mayor of the City of West Point Steve Tramell expressed thrill over Hyundai TRANSYS's long-term investment in West Point and the creation of new jobs.
- Mayor Steve Tramell thanked the West Point Development Authority, the State of Georgia, and Troup County for their contributions to making the project a reality.
- Hyundai TRANSYS has been a community partner with West Point and Troup County for over a decade.
