# RealTruck Inc.

## Facts

- RealTruck Inc. is a global aftermarket product and accessory brand for truck, Jeep, Bronco, and off-road enthusiasts.
- RealTruck is returning for its 16th year at the SEMA Show in Las Vegas, Nevada from November 4-8.
- RealTruck will occupy booth #50027 to showcase 17 in-booth vehicle builds and its latest product portfolio.
- The company is highlighting celebrity build collaborations with automotive design icon Chip Foose, global adventurist Bear Grylls, and basketball player Dwyane Wade.
- RealTruck is showcasing a one-of-a-kind build in collaboration with Makita U.S.A. featuring workplace utility on four wheels.
- RealTruck will host two press conferences: the first on Tuesday, November 5 at 11:30 a.m. PST to unveil the Chip Foose collaboration, and the second on Thursday, November 7 at 11:30 a.m. PST featuring Bear Grylls and his two customized vehicles.
- RealTruck Vice President of Marketing Lee Riser stated that SEMA is the company's biggest event of the year, providing an ideal platform to engage customers and reinforce leadership in the aftermarket industry for trucks, Jeeps, Broncos, and off-road enthusiasts.
- Some of the showcased builds will be auctioned in the coming months to support special charity organizations.
- RealTruck is collaborating with automotive designer Chip Foose on a one-of-a-kind F-150 build unveiled via live stream press conference on Tuesday, November 5 at 11:30 a.m. PST.
- The Chip Foose F-150 will be auctioned at Barrett-Jackson in Scottsdale, Arizona in January 2025, with all proceeds benefiting The Progeria Research Foundation.
- RealTruck announced an integrated partnership with Bear Grylls and his inaugural “Adventure Garage with Bear Grylls” show airing globally in 2025.
- As part of the Bear Grylls partnership, RealTruck customized a 2021 Ford F-150 and 2024 Toyota Tacoma as permanent fixtures on the show.
- RealTruck products will be seamlessly integrated into each episode of the Bear Grylls show for extreme adventures.
- RealTruck is collaborating with basketball legend Dwyane Wade on a customized one-of-a-kind F-250 build revealed at the show and auctioned at Barrett-Jackson in Palm Beach, Florida in April 2025.
- Proceeds from the Dwyane Wade F-250 auction will benefit his non-profit organization Social Change Fund United, supporting underrepresented communities of color.
- RealTruck collaborated with Makita U.S.A., Inc. on a construction-focused Ford F-150 build featuring Husky Liners Wheel Well Guards, Universal Mud Guards, Low Profile Vent Visors, Go Rhino XVenture Gear Tool Wrench Rolls, Recovery Bag, Large Hard Case, AMP Research Bed Step, A.R.E. Double Drop Down HD Aluminum Kit, and a custom Makita wrap with an exclusive first look at their new modular storage solution.
- RealTruck announced four new products: TruXedo Pro X15 TS, BAKFlip MX4 TS, Sport Bar, and Go Rhino Hitch Based Swing-A-Way Tire Carrier.
- The TruXedo Pro X15 TS is a soft truck bed cover with versatile T-slot channels in the rails for expanding cargo-carrying abilities, featuring 15-degree side rails that sit flush with bed rails when closed.
- The BAKFlip MX4 TS is a top-selling truck bed cover constructed from alloy materials including heavy-duty aluminum top panels and side rails, with a textured automotive-graded finish and integrated T-slot rails compatible with racks and carriers.
- The RealTruck Sport Bar features a dual-tube design in single or double front tube options with a detachable front bar, constructed from high-strength 2-1/2” OD steel tubing with a textured black powder coat finish and molded side panel inserts with 3D-effect venting.
- The Sport Bar includes a versatile light multi-mount system compatible with factory track systems, most aftermarket track-style bed covers, clamp-to-truck bed caps, and most retractable bed covers.
- RealTruck’s Go Rhino Hitch Based Swing-A-Way Tire Carrier is engineered to install into any universal 2-inch receiver hitch, built from high strength 3/16-inch-thick steel with black geomet-coated hardware, featuring a fully adjustable wheel mount accommodating tire combinations up to 37-inch diameter and 200 pounds weight capacity.
- RealTruck products are categorized under Restyling & Car Care Accessories, Trucks, SUV's & Off-Road, and Wheels & Accessories at the show.
