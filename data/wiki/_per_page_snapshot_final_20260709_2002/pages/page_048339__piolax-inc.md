# PIOLAX, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Designs, develops, produces, and sells various parts and products for the automotive, medical, and consumer and security industries in Japan, the rest of Asia, the United States, Europe, and internationally.
- Offers valves and fuel parts for filling, storing, and providing petrol.
- Offers connectors for piping used around radiators.
- Offers compact filters for fuel and oil systems.
- Offers evapo-tube ASSY products.
- Offers connectors with invented resin tubes.
- Provides powertrain parts, which include various springs that are used for transmissions, and snap rings used for holding parts.
- Provides open-close parts, including air dampers, latches, and cushions for glove boxes.
- Provides resin and metal fasteners for fixing automobile interior/exterior parts and connecting pipes to car body.
- Provides harnesses for bundling electric wires.
- Offers precision parts comprising retainers and tension springs to hold wheel caps.
- Offers injection-molded parts for automotive interior/exterior.
- Offers visor-related parts and luggage board handles, as well as plate clamps and screw clamps.
- Provides medical appliances, such as spiral catheters and stents.
- Provides consumer and security products, including springs for combination faucets, security parts to seal money bags or container vehicles, hot water blocking valves, and headphone wire bands.
- Was formerly known as Kato Hatsujo Inc. and changed its name to PIOLAX, Inc. in October 1995.
- Founded in 1933 and is headquartered in Yokohama, Japan.
