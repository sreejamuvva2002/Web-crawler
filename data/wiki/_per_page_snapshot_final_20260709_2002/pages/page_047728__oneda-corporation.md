## Facts

- Oneda Corporation is a family-owned, Georgia-based contract manufacturer specializing in precision metal stamping, assembly, and tool and die designs.
- The company utilizes innovative robotic transfer and progressive stamping capabilities with press sizes ranging from 25 to 500 tons.
- Oneda Corporation is located in Columbus, Georgia.
- It operates as a subsidiary of Oneda Electric Corporation.
- The company serves the automotive, medical, heating and air, transportation, refrigeration, and consumer electronics industries.
- Oneda Corporation is an American Made, third-generation, family-owned manufacturer based in Georgia.
- The company maintains an internal Die Shop Apprenticeship program to train the next generation of tool and die makers.
- Oneda Corporation holds ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, and IATF16949:2016 certifications.
- The company was formed to support US OEMs for custom metal stampings, assemblies, and tool & die operations.
- Operations include Stamping, Tool & Die, Electronic Assembly, Main Electronic Assembly Plant, Assembly Operations Plant, Secondary Stampings, Large Metal Stamping, and Metal/Plastic Assembly.
- On average, Oneda's team members have worked at the manufacturing plant for more than 10 years.
- The company states it is always looking for great people to join its team.
