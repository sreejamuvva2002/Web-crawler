## Facts

- Murata Electronics North America Inc. offers the RCE Series monolithic ceramic capacitor (MLCC) line groomed for automotive applications.
- The RCE Series complies with both the AEC-Q200 requirement and the ISO7636-2 surge test.
- The series measures from 3.6 mm x 3.5 mm to 7.7 mm x 13 mm.
- The series has a rated voltage of 25 Vdc to 1 kVdc.
- The series has a capacitance range of 1 pF to 22 µF.
- The series has an operating temperature range of -55°C to +125°C.
- Samples of the RCE series are available starting at $0.06.
- Samples have an approximate lead time of 10 weeks.
- Murata Electronics North America Inc. is located in Smyrna, GA.
