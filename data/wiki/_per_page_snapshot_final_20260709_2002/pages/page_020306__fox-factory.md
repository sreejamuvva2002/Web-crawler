# Fox Factory

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-02-26

**Source:** [Fox Factory notches sales of $1.6 billion in 2022, up 23% | Bicycle Retailer and Industry News](https://www.bicycleretailer.com/industry-news/2023/02/26/fox-factory-notches-sales-16-billion-2022-23)  ·  `page_020306`

## Facts

- Fox Factory reported a company record of $1,602.5 million in sales for 2022, up 23.4% from the prior year.
- Sales in Fox's powered vehicle business increased by 28% in 2022.
- Sales in Fox's specialty sports business, which comprises its bicycle brands, increased by 17.6% for the year.
- Fox Factory's bike-related brands include Fox, Easton, RaceFace, and Marzocchi.
- In the fourth quarter of 2022, company-wide sales rose 19.4%, while specialty sports business sales declined 1.9% due to a return to seasonality after pandemic-driven growth.
- Fox Factory cited continued improvement at its Gainesville, Georgia facility in 2022, which the company stated provides a solid foundation for further improvements in 2023.
- CEO Mike Dennison attributed the company's strong performance to leveraging a diverse portfolio to combat supply chain and labor constraints, as well as inflation threats.
- Fox Factory announced the acquisition of Custom Wheel House, a California-based manufacturer of wheels for cars, trucks, and off-road vehicles, for $132 million.
- For the full year 2022, Fox Factory's gross margin was 33.2%, representing a 10 basis point decrease from 2021.
- Fox Factory's net income for fiscal year 2022 was $205.3 million, compared to $163.8 million in the prior fiscal year.
- Earnings per diluted share for fiscal year 2022 were $4.84, up from $3.87 in fiscal year 2021.
- For the first quarter of fiscal 2023, Fox Factory expects sales between $380 million and $400 million, with non-GAAP adjusted earnings per diluted share between $1.10 and $1.30.
- For fiscal year 2023, Fox Factory projects sales between $1.67 billion and $1.7 billion, with non-GAAP adjusted earnings per diluted share between $5.15 and $5.45.
- Fox Factory's shares trade on Nasdaq under the symbol FOXF.
