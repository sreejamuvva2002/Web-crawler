## Facts

- Georgia’s top industries thrive thanks to competitive business costs, skilled workforce, and world-class transportation infrastructure that provides unmatched access to global markets.
- Select Georgia produces updated annually publications for target industries including Electric Transportation and Automotive.
- Marion Phillips serves as Research Manager at Select Georgia.
- The industry publications are produced by Georgia Power’s award-winning research team of analysts and data scientists.
- The publications offer a statewide view of Georgia’s target industries, covering features such as the technical workforce, innovation centers, recent announcements, and company locations.
- Available industry publications cover Aerospace, Automotive, Chemical Manufacturing, Digital Media & Entertainment, Electric Transportation, Metal Manufacturing, FinTech, Food Processing, Information & Communication Technology, Life Sciences, Plastics & Rubber Manufacturing, and Warehouse Distribution.
