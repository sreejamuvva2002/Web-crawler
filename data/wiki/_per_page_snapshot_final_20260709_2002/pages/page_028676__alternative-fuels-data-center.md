## Facts

- The Alternative Fuels Data Center provides a Medium- and Heavy-Duty Vehicle Search tool to find and compare alternative fuel vehicles, engines, and power sources.
- Peterbilt 548 is available in Class 8 with CNG or LNG options, featuring Cummins L9N 8.9L Near Zero or Cummins B6.7N Near Zero power sources.
- The Peterbilt 548EV electric variant offers an electric-only range of 135 to 250 miles and a battery capacity of 250 to 375 kWh.
- The Peterbilt 548EV uses an automatic transmission and is powered by a PACCAR ePowertrain available in 470 HP, 540 HP, or 605 HP configurations.
- According to the manufacturer, the Peterbilt 548EV has a gross combined weight rating (GCWR) of 82,000 and supports a maximum charging speed of 350 kW for up to an 80% charge in 80 minutes.
- The Peterbilt 567EV electric variant offers an electric-only range of 100 to 250 miles and a battery capacity of 250 to 625 kWh.
- The Peterbilt 567EV uses an automatic transmission and is powered by a PACCAR ePowertrain available in 470 HP, 540 HP, or 605 HP configurations.
- According to the manufacturer, the Peterbilt 567EV has a gross combined weight rating (GCWR) of 82,000 and supports a maximum charging speed of 350 kW for up to an 80% charge in 80-120 minutes.
- The Peterbilt 579EV electric variant offers an electric-only range of 200 miles and a battery capacity of 250 to 500 kWh.
- The Peterbilt 579EV uses an automatic transmission and is powered by a PACCAR ePowertrain available in 470 HP, 540 HP, or 605 HP configurations.
- According to the manufacturer, the Peterbilt 579EV has a gross combined weight rating (GCWR) of 82,000 and supports a maximum charging speed of 350 kW for up to an 80% charge in 90 minutes.
- RAM 2500/3500 HD and RAM 3500/4500/5500 heavy-duty trucks are available with Biodiesel (B20) fuel options.
- The RAM heavy-duty truck variants utilize a Cummins ISB6.7 power source paired with a RAM 8-Speed TorqueFlite HD automatic transmission.
- Users can select up to four items from the search results to compare vehicle specifications side-by-side.
