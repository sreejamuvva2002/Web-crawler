## Facts

- Centrally located in Middle Georgia half-way between Atlanta and Savannah on Interstate 16.
- From anywhere in the county, it is a less than 2-hour drive to either location.
- A 331-acre site is available at the HWY 257 GRAD Select Site.
- The total workforce for the region is 301,204.
- Market connectivity includes being less than 2 hours from Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport.
- Key industrial sectors in the area include Distribution, Automotive, and Manufacturing.
