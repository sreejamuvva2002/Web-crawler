# Jackson County Economic Development Alliance

**Type:** Government Program  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[government-support|Government Support]]  ·  **Location:** Jackson County, GA, Jackson County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

The Jackson County Economic Development Alliance promotes and supports economic development in Jackson County, Georgia through public-private partnerships, marketing the county’s strategic location, industrial sites, and logistics advantages.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Proximity to the SK Innovation battery facility makes Jackson County a strategic logistics hub for EV supply-chain components and distribution.

## Key facts

- Jackson County’s strategic location just northeast of Atlanta on I-85 attracts businesses seeking quick regional access.
- Jackson County has some of the best industrial sites and buildings in Georgia.
- Commerce Crossroads Building 400 offers 172,000 SF of Class A industrial space with 32-foot clear ceilings, over 50 dock doors, and prime access to I-85.
- The building is positioned near major manufacturing hubs, making it ideal for distribution or production operations.
- Proximity to the SK Innovation facility enhances its appeal for strategic partners and suppliers in the EV sector.
- Leasing options are available starting at $6.30 per SF per year.
- Both public and private organizations have partnered together to promote and support Jackson County’s economic development, forming the Jackson Alliance.
