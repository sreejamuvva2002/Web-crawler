# Textron Specialized Vehicles

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Textron Specialized Vehicles Recalls Prowler Pro and Tracker Utility Vehicles (UTVs) Due to Fire Hazard | CPSC.gov](https://www.cpsc.gov/es/Noticias/2024/Textron-Specialized-Vehicles-Recalls-Prowler-Pro-and-Tracker-Utility-Vehicles-UTVs-Due-to-Fire-Hazard?language=ko)  ·  `page_029531`

## Facts

- The fuel tank cap does not fit correctly, causing a fuel leak around the filler neck, posing a fire hazard.
- About 10,300 utility vehicles are affected by the recall.
- An additional approximately 1,000 units were sold in Canada.
- The recall involves model years 2022 and 2023 Prowler Pro, Prowler Pro Crew, Tracker 800SX, and Tracker 800SX Crew utility vehicles.
- The recalled vehicles were sold in various colors including green, gray, red, fossil, and timber.
- "Prowler" or "Tracker" is stamped on the side of the cargo box.
- The 10th digit of the vehicle identification number (VIN) is printed on the operator side of the frame under the cargo box.
- A VIN 10th digit of "N" indicates a model year 2022 vehicle.
- A VIN 10th digit of "P" indicates a model year 2023 vehicle.
- Customers should immediately stop using the recalled utility vehicles.
- Customers should contact an authorized dealer for a free inspection and repair.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles is contacting all known purchasers directly regarding the recall.
- The firm has received 48 reports of incidents involving fuel leakage around the cap.
- No injuries have been reported related to the fuel leakage issue.
