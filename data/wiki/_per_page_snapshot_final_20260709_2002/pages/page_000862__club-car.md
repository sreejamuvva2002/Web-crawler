# Club Car

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-04

**Source:** [Club Car Delivers Nearly 500 Vehicles for Ryder Cup 2025 at Bethpage Black](https://www.clubcar.com/en/our-company/newsroom/club-car-fleet-at-2025-ryder-cup)  ·  `page_000862`

## Facts

- Club Car is headquartered in Augusta, Georgia.
- Club Car delivered 443 vehicles to Bethpage Black for the 2025 Ryder Cup.
- The fleet includes seven custom golf cars crafted exclusively for U.S. Captain Keegan Bradley, European Captain Luke Donald, and their vice-captains.
- Fred Palmer serves as Vice President of Golf Business Development at Club Car.
- Club Car has served as a Worldwide Supplier to the Ryder Cup since 2022, with a partnership dating back to 1991.
- Club Car has partnered with the PGA of America since 1990.
- Club Car's product portfolio includes vehicles for commercial, personal, and street-legal low-speed use.
- The Club Car Vehicle Group includes the luxury electric vehicle brand Garia and the lightweight electric utility vehicle brand Melex.
- Club Car has over six decades of history in superior design and performance.
