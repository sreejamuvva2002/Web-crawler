# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-01

**Source:** [Novelis Inc. Fiscal 2026 Annual Incentive Plan | Novelis Inc. | Business Contracts | Justia](https://contracts.justia.com/companies/novelis-inc-2701/contract/1372505/)  ·  `page_047577`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. established the Fiscal 2026 Annual Incentive Plan (AIP) for eligible employees in certain job bands, corporate offices, and the Bay Minette Project.
- The plan covers a performance period from April 1, 2025, to March 31, 2026.
- Payouts under the plan are based on company and individual performance measures, including safety, adjusted operating EBITDA, and cash flow.
- Eligibility includes employees in job bands 7 and higher, all corporate and regional headquarters office employees, and all employees assigned to the Bay Minette Project Plan.
- Performance weightings for Bay Minette project participants vary by role, including Steering Committee, Operational Readiness Committee, Construction Project Team, and Operational Readiness Project Team.
- The Bay Minette Project Milestones metric tracks Earned Construction, Employment Level Adherence, Core Process Readiness, and Supporting Functions Schedule Plan.
- An 'active employee' for the Bay Minette project is defined as a candidate who has accepted an offer and has been actively employed for 30 days by February 28, 2026.
- Core Process Readiness is measured by the completion of scheduled deliverables via a gate review process, with the Steering Committee authorized to modify the number of deliverables.
- The Supporting Functions Schedule Plan tracks achievement for IT, Finance, Procurement, Metal Procurement, and Supply Chain.
- The overall AIP plan threshold for payout on any metric requires at least 75% EBITDA.
- Novelis Inc. administers the plan through its Human Resources department and Compensation Committee.
