# Valeo

## Facts

- Valeo is the world leader of high and low-voltage electric powertrain solutions.
- Valeo is the world No. 2 in thermal management systems that ensure the performance and range of electric vehicles.
- The automotive sector has largely bet on electrification to quickly reduce CO2 emissions as part of the 2016 Paris Agreement, which aims to limit global warming to a level below 2°C compared to pre-industrial levels.
- 600 new models of EVs are due to hit the market in 2025.
- Valeo has invested more than 10 billion euros in CO2 emissions reduction technologies over the last decade.
- Valeo has nearly 100 years of experience in propulsion and thermal systems.
- In terms of OEM sales, Valeo is the world leader in automotive electrification for high- and low-voltage solutions.
- Valeo is the inventor of the Stop-Start system and affordable hybrid solutions thanks to its 48V systems that enable electrification at the lowest cost.
- A third of existing electric vehicles in the world are equipped with Valeo solutions to reduce CO2 emissions.
- Valeo's sales in electrification increased 26-fold between 2009 and 2021.
- Valeo is the undisputed world leader in 48V systems with a market share of more than 30% of the world market.
- Valeo acquired Valeo Siemens eAutomotive in July 2022.
- Valeo's solutions are found on both the standard and premium platforms of more than 20 car manufacturers worldwide.
- By the end of 2022, 90 electric and plug-in hybrid car models equipped with Valeo’s high-voltage technologies will be marketed globally.
- An EV contains 2.5 times more equipment related to thermal systems than a traditional combustion vehicle.
- The market for thermal management systems for electric vehicles (coolant heater, battery cooler, electric compressor, heat pump) will reach 21 billion euros in 2025.
- Valeo equipped the first hybrids in 2006 with its cooling solution.
- Valeo is the world leader in all cooling solutions (air, liquid or refrigerant) and for all types of cells (cylindrical, prismatic, pouch cells) in series.
- The electrification market is witnessing very strong growth of +30% between 2021 and 2025.
- Valeo develops solutions for two-, three- or four-wheeled vehicles including electric bicycles, scooters, motorcycles, robot-taxis, urban quadricycles, and delivery droids using its 48V expertise.
- Valeo has recorded more than 200 million euros in orders for the electrification of new mobility solutions.
- Valeo is targeting more than 500 million euros in revenues in the new mobility market in 2030.
