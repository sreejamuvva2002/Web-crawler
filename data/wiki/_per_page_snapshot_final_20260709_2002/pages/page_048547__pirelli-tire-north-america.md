# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Pirelli to Build Cyber Tire in U.S. | Modern Tire Dealer](https://www.moderntiredealer.com/suppliers/news/55375666/pirelli-to-build-cyber-tire-in-us)  ·  `page_048547`

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America will manufacture the Pirelli Cyber Tyre at its plant in Rome, Ga.
- The manufacturing move reinforces the firm's long-term commitment to the United States.
- Pirelli officials state the development highlights the strategic importance of the U.S. for Pirelli’s global growth roadmap.
- The development strengthens the company’s integrated industrial and technological presence in the country.
- The announcement was made at the SelectUSA Investment Summit.
- The SelectUSA Investment Summit is promoted by the U.S. Department of Commerce.
- At the summit, Cyber Tyre is being showcased as a core innovation shaping the future of smart mobility.
- The Cyber Tire collects data and information from sensors embedded in tires.
- It processes this data through Pirelli’s proprietary software and algorithms.
- It communicates in real time with the vehicle’s electronics.
- This enables new functionalities integrated with driving and control systems to enhance the driving experience and increase safety levels.
- The technology supports connected infrastructure.
- In the U.S., where digital and smart mobility solutions are rapidly expanding, Cyber Tyre represents a distinctive competitive advantage for Pirelli.
- Pirelli is finalizing the introduction of the latest version of the MIRS (Modular Integrated Robotized System) production process to enhance Rome's role as a high-tech production site.
- The MIRS process will be the most advanced manufacturing process for high-end, premium production within the entire Pirelli Group.
- This process is exclusive to the Georgia factory.
- The MIRS process enhances robotized production capabilities, increasing productivity, flexibility, and quality.
- The start of Cyber Tyre production at the Rome, Ga., plant is a significant milestone for Pirelli in the U.S., according to Claudio Zanardo, CEO of Pirelli Tire North America.
- The production start reflects a commitment to bringing advanced technologies like Cyber Tyre closer to the market.
- It further strengthens Pirelli’s industrial footprint and innovation capabilities in the United States.
- The development lays the groundwork for growth in Pirelli’s production capacity in Rome.
- This growth is an integral part of the Pirelli group’s industrial development plans.
- It will further strengthen Pirelli’s presence in the United States, one of its most important and strategic markets.
- Details regarding production will be communicated in the coming months following the finalization of development plans.
- Once development plans are fully finalized, Pirelli will communicate more concrete information, including timing and scale.
- The Cyber Tyre is already being introduced through original equipment (OE) partnerships with select automakers.
- It is available on certain vehicles in markets including the U.S.
- At this stage, the Cyber Tyre technology is primarily tied to OEM collaborations.
- The Cyber Tyre is already being introduced on cars like Aston Martin and Pagani.
- Pirelli is working on additional OE projects with other car brands.
