## Facts

- Novelis Inc., a leading sustainable aluminum solutions provider and the world leader in aluminum rolling and recycling, announced that 10 of its manufacturing plants have achieved the first of four levels of certification in the Novelis Operating System (NOS) as of the end of fiscal year 2026.
- Emilio Braghi, chief operating officer of Novelis Inc., stated that having world-class operations is fundamental to the company's business and essential to exceeding customer expectations.
- Reaching the first level of NOS certification reflects Novelis' commitment to safe and consistent execution, operational reliability, and continuous improvement and innovation.
- The company customized the Novelis Operating System (NOS) specifically for the manufacturing requirements of aluminum rolling and recycling plants based on the principles of leading manufacturing operating systems.
- While Novelis has long had a continuous improvement focus, NOS standardizes this approach across its global manufacturing footprint.
- The Berea, Kentucky, United States plant earned first-level NOS certification in Novelis' recently concluded 2026 fiscal year.

- The Santo Andre, Brazil plant earned first-level NOS certification in Novelis' recently concluded 2026 fiscal year.

- The Yeongju, South Korea plant earned first-level NOS certification in Novelis' recently concluded 2026 fiscal year.
- Novelis plans to certify all of its 29 manufacturing facilities in NOS and advance them through the four progressive levels of NOS certification to further strengthen performance, reliability and service for customers worldwide.
