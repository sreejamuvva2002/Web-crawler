## Facts

- The Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) is seeking a director to run its Innovation and Technology Office.
- The director will be responsible for implementing and expanding a statewide technology and innovation-based economic development program.
- The program encompasses a full range of sales, marketing, business recruitment, business development, community development, and public relations activities.
- A bachelor's degree in the life sciences, engineering, or a closely related field is required for the position.
- A master's degree in these fields is preferred.
