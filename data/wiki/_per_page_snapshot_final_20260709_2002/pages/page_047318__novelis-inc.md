# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2026-03-05

**Source:** [Novelis resumes operations in Greensboro, Georgia - Recycling Today](https://www.recyclingtoday.com/news/novelis-resumes-operations-in-greensboro-georgia/)  ·  `page_047318`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. has resumed operations at its site in Greenboro, Georgia.
- The Greenboro plant was the site of an explosion and fire on Sunday, March 1.
- An explosion occurred in the plant, damaging a baghouse and cold-end equipment.
- No one was injured during the incident.
- The plant was shut down from Sunday evening until Wednesday, March 4.
- During the shutdown, employees assessed the situation and cleaned the affected areas.
- The plant restarted casting operations as of March 4.
- Novelis is investigating the cause of the incident.
- Customers are not expected to be significantly affected by the incident.
