# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-01-05

**Source:** [TCI Powder Coatings expands to Americus](https://www.walb.com/story/30888083/tci-powder-coatings-expands-to-americus/)  ·  `page_051178`

## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings has announced the opening of a new office in Americus, Georgia.
- The new office will function as corporate headquarters for the business.
- The facility will house the administrative, customer service, finance and information systems departments.
- Doug Greene, President, stated the addition allows accommodation of steady business growth.
- In the near future, TCI plans to add laboratory and training facilities, sample product preparation and light warehousing in the new facility.
- This expansion is part of a multi-year strategy to improve capabilities and provide innovative, premium solutions and service to customers.
- TCI expressed gratitude to the Americus-Sumter Payroll Development Authority for the work done to make the new facility possible.
- TCI has been part of the community for 28 years as of the article's publication.
- The company expects to open the office in February, 2016.
- The office will initially have approximately 20 employees.
- The facility is expected to accommodate up to 75 full time employees.
- Barbara Grogan, Executive Director of the Payroll Development Authority, stated that supporting the expansion of local and regional industries is critical to Sumter County's economic development strategy.
- Grogan noted that growth of long-term companies such as TCI Powder Coatings benefits Sumter County, particularly through their ability to hire locally from the existing workforce.
- The Development Authority partnered with TCI and will continue to support their expansion needs.
- For 28 years, TCI has been manufacturing powder coatings and dedicating itself to developing superior thermoset powder coatings for customers.
