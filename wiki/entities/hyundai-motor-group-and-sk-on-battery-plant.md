# Hyundai Motor Group & SK On Battery Plant

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Bartow Centre industrial site, Highway 411, Bartow County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Hyundai Motor Group and SK On are constructing a new lithium-ion battery manufacturing facility in Bartow County, Georgia, investing approximately $4-5 billion, creating more than 3,500 jobs, and slated to begin operations in 2025.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The plant will supply battery cells to automotive manufacturers across the United States, strengthening Georgia’s electric-vehicle ecosystem.
