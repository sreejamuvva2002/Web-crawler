# Seohan Auto Georgia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-09

**Source:** [Seohan Auto Georgia is Open for Business in Liberty County - Middle Georgia CEO](https://middlegeorgiaceo.com/news/2024/09/seohan-auto-georgia-open-business-liberty-county-news/)  ·  `page_059440`

## Facts

- Seohan Auto Georgia held a Grand Opening Ceremony for its new manufacturing facility in the Tradeport East Business Center on Friday, September 6th, 2024.
- The new facility will produce H/shafts, axles, and brake systems for Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs).
- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America is a customer for the facility's products.
- Operations at the Liberty County site will create at least 180 new jobs.
- The project represents an investment of more than $72 million in Liberty County.
- Georgia Department of Economic Development Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that community investment in the Tradeport East Business Center allowed Seohan Auto Georgia to meet a challenging timeline to get up and running.
- State Representative Al Williams, LCDA Board Chairman, noted that the effort to locate the facility began in November 2022 and required winning the bid against other considered locations.
- Korean, state, and local officials joined the grand opening ceremony.
- Seohan Group leadership in attendance included Chairman Yong Suk Kim, President of Sales Headquarters Yong Jun Kim, CEO Jung Kee Koo, Vice Chairman Woon Dae Paek, and Seohan Auto USA President Myon Han.
- Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) officials in attendance included COO Sydne Smith, Deputy Commissioner Kristi Brigman, and Commissioner Pat Wilson.
- Liberty County Development Authority (LCDA) Board members in attendance included Chairman State Representative Al Williams, Luke Moses, Melissa Carter Ray, Mayor Karl Riles, Willa Lewis, Marcus Sack, and Liberty County Commission Chairman Donald Lovett.
