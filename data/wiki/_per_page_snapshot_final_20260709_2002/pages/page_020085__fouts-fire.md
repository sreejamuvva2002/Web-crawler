## Facts

- Fire Service Inc adds Fouts Fire to their list of trusted fire truck manufacturers.
- Fire Service Inc currently sells E-ONE, KME, Ferrara (WI ONLY), and Fouts Fire.
- Fouts Fire is family-owned and operated since 1952.
- Fouts builds trucks that fight fires in every state in the continental U.S.
- Fouts Fire is based out of a 700,000 square foot facility in Milledgeville, GA.
- Fouts Fire operates a second facility in La Grange, TX.
- Unlike other American fire apparatus manufacturers, Fouts Fire's business model is based on a standardized line of trucks honed and perfected over many years.
- Fouts Fire's repeatable processes allow them to build more fire trucks for less money and deliver them quickly.
- Fouts Fire has deep supplier partnerships cemented over seven decades of heavy-duty manufacturing and fabrication experience.
- Fouts Fire's supply line is always full, enabling delivery even when competitors cannot deliver a truck until the year after next.
- In 1952, CJ Fouts and his brother Jerry bought a Sinclair service station in Smyrna, GA, founding Fouts Bros Inc.
- The brothers invested in auto and truck dealerships, commercial body manufacturing, real estate, and fire trucks.
- Fouts Bros is now in the hands of the third generation of the family.
- Fouts Fire is dedicated to offering the best value fire apparatus in the country.
- Fouts Fire's product lineup includes industry-leading 3000 gallon tankers and the newest product, the Fouts FOUR.
- Fouts Fire's production facilities have expanded and its engineering standards are cutting-edge.
- Fouts Fire's core values are service, quality, and family first.
- Seventy years after founding, Fouts Bros remains family-owned and family-operated.
