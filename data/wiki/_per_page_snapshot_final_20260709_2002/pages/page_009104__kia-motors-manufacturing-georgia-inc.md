# Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc.

## Facts

- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia has an estimated annual revenue of $1.3 billion.
- The company's estimated revenue per employee is $462,000.
- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia employs a total of 2,826 people.
- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc. (KMMG) is the first manufacturing site in North America for Seoul-based Kia Motors Corporation.
- The facility occupies 2,200 acres in West Point, Georgia.
- KMMG began mass production on November 16, 2009.
- The plant produces the 2013 Sorento CUV and the 2013 Optima mid-size sedan, which are two of Kia's top-selling models.
- Beginning in 2012, KMMG has an annual production capacity of 360,000 vehicles using U.S. and globally-sourced parts.
- The state of Georgia offered Kia Motors more than $400 million in incentives or funding.
- In March 2022, it was reported that Aspen Aerogels is building a $325 million manufacturing plant near Statesboro in Bulloch County, Georgia.
