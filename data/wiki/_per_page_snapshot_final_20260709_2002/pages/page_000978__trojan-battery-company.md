# Trojan Battery Company

## Facts

- Trojan Battery Company is the leading brand of flooded lead acid, AES AGM, standard AGM, and lithium-ion batteries.
- Trojan products are the standard for motive applications including golf carts, LSVs, floor machines, boats, RVs, work platforms, industrial equipment, solar and renewable energy, and more.
- Trojan has manufactured high-performing, innovative deep cycle batteries since 1925.
- The company was founded by George Godber and Carl Speer.
- In 1952, Trojan developed the first golf cart battery for the Autoette vehicle, pioneering deep cycle battery technology for the golf cart industry.
- Trojan is headquartered in Santa Fe Springs, California.
- Trojan operates three ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing plants located in California and Georgia.
- Trojan operates two advanced research and development centers dedicated exclusively to deep cycle battery technologies.
- Trojan maintains international offices across the Americas, Europe, Africa, Middle East, and Asia.
- Trojan maintains a global worldwide network of master distributors.
- Trojan is a proud member of the Battery Council International (BCI) and the Advanced Lead Acid Battery Consortium.
- Trojan applies rigorous industry battery testing procedures that adhere to BCI and International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC) test standards.
- In 2018, Trojan Battery Company was acquired by C&D Technologies.
- The acquisition merged two iconic brands, both leading specialty battery manufacturers with over 100 years of market leadership.
- The partnership between Trojan and C&D Technologies features global distribution and has created one of the world's largest energy storage providers.
- Trojan offers a wide range of deep cycle flooded, AGM, gel, and lithium-ion battery solutions.
- Quality Golf Carts in South Carolina uses the Trojan OnePack for lithium battery golf cart conversions.
- Over nearly 100 years, Trojan batteries have earned awards for innovation, efficiency, technology, performance, reliability, sustainability, safety, and value.
- Trojan is committed to continuous process improvement, reliability, world-class service and support for dealers, distributors, OEMs, and consumers.
- The Maroon Standard has set the pace since the first electric golf cart was turned on, ensuring Trojan products are built to never falter as technologies change.
