# Nisshinbo Automotive Manufacturing, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-09-04

**Source:** [Nisshinbo Automotive Manufacturing, Inc. to create 100 jobs in Covington, GA - Location Georgia](https://www.locationgeorgia.com/2018/09/04/nisshinbo-automotive-manufacturing-inc-to-create-100-jobs-in-newton-county/)  ·  `page_058265`

## Facts

- Gov. Nathan Deal announced that Nisshinbo Automotive Manufacturing, Inc. (NAMI) will create 100 jobs and invest $72 million in an expansion of its Covington facility.
- The facility expansion is located in Newton County, Georgia.
- NAMI is a leading manufacturer and supplier of environmentally friendly brake pads.
- Governor Nathan Deal stated that Georgia provides the business climate, skilled workforce, and thriving automotive sector necessary for manufacturers like NAMI to enjoy long-term growth.
- The Covington facility expansion allows NAMI to continue benefiting from close proximity to top vehicle manufacturers while reaching customers across the nation and around the world using Georgia’s logistics infrastructure.
- Governor Nathan Deal noted that NAMI’s commitment directly reflects the strength of Georgia’s manufacturing environment for global industry leaders and appreciated NAMI’s investment over the last 20 years.
- NAMI has maintained a presence in Newton County for more than two decades.
- Along with its business development center in Sterling Heights, Michigan, NAMI’s Newton County facility produces high-quality friction materials for automotive manufacturers.
- The Newton County facility develops environmentally friendly, copper-free brake pads.
- New jobs at the expanded facility will include positions in production and administration.
- Select Newton Vice President of Economic Development David Bernd stated that Nisshinbo has been an indispensable partner in Covington for 20 years.
- David Bernd highlighted that the project represents more than $70 million in Foreign Direct Investment for 100 well-paying advanced manufacturing careers.
