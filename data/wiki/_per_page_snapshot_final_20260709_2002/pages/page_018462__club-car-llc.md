# Club Car, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-16

**Source:** [Club Car: Vice president Mark Rickell talks tariffs, new products](https://www.firstcallgolf.com/industry-news/release/2025-04-16/club-car-vice-president-talks-tariffs-new-products-and-more-in-news-broadcast)  ·  `page_018462`

## Facts

- Club Car is a global leader in innovation and design for golf cars, low-speed utility vehicles, and personal-use transportation.
- Mark Rickell serves as Vice President of Sales at Club Car and was featured in The Means Report news broadcast on April 16, 2024.
- In 2024, American manufacturers in the low-speed personal transport vehicle (LSPTV) industry petitioned the International Trade Commission to address illegal dumping and subsidies on LSPTVs imported from China.
- Club Car joined a petition to the ITC and Department of Commerce targeting illegally subsidized products dumped into the U.S. market in violation of commerce and trade laws.
- Rickell stated that while the petition saw success, the company must remain vigilant as competing companies try to evade tariffs.
- Club Car manufactures its vehicles to automotive standards and utilizes road load simulators to test every aspect of product safety and reliability.
- The company recently previewed the Onward LSV and new Tempo upgrades as latest additions to its product line for recreational and commercial users.
- Club Car's product portfolio has expanded from golf fleet operations to include vehicles for commercial, personal, and street-legal low-speed use.
- The Club Car Vehicle Group includes the luxury electric vehicle brand Garia and the lightweight electric utility vehicle brand Melex.
- Club Car is headquartered in Augusta, Georgia, with its corporate address at 4125 Washington Road, Evans, GA 30809.
- Lynthia Ross serves as Communications Leader for Club Car, LLC.
- The Club Car Championship at The Landings Golf & Athletic Club, a Korn Ferry Tour event, was held April 3-6, 2025.
