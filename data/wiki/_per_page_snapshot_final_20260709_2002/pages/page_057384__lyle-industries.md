# Lyle Industries

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Lyle Industries is a premier supplier of automotive textile carpet for molded flooring, interior trim, and floor mats.
- The company serves OEM, Aftermarket, and Accessory markets for tufting, precision cutting, and engineered finishing solutions.
- Lyle Industries has 34.5 million square yards of annual tufting capacity for automotive and specialty markets.
- Production utilizes multiple gauge configurations including 1/10 Gauge, 5/64 Gauge, loop machines, high-speed creel, and beam systems tuned to OEM specifications.
- The company uses Lectra CNC and press-based cutting systems, specifically the Bruno FH350 Ton Press, Bruno FH350T Press, and Freeman Schwabe 300 Ton Press, for dimensional accuracy.
- Manufacturing processes include advanced latex and polyethylene coating tailored to exact performance requirements.
- The company implements thermal bonding, moisture barriers, and specialty finishes configured to application and market specifications.
- Products are engineered to meet durability and compliance standards for passenger cars, light trucks, and heavy transport vehicles.
- Lyle Industries is certified to ISO 9001:2015 with operations structured around documented quality systems and traceability.
- The company actively evaluates material inputs and production methods to reduce waste, improve recyclability, and align with OEM sustainability requirements.
- Lyle Industries sponsors charities supporting healthcare, youth vocation, and community improvement in its operating regions.
- The company operates as a Georgia employer committed to safe working conditions, fair labor practices, and contributing to the Dalton region's manufacturing workforce.
- Lyle Industries was founded in 1981 by Lamar and Loutrelle Lyle.
- The company is headquartered in Dalton, Georgia.
- The leadership team brings decades of automotive textile manufacturing expertise and deep industry relationships.
