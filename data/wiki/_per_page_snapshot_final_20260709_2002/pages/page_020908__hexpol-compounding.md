## Facts

- Originally manufactured tread rubber products.
- Has grown into a manufacturer of various rubber compounds for use in such markets as textile, belting, sporting goods and industrial.
- Headquartered in Tallapoosa, Ga.
- Maintains manufacturing facilities in Georgia, Alabama and Tennessee.
