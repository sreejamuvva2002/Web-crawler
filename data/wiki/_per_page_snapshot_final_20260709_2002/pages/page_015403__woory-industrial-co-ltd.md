# Woory Industrial Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-02

**Source:** [Woory Industrial Company Opening New Facility In Georgia, Creating Over 130 New Jobs And Investing $18 Million | Doc and the Block](https://docandtheblock.com/woory-industrial-company-opening-new-facility-in-georgia-creating-over-130-new-jobs-and-investing-18-million/)  ·  `page_015403`

## Facts

- Republican Georgia Governor Brian Kemp announced that Woory Industrial Co., Ltd., an automotive parts manufacturer, would be opening a new manufacturing facility in Dublin, Georgia.
- The new facility will create over 130 new jobs.
- The company will invest $18 million in Dublin-Laurens County.
- Woory Industrial Company specializes in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning components and systems for the automotive industry.
- Woory has recently begun concentrating on the development of new products for electric vehicles (EVs) and hydrogen-fueled cars.
- Jungwoo Kim serves as the CEO of Woory Industrial Company.
- The company's new facility, located at 404 Kellam Road in Dublin, will be its first U.S. manufacturing location.
- The facility will produce electrical heaters, control units, and actuators for EV and internal combustion carmakers in North America.
- Operations at the new facility are expected to begin in November 2023.
- Unlike internal combustion engine vehicles, electric vehicles do not have a heating source, requiring dedicated heaters for cabin heating and battery temperature maintenance.
- Woory's pioneering development of electric heaters over the last 10 years has established it as a leader in this growing market segment.
- From July 1 through December 31, 2022, the automotive industry was the second-largest source of new jobs and investment in Georgia.
- Since 2020, Georgia's pursuit of job creation along the entire EV supply chain has resulted in over $22.7 billion in investment and the creation of more than 28,400 jobs.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Woory Industrial is helping Georgia on the path to becoming the e-mobility capital of the nation.
- CEO Jungwoo Kim stated that the transition to electric vehicles is the future and that the U.S. EV market is the key market among all automotive markets.
- CEO Jungwoo Kim added that Georgia offers a good opportunity for Woory to advance as part of the vanguard of EV development.
