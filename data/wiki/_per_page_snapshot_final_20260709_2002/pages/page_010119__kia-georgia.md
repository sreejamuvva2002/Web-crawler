## Facts

- Kia Georgia officially began production of the all-new 2023 Sportage subcompact SUV at its facility in West Point, Georgia on Tuesday, February 2, 2022.
- The 2023 Sportage is Kia's longest-running nameplate and has undergone its most significant changes to date as part of Kia's "Movement that inspires" transformation initiative.
- The celebratory first vehicle off the assembly line was jungle green in color with the X-Pro Prestige trim package.
- Sean Yoon, president and CEO for Kia North America, introduced the new production run at the facility.
- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp drove the inaugural 2023 Sportage off the West Point assembly line.
- James Watson, vice president of production for Kia Georgia, also drove the first vehicle off the production line.
