## Facts

- Located at 2102 Old Savannah Rd Augusta, GA 30906.
- Also known as Morgan Thermal Ceramics Inc and Thermal Ceramics Inc.
- In 1988, became a subsidiary of the Morgan Crucible Company of Windsor, England.
- Linked to a global network of refractory and insulation companies.
- Provides insulation systems for industrial, transportation, appliance, and fire protection markets.
- Manufactures blankets, block insulation, ceramic fiber modules, fired refractory shapes, fire protection duct systems, firebricks, insulating firebricks, micro porous insulation, mortars, paper and felt, pumpables, coatings, mixes, textiles, and refractory castables.
- Acquired an additional line of advanced thermal management products and systems that include Min-K Microporous Silica Material, RPC Vacuum Formed shapes, Thermic Refractories, Structural Board Products, MRI Fired Shapes, and the Manville US Fiber Operations.
- Holds its main plant and headquarters in Augusta, Georgia.
