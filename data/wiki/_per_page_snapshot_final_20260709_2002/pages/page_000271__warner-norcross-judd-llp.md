## Facts

- Warner Norcross + Judd LLP provides proactive legal counsel to auto suppliers throughout Detroit, North America, and globally.
- The firm adheres to a "Supplier First and Supplier Only" policy, meaning it does not represent original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) on supply chain disputes.
- Warner has served as antitrust counsel for MEMA Original Equipment Suppliers (formerly OESA) for more than a decade.
- The firm represents over 200 domestic and international auto suppliers conducting business in the U.S.
- Tom Manganello serves as co-chair of the Warner automotive industry group and is active in the formation of MICHAuto.
- Warner's attorneys handle motor vehicle safety regulations, including TREAD Act compliance, NHTSA reporting requirements, defect investigations, and recall requirements.
- The firm provides legal analysis on EV industry developments, including updates titled "The Future is Electric, but What About the Charging Stations?" and "ITC Backs LG Chem in EV Battery Dispute".
- Warner addresses supplier risks related to prototype testing, citing content on "Tesla Model 3: The Decision to Skip Prototype Testing Means Added Risk for Suppliers".
- The firm tracks tariff impacts on the auto industry, including IEEPA tariff refund litigation, Section 301 tariffs, and Section 232 calculations.

- The firm provides legal services for automotive supplier bankruptcy, commercial litigation, contract drafting, and dispute resolution.
- Warner's attorneys advise on environmental compliance including air quality regulations (NSR, PSD, NSPS, NESHAPs), brownfield redevelopment, hazardous waste management, and Superfund litigation.
