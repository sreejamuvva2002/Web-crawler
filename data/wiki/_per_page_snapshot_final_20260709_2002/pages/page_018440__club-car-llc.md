# Club Car, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-10

**Source:** [Club Car Launches Lithium Option for Street-Legal CRU Vehicle](https://www.tmcnet.com/usubmit/2025/07/10/10222221.htm)  ·  `page_018440`

## Facts

- Club Car, headquartered in Augusta, Georgia, announced the launch of its all-new CRU Lithium option for its street-legal CRU Neighborhood Electric Vehicle (NEV).
- The CRU Lithium NEV features an advanced lithium-ion battery that delivers faster charging, lighter weight, longer range, and improved power.
- Brant Mitchell, Senior Director of Product Management of Club Car, stated that the CRU Lithium combines style, comfort, and social design with lithium performance for a smarter, longer-lasting power solution.
- The CRU Lithium NEV is available now through authorized Club Car dealers.
- Club Car has over six decades of history in superior design and performance and is known for quality craftsmanship ensuring long-term reliability.
- The Club Car product portfolio includes vehicles for commercial, personal, and street-legal low-speed use.
- The Club Car Vehicle Group also includes the luxury electric vehicle brand Garia and the lightweight electric utility vehicle brand Melex.
