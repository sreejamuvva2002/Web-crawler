# LG Energy Solution-Honda Joint Venture EV Battery Plant

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-12-29

**Source:** [Honda to acquire LG Energy share of Fayette Co. battery plant in $2.85 billion deal - Record Herald](https://www.recordherald.com/2025/12/29/honda-to-acquire-lg-energy-share-of-fayette-co-battery-plant-in-2-85-billion-deal/)  ·  `page_008939`

## Facts

- LG Energy Solution will sell its electric vehicle battery production plant to Honda Development and Manufacturing of America.
- The plant is located on the Jefferson Township mega-site in Fayette County, Ohio.
- The facility was previously a joint venture between LG Energy Solution and Honda.
- Through a sale-leaseback arrangement, the plant will continue operations.
- The $2.85 billion sale includes the building and related assets.
- The transaction is scheduled to close in February 2026.
- Honda previously purchased 454 acres from Martin Land Co. for $23.3 million in November 2022.
- The investments are concentrated in Jefferson Township, west of State Route 729 and south of Interstate 71.
- Fayette County Commissioner Tony Anderson stated that Honda had no specific obligation to inform the county of the purchase.
- Commissioner Anderson noted the county's primary role regarding the site was providing infrastructure for water and the water treatment plant.
