# Honda Development & Manufacturing of America, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Honda is seeking qualified individuals with diverse backgrounds to join its team through the Engineering Development Program (EDP) targeting 2026 graduates.
- The EDP is administered by Honda Development & Manufacturing of America, LLC (HDMA).
- Over the last 25 years, HDMA's EDP has served as a critical program for developing entry-level engineers.
- The program duration is one to two years and requires participants to rotate through various departments and roles throughout HDMA.
- Successful graduates of the EDP will return to a designated "Home" department role after completing their rotations.
- Program openings are available across multiple locations, including Tallapoosa, Georgia.
- Quality Engineering positions are explicitly available in Ohio and Georgia.
- Manufacturing Engineers lead, create, and implement technical activities and solutions across manufacturing operations while monitoring equipment and processes to ensure optimal performance and minimize operating expenses.
- Equipment/Tooling Design Engineers design manufacturing equipment including stamping, hardware & software, and die & mold to ensure efficiency and stability for new models and technology introduction.
- Materials Development Engineers focus on researching and developing resins, fabrics, and other non-metal materials within the Organics Group, specifically seeking materials or chemical engineering majors.
- Safety Engineers apply scientific and practical knowledge to innovate, design, and improve systems, processes, equipment, and tools to align with daily operations, new models, and business plans.
- Data Analytics Engineers review multiple data sources to evaluate, summarize, and draw conclusions identifying trends that lead to countermeasure activity across quality, delivery, safety, and cost information.
- Applicants must hold a bachelor's degree from an ABET or ATMAE accredited program in Mechanical, Electrical, Industrial, or other equivalent engineering fields.
- Candidates are preferred to have 3 months of co-op or internship experience in a relevant environment.
- Eligible candidates must have less than 2 years of engineering work experience.
- Honda does not provide sponsorship for employment visa status, including F-1 CPT/OPT, H-1B, and TN visas.
- Compensation includes a competitive base salary based on variables like geographic location and work experience, paid overtime, and regional bonuses when applicable.
- Benefits include industry-leading medical, dental, vision, and prescription plans, paid time off for vacation, holidays, and shutdowns, and company-paid short-term and long-term disability.
- The company offers a 401K plan with a company match plus an additional contribution, along with relocation assistance if eligible.
- Career growth offerings include advancement opportunities, career mobility, education reimbursement for continued learning, and training and development programs.
- Additional employee offerings include tuition assistance, student loan repayment, lifestyle accounts, childcare reimbursement, elder care support, wellbeing programs, community service engagement, product programs, and free drinks onsite.
- Honda's corporate vision targets 2030 goals focused on innovation, achieving a "zero environmental footprint," and realizing the "joy and freedom of mobility."
