# Aurubis AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2021-11-12

**Source:** [This Morning in Metals: Aurubis AG to invest €300M in U.S. metals recycling plant - MetalMiner](https://agmetalminer.com/2021/11/12/this-morning-in-metals-aurubus-ag-to-invest-e300m-in-u-s-metals-recycling-plant/)  ·  `page_017377`

## Facts

- German firm Aurubis AG announced plans to construct a new metals recycling plant in the U.S.
- Aurubis AG will invest €300 million in the new facility located in Georgia.
- The facility will be the first ever secondary smelter for multimetal recycling in the United States.
- The plant will process circuit boards, copper cable, and other recycling materials containing metals into 35,000 t of blister copper annually.
- Aurubis will further process the intermediate products into various industrial and precious metals at its European smelter sites to a great extent, but also sell them directly in the US market.
- The facility will have an annual capacity of 90,000 tons of complex recycling materials.
- Aurubis plans to begin construction on the plant in 2022.
- Aurubis plans to commission the plant in the first half of 2024.
