## Facts

- Pak-Lite, Inc. was founded in Atlanta, Georgia by Frank Pritchard in 1972 as a manufacturer of protective packaging products.
- The company has manufacturing facilities in GA, NC, and Taiwan.
- PLI has over 300+ dedicated professionals committed to making customer projects a success before, during, and after the design and manufacturing of your product.
- Pak-Lite is positioned as a leading North American converter/manufacturer of US-sourced foams, rubber, nonwovens, foils, films, and specialty materials.
- The company's customer base includes many of the top OEM and industrial companies in the world.
- Manufacturing capabilities include extrusion lamination to foams and rubber substrates.
- Pak-Lite combines materials to composite form and supplies die-cut parts, components, roll stock, or sheeting.
- Finished parts often include pressure-sensitive adhesives and release liners.
- Additional capabilities include material selection, prototyping, skiving/slitting, rotary/flatbed die cutting, kiss cutting, spooling, precision parts manufacturing, assembly, and much more.
- Pak-Lite is ISO 9001:2015 and IATF 16949 certified.
- The company provides a wide selection of capabilities in converting foams, rubbers, and nonwoven fabrics for the OEMs and Tier 1 automotive industry.
- PLI assists customers in designing exact components to meet demanding specifications.
- The company has extensive experience with 3M Adhesives and Avery Adhesives.
- Pak-Lite sources top foam, rubber, polymer, packaging, nonwoven, and fiber and film suppliers worldwide.
