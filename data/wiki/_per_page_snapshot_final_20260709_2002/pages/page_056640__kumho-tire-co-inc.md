# Kumho Tire Co. Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-03-31

**Source:** [Kumho Tire of Georgia Expansion - MBCIA](https://mbcia.com/industry/kumho-tire-of-georgia-expansion/)  ·  `page_056640`

## Facts

- Kumho Tire approved a $21.8 million investment to add approximately 20,500 square feet to Kumho Tire of Georgia (KTG) for a new Automated Production Unit (APU).
- The new APU expansion is designed to boost product quality and manufacturing efficiency at the KTG facility.
- The expansion announcement was made at the Greater Macon Chamber of Commerce’s State of the Community luncheon by Macon-Bibb Mayor Lester Miller.
- The investment will provide KTG with an additional annual production capacity of 500,000 tires.
- The groundbreaking for the KTG expansion is scheduled for May 2021.
- Keith Lolley, Director of Human Resources at Kumho Tire Georgia, commented on the positive impact of the expansion for employees and the Macon-Bibb community.
- Macon-Bibb County Mayor Lester Miller highlighted Kumho's recognition of the community as a favorable business location and emphasized the county's economic development strategy of supporting existing industries.
- Kumho simultaneously approved a $305 million expansion for its Vietnam plant, which is expected to double that facility's capacity.
- The investments in both KTG and the Vietnam plant are part of Kumho's corporate strategy to increase production capacity ahead of growing demand.
- Kumho Tire President and CEO II Taik Jung stated that increasing production capacity aims to ensure product availability for customers and support continued growth partnerships in the North American market.
- The Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority originally brought Kumho Tire’s first North American manufacturing facility to Macon-Bibb County in May 2016.
- The original KTG facility represented a $600 million investment across approximately 1,000,000 square feet and generated over 400 new jobs.
- Kumho Tire USA is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, and serves as the U.S. sales, marketing, product development, and distribution arm of Kumho Tire Co. Inc.
- Kumho Tire currently operates eight manufacturing facilities globally: one in the U.S., three in Korea, three in China, and one in Vietnam.
