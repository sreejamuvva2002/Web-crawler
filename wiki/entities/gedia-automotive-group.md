# GEDIA Automotive Group

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** Dalton, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

German auto supplier GEDIA Automotive Group plans to build an $85 million parts plant near Dalton, Georgia, targeting the growing electric-vehicle market and aiming to supply Mercedes-Benz and potentially Volkswagen.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The project is explicitly aimed at serving the expanding electric-vehicle market by providing parts for EV manufacturers Mercedes-Benz and possibly Volkswagen.
