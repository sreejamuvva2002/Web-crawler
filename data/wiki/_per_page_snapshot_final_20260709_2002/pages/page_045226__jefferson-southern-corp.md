# Jefferson Southern Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2013-07-24

**Source:** [Jefferson Southern to expand in Georgia](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/shopmanagement/jefferson-southern-to-expand-in-georgia)  ·  `page_045226`

## Facts

- Jefferson Southern Corp. is a Tier 1 supplier of structural steel parts to Honda.
- Jefferson Southern Corp. announced plans for a $22.8 million expansion of its production facility in Rockmart, Ga.
- The announcement was made on July 24, 2013.
- The expansion will add 81,000 sq. ft. to the plant.
- The expansion includes new presses and an expanded recycling system.
- The expansion will create 40 new jobs over the next two years.
