## Facts

- Volvo Trucks North America is executing what it describes as its largest investment program in its North American history.
- The investment plan involves more than two billion dollars for new products.
- Four hundred million dollars will be allocated to modernize an assembly plant in Virginia.
- Seven hundred million dollars will fund a new factory in Monterrey, Mexico.
- The new Mexican facility is expected to start low-volume production in the summer of 2026.
- Company leadership stated that the new Mexican plant adds capacity and does not replace existing U.S. operations.
- A fleet leasing provider based in Chattanooga placed an order for 400 units of a specific sleeper truck model.
- The Chattanooga order is noted as one of the largest single orders for this new flagship model to date.
- Company officials stated that the order reflects fleet confidence in the platform.
- The new truck model has accumulated over fifteen million customer miles, with thousands of units now in commercial service.
- The company confirms the trucks are delivering a ten percent improvement in fuel efficiency compared to previous models.
- Leadership calculated that at current diesel prices, these fuel savings can directly increase a carrier's operating margin.
