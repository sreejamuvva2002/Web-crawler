## Facts

- Denkai America Inc. is located in South Carolina, United States.
- Denkai America Inc. has 11-50 employees.
- Denkai America manufactures high-quality electrodeposited copper foils for printed circuit board (PCB), industrial, and energy storage applications.
- Denkai America has over 40 years of experience manufacturing copper foil with the highest level of quality and service.
- Copper foils are electroplated on revolving titanium drums, which is unique for the ability to deposit copper foils with a high-quality surface.
- Rolls of the plated foils are subjected to a variety of surface treating operations specially designed for a wide range of end-use requirements.
- During treating, copper dendrites are deposited onto the foil surface to enhance bonding to various resins systems during lamination.
- A barrier-layer is deposited for promoting reliability in thermal applications.
- Specific proprietary organic layers are applied to promote chemical adhesion.
- Foils are stain-proofed to prevent oxidation in shipping, storage, lamination, and subsequent customer processing.
