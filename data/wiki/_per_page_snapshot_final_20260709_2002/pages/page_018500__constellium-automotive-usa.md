# Constellium Automotive USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-06

## Facts

- Constellium Automotive USA is significantly reducing its workforce, with dozens of employees set to lose their jobs later this summer.
- A notice filed with the Georgia Department of Labor indicates that Constellium Automotive USA, located on Constellium Drive in White, plans to eliminate 57 positions from its workforce of 99 employees.
- The filing classifies the action as a permanent reduction in force.
- The layoffs will affect workers across multiple departments and job classifications, including operators, material handlers, maintenance technicians, operations supervisors, shift supervisors, quality management personnel, and environmental health and safety staff.
- According to the WARN notice, employee separations are scheduled to begin on August 1.
- Constellium Automotive USA has operated in the Highland 75 Industrial Park since opening its facility in 2017.
- The plant manufactures lightweight aluminum structural components and crash management systems used by automotive manufacturers throughout the Southeast and across North America.
- The company is part of Constellium N.V., an international aluminum producer with headquarters in France and the Netherlands.
- Constellium focuses on the design, engineering, manufacturing, and recycling of aluminum products serving the aerospace, automotive, and packaging sectors.
