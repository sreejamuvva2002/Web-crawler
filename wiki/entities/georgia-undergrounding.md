# Georgia Undergrounding

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Cartersville, GA, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Georgia Undergrounding provides underground utility and infrastructure construction services in Cartersville, GA using tunnel construction, horizontal auger boring, and Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD). The company installs pipelines for electric power, fiber optic, sewer, water, oil, gas, and other utilities without disturbing the surface.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Provides underground utility construction for electric power lines and related infrastructure that support electric vehicle charging networks and grid reliability in Georgia.

## Key facts

- Over 25 years of directional drilling experience, having bored and installed utility pipelines under trees, city roads, bridges, railway tracks, and rivers.
- Uses state-of-the-art horizontal directional drilling (HDD) machines for commercial, residential, and municipal projects.
- Directional boring offers lower cost, reduced traffic disruption, shorter project completion times, deeper pipe installations, and environmental safety benefits.
- Drilling fluid is classified as non-toxic and can be disposed of accordingly.
- Provides underground utility construction for electric power, fiber optic, sewer, water, oil, gas, petroleum, steam, and irrigation crossings.
- Underground boring preserves existing landscape integrity and reduces noise compared with open-cut excavation.
