## Facts

- A French auto parts maker will build a $225 million manufacturing plant in McAllen, according to city leaders.
- Valeo is based out of Paris, France.
- Valeo is a leading automotive technology company with operations in more than 30 countries worldwide.
- The 200,000-square-foot facility will be built near Ware Road and Idela Avenue.
- The facility is expected to be fully operational by January 2027.
- The facility is expected to create up to 500 new jobs.
- The facility will produce transformative automotive technology that advances the future of mobility and strengthens domestic manufacturing capacity.
- A news release stated that Valeo's decision to invest in McAllen represents a significant expansion of advanced manufacturing in South Texas.
- The investment reinforces McAllen's position within the regional and binational automotive supply chain.
- Valeo North America President Jeffrey Shay stated that McAllen offers a highly skilled workforce, a strategic location within the North American automotive corridor, and strong collaboration among local and regional partners.
- The investment will support customers with advanced, innovative technologies while contributing to the continued growth of the U.S. automotive industry.
