# IFM Energy

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Atlanta, Georgia, United States

## Overview

IFM Energy provides professional electric vehicle (EV) charging station installation services for businesses throughout Metro Atlanta, offering Level 2 and DC Fast Charging solutions, fleet electrification, and load management systems.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Provider of EV charging infrastructure installation in Georgia, supporting the growth of electric vehicle adoption across commercial sites.

## Workforce & partners

- MBE/DBE Certification — Certification program enabling diversity compliance for government contracts and corporate supplier diversity initiatives.. IFM Energy is MBE/DBE certified, helping Atlanta businesses meet diversity requirements.

## Key facts

- Provides Level 2 (240V) and DC Fast Charging stations for workplaces, apartment complexes, retail locations, and fleet operations.
- Helps businesses attract eco-conscious customers, meet sustainability goals, and comply with Atlanta’s green building initiatives.
- Offers Level 2 charging (8-12 hour full charge) and DC Fast Charging (30-60 minute charge).
- Provides fleet electrification multi-port installations for delivery vans, trucks, and service vehicles.
- Implements load management systems to minimize electrical infrastructure upgrades.
- Service areas include Fulton County, DeKalb County, Cobb County, Gwinnett County, Midtown Atlanta, Buckhead, Alpharetta, Marietta, and the Perimeter area.
- MBE/DBE certification assists clients in meeting diversity requirements for government contracts and corporate supplier-diversity programs.
- Nationally certified minority and disadvantaged business enterprise.
- 24/7 availability for emergency fuel delivery needs.
- Coast-to-coast nationwide coverage with local expertise in the Atlanta area.
- Competitive pricing with volume discounts and flexible payment terms.
