## Facts

- Dalton-Whitfield County in Northwest Georgia sits just outside major markets like Atlanta, GA and Chattanooga, TN located on I-75.
- Dalton is the Floor Covering Capital of the World.
- The area is home to data centers and companies that make automotive parts, solar panels, chemicals, plastics, and many other industries.
- The Greater Dalton community invests in its talent pipeline to provide skilled workers for today’s manufacturers.
- Notable companies started in Dalton include Shaw Industries, Inc. and Engineered Floors.
