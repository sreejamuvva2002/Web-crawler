# Power Solutions Inc.

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Georgia, United States

## Overview

Electrical contracting and facility maintenance company founded in 2000 that specializes in renewable energy, electrification, and EV charging solutions.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Provides EV charging solutions and promotes EV infrastructure through its she-EV initiatives.

## Timeline

- **2000** — Founded Power Solutions, Inc.
- **2015** — Launched Women Do Everything LLC
- **November 2019** — Featured in Forbes Japan
- **March 2020** — Recognized in the New York Times
- **2020** — Founded she-EV, Inc. during the pandemic
- **2021** — Named Minority Contractor of the Year by NAMC - Atlanta Chapter
- **2022** — Participated in Leadership Atlanta Class of 2022
- **2023** — Selected for Leadership America program by Leadership Women
- **2023** — Honored as one of the Top 100 Women in Construction
- **2024** — Launch of the she-EV Car Club

## Key facts

- Power Solutions, Inc. specializes in renewable energy, electrification, and EV charging solutions.
- The company has expanded its operations internationally.
- Tonya Hicks broke barriers as the first woman Journeyman Inside Wireman Electrician in IBEW Local 917.
- She was also the first Black Woman to become an IBEW Journeyman Electrician in Mississippi.
- Women Do Everything LLC trains and supports women in male-dominated industries through employment and entrepreneurship.
- she-EV, Inc. provides safer EV charging locations for women, elderly, and disabled persons.
- The she-EV Car Club, launched in 2024, offers community support, educational resources, and trusted products to members.
- Tonya Hicks serves as a Sustainability Ambassador for the City of Atlanta.
- She was named Minority Contractor of the Year by NAMC - Atlanta Chapter in 2021.
- Included in Project Drawdown’s Drawdown Neighborhood Series for Atlanta, GA.
- Recognized by Forbes Japan (Nov 2019) and New York Times (Mar 2020).
- Participated in Leadership Atlanta Class of 2022 and Leadership America program by Leadership Women in 2023.
