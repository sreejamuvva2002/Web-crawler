# Superior Essex Inc.

## Facts

- Superior Essex Inc. maintains its principal executive offices at 150 Interstate North Parkway, Atlanta, Georgia 30339.
- The company is a manufacturer and supplier of wire and cable products for the communications, energy, automotive, industrial, and commercial/residential end-markets.
- Superior Essex manufactures magnet wire, fabricated insulation products, and copper and fiber optic communications wire and cable products.
- The company distributes magnet wire, insulation, and related products to smaller original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and motor repair facilities.
- Superior Essex converts copper cathode to copper rod for internal consumption and for sale to other wire and cable manufacturers and OEMs.
- The company operates manufacturing facilities in the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, France, Germany, Portugal, Italy, Mexico and China.
