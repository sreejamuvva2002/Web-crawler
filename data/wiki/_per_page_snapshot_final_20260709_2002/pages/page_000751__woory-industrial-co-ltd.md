# Woory Industrial Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-07

**Source:** [EV parts manufacturer to supply Hyundai factory in Bryan County](https://www.savannahnow.com/story/business/automotive/2023/06/07/ev-parts-manufacturer-supply-hyundai-factory-bryan-county/70297280007/)  ·  `page_000751`

## Facts

- Woory Industrial Co., Ltd. is an EV parts manufacturer and Hyundai supplier coming to South Georgia.
- The company will build a new manufacturing facility in Dublin.
- Woory Industrial manufactures heating, ventilation, and air conditioning components and systems.
- The company will invest $18 million into the new manufacturing facility.
- The project will create more than 130 jobs.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp welcomed Woory Industrial as a job creator helping the state become the "e-mobility capital of the nation."
- Governor Kemp stated that the emerging industry continues to bring historic levels of new jobs and investment to communities across the state, thanks to a highly-skilled workforce and reliable infrastructure network.
- Governor Kemp expressed confidence that Woory will have a long and beneficial partnership with Dublin-Laurens County.
