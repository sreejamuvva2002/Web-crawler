# Continental Tire the Americas, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-05-01

**Source:** [Continental Announces Leadership Transition in its Replacement Tires Business in the Americas | Continental Tire](https://continentaltire.com/learn/continental-announces-leadership-transition-its-replacement-tires-business-americas)  ·  `page_018873`

## Facts

- Dr. Jochen Etzel will retire as CEO of Continental Tire the Americas, LLC, after a more than 30-years career with Continental.
- Tansu Işık will assume the CEO role on May 1, 2025.
- The leadership transition was announced in Hanover, Germany, and Fort Mill, S.C., USA, on March 18, 2025.
- Tansu Işık will succeed Dr. Etzel as the new Head of Continental’s Business Area Replacement Tires the Americas.
- Işık will report directly to Christian Koetz, Executive Board Member of Continental and Head of Continental Tires.
- Jochen Etzel has served in this capacity since 2011.
- Etzel played an important role in uniting various Continental tire activities in the Americas region and strengthening their competitive position.
- Etzel started his career as a lawyer in Continental’s corporate law department.
- Etzel later took over a financial manager position responsible for developing the company’s tire business in regions outside of Europe.
- Etzel previously led Continental’s corporate Mergers and Acquisitions team.
- Christian Koetz thanked Etzel on behalf of the entire Tires Board for his leadership, dedication, and outstanding contributions throughout his tenure.
- Tansu Işık brings more than 20 years of international experience in marketing, supply chain, strategy, and key account management within Continental’s tires business.
- Işık began his career as a Business Development Specialist in Istanbul, Türkiye.
- Işık moved to Continental Tires’ Headquarters in Hanover, Germany, to become Head of Pricing for the Europe, Middle East and Africa (EMEA) region.
- Işık then moved to Shanghai, China, as Director and later Vice President for Key Account Management (OE) China and the Asia-Pacific region (APAC).
- From 2017, Işık worked as Head of Marketing for Continental’s replacement tire business in the EMEA region.
- Işık previously led the Strategic Business Development and Global Marketing organization of Continental Tires.
- Currently, Işık serves as the Head of Region South & Channels Fleet and OE Trailer for Continental replacement tire business in the EMEA region.
- In his current role, Işık oversees business operations in multiple countries, leads Continental’s truck tire business in EMEA, and is responsible for its retread and digital solutions business in the region.
- Işık holds an Executive MBA from Koç University and trainings at institutions such as INSEAD, Duke University, and Ashridge University.
- Continental was founded in 1871 and develops pioneering technologies and services for sustainable and connected mobility of people and their goods.
- In 2024, Continental generated sales of €39.7 billion and currently employs around 190,000 people in 55 countries and markets.
- The Tires group sector offers a premium portfolio encompassing car, truck, bus, two-wheel, and specialty tires as well as smart solutions and services for fleets and tire retailers.
- Continental is one of the world’s largest tire manufacturers.
- In fiscal 2024, the Tires group sector generated sales of €13.9 billion.
- Continental's tire division employs more than 57,000 people worldwide.
- Continental's tire division has 20 production and 16 development sites globally.
