## Facts

- The facility is located in Griffin, Georgia.
- It serves as an automotive electronics parts manufacturing facility.
- The original 253,000 s.f. facility was completed in 1994.
- Subsequent expansions have increased the size of the facility to almost 500,000 s.f.
- Shimizu North America provided a complete turnkey project management approach to build this 253,000 s.f. facility to manufacture automotive electronic parts.
- Shimizu North America provided a wide range of management services, including architectural design and construction management.
