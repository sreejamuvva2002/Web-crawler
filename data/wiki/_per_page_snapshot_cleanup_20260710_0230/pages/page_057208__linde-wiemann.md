## Facts

- The company operates globally at 19 locations in 8 countries across 4 continents, with its strategic hub in Dillenburg, central Hesse, Germany, nearly 100 kilometers north of Frankfurt.
- The company has been developing innovative technologies for over 80 years and focuses on quality, strong teamwork, and supportive training conditions for apprentices and students.
- Open positions, apprenticeships, and internships are available through an applicant portal where candidates can upload cover letters, CVs, and certificates.
- The application process involves three steps: submission via the portal, prompt confirmation of receipt by HR, and a potential phone interview followed by an in-person interview.
- Vacancies are divided into five German locations (Dillenburg, Hagenbach, Elstra, Ingolstadt, and Süderbrookmerland) across categories for students, experienced professionals, and young professionals.
