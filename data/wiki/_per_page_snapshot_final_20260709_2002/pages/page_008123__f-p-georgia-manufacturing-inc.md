# F & P Georgia Manufacturing Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-06-24

**Source:** [F & P Georgia Data Breach Affects 12,132 Individuals](https://www.claimdepot.com/data-breach/f-p-georgia-2026)  ·  `page_008123`

## Facts

- F & P Georgia Manufacturing Inc. is a Tier 1 automotive parts supplier.
- The company is based in Rome, Georgia.
- A data breach affected approximately 12,132 individuals in the United States, including 22 Indiana residents.
- An unauthorized actor gained access to the company's computer network between June 24, 2025, and July 2, 2025.
- The unauthorized actor copied or viewed certain files during the breach.
- Exposed personal information included Social Security numbers and driver's license information.
- The breach was reported to the Indiana Attorney General and the Massachusetts Office of Consumer Affairs and Business Regulation starting on April 20, 2026.
- Consumer notifications regarding the breach began on April 20, 2026.
- Affected individuals are offered 12 months of free credit monitoring and identity restoration services.
- The monitoring services include single-bureau credit monitoring, a credit report, and a credit score.
- Enrolled individuals will receive same-day alerts when changes occur to their credit file.
- Affected individuals can enroll by visiting Cyberscout's activation page and entering a unique code from their notification letter.
- Enrollment must be completed within 90 days of the notification letter date.
- A dedicated assistance line is available for additional questions.
- The company's mailing address is 88 Enterprise Drive, Acworth, GA 30101.
