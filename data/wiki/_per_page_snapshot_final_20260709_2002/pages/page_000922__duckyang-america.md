# Duckyang America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [News | Electric vehicle supplier Duckyang America sparks interest in western Georgia](https://www.costar.com/article/1009619364/electric-vehicle-supplier-duckyang-america-sparks-interest-in-western-georgia)  ·  `page_000922`

## Facts

- Duckyang America leased a newly built, 270,144-square-foot distribution center at 89 Pegasus Parkway in LaGrange, Georgia.
- Duckyang America is an affiliate of a Korean manufacturer and importer of automotive parts, including battery modules and related components.
- The lease was one of the largest lease transactions in the LaGrange area.
- The lease added critical momentum to the development of the surrounding Lafayette Logistics Park.
- The deal sparked interest from other suppliers in the region.
- The transaction earned a CoStar Impact Award, judged by local real estate professionals familiar with the market.
- Duckyang America's distribution center is part of a new 134-acre industrial campus located near Interstate 85 known as the Lafayette Logistics Park.
- The Lafayette Logistics Park was developed by Grandview Partners, a private real estate investment management firm headquartered in Greenwich, Connecticut.
- Grandview Partners aims to serve manufacturing and supply chain companies with its developments.
- Electric vehicle and component makers have invested some $27.3 billion in Georgia since 2018, according to the Georgia Department of Economic Development.
- Missy Kendrick, chief executive of the economic development organization Choose Columbus, stated that the full building lease to an advanced automotive manufacturing operation will multiply economically and have a larger ripple effect in the community.
- Missy Kendrick also noted that the deal further expands the regional market for advanced manufacturing.
- Managing Director Wesley Budd, Senior Vice President Charlie Adams, and Vice President Jamie Leigh Boyd from NAI Brannen Goddard represented the landlord, Grandview Partners.
- Associate Broker Kelly Choi of KW Commercial Atlanta Partners represented the tenant.
