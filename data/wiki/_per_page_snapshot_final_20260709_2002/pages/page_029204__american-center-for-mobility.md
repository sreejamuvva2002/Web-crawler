# American Center for Mobility

**Type:** research_center  ·  **Role:** research_and_development  ·  **Published:** 2024-09-19

**Source:** [Alternative Fuels Data Center: Validating Charging Station Interoperability and New Automotive Technology](https://afdc.energy.gov/case/3138)  ·  `page_029204`

## Facts

- The American Center for Mobility is providing a test track for self-driving vehicles.
- Through a Joint Office of Energy and Transportation grant, the center provides a test facility for electric vehicle charging equipment.
- The testing ensures every EV charger works every time with every kind of vehicle.
- Videos related to the project are provided by Clean Cities and Communities and FuelEconomy.gov.
