# Continental Tire the Americas LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-05-16

**Source:** [Tire Maker Fined after Separate Incidents - ISSSource](https://www.isssource.com/tire-maker-fined-after-separate-incidents/)  ·  `page_018793`

## Facts

- Last Nov. 13, OSHA received an employer-reported referral from Continental Tire the Americas LLC after an employee unjamming a machine suffered the amputation of three fingers.
- The next day, one employee suffered severe burns requiring hospitalization and another employee suffered a concussion after an industrial rubber-compound mixer exploded.
- Inspectors said Continental Tire had deficient safety procedures for energy control, combustible dust preventative engineering, housekeeping, personal protective equipment, emergency egress routes and hazard communication.
- As a result of the cited deficiencies, the company faces $341,866 in fines.
- OSHA Area Director Aaron Priddy stated that OSHA standards are put in place to prevent workers from suffering life-altering injuries and directed Continental Tire to review safety procedures and employee training to ensure worker safety.
- Continental Tire the Americas LLC is based in Fort Mill, South Carolina.
- The company manufactures passenger, light truck and commercial tires at facilities in Barnesville, Georgia; Mt. Vernon and Sumter, South Carolina.
- Continental is the North American subsidiary of the German-based tire and automotive component manufacturer Continental AG.
