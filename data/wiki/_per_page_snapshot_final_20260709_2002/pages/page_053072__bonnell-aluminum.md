# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-04-09

**Source:** [Bonnell Aluminum Announces Opening of an On-Site Health Center | AP News](https://apnews.com/article/business-newnan-tredegar-corp-39e531aeb5934f929acb0c56cbf4089b)  ·  `page_053072`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation (NYSE:TG).
- Bonnell Aluminum announced it has broken ground on a future on-site health center for employees and family members at its Carthage, Tennessee manufacturing site.
- The on-site health facility is scheduled to open in the middle of 2019.
- The facility will include a medical clinic and fitness amenities.
- The health center will significantly benefit over 500 employees and their families.
- Brook Hamilton, President of Bonnell Aluminum, commented that the company first became interested in this concept after acquiring Futura Industries, which has a similar facility in Utah.
- Hamilton stated that the concept is expected to produce higher employee satisfaction and lower healthcare costs.
- Onsite Care Clinics will serve as the medical care provider at the new facility.
- Bonnell Aluminum plans to work with Onsite Care Clinics to develop innovative healthcare solutions at its additional sites outside of Utah.
- Bonnell Aluminum's operating divisions include AACOA and Futura Industries.
- The company is a leading manufacturer of customer-fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions serving the building and construction, automotive and transportation, consumer durables, machinery and equipment, and electrical and distribution markets.
- Bonnell Aluminum has manufacturing facilities located in Newnan, Georgia; Carthage, Tennessee; Niles, Michigan; Elkhart, Indiana; and Clearfield, Utah.
