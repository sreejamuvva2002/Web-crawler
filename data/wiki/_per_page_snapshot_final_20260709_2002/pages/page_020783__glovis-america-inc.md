## Facts

- Provides integrated third party logistics services including transportation, storage/loading and unloading, international logistics, logistics equipment leasing, and packing services to customers in various industries.
- Operates as a licensed Ocean Transportation Intermediary, a Non-Vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC), and utilizes a network of owned vessels.
- Provides customizable ground transportation solutions utilizing a global network and optimized technologies for reliable and safe delivery of shipments.
- Trained project experts develop secure and efficient transportation plans for oversized, heavy-lift, or dangerous goods with unusual amounts and types of freight.
- Functions as a licensed customs broker offering pre-shipment Import Security Filing to post-entry services.
- Logistics history is rooted in the time-sensitive and demanding automotive industry, with expertise in air freight and optimizing routing, pricing, and speed.
- Provides complete Vendor Managed Inventory (VMI) services covering inbound and outbound items, inventory management, repackaging, and sub-assembly.
- Provides execution-oriented logistics consulting services to improve customer logistics competitiveness.
- Serves as a trusted provider of reliable and customized logistics and value chain solutions in the automotive, consumer goods, and industrial products industries.
- Business has grown since its inception in 2002 to support end-to-end logistic and supply chain services globally from freight transportation to warehouse management.
- Aims to drastically reduce global footprint by reducing CHG emissions, investing in environmentally-friendly energy, and adhering to an environmental management system.
- Smart logistics strategy is underpinned by electrified autonomous mobility, robots, artificial intelligence (AI), automated facilities, and advanced transportation platforms.
- Hyundai Glovis launched hydrogen-powered trucks in June 2023 to provide drayage services as part of efforts to eliminate carbon emissions within supply chain ecosystems.
