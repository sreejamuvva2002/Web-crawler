## Facts

- Valeo is a global tech company designing breakthrough solutions to reinvent mobility.
- Valeo serves as an automotive supplier partner to automakers and new mobility actors worldwide.
- The company's vision focuses on intuitive driving and reducing CO2 emissions.
- Valeo Group is one of the world's largest Automotive suppliers, focused on innovative products, development, and customer satisfaction.
- The Santena plant operates under Valeo's Brain Division, which leads the market in parking and driving assistance components.
- Valeo Smart Cookpit Italy specializes in switches, Human Machine Interface (HMI) control panels, and displays.
- A Purchasing trainee supports the Purchasing team in daily activities related to supplier management and component sourcing processes.
- Trainees work closely with the Purchasing Manager and other team members to contribute to department objectives.
- Key tasks include data analysis, market research, supporting negotiations with suppliers, and helping implement new purchasing strategies.
- Responsibilities involve supporting project awards during the CAA stage by aligning competitive costs with segment strategy.
- Trainees build up project supplier target panels from pre-SOCO alongside the commodity organization.
- Employees must define and control communication flows between project teams and suppliers.
- Daily operations must comply with internal audit rules, including Financial audit and V5000 audit.
- Desired qualifications include enrollment in an Engineering degree course.
- Candidates need strong communication skills, teamwork ability, English fluency, independence, initiative, attention to detail, and advantageous knowledge of procurement practices.
- Valeo employs more than 20,000 engineers dedicated to Research & Development.
- The company fosters a multi-cultural environment that values diversity and international collaboration.
- Valeo has over 100,000 colleagues working across 31 countries.
- Corporate Knights ranks Valeo as the number one company in the automotive sector for sustainable development.
