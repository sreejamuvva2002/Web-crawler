## Facts

- Norwegian battery company Freyr announced plans to build a $2.6 billion battery factory in Coweta County, Ga., in November 2022.
- In February 2025, Freyr announced it was canceling the project and putting up for sale a large swath (368 acres) of woodlands it owns.
- The property sale in Coweta County could garner $50 million.
- Freyr will use part of the sale proceeds to pay back about $27 million it has already received in incentives for the project.
- The property is located at Interstate 85 and Bethlehem Church Road, south of the Newnan-Coweta County Airport.
- Freyr was set to receive a total of $358 million in grants and incentives for its clean energy tech project.
