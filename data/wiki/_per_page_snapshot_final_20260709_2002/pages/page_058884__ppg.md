## Facts

- PPG was founded by Captain John B. Ford and John Pitcairn in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.
- PPG is a global supplier of paints, coatings, optical products, and specialty materials.
- PPG operates and innovates in more than 70 countries with headquarters in Pittsburgh.
- PPG reported net sales of $15.8 billion in 2024.
- PPG serves customers in construction, consumer products, industrial, and transportation markets and aftermarkets.
- PPG employs approximately 46,000 people worldwide as of May 2025.
- PPG employs approximately 8,000 people in the Asia Pacific region as of May 2025.
- PPG is ranked 234 on the Fortune 500.
- PPG operates 177 manufacturing facilities worldwide.
- PPG operates 61 manufacturing sites in the United States and Canada.
- PPG operates 18 manufacturing sites in Latin America.
- PPG operates 61 manufacturing sites in EMEA.
- PPG operates 37 manufacturing sites in Asia Pacific.
- In 2024, PPG and the PPG Foundation invested $18.4 million in communities around the world.
- PPG provides global OEM approvals for its products to original equipment manufacturers.
