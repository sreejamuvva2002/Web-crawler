# Anovion

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-21

**Source:** [Anovion still plans to build in Bainbridge but the project delayed to 2026](https://www.wtxl.com/bainbridge/anovion-still-plans-to-build-in-bainbridge-but-the-project-delayed-to-2026)  ·  `page_052145`

## Facts

- The $800 million Anovion project has been delayed until 2026.
- Construction for the project is expected to begin sometime in 2025.
- Anovion was initially expected to begin construction in 2024.
- Chief Commercial Officer Patrick Donnelly explained that the delay is due to legislation related to the Inflation Reduction Act.
- Development Authority of Bainbridge and Decatur County executive director Rick McCaskill stated that Anovion will begin construction in the first half of next year.
- There was an expectation that a tax credit for refraining from using Chinese synthetic graphite would take effect in 2025.
- Chief Commercial Officer Patrick Donnelly said Anovion plans to operate a small-scale version of the original plan in 2026.
- The full-scale projected project is expected to be up and running in 2027.
- The project is planned for Bainbridge, Georgia.
