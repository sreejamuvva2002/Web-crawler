# Flambeau, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-02-20

**Source:** [Latest Corporate News and Updates | Flambeau, Inc](https://www.flambeau.com/news/)  ·  `page_054828`

## Facts

- Flambeau’s plastic manufacturing facility in Sharon Center, Ohio commissioned a 3 lb. Sterling dual head blow molding machine, increasing production capacity.
- The Sharon Center plant is one of eight facilities across the United States.
- A brand new 1,900-ton injection molding machine (Engel Duo press) was commissioned at Flambeau’s Columbus, Indiana facility, increasing the range of existing injection molding machines.
- Flambeau, Inc. continues to be named one of the top plastics manufacturers in North America, rising in rankings for 2022 and holding positions in others.
- Production was ramped up to full capacity on new injection moulding machines processing plastic parts for Makita power tools such as planers, drills, grinders, and saws.
- Flambeau, Inc. reached its 75th year of business in 2022 after being founded in 1947.
- Associates at the Baraboo injection molding plant achieved a zero-accident milestone of 365 days and 251,134 hours worked by September 30, 2021.
- Flambeau EuroPlast committed to a substantial investment in its Ramsgate manufacturing facility with four new Haitian™ injection moulding machines to manufacture plastic case assemblies for Makita.
- Surges in demand led Flambeau to invest significantly in Middlefield operations, adding three new presses ranging from 330-ton to 730-ton dedicated to stock boxes and cases for Flambeau Outdoors™ and ArtBin®.
- A new 750-ton press was added to Columbus operations primarily for proprietary products including Flambeau® Brute™ tool boxes, gun cases, and tackle boxes.
- Two new presses were added to Madison operations to increase production of stock and custom products for fluids and packaging divisions.
- Flambeau’s plant in Saltillo, Coahuila, Mexico received a 2020 quality award from PACCAR for exceeding its “10 ppm” quality standard.
- In the 2020 Plastics News Magazine ranking of blow molders, Flambeau came in at #20.
- Plants in Madison, Georgia, and Saltillo, Coahuila, Mexico manufacture thermoplastic molded parts for the automotive industry and received General Motors (GM) Supplier Quality Excellence Awards for 2019.
- The award from GM is given only to top-performing suppliers.
- For the Mexico facility, it was the sixth straight year receiving this recognition from GM.
- Flambeau expedited engineering and tool making processes to produce a key component of personal protective equipment (PPE) for frontline healthcare workers at its Baraboo, Wisconsin injection molding facility.
