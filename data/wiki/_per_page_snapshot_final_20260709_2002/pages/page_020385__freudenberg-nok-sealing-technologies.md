# Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-11-09

**Source:** [Freudenberg-NOK named a top workplace third year in a row | Sealing & Contamination Control Tips](https://www.sealingandcontaminationtips.com/freudenberg-nok-named-a-top-workplace-third-year-in-a-row/)  ·  `page_020385`

## Facts

- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies has been honored with a 2020 Top Workplaces Award from the Detroit Free Press.
- The award represents the third consecutive year since 2018 that employees at Freudenberg-NOK have identified the company as a great place to work.
- Freudenberg-NOK runs the business operations for Freudenberg Sealing Technologies in the Americas.
- Employees working at the innovative sealing and electric mobility technology company participated in an anonymous survey sponsored by the Detroit Free Press, which resulted in the honor.
- They broke into applause at a recent virtual town hall meeting when they learned of the award.
- In a difficult, pandemic-influenced year, Freudenberg-NOK continued to improve its survey scores to move up in Top Workplaces ranking.
- Matthew Portu serves as President of Freudenberg-NOK.
- Matthew Portu stated that the survey results provide welcome testimony from employees that they consider Freudenberg-NOK an employer of choice despite 2020’s extraordinary business challenges.
- Matthew Portu noted that the company continued to embrace excellence, flexibility, innovation, diversity and open communications that benefited employees, customers and business partners.
- Matthew Portu expressed gratification by the results and achievements of Freudenberg-NOK as reflected in this competition.
- Matthew Portu stated that the company will use these survey results to look for new opportunities for improvement and growth.
- Freudenberg-NOK ranked 43rd among 55 midsized Michigan companies which participated in the program, moving 11 spots higher on the list compared to its 2019 results.
- Freudenberg-NOK has approximately 5,000 employees working at 20 facilities across the Americas.
- Small, medium and large companies participate in the Top Workplace competition in separate categories; 150 companies in total were honored with Top Workplace status.
- The Detroit Free Press Top Workplaces Program has been a part of the corporate work scene in Michigan for the past 13 years.
- Companies are nominated and rated by their employees through an anonymous survey conducted by Energage, a Free Press research partner that tracks key workplace practices that influence how employees feel about their companies.
- Freudenberg employees participating in the 2020 survey indicated satisfaction with the company in a variety of areas including culture, benefits and pay, career opportunities, financial stability, customer relationships, leadership and technology.
