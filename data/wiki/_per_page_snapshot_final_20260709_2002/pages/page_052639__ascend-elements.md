## Facts

- The company's target market is centered around the electric vehicle (EV) and energy storage industries.

- The company invested in large-scale facilities like the Apex 1 plant in Hopkinsville, Kentucky, designed to produce sustainable cathode precursor (pCAM) and cathode active materials (CAM).
