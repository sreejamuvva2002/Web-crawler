## Facts

- Ecoplastic Corporation was founded in 1984 as a Korean-based automotive parts manufacturer.
- It serves as a Tier-1 supplier for Hyundai Motor Group and Kia.
- The company produces bumpers, consoles, trim, plastic molds, and other automotive interior and exterior plastic parts.
- Ecoplastic America Corp operates as the US production subsidiary of Ecoplastic Corporation.
- It runs an automotive parts manufacturing facility in Bulloch County, Georgia.
- The Georgia facility supplies automotive parts to HMGMA.
