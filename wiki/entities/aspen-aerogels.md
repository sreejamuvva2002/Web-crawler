# Aspen Aerogels

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** near Statesboro, Georgia, Bulloch County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Aspen Aerogels is a Massachusetts-based electric vehicle supplier that planned to build a new facility in Georgia to produce EV insulation and fire protection materials, but has now ceased construction.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Produces electric-vehicle insulation and fire-protection materials for the EV supply chain.
