# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Aircraft Assembly Plant to Open in Georgia, Create 1,000 Jobs | ASSEMBLY](https://www.assemblymag.com/articles/97473-aircraft-assembly-plant-to-open-in-georgia-create-1-000-jobs)  ·  `page_016816`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. will invest $118 million over the next 10 years to build a new assembly plant in Covington, GA.
- The facility will produce electric vertical takeoff and landing (eVTOL) aircraft.
- The new plant will be located alongside the Covington Municipal Airport.
- The facility is expected to create 1,000 jobs.
- Archer Aviation Inc. is based in Santa Clara, CA.
- Archer is currently developing and manufacturing eVTOL aircraft for use in urban air mobility networks.
- Adam Goldstein, Archer’s founder and CEO, announced the decision to locate the company's first manufacturing facility in Covington as it progresses toward commercialization.
- Goldstein stated that creating urban air mobility networks across major cities nationwide requires ramping production of their aircraft.
- Archer’s new facility will consist of three buildings across more than 96 acres.
- Construction is expected to begin in December 2022 and follow a phased buildout.
- At full capacity, the plant will be able to produce up to 2,300 aircraft per year.
- Hiring is expected to begin in late 2023.
- The facility is slated to open in the first half of 2024.
- Glen Burks, Archer’s vice president of manufacturing, stated that the new manufacturing facility is a crucial next step in bringing their vision for eVTOL travel to life and expressed happiness with Covington as a location and partner.
