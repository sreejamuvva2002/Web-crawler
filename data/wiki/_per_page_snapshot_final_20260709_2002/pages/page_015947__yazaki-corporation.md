## Facts

- Yazaki is an automotive infrastructure supplier advancing research and development that supports the future mobility society through 'connecting technologies'.
- The company focuses on wire harnesses for vehicles and meters.
- Yazaki pursues technology to reliably and accurately transmit power and information to where it is needed.
- The company offers a wide variety of products that connect the inside of the car, people to cars, and society to cars.
- EEDDS is a technology that reliably and accurately conveys the information and power needed for a vehicle.
- EEDDS consists of EEDS, which optimally distributes and connects power and information inside the car, and HMI, which optimally conveys information inside and outside the car to the driver.
- The fusion of EEDS and HMI creates EEDDS (Electrical/Electronic Distribution & Display System), connecting the inside of a car, a car to the outside, and a car to a person.
- Yazaki provides solutions to address changes in ECU and other electrical/electronic equipment and networks as CASE and MaaS evolve.
