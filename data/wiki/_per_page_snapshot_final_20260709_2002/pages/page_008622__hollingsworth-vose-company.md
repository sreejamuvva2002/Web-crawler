# Hollingsworth & Vose Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hollingsworth & Vose Company acquires Raman FibreScience in India - The Economic Times](https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/industry/healthcare/biotech/healthcare/hollingsworth-vose-company-acquires-raman-fibrescience-in-india/articleshow/45059981.cms)  ·  `page_008622`

## Facts

- US-based Hollingsworth & Vose Company (H&V) announced the acquisition of Mysore-based Raman FibreScience Pvt Ltd for an undisclosed amount.
- Raman FibreScience is a technology and innovation-driven company specialising in wet-laid, fiber-based materials for filtration media and battery separator applications.
- Hollingsworth & Vose Company is a global leader in these markets with operations worldwide.
- H&V CEO Val Hollingsworth stated that Raman FibreScience has a highly professional team with considerable experience in fibre forming technology.
- Raman FibreScience has a unique culture where research and development and manufacturing are highly integrated.
- The company leveraged this capability to develop and take to international markets some very unique and innovative products.
- India represents an exciting market for H&V, and the RFS Mysore location and team provide H&V a strong on-the-ground operational base with which to build and grow its India presence.
- Aroon Raman, Managing Director of Raman Fibre Science, stated that both companies are privately held with world-class technical capabilities and a policy of investing in innovation for the long term.
- Raman will continue on the board of the acquired company following the acquisition.
- The acquisition is effective immediately.
- Veda Corporate Advisors served as advisors to the transaction in India.
