# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-11-07

**Source:** [TCI Powder Coatings gains Qualicoat approval](https://www.canadianmetalworking.com/canadianfabricatingandwelding/news/metalfinishing/tci-powder-coatings-gains-qualicoat-approval)  ·  `page_051181`

## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is located in Americus, Ga.
- The company has achieved Qualicoat approval for the powder coating TCI Gloss Polyester.
- The certification is Class 1, Gloss Category 3.
- The certification guarantees that the durable, high-gloss powder coating is of high quality and long-lasting value.
- This qualification will help the company meet the requirements of customers in Mexico, South America, and North America.
