# Club Car, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-10

**Source:** [Club Car Launches Lithium Option for Street-Legal CRU Vehicle](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/club-car-launches-lithium-option-for-street-legal-cru-vehicle-302502707.html)  ·  `page_005256`

## Facts

- Club Car is headquartered in Augusta, Georgia.
- Club Car announced the launch of its all-new CRU Lithium option for its street-legal CRU Neighborhood Electric Vehicle (NEV).
- The CRU Lithium NEV features an advanced lithium-ion battery that delivers faster charging, lighter weight, longer range, and improved power.
- The new lithium battery option allows the vehicle to go three times further with more efficient energy use and consistent performance.
- The CRU Lithium NEV offers a lightweight design that improves handling and acceleration with reduced battery weight.
- The lithium-ion technology eliminates the need for watering and reduces corrosion, resulting in low maintenance requirements.
- The lithium-ion batteries are individually encased and tested to applicable standards to ensure safety and reliability.
- The CRU Lithium NEV is available now through authorized Club Car dealers.
- Club Car has a history spanning over six decades and is known for quality craftsmanship ensuring long-term reliability and customer satisfaction.
- Though rooted in golf fleet and operations, Club Car's product portfolio includes vehicles for commercial, personal, and street-legal low-speed use.
- The Club Car Vehicle Group also includes the luxury electric vehicle brand Garia and the lightweight electric utility vehicle brand Melex.
- Brant Mitchell, Senior Director of Product Management at Club Car, stated that CRU Lithium is a game changer that provides a smarter, longer-lasting power solution fitting customer lifestyles.
