# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-04

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group Deploys XCIENT Hydrogen Fuel Cell Trucks for HMGMA Clean Logistics](https://www.hyundai.com/worldwide/en/newsroom/detail/hyundai-motor-group-deploys-xcient-hydrogen-fuel-cell-trucks-for-hmgma-clean-logistics-0000000879)  ·  `page_006023`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA), in cooperation with Glovis America, deployed Hyundai XCIENT heavy-duty hydrogen fuel-cell electric trucks for clean logistics operations in Ellabell, Ga.
- Initially, a total of 21 XCIENT trucks will be in operation at the facility.
- Oscar Kwon, HMGMA CEO, stated that the company is committed to creating sustainable transportation solutions and that the zero-emission hydrogen fuel-cell trucks offer a cleaner alternative to traditional freight logistics methods.
- The deployment represents a significant step in HMGMA’s efforts to reduce its carbon footprint and actively promote sustainable practices in daily logistics operations.
- The Hyundai XCIENT Class 8 heavy-duty trucks will transport vehicle parts from HMGMA suppliers across the region to the Megasite on a daily basis.
- During the initial rollout, the XCIENT trucks will transport parts between suppliers and the on-site Consolidation Center before expanding to a broader network of suppliers.
- The 21 XCIENT vehicles will represent more than one-third of Glovis America’s truck fleet at HMGMA.
- Earlier this year at CES, Hyundai Motor Group announced HTWO, its new hydrogen value chain business brand encompassing businesses and affiliates across production, storage, transportation, and utilization.
- Jim Park, SVP of commercial vehicle and hydrogen business development at Hyundai Motor North America, stated that HMGMA’s deployment of XCIENT for logistics is one of the main initiatives of HTWO and delivers on their strategy to showcase a closed-loop hydrogen logistics ecosystem.
- HTWO Logistics, a joint venture between Hyundai Motor Company and Glovis America, will install a mobile hydrogen refueling station at the Megasite for efficient refueling.
- A hydrogen production and refueling station is being developed for the Megasite, with details to be announced at a later date.
- HMGMA is Hyundai Motor Group’s first dedicated electric vehicle mass-production plant, located in Bryan County, Ga.
- The Georgia facility will feature a highly connected, automated, and flexible manufacturing system that organically connects all elements of the EV ecosystem.
- All production processes at the facility—including order collection, procurement, logistics, and production—will be optimized utilizing AI and data.
- The innovative manufacturing system aims to create a human-centered work environment with robots assisting human workers.
- Hyundai Motor Group is a global enterprise with approximately 250,000 employees worldwide and mobility brands including Hyundai, Kia, and Genesis.
