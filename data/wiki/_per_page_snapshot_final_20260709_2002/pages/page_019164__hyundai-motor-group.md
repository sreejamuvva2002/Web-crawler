## Facts

- The rankings evaluated over 400 model-year 2026 vehicles.
- Evaluation criteria ranged from final assembly locations and domestic part percentages to the origin of engines and transmissions.
- Hyundai secured five spots on the 2026 American-Made Index.
- Hyundai’s U.S. presence is anchored by two primary hubs: Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama and the Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America in Georgia.
- The IONIQ 5 secured the 21st position on the index.
- The Santa Cruz secured the 24th position.
- The Tucson secured the 31st position.
- The Santa Fe secured the 33rd position.
- The Santa Fe Hybrid secured the 81st position.
- Patrick Masterson serves as the lead researcher for the index.
- All five qualifying models improved their standing compared to the previous year.
- Hyundai has an ongoing $26 billion commitment to U.S. operations through 2028.
