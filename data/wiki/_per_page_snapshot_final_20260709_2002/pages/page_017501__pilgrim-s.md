## Facts

- Fabio Sandri, Pilgrim’s CEO, stated that as consumer demand shifts and customers grow, the investment ensures operations are positioned for the future.
- Fabio Sandri described Ellijay as a strong-performing facility with a talented team.
- The expansion will allow Pilgrim’s to optimize its portfolio and improve efficiency.
