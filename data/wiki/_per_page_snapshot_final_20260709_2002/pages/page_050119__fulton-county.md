# Fulton County

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-10

**Source:** [Fulton County Soars to #4 Nationally for Economic Development Projects - The ATL Airport Chamber](https://airportchamber.com/fulton-county-soars-to-4-nationally-for-economic-development-projects/)  ·  `page_050119`

## Facts

- Fulton County moved from #11 last year to #4 in the national Top 20 Best Counties in America rankings for economic development projects.
- Fulton County achieved the #1 position in the I-85 Corridor Report.
- Site Selection Magazine published the rankings in its July issue, utilizing the Conway Data Project Database to evaluate 3,142 U.S. counties based on new investments and job creation.
- Fulton County is ranked as the #1 county in the Southeast and is the only county in Georgia to make the top 20 rankings.
- Robb Pitts, Chairman of the Fulton County Board of Commissioners, stated the recognition highlights collaborative work with partners at the State of Georgia, the region, and its 15 cities.
- Kristi Brigman, Deputy Commissioner of Global Commerce at the Georgia Department of Economic Development, noted that diverse, well-educated talent and a collaborative ecosystem are fostering growth in innovative industries such as tech and life sciences.
- Recent major corporate wins and investments in Fulton County include Adobe, SK Battery America, Amazon Music, Cummins, Resia Manufacturing, Microsoft, GCI Restaurant Group, Mastercard, Newegg Commerce, Nike, PrizePicks, and Cargill.
- These recent projects span various sectors including technology, manufacturing, and finance, strengthening the regional economy and creating job opportunities for Georgians.
- Fulton County is the most populous county in Georgia and is home to 15 distinct cities, including Atlanta.
- Metro Atlanta is currently the country’s sixth-largest region and continues to attract top talent as businesses choose locations with strong workforces.
- Samir Abdullahi, Director of Economic Development and Select Fulton, emphasized that Select Fulton serves as the central hub for economic and workforce development services throughout the county, cultivating new job prospects and investment opportunities.
- The article was published courtesy of Georgia CEO on July 10, 2024.
