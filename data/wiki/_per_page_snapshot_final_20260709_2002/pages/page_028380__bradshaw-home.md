# Bradshaw Home

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2023-07-25

**Source:** [Bradshaw Home to Create More than 230 Logistics Jobs in Effingham Coun | Georgia](https://georgia.org/press-release/bradshaw-home-create-more-230-logistics-jobs-effingham-county)  ·  `page_028380`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced on July 25, 2023 that Bradshaw Home will build a new distribution center in Effingham County.
- The project will create more than 230 new logistics jobs.
- Bradshaw Home is a leading designer and marketer of kitchen and cleaning products.
- The company's product portfolio includes kitchen tools, gadgets, bakeware, cookware, food storage, and cleaning products.
- Products are marketed under brands including GoodCook®, Casabella®, and Evercare®.
- More than 75% of Bradshaw's customers are located east of the Rocky Mountains.
- Fifty-five percent of Bradshaw's customers are located east of the Mississippi River.
- CEO Tony Hair stated that opening an East Coast location expands the distribution network to service customers more effectively while significantly reducing freight miles.
- Bradshaw will partner with NFI to operate the new facility.
- NFI brings over 90 years of supply chain expertise and maintains a strong presence in Savannah with warehouses, transload facilities, and dedicated and port drayage terminals.
- NFI recently partnered with the Georgia Ports Authority to build a new transload facility at the Garden City Terminal, set to open in 2023.
- The new distribution center will be 1.2 million square feet.
- The facility is located at the Savannah Portside International Park near Meldrim in Effingham County.
- The facility is projected to open in November.
- Hiring positions include forklift operators for loading and unloading, material handlers for kitting, warehouse operation leads, and other roles.
- Speaker of the House Jon Burns thanked local public and private sector partners, particularly the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority and the Georgia Department of Economic Development, for making the announcement possible.
- Matt Saxon, Chairman of the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority, thanked development partners at Panattoni Development Company for their investment in attracting companies to Effingham County.
- Griff Lynch, Executive Director of the Georgia Ports Authority, stated the facility is ideally positioned to reach domestic markets spanning from Dallas to Chicago.
- Georgia's logistics network features 35 weekly containership calls, Class I rail service from CSX and Norfolk Southern, and interstate connectivity.
- Dorie Bacon, Senior Regional Project Manager for the GDEcD Global Commerce team, represented the department on the project alongside the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority and Georgia Ports Authority.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson noted that counties near the Port of Savannah are experiencing unprecedented growth, attracting companies with national and global footprints in sectors ranging from automotive to logistics.
- Bradshaw Home is headquartered in Rancho Cucamonga, California.
- Bradshaw maintains the number one market share for kitchenware in grocery stores.
- Bradshaw currently distributes its products in more than 100,000 retail store outlets in North America.
- NFI is a fully integrated North American supply chain solutions provider headquartered in Camden, New Jersey.
- NFI has been privately held by the Brown family since 1932.
- NFI generates more than $3.9 billion in annual revenue and employs over 16,800 associates.
- By 2023, NFI will operate a 100% zero-emission drayage fleet.
- NFI's business lines include dedicated transportation, warehousing and distribution, eCommerce fulfillment, brokerage, transportation management, port drayage, intermodal, global logistics, and industrial real estate.
