# TI Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-04-13

**Source:** [TI Automotive Opens New Manufacturing Facility In Monterrey, Mexico](https://tifluidsystems.com/ti-automotive-opens-new-manufacturing-facility-in-monterrey-mexico/)  ·  `page_011400`

## Facts

- TI Automotive, a leading global supplier of automotive fluid systems technology, has opened a new manufacturing facility in Monterrey, Mexico.
- The new manufacturing facility is 53,000 square feet.
- The facility will produce fluid carrying systems for the new Hyundai/Kia manufacturing facility located nearby.
- TI Automotive is expected to employ around 100 people at full production.
- Full production at the new facility is expected in May 2016.
- The new facility serves as a key additional site for TI Automotive’s global Fluid Carrying Systems (FCS) Division.
- The facility will produce brake and fuel bundles, brake line assemblies, and clutch lines with flared tubes.
- The facility will be equipped to produce engine line products including water, oil, and vacuum tubes.
- The facility will also produce transmission lines and exhaust gas reduction (EGR) pipes.
- Bill Kozyra, chairman, CEO and president of TI Automotive, stated that Mexico is a growing automotive market for their customers and the new facility supports their global growth strategy of going where customers need them.
- Bill Kozyra noted that TI Automotive has enjoyed a great partnership with Hyundai/Kia worldwide and looks forward to continued success in Monterrey.
- TI Automotive currently operates nine production facilities in Mexico.
- TI Automotive's existing Mexican facilities are located in Mexico City, Puebla, Reynosa, and San Luis Potosi.
