# Honda Motor Co.

## Facts

- Honda Motor Co. broke ground on May 11, 2005 on a manufacturing facility in Tallapoosa, Ga.
- The manufacturing facility will be 250,000-sf in size.
- The plant represents a $100-million investment.
- The facility will produce 300,000 automatic transmissions annually.
- Production is scheduled to begin in fall 2006.
- The plant will employ 400 workers.
