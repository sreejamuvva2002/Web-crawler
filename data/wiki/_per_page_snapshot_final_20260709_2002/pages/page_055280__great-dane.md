## Facts

- Great Dane is being recognized by the Georgia General Assembly in commemoration of the company’s 125th anniversary.
- Rick Mullininx serves as president and COO of Great Dane.
- The 125-year milestone was marked by proclamations read in both the House and Senate chambers.
- Great Dane received a special commendation from Governor Brian Kemp.
- The recognition highlights the company’s enduring history of innovation, quality, and leadership in the transportation industry.
- State Rep. Ron Stephens from Savannah, Ga., sponsored the House proclamation.
- Savannah, Ga. is the city where Great Dane was founded in 1900.
- State Sen. Ben Watson from Savannah sponsored the Senate proclamation.
- Chris Hammond, Executive Vice President of Sales at Great Dane, accepted the Senate proclamation during the Senate session.
- Great Dane stated that its 125 years of success reflects the resilience of its teams and the trust built with customers and partners across generations.
- The company underscored its focus on the future, enabled by technology and customer relationships.
