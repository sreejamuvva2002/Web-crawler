## Facts

- The 2026 Digital FactBook is an annual report that provides an overview of Honda's global operations, as well as detailed historical and up-to-date information concerning Honda's sales, manufacturing and R&D operations in North America.
- Honda established operations in North America in 1959.
- The company employs about 42,000 associates in the region engaged in the development, manufacturing, sales and service support of Honda and Acura automobiles, Honda power equipment, Honda powersports products and the HondaJet Elite II advanced light jet.
- Honda operates 18 major manufacturing plants in North America.
- Honda works with over 750 suppliers in the region to produce a diverse range of quality products.
- Honda has built automobiles in North America for over 40 years.
- In 2025, nearly 99% of all Honda and Acura automobiles sold in the U.S. were produced in North America.
- Honda conducts research and development activities at 21 facilities in North America where it fully designs, develops and engineers many of the company’s North American-made products.
- Honda and Acura high-performance racing programs are conducted in North America including the design and development of powertrain, chassis, electronics, and performance parts, as well as technical and race support.
