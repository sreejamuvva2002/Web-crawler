# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Superior Essex Inc. is based in Atlanta, Georgia.
- The company is a premier global manufacturer of magnet wire and communication cables.
- It serves as a critical supplier for the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- At its peak as a public entity, it was recognized as one of the largest producers of copper and aluminum wire products in North America.
- In 2008, South Korean conglomerate LS Group, specifically its subsidiary LS Cable, acquired Superior Essex for approximately $900 million.
- The acquisition deal valued the company at $45.00 per share, representing a significant premium at the time.
- Following the buyout, Superior Essex was delisted from the Nasdaq and transitioned into a key international subsidiary of LS Group.
- As of late 2023 and early 2024, reports indicate that LS Group is exploring a "Re-IPO" for Superior Essex units.
- The subsidiary Superior Essex ABL (SEABL) has been discussed for a potential listing on the KOSPI or U.S. markets by 2026.
- The Re-IPO aims to capitalize on the growing demand for EV motor components and high-voltage cables.
- Superior Essex is central to LS Group's "Vision 2030" strategy.
- As of early 2024, the company is pivoting heavily toward the electric vehicle (EV) market.
- The company produces specialized magnet wires for EV motors.
- The company is expanding its 5G network infrastructure.
- Superior Essex recently raised $150 million in pre-IPO funding from firms like SKS Credit to support expansion.
- This funding rounds out the valuation of its business units as it prepares for potential public listings in the coming years.
