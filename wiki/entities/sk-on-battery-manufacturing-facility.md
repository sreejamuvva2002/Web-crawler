# SK On Battery Manufacturing Facility

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Bartow County, Georgia, Bartow County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

A new electric-vehicle battery manufacturing plant selected by Hyundai Motor Group and SK On to be built in Bartow County, Georgia. The facility will supply batteries for Hyundai EV plants nationwide and is expected to begin operations in 2025.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Battery plant that will produce lithium-ion cells for Hyundai electric-vehicle production, creating a domestic supply chain in Georgia.
