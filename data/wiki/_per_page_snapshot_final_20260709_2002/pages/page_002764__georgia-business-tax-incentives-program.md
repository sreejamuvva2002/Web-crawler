## Facts

- Businesses may qualify for state tax exemptions and credits in Georgia.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) can help businesses navigate available incentives, support attraction efforts, and provide guidance on options.
- The Georgia Department of Revenue (DOR) administers tax programs and handles filings, compliance, and related inquiries.
- State business tax incentives are available to help grow businesses in Georgia.
