# Norfolk Southern

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-26

**Source:** [Atlanta Business Chronicle: Powering Georgia’s manufacturing from the ground up | Norfolk Southern](https://www.norfolksouthern.com/en/newsroom/story-yard/georgia-manufacturing-from-the-ground-up)  ·  `page_009163`

## Facts

- Norfolk Southern serves as a trusted rail partner for Georgia manufacturers, delivering raw materials in and shipping finished goods out to markets worldwide.
- The company connects with major Georgia manufacturing companies including Coca-Cola, Georgia-Pacific, Smurfit/WestRock, Fieldale Farms, Wayne-Sanderson Farms, Argos USA, Southern Chips, ADM, and Cargill.
- NS rail operations move enough fertilizer to treat more than 15 million farms in Georgia.
- NS moves enough tires to equip over 1.4 million vehicles, supporting Georgia drivers and auto manufacturers.
- NS transports enough pulpboard to produce 38 billion egg cartons for Georgia's poultry industry.
- NS ships enough cement to build 44,000 swimming pools, fueling construction projects from Atlanta to Augusta.
- Atlanta serves as a key rail hub linking Georgia manufacturers to key Southeastern markets.
- Savannah and Brunswick ports provide direct access to global trade, with NS rail lines ensuring goods flow seamlessly to overseas buyers.
- NS operates over 1,000 miles of track statewide, allowing both multinational corporations and local businesses to scale.
- NS rail operations in Georgia avoid over 930,400 metric tons of CO2 emissions annually.
- This annual CO2 reduction is equivalent to taking more than 3.9 million trucks off the highway each year.
- NS supports workforce development and economic growth initiatives for communities where it operates.
