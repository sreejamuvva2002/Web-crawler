## Facts

- In 1954, in a one-room machine shop in Augusta, Georgia, the first Textron Specialized Vehicles products were born.
- Two brothers started the company with a belief that they could build a better golf car that met customers' needs better than any other vehicle on the market.
- The Textron Specialized Vehicles product line includes golf cars, ground support equipment for the aviation industry, industrial utility vehicles, and recreational side-by-side UTVs.
- The company has become a world leader in transportation and a leading global manufacturer of golf cars, side-by-sides, utility vehicles, personal transportation vehicles and aviation ground support equipment.
- The company builds thousands of vehicles each year, many featuring game-changing technology and innovations to improve vehicle efficiency and enhance the user experience.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles includes brands that are leaders in their respective industries: Cushman®, E-Z-GO®, Arctic Cat®, Jacobsen®, Ransomes®, TUG™, Douglas™, Premier™, and Safeaero™.
- Since 1960, the company has been part of Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT), a global multi-industry company headquartered in Providence, RI.
- Textron Inc. is a Fortune 500 company with more than 35,000 employees worldwide and operations in 25 countries.
- Textron's notable brands include E-Z-GO, Cessna, Beechcraft, Hawker, Bell, and Kautex, among others.
- The company maintains a single belief to build vehicles and services that exceed customers' expectations better than any other offering on the market.
- The company operates in the Motor Vehicle Manufacturing industry.
