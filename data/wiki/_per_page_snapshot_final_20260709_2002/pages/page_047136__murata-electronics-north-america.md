# Murata Electronics North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Murata Launches World's Smallest 2.4 GHz Balanced Output Filter](https://www.rfglobalnet.com/doc/murata-launches-worlds-smallest-24-ghz-0001)  ·  `page_047136`

## Facts

- Murata Electronics North America announced the launch of its LFB182G45BG2D280 balanced output matching filter.
- The filter is aimed at a broad range of 2.4 GHz wireless applications using ZigBee and Bluetooth low energy technologies.
- The new filter has been designed specifically for use with wireless connectivity system-on-chips (SoCs) from Texas Instruments Incorporated (TI), the CC253x and CC254x for ZigBee and Bluetooth technology networking respectively.
- Murata's solution measures just 1.6 x 0.8 mm.
- The filter incorporates all the necessary RF circuitry of a matching network, balun and filter within a single package.
- Scott Klettke, Marketing at Murata Electronics North America, stated that the launch demonstrates continual success in delivering advanced products to emerging markets.
- Klettke noted that the level of integration achieved is unprecedented and that it replaces nine discrete 0402 components, speaking to ease of use and time to market savings when using a TI solution.
- Sample pricing for Murata's output filter is $.25 per piece.
- The approximate lead-time for the filter is two to four weeks.
- Murata Electronics North America is a wholly owned subsidiary of Murata Manufacturing Co., Ltd., whose global headquarters are in Kyoto, Japan.
- Murata Manufacturing was established in 1944 and is a worldwide leader in research, design, manufacture and sale of ceramic based passive electronic components and power supply modules.
- Murata has annual revenues in excess of $7B dollars.
- Murata's products are found in applications including consumer, mobile communications, computers and networking equipment, automotive electronics, advanced metering equipment, digital home electronics, lighting and illumination, and lifestyle healthcare devices.
