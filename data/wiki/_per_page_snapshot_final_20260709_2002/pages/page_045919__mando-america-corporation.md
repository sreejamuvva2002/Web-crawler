## Facts

- Mando America Corporation is a global leader in automotive parts manufacturing, producing advanced braking, steering, and suspension systems for top-tier automakers.
- Its Georgia casting plant serves as a key facility in the company’s North American operations, supporting high-volume, precision component production.
- The company initiated a major addition to its casting plant to increase operational capacity and streamline logistics.
- The expansion included a 120,000-square-foot addition to the existing casting plant.
- The project also included a separate 20,000-square-foot miscellaneous one-story steel framed storage building addition.
- The facility expansion is dated to 2015.
- The new casting plant wing was developed as a mirrored layout of the current facility.
- Structural updates included minor modifications to the existing foundation system.
- Concrete pit locations and dimensions were adjusted.
- Roof height changes were made to accommodate new equipment and functional requirements.
- The expansion scope included exterior concrete retaining walls adjacent to the existing loading dock and office areas.
- New loading docks were added at auxiliary buildings.
- Concrete pads were installed for a new exterior tank farm.
- Structural upgrades within the existing casting plant involved modification of the lateral bracing system.
- Column adjustments or removals were made to extend the crane runway effectively.
- Two new monitoring offices were designed and incorporated within the expanded footprint to improve plant oversight and operations.
- Detailed structural construction specifications were prepared for both the casting plant and the new storage building.
- Ongoing revisions and plan updates were provided in response to county plan review feedback to ensure timely compliance and approval.
- The integrated structural solutions and regulatory coordination enabled Mando to significantly scale its operations while maintaining safety, efficiency, and structural integrity across its expanded Georgia manufacturing campus.
