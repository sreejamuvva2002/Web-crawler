## Facts

- ADVICS Manufacturing Ohio is located in Lebanon, OH.
- The facility hires for entry-level technician roles that require either an associate degree or 10 years of experience.
- Interviews at the facility have been conducted in August 2021 and February 2026.
- The interview process includes a brief skills test, a technical test featuring complex diagrams of electrical or machining parts, and a basic math test.
- Applicants are given a tour of the manufacturing plant during the interview process.
- Candidates can apply for positions online, through a recruiter, or by attending in-person open interviews.
- The application to offer process can take as little as one day.
- Interviewers ask basic questions such as how candidates heard about the job and why they want to work there.
- During one interview, an interviewer left early while another demeaned the candidate's prior training and knowledge.
