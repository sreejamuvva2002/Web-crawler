# Mando America Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-12-29

**Source:** [Mando America Corporation Continues to Expand - The Observer](https://opelikaobserver.com/2021/12/29/mando-america-corporation-continues-to-expand/)  ·  `page_045910`

## Facts

- Mando America Corporation will expand its Opelika operations and add an additional $8.9 million for equipment and building improvements.
- The company plans a total investment of more than $236 million in Opelika.
- Mando plans to hire an additional 22 employees to support the expansion.
- The hiring will bring Mando's total employment level in Opelika to over 400 team members.
- Opelika Mayor Gary Fuller expressed appreciation for Mando’s continued investment and stated that the company is one of the city's largest manufacturing employers, providing employment opportunities for citizens.
- City of Opelika Economic Development Director Lori Huguley described Mando as a valued community partner and expressed appreciation for its additional investment in Opelika.
- Mando’s Opelika operations continue to produce brake calipers and suspension systems to support its customer base located throughout the U.S.
- Mando Corporation is one of the leading automotive parts manufacturers in the world, specializing in internationally competitive state-of-the-art chassis components and systems.
- Through extensive research and development, Mando became the first manufacturer in Korea to develop advanced products such as ABS (Anti-Lock Brake System), ESP (Electronic Stability Program), CDC (Continuous Damping Control), ESC (Electronic Stability Control) and EPS (Electric Power Steering).
- Mando Corporation is headquartered in South Korea.
- The company operates 20 manufacturing plants and 18 R&D centers in 10 countries.
- Mando employs over 11,700 associates worldwide.
