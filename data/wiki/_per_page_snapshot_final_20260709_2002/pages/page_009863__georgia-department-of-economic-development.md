# Georgia Department of Economic Development

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2023-01

**Source:** [GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson Named ‘Georgian of the Year’ by Georgia Trend - Albany CEO](https://albanyceo.com/news/2023/01/gdecd-commissioner-pat-wilson-named-georgian-year-georgia-trend/)  ·  `page_009863`

## Facts

- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson was named the 2023 “Georgian of the Year” by Georgia Trend on Friday, January 20th, 2023.
- The award recognizes Commissioner Wilson as the top salesperson for the state and commends his work promoting the advantages of Georgia to attract investments and create jobs.
- A cover story credits the state’s success to a team approach when conducting business and highlights Wilson’s focus on keeping relationships strong with partners and businesses across the state and globe.
- Wilson was appointed economic development Commissioner by former Governor Nathan Deal six years prior to the article's publication.
- Georgia maintained its place as the No. 1 state in the nation to do business for the ninth consecutive year in 2022.
- Former Georgia Governor Sonny Perdue credited Wilson with helping make Georgia one of the best places to grow a business by communicating all the great things that the Georgia governor and legislature have done during those years.
- In August 2022, GDEcD announced that in FY22, investments in expansions and new locations totaled more than $21.2 billion.
- Those FY22 investments would create 51,132 direct new jobs in the state, marking both new records for Georgia.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development’s work providing solutions for companies and keeping communication open were two key factors that helped Georgia land two of its largest-ever investments in FY22.
- Wilson stated he gets emotional when hearing how jobs have changed people's lives and expressed gratitude to the governors who gave him the chance to do his job.
- GDEcD teammates Lee Thomas, Director of the Georgia Film Office, and David Nuckolls, Executive Director of the Center of Innovation, were also recognized in Georgia Trend’s lists for their contributions to the state.
- GDEcD is described as the “quarterback” that brings partners across the state together to make business happen.
