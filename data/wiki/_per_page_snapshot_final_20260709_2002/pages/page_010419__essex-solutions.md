# Essex Solutions

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [LS Essex Solutions, $200 million pre-IPO success](https://en.topdaily.kr/articles/4149)  ·  `page_010419`

## Facts

- Essex Solutions, a US subsidiary of LS Corporation, has succeeded in its Pre-IPO investment attraction.
- The total pre-IPO investment amount is USD 200 million (KRW 290 billion), noted as the largest for a single domestic project fund established in 2024.
- The investment was structured as investors purchasing shares issued by the Mirae Asset-KCGI consortium via a third-party allocation paid-in capital increase.
- The Mirae Asset-KCGI consortium acquired approximately 20% of Essex Solutions' shares.
- Essex Solutions' pre-IPO market capitalization amounts to about USD 1 billion, or approximately KRW 1.45 trillion.
- Leading investment firms Bain Capital, Goldman Sachs, and IMM participated in competitive bidding for the pre-IPO, which was directly led by LS Corporation.
- Essex Solutions' predecessor, Essex Wire Corporation, was an American wire company established in 1930.
- The predecessor acquired a telecommunications wire business in 1954 and later changed its name to Superior Essex (SPSX).
- LS Group acquired SPSX in 2008 for approximately KRW 1 trillion.
- SPSX has steadily invested in R&D in preparation for the electric vehicle era since turning a profit in 2016.
- In 2020, Essex Solutions established a joint venture named 'Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire' with Japan's Furukawa Electric to advance into the global winding market.
- EV coils are copper wires wound around electric vehicle drive motors to convert electrical energy into kinetic energy.
- Essex Solutions acquired all of EFMW's shares from Furukawa Electric in April of last year and vertically integrated the group's winding corporations to launch Essex Solutions.
- Main products include 'special windings for electric/hybrid vehicles' and 'special windings for large-capacity transformers'.
- Special windings for electric vehicles are core materials for drive motors that require technology capable of withstanding high voltages.
- Essex Solutions possesses the world's best technological edge in this area.
- Products are supplied to the world's number one electric vehicle manufacturer and global leading automakers requiring high quality.
- Essex Solutions holds an overwhelming market share of 1st place in North America, excluding China, where EV production is most active.
- The company maintains production bases in global markets including North America, South America, Europe, and Asia to respond to regional protectionist measures.
- Essex Solutions aims to increase its electric vehicle market share in North America to 70% and in Europe to over 50% within the next five years.
- LS Group plans to complete the selection of an underwriter by early this year and pursue a full-scale listing within 2025.
- The company initially planned to list on KOSPI but is also considering listing on NASDAQ due to strong interest from local U.S. investment banks.
- An LS official stated that the market has recognized Essex Solutions' future business value following its large-scale pre-IPO success, which is essential in the era of electric vehicles and power super cycles.
- The official added that Essex Solutions will continue to solidify its position as a leader in the winding industry through continuous R&D development and ultra-gap technological superiority.
