# Hitachi Automotive Electric Motor Systems America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hitachi’s electric future - Kentucky Living](https://www.kentuckyliving.com/energy/business-spotlight/hitachis-electric-future)  ·  `page_008525`

## Facts

- The Hitachi Automotive Electric Motor Systems America, Inc. plant in Berea plans to manufacture motors for electric vehicles.
- Production could begin between April 2022 and March 2023, in part based on the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic.
- The operation currently employs 18 people and is expected to grow to approximately 200 in 2023.
- Established in Kentucky in March 2020, Hitachi Automotive Electric Motor Systems America will supply motors to Honda of America Manufacturing, Inc.
- Shingo Nakamura, president of Hitachi Automotive Electric Motor Systems America, Inc., stated that the company is pleased to establish this new business in Berea, where Hitachi Automotive Systems Americas has forged a robust, ongoing relationship with the local community and state officials over the years.
- Shingo Nakamura noted that the new venture is a critical piece in strengthening Hitachi’s foundation in the fast-growing electric vehicle market and supporting the revitalization of the economy in Kentucky through job creation in response to COVID-19.
- Kentucky Gov. Andy Beshear stated that the new venture, along with the jobs and wages it will create, adds true momentum to economic recovery and points to a bright future as they build a better Kentucky.
- Gov. Andy Beshear emphasized that from an automotive workforce and industry perspective, it is crucial that Kentucky continues to attract forward-looking projects as the auto industry undergoes a years-long tectonic shift to electric vehicles.
- The new subsidiary joins more than 500 other automotive suppliers and four automotive assembly plants operating in Kentucky.
- Kentucky’s geographic position in the middle of the country’s automotive corridor provides suppliers quick access to dozens of automotive assembly plants within a day’s drive.
- In Kentucky, Hitachi Automotive Systems Americas operates four facilities.
- Two of these facilities are manufacturing plants in Berea making brake and suspension components.
- The other two facilities are manufacturing and warehousing operations in Harrodsburg producing electronic control systems, actuators and fuel system components.
- Mike Williams, president and CEO of Blue Grass Energy, stated that his company is proud to supply electricity for Hitachi Automotive Electric Motor Systems America, Inc. in Berea and is excited to see the development of electric vehicle technology and production in their service area.
- Mike Williams added that their community is fortunate to count Hitachi as one of their own.
- On April 1, 2021, Hitachi Automotive Systems Americas, Inc. will change its name to Hitachi Astemo Americas with its subsidiaries also changing their names to follow the new naming convention.
