## Facts

- EON Reality announced a strategic initiative to cultivate the skilled workforce for Georgia’s automotive and EV manufacturing ecosystem.

- The initiative is amplified by EON Reality’s Spatial AI technology.
- EON Reality will forge partnerships with Georgia’s state agencies, leading educational institutions, and key automotive manufacturers.
- The goal is to deploy XR and AI training infrastructure to equip thousands of Georgians with skills for modern vehicle production careers.
- Georgia is securing its position as a major hub for next-generation mobility.
- Hyundai’s $5.5B+ MetaPlant America near Savannah is attracting massive investments.
- The Hyundai plant is poised to create over 8,100 jobs.
- There are significant expansions in the broader automotive supply chain.
- Modern automotive and EV facilities demand proficiency in EV battery assembly, high-voltage systems safety, robotics operation, complex assembly techniques, and collaboration with AI-driven manufacturing systems.
- Conventional training methods struggle to deliver these competencies at the required speed, scale, and safety levels.
- EON Reality provides an AI-enhanced solution for Georgia’s automotive workforce needs.
- The initiative leverages the EON-XR Platform, a cloud-based SaaS solution for creating and deploying photorealistic, interactive 3D training simulations.
- The platform features a Spatial AI Text-to-XR engine that transforms complex automotive assembly instructions, EV battery manuals, or SOPs into dynamic XR training modules in under 80 seconds.
- This dramatically accelerates curriculum development and updates.
- The Specialized AI Academy for Income – Automotive/EV Track integrates immersive XR simulation with AI-driven personalized learning paths.
- The program focuses on demonstrable skill mastery, awards industry-recognized micro-credentials, and creates direct hiring funnels with leading automotive employers in Georgia.
- EON Career Guide & Compass provides intelligent AI career advisors to help Georgians explore automotive manufacturing roles, assess skills, and map training journeys.
- The AI-Era Automotive Curriculum is co-created with industry experts from Georgia’s automotive sector.
- Modules build proficiency in technical tasks, interacting with AI, interpreting data from connected machines, and collaborating with robotic systems.
- Trainees are immersed in realistic virtual assembly lines and simulated high-voltage environments.
- EON Reality’s methodology enables learners to grasp complex skills up to 4x faster.
- Graduates are designed to be productive and safe from their first day.
- EON Reality is actively engaging with Georgia’s key stakeholders through deep partnerships.
