## Facts

- Kumho Tire U.S.A. is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, and serves as the US sales, marketing, and distribution arm of Kumho Tire Co. Inc.
- Kumho Tire U.S.A. announced a new lineup of ultra-high performance tires: the Ecsta Sport All-Season PA71, Ecsta Sport PS72, and Ecsta Sport S PS72.
- The new tire lineup leverages advancements in AI and long-standing relationships with OE manufacturers for rapid iterative design testing.
- The Ecsta Sport All-Season PA71 features next-generation compound technology designed to enhance grip and traction rain or shine without sacrificing performance characteristics.
- The Ecsta Sport All-Season PA71 includes a 50,000-mile treadwear warranty.
- The Ecsta Sport All-Season PA71 utilizes wide and stiff outside tread blocks to add stability when cornering on high-horsepower vehicles.
- The Ecsta Sport All-Season PA71 incorporates lateral grooves in the tread design to aid water drainage for maintained performance in wet weather.
- Snow performance for the Ecsta Sport All-Season PA71 is enhanced with zig-zag grooves on the inside tread blocks.
- The Ecsta Sport All-Season PA71 minimizes road noise using rounded edges of tread blocks and a proprietary K-NOISE DEFENDER dimple design to interrupt air flow through tire grooves.
- The Ecsta Sport PS72 and Ecsta Sport S PS72 are ultra-high performance summer tires available in two tread configurations.
- The summer tire lines provide maximum traction with an enhanced compound for heightened handling, stability, cornering, and steering response.
- The summer tires include a 30,000-mile treadwear warranty.
- The Ecsta Sport All-Season PA71 is available in 56 sizes ranging from 16-inch to 21-inch wheel diameters.
- The Ecsta Sport PS72 is available in 56 sizes from 17-inch to 21-inch wheel diameters.
- The Ecsta Sport S PS72 is available in 52 different sizes from 18-inch to 22-inch wheel diameters.
- The new tires will debut at SEMA 2024, running Nov. 5-8 in Las Vegas, and will be showcased at Booth #81398 outside the convention center in the Silver Lot.
- The new tire lineup will be available to customers in the spring of 2025.
- Shawn Denlein serves as the president of sales and marketing for Kumho Tire U.S.A.
- Kumho Tire has more than 60 years of history pioneering innovative approaches to developing tires.
