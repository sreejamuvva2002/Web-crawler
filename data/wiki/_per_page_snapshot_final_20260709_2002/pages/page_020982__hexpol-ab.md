# HEXPOL AB

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-19

**Source:** [Kenneth Bloom Appointed Interim President of HEXPOL Compounding Americas - TipRanks.com](https://www.tipranks.com/news/company-announcements/kenneth-bloom-appointed-interim-president-of-hexpol-compounding-americas)  ·  `page_020982`

## Facts

- HEXPOL AB has appointed Kenneth Bloom as the interim President of HEXPOL Compounding Americas, effective September 19, 2025.
- The appointment follows the departure of Gary Moore.
- Bloom previously held the same position from 2020 to 2022 and brings over 20 years of experience in the polymer industry.
- Bloom is expected to lead the company through its next phase of growth and value creation until a permanent successor is found.
- HEXPOL is a leading global polymers group specializing in advanced polymer compounds, gaskets for plate heat exchangers, and polymer wheels for truck and castor wheel applications.
- The company serves system suppliers in industries such as automotive, engineering, building and construction, transportation, energy, consumer goods, and medical equipment.
- HEXPOL is organized into two main business areas: HEXPOL Compounding and HEXPOL Engineered Products.
- The company reached sales of 20,437 MSEK in 2024.
- HEXPOL maintains a workforce of approximately 5,000 employees across fourteen countries.
