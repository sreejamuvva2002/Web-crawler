# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-03-01

**Source:** [Economic Champion Spotlight: Novelis Inc. | CenterState CEO](https://centerstateceo.com/our-members/member-spotlight/economic-champion-spotlight-novelis-inc)  ·  `page_047421`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is the world leader in aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Novelis will invest approximately $130 million to upgrade its operations in Oswego.
- The investment aims to meet growing customer demand for sustainable, aluminum flat rolled products.
- The project increases hot mill capacity by 124,000 metric tons at the plant.
- The Oswego plant serves the can, automotive, and specialty products markets.
- The project enhances finishing capabilities for automotive sheet.
- The investment includes upgrades to the plant’s hot reversing mill motors and drive train.
- The investment includes upgrades to the plant’s hot finishing mill coolant systems.
- The investment includes an enhancement of batch annealing capabilities.
- The project aligns with Novelis’ sustainability goal to reduce energy use by 10% by 2026.
- These projects will begin in spring 2022.
- These projects are expected to be completed in 2024.
- The investment follows the expansion of automotive finishing capabilities and recycling capacity at the Oswego plant over the last decade.
- Over the last decade, the Oswego plant added more than 300,000 metric tons of state-of-the-art automotive finishing capacity.
- Over the last decade, the Oswego plant expanded its recycling center to process roughly 25 million pounds of automotive aluminum scrap per month.
- Novelis is the world’s leading provider of aluminum automotive sheet used for making structural components and exterior body panels.
- Novelis aluminum can be found in more than 225 vehicle models produced by leading automakers around the globe.
- Novelis aluminum supports safe, sustainable, and cost-effective lightweighting of vehicles and commercial trucks.
- Lightweighting with Novelis aluminum results in better performance, agility, increased fuel economy, and reduced carbon emissions.
