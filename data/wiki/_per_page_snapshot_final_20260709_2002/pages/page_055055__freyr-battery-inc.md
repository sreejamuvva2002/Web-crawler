## Facts

- FREYR Battery has completed its redomicile from Luxembourg to the U.S.
- Each issued and outstanding Ordinary Share and public/private Warrant became, on a one-for-one basis, shares of Common Stock and Warrants of FREYR Battery, Inc.
- The Board of Directors believes redomiciling will simplify corporate structure and streamline reporting requirements for consolidated regulatory compliance.
- Redomiciling provides opportunities to improve operational efficiencies and financial flexibility in corporate treasury, cash management, risk management, and tax functions.
- The move enhances eligibility for inclusion in equity indexes and triggers benchmarking from actively managed funds, delivering significant uplift in fund flows.
- FREYR will benefit from well-established principles of corporate governance under Delaware law, which are more closely aligned with NYSE listing standards and SEC governance requirements.
- The redomicile positions FREYR to better respond to global tax developments and U.S. incentive programs for battery manufacturers, considering recent changes in U.S. tax laws and federal incentive programs.
- FREYR Battery is a developer of clean, next-generation battery cell production capacity.
- The company's mission is to accelerate the decarbonization of global energy and transportation systems by producing clean, cost-competitive batteries.
- FREYR seeks to serve primary markets of energy storage systems (ESS) and commercial mobility, with an ambition to serve the passenger electric vehicles market (EV).
- FREYR is commissioning and operating its Customer Qualification Plant (CQP) for technology development in Mo I Rana.
