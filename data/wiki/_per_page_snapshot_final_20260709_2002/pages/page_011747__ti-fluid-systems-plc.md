## Facts

- TI Fluid Systems plc is a global industry leader in highly engineered automotive fluid storage, carrying and delivery systems and thermal management products and systems.
- The Group announced its financial results for the year ended 31 December 2024 on 11 March 2025.
- Adjusted Revenue for 2024 was €3,360.3 million, a decrease of 4.4% (or 3.5% in constant currency) from €3,516.2 million in 2023.
- Adjusted EBIT for 2024 was €260.8 million, an increase of 0.5% (or 1.9% in constant currency) from €259.6 million in 2023.
- Adjusted EBIT Margin % for 2024 was 7.8%, an increase of 40 basis points from 7.4% in 2023.
- Adjusted Net Income for 2024 was €136.7 million, compared to €132.8 million in 2023.
