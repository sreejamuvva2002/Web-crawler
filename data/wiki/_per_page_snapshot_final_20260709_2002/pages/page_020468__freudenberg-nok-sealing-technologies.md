# Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [FN Lube and FN Lube Plus for O-ring installation](https://sealingtechnologies.eu/contact/news/freudenberg-nok-introduces-fn-lube-and-fn-lube-plus/)  ·  `page_020468`

## Facts

- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies introduced two new lubricants for the installation of static seals: FN Lube and FN Lube Plus.
- The products were developed with support from Klueber Lubrication, which is part of the Freudenberg Group.
- The lubricants are aimed primarily at applications involving O-rings.
- Lubrication plays an important role during O-ring installation to prevent twisting, damage, or incorrect positioning caused by excessive friction.
- FN Lube and FN Lube Plus were developed to reduce installation friction while remaining compatible with different elastomer materials.
- Freudenberg-NOK tested the lubricants for compatibility with elastomers, specifically examining the retention of tensile strength and elongation.
- The new products are intended for static sealing applications where O-rings must remain in place during installation without being stressed by excessive friction or unsuitable lubricants.
- The collaboration between Freudenberg-NOK and Klueber Lubrication combines sealing technology knowledge with specialist lubricant experience.
- The resulting product is an installation aid that becomes part of the overall sealing system rather than a separate component.
- FN Lube and FN Lube Plus are available through Freudenberg-NOK’s North American distribution network.
- The products have been integrated into the company’s digital catalogue and PartnerHUB platform.
- Freudenberg-NOK aims to support customers in achieving more reliable installation of O-rings and other static seals.
- Consistent installation during series production can help prevent damage, rejects, and later sealing problems.
