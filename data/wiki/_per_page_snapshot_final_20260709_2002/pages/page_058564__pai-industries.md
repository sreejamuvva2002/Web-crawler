# PAI Industries

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-08-28

**Source:** [PAI Industries Bolsters Capabilities With In-House Carburizing Vacuum System - Heat Treat Today](https://www.heattreattoday.com/pai-industries-bolsters-capabilities-with-in-house-carburizing-vacuum-system/)  ·  `page_058564`

## Facts

- PAI Industries is a Georgia-based manufacturer of parts for the heavy-duty truck industry.
- The company expanded its in-house heat treatment capabilities with a NANO vacuum furnace system.
- PAI partnered with vacuum furnace manufacturer ECM USA to transition its production line from outsourced heat treatment to in-house vacuum technology.
- The new setup performs low pressure carburizing and through hardening around the clock.
- The upgraded system will increase capacity and expedite production at PAI’s 112,000 square foot manufacturing facility.
- The NANO furnace system is comprised of one module with three stacked heating cells.
- The heating cells can be extended to six heating cells corresponding to two heating modules.
- The system includes gas quenching cells for quenching parts.
- The carburizing cells are stacked to minimize the installation footprint.
- The furnace system integrates directly into the production line to reduce cycle time.
- The integration simplifies the flow between machining and heat treatment.
