# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2010-07-09

**Source:** [Pirelli NYC Move to Include 10 People: Report - Tire Review Magazine](https://www.tirereview.com/pirelli-nyc-move-to-include-10-people-report/)  ·  `page_048516`

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America will move as many as 10 of its Georgia-based staff to its new sales and marketing offices in New York City.
- Pirelli plans to replace those 10 relocated employees from its Rome, Ga., headquarters with either sales and marketing support staff or additional production staff.
- PTNA announced it would open a sales and marketing office in New York to “improve our access to key partners in the financial, fashion and advertising sectors,” and to better serve the northeast U.S. and Canada.
- PTNA’s Rome headquarters facility includes its lone U.S. manufacturing facility.
- PTNA’s Rome headquarters employs some 225 people.
