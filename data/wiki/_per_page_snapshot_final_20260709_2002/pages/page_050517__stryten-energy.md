# Stryten Energy

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-09-21

**Source:** [Stryten Energy To Supply Batteries For Continental Battery’s Aftermarket Business](https://www.counterman.com/stryten-energy-to-supply-batteries-for-continental-batterys-aftermarket-business/)  ·  `page_050517`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy is an Alpharetta, Georgia-based provider of energy-storage solutions.
- Stryten Energy announced it has entered into an agreement with Continental Battery Co. to supply batteries for its automotive aftermarket business.
- Continental Battery’s aftermarket business includes more than 100 branches and a distribution network of more than 30,000 dealer locations.
- Under the new agreement, Continental Battery Co. will gain access to Stryten’s portfolio of automotive batteries.
- Stryten’s battery portfolio includes a large range of options from standard flooded batteries to advanced technologies such as enhanced flooded batteries (EFB).
- Bill Nonnamaker, vice president, transportation sales for Stryten Energy, stated they are looking forward to the partnership to supply high-quality and top-performing automotive batteries.
- Nonnamaker noted that Stryten’s manufacturing operations are scaled to meet the increasing demand Continental is seeing in the automotive aftermarket.
- Grant Carona, senior vice president, supply chain for Continental Battery Co., stated that Stryten offers a new supply of quality batteries for their automotive aftermarket business.
- Carona noted that Continental’s automotive aftermarket business is experiencing tremendous growth.
- Carona added that the partnership will help ensure their nationwide branches and dealer network are reliably supplied with the right mix of automotive batteries to meet customer needs.
