# Hanon Systems

## Facts

- Hanon is a global supplier of thermal and energy management solutions for electric and conventional vehicles.
- Hanon’s products include components used in the heating and/or cooling operations of battery boxes and vehicle interiors.
- In an effort to increase Hanon’s presence within the US electric vehicle EV market, Hanon is expanding its operations to rural Statesboro, Georgia.
- Hanon is building a manufacturing facility to serve a nearby EV manufacturing plant.
- The facility will total approximately 112,000 sq. ft.
- The facility will have the opportunity to expand to 330,000 square feet, should Hanon need additional space.
