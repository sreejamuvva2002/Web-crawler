# Lehigh Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-07-18

**Source:** [Lehigh Technologies opens new Micronize Rubber Power plant in Spain](https://www.tractionnews.com/lehigh-technologies-starts-production-micronized-rubber-powder-plant-spain/)  ·  `page_057119`

## Facts

- Lehigh Technologies is a Michelin subsidiary that produces sustainable raw materials from end-of-life tires and post-industrial rubber.
- Lehigh Technologies started production at its new Murillo el Fruto Micronized Rubber Power (MRP) Plant in Navarra, Spain.
- The Murillo plant is the first facility for Lehigh Technologies outside of the United States.
- The Murillo plant was constructed in partnership with Lehigh’s Spanish partner Hera Holding.
- The Murillo plant incorporates Lehigh’s proprietary cryogenic turbo mill technology.
- The Murillo plant has a production capacity of 10,000 metric tons of MRP per year.
- The Murillo plant will produce Lehigh’s PolyDyne and MicroDyne range of MRP products.
- Lehigh’s MRP products are currently used by customers in more than 18 countries worldwide.
- Jason Stravinski serves as Lehigh’s VP of Operations.
- Engineers from Lehigh U.S., Lehigh Spain, and Hera Holding collaborated with the Jex project team to complete the Murillo plant on time and on budget.
- "I’m very proud of the international team that made this project successful," said Jason Stravinski, Lehigh’s VP of Operations.
- Lehigh expects the Murillo plant to be a crucial component in meeting rising demand for green chemicals in Europe and beyond.
- Construction of the Navarra plant was managed by Lehigh and executed by UK-based Jex Engineering.
- The Navarra plant construction received support from the Dennis Group and Air Products.
- The Navarra plant was commissioned at the end of June 2018.
- The Navarra plant employs the exact same layout and equipment as Lehigh’s Tucker, Georgia plant in the U.S.
- Using the same layout and equipment as the Tucker, Georgia plant ensures consistent quality and products specification across all locations.
- "Demand for Lehigh’s products is increasing rapidly and this new plant comes at the perfect time to support the growth," said Lluís Molina, General Manager Lehigh Spain.
- The Navarra plant will supply the tire, bitumen, coatings, construction, and polyurethane industries in Europe and the Middle East with Lehigh’s MRP products.
- "The Murillo plant is the next step in Lehigh’s mission to commercialize MRP for customers in a wide range of performance driven markets."
