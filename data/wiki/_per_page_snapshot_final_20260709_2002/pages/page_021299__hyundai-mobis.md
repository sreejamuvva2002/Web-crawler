## Facts

- Hyundai MOBIS is one of the largest leading auto parts specialist companies in the world.
- Hyundai MOBIS forms the parts and service arm for South Korean automakers Kia Motors Georgia.
- The Hyundai MOBIS Georgia Plant wields its cutting-edge vehicle modularization technology to manufacture and supply chassis modules, cockpit modules and front-end modules to the KIA Georgia factory.
- In addition to the three major modules, our Team Members assemble front and rear bumpers in a just-in-sequence manufacturing environment.
