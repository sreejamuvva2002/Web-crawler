# Fanello Industries

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Ryerson acquires laser cutting services provider Fanello Industries | Laser Focus World](https://www.laserfocusworld.com/industrial-laser-solutions/article/14220889/ryerson-acquires-laser-cutting-services-provider-fanello-industries)  ·  `page_054511`

## Facts

- Industrial metal processor and distributor Ryerson Holding Corporation (Chicago, IL) has acquired Fanello Industries (Lavonia, GA).
- Fanello Industries supplies laser cutting, blanking, stamping, bending, and machining metal solutions to several industries in the Southeastern U.S.
- Fanello Industries employs approximately 150 people.
- Fanello Industries has annual revenue of approximately $20 million.
- Ryerson has worked closely with Vince Fanello and his management team for many years both as a customer and a supplier of value-added fabrication services, according to Kevin Richardson, Ryerson's President, South-East Region.
- Fanello will continue to serve the market under its own brand name following the acquisition.
- Ryerson has also acquired metal fabricator Laserflex.
