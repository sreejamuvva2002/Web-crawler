# Carcoustics

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Automotive industry supplier to create 200 jobs in Buford](https://www.ajc.com/news/local/automotive-industry-supplier-create-200-jobs-buford/JANxPn5QmoSpjUcw0zJSPL/)  ·  `page_018330`

## Facts

- German manufacturer Carcoustics, a supplier to the automotive industry, will create 200 jobs and invest $6 million in a new Buford facility over the next five years.
- The newly created jobs will include positions in manufacturing.
- The new 130-square-foot manufacturing facility is located at 4620 Thompson Mill Road in Buford.
- The facility will house the manufacturing of solution-oriented acoustic and thermally effective components for the automotive industry.
- Stefan Vallentin, project manager at Carcoustics, stated they are very happy to have found the location for their new plant in the South of the U.S. here in Georgia.
- Vallentin noted a great international transport connection and that the site is logistically well located between BMW in South Carolina and Mercedes in Alabama.
- Potential customers include Automotive Tier I/ Tier II, Volkswagen, Mercedes Vans, Volvo, and Asian OEMs like Nissan, Honda, Toyota, Kia, and Hyundai.
- The location offers new possibilities regarding Carcoustics' growth strategy, especially in the U.S.
