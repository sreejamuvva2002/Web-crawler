# Vanguard National Trailer Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2015-09-03

**Source:** [Vanguard Opens Plant in Trenton, Georgia | Trailer Body Builders](https://www.trailer-bodybuilders.com/trailers/article/21741077/vanguard-opens-plant-in-trenton-georgia)  ·  `page_013076`

## Facts

- Vanguard National Trailer Corporation started production today at its new 356,000-square-foot dry van trailer manufacturing plant in Trenton, Georgia.
- The facility is located 30 miles southwest of Chattanooga, Tennessee.
- This location serves as Vanguard’s second dry van manufacturing plant, joining the primary facility in Monon, Indiana.
- Planning for the facility began three years ago, with Trenton selected after considering locations in Tennessee, Alabama and other locations in Georgia.
- The company broke ground on the 100-acre site in the Dade County Industrial Park on Sept. 3, 2015.
- President Charlie Mudd stated the project came in on time and under the $36 million budget.
- Vanguard noted there is not currently a dry van manufacturing plant in the Southeast United States, prompting the decision to locate one there.
- The company designed the facility as a relatively small 10,000-trailer capacity plant to better service customers in the Southeast.
- The state of Georgia, Dade County and the city of Trenton provided very strong support for the project.
- At full capacity, the plant is anticipated to add 400 jobs to the area.
- So far, 70 positions have been filled at the new facility.
- The plant’s estimated 10,000-trailer capacity is expected to provide substantial relief from backlogs that are stretching out almost a year in some cases.
- Vanguard’s written plan targets full capacity within three years, with an acknowledgment that start-up will be very slow.
- The entire project was designed and planned by the Vanguard team.
- The plant is equipped to operate at high efficiency while maintaining the capability to provide wide customization for customers.
- Vanguard National & CIMC Intermodal ranked fifth on Trailer/Body Builders’ list of the top 25 manufacturers for 2015.
- Vanguard National & CIMC Intermodal produced a total of 42,594 units in 2015, up from 28,374 in 2014.
- The 2015 production volume included 10,835 dry freight vans, 2700 refrigerated units, 15,116 ISO container chassis, and 13,943 domestic chassis.
