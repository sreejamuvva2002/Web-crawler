# AAMCO Transmission Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-11-30

**Source:** [AAMCO Partners with Global Powertrain Systems | November 30, 2015  | Ratchet and Wrench](https://www.ratchetandwrench.com/articles/3511-aamco-partners-with-global-powertrain-systems)  ·  `page_020618`

## Facts

- AAMCO Franchise announced a partnership with its sister company, Global Powertrain Systems (GPS).
- The partnership gives AAMCO franchisees immediate access to a vertically integrated supply chain with preferred pricing on the brand’s core transmission category.
- Brian O'Donnell, senior vice president of franchise sales at AAMCO Transmission Inc., stated that Global Powertrain Systems allows franchisees to control the cost, quality and speed of delivery on their highest ticket item, transmissions.
- The partnership has driven greater efficiencies for the brand.
- It provides an opportunity to focus on growing the Total Car Care business and capture more market share in the general automotive repair industry.
- Sherri Underwood, co-owner of an AAMCO service center in Levittown, Pa., noted that the partnership makes the business model very simple to operate and easier to run a successful business with less support.
- Benefits of the partnership include a better warranty for customers.
- The partnership saves salary costs that would otherwise be paid to a builder.
- It reduces challenges associated with maintaining a specialized labor workforce.
