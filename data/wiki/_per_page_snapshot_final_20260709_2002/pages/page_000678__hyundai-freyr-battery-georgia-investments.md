# Hyundai & Freyr Battery Georgia Investments

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-24

**Source:** [Georgia - Bid Notices - Welcome to US Public Works -](https://uspublicworks.com/georgia-bid-notices-city-county-state-municipal-dot-hyundai/)  ·  `page_000678`

## Facts

- Hyundai announced plans on November 24, 2022 to invest nearly $1 billion ($926 million) in a new manufacturing plant in Bryan County, Georgia.
- The plant will manufacture EVs and EV powertrains.
- Construction for the facility is scheduled to start early in 2023.
- Production at the facility is scheduled to begin in 2025.
- The plant is expected to produce 900,000 EV power systems and 450,000 integrated charging control units.
- The Hyundai Mobis plant will be located in Belfast Commerce Park in Richmond Hill, Georgia.
- Georgia Governor Kemp announced on November 11, 2022 that Freyr Battery is planning to invest about $1.6 billion in a new EV battery manufacturing plant in Newnan, Georgia (Coweta County).
- The company is calling the Freyr Battery project “Giga America”.
- The first phase of the Giga America project will be valued at $1.7 billion.
- The company plans to start engineering work on the project in a few months from the November 2022 announcement.
- Georgia provided financial incentives to attract the development of the Freyr Battery project.
- Hyundai announced on May 22, 2022 its plan to build a $6.5 billion Hyundai EV Car Assembly Plant and EV battery Manufacturing Plant located just south of Savannah, Georgia.
- Information regarding the Savannah plant is hosted on the Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority website.
- Over $5 billion of work related to the Savannah plant is available for bidding through the Joint Development Authority.
