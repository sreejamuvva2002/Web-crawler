# Adient

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-04-27

**Source:** [Adient buys Romulus seat-foam plant from Woodbridge | ADNT Stock News](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/ADNT/adient-acquires-automotive-seating-foam-plant-in-romulus-0rrfn8z8gez3.html)  ·  `page_015355`

## Facts

- Adient announced the acquisition of a foam production plant in Romulus, MI on April 27, 2026.
- The acquired facility joins Adient's network of 10 foam plants in the Americas.
- The purchase brings Adient's global foam manufacturing footprint to 30 plants.
- The acquisition agreement included the building, land, production equipment, inventory, and related assets.
- Existing Romulus plant employees will transfer to become Adient employees.
- The existing collective bargaining agreement with the UAW will remain in place for the transferred workforce.
- Adient stated the acquisition supports its strategic growth plan.
- The addition expands Adient's manufacturing footprint to serve multiple automaker customers.
- Adient purchased the Romulus plant from automotive supplier Woodbridge.
- Adient is a global leader in automotive seating with more than 65,000 employees across 29 countries.
- Adient operates approximately 200 manufacturing and assembly plants worldwide.
- Adient produces and delivers automotive seating for all major OEMs, ranging from complete seating systems to individual components.
