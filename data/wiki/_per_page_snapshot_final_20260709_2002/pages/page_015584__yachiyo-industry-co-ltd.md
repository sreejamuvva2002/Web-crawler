# Yachiyo Industry Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-03-31

**Source:** [Yachiyo Industry Co., Ltd. Business Report FY ended Mar. 2018 - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/cf/s500_209_hl2017)  ·  `page_015584`

## Facts

- Yachiyo Industry Co., Ltd. reported FY ended Mar. 31, 2018 revenue of 165,562 million JPY, a 1.6% decrease from FY 2017.
- Operating profit for FY ended Mar. 31, 2018 was 9,369 million JPY, down 14.9% year-over-year.
- Profit before tax for FY ended Mar. 31, 2018 was 9,223 million JPY.
- Profit attributable to owners of the parent for FY ended Mar. 31, 2018 was 4,796 million JPY.
- Revenue growth factors included increased product orders for auto parts outside Japan and favorable currency translation.
- Operating profit changes were driven by higher revenue, optimized product selling prices, a single impairment loss, cost increases for new models in North America, and higher expenses for increasing production volumes.
- The company announced it will transfer its vehicle assembly business to Honda Motor Co., Ltd. in April 2018 to consolidate managerial resources into parts businesses for sustainable growth.
- A new headquarters will be established to supervise global production sites and strengthen automotive parts production, with current business management operations transferred there.
- Yachiyo is developing lightweight, 70MPa high-pressure hydrogen tanks for zero-emission vehicles.
- Yachiyo is developing 45kPa high-pressure, hermetically sealed resin fuel tanks for PHEVs.
- The developed fuel tanks are compliant with LEV III Standards.
- Commercial production of a next-generation panoramic sunroof with a 35% wider opening is planned for 2020.
- Yachiyo is commercializing carbon fiber reinforced plastic products using pre-preg-compression-molding and resin-transfer-molding technology.
- R&D activities include developing metal-honeycomb, high-precision fuel-cell carriers that comply with exhaust-gas regulations worldwide.
- R&D activities cover plastic fuel tanks with new structural designs, new types of sunroofs, environmental technology for automotive parts, special-needs vehicles, and driver-assist systems.
- Yachiyo of America Inc. is located in Ohio, U.S., established in July 2008, and handles designing and testing of sunroofs and fuel tanks manufactured in North America.
- R&D expenditure for FY ended Mar. 31, 2018 was 4,305 million JPY.
- Capital expenditure for FY ended Mar. 31, 2018 totaled 9,460 million JPY overall, with 2,402 million JPY allocated to the Americas.
- Yachiyo Manufacturing of America, LLC is located in Georgia, USA, and has planned production facility investments of 314 million yen from Apr. 2018 to Feb. 2019 to renew facilities, reduce energy consumption, and streamline operations.
- US Yachiyo Inc. in Ohio, USA has planned production facility investments of 2,501 million yen from Apr. 2018 to Mar. 2020 to renew facilities, reduce energy consumption, and streamline operations.
- AY Manufacturing Ltd. in Ohio, USA has planned production facility investments of 383 million yen from Jan. 2018 to Mar. 2019 to renew facilities, reduce energy consumption, and streamline operations.
- Sales forecast for FY ended Mar. 31, 2019 for automotive parts is 1,630 (in 100 million JPY), representing a 1.6% decrease from actuals.
- Sunroof sales forecast for FY ended Mar. 31, 2019 is 540 (in 100 million JPY).
- Fuel tank sales forecast for FY ended Mar. 31, 2019 is 397 (in 100 million JPY).
