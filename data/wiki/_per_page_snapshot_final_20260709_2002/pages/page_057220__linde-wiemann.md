## Facts

- For over 80 years, Linde + Wiemann has been a trusted partner to the automotive industry, developing and manufacturing structural body components such as sills, B- and A-pillars, front-end, cockpit, and seat structures.
- The company develops and manufactures structural and crash-relevant components and assemblies for the automotive industry.
- Linde + Wiemann utilizes a proprietary hot forming technology called ACCRA to enable innovative solutions that meet high standards for quality, safety, comfort, and lightweight design.
- Linde + Wiemann is based in Dillenburg, Germany, and also operates in the Czech Republic, Spain, Hungary, China, the USA, and South Africa.
- As of 2024, Linde + Wiemann employs more than 2,400 people.
- In 2024, Linde + Wiemann reported over €625 million in revenue.
- Since 2018, the company has used CIM Database PLM to ensure consistent product data, efficient processes, and reliable calculations for product and project costs.
- The adopted PLM system replaces Windows folders and Excel spreadsheets for product data management.
- The platform provides powerful document management capabilities.
- The system supports multi-CAD data management.
- The PLM platform enables process-secure control and execution of projects.
- It provides a reliable calculation basis for product and project costs.
- CIM Database serves as the central platform for product lifecycle management.
- The system includes CAD data management specifically for Catia and NX software.
- It features comprehensive project management functionalities for company-wide project organization.
- Document management is integrated into the product data and project context, including MS Office integration.
- The platform enables integrated calculation of project and product costs to simplify quote calculations.
- Integrated project cost calculation ensures reliable adherence to project budgets.
- A gradual global rollout of the PLM system is being implemented across all company locations.
- The system promotes efficiency and transparency through standardized processes.
- It provides company-wide consistent and reliable product data.
- Users have quick and easy access to all relevant product data and documents through a clear system and powerful enterprise search.
- Tobias Schnüttgen, PLM Team Manager at Linde + Wiemann, stated that CIM Database PLM is particularly open and flexible and can be optimally adapted to their requirements.
