## Facts

- The HL-GA Battery Co. facility is located near Savannah in Ellabell, GA.
- The Ellabell facility is expected to be operational next year.
- The Ellabell facility aims to employ up to 8,500 people eventually.
- Hyundai's footprint in Georgia includes a $5 billion battery joint venture in Cartersville.
- Hyundai also operates a steel mill in Donaldsonville, Louisiana.
- Hyundai is expanding assembly plants in Alabama and Georgia alongside robotics hubs.
