# Adient

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-11-05

## Facts

- Adient is a global leader in automotive seating.
- Adient has approximately 70,000 employees across 29 countries.
- Adient operates approximately 200 manufacturing and assembly plants worldwide.
- Adient produces and delivers automotive seating for all major OEMs.
- Adient's expertise spans from complete seating systems to individual components.
- Q4 2025 GAAP net income was $18M with diluted EPS of $0.22.
- Q4 2025 Adjusted diluted EPS was $0.52.
- Q4 2025 Adjusted EBITDA was $226M with a margin of 6.1%.
- Full-year FY25 generated $204M in free cash flow.
- Adient returned $125M to shareholders via share repurchases in FY25, representing approximately 7% of shares outstanding at the beginning of the fiscal year.
- Gross debt totaled approximately $2.4B and net debt approximately $1.4B as of September 30, 2025.
- Cash and cash equivalents were $958M as of September 30, 2025.
- FY26 outlook expects improved business performance to be offset by lower customer production volumes and increased growth investments.
- Jerome Dorlack serves as Adient's president and chief executive officer.
- Mark Oswald serves as Adient's executive vice president and chief financial officer.
