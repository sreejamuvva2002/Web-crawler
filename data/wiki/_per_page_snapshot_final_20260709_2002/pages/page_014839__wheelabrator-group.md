## Facts

- The company designs and manufactures a comprehensive range of shot blasting, air blasting, and shot peening equipment, parts, and services.
- Wheelabrator serves the automotive industry alongside aerospace, foundry, forging, shipbuilding, and general manufacturing.
- Wheelabrator Group operates as a core brand within the Norican Group structure.
- The company maintains a worldwide network of technology centers, manufacturing facilities, sales offices, and service teams across the Americas, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and other key industrial regions.
