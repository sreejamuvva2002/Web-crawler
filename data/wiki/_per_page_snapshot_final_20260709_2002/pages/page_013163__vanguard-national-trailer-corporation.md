# Vanguard National Trailer Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-09-01

**Source:** [400 news jobs coming to Dade County |  | local3news.com](https://www.local3news.com/400-news-jobs-coming-to-dade-county/article_c348b416-e166-51af-bc3d-35705befb373.html)  ·  `page_013163`

## Facts

- Vanguard National Trailer Corporation, a manufacturer of semi-trailers, will expand into Dade County, bringing 400 new jobs to the area.
- The company, based in Indiana, will invest $30 million in the expansion.
- That expansion will require development of nearly 100 acres of land and the building of a new 350,000 square-foot facility near Trenton.
- The new jobs will include both office and manufacturing positions.
- Vanguard president and CEO Charlie Mudd stated the team is excited about expanding production capabilities and thanked the State of Georgia and the Georgia Department of Economic Development for their efforts to help with the project.
- Vanguard manufactures dry freight and refrigerated semi-trailer vans for the North American transportation industry.
- The company has about 47,000 employees world-wide.
