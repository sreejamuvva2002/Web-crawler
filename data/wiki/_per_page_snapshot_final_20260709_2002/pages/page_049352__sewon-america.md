# Sewon America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-02-22

**Source:** [Sewon America Announces 740 New Jobs in Effingham County - Expansion Solutions](https://www.expansionsolutionsmagazine.com/sewon-america-announces-740-new-jobs-in-effingham-county/)  ·  `page_049352`

## Facts

- Sewon America is a subsidiary of Korean-based Sewon Precision Industry Co.
- Sewon America will create 740 new jobs in Rincon.
- Sewon America will invest more than $300 million in a new Effingham County manufacturing facility.
- This project is the largest known private investment in the City of Rincon to date.
- The announcement fulfills the commitment to create jobs and investment in all four Savannah Harbor Interstate-16 Joint Development Authority (JDA) counties.
- The JDA commitments are a result of Hyundai’s Metaplant location in Bryan County.
- Hyundai’s Metaplant is the largest economic development project in state history.
- Suppliers for the Metaplant have now committed over $1.8 billion in investment.
- Supplier investments surpass initial estimates by at least $800 million.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that his first international trip as governor to Korea included congratulating Chairman Kim on recent expansion and discussing future collaboration.
- Speaker of the House Jon Burns, who represents part of Effingham County, helped secure the project and engage Sewon America executives.
- Lt. Governor Burt Jones commended Governor Brian Kemp, Sewon America, and stakeholders on the historic economic development announcement for the City of Rincon.
- Sewon America operates one of the largest automobile body parts manufacturing plants in North America.
- The company opened a manufacturing facility in LaGrange in 2008.
- The LaGrange facility has since doubled in size.
- Following completion of the Rincon facility, Sewon is expected to support 1,600 jobs in Georgia.
- Vice Chairman Kim stated that Sewon America was founded 15 years ago in west Georgia and is building another facility in east Georgia.
- The growth is supported by Governor Kemp, the Georgia Department of Economic Development, and Effingham County.
- Sewon’s new facility will be located at the Grande View industrial park in Rincon.
- The new manufacturing location will produce electric vehicle (EV) body parts for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs), including Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.
- Operations at the new facility are expected to begin in 2025.
- Rincon Mayor Ken Lee welcomed Sewon America to the community and commended the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority for bringing the largest employment and private investment project in the city’s history.
- Chairman of the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority (IDA) Troy Smith stated that Sewon selected Grande View industrial park for significant investment to allow current and future generations to have fulfilling, well-paying careers without leaving Effingham County.
- Executive Director of the Georgia Ports Authority Griff Lynch called the announcement terrific news for Effingham County and the State of Georgia.
- Director of Project Implementation and Supplier Strategy Alyce Thornhill represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team on this competitive project.
- The GDEcD partnered with the Effingham County Industrial Development Authority, Georgia Ports Authority, Georgia Quick Start, and Georgia EMC for this project.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that Sewon has integrated into Georgia’s automotive community through local colleges and Georgia Quick Start.
- Sewon supports Hyundai Motor Group’s operations from Effingham County, which has the skilled workforce, infrastructure, and collaborative spirit to support growth.
- On October 25, 2022, Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America officially broke ground in neighboring Bryan County on its first fully dedicated EV and battery manufacturing facility.
- Georgia is positioned as a hub for the electric mobility industry.
- Since 2020, more than 35 EV-related projects have announced more than $21 billion in investment and 26,700 jobs in Georgia.
- Sewon America was established in 2008 in LaGrange as a Georgia company.
- Sewon primarily operates in the Motor Vehicles and Car Bodies industry within the Transportation Equipment sector, producing stamped body components.
- Sewon Group is celebrated for high-quality automotive parts.
