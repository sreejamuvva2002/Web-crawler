# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-24

**Source:** [Hanon Systems investing more than $40 million in manufacturing facility in Bulloch County](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/hanon-systems-investing-more-40-134156558.html)  ·  `page_055726`

## Facts

- Hanon Systems specializes in automotive climate control systems.
- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp's office announced Hanon Systems will invest more than $40 million for a manufacturing facility in Bulloch County.
- The move is expected to create 160 jobs.
- The facility will be located at Gateway Regional Industrial Park.
- The facility is expected to open in May 2024.
- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp stated, "Georgia's automotive and e-mobility industries continue to grow as we proudly welcome Hanon Systems to Georgia."
- Gov. Kemp noted, "As our state furthers its position as the EV capital of America, new jobs and opportunities like these are coming to hardworking Georgians all over the state and will continue to do so."
- Hyundai's EV assembly plant and battery manufacturing facility is under construction near Ellabell in Bryan County and will open in late 2024.
- Joon Georgia Inc. is investing $317 million into a facility that will manufacture the frame for Hyundai vehicles.
- Ecoplastic will invest more than $200 million for a facility that will make consoles, bumpers and more.
- The auto parts manufacturer will bring more than 450 jobs to the area.
- Billy Allen, chair of the Development Authority of Bulloch County, said, "Statesboro-Bulloch County is honored by Hanon Systems' decision to locate its new facility here."
- Billy Allen noted the company will work closely with the Hyundai Metaplant and the electric vehicle industry.
- Pat Wilson, commissioner for the Georgia Department of Economic Development, stated that suppliers like Hanon Systems are able to successfully support larger industry ecosystems through partnership-driven solutions ranging from workforce training to logistics systems efficiencies.
- Bulloch County is part of the Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority.
