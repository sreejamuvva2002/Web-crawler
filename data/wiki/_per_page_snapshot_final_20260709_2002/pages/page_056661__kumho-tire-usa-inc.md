# Kumho Tire USA Inc.

## Facts

- Production capacity at Kumho Tire USA Inc.'s consumer tire manufacturing plant in Macon, Ga., will increase by an additional 500,000 units per year as part of an expansion initiative that is expected to be completed by the fourth quarter of 2022.
- Currently, Kumho's Macon plant can produce 11,000 passenger tires at capacity, according to MTD's 2021 Facts Issue.
- Parent company Kumho Tire is investing $305 million to expand its tire manufacturing plant in Vietnam.
- According to Kumho officials, upon completion of the expansion in Vietnam, the company forecasts to produce at least 9.3 million tires per year.
- The Vietnam expansion project will gradually be funded beginning from the third quarter of 2021 through the first quarter of 2023, adding a new section of the building to vacant lot space within the overall facility compound.
- Kumho has eight plants spread across Vietnam, the U.S., China and its native South Korea.
- The Macon plant is Kumho's only production facility in the U.S.
- The Macon plant opened in 2015.
- Il Taik Jung, Kumho Tire's president and CEO, stated that increasing production capacity ahead of growing demand is part of their strategy to ensure product availability for customers and support continued growth of partnerships in the North American market.
