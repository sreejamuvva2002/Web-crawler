# GSC Steel Stamping

## Facts

- GSC Steel Stamping operates in Lake City, Georgia occupying 200,000 sq. ft.
- GSC specializes in the automotive parts supply business with Tier 1 supply relationships with some of North America's leading automotive OEMs.
- The facility houses over 40 metal stamping presses consisting of line, progressive, transfer, and tandem operations.
- Press sizes range up to 1000 tons.
- Press bed sizes go up to 3.70 meters x 2.15 meters.
- Press speeds reach up to 80 SPM.
- The facility contains 32 workstations.
- Mechanical assembly processes include component insertion and staking.
- GSC provides Welded and Sub-Assembly services through 26 welding stations.
- Technicians at the welding stations are trained in robotic MIG, projection, robotic stud, and spot-welding.
- The skilled engineering team provides advanced engineering services and product development support to OEM customers.
- GSC utilizes a LEAN manufacturing process to develop optimized production methods for high-quality parts.
- An in-house tool shop designs and constructs tooling systems, performs tool maintenance, and effects engineering changes.
- Tool makers have years of experience acquired from top-notch tooling shops in North America.
- The company maintains a stringent Quality Management System that conducts assessments at each production stage to guarantee part safety and reliability.
- GSC incorporates customer-specific requirements into its quality system.
- The company invests in training and innovative technology to meet evolving automotive sector demands.
- Continuous improvement processes are employed to reduce defects, eliminate waste, and add value.
- Program Managers coordinate internal resources for successful program launches and efficient ongoing production.
- Program Managers act as liaisons to customers and maintain ownership for program success throughout its lifecycle.
- Program Managers handle Process Development, Gray and Black Box Testing, APQP, PPAP, and Sample Warrants.
