# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-03-03

**Source:** [YAMAHA MOTOR MANUFACTURING AND YAMAHA GOLF-CAR COMPANY DONATE NEW GOLF CAR TO HELP PATIENTS OF PIEDMONT NEWNAN HOSPITAL | Golf Cart Resource](https://golfcartresource.com/yamaha-motor-manufacturing-and-yamaha-golf-car-company-donate-new-golf-car-to-help-patients-of-piedmont-newnan-hospital/)  ·  `page_015746`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America operates a facility in Newnan, GA.
- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America and Yamaha Golf-Car Company donated a new golf car to Piedmont Newnan Hospital.
- The donated vehicle is designated for hospital volunteers and to provide a free shuttle service for patients arriving at and departing from the hospital.
- Mike Robertson serves as the CEO of Piedmont Newnan Hospital.
- The recent opening of Piedmont Medical Plaza II has increased patient volumes, creating a need for additional shuttle capacity.
- Following this donation, the hospital now operates three golf cars.
- The additional golf car enables the shuttle service to transport more individuals across the hospital campus without long wait times.
- Piedmont's volunteer drivers provide shuttle services for patients or visitors with impaired mobility, health risks that prevent walking long distances, those accompanied by small children, or general visitors.
- The hospital has implemented COVID-19 safety measures to ensure rider safety on the shuttle service.
- Mike Chrzanowski holds the title of Chairman at YMMC (Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America).
- Tom McDonald serves as the President of Yamaha Golf Car Company and emphasized that supporting the local community is a serious priority for Yamaha, specifically highlighting assistance for patients, visitors, and staff navigating the hospital parking complex.
- This marks the second golf car Yamaha has donated to Piedmont Newnan since the hospital opened its new campus on Poplar Road in 2012.
- Piedmont Newnan operates as a nonprofit community hospital where donations are vital for supporting their mission and directly benefiting patient health and wellness.
