# Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2013-10-17

**Source:** [Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies Announces New Simmerring® Shaft Seal Ring for Vehicles with Electric Drive - EDN](https://www.edn.com/freudenberg-nok-sealing-technologies-announces-new-simmerring-shaft-seal-ring-for-vehicles-with-electric-drive/)  ·  `page_020433`

## Facts

- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies announced a new Simmerring® Shaft Seal Ring for vehicles with electric drive on October 17, 2013.
- The new seal features electrically conductive nonwoven material to help protect electric drive vehicle systems from damage.
- The seal reliably seals shafts while safely draining off build-up of static electricity.
- It was especially developed for vehicles with electric drives to allow axle drives to be sealed extremely reliably.
- The seal drains off electrical charge that normally builds up on the drive shaft, conducting it safely to the housing.
- This prevents static charge from traveling along the shaft into the transmission and damaging machine elements due to dielectric breakdown.
- Traditionally, transmission input shaft seals are enhanced with a conductive nonwoven on the air side to create a conductive bridge between the shaft and the housing or grounding.
- The new seal provides manufacturers of drive components in electric vehicles with a proven method to seal shafts while conducting away electrical charges without needing additional discharge measures.
- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies is the Americas joint venture between Freudenberg & Co. in Germany and NOK Corp. in Japan.
- Freudenberg-NOK operates through Automotive, Merkel, Process Seals, and Simrit sales channels to produce advanced sealing technologies for markets including aerospace, agriculture, appliance, automotive, construction, diesel engine, energy, food and beverage, heavy industry, and pharmaceutical.
- The company was founded in 1989 under the legal name Freudenberg-NOK General Partnership.
- Freudenberg-NOK is headquartered in Plymouth, Mich., and operates more than 20 facilities across the Americas.
- For nearly 90 years, the Freudenberg Simmerring® Shaft Seal has been a synonym for reliable shaft sealing.
