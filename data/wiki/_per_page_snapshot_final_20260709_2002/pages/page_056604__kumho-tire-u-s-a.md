# Kumho Tire U.S.A.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-04-20

**Source:** [Kumho Tire USA names Carolina Wagner marketing chief By Investing.com](https://www.investing.com/news/company-news/kumho-tire-usa-names-carolina-wagner-marketing-chief-93CH-4638448)  ·  `page_056604`

## Facts

- Kumho Tire U.S.A. appointed Carolina Wagner as Vice President of Marketing.
- Wagner will oversee brand strategy, product marketing, integrated communications, digital marketing, demand generation, and sales support for the tire manufacturer’s U.S. operations covering passenger, light truck, and commercial vehicle segments.
- The executive brings more than 25 years of experience in the tire industry, having held positions at Continental Tires the Americas and Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co.
- At Continental, Wagner served as Executive Marketing Manager for passenger, light truck, and commercial tire segments across the Mercosur region in São Paulo, Brazil.
- She later held Director of Marketing roles at Continental Tires Americas, leading teams for the commercial vehicle segment across the United States, Canada, and Latin America.
- Wagner began her career at Goodyear in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, advancing through executive leadership positions in Brazilian and Latin American markets.
- Ed Cho serves as CEO of Kumho Tire U.S.A.
- "Carolina’s arrival marks a pivotal moment for Kumho Tire," said Ed Cho. "Her deep expertise in the tire industry, combined with her proven ability to build integrated marketing strategies that deliver measurable results, makes her the ideal leader to drive our next chapter."
- Wagner holds a bachelor’s degree in business administration from Universidade Santa Úrsula and a Master of Business Administration from IBMEC, both in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
- Kumho Tire Co. Inc., headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, serves as the U.S. sales, marketing, product development, and distribution arm of the South Korea-based tire manufacturer.
