# Solvay Battery-Grade PVDF Manufacturing Facility

**Type:** Facility  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-materials|Battery Materials]]  ·  **Location:** Richmond County, Georgia, Richmond County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

A proposed chemical manufacturing facility in Richmond County, Georgia that will produce battery-grade polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF) for use in electric vehicle batteries. The project is being evaluated in a Draft Environmental Assessment (DOE/EA-2237) and receives financial assistance from the U.S. Department of Energy.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Produces PVDF, a key polymer binder used in electric vehicle batteries, supporting the production of more than 5 million EV batteries per year.
