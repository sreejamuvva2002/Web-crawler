# SKF USA Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-10-20

**Source:** [SKF To Showcase Its Latest Vehicle Solutions At AAPEX 2016](https://www.brakeandfrontend.com/skf-aapex-2016/)  ·  `page_059959`

## Facts

- SKF announced its latest wheel end and driveline solutions will be on display at booth No. 4619 during AAPEX show in Las Vegas from Nov. 1-3.
- SKF will showcase innovations through detailed cutaways of hub bearings, u-joints, seals and more.
- A custom 2013 Dodge Ram 1500 Longhorn equipped with SKF hub bearing units will be on display at the booth.
- The booth will showcase SKF’s updated u-joint product line featuring broader coverage and an expanded range of its high-performance Brute Force line.
- SKF Universal Joints are available in greasable and non-greasable versions and meet or exceed OE specifications.
- Each universal joint part is precision-manufactured to provide reliable performance and maximum service life.
- The expanded universal joint product line provides complete coverage for vehicles including cars; light, medium- and heavy-duty trucks; buses; RVs; construction and off-highway equipment; agricultural and industrial equipment; and marine applications up to the 2015 model year.
- Brian Jungmann, automotive communications manager at SKF USA Inc., stated SKF is looking forward to returning to AAPEX to feature innovative solutions for the automotive aftermarket.
- Jungmann noted SKF will share industry-leading expertise in bearing and seal technology and highlight high-performance solutions customers expect from SKF.
- SKF will highlight its hub bearing kits at the show, which are designed to save time by providing everything an automotive technician needs to replace a press-in style hub bearing.
- Each SKF hub bearing kit includes a cartridge-style wheel bearing, c-clip, axle nut, seals and a premium hub flange.
- SKF says its hub bearing kits reduce the chance of customer returns by providing quality parts designed to fit and work properly together, eliminating the hassle of sourcing separate parts from separate suppliers.
