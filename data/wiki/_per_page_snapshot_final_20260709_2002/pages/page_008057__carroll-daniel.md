## Facts

- Carroll Daniel is a construction and engineering firm based in Gainesville, GA.
- The company maintains offices in Atlanta, Greenville, Mobile, Nashville, and Savannah.
- Carroll Daniel provides project planning, engineering, procurement, and construction services for industrial and public markets across the Southeastern U.S. and beyond.
- The firm is currently operating under its third generation of family leadership.
- Carroll Daniel consistently ranks among the nation’s Top 400 Contractors and Engineering firms according to ENR Magazine.
- The company has earned multiple awards including ENR Top 400 Construction Firms, ENR Top 500 Design Firms, Design-Build Institute of America Award Winner, Design-Build Southeast Multi-Award Winner, Build America Multi-Award Winner, Construction Management Association of America Multi-Award Winner, Construction Management Association Southeast Multi-Award Winner, Associated Builders & Contractors Safety Excellence Award Winner, and Business Facilities Deal of the Year: Impact Award Winner.
- Carroll Daniel's core values emphasize Relationships (servant leadership and client advocacy), Integrity, Stewardship (investing in employees and supporting local communities), and Excellence (passion for building and dedication to quality).
