# Novelis Inc.

## Facts

- The County of Oswego Industrial Development Agency (IDA) approved a $15 million sales and use tax exemption for Novelis.
- The funding is designated to help rebuild after a pair of fires last year in the hot mill at its aluminum plant in Scriba.
- The IDA board unanimously approved the application during a special meeting.
- Novelis is a world leader in aluminum sheet manufacturing.
- Its products and solutions serve the beverage can, automotive, construction, aerospace, and other industries.
- The company announced it will hire 200+ jobs as part of the project.
- Production capacity will increase to 300,000 tons of aluminum sheet annually.
- Novelis is the county's largest employer.
- The company provides employment opportunities for individuals with disabilities.
- Novelis has donated over $1.4 million to the Scriba community.
