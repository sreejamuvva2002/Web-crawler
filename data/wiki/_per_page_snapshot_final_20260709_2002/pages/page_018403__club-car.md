## Facts

- Club Car is a global industry-leading manufacturer of small-wheel, zero emissions vehicles.
- Club Car recently celebrated the opening of the new Club Car Appling Manufacturing Facility with tours and a ribbon cutting ceremony.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp and First Lady Marty Kemp attended the event alongside state and local elected officials and area business leaders.
- Officials and business leaders toured the new manufacturing facility and learned about the Club Car Urban vehicle.
- The Club Car Appling Manufacturing Facility is a 105,000-square-foot flexible production and warehouse storage space.
- Club Car opened the facility to improve customer experience as demand in the United States for the Club Car Urban vehicle increases.
- Club Car has committed nearly $8 million in capital investments into the facility over two years.
- The new facility currently operates a state-of-the-art production line.
- The facility can produce up to 20 vehicles per day.
- Mark Wagner, Club Car President and CEO, stated that their innovative vehicles are impacting commercial in commercial fleet electrification, mini-mobility, and last mile delivery.
- Club Car has been a Georgia-based company for more than 60 years.
- The new facility features award-winning architecture.
