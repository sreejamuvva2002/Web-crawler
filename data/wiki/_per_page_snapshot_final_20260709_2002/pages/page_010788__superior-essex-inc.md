# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-01

**Source:** [Superior Essex Completes Essex Furukawa Buyout from Furukawa Electric - AFV NEWS](https://afvnews.ca/2024/05/01/superior-essex-completes-essex-furukawa-buyout-from-furukawa-electric/)  ·  `page_010788`

## Facts

- The press release regarding the transaction was issued from Atlanta, Georgia, United States on May 1, 2024.
- Superior Essex successfully completed the acquisition of the remaining minority interest in Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire from Furukawa Electric Co., Ltd.
- The transaction became effective April 30, 2024, establishing Superior Essex as the sole owner of the brand, assets, and operations previously conducted by the global joint venture with Furukawa Electric.
- Superior Essex plans to relaunch its magnet wire business under a new company name later in 2024.
- The company will continue to operate all current facilities of the Essex Furukawa joint venture worldwide, including magnet wire facilities in Japan and Malaysia (Kuala Lumpur) contributed by Furukawa Electric in October 2020.
- Daniel Choi serves as the CEO of Superior Essex.
- CEO Daniel Choi stated that over the last several years they accomplished many great things working with the team from Furukawa Electric and expressed pride and gratitude for their contributions.
- The acquisition solidifies Superior Essex's control over global operations and reinforces its commitment to delivering exceptional products and services to customers worldwide.
- Superior Essex plans to continuously respond to market demands in the xEV automotive industry, power, electrification, and renewable energy markets.
- The company aims to strengthen its position by actively pursuing decarbonization and sustainability.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, Essex Energy Italy, IVA, Lacroix + Kress, Hi-Wire, and Essex Brownell.
- The company has over 3,000 employees in 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is the leading global provider of magnet wire and is at the forefront of communications fiber application as well as Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company is instrumental in creating disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Furukawa Electric Group was established in 1884 and includes 127 group companies with approximately 50,000 employees around the world.
- Furukawa Electric provides technologies, products, and services for infrastructure fields such as information and energy, automotive systems fields, and electronics fields using core technologies of metals, polymers, photonics, and high frequency.
