# Georgia Economic Development & Investment Announcements

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2016-10-25

**Source:** [Press Releases Archives - Page 26 of 34 - Location Georgia](https://www.locationgeorgia.com/category/press-releases/page/26/)  ·  `page_058164`

## Facts

- Helena Industries celebrated the ribbon-cutting and opening of its new 180,000 square foot warehouse facility in Cordele, Georgia on October 25, 2016.
- The new Helena Industries facility is adjacent to its existing manufacturing and administrative buildings.
- Helena Industries will employ 10-12 new workers to operate the new facility, which will house inventory related to its manufacturing operations.
- These new Helena Industries workers are in addition to the company's existing Cordele workforce of approximately 95 permanent full-time workers and 50-150 temporary seasonal workers.
- On September 8, 2016, Gov. Nathan Deal announced that Sentury Tire, the fifth-largest and fastest-growing tire manufacturer in China, will create more than 1,000 jobs and invest $530 million in a car and light truck tire manufacturing operation in Troup County.
- Sentury Tire's manufacturing operation will be located in LaGrange, Troup County.
- Gov. Nathan Deal stated that Sentury Tire’s commitment to locate in LaGrange speaks to Georgia’s business-friendly climate and the strong relationship maintained with economic partners in China.
- Gov. Nathan Deal emphasized that Georgia’s automotive manufacturing and technology capabilities will be assets to Sentury Tire as it serves a growing customer base.
- On August 29, 2016, Gov. Nathan Deal announced that Variety Wholesalers, Inc., a retail store chain, will establish its second major distribution center in Coweta County, creating 320 jobs and investing approximately $10.5 million in the Newnan area.
- Gov. Nathan Deal noted that Georgia’s business climate and transportation infrastructure continue to attract major businesses like Variety Wholesalers to the state.
- Gov. Nathan Deal highlighted that Variety Wholesalers’ expansion demonstrates significant investment opportunities offered by Georgia's ports and will further establish the state as an industrial magnet.
- On June 22, 2016, Gov. Nathan Deal announced that Amazon will create more than 500 full-time jobs in the Braselton area of Jackson County.
- The 600,000 square-foot Amazon fulfillment center in Jackson County will be used for packing and shipping large items such as household decor, sporting equipment, and gardening tools.
- Gov. Nathan Deal stated that Amazon’s decision to develop in Jackson County speaks to the strength and vitality of Georgia’s business-friendly environment.
- On May 9, 2016, Gov. Nathan Deal announced that Dollar General will build a 1-million-square-foot distribution center in Butts and Spalding Counties, creating more than 500 jobs and representing a capital investment of $85 million.
- Gov. Nathan Deal stated that Dollar General’s decision to build a fulfillment center in Georgia reflects the high quality of the workforce and the state's strategic location for accessing the Southeastern market.
- Gov. Nathan Deal emphasized that Georgia is perfectly positioned to support Dollar General's fulfillment center and serves as the No.1 state in which to do business.
