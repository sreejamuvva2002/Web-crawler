## Facts

- Novelis Inc, a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries, plans to raise USD 750 million through a bond offering to repay debt and strengthen its balance sheet.
- Novelis intends to use the net proceeds from the proposed offering to repay USD 738 million of outstanding debt and any remaining proceeds to fund cash on its balance sheet.
- Novelis Corporation, an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Novelis Inc, has priced an offering of USD 750 million aggregate principal amount of 6.875 per cent senior notes due in January 2030.
- The bond offering represents an increase of USD 250 million from the offering size previously announced.
- Novelis Inc reported an 18 per cent decline in net income to USD 128 million in the September quarter of 2024-25.
- The firm deals in aluminium rolled products and had reported a net income of USD 157 million in the same period of the preceding financial year.
- Net sales for the second quarter rose by 4.5 per cent to USD 4,295 million from USD 4,107 million in the year-ago period.
- The sales increase was mainly driven by higher average aluminium prices and a one per cent rise in total flat rolled product shipments to 945 kilo tonnes.
- Novelis achieved record beverage packaging shipments in the quarter due to its global footprint.
- The company mitigated customer impact from a flooding-related outage at Sierre through its global footprint.
- Steve Fisher, President and CEO of Novelis Inc, stated that the global footprint allowed for record beverage packaging shipments and mitigated flooding-related outage impacts.
- Devinder Ahuja, executive vice president and CFO of Novelis Inc, stated the company is focused on diligently managing the balance sheet while progressing growth investments underway.
