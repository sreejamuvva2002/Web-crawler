# SEG Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-01-11

**Source:** [Bosch subsidiary turns into SEG Automotive - electrive.com](https://www.electrive.com/2018/01/11/bosch-subsidiary-turns-seg-automotive/)  ·  `page_018068`

## Facts

- Bosch completes the sale of its starter motor and generator division that will now be known as SEG Automotive.
- The newly formed SEG Automotive belongs to the Zhengzhou Coal Mining Machinery Group with immediate effect.
- SEG Automotive is now employing 8,000 employees at 16 sites throughout the world.
- Besides continuing the business with starter motors and generators, the company wants to turn towards electrification, too.
- Together with a group of investors, SEG Automotive plans to deliver solutions for electrification such as the BRM (Boost Recuperation Machine).
- The BRM recuperates energy from braking to distribute it through the 48 Volt on-board system.
