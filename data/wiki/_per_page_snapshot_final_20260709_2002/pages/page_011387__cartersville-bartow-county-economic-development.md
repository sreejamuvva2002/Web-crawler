## Facts

- Cartersville-Bartow County is located north of Atlanta on Interstate 75 and has been a location of choice for industry since 1836.
- More than 207 diverse industries currently call Bartow County home.
- The county and cities have collaborated to make business investment decisions both easy and cost-effective.
- BCJDA reserves the third Tuesday monthly at noon for regular meetings at the Clarence Brown Conference Center (5450 GA. Hwy. 20, Cartersville, GA 30121).
- On Apr 22, 2026, Anheuser-Busch announced it is expanding its presence in Cartersville as part of a broader $600 million manufacturing investment.
- On Jan 14, 2026, Rivulet Energy opened a solar panel facility in Cartersville, Georgia.
- On Oct 27, 2025, the Economic Development Office, Chamber of Commerce, and CVB launched BartowCareers.com to reclaim local talent.
- On Sep 11, 2025, Anheuser-Busch announced a $9.2 million investment in its Cartersville plant to upgrade brewing equipment.
- The Economic Development Office contact persons are Melinda Lemmon, Matthew Thomas, and Connor Nicholson.
