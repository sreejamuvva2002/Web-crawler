# Georgia Trend Magazine Economic Development Roundup

**Type:** event  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2024-09-30

**Source:** [Economic Development Around the State - Georgia Trend Magazine](https://www.georgiatrend.com/2024/09/30/economic-development-around-the-state-89/)  ·  `page_015124`

## Facts

- Gas South recently relocated its headquarters to The Battery Atlanta in a 31,000-square-foot space designed for the company’s hybrid work model.
- The relocation provides closer proximity to Gas South’s partner, the Atlanta Braves, and the Braves Development Company headquarters.
- Gulfstream Aerospace Corp. recently opened its expanded customer support service center at the Savannah/Hilton Head International Airport.
- Gulfstream invested more than $150 million into the 367,000-square-foot Savannah Service Center East.
- The Savannah Service Center East expansion added more than 200 new jobs.
- Fresh meal delivery company FreshRealm Inc. is expanding in Macon County with a $6.3 million investment.
- The FreshRealm expansion will create more than 300 new jobs and add enhancements and several new production lines for ready-to-heat meals, ready-to-cook meals, and meal kits.
- Belgian heavy equipment producer Aertssen Logistics recently opened a processing and logistics services location in Effingham County.
- The 35-acre equipment processing center will provide indoor and outdoor storage, logistics services, and technical services for manufacturers of agriculture, mining, and construction machinery.
- South African restaurant group Nando’s recently opened the first two locations of its peri-peri chicken concept in Georgia at the High Street development in Dunwoody and The Forum in Peachtree Corners.
- These new restaurants are the latest additions to Nando’s more than 1,200 locations around the world.
