## Facts

- Blue Bird Corporation is the only U.S.-owned and operated school bus manufacturer.
- The company has nearly 100 years of history and experience as a recognized technology leader in school buses.
- Blue Bird is reflecting on nearly 100 years of helping build an American institution of safety and reliability as it looks toward the America 250 milestone.
- For almost a century, Blue Bird has safely connected millions of students to their education every day.
- The company is leading the industry into the next century with zero-emission technology and manufacturing innovation.
- Blue Bird is a leader in low and zero-emission school buses.
- Choosing Blue Bird's state-of-the-art buses allows schools to reduce their carbon footprint and positively impact student health, safety, and overall well-being.
- The company utilizes innovative technologies and cutting-edge designs at the forefront of the green transportation movement.
- All Blue Bird buses are built to pass trusted safety tests as a standard.
- Backup cameras are standard equipment on all buses.
- Electronic stability control is standard equipment on all buses.
- All Propane Buses from Blue Bird have certified lowest NOx levels.
- Blue Bird joined fleet leaders at ACT Expo to discuss mixed-energy fleets, including diesel, gasoline, propane, and electric buses for school and commercial transportation applications.
- Blue Bird supports local schools through career pathways, mentorship, and student success initiatives.
- The company provides a summer school bus maintenance checklist covering safety instructions, technician tips, and essential replacement parts from Blue Bird dealers.
