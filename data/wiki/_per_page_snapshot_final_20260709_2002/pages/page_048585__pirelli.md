# Pirelli

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05

**Source:** [Pirelli Will Start Cyber Tyre Production in Georgia - Metro Atlanta CEO](https://metroatlantaceo.com/news/2026/05/pirelli-will-start-cyber-tyre-production-georgia-underlining-strategic-importance-us-market/)  ·  `page_048585`

## Facts

- Pirelli is reinforcing its long-term commitment to the United States with a key step in its product and industrial strategy.
- The Georgia plant, already dedicated to technologically advanced products for the U.S. market in the High-Value segment and Motorsport, will be enhanced by production of connected tires featuring Cyber™ Tyre technology.
- This development highlights the strategic importance of the U.S. for Pirelli's global growth roadmap and strengthens its integrated industrial and technological presence in the country.
- The announcement was made on Thursday, May 7th, 2026, as Pirelli participated in the SelectUSA Investment Summit, a flagship event promoted by the U.S. Department of Commerce.
- Cyber™ Tyre is being showcased at the summit as a core innovation shaping the future of smart mobility.
- Cyber™ Tyre is the world's first hardware-and-software system capable of collecting data and information from sensors embedded in tires, processing them through Pirelli's proprietary software and algorithms, and communicating in real time with vehicle electronics to enable new functionalities integrated with driving and control systems.
- The technology enhances the driving experience and increases safety levels while supporting connected infrastructure.
- In the highly advanced U.S. market where digital and smart mobility solutions are rapidly expanding, Cyber™ Tyre represents a distinctive competitive advantage for Pirelli.
- At SelectUSA, Cyber™ Tyre was showcased at the Georgia State booth, highlighting Pirelli's industrial footprint in the state where it has been established for more than 20 years.
- Claudio Zanardo, CEO of Pirelli North America, stated that the start of Cyber™ Tyre production in the Rome, Georgia plant is a significant milestone for Pirelli in this country.
- The milestone reflects Pirelli's commitment to bringing advanced technologies like Cyber™ Tyre closer to the market and further strengthening its industrial footprint and innovation capabilities in the United States.
- To enhance Rome's role as a high-tech production site, Pirelli is finalizing the introduction of the latest version of the MIRS (Modular Integrated Robotized System) production process.
- The MIRS process will be the most advanced manufacturing process for high-end, premium production within the entire Pirelli Group and is exclusive to the Georgia factory.
- The process further enhances robotized production capabilities, increasing productivity, flexibility, and quality.
- It is a highly digitalized system that enables a direct link between product design and its industrial application.
- This development lays the groundwork for growth in Pirelli's production capacity in Rome, an integral part of the Group's industrial development plans, and will further strengthen Pirelli's presence in the United States as one of its most important and strategic markets.
- The Rome, Georgia plant specializes in high-value tire production for the North American market.
- The facility also hosts a dedicated R&D center, further strengthening its role in technological development and product innovation.
- The facility is recognized for responsible sourcing practices, including the use of FSC®-certified natural rubber, underscoring the company's broader environmental commitment across its supply chain.
