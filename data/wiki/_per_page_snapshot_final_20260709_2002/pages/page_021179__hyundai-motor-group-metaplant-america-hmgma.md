# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [How Hyundai's Metaplant Is Reshaping Georgia's Supply Chain | Savannah 3PL](https://savannah3pl.com/blog/hyundai-metaplant-georgia-supply-chain)  ·  `page_021179`

## Facts

- In 2022, Hyundai Motor Group announced a $7.6 billion electric vehicle and battery manufacturing complex in Bryan County, Georgia.
- The complex is located just 25 miles from the Port of Savannah.
- The facility is known as the Metaplant.
- It is a dedicated electric vehicle manufacturing facility spanning 2,923 acres in Bryan County.
- The plant will produce Hyundai, Kia, and Genesis electric vehicles along with their battery packs.
- At full capacity, it is expected to produce approximately 300,000 vehicles per year.
- It is one of the largest automotive manufacturing plants in the United States.
- Automotive manufacturing for the facility requires a vast network of Tier 1 and Tier 2 suppliers providing components including battery cells, electric motors, seats, dashboards, wiring harnesses, and body panels.
- Many of these components will be imported through the Port of Savannah.
- The supply chain creates massive demand for automotive parts warehousing requiring JIT (Just-In-Time) delivery with precise inventory management.
- The supply chain requires parts sequencing and kitting where components arrive at the assembly line in exact order and configuration.
- The supply chain requires container drayage with fast, reliable transport from Garden City Terminal.
- The supply chain requires transloading to convert ocean containers to domestic trailers optimized for plant delivery schedules.
- The supply chain requires customs brokerage to manage duty classifications, FTZ benefits, and import compliance for automotive parts.
- The Metaplant is creating a new category of logistics demand in the Savannah region.
- Automotive supply chains for the facility require precision timing, quality assurance, and the ability to scale with production schedules.
- Third-party logistics providers in Savannah offering kitting and assembly, JIT warehousing, and FTZ services are positioned to capture significant business from Hyundai directly and its supplier network.
- Georgia is home to Kia's manufacturing plant in West Point.
- Georgia has a growing network of EV battery and component manufacturers.
- The Southeast United States has emerged as the new center of gravity for American automotive manufacturing.
- Other regional plants include BMW in South Carolina, Mercedes-Benz in Alabama, Volkswagen in Tennessee, and Rivian in Georgia.
- Savannah's port and logistics infrastructure serves as the natural import gateway for this regional automotive corridor.
