# OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- OTR Engineered Solutions was recognized with a Supplier Excellence Award from Kubota Manufacturing of America (KMA) and Kubota Industrial Equipment (KIE).
- The award was presented during KMA's and KIE's annual supplier conference.
- The yearly award is given to KMA’s and KIE’s top suppliers for outstanding quality, delivery, and cost commitment.
- OTR has been a long-time supplier to KMA and KIE, as well as Kubota Tractor Service (KTC), Kubota Canada Ltd. (KCL), Kubota-Mexico (KMX), and Kubota in Europe and Thailand.
- Beyond serving a typical supplier role, OTR partners with Kubota facilities, providing tire and wheel assemblies along with value-added services such as mounting, sequencing, warehousing, and dealer support.
- Mike Stoeckel serves as the Global VP of Sales & Marketing at OTR.
- Mike Stoeckel stated that OTR is committed to its partnership with Kubota Corporation and dedicated to supporting their vision of becoming a “Global Major Brand.”
- OTR supports this partnership through ongoing investments in people, facilities, technologies, and innovation.
- OTR Engineered Solutions is a market-leading global enterprise specializing in off-the-road terrain engaging solutions for original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and aftermarkets.
- OTR is known for market-leading tire and wheel innovations.
- The firm has long established strategic partnerships with OEMs covering Construction, Lawn & Garden, Powersports, Agriculture, Forestry, Mining, Material Handling, and Specialty Vehicle markets.
- OTR’s infrastructure comprises 31 facilities and warehouse locations throughout the U.S., Canada, Europe, and Asia.
- The Supplier Excellence Award is an accolade given to suppliers who demonstrate exceptional performance, product quality, and customer service.
- OTR (Off The Road) Wheel Engineering, Inc. received the award in recognition of outstanding achievements in supplying high-quality tire and wheel solutions to customers across various industries, including construction, agriculture, and mining.
- OTR Wheel Engineering, Inc. specializes in the design, manufacturing, and distribution of high-quality tire and wheel solutions for off-road applications.
- OTR's products cater to a wide range of industries, including construction, agriculture, mining, and material handling.
