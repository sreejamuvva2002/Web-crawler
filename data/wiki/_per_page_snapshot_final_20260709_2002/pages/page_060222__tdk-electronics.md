## Facts

- TDK manufactures aluminum electrolytic capacitors used in frequency converters, wind power converters, solar inverters, professional power supplies and UPS, medical applications, professional photoflash units, and a wide range of automotive applications.
- The capacitance of TDK's aluminum electrolytic capacitors is defined by the surface area of the aluminum anode foil.
- Special etching processes are used to maximize the anode foil surface area.
- These etching processes give the capacitors the highest volumetric capacitance of all capacitor technologies.
- The resulting design provides an attractive cost per capacitance ratio.
- TDK aluminum electrolytic capacitors feature outstanding ripple current capabilities.
- TDK aluminum electrolytic capacitors offer very long-term reliability.
