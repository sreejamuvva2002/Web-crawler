# Superior Essex

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-05-11

**Source:** [Superior Essex, Essex Furukawa Partner With Waybridge On Agile Digitization Initative | Essex Solutions](https://essexsolutions.com/news/superior-essex-essex-furukawa-partner-with-waybridge-on-agile-digitization-initative/)  ·  `page_010468`

## Facts

- Superior Essex announced an expanded partnership with Waybridge, a supply chain operating system for raw materials.
- The announcement continues Vision 2030 efforts for Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire through agile digitization of manufacturing plants in Europe, North America, and Malaysia.
- Superior Essex is among the early adopters of Waybridge’s platform, utilizing the technology at two North American magnet wire manufacturing plants in the U.S. and Mexico since August 2021.
- The expanded partnership will incorporate Waybridge into 9 new plants located in Canada, Germany, Italy, Serbia, and the U.S.
- Strategic Sourcing VP Scott Wilde stated that streamlining operations and predicting supply chain issues like stockouts is necessary to provide optimal experiences for customers and suppliers.
- Since partnering with Waybridge, Superior Essex changed its view of full inventory and workflow processes, gaining access to critical predictive information to handle supply chain issues proactively.
- Inefficient supply chains and stockouts can increase logistics costs, damage brand image, and hinder future demand due to unexpected demand increases, shipment and supplier delays, human error, or lack of accurate inventory data.
- Waybridge’s Stock & Flow feature aggregates data from shipping forecasts, real-time shipment locations, predicted ETAs, and production plans to offer predictive capabilities.
- In 2021 alone, Waybridge allowed Superior Essex to avoid four material stockouts that would have directly affected customers.
- Through Waybridge’s interconnected platform, Superior Essex reduced company-wide Excel usage for procurement by 90% and reduced email volume around procurement and tracking by 50%.
- Waybridge CEO and cofounder Scott Evans stated that Superior Essex is a forward-thinker in the commodities space and an ideal partner due to their commitment to standardization on a common platform.
- Waybridge Chief Product Officer Kyle McCarter noted that Superior Essex provided incredible product feedback since the partnership start, helping evolve the product for all customers.
- As one of the largest magnet wire producers in the world, Superior Essex values its role as a build partner and major participant in global markets.
- Superior Essex currently requires all North American material to be managed via Waybridge and plans to expand the technology into Asian plants in the near future.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, and Essex Brownell.
- The company has over 3,000 employees across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is the leading global provider of magnet wire and is at the forefront of communications fiber application and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company is instrumental in creating disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex is committed to pioneering smart building technology and setting the pace on sustainability.
