# Superior Essex

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Superior Essex, Essex Furukawa Partner With Waybridge On Agile Digitization Technology - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.it/news/superior-essex-essex-furukawa-partner-with-waybridge-on-agile-digitization-technology/)  ·  `page_010364`

## Facts

- Superior Essex announced the expansion of its partnership with Waybridge, a raw material supply chain operating system.
- The initiative supports Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire's Vision 2030 plan objectives focused on agile digitization of manufacturing facilities in Europe, North America, and Malaysia.
- Superior Essex was one of the first users of the Waybridge platform, initially deploying it in two North American magnet wire production facilities in the United States and Mexico starting in August 2021.
- The partnership expansion will implement Waybridge across nine new manufacturing facilities located in Canada, Germany, Italy, Serbia, and the United States.
- Scott Wilde, Vice President of Strategic Sourcing for Superior Essex, stated that making innovation a core component of sourcing and achieving holistic sustainability has been a primary focus.
- Scott Wilde noted that the company needed to streamline operations and predict supply chain issues like stockouts before they impacted customers or the company.
- Collaboration with Waybridge radically changed how Superior Essex visualizes inventory and workflow, providing crucial predictive information to operate agilely on supply chain problems.
- Inefficient supply chains and stockouts can increase logistics costs, damage brand image, and hinder future demand due to unexpected demand spikes, shipping/supplier delays, human error, and inaccurate inventory data.
- Waybridge's Stock & Flow function aggregates real-time data from shipping forecasts, locations, ETAs, and production plans to offer predictive inventory capabilities.
- In 2021 alone, Waybridge enabled Superior Essex to avoid four inventory stockout situations that would have directly impacted customers.
- Thanks to Waybridge's interconnected platform, Superior Essex reduced company-wide Excel usage by 90% and procurement/tracking emails by 50%.
- Scott Evans, CEO and Co-founder of Waybridge, stated that Superior Essex is a pioneer in raw materials and understands the importance of standardizing management on a common platform to benefit the company, clients, and suppliers.
- Kyle McCarter, Chief Product Officer of Waybridge, noted that Superior Essex provided incredible feedback since the partnership began, allowing their product to evolve and benefit all clients.
- Superior Essex currently requires all material management in North America to be handled through Waybridge.
- Superior Essex plans to expand Waybridge technology to its Asian facilities in the near future.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, and Essex Brownell.
- Superior Essex employs over 3,000 people across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is a global leading supplier of magnet wire and is pioneering communication fiber and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company's technology is fundamental to disruptive technological advances in the electrification of automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- The partnership expansion was announced on May 11, 2022.
- Superior Essex is headquartered at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
