# Freightliner Custom Chassis Corp

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-01-20

**Source:** [Daimler Trucks North America Announces $22.7M Investment to Expand Facilities and Improve Operations at Freightliner Custom Chassis - Thomas Built Buses](https://thomasbuiltbuses.com/resources/news/daimler-trucks-north-america-announces-22-7m-2016-01-20/)  ·  `page_053537`

## Facts

- Daimler Trucks North America (DTNA) announced an investment of $22.7 million in Freightliner Custom Chassis Corp (FCCC).
- The investment is designated to expand facilities and improve operations in Gaffney, South Carolina.
- The investment includes the construction of a new logistics center.
- The investment includes expansions of FCCC's current office building.
- The investment includes expansion of the employee parking lot.
- The investment includes improvements to completed chassis parking and loading/delivery operations.
- The investment will drive implementation of a new line side delivery process that utilizes nearly 60 percent more component kitting.
- David Carson, president of Freightliner Custom Chassis Corp, stated the investment demonstrates commitment to employees in Gaffney, customers, and the future of FCCC.
- Carson noted the company will continue building chassis with best features and customer support to ensure positioning for future growth.
- Carson thanked officials from the state of South Carolina and Cherokee County for their assistance in making the project possible.
- The office building expansion will provide a 25 percent increase in personal work stations.
- The office building expansion will increase training room capacity from 25 to 100.
- The office building expansion will double the amount of available team work space.
- The office building expansion will increase cafeteria capacity from 15 to 119.
- DTNA leadership aims to ensure the company has necessary tools and facilities to deliver chassis for customers across all product lines.
- FCCC is a leading manufacturer of premium diesel chassis for the luxury RV market.
- FCCC provides 24/7 factory direct support, a nationwide service network with more than 400 dealers in the U.S. and Canada, and an official customer-support mobile app for its chassis.
- DTNA is headquartered in Portland, Oregon, and is the leading heavy-duty truck manufacturer in North America.
- DTNA manufactures, sells, and services commercial vehicles under the Freightliner, Western Star, Detroit, and Thomas Built Buses nameplates.
- DTNA is a subsidiary of Daimler, the world’s leading commercial vehicle manufacturer.
