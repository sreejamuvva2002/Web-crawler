## Facts

- Dekalb Tool & Die, Inc. was founded in 1962.
- The company is based in Atlanta, Georgia.
- It specializes in custom tool & die, metal stampings, and metal fabrications.
- Product offerings include progressive dies, transfer dies, draw dies, and single-hit tooling to meet specific requirements of different industries.
- The company provides CAD design services and delivers products via its own fleet of trucks.
- It provides tooling services for the aerospace, defense, medical, and other industrial industries.
