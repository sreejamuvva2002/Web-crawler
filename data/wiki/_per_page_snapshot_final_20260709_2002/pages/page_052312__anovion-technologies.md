# Anovion Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-03-25

**Source:** [Anovion Technologies Launches as a Leader in North American Battery Materials Supply Chain](https://www.m2graphite.com/news/anovion-launches-as-a-leader-in-the-north-american-battery-materials-supply-chain/)  ·  `page_052312`

## Facts

- Anovion Technologies launched on March 25, 2022.
- Anovion was formed through the combination of key assets from affiliates of Amsted Graphite Materials and the acquisition of Pyrotek’s battery materials division.
- The combination creates a leader in North America’s rapidly growing battery materials supply chain and the largest commercially operational production capacity of synthetic graphite anode material in the United States.
- Anovion has over 140 years of combined history in synthetic graphite production.
- Synthetic graphite is critical to the USA’s migration to an electrified future powered by rechargeable batteries, according to CEO Eric Stopka.
- Foreign producers, overwhelmingly from China, have controlled the global supply of anode material.
- Launching Anovion is intended to secure the supply chain for the electrified future in the United States and throughout North America.
- Synthetic graphite is the key advanced material in the anode of lithium-ion batteries used in electric vehicles, consumer electronics, critical defense applications, and stationary storage for electric grids.
- Long-life batteries require high purity, highly consistent anode material, driving a strong preference for synthetic graphite, according to Chief Growth Officer Ian McCallum.
- Anovion's integrated supply chain is 100% domestic.
- The supply chain includes USA-sourced raw materials, multiple manufacturing operations, research and development hubs, and other office locations throughout the country.
- Amsted Graphite Materials is located in Clarksburg, West Virginia.
- Pyrotek’s battery materials division operates at locations outside of Niagara Falls, New York, and in Spokane, Washington.
- Anovion is headquartered in Chicago, Illinois.
- Anovion uses proven, reliable, scalable graphitization technology to ensure products with high crystallinity and low impurities.
- Anovion currently has the largest anode powder graphitization capacity in North America at 5,000 tonnes per annum (tpa).
- A first expansion plant is planned to launch by 2025, which will boost capacity up to 50,000 tpa of finished product.
- The company has a longer-term roadmap targeting 150,000 tpa in capacity or more.
- Dr. Jeremy Schrooten serves as Vice President of Technology at Anovion and is formerly Pyrotek’s Technical Director.
- Joining Amsted Graphite Materials to launch Anovion aims to create supply chain security and efficiencies in the rapidly growing market, according to Dr. Schrooten.
- Anovion follows environmentally responsible manufacturing practices that yield high purity and exceptional quality without compromising throughput.
- Anovion’s pilot operations include a proprietary thermal treatment process that brings its direct emissions profile to near zero.
- The company uses clean, cost-effective hydroelectric power to achieve its sustainability targets.
- The investor group supporting Anovion includes affiliates of Amsted Industries and Monomyth Group.
- Both Amsted Industries and Monomyth Group are headquartered in Chicago, Illinois.
- Anovion operates North America’s first commercially operational capacity dedicated to synthetic graphite anode material.
- Anovion has domestic commercial production and ships to North American customers.
- Anovion has continually expanded its portfolio of high-performing anode material for a wide range of lithium-ion battery applications.
- Anovion products are already qualified and scaling production with customers in commercial electric vehicles, aerospace, and defense markets.
- Anovion is undergoing qualification testing with leading automotive electric vehicle OEMs and suppliers.
