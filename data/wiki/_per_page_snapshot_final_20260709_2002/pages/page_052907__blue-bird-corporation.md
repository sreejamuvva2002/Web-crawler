## Facts

- Blue Bird Corporation is a Georgia-based company that manufactures school buses, including electric and propane-powered models.
- The company focuses on serving public and private educational institutions across North America.
- Blue Bird accumulated market share through its early adoption of clean-energy drivetrains.
- Tough competitors in the market include Thomas Built Buses and IC Bus.
- Government incentives and the growing demand for sustainable transit solutions enable the company to thrive in the school bus industry.
- The company delivered over 130 electric-powered buses in the first quarter of 2025 ahead of schedule.
- Blue Bird maintains a significant backlog of approximately 1,000 EV buses.
- In its first-quarter results, the company reaffirmed full-year 2025 net revenue guidance ranging between $1.4 billion and $1.5 billion.
