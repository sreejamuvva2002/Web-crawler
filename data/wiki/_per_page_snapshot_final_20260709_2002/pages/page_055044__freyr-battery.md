# FREYR Battery

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Energy, Automobile, EV, Renewable News | FREYR Battery Appoints Executive to Lead Global Corporate Development and U.S. Execution](https://oilandgaspress.com/freyr-battery-appoints-executive-to-lead-global-corporate-development-and-u-s-execution/)  ·  `page_055044`

## Facts

- FREYR Battery has announced the appointment of Jeremy Bezdek to the position of Executive Vice President of Global Corporate Development and President for FREYR Battery U.S.
- The Giga America phased development is accelerating, catalyzed by the Inflation Reduction Act.
- The project is based on the Giga Arctic blueprint for a world leading Gigafactory in Coweta County, Georgia.
- FREYR is significantly expanding its strategic ecosystem of technologies, customers, and industrial scaling partners in parallel with the U.S. development.
- Tom Einar Jensen, Co-Founder and CEO of FREYR, stated that seasoned leaders with deep expertise in innovation, business development, and strategy are needed for this important phase of development in the U.S. and globally.
- Jeremy Bezdek comes from the position of Managing Director, Strategic Platforms at Koch Industries, where he spent over 26 years in various executive and leadership roles.
- Prior to FREYR, Bezdek led Flint Hills Resources Biofuels and Ingredients business and Innovation and M&A team, leading active acquisitions, joint ventures, and investments in early-stage development companies related to refining, biofuels, and chemicals industries.
- In his most recent role at Koch Strategic Platforms (KSP), Bezdek led investments as part of KSP’s energy transformation vertical and served on the board of several energy transition companies.
- Bezdek graduated from the University of Kansas and currently resides in Kansas.
- As a former Board Director now joining the executive team, Bezdek stated he will play a role in driving FREYR's development in an operational capacity during a monumental shift in the battery industry.
- FREYR recently announced the selection and purchase of a site in Coweta County, Georgia for the multi-phase Giga America clean battery manufacturing project.
- Giga America positions FREYR to meet the growing demand for energy storage systems (ESS) with U.S.-based conditional offtake partners.
- FREYR is evaluating clean power supply solutions for Giga America with utility providers in the region, including the potential development of a dedicated solar plus storage facility.
- Upon completion of all contemplated construction phases, the Giga America complex is expected to be one of the largest battery cell manufacturing developments globally.
- FREYR previously announced the opening of its technology center in Boston, located in close proximity to technology partner 24M, to enhance technology development, collaboration, personnel development, and strategic coordination.
- FREYR Battery aims to provide industrial scale clean battery solutions to reduce global emissions and produce green battery cells to accelerate the decarbonization of energy and transportation systems globally.
- FREYR has commenced building the first of its planned factories in Mo i Rana, Norway and announced potential development of industrial scale battery cell production in Vaasa, Finland, and the United States.
- FREYR intends to install 50 GWh of battery cell capacity by 2025, 100 GWh annual capacity by 2028, and 200 GWh of annual capacity by 2030.
