# Savannah Economic Development Authority

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Savannah Economic Development Authority | SEDA](https://seda.org/)  ·  `page_059635`

## Facts

- The Savannah region is home to more than one million people.
- The available skilled workforce for businesses is located within a 60-mile radius of the Savannah region.
- More than 4,000 military members transition to the civilian workforce annually in the Savannah region.
- There are 16 area colleges and universities within the Savannah region.
- These 16 colleges and universities have more than 78,000 students producing a talented workforce for industries including creative technologies, entertainment production, and advanced manufacturing.
- The region offers workforce training programs like Quick Start, which is ranked as the state’s number one workforce training program in the United States.
- The Port of Savannah provides a global carrier network and superior location for faster-to-market service linking to U.S. and international markets.
- Logistics connectivity includes two Class I railroads on terminal and Interstates 95 and 16, enabling access to key cities throughout the U.S. in one- to two-day drives.
- SEDA offers creative solutions, connections, and resources for businesses looking to locate to the Savannah region.
- SEDA maintains an award-winning business retention and expansion team to support companies at every step as they grow in Savannah.
