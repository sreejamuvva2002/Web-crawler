# Porsche Cars North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Porsche Reports US Retail Sales for 2023 - Porsche Newsroom USA](https://newsroom.porsche.com/en_US/2024/company/porsche-reports-US-retail-sales-for-2023.html)  ·  `page_048886`

## Facts

- 2023 retail deliveries in the United States totaled 75,415 cars, a new record and a 7.6 percent increase versus the previous record set in 2022.
- Timo Resch serves as President and CEO of PCNA.
- PCNA ranked number one among luxury brands in the 2023 J.D. Power U.S. Sales Satisfaction Index Study.
- The 911 model sold 11,692 units in 2023, an increase of 14.6 percent versus 2022.
- The Macan set a new annual record with 26,947 deliveries against a previous high of 24,716 set in 2021, growing 13.8 percent versus last year.
- Sales of the Taycan grew by 4.1 percent to 7,570 cars, representing 10 percent of PCNA sales in 2023.
- The 718 was the fastest-growing model line in 2023 with an increase of nearly 30 percent to total 4,526 cars.
- Sales of the third-generation Cayenne drove sales past 20,000 vehicles for only the second time in PCNA history.
- Porsche Approved Certified Pre-Owned (CPO) sales in the U.S. totaled a record 38,788 cars, a 17.7 percent year-over-year increase.
- PCNA reported new car sales from January 4, 2023, to December 31, 2023.
- PCNA is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, at One Porsche Drive, Atlanta, GA 30354 USA.
- Established in 1984, PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, Panamera and Taycan.
- PCNA has been headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia since 1998.
- The Atlanta campus is home to the first Porsche Experience Center in North America, which features two module-based 1.6 mile driver development tracks, a business center and Restaurant 356.
- The Atlanta campus is also home to the U.S. headquarters of Porsche Classic.
- PCNA operates a second Porsche Experience Center near Los Angeles featuring a driver development track with eight educational modules totaling 4.1 miles, a business center, Restaurant 917 and the headquarters of Porsche Motorsport North America.
- PCNA supports 198 independently owned and operated Porsche centers, two Porsche studios and five satellite stores in the U.S., including supplying parts, service, marketing, and training.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Porsche boasts some 30,000-plus motorsport wins to date.
