## Facts

- Battery boxes are a central element of electric vehicles that must meet high requirements for crash resistance and fire safety.
- Cost-effectiveness from design to production and a "green footprint" are also criteria for battery box components.
- The Metal Forming Division of voestalpine serves as a partner for the e-vehicle industry for all battery box components.
- voestalpine Automotive Components recognized the future importance of e-mobility and ultra-high-strength steel for safety-relevant battery boxes years ago and built expertise in this area.
- voestalpine offers all battery box components from a single source through its "Battery Box Power Team".
- The company provides coordinated solutions for individual components or component groups across every series size.
- voestalpine operates as a one-stop-shop with a single point of contact, covering the entire process chain from primary material, tailor welded blanks, structural parts, and profiles to assemblies of ready-to-install components.
- Experts provide consulting and engineering support starting from the design and configuration phase, including expert steel recommendations, material data/maps, and geometry recommendations.
- voestalpine offers a broad portfolio of innovative, high-strength steels for lightweight automotive construction that meet high requirements for safety, formability, and weight optimization.
- voestalpine companies are proficient in hot forming, cold forming, and roll forming technologies to manufacture all battery box parts.
- The combined expertise in battery boxes, materials, and production ensures highly economical solutions.
- voestalpine manufactures where its customers are, utilizing a global network of specialized companies in Europe, Asia, America, and Africa.
- The company offers "greentec Steel" solutions with certified evidence for customers.
- voestalpine has a "CO2UNTDOWN TO ZERO" mission to achieve CO2-neutrality as an entire division by 2035.
