# Pirelli S.p.A.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-06

**Source:** [Cyber Tyre output starts at Pirelli's Georgia factory | Automotive World](https://www.automotiveworld.com/news/cyber-tyre-output-starts-at-pirellis-georgia-factory/)  ·  `page_048476`

## Facts

- Pirelli is to start producing its Cyber Tyre connected tyre technology at its Rome, Georgia plant in the US.
- Investment and output details will follow once development plans are finalised.
- The announcement marks the technology’s first US production location.
- The Italian firm is pitching the move as a deepening of its industrial commitment to a strategic market.
- Cyber Tyre is a hardware-and-software system using sensors embedded in the tyre to collect operating data.
- Operating data is processed through Pirelli’s proprietary algorithms and communicated in real time to the vehicle’s electronics.
- The technology supports new driving functionalities, safety features and connected infrastructure use cases.
- Claudio Zanardo, Chief Executive of Pirelli North America, stated that the start of Cyber Tyre production in Rome, Georgia is a significant milestone for Pirelli in this country.
- Zanardo noted the move reflects a commitment to bringing advanced technologies like Cyber Tyre closer to the market.
- The Rome plant is being upgraded with the latest version of Pirelli’s Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) process.
- The MIRS upgrade will be the most advanced across the Pirelli Group and exclusive to Georgia.
- The site has operated for more than 20 years and houses a dedicated R&D centre.
- Pirelli is showcasing Cyber Tyre at the SelectUSA Investment Summit at the Georgia State booth.
- The showcase is timed to coincide with the US Department of Commerce-promoted event.
