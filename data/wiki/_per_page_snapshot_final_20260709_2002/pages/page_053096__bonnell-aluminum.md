# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-17

**Source:** [Bonnell Aluminum Installs Advanced Profile Measuring System for Enhanced Quality Control - Light Metal Age Magazine](https://www.lightmetalage.com/news/industry-news/extrusion/bonnell-aluminum-installs-advanced-profile-measuring-system-for-enhanced-quality-control/)  ·  `page_053096`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum completed the installation of a profile measuring system at its plant in Carthage, Tennessee.
- The installed system is an ASCONA promex EXPERT system designed to improve the quality of extruded profiles.
- This marks the second profile measurement system of this type installed by Bonnell Aluminum, with the first introduced at its manufacturing plant in Newnan, Georgia.
- The system is supplied by iNOEX, LLC and functions as a high-end optical system that measures aluminum profile geometry.
- The optical system measures profiles with a high degree of accuracy and monitors extrusion quality in real time.
- Bonnell Aluminum has utilized the ASCONA promex EXPERT system since 2012 as part of its commitment to continuous quality improvement.
- Carl Czarnik, vice president of Operations for Bonnell Aluminum, stated that the systems have improved both extrusion quality and production efficiency.
- Bonnell Aluminum plans to install two additional promex EXPERT units across its four nationwide extrusion plants.
- The company has been in business for nearly 70 years and operates five manufacturing plants in the U.S.
- Bonnell's products are utilized across multiple industries, including construction, automotive, consumer durables, electric, energy, machinery and equipment, and distribution.
- iNOEX specializes in developing and manufacturing measuring, control, and automation systems for the extrusion industry.
