# Freudenberg-NOK

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-06

**Source:** [Truett McConnell School of Business helps Freudenberg-NOK employees with college finances - The Christian Index](https://christianindex.org/stories/truett-mcconnell-school-of-business-partners-with-freudenberg-nok,45695)  ·  `page_020417`

## Facts

- Truett McConnell University’s Hans Hut School of Business announced a regional initiative with Freudenberg-NOK in Cleveland, Ga.
- Freudenberg Sealing Technologies is a leading market and technology partner for demanding applications, including sealing technology and electro-mobility.
- Established in 1989, Freudenberg-NOK is the Americas joint venture between Freudenberg in Germany and NOK Corporation in Japan.
- The company boasts 5,000 employees in North America and over 50,000 globally.
- Freudenberg-NOK is one of the largest employers in White County, GA where Truett McConnell University resides.
- As part of the new initiative, Truett McConnell will be a preferred online education provider for Freudenberg-NOK employees.
- Initially, the partnership will focus on undergraduate and graduate degree programs in business with the opportunity to broaden degree programs and the languages in which they are offered.
- Tiffani Carson, Human Resource Manager for Freudenberg-NOK stated the company is excited to partner with TMU, values growth for associates, and noted team members are looking forward to furthering their education while working through online, hybrid and onsite classes.
- Dr. Emir Caner, President of TMU offered that Truett McConnell University is honored to partner with Freudenberg-NOK, the leading specialist in sealing technology globally, and combining innovators with their MBA program will see a new generation of business professionals.
- Dr. Kimberly Maiocco, Dean of the Hans Hut School of Business added it is an honor to receive the trust of Freudenberg-NOK in supporting professional development of employees globally, noting Freudenberg values collaborative innovation and looking forward to a long relationship providing expanding academic opportunities through business programs.
- Dr. Stacy Hall, Associate Professor of Business and Program Director of Strategic Partnerships for TMU’s Hans Hut School of Business said the partnership underscores the School of Business’ commitment to partner with leading companies around the globe to provide quality education, collaboration and employment opportunities for students.
- Truett McConnell University is a private Christian liberal arts and sciences university located in Cleveland, Georgia, offering 50 different programs of study including baccalaureate and master’s degrees.
- The University boasts a 10:1 students to faculty ratio while 100% of freshmen receive financial aid.
