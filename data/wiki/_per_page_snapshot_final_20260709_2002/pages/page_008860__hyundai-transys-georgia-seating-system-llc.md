# Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-04-06

**Source:** [Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System, LLC Expands Manufacturing Operations in Georgia - Expansion Solutions](https://www.expansionsolutionsmagazine.com/hyundai-expands-manufacturing-georgia/)  ·  `page_008860`

## Facts

- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System, LLC will invest $9 million in expanding seating manufacturing operations in West Point, Georgia.
- The expansion will create more than 150 jobs in Troup County.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp stated that Georgia’s highly skilled workforce continues to attract manufacturing jobs across the state.
- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System manufactures seats and seat foam pads for the Hyundai Santa Fe, KIA Sorento, and other models.
- The company provides seating structures for automobile manufacturers.
- The company supplies seats to leading electric vehicle makers.
- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System’s President and CEO Sangkil Jung expressed excitement about expanding their presence in the West Point community.
- Sangkil Jung stated pride in the partnership with the State of Georgia.
- The expansion will take Hyundai TRANSYS’ total employment in Georgia to more than 1,890 jobs.
- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System is continuing to hire for careers in production seat assembly, foam, quality, and logistics.
- West Point Development Authority Chairman Kevin Patrick thanked Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System for its continued investment.
- Kevin Patrick stated that automotive and existing industries are the backbone of West Point's economic success.
- Senior Project Manager Amanda Fields represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce division on this competitive project.
- The project was executed in partnership with the West Point Development Authority, Georgia Power, and Georgia Quick Start.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that Hyundai TRANSYS contributes more than $3 billion to Georgia’s economy.
- Pat Wilson noted the state is committed to growing Georgia’s robust automotive ecosystem and creating solid manufacturing jobs of the future.
- Pat Wilson thanked partners at local, state, and federal levels for fostering a pro-business environment that gives automotive partners confidence to invest.
- Georgia is home to more than 200 automotive-related facilities.
- Georgia remains a national leader in advanced manufacturing, outpacing the United States in 10-year GDP growth in machinery, electrical equipment and components, and fabricated metals manufacture.
- Hyundai TRANSYS is an automotive parts company that produces critical automobile parts including transmissions, axles, and seats.
- Hyundai TRANSYS is the only company in the world specializing in transmissions.
- Hyundai TRANSYS produces all types of automatic transmissions, manual transmissions, dual clutch transmissions (DCT), and continuously variable transmissions.
- Hyundai TRANSYS develops and produces top quality seats for Genesis, Santa Fe, Sorento, and other models.
