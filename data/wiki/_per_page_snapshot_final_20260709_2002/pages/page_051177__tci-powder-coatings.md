## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is opening a new office located in Americus, GA this month.
- The addition will be located just a short distance away from Ellaville.
- The Ellaville office will remain fully operational.
- The new Americus facility will kick-start expected growth of laboratory and training facilities, sample product preparation, light warehousing, as well as innovative solutions and increased customer service.
- Doug Greene is the President of TCI Powder Coatings.
- The addition allows the company to accommodate the steady growth of its business.
- In the near future, the company will be able to add laboratory and training facilities as well as sample product preparation and light warehousing in the new facility.
- The facility will contribute to increased employment within the Americus community.
- It is estimated that the office will be able to accommodate up to 75 full-time employees.
- Barbara Grogan is the Executive Director of the Payroll Development Authority.
- Growth of long-term companies such as TCI Powder Coatings is a great benefit to Sumter County, particularly in their ability to hire locally from the existing workforce.
- TCI has been in the region for many years and has an outstanding reputation.
- TCI provides a positive work environment for citizens and a product that is used by many industries worldwide.
