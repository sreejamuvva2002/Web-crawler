# Michelin Retread Technologies

## Facts

- Michelin Retread Technologies (MRT) is expanding its Covington GA manufacturing facility.
- This marks the first major expansion of the facility since it began operation in November 1999.
- The expansion will double the production volume at the site.
- The expansion includes adding high-technology volume to the facility's output.
- John Yarbro serves as the facility manager for the Covington location.
- The facility is located in Lochridge Industrial Park in Covington, southeast of Atlanta.
- The facility produces various Pre-Mold treads for MRT franchises and retread plants across North America.
