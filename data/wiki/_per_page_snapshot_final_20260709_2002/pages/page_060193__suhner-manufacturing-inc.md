# Suhner Manufacturing Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2017-03-08

**Source:** [Suhner Offers Small Diameter Medical Grade Flexible Shafts | Medical Product Outsourcing](https://www.mpo-mag.com/breaking-news/suhner-offers-small-diameter-medical-grade-flexible-shafts/)  ·  `page_060193`

## Facts

- Suhner Manufacturing Inc. has announced the availability of standard and custom flexible shafts for rotary power transmission on various medical, orthopedic, endoscopic and laparoscopic instruments.
- In minimally invasive procedures, torsion stable rotary flexible shafts are vital for providing rotary motion on the surgical tool at the focal point of the work being performed.
- Suhner flexible shafts offer torsional strength and minimal deflection with a uniformly smooth rotational behavior to ensure proper torsion and tactile feel on the distal end for the surgeon.
- These rotary flexible shafts are available with options such as passivation, electropolishing and Teflon coating.
- They are provided as cleaned and assembled product to suit the stringent applications of various medical procedures.
- Suhner medical grade flexible shafts provide precisely controlled rotary power transmission in a limited space.
- On one current application, a Suhner flexible shaft measuring only 0.024 inches in diameter is in use for endoscopic surgeries.
- Other applications include endovascular aneurysm/aortic repair (EVAR) and thoracic endovascular (TEVAR) surgery, as well as stapling devices, orthopedic drills, surgical screwdrivers and laparoscopic devices.
- Suhner rotary flexible shafts for medical instruments are available in clockwise, counterclockwise and bi-directional motion designs.
- Shafts are made from Type 302, 304, 316 and 17-7 stainless steel, as well as high-carbon steel, depending on the application.
- Suhner engineering works with OEMs to devise functional solutions for specific instrument, tool and device applications.
- Medical grade rotary flexible shafts are available in various diameters from 0.024 to 0.500 inches.
- Suhner Manufacturing Inc. is part of the Suhner corporation.
- The company was founded in 1976 as a flexible shaft manufacturer to provide for the automobile sector.
- The Rome, Ga.-based company operates a production facility with a floor space of 18,300 meters squared.
- The facility supplies electrical power tools and abrasives, automation and cutting units.
