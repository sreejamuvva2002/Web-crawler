# Bridgestone Bandag Tire Solutions

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Bridgestone Bandag P&D Tires Are SmartWay-Approved | Heavy Duty Trucking](https://www.truckinginfo.com/articles/bridgestone-bandag-pd-tires-are-smartway-approved)  ·  `page_018266`

## Facts

- Bridgestone Bandag Tire Solutions, a division of Bridgestone Americas Tire Operations, announced that two of its pickup and delivery tires were approved for use on Environmental Protection Agency SmartWay-certified equipment.
- The R260F and R250F all-position radials are designed to promote long tread life and low cost per mile in pickup and delivery, urban and regional hauling environments.
- Chris Hoffman, manager of TBR and Retread Product Marketing, stated that the approval is a tremendous benefit for customers participating in SmartWay, as most previously approved tires were long-distance radials not built for high-scrub environments like beverage hauling, package and parcel delivery, grocery, food service, and other P&D operations.
- Adding the R260F and R250F to the verified technology list allows SmartWay fleets to specify the right tire for the job.
- The R260F has 22/32-inch tread depth, uses Bridgestone's patented Equalizer Rib structures to fight irregular wear and groove-bottom platforms to enhance retreadability, and features thick protector ribs on both sidewalls to fight curbing, with ribbed sidewalls allowing reversal on the wheel for continued protection.
- The R250F has straight-line grooves that promote traction and durable protector ribs on both sidewalls to fight damage from curbing, cuts and abrasions, with a casing incorporating four steel belts with an all-steel casing ply for long life and retreadability.
- Chris Hoffman noted that the R260F and R250F are California Air Resources Board (CARB) compliant, which benefits regional and P&D fleets operating in California.
