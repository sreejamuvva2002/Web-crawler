# Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc.

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc. manufactures and markets engine management, electric powertrain, and integrated vehicle controls for major automotive manufacturers worldwide.
- The company adheres to ISO/TS:16949:2009 industry standards.
- Gary Burns was named the new Ecommerce Sales Manager for the Hitachi Astemo Americas Aftermarket Division on Nov. 25, 2022.
- In his role, Burns will take the lead on strategy to increase both online brand awareness and online sales of Hitachi Astemo Americas products.
- Burns will work under the guidance of Sales Director Ryan Pelkey and closely with Marketing Manager Hannah Hua.
- Burns will take a leadership role in establishing new business partnerships and scaling online business for the Aftermarket Division.
- Burns' background includes managing Amazon Seller, eBay, and Walmart accounts to support Hitachi Astemo Americas' growing B2B and B2C digital sales.
- Gary Burns stated that throughout his career he has been immersed in almost every aspect of digital marketing, advertising, and sales, and is ready to lead the team that expands the brand's digital reach as Hitachi Astemo Americas increases its lineup of world-class quality products.
- Prior to joining Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc., Burns held leadership positions at Newegg.com and Xtractor Depot.
- Burns has produced photographic and written marketing content, analyzed, budgeted, and forecasted, and sharpened his business development skills.
- Burns holds certificates in Google Ads management and web development.
