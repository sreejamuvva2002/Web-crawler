# Yamaha Motor Corporation, U.S.A.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-02-26

**Source:** [Announcement Concerning the Relocation of Our U.S. Subsidiary and the Sale of Fixed Assets - News releases | Yamaha Motor Co., Ltd.](https://global.yamaha-motor.com/news/2026/0226/subsidiary.html)  ·  `page_010932`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Co., Ltd. has decided to proceed with the relocation of its U.S. subsidiary (YMUS) and the sale of all fixed assets owned by YMUS in Cypress, including land, offices and warehouses.
- This initiative is positioned as one of the Company's key measures aimed at improving asset efficiency and enhancing profitability in the United States.
- The Cypress facility has served as the headquarters of YMUS for approximately 50 years, since Yamaha Motor Co., Ltd. acquired the land in 1978 and established the office in 1979.
- YMUS Marine Business was relocated to Kennesaw, Georgia in 1999 to serve the customer better.
- YMUS Motorsports Business was relocated to Kennesaw, Georgia in 2019.
- The Cypress facility currently primarily houses corporate functions and the Financial Services business.
- The total site area of the Cypress facility is approximately 25.1 acres (approximately 102,000 square meters).
- The Company plans to utilize a sale-and-leaseback arrangement for a certain period in order to ensure business continuity and to facilitate a smooth relocation to Kennesaw.
- Details such as the sale price, purchaser, and timing of the transaction are currently under review.
- Yamaha Motor Co., Ltd. is undertaking structural reforms aimed at improving the profitability of its U.S. operations in response to cost increases resulting from U.S. tariffs and changes in the market environment.
- The Company is implementing cross-business cost reduction initiatives alongside these structural reforms.
- Over the medium to long term, the Company seeks to build a profit structure that is not solely dependent on top-line growth, thereby transforming itself into a more resilient and robust organization capable of adapting to change.
