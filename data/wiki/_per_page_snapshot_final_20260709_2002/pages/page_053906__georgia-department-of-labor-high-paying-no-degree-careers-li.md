## Facts

- Nine 'hot' Georgia careers pay more than $40,000 annually without requiring a college degree.
- Labor market data is sourced from the state Department of Labor (GDOL).
- A high school diploma or equivalent GED satisfies the education requirement for these nine jobs.
- Associate managers in property and real estate have the highest salary among the listed roles.
- Sales representatives for wholesale and manufacturing (excluding tech and scientific products) have the largest annual openings.
- Electricians had a 2015 annual wage of $45,150.
- Electricians had 710 projected annual openings between 2012 and 2022.
- Plumbers, pipefitters, and steamfitters are included in the list of nine hot jobs.
- The remaining listed positions cover various construction, manufacturing, and service trades with comparable wage and opening data.
