## Facts

- E-Z-GO Certified Pre-Owned RXV vehicles are tested, reconditioned, and certified to the company's highest standards.
- CPO vehicles are refurbished in Augusta, Georgia, with component replacements and a comprehensive inspection.
- Customers can now build and price a Certified Pre-Owned RXV vehicle online to explore available options and understand pricing upfront.
- The CPO inspection covers the body, cowl, bumper, tires, steering wheel, tops, struts, windshield, front and rear facing seats, floormats, headlights, tail lights, turn signals, trim, logos, and hardware.
- The re-certification process also includes checking the steering unit, alignment, axels, floorboards, dash, battery amps, state of charge meter, and 14 other key areas.
- Certified Pre-Owned E-Z-GO vehicles provide access to an industry-leading service network comprising over 80 E-Z-GO-certified technicians nationwide.
- The service network includes a call center team with a 35-second wait time.
- Every E-Z-GO Certified Pre-Owned vehicle comes with a 1-year parts and accessory warranty.
- Extended warranty coverage is available for CPO vehicles.
- Lease terms of up to 4 years are available for these vehicles.
- E-Z-GO has handcrafted every vehicle in Augusta, Georgia since 1954.
