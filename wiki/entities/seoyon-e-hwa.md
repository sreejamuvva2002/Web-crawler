# Seoyon E-HWA

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** Savannah, Chatham County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Seoyon E-HWA is a global auto parts supplier that announced a $76 million investment to build a new manufacturing facility in the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center, creating 740 new jobs and expanding its EV-related interior component production in Georgia.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Supplies electric vehicle interior parts such as door trims, headlining, and tailgate trims for Hyundai Motor Group and other OEMs.
