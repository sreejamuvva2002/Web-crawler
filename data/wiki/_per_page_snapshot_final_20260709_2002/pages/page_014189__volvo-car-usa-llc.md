# Volvo Car USA LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-03

**Source:** [Volvo Car USA](https://news.cision.com/volvo-car-usa/Index/itiviti-gears-up-to-accelerate-sell-side-oms-adoption--providing-upgrade-path-for-bloomberg-sseoms-c)  ·  `page_014189`

## Facts

- Headquartered in Mahwah, New Jersey.
- Volvo Car USA LLC (VCUSA) is a subsidiary of Volvo Car Group of Gothenburg, Sweden (Nasdaq Stockholm VOLCAR B).
- Provides sales, marketing, parts, service, technology, and training support to Volvo automobile retailers in the United States since the 1950s.
- Operations include a manufacturing campus in Ridgeville, SC that underlines the company’s long-term commitment to the United States as a key market.
- Maintains offices in California and parts distribution and training centers across the country.
- For the full year 2024, Volvo Car Group recorded a record-breaking core operating profit of SEK 27 billion.
- Revenue in 2024 amounted to an all-time high of SEK 400.2 billion.
- Global sales reached a record 763,389 cars in 2024.
- Volvo Cars was founded in 1927 and is one of the most well-known and respected car brands in the world with sales to customers in more than 100 countries.
- Listed on the Nasdaq Stockholm exchange under ticker “VOLCAR B”.
- Company purpose is "For life. To give people the freedom to move in a personal, sustainable and safe way."
- Ambition to become a fully electric car maker.
- Committed to an ongoing reduction of its carbon footprint, with ambition to achieve net-zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2040.
- As of December 2024, Volvo Cars employed approximately 42,600 full-time employees.
- Head office, product development, marketing and administration functions are mainly located in Gothenburg, Sweden.
- Production plants are located in Gothenburg, Ghent (Belgium), South Carolina (US), Chengdu, Daqing and Taizhou (China).
- R&D and design centers are located in Gothenburg and Shanghai (China).
