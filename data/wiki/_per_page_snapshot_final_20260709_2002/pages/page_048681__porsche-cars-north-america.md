## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America (PCNA) celebrated its 40th anniversary, reflecting on its incredible journey since 1984.
- In 1998, PCNA relocated its headquarters to Atlanta, Georgia.
- One Porsche Drive opened in 2015 as PCNA's new U.S. headquarters facility.
- The One Porsche Drive facility includes the Porsche Experience Center Atlanta and serves as a home for Porsche Classic.
- PCNA's model lineup expanded from three models in 1984 to six today: the 718, 911, Taycan, Panamera, Macan, and Cayenne.
- The Taycan is included in PCNA's current vehicle lineup.
- Porsche operates over 200 Porsche Centers across the United States.
- Porsche achieved a remarkable sales figure of over 75,000 cars in 2023.
