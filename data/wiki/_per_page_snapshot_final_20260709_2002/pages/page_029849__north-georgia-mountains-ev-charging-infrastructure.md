# North Georgia Mountains EV Charging Infrastructure

**Type:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Best Electric Vehicle Road Trips in the North Georgia Mountains | Explore Georgia](https://exploregeorgia.org/itinerary/best-electric-vehicle-trips-in-the-north-georgia-mountains)  ·  `page_029849`

## Facts

- The North Georgia region does not have as many chargers as the state’s major metropolitan areas.
- EV drivers can power up at public and private charging stations at hotels, resorts, and attractions throughout the area.
- Charging stations are located in the small towns of Blue Ridge, Ellijay, Young Harris, and Dillard.
- Drivers should keep their EV’s battery fully charged and not dip below 20 percent.
- Remote mountain roads have longer stretches between charging stations.
- Hampton Inn Blue Ridge offers guests access to four EV chargers.
- Blue Ridge Inn Bed & Breakfast is equipped with one EV charger, which is a Tesla plug-in exclusively for guests.
- Gilmer Chamber & Welcome Center has three EV chargers.
- Brasstown Valley Resort & Spa has two 16kW Tesla chargers.
- Asiano, a restaurant in downtown Hiawassee, has a dual charging station for EVs.
- Julep Farms offers one of two Gen-3 Tesla wall connectors available for guest use.
- Clayton has four public charging station ports for EV vehicles.
