# Robert Bosch GmbH

## Facts

- Robert Bosch GmbH (“Bosch”) is a German engineering and technology group headquartered in Gerlingen, near Stuttgart.
- Founded in 1886, Bosch has grown into a global supplier of technology and services spanning mobility (automotive and software-driven mobility), industrial technology, consumer goods, and energy/building technology.
- Bosch is privately held and reports figures across multiple pages and reports.
- Bosch reported €90.3 billion in sales revenue in 2024 with €3.1 billion EBIT from operations.
- Bosch employed 417,859 associates worldwide as of Dec. 31, 2024.
- For 2025 preliminary figures, Bosch lists €91.0 billion in sales revenue, €1.7 billion EBIT from operations, and 412,400 associates worldwide as of Dec. 31, 2025.
- In 2024, Bosch reported €7.8 billion in R&D spending, with 86,840 R&D associates across 136 locations.
- Bosch reports having subsidiaries and regional companies in some 60 countries (preliminary as of Dec. 31, 2025).
- Bosch reports 490 subsidiaries and regional companies (preliminary as of Dec. 31, 2025).
