# Kumho Tire

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-05-15

**Source:** [Kumho Crugen Premium KL33 selected as OE tire for 2018 Atlas SUV](https://www.tractionnews.com/kumho-crugen-premium-kl33-selected-oe-tire-2018-atlas-suv/)  ·  `page_056666`

## Facts

- Kumho Tire has been selected by the Volkswagen Group as an original equipment (OE) supplier for the new 2018 Atlas SUV.
- The 2018 Atlas is a seven-passenger family-ready SUV and the newest and largest member of the Volkswagen portfolio.
- The Atlas will be available with the Kumho Crugen Premium KL33 in a 245/60R18 fitment.
- The Atlas will also be equipped with standard Kumho Crugen HP91 tires in 245/60R18 and 255/50R20 fitments.
- The North American market will offer Crugen Premium KL33 equipped Atlas SUVs.
- The overseas market will offer the Crugen HP91 equipped vehicles.
- Kumho’s Crugen Premium KL33 and Crugen HP91 tires will be supplied to Volkswagen’s factory in Chattanooga, Tennessee.
- Volkswagen's Chattanooga factory will assemble the spacious and multi-trim level Atlas SUV.
- The Atlas features advanced interior technology and a unique Automatic Post-Collision Braking System.
- Harry Choi is the CEO of Kumho Tire USA.
- Harry Choi stated that Kumho is elated to be associated with Volkswagen’s new entry into the SUV category by supplying the 2018 Atlas with their Crugen Premium tires.
- The collaboration with Volkswagen demonstrates Kumho's strategic and steadfast focus on expanding its global OE business.
- The Kumho Crugen Premium KL33 features an upgraded silica compound and noise-canceling tread that reduces road noise and vibration for exceptional all-weather performance and a smooth, comfortable, quiet ride.
