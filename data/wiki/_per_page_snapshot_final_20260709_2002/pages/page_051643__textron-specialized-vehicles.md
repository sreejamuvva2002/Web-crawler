# Textron Specialized Vehicles

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2016-10-21

**Source:** [Textron and Jacobsen to combine bringing about 150 jobs to Augusta in 2017](https://www.wrdw.com/content/news/Textron-and-Jacobsen-to-combine-bringing-about-150-jobs-to-Augusta-in-2017-397961021.html)  ·  `page_051643`

## Facts

- Textron Specialized Vehicles and Jacobsen, both Textron Incorporated companies, have announced they will combine their operations in Augusta by the end of 2017.
- Jacobsen’s operation in Charlotte will relocate to the Textron Specialized Vehicles facilities in early 2017 with their first phase of manufacturing.
- Approximately 150 new jobs will be created as part of the combined operations.
- Augusta's Economic Development Authority Chairman Henry Ingram stated that the relocation of the Jacobsen operation is another prime example of an outstanding existing industry that is continuing to grow in our region.
- Jacobsen is a leading manufacturer of professional turf-care equipment under the Jacobsen®, Dixie Chopper® and Ransomes® brands.
