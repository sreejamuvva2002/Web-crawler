## Facts

- Rowley Spring & Stamping Corp. has, for many years, been a leading supplier of spring and stamping components used in hundreds of products in various applications.
- Since its inception in 1954, Rowley Spring & Stamping Corp. has grown from a small manufacturer with 13 employees and a dozen customers to 125 employees and over 700 customers.
- The company operates under NAICS code 332 for Fabricated Metal Product Manufacturing.
- Rowley Spring & Stamping made a pledge in April 2014 as part of the Better Plants Challenge.
- The company is located in Bristol and participates in the Better Buildings & Better Plants Initiative.
