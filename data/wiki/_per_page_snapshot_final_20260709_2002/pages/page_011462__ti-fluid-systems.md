# TI Fluid Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-15

**Source:** [An 'Innovation' Day to Remember - TIFS](https://tifluidsystems.com/gwm-tech-day-23/)  ·  `page_011462`

## Facts

- Team members from TI Fluid Systems (TIFS) China recently delivered an Innovation Day at the global headquarters of Great Wall Motors (GWM).
- The event provided TIFS engineers an opportunity to showcase their evolving product portfolio and explore supporting GWM in developing its next generation of vehicles.
- TIFS led presentations detailing their coolant and refrigerant product portfolios, with many latest products displayed in GWM’s exhibition hall for hands-on attendee interaction.
- GWM Vice President Zhao Guoqing (Mark) and Chief Technical Officer Wang Yuanli (Julien), PhD attended the event alongside other purchasing and product development leaders.
- GWM provided positive feedback on the showcase, specifically expressing interest in TIFS's system integration capability and new ITMA, ITMR, and ITMS products.
- The TIFS Modules & Systems team received a request for quotation (RFQ) from GWM for a coolant module following the event.
- Additional opportunities to expand work with GWM are currently being explored as a result of the Innovation Day.
- This marked the first of several Innovation Days planned for the year, designed to update global OEM customers on latest products and technologies while fostering discussion and collaboration.
