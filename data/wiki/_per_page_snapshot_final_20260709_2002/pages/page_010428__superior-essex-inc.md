# Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Superior Essex Continues Commitment To Sustainability, Partners With Copper Mark - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.de/news/superior-essex-continues-commitment-to-sustainability-partners-with-copper-mark/)  ·  `page_010428`

## Facts

- Superior Essex is headquartered at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327 – USA.
- Superior Essex has entered an agreement to become the eighth corporate partner of The Copper Mark.
- The Copper Mark is described as the only comprehensive social and environmental assurance program for the copper industry.
- The partnership aligns with Superior Essex's sustainability goals and its recently announced Vision 2030 for the Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire brand.
- The partnership aims to source responsibly for magnet wire products.
- Superior Essex Communications is one of the world's leading manufacturers of communications cables.
- The company has been a leader in sustainability since 2011 and collaborates with copper suppliers to reduce product impacts by sourcing materials from responsible sources.
- The Copper Mark is an assurance framework used to demonstrate responsible production practices and contribution to UN Sustainable Development Goals.
- The framework evaluates copper producers against criteria covering 32 environmental, social, and governance topics related to mineral and metal procurement.
- Scott Wilde serves as Global Vice President for Strategic Sourcing at Superior Essex.
- CEO Brian Kim recently made Vision 2030 commitments regarding environmentally friendly production and product improvement from the source.
- Michèle Brülhart is the Managing Director of The Copper Mark.
- Annie Bevan is the Global Head of Sustainability at Superior Essex.
- The Copper Mark evaluates management practices of copper mines, smelters, and refineries across human rights, labor conditions, community, environment, and general governance categories.
- Company processes are measured against standards defined in a risk assessment by the Responsible Minerals Initiative (RMI), which comprises over 380 cross-industry companies.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, and Essex Brownell.
- The company employs over 3,000 employees across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is the world's leading provider of magnet wire and a leader in communications fiber applications and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company contributes significantly to disruptive technological advances in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- The Copper Mark was originally developed and funded by the International Copper Association (ICA).
- The Copper Mark is an independent entity headquartered in the UK.
