# Fictiv

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-08

**Source:** [Fictiv and EVCO Plastics Announce Strategic Partnership to Deliver End-to-End Injection Molding Supply Chain Solutions](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/fictiv-evco-plastics-announce-strategic-130000880.html)  ·  `page_054386`

## Facts

- Fictiv has created a strategic partnership with EVCO Plastics to deliver end-to-end injection molding supply chain solutions.
- The collaboration aims to tariff-proof manufacturing for U.S. OEMs by combining Fictiv's digitally connected global supply chain with EVCO's manufacturing capabilities.
- EVCO Plastics provides over 1 million square feet of manufacturing space and hundreds of injection molding machines, including presses as big as 4,400 tons.
- The partnership leverages deep expertise in design for manufacturability (DFM), AI, human-powered materials data and insights, program management, and global logistics.
- The alliance aims to mitigate tariff concerns and deliver a highly resilient, fully managed supply chain to OEMs across multiple industries.
- Fictiv addresses complexities in Mexico's emerging ecosystem for highly engineered products by streamlining tooling and specialized secondary processes.
- Innovative solutions like Fictiv GlobalFlex integrate tooling inserts produced in China with next-generation manufacturing facilities in the U.S. and Mexico.
- This integration enhances gross margins, accelerates production timelines, and elevates customer experiences.
- The collaboration supports the entire product lifecycle, encompassing 3D printing, injection molding, assembly, testing, and packaging.
- Advanced manufacturing capabilities include overmolding, gas-assist molding, in-mold decorating, and liquid silicone rubber (LSR) production.
- The partnership holds ISO 9001:2015, ISO 13485, IATF 16949, and FDA compliance certifications to ensure quality and reliability throughout the supply chain.
- Dave Evans, Co-Founder and CEO of Fictiv, stated that the partnership marks a crucial step in simplifying global manufacturing supply chains.
- Carlos Gonzales, head of EVCO Plastics de Mexico, noted that Fictiv's capabilities allow EVCO to concentrate on manufacturing excellence while Fictiv tackles complex workflow and supply chain challenges.
- The collaboration underscores a shared commitment to innovation, quality, and sustainability in the manufacturing sector.
- Fictiv operates four global manufacturing centers located in India, Mexico, China, and the U.S.
- To date, Fictiv has delivered more than 35 million commercial and prototype parts for early-stage companies and large enterprises.
- EVCO Plastics was founded in 1964, employs over 2,100 people, and operates 270 injection molding machines ranging from 28 to 4,400 tons across 13 manufacturing facilities worldwide.
