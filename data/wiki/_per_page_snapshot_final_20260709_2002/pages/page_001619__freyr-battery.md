## Facts

- Freyr has completed the acquisition of Chinese firm Trina Solar’s Texas solar manufacturing assets.
- The acquisition completes Freyr's transition from a European battery company to a US solar one.
- NYSE-listed battery startup Freyr has pivoted strategy and acquired a 5GW solar module facility in Texas, US, from Chinese firm Trina Solar.
- The acquisition occurred on the same day that Donald Trump was declared to have won the presidential election (6 November).
- CEO Birger Steen discussed the company's recent decision to minimize European investments in light of the Inflation Reduction Act.
- Freyr is discussing a potential policy response and its approach to energy storage systems (ESS) and using conventional technologies.
- The US Department of Energy (DOE) has earmarked up to US$3.5 billion of new capital for battery manufacturing.
- This DOE funding announcement came a week after Freyr announced it would only be scaling in the US for now.
