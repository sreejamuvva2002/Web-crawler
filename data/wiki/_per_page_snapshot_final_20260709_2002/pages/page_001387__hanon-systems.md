# Hanon Systems

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-23

**Source:** [Gov. Kemp: Automotive Supplier Hanon Systems to Create 160 New Jobs in Bulloch County  - Grice Connect](https://www.griceconnect.com/business/gov-kemp-automotive-supplier-hanon-systems-to-create-160-new-jobs-in-bulloch-county-7036587)  ·  `page_001387`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Hanon Systems will create 160 new jobs.
- Hanon Systems will invest more than $40 million in a new manufacturing facility.
- The new manufacturing facility is located in Bulloch County.
- Hanon Systems is a global provider of automotive thermal and energy management solutions.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp made the announcement while on an economic development trip to Israel.
- The facility will be located on a 35 acre tract in the Gateway Industrial Park.
- The site is near Cardinal Glass, just outside of Statesboro off of Hwy. 301 South.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia’s automotive and e-mobility industries continue to grow.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia is furthering its position as the EV capital of America.
- Min Sung, President and CEO of Hanon Systems, stated the company is contributing to the local economy of Bulloch County through job creation.
- Min Sung thanked the State of Georgia and the cooperation between its agencies for guiding the project.
- The announcement aligns with Hanon Systems' strategy to support the industry shift toward electrification.
- The announcement aligns with Hanon Systems' strategy to grow its North America footprint.
- Hanon Systems will build the new facility at Gateway Regional Industrial Park to assemble automotive thermal solutions.
- Operations at the new facility are expected to begin in May 2024.
- Billy Allen, Chair of the Development Authority of Bulloch County, stated the county is honored by Hanon Systems' decision to locate its new facility there.
- The new facility will work closely with the Hyundai Metaplant and the electric vehicle industry.
- Georgia Ports Authority (GPA) Executive Director Griff Lynch stated the announcement further solidifies Georgia's position as a growing hub for auto manufacturing.
- The GPA supports the operations of Hyundai Motor Group and its suppliers to deliver jobs and opportunity for Georgia residents.
- Alyce Thornhill, Director of Project Implementation and Supplier Strategy, represented the GDEcD's Global Commerce team on this competitive project.
- The project was executed in partnership with the Development Authority of Bulloch County, Georgia Ports Authority, Georgia Power, and Georgia Quick Start.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that suppliers like Hanon Systems support larger industry ecosystems through partnership-driven solutions ranging from workforce training to logistics systems efficiencies.
- Bulloch County is part of the Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority.
- Georgia is positioned as a hub for the electric mobility industry.
- Since 2020, over 40 EV-related projects have announced more than $22.7 billion in investment in Georgia.
- Since 2020, over 40 EV-related projects have announced 28,400 jobs in Georgia.
- On October 25, Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America officially broke ground on its first fully dedicated EV and battery manufacturing facility.
- The Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America facility is located in neighboring Bryan County.
- Hanon Systems was formed in 1986.
- Hanon Systems is a leading supplier of automotive thermal and energy management solutions for electrified and conventional vehicles.
- Hanon Systems offers solutions in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning; powertrain cooling; compressors; fluid transport; and electronics and fluid pressure.
