## Facts

- JLL Capital Markets announced the sale and acquisition financing secured for 8540 Zell Miller Road.
- The property is a fully occupied Class A light manufacturing and distribution facility in Statesboro, Georgia.
- The site serves as an extension of the established Savannah, GA industrial market.
- JLL represented the seller, which is a partnership between Graham Capital and Chesterfield.
- Spaulding & Slye Investments acquired the property.
- JLL arranged the acquisition financing with Seacoast Bank on behalf of the new owner.
- The facility is designated as a Hanon Systems Build-to-Suit industrial property.
