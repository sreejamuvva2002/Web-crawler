# GEON Performance Solutions

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-11-08

**Source:** [GEON® Performance Solutions Announces New Chief Executive Officer](https://www.geon.com/news/geon-performance-solutions-announces-new-chief-executive-officer)  ·  `page_021092`

## Facts

- Tracy Garrison appointed as new Chief Executive Officer of GEON Performance Solutions.
- Appointment effective January 2020.
- Mr. Garrison will assume the role held by interim CEO David Mezzanotte, a Director of SK Capital Partners.
- Jack Norris, Managing Director of SK Capital, stated that Tracy Garrison has a record of driving growth at businesses he has led.
- Most recently, Mr. Garrison served as President and Chief Executive Officer of Hexpol Compounding Americas.
- Hexpol Compounding Americas operates 20 factories in the United States and Mexico.
- Hexpol Compounding Americas employs more than 2000 associates.
- Previously, Mr. Garrison spent five years at Rhein Chemie Corporation leading their commercial organization as Vice President of Sales and Marketing.
- Mr. Garrison held a Business Development Manager role at MA Hanna.
- Mr. Garrison holds a B.S. in Chemistry from the University of Tennessee Martin.
- Mr. Garrison began his career at Colonial Rubber Works.
- Following Mr. Garrison's start, Mr. Mezzanotte will remain closely involved to support a successful transition and continue serving as a member of the Company’s Board of Directors.
- GEON Performance Solutions is a global leader in the formulation, development, and manufacture of performance polymer solutions.
- GEON combines highly adaptable vinyl and polyolefin polymer technologies with a full-service manufacturing business.
- GEON serves markets including appliances, building & construction, electronics, healthcare, transportation, wire & cable, and more.
- GEON Performance Solutions has 1,100 global associates.
- GEON Performance Solutions operates 12 world class manufacturing plants.
- GEON Performance Solutions headquarters is located in a western suburb of Cleveland, Ohio.
- GEON Performance Solutions is a portfolio company of SK Capital Partners.
- Hexpol Compounding Americas is the leading subsidiary of publicly-traded Hexpol AB with strong global market positions in rubber and advanced polymer compounding.
