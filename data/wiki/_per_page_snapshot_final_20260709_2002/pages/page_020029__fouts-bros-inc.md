# Fouts Bros Inc.

## Facts

- Fouts Bros Inc. was founded in 1952 and remains family owned and operated.
- Fouts Bros Inc. is one of the largest and most diverse commercial truck, van, and automotive equipment manufacturers in the U.S.
- The company grew from a single service station in Smyrna, GA into a collection of industrial brands over the last seven decades.
- Fouts Defense serves as the newest branch of the family of brands.
- Fouts Defense provides American fire tankers, pumpers, wildland trucks, quick attacks, and commercial vans and trucks to U.S. Armed Forces personnel worldwide.
- Fouts Defense is led by industry veteran George Bergamini.
- Fouts Defense apparatus will be listed on both the DLA and the GSA in the coming weeks.
- Fouts Defense operates as a wholly owned subsidiary of Fouts Bros Inc.
- Fouts Defense specializes in equipping U.S. military and government agencies with commercial and fire apparatus.
- Fouts Bros Inc. is dedicated to the manufacturing, upfitting, and distribution of American work trucks for private and public sectors.
- The corporate headquarters is located at 138 Roberson Mill Rd., Milledgeville, GA 31061.
- The headquarters facility spans 700,000 square feet.
- The company operates a second facility in La Grange, TX.
