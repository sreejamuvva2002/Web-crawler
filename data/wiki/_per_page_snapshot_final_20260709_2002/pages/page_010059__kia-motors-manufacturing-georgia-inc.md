# Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2017-09-21

**Source:** [Team Members with Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia Support Hurricane Relief Efforts - Kia Georgia](https://www.kiageorgia.com/team-members-with-kia-motors-manufacturing-georgia-support-hurricane-relief-efforts/)  ·  `page_010059`

## Facts

- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc. (KMMG) is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Motors Corporation based in Seoul, Korea.
- KMMG has an annual capacity of 360,000 units.
- KMMG is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Georgia.
- KMMG began mass production on Nov. 16, 2009.
- KMMG is home to the Sorento CUV and the Optima mid-size sedan, two of the brand’s top selling models in the U.S.
- Stuart C. Countess serves as KMMG’s chief administrative officer.
- Gov. Nathan Deal publicly acknowledged KMMG's corporate presence and community contributions in Georgia.
