# Volvo Group North America

## Facts

- Kingston, Ontario purchased two Mack LR Electric refuse models as its first battery-electric vehicles (BEVs) for its Solid Waste fleet.
- The City of Kingston aligned these purchases with its vision of being “Canada’s Most Sustainable City” and furthering its commitment to climate action.
- Volvo Trucks increased the use of recycled and renewable materials in its newest heavy-duty truck models to reduce manufacturing environmental impact.
- Mack Defense received $47 million in programmatic funding to continue building the M917A3 Heavy Dump Truck at its Allentown, Pennsylvania facility.
- U.S. Rep. Ryan Mackenzie announced the Mack Defense funding during an event at the facility.
- The Mack Defense funding is part of a new five-year contract awarded in June 2025 for up to 450 trucks with a total contract value of up to $221.8 million.
- Mack Trucks highlighted the driver-centric cab and interior design of the all-new Mack Granite, which was engineered from the ground up around the people behind the wheel.
- Volvo Trucks is introducing over-the-air software updates for fleets that can run overnight, during driver breaks, or while parked without requiring drivers to stay with the vehicle.
- The over-the-air software update feature will launch later in 2026.
- The New York City Department of Sanitation and its nonprofit partner the Sanitation Foundation unveiled five new hand-painted Mack LR refuse vehicles during New York City’s Trucks of Art program.
- Clinton Aluminum, the largest distributor of aluminum mold plate in North America, is using Mack MD7 medium-duty trucks to haul heavy materials due to their strong performance and dependability.
- Clinton Aluminum plans to convert its fleet of 34 vehicles to an all-Mack fleet.
- Volvo Autonomous Solutions (V.A.S) and DSV marked the start of autonomous freight operations in Texas, hauling the first commercial truckload using the Volvo VNL Autonomous.
- The autonomous freight partnership between V.A.S and DSV has ambitions to expand to additional lanes over time.
- Mack Trucks announced a comprehensive redesign of its PartsASIST online parts-ordering platform featuring a modernized user interface, faster performance, and new tools for customers and dealers.
- The Mack MP13 engine is the most powerful and efficient engine Mack has ever produced and will be EPA 2027-compliant.
- Mack showcased the MP13 engine during the Advanced Clean Transportation (ACT) Expo 2026 from May 4-7 at the Las Vegas Convention Center in Las Vegas, Nevada.
- Mack Trucks added ABM and Lane Valente Industries as new full-service providers to its Turnkey Solutions program for battery-electric vehicle customers.
- Volvo Trucks North America is expanding electric truck capabilities in North America by adding new capabilities to the Volvo VNR Electric.
- A next-generation Volvo engine combines expanded power, strong fuel efficiency, and the lowest NOx levels Volvo has ever offered.
- Mack Trucks announced the availability of the Mack ImpactShield, which features the first-ever use of Corning Fusion5 Glass in a Class 8 heavy-duty truck.
- The Mack ImpactShield is standard on the all-new Mack Granite and delivers advancements in windshield durability, damage resistance, and total cost of ownership.
- Gulf Highway Equipment announced plans to purchase 160 additional Mack MD Series cab and chassis units for its construction company and government agency clients.
- Coke Canada Bottling more than doubled its fleet of Volvo VNR Electric trucks across the country.
