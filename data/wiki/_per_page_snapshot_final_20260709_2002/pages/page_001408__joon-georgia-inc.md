# Joon Georgia Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-12

**Source:** [Hyundai supplier Joon Georgia Inc. to build facility in Bulloch County](https://www.savannahnow.com/story/business/manufacturing/2022/11/12/hyundai-bryan-county-joon-georgia-inc-facility-bulloch-county/8293763001/)  ·  `page_001408`

## Facts

- Auto parts maker Joon Georgia Inc. announced it will build a new manufacturing facility in Bulloch County.
- The project will create more than 600 jobs.
- The company is investing $317 million into Bulloch County for the facility.
- Joon Georgia Inc. serves as the first supplier for Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America in Bryan County.
- The new facility will manufacture frames for vehicles at the Hyundai EV plant in Black Creek.
- Joon Georgia Inc. is part of Ajin USA.
- Ajin USA is already a supplier for several other Hyundai Motor Group facilities.
- The announcement regarding the facility was made on November 12, 2022.
