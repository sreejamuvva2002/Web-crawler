# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America is developing a tire manufacturing facility in Rome, Ga.
- Site work has begun on the $140 million, 400,000-sf building housing office, research and manufacturing facilities.
- The plant is scheduled to open in mid-2002.
- The facility will provide tires for both new vehicles and the replacement tire market.
- H&M Co. of Jackson, Tenn., holds the design-build contract.
