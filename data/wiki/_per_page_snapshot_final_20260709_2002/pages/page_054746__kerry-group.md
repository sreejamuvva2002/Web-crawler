# Kerry Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2022-03-16

**Source:** [Kerry Group Opens $137M Plant in Georgia with 250 Employees | Food Manufacturing](https://www.foodmanufacturing.com/capital-investment/news/22118977/kerry-group-opens-137m-plant-in-georgia-with-250-employees)  ·  `page_054746`

## Facts

- Kerry Group has officially opened its newly upgraded facility in Rome, GA.
- A total of $137.4 million has been invested to create what the Ireland-based company calls one of the most state-of-the-art food manufacturing facilities in the United States.
- The investment represents Kerry’s largest-ever capital expenditure investment and one of the biggest in Georgia’s history.
- Officials from Georgia and Ireland attended the March 14 ribbon-cutting ceremony.
- Attendees at the event included Irish Minister for Justice Helen McEntee, Kerry CEO Edmond Scanlon, chairman of the board Philip Toomey, Georgia Economic Development Commissioner Pat Wilson, and state and regional elected officials.
- The facility now employs 250 people.
- The facility provides integrated taste and nutrition solutions to help customers meet growing consumer demand in the poultry, seafood, and alternative protein markets across the US and Canada.
- The facility has a footprint of 316,000-square-feet and offers the latest food technologies and systems with high standards of food safety.
- As part of Kerry’s sustainability strategy, Beyond the Horizon, the company prioritized sustainability initiatives across the entire facility.
- Sustainability initiatives include using 100% renewable electricity, zero waste to landfill, bulk receiving of materials, local sourcing where possible, and extensive use of energy efficient equipment.
- Oliver Kelly, president and CEO of Kerry in North America, stated that the new facility more than doubles their capacity in Rome and is now one of the most advanced and modern food manufacturing facilities in the country.
- Kelly noted that increased and changing consumer demand for tasty, nutritious, and sustainable food and beverages is driving accelerated change and reshaping the entire food industry.
- Investing in world-class facilities like this one in Georgia allows Kerry to continually work and co-create with customers to respond to changing demand.
- The Rome, Georgia facility is an example of Kerry’s dedication to its goal of reaching over two billion people with sustainable nutrition solutions by 2030.
- Kerry expressed thanks for the support received from the Governor of Georgia’s office and looks forward to continued partnership.
- Kerry is the world’s leading taste and nutrition partner for the food, beverage, and pharmaceutical markets, with ingredient solutions reaching over 1 billion consumers around the world.
- Kerry's consumer insights, global RD&A team of 1,100+ food scientists, and extensive global footprint enable the company to solve customers’ complex challenges with differentiated solutions.
