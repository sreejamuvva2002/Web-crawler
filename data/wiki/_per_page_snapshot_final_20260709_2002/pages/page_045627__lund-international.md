# Lund International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-11-15

**Source:** [Automotive - LUND INTERNATIONAL® CELEBRATES ITS BEST SEMA SHOW TO DATE](https://kahnmedia.com/lund-international-celebrates-best-sema-show-date/)  ·  `page_045627`

## Facts

- Lund International is based in Buford, Georgia.
- Lund International designs, engineers, manufactures, and markets branded automotive aftermarket accessories for passenger cars, SUVs, CUVs, light trucks, Jeeps, and heavy trucks.
- Lund International has a total of 1,200 culturally diverse employees.
- 500 of Lund International's employees are based in Georgia, many of whom are multi-generational.
- Nearly all of Lund International's products are made in the U.S.A.
- The state of Georgia honored Lund International as its Automotive Company of the Year in 2017.
- Lund International CEO is Mitch Fogle.
- At the SEMA Show, Lund International debuted 18 new products across its consumer light truck and automotive accessory brands.
- Lund International's Jeep-focused brand, Rampage Products, released the most new products at the show, including accessories for the new Jeep Wrangler JL.
- Rampage Products introduced the TrailCrawler line, which includes the TrailCrawler Skid Plate, TrailCrawler Steps, and TrailCrawler Bumper.
- AVS won SEMA's "Best New Exterior Accessory Product" award with its Aeroskin LightShield Hood Protector.
- The Aeroskin LightShield Hood Protector features an integrated light bar comprised of amber LED marker lights designed into the classic Aeroskin Hood Protector.
- Roll-N-Lock was selected for a 2019 Global Media Award for the launch of its QwikRak Rec Bars Rack System.
- The QwikRak Rec Bars Rack System adds the ability to hold up to 350 pounds of cargo above the truck bed while maintaining security.
- One of Lund International's nine booth vehicles was selected to compete in the 2018 SEMA "Battle of the Builders" competition, making it into the Truck/Off-Road Top 10.
- The 1973 International Scout II built by VIK Kustoms used products from Lund International brands AMP Research, Bushwacker, and Rampage Products.
- Lund International was recognized in 2018 with "Platinum" status for its product data with SEMA Data Co-Op, the highest rating achievable through the organization.
- Lund International's portfolio of brands includes AMP Research, AVS, Belmor, Bushwacker, LUND, Rampage Products, RoadWorks Manufacturing, Roll-N-Lock, Stampede, and Tonno Pro.
- Lund International offers the industry's broadest range of functional body and appearance products that provide customers with ways to protect, customize, and personalize vehicles.
- CEO Mitch Fogle stated that Lund International remains dedicated to its American workforce in plants across the United States and to making parts using American skilled labor.
- CEO Mitch Fogle stated that Lund International is finalizing plans for 2019 and beyond to continue growing its brands and product offerings.
