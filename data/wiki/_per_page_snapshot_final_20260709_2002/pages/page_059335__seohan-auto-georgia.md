## Facts

- Seohan Auto Georgia is committed to leading the future of eco-friendly mobility as a core component manufacturer, driven by an unwavering focus on sustainable growth and operational excellence.
- The company was established in October 2022 as part of Seohan Group’s global expansion and strategic commitment to the U.S. automotive market.
- Its new manufacturing facility is located in Midway, Georgia.
- Mass production at the Midway, Georgia facility began in October 2024.
- The company operates under three key strategic pillars: securing and expanding volume, enhancing production competitiveness, and preparing for stable future growth.
- Seohan Auto Georgia has set a long-term volume target of over 500,000 units per year.
- The facility produces EV and HEV models, including new vehicle developments.
- Production line enhancements include re-tooling scheduled to be completed by October 2025.
- New equipment setups are planned for February 2026.
- The company is continuously exploring further capacity expansion opportunities.
- Seohan Auto Georgia is actively building a robust ESG governance structure tailored to its Georgia operations.
- ESG initiatives include establishing transparent management practices and strengthening internal control systems.
- The company is defining clear ESG performance metrics, such as greenhouse gas reduction targets, after accurately assessing its emissions.
- Jungkee Koo serves as the CEO of Seohan Auto Georgia.
