# Dade County, Georgia

## Facts

- New construction requires obtaining an electrical permit from the Dade County Commission Office.
- In most cases, new construction also requires a septic system permit from the Dade County Health Department.
- State Food Services permitting may be obtained from the local Public Health Department located in the Administrative Building at 71 Case Avenue, Trenton, Georgia 30752.
- State permits concerning environmental issues may be obtained from the Georgia Environmental Protection Division.
- All other state license questions can be directed to the Georgia Secretary of State’s Administrative Offices of the Professional Licensing Boards.
- The notice provides general information on permits currently required in Dade County and should not be relied upon for legal advice regarding county, state, or federal statutes or regulations.
- Nothing in the notice restricts the County from adopting new or different ordinances or regulations in the future.
- Information about opening a business in Dade County is available via the Dade County Business Guide, which can be requested or accessed online.
