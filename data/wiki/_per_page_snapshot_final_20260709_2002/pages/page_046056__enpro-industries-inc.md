# EnPro Industries Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-09-27

**Source:** [EnPro Industries Sells Business to MAT HD - The BRAKE Report](https://thebrakereport.com/enpro-industries-sells-business-in-rome-ga/)  ·  `page_046056`

## Facts

- EnPro Industries, Inc. (NYSE: NPO) announced the sale of its Rome, Ga. business to MAT HD.
- The sold Rome, Ga. business was part of EnPro’s STEMCO heavy-duty unit.
- The facility produces brake shoes, brake kits, and lined shoes for medium- and heavy-duty truck and bus applications.
- The business will become part of the brake products group at MAT HD.
- MAT HD manufactures and distributes products for the automotive, hardware, and power equipment sectors.
- Marvin Riley, President and Chief Executive Officer of EnPro, stated the sale aligns with a strategy to shift the portfolio to markets where EnPro can add the most value for customers.
- EnPro expects to record total charges related to the sale of approximately $14.5 million.
- These charges will be recognized in the third quarter of 2019.
- Approximately $2.7 million of the charges are for contract cancellation costs, severance, and other expenses.
- The remaining portion of the $14.5 million charges are non-cash.
- EnPro Industries is a leader in sealing products, metal polymer and filament wound bearings, components, and service for reciprocating compressors, diesel and dual-fuel engines, and other engineered products.
