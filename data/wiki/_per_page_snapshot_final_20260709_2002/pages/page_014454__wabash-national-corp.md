# Wabash National Corp.

**Type:** investment_announcement  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Wabash National is investing more than $2 million in a plant for its Wabash Composites facility in Frankfort, IN.
- The expansion supports the company's strategic growth and diversification efforts.
- Dick Giromini serves as president and CEO of Wabash National.
- Mayor Chris McBarnes and city officials provided support for the project.
- The 100,000 sq. ft. facility will house manufacturing operations for semi-trailer aerodynamic products, mobile storage containers, mobile offices, and other cargo units.
- Wabash National expects to create more than 100 jobs over the next five years at the facility.
- The expansion revitalizes a formerly vacant site located at 901 West Morrison Street in Frankfort.
- Frankfort's Economic Development Plan, low-cost utilities, fiber optics, and a business-friendly environment contributed to attracting Wabash National.
- The hiring process for the new facility will begin in October.
- Initial operations at the expanded facility are expected to begin in December.
