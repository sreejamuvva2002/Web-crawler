# Daimler Truck North America LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-11-07

**Source:** [Daimler Truck North America Enters Strategic | decarbonfuse.com](https://decarbonfuse.com/posts/daimler-truck-north-america-enters-strategic-partnership-with-hexagon-purus-for-battery-electric-freightliner-em2-for-vocational-applications)  ·  `page_053553`

## Facts

- Daimler Truck North America LLC (DTNA), the nation’s largest manufacturer of Class 6-8 commercial vehicles, entered into a long-term agreement with Hexagon Purus on November 6, 2023.
- The agreement provides complete vehicle integration of the battery electric Freightliner eM2, developed to serve vocational applications.
- Hexagon Purus is a leading manufacturer of zero-emission mobility and infrastructure solutions.
- Hexagon Purus will incorporate its proprietary zero-emission technology into the integration, including battery systems, auxiliary modules, power modules, and vehicle-level software.
- The integration will also include power-take-off (PTO) options to supply power to the vocational body and equipment.
- Earlier in 2023, DTNA unveiled a vocational Freightliner eM2 prototype truck with vocational upfit options as part of an innovation project together with selected Truck Equipment Manufacturers Alamo and Altec.
- The innovation project aims to lay the groundwork for expanding zero-emission solutions for vocational customers in the utility, sweeper, dump, construction, towing, and refuse segments.
- The partnership underscores a joint commitment by DTNA and Hexagon Purus to drive innovation and sustainability in the vocational vehicle sector.
- The collaboration will deliver products that complement DTNA’s eCascadia and eM2 battery electric portfolio, which is already in production.
- Aaron Scates, vice president of vocational and medium-duty market development at DTNA, stated excitement about the collaboration's potential for the future of electric vocational trucks.
- Morten Holum, CEO of Hexagon Purus, expressed pride in continuing their long-standing relationship and supporting DTNA’s transition to zero-emission mobility.
- DTNA has been an important zero-emission technology development partner for Hexagon Purus in North America for several years through the Innovation Fleet program.
- Prior to this collaboration, DTNA worked with Hexagon's Agility division on the development of Freightliner natural gas fuel tank integration.
- Hexagon Purus’ high-voltage battery technology is known for its efficient kWh per meter of frame length, which aligns with the packaging needs of medium-duty vocational applications.
- Daimler Truck North America LLC is headquartered in Portland, Oregon.
- DTNA designs, engineers, manufactures and markets medium- and heavy-duty trucks, school buses, vehicle chassis and their associated technologies and components under the Freightliner, Western Star, Thomas Built Buses, Freightliner Custom Chassis Corp and Detroit brands.
- Daimler Truck North America is a subsidiary of Daimler Truck, one of the world’s leading commercial vehicle manufacturers.
- Hexagon Purus is a world leading provider of hydrogen Type 4 high-pressure cylinders and systems, battery systems and vehicle integration solutions for fuel cell electric and battery electric vehicles.
- Hexagon Purus' products are used in a variety of applications including light, medium and heavy-duty vehicles, buses, ground storage, distribution, refueling, maritime, rail and aerospace.
