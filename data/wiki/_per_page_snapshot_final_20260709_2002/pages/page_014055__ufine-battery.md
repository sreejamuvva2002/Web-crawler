## Facts

- The global golf cart battery market value is projected around USD 158.0 million in 2025 and expected to grow to roughly USD 267.3 million by 2035.
- Lead-acid batteries still account for over 60% of current installs due to affordability and legacy installations.
- Lithium-ion solutions are rapidly gaining market share due to lighter weight, maintenance-free design, and longer cycle life.
- North America commands the largest regional market share, followed closely by Asia-Pacific and Europe.
- A 48V lead-acid golf cart battery weighs 30–40 kg with a cycle life of 500–1,000 cycles.
- A 48V lithium-ion golf cart battery weighs 15–25 kg with a cycle life of 2,000–4,000+ cycles.
- Lead-acid batteries require frequent maintenance such as water and cleaning, while lithium-ion requires none.
- Lead-acid offers moderate runtime and lower upfront cost, whereas lithium-ion provides longer consistent runtime with higher upfront but lower long-term costs.
- Trojan Battery Company (USA) developed the first golf cart battery in 1952 and remains a market leader.
- Trojan offers deep-cycle flooded lead-acid, AGM, and lithium-ion solutions ranging from 2V to 48V.
- The Trojan OnePack Lithium range includes 48V 105Ah options with integrated management systems for durability and runtime.
- U.S. Battery Mfg. Co. (USA) is a century-old American manufacturer specializing in deep-cycle batteries for golf carts, industrial vehicles, and RV/Marine applications.
- U.S. Battery offers flooded lead-acid and Essential Li® lithium-ion batteries engineered with robust BMS systems and fast charging capabilities.
- The US 48VGC2 lithium battery features IP67 waterproofing and fast recharge times for performance-minded users.
- Ufine Battery is a Chinese custom battery manufacturer specializing in lithium batteries, founded in 2008 with headquarters in Guangdong.
- Ufine Battery operates under a research and development program focused on lithium battery technology innovations.
- Ufine Battery provides customization backed by certifications including KC, UL, MSDS, CB, UL1642, IEC62133, UL2054, PSE, ROHS, and UN38.3.
- Ufine Battery ships tens of thousands of battery units worldwide for electric vehicles, solar energy storage, RVs, home appliances, smart gadgets, and power tools.
- Ufine Battery offers a 48V 100Ah LiFePO4 golf cart battery with over 2,000 cycle life, weighing 23 kg, operable in extreme temperatures, and featuring a built-in BMS with 20+ protections.
- Ufine Battery can customize capacity, voltage, curvature, and dimensions for prototype testing or volume production.
- East Penn Manufacturing (Deka) is part of EnerSys’s portfolio and serves as a major supplier of deep-cycle batteries in North America.
- East Penn produces high-quality lead-acid and AGM lines known for excellent discharge performance and durability under varied conditions.
- Exide Technologies is a large global manufacturer producing lead-acid and specialty batteries suitable for golf carts.
- Exide is known for advanced AGM designs that deliver stable voltage during frequent partial discharge scenarios common in frequently charged carts.
- RELiON Battery is a U.S.-based innovative lithium-ion maker focused on high-performance LiFePO4 solutions for golf carts, RVs, marine, and solar storage.
- RELiON offers intelligent drop-in lithium batteries like the InSight Series that fit standard battery trays without modification.
- RELiON BMS systems are designed to optimize lifespan, reduce heat, and provide real-time performance monitoring.
- Battle Born Batteries is a U.S.-based player specializing in LiFePO4 lithium batteries, including 48V golf cart systems.
- Battle Born batteries withstand deep cycling with minimal degradation and often come with industry-leading warranties of approximately 10 years.
- Dakota Lithium (USA) specializes in high-performance lithium batteries for golf carts and other recreational vehicles.
- Dakota Lithium is known for lightweight LiFePO4 packs that maintain strong performance under heavy use and are easier to install than bulky lead-acid banks.
- Interstate Batteries (USA) primarily markets automotive batteries but also supplies deep-cycle units for golf cart use through partnerships and distribution networks.
- Leoch International / Allied / Discover Battery Group is a global OEM group, often China-based, supplying deep-cycle and lithium packs under private-label brands like Allied, ECO, or Discover Battery.
- The Leoch group provides competitive cost options with international certifications and flexible customization for OEM clients.
- The average replacement cycle for lead-acid golf cart batteries is typically 4–6 years depending on usage and maintenance.
- Lithium golf cart batteries can last 8–12 years.
- Mixing different brands of golf cart batteries is not recommended because voltage and internal resistance differences may reduce performance and lifespan.
- Extreme heat accelerates battery degradation while extreme cold temporarily reduces charge capacity, though lithium handles temperature swings better than lead-acid.
- Some manufacturers like Eco Battery and Battle Born focus on lithium-ion with recyclable materials and lower environmental impact.
