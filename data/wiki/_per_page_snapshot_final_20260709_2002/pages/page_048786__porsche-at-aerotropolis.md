# Porsche at Aerotropolis

## Facts

- The Porsche at Aerotropolis project is located in Atlanta, Georgia.
- The site is highly visible and adjacent to Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport.
- In 2006, Ford Motor Company announced the closure of its assembly plant in Hapeville, Georgia, directly adjacent to Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport.
- Jacoby Development acquired the former Ford site.
- Jacoby Development demolished its structures.
- Jacoby Development put together an ambitious redevelopment plan for the site.
- In 2011, Porsche Cars North America announced the plan to develop a new North American headquarters facility at the site.
- The Porsche facility includes a 220,000 SF headquarters office building.
- The facility includes the Porsche Technical Training Center.
- The facility includes the Porsche Customer and Driving Experience Center.
- The Porsche Customer and Driving Experience Center features a 1.6-mile test track and handling circuit.
- Porsche opened its $100 million facility in 2015 to great acclaim.
- An adjacent hotel opened in late 2017.
- These investments have inspired additional investments and commercial developments around the airport.
