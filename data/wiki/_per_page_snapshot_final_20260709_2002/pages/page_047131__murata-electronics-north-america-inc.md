# Murata Electronics North America Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 2011-04-01

**Source:** [Murata Appoints New CEO | Microwave Journal](https://www.microwavejournal.com/articles/10847-murata-appoints-new-ceo)  ·  `page_047131`

## Facts

- Murata Electronics North America announced the appointment of David M. Kirk as President, Chief Executive Officer (CEO) and a Director of MENA effective April 1, 2011.
- Kirk brings to the new role more than 27 years of engineering, marketing and management experience in the electronics industry.
- Before joining Murata Electronics North America Inc., Kirk held the position of President, CEO and a Director of RF Monolithics Inc.
- Prior to that, he spent 14 years with Murata Electronics North America where he held positions ranging from product management of various product lines to Director of Marketing for North America.
- Kirk succeeds Satoshi Sonoda, who will continue his assignment in MENA for a couple of months, to facilitate the transition of his responsibilities to Kirk.
- Kirk said: "I am honored to be the first non-Japanese CEO of North America operations."
- Kirk noted Murata's progressive forward thinking to recognize the importance of the North American market and the desire for local knowledge on opportunities within emerging markets such as energy and healthcare.
- Kirk stated this continues the aggressive positioning of Murata in the global marketplace, noting they are no longer just considered the world largest chip capacitor supplier but the world's largest module supplier for wireless applications.
- Kirk will be based out of Murata Electronics North America corporate offices in Smyrna, GA (US).
