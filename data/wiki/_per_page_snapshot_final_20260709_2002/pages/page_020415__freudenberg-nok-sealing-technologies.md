# Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2017-11-07

**Source:** [Freudenberg-NOK announces supplier excellence achievement award winners | Sealing & Contamination Control Tips](https://www.sealingandcontaminationtips.com/freudenberg-nok-announces-supplier-excellence-achievement-award-winners/)  ·  `page_020415`

## Facts

- Freudenberg-NOK Sealing Technologies announced the recipients of its 2016 Supplier Excellence Achievement Level (SEAL) Awards.
- Freudenberg-NOK runs the business operations for Freudenberg Sealing Technologies in the Americas and is a leading global specialist in sealing applications across multiple industries.
- The SEAL awards are now in their 10th year and recognize raw material and metal fabrication partners who have consistently maintained excellent standards in areas like quality, delivery, cost management, technology and customer service.
- The company awards three levels of recognition – Gold, Silver and Bronze – to its top-performing suppliers.
- Chemical raw material supplier The Chemours Company (Wilmington, Del.) received a 2016 SEAL Award Gold.
- Chemical raw material supplier Solvay Specialty Polymers business unit (Alpharetta, Ga.) received a 2016 SEAL Award Silver.
- Chemical raw material supplier H.B. Chemical Corporation (Twinsburg, Ohio) received a 2016 SEAL Award Bronze.
- Metal & Metal Fabrication supplier Headly/Tryson Group (Broadview, Ill.) received a 2016 SEAL Award Gold.
- Metal & Metal Fabrication supplier Rima Manufacturing Co. (Hudson, Mich.) received a 2016 SEAL Award Silver.
- Metal & Metal Fabrication suppliers Elgiloy Specialty Metals and Combined Metals (Bellwood, Ill.) received a 2016 SEAL Award Bronze.
- Michael Miller, VP, Americas, Strategic Sourcing / Purchasing at Freudenberg-NOK, stated that the company focuses on technical excellence, superior quality, and value for customers, and that their supply base is critical to achieving this level of service.
- Suppliers supporting Freudenberg-NOK’s business operations in the Americas are ranked against an annual performance scorecard to identify top achievers and to encourage adoption of continuous improvement and lean operations.
- Freudenberg-NOK’s internal continuous improvement and lean program, Growtth (Get Rid of Waste Through Team Harmony), is a manufacturing benchmark in the industry and incorporates lean supply chain practices as part of its foundation.
