## Facts

- Ascend Elements is the global leader in circular battery materials.
- The company announced a series of year-end milestones that cap a transformational 2025 and position it for accelerated growth through 2026 and beyond.
- Ascend Elements stands alone as the proven, US- and EU-based answer to delivering circularity and security from core battery material recycling to engineered-component production at scale.
- Linh Austin serves as President and CEO of Ascend Elements.
- In 2025, the company proved circularity works at commercial scale, establishing itself as North America’s most advanced platform for integrated, domestic production of essential battery ingredients with industry-leading cost and carbon footprint reduction.
- In May, Ascend Elements secured a $340M non-dilutive grant commitment from the Republic of Poland to support development of advanced pCAM and lithium processing capacity in Europe.
- In September, Ascend Elements became America’s first commercial-scale producer of recycled lithium carbonate with greater than 99% purity.
- The company has production operations in Georgia.
- Near-term expansion is planned for Poland.
- The company plans to deliver more than 15,000 tons of recycled Li₂CO₃ annually by 2027.
- This output directly addresses critical mineral shortages in the US and Europe.
- In November, Ascend Elements executed a 15,000 metric ton take-or-pay lithium carbonate agreement with Trafigura.
- The Trafigura agreement assures multi-year revenue and reinforces market confidence in its lithium-first strategy.
- The company executed a multi-year, nearly $1B supply contract with a leading global automaker.
- This contract demonstrates clear and compelling OEM demand for certified, circular, domestic supply at scale.
- Ascend is the only Western firm delivering full, commercial-scale vertical integration from recycling to cathode-active pCAM and battery-grade lithium carbonate today, not in pilot.
- The company utilizes a patented Hydro-to-Cathode® Process that upgrades recycled feedstock straight into high-value components, including Lithium Carbonate and pCAM.
- This process cuts cost, steps, and reduces CO₂e by up to 90% over mining-based approaches.
- Commercial production of recycled lithium carbonate is already operational in the U.S.
- The company's model eliminates dependency on imported, high-impact materials.
- Active U.S. and EU sites, rapid expansion, and long-term offtakes ensure locally sourced, clean, and transparent supply for electric vehicles and grid storage.
- Upon financial close expected in Q1/Q2 2026, Ascend will scale its Kentucky and Poland facilities.
- The company's growth plan will power robust revenue and EBITDA expansion through the decade.
- The company supports national security, energy transition, and climate targets.
- Ascend Elements converts spent lithium-ion batteries and manufacturing scrap into premium precursor cathode material (pCAM) and battery-grade lithium carbonate.
- The company delivers circular, sustainable supply for EV and energy storage markets through plants operating at scale and a proven technology platform.
