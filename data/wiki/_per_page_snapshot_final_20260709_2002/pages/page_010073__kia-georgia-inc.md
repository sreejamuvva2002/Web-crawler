# Kia Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-02-24

**Source:** [KIA GEORGIA USA Celebrates 5 Millionth American Car Built](https://www.theautochannel.com/news/2026/02/24/1637201-kia-georgia-usa-celebrates-5-millionth-american-car-built.html)  ·  `page_010073`

## Facts

- Kia Georgia celebrated the start of production for the all-new 2027 Kia Telluride at its West Point facility on Feb. 24, 2026.
- The ceremonial vehicle represented three major milestones: the start of production for the 2027 Kia Telluride, the first hybrid electric vehicle (HEV) assembled in Georgia, and the 5 millionth vehicle produced at Kia Georgia.
- Georgia Governor Brian P. Kemp attended the ceremony alongside team members and state/community leaders.
- Sean Yoon, President and CEO of Kia North America and Kia America, stated that Kia Georgia is the only plant in the world that builds the Telluride.
- The 2027 Telluride launch marks Kia's first US-assembled HEV.
- Since beginning production in 2009, Kia Georgia has grown into one of the region’s largest employers and a cornerstone of automotive manufacturing in the Southeast.
- The Telluride has been exclusively manufactured in Georgia since its introduction in 2019.
- Stuart Countess, Kia Georgia’s President and CEO, emphasized that producing world-class gasoline-powered vehicles, EVs, and HEVs demonstrates the versatility and talent of the plant's team.
- Kia Georgia, Inc. is the first manufacturing site in North America for Seoul-based Kia Corporation.
- Kia Georgia has an annual production capacity of 350,000 units.
- The facility is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Ga.
- Kia Georgia began mass production on Nov. 16, 2009.
- Kia Georgia currently produces the Telluride, Sorento (excludes HEV and PHEV models), Sportage (excludes HEV and PHEV models) SUVs, and the all-electric EV9 and EV6.
