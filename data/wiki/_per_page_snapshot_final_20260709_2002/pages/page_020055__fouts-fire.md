# Fouts Fire

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-03

## Facts

- Established in 1952 and proudly family-owned, Fouts Fire is a premier manufacturer of high-quality fire apparatus headquartered in Milledgeville, Georgia.
- Specializes in the design and production of reliable, cost-effective fire apparatus, including pumpers, tankers, and rescue vehicles.
- Designs nine standard models engineered to meet the evolving demands of fire departments nationwide.
- Utilizes a precision manufacturing process that standardizes designs to ensure every component is manufactured to exacting specifications for consistent quality.
- Employs a streamlined assembly process with fewer customizations and a focus on essential features to reduce production bottlenecks and accelerate build times.
- Maintains one of the largest dealer networks in the country to provide local support, training, and service for delivery and deployment.
