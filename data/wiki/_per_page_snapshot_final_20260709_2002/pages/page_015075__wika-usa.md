## Facts

- WIKA USA maintains its main campus in Lawrenceville, Georgia.
- WIKA USA is a worldwide leader in industrial measurement technology.
- WIKA USA operates a peer-nominated Core Value Award program that has been running for two decades to recognize employees who exemplify seven core values.
- Award recipients receive a plaque, a monetary award, and a designated parking spot at the main campus.
- Five employees received awards in November 2020: Jessica Woodside (Marketing), Eric Castillo (Information Technology), Angela Villalba (Planning), Greg Clark (Diaphragm Seals Division), and Stephorn Cousin (Diaphragm Seals Division).
