# JTEKT North American Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-04-30

**Source:** [East Tennessee manufacturing plant closing, affecting more than 100 workers](https://www.wvlt.tv/2025/12/08/east-tennessee-manufacturing-plant-closing-affecting-more-than-100-workers/)  ·  `page_045343`

## Facts

- JTEKT North American Corporation issued a WARN Notice on Dec. 4.
- The company intends to permanently close its manufacturing plant located in Telford, Tennessee.
- The closure will affect 136 Tennessee employees.
- Plant closure is scheduled to begin “on or around April 30, 2026” continuing through August 31, 2026.
- The Northeast Workforce Development Board’s rapid response team, employed by the First Tennessee Development District, was notified to coordinate services with the employer and affected employees.
- JTEKT North American Corporation describes itself as a “global leader in engineering and manufacturing automotive systems.”
- The company also works in bearings and machine tools.
- JTEKT employs 5,000 people across 23 facilities globally.
- Two of JTEKT's facilities are located in Morristown and Vonore, Tennessee.
