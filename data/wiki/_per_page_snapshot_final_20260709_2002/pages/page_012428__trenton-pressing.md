# Trenton Pressing

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-04-06

**Source:** [Trenton Pressing breaks ground on new $10 million manufacturing facility – Discover Dade](https://www.discoverdade.com/local/trenton-pressing-breaks-ground-on-new-10-million-manufacturing-facility/)  ·  `page_012428`

## Facts

- Trenton Pressing broke ground on a new $10 million manufacturing facility in Trenton, Ga., on April 6, 2023.
- The new facility will be 105,000 square feet and will allow for expanded manufacturing and assembly services, significantly increasing the organization’s manufacturing capacity.
- Construction on the new facility is expected to be completed by December of 2023.
- In just over two years prior to the announcement, Trenton Pressing purchased a struggling manufacturing company and completely turned it around through a reorganization of management, technology and equipment upgrades, and personnel training.
- The expanded facility will allow the company to better service its growth and meet the manufacturing needs of its clients, which include BMW, Club Car, ZF, and more.
- Trenton Pressing is currently one of the top five employers in Dade County with 220 employees.
- The company plans to add 20 more jobs with the facility expansion.
- Trenton Pressing began operations in Dade County in 2021.
- Trenton Pressing provides metal stamping processes, welding and assembly processes, warehousing and shipping.
- The company offers a wide range of products and services including automatic and hand metal stamping, welding, engineering, and product assembly.
- Hans Pfunder, chief operating officer for Trenton Pressing, stated: “We are extremely grateful for Dade County’s support throughout this expansion process.”
- Hans Pfunder noted: “It was exciting to see the tremendous growth over the past two years after the reorganization process. This achievement speaks to the dedication and commitment of our employees.”
- Dade County Executive Ted Rumley stated: “Trenton Pressing has been a great community partner since it began operations in our county in 2021. They have not only provided great jobs, but they have also supported our schools and the environment.”
- Trenton Mayor Alex Case stated: “Trenton Pressing has been a great asset to our city since they first came here just over two years ago. We are grateful that they are providing us with much needed jobs and also for their overall commitment to help improve the quality of life for our citizens.”
- Jeff Mullis, president and CEO of the Northwest Georgia Joint Development Agency and retired state senator, stated: “I want to congratulate Trenton Pressing on their expansion efforts and commend them for adding jobs in the Northwest Georgia area.”
