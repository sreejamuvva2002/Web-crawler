## Facts

- Government regulators worldwide have outlined decarbonization roadmaps with stringent CO2 emissions targets, including zero carbon emissions by 2030-2050 depending on the region.
- Automotive manufacturers are placing increased requirements on 12V advanced lead batteries to address CO2 emissions regulations.
- 12V battery requirements are increasing for every powertrain vehicle type, including mild-hybrid, full-hybrid, and Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV).
- In prior years, 12V batteries exclusively powered vehicle electronics and remained at a full State of Charge.
- Today, the 12V advanced battery must operate in Partial State of Charge (PSoC) during Stop/Start events to power the entire vehicle when the engine is off.
- The advanced lead battery enables BEV vehicles to be steered and stopped off the roadway if the Li-Ion propulsion battery fails.
- AGM (absorbent glass mat) advanced lead batteries have been proven in Stop/Start powertrains and are the 12V advanced battery of choice for future powertrains including BEVs.
- AGM batteries are a technological improvement to traditional flooded lead batteries and have become the advance lead battery choice in the automotive industry.
- A notable advantage of AGM batteries is that they typically last longer than flooded alternatives, making them suitable for passenger vehicles.
- The AGM separator provides compression against electrodes to prevent active mass shedding.
- This additional compression ensures the advanced AGM battery lasts longer, especially when prone to failure in PSoC conditions.
- All-glass AGM separators for passenger vehicles are designed to improve battery performance and withstand PSoC operations to improve battery life.
- H&V all-glass AGM separators offer various configuration options to meet processability needs.
- They feature lower electrical resistance to improve battery performance.
- They are offered in a range of surface areas to improve electrolyte filling and cycle life.
- They provide best-in-class thickness retention to increase battery life.
- They are ideal for powertrains with Stop/Start functionality, mild/full-hybrid, and BEV applications.
- H&V is the global leader partnering and supplying AGM separators to the automotive market.
- H&V battery separators offer the highest standards for performance and battery assembly.
