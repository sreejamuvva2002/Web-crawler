# Rivian

## Facts

- Rivian has announced construction of a $120m supplier park attached to its EV manufacturing plant in Illinois.
- Construction of the supplier park is expected to be completed by the end of 2026.
- Expansion of Rivian's main manufacturing plant in Normal, Illinois is already underway.
- The supplier park will be located a short distance from the main manufacturing plant in Normal, Illinois.
- The supplier park is expected to host several of Rivian's key suppliers.
- Co-locating suppliers aims to drastically reduce timelines and costs associated with logistics, especially shipping and warehousing.
- Nearshoring production, including parts and materials for its EVs, will improve supply chain resilience through domestic production.
- Nearshoring will also increase the speed at which units can be produced.
- The supplier park is intended to reduce supply chain costs and improve efficiency for Rivian, an EV-only OEM based in the US.
- Rivian claims the supplier park will be a “key enabler” to its operations as it begins manufacture of the R2 van.
- Rivian founder and CEO RJ Scaringe stated that the supplier park will be a “key enabler” in increasing the plant’s output in 2026 as product lines diversify.
