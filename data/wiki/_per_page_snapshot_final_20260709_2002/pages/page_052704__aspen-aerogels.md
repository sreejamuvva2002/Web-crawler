# Aspen Aerogels

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Gov. Kemp: Aspen Aerogels to Open Advanced Manufacturing Facility in Bulloch County - EIN Presswire](https://www.einnews.com/pr_news/563418313/gov-kemp-aspen-aerogels-to-open-advanced-manufacturing-facility-in-bulloch-county)  ·  `page_052704`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Aspen Aerogels (NYSE: ASPN) plans to construct an advanced manufacturing facility at Bulloch County’s Southern Gateway Commerce Park.
- Aspen intends to invest a minimum of $325 million in the project.
- The company will create more than 250 advanced manufacturing jobs at its new site near Statesboro.
- Production at the Georgia facility is expected to begin in late 2023.
- Governor Kemp stated that Aspen Aerogels has put confidence in Georgia, its people, and the growing electric mobility sector in the state.
- Combined with Aspen’s original East Providence, Rhode Island plant, the planned facility is designed to triple Aspen’s nameplate aerogel capacity.
- The expansion supports Aspen’s thermal barrier expansion plans in the fast-growing electric vehicle market.
- Aspen aims to capitalize on increased demand for its Aerogel Technology Platform™ as EV OEMs continue to adopt PyroThin® aerogel thermal barrier solutions.
- Don Young, President and CEO of Aspen, noted the company is pleased to be in Georgia for the siting, development, and construction of its PyroThin materials plant.
- The City of Statesboro and Bulloch County region offer Aspen access to efficient ports, a strong available workforce, secure low-cost utilities, and critical raw materials.
- Aspen is increasingly being asked to provide broader solutions for improved battery performance and safety centered on PyroThin materials that provide thermal management, mechanical stability, and fire protection properties to EV battery systems.
- Aspen plans to build a 500,000-square-foot advanced manufacturing facility situated on a 90-acre parcel in southeast Georgia.
- The company plans to begin hiring personnel in the second half of 2022 across skilled manufacturing operators, advanced process engineers, quality and maintenance technicians, and professional support roles.
- Billy Allen, Vice Chair of the Development Authority of Bulloch County, welcomed Aspen Aerogels to the Statesboro-Bulloch County community and region.
- The Development Authority highlighted that Georgia Southern University and Ogeechee Technical College will provide critical education resources to help Aspen flourish.
- Assistant Director Alyce Thornhill represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s Global Commerce division on this project in partnership with the Development Authority of Bulloch County, Georgia Quick Start, and Georgia EMC.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson welcomed Aspen Aerogels’ innovative, sustainable solutions for the electric vehicle and energy storage markets to Georgia.
- The GDEcD looks forward to having Georgia employees collaborate with Aspen’s network of world-class scientists, engineers, and additional skilled workforce to solve mobility challenges.
- Southern Gateway Commerce Park is a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) Certified park requiring due diligence and specific certification requirements prior to business visits.
- Aspen Aerogels is a technology leader in sustainability whose aerogel technology enables customers to achieve objectives around resource efficiency, electrification, and sustainability.
- PyroThin® thermal barrier products enable solutions to thermal runaway challenges within the electric vehicle and energy storage markets.
- Aspen’s carbon aerogel program seeks to increase the performance of lithium-ion battery cells to enable EV manufacturers to extend driving range and reduce the cost of electric vehicles.
- Aspen’s Spaceloft® sustainable building materials provide industry-leading energy efficiency and fire safety to building owners.
- Aspen’s Cryogel® and Pyrogel® products are valued by the world's largest energy infrastructure companies.
- Aspen is headquartered in Northborough, MA, and manufactures its products at its East Providence, R.I. facilities.
