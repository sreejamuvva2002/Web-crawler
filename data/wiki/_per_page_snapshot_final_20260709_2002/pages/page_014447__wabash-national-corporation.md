# Wabash National Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-25

**Source:** [Wabash National Corporation - Wabash Expands Southeast Footprint to Strengthen Customer Access and Service](https://ir.onewabash.com/news/news-details/2025/Wabash-Expands-Southeast-Footprint-to-Strengthen-Customer-Access-and-Service/default.aspx)  ·  `page_014447`

## Facts

- Wabash National Corporation announced a significant expansion of its network in the Southeast.
- The company is strengthening customer access and service in the greater Atlanta region through three strategic growth moves.
- One strategic move involves opening a new Parts & Service center in the Atlanta area.
- A second strategic move involves expanding its dealer relationship with Fleetco.
- A third strategic move involves adding CS Truck and Trailer to its Preferred Partner Network.
- Dave Hill serves as vice president of Parts & Services at Wabash.
- The expansions are designed to put parts, service, and support closer to customers operating across Georgia, Alabama, South Carolina, and Tennessee.
- The new Atlanta-area Parts and Services center is strategically located at the heart of the Southeast’s trucking corridor.
- The center provides faster access to parts, service, and truck body upfitting.
- It will offer Wabash’s Ready-to-Mount (RTM) truck bodies for dry freight and platform applications with rapid turnaround times for in-stock chassis.
- The Atlanta site joins existing Wabash service centers in California, Florida, Ohio, Pennsylvania, and Texas.
- Additional service center locations are on the way.
- Doors at the new Atlanta center opened September 18, 2025.
- Fleetco, a long-standing Wabash dealer, will now represent Wabash in the greater Atlanta market with trailer sales.
- This expansion broadens Wabash’s dealer footprint in the Southeast and gives customers additional access to new and used equipment through a trusted partner.
- Bill Tirrill serves as president at Fleetco Inc.
- Fleetco has more than 14 years of experience working closely with Wabash.
- The partnership allows Fleetco to expand its sales force across the region and provide immediate access to new and used trailers.
- Wabash added three CS Truck and Trailer locations to its Preferred Partner Network in Georgia.
- This addition brings expert service, maintenance, and Wabash Genuine Parts to more customers through authorized third-party dealerships.
- The Preferred Partner Network model is central to Wabash’s strategy of making high-quality parts more accessible through a seamless national network.
- Expanding the dealer and partner network is critical to delivering the speed and service customers expect as the customer base grows.
- Wabash combines physical and digital technologies to deliver innovative, end-to-end solutions that optimize supply chains across transportation, logistics, and infrastructure markets.
- The company is headquartered in Lafayette, Indiana.
- Wabash designs, manufactures, and services an extensive range of products supporting first-to-final mile operations, including dry and refrigerated trailers and truck bodies, platform trailers, tank trailers, and structural composites.
- Through the Wabash Marketplace and Wabash Parts, customers gain access to a nationwide parts and service network, Trailers as a Service (TaaS)℠, and advanced tools designed to streamline operations and drive growth.
