## Facts

- Daechang Seat Corp USA is constructing a new manufacturing facility in Savannah, GA.
- The facility will span 148,900 square feet.
- LJB delivered structural engineering services for the project from concept through construction documentation.
- The facility supports Hyundai’s Metaplant America.
- The facility features tilt-up concrete walls.
- The facility features open-web steel joists.
- The facility features slab-on-grade floors.
- The facility features shallow foundations.
- LJB provided designs for a standalone pump house.
- LJB provided designs for truck dock ramps.
- LJB coordinated closely with civil, MEP, and architectural teams.
- All design documents were developed in Revit.
- All design documents were aligned with Georgia building codes.
- The engineering firm reviewed key construction submittals.
- The engineering firm participated in weekly remote project meetings.
- The structure is designed with room for future expansion built into it.
- Evans General Contractors served as the client for the project.
