# Georgia Manufacturing Alliance

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2017-05-25

**Source:** [Georgia Manufacturing Alliance tours high-tech manufacturing facilities across the state](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/shopmanagement/georgia-manufacturing-alliance-tours-high-tech-manufacturing-facilities-across-the-state)  ·  `page_012085`

## Facts

- The Georgia Manufacturing Alliance is conducting five plant tours throughout the state in June 2017.
- A Peening Technologies plant tour was scheduled for the morning of June 7 in Austell.
- Attendees will see the latest technology in shot peening, abrasive blasting and surface enhancement services to the aerospace, automotive, power generation, medical, and oil and gas exploration industries.
- A Kia Motors plant tour was scheduled for the morning and afternoon of June 13 in West Point.
- Due to high demand this tour is open to Georgia Manufacturing Alliance members only.
- Tour attendees will see the Kia Sorento® and Kia Optima® produced from rolled metal all the way to the finished product using high-tech stamping, weld shop robotics, and assembly processes.
- A KRAIBURG plant tour was scheduled for the morning of June 15 in Buford.
- Kraiburg TPE is a global manufacturer of themoplastic elastomers and is a pioneer in TPE compounds used in the automotive, industrial, consumer, and strictly regulated medical sectors.
- Its production lines are processed by injection molding or extrusion.
- The Georgia Manufacturing Alliance (GMA) is a membership-based industry organization founded in 2008 to support Georgia’s manufacturing community.
