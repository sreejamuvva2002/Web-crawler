## Facts

- F&P Georgia is a tier one automotive parts supplier located in Rome, Georgia, USA.
- F&P Georgia has between 201 and 500 employees.
- The parent company of F&P Georgia is Ftech Inc., with its head office located in Saitama Prefecture, Japan.
- In North America, F&P Georgia operates as one of five subsidiaries.
- Two other North American subsidiaries are located in Tottenham and Stratford, Ontario, Canada.
- One other North American subsidiary is located in Troy, Ohio, USA.
- Another facility is located in Santa Rosa Jauregui, Queretaro, Mexico.
- F&P Georgia operates over 20 facilities throughout the globe.
