## Facts

- The annual Tax Expenditure Report provides a comprehensive listing of the state's statuary provisions that promote quality of life and economic growth through beneficial tax incentives.
- Tax expenditures represent an allocation of government resources that could have been collected and appropriated if not for this preferential tax treatment.
- The most current report available is the FY 2027 Tax Expenditure Report.
- Historical reports are also available on the page.
