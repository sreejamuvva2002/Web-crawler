## Facts

- Superior Essex offers cable and wire technology.
- The company provides in-house electrical product distribution for the automotive, commercial, residential, industrial, energy, and communications markets.
- Superior Essex was founded in 1954.
- Superior Essex is based in Atlanta, Georgia.
- Superior Essex headquarters is located at 5770 Powers Ferry Road NW Suite 400, Atlanta, Georgia, 30327, United States.
- Superior Essex has acquired a total of 2 companies.
- The latest acquisition by Superior Essex was Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire on March 25, 2024.
- Superior Essex has 10 strategic partners and customers.
- Superior Essex partnered with Legrand on February 2, 2023.
- On February 6, 2023, Legrand and Superior Essex teamed up at the BICSI Exhibition to launch the nCompass Systems Extended Distance cabling system.
- On May 11, 2022, Superior Essex announced an expanded partnership with Waybridge, a supply chain operating system for raw materials.
- On September 28, 2021, MHT and Superior Essex announced a Smart Building Technology Partnership.
- Akram Khalis, MHT's CTO, stated they are excited to announce their partnership with Superior Essex.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Furukawa, Superior Essex, and Essex Brownell.
- Justin Deedy serves as the current Chief Executive Officer of Superior Essex.
- In 2018, Superior Essex reduced the outside diameter of its 10Gain XP Category 6A cable to 0.25 inches.
- Amir Sekhavat, Superior Essex's director of global product management for premises copper products, commented that the 10Gain XP cable offers significant size advantages in tight spaces and less environmental impact without compromising on performance or safety.
- Superior Essex references bend radius dozens of times in its "Premises cable installation guideline" document.
