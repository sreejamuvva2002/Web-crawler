# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2006-12-15

**Source:** [Pirelli Gets Q1 Status from Ford - Tire Review Magazine](https://www.tirereview.com/pirelli-gets-q1-status-from-ford/)  ·  `page_048435`

## Facts

- The plant, featuring Pirelli’s MIRS production system, supplies OE tires for various Ford vehicles.
- Francesco Gori, managing director of Pirelli Tire, stated the Q1 award is a prestigious indication of the company's rapidly developing capability in the U.S.
- Pirelli Tire North America is focused on selective growth in both the replacement and original equipment markets in the U.S., with Ford being very much part of that strategy.
- Q1 Accreditation is a set of quality and manufacturing guidelines developed by Ford for its suppliers, which Pirelli met or exceeded each criteria for.
