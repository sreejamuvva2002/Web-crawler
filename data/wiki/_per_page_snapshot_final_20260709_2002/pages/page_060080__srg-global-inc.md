# SRG Global®, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Merritt Gaunt Named President of SRG Global®, Inc. | glassonweb.com](https://www.glassonweb.com/news/merritt-gaunt-named-president-srg-globalr-inc)  ·  `page_060080`

## Facts

- SRG Global is one of the world’s largest manufacturers of chrome-plated plastic parts for the automotive and commercial truck industries.
- Merritt Gaunt was named president of SRG Global, directing the company’s global operations to advance its vision, culture, and strategy for long-term sustainable growth.
- Gaunt takes over for Dave Prater, who announced his retirement earlier in 2019.
- Prior to this appointment, Gaunt served as vice president of Africa, Middle East, India and Asia (AMEIA) for Guardian Glass, a Guardian company.
- Gaunt joined Guardian Industries in 2013 as managing director of Corporate Development.
- He was later named vice president of Operations for Guardian Glass before assuming the AMEIA role.
- Ron Vaupel, president and CEO of Guardian Industries, stated that Gaunt will apply Market-Based Management® to achieve superior results for the company, its customers, employees, and strategic partners.
- Vaupel noted that this is a time of change and opportunity for SRG Global and the automotive industry.
- Gaunt has more than 16 years of operations, finance, marketing, and business development experience.
- He previously held various positions at Koch Industries in the Koch Equity Development Group.
- In 2006, Gaunt joined the strategy and business development group of Georgia-Pacific.
- In 2007, he was named vice president of Finance and Business Development for Georgia-Pacific’s European operations.
- In 2010, he took responsibility for Georgia-Pacific’s consumer brand paper towel business.
- He was subsequently named vice president and general manager for the skincare category and new category development at Georgia-Pacific before joining Guardian.
- Both Guardian Industries and Georgia-Pacific are wholly owned subsidiaries of Koch Industries.
- Gaunt earned Bachelor of Science degrees in entrepreneurship and accounting from Wichita State University, with minors in physics and French.
- SRG Global is headquartered in Troy, Michigan, USA.
- The company operates a Research and Development Center in Taylor, Michigan, USA.
- SRG Global maintains a manufacturing presence in major world regions including North America, Western and Central Europe, and Asia.
- The company engineers solutions for greater surface durability, structural integrity, enhanced functionality, vehicle efficiency, and design flexibility.
- SRG Global develops innovative exterior and interior products and processes for the automotive and commercial truck industries.
