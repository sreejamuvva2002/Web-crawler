# DENSO Manufacturing Michigan Iowa Plant (DMIA)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2013-05-09

**Source:** [DENSO Manufacturing Michigan Iowa Plant Celebrates Opening Of Urbandale Facility](https://www.counterman.com/denso-manufacturing-michigan-iowa-plant-celebrates-opening-of-urbandale-facility/)  ·  `page_053792`

## Facts

- DENSO celebrated the opening of its new thermal assembly plant and warehouse facility in Urbandale, Iowa.
- The new facility is named DENSO Manufacturing Michigan Iowa Plant (DMIA).
- The facility represents a $500,000 investment.
- The facility created seven new jobs.
- The company expects to expand the workforce to 20 employees within two years.
- The company expects to nearly double its production within two years.
- The Urbandale facility assembles cooling modules for the agricultural and construction industries.
- The plant handles pass-through products, including radiator assemblies and cooling modules for the construction, agriculture, and electric power industries.
- The plant is localizing products that were previously assembled in the United Kingdom for U.S. customers.
- This opening is part of DENSO’s recent expansion initiative to invest nearly $2 billion in North America.
- More than $750 million of DENSO’s expansion investment is allocated to the United States.
- The expansion aims to strengthen and expand DENSO’s product development and manufacturing capabilities across the region.
- DENSO executives and local city officials celebrated DMIA’s grand opening with a ribbon cutting ceremony.
- Urbandale Mayor Bob Andeweg attended the celebration.
- DENSO International America President and CEO Howard Sugi attended the celebration.
- DENSO Manufacturing Michigan President John Kumagai attended the celebration.
- DENSO Marston President David Hanson from the U.K. attended the celebration.
- DMIA Plant Manager Paolo Vercelli attended the celebration.
- Howard Sugi stated that while the facility extends existing thermal operations in Michigan and the U.K., it is very much an Iowa facility.
- DENSO has been operating in Iowa since 1974, when it opened its first facility in Cedar Falls to support heavy-duty, off-highway engine management and others.
- Howard Sugi expressed excitement about opportunities for DENSO’s heavy-duty business in Iowa in the years ahead.
- Paolo Vercelli credited the Greater Des Moines Partnership, the city of Urbandale, and the Urbandale Development Association for their strong support.
- Vercelli also thanked the Urbandale Chamber of Commerce for its warm welcome and assistance.
- DENSO’s motto is “Protecting lives, preserving the planet and preparing a bright future for generations to come.”
