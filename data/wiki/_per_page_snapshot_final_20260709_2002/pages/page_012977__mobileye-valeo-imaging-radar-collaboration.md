# Mobileye-Valeo Imaging Radar Collaboration

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-09-04

**Source:** [Mobileye and Valeo Launch Partnership for World-Class Imaging Radars | Mobileye News](https://www.mobileye.com/news/mobileye-and-valeo-launch-partnership-for-world-class-imaging-radars/)  ·  `page_012977`

## Facts

- Mobileye and Valeo announced a new partnership to deliver software-defined, best-in-class imaging radars for next-generation driver assist and automated driving features.
- The partnership was announced at IAA Mobility 2023 in Munich on September 4, 2023.
- Imaging radar will serve as an enabling element for more advanced hands-off ADAS solutions and eyes-off automated driving features on highways and urban streets.
- Nimrod Nehushtan, Mobileye’s Executive Vice President of Business Development and Strategy, stated the collaboration significantly advances a new phase in automotive safety and performance.
- Marc Vrecko, President of Valeo’s Comfort and Driving Assistance Systems Business Group, said the partnership reinforces the strong relationship between Valeo and Mobileye and illustrates Valeo’s technological leadership in ADAS.
- Mobileye’s imaging radars utilize advanced architecture including Massive MIMO antenna design, high-end radio frequency design developed in-house, and high-fidelity sampling.
- The imaging radar delivers a detailed, four-dimensional image of surroundings up to 300 meters away and beyond.
- The radar features a 140-degree field-of-view at medium range and a 170-degree field-of-view in close range.
- Automakers have shown high market interest for Mobileye’s Imaging radar as they look to expand the operational design domains of their automated driving features.
- Valeo, a world leader in Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS), has been developing and mass-producing radar technologies since 2006.
- Valeo will lead the system design of the new imaging radar product by integrating Mobileye’s groundbreaking imaging radar technology and corresponding software and algorithms embedded in the Mobileye Radar chipset into Valeo’s automotive software and hardware radar solutions.
- The complete Imaging radar solution will be produced by Valeo leveraging its expertise in producing automotive technologies at scale and its global industrial footprint.
- Since 2015, Mobileye and Valeo have delivered more than 15 million Smart Front Cameras worldwide as part of their successful collaboration on front-facing cameras and other driver assist solutions.
