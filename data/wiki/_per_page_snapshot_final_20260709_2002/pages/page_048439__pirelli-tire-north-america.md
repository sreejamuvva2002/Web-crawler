# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-01-08

**Source:** [The Motor Enclave Tampa Announces Partnership With Pirelli Tire North America](https://www.accessnewswire.com/newsroom/en/automotive/the-motor-enclave-tampa-announces-partnership-with-pirelli-tire-north-america-818527)  ·  `page_048439`

## Facts

- The Motor Enclave announced a sponsorship agreement with Pirelli Tire North America to become "The Official Tire" of the 200-acre experiential motorsports venue.
- The venue is conveniently located at the corner of I-4 and I-75, less than 15 minutes from downtown Tampa.
- Pirelli will receive exposure in key locations throughout the development, most notably its trademark yellow and red branding throughout the newly named "Pirelli Pit Lane".
- All events on the 1.6-mile Hermann Tilke designed performance circuit begin and end at the Pirelli Pit Lane.
- The Pirelli Pit Lane will incorporate a visually stunning "Pirelli Podium" where members and guests can capture photos to commemorate their visit.
- The podium will showcase authentic Pirelli Formula 1 tires.
- Brad Oleshansky, Founder and CEO of The Motor Enclave, stated they are elated to secure Pirelli as one of their premiere sponsors.
- Oleshansky noted that Pirelli is synonymous with innovation and quality, aligning with their mission to build and operate the premiere motorsports venue in the world.
- Oleshansky added that Pirelli's heritage in racing and current partnership with Formula 1 ties in beautifully with their desire to deliver unique experiences to automotive enthusiasts.
- Andre Oosthuizen, Chief Marketing Officer of Pirelli Tire North America, stated they have been tracking the development of The Motor Enclave Tampa since it was announced in 2019.
- Oosthuizen mentioned that after seeing the success of Brad Oleshansky's initial project in Michigan, M1 Concourse, they knew his next iteration would be the perfect platform to highlight Pirelli's full product line.
- Oosthuizen expressed excitement about demonstrating their full product line across the multitude of driving surfaces at The Motor Enclave, including the performance circuit, vehicle dynamics pad, and off-road experience.
- Pirelli was founded in Milan in 1872 and stands as a global brand known for cutting-edge technology, high-end production excellence, and passion for innovation drawing heavily on its Italian roots.
- The company's product range consists of innovative tires for cars, motorcycles, and bicycles due to solid partnerships with the most prestigious manufacturers.
- These collaborations allow Pirelli to develop tailor-made tires depending on the model to satisfy the specific needs of sophisticated consumers.
- Pirelli Tire North America includes a Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) facility and research and development center at its Rome, Georgia headquarters.
- Pirelli Tire North America also includes a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant in Silao, Mexico.
- Pirelli Tire North America maintains sales and marketing offices in New York City, Los Angeles, Detroit, Montreal, and Mexico City.
- The company manufactures, distributes, and markets original equipment and replacement tires for export and domestic car/motorcycle applications.
- The Motor Enclave is the premier developer of experiential motorsports venues in North America.
- The Tampa development includes a 1.6-mile Hermann Tilke designed driving circuit.
- The Tampa development includes a 2-acre vehicle dynamics pad.
- The Tampa development includes a 100-acre off-road experience with miles of purpose-built trails.
- The Tampa development includes a 37,000 square foot corporate event center.
- The Tampa development features the largest Private Garage community in the world with more than 300 privately owned luxury garages.
