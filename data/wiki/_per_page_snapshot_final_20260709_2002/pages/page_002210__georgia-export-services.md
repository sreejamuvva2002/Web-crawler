# Georgia Export Services

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Georgia Export Services | Georgia](https://georgia.org/international/trade)  ·  `page_002210`

## Facts

- The Georgia Export Grant has supported 99 unique Georgia companies, making it possible for them to make international connections.
- Georgia has broken export records for five years in a row and is a top 10 state for exports.
- The trade team connects Georgia businesses with more than 60 international markets to help local entrepreneurs grow.
- The Georgia Export Grant, launched in partnership with the U.S. Small Business Administration, provides reimbursement funding to eligible Georgia exporters for pre-approved export activities.
- This competitive grant program is intended to increase the number of small businesses that export, assist small firms with export development and expand international sales.
- Services provided include market intelligence, trade show assistance, grant funding opportunities, competitive analysis, distributor and buyer identification, and resource connection.
- Upcoming trade shows or missions are open to help participants learn firsthand about a market of interest.
- A monthly newsletter provides the latest international sales opportunities for Georgia exporters.
