# Kia

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Kia will invest more than $200 million at its West Point plant.
- Kia will create nearly 200 new jobs at the facility.
- The investment and hiring are to accommodate assembly of the company's all-electric 2024 EV9 three-row SUV.
- The production line transformation will make the EV9 the fifth model assembled at Kia Georgia.
- The EV9 will join the Telluride, Sorento, and Sportage SUVs, and the K5 mid-size sedan as models assembled at the plant.
- EV9 assembly is expected to begin in the second quarter of 2024.
