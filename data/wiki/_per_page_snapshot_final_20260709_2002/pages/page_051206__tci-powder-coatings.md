# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-05-24

**Source:** [TCI Powder Coatings hires business development manager](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/finishing/tci-powder-coatings-hires-business-development-manager)  ·  `page_051206`

## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is based in Ellaville, Georgia.
- TCI Powder Coatings is a subsidiary of RPM Intl. Inc.
- TCI Powder Coatings has named Rich Chappel as business development manager for the Southwest region.
- The Southwest region covered by the new role includes Texas, Louisiana, Colorado, New Mexico, and Oklahoma.
- Rich Chappel has more than 25 years of experience in powder coatings and the industrial market segment.
- For the past 16 years, Chappel held several positions at Valspar in sales and market segment management.
- Before that he helped launch the Lilly Powder Coatings business.
- He holds a bachelor’s degree in business administration from the University of North Texas.
- The hiring announcement was published on May 24, 2016.
