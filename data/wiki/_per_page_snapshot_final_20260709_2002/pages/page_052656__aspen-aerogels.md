## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels is a mid-sized manufacturing company focused on aerogel-based insulation and thermal barrier products.
- The company has between 201 and 500 employees.
- Its insulation solutions include Cryogel and Pyrogel.
- These insulation solutions are used by energy infrastructure operators and automakers to improve heat management and protect equipment, supporting efficiency and safety in demanding environments.
- Aspen Aerogels is headquartered in Northborough, Massachusetts.
- The company also manufactures at a facility in East Providence, Rhode Island.
- Aspen serves the energy infrastructure sector and automotive manufacturers.
- The company addresses corrosion under insulation and thermal management challenges in electrified systems.
- In April 2026, an explosion occurred at the East Providence site.
- The explosion led to a temporary closure of the East Providence site.
- The East Providence site partially reopened following the explosion.
