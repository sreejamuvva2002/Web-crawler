## Facts

- Honda opened its first U.S. storefront in Los Angeles in 1959.
- Honda operates across 12 U.S. manufacturing plants.
- Associates at these plants build Honda and Acura vehicles, light trucks, powersports products, power equipment, and a light duty jet.
- Honda products are built using U.S. and global parts.
- Honda celebrates 50 years of research and development in America.
- Honda will export the U.S.-built Passport SUV to Japan.
- Honda has an innovative partnership with USA Bobsled/Skeleton, highlighted in a behind-the-scenes documentary.
