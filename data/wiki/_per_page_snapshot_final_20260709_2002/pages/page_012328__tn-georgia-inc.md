# TN GEORGIA, INC.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-01-29

**Source:** [TN GEORGIA, INC. Obtains Nadcap Certification for Non-Destructive Testing in Aerospace Manufacturing | Tsubaki Nakashima](https://tsubaki-nakashima.com/en/tn-georgia-inc-obtains-nadcap-certification-for-non-destructive-testing-in-aerospace-manufacturing/)  ·  `page_012328`

## Facts

- TN GEORGIA, INC., a U.S. subsidiary of Tsubaki Nakashima, obtained Nadcap certification for Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) at its Cumming plant in November 2025.
- The certification applies to NDT processes required in the production of aerospace bearing balls.
- Nadcap is an internationally recognized accreditation program for special processes and products in the global aerospace and defense industry, administered by the U.S.-based nonprofit organization Performance Review Institute (PRI).
- Achieving Nadcap accreditation requires exceptionally stringent quality control and audit standards.
- The Cumming plant had already obtained Nadcap accreditation for Fluorescent Penetrant Inspection and Eddy Current Testing in 2024.
- With the addition of the Surface Temper Etch certification required for aerospace bearing balls, the plant is now able to expand its product offerings to support a wider range of safety-critical aerospace applications.
- This expansion will contribute to minimizing supply chain risk for customers.
- The Aerospace and Defense segment has been identified as one of Tsubaki Nakashima's key growth areas in its Mid-Term Business Plan announced last year.
- The new certificate represents an important step toward enhancing in-house manufacturing capabilities in the future and will further support the global expansion of production for Aerospace and Defense components.
- Tsubaki Nakashima specializes in the manufacturing and sale of precision components for industrial applications, utilizing materials such as ceramics, steels, and plastics to produce more than 20,000 product types.
- Product types include high-precision balls, rollers, large-scale blowers, and contract injection molding.
- The company has over 90 years of expertise serving the automotive, industrial, aerospace, and medical industries.
- Tsubaki Nakashima is listed under the prime market of the Tokyo Stock Exchange (securities code: 6464).
