# Wabash National Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-03-23

**Source:** [Wabash National set to open collaboration space in Purdue’s Discovery Park District - Purdue University News](https://www.purdue.edu/newsroom/archive/releases/2021/Q1/wabash-national-set-to-open-collaboration-space-in-purdues-discovery-park-district.html)  ·  `page_014601`

## Facts

- Wabash National Corp. is expanding its Greater Lafayette presence with a new space in Purdue University’s Discovery Park District.
- The company will open a 2,000-square-foot space in the Convergence Center for Innovation and Collaboration.
- Dave Teeter, senior director of talent and organizational effectiveness for Wabash National, stated, “We are excited to expand our partnership with Purdue by opening this new space to enrich our collaboration with students, faculty and other Discovery Park businesses.”
- Teeter noted that Purdue offers extraordinary talent that will help the company continue to grow in Greater Lafayette.
- The new campus space will be used to engage with the Purdue community on co-sponsored projects, student outreach, career development, research, and innovation.
- Wabash National joins a growing list of businesses opening spaces in Discovery Park District, which also will be home to novel 5G technologies.
- The district is described as a 400-acre launchpad where businesses of all sizes can access a business-minded university, global talent pipelines, and advanced research and development facilities to solve business problems.
- Wabash National specializes in the design and production of engineered solutions for the transportation, logistics, and distribution industries.
- The company designs and manufactures dry freight and refrigerated trailers, platform trailers, tank trailers, dry and refrigerated truck bodies, structural composite panels and products, trailer aerodynamic solutions, and specialty food grade and pharmaceutical equipment.
- Greg Deason, Purdue Research Foundation’s senior vice president for alliances and placemaking, stated that Wabash National joining the district opens new opportunities for impact with students, faculty, and other members of the Purdue innovation ecosystem.
- Wabash National Corporation is publicly traded on the NYSE under the ticker symbol WNC.
- The company’s products are sold under brand names including Wabash National, Benson, Brenner Tank, Bulk Tank International, DuraPlate, Extract Technology, Supreme, Transcraft, Walker Engineered Products, and Walker Transport.
