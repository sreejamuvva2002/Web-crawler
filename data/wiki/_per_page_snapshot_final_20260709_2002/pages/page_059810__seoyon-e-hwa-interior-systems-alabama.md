## Facts

- Seoyon E HWA Interior Systems Alabama will add 60 jobs and $4.2 million to Selma and Dallas County.
- The expansion was announced by Selma-Dallas County Economic Development Authority Executive Director Wayne Vardaman at a press conference on Thursday at the Selma-Dallas County Public Library.
- Seoyon E HWA is a Tier I Hyundai supplier located in Craig Industrial Park.
- The company manufactures several kinds of plastic interior parts for Hyundai Motor Manufacturing Alabama.
- The expansion is in conjunction with HMMA's upcoming production of the updated Santafe MX5A.
- The updated Santafe MX5A will be manufactured later this year at the HMMA Hope Hull Assembly Plant.
- Selma Mayor James Perkins Jr. was in office when he helped the EDA successfully bring Seoyon E HWA to Selma in 2005.
- State Rep. Prince Chestnut noted that Seoyon currently employs 439 employees at the plant.
- Craig Field Airport and Industrial Authority Executive Director Jim Corrigan stated that Seoyon has been bringing jobs to Selma for 18 years.
- Dallas County Commissioner Curtis Williams attended the press conference expressing gratitude for Seoyon's expansion and job creation.
- Wayne Vardaman said, "I've been working with this company for a very long time... They're a very good company and we appreciate them. It took a lot of hard work to get them here."
- James Perkins Jr. said, "We're excited to have their expansion and bringing more jobs to Selma."
- Prince Chestnut said, "We're really appreciative of Seoyon investing in our community... We see the potential for more growth."
- Jim Corrigan said, "It's another good day in Selma... For 18 years, they've been bringing jobs to Selma."
