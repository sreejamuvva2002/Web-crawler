# Seohan Auto Georgia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-04-17

**Source:** [Seohan Auto Georgia to Develop $72M Manufacturing Facility Near Savannah - REBusinessOnline](https://rebusinessonline.com/seohan-auto-georgia-to-develop-72m-manufacturing-facility-near-savannah/)  ·  `page_059350`

## Facts

- Seohan Auto Georgia, a subsidiary of South Korean automobile parts manufacturer Seohan Group, will construct a new facility in Midway, approximately 35 miles outside Savannah.
- Seohan plans to invest $72 million into the development.
- The facility will be situated within the Tradeport East Business Center, a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) site.
- Production at the facility is scheduled to begin in late 2024.
- Alyce Thornhill of the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD), in partnership with the Liberty County Industrial Development Authority, Georgia Ports Authority, Georgia Quick Start and Georgia Power, negotiated the terms of the project.
- Seohan manufactures front and rear axles and drive shafts.
- Seohan is one of many automotive suppliers expanding near Hyundai’s planned $5.5 billion campus in the Savannah region.
