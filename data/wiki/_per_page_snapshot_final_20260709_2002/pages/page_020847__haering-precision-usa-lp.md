# Haering Precision USA LP

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-21

**Source:** [Haering Precision USA LP Named 2024 Impact Business of the Year by Hart County Chamber of Commerce - 92.1 WLHR](https://921wlhr.com/haering-precision-usa-lp-named-2024-impact-business-of-the-year-by-hart-county-chamber-of-commerce/)  ·  `page_020847`

## Facts

- Haering Precision USA LP was recognized as the 2024 Impact Business of the Year by the Hart County Chamber of Commerce.
- The award highlights businesses with over 50 employees that have made a significant economic and community impact through job creation, corporate citizenship, and community engagement.
- Dustin Chambers, Commercial Plant Manager at Haering Precision, stated that receiving the Impact Business of the Year award reflects the team's dedication to making a difference.
- Dustin Chambers noted that Haering believes in investing in people, developing talent, investing in future generations, and supporting initiatives contributing to Hart County's well-being.
- Last fall, Haering participated in National Manufacturing Week by hosting and participating in key events emphasizing manufacturing's connection to education, innovation, and community engagement.
- The Haering plant is located in the Gateway 2 Industrial Park.
- Every year, the Haering plant hosts the Haering Precision Skills Invitational in partnership with North Georgia Technical College.
- The Skills Invitational pairs participants from local high schools with senior precision machining students from North Georgia Tech in a pro-am style contest testing technical skills, creativity, and teamwork.
- The award was presented at the Chamber’s Annual Awards Banquet on February 21, 2025.
- Dwayne Dye, former Director of Economic Development for the Hart County Industrial Building Authority, emphasized Haering’s commitment to offering career opportunities and its role as a driving force in the local economy during the presentation.
- Dwayne Dye stated that the potential for long-term positive impact for local area students, apprentices, workforce, and the Northeast Georgia Region is only the beginning.
- The company remains committed to workforce development, education, and innovation to ensure continued growth and success for employees and the community.
