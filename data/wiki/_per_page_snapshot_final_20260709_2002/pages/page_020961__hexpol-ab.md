# HEXPOL AB

## Facts

- HEXPOL is a world-leading polymers group with strong global positions in advanced polymer compounds (Compounding), gaskets for plate heat exchangers (Gaskets), and plastic and rubber materials for truck and castor wheel applications (Wheels).
- Customers are primarily systems suppliers to the global automotive and engineering industry, construction industry, the energy sector, medical equipment manufacturers and OEM manufacturers of plate heat exchangers and forklifts.
- The Group is organised in two business areas, HEXPOL Compounding and HEXPOL Engineered Products.
- The HEXPOL Group has approximately 5,100 employees in fourteen countries.
- America showed overall a continued strong development during the fourth quarter of 2022.
- Development for HEXPOL Engineered Products was very strong during the fourth quarter of 2022, driven by increased demand for energy saving products.
