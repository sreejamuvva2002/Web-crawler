# Wabash

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Wabash expands southeast footprint - NewsBreak](https://www.newsbreak.com/thetrucker-com-315850146/4265561823360-wabash-expands-southeast-footprint)  ·  `page_014587`

## Facts

- Wabash is announcing a significant expansion of its network in the southeast.
- Dave Hill, vice president of parts and services at Wabash, stated that these moves reflect how Wabash is building an extensive partner ecosystem that puts parts, service and support closer to customers.
- The Atlanta expansions aim to provide faster turnaround, more coverage and better reliability for fleets operating across Georgia, Alabama, South Carolina and Tennessee.
- Wabash is strengthening customer access and service in the greater Atlanta region with three strategic growth moves.
- One move is opening a new Parts & Service center.
- Another move is expanding its dealer relationship with Fleetco.
- The third move is adding CS Truck and Trailer to its Preferred Partner Network.
- The new Atlanta-area Parts and Services center is strategically located at the heart of the Southeast’s trucking corridor.
- The Atlanta site provides faster access to parts, service and truck body upfitting.
- The Atlanta site offers Wabash’s Ready-to-Mount (RTM) truck bodies for dry freight and platform applications with rapid turnaround times for in-stock chassis.
- The Atlanta site joins Wabash service centers in California, Florida, Ohio, Pennsylvania and Texas, with additional locations on the way.
- Fleetco, a long-standing Wabash dealer, will now represent Wabash in the greater Atlanta market with trailer sales.
- This expansion broadens Wabash’s dealer footprint in the Southeast, giving customers additional access to new and used equipment through a trusted partner.
- Bill Tirrill, president of Fleetco, stated they are proud to grow their existing partnership with Wabash in the greater Atlanta market.
- With more than 14 years of experience working closely with Wabash, this expansion allows Fleetco to expand its sales force across the region and provide clients immediate access to new and used trailers.
- Fleetco will improve the expertise and commitment clients expect from the Fleetco/Wabash team to create more opportunities to serve clients.
- Wabash is adding three CS Truck and Trailer locations to its Preferred Partner Network in Georgia.
- This addition brings expert service, maintenance and Wabash Genuine Parts to more customers through authorized third-party dealerships.
- The Preferred Partner Network model is central to Wabash’s strategy of making high-quality parts more accessible through a seamless national network.
- Dave Hill noted that expanding the dealer and partner network is critical to delivering the speed and service customers expect as their customer base grows.
- Atlanta serves as an example of executing this strategy by combining service centers, dealer partners, and the Preferred Partner Network to increase coverage and elevate the customer experience.
