# Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-01-04

**Source:** [Panasonic Automotive Unveils EV Audio System for Electric Vehicles that Offers High Quality, Low Power Listening Experience](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/panasonic-automotive-unveils-ev-audio-system-for-electric-vehicles-that-offers-high-quality-low-power-listening-experience-301713237.html)  ·  `page_029048`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America, a tier one automotive supplier and division of Panasonic Corporation of North America, announced at CES 2023 the launch of a unique modular audio system designed specifically for electric vehicles.
- The company is headquartered in Peachtree City, Georgia, with sales, marketing, and engineering operations located in Farmington Hills, Michigan.
- The patent-pending audio system features a modular and scalable design that utilizes the vehicle's cabin environment for sound calibration.
- It includes a power-saving EV audio option that reconfigures speaker placement to optimize sound and directs audio directly to the ears of the driver and passenger instead of saturating the cabin with traditional door-mounted speakers.
- Andrew Poliak, CTO of Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America, stated that the design lowers the cost of operating an EV by reducing half of the power output found in traditional systems without compromising sound quality.
- The system's modular, space-saving speaker design maps the base audio across the cabin to drastically simplify the number of speakers needed, eliminating bulky door speakers to free up driver reach zone space and reduce vehicular weight.
- Rigorous technical testing showed the application of bass delivery, speaker location, and sound tuning meets or exceeds performance thresholds of larger systems.
- Testing demonstrated an up to 67% reduction in power consumed by the new system design, conserving energy that can be redirected to add up to more miles on the road for the average driver.
- The sustainable design features a simplified bill of materials requiring reduced gauge wiring that weighs less, takes up less space, and relies less on costly metals like copper compared to traditional wiring.
- Panasonic developed this EV audio system to improve vehicle space, weight, power, and cost aspects while supporting its GREEN IMPACT initiative aimed at achieving virtually net-zero CO2 emissions by 2030 and 300 million tons in avoided emissions by 2050.
- A recent AAA consumer survey suggests electric vehicle sales are poised for significant growth, with 30 percent of Millennial buyers planning to choose an EV for their next purchase.
