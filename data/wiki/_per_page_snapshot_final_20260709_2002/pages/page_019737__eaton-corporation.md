# Eaton Corporation

## Facts

- The role of home energy management in the electrification of homes is discussed as part of today's energy transition offering homeowners opportunities to leverage intelligent, connected technologies for sustainable, autonomous, and energy-efficient homes.
- Eaton and industry experts from Ample Electric, Rewiring America, and Tesla will discuss opportunities for whole home electrification to reduce homeowner carbon footprints, keep the power on, and help support the electric grid.
- The discussion will cover hurdles that stand in the way to home electrification and how intelligent technologies help accelerate the transformation.
- Solar-ready meter breakers integrate solar power into residential electrical systems by combining utility and solar PV power in one enclosure.
- These breakers offer cost savings, ease of installation, and increased home market value by allowing future solar additions without panel changes.
- The breakers maximize PV system potential and meet NEC compliance.
- Recent code updates for Arc-Fault Circuit Interrupters (AFCI) require AFCI protection in guest rooms, nursing homes, and sleeping quarters in fire stations and police stations.
- Power management companies are extending AFCI technology beyond dwelling units to ensure safety across various locations.
- Eaton is helping customers transform homes into strategic energy hubs to take advantage of the new power paradigm, better manage existing electrical infrastructure, maximize electrical safety, and prepare for future energy requirements.
- This initiative is called the Home as a Grid strategy, creating flexible energy systems that accelerate decarbonization, boost resilience, reduce energy costs, and exceed the demands of the future.
- The Bussmann series CAD configurator provides access to hundreds of products as fully integrated, intelligent 3D CAD models with technical details and data sheets.
- Eaton will host the IEEE IAS Electrical Safety Workshop #412, 414, 416, 418 in Jacksonville, FL from Mar 3-7.
- Eaton will host the ASHE PDC Summit #1010 in Atlanta, GA from Mar 9-12.
- Eaton will host TechAdvantage 2025 in Atlanta, GA from March 10-12.
- Additional hosted events include DISTRIBUTECH (Mar 25-27 | Dallas, TX), Automation and technical showcase (March 24 | Dallas, TX), AISTech (May 5-8 | Nashville, TN), and IEEE Cement Conference (May 4-8 | Nashville, TN).
