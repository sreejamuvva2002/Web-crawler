## Facts

- Fox Factory Holding Corp. issued this financial report from Duluth, Ga.
- The company designs and manufactures performance-defining ride dynamics products primarily for bicycles, on-road and off-road vehicles and trucks, side-by-side vehicles, all-terrain vehicles, snowmobiles, specialty vehicles and applications, motorcycles, and commercial trucks.
- Fox Factory is a direct supplier to leading powered vehicle original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).
- The company also supplies top bicycle OEMs and their contract manufacturers, and provides aftermarket products to retailers and distributors.
- Net sales for the Powered Vehicles Group (PVG) increased by $13.6 million (12.4%) in the third quarter of fiscal 2023 compared to the prior year.
- PVG net sales growth was primarily driven by strong demand in the original equipment manufacturer (OEM) channel, partially offset by the impact of the United Auto Workers (UAW) strike.
- Net sales for the Aftermarket Applications Group (AAG) increased by $10.3 million (8.2%) in the third quarter of fiscal 2023, primarily due to revenue from the Custom Wheel House subsidiary acquired in March 2023.
- Gross margin expenses included costs associated with retaining a skilled workforce as production slowed due to the UAW strike.
- Full year 2023 sales guidance was adjusted to $1.43 billion to $1.47 billion, primarily due to the impact from the United Auto Workers strike and continued inventory destocking in Specialty Sports Group (SSG) markets.
- Fox Factory reaffirmed its 2025 vision targeting $2 billion in sales and a 25.0% adjusted EBITDA margin.
