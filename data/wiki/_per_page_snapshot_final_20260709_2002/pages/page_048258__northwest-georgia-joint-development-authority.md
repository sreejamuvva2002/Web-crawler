# Northwest Georgia Joint Development Authority

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- The Northwest Georgia Joint Development Authority helps companies evaluate why a move to the “Top of Georgia” is the right one.
- Top of Georgia serves as a nonprofit fundraising arm that enhances the marketing efforts of the publicly funded Northwest Georgia Joint Development Authority.
- Top of Georgia offers private individuals and companies a way to contribute to the economic well being of Northwest Georgia.
- Top of Georgia contracts with the Northwest Georgia Joint Development Authority for marketing initiatives including print materials.
- Top of Georgia funds advertising directed toward site selectors, project managers, and target industry executives.
- Top of Georgia sponsors meetings at which site selectors, project managers, and target industry executives are present.
- Top of Georgia covers attendance at state, regional, national and international trade shows for economic development purposes.
- Top of Georgia facilitates one-on-one visits with site selectors, project managers, and target industry executives who have expressed interest in bringing or expanding their business in Northwest Georgia.
- Ashleigh Givens serves as the Director of Marketing/Project Manager for the Northwest Georgia Joint Development Authority.
