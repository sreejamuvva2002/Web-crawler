## Facts

- Teklas is one of the leading suppliers to prominent car manufacturers.
- Teklas operates 11 plants in 5 different countries.
- Teklas is a leading global supplier of automotive fluid systems.
- Teklas provides Thermal Management Systems.
- Teklas develops production constructions for OEMs, including rubber, plastics, metal solutions, and water injection technology (WIT) to reduce vehicle weight and boost recycling rates.
- Teklas is one of the largest manufacturers of connectors in Europe.
- Teklas constantly develops innovative hose structures to follow emerging automotive technologies and adapt to changing regulations.
- Teklas has more than 50 years of experience in rubber compound and high pressure hose production.
- Teklas develops air conditioning hoses with considerably low permeability values (<0.3 g/m*day).
- Teklas develops rubber hoses and metal tubes resistant to fluids and refrigerants for customers at given temperatures.
- Teklas leads the future of the automotive industry through its R&D centers staffed by competent engineers and experts.
- Teklas's R&D team develops ideas based on specific automotive industry needs, enabling flawless production after validation.
- Teklas develops and deploys Robotic Automation Process at scale in its production process.
- Teklas's vision includes an automation-based approach in manufacturing to achieve a sustainable future and business.
