## Facts

- Ozburn Electrical is a family-owned electrical contracting company founded by Harold T. Ozburn in 1984.
- The company provides commercial and industrial electrical services across Social Circle, Georgia; Monroe, Georgia; Walton County, GA; and metro Atlanta.
- Ozburn Electrical offers EV Charging Stations, including Level 2 EV Chargers and Fast DC EV Charging, for industrial, commercial, manufacturing, retail, apartments, and office spaces in Social Circle, GA and Walton County.
- The company provides Electric Vehicle (EV) Charger Installation services, specifically covering Level 2 and Level 3 Fast DC Charging.
- Ozburn Electrical performs electrical work for assembly line and manufacturing operations.
- The firm provides automation and PLC work tailored for manufacturing facilities.
- All electrical installations and upgrades are performed in compliance with the National Electrical Code (NEC), State, County, Municipality, and Electrical Power Provider Guidelines.
- Electricians complete a 2-year or 4-year electrical apprenticeship program established by the Independent Electrical Contractors Association (IEC).
- The company works directly with facility managers to ensure EV charger and industrial electrical projects are completed correctly and within budget.
