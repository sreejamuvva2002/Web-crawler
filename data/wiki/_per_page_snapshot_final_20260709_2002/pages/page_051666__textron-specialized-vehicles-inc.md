## Facts

- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a Textron Inc. company, is based in Augusta, GA.
- The company announced the transition of its Bad Boy Off Road brand to Textron Off Road.
- Side-by-side models in the current line remain the same during the brand transition.
- Vehicles with the new Textron Off Road branding will begin to appear in authorized dealerships by April 2017.
- The Textron Off Road product line features gasoline-powered Stampede and Stampede XTR side-by-sides.
- The Textron Off Road product line includes all-electric Recoil, Recoil iS, and Recoil iS Crew vehicles.
- The Textron Off Road product line includes the hybrid Ambush iS vehicle.
- Textron Off Road pioneered electrically powered 4x4 vehicles for hunting and outdoor recreation due to their silent, odorless operation and emissions-free drivetrains.
- John Collins, vice president of consumer for Textron Specialized Vehicles, stated the brand change reflects detailed design, precision engineering, manufacturing expertise, and high performance.
- Changes to the company's website and social media platforms are complete and online.
- Textron Off Road is a brand of Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT) company.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. is a leading global manufacturer of golf cars, utility and personal transportation vehicles, snowmobiles, side-by-sides, all-terrain vehicles, professional turf-care equipment, and ground support equipment.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles markets products under the E-Z-GO, Cushman, Arctic Cat, Textron Off Road, Jacobsen, Dixie Chopper, Ransomes, TUG, Douglas, Premier, and Safeaero brands.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company that leverages its global network of aircraft, defense, industrial, and finance businesses to provide customers with innovative solutions and services.
