# Norican Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-04-08

**Source:** [Norican Group Americas Expands LaGrange, Georgia Technology Hub – Shot Peener Magazine](https://theshotpeenermagazine.com/norican-group-americas-expands-lagrange-georgia-technology-hub/)  ·  `page_014877`

## Facts

- Norican Group is expanding operations at its LaGrange, Georgia Technology Hub.
- The facility has been operated as the Wheelabrator Group Americas headquarters since 1987 and offers manufacturing, inventory, and logistics capabilities.
- The planned expansion will move all Norican North America business operations into the LaGrange technology hub.
- Consolidated brands include Wheelabrator, DISA, StrikoWestofen, and Italpresse Gauss.
- The company will ramp up current resources to meet commercial operations and administration requirements for all lines of business.
- Inside sales and customer support activities will join centralized inventory and logistics in LaGrange.
- Current operations of DISA in Oswego, Illinois will be transferred to LaGrange as part of the expansion.
- Current operations of StrikoWestofen and Italpresse Gauss in Holland, Michigan will be transferred to LaGrange as part of the expansion.
- Mike Lewis, Vice President of DISA & Aluminum Products, stated the move aims to improve speed to serve customers and optimize order fulfillment with all capabilities in a single location.
- Norican will complete these expansions in the 2nd quarter of 2020.
- Norican Group is home to four globally operating brands: Wheelabrator, DISA, StrikoWestofen, and Italpresse Gauss.
- The company provides end-to-end grey iron foundry equipment, integrated light alloy castings solutions, high-end furnace technology, and surface preparation technology and services.
- The LaGrange facility is located at 1606 Executive Drive, LaGrange, Georgia 30240 USA.
