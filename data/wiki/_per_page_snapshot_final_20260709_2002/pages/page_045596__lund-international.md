## Facts

- Lund International operates in the truck and automotive accessories sector.
- The company was founded in 1965 by Allan W. Lund, who previously worked as a teacher and insurance salesman.
- Before entering the truck accessory market, Lund designed fiberglass prototypes for recreational vehicles.
- In 1974, Lund began producing the Lund SunVisor from a chicken barn in Minnesota.
- The Lund SunVisor attaches to the cab of a truck or van.
- As light truck sales grew in the late 1970s, Lund expanded its offerings to include a variety of appearance accessories.
- In 1997, investment firm Harvest Partners bought out Allan Lund and prompted the acquisition of Deflecta-Shield Corporation.
- The merger with Deflecta-Shield positioned the combined entity as a major player in the truck accessories market.
- Lund purchased Ventshade Company in 1998.
- Lund acquired Smittybilt, Incorporated in 1999.
- Over the years, Lund made numerous acquisitions, including AMP Research in 2013, which significantly broadened its product lines.
- The company underwent several ownership changes, including a period under Highlander Partners.
- In May 2019, Truck Hero, a major player in the truck and Jeep accessory market, announced its strategic acquisition of Lund International.
- The merger with Truck Hero created one of the largest automotive accessory providers in the United States.
