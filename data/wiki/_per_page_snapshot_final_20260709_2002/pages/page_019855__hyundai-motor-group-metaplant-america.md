# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-03-28

**Source:** [Hyundai, Bulloch plants set massive job fair - Statesboro Herald](https://www.statesboroherald.com/local/hyundai-bulloch-plants-set-massive-job-fair/)  ·  `page_019855`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America is holding a job fair in Savannah.
- Recruiters for three plants currently under construction in Bulloch County that will supply parts to the Hyundai plant will attend the job fair.
- The job fair is scheduled for 10 a.m.-4 p.m. on Thursday, April 4.
- The event will be held at the Eckburg Auditorium of Savannah Technical College on White Bluff Road.
- WorkSource Georgia is partnering with the Metaplant as a sponsor for the job fair.
- Approximately 3,500 jobs will be available between the Hyundai EV plant and its local suppliers through 2024.
- Career opportunities at the event will include roles in manufacturing, logistics, technology, and administration.
- Ecoplastic America Corporation, a supplier of injection-molded plastic automotive body parts to Hyundai Motor Group, is building a $205-million plant off Highway 301 South.
- The Ecoplastic America plant will create 456 jobs phased in over eight years.
- Ajin USA, a supplier of metal auto body parts to Hyundai, is investing $317 million.
- Ajin USA will eventually employ 630 people at its Joon Georgia plant on 83 acres in the Bruce Yawn Commerce Park off Interstate 16.
- Hanon Systems, a manufacturer of vehicle air conditioning systems, will build a plant in Statesboro’s Gateway Regional Industrial Park off Highway 301 South.
- Hanon Systems' $40 million investment is projected to create 160 jobs.
- Recruiters from DSC Savannah will attend the job fair.
- Recruiters from Glovis EV Logistics America will attend the job fair.
- Recruiters from Hyundai Materials will attend the job fair.
- Recruiters from Hyundai Steel Georgia will attend the job fair.
- Recruiters from Hyundai Transys GA will attend the job fair.
- Recruiters from Kyungshin America will attend the job fair.
- Recruiters from Mobis Alabama, Savannah Plant will attend the job fair.
- Recruiters from Mobis North America Electrified Powertrain will attend the job fair.
- Recruiters from PHA Georgia will attend the job fair.
- Recruiters from Seohan Auto Georgia will attend the job fair.
- Recruiters from Seoyon E-Hwa will attend the job fair.
- Recruiters from Sewon America will attend the job fair.
- The job fair is a free event open to candidates of all ages and experience levels.
- Attendees are encouraged to come dressed for success and bring résumés.
- Job seekers must create an account and résumé on the WorkSource Georgia portal to attend.
- A WorkSource Georgia portal account and résumé will expedite the admission process.
