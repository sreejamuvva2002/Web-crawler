# Constellium N.V.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-11-30

**Source:** [Constellium to build automotive structure manufacturing facility in Georgia](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/bending/constellium-to-build-automotive-structure-manufacturing-facility-in-georgia)  ·  `page_018518`

## Facts

- Amsterdam-based Constellium N.V. announced plans to construct a new facility in Bartow County, Ga., for the manufacture of aluminum automotive structures.
- Construction of the plant will begin in early 2016.
- The project involves a $20 million investment by Constellium and a $12 million investment by developer Seefried Properties.
- Start of production is anticipated in 2017.
- The planned facility will be an 84,000-sq.-ft. greenfield site.
- The facility may be expanded up to 220,000 sq. ft. to adapt to customers' supply needs in the future.
- Constellium expects to create about 150 high-tech manufacturing jobs at this location by 2019.
