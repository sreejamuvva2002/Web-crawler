# Mines and Metals Capacity Expansion – Piloting By-Product Critical Minerals and Materials Recovery at Domestic Industrial Facilities

## Facts

- The page is a Notice of Intent (NOI) to issue Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (IIJA) Notice of Funding Opportunity (NOFO) No. DE-FOA-0003583.
- The planned NOFO is titled 'Mines and Metals Capacity Expansion – Piloting By-Product Critical Minerals and Materials Recovery at Domestic Industrial Facilities.'
- The goal of the NOFO is to facilitate the development of large pilot-scale facilities to recover domestic critical minerals and materials (CMM).
- Targeted materials include rare earth elements (REE), graphite, and other value-added products.
- Recovery will occur from operating industrial facilities.
- Example industries with byproduct production potential include phosphate, fertilizer, oil and gas, smelting, mining, and waste management.
- Goal-related resources will be eligible in this program.
- The NOI serves as a disclaimer that DOE may issue a NOFO significantly different than described, or may not issue a NOFO at all.
- Any information contained within the NOI is subject to change.
- No concept papers, letters of intent, or full applications are being requested nor accepted in response to this NOI.
- The Department is not seeking comments on the notice and will not respond to questions concerning it.
- Upon release of a NOFO, an avenue for potential applicants to submit questions will be provided.
