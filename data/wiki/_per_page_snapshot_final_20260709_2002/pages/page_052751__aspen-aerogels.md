## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels plans to invest $325,000,000.00 to build out 500,000 square feet of new space in Statesboro, Georgia.
- The company plans to occupy the new space in Statesboro on or about October 1, 2024.
- Aspen Aerogels is a technology leader in sustainability according to its website.
- The company's Aerogel Technology Platform enables customers and partners to achieve their own objectives around the global megatrends of resource efficiency, electrification, and sustainability.
- Aspen Aerogels' strategy is to partner with world-class industry leaders to leverage its patented aerogel technology into additional high-value markets.
- The company is headquartered in Northborough, Massachusetts.
- Aspen Aerogels manufactures its products at its East Providence, Rhode Island facilities.
- PyroThin thermal barrier products enable solutions to thermal runaway challenges within the EV and energy storage markets.
- The Carbon aerogel program seeks to increase performance of lithium-ion battery cells to enable EV manufacturers to extend driving range and reduce cost of EVs.
- Spaceloft sustainable building materials provide industry-leading energy efficiency and fire safety to building owners.
- Cryogel and Pyrogel are valued by the world's largest energy infrastructure companies.
- Don Young serves as the Chief Executive Officer of Aspen Aerogels.
