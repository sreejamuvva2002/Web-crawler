# Boring Bros.

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Dalton, GA, Whitfield County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Boring Bros. provides professional directional drilling services for underground utility installation in Dalton, Georgia, serving residential, commercial, and municipal projects with trenchless technology that reduces surface disruption and protects existing infrastructure.
