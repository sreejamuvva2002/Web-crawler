# Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2020-12-14

**Source:** [Panasonic Automotive Launches New Podcast Series on The Future of Audio and Sound Innovation](https://www.theautochannel.com/news/2020/12/14/924342-panasonic-automotive-launches-new-podcast-series-future-audio-and-sound.html)  ·  `page_047954`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America will release the first episode of The Panasonic Sound Experience, a new four-episode podcast that reveals the emerging innovations of immersive sound design in vehicles, and what it takes to build the perfect audio system.
- Andrew Poliak, CTO of Panasonic Automotive Systems, stated that Panasonic has a long history of developing cutting-edge technology to create a premium in-car audio experience that makes driving both safer and more enjoyable for consumers.
- Panasonic provides fully-integrated solutions revolutionizing the driving experience from navigation and head-up display, to infotainment systems and e-cockpit design.
- Panasonic partners with ELS, Fender and Klipsch to deliver next-gen audio that delivers the best in-vehicle experience possible.
- The podcast is produced by Muddhouse Media, a start-up original content podcast network that brings creative minds, intellectual experts and gifted storytellers together.
- The first episode of The Panasonic Sound Experience focuses on capturing authenticity, artistry and emotion in automotive sound design and features interviews with Panasonic Automotive Executive Vice President Allen Kudla, Grammy Award winning producer and sound engineer Elliot Scheiner and recording producer Matthew Scheiner.
- Allen Kudla stated the goal is to share behind-the-scenes innovation in a podcast series to highlight the technology enabling Panasonic to deliver an in-vehicle sound that's been compared to what you'd hear at concerts by today's greatest artists.
- Future episodes of the podcast will cover how engineers create an intuitive audio environment through noise cancellation, voice enhancement, voice scrubbing, and voice recognition.
- Future episodes will discuss how vehicles have become an essential second space for consumers during the pandemic and why premium audio can elevate the dimensional sound experience with Mark Casavant, Vice President of Sales and Business Development Klipsch Group, and Brett Crockett, Vice President R&D with Dolby Atmos.
- A future holiday master class will chart the pursuit for perfect sound across multiple genres of music from rock to classical with a special iconic audio guest.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America is a division company of Panasonic Corporation of North America and is a leading global supplier of automotive infotainment and connectivity system solutions.
- Panasonic Automotive is headquartered in Peachtree City, Georgia, with sales, marketing and engineering operations in Farmington Hills, Michigan.
