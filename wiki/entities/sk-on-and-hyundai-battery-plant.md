# SK On & Hyundai Battery Plant

**Type:** Joint Venture  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Bartow County, Georgia, Bartow County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

South Korean conglomerates SK Innovation and Hyundai Motor Group plan a sprawling electric vehicle battery plant in Bartow County, northwest of Atlanta. The joint venture between SK On and Hyundai is expected to create more than 3,500 jobs and will be the third-largest corporate investment in Georgia’s history.
