# DAS Corp

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [DAS Corp Chooses Right to Work Georgia Location | NILRR](https://nilrr.org/das-corp-chooses-right-to-work-georgia-location/)  ·  `page_019134`

## Facts

- DAS Corp is investing $35 million in order to establish a new manufacturing facility in Right to Work Metter, Georgia.
- The company will create 300 new jobs.
- A strategic location near prominent cities like Savannah and Macon, facilitating convenient access, played a pivotal role in the decision-making process.
- DAS Corp deemed Metter an ideal location for future expansion prospects in anticipation of its role as a global electric vehicle production hub thanks to close proximity to Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.
- Georgia is well-positioned for the arrival of Hyundai’s supplier network as the result of years of consistent investment in site preparation, workforce solutions, and connectivity.
- DAS Corp is considered another exciting addition to Georgia’s automotive community, with congratulations extended to the company along with partners in the City of Metter and Candler County.
- The Georgia Ports Authority is proud to do its part to support the growing automotive manufacturing presence in the state as it works together to drive economic activity and jobs.
