## Facts

- Peterson Spring, also known as Peterson American Corporation (PAC), has launched a new master distribution program with Signature Engineered Solutions (SES).
- The partnership aims to service and support industrial customers while allowing Peterson to remain focused on leading research and technologies to deliver world-class metal engineered components for the industrial, automotive, and motorsports industries.
- Steven Crain, CEO of Peterson American Corporation, stated that as a 105-year-old company, they chose SES as their master stocking distributor to simplify and improve service and support for industrial customers.
- Peterson’s research and development utilizes advanced engineering expertise, racing technology, and validation laboratories to develop new products for global customers that reduce assembly time, cost, and weight.
- Steven Crain noted that the company's experience and technical know-how have earned credibility in the industry, with customers and suppliers relying on them to solve problems and identify opportunities for improvement.
- Peterson Spring is owned by MiddleGround Capital and is one of the largest privately owned spring making groups in North America.
- The company specializes in designing, prototyping, testing, and manufacturing springs, rings, spring steel stampings, and wire forms for various markets.
- PAC currently operates 12 manufacturing and distribution facilities across four countries: the United States, Canada, Mexico, and the United Kingdom.
- Peterson supplies over 450 customers in 48 different countries.
- Signature Engineered Solutions provides lean product assembly solutions for original equipment manufacturers and related supply bases for all spring and fastener requirements from concept to production.
