## Facts

- Georgia is home to some of SK’s most significant investments, including manufacturing sites for advanced batteries that are part of U.S. “battery belt.”
- Absolics, a subsidiary of SKC, manufactures a glass substrate for semiconductor circuit boards that can significantly increase performance and energy efficiency of chipsets.
- The Absolics facility is located at 1 SKC Drive, Covington, GA 30014.
- SK Battery America is the first U.S. battery manufacturing site of SK On.
- SK Battery America currently operates two EV battery plants in Commerce, Georgia.
- The SK Battery America facility is located at 1760 SK Boulevard, Commerce, GA 30529.
- SK Inc. C&C is an advanced IT company providing local support in Georgia for the SK Battery America EV battery site.
- The SK Inc. C&C facility is located at 2915 Premiere Parkway, Duluth, GA 30097.
- Hyundai Motor Group and SK On formed a joint venture to establish an EV battery cell plant in Bartow County, Georgia.
- The joint venture plant is expected to be operational by the end of 2025.
- The joint venture plant location is Cartersville, GA.
- SK Tes, a subsidiary of SK Ecoplant, is an electronic waste management company and global leader in e-waste recycling, IT asset disposition, and battery recycling.
- SK Tes has locations at 1100 Mary Crest Rd, Suite A, Henderson, NV 89074; 9601 Cosner Dr, Fredericksburg, VA 22408; 5238 Royal Woods Pkwy, Suite 110, Tucker, GA 30084; and 1208 Andover Park E, Tukwila, WA 98188.
