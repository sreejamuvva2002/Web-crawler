## Facts

- SungEel HiTech plans to invest $37,000,000.00 to build out new space in Toccoa, Georgia.
- The company plans to occupy the new space at Hayestone Brady Business Park in Toccoa on or about March 1, 2024.
- SungEel HiTech describes itself as a global leader in lithium-ion battery recycling and a battery material re-supplier.
- Since its establishment in 2000, SungEel HiTech has focused on closed-loop systems in the lithium-ion battery industry.
- The company extracts valuable materials from end-of-life or spent batteries and their scraps by segregating and refining them to supply to battery material manufacturers and other customers.
- All chemical products produced by SungEel HiTech meet battery grade quality standards.
- SungEel HiTech is focusing on recycling valuable materials from spent electric vehicle batteries in a rapidly growing market.
- Yi Kang-Myung serves as the Chief Executive Officer of SungEel HiTech.
