# Fox Factory

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-12-16

**Source:** [Fox Factory Corporate Relocation - DCCM](https://dccm.com/portfolio/fox-factory-corporate-relocation/)  ·  `page_020209`

## Facts

- Fox Factory is a premier designer and manufacturer of high-performance suspension systems.
- Fox Factory relocated its headquarters from California to Georgia.
- DCCM was selected to support the corporate relocation transition from the ground up.
- DCCM provided strategic site selection and civil engineering services to help identify and evaluate multiple locations before securing a 23-acre site for the company’s new home.
- The resulting campus spans 350,000 square feet and is vertically integrated.
- The facility houses manufacturing, distribution, and office operations for more than 800 employees.
- DCCM worked in collaboration with Carroll Daniel Construction Co. as part of a design-build team.
- DCCM delivered civil site services that contributed to a 16-month, on-budget completion.
- The total project cost was $47 million.
