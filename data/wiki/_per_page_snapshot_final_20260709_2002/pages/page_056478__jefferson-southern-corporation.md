# Jefferson Southern Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-01

**Source:** [Man Pinned by Machine at Jefferson Southern in Rockmart - Coosa Valley News](https://coosavalleynews.com/2016/01/man-pinned-by-machine-at-jefferson-southern-in-rockmart/)  ·  `page_056478`

## Facts

- An employee at Jefferson Southern in Rockmart was airlifted to an Atlanta hospital after he was found pinned by a machine.
- Officials were dispatched to the autoparts maker just before 9am Wednesday.
- Chief Todd Queen from the Rockmart Fire Department said that when they arrived the patient had already been removed from the machine.
- Jefferson Southern has not made a comment and no word on the medical condition of the patient.
- Jefferson Southern Corporation, or JSC, is a Tier One automotive supplier.
- The company produces and sells parts that are ready to be installed in automobiles.
- JSC's parts are stamped out of steel and then welded.
- JSC produces several parts that are part of the “white body” or interior shell of the car.
- These parts are not very visible after the vehicle is completed, but they are vital to the strength, safety and performance of the car.
