# Woodbridge Foam Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-07-10

**Source:** [Canada’s Woodbridge Foam Investing $100 Million On Upgrades, Research Centre In Ontario - Canadian Plastics](https://www.canplastics.com/automotive/canadas-woodbridge-foam-investing-100-million-on-upgrades-research-center-in-ontario/1003450355/)  ·  `page_015207`

## Facts

- Woodbridge Foam Corp. is investing up to $100 million to modernize two manufacturing sites in Ontario.
- The Canadian government will contribute up to $20 million to the project through its Strategic Innovation Fund (SIF).
- The SIF supports business investments in Canada’s business and manufacturing sectors.
- Modernization of the two plants in Woodbridge and Blenheim, Ontario, will create an estimated 110 jobs and maintain over 700 additional jobs.
- The total investment will help create and maintain more than 800 jobs.
- Woodbridge will establish a Centre of Excellence in Woodbridge, Ontario, to centralize its global research into advanced materials and product design of molded foam products.
- Products include car seats and armrests.
- The company states the Centre of Excellence will allow for more intellectual property to be developed and retained in Canada.
- The company plans to expand collaboration with high schools and post-secondary institutions.
- Charles Daly, Woodbridge’s president and CEO, stated: 'With the support of the Canadian government, this significant investment will result in world-class manufacturing capabilities and accelerating innovation in our technology advantage.'
- Daly added that they view the project as an investment in global competitiveness to create and sustain rewarding jobs while preparing for the future of the global automotive industry.
- Woodbridge was founded in 1978 and operates more than 60 facilities around the world.
- The company specializes in manufacturing foam polymers and molding for the automotive industry.
