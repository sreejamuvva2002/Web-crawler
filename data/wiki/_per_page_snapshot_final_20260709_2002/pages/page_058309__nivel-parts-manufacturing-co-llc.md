# Nivel Parts & Manufacturing Co., LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-01-17

**Source:** [Nivel Parts & Mfg. Acquires Snorkel Your ATV LLC - Nivel](https://www.nivel.com/2022/01/18/nivel-parts-mfg-acquires-snorkel-your-atv-llc/)  ·  `page_058309`

## Facts

- Nivel Parts & Manufacturing Co., LLC announced the acquisition of Snorkel Your ATV LLC on January 17, 2022.
- The purchase brings together Nivel’s manufacturing, sourcing, and distribution capabilities with Snorkel Your ATV’s highly regarded product line and new product development expertise.
- Kyle Tallman, founder and former owner of Snorkel Your ATV LLC, stated it has been exceptionally enjoyable working with Nivel and expressed respect for the company while looking forward to growing the business further.
- Nivel recognizes increasing growth in the UTV and ATV segments.
- Through the acquisition of Snorkel Your ATV, Nivel expands its snorkel product line with a well-recognized brand and gains important product insight.
- Nivel CEO Brett Hankey commented that they are excited to welcome Snorkel Your ATV into the Nivel family, praising Kyle’s focus on new product development, quality production, and customer-focused sales and service.
- Founded in 1968, Nivel is a global manufacturer, distributor, and seller of aftermarket parts and accessories for niche vehicles and heavy-duty equipment.
- Nivel is a trusted business partner to thousands of dealers, distributors, and consumers in the US and international markets.
- Nivel owns the most valuable brands in the aftermarket niche vehicle parts and accessories categories: MadJax, Red Dot, GTW, Jake’s, Reliance, Seizmik and Highlifter.
- Founded in 2008, Snorkel Your ATV LLC is an industry-leading manufacturer that engineers, fabricates and direct-sells over 160 proprietary accessories for ATVs and UTVs.
- The primary products are snorkel kits allowing the ATV/UTV to operate with its engine underwater.
- Snorkel Your ATV’s products allow ATV/UTV owners to customize, increase performance, and protect their vehicles.
