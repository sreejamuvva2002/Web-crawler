# The Hertz Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2012-07-10

**Source:** [Hertz Introduces Porsche Sports Cars Into Its U.S. Fleet Hertz becomes the first U.S. car rental company to purchase vehicles from Porsche -](https://newsroom.hertz.com/press-releases/press-release-details/hertz-introduces-porsche-sports-cars-into-its-u-s-fleet-hertz-becomes-the-first-u-s-car-re/)  ·  `page_048771`

## Facts

- Hertz Corporation became the first U.S. car rental company to purchase vehicles from Porsche and the first major U.S. car rental company to rent Porsche sports cars.
- Through its relationship with Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) and Porsche Motorsport North America, Inc. (PMNA), Hertz enabled consumers in select markets to rent a limited number of Panamera sport sedans as part of the Hertz Prestige Collection.
- Mark P. Frissora served as Hertz Chairman and CEO at the time of the announcement.
- Hertz was voted Top-Rated for Vehicles in the Zagat 2012/13 U.S. Car Rental Survey.
- The Porsche Panamera sports sedan provides sports car technology for four passengers, combining comfort and everyday practicality without compromise.
- Hertz and Porsche Motorsport North America agreed that Hertz would be named an official sponsor of the 2012 Porsche IMSA GT3 Cup Challenge by Yokohama.
- Hertz was also named a sponsor of the 2012 JDX Racing Team as part of the agreement.
- As part of the sponsorship, Hertz will be integrated into the GT3 Cup Challenge and displayed prominently on all vehicles.
- The 2012 JDX Racing Porsche GTC car competed throughout 2012 in the American Le Mans Series.
- The Porsche Panamera has a manufacturer’s suggested retail price starting at $75,850.
- Hertz customers in select California, Florida, and Nevada markets could rent the vehicle with rates starting at $250 per weekend day.
- Hertz operates one of the youngest and most diverse fleets in the industry.
- Hertz offers a mobile SMS text and email service called Carfirmations that confirms Gold members’ reservations and advises them of their car and location prior to arrival.
- Hertz offers Choose Control Gold Choice, which allows Gold members to keep their reserved car or choose a different car from the Gold Choice area.
- Hertz offers Zap Technology e-Return, which emails receipts to customers to ensure the fastest car rental drop-off.
- Hertz operates Acceler-Rental ExpressRent Interactive Kiosks that allow customers to rent cars with or without reservations through live, face-to-face video kiosks.
- Hertz pioneered vehicle specific reservation capabilities via its Collections and maintains the largest fleet of make and model reservable cars in the industry.
- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA), based in Atlanta, Ga., is the exclusive U.S. importer of Porsche sports cars, the Cayenne SUV, and the Panamera sports sedan.
- PCNA was established in 1984 as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- PCNA employs approximately 220 people who provide parts, service, marketing, and training for 192 dealers.
- Hertz operates from approximately 8,650 corporate and licensee locations in approximately 150 countries.
- Hertz is the number one airport car rental brand in the U.S. and at 119 major airports in Europe.
- Hertz was voted the Best Overall Car Rental Company in Zagat’s 2012/13 U.S. Car Rental Survey, earning top honors in 14 additional categories.
- Hertz swept global awards for Best Rewards Program and Best Overall Benefits from FlyerTalk.com.
- Hertz owns Donlen Corporation, a vehicle leasing and fleet management leader.
- Hertz operates the Hertz On Demand car sharing business.
- Hertz owns Hertz Equipment Rental Corporation, which includes Hertz Entertainment Services.
