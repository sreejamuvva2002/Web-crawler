## Facts

- Volvo Trucks North America will showcase the Class 8 Volvo VNR Electric truck at the Green Transportation Summit & Expo (GTSE) from August 20-22 in Tacoma, Washington.
- Volvo Trucks is a Presenting Ride & Drive Sponsor of the 13th annual West Coast expo.
- The GTSE ride and drive event takes place on Tuesday, August 20, from 2:00 to 4:30 pm PT at the Tacoma Dome.
- Participants must register, complete a waiver, and present a valid commercial driver’s license (CDL) to operate the battery-electric heavy-duty truck on a closed course.
- A Volvo VNR Electric will be displayed in the exhibit hall for attendees to examine specifications and speak with a product expert.
- The Volvo VNR Electric is engineered to achieve a range of up to 275 miles.
- The VNR Electric offers customizable vehicle configurations tailored for local and regional pickup and delivery, drayage hauls, and food-related product distribution.
- There are currently over 540 Volvo VNR Electric trucks in customer operations.
- The VNR Electric has accumulated over 6.5 million miles of service since its commercial launch in 2021.
- Dawn Fenton, vice president of government relations and public affairs for Volvo Group North America, will moderate the kick-off plenary session 'Paving the Way for ZEV (Zero-Emission Vehicles) Trucks in Washington State' on August 21 from 10:15 to 11:30 am PT.
- Dawn Fenton leads a panel of real-world practitioners discussing incentive programs and reliable charging infrastructure networks to support zero-emission commercial vehicle adoption in Washington state.
- Dawn Fenton serves as board chair for the Powering America’s Commercial Transportation (PACT) coalition, which advocates for ZEV infrastructure for medium- and heavy-duty vehicles.
- Dawn Fenton has 30 years of experience in government relations across e-mobility, energy, environment, sustainability, foreign policy, and trade-related issues.
- Keith Brandis, vice president of policy and regulatory affairs at Volvo Group Trucks Technology, stated that GTSE is a premier platform for showcasing zero-emission technologies for clean freight transportation.
- The Volvo VNR Electric is highlighted as a standout in the zero-emission technology category.
- Volvo Group aims to achieve 35% zero-emission vehicle sales by 2030.
- Volvo Group targets a 100% CO2 reduction by 2040.
- As part of the PACT coalition, Volvo Group contributes to clean transportation efforts by informing industry stakeholders, policymakers, fleet owners, and utility companies about sustainable future achievements.
- GTSE is a leading Pacific Northwest resource for fleets, community organizations, industry professionals, policymakers, and thought leaders to advance clean transportation.
- The three-day GTSE event includes networking opportunities, information sessions, workshops, and vehicle walkthroughs.
- In 2023, GTSE attracted more than 1,000 attendees, 75 exhibitors, and 135 subject matter experts on mobility-related subjects.
