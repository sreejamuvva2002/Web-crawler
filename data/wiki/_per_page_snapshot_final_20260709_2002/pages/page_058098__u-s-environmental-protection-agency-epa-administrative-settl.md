# U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Administrative Settlement Agreements

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-08

**Source:** [‘Citizens have a right to know’: EPA fines 4 Georgia companies, others](https://www.yahoo.com/news/citizens-know-epa-fines-4-222145008.html)  ·  `page_058098`

## Facts

- The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) reached multiple administrative settlement agreements with various companies across the Southeast region in 2024.
- These agreements resolved allegations that the companies violated Tier II reporting requirements of the Emergency Planning and Community Right-to-Know Act (EPCRA) Section 312 and Toxic Release Inventory (TRI) reporting requirements of EPCRA Section 313.
- Nidec Elesys Americas Corporation is located in Suwanee, Georgia.
- Nidec Elesys manufactures automotive safety electronic controls including occupant detection, radar technology, and the safety system ECU.
- On July 8, 2024, a CAFO was filed regarding Nidec Elesys Americas Corporation for alleged violations of Section 313 of EPCRA for failing to submit required Form R for lead compounds processed at the facility during calendar years 2021 and 2022.
- The CAFO requires Nidec Elesys Americas Corporation to pay a penalty of $34,730.
- Purafil Inc. is located in Doraville, Georgia.
- Purafil manufactures air-filtration chemicals and equipment to remove gaseous contaminants and particulates from airports, schools, office buildings, museums, and government buildings.
- On July 8, 2024, a CAFO was filed regarding Purafil Inc. for alleged violations of Sections 312 and 313 of EPCRA for failure to timely file Tier II and Form R reports.
- The CAFO requires Purafil Inc. to pay a civil penalty of $82,700.
- Hussmann Corporation is located in Suwanee, Georgia.
- Hussmann provides merchandising cases, refrigeration systems, aftermarket services, and other items for food retailers.
- On July 16, 2024, a CAFO was filed regarding Hussmann Corporation for alleged violations of Section 313 of EPCRA for failing to submit required Form R for chromium, copper, and manganese processed at the facility during calendar year 2021.
- The CAFO requires Hussmann Corporation to pay a penalty of $74,123.
- OFS Fitel, LLC is located in Norcross, Georgia.
- OFS Fitel manufactures and provides optical fiber, optical fiber cable, connectivity, FTTX, and specialty photonics solutions.
- On August 19, 2024, a CAFO was filed regarding OFS Fitel, LLC for alleged violations of Section 312 of EPCRA for failing to submit required Tier II forms.
- The CAFO also resolves alleged violations of Section 313 of EPCRA for OFS Fitel for failing to submit required Form R for chlorine and hydrofluoric acid otherwise used at the facility during calendar year 2020.
- The CAFO requires OFS Fitel, LLC to pay a penalty of $38,852.
- Keriema Newman, director of the Enforcement and Compliance Assurance Division in EPA's Southeast region, stated: 'Citizens have a right to know about dangerous and toxic substances in their communities.'
- Newman noted that homes, schools, businesses, fire stations, and churches are near these facilities, some of which are near population centers.
- The EPA action underscores the importance of companies understanding the hazards of using hazardous substances like compounds containing lead and undertaking safe handling practices to protect public safety.
- The purpose of submitting Tier II forms is to provide state, tribal, local officials, and the public with specific information about potential hazards, including locations and amounts of hazardous chemicals present at facilities during the previous calendar year.
- Under federal TRI regulations, companies that use certain listed toxic chemicals must report their releases each year to the EPA using Form R (EPA Form 9350-1).
- This information serves as the basis for the TRI, a collection of data readily reviewed by communities, government, and industry.
- Public availability of this information incentivizes companies to reduce harmful chemical use and improve environmental performance.
- TRI reporting informs surrounding communities about toxic chemicals that could potentially harm public health and the environment, offering transparency.
- The Carlstar Group is located in Clinton, Tennessee.
- On April 15, 2024, a CAFO was filed regarding The Carlstar Group for alleged violations of Section 313 of EPCRA for failure to report certain chemicals on Form R.
- The CAFO requires The Carlstar Group to pay a civil penalty of $66,392.
- Karnak-South, Inc. is located in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.
- On June 11, 2024, a CAFO was filed regarding Karnak-South, Inc. for alleged violations of Section 313 of EPCRA for failing to submit required Form R for 1,2,4-Trimethylbenzene processed during calendar years 2020 and 2021.
- The CAFO requires Karnak-South, Inc. to pay a penalty of $12,639.
- Englander Enterprises (d.b.a. EEI Manufacturing Services) is located in Clearwater, Florida.
- On July 8, 2024, a CAFO was filed regarding Englander Enterprises for alleged violations of Section 313 of EPCRA for failing to submit required Form R for lead processed during calendar years 2021 and 2022.
- The CAFO requires Englander Enterprises to pay a penalty of $38,425.
- Koch Foods of Gadsden is located in Gadsden, Alabama.
- On August 21, 2024, a CAFO was filed regarding Koch Foods of Gadsden for alleged violations of Sections 312 EPCRA for failing to timely file its Tier II form.
- The CAFO requires Koch Foods of Gadsden to pay a civil penalty of $1,500.
- Loparex LLC is located in Eden, North Carolina.
- On August 21, 2024, a CAFO was filed regarding Loparex LLC for alleged violations of Section 313 of EPCRA for failing to timely file its Form R.
- The CAFO requires Loparex LLC to pay a civil penalty of $33,710.
- Refresco Beverages US Inc. is located in Lakeland, Florida.
- On August 26, 2024, a CAFO was filed regarding Refresco Beverages US Inc. for alleged violations of Section 313 of EPCRA for failing to submit required Form R for nitric acid used during calendar year 2020.
- The CAFO requires Refresco Beverages US Inc. to pay a penalty of $25,110.
- Bharat Forge PMT Technologie is located in Surgoinsville, Tennessee.
- On August 26, 2024, a CAFO was filed regarding Bharat Forge PMT Technologie for alleged violations of Sections 312 and 313 of EPCRA for failing to timely file its Tier II and Form R.
- The CAFO requires Bharat Forge PMT Technologie to pay a civil penalty of $39,445.
