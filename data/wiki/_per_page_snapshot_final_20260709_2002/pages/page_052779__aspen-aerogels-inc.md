# Aspen Aerogels, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-29

**Source:** [Aspen Aerogels, Inc. - Aspen Aerogels, Inc. Announces $100 Million Secured Loan Agreement with General Motors](https://ir.aerogel.com/news/news-details/2022/Aspen-Aerogels-Inc.-Announces-100-Million-Secured-Loan-Agreement-with-General-Motors/default.aspx)  ·  `page_052779`

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels, Inc. announced a definitive loan agreement with General Motors Holdings LLC for a secured lending commitment of $100 million.
- The $100 million loan proceeds will be available to Aspen to draw on a periodic basis from January 2023 through September 2023.
- The loan carries a maturity date of March 31, 2025 and has no prepayment penalty.
- The financing is in connection with the construction and equipping of Aspen's second aerogel manufacturing facility in Statesboro, Georgia.
- The new Georgia facility is designed to triple Aspen's nameplate aerogel capacity.
- Phase I of Plant II at the Georgia facility is expected to be commissioned in the first half of 2024.
- Donald R. Young, Aspen's President and CEO, stated that GM's investment will support the construction of the new state-of-the-art aerogel manufacturing facility in Georgia.
- Aspen has been a key supplier to General Motors for over two decades.
- Earlier this year, General Motors named Aspen an Overdrive Award winner as part of GM's 30th Annual Supplier of the Year awards.
- Aspen signed GM's ESG Supplier Pledge, outlining a shared vision for a collaborative supply chain that minimizes environmental impact and enhances long-term sustainable electrification.
- In November 2022, Aspen was awarded a Silver EcoVadis Medal for its overall sustainability scorecard, placing it in the 87th percentile of companies assessed by EcoVadis.
- Aspen's PyroThin® products are designed to enable solutions to thermal runaway challenges within the electric vehicle market.
- Aspen Battery Materials, the company's carbon aerogel initiative, seeks to increase the performance of lithium-ion battery cells to help EV manufacturers extend driving range and reduce costs.
- Aspen currently manufactures its products at its East Providence, R.I. facilities, which house three existing production lines.
- Aspen also operates a Mexico parts fabrication facility for thermal barrier assembly operations.
- The company's Aerogel Technology Platform™ is leveraged to bring sustainability solutions into large markets including e-mobility and clean energy.
