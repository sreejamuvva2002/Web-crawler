# Kautex of Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2014-01-30

**Source:** [Auto Parts Manufacturer to Expand in Lavonia | Georgia Public Broadcasting](https://www.gpb.org/blogs/georgia-works/2014/01/30/auto-parts-manufacturer-expand-in-lavonia)  ·  `page_045430`

## Facts

- Kautex of Georgia, Inc. has announced it will expand its production facility in Lavonia by adding a 95,000 square foot warehouse.
- The company is a subsidiary of German-based Textron Incorporated.
- Currently the plant manufactures fuel tanks, catalytic converters and other parts used in automobile manufacturing.
- The expansion is expected to cost $3.3 million.
- The expansion is expected to be completed by May.
- The expansion in Lavonia means Kautex will continue to play a major role in local job creation.
- Tyler Miles, Director of Operations for the Lavonia plant at Kautex of Georgia, Inc., states the new warehouse will allow the company to solidify its operations in the community and better meet the needs of customers throughout the United States.
- Tyler Miles notes that continuous collaboration with various agencies to remain a preeminent employer in the Lavonia area is one of the company's main priorities.
