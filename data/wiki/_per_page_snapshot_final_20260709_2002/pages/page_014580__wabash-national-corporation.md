# Wabash National Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Wabash National Corp announced it has recently received orders exceeding $100 million from certain of its customers.
- Acting CEO Richard E. Dessimoz stated that indications from core customers show their 2002 requirements will exceed orders placed in 2001.
- Dessimoz noted that the company does not anticipate industry fundamentals to dramatically improve in the short term.
- A number of key customers have performed relatively well during difficult economic times, which appears to be driving demand for new trailers.
- Wabash National Corporation designs, manufactures, and markets standard and customized truck trailers under the Wabash and Fruehauf brands.
- The company also manufactures composite trailers under the RoadRailer name.
