# Hyundai Mobility Training Center

**Type:** Workforce Training  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[workforce-training|Workforce Training]]  ·  **Location:** Ellabell, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

A state-operated training center in Ellabell, GA that will teach workers to build next-generation electric vehicles for Hyundai’s new assembly plant.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Supports workforce development for electric vehicle manufacturing at Hyundai's new plant in Georgia.

## Key facts

- The new Hyundai Mobility Training Center will support the automaker’s assembly plant.
- The 89,000-square-foot facility will teach workers how to build next-generation electric vehicles.
- The center is operated by the state’s workforce development program and is part of the Technical College System of Georgia.
- At full capacity, it can train up to 824 individuals at a time.
