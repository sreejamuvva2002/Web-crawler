## Facts

- Duckyang USA plans to invest $10,000,000.00 to build out 230,000 square feet of new space in Braselton, Georgia.
- The company plans to occupy the new space at 984 Broadway Avenue in Braselton on or about January 1, 2022.
- Duckyang Ind. is a leading auto component provider founded in July 1977 in Ulsan, Korea.
- The company's founding belief was that “manufacturing is the foundation for industrial activity which drives the nation’s economy.”
- Duckyang remains committed to its motto of “responsibility, creativity, and cooperation.”
- The company emphasizes a moral and transparent management system focused on developing employees into leading creative talents.
- Dong-in Son serves as the Chief Executive Officer of Duckyang USA.
- State and local economic development sources confirmed the investment and occupancy plans.
