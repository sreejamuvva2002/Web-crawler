**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Over 100 new jobs will soon be coming to Bulloch County.
- Aspen Aerogels will invest more than $70 million in Statesboro.
- Aspen Aerogels manufactures energy infrastructure insulation used in refineries, lLNG facilities, and power plants.
- The company will build its manufacturing plant in the Gateway Industrial Center across from Great Dane.
- Benjy Thompson, CEO of the Development Authority of Bulloch County, stated that the advanced manufacturing company fits into the existing industry in Bulloch County.
- Benjy Thompson noted that the project brings a significant amount of jobs and a high level of capital investment, which is a trend for advanced manufacturing projects, and that they pay a very good wage.
- Construction on the new plant will start early next year.
- Production at the new plant is scheduled to begin in late 2017.
