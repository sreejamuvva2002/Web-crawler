# Yamaha

## Facts

- Yamaha has shifted from being a brand known for pianos and motorcycles to becoming a pioneer in the manufacture of engines for electric vehicles.
- The brand has been developing motors for e-bikes and working on zero-emission technologies for over 30 years.
- Recent advances have led to the creation of a compact and powerful electric motor with capabilities ranging from 35 kW to 200 kW.
- Yamaha formed an alliance with Caterham to develop an electric motor for a revolutionary sports car project.
- The collaboration with Caterham also involves VT Holdings and the designer Italdesign.
- The Yamaha motor used in Caterham's Project V incorporates IPMSM motor technology.
- The motor can reach 272 HP.
- The development includes an impressive thermal management system.
- Yamaha continues to work on innovations such as outboard motors for boats.
- Yamaha has long-range visions including the development of hydrogen engines.
- Past collaborations with Toyota and other industrial giants reinforce its ability to lead this transformation.
- Yamaha's electric motor is adaptable to a variety of vehicles, from e-bikes to cars.
- Since 1887, Yamaha has been synonymous with quality and innovation in the production of musical instruments.
- Yamaha's foray into the world of motorcycles began in 1955.
- The continued investment in electric technology reflects a commitment to the environment and to the evolving market towards electric mobility.
