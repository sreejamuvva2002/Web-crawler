# Denkai America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Denkai America, Inc. expanding operations in Kershaw County | S.C. Governor Henry McMaster](https://governor.sc.gov/news/2021-06/denkai-america-inc-expanding-operations-kershaw-county)  ·  `page_005553`

## Facts

- Denkai America, Inc., a subsidiary of Nippon Denkai, Ltd., announced plans to expand operations in Kershaw County.
- The expansion involves a $14 million investment.
- The investment will create 10 new jobs.
- Denkai America, Inc. has been operating for more than 40 years.
- The company is a leading manufacturer of high-quality electrodeposited copper foils for printed circuit board, industrial and energy storage applications.
- The facility is located at 29 Battleship Extension Road in Camden.
- The expansion will increase the company’s capability and capacity to meet growing demand for anticipated business growth into the energy storage market segment.
- The project includes the modernization of the current manufacturing facility.
- The project includes the construction of additional factory space.
- Electrodeposited copper foil is a key component in lithium ion batteries which power electric vehicles.
- Denkai America, Inc. is positioned to participate in the rapidly growing vehicle electrification market.
- The expansion is expected to be completed by October 2022.
- Denkai America, Inc. directs interested job applicants to visit the company’s careers webpage.
- Denkai America, Inc. Chief Operating Officer Michael Coll stated the company is excited to revitalize its manufacturing facility and prepare for growth into new markets.
- On behalf of CEO Dr. Nakajima, Michael Coll extended gratitude to the business development teams from the state of South Carolina and Kershaw County.
- Michael Coll noted the investment is necessary for ensuring continued prosperity and the ability to create additional high-quality jobs in Camden.
- Governor Henry McMaster congratulated Denkai America, Inc. on its latest expansion in Kershaw County and praised South Carolina’s workforce excellence in the manufacturing sector.
- Secretary of Commerce Bobby Hitt stated the expansion is proof that manufacturing continues to attract jobs and capital investment in South Carolina.
- Bobby Hitt noted the expansion will benefit Kershaw County and the state for years to come, highlighting that South Carolina is “Just right” for making quality products.
- Kershaw County Council Chairman Julian Burns welcomed the expansion and jobs announcement, calling it great news for home-team high performers and proof that the community competes globally.
- Central SC Alliance Chairman W. Keller Kissam congratulated Denkai America, Inc. and partners in Kershaw County, noting the expansion amplifies the company’s portfolio and career advancement opportunities for workers.
