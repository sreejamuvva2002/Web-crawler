# TI Automotive Limited

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-15

## Facts

- The Company is a leading global manufacturer of fluid storage, carrying, delivery, and thermal management products and systems for all vehicle types, from internal combustion engines to battery electric vehicles.
- The Company is the number one supplier of brake and fuel lines.
- The Company is a top three supplier of fuel tanks and refrigeration lines and connectors.
- The Company is a top five supplier of coolant modules.
- Effective from 1 January 2024, the Company operates under a new regional reporting structure with three geographic regions: Americas, Europe, the Middle East and Africa, and Asia Pacific.
- On April 15, 2025, the Company was acquired by ABC Technologies Inc for an enterprise value of over GBP 1.8 billion.
- The combined business following the acquisition will be rebranded as TI Automotive.
- The combined business will have a propulsion agnostic portfolio positioned to support a diverse range of customers.
- The combined business will generate USD 5.4 billion in revenue.
- The combined business will employ 34,600 people across 26 countries.
- TI Automotive will be led by Terry Campbell, who serves as ABC President and Chief Executive Officer.
- TI Automotive will be headquartered in Auburn Hills, Michigan.
- TI Automotive will be managed through a Regional Operating Model to ensure customer proximity and agility to respond rapidly to local market dynamics.
- The Company was delisted from the London Stock Exchange following its acquisition by ABC Technologies Inc on April 15, 2025.
- ABC Technologies is now the sole shareholder of the Company.
