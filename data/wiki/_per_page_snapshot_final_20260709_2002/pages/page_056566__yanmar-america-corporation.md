# YANMAR America Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [YANMAR America Corporation will create 25 new jobs in Cherokee County, invest $20M | WBHF](https://wbhfradio.org/yanmar-america-corporation-will-create-25-new-jobs-in-cherokee-county-invest-20m/)  ·  `page_056566`

## Facts

- The Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) announced on November 1 that YANMAR America Corporation will open a new training and customer experience facility in Cherokee County.
- The new facility will create 25 new jobs.
- YANMAR America Corporation is investing $20 million into the local community.
- YANMAR America Corporation manufactures advanced performance diesel engines, equipment, and gas engine based energy systems.
- YANMAR's North, South, and Central American regional headquarters operation is located in Adairsville, Georgia.
- This new investment represents an expansion of YANMAR's presence in Georgia.
- The 25 jobs will cover administration, marketing, service, training, and management roles.
- Cherokee County was selected for its natural beauty, welcoming officials, shared vision for sustainability and agriculture, proximity to the current Adairsville headquarters, and proximity to the Atlanta airport.
- The new facility will be located in the Cherokee 75 Corridor at 1 YANMAR Drive, at the corner of Highway 92 and Old Alabama Road.
- GDEcD Project Manager Joseph Huntemann represented the Global Commerce division in partnership with the Cherokee Office of Economic Development and Georgia EMC.
- Cherokee County Commission Chairman Buzz Ahrens stated that YANMAR is among several new and expanding international corporations choosing Cherokee due to its superb location and access to talent.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson said, 'We are in the business of bringing quality jobs and investment to Georgia, and it is always a great day when we are able to celebrate this good news with statewide partners and our corporate citizens.' She added, 'We are happy to welcome YANMAR’s newest operation, and we are confident they will thrive in our pro-business environment.'
- YANMAR America president Tim Fernandez said, 'Cherokee County was a desirable location for our new facility for many reasons. The area itself offers natural beauty, and the Cherokee County officials were very welcoming and share our vision for promoting sustainability and agriculture. The site’s proximity to our current headquarters in Adairsville and the Atlanta airport is also ideal for bringing in our customers from all over the globe.'
- Cherokee County Commission Chairman Buzz Ahrens said, 'We are delighted to have YANMAR’s state-of-the-art training facility in Cherokee. They are among several new and expanding international corporations choosing Cherokee because of our superb location and access to talent. We look forward to YANMAR’s success as they help to build our sustainable vision for Cherokee.'
- YANMAR America Corporation serves as the regional headquarters for Yanmar Co., Ltd., a global company based in Osaka, Japan.
- Yanmar Co., Ltd. was established in 1912 and became the world's first manufacturer to develop a practical small diesel engine in 1933.
- YANMAR America Corporation's current headquarters address is 101 International Parkway, Adairsville, GA 30103.
