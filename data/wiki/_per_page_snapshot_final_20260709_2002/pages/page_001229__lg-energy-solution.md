## Facts

- LG Energy Solution is telling some Georgia officials that it could build its own factory in the state if rival SK Innovation cannot proceed.
- LG Energy Solution CEO Jong Hyun Kim wrote a Wednesday letter to Democratic U.S. Sen Raphael Warnock on Wednesday saying LG “is prepared to do whatever we can to help the people and workers of Georgia.”
- If some other entity acquires the SK plant, LG could help run the $2.6 billion electric vehicle battery plant in Commerce.
- SK Innovation plans to hire 2,600 workers for the Commerce facility.
