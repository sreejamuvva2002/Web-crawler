## Facts

- Volvo Trucks North America is headquartered in Greensboro, North Carolina.
- Every Volvo truck is assembled at the Volvo Trucks New River Valley manufacturing facility in Dublin, Virginia.
- The Dublin, Virginia manufacturing facility meets the internationally recognized ISO 9001 standard for quality.
- The Dublin, Virginia manufacturing facility meets the ISO 14001 standard for environmental care.
- The Dublin, Virginia facility holds a dual ISO 50001/Superior Energy Performance certification at the platinum level for sustained excellence in energy management.
- Volvo Trucks North America offers a full range of diesel, alternative-fuel, and all-electric vehicles as part of its complete transport solutions.
- Peter Voorhoeve serves as president of Volvo Trucks North America.
- The new Volvo VNL is designed specifically for North America and is the most connected truck Volvo has ever built.
- Volvo Connect is the fleet management platform that brings vehicle insights, remote diagnostics, fuel economy reports, and over-the-air software updates together in one portal.
- Uptime services use wireless connectivity to track vehicle health in real time, spot potential issues early, schedule maintenance, and install over-the-air software updates while a truck is parked.
- Productivity services include fleet planning systems, fuel and safety reports, and driver support features.
- The Volvo My Truck app allows drivers to check key vehicle information such as fluid levels and light status, and set cab climate control via phone or tablet before starting a shift.
- Uptime Services are delivered by a network of nearly 400 authorized dealers across North America and the 24/7 Volvo Trucks Uptime Center.
- Customer support is provided via a global dealer network with 2,200 service points in about 130 countries.
- Volvo trucks are assembled in 12 countries across the globe.
- In 2025, approximately 120,000 Volvo trucks were delivered worldwide.
- Volvo Trucks is part of the Volvo Group, which also provides complete solutions for financing and service.
