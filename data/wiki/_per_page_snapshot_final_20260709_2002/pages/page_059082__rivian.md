# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-08

**Source:** [Rivian reports a drop in sales - electrive.com](https://www.electrive.com/2024/11/08/rivian-reports-a-drop-in-sales/)  ·  `page_059082`

## Facts

- Rivian reported a net loss of $1.1 billion for Q3 2024, compared to $1.37 billion in the same period last year and $1.46 billion in Q2 2024.
- Operating expenses decreased from $963 million in Q3 2023 to $777 million in Q3 2024.
- Adjusted EBITDA for Q3 2024 was -$757 million, compared to -$902 million in the same period last year.
- Rivian produced 13,157 vehicles and delivered 10,018 units between July and September 2024.
- Rivian lowered its 2024 annual production target from 57,000 units to between 47,000 and 49,000 vehicles after missing parts paralyzed production for months.
- Rivian's annual delivery target for 2024 remains unchanged despite the production cut.
- A component shortage affected Rivian's Enduro motor, which serves as the single-motor-per-axle system in the Dual-Motor variant of the R1.
- The Enduro motor is also used in the Tri-Motor variant, where it functions as only one of the three motors.
- To mitigate the supply constraint, Rivian expects to produce more Tri-Motor vehicles and single-motor commercial vans.
- Amazon is increasing its delivery order of commercial vans for the fourth quarter of 2024.
- The component shortage limited R1 supply in Q3 2024, while a challenging consumer backdrop also negatively impacted sales.
- Rivian expects to return to profitability in the current quarter.
- Rivian anticipates increased revenue per unit driven by non-vehicle revenues like service and higher prices for premium variants such as the Tri-Motor R1.
- Rivian and Volkswagen Group agreed to jointly develop car software at the end of June, with plans to close the joint venture in Q4 2024.
- The approximately $5 billion joint venture deal with Volkswagen, combined with current cash and short-term investments, will fund operations through the ramp-up of the R2 in Normal.
- Funding from the deal will also support a midsize platform in Georgia to help achieve positive free cash flow and meaningful scale.
