# Seohan Auto Georgia Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-09-05

**Source:** [Seohan Auto Georgia is Open for Business in Liberty County – LCDA](https://comegrow.global/2024/09/05/seohan-auto-georgia-is-open-for-business-in-liberty-county/)  ·  `page_059347`

## Facts

- Seohan Auto Georgia held a Grand Opening Ceremony for its new manufacturing facility in the Tradeport East Business Center on September 5, 2024.
- The new facility is located in Midway, Liberty County, Georgia.
- The facility will produce H/shafts, axles, and brake systems for Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs).
- One of the facility's customers is Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.
- Seohan Auto Georgia's operations will create at least 180 new jobs.
- The project represents an investment of more than $72 million in Liberty County.
- Seohan Auto Georgia Corporation is a new subsidiary of Korea Movenex Co., Ltd.
- Parent company Korea Movenex Co., Ltd. was established in 1974 and employs more than 2,800 employees globally.
- The process to locate the manufacturing facility in Liberty County began in November 2022.
- Other locations were considered before Liberty County won the project for Seohan Auto Georgia.
- Georgia Department of Economic Development Commissioner Pat Wilson stated: “Economic development does not happen by accident, and the community’s investment in the Tradeport East Business Center was not only an investment in your own future, but also allowed Seohan Auto Georgia to meet a challenging timeline to get up and running.”
- State Representative Al Williams serves as the Liberty County Development Authority Board Chairman and said: “As the Liberty County Development Authority Board Chairman, I am proud to welcome Seohan to Liberty County. I was closely involved in the effort to locate this impressive manufacturing facility here, a process that began in November of 2022. There were other locations being considered, so we had to win this one for our community.”
- The Liberty County Development Authority (LCDA) recruits industries, develops industrial parks, constructs shell buildings, supports educational and workforce development programs, and forms partnerships with public and private entities.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) serves as the state’s sales and marketing arm and lead agency for attracting new business investment.
