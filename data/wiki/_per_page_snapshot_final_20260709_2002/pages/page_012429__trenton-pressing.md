# Trenton Pressing

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-04-13

**Source:** [Trenton Pressing Breaks Ground on $10M Expansion – Dade County Sentinel](https://www.dadecountysentinel.com/2023/04/13/trenton-pressing-breaks-ground-on-10m-expansion/)  ·  `page_012429`

## Facts

- Project Highland is a $10 million expansion of Trenton Pressing.
- The expansion will add 105,000 square feet of space to the company.
- A groundbreaking ceremony for the project took place on April 6.
- Mike Pfunder, owner of Trenton Pressing, explained that the company chose its name because of its heart for the community.
- Trenton Pressing provides automatic and hand metal stamping, welding, engineering, product assembly, and more.
- Trenton Pressing's customers include BMW, Club Car, and ZF.
- The expansion will add 20 jobs to the company’s current workforce of 220 employees.
- Construction on the expansion is expected to be completed by the end of 2023.
- Attendees and speakers at the groundbreaking ceremony included Mike Pfunder (owner), Hans Pfunder (chief operating officer), Evan Stone (IDA Executive Director), Mayor Alex Case, County Executive Ted Rumley, Jeff Mullis (president and CEO of Northwest Georgia Joint Development Authority), and Georgia State Representative Mike Cameron.
- Mike Pfunder stated regarding the new building: “Once it is built, this building will be here for 100 years.”
