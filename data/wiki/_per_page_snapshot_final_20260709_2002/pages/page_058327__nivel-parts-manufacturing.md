# Nivel Parts & Manufacturing

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Georgia Jobs – Golf Car News](https://www.golfcarnews.com/georgia-jobs/)  ·  `page_058327`

## Facts

- Nivel Parts & Manufacturing, North America’s largest independent provider of golf car aftermarket parts and accessories, will create more than 50 jobs in Cairo, Georgia.
- The company will invest heavily in its existing facility in Cairo, increasing manufacturing production at the site.
- Building improvements and machinery relocation at the Cairo facility began in February 2019.
- Nivel has already started hiring new employees for the expansion and will continue hiring through July 2020.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced the expansion, stating that Nivel is a fantastic partner with the local community in Grady County, offering internships through the local high school and creating job opportunities for students entering the workforce.
- Rob Darrow, Director of Change Management at Nivel, stated that Georgia was chosen as a center of excellence for research, design, and manufacturing to fit the company's values of providing high-quality products at competitive prices.
- Grady County Commissioner Chairwoman LaFaye Copeland expressed pleasure over Nivel choosing Grady County, noting the county has dedicated citizens eager to work and learn.
- The Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) Regional Project Manager Tina Herring represented the Global Commerce Division in partnership with the Grady County Joint Development Authority and Quick Start.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson congratulated those involved, noting that Nivel’s expansion highlights economic development assets in rural communities and golf industry assets, as all three of Nivel’s manufacturing customers are located in Georgia.
- Nivel has been involved with the Cairo High School’s College and Career Academy and will continue to support Grady County with internship opportunities.
- The company was established in 1968 and is headquartered in Jacksonville, Florida.
- Nivel offers a broad product line of over 10,000 items for all golf car models from major OEMs including Club Car, E-Z-GO, and Yamaha, as well as Columbia/Harley Davidson, Cushman, Hyundai, Jacobson, Legend, Melex, Noland, Nordskog, Pargo, Taylor Dunn, and vintage or discontinued golf cars.
- Nivel specializes in providing exclusive, hard-to-find, and discontinued parts for the golf car industry.
