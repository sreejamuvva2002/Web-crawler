## Facts

- Superior Essex Communications is a leading manufacturer and provider of communication cables in North America.
- The company received recognition in more than a dozen industry awards and finalist nominations throughout 2020.
- Awards recognized the company's product and solution innovation, sustainability efforts, marketing campaigns, and assets.
- Brad R. Johnson serves as President of Superior Essex Communications.
- Melanie Singleton is the Director of Marketing Communications at Superior Essex International LP - Communication.
- The company leads in innovation through Power-over-Ethernet, smart buildings and cities, 5G fiber infrastructure including hybrid and composite solutions, and technological advancements in its product development center.
- Superior Essex Communications was the first company to receive Zero Waste to Landfill certification at its Hoisington (KS) facility.
- It is the first wire and cable manufacturer to contribute points toward LEED certification.
- It is the first to offer verified sustainable cables across more than 55 product families.
