# Go-Station

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Georgia, United States

## Overview

Go-Station is an EV charging company that offers a complete range of EV charging services, including turn-key solutions for multi-family developments, workplaces, parking facilities, healthcare, retail centers, fueling stations, and fleet operators transitioning to electrification.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Announced a new DC fast charging site for passenger cars and heavy-duty trucks at the Savannah Port Fueling Center, expanding Georgia's EV infrastructure.

## Key facts

- The new charging site will serve both passenger cars and heavy-duty trucks, addressing growing EV demand in the Savannah area.
- The Savannah Port Fueling Center opens this month as a state-of-the-art food and fueling facility for drayage and freight operators.
- Facility amenities include live digital monitors at interchange gates, overnight parking for up to 80 trucks, an EV shuttle service, medical facility, onsite truck repair, handwashing stations, showers, paperwork drop boxes, fresh and prepared foods, a Wendy's® restaurant with drive-thru, 16 gasoline pumps, 10 DEF diesel lanes, and 3 CNG fuel lanes.
- Port of Savannah houses the largest single-terminal container facility in North America, comprising Garden City Terminal (fourth busiest U.S. container terminal) and Ocean Terminal, spanning over 1,500 acres and moving more than 5 million TEU last year.
- "The fueling center's EV charging site will not only address the growing demand of EVs traveling in the area, but the rapidly expanding population of EV light, medium, and heavy-duty trucks hitting the roads soon," said Andrew Hisey, Go-Station president and CEO.
- "The Savannah Port Fueling Center is a prime example of our commitment to economic expansion, job growth, a more sustainable future, and our customers. We are thrilled to be partnering with Go-Station in making such a significant contribution to Savannah and EV transportation," said Sean Register, Managing Director of the Fuel Center.
