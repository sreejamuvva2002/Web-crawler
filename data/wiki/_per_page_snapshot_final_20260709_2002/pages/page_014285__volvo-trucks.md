# Volvo Trucks

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** 2023-12-05

**Source:** [Volvo Trucks certified dealer network adds five new dealer locations in the US - electrive.com](https://www.electrive.com/2023/12/05/volvo-trucks-certified-dealer-network-adds-five-new-dealer-locations-in-the-us/)  ·  `page_014285`

## Facts

- Volvo Trucks North America announced that Vanguard Truck Centers completed its Volvo Trucks Certified EV Dealer programme at five locations.
- The certified locations include sites in Arizona, Georgia, North Carolina, Austin, and Houston in Texas.
- These newly certified locations are now officially capable of providing comprehensive sales and service support for Volvo Trucks customers.
- The expansion brings the company’s Certified EV Dealer network into three additional states: Arizona, Georgia, and North Carolina.
- Volvo Trucks now has a total of 50 certified dealership locations across 24 US states and three Canadian provinces.
- To complete the certification programme, dealerships must install onsite charging stations.
- Dealerships must also install dedicated EV service bays.
- Dealerships are required to acquire diagnostic tools for technicians working with high-voltage systems.
- Dealerships must purchase personal protective equipment for technicians working with high-voltage systems.
- Dealerships must purchase parts inventory specifically for the VNR Electric model.
- Volvo Financial Services (VFS) introduced financing options for fleets to support the cost of installing charging infrastructure.
- Volvo Financial Services (VFS) also introduced service and maintenance plans to support the transition.
- Peter Voorhoeve serves as president of Volvo Trucks North America.
- Peter Voorhoeve highlighted that Vanguard Truck Centers is investing to certify multiple locations simultaneously across the United States.
- Peter Voorhoeve noted that cost remains a significant barrier to electrification, particularly at scale.
- Peter Voorhoeve stated that new financing options from Volvo Financial Services aim to support customers and dealers in their shift to zero-emission solutions.
