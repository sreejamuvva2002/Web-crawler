## Facts

- Motherson Yachiyo Automotive Tech Products of America, Inc. is a Tier 1 supplier to the automotive industry since 1997.
- The company specializes in designing and producing plastic fuel tanks and sunroofs.
- Motherson Yachiyo was established in 1947 by Eiichi Otake in Japan.
- Since its establishment, the company has grown its facilities worldwide.
- Motherson Yachiyo of America was founded in 1997 to serve a new demographic in the company's global capabilities.
- The company aims to become one of the world’s top-level manufacturers of automotive body parts.
- The company seeks to supply superior quality while maintaining economical products in direct response to customer needs.
