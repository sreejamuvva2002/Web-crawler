## Facts

- Dragonfly Energy inked a $30 million Strategic Partnership with Stryten Energy, a leading North American battery manufacturer.
- The partnership aims to expand the Battle Born Batteries® brand into new markets including military, automotive, marine, power sports, lawn & garden, and golf carts.
- Expansion will be achieved through brand licensing, contract manufacturing, and battery pack design.
- Stryten Energy will leverage its vast distribution network to introduce Battle Born Batteries® branded products to new retail markets.
- The partnership aims to establish a domestic circular economy for lithium batteries.
- Stryten Energy will leverage its existing lead battery recycling model for end-of-life Battle Born Batteries products.
- The agreement allows Stryten Energy to manufacture and distribute Battle Born Batteries under license, leveraging the brand’s strong reputation.
- The agreement is expected to significantly increase brand awareness for Battle Born Batteries® and unlock tremendous growth for both companies.
- The agreement paves the way for future collaboration where Stryten’s numerous U.S. lithium battery initiatives could incorporate Dragonfly Energy’s cell manufacturing technology.
- Future collaboration aims to reduce reliance on foreign suppliers and create a vertically integrated North American lithium battery production chain.
- Denis Phares serves as Chief Executive Officer of Dragonfly Energy.
- Mike Judd serves as Chief Executive Officer of Stryten Energy.
- Dragonfly Energy Holdings Corp. (Nasdaq: DFLI) is a comprehensive lithium battery technology company specializing in cell manufacturing, battery pack assembly, and full system integration.
- Through the Battle Born Batteries® brand, Dragonfly Energy has deployed hundreds of thousands of reliable battery packs through top-tier OEMs and a diverse retail customer base.
- Dragonfly Energy’s patented dry electrode manufacturing process can deliver chemistry-agnostic power solutions for applications including energy storage systems, electric vehicles, and consumer electronics.
- Dragonfly Energy's mission includes the future deployment of its proprietary, nonflammable, all-solid-state battery cells.
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- Stryten Energy is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Stryten Energy partners with recognized companies to meet growing demand for reliable and sustainable energy storage capacity.
- Stryten Energy powers applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- Stryten Energy’s stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- Stryten Energy is backed by more than a century of industry expertise.
