# Fox Factory Holding Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-06

## Facts

- Fox Factory Holding Corp. is located in Duluth, Ga.
- The company designs, engineers, and manufactures premium products for specialty sports and on- and off-road vehicles.
- It serves as a direct supplier of shocks, suspension, and components to leading powered vehicle and bicycle original equipment manufacturers.
- Its brand portfolio includes FOX, Marucci, Method Race Wheels, and others.
- The company acquires complementary businesses to integrate engineering and manufacturing expertise beyond its core shock and suspension segment.
- These acquisitions aim to diversify product offerings and increase market potential.
- It distributes products in the aftermarket through a global network of retailers and distributors, alongside direct-to-consumer channels.
- The company will announce fourth quarter 2024 earnings results on Thursday, February 27, 2025, after the market close.
- The fourth quarter results cover the period ended January 3, 2025.
