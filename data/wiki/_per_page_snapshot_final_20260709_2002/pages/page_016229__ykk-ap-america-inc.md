# YKK AP America Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [YKK AP Begins Operations at New Residential Manufacturing Facility | Window + Door](https://www.windowanddoor.com/news/ykk-ap-begins-operations-new-residential-manufacturing-facility)  ·  `page_016229`

## Facts

- YKK AP America Inc. started operations at its new manufacturing facility in Macon, Georgia.
- The 400,000-square-foot purpose-built facility is located at 100 YKK AP Way in Macon’s industrial district.
- The facility manufactures residential windows and doors for homebuilders and homeowners throughout the Southeast region.
- The opening of the facility follows more than 18 months of design and planning.
- The new facility doubles production capacity to enhance customer satisfaction amid a growing residential market.
- The facility sets the stage for producing upcoming new and upgraded StyleView Classic series products on new manufacturing lines.
- All employees have been relocated from the previous manufacturing facility to the new site.
- Oliver Stepe serves as president and CEO of YKK AP America.
- The facility integrates vinyl extrusion, fabrication, and glazing processes under one roof with a strategic floor layout.
- The company plans to add automated goods-to-person technology to reduce the manual movement of heavy windows across the plant.
- The facility is 100% climate controlled.
- The facility was designed with increased natural light, updated furnishing, and outdoor spaces including a walking trail and basketball/pickleball court.
- The company utilizes sustainable manufacturing practices to help build the future in a meaningful way.
