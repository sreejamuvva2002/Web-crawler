# Woody Industrial Co., Ltd.

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[thermal-management|Thermal Management]]  ·  **Location:** Dublin, Georgia, Laurens County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Woory Industrial Co., Ltd., an automotive parts manufacturer specializing in HVAC components, announced the establishment of its first U.S. manufacturing and R&D facility in Dublin, Laurens County, Georgia to produce electric heaters, control units and actuators for EVs, creating over 130 jobs with an estimated $18 million investment.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The new facility will produce electrical heaters, control units and actuators for electric vehicles, addressing cabin heating and battery temperature management, and positions Woory as a supplier to major EV manufacturers.
