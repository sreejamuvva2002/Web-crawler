# Archer Aviation, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Archer Raises $850 Million Amid eVTOL Push by White House | Travel Agent Central](https://www.travelagentcentral.com/transportation/archer-raises-850m-amid-evtol-push-white-house)  ·  `page_016877`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation has raised an additional $850 million following the White House’s recent announcement of an Executive Order by President Trump to implement an eVTOL Integration Pilot Program in the United States.
- The eVTOL Integration Pilot Program is focused on accelerating the deployment of electric vertical take-off and landing aircraft in the U.S.
- Archer intends to closely coordinate with the White House, Department of Transportation, and the Federal Aviation Administration on integrating the program into its plans to ramp operations in the U.S. ahead of the LA 28 Olympic Games.
- Archer will serve as the official air taxi provider for the LA 28 Olympic Games and Team USA.
- Archer believes cross-industry collaboration will be key to the success of the eVTOL Integration Pilot Program and the U.S. achieving its goal of “dominance” within this new category of aircraft.
- Archer is showcasing its Midnight aircraft at the Paris Air Show and hosting delegations from more than 20 countries, including leadership from partner organizations in the U.A.E., which is Archer’s first target “Launch Edition” market.
- Archer CEO and founder Adam Goldstein is set to meet with United States Secretary of Transportation Sean P. Duffy and acting FAA administrator Chris Rocheleau at the Paris Air Show.
- The new capital bolsters Archer's balance sheet, cementing a pro forma liquidity position of approximately $2 billion.
- Archer is strategically positioned to execute across its engineering, certification, and commercialization efforts in the United States and abroad.
- Financing was provided for the purchase and sale of 85,000,000 shares of Archer’s Class A common stock at $10 per share through a registered direct offering.
- Net proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes, focusing on expanding commercial capabilities, particularly infrastructure to support recently announced initiatives in the U.S. and “Launch Edition” markets, and developing an AI-based aviation software platform.
- The Class A common stock was offered pursuant to an automatic shelf registration statement on Form S-3ASR (File No. 333-284812) filed with the SEC on February 11, 2025, which became automatically effective upon filing.
- Moelis & Company is acting as the exclusive placement agent in connection with this offering.
