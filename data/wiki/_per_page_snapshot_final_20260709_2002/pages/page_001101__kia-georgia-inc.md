## Facts

- Kia Georgia, Inc. has re-opened its online application process for hourly production positions for the first time since completing a third wave of hiring at the West Point automotive facility in 2011.
- This marks the first time hourly production positions have been available for direct hires at the plant since then.
- Chris Miller, Kia Georgia's chief administrative officer, stated that the team has become synonymous with success since launching the Sorento in November 2009.
- The company is adding to a team that has set new standards for safety, quality, and efficiency.
- Hiring coincides with the recent launch of Plan S and initiatives following a dramatic brand shift during the next decade.
- Kia Georgia produces the award-winning and top-selling Telluride SUV, Sorento SUV, and K5 mid-size sedan.
- To date, Kia Georgia has produced almost 3.5 million vehicles.
- The facility offers pay, medical coverage, a 401K matching program, and paid holidays as part of its benefits package.
- Kia Georgia, Inc. is the first manufacturing site in North America for Seoul-based Kia Corporation.
- The plant has an annual production capacity of 340,000 units.
- The facility is located on 2,200 acres in West Point.
- Kia Georgia began mass production on November 16, 2009.
