# Georgia Department of Transportation (GDOT)

## Facts

- Georgia DOT provides transportation permits to contractors, consultants, truck operators and businesses.
- Georgia’s National Electric Vehicle Infrastructure Program (NEVI) is derived from the 2021 Bipartisan Infrastructure Law enacted as the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (IIJA).
- The Connected Vehicle (CV) Program includes a Vehicle to Everything (V2X) Roadmap Program that aims to link road users, vehicles, and infrastructure.
- The Carbon Reduction Program (CRP) provides funding to states for projects that result in transportation emission reductions.
- GDOT permit programs include Access Management, Outdoor Advertising, Oversize Permits, Utility Permits, Vegetation Management, GPAS, Movie & TV Production, LOGO Sign Program, Memorial Markers, Red Light Running Photo, Automated Traffic Enforcement Safety Device (ATESD), and Automated License Plate Readers (ALPR).
