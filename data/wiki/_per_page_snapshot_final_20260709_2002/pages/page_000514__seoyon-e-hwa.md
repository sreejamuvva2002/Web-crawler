# Seoyon E-HWA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-02-03

**Source:** [Hyundai supplier Seoyon E-HWA to build facility in chatham county](https://www.savannahnow.com/story/business/2023/02/01/hyundai-supplier-seoyon-e-hwa-to-build-facility-in-chatham-county/69862504007/)  ·  `page_000514`

## Facts

- Seoyon E-HWA plans to open a manufacturing facility in the Savannah area.
- The facility will be located in Chatham County.
- Georgia Gov. Brian Kemp announced on Wednesday that Seoyon E-HWA will invest nearly $76 million into the project.
- The investment funds a 700-plus employee factory at the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center near Bloomingdale.
- Seoyon E-HWA specializes in interior and exterior components such as headlinings and bumpers.
- The company manufactures these components for Hyundai vehicles.
- Operations are scheduled to begin in October 2024.
