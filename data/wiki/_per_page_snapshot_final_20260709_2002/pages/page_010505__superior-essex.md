# Superior Essex

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** 2015-07-08

**Source:** [Superior Essex Tours Pollution Control - Pollution Control Products Blog](https://www.pcpconline.com/blog/superior-essex-tours-pollution-control/)  ·  `page_010505`

## Facts

- Superior Essex is a global leader in the distribution and manufacture of wire and cable products.
- Pollution Control hosted a meeting and tour of its facility for Superior Essex, one of its major distributors.
- The tour was held in preparation for the Electrical Apparatus Service Association’s (EASA) 2015 Trade Show.
- Pollution Control Products showcased the latest burn-out cleaning technology at EASA in July.
- Twenty-five sales reps toured Pollution Control's sales and service offices, marketing offices, and both manufacturing warehouses.
- Reps received an in-depth look inside oven production and detailed knowledge of the entire burn-off process.
- Gary Lyon, principal engineer at Pollution Control, guided the tour.
- The tour was especially beneficial to new Essex reps who had just joined the company.
- Superior Essex has served as a distributor for Pollution Control's furnaces for over 40 years.
- When known as Brownell, Essex was one of Pollution Control's first company partners and was integral in the initial distribution of Pollution Control furnaces.
- Peyton Simpson, owner and founder of Pollution Control Products Company, established the partnership between the two companies.
