# Aspen Aerogels, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-14

**Source:** [Aspen Aerogels, Inc. stops construction of manufacturing facility in Statesboro](https://www.wtoc.com/2025/02/14/aspen-aerogels-inc-stops-construction-manufacturing-facility-statesboro/)  ·  `page_052655`

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels, Inc. has stopped construction of its planned second aerogel manufacturing facility in Register near the interchange of I-16 and US Highway 301.
- Hundreds of new jobs will no longer be coming to Bulloch County due to the halted project.
- The plant was touted by Georgia Governor Brian Kemp and the Development Authority of Bulloch County.
- The facility was expected to bring $325 million of investment when it was announced.
- The project was expected to create more than 250 manufacturing jobs when announced.
- The plant was intended to make thermal barriers that insulate the batteries on electric cars.
- In an investor call this week, officials with Aspen Aerogels said its existing facilities will instead increase their capacity.
- The company says it will be assessing options to relocate some equipment from the Register plant to upgrade and expand its existing plants.
- The Register site was being built on land owned by the Development Authority of Bulloch County, which negotiated local incentives for the project.
- The Development Authority of Bulloch County stated it is disappointed that Aspen Aerogels has indicated it will cease construction of the planned advanced manufacturing facility in Bulloch County.
- The Development Authority noted that taxpayer protection is a priority and clawback provisions are in place to recoup local investment should the project not move forward any further.
- Ricardo C. Rodriguez, Chief Financial Officer and Treasurer, stated that in early 2023, pre-empting a reset in EV demand expectations, Aspen Aerogels decided to right-time the construction of its planned second aerogel manufacturing facility in Statesboro, Georgia.
- Rodriguez noted the company subsequently ramped up its external manufacturing capacity for its Energy Industrial business.
- The company claims its external manufacturing model has fully demonstrated its ability to efficiently increase aerogel supply.
- Aspen Aerogels believes a capital-light and modular capacity plan provides the most efficient path to creating value.
- Rodriguez stated the company is fully funded by a strong balance sheet that enables it to evaluate opportunities including additional paths for organic and inorganic growth, optimizing the capital structure, and returning capital to shareholders.
