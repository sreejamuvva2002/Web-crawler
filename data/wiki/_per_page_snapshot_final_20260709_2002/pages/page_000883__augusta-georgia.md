## Facts

- The city of Augusta in Georgia has 126 public charging station ports (Level 2 and Level 3) within 15km.
- 56% of the ports are level 2 charging ports.
- 71 total Level 2 stations exist in the area.
- 44% of the ports are Level 3 stations.
- 55 total Level 3 stations exist in the area.
- 32% of the ports offer free charges for electric cars.
- 40 total free charging stations are available.
- The main charging networks operating in Augusta are Tesla, ChargePoint, and Flash.
