# Hyundai Motor Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-05-20

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group – Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority](https://savannahjda.com/hyundai-motor-group/)  ·  `page_002806`

## Facts

- At a May 20, 2022 signing ceremony, Governor Brian P. Kemp and Hyundai Motor Group Chairman Chung Euisun announced that Hyundai Motor Group would open a state-of-the-art facility at the Bryan County Megasite.
- The initial investment amount announced on May 20, 2022 was $5.54 billion.
- The project represents Hyundai Motor Group's first state-of-the-art U.S. smart factory.
- The initial announcement included an estimated 8,100 new jobs for Georgia’s coastal region.
- At the time of the May 20, 2022 announcement, it was announced that Hyundai suppliers would invest approximately another $2 billion in the project.
- On Aug. 31, 2023, Hyundai Motor Group and LG Energy Solution announced an additional $2 billion in capital investment.
- The Aug. 31, 2023 announcement included another 400 new jobs.
- Following the Aug. 31, 2023 announcement, total capital investment reached $7.59 billion and total new jobs reached 8,500.
- To date, Hyundai Motor Group and its suppliers have announced $10 billon in capital investment.
- To date, Hyundai Motor Group and its suppliers have announced 15,716 new jobs.
- The Bryan County Megasite was purchased by the State of Georgia and the Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority (JDA) for such an investment less than a year before the announcement.
- The Bryan County mega site covers 2,923 acres.
- The site is located in Bryan County adjacent to Intestate 16.
- The site offers immediate access from I-95 and I-16 to 250 major metro areas including Atlanta, Birmingham, Charlotte, Memphis and Orlando.
- The single-largest and fastest-growing container terminal in the U.S., the Port of Savannah, is 28 miles from the site.
- The Port of Savannah offers two Class I rail facilities.
- The site was purchased in 2021 through a partnership between the State of Georgia and the Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor JDA.
- The Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor JDA includes Bryan County, Bulloch County, Chatham County, and Effingham County.
- In choosing Georgia, Hyundai Motor Company cited speed-to-market, workforce, and the state’s ability to meet company carbon neutrality standards.
- Georgia boasts a large existing network of Hyundai subsidiaries and suppliers.
