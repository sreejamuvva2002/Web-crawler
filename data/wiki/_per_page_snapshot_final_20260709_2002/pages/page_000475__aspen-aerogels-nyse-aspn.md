# Aspen Aerogels (NYSE: ASPN)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2022-02

**Source:** [Manufacturing company to open facility in Bulloch Co.](https://valdostatoday.com/business-2/2022/02/manufacturing-company-to-open-facility-in-bulloch-co/)  ·  `page_000475`

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels plans to open an advanced manufacturing facility in Bulloch County, Georgia creating over 250 manufacturing jobs.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Aspen Aerogels (NYSE: ASPN) plans to construct the facility at Bulloch County’s Southern Gateway Commerce Park near Statesboro.
- Aspen intends to invest a minimum of $325 million and create more than 250 advanced manufacturing jobs at the new site.
- Production at the new Georgia facility is expected to begin in late 2023.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp stated that Aspen Aerogels has put confidence in Georgia, its people, and the growing electric mobility sector in the state.
- Combined with Aspen’s original East Providence, Rhode Island plant, the planned Georgia facility is designed to triple Aspen’s nameplate aerogel capacity.
- The expansion supports Aspen’s thermal barrier expansion plans in the fast-growing electric vehicle (EV) market.
- Aspen aims to capitalize on increased demand for its Aerogel Technology Platform™ as EV OEMs adopt PyroThin® aerogel thermal barrier solutions.
- Don Young, President and CEO of Aspen, noted the company is pleased to site, develop, and construct a PyroThin materials plant in Georgia.
- The City of Statesboro and Bulloch County region offer access to efficient ports, a strong available workforce, and secure low-cost utilities and critical raw materials.
- Aspen is increasingly being asked to provide broader solutions for improved battery performance and safety centered on PyroThin materials that provide thermal management, mechanical stability, and fire protection properties to EV battery systems.
- Aspen plans to build a 500,000-square-foot advanced manufacturing facility situated on a 90-acre parcel in southeast Georgia.
- The company plans to begin hiring personnel in the second half of 2022 across skilled manufacturing operators, advanced process engineers, quality and maintenance technicians, and professional support roles.
- Billy Allen, Vice Chair of the Development Authority of Bulloch County, welcomed Aspen Aerogels to the Statesboro-Bulloch County community and region.
- Allen cited great site and logistics advantages as reasons for Aspen's decision, citing the region's commitment to innovation in the electric mobility ecosystem.
- Allen expressed confidence that education resources including Georgia Southern University and Ogeechee Technical College will provide critical support to help Aspen flourish.
- Assistant Director Alyce Thornhill represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce division on this project in partnership with the Development Authority of Bulloch County, Georgia Quick Start, and Georgia EMC.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson welcomed Aspen Aerogels’ innovative, sustainable solutions for the electric vehicle and energy storage markets to Georgia.
- Wilson stated they look forward to Georgia employees collaborating with Aspen’s network of world-class scientists, engineers, and additional skilled workforce to solve mobility challenges.
- Southern Gateway Commerce Park is a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) Certified park requiring due diligence and specific certification requirements prior to prospective business visits.
- Aspen is a technology leader in sustainability whose aerogel technology enables customers to achieve objectives around resource efficiency, electrification, and sustainability.
- Aspen’s PyroThin® thermal barrier products enable solutions to thermal runaway challenges within the electric vehicle and energy storage markets.
- The company’s carbon aerogel program seeks to increase the performance of lithium-ion battery cells to enable EV manufacturers to extend driving range and reduce EV costs.
- Aspen’s Spaceloft® sustainable building materials provide industry-leading energy efficiency and fire safety to building owners.
- Cryogel® and Pyrogel® products are valued by the world's largest energy infrastructure companies.
- Aspen’s strategy is to partner with world-class industry leaders to leverage its Aerogel Technology Platform™ into additional high-value markets.
- The company is headquartered in Northborough, MA, and manufactures products at its East Providence, R.I. facilities.
