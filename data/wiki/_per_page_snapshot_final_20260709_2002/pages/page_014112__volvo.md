## Facts

- Swedish giant Volvo is considering Georgia, along with other Southern states, for a possible new manufacturing plant.
- It is the second major automotive plant Georgia is pursuing.
- Georgia has also been in the mix with other states and potentially European nations for a Jaguar Land Rover factory.
- No deals have been signed with Volvo or U.K.-based Jaguar Land Rover.
- Decisions on the potential factories could be weeks away.
- A person with knowledge of the situation told The Atlanta Journal-Constitution about the status of the pursuit.
- A spokeswoman for the Georgia Department of Economic Development had no immediate comment.
- Volvo declined to comment on the reports.
