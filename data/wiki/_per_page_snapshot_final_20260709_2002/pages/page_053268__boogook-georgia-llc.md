# Boogook Georgia, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Boogook Georgia, LLC - MBCIA](https://mbcia.com/boogook-georgia-llc/)  ·  `page_053268`

## Facts

- Boogook Georgia, LLC selected Macon for its first U.S. facility.
- The company is a subsidiary of South Korea-based Boogook Industries.
- Boogook Georgia is redeveloping an existing site at the Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority (MBCIA) Industrial Park on Weaver Road.
- The redevelopment represents a $6.8 million investment.
- The company specializes in fluid-carrying systems for vehicles.
- Major automotive clients served include Hyundai and Kia.
- The new location will enhance distribution capacity and align with the area's proximity to Hyundai Motor’s Metaplant America in Savannah and Kia’s West Point manufacturing facility.
- Site improvements are planned with a target launch in 2024.
- The expansion is expected to generate 80 new full-time jobs.
- New job fields include management, manufacturing, assembly, quality assurance, and logistics.
- Mayor Lester M. Miller stated that the facility selection underscores the region's growing significance in the automotive supply chain.
- Stephen Adams, Executive Director of the MBCIA, noted that Macon’s strategic location with rapid access to major highways and an established logistics network makes it a premier destination for expansion.
- The Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority and the Middle Georgia region are set to benefit from the new roles and the company's specialized industry expertise.
