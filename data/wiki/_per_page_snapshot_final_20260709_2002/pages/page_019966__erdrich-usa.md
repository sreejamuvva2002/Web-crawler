## Facts

- Erdrich provides manufacturing processes for Tier 1 automotive customers at their 125,000 sq. ft. facility in Dublin.
- Erdrich is a global company with $300 million in annual sales.
- The company operates 5 locations worldwide.
- Erdrich employs approximately 1,700 employees world-wide.
- They produce electronic parking brake systems via advanced automated technologies that process metal forming from a raw to a finished state.
- The facility tour showcases new machining and assembly processes.
- The showcased manufacturing processes include the operation of press machines ranging from 500T to 800T.
