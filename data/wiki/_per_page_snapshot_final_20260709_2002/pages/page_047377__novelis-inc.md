## Facts

- Novelis Inc. announced last week that it has chosen Atlanta for its global executive headquarters.
- Novelis Inc. is to operate 37 facilities in 12 countries throughout North and South America, Europe and Asia.
- Gov. Sonny Perdue spoke during a press conference at the Georgia Capitol regarding the announcement.
