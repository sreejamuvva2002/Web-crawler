# Peterson Spring Corporation

## Facts

- Peterson Spring Corporation is a century-old manufacturer of engineered metal products based in Kalamazoo, Michigan.
- The spring manufacturer lowered expenses by 6% by optimizing procurement with AFFLINK.
- AFFLINK’s Industrial Business Development Manager Carter Crutchfield led the initiative alongside Ship-Pac’s John Lewis and Anthony Markos to service accounts in the packaging, safety, and MRO channels.
- AFFLINK utilized its proprietary ELEVATE approach to analyze savings across product and process for Peterson Spring.
- The analysis established universal business needs, provided cost-saving product solutions, and confirmed savings through order consolidation.
- Corporate headquarters are located in Southfield, Michigan.
- ELEVATE explorations were conducted at the Athens, Georgia location.
- A Site Needs Analysis and Impact Report for the Athens, Georgia location realized up to 6% in product and efficiency savings across packaging, safety, and janitorial categories.
- An in-depth Supply Chain Analysis secured an additional $8,000.00 in savings for the Athens, Georgia headquarters.
- Thornton Brothers of Athens, Georgia was brought on board as a supplier to service the account.
- The Packaging Wholesalers of Schaumburg, Illinois was also brought on board as a supplier to service the account.
- The growth of the Peterson Springs national program has the Midwest and Canada branches on the radar for future cost savings.
