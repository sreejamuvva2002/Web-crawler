# Honda Motor Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-03-31

**Source:** [ANNUAL REPORT](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/715153/000119312524163995/d767050d20f.htm)  ·  `page_002721`

## Facts

- Launched the PROLOGUE ZDX co-developed with General Motors Company (GM) in North America.
- Plans to launch a mid-to large-size EV incorporating a new E&E architecture based on Honda’s proprietary EV platform in 2025 in North America.
- Plans to launch the first model of the Honda 0 Series in North America.
- Honda Development and Manufacturing of America, LLC is a U.S.A.-based subsidiary responsible for Research & Development and Manufacturing with 100% ownership.
- American Honda Motor Co., Inc. coordinates subsidiaries' operations, research & development, manufacturing, and sales in the U.S.A. with 100% ownership.
- Automobile revenue reached ¥13,567.5 billion in North America during the fiscal year ended March 31, 2024.
- Honda Group Unit Sales for automobiles in North America reached 1,628 thousand units during the fiscal year ended March 31, 2024.
- Marysville, Ohio facility employs 5,912 people and manufactures Automobiles.
- East Liberty, Ohio facility employs 2,556 people and manufactures Automobiles.
- Lincoln, Alabama facility employs 4,544 people and manufactures Automobiles and engines.
- Greensburg, Indiana facility employs 2,380 people and manufactures Automobiles.
- Anna, Ohio facility employs 2,469 people and manufactures Engines.
- Greensboro, North Carolina facility employs 795 people and manufactures Aircraft.
- Burlington, North Carolina facility employs 96 people and manufactures Aircraft engines.
- Honda targets a 30% sales ratio of electrified products for automobiles by 2030.
- Honda aims to reduce the CO2 emissions intensity of product use for automobiles by 27.2% by 2030 compared to FY2020.
- Honda plans to introduce innovative environmental technologies, such as electrification, to become carbon neutral and diversify energy sources.
- Regulatory updates include amendments to electric vehicles safety requirements.
- Regulatory updates include amendments to connection sets for conductive charging of electric vehicles – Part 1: General requirements.
- Regulatory updates include amendments to specifications for battery electric passenger cars.
- Regulatory updates include amendments to test methods for power performance of hybrid electric vehicles.
- Regulatory updates include amendments to test methods for power performance of battery electric vehicles.
- North America is identified as a major geographic area including the United States, Canada, and Mexico.
- Honda's transition risk response includes increasing sales through the expansion of electrified products and services.
- Advanced Safety Equipment Application Rate targets 100% for automobiles in developed countries using Honda SENSING 360 by 2030.
- Honda plans to formulate and revise business continuity planning (BCP), implement countermeasures through training, and promote a stable production structure via a robust supply chain.
