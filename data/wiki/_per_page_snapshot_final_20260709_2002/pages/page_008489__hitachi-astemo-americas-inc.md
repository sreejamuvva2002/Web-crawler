# Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-28

**Source:** [Clark Named CEO, President of Hitachi Astemo Americas - The BRAKE Report](https://thebrakereport.com/clark-named-ceo-president-of-hitachi-astemo-americas/)  ·  `page_008489`

## Facts

- Tim Clark has been appointed CEO and president of Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc.
- Clark is a senior vice president and executive officer of Hitachi Astemo, Ltd.
- Clark previously served as president of Hitachi Astemo Europe, based in Munich.
- Paul Carroll recently was named global account manager for Hitachi America, Ltd., succeeding Clark.
- Clark previously served as vice president of sales and marketing for Hitachi Automotive Systems in Farmington Hills, Michigan.
- Clark previously was general manager in charge of international sales for Hitachi Automotive Systems based in Tokyo.
- Prior to joining Hitachi in 2003, Clark was general manager of Unisia North America.
- Clark previously held executive positions at Nissan North America and with Mazda in Japan.
- Clark is fluent in Japanese and holds a bachelor’s degree in Japanese Studies from Brigham Young University.
- Hitachi Astemo Americas has more than 20 manufacturing plants, research-and-development centers and sales offices in the U.S., Canada, Mexico and Brazil.
- Hitachi Astemo was created in 2021 by the merger of Hitachi Automotive Systems with three former Honda affiliates: Nissin Kogyo, Keihin and Showa.
- Hitachi Astemo is a joint venture between Hitachi, Ltd. and Honda Motor Co. headquartered in Tokyo.
- Hitachi Astemo develops, manufactures, sells, and services automotive and transportation components as well as industrial machinery and systems.
- Hitachi Astemo Americas manufactures and markets engine management, electric powertrain and integrated vehicle controls for major automotive manufacturers worldwide.
- Hitachi Astemo adheres to ISO/TS:16949:2009 industry standards.
