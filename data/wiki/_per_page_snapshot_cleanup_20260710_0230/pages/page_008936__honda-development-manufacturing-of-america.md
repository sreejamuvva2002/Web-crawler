# Honda Development & Manufacturing of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [2026 Digital FactBook – North America Manufacturing](https://hondanews.com/en-US/releases/digital-factbook-north-america-manufacturing)  ·  `page_008936`

## Facts

- Honda is investing over $1 billion to retool the Marysville Auto Plant, East Liberty Auto Plant and Anna Engine Plant to create the flexibility to build gas-powered, hybrid, and battery-electric models on the same production lines.
- The Marysville Auto Plant in Ohio produces the Accord, Acura Integra, Accord hybrid, and Type S variant, with an annual capacity of 220,000 vehicles, employment of 4,400 workers, and a total investment of $5.5 billion.
- The Marysville plant performs subassembly of the hybrid battery unit for the Accord hybrid and is the exclusive global production facility for the Acura Integra.
- The East Liberty Auto Plant in Ohio produces the CR-V, CR-V hybrid, Acura MDX, and MDX Type S, with an annual capacity of 240,000 vehicles, employment of 2,600 workers, and a total investment of $1.9 billion.
- The East Liberty plant is part of the company’s retooling efforts to support flexible manufacturing of gas-powered, hybrid and battery-electric vehicles on the same production line in the Honda Ohio plants.
- The Performance Manufacturing Center in Marysville, Ohio, began production of the Honda Fastport eQuad micromobility delivery vehicle in April 2016 and the CR-V e:FCEV hydrogen fuel cell electric vehicle in 2024.
- The Anna Engine Plant in Ohio produces engines for multiple Honda hybrid models, including the 2.0-liter Atkinson-cycle four-cylinder engine, and began production of a 3.0-liter turbo V6 engine for Acura Type S models in 2021 as part of a $200 million plant expansion.
- The Alabama Auto Plant in Lincoln, Alabama, produces the Pilot, Passport, Odyssey, Ridgeline, TrailSport models, and their V6 engines, with an annual capacity of 340,000 vehicles, employment of 4,500 workers, and a total investment of $3 billion.
- The Indiana Auto Plant in Greensburg, Indiana, began production of the Civic Hatchback hybrid following a $50.2 million investment in September 2021 to prepare the plant for mass production.
- The Georgia Transmission Plant in Tallapoosa, Georgia, produces state-of-the-art 10-speed automatic transmissions for the Honda Pilot, Passport and Odyssey and the Acura MDX, with an annual capacity of 375,000 V-6 transmissions, employment of 480 workers, and a total investment of $485 million.
- The Georgia plant expanded from 100,000 to 514,000 square feet in 2017 and invested an additional $100 million for a new assembly line and other modifications.
- The Ohio Transmission Plant in Russells Point produces two-motor hybrid systems that help power the Honda Accord hybrid, Civic hybrid and CR-V hybrid trims, with an annual capacity of 500,000 hybrid power systems and employment of 1,200 workers.
- L-H Battery Company, Inc., a joint venture between Honda and LG Energy Solution established in 2022, is located in Jeffersonville, Ohio, and began production of pouch-type lithium-ion batteries for EVs and hybrids in 2026 with a projected investment of $4.4 billion.
- The L-H Battery Company facility spans over 2 million square feet and will also produce Energy Storage Systems alongside hybrid batteries.
- Honda Canada Manufacturing plants in Alliston, Ontario, announced an investment of $1.38 billion over six years in 2022 to upgrade manufacturing plants in preparation for production of electrified vehicles, starting with the CR-V hybrid.
- Total U.S. vehicle production reached 32,903,583 units as of 2025, with the Accord reaching 12,674,388 units and the CR-V reaching 2,995,166 units.
- Total U.S. hybrid battery production for the Accord hybrid reached 449,786 units as of 2025, while CR-V hybrid production reached 691,897 units.
- Multiple Honda U.S. facilities including Marysville, East Liberty, Anna Engine, Alabama, Indiana, Georgia Transmission, Ohio Transmission, and Canada plants earned the 2025 U.S. Environmental Protection Agency ENERGY STAR Certification.
- Several Honda U.S. facilities operate as zero-waste-to-landfill plants, including the Performance Manufacturing Center, Alabama Auto Plant, and Georgia and Ohio Transmission Plants.
