# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-15

**Source:** [Tredegar Announces Bonnell Aluminum Leadership Changes](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260601669747/en/Tredegar-Announces-Bonnell-Aluminum-Leadership-Changes)  ·  `page_029590`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation.
- Tredegar Corporation trades on the New York Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol TG.
- Brook Hamilton, President of Bonnell Aluminum, will retire in July 2026 after more than two decades of leadership and service.
- Carl Czarnik has been promoted to Senior Vice President and General Manager of Bonnell, effective June 15, 2026.
- Mr. Hamilton has led Bonnell since 2013, following a career with the company that began in 2005.
- Over his tenure, Hamilton played a pivotal role in shaping the organization’s strategy, strengthening its culture, and building lasting customer relationships, helping position Bonnell as a respected leader in the U.S. aluminum industry.
- Mr. Czarnik joined Bonnell in 2019 as Vice President of Operations.
- As Senior Vice President and General Manager, Czarnik will be responsible for Bonnell’s profit and loss performance, operational execution, and strategic direction.
- Prior to joining Bonnell, Czarnik served as Vice President of Operations at Gerdau Long Steel and Vice President of Human Resources for Gerdau North America.
- Mr. Czarnik served in the U.S. military from 1987 to 1994, including active duty during Operations Desert Shield and Desert Storm, and was honorably discharged as a Captain after earning the Army Commendation Medal for his distinguished service.
- Dr. Arijit (Bapi) DasGupta is president and CEO of Tredegar Corporation.
- Dr. DasGupta stated that Hamilton’s leadership has been instrumental in building Bonnell into the strong, respected organization it is today, and expressed deep gratitude for his contributions over the years.
- Dr. DasGupta added that Czarnik’s proven leadership, operational discipline, and track record of driving performance position him well to lead Bonnell forward and capitalize on the opportunities ahead.
- Bonnell Aluminum is a leading manufacturer of custom-fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions serving the building and construction, automotive and transportation, consumer durables, machinery and equipment, electrical and distribution markets.
- Branded product lines include TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum and Futura Transitions by Bonnell Aluminum.
- The Company has manufacturing facilities located in Newnan GA, Carthage TN, Niles MI, Elkhart IN, and Clearfield, UT.
