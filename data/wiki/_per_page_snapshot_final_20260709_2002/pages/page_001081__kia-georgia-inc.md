## Facts

- Operates as Kia Motors Corporation's first manufacturing site in North America.
- Located in West Point, Georgia.
- The facility sits on about 2,200 acres.
- Began mass production on November 16, 2009.
- Produces the Sorento, the Telluride, and the K5 mid-size sedan.
- Has the capacity to assemble up to 360,000 vehicles per year from U.S.- and globally sourced parts.
- Operates within the motor vehicle manufacturing industry.
- Serves as a major production hub for Kia in North America.
- Supports thousands of jobs.
- Sits within Georgia's automotive ecosystem.
- Recent developments in 2026 include a collaboration with The Ray C. Anderson Foundation to support infrastructure around the West Point test track.
