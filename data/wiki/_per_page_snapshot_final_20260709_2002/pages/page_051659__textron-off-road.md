# Textron Off Road

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2017-03-21

**Source:** [New Textron Off Road Brand Reflects Heritage of High Performance](https://www.ammoland.com/2017/03/new-textron-off-road-brand-reflects-heritage-high-performance/)  ·  `page_051659`

## Facts

- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a Textron Inc. company, announced the transition of its Bad Boy Off Road brand to Textron Off Road.
- The announcement originated from Augusta, GA.
- Side-by-side models in the current line remain the same; vehicles with the new Textron Off Road branding will begin to appear in authorized dealerships by April 2017.
- The Textron Off Road brand features gasoline-powered Stampede and Stampede XTR side-by-sides.
- The Textron Off Road brand features all-electric Recoil, Recoil iS, and Recoil iS Crew models.
- The Textron Off Road brand features the hybrid Ambush iS model.
- Textron Off Road pioneered the category of electrically powered 4x4 vehicles used for hunting and outdoor recreation due to their silent, odorless operation and environmentally friendly, emissions-free drivetrains.
- The brand name change signifies the strong engineering and manufacturing legacy Textron has in industries such as aerospace, defense, and automotive.
- John Collins, vice president of consumer for Textron Specialized Vehicles, stated that the new name reflects detailed design, precision engineering, manufacturing expertise, and high performance, demonstrating how serious Textron is about its future in the powersports market.
- Changes to the company's website and social media platforms were complete and online as of the publication date.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. is a leading global manufacturer of golf cars, utility and personal transportation vehicles, snowmobiles, side-by-sides, all-terrain vehicles, professional turf-care equipment, and ground support equipment.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company that leverages its global network of aircraft, defense, industrial, and finance businesses to provide customers with innovative solutions and services.
