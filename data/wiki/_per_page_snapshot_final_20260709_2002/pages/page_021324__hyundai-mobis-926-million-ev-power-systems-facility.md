# Hyundai Mobis $926 million EV Power Systems Facility

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-06

**Source:** [$926 million Hyundai EV systems facility coming to Georgia - Drive Tesla](https://driveteslacanada.ca/news/926-million-hyundai-ev-systems-facility-coming-to-georgia/)  ·  `page_021324`

## Facts

- The county of Bryan in Georgia will host a $926 million plant for Hyundai Mobis.
- Hyundai Mobis will employ more than 1,500 workers at the new plant to make Electric Vehicle Power system parts.
- Construction will start in January 2023 and be completed in 2024.
- The site is located in Belfast Commerce Park in Richmond Hill.
- The new plant joins another related project already announced in Georgia.
- Joon Georgia earlier announced a $317 million plant in Bulloch County.
- The Joon Georgia plant will employ 630 people to supply Hyundai Motor Group’s Metaplant America.
- Hyundai Mobis already has a plant in Georgia that employs about 1,200 workers.
- The existing Hyundai Mobis site has been operating since 2009.
- The existing plant supplies Kia Georgia with cockpit and chassis modules, among other things.
- At full capacity, the 1,200,000 sq-ft Bryan plant will produce more than 900,000 EV Power Electric systems per year.
- At full capacity, the new plant will produce 450,000 Integrated Charging Control Units per year.
- Gov. Brian Kemp commented on the effect of the new plant on the state’s economy.
- Gov. Kemp stated that celebrating the groundbreaking of Hyundai’s new electric vehicle and battery manufacturing facility in Bryan County showed it would unleash transformational job creation and investment in that entire region.
- Gov. Kemp noted they are announcing the second supplier to locate in that area in just two weeks.
- Gov. Kemp expressed excitement about their impact on surrounding communities and the growing list of other job creators that will soon follow.
- Days ago, the Hyundai Ioniq 6 made its US debut.
- The Hyundai Ioniq 6 debut was followed by the launch of the Hyundai Home ecosystem for charging EVs at home.
