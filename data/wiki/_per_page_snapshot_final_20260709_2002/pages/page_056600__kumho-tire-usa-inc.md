# Kumho Tire USA, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-09-09

**Source:** [Kumho Tire Appoints Yong-Sik Shin as Head of America Business Division - Tire Review Magazine](https://www.tirereview.com/kumho-tire-yong-sik-shin-head-americas/)  ·  `page_056600`

## Facts

- Kumho Tire USA, Inc. appointed Yong-Sik Shin to a newly created role as Head of America Business Division.
- The appointment ties together Kumho Tire’s three main subsidiaries in North America from a leadership standpoint.
- Shin will lead sales and overall operations for Kumho Tire USA, Inc., Kumho Tire Canada, and the Kumho Tire Georgia Plant.
- Shin spent the last 11 years of his career leading Kumho Tire’s international sales, marketing, and logistics initiatives for both OE and RE markets.
- Shin's latest role within the company prior to this appointment was serving as head of global sales.
