## Facts

- Freightliner Trucks is an indispensable part of the North American haulage sector.
- In weight classes 5 to 8, Freightliner trucks serve a wide range of truck applications.
- Freightliner is the top-selling truck brand in North America in weight classes 6-8.
- State-of-the-art technology, superb efficiency and optimum safety make Freightliner trucks a compelling proposition for customers.
- Freightliner Trucks is a Daimler Truck North America brand with production locations in the United States and Mexico.
- The Freightliner New Cascadia® is the flagship in the long-distance haulage segment.
- The vocational segment portfolio includes a wide range of customizable vehicles for numerous applications.
- Fleet managers and independent companies associate Freightliner with efficient, reliable vehicles used day-in, day-out even in grueling conditions.
- With the eCascadia and eM2 models, Freightliner offers fully electric medium and heavy-duty trucks for the North American market.
- The brand's product portfolio includes natural gas and hybrid models as further alternative drive technologies for specific customer requirements.
