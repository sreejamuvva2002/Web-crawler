# Rizon

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-11-25

**Source:** [Daimler Truck’s Rizon Brand Expands US Dealership Network - TT](https://www.ttnews.com/articles/daimler-truck-rizon-expands)  ·  `page_053593`

## Facts

- Daimler Truck announced Nov. 24 that its Rizon brand has expanded its sales network into Arizona, Washington, New York and New Jersey through new dealership partnerships.
- Rizon is the company’s newest and ninth brand, building medium-duty Classes 4 and 5 electric trucks.
- The brand has been growing steadily since its debut at the ACT Expo in May 2023 in Anaheim, Calif.
- The brand added three dealers as it broadens its North American footprint: Diversified Truck and Equipment Sales in Arizona, Range Zero Emissions in Washington and Alta eMobility serving New Jersey and New York.
- Diversified has supplied commercial fleets since 1976.
- Range Zero focuses on electric vehicle support and incentive navigation.
- Alta eMobility, a division of Alta Equipment Group, provides end-to-end fleet electrification services.
- Rizon said its trucks are in use across industries from municipal services to hospitality and fuel delivery.
- Alex Voets, general manager of Rizon USA, stated that seeing Rizon trucks at work in such a wide range of industries underscores the practicality of electric trucks in everyday operations.
