# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-03-03

**Source:** [Rivian - the American-made Electric Vehicle: Designed and Assembled in the USA](https://www.llctlc.com/post/rivian-american-made-electric-vehicle)  ·  `page_060564`

## Facts

- Rivian is an American-born, designed, programmed, and assembled electric vehicle brand founded in 2009 by RJ Scaringe.
- The company's headquarters are located in Irvine, California.
- Rivian's first production facility is a repurposed Mitsubishi plant in Normal, Illinois.
- The Illinois plant assembles the R1T pickup truck, R1S SUV, and Amazon electric delivery vans.
- Rivian plans to build a second U.S. manufacturing plant in Stanton Springs North, Georgia.
- The new Georgia facility will produce the R2 and R3 models, which are smaller, more affordable electric SUVs aimed at mainstream consumers.
- In January 2025, Rivian secured a $6.6 billion loan from the U.S. Department of Energy to fund the new Georgia plant.
- The first phase of production at the Georgia facility is set to begin in 2028.
- The initial capacity for the Georgia plant will be 200,000 vehicles per year.
- A second phase at the Georgia facility will eventually double this output capacity.
- By 2030, Rivian estimates the Georgia expansion will generate 7,500 permanent operational jobs in manufacturing, engineering, and logistics.
- The Georgia expansion will also create 2,000 construction jobs.
- Rivian's expansion strategy aims to increase domestic EV production capacity, create thousands of American jobs, strengthen the U.S. EV supply chain, and make EVs more accessible and affordable.
- At its Normal, Illinois factory, Rivian stamps its own aluminum panels in-house to reduce reliance on third-party suppliers.
- Each Rivian battery pack consists of 7,776 cylindrical cells that are assembled and tested in-house.
- Rivian builds its own quad-motor drive units for superior off-road performance.
- The R2 mid-size SUV is expected to start around $45,000 and production will begin at the new Georgia factory in 2026.
- The R3 is Rivian's smallest and most affordable model, designed to compete with the Tesla Model Y and Ford Mustang Mach-E, and will be available in an off-road-focused "R3X" variant.
- Some parts and materials for Rivian vehicles are globally sourced, but design, engineering, and final assembly take place on U.S. soil.
