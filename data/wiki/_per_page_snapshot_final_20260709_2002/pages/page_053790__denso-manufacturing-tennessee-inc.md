## Facts

- DENSO Manufacturing Tennessee, Inc. (DMTN) is located in Maryville, Tennessee.
- DMTN focuses on the manufacture of inverters, instrument clusters, electronics (engine, safety, and device products), and body electronics (keyless entry, air conditioning control units, tire pressure monitoring systems and body control modules) for its global customers.
- The Maryville campus comprises more than 2.6 million square feet of manufacturing, warehouse and administrative floor space in 15 buildings on 175 acres of land.
- DMTN was established in 1988.
- The facility's main business field manufactures electrification and safety components, instrument clusters, and automotive electronic products.
- DMTN is located at 1720 Robert C Jackson Drive, Maryville, TN 37801 US.
- Chuntao Ye serves as President of DMTN.
- DENSO holds 100% ownership of DMTN.
- DMTN maintains ISO 14001:2015 Advanced Waste Management Systems certification (Certificate Number: E00030).
- DMTN maintains IATF 16949:2016 certification with DQS Certificate Numbers DMTN-101: 10000650, DMTN-201: 10000652, DMTN-202: 10000651, and DMTN-203/204: 10003548.
- DMTN maintains ISO 9001 2015 certification with DQS Certificate Numbers DMTN-101: 10000650, DMTN-201: 10000652, DMTN-202: 10000651, and DMTN-203/204: 10003548.
