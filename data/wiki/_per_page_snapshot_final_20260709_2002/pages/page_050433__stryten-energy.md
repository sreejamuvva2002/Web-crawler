## Facts

- Stryten Energy is a U.S. energy storage solutions manufacturer based in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- The company produces nearly 15 gigawatt-hours of energy storage annually through domestic manufacturing facilities.
- It develops solutions for transportation, essential power, and motive power applications.
- Stryten Energy is committed to environmental sustainability, achieving a 99% recycling rate for lead batteries.
- The company uses up to 90% recycled materials in its production.
- Its product portfolio spans advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium battery technologies for industries from automotive to military.
