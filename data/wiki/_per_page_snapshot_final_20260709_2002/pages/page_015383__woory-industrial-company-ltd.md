# Woory Industrial Company, Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-07

**Source:** [Electric Vehicle Parts Manufacturer Announces $18 Million Investment in Dublin-Laurens County - Tennessee Star](https://tennesseestar.com/news/electric-vehicle-parts-manufacturer-announces-18-million-investment-in-dublin-laurens-county/khousler/2023/06/08/)  ·  `page_015383`

## Facts

- Woory Industrial Company, Ltd., a Korea-based automotive parts manufacturer, recently announced it will establish its first U.S. manufacturing facility in Dublin, Georgia.
- The company develops and produces HVAC auto components for all types of vehicles, including electric, internal combustion engine, eco-friendly, and hydrogen-fueled cars.
- Georgia Governor Brian P. Kemp stated that Georgia is proud to be the first U.S. home of Woory Industrial as it moves closer to its goal of becoming the e-mobility capital.
- Governor Kemp noted that being No. 1 for business means creating opportunity in all parts of the state and expressed excitement about the new partnership in Dublin-Laurens County.
- The company’s new facility will be located at 404 Kellam Road in Dublin.
- According to a press release by the Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD), the facility will produce electrical heaters, control units, and actuators for electric vehicles (EV) and internal combustion carmakers in North America.
- Woory is making an $18 million investment for the new facility.
- The investment is expected to create more than 130 new jobs in Dublin-Laurens County.
- New operations at the facility are expected to begin towards the end of this year, according to GDEcD.
- Jungwoo Kim, CEO of Woory Industrial Company, stated that over the past 10 years, Woory has successfully grown into a specialized EV component company through continuous innovation.
- Jungwoo Kim added that Georgia will be a good opportunity for Woory to take another step forward as part of the vanguard of EV development.
- Jungwoo Kim expressed confidence that Woory's experience and technology will contribute to the prosperity of the community and thanked the partnership of the State of Georgia and the City of Dublin.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp welcomed Woory Industrial as the latest job creator helping Georgia become the e-mobility capital of the nation.
- Governor Kemp noted that the emerging EV industry continues to bring historic levels of new jobs and investment to communities across the state, thanks to Georgia's highly-skilled workforce and reliable infrastructure network.
- According to GDEcD, from July 1, 2022, to December 31, 2022, the automotive industry was the second-largest source of new jobs and investment in Georgia.
