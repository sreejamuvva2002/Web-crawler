# WorkSource Georgia Mountains

**Type:** workforce_training  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** 2022-10-06

**Source:** [October 2022 #MFGDay22 – Georgia Mountains Works](https://georgiamountainsworks.com/124-2/)  ·  `page_056259`

## Facts

- WorkSource Georgia Mountains celebrated National Manufacturing Day on October 6, 2022, to raise awareness about advanced manufacturing careers and prepare the next generation workforce for the future.
- In the Georgia Mountains region, 23 manufacturing employers offered tours during the month to over 700 students in local school systems including Dawson, Forsyth, Hall, Lumpkin, Stephens, Union, Gainesville City, and Mountain Education School Systems.
- ABB Motors and Mechanical Inc. in Flowery Branch, GA manufactures electric motors.
- Advanced Digital Cable in Blairsville, GA produces wires and cables.
- ASI Southeast Inc in Eastanollee, GA makes commercial partitions.
- Barbe America in Flowery Branch, GA produces antiadhesives.
- Fox Factory in Gainesville, GA manufactures performance defining systems.
- GAF Materials Corp. in Cumming, GA produces roofing materials.
- Georgia Poultry Laboratory Network in Gainesville, GA provides poultry related diagnostics.
- Haering Precision USA in Lavonia, GA manufactures automotive precision parts.
- Harris Products Group in Gainesville, GA produces gas apparatuses.
- Hawg Halters in Dahlonega, GA makes motorcycle parts and accessories.
- IMS Gear Georgia Inc. in Gainesville, GA produces gears, housing and assemblies.
- Kubota Manufacturing of America Corp in Gainesville, GA manufactures tractors and RTVs.
- LG-MRI in Alpharetta, GA produces outdoor digital displays.
- Mencom Corporation in Oakwood, GA makes electrical connectors.
- Patterson Pump in Toccoa, GA manufactures pumps.
- Performance Foodservice in Oakwood, GA handles food distribution.
- PPG Industries in Oakwood, GA produces latex paint.
- Siemens Automotive in Alpharetta, GA operates in the automotive sector.
- Solvay Specialty Polymers USA LLC in Alpharetta, GA manufactures polymers.
- Specialty Appliances in Cumming, GA runs an orthodontic laboratory.
- Steel Tech Industries LLC in Gainesville, GA produces fabricated structural metal.
- StruXure Outdoors in Dahlonega, GA makes outdoor products.
- Swagelok Georgia in Alpharetta, GA manufactures fluid systems.
- Wahoo Docks in Dahlonega, GA produces aluminum docks.
- ZF Gainesville LLC in Gainesville, GA operates in the automotive and industrial sectors.
- Manufacturing is #1 in Industrial Employment in the area with an average salary of over $55,000/year.
- The region’s high schools offer career pathways in Drafting & Design, Electrical, Logistics, Machine Operations, Manufacturing, Mechatronics, Metal Fabrication, Robotics, Technical Systems, Welding, and Engineering & Technology all of which can lead to careers in manufacturing.
- In the Georgia Mountains Region, manufacturing is #1 in Industry Employment at 16.4% as of 1Q22.
- The region’s average hourly wage for manufacturing was $26.75 as of 1Q22.
- The region had a 2.7% unemployment rate as of August 2022.
- WorkSource Georgia Mountains and Workforce Strategies Group, LLC created the Manufacturing Sector Strategy to collaborate with area stakeholders to align education and workforce training programs with the needs of manufacturing businesses.
- WorkSource Georgia Mountains, under the Georgia Mountains Regional Commission, administers federal Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act funds which encourages sector strategies and partnerships.
- WorkSource Georgia Mountains Region 2 includes the counties of Banks, Dawson, Franklin, Forsyth, Habersham, Hall, Hart, Lumpkin, Rabun, Stephens, Towns, Union, and White.
