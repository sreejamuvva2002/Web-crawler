# CelLink

## Facts

- Tesla supplier CelLink has received a $362 million loan from the US Department of Energy.
- The loan is intended to boost domestic manufacturing of lighter and more efficient flexible circuit wiring harnesses for automotive and other industries.
- The funding will be used to construct a new manufacturing facility in Georgetown, Texas.
- Construction of CelLink’s manufacturing facility is anticipated to create 165 construction jobs.
- When fully operational, the project is expected to create more than 1,200 permanent jobs.
- Wiring harnesses are key components of cars, acting as sets of wires and related equipment that relay information and carry electricity throughout vehicles.
- CelLink’s flexible circuit wiring harnesses are called “flex harnesses.”
- Flex harnesses are lighter, smaller, faster to produce, and can be less expensive than conventional wiring harnesses.
- CelLink’s technology is now in demand in the electric vehicle (EV) and general automotive markets.
- The technology also has applications across numerous other industries, including aviation, space, consumer electronics, and appliances.
