# Volvo Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-16

**Source:** [North American deal sees Volvo and Pilot Company up the ante on charging stations - TradeTrucks - Used Truck Sales](https://www.tradetrucks.com.au/north-american-deal-sees-volvo-and-pilot-company-up-the-ante-on-charging-stations/)  ·  `page_014316`

## Facts

- A new deal between The Volvo Group and North American-based service station group Pilot Company will make high-performing charging infrastructure for electric battery-operated vehicles publicly available.
- Volvo Group intends to enter a strategic partnership combining its market leadership and experience in medium and heavy-duty battery-electric trucks and charging solutions with Pilot Company’s extensive network of more than 750 Pilot and Flying J locations across North America to install high-performing charging infrastructure.
- Pilot Company intends to install high-performance charging infrastructure at selected Pilot and Flying J travel centres across the U.S.
- The installed charging infrastructure will be open to medium and heavy-duty vehicles of all brands.
- Volvo Group and its commercial truck brands in North America – Volvo Trucks and Mack Trucks – will identify selected Pilot and Flying J locations to be prioritized for high-performance charging.
- Location prioritization will be based on customer and driver needs, current and anticipated battery-electric truck density, and the availability of public funding to support infrastructure costs.
- The initiative will provide customers of any commercial truck brand with the necessary peace of mind to consider adopting electric trucks.
- The initiative aims to eliminate range anxiety for fleet customers.
- The initiative aims to give North American fleet customers the prerequisites to meet their environmental goals on their decarbonisation journeys.
- The intended partnership places Volvo Group and Pilot Company at the forefront of accelerating electrification and developing sustainable and reliable transportation solutions in North America for generations of battery-electric trucks to come.
