# Kia Georgia Manufacturing Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- West Point, Georgia facility is the only Kia manufacturing plant in the United States.
- The plant covers 2,200 acres with a total investment of $3.2 Billion.
- Manufactures the Telluride, Sorento, Sportage, EV6 and EV9.
- Currently produces 350,000 vehicles a year at this location.
- Operates 3 shifts a day, 24 hours a day.
- Ships vehicles to sales markets around the globe, including the United States, Canada, Mexico, the Caribbean, Middle East and Pacific nations.
- The Kia Georgia Training Center is jointly operated by Kia Georgia and Georgia Quick Start.
- The training center houses robotics, welding and electronics labs, classrooms and equipment for training on state-of-the-art programmable logic controllers.
- The Kia Georgia Training Center was a key part of Kia Georgia’s growth to more than 3,200 team members.
- The training center continues to provide world class training to the Kia Georgia team.

- Kia Georgia and every Team Member is committed to the prevention of injury and ill health through the continual improvement of the health and safety management system.
