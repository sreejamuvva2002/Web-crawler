## Facts

- Atlanta is home to Georgia-Pacific and other Fortune 500-caliber companies.
- Thirty-one Atlanta-based companies are featured on the 2022 Fortune 1000 list.
- Seventeen of those Atlanta-based companies are ranked among the Fortune 500.
- Georgia-Pacific and other Atlanta-based companies celebrated the 5th anniversary of Engage, a local startup accelerator that deploys capital and enterprise expertise from founding companies to help startups break corporate barriers.
- Since its inception, Engage has assisted 75 startups across 10 cohorts.
- The idea for Engage grew out of Atlanta's success as a headquarters city to help propel the city to the future and stay relevant in the business environment.
- Engage aims to establish Atlanta as the best place in the country for an enterprise-focused startup to do business.
- Seventy percent of Engage startups are Southeast-headquartered, with forty percent based in Atlanta.
- Some companies have relocated or opened an Atlanta office since joining Engage.
- Engage startups employ more than 1,300 people, with over 200 located in Atlanta.
- The Engage venture fund is comprised of 15 corporate partners and one university that invests in companies selected for the Engage Go-To-Market Program.
- Engage corporate partners include Chick-fil-A, The Coca-Cola Company, Cox Enterprises, Delta Air Lines, Georgia-Pacific, Georgia Power Foundation, Georgia Tech, Goldman Sachs, The Home Depot, Honeywell Connected Enterprise, Intercontinental Exchange (ICE), Inspire Brands, Invesco, Invest Georgia, UPS, and Wellstar Health System.
- Tech Square Ventures manages the Engage fund.
- Georgia-Pacific is a founding corporate partner of Engage and has had 19 contracts with startups from the past ten cohorts.
- Jim DiAndreth, Managing Director of GP Ventures, states that Engage selects companies based on whether their strategic focus area aligns with a business unit or capability within Georgia-Pacific.
- Working with Engage startups exposes Georgia-Pacific executives to the startup world and allows startups to receive feedback and potentially become a new customer for a Georgia-Pacific business unit.
- A benefit of being an Engage corporate partner is the knowledge sharing Georgia-Pacific receives from other partners, some of whom are its customers.
- Georgia-Pacific is currently conversing with six of the ten startups in the 10th cohort of Engage.
- Engage has helped 75 startups raise about $2.7 billion in funding.
- Civic Eagle creates cutting-edge software to make public policy data discoverable, interpretable, collaborative and machine readable.
- Coginti accelerates time to insights for the enterprise analytics community by letting them explore data where it resides and share best practices while collaborating on key business questions.
- Manifest Climate's intuitive climate risk planning solution helps corporations understand, manage and disclose their climate-related risks and opportunities and advance their climate-related financial reporting.
- Qualytics is a data quality platform that helps improve the accuracy of enterprise decision-making with real-time, automated anomaly detection and remediation.
