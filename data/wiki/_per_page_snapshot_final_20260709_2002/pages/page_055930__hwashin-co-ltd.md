# Hwashin Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-31

**Source:** [Hwashin Co., Ltd. - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/hwashin)  ·  `page_055930`

## Facts

- The Company manufactures automotive chassis parts, body parts, and powertrain products.
- Its major customers are the Hyundai Motor Group, including affiliates of Hyundai Motor and Kia Motors.
- Chassis products include front cross members type #, front cross members type H, rear cross members type #, rear cross members type H, front lower arms, rear CTBA, rear lower arms, rear trailing arms, rear assist arms, front upper arms, rear axle housing assembly, pedals, and fuel tanks.
- Body products include side outer reinforcements, fender aprons & members, center floors, rear floors, cowl completes, cowl cross bars, bumper rails, and sun roof reinforcements.
- Products for environmentally friendly vehicles and EVs include Al Front Cross Member (Subframe), Al Front lateral arms, Al rear lower arms, Al Front Compression (Tension) Arm, Battery Pack Cases, Digital Clusters, Electric Water Pump, Motor Control Unit, DC/DC Converter, and Body Control Unit.
- The company was established as Hwashin Industries in Oct. 1969.
- Hwa Shin Manufacturing Co., Ltd. was established in Jul. 1975.
- An R&D center was established in Aug. 1987.
- Construction of a new plant in Eonha, Yeongcheon City was completed in Jun. 1990.
- Construction of a new plant in Bong-Dong, Yeongcheon City was completed in Jan. 1993, and headquarters functions were moved to this facility.
- The company was listed on the Korea Stock Exchange in Jan. 1994.
- The corporate name was adopted as Hwashin Co., Ltd. in Mar. 1995.
- ISO9002 certification was acquired in Nov. 1995.
- QS9000 certification was acquired in Dec. 2000.
- Hwashin Automotive India Private Ltd. was established in Jan. 2002.
- Beijing Hwashin Automobile Parts Co.,Ltd. was established in Nov. 2002.
- Hwashin America Corporation was established in the USA in Feb. 2003.
- ISO/TS16949 and ISO14001 certifications were acquired in Dec. 2004.
- The Q1 Award from Ford India was received in Mar. 2005.
- Hwashin Fabricante de Pecas Automotivas Brasil Ltda was established in Brazil in Mar. 2010.
- Hwashin BHAP (Cangzhou) Automotive Parts Co., Ltd. was established in China in Jun. 2015.
- Hwashin BHAP (Chongqing) Automotive Parts Co., Ltd. was established in China in Nov. 2015.
- The Excellent Supplier Award from the Hyundai Motor Group was received in Jan. 2017.
- The operations of Sae Hwashin Co., Ltd. were acquired and merged in Jan. 2018.
- Thanh Cong Hwashin Ltd. Liab. Company was established in Vietnam in Jan. 2021.
- Hwashin Georgia Corporation was established in the United States in Jul. 2023.
- Yeongcheon High-Tech Park Factory was established in Feb. 2024.
- Hwashin Automotive Pune P.L. was established in India in Apr. 2024.
- As of Dec. 31, 2024, major shareholders include Global Auto Trading with a 16.43% interest ratio, Hwashin Precision Co., Ltd. with a 5.75% interest ratio, and individual shareholders with 5.40%, 5.20%, and 5.19% interest ratios respectively.
