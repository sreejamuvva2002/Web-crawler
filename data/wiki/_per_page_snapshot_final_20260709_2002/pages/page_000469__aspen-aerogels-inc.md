# Aspen Aerogels Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2025-02-17

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels Inc., a Massachusetts-based electric vehicle supplier, has canceled plans to build a manufacturing facility in Bulloch County near Statesboro.
- The company announced the cancellation during an investor call on Thursday.
- Instead of the Georgia plant, Aspen Aerogels will increase production capacity at its existing East Providence, R.I., facility.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp originally announced the Statesboro project in 2022.
- The canceled project was planned to involve a $325 million investment.
- The canceled project was expected to create more than 250 jobs.
- Production at the canceled Statesboro plant was originally expected to begin in late 2023.
- Aspen Aerogels manufactures thermal barriers designed to insulate electric car batteries from fires.
