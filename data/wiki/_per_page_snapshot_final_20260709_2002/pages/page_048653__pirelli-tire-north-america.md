# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2004-10-29

**Source:** [Pirelli, Yokohama Announce Price Increases - AftermarketNews](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/pirelli-yokohama-announce-price-increases/)  ·  `page_048653`

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America is based in Rome, Ga.
- Pirelli Tire North America announced that it is increasing prices on all of its passenger and light truck/SUV tires by between 3 and 5 percent.
- The price increase will take effect this December.
- The increase was due to escalating raw material costs.
- Pirelli SpA announced a global 3 to 5 percent price increase on all types, sizes and brands of tires the company makes around the world.
- Yokohama Tire Corp. is increasing prices across the board on its consumer, commercial and OTR equipment tires effective Dec. 31.
- Prices on passenger and light truck/SUV tires from Yokohama will increase 3 to 4 percent, while commercial lines and OTR tire lines will increase by 3 to 5 percent.
- Yokohama cited rising business costs, particularly rising raw material costs, for the price adjustments.
