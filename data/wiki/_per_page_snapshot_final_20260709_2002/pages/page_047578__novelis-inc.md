# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-15

**Source:** [Novelis to Support New U.S. Government Child Savings Accounts Program :: Novelis Inc.](https://investors.novelis.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/1419/novelis-to-support-new-u-s-government-child-savings-accounts-program)  ·  `page_047578`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is a leading sustainable aluminum solutions provider and the world leader in aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Novelis announced it will match up to $1,000 for eligible employee contributions to the U.S. government's new children's savings account program, also known as "Trump Accounts."
- Steve Fisher serves as president and chief executive officer of Novelis Inc.
- Trump Accounts provides a $1,000 contribution from the U.S. Treasury into a tax-advantaged account for eligible children born in the U.S. between 2025 and 2028.
- Novelis maintains a longstanding charitable focus on strengthening future generations through education and workforce development programs.
- Novelis is the first aluminum manufacturer to join dozens of other companies supporting the Trump Funds.
- U.S. Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent stated that Trump Accounts are a transformative policy initiative and noted Novelis's support alongside other leading companies.
- Novelis is a global leader in the production of innovative aluminum products and solutions and the world's largest recycler of aluminum.
- Novelis aims to be the leading provider of low-carbon, sustainable aluminum solutions and achieve a fully circular economy by partnering with suppliers and customers in the aerospace, automotive, beverage can, and specialties industries across North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- Novelis reported net sales of $17.1 billion in fiscal year 2025.
- Novelis is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited, an industry leader in aluminum and copper.
- Hindalco Industries Limited is the metals flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group, a multinational conglomerate based in Mumbai.
