## Facts

- Ascend Elements is creating a domestic supply of critical minerals and engineered lithium-ion battery materials through “urban mining” and the innovative Hydro-to-Cathode® direct precursor synthesis technology.
- The company is working toward higher rates of Li-ion battery recycling by improving recycling economics.
- Ascend Elements returns 100% of critical battery active materials back into the domestic supply chain.
- The company preserves and uplifts critical materials for the batteries that power the energy transition.
- Ascend Elements transforms lithium-ion battery material production by establishing an efficient supply chain using recycled feedstock.
- Its patented Hydro-to-Cathode® direct precursor synthesis technology produces new cathode materials from spent lithium-ion cells more efficiently than traditional methods.
- The company's approach results in reduced cost, improved performance, and lowered process emissions.
- Ascend Elements launches a truly closed-loop circular battery economy through its operations.
