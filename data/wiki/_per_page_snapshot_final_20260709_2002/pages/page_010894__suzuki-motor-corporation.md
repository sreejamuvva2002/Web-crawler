# Suzuki Motor Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-01

**Source:** [Notice regarding Acquisition of All-solid-state Lithium-ion Battery Business from Kanadevia Corporation | GLOBAL NEWS | Global Suzuki](https://www.globalsuzuki.com/globalnews/2026/0304.html)  ·  `page_010894`

## Facts

- Suzuki Motor Corporation announced that it has entered into a business transfer agreement with Kanadevia Corporation to acquire Kanadevia’s all-solid-state lithium-ion battery business.
- The acquisition is effective July 1, 2026.
- Execution of the acquisition is subject to the fulfillment of customary closing conditions.
- Kanadevia began the development of all-solid-state lithium-ion batteries in 2006.
- Through its proprietary dry manufacturing process, Kanadevia’s all-solid-state lithium-ion batteries offer high safety, including the absence of liquid leakage, excellent environmental resistance, and operation across a wide temperature range.
- These characteristics make them particularly strong for specialized applications such as in aerospace or in high-temperature, vacuum conditions.
- Through this Business Acquisition, Suzuki will inherit and further develop the all-solid-state lithium-ion battery technologies cultivated by Kanadevia.
- The acquired business includes technology development, design, and sales of all-solid-state lithium-ion batteries.
- Financial performance, assets and liabilities of the acquired business are not disclosed.
- Acquisition price is not disclosed.
- Payment method for the acquisition is cash.
