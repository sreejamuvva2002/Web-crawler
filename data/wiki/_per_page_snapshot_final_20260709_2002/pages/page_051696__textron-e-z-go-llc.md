# Textron E-Z-GO LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-03

**Source:** [CONSUMERS CAN NOW BUILD AND BUY THEIR DREAM E-Z-GO® VEHICLE FROM THE COMFORT OF HOME](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/consumers-now-build-buy-dream-170300167.html)  ·  `page_051696`

## Facts

- Textron E-Z-GO LLC announced a new "Reserve Now" online feature that allows customers to customize and order E-Z-GO golf cars, personal-transport vehicles, and street-legal low-speed vehicles without an initial dealership visit.
- The online ordering system enables buyers to select from multiple color, seating, lighting, and accessory options to customize their vehicle.
- E-Z-GO designs and manufactures its vehicles at a facility in Augusta, Ga.
- Rob Scholl serves as the president and CEO of Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.
- The "Reserve Now" ordering process requires customers to use an online vehicle builder on the E-Z-GO website and place a $500 deposit.
- Custom vehicles are built to order and delivered to the nearest Authorized E-Z-GO Dealer for final preparation, payment, and customer pickup.
- Most custom orders will be fulfilled within eight weeks, while some models and option packages may require longer lead times.
- Customers can search nearby dealer inventories for available vehicles and request online quotes for new and used stock.
- The "Reserve Now" feature is applicable to E-Z-GO Freedom®, Liberty, street-legal Liberty LSV, Express® and Valor® models.
- Throughout 2025, E-Z-GO will release new special-edition vehicles featuring new colors and special options.
- The new Phantom limited edition vehicle is a pure-white Freedom RXV equipped with black premium seats, a modular top, tinted windshield, LED lights, a premium steering wheel, soundbar, and the IntelliGauge™ smart gauge system.
- E-Z-GO was founded in Augusta, Ga. in 1954.
- The company is recognized for its use of innovative sustainable electric-vehicle and powertrain technology.
- E-Z-GO's product lineup includes RXV® fleet golf cars; Freedom® and Valor personal golf cars; and E-Z-GO Express™ personal utility vehicles.
- The Liberty™ model is the industry's first vehicle to provide four forward-facing seats within a compact, golf-car-sized footprint.
- E-Z-GO became part of Textron Inc. in 1960 and currently operates as a Textron Specialized Vehicles business.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry corporation that utilizes a global network of aircraft, defense, industrial, and finance businesses to provide customer solutions.
