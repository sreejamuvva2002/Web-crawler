# Wabash National Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-09-09

**Source:** [Wabash National Corp. Announces Organizational Changes](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/wabash-national-corp-announces-organizational-changes/)  ·  `page_014626`

## Facts

- Wabash National Corp. announced organizational changes within its Composites business, which designs and manufactures composite panels and doors, trailer aerodynamic solutions, portable storage containers, and mobile shelters.
- Jeff Cole was appointed director of business development for Wabash Composites to lead all business development activities supporting the company's composite growth initiatives.
- Jeff Cole previously worked at Solvay with sales responsibility for its Industrial Business Division and has spent his career in the global composite materials industry serving aerospace, aviation, automotive, and wind energy markets.
- Frank Pendleton was appointed director of sales for Wabash Composites to lead the sales team toward strategic growth initiatives.
- Frank Pendleton previously served as vice president and general manager at Scott Intermodal in Jacksonville, Florida, and has over 25 years in transportation industry sales and operations roles.
- Joseph Perry was appointed sales manager for Wabash Composites to sell the product portfolio, including aerodynamic trailer solutions.
- Joseph Perry previously worked at Michelin and Ziegler Tire for 10 years, managing sales teams and key customer accounts.
- Blake Robertson was appointed director of manufacturing for Wabash Composites to lead manufacturing teams in Lafayette and Frankfort, Indiana.
- Blake Robertson previously spent 23 years in the extrusion industry, most recently as vice president and general manager for the U.S. Midwest Region at Sapa.
