# Hyundai Motor Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-08-26

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group Increases U.S. Investment to $26 Billion to Accelerate Growth and Innovation](https://www.hyundai.com/worldwide/en/newsroom/detail/hyundai-motor-group-increases-u.s.-investment-to-$26-billion-to-accelerate-growth-and-innovation-0000001003)  ·  `page_006085`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group announced an increase in U.S. investment to USD 26 billion between 2025 and 2028.
- This new commitment represents an additional USD 5 billion on top of a USD 21 billion allocation announced in March 2025.
- The investment is dedicated to advancing strategic initiatives in automotive, steel, and robotics.
- A new steel mill will be built in Louisiana to support strategic U.S. industries such as automotive manufacturing, strengthening local supply chains and industrial resilience.
- Hyundai Motor Company and Kia Corporation will substantially expand their U.S. auto production capacity.
- The Group will establish a state-of-the-art robotics facility with an annual capacity of 30,000 units in the U.S., serving as a hub for design, manufacturing, testing, and deployment.
- Hyundai Motor Group expects to create around 25,000 new direct jobs in the United States over the next four years.
- The Group is accelerating technology commercialization through its U.S.-based tech affiliates Boston Dynamics for robotics and Motional for autonomous driving.
- The Group is expanding collaboration with leading American companies in future technologies, including AI, robotics, and autonomous driving.
- Since entering the U.S. market in 1986, Hyundai Motor Group has invested more than USD 20.5 billion.
