# E-Z-GO (Textron Specialized Vehicles)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2017-01-26

**Source:** [E-Z-GO® Launches ELiTE™ Series | Dynamic Drives & Design](https://dynamicdrivesdesign.com/e-z-go-launches-innovative-elite-series-vehicles-to-industry/)  ·  `page_051700`

## Facts

- E-Z-GO, a Textron Specialized Vehicles business, launched its ELiTE Series lithium golf cars as a fleet offering on January 26, 2017.
- The ELiTE Series vehicles are activated by Samsung SDI lithium technology and feature zero-maintenance batteries backed by a five-year unlimited amp-hour warranty.
- E-Z-GO vehicles are designed and manufactured in Augusta, Ga. by Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT) company.
- Michael R. Parkhurst, Vice President, Golf for Textron Specialized Vehicles, stated that the partnership with Samsung SDI resulted in a giant step forward for high-efficiency vehicles and zero-maintenance battery power.
- The ELiTE Series vehicles are powered by hundreds of Samsung SDI lithium cells loaded into a single battery pack controlled by an advanced Battery Management System that monitors efficiency, temperature, state of charge, and health.
- These batteries eliminate the need for watering, terminal post checkups, and cleaning required by traditional lead-acid batteries, reducing maintenance time and increasing vehicle uptime.
- The ELiTE Series vehicles are 59 percent more efficient than the Club Car Precedent and 52 percent more efficient than the Yamaha Drive AC.
- The vehicles support opportunity charging, allowing courses to plug them in for quick sessions between rounds to rapidly restore energy, significantly reducing overall charging time compared to lead-acid batteries.
- Golf course managers can cut energy costs and increase revenue from all-day uptime due to the lower power requirements for charging ELiTE Series vehicles compared to leading lead-acid competitors.
- The ELiTE Series vehicle batteries are half the size and a fraction of the weight of traditional lead-acid batteries, which reduces turf damage and soil compaction caused by vehicle weight.
- E-Z-GO tested the ELiTE Series vehicles at Tijeras Creek Golf Club in Rancho Santa Margarita, Calif., where 73,000 rounds of golf were played last year on challenging hilly terrain.
- Rob Heslar, Director of Golf at Tijeras Creek Golf Club, reported that the ELiTE Series vehicles operate for 36 to 54 holes a day without needing to be recharged during that timeframe.
- The exclusive ELiTE lithium technology will be available in six 2017 models: RXV® ELiTE, Freedom® RXV ELiTE, Freedom RXV 2+2 ELiTE, TXT® ELiTE, Freedom TXT ELiTE, and Freedom TXT 2+2 ELiTE.
- The ELiTE Series vehicles were scheduled for display during the PGA Merchandise Show in Orlando, Florida.
- E-Z-GO was founded in 1954 in Augusta, Georgia, and became part of Textron Inc. in 1960, operating today within the Textron Specialized Vehicles division.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles markets vehicles under multiple brands including E-Z-GO®, Cushman®, Textron Off Road, Jacobsen®, Dixie Chopper®, Ransomes®, TUG™, Douglas™, Premier™, and Safeaero™ for environments ranging from golf courses to airports and theme parks.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company leveraging aircraft, defense, industrial, and finance businesses, known for brands such as Bell Helicopter, Cessna Aircraft Company, and E-Z-GO.
