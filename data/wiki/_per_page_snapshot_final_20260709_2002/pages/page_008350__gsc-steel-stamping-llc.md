## Facts

- GSC Steel Stamping, LLC is a minority- and family-owned automotive parts manufacturer.
- The company has acquired Dixien, LLC's assets, continuing its 43-year history of automotive parts stamping.
- GSC Steel Stamping is led by CEO Vincent Henderson.
- CEO Vincent Henderson is known for turning around award-winning operations.
- The company is committed to excellence, investing in technology, and maintaining a highly experienced staff.
- GSC Steel Stamping operates a 200,000 sq. ft. facility.
- The company's facility is located in Lake City, GA.
- GSC Steel Stamping is recognized as one of the world's largest minority-owned automotive stamping plants.
