# Sewon America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-01-29

**Source:** [Sewon Drives Positive Growth: An Update on Rincon’s Largest Economic Development Project | Effingham County Development Authority](https://effinghamindustry.com/news/article/sewon-drives-positive-growth-an-update-on-rincons-largest-economic-development-project)  ·  `page_049404`

## Facts

- Sewon America first announced an investment of more than $300 million in February 2023 to construct its new facility.
- The facility is located at Grande View Industrial Park on Old Augusta Road in Rincon, Effingham County, with a dedicated address of 1000 Sewon EV Road.
- In June 2023, a delegation from South Korea visited the site and signed a memorandum of understanding with Savannah Technical College to create specialized training for future workers.
- Lucas Jeung Gi Lee, President/CEO of Sewon America, stated that Effingham County was chosen for its existing infrastructure, proximity to the new Hyundai Metaplant, and ability to hire high-quality workers.
- Proprietary machinery, including state-of-the-art robotics for making electric vehicle parts, is being assembled in South Korea.
- The new equipment will enable the production of lighter aluminum components instead of steel, reducing final vehicle weight and spark output during welding for safer manufacturing.
- The equipment and a team of assemblers from South Korea are scheduled to be sent to the U.S. for installation at the Rincon facility in February.
- Sewon America Director of Human Resources Robert Vazquez visited multiple plants in South Korea and described the company culture as high quality and professional.
- The first cohort of students completed a week-long training program at Savannah Technical College in January.
- Standout students from each cohort will be selected for additional opportunities, with thirty high achievers primed for leadership through further training at Sewon’s LaGrange facility.
- The hiring process has begun for 50 technical positions required for the production of the first round of parts.
- Vazquez noted that because this type of manufacturing is new to the area, they are seeking candidates with great learning capacity and commitment to quality across HR, IT, safety, quality, production line, engineering, and maintenance roles.
- Once the facility is complete, Sewon America-Rincon will employ 740 people at its Effingham County location.
- The total number of jobs created by Sewon in Georgia will exceed 1,600.
- To date, Sewon has donated $40,000 to the Effingham County School District, Savannah Technical College, City of Rincon, and Effingham County Board of Commissioners to support infrastructure and educational outcomes.
- The company is partnering with the student mentorship program within the Effingham County School District to build a talent pipeline starting in middle school.
- Sewon plans to deliver high-quality products to the new Hyundai Metaplant as its first electric vehicles roll off the production line.
- The company hopes to deliver its first shipment in October 2024 and be fully operational in 2025.
- Due to the rapid construction timeline of the Hyundai Metaplant, no groundbreaking ceremony was held for Sewon's project, but a grand opening will be held once production begins.
- Effingham County Industrial Development Authority CEO Brandt Herndon stated that the partnership with Sewon will bring a prosperous era for the county and its residents.
