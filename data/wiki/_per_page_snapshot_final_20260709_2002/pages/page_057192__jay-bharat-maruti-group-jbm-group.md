# Jay Bharat Maruti Group (JBM Group)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-02-25

**Source:** [Jay Bharat Maruti: Jay Bharat Maruti Group acquires Germany's Linde-Wiemann - The Economic Times](https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/markets/stocks/news/jay-bharat-maruti-group-acquires-germanys-linde-wiemann/articleshow/68150643.cms?from=mdr)  ·  `page_057192`

## Facts

- The transaction — JBM Group’s biggest acquisition to date — was handled through group flagship Neel Metal Products Ltd.
- Group executive director Nishant Arya has taken charge as chairman of Linde-Wiemann.
- People close to the development said JBM Group will finance the transaction through internal accruals and debt, with fine details to be finalised next week.
- JBM supplies components to automotive manufacturers such as Bajaj Auto, Ford, and Ashok Leyland.
- The acquisition stands to benefit JBM from new synergies with Linde-Wiemann in terms of new products, new global customers, and technologies.
- Linde-Wiemann supplies structural and safety components to auto manufacturers such as BMW, Volkswagen, Audi, Skoda, Porsche, Daimler, Ford, and PSA globally.
- Nishant Arya stated the company is very strong at working with new materials like aluminium hot forming, which is important for meeting new emission norms and new safety norms.
- Aluminium components make vehicles lighter and sturdier, improving their fuel efficiency and crash-test performance.
- While Indian auto manufacturers may find the cost of procuring these components high, they will bring down expenditure on honouring warranties, thus cutting down the total cost of ownership.
- Linde-Wiemann has 17 manufacturing facilities across eight countries and revenue of $700 million, or about Rs 4,976 crore.
- JBM Group’s consolidated revenue now stands at an estimated $2.2 billion, or about Rs 15,625 crore.
