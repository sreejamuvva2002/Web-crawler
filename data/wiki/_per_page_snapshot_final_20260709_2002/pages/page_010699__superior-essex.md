# Superior Essex

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-01

**Source:** [Superior Essex Becomes Sole Owner of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire Company - Wire & Cable India](https://www.wirecable.in/superior-essex-becomes-sole-owner/)  ·  `page_010699`

## Facts

- Superior Essex successfully completed the acquisition of the remaining minority interest in Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire from Furukawa Electric Company Limited.
- The acquisition transaction was effective April 30, 2024.
- Effective April 30, 2024, the completion establishes Superior Essex as the sole owner of the brand, assets, and operations previously conducted by the global joint venture with Furukawa Electric.
- Superior Essex plans to relaunch its magnet wire business under a new company name to be announced later in 2024.
- The company will continue to operate all current facilities of the Essex Furukawa joint venture worldwide.
- Existing facilities include magnet wire facilities in Japan and Malaysia (Kuala Lumpur) that were contributed by Furukawa Electric in October 2020.
- Daniel Choi serves as the CEO of Superior Essex.
- CEO Daniel Choi stated, “Over the last several years we have been able to accomplish many great things working with the team from Furukawa Electric. I am proud of what was created and thankful for their contributions.”
- The acquisition marks a significant milestone for Superior Essex, solidifying its control over global operations and reinforcing its commitment to delivering exceptional products and services to customers worldwide.
- With this acquisition, Superior Essex is well-positioned to continue its legacy of innovation and excellence in the wire and cable industry.
- CEO Daniel Choi added that Superior Essex plans to continuously respond to market demands in the xEV automotive industry, power, electrification, and renewable energy markets.
- The company aims to pave the way for future technologies as a global leader.
- Superior Essex will strengthen its position by actively pursuing decarbonization and sustainability.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, Essex Energy Italy, IVA, Lacroix + Kress, Hi-Wire, and Essex Brownell.
- Superior Essex is the leading global provider of magnet wire and is on the forefront of communications fiber application as well as Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- Superior Essex is instrumental in creating disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- The company is committed to pioneering smart building technology while also setting the pace on sustainability.
