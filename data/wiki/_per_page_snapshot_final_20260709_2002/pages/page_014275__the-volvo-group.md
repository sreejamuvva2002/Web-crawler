# The Volvo Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-11-14

**Source:** [Volvo Group Unveils Growth Strategy: North American Expansion, Service Growth in Focus | VLVLY Stock News](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/VLVLY/volvo-group-capital-markets-day-gearing-up-for-7q7c9tzyyfrr.html)  ·  `page_014275`

## Facts

- Volvo Group held its Capital Markets Day at the Volvo Trucks Customer Center in New River Valley, Virginia, USA on November 14, 2024.
- President and CEO Martin Lundstedt outlined the company's strategic direction to improve performance and strengthen earnings resilience across the business cycle while continuing to invest and accelerate growth through transformation.
- The Volvo Group aims to increase market shares, particularly in North America, and expand its global service business.
- The company emphasizes a commitment to sustainable transport solutions across all technologies, including internal combustion engines, battery-electric vehicles, and fuel cell-electric vehicles.
- Martin Lundstedt stated the company is evolving its performance culture based on a decentralized organization that empowers those closest to the customer to make decisions and drives continuous improvement across the Group.
- The Group is maneuvering from a position of strength based on a strong net financial position, a broad customer base with long-standing relationships, leading market positions in many markets, and a growing service business.
- Martin Lundstedt noted that leveraging partnerships will accelerate innovation efforts, while portfolio optimization and disciplined capital allocation will help drive value creation and earnings resilience.
- The company aims to deliver continued strong earnings and cashflow as a base for providing good total shareholder returns regardless of the speed of the transformation.
- Volvo Group's headquarters is located in Gothenburg, Sweden.
- The company employs more than 100,000 people.
- Volvo Group serves customers in almost 190 markets.
- In 2023, net sales amounted to SEK 553 billion (EUR 48 billion).
- Volvo Group offers trucks, buses, construction equipment, power solutions for marine and industrial applications, financing, and services that increase customer uptime and productivity.
- The company was founded in 1927.
- Claes Eliasson serves as the Head of Media Relations for the Volvo Group.
