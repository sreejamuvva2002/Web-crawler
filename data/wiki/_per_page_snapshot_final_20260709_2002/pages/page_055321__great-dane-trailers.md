## Facts

- Great Dane Trailers has announced the addition of Superstition Trailers to its North American distribution network.
- Superstition Trailers is headquartered in Phoenix, Ariz.
- Superstition Trailers operates two locations in Tucson and two locations in Phoenix.
- Superstition Trailers grew from a single location in 1994 to become the region's top distributor of trailers, parts, and service.
- Superstition Trailers employs more than 125 staff members.
- Superstition Trailers recently completed the renovation of a 43,000-square-foot facility in Phoenix.
- Superstition's service department is fully equipped to handle all types of trailer repair, fabrication, and installation.
- Superstition's service department offers certified brake and federal inspection as well as mobile repair service.
- The name "Superstition" was adopted from the Superstition mountain range located east of the Phoenix metro area.
- Great Dane Trailers manufactures dry van, refrigerated, and platform trailers.
- Great Dane Trailers has headquarters in Savannah, Ga., and Chicago, Ill.
- Great Dane Trailers operates nine manufacturing plants in the United States.
