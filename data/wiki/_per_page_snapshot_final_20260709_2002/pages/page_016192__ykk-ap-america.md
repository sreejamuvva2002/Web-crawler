# YKK AP America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2022-08-18

**Source:** [Incentives Unknown as Georgia Announces Expansion at YKK AP Facility - Tennessee Star](https://tennesseestar.com/the-south/georgia/incentives-unknown-as-georgia-announces-expansion-at-ykk-ap-facility/tcsquare/2022/08/18/)  ·  `page_016192`

## Facts

- Atlanta-based YKK AP America, a subsidiary of Tokyo-based YKK AP, plans to invest up to $125 million to expand its middle Georgia facility.
- The expansion will create 100 additional jobs at the Bibb County manufacturing facility.
- State officials declined to divulge whether the state offered any incentives for the expansion or what it might cost Georgia taxpayers.
- Jessica Atwell, a Georgia Department of Economic Development communications specialist, confirmed the project is located in a Tier 1 tax credit zone.
- Under the Tier 1 credit program, companies that create at least two jobs are eligible for a $4,000 tax credit per new job.
- Companies may use 100% of the Tier 1 tax credit toward their tax liability.
- Georgia Department of Economic Development Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that YKK AP has a strong history of growth and success in Georgia.
- YKK AP opened its first branch in Atlanta in 1986.
- YKK AP America established its manufacturing presence in middle Georgia five years after 1986.
- The company has expanded its presence in Georgia and built relationships with local communities over the years.
- Companies in Georgia's advanced manufacturing industry, like YKK AP, played a key role in bringing jobs and opportunities to the state, creating the second most new jobs in Georgia last year.
- The expansion project involves adding machinery and equipment at the facility located in the Macon-Bibb I-75 Industrial Park.
- YKK AP currently supports 250 employees in Macon.
- YKK AP America president Oliver Stepe said the new facility will help introduce further vertical integration of vinyl extrusion, glass, and window production.
- The expanded operations will allow the company to create enhanced efficiencies to manufacture high-quality windows and doors for homebuilders and homeowners.
