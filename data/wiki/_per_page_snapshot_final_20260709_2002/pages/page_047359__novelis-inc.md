# Novelis Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2025-08-15

**Source:** [Novelis' Tender Offer Results for Senior Notes Announced - Investors Hangout](https://investorshangout.com/novelis-tender-offer-results-for-senior-notes-announced-363793-/)  ·  `page_047359`

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. completed a cash tender offer for its 3.250% Senior Notes due in November 2026.
- Approximately $738 million of the Senior Notes were validly tendered at the close of the offer.
- The tendered amount represents 98.4% of the $750 million total principal amount currently outstanding.
- Successful participants will receive a tender offer consideration of $997.50 for every $1,000 principal value of the notes.
- Holders will also receive accrued interest from the latest semi-annual payment leading up to the settlement date.
- Novelis plans to proceed with the redemption of the notes not purchased during the tender offer in accordance with governing indenture rules.
- BNP Paribas Securities Corp. serves as the Dealer Manager for this tender offer.
- Novelis Inc. is a frontrunner in the aluminum production sector, aiming to become the top provider of low-carbon aluminum solutions and achieve a fully circular economy.
- The company partners with various industries, including automotive and aerospace, to deliver innovative solutions tailored to meet the evolving needs of its global clientele.
- During the latest fiscal year, Novelis achieved net sales of $17.1 billion.
