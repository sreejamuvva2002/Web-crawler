# Essex Brownell

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Essex Brownell Announces Investment At McDonough, Georgia Distribution Center - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.ms/news/essex-brownell-announces-investment-at-mcdonough-georgia-distribution-center/)  ·  `page_010399`

## Facts

- On May 15, 2023, Essex Brownell announced it will expand its operational footprint in the Southeast United States by moving and expanding its distribution center in McDonough, Georgia.
- The new facility will be located within the same industrial complex as the previous location to ensure uninterrupted or accelerated service for customers.
- Essex Brownell is expanding its facility size from 25,000 square feet to over 133,000 square feet.
- The company will retain its current staff and create opportunities to double jobs at the new location.
- Rebecca Hunnicutt, Vice President of Operations & Customer Service, stated that the expansion opportunity was an easy choice due to additional benefits.
- Essex Brownell has been located in McDonough since 1999 and values its excellent staff and strong relationships with the local property management team.
- The expansion aligns with Essex Brownell's business goals of 'continuous investment in this region' and 'better support for our customers.'
- This marks the second operational expansion within one year, following the opening of a state-of-the-art facility in Reno, Nevada in the second half of 2022.
- Current customers serviced outside this location will not experience service disruptions, while others can expect increased service speed.
- The expansion avoids traditional growing pains like retraining or painful processes, making the company larger and closer to its customers.
- The move allows Essex Brownell to reduce post-pandemic supply chain redundancies and overlaps, ensuring the right materials are delivered at the right time and place.
- The expansion increases agility and speed in product delivery, which remains a long-term focus for the organization.
- Essex Brownell is an international distributor of magnet wire and related products used to build and repair motors, generators, transformers, and electrical components.
- In addition to discrete materials, Essex Brownell provides custom fabrication and rewinding services through its Essex Active conversion facility.
- Essex Brownell operates under the parent company Superior Essex, headquartered at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
