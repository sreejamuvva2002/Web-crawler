# Kia Motors Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2014-03-18

**Source:** [Byung Mo Ahn promoted to Vice Chairman of Kia Motors Corporation - Kia Georgia](https://www.kiageorgia.com/byung-mo-ahn-promoted-to-vice-chairman-of-kia-motors-corporation/)  ·  `page_015739`

## Facts

- Byung Mo Ahn was promoted to Vice Chairman of Kia Motors Corporation on March 18, 2014.
- Under Mr. Ahn's leadership, U.S. sales exceeded 500,000 units in both 2012 and 2013.
- Mr. Ahn will continue overseeing Kia Motors America (KMA) and Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia (KMMG).
- In his new role, Mr. Ahn serves as group vice chairman and CEO of KMA and KMMG.
- Mr. Ahn will lead the implementation of the brand's long-term strategy in the U.S. as the product portfolio, customer base, and units in operation continue to expand.
- Mr. Ahn previously served as the group president and CEO of KMA and KMMG since 2008.
- During Mr. Ahn's tenure, Kia's U.S. sales have nearly doubled.
- The West Point, Georgia-based manufacturing plant began production in 2009.
- The West Point, Georgia-based manufacturing plant has produced more than 1.2 million vehicles.
- In 2013, the Optima midsize sedan assembled at KMMG was KMA's best-selling vehicle.
- The Optima set an all-time, single-nameplate annual sales record for the brand at 155,893 vehicles in 2013.
- Sales of the iconic Soul urban passenger vehicle increased for the fourth consecutive year in 2013.
- 2014 marks Kia's 20th anniversary in the U.S.
- Mr. Ahn's promotion furthers the organization's efforts to concentrate on quality, strengthen the brand, elevate the ownership experience, and identify new segments for growth.
- KMA launched the Cadenza premium sedan in 2013.
- KMA is currently introducing the K900 luxury sedan, signaling the beginning of a new era for the brand in the United States.
- Mr. Ahn possesses more than 37 years of experience with the Hyundai Motor Group.
- From 1999 through 2001, Mr. Ahn served as president and CEO of KMA.
- Mr. Ahn previously served as executive vice president and COO of Hyundai Motor Company, responsible for overseas business operations including supervising the production activities of nine factories worldwide.
- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc. (KMMG) is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Motors Corporation based in Seoul, Korea.
- KMMG has an annual capacity of 360,000 units.
- KMMG is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Georgia.
- KMMG began mass production on November 16, 2009.
- KMMG produces the Sorento CUV and the Optima mid-size sedan, two of the brand's top-selling models in the U.S.
