# Vanguard National Trailer Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2008-02-13

**Source:** [Slowing orders hit another trailer maker – Indianapolis Business Journal](https://www.ibj.com/articles/9426-slowing-orders-hit-another-trailer-maker)  ·  `page_013231`

## Facts

- Vanguard National Trailer Corp. has reduced its operations from two shifts to one.
- Vanguard National Trailer Corp. did not cut workers during the shift reduction.
- Vanguard National Trailer Corp. joined other truck-trailer makers responding to a slowdown in the market.
- Vanguard National is a unit of China International Marine Containers Co. Ltd.
- Vanguard National's operations are located in the northwestern Indiana city of Monon.
- Wabash National Corp. of Lafayette cut 150 workers due to a decline in orders.
- The decline in orders is spreading across the trailer market.
- Great Dane Trailers of Savannah, Ga., laid off about 260 workers in the past month.
- Great Dane Trailers' layoffs occurred at plants in Brazil and Terre Haute.
