# Thomas Built Buses, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-12-08

**Source:** [Thomas Built Buses Expands Type A Minotour Production Capacity at Plant in High Point - School Transportation News](https://stnonline.com/industry-releases/thomas-built-buses-expands-type-a-minotour-production-capacity-at-plant-in-high-point/)  ·  `page_053611`

## Facts

- Thomas Built Buses (TBB) is a leading manufacturer of school buses in North America and a division of Daimler Truck Specialty Vehicles (DTSV).
- TBB is expanding its Type A Minotour production capacity at its legacy manufacturing plant located at 1408 Courtesy Road in High Point, N.C.
- The expansion builds on TBB's legacy in High Point, which has been home to the company's original facility dating back to 1916 when it first produced streetcars before evolving to build Type C, Type D, and now Type A buses.
- The project reflects TBB's streamlined production strategy following the relocation of Type D production to the Saf-T-Liner plant earlier this year.
- Work on the Type A expansion began in late 2024 and is expected to be completed by 2026.
- T.J. Reed, president and CEO of DTSV, stated that investing in the legacy facility honors more than a century of craftsmanship in High Point while continuing to modernize for the future.
- The expansion will give teams the capacity and tools needed to build even more Type A Minotours for customers across North America.
- The project includes a series of modernization efforts aimed at improving workflow, enhancing safety, and supporting long-term sustainability.
- The expansion will increase production capacity by more than 50 percent.
- Dedicated space for Minotour production has nearly tripled, now encompassing roughly 72,000 square feet of the building.
- Jeff Allen, senior vice president of operations and specialty vehicles at Daimler Truck North America (DTNA), stated that investing in North Carolina strengthens operations while ensuring the company can serve more customers with greater efficiency, sustainability, and innovation.
- Thomas Built Buses was founded in 1916 and is headquartered in High Point, North Carolina.
- TBB is a subsidiary of Daimler Truck North America LLC, which designs, engineers, manufactures, and markets medium- and heavy-duty trucks, school buses, vehicle chassis, and associated technologies and components under the Freightliner, Western Star, Thomas Built Buses, Freightliner Custom Chassis Corp, and Detroit brands.
- Thomas Built Buses and Freightliner Custom Chassis Corp together form Daimler Truck Specialty Vehicles.
- Daimler Truck North America is a subsidiary of Daimler Truck, one of the world’s leading commercial vehicle manufacturers.
