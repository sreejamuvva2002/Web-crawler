# EVCO Plastics

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- EVCO Plastics and Kistler have formed a partnership for zero-defect production in injection molding.
- EVCO Plastics is a globally recognized leader in injection molding and additional technologies.
- The family-run business EVCO Plastics was founded in Wisconsin in 1964.
- EVCO Plastics has a workforce of over 1,800 employees spread across eleven facilities in the US, China, and Mexico.
- Anna Bartz serves as the Director of Communications at EVCO Plastics.
- Following recent growth, EVCO Plastics operates more than 260 molding machines.
- EVCO Plastics provides technologies and services across the entire value chain, including automation, packaging, labeling, and more.
- Precision Molding is one of EVCO's four main divisions.
- EVCO's products and solutions cover diverse industries ranging from agriculture and powersports to packaging and consumer goods.
- EVCO explicitly does not produce automotive parts or implantable devices.
- Medical technology accounts for roughly 20 percent of EVCO's annual sales.
- Two EVCO sites are equipped with an ISO Class 8 Clean Room: the headquarters in Deforest, Wisconsin, and a production facility in Dongguan, China.
