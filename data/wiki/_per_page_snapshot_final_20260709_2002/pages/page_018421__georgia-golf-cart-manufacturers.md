# Georgia Golf Cart Manufacturers

## Facts

- Club Car and E-Z-GO manufacture both electric vehicles and gas-powered golf carts in Augusta, Ga.
- Yamaha manufactures electric vehicles and gas-powered golf carts in Newnan, Ga.
- Bridgestone Golf, Mizuno, and U.S. Kids Golf all manufacture and/or have headquarters in Georgia.
- Golf manufacturers represent $679 million in direct impacts providing 2,938 jobs and $150 million in direct wages.
- Total manufacturing output for the golf sector is valued at $1.1 billion including trickle-down effects.
