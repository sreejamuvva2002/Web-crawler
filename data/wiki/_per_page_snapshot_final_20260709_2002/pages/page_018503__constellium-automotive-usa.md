# Constellium Automotive USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-04

**Source:** [Constellium Automotive USA in Bartow County announces layoffs | Local News Now (WRGA AM)](https://www.wrganews.com/2026/06/04/constellium-automotive-usa-in-bartow-county-announces-layoffs/)  ·  `page_018503`

## Facts

- A manufacturing plant located in White has announced the layoff of more than half of its employees.
- According to a WARN notice from the Georgia Department of Labor, Constellium Automotive USA is laying off 57 of its 99 employees.
- The facility is located at 1 Constellium Drive in White.
- The notification classifies the event as a layoff or closure listed as a permanent reduction in force.
- Affected positions include Operator, Material Handler, Maintenance Tech, Quality Manager, EHS Specialist, Operations Supervisor, and Shift Supervisor.
- August 1st is the first date of separation for the laid-off employees.
- The plant opened in 2017 in Highland 75 Industrial Park.
- The facility produces lightweight aluminum structural components and crash management systems for automakers in the southeast and North America.
- Constellium N.V., headquartered in France and the Netherlands, is the parent organization that specializes in the design, development, and recycling of aluminum products for the aerospace, automotive, and packaging industries.
