# Grudem

## Facts

- Grudem, located in Segovia (Spain) and associated with SERNAUTO, opens its first production center in the United States in Albany, Georgia.
- Grudem operates as a Tier 1 & 2 supplier in the automotive industry.
- The company is a pioneer in the development and production of composites based on honeycomb structures, fundamentally used in the automotive industry.
- The new production center will generate more than 60 new jobs.
- The planned investment for the new facility is $5,000,000.
- Grudem focuses mainly on manufacturing load floors and upper trunk trays based on sandwich or composite polyurethane and honeycomb technology.
- This technology generates very lightweight parts with high mechanical resistance, allowing different geometries, thicknesses, and coatings.
- The new factory is located at 1113 Seminole Ln 31707 Albany.
- The facility covers approximately 3,000 square meters.
- The facility has the possibility to double its surface area.
- The plant will be equipped with thermoforming presses and carpet lamination/edging machines.
- The facility will supply substrates as well as semi-finished or fully finished parts.
