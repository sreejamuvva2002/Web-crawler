## Facts

- DSC is a company that has grown based on technology and trust.
- DSC specializes in seat frames.
- DSC was established in 1979.
- DSC holds TS16949 / ISO14001 certification, recognized for its quality and technology in automobile seats.
- DSC pursues 'a futuristic first-class company that respects humanity'.
- DSC is committed to global marketing and green management.
- DSC aims to become the world's leading car seat frame manufacturer.
- Domestic company sales were 5,079 in 2020, 5,706 in 2021, 6,606 in 2022, and forecasted at 6,890 for 2023.
- Overseas company sales were 4,148 in 2020, 5,097 in 2021, 7,087 in 2022, and forecasted at 8,336 for 2023.
- Total sales were 9,227 in 2020, 10,803 in 2021, 13,693 in 2022, and forecasted at 15,226 for 2023.
