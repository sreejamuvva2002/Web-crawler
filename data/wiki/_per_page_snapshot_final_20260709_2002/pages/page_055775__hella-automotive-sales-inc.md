# HELLA Automotive Sales Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-08

**Source:** [HELLA Automotive Sales Names VP of Sales, USA & Canada Aftermarket](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/hella-automotive-sales-names-vp-of-sales-usa-canada-aftermarket/)  ·  `page_055775`

## Facts

- HELLA Automotive Sales Inc. has appointed Robert Tinson as vice president of Aftermarket Sales for the U.S. and Canada, effective Dec. 5.
- Tinson brings more than 18 years of senior-level executive experience in the automotive aftermarket with a proven record of developing sales teams and strategies and cultivating customer relationships while reaching commercial targets.
- Over the past 10 years, Tinson led aftermarket sales in North America for Sensata Technologies and its aftermarket brand, Schrader TPMS Solutions.
- Tinson stated that as vehicle technology evolves, demand will increase for introducing and delivering highly innovative products into the aftermarket.
