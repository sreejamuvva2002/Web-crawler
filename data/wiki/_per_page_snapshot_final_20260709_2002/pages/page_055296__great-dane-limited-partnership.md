# Great Dane Limited Partnership

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Great Dane Trailers Breaks Ground on New Plant in Georgia | IndustryWeek](https://www.industryweek.com/expansion-management/industry-clusters/article/21940709/great-dane-trailers-breaks-ground-on-new-plant-in-georgia)  ·  `page_055296`

## Facts

- Great Dane Trailers broke ground on the site of its new refrigerated trailer manufacturing plant on Oct. 18.
- The selected site covers 118 acres in Statesboro, Georgia.
- Georgia Gov. Sonny Perdue, Statesboro Mayor Joe Brannen and Bulloch County Commission Chairman Garrett Nevil hailed cooperation among the state, local economic developers and Great Dane as key reasons for the success of the selection process.
- Bill Crown, President and CEO of CC Industries and CEO of Great Dane Limited Partnership, stated that keeping the new site close to the company's deep roots in Savannah was an important factor in the selection.
- Bill Crown noted that the project honors the heritage of excellence for which Great Dane's trailers have become known while breaking ground on a future for the company and the city of Statesboro.
- The plant will encompass 450,000 square feet, including office and showroom space.
- The facility will have the capacity to produce more than 5,000 trailers annually.
- The plant will employ more than 400 people.
- Construction is expected to get underway later this year to bring the plant into production in 2012.
- Gov. Perdue stated that Great Dane is a great example of advanced manufacturing companies continuing to find Georgia an advantageous place to locate and flourish.
- Gov. Perdue added that the groundbreaking marks the beginning of a partnership between the company and the Georgia workers who can help it thrive here.
