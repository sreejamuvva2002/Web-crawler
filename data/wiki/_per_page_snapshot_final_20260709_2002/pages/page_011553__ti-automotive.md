# TI Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-15

**Source:** [ABC Technologies Completes Acquisition of TI Fluid Systems; Combined Business Will Rebrand as TI Automotive - ABC Technologies](https://abcgroup.ca/abc-technologies-completes-acquisition-of-ti-fluid-systems-combined-business-will-rebrand-as-ti-automotive/)  ·  `page_011553`

## Facts

- The transaction brings together two highly complementary businesses to create a stronger, more diversified Tier 1 manufacturer supplying market-leading safety and performance critical products and highly engineered components.
- The combined business will have a propulsion agnostic portfolio positioned to support a diverse range of customers.
- Combined pro forma 2024 revenue is $5.4 billion.
- The combined workforce consists of 34,600 employees across 26 countries.
- TI Automotive will be led by ABC President & Chief Executive Officer Terry Campbell.
- An Executive Leadership Team combining the extensive skills and expertise of both ABC and TIFS will support Campbell.
- TI Automotive will be headquartered in Auburn Hills, Michigan.
- The business will be managed through a Regional Operating Model to ensure customer proximity and agility to respond rapidly to local market dynamics.
- Terry Campbell, President & CEO of TI Automotive, stated the combination creates opportunities to drive innovation and manufacturing excellence while establishing foundations for future growth.
- TI Automotive is a Tier 1 supplier of market-leading safety and performance critical products and highly engineered components to the global automotive industry.
- The company has more than 100 years’ experience solving customers’ safety, efficiency and environmental challenges.
- TI Automotive was created through ABC Technologies’ acquisition of TI Fluid Systems in April 2025.
- TI Automotive is majority owned by certain affiliated funds of Apollo Global Management, Inc. and its subsidiaries.
- Funds managed by Oaktree Capital Management, L.P. own a minority equity interest in TI Automotive.
