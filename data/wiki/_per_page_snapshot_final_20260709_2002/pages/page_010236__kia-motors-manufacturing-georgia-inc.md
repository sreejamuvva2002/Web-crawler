# Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2012-02-29

**Source:** [Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia Produces 500,000th Vehicle - Kia Georgia](https://www.kiageorgia.com/kia-motors-manufacturing-georgia-produces-500000th-vehicle/)  ·  `page_010236`

## Facts

- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc. (KMMG) celebrated production of the 500,000th vehicle at the West Point, Ga. automobile manufacturing facility on Feb. 29, 2012.
- KMMG began mass production of the Kia Sorento CUV on Nov. 16, 2009.
- The Kia Sorento CUV quickly became the brand’s best-selling product in the U.S.
- KMMG began production of the Kia Optima mid-size sedan on Sept. 2, 2011.
- The Kia Optima led the brand’s U.S. sales in December 2011 and January.
- Since opening, KMMG has added two additional shifts of production.
- More than 3,000 team members have been hired by the plant to date.
- In 2011, KMMG completed a $100 million expansion.
- The expansion pushed the plant’s total capacity to 360,000 units per year.
- Byung Mo Ahn, Group President and CEO for Kia Motors America and KMMG, said building 500,000 vehicles in just over two years is a milestone for their first U.S. manufacturing plant.
- Ahn stated that the commitment from each team member is a big reason they achieved 17 consecutive months of record sales.
- Vehicle number 500,000 was a snow white pearl Optima SX.
- The vehicle will be allocated to one of KMA’s more than 755 dealerships nationwide.
- KMMG is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Motors Corporation based in Seoul, Korea.
- KMMG is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Georgia.
- Beginning in 2012, the plant has the ability to build 360,000 vehicles annually from U.S. and globally-sourced parts.
