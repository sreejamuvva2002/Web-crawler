# voestalpine Metal Forming

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2012-08-27

**Source:** [Auto industry supplier voestalpine to open in Georgia](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/shopmanagement/auto-industry-supplier-voestalpine-to-open-in-georgia)  ·  `page_013861`

## Facts

- Automotive industry supplier voestalpine Metal Forming will build a $62 million manufacturing facility near Cartersville, Ga.
- The new facility will create 220 jobs.
- The plant will provide pressed parts in the body-in-white segment.
- The plant will also supply structural components.
- voestalpine Metal Forming operates under its automotive body parts unit.
- voestalpine Metal Forming is part of the Austria-based voestalpine Group, a steel processing and technology group that operates manufacturing and processing facilities worldwide.
- voestalpine Metal Forming operates business units including automotive body parts, tubes and sections, and precision strip.
