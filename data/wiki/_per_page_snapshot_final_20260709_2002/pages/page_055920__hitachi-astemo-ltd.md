# Hitachi Astemo, Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo, Ltd. has developed an autonomous driving technology that helps safely and smoothly pass by oncoming vehicles on narrow roads by utilizing 3D sensing results of the surrounding vehicle environment.
- The company aims to strengthen cost competitiveness through the use of 3D sensing data obtained from new stereo cameras with improved cost benefits instead of LiDAR.
- Hitachi Astemo is working towards collaborating with a high-precision, versatile new stereo camera launched in March 2023.
- The new stereo camera can detect pedestrians and bicycles with a high degree of accuracy and measure distances by combining long-range detection with a wide viewing angle.
- The stereo camera stores identification patterns in advance using machine learning techniques to contribute to collision prevention during right and left turns at intersections.
- Hitachi Astemo has created software processing that enables advanced image recognition and vehicle control on cost-competitive electronic control units by utilizing its AI and recognition technology.
- Collaborating with the new stereo camera will enable further enhancement of the cost competitiveness of autonomous driving technology that enables cooperative behavior in narrow roads.
- Hitachi Astemo’s autonomous driving technology using cooperative behavior in narrow roads and the new stereo camera will be introduced at the “Automotive Engineering Exposition 2023 Yokohama” (booth number: 148) held at Pacifico Yokohama from May 23.
- Hitachi Astemo is committed to strengthening its business through a strategic portfolio including Powertrain & Safety Systems, Chassis, Motorcycle, Software and Aftermarket businesses.
- Aiming for growth based on the pillars of “green,” “digital,” and “innovation,” Hitachi Astemo will contribute to a better global environment by developing xEV systems and highly efficient internal combustion engine systems that reduce emissions.
- The company delivers enhanced safety and comfort through autonomous driving systems, advanced driver assistance systems and advanced chassis systems.
