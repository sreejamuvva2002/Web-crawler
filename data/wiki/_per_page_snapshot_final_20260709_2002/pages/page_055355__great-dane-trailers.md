# Great Dane Trailers

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2006-09-12

**Source:** [Great Dane Trailers Adds New Dealer Location in Arizona - AftermarketNews](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/great-dane-trailers-adds-new-dealer-location-in-arizona/)  ·  `page_055355`

## Facts

- Great Dane Trailers announced the addition of Superstition Trailers to its North American distribution network.
- Superstition Trailers is headquartered in Phoenix, AZ.
- Superstition Trailers operates two locations in Tucson, AZ, and two locations in Phoenix.
- Superstition Trailers provides trailer sales, parts, and service.
- Chris Hammond, vice president of Dealer Sales at Great Dane Trailers, stated the partnership aims to better serve Great Dane customers in Arizona and the southwest market.
- Chris Hammond described Superstition Trailers as a first-class organization that will be a significant asset to Great Dane.
- Mark Otto is the owner of Superstition Trailers.
- Mark Otto stated becoming a Great Dane dealer is a great benefit for them and noted their commitment to offering high-quality trailers, parts, and service.
- Under Mark Otto's direction, along with his brother and partner Alan, Superstition Trailers grew from a single location in 1994.
- Superstition Trailers became the region’s top distributor of trailers, parts, and service.
- The company employs more than 125 staff members.
- Superstition Trailers recently completed the renovation of a 43,000 square foot, state-of-the-art facility in Phoenix.
- Sales manager Tom Meissner and his team of seven salespeople represent Great Dane’s complete product line.
- The sales team also represents other markets including tanks, dump trailers, lowboys, and agricultural equipment.
- Superstition’s service department handles all types of trailer repair, fabrication, and installation.
- The service department offers certified brake and federal inspection as well as mobile repair service.
- The name "Superstition" was adopted from the Superstition mountain range located east of the Phoenix metro area.
