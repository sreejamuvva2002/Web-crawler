## Facts

- Trenton Pressing’s $12 million expansion project (which began last spring) was officially marked complete with a ribbon cutting on June 28.
- The new facility adds 100,000 square feet to the automotive supplier’s manufacturing operations.
- Hans Pfunder (chief operating officer) stated pride in creating parts and products for some of the best-known brands in the world right here in Dade County, Ga., noting the facility will allow them to put even more products in the market thanks to local talent.
- Evan Stone (IDA executive director) emphasized retaining and growing their existing industrial base is high on the list of priorities in local economic development, stating that more jobs mean more opportunities for local talent to make a good wage, which creates economic mobility and success.
