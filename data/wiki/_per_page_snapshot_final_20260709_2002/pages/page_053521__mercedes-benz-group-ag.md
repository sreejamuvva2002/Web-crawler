# Mercedes-Benz Group AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-02-01

**Source:** [Daimler embarks on a new era as Mercedes-Benz Group | Mercedes-Benz Group > Company > News](https://group.mercedes-benz.com/company/news/daimler-becomes-mercedes-benz-group.html)  ·  `page_053521`

## Facts

- Daimler AG will become Mercedes-Benz Group AG on February 1, 2022.
- Following the successful stock market debut of Daimler Truck, the group's renewed focus is on the automotive business.
- The world-famous brand Mercedes-Benz was created in 1926 when the predecessor companies of Carl Benz and Gottlieb Daimler were merged with the aim of revolutionizing automobile production.
- The company's stock exchange symbol changes from »DAI« to »MBG», with no further changes for shareholders, and shares will remain listed on Germany’s blue-chip DAX stock index.
- The renaming underlines a renewed strategic focus on building the most desirable cars in the world.
- The company aims to take the lead in electric mobility and vehicle software.
- Ola Källenius serves as Chairman of the Board of Management of Mercedes-Benz Group AG.
- Daimler Mobility AG is adapting its brand to Mercedes-Benz Mobility AG, offering mobility services for passenger cars and vans in financing, leasing, and insurance.
- The finance and mobility division enables customers to use vehicles flexibly via rental and subscription models, fleet management, and digital services for charging and payment.
- The historic realignment was approved by shareholders with an overwhelming majority, completing a process that began last year.
- On December 10, 2021, Daimler Truck Holding AG was launched as an independent company on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange.
- The new Mercedes-Benz Group fully concentrates on its passenger car brands Mercedes-Benz, Mercedes-AMG, and Mercedes-Maybach as well as vans.
- Mercedes-Benz Group holds a minority stake of 35% in Daimler Truck Holding AG, with around 5% held in pension assets.
