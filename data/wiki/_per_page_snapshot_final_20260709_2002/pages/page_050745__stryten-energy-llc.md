## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- The company provides a broad range of energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military and Government sectors.
- Stryten Energy partners with some of the world's most recognized companies to meet the growing demand for reliable and sustainable energy storage capacity.
- The company's products power applications including submarines, subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains and trucks.
- Stryten Energy's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers and energy performance management software.
- Mike Judd serves as Chief Executive Officer and President of Stryten Energy.
