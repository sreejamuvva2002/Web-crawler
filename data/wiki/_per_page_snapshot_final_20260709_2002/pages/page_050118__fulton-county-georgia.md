# Fulton County, Georgia

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2024-07

**Source:** [Fulton County Soars to #4 Nationally for Economic Development Projects - Metro Atlanta CEO](https://metroatlantaceo.com/features/2024/07/fulton-county-soars-4-nationally-economic-development-projects/)  ·  `page_050118`

## Facts

- Fulton County, Georgia, moved from #11 last year to #4 in the national Top 20 Best Counties in America rankings.
- Fulton County achieved the #1 position in the I-85 Corridor Report featured in Site Selection Magazine's July issue.
- Site Selection Magazine, published by Conway Data, Inc., evaluated all 3,142 U.S. counties using its proprietary Conway Data Project Database based on new investments and job creation.
- Robb Pitts, Chairman of the Fulton County Board of Commissioners, stated that the recognition is a testament to collaborative work with partners at the State of Georgia, the region, and 15 cities.
- Kristi Brigman, Deputy Commissioner of Global Commerce for the Georgia Department of Economic Development, noted that diverse, well-educated talent and a collaborative ecosystem are fostering growth in tech and life sciences.
- Kristi Brigman highlighted that Georgia’s global gateways, such as Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport, facilitate access to U.S. and global markets.
- Recent major corporate wins for Fulton County include Adobe, SK Battery America, Amazon Music, Cummins, Resia Manufacturing, Microsoft, GCI Restaurant Group, Mastercard, Newegg Commerce, Nike, PrizePicks, and Cargill.
- These recent projects span technology, manufacturing, and finance sectors, strengthening the regional economy and creating job opportunities for Georgians.
- Fulton County is the most populous county in Georgia and is home to 15 distinct cities, including Atlanta.
- Samir Abdullahi, Director of Economic Development and Select Fulton, stated that moving up from #11 to #4 is a notable accomplishment and emphasized Select Fulton's mission to cultivate new job prospects and investment opportunities across all communities.
- SK Battery America is listed among the high-profile companies securing recent major wins in Fulton County.
