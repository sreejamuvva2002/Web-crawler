# Mr. Electric

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Social Circle, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Mr. Electric provides residential electric vehicle (EV) charger installation services in Social Circle, Georgia, offering a range of charger types, permitting assistance, and code-compliant electrical work.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Enables Georgia residents to install home EV chargers, supporting electric vehicle adoption and the state’s charging infrastructure ecosystem.

## Key facts

- Mr. Electric offers EV charger installation for garages, driveways, or carports, cutting charging times in half.
- The company provides a full range of chargers including hardwired high-amperage units, plug-in models, and smart Wi-Fi options with load sharing.
- Installation includes permit acquisition, electrical panel evaluation, dedicated 240 V circuit installation, and final inspection to ensure code compliance.
- Services feature whole-home surge protection, GFCI protection, weather-rated enclosures for outdoor chargers, and UL-listed components.
- Upfront per-project pricing is provided with no hidden costs.
- Local service professionals arrive in uniform, equipped to complete most jobs in a single day.
- 24/7 emergency electrical support is available year-round.
- The company emphasizes safety through thorough home electrical safety checks and adherence to code requirements.
- Mr. Electric can diagnose and repair existing EV charging stations, replacing cables, connectors, holsters, breakers, and other components.
- Customer reviews highlight professionalism, timely work, fair pricing, and friendly technicians Stephen and Kenny.
