# Aurubis AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2024-04-01

**Source:** [Aurubis to spend $340,000,000.00 to expand into new space in Augusta Georgia. - intelligence360](https://www.intelligence360.news/aurubis-to-spend-340000000-00-to-expand-into-new-space-in-augusta-georgia/)  ·  `page_017312`

## Facts

- Aurubis plans to invest $340,000,000.00 to build out new space in Augusta, Georgia.
- The company plans to occupy the new space at Augusta Corporate Park in Augusta on or about April 1, 2024.
- Aurubis AG is a leading global provider of non-ferrous metals and one of the largest copper recyclers worldwide.
- The company processes complex metal concentrates, scrap metals, and metal-bearing recycling materials into metals of the highest quality.
- Aurubis produces more than 1 million tons of copper cathodes annually.
- From its copper cathodes, Aurubis produces wire rod, continuous cast shapes, profiles, and flat rolled products made of copper and copper alloys.
- Aurubis also produces precious metals, selenium, lead, nickel, tin, and zinc.
- The company's product portfolio includes sulfuric acid and iron silicate.
- Sustainability is a fundamental part of the Aurubis strategy, with the company integrating sustainable conduct and business activities into its corporate culture.
- Aurubis has about 7,200 employees globally.
- The company operates production sites in Europe and the US, along with an extensive service and distribution system across Europe, Asia, and North America.
- Roland Harings serves as the Chief Executive Officer of Aurubis.
