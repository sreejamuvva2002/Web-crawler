# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-09-27

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation (NYSE: TG).
- Bonnell Aluminum is pleased with the U.S. Department of Commerce’s final ruling to institute anti-dumping and countervailing duties on unfairly traded imports of aluminum extrusions from 14 countries.
- The company manufactures custom-fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions.
- Bonnell Aluminum serves the building and construction, automotive and transportation, consumer durables, machinery and equipment, and electrical and distribution markets.
- Branded product lines include TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum and Futura Transitions by Bonnell Aluminum.
- The company has manufacturing facilities located in Newnan GA, Carthage TN, Niles MI, Elkhart IN, and Clearfield UT.
- Additional details regarding the Commerce Department's ruling were issued by The U.S. Aluminum Extruders Coalition on September 27, 2024.
