# Bonnell Aluminum

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-05

**Source:** [Bonnell Aluminum Commenting on Recent Trade Filing from Aluminum Extruders Coalition and United Steelworkers Union | Tredegar Corporation](https://ir.tredegar.com/news-releases/news-release-details/bonnell-aluminum-commenting-recent-trade-filing-aluminum/)  ·  `page_053058`

## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation (NYSE: TG).
- Bonnell Aluminum applauds the U.S. Aluminum Extruders Coalition and the United Steelworkers union for filing antidumping and countervailing duty petitions on October 4, 2023.
- The petitions target unfairly traded imports of aluminum extrusions from China, Colombia, the Dominican Republic, Ecuador, India, Indonesia, Italy, Malaysia, Mexico, South Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, Turkey, the United Arab Emirates, and Vietnam.
- The petitions were filed concurrently with the U.S. Department of Commerce and the U.S. International Trade Commission (USITC).
- Unfairly priced imports have materially injured the American aluminum extrusion industry since 2019.
- Producers in the subject countries used dumped and subsidized prices to gain significant market share in the United States at the direct expense of the U.S. industry and thousands of USW workers.
- Increasing volumes of dumped and subsidized imports have prevented U.S. extruders from capitalizing on market growth, causing declines in production, sales, profitability, and market share.
- Brook Hamilton, president of Bonnell Aluminum, stated the company is determined to end the surge of unfairly dumped and subsidized imports flooding the U.S. market.
- Hamilton emphasized that enforcing trade laws will ensure U.S. aluminum manufacturers compete on a level playing field and help end continuous layoffs devastating workers, the industry, and communities.
- Bonnell Aluminum is a leading manufacturer of custom-fabricated and finished aluminum extrusions serving the building and construction, automotive and transportation, consumer durables, machinery and equipment, and electrical and distribution markets.
- Branded product lines include TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum and Futura Transitions by Bonnell Aluminum.
- The company operates manufacturing facilities in Newnan, GA; Carthage, TN; Niles, MI; Elkhart, IN; and Clearfield, UT.
