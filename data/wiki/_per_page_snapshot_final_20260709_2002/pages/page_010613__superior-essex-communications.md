# Superior Essex Communications

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- On February 19, 2020, Superior Essex Communications announced it has joined GeSI, an organization focused on enabling digital sustainability.
- Annie Bevan, Global Head of Sustainability for Superior Essex Communications, stated that joining GeSI required significant time and demonstrates the company's support for global sustainability efforts.
- Superior Essex Communications is the only cable manufacturer to achieve Living Product Challenge certification.
- It is the only company to offer externally verified EPD, HPD, and multi-attribute labels for over 55 product families.
- Many of its products are manufactured in a Zero Waste to Landfill certified facility.
- Its products are designed to help customers achieve sustainability goals and contribute to green building certifications including Living Building Challenge, WELL, and LEED v2009 and v4.
- Superior Essex is a leading cable supplier supporting Power-over-Ethernet systems, which result in energy savings and increased occupant productivity and well-being.
- Will Bryan, VP of Broadband Sales, stated that the company considers its GeSI membership a way to participate in and facilitate global sustainability vision discussions.
- GeSI is described as a globally recognized thought leader and proactive driver of the ICT sustainability agenda, with members focusing on climate change, energy/resource efficiency, e-waste management, responsible supply chain practices, labor rights, and public policy.
- Luis Neves, CEO of GeSI, welcomed Superior Essex Communications as a member, noting its commitment strengthens GeSI's work to apply digital solutions across the industry value chain.
- Superior Essex International LP produces a wide portfolio of wire and cable products and accessories, including copper and fiber optic cables for premises, outside plant (OSP), and DAS systems serving commercial, industrial, and service markets.
- The company practices high levels of environmental sustainability in its manufacturing processes, conducting operations safely and responsibly from an environmental perspective.
- The company's corporate address is listed as 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
