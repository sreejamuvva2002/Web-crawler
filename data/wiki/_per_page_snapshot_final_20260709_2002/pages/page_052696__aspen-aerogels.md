# Aspen Aerogels

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Aspen Aerogels in line for $671 million US loan](https://cen.acs.org/business/biobased-chemicals/Aspen-Aerogels-line-671-million/102/i33)  ·  `page_052696`

## Facts

- The US Department of Energy has conditionally committed to give Aspen Aerogels a loan of up to $671 million.
- The loan will help finance the construction of an aerogel blanket plant in Register, Georgia.
- The blankets are used as a protective layer within electric vehicle batteries to slow or prevent thermal runaway.
- Aspen says it already has contracts with carmakers including General Motors and Toyota.
- Along with Cabot, Aspen is one of two US makers of aerogel.
- Aerogel is an ultralight silica-based material used in insulation applications.
