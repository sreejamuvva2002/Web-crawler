# HL Mando

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-08

**Source:** [HL Mando Secures Fifth Consecutive GM Supplier Award - The BRAKE Report](https://thebrakereport.com/hl-mando-secures-fifth-consecutive-gm-supplier-award/)  ·  `page_045895`

## Facts

- HL Mando has been named General Motors’ Supplier of the Year for 2024.
- This marks the fifth consecutive year and the eleventh time overall that HL Mando has earned this GM award.
- HL Mando was recognized in the Brake Apply & Controls category for its Motor on Caliper (MoC) product.
- The MoC product is manufactured at HL Mando’s Alabama plant.
- HL Mando was praised for its localization efforts and technological innovation.
- Less than 1% of GM’s suppliers receive this Supplier of the Year accolade.
- The award was presented at General Motors’ 33rd annual Supplier of the Year event on April 8, 2025, in Phoenix, Arizona.
- HL Mando stood out among 92 companies from 12 countries honored as exceptional GM partners.
- As part of HL Group, HL Mando is a Tier 1 supplier specializing in Software Defined Vehicle (SDV) technologies.
- HL Mando’s North American journey began in 1996 with an R&D center in Michigan.
- HL Mando has manufacturing facilities in Alabama and Georgia, plus a second R&D center focused on future automotive advancements.
- HL Mando has invested approximately $700 million in North American operations.
- Jeff Morrison, GM’s global chief procurement officer, praised the suppliers’ innovation and resilience for delivering advanced technology to GM customers.
- The Supplier of the Year award evaluates performance in safety, innovation, and resilience, alongside alignment with GM’s values.
- Jason (Jae-hyuk) Kim, Head of HL Mando Americas, expressed pride in localization efforts and excitement for future collaborations with GM in automotive software and autonomous driving.
