# Corpus Overview — Georgia EV Supply Chain

*Hand-maintained map of the wiki. For the full catalog see [[index|the index]]; for the
argument the sources add up to see [[synthesis|the synthesis]].*

## What this covers

The wiki compiles **227 canonical entities** and **~647 facilities** drawn from **480
source-grounded records** (crawled from Georgia government, economic-development, trade-press,
and company sources). The subject is the build-out of an **electric-vehicle manufacturing
cluster in Georgia** since roughly 2020: battery cells and materials, vehicle assembly,
components, charging, recycling, and the public programs and people around them.

## Where it's happening

The cluster is geographically concentrated. The heaviest counties in the corpus:

- **[[county-bryan-county|Bryan County]]** — home to the **Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant
  America** mega-site and its supplier park (the single largest node).
- **[[county-jackson-county|Jackson County]]** — **[[sk-battery-america|SK Battery America]]**'s
  two-plant campus in Commerce.
- **[[county-bartow-county|Bartow County]]** — battery/solar manufacturing corridor.
- **[[county-bulloch-county|Bulloch County]]** and **[[county-chatham-county|Chatham County]]** —
  Savannah-area logistics and supplier growth feeding the Metaplant.
- **[[county-coweta-county|Coweta County]]**, **[[county-troup-county|Troup County]]** (Kia,
  West Point), and others across the I-85 / I-16 corridors.

Browse all counties from the [[index#geography-counties|geography section of the index]].

## Anchor projects (start here)

- **[[hyundai-motor-group-metaplant-america|Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America]]** — the
  flagship EV assembly plant in Bryan County, with a co-located **Hyundai–LG battery-cell
  joint venture**.
- **[[sk-battery-america|SK Battery America]]** — two lithium-ion cell plants in Commerce
  supplying the Ford F-150 Lightning and VW ID.4.
- **[[anovion-technologies|Anovion Technologies]]** — synthetic graphite anode material plant
  in Bainbridge ([[county-decatur-county|Decatur County]]).
- **[[rivian|Rivian]]** — planned EV assembly east of Atlanta.
- **[[kia-georgia|Kia Georgia]]** — established West Point assembly plant now building EVs.
