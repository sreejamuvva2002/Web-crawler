# Ecoplastic Corporation

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** 4822 Highway 301 South Register near Statesboro, Bulloch County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Korea-based Tier-1 automotive parts supplier Ecoplastic Corporation, a longtime supplier to Hyundai Motor Group and Kia, announced a $205 million investment to build a new manufacturing facility in Bulloch County, Georgia that will create 456 jobs and produce plastic interior and exterior components for electric vehicles.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The new plant will manufacture exterior and interior plastic parts-including bumpers, garnishes, consoles, and trims-for electric vehicles, supporting the Hyundai Metaplant supply chain in Georgia.
