# WIKA USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-08

**Source:** [WIKA USA Launches MH-4 CAN   | ManufacturingTomorrow](https://www.manufacturingtomorrow.com/news/2023/06/08/wika-usa-launches-mh-4-can-/20760)  ·  `page_015024`

## Facts

- WIKA USA, a leading manufacturer of pressure and temperature measurement instrumentation, is located in Lawrenceville, GA.
- WIKA USA launched the MH-4 CAN, a cost-effective and technically capable OEM CAN pressure sensor for mobile working machines.
- The MH-4 CAN features advanced electronics, robust sensor design, and comprehensive validation testing to ensure performance stability and operational reliability throughout its life cycle.
- Chad Reinhard, Senior Product Manager at WIKA USA, stated that the MH-4 CAN is a game-changer for the mobile working machine industry.
- According to Chad Reinhard, customers can benefit from exceptional performance, stability, and reliability, along with the ability to customize and adapt the sensor to specific needs.
- The MH-4 and MH-4 CAN are ideal for high-volume production as OEM pressure sensors for any kind of mobile working machinery.
- WIKA has over 75 years of experience advancing instrumentation for pressure, temperature, level, and flow measurement.
- WIKA embraces digitalization, decarbonization, and demographic change while developing stock and custom instrumentation and engineering services.
- WIKA is a global leader in lean manufacturing with manufacturing locations around the globe to meet high delivery demands.
