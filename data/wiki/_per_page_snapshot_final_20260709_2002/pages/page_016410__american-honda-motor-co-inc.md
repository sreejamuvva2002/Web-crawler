# American Honda Motor Co., Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-06-11

**Source:** [Honda Honors 2020 Top OEM and Service Parts Suppliers in North America | MOTOR](https://www.motor.com/2021/06/honda-honors-2020-top-oem-and-service-parts-suppliers-in-north-america/)  ·  `page_016410`

## Facts

- Honda honored 61 North American suppliers for their performance and perseverance in supplying parts and materials during 2020.
- Suppliers earned awards for supporting Honda with mass production parts and materials, as well as service parts that serve customers after purchasing a Honda or Acura product.
- Honda purchased $27 billion in parts for mass production from North American suppliers in 2020.
- These purchases supported the sales of more than 1.3 million Honda and Acura vehicles in North America in 2020.
- Honda purchased approximately $1 billion in parts from its Service Parts suppliers in North America.
- Honda recognizes suppliers with awards during its annual Honda Supplier Conference, held each spring for more than 35 years.
- The 2020 conference was conducted as a virtual event due to the COVID-19 pandemic's focus on health and safety.
- Mark Willoughby, vice president of procurement at Honda Development and Manufacturing of America, stated that supplier effort helped keep Honda plants running to meet customer needs during the pandemic.
- Mark Wiles, parts division assistant vice president at American Honda Motor Co., Inc., thanked service parts suppliers for their support despite supply chain challenges.
- OEM suppliers provide Honda with parts and materials for mass production.
- Honda recognized 39 OEM suppliers in 2020 across three categories: quality and delivery, value, and sustainability.
- The Supplier Sustainability Award recognizes a company committed to social responsibility, compliance, ethics, environmental governance, diversity, health and safety, and trade compliance.
- East Penn Manufacturing of Lyons Station, Pennsylvania, received the 2020 Supplier Sustainability Award.
- Honda does business with 743 OEM suppliers across North America.
- These OEM suppliers provide parts to Honda’s 16 plants in the region that produce Honda and Acura cars, light trucks, engines, and transmissions.
- American Honda Motor Co., Inc. honored 22 service parts suppliers for excellent performance in providing post-purchase service parts.
- Service Parts supplier awards reflect combined performance in quality, cost, and delivery.
- American Honda does business with nearly 1,200 service parts suppliers in North America.
- These suppliers provide service and accessory parts for more than 20 million Honda and Acura automobiles in operation in the United States, as well as for powersports and power equipment products.
- American Honda manages a national network of parts and distribution facilities delivering high-quality service parts to dealers nationwide and supporting global parts operations.
- Honda established operations in North America in 1959.
- Honda employs more than 40,000 associates in the region engaged in development, manufacturing, sales, and service support.
- Honda operates 18 major manufacturing plants in North America.
- Honda works with nearly 800 suppliers in the region to produce its diverse product range.
- In 2020, more than 95 percent of Honda and Acura automobiles sold in the U.S. were produced in North America using domestic and globally sourced parts.
- Honda operates 21 research and development facilities in North America that fully design, develop, and engineer many products made in the region.
- Nidec Elesys Americas Corporation in Atlanta, Georgia, received an OEM Supplier Award for Excellence in Quality and Delivery.
- ACM Georgia LLC in Warrenton, Georgia, received a Service Parts Supplier Award.
