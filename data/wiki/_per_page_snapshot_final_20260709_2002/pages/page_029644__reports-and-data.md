## Facts

- Industrial rubber market expected to reach USD 38.31 billion by 2026, at a CAGR of 4.3% during the forecast period.
- Market growth is governed by growing demand from automotive industry and government investment for eco-friendly rubber.
- Industrial rubber is primarily used for making tires.
- North America region accounted for the second largest share of 30.3% of the market in 2018.
- In the United States, currently, 111 companies are working within the tire and rubber industry, accounting for more than 42,000 direct employment possibilities.
- Synthetic rubber type accounts for a larger share of 63.8% in 2018.
- Mechanical Rubber Good product type is anticipated to grow at the highest rate of 4.4% during the forecast period.
- Building and construction end user segment is anticipated to grow at the highest rate of 4.6% during the forecast period.
- Asia Pacific region accounts for the largest share of 31.3% in 2018, leading in consumption and production of rubber.
- Midwest Industrial Rubber acquired New England Belting Company (NEBCO) on May 08, 2018.
