# Anovion Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-17

**Source:** [Anovian to Bring $800M Manufacturing Facility to SW Georgia](https://www.batterytechonline.com/materials/anovion-technologies-to-bring-800m-manufacturing-facility-to-southwest-georgia)  ·  `page_000339`

## Facts

- Anovion Technologies announced its first large-scale expansion of manufacturing capacity for premium synthetic graphite anode materials will be in Decatur County, Southwest Georgia.
- The project represents an $800 million investment for the new manufacturing facility.
- The new facility will span 1,500,000 square feet.
- The facility is expected to produce more than 44,000 tons per year of synthetic graphite anode material for lithium-ion batteries once fully operational.
- The project will create hundreds of high-quality local clean energy jobs in the surrounding region.
- Anovion chose the Decatur County location due to its proximity to existing and planned low- and carbon-free energy sources, short supply chains, access to existing rail infrastructure, a highly valuable skilled workforce, and a business-friendly environment.
- Eric Stopka, Chief Executive Officer of Anovion, stated that the company conducted a thorough search to identify a location addressing energy, transportation, logistics, and human capital needs to ensure an ecosystem for producing high-quality and sustainable synthetic graphite anode material.
- Eric Stopka noted that proximity to other battery and EV manufacturing plants will allow Anovion to further reduce its carbon footprint.
- In October 2022, Anovion was awarded a grant totaling $117 million from the US Department of Energy under the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law to help secure the domestic battery manufacturing supply chain.
- The new expansion facility will be the first of this scale in North America and is considered a crucial first step to reshoring synthetic graphite anode production.
- Anovion will utilize its innovative, sustainable, and technologically advanced manufacturing process at the new facility.
- The manufacturing process will rely on the region’s rapidly decarbonizing energy mix to power operations.
- The facility will introduce Anovion’s proprietary furnace technologies, which substantially reduce greenhouse gas emissions and lay out a roadmap for achieving a carbon-neutral footprint over time.
- Chip Dunn, Executive Chairman of Anovion, stated the company's long-term strategic vision is to focus on growth and innovation, positioning Anovion as a leader in the US energy transition and securing a domestic source for critical materials required for US lithium-ion battery production.
- Chip Dunn noted that from continued research and development in electrochemistry to technology improvements in production processes and climate tech initiatives, the goal is to become carbon-neutral over time, mitigate greenhouse gas emissions, and position Anovion as a future climate tech leader.
- In addition to the Georgia expansion, Anovion is expanding its Anovion Center of Excellence and its hydro-powered sister manufacturing facility near Niagara Falls, NY.
- The Niagara Falls facility is notably one of the only qualified US sources of battery-grade synthetic graphite that is commercially shipping product today.
- Anovion Technologies is headquartered in Chicago and is a US-owned and US-based advanced materials business.
- Anovion is currently one of America’s first commercial-scale manufacturers of premium, cleaner anode-grade synthetic graphite across its existing footprint near Niagara Falls, NY, and Clarksburg, WV.
- Anovion began commercial production in early 2021.
- Anovion is among a limited number of graphite anode producers in America to have successfully gained qualification for EV applications.
