# Kumho Tire Georgia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-04-15

**Source:** [Macon's Kumho Tire facility to expand with $21.8 million investment](https://wgxa.tv/news/local/macons-kumho-tire-facility-to-expand-with-218-million-investment)  ·  `page_056659`

## Facts

- Kumho Tire in Macon will be expanding with a $21.8 million investment.
- The expansion plans, announced Thursday, will add about 20,500 square feet to the facility.
- A new Automated Production Unit (APU) will be brought in to help improve quality and efficiency.
- The expansion means about 500,000 more tires can be produced annually.
- The announcement was made at the 2021 Greater Macon Chamber of Commerce’s State of the Community address.
- Mayor Lester Miller stated he is excited to see Kumho’s progress and growth, noting the company recognizes Macon as a great place to do business.
- Mayor Lester Miller emphasized that economic development focuses on attracting new industries and supporting existing ones, calling it a great day for Macon-Bibb County.
- The groundbreaking for the expansion is scheduled to happen in May.
- Keith Lolley, Director of Human Resources of Kumho Tire Georgia, stated the company is thrilled about the expansion and called it great news for team members and the Macon-Bibb community.
