# WIKA Instrument LP

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [WIKA USA Expands Strategic Partnership with AWC - Industry Today - Leader in Manufacturing & Industry News](https://industrytoday.com/wika-usa-expands-strategic-partnership-with-awc/)  ·  `page_014976`

## Facts

- WIKA USA announced an expanded strategic partnership with AWC, a respected automation and controls technology company headquartered in Baton Rouge, LA.
- The partnership marks an exciting new phase in their nearly decade-long collaboration, reflecting WIKA USA’s commitment to extending its service reach and enhancing customer satisfaction.
- This collaboration will not lead to any alterations in the manufacturing or design of the WIKA product portfolio, assuring the steadfast maintenance of product quality and uninterrupted service.
- WIKA USA President Todd Gardner stated that the expanded geographic partnership with AWC marks a significant milestone reinforcing the strength of their partner relationship and bolstering their commitment to serving partners more effectively.
- The WIKA Group is a global market leader in pressure and temperature measurement, employing 11,200 people as a family-run business focused on high-precision products, solutions, and services for sensing technology.
- AWC has over 600 employee-owners focused on Process Technology & Instrumentation and Industrial Automation, operating as an authorized local sales, support, and service partner to leading manufacturing partners.
- Founded in 1965, AWC operates 32 full-service locations across the U.S., spanning from Denver, CO to Tampa, FL, with a heavy concentration along the Gulf Coast of TX and LA.
- WIKA Instrument LP is located at 1000 Wiegand Boulevard, Lawrenceville, GA 30043, USA.
