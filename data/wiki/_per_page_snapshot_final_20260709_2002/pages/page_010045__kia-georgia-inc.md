# Kia Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-02-24

**Source:** [Kia Georgia celebrates trio of major milestones, including Start of Production for 2027 Kia Telluride](https://www.barchart.com/story/news/400518/kia-georgia-celebrates-trio-of-major-milestones-including-start-of-production-for-2027-kia-telluride)  ·  `page_010045`

## Facts

- Kia Georgia team members marked a historic day at its West Point facility on Feb. 24, 2026, with the start of production for the all-new 2027 Kia Telluride.
- The 2027 Kia Telluride marked the first hybrid electric vehicle (HEV) assembled in the state of Georgia.
- The 2027 Kia Telluride also represented the 5 millionth vehicle produced at Kia Georgia.
- A ceremony for the milestone was attended by Georgia Governor Brian P. Kemp, team members, and state/community leaders.
- Kia Georgia is the only plant in the world that builds the Telluride.
- Sean Yoon serves as President and CEO of Kia North America and Kia America.
- The 2027 Kia Telluride is Kia's first US-assembled HEV.
- Kia Georgia began mass production in 2009.
- Since beginning production, Kia Georgia has grown into one of the Southeast region's largest employers.
- The Telluride has been exclusively manufactured in Georgia since its introduction in 2019.
- Stuart Countess serves as Kia Georgia's President and CEO.
- The Kia Georgia plant produces gasoline-powered vehicles, EVs, and HEVs.
- Kia Georgia, Inc. is Kia Corporation's first manufacturing site in North America.
- Kia Georgia has an annual production capacity of 350,000 units.
- Kia Georgia is located on a 2,200-acre site in West Point, Georgia.
- Kia Georgia officially began mass production on Nov. 16, 2009.
- Kia Georgia manufactures the Telluride, Sorento SUVs (excluding HEV and PHEV models), Sportage SUVs (excluding HEV and PHEV models), and the all-electric EV9 and EV6.
