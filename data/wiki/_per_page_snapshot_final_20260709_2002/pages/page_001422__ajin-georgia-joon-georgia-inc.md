# AJIN Georgia (Joon Georgia, Inc.)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-07-18

**Source:** [AJIN Georgia Grand Opening](https://kbd.group/grand-opening-of-ajin-georgias-state-of-the-art-facility/)  ·  `page_001422`

## Facts

- On July 18, 2024, AJIN Georgia in Bulloch County’s Bruce Yawn Commerce Park celebrated the grand opening of their new automotive metal structural parts facility.
- The facility is the first Tier 1 supplier for Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America in Bulloch County and will manufacture interior metal and structural parts.
- About 285 people attended the grand opening ceremony, including AJIN executives, local and state leaders, and prominent guests.
- The gathering featured Jamie Calloway (HR General Manager at AJIN Georgia), CEO Onchung Gwon of HMGMA, delegates from MOBIS, officials from state and local governments, the Korean consulate, utility service providers, regional press, and delegates from Ajin USA.
- KBD Group served as the design-builder for the project.
- The facility spans 853,000 square feet.
- Over a hundred employees are currently inside making parts for an all-electric Hyundai Motor Group crossover SUV.
- The manufactured parts include the main metal portions of a vehicle’s doors, dash, fenders, and floorboard.
- The project represents a $317-million corporate investment in Statesboro Georgia.
- When production begins, tractor-trailer trucks will haul parts from the AJIN Georgia plant to Hyundai Motor Group’s Metaplant America in northern Bryan County about 29 miles down Interstate 16.
- The tour featured hundreds of one-armed robots and large stamping presses that exert up to 1,500 tons of force along with rolls of steel that feed them.
- AJIN Georgia is located 30 miles west of the under-construction Hyundai EV plant in Bryan County.
- According to a news release from Gov. Brian Kemp’s office, the company plans to hire 630 workers for the future 7 million parts factory.
- Jung Ho Sea, CEO of Ajin USA, stated: “We are excited to be one of the first Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America automotive suppliers to build an automotive parts manufacturing facility in Bulloch County and for our first plant in the great State of Georgia.”
- Jung Ho Sea added: “Joon Georgia, Inc. will create approximately 630 new jobs with competitive benefits in Bulloch County and the State of Georgia.”
- Jung Ho Sea stated their commitment is to be the best parts supplier for automotive and EV companies like Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America, as well as the best employer and corporate citizen giving back to the community of Bulloch County and the City of Statesboro.
- KBD Group completed the AJIN Georgia project on time and within budget without any injuries.
