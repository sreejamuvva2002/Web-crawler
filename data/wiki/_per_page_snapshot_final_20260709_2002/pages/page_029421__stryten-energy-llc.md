## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC is a U.S.-based energy storage solutions provider headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- The company was honored with the 2025 Women’s Choice Award® for Best Companies to Work – Inclusion.
- Wendy Henderson serves as Senior Vice President and Chief Human Resource Officer at Stryten Energy.
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- The company's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries.
- Stryten Energy manufactures intelligent chargers and energy performance management software.
- Stryten Energy powers applications ranging from submarines and subcompacts to microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- The company is very active in the Women in Auto Care Association, which focuses on connecting, empowering, and mentoring women within the auto care industry.
- Stryten Energy participates in the Women in the Global Battery Industry (WGBI), an organization formed by Battery Council International to promote and develop the growth of women in the battery industry.
- The company provides training and skills development programs for its employees.
- Stryten Energy partners with some of the world's most recognized companies to meet growing demand for reliable and sustainable energy storage capacity.
