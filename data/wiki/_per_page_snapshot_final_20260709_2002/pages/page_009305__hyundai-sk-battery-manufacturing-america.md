# Hyundai SK Battery Manufacturing America

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-04-25

**Source:** [Hyundai Motor Group, SK On Finalize U.S. Battery JV Name as 'HSBMA' - Seoul Economic Daily](https://en.sedaily.com/finance/2026/03/27/hyundai-motor-group-sk-on-finalize-us-battery-jv-name-as)  ·  `page_009305`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group and SK On finalized the name of their North American battery production joint venture as HSBMA.
- HSBMA stands for Hyundai SK Battery Manufacturing America.
- The official corporate name was confirmed as HSBMA according to industry sources on Monday.
- The joint venture's launch was announced on April 25, 2023.
- HSBMA has completed more than 90% of construction.
- HSBMA aims to begin commercial operations this year.
- The facility is located in Bartow County, Georgia.
- The facility is planned to have an annual production capacity of 35 gigawatt-hours (GWh).
- The planned capacity is enough to supply battery cells for approximately 300,000 electric vehicles.
- Hyundai Motor Group and SK On invested a total of $5 billion in the joint venture.
- The two companies hold equal 50% stakes in HSBMA.
- Battery cells produced at HSBMA will be assembled into battery packs by Hyundai Mobis.
- The battery packs will be supplied entirely to Hyundai Motor, Kia, and Genesis electric vehicles manufactured in the United States.
- Hyundai Motor's stock ticker is 005380.KS.
- Kia's stock ticker is 000270.KS.
- HSBMA is located near Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA).
- HSBMA is located near Kia's Georgia plant.
- HSBMA is located near Hyundai Motor's Alabama plant.
- The possibility of expanding HSBMA's supply in line with future production increases at HMGMA has been discussed.
- SK On plans to further strengthen its North American production capacity through its existing standalone plant in Georgia, SK Battery America (22 GWh).
- SK On plans to further strengthen its North American production capacity through the upcoming HSBMA plant (35 GWh).
- SK On plans to further strengthen its North American production capacity through the BlueOval SK Tennessee plant (45 GWh).
- The BlueOval SK Tennessee plant is set to be converted into a standalone facility.
