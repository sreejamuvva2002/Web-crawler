# EnviroSpark Energy Solutions

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Atlanta, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

National EV charging company founded in Atlanta, Georgia that operates over 8,200 charging plugs across North America and is expanding its owned and operated network through new investment and NEVI-funded projects in Georgia and Tennessee.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Operates a large EV charging network, received NEVI awards to build DC fast-charging stations in Georgia, and is expanding infrastructure with new investment.

## Key facts

- EnviroSpark operates over 8,200 charging plugs across North America.
- The company was selected by GDOT to construct DC fast-charging projects in Tifton and Metter, Georgia.
- EnviroSpark received a NEVI award for an installation at a Waffle House in Lakeland, Tennessee, funded through the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law.
- Strategic partnerships include RaceTrac, Waffle House, IHG Hotels & Resorts, Ford Dealerships, HHHunt, Asset Living, Tesla, Volkswagen, Volta (Shell), and Starwood Capital Group.
