# Mercedes-Benz AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-05-24

**Source:** [Georgia Gov. Kemp celebrates major win as Mercedes-Benz deepens roots in Georgia with 500 new jobs](https://alpharettalifestylepubs.com/georgia-gov-kemp-celebrates-major-win-as-mercedes-benz-deepens-roots-in-georgia-with-500-new-jobs/)  ·  `page_046399`

## Facts

- Mercedes-Benz is set to expand its footprint in Georgia, announcing that Metro Atlanta will soon become the company’s North American headquarters.
- Governor Brian Kemp announced the decision on Monday, calling it a big step forward for the state’s increasing image as a center for innovation and the auto sector.
- The decision means Mercedes-Benz will move some of its most important business teams to the “1MB” building in Sandy Springs.
- About 800 Georgians already work at the “1MB” site, which debuted in 2018.
- As the corporation becomes more committed to Georgia, up to 500 more jobs are expected to be opened in the area.
- Mercedes-Benz is moving its North American headquarters to Metro Atlanta and building a new, high-tech Research & Development center near the Sandy Springs site.
- This investment will provide new job opportunities for people in the area and show that the brand is committed to pushing the limits of automotive technology.
- Officials think that the whole change will be done by August 2026.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated: “Georgia continues to lead the way in the future of mobility and technical innovation, attracting world-class companies like Mercedes-Benz that are driving the automotive industry forward.”
- Governor Brian Kemp added: “We’re excited that a job creator that already has close ties to Georgia is doubling down on that choice and growing their presence here in the best state for business and opportunity.”
- Jason Hoff, CEO of Mercedes-Benz North America, expressed gratitude to the state for the help the company received.
- Hoff stressed that the expansion will bring team members closer together, making the organization more flexible and more capable to provide outstanding client service.
- Hoff noted that the Atlanta area’s world-class environment will assist Mercedes-Benz keep growing in the U.S. market.
- The “1MB” site continues to be home to the sales and financial services departments at Mercedes-Benz.
- The new R&D hub will focus on making the next generation of cars and mobility solutions.
- Officials from Georgia, including the Department of Economic Development and partners like the City of Sandy Springs, Select Fulton, the Metro Atlanta Chamber, and Georgia Power, all worked together to get this important project.
- Mercedes-Benz AG has over 175,000 employees around the world.
- The company is known for its high-end cars, high quality, and strong concentration on electric mobility and vehicle software.
- The company’s most recent investment in Atlanta shows that it wants to stay at the top of the quickly changing auto industry.
