# YKK AP America Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-06

**Source:** [YKK AP Holds Ribbon Cutting Ceremony for New Residential Manufacturing Facility](https://www.dwmmag.com/2024/05/06/ykk-ap-holds-ribbon-cutting-ceremony-for-new-residential-manufacturing-facility/)  ·  `page_016183`

## Facts

- YKK AP America Inc. held a ribbon cutting ceremony last week, marking the official opening of its first greenfield residential manufacturing facility in Macon-Bibb County, Georgia.
- The event was held in Macon with commissioner Pat Wilson of the Georgia Department of Economic Development, Mayor Lester Miller of Macon-Bibb County, chairperson Robby Fountain of the Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority, and other local leaders in attendance.
- Macon-Bibb County Mayor Lester Miller stated that YKK AP’s expansion into a new, leading-edge facility is a testament to its strong connection with the community and marked the beginning of a new chapter offering more opportunities to enrich the economy, improve the lives of families, and strengthen the area's position as a hub of innovation and industry.
- The facility completed construction in December 2023 and began operations in January 2024.
- The facility currently employs more than 250 people in the Macon community.
- YKK AP America Inc. president and CEO Oliver Stepe stated that the event marked more than a celebration of the new residential manufacturing facility, symbolizing growth and confidence in the business and employees moving forward, and aligned with the company's purpose to build a better society through architectural products.
- Oliver Stepe expressed gratitude to the state of Georgia, the Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority, and all partners for helping celebrate the milestone.
