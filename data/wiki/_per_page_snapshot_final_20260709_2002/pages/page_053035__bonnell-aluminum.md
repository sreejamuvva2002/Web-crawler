## Facts

- Bonnell Aluminum is a subsidiary of Tredegar Corporation.
- Bonnell Aluminum is expanding its TSLOTS line into the Southeastern United States.
- The expansion includes a new manufacturing and distribution facility in Newnan, Georgia.
- The strategic move aims to cater to the region's growing demand for versatile aluminum structural framing systems.
- Bonnell Aluminum is a premier manufacturer of custom-fabricated aluminum extrusions.
- Its products serve diverse sectors including construction and automotive industries.
- Randy Johnson serves as the Senior Brand Manager for TSLOTS at Bonnell Aluminum.
- The expansion focuses on reducing shipping costs and customer lead times by positioning manufacturing and distribution sites closer to customers.
- Randy Johnson stated: "We have been successful in growing our TSLOTS product line by positioning our manufacturing and distribution sites closer to where our customers operate."
- This represents the second major investment in the TSLOTS product line in two years.
- It follows a successful expansion into the Midwest in 2021.
- The capacity addition will increase Bonnell Aluminum's footprint across the United States.
- Customers will receive reduced shipping costs and shorter lead times.
- TSLOTS by Bonnell Aluminum provides comprehensive services including project design assistance, fabrication, and full assembly.
- The company ensures customers receive tailored solutions to meet specific requirements.
