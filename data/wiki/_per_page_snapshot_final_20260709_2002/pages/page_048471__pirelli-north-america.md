# Pirelli North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-05

**Source:** [PIRELLI WILL START CYBER™ TYRE PRODUCTION IN GEORGIA UNDERLINING THE STRATEGIC IMPORTANCE OF THE U.S. MARKET PR Newswire](https://pr.daltoncitizen.com/article/PIRELLI-WILL-START-CYBERtm-TYRE-PRODUCTION-IN-GEORGIA-UNDERLINING-THE-STRATEGIC-IMPORTANCE-OF-THE-US-MARKET?storyId=69fa635df625e3842813917b)  ·  `page_048471`

## Facts

- Pirelli will start Cyber™ Tyre production in Georgia.
- Investment and output expansion details will be communicated in the coming months following the finalization of the development plans.
- The Georgia plant is already dedicated to producing technologically advanced products for the U.S. market, including the High-Value segment and Motorsport.
- Pirelli participated in the SelectUSA Investment Summit, a flagship event promoted by the U.S. Department of Commerce, where Cyber™ Tyre was showcased as a core innovation shaping smart mobility.
- Cyber™ Tyre is the world's first hardware-and-software system capable of collecting data and information from sensors embedded in tires.
- The system processes sensor data through Pirelli's proprietary software and algorithms.
- Cyber™ Tyre communicates in real time with vehicle electronics to enable new functionalities integrated with driving and control systems.
- These functionalities enhance the driving experience, increase safety levels, and support connected infrastructure.
- Cyber™ Tyre was showcased at the Georgia State booth during the SelectUSA summit.
- Pirelli has been established in Georgia for more than 20 years.
- Claudio Zanardo, CEO of Pirelli North America, stated that the start of Cyber™ Tyre production in the Rome, Georgia plant is a significant milestone for Pirelli in the country.
- The production start reflects Pirelli's commitment to bringing advanced technologies closer to the market and strengthening its industrial footprint and innovation capabilities in the United States.
- Pirelli is finalizing the introduction of the latest version of the MIRS (Modular Integrated Robotized System) production process at its Rome, Georgia plant.
- The MIRS process will be the most advanced manufacturing process for high-end, premium production within the entire Pirelli Group and is exclusive to the Georgia factory.
- The new process enhances robotized production capabilities, increasing productivity, flexibility, and quality.
- MIRS is a highly digitalized system that enables a direct link between product design and its industrial application.
- This development lays the groundwork for growth in Pirelli's production capacity in Rome as part of the Group's industrial development plans.
- The Rome, Georgia plant specializes in high-value tire production for the North American market.
- The facility hosts a dedicated R&D center to strengthen technological development and product innovation.
- The facility is recognized for responsible sourcing practices, including the use of FSC®-certified natural rubber.
