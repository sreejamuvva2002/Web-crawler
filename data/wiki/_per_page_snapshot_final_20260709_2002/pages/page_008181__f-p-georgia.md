# F&P Georgia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Tesla and Honda Contracts Lead to $22 Million Expansion at Rome's F&P Facility - NewsBreak](https://www.newsbreak.com/rome-news-tribune-1598303/3212331223867-tesla-and-honda-contracts-lead-to-22-million-expansion-at-rome-s-f-p-facility)  ·  `page_008181`

## Facts

- F&P Georgia announced a $22 million expansion to its Rome facility.
- The expansion is driven by contracts with automakers Honda and Tesla.
- F&P's facility is located on 51 acres of the Floyd County Industrial Park.
- F&P operates as a tier one automotive supplier.
- This expansion is only the most recent in a series of expansions at the company’s Rome plant.
- The expansion will add an additional 25 jobs to the company's current workforce.
- Nat Massey, senior manager at F&P Manufacturing, confirmed the addition of 25 jobs.
- The Rome-Floyd County Development Authority signed off on backing the expansion and a 10-year tax abatement on Monday.
- F&P will manufacture suspension components for Tesla's Cyber Truck, which will be made in Austin, Texas.
- Nat Massey stated, “We are very excited about this new venture with Tesla and our continued work with Honda.”
- Plant Manager Bruce Critchlow has been with F&P since its beginnings in Floyd County.
- Bruce Critchlow thanked Rome, Floyd County, and the Rome-Floyd County Development Authority for their continued support of F&P Georgia.
- Critchlow stated, “From our initial construction to the most recent of six major expansions, the Rome and Floyd County communities have always supported F&P and our plans for growth.”
- Critchlow noted that when he started at F&P, there was just an empty field and a handful of associates.
- The facility now has 400,000 square feet under its roof and employs 520 top notch associates.
- F&P opened in Rome in 2001 with 70 employees.
- The company expanded several times before 2012, when it went into a second building in the industrial park.
- In 2014, F&P added another 50,000 square feet to that building.
- The most recent expansions prior to this announcement occurred in 2020 and again in 2022.
- RFCDA Chair Jimmy Byars stated that F&P is already one of the top 10 employers in Floyd County.
- Jimmy Byars added, “Our existing industries are extremely valuable to our community, and we will support them in every way possible.”
