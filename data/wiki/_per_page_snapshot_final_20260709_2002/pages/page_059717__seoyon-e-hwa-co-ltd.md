# Seoyon E-Hwa Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2014-12-31

**Source:** [Seoyon E-Hwa Co., Ltd. Business Report FY ended Dec. 2014 - MarkLines Automotive Industry Portal](https://www.marklines.com/en/top500/cf/s500_227_hl2014)  ·  `page_059717`

## Facts

- Hanil E-Hwa Interior Systems Georgia, Inc., which is a U.S. subsidiary of the Company, will supply interior components for the successor (development code: JFA) to Kia Motors' K5.
- The new vehicle will be assembled at Kia Motors' Georgia plant.
- Hanil E-Hwa Interior Systems Georgia generated USD 100 million in sales during 2013.
- It aims to double its sales to USD 200 million within the next three years.
- The Company has been awarded a new contract to supply door trims for Kia Motors' Optima mid-sized sedan for the North American market.
- The door trims will be produced at Hanil E-Hwa Interior Systems Georgia, Inc. in Georgia, U.S.A.
- The Company announced that it will invest USD 5.5 million to expand the plant of Hanil E-Hwa Interior Systems Georgia, Inc.
- The U.S. subsidiary mainly produces door trims, tailgates and pillars for Kia Motors' vehicles built in Georgia.
- The production capacity for door trims at the Georgia, USA facility is 66 thousand units.
- The production result for door trims at the Georgia, USA facility was 57 thousand units.
- The capacity utilization rate for door trims at the Georgia, USA facility was 86%.
- The project period for supplying door trims for the successor model to Optima (JFA) in the USA was Jan. 2014 - Jun. 2015, and it was completed.
