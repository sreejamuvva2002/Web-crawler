# Denkai America

## Facts

- Denkai America is recognized as the technological leader in the manufacture of high-quality electrodeposited copper foils for the printed circuit board industry.
- Denkai America produces both conventional cladding and application specific copper foils required by technology’s leading edge companies.
- At Denkai America, copper foils are electroformed on revolving titanium drums, unique for the ability to deposit copper foils with a high-quality surface.
- Electroformed foils are subjected to a variety of surface treating operations contingent upon end-use requirements.
- Camden Copper HP2 is a domestically made copper foil designed for highest peel strength and O/L bonding, featuring a high copper nodule density.
- Peel strength is critical when using no flow or low flow resin systems, as failure to bond could lead to delamination in the field.
- Camden Copper HP2 is released to IPC 4562 grade 3 standard to address concerns regarding bond failure and delamination.
- Due to its superior surface chemistry and profile, Camden Copper HP2 can exceed IPC4101 slash sheet minimums.
- Camden Copper HP2 can be used on various resins including epoxy, Pi, PPO, and PPE resins.
- Camden Copper HP2 is available in several thicknesses.
- Denkai America also produces a highly versatile IPC 4562 grade 3 foil designed for high temperature applications with epoxy and PI resins.
- This high temperature foil balances bond strength and signal performance needs while simplifying material requirements that previously specified TOBIII separately.
- The high temperature foil is domestically made and available in several thicknesses.
