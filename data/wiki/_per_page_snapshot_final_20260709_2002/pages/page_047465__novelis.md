## Facts

- Novelis is the leading producer of innovative, flat-rolled aluminum products and the world's largest recycler of aluminum.
- The company’s business strategy is built on sustainability, with an ambition to be the world’s leading provider of low-carbon, sustainable aluminum solutions that advance the company’s business, the aluminum industry and society as a whole toward the benefits of a circular economy.
- Novelis has partnered with Georgia Tech to collaborate on research and development (R&D) and support the education of tomorrow’s scientists and engineers dedicated to making better products that lead to a more sustainable world.
- Novelis is headquartered in Atlanta.
- The company operates 33 manufacturing locations in nine countries.
- Novelis employs 14,650 employees worldwide.
- The company recorded $12.3 billion in revenue for its 2021 fiscal year.
- Novelis is a subsidiary of Hindalco Industries Limited, an industry leader in aluminum and copper.
- Hindalco Industries Limited serves as the metals flagship company of the Aditya Birla Group, a multinational conglomerate based in Mumbai.
- Novelis’ Global Research and Technology Center (NGRTC) is located in Kennesaw, Georgia.
- The NGRTC employs approximately 200 people, including world-class engineers, metallurgists, chemists and scientists.
- The facility houses state-of-the-art lab equipment and a range of product and process pilot lines that bring innovative solutions to customers in the automotive, beverage can and specialty products markets.
- Novelis operates R&D centers and Customer Solution Centers in North America, Europe and Asia that work collaboratively with the NGRTC to solve global technical challenges in close partnership with customers in packaging, auto, specialties and aerospace markets.
