# E-Z-GO

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[vehicle-manufacturing|Vehicle Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Augusta, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

E-Z-GO offers Certified Pre-Owned (CPO) RXV fleet vehicles that are tested, reconditioned, and certified to the company's highest standards. The vehicles are refurbished in Augusta, GA with comprehensive component replacements and inspections, and come with a 1-year warranty and up to four-year lease terms.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Manufacturer of electric utility vehicles providing certified pre-owned fleet options that support electric vehicle adoption in Georgia.

## Timeline

- **1954** — E-Z-GO began handcrafted vehicle production in Augusta, Georgia.

## Key facts

- Certified Pre-Owned RXV vehicles are tested, reconditioned, and certified to E-Z-GO's highest standards.
- Vehicles are refurbished in Augusta, GA with component replacements and a comprehensive inspection covering body, steering unit, tires, alignment, axles, seats, dash, battery amps, state of charge meter, and 14 other key areas.
- Each Certified Pre-Owned vehicle includes a 1-year parts and accessory warranty, with optional extended coverage available.
- E-Z-GO offers up to four-year lease terms for its Certified Pre-Owned fleet vehicles.
- Customers receive access to an industry-leading service network of over 80 E-Z-GO-certified technicians nationwide and a call center team with a 35-second wait time.
- Since 1954, E-Z-GO has proudly handcrafted every vehicle in Augusta, Georgia.
