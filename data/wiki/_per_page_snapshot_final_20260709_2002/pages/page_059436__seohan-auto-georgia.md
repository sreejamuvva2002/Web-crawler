## Facts

- The Hyundai Metaplant is projected to bring thousands of jobs to the Liberty County area.
- Governor Brian Kemp announced the most recent development in Liberty County.
- Seohan Auto Georgia is a supplier for Hyundai.
- Seohan Auto Georgia is expected to bring 180 new jobs to Liberty County.
- Many counties surrounding the Hyundai Metaplant have experienced development since the plant was announced.
- Seohan Auto is the first manufacturing plant to come to Liberty County as a result of the Metaplant's announcement.
- Seohan Auto is expected to invest more than $72 million in its Georgia facility.
- Liberty County Commission Chairman Donald Lovette stated that the facility is a great enhancement for competitive jobs in the area.
- Donald Lovette noted that many residents currently commute to other jobs in and around the Coastal Empire and will now be able to work locally.
- The Seohan Auto Georgia facility will be located in Liberty County's industrial park at Tradeport East, just off exit 76 on I-95.
- Seohan Auto Georgia is a tier one manufacturer for the Hyundai Metaplant, producing parts needed early in the production process.
- Donald Lovette stated that being a tier one provider puts the plant's construction on the fast track.
- Donald Lovette also serves on the Liberty County Development Authority.
- Donald Lovette stated that being a tier one provider puts Liberty County on the cutting edge of supplying parts for the Hyundai megaplant.
- Donald Lovette said the county's location made it the ideal spot for the development.
- Donald Lovette cited Liberty County's status as a coastal county and I-95 running through it as reasons the location is a perfect fit for manufacturers.
- The facility's location is in close proximity to the Georgia Ports Authority.
- Seohan Auto is expected to begin production on its automobile parts next year.
