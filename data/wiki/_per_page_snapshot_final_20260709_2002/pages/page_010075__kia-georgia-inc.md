# Kia Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-02-24

**Source:** [Kia Georgia celebrates trio of major milestones, including Start of Production for 2027 Kia Telluride | NY Business Times](https://www.nybusinesstimes.com/kia-georgia-celebrates-trio-of-major-milestones-including-start-of-production-for-2027-kia-telluride/)  ·  `page_010075`

## Facts

- Kia Georgia team members marked a historic day at its West Point facility on Tuesday with the start of production of the all-new 2027 Kia Telluride.
- The ceremonial vehicle represented two other major milestones: the first hybrid electric vehicle (HEV) assembled in the state of Georgia and the 5 millionth vehicle produced at Kia Georgia.
- A ceremony for the milestone was attended by Georgia Governor Brian P. Kemp, team members, and state/community leaders.
- Sean Yoon, President and CEO of Kia North America and Kia America, stated that as the only plant in the world that builds Telluride, the launch reflects the strength of the West Point team, customer trust, and Kia's commitment to building the future of mobility in Georgia.
- Reaching five million vehicles and introducing Kia's first US-assembled HEV is a testament to Kia's history and direction going forward, according to Yoon.
- Since beginning production in 2009, Kia Georgia has grown into one of the region’s largest employers and a cornerstone of automotive manufacturing in the Southeast.
- The Telluride is one of the industry’s most awarded vehicles, has become a flagship model for the brand, and is a symbol of quality built in West Point.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp stated that since its introduction in 2019, the Telluride has been exclusively Georgia-made and has become one of the most successful vehicles in Kia’s lineup.
- Governor Kemp attributed the Telluride's success to a proven partnership approach creating long-lasting relationships between Kia Georgia, the local community, and the entire state.
- Stuart Countess, Kia Georgia’s President and CEO, emphasized that the day’s milestones were made possible by the dedication of team members at Kia Georgia.
- Countess stated that five million vehicles are the result of teamwork and that producing world-class gasoline-powered vehicles, EVs, and now HEVs speaks to the versatility and talent in the plant.
- Kia Georgia, Inc. is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Corporation based in Seoul, Korea.
- Kia Georgia has an annual capacity of 350,000 units.
- Kia Georgia is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Ga.
- Kia Georgia began mass production on Nov. 16, 2009.
- Kia Georgia produces the Telluride, Sorento (excludes HEV and PHEV models), Sportage (excludes HEV and PHEV models) SUVs, and the all-electric EV9 and EV6.
