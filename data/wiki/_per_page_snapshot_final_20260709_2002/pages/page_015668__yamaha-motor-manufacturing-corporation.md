# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2022-06

**Source:** [Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation Breaks Ground on Innovative SMART Warehouse in Newnan - Metro Atlanta CEO](https://metroatlantaceo.com/news/2022/06/yamaha-motor-manufacturing-corporation-breaks-ground-innovative-smart-warehouse-newnan1/)  ·  `page_015668`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation (YMMC), based in Newnan, Georgia, broke ground on a new, advanced, and innovative “SMART” Warehouse.
- The facility is the first-of-its-kind SMART Warehouse for Yamaha Motor Company worldwide.
- The new warehouse will span 200,000 square feet.
- The facility will use state-of-the-art automated storage and retrieval systems to reduce response time for fulfilling dealer orders for Yamaha WaveRunners, Golf Cars, ATVs, and Side-by-Sides by more than 50 percent.
- SMART is an acronym for Sequenced Material and Reduced Transportation.
- The medium parts automated storage system will retrieve and put away 240 containers per hour, compared to the current rate of 170 containers per hour.
- The small parts automated system will handle 2400 containers per hour, compared to the current capability of 550 containers per day.
- Automatic conveyors and tuggers will move parts through the SMART center to factories, enabling a 50 percent reduction in forklifts.
- Mike Chrzanowski, President and CEO of Yamaha Motor Corporation, stated the warehouse will improve operational efficiency and material flow velocity to factory assembly lines to get product to dealers and customers faster.
- Chrzanowski noted that demand for all outdoor recreational products produced at the factory continues to exceed supply.
- The significant investment in the new facility underscores Yamaha's long-term commitment to YMMC employees, the City of Newnan, and Coweta County.
- The warehouse will bring higher paid and more skilled jobs into Coweta County, including positions for robotics experts, software developers, and systems engineers.
- The facility was designed by SSI SCHAEFER.
- The SMART Warehouse will include an observation platform for groups such as local school students to learn about manufacturing and automated processes.
- Yamaha expects to complete the SMART Warehouse in 2024.
