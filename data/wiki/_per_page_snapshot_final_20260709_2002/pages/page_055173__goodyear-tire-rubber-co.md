# Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2018-10-19

**Source:** [Massive Goodyear distribution center will bring 160 jobs to Forney's Gateway](https://www.dallasnews.com/business/real-estate/2018/10/19/massive-goodyear-distribution-center-will-bring-160-jobs-to-forney-s-gateway/)  ·  `page_055173`

## Facts

- Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co. is locating a 1.2 million-square-foot distribution center on U.S. Highway 80 in Forney.
- The city will rezone 102 acres for the project, which will be built on agricultural land at Gateway Boulevard.
- The project will be part of Dallas-based Petro-Hunt's 2,000-acre Gateway mixed-use development, which also includes homes and apartments.
- Hillwood, one of North Texas' largest industrial builders, is developing the project.
- Hillwood vice president Todd Finely stated that Hillwood will own the building and Goodyear will lease it from them.
- The center will employ 160 people.
- City Manager Tony Carson expressed excitement about welcoming one of the nation's most recognizable brands to Forney, noting its evolving role in commerce and innovation in distribution.
- Several Forney residents spoke against the project, citing concerns about traffic, pollution and crime.
- Warren Ketteman, Forney's executive director of economic development, said the city worked on the warehouse deal for several months under the code name "Project Fireball."
- The property tax impact of the project to the city is equivalent to over half the impact of the 714 residential permits issued last year.
- Goodyear already has a shipping hub east of Forney, with a current much smaller facility in Terrell that will be moved to this new facility as an expansion bringing more jobs.
- Goodyear has $15 billion in annual revenue and ranked 187th on the Fortune 500 list.
- The Akron, Ohio-based tiremaker traces its roots to 1839.
