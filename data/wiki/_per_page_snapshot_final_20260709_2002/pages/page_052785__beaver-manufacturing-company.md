# Beaver Manufacturing Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Beaver Manufacturing Company is headquartered at 12 Ed Needham Drive in Mansfield, Georgia 30055.
- The company provides engineered fiber solutions and high-performance technical fibers for vehicles, including hoses, power transmission belts, air springs, couplings, and tires.
- Beaver transforms technical yarns such as aramid, polyester, polyvinyl alcohol, rayon, nylon, and glass into advanced high-tech components that are ultra-strong, resilient, and adaptable.
- The company's engineered fibers are used for reinforcement, strength, and performance in hoses, power transmission belts, mechanical rubber goods, and fiber optic cables across markets including agricultural, aerospace, chemical, construction, energy, equipment, food and beverage, information technology, marine, general industrial, and transportation.
- Beaver offers high-performance fibers designed to withstand extreme conditions for umbilical, subsea, bundle, hydraulic, fracking, and drilling hoses used in onshore and offshore marine applications.
- The company claims to offer more types of industrial fiber than any other single supplier, with an emphasis on sustainability.
- Beaver Manufacturing Company operates additional facilities in Tepeji del Río, Hidalgo, Mexico, and Mühlhausen/Thüringen, Germany.
