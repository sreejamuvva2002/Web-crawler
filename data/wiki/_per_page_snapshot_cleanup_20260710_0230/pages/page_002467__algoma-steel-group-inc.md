## Facts

- Algoma Steel Group Inc. is a leading Canadian producer of hot and cold rolled steel sheet and plate products.
- The company supplies steel products to customers in North America.
- Its products serve applications in the automotive, construction, energy, defense, and manufacturing sectors.
- Algoma operates a state-of-the-art Direct Strip Production Complex (DSPC), which is one of the lowest-cost producers of hot rolled sheet steel (HRC) in North America.
- The company is modernizing its plate mill and adopting electric arc technology to significantly lower carbon emissions as part of a transformation toward becoming a leading producer of green steel.
- Algoma Steel Group Inc. will release its 2025 first quarter financial results after the market closes on Tuesday, April 29, 2025.
- A webcast and conference call to review Q1 2025 results will be held on Wednesday, April 30, 2025, at 11:00 a.m. Eastern Time.
- Michael Moraca serves as Vice President – Corporate Development and Treasurer for Algoma Steel Inc.
