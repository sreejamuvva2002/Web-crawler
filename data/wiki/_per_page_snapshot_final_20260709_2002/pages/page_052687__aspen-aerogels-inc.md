# Aspen Aerogels, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2024-08-19

**Source:** [Why Aspen Aerogels Shares Are Seeing Blue Skies On Wednesday — TradingView News](https://www.tradingview.com/news/benzinga:7b15bac40094b:0-why-aspen-aerogels-shares-are-seeing-blue-skies-on-wednesday/)  ·  `page_052687`

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels, Inc. won a conditional commitment for a proposed DOE loan worth up to $670.6 million.
- The loan is part of the Advanced Technology Vehicles Manufacturing program.
- The loan will finance the construction of Aspen’s second aerogel manufacturing facility in Register, Georgia.
- Both the DOE and Aspen Aerogels must meet specific technical, legal, environmental, and financial conditions before finalizing the loan agreement and disbursing funds.
- The Register Plant will manufacture PyroThin aerogel blankets, ultrathin thermal barriers designed to prevent thermal runaway in batteries.
- The plant is projected to generate $1.2 billion to $1.6 billion in initial revenue.
- The plant is projected to create up to 550 construction jobs and 255 permanent positions.
- Donald R. Young, Aspen’s President and CEO, stated that PyroThin can enable increased battery safety and performance and solves an important and challenging problem.
- Young noted the company is excited to further the DOE’s mission to onshore and re-shore domestic manufacturing technologies critical to meeting the federal goal of having half of all new vehicles sold in 2030 be zero-emissions vehicles.
- Aspen announced preliminary results for the third quarter of 2024, expecting quarterly revenue of approximately $117 million compared to a consensus of $95.5 million.
- The company expects a net loss of around $13 million for Q3 2024, which includes a one-time charge of $27.5 million related to the extinguishment of its convertible note on August 19, 2024.
- Aspen sees adjusted EBITDA of approximately $25 million for the quarter.
- As of September 30, the company held about $113 million in cash and cash equivalents.
- Aspen plans to report its third-quarter results on November 7, 2024.
- Following the announcement, Aspen Aerogels (ASPN) shares rose 13.1% to $25.38.
