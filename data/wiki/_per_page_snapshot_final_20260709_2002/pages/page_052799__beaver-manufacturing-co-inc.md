# Beaver Manufacturing Co. Inc.

## Facts

- Beaver Manufacturing Co. Inc., a Georgia-based manufacturer of industrial fiber for the reinforcement of hose and mechanical rubber goods, opened a new plant in Tepeji, Hidalgo.
- The amount of investment related to the new operation remained undisclosed.
- The number of job openings related to the new operation remained undisclosed.
- The facility will expand Beaver’s global presence and increases its converting capacity of meta- and para-aramid, rayon and polyester products that support automotive, oil and gas and other industrial applications in Mexico and South America.
- The headquarters and main facility in Mansfield will continue to operate in its full capacity.
- The addition of the Mexico plant will complement operations at the main facility without disruption and help the company efficiently handle projections for growth.
- Mike Dubin, senior vice president and chief operating officer at Beaver, stated the new plant is in a prime location for expanding the company's aramid product market.
- Major growth is projected in Mexico and South America for the automotive and oil and gas industries, according to Mike Dubin.
