# JTEKT North America / Koyo Bearings North America LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [JTEKT - Dahlonega Plant | Manufacturers - Dahlonega-Lumpkin Chamber of Commerce - Dahlonega-Lumpkin County Chamber of Commerce](https://members.dlcchamber.org/list/member/jtekt-dahlonega-plant-453.htm)  ·  `page_045295`

## Facts

- JTEKT North America was established in 2013 bringing together all of JTEKT’s global products under one regional umbrella.
- The companies that make up JTEKT North America have been doing business since 1958.
- JTEKT North America has over 5,400 people and 14 manufacturing facilities.
- JTEKT North America supplies bearings, driveline components, steering systems, and machine tools.
- Koyo Bearings North America LLC operates as the US operations for Koyo Brand Ball, Needle, and Roller Bearings.
- Koyo Bearings is a major supplier to both OE and aftermarket customers in a wide variety of industries including: Agri-con, Automotive, Electric Motor Repair, Machine Tool, Power Sports, Steel, and Wind.
- Koyo Bearings North America LLC was formerly doing business under the names Koyo Corporation of USA and Koyo Bearings USA, LLC.
- The company operates a manufacturing plant in Dahlonega.
