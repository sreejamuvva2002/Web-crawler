# Stryten Energy LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-19

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Debuts Trailblazing Mobile Microgrid Solution to Advance U.S. Energy Resilience at CES 2025](https://www.bastillepost.com/global/article/4421168-stryten-energy-debuts-trailblazing-mobile-microgrid-solution-to-advance-u-s-energy-resilience-at-ces-2025)  ·  `page_050500`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC is a U.S.-based energy storage solutions provider.
- Stryten Energy will spotlight Reluctance, an innovative mobile microgrid example of a resilient energy ecosystem, at CES 2025 in Las Vegas.
- Reluctance is a modified concept Jeep by Stryten that can bring power to remote terrain for off-grid adventures or provide disaster relief with emergency power resources.
- The hybrid diesel-electric vehicle is enabled by proprietary software that efficiently manages a variety of energy sources, both carbon-based and renewable, and effectively stores that power for use on demand during prolonged power outages.
- Mike Judd serves as the President and CEO of Stryten Energy.
- Mike Judd stated that Reluctance challenges the status quo with an alternative approach to energy resiliency and emphasized capturing all energy produced from renewables and hydrocarbons in stationary energy storage solutions and mobile systems.
- Stryten Energy focuses on strengthening U.S. energy resilience with a suite of domestically manufactured energy storage solutions.
- Stryten utilizes a technology-agnostic Earth to Energy™ model focused on building a sustainable, domestic supply chain for each battery chemistry in its product portfolio.
- Stryten uses the circular economy of lead batteries as the model for lithium and vanadium energy storage solutions.
- Stryten is creating an energy ecosystem of the future with a battery-first design that provides grid stability and drives efficient use of power generated from renewable and carbon-based sources.
- This approach enables long-duration energy storage that can be scaled in size and duration to meet the needs of utilities, commercial and industrial, military bases and emergency response applications.
- Mike Judd stated that batteries are critical to achieving energy resilience and that no single technology can do it all.
- Mike Judd noted that Stryten Energy’s domestic, vertically integrated supply chain is the foundation for the energy ecosystem of the future that will set the U.S. on the path to energy independence.
- At CES, Stryten Energy will host two panel discussions with industry experts and government officials exploring grid modernization, battery supply chains, and energy storage systems critical to achieving energy resilience and security.
- Stryten Energy helps solve energy challenges with a broad range of energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military and Government sectors.
- Stryten Energy is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Stryten partners with some of the world’s most recognized companies to meet the growing demand for reliable and sustainable energy storage capacity.
- Stryten powers applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains and trucks.
- Stryten's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers and energy performance management software.
- Stryten is backed by more than a century of expertise.
