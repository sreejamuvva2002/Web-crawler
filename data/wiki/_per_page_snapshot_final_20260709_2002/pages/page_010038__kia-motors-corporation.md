# Kia Motors Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-07-12

**Source:** [Kia to invest $200M in Georgia plant to build EV9 SUV | TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2023/07/12/kia-to-invest-200m-in-georgia-plant-to-build-ev9-suv/)  ·  `page_010038`

## Facts

- South Korean automaker Kia plans to invest over $200 million in its assembly plant in Georgia.
- Kia will begin production of the electric EV9 SUV at the Georgia plant next year.
- Foreign automakers are increasing North American production to qualify for federal EV tax credits.
- Vehicle assembly in North America is required for eligibility for up to $7,500 in federal EV tax credits under the Inflation Reduction Act (IRA).
- The Inflation Reduction Act was signed in August 2022.
- Hyundai Motor Group, which owns Kia, is working with battery manufacturer SK On to build a new battery-manufacturing plant in Georgia.
- SK Innovation has opened a $2.6 billion battery plant in Georgia to produce batteries for Ford.
- Kia will add about 200 jobs at the Georgia plant to build the EV9 SUV and four other models.
- The EV9 will be Kia's first EV assembled in the United States.
- Kia aims to hit $122 billion in gross revenue by 2030 as part of its push to increase EV sales.
- Kia's electrification strategy is backed by a $24 billion investment.
- Kia aims to sell 1 million EVs by 2026 as part of its electrification strategy.
- Kia aims to begin operating its first dedicated EV factory in Gwangmyeong in 2024.
- Kia plans to release 15 new EV models by 2027.
- Kia first revealed the EV9 SUV in March.
- The EV9 is considered well-suited for the North American market due to its affordability, size, and range.
- The EV9 can act as a mobile power source.
- The EV9 features an array of sensors including cameras, lidar, and radar to enable an advanced driver assistance system.
- The EV9 represents a brand shift for Kia from a reputation for cheap airport rentals to a high-tech company.
- The EV9's infotainment and software suite enables over-the-air updates for nearly every function on the car.
- Over-the-air updates for nearly every function are a first for Kia.
