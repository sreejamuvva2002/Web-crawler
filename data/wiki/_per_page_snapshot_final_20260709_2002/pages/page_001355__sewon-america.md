# Sewon America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-10

**Source:** [Sewon America to build $300 million facility in Georgia](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/electricvehicles/sewon-america-to-build-300-million-facility-in-georgia)  ·  `page_001355`

## Facts

- Sewon America is a subsidiary of Korean-based Sewon Precision Industry Co.
- Sewon America announced plans to build a $300 million facility in Rincon, Ga.
- The new facility will create 740 jobs.
- The plant will produce electric vehicle body parts for OEMs.
- Operations at the new Rincon facility are expected to begin in 2025.
- Sewon America opened a manufacturing facility in LaGrange, Ga., in 2008.
- The LaGrange facility has since doubled in size.
- Following the completion of the Rincon facility, Sewon is expected to support 1,600 jobs in Georgia.
