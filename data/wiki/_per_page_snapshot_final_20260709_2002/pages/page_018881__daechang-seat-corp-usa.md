# Daechang Seat Corp. USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-10

**Source:** [Automotive Supplier Daechang Seat Corp. USA to Create 500 Jobs in Chatham County - Savannah CEO](https://savannahceo.com/features/2023/10/automotive-supplier-daechang-seat-corp-usa-create-500-jobs-chatham-county/)  ·  `page_018881`

## Facts

- Governor Brian P. Kemp announced that Daechang Seat Corp. USA will invest $72.5 million in the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- The facility is located in close proximity to Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA).
- The project will create over 500 jobs.
- Daechang Seat Corp. USA specializes in automotive seat frames.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that suppliers for the Hyundai Metaplant project alone reach at least nine counties.
- Jinsuk Lee, CEO of Daechang Seat Savannah Corp., expressed appreciation to the State of Georgia, local communities, and the City of Savannah for creating improved opportunities and employment prospects in Chatham County and the Savannah area.
- Daechang Seat Corp. (DSC) was established in 1979 in Kyungju, South Korea.
- DSC has become one of the largest seat frame producers in the world.
- Globally, DSC employs more than 5,000 people across seven countries.
- The company's production process includes fully automatic lines with arc welding and spot-welding machines.
- DSC's primary objective is to drive growth through facility expansions, ultimately transforming into a global entity.
- DSC currently holds a prominent partnership with Hyundai Transys and Hyundai Mobis at HMGMA.
- DSC hopes to collaborate with even more projects in the future.
- DSC’s new facility will be located at the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- The company will hire for assembly operators, equipment technicians, mechanical engineers, and electrical engineers through local job fairs and website postings in the future.
- Griff Lynch, Georgia Ports Authority President and CEO, noted that the facility is only minutes from the Port of Savannah.
- Trip Tollison, Savannah Economic Development Authority President and CEO, announced that Daechang Seat Corp. USA will be the third Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America supplier to locate in the Savannah Chatham Manufacturing Center.
- Alyce Thornhill, Director of Project Implementation and Supplier Strategy at the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce team, represented GDEcD on this competitive project.
- The project was a partnership with the Savannah Economic Development Authority, Georgia Ports Authority, Georgia Power, and Georgia Quick Start.
- Pat Wilson, GDEcD Commissioner, stated that countless partners are helping transform Georgia into a center of the automotive and mobility industry.
- Since 2018, Georgia has pursued the entire EV supply chain, creating more than $25 billion in investments and 30,000 jobs.
- In fiscal year 2023, job creation in the automotive industry increased by 324 percent compared to FY21.
- Suppliers attracted by Georgia’s mobility original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) resulted in over $2 billion in investment across the state in FY23.
