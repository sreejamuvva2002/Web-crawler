## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. has completed its 400,000-square-foot manufacturing facility, ARC, in Covington, Georgia, adjacent to Covington Municipal Airport.
- The ARC facility will produce the Midnight eVTOL aircraft.
- Production at the facility is scheduled to start in early 2025.
- Archer aims to produce two aircraft per month by the end of 2025.
- The company plans to manufacture 650 aircraft annually by 2030.
- Archer is partnering with Stellantis, which provides capital and expertise for the project.
- The facility represents a $118 million investment.
- Governor Brian Kemp praised the project for its innovation and job creation potential.
- Congressman Mike Collins praised the project for its innovation and job creation potential.
- The project aims to transform urban travel with sustainable, low-noise aircraft.
