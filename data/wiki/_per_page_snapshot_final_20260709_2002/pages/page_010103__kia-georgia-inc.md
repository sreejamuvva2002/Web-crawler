## Facts

- Kia Georgia, Inc. is the first manufacturing site in North America for Kia Motors Corporation.
- Kia Motors Corporation is based in Seoul, South Korea.
- Kia Georgia is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Georgia.
- Kia Georgia began mass production on Nov. 16, 2009.
