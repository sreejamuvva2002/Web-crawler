## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America (YMMC) operates a manufacturing facility in Newnan, Georgia.
- YMMC produces golf cars, personal watercraft, Side-by-Side vehicles, and all-terrain vehicles (ATVs).
- YMMC holds ISO9001 Quality Management System and ISO14001 Environmental Management System certifications for quality, safety, and environmental manufacturing.
- Yamaha Motor Corp., USA (YMUS) maintains two corporate offices in Georgia, including one in Marietta.
- YMUS has factory operations in Tennessee and Georgia, along with facilities in Wisconsin and Alabama.
- YMUS product portfolio includes power assist bicycles, golf cars, ATVs, Side-by-Side vehicles, motorcycles, scooters, snowmobiles, WaveRunner personal watercraft, boats, outboard motors, outdoor power equipment, power assist wheelchair systems, surface mount technology (SMT), robotic machines, and unmanned helicopters.
- Bob Brown serves as Vice President, Finance and Operations Support at YMMC.
- YMUS operates additional U.S.-based subsidiaries including Yamaha Marine Systems Company (YMSC), Skeeter Boats, and Yamaha Precision Propeller.
