## Facts

- Tonya Hicks serves as the President and CEO of Power Solutions, Inc.
- Power Solutions, Inc. was founded in 2000 by Tonya Hicks at age 28.
- The company specializes in electrical contracting, facility maintenance, renewable energy, electrification, and EV charging solutions.
- Power Solutions has expanded its operations internationally, with its success featured in Forbes Japan in November 2019.
- Tonya Hicks was the first woman to become a Journeyman Inside Wireman Electrician in the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers (IBEW), Local 917.
- Tonya Hicks was the first Black Woman to become an IBEW Journeyman Electrician in the State of Mississippi.
- During the pandemic, Tonya Hicks founded she-EV, Inc. to provide safer EV charging locations for women, elderly, and disabled persons.
- The she-EV Car Club is scheduled to launch in 2024 as the first of its kind, providing community support, educational resources, and trusted products.
- Tonya Hicks launched Women Do Everything LLC in 2015 to promote diversity and inclusion by training and supporting women in male-dominated industries through employment and entrepreneurship.
- Tonya Hicks participated in the Leadership Atlanta Class of 2022.
- Tonya Hicks is recognized as a Sustainability Ambassador for the City of Atlanta.
- Tonya Hicks was named Minority Contractor of the Year by the National Association of Minority Contractors (NAMC) – Atlanta Chapter in 2021.
- Tonya Hicks was included in Project Drawdown’s Drawdown Neighborhood Series for Atlanta, Ga., due to her expertise in climate change.
- Tonya Hicks was selected to participate in the Leadership America program by Leadership Women in 2023.
- Tonya Hicks was honored as one of the 2023 Top 100 Women in Construction by Top 100 Women.
