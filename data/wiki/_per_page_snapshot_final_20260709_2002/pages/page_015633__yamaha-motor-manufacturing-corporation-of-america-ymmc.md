# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America (YMMC)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2020-08-31

**Source:** [YAMAHA HIRING OVER 100 NEW EMPLOYEES IN NEWNAN, GEORGIA - Coweta County Development Authority](https://choosecoweta.com/2020/08/31/yamaha-hiring-over-100-new-employees-in-newnan-georgia/)  ·  `page_015633`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation of America announced plans to immediately hire over 100 new employees in Newnan, Georgia.
- The hiring is intended to assist with assembly line functions to keep pace with growing demand for recreational products assembled at the Newnan facility.
- YMMC designs and assembles four key product lines: Golf Cars, All Terrain Vehicles (ATVs), Personal Watercraft, and Recreational Off-road Vehicles (ROVs/Side-by-Sides).
- Vice President of Finance and Operations Support Bob Brown stated that demand for outdoor products increased as people sought family and friends activities while maintaining social distance.
- The company is seeking eager new talent to immediately join the team to help assemble products for customers.
- Brown noted that Yamaha's worldwide reputation stems from the quality, reliability, and durability built into each product, which results from employee dedication and skill.
- The company aims to expand its production capacity through the addition of new team members.
- Coweta County Development Authority President Trae Westmoreland described YMMC as the largest private employer in Coweta County and an essential community partner for over 30 years.
- Westmoreland highlighted that industrial and economic growth go hand in hand and expressed excitement over increases in labor productivity and standard of living during unprecedented times.
- Coweta County Development Authority Chairman Ronnie Clotfelter stated that YMMC has been an integral stakeholder for many years, providing a solid foundation for Coweta County.
- Open positions include assemblers, material handlers, welders, certified forklift operators, and other roles.
- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Corporation was formed in 1986 and began manufacturing units in 1988 at the Newnan location.
- In January 2015, YMMC manufactured its 3 millionth unit.
- The company celebrated 30 years of manufacturing at its Newnan location in June 2018.
- Products are marketed through Yamaha Motor Corporation, USA (YMUS), a recognized leader in the outdoor recreation industry.
- YMUS product offerings include Motorcycles and Scooters, ATV and Side-by-Side vehicles, Snowmobiles, WaveRunner Personal Watercraft, Boats, Outboard Motors, Outdoor Power Equipment, Power Assist Bicycles, Golf Cars, Power Assist Wheelchair Systems, Surface Mount Technology (SMT), Robotic Machines, Unmanned Helicopters, Accessories, Apparel, Yamalube products, and more.
- YMUS sells its products through a nationwide network of distributors and dealers in the United States.
- YMUS maintains a corporate office in California, two corporate offices in Georgia, facilities in Wisconsin and Alabama, and factory operations in Tennessee and Georgia.
- Additional U.S.-based subsidiaries include Yamaha Marine Systems Company (with divisions Bennett Marine in Florida and Kracor Systems in Wisconsin), Skeeter Boats in Texas (with division G3 Boats in Missouri), and Yamaha Precision Propeller in Indiana.
