# Pirelli Tire North America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-01-08

**Source:** [The Motor Enclave Tampa Announces Partnership With Pirelli Tire North America |

## Facts

- The Motor Enclave announced a sponsorship agreement with Pirelli Tire North America to become “The Official Tire” of its 200-acre experiential motorsports venue in Tampa, Florida.
- Pirelli will receive exposure throughout the development, most notably in the newly named “Pirelli Pit Lane” where events on the 1.6-mile Hermann Tilke-designed performance circuit begin and end.
- The venue will feature a “Pirelli Podium” showcasing authentic Pirelli Formula 1 tires for members and guests.
- Brad Oleshansky, Founder and CEO of The Motor Enclave, stated that Pirelli is synonymous with innovation and quality and aligns with their mission to build the premiere motorsports venue in the world.
- Andre Oosthuizen, Chief Marketing Officer of Pirelli Tire North America, noted they have tracked The Motor Enclave Tampa's development since it was announced in 2019.
- Oosthuizen added that after seeing the success of Oleshansky’s initial project in Michigan, M1 Concourse, they knew this venue would be a platform to highlight Pirelli’s full product line across multiple driving surfaces including a performance circuit, vehicle dynamics pad, and off-road experience.
- Pirelli was founded in Milan in 1872 and stands as a global brand known for cutting-edge technology, high-end production excellence, and innovation drawing on Italian roots.
- Pirelli’s product range includes innovative tires for cars, motorcycles, and bicycles developed through partnerships with prestigious manufacturers to satisfy sophisticated consumers.
- Pirelli Tire North America (PTNA) includes a Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) facility and research and development center at its Rome, Georgia headquarters.
- PTNA also operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant in Silao, Mexico, along with sales and marketing offices in New York City, Los Angeles, Detroit, Montreal, and Mexico City.
- The company manufactures, distributes, and markets original equipment and replacement tires for export and domestic car/motorcycle applications.
- The Motor Enclave development includes a 1.6-mile Hermann Tilke-designed driving circuit, a 2-acre vehicle dynamics pad, a 100-acre off-road experience with purpose-built trails, a 37,000-square-foot corporate event center, and over 300 privately owned luxury garages.
