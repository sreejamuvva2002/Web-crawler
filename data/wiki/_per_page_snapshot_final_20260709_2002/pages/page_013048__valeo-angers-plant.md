# Valeo Angers Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Valeo Angers Completes Modernization for Next-Generation Lighting Systems | Auto-Innovations International](https://www.auto-innovations.net/application-stories/112544-valeo-angers-completes-modernization-for-next-generation-lighting-systems)  ·  `page_013048`

## Facts

- A €45 million investment transforms the historic French plant into a European competence center for complex optics and heavy-duty vehicle lighting.
- The Valeo industrial site in Angers finalized its €45 million modernization plan, rolled out between 2023 and 2026.
- The transformation aligns with the Valeo Elevate 2028 strategic plan to meet aggressive growth targets in the global automotive lighting market.
- The facility entirely overhauled its workshops and deployed new optical technologies to satisfy increasing demand from European manufacturers for complex bi-color optical lenses.
- Thermoplastic injection workshops were upgraded with new high-performance automated injection press lines.
- The high-tech facility operates with 25 injection presses, five varnishing lines, 11 assembly lines, and 181 robots.
- Manufacturing processes are optimized through the integration of digital tools and Artificial Intelligence for predictive simulation and real-time system validation.
- The site operates with over 1,200 highly qualified employees across Angers and Blois.
- Valeo's regional industrial operations supply 9,000 headlights and 22,000 complex modules daily to the world’s leading manufacturers.
- The local R&D center currently has 24 projects under development, with six major initiatives specifically allocated to the Angers plant.
- Jean-Luc Terrasse, CEO of Valeo’s Light Division, stated that lighting is at the heart of mobility challenges regarding safety, design, and intelligent interaction with the environment.
- The €45 million investment allows the factory to consolidate its industrial excellence and demonstrates that a French factory can design and produce systems of unprecedented optical complexity at the forefront of European Industry 4.0.
- Over the last three years, the site completed 168 new hires, including 15 specialized technicians joining the workshops in 2026 to configure newly installed presses.
- The company fosters long-term employability through an apprenticeship policy covering 6.3% of the workforce.
- The Valeo Lighting Academy provides over 3,150 hours of continuous training annually to maintain team excellence.
- Two major client launches are planned for 2027 and 2028.
- Production volume related to heavy-duty vehicles will double from 20% to 40%.
- By 2030, six out of seven European road transport leaders will utilize headlights developed in Angers.
- The Angers site is officially cemented as the Group's European Product and Process Competence Center for Heavy-Duty Vehicle front headlights.
