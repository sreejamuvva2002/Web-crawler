# FORVIA HELLA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-06-19

**Source:** [HELLA Heads To Pikes Peak With Robb Holland, a Porsche 911 GT3 RS, Cayman GT4 RS, Race Sponsorship & Special Awards](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/hella-heads-pikes-peak-robb-143500976.html)  ·  `page_055817`

## Facts

- HELLA GmbH & Co. KGaA ("FORVIA HELLA") sponsors the 2025 Time Attack 1 Division Award at the 2025 Pikes Peak International Hill Climb.
- HELLA will provide the HELLA Hype & HELLA Heritage Awards plus cash rewards for the event.
- Pikes Peak veteran Robb Holland will drive a HELLA-sponsored 2022 Porsche 911 GT3 RS Cup car prepared by Rotek Racing.
- Veteran Clint Vahsholtz will drive a HELLA-sponsored 2023 Porsche 718 Cayman GT4 RS Clubsport.
- HELLA is continuing a program to further increase its brand awareness in categories including brake systems, leveraging its Original Equipment competency and history with high-performance vehicles.
- Robb Holland's 911 features minimal upgrades for the climb except JRZ active dampers and a Starlink datalink to livestream his attempt at the 103rd running of the PPIHC on June 22.
- The race climbs to a 14,115ft summit over a public street course.
- Holland is expected to be the first driver to livestream his Pikes Peak attempt for fans.
- Robb Holland's racing career dates back to 2011 and he set a front-wheel drive record at his first Pikes Peak attempt in 2016.
- Holland is using extensive experience and simulator preparation to aim for the HELLA Time Attack 1 class winner's trophy.
- Clint Vahsholtz's Cayman GT4 RS won both the 2025 Pirelli GT America and GT4 America road racing championships.
- Vahsholtz aims to claim the Pikes Peak GT4 Trophy by Yokohama driving the car with a HELLA livery.
- Clint Vahsholtz has competed at Pikes Peak since 1992, initially rode motorcycles before switching to cars in 1996, and holds 24 class wins predominantly in Super Stock and Open Wheel.
- Robert Tinson, Vice President at HELLA, stated the company celebrates more than 125 years of engineering excellence and shares the spirit of innovation, performance, and endurance exemplified by the race.
- The Time Attack 1 Division includes close-bodied modified and specialized production-based two- and four-wheel drive vehicles with eligibility restricted to original configuration for air/fuel delivery, number of cylinders, and number of drive wheels.
- The division is dominated by Porsche models but also includes a Subaru Impreza WRX STI, BMW M2 CS Racing, Mitsubishi Lancer Evo, and others.
- The HELLA Hype Award allows fans to nominate drivers via online voting at Fan Fest on Friday June 20, with the winner receiving a $2000 reward presented by Vice President Robert Tinson.
- The HELLA Heritage Award plus $2000 cash reward will be presented to the driver with the most finishes during their competitive career in the historic race.
- All trophies and awards will be presented during the PPIHC trophy ceremony.
- FORVIA HELLA is an international automotive supplier specializing in high-performance lighting technology, vehicle electronics, and Lifecycle Solutions for spare parts, workshop business, and special vehicle manufacturers.
- FORVIA HELLA has approximately 36,500 employees across over 125 locations worldwide and generated €8.1 billion in adjusted sales in the 2024 financial year.
- Parent company FORVIA is the seventh largest supplier of automotive technology globally, combining strengths of Faurecia and HELLA with approximately 260 industrial sites, 78 R&D centers, over 150,000 employees (including more than 15,000 R&D engineers) in over 40 countries.
- FORVIA holds a portfolio of over 13,000 patents and aims to be the preferred innovation and integration partner for automotive manufacturers worldwide.
- Contact details for HELLA Automotive Sales, Inc are located in Peachtree City, GA.
