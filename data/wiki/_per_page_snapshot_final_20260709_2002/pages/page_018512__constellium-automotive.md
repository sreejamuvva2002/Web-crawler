# Constellium Automotive

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-20

**Source:** [Constellium Workers Ratify New Contract, Win Strong Pay Increases, More - UAW | United Automobile, Aerospace and Agricultural Implement Workers of America](https://uaw.org/constellium-workers-ratify-new-contract-win-strong-pay-increases/)  ·  `page_018512`

## Facts

- UAW Local 174 members at Constellium Automotive in Van Buren Charter Township, MI, ratified a new contract after standing on the picket line for nearly a month.
- The ratified agreement provides improved contractual language, including a stronger grievance procedure and additional holidays.
- The agreement secures strong pay increases for workers.
- UAW Local 174 members returned to work on Monday following the contract ratification.
- Region 1A Director Laura Dickerson stated that contract negotiations were very difficult because the company was unwilling to work with them and repeatedly cancelled negotiating sessions.
- Laura Dickerson noted that members stood together and forced Constellium to offer a contract that reflects their immense value to the company.
- Constellium Van Buren supplies parts for the Ford F-150, F-150 Lightning, Explorer, and Super Duty at six UAW-represented Ford Assembly plants.
