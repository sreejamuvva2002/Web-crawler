# Essex Solutions

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-08-19

**Source:** [Superior Essex Announces Essex Solutions, Relaunching Magnet Wire Brand | Essex Solutions](https://essexenergy.eu/2024/08/19/superior-essex-announces-essex-solutions-relaunching-magnet-wire-brand/)  ·  `page_010361`

## Facts

- Superior Essex, a global leader in the magnet wire and communication cables industry, is announcing the next phase in the rebranding of its magnet wire business.
- As part of its recent buyout of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, the company will change its brand name to Essex Solutions.
- Under the new name, Essex Solutions will encompass all current Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire locations worldwide.
- Essex Energy in Italy will now be included in the organization, further reinforcing the company’s global reach and capabilities.
- The related businesses Lacroix + Kress, IVA Insulations, and Hi-Wire will join the Essex Solutions organization while continuing to operate under their existing brand identities.
- This consolidation will enable better coordination and collaboration among the brands, resulting in improved efficiency and effectiveness.
- Daniel Choi, President and CEO of Superior Essex, stated that aligning the brands under one grouping allows the company to offer a complete suite of solutions for customers and take further advantage of vertical integration and global reach.
- Choi noted the company's ability to proactively shape the xEV automotive industry, as well as power, electrification, and renewable energy markets experiencing rapid transformations.
- During the transition period, customers and stakeholders may see materials bearing either the existing Essex Furukawa or the new Essex Solutions name.
- All brand name changes are expected to be completed by April 30, 2025.
- Essex Solutions will unveil a new logo in the near future to reflect its evolution and commitment to innovation and excellence.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Solutions, Superior Essex Communications, IVA, Lacroix + Kress, Hi-Wire, and Essex Brownell.
- The company has over 3,000 employees across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is instrumental in creating disruptive technological advancements in the electrification of the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- The company is committed to pioneering smart building technology while setting the pace on sustainability.
- Essex Solutions maintains an office at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
