## Facts

- The office building spans 119,343 square feet.
- The facility is located in Greensboro, NC.
- West and Stem was hired as part of a design and construction team for the three-story office building.
- The design objective was to introduce a new, more efficient, flexible and collaborative working environment within walking distance of Volvo Group North America's main campus.
- The building houses product, customer, and dealer support personnel.
- The building houses groups responsible for parts, logistics, and shared business services.
- The building has expansion capacity for an additional 45,000 square feet.
