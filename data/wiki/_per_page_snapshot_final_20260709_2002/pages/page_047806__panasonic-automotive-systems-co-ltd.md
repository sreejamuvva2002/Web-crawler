# Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-03-29

**Source:** [Apollo Funds Announce Strategic Partnership with Panasonic Automotive Systems, a Leading Global Supplier of Advanced In-Vehicle Technologies – Company Announcement - FT.com](https://markets.ft.com/data/announce/detail?dockey=1330-9081482en-5BO22GKFV2GG3L90J2M30TJ987)  ·  `page_047806`

## Facts

- Apollo Funds entered into a definitive agreement to acquire a majority stake in Panasonic Automotive Systems Corporation from Panasonic Holdings Corporation.
- The transaction is valued at a total enterprise value of ¥311 billion, subject to certain adjustments at closing.
- Panasonic Holdings Corporation will retain a minority stake in the company following the acquisition.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Corporation will maintain its strategic relationship with the Panasonic Group.
- Panasonic Automotive is a tier one global automotive supplier that designs and manufactures advanced infotainment systems and other in-vehicle solutions for leading auto manufacturers.
- The company has a longstanding track record of technological and manufacturing innovation and benefits from strong market positions across its core segments with customer relationships spanning multiple decades.
- Tetsuji Okamoto serves as Partner and Head of Japan at Apollo.
- Michael Reiss serves as Partner at Apollo.
- Yuki Kusumi is Panasonic’s Group CEO.
- Masashi Nagayasu is Representative Director, CEO of Panasonic Automotive.
- Subject to satisfaction of customary closing conditions including regulatory approvals, the transaction is expected to be completed by the end of the first quarter of 2025.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Co., Ltd. was established on April 1, 2022.
- The company provides advanced technologies such as infotainment systems to automakers around the world.
- The company has annual sales of JPY1,297.5 billion.
- The company operates in 22 countries and regions around the world and has about 30,000 employees.
- Apollo is a high-growth, global alternative asset manager with approximately $651 billion of assets under management as of December 31, 2023.
- Panasonic Holdings Corporation reported consolidated net sales of 8,378.9 billion yen for the year ended March 31, 2023.
