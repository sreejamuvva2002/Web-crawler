# Kumho Tire

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Kumho Tire Signs Partnership with Ansible Motion to Develop Next-Generation Digital Tires < Business Exchange < Business & Economy < Article - The World Korea](https://www.twk.co.kr/news/articleView.html?idxno=1403)  ·  `page_056692`

## Facts

- Partnership agreement was signed late last year at the Kumho Tire Yongin Central Research Institute in Gyeonggi Province.
- Attendees included Kim Young-jin, Executive Vice President and Head of R&D at Kumho Tire; Kim Gi-woon, Executive Vice President and Head of the VPD Center; Dan Clark, CEO of Ansible Motion; and Salman Safdar, Sales Director of Ansible Motion.
- Operating under the slogan “Kumho Tire with Ansible Motion: Driving the Future with Digital Tires,” the two companies will collaborate to enhance tire performance validation systems using advanced digital simulation technologies.
- The partnership aims to strengthen competitiveness in tire development for electric vehicles (EVs) and high-performance vehicles.
- Ansible Motion is a global leader in high-fidelity driving simulator technology that accurately reproduces real-world driving conditions.
- Its solutions enable virtual verification of key driving characteristics such as vehicle dynamics, driving safety, and ride comfort.
- By integrating Ansible Motion’s latest driving simulators into its R&D processes, Kumho Tire expects to significantly reduce the time and cost associated with physical vehicle testing and prototype development.
- Kumho Tire anticipates achieving more precise and efficient performance validation from the early stages of product development.
- This initiative is part of Kumho Tire’s broader strategy to respond more rapidly to the evolving mobility landscape while strengthening its digital R&D capabilities.
- Kim Young-jin, Executive Vice President and Head of R&D at Kumho Tire, stated: “Collaboration with Ansible Motion marks an important step in advancing Kumho Tire’s R&D paradigm toward a digital-centered approach.”
- Kim Young-jin added: “By actively leveraging advanced simulation technologies, we will introduce high-performance, high-value-added products optimized for future mobility and continue to enhance our global competitiveness.”
- Dan Clark, CEO of Ansible Motion, commented: “This partnership with Kumho Tire will serve as a meaningful example of how driving simulation technology can accelerate innovation in the tire industry.”
- Dan Clark added: “We look forward to contributing to the advancement of the global mobility industry through the technical synergy between our two companies.”
- The partnership forms part of Kumho Tire’s ongoing digital transformation and sustainable technology innovation strategy.
- Kumho Tire plans to apply advanced driving simulation technologies across its entire R&D process to further improve development efficiency and precision.
- Through these efforts, Kumho Tire aims to accelerate the development of next-generation digital tires and secure differentiated technological competitiveness in the EV and high-performance tire markets.
