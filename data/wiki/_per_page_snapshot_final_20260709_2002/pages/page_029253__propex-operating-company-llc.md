# Propex Operating Company, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2013-06-03

**Source:** [Propex Announces Georgia Expansion Plan](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/propex-announces-georgia-expansion-plan-209961981.html)  ·  `page_029253`

## Facts

- Propex Operating Company, LLC will expand its carpet backings manufacturing facility in Hazlehurst, GA.
- The expansion involves adding jobs and investing capital to increase production capacity.
- The investment specifically targets increased capacity to produce Isis®, a new innovative platform of backings used in commercial carpet tile manufacturing.
- Isis is the first recycled polyester woven backing for carpet tile.
- Propex introduced Isis in 2011 after years of development.
- Prior to this expansion, Isis was developed and manufactured by Propex at their facility in Gronau, Germany.
- Increasing global demand for Isis necessitates further capacity investment to better serve customers.
- Dave Gartshore, Vice President of Propex Furnishings Solutions and Curv® Composites Divisions, stated that manufacturing Isis in Georgia demonstrates a commitment to providing innovative products to support the continued growth of the US carpet industry.
- Mike Gorey, President and CEO of Propex, commented that Propex is committed to innovation through collaboration with partnered customers to drive sustainable value creation and that this expansion supports their vision as a leader in innovative flooring products.
- Furnishing Solutions by Propex develops backing materials that support new products and revolutionize industries.
- For more than 100 years, the company has brought innovation and leadership to the markets it serves.
- Propex develops engineered solutions for flooring, recreation, construction, engineering, erosion control, water management, and transportation industries.
