# Superior Essex

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Superior Essex, Essex Furukawa Partner With Waybridge On Agile Digitization Technology - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.ms/news/superior-essex-essex-furukawa-partner-with-waybridge-on-agile-digitization-technology/)  ·  `page_010701`

## Facts

- Superior Essex announced an expanded partnership with Waybridge, a raw material supply chain operating system.
- The announcement continues Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire's Vision 2030 initiative through agile digitization of manufacturing plants in Europe, North America, and Malaysia.
- Superior Essex is an early adopter of the Waybridge platform, utilizing its technology at two North American magnet wire manufacturing plants (in the U.S. and Mexico) since August 2021.
- The new expansion will integrate Waybridge into nine new plants located in Canada, Germany, Italy, Serbia, and the U.S.
- Scott Wilde of NP Strategic Sourcing emphasized making innovation part of sourcing and holistic sustainability, describing the Waybridge partnership as both a technical extension and an innovation.
- Scott Wilde stated that aligning operations and predicting supply chain issues like stockouts before they impact customers is necessary to deliver optimal experiences for customers and suppliers.
- Wilde noted that partnering with Waybridge completely changed how the company views full inventory and end-to-end workflow processes, enabling access to critical forecasting information to address issues proactively.
- Inefficient supply chains and stockouts can cost companies more than direct sales losses by increasing logistics costs, damaging brand image, and hindering future demand.
- Stockout causes can be traced to increased demand, delivery delays, suppliers, human error, and unexpected inaccurate inventory data across multiple points in the supply chain.
- Waybridge's Stock & Flow feature directly addresses inventory challenges by aggregating data from delivery forecasts, real-time delivery locations, predicted ETAs, and production schedules to offer unmatched forecasting capabilities.
- In 2021 alone, Waybridge enabled Superior Essex to avoid four raw material stockouts that would have directly impacted customers.
- Through the interconnected Waybridge platform, Superior Essex can reduce company-wide Excel usage in procurement by 90% and cut email volume around procurement and tracking by 50%.
- Scott Evans, CEO and co-founder of Waybridge, praised Superior Essex as a forward-thinker in the commodity space for standardizing on a common platform to benefit customers and suppliers.
- Kyle McCarter, Chief Product Officer at Waybridge, highlighted that Superior Essex's product feedback has allowed Waybridge's product to continuously evolve, benefiting all customers.
- Superior Essex now requires all North American materials to be managed through Waybridge and plans to expand the technology to Asian plants in the near future.
- Superior Essex is the parent brand of Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire, Superior Essex Communications, and Essex Brownell.
- The company has over 3,000 employees across 11 countries on three continents.
- Superior Essex is a leading global magnet wire provider at the forefront of fiber communication applications and Power over Ethernet (PoE) technology.
- The company plays a role in creating disruptive technological advancements in automotive electrification, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Superior Essex is committed to pioneering smart building technology and advancing sustainability.
- Superior Essex's headquarters address is 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
