# Storion Energy LLC

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2024-12-19

**Source:** [Largo and Stryten Energy to Form Storion Energy for the Manufacture of Vanadium Electrolyte and Battery Components for Long Duration Energy Storage - Junior Mining Network](https://www.juniorminingnetwork.com/junior-miner-news/press-releases/663-tsx/lgo/172534-largo-and-stryten-energy-to-form-storion-energy-for-the-manufacture-of-vanadium-electrolyte-and-battery-components-for-long-duration-energy-storage)  ·  `page_050552`

## Facts

- Storion Energy LLC intends to become a leading U.S.-based manufacturer of vanadium electrolyte and develop additional vanadium flow battery solutions for the long duration energy storage sector.
- The company is established as a joint venture between Largo Clean Energy Corp., a subsidiary of Largo Inc., and Stryten Critical E-Storage, LLC, an affiliate of Stryten Energy LLC.
- Storion will operate from locations in Wilmington, Massachusetts and Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Upon closing, Stryten will pay US$1 million in cash directly to Largo and contribute a total of US$6 million in cash to Storion over time to fund operations.
- LCE and Stryten will each hold a 50% equity interest in Storion upon closing, with customary pre-emption rights and anti-dilution protections.
- Board representation for Storion will be generally proportional to ownership, with Stryten holding one additional seat as long as LCE and Stryten hold similar ownership interests.
- At closing, Largo will amend its Safekeeping and Supply Agreements with LPV to adjust commercial terms and assign the Safekeeping Agreement to Storion.
- At closing, Largo and Storion will enter into a separate supply agreement providing Storion a right of first offer to purchase vanadium products from LPV.
- Francesco D’Alessio, Chief Commercial Officer of Largo and President of LCE, stated the partnership aims to maximize the value of Largo’s vanadium products and leverage Storion’s patented purification process to accelerate vanadium electrolyte manufacture and deployment.
- The transaction is expected to increase demand for vanadium from Largo Physical Vanadium Corp. and benefit the vanadium sector as a whole.
- Storion Energy aims to remove barriers to entry for battery manufacturers by providing domestically sourced, price-competitive electrolyte for vanadium redox flow batteries used in long-duration energy storage solutions greater than four hours.
- VRFB technology is described as a safe and reliable option to provide long-duration energy storage greater than four hours to help ensure grid stability and facilitate increased utilization of renewables for businesses and consumers.
- Gallatin Capital LLC acted as financial advisor to Largo, and Linklaters LLP acted as legal counsel for the transaction.
- The joint venture is subject to customary closing conditions and forward-looking statements regarding execution of strategic goals and ability to raise additional funds.
