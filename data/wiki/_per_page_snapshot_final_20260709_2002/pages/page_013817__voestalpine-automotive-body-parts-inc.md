# voestalpine Automotive Body Parts Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-08-16

**Source:** [Updated: voestalpine Automotive Body Parts Inc. expanding in Bartow County, adding 150 jobs and investing $50 million. | Local | northwestgeorgianews.com](https://www.northwestgeorgianews.com/polk_standard_journal/news/local/updated-voestalpine-automotive-body-parts-inc-expanding-in-bartow-county-adding-150-jobs-and-investing/article_b60203ce-aa2c-503f-a9c3-4054d2593cc1.html)  ·  `page_013817`

## Facts

- voestalpine Automotive Body Parts Inc. is expanding in Bartow County, Georgia.
- The expansion adds 150 jobs.
- The company is investing $50 million.
- The announcement comes four years after their first commitment to come to Bartow County.
- New jobs created will range from entry-level manufacturing to high-paying engineering and administrative positions.
- The company currently employs about 100 workers at the facility.
- The company offers excellent training opportunities, including an apprenticeship program with the Cartersville-Bartow Economic Development Authority in Cartersville.
- voestalpine Automotive Body Parts Inc. is a subsidiary of the Voestalpine Group of Austria.
- The Voestalpine Group produces high-value added steel products, tools and construction components, as well as ready-made, developed and rolled finished products.
- Cartersville-Bartow Mayor Keith Maycock said they are proud to support Georgia’s investment in Cartersville.
- Mayor Keith Maycock noted voestalpine was the first company to build in Richland 40 and their major additions continue to contribute to growth and creation of jobs.
- The expansion of voestalpine and Richland is a testament to the connection between Cartersville, the City of Cartersville and all state and county partners.
- Georgia Governor Nathan Deal and Lt. Gov. Casey Cagle welcomed the announcement.
- As the state’s automotive industry grows and manufacturing companies continue to invest in Georgia, confidence is expressed that voestalpine will benefit from the workforce and resources available in Cartersville.
