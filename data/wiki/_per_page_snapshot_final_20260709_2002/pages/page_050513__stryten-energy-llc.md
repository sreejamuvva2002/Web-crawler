# Stryten Energy LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-20

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Adds Enterprise Capability to Motive Power Management Software](https://www.stryten.com/stryten-energy-adds-enterprise-capability-to-motive-power-management-software/)  ·  `page_050513`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC announced the launch of expanded functionality in the Monitor module of Stryten Energy inCOMMAND™ on March 20, 2023.
- Stryten Energy inCOMMAND, formerly GNB Cloud, is an energy performance management software tool that makes it easy to design and deploy web-connected battery and charger solutions.
- The platform includes modules for executing power studies, modeling battery and charger solutions, monitoring systems, and managing asset configuration.
- The enhanced Monitor module enables users to remotely manage motive power systems at scale.
- Multi-site customers can view battery health data organized by region or location and drill down to the individual asset level for complete visibility.
- Dealers can manage rental and service customers from a single dashboard within the software.
- The system utilizes self-adaptive IoT technology enabled by smart gateways to automatically update batteries equipped with PowerLoggers in the software when they move between customer sites for location tracking.
- The enhanced Monitor module tracks fleet energy usage, allowing customers to monitor site efficiency and identify opportunities to optimize performance and reduce costs across corporate fleets.
- For dealers, the software enables new monetization opportunities such as a “Power by the Hour” model that monitors energy usage and bills customers only for battery use instead of charging expensive monthly fees.
- Battery licenses are issued upfront with an easy one-time payment that guarantees data access for the life of the battery and includes capability for multiple users.
- Matt Gould serves as Vice President of Sales and Service, Industrial at Stryten Energy.
- Stryten Energy provides a broad range of energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- The company is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Stryten Energy partners with recognized companies to meet the growing demand for reliable and sustainable energy storage capacity.
- Stryten powers applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, microgrids, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- The company's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
