## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels, Inc. sells aerogel materials used in energy industrial applications, sustainable insulation, and electric vehicle (EV) markets.
- The company announced that it had halted construction of its second manufacturing facility in Statesboro, Georgia.
- Instead of building the new facility, Aspen Aerogels opted to maximize capacity at its existing East Providence plant and rely on external manufacturing.
- Aspen Aerogels (NYSE:ASPN) projected revenue between $75 million and $95 million for Q1 2025, significantly below analyst expectations of $104.2 million.
- The company reported strong Q4 2024 results but issued weak guidance for Q1 2025.
