# Macon-Bibb County Industrial Authority

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Making the Case in Canada: Why Global Companies Keep Choosing Macon-Bibb - MBCIA](https://mbcia.com/mbcia-news/seus-cp-conference-macon-bibb-global-investment/)  ·  `page_016197`

## Facts

- The MBCIA Executive Director represented Macon-Bibb County at the SEUS-CP Conference in New Brunswick, Canada in June.
- The SEUS-CP Conference is an annual gathering that brings together business-minded leaders from six Southeastern U.S. states and six Canadian provinces to focus on trade, investment, and economic development.
- Georgia’s delegation to the conference was led by Governor Brian Kemp.
- First Lady Marty Kemp joined the Georgia delegation at the conference.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson attended the conference as part of the state delegation.
- Deputy Commissioner of International Trade Lizann Grupalo was part of the Georgia delegation.
- International Relations Manager Laura Greenway represented Georgia at the SEUS-CP Conference.
- The MBCIA Executive Director visited the headquarters of Irving Consumer Products alongside Governor Brian Kemp, First Lady Marty Kemp, and state partners during the trip to Canada.
- Irving Consumer Products has invested more than $1.5 billion in Macon-Bibb County since 2017.
- Irving Consumer Products recently completed a $600 million expansion at its Irving Tissue facility in Macon.
- The recent Irving Consumer Products expansion is adding 100 new jobs at the Macon facility.
- Long-standing Japanese manufacturing partners operating in Macon-Bibb County include YKK USA, YKK AP, and Nichiha.
- South Korean tire manufacturer Kumho Tire has also chosen to invest in Macon-Bibb County.
- The MBCIA emphasizes building lasting partnerships with global companies rather than just landing projects.
- Macon-Bibb County leverages its workforce, location, infrastructure, and proven track record to compete for global investment.
- The MBCIA focuses on consistently showing up, following through, and delivering results to maintain trust with world-class companies.
