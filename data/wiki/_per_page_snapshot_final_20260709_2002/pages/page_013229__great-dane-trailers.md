# Great Dane Trailers

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2011-10-31

**Source:** [Trailer Orders Jump by 31%; Backlog Stretches Into 2012 - TT](https://www.ttnews.com/articles/trailer-orders-jump-31-backlog-stretches-2012)  ·  `page_013229`

## Facts

- Great Dane is opening a new factory in Statesboro, Ga., in the first quarter of 2012 to build refrigerated trailers.
- Production at the new plant will start modestly and carefully.
- The plant will employ 400 people when it becomes fully staffed.
- Chris Hammond, vice president of dealer sales at Great Dane Trailers in Savannah, Ga., reported that quoting and ordering activity had picked up over a slow June, July, and August period.
- Great Dane’s backlog for dry vans extends well into the middle of the first quarter of next year on some lines and into the second quarter on others.
