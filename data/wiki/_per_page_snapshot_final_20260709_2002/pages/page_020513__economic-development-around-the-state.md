# Economic Development Around the State

**Type:** event  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2025-06-30

**Source:** [Economic Development Around the State - Georgia Trend Magazine](https://www.georgiatrend.com/2025/06/30/economic-development-georgia/)  ·  `page_020513`

## Facts

- Mercedes-Benz is shifting up to 500 jobs to its existing location in Fulton County and investing in a new research and development facility nearby, setting the campus up as the company’s North American headquarters.
- The Mercedes-Benz move is expected to be completed by August 2026.
- Kumho Tire is relocating its North American headquarters to Brookhaven, centralizing its regional operations into a 14,000-square-foot space at Perimeter Summit for 50 employees.
- Amazon is opening a new package sorting center in Troup County, which will be one of 22 fulfillment and sortation centers Amazon operates in Georgia.
- The 1.6-million-square-foot Amazon facility will bring more than 1,000 full- and part-time jobs to the community when it opens in 2027.
- Ecocycle by Qcells, an offshoot of a clean energy company that addresses waste and sustainability, is planning to launch recycling operations in Cartersville to keep solar panels out of landfills.
- The Ecocycle by Qcells facility will have the ability to recycle around 500,000 panels per year – repurposing materials like aluminum, glass, silver and copper.
