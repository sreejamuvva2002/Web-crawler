# Kumho Tire U.S.A.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-12

**Source:** [Kumho Tire U.S.A. Announces Road Venture HT Highway-Terrain Tire - Traction News](https://www.tractionnews.com/kumho-tire-u-s-a-announces-road-venture-ht-highway-terrain-tire-2/)  ·  `page_056614`

## Facts

- Kumho Tire U.S.A. announced the all-new Road Venture HT, an advanced all-season Highway-Terrain tire engineered for optimal performance across any road condition.
- The Road Venture HT is purpose-built for light truck and SUV owners seeking a dependable everyday driving tire.
- It offers enhanced wet and snow traction, extended tread-life mileage, and a smooth, quiet ride.
- The Road Venture HT is EV-compatible.
- Shawn Denlein serves as Kumho Tire U.S.A. president of sales and marketing.
- The tire features an optimized structural design and a new generation polymer compound with High Dispersible Micro (HDM) Silica.
- These materials contribute to superior wear resistance, providing a 10% improvement in tread life compared to its predecessor.
- 3D chamfers and integrated tie-bar noise reduction technology ensure a quiet and comfortable ride.
- Reinforced polyester body plies strengthen sidewall stiffness and improve cornering stability.
- The tire features a high-grip resin and four wide circumferential grooves for efficient water evacuation.
- Wider lateral shoulder grooves, multi-angle sipes, and independent tread blocks with enhanced biting edges deliver superior traction and stability.
- A zigzag tread design and waved 3D interlocking sipes deliver enhanced snow traction.
- The tire earned Three-Peak Mountain Snowflake (3PMSF) certification for winter-rated capability.
- It is engineered to handle the unique demands of EVs, including higher weight and increased torque.
- Kumho Tire conducted independent testing at a third-party facility and benchmarked performance against leading brands in the category.
- Testing showed the Road Venture HT outperformed competitors in ride comfort and wet handling, plus delivered superior results in dry and wet braking.
- The tire will be available to dealers in May and consumers in June.
- It comes in more than 40 sizes, ranging from 15-inch to 22-inch rim diameters.
- It is backed by an industry-leading 75,000-mile limited treadwear warranty for P-metric sizes.
- It includes a 50,000-mile limited treadwear warranty for LT-metric sizes.
- A road hazard warranty and a 30-day satisfaction guarantee trial are included with the tire.
- Kumho Tire is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- Kumho Tire U.S.A. serves as the US sales, marketing, product development, and distribution arm of Kumho Tire Co. Inc.
- Kumho Tire has more than 60 years of history of pioneering innovative approaches to developing tires.
