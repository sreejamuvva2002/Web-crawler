## Facts

- Adient is a leading global automotive seating supplier supporting all major automakers.
- The company reported more than $15 billion in sales in FY2025.
- Adient employs more than 65,000 people across 29 countries.
- The company operates approximately 200 manufacturing and assembly plants worldwide.
- Adient produces and delivers automotive seating for all vehicle classes and major OEMs.
- The company's capabilities span from research and design to engineering and manufacturing.
- Adient is one of the largest suppliers of just-in-time seating in China.
- In China, Adient operates through seven joint ventures and approximately 38 manufacturing locations across 24 cities.
- Partnerships with major auto groups in China have given Adient broad market penetration relative to its seating competitors.
