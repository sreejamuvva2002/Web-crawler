# Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Company

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-19

**Source:** [Celebrating Women's Day at Yamaha. | Yamaha](https://www.yamaha-motor-georgia.com/node/71)  ·  `page_015719`

## Facts

- Yamaha Motor Manufacturing Company (YMMC) celebrated International Women’s Day on Mar/08/2025 under the theme “Accelerate Action.”
- The celebration emphasized taking swift and decisive steps to achieve gender equality and addressing systemic barriers and biases women face in personal and professional spheres.
- Yamaha is recognizing female employees and championing STEM education for aspiring young women.
- Kristin Webb serves as Manager of Communications and Corporate Social Responsibility at Yamaha.
- Kristin Webb stated, “We believe in fostering the next generation of female leaders in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, ensuring they have the tools and support to succeed.”
- Yamaha partnered with the Coweta Community Foundation for the 3rd annual Coweta Grand Prix.
- Yamaha hosts the Downhill Dreams Academy program where students collaborate with Yamaha engineers to design, build, and race their own soapbox cars.
- The Coweta Grand Prix event took place on April 19, 2025.
- The event featured young innovators, including scholarship participants from the Howard Warner Boys & Girls Club where 56% are girls.
- The Downhill Dreams Academy program develops hands-on STEM skills while embracing teamwork, creativity, and perseverance.
- Kristin Webb stated, “In addition to supporting future generations, we are celebrating the incredible women at Yamaha today.”
- 48% of hourly employees at Yamaha's Newnan facility are women.
- Female employees' contributions drive innovation, create meaningful connections across the global workforce, and shape a company culture built on inclusion, empowerment, and excellence.
- Yamaha presented gifts to all female employees as a token of appreciation for their dedication and hard work.
- Recognizing and celebrating female achievements is considered essential for fostering an inclusive and supportive workplace at Yamaha.
- Kristin Webb stated, “As we celebrate International Women’s Day, we reaffirm our commitment to creating opportunities for women to thrive—within Yamaha, in our communities, and in the industries we serve. We are proud to support a future where all women and girls can pursue their passions, break barriers, and lead with confidence.”
- Yamaha is hiring for those interested in joining a company that values innovation, diversity, and excellence.
- Kristin Webb concluded, “Together, we celebrate, empower, and inspire.”
- YMMC employs more than 2,100 metro Atlanta residents to design and build recreational vehicles at its Newnan manufacturing facilities.
- All of the world’s Yamaha golf cars and Side-by-Sides are manufactured at the plant in Coweta County, Georgia.
- Most of Yamaha's WaveRunners and ATVs are manufactured at the plant in Coweta County, Georgia.
