## Facts

- Graphite is in virtually all EV batteries.
- Chicago-based Anovion Technologies is opening the largest graphite production facility in North America.
- Anovion is initially investing $800 million to open a 1.5 million-square-foot graphite production factory in Bainbridge, in Decatur County, Georgia.
- The new facility will create around 400 jobs.
- This expansion facility is the first of this scale for Anovion Technologies.
- The factory will produce 40,000 metric tonnes per year of synthetic graphite anode material for lithium-ion batteries.
- Anovion chose Bainbridge due to existing and planned low- and carbon-free energy sources for power.
- Anovion chose Bainbridge due to short supply chains.
- Anovion chose Bainbridge due to access to existing rail infrastructure.
