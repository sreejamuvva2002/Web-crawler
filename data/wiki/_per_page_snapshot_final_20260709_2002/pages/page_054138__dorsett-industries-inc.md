# Dorsett Industries, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Dorsett Industries designs and manufactures quality automotive and marine carpet to the replacement market, as well as to the original equipment manufacturers (OEM).
- Product styles include the industry’s leading El Dorado-800 series, the Corvette-500 series, and innovative Berber, graphic, and woven vinyl marine carpet.
- Dorsett Industries serves as a supplier of carpet to automotive and mat manufacturers worldwide.
- Manufacturing and distribution facilities are expanding in Dalton, Georgia and Chattanooga, Tennessee.
- Products are sold in full rolls to distributors, mat manufacturers, molders and Tier I & II suppliers to the truck, automotive and marine manufacturers.
- The company supplies OEM - Tier II & III Automotive Carpet Suppliers, Aftermarket - Replacement Carpet, and Marine Carpet.
- Dorsett Industries was founded as a privately-owned company in 1961.
- Ownership changed on January 1st, 2016, when Dorsett became an employee owned company through the implementation of an E.S.O.P.
- Dorsett Industries is ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System Certified and adheres to strict material requirements.
