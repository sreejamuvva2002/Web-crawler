## Facts

- In 1935, Vernay founder Sergius Vernet invented the wax expansion element that revolutionized the automotive thermostat.
- The original wax element thermostat, created more than seven decades ago, is still used in every car on the road.
- Vernay has developed into a trusted innovative fluid solutions provider to leading OEMs and emerging companies in the Automotive, Medical, Mobility, Specialty, Printer and Small Engine industries.
- Vernay engineers and chemists are recognized throughout the industry for their tenacious problem-solving drive and for providing co-designed, custom fluid control solutions.
- Vernay continues to expand the capabilities of its in-house, world-class R&D laboratories in the U.S., Europe, and Asia to develop proprietary material formulations and designs for customers.
