# Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [TXT Press Release: Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG Receives A- Clima...](https://marketchameleon.com/PressReleases/i/1609809/TXT/kautex-textron-gmbh-co-kg-receives-a)  ·  `page_045479`

## Facts

- Kautex Textron GmbH & Co. KG, a Textron Inc. company, announced it has received an "A-" Climate Change rating from CDP.
- Kautex announced its sustainability roadmap in November 2021, committing to target an 80 percent reduction in Scope 1 and 2 emissions and a 30 percent reduction in Scope 3 emissions by 2030.
- The company aspires to be net-zero by 2050 in all three scopes.
- In 2022, Kautex challenged its 30-plus plants worldwide to reduce their Scope 1 and 2 emissions through practical steps like energy audits and strategic investments in renewable energy.
- In 2022, all the Company's facilities on mainland Europe, China, Mexico and in Lavonia, GA are sourcing renewable energy – a significant step in reducing Scope 2 emissions.
- The company joined CDP as a Supply Chain Corporate member and is challenging its suppliers to join CDP to disclose their own environmental impact and take concrete steps to reduce their impact over time.
- Sylvain Genesi, Kautex's vice president, Global Supply Chain and Sustainability, stated that the company took significant steps to improve its own Scope 1 and 2 emissions and drive its suppliers to do the same.
- Sophie Louis, global director, Energy and Sustainability, noted that Kautex's sustainability commitments are a commitment to customers and that as a Tier One supplier, their emissions directly impact the overall emissions of the OEM.
- To assist customers with product life cycle assessment, Kautex developed an application to help customers estimate the carbon impact of their Kautex-designed products.
- As a Tier One automotive supplier with more than 30 plants in 14 countries, Kautex designs, develops and manufactures traditional and hybrid fuel systems, advanced cleaning solutions for assisted and autonomous driving, engine camshafts and plastic industrial packaging solutions.
- Kautex is expanding its portfolio to offer smart products and data-driven services to customers, including smart fuel systems, thermoplastic composite and composite-metal hybrid battery systems.
- From a lightweight battery system to a hybrid fuel system to autonomous drive vehicle cleaning systems, Kautex is committed to pioneering solutions for the era of new mobility.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company that leverages its global network of aircraft, defense, industrial and finance businesses to provide customers with innovative solutions and services.
