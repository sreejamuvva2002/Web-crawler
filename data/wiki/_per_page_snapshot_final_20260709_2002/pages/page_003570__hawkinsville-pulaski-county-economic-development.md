## Facts

- Hawkinsville-Pulaski County offers available and affordable buildings and developable sites for business location.
- The county is located less than 3 hours from the Port of Savannah.
- The county is located less than 2 hours from Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport.
- The county is located only 25 minutes from Robins Air Force Base and the Crisp County Inland Port.
- The area features an educated workforce with a high work ethic, including former and retired military personnel.
- State-sponsored labor recruitment and training programs are available in the area.
- Hawkinsville-Pulaski County holds Entrepreneur-Friendly certification.
- Hawkinsville-Pulaski County holds Certified Work Ready status.
- Hawkinsville-Pulaski County holds Camera Ready certification.
- The county is situated in Middle Georgia.
