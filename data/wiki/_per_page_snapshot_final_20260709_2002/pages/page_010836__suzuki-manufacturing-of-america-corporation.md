# Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Suzuki merged two parts of its ATV business by making Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corporation (SMAC) a 100 percent subsidiary of Suzuki Motor USA.
- The corporate restructuring aims to better expand the company's powersports business.
- SMAC opened in Rome, Georgia, in 2001.
- SMAC has built every KingQuad ATV the company has sold in the US and overseas.
- The move unites the ATV production arm (SMAC) with the ATV sales arm (Suzuki Motor USA).
- Suzuki states the restructuring will result in a more nimble business better able to respond to demand and expand operations.
- The KingQuad ATV received meaningful updates this year but is described as getting long in the tooth.
- The article notes potential for new energy behind the ATV market, speculating about a future Suzuki side-by-side.
