# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07

**Source:** [Despite looming chip shortage, Rivian increases delivery outlook after R2 launch](https://www.ajc.com/business/2026/07/rivian-delivers-more-evs-as-it-launches-r2-model-but-chip-shortage-looms/)  ·  `page_060568`

## Facts

- Rivian announced it has expanded its expectations for how many electric vehicles it will deliver to customers this year.
- The California-based automaker is in the process of building an EV factory an hour east of Atlanta.
- Rivian increased its 2026 delivery outlook by 3,000 vehicles.
- Rivian's updated delivery guidance for the year is 65,000 to 70,000 vehicles.
- The increase in delivery guidance was prompted by a busier-than-anticipated second quarter bolstered by the launch of a new midsize SUV called R2.
- Initial R2s are being built at the company’s sole plant in Illinois.
- Rivian plans to expand production capacity at its future Georgia plant.
- The success of the R2 is critical to Rivian’s future.
- The company is securing $5.1 billion in tax incentives for its commitment to build a plant in the Georgia I-20 corridor.
- Rivian is facing a looming chip shortage that could disrupt its supply chain and production.
