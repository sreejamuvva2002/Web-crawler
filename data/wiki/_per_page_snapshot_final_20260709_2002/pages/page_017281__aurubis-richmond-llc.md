# Aurubis Richmond LLC

## Facts

- Aurubis Richmond is the first secondary smelter specializing in multimetal recycling in the US.
- Construction of the facility started in June 2022.
- The company is making a targeted investment to expand its own recycling business by building this plant.
- The state-of-the-art plant located in Augusta (Richmond County), Georgia, will process about 90,000 t of complex recycling materials annually.
- The facility supports Aurubis’ commitment to the circular economy and its goal of achieving carbon neutrality well before 2050.
- The plant is scheduled to be commissioned in the first half of 2024.
- The facility is located at 439 Valencia Way, Augusta, GA 30906.
