# Dade County Industrial Development Authority

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Industrial Development | Dade County, GA](https://dadecounty-ga.gov/311/Industrial-Development)  ·  `page_003761`

## Facts

- The Industrial Development Authority’s mission is to entice businesses to re-locate to Dade County and support businesses that are already here, so that the people of Dade County may benefit from good jobs and improved quality of life.
- The authority aims to retain existing jobs.
- The authority aims to create new job opportunities through the expansion of existing industry.
- The authority aims to create new job opportunities through the attraction of new industry.
- The authority aims to support capital investment by new and expanding businesses.
- The authority works cooperatively with state government, local governments, and school board to further economic development.
- The authority aims to increase the County’s tax base.
