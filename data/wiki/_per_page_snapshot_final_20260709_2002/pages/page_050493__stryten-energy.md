## Facts

- Stryten is an industry leader backed by more than a century of expertise.
- It is the only energy storage solutions provider in North America who can offer advanced lead, lithium and vanadium solutions to solve complex energy storage challenges.
- The company operates a manufacturing, service and distribution network throughout North America.
- Stryten Energy has a growing team of 2,500+ employees.
- Stryten relies on sister-company Motrex LLC to provide personnel for professional services roles including information technology, finance and accounting, logistics, EH&S, project management, quality and human resources.
- Stryten Energy has been named a 2025 Silver Military Friendly® Employer in the annual list from militaryfriendly.com.
- The company highlights its involvement with Rivers of Recovery, an organization dedicated to supporting military veterans through outdoor-based activities.
- Stryten Energy provides training and skills development for manufacturing careers, particularly targeting women.
- Stryten Energy received the Women’s Choice Award in 2024 and 2025 as one of the Best Companies to Work in the USA.
- The company offers competitive benefit programs including comprehensive healthcare, retirement savings plans, and paid leave.
- Stryten Energy welcomes dozens of interns, apprentices, co-ops and new graduates every year through a structured program comprised of meaningful projects and interaction with coworkers.
- Internship opportunities span engineering, accounting, marketing communications, human resources, logistics, product marketing, plant operations, and more.
