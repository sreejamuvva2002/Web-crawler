# Kia Manufacturing Georgia

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-01-19

**Source:** [Kia Georgia Goes Green with Solar Panel Project | Jim Ellis Kia of Kennesaw](https://www.jimelliskiaofkennesaw.com/kia-plant-solar-project/)  ·  `page_001083`

## Facts

- A massive hailstorm damaged over 13,000 vehicles at the Kia Georgia plant in 2023.
- The company implemented a cutting-edge solar canopy system from VPS to protect vehicles from severe weather and power the plant with clean energy.
- The system will include 17,000 solar panels.
- The system will generate over 10 megawatts of electricity.
- The generated electricity is enough to supply up to 30% of the plant’s annual energy needs.
- The project is part of Kia’s commitment to reducing carbon emissions by 97% by 2045.
- The Kia Georgia plant is located in West Point.
- The plant builds vehicle models including the Telluride, Sorento, Sportage, EV9, and EV6.
- The solar project is about 40% complete.
- The solar project is set to finish by the end of 2025.
- The initiative aligns with Hyundai Motor Group’s multi-billion-dollar investment in EV and battery production across Georgia.
