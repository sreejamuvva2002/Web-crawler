## Facts

- Panasonic has defined cockpit high performance computing (HPC) and cabin user experience (UX) as important factors for planning strategies for the evolution of a mobility society and the diversification of people’s values.
- The company identifies reducing environmental impact and contributing to a society that requires the reduction of CO2 emissions as key market opportunities.
- Quick charging to shorten EV charging time is identified as a critical issue facing the widespread use of EVs.
- Panasonic possesses industry-leading technologies that support high-voltage, high-powered chargers and skills in power conversion efficiency.
- The speed of software evolution determines the value of automobiles with the progress of SDV (Software Defined Vehicle) and integrated HPC.
- Panasonic holds a large share of IVI (In-Vehicle Infortainment) by leveraging enhanced operability acquired from know-how on digital AV and development capability of large-scale software.
- The company has the ability to propose a platform that integrates various types of large-scale software necessary for HPC.
- With the shift to EVs and autonomous driving, the added value of automobiles is shifting to the value of the experience in the mobility space.
- Panasonic leverages knowledge of people acquired from housing and consumer electronics businesses and the ability to design experiences that enhance the value of mobility.
- Top management’s commitment to environmental contribution and the ability to execute tasks steadily together with all employees are cited as core strengths.
