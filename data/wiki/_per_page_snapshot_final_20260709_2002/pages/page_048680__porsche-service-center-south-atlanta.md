# Porsche Service Center South Atlanta

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Porsche opens nation’s first freestanding service center on its Atlanta campus](https://www.ajc.com/news/atlanta-news/porsche-opens-nations-first-freestanding-service-center-on-its-atlanta-campus/HMRSXRZMUFFKFLDT45PETYIOVE/)  ·  `page_048680`

## Facts

- Porsche has opened the nation’s first freestanding service center on the campus of its North American headquarters near Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport.
- The facility is named the Porsche Service Center South Atlanta.
- The service center is a 22,000-square foot facility.
- 12,000 square feet of the facility is dedicated to servicing vehicles.
- This project marks the first phase of a multimillion-dollar effort at Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA).
- The PCNA initiative also includes a significant expansion of the existing Porsche Experience Center track.
- The service center features 13 lift bays.
- The interior design utilizes the “Destination Porsche” corporate architecture, which was first piloted in the U.S. at Porsche Palm Springs in 2019.
- Kjell Gruner, President and CEO of PCNA, stated that opening the service center kicks off expansion plans at their Atlanta headquarters and highlights a shared commitment to customer experience.
- Gruner noted that bringing two dealerships together to create a first-class service experience is a significant milestone.
- PCNA expressed appreciation for the dealerships' investment and expects customers will appreciate the new facility as well.
