## Facts

- In July 2024, the Georgia Department of Transportation (GDOT) intended to solicit responses to design, construct, install, finance, operate, and maintain electric vehicle charging infrastructure compliant with the National Electric Vehicle Infrastructure (NEVI) Program.
- Georgia's NEVI Deployment Program: Round 2 identified 33 locations in a Request for Proposal (RFP).
- The installation of electric vehicle charging infrastructure at these 33 locations will fill gaps in the state's Alternative Fuel Corridors charging station network.
