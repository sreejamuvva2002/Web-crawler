# JAC Products, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2016-11-04

**Source:** [Hall Capital & Argonaut Private Equity Acquire Michigan-Based JAC Products – Hall Capital](https://hall-capital.com/hall-capital-argonaut-private-equity-acquire-michigan-based-jac-products/)  ·  `page_056335`

## Facts

- Oklahoma City-based Hall Capital and Tulsa-based Argonaut Private Equity jointly acquired Pontiac, Michigan-based JAC Products, Inc.
- The sale became effective on Oct. 28, 2016, with terms not disclosed.
- JAC Products is the leading global designer and manufacturer of roof racks, cargo management systems, assist steps, and related products for automobiles.
- JAC Products operates factories in Michigan, Georgia, Portugal, and China.
- JAC Products currently employs more than 1,500 employees globally.
- The existing management team, including Chief Executive Officer Mike Wood, will remain with the company to help drive strategic growth plans.
- Strategic growth plans include diversifying its product mix and expanding globally into new markets.
- CEO Mike Wood stated: “JAC is in a major growth phase and this new ownership is committed to the implementation of our strategic plan.”
- The JAC acquisition will be part of Hall Capital Partners Fund II, which was launched in 2011 with $92 million in committed capital.
- Fred J. Hall, Chairman & CEO of Hall Capital, stated: “The JAC Products acquisition by our Fund II brings to Hall Capital a solid, mature company that is well poised for growth and operating in an industry in which we have generations of experience.”
- Argonaut Private Equity will add JAC Products to its third fund.
- Steve Mitchell, CEO of Argonaut Private Equity, stated: “This is an exciting and significant acquisition for Fund III.”
- Hall Capital is a family-owned private investment company with offices in Oklahoma City, Nashville, Chicago, and Dallas.
- Hall Capital's roots extend back to legendary automotive pioneer Fred Jones’ first enterprise in 1920.
- Hall Capital is led by Fred Jones’ grandsons: Fred Hall, Kirkland Hall, and Brooks “Boots” Hall.
- Hall Capital focuses on private equity funds and investments in the real estate, automotive, and oil & gas sectors.
- Argonaut Private Equity was founded in 2002 and is based in Tulsa.
- Argonaut Private Equity manages investments across multiple asset classes with over $3 billion of capital deployed in direct investments.
- Argonaut has made over 100 investments and fosters a global network to discover and partner with best-in-class management teams.
- JAC Products was founded in 1967.
- JAC Products supplies OEM manufacturers worldwide with engineering, sales, manufacturing, and partnership locations in North America, Europe, China, Japan, and South America.
