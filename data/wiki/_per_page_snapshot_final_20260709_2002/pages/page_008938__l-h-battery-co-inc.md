# L-H Battery Co. Inc.

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-02

**Source:** [Honda battery plant has started full-time production | Local | daytondailynews.com](https://www.daytondailynews.com/local/honda-battery-plant-says-it-has-launched-full-time-production/article_8129e489-7111-5155-88e9-dbb10ebbe4fb.html)  ·  `page_008938`

## Facts

- Honda’s Jeffersonville plant has begun production of lithium batteries for electric grid and storage purposes, not for EV batteries, the company said Wednesday, July 1.
- L-H Battery Co. Inc. said it has launched lithium-ion battery cell mass production.
- Construction of the plant began more than three years ago.
- The plant is located west of Ohio 729 and south of Interstate 71.
- The plant spans more than 2 million square feet.
- The plant has more than 1,000 employees.
- L-H Battery stated it will remain flexible with its products and production as the battery cell market evolves.
- The plant was originally envisioned to begin mass production to supply battery cells for electric vehicles.
- The plant pivoted to support energy storage systems and future production of hybrid-electric vehicle battery cells in North America.
- A spokeswoman for L-H Battery referred questions about EV battery production start dates to Honda.
- LG Energy Solution will sell its stake in the joint venture EV battery production site in Fayette County to Honda Development and Manufacturing of America for $2.85 billion.
- The sale was reported in a Korean regulatory filing in December 2025.
- The first battery cells produced at L-H Battery will be packaged into energy storage systems by LG Energy Solution Vertech.
- LG Energy Solution Vertech is the U.S. energy storage division of LG Energy Solution.
- The energy storage systems are delivered to customers for grid-scale and other technology applications, including residential homes, commercial and industrial operations, and large-scale utility grids.
- These systems capture and store electricity created by traditional energy sources like oil and gas as well as renewables like wind and solar.
- L-H Battery Chief Executive Chahun Ku said such systems are an important part of L-H Battery’s future, along with production of battery cells for application in hybrid-electric vehicles.
- Rick Riggle, chief operating officer at L-H Battery, said it has been nearly four years since the establishment of L-H Battery and expressed gratification over progress from completing construction to establishing a new workforce and delivering economic benefits for Fayette County.
- Global lithium-ion battery deployment in 2025 was six times that of 2020, according to the International Energy Agency as reported by Reuters.
