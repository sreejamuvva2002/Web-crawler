## Facts

- Lehigh Technologies, Inc. is a U.S.-based company located in Tucker, Georgia.
- The company utilizes patented grinding technology to cryogenically grind end-of-life tire rubber into an ultra-fine powder.
- This ultra-fine powder is orders of magnitude finer in size than traditional crumb rubber or ground tire rubber.
- Lehigh's high-quality rubber powder enables manufacturers to formulate performance characteristics in their product lines.
- The company has the capability to manufacture the powder in different particle sizes.
- Lehigh's Technical Center offers personalized technical guidance for formulating with PolyDyne™ and MicroDyne™.
- These materials are applied in tires, coatings, paints, sealants, plastics, and rubber products.
