# Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-06-21

**Source:** [PANASONIC AUTOMOTIVE POWERS FIRST UL-CERTIFIED EBIKE WITH TOTEM USA, BLENDING USER-FRIENDLY DESIGN AND SAFETY - Cerebral-Overload](https://cerebral-overload.com/2022/01/panasonic-automotive-powers-first-ul-certified-ebike-with-totem-usa-blending-user-friendly-design-and-safety/)  ·  `page_047940`

## Facts

- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America, a division of Panasonic Corporation of North America, announced a collaboration with Totem USA to bring Totem’s first UL-certified eBike to the U.S. market, “Zen Rider.”
- In 2019, Panasonic received the industry’s first e-Bike safety standard certification from UL (UL2849).
- The Totem USA eBike “Zen Rider” is powered by a Panasonic system and offers an affordable, family-friendly option for the growing eBike market.
- NPD research indicated a 240% growth rate for electric bicycles in the 12 months leading up to July 2021, with sustained interest expected following pandemic-era cycling spending.
- Derek Arciniaga, senior manager of business development at Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America, stated: “We’re proud to announce Totem USA’s first eBike, with a UL-certified Panasonic system, to expand mobility transformation in the United States.”
- The Zen Rider features a low step-through mounting design, making its motor and battery system especially suited for urban areas.
- Panasonic’s second UL 2849 certified motor and battery system includes a 2-Axle motor plus battery eBike system.
- The drive system provides 75 Nm of max torque and a 404 Wh battery.
- Pedal assistance operates up to 15mph, aligning with city or local ordinances that enforce 15mph speed limitations.
- Totem USA is prepared to design, develop, and produce custom eBike offerings for companies seeking UL certified eBikes in collaboration with Panasonic.
- The Panasonic drive system supports two-wheel and three-wheel city, touring, or cargo eBike variants.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America is a leading global supplier of automotive infotainment and connectivity system solutions.
- Panasonic Automotive is headquartered in Peachtree City, Georgia, with sales, marketing, and engineering operations in Farmington Hills, Michigan.
- Totem USA is located in McKinney, Texas, and operates as a division of the Golden Wheel Group, the 4th largest manufacturer globally of bicycles and eBikes.
