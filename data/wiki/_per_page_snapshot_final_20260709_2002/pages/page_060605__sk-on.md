# SK On

## Facts

- South Korean battery manufacturer SK On has announced significant layoffs at its Georgia-based battery plant due to a decline in electric vehicle (EV) demand.
- SK On's U.S. subsidiary, SK Battery America Inc., terminated 968 employees at its Commerce, Georgia facility.
- The reduction represents approximately 37% of the plant's total workforce of around 2,500 employees.
- The company attributes the layoffs to a slowdown in EV sales and changing market conditions, which have necessitated restructuring efforts.
- The layoffs were disclosed through a Worker Adjustment and Retraining Notification (WARN) filed on a Georgia state website.
