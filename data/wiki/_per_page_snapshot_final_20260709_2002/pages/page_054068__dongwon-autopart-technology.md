## Facts

- The project will bring new jobs to Emanuel County and see its impact on this community for years to come.
- The new manufacturing facility will be located at the Hwy 297 Industrial Park near Swainsboro.
- The new facility is estimated to create 200 new jobs over the next few years.
- Hiring for the new facility is expected to begin this year.
- The company will be hiring for positions such as management, system leads, and production personnel.
- Dongwon Autopart Technology began business back in 1971.
- Dongwon Autopart Technology is a components supplier for doorframes, seat frames, and battery frames to automotive original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).
- Dongwon has a facility located in Hogansville, Georgia.
- Dongwon currently supports more than 500 direct jobs in the Southeast.
