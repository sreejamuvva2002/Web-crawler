## Facts

- The company is expanding its manufacturing footprint and technical centers in North America.
- Astemo Americas delivers advanced mobility solutions for the automotive and motorcycle industries.
- The company focuses on electric powertrains, autonomous driving systems, and integrated vehicle controls.
- Astemo Americas was formed through the merger of Hitachi Automotive Systems, Keihin, Showa, and Nissin Kogyo.
- Astemo Americas operates more than 20 manufacturing sites across the United States, Canada, Mexico, and Brazil.
- The company serves some of the world’s largest automobile manufacturers.
