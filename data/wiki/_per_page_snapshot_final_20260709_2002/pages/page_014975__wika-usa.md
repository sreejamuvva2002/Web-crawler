## Facts

- WIKA is expanding its partnership with AWC to bring WIKA precision products and services to 12 states in the southeastern US with local AWC support.
- WIKA's comprehensive evaluation affirms that AWC’s expanded geographic footprint will deliver unparalleled advantages to both existing and prospective clientele.
- The collaboration will not lead to any alterations in the manufacturing or design of the WIKA product portfolio, assuring steadfast maintenance of product quality and uninterrupted service.
- WIKA and AWC have maintained an enduring partnership spanning nearly a decade.
- Todd Gardner serves as the WIKA USA President.
- Todd Gardner stated that the expanded geographic partnership with AWC marks a significant milestone reinforcing the strength of their partner relationship.
- Todd Gardner noted the strategic expansion bolsters commitment to serving partners effectively and exemplifies synergy fostering innovation and excellence.
- The expanded partnership guarantees a smooth and streamlined transition for customers, ensuring uninterrupted access to products and services.
- WIKA Group employs 11,200 people globally.
- WIKA is a global market leader in pressure and temperature measurement, setting standards in level, force, flow measurement, and calibration technology.
- WIKA offers high-precision products, solutions, and services under the “Smart in sensing” initiative.
- AWC is an Automation and Controls Technology company with 600+ employee-owners.
- AWC focuses on two main areas: Process Technology & Instrumentation and Industrial Automation.
- AWC operates as an authorized local sales, support, and service partner to leading manufacturing partners.
- AWC provides a full suite of products, services, and integrated systems to optimize US Manufacturing and Utility operations.
- AWC was founded in 1965.
- AWC operates 32 full-service locations across the U.S., spanning from Denver, CO to Tampa, FL.
- AWC maintains a heavy concentration of locations along the Gulf Coast of TX and LA.
- Trey Castleberry serves as the AWC Marketing Director.
