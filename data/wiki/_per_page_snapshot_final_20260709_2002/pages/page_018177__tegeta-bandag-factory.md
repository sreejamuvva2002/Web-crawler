# Tegeta Bandag Factory

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-12

**Source:** [Bandag partners with Tegeta Motors to build state-of-the-art retreading factory in Georgia - Tyrepress](https://www.tyrepress.com/2025/12/bandag-partners-with-tegeta-motors-to-build-state-of-the-art-retreading-factory-in-georgia/)  ·  `page_018177`

## Facts

- Bandag, a global leader in tyre retreading solutions, has partnered with Tegeta Motors and Bridgestone MEA to establish the first Bandag retreading factory in the Caucasus region.
- The facility is located in Rustavi, Georgia.
- The project is backed by an initial investment of US$2 million.
- The facility is expected to produce up to 5,000 retreaded tyres annually.
- Plans are in place to double capacity within five years.
- Construction will commence this year (2025).
- Completion is targeted for late 2026.
- The Tegeta Bandag Factory represents a major step towards expanding sustainable mobility practices across the region.
