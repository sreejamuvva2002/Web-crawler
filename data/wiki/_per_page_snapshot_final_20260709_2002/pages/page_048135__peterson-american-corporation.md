## Facts

- The Peterson Spring-Georgia Plant is located in Athens, GA 30601.
- Peterson American Corporation was set up in 1914.
- Peterson Spring is the biggest privately held spring manufacturer in North America.
- The company's mission is to work with clients to supply the finest products available to the world market.
- It draws on worldwide resources to improve product quality and reduce costs across practical, innovative applications.
- The company maintains equitable relationships with its clients, employees, shareholders, and communities.
- Peterson production customers have access to the Specialty Products Division (SPD) for small order and prototype requirements across the entire product mix.
- Engineers assist customers with product design and offer suggestions for raw materials.
- SPD offers special services including wire shaping (square/rectangular/bevel cross sections), piercing, bending, coining, grinding, packaging, assembly, and painting.
- Peterson Spring manufactures engineered metal products encompassing engineering expertise, laboratory capabilities, and materials design and selection.
- The company employs its talent and engineering tenacity to provide customized designs, manufactured materials, and responsive service.
- The firm is recognized for the dependability, durability, and quality of its products, validating its position as a go-to authority for engineered metal mastery.
