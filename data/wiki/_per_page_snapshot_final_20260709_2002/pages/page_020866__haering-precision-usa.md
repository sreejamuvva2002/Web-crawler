# Haering Precision USA

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-11-04

## Facts

- German auto parts maker Haering Precision USA is opening its doors to the public for a grand open house event.
- The grand opening will take place from 11 a.m. to 6 p.m. on Saturday.
- Dr. Jurgen Haering serves as the owner and president of the Haring Group.
- The facility features a 150,000 square foot production space containing 40 acres of state-of-the-art machinery.
- In 2014, former Gov. Nathan Deal announced Haering’s plan to locate its first U.S. manufacturing facility in the Gateway II Industrial Park in Lavonia.
- The project includes plans to create 800 jobs by 2025.
- The company plans to invest $54 million into the project.
- Häering was founded in 1961 and has grown to become one of the leading partners of the automotive industry worldwide.
- The company manufactures precision components and sub-assemblies for the automotive industry.
- Haering is one of more than 120 automotive manufacturers that have invested more than $5 billion in the state of Georgia.
- Haering Precision USA is located off Knox Bridge Crossing Road in the Gateway II Industrial Park in Lavonia.
