# Pirelli Tire North America (PTNA)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Pirelli Motorsport Commitment in North America - 360 MAGAZINE - GREEN | DESIGN | POP | NEWS](https://www.the360mag.com/pirelli-motorsport-commitment-in-north-america/)  ·  `page_048615`

## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America (PTNA) maintains its Rome, Georgia headquarters, which houses a Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) facility and a research and development center.
- PTNA operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant in Silao, Mexico.
- The company manufactures, distributes, and markets original equipment and replacement tires for domestic car and motorcycle applications.
- Pirelli has commercial presence in over 160 countries and operates 19 factories across 13 countries.
- Motorsport serves as an essential part of Pirelli's research and development program, functioning as an open-air testing laboratory to develop Ultra High Performance tires for the road.
- Pirelli USA renewed its agreement with Porsche Club of America for three years, adding support for Werks Reunion Amelia Island alongside five national programs: PCA Club Racing, PCA High Performance Driver Education (HPDE), PCA Autocross, Porsche Parade, and PCA Treffen.
- Pirelli will supply tires and provide trackside support for the renewed Porsche Club of America partnership.
- For 2019, Pirelli supports the Air-Cooled Cup USA championship for two years, featuring seven rounds starting in April at Portland International Raceway in Oregon.
- Pirelli's partnership with the Trans Am Series was extended for five years through 2025, marking its tenure as headline sponsor since 2017, with 12 races scheduled for the 2019 season.
- Pirelli has equipped the Intercontinental GT Challenge since 2014 and continues to provide P Zero slick tires for high-performance prestige brands.
- The 2019 Intercontinental GT Challenge begins in February at Liqui-Moly Bathurst 12 Hours in Australia and visits Laguna Seca track in California one month later for the California 8 Hours.
- Key parts of the Pirelli USA program include P Zero-equipped single-make championships from Ferrari (Ferrari Challenge) and Lamborghini (Lamborghini Super Trofeo).
- Pirelli supports the Challenge Xtreme Elite rally series in Canada for another three years, with its first event in February in Quebec.
- In Mexico, Pirelli supports the Formula 4 grassroots motorsport series running on P Zero tires.
- Pirelli was founded in 1872 and maintains headquarters in Milan, Italy, focusing on the high-end, premium product segment where it is a world leader.
- The company is currently the exclusive supplier to the Formula 1 championship and the Superbike World Championship.
- PTNA maintains sales and marketing offices in New York City, Los Angeles, Detroit, Montreal, and Mexico City, along with a prestige flagship store in Los Angeles.
