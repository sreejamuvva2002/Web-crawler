# Novelis Inc.

## Facts

- Novelis Inc. is an American industrial aluminum smelting company headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, United States.
- The company produces rolled aluminum and operates as an aluminum recycler.
- Novelis supplies aluminum to sectors including beverage cans, automotive, aerospace, consumer electronics, construction, foil, and packaging.
- Novelis has been an independent subsidiary of Indian multinational aluminum and copper manufacturing company Hindalco Industries since 2007.
- Hindalco Industries is a subsidiary of the Aditya Birla Group.
- The company was incorporated in 2004 after being spun off from Alcan, a Canadian mining and aluminum manufacturer now part of Rio Tinto Alcan.
- In 2007, Hindalco Industries acquired Novelis for $6 billion.
- In May 2024, Novelis filed its registration statement for a proposed IPO.
- Novelis withdrew its IPO plans in October 2024.
- In March 2025, Novelis announced the closure of its operation in Fairmont, West Virginia, at a 100-year-old production plant.
- Novelis is the world's largest producer of rolled aluminum sheet.
- The company operates in 11 countries and divides its operations into four regions: North America, Europe, Asia, and South America.
- Regional headquarters are located in Atlanta, Zurich, Seoul, and São Paulo.
- Novelis also has operations in the United Kingdom, Germany, Italy, France, Canada, China, Malaysia, and Vietnam.
- The company invested $100 million in its first manufacturing facility in China, which opened in 2014.
- Novelis announced a $400 million capital investment for an expansion in Asia.
- Novelis announced a $300 million capital investment for an expansion in Brazil.
- Novelis announced a $200 million capital investment for an expansion in North America.
- In July 2014, Reynolds Consumer Products Inc., a subsidiary of the Rank Group of New Zealand, purchased Novelis's North American consumer products division for CAD $33.75 million.
- Novelis recycles more than 70 billion used beverage cans each year.
- In May 2022, Novelis announced plans to invest $2.5 billion in a new, low-carbon aluminum plant in Bay Minette, Alabama.
- Once complete, the Bay Minette facility will be the first fully integrated aluminum mill built in 40 years.
- On September 16, 2025, the Oswego, New York facility suffered a three-alarm fire in the "hot mill" section that brought down part of the factory's roof.
- Novelis produces aluminum structures and components for transportation products in automotive, automotive heat exchanger, and mass transportation applications.
- On April 15, 2020, Novelis acquired Aleris Corporation for $2.8 billion, entering the high-end aerospace segment.
- In 2012, Novelis received the Georgia Governor's International Award for establishing its Global Research & Technology Center in Kennesaw, Georgia.
- Steven Fisher serves as President and CEO of Novelis.
- As of 2025, Novelis reported revenue of US$17.1 billion and an additional financial figure of US$683 million.
- As of 2025, Novelis reported total assets of US$16.51 billion and total equity of US$4.51 billion.
- As of 2025, Novelis employed 13,250 people.
- In 2013, Novelis was named metals company of the year, and CEO Phil Martens was named CEO of the year at the Platts Global Metals Awards in London.
- In 2011, Novelis received an Innovation Award from BMW for lightweight vehicle construction.
