# Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire Europe

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Opening ceremony of the Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire Europe facility in Bad Arolsen, Germany - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.de/news/opening-ceremony-of-the-essex-furukawa-magnet-wire-europe-facility-in-bad-arolsen-germany/)  ·  `page_010351`

## Facts

- Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire Europe announced the completion of a high-voltage winding wire facility in its existing plant in Bad Arolsen, Germany.
- The opening ceremony for the new facility took place on November 8, attended by board members of Essex Europe and Furukawa Electric, customers, and local authorities.
- A new investment focused on the production of high-voltage winding wires, an innovative product that offers benefits and efficiency for next-generation electric vehicle drives.
- The innovative wire is based on extruded resin-coated rectangular enameled wire and offers properties that allow the automotive industry to shrink motors and increase electric vehicle efficiency.
- Following strong interest from automakers and suppliers in recent months and technical exchanges about this new wire, Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire can now deliver samples for customers to test for integration into future developments.
- Essex Magnet Wire is the world's leading provider of magnet wire, used by most major OEMs and industry leaders in the automotive, energy, and industrial sectors.
- Essex Magnet Wire has branches in 11 countries across three continents and operates globally while serving locally.
- With 90 years of experience, Essex Magnet Wire delivers innovations that meet customer demands and is vertically integrated into the manufacturing process to ensure quality and seamless delivery services from global distribution centers.
- The Essex MagForceX® innovation center drives technological advancement in magnet wire through research and development providing custom solutions for future challenges.
- Furukawa Electric Co., Ltd. was founded in 1884 as a copper smeltery and wire factory and has become a pioneer of the latest technologies.
- Furukawa Electric's products span telecommunications, electronics, automobiles, and construction, focusing on optics, plastics, and metals, with many achieving the highest global market share.
- Furukawa Electric reported consolidated revenue of 843.3 billion JPY (approximately 7.8 billion USD) for the fiscal year ending March 31, 2017.
- Superior Essex is headquartered at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327 – USA.
