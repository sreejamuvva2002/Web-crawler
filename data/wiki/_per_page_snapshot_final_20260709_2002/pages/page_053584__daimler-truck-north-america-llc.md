# Daimler Truck North America LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-04-02

**Source:** [Daimler Truck North America Appoints Adam Morgan as Head of Corporate Communications | Daimler Truck North America](https://northamerica.daimlertruck.com/news-stories/2026/daimler-truck-north-america-appoints-adam-morgan-as-head-of-corporate-communications)  ·  `page_053584`

## Facts

- Daimler Truck North America LLC appointed Adam Morgan as Head of Corporate Communications on April 2, 2026.
- In this role, Morgan will lead internal and external communications, media relations, public affairs, corporate branding, brand partnerships, and corporate giving.
- John O’Leary, president and CEO of Daimler Truck North America, described Morgan as a strategic, highly experienced communicator and proven leader whose experience across complex, global industries will help DTNA grow, evolve, and strengthen connections with employees, customers, and communities.
- Morgan joins DTNA from Bristow Group Inc., where he served as Director of Global Communications and Marketing.
- Prior to Bristow, Morgan held senior communications roles at McDermott International, Inc.
- Morgan spent a decade at The Boeing Company, including leading Communications for Boeing’s space division and serving as Regional Director of International Communications for Africa, Latin America, and the Caribbean.
- Morgan has a professional background spanning aerospace, energy, and manufacturing, and previously served in public and legislative affairs with the U.S. Forest Service.
- Morgan is a veteran of the United States Navy.
- Morgan holds a master’s degree in communication and leadership from Gonzaga University and a bachelor’s degree in mass communications from Portland State University.
- Morgan succeeds Andy Johnson, who recently transitioned to Head of Global Communications & External Affairs at Daimler Truck in Germany.
- Morgan will be based at DTNA’s headquarters in Portland, Oregon.
- Daimler Truck North America LLC is headquartered in Portland, Oregon.
- DTNA is the leading heavy-duty truck manufacturer in North America and a Daimler Truck company.
- The company produces and markets commercial vehicles under the Freightliner, Western Star, Thomas Built Buses, and Detroit brands.
- DTNA is committed to shaping the future of transportation through innovation, safety, and sustainable technologies.
