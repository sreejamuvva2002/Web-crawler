## Facts

- Elan Technology has been a leader in the glass-ceramic, toll spray drying, and rotary calcining material processing industries for over 70 years.
- Elan Technology is the largest independent U.S. company in its field.
- The company produces preforms for hermetic seals and spacers using technical glass-ceramics and glass-ceramic composites.
- Elan offers a variety of advanced ceramic materials, including alumina, steatite, mullite, cordierite, and zirconia.
- Over 600 standard and custom glass compositions are available to provide engineers with design solutions.
- Elan's research and development department continues to search for new and innovative materials to meet demanding requirements.
- Elan offers material processing services, including toll spray drying and contract rotary calcining.
- The company is located in the United States.
