## Facts

- Piolax is known worldwide for its degree of excellence in automobile parts and is committed to growing along with its customers.
- Due to a commitment to supply quality products to the automotive industries, the company established a location in the United States.
- Piolax US began production in Canton, Georgia in August 1988.
- Piolax is a leading supplier of plastic and metal products that continually strengthens its position by expanding global marketing and production capabilities.
- The company provides in-house manufacturing for plastic and metal fastening products.
- In August 1988, KHK established the Georgia Corporation.
- In January 1990, KHK of America was merged with Piolax Corporation.
- In October 1991, the company purchased a 48,000 sq ft building for plastic fastener production.
- In April 1993, the facility expanded by 6,000 sq ft for a heat treat factory.
- In April 1995, the facility expanded by 23,000 sq ft for plastic fastener and assembly parts production.
- In August 1996, the company achieved ISO-9002 registration.
- In May 1997, the company achieved QS-9000 registration.
- In January 2000, the company implemented a new SAP ERP system.
- In August 2001, the company started operations in Mexico with a 30,000 sqft facility.
- In November 2001, the facility expanded by 25,000 sqft for assembly, plastic, and warehouse space.
- In November 2003, the company achieved TS-16949 registration.
- In January 2004, the company achieved ISO-14001 registration.
- In July 2004, TS-16949 registration was achieved at the Mexico plant.
- In April 2006, the Mexico facility expanded by 30,000 sq ft for production.
- In January 2012, the Mexico plant became an independent corporation.
- In October 2015, in-house metal surface treatment production started.
- In September 2016, the facility expanded by 80,500 sq ft for an automated warehouse.
- In December 2017, the company achieved IATF-16949 certification.
- In July 2018, the facility expanded by 5,000 sq ft for #3 heat treat machinery for metals production.
- In August 2018, the company celebrated its 30th anniversary.
- In September 2018, the Michigan Sales & Design office moved to a new location in Plymouth, Michigan.
- In July 2019, an additional 26,719 sq ft building was added for plastic and assembly production.
