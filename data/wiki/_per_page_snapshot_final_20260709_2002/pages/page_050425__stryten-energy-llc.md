## Facts

- Stryten Energy maintains operational locations in Alpharetta, Georgia, and Cumming, Georgia.
- Stryten Energy’s Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) provides power resiliency to the Atlanta community through a lead BESS installation at the VICARS Community Center, which serves the Southwest Atlanta community as part of Community Church Atlanta.
- Stryten Energy introduced new E-Series AGM Multi-Terminal Batteries designed for harsh environments, offering superior performance and reliability in long-duration discharge applications for telecommunications and utility sectors.
- Stryten Energy offers a Mobile Microgrid solution engineered to deliver remote power and disaster relief by restoring electricity to communities after storms disrupt grid access.
- At CES 2026, Stryten Energy convened industry experts to discuss how batteries support data center infrastructure as AI adoption accelerates energy demand across hyperscale facilities.
- Stryten Energy emphasizes domestically sourced and manufactured battery energy storage solutions to address increasing U.S. power demand and enhance energy security.
- Stryten Energy showcased lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow battery technologies at CES 2026, designed for grid resilience, renewable energy integration, data centers, manufacturing, and EV charging applications.
- Stryten Energy partnered with Georgia Tech to install a lead battery energy storage system that serves as a critical component in the institute’s carbon-neutral initiatives.
- Stryten Energy delivered advanced energy storage solutions to BCI Flow Batteries North America to ensure uninterrupted power for commercial, industrial, and utility-scale applications amid grid disruptions and peak demand.
- The Inflation Reduction Act provides incentives for domestic clean energy manufacturing, including the Advanced Manufacturing Production Credit (AMPC), which encourages companies to invest in U.S.-based production.
- Stryten Energy formed a joint venture with Storion Energy and Largo to establish a domestic supply chain for vanadium flow battery components and deliver price-competitive vanadium electrolyte via a leasing model to accelerate commercialization.
