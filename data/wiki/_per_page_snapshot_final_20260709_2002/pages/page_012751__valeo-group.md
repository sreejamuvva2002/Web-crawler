## Facts

- The news announcement was published on October 25, 2022.
- Valeo showcased its latest automotive innovations at the 2022 AAPEX Show in Booth #A1642.
- Highlights of Valeo's showcase included new product launches in Electrical Systems, Transmission Systems, and Wiping Systems.
- Valeo displayed an award-winning Pendulum Dual Mass Flywheel Gen. 2 at the event.
- By investing heavily in innovation, Valeo strives for its position at the epicenter of the three revolutions redefining the automotive industry: electrification, autonomous vehicles, and digital mobility.
- Alongside innovating for future cars, Valeo continues to provide cutting-edge automotive technologies for replacement parts and the aftermarket for customer vehicles long after they leave the production floor.
- During the three-day event, Valeo introduced visitors to its latest launches across Electrical Systems, Transmission Systems, and Wiping Systems product lines.
- Valeo debuted its comprehensive range of hydraulic systems through its clutch expertise.
- Valeo's hydraulic system capabilities were expanded by its acquisition of industry specialist FTE Automotive in October 2017.
- Valeo presents its visibility excellence through an exclusive distribution partnership with global leader PIAA established in April 2021.
- Valeo showcased at the launch of the newest addition to the “Joe’s Garage” experience at AAPEX 2022, a real-world shop setup where exhibiting brands provide demonstrations and real-time training across eight service bays.
- In the Product Display area at Joe’s Garage, Valeo displayed an array of powertrain, wiping systems, thermal systems, and lighting products.
- Valeo Group won a 2022 Automotive News PACE (Premier Automotive Suppliers' Contribution to Excellence) Award for its Pendulum Dual Mass Flywheel Gen. 2.
- The Pendulum Dual Mass Flywheel Gen. 2 reduces noise, vibration, and harshness associated with the start and stop mechanism of hybrid vehicles.
- This technology is essential for hybrid vehicle performance, providing a strong filtration system to maintain both longevity and comfort within the vehicle.
- Valeo has won 13 total Automotive News PACE Awards.
- In 2021, Valeo won a PACE Award for its 48V eAccess.
- In 2020, Valeo won a PACE Award for its Valeo XtraVue™ Trailer.
- In 2018, Valeo won a PACE Award for its Valeo SCALA®and R-744 HVAC system.
- In 2016, Valeo won a PACE Award for its Electric Supercharger and Water-cooled Condenser.
- In 2015, Valeo won a PACE Award for its EG Efficiency Alternator.
- In 2014, Valeo won a PACE Award for its Back-Over Protection System.
- In 2013, Valeo won a PACE Award for its Air Intake Module.
- In 2012, Valeo won a PACE Award for its AquaBlade®/VisioBlade® wiper system.
- In 2008, Valeo won a PACE Award for its Park4U® park assist system.
- In 2007, Valeo won a PACE Award for its Multi-Beam Radar blind spot detection system.
- In 2006, Valeo won a PACE Award for its StARS micro-hybrid system.
- In 2005, Valeo won a PACE Award for its LaneVue™ lane departure warning system.
