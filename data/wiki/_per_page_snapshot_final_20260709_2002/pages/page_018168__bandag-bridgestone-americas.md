# Bandag (Bridgestone Americas)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-18

**Source:** [Bridgestone Announces Retread Tire Plant Expansion](https://www.tirereview.com/bridgestone-bandag-plant-expansion/)  ·  `page_018168`

## Facts

- Bridgestone Americas celebrated the expansion of its Bandag manufacturing plant in Abilene, Texas with an official groundbreaking ceremony.
- The 50,000-square-foot, $60 million expansion of the Abilene plant is aimed at meeting growing demand for tread rubber products.
- The expansion includes an immediate increase in operational activity by running on six- and seven-day production schedules.
- New mixing operations are being constructed at the facility.
- The new mixing operations are expected to be completed and in operation by January 2025.
- Since 1957, Bandag has been responsible for keeping approximately 300 million tires out of the waste system.
- The retreading process helps reduce material emissions, water, and energy use associated with new tire production.
- The company's retreading process has saved four billion gallons of oil since it began 50 years ago.
