# Continental Tire the Americas, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-05-01

**Source:** [Continental Tires The Americas CEO Announces Retirement - Integrated Media Publishing](https://www.integratedmediapublishing.com/stories/continental-tires-the-americas-ceo-announces-retirement,6820)  ·  `page_018871`

## Facts

- Continental Tires has its U.S. headquarters in Fort Mill.
- Jochen Etzel, chief executive officer of Continental Tire the Americas, LLC, announced his retirement effective May 1, 2025.
- Tansu Işık will succeed Etzel as the new head of Continental’s Business Area Replacement Tires the Americas.
- Işık will report directly to Christian Koetz, executive board member of Continental and head of Continental Tires.
- Etzel has served in this capacity since 2011.
- Etzel played an important role in uniting the various Continental tire activities in the region and further strengthening their competitive position.
- Etzel started his career as a lawyer in Continental’s corporate law department.
- He then took over a financial manager position responsible for developing the company’s tire business in regions outside of Europe before leading Continental’s corporate Mergers and Acquisitions team.
- Christian Koetz, executive board member of Continental and head of Continental Tires, stated that Etzel’s impact on the tire business has been immeasurable and thanked him for his leadership, dedication, and contributions throughout his tenure.
- Işık brings more than 20 years of international experience in a wide variety of markets and functions within Continental’s tires business, including marketing, supply chain, strategy, and key account management.
- Işık began his career as a business development specialist in Istanbul, Turkey.
