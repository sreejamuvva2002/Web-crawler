# Georgia Department of Economic Development

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2024-06-28

**Source:** [Georgia's New Business Investment - The ATL Airport Chamber](https://airportchamber.com/georgias-new-business-investment/)  ·  `page_060625`

## Facts

- The Georgia Department of Economic Development (GDEcD) announced that Georgia was awarded a Gold Shovel by economic development business publication Area Development for achieving significant success in attracting new business investment and job creation in 2023.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp stated that thousands of jobs were created across Georgia thanks to the work of economic development teams in all four corners of the state.
- Kemp noted that GDEcD's efforts helped Georgia land record-breaking investments and hundreds of thousands of jobs for its communities.
- Kemp attributed Georgia's continued status as No. 1 for business to a pro-business approach, an unmatched workforce, and world-renowned logistics.
- Area Development’s annual Gold and Silver Shovel Awards recognize state economic development agencies that demonstrate excellence in creating a business-friendly environment, fostering innovation, and implementing strategies that successfully attracted high-quality investment projects.
- All states were invited to submit information to Area Development on major groundbreakings, investments, and business expansions occurring in 2023.
- Andy Greiner, Editor of Area Development Magazine, stated that the awards highlight the exceptional efforts of state economic development teams and their commitment to building strong economies.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson highlighted Georgia’s diversity of industries leading to success, specifically naming advanced manufacturing, aerospace, e-mobility, life sciences, and fintech as instrumental in achieving a Shovel Award again.
- Wilson reflected on back-to-back record-breaking years and expressed anticipation for celebrating upcoming milestones with project partners.
- Wilson congratulated Governor Kemp, the state legislature, the state economic development team, and all partners across Georgia for their dedication to creating long-term community opportunities.
