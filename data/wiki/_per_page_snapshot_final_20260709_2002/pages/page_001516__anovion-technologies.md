# Anovion Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-02

**Source:** [Anovion Technologies Opening New Manufacturing Facility In Georgia, Creating More Than 400 Jobs | Doc and the Block](https://docandtheblock.com/anovion-technologies-opening-new-manufacturing-facility-in-georgia-creating-more-than-400-jobs/)  ·  `page_001516`

## Facts

- Republican Georgia Governor Brian Kemp announced that Anovion Technologies would be building a new manufacturing facility in Bainbridge.
- The project is projected to create more than 400 jobs.
- The project involves over $800 million in investment in Decatur County.
- Anovion is a supplier of premium synthetic graphite anode materials for lithium-ion batteries.
- Anovion is a climate tech-driven advanced materials company.
- Anovion is one of the first qualified U.S. suppliers of synthetic graphite anode materials for use in e-mobility applications.
- Synthetic graphite anode materials are the largest battery material used in electric vehicles by mass.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia is fast becoming the e-mobility capital of the nation.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that job creators recognize every corner of Georgia has the resources and workforce needed for success.
- Anovion’s new facility will be 1,500,000 square feet.
- The facility will utilize proprietary furnace technologies.
- The facility is located at Downrange Industrial Park in Bainbridge.
- The facility will be the first of its size and scale in North America.
- The facility will produce synthetic graphite for use in batteries that power EVs, electric storage systems, electronics, military applications, and other products.
- Operations are expected to begin in late 2025.
- Eric Stopka is the Chief Executive Officer of Anovion.
- This will be Anovion's first large-scale expansion manufacturing facility in Georgia.
- The state’s strong technical workforce and top-tier schools will help advance research partnerships for Anovion.
- The state’s resources will contribute to a highly skilled talent pipeline in support of Anovion’s innovation and climate technology jobs.
- Anovion is encouraged by the region’s growing availability of clean energy, which will increase sustainability for its manufacturing process.
- Anovion aims to secure the country’s electrified future with support from partners in Georgia and the Federal government.
- Georgia is positioned as a hub for the electric mobility industry, building on assets that make the automotive industry successful.
- Since 2020, 40 EV-related projects have announced more than $21.9 billion in investment in Georgia.
- Since 2020, those 40 EV-related projects have announced 28,000 jobs in Georgia.
- More than 10 of these projects are battery manufacturers, recyclers, and suppliers.
- The battery-focused projects represent approximately $8.5 billion in investment.
- The battery-focused projects represent 6,000 jobs.
