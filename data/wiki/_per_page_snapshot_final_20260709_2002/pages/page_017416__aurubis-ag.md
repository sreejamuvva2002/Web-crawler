# Aurubis AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2025-09-11

**Source:** [Aurubis AG: Aurubis secures € 200 million from EIB to drive recycling and copper production | Corporate - EQS News](https://www.eqs-news.com/news/corporate/aurubis-ag-aurubis-secures-e-200-million-from-eib-to-drive-recycling-and-copper-production/bd5e5d92-9af2-4d81-9d46-730078597e39_en)  ·  `page_017416`

## Facts

- Aurubis AG formally signed an agreement with the European Investment Bank (EIB) for a €200 million investment loan on September 11, 2025.
- The five-year loan will support two strategic projects: expanding copper refining at its Bulgarian site and scaling up metal recycling and environmental protection at its Hamburg plant.
- Steffen Hoffmann, Chief Financial Officer at Aurubis, stated the financing commitment acknowledges the social relevance and outstanding quality of their investment projects.
- The loan's attractive terms compared to market conditions enable Aurubis to further diversify its financing base.
- Nicola Beer, Vice President of the European Investment Bank, said securing critical raw materials like copper and expanding recycling activities in Europe are essential to Europe’s resilience, sustainable development, and industrial competitiveness.
- Copper is described as driving innovation in electric cars and renewable energy.
- The EIB loan is the first following the adoption of its new broad-ranging critical raw materials strategy and supports the rollout of the EU Critical Raw Materials Act.
- Both projects reinforce sustainable metal production and support key EU policy objectives such as economic growth, regional development, and sustainable business practices to create new jobs.
- The financing commitment represents the first EIB financing for the sector since the adoption of the new EIB Group strategy to secure Europe’s access to critical raw materials earlier this year.
- Aurubis is investing €120 million to expand the tankhouse at its Bulgarian production site and boost cathode production to cover growing European demand for copper.
- Aurubis anticipates commissioning in fiscal year 2025/26 for the Bulgarian expansion.
- This is the largest single investment at the Bulgarian plant since its acquisition in 2008.
- The Bulgarian expansion represents an around 50% increase in production at the site to 340,000 tons of refined copper per year.
- The expansion will support Bulgarian economic development, boost exports, and create additional jobs.
- Copper plays a crucial role in enabling the energy transition, AI and data centers, and underpins Europe’s security architecture.
- With increased production capacity, Aurubis is positioned to better serve Europe’s rising demand for copper in the future.
- Experts predict that global demand for copper will double by 2050.
- The loan will also back the €190 million Complex Recycling Hamburg (CRH) investment program at the North German site.
- Aurubis expects operations to begin in the coming fiscal year for the CRH facility.
- The new Aurubis facility will process around 30,000 additional tons of recycled material along with a larger amount of complex internal smelting intermediates.
- The CRH project closes both internal and external material loops and reinforces the company’s contribution to the circular economy.
- The EIB Group Strategic Initiative for Strengthening EU’s Critical Raw Materials (CRM) Supply Chain was adopted in March 2025.
- The initiative aligns with the EU’s Critical Raw Materials Act, setting an ambitious annual lending orientation of EUR 2 billion to support critical raw materials across extraction, processing, recycling, and substitution.
- It delivers a targeted 9-point action plan to catalyse investment, support innovative and sustainable technologies, and strengthen the entire CRM value chain inside and outside the EU.
- Aurubis AG processes complex metal concentrates, scrap metals, organic and inorganic metal-bearing recycling materials, and industrial residues into metals of the highest quality.
- Aurubis produces more than 1 million tons of copper cathodes annually.
- From copper cathodes, Aurubis produces wire rod, continuous cast shapes, profiles, and flat rolled products made of copper and copper alloys.
- Aurubis also produces precious metals, selenium, lead, nickel, tin, zinc, sulfuric acid, and iron silicate.
- Aurubis has around 7,100 employees and production sites in Europe and the US.
