## Facts

- Georgia offers $2.4B+ in annual business incentives across 65+ programs.
- The state utilizes an aggressive Tier system ranking counties from Tier 1 (most distressed) to Tier 4 (least distressed).
- Businesses creating new full-time jobs in Tier 1 counties qualify for a Job Tax Credit of $750 to $4,000 per job per year for 5 years.
- No equity is required for the Job Tax Credit or Georgia Quick Start programs.
- In Tier 1 counties, Job Tax Credits can offset 100% of state income tax liability and excess credits can be applied against payroll withholding taxes.
- Job creation thresholds for the credit vary by tier, ranging from 2 to 25 jobs.
- Strategic industries eligible include manufacturing, telecommunications, broadcasting, tech, headquarters operations, and processing.
- Form IT-CA must be filed with the annual state corporate income tax return to claim Job Tax Credits.
- Credits can offset 50% to 100% of state income tax liability depending on the county tier.
- Georgia provides an Apprenticeship Program Tax Credit of up to $25,000 per apprentice per year, capped at $100k per employer.
- The apprenticeship credit requires a registered apprenticeship with the US Dept of Labor or state agency and focuses on manufacturing and high-demand trades.
- Georgia Quick Start offers free customized workforce training valued at thousands of dollars per employee for companies creating 15 or more new jobs.
- Quick Start eligibility targets manufacturing, biotech, distribution, or HQ operations requiring complex skills.
- The state builds custom mobile training centers and supplies instructors to train initial workforces entirely for free through Quick Start.
- A high-wage Job Tax Credit offers $2,500 to $5,000 per job per year for 5 years for positions paying 110% to 200% of the local average county wage.
- The high-wage credit requires creating 50 or more jobs within 12 to 24 months and usually requires an investment threshold.
- A mega-project statutory credit offers $5,250 per job per year for 5 years for transformational investments of $450 million or more that create 1,800 new jobs.
- The mega-project credit is designed for automotive and major manufacturing anchor projects.
- The Georgia Film Tax Credit provides up to a 30% transferable tax credit on qualified local production spend of $500,000 or more.
- Productions receive a 20% base tax credit plus a 10% uplift for including the Georgia logo in credits.
- Transferable film credits can be sold to corporations for 85 to 90 cents on the dollar to generate immediate cash.
