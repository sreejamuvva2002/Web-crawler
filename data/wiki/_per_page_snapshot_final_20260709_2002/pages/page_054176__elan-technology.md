## Facts

- For over 70 years Elan Technology has been a leader in the glass, ceramic and toll spray drying industries.
- Elan Technology is the largest independent U.S. company producing glass preforms for hermetic seals and spacers using technical glass, ceramics, and glass-ceramic composites.
- Technical glass and ceramic parts from Elan Technology can be found in the defense industry, machinery, space exploration equipment, cell phones, refrigerators, automobiles, flashlights and numerous other applications.
- Elan Technology toll spray dries products used to dye the yellow thread in jeans.
- Elan Technology toll spray dries products used to make gasoline.
- Elan Technology toll spray dries a product that helps grass grow without harmful chemicals.
- The company's plant was constructed in 1997 and has a 90,000 sq.ft. footprint on 22 acres.
- Elan Technology is located in Midway, GA.
- Elan Technology has between 10 and 49 employees.
- Elan Technology was founded in 1997.
