# Vanguard National Trailer Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [GA: Vanguard National Trailer Corp. to Invest $30M, Create 400 Jobs | Trade and Industry Development](https://www.tradeandindustrydev.com/industry/automotive/news/ga-vanguard-national-trailer-corp-invest-30m-10714)  ·  `page_013070`

## Facts

- Gov. Nathan Deal announced that Vanguard National Trailer Corp., a leading semi-trailer manufacturer based in Monon, Ind., will invest $30 million in a new manufacturing facility in Dade County.
- Once operating at full capacity, the new location will create 400 jobs.
- Vanguard National Trailer Corp. will develop nearly 100 acres of land and build a new 350,000 square-foot facility near Trenton.
- The newly-created jobs will include both office and manufacturing positions.
- Vanguard National Trailer will take advantage of Georgia’s Quick Start program, the nation’s No. 1 workforce training program.
- Vanguard president and CEO Charlie Mudd stated the company is excited to expand production capabilities and thanked the State of Georgia and the Georgia Department of Economic Development for their project support.
- Vanguard National Trailer Corp. manufactures dry freight and refrigerated semi-trailer vans for the North American transportation industry.
- Dade County Commission chairman and county executive Ted Rumley noted that the capital investment and 400 new jobs will have a significant impact on the community and phase II of a new industrial park.
- GDEcD senior project manager Brittany Holtzclaw supported the company in partnership with the Dade County Industrial Development Authority, the Northwest Georgia Joint Development Authority, Georgia Power, and the Georgia Department of Transportation.
- GDEcD Commissioner Chris Carr highlighted Quick Start as one of the state’s greatest economic development assets and expressed confidence in Vanguard's long-term success in Georgia.
- Vanguard National Trailer Corp. is a subsidiary of CIMC, the world’s leading manufacturer of dry and refrigerated container and trailer equipment.
- CIMC operates over 100 subsidiaries with 47,000 employees across North America, China, Europe, Asia, and Australia.
- CIMC is listed in Forbes Top 2000 World Leading Companies and Business Weekly’s Top 50 Asian Companies.
