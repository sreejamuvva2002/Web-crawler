## Facts

- Piolax is a leading supplier of plastic and metal fastening products with global marketing, knowledge, and technological capabilities.
- To meet the demands of the global automotive industry, Piolax established a U.S. presence in Canton, Georgia, in August 1988.
- The company's in-house manufacturing expertise enhances its ability to deliver high-quality products to the automotive market.
- Piolax is a major manufacturer of precision automotive springs and industrial fasteners.
- Automotive products account for 95% of Piolax's sales.
- The company partners with all major domestic automobile and truck manufacturers, as well as housing equipment manufacturers.
- Piolax holds a leading market share in each product category it serves.
- As the industry evolves, especially with the rise of electric vehicles, Piolax remains committed to developing and providing innovative products to meet the needs of a wide range of electronic and automotive applications.
- Piolax’s product offerings fall into three main categories: Fasteners, Fuel Systems, and Open/Close Mechanisms.
- Piolax’s fasteners are produced using advanced elastic microfabrication technology, material processing techniques for metals and synthetic resins, and applied technology for shape-memory alloys.
