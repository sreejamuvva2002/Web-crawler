## Facts

- The facility is located in Pendergrass, Georgia.
- Assembly uses manual skills and automated systems to manufacture AC compressors.
- Machining uses CNC tools and machinery to custom-build components for AC compressors.
- Sand casting forms cavities within packed and bonded sand charged with molten metal.
- Toyota Industries Group makes the Lug plate and Swash parts for the automobile variable compressor using the sand casting method.
- Die casting injects molten metal under high pressure into a reusable mold to create complex metal parts with precise dimensions.
- Logistics coordinates and connects all manufacturing processes to keep systems on schedule.
- Engineering focuses on continuous improvement and innovation for state-of-the-art robotic automation unique to the industry.
