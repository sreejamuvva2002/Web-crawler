# Pak-Lite, Inc.

## Facts

- PLI (Pak-Lite) was founded in Atlanta, Georgia in February 1972 by Frank M Pritchard.
- The company began as a manufacturer of protective packaging products and in May of 1983 opened its second manufacturing facility in Mebane, NC.
- In 1985, the leadership team at Pak-Lite consisting of David M. Pritchard, Stanley J. Beard and William H. Beard purchased the company from Frank Pritchard.
- David and William still remain the owners of PLI and they continue to operate the company with an emphasis on simplicity and the basic principle of quality above all.
- PLI (Pak-Lite, Inc.) is a leading North American converter/manufacturer of foams, nonwovens, foils, films, specialty materials, and fabrics.
- The company specializes in various markets including automotive, marine, HVAC, flooring, construction, packaging, transportation, and manufacturing.
- PLI's customer base includes many of the top OEM and industrial companies in the world.
- Manufacturing facilities are located in Georgia, North Carolina, and Taiwan with a variety of distribution affiliates on both the East and West Coasts of the United States and Canada.
- The company has over 300+ dedicated professionals committed to making projects a success.
- In July 2004, PLI opened its 3rd state of the art facility in Suwanee, Ga., and moved its corporate offices to that location.
- Recent purchases of skiving, cam cutting, laminating, and spooling equipment have greatly strengthened PLI's commitment and value to customers.
- The company's most recent state-of-the-art flooring plant opened in April 2023.
- The company has enjoyed sales growth in the past years and continues to grow at an exceptional level to this day.
