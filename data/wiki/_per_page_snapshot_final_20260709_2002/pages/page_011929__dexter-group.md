# Dexter Group

## Facts

- Dexter Group acquired the axle, brake and actuation business assets from Tie Down Engineering, Inc. (“Tie Down”).
- The acquired assets are located in Atlanta, GA.
- Tie Down was founded in 1969 by Chuck MacKarvich.
- Tie Down serves the construction, utility, industrial & marine markets.
- Tie Down will continue to operate, providing products and services from their manufactured housing, roofing, marine and industrial laser divisions.
- The acquisition adds Tie Down’s axle, brake and actuation products to Dexter's marine product portfolio.
- The acquisition broadens Dexter's aftermarket offering and allows growth in adjacent areas.
- Fred Bentley serves as the CEO of DexKo Global.
- Adam Dexter serves as the President and CEO of Dexter.
- Dean Samuelson serves as the President of Tie Down Engineering and will continue to lead the acquired business.
- Tie Down’s product offering complements Dexter’s prior acquisitions of UFP and Titan.
- DexKo Global and Dexter are majority owned by a fund managed by The Sterling Group.
- The Sterling Group is an operationally-focused middle market private equity firm based in Houston, Texas.
- The Sterling Group has $2.2 billion of assets under management.
