# Stryten Energy LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-06

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Showcases New 48V 105Ah Golf Cart Battery at AAPEX | DFLI Stock News](https://www.stocktitan.net/news/DFLI/stryten-energy-showcases-breakthrough-battery-technologies-at-aapex-yaxckg688oke.html)  ·  `page_050591`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy showcased breakthrough battery technologies at AAPEX 2025.
- The AAPEX event took place from November 4-6 at the Venetian Expo.
- Stryten featured its Enhanced Flooded Battery (EFB) technology and the Battle Born® powered by Stryten Energy brand of lithium batteries.
- Vehicle computerization and electrical demands are increasing, making battery selection more critical than ever.
- EFB technology delivers reliable performance for stop-start and hybrid vehicle applications.
- Dan Autey serves as Executive Vice President, Commercial, for Stryten Energy.
- According to Dan Autey, vehicle electronics perform well with a Stryten Energy EFB due to its exceptional cycling performance, superior start-stop capability, and outstanding durability in extreme hot and cold conditions.
- According to Dan Autey, EFBs deliver greater dynamic charge acceptance, enabling faster recharging compared to conventional flooded batteries.
- Battle Born powered by Stryten Energy Batteries are premium LiFePO₄ lithium solutions designed for off-grid living, marine, and powersports applications.
- Stryten Energy markets and distributes Battle Born Batteries across most business-to-business markets through an exclusive licensing agreement.
- The partnership with Dragonfly Energy (NASDAQ: DFLI) enables Stryten to offer customers a comprehensive portfolio of battery technologies.
- A Jeep Gladiator outfitted with an EFB and three Battle Born Batteries was displayed at Stryten's booth.
- The booth featured an electric people mover showcasing the new 48v, 105Ah Battle Born by Stryten Energy lithium Golf Cart Battery.
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- Melissa Floyd holds the title of Vice President, Corporate Communications & Digital Marketing at Stryten Energy LLC.
- Stryten Energy's exhibition booth was located at Booth #4216 in the Underhood section of the Venetian Expo.
