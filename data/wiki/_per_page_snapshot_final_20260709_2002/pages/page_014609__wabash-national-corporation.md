# Wabash National Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-11

**Source:** [Wabash Strengthens Manufacturing Capacity - Trailer Technician](https://trailertechnician.com/2021/11/wabash-strengthens-manufacturing-capacity/)  ·  `page_014609`

## Facts

- Wabash National Corporation has announced a 10-year agreement with Hydro, a leader in the global aluminum and energy industry.
- Under the agreement, Hydro will be a key supplier for Wabash National’s demand for dry van trailers, refrigerated trailers with molded structural composite (MSC) technology, flatbed trailers, and truck bodies through 2031.
- The agreement also covers requirements of new products Wabash National may release through 2031.
- The enhanced supply aligns with Wabash National’s recent announcement of additional dry van capacity set to come online at the beginning of 2023.
- Future capacity for MSC Technology™ products is also planned.
- Richard Mansilla, Vice President, Global Supply Chain at Wabash National, stated they selected Hydro as a strategic supplier partner due to shared values and vision for the industry.
- The partnership aims to address aluminum supply needs in the near term to aid production increases of dry van trailers, refrigerated trailers, and truck bodies with MSC Technology.
- In the longer term, Wabash National plans to work more closely with Hydro on innovation and footprint optimization.
- Hydro has supplied Wabash National with aluminum extrusions for over 12 years prior to this agreement.
- Hydro is a three-time Wabash National supplier award winner and was named Supplier of the Year in 2020.
- Jerrod Hoeft, Hydro’s VP National Accounts, Transportation, expressed excitement about working more closely on sustainability and innovation.
- Wabash National designs and manufactures dry freight and refrigerated trailers, platform trailers, tank trailers, dry and refrigerated truck bodies, structural composite panels and products, trailer aerodynamic solutions, and specialty food grade processing equipment.
- Wabash National's innovative products are sold under brand names including Wabash National®, Benson®, Brenner® Tank, Bulk Tank International, DuraPlate®, Supreme®, Transcraft®, Walker Engineered Products, and Walker Transport.
