## Facts

- Mando America Corporation manufacturers world-class Brake Systems to domestic and global automobile manufacturers such as BMW, Chrysler, FIAT, Ford, GM, KIA, and Volkswagon.
- The company has 2 plants in Georgia.
- Tour attendees viewed behind-the-scenes manufacturing processes at their 445,000 sq. ft. facility in Hogansville, which uses high-end technology to produce Caliper and Automatic Brake Systems.
- Manufactured systems include Active Retraction Caliper, Automatic Brake Systems, Caliper Brakes, Electronic Stability Control, and Electronic Stability Program.
