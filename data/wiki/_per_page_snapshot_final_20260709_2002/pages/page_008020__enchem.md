# Enchem

**Source:** [Korean battery supplier to locate in Jackson County](https://www.ajc.com/business/real-estate/korean-battery-parts-supplier-open-plant-jackson-county/hdoLYjWEu13P6c4yyOLK9O/)  ·  `page_008020`

## Facts

- Georgia has recruited Enchem, a South Korean company that makes parts for batteries used in electric cars.
- Enchem will build two facilities in Jackson County to make electrolytes used in rechargeable batteries.
- Gov. Brian Kemp’s office announced the project on Wednesday.
- State officials describe the project as a $61 million investment.
- The project will create about 300 jobs.
- Enchem factories will supply products to SK Innovation.
- SK Innovation is another South Korean company building a separate $1.7 billion plant near Commerce in Jackson County.
- SK Innovation's plant will make lithium-ion batteries.
- Gov. Brian Kemp said in a news release: “This is exactly the type of quality project that we hoped to attract when visiting Korea on our first international trip last June.”
