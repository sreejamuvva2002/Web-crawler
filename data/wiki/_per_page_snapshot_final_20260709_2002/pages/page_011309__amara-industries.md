# Amara Industries

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-12-09

**Source:** [Amara Industries Acquires Thomson Plastics](https://www.webull.com/news/13998091231667200)  ·  `page_011309`

## Facts

- Amara Industries, a parent company to the Gracious Living group of companies, announced the acquisition of Thomson Plastics on Dec. 9, 2025.
- Thomson Plastics is a recognized leader in large tonnage injection molding in the Southeastern United States.
- Thomson Plastics currently operates from two locations in Georgia and North Carolina.
- The acquisition brings Amara Industries' total manufacturing facilities to five.
- Thomson Plastics has clamping force capacities up to 3,700 tons, with 30% of its fleet exceeding 1,000 tons.
- Thomson Plastics utilizes advanced robotics and automation for high-volume production environments.
- Thomson Plastics' technical portfolio includes injection molding, gas-assisted molding, over-molding, insert molding, in-mold labeling, robotic part picking, pad printing, and heat transfer decorating.
- Vince Orlando serves as Co-President at Amara Industries.
- Amara Industries aims to serve customers with scale, speed, and precision across North America.
- The acquisition positions the unified company to serve rising demand for high-performance molded products in key sectors including automotive, consumer goods, industrial, and commercial markets.
- The expansion into the Southeastern United States aims to provide customers with faster delivery, greater flexibility, and improved supply chain efficiency.
- Amara Industries is a provider of comprehensive custom manufacturing solutions.
- Amara Industries employs over 500 people.
- Amara Industries offers end-to-end capabilities spanning design, product development, engineering, and materials science.
- Amara Industries specializes in injection molding, assembly, profile extrusion, decorating, and material compounding.
- Amara Industries holds key certifications including ISO, IATF, and CTPAT.
