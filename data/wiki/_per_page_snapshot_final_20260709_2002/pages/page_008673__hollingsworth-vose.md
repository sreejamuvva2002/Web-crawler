# Hollingsworth & Vose

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hollingsworth & Vose wins 2026 BCI Innovation Award  - Renewable Energy News Today - EIN Presswire](https://renewableenergy.einnews.com/pr_news/907576437/hollingsworth-vose-wins-2026-bci-innovation-award)  ·  `page_008673`

## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose won the 2026 BCI Innovation Award for its Glass Electrode Material (GEM) technology.
- GEM enhances lead battery chargeability by generating and supporting a unique microporous structure within the lead active mass to improve chargeability performance and cycle life reliability without impacting water loss.
- GEM improves recharge efficiency through enhanced charge acceptance, which reduces fuel-related emissions while improving battery performance, durability, and cycle life reliability.
- GEM features approximately three times higher Dynamic Charge Acceptance (DCA) compared to standard materials.
- GEM enables next-generation start-stop systems that support fuel economy gains of up to 5%.
- The technology's fast recharge response makes it suitable for advanced 12-V architectures in hybrid (HEV, PHEV) and electric vehicle auxiliary systems.
- The GEM technology platform is applicable across multiple energy storage applications, including automotive batteries, motive power for forklifts, and stationary backup power solutions.
- Hollingsworth & Vose developed the GEM technology platform in partnership with ArcActive.
- Nicolas Clement, R&D Director, and Dr. Amogh Karwa, Director of Product management at Hollingsworth & Vose, stated that GEM has true potential for creating a more sustainable future and will serve as a great tool for battery manufacturers to enable lead batteries to compete better with alternative chemistries.
- East Penn Manufacturing and Silicon Joule received Honorable Mention recognition for their joint work on the first commercial bi-pole battery based on lead chemistry and treated silicon wafers.
- Battery Council International (BCI) annually presents the Innovation Award to celebrate innovation in equipment, processes, services, and products that advance the lead battery industry.
- The award was established to honor industry thought leader Sally Breidegam Miksiewicz, the former CEO of East Penn Manufacturing Co.
- Hollingsworth & Vose is a global manufacturer of advanced materials used in filtration, battery, and industrial applications, and has been family-owned for seven generations.
- Hollingsworth & Vose is headquartered in East Walpole, Massachusetts, U.S.A.
