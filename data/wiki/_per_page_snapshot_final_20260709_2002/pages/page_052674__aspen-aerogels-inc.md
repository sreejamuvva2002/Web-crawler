# Aspen Aerogels, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2022-02-17

**Source:** [Aspen Aerogels, Inc. - Aspen Aerogels to Open Advanced Manufacturing Facility in Bulloch County](https://ir.aerogel.com/news/news-details/2022/Aspen-Aerogels-to-Open-Advanced-Manufacturing-Facility-in-Bulloch-County/default.aspx)  ·  `page_052674`

## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels, Inc. (NYSE: ASPN) plans to construct an advanced manufacturing facility at Bulloch County's Southern Gateway Commerce Park.
- Aspen intends to invest a minimum of $325 million at the new site near Statesboro.
- The company plans to create more than 250 advanced manufacturing jobs at the new facility.
- Production at the new Georgia site is expected to begin in late 2023.
- Combined with Aspen's original East Providence, Rhode Island plant, the planned facility will triple Aspen's nameplate aerogel capacity.
- The project aims to capitalize on increased demand for Aspen's Aerogel Technology Platform™ as EV OEMs adopt its PyroThin® thermal barrier solutions.
- Governor Brian P. Kemp stated that Aspen Aerogels has put its confidence in Georgia and the growing electric mobility sector, adding an American company to the state's rapidly expanding EV portfolio.
- Governor Kemp noted that the state is utilizing its assets to bring jobs to Georgians and highlighted this as the latest project to come to Bulloch County and southeast Georgia.
- Don Young, President and CEO of Aspen, expressed pleasure at being in Georgia for the siting, development, and construction of the company's second aerogel manufacturing plant.
- Young cited the City of Statesboro and Bulloch County region offering superior operating features, including access to efficient ports, a strong available workforce, and secure low-cost utilities and critical raw materials.
- Aspen is increasingly being asked to provide broader solutions for improved battery performance and safety centered on PyroThin materials that provide thermal management, mechanical stability, and fire protection properties to EV battery systems.
- Aspen plans to build a 500,000-square-foot advanced manufacturing facility situated on a 90-acre parcel in southeast Georgia.
- The company plans to begin hiring personnel in the second half of 2022 across multiple disciplines.
- Hiring will span roles including skilled manufacturing operators, advanced process engineers, quality and maintenance technicians, and professional support staff.
- Billy Allen, Vice Chair of the Development Authority of Bulloch County, welcomed Aspen Aerogels to the Statesboro-Bulloch County community.
- Allen highlighted the region's site and logistics advantages as a testament to its commitment to innovation in the electric mobility ecosystem.
- He expressed confidence that local education resources, including Georgia Southern University and Ogeechee Technical College, will provide critical support to help Aspen flourish.
- Assistant Director Alyce Thornhill represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development's (GDEcD) Global Commerce division on the project.
- The GDEcD partnered with the Development Authority of Bulloch County, Statesboro, Georgia Quick Start, and Georgia EMC on this project.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson welcomed Aspen Aerogels' innovative, sustainable solutions for the electric vehicle and energy storage markets to Georgia.
- Wilson looked forward to Georgia employees collaborating with Aspen's network of world-class scientists, engineers, and additional skilled workforce to solve challenges in the changing mobility landscape.
- Southern Gateway Commerce Park is a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) Certified park.
- To earn GRAD certification, a property must undergo due diligence and complete specific certification requirements prior to a visit by a prospective business.
- Aspen's PyroThin® thermal barrier products enable solutions to thermal runaway challenges within the electric vehicle and energy storage markets.
- The company's carbon aerogel program seeks to increase the performance of lithium-ion battery cells to enable EV manufacturers to extend driving range and reduce the cost of electric vehicles.
- Aspen is headquartered in Northborough, MA, and manufactures its products at its East Providence, R.I. facilities.
