# Archer Aviation ARC Manufacturing Facility

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-12

**Source:** [Archer Completes Construction of eVTOL Manufacturing Facility - Advanced Air Mobility Intl](https://www.aaminternational.com/2024/12/archer-completes-construction-of-evtol-manufacturing-facility/)  ·  `page_017072`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation has received the certificate of occupancy for its high-volume eVTOL manufacturing facility, ARC.
- ARC is connected to the Covington Municipal Airport in Georgia.
- The facility is located in Newton County, Georgia.
- Archer is currently working to complete the tooling load-in for the initial manufacturing line at ARC.
- Production at ARC is planned to begin in early 2025.
- Archer aims to ramp production to a rate of two aircraft per month by the end of 2025.
- Archer built ARC in close partnership with Stellantis.
- Archer and Stellantis are continuing to progress plans to mass-produce Archer’s Midnight aircraft.
- Stellantis is contributing capital, advanced manufacturing technology, expertise, and experienced personnel to the project.
- The partnership aims to scale ARC's production capacity to 650 aircraft annually by 2030.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp stated that Georgia remains committed to working with innovative companies like Archer to create quality jobs and opportunities for years to come.
- U.S. Congressman Mike Collins of Georgia sits on the House Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure and the Committee on Science, Space, and Technology.
- Congressman Mike Collins stated that the facility's completion highlights Georgia’s leadership in innovation, advanced manufacturing, and job creation.
- Congressman Collins noted the project will drive progress, deliver economic benefits for Georgia, and create opportunity for everyone involved with and around the facility.
- Adam Goldstein is Archer’s founder and CEO.
- Goldstein stated that the facility's completion marks a shift in the industry from R&D into commercialization.
- Goldstein noted that construction on ARC is complete and the team is focused on execution and scaling after production starts early next year.
- Goldstein praised the Archer team and partners for completing the project quickly and on budget.
- Arnaud Deboeuf is Stellantis's Chief Manufacturing and Supply Chain Officer.
- Deboeuf stated that supporting Archer’s electric aircraft growth with Stellantis's deep manufacturing expertise is a great opportunity for both companies.
- Deboeuf congratulated the Archer team on the milestone, calling it a significant step in taking clean mobility to the sky.
- ARC will be used to produce Archer’s eVTOL aircraft, Midnight.
- The Midnight aircraft is designed to offer passengers a sustainable, low-noise, and safe alternative to ground transportation in congested cities around the world.
