# Aspen Aerogels

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-16

**Source:** [Aspen Aerogels receives federal loan to build new plant in Georgia](https://www.bostonglobe.com/2024/10/16/business/northborough-aspen-aerogels-federal-loan-plant-talking-points/)  ·  `page_052683`

## Facts

- Northborough-based Aspen Aerogels disclosed that the US Department of Energy approved a loan of up to $671 million to finance the construction of a manufacturing plant in Georgia.
- Shares in Aspen Aerogels rose by more than 13 percent on Wednesday following the disclosure.
- The Georgia plant will manufacture PyroThin aerogel blankets, which serve as thermal barriers to prevent out-of-control vehicle battery fires.
- Aspen already operates one manufacturing plant in East Providence, R.I.
- Aspen opened an engineering and prototyping center in Marlborough last year.
- The company currently outsources some of its manufacturing work to suppliers in China.
- Aspen currently employs about 550 people.
- Aspen plans to employ 255 full-time workers at the new Georgia plant.
- Aspen chief executive Donald Young stated that the Department of Energy loan will fully fund the company’s capital expenses for building the Georgia factory.
