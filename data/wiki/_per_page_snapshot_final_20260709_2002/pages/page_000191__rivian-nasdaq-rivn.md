# Rivian (NASDAQ: RIVN)

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-07-17

**Source:** [Rivian Announces New East Coast Headquarters in Atlanta - Newsroom - Rivian](https://rivian.com/newsroom/article/rivian-announces-new-east-coast-headquarters-in-atlanta)  ·  `page_000191`

## Facts

- Rivian announced a new East Coast headquarters in Atlanta, Georgia on July 17, 2025.
- The announcement was made jointly by Rivian, Georgia Governor Brian Kemp, and Atlanta Mayor Andre Dickens.
- The new headquarters will feature a 500-person office space to complement the company's new manufacturing facility in Georgia.
- The headquarters office is scheduled to open in late 2025.
- Further expansion of the headquarters will occur in 2026 as construction accelerates at Rivian's new manufacturing site in Social Circle.
- Rivian will occupy the top floor and lobby of Portman Holdings' Junction Krog District building located at 667 Auburn Ave NE.
- The headquarters location is adjacent to the Eastside Trail of the Atlanta Beltline and is easily accessible by public transportation.
- Rivian expects to employ approximately 100 people at the Atlanta site by the end of 2025.
- The fully built headquarters will house approximately 500 employees.
- Rivian Founder and CEO RJ Scaringe stated that Atlanta embodies top talent, exceptional creativity, and a desire to always be moving forward.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp noted that Georgia is a prime location for company headquarters and expressed excitement about Rivian joining the list of brands based in the state capital.
- Atlanta Mayor Andre Dickens highlighted that Atlanta continues to lead in EV innovation and technology integration, reinforcing the city's role in shaping its future economy.
- Rivian plans to build a 7,500-person manufacturing facility in Stanton Springs North, near Social Circle, Georgia.
- As it scales, Rivian plans to establish partnerships with universities, technical colleges, and local, regional, and state institutions in Georgia.
- Rivian is an American automotive manufacturer that develops and builds electric vehicles and accessories.
- The company's goal is to accelerate the global transition to zero-emission transportation and energy.
- Rivian vehicles are manufactured in the United States and sold directly to consumer and commercial customers.
