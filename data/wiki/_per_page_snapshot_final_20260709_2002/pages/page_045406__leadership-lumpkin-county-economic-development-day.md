# Leadership Lumpkin County Economic Development Day

**Type:** event  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** 2023-01-05

**Source:** [DALC hosts Leadership Lumpkin County Economic Development Day – Development Authority of Lumpkin County](https://picklumpkincounty.org/dalc-hosts-leadership-lumpkin-county-economic-development-day/)  ·  `page_045406`

## Facts

- On January 5, 2023 the Development Authority of Lumpkin County (DALC) hosted the Dahlonega-Lumpkin County Chamber of Commerce Leadership Lumpkin County (LLC) Economic Development Day at the JTEKT North America plant in Lumpkin County.
- The event included a group of 29 participants who started with fundamentals of economic development and a community profile of Lumpkin County.
- LLC Day Chair Dathan Harbert stated that Economic Development Day is an integral part of the LLC program and opens eyes to the diversity of businesses in Dahlonega-Lumpkin County.
- A Workforce Development Panel featured Cory Addison, Director of Business & Industry at Lanier Technical College.
- The panel also included Bobbi Larson, Director of Economic Development & Community Engagement at the University of North Georgia.
- The panel also included Jason Lemley, Director of Community Engagement at the Lumpkin County School System.
- These workforce partners shared how they collaborate with DALC, local businesses, and community partners to prepare students for careers in Lumpkin County and the region.
- The class included a MythBusters Panel where participants asked questions about traffic issues, affordable housing, attracting jobs to the area, and more.
- MythBusters Panel member Ariel Alexander serves as Main Street Manager with City of Dahlonega.
- MythBusters Panel member Sean Phipps serves as Executive Director with Lumpkin County Water and Sewerage Authority.
- MythBusters Panel member Rhett Stringer serves as District 3 Commissioner with Lumpkin County Board of Commissioners.
- MythBusters Panel member Rebecca Mincey serves as Executive Director with the Development Authority of Lumpkin County.
- The group toured two local manufacturers: JTEKT North America and North Georgia Still Company.
- Both JTEKT North America and North Georgia Still Company contribute to Lumpkin County through capital investment, corporate responsibility, and workforce development.
