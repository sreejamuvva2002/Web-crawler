# Great Dane Trailers

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Great Dane Trailers Announces New Organizational Structure | Desi Trucking](https://desitrucking.com/great-dane-trailers-announces-new-organizational-structure/)  ·  `page_055356`

## Facts

- Great Dane Trailers announced the reorganization of its management structure.
- The changes are part of an ongoing process aimed at enhancing leadership, business fundamentals, and accelerating future growth.
- Bill Crown serves as President and Chief Executive Officer of Great Dane Trailers.
- Phill Pines is retiring from his role as President of Great Dane.
- Jim Pines holds the title of Executive Vice President, Sales and Operations.
- Jim Pines will continue to oversee all Sales activities, including Factory Direct Sales, Retail Sales, Aftermarket Parts and Service, and Used Trailers.
- Jim Pines will also oversee Great Dane’s Manufacturing Operations.
- Jim Pines will report directly to Bill Crown.
- Rick Mullininx is a 28-year Great Dane veteran promoted to Executive Vice President, Engineering.
- Rick Mullininx will report directly to Bill Crown and oversee Great Dane’s entire Engineering and Product Development organization.
- Dean Engelage joins the Great Dane team as Executive Vice President, Strategic Planning and Administration.
- Dean Engelage previously spent more than 15 years with CC Industries, which is Great Dane’s parent company.
- Dean Engelage will direct Business Development, Marketing, Management Information Systems, Human Resources, and Johnson Refrigerated Truck Bodies.
- Dean Engelage will report directly to Bill Crown.
- Jim Petrarca serves as Chief Financial Officer overseeing all of the Company’s Finance and Accounting activities.
- Jim Petrarca will report directly to Bill Crown.
- The Executive Committee responsible to the Board for all Operations and Planning at Great Dane Trailers comprises Jim Pines, Rick Mullininx, Dean Engelage, Jim Petrarca, Sam Gupta, and Bill Crown.
- Sam Gupta holds the title of Senior Vice President, Manufacturing.
- The organizational changes are effective immediately.
- The announcement was issued from Savannah, GA and Chicago, IL.
