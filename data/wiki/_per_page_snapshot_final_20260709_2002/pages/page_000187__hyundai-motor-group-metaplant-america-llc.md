# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America LLC

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hyundai Metaplant America EV Plant | Tim Moran Hyundai](https://www.timmoranhyundai.com/hyundai-motor-group-breaks-ground-on-metaplant-america-dedicated-ev-and-battery-plant)  ·  `page_000187`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Executive Chair Euisun Chung, along with Governor Brian P. Kemp, company officials, and state and local leaders officially broke ground on Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) LLC’s new manufacturing plant at the Bryan County Mega site.
- The celebration included a morning groundbreaking on site followed by a future technology expo at Enmarket Arena in Savannah where the company highlighted “advanced technology and extraordinary human achievement” with exhibits for hundreds of community attendees.
- A $5.54 billion investment by Hyundai Motor Group and its affiliate suppliers was announced five months ago.
- The project includes plans to produce a diverse range of innovative Hyundai, Genesis and Kia electric vehicles (EVs).
- The project includes plans to build a new battery manufacturing facility.
- HMGMA will create more than 8,100 jobs over the next few years.
- The company aims to establish a stable supply chain for EV battery and other EV components in the U.S. market.
- HMGMA’s new smart factory is expected to begin commercial production in the first half of 2025.
- Hyundai Motor Group Executive Chair Euisun Chung stated that Hyundai and the people of Georgia share qualities including respect for histories, ingenuity, creativity, and determination to make the world better for the next generation.
- Euisun Chung noted that Hyundai's EVs are currently recognized as best in class.
- Euisun Chung expressed determination to be the global leader in electrification, safety, quality, and sustainability through this partnership.
- Euisun Chung added that with Metaplant America, Hyundai Motor Group will evolve beyond an automaker to become the world’s leading mobility solutions provider.
- Governor Brian Kemp stated that more Georgians are working than ever before with record jobs and investment coming to all parts of the state.
- Governor Kemp highlighted award-winning workforce development programs and infrastructure as drivers for Georgia's economy reaching new heights.
- Governor Kemp said the partnership with Hyundai Motor Group and the groundbreaking exemplify unprecedented success.
- Governor Kemp expressed a long-term commitment to improving lives and livelihoods, looking forward to seeing the facility's impact on the community and state.
