# Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2019-01-24

**Source:** [Textron Delivers Closed-Loop EFI Tech for E-Z-GO - The Golf Wire](https://thegolfwire.com/textron-delivers-closed-loop-efi-tech-for-e-z-go/)  ·  `page_051648`

## Facts

- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., a Textron Inc. (NYSE: TXT) company, introduces closed-loop electronic fuel injection technology on all E-Z-GO RXV and TXT golf car models and for Cushman’s Hauler, Refresher and Shuttle vehicles.
- Closed-loop EFI technology adjusts the fuel-to-air ratio in the engine to improve fuel economy, reduce engine service needs and lower emissions.
- Michael R. Parkhurst, Vice President, Golf & Turf North America for Textron Specialized Vehicles, stated that the new engine will run more efficiently, offer customers increased performance, lower costs of operation and a greener ecological footprint.
- Closed-loop EFI uses an oxygen sensor to read exhaust levels, enabling sensors in the new engine to adjust the fuel-to-air ratio for better performance in all weather conditions.
- The new EFI technology increases the number of operating hours between service intervals, resulting in less maintenance.
- Exclusive sound-deadening techniques on the E-Z-GO TXT include an extended air intake hose and sound-absorbing foam under the seat and rear-body access panels to reduce engine noise and create a more pleasing engine pitch.
- Closed-loop EFI is now available on all gas-powered E-Z-GO RXV and TXT vehicles, as well as Cushman Hauler, Refresher and Shuttle models.
- E-Z-GO was founded in 1954 in Augusta, Ga., became part of Textron Inc. in 1960, and today operates as part of Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc.
- Products sold under the E-Z-GO brand include RXV and TXT fleet golf cars, Freedom RXV and TXT personal golf cars, E-Z-GO Express personal utility vehicles, and the 2Five street-legal low-speed vehicle.
- E-Z-GO’s newest offerings include the ELiTE series of lithium-ion powered golf cars and PTVs, and the exclusive 72-volt AC electric powertrain found in its latest Express series vehicles.
- Cushman vehicles are designed and manufactured by Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc., with products ranging from Hauler utility vehicles to the Titan fully electric material-handling vehicle for indoor environments with a load capacity of 3,000 pounds, and the Minute Miser for transporting people and small loads.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles Inc. is a leading global manufacturer of golf cars, utility and personal transportation vehicles, snowmobiles, side-by-sides, all-terrain vehicles, professional turf-care equipment, and ground support equipment.
- Textron Specialized Vehicles markets products under the E-Z-GO, Cushman, Arctic Cat, Jacobsen, Ransomes, TUG, Douglas, Premier and Safeaero brands.
- Textron Inc. is a multi-industry company that leverages its global network of aircraft, defense, industrial and finance businesses to provide customers with innovative solutions and services.
