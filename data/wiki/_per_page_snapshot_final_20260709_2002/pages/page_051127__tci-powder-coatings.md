## Facts

- Ellaville, Ga.-based TCI Powder Coatings has announced the opening of a new office in Americus, Ga.
- The announcement was made on January 12, 2016.
- The new office functions as corporate headquarters for the business.
- The facility houses the administrative, customer service, finance, and information systems departments.
- The company plans to add laboratory and training facilities in the near future.
- The company plans to add sample product preparation and light warehousing in the near future.
- The facility begins with 20 employees.
- The facility is expected to accommodate up to 75 full-time workers.
