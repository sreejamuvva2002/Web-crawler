## Facts

- Novelis submitted a draft registration statement with the US Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) for its proposed initial public offering.

- Novelis is a leading sustainable aluminum solutions provider and the world leader in aluminum rolling and recycling.
- Novelis operates an integrated network of technically advanced rolling and recycling facilities across North America, South America, Europe and Asia.
