## Facts

- YKK AP America announces key leadership appointments to position the organization for future growth across North America.
- Mike Turner has been appointed as president of the company’s commercial business group, effective April 1.
- Josh Jensen has been appointed as president of the company’s residential business group, effective April 1.
- The commercial business group includes the YKK AP brand division and the company’s subsidiary Erie Architectural Products.
- Tim Thomas leads Erie Architectural Products as its president.
- Turner and Jensen will report to North American Group president and CEO Oliver Stepe.
- These appointments reflect continuous growth in the company’s commercial façade systems and residential windows and doors business.
- YKK AP is committed to continued investment in its manufacturing and sales operations in the State of Georgia and across the United States.
- Turner currently serves as senior vice president of sales and marketing and has worked for YKK AP for more than 30 years.
- Turner oversaw consolidated revenue growth and a substantial increase in business complexity since 2016 when he assumed leadership of sales and marketing.
- Josh Jensen joined the company in January 2024 and brings 25 years of experience in the windows and doors industry.
- Jensen has progressed from sales management up to general manager and president.
- Jensen is an accomplished strategic leader with comprehensive knowledge and experience across operations, sales, marketing, quality assurance, finance, procurement, and human resources.
- Oliver Stepe, president and CEO of YKK AP America, states that the appointments reflect a commitment to business growth and executive expertise.
- YKK AP is ramping up operations at its brand new, state-of-the-art $125 million residential windows and doors manufacturing facility in Macon, Georgia.
- Josh Jensen’s leadership of the residential business group will be instrumental as YKK AP works to achieve its vision to become a global leading company through the evolution of architectural products.
- YKK AP America Inc. is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
- All building products are manufactured in world-class production facilities located in Dublin, Ga., Macon, Ga., and Lakeshore, Ontario.
- Together with its subsidiary brand Erie Architectural Products, the company serves architects, glazing contractors, homebuilders, and resellers across the country.
- YKK AP America Inc. is a subsidiary of YKK AP Inc., a global architectural products company based in Tokyo, Japan.
