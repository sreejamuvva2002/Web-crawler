## Facts

- TN Americas Holdings, Inc. is located at 2200 Pendley Road, Cumming, Georgia, United States ZIP: 30041.
- The company specializes in the manufacture of precision and semi-precision balls to various industries including automotive, aerospace, anti-friction bearings, pumps, medical, pen, paints, oil, cosmetics, jewelry and furniture.
- TN Americas Holdings, Inc. supplies a complete range of materials and has the ability to manufacture metal and ceramic balls to Grade 3 specifications.
- Plastic can be manufactured to Grade 0 specification; using a different Grade scale than metals and ceramics.
- The company offers high precision, precision, semi-precision, imported, specialty, and medical ball products that represent the very best in the industry.
- The High-Precision Division offers superior performance through highly specialized materials supported by experienced engineers and knowledgeable customer service representatives.
- The Semi-Precision Division & Distribution Center supplies custom orders generally suited for more standard applications; in addition to, stocking a wide selection of ISO-3290 and/or ABMA parts for immediate delivery.
- TN Americas Holdings, Inc. balls are found in the majority of America's automotive, aircraft, marine, industrial, construction and scientific applications.
- The company manufactures balls in sizes from 17-thousandths of an inch diameter for use in ball point pens, to 4 1/2 inches in diameter for use in heavy duty military and industrial applications.
- Tolerances are held to within 25-millionths of an inch for bearing-quality lapped balls, and to within 3-millionths of absolute roundness for precision balls.
- Surface finish is less than one RMS.
- The production process combines operator skill with ongoing research and development program designed to infuse the manufacturing process with the latest scientific development and technology.
- In 2002, the company established THT (Tsubaki Hoover Taicang) to produce precision Chrome balls.
- In 2003, the company acquired Polish ball and roller manufacturer ZET to form THP (Tsubaki Hoover Poland).
- In 2006, the company acquired Chongqing Steel Ball Company located in Chongqing, China.
- In 2006, the Washington, Indiana Distribution Center was moved to Cumming, Georgia.
- In 2007, Connecticut and Tennessee facilities were consolidated and transferred to the Georgia facility.
- In 2010, the company established THI (Tsubaki Hoover India) to produce precision Chrome balls.
- In 2013, the company acquired Spheric Trafalgar STE and STT in England and Thailand.
- In 2013, Hoover celebrated 100 years of business.
- In 2015, there was an announcement of listing on Tokyo Stock Exchange.
- The company's parent company is located in Japan.
- The company has a Specialty Division (Plastics/Medical) located in Sault Ste. Marie, MI.
