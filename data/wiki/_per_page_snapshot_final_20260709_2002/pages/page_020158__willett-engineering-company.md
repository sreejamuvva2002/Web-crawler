# Willett Engineering Company

## Facts

- Willett Engineering Company is a structural design firm that has been in business for over 30 years.
- The company specializes in tilt-up concrete design, erection engineering for long-span structural steel framing systems, connection design for the steel fabrication industry, and preservation of historical structures.
- WEC serves markets including manufacturing, distribution, K-12 education, retail & restaurant, multi-family residential, office, healthcare, theater & film, government & civic, and prototypical design.
- The firm is located in the metro Atlanta area with a satellite office in Port St. Joe, Florida, and its engineers are licensed in 47 states and territories.
- Over 95% of WEC's business comes from repeat clients.
- WEC provided structural design for Fox Factory's 350,000-sf vertically integrated manufacturing and distribution facility in Gainesville, Georgia.
- The Fox Factory facility design includes a large office mezzanine, anodizer room, clean rooms, truck canopy, and fuel storage area.
- WEC conducted structural renovation and historic preservation on the 1928 Historic Ford Auditorium at Berry College in Rome, Georgia, creating 3D models of original roof trusses to install a new acoustic ceiling.
- The Historic Ford Auditorium renovation won the Architecture MasterPrize 2020 award in the Restoration & Renovation Category.
- WEC designed a new building for Midtown High School in Atlanta, Georgia, spanning 65,000 sf with a construction cost of $17 million.
- WEC completed a new building design and structural renovation for the Barack & Michelle Obama Academy in Atlanta, Georgia, including a 10,000 sf heavy renovation, 3,000 sf new media addition, 8,050 sf new gymnasium addition, and 400 sf new entrance addition at a construction cost of $11 million.
- Willett Engineering is currently hiring Structural Engineers at multiple experience levels in Tucker, Georgia (metro Atlanta area).
- Job qualifications require a BS in Civil Engineering with an emphasis in Structural Engineering from an ABET-accredited program and proficiency with Revit, Enercalc, and RISA software.
- Advanced level engineering positions prefer an SE license and extensive relevant design experience.
- The company offers competitive salary based on experience, comprehensive benefits, flexible PTO, professional development opportunities, and a collaborative work environment.
