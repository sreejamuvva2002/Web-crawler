# Braselton 85 Phase II

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [About - Braselton 85 Phase II](https://www.braselton85.com/about/)  ·  `page_007740`

## Facts

- Rockefeller Group recently completed two distribution buildings totaling 427,658 square feet on 39 acres in Braselton, Ga.
- Braselton 85 Phase II is located at Jesse Cronic Road and Highway 124 along Atlanta’s I-85 Northeast industrial corridor.
- The development includes two rear-load distribution buildings totaling 207,009 square feet and 220,649 square feet respectively.
- Both buildings feature 32′ clear ceiling heights and 52′ x 50′ column spacing.
- The site provides ample auto and trailer parking.
- Braselton’s location along I-85 offers a distribution channel to Atlanta’s population of more than 6.2 million people and all major Southeastern population centers.
- Rockefeller Group sold the 207,009 square foot building to Great Lakes Technologies (“GLT”), a WEN Company.
- GLT will utilize the new state-of-art facility for warehousing and distribution purposes.
- The acquisition marks an expansion of GLT in the Southeast region.
- This business park represents Rockefeller Group’s second development in Braselton.
- Rockefeller Group previously completed and sold Braselton 85 Phase I in 2021, a 222,618-square-foot distribution center located one mile from the new project.
- Braselton 85 Phase I was leased to Duckyang America Inc., a Korean automotive equipment supplier, before being sold to Cabot Properties.
