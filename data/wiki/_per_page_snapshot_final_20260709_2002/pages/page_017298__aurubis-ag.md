# Aurubis AG

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_recycling  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-20

**Source:** [Aurubis AG: Aurubis adopts € 530 million investment package to accelerate growth and sustainability | Corporate - EQS News](https://www.eqs-news.com/news/corporate/aurubis-ag-aurubis-adopts-e-530-million-investment-package-to-accelerate-growth-and-sustainability/30bc6b85-812f-4a23-ab19-a0bf29f3dad0_en)  ·  `page_017298`

## Facts

- The Supervisory Board of Aurubis AG approved an additional comprehensive investment package of around € 530 million to accelerate growth and sustainability.
- The investment package includes expansion to double the processing capacity of the Aurubis Richmond recycling plant in Georgia, USA.
- A second recycling module with an investment volume of around € 250 million is slated to be added to the Aurubis Richmond plant currently under construction in the US state of Georgia.
- An additional € 90 million has been approved for a capital expenditure adjustment for infrastructure requirements and inflation at the Georgia site.
- Production at the expanded Aurubis Richmond plant is scheduled to begin in 2026.
- The expansion will double the throughput volume from the previously planned 90,000 tons of complex recycling materials to 180,000 tons per year.
- The additional plant will be built in accordance with the highest environmental standards to allow for environmentally friendly recycling of input materials.
- Aurubis anticipates creating around 80 additional jobs in the region due to the expansion.
- Once both recycling modules are completed, Aurubis anticipates employing around 200 people at the Richmond site.
- Construction is already underway on the first plant at the Richmond site.
- Together with the planned expansion approved today, Aurubis anticipates total investments in the Georgia facility of € 640 million.
- Starting in 2025/26, Aurubis anticipates additional EBITDA totaling around € 90 million per year from the expansion phase approved today.
- The Richmond plant will profit from attractive local electricity and natural gas prices to positively impact cost efficiency.
- Aurubis plans to finance the projects approved today primarily out of current cash flow with no need for a capital increase.
- Recycling in the USA is growing by 5% every year, resulting in a rapid increase in the availability of complex recycling materials that support the expansion.
