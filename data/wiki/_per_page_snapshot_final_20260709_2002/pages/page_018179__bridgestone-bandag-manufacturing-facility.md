# Bridgestone Bandag Manufacturing Facility

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Bridgestone announces Bandag manufacturing plant expansion | Tire Technology International](https://www.tiretechnologyinternational.com/news/manufacturing-facilities/bridgestone-announces-bandag-manufacturing-plant-expansion.html)  ·  `page_018179`

## Facts

- Manufacturer Bridgestone is set to invest US$60m to expand the Bridgestone Bandag manufacturing facility in Texas.
- The investment will be used to expand the existing 200,000ft2 site by a further 50,000ft2.
- The expansion will immediately increase the site’s operational activities by running on six and seven-day production schedules.
- New mixing operations will also be constructed as part of the expansion plans.
- Upon scheduled completion of the mixing operations in January 2025, the plant’s output will be increased by an estimated 16%.
- The expansion at the Abilene plant addresses the growing demand for Bridgestone tread rubber products driven by the rapid growth of the company’s retread business.
- Since 1957, Bandag has kept an estimated 300 million tires out of the waste system, helping to reduce material emissions and water and energy usage needed for tire production.
- The company’s retreading process has saved four billion gallons of oil to date.
- Barry Owens, senior vice president of Bridgestone Americas Manufacturing Group, stated that the plant's growth is celebrated through new investment and physical expansion driven by growing product demand and the team's commitment to innovation and quality.
- Owens noted that the company and Abilene team play a vital role in the transformation of mobility by building greener retreads and maximizing fuel efficiency for fleets.
