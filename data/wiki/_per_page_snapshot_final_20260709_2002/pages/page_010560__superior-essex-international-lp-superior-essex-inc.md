# Superior Essex International LP / Superior Essex Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** charging_infrastructure  ·  **Published:** 2019-06-25

**Source:** [Superior Essex Communications Releases First-of-Its-Kind Hybrid Indoor/Outdoor Cable - Superior Essex – Corporate](https://www.superioressex.ms/news/superior-essex-communications-releases-first-of-its-kind-hybrid-indoor-outdoor-cable/)  ·  `page_010560`

## Facts

- Superior Essex Communications announced the release of the Hybrid I/O Riser 2x2 cable for indoor/outdoor applications on June 25, 2019.
- The Hybrid I/O Riser 2x2 is the first and only hybrid communication cable listed at Wet Locations UL; UL Direct Burial listed for interlock armor version, and UL listed for use in Class 3 and Class 2 circuits.
- The cable meets ICEA 696 for indoor and outdoor use, with dry water-blocking technology in the core and in simplex 2 mm.
- Both stranded conductors are protected with special PVC designed to meet UL 'wet location' test requirements and available in AWG 12, 14, and 16 conductor sizes.
- The I/O Riser 2x2 Hybrid can be used without armor for standard indoor applications or with interlock armor making it the only fiber cable also rated by UL for Direct Burial without conduit.
- Both products, F3C4 (unarmored) and F1C4 (armored), feature a UL sunlight-rated PVC jacket, annealed stranded copper conductors, wet location PVC insulation, buffer optical fiber, dielectric aramid strength member, dry water-blocking cord, and glass yarn.
- Product F3C4 has a diameter of 0.33 while product F1C4 adds interlock aluminum armor expanding it to a diameter of 0.64.
- Brian Ensign, Vice President of Manufacturing Demand and Sales for Superior Essex Communications, stated that this pioneering technology is an important step to expand PoE technology beyond the 100-meter limit.
- Brian Ensign noted there is a market gap to expand the use of fiber and hybrid cable technology by filling it with this product, opening many possibilities for what and where fiber products can be done and used.
- For Superior Essex Communications, this represents an exciting technology extension in the market, and they are happy to take a leadership position.
- The I/O Riser 2x2 Hybrid is another product in the PowerWise Fiber product line released by Superior Essex Communications.
- The PowerWise Fiber platform is cabling technology that enables delivering power and data through one cable, offering capabilities to optimize, customize, and connect any environment to meet unique business demands.
- Superior Essex is a leading manufacturer and supplier of communication cables and accessory products serving the communications industry and related distribution markets for over 80 years.
- Superior Essex is a trademark of Superior Essex International LP and other companies from Superior Essex Inc.
- Superior Essex corporate address is located at 5770 Powers Ferry Rd. NW Suite 400, Atlanta, GA 30327.
