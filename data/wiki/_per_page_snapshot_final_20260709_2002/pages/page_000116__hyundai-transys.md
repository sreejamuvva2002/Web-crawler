## Facts

- Quick Start is continuing its longtime partnership with HYUNDAI TRANSYS in a new location.
- HYUNDAI TRANSYS makes seating systems and powertrains.
- HYUNDAI TRANSYS is a major supplier of Kia Motors in West Point, Ga.
- HYUNDAI TRANSYS will be a core affiliate of Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America near Savannah, Ga.
- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America is slated to start production by early 2025.
- Quick Start has begun providing HYUNDAI TRANSYS with company-specific training to prepare for on-site work.
- Training topics include seat assembly processes on various Hyundai EV models, electrical motor controls, and a manufacturing process overview.
- HYUNDAI TRANSYS trainees are provided with safety and organizational development courses such as Lean, 5S, and workplace ethics.
- Quick Start is using its main Advanced Manufacturing Training Center in Pooler, Georgia, to provide the training.
- Two interim facilities are being used by Quick Start alongside the main center for training purposes.
- The new Hyundai Mobility Training Center of Georgia is under construction adjacent to the Metaplant.
- Eddie Mabe serves as the Senior Manager of Human Resources and Administration at HYUNDAI TRANSYS in West Point, Georgia.
