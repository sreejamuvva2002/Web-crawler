## Facts

- Seoyon E-HWA announced it will invest $76 million in a new plant near Savannah, Ga.
- The facility will manufacture interior and exterior parts for Hyundai Motor Group, including door trim, headlining, and tailgate trim.
- Parts will be supplied to Hyundai's upcoming in-plant located in nearby Ellabell.
- Seoyon E-HWA plans to hire more than 500 workers for the new facility.
- The company plans to begin production at the plant in October 2024.
