# Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-01-04

**Source:** [PANASONIC AUTOMOTIVE POWERS FIRST UL-CERTIFIED EBIKE WITH TOTEM USA, BLENDING USER-FRIENDLY DESIGN AND SAFETY - Jan 4, 2022](https://ces.vporoom.com/2022-01-04-PANASONIC-AUTOMOTIVE-POWERS-FIRST-UL-CERTIFIED-EBIKE-WITH-TOTEM-USA,-BLENDING-USER-FRIENDLY-DESIGN-AND-SAFETY)  ·  `page_047842`

## Facts

- At CES 2022 on Jan. 4, 2022, Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America announced a collaboration with Totem USA to bring Totem's first UL-certified eBike, "Zen Rider," to the U.S. market.
- In 2019, Panasonic received the industry's first e-Bike safety standard certification (UL2849) from UL, a not-for-profit safety standards development organization and leading safety science company.
- The Totem USA eBike "Zen Rider" is powered by Panasonic and provides consumers with an affordable, family-friendly option to meet growing demands of the eBike market.
- Research from NPD indicated a growth rate for electric bicycles of 240% in the 12 months leading up to July 2021, with sustained interest expected after pandemic spending on cycling and sporting equipment.
- Derek Arciniaga, senior manager of business development at Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America, stated they are proud to announce Totem USA's first eBike with a UL-certified Panasonic system to expand mobility transformation in the United States.
- The Zen Rider features a low step-through mounting design and a motor/battery system especially suited for urban areas.
- This marks Panasonic's second UL 2849 certified motor & battery system.
- Totem USA, in collaboration with Panasonic, is prepared to design, develop, and produce custom eBike offerings for companies interested in UL-certified eBikes.
- The Panasonic drive system is suitable for two-wheel and three-wheel city, touring, or cargo eBike variants.
- Panasonic Automotive Systems Company of America is a division company of Panasonic Corporation of North America and a leading global supplier of automotive infotainment and connectivity system solutions.
- Panasonic Automotive is headquartered in Peachtree City, Georgia, with sales, marketing, and engineering operations in Farmington Hills, Michigan.
- Panasonic Corporation of North America is based in Newark, New Jersey, and serves as the principal North American subsidiary of Osaka, Japan-based Panasonic Corporation.
- Panasonic was named one of Interbrand's Top 100 Best Global Brands of 2021.
- Totem USA is located in McKinney, Texas, and operates as a division of the Golden Wheel Group, which is the fourth largest manufacturer in the world of bicycles and eBikes.
