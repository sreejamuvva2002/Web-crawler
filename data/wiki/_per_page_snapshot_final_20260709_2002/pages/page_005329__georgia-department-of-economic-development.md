# Georgia Department of Economic Development

## Facts

- The expanding electric vehicle industry in Georgia represented almost a third of both job creation and capital investment in 2023.
- A total of 26,474 jobs were created in Georgia in 2023.
- A total of $18.2 billion in capital investment was recorded in Georgia in 2023.
- Seven automotive industry projects were announced over the last three months of 2023, stretching from the coastal region to northwest Georgia.
- A total of 4,990 jobs were created in Georgia during the fourth quarter of 2023.
- A total of $3.4 billion in capital investment was recorded in Georgia during the fourth quarter of 2023.
- A wave of automotive parts manufacturers located across the state in support of the electric vehicle industry in 2023.
- Hyundai Industrial established a new facility in Coweta County.
- Hwashin established automotive parts manufacturing operations in Dublin.
- New automotive manufacturing facilities are located surrounding the Hyundai megasite in coastal Georgia.
- Daechang Seat Corp. is creating 500 jobs in Savannah for automotive seat frames.
- Hwashin is creating 460 jobs in Dublin for automotive chassis and body parts manufacturing.
- Toyota Industries is creating 255 jobs in Pendergrass for automotive parts manufacturing.
- Club Car is creating 150 jobs in Appling for compact, street-legal electric vehicle manufacturing.
- Total jobs and capital investment estimates are derived from project announcements made in partnership with the State of Georgia Department of Economic Development.
- These estimates may not include all projects and are subject to change as yearly totals continue to be reported by businesses.
