## Facts

- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) has been headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, since 1998.
- PCNA operates a Porsche Experience Center in Atlanta that features a module-based 1.6-mile driver development track, a business center, and the fine dining restaurant 356.
- PCNA supports 193 independently owned and operated Porsche dealerships in the U.S., supplying them with parts, service, marketing, and training.
- PCNA is the exclusive U.S. importer of the Porsche Taycan electric vehicle, along with the 911, 718 Boxster, 718 Cayman, Macan, Cayenne, and Panamera models.
- PCNA is an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Porsche AG, which is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany.
- Goodr is an Atlanta-based social impact startup that collaborates with PCNA on hunger relief and food waste reduction initiatives.
- PCNA previously funded eight pop-up grocery distributions by Goodr that collectively distributed 77,880 meals.
- PCNA operates a second Porsche Experience Center near Los Angeles, comprising a 53-acre complex with an 4.1-mile driver development track across eight educational modules, a business center, and Restaurant 917.
- The company's dealership network works to provide customers with a best-in-class experience aligned with the Porsche brand's leadership in vehicle performance, safety, and efficiency.
- Porsche has a motorsport heritage boasting over 30,000 wins to date.
