# Constellium N.V.

## Facts

- Constellium N.V. has announced the grand opening of its new plant in White, Georgia.
- The White, Georgia plant is dedicated to the production of advanced aluminium automotive structural components and crash management systems.
- The facility spans 135,000 sq. ft.
- The facility is strategically located to supply automakers in the Southeast U.S.
- The facility may be expanded to 220,000 sq. ft. to meet customers’ supply needs in the future.
- Constellium expects to have 150 employees in White, Georgia, by 2019.
- Paul Warton, President of Constellium’s Automotive Structures & Industry business unit, stated he is pleased to celebrate the opening of the new plant and its state-of-the-art manufacturing capability.
- The plant's team is committed to work in partnership with automotive customers.
- Being closer to customer assembly plants will allow Constellium to better serve automakers in their mission to make vehicles lighter and safer.
- The plant responds to the industry’s growing demand for aluminium structural parts.
- Eric Krepps, Vice President and General Manager of Constellium Automotive Structures North America, extended gratitude to the State of Georgia, Bartow County, local partners, and employees for their strong support in establishing the White, Georgia plant.
- Constellium is honoured to be part of the White, Georgia community.
