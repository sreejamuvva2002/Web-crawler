# YKK AP

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- The 400,000-square-foot new residential window and door manufacturing facility serves as an advanced, model plant for YKK’s AP’s North American operations.
- YKK AP was one of the first Japanese companies to establish operations in the state of Georgia.
- The new plant is located on over 50 acres.
- The facility expanded YKK AP’s residential manufacturing business with the addition of new machinery and technologies, advanced sustainable manufacturing processes, and additional capacity to meet market demand for single and multi-family residential window products.
- JE Dunn worked closely with SSOE on this highly collaborative project to deliver the new plant.
- The project includes 12,000 square feet of office space.
- The project includes all required campus infrastructure, roads, parking, truck docks, and other site requirements.
- YKK AP’s new plant added nearly 100 new jobs in Macon.
- YKK AP’s residential products business has been headquartered in Macon since it was established.
