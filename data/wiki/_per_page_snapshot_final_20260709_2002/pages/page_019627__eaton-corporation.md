# Eaton Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Eaton Corporation operates a supercharger manufacturing facility located at 695 Indian Hills RoadAthens, GA 30601-3513, United States.
- The Athens facility occupies 115,000 square feet of space and employs 70 people.
- Eaton Corporation is headquartered in Cleveland, Ohio.
- The company manufactures superchargers and supercharger components for passenger cars, aftermarket applications, and jet skis.
- Product customers include manufacturers such as GM, Ford, Jaguar, Kawasaki, and John Deere.
- Eaton developed the Roots style supercharger in 1949.
- The company has produced over 5 million superchargers since 1988.
- Eaton partners with local organizations including Athens Tech and the University of Georgia (UGA), as well as various non-profits.
- Finished bolt-on products manufactured at the facility increase power and fuel efficiency to passenger cars.
- The facility features state-of-the-art automated assembly lines, machining cells, an assembly process, and unit testing capabilities.
