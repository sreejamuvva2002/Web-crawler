## Facts

- RACEMARK was founded in 1964 by Bob Bailey, a Porsche of America team world-class race car driver.
- Mark Donohue partnered with Bob Bailey and joined RACEMARK in 1972.
- The company's mission is to create innovative and sustainable automotive floor products that protect and enhance car interiors.
- RACEMARK's vision is to be the global leader in sustainable automotive floor protection and design by reducing environmental impact, innovating products and processes, and expanding into new markets.
- Core company values include Respect, Integrity, Initiative, Creativity, Leadership, and Sustainability.
- In 1973, RACEMARK became the first company in its industry to create a custom-fit carpet car mat.
- The company is recognized as the reference point for innovative and stylish car mats in the automotive market.
- High material standards, competitive manufacturing processes, and dedication to quality result in optimized product life cycle cost.
- Capabilities include high volume production as well as premium and luxury designs for customized products supporting a wide range of vehicle segments.
- RACEMARK International includes manufacturing plants located in the United States, Germany, Hungary, and Switzerland.
- The company maintains a strong presence in North America and Europe while following international market trends of the highest quality brands in the automotive and textile industries.
- RACEMARK delivers features and product enhancements to achieve “best in class” parts for the global automotive industry.
- The company has expanded globally to supply just-in-time delivery to customer assembly plants, enabling the shortest lead-times possible at a very competitive price.
- Manufacturing plants are dedicated to producing automobile floor mats for premier brands in the automotive industry.
- RACEMARK products are installed in the most luxurious automobiles in the world.
- In 1970, founder Bob Bailey placed 2nd in Class racing for the Porsche Team of America at the Sebring 12-Hour Grand Prix alongside Mario Andretti and Steve McQueen.
