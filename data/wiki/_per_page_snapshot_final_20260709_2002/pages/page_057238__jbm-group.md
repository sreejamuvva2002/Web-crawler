# JBM Group

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-07

**Source:** [JBM acquires majority stake in German auto firm Linde-Wiemann](https://www.livemint.com/companies/news/jbm-acquires-majority-stake-in-german-auto-firm-linde-wiemann-1551121650776.html)  ·  `page_057238`

## Facts

- Listed auto-components maker JBM Group has acquired a majority stake in Linde-Wiemann, a German manufacturer and supplier of structural components and assemblies to automobile companies globally.
- JBM Group announced the acquisition via a stock exchange filing on Monday.
- The transaction was financed by ICICI Bank.
- Financial details of the deal were not disclosed.
- Linde-Wiemann specializes in hot forming, tubular assemblies, and the use of alternative materials such as aluminium to create lightweight auto systems.
- Linde-Wiemann operates 17 manufacturing plants across Germany, the US, China, Spain, Turkey, Czech Republic, Hungary, and South Africa.
- Linde-Wiemann employs more than 2,300 people.
- The acquisition will help JBM access new products, increase its number of global customers, and add to its competitiveness in India.
- JBM will benefit from Linde-Wiemann’s strengths in electric vehicle components and battery structure and systems.
- Linde-Wiemann’s clients include Volkswagen, Audi, Skoda, Porsche, Daimler, and Ford.
- Nishant Arya, executive director of JBM Group, stated that the combined entity will focus on developing new products to improve vehicle safety and performance through light-weighting, increasing product quality and minimizing time to market for OEMs.
- Arya noted the acquisition opens opportunities in technology, highlighting Linde-Wiemann's strength in alternate technology and work on global platforms for Volkswagen, General Motors, PSA, and Ford.
- Arya claimed the deal will make JBM an automatic choice for those OEMs.
- Linde-Wiemann possesses EV components and battery technology that JBM hopes to utilize in its buses.
- JBM manufactures safety-critical items including chassis and suspension systems, axles, twist beams, exhaust systems, air tanks, and fuel tanks for passenger cars, two-wheelers, three-wheelers, commercial vehicles, and farm and construction equipment.
