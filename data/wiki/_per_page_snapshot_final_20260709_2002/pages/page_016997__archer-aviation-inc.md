# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-07

**Source:** [Archer Aviation Completes Construction of High-Volume Manufacturing Facility - Green Stock News](https://greenstocknews.com/news/nyse/achr/archer-completes-construction-of-high-volume-manufacturing-facility)  ·  `page_016997`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. announced on Dec 19, 2024, that it received the certificate of occupancy for its high-volume manufacturing facility, ARC, earlier this week.
- ARC is connected to the Covington Municipal Airport in Georgia (Newton County).
- Archer is working to complete the tooling load-in for the initial manufacturing line at ARC.
- Production at ARC is planned to begin in early 2025, with a goal of ramping to two aircraft per month by the end of 2025.
- Archer built ARC in close partnership with Stellantis to mass-produce Archer’s Midnight aircraft.
- Stellantis is contributing capital, advanced manufacturing technology and expertise, and experienced personnel to scale the facility to 650 aircraft annually by 2030.
- ARC will serve as the production site for Archer's all-electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft, Midnight.
- The Midnight aircraft is designed to offer passengers a sustainable, low-noise, and safe alternative to ground transportation in congested cities around the world.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp stated that Archer’s choice to build the facility in Newton County marks the start of an incredible relationship and reaffirms Georgia's commitment to creating quality jobs and opportunities for years to come.
- Congressman Mike Collins of Georgia, who sits on the House Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure and Committee on Science, Space, and Technology, stated that the completion highlights Georgia’s leadership in innovation, advanced manufacturing, and job creation, promising economic benefits for the state.
- Archer founder and CEO Adam Goldstein noted that with construction complete, the team is focused on production execution and scaling, and expressed pride that the project was completed fast and on budget.
- Stellantis Chief Manufacturing and Supply Chain Officer Arnaud Deboeuf stated that supporting Archer’s electric aircraft growth with deep manufacturing expertise is a significant step in taking clean mobility to the sky.
- Archer Aviation Inc. designs and develops key enabling technologies and aircraft necessary to power the future of aviation.
- Stellantis N.V. is executing its Dare Forward 2030 strategic plan, aiming to become a carbon net zero mobility tech company by 2038 while creating added value for all stakeholders.
