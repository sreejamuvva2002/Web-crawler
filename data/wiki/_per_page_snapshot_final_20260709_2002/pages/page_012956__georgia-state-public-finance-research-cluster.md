# Georgia State Public Finance Research Cluster

**Type:** research_center  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2026-01-16

**Source:** [PFRC Research Team Briefs Georgia Lawmakers on Tax Incentive Evaluations - Georgia State Public Finance Research Cluster](https://pfrc.gsu.edu/2026/01/16/pfrc-research-team-briefs-georgia-lawmakers-on-tax-incentive-evaluations/)  ·  `page_012956`

## Facts

- The Public Finance Research Cluster’s Fiscal Research Center (FRC) visited the Georgia State Capitol to brief legislators and staff on tax incentive evaluations completed in 2025.
- FRC answered questions from committee members, legislative staff, and other stakeholders as the 2026 session gets underway.
- Six evaluations were completed under contract with the Georgia Department of Audits and Accounts (DOAA).
- The evaluations reflect Georgia’s ongoing tax incentive evaluation process, which gives lawmakers and the public independent analysis of whether selected credits, deductions, and exclusions are meeting their intended goals.
- The evaluations include estimates of fiscal impact and other outcomes for state tax preferences.
- The 2025 evaluations were requested by the Senate Finance Committee and the House Ways and Means Committee under the state’s tax preference evaluation framework.
