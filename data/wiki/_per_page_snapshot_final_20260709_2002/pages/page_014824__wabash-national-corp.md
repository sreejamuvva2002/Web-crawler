# Wabash National Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2014-07-29

**Source:** [Wabash’s Second-Quarter Income Rises - TT](https://www.ttnews.com/articles/wabashs-second-quarter-income-rises)  ·  `page_014824`

## Facts

- Trailer maker Wabash National Corp. reported its second-quarter net income increased as trailer demand exceeded expectations.
- Net income rose to $16.2 million, or 23 cents per share, from $14.1 million, or 20 cents, a year ago.
- Revenue rose 18% to $486 million.
- The number of trailers shipped rose to about 14,950, exceeding the company’s previous guidance of 13,500 to 14,500.
- CEO Dick Giromini stated they are pleased with the results for the second quarter and the continued strong performance of the business, particularly in the commercial-trailer products segment.
- Wabash expects to ship 53,000 to 55,000 trailers this year, representing a 13% to 18% increase from the prior year.
