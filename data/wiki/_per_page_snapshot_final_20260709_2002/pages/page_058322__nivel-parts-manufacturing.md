# Nivel Parts & Manufacturing

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-05-21

**Source:** [Capital Partners Completes Investment in Nivel | Morgan Stanley | Morgan Stanley](https://www.morganstanley.com/im/en-gb/institutional-investor/insights/press-release/ms-capital-partners-completes-investment-nivel-parts.html)  ·  `page_058322`

## Facts

- Investment funds managed by Morgan Stanley Capital Partners (“MSCP”) have completed an investment in Nivel Parts & Manufacturing (“Nivel”).
- Nivel was sold by funds managed by Kelso & Company.
- MSCP is partnering with the current management team led by Brent Moore, who will remain as CEO.
- Nivel is headquartered in Jacksonville, FL.
- Nivel is the leading manufacturer and supplier of branded aftermarket parts for personal transportation vehicles, utility terrain vehicles and heavy-duty equipment.
- The Company offers over 100,000 SKUs in accessories and maintenance parts across the full vehicle lifecycle to a diversified customer base including dealers, distributors and consumers.
- Aaron Sack serves as Managing Director and Head of Morgan Stanley Capital Partners.
- Aaron Sack stated: “We are delighted to partner with Nivel and its talented management team led by Brent Moore. The Company has differentiated itself through continued product innovation across vehicle categories and by providing best-in-class customer service to its highly passionate customer base. We look forward to working with Brent and the team to further scale the company through organic growth and strategic acquisitions.”
- Nivel is MSCP’s most recent investment following AWT Labels & Packaging in December 2020 and US HealthConnect in October 2020.
- The investment represents a continuation of MSCP’s focus on high-growth, consumer enthusiast companies with strong competitive advantages.
- Brent Moore stated: “We could not be more excited to be working with Morgan Stanley Capital Partners as we enter this exciting new phase. Our team has always been uniquely focused on delivering a seamless experience and comprehensive product array to our customers. With MSCP’s deep expertise in advancing client-centric companies on their growth trajectories, we will be well-equipped to expand our current capabilities and products while continuing to provide the highest quality service to our customers.”
- Jones Day acted as legal advisor to MSCP.
- Morgan Stanley Capital Partners focuses on privately negotiated equity and equity-related investments primarily in North America across business services, consumer, healthcare and industrials markets.
- Morgan Stanley Investment Management has more than 731 investment professionals around the world and $1.4 trillion in assets under management or supervision as of March 31, 2021.
- Nivel was founded in 1968.
- Nivel is a global manufacturer, distributor, and seller of aftermarket parts and accessories for niche vehicles and heavy-duty equipment.
- Nivel owns the most valuable brands in the aftermarket niche vehicle parts and accessories categories: MadJax, Red Dot, GTW, Jake's, Reliance, Seizmik and High Lifter.
