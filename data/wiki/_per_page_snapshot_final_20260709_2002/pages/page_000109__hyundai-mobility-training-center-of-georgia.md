# Hyundai Mobility Training Center of Georgia

**Type:** workforce_training  ·  **Role:** workforce_training  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- When Hyundai Motor Group was deciding where to locate their visionary new Metaplant, they found much to like at the Bryan County site.
- The State of Georgia committed resources to Quick Start to build and operate a training center dedicated to future mobility as an incentive that helped seal the deal for the Metaplant location.
- A custom-designed training center was created as a complex of spaces and technologies derived from detailed studies of the industry’s needs.
- Countless sessions with subject-matter experts, detailed research into the fabrication and assembly of electric vehicles, and a design vision that could create inspirational spaces were all needed to come up with the final building.
- The Hyundai Mobility Training Center of Georgia is currently under construction.
- The Hyundai Mobility Training Center of Georgia will open in 2025.
- The training center will be operated by Georgia Quick Start.
- Atlanta is listed as a location associated with the project.
- The center will provide customized training for HMGMA Meta Pros as well as team members at affiliate suppliers located on the same site as the Hyundai Metaplant.
- The initiative aims to ensure quality standards throughout the supply chain.
- Savannah Technical College is listed as a partner or resource for the project.
- Ogeechee Technical College is listed as a partner or resource for the project.
- Oconee Fall Line Technical College is listed as a partner or resource for the project.
