# Volvo Car USA and Canada

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-01-08

**Source:** [Volvo Car USA and Canada Reports Sales of 138,647 in 2024](https://autoscommunity.com/2025/01/08/volvo-car-usa-and-canada-reports-sales-of-138647-in-2024-with-strong-electrified-results/)  ·  `page_014190`

## Facts

- Volvo Car USA and Canada reported total sales of 138,647 cars in 2024, down 2.1 percent from the previous year.
- Electrified models, defined as those with a fully electric or plug-in hybrid powertrain, accounted for 35.4 percent of Volvo's total sales in 2024, up 22.3 percent from last year.
- In December, Volvo Car USA and Canada sold 15,131 cars, up 2.1 percent from the previous year.
- Electrified models represented 35.7 percent of Volvo's December sales, an increase of 44.5 percent compared to the previous year.
- US car sales for Volvo reached 125,243 for the entire year of 2024, down 2.7 percent from the previous year.
- Electrified models accounted for 34.3 percent of Volvo's total US sales in 2024, up 20.3 percent from last year.
- December sales in the US reached 14,077 cars, up 3.4 percent over the same period last year.
- Sales of electrified models represented 33.9 percent of Volvo's US December sales, an increase of 47.6 percent compared to the previous year.
- In 2024, Canada set a new annual sales record for Volvo with 13,404 cars sold, up 4.2 percent compared with the previous record-setting year.
- Sales of electrified models accounted for 46.1 percent of Volvo's Canadian sales during 2024, up 38.2 percent compared to last year.
- December sales in Canada were down 12.7 percent from the previous year, with a volume of 1,054 cars.
- Sales of electrified models accounted for 59.6 percent of Volvo's Canadian December sales, an increase of 24.1 percent compared to the previous year.
- In the US, the XC60 compact luxury SUV was Volvo's top-selling model, with a record-setting 5,245 cars sold in December.
- The XC90 midsize luxury SUV followed as the second best-selling model in the US in December, with 5,006 vehicles sold.
- The XC40 small luxury SUV was the third best-selling model in the US in December, with 2,591 cars sold.
- Mike Cottone, President of Volvo Car USA and Canada, stated that demand from customers for their cars continued through the end of the year, especially for electrified models.
- Mike Cottone thanked retail partners, loyal customers, and valued employees for their commitment to Volvo Cars as the company continues to build momentum in the new year.
