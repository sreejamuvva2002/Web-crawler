# Suzuki Manufacturing of American Corp.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2002-05-31

## Facts

- Suzuki Motor Corp. (SMC) becomes a bonafide "made-in-America" manufacturer with the opening of a 108,350-square-foot Suzuki Manufacturing of American Corp. (SMAC) ATV manufacturing facility in Rome.
- The SMAC facility is located approximately 60 miles northwest of Atlanta.
- The plant opened on May 31, 2002.
