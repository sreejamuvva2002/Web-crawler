## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose is a global leader in the advanced filtration industry that develops sustainable filtration solutions for a cleaner, healthier world.
- Josh Ayer serves as the CEO of Hollingsworth & Vose and joined the company in 2009 as Vice President and Managing Director for the Asia Pacific Region before becoming President and Managing Director of the Americas region, followed by President and Chief Operating Officer.
- The company has expanded its manufacturing and research development into facilities in America, China, Europe, and India since its origins in 1728.
- Hollingsworth & Vose operates 13 worldwide research and development and manufacturing facilities to partner economically and efficiently with customers.
- During the COVID-19 pandemic, the company provided filtration media for respirators, ventilators, medical wear, and COVID-19 testing materials.
- The company's battery separators supported medical equipment and backup power sources for overtaxed emergency vehicles, hospitals, and other healthcare facilities during the pandemic.
- Cabin air filtration will play a huge part in the transportation sector as the industry moves toward more energy-efficient vehicles, such as hybrids and electric vehicles (EVs).
- Hollingsworth & Vose is a family-run company that maintains a mission of creating a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable world through sustainable filtration media.
