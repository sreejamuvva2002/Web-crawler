# Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire released its first annual sustainability report and announced CO2 reduction goals tied to its Vision 2030 initiative.
- The sustainability report will be published annually going forward to track progress toward "Vision 2030: A sustainable future, our driving force."
- The report covers results and targets for avoiding landfill waste at production facilities, reducing energy consumption, and achieving renewable energy goals.
- Vision 2030 is built on three core values: breakthrough innovation, eco-friendly production, and agile digitalization.
- CEO Daniel Choi stated that the report will help the company achieve its publicly set sustainability goals.
- CEO Daniel Choi said: "The level of transparency in this report reflects the commitment we made when announcing Vision 2030 last April."
- Austin Robinson, Sustainability Project Manager at Essex Furukawa, noted that consistent communication efforts will enable internal and external transparency.
- Austin Robinson stated: "On the path to a sustainable future, we must consistently analyze our data, find additional solutions, and take a leading role in sharing results."
- The Vision 2030 journey serves as a benchmark and course-setting for the future, with the first report acting only as an initial milestone.
- Essex Furukawa Magnet Wire LLC is the leading global provider of magnet wire (Lackdraht).
- The company's magnet wire is used by most major OEMs, Tier-1 suppliers, and industry leaders in automotive, energy, industrial, commercial, and residential construction markets.
- The Atlanta-based company operates as a global joint venture founded in 2020 between Essex Magnet Wire and Furukawa Electric Co., Ltd.
- Both parent companies are recognized leaders in developing magnet wire products and custom solutions.
- The combined entity leverages over two centuries of shared experience and expertise to drive innovation and manufacturing quality.
- Vision 2030 will guide the company's next decade through new innovations, developments, and process refinements.
