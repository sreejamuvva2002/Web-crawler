## Facts

- GEDIA North America operates in the Motor Vehicle Manufacturing sector in Georgia, United States.
- The company has between 51-200 employees.
- The GEDIA Automotive group develops and manufactures car body press parts and welding components for the automotive industry.
- The group also manufactures chassis components.
- GEDIA operates eight production plants located in Germany, China, Hungary, India, Mexico, Poland, Spain, and the USA.
- The company maintains joint ventures and cooperations in the USA, Mexico, and China.
- GEDIA serves as an associate partner of research and development companies.
- GEDIA currently has more than 4,200 employees worldwide.
- About 900 GEDIA employees work at the headquarters in Attendorn.
