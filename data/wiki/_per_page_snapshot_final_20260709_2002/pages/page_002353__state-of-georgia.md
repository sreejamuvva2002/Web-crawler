## Facts

- Georgia stands out as the nation’s premier business destination.
- The state is home to 16 Fortune 500 company headquarters.
- Proven workforce programs like Georgia Quick Start and a robust, 88-campus technical college system bolster local industries.
- The world’s busiest and most efficient airport, fastest-growing port, and largest rail network in the Southeast are proof that Georgia’s infrastructure excels across air, sea, and rail.
- Georgia’s government promotes favorable environments for businesses to thrive, including reducing the corporate tax rate to 4.99%.
- From the mountains to the coast, 159 counties present unique advantages and vibrant communities ripe with opportunity, each offering access to qualified labor, workforce development resources, and logistical advantages.
