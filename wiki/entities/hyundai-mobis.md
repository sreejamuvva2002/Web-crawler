# Hyundai Mobis

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[power-electronics|Power Electronics]]  ·  **Location:** Belfast Commerce Park, Richmond Hill, Georgia, Bryan County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Hyundai Mobis, one of the world’s largest automotive suppliers, announced a $926 million investment to build an EV Power Electric system plant in Bryan County, Georgia, creating at least 1,500 jobs and expanding its footprint in the state’s electric-vehicle supply chain.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Manufactures EV Power Electric systems and Integrated Charging Control Units for Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America and other regional factories, supporting Georgia’s EV supply chain.
