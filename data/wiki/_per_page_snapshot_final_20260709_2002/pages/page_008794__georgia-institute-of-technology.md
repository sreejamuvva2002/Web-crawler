# Georgia Institute of Technology

**Type:** research_center  ·  **Role:** research_and_development  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-06

**Source:** [The Crest of the Wave: Advanced and Additive Manufacturing - Expansion Solutions](https://www.expansionsolutionsmagazine.com/the-crest-of-the-wave-advanced-and-additive-manufacturing/)  ·  `page_008794`

## Facts

- Georgia Tech will oversee nine projects within the larger Georgia AI Manufacturing (GA-AIM) technology corridor project.
- The GA-AIM project aims to transform the Advanced Manufacturing Pilot Facility (AMPF) into the Artificial Intelligence Manufacturing Pilot Facility (AI-MPF).
- Georgia Tech received a new $65 million grant from the U.S. Department of Commerce’s Economic Development Administration for the initiative.
- The 24,000-square-foot AI-MPF facility will serve as a flagship component of the Georgia Tech Manufacturing Institute (GTMI).
- The facility will serve as a testbed where basic research results are scaled up and translated into implementable technologies, coupled with education, and workforce training.
- Targeted fields of interest for the project include additive/hybrid manufacturing, composites, digital manufacturing/Industry 4.0, and industrial robotics.
