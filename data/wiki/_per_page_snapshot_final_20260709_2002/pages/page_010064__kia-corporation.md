# Kia Corporation

**Source:** [Kia to Build Sportage at Metaplant, Targeting 550,000 US Output by 2030 - Seoul Economic Daily](https://en.sedaily.com/finance/2026/06/03/kia-to-expand-us-production-to-550000-units-by-2030-with)  ·  `page_010064`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group is strengthening its push into the US market by adding Kia's (000270.KS) Sportage to production at its American flagship plant, Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA).
- Kia plans to expand its annual US production to 550,000 units by 2030.
- Kia held a ceremony on Tuesday local time at the Metaplant in Ellabell, Georgia, to mark the start of Sportage Hybrid production.
- HMGMA is a plant Hyundai Motor (005380.KS) Group completed in March last year to target the US market.
- The Metaplant had been producing Hyundai electric vehicles such as the Ioniq 5 and Ioniq 9 before this expansion.
- This marks the first time a Kia hybrid model has been added to HMGMA's production mix.
- After the Donald Trump administration scaled back EV support, HMGMA's output fell sharply.
- The group broadened the plant's product mix with a hybrid model in response to reduced EV support.
- HMGMA's utilization rate dropped from 65.3% at the end of last year to 38.2% in the first quarter of this year.
- Hyundai Motor plans to lift productivity by raising the level of automation at the HMGMA plant.
- At the ceremony, Hyundai showcased its automation capabilities by using an autonomous mobile robot (AMR) to move the first Sportage produced at the plant onto the stage.
- HMGMA expands Kia's US manufacturing footprint beyond its existing Georgia plant.
- Through the two plants, Kia plans to raise its local production capacity to 550,000 units by 2030.
- The planned capacity represents more than 57% above the current level of about 350,000 units a year.
- Yoon Seung-kyu, president of Kia's North American Region and Kia America, stated that the Metaplant is Kia's second major investment in Georgia and that they highly value Georgia's continued growth potential as a hub of the auto industry.
- Yoon Seung-kyu noted that the Sportage Hybrid, whose enhanced model was unveiled last year, is Kia's best-selling model and expects production at HMGMA to further strengthen Kia's growth in the United States.
