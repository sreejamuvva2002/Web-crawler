# Woodbridge Corp – Atlanta Foam

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-29

**Source:** [UAW Members at Woodbridge Corp Walk Out on Strike - UAW | United Automobile, Aerospace and Agricultural Implement Workers of America](https://uaw.org/uaw-members-at-woodbridge-corp-walk-out-on-strike/)  ·  `page_015249`

## Facts

- UAW-represented Woodbridge Corp – Atlanta Foam workers of Local 472 walked out on strike early Wednesday morning on October 30 in Lithonia, Ga.
- Management has yet to make a significant offer on three critical issues for workers: wages, benefits, and seniority.
- Workers at Woodbridge produce seating and dashboard components for Yamaha and Nissan.
- Workers have been working under an expired contract since September 30.
- On August 22, workers voted 98% in favor of authorizing a strike.
- UAW Local 472 President Rachel Johnson stated she is on strike for better wages, healthcare, and respect for the work she does.
- UAW Region 8 Director Tim Smith stated that despite numerous meetings and bargaining sessions, management has yet to make a significant offer on three critical issues for workers: wages, benefits, and seniority, and that the company has no intention of reaching a fair and equitable agreement.
- Woodbridge workers are the latest UAW members to Stand Up during contract negotiations following the historic Stand Up strike that led autoworkers at the Big Three to win record raises and benefits.
