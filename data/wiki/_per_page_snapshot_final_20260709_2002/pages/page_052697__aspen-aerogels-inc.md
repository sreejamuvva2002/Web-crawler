## Facts

- Aspen Aerogels, Inc. develops and manufactures high-performance aerogel insulation materials and custom engineered solutions.
- The company was founded in 2001 as a spin-out from Department of Energy research.
- Aspen Aerogels pursued an initial public offering on the NYSE in 2014 under the ticker ASPN.
- The company combines proprietary aerogel formulations with advanced manufacturing processes to deliver products known for low thermal conductivity, lightweight construction, and robust mechanical properties.
- The product portfolio spans blanket insulation, boards, and custom shapes built around proprietary brands including Pyrogel, Cryogel, and Spaceloft.
- Pyrogel and Cryogel insulation blankets are widely used in oil & gas and petrochemical applications to minimize heat loss and improve operational efficiency.
- Spaceloft aerogel products target oilfield tubulars, subsea pipelines, and lightweight structural insulation.
- Business segments benefiting from Aspen’s thermal management offerings include power generation, chemical processing, and building materials.
- The company leverages its aerogel expertise to develop advanced materials for battery thermal management, aerospace, and specialty markets.
- Aspen Aerogels serves customers across North and South America, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia-Pacific.
- The company operates manufacturing facilities in the United States and the Netherlands alongside a network of distribution partners worldwide.
- Under the leadership of President and Chief Executive Officer Suzanne H. Watson, the company continues to invest in research and development and expand production capacity.
- By aligning product innovation with clean-energy transitions, Aspen Aerogels aims to enable end users to reduce emissions, improve process efficiency, and lower lifetime operational costs.
