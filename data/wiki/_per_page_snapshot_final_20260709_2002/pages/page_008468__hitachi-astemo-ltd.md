# Hitachi Astemo, Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-07-28

**Source:** [HITACHI-SPONSORED TEAM PENSKE CAR TAKES CHECKERED FLAG IN IOWA](https://sportsdigest.in/hitachi-sponsored-team-penske-car-takes-checkered-flag-in-iowa/33063/)  ·  `page_008468`

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo provides fuel-system components for INDYCAR engines.
- Hitachi Astemo is a technology partner with Chevrolet in the development of its highly successful INDYCAR engine program.
- Hitachi Astemo provides fuel-system components for all 2.2-liter, twin-turbocharged INDYCAR racing engines.
- The Hitachi name appears on the sides and rear wing of Team Penske’s No. 2 car, while Astemo appears on both front wings.
- Hitachi will be the primary sponsor of Team Penske’s No. 2 car at Portland International Raceway in Portland, Oregon, on Sept. 4.
- Hitachi will also be the primary sponsor at WeatherTech Raceway Laguna Seca in Monterey, California, on Sept. 11.
- Hitachi Astemo is a global automotive technology company formed in 2021 by the merger of Hitachi Automotive Systems with Honda’s Keihin, Showa and Nissin Kogyo groups.
- Hitachi Astemo has approximately 90,000 employees globally.
- Hitachi Astemo holds leadership positions in AD/ADAS, software, powertrain, chassis, and motorcycle systems.
- Hitachi Astemo Americas is headquartered in Farmington Hills, Michigan.
- Hitachi Astemo Americas manufactures and markets engine management, electric powertrain, and integrated vehicle controls for major automotive manufacturers worldwide.
- Hitachi Astemo Americas adheres to ISO/TS:16949:2009 industry standards.
- Rob Sharpe serves as senior vice president of sales & marketing for Hitachi Astemo Americas.
