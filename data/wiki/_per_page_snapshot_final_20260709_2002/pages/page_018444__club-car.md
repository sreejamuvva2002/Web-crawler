## Facts

- Club Car has set the standard in golf carts, utility vehicles, and personal transportation for more than 60 years.
- The company was founded in Augusta, Georgia.
- Its legacy of innovation, safety, and design excellence has grown from golf courses to communities, commercial operations, and personal use worldwide.
- Every detail from design and fabrication to assembly reflects a focus on durability, comfort, and performance.
- Club Car earned recognition as one of EHS Today’s America’s Safest Companies.
- The company operates a global network of 400+ authorized dealers and distributors.
- Club Car is the Official Golf Car of the PGA of America, PGA TOUR’s TPC Network, and the European Tour.
- Its vehicles are showcased at the Ryder Cup, President's Cup, U.S. Kids Golf, and First Tee.
- Club Car was founded in 1958 in Houston, Texas.
- The company moved to Augusta, Georgia in 1962 under Bill Stevens.
- In 1979, “The Original 8,” a team of former EZGO executives, acquired the business and reshaped the industry with groundbreaking products.
- In 1995, Club Car joined Ingersoll Rand, expanding its reputation as the global leader in golf and utility vehicles.
- In 2021, Club Car entered a new era under Platinum Equity, continuing its mission to innovate and deliver best-in-class vehicles worldwide.
