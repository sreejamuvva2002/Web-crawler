# Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System, LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-04-05

**Source:** [Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System, LLC Expands Manufacturing Operations in Georgia - AllOnGeorgia](https://allongeorgia.com/georgia-state-news/hyundai-transys-georgia-seating-system-llc-expands-manufacturing-operations-in-georgia/)  ·  `page_009585`

## Facts

- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System, LLC will invest $9 million in expanding its seating manufacturing operations in West Point, Georgia.
- The expansion will create more than 150 jobs in Troup County.
- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System manufactures seats and seat foam pads for the Hyundai Santa Fe, KIA Sorento, and other models.
- The company supplies seating structures to automobile manufacturers and leading electric vehicle makers.
- The expansion will take Hyundai TRANSYS' total employment in Georgia to more than 1,890 jobs.
- Hyundai TRANSYS Georgia Seating System is continuing to hire for careers in production seat assembly, foam, quality, and logistics.
- Senior Project Manager Amanda Fields represented the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s (GDEcD) Global Commerce division on this project.
- The project was executed in partnership with the West Point Development Authority, Georgia Power, and Georgia Quick Start.
- Governor Brian Kemp announced the investment and praised Georgia's highly skilled workforce for attracting manufacturing jobs statewide.
- Hyundai TRANSYS President and CEO Sangkil Jung stated the company is excited to expand its presence in the West Point community and proud of its partnership with the State of Georgia.
- West Point Development Authority Chairman Kevin Patrick thanked Hyundai TRANSYS for its continued investment, noting automotive and existing industries are the backbone of local economic success.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson stated that contributing more than $3 billion to Georgia’s economy, the state is committed to growing Georgia’s robust automotive ecosystem and creating solid manufacturing jobs of the future.
- Georgia is home to more than 200 automotive-related facilities.
- Georgia remains a national leader in advanced manufacturing, outpacing the United States in 10-year GDP growth in the manufacture of machinery, electrical equipment and components, and fabricated metals.
- Hyundai TRANSYS produces critical automobile parts including transmissions, axles, and seats.
- Hyundai TRANSYS is the only company in the world specializing in transmissions.
- Hyundai TRANSYS produces all types of automatic transmissions, manual transmissions, dual clutch transmissions (DCT), and continuously variable transmissions.
- Hyundai TRANSYS develops and produces top quality seats for Genesis, Santa Fe, Sorento, and other models.
