# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) LLC

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-07-03

**Source:** [Hyundai’s EV & Battery manufacturing plant to open 8,100 jobs in Georgia](https://www.teslarati.com/hyundai-georgia-ev-battery-plant-jobs/)  ·  `page_000995`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group’s Executive Chair Euisun Chung and Georgia’s Governor Brian P. Kemp recently broke ground on Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) LLC’s new EV and battery plant at the Bryan County Mega site.
- A future technology expo highlighting “advanced technology and extraordinary human achievement” was held at the Enmarket Arena in Savannah following the groundbreaking ceremony.
- Five months ago, Hyundai and its affiliated suppliers announced a $5.54 billion investment.
- The investment includes plans to build a new battery production plant.
- The investment also covers plans to produce a variety of cutting-edge Hyundai, Genesis, and Kia electric cars (EVs).
- HMGMA is expected to add more than 8,100 jobs over the coming years.
- HMGMA is working to build a reliable supply chain for EV batteries and other EV parts for the American market.
- Commercial production at HMGMA’s new smart factory is anticipated to start in the first half of 2025.
- Hyundai Motor Group Executive Chair Euisun Chung stated that Hyundai and Georgians share qualities including respect for history, ingenuity, creativity, and determination.
- Chung noted that Hyundai's EVs are recognized as best in class and expressed determination to be a global leader in electrification, safety, quality, and sustainability through this partnership.
- Chung stated that the Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America will help the company evolve beyond an automaker to become the world’s leading mobility solutions provider.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp stated that record jobs and investment are coming to all parts of Georgia alongside award-winning workforce development programs and infrastructure.
- Kemp described the partnership with Hyundai Motor Group and the groundbreaking as exemplifying unprecedented economic success for the state.
- Kemp expressed a long-term commitment to improving lives and livelihoods and anticipated the facility's impact on both the local community and the state.
