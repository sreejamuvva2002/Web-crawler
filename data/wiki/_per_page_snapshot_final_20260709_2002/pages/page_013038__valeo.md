# Valeo

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-06-18

**Source:** [Valeo to build electric car manufacturing plant in Abu Dhabi - UAE Times - Gulf News, breaking news uae, Business, Tech & Lifestyle](https://uaetimes.ae/valeo-to-build-electric-car-manufacturing-plant-in-abu-dhabi/)  ·  `page_013038`

## Facts

- The Abu Dhabi Department of Economic Development (ADDED) partnered with Valeo to establish a technology center in Abu Dhabi for manufacturing components for autonomous and electric vehicles.
- The new center will develop industrial solutions to support local electric vehicle start-ups in Abu Dhabi and collaborate with local EV startup companies.
- Valeo plans to explore new development areas by combining its advanced technologies with a sustainable vision of green cities.
- The agreement aims to improve smart and sustainable mobility and contribute to the transport sector's development, which is targeted by the Abu Dhabi Industrial Strategy (ADIS) as one of its sub-sectors.
- The agreement was signed by Arafat Al Yafei, Executive Director of ADDED’s Industrial Development Agency, and Tamer Aly, Vice President of Group Technology Center and General Manager of Valeo Egypt.
- The signing occurred in the presence of Rashed Abdulkarim Al Blooshi, General Manager of Valeo Egypt, and the Deputy Minister of ADDED.
- Arafat Al Yafei noted that the collaboration to develop a smart mobility center for electric and autonomous vehicle components aligns with Abu Dhabi's initiative to ensure growth, sustainability, and advanced technology go hand in hand across all aspects of economic development.
- Tamer Aly stated: “I am very pleased with the signing of this MoU, which enables Valeo, a market leader in the design and production of high-tech automotive components for autonomous and electric vehicles, to have a presence in Abu Dhabi, Utilize their components and technology for Abu Dhabi’s nascent automotive industry and transfer this experience to Abu Dhabi’s high-tech and advanced education society.”
- Valeo is a French global automotive supplier headquartered in France that offers a wide range of products to automakers and the aftermarket.
- Valeo employs 113,600 people across 33 countries worldwide.
- The Valeo group operates 186 production plants, 59 R&D centers, and 15 distribution platforms globally.
