# Stryten Energy LLC

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC is a US-based energy storage solutions provider.
- Stryten Energy installed its advanced vanadium redox flow battery (VRFB) at Snapping Shoals EMC.
- Snapping Shoals EMC is a utility provider for some of the fastest-growing areas in the nation.
- The partnership between Stryten Energy and Snapping Shoals will focus on demonstrating the VRFB’s energy storage capabilities.
- The partnership will evaluate additional use cases for the cutting-edge long-duration energy storage technology.
- This project represents the first VRFB energy storage system manufactured and installed in the state of Georgia.
- Shaun Mock is President and CEO of Snapping Shoals EMC.
- Snapping Shoals has provided service to its members for 80-plus years.
- Snapping Shoals serves over 100,000 residential and business customers.
- Mike Judd is CEO and President of Stryten Energy.
- Long-duration energy storage technologies are needed to support the wide scale power generation from renewable energy sources.
- VRFB is ideally suited for long-duration energy storage applications.
- The demonstration project aims to prove VRFB capabilities in utility use cases such as energy cost control, peak shaving and avoiding curtailment.
- Stryten Energy’s VRFB offers industry-leading power density with a versatile, modular platform that is independently scalable in power and capacity.
- With proper maintenance, the VRFB system has an unlimited cycle life.
- The VRFB system can operate for more than 20 years without the electrolyte losing energy storage capacity.
- The technology is uniquely suited for applications that require medium to long-duration energy storage of six or more hours.
- The system aims to help ensure grid stability and facilitate increased utilisation of renewables for businesses and consumers across the US.
