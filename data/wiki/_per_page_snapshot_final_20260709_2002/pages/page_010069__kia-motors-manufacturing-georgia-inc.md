# Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2010-04-21

**Source:** [Strong Interest Prompts KMMG to Extend Application Period - Kia Georgia](https://www.kiageorgia.com/strong-interest-prompts-kmmg-to-extend-application-period/)  ·  `page_010069`

## Facts

- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc. (KMMG) extended its online application period by two weeks due to strong regional interest.
- Since re-opening the application process in November, more than 42,000 individuals have applied for Production Team Member, Maintenance Team Member, or Die Maintenance Team Member positions.
- The application window will remain open until May 7 to allow everyone a chance to apply.
- Only online applications are being accepted at this time.
- Applicants can submit their applications online, view videos of current team members, and get an inside look at the facility.
- Randy Jackson, director of human resources for KMMG’s West Point, Ga., facility, expressed appreciation for the overwhelming response from interested individuals.
- The success of the new Kia Sorento has led to increased vehicle demand, which accelerated the hiring schedule for a second wave of team members.
- More than 1,300 team members are currently employed at KMMG.
- The company anticipates hiring hundreds more in preparation for another production shift beginning in the fall.
- Randy Jackson stated that consumer response to the quality and appeal of the new Kia Sorento is a testament to the dedication and quality of current team members and the effectiveness of support received from Georgia Quick Start.
- KMMG’s online application process was first launched in 2008, marking an industry-first innovation developed for Kia by Georgia Quick Start.
- Georgia Quick Start is the state’s acclaimed workforce training organization.
- Jackson noted that the partnership with Quick Start has helped the company truly “select the best” from applicants.
- KMMG anticipates additional future growth as it adds production of additional car models at the facility, at which point the online application process will be re-opened again.
- Individuals can subscribe to a FirstAlert! email notification for future hiring opportunities.
- Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, Inc. (KMMG) is the first manufacturing site in North America for Seoul, Korea-based Kia Motors Corporation.
- KMMG is located on 2,200 acres in West Point, Georgia.
- The facility began full mass production of the 2011 Kia Sorento on November 16, 2009.
- At full capacity, the plant will have the ability to build 300,000 vehicles annually.
- The 2011 Kia Sorento is assembled in the United States from U.S. and globally-sourced parts.
