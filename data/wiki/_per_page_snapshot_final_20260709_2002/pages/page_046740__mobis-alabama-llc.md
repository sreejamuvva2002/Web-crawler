# MOBIS Alabama, LLC

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2007-05-17

**Source:** [Topic: [MOBIS AL LLC – Georgia Plant] Sales Specialist | WorkingUS.com](https://www.workingus.com/forums/topic/mobis-al-llc-georgia-plant-sales-specialist/)  ·  `page_046740`

## Facts

- MOBIS Alabama, LLC’s Georgia Plant is located in West Point, Georgia.
- The facility operates as a Tier 1 supplier to Kia Motors Manufacturing Georgia, LLC (KMMG).
- The Georgia plant was established on May 17, 2007.
- MOBIS Georgia functions as a three-shift manufacturing operation.
- The plant assembles four major modules: Front Chassis, Rear Chassis, Cockpit, and Front End.
- The facility also produces front and rear bumpers for the three vehicles manufactured by KMMG.
- A posted Sales Specialist position requires accomplishing day-to-day tactical objectives to support company and department goals.
- Employees must adhere to company policies supporting safety, quality, delivery, cost, and morale (SQDCM) initiatives.
- The workplace maintains a union-free environment that promotes ethical practices and legal compliance.
- Sales Specialist duties include managing monthly costs, billing parts, trading goods, and handling price negotiations.
- The role involves collaborating with plant teams and HQ in Korea from RFQ through product specification and pricing negotiation.
- Staff prepare price decisions based on cost calculations coordinated with HQ in Korea.
- Employees negotiate parts prices with the OEM for new projects and engineering changes.
- Staff also negotiate tooling costs and other expenses with the OEM.
- The position requires bilingual communication in English and Korean regarding sales issues.
- Personnel monitor daily billing for modules and training goods to facilitate monthly closing.
- Sales goals are tracked on a monthly, quarterly, and yearly basis, including the creation and analysis of sales results reports.
- Staff monitor accounts receivable (A/R) from the OEM.
- The plant receives daily production requirements from the OEM through internal systems.
- Minimum education for the Sales Specialist role is a Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration, Industrial Engineering, or a related field.
- Candidates require 0 to 3 years of experience in Sales, Manufacturing, or Automotive Manufacturing.
- Required technical skills include proficiency with Microsoft Office Products and Project Management Skills.
- Bilingual fluency in English and Korean is mandatory for the role.
