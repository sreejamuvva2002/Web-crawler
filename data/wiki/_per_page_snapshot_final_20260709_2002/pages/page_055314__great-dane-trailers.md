## Facts

- Great Dane Trailers is nearing the completion of its multi-stop Innovation Unleashed Road Tour.
- The tour began April 16 with a stop in Nashville at the local football stadium.
- The Nashville event was hosted by the Great Dane branch in Nashville and balanced displays of trailer technology with an opportunity to meet Tennessee Titan cheerleaders and several members of the Titans football team.
- A similar format was used in Dallas on May 21, where the Dallas Cowboys stadium served as the venue and former Cowboys Drew Pearson and Jay Novacek greeted visitors.
- Great Dane displays included a Weight-Saving 53’ Truckload Everest TL Reefer.
- Great Dane displays included a Redesigned 48’ Freedom XP All-Aluminum Flatbed.
- Great Dane displays included a Corrosion-Busting 48’ Combo Freedom LT Flatbed.
- Great Dane displays included a Johnson Alpine Aluminum Sheet-and-Post Refrigerated Truck Body.
- Great Dane displays included a 53’ Bonded Construction Composite Plate Dry Van.
- Great Dane displayed The Walmart Advanced Vehicle Experience, a one-of-a-kind prototype that offers a whole package of trailer manufacturing and construction “firsts.”
- The road tour also took the tour through Salt Lake City on May 4.
- The tour recently stopped in Montreal on June 3.
- Remaining stops include Charlotte on June 18 and Laredo, Texas later this fall.
- The event is geared toward private and for-hire fleet owners and managers including owner/principals, vice presidents and general managers, fleet or fleet maintenance managers, and other operations personnel.
