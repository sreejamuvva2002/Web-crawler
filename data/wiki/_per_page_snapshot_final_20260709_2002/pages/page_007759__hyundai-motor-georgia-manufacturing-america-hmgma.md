# Hyundai Motor Georgia Manufacturing America (HMGMA)

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-06-19

**Source:** [Hyundai's Metaplant First Production Vehicle to Be 2025 IONIQ 5](https://thekoreancarblog.com/hyundais-metaplant-first-production-vehicle-to-be-2025-ioniq-5/)  ·  `page_007759`

## Facts

- Hyundai officially confirmed that its Metaplant's first production vehicle will be the 2025 IONIQ 5.
- HMGMA is actively training and preparing its workforce to begin production in the fourth quarter of 2024.
- The new facility will manufacture all trim lines of the IONIQ 5, with the exception of the IONIQ 5 N.
- The Metaplant will eventually become the sole U.S. production site for the IONIQ 5 model.
- Oscar Kwon, president and CEO of HMGMA, stated that the facility is in the final phase of construction and getting ready for the 2025 Hyundai IONIQ 5 to roll off the line.
- The IONIQ 5 has received numerous awards this year, including Best Electric SUV, Best EV for the Money, One of the Best Cars for Families by U.S. News & World Report, and Best Electric Vehicle for the second consecutive year by Cars.com.
- The IONIQ 5 previously won the 2022 World Car of the Year, 2023 MotorTrend SUV of the Year, and 2022 Car and Driver EV of the Year.
- The IONIQ 5 earned the highest TOP SAFETY PICK+ award from the Insurance Institute For Highway Safety (IIHS).
- The Hyundai IONIQ 5 remains Hyundai’s highest volume EV, with a 43% increase in sales in 2024 and an 82% surge in May alone.
- Hyundai announced June incentives for the 2024 IONIQ 5, offering buyers a $7,500 cash rebate and lease customers a $7,500 federal tax credit.
- Hyundai Motor Group is making a substantial investment of $7.59 billion to develop HMGMA, creating 8,500 direct jobs.
- In partnership with SK On, Hyundai is constructing a $5 billion EV battery cell plant in Bartow County, Georgia, expected to employ 3,500 workers.
- A recent study by the Center for Automotive Research estimates that Hyundai’s investments in Georgia will generate nearly 40,000 jobs and $4.6 billion in individual earnings annually.
- Additional vehicles from Hyundai, Genesis, and Kia are planned for future production at the Metaplant.
