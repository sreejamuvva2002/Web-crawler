# Adient PLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2019-04-01

## Facts

- Adient PLC is a global leader in automotive seating.
- Adient operates roughly 238 manufacturing and assembly plants in 34 countries.
- Adient shares rose more than 10% on Monday afternoon following a broader auto-parts stocks rally and an analyst upgrade.
- SORL Auto posted fourth-quarter revenue of $123.2 million, exceeding analysts' estimates by $18.35 million.
- Robert W. Baird upgraded Adient from underperform to neutral with a price target of $13.22.
- Adient's stock traded between $13.46 and $14.57 per share on Monday afternoon, suggesting potential downside from the analyst's price target.
- Adient was spun off from Johnson Controls in late 2016.
- Adient holds nearly 40% market share in the North American and European automotive seating markets.
- Adient holds a 45% market share in the Chinese automotive seating market.
- Adient's stock trades at 4.4 times the consensus estimate for forward earnings.
- Global automakers need suppliers that can deliver consistently on short notice to match demand.
