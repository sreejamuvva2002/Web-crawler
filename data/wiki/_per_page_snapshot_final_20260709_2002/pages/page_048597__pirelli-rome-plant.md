# Pirelli Rome Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Pirelli to manufacture Cyber Tyre in Rome - NewsBreak](https://www.newsbreak.com/rome-news-tribune-1598303/4636503861038-pirelli-to-manufacture-cyber-tyre-in-rome)  ·  `page_048597`

## Facts

- Pirelli North America announced it will manufacture its Cyber Tyre in its Rome plant in the near future.
- The Cyber Tyre is a combined hardware and software system capable of collecting data and information from sensors embedded in tires.
- The system processes and communicates collected information in real time with the vehicle’s electronics.
- Pirelli North America CEO Claudio Zanardo stated that the start of Cyber Tyre production in the Rome, Georgia plant is a significant milestone for Pirelli in the country.
- Claudio Zanardo noted the move reflects Pirelli's commitment to bringing advanced technologies like Cyber Tyre closer to the market and strengthening its industrial footprint and innovation capabilities in the United States.
- Specific investment and expansion details will be communicated in the coming months following finalization of development plans, according to Pirelli officials.
- Pirelli is finalizing the introduction of the latest version of the Modular Integrated Robotized System production process at the Rome plant.
- The new manufacturing process will be the most advanced for high-end, premium production within the entire Pirelli Group and is exclusive to the Rome factory.
- Floyd County Commissioner Scotty Hancock stated that the announcement signals Pirelli’s commitment to Rome and Floyd County and called them an important community partner.
- The initial investment for the project is approximately $20 million.
- Pirelli expects to create as many as 20 new jobs initially.
- The Rome plant specializes in high-value tire production for the North American market.
- The facility hosts a dedicated R&D center to strengthen its role in technological development and product innovation.
- The facility is located at 100 Pirelli Drive in the Floyd County Industrial Park in Lindale.
- The Rome plant currently employs approximately 225 people.
