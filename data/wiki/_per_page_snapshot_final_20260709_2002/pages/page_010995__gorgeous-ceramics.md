## Facts

- Thermal ceramics are a class of advanced ceramic materials and components specifically designed to withstand, generate, manage, or retain heat in high-temperature environments.
- Product categories include ceramic heaters, thermal radiation ceramics, thermal insulation ceramics, and ceramics for high-temperature load-bearing applications.
- GORGEOUS CERAMICS utilizes advanced processes such as isostatic pressing, dry pressing, injection molding, tape casting, precision machining, and high-temperature sintering to customize parts according to client drawings.
- Customizable products include thermocouple protection tubes, crucibles, firing plates, furnace structural components, heating assemblies, and various customized high-temperature parts.
- These products can meet the high-temperature application needs of industries such as semiconductors, metallurgy, heat treatment, chemicals, and new energy.
- Commonly used material systems for thermal ceramics include alumina (Al₂O₃), zirconium oxide (ZrO₂), silicon carbide (SiC), silicon nitride (Si₃N₄), and aluminum nitride (AlN).
- Ceramic heating elements are mainly used in industrial, semiconductor, energy, and medical industries to provide fast, stable, and precise heating.
- Ceramic crucibles are mainly used in metallurgy, semiconductors, chemicals, and laboratories for melting and sintering materials at high temperatures.
- Thermal radiation ceramics are used to emit and distribute infrared heat for high-temperature heating, drying, and heat treatment systems.
- Thermal insulation ceramics effectively reduce heat loss and protect equipment by maintaining a stable temperature in high-temperature, high-energy-consumption systems.
- Ceramic igniters provide rapid ignition for combustion systems in industrial equipment and household appliances.
- Forming and manufacturing technologies include dry pressing, isostatic pressing, injection molding, extrusion molding, and casting.
- Post-processing and finishing capabilities include high-temperature sintering, atmosphere-controlled sintering, CNC precision machining, precision grinding and polishing, and high-precision dimensional control and tolerance management.
- The company accepts customization for all types of ceramic designs based on provided drawings.
