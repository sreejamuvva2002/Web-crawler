# Stryten Energy LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** power_electronics  ·  **Published:** 2023-03-20

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Adds Enterprise Capability to Motive Power Management Software](https://stryten.flywheelstaging.com/stryten-energy-adds-enterprise-capability-to-motive-power-management-software/)  ·  `page_050621`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy LLC announced the launch of expanded functionality in the Monitor module of its inCOMMAND™ energy performance management software on March 20, 2023.
- inCOMMAND, formerly known as GNB Cloud, is an energy performance management software tool that enables users to design and deploy web-connected battery and charger solutions.
- The software includes modules for executing power studies, modeling battery and charger solutions, monitoring systems, and managing asset configuration.
- The enhanced Monitor module provides remote management capabilities for motive power systems at scale.
- Multi-site customers can view battery health data organized by region or location and drill down to the individual asset level.
- Dealers can manage rental and service customers from a single dashboard within the software.
- The system uses self-adaptive IoT technology enabled by smart gateways to automatically update location tracking for batteries equipped with PowerLoggers as they move between customer sites.
- The enhanced Monitor module tracks fleet energy usage, allowing customers to monitor site efficiency, discover optimization opportunities, and reduce costs across corporate fleets.
- The software enables new monetization opportunities for dealers, such as a “Power by the Hour” model that bills customers only for battery use instead of charging expensive monthly fees.
- Battery licenses are issued upfront with a one-time payment that guarantees data access for the life of the battery and includes multi-user access capability.
- Matt Gould, Vice President of Sales and Service, Industrial at Stryten Energy, stated that managing IoT at scale with mobile assets requires smart solutions to track asset locations and configurations across an enterprise to help dealers scale service businesses efficiently and maximize customer value.
- Gould added that inCOMMAND offers a 360-degree approach to energy and motive power fleet operation, with the Monitor module supplying necessary data to improve ongoing battery management for dealers and customers.
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions across the Essential Power, Motive Power, Transportation, Military, and Government sectors.
- The company is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Stryten Energy's stored energy technologies include advanced lead, lithium, and vanadium redox flow batteries, intelligent chargers, and energy performance management software.
- The company has over a century of industry expertise and partners with major companies to meet the growing demand for reliable and sustainable energy storage capacity.
