## Facts

- Anovion Technologies announced plans for a 1.5 million-square-foot manufacturing facility in Bainbridge, Ga.
- The project involves an $800 million investment.
- The facility will generate more than 400 jobs in Decatur County.
- The new facility will house synthetic graphite anode material production.
- Production is scheduled to start in late 2025.
- This project marks Anovion Technologies’ first large-scale manufacturing development.
- The facility will be located in Downrange Industrial Park, a more than 750-acre master-planned industrial campus.
- The site is situated in Decatur County, Ga.
- The project will be one of the largest industrial developments of its kind in North America.
- The development team includes the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s Global Commerce team, together with the Development Authority of Bainbridge and Decatur County, the City of Bainbridge, Georgia EMC, Georgia Quick Start and the University System of Georgia.
- Graphite is by far the largest component by volume and mass in electric vehicle batteries.
- Georgia became a hub for the electric mobility industry in the past years, with some 40 EV-related projects announced since 2020.
- These projects have generated more than $21.9 billion in investments.
- These projects have generated 28,000 new jobs.
- The country’s current administration is prioritizing climate-friendly technology and offers government incentives to EV companies.
- Competition in the EV industry is growing according to CommercialEdge data.
- LG Energy Solution announced a $5.5. billion project in suburban Phoenix.
- LG Energy Solution plans to build two manufacturing facilities for cylindrical battery manufacturing.
- The LG Energy Solution project is expected to break ground in 2023.
- The LG Energy Solution project is one of the largest such investments in North America.
