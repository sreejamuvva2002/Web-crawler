# Duckyang Ind. Co., Ltd.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-01-19

**Source:** [Automotive Interior Components – Korean-Machinery.com](https://korean-machinery.com/2022/01/19/automotive-interior-components/)  ·  `page_007767`

## Facts

- Duckyang Ind. Co., Ltd. was established in 1977.
- The company's headquarters and main plant are located in Ulsan, Korea, which is considered the main industrial city of Korea.
- Its second and third plants are located in Gyeongju and Yesan, Korea.
- Duckyang’s annual sales amount is approximately $11 billion.
- The company started its business by supplying crash pads, a plastic item covering the front automotive part on the dashboard for Hyundai Motor Company’s first-ever car, the Pony.
- The Pony was the original model leading up to Hyundai Motor’s latest electric car, the IONIQ 5.
- Duckyang currently manufactures cockpit modules, door trims, and crash pads as automotive interior components.
- The cockpit module is the company’s best-selling item.
- Cockpit modules are assembled to accommodate audio, air conditioner, airbag, etc., for a total of approximately 130 items in the front automotive parts around the driver’s seat.
- This assembly process is known as ‘Modulization.’
- Duckyang supplies these items for most of Hyundai Motor’s models manufactured in its Ulsan Plant, Korea.
- Duckyang contributes to the Korean automotive export industry by supplying interior parts to exported Hyundai Motor models, known as indirect export.
- The company developed components for EVs due to paradigm shifts in the auto industry from ICE cars to EVs.
- These EV components include BMA (Battery Module Assembly) and ESS (Energy Storage System).
- Regarding BMA, Duckyang was awarded as an R&D national organization hosted by KIAT (Korea Institute for Advancement of Technology).
- Duckyang’s 2020 exports increased by 4% compared to the 2019 export amount despite challenging conditions caused by COVID-19.
- Increasing demand for EVs is expected to further increase export amounts.
- Duckyang has a separate R&D department with facilities located in Suwon and Gyeongju, Korea.
- Through R&D on cockpit modules, Duckyang successfully focused on decreasing weight while ensuring top-class quality.
- Duckyang received the 2021 National Quality Competitiveness Excellent Corporation award from the Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy for eight consecutive years.
- The company is establishing Duckyang America in the state of Georgia, USA, with SK Innovation for BMA and ESS business.
