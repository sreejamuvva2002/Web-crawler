# Pratt Industries

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2023-09-07

**Source:** [Press Releases Archives - Page 4 of 34 - Location Georgia](https://www.locationgeorgia.com/category/press-releases/page/4/)  ·  `page_019050`

## Facts

- Georgia-based recycled paper and packaging company Pratt Industries plans to build a new manufacturing facility in Peach County.
- The new facility will create more than 125 jobs.
- The project involves over $120 million in investment.
- The new Pratt 496,000-square-foot facility will be located at Robins Industrial Park.
- Robins Industrial Park is a Georgia Ready for Accelerated Development (GRAD) certified site.
- The facility will be located in the portion of Warner Robins within Peach County.
