# SKF USA Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2011-10-26

**Source:** [SKF to Sponsor Penske Racing Teams in 2012 - AftermarketNews](https://www.aftermarketnews.com/skf-to-sponsor-penske-racing-teams-in-2012/)  ·  `page_059962`

## Facts

- SKF USA Inc. announced it will be a major associate sponsor of the Penske Racing teams beginning with the 2012 racing season.
- The SKF brand will be featured in the NASCAR Sprint Cup Series, the NASCAR Nationwide Series, and the IZOD IndyCar Series in 2012.
- SKF is entering its 15th year as a major associate sponsor of auto racing in North America.
- In 2012, SKF will be featured on the No. 2 Miller Lite Dodge driven by Brad Keselowski and the No. 22 Shell Pennzoil Dodge driven by Kurt Busch in the Sprint Cup Series.
- In 2012, SKF will be featured on the No. 12 Alliance Truck Parts Dodge driven by Sam Hornish Jr. in the Nationwide Series.
- In 2012, SKF will be featured on the No. 3 Team Penske IndyCar driven by three-time Indianapolis 500 Champion Helio Castroneves.
- Poul Jeppesen, president of SKF North America, stated that Penske Racing's reputation for quality, preparation, and consistency matches SKF's products and services.
- Roger Penske stated that SKF's technical knowledge and specialized mechanical expertise will significantly benefit the teams' ability to win across all racing categories.
- Since 1947, SKF has partnered with Formula One’s Scuderia Ferrari, creating the longest technical supplier relationship in the sport.
- The Scuderia Ferrari F1 machines contain over 150 SKF components designed and manufactured exclusively by SKF.
- SKF maintains a close racing partnership with Ducati.
