## Facts

- Hollingsworth & Vose is a seventh-generation, family-run company and a global leader in the supply of advanced materials for filtration, battery separator, and industrial applications.
- The company specializes in creating high-performance, custom-engineered materials that are critical components in air and liquid filtration, energy storage, and specialized industrial processes.
- Hollingsworth & Vose operates a global network of manufacturing plants, research and development centers, and sales offices across the Americas, Europe, and Asia.
- Key functions supported globally include advanced materials manufacturing, product innovation, application development, sales and marketing, and supply chain management.
- The company's headquarters is located at 112 Washington Street, East Walpole, MA, USA.
- H&V Advanced Materials (Suzhou) Co., Ltd. is located at 56 Songbei Road, Suzhou Industrial Park, Suzhou, Jiangsu 215126, P.R. China.
- The Suzhou site supports strategic expansion in Asia, enabling localized production and development of products tailored to Asian market needs, particularly in areas like air quality and electric vehicle components.
- Hollingsworth & Vose GmbH is located at Im Industriegebiet 1, 35116 Hatzfeld (Eder), Germany.
- The Hatzfeld facility serves as a critical hub for European operations, providing customized solutions and technical support to European customers.
- A North American manufacturing site is located at 169 H&V Drive, Floyd, VA 24091, USA.
- The Floyd, Virginia facility strengthens supply chain and manufacturing capabilities within North America, catering to the automotive and heavy-duty equipment sectors.
- As of April 2025, Hollingsworth & Vose's leadership team was strengthened by a key new hire and several internal promotions, particularly enhancing leadership in Human Resources, Global Sales & Marketing, and Global Technology.
- On January 17, 2024, Hollingsworth & Vose announced participation in the AHR Expo 2024 to feature its newest molecular filtration media designed to improve indoor air quality by removing VOCs, odors, and other gaseous pollutants.
- On May 23, 2023, H&V announced the expansion of its NanoWave® filtration media production line at its Hatzfeld, Germany facility to meet growing demand for high-efficiency filtration solutions in Europe.
- On March 22, 2023, Hollingsworth & Vose announced the appointment of Deirdre Murphy as Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO), effective March 20, 2023.
