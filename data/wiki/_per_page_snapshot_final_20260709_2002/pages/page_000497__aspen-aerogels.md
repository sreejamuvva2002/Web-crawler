# Aspen Aerogels

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** thermal_management  ·  **Published:** 2022-02-17

**Source:** [New company bringing jobs to Bulloch Co.](https://www.wtoc.com/2022/02/17/new-company-brining-jobs-bulloch-co/)  ·  `page_000497`

## Facts

- Governor Brian Kemp announced that Aspen Aerogels will open a manufacturing plant in Bulloch County, Ga.
- The project could add more than 250 jobs and more than $300 million in investment.
- Hundreds of new jobs are on the way to Bulloch County.
- They will build in the Southern Gateway Commerce Park near the interchange of I-16 and US Hwy 301.
- The plant will make thermal barriers that will insulate batteries on electric cars.
- Aspen will be the first industry to build at the Southern Gateway Commerce Park.
- Transport America is already building a huge convenience center and truck maintenance shop inside the park.
- Benjy Thompson, Development Authority of Bulloch County, stated that the project represents advanced manufacturing in the electric vehicle space, noting that the number of jobs, amount of investment, and pay make it a huge project.
