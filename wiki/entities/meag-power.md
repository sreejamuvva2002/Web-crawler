# MEAG Power

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[government-support|Government Support]]  ·  **Location:** Georgia, United States

## Overview

MEAG Power communities deliver reliable, affordable and clean energy to support the growth of businesses across Georgia.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Provides reliable, affordable and clean electricity that powers electric-vehicle manufacturing facilities, charging infrastructure projects and other supply-chain activities in Georgia.
