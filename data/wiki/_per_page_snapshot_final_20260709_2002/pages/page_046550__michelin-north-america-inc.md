# Michelin North America, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2025-10-16

**Source:** [MICHELIN ANNOUNCES FULL ROLLOUT OF TREADEYE TECHNOLOGY ACROSS U.S. RETREAD NETWORK](https://markets.financialcontent.com/wral/article/gnwcq-2025-10-16-michelin-announces-full-rollout-of-treadeye-technology-across-us-retread-network)  ·  `page_046550`

## Facts

- Michelin announces the full deployment of TreadEye, a proprietary technology developed to enhance the retread process and deliver measurable value to fleets.
- After phased implementation, TreadEye is now available across the Michelin Retread Technologies franchise network in the United States.
- TreadEye is an industry-leading tread depth assessment tool that helps fleets maximize their tire investment because every 32nd of an inch counts.
- The system accurately and consistently collects 1,200 measurement points to provide precise insights into tread wear and casing condition.
- “TreadEye is a game-changer for our retread operations and for the fleets we serve,” said Nate Kirian, Vice President of Sales and Distribution, Commercial Business Lines for Michelin North America, Inc.
- Key benefits of TreadEye include identification of pull points by fleet location.
- The technology helps maximize rubber on the road by avoiding early pulls.
- TreadEye helps prevent casing damage, reduces DOT violations, and minimizes downtime from late pulls.
- The system improves efficiency in the tire removal process, directly impacting a fleet’s bottom line.
- Michelin North America, Inc. is headquartered in Greenville, S.C.
- Michelin North America has approximately 23,500 employees.
- Michelin North America operates 36 production facilities in the United States and Canada.
- Michelin is a world-leading manufacturer of engineered materials and composites with over 130 years of history.
- Drawing on deep knowledge in polymer composites, Michelin manufactures high-quality tires and components for mobility, construction, aeronautics, low-carbon energies, and healthcare.
- Michelin offers data- and AI-based connected solutions for professional fleets.
