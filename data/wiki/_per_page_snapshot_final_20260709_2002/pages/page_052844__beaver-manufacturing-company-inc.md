# Beaver Manufacturing Company, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Beaver Manufacturing expands with acquisition of Intercord Thüringen](https://www.innovationintextiles.com/beaver-manufacturing-expands-with-acquisition-of-intercord-thringen/)  ·  `page_052844`

## Facts

- Beaver Manufacturing Company, Inc. (BMC) is a North American supplier of technical fibre reinforcements for hoses and other industrial applications.
- BMC announced plans to expand its global presence by acquiring Intercord Thüringen GmbH of Germany, a producer of high-quality technical fibres.
- The acquisition adds RFL treating technology to BMC's product line.
- The deal gives BMC its third production plant across the globe, alongside existing plants in the US and Mexico.
- The new manufacturing facility is located in Mühlhausen Thüringen, Germany.
- Michael Dubin, Sr. Vice President & Chief Operating Officer of BMC, stated the acquisition represents a significant expansion into the European market and entry into the tyre and belt industry.
- The German plant will continue to produce RFL treated yarns and cords, as well as hose products that incorporate Beaverloc treatments.
- Intercord's technology has produced cords for tyres, hoses, and transmission belts in the automotive and industrial industries for more than 20 years.
- Ramazan Yasbay will continue as Managing Director of the German facility for BMC after the acquisition.
- Ramazan Yasbay stated that adding BMC's technology to theirs will increase innovation in the industry throughout Europe and the Americas.
- Despite a sluggish global economy and the pandemic, BMC executives project the partnership will continue growth, increase production, and create jobs into 2021.
- The acquisition occurs only two years after BMC opened its manufacturing site in Mexico.
- BMC is located in Mansfield, GA.
- Craig Chandler serves as Vice President of Finance at BMC.
- Walter vom Stein is the former owner of Intercord.
