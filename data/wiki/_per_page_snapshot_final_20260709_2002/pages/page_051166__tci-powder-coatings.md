# TCI Powder Coatings

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-02-07

**Source:** [TCI Powder Coatings Welcomes New Account Manager - Coatings World](https://www.coatingsworld.com/breaking-news/tci-powder-coatings-welcomes-new-account-manager/)  ·  `page_051166`

## Facts

- TCI Powder Coatings is a subsidiary of RPM International Inc.
- Drew Spencer has been hired as the new account manager for TCI Powder Coatings’ Ohio/Western Penn./N.Y. territory.
- Spencer previously worked with Mercedes Benz.
- Spencer previously served as a sales and marketing manager with ZeroBase Energy.
- Spencer completed a comprehensive three-month training period in Georgia that included hands-on experience in TCI’s Operations, Product Development, Customer Service, Quality, and Field Technical Service departments.
- VP of Sales Jack Bostock stated that Spencer has a unique set of skills to serve customers and advance the company's premium brand position in the powder coatings market.
