## Facts

- Pirelli Tire North America designs, develops, manufactures and markets tires for passenger vehicles in both the original equipment and replacement markets.
- PTNA also markets and distributes tires for motorcycles and motorsports.
- Pirelli’s Modular Integrated Robotized System (MIRS) facility is located in Rome, Georgia.
- The MIRS facility employs state-of-the-art technology to manufacture tires for both export and domestic markets.
