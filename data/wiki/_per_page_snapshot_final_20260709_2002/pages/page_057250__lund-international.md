# Lund International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2003-12-12

**Source:** [Lund Announces Appointments - Auto Service World](https://www.autoserviceworld.com/jobbernews/lund-announces-appointments/)  ·  `page_057250`

## Facts

- Lund International announced two executive appointments effective immediately.
- Jerry Hughes becomes corporate vice president, product development and quality for Lund International.
- Hughes joined Lund International in September 2002 as general manager of the Autotron Accessories Division in Longmont, Colorado.
- Hughes previously held senior positions in the automotive aftermarket and original equipment industry.
- Scott Winner is appointed general manager of the Autotron Accessories Division.
- Winner joined Lund International in December 2001, most recently serving as plant manager.
- The company says these appointments demonstrate Lund International’s commitment to continuously improve the company’s leadership.
- Lund International designs, manufactures and markets a wide variety of appearance products for the automotive aftermarket, including window vents, visors, hood shields, running boards, bed covers, spoilers, toolboxes, cargo management and other accessories.
- Products are sold under the brand names Auto Ventshade, Belmor, Deflecta-Shield, Lund, and Nifty.
- The company is headquartered in Duluth, Georgia.
- The company has eight locations.
- The company has more than 1,000 employees.
