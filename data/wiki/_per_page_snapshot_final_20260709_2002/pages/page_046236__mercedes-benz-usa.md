## Facts

- Mercedes-Benz USA (MBUSA) is the distributor for Mercedes-Benz passenger cars in the United States.
- MBUSA is headquartered in Sandy Springs, a suburb of Atlanta, Georgia.
- MBUSA operates as a subsidiary of Mercedes-Benz Group.
- The company was founded in 1965.
- Adam Chamberlain serves as President & CEO.
- MBUSA's products include luxury vehicles and commercial vehicles.
- MBUSA employs about 1,400 people, while the infobox lists 11,069 employees.
- MBUSA has been awarded several times by Fortune as a top employer.
- In July 2015, MBUSA relocated its headquarters from Montvale, New Jersey to Sandy Springs, Georgia.
- The relocation moved about 1,000 employees to the region and created new jobs in the northern suburb of Atlanta.
- From 2011 to 2021, MBUSA was the name sponsor of the Louisiana Superdome.
- In 2015, MBUSA acquired the naming rights to Mercedes-Benz Stadium, home to the NFL franchise Atlanta Falcons and MLS franchise Atlanta United FC.
