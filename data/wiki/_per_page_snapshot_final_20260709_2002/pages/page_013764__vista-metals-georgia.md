# Vista Metals Georgia

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-02

**Source:** [Vista Completes Seventh Georgia Casthouse - Metal Center News - Solutions for Profitable Distribution](https://www.metalcenternews.com/editorial/metal-industry-news/vista-completes-seventh-georgia-casthouse/45414)  ·  `page_013764`

## Facts

- Vista Metals Georgia has completed the company's seventh casthouse.
- The completion of the seventh casthouse is a significant milestone in Vista's ongoing $100 million long-range capital plan.
- This expansion represents the second phase of the company’s plan.
- The expansion includes the installation of melting furnaces, homogenizing ovens and support equipment at its Adairsville, Ga., facility.
- Kelly Thomas is president of Vista Metals Georgia.
- Vista’s investment in this expansion will further support growth in core markets including outer space, commercial aviation, defense and industrial applications.
- The company is completing final equipment installation in a new world class finishing facility which is a critical part of this expansion phase.
- Vista Metals previously announced its investment in a new location to serve the aerospace industry with specialty aluminum products.
- The new facility is located in Bowling Green, Ky.
- The new facility is part of Vista Metals’ long range investment plan.
- The new facility will expand upon Vista Metals’ existing capabilities in California and Georgia.
