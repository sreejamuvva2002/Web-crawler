# Lund International

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2015-11-05

**Source:** [Lund International Wins 2015 PWA Manufacturer of the Year - ATVILLUSTRATED](https://atvillustrated.com/content/lund-international-wins-2015-pwa-manufacturer-of-the-year/)  ·  `page_045655`

## Facts

- Lund International is a manufacturer of premium automotive accessories.
- Lund International was awarded Manufacturer of the Year by PWA (Performance Warehouse Association) on Nov. 5, 2015.
- The award was presented during the SEMA Show in Las Vegas.
- The PWA Manufacturer of the Year award is given for outstanding contributions by a manufacturer to the industry and honors diligent efforts to support the warehouse distributor segment with proactive and effective business practices.
- Mitch Fogle, President and CEO of Lund International, stated that supporting customers in the warehouse distribution channel is critical to success for both Lund International and its partners.
- Mitch Fogle noted that Lund International strives to be the easiest company to do business with and values great relationships with customers.
- PWA was founded in 1970.
- PWA's mission is to assist the specialty automotive industry in efforts to expand, maintain, and promote the profitable distribution of specialty automotive products through the manufacturer/distributor/retailer/consumer channel.
- PWA is a non-profit organization composed of specialty automotive parts distributors joined together to deal with management, financial, and legislative matters.
- Lund International is headquartered in Buford, GA.
- Lund International designs, manufactures, and markets branded automotive aftermarket accessories primarily for light and heavy trucks, sport utility vehicles (SUVs), crossover utility vehicles (CUVs), vans, and passenger cars.
- Lund International serves a diversified distribution network including warehouse distributors, automotive parts retailers, online retailers, and original equipment manufacturers (OEMs).
- Lund International features eight brands: AMP Research, AVS, Belmor, Bushwacker, LUND, Rampage Products, Stampede Products, and TonnoPro.
