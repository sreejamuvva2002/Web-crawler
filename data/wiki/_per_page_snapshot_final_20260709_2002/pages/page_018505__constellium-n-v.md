# Constellium N.V.

## Facts

- Constellium N.V. announced the grand opening of its new plant in White, Georgia.
- The facility is dedicated to the production of advanced aluminium automotive structural components and crash management systems.
- The plant occupies 135,000 sq. ft. of space.
- The facility is strategically located to supply automakers in the Southeast U.S.
- Constellium may expand the facility to 220,000 sq. ft. in the future to meet customer supply needs.
- Constellium expects to have 150 employees at the White, Georgia location by 2019.
- Paul Warton, President of Constellium’s Automotive Structures & Industry business unit, stated that being closer to customers’ assembly plants will allow better service to automakers in making vehicles lighter and safer and responding to growing demand for aluminium structural parts.
- Eric Krepps, Vice President and General Manager of Constellium Automotive Structures North America, extended gratitude to the State of Georgia, Bartow County, local partners, and employees for their strong support in establishing the White, Georgia plant.
- Aluminium is highlighted as a material of choice in the automotive industry to reduce vehicle weight, improve fuel economy, lower emissions, and increase safety.
