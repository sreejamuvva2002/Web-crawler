# Continental Tire the Americas LLC

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2021-11-13

**Source:** [OSHA cites Continental Tire after investigating injuries of three workers | Mount Vernon | thesouthern.com](https://thesouthern.com/news/local/communities/mtvernon/osha-cites-continental-tire-after-investigating-injuries-of-three-workers/article_1211c032-f60a-5df4-8e3a-cf0be96f1f1c.html)  ·  `page_018820`

## Facts

- OSHA issued Continental Tire the Americas LLC citations for two repeated violations, 16 serious violations, and five other than serious violations after an investigation that began in November 2021.
- Inspectors alleged deficient safety procedures for energy control, combustible dust preventative engineering, housekeeping, personal protective equipment, emergency egress routes, and hazard communication.
- Continental Tire the Americas LLC faces $341,866 in proposed penalties from OSHA.
- In two separate incidents on consecutive days in November 2021, three workers at the Southern Illinois tire plant suffered severe injuries associated with combustible dust, flammable gas, unguarded moving equipment, and failure to control unintentional equipment energization during servicing.
- On Nov. 13, 2021, OSHA received an employer-reported referral after an employee suffered amputation of three fingers while unjamming a machine.
- The day after the Nov. 13 incident, one employee suffered severe burns requiring hospitalization and another suffered a concussion from an industrial rubber-compound mixer explosion.
- OSHA Area Director Aaron Priddy stated that Continental Tire must review company safety procedures and employee training to ensure workers are safe on the job.
- Continental Tire the Americas LLC is based in Fort Mill, South Carolina.
- The company manufactures passenger, light truck, and commercial tires at facilities in Barnesville, Georgia; Mount Vernon and Sumter, South Carolina.
- Continental Tire the Americas LLC is a subsidiary of the German-based tire and automotive component manufacturer Continental AG.
