# Stryten Energy

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2021-11-11

**Source:** [Stryten Energy Named a 2022 Military Friendly Employer and Brand](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/stryten-energy-named-2022-military-142500745.html)  ·  `page_050486`

## Facts

- Stryten Energy has been named a 2022 Silver Military Friendly® Employer and a Military Friendly® Brand in the annual list from militaryfriendly.com.
- The recognition highlights the company's creation of sustainable career paths, community outreach, brand enthusiasm, and enduring partnerships benefiting the military community.
- Stryten hires military members across every level of the company, from executive management to the plant floor.
- The company maintains a dedicated section on its Jobs website for military and veteran job seekers.
- Mike Judd serves as President and Chief Operating Officer at Stryten Energy.
- Stryten proactively translates skills gained by veterans in the military into jobs within the company.
- The Military Friendly Employers list is produced annually by Viqtory, Inc., a service-disabled, veteran-owned small business.
- List ratings are based on public data sources, proprietary survey data, and personal surveys of veterans, processed via an algorithm overseen by The Military Friendly® Advisory Council and audited by Ernst & Young.
- Stryten maintains high retention rates for military personnel, with many progressing through the company via multiple promotions.
- The company currently has job openings for military members across numerous U.S. locations.
- Stryten sponsors Rivers of Recovery, a national nonprofit that uses outdoor-based activities to treat post-traumatic stress, depression, and anxiety in combat veterans and active-duty military.
- Stryten Energy provides energy storage solutions across the transportation, motive power, essential power, and military sectors.
- The company is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
- Stryten's stored energy technologies include advanced lead and lithium batteries, intelligent chargers, and cloud-based software.
- The company's products power applications ranging from submarines to subcompacts, warehouses, distribution centers, cars, trains, and trucks.
- Stryten Energy is backed by more than a century of industry expertise.
