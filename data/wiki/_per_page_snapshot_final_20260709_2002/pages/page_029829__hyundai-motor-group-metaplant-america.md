# Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-10-01

**Source:** [HMGMA begins production – Savannah Harbor-Interstate 16 Corridor Joint Development Authority](https://savannahjda.com/2024/10/hmgma-begins-production/)  ·  `page_029829`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America (HMGMA) officially began production the week of October 1, 2024.
- Production began not even two years after breaking ground and ahead of the projected schedule.
- The first electric vehicle to roll off the assembly line was an IONIQ 5.
- Several models of Hyundai, Genesis, and Kia vehicles will be produced at the Bryan County facility.
- Trip Tollison with the Savannah Economic Development Authority stated that more than two years of hard work and collaboration came to fruition with the start of production at HMGMA.
- HMGMA’s American-made electric vehicles will land at U.S. dealerships before the end of the year.
- The manufacturing facility represents a $7.59 billion investment.
- Affiliate LG Energy Solution is associated with the Bryan County site.
- The Bryan County facility will employ roughly 8,500 individuals.
- Vehicle production areas at the plant have completed construction.
- The plant is staffed by more than 1,000 workers referred to as "metapros".
- An employee-focused event was held on site to celebrate the milestone and the metapros’ work.
