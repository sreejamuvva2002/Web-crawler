## Facts

- Georgia Senator Raphael Warnock visited Blue Bird Corporation in Fort Valley, Ga. as part of a statewide tour promoting infrastructure and clean energy funding.
- During the visit, Blue Bird Corporation unveiled the test drive of an electric bus with zero emissions.
- Senator Raphael Warnock is a co-sponsor of the Clean Commute for Kids Act to provide further investments for clean transportation.
- The Clean Commute for Kids Act reauthorizes through FY2025 the Clean School Bus Program.
- The Clean School Bus Program authorizes the Environmental Protection Agency to award grants for up to 60% of the replacement costs of acquiring clean school buses and charging and fueling infrastructure.
- Senator Warnock stated that increasing clean transportation services in Georgia requires help from both the federal government and partnerships with private investors.
- As part of his statewide tour, Senator Warnock is also discussing economic recovery from the pandemic with Georgians.
