# Hitachi Astemo

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-19

**Source:** [HITACHI ASTEMO INSTALLS 3 MW SOLAR POWER PLANT IN JALGAON | Electrical India Magazine](https://www.electricalindia.in/hitachi-astemo-installs-3-mw-solar-power-plant-in-jalgaon/)  ·  `page_055925`

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo is a joint venture between Hitachi, Ltd. and Honda Motor Co., headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
- Hitachi Astemo installed its ground mounted solar power plant of 3 megawatt (MW) at its Jalgaon manufacturing plant.
- The Solar Power Plant is built in an area of 43,301 sqm.
- The installation consists of 7,128 ground mounted solar panels and 10 inverters.
- This is the first Solar power plant of Hitachi Astemo in India.
- The initiative contributes to fulfilling the Prime Minister’s vision of making India carbon neutral by 2070.
- The initiative aims towards achieving Hitachi’s mission of becoming carbon neutral by 2050.
- The plant will eliminate close to 4,000 tons of carbon dioxide emissions each year.
- The annual emission reduction is equal to planting approx.150,000 trees.
- The installation will support in reducing electricity consumption charges.
- In the first phase, Hitachi Astemo has installed in Jalgaon a 3-megawatt solar power plant.
- Hitachi Astemo will commission an additional 1.5 megawatt Solar Plant by March 2023.
- Hitachi Astemo is dedicated to the development, manufacture, sale, and service for automotive and transportation components, as well as industrial machinery and systems.
- At its Jalgaon plant, the company manufactures brake systems (disc brakes & drum brakes) for 3-wheeler and 4-wheelers.
- The Jalgaon plant also includes foundry operations.
