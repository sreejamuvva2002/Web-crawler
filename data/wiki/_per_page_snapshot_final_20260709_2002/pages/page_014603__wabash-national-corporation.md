# Wabash National Corporation

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2026-01-05

**Source:** [Wabash National Announces Plant Idling and Restructuring Plan - TipRanks.com](https://www.tipranks.com/news/company-announcements/wabash-national-announces-plant-idling-and-restructuring-plan)  ·  `page_014603`

## Facts

- On January 5, 2026, Wabash National Corporation announced and initiated a plan to idle its facilities in Little Falls, Minnesota, and Goshen, Indiana.
- The restructuring will result in job reductions of approximately 3 salaried and 53 hourly workers at the Little Falls facility.
- The restructuring will result in job reductions of approximately 21 salaried and 193 hourly workers at the Goshen facility.
- The idling and restructuring actions are expected to be substantially complete by the end of the second quarter of 2026.
- The company expects to incur total charges of $15 million to $20 million, primarily non-cash asset impairment charges.
- $12 million to $15 million of the restructuring charges will be recorded in the fourth quarter of 2025.
- $3 million to $5 million of the restructuring charges will be recorded in the first half of 2026.
- The measures are anticipated to deliver fixed cost reductions in 2026 and beyond without discontinuing any business lines.
- Wabash National Corporation operates in the transportation equipment manufacturing industry, producing trailers and related equipment for freight and logistics customers.
- The company focuses on efficient, large-scale industrial manufacturing across multiple U.S. facilities.
