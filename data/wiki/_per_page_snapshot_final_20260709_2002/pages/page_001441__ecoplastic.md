# Ecoplastic

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2023-01-11

**Source:** [Hyundai supplier Ecoplastic invests in new plant in Bulloch County GA](https://www.savannahnow.com/story/business/automotive/2023/01/11/hyundai-supplier-ecoplastic-bulloch-county-ga/69793034007/)  ·  `page_001441`

## Facts

- Ecoplastic is a Korean-based auto parts manufacturer.
- The company is investing $205 million into a manufacturing facility in Bulloch County.
- The company operates as a tier-1 supplier for Hyundai.
- Ecoplastic produces products such as consoles, bumpers and more.
- The facility will be located in Statesboro, Bulloch County.
- The plant will be able to serve the Hyundai Metaplant well from Statesboro.
- On January 11, 2023, Georgia Governor Brian Kemp welcomed Ecoplastic to the state's growing ecosystem of manufacturers, logistics professionals, suppliers and more.
- Governor Kemp noted the site's close proximity to Georgia's ports system as a key advantage for the company.
