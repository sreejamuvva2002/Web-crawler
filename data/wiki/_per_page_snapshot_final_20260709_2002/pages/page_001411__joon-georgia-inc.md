# Joon Georgia Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-12-03

**Source:** [Joon Georgia factory plan moves forward with county tax break - Statesboro Herald](https://www.statesboroherald.com/local/joon-georgia-factory-plan-moves-forward-county-tax-break/)  ·  `page_001411`

## Facts

- Joon Georgia Inc. is a new unit of established auto body parts manufacturer Ajin USA.
- Joon Georgia Inc. plans to build a $317 million factory in the Bruce Yawn Commerce Park.
- The company will receive a 10-year partial break from Bulloch County property taxes on the planned factory.
- The tax incentive is provided in exchange for the anticipated, eventual creation of 630 jobs.
- The Joon Georgia factory plan moves forward with the county tax break.
