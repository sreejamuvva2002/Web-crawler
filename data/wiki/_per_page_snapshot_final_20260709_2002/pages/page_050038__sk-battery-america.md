## Facts

- SK Battery America exceeded its hiring goal of 2,600 employees two years ahead of schedule for its two lithium-ion battery manufacturing facilities in Commerce, Georgia.
- Gov. Brian P. Kemp announced that the company met its hiring goal at the end of 2022.
- SK Battery America is expanding its workforce to approximately 3,000 employees over the next year to meet growing electric vehicle demand.
- Gov. Brian P. Kemp stated that Georgia is seeing unprecedented growth and its citizens are benefiting from it.
- Gov. Brian P. Kemp praised SK Battery America for going above and beyond in delivering on its commitments.
- Gov. Brian P. Kemp noted that SK Battery America was one of the first companies to put Georgia on the e-mobility map.
- SK Battery America announced its first project in Georgia in 2018 with an initial planned investment of $1.6 billion.
- Since 2019, SK Battery America has invested $2.6 billion to build two battery manufacturing facilities in Jackson County.
- The facilities will supply batteries for electric vehicles including the Ford F-150 Lightning and the Volkswagen ID.4.
- SK Battery America started mass production at its Georgia facilities in early 2022.
- SK Battery America CEO Timothy Jeong credited the company's partnership with the State of Georgia for meeting its employment goal ahead of schedule.

- SK Battery America CEO Timothy Jeong stated that more than 300,000 new electric vehicles a year will be on American roads because of the company's work in Georgia.
- SK Battery America has worked with Georgia Quick Start, the state's top-ranked job training program through the Technical College System of Georgia, since announcing its first project in 2018.
- In July 2022, SK Battery America announced a partnership with the Georgia National Guard’s Work for Warriors Georgia program to focus on hiring veterans, servicemembers, retirees, reservists, and their families.
- SK Battery America has held numerous career fairs, visited schools, and held community events in recent months to attract talent.
