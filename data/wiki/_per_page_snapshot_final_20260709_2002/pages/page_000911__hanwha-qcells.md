# Hanwha Qcells

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2026-01-27

**Source:** [LG Energy Solution Vertech and Qcells partner to deliver energy storage projects](https://www.hanwha.com/newsroom/news/press-releases/hanwha-qcells-and-lg-energy-solution-vertech-partner-to-deliver-5-gwh-of-us-energy-storage-projects.do)  ·  `page_000911`

## Facts

- Hanwha Qcells and LG Energy Solution Vertech signed a multiyear agreement to install reliable, affordable, American-made storage products for Qcells’ development projects across the country.
- The agreement includes 5 GWh of lithium-ion energy storage projects for utility-scale sites.
- The partnership includes lifecycle services from LG Energy Solution Vertech.
- Qcells’ engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) projects under this agreement are scheduled for 2028-2030.
- In May 2024, Qcells entered into battery purchase agreements with LG Energy Solution Vertech totaling 4.8 GWh, which were installed in energy storage system (ESS) projects developed across the U.S.
- The partnership secures ESS for Qcells that meet domestic content requirements because LG Energy Solution’s battery energy storage products are made in Michigan and Qcells’ solar panels are made in Georgia.
- Leveraging made-in-America products supports U.S. manufacturing jobs, strengthens the nation’s clean-energy supply chains, enhances grid reliability, and reduces exposure to trade risks and tariffs.
- Chris Hodrick, CEO of Qcells EPC, stated that Qcells is proud to partner with LG Energy Solution on a project showing what is possible when energy development supports U.S. manufacturing.
- Hodrick noted the collaboration reflects a shared commitment to building clean energy the right way by creating American jobs, strengthening supply chains, and delivering reliable, affordable power for communities across the country.
- Qcells operates the largest solar panel manufacturing facility of its kind in the U.S., located in Dalton, Georgia.
- The company is building a second factory in Cartersville, Georgia, where it will manufacture ingots, wafers, cells and finished panels.
- This second factory represents the largest investment in U.S. solar history to create an integrated, sustainable domestic supply chain.
- Once fully operational, Qcells’ annual solar panel production capacity in the U.S. will reach 8.4 gigawatts, producing nearly 46,000 panels every day.
- The produced panels will be enough to power more than 1.3 million American homes each year.
- Jaehong Park, CEO and President of LG Energy Solution Vertech, expressed excitement to continue the partnership and emphasized a commitment to supporting projects throughout their lifecycles for long-term customer success.
- The ESS included in this commitment are built using the latest LG Energy Solution containerized lithium-ion battery solutions with LFP cells manufactured at U.S. facilities.
- LG Energy Solution is manufacturing up to 16.5 GWh of battery energy storage cells in Holland, Michigan.
- LG Energy Solution production is set to ramp up to around 50 GWh at sites across North America by the end of 2026.
- Qcells EPC provides complete utility-scale turnkey solutions through the entire project lifecycle, including modules, solar and energy storage project development, and engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) services to the U.S. utility-scale market.
- Qcells USA has a track record that includes 8.4 GW of sustainably produced module manufacturing capabilities in Georgia and nearly 2 GW of developed, constructed, and operating projects.
- Qcells EPC is a flagship company of Hanwha Group, a FORTUNE Global 500 firm and a Top 7 business enterprise in South Korea.
- LG Energy Solution Vertech is an energy storage systems integrator backed by LG Energy Solution that delivers integrated, reliable, and bankable grid-scale energy storage systems supported by in-house services and software.
- In 2022, LG Energy Solution purchased NEC Energy Solutions and established LG Energy Solution Vertech Inc. to vertically integrate the battery manufacturer and integrator.
- Via a single contract, LG Energy Solution Vertech provides customers with systems, warranties, services, and software through a system-level contracting process.
- The combination of technology excellence and nearly two decades of energy storage integration positions LG Energy Solution Vertech as a leader in the global energy storage market.
