## Facts

- Novelis Inc announced that its indirect wholly-owned subsidiary Novelis Corporation has priced an offering of USD 750 million aggregate principal amount of 6.875 per cent senior notes due in January 2030.
- The bond offering represents an increase of USD 250 million from the offering size previously announced.
- Novelis Inc operates as a sustainable aluminum solutions provider that deals in aluminium rolled products.
- The company reported an 18 per cent decline in net income to USD 128 million for the September quarter of fiscal year 2024-25.
- In the same period of the preceding financial year, Novelis reported a net income of USD 157 million.
- Net sales for the second quarter rose by 4.5 per cent to USD 4,295 million compared to USD 4,107 million in the year-ago period.
- The increase in net sales was mainly driven by higher average aluminium prices and a one per cent rise in total flat rolled product shipments reaching 945 kilo tonnes.
- Novelis achieved record beverage packaging shipments during the quarter.
- The company's global footprint allowed it to mitigate customer impact from a flooding-related outage at Sierre.
- Steve Fisher serves as President and CEO of Novelis Inc.
- Devinder Ahuja holds the position of executive vice president and CFO at Novelis Inc.
