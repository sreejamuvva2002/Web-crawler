# Wheelabrator Group LaGrange Facility

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Press Release / Wheelabrator LaGrange Facility Announces Twenty Years in Business - Heavy Machinery / Handling Solutions - Flammer Relations, Inc. | Denim Marketing | PRZOOM](https://www.przoom.com/news/21401/Wheelabrator-LaGrange-Facility-Announces-Twenty-Years-in-Business/)  ·  `page_014928`

## Facts

- Wheelabrator Group will celebrate its twenty years in operation at its LaGrange, Ga. facility in September.
- The LaGrange location opened on Sept. 21, 1987.
- The facility is the home of Wheelabrator Plus, the aftermarket-sales division that provides a full range of services, replacement parts and training.
- LaGrange is also home to the Wheelabrator Group North American Research and Development Center.
- The site houses a one-of-a-kind Customer Demonstration and Testing Facility.
- The Equipment Modernization Program (EMP) Team focuses on providing equipment upgrades that are effective solutions for customers’ requested improvements in productivity and to reduce the costs associated with the operation and maintenance of their existing blasting equipment.
- A core group of employees at this location moved to LaGrange from Mishawaka, Ind., in 1987 to start up the facility.
- The LaGrange site is located southwest of Atlanta on I-85.
- Wheelabrator Vice President North American Supply Chain Pierre Tanguay stated the location was chosen because of its proximity to a major interstate and Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport in Atlanta.
- The City of LaGrange worked closely with Wheelabrator to train the local workforce.
- The LaGrange facility now employs 130 people, with more than 20 additional employees working in field sales and service.
- The LaGrange facility will celebrate its 20th anniversary with a luncheon and employee recognition this fall.
- Wheelabrator will honor another historical milestone in 2008 as the company celebrates its 100th anniversary.
- The company was founded in 1908 in Ohio as the Homogeneous Sand Mixing Machine Company.
- The company developed the Wheelabrator® airless blast cleaning wheel in 1932, a simple mechanical unit that used controlled centrifugal force instead of compressed air for abrasive blasting.
- The Wheelabrator trademark became part of the corporate name spanning the decades from the 1940’s to the present day.
- Wheelabrator Group is the global leader in providing surface preparation and finishing solutions.
- Wheelabrator Group works closely with customers to deliver the best solutions for their specific requirements using a broad array of technologies, products, services and technical know-how.
- The company's strategically located and electronically linked offices allow Wheelabrator Group to provide unrivaled customer service and support worldwide.
