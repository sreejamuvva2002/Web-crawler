# Hapag-Lloyd

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [German Shipping Company Selects Georgia For Headquarters | AM 1180 Radio](https://chattooga1180.com/german-shipping-company-selects-georgia-for-headquarters/)  ·  `page_015987`

## Facts

- GDEcD announced that German multinational liner shipping company Hapag-Lloyd will invest $18 million in its new North American headquarters.
- The new headquarters will be located at Three Ravinia in Dunwoody, Georgia.
- The project will create an additional 250 jobs in the metro Atlanta region.
- Hapag-Lloyd has been moving people and cargo around the globe for over 150 years.
- Logistics is an innate part of Georgia’s economic prowess, with the state’s economy connected to the movement of goods and people via sea, rail, air, and road.
- Hapag-Lloyd is based in Hamburg, Germany.
- Hapag-Lloyd operates a fleet of over 250 modern container ships.
- The company's fleet consists of 257 modern container ships.
- Hapag-Lloyd has a total transport capacity of 1.8 million TEUs.
- Hapag-Lloyd has a container capacity of approximately 3 million TEU, including one of the largest and most modern fleets of reefer containers.
- The company currently employs more than 500 Georgians across the state.
- Once the North American headquarters project is complete, Hapag-Lloyd will employ more than 750 people in Georgia.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson met with company leadership in Hamburg during a November economic development mission to Germany.
- Uffe Ostergaard serves as President of Hapag-Lloyd’s North American region.
- The new headquarters will allow the company to bring more team members together under one roof to better serve customers and expand career pathways for staff.
- The 125,000-square-foot headquarters is scheduled to open by summer 2022.
- Hapag-Lloyd will be hiring for careers in finance, human resources, IT services, and more.
- Dunwoody Mayor Lynn Deutsch welcomed Hapag-Lloyd and its team members to Dunwoody, citing a longstanding partnership with the State of Georgia and DeKalb County to grow the Dunwoody Perimeter Market.
- Dorian DeBarr is President of Decide DeKalb.
- DeKalb County offers a skilled workforce, easy access to major highways and transportation hubs, and a business-friendly community for settling or expanding businesses.
- Kristi Brigman is the Georgia Department of Economic Development’s Director of Corporate Solutions and Cybersecurity in Global Commerce.
- Brigman represented GDEcD on this project in partnership with GDEcD’s Europe office, Decide DeKalb, the Dunwoody Development Authority, and Georgia Power.
- Hapag-Lloyd has around 13,900 employees globally.
- The company operates 418 offices in 137 countries.
- A total of 129 liner services worldwide ensure connections between more than 600 ports on all continents.
- Hapag-Lloyd is one of the leading operators in the Transatlantic, Middle East, Latin America and Intra-America trades.
- GDEcD serves as Georgia’s sales and marketing arm and lead agency for attracting new business investment, encouraging industry expansion, locating markets for Georgia products, promoting tourism, and planning state resources for economic development.
