## Facts

- Automotive supplier Shinsung Petrochemical is investing $11.2 million to build a new manufacturing facility in Toombs County.
- The facility will create more than 30 new jobs when it begins operations in 2025.
- The location will be a key supplier for the Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant in Bryan County.
- Aurubis AG recently celebrated the grand opening of its $800 million facility in Augusta.
- The plant will process more than 180,000 tons of complex recycling materials like circuit boards and copper cable annually.
- The Aurubis facility will provide more than 230 jobs.
- VT Industries plans to invest $1.6 million in a facility on 21 acres at the Battery Business Complex to expand its footprint in Floyd County.
