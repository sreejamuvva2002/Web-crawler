# YKK Americas

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [YKK’s Georgia Regional Headquarters Expands to Include South America - YKK Americas](https://ykkamericas.com/ykks-georgia-regional-headquarters-expands-to-include-south-america/)  ·  `page_016219`

## Facts

- YKK Corporation expanded the jurisdiction of its North and Central America Group headquarters, located in Marietta, Georgia, to include YKK’s South America Group.
- The newly formed regional group is called “YKK Americas”.
- YKK Americas is led by Jim Reed, President of YKK Corporation of America and YKK Corporation Group Officer.
- John Smith, President of YKK (U.S.A.) Inc., has expanded his duties to become the YKK Americas Fastening Business Leader.
- The organizational change is part of a global reorganization at YKK Corporation aimed at expediting communication and time to market while ensuring proper governance.
- The leader of each regional group reports directly to YKK’s global president in Tokyo.
- Previously, the YKK North and Central America Group covered eleven operating companies in Canada, the United States, Mexico, El Salvador, Guatemala, Honduras, Nicaragua, and Colombia.
- The new YKK Americas Group covers five additional companies in Brazil, Argentina, and Chile.
- The expanded YKK Americas Group consists of a total of approximately 2,400 employees.
- Jim Reed stated that the group is committed to solving complex fastening and attaching challenges for customers and will leverage regional engineering expertise, integrated production capabilities, and regulatory compliance to solve problems quickly and sustainably.
- John Smith stated his focus is building a united Americas team to meet individual market needs, leveraging regional knowledge to support customer growth and adapt to evolving business needs.
