# Seohan Auto Georgia Corporation

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[automotive-components|Automotive Components]]  ·  **Location:** Liberty County, Georgia, Liberty County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

Seohan Auto Georgia is a Tier-1 automotive parts supplier for Hyundai Motor Group America. The company announced an investment of more than $72 million to build a new manufacturing facility in Liberty County that will create at least 180 jobs and produce H/shafts, axles, and brake systems for electric-vehicle OEMs.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Supplier of automotive components (H-shafts, axles, brake systems) for EV manufacturers such as Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.

## Key facts

- Seohan Auto Georgia will create at least 180 new jobs and invest more than $72 million in a new manufacturing facility in Liberty County.
- The new facility will produce H/shafts, axles, and brake systems for OEMs including Hyundai Motor Group Metaplant America.
- Facility scheduled to begin production in late 2024.
- Seohan Auto Georgia is a Tier-1 automotive parts supplier for Hyundai Motor Group America.
- The site is located at the Dunwoody Court Site in the Tradeport East Business Center, a GRAD Certified site.
- Governor Kemp said the Hyundai Metaplant is an economic driver for the region and welcomed Seohan as a new supplier in the fast-emerging EV sector.
- CEO Jung Kee Koo expressed confidence that Georgia will be the center of the EV industry and that Seohan’s experience will contribute to local prosperity.
- Allen Brown highlighted Seohan’s responsible, thorough, progressive organization and its selection of Liberty County for growth.
- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson noted the transition to e-mobility creates opportunities and praised Georgia’s competitiveness and partners.
- Since 2020, more than 35 EV-related projects have announced over $21 billion in investment and 27,800 jobs in Georgia.
- Hyundai Metaplant America broke ground in Bryan County, with offsite suppliers predicted to invest about $1 billion in other locations around the state.
- Seohan Auto Georgia is a subsidiary of Korea Movenex Co., Ltd, which employs over 2,800 employees globally and was established in 1974.
