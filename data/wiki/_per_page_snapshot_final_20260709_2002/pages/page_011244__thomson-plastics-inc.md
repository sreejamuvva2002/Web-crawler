# Thomson Plastics, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-03-07

**Source:** [Advantage Capital Invests $2.75 Million in Thomson Plastics, Bringing Economic Growth to Rural Georgia | Advantage Capital](https://www.advantagecap.com/news/advantage-capital-invests-2-75-million-in-thomson-plastics-bringing-economic-growth-to-rural-georgia/)  ·  `page_011244`

## Facts

- Advantage Capital announced a $2.75 million investment in Thomson-based plastic injection molder Thomson Plastics, Inc. on March 7, 2022.
- The funding is provided in connection with the Georgia Agribusiness and Rural Jobs Act (GARJA).
- The investment will provide working capital necessary to execute on growth opportunities and continue expanding business in Thomson.
- CEO Steve Dyer stated the investment comes at an opportune time in the company’s growth trajectory and provides flexibility for growth in the large tonnage molding market.
- Thomson Plastics designs, manufactures, and assembles custom plastic injection molded products for large industrial customers.
- The company specializes in high tonnage molding using specially designed models, gas assisted technology, and injection molding presses.
- Thomson Plastics currently employs 167 people in the surrounding community.
- This marks Advantage Capital’s seventh investment under the GARJA program.
- Advantage Capital has now invested more than $20 million in small businesses across Georgia through the GARJA program.
- Principal Tim Perusek noted that each GARJA investment offers a customized financing solution positioned for future growth and job expansion in local communities.
- The GARJA program was first passed in Georgia in 2017 to provide access to capital for small businesses and stimulate economic expansion in rural parts of the state.
- State Senator Max Burns stated that the program’s goal was to drive capital into areas of the state that have traditionally been overlooked.
- The plastic injection molding industry is expected to grow to $7.2 billion in the next five years.
- Industry revenue was projected to rise 6.5% in 2021 alone as the economy rebounds from the COVID-19 pandemic.
- Thomson Plastics currently operates a 180,000 square foot facility in Thomson dedicated to manufacturing, assembly, and warehousing of plastic products.
- Advantage Capital has invested more than $3.8 billion in companies from diverse industry sectors since 1992.
- Advantage Capital has helped support more than 50,000 jobs through its financing activities.
- Joe Stosberg is the Communications Specialist at Advantage Capital.
