## Facts

- Lithium-ion battery pack manufacturing is a crucial process in producing energy storage solutions for various applications, including electric vehicles, consumer electronics, and renewable energy systems.
- The process involves carefully assembling individual lithium-ion cells into packs to ensure optimal performance, safety, and longevity.
- Key stages of the manufacturing process include cell selection, module assembly, battery management system integration, and quality control.
- Advancements in technology have led to improvements in energy density, charging efficiency, and environmental sustainability for lithium-ion batteries.
- AceOn specialises in producing bespoke lithium-ion battery packs as a leading supplier in the UK.
- AceOn's skilled engineering team has extensive expertise in lithium-ion technology to help clients select ideal battery cells tailored to specific needs.
- AceOn provides solutions for industrial use, renewable energy systems, and electric vehicles.
- AceOn handles everything from prototype development to small production runs and large-scale mass manufacturing.
- AceOn primarily focuses on crafting Lithium-Ion 18650 battery packs.
- AceOn utilises reputable cell manufacturers such as Samsung, LG Chem, Panasonic, and Sanyo.
- AceOn can produce battery packs using various cells, including Li-ion 21700 cells, Li-ion prismatic cells, and Li-ion polymer cells.
- AceOn provides affordable UN38.3 testing for lithium-ion batteries, significantly reducing costs compared to UK and European testing facilities.
- AceOn already has several lithium battery packs with UN38.3 transportation approval.
- Custom lithium-ion battery packs can be tailored to specific business needs by considering factors such as voltage, capacity, size, and shape.
- Custom packs can include advanced Battery Management Systems (BMS) explicitly designed for intended applications to enable enhanced monitoring, control, and protection against overcharging, overheating, and short circuits.
- Integrating BMS improves safety, extends battery lifespan, reduces the need for frequent replacements, and provides a cost-effective solution.
- Custom battery packs support scalability, allowing businesses to adjust production based on demand from small prototype batches to large-scale runs.
- Using custom lithium-ion battery packs can lead to long-term cost savings by optimising design and performance for specific applications to reduce energy consumption and improve overall efficiency.
- Custom battery packs may improve brand differentiation by offering unique solutions tailored to specific products in a competitive market.
- AceOn operates from a site in Telford, Shropshire.
- AceOn prioritises quality control and safety, ensuring all products meet rigorous standards including UN38.3 transportation approval.
- AceOn's flexible manufacturing capabilities accommodate small-scale and large-scale production runs without sacrificing precision or quality.
- Lead times for production vary depending on the complexity and scale of the order.
- Prototypes can generally be provided quickly, while larger production runs may take several weeks.
