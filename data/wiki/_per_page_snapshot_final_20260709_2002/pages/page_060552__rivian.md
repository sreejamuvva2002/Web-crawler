# Rivian

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2022-08-12

**Source:** [Rivian to site new manufacturing plant in Georgia - The Green Living Guy](https://greenlivingguy.com/2022/08/rivian-to-site-new-manufacturing-plant-in-georgia/)  ·  `page_060552`

## Facts

- Rivian plans to expand its manufacturing operations by locating its second U.S. plant in the state of Georgia.
- The new campus is planned east of Atlanta, located in Morgan and Walton Counties.
- The campus is designed to be carbon-conscious.
- The project represents a $5-billion site development and manufacturing investment.
- The plant intends to employ more than 7,500 workers.
- The expansion is a key next step as Rivian scales aggressively toward higher-capacity production for its future generation of products.
- Rivian unveiled its Electric Adventure Vehicles (the R1T all-electric pickup and the R1S all-electric SUV) in 2019 at events surrounding the LA Auto Show.
- The F-150 and some serious amount of Amazon trucks will be built on this platform.
- Once ramped, the Georgia facility will be capable of producing up to 400,000 vehicles per year.
- Construction on the facility is expected to begin in summer 2022.
- The start of production is slated for 2024.
- Site considerations included logistics, environmental impact and renewable energy production, availability and quality of talent, and fit with Rivian company culture.
- Rivian’s almost 2,000-acre parcel includes abundant natural space.
- Workforce training programs are available in the area.
- Rivian’s Normal plant was recently approved for a 623,000-square-foot expansion, bringing the total footprint to around 4 million square feet.
- There are further plans to extend the warehouse, storage and production capacity onsite.
- Rivian’s hiring in Normal is scaling rapidly with plans to hire an additional 800-1,000 employees in Normal by the second quarter of 2022.
- New Normal and Georgia-based positions are posted.
- Rivian and Georgia are planning 2022 town hall events near the manufacturing site so area residents can learn more about the company and the site plan for Rivian’s plant.
- Rivian plant employees enjoy industry-leading compensation, generous benefits, and equity in the company to drive a real sense of ownership.
