## Facts

- Georgia won a Gold Shovel Award in the 8 to 12 million population category at the 19th Annual Area Development Gold and Silver Shovel Awards.
- The state's biggest deals collectively are worth nearly $13 billion in investment and more than 16,000 jobs.
- Georgia reports three projects with 10-figure price tags.
- SK On and Hyundai Motor Group announced a $5 billion Project of the Year.
- The project is an EV battery cell plant in Bartow County.
- The SK On and Hyundai Motor Group EV battery cell plant is potentially worth 3,750 jobs.
- QCells expanded its Dalton factory and is developing a solar supply chain facility in Cartersville.
- QCells' investments total about $2.5 billion.
- LG Energy Solution and Hyundai Motor Group announced a $2 billion battery cell manufacturing plant adjacent to Hyundai’s Ellabell manufacturing campus.
- Anovion Technologies is building a plant in Georgia to produce synthetic graphite anode materials as part of the EV supplier boom.
- The investment from Anovion Technologies totals nearly a billion dollars.
- Hanwha Q Cells Co., Ltd. is listed among Clean Tech Projects Of The Year (2023) for its Cartersville facility, promising 2,000 jobs and $2.3 billion in investment.
