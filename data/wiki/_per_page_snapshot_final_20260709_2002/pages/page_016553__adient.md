# Adient

## Facts

- Adient is a global leader in automotive seating with approximately 72,000 employees across 31 countries.
- Adient operates more than 200 manufacturing and assembly plants worldwide.
- The company produces and delivers automotive seating for all major OEMs.
- Adient's product range spans from complete seating systems to individual components.
- The company's expertise covers every step of the automotive seat-making process.
- Adient's integrated in-house skills enable it to take products from research and design to engineering and manufacturing.
- Adient supplies its products into more than 20 million vehicles every year.
- The company expects increased earnings, margin, and free cash flow in fiscal year 2023.
- Adient's Board of Directors approved a $600M share repurchase program.
- Douglas Del Grosso serves as Adient's president and chief executive officer.
- Jeff Stafeil serves as Adient's executive vice president and chief financial officer.
- Adient cites risks related to the Ukraine conflict and COVID lockdowns in China impacting regional economies and supply chains.
- The company notes potential work stoppages due to supply chain disruptions and similar events.
- Adient highlights risks regarding the availability of raw materials and component products, including semiconductors required by customers for vehicle manufacturing.
- The company faces uncertainty regarding its ability to execute its turnaround plan and meet debt service requirements.
- Adient notes that global climate change and related emphasis on ESG matters may impact consumer demand and market shifts.
