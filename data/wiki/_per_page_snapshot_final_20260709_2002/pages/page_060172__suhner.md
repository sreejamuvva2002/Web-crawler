## Facts

- Founded in Switzerland in 1914, SUHNER is a family-owned business now in its fifth generation.
- The company maintains manufacturing facilities in Switzerland, Germany, Italy, China, and the United States.
- SUHNER builds long-term partnerships across industries including automotive, aerospace, industrial manufacturing, and medical technology.
- Ben Davis serves as SUHNER’s Marketing Manager.
- Mechanical Engineer Juan Martinez highlights SUHNER’s Electric Force Compliance modular (EFCm-02) system for grinding, sanding, and polishing.
- The EFCm-02 system is completely modular, reduces costs, simplifies programming, minimizes material waste, and optimizes resource use.
- SUHNER provides advanced metalworking solutions including grinding and surface treatment, machining technologies, automated manufacturing, and high-quality components such as flex shaft technology, spiral bevel gears, 3D printing, and OEM motor solutions.
- SUHNER’s drive technology solutions provide high-precision components noted for superior performance, durability, adaptability, and reliability in automation and smart manufacturing.
- The company is integrating Smart Technology with 4G capabilities to enable smarter preventative maintenance and more efficient production cycles.
- SUHNER offers U.S.-based repair services and ongoing technical support, connecting customers with a vast network of global integrators for equipment integration and production line optimization.
- An application center allows customers to experience technology firsthand through practical demonstrations, personal training, and digital resources.
- The company leverages data analytics to provide preventative maintenance, helping customers anticipate potential issues before they become major problems.
- SUHNER has realigned its marketing and sales teams to break down siloed operations and foster a more cooperative approach across its divisions.
- The company is strategically implementing and supporting customers in Industry 4.0 technologies, including smart factories, AI-driven production lines, and IoT-enabled machinery.
- SUHNER focuses on sustainability by seeking ways to reduce its carbon footprint, improve energy efficiency, and develop eco-friendly solutions.
- The company offers turnkey automation systems alongside core products to help manufacturers streamline processes and increase efficiency.
- SUHNER has available land at its Rome, Georgia facility and is prepared to scale operations as customer needs evolve.
- The company is investing in research and development and forming strategic partnerships to stay ahead of technological advancements.
- SUHNER is actively exploring ways to incorporate AI and machine learning into its solutions to help customers achieve higher levels of efficiency.
