# Hyundai Motor Group and SK On

**Type:** joint_venture  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Hyundai Announces New Georgia Battery Manufacturing Plant | THE SHOP](https://theshopmag.com/news/hyundai-announces-new-georgia-battery-manufacturing-plant/)  ·  `page_060332`

## Facts

- Hyundai Motor Group (HMG) and SK On have selected a site in Bartow County, Georgia, for a new electric vehicle (EV) battery manufacturing facility.
- The new facility will supply Hyundai Motor Group’s plants in the U.S.
- The companies will invest approximately $4-5 billion in the plant.
- The project is subject to execution of relevant agreements and final board decisions by HMG and SK On.
- The new facility will be located at Bartow Centre, a zoned manufacturing and industrial site located on Highway 411.
- The companies are aiming to begin operations in 2025.
