## Facts

- Trenton Pressing broke ground on a $10 million facility to expand its manufacturing.
- The expansion will add about 20 new jobs to the company's existing workforce of 220.
- The new facility will be 105,000 square feet.
- The project will help the company expand its assembly services and increase its manufacturing capacity.
- Construction should be completed by December 2023, according to the company.
- The expansion will allow the company to better service its growth and meet manufacturing needs.
- The company's clients include BMW, Club Car, and ZF, among others.
