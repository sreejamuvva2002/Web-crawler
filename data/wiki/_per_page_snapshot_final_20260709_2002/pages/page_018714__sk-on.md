# SK On

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_cell_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

## Facts

- SK On has appointed an automotive sales expert from Continental as its new head of European operations, overseeing all sales activities in the region.
- According to industry sources on September 17, SK On appointed Thomas Eller, a former Vice President at the German automotive parts company Continental, as its European representative earlier this month.
- Thomas Eller is a sales expert in the automotive industry who worked at Continental for about 20 years from 2005 until May of this year, holding roles such as Global Head of Sales.
- Prior to Continental, Thomas Eller worked at Motorola Solutions for approximately five years.
- Based in Germany, where major automakers such as BMW and Mercedes-Benz are concentrated, he is expected to oversee local sales, expand new orders in the European market, and manage client relationships.
- SK On aims to build a "customized cooperation system for local clients" and focus on expanding demand in the European market through the new European representative position.
- The new structure minimizes time differences and physical distance compared to the previous approach where headquarters handled all client interactions, enabling faster and more efficient responses.
- SK On currently operates three plants in Komarom and Ivancsa, Hungary.
- Increasing the operational rate of these Hungarian plants through new orders to enhance profitability is considered a key task for SK On.
- In February, SK On established a North American representative position to strengthen its local sales capabilities.
- Rob Schnell, a sales expert with 30 years of experience in the automotive industry and an engineering background, is serving as the North American representative.
- Rob Schnell has collaborated with global automakers such as Ford, General Motors (GM), and Stellantis.
