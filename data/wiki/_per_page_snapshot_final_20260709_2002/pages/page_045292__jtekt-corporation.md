# JTEKT Corporation

## Facts

- JTEKT Corporation is a Japanese corporation created in January 2006 upon the merger of two companies: Koyo Seiko Co. and Toyoda Machine Works.
- JTEKT trades on the Tokyo Stock Exchange under ticker TYO: 6473.
- The company's headquarters are located in Japan.
- Key people include Tetsuo Agata.
- Revenue was 1,441.1 billion yen for FY 2017.
- Number of employees is 40,756 (Consolidated) and 10,563 (Nonconsolidated).
- Parent company is Toyota Group.
- Toyoda Machine Works handles machine tool sales for North, Central and South America, aftermarket support services, machine re-manufacturing and engineering services supplied by Jtekt Toyoda Americas Corporation H.Q. in Arlington Heights, Illinois.
- The Re-manufactured Products Division operates out of Wixom, Michigan, with support offices in Monterrey, Mexico and Itu, Brazil.
- JTEKT Corporation machine tools are manufactured in Japan by the JTEKT Corporation, and in Taiwan, by WELE Mechatronics, a "Toyoda Strategic Alliance Company", and exported globally.
- JTEKT North America is a separate legal entity headed by COO Gary Bourque who reports to the CEO Keita Mizuno.
- In 2009, JTEKT NA purchased the needle bearing business (automotive bearings) from Timken.
- The needle roller bearing business was a part of the Torrington Company which Timken purchased in 2003.
- JTEKT gained five manufacturing locations along with a technical center and a business office in North America, as well as eight other locations worldwide through the acquisition.
- Headquarters for JTEKT North America operations include A-TEC in Plymouth, MI.
- Headquarters for JTEKT North America operations include Koyo Industrial Sales in Westlake, OH and Automotive in Greenville, SC.
- Headquarters for JTEKT North America operations include Toyoda Machinery in Arlington Heights, IL.
- Technical Centers include JTEKT-A-TEC in Plymouth, MI.
- Technical Centers include JTEKT-JSCI in Halifax, NS.
- Technical Centers include Koyo-GTC in Greenville, SC.
- Technical Centers include Jtekt Toyoda Americas Corp Northeast Tech Center in Shrewsbury, MA.
- Technical Centers include Upper Midwest Tech Center in New Brighton, MN.
- Technical Centers include West Coast Tech Center in Placentia, CA.
- Manufacturing locations include a Steering/Driveline Plant (JATM) in Morristown, TN.
- Manufacturing locations include a Steering Plant (JATV) in Vonore, TN.
- Manufacturing locations include a Steering Plant (JATX) in Ennis, TX.
- Manufacturing locations include a Driveline Plant (JASC) in Piedmont, SC.
- Manufacturing locations include a Taper Roller Bearing Plant (Koyo Washington) in Telford, TN.
- Manufacturing locations include a Wheel Hub Bearing Plant (Koyo Richland) in Blythewood, SC.
- Manufacturing locations include a Taper Roller Bearing plant (Koyo Orangeburg) in Orangeburg, SC.
- Manufacturing locations include a Thrust Bearing Plant (Walhalla/Classic Torrington) in Walhalla, SC.
- Bearings operations (Cairo/Classic Torrington) are located in Cairo, GA.
- Drawn Cup Bearings operations (Sylvania/Classic Torrington) are located in Sylvania, GA.
- Loose Needle Rollers operations (Dahlonega/Classic Torrington) are located in Dahlonega, GA.

- Additional manufacturing locations include a Canadian Warehouse and Operations in Burlington, Ontario.
- Mexico sales office is located in Monterrey, Mexico.
- Brazil Tech center is located in Itu, São Paulo, Brazil.
