## Facts

- On 7 May 2024, Syensqo announced the extension of its Ajedium™ Films offering for slot liners in electric motors to a range of polyphenylene sulfone (PPSU) based products developed to replace conventional paper slot liners in motors up to 400V.
- On 25 April 2024, Syensqo broke ground on its future battery-grade polyvinylidene fluoride (PVDF) facility in Augusta, Georgia.
- Tom Perez, Senior Advisor and Assistant to the President and Director of the White House Office of Intergovernmental Affairs, joined Syensqo for the groundbreaking event.
- Giulia Siccardo, Director of the Department of Energy’s Office of Manufacturing and Energy Supply Chains, joined Syensqo for the groundbreaking event.
- The critical support of the Biden-Harris Administration underscores the project's significance for the U.S. EV market.
