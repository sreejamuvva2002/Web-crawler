# YKK AP AMERICA INC.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** unknown  ·  **Published:** 2023-12-28

**Source:** [YKK AP AMERICA INC. — Operations Begin at New Macon Factory | YKK AP Global Website](https://www.ykkapglobal.com/en/newsroom/releases/20240109)  ·  `page_016054`

## Facts

- YKK AP AMERICA INC., a local subsidiary of YKK AP Inc. headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia, announced that its new residential vinyl window production facility in Macon, Georgia, began operations on December 28, 2023.
- Construction of the Macon Factory started in October 2022 and was completed in December 2023.
- The factory is located at 100 YKK AP Way, Macon, Bibb County, Georgia, on a new site approximately 20 minutes by car from the former Macon Factory.
- All manufacturing functions previously dispersed across two locations have been consolidated into this integrated production system for vinyl windows.
- The facility aims to strengthen production and supply capabilities to increase sales of residential products throughout six southern U.S. states.
- Production flow runs from the west side to the east side, following the order of extrusion of vinyl materials, processing of glass and windows, assembly, and shipping.
- Automation technology adopted in Japan's APW Line will be implemented to increase productivity by 212% compared to current levels.
- The factory includes a new hung window line for producing the upcoming StyleView® Classic series alongside existing vinyl window products.
- GTP (Goods To Person) technology, involving shelf-transporting robots, has been added to reduce physically demanding tasks like heavy lifting and picking during production, warehousing, and shipment.
- A remote support system, equipment specifications maintainable by local operators, and in-process inspections independent of human operators have been implemented to ensure safety and quality.
- The building design incorporates increased natural light and improved roof and wall insulation for energy efficiency, with phased-in energy-generation technologies aimed at achieving carbon neutrality.
- The facility's design is scalable to accommodate additional automation, increased capacity, and future business expansion.
- An emergency evacuation site has been secured to protect employees from frequent southern U.S. tornadoes.
- Main facilities include resin extrusion machines and production lines for laminate processing, insulating glass, and vinyl windows.
- Annual production capacity is approximately 450,000 windows per year.
- The total site area is 202,343 square meters from a recent acquisition.
- The total floor area is about 36,000 square meters.
- The building structure is steel construction and reinforced concrete, one story above ground.
- Employment at the Macon Factory stands at 250 employees as of December 2023.
- Total investment amount for land, building, and production facilities is $111.92 million USD (approximately 15.6 billion yen).
- YKK AP AMERICA INC. was founded on April 24, 1991, and is led by President Oliver Stepe.
- The company's broader employee count is about 1,200 as of the end of March 2023.
- YKK AP AMERICA INC. operates plants in Dublin, GA; Macon, GA; Dallas, TX; Cincinnati, OH; and Lake Shore, Canada, with branches across multiple U.S. cities including Atlanta, Orlando, Baltimore, Greensboro, Boston, Chicago, Houston, and Novi.
- The company sells aluminum architectural products for stores, residential/commercial buildings, curtain wall products, and residential vinyl windows.
