# Archer Aviation Inc.

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** ?

**Source:** [Archer Completes Construction of High-Volume Manufacturing Facility | Aviation Pros](https://www.aviationpros.com/aircraft-maintenance-technology/aircraft-technology/press-release/55250836/archer-completes-construction-of-high-volume-manufacturing-facility)  ·  `page_016964`

## Facts

- Archer Aviation Inc. (NYSE: ACHR) announced it received the certificate of occupancy for its high-volume manufacturing facility, ARC.
- The ARC facility is connected to the Covington Municipal Airport in Georgia.
- Archer is currently working to complete the tooling load-in for the initial manufacturing line at the ARC facility.
- Production at the ARC facility is planned to begin in early 2025.
- Archer aims to ramp production to a rate of two aircraft per month by the end of 2025.
- Archer built the ARC facility in close partnership with Stellantis.
- Archer and Stellantis are continuing to progress plans to mass-produce Archer's Midnight aircraft.
- Stellantis is contributing capital, advanced manufacturing technology and expertise, and experienced personnel to the ARC facility.
- The goal is to scale the ARC facility to produce 650 aircraft annually by 2030.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp commented on Archer's choice to build the new manufacturing facility in Newton County.
- Governor Kemp reaffirmed Georgia's commitment to working with innovative companies like Archer to create quality jobs and opportunities for years to come.
- Congressman Mike Collins of Georgia sits on the House Committee on Transportation and Infrastructure and the Committee on Science, Space, and Technology.
- Congressman Mike Collins stated that the completion of Archer's manufacturing facility in Covington highlights Georgia’s leadership in innovation, advanced manufacturing, and job creation.
- Congressman Mike Collins expressed excitement to support this transformative chapter in aviation, which will drive progress, deliver economic benefits for Georgia, and create opportunity for everyone involved with and around the facility.
- Adam Goldstein, Archer's founder and CEO, stated that the completion of the facility is a testament to the industry shifting from R&D into commercialization.
- With construction on ARC complete, Archer's team is focused on the start of production planned for early next year.
- Adam Goldstein expressed pride in the Archer team and partners for completing the project fast and on budget.
- Stellantis Chief Manufacturing and Supply Chain Officer Arnaud Deboeuf stated that supporting Archer's electric aircraft growth with deep manufacturing expertise is a great opportunity for both companies.
- Arnaud Deboeuf congratulated the Archer team on this milestone, calling it a significant step in taking clean mobility to the sky.
