# Commerce, Georgia

**Type:** Charging Infrastructure  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[charging-infrastructure|Charging Infrastructure]]  ·  **Location:** Commerce, Georgia, Georgia, United States

## Overview

The city of Commerce in Georgia, United States, provides public electric vehicle charging infrastructure consisting of 28 ports (Level 2 and Level 3) within a 15 km radius.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Public EV charging stations serving drivers in Commerce, GA.

## Related organizations & people

- ChargePoint — Charging network provider
- Tesla — Charging network provider
- Electrify America — Charging network provider
- ChargeHub — Data source / information platform

## Key facts

- Commerce has a total of 28 public charging station ports within a 15 km area.
- 43% of the ports are Level 2 stations (12 total).
- 57% of the ports are Level 3 stations (16 total).
- 14% of the ports offer free charging, amounting to 4 free stations.
- The main charging networks operating in Commerce are ChargePoint, Tesla, and Electrify America.
