# Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2022-05-12

## Facts

- Hitachi Astemo Americas, Inc. announced the expansion of its ignition coils lineup.
- The company now has 276 SKUs available that cover 260 million vehicles in operation (VIO).
- The OE-quality ignition coils are designed to reduce time-consuming part searches and avoid returns for customers.
- Hitachi Astemo produces millions of ignition coils each year featuring durability and long-lasting reliability.
- The ignition coils are designed to perform consistently in extreme temperatures and humidity.
- The ignition coils deliver consistent voltage output at various load and rpm levels.
- The ignition coils are subjected to extensive testing before packaging.
- Ryan Pelkey serves as the North American marketing manager for Hitachi Astemo Americas.
- Pelkey stated that the team is dedicated to engine management systems and focused on categories where they have extensive OE experience when expanding coverage.
- Pelkey noted that consumers and shops associate quality with the Hitachi brand.
- Pelkey emphasized that customers receive the same OE quality they have come to expect from Hitachi over the last 30 years.
- The company's pledge is that if a product is not OE quality, it will not be packaged in their box.
