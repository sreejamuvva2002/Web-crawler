# SK Battery America

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Commerce, Georgia, Jackson County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

SK Battery America exceeded its hiring goal of 2,600 employees two years ahead of schedule and is on track to reach a workforce of about 3,000 workers across its two lithium-ion battery manufacturing facilities in Commerce, Georgia, supplying batteries for electric vehicles such as the Ford F-150 Lightning and Volkswagen ID.4.

## EV supply-chain relevance

The company's two battery plants in Jackson County produce lithium-ion cells that power electric vehicles, including the Ford F-150 Lightning and Volkswagen ID.4, with a combined annual capacity of 22 GWh-enough for roughly 300,000 EVs.
