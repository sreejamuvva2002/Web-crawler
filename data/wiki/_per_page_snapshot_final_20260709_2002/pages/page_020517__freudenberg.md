## Facts

- Freudenberg developed the DIAvent high-flow pressure compensation valve.
- The DIAvent valve combines regular battery pressure regulation with rapid emergency degassing into a single component.
- It achieves improved performance and long-term, repeatable reliability using 50 to 70 percent less space than competitive pressure regulation and degassing solutions for the same airflow.
- The design can be adapted to fit any battery space in support of the industry’s move toward higher-density batteries.
- Freudenberg's first customer for the DIAvent valve is undisclosed.
