# Georgia Department of Economic Development

**Type:** government_program  ·  **Role:** government_support  ·  **Published:** 2023-01

**Source:** [GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson Named ‘Georgian of the Year’ by Georgia Trend - Columbus CEO](https://thecolumbusceo.com/news/2023/01/gdecd-commissioner-pat-wilson-named-georgian-year-georgia-trend/)  ·  `page_008267`

## Facts

- GDEcD Commissioner Pat Wilson was named the 2023 “Georgian of the Year” by Georgia Trend.
- The award recognizes Commissioner Wilson as the top salesperson for the state and commends his work promoting the advantages of Georgia to attract investments and create jobs.
- Former Governor Nathan Deal appointed Wilson as economic development Commissioner, with his tenure spanning six years as of January 2023.
- Georgia maintained its place as the No. 1 state in the nation to do business for the ninth consecutive year in 2022.
- Former Georgia Governor Sonny Perdue credited Wilson with helping make Georgia one of the best places to grow a business by communicating the actions taken by the governor and legislature.
- In August 2022, GDEcD announced that in FY22, investments in expansions and new locations totaled more than $21.2 billion.
- The FY22 investments were projected to create 51,132 direct new jobs in the state, setting new records for Georgia.
- GDEcD’s work providing solutions for companies and keeping communication open helped Georgia land two of its largest-ever investments in FY22.
- GDEcD teammates Lee Thomas, Director of the Georgia Film Office, and David Nuckolls, Executive Director of the Center of Innovation, were also recognized by Georgia Trend for their contributions to the state.
- GDEcD describes itself as the “quarterback” that brings partners across the state together to make business happen.
