# Club Car

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2023-10-13

**Source:** [Club Car Preparing to Expand into New Appling Facility | Club Car News](https://www.clubcar.com/en/our-company/news/expansion-new-appling-georgia-facility)  ·  `page_018399`

## Facts

- Club Car announced plans to expand into a new 105,000 square foot facility in Appling, Georgia.
- The new facility will be located in the White Oak Industrial Park near an existing Club Car facility that opened in 2019.
- Club Car plans to use the additional space to support warehousing and manufacturing functions.
- Construction of the new facility is expected to be completed by December 2023.
- Mark Wagner, Club Car President, stated that the new facility gives the company the opportunity to extend operations in the United States.
- Rick Evans, Chair of the Board of Directors for the Development Authority of Columbia County, highlighted Club Car's long-standing relationship with Columbia County and valued its continued investments to expand and manufacture golf cars and utility vehicles there.
- Doug Duncan, Chairman of Columbia County Board of Commissioners, described Club Car as a great community partner and expressed excitement over its continued growth in Columbia County.
- Club Car has maintained multi-purpose operations in Augusta since it was founded in 1958.
- Today, Club Car designs and manufactures golf cars, commercial utility vehicles, and personal-use transportation.
- Club Car is a leading manufacturer of gas and electric golf, utility, and personal transportation vehicles with over 60 years of experience.
- The Club Car Vehicle Group includes the luxury electric vehicle brand Garia and the lightweight electric utility vehicle brand Melex.
- Club Car positions itself as a global leader in sustainability and electrification and is at the forefront of environmentally responsible Zero Emission Vehicle (ZEV) technologies.
