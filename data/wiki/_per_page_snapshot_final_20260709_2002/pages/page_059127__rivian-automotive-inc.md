# Rivian Automotive, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2024-05-08

**Source:** [Rivian plans to produce 155,000 units of the R2 per year - electrive.com](https://www.electrive.com/2024/05/08/rivian-plans-to-produce-155000-units-of-the-r2-e-suv-per-year-in-future/)  ·  `page_059127`

## Facts

- Rivian announced receiving an incentive package totaling $827 million from the US state of Illinois to expand its existing plant in Normal.
- The expanded Normal plant will produce premium vehicles including the R1T pickup, R1S SUV, Rivian delivery vans, and the new electric mid-range SUV R2.
- Rivian is putting the construction of a planned second plant in Georgia on hold for the time being.
- Following the Normal plant expansion, total production capacity is expected to reach 215,000 vehicles per year.
- Of the expanded capacity, the new R2 model will account for 155,000 units annually.
- The current plant capacity prior to expansion is expected to be 150,000 units per year.
- Rivian did not specify a completion date for the Normal plant expansion in its Q1 report.
- The electric mid-range R2 SUV is scheduled to launch on the market in 2026.
- Rivian continues to pursue a goal with Amazon to put 100,000 “EDV” electric delivery vans on the roads for the online delivery giant.
- Rivian is also selling its delivery van to third parties under the name ‘RCV’.
- Rivian's turnover in Q1 2024 totaled $1.2 billion, primarily driven by the delivery of 13,588 electric cars.
- The company reported a net loss of approximately $1.45 billion for January to March 2024 due to high investments, representing an increase of about $100 million compared to the same period last year.
- Rivian announced in April 2024 that it produced significantly fewer electric vehicles in Q1 2024 than in the previous quarter.
- Rivian maintains its production forecast of 57,000 units for all of 2024.
- This 2024 forecast matches the 2023 full-year output of 57,232 vehicles.
- In Q1 2024, Rivian produced 13,980 vehicles and delivered 13,588 units.
- Vehicle deliveries fell by 384 units in Q1 2024 compared to Q4 2023.
- Production output declined by 3,561 vehicles in Q1 2024 compared to Q4 2023.
- In Q4 2023, Rivian produced 17,541 electric cars and delivered 13,972 units.
- In Q2 2023, Rivian recorded 13,992 vehicles produced and 12,640 deliveries.
- In Q3 2023, Rivian recorded 16,304 vehicles produced and 15,564 deliveries.
- Production declined by 20.3% from Q4 2023 to Q1 2024.
- Deliveries decreased by 2.7% from Q4 2023 to Q1 2024.
- Rivian currently produces the R1T pickup, R1S SUV, and EDV for Amazon on a separate production line.
- The company does not provide a breakdown of production volumes by model series in its reports.
- Financially, 2024 is expected to be a challenging year for Rivian.
- In 2023, Rivian's net loss totaled $5.4 billion (€4.98 billion).
- Within three years, Rivian has accumulated total losses of $16.8 billion.
- The manufacturer has expanded drive options for the R1 models downward over time and introduced the smaller R2 in March.
- Rivian teased an even more compact R3 model alongside the R2 launch.
- Increasingly affordable vehicles are expected to pave the way for Rivian to enter the mass market.
