# Hyundai Mobis Manufacturing Facility

## Facts

- The new facility is set to become a major manufacturing hub for automotive parts for Hyundai.
- The state-of-the-art building spans 860,070 square feet.
- The facility is designed to produce over 1 million automotive parts each year.
- Faber by Kalisch was approached to provide multiple structural steel solutions.
- More than 750,000 square feet of 18 GA Galvanized Grade 50 Composite Steel Deck were provided.
- The facility features cutting-edge technology and top-of-the-line equipment.
- The building is a double-story structure.
- Other solutions provided included SJI-certified steel joists.
- Multiple structural components resulted in the creation of a world-class facility.
