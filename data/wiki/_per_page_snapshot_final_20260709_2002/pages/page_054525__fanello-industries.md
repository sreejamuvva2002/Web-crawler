# Fanello Industries

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2018-04-05

**Source:** [Ryerson acquires Fanello Industries](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/lasercutting/ryerson-acquires-fanello-industries)  ·  `page_054525`

## Facts

- Ryerson Holding Corp., Chicago, a value-added processor and distributor of industrial metals, has acquired Fanello Industries.
- Fanello Industries is a privately owned metal service company located in Lavonia, Georgia.
- Fanello supplies blanking, stamping, laser cutting, bending, and machining services to companies in the southeastern United States.
- The company employs about 150 people.
- Fanello will continue to do business under its own brand name following the acquisition.
