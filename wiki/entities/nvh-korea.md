# NVH Korea

**Type:** Company  ·  **Supply-chain role:** [[battery-cell-manufacturing|Battery Cell Manufacturing]]  ·  **Location:** Gardner Logistics Park, Colvin Drive, Locust Grove, Henry County, Georgia, United States

## Overview

NVH Korea, an automotive parts manufacturer based in Ulsan, South Korea, announced a $72 million investment to build a new facility in Locust Grove, Henry County, Georgia that will produce electric-vehicle battery system components and create over 160 jobs.

## EV supply-chain relevance

Produces EV battery system components that protect, connect, and sense battery cells for electric-vehicle manufacturers in the United States.
