## Facts

- Retrofitting transforms an I.C.E. vehicle into an electric one by replacing the petrol/diesel engine and fuel tank with an electric motor and battery.
- Valeo's 'I Care for the Planet' program aims to reduce the environmental impact of the automotive aftermarket.
- Regulations for emissions have become increasingly severe around the world, and major cities are implementing low-emission zones and sometimes outright diesel bans to combat air pollution and meet climate targets.
- Most light commercial vehicles (LCVs) run on diesel, so city entry bans directly impact businesses, fleet owners, and small businesses that must urgently switch to electric vehicles.
- Buying new electric vehicles is expensive, making retrofitting a perfect alternative.
- Valeo, a leading automotive supplier in Electrification, offers a range of products and solutions for EVs that can be used for retrofitting conventional vehicles into EVs.
- Products include e-motors, OBC DCDC, Inverters, HV heaters, and Electric driven compressors (EDC).
- These components can all be integrated into retrofit projects to convert Internal Combustion Engine (I.C.E.) LCVs into electric ones.
- The cost saving of converting existing vehicles into electric vehicles is estimated to be about 50% the cost of buying a new eLCV.
- Retrofitting contributes to automotive decarbonization and saves at least 50% CO2 emissions over 10 years.
- By not scrapping the vehicle, retrofitting also contributes to the circular economy.
- Governments are increasingly offering incentives and support programs to facilitate retrofitting in recognition of its importance in transitioning to cleaner transportation.
- Some governments, like France, are gradually reducing subsidies for buying new electric vehicles, which further enhances the economic appeal of retrofitting.
- In Europe, there are more than 30 million active LCVs facing the challenge of entering urban spaces.
- Valeo is well-placed to support the retrofit industry and help convert part of the LCV car parc into electric vehicles for the future due to its automotive electrification expertise and established relationships.
