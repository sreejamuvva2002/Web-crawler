# Anovion Technologies

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** battery_materials  ·  **Published:** 2023-05-15

**Source:** [New manufacturing facility coming to Bainbridge, creating hundreds of jobs](https://www.walb.com/2023/05/15/new-manufacturing-facility-coming-bainbridge-creating-hundreds-jobs/)  ·  `page_052204`

## Facts

- Anovion Technologies is a supplier of premium synthetic graphite anode materials for lithium-ion batteries.
- Anovion Technologies will build a new manufacturing facility in Bainbridge, Georgia.
- The project involves an $800 million investment.
- The project will bring over 400 jobs to Decatur County.
- Georgia Governor Brian Kemp welcomed Anovion to Georgia, stating the state is fast becoming the e-mobility capital of the nation.
- Governor Brian Kemp noted that job creators are recognizing every corner of Georgia has the resources and workforce needed for success.
- Governor Brian Kemp expressed looking forward to celebrating the partnership with the hardworking Georgians of Decatur County after meeting their team personally.
- The new facility will be 1,500,000 square feet in size.
- The facility will be located at Downrange Industrial Park in Bainbridge.
- The facility will be the first of its size and scale in North America.
- The facility will produce synthetic graphite for use in batteries that power EVs, electric storage systems, electronics, military applications, and other products.
- Bainbridge Mayor Edward Reynolds welcomed Anovion to the city.
- Mayor Edward Reynolds highlighted the $800 million investment and the creation of over 400 high-paying, quality jobs as a huge win for the community.
- Mayor Edward Reynolds stated that the local workforce is ready to embrace the opportunity and expressed confidence in shared prosperity.
- Operations at the new facility are expected to begin in late 2025.
- The company will be hiring for technical, production, and engineering roles, along with administrative and management positions.
