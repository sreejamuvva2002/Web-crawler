# Wabash National Corp.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** logistics  ·  **Published:** 2005-03-02

**Source:** [Wabash to invest $40 million to replace trailer assembly lines - Truck News](https://www.trucknews.com/transportation/wabash-to-invest-40-million-to-replace-trailer-assembly-lines/1003116389/)  ·  `page_014553`

## Facts

- Its board of directors approved two major capital investment programs.
- An initial $10 million first phase of what is expected to be a $40 million multi-year program to replace four trailer assembly lines.
- $20 million for engineering and business process systems improvements.
- Wabash National Executive Vice-President and Chief Operating Officer Dick Giromini said the investment in production capabilities will help to revolutionize trailer manufacturing, incorporating the use of technology proven in the automotive industry.
- When completed, the new assembly process will result in lower production costs and improved safety and product quality.
- Each new line will produce annual cost savings of approximately $7 million when completed.
- This expenditure marks its commitment to the greater Lafayette community.
- Wabash National designs, manufactures, and markets standard and customized truck trailers under the Wabash brand name throughout the U.S. and Canada.
