# Unified Legacy

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** automotive_components  ·  **Published:** 2026-05-18

**Source:** [Unified Legacy to create 500 jobs with Georgia expansion](https://www.thefabricator.com/thefabricator/news/shopmanagement/unified-legacy-to-create-500-jobs-with-georgia-expansion)  ·  `page_011862`

## Facts

- Precision metal fabricator and manufacturer Unified Legacy has announced it will invest $125 million in a new manufacturing facility in Macon, Ga.
- The expansion is creating 500 new jobs over the next several years.
- The company manufactures complex products and components for the defense, aerospace, data center, and industrial markets.
- With advanced machining, welding, and fabrication capabilities, the company delivers custom ground support equipment, welded assemblies, generator enclosures, fuel storage tanks, and precision-machined components.
- The company’s existing manufacturing facility, Unified Defense, has been operating in Byron, Ga., since 2022.
- Construction of the new Macon facility is expected to begin this year.
