# Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corporation

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2011-02-25

**Source:** [Suzuki Manufacturing of America Celebrates Its 10-Year Anniversary - Racer X](https://racerxonline.com/2011/02/25/suzuki-manufacturing-of-america-celebrates-its-10-year-anniversary)  ·  `page_010865`

## Facts

- Suzuki Manufacturing of America Corporation (SMAC) celebrated its 10-year anniversary on February 25, 2011.
- The plant was built in the United States in 2001 to demonstrate Suzuki’s commitment to the all terrain vehicle (ATV) business.
- Initially the plant built the all-new 400cc Eiger and 500cc Vinson ATVs.
- Currently, SMAC assembles the award winning KINGQUAD line of ATVs for the North American and global markets.
- Mr. Nobuyuki Yamane serves as President of SMAC.
- The overall economic situation made the last couple of years very difficult for SMAC and affected the entire ATV industry.
- SMAC has recently increased production capacity.
- SMAC recalled some team members who were affected by previous workforce reductions.
- SMAC looks forward to increasing production in FY2011.
- SMAC is located in Rome, Ga.
