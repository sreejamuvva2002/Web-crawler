# Kia Georgia Manufacturing Plant

**Type:** facility  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-04-09

**Source:** [Kia Georgia Facility Tour – Next Generation Manufacturing](https://nextgenerationmfg.org/event/kia-georgia-facility-tour-2/)  ·  `page_010008`

## Facts

- The event is scheduled for April 9, 2025 @ 1:00 pm – 3:15 pm EDT.
- Registration for this event is open through COB, Wednesday, April 2nd in compliance with Kia Georgia security.
- No refunds or substitutions are offered after that date.
- The facility is located in West Point, GA.
- It is the only Kia manufacturing plant in the United States and the only automaker in Georgia.
- The plant covers 2200 acres with a total investment of over $2.8 billion.
- The Kia Georgia team manufactures the Telluride, Sorento and Sportage SUVs (and soon the EV9).
- Total production is 350,000 vehicles per year at this location.
- Operations run on 3 shifts a day, 24 hours a day.
- The Kia Georgia facility ships vehicles to sales markets around the globe, including the United States, Canada, Mexico, the Caribbean, Middle East and Pacific Nations.
- Kia Georgia states that environmental protection and regulatory compliance are core values while producing high quality, affordable vehicles to enhance both economic value and sustainability.
- Registration is open to executives, directors and senior managers of manufacturing companies.
