# Porsche Financial Services, Inc.

**Type:** company  ·  **Role:** vehicle_manufacturing  ·  **Published:** 2025-05-28

**Source:** [Porsche Financial Services Issues First Prime Auto Lease ABS Transaction Of 2025 - DriveSpark News](https://www.drivespark.com/four-wheelers/2025/porsche-financial-services-issues-prime-auto-lease-abs-2025-011-70955.html)  ·  `page_048711`

## Facts

- Porsche Financial Services, Inc. (PFS) is located in Atlanta, Georgia.
- PFS launched an auto lease Asset Backed Securities (ABS) offering in the United States with a principal amount of $810 million.
- This marks the fourth bond issuance by PFS within a year.
- PFS is a wholly owned subsidiary of Dr. Ing. h.c. F. Porsche AG, a German luxury car manufacturer.
- The securities issued in this Rule 144A transaction retained a 'AAA' rating from rating agencies and achieved competitive pricing.
- The transaction was divided into five tranches, including one with a floating rate, and was backed by auto lease contracts for Porsche vehicles.
- The deal saw strong subscription levels across all tranches from a diverse investor base that included investment funds, asset managers of financial institutions, trusts, banks, and corporates.
- Barclays Capital Inc. led the transaction with support from CIBC World Markets Corp., Mizuho, US Bancorp, and Wells Fargo Securities as book runners.
- Tobias Hausladen serves as Treasurer & Chief Financial Officer at PFS.
- Tobias Hausladen stated that PILOT 2025-1 is the first lease transaction to include battery-electric vehicles (BEVs), enabling PFS to securitize the entire range of model lines within the Porsche portfolio.
- PFS was founded in 1991 and provides leasing and financing products for Porsche customers and dealers in the U.S.
- In 2012, PFS expanded operations to become the captive finance provider for Volkswagen Group's exclusive brands including Bentley, Lamborghini, and Bugatti.
- Porsche Cars North America, Inc. (PCNA) was established in 1984 and has been headquartered in Atlanta since 1998.
- PCNA is the sole U.S. importer of models such as the Porsche 911 and Taycan.
- PCNA operates two Porsche Experience Centers in North America, one in Atlanta and another near Los Angeles, featuring driver development tracks and business centers.
- PCNA supports over 200 independently owned Porsche dealerships across the U.S., along with several studios and satellite stores.
- These dealerships provide parts, service, marketing, and training to ensure customers receive top-notch experiences aligned with Porsche's legacy of vehicle performance excellence.
- Porsche has a racing heritage with over 30,000 motorsport victories to date.
